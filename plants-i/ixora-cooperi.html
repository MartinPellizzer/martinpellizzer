Ixora cooperi

Ixora cooperi, commonly known as the flame bush, is a flowering shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of bright orange-red flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are glossy and arranged in whorls around the stems. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Ixora cooperi is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
