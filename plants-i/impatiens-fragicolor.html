Impatiens fragicolor

Impatiens fragicolor, commonly known as the rainbow impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a range of colors including red, pink, and white. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with upright stems and broad, heart-shaped leaves. Its flowers are borne in clusters and are known for their delicate appearance and fragrance.

Impatiens fragicolor thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
