Impatiens perezii

Impatiens perezii, commonly known as the Peruvian impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, which bloom throughout the growing season. Its leaves are ovate and slightly hairy, with a dark green color that provides a striking contrast to the vibrant flowers.

Impatiens perezii thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden beds.
