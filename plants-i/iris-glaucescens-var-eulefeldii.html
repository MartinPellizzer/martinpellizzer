Iris glaucescens var. eulefeldii

Iris glaucescens var. eulefeldii, commonly known as the Eulefeld iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features upright, grass-like leaves and produces striking blue to violet flowers with a distinctive white or pale yellow center. The plant is characterized by its hardy nature and ability to thrive in well-drained soils. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

The species is also valued for its resilience to cold climates and its ability to bloom reliably in late spring.
