Ilex aquifolium var. thunbergiana

Ilex aquifolium var. thunbergiana, commonly known as the Japanese holly, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It features dense, glossy, dark green leaves with spiny margins, resembling those of the common holly. The plant produces small, red berries that are ornamental but not edible for humans. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Its hardy nature makes it suitable for a variety of garden settings.
