Impatiens gamblei

Impatiens gamblei, commonly known as the golden impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers and heart-shaped, glossy green leaves. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 feet. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Impatiens gamblei is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
