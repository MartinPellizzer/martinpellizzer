Iriartea xanthorhiza

Iriartea xanthorhiza, commonly known as the yellow-stemmed bismarckia, is a member of the Arecaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces striking, yellowish-orange flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, adding to its ornamental value. Its growth habit makes it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation in warm climates.

However, it requires well-drained soil and protection from frost to thrive.
