Impatiens crassisepala

Impatiens crassisepala, commonly known as the broad-sepaled impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has broad, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens crassisepala is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
