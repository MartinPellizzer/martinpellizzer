Ixia scillaris var. subundulata

Ixia scillaris var. subundulata, commonly known as the wavy bellflower, is a perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It produces striking, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often with a distinctive wavy edge on the petals. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure. It is typically grown as a garden ornamental due to its vibrant blooms and ease of cultivation.

The variety subundulata is distinguished by its unique petal morphology compared to other Ixia species.
