Iberis ciliata var. contracta

Iberis ciliata var. contracta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved candytuft, is a low-growing, flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has narrow, upright leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft, fuzzy appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a valuable addition to both ornamental and ecological landscapes.
