Ilex floridana

Ilex floridana, commonly known as the Florida holly, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is an evergreen shrub or small tree that typically grows in moist, well-drained soils. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves that are alternate and often have spiny margins. It produces small, white to pale yellow flowers in the spring, which are followed by red, fleshy berries in the fall.

Ilex floridana is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in local ecosystems.
