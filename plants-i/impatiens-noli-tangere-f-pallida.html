Impatiens noli-tangere f. pallida

Impatiens noli-tangere f. pallida, commonly known as the white impatiens, is a hybrid cultivar of the species *Impatiens noli-tangere*.

It is characterized by its pale, almost white flowers, which contrast with its deep green foliage. This plant is a member of the Balsaminaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa. It thrives in partial shade and requires well-drained, moist soil to flourish.

The varietal form 'pallida' is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
