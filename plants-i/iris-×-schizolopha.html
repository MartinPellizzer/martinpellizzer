Iris × schizolopha

Iris × schizolopha, commonly known as the hybrid bearded iris, is a botanical cross between Iris germanica and Iris pallida.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically feature a central beard and a range of vibrant colors. The plant is a perennial herb with rhizomatous growth, often found in moist, well-drained soils. Its foliage consists of broad, sword-shaped leaves, and it blooms in late spring to early summer.

This hybrid is widely cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
