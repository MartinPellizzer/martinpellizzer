Impatiens mannii

Impatiens mannii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows as an upright herb with branched stems and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial shade, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Impatiens mannii is also valued for its ability to bloom continuously throughout the growing season.
