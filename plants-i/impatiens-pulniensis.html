Impatiens pulniensis

Impatiens pulniensis, commonly known as the purple impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Balsaminaceae and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and purple. Impatiens pulniensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
