Iris atropurpurea var. purpurea

Iris atropurpurea var. purpurea, commonly known as the purple iris, is a striking perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features tall, slender stems that bear showy, violet-blue to purple flowers with a distinctive three-parted structure. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are arranged in a fan-like pattern at the base. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant color and elegant form.
