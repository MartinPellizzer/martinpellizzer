Indigofera mangokyensis

Indigofera mangokyensis, commonly known as the Madagascar indigo plant, is a flowering plant species native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant is typically shrub-like, with woody stems and can grow up to several meters in height. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and potential medicinal applications.
