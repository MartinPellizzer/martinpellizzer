Ischyrolepis unispicata

Ischyrolepis unispicata, commonly known as the single-spiked orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and green. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense colonies in suitable habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
