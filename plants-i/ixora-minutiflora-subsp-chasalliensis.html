Ixora minutiflora subsp. chasalliensis

Ixora minutiflora subsp.

chasalliensis, commonly known as the Chasallien ixora, is a shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that are typically bright red or orange in color.

The plant has opposite, simple leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in coastal areas or near water bodies.

The flowers are clustered in inflorescences and are known for their ornamental value.
