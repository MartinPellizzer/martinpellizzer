Ivesia aperta

Ivesia aperta, commonly known as the open ivesia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It typically grows in dry, rocky habitats such as slopes and gravelly soils. The plant features basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. Its flowering stems bear clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in early spring.

Ivesia aperta is characterized by its low-growing habit and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.
