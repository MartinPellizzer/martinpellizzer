Ilex tetramera var. glabra

Ilex tetramera var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth evergreen holly, is a shrub or small tree native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are smooth and leathery in texture. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters, followed by bright red berries that are edible for birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its hardy nature and attractive appearance make it a valuable addition to native plant landscapes.
