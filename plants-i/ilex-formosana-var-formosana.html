Ilex formosana var. formosana

Ilex formosana var. formosana, commonly known as the Formosan holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to Taiwan.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, glossy leaves that remain throughout the year. The plant produces small white flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Its hardy nature and aesthetic appeal contribute to its value in both natural and cultivated environments.
