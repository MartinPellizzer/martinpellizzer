Ilex × nettletoniana

Ilex × nettletoniana, commonly known as the American holly, is a hybrid evergreen shrub or small tree native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its glossy, spiny leaves that are typically dark green with white or yellow veins. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its hardy nature and year-round foliage make it a valuable addition to gardens and landscapes.
