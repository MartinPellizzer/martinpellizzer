Ixora oblonga

Ixora oblonga, commonly known as the oblong flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a striking appearance. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Ixora oblonga is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
