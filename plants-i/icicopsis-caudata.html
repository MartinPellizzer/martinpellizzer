Icicopsis caudata

Icicopsis caudata, commonly known as the long-tailed bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its long, slender stems and showy blooms make it a popular choice for rock gardens and floral arrangements.

Icicopsis caudata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its beauty.
