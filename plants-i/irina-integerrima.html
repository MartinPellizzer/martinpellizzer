Irina integerrima

Irina integerrima, commonly known as the hardy rock rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves that are resistant to drought and salt. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Irina integerrima is valued for its ornamental beauty and hardy nature.
