Iris tadshikorum

Iris tadshikorum, commonly known as the Tadshikorum iris, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are grass-like, and it produces a single flower stem with several blooms.

Iris tadshikorum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
