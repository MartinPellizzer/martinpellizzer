Ixia aristata var. elegans

Ixia aristata var. elegans, commonly known as the elegant star lily, is a striking South African bulbous plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that bear clusters of bright yellow or orange flowers with a distinctive star-like shape. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that form a basal rosette, and its flowers are typically borne on long, slender pedicels. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

This variety is prized for its vibrant color and unique floral structure, which make it a standout in gardens during the spring and early summer.
