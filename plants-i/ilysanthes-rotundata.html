Ilysanthes rotundata

Ilysanthes rotundata, commonly known as the roundleaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, herbaceous growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem. Its leaves are rounded and glossy, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Ilysanthes rotundata thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
