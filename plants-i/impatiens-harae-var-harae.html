Impatiens harae var. harae

Impatiens harae var. harae, commonly known as the Harae Impatiens, is a species native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and a slightly hairy underside. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found growing in the understory of dense forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it prefers humid and cool conditions.
