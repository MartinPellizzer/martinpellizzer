Ilex densiflora

Ilex densiflora, commonly known as the denseflower holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves that are densely packed and provide a dense canopy. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters, which are followed by bright red berries that are attractive to birds. Ilex densiflora thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in suitable climates.
