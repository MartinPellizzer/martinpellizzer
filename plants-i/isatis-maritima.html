Isatis maritima

Isatis maritima, commonly known as sea kale, is a perennial plant native to coastal regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cabbage and mustard. The plant is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in saline and sandy soils, often found in tidal zones and dunes.

Sea kale is also valued for its edible leaves, which have a slightly bitter taste and are sometimes used in local cuisines.
