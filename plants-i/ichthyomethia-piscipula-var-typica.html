Ichthyomethia piscipula var. typica

Ichthyomethia piscipula var. typica, commonly known as the aquatic leaf plant, is a species native to freshwater environments in tropical regions.

It is characterized by its elongated, ribbon-like leaves that float on the water surface, providing shade and oxygenation. The plant has a rapid growth rate, making it popular for aquariums and pond landscapes. Its roots are submerged, absorbing nutrients from the water, while its stems exhibit a unique spiral growth pattern.
