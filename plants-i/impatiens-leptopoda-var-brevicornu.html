Impatiens leptopoda var. brevicornu

Impatiens leptopoda var. brevicornu, commonly known as the short-horned impatiens, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, spurred petals and a compact growth habit. The plant typically produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its foliage is usually glossy and dark green, adding to its ornamental appeal.
