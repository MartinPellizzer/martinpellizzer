Iris × australis var. tinaei

Iris × australis var. tinaei, commonly known as the Tinaei iris, is a hybrid perennial plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that bear striking blue to violet flowers with a distinctive white or pale yellow beak. The plant typically grows in damp, shaded environments such as wetlands and forest floors. Its foliage consists of sword-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in moist conditions.
