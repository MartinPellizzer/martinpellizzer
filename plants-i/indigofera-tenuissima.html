Indigofera tenuissima

Indigofera tenuissima, commonly known as slender indigo, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific Islands. The plant is characterized by its small, delicate leaves and clusters of purple or pink flowers. It has a fibrous root system and produces small, pod-like fruits containing several seeds.

Indigofera tenuissima is often used in traditional medicine and as a source of natural dyes.
