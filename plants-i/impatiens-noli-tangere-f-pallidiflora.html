Impatiens noli-tangere f. pallidiflora

Impatiens noli-tangere f. pallidiflora, commonly known as the pale-flowered impatiens, is a hybrid cultivar within the genus Impatiens, characterized by its delicate, pale pink to white flowers.

It is a herbaceous perennial that thrives in moist, shady environments, often found in woodland gardens. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves and produces small, showy blooms that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its flowers open in clusters and have a subtle fragrance, enhancing its ornamental value.

This cultivar is prized for its ability to add a soft, ethereal touch to garden landscapes.
