Iris × piatrae

Iris × piatrae, commonly known as the Piatra Iris, is a hybrid ornamental plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking violet-blue flowers with a distinctive yellow beard in the center. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and features sword-shaped, grass-like leaves. Native to the Carpathian Mountains in Romania, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance and hardiness, it is a popular choice for gardeners seeking to add color and texture to shady or wet garden areas.
