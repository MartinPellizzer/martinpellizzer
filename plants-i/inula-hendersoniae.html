Inula hendersoniae

Inula hendersoniae, commonly known as Henderson's Inula, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, thriving in cool and moist environments. The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its distinctive appearance.
