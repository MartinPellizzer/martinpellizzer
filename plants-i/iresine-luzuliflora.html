Iresine luzuliflora

Iresine luzuliflora, commonly known as the purple leaf iron plant, is a strikingly ornamental shrub native to South America.

It features vibrant, velvety leaves that range from deep purple to red, often with a metallic sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in partial to full sun and prefers well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for adding color and texture to landscapes.
