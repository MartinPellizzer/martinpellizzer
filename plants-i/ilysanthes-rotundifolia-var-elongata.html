Ilysanthes rotundifolia var. elongata

Ilysanthes rotundifolia var. elongata, commonly known as the elongated broadleaf fern, is a variant of the broadleaf fern species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, round leaves that are elongated in shape, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as along stream banks or in dense undergrowth. Its leaves are often covered in a fine layer of hair, which helps to retain moisture.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
