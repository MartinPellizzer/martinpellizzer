Impatiens bulbosa

Impatiens bulbosa, commonly known as the bulbous touch-me-not, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive swollen, bulb-like base and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate in shape, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Impatiens bulbosa thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.
