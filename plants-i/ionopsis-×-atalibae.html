Ionopsis × atalibae

Ionopsis × atalibae, commonly known as the hybrid sunflower.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Ionopsis hirta and Ionopsis perennis, both native to South America. This plant is characterized by its vibrant yellow to orange flowers and narrow, grass-like leaves. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

The hybrid is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
