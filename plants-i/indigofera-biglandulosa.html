Indigofera biglandulosa

Indigofera biglandulosa, commonly known as the large-fruited indigo, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces elongated, cylindrical pods that contain several seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
