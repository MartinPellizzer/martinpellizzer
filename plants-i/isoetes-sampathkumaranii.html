Isoetes sampathkumaranii

Isoetes sampathkumaranii, commonly known as the Sri Lankan watermace, is a rare aquatic fern belonging to the genus Isoetes.

It is characterized by its unique spore-bearing structures called sporangia, which are typically found in clusters on specialized leaves called sporophylls. The plant thrives in freshwater environments, often in still or slow-moving water bodies. Its morphology includes a creeping rhizome with leaf-like structures that are adapted for underwater growth.
