Isatis corymbosa

Isatis corymbosa, commonly known as Chinese yellow root, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family and is characterized by its yellow flowers and pinnately compound leaves. The plant produces long, slender roots that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Isatis corymbosa is also valued for its ornamental qualities in gardens.
