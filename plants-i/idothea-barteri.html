Idothea barteri

Idothea barteri, commonly known as the Barter's tree fern, is a member of the Cyatheaceae family and is native to tropical Africa.

It is a tall, tree-like fern that can grow up to 10 meters in height, with a trunk covered in fibrous, dark brown to blackish bark. The plant has large, pinnately compound fronds that are typically arching and can reach lengths of up to 3 meters. Its rhizome is robust and serves as the main storage organ for nutrients and water.

Idothea barteri thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.
