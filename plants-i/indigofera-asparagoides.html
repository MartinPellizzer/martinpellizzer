Indigofera asparagoides

Indigofera asparagoides, commonly known as blue bean or wild indigo, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its climbing habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to purple, and it is often found in moist, disturbed soils.
