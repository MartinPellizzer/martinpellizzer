Ilex soderstromii

Ilex soderstromii, commonly known as the Soderstrom holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically leathery and elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for various bird species. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is often cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
