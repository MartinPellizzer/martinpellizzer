Impatiens dempoana

Impatiens dempoana, commonly known as the Madagascar impatiens, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom throughout the growing season. Its leaves are ovate with a slightly hairy texture, and it prefers moist, well-drained soil in partial shade.

Impatiens dempoana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of garden conditions.
