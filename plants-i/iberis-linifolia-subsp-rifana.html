Iberis linifolia subsp. rifana

Iberis linifolia subsp.

rifana, commonly known as Rifan candytuft, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features dense clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in early spring.

The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
