Indigofera cornuligera subsp. flindersensis

Indigofera cornuligera subsp.

flindersensis, commonly known as Flinders indigo, is a flowering plant native to Australia. It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
