Ilex nitens

Ilex nitens, commonly known as the silver leaf holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that have a silvery underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible for birds but not for humans. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Ilex nitens is also valued for its ability to tolerate urban environments and air pollution.
