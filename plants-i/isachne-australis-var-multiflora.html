Isachne australis var. multiflora

Isachne australis var. multiflora, commonly known as the Australian flat sedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to Australia.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences that are typically green or brown in color. The plant thrives in wet or moist environments, often found in swampy areas or along waterways. It has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread and form dense mats.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species in wetland ecosystems.
