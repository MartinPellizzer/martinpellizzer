Isodon excisus var. shikokianus

Isodon excisus var. shikokianus, commonly known as the Japanese skullcap, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its flowers are small, tubular, and often purple or blue in color, attracting various pollinators.

The leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly serrated edge, contributing to its distinctive botanical appearance.
