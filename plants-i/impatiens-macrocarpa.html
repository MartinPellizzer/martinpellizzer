Impatiens macrocarpa

Impatiens macrocarpa, commonly known as the large-fruited impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, round seeds. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens macrocarpa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
