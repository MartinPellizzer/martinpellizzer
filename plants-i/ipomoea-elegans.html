Ipomoea elegans

Ipomoea elegans, commonly known as the delicate morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep purple. The plant is a vigorous climber, often using its tendrils to ascend supports such as trellises or fences. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Ipomoea elegans is also known for its rapid growth and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
