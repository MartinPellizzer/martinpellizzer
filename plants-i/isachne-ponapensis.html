Isachne ponapensis

Isachne ponapensis, commonly known as the Ponape grass, is a species of grass native to the tropical regions of the Pacific Islands.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes numerous other grass species. This plant is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. It typically thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.

Isachne ponapensis plays a role in local ecosystems and has cultural significance in some island communities.
