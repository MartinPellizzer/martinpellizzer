Impatiens megacalyx

Impatiens megacalyx, commonly known as the giant calyx impatiens, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant has opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves that are usually hairy and have a distinctive glandular texture. Its stems are often covered in fine, soft hairs, and the calyx is notably enlarged and persistent.

Impatiens megacalyx thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
