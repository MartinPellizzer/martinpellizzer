Ipomoea tenuissima

Ipomoea tenuissima, commonly known as the slender morning glory, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It produces small, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pale blue to white in color. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is known for its rapid growth habit. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Ipomoea tenuissima is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and flowering patterns.
