Iris Pseudopallida F. Dalmatica

Iris pseudopallida f. dalmatica is a rare and beautiful variant of the iris plant native to the Balkans. It is known for its striking pale blue to white flowers that bloom in early spring. This specific form is often found in moist, calcareous soils along streams and riverbanks. Its delicate appearance makes it a sought-after plant among horticultural enthusiasts. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species requiring careful conservation efforts.
