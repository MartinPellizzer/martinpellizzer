Itea rhamnoides

Itea rhamnoides, commonly known as smooth hydrangea, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It features compound leaves that are alternate and pinnately arranged, with serrated margins. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in panicles during the summer months. Its fruit is a dry, three-lobed capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

Itea rhamnoides is valued in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in moist, woodland environments.
