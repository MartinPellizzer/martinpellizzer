Impatiens multiramea

Impatiens multiramea, commonly known as the multibranched touch-me-not, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, spurred flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and violet. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique branching pattern and rapid growth make it a distinctive member of the Balsaminaceae family.
