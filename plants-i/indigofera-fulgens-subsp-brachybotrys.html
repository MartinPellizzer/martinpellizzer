Indigofera fulgens subsp. brachybotrys

Indigofera fulgens subsp.

brachybotrys, commonly known as the dwarf flame bean, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa. It is characterized by its small, clustered leaves and vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the growing season.

The plant produces elongated, bean-like pods that contain several seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used traditionally in some regions for medicinal purposes.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and visually appealing member of the legume family.
