Ione soidaoensis

Ione soidaoensis, commonly known as the Soidao endemic flora, is a rare plant species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale pink. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a smooth texture, while its stems are slender and slightly woody.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, Ione soidaoensis is considered a vulnerable species.
