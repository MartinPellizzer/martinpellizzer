Impatiens hypophylla f. glabra

Impatiens hypophylla f. glabra, commonly known as the smooth-leaved impatiens, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous leaves and showy, spurred flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or white. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its rapid growth and attractive appearance make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
