Impatiens bardotiae

Impatiens bardotiae, commonly known as the Bardot's impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of western Africa.

It belongs to the Balsaminaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.
