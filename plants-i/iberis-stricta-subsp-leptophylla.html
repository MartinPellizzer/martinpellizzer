Iberis stricta subsp. leptophylla

Iberis stricta subsp.

leptophylla, commonly known as the slenderleaf candytuft, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountains of southern Europe. It features dense clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring.

The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs. It thrives in well-drained, sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in alpine or rocky habitats.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
