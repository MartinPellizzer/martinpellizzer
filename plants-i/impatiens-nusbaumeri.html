Impatiens nusbaumeri

Impatiens nusbaumeri, commonly known as the Nusbaumer's Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to white. The plant typically blooms during the wet season and is often found in shaded, humid environments such as rainforests. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped and covered in a fine, soft hair, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Impatiens nusbaumeri is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in partial shade.
