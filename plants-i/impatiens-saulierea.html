Impatiens saulierea

Impatiens saulierea, commonly known as the Madagascar impatiens, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Balsaminaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, spurred flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with slender stems and broad, ovate leaves. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens saulierea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
