Indigofera hebepetala var. hebepetala

Indigofera hebepetala var. hebepetala, commonly known as the slender indigo, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing habit and trifoliate leaves with elliptical leaflets. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of blue or purple, which bloom in clusters. Its root system is fibrous, and it prefers well-drained soils in full sun to partial shade.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional uses in some cultures.
