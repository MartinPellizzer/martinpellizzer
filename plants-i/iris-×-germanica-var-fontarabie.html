Iris × germanica var. fontarabie

Iris × germanica var. fontarabie, commonly known as the Fontarabie iris, is a hybrid perennial plant characterized by its striking violet-blue flowers.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is native to the Mediterranean region. The plant features tall, slender stems that bear showy, spurred flowers with three upright petals and three drooping sepals. Its foliage consists of sword-shaped, dark green leaves that are often used in ornamental gardening.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
