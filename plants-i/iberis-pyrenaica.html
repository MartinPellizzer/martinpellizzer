Iberis pyrenaica

Iberis pyrenaica, commonly known as the Pyrenean candytuft, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the family Brassicaceae, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. Its leaves are pinnately divided, giving it a delicate, fern-like appearance.
