Isachne lisboae

Isachne lisboae, commonly known as Lisbon grass, is a perennial grass species native to Portugal.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Botanically, it is notable for its morphological adaptations to Mediterranean climates.
