Impatiens decaryana

Impatiens decaryana, commonly known as the scarlet impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or orange flowers, which bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens decaryana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
