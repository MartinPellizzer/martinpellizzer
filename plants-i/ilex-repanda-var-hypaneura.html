Ilex repanda var. hypaneura

Ilex repanda var. hypaneura, commonly known as the broadleaf holly, is a variant of the broadleaf holly species native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a evergreen shrub or small tree characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter on the underside. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in the spring, followed by red berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Its dense foliage and year-round appearance make it a valuable addition to gardens and natural habitats.
