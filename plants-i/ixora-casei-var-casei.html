Ixora casei var. casei

Ixora casei var. casei, commonly known as the Chinese flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year, making it a popular ornamental plant. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth, glossy surface. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Ixora casei var. casei is often cultivated for its vibrant floral display and is used in landscaping and traditional medicine.
