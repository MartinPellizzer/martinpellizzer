Impatiens ombrophila

Impatiens ombrophila, commonly known as the rain impatiens, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and are borne on slender stems. The plant prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories where it thrives in high humidity. Its leaves are ovate and glossy, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Impatiens ombrophila is valued in horticulture for its ability to bloom continuously throughout the growing season.
