Indocalamus youxiuensis

Indocalamus youxiuensis, commonly known as the Youxiu bamboo, is a species of grass in the family Poaceae.

It is native to China, specifically the Yunnan and Sichuan provinces, where it grows in humid, mountainous regions. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can reach heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are long and narrow, with a smooth texture and a slightly glossy surface.

This bamboo is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
