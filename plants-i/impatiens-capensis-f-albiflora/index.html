Impatiens Capensis F. Albiflora

Impatiens capensis f. albiflora is a striking variant of the common jewelweed plant known for its pale, almost white flowers. This unique form stands out in the wild with its reduced pigmentation, creating a soft, ethereal appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found along stream banks and in woodland areas. Despite its delicate look, it is a resilient plant capable of surviving in challenging ecological conditions. Its beauty and rarity make it a subject of interest for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike.
