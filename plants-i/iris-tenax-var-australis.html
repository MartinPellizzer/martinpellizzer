Iris tenax var. australis

Iris tenax var. australis, commonly known as the Southern Flag Iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers, which bloom in early spring. The plant has sword-shaped, grass-like leaves that are typically bright green and grow in dense clumps. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is noted for its hardiness and ability to naturalize in suitable climates.
