Ixia avellana

Ixia avellana, commonly known as the yellow ox-eye, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that resemble daisies. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Ixia avellana is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its vibrant, long-lasting flowers.
