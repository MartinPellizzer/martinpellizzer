Ifloga labillardierei

Ifloga labillardierei, commonly known as the golden rain tree, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a striking visual display. The tree has compound leaves with multiple leaflets, and its bark is smooth and grayish in color. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The plant is also valued for its ability to tolerate urban environments and air pollution.
