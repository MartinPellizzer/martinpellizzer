Ilex guianensis var. macoucoua

Ilex guianensis var. macoucoua, commonly known as the Macoucoua holly, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a evergreen shrub or small tree that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves that are often elliptical in shape. Its small, white flowers are followed by red berries that are attractive to birds.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
