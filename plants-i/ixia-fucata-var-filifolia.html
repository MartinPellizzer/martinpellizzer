Ixia fucata var. filifolia

Ixia fucata var. filifolia, commonly known as the featherleaf oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the genus Ixia in the family Iridaceae, which includes irises and related plants. The plant is characterized by its delicate, fern-like foliage and clusters of bright yellow flowers with purple markings. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, typically from late winter to early spring.
