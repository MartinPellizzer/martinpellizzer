Impatiens holstii

Impatiens holstii, commonly known as the Holst's impatiens, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens holstii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to add color to shaded garden spaces.
