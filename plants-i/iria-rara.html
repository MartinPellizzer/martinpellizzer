Iria rara

Iria rara, commonly known as the rare orchid, is a unique plant native to specific regions with high humidity and shaded environments.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has slender, wiry stems and lance-shaped leaves that help it conserve moisture. Due to its limited habitat and threats from deforestation, Iria rara is considered endangered.

Conservation efforts are crucial to preserve this rare botanical species.
