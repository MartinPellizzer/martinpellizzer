Impatiens noli-tangere f. pallescens

Impatiens noli-tangere f. pallescens, commonly known as the pale impatiens, is a hybrid form of the popular garden impatiens.

It is characterized by its pale green foliage and smaller, delicate flowers compared to the standard variety. This cultivar is prized for its ability to thrive in shaded environments, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped blooms in shades of white to pink, adding a soft floral touch to shaded landscapes.

Its botanical appeal lies in its unique coloration and adaptability to various growing conditions.
