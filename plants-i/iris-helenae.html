Iris helenae

Iris helenae, commonly known as the Helen's iris, is a species of flowering plant in the genus Iris.

It is native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe, particularly in countries like Italy and Greece. The plant features tall, slender stems that bear showy, violet-blue flowers with a distinctive white or pale blue throat. Its leaves are sword-shaped and grass-like, typical of many iris species.

Iris helenae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
