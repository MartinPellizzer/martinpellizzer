Ionoxalis angulata

Ionoxalis angulata, commonly known as the zigzag sorrel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves with a distinctive zigzag pattern along the leaflets. The plant produces small, delicate white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Ionoxalis angulata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and unique appearance.
