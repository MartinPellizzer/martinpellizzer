Indigofera okinawae

Indigofera okinawae, commonly known as the Okinawa indigo.

It is a flowering plant native to Japan, specifically the Ryukyu Islands. The plant belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, clustered flowers. It produces pods that contain seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in its native habitat.
