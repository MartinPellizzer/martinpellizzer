Imatophyllum miniatum vanhouttei

Imatophyllum miniatum vanhouttei, commonly known as the miniature fern, is a small, delicate fern species native to tropical regions.

It features finely divided, lance-shaped fronds that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. The plant has a compact growth habit, often forming dense clusters of foliage. Its leaves are covered in a fine, silky texture, giving it a soft appearance.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.
