Iberis conferta

Iberis conferta, commonly known as the creeping winter jasmine, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Europe.

It features dense clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has aromatic, evergreen foliage that remains lush throughout the year. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and as a ground cover.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
