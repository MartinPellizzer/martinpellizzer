Ipomoea capillifolia

Ipomoea capillifolia, commonly known as the silky leaf morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its delicate, heart-shaped leaves and striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to white. The plant is an annual vine that thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil. Its rapid growth and showy blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

However, it can become invasive in certain environments if not managed properly.
