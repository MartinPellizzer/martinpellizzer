Iresine celosioides var. macrophylla

Iresine celosioides var. macrophylla, commonly known as the Crimson Beauty or Red Leaf Iron Plant, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, velvety red or purple leaves, which are often adorned with contrasting white or silver veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and as a decorative plant in shaded outdoor settings.
