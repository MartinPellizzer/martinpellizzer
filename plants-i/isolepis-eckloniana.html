Isolepis eckloniana

Isolepis eckloniana, commonly known as the flat sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in wetland environments such as marshes, swamps, and along the edges of ponds. The plant has cylindrical, grass-like leaves that are often dark green and slightly hairy. Its inflorescence consists of dense, spike-like clusters of small, three-lobed flowers.

Isolepis eckloniana plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wetland organisms.
