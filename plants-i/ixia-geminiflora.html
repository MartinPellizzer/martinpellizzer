Ixia geminiflora

Ixia geminiflora, commonly known as the twin-flowered ixia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant typically grows from a bulbous stem and produces clusters of flowers that are often arranged in pairs, giving it its common name. Ixia geminiflora thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Its unique appearance and vibrant colors make it a standout addition to any botanical collection.
