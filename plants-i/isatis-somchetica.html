Isatis somchetica

Isatis somchetica, commonly known as Thai woad, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like mustard and cabbage. The plant is characterized by its striking blue flowers and heart-shaped leaves. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and historical use in dye production.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in both horticulture and ecological studies.
