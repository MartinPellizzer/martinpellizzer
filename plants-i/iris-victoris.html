Iris victoris

Iris victoris, commonly known as the purple iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, slender stems that bear showy, violet-blue flowers with a distinctive three-parted structure. The plant has sword-shaped, grass-like leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Iris victoris is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its striking floral display.
