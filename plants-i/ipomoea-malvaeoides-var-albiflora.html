Ipomoea malvaeoides var. albiflora

Ipomoea malvaeoides var. albiflora, commonly known as the whiteflower morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in the morning.
