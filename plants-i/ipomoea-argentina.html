Ipomoea argentina

Ipomoea argentina, commonly known as the Argentine morning glory, is a climbing vine native to South America.

It produces vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep purple, often opening in the morning. The plant has heart-shaped, velvety leaves and thrives in warm, sunny conditions. Its rapid growth habit makes it suitable for trellises or as a ground cover.

Ipomoea argentina is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for its edible tubers.
