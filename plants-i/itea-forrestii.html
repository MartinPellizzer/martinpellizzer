Itea forrestii

Itea forrestii, commonly known as the Chinese spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in flat-topped clusters, typically in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, arching stems and finely divided, feather-like leaves that give it a delicate appearance. It prefers moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

Itea forrestii is often used in ornamental horticulture for its attractive foliage and floral display.
