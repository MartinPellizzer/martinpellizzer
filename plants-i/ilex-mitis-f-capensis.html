Ilex mitis f. capensis

Ilex mitis f. capensis, commonly known as the Cape holly, is a shrub or small tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are typically leathery and elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, white flowers that are followed by bright red berries, which are an important food source for local wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
