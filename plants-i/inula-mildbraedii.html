Inula mildbraedii

Inula mildbraedii, commonly known as the mildbraed's ragwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Afghanistan and Pakistan. The plant is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and rough, hairy leaves. It thrives in rocky and arid environments, often growing at high altitudes.

Inula mildbraedii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
