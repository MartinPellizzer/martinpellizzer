Iris setosa var. interior

Iris setosa var. interior, commonly known as the Interior Setosa Iris, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers with a white or yellow center. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in open woodlands or meadows. Its rhizomatous root system allows it to spread and form dense clumps.

It blooms in late spring to early summer, adding vibrant color to its native habitat.
