Iria leucocolea

Iria leucocolea, commonly known as the white-leaved iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking white to pale blue flowers. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy inflorescences that bloom in spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Iria leucocolea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
