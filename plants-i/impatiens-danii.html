Impatiens danii

Impatiens danii, commonly known as the Danii Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and grows as an annual or perennial depending on the climate. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its delicate appearance and ability to attract pollinators make it a valuable addition to ornamental gardens.
