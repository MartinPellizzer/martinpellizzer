Iberis lamottii

Iberis lamottii, commonly known as the lamott's candytuft, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of white or pink flowers in early spring. The plant has a compact, mat-forming growth habit with small, rounded leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and ability to attract pollinators.
