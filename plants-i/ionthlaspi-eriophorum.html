Ionthlaspi eriophorum

Ionthlaspi eriophorum, commonly known as the woolly rockcress, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It typically thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine environments. The plant features basal leaves that are lobed and covered in a dense layer of white, woolly hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its small, yellow flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom in early spring.

Ionthlaspi eriophorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in rock gardens.
