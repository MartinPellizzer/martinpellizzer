Iresine pedicellata

Iresine pedicellata, commonly known as the red leaf hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety red foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant leaves and delicate blooms.

Iresine pedicellata is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
