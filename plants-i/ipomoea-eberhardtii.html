Ipomoea eberhardtii

Ipomoea eberhardtii, commonly known as the Eberhardt's morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous annual vine that typically grows up to 3 meters in length. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale purple, often opening in the early morning. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine, silky hairs.
