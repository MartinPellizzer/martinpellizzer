Illicium montanum

Illicium montanum, commonly known as mountain star anise, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, star-shaped fruits that resemble those of the star anise (Illicium verum). The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers followed by dark purple berries. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly serrated margin.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
