Iria multifolia

Iria multifolia, commonly known as the leafy broom or multifoliate broom, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant has finely divided, feather-like leaves that give it a delicate appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often growing in rocky or sandy habitats.

Iria multifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and seasonal blooms.
