Isodon excisoides

Isodon excisoides, commonly known as the Chinese skullcap, is a perennial herb native to Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks.

The leaves and flowers are used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
