Itea bodinieri

Itea bodinieri, commonly known as the Chinese spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features slender, arching stems that produce clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers in early summer. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sun.

Its ornamental value comes from its delicate flowers and graceful form.
