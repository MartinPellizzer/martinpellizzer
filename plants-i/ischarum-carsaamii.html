Ischarum carsaamii

Ischarum carsaamii, commonly known as the alpine sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically thrives in rocky and arid environments. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are usually succulent and adapted to conserve water in harsh conditions.

Ischarum carsaamii plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native habitat and is of interest to botanists studying alpine flora.
