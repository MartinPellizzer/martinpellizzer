Ixora colebrookei

Ixora colebrookei, commonly known as the red ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a striking visual appeal. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

Ixora colebrookei is also valued for its ability to tolerate partial shade and its relatively low maintenance requirements.
