Iris × germanica variegata

Iris × germanica variegata, commonly known as the variegated bearded iris, is a hybrid perennial plant native to Europe.

It features showy, colorful flowers with a distinctive beard on the lower petals. The foliage consists of broad, grass-like leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. This variety thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom in early summer, adding vibrant color to landscapes.
