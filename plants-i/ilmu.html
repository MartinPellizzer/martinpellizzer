Ilmu

Ilmu, commonly known as the "spider plant," is a popular ornamental plant native to Africa.

It is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, white, bell-shaped flowers that appear on long, slender stalks. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in a variety of light conditions.

The name "spider plant" refers to the way its offsets resemble tiny spiders clinging to the mother plant.
