Iozoste mollis

Iozoste mollis, commonly known as the soft-stemmed iozoste, is a herbaceous plant native to the temperate regions of southern Africa.

It features slender, erect stems covered in fine, silky hairs that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the spring and early summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a slightly waxy texture.

Iozoste mollis is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.
