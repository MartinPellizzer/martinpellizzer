Iberis linifolia subsp. balansae

Iberis linifolia subsp.

balansae, commonly known as the Balansae candytuft, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features dense clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring.

The plant has hairy, linear leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white powder. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its prolonged flowering period and hardy nature.
