Isolona campanulata

Isolona campanulata, commonly known as the Cape bellflower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its unique appearance.

Isolona campanulata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
