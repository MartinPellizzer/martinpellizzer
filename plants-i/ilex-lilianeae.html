Ilex lilianeae

Ilex lilianeae, commonly known as the Liliane's holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to the high-altitude regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, glossy leaves that are typically oval in shape. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. Ilex lilianeae is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded environments.

It is also valued for its ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
