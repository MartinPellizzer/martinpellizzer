Iberis corymbosa

Iberis corymbosa, commonly known as candytuft, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces dense clusters of white or pink flowers in spring. The plant features succulent, lobed leaves and is often used in rock gardens or as a border plant. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy and easy-to-care-for species.

Its ornamental value comes from its showy blooms and compact growth habit.
