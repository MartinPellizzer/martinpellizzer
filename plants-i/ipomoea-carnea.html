Ipomoea carnea

Ipomoea carnea, commonly known as scarlet morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in the morning. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil.

Ipomoea carnea is a fast-growing vine that can reach lengths of up to 10 meters.
