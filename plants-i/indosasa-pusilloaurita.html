Indosasa pusilloaurita

Indosasa pusilloaurita, commonly known as the small-leafed indosasa, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, lanceolate leaves. The plant produces daisy-like yellow flowers with a central disk surrounded by ray florets. It is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive species within its native range in Southeast Asia.
