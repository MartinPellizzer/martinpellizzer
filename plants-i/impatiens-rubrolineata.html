Impatiens rubrolineata

Impatiens rubrolineata, commonly known as the red-lined impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Balsaminaceae and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces clusters of trumpet-shaped blooms in shades of red, pink, and white. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens and greenhouses.

Its rapid growth and ability to bloom throughout the growing season make it a popular choice for container gardening.
