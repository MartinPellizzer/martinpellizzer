Iris tolmieana

Iris tolmieana, commonly known as the Tolmie's iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers with a distinctive dark purple stripe down the center. The plant has sword-shaped leaves that are typically bluish-green in color and are arranged in a basal rosette. It produces tall, slender flowering stems that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height.

Iris tolmieana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well adapted to rocky and well-drained soils.
