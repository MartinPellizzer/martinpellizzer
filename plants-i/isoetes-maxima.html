Isoetes maxima

Isoetes maxima, commonly known as the giant water fern, is a species of aquatic plant belonging to the order Isoetales.

It is characterized by its unique morphology, featuring a spore-bearing stalk with specialized leaves called strobila. The plant thrives in freshwater environments, particularly in shallow, nutrient-rich waters. Its spores are dispersed by water currents, allowing it to colonize new areas.

Isoetes maxima plays an important ecological role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various microorganisms and invertebrates.
