Inula × simonkaiana

Inula × simonkaiana, commonly known as the hybrid elecampane, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between Inula helenium and Inula crithmoides, resulting in a plant with distinctive features combining traits from both parents. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring gray-green, hairy stems and large, yellow-orange composite flowers. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a rough texture, often with a strong, camphor-like scent.

Inula × simonkaiana is native to Europe and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils.
