Ixora pendula var. montana

Ixora pendula var. montana, commonly known as the mountain ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features opposite, simple leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth upper surface. Its showy, clustered inflorescences consist of small, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
