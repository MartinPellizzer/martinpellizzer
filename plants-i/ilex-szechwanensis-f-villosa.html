Ilex szechwanensis f. villosa

Ilex szechwanensis f. villosa, commonly known as the Sichuan holly, is a shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, glossy evergreen foliage, which is often covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, red berries that are attractive to birds and can persist on the plant throughout the winter. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value and adaptability make it a popular choice for horticultural use in temperate climates.
