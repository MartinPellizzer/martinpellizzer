Ixeris longirostra

Ixeris longirostra, commonly known as the long-beaked centipede grass, is a perennial flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flower clusters. It is often used in ornamental gardening due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.

Ixeris longirostra is also valued for its ability to thrive in various soil types and climates.
