Isatis callifera

Isatis callifera, commonly known as the Chinese yellow rocket, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants such as cabbage and mustard. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and lance-shaped leaves. It typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

Isatis callifera is valued for its ornamental appeal and has also been used in traditional medicine.
