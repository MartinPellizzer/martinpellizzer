Iris schelkownikowii f. emaculata

Iris schelkownikowii f. emaculata, commonly known as the slender iris, is a botanical variant characterized by its delicate and elongated foliage.

This subspecies belongs to the Iridaceae family and is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. Its flowers are typically pale blue or white, with a narrow, slender structure that distinguishes it from other iris species. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and prefers sunny to partially shaded environments.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
