Imperatoria caucasica

Imperatoria caucasica, commonly known as Caucasian spurge, is a herbaceous plant native to the Caucasus region.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy stems and leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers clustered in cyathia, which are typical of the spurge family. Imperatoria caucasica is often found in rocky or disturbed soils and is adapted to semi-arid conditions.

It has a shallow root system and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
