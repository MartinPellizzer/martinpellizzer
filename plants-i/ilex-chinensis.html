Ilex chinensis

Ilex chinensis, commonly known as Chinese holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, white flowers in the spring, which are followed by bright red berries in the fall. Ilex chinensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
