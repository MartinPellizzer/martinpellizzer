Impatiens gagnepainii

Impatiens gagnepainii, commonly known as the Gagnepain's impatiens, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Balsaminaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white. Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
