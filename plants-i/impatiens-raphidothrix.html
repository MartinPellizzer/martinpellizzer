Impatiens raphidothrix

Impatiens raphidothrix, commonly known as the red-rooted impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine, soft hairs.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
