Iris histrio

Iris histrio, commonly known as the Italian iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, upright stems that bear showy, violet-blue flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with sword-shaped, grass-like leaves. Its rhizomatous root system allows it to thrive in well-drained soils and full sun.

The flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, adding vibrant color to gardens and natural landscapes.
