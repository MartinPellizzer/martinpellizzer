Impatiens baronii

Impatiens baronii, commonly known as the Baron's impatiens, is a herbaceous perennial native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are usually hairy and have a velvety texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its rapid growth and ability to produce numerous flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
