Ixora coccinea var. intermedia

Ixora coccinea var. intermedia, commonly known as the red flame ixora, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of bright red, tubular flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that provide a striking contrast to its vibrant floral display. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and as a houseplant.
