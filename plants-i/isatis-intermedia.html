Isatis intermedia

Isatis intermedia, commonly known as yellow rocket, is a biennial plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cabbage and mustard. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, typically from April to June, and it has alternate, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It grows up to 1.5 meters tall and is often found in disturbed soils, roadsides, and waste areas.

Isatis intermedia is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used historically in traditional herbal remedies.
