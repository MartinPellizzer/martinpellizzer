Imperatoria flavescens

Imperatoria flavescens, commonly known as the yellow goosefoot, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its distinctive yellow leaves and small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Imperatoria flavescens is known for its hardy nature and ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
