Isoetes canadensis var. robbinsii

Isoetes canadensis var. robbinsii, commonly known as the Robbins' watermilfoil, is a species of aquatic plant belonging to the genus Isoetes.

It is characterized by its spore-bearing structures called sporangia, which are typically located on specialized leaves called sporophylls. This variety is native to North America and is often found in shallow, freshwater environments such as ponds and lakes. The plant exhibits a unique morphology with a central axis and a crown of leaves that are often ribbon-like in appearance.

It plays an important ecological role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various aquatic organisms.
