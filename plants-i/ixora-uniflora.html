Ixora uniflora

Ixora uniflora, commonly known as the single-flower ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 3 meters. The plant produces clusters of small, bright red flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and elliptical in shape, with a smooth surface.

Ixora uniflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to tropical and subtropical climates.
