Illicium yunnanense

Illicium yunnanense, commonly known as the Yunnan star anise, is a deciduous shrub native to southwestern China.

It belongs to the family Schisandraceae and is characterized by its aromatic, star-shaped fruits that resemble those of the star anise. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by the distinctive, aromatic berries. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a glossy surface.

Illicium yunnanense is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
