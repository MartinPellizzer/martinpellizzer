Ianhedgea minutiflora subsp.

minutiflora, commonly known as the small-flowered hibiscus, is a species native to tropical regions of South America. It is a shrub characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, showy flowers that are typically pink or red.

The flowers are usually borne in clusters and have five petals, with a prominent central stigma. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
