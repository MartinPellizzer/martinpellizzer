Iris bisflorens

Iris bisflorens, commonly known as the double-flowered iris, is a striking perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that bear large, showy flowers with two distinct rows of petals. The flowers typically bloom in late spring to early summer, showcasing a range of colors from blue to purple. This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for adding visual interest to floral arrangements and botanical collections.
