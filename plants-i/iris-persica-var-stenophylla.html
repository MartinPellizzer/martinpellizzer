Iris persica var. stenophylla

Iris persica var. stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf Persian iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features upright, grass-like leaves that are narrow and linear, typically ranging from 30 to 60 centimeters in length. The plant produces striking blue to violet flowers with three upright petals and three spreading sepals, blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its elegant appearance and adaptability to various growing conditions.
