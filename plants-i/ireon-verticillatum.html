Ireon verticillatum

Ireon verticillatum, commonly known as the spiral leaf plant, is a unique species characterized by its distinctive whorled arrangement of leaves.

It is native to arid and semi-arid regions, where it has adapted to thrive in harsh environmental conditions. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that spiral around the stem, creating an elegant, decorative appearance. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color.

Due to its ornamental value and resilience, it is often cultivated in xeriscaped gardens and rockeries.
