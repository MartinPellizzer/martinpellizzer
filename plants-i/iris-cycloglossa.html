Iris cycloglossa

Iris cycloglossa, commonly known as the cycloglossa iris, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a perennial herb with striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and a rhizomatous root system. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
