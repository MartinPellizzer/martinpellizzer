Ilex aquifolium var. hybrida

Ilex aquifolium var. hybrida, commonly known as the British holly, is a hybrid species within the holly family, Ixaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny leaves that are typically dark green on the upper surface and lighter on the underside. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for birds during the winter months. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its evergreen foliage and seasonal berries.

This variety is native to the United Kingdom and is well adapted to temperate climates.
