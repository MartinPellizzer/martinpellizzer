Ixia purpurea

Ixia purpurea, commonly known as the purple fire lily, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from white to deep purple. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.

Ixia purpurea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
