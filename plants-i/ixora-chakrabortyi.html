Ixora chakrabortyi

Ixora chakrabortyi, commonly known as the Chakraborty's flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of bright orange-red flowers that bloom throughout the year, making it a striking ornamental plant. The leaves are simple, opposite, and have a glossy, dark green surface. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

This plant is often cultivated for its vibrant floral display and is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal.
