Ida × troyanoi

Ida × troyanoi, commonly known as the hybrid hibiscus, is a cross between *Hibiscus troyanensis* and *Hibiscus isabelianus*.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the tropical regions of Africa. The plant is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. Its foliage consists of broad, ovate leaves with a glossy surface.

Ida × troyanoi is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
