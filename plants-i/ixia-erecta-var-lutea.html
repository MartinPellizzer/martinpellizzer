Ixia erecta var. lutea

Ixia erecta var. lutea, commonly known as the yellow star lily, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers with a star-like shape. The plant produces tall, slender stems that bear clusters of tubular flowers, which open in a spiral pattern. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, growing in dense clumps at the base of the plant.

Ixia erecta var. lutea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
