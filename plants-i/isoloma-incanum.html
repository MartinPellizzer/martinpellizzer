Isoloma incanum

Isoloma incanum, commonly known as the silverleaf dandelion, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, silvery-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by fluffy, white seed heads. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Isoloma incanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
