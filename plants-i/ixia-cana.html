Ixia cana

Ixia cana, commonly known as the orange lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped orange flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and thrives in well-drained, sandy soils. It blooms in late winter to early spring, adding a vibrant splash of color to the landscape.
