Iris kaempferi

Iris kaempferi, commonly known as the Japanese iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It features striking blue to violet flowers with a prominent white or yellow beard in the center. The plant has sword-shaped, dark green leaves that are arranged in a fan-like pattern. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Iris kaempferi is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to bloom in early spring.
