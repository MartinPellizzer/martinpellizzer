Ischnosiphon orbiculatus

Ischnosiphon orbiculatus, commonly known as the globe-shaped sponge spore plant, is a unique member of the Lecanoraceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, spongy thallus that is typically yellow to orange in color. The plant produces small, orbicular spores that are released from specialized structures called soralia. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found on decaying wood or soil in temperate regions.

Its distinctive morphology and spore-producing structures make it an interesting subject for lichenological study.
