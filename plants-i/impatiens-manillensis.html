Impatiens manillensis

Impatiens manillensis, commonly known as the Manila impatiens, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and a slightly hairy underside. Its flowers are showy, with five petals that are usually pink or red in color, and they bloom in clusters.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
