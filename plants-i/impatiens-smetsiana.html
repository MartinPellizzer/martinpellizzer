Impatiens smetsiana

Impatiens smetsiana, commonly known as the Smets's impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of West Africa.

It is a member of the Balsaminaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, spurred flowers that typically range in color from pink to white. Due to its unique floral structure and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
