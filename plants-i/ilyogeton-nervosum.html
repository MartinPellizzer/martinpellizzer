Ilyogeton nervosum

Ilyogeton nervosum, commonly known as the slender leafed orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, slender leaves and small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pale pink. Native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. It has a fibrous root system and is often found growing in moist, well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
