Impatiens xanthinoides

Impatiens xanthinoides, commonly known as the golden impatiens, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its flowers are tubular and attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Impatiens xanthinoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.
