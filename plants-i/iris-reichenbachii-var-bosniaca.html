Iris reichenbachii var. bosniaca

Iris reichenbachii var. bosniaca, commonly known as the Bosnian iris, is a striking perennial herb native to the Balkan region.

It features tall, slender stems that bear showy, violet-blue flowers with a distinctive white center. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and resilience.
