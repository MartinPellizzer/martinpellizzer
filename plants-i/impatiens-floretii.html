Impatiens floretii

It belongs to the family Balsaminaceae and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is more commonly encountered in its natural habitat.
