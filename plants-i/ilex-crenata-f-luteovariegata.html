Ilex crenata f. luteovariegata

Ilex crenata f. luteovariegata, commonly known as the golden variegated holly, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It features small, glossy dark green leaves with striking yellow or cream variegation. The plant produces small, white flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage.

Its unique coloration makes it a popular choice for borders, rock gardens, and container planting.
