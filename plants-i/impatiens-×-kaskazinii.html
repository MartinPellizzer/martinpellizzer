Impatiens × kaskazinii

Impatiens × kaskazinii, commonly known as the "butterfly impatiens," is a hybrid plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, red, and white. The plant typically grows as an annual, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters, and thrives in moist, well-drained soil. Its foliage is heart-shaped with a glossy texture, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and containers.

Impatiens × kaskazinii is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as butterflies and bees.
