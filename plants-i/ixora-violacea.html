Ixora violacea

Ixora violacea, commonly known as the purple flame or purple ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, pink to purple flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and elliptical with a smooth surface.

Ixora violacea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a hedge plant.
