Ipomoea maingayi

Ipomoea maingayi, commonly known as the Maingay's morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a climbing vine that produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of pink to purple. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is often found in moist, shaded environments. It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family, which includes many other species of morning glories.
