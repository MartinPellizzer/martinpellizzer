Imbralyx leucanthus var. buteoides

Imbralyx leucanthus var. buteoides, commonly known as the silverleaf moonflower, is a rare and striking plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with silvery-gray foliage that reflects moonlight, giving it its common name.
