Impatiens chumphonensis

Impatiens chumphonensis, commonly known as Chumphon Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to Thailand.

It belongs to the Balsaminaceae family and is characterized by its striking, vibrant flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with slender stems and heart-shaped leaves. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking colorful, low-maintenance plants.
