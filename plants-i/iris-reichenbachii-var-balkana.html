Iris reichenbachii var. balkana

Iris reichenbachii var. balkana, commonly known as the Balkan iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It features upright, grass-like leaves and produces showy, violet-blue flowers with a distinctive three-parted structure. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or meadow habitats. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking blooms.

The species is also noted for its adaptability to a range of environmental conditions.
