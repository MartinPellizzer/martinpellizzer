Isanthera discolor var. austrokiushiuensis

Isanthera discolor var. austrokiushiuensis, commonly known as the Japanese white-flowered isanthera, is a rare variant of the isanthera genus native to Japan.

It is characterized by its pale white flowers and distinctive leaf morphology. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in mountainous regions. Its botanical features include lance-shaped leaves and a compact growth habit.
