Impatiens platypetala

Impatiens platypetala, commonly known as the flat-petaled impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

Impatiens platypetala is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
