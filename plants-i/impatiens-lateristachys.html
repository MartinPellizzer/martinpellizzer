Impatiens lateristachys

Impatiens lateristachys, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or white. The leaves are usually ovate to elliptic in shape, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface depending on the variety. This species thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in warm climates.
