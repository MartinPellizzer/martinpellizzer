Iberis repens

Iberis repens, commonly known as winter sweet, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features dense clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has succulent, mat-forming leaves that are covered in fine hairs, aiding in moisture retention. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
