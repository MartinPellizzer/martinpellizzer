Isoetes rimbachiana

Isoetes rimbachiana, commonly known as the Rimbach's watermoss, is a species of aquatic plant belonging to the genus Isoetes.

It is characterized by its spore-bearing structures called sporangia, which are typically found on specialized leaves called sporophylls. This plant is typically found in freshwater environments such as ponds and lakes, where it thrives in still or slow-moving water. Isoetes rimbachiana is notable for its morphological adaptations that allow it to survive in submerged conditions.
