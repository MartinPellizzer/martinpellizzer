Impatiens nepalensis

Impatiens nepalensis, commonly known as the Nepal balsam, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, colorful flowers that bloom in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous areas. Its leaves are ovate with a smooth texture, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Impatiens nepalensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
