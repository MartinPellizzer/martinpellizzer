Iseilema Laxum

Iseilema laxum is a unique and lesser-known flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia. This species belongs to the family Araceae, which includes many familiar plants like philodendrons and peace lilies. Its delicate, waxy leaves and inconspicuous flowers make it a subtle addition to botanical gardens and greenhouses.
