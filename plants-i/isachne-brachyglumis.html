Isachne brachyglumis

Isachne brachyglumis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved isachne, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It typically grows in moist or wet environments such as marshes, swamps, and along riverbanks. The plant has slender, erect stems that are often branched, and its leaves are narrow and grass-like with a smooth surface. Its small, spike-like inflorescence contains numerous tiny flowers, which are wind-pollinated.

Isachne brachyglumis is valued for its ability to stabilize soil and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
