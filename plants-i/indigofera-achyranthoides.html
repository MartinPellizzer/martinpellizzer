Indigofera achyranthoides

Indigofera achyranthoides, commonly known as Indian blue bean, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Asia, including India, and is characterized by its erect, branching stems and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to purple, and it bears pods containing edible seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for various health purposes.
