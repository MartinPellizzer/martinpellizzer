Impatiens madapurae

Impatiens madapurae, commonly known as the Madapura impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Balsaminaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
