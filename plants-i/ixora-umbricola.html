Ixora umbricola

Ixora umbricola, commonly known as the shade ixora, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, bright orange-red flowers. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth. Its leaves are typically leathery and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant blooms.

Ixora umbricola is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with partial shade.
