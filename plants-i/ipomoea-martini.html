Ipomoea martini

Ipomoea martini, commonly known as the Martin's morning glory, is a flowering vine native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its rapid growth and heart-shaped, palmate leaves. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Ipomoea martini is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
