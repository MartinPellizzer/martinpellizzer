Ischnolepis natalensis

Ischnolepis natalensis, commonly known as Natal's ischnolepis, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
