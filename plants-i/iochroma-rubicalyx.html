Iochroma rubicalyx

Iochroma rubicalyx, commonly known as the scarlet bellflower, is a species native to South America, particularly found in countries like Peru and Ecuador.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height, characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers. The plant produces clusters of bright red or orange flowers that bloom in the spring and summer months. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a velvety texture.

Iochroma rubicalyx is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
