Ixora fulgens

Ixora fulgens, commonly known as the flame ixora, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, bright orange-red flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has opposite, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Ixora fulgens is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
