Ipheion felipponei

Ipheion felipponei, commonly known as the Florida starflower, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the southeastern United States.

It features small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale blue, blooming in early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in well-drained, sandy soils. It is often found in open woodlands and pine flatwoods.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for native plant gardens.
