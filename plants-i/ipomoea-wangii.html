Ipomoea wangii

Ipomoea wangii, commonly known as the Chinese morning glory, is a flowering vine native to China.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep purple. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces showy blooms that open in the morning and close at night. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Ipomoea wangii thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
