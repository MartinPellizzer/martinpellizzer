Ixora pendula var. pendula

Ixora pendula var. pendula, commonly known as the hanging ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its drooping clusters of bright orange-red flowers, which bloom throughout the year in warm climates. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a slightly glossy surface. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and as a houseplant.
