Iris korolkowii var. violacea

Iris korolkowii var. violacea, commonly known as the violet flag iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It features upright, slender stems that bear striking violet-blue flowers with a distinctive white or pale yellow throat. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and its ability to bloom reliably in suitable climates.
