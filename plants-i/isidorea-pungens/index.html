Isidorea Pungens

Isidorea pungens is a striking member of the Rubiaceae family known for its vibrant, often contrasting foliage. Native to tropical regions of South America, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are typically glossy and deeply veined, displaying a range of colors from deep green to bright red. The plant is also called the "Red-leaved Bitterleaf" due to its strong, peppery scent. Despite its beauty, it is often cultivated for its ornamental value rather than its edibility.
