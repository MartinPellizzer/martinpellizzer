Iodes reticulata

Iodes reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, net-like venation on its leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pale pink.

Iodes reticulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
