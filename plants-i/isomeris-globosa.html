Isomeris globosa

Isomeris globosa, commonly known as the globular isomeris, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its compact, spherical growth habit and silvery-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Isomeris globosa thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a valuable addition to native plant collections.
