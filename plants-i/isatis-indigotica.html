Isatis indigotica

Isatis indigotica, commonly known as Chinese woad, is a biennial plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant is known for its deep blue pigments, which are extracted from its leaves to produce indigo dye. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in temperate regions.

Isatis indigotica has also been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
