Impatiens sacculifera

Impatiens sacculifera, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is a popular ornamental plant due to its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms.

Impatiens sacculifera is often cultivated as a houseplant or in greenhouse settings.
