Impatiens brandisii

Impatiens brandisii, commonly known as the purple impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple to pink flowers, which bloom in clusters at the ends of its stems. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens brandisii is a popular ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and adaptability to various growing conditions.
