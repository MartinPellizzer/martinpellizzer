Impatiens aliciae var. aliciae

Impatiens aliciae var. aliciae, commonly known as the Alice's Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red, with a distinctive spurred petal. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden beds.

Its ornamental value has led to its cultivation in various horticultural settings.
