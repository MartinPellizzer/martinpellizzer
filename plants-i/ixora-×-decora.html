Ixora × decora

Ixora × decora, commonly known as the Chinese hibiscus, is a hybrid shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange to red flowers, which are borne in clusters and bloom throughout the year. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Ixora × decora is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.
