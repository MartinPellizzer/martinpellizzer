Isika lucida

Isika lucida, commonly known as the shining cactus, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It features a tall, spiny stem that can grow up to several meters in height, with dense clusters of small, waxy leaves. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the dry season, which attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. Its thick, fleshy stems store water, allowing it to survive in harsh, drought-prone environments.

Isika lucida is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its ability to thrive in low-maintenance conditions.
