Ixora elisae

Ixora elisae, commonly known as the Christmas hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the winter months. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.

Ixora elisae prefers warm, humid climates and well-drained soil for optimal growth.
