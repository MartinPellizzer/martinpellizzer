Isodon tenuifolius

Isodon tenuifolius, commonly known as narrow-leaved isodon, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features slender, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests or rocky areas.

The species is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
