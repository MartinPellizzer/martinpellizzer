Indigofera carlesii

Indigofera carlesii, commonly known as the purple wisteria, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in pendulous clusters. The plant has trifoliate leaves, each composed of three oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing as a climbing vine.

Its botanical features make it a popular ornamental plant in gardens and landscapes.
