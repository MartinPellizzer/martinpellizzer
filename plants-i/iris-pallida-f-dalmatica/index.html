Iris Pallida F. Dalmatica

Iris pallida f. dalmatica is a rare and striking variant of the pale iris known for its delicate, pale blue to white flowers. This cultivar is native to the Dalmatian region of Croatia and is often found in moist, shaded environments near streams or in woodland areas. Its unique coloration and elegant form make it a prized addition to gardens and botanical collections. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full shade, requiring careful attention to maintain its health. Due to its rarity and beauty, it is increasingly sought after by horticulturists and plant enthusiasts alike.
