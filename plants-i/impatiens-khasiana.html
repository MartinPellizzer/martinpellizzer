Impatiens khasiana

Impatiens khasiana, commonly known as the Khasi impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the northeastern regions of India.

It is a herbaceous species characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and its stems are often covered in fine pubescence. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens khasiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
