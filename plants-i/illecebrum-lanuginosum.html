Illecebrum lanuginosum

Illecebrum lanuginosum, commonly known as the silky nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features hairy, glandular stems and leaves that are often covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically purple or blue in color, blooming in the summer months. Its fruit is a dry, capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds.

Illecebrum lanuginosum is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in dry, rocky habitats.
