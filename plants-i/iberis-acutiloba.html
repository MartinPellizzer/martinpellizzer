Iberis acutiloba

Iberis acutiloba, commonly known as the sharp-leaved candytuft, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features dense, evergreen foliage with finely cut, gray-green leaves. Its flowers are small, white or pale pink, and are arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
