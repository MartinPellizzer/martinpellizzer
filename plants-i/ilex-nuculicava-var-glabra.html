Ilex nuculicava var. glabra

Ilex nuculicava var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth evergreen holly, is a shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and leathery to the touch. The plant produces small, red berries that are typically eaten by birds, aiding in seed dispersal. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
