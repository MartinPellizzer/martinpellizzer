Ilex domestica var. glabra

Ilex domestica var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth-leaved holly, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, glossy dark green leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, white flowers in the spring, followed by red berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often used in ornamental landscaping.
