Indigofera subulifera var. polysperma

Indigofera subulifera var. polysperma, commonly known as the African indigo or African blue bean, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its small, needle-like leaves and clusters of bright yellow flowers. The plant produces elongated, bean-like pods that contain several seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of climatic conditions.

Botanically, it is notable for its use in traditional medicine and as a source of natural dyes.
