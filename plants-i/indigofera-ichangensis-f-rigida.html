Indigofera ichangensis f. rigida

Indigofera ichangensis f. rigida, commonly known as the rigid indigofera, is a shrub native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its sturdy, upright growth habit. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.

This variety is also noted for its potential in ecological restoration projects due to its nitrogen-fixing capabilities.
