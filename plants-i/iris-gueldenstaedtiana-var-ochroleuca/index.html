Iris Gueldenstaedtiana Var. Ochroleuca

Iris gueldenstaedtiana var. ochroleuca is a rare and beautiful iris species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It is known for its striking white and pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. This variety is highly sought after by botanists and gardeners due to its unique coloration and delicate appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a challenging but rewarding plant to cultivate. Its conservation status highlights the importance of preserving such rare botanical treasures.
