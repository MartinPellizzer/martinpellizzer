Iberis tenuifolia

Iberis tenuifolia, commonly known as slender candytuft, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features dense clusters of small, white, four-petaled flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has aromatic, hairy leaves that are typically gray-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact size and ornamental flowers make it a valuable addition to both garden and horticultural settings.
