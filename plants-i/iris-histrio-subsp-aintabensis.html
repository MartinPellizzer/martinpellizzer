Iris histrio subsp. aintabensis

Iris histrio subsp.

aintabensis, commonly known as the Aintab iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Turkey. It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky and dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid environments. Its leaves are grass-like, and the flowers bloom in early summer.

This subspecies is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and limited distribution.
