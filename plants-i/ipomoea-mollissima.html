Ipomoea mollissima

Ipomoea mollissima, commonly known as silky morning glory, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to violet. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, silky hair, giving it its common name. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in moist, shaded areas.

Ipomoea mollissima is a climbing vine that can grow quite long, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
