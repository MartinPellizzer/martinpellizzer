Isachne pallens

Isachne pallens, commonly known as the silvergrass, is a perennial grass species native to Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage that gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks. Its inflorescence consists of a dense, spike-like arrangement of small, two-lobed spikelets.

Isachne pallens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
