Ipomoea estrellensis

Ipomoea estrellensis, commonly known as the Estrellensis morning glory, is a flowering vine native to Mexico.

It produces striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep purple, often appearing in the early morning. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and is known for its rapid growth habit. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Ipomoea estrellensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
