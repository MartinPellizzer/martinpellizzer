Impatiens talbotii

Impatiens talbotii, commonly known as the Talbot's impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a slightly waxy texture and a pleasant, spicy fragrance. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens talbotii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
