Ixora Leucoxylon

Ixora leucoxylon is a striking evergreen shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia. Known for its vibrant clusters of orange-red flowers, it adds a bold splash of color to gardens and landscapes. This plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It features glossy dark green leaves that provide a striking contrast to its bright blooms. Despite its beauty, it requires well-drained soil and protection from frost to flourish.
