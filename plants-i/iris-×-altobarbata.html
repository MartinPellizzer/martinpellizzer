Iris × altobarbata

Iris × altobarbata, commonly known as the hybrid bearded iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in late spring or early summer. The plant features upright, sword-shaped leaves and produces tall, branched stems that support its vibrant, often purple or blue-colored blossoms. Iris × altobarbata is native to the eastern United States and is widely cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardeners.
