Iris kirkwoodiae var. macrotepala

Iris kirkwoodiae var. macrotepala, commonly known as the Kirkwood's iris, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, upright stems that bear showy, violet-blue flowers with a distinctive white center. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are dark green and slightly hairy. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its unique floral structure and vibrant color make it a valuable addition to botanical collections.
