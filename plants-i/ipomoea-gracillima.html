Ipomoea gracillima

Ipomoea gracillima, commonly known as the slender morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant produces small, round, green fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

Ipomoea gracillima is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
