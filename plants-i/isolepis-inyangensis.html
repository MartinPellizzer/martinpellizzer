Isolepis inyangensis

Isolepis inyangensis, commonly known as the Inyangensis tussock grass, is a species of grass found in wetland and marshy environments.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny florets. It plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wetland organisms.

Isolepis inyangensis is typically found in tropical and subtropical regions, particularly in Africa.
