Ilex glabra

Ilex glabra, commonly known as the smoothleaf holly, is a deciduous evergreen shrub or small tree native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green, oval-shaped leaves that are smooth on both sides. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full shade.

Ilex glabra is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardiness.
