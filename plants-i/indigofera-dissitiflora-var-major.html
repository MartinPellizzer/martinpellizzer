Indigofera dissitiflora var. major

Indigofera dissitiflora var. major, commonly known as the large-flowered indigo, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree characterized by its compound leaves and showy, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces pods that contain small, round seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.
