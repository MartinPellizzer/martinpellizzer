Ida barrowiorum

Ida barrowiorum, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a rare and striking plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its unique, silver-gray foliage and delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale purple. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot. Its leaves are covered in a fine, waxy coating that helps reduce water loss in its arid habitat.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Ida barrowiorum is considered a vulnerable species.
