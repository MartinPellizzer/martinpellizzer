Ixora floribunda

Ixora floribunda, commonly known as the flame bush, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of showy, red or orange-red flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Ixora floribunda is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
