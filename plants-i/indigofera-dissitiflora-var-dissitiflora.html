Indigofera dissitiflora var. dissitiflora

Indigofera dissitiflora var. dissitiflora, commonly known as the slender indigo, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and racemose inflorescences bearing small, violet to blue flowers. The plant produces pods that contain lens-shaped seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or shrubland habitats.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and ethnobotanical research.
