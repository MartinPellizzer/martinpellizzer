Impatiens mearnsii

Impatiens mearnsii, commonly known as the Mexican impatiens, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens mearnsii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
