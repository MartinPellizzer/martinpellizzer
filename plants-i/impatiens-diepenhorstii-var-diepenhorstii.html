Impatiens diepenhorstii var. diepenhorstii

Impatiens diepenhorstii var. diepenhorstii, commonly known as Diepenhorst's impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of western Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are arranged oppositely along its stems. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in tropical gardens.
