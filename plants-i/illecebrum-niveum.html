Illecebrum niveum

Illecebrum niveum, commonly known as the snow vine, is a perennial climbing plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its pale green to white stems and leaves, which are often covered in a fine, silky hair. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are typically opposite and have a heart-shaped or ovate form.

Illecebrum niveum is adapted to cold climates and thrives in well-drained, rocky soils.
