Isachne globosa var. daviumbuensis

Isachne globosa var. daviumbuensis, commonly known as the African bulbous grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bulbous base and narrow, grass-like leaves that grow from the base. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in dense clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky outcrops.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ornamental species within the Poaceae family.
