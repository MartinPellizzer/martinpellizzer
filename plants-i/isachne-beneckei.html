Isachne beneckei

Isachne beneckei, commonly known as the narrow-leafed isachne, is a grass species native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks. The plant has slender, grass-like leaves that are usually less than 10 centimeters in length. Its inflorescence consists of spikelets that are arranged in a loose panicle.

Isachne beneckei is characterized by its small, inconspicuous flowers and is often found in association with other shade-tolerant flora.
