Ixora densiflora

Ixora densiflora, commonly known as the denseflower ixora, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, bright red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Ixora densiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a hedge plant.
