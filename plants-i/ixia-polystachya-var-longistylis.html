Ixia polystachya var. longistylis

Ixia polystachya var. longistylis, commonly known as the long-styled ixia, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its bold, eye-catching blooms.
