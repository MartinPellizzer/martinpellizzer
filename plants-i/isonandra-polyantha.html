Isonandra polyantha

Isonandra polyantha, commonly known as the clustered snowbell, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, bell-shaped white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It prefers moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Isonandra polyantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
