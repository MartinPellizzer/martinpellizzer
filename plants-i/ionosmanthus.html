Ionosmanthus

Ionosmanthus, commonly known as the Chinese beautyberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its striking clusters of violet-blue berries that appear in the fall. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers in the spring.

Ionosmanthus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil.
