Iris Setosa Subsp. Interior

The iris setosa subsp. interior is a rare and delicate wildflower native to the western united states. it thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks. this subspecies is distinguished by its vibrant blue to violet petals and narrow, grass-like leaves. due to habitat loss and overharvesting, it faces significant conservation challenges. protecting this plant is crucial for maintaining biodiversity in its native ecosystems.
