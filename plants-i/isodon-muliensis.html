Isodon muliensis

Isodon muliensis, commonly known as the Yunnan skullcap, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in mountainous regions and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

The leaves and flowers are used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.
