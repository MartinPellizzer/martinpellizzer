Ipomoea morongii

Ipomoea morongii, commonly known as the Morong's morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is a member of the Convolvulaceae family, characterized by its climbing habit and trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant produces vibrant purple to blue flowers that bloom in the morning, hence its common name. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often have a velvety texture.

Ipomoea morongii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
