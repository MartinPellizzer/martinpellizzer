Ixora ixoroides

Ixora ixoroides, commonly known as the flame flower or red flame plant, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, showy clusters of bright red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Ixora ixoroides is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
