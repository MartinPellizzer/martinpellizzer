Ilex aquifolium f. pyramidalis

Ilex aquifolium f. pyramidalis, commonly known as the Pyramidal Holly, is a cultivar of the common holly, characterized by its dense, upright growth habit.

It features dark green, leathery leaves with spiny margins, typical of the genus Ilex. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for birds. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and evergreen foliage.

This form is particularly valued for its compact shape and suitability in formal garden settings.
