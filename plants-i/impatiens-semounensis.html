Impatiens semounensis

Impatiens semounensis, commonly known as the Semoun's impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Balsaminaceae and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white. Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though its natural habitat remains primarily in the wild.
