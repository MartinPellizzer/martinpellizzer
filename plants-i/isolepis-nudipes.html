Isolepis nudipes

Isolepis nudipes, commonly known as the naked sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetland and marshy environments.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related grass-like plants. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikelets that are typically wind-pollinated. It thrives in moist soils and is often found in temperate regions.
