Isodon rugosiformis

Isodon rugosiformis, commonly known as the rough-leaved isodon, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its rugged, serrated leaves and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to purple. It often grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
