Ilex fargesii var. angustifolia

Ilex fargesii var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Chinese holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries that are an important food source for birds. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it suitable for shaded garden landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits, it is often cultivated in horticultural settings.
