Ilex ciliospinosa

Ilex ciliospinosa, commonly known as the spiny-leaf holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often covered with fine, silky hairs, giving them a distinctive silvery appearance. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for various birds and wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Ilex ciliospinosa is also valued for its ability to tolerate urban environments and its year-round aesthetic appeal.
