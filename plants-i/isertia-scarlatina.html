Isertia scarlatina

Isertia scarlatina, commonly known as the scarlet isertia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers, which bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is a perennial herb that thrives in full sun and well-drained conditions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
