Ipomoea kituiensis var. kituiensis

Ipomoea kituiensis var. kituiensis, commonly known as the Kitui morning glory, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and striking blue to violet flowers that open in the morning. The plant is a climbing vine with tendrils that allow it to ascend supports or other structures. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in woodland or disturbed habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and botanical collections.
