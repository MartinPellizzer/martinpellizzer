Iridorkis maxima

Iridorkis maxima, commonly known as the giant orchid, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its large, showy flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to certain regions of South America. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and produces blossoms in shades of white, pink, and purple. Iridorkis maxima thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
