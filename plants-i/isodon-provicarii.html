Isodon provicarii

Isodon provicarii, commonly known as the African germander, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils. Its small, tubular flowers are usually blue or purple, and it produces small, oblong fruits.

Isodon provicarii is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
