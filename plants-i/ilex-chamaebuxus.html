Ilex chamaebuxus

Ilex chamaebuxus, commonly known as the common holly, is a dense, evergreen shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with spiny margins, and small, red berries that persist through winter. The plant has a compact, rounded growth habit and can reach heights of up to 10 feet. It thrives in partial to full shade and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Ilex chamaebuxus is also valued for its ability to provide habitat and food for wildlife.
