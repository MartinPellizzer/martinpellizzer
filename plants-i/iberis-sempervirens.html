Iberis sempervirens

Iberis sempervirens, commonly known as evergreen candytuft, is a low-growing perennial shrub native to southern Europe.

It features dense clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves that remain evergreen throughout the year. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact size and ornamental flowers make it a valuable addition to ornamental horticulture.
