Iris splendens

Iris splendens, commonly known as the splendid iris, is a striking perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems that bear showy, spurred flowers. The flowers are typically blue or violet in color, with three upright petals and three downward-curled sepals. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also an important species for wetland restoration projects.
