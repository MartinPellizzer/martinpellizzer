Isodon cavaleriei

Isodon cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese skullcap, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It is often found in rocky or mountainous regions, preferring well-drained soils.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional Chinese medicine.
