Ixeris chinensis

Ixeris chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese groundsel, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant features small, yellow composite flowers that are surrounded by pale green to white ray florets. Its leaves are typically linear to lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Ixeris chinensis is often used in traditional Chinese medicine and is valued for its ornamental qualities in garden settings.
