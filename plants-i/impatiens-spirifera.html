Impatiens spirifera

Impatiens spirifera, commonly known as the spiral impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually hairy and have a distinct veining pattern. Its flowers are borne in clusters and are known for their unique spiral arrangement of petals.

Impatiens spirifera is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
