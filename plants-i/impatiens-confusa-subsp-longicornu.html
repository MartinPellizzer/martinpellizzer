Impatiens confusa subsp. longicornu

Impatiens confusa subsp.

longicornu, commonly known as the long-horned impatiens, is a species native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white.

The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique floral structure and ornamental value make it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation.
