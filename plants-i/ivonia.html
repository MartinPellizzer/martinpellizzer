Ivonia

Ivonia, commonly known as the ghost plant, is a rare and elusive flowering plant native to the wetlands of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, translucent leaves that appear to shimmer in the sunlight, giving it its ethereal appearance. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom during the rainy season, often in dense clusters. Ivonia thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found near stagnant water bodies.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly valued in botanical research and conservation efforts.
