Illigera megaptera

Illigera megaptera, commonly known as the giant winged fern, is a unique member of the Polypodiaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, elongated fronds that can reach up to several meters in length. The fronds are often divided into segments with distinctive, broad, wing-like lobes. This plant is typically found in tropical and subtropical regions, often growing in shaded, moist environments.

Its botanical features make it a striking and recognizable species within its ecological niche.
