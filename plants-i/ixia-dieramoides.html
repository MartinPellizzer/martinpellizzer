Ixia dieramoides

Ixia dieramoides, commonly known as the yellow star orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating.

Ixia dieramoides is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.
