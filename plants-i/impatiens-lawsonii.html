Impatiens lawsonii

Impatiens lawsonii, commonly known as the Himalayan Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Impatiens lawsonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
