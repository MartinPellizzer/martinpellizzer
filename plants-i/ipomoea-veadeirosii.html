Ipomoea veadeirosii

Ipomoea veadeirosii, commonly known as the Veadeirosii morning glory, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from violet to deep purple. Its flowers bloom in the morning, hence the common name, and are known for their ornamental value.

Ipomoea veadeirosii is often cultivated for its beauty and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
