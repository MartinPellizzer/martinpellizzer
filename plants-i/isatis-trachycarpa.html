Isatis trachycarpa

Isatis trachycarpa, commonly known as the rough-seeded woad, is a flowering plant native to central and eastern Europe.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cabbage and mustard. The plant is characterized by its rough, hairy stems and leaves with a distinctive, serrated appearance. Its small, yellow flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom in the summer months.

Isatis trachycarpa is often found in dry, disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
