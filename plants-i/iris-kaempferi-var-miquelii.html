Iris kaempferi var. miquelii

Iris kaempferi var. miquelii, commonly known as the Miquel's iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern China.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems that bear striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sun. Its foliage consists of narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are often a vibrant green.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and resilience.
