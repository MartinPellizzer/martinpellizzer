Impatiens conchibracteata

Impatiens conchibracteata, commonly known as the horned impatiens, is a species of flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its distinctive, showy flowers that are typically red or pink in color. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The unique bracts that resemble horns are a notable feature of this species.
