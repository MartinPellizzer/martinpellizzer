Ixia reticulata

Ixia reticulata, commonly known as the netted ixia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that come in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of tubular flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Ixia reticulata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.
