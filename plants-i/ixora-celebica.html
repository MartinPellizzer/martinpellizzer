Ixora celebica

Ixora celebica, commonly known as the Java flame or flame ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height, characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of bright red or orange flowers, which are highly attractive to pollinators like bees and birds. Ixora celebica thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sun exposure.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping in warm regions.
