Iozoste lanata

Iozoste lanata, commonly known as the woolly iozoste, is a perennial herb native to the high-altitude regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, white, woolly foliage that covers its stems and leaves, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens for its ornamental value.
