Isachne guangxiensis

Isachne guangxiensis, commonly known as Guangxi isachne, is a species of grass native to southern China.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit. The plant typically features narrow leaves with a smooth surface and produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species within its native region.
