Ilex kwangtungensis

Ilex kwangtungensis, commonly known as the Guangdong holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to southern China.

It belongs to the family Aquifoliaceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in the spring, which are followed by bright red berries in the fall.
