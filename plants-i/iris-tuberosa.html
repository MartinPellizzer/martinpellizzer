Iris tuberosa

Iris tuberosa, commonly known as the Turkish iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has sword-shaped, grass-like leaves that grow from a rhizomatous root system. Its flowers are typically borne on tall, slender stems and are often used in floral arrangements.

Iris tuberosa is valued for both its ornamental beauty and its historical use in traditional medicine.
