Impatiens fulva f. albiflora

Impatiens fulva f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered touch-me-not, is a variant of the common impatiens species.

It is characterized by its striking white flowers, which contrast with its deep green foliage. The plant is native to tropical regions of Africa and thrives in humid, shaded environments. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that release seeds when disturbed.

This cultivar is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
