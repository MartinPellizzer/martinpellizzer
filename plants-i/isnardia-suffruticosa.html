Isnardia suffruticosa

Isnardia suffruticosa, commonly known as the purple-flowered rock rose, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features woody stems and small, evergreen leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces clusters of showy, violet or purple flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its hardiness and ornamental value, it is a popular choice among gardeners in temperate climates.
