Ixora eludens

Ixora eludens, commonly known as the golden flame or red flame ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters tall, featuring glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of bright red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year, making it a popular ornamental species. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Ixora eludens is often used in landscaping for its vibrant floral display and adaptability to various growing conditions.
