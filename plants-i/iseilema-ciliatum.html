Iseilema ciliatum

Iseilema ciliatum, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a distinctive waxy coating. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in disturbed soils.

Iseilema ciliatum is known for its rapid growth and is sometimes considered an invasive species in certain regions.
