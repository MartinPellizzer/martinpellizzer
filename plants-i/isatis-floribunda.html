Isatis floribunda

Isatis floribunda, commonly known as Chinese yellow flower or yellow flag, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cabbage and mustard. The plant is characterized by its yellow, butterfly-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in a fine layer of white hair.

Isatis floribunda is often found in moist soils near water bodies and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
