Ilex soderstromii var. soderstromii

Ilex soderstromii var. soderstromii, commonly known as the Soderstrom's holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, white flowers in the spring, which are followed by red, fleshy berries that are an important food source for wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight, making it a hardy and adaptable plant for various garden settings.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular choice for landscaping in warmer climates.
