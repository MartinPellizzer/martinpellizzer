Ilex Vietnamensis

Ilex vietnamensis is a rare evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of Vietnam. It belongs to the family Aquifoliaceae and is known for its glossy dark green leaves and small, bright red berries. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in dense forests. Due to its limited distribution and habitat destruction, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique species for future generations.
