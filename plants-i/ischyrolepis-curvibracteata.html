Ischyrolepis curvibracteata

Ischyrolepis curvibracteata, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, spirally twisted floral bracts. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are small and fragrant, often displaying a range of colors from white to pale yellow.

Ischyrolepis curvibracteata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its distinctive appearance.
