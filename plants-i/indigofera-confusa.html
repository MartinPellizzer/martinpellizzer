Indigofera confusa

Indigofera confusa, commonly known as the purple indigo or small-leaved indigo, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small, pea-like flowers. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, and it is often found in disturbed soils and open woodland areas.
