Ixora nigricans var. ovalis

Ixora nigricans var. ovalis, commonly known as the oval-leaf flame bush, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded shrub form and produces clusters of bright red or orange flowers throughout the year. The leaves are oval-shaped, glossy, and arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sun exposure.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a hedge due to its vibrant blooms and hardy nature.
