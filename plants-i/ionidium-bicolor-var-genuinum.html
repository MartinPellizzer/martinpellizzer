Ionidium bicolor var. genuinum

Ionidium bicolor var. genuinum, commonly known as the pink and white orchid, is a rare and striking species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique inflorescence, which features a mix of pink and white flowers arranged in a spiral pattern. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its delicate, fragrant blooms that bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its rarity and aesthetic appeal, it is often cultivated in specialized botanical gardens and by experienced growers.
