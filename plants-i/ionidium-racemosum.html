Ionidium racemosum

Ionidium racemosum, commonly known as the racemose ionidium, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Iridaceae.

It is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences that consist of multiple flowers arranged in a raceme. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in temperate climates. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers come in a range of colors, including blue, purple, and white.

Ionidium racemosum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
