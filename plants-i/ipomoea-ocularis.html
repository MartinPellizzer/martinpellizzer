Ipomoea ocularis

Ipomoea ocularis, commonly known as the eye of the bat, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central and South America.

It is a member of the Convolvulaceae family, characterized by its trailing vines and showy flowers. Ipomoea ocularis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and tropical landscapes.
