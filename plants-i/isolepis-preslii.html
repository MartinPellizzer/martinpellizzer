Isolepis preslii

Isolepis preslii, commonly known as the Presle's spike-rush, is a perennial grass-like plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and narrow, cylindrical stems that often form compact clumps. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are typically greenish or brownish in color. It is commonly found in wetland environments such as marshes, fens, and along the edges of ponds.

Isolepis preslii plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
