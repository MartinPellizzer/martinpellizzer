Illicium parvifolium subsp. oligandrum

Illicium parvifolium subsp.

oligandrum, commonly known as the small-leaved star anise, is a species native to China. It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its aromatic, star-shaped fruits that are used in traditional medicine and as a spice.

The plant features simple, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and a faintly fragrant scent. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and medicinal properties.
