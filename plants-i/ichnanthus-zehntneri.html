Ichnanthus zehntneri

Ichnanthus zehntneri, commonly known as the Zehntner's marsh orchid, is a herbaceous plant native to the wetlands of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, acidic soils and thrives in environments with high humidity. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the waterlogged substrate.

Ichnanthus zehntneri is often found in marshes and along the edges of lakes and rivers.
