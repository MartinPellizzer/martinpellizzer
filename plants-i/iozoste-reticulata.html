Iozoste reticulata

Iozoste reticulata, commonly known as the reticulated iozoste, is a unique flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its intricate, lace-like leaf patterns, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
