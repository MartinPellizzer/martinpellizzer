Ixora gracilis

Ixora gracilis, commonly known as the slender flame bush, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and its clusters of bright orange-red flowers. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are usually opposite in arrangement. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Ixora gracilis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
