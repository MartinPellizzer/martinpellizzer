Ixora nigricans var. genuina

Ixora nigricans var. genuina, commonly known as the native ixora, is a flowering shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of bright red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically orange to red in color. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sunlight.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a hedge plant.
