Impatiens sulcata var. minor

Impatiens sulcata var. minor, commonly known as the miniature impatiens, is a compact, trailing plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features small, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a slightly textured surface. The flowers are usually pink or white, with five petals and a distinctive spurred shape. It thrives in moist, well-draining soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets and container gardens.

This variety is prized for its delicate appearance and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
