Ilex scutiiformis var. genuina

Ilex scutiiformis var. genuina, commonly known as the Mexican evergreen holly, is a shrub or small tree native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Aquifoliaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with spiny margins. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for ornamental landscaping.

Its evergreen nature provides year-round visual interest in gardens.
