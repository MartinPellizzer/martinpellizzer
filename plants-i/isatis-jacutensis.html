Isatis jacutensis

Isatis jacutensis, commonly known as the Chinese yellow rocket, is a herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and lance-shaped leaves. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and traditional medicine.
