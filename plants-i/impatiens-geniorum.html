Impatiens geniorum

Impatiens geniorum, commonly known as the "Garden Impatiens," is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the family Violaceae and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white.
