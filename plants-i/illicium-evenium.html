Illicium evenium

Illicium evenium, commonly known as the Chinese star anise, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern China, Vietnam, and Indonesia.

It belongs to the family Schisandraceae and is characterized by its aromatic, star-shaped fruit that resembles a tiny star. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by the distinctive, fragrant seed pods. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly serrated margin.

Illicium evenium is valued for its essential oil, which is used in both culinary and medicinal applications.
