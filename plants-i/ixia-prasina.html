Ixia prasina

Ixia prasina, commonly known as the golden rain lily, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Ixia prasina is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant color and ease of care.
