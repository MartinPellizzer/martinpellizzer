Indigofera microscypha

Indigofera microscypha, commonly known as the small-leaved indigo, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and clusters of pink or purple flowers. The plant produces small, cylindrical pods that contain lens-shaped seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in semi-arid environments.
