Ipomoea balansae

Ipomoea balansae, commonly known as the Balansae morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its trumpet-shaped, vibrant blue to violet flowers that bloom in the morning. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family, which includes many other flowering vines.

Ipomoea balansae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
