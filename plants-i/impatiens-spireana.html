Impatiens spireana

Impatiens spireana, commonly known as the spiral impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red, with a distinctive spiral pattern on the petals. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, soft hairs. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found growing near streams or in forest understories.

Impatiens spireana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
