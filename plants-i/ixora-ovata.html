Ixora ovata

Ixora ovata, commonly known as the ovate ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of bright red or orange flowers, which are borne on short pedicels. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate in shape, with a smooth surface.

Ixora ovata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
