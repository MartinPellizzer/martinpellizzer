Ionopsis zonalis

Ionopsis zonalis, commonly known as the zonal morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its trumpet-shaped, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often grown as an ornamental plant.

Ionopsis zonalis is also known for its rapid growth and ability to climb or trail, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.
