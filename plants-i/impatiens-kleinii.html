Impatiens kleinii

Impatiens kleinii, commonly known as the dwarf impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows to a height of less than 30 centimeters, making it a popular choice for container gardening and rock gardens. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade, often blooming throughout the rainy season.

Impatiens kleinii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to add color to shaded garden spaces.
