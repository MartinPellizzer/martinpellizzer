Impatiens cardiophylla

Impatiens cardiophylla, commonly known as the heart-leaf impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
