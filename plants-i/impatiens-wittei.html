Impatiens wittei

Impatiens wittei, commonly known as the white impatiens, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is a member of the Balsaminaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pale pink. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves and grows as an annual or perennial depending on the climate. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking shade-tolerant ornamental plants.

Its rapid growth and ability to bloom throughout the growing season contribute to its appeal in horticultural settings.
