Ilex rimbachii

Ilex rimbachii, commonly known as the dwarf holly, is a compact evergreen shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features small, glossy, dark green leaves that are typically arranged in pairs along the stems. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its dense, upright growth habit makes it an excellent choice for hedges or container planting.
