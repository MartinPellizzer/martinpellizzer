Izabalaea excelsa

Izabalaea excelsa, commonly known as the giant orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 30 centimeters in diameter, often in shades of pink and white. The plant has a robust root system and thrives in humid, shaded environments with high rainfall. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, contributing to its lush appearance.

Due to its rarity and ecological significance, it is often protected in its natural habitat.
