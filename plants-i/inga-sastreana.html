Inga sastreana

Inga sastreana, commonly known as the purple-leaf inga, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Colombia and Ecuador. The plant is characterized by its striking purple leaves and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. Its fruits are legumes that contain edible seeds, making it a valuable resource in some local communities.

Inga sastreana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and potential use in agroforestry systems.
