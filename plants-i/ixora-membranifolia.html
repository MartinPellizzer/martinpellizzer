Ixora membranifolia

Ixora membranifolia, commonly known as the red flame ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant produces clusters of small, bright red flowers that bloom throughout the year, especially during the warmer months. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a leathery texture with a distinctive reddish-brown underside.

Ixora membranifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticulture for its vibrant floral display.
