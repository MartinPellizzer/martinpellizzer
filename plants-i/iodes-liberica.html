Iodes liberica

Iodes liberica, commonly known as Liberica coffee, is a species of flowering plant in the Rubiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, Southeast Asia, and the Pacific Islands. The plant produces small, white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries containing seeds. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a glossy surface.
