Isatis vermia

Isatis vermia, commonly known as yellow root, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cabbage and mustard. The plant is characterized by its yellow roots, which are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly bitter taste, while the flowers are small and typically white or pale yellow.

Isatis vermia thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland areas.
