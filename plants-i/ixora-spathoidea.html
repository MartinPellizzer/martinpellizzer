Ixora spathoidea

Ixora spathoidea, commonly known as the red torch flower, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows up to 3 meters tall, featuring dense, evergreen foliage. The plant is renowned for its clusters of bright red, tubular flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its flowers are borne on short, bract-like structures, giving it a striking appearance.

Ixora spathoidea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a hedge plant.
