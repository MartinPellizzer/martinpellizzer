Iberis linifolia f. senneniana

Iberis linifolia f. senneniana, commonly known as the Sennenian sweet rocket, is a cultivar of the flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It is a low-growing, herbaceous perennial that produces clusters of small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers. The plant features finely divided, silver-green foliage that adds ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
