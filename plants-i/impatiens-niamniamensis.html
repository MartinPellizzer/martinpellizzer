Impatiens niamniamensis

Impatiens niamniamensis, commonly known as the Niamniam impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of Cameroon.

It belongs to the Balsaminaceae family and is characterized by its showy, colorful flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and is often found growing in shaded, moist environments.
