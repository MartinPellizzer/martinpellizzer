Ixora javanica var. descripta

Ixora javanica var. descripta, commonly known as the flame flower or Chinese hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences of bright red or orange-red flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has simple, opposite, elliptical leaves with a glossy surface and a slightly serrated margin. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and as a houseplant.
