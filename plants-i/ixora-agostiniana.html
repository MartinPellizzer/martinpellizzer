Ixora agostiniana

Ixora agostiniana, commonly known as the red flame ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of bright red, orange, or yellow flowers, which are borne on long, branched inflorescences. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and elliptical in shape, with a smooth surface.

Ixora agostiniana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
