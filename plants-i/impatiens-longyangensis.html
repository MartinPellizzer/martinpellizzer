Impatiens longyangensis

Impatiens longyangensis, commonly known as the Longyang Impatiens, is a flowering plant species native to China.

It belongs to the family Balsaminaceae and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and a delicate, trailing growth habit, making it suitable for hanging baskets or garden borders. Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is occasionally cultivated in horticulture.
