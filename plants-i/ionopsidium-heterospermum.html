Ionopsidium heterospermum

Ionopsidium heterospermum, commonly known as the desert broom.

It is a flowering plant native to arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and gray-green, adapted to reduce water loss in hot, dry environments.

The species is characterized by its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions and its role in stabilizing sandy soils.
