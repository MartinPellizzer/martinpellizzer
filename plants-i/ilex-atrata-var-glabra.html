Ilex atrata var. glabra

Ilex atrata var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth evergreen holly, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree that typically grows to a height of 3 to 10 meters. The plant features glossy, dark green, elliptical leaves that are smooth and often have a waxy texture. Its small, white flowers appear in clusters and are followed by bright red berries that are edible for birds but toxic to humans.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in landscaping due to its dense foliage and year-round appearance.
