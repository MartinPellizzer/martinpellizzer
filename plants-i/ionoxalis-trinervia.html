Ionoxalis trinervia

Ionoxalis trinervia, commonly known as the slender wood sorrel, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It features trifoliate leaves with three heart-shaped leaflets, each adorned with fine veins. The plant produces small, delicate white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Ionoxalis trinervia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate foliage and flowers.
