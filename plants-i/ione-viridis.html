Ione viridis

Ione viridis, commonly known as the green hawksbeard, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single flower. The plant features bright yellow ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its drought-resistant nature.
