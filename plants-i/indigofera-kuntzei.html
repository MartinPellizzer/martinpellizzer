Indigofera kuntzei

Indigofera kuntzei, commonly known as blue bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant produces small, bean-like pods that contain edible seeds, which are often used in traditional cuisines. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three leaflets, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

Indigofera kuntzei is also valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
