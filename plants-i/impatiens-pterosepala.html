Impatiens pterosepala

Impatiens pterosepala, commonly known as the winged impatiens, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly wavy margin. This species thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.
