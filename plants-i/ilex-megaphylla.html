Ilex megaphylla

Ilex megaphylla, commonly known as the giant holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves that can grow up to 15 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for various bird species. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

Ilex megaphylla is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
