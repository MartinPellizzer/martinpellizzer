Isoetes panchananii

Isoetes panchananii, commonly known as the Panchanan's water fern, is a species of aquatic plant belonging to the genus Isoetes.

It is characterized by its spore-bearing leaves and is typically found in freshwater habitats such as ponds and marshes. The plant has a unique morphology with a central axis and specialized structures for spore dispersal. It is native to regions in Asia, including India, and is often associated with wetland ecosystems.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
