Iberis linifolia subsp. welwitschii

Iberis linifolia subsp.

welwitschii, commonly known as the African rock rose, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a low-growing, mat-forming shrub that produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers.

The plant has hirsute (hairy) foliage and is adapted to arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
