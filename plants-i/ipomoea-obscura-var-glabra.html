Ipomoea obscura var. glabra

Ipomoea obscura var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its smooth, hairless stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale purple, typically blooming in the morning. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
