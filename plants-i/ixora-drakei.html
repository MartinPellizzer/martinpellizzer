Ixora drakei

Ixora drakei, commonly known as the Drake's flame bush, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has small, narrow leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sun.

Ixora drakei is also valued for its ability to tolerate dry conditions once established.
