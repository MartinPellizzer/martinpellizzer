Ixora aneityensis

Ixora aneityensis, commonly known as the Java flame or flame of the forest, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bright red or orange flowers, which are clustered in dense inflorescences and bloom throughout the year. The plant has simple, opposite, and leathery leaves that are dark green in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Ixora aneityensis is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
