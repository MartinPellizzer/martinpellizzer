Ilex nuculicava f. brevipedicellata

Ilex nuculicava f. brevipedicellata, commonly known as the small-fruited holly, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are typically elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, red berries that are an attractive feature, though they are not typically consumed by humans. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Its compact growth habit and year-round foliage make it suitable for use in gardens and as a hedging plant.
