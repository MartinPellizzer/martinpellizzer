Impatiens muscicola

Impatiens muscicola, commonly known as the musk impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, spurred flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens muscicola is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
