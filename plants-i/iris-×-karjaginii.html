Iris × karjaginii

Iris × karjaginii, commonly known as the Karjagin iris, is a hybrid perennial plant native to the Caucasus region.

It features tall, slender stems that bear showy, violet-blue flowers with a distinctive dark center. The plant has narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

Iris × karjaginii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking blooms.
