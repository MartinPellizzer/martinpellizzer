Isachne fischeri

Isachne fischeri, commonly known as Fischer's isachne, is a perennial grass species native to certain regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant typically features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetland or marshy habitats.

Due to its limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
