Ixora banjoana

Ixora banjoana, commonly known as the red flame ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom in dense, branched clusters. The plant has simple, opposite, and elliptical-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Ixora banjoana is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
