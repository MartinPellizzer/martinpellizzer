Inga Tenuis

Inga tenuis is a small evergreen tree native to the tropical regions of Central and South America. It is known for its slender, drooping branches and delicate, heart-shaped leaves. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of rainforests. Its flowers are small and fragrant, attracting a variety of pollinators. Inga tenuis plays an important role in maintaining the ecological balance of its native habitats.
