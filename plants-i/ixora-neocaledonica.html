Ixora neocaledonica

Ixora neocaledonica, commonly known as the New Caledonia flame tree, is a shrub or small tree native to the island of New Caledonia in the South Pacific.

It is characterized by its bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a striking visual display. The plant has opposite, simple, and lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sun exposure.

Ixora neocaledonica is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a landscape plant.
