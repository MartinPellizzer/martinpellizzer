Impatiens crassicornu

Impatiens crassicornu, commonly known as the broad-horned impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a waxy surface, and its stems are often hairy. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens crassicornu is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
