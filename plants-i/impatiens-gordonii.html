Impatiens gordonii

Impatiens gordonii, commonly known as Gordon's impatiens, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens gordonii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
