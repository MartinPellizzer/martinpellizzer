Impatiens ciliifolia subsp. ciliifolia

Impatiens ciliifolia subsp.

ciliifolia, commonly known as the silky leaf impatiens, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa. It is characterized by its delicate, ovate leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving them a soft, velvety texture.

The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming during the wet season. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in warm climates.
