Ixora villosa var. chevalieri

Ixora villosa var. chevalieri, commonly known as the silky ixora, is a flowering shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and clusters of bright orange or red flowers. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a hedge plant.

The flowers are typically visited by birds and insects, contributing to its ecological importance.
