India arunachalensis

India arunachalensis, commonly known as the Arunachal bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the eastern Himalayas, specifically in the state of Arunachal Pradesh in India.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue or violet flowers that bloom in the summer months.
