Isatis megacarpa

Isatis megacarpa, commonly known as the giant woad, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other plants like cabbage and mustard. The plant is characterized by its large, lobed leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Its seeds are encased in a thick, fleshy capsule, which is a distinctive botanical feature.

Isatis megacarpa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and historical use in traditional medicine.
