Indigofera charlieriana

Indigofera charlieriana, commonly known as the blue bean or wild indigo, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height, characterized by its trifoliate leaves and distinctive blue or violet flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

It is often found in moist, shaded areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional uses.
