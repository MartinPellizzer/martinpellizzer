Illus

Illus, commonly known as the common illus, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which consist of three oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to purple. It is often found in temperate regions and thrives in well-drained soils.

The seeds are enclosed in pods and are an important food source for various wildlife species.
