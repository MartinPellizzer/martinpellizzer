Isatis takhtajanii

Isatis takhtajanii, commonly known as the Takhtajan woad, is a species of flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cabbage and mustard. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, yellow flowers. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in a fine layer of hair.

Isatis takhtajanii is primarily valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in ecological habitats.
