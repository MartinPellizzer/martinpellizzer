Indigofera ornithopodioides

Indigofera ornithopodioides, commonly known as the silverleaf indigo, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, violet-blue flowers. The plant is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions. Its seeds are encased in pods and are adapted for dispersal by wind or animals.
