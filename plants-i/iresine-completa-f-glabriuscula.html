Iresine completa f. glabriuscula

Iresine completa f. glabriuscula, commonly known as the velvetleaf iresine, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, often displaying shades of red, purple, and green. The leaves are typically broad and velvety to the touch, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color.
