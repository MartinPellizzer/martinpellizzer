Impatiens polyantha

Impatiens polyantha, commonly known as the Chinese impatiens, is a flowering plant native to Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and produces small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Impatiens polyantha is well-suited for garden borders and containers due to its compact growth habit.
