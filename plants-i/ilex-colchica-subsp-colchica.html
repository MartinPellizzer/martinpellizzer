Ilex colchica subsp. colchica

Ilex colchica subsp.

colchica, commonly known as the Crimean holly, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the Crimean Peninsula. It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath.

The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters, followed by bright red berries that are edible for birds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to the Mediterranean climate of its native region. Ilex colchica subsp.

colchica is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
