Impatiens nematoceras

Impatiens nematoceras, commonly known as the horned impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, elongated, horn-like calyx that resembles a small horn or tusk. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom throughout the growing season. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Impatiens nematoceras is a popular ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and ability to thrive in moist, shady conditions.
