Indigofera obscura

Indigofera obscura, commonly known as the dark indigo plant, is a species of legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its small, often clustered flowers that range in color from pale blue to deep purple. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are pinnate, with multiple leaflets that are often hairy.

Indigofera obscura is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
