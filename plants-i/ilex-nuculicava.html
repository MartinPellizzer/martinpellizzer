Ilex nuculicava

Ilex nuculicava, commonly known as the Florida holly, is a deciduous evergreen shrub or small tree native to the southeastern United States.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are glossy on top and lighter in color underneath. The plant produces small, bright red berries that are an important food source for various wildlife species. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full shade.

Ilex nuculicava is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
