Iris wattii

Iris wattii, commonly known as the Watt's iris, is a species of flowering plant in the genus Iris.

It is native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Africa. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and striking blue or purple flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, forming a dense rosette at the base.

Iris wattii typically blooms in the spring and is valued for its ornamental beauty in gardens.
