Ida reichenbachii

Ida reichenbachii, commonly known as the Reichenbach's lily, is a flowering plant belonging to the Liliaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Kazakhstan and Uzbekistan. The plant features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, with a strong, spicy fragrance. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, and it thrives in well-drained, rocky soils.

Ida reichenbachii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking blooms.
