Impatiens butaguensis

Impatiens butaguensis, commonly known as the Butaguan Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Central and South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features showy, bilaterally symmetrical flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom in clusters. Its leaves are ovate to elliptical in shape, with a smooth or slightly serrated margin.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of dense forests.
