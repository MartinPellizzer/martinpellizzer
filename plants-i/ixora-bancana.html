Ixora bancana

Ixora bancana, commonly known as the red torch flower, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of bright red flowers that bloom throughout the year, making it a popular ornamental plant. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Ixora bancana is often used in landscaping for its vibrant color and ability to withstand partial shade.
