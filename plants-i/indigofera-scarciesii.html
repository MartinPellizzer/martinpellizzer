Indigofera scarciesii

Indigofera scarciesii, commonly known as the Ethiopian indigo plant, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to the highlands of Ethiopia and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, pale blue to white flowers arranged in racemes, which are typically visited by various pollinators. Its seeds are enclosed in small, hairy pods and are adapted for dispersal by wind.

This species is of interest for its potential in traditional medicine and as a source of natural dyes.
