Isodon shikokianus var. intermedius

Isodon shikokianus var. intermedius, commonly known as the intermediate isodon, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are lance-shaped with a serrated margin, and it produces small, tubular flowers in shades of purple or blue.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
