Ibatia mollis

Ibatia mollis, commonly known as the yellow bell, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers, which are typically bell-shaped and clustered in racemes. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in humid lowland areas.

Ibatia mollis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
