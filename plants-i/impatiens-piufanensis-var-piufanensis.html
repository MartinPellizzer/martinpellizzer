Impatiens piufanensis var. piufanensis

Impatiens piufanensis var. piufanensis, commonly known as the Piufan impatiens, is a species native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its delicate, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of vibrant, showy flowers. The flowers typically bloom in shades of pink and white, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
