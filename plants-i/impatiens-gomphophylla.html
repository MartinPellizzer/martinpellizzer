Impatiens gomphophylla

Impatiens gomphophylla, commonly known as the giant impatiens, is a striking tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, velvety leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 meters, and thrives in humid, shaded environments. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.

Its unique floral structure and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for tropical landscaping.
