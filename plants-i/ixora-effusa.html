Ixora effusa

Ixora effusa, commonly known as the Chinese flame or Chinese hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant produces clusters of bright red or orange flowers, which are often used in ornamental gardening. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly waxy texture.

Ixora effusa is known for its ability to thrive in tropical and subtropical climates.
