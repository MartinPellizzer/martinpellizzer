Isatis rugulosa

Isatis rugulosa, commonly known as Chinese yellow mustard, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and pinnately compound leaves. The plant belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other mustard plants. It is often cultivated for its seeds, which are used in traditional medicine and as a condiment.

Isatis rugulosa is also valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
