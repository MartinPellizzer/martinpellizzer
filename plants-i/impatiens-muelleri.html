Impatiens muelleri

Impatiens muelleri, commonly known as the Mueller's Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are usually pink or red in color, with five petals. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
