Impatiens palniensis

Impatiens palniensis, commonly known as the Palni impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

The plant features showy, bilaterally symmetrical flowers that are typically red or orange in color, with five petals and a spurred lower petal.
