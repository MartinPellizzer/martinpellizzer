Iresine edmonstonei

Iresine edmonstonei, commonly known as the redleaf ironplant, is a tropical shrub native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety red leaves that are often variegated with green and purple tones. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Iresine edmonstonei is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant foliage.
