Impatiens yunnanensis

Impatiens yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan's impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Balsaminaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, spurred flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, red, and white. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.

Impatiens yunnanensis is also noted for its adaptability to different soil types, making it a popular choice for horticultural use.
