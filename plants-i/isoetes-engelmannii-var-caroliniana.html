Isoetes engelmannii var. caroliniana

Isoetes engelmannii var. caroliniana, commonly known as the Carolinian water clover, is a species of aquatic plant belonging to the genus Isoetes.

It is characterized by its unique, ribbon-like leaves that are often submerged or floating in shallow water. The plant produces spore-bearing structures called sporangia, which are typically located on the upper surface of the leaves. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and wetlands.

Its presence is an indicator of good water quality and ecological stability in its habitat.
