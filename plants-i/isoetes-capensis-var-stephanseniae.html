Isoetes capensis var. stephanseniae

Isoetes capensis var. stephanseniae, commonly known as the Cape clubmoss, is a species of aquatic plant belonging to the family Isoetaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, ribbon-like leaves that form a dense mat on the water's surface or in shallow water. The plant is native to southern Africa, particularly found in freshwater environments such as ponds and lakes. It plays a role in stabilizing aquatic ecosystems by preventing erosion and providing habitat for microorganisms.

Its unique morphology and ecological significance make it an important component of its native habitat.
