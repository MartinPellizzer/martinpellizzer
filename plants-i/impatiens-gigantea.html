Impatiens gigantea

Impatiens gigantea, commonly known as the giant impatiens, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range from pink to red, often with contrasting white veins. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are dark green and provide a striking backdrop to its vibrant blooms. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden areas.

Its rapid growth and long blooming season make it a favorite among gardeners seeking bold floral displays.
