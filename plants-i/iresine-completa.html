Iresine completa

Iresine completa, commonly known as redroot ironweed, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its striking red stems and leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to purple, attracting various pollinators. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Iresine completa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
