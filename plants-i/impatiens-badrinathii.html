Impatiens badrinathii

Impatiens badrinathii, commonly known as the Himalayan Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and grows in moist, shaded environments. It is a member of the Balsaminaceae family, known for its rapid growth and short lifespan.
