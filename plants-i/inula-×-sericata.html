Inula × sericata

Inula × sericata, commonly known as the silverleaf inula, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its silvery-white, velvety foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy yellow flower heads that are composed of numerous small florets. It is native to Europe and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

Inula × sericata prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy addition to rock gardens and borders.
