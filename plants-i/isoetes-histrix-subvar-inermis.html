Isoetes histrix subvar. inermis

Isoetes histrix subvar. inermis, commonly known as the sword fern, is a species of aquatic plant belonging to the order Isoetales.

It is characterized by its spore-bearing fronds that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant thrives in freshwater environments, such as ponds and lakes, where it attaches to submerged substrates. Its morphology includes a short, thick rhizome and elongated, sword-shaped leaves.

The plant plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for microorganisms and small aquatic life.
