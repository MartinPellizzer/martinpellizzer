Indigofera parkesii var. parkesii

Indigofera parkesii var. parkesii, commonly known as the African indigo, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height, featuring compound leaves with three to five leaflets. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, which bloom in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or savanna habitats.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental qualities and traditional uses in some regions.
