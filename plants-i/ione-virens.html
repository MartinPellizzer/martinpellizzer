Ione virens

Ione virens, commonly known as the green bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to the western United States.

It features upright stems with lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. It thrives in well-drained soils and full to partial sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its botanical name reflects its vibrant green foliage and bell-like flowers.
