Indigofera mildrediana

Indigofera mildrediana, commonly known as the Mildred's indigo, is a flowering plant species native to tropical regions of Africa.

The plant is notable for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it valuable in ecological and agricultural contexts. The seeds of Indigofera mildrediana are often used in traditional medicine and can also serve as a source of dye.
