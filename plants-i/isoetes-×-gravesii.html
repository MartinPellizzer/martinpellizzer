Isoetes × gravesii

Isoetes × gravesii, commonly known as the hybrid water fern, is a species belonging to the genus Isoetes, which includes spike mosses.

It is a submerged aquatic plant characterized by its spore-bearing structures called sporangia. The plant typically grows in freshwater environments such as ponds and lakes. Its leaves are long and ribbon-like, often forming dense underwater mats.

Isoetes × gravesii plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and oxygen through photosynthesis.
