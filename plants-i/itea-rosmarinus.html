Itea rosmarinus

Itea rosmarinus, commonly known as sweet fern, is a shrub native to North America.

It is characterized by its aromatic, fern-like leaves that emit a pleasant scent when crushed. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in wetland areas.

Itea rosmarinus is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing habitat for various wildlife species.
