Indigofera mupensis subsp.

mupensis, commonly known as the Mupen indigo, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa. It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces pods that contain seeds, and it is often found in open woodlands and savannas.
