Ixora gautieri

Ixora gautieri, commonly known as the African flame or red flame tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters tall, featuring dense, glossy green leaves. The plant produces clusters of bright red flowers, which are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for warm, humid climates.

Its ornamental value is due to its vibrant floral display and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
