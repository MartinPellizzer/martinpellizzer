Ixora minor

Ixora minor, commonly known as the small flame flower, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of bright orange-red flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that are usually glossy green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Ixora minor is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a hedge plant.
