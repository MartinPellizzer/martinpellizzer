Ixora batuensis

Ixora batuensis, commonly known as the Batuensis flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a yellow or orange center. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal areas or near water sources.

Ixora batuensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
