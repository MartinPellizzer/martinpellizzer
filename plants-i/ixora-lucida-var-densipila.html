Ixora lucida var. densipila

Ixora lucida var. densipila, commonly known as the dense-leaved ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and clusters of bright orange or red flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping in warm regions.
