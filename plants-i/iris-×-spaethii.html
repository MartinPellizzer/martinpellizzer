Iris × spaethii

Iris × spaethii, commonly known as the Späth iris, is a hybrid perennial plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that bear showy, violet-blue flowers with a distinctive yellow center. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and thrives in well-drained soils with full sun exposure. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a fan shape.

Iris × spaethii is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
