Ilex versteeghii

Ilex versteeghii, commonly known as the Versteeg's holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically leathery in texture. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by red, fleshy berries that are an important food source for local wildlife. Ilex versteeghii thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native environment.
