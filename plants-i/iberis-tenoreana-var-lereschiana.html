Iberis tenoreana var. lereschiana

Iberis tenoreana var. lereschiana, commonly known as the Sicilian candytuft, is a flowering plant native to the island of Sicily.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. It is characterized by its low-growing habit and mat-forming growth pattern.

The foliage consists of narrow, gray-green leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white hairs.
