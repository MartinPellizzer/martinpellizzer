Ixora acutiflora

Ixora acutiflora, commonly known as the flame bush, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has simple, opposite, and often hairy leaves that are dark green in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Ixora acutiflora is also valued for its ability to tolerate coastal conditions and air pollution.
