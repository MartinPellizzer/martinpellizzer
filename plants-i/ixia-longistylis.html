Ixia longistylis

Ixia longistylis, commonly known as the long-styled ixia, is a striking South African flowering plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant produces showy, clustered blooms that are highly attractive to pollinators such as bees and birds. Ixia longistylis thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in grassland and rocky habitats.

Its distinctive long stamens make it a popular choice for both ornamental gardening and botanical study.
