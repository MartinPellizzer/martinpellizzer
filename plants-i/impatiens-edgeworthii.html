Impatiens edgeworthii

Impatiens edgeworthii, commonly known as the Edgeworth's impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its strikingly colorful, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy and have a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
