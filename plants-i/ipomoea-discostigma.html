Ipomoea discostigma

Ipomoea discostigma, commonly known as the zigzag morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive zigzag pattern along the veins, giving it its common name. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found growing wild in disturbed soils.

Ipomoea discostigma is a climbing vine that can reach lengths of several meters, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.
