Isonandra perrottetiana var. anaimalaiana

Isonandra perrottetiana var. anaimalaiana, commonly known as the Anaimalaiana flame tree, is a tropical species native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery orange to red flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual impact. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and can grow as a shrub or small tree, depending on environmental conditions. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes for its vibrant floral display.
