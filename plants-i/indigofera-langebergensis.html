Indigofera langebergensis

Indigofera langebergensis, commonly known as the Natal indigo, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant produces pods that contain seeds, and it is often found in grassland and woodland habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
