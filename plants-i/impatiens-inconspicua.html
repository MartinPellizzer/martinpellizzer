Impatiens inconspicua

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
