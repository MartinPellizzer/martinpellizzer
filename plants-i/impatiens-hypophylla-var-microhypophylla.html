Impatiens hypophylla var. microhypophylla

Impatiens hypophylla var. microhypophylla, commonly known as the miniature impatiens, is a delicate flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, heart-shaped leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale pink flowers. The plant is typically found in shaded, moist environments such as forests or rocky crevices. Its foliage is often covered in fine, soft hairs, contributing to its unique texture.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
