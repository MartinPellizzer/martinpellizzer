Impatiens wohlhauseri

Impatiens wohlhauseri, commonly known as the Wohlhauser's Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Central Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, spurred flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white pubescence. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing along forest floors or in dense undergrowth.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a subject of interest for botanists and horticulturists alike.
