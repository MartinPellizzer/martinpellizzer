Impatiens putii

Impatiens putii, commonly known as the pink impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Balsaminaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to white. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and a slightly hairy underside. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens putii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
