Indigofera macra var. aequalis

Indigofera macra var. aequalis, commonly known as the slender indigo, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant produces cylindrical pods that contain several elliptical seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in moist, shaded environments.
