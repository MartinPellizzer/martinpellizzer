Indigofera thoothukudiensis

Indigofera thoothukudiensis, commonly known as the Thoothukudi indigofera, is a flowering plant native to southern India.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is typically found in coastal and wetland areas, where it thrives in moist, sandy soils.
