Iris kerneriana

Iris kerneriana, commonly known as the Kerner iris, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a tall, herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features striking blue to violet flowers with three upright petals and three drooping sepals. Its rhizomatous root system allows it to thrive in well-drained soils.

The leaves are sword-shaped and form a dense, grass-like foliage that adds to its ornamental value.
