Iris japonica

Iris japonica, commonly known as Japanese iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It features striking blue to purple flowers with a distinctive three-dimensional structure. The plant has sword-shaped, grass-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Iris japonica is widely cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
