Ixia fucata

Ixia fucata, commonly known as the yellow fire lily, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow. Ixia fucata is commonly cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
