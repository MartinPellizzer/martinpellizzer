Impatiens centiflora

Impatiens centiflora, commonly known as the hundred-leaved impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 60 centimeters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Impatiens centiflora thrives in moist, shaded environments and is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value.
