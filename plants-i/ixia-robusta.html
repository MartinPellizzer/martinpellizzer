Ixia robusta

Ixia robusta, commonly known as the fire lily, is a striking perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a red or purple throat. The plant has narrow, strap-like leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating. Ixia robusta thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its blooming period typically occurs in late winter to early spring, adding vibrant color to the landscape during cooler months.
