Ixia rapunculoides var. rigida

Ixia rapunculoides var. rigida, commonly known as the rigid oxalis, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features striking, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a dark central stripe. The plant has a basal rosette of lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It produces elongated, slender stems that bear the showy flowers above the foliage.

This species thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
