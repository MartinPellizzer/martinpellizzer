Ionidium hybanthus

Ionidium hybanthus, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking red flowers that resemble spiders, hence its common name. The plant belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in gardens. It typically grows as a bulbous perennial, producing showy blooms in late spring or early summer.
