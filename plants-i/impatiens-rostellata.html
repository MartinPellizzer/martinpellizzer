Impatiens rostellata

Impatiens rostellata, commonly known as the red-rooted impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, spurred petals and vibrant, often pink or red flowers. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with slender stems and opposite leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in temperate climates.
