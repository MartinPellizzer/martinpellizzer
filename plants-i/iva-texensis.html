Iva texensis

Iva texensis, commonly known as Texas ragweed, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It is an annual herb that typically grows in disturbed soils and along roadsides. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture and produces small, yellow-green flowers in clusters. It is known for its allergenic pollen, which can cause respiratory issues in sensitive individuals.

Botanically, it belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and ragweeds.
