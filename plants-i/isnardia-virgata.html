Isnardia virgata

Isnardia virgata, commonly known as the slender feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and fine, feather-like leaves that give it a delicate appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. Its inflorescence consists of a narrow cluster of small, spikelet-like flowers.

Isnardia virgata is often used in ornamental gardening for its elegant, wind-swaying foliage.
