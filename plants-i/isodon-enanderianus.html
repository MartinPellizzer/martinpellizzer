Isodon enanderianus

Isodon enanderianus, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its opposite, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of tubular, fragrant flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Isodon enanderianus is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
