Isodon lungshengensis

Isodon lungshengensis, commonly known as the Lungsheng skullcap, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and has lance-shaped leaves with a hairy surface. Its flowers are small and clustered, often in whorls, and display a range of colors from purple to blue.

It is valued in traditional medicine for its potential medicinal properties.
