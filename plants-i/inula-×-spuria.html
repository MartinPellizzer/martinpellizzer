Inula × spuria

Inula × spuria, commonly known as the common fleabane, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its yellow to orange composite flowers, which are arranged in a flat, disc-like structure. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as fields, roadsides, and meadows. Its leaves are usually hairy and alternate, with a rough texture.

Inula × spuria is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
