Impatiens lantziana

Impatiens lantziana, commonly known as the Lantz's impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 feet. The plant features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are usually pink or red, with five petals and a distinctive spur. Its leaves are ovate and slightly hairy, providing a textured appearance.

It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.
