Impatiens opinata

Impatiens opinata, commonly known as the blue impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant blue or violet flowers, which are typically borne in clusters on slender stems. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens opinata is a popular ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
