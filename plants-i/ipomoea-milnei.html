Ipomoea milnei

Ipomoea milnei, commonly known as the milne's morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a climbing vine that produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of purple and blue. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is known for its rapid growth habit. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Ipomoea milnei is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant.
