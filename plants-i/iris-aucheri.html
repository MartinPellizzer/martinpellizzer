Iris aucheri

Iris aucheri, commonly known as the Persian iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. The plant produces showy, violet-blue flowers with three upright petals and three downward-curving sepals. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its early spring blooming period.
