Ilex hanceana f. rotundata

Ilex hanceana f. rotundata, commonly known as the rounded holly, is a shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage with thick, leathery leaves that are oval to rounded in shape. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters, followed by bright red berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Its compact form and winter hardiness make it suitable for use in gardens and as a hedge.
