Ixora cuspidata

Ixora cuspidata, commonly known as the flame flower or red ixora, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, clustered inflorescences of bright red, orange, or yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens and landscapes.

Ixora cuspidata is also valued for its ability to attract birds and insects, contributing to ecological biodiversity.
