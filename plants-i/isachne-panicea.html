Isachne panicea

Isachne panicea, commonly known as slender false panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as marshes, swamps, and along stream banks. The plant has slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences. Its flowering period occurs in late summer to early autumn, and it is often found in wetland ecosystems.

Isachne panicea plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
