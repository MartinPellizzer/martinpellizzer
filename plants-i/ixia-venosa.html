Ixia venosa

Ixia venosa, commonly known as the flame lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fiery orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features strap-like, linear leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. Ixia venosa thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is further enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and ability to naturalize in suitable climates.
