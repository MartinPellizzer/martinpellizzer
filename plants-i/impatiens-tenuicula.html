Impatiens tenuicula

Impatiens tenuicula, commonly known as the slender impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant features hairy stems and oval-shaped leaves with a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens tenuicula is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to add color to shaded garden spaces.
