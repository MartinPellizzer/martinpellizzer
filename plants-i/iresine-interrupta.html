Iresine interrupta

Iresine interrupta, commonly known as red leaf iron plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, which often features red, purple, or maroon hues. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
