Ixora orohenensis

Ixora orohenensis, commonly known as the Philippine flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its bright red or orange inflorescences, which are clustered in panicle-like structures. The plant has simple, opposite, elliptical leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal areas or near water sources.

Ixora orohenensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its vibrant floral displays.
