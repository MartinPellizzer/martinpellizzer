Isatis bannatica

Isatis bannatica, commonly known as the yellow flag or Chinese yellow flag, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a waxy surface and can grow up to 2 meters tall. It thrives in wetlands and marshy areas, often forming dense stands.

Its roots contain compounds used in traditional medicine and dye production.
