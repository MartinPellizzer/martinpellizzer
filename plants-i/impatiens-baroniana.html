Impatiens baroniana

Impatiens baroniana, commonly known as the "Baron's Impatiens," is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, spurred petals and vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to white. The plant grows as an herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 3 feet, and prefers shady, moist environments. Its foliage consists of heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Impatiens baroniana is valued in horticulture for its beauty and ability to thrive in partial shade.
