Isodon longitubus var. contractus

Isodon longitubus var. contractus, commonly known as the narrow tubular isodon, is a flowering plant species native to specific regions of China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender, tubular flowers that are typically pale blue or purple in color. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in fine hairs.
