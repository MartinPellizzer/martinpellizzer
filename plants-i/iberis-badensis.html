Iberis badensis

Iberis badensis, commonly known as the Balkan candytuft, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan region.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers. The plant has dark green, lobed leaves and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils. It blooms in early spring and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.

Iberis badensis is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
