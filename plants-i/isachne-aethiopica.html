Isachne aethiopica

Isachne aethiopica, commonly known as African grass or Ethiopian grass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny florets. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and along riverbanks.

Isachne aethiopica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
