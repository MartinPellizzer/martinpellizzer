Ipomoea valenzuelensis f. glabrescens

Ipomoea valenzuelensis f. glabrescens, commonly known as the velvet morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Convolvulaceae family and is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep purple.
