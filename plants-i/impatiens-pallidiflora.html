Impatiens pallidiflora

Impatiens pallidiflora, commonly known as the pale-flowered impatiens, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Himalayas.

It features showy, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in late summer to early autumn. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with slightly serrated edges, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments. It is often found in woodland areas and along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking shade-tolerant plants.
