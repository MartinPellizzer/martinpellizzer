Ixora arnottiana

Ixora arnottiana, commonly known as the red ixora, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of vibrant red flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually oval-shaped and have a smooth texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Ixora arnottiana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and ability to attract pollinators.
