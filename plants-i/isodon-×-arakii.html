Isodon × arakii

Isodon × arakii, commonly known as the Japanese skullcap, is a flowering plant in the Lamiaceae family.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Isodon japonicus and Isodon amethystinus. The plant is characterized by its square, hairy stems and opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. Its small, tubular flowers are typically blue to purple in color, arranged in whorled clusters.

It is native to Japan and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and medicinal properties.
