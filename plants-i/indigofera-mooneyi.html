Indigofera mooneyi

Indigofera mooneyi, commonly known as Mooney's indigo, is a flowering plant species native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing adaptations to drought conditions. Its seed pods contain small, hard seeds that are often dispersed by wind.
