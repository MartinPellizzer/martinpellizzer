Ixia furva

Ixia furva, commonly known as the yellow fire lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a contrasting purple or red throat. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are evergreen during the growing season.

Ixia furva is known for its vibrant blooms and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
