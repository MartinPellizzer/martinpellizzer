Ixora coccinea var. caudata

Ixora coccinea var. caudata, commonly known as the flame bush or red flame plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, clustered inflorescences of bright red, tubular flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has opposite, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant floral display. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a hedge due to its hardy nature and long-lasting blooms.
