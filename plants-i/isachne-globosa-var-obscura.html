Isachne globosa var. obscura

Isachne globosa var. obscura, commonly known as the dark globular grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to certain regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, rounded inflorescence that gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

The flowers are small and inconspicuous, arranged in dense clusters at the top of the plant.
