Isatis stocksii

Isatis stocksii, commonly known as yellow flag or yellow isatis, is a perennial herb native to eastern North America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly rough texture.

Isatis stocksii is often found in wetlands, stream banks, and other moist habitats, where it thrives in full sun to partial shade.
