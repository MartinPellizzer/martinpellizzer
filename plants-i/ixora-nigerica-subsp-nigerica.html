Ixora nigerica subsp. nigerica

Ixora nigerica subsp.

nigerica, commonly known as the black ixora, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and clusters of bright red flowers that bloom throughout the year.

The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are opposite in arrangement, and its flowers are typically arranged in terminal inflorescences. It thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The subspecies is closely related to other ixora species and is valued for its adaptability to various soil types.
