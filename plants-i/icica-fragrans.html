Icica fragrans

Icica fragrans, commonly known as the sweet-scented orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces elegant, fragrant flowers with a delicate, sweet aroma, often used in perfumery and traditional medicine. Its roots contain compounds that have been studied for their potential medicinal properties.

Icica fragrans is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its cultural significance in local communities.
