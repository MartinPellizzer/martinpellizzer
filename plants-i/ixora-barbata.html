Ixora barbata

Ixora barbata, commonly known as the hairy ixora, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of bright red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish.

Ixora barbata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
