Ixora × tenuifolia

Ixora × tenuifolia, commonly known as the dwarf ixora, is a hybrid shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape and have a smooth texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Ixora × tenuifolia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.
