Ichnanthus pallens f. monstrosus

Ichnanthus pallens f. monstrosus, commonly known as the variegated marsh orchid, is a unique variant of the marsh orchid species.

It is characterized by its striking variegated leaves, which display a mix of green and white or yellow markings. This form is a natural mutation that enhances its ornamental value. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy environments, thriving in moist, acidic soils.

Its botanical interest lies in its genetic variation and the aesthetic appeal of its foliage.
