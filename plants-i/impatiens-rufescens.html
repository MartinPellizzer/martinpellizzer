Impatiens rufescens

Impatiens rufescens, commonly known as the red-stemmed impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Balsaminaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range from pink to red. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens rufescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
