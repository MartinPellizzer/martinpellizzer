Impatiens jacquesii

Impatiens jacquesii, commonly known as the red-veined impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red, with prominent red veins on the petals. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens jacquesii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
