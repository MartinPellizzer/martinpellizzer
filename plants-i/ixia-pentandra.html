Ixia pentandra

Ixia pentandra, commonly known as the Orange-Ixia, is a perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllis family (Amaryllidaceae) and is characterized by its striking orange-red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically borne on a single, erect stem. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, emerging from a bulbous base.

Ixia pentandra is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
