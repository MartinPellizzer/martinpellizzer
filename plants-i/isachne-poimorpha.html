Isachne poimorpha

Isachne poimorpha, commonly known as the narrowleaf isachne, is a species of grass belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to various regions of Asia, including China, Japan, and the Korean Peninsula. This plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a distinctive silver or grayish hue on the underside.

Isachne poimorpha is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in partial shade.
