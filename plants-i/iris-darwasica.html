Iris darwasica

Iris darwasica, commonly known as the Darwasica iris, is a species of flowering plant in the genus Iris.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Afghanistan and Pakistan. The plant is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are sword-shaped and lanceolate, typically growing in dense clumps.

Iris darwasica is often found in moist, well-drained soils along streambanks and in meadows.
