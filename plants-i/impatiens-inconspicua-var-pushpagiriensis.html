Impatiens inconspicua var. pushpagiriensis

Impatiens inconspicua var. pushpagiriensis, commonly known as the Himalayan Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Nepal and northern India.

It is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and grows in moist, shaded environments. It is a member of the Balsaminaceae family, known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in humid climates.
