Iris kaempferi var. lemoinei

Iris kaempferi var. lemoinei, commonly known as the Lemoine iris, is a striking ornamental plant native to the mountainous regions of Japan.

It is a rhizomatous perennial that produces tall, slender stems with showy, violet-blue flowers in early to mid-summer. The plant features lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its delicate appearance and vibrant blooms make it a valuable addition to both natural and cultivated landscapes.
