Impatiens giorgii

Impatiens giorgii, commonly known as the Giant Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually hairy and covered in fine pubescence. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens giorgii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
