Iris caucasica var. multiflora

Iris caucasica var. multiflora, commonly known as the Caucasian multiflora iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Caucasus region.

It features tall, upright stems that bear clusters of striking blue to purple flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant blooms and elegant foliage.
