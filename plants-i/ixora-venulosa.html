Ixora venulosa

Ixora venulosa, commonly known as the red flame or flame ixora, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of bright red flowers, which bloom throughout the year in warm climates. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that are usually glossy green. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract birds and butterflies.
