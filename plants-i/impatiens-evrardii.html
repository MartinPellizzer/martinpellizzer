Impatiens evrardii

Impatiens evrardii, commonly known as the Congo impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features showy, spurred flowers that are usually pink or red in color, with five petals and a distinctive nectar spur. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.
