Ixora cauliflora var. cauliflora

Ixora cauliflora var. cauliflora, commonly known as the Christmas ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form and is known for its bright, showy flowers that bloom throughout the year. The flowers are typically red or orange and are borne in clusters, giving the plant a striking appearance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The plant has small, leathery leaves that are arranged in whorls around the stem.
