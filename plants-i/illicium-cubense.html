Illicium cubense

Illicium cubense, commonly known as the Cuban star anise, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the family Schisandraceae and is characterized by its aromatic, star-shaped fruits. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into dark brown, capsule-like fruits. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a serrated margin.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
