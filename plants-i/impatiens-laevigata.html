Impatiens laevigata

Impatiens laevigata, commonly known as the smooth impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of showy, spurred flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptic in shape, with a smooth, glossy surface. This species thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
