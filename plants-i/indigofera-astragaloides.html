Indigofera astragaloides

Indigofera astragaloides, commonly known as the desert indigo, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa and the Middle East.

The plant produces yellow to orange flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
