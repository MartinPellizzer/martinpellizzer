Iris × hybrida

Iris × hybrida, commonly known as the garden iris, is a hybrid species resulting from the crossbreeding of various iris species.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stems that bear showy, often purple or blue flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically has broad, sword-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Iris × hybrida is valued for its ornamental beauty and is widely cultivated for its vibrant blooms.
