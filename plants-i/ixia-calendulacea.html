Ixia calendulacea

Ixia calendulacea, commonly known as the orange lily, is a striking flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family, which includes many ornamental plants with showy flowers. The plant produces vibrant orange-red blooms with a distinctive yellow center, resembling a sunflower. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its botanical features include narrow, grass-like leaves and a short, sturdy stem that supports its colorful inflorescence.
