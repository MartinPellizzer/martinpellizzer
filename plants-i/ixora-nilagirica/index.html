Ixora Nilagirica

Ixora nilagirica is a small evergreen shrub native to the Western Ghats of India. It is known for its vibrant, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. This plant thrives in humid, tropical climates and prefers well-drained soil. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure compared to other members of the Rubiaceae family.
