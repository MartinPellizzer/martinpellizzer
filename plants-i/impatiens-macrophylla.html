Impatiens macrophylla

Impatiens macrophylla, commonly known as the giant impatiens, is a striking tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep pink, often with contrasting veining. The plant features broad, ovate leaves that are glossy and can grow up to 10 inches in length. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a ornamental plant.

Impatiens macrophylla is known for its rapid growth and ability to bloom continuously throughout the growing season.
