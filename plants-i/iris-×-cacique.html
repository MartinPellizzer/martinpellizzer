Iris × cacique

Iris × cacique, commonly known as the Cacique iris, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Iris germanica and Iris pallida.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically feature a deep violet to purple coloration with a white or pale yellow center. The plant is a perennial herb that thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation. Its foliage consists of sword-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and stiff.

The Cacique iris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and floral arrangements.
