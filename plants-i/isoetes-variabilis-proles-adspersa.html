Isoetes variabilis proles adspersa

Isoetes variabilis proles adspersa, commonly known as the scattered spore water fern, is a species of aquatic plant belonging to the genus Isoetes.

It is characterized by its unique morphology, featuring a spore-bearing stalk that is often scattered among submerged vegetation. This plant thrives in freshwater environments, such as ponds and slow-moving streams, where it can anchor itself in muddy substrates. Its spores are dispersed by water currents, contributing to its distribution across various aquatic habitats.

The plant plays a role in maintaining water quality and providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.
