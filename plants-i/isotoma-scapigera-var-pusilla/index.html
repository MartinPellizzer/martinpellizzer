Isotoma Scapigera Var. Pusilla

Isotoma scapigera var. pusilla is a small, delicate flowering plant known for its compact growth and charming blue-violet blooms. Native to mountainous regions of Europe, it thrives in rocky and well-drained soils. This variant of Isotoma scapigera is particularly prized for its ability to flourish in shaded environments. Its tiny stature makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine displays. Despite its modest size, it adds a touch of elegance and color to any garden setting.
