Ixora Biflora Var. Typica

Ixora biflora var. typica is a vibrant flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia. It is known for its clusters of bright red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. This variety is particularly popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and hardiness. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil. Its compact growth habit makes it a favorite for garden borders and container planting.
