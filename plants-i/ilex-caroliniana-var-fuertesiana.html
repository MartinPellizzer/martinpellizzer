Ilex caroliniana var. fuertesiana

Ilex caroliniana var. fuertesiana, commonly known as the Fuertes's holly, is a compact evergreen shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are glossy dark green on top and lighter green underneath. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for ornamental landscaping.

Its dense growth habit and year-round foliage make it a popular choice for hedges and foundation plantings.
