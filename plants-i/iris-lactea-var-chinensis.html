Iris lactea var. chinensis

Iris lactea var. chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It features striking white to pale blue flowers with a distinctive, often darker central stripe. The plant has sword-shaped, grass-like leaves that are typically green and arranged in a fan-like pattern. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant blooms.
