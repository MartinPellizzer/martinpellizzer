Ilex spicata var. spicata

Ilex spicata var. spicata, commonly known as the spiny holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and small, dark blue berries that are produced in the fall. The plant typically grows in moist, acidic soils and thrives in partial to full shade. Its dense foliage makes it a popular choice for hedges and ornamental landscaping.

It is also valued for its ecological role in providing food and shelter for wildlife.
