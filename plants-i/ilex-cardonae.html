Ilex cardonae

Ilex cardonae, commonly known as the cardoon ilex, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage and small, spiny leaves that provide a rugged appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by red, fleshy berries that are edible for certain bird species. Ilex cardonae thrives in arid and rocky environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

Its ecological significance lies in its role as a food source and habitat for local wildlife.
