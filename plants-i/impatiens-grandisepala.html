Impatiens grandisepala

Impatiens grandisepala, commonly known as the large-sepal impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that typically range in color from white to pink. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its sepals are notably large and prominent, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
