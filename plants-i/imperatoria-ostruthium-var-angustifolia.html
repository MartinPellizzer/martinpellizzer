Imperatoria ostruthium var. angustifolia

Imperatoria ostruthium var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf bedstraw, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has thin, ascending stems covered with fine hairs and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

Its botanical structure makes it a characteristic species of wetland and grassland habitats.
