Isodon liangshanicus

Isodon liangshanicus, commonly known as the Yunnan honeysuckle, is a perennial herb native to China, particularly found in the Yunnan province.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and has lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. Its flowers are usually pink or white, arranged in clusters, and bloom during the summer months.

Isodon liangshanicus is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
