Isatis elegans

Isatis elegans, commonly known as the Chinese yellowflower, is a biennial plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is native to East Asia and is characterized by its yellow flowers and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, round seeds that are often used in traditional herbal medicine. Its leaves and roots contain compounds such as glucosinolates, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Isatis elegans is also cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant yellow blooms.
