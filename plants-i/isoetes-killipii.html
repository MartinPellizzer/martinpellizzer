Isoetes killipii

Isoetes killipii, commonly known as the Killip's watermace, is a species of aquatic plant belonging to the order Isoetales.

It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage composed of small, needle-like leaves that grow in a spiral arrangement. This plant typically inhabits still or slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and wetlands.
