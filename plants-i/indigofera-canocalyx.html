Indigofera canocalyx

Indigofera canocalyx, commonly known as Chinese indigo or blue bean, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height, characterized by its compound leaves and showy, pink to purple flowers. The plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial in agricultural settings.

Its seeds are often used in traditional medicine, while the plant itself has been historically used for dyeing fabrics with a deep blue color.
