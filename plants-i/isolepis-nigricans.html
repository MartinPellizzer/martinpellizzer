Isolepis nigricans

Isolepis nigricans, commonly known as black spike-rush, is a perennial grass-like plant native to wetland and marshy environments.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and other similar plants. The plant is characterized by its dark, slender stems and dense, spike-like clusters of small, brownish flowers. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, often growing in dense tufts.
