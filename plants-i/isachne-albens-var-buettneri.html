Isachne albens var. buettneri

Isachne albens var. buettneri, commonly known as the silver hairgrass, is a variant of the silvergrass species native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial grass characterized by its fine, silver-tinged foliage and delicate, feathery inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and along stream banks. Its unique coloration and graceful appearance make it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
