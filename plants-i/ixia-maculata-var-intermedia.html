Ixia maculata var. intermedia

Ixia maculata var. intermedia, commonly known as the spotted flame lily, is a striking flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range from yellow to orange with red streaks. Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and unique foliage.
