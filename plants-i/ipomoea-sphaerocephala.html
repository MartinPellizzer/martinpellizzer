Ipomoea sphaerocephala

Ipomoea sphaerocephala, commonly known as the globeflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, spherical-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant produces heart-shaped leaves and thrives in warm, humid climates. It is often found in open woodlands and along riverbanks.

The flowers bloom in clusters and are pollinated by insects, making it an important plant in its native ecosystem.
