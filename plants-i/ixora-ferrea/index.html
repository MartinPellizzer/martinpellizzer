Ixora Ferrea

Ixora ferrea is a striking evergreen shrub known for its vibrant red flowers and lush green foliage. Native to tropical regions of Asia, it thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil. This plant is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to bloom throughout the year. Its compact growth habit makes it ideal for gardens, containers, and landscape design. With proper care, Ixora ferrea can become a focal point in any tropical or subtropical garden.
