Impatiens margaritifera var. purpurascens

Impatiens margaritifera var. purpurascens, commonly known as the purple-flowered impatiens, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Balsaminaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range from pink to deep purple. Its flowers are usually borne in clusters and have a distinctive spurred structure at the base of the petals.

This variety is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments.
