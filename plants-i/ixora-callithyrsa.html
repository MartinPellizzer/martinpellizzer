Ixora callithyrsa

Ixora callithyrsa, commonly known as the flame-of-the-forest, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters, making it a striking ornamental plant. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Ixora callithyrsa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in landscaping and as a hedge plant.
