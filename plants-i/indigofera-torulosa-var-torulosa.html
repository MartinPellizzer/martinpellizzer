Indigofera torulosa var. torulosa

Indigofera torulosa var. torulosa, commonly known as the purple wisteria, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its woody vines and clusters of small, fragrant, violet-blue flowers. The plant has pinnate leaves with numerous leaflets and produces small, pod-like fruits containing seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
