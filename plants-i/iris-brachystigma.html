Iris brachystigma

Iris brachystigma, commonly known as the dwarf iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, grass-like leaves and produces striking blue to violet flowers with three upright petals. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters, making it a compact ornamental choice. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Iris brachystigma is valued for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
