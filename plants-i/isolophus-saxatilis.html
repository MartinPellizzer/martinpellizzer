Isolophus saxatilis

Isolophus saxatilis, commonly known as the rock spurge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to rocky and arid environments.

Isolophus saxatilis is known for its resilience to drought and extreme temperatures, making it a hardy species in Mediterranean and semi-desert climates.
