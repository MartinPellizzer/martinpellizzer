Ixora stricta var. amoena

Ixora stricta var. amoena, commonly known as the red flame ixora, is a flowering shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and is often cultivated for its vibrant, showy flowers. The plant produces clusters of small, bright red flowers that bloom throughout the year in warm climates. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a glossy appearance.

Ixora stricta var. amoena is relatively drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
