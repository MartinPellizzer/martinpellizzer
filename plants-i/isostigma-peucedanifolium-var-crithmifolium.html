Isostigma peucedanifolium var. crithmifolium

Isostigma peucedanifolium var. crithmifolium, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are often covered in a powdery coating. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and distinctive foliage.
