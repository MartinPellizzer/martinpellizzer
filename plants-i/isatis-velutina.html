Isatis velutina

Isatis velutina, commonly known as velvetleaf woad, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a velvety texture. Its flowers are small, yellow, and clustered in racemes, typical of the mustard family.

Isatis velutina is often found in disturbed soils and can become a weed in agricultural settings.
