It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. Ipomoea rojasii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
