Ixora africana

Ixora africana, commonly known as the African flame or red flame bush, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its clusters of bright red, tubular flowers that bloom throughout the year in warm climates. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Ixora africana is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and birds.
