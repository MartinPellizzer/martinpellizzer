Ilex theezans var. gracilior

Ilex theezans var. gracilior, commonly known as the slender Chinese holly, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It features glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries that are edible for birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for ornamental landscaping.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a low-maintenance, decorative plant.
