Iris × belouinii

Iris × belouinii, commonly known as the Belouin iris, is a hybrid perennial plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters, featuring grass-like leaves and a robust rhizomatous root system. Native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
