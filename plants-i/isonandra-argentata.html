Isonandra argentata

Isonandra argentata, commonly known as silverleaf isonandra, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
