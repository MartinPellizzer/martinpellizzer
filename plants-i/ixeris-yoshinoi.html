Ixeris yoshinoi

Ixeris yoshinoi, commonly known as Yoshino's carpetweed, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Japan.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, adding a vibrant touch to gardens. The plant has creeping stems that spread along the ground, forming a dense mat of foliage. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, with a grass-like appearance.

Ixeris yoshinoi is often used in rock gardens and as a ground cover due to its hardy nature and ornamental value.
