Ixia rigida

Ixia rigida, commonly known as the hardy fire lily, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, upright stems that bear showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are evergreen and remain throughout the year. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Ixia rigida is known for its resilience and ability to bloom reliably in suitable climates.
