Impatiens polycycla

Impatiens polycycla, commonly known as the tropical impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, spurred flowers that come in a range of colors, including white, pink, and purple. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous annual, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are slender and often hairy.

Impatiens polycycla thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
