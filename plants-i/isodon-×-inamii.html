Isodon × inamii

Isodon × inamii, commonly known as the Japanese blue grass, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Isodon × inamii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
