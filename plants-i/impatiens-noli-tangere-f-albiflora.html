Impatiens noli-tangere f. albiflora

Impatiens noli-tangere f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered impatiens, is a striking hybrid within the Impatiens genus.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that contrast with its dark green, heart-shaped leaves. The plant is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. It is a popular choice for gardeners due to its vibrant blooms and ability to flourish in partial shade.

The 'albiflora' variety specifically refers to its distinctive white flower color, making it a sought-after ornamental plant.
