Isachne commelinifolia

Isachne commelinifolia, commonly known as the slender isachne, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Commelinaceae, which includes a variety of grass-like and flowering plants. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories or wetlands. Its leaves are linear and often have a bluish-green hue, while its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences.

Isachne commelinifolia is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread vegetatively and form dense mats.
