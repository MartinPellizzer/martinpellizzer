Ixia viridis

Ixia viridis, commonly known as the green ox-eye, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllis family (Amaryllidaceae) and is characterized by its bright green foliage and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its flowers bloom in spring and are often used in floral arrangements due to their vibrant color.

Ixia viridis is also cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in gardens.
