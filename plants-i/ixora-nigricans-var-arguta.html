Ixora nigricans var. arguta

Ixora nigricans var. arguta, commonly known as the smooth ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of bright orange to red flowers. The leaves are typically smooth and glossy, with a lanceolate shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

This variant is distinguished by its slightly smaller and more compact growth compared to other ixora species.
