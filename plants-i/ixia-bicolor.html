Ixia bicolor

Ixia bicolor, commonly known as the yellow fire lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, narrow leaves and produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant blooms in late winter to early spring, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking winter color. Ixia bicolor thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.

Its unique appearance and seasonal bloom make it a valuable addition to both gardens and floral arrangements.
