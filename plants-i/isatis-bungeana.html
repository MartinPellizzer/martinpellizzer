Isatis bungeana

Isatis bungeana, commonly known as Chinese woad, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cabbage and mustard. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. It is cultivated for its leaves, which can be used to produce indigo dye.

Isatis bungeana is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
