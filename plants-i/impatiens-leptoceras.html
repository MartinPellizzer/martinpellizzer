Impatiens leptoceras

Impatiens leptoceras, commonly known as the slender touch-me-not, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, spurred flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and a slender, branching stem that grows upright. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens leptoceras is a member of the Balsaminaceae family, known for its rapid growth and ornamental value.
