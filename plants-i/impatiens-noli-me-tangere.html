Impatiens noli-me-tangere

Impatiens noli-me-tangere, commonly known as the "Impatient" or "Blushing bride," is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a range of colors, including pink, red, and white. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Its rapid growth and ability to bloom throughout the growing season make it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
