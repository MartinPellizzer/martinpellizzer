Iris atropurpurea var. atrofusca

Iris atropurpurea var. atrofusca, commonly known as the dark purple iris, is a striking perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of the Eastern Mediterranean.

It features tall, sturdy stems that bear showy, dark purple to almost black flowers with a distinctive, intricate pattern on the petals. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are arranged in a fan-like manner at the base. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its dramatic color and architectural form.
