Indocalamus tongchuensis

Indocalamus tongchuensis, commonly known as the Tongchuan bamboo, is a species of grass in the family Poaceae.

It is native to China, particularly found in the provinces of Sichuan and Shaanxi. This bamboo is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential ecological and economic benefits.
