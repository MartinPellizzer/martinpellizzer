Iris Zaprjagajevii

Iris zaprjagajevii is a rare and elusive species of iris native to the mountainous regions of central Asia. This plant is known for its striking blue-violet flowers and delicate foliage, which thrive in cool, moist environments. Due to its limited distribution and fragile habitat, it is considered endangered and faces significant conservation challenges. Its unique characteristics make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike. Protecting Iris zaprjagajevii is crucial for preserving biodiversity in its native ecosystems.
