Ixora affinis var. arguta

Ixora affinis var. arguta, commonly known as the argute ixora, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, fragrant clusters of orange-red flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a hedge plant.
