Iresine aurata

Iresine aurata, commonly known as the red leaf iron plant, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety red or purple foliage, which contrasts beautifully with its green stems. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. Iresine aurata thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant, colorful leaves.
