Impatiens tinctoria subsp. elegantissima

Impatiens tinctoria subsp.

elegantissima, commonly known as the elegant touch-me-not, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions of Central and South America. It features showy, spurred flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, often with a contrasting throat.

The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are typically hairy and have a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its rapid growth and attractive blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
