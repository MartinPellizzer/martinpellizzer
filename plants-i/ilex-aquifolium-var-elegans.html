Ilex aquifolium var. elegans

Ilex aquifolium var. elegans, commonly known as the English holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves that are typically oval in shape with spiny margins. The plant produces small, red berries that are toxic to humans and animals. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its hardy nature and year-round foliage make it a valuable addition to gardens and landscapes.
