Indigofera cavaleriei

Indigofera cavaleriei, commonly known as the Cavalerie's indigo, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are pinnately compound, and it produces pods that contain several seeds.
