Isachne subglobosa

Isachne subglobosa, commonly known as the globular isachne, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its compact, spherical growth habit, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences. It typically thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a valuable subject for ecological and taxonomic studies.
