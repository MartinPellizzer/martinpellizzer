Impatiens pallida f. pallida

Impatiens pallida f. pallida, commonly known as the pale impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its pale pink to white flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Impatiens pallida f. pallida is valued in gardens for its delicate appearance and ability to thrive in partial shade.
