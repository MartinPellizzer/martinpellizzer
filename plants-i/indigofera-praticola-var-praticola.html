Indigofera praticola var. praticola

Indigofera praticola var. praticola, commonly known as the narrowleaf indigo.

It is a perennial herb native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas. The plant features slender, aromatic leaves with a distinctive narrow shape. Its flowers are typically small and clustered in dense spikes, often displaying shades of blue or purple.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
