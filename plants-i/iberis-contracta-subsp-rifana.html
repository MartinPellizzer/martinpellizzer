Iberis contracta subsp. rifana

Iberis contracta subsp.

rifana, commonly known as the Rif carnation, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features dense clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, blooming in early spring.

The plant has a mat-forming habit with silver-green, lobed leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact size and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking drought-tolerant plants.
