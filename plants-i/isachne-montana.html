Isachne montana

Isachne montana, commonly known as mountain isachne, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in high-altitude environments. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is often found in alpine meadows and is adapted to cold, harsh climates.

Botanically, it is characterized by its compact growth habit and resilience to environmental stress.
