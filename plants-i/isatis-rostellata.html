Isatis rostellata

Isatis rostellata, commonly known as the Chinese yellow mustard, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other mustard species. The plant is characterized by its yellow flowers and pinnately divided leaves. It produces small, elongated pods that contain seeds used in traditional medicine.

Isatis rostellata is also cultivated for its oil-rich seeds and as a potential crop for biofuel production.
