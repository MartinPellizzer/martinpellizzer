Impatiens magnifica

Impatiens magnifica, commonly known as the magnificent impatiens, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, large, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically grows as an upright herb with opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The flowers are short-lived but attract a variety of pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
