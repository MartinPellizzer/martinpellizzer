Impatiens mooreana

Impatiens mooreana, commonly known as the Moore's impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens mooreana is a popular ornamental plant due to its attractive blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
