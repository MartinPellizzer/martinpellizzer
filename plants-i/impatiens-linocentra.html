Impatiens linocentra

Impatiens linocentra, commonly known as the golden flame impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright orange to red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Impatiens linocentra is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
