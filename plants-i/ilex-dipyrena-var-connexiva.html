Ilex dipyrena var. connexiva

Ilex dipyrena var. connexiva, commonly known as the Connexiva holly, is a variant of the evergreen shrub or small tree native to China.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are typically oval in shape with spiny margins. The plant produces small, white flowers in the spring, followed by red berries that are attractive to birds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

This variety is well-suited to USDA hardiness zones 6 through 9.
