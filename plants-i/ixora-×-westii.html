Ixora × westii

Ixora × westii, commonly known as the Chinese hibiscus, is a hybrid shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant has opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sun.

Ixora × westii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
