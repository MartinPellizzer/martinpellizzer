Isodon lihsienensis

Isodon lihsienensis, commonly known as the Lihsien honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as mountain forests. Its leaves are often used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and ecological importance in its native habitat.
