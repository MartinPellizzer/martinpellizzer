Impatiens thulunadensis

Impatiens thulunadensis, commonly known as the Thulunad impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a herbaceous species that typically grows in moist, shaded forest understories. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are usually bright red or orange in color. Its leaves are ovate and have a smooth, glossy surface.
