Ipomoea spectata

Ipomoea spectata, commonly known as the Crown of Thorns, is a striking flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that can grow up to 3 meters tall, featuring dense, spiny branches and dark green, leathery leaves. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers with five petals, often in clusters, which bloom throughout the growing season. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a notable addition to xeriscaped landscapes.
