Ipomoea thailandica

Ipomoea thailandica, commonly known as Thai morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a climbing vine that produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of blue, purple, and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in warm, humid climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Its rapid growth and ability to climb make it a popular choice for trellises and vertical gardening.
