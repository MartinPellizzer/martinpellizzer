Impatiens bullata var. bullatissima

Impatiens bullata var. bullatissima, commonly known as the bullate impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, waxy, and often brightly colored flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a velvety texture, adding to its ornamental appeal. This species thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
