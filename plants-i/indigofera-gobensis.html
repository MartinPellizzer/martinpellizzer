Indigofera gobensis

Indigofera gobensis, commonly known as Gobeni indigo, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and distinctive purple or blue flowers. The plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.

Its seeds are often used in traditional medicine and as a source of natural dyes.
