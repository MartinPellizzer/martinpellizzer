Isotrema pilosistylum

Isotrema pilosistylum, commonly known as the silky thistle, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its silvery, hairy leaves and purple to pink flowers that bloom in dense clusters. Native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico, it thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant has a woody base and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.

Its botanical features make it both ecologically significant and aesthetically notable in its native habitat.
