Ilex yunnanensis var. parvifolia

Ilex yunnanensis var. parvifolia, commonly known as the Yunnan holly, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible but often contain seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and as a landscape plant.
