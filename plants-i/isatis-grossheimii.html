Isatis grossheimii

Isatis grossheimii, commonly known as the Chinese yellow rocket, is a biennial plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and robust, hairy stems. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and features alternate, lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. Its flowers are small and clustered in racemes, and it produces round, green pods that contain several seeds.

Isatis grossheimii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.
