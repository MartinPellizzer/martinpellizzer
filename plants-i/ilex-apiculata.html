Ilex apiculata

Ilex apiculata, commonly known as the slender winterberry, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its narrow, upright growth habit and bright red berries that persist through winter. The leaves are typically alternate, elliptical, and have a leathery texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its vibrant seasonal color.

The plant is also important for wildlife, providing food for birds and small mammals.
