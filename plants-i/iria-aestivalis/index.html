Iria Aestivalis

Iria aestivalis is a striking flowering plant known for its vibrant purple blooms that catch the eye in early summer. Native to the wetlands and marshy areas of southern Europe, this species thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils. Its delicate, lance-shaped leaves and tall flowering stalks make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a touch of wild beauty. The plant is also valued for its role in supporting local pollinators and maintaining ecological balance. Despite its modest size, Iria aestivalis holds a special place in both botanical studies and horticultural practice.
