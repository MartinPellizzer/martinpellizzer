Illicium leiophyllum

Illicium leiophyllum, commonly known as the smooth-leafed star anise, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Schisandraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, star-shaped fruits. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has compound leaves with a smooth texture. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, often clustered in panicles.
