Isolepis holoschoenus var. panormitana

Isolepis holoschoenus var. panormitana, commonly known as the Mediterranean flat sedge, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wet or damp environments such as marshes and pond edges. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It is characterized by its ability to thrive in saline or brackish water conditions.

This variety is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its adaptability and role in stabilizing wetland ecosystems.
