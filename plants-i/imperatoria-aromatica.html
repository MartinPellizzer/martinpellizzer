Imperatoria aromatica

Imperatoria aromatica, commonly known as sweet everlasting, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that produces small, fragrant yellow flowers. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and stems, which emit a pleasant scent when crushed. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Imperatoria aromatica is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal medicine.
