Isocoma palmeri

Isocoma palmeri, commonly known as Palmer's blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Isocoma palmeri is often found in desert scrub and grassland habitats, adapting to harsh environmental conditions.
