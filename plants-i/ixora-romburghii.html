Ixora romburghii

Ixora romburghii, commonly known as the red flame ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a striking visual appeal to gardens. The plant has opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are dark green in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Ixora romburghii is also valued for its ability to grow in well-drained soil and its relatively low maintenance requirements.
