Impatiens amphorata var. umbrosa

Impatiens amphorata var. umbrosa, commonly known as the shade impatiens, is a species native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its growth habit is typically herbaceous, with stems that can be semi-woody at the base.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
