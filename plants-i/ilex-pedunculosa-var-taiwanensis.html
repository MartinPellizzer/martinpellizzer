Ilex pedunculosa var. taiwanensis

Ilex pedunculosa var. taiwanensis, commonly known as the Taiwan holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the family Aquifoliaceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with spiny margins. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters, followed by bright red berries that are an important food source for birds.
