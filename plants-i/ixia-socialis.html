Ixia socialis

Ixia socialis, commonly known as the social ixia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. Ixia socialis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its vibrant, long-lasting flowers.
