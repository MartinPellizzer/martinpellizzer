Isochilus linifolius

Isochilus linifolius, commonly known as the slender-leaved isochilus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers a sunny to partially shaded environment. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Isochilus linifolius is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
