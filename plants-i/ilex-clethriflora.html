Ilex clethriflora

Ilex clethriflora, commonly known as the Chinese holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Aquifoliaceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. The plant produces small, red berries that are toxic to humans but serve as a food source for birds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its dense foliage and winter interest.

Ilex clethriflora prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it adaptable to a variety of garden settings.
