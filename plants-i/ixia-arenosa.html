Ixia arenosa

Ixia arenosa, commonly known as the sand lily, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid environments. Ixia arenosa is a perennial herb with narrow, grass-like leaves and produces its flowers in late summer.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
