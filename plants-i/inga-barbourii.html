Inga barbourii

Inga barbourii, commonly known as the Barbour's inga, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, particularly in countries like Brazil and Colombia. The plant is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub, typically reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are compound, with multiple pairs of oval-shaped leaflets, and it produces small, clustered flowers that are often white or pale yellow.
