Iris × distincta

Iris × distincta, commonly known as the hybrid bearded iris, is a perennial plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, upright flower spikes that bear three to six petals, with the outer ones often being larger and more prominent. The plant typically grows to a height of 2 to 4 feet and prefers well-drained soil in full sun. Its foliage consists of broad, sword-shaped leaves that are dark green and often have a slight sheen.

Iris × distincta is valued in gardens for its vibrant colors and long blooming period, typically from late spring to early summer.
