Ixora miranda

Ixora miranda, commonly known as the flame-of-peace, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bright orange to red flowers that bloom in dense, rounded clusters. The plant has opposite, simple, and leathery leaves that are dark green in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Ixora miranda is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
