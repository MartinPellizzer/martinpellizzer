Ixora salicina

Ixora salicina, commonly known as the Chinese flame bush, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 2 to 4 meters, featuring dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces clusters of bright orange to red flowers, which are borne on long, slender inflorescences. Ixora salicina is known for its ability to thrive in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its flowers are not only visually striking but also attract pollinators such as bees and birds.
