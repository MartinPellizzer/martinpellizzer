Ilex shukunii

Ilex shukunii, commonly known as the Japanese holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to Japan.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, white flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries that are edible for birds. Ilex shukunii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and seasonal interest.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
