Iria globulosa

Iria globulosa, commonly known as the globe iris, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its compact, globular shape. The plant features showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, forming a rosette at the base.

Iria globulosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
