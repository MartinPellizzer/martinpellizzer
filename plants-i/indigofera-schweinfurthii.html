Indigofera schweinfurthii

Indigofera schweinfurthii, commonly known as African indigo, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces pods that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ecological and potential medicinal studies.
