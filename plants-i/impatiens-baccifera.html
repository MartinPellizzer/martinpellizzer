Impatiens baccifera

Impatiens baccifera, commonly known as the red-rooted impatiens, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, spurred flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant produces small, round, fleshy fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in tropical and subtropical regions.
