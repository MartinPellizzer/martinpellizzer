Ilex triflora var. javensis

Ilex triflora var. javensis, commonly known as the Java holly, is a species of flowering plant in the family Aquifoliaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia, and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, shaded environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
