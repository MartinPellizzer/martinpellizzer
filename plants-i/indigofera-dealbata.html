Indigofera dealbata

Indigofera dealbata, commonly known as the white indigo or white coral tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific.

The flowers are typically white or pale blue, appearing in clusters and blooming during the dry season.
