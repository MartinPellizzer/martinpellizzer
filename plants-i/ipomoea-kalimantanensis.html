Ipomoea kalimantanensis

Ipomoea kalimantanensis, commonly known as the Bornean morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Borneo.

It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that typically bloom in vibrant shades of purple or blue. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native habitat.
