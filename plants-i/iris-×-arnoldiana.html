Iris × arnoldiana

Iris × arnoldiana, commonly known as the German Iris, is a hybrid perennial plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that bear showy, violet-blue flowers with a distinctive white or yellow center. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and produces sword-shaped, dark green leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Iris × arnoldiana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
