Impatiens balsamina var. gracillima

Impatiens balsamina var. gracillima, commonly known as the slender touch-me-not, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, slender stems and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to white. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its leaves are ovate with a slightly hairy surface, and the flowers bloom in clusters at the leaf axils.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is commonly cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
