Isatis sivasica

Isatis sivasica, commonly known as the Sivas woad, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cabbage and mustard. The plant is characterized by its striking blue-violet flowers and serrated leaves. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
