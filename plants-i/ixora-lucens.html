Ixora lucens

Ixora lucens, commonly known as the shining ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant produces clusters of bright orange or red flowers, which are borne on long, branched inflorescences. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and elliptic in shape, with a smooth surface and a slightly glossy texture.

Ixora lucens is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
