Impatiens eryaleia

Impatiens eryaleia, commonly known as the white impatiens, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves and thrives in moist, shaded environments. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
