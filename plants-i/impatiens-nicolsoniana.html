Impatiens nicolsoniana

Impatiens nicolsoniana, commonly known as the Nicolson's impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a glossy surface and a trailing growth habit, making it suitable for hanging baskets or containers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
