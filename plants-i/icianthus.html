Icianthus

Icianthus, commonly known as the golden crown, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that resemble a crown, hence its common name. Icianthus thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for gardens in warm climates.
