Impatiens heterophylla

Impatiens heterophylla, commonly known as the variable impatiens, is a species of flowering plant in the family Balsaminaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its diverse leaf shapes and sizes, which vary depending on environmental conditions. Impatiens heterophylla is valued for its ornamental qualities and is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses worldwide.
