Isometrum nanchuanicum

Isometrum nanchuanicum, commonly known as the Nanchuan bellflower, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous areas and thrives in well-drained soils. Its leaves are lanceolate and often covered with fine hairs.
