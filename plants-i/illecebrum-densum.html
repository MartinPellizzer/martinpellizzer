Illecebrum densum

Illecebrum densum, commonly known as the dense trailing plant, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features small, yellowish to reddish flowers arranged in clusters, often accompanied by narrow, succulent leaves. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its trailing growth habit makes it a popular choice for ground cover in botanical gardens and restoration projects.
