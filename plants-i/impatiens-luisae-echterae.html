Impatiens luisae-echterae

Impatiens luisae-echterae, commonly known as the Luisae-Echterae Impatiens, is a species native to the tropical rainforests of Central America.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red. The leaves are typically ovate and have a smooth, glossy surface. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found growing in the understorey of dense forests.

This plant is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
