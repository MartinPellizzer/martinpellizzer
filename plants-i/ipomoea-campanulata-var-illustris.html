Ipomoea campanulata var. illustris

Ipomoea campanulata var. illustris, commonly known as the illustrated bell vine, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its pendulous, trumpet-shaped flowers.

The flowers are typically bright yellow or orange, with a prominent, showy throat that adds to its ornamental appeal. It is a member of the Convolvulaceae family, known for its climbing habit and heart-shaped leaves. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and hanging baskets.
