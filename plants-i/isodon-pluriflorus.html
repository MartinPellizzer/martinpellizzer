Isodon pluriflorus

Isodon pluriflorus, commonly known as the Chinese skullcap, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and a strong, bitter scent. Its flowers are typically blue to purple, clustered in whorls along the stem.

Isodon pluriflorus is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional Chinese medicine.
