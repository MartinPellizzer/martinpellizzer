Ixora zippeliana

Ixora zippeliana, commonly known as the Zippeliana flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange to red flowers that bloom in dense, branched clusters. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly rough texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Ixora zippeliana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a hedge plant.
