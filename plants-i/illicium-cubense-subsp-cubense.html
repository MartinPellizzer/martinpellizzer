Illicium cubense subsp. cubense

Illicium cubense subsp.

cubense, commonly known as the Cuban star anise, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Caribbean, particularly Cuba. It belongs to the Schisandraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, star-shaped fruits that resemble those of the star anise.

The plant produces small, white flowers followed by dark purple to black berries, which contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its unique flavor and potential medicinal properties, it is of interest to botanists and herbalists alike.
