Impatiens beddomei

Impatiens beddomei, commonly known as the beddome's impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and are borne on slender stems. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and arranged oppositely along the stem. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens beddomei is a popular ornamental plant due to its beauty and ease of cultivation.
