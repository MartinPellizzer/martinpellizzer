Iris zenaidae

Iris zenaidae, commonly known as the Zenaida iris, is a species of flowering plant in the genus Iris.

It is native to the Canary Islands and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in sunny conditions. Its leaves are grass-like, and the flowers bloom in early spring.

The Zenaida iris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
