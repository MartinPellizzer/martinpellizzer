Inga pycnostachya

Inga pycnostachya, commonly known as the dense-stamen inga, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly the Amazon Basin, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Inga pycnostachya is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it an important species in ecological restoration projects.
