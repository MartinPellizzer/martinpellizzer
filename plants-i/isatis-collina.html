Isatis collina

Isatis collina, commonly known as yellow indigo, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes plants like cabbage and mustard. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and palmately lobed leaves. It typically grows in moist soils along stream banks and wet meadows.

Isatis collina is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in dye production.
