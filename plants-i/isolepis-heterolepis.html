Isolepis heterolepis

Isolepis heterolepis, commonly known as the variable spike-rush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetland habitats in North America.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes grass-like plants known as sedges. The plant features grass-like leaves and produces dense, cylindrical clusters of small, spike-like flowers. It thrives in moist to aquatic environments, often found in marshes and along the edges of ponds.
