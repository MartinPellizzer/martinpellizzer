Impatiens filipes

Impatiens filipes, commonly known as the slender touch-me-not, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that can grow up to 1 meter in height, often with a trailing or climbing habit. The plant produces showy, spurred flowers in shades of pink, red, or white, typically blooming from summer to autumn. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface, and it thrives in moist, shady environments.

Impatiens filipes is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade.
