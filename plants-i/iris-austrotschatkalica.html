Iris austrotschatkalica

Iris austrotschatkalica, commonly known as the Tien Shan iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and showy, blue to violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine regions, preferring well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its rhizomatous root system allows it to thrive in harsh mountain environments.

The flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, adding a striking visual element to its natural habitat.
