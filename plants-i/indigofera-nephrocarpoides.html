Indigofera nephrocarpoides

Indigofera nephrocarpoides, commonly known as the kidney-fruit indigo, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces distinctive kidney-shaped fruits, which are a key feature in its identification. It is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, growing in moist, shaded environments.
