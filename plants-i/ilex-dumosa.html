Ilex dumosa

It belongs to the family Aquifoliaceae and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces small, white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. Its leaves are typically elliptical with spiny margins, and it can grow up to 10 meters in height.
