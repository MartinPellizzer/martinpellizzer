Ilex aquifolium var. ciliata-minor

Ilex aquifolium var. ciliata-minor, commonly known as the narrow-leaved holly, is a shrub or small tree native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its elliptical, dark green leaves with finely serrated edges and a pale underside. The plant produces small, bright red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
