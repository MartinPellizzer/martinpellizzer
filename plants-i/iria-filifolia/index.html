Iria Filifolia

Iria filifolia is a striking member of the Rubiaceae family known for its vibrant blue flowers and delicate foliage. Native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for both ornamental gardens and indoor plant collections. The plant's slender stems and cascading flowers add an elegant touch to any setting. With proper care, Iria filifolia can flourish and bring a sense of exotic beauty to any space.
