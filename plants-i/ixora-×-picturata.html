Ixora × picturata

Ixora × picturata, commonly known as the painted ixora, is a hybrid shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant has opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are dark green in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Ixora × picturata is widely cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
