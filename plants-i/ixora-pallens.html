Ixora pallens

Ixora pallens, commonly known as the pale ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, featuring opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, yellowish-white flowers that bloom throughout the year, particularly in the warmer months. Its inflorescence is a panicle, with flowers arranged in a branched pattern.

Ixora pallens is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
