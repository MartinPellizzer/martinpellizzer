Iris taochia

Iris taochia, commonly known as the Chinese iris, is a species of flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight. Its rhizomatous root system allows it to spread and form dense colonies.

Iris taochia is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been cultivated for centuries in traditional gardens.
