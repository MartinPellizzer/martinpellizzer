Ixia erecta

Ixia erecta, commonly known as the fire lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, upright stems that bear clusters of vibrant orange or red flowers, which resemble torches. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically green and covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Ixia erecta is known for its short blooming period, usually in late winter to early spring.
