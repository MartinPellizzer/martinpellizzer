Impatiens manaharensis

Impatiens manaharensis, commonly known as the Manaharensis Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Balsaminaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is occasionally cultivated in horticultural settings.
