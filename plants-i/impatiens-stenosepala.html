Impatiens stenosepala

Impatiens stenosepala, commonly known as the narrow-sepaled impatiens, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink and white. The leaves are ovate to elliptic in shape, with a smooth margin and a slightly hairy surface. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
