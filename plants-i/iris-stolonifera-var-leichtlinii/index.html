Iris Stolonifera Var. Leichtlinii

Blue flag iris, scientifically known as Iris stolonifera var. leichtlinii, is a striking wetland plant native to the eastern United States. This variety of the blue flag iris is characterized by its vibrant blue to purple flowers and grass-like leaves that thrive in moist environments. It plays an important role in wetland ecosystems by stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species. The plant is often found in marshes, pond edges, and along streams where it contributes to biodiversity. Its beauty and ecological value make it a popular choice for wetland restoration projects.
