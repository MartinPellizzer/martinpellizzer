Iris × rosilutea

Iris × rosilutea, commonly known as the Rosilutea Iris, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Iris rosenfeldii and Iris sibirica.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant with striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in early summer. The plant features upright, sword-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. Its flowers are usually lavender to pink in color, with a distinctive white or pale yellow center.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
