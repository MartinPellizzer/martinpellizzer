Impatiens siculifera var. porphyrea

Impatiens siculifera var. porphyrea, commonly known as the purple-stemmed impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple stems and heart-shaped, glossy leaves. The plant produces clusters of showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking colorful, shade-tolerant plants.
