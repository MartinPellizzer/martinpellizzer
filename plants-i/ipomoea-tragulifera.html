Ipomoea tragulifera

Ipomoea tragulifera, commonly known as the African morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep purple. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces showy blooms that open in the early morning. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Ipomoea tragulifera is also known for its rapid growth and ability to climb, making it a popular choice for vertical gardening.
