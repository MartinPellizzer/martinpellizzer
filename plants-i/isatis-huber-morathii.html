Isatis huber-morathii

Isatis huber-morathii, commonly known as the Morath's woad, is a species of flowering plant in the family Brassicaceae.

It is native to Central Asia and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and blue to violet flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits containing several seeds. It is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments.
