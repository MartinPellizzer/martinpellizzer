Impatiens acmanthera

Impatiens acmanthera, commonly known as the red impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Balsaminaceae and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves and produces capsule fruits that release seeds when mature. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for gardeners in temperate climates.

Its ornamental value has led to its cultivation in various horticultural settings worldwide.
