Indigofera oxyrachis

Indigofera oxyrachis, commonly known as the scarlet pimpernel, is a herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, showy flowers that are typically pink or red, which change color as the plant matures. The plant has trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

Indigofera oxyrachis is also known for its rapid growth and ability to reseed itself, making it a common weed in many regions.
