Ipomoea Gracilisepala Var. Lyciifolia

Ipomoea gracilisepala var. lyciifolia is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the southern United States. It is known for its striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall. This variety is a striking variant of the common morning glory, distinguished by its unique leaf shape and coloration. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun. Due to its beauty and rarity, it is often sought after by horticulturists and plant enthusiasts alike.
