Iris iberica subsp. elegantissima

Iris iberica subsp.

elegantissima, commonly known as the elegant iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Iberian Peninsula. It features upright, slender stems that bear showy, violet-blue flowers with a distinctive white stripe on the lower petal.

The plant has lance-shaped, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
