Ilex × attenuata

Ilex × attenuata, commonly known as the Chinese holly, is a hybrid evergreen shrub or small tree native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and small, dark green leaves that are typically oval in shape. The plant produces bright red berries that are toxic to humans and animals. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its hardy nature and winter interest make it a valuable addition to gardens and landscapes.
