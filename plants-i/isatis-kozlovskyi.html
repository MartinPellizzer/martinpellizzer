Isatis kozlovskyi

Isatis kozlovskyi, commonly known as Kozlovsky's woad, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cabbage and mustard. The plant is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. It produces round, smooth-seeded pods that contain the seeds, which are used for propagation.

This species is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is valued for its ornamental and potential medicinal properties.
