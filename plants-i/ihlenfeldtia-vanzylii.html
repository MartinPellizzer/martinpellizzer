Ihlenfeldtia vanzylii

Ihlenfeldtia vanzylii, commonly known as the Zambesi orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically blooms in late summer, producing clusters of pale yellow to orange flowers with a distinctive, intricate pattern. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
