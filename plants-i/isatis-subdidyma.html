Isatis subdidyma

Isatis subdidyma, commonly known as the yellow rocket, is a herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cabbage and mustard. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and pinnately divided leaves. It typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

Isatis subdidyma is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
