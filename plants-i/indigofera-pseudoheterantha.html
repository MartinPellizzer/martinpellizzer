Indigofera pseudoheterantha

Indigofera pseudoheterantha, commonly known as the slender indigo.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is native to tropical regions of Africa. The plant is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. It produces elongated pods that contain small, kidney-shaped seeds.

This species is often found in disturbed habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential ecological benefits.
