Ipomoea triloba f. ramonii

Ipomoea triloba f. ramonii, commonly known as the Ramonii Morning Glory, is a flowering plant belonging to the Convolvulaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep blue or purple. The plant is native to Central America and thrives in warm, humid climates. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.
