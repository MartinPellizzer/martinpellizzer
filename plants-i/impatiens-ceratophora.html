Impatiens ceratophora

Impatiens ceratophora, commonly known as the horned impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, spurred flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows as an annual herb, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its foliage consists of ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Impatiens ceratophora thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or partially shaded environments.
