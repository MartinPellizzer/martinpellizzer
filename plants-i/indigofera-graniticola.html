Indigofera graniticola

Indigofera graniticola, commonly known as the granite indigo, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive trifoliate leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils, often found in arid or semi-arid environments.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it an important species in soil enrichment and ecological restoration.
