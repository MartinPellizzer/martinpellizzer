Ixora coccinea

Ixora coccinea, commonly known as the flame flower or Chinese hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or orange flowers, which are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. The plant has simple, opposite, and often hairy leaves that are elliptical in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Ixora coccinea is widely cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
