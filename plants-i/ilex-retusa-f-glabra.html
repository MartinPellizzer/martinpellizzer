Ilex retusa f. glabra

Ilex retusa f. glabra, commonly known as the smooth-leaved holly, is a variant of the holly plant characterized by its smooth, glabrous foliage.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia, particularly China. The leaves are typically elliptical, glossy, and have a leathery texture, while the small white flowers appear in clusters. It produces red berries that are edible for birds but toxic to humans.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature in gardens.
