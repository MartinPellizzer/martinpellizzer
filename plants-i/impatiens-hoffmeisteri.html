Impatiens hoffmeisteri

Impatiens hoffmeisteri, commonly known as the Hoffmeister's impatiens, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically come in shades of pink, white, and red. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves and grows as an upright herbaceous perennial. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens hoffmeisteri is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
