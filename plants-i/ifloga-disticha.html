Ifloga disticha

Ifloga disticha, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, zigzagging stems that give it its common name. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ifloga disticha is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its distinctive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
