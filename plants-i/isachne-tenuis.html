Isachne tenuis

Isachne tenuis, commonly known as slender isachne, is a grass species native to various regions of Asia, including China, Japan, and the Himalayas.

It is a perennial plant characterized by its fine, grass-like leaves and compact growth habit. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and stream banks. Its inflorescence consists of a single spikelet, which is often subtended by a leaf-like bract.

Isachne tenuis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade.
