Iris korolkowii var. concolor

Iris korolkowii var. concolor, commonly known as the Siberian iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It features grass-like, sword-shaped leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. The plant produces showy, violet-blue flowers with a distinctive three-parted lip, blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.
