Iris biglumis

Iris biglumis, commonly known as the broadleaf iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It features upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. The plant produces showy blue to purple flowers with three upright petals and three drooping sepals. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks.

The botanical structure of Iris biglumis is characterized by its rhizomatous root system and its ability to spread vegetatively.
