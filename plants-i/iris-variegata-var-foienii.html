Iris variegata var. foienii

Iris variegata var. foienii, commonly known as the Foienii iris, is a variant of the variegated iris native to specific regions of Europe.

This plant is characterized by its strikingly patterned leaves and showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight. The leaves are typically variegated with white or cream streaks, adding visual interest to gardens.

It is a herbaceous perennial that is often cultivated for its ornamental value and easy maintenance.
