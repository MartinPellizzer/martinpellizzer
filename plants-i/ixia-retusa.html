Ixia retusa

Ixia retusa, commonly known as the slender oxalis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and has narrow, grass-like leaves. It produces bright, showy blooms that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Ixia retusa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
