Impatiens linearis var. petiolata

Impatiens linearis var. petiolata, commonly known as the petiolate impatiens, is a herbaceous perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It features slender, linear leaves with distinct petioles, giving it a unique appearance compared to other impatiens species. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking shade-tolerant, flowering plants.
