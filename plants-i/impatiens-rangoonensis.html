Impatiens rangoonensis

Impatiens rangoonensis, commonly known as the Rangoon impatiens, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, spurred flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Impatiens rangoonensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
