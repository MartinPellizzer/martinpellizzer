Ilex sinica

Ilex sinica, commonly known as Chinese holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically oval in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. Ilex sinica is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes due to its dense foliage and year-round appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a versatile plant for various climates.
