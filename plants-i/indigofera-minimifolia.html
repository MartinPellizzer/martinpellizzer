Indigofera minimifolia

Indigofera minimifolia, commonly known as the dwarf indigo.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features small, trifoliate leaves and produces clusters of small, pale blue to white flowers. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine.

Its botanical characteristics make it well-suited for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
