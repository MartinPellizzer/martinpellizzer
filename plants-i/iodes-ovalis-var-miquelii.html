Iodes ovalis var. miquelii

Iodes ovalis var. miquelii, commonly known as the Miquel's oval orchid, is a rare and delicate orchid species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It features slender, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest undergrowth. Its flowers are characterized by a distinctive lip that is often marked with intricate patterns.

Due to habitat loss, this variety is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
