Inulaster

Inulaster, commonly known as the starflower, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive composite flower heads, which consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in temperate regions and is often found in meadows, woodlands, and disturbed soils. Inulaster species are known for their ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and their resilience to environmental stress.
