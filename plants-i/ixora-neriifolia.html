Ixora neriifolia

Ixora neriifolia, commonly known as the black-leafed ixora, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically orange or red when mature. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Ixora neriifolia is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
