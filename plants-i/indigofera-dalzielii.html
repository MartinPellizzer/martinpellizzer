Indigofera dalzielii

Indigofera dalzielii, commonly known as Dalziel's indigo, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters, with compound leaves and small, stipulate stipules. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or savannah habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and traditional uses, it is cultivated in some regions for horticultural purposes.
