Indocourtoisia assimilis

Indocourtoisia assimilis, commonly known as the Indian courtoisia, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of India.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small yellow flowers arranged in clusters. It is adapted to survive in dry climates with minimal water requirements.

Its botanical structure supports its role in stabilizing soil in semi-arid environments.
