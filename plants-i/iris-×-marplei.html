Iris × marplei

Iris × marplei, commonly known as the Marple iris, is a hybrid perennial plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, sturdy stems and showy, iris-like flowers that come in a variety of colors, including blue, purple, and white. The plant typically grows to a height of 2 to 3 feet and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun to partial shade. Its foliage consists of broad, sword-shaped leaves that are often a deep green.

The Marple iris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking blooms and hardy nature.
