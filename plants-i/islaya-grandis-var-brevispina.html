Islaya grandis var. brevispina

Islaya grandis var. brevispina, commonly known as the short-spined Islaya, is a shrub native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It features lance-shaped leaves with a smooth upper surface and a slightly hairy underside. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Its compact growth habit and unique spiny structures distinguish it from other members of the genus.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
