Iris × agatha

Iris × agatha, commonly known as the Agatha iris, is a hybrid perennial plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking violet-blue flowers with a distinctive yellow beard in the center. The plant typically grows to a height of 2 to 3 feet and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage consists of sword-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and its ability to bloom reliably in well-drained soil.
