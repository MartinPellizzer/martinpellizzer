Ilysanthes succosa

Ilysanthes succosa, commonly known as the desert rose, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of South America.

It features fleshy, water-storing leaves that help it survive in dry environments. The plant produces showy, pink or red flowers that bloom during the warmer months. Its root system is deep and extensive, allowing it to access groundwater.

Ilysanthes succosa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements.
