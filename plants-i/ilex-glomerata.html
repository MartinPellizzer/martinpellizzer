Ilex glomerata

Ilex glomerata, commonly known as the California holly, is a evergreen shrub or small tree native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage and small, dark green leaves that remain throughout the year. The plant produces clusters of bright red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. Ilex glomerata typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for landscaping in coastal and Mediterranean climates.
