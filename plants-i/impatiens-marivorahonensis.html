Impatiens marivorahonensis

Impatiens marivorahonensis, commonly known as the Madagascar water impatiens, is a herbaceous perennial native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found near streams or in humid forest understories.

Its unique floral structure and habitat preferences make it a notable species within the Impatiens genus.
