Ipsea thailandica

Ipsea thailandica, commonly known as the Thai ipsea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand.

It belongs to the family Gesneriaceae and is characterized by its tubular flowers, which are typically brightly colored. The plant is evergreen, with glossy, elliptical leaves that are often arranged in opposite pairs. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
