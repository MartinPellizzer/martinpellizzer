Illicium petelotii

Illicium petelotii, commonly known as the Petelot's star anise, is a member of the Schisandraceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen shrub characterized by its fragrant, star-shaped fruits that resemble those of the star anise. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers and has dark green, leathery leaves. Its fruits are used in traditional medicine and have a complex aroma similar to star anise.

Illicium petelotii is valued for its ornamental qualities and its potential medicinal properties.
