Ipomoea supersticiosa

It is a member of the Convolvulaceae family and is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as swamps and woodlands. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often have a velvety texture.
