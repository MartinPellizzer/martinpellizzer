Indocalamus guangdongensis var. mollis

Indocalamus guangdongensis var. mollis, commonly known as the soft bamboo, is a variant of the Guangdong bamboo species native to southern China.

It is characterized by its slender, flexible stems and a dense, bushy growth habit. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and slightly curled at the edges. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in traditional Chinese horticulture.
