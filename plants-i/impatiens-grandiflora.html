Impatiens grandiflora

Impatiens grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered impatiens, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, spurred flowers. The plant produces vibrant blooms in shades of red, pink, and white, typically in clusters at the tips of the stems. Its leaves are ovate to elliptical, with a glossy surface and a slightly serrated edge.

Impatiens grandiflora thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
