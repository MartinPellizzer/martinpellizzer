Icacina claessensii

Icacina claessensii, commonly known as the Java hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. Its showy flowers are typically red or orange with five petals and a prominent yellow center.

Icacina claessensii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
