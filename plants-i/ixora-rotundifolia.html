Ixora rotundifolia

Ixora rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leaf ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of bright red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. The leaves are typically rounded and glossy, providing a striking contrast to the vibrant floral displays. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sunlight.

This plant is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and as a hedge due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
