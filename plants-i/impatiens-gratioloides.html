Impatiens gratioloides

Impatiens gratioloides, commonly known as the Madagascar impatiens, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features showy, spurred flowers that come in a range of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. Its leaves are ovate and often have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
