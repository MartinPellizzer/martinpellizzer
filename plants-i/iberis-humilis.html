Iberis humilis

Iberis humilis, commonly known as the candytuft, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features dense clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has hairy, gray-green leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Iberis humilis is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
