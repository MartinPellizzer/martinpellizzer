Impatiens helferi

Impatiens helferi, commonly known as the Helfer's impatiens, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are usually pink or red in color, with five petals and a distinctive spur. Its foliage consists of heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are arranged oppositely along the stems.

Impatiens helferi thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
