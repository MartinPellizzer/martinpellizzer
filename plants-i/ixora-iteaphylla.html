Ixora iteaphylla

Ixora iteaphylla, commonly known as the flame tree or red flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, bright red or orange-red inflorescences that bloom in clusters, creating a striking visual display. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are opposite in arrangement and are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Ixora iteaphylla is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
