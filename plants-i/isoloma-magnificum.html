Isoloma magnificum

Isoloma magnificum, commonly known as the royal flame lily, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from deep red to orange, often with intricate patterns. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green, providing a striking contrast to its colorful blooms. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Isoloma magnificum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
