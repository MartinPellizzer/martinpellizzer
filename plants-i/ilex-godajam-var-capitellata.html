Ilex godajam var. capitellata

Ilex godajam var. capitellata, commonly known as the small-leaf holly, is a variant of the holly genus native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in shaded to semi-shaded environments and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature in garden settings.
