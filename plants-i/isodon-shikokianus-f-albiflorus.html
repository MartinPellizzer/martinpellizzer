Isodon shikokianus f. albiflorus

Isodon shikokianus f. albiflorus, commonly known as the white-flowered isodon, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a delicate and refined appearance. Its leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic beauty.
