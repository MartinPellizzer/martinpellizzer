Impatiens laticornis

Impatiens laticornis, commonly known as the broadhorn impatiens, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptic, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
