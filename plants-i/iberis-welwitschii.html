Iberis welwitschii

Iberis welwitschii, commonly known as the Namib Desert candytuft, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Namibia and Angola.

It is a low-growing shrub that produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters. The plant has succulent leaves that help it retain water in its harsh, dry environment. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand high temperatures and limited rainfall.

Iberis welwitschii plays a role in stabilizing sandy soils and providing habitat for local wildlife.
