Indigofera asperifolia f. longepedunculata

Indigofera asperifolia f. longepedunculata, commonly known as the long pedunculate indigofera, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its woody stems and compound leaves with small, ovate leaflets. The plant produces clusters of small, pea-like flowers that are typically violet or blue in color. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

This variety is noted for its distinctive elongated peduncles, which give it its specific epithet.
