Ixeridium dentatum subsp. dentatum

Ixeridium dentatum subsp.

dentatum, commonly known as the serrated pincushion, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, spiny heads of yellow flowers.
