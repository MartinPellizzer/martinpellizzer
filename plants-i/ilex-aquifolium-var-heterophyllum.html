Ilex aquifolium var. heterophyllum

Ilex aquifolium var. heterophyllum, commonly known as the European holly with variegated leaves, is a variant of the common holly.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green foliage with striking white or yellow variegation. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible for birds but toxic to humans. It is evergreen, retaining its leaves throughout the year, and thrives in well-drained soil in full sun to partial shade.

This variety is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and landscapes.
