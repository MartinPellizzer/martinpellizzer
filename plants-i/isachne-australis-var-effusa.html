Isachne australis var. effusa

Isachne australis var. effusa, commonly known as the slender flat sedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to Australia.

It typically grows in wet or seasonally flooded areas, such as marshes and swampy regions. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is characterized by its distinctive, flat, ribbon-like leaves that are often densely arranged.

This variant is well adapted to aquatic or semi-aquatic environments, contributing to the ecological diversity of its habitat.
