Ixora linderi

Ixora linderi, commonly known as the Linder's flame bush, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a striking visual appeal to its surroundings. The plant has small, leathery leaves that are arranged in opposite pairs along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Ixora linderi is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
