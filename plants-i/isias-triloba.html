Isias triloba

Isias triloba, commonly known as the three-lobed isias, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to specific regions of South America.

It features opposite, ovate leaves with three distinct lobes, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, which bloom in the spring. Its roots are fibrous and often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Isias triloba thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded to partially shaded environments.
