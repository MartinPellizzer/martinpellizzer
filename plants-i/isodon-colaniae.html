Isodon colaniae

Isodon colaniae, commonly known as the mountain hibiscus, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy, erect stems and showy yellow flowers. Isodon colaniae is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
