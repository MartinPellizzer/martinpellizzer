Impatiens dindigulensis

Impatiens dindigulensis, commonly known as Dindigul Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its leaves are ovate with a smooth texture, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
