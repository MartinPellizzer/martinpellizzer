Ixora wallichii

Ixora wallichii, commonly known as the flame flower or red ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of bright red or orange-red flowers that bloom throughout the year, especially during the warmer months. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a leathery texture with a glossy surface.

Ixora wallichii is widely cultivated for its ornamental value and is often used in landscaping and floral arrangements.
