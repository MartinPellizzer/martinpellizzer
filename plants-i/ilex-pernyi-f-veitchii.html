Ilex pernyi f. veitchii

Ilex pernyi f. veitchii, commonly known as the Veitch's holly, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features dense, dark green, leathery leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers in the spring, followed by red berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value make it suitable for both landscape and container cultivation.
