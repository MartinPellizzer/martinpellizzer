Ionopsis tenera

Ionopsis tenera, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its hairy, velvety leaves. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a red throat. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and abandoned fields.

Ionopsis tenera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
