Iresine scandens

Iresine scandens, commonly known as redroot ironweed, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red stems and heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Iresine scandens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking foliage.
