Indigofera obermeijerae

Indigofera obermeijerae, commonly known as the Mexican indigo plant, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its distinctive blue flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, which makes it valuable in ecological restoration projects. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Its ornamental value has led to its cultivation in gardens and landscapes.
