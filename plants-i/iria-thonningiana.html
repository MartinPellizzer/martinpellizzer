Iria thonningiana

Iria thonningiana, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often found in open woodlands and grasslands. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and usually have a rough texture.

Iria thonningiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
