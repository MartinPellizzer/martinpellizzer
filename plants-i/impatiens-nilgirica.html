Impatiens nilgirica

Impatiens nilgirica, commonly known as the Nilgiri Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and grows in moist, shaded environments. It is a member of the Balsaminaceae family and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens nilgirica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
