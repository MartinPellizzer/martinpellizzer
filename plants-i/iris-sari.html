Iris sari

Iris sari, commonly known as the Persian iris, is a flowering plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, including parts of Iran and Afghanistan. The plant features tall, slender stems that bear showy, violet-blue flowers with three upright petals and three drooping sepals. Its rhizomatous root system allows it to thrive in well-drained, sandy soils.

The Persian iris is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been cultivated for centuries in traditional gardens.
