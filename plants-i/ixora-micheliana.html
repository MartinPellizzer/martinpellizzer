Ixora micheliana

Ixora micheliana, commonly known as the red flame ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height, featuring glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of bright red, tubular flowers that bloom throughout the year, attracting pollinators such as birds and insects. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sunlight.

Ixora micheliana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in landscaping and garden design.
