Ilex szechwanensis f. puberula

Ilex szechwanensis f. puberula, commonly known as the Sichuan holly, is a shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, glossy dark green leaves that are covered with fine, soft hairs, giving it a velvety texture. The plant produces small, bright red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in garden settings.
