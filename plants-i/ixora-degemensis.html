Ixora degemensis

Ixora degemensis, commonly known as the Madagascar flame tree, is a species of flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree that typically grows up to 6 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its showy, clustered red or orange-red flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate, with a glossy green surface.

Ixora degemensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.
