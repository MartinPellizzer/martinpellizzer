Iresine vermicularis

Iresine vermicularis, commonly known as redroot ironweed, is a herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It features striking, velvety red foliage and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has a fibrous root system and is often found in moist, wooded areas. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate in shape and have a distinctive red underside.

Iresine vermicularis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
