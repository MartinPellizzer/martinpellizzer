Ionidium linifolium

Ionidium linifolium, commonly known as the silverleaf fern, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that give it a graceful appearance. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread and form dense colonies. Its leaves are typically grayish-green, covered in fine, silvery hairs that provide a unique visual texture.

Ionidium linifolium prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid tropical forests.
