Iris boltoniana

Iris boltoniana, commonly known as the Bolton's iris, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 feet. The plant features striking blue to purple flowers with three upright petals and three drooping sepals. Its leaves are sword-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette.

Iris boltoniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
