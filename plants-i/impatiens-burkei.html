Impatiens burkei

Impatiens burkei, commonly known as the Burkill's impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and a compact growth habit, making it suitable for shaded garden environments. It prefers moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Impatiens burkei is often cultivated for its ornamental value in horticulture.
