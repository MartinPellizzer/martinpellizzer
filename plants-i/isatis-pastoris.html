Isatis pastoris

Isatis pastoris, commonly known as the yellow rocket, is a biennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cabbages and mustard. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in gardens and along roadsides.

Isatis pastoris is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
