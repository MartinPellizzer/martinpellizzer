Iris bungei

Iris bungei, commonly known as the Chinese flag iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It features striking blue to violet flowers with a distinctive white or pale yellow stripe running down the center. The plant has sword-shaped, grass-like leaves that are typically dark green and arranged in a fan-like pattern. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Iris bungei is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
