Indigofera hendecaphylla var. angustata

Indigofera hendecaphylla var. angustata, commonly known as narrow-leaf indigo, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its slender, angular leaves and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. The plant produces cylindrical pods that contain several seeds, and it is often found in open woodland or grassland habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
