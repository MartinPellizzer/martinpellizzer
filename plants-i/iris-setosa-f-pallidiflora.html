Iris setosa f. pallidiflora

Iris setosa f. pallidiflora, commonly known as the pale-flowered iris, is a flowering plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It is a subspecies of Iris setosa, characterized by its pale blue to white flowers with a distinctive yellowish throat. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in temperate regions. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy blooms in early to mid-summer.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
