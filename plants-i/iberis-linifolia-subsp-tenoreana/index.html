Iberis Linifolia Subsp. Tenoreana

Iberis linifolia subsp. tenoreana is a rare and hardy plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is known for its delicate white flowers and compact growth habit. This subspecies thrives in rocky soils and full sun exposure. It is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance requirements. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in horticultural circles.
