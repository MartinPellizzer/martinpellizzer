Indigofera tengyuehensis

Indigofera tengyuehensis, commonly known as the Yunnan indigo plant, is a flowering plant native to southern China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant is notable for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, which makes it beneficial for soil enrichment.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and mountainous regions.
