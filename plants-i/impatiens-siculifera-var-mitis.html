Impatiens siculifera var. mitis

Impatiens siculifera var. mitis, commonly known as the slender touch-me-not, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink. The plant features slender, erect stems and heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

This variety is noted for its rapid growth and ability to spread through seeds and rhizomes.
