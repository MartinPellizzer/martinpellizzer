Ichthyosma wehdemannii

Ichthyosma wehdemannii, commonly known as the African starfish or starfish plant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that resemble the appearance of a starfish, hence its common name. The plant typically grows as a shrub with woody stems and produces small, clustered leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

The flowers are often visited by pollinators such as bees and butterflies, contributing to its ecological significance.
