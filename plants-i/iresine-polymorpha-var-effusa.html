Iresine polymorpha var. effusa

Iresine polymorpha var. effusa, commonly known as redleaf iron plant, is a strikingly colorful plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features vibrant, velvety leaves that range from deep red to purple, often with contrasting green veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardens and indoor plant collections.
