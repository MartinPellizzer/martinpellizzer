Ixora salicifolia var. variegata

Ixora salicifolia var. variegata, commonly known as the variegated flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright orange to red flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to landscapes. The leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a variegated pattern of green and white, enhancing its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This plant is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
