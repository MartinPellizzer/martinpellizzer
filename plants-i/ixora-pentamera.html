Ixora pentamera

Ixora pentamera, commonly known as the red flame shrub, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant red flowers. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that bloom throughout the year in tropical climates. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a glossy green appearance.

Ixora pentamera is also valued for its ability to thrive in well-drained soils and its tolerance to partial shade.
