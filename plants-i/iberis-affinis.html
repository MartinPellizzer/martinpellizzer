Iberis affinis

Iberis affinis, commonly known as the Persian shield, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and small, shield-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.

Iberis affinis prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
