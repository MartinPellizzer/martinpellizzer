Impatiens royleana

Impatiens royleana, commonly known as the Himalayan impatiens, is a species of flowering plant native to the eastern Himalayas.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features striking, showy flowers that are usually pink or red, with five petals and a distinctive spur. Its leaves are ovate and slightly hairy, contributing to its ornamental value.

Impatiens royleana is valued in horticulture for its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various garden conditions.
