Ilex Hanceana Var. Lohfauensis

Chinese holly, scientifically known as Ilex hanceana var. lohfauensis, is a striking evergreen shrub native to southern China. It is prized for its glossy dark green leaves and vibrant red berries that persist through winter. This plant thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Its hardy nature and ability to tolerate urban conditions have made it a favored species in landscaping. With its elegant appearance and ecological benefits, the Chinese holly remains a cherished addition to gardens and natural habitats.
