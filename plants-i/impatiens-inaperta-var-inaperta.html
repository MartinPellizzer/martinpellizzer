Impatiens inaperta var. inaperta

Impatiens inaperta var. inaperta, commonly known as the slender impatiens, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its delicate, upright stems and heart-shaped leaves. The flowers are typically pale pink to white, with five petals and a spurred structure at the base. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its beauty and ease of care.
