Isachne mayocoensis

Isachne mayocoensis, commonly known as the Mayoco grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, wiry stems. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often with a slightly rough texture.

Isachne mayocoensis typically produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.
