Impatiens lecomtei

Impatiens lecomtei, commonly known as Lecomte's impatiens, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and a compact growth habit, making it suitable for both garden beds and container planting. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, often found in forest understories.

Impatiens lecomtei is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to add color to shaded garden spaces.
