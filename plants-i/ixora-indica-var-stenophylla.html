Ixora indica var. stenophylla

Ixora indica var. stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaved flame bush, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, bright orange to red inflorescences that bloom throughout the year. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are typically arranged in whorls around the stems. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sunlight.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a hedge plant.
