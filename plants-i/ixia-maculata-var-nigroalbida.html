Ixia maculata var. nigroalbida

Ixia maculata var. nigroalbida, commonly known as the spotted flame flower, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically orange-red with contrasting black and white markings. The plant produces bright yellow stamens that add to its visual appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is heightened by its unique coloration and vibrant blooming period in late winter to early spring.
