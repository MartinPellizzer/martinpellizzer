Ixora minutiflora

Ixora minutiflora, commonly known as the dwarf flame bush, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces clusters of small, bright red or orange-red flowers, which are arranged in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and elliptical in shape, with a glossy surface.

Ixora minutiflora is well adapted to tropical climates and thrives in well-drained soil.
