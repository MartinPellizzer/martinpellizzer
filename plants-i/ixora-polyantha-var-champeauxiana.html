Ixora polyantha var. champeauxiana

Ixora polyantha var. champeauxiana, commonly known as the Champeauxiana ixora, is a compact shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features showy, clustered flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom throughout the year. The plant has small, leathery leaves that are glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant flowers. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant due to its compact size and continuous flowering.
