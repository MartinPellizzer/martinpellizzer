Ixia polystachya var. bicolorata

Ixia polystachya var. bicolorata, commonly known as the rainbow lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in a range of colors, including red, orange, and yellow, often in a gradient pattern. The plant produces multiple flower clusters on long, branched stems, creating a dramatic display. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its unique coloration and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking visual interest.
