Impatiens andohahelae

It belongs to the Balsaminaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows as an upright herb with hairy stems and alternate, ovate leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.
