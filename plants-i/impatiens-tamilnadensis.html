Impatiens tamilnadensis

Impatiens tamilnadensis, commonly known as the Tamil Nadu impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the family Balsaminaceae and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface, and its stems are often hairy as well. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens tamilnadensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
