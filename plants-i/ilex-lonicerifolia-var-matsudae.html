Ilex lonicerifolia var. matsudae

Ilex lonicerifolia var. matsudae, commonly known as the Japanese holly, is a variant of the evergreen holly species native to Japan.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically ovate to elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

This variety is valued for its hardiness and ability to maintain its foliage year-round.
