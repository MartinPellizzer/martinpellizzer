Ixia anemoniflora

Ixia anemoniflora, commonly known as the yellow star flower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters, with linear, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are borne on tall, slender stems and are often used in ornamental gardening for their vibrant color and unique appearance.

Ixia anemoniflora prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
