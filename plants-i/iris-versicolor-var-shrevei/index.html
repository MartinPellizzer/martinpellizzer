Iris Versicolor Var. Shrevei

Iris versicolor var. shrevei is a rare and striking variant of the blue flag iris known for its unique floral characteristics. Native to specific regions of the southeastern United States, this plant thrives in wetland environments and swampy woodlands. Its flowers display a blend of blue and white hues, creating a visually captivating display during the spring season. Due to habitat loss and environmental changes, this variety faces increasing threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving its beauty and ecological role in native ecosystems.
