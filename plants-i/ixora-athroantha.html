Ixora athroantha

Ixora athroantha, commonly known as the red ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, upright growth habit and clusters of vibrant red flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Ixora athroantha is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and long-lasting blooms.
