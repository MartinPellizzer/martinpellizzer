Ixora schottiana

Ixora schottiana, commonly known as the Schott's flame bush, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Ixora schottiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
