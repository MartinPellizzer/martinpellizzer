Impatiens maguanensis

Impatiens maguanensis, commonly known as the Maguan impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It belongs to the family Balsaminaceae and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of tropical forests.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
