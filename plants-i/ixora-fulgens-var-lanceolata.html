Ixora fulgens var. lanceolata

Ixora fulgens var. lanceolata, commonly known as the flame ixora, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features showy, clustered inflorescences of bright orange-red flowers that bloom throughout the year. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth, glossy surface. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
