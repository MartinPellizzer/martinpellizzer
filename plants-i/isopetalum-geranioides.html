Isopetalum geranioides

Isopetalum geranioides, commonly known as the pink geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy pink or red flowers. The plant features palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, and its stems are often hairy. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is adapted to well-drained soils.

Isopetalum geranioides is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
