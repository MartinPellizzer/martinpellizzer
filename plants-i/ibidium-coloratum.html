Ibidium coloratum

Ibidium coloratum, commonly known as the pink ghost orchid, is a rare epiphytic orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its striking pinkish-white flowers and unique, ghost-like appearance. The plant typically grows on the bark of trees in moist, shaded forest habitats. It has a delicate root system and requires specific environmental conditions to thrive.

Due to its rarity and habitat specificity, it is considered endangered and is protected in many regions.
