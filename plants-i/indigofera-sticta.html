Indigofera sticta

Indigofera sticta, commonly known as the slender indigo, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant produces pods that contain seeds, and it is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.
