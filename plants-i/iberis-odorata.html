Iberis odorata

Iberis odorata, commonly known as sweet rocket, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and is often used in rock gardens or as a ground cover. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, blooming from spring to early summer.

Iberis odorata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
