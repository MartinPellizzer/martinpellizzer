Ixora scortechinii

Ixora scortechinii, commonly known as the red ixora, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bright red flowers that bloom in dense clusters, giving it a striking visual appearance. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a slightly rough texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Ixora scortechinii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant floral display.
