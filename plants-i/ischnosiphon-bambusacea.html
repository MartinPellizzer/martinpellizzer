Ischnosiphon bambusacea

Ischnosiphon bambusacea, commonly known as the bamboo-like orchid, is a unique member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, bamboo-like stems and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. Native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are typically long and narrow, resembling those of true bamboo.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
