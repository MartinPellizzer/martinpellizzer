Ixora stenothyrsa

Ixora stenothyrsa, commonly known as the narrow-throat ixora, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bright red or orange inflorescences, which are composed of numerous small, tubular flowers. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Ixora stenothyrsa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
