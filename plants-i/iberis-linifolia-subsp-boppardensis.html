Iberis linifolia subsp. boppardensis

Iberis linifolia subsp.

boppardensis, commonly known as the Boppardensis candytuft, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Europe. It features dense clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in early spring.

The plant has finely divided, silvery-gray foliage that adds ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

This subspecies is valued for its hardiness and ability to attract pollinators.
