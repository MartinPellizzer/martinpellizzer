Ilex theezans var. augusti

Ilex theezans var. augusti, commonly known as the Augustus holly, is a variant of the evergreen holly species native to China.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, red berries that appear in the winter. The plant is typically shrub-like, with a dense, compact growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for ornamental landscaping.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a hedge plant.
