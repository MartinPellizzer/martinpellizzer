Ionopsis costaricensis

Ionopsis costaricensis, commonly known as the Costa Rican ionopsis, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Central America, particularly Costa Rica and Panama. The plant features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
