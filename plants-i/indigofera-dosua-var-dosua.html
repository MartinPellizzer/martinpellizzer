Indigofera dosua var. dosua

Indigofera dosua var. dosua, commonly known as African indigo, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical Africa and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, violet-blue flowers. The plant is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to several meters in height. It is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it valuable in soil enrichment.

Its seeds are often used in traditional medicine and as a source of natural dyes.
