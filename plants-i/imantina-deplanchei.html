Imantina deplanchei

Imantina deplanchei, commonly known as the desert star.

It is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom during the cooler months. It has a succulent stem and leaves that are adapted to store water, allowing it to survive in harsh, dry environments.

Imantina deplanchei is often found growing in rocky soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
