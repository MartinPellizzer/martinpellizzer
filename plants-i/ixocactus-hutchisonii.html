Ixocactus hutchisonii

Ixocactus hutchisonii, commonly known as the Mexican star cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a flowering cactus belonging to the Cactaceae family, characterized by its spiny stems and vibrant flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, thriving in well-drained soil. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing in clusters during the spring or summer.

Ixocactus hutchisonii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
