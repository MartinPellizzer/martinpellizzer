Ipomoea pes-tigridis var. strigosa

Ipomoea pes-tigridis var. strigosa, commonly known as the zigzag morning glory, is a flowering vine native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its distinctive, zigzagging stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its rapid growth habit makes it suitable for trellises or as a ground cover in gardens.
