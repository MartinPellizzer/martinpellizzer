Icacina senegalensis

Icacina senegalensis, commonly known as African satinwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and opposite, elliptical leaves. The tree is valued for its hard, durable wood and has traditional medicinal uses in various African cultures.
