Impatiens semiverticillata

Impatiens semiverticillata, commonly known as the spiral impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it a unique and ornamental appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or white, with five petals and a prominent spur. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Impatiens semiverticillata is valued in horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
