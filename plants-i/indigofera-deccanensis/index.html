Indigofera Deccanensis

Indigofera deccanensis is a flowering plant native to the Indian subcontinent. It belongs to the legume family and is known for its striking blue flowers. This species is often found in dry, rocky areas and is adapted to arid conditions. Its leaves are trifoliate, and it has a deep root system that helps it survive in poor soil. The plant has traditional uses in medicine and has also been studied for its potential in ecological restoration.
