Ixora volkensii var. lanceolata

Ixora volkensii var. lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceolate ixora, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, bright red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. The leaves are typically lance-shaped, hence the variety name, and are arranged oppositely along the stems. This plant thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and ability to withstand various environmental conditions.
