Ixora krewanhensis

Ixora krewanhensis, commonly known as the flame-of-the_forest, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bright orange to red flowers, which bloom in dense clusters throughout the year. The plant has small, leathery leaves that are opposite in arrangement and often have a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Ixora krewanhensis is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
