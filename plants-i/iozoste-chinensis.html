Iozoste chinensis

Iozoste chinensis, commonly known as Chinese ironwood, is a small evergreen shrub native to China.

It belongs to the family Theaceae and is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves and showy, fragrant yellow flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain seeds surrounded by a fleshy, red aril. It is valued for its hard, durable wood and is often used in traditional Chinese medicine.

Iozoste chinensis thrives in well-drained soils and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.
