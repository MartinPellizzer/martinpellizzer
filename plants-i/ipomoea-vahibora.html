Ipomoea vahibora

Ipomoea vahibora, commonly known as the Madagascar morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking violet to blue flowers that bloom in the early morning. The plant belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is known for its rapid growth and climbing habit. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are slender and twining.
