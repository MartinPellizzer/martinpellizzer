Ilex arisanensis

Ilex arisanensis, commonly known as the Arisan holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Aquifoliaceae and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are usually dark green on top and lighter underneath. Ilex arisanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to provide year-round interest in the landscape.
