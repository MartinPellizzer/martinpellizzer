Illicium micranthum subsp. tsangii

Illicium micranthum subsp.

tsangii, commonly known as the Yunnan star anise, is a shrub native to southwestern China. It belongs to the Schisandraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, star-shaped fruits that resemble those of star anise.

The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers and has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its fruits are used in traditional medicine and have a distinctive licorice-like aroma.

The subspecies is distinguished by its smaller flowers and fruits compared to the main species.
