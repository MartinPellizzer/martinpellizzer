Isodon grandifolius var. grandifolius

Isodon grandifolius var. grandifolius, commonly known as the large-leaved isodon, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, pale blue to purple flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
