Ixora congesta

Ixora congesta, commonly known as the Chinese flame tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of bright red or orange-red flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has simple, opposite, and ovate leaves that are usually glossy green. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Ixora congesta is also valued for its ability to tolerate poor soil conditions and partial shade.
