Isoetes echinospora subsp. muricata

Isoetes echinospora subsp.

muricata, commonly known as the spiny water fern, is a species of aquatic plant belonging to the genus Isoetes. It is characterized by its unique, spiky spores that are often covered in a thick, calcified layer.

The plant typically grows in freshwater environments, such as lakes and ponds, where it anchors itself to the substrate. Its leaves are usually long and ribbon-like, with a distinctive appearance that aids in its identification.

This subspecies is notable for its ecological role in stabilizing sediment and providing habitat for microorganisms.
