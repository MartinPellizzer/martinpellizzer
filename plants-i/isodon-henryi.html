Isodon henryi

Isodon henryi, commonly known as Henry's isodon, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that are typically purple or blue. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

Isodon henryi is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional Chinese medicine.
