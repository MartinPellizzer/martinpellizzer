Ixora coronata

Ixora coronata, commonly known as the flame flower or crown flame, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 6 meters. The plant is characterized by its bright orange to red flowers, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a smooth texture.

Ixora coronata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a decorative plant.
