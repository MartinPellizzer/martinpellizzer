Impatiens pygmaea

Impatiens pygmaea, commonly known as the dwarf impatiens, is a delicate flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its small, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, white, and red. The plant typically grows to a height of only a few inches, making it ideal for container gardening or as a ground cover. Its leaves are heart-shaped and glossy, providing a lush backdrop for its vibrant blooms.

Impatiens pygmaea thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden spaces.
