Ipomoea seineri

Ipomoea seineri, commonly known as the Hawaiian morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a herbaceous vine that produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of purple and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is characterized by its rapid growth habit. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded areas.

Ipomoea seineri is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in the ecological diversity of its native habitat.
