Ilex julianii

Ilex julianii, commonly known as Julian's holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Aquifoliaceae and is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves that are often adorned with small spines along the margins. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers in the spring, which are followed by bright red berries in the fall. Ilex julianii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its dense foliage and seasonal color changes.
