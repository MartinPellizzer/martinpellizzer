Impatiens capillipes

Impatiens capillipes, commonly known as the slender touch-me-not, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It features slender, erect stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, spurred flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, typically blooming from spring to autumn. Its leaves are ovate to elliptic in shape, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Impatiens capillipes thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shade gardens.
