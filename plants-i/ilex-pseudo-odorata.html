Ilex pseudo-odorata

Ilex pseudo-odorata, commonly known as the Chinese holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often adorned with white or yellow spots. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Ilex pseudo-odorata is also valued for its ability to tolerate urban environments and air pollution.
