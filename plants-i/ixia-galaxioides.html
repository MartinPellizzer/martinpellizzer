Ixia galaxioides

Ixia galaxioides, commonly known as the Galaxias lily, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens. Its blooming period occurs during the late spring to early summer, adding vibrant color to the landscape.

Ixia galaxioides is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its beauty in gardens.
