Impatiens buennemeijeri

Impatiens buennemeijeri, commonly known as the velvetleaf impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, velvety leaves and clusters of bright red, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 3 feet. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid forests.

Its unique floral structure attracts pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
