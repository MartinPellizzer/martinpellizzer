Ixia patens var. kermesina

Ixia patens var. kermesina, commonly known as the Kermesina torch lily, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, torch-like inflorescence composed of numerous tubular, orange-red flowers. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped blooms that are typically borne on a single, upright stem. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

The variety is known for its vibrant color and long-lasting display, often blooming in late winter to early spring.
