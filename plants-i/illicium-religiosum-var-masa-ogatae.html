Illicium religiosum var. masa-ogatae

Illicium religiosum var. masa-ogatae, commonly known as the Japanese star anise, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Schisandraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, star-shaped fruit that resembles the common star anise. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into dark brown, fragrant pods. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a slightly serrated edge.
