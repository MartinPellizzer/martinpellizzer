Impatiens janthina

Impatiens janthina, commonly known as the purple impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Balsaminaceae and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to deep purple. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in humid tropical forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses around the world.
