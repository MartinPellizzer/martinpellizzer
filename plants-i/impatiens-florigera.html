Impatiens florigera

Impatiens florigera, commonly known as the "Chinese impatiens," is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and thrives in temperate climates. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are hairy and often trailing.

Impatiens florigera is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
