Inula pseudoconyza

Inula pseudoconyza, commonly known as false conyza, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grasslands and open areas, thriving in well-drained soils. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in a flat-topped cluster, making it visually distinctive.

Inula pseudoconyza is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
