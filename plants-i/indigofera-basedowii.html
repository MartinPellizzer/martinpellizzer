Indigofera basedowii

Indigofera basedowii, commonly known as the African indigo or Cape indigo, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its compound leaves and showy, pea-like flowers that range in color from blue to violet. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and traditional uses, it is cultivated in some regions for horticultural purposes.
