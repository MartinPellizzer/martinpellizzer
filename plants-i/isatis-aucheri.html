Isatis aucheri

Isatis aucheri, commonly known as the white coltsfoot, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cabbage and mustard. The plant is characterized by its basal rosette of broad, waxy leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Isatis aucheri is noted for its ornamental value and has been used historically in traditional medicine.
