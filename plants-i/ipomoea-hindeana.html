Ipomoea hindeana

Ipomoea hindeana, commonly known as Hinde's morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant blue or violet flowers that bloom in the morning and close in the afternoon. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces distinctive, spiral-shaped seed pods. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal areas and disturbed soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
