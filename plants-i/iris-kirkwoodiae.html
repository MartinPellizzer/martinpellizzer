Iris kirkwoodiae

Iris kirkwoodiae, commonly known as the Kirkwood iris, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, slender stems that bear showy, violet-blue flowers with a distinctive black center. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that form a dense clump, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Due to its unique appearance and hardiness, it is a popular choice among gardeners seeking drought-tolerant plants.
