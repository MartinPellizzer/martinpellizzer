Ilex aquifolium var. atrovirens

Ilex aquifolium var. atrovirens, commonly known as the dark-green holly, is a variant of the common holly characterized by its deep, glossy green foliage.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 3 to 6 meters. The leaves are elliptical, with spiny margins and a leathery texture, and they remain evergreen in milder climates. It produces small, red berries in the autumn, which are an important food source for birds.

This plant thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
