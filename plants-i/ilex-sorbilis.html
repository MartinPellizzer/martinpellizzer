Ilex sorbilis

Ilex sorbilis, commonly known as the Mexican holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to Mexico.

It belongs to the family Aquifoliaceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically opposite in arrangement. Ilex sorbilis is also valued for its ability to withstand drought conditions once established.
