Ixora decora

Ixora decora, commonly known as the ornamental ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, bright red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. Ixora decora thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a hedge due to its attractive foliage and persistent blooms.
