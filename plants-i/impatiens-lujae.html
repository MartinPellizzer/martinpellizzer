Impatiens lujae

Impatiens lujae, commonly known as the Lujan's Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and a slightly waxy surface. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid forests.

Impatiens lujae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
