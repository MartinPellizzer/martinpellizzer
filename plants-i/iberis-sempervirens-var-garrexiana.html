Iberis sempervirens var. garrexiana

Iberis sempervirens var. garrexiana, commonly known as the Garrexian evergreen candytuft, is a low-growing perennial shrub native to southern Europe.

It features dense clusters of small, bright white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves that remain evergreen throughout the year. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its compact size and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.
