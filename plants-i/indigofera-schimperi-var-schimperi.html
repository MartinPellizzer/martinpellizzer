Indigofera schimperi var. schimperi

Indigofera schimperi var. schimperi, commonly known as Ethiopian indigo, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its striking blue flowers. The plant is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height. Its leaves are trifoliate, and it produces pods that contain seeds.

This species is valued for its traditional use in dyeing textiles with a deep blue color.
