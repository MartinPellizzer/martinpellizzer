Indigofera Engleri

Indigofera engleri is a striking plant native to parts of Southeast Asia known for its vibrant blue flowers. This species belongs to the legume family and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its unique coloration makes it a popular choice among horticulturists and gardeners. The plant is also valued for its ecological benefits, providing habitat and food for various insects. Despite its beauty, it requires specific care to thrive in different environments.
