Isoetes × brochonii

Isoetes × brochonii, commonly known as the hybrid water starwort, is a species of aquatic plant belonging to the genus Isoetes.

It is a spore-bearing plant that thrives in freshwater environments, particularly in shallow, still waters. The plant has a distinctive morphology, characterized by its spiky, segmented stems and small, clustered spore capsules. It plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for microorganisms and invertebrates.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
