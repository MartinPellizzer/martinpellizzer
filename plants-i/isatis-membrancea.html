Isatis membrancea

Isatis membrancea, commonly known as Chinese yellow mustard, is a herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other mustard plants. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and pinnately lobed leaves. It produces small, round pods containing numerous seeds.

Isatis membrancea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has historical uses in traditional medicine.
