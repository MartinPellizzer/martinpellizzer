Iris Interior

The iris interior is a rare and striking plant known for its elegant blue-violet flowers. Native to the mountainous regions of California, it thrives in cool, moist environments. Its slender stems and delicate petals make it a prized addition to gardens and botanical collections. Despite its beauty, the iris interior is endangered due to habitat loss and climate change. Conservation efforts are underway to protect this unique species for future generations.
