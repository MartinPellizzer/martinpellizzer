Ixia deusta

Ixia deusta, commonly known as the Orange Sunset lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from orange to red, often with a contrasting yellow throat. The plant typically blooms in late summer to early autumn, adding vibrant color to gardens. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that emerge from a bulbous base.

Ixia deusta is well-suited for rock gardens or containers and requires well-drained soil and full sun.
