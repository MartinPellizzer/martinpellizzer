Impatiens stolonifera

Impatiens stolonifera, commonly known as the trailing impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant produces small, heart-shaped leaves and has a fibrous root system that helps it anchor in moist soil. It thrives in shaded environments and is often used in hanging baskets or as ground cover.

Impatiens stolonifera is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
