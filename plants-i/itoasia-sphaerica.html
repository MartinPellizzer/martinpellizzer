Itoasia sphaerica

Itoasia sphaerica, commonly known as the globe sphaerica, is a unique succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a rounded, globular shape with thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a subtle ornamental touch. Its leaves are typically a deep green with a waxy coating, enhancing its drought tolerance.

Itoasia sphaerica is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements.
