Iresine hebanthoides

Iresine hebanthoides, commonly known as Crimson Rose or Redleaf, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its vibrant, bi-colored foliage, with deep red upper surfaces and dark green undersides. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow, which contrast with its showy leaves. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Iresine hebanthoides is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
