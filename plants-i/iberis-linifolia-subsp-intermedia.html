Iberis linifolia subsp. intermedia

Iberis linifolia subsp.

intermedia, commonly known as the intermediate candytuft, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features dense clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring.

The plant has greyish-green, mat-forming foliage that is covered in fine hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
