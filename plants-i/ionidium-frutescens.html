Ionidium frutescens

Ionidium frutescens, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, fertile soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and clearings.

Ionidium frutescens is also known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through both seeds and vegetative propagation.
