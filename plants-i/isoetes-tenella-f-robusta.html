Isoetes tenella f. robusta

Isoetes tenella f. robusta, commonly known as the robust spore moss, is a rare fern-like plant belonging to the order Isoetales.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and its ability to thrive in wet, marshy environments. The plant produces small, spore-bearing structures called sporangia, which are typically clustered along the stems. Its leaves are narrow and often arranged in a spiral pattern, giving it a distinctive appearance.

This species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological adaptations.
