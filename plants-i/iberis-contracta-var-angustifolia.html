Iberis contracta var. angustifolia

Iberis contracta var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf candytuft, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It features dense clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has finely divided, grass-like leaves that are typically narrow and linear. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a valuable addition to ornamental horticulture.
