Ixora modesta

Ixora modesta, commonly known as the small flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of bright red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape and have a smooth texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Ixora modesta is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a landscape plant.
