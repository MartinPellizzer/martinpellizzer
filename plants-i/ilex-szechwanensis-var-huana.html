Ilex szechwanensis var. huana

Ilex szechwanensis var. huana, commonly known as the Huana Chinese holly, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that turn reddish-brown in the fall. The plant produces small, white flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries that are a food source for birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping.
