Iozoste areolata

Iozoste areolata, commonly known as the spiny leaf cactus, is a succulent plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its thick, spiny stems and fleshy, lobed leaves that store water. The plant produces yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding visual interest to its appearance. It thrives in arid environments and requires minimal water to survive.

Its unique morphology makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
