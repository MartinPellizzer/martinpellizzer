Ixora calliantha

Ixora calliantha, commonly known as the flame flower or red ixora, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of bright red, tubular flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Ixora calliantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a hedge plant.
