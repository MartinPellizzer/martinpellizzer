Isachne walkeri

Isachne walkeri, commonly known as the slender grass or narrowleaf isachne, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in temperate climates. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are usually less than 10 centimeters in length. Its inflorescence consists of a single spikelet, which is a characteristic feature of the grass family.

Isachne walkeri is often used in ornamental gardening due to its delicate appearance and adaptability to various growing conditions.
