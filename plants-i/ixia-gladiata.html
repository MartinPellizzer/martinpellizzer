Ixia gladiata

Ixia gladiata, commonly known as the sword lily, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, sword-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-like flowers. Its ornamental value and ease of cultivation have contributed to its widespread use in horticulture.
