Indigofera atriceps subsp. rhodesiaca

Indigofera atriceps subsp.

rhodesiaca, commonly known as Rhodesian indigo, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and raceme of small, fragrant flowers. The plant produces pods that contain lens-shaped seeds, and it is adapted to grow in dry, rocky habitats.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and traditional medicine.
