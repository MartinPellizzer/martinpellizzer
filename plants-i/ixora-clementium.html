Ixora clementium

Ixora clementium, commonly known as the Chinese hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. Ixora clementium thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
