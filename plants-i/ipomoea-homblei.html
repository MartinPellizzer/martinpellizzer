Ipomoea homblei

Ipomoea homblei, commonly known as the Hombli morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from deep purple to bright blue. The plant produces heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Ipomoea homblei is also known for its rapid growth and ability to climb, making it a popular choice for trellises and garden structures.
