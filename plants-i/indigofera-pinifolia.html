Indigofera pinifolia

Indigofera pinifolia, commonly known as the needleleaf indigo, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

Indigofera pinifolia is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it useful in agricultural settings.
