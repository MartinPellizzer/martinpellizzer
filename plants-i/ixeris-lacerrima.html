Ixeris lacerrima

Ixeris lacerrima, commonly known as the golden groundsel, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellow flowers in the spring. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Ixeris lacerrima is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in erosion-prone areas.
