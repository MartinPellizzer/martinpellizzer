Isoetes echinospora

Isoetes echinospora, commonly known as the spiny spikerush, is a member of the water fern family, Isoetaceae.

It is a submerged aquatic plant characterized by its dense, spiky spore-bearing structures called strobila. The plant thrives in freshwater environments such as lakes, ponds, and slow-moving streams. Its leaves are ribbon-like and form dense tufts, contributing to its overall appearance.

Isoetes echinospora plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and oxygen through photosynthesis.
