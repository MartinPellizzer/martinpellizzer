Ilex diuretica

Ilex diuretica, commonly known as the diuretic holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Caribbean islands.

It belongs to the family Aquifoliaceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and red berries. The plant is notable for its medicinal properties, particularly its diuretic effects, which have been traditionally used in herbal remedies. Its berries are toxic to humans and animals, making it important to handle with care.
