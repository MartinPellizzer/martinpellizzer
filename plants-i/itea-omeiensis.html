Itea omeiensis

Itea omeiensis, commonly known as the Chinese spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in dense, rounded clusters during the summer months. The plant has slender, arching stems and dark green, serrated leaves that turn reddish in the fall. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value comes from its showy flowers and attractive foliage throughout the growing season.
