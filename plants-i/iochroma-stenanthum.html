Iochroma stenanthum

Iochroma stenanthum, commonly known as the narrow-flowered bellflower, is a species native to South America, particularly found in regions of Peru and Bolivia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant features showy, bell-shaped flowers that are usually blue or violet in color, which attract pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often covered in a fine pubescence.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
