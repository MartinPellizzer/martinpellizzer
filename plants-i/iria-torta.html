Iria torta

Iria torta, commonly known as the twisted iris, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that come in shades of blue, purple, and white. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces blooms that are often borne on tall, slender stems. Iria torta is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
