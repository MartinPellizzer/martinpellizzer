Illigera reticulata

Illigera reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, reticulate venation pattern on its leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pale green to yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and rarity, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
