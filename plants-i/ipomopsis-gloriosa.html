Ipomopsis gloriosa

Ipomopsis gloriosa, commonly known as the glory flower, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped blooms that range from pink to purple. Ipomopsis gloriosa is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
