Ilex cristalensis

Ilex cristalensis, commonly known as the Cristalensis holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Aquifoliaceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often spiny along the edges. The plant produces small, white to yellowish flowers in the spring, followed by red berries that are a food source for various birds and mammals.
