Isachne soderstromii

Isachne soderstromii, commonly known as the Soderstrom's isachne, is a species of grass native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a smooth texture and a bluish-green color.

Isachne soderstromii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
