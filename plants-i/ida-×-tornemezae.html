Ida × tornemezae

Ida × tornemezae, commonly known as the hybrid tea rose, is a popular ornamental plant characterized by its large, showy blooms.

It is a cross between Rosa ida and Rosa tornemey, resulting in a variety that combines the best traits of both parent species. The plant typically features dark green, glossy foliage and thorny stems, which are typical of many rose species. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, requiring regular watering and pruning for optimal growth.

Ida × tornemezae is widely cultivated for its fragrance and vibrant flower colors, making it a favorite in gardens and floral arrangements.
