Impatiens graciliflora

Impatiens graciliflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Balsaminaceae and is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white.
