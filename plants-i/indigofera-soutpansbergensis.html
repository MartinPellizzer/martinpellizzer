Indigofera soutpansbergensis

Indigofera soutpansbergensis, commonly known as the Soutpansberg indigo, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive blue to violet flowers. The plant has compound leaves and produces pods that contain seeds. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Its botanical features make it an important species in its native ecosystem.
