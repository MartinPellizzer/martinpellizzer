Ixora spectabilis

Ixora spectabilis, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bright red or orange flowers that bloom in dense, clustered inflorescences, creating a striking visual display. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth surface. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Ixora spectabilis is also valued for its ability to grow in a variety of soil types and its relatively low maintenance requirements.
