Impatiens declercqii

Impatiens declercqii, commonly known as the Congo impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the Democratic Republic of the Congo.

It belongs to the Balsaminaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white. Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
