Ixia columnaris

Ixia columnaris, commonly known as the slender flame lily, is a striking South African bulbous plant characterized by its tall, slender stems and vibrant orange or red tubular flowers.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is native to the Western and Northern Cape provinces of South Africa. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped blooms that attract pollinators such as bees and birds. Ixia columnaris thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that emerge from a bulbous base, adding texture to the landscape.
