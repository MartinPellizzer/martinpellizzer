Impatiens scenarioi

Impatiens scenarioi, commonly known as the scenario impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft, fuzzy appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Its rapid growth and ability to spread quickly make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a vibrant, low-maintenance ornamental plant.
