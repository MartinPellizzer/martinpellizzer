Iris tenuis

Iris tenuis, commonly known as the slender iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features tall, slender stems that bear showy, violet-blue flowers with three upright petals and three drooping sepals. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are usually smooth and grass-like in appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

Iris tenuis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant blossoms.
