Ilex regnelliana

Ilex regnelliana, commonly known as the Chinese holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, bright red berries that are produced in the winter. The plant belongs to the family Aquifoliaceae and is closely related to other holly species. Ilex regnelliana typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full shade.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and as a hedge plant.
