Iris setosa f. zonalis

Iris setosa f. zonalis, commonly known as the Zonal Iris, is a hybrid form of the slender iris.

It is characterized by its distinctive zonal pattern on the petals, which features a darker band near the base. This cultivar is typically found in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or garden settings. The plant produces striking blue or violet flowers with a yellow center.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its elegance and hardiness.
