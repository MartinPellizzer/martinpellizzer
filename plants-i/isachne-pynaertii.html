Isachne pynaertii

Isachne pynaertii, commonly known as the Pynaert's isachne, is a species of grass native to Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial plant that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is characterized by its delicate appearance and is often found in montane regions.

Botanically, it belongs to the Poaceae family and is noted for its ornamental value in horticulture.
