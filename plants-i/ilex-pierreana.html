Ilex pierreana

Ilex pierreana, commonly known as the dwarf holly, is a small evergreen shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically oval in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Ilex pierreana is also valued for its compact growth habit and year-round foliage.
