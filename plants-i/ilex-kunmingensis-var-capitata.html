Ilex kunmingensis var. capitata

Ilex kunmingensis var. capitata, commonly known as the Yunnan holly, is a shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters, followed by bright red berries that are edible for birds but toxic to humans. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
