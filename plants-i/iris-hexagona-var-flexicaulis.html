Iris hexagona var. flexicaulis

Iris hexagona var. flexicaulis, commonly known as the flexible hexagon iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It features upright, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. The plant produces striking, showy flowers with a violet-blue to purple hue, often with a white or light blue throat. Its bloom period occurs in late spring to early summer, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

This variety is noted for its adaptability and is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
