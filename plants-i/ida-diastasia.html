Ida diastasia

Ida diastasia, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, heart-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers warm, humid environments. Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
