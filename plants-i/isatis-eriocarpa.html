Isatis eriocarpa

Isatis eriocarpa, commonly known as the woolly cabbage or woolly turnip, is a species of flowering plant in the family Brassicaceae.

It is native to western North America, particularly found in regions like California and Nevada. The plant is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. It produces small, round, green pods that contain seeds with a tuft of white hairs.

Isatis eriocarpa is often found in dry, disturbed soils and is considered a weed in some agricultural areas.
