Isoetes longissima subsp. tenuissima

Isoetes longissima subsp.

tenuissima, commonly known as the slender water fern, is a species of aquatic plant belonging to the genus Isoetes. It is characterized by its delicate, ribbon-like leaves that are typically less than 1 mm in width.

This subspecies is native to freshwater environments in temperate regions, often found in still or slow-moving waters. The plant has a unique sporophyll structure, with sporangia attached to the lower surface of the leaves.

It plays an important ecological role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various microorganisms and invertebrates.
