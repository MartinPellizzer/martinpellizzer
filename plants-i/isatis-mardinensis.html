Isatis mardinensis

Isatis mardinensis, commonly known as Mardin woad, is a flowering plant native to the regions of Turkey, Syria, and Iraq.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cabbage and mustard. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months, followed by pods containing seeds. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly rough texture, while the plant can grow up to 1 meter in height.

Isatis mardinensis is often found in rocky or arid environments, showing adaptability to harsh conditions.
