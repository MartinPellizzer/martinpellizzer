Isachne perpusilla

Isachne perpusilla, commonly known as the small isachne, is a small, perennial grass native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or wet meadows. The plant has slender, tufted shoots with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its inflorescence consists of compact, spike-like clusters of tiny flowers.

Isachne perpusilla is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in shaded conditions.
