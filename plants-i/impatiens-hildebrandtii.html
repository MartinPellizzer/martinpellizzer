Impatiens hildebrandtii

Impatiens hildebrandtii, commonly known as the Hildebrandt's impatiens, is a species native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is a flowering plant characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and undergrowth. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often have a velvety texture.

Impatiens hildebrandtii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in various garden settings.
