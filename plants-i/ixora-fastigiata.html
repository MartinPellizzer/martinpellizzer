Ixora fastigiata

Ixora fastigiata, commonly known as the upright ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and is often cultivated as a decorative shrub. The plant produces clusters of small, bright red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year in many regions. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a glossy surface.

Ixora fastigiata is well-suited for tropical and subtropical climates and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
