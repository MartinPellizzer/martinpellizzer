Ixora memecylifolia

Ixora memecylifolia, commonly known as the red flame ixora, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, glossy green foliage and clusters of bright orange-red flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has small, elliptical leaves and produces small, yellowish fruits that are not typically consumed by humans. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Ixora memecylifolia is also valued for its ability to tolerate partial shade and its resistance to pests and diseases.
