Ilex urbaniana

Ilex urbaniana, commonly known as the urban holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, white flowers in the spring, which are followed by bright red berries in the fall. Ilex urbaniana is well adapted to urban environments, tolerating pollution and compacted soils.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of conditions.
