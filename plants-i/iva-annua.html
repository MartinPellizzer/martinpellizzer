Iva annua

Iva annua, commonly known as common marshelder, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It typically grows in wetland environments such as marshes and along riverbanks. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. Its seeds are dispersed by wind, contributing to its spread in moist habitats.

Iva annua is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in both full sun and partial shade.
