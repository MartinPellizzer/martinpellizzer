Ilex aquifolium var. revoluta

Ilex aquifolium var. revoluta, commonly known as the English holly with revolute leaves, is a cultivar of the common holly.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green foliage with wavy, rolled margins that give it a distinctive appearance. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptical in shape and remain evergreen throughout the year. This variety produces small, red berries that are an important food source for birds during the winter months.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
