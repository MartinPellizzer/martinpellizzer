Ipomoea orizabensis subsp. novogaliciana

novogaliciana, commonly known as the Mexican morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Mexico. It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers.
