Inula prostata

Inula prostata, commonly known as the prostrate inula, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flower heads that bloom in late summer. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it an adaptable species suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
