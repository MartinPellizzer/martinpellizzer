Ipomoea juliagutierreziae

Ipomoea juliagutierreziae, commonly known as Julia's morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and purple.
