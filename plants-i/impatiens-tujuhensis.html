Impatiens tujuhensis

Impatiens tujuhensis, commonly known as the seven-leaf impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of hairs. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens tujuhensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
