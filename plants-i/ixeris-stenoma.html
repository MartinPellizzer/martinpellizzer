Ixeris stenoma

Ixeris stenoma, commonly known as the narrowleaf carpetweed, is a flowering plant native to Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically forms dense mats on the ground. The plant has small, lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow flowers in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Ixeris stenoma is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticulture.
