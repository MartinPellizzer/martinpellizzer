Impatiens malabarica

Impatiens malabarica, commonly known as the Malabar Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves and is often found in moist, shaded environments. It belongs to the family Balsaminaceae and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.

Impatiens malabarica is also known for its ability to thrive in partial shade and well-drained soil.
