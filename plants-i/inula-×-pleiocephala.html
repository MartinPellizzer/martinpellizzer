Inula × pleiocephala

Inula × pleiocephala, commonly known as the hybrid fleabane, is a perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is a hybrid between Inula helenium and Inula crithmifolia, exhibiting characteristics of both parent species. The plant features showy yellow to orange composite flowers, typically arranged in a rounded inflorescence. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and along roadsides.
