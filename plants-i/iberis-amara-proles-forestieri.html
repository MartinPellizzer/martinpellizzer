Iberis amara proles forestieri

Iberis amara proles forestieri, commonly known as the Corsican candytuft, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant features basal rosettes of narrow, gray-green leaves and produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. It is typically found in rocky or coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils.
