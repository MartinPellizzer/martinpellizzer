Impatiens hypophylla var. koreana

Impatiens hypophylla var. koreana, commonly known as Korean impatiens, is a flowering plant native to Korea.

It is a member of the Balsaminaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, spurred flowers that typically range in color from white to pink. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
