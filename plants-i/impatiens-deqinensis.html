Impatiens deqinensis

Impatiens deqinensis, commonly known as the Deqin touch-me-not, is a flowering plant native to the Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Balsaminaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white. Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
