Impatiens rivulicola

Impatiens rivulicola, commonly known as the water impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found near streams or in humid forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is also cultivated in gardens and greenhouses around the world.
