Iresine grandis

Iresine grandis, commonly known as red leaf iron plant, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, with leaves that can range from deep red to purple and green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically pink or white. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Iresine grandis is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
