Indigofera macrocalyx

Indigofera macrocalyx, commonly known as the large-flowered indigo, is a perennial shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, violet to blue flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, elongated pods containing several seeds. It is often found in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sunlight.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ornamental and ecological purposes.
