Inula disenterica

Inula disenterica, commonly known as the African sunflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features bright yellow ray flowers surrounding a central disk of smaller, tubular flowers. Its leaves are alternate, rough, and covered in fine hairs.

The plant is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.
