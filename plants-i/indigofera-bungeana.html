Indigofera bungeana

Indigofera bungeana, commonly known as Chinese indigo, is a perennial shrub native to China and other parts of East Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil through its root nodules, making it beneficial for soil fertility.

Its seeds are often used in traditional medicine and as a source of natural dyes.
