Ixora decaryi

Ixora decaryi, commonly known as the African flame or red flame plant, is a flowering shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its bright red or orange flowers that bloom in dense clusters, attracting pollinators such as birds and bees. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are usually arranged in opposite pairs along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ixora decaryi is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a hedge plant.
