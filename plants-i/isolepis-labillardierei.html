Isolepis labillardierei

Isolepis labillardierei, commonly known as the narrowleaf tussock grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, commonly known as the sedge family. This plant is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in wetland environments and is often found in swampy areas or along the edges of lakes and rivers.
