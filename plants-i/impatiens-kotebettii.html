Impatiens kotebettii

Impatiens kotebettii, commonly known as the Kotebettii Impatiens, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is primarily of interest in botanical research.
