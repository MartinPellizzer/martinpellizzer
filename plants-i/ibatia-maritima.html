Ibatia maritima

Ibatia maritima, commonly known as the sea hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to red. The plant thrives in saline environments and is often found in sandy soils near the shoreline. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a rough texture.

Ibatia maritima is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
