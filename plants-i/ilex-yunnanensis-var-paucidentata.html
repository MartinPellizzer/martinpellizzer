Ilex yunnanensis var. paucidentata

Ilex yunnanensis var. paucidentata, commonly known as the Yunnan holly, is a variant of the holly genus native to China.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree characterized by its dense, spiny foliage. The leaves are typically narrow, elliptical, and have a leathery texture with a glossy surface. It produces small, white flowers in the spring, followed by dark blue berries that are edible for birds.

This plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in landscaping due to its hardy nature and attractive appearance.
