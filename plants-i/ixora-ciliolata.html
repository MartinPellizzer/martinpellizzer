Ixora ciliolata

Ixora ciliolata, commonly known as the velvet tamarind or flame flower, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of bright red or orange-red flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has simple, opposite leaves with fine, silky hairs along the edges, giving it a velvety texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Ixora ciliolata is also used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
