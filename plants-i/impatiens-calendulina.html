Impatiens calendulina

Impatiens calendulina, commonly known as the orange impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and vibrant orange or red flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are ovate and usually have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
