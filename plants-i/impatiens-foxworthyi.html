Impatiens foxworthyi

Impatiens foxworthyi, commonly known as the Foxworth's impatiens, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of western Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are usually pink or red in color, with five petals and a spur. Its leaves are ovate and slightly hairy, providing a textured appearance.

Impatiens foxworthyi thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of dense forest canopies.
