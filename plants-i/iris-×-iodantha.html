Iris × iodantha

Iris × iodantha, commonly known as the blue flag iris, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Iris sibirica and Iris germanica.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers with a yellow beard, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant is a perennial herb with sword-shaped, grass-like leaves that form a dense rosette. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in suitable climates.
