Isodon daitonensis

Isodon daitonensis, commonly known as the Japanese skullcap, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that are typically purple or blue in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Isodon daitonensis is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
