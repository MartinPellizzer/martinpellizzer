Ilex tectonica

Ilex tectonica, commonly known as the Indian holly, is a species of flowering plant in the family Aquifoliaceae.

It is native to the Indian subcontinent and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and spiny leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.
