Ilex phanerophlebia

Ilex phanerophlebia, commonly known as the silver leaf holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage with a silvery-white underside, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by bright red berries, which are an important food source for birds. Ilex phanerophlebia typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full shade.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and ability to provide year-round interest in landscapes.
