Iris reticulata var. sophenensis

Iris reticulata var. sophenensis, commonly known as the Persian iris, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features striking blue to violet flowers with a distinctive reticulate pattern on the petals. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy blooms in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its early blooming habit and compact growth form.
