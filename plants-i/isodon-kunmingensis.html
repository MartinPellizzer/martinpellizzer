Isodon kunmingensis

Isodon kunmingensis, commonly known as the Kunming isodon, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and has lance-shaped leaves with a hairy surface. Its flowers are small and clustered, often displaying shades of purple or pink.

Isodon kunmingensis is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
