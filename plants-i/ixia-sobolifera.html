Ixia sobolifera

Ixia sobolifera, commonly known as the red-hot poker, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, upright stems that bear dense clusters of vibrant red, orange, or yellow tubular flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette at the base. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its unique appearance and long blooming period make it a popular choice among gardeners.
