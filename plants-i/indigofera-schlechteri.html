Indigofera schlechteri

Indigofera schlechteri, commonly known as the African indigo.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is native to tropical Africa. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to violet. It produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds.

This species is often grown for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
