Icica icicariba

Icica icicariba, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that resemble flames, which bloom in clusters along its upright stems. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant flowers. It thrives in humid, warm environments with high levels of indirect sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
