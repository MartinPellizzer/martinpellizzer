Ilex aquifolium var. aureomarginata

Ilex aquifolium var. aureomarginata, commonly known as the golden-margin holly, is a cultivar of the common holly.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with striking golden-yellow margins. The plant is evergreen, retaining its foliage year-round, and thrives in well-drained soil with partial to full sunlight. It produces small white flowers in spring, followed by bright red berries in autumn.

This variety is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its aesthetic appeal and hardy nature.
