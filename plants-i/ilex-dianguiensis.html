Ilex dianguiensis

Ilex dianguiensis, commonly known as the Guangxi holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Aquifoliaceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement.
