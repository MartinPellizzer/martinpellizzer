Ixora parkeri

Ixora parkeri, commonly known as the golden flame ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters tall, featuring glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of bright orange-red flowers, which are borne in terminal inflorescences. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Ixora parkeri is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
