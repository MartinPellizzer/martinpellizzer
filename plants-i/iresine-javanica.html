Iresine javanica

Iresine javanica, commonly known as the flame flower or red leaf plant, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, vibrant red or purple foliage, which contrasts beautifully with its small, inconspicuous white flowers. The plant belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in moist, well-drained soils. Iresine javanica is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its eye-catching leaves and ease of care.

It prefers full sun to partial shade and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.
