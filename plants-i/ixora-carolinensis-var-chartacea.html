Ixora carolinensis var. chartacea

Ixora carolinensis var. chartacea, commonly known as the paper ixora, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves that are often a striking white or pale green, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, bright orange-red flowers that bloom throughout the year in warm climates. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.
