Impatiens sashinborthakurii

Impatiens sashinborthakurii, commonly known as the Sashinborthakur's Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan region of Nepal.

It is a herbaceous perennial with showy, bilaterally symmetrical flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and a hairy texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique floral structure and coloration make it a notable species within the Impatiens genus.
