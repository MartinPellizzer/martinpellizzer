Inga disticha var. negrensis

Inga disticha var. negrensis, commonly known as the Negrense Inga, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and legume pods. The plant is notable for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in shaded understory conditions. Its flowers are typically clustered in dense inflorescences, and the fruit is a pod containing several seeds.

The species is also valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, contributing to soil fertility in its natural habitat.
