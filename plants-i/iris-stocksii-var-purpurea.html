Iris stocksii var. purpurea

Iris stocksii var. purpurea, commonly known as the purple iris stock, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and striking purple to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy, spurred petals that attract pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and relatively low maintenance requirements.
