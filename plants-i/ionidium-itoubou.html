Ionidium itoubou

Ionidium itoubou, commonly known as the African violet.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa, characterized by its velvety, heart-shaped leaves and showy, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil. Its flowers come in a variety of colors, including purple, pink, and white, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Ionidium itoubou is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and aesthetic appeal.
