Ixia verrucosa

Ixia verrucosa, commonly known as the yellow rain lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range from yellow to orange in color, often with a distinctive red or orange throat. The plant produces a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that store water, aiding in its survival in arid conditions. Ixia verrucosa blooms during the summer months, typically from November to February, and thrives in well-drained soil.

It is a popular ornamental plant due to its vibrant colors and unique floral structure.
