Illecebrum maritimum

Illecebrum maritimum, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is a perennial herb that thrives in saline and sandy soils, often found along shorelines and dunes. The plant features spiny, leaf-like structures that help reduce water loss and protect against herbivores. Its flowers are typically blue or purple, with a distinctive appearance that resembles a crown.

Illecebrum maritimum plays an important ecological role in stabilizing coastal ecosystems and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
