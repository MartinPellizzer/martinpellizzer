Indigofera mearnsii

Indigofera mearnsii, commonly known as Australian wattle, is a shrub or small tree native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its feathery, bipinnate leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which are followed by elongated, bean-like pods. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a hardy species.

The plant is also known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility.
