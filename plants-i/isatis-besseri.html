Isatis besseri

Isatis besseri, commonly known as Chinese cabbage or turnip mustard, is a biennial plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other cruciferous vegetables like broccoli and cauliflower. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slightly hairy texture. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in clusters, typical of the mustard family.

Isatis besseri is valued for its nutritional content and has been used in traditional medicine for centuries.
