Impatiens cornigera

Impatiens cornigera, commonly known as the horned impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and southern Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, spurred flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb, reaching heights of up to 3 feet. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered with fine, silky hairs.

Impatiens cornigera thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking vibrant, easy-to-care-for blooms.
