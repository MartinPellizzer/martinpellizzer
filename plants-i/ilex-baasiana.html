Ilex baasiana

Ilex baasiana, commonly known as the Baas's holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter on the underside. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for various bird species. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Ilex baasiana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to different soil types.
