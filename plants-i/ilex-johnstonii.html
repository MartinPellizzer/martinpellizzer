Ilex johnstonii

Ilex johnstonii, commonly known as Johnston's holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy appearance. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for various bird species. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Ilex johnstonii is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
