Iris annae

Iris annae, commonly known as the Ann's iris, is a species of flowering plant in the family Iridaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central America, particularly in Mexico and Guatemala. The plant is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers, which bloom in early spring. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, forming a dense rosette at the base.

Iris annae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and unique form.
