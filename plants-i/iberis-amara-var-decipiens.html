Iberis amara var. decipiens

Iberis amara var. decipiens, commonly known as the bitter candytuft, is a perennial flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the family Brassicaceae, which includes mustard and cress plants. The plant features basal rosettes of hairy, dark green leaves and produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. It is characterized by its bitter taste, which is due to the presence of glucosinolates.
