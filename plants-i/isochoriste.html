Isochoriste

Isochoriste, commonly known as the African milk tree, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Euphorbiaceae.

It consists of a single species, Isochoriste africana, native to southern Africa. The plant is characterized by its milky sap, which is toxic and can cause skin irritation. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, featuring thick, waxy leaves and yellow flowers.

The genus is notable for its ecological role in arid and semi-arid regions.
