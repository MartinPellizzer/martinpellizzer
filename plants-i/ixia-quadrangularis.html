Ixia quadrangularis

Ixia quadrangularis, commonly known as the fire lily, is a striking bulbous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, straight stems that bear clusters of vibrant orange-red flowers resembling flames. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette at the base. Its flowers bloom in late summer to early autumn, adding a dramatic display to gardens.

Ixia quadrangularis is well-suited to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
