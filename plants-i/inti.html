Inti

Inti, commonly known as the "Sun Plant," is a flowering plant native to the Andean region of South America.

It belongs to the genus *Calotropis* and is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has thick, waxy leaves that help it conserve water in its arid habitat. Inti is also known for its medicinal properties, with various parts of the plant used in traditional healing practices.

Its resilience to harsh environmental conditions makes it an important species in ecological restoration efforts.
