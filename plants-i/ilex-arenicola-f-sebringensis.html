Ilex arenicola f. sebringensis

Ilex arenicola f. sebringensis, commonly known as the Sebring holly, is a rare evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Florida Keys.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green, often with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, bright red berries that are an important food source for local wildlife. It thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and is adapted to the tropical and subtropical climate of its native habitat.
