Impatiens amphibia

Impatiens amphibia, commonly known as the amphibian impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found near streams or in dense undergrowth.

Impatiens amphibia is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to add color to shaded garden spaces.
