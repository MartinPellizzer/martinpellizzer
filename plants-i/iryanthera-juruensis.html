Iryanthera juruensis

Iryanthera juruensis, commonly known as the Juruena orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has a slender, erect stem with a few leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on tree trunks or branches.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists alike.
