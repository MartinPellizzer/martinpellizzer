Ixora congesta var. maritima

Ixora congesta var. maritima, commonly known as coastal ixora, is a shrub native to coastal regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, red-orange flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to its habitat. The plant thrives in saline and sandy soils, making it well adapted to coastal environments. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Ixora congesta var. maritima is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in challenging coastal conditions.
