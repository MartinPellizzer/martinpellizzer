Imperata allang

Imperata allang, commonly known as the red grass or purple grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and produces striking purple or red inflorescences. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open areas such as fields and roadsides. Its rhizomatous root system allows it to spread rapidly and form extensive colonies.

Imperata allang is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional practices in various cultures.
