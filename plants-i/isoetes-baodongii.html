Isoetes baodongii

Isoetes baodongii, commonly known as the Baodong watermace, is a rare aquatic fern belonging to the genus Isoetes.

It is characterized by its unique, spiky morphology with specialized leaf structures called "spore-bearing spikes.". The plant thrives in freshwater environments, particularly in swampy or marshy areas with poor drainage. Its spores are dispersed by water, contributing to its reproductive strategy.

Due to habitat loss and environmental changes, Isoetes baodongii is considered endangered in its native range.
