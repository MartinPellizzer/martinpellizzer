Impatiens vittata

Impatiens vittata, commonly known as the spotted impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant features heart-shaped, velvety leaves with distinctive white or pale green markings. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens vittata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
