Isodon chionanthus

Isodon chionanthus, commonly known as the snow-flower isodon, is a herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a distinctive minty aroma.

Isodon chionanthus is often found in rocky or well-drained soils at higher elevations.
