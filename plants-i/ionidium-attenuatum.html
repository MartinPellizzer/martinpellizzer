Ionidium attenuatum

Ionidium attenuatum, commonly known as the slender tailed orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in the understory of rainforests. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the forest floor.

Ionidium attenuatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in local ecosystems by supporting pollinators.
