Ixia sceptrum

Ixia sceptrum, commonly known as the Crown of Thorns, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of trumpet-shaped flowers. The flowers are typically bright yellow or orange, with a distinctive red or orange stripe down the center, adding to its ornamental appeal. The plant produces a bulbous root system that helps it survive in arid conditions.

Ixia sceptrum thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
