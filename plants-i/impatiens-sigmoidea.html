Impatiens sigmoidea

Impatiens sigmoidea, commonly known as the zigzag impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface and produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. Its flowers are borne on pedicels and have five petals, with the lower petal often being the most prominent.

Impatiens sigmoidea thrives in moist, well-drained soil and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
