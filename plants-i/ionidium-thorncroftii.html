Ionidium thorncroftii

Ionidium thorncroftii, commonly known as the Thorncroft's ionidium, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from pale yellow to deep orange. The plant has narrow, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, silky hair. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to habitat loss, Ionidium thorncroftii is considered endangered and is the subject of conservation efforts.
