Iberis spathulata proles lapeyrousii

Iberis spathulata proles lapeyrousii, commonly known as the Lapeyrouse's candytuft, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of white or pink flowers in early spring. The plant has rounded, lobed leaves and is typically found in rocky or gravelly soils. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens.
