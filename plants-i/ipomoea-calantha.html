Ipomoea calantha

Ipomoea calantha, commonly known as the scarlet morning glory, is a climbing vine native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It produces vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from red to orange, typically blooming in the morning. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in full sun with well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.

Ipomoea calantha is also known for its rapid growth and ability to climb various structures.
