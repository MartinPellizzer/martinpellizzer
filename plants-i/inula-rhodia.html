Inula rhodia

Inula rhodia, commonly known as the yellow fleabane, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in sunny environments. Its yellow flowers, which bloom in the summer, are composed of both ray and disk florets.

Inula rhodia is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides.
