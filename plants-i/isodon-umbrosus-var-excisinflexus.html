Isodon umbrosus var. excisinflexus

Isodon umbrosus var. excisinflexus, commonly known as the dark isodon, is a lesser-known variant of the isodon genus.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a dark green hue and small, clustered flowers that bloom in the spring. Its botanical characteristics include a fibrous root system and a preference for moist, shaded environments.

Due to its limited distribution and unique morphology, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
