Ivesia arizonica

Ivesia arizonica, commonly known as Arizona ivesia, is a perennial flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often covered in fine hairs.
