Ixia polystachya var. flavescens

Ixia polystachya var. flavescens, commonly known as the yellow fire lily, is a striking South African plant characterized by its vibrant orange to yellow tubular flowers.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is native to the Cape Province, where it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. The species is also valued for its resilience and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and birds.
