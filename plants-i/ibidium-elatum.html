Ibidium elatum

Ibidium elatum, commonly known as the tall ibidium, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand prolonged periods of drought. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in a fine layer of white wax, which helps reduce water loss.

The tall ibidium is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
