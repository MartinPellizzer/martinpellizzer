Impatiens herbicola

Impatiens herbicola, commonly known as the herbaceous impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, spurred petals and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as under forest canopies. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped and have a smooth, glossy surface.

Impatiens herbicola is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade.
