Iris × intercedens

Iris × intercedens, commonly known as the hybrid iris, is a perennial plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It is a cross between Iris germanica and Iris pallida, resulting in a plant with distinctive, showy flowers. The flowers typically feature a vibrant violet or blue color with a white or pale yellow center, and they bloom in early summer. The plant has sword-shaped, grass-like leaves that grow from a rhizome, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to add color to shady areas.
