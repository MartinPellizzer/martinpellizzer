Ixia grandiflora

Ixia grandiflora, commonly known as the Orange Kaffir Lily, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a red or purple throat. The plant produces long, narrow leaves that are typically blue-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Ixia grandiflora is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom in late spring to early summer.
