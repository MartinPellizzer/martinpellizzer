Iris milesii

Iris milesii, commonly known as the Chinese iris, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It features striking blue to purple flowers with three upright petals and three drooping sepals. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 feet and has sword-shaped, grass-like leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Iris milesii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
