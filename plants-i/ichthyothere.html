Ichthyothere

Ichthyothere, commonly known as the sea hag, is a unique plant found in coastal regions.

It has a distinctive appearance with long, flexible stems that resemble seaweed. The plant possesses small, translucent leaves that help it absorb sunlight underwater. It thrives in shallow, brackish waters and is often found near tidal pools.

Ichthyothere plays an important role in marine ecosystems by providing habitat for various small aquatic organisms.
