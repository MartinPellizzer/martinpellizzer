Ilex editicostata var. chowii

Ilex editicostata var. chowii, commonly known as the Guangxi holly, is a shrub or small tree native to southern China.

It is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are elliptical and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters, followed by bright red berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits, it is cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
