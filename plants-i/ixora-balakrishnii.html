Ixora balakrishnii

Ixora balakrishnii, commonly known as the red ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and vibrant red or orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are glossy in appearance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Ixora balakrishnii is also valued for its ability to tolerate partial shade and its relatively low maintenance requirements.
