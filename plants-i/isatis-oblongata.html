Isatis oblongata

Isatis oblongata, commonly known as Chinese kale or Chinese cabbage, is a biennial plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other cruciferous vegetables like broccoli and cauliflower. The plant is characterized by its thick, dark green leaves that are often used in Asian cuisine. It produces small, yellow flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Isatis oblongata is also valued for its nutritional content and has been used in traditional medicine for centuries.
