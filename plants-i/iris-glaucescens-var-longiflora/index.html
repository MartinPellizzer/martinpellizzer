Iris Glaucescens Var. Longiflora

Iris glaucescens var. longiflora is a striking member of the iris family known for its vibrant blue and white flowers. Native to the mountainous regions of western North America, this plant thrives in cool, moist environments. Its long, slender petals and robust foliage make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking both beauty and resilience. The variety's unique coloration and elegant form have earned it a place in botanical gardens and native plant collections. With proper care, it can flourish and add a touch of natural elegance to any landscape.
