Ixora nitidula

Ixora nitidula, commonly known as the glossy ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, featuring dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, bright orange-red flowers, which are highly attractive to pollinators. Its leaves are glossy and lance-shaped, contributing to its ornamental value.

Ixora nitidula is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions for its vibrant blooms and hardy nature.
