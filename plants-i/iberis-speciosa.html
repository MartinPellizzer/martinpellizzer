Iberis speciosa

Iberis speciosa, commonly known as the showy iberis, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, mat-forming growth with bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has holly-like leaves that are evergreen and provide ornamental interest throughout the year. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact size and long blooming period make it a valuable addition to gardens seeking low-maintenance, attractive flora.
