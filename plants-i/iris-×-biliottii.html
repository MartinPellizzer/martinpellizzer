Iris × biliottii

Iris × biliottii, commonly known as the Biliotti iris, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Iris germanica and Iris pallida.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers with a yellow center, typically blooming in late spring. The plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

The Biliotti iris is also valued for its resilience and ability to adapt to various growing conditions.
