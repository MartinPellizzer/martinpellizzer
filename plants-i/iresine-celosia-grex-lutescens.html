Iresine celosia grex lutescens

Iresine celosia grex lutescens, commonly known as the flame flower or red flame plant, is a strikingly colorful ornamental plant native to tropical regions.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, feather-like bracts that range from red to orange and yellow. The plant typically grows as an annual or short-lived perennial, reaching heights of 30 to 60 centimeters. Its leaves are ovate with a slightly hairy texture, and the flowers are small and inconspicuous, overshadowed by the showy bracts.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden displays.
