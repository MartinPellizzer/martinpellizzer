Iris decora var. leucantha

Iris decora var. leucantha, commonly known as the White Decorative Iris, is a striking ornamental plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features tall, upright stems that bear showy, six-petaled flowers in shades of white to pale blue. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are densely packed along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its elegant appearance and relatively low maintenance make it a popular choice among gardeners.
