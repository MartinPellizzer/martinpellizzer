Isorium

Isorium, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Nelumbonaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom on long, buoyant stalks. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in still water, often found in ponds and lakes. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, while its flowers can be pink, white, or yellow, depending on the species.

The lotus holds cultural and spiritual significance in many Asian traditions.
