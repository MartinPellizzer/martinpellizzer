Iria spadicea var. pallida

Iria spadicea var. pallida, commonly known as the pale spadix iris, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, spadix-like inflorescence that is typically pale green or yellow in color. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, with a smooth, waxy texture.

The pale spadix iris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
