Ione dichroma

Ione dichroma, commonly known as the twinleaf plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to the western United States.

It features two opposite leaves on each stem, a characteristic that gives it its scientific name. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers in the spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland areas.

Ione dichroma is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to local wildlife.
