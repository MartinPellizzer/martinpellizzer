Iris reticulata var. krelagei

Iris reticulata var. krelagei, commonly known as the Krelage iris, is a species native to the Caucasus region.

It is a bulbous plant characterized by its compact growth and early blooming habit. The flowers are typically blue or violet, with a reticulate pattern on the petals. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.
