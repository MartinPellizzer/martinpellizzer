Ipheion dialystemon

Ipheion dialystemon, commonly known as the narrow-leafed white-eyed grass, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage and small, white to pale blue flowers. Ipheion dialystemon is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
