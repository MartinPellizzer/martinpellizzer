Isonandra macrophylla

Isonandra macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf isonandra, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the year. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
