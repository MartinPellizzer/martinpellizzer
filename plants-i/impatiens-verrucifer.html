Impatiens verrucifer

Impatiens verrucifer, commonly known as the warty impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, warty stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink and white. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for gardeners in warm climates.
