Iris × brzhezitzky

Iris × brzhezitzky, commonly known as the Brzhezitzky iris, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between different iris species.

It is characterized by its striking, tall flower stalks that bear showy, often violet or blue-purple blooms. The plant typically thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full to partial sunlight. Its foliage consists of sword-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and lanceolate in form.

This hybrid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic beauty.
