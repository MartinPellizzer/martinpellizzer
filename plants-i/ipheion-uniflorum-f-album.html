Ipheion uniflorum f. album

Ipheion uniflorum f. album, commonly known as the silver leaf blue-eyed grass, is a variant of the common blue-eyed grass.

It is a low-growing perennial plant native to the southeastern United States. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, white to pale blue flowers. Its foliage is characterized by a silvery or grayish hue, which distinguishes it from the typical green leaves of the species.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or rocky areas.
