Ixora balansae

Ixora balansae, commonly known as the Balansae flame bush, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of bright red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are dark green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant floral display and adaptability to various climates.
