Indigofera floresii

Indigofera floresii, commonly known as the Flores legume, is a flowering plant native to the Indonesian island of Flores.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in coastal and lowland areas, thriving in tropical climates with well-drained soils.

Its botanical features make it an important species in local ecosystems and traditional uses.
