Impatiens firmula var. macrosepala

Impatiens firmula var. macrosepala, commonly known as the large-sepaled impatiens, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest undergrowth. Its leaves are oval and glossy, with a slightly hairy texture.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
