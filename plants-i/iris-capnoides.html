Iris capnoides

Iris capnoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, grass-like leaves that are typically narrow and dark green. The plant produces striking blue to purple flowers with three upright petals and three drooping sepals. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for rock gardens and native plant landscapes.
