Indigofera ichangensis f. leptantha

Indigofera ichangensis f. leptantha, commonly known as the slender-leaved Chinese indigo, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves and small, clustered pinkish-white flowers.
