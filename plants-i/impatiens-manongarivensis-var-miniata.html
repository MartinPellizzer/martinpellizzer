Impatiens manongarivensis var. miniata

Impatiens manongarivensis var. miniata, commonly known as the miniature impatiens, is a compact, flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Madagascar.

It features delicate, heart-shaped leaves and produces small, showy flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to thrive in shaded, humid environments. It is often cultivated as a ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
