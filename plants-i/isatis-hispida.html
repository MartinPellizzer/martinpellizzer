Isatis hispida

Isatis hispida, commonly known as the rough yellow rocket, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its upright, hairy stems and yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other mustard family members. Its leaves are pinnately lobed and often have a distinctive rough texture.

Isatis hispida is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of habitats.
