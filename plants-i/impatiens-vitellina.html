Impatiens vitellina

Impatiens vitellina, commonly known as the yellow water impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in wet, shady environments such as marshes and along stream banks. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Impatiens vitellina is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and wetland habitats.
