Ilex integra

Ilex integra, commonly known as the Chinese holly, is a broadleaf evergreen shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage with elliptical to ovate leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for ornamental landscaping.

Ilex integra is valued for its year-round visual appeal and hardy nature in temperate climates.
