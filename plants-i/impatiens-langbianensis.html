Impatiens langbianensis

Impatiens langbianensis, commonly known as the Langbian balsam, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It belongs to the Balsaminaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a glossy surface and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories, where it plays a role in local ecosystems.
