Isostigma riedelii

Isostigma riedelii, commonly known as the Riedel's isostigma, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip that is often marked with purple or brown streaks. Native to the mountainous regions of Central and South America, it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its natural range.
