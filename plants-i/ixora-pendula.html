Ixora pendula

Ixora pendula, commonly known as the weeping ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its pendulous clusters of bright orange-red flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy in appearance. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sun exposure.

Ixora pendula is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant floral display and attractive foliage.
