Ixia flabellularis

Ixia flabellularis, commonly known as the orchid oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that resemble orchids. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped and have a distinctive reddish or purplish hue.

Ixia flabellularis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
