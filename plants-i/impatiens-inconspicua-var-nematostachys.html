Impatiens inconspicua var. nematostachys

Impatiens inconspicua var. nematostachys, commonly known as the slender impatiens, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, upright stems and small, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with a smooth texture and a slightly hairy underside. This species thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique floral structure and its role in local ecosystems.
