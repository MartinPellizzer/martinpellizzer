Ixora laxiflora var. linderi

Ixora laxiflora var. linderi, commonly known as the slender-flowered ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, bright red or orange-red flowers. The leaves are opposite, simple, and elliptical, often with a slightly waxy texture. It thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

This variety is closely related to other ixora species and is valued for its resilience and attractive blooms.
