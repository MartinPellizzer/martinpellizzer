Illigera coryzadenia

Illigera coryzadenia, commonly known as the mountain orchid, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its roots are often covered in a dense network of fine, white roots, which help it absorb nutrients from the poor soils of its habitat.
