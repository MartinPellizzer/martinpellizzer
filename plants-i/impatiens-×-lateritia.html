Impatiens × lateritia

Impatiens × lateritia, commonly known as the hybrid impatiens, is a flowering plant species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its showy blooms. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden areas.

Impatiens × lateritia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in a variety of garden settings.
