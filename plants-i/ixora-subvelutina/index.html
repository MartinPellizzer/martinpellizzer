Ixora Subvelutina

Ixora subvelutina is a rare and striking member of the Rubiaceae family known for its vibrant orange-red flowers. Native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its unique velvety foliage and clusters of showy blooms make it a sought-after ornamental species. Despite its beauty, Ixora subvelutina remains relatively unknown to the general public. Cultivating this plant requires specific care to ensure its health and longevity.
