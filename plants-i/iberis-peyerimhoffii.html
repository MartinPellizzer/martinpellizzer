Iberis peyerimhoffii

Iberis peyerimhoffii, commonly known as the Peyer's candytuft, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Europe.

It features dense clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has aromatic, dark green leaves that are hairy to the touch. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.
