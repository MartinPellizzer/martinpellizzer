Isanthera permollis

Isanthera permollis, commonly known as the silky tamarind, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves with soft, silky hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in racemes. Its fruit is a legume pod that splits open to release seeds.

Isanthera permollis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local medicine.
