Ilex balearica var. cordata

Ilex balearica var. cordata, commonly known as the Balearic holly, is a shrub or small tree native to the Balearic Islands.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and dark green, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in coastal environments.
