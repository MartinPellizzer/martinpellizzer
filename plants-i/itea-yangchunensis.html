Itea yangchunensis

Itea yangchunensis, commonly known as the Yangchun spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to southern China.

It features slender, arching stems that bear small, white to pale pink flowers in early summer. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
