Iresine spiculigera f. pauciglandulosa

Iresine spiculigera f. pauciglandulosa, commonly known as the sparsely glandular irene, is a variant of the species Iresine spiculigera.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its striking, colorful foliage that often displays shades of red, purple, and green. The leaves are typically ovate to lanceolate in shape and may have a waxy texture. This plant is native to tropical regions and thrives in warm, humid climates.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant leaf coloration and compact growth habit.
