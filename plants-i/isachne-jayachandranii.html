Isachne jayachandranii

Isachne jayachandranii, commonly known as Jayachandran's isachne, is a grass species native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant typically features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and along water bodies.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
