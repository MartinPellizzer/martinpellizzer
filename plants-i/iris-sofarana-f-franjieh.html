Iris sofarana f. franjieh

Iris sofarana f. franjieh, commonly known as the Franjieh's iris, is a rare and unique botanical variant of the Iris sofarana species.

This subspecies is native to specific regions in the Middle East, particularly Lebanon, where it thrives in rocky and semi-arid environments.
