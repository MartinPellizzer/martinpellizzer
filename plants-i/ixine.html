Ixine

Ixine, commonly known as the rock cress, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Brassicaceae.

These plants are typically small, herbaceous perennials that thrive in rocky or sandy soils. They are native to regions across Europe, Asia, and North Africa. Ixine species often feature delicate, clustered flowers in shades of white or yellow.

The plants are valued for their ornamental appeal and are sometimes used in rock gardens or as ground cover.
