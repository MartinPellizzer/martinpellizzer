Isolepis nitens

Isolepis nitens, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to wetland environments in the Americas.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges, and is characterized by its triangular stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, which are clustered along the stem.

Isolepis nitens plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic and terrestrial organisms.
