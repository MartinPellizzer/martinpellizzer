Indocalamus mairei

Indocalamus mairei, commonly known as the Maire bamboo, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can reach heights of up to 10 meters. Indocalamus mairei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
