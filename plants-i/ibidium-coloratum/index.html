Ibidium Coloratum

Blue-eyed mary, scientifically known as Ibidium coloratum, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa. With its vibrant blue petals and a central white eye-like marking, it captivates the attention of both botanists and casual gardeners alike. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and drought-tolerant landscapes. Its unique appearance and resilience have made it a symbol of adaptability in harsh environments. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively unknown to the general public, hiding in the shadows of more mainstream flora.
