Isoloma × ortgiesii

Isoloma × ortgiesii, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a naturally occurring hybrid between Isoloma alba and Isoloma viridiflora.

It is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored flowers that display a range of hues including white, yellow, and green. The plant typically grows in coastal areas of southern Africa, where it thrives in sandy soils and mild climates. Its foliage consists of broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, Isoloma × ortgiesii is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
