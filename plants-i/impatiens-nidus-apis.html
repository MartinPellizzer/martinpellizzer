Impatiens nidus-apis

Impatiens nidus-apis, commonly known as the bee nest impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant produces small, heart-shaped leaves and has a trailing growth habit, making it suitable for hanging baskets or as a ground cover. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
