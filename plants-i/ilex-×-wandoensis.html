Ilex × wandoensis

Ilex × wandoensis, commonly known as the Wando holly, is a hybrid evergreen shrub or small tree native to the coastal regions of South Korea.

It belongs to the family Aquifoliaceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that remain throughout the year.
