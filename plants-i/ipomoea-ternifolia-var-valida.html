Ipomoea ternifolia var. valida

Ipomoea ternifolia var. valida, commonly known as the velvetleaf morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.
