Ixora scortechinii var. caulescens

Ixora scortechinii var. caulescens, commonly known as the slender ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub characterized by its branched, often sparsely foliaged stems and clusters of small, bright orange-red flowers. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture, while the plant produces small, fleshy fruits that are typically red when mature. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, well-drained soils.

This species is valued for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
