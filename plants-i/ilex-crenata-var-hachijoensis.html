Ilex crenata var. hachijoensis

Ilex crenata var. hachijoensis, commonly known as the Hachijo Island holly, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It features glossy, dark green, ovate leaves with wavy edges, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, red berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a foundation plant.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its dense foliage and year-round color.
