Iberis linifolia subsp. dunalii

Iberis linifolia subsp.

dunalii, commonly known as the Dunal's candytuft, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features dense clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring.

The plant has silver-gray foliage with finely divided leaves, giving it a distinctive, ornamental appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

This subspecies is valued for its hardiness and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
