Indigofera excelsa

Indigofera excelsa, commonly known as the tall indigo or blue bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a legume characterized by its erect growth habit and pinnate leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. Its seeds are encased in long, slender pods and are used in traditional medicine and as a source of dye.

Indigofera excelsa also plays a role in nitrogen fixation, enhancing soil fertility.
