Ixora helferi

Ixora helferi, commonly known as the Helfer's flame or Helfer's ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant is characterized by its bright red or orange-red flowers, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and elliptic in shape, with a glossy surface.

Ixora helferi is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes due to its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various soil types.
