Ixora aciculiflora

Ixora aciculiflora, commonly known as the needle-leaved ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, needle-like leaves and clusters of small, bright red flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Ixora aciculiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly used in horticulture and landscaping.
