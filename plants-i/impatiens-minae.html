Impatiens minae

Impatiens minae, commonly known as the Mina's Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30-60 centimeters. The plant features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are usually pink or red in color, with five petals. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The leaves are ovate and covered in fine, soft hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal.
