Ilex × camelliifolia

Ilex × camelliifolia, commonly known as the Chinese holly, is a hybrid evergreen shrub or small tree native to China.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are typically leathery and have a pointed shape. The plant produces small, bright red berries that are attractive to birds but toxic to humans and livestock. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its adaptability and aesthetic appeal contribute to its widespread cultivation in gardens and landscapes.
