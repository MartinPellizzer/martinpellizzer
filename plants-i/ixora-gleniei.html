Ixora gleniei

Ixora gleniei, commonly known as the Madagascar flame tree, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a striking appearance. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually oval or elliptical in shape. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sun exposure.

Ixora gleniei is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a landscape plant.
