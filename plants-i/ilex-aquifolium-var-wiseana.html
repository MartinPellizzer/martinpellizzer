Ilex aquifolium var. wiseana

Ilex aquifolium var. wiseana, commonly known as the Wiseman holly, is a variant of the common holly, characterized by its distinctive foliage and growth habits.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the British Isles, particularly found in the Scottish Highlands. The leaves are typically leathery, dark green on top, and have a silvery-white or pale underside, with spiny margins. It produces small, red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in garden settings.
