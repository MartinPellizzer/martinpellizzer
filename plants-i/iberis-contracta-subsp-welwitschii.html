Iberis contracta subsp. welwitschii

Iberis contracta subsp.

welwitschii, commonly known as the Cape Jasmine, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a low-growing, evergreen shrub that features dense clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers.

The plant has holly-like leaves that are glossy and slightly toothed. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its fragrant blooms and compact growth habit.
