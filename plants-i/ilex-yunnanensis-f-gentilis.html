Ilex yunnanensis f. gentilis

Ilex yunnanensis f. gentilis, commonly known as the Yunnan holly, is a shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Aquifoliaceae family and is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage. The leaves are typically dark green and glossy, with serrated margins. It produces small, red berries that are an important food source for birds.

This plant is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
