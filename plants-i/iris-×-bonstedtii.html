Iris × bonstedtii

Iris × bonstedtii, commonly known as the Bonstedt iris, is a hybrid perennial plant native to the United States.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers with a distinctive black or dark purple center. The plant typically grows to a height of 2 to 3 feet and features sword-shaped, grass-like leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Iris × bonstedtii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
