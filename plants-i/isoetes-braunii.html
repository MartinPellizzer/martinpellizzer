Isoetes braunii

Isoetes braunii, commonly known as the Braun's quillwort, is a species of aquatic plant belonging to the order Isoetales.

It is characterized by its dense, ribbon-like leaves that form a dense mat on the bottom of freshwater habitats. The plant is typically found in shallow, still waters such as ponds and wetlands. Isoetes braunii has a unique reproductive structure, with sporangia that release spores into the water.

This plant plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various microorganisms and invertebrates.
