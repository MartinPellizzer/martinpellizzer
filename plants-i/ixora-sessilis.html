Ixora sessilis

Ixora sessilis, commonly known as the sessile ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of small, brightly colored flowers, usually in shades of red, orange, or yellow. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a glossy texture.

Ixora sessilis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
