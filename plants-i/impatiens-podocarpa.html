Impatiens podocarpa

Impatiens podocarpa, commonly known as the mountain impatiens, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, spurred petals and upright growth habit. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its foliage is typically heart-shaped with a velvety texture.
