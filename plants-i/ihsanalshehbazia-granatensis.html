Ihsanalshehbazia granatensis

Ihsanalshehbazia granatensis, commonly known as the Granatense orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in late spring. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

The orchid thrives in well-drained soil and requires partial shade for optimal growth.
