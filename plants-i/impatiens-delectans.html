Impatiens delectans

Impatiens delectans, commonly known as the chocolate ice plant, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that help it store water, adapting it to arid environments. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
