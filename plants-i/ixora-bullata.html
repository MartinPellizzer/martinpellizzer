Ixora bullata

Ixora bullata, commonly known as the flame flower or red ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows up to 3 meters tall, featuring glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of bright red, flame-like flowers that bloom throughout the year, especially during the warmer months. Ixora bullata is well-suited to tropical and subtropical climates, thriving in full sun and well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
