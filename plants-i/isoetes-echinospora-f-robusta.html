Isoetes echinospora f. robusta

Isoetes echinospora f. robusta, commonly known as the robust spiny watermace, is a fern-like aquatic plant belonging to the genus Isoetes.

It is characterized by its spiky, spore-bearing structures called sporangia, which are typically arranged in a rosette pattern on the plant's stem. The plant thrives in freshwater environments, such as lakes and wetlands, where it anchors itself in the substrate. Its morphology includes a central stem with leaf-like structures that are often submerged or partially emergent.

This fern is notable for its unique reproductive features and ecological role in aquatic ecosystems.
