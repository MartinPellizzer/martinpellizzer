Isachne bhatii

Isachne bhatii, commonly known as the Bhati grass, is a species of grass native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, often hairy leaves. The plant typically grows in moist environments such as wetlands and marshy areas. Its inflorescence consists of a single spikelet, which is a distinctive feature of the genus Isachne.

Isachne bhatii plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various small organisms in its native habitat.
