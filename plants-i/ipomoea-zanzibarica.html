Ipomoea zanzibarica

Ipomoea zanzibarica, commonly known as the Zanzibar morning glory, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep purple. The plant produces heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Ipomoea zanzibarica is also known for its rapid growth and ability to climb, making it a popular choice for vertical gardening.
