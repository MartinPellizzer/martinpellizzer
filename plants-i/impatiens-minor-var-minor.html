Impatiens minor var. minor

Impatiens minor var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf impatiens, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It typically reaches a height of only a few inches, with delicate, spurred flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a velvety texture and a prominent midrib. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its rapid growth and prolific flowering make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a compact, ornamental plant.
