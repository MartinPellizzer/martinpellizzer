Iris × nyaradyana

Iris × nyaradyana, commonly known as the Nyaradyana iris, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Iris pseudacorus and Iris sibirica.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers with a distinctive yellow beard on the lower petals. The plant typically grows in wet or moist environments, such as marshes and along waterways. It has narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are usually dark green in color.

This hybrid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with adequate water supply.
