Iris prilipkoana

Iris prilipkoana, commonly known as the Philippine iris, is a species native to the Philippines.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its tall, slender stems and showy, violet-blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as forest understories and stream banks. Its leaves are grass-like, narrow, and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Iris prilipkoana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also important in local ecosystems.
