Indigofera trita subsp. subulata

Indigofera trita subsp.

subulata, commonly known as the slender indigo. It is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions, characterized by its narrow, needle-like leaves.

The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Its botanical features make it a valuable plant for both ornamental and ecological purposes.
