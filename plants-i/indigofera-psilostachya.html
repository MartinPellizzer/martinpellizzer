Indigofera psilostachya

Indigofera psilostachya, commonly known as the slender indigo, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit and trifoliate leaves with small, pointed leaflets. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale purple, typically blooming in the spring or early summer. Its seeds are enclosed in elongated pods, and the plant is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its delicate appearance.

Indigofera psilostachya is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
