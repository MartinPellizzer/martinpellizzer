Ixora collina var. minor

Ixora collina var. minor, commonly known as the small flame bush, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant, red flowers that bloom in clusters, often giving it a fiery appearance. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sun.

Ixora collina var. minor is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a landscape plant.
