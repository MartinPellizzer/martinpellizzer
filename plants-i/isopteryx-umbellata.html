Isopteryx umbellata

Isopteryx umbellata, commonly known as the umbrella fern, is a tropical plant characterized by its distinctive, umbrella-like fronds.

The plant features a creeping rhizome with clustered, pinnately divided leaves that give it its characteristic appearance.
