Ischaemum barbatum var. glaberrimum

Ischaemum barbatum var. glaberrimum, commonly known as the smooth brome grass, is a perennial grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically smooth and hairless. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained, fertile ground.

This variety is often used in forage and erosion control due to its hardiness and rapid growth.
