Indigofera viscidissima subsp.

viscidissima, commonly known as the sticky indigo. It is a flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae, characterized by its distinctive blue to purple flowers.

The plant is native to tropical regions of Africa and is often found in moist, shaded environments. It has hairy, sticky leaves and stems, which contribute to its common name.
