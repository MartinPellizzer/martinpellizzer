Ixora kurzii

Ixora kurzii, commonly known as the red torch plant, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, bright red flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a striking appearance. The plant has opposite, simple, and usually elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Ixora kurzii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
