Ixora parviflora var. zeylanica

Ixora parviflora var. zeylanica, commonly known as the Zeylanica ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact shrub form and clusters of small, bright orange-red flowers. The leaves are opposite, simple, and elliptical with a glossy surface. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sun.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a hedge.
