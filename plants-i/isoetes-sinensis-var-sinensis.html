Isoetes sinensis var. sinensis

Isoetes sinensis var. sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese water clover, is a species of aquatic fern belonging to the family Isoetaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, ribbon-like leaves that form dense, floating mats on the water surface. The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and lakes. Its reproductive structures, known as sporangia, are clustered in specialized leaf-like structures called sporophylls.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in water gardens and ecological restoration projects.
