Ionoxalis immaculata

Ionoxalis immaculata, commonly known as the silverleaf oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It features delicate, heart-shaped leaves with a silvery-white underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, five-petaled flowers in shades of pink, white, and yellow, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
