Icica protium

Icica protium, commonly known as the sacred fig, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Moraceae family and is characterized by its broad, lobed leaves and distinctive fruit. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in humid, tropical climates. Its roots form a dense network, making it a keystone species in its ecosystem.

The tree is also valued for its medicinal properties and cultural significance in various regions.
