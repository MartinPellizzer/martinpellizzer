Impatiens pianmaensis

Impatiens pianmaensis, commonly known as the Pianma Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Balsaminaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
