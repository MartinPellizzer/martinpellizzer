Indigofera minutiflora

Indigofera minutiflora, commonly known as the dwarf indigo or small-flowered indigo, is a perennial shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features small, delicate flowers that range in color from blue to purple, typically blooming in spring. The plant has trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
