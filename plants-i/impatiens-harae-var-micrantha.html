Impatiens harae var. micrantha

Impatiens harae var. micrantha, commonly known as the dwarf impatiens, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden beds.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to produce numerous blooms throughout the growing season.
