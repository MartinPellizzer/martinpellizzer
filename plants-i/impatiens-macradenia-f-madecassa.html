Impatiens macradenia f. madecassa

Impatiens macradenia f. madecassa, commonly known as the Madagascan impatiens, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Balsaminaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to pink. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in warm climates.
