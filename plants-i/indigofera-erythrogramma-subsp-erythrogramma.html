Indigofera erythrogramma subsp. erythrogramma

Indigofera erythrogramma subsp.

erythrogramma, commonly known as the red-stemmed indigo, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family Fabaceae. It is characterized by its slender, often reddish stems and trifoliate leaves with small, ovate leaflets.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and traditional medicine.
