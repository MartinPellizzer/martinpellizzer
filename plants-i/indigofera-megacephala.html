Indigofera megacephala

Indigofera megacephala, commonly known as the giant head indigo.

It is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its large, showy flowers that are typically violet or blue in color. Its leaves are alternate and pinnate, with small, oval-shaped leaflets.

The plant is often used in traditional medicine and has potential applications in modern pharmacology.
