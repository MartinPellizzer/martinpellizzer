Ilex yunnanensis var. gentilis

Ilex yunnanensis var. gentilis, commonly known as the Yunnan holly, is a deciduous evergreen shrub or small tree native to China.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
