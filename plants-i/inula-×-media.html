Inula × media

Inula × media, commonly known as the hybrid fleabane, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between Inula helenium and Inula crithmoides, characterized by its yellow-orange composite flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture. Its flowering period occurs in late summer to early autumn, and it thrives in full sun to partial shade in well-drained soils.

The plant is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and meadows.
