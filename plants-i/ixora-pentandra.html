Ixora pentandra

Ixora pentandra, commonly known as the flame bush, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, bright orange-red inflorescences that resemble flames, which bloom throughout the year in warm climates. The plant has opposite, simple, elliptic leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

Its adaptability and vibrant flowers make it a valuable addition to both landscape design and horticultural collections.
