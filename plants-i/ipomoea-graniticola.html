Ipomoea graniticola

Ipomoea graniticola, commonly known as the granite morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in the early morning. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is often found growing in rocky or calcareous soils. It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family, which includes many other flowering vines and climbers.
