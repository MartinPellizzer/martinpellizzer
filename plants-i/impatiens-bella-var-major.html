Impatiens bella var. major

Impatiens bella var. major, commonly known as the giant impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is popular in gardens for its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
