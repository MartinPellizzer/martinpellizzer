Illecebrum sericeum

Illecebrum sericeum, commonly known as silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its silver-gray foliage and small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant produces small, yellowish berries that are toxic to humans and animals. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky soils.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and traditional medicine.
