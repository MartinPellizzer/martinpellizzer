Isatis tomentella

Isatis tomentella, commonly known as the woolly cabbage or woolly Chinese cabbage, is a herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other cruciferous vegetables like broccoli and cauliflower. The plant is characterized by its hairy, grayish-green leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Isatis tomentella is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.
