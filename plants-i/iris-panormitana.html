Iris panormitana

Iris panormitana, commonly known as the Panormitana iris, is a species of flowering plant in the genus Iris.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and striking blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are sword-shaped and form a dense rosette at the base, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Iris panormitana is valued in gardens for its beauty and is often used in landscape design.
