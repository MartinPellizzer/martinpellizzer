Ipomoea stocksii

Ipomoea stocksii, commonly known as the Hawaiian morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a herbaceous vine that produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of blue, purple, and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is characterized by its rapid growth habit. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often climbing supports as it grows.

Ipomoea stocksii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also studied for its potential medicinal properties.
