Iris × nostras

Iris × nostras, commonly known as the Dutch iris, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Iris germanica and Iris pallida.

It is characterized by its tall, sturdy stems that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters. The flowers are typically large, showy, and come in a variety of colors including purple, blue, white, and yellow. The plant is native to the Netherlands and has been widely cultivated for its ornamental value.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.
