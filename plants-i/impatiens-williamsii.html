Impatiens williamsii

Impatiens williamsii, commonly known as Williams' impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Balsaminaceae and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. Impatiens williamsii is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
