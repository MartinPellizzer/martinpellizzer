Ilex perado subvar. spinulososerrata

Ilex perado subvar. spinulososerrata, commonly known as the spinulose-serrate holly, is a shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage with elliptical leaves that have a distinctive spinulose-serrate margin. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by bright red berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for shaded garden environments.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and seasonal color changes.
