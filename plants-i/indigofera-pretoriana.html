Indigofera pretoriana

Indigofera pretoriana, commonly known as the Pretoria indigo, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are pinnate, with small, oval-shaped leaflets, and it produces elongated pods containing seeds.

Indigofera pretoriana is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.
