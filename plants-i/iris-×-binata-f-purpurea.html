Iris × binata f. purpurea

Iris × binata f. purpurea, commonly known as the purple flag iris, is a hybrid perennial plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers, which typically bloom in early summer. The plant features sword-shaped, grass-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color. It is native to regions of Europe and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

This variety is known for its hardiness and ability to thrive in moist, well-drained soils.
