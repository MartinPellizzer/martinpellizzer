Iria thermalis

Iria thermalis, commonly known as the thermal iris, is a unique flowering plant native to specific thermal springs in certain regions.

It thrives in warm, mineral-rich soils and has adapted to high temperatures and alkaline conditions. The plant features striking violet-blue flowers with a distinctive yellow center, blooming during the spring season. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often growing in dense clusters.

Iria thermalis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also studied for its potential medicinal properties.
