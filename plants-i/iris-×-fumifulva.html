Iris × fumifulva

Iris × fumifulva, commonly known as the purple iris, is a hybrid perennial plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant produces sword-shaped, grass-like leaves that are typically a deep green color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Iris × fumifulva is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for.
