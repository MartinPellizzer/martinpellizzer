Iberis procumbens

Iberis procumbens, commonly known as candytuft, is a low-growing, flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense clusters of small, white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has a mat-forming habit with silver-gray foliage that is both ornamental and drought-tolerant. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact size and long flowering period make it a valuable addition to garden landscapes.
