Indigofera souliei

Indigofera souliei, commonly known as the Chinese indigo plant, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to violet. The plant produces pods containing seeds, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Indigofera souliei is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.
