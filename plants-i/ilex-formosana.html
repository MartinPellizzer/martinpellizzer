Ilex formosana

Ilex formosana, commonly known as the Formosan holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to Taiwan.

It is characterized by its dense, glossy dark green leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, white flowers in the spring, which are followed by bright red berries in the autumn. Ilex formosana is well adapted to a variety of soil types and can thrive in both full sun and partial shade.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and seasonal fruit display.
