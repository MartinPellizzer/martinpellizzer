Iresine heliotropifolius f. robusta

Iresine heliotropifolius f. robusta, commonly known as the redleaf plant or velvetleaf, is a striking ornamental plant characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is native to tropical regions of South America. The plant features large, broad leaves that often display a mix of red, purple, and green hues, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance and relatively easy care make it a favored addition to modern horticultural collections.
