Iris suaveolens

Iris suaveolens, commonly known as the sweet-scented iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, violet-blue to purple flowers with a distinctive, fragrant center. The plant has sword-shaped, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate appearance and subtle, sweet aroma.
