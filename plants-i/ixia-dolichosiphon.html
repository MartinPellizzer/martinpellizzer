Ixia dolichosiphon

Ixia dolichosiphon, commonly known as the long-beak lily, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllis family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its bloom period occurs during the summer months, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking vibrant, seasonal color.
