Iresine lindenii

Iresine lindenii, commonly known as the crimson flame plant, is a striking herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, bi-colored foliage, with deep green leaves featuring bright red or orange veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically pink or white, often overlooked in favor of its ornamental leaves. Iresine lindenii thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and indoor plant collections.
