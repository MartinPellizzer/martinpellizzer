Ianthe schlechteri

Ianthe schlechteri, commonly known as the Schlechter's lily, is a striking plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It belongs to the Alstroemeriaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant features strap-like leaves and can grow up to 2 meters in height, making it a notable addition to botanical gardens. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species in its natural range.
