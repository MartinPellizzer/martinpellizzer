Isertia laevis

Isertia laevis, commonly known as the smooth isertia, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that are often covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in dense, terminal clusters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a smooth, waxy texture.

Isertia laevis is adapted to grow in dry, sandy soils and is often found in grassland habitats.
