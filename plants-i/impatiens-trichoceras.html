Impatiens trichoceras

Impatiens trichoceras, commonly known as the hairy impatiens, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink. The plant features hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Impatiens trichoceras is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
