Ischaemum triticeum

Ischaemum triticeum, commonly known as slender brome, is a grass species native to Europe, Asia, and Africa.

It is a perennial plant that typically grows in moist meadows, grasslands, and along stream banks. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences. Its botanical features include a fibrous root system and a distinctive, slender stem.

It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
