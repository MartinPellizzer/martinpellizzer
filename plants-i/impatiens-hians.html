Impatiens hians

Impatiens hians, commonly known as the "Chinese violet," is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of pink, white, and purple. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets and garden borders.

Impatiens hians is valued for its ornamental beauty and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
