Impatiens shailajae

Impatiens shailajae, commonly known as the Shailajae balsam, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan regions of India.

It belongs to the Balsaminaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a range of colors, including pink, red, and white. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has heart-shaped leaves with a smooth texture.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses for its aesthetic qualities.
