Inodes schwarzii

Inodes schwarzii, commonly known as the blackwood plant, is a species native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its dark, almost black, woody stems and leaves that are often glossy and lance-shaped. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. Its leaves are used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Inodes schwarzii is also valued for its ornamental appeal due to its striking appearance and hardy nature.
