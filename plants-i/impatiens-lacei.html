Impatiens lacei

Impatiens lacei, commonly known as the lacey impatiens, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or stream banks. Its flowers are often visited by pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Impatiens lacei is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade.
