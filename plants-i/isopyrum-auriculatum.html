Isopyrum auriculatum

Isopyrum auriculatum, commonly known as the auriculate bistort, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Polygonaceae, which includes plants known for their distinctive leaf shapes and sometimes edible roots. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
