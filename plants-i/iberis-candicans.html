Iberis candicans

Iberis candicans, commonly known as the candytuft, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, white or yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has a low-growing habit with gray-green, lobed leaves that are often covered in a powdery coating. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Iberis candicans is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
