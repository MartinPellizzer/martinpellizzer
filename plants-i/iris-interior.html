Iris interior

Iris interior, commonly known as the interior iris, is a species native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its striking violet-blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade. Its leaves are sword-shaped and form a dense rosette near the ground.

The iris interior is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and delicate appearance.
