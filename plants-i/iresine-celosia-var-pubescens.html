Iresine celosia var. pubescens

Iresine celosia var. pubescens, commonly known as the flame flower or red flame, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its vibrant, feather-like bracts that range from red to orange in color. The plant has small, inconspicuous flowers that are surrounded by the showy bracts. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Iresine celosia var. pubescens is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its striking appearance.
