Iris baldshuanica

Iris baldshuanica, commonly known as the Chinese iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It features tall, slender stems that bear showy, violet-blue flowers with a distinctive three-lobed petal structure. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a fan-like pattern at the base. It typically blooms in late spring to early summer, adding a striking visual element to gardens.

Iris baldshuanica is well-suited for moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.
