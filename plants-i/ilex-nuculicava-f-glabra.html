Ilex nuculicava f. glabra

Ilex nuculicava f. glabra, commonly known as the smooth-leaved holly, is a variant of the holly plant characterized by its smooth, glossy leaves.

It is a evergreen shrub or small tree native to parts of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant features small, dark berries that are typically consumed by birds, aiding in seed dispersal. Its foliage is dense and provides excellent ornamental value in gardens.

The smooth texture of its leaves distinguishes it from other holly varieties, making it a unique addition to horticultural collections.
