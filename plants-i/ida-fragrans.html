Ida fragrans

Ida fragrans, commonly known as the fragrant ida, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a pale yellow or white hue. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a smooth texture with a glossy appearance.

Ida fragrans is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its use in traditional medicine.
