Impatiens hochstetteri subsp. fanshawei

Impatiens hochstetteri subsp.

fanshawei, commonly known as the Fanshawei Impatiens, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white.

The plant has heart-shaped leaves and a compact growth habit, making it suitable for both garden beds and container planting. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, preferring cooler temperatures.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in horticultural gardens.
