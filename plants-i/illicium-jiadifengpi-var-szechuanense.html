Illicium jiadifengpi var. szechuanense

Illicium jiadifengpi var. szechuanense, commonly known as Sichuan star anise, is a variant of the star anise plant native to China.

It is characterized by its aromatic, star-shaped fruits that contain essential oils rich in compounds like shikimic acid. The plant belongs to the Schisandraceae family and is typically grown in subtropical regions with well-drained soil. Its leaves are dark green and glossy, while its flowers are small and yellowish-green.

This variety is valued both for its culinary uses and traditional medicinal properties.
