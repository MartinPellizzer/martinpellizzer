Ixia amethystina

Ixia amethystina, commonly known as the amethyst lily, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The flowers are typically deep purple or violet, with a distinctive white throat, and bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant features strap-like, grassy leaves that are evergreen in mild climates.

Ixia amethystina is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
