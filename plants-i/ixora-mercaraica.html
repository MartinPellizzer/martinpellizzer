Ixora mercaraica

Ixora mercaraica, commonly known as the flame flower, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bright red or orange inflorescences, which consist of numerous small, tubular flowers clustered together. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and elliptical in shape, with a smooth texture.

Ixora mercaraica thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
