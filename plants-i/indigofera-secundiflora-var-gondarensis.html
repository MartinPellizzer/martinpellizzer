Indigofera secundiflora var. gondarensis

Indigofera secundiflora var. gondarensis, commonly known as the Gondar indigo plant, is a flowering shrub native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces pods that contain seeds, which are often used in traditional practices. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showing adaptability to poor soil conditions.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological and agricultural studies in its native region.
