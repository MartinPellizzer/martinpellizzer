Isatis pavlii

Isatis pavlii, commonly known as Chinese yellow mustard, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other mustard plants. The plant is characterized by its yellow flowers and elongated, ribbed pods that contain seeds. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Isatis pavlii is used in traditional medicine and as a source of oilseed.
