Isolepis kyllingioides

Isolepis kyllingioides, commonly known as the slender tussock grass, is a perennial plant native to wetland and marsh environments.

It forms dense, tussock-like clumps with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and colonize moist soils effectively.

Isolepis kyllingioides plays an important ecological role in stabilizing wetland soils and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
