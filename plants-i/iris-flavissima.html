Iris flavissima

Iris flavissima, commonly known as the Japanese flag iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It features upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. The plant produces striking blue to violet flowers with a distinctive yellow beard on the lower petals. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sun.

Iris flavissima is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and historical significance in Japanese culture.
