Ixora kroneana

Ixora kroneana, commonly known as the Krone's flame flower, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that can grow up to 3 meters tall, characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces clusters of bright red, orange, or yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year, especially during the warmer months. Its flowers are typically tubular with five lobes, and it has small, oval-shaped leaves.

Ixora kroneana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant.
