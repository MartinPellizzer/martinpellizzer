Ipomoea tastensis

Ipomoea tastensis, commonly known as the Tastensian morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in the early morning, hence its common name. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces vibrant, trumpet-shaped blossoms in shades of purple, pink, and white. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found growing in moist, shaded areas.

The vine-like growth habit of Ipomoea tastensis makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
