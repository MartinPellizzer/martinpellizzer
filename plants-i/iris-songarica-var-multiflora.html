Iris songarica var. multiflora

Iris songarica var. multiflora, commonly known as the Songaric iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features upright, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color and can grow up to 60 cm in height. The plant produces striking, showy flowers with three upright petals and three drooping sepals, often in shades of blue, purple, or white. It blooms in early summer and is adapted to well-drained, rocky soils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
