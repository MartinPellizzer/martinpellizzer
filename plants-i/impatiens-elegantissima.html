Impatiens elegantissima

Impatiens elegantissima, commonly known as the elegant impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that come in shades of pink, white, and red. The plant typically grows as an annual or biennial, reaching heights of up to 30 inches. Its foliage consists of heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for gardeners in temperate climates.
