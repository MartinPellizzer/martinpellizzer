Impatiens shiyomiensis

Impatiens shiyomiensis, commonly known as the Shiyomi Island Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the Ryukyu Islands of Japan.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments. It is often found growing in forest understories and along stream banks.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
