Ixora Hispidula

Ixora hispidula is a tropical evergreen shrub known for its vibrant, clustered flowers that bloom throughout the year. Native to Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in humid climates and well-drained soils. Its dense foliage and showy blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and landscape design. Easy to care for, it requires regular watering and partial to full sun exposure. Whether grown in containers or in the ground, Ixora hispidula adds lasting color and beauty to any garden.
