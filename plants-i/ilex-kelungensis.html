Ilex kelungensis

Ilex kelungensis, commonly known as the Kelung holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Aquifoliaceae and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in the spring, which are followed by red berries that are an important food source for birds.

Ilex kelungensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its dense foliage and year-round appearance.
