Ixora grandifolia var. arborescens

Ixora grandifolia var. arborescens, commonly known as the red flame ixora, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves and is renowned for its showy clusters of bright red flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant floral display and adaptability to various garden settings.

Its compact size and continuous blooming make it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical and subtropical regions.
