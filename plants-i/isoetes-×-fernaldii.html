Isoetes × fernaldii

Isoetes × fernaldii, commonly known as the hybrid water starwort, is a member of the water starwort family (Liliaceae) and is characterized by its unique morphology.

The plant is native to North America and is often found in freshwater habitats such as ponds and lakes. Isoetes × fernaldii is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Isoetes tetragona and Isoetes echinata, showcasing genetic diversity within the genus.

Its structure and growth habits make it an important component of aquatic ecosystems, providing habitat for various microorganisms and invertebrates.
