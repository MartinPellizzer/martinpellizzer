Ixora kingiana

Ixora kingiana, commonly known as the King's ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its clusters of small, bright red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a smooth surface with a slightly waxy texture.

Ixora kingiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
