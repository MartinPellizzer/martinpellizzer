Ipomoea desrousseauxii

Ipomoea desrousseauxii, commonly known as the Desrousseaux's morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its vibrant blue or violet flowers that bloom in the morning and close by midday. The plant has heart-shaped, velvety leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and botanical collections.
