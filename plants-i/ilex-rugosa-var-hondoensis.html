Ilex rugosa var. hondoensis

Ilex rugosa var. hondoensis, commonly known as the Hondo holly, is a shrub or small tree native to Japan.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and dark green, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, white flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a hardy ornamental plant.

Its compact growth habit and winter interest make it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
