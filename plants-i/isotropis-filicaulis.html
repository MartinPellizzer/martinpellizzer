Isotropis filicaulis

Isotropis filicaulis, commonly known as the slender leaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a daisy-like pattern. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Isotropis filicaulis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
