Impatiens griffithii var. sarcantha

Impatiens griffithii var. sarcantha, commonly known as the Kashmir impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan regions of India and Pakistan.

It is characterized by its delicate, spurred flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves and thrives in shaded, moist environments. It belongs to the genus Impatiens in the family Balsaminaceae.

This variant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
