Ixia columnaris var. purpurea

Ixia columnaris var. purpurea, commonly known as the purple columnar ixia, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, slender stems that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height, adorned with showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of purple and pink. The flowers bloom in spring, creating a dramatic display that attracts pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its unique appearance and vibrant color make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
