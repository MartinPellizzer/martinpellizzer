Indigofera goniodes var. damarensis

Indigofera goniodes var. damarensis, commonly known as the Damarensis indigo plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces pods that contain seeds, and it is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
