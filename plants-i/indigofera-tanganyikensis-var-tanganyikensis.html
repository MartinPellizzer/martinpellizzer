Indigofera tanganyikensis var. tanganyikensis

Indigofera tanganyikensis var. tanganyikensis, commonly known as the Tanganyika indigofera, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in moist, well-drained soils. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
