Ixia columnaris var. angustifolia

Ixia columnaris var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved candlebush, is a perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, candle-like flowers that are typically orange to red in color, with a distinctive tubular shape. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique and ornamental addition to botanical gardens and native plant collections.
