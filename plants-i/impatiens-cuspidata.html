Impatiens cuspidata

Impatiens cuspidata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf impatiens, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of delicate, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Impatiens cuspidata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
