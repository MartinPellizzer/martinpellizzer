Impatiens macrovexilla var. macrovexilla

Impatiens macrovexilla var. macrovexilla, commonly known as the broad-veined impatiens, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The leaves are usually ovate to elliptical in shape, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique floral structure and role in local ecosystems.
