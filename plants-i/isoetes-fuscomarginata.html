Isoetes fuscomarginata

Isoetes fuscomarginata, commonly known as the black-margin water fern, is a species of aquatic plant belonging to the genus Isoetes.

It is characterized by its spiky, ribbon-like leaves that are typically submerged in water. The plant has a unique reproductive structure called a spore sac, which is located at the base of the leaf. It thrives in freshwater environments such as lakes and ponds, often in shallow, still waters.

Its distinctive black margins on the leaf blades help in identifying it among other similar species.
