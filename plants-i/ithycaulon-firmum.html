Ithycaulon firmum

Ithycaulon firmum, commonly known as the hardy ground orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the temperate regions of southern Africa.

It typically grows to a height of 10-20 centimeters, featuring a fibrous root system and dense, grass-like foliage. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white and pale pink, often blooming in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
