Impatiens balsamina var. brevicalcarata

Impatiens balsamina var. brevicalcarata, commonly known as the dwarf touch-me-not, is a compact variety of the common impatiens.

It features showy, spurred flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden borders and containers.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
