Ixora Paludosa

Ixora paludosa is a fascinating flowering plant native to wetland areas of Southeast Asia. It is known for its vibrant red or orange blooms that attract pollinators like bees and birds. This species thrives in moist, swampy environments and is often found in mangrove forests. Its unique ability to tolerate waterlogged soils makes it an important part of coastal ecosystems. Despite its beauty, Ixora paludosa remains relatively under-studied compared to more common ornamental plants.
