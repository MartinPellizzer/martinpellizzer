Ipomoea stenophylla f. glabrata

Ipomoea stenophylla f. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth-leaved morning glory, is a variant of the Ipomoea stenophylla species.

It is characterized by its smooth, hairless leaves and vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of blue or purple. This plant is native to tropical regions and thrives in warm, humid climates. It is a climbing vine that can grow quite long, often requiring support structures for its growth.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and horticultural collections.
