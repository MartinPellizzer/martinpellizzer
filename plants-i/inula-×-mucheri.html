Inula × mucheri

Inula × mucheri, commonly known as the hybrid fleabane, is a perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a cross between Inula helenium and Inula crithmoides, resulting in a plant with distinctive yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring sturdy stems and lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence, and it thrives in well-drained soils in full sun.

Inula × mucheri is often found in meadows, roadsides, and disturbed areas, showcasing its adaptability to various environments.
