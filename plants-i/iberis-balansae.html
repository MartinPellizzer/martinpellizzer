Iberis balansae

Iberis balansae, commonly known as the Balansae candytuft, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in spring. Its leaves are simple and alternate, with a slightly hairy texture.

Iberis balansae is often grown as an ornamental plant for its delicate appearance and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
