Inula bourgaeana

Inula bourgaeana, commonly known as the African buttercup, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Inula bourgaeana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
