Iris × germanica var. illyrica

Iris × germanica var. illyrica, commonly known as the Illyrian iris, is a hybrid iris species native to the Balkan region.

It is characterized by its striking purple to violet flowers with a distinctive white or light blue center. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm, featuring sword-shaped, dark green leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
