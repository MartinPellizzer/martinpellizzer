Ilex microcarpa

Ilex microcarpa, commonly known as the dwarf holly, is a small evergreen shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. Ilex microcarpa thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping in shaded garden settings.
