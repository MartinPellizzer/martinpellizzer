Ixora oreogena

Ixora oreogena, commonly known as the mountain ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of bright orange-red flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually ovate to lanceolate in shape. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for tropical and subtropical climates.

Ixora oreogena is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
