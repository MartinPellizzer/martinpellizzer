Isoetes braunii var. maritima

Isoetes braunii var. maritima, commonly known as the sea spike rush, is a species of aquatic plant belonging to the genus Isoetes.

It is characterized by its spore-bearing structures called sporangia, which are typically found on specialized leaves called sporophylls. This plant is adapted to grow in brackish or saline environments, such as coastal marshes and estuaries. Its morphology includes long, narrow leaves that are often submerged or floating.

Isoetes braunii var. maritima plays an important ecological role in stabilizing sediments and providing habitat for aquatic organisms.
