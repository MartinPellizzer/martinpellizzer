Iberis intermedia subsp. boppardensis

Iberis intermedia subsp.

boppardensis, commonly known as the Boppardensis candytuft, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Europe. It features dense clusters of white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring.

The plant has a mat-forming habit with silver-gray foliage that is covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value comes from its delicate flowers and compact growth form.
