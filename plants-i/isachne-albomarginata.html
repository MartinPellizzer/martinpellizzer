Isachne albomarginata

Isachne albomarginata, commonly known as the white-margin grass, is a species of grass native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, blade-like leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences, which are typically green to reddish in color. Its leaves often have a distinctive white or pale margin, giving it its common name.

Isachne albomarginata is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
