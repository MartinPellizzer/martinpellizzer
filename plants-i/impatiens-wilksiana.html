Impatiens wilksiana

Impatiens wilksiana, commonly known as the Wilks' impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves and a trailing growth habit, making it popular for hanging baskets and containers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, requiring consistent moisture to flourish.

Its ornamental value makes it a favored choice in gardens and floral arrangements.
