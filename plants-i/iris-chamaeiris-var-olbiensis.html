Iris chamaeiris var. olbiensis

Iris chamaeiris var. olbiensis, commonly known as the Olbiense Iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It features upright, slender stems that bear narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a bluish-green hue. The plant produces striking violet-blue flowers with a distinctive three-lobed lower petal, typical of the iris family. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its ornamental value and adaptability contribute to its popularity among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts.
