Ixora brachyantha

Ixora brachyantha, commonly known as the red ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, branched growth habit and clusters of small, bright red flowers. The plant produces small, yellowish fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Ixora brachyantha is also valued for its ability to tolerate poor soil conditions and its use in traditional medicine.
