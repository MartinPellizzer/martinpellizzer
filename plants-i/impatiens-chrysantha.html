Impatiens chrysantha

Impatiens chrysantha, commonly known as the golden touch plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features striking, showy yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Impatiens chrysantha is popular in gardens for its vibrant color and ease of cultivation.
