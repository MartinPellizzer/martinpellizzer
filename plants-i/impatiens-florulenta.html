Impatiens florulenta

Impatiens florulenta, commonly known as the fragrant impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters and thrives in moist, well-drained soil. It prefers partial to full shade and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens florulenta is valued for its ornamental beauty and is commonly cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
