Ixia lapeirousia

Ixia lapeirousia, commonly known as the Orange Rainflower, is a striking South African bulbous plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It is native to the Western Cape region and is characterized by its vibrant orange flowers that resemble raindrops. The plant produces a single, showy flower per stem, which blooms in late winter to early spring. Ixia lapeirousia thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Its unique appearance and seasonal blooming make it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
