Inula × paiensis

Inula × paiensis, commonly known as the hybrid yellowhead, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of central and eastern China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant produces yellow to orange composite flowers, which are arranged in a single terminal head, giving it a striking appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade, making it adaptable to various garden environments.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant color and ease of cultivation.
