Iresine hassleriana var. macrophylla

Iresine hassleriana var. macrophylla, commonly known as the red leaf iron plant, is a strikingly ornamental shrub native to South America.

It features large, velvety, ovate leaves that are typically a deep red or maroon color, often with a contrasting green underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or pink flowers that appear in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

This species is popular in gardens for its vibrant foliage and low maintenance requirements.
