Indigofera tanganyikensis var. strigulosior

Indigofera tanganyikensis var. strigulosior, commonly known as the Tanganyika indigofera, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant is typically found in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and riverine areas. It produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or water.

This species is of interest for its ecological role and potential medicinal properties.
