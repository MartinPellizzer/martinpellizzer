Ixia scariosa

Ixia scariosa, commonly known as the red-hot poker, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, torch-like inflorescences. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically red, orange, or yellow in color. Ixia scariosa thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its foliage consists of strap-shaped leaves that are narrow and grass-like in appearance.
