Iberis linifolia f. denticulata

Iberis linifolia f. denticulata, commonly known as the denticulate candytuft, is a compact, low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has gray-green, hairy leaves that are often lobed or toothed, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
