Inga splendens f. superba

Inga splendens f. superba, commonly known as the royal flame tree, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual impact. The plant produces elongated, woody pods that contain edible seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. Its rapid growth rate and adaptability make it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

The foliage provides dense shade, making it suitable for both ornamental and functional uses in tropical gardens.
