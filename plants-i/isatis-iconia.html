Isatis iconia

Isatis iconia, commonly known as the Chinese yellow flag, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cabbage and mustard. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in moist environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Isatis iconia is also noted for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.
