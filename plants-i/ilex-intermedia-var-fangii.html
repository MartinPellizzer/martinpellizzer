Ilex intermedia var. fangii

Ilex intermedia var. fangii, commonly known as the Chinese holly, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves that are often glossy and have a dense, spiny appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
