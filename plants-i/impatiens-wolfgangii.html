Impatiens wolfgangii

Impatiens wolfgangii, commonly known as the Wolfgang's impatiens, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and grows as an erect herbaceous perennial. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens wolfgangii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses for its striking floral display.
