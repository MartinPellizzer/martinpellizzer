Isachne jardinii

Isachne jardinii, commonly known as the garden isachne, is a grass species native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, narrow leaves that are typically arranged in a compact rosette. The plant produces delicate inflorescences composed of spikelets, which are often clustered at the tips of the stems. Isachne jardinii is adapted to a variety of soil types and can thrive in both moist and dry conditions.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in ecological and horticultural studies.
