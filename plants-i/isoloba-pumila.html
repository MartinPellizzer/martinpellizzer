Isoloba pumila

Isoloba pumila, commonly known as the dwarf lily of the Nile, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces striking white or pale pink flowers with a distinctive spurred petal. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Isoloba pumila is adapted to arid environments and prefers well-drained soil.
