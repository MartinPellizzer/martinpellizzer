Ischaemum paniceum

Ischaemum paniceum, commonly known as Panicled Barnyard Grass, is a species of grass native to temperate regions of Europe, Asia, and North America.

It is an annual plant characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, three-lobed florets. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural fields and along roadsides.

Ischaemum paniceum is known for its adaptability and can be a weed in cultivated areas.
