Impatiens bracteolata

Impatiens bracteolata, commonly known as the bracteolate impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically come in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and its stems are often hairy as well. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens bracteolata is a popular choice for ornamental gardening due to its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various growing conditions.
