Ipomoea mombassana var. mombassana

Ipomoea mombassana var. mombassana, commonly known as the Mombassa morning glory, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves and produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of pink to purple. The plant is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant.

The flowers typically bloom in the early morning, giving the plant its common name.
