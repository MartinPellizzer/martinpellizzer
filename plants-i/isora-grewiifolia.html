Isora grewiifolia

Isora grewiifolia, commonly known as the Cape jasmine, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.
