Impatiens oblongipetala

Impatiens oblongipetala, commonly known as the broad-petaled impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens oblongipetala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses worldwide.
