Impatiens cinnabarina

Impatiens cinnabarina, commonly known as the red impatiens, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red, showy flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Impatiens cinnabarina is a popular ornamental plant due to its vibrant color and ease of cultivation.
