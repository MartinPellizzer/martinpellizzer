Isachne smitinandiana

Isachne smitinandiana, commonly known as the Smitinandian grass, is a perennial grass species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are typically arranged in panicles. Its botanical features include a fibrous root system and a preference for moist, well-drained soils.

Isachne smitinandiana is often found in wetlands and along the edges of water bodies in its native habitat.
