Ilex sugerokii var. longepedunculata

Ilex sugerokii var. longepedunculata, commonly known as the long-peduncled holly, is a variant of the holly genus native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and red berries that provide winter interest. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its year-round foliage and seasonal berries.
