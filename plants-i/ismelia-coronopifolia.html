Ismelia coronopifolia

Ismelia coronopifolia, commonly known as the crownleaf.

It is a flowering plant native to the western United States, particularly found in California and Oregon. The plant has compound leaves with a distinctive crown-like arrangement of leaflets. Its flowers are small, white, and clustered in dense inflorescences.

Ismelia coronopifolia is often found in rocky habitats and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
