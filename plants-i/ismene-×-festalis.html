Ismene × festalis

Ismene × festalis, commonly known as the hybrid lily, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Liliaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with strap-like leaves that are broad and lance-shaped. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, and it thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.

Ismene × festalis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
