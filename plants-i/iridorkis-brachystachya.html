Iridorkis brachystachya

Iridorkis brachystachya, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed iridorkis, is a perennial herb native to the grasslands of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, clustered inflorescences that produce delicate, blue to violet flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for dry, open habitats. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often hairy to the touch.

Iridorkis brachystachya is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
