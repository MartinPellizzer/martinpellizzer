Ilex montana var. orientalis

Ilex montana var. orientalis, commonly known as the oriental holly, is a shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for ornamental landscaping.

Due to its hardiness and attractive appearance, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a hedging plant.
