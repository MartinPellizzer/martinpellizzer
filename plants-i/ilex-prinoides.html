Ilex prinoides

Ilex prinoides, commonly known as the dwarf holly, is a compact evergreen shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features small, glossy, dark green leaves that are typically oval in shape. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Its dense growth habit makes it an excellent choice for hedges or topiary in gardens.
