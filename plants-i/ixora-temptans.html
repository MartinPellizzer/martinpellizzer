Ixora temptans

Ixora temptans, commonly known as the flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of bright orange-red flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are elliptical in shape. Ixora temptans thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its striking appearance and long-lasting blooms make it a valuable addition to gardens and landscapes.
