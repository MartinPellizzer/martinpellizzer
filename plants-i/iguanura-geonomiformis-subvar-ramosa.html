Iguanura geonomiformis subvar. ramosa

Iguanura geonomiformis subvar. ramosa, commonly known as the hairy sycamore, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
