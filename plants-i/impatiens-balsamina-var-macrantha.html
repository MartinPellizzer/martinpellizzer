Impatiens balsamina var. macrantha

Impatiens balsamina var. macrantha, commonly known as the giant impatiens, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range from pink to red in color. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its rapid growth and ability to spread quickly make it both a popular garden plant and a potential weed in certain regions.
