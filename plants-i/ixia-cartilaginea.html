Ixia cartilaginea

Ixia cartilaginea, commonly known as the cartilaginous ixia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant features strap-like, grassy leaves and produces tall, slender stems that support its vibrant blooms. Ixia cartilaginea thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value has led to its cultivation in various regions beyond its native habitat.
