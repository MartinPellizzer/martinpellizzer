Ixora pumila

Ixora pumila, commonly known as the dwarf flame bush, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of bright orange-red flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for container gardening.

Ixora pumila is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its vibrant floral display.
