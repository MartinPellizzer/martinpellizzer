Ilex Triflora Var. Viridis

Greenleaf holly, scientifically known as Ilex triflora var. viridis, is a unique and resilient evergreen shrub native to the southeastern United States. This plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that provide year-round visual interest in gardens and landscapes. It thrives in partial to full shade and is adaptable to a variety of soil conditions, making it a popular choice for ornamental use. With its compact growth habit and winter hardiness, it is well-suited for use as a hedge or foundation plant. Its ecological value also makes it an important species for supporting local wildlife.
