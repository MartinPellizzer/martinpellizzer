Indigofera minbuensis

Indigofera minbuensis, commonly known as the Burmese indigo plant, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family Fabaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in Myanmar and Thailand. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. It produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds.

This species is often cultivated for its potential use in traditional medicine and as a source of natural dyes.
