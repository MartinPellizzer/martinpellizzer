Iberis rollii

Iberis rollii, commonly known as the Canary Islands candytuft, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cress plants. The plant features small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in racemes, and its leaves are simple and alternate. It thrives in rocky and coastal environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.
