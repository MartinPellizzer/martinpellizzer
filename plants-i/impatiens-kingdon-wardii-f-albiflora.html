Impatiens kingdon-wardii f. albiflora

Impatiens kingdon-wardii f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered Indian impatiens, is a striking hybrid plant native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy white flowers that contrast beautifully with its dark green foliage. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, and thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its flowers are borne in clusters and are known for their long-lasting bloom, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

This variety is valued for its unique coloration and adaptability to a range of growing conditions.
