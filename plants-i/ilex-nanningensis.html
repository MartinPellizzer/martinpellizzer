Ilex nanningensis

Ilex nanningensis, commonly known as Nanning holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to southern China.

It belongs to the family Aquifoliaceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for local wildlife. It typically grows in forested areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Ilex nanningensis is also used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
