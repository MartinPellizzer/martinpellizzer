Iberis linifolia subsp. stricta

Iberis linifolia subsp.

stricta, commonly known as the narrowleaf candytuft, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It features dense clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring.

The plant has wiry, upright stems and narrow, linear leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its fragrant blossoms and drought tolerance.
