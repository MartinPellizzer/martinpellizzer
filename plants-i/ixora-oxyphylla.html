Ixora oxyphylla

Ixora oxyphylla, commonly known as the flame tree or red flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences of bright red or orange-red flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has opposite, simple, elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The bark is smooth and reddish-brown, and the plant is relatively drought-tolerant once established.
