Isoloma strictum

Isoloma strictum, commonly known as the narrowleaf sandmat, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are yellow and occur in clusters, adding visual interest to the landscape.

Isoloma strictum is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils and is often found in sandy or gravelly habitats.
