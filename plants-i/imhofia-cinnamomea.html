Imhofia cinnamomea

Imhofia cinnamomea, commonly known as the cinnamon flame lily, is a striking herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features tall, sword-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple hues, adding visual interest to its appearance. The plant produces vibrant orange to red flowers that bloom in summer, creating a dramatic display. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its unique coloration and showy blooms make it a standout in the botanical world.
