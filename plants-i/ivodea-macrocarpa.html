Ivodea macrocarpa

Ivodea macrocarpa, commonly known as the giant ivy, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of white or pale yellow. Its fruits are fleshy berries that turn red when mature, attracting various bird species.

Ivodea macrocarpa is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.
