Ilex cornuta var. burfordii

Ilex cornuta var. burfordii, commonly known as Burford holly, is a deciduous evergreen shrub or small tree native to the southeastern United States.

It features lance-shaped, dark green leaves that turn red in the fall, providing seasonal visual interest. The plant produces small, bright red berries that are an important food source for birds. It has a dense, compact growth habit and is often used as a hedge or ornamental plant.

Its adaptability to various soil types and moderate drought tolerance make it a popular choice in landscaping.
