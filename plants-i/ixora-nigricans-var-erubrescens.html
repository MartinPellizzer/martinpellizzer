Ixora nigricans var. erubrescens

Ixora nigricans var. erubrescens, commonly known as the red-flowered ixora, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or orange-red flowers that bloom in dense clusters, making it a popular ornamental plant. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and glossy, providing a striking contrast to the bright flowers. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
