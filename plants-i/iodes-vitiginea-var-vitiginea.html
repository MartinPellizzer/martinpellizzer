Iodes vitiginea var. vitiginea

Iodes vitiginea var. vitiginea, commonly known as the violet tamarisk, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from pale purple to deep violet. The plant has small, narrow leaves and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is well adapted to arid conditions and can thrive in full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens in warm climates.
