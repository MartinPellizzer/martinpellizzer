Impatiens johnsiana

Impatiens johnsiana, commonly known as the red-eyed impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically feature a bright red center surrounded by white or pale pink petals. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of hair. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Impatiens johnsiana is a popular ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and adaptability to cultivated environments.
