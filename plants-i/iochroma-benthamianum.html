Iochroma benthamianum

Iochroma benthamianum, commonly known as the silverleaf bellflower, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades, and is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are usually silver-white in color, giving it a distinctive appearance.
