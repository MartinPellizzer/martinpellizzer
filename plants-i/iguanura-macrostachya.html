Iguanura macrostachya

Iguanura macrostachya, commonly known as the giant marsh orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, showy flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in wet, marshy environments and is often found in swampy forests. Its unique floral structure includes long, slender petals and a distinctive, elongated lip.

Iguanura macrostachya is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays an important ecological role in its native habitat.
