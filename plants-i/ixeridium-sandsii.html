Ixeridium sandsii

Ixeridium sandsii, commonly known as the sandstone daisy, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of white and yellow, often blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

Ixeridium sandsii is often found in coastal and inland sandstone regions, where it forms part of the local flora.
