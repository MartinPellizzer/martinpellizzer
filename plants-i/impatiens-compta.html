Impatiens compta

Impatiens compta, commonly known as the dwarf impatiens, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, showy flowers. The plant typically produces vibrant pink or red blossoms that bloom throughout the growing season. Its leaves are heart-shaped and glossy, providing a lush backdrop to the flowers.

Impatiens compta is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in partial shade.
