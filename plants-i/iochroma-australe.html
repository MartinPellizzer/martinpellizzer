Iochroma australe

Iochroma australe, commonly known as the Andean bellflower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades, and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and thrives in cooler climates. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.
