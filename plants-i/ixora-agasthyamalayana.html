Ixora agasthyamalayana

Ixora agasthyamalayana, commonly known as the Agasthyamalai ixora, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded areas of dense forests. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually bright red or orange in color. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a smooth texture.

Ixora agasthyamalayana is valued for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
