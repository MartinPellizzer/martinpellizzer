Iris virescens subsp. bosniaca

Iris virescens subsp.

bosniaca, commonly known as the Bosnian green iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Balkan region. It features upright, slender stems that bear striking blue to violet flowers with a distinctive white to pale yellow center.

The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after plant for gardens and botanical collections.
