Ixora coccinea var. aureorosea

Ixora coccinea var. aureorosea, commonly known as the golden rose ixora, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range from orange to deep red, often with a golden or yellowish hue. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves that provide a striking contrast to its colorful blooms. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

This variety is popular in ornamental gardening for its long-lasting floral display and adaptability to tropical and subtropical environments.
