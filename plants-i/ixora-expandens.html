Ixora expandens

Ixora expandens, commonly known as the flame bush, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of bright red or orange flowers, which bloom throughout the year in many regions. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Ixora expandens is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant floral display.
