Iris × Germanica Var. Australis

Australian iris, scientifically known as Iris × germanica var. australis, is a striking hybrid plant native to Australia. It is prized for its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from deep purples to soft blues. This variety thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners in temperate climates. Its unique appearance and resilience have made it a favorite in both ornamental gardens and natural landscapes. Despite its popularity, it remains relatively uncommon in cultivation compared to other iris species.
