Iresine aurata var. amazonica

Iresine aurata var. amazonica, commonly known as the red flame plant, is a strikingly colorful shrub native to South America.

It features vibrant, velvety leaves that range from deep red to orange and pink, depending on light exposure. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red, adding a subtle touch to its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular in tropical gardens and as a houseplant due to its eye-catching foliage.
