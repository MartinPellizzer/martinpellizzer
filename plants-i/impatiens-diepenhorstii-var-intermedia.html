Impatiens diepenhorstii var. intermedia

Impatiens diepenhorstii var. intermedia, commonly known as the hybrid impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and clusters of showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptic, with a smooth or slightly serrated margin. This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.

It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers moist, well-drained soil.
