Iris histrioides var. sophenensis

Iris histrioides var. sophenensis, commonly known as the Sierra iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It features tall, slender stems that bear showy, violet-blue flowers with a distinctive yellow center. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in meadows and open woodlands.

Its botanical structure is adapted to the dry, mountainous environments of its native range.
