Ixora scheffleri

Ixora scheffleri, commonly known as the flame-of-the-forest, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange-red flowers that bloom in dense clusters, giving it a striking appearance. The plant has simple, opposite, and elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Ixora scheffleri is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a hedge plant.
