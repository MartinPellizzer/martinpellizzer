Ilex sugerokii subsp. brevipedunculata

Ilex sugerokii subsp.

brevipedunculata, commonly known as the short-stemmed holly, is a shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia. It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and small, dark green leaves that are typically elliptical in shape.

The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in temperate climates.
