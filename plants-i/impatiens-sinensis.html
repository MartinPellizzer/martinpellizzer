Impatiens sinensis

Impatiens sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese violet, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a range of colors including pink, red, and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Impatiens sinensis is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
