Isoetes braunii f. robusta

Isoetes braunii f. robusta, commonly known as the robust spikerush, is a aquatic plant belonging to the genus Isoetes.

It is characterized by its spiky, segmented spore-bearing structures called strobila, which are typically found submerged in freshwater environments. The plant has a unique morphology with a central axis and specialized leaves that help in anchoring it to the substrate. It is adapted to tolerate low light conditions and is often found in still or slow-moving waters.

Its reproductive structures are essential for its propagation and ecological role in aquatic ecosystems.
