Isachne streptostachys

Isachne streptostachys, commonly known as the zigzag grass, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, linear leaves with a slight zigzag pattern along the edges. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in dense clusters, which are typically arranged in panicle inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Isachne streptostachys is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
