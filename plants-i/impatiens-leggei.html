Impatiens leggei

Impatiens leggei, commonly known as the Malagasy impatiens, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Balsaminaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. Impatiens leggei thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
