Iris unguicularis f. pavonia

Iris unguicularis f. pavonia, commonly known as the Pavonia iris, is a hybrid species within the Iridaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking violet-blue flowers with a white or pale yellow center. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun. Its foliage consists of sword-shaped leaves that are often a deep green.

This ornamental plant is valued for its beauty and is commonly cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
