Iris × iocyanea

Iris × iocyanea, commonly known as the blue flag iris, is a hybrid perennial plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of ponds, preferring moist, well-drained soils. Its leaves are sword-shaped and form a dense rosette, while its rhizomes help it spread vegetatively.

Iris × iocyanea is valued for its ornamental beauty and its role in wetland ecosystems.
