Ipomopsis guttata

Ipomopsis guttata, commonly known as the spotted flaxflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its tall, branching stems and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple.
