Ione sasakii

Ione sasakii, commonly known as the Pacific Coast iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western coast of North America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and along stream banks. The plant features tall, slender stems that bear showy, violet-blue flowers with a distinctive white and purple pattern. Its leaves are sword-shaped and form a basal rosette, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Ione sasakii plays an important ecological role by providing habitat and nectar for pollinators.
