Isodon shikokianus var. shikokianus

Isodon shikokianus var. shikokianus, commonly known as the Shikoku isodon, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are lanceolate and have a serrated margin, while the flowers are small and clustered in whorls.

The species is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
