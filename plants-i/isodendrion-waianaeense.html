Isodendrion waianaeense

Isodendrion waianaeense, commonly known as the Kauai mountain fern, is a rare and endemic species native to the island of Kauai in Hawaii.

It is a fern-like plant characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that grow in a rosette pattern. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in rocky crevices and along stream banks. Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.

Due to habitat loss and invasive species, it is considered vulnerable, highlighting the need for preservation efforts.
