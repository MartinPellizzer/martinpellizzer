Ixora volkensii

Ixora volkensii, commonly known as the African flame or red flame bush, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of bright red flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant blooms. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes in warm climates.

Ixora volkensii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in hedges or as a specimen plant.
