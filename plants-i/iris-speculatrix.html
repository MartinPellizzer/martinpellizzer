Iris speculatrix

Iris speculatrix, commonly known as the mirror iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant features sword-shaped, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. Its rhizomatous root system allows it to spread and form dense colonies in well-drained soils.

Iris speculatrix is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to full sun to partial shade conditions.
