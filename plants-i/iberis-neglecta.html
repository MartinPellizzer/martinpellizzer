Iberis neglecta

Iberis neglecta, commonly known as the rock cress, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in arid environments. It thrives in rocky or well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Iberis neglecta is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate harsh conditions.
