Isolepis elachista

Isolepis elachista, commonly known as the slender bulrush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetland environments in North America.

It typically grows in shallow water or moist soil, forming dense stands that help stabilize shorelines. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences that bloom in late summer. Its rhizomes are fibrous and contribute to its ability to thrive in aquatic conditions.

Isolepis elachista plays an important ecological role by providing habitat and food for various wetland organisms.
