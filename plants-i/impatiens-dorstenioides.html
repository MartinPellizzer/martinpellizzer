Impatiens dorstenioides

Impatiens dorstenioides, commonly known as the dwarf impatiens, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features delicate, showy flowers that are typically pink or white, with five petals and a spurred lower petal. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually hairy and have a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its rapid growth and ornamental value make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a hardy, shade-tolerant flowering plant.
