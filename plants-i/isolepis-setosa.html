Isolepis setosa

Isolepis setosa, commonly known as tufted flatsedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to wetland environments.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, commonly referred to as sedges. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are typically wind-pollinated. Isolepis setosa thrives in moist soils and is often found in marshes, pond edges, and other aquatic habitats.
