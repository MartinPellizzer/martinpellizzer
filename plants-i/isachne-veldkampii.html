Isachne veldkampii

Isachne veldkampii, commonly known as the Veldkamp's isachne, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands and savannas, where it thrives in well-drained soils. The plant has slender, erect stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often hairy along the edges. Its inflorescence consists of a single spikelet, which produces small, wind-pollinated flowers.

Isachne veldkampii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
