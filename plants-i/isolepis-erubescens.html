Isolepis erubescens

Isolepis erubescens, commonly known as the red spikegrass, is a flowering plant native to wetland environments in South America.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, commonly known as the sedge family. The plant is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and produces small, clustered inflorescences that are typically reddish in color. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to form extensive mats in moist soils.
