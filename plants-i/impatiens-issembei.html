Impatiens issembei

Impatiens issembei, commonly known as the African violet.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa. The plant features showy, clustered flowers in shades of pink, purple, and white. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and covered in a fine layer of white wax.

Impatiens issembei thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.
