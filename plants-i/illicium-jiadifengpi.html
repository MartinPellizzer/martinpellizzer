Illicium jiadifengpi

Illicium jiadifengpi, commonly known as Chinese star anise, is a deciduous shrub native to southern China.

It belongs to the family Schisandraceae and is characterized by its aromatic, star-shaped fruits that resemble anise. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by dark brown, spice-like berries. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly rough texture.
