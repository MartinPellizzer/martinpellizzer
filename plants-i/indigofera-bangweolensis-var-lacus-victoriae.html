Indigofera bangweolensis var. lacus-victoriae

Indigofera bangweolensis var. lacus-victoriae, commonly known as the Victoria Lake indigo.

This variant is a flowering plant native to the region around Lake Victoria in East Africa. It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found near water bodies.

It plays a role in local ecosystems and has been studied for its potential in traditional medicine.
