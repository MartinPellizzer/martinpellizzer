Ixora congestiflora

Ixora congestiflora, commonly known as the clustered ixora, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, bright red flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Ixora congestiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a hedge plant.
