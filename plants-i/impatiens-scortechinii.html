Impatiens scortechinii

Impatiens scortechinii, commonly known as the giant impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid forests.

Impatiens scortechinii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses worldwide.
