Ipomoea Petiolaris

Climbing morning glory, scientifically known as Ipomoea petiolaris, is a fast-growing vine native to the Americas. It is prized for its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in the early morning. This plant thrives in warm climates and requires full sun to flourish. Its rapid growth makes it an excellent choice for trellises or garden structures. However, it can become invasive if not properly managed.
