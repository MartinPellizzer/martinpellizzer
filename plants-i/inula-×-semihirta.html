Inula × semihirta

Inula × semihirta, commonly known as the hybrid yellow fleabane, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is a hybrid between Inula hirta and Inula crithmoides, characterized by its yellow composite flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields. It produces small, wind-dispersed seeds and is often found in temperate regions of Europe and North America.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in ecological studies and horticulture.
