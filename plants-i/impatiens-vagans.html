Impatiens vagans

Impatiens vagans, commonly known as the wandering impatiens, is a species of flowering plant in the family Balsaminaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its delicate, spurred flowers that come in a variety of colors. Impatiens vagans prefers moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.
