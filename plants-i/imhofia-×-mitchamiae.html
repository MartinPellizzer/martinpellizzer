Imhofia × mitchamiae

Imhofia × mitchamiae, commonly known as the hybrid calla lily, is a tropical perennial plant native to the warm climates of South Africa.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves and showy, spatulate flowers. The plant produces inflorescences that consist of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can range in hue from white to deep purple. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth.

Imhofia × mitchamiae is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
