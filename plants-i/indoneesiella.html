Indoneesiella

Indoneesiella, commonly known as the Java orchid, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

The plants typically have slender stems and produce small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. Indoneesiella species are often epiphytic, meaning they grow on other plants without being parasitic.
