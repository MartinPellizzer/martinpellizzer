Iltisiella humilis

Iltisiella humilis, commonly known as the small-flowered iltisiella, is a species of flowering plant in the family Acanthaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, clustered flowers. Iltisiella humilis is often found in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories or along stream banks.
