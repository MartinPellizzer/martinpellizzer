Iris verna var. smalliana

Iris verna var. smalliana, commonly known as the smallleaf blue flag iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wet meadows. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves and produces striking blue to violet flowers with a distinctive dark purple throat. It is characterized by its compact size and is often found in the eastern United States.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
