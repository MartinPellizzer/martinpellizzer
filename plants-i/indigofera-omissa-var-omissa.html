Indigofera omissa var. omissa

Indigofera omissa var. omissa, commonly known as the Indian blue bean, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and erect growth habit. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to purple, followed by cylindrical pods containing seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
