Iris decora

Iris decora, commonly known as the showy iris, is a striking perennial plant native to the western United States.

It features tall, slender stems that bear showy, violet-blue flowers with a distinctive white or pale pink center. The plant's leaves are sword-shaped and dark green, providing a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms. Iris decora thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
