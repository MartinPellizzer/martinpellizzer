Ilex cornuta f. burfordii

Ilex cornuta f. burfordii, commonly known as Burford holly, is a compact evergreen shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, red berries that are attractive to birds and add visual interest in winter. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Its dense growth habit and year-round foliage make it an excellent specimen plant for gardens and landscapes.
