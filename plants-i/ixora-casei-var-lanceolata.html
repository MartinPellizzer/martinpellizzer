Ixora casei var. lanceolata

Ixora casei var. lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceleaf ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and clusters of bright orange or red flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical climates, often in coastal areas or near mangroves. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in landscaping and horticulture.

The species is also known for its ability to thrive in saline environments, making it suitable for coastal gardens.
