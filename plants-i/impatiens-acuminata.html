Impatiens acuminata

Impatiens acuminata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, spurred flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are usually glossy and slightly hairy. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens acuminata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
