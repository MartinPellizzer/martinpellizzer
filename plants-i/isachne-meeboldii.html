Isachne meeboldii

Isachne meeboldii, commonly known as the meebold grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences. It is characterized by its clumping growth habit and is often found in well-drained soils.

Botanically, it belongs to the Poaceae family and is noted for its adaptability to various environmental conditions.
