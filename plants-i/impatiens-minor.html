Impatiens minor

Impatiens minor, commonly known as the dwarf impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Balsaminaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, white, and red. The plant typically grows to a height of less than 30 centimeters, making it ideal for container gardening or as a bedding plant. Impatiens minor thrives in partial shade and well-draining soil, requiring consistent moisture for optimal growth.
