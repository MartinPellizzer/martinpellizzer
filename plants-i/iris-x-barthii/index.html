Iris × Barthii

Iris × barthii is a hybrid iris species known for its striking blue to violet flowers. It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Kazakhstan and Uzbekistan. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. Its unique appearance and hardy nature have made it a favorite among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it requires careful cultivation to ensure its survival in changing environmental conditions.
