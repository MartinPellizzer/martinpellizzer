Isachne stenantha

Isachne stenantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved isachne, is a perennial grass species native to the temperate regions of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks. The plant has slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves with a bluish-green color. Its inflorescence consists of compact, spike-like clusters of small, two-lobed spikelets.

Isachne stenantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in ecological restoration projects.
