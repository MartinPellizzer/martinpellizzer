Ixora hartiana

Ixora hartiana, commonly known as the flame flower or red ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters tall, featuring glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of bright red or orange flowers, which are highly attractive to birds and insects. It thrives in tropical climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Ixora hartiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
