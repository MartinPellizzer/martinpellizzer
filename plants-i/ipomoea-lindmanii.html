Ipomoea lindmanii

Ipomoea lindmanii, commonly known as the Mexican morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its large, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep violet. The plant produces heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in warm, sunny environments and is often found in disturbed soils.

Ipomoea lindmanii is a climbing vine that can grow up to several meters in length.
