Isochilus dubius

Isochilus dubius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pimpernel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Isochilus dubius thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy addition to gardens in suitable climates.
