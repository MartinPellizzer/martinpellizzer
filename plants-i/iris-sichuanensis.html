Iris sichuanensis

Iris sichuanensis, commonly known as the Sichuan iris, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Sichuan Province in China.

It is a perennial herb with tall, slender stems that bear showy, violet-blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and meadows. Its leaves are long and sword-shaped, with a smooth texture and a bluish-green color.

The flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, adding ornamental value to its natural habitat.
