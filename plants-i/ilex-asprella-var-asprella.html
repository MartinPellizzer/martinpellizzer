Ilex asprella var. asprella

Ilex asprella var. asprella, commonly known as the narrow-leaved holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, lanceolate leaves that are typically narrow and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for various bird species. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is also cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
