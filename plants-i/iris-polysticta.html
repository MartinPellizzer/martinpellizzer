Iris polysticta

Iris polysticta, commonly known as the Japanese iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It features showy, upright flowers that typically bloom in late spring or early summer. The plant has sword-shaped, grass-like leaves that are usually a deep green color. Its flowers come in shades of blue, purple, and white, with a distinctive three-parted structure.

Iris polysticta thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
