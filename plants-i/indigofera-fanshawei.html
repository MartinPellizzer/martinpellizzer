Indigofera fanshawei

Indigofera fanshawei, commonly known as the Chinese indigo plant, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family Fabaceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its striking blue-violet flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces cylindrical pods that contain several seeds. It is often grown for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in Chinese medicine.

Its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil makes it beneficial for soil enrichment in agricultural settings.
