Iris yunnanensis

Iris yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan iris, is a flowering plant native to China, particularly the Yunnan province.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as mountain forests and meadows. Its rhizomatous root system allows it to thrive in well-drained soil.

Iris yunnanensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
