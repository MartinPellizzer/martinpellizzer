Ixora carolinensis var. lanceolata

Ixora carolinensis var. lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceleaf flame bush, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of South America.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of bright red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun exposure.

Ixora carolinensis var. lanceolata is a hardy shrub that is relatively disease-resistant and easy to maintain.
