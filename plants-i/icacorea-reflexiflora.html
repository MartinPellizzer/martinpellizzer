Icacorea reflexiflora

Icacorea reflexiflora, commonly known as the coastal hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are usually alternate, simple, and have a rough texture.

Icacorea reflexiflora is often used in coastal landscaping due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.
