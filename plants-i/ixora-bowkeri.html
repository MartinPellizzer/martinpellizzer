Ixora bowkeri

Ixora bowkeri, commonly known as the golden flame ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, featuring dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces clusters of bright orange-red flowers, which are borne on long, branched inflorescences. Its leaves are elliptical, glossy, and arranged oppositely along the stems.

Ixora bowkeri is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and as a hedge plant.
