Itea luzonensis

Itea luzonensis, commonly known as the Luzon flameflower, is a species native to the Philippines.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its striking red or orange flower clusters, which appear in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a serrated edge.

Itea luzonensis is valued for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
