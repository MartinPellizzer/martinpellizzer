Impatiens agumbeana

Impatiens agumbeana, commonly known as the Agumbe Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are often glossy and arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens agumbeana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
