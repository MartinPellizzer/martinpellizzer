Ilysanthes lobelioides

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, strap-like leaves and delicate, purple or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wetlands. It is a perennial herb that forms a rosette of leaves at the base.
