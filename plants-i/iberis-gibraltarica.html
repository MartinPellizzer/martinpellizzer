Iberis gibraltarica

Iberis gibraltarica, commonly known as the Gibraltar carnation, is a flowering plant native to the rocky shores of Gibraltar.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of bright pink or white flowers. The plant features small, lobed leaves and thrives in well-drained, sunny conditions. It is well adapted to coastal environments due to its drought tolerance and salt resistance.

The Gibraltar carnation is also cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens.
