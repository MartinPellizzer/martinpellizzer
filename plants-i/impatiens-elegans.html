Impatiens elegans

Impatiens elegans, commonly known as the elegant touch-me-not, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features delicate, spurred flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, often with a contrasting throat. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are typically hairy and have a velvety texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Impatiens elegans is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
