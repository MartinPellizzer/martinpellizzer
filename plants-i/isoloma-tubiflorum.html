Isoloma tubiflorum

Isoloma tubiflorum, commonly known as the tuberous bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features basal rosettes of narrow, gray-green leaves and produces striking, bell-shaped flowers in shades of violet or blue. The plant's flowering period typically occurs in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Isoloma tubiflorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
