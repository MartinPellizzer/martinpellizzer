Ixora × venusta

Ixora × venusta, commonly known as the Chinese flame bush, is a hybrid shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year, making it a popular ornamental plant. The plant has opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are dark green in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Ixora × venusta is commonly used in gardens and landscapes for its showy floral display and hardy nature.
