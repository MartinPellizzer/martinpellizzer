Illicium tsaii

Illicium tsaii, commonly known as Chinese star anise, is a deciduous shrub native to southern China.

It belongs to the family Schisandraceae and is characterized by its aromatic, star-shaped fruits that resemble those of the star anise. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by dark, glossy berries that are rich in essential oils. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate with a slightly serrated margin.

Illicium tsaii is valued for its culinary and medicinal uses, particularly in traditional Chinese medicine.
