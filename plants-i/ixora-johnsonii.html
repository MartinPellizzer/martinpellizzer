Ixora johnsonii

Ixora johnsonii, commonly known as the Johnson's ixora, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of bright red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has opposite, simple, and usually hairy leaves that are elliptical in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Ixora johnsonii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant floral display.
