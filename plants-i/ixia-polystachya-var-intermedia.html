Ixia polystachya var. intermedia

Ixia polystachya var. intermedia, commonly known as the Orange River lily, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features vibrant orange to yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has strap-like, grassy leaves that are typically blue-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and compact growth habit.
