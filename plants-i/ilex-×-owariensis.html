Ilex × owariensis

Ilex × owariensis, commonly known as the Japanese holly, is a hybrid evergreen shrub or small tree native to Japan.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green, leathery leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

Its hardy nature and seasonal color changes make it a versatile choice for landscaping in temperate climates.
