Ilex kelabitana

Ilex kelabitana, commonly known as the Kelabit holly, is a species of flowering plant in the family Aquifoliaceae.

It is native to the tropical rainforests of Borneo, specifically found in the Kelabit region of Sarawak. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, clustered white flowers that appear in the spring. Its berries, which are red when mature, are an important food source for various wildlife species.

Ilex kelabitana is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in horticultural settings.
