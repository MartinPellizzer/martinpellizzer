Ixora Blumei

Ixora blumei is a vibrant evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa. Known for its clusters of bright orange-red flowers, it is a popular ornamental plant in gardens and landscapes. This plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish. Its striking appearance makes it a favorite among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts. Ixora blumei is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators like bees and birds.
