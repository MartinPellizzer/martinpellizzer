Ixora buchholzii

Ixora buchholzii, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are dark green in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for tropical and subtropical climates.

Ixora buchholzii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant flowers and adaptability.
