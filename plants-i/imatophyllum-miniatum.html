Imatophyllum miniatum

Imatophyllum miniatum, commonly known as the miniature leaf fern, is a small, delicate fern native to tropical regions.

It features fronds that are narrow and elongated, with a soft, feathery appearance. The plant produces spores on the underside of its leaves, which are essential for its reproductive cycle. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
