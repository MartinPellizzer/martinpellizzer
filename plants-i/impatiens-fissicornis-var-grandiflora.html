Impatiens fissicornis var. grandiflora

Impatiens fissicornis var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered impatiens, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to white, with a distinctive two-lipped structure. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses worldwide.
