Impatiens seretii f. etentaculifera

Impatiens seretii f. etentaculifera, commonly known as the broad-leafed impatiens, is a flowering plant species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its lush, broad leaves and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its growth habit is typically herbaceous, with a compact and spreading form.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade.
