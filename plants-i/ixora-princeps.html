Ixora princeps

Ixora princeps, commonly known as the red flame or flame bush, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a yellow or orange center. The plant has opposite, simple, and elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Ixora princeps is also valued for its ability to tolerate poor soil conditions and its use in traditional medicine.
