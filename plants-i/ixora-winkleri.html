Ixora winkleri

Ixora winkleri, commonly known as the Winkler's ixora, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange to red flowers, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. It thrives in tropical climates and requires well-drained soil and full sunlight.

Ixora winkleri is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
