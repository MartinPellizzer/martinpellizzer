Isatis yezoensis

Isatis yezoensis, commonly known as the Japanese woad, is a biennial plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is known for its ability to produce a blue dye from its leaves, similar to true woad. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

Isatis yezoensis is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
