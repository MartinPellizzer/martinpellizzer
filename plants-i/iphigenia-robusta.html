Iphigenia robusta

Iphigenia robusta, commonly known as the robust iphigenia, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, woody stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its hardiness and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for gardeners seeking drought-resistant plants.
