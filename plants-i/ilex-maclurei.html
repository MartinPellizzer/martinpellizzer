Ilex maclurei

Ilex maclurei, commonly known as the Maclure's holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically oval in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for various bird species. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in oak woodlands and chaparral habitats.

Ilex maclurei is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to provide habitat and food in native landscapes.
