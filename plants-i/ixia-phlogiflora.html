Ixia phlogiflora

Ixia phlogiflora, commonly known as the flame lily, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, upright stems that bear clusters of vibrant orange-red flowers, which resemble flames. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are dark green and arranged in a rosette pattern at the base. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Ixia phlogiflora is known for its long blooming period, typically from late spring to early summer.
