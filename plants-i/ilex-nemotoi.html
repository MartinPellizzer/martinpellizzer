Ilex nemotoi

Ilex nemotoi, commonly known as the Japanese holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Aquifoliaceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically opposite in arrangement. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for garden landscapes in temperate climates.
