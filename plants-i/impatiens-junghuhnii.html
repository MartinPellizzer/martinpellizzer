Impatiens junghuhnii

Impatiens junghuhnii, commonly known as the jungle impatiens, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

Impatiens junghuhnii thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
