Ixia burmanni

Ixia burmanni, commonly known as the Burmann's fire lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It produces tall, slender stems with showy, funnel-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red. The plant belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family, which includes many ornamental and economically important species. Ixia burmanni thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often growing in rocky or sandy environments.

Its unique floral display makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
