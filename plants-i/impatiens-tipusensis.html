Impatiens tipusensis

Impatiens tipusensis, commonly known as the Tumultuous Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central Africa.

It belongs to the family Balsaminaceae and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a glossy surface and grows as an herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of 30 to 60 centimeters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is noted for its rapid growth and ability to spread through seed dispersal by wind and water.
