Islaya grandiflorens var. spinosior

Islaya grandiflorens var. spinosior, commonly known as the spiny islaya, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its robust, spiny stems and large, showy flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in dry, open habitats. Its foliage consists of broad, lance-shaped leaves with a rough, textured surface.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and adaptability to arid environments.
