Indigofera deflersii

Indigofera deflersii, commonly known as the African indigo, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters tall, characterized by its trifoliate leaves and showy, pea-like flowers. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, and it is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and traditional contexts.
