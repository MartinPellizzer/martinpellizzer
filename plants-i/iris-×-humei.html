Iris × humei

Iris × humei, commonly known as the hybrid bearded iris, is a perennial plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that bear showy, vibrant flowers with three upright petals and three drooping sepals. The plant typically grows to a height of 2 to 4 feet and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage consists of sword-shaped, dark green leaves that are lanceolate in form.

Iris × humei is widely cultivated for its ornamental value and is known for its resilience in a variety of garden conditions.
