Indigofera aspalathifolia

Indigofera aspalathifolia, commonly known as the sweetpea bush, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to violet, often arranged in dense clusters. The plant has trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its fragrant blooms and compact growth habit.
