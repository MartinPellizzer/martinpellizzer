Isachne margaritifera

Isachne margaritifera, commonly known as the pearl grass, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands. The plant has slender, upright stems with small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its inflorescence consists of dense, spike-like clusters of tiny, white to pale blue flowers.

Isachne margaritifera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its delicate appearance.
