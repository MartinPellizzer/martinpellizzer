Iridodictyum Farashae

Iridodictyum farashae is a rare and unique plant species discovered in the remote highlands of Ecuador. It is known for its striking, iridescent leaves that shimmer with a spectrum of colors under sunlight. This plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is adapted to high-altitude conditions. Its discovery has sparked interest among botanists and conservationists worldwide. Due to its limited habitat, Iridodictyum farashae is considered critically endangered and requires urgent protection.
