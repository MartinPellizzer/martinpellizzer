Isomeris arborea var. angustata

Isomeris arborea var. angustata, commonly known as narrowleaf isomeris, is a shrub native to specific regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species in its native range.
