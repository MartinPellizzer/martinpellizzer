Iris moggridgei

Iris moggridgei, commonly known as the Moggridge iris, is a striking species native to the mountainous regions of central Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of blue and purple. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in rocky or well-drained soils. It is a perennial herb that thrives in full sun and is adapted to arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
