Iresine herbstii var. acuminata

Iresine herbstii var. acuminata, commonly known as the red leaf hibiscus, is a striking plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage, which can range from deep red to purple and green, depending on light exposure. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are not as showy as its leaves. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
