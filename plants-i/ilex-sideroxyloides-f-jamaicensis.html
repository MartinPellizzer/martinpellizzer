Ilex sideroxyloides f. jamaicensis

Ilex sideroxyloides f. jamaicensis, commonly known as the Jamaican holly, is a shrub or small tree native to the Caribbean, particularly Jamaica.

It belongs to the family Aquifoliaceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by red, fleshy berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife.
