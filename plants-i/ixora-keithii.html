Ixora keithii

Ixora keithii, commonly known as the Keith's ixora, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from red to orange, which bloom throughout the year in warm climates. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are typically elliptical in shape and have a smooth, glossy surface. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Ixora keithii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
