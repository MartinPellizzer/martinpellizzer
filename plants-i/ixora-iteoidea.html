Ixora iteoidea

Ixora iteoidea, commonly known as the pink flame tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences that bloom in shades of pink and red, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant flowers. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing as a shrub or small tree.

Ixora iteoidea is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand partial shade.
