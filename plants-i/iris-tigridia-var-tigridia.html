Iris tigridia var. tigridia

Iris tigridia var. tigridia, commonly known as the scarlet iris, is a striking perennial plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It features tall, slender stems that bear showy, spurred flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has broad, sword-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to both ornamental gardens and native plant landscapes.
