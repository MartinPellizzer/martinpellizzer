Inula conyzae

Inula conyzae, commonly known as yellow fleabane, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months, attracting various pollinators. It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and roadsides.

Inula conyzae is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
