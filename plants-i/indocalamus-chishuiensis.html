Indocalamus chishuiensis

Indocalamus chishuiensis, commonly known as the Chishui bamboo, is a species of grass belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to China, particularly found in the Chishui River region of Sichuan Province. This bamboo is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous areas.

The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
