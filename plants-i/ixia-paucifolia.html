Ixia paucifolia

Ixia paucifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ixia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. Ixia paucifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
