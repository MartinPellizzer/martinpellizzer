Ilex macrocarpa var. genuina

Ilex macrocarpa var. genuina, commonly known as the California coffeeberry, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to California.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces bright red berries that are edible for birds and small mammals. The plant thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions. It is often found in chaparral and coastal sage scrub habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in Mediterranean climates.
