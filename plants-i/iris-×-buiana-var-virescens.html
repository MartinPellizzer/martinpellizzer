Iris × buiana var. virescens

Iris × buiana var. virescens, commonly known as the green iris, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers with a distinctive yellow beard on the lower petals. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight. Its foliage consists of sword-like, dark green leaves that add visual interest throughout the growing season.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and its ability to thrive in a variety of climates.
