Impatiens maculifera

Impatiens maculifera, commonly known as the spotted impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red, with a distinctive white or pale yellow throat. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often speckled with dark spots, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens maculifera is valued in horticulture for its beauty and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
