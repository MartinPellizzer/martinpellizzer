Ilex Crenata Var. Mutchagara

Ilex crenata var. mutchagara is a unique variant of the common holly known for its dense, compact growth and attractive foliage. This evergreen shrub features glossy, dark green leaves with a distinctive wavy edge that sets it apart from other holly varieties. It is prized in landscaping for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. The plant is also popular for its ornamental value and ease of maintenance. Its adaptability makes it a favored choice for gardeners seeking a reliable and visually appealing plant.
