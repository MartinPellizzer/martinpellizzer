Ilex memecylifolia var. nummulariifolia

Ilex memecylifolia var. nummulariifolia, commonly known as the Mexican holly, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Mexico.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, red berries that are a food source for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its ornamental value comes from its dense foliage and seasonal color changes.
