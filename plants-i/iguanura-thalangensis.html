Iguanura thalangensis

Iguanura thalangensis, commonly known as the Thai hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers that are usually red or pink in color. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a rough texture.

It thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
