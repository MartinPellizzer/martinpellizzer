Iris Virginica Var. Shrevei

Iris virginica var. shrevei is a rare and striking species of iris native to the southeastern united states. it is known for its vibrant blue to violet flowers and distinctive leaf structure. this variety thrives in wetland environments and is often found in swamps and along stream banks. due to habitat loss and environmental changes it is considered endangered. conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique botanical gem.
