Iris sarajevoensis

Iris sarajevoensis, commonly known as the Sarajevo iris, is a rare and endemic species native to the Balkan region, particularly Bosnia and Herzegovina.

It is characterized by its striking blue-violet flowers and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils in mountainous areas. It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is known for its ornamental value.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered vulnerable and requires conservation efforts.
