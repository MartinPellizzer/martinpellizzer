Isodon kangtingensis

Isodon kangtingensis, commonly known as the Kangting skullcap, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in mountainous regions. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually in whorls, and are typically blue or purple in color.

Isodon kangtingensis is valued for its medicinal properties, traditionally used in Chinese herbal medicine.
