Ipomoea celebica

Ipomoea celebica, commonly known as the Celebes morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Indonesia.

It is a vine that produces trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of violet and blue. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is known for its rapid growth habit. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Ipomoea celebica is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
