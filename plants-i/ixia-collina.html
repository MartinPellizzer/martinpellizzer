Ixia collina

Ixia collina, commonly known as the Orange Kaffir lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of orange and yellow. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers. Ixia collina is part of the Amaryllidaceae family, which includes other ornamental bulbous plants.

Its blooming period typically occurs in late winter to early spring, adding vibrant color to gardens during the cooler months.
