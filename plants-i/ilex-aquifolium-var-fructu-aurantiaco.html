Ilex aquifolium var. fructu-aurantiaco

Ilex aquifolium var. fructu-aurantiaco, commonly known as the golden fruit holly, is a variant of the common holly that features strikingly bright orange berries.

This plant is native to parts of Europe and is characterized by its dark, glossy evergreen leaves. The berries, which are non-toxic to humans, add a vibrant splash of color to the landscape during the winter months. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a versatile choice for ornamental planting.

The plant's botanical features make it a popular selection for hedging and garden decoration.
