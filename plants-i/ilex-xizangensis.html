Ilex xizangensis

Ilex xizangensis, commonly known as the Yunnan holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to the mountainous regions of southwest China.

It belongs to the family Aquifoliaceae and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green on the upper surface and lighter on the underside. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for various bird species. It thrives in temperate climates with high humidity and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
