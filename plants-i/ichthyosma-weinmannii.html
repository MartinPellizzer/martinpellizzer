Ichthyosma weinmannii

Ichthyosma weinmannii, commonly known as the silverleaf iceplant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine, waxy coating, which helps it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It is well adapted to rocky soils and full sun, making it a hardy choice for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular ornamental plant in dry climates.
