Iresine diffusa var. spiculigera

Iresine diffusa var. spiculigera, commonly known as redleaf sorrel, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety foliage that ranges from deep red to purple in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or pink flowers that appear in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
