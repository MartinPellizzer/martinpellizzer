Impatiens serusiauxii

Impatiens serusiauxii, commonly known as the Serusiaux's impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Madagascar.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom during the rainy season. Its leaves are ovate and slightly hairy, and it prefers shaded, humid environments.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade.
