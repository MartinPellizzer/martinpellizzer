Iris chamaeiris var. balkana

Iris chamaeiris var. balkana, commonly known as the Balkan grass iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southeastern Europe.

It typically grows in moist meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are usually blue to violet in color, with three upright petals and three spreading sepals.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and its role in supporting local pollinator populations.
