Ixora corymbosa

Ixora corymbosa, commonly known as the flame-of-peace, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, clustered red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has opposite, simple, and usually hairy leaves that are elliptical in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Ixora corymbosa is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
