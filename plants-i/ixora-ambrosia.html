Ixora ambrosia

Ixora ambrosia, commonly known as the golden flame or sacred flame, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bright orange to red flowers that bloom in dense, clustered inflorescences. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Ixora ambrosia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
