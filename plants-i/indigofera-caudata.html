Indigofera caudata

Indigofera caudata, commonly known as the purpleheart or blue bean, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

Its leaves are trifoliate, with three oval-shaped leaflets, and it produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.
