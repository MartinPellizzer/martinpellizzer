Itoa

Itoa, commonly known as the bluebell, is a flowering plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking bell-shaped flowers that range from blue to purple, often blooming in early spring. The plant typically grows in woodland areas and prefers well-drained, moist soil. Its leaves are strap-like and grassy, forming a basal rosette.

Itoa is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant, early-season blooms.
