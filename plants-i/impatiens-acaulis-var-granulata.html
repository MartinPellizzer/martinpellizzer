Impatiens acaulis var. granulata

Impatiens acaulis var. granulata, commonly known as the granulate wood impatiens, is a unique variant of the wood impatiens species.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and smooth, dark green leaves. The flowers are typically pink or white, with a distinctive, spurred structure at the base of each bloom. This variety is native to tropical regions of Africa and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

It thrives in shaded to partially shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
