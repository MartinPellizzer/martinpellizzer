Impatiens shangjiangensis

Impatiens shangjiangensis, commonly known as the Shangjiang Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Balsaminaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically come in shades of pink and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
