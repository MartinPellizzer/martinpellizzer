Isatis pilosa

Isatis pilosa, commonly known as Chinese yellow flower or yellow root, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family and is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in temperate climates. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.

Isatis pilosa is also valued for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional Chinese medicine.
