Ipomoea bimbim

Ipomoea bimbim, commonly known as the blue morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in the morning and close by midday. The plant produces heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and thrives in warm, humid climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Ipomoea bimbim is a climbing vine that can reach significant heights if provided with support.
