Ionidium hieronymi

Ionidium hieronymi, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of violet, pink, and white, often with contrasting markings. Its leaves are typically velvety and dark green, and it thrives in bright, indirect light.
