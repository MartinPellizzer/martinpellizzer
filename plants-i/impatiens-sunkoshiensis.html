Impatiens sunkoshiensis

Impatiens sunkoshiensis, commonly known as the Sunkoshi impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan regions of Nepal and India.

It belongs to the Balsaminaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
