Isoetes echinospora f. astoma

Isoetes echinospora f. astoma, commonly known as the star-shaped water fern, is a rare fern species characterized by its unique morphology and ecological adaptations.

It belongs to the genus Isoetes, which is known for its spore-bearing structures called sporangia. The plant exhibits a specialized aquatic lifestyle, typically inhabiting freshwater environments with stable water levels. Its fronds are often reduced and modified, reflecting its adaptation to submergent conditions.

This fern is of particular interest in botanical studies due to its distinct reproductive features and limited distribution.
