Ixora collina

Ixora collina, commonly known as the flame bush, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 6 meters. The plant is characterized by its clusters of bright orange or red flowers, which bloom throughout the year in some regions. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a smooth surface with a slightly waxy texture.

Ixora collina is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
