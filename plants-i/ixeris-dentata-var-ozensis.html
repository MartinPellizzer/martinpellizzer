Ixeris dentata var. ozensis

Ixeris dentata var. ozensis, commonly known as the Korean carpetweed, is a low-growing perennial plant native to East Asia.

It features a dense mat of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, adding a delicate touch to its foliage. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in gardens and naturalized areas.

Its hardy nature makes it a popular choice for ground cover in temperate climates.
