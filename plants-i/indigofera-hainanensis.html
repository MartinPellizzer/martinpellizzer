Indigofera hainanensis

Indigofera hainanensis, commonly known as Hainan indigofera, is a flowering plant native to China's Hainan Island.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant is notable for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, which benefits surrounding vegetation.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments within tropical and subtropical regions.
