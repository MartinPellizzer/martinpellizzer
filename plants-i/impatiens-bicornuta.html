Impatiens bicornuta

Impatiens bicornuta, commonly known as the two-horned impatiens, is a herbaceous perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red, with two distinct lobes at the base, giving it its common name. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its delicate appearance and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
