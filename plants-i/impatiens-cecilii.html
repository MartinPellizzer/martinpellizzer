Impatiens cecilii

Impatiens cecilii, commonly known as Cecil's impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink, white, and red. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens cecilii is a popular ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and ease of cultivation.
