Ilex dicarpa

Ilex dicarpa, commonly known as the Florida holly, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the southeastern United States.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that turn red in the fall, adding ornamental value to its appearance. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

Ilex dicarpa is often used in landscaping for its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.
