Impatiens dalzellii var. umbellata

Impatiens dalzellii var. umbellata, commonly known as the Umbellate Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of showy, spurred flowers that range in color from white to pink. The leaves are typically ovate and have a smooth texture, often with a slightly hairy surface. This species thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
