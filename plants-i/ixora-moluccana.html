Ixora moluccana

Ixora moluccana, commonly known as the Moluccan ixora, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant produces clusters of bright red or orange flowers, which are highly attractive to pollinators such as bees and birds. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a glossy appearance.

Ixora moluccana is valued for its ornamental qualities and is commonly cultivated in gardens and as a hedging plant.
