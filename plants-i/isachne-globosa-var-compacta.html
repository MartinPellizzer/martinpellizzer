Isachne globosa var. compacta

Isachne globosa var. compacta, commonly known as the compact velvetgrass, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and compact, rounded clumps. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a soft appearance. Its inflorescence consists of small, clustered spikelets that are typically pale green to brown in color.

This variety is well-suited for ornamental use in gardens and landscapes due to its neat, compact form and attractive foliage.
