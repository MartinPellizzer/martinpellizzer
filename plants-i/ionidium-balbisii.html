Ionidium balbisii

Ionidium balbisii, commonly known as the Balbisii ionidium, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated leaves that are often variegated with shades of green and white. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.
