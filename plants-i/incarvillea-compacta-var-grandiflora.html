Incarvillea compacta var. grandiflora

Incarvillea compacta var. grandiflora, commonly known as the Chinese trumpetflower, is a perennial shrub native to China.

It features striking orange to red tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are hairy and grayish-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its unique floral structure and vibrant colors make it a popular choice among gardeners seeking ornamental plants.
