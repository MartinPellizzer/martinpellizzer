Iberis lapeyrousii

Iberis lapeyrousii, commonly known as the Armenian candytuft, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Armenia and Turkey.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with distinctive four-petaled flowers. Its leaves are pinnately divided, giving it a delicate, fern-like appearance.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
