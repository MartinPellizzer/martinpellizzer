Ixora riparia

Ixora riparia, commonly known as the river ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of bright red or orange flowers, which bloom throughout the year. The plant has opposite, simple, and elliptical leaves that are usually dark green in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found near water bodies.

Ixora riparia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
