Indigofera biflora

Indigofera biflora, commonly known as the two-flowered indigo, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets, and it typically grows as an herbaceous annual or perennial.
