Ixora accedens

Ixora accedens, commonly known as the red flame or red ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, glossy green foliage and clusters of bright orange-red flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant is a shrub or small tree, typically reaching heights of up to 6 meters. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Ixora accedens is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a hedge plant.
