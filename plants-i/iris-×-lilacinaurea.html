Iris × lilacinaurea

Iris × lilacinaurea, commonly known as the golden lilac iris, is a hybrid plant that combines traits from different iris species.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically feature a mix of purple and gold hues. The plant produces narrow, grass-like leaves that are often a vibrant green. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

This ornamental plant is popular in gardens for its aesthetic appeal and hardiness.
