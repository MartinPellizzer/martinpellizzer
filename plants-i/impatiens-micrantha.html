Impatiens micrantha

Impatiens micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are usually white or pale pink in color. Its leaves are ovate and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Impatiens micrantha thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
