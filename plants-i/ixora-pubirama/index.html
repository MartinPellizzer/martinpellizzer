Ixora Pubirama

The ixora pubirama is a strikingly beautiful shrub known for its vibrant, clustered flowers. Native to tropical regions, it thrives in warm, humid climates with plenty of sunlight. Its foliage is dense and glossy, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping. The plant's unique pubescent leaves and showy blooms make it a standout in garden settings. With proper care, it can flourish and add a splash of color to any garden space.
