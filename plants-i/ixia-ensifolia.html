Ixia ensifolia

Ixia ensifolia, commonly known as the sword lily, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, narrow leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The flowers, which come in shades of yellow, orange, and red, bloom in late summer to early autumn. Ixia ensifolia thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Its unique appearance and vibrant colors make it a standout in both natural and cultivated settings.
