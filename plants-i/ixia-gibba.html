Ixia gibba

Ixia gibba, commonly known as the spurred flame lily, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range from orange to red. The plant features strap-like, narrow leaves that are typically blue-green in color. Ixia gibba thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its unique spurred petals and showy blooms make it a standout in both cultivated and natural settings.
