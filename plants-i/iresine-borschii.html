Iresine borschii

Iresine borschii, commonly known as the red leaf iron plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety red or purple foliage, which is often variegated with contrasting green or white markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in shades of pink or white. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Iresine borschii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant leaf color and ease of care.
