Indigofera onobrychioides

Indigofera onobrychioides, commonly known as the Indian blue bean, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant produces elongated pods containing small, edible seeds that are rich in nutrients. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas or along roadsides.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ecological and agricultural purposes.
