Indigofera komiensis

Indigofera komiensis, commonly known as the Chinese indigo plant, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to violet. The plant produces pods containing lens-shaped seeds, and it is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
