Ida ecuadorensis

Ida ecuadorensis, commonly known as the Ecuadorian ida, is a species of flowering plant native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

Ida ecuadorensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique appearance.
