Isocoma menziesii

Isocoma menziesii, commonly known as the Menzies' goldenweed, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.
