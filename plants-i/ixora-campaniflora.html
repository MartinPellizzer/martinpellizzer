Ixora campaniflora

Ixora campaniflora, commonly known as the bell ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, bell-shaped orange-red flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Ixora campaniflora is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant flowers and compact growth habit.
