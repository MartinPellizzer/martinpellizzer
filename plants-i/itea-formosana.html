Itea formosana

Itea formosana, commonly known as the Japanese spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It features slender, arching stems that bear clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers in early summer. The plant has finely divided, feather-like leaves that give it a delicate, lace-like appearance. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, moist soil.

Itea formosana is often used in ornamental landscaping for its attractive foliage and showy blooms.
