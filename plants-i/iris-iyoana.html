Iris iyoana

Iris iyoana, commonly known as the African iris, is a striking perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features showy, violet-blue flowers with a distinctive three-dimensional structure, often with a darker throat. The plant has sword-shaped, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
