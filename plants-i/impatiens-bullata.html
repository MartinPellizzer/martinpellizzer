Impatiens bullata

Impatiens bullata, commonly known as the bullate impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, showy flowers that are typically pink or white with a prominent yellow throat. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Impatiens bullata is a popular choice for gardeners due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
