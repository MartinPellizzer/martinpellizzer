Ixia duckittiae

Ixia duckittiae, commonly known as the Orange Kaffir lily, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features strap-shaped, dark green leaves that are narrow and grass-like in appearance. Ixia duckittiae prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and ability to thrive in a variety of climates.
