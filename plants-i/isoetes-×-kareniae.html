Isoetes × kareniae

Isoetes × kareniae, commonly known as the hybrid water fern, is a member of the genus Isoetes, which includes species of spore-bearing plants known as spike mosses.

It is a hybrid resulting from the cross between Isoetes australis and Isoetes taiwanensis, showcasing genetic diversity within the genus. The plant is characterized by its distinctive, spike-like structures that bear spores, typical of the Isoetes family. It thrives in wetland environments, often found in shallow water or moist soil, and plays a role in aquatic ecosystems.

Its morphology and reproductive strategies make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
