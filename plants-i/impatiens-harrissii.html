Impatiens harrissii

Impatiens harrissii, commonly known as Harriss's impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, hairy texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical forests.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
