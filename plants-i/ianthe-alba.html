Ianthe alba

Ianthe alba, commonly known as the white lily, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its elegant, white, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and grows from a bulb, making it a member of the Amaryllidaceae family. Ianthe alba prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, thriving in temperate climates.

Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
