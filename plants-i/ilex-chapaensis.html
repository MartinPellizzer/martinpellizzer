Ilex chapaensis

Ilex chapaensis, commonly known as the Chapa holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to central China.

It belongs to the family Aquifoliaceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically opposite in arrangement. Ilex chapaensis is valued for its dense foliage and winter interest, contributing to ecological diversity in its native habitat.
