Ilex bioritsensis

Ilex bioritsensis, commonly known as the Bioritsensis holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to specific regions in Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for various wildlife species. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in horticultural settings.
