Isatis glabrisiliqua

Isatis glabrisiliqua, commonly known as the smooth salsify, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is native to parts of Europe and Asia and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and lobed leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, followed by long, slender seed pods. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and along roadsides.

Due to its historical use in herbal medicine, it has been cultivated for its nutritional and medicinal properties.
