Isachne pangerangensis

Isachne pangerangensis, commonly known as the Pangerang grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia, and is typically found in moist, tropical environments. The plant has slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are often hairy. Its inflorescence consists of spikelets that are clustered in dense, compact arrangements.

Isachne pangerangensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its fine texture and visual interest.
