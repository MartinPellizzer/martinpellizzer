Iris demetrii

Iris demetrii, commonly known as the Demetrius iris, is a species of flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils along stream banks and in woodland areas. Its leaves are sword-shaped and can reach lengths of up to 30 inches, providing a dramatic visual presence.

Iris demetrii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and unique foliage.
