Ionidium multiflorum

Ionidium multiflorum, commonly known as the clustered bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its clusters of bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple. Ionidium multiflorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
