Ilex maigualidensis

Ilex maigualidensis, commonly known as the Maigualidensis holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to the mountainous regions of Mexico.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes grown in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
