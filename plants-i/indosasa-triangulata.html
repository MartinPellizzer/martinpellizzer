Indosasa triangulata

Indosasa triangulata, commonly known as the triangular leaf fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive triangular-shaped fronds that are often divided into smaller, lobed segments. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along stream banks. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce clusters of small, delicate fronds.

The botanical features of Indosasa triangulata make it a valuable species for both ornamental horticulture and ecological studies.
