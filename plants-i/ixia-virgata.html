Ixia virgata

Ixia virgata, commonly known as the Orange Rain Lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking orange-colored flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped blooms that are often borne on long, slender stems. Ixia virgata is a bulbous plant with narrow, grass-like leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
