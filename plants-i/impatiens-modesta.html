Impatiens modesta

Impatiens modesta, commonly known as the Chinese impatiens, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that burst open to disperse its seeds.

Impatiens modesta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
