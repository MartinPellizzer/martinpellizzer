Ilex kanehirae var. glabra

Ilex kanehirae var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth-leaf holly, is a variant of the holly genus native to Japan.

It is characterized by its smooth, glossy leaves that are typically dark green and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible for birds but generally not for humans. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for ornamental gardening.

Its compact growth habit and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for hedges and landscape design.
