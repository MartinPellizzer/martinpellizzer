Ixora nicobarica

Ixora nicobarica, commonly known as the Nicobar flame flower, is a flowering plant native to the Andaman and Nicobar Islands.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height, characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, bright red flowers that are highly attractive to pollinators such as birds and insects. Its leaves are simple, elliptical, and have a smooth texture with a glossy appearance.

Ixora nicobarica thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
