Iberis roseopurpurea

Iberis roseopurpurea, commonly known as the purple flowering Iberis, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the family Brassicaceae, which includes mustard and cabbages, and is characterized by its compact growth habit. Iberis roseopurpurea is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
