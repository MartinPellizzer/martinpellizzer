Illicium cavaleriei

Illicium cavaleriei, commonly known as Chinese star anise, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the family Schisandraceae and is characterized by its aromatic, star-shaped fruit that resembles the more familiar star anise. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into the distinctive seed pods. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly serrated margin.

The fruit is used in traditional Chinese medicine and as a spice, similar to star anise.
