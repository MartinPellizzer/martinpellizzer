Ixia rouxii

Ixia rouxii, commonly known as the Orange-Ixia, is a striking South African bulbous plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from orange to yellow, often with a distinctive red or purple stripe down the center. The plant typically blooms in late spring to early summer, producing clusters of flowers on sturdy stems. Its foliage consists of strap-like, grassy leaves that emerge from a bulbous base.

Ixia rouxii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and is well-suited to well-drained soils and full sun.
