Ilex argutidens

Ilex argutidens, commonly known as the Chinese holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green with a leathery texture. The plant produces small, red berries that are toxic to humans and animals. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Ilex argutidens is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
