Isoetes histrix f. loricata

Isoetes histrix f. loricata, commonly known as the armored water fern, is a species of aquatic plant belonging to the genus Isoetes.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spiky, and often armored spore cases, which give it its common name. The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and lakes. Its morphology includes a central axis with spirally arranged leaves that are often submerged.

The spore-producing structures, called sporangia, are typically located at the tips of the leaves and are protected by a tough, leathery covering.
