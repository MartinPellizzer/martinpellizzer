Ixeris Sagittarioides

Ixeris sagittarioides, commonly known as the arrowleaf groundcherry, is a hardy perennial plant native to eastern Asia. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a versatile addition to gardens. With its vibrant yellow flowers and lush green foliage, it adds both color and texture to landscapes. This plant is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. Its resilience and low maintenance make it a favorite among gardeners seeking easy-to-care-for plants.
