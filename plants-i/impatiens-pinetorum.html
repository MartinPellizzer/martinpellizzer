Impatiens pinetorum

Impatiens pinetorum, commonly known as the pine forest impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant prefers moist, well-drained soils and thrives in shaded to semi-shaded environments. Its leaves are ovate and covered in a fine, soft pubescence.

Impatiens pinetorum is often found growing in the understory of pine forests, where it plays a role in the local ecosystem by providing nectar for pollinators.
