Impatiens levingei

Impatiens levingei, commonly known as the Levingei Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, colorful flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and slightly hairy, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden areas.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a decorative plant.
