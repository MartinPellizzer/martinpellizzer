Itoa orientalis var. glabrescens

Itoa orientalis var. glabrescens, commonly known as the oriental itoa, is a herbaceous perennial native to East Asia.

It features slender, branching stems covered with small, lance-shaped leaves that are typically glabrous or nearly hairless. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow to greenish-white flowers during the spring and early summer. Its botanical characteristics include a fibrous root system and a preference for moist, well-drained soils.

It is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.
