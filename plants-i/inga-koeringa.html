Inga koeringa

Inga koeringa, commonly known as the "butterfly tree," is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and legume pods. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its rapid growth and ability to thrive in various soil types make it a common species in rainforests.

The bark and leaves have been used in traditional medicine for their potential health benefits.
