Ilex caroliniana var. jejuna

Ilex caroliniana var. jejuna, commonly known as the Florida holly, is a shrub or small tree native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in coastal and urban environments.
