Ixia sobolifera subsp. sobolifera

Ixia sobolifera subsp.

sobolifera, commonly known as the red-hot poker, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa. It features striking, fiery red to orange tubular flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant has linear, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette. It produces a tall, upright stem that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height. Ixia sobolifera subsp.

sobolifera is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
