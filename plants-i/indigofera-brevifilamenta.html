Indigofera brevifilamenta

Indigofera brevifilamenta, commonly known as the short filament indigofera, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces pods that contain lens-shaped seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine.

Its growth habit is typically shrub-like, with woody stems and a preference for well-drained soils.
