Ixora burchelliana

Ixora burchelliana, commonly known as the flame bush, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its clusters of bright orange-red flowers that bloom throughout the year, attracting various pollinators. The plant has simple, opposite, and often hairy leaves that are dark green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for warm climates.

Ixora burchelliana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a hedge plant.
