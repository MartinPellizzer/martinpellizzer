Ipomoea mojangensis

Ipomoea mojangensis, commonly known as the Mojang morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from purple to blue. The plant produces heart-shaped leaves and thrives in warm, humid climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine.

Ipomoea mojangensis is a member of the Convolvulaceae family, which includes other morning glory species.
