Ipomoea × sloteri

Ipomoea × sloteri, commonly known as the hybrid morning glory, is a flowering plant belonging to the Convolvulaceae family.

It is a perennial vine characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep purple. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Ipomoea × sloteri thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or partially shaded environments.

Its rapid growth and ornamental value make it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
