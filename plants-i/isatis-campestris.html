Isatis campestris

Isatis campestris, commonly known as wild mustard, is an annual herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cabbage and broccoli. The plant has alternate, pinnately compound leaves that are often hairy and have a distinct mustard scent. Its small, yellow flowers are arranged in racemes and bloom from late spring to early summer.

The plant produces round, green pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
