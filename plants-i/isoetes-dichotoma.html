Isoetes dichotoma

It is characterized by its spore-bearing structures called sporangia, which are clustered in a distinctive, often branching pattern. The plant typically grows in shallow, freshwater environments such as lakes and ponds. Its leaves are ribbon-like and form dense, floating mats on the water surface.
