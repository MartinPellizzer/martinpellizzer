Iodes ovalis

Iodes ovalis, commonly known as the oval hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are oval-shaped and have a rough, slightly hairy texture.

Iodes ovalis thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
