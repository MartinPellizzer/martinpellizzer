Isachne vulcanica

Isachne vulcanica, commonly known as the volcanic isachne, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in areas with high humidity. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves with a distinct bluish-green coloration. Its flowering stems bear small, spike-like inflorescences that produce tiny grains of pollen.

Isachne vulcanica is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in challenging environmental conditions.
