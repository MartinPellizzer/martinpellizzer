Isatis bornmulleriana

Isatis bornmulleriana, commonly known as the Turkish woad, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is native to Turkey and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats and has hairy, lance-shaped leaves. It is closely related to other members of the woad genus, which are known for their historical use in dye production.

Isatis bornmulleriana is also valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
