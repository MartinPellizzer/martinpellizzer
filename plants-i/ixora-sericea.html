Ixora sericea

Ixora sericea, commonly known as silky ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows up to 2 meters tall, featuring dense, glossy green leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, bright orange-red flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its flowers are composed of four to five petals and are highly attractive to birds and insects.

Ixora sericea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a hedge plant.
