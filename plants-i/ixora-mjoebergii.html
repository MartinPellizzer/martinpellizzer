Ixora mjoebergii

Ixora mjoebergii, commonly known as the Cape rose or red ixora, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, fragrant clusters of bright red flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has opposite, elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant floral display. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Ixora mjoebergii is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its ornate appearance and resilience in warm climates.
