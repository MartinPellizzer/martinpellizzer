Indigofera baumiana var. hockii

Indigofera baumiana var. hockii, commonly known as the Hockii indigo plant, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family Fabaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has also been used in traditional medicine.

The plant thrives in tropical climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
