Impatiens reptans

Impatiens reptans, commonly known as the trailing impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets and container gardening.

Its ability to bloom throughout the growing season adds continuous color to gardens and landscapes.
