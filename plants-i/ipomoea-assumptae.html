Ipomoea assumptae

Ipomoea assumptae, commonly known as the skyflower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and violet, often with a distinctive purple throat. Ipomoea assumptae is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant.
