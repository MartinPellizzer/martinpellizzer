Iochroma suffruticosum

Iochroma suffruticosum, commonly known as the silverleaf trumpetflower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple. Its leaves are usually silver-gray in appearance, giving the plant its common name.

Iochroma suffruticosum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
