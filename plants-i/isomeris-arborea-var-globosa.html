Isomeris arborea var. globosa

Isomeris arborea var. globosa, commonly known as the globe tree, is a unique species characterized by its spherical growth habit.

It features dense, compact foliage that forms a rounded shape, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant has small, narrow leaves that are typically arranged in a spiral pattern around the stems. It produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, often blooming in the spring.

This variety is native to specific regions in South America and thrives in well-drained soils with adequate sunlight.
