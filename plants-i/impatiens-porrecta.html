Impatiens porrecta

Impatiens porrecta, commonly known as the slender touch-me-not, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 feet. The plant features showy, spurred flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a distinctive yellow or orange throat. Its foliage consists of heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are arranged oppositely along the stems.

Impatiens porrecta thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.
