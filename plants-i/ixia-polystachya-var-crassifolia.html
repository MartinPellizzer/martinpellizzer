Ixia polystachya var. crassifolia

Ixia polystachya var. crassifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf ixia, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a red throat. The plant has a rosette of broad, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, typically from late winter to early spring.
