Ismene crinifolia

Ismene crinifolia, commonly known as the laceleaf ismene, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, often during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Ismene crinifolia is valued for its ornamental beauty and is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
