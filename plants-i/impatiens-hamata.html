Impatiens hamata

Impatiens hamata, commonly known as the spurred impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and distinctive spurred petals that give it its common name. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens hamata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to add color to gardens.
