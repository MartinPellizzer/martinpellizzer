Isodon aurantiacus

Isodon aurantiacus, commonly known as the orange verbena, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant orange to red flowers. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.
