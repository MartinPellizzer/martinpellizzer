Iris speciosissima

Iris speciosissima, commonly known as the Chinese flag iris, is a striking perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It features tall, slender stems that bear showy, violet-blue flowers with a distinctive white or pale yellow center. The plant's leaves are narrow and grass-like, typically arranged in a fan shape at the base. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Iris speciosissima is valued in gardens for its ornamental beauty and ability to add color to shady landscapes.
