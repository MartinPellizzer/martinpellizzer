Ixia ovata

Ixia ovata, commonly known as the Orange Kaffir lily, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking orange-red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces long, slender flower spikes. Ixia ovata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
