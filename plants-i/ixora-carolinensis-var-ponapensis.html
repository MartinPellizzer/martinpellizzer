Ixora carolinensis var. ponapensis

Ixora carolinensis var. ponapensis, commonly known as the Ponape ixora, is a tropical shrub native to the Caroline Islands.

It features dense clusters of bright orange-red flowers that bloom throughout the year, attracting various pollinators. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Its ornamental value and hardy nature make it a popular choice in island landscapes.
