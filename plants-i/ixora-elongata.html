Ixora elongata

Ixora elongata, commonly known as the long-stemmed flame bush, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of bright red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has opposite, simple, and often lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. Its stems are woody and can grow quite long, giving it its common name.

Ixora elongata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and as a garden plant.
