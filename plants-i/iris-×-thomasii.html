Iris × thomasii

Iris × thomasii, commonly known as Thomas' iris, is a hybrid perennial plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sunlight. Its foliage consists of broad, sword-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings.

Iris × thomasii is valued in gardens for its ornamental beauty and is relatively easy to cultivate.
