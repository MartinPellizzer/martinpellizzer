Iris × barthiiformis

Iris × barthiiformis, commonly known as the hybrid iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features tall, slender stems that bear showy, violet-blue flowers with a distinctive three-lobed structure. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically bright green and arranged in a fan-like pattern. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often used in landscaping and floral arrangements.
