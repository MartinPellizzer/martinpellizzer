Iris scorpioides

Iris scorpioides, commonly known as the scorpion iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features tall, slender stems that bear showy, blue to violet flowers with a distinctive spiky structure. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically arranged in a fan shape at the base. Iris scorpioides thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its unique appearance and adaptability contribute to its ornamental value in horticulture.
