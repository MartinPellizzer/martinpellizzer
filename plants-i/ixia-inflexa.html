Ixia inflexa

Ixia inflexa, commonly known as the Orange Kaffir lily, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking orange-red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces narrow, strap-like leaves and has a bulbous underground stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Ixia inflexa is also valued for its ornamental beauty and ability to attract pollinators.
