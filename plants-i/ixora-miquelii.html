Ixora miquelii

Ixora miquelii, commonly known as the Miquel's ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in the Philippines.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant produces clusters of bright orange-red flowers, which are borne on short pedicels and are highly ornamental. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth surface with a slightly waxy texture.

Ixora miquelii thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
