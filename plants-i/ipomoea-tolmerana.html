Ipomoea tolmerana

Ipomoea tolmerana, commonly known as Tolmer's morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

Ipomoea tolmerana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
