Impatiens manaharensis var. tenuicalcar

Impatiens manaharensis var. tenuicalcar, commonly known as the slender-spurred impatiens, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Balsaminaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink. The species features heart-shaped leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface, and its stems are often hairy as well. The flowers have a unique spur that is slender and elongated, distinguishing it from other impatiens species.

This plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.
