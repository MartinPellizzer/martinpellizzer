Iris acutiloba var. schelkownikowii

Iris acutiloba var. schelkownikowii, commonly known as the Schelkownikowii iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It features slender, grass-like leaves and produces striking blue to violet flowers with three upright petals and three drooping sepals. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or meadow habitats. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.

This variety is notable for its compact growth and adaptability to a range of environmental conditions.
