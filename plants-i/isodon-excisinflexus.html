Isodon excisinflexus

Isodon excisinflexus, commonly known as the flexible isodon, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic plants known for their medicinal properties. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. Its flowers are typically small and clustered in whorls, producing a subtle fragrance.

Isodon excisinflexus is valued in traditional medicine for its potential anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.
