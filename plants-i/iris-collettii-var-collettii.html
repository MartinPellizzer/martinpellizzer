Iris collettii var. collettii

Iris collettii var. collettii, commonly known as the Collett's iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features upright, slender stems that bear showy, violet-blue flowers with a distinctive, dark purple throat. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
