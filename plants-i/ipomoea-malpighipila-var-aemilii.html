Ipomoea malpighipila var. aemilii

Ipomoea malpighipila var. aemilii, commonly known as the Malpighiopsis aemilii, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of South America.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves with a deep green color and prominent veins. The flowers are trumpet-shaped, typically pale yellow to white with a distinctive purple throat. It produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, smooth seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
