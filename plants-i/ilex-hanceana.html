Ilex hanceana

Ilex hanceana, commonly known as the Chinese holly, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ilex hanceana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
