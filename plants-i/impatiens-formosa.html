Impatiens formosa

Impatiens formosa, commonly known as the Chinese balsam, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are ovate with a smooth texture, and it produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Impatiens formosa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of garden conditions.
