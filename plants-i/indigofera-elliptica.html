Indigofera elliptica

Indigofera elliptica, commonly known as the African indigo or velvet bean, is a tropical shrub native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its elliptical, dark green leaves and clusters of small, pale blue to purple flowers. The plant produces cylindrical pods that contain several elliptical, brown seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of natural dye.

Additionally, it is used in traditional medicine and has nitrogen-fixing properties that benefit soil fertility.
