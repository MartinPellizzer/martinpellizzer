Indigofera yuanjiangensis

Indigofera yuanjiangensis, commonly known as the Yunnan indigo plant, is a flowering plant species native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and erect, branching stems. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pale purple, often arranged in dense clusters. It is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it an important plant in soil enrichment.

Its ecological significance and ornamental value have made it a subject of interest in botanical research and horticulture.
