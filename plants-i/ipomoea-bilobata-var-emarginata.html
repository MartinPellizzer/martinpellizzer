Ipomoea bilobata var. emarginata

Ipomoea bilobata var. emarginata, commonly known as the zigzag morning glory, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive double lobe, giving it its scientific name. The flowers are typically pale blue to violet, with a unique zigzag pattern on the petals. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires support for its vigorous growth.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a popular choice in gardens and as a houseplant.
