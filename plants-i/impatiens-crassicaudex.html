Impatiens crassicaudex

Impatiens crassicaudex, commonly known as the broad-tailed impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves and clusters of showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in warm climates.
