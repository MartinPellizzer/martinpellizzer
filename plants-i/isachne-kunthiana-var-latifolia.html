Isachne kunthiana var. latifolia

Isachne kunthiana var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf isachne, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It typically grows in moist to wet environments, such as marshes and along stream banks. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves with a smooth texture and can reach heights of up to 1 meter. Its inflorescence consists of a panicle with small, spikelet-like flowers.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing wetland soils.
