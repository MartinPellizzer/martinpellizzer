Isatis deserti

Isatis deserti, commonly known as desert woad, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of North America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like mustard and cabbage. The plant is characterized by its upright, branched stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

Isatis deserti is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments, making it an important component of desert ecosystems.
