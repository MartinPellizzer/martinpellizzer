Ionidium villosissimum

Ionidium villosissimum, commonly known as the hairy ironwood, is a shrub or small tree native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, woody structure and hairy, often grayish foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often covered in fine pubescence.

Ionidium villosissimum is valued for its hardy nature and is sometimes used in ornamental horticulture.
