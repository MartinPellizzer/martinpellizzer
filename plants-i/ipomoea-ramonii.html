Ipomoea ramonii

Ipomoea ramonii, commonly known as the Ramonii morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to violet. The plant produces heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Ipomoea ramonii is a climbing vine that can grow up to several meters in length.
