Impatiens leptopoda var. madagascariensis

Impatiens leptopoda var. madagascariensis, commonly known as the Madagascar impatiens, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically grows as an annual herb, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are ovate and slightly hairy, while its stems are slender and branching.

This species is widely cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
