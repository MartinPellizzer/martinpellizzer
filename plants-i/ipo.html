Ipo

Ipo, commonly known as the "tree of life," is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves and can grow up to 30 meters tall. The plant has a fibrous trunk and produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The ipo plant thrives in humid climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish.
