Impatiens gongchengensis

Impatiens gongchengensis, commonly known as the Gongcheng Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Balsaminaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
