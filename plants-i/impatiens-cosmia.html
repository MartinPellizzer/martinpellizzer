Impatiens cosmia

Impatiens cosmia, commonly known as the sacred violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of pink, purple, and white. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Impatiens cosmia is valued for its ornamental beauty and has cultural significance in some African communities.
