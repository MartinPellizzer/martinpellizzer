Ione scariosa

Ione scariosa, commonly known as the scarlet lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Liliaceae family and is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep red. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or meadow habitats. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a waxy coating, while its stems are often hairy.

Ione scariosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking blooms.
