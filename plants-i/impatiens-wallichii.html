Impatiens wallichii

Impatiens wallichii, commonly known as the Himalayan Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically come in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually hairy and have a velvety texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens wallichii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
