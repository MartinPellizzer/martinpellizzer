Impatiens pyrrhotricha var. grandis

Impatiens pyrrhotricha var. grandis, commonly known as the giant flame flower, is a striking tropical species native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from deep red to orange, often with a contrasting yellow throat. The plant produces opposite, ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green, providing a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, rich soil to flourish.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses, where it can add dramatic visual impact.
