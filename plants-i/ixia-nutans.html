Ixia nutans

Ixia nutans, commonly known as the bowstring lily, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, pendulous flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Ixia nutans thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
