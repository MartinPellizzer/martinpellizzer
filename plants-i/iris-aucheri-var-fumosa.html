Iris aucheri var. fumosa

Iris aucheri var. fumosa, commonly known as the smoke iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-gray in color, contributing to its distinctive appearance. The plant produces striking violet-blue flowers with a contrasting white or pale yellow center, which bloom in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or alpine settings.

Its unique foliage and elegant blooms make it a popular choice among gardeners seeking ornamental plants with a touch of the wild.
