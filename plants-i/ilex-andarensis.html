Ilex andarensis

Ilex andarensis, commonly known as the Andean holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Aquifoliaceae and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and small, dark berries.
