Isoetes × blondeaui

Isoetes × blondeaui, commonly known as the hybrid water fern, is a member of the genus Isoetes, which includes spike-mosses or quillworts.

It is a submerged aquatic plant characterized by its spore-bearing structures called strobila, which are typically found at the tip of its stems. The plant thrives in freshwater environments, such as ponds and slow-moving streams, where it plays a role in aquatic ecosystems. Its morphology includes small, root-like structures called rhizoids and long, ribbon-like leaves.

The hybrid nature of Isoetes × blondeaui results from crosses between different species within the genus, contributing to its genetic diversity.
