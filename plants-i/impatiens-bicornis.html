Impatiens bicornis

Impatiens bicornis, commonly known as the two-horned impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that come in a range of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, with opposite, ovate leaves that have a slightly hairy texture. Its flowers are borne in clusters at the ends of the stems, and the species is known for its ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments.

Impatiens bicornis is a popular choice for ornamental gardening due to its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various growing conditions.
