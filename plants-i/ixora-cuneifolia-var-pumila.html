Ixora cuneifolia var. pumila

Ixora cuneifolia var. pumila, commonly known as dwarf ixora, is a compact shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, ovate leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces clusters of bright orange-red flowers, which are borne on short, branched inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for tropical and subtropical gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its dense growth habit and prolonged flowering period.
