Iria sphaerocephala

Iria sphaerocephala, commonly known as the globe-headed iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking, spherical-shaped flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy blooms during the spring and early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Iria sphaerocephala is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
