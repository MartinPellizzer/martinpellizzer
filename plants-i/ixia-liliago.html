Ixia liliago

Ixia liliago, commonly known as the Orange Rainlily, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped orange flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 cm and features strap-like, grassy leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, blooming in late winter to early spring.

Ixia liliago is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
