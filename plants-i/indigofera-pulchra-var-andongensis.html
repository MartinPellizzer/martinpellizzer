Indigofera pulchra var. andongensis

Indigofera pulchra var. andongensis, commonly known as the Andong indigo plant, is a flowering shrub native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its striking blue-purplish flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces cylindrical pods containing several seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used traditionally in local herbal medicine.
