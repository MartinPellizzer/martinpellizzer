Ilex Crenata Subsp. Fukasawana

Japanese holly, scientifically known as Ilex crenata subsp. fukasawana, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Japan. It is prized for its dense foliage, glossy dark green leaves, and ability to thrive in a variety of climates. This plant is commonly used in landscaping for its ornamental value and versatility in shaping. Its slow growth rate makes it ideal for topiary and formal garden designs. With proper care, Japanese holly can provide year-round beauty and structure to any garden setting.
