Incarvillea mairei var. multifoliolata

Incarvillea mairei var. multifoliolata, commonly known as the multileaf incarvillea, is a perennial herb native to China.

It features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has palmately divided leaves with multiple lobes, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and drought tolerance.
