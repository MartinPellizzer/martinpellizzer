Iris × rosipurpurea

Iris × rosipurpurea, commonly known as the Rosy Purple Flag Iris, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Iris rosenfeldii and Iris purpurea.

It is a tall perennial herb with striking violet-blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features sword-shaped, grass-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is typically found in wetlands or along the edges of ponds.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for water garden landscapes.
