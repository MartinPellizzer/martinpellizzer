Impatiens mannii var. adenopus

Impatiens mannii var. adenopus, commonly known as the Madagascar impatiens, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
