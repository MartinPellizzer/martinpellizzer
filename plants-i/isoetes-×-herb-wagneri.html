Isoetes × herb-wagneri

Isoetes × herb-wagneri, commonly known as the hybrid water parsley, is a species of aquatic plant belonging to the genus Isoetes, which includes various species of spike mosses.

The plant typically grows in freshwater environments, such as ponds and lakes, where it forms dense underwater stands. Its spore-bearing structures, known as sporangia, are clustered in a spore sac, a feature typical of the genus Isoetes.
