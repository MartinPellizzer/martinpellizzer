Iris rubromarginata var. mellita

Iris rubromarginata var. mellita, commonly known as the scarlet-margin iris, is a striking perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that bear showy, violet-blue flowers with distinctive red margins. The plant typically grows in well-drained, rocky soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are dark green and densely packed.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
