Ipomoea leprieurii

Ipomoea leprieurii, commonly known as the Leprieur's morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is a herbaceous vine that produces striking, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of violet, blue, and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can climb supports or trail along the ground.

Ipomoea leprieurii is also notable for its rapid growth and ability to adapt to various growing conditions.
