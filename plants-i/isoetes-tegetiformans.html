Isoetes tegetiformans

Isoetes tegetiformans, commonly known as the spikerush, is a species of aquatic plant belonging to the genus Isoetes in the family Isoetaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and a distinctive, spiky inflorescence that bears small, clustered spores. This plant typically grows in freshwater environments such as ponds, lakes, and wetlands. Its rhizomes are often covered in a thick, protective sheath, which helps it anchor in muddy substrates.

Isoetes tegetiformans plays a role in stabilizing aquatic ecosystems and providing habitat for various microorganisms and invertebrates.
