Indigofera decora var. ichangensis

Indigofera decora var. ichangensis, commonly known as the Chinese indigo plant, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and showy, pea-like flowers. The plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and traditional uses.
