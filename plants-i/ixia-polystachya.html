Ixia polystachya

Ixia polystachya, commonly known as the fire lily, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, orange-red flowers that bloom in late summer. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
