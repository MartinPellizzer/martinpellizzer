Impatiens hunanensis

Impatiens hunanensis, commonly known as the Hunan impatiens, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Balsaminaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, spurred flowers that typically range in color from white to pink. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in moist, shaded environments. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.

Its growth habit is herbaceous, with stems that can be either erect or trailing.
