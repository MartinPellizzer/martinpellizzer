Iridorkis zeylanica

Iridorkis zeylanica, commonly known as the Ceylon iris, is a flowering plant native to Sri Lanka.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of blue and purple. The plant features strap-like leaves and produces small, delicate flowers arranged in a panicle. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
