Ilex lasseri

Ilex lasseri, commonly known as the Lasseri holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, glossy dark green leaves that are typically oval in shape. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. Ilex lasseri thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
