Iris gueldenstaedtiana var. caerulescens

Iris gueldenstaedtiana var. caerulescens, commonly known as the Azure Flag Iris, is a striking perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features slender, grass-like leaves and produces striking blue to violet flowers with a distinctive white or pale blue beard on the lower petals. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun exposure. Its ornamental value lies in its vibrant color and elegant appearance, making it a sought-after species in horticulture.

It is also valued for its ecological role in supporting pollinators and maintaining biodiversity in its native habitats.
