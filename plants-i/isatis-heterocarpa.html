Isatis heterocarpa

Isatis heterocarpa, commonly known as the Chinese yellow mustard, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other mustard plants. The plant is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems, and it produces spherical seed pods that contain numerous small, dark seeds. It is often grown as a medicinal plant due to its potential health benefits.

Its botanical features make it distinct from other members of the mustard family.
