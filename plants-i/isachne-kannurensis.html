Isachne kannurensis

Isachne kannurensis, commonly known as the Kanyakumari grass, is a species of grass native to the southern regions of India.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant typically grows in moist, sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a distinct blue-green coloration.

Isachne kannurensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
