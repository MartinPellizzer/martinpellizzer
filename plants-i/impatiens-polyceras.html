Impatiens polyceras

Impatiens polyceras, commonly known as the horned impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spurred petals that give it a unique appearance. The plant typically grows as an annual or short-lived perennial, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are ovate and slightly hairy, while its flowers are usually pink or white.

Impatiens polyceras thrives in moist, shady environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
