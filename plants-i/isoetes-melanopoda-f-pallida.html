Isoetes melanopoda f. pallida

Isoetes melanopoda f. pallida, commonly known as the pale spore sedge, is a rare aquatic plant belonging to the genus Isoetes.

It is characterized by its dark green, sword-shaped leaves that are typically submerged in freshwater environments. The plant produces small, dark spores rather than flowers, which are released into the water for reproduction. It is often found in still or slow-moving waters with muddy or sandy substrates.
