Ixora patens

Ixora patens, commonly known as the flame tree or red flame bush, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences of bright red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually ovate to elliptical in shape. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often used in ornamental landscaping.

Ixora patens is also valued for its ability to grow in a variety of soil types and its relatively low maintenance requirements.
