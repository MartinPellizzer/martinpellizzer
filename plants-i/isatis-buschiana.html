Isatis buschiana

Isatis buschiana, commonly known as Chinese yellow flag, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cabbage and mustard. The plant is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. Its striking yellow flowers, which appear in clusters, bloom in late summer and attract various pollinators.

Isatis buschiana is also noted for its medicinal properties, historically used in traditional Chinese medicine.
