Ixora eximia

Ixora eximia, commonly known as the flame-of-the-forest, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom in dense clusters, giving it a striking appearance. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a slightly glossy surface.

Ixora eximia thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sunlight.
