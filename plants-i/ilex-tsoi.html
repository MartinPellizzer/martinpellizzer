Ilex tsoi

Ilex tsoi, commonly known as the Chinese holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Aquifoliaceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, red berries. The plant is known for its ornamental value, often cultivated for its dense foliage and winter interest. Ilex tsoi is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
