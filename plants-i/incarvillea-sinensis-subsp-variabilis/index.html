Incarvillea Sinensis Subsp. Variabilis

Variable incarvillea, scientifically known as Incarvillea sinensis subsp. variabilis, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the arid regions of western China. This resilient species is prized for its vibrant, often bi-colored blooms that range from deep purples to soft pinks and yellows. Its ability to thrive in harsh, dry conditions makes it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens. With its unique foliage and showy flowers, variable incarvillea adds both visual interest and ecological value to landscapes. Despite its hardiness, it requires careful cultivation to ensure optimal growth and blooming.
