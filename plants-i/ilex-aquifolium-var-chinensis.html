Ilex aquifolium var. chinensis

Ilex aquifolium var. chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese holly, is a variant of the common holly native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically serrated and have a distinctive spiny appearance. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for ornamental gardening.

This variety is often cultivated for its aesthetic value and hardy nature in temperate climates.
