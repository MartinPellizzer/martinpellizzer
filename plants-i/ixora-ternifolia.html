Ixora ternifolia

Ixora ternifolia, commonly known as the three-leaf ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, clustered inflorescences of small, bright red or orange-red flowers. The plant has opposite, simple, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Ixora ternifolia is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported therapeutic properties.
