Isodendrion lydgatei

Isodendrion lydgatei, commonly known as the Lydgate's thistle, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny, compound leaves and purple-blue flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It is a perennial shrub that can reach up to 1 meter in height.
