Ilex triflora var. horsfieldii

Ilex triflora var. horsfieldii, commonly known as Horsfield's holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters, followed by bright red berries that are a food source for various birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for ornamental gardening.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and seasonal color changes.
