Impatiens yui

Impatiens yui, commonly known as the Yui impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the family Balsaminaceae and is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white. Impatiens yui is also valued for its ecological role in supporting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
