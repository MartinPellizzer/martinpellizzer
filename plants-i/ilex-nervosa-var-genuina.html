Ilex nervosa var. genuina

Ilex nervosa var. genuina, commonly known as the Florida holly, is a small evergreen shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It has glossy, dark green leaves that are typically elliptical in shape with prominent veins. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

Its dense foliage and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for landscaping in coastal and sandy environments.
