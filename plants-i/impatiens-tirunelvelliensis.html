Impatiens tirunelvelliensis

It is native to the tropical regions of southern India, particularly the state of Tamil Nadu. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
