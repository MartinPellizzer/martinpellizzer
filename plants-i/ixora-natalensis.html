Ixora natalensis

Ixora natalensis, commonly known as Natal flame or Natal ixora, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of bright orange-red flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for coastal and tropical climates. Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant color and long-lasting blooms.

Ixora natalensis is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in warm, humid conditions.
