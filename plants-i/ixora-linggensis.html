Ixora linggensis

Ixora linggensis, commonly known as the Chinese flame bush, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of bright orange-red flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Ixora linggensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
