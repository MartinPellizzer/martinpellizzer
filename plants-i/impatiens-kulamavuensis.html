Impatiens kulamavuensis

Impatiens kulamavuensis, commonly known as the Kulamavu Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Sri Lanka.

It belongs to the family Balsaminaceae and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
