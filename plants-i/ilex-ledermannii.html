Ilex ledermannii

Ilex ledermannii, commonly known as the Ledermann holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and small, dark green leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces bright red berries that are an important food source for various bird species. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal, it is often cultivated in gardens and natural habitats across its native range.
