Iresine arbuscula

Iresine arbuscula, commonly known as the red leaf iron plant, is a strikingly ornamental shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, velvety red foliage that contrasts beautifully with its green stems and yellow flowers. The plant features small, clustered inflorescences that bloom in shades of yellow or orange, adding visual interest. Iresine arbuscula thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and indoor plant collections.
