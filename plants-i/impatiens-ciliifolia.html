Impatiens ciliifolia

Impatiens ciliifolia, commonly known as the silky touch-me-not, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and southern Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, spurred flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant features heart-shaped, velvety leaves with fine, silky hairs along the edges, giving it a soft appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens ciliifolia is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
