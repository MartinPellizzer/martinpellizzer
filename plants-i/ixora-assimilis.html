Ixora assimilis

Ixora assimilis, commonly known as the flame-of-the-forest, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bright orange-red inflorescences, which bloom profusely during the rainy season. The plant has simple, opposite, and elliptical leaves that are dark green in color. It thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Ixora assimilis is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
