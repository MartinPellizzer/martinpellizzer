Inula visianii

Inula visianii, commonly known as the yellow gentian, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

Inula visianii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
