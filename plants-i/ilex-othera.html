Ilex othera

Ilex othera, commonly known as the Chinese holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, red berries that are toxic to humans and animals. Ilex othera thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sun exposure.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
