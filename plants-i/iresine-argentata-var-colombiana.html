Iresine argentata var. colombiana

Iresine argentata var. colombiana, commonly known as silverleaf irtes, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to South America.

It features velvety, silver-green leaves with a metallic sheen that catch the light beautifully. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of red, pink, or white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-drained soil.

Due to its unique foliage, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded outdoor gardens.
