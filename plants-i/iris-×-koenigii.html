Iris × koenigii

Iris × koenigii, commonly known as the Japanese iris, is a hybrid perennial plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically range in color from blue to purple, with a distinctive white or yellow center. The plant features tall, upright stems that bear sword-shaped, dark green leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Iris × koenigii is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
