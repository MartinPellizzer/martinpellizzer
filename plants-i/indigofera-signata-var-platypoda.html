Indigofera signata var. platypoda

Indigofera signata var. platypoda, commonly known as the flatpod indigo.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three leaflets that are ovate to elliptic in shape.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
