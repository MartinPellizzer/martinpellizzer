Ilex Alternifolia

Ilex alternifolia is a unique evergreen shrub native to the eastern United States. It is known for its striking, alternate arrangement of leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. This plant thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in swampy or wetland environments. Its small, red berries are a food source for various wildlife species. Despite its ornamental value, Ilex alternifolia remains relatively obscure in horticultural circles.
