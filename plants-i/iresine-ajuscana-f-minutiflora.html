Iresine ajuscana f. minutiflora

Iresine ajuscana f. minutiflora, commonly known as the small-flowered balsam, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South America.

It features striking, colorful leaves that range from red to purple, often with contrasting veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically pink or white, arranged in clusters at the leaf axils. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental foliage, it is often cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
