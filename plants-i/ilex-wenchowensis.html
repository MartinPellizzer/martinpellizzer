Ilex wenchowensis

Ilex wenchowensis, commonly known as the Wenchow holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Aquifoliaceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for various bird species. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is often cultivated in horticultural settings.
