Ixora planifolia

Ixora planifolia, commonly known as the flat-leaf ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of bright orange or red flowers, which are highly attractive to pollinators. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and usually elliptical in shape.

Ixora planifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
