Isachne globosa var. brevispicula

Isachne globosa var. brevispicula, commonly known as the short-spurred globular grass, is a perennial grass species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, globular growth habit and produces small, spikelet-like inflorescences. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and is typically found in grassy areas or on rocky slopes. It is adapted to dry conditions and has a fibrous root system that helps it survive in nutrient-poor soils.

Its unique morphology makes it a distinctive and interesting subject for botanical study.
