Iti

Iti, commonly known as the "butterfly tree," is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that resemble butterflies, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, round fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are palmately lobed and can be used for various practical purposes.

Iti thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
