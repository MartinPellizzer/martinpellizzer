Ilex nepalensis

Ilex nepalensis, commonly known as the Nepal holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Himalayan region.

It is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters, followed by red berries that are an important food source for birds. It thrives in temperate to subtropical climates and is often found in mountainous areas with well-drained soil.

Ilex nepalensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitat.
