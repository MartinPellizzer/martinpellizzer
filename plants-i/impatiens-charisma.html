Impatiens charisma

Impatiens charisma, commonly known as the "charismatic impatiens," is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a range of colors, including pink, red, and white. The plant typically grows as an annual or short-lived perennial, reaching heights of 12 to 24 inches. Its foliage is heart-shaped and glossy, providing a striking backdrop to its colorful blooms.

Impatiens charisma thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden beds.
