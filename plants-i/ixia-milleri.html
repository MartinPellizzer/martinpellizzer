Ixia milleri

Ixia milleri, commonly known as the Orange Rain lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from orange to yellow, with a distinctive red throat. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters and produces multiple blooms during the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.

Ixia milleri is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
