Iozoste speciosa

Iozoste speciosa, commonly known as the showy iozoste, is a rare and striking plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, brightly colored flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has a robust root system that helps it survive in rocky, nutrient-poor soils. Its leaves are broad and waxy, adapted to reduce water loss in its arid environment.

Iozoste speciosa is valued for its ornamental beauty and plays a unique role in its native ecosystem.
