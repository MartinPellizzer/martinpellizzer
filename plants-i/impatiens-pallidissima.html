Impatiens pallidissima

Impatiens pallidissima, commonly known as the pale impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pale pink. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft, fuzzy appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens pallidissima is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
