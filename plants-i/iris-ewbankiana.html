Iris ewbankiana

Iris ewbankiana, commonly known as the Ewbank's iris, is a striking perennial herb native to the western United States.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or streamside habitats. The plant features tall, slender stems that bear showy blue to violet flowers with a distinctive three-parted lip. Its leaves are sword-shaped and form a dense rosette near the base.

Iris ewbankiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local pollinators.
