Isonandra laevifolia

Isonandra laevifolia, commonly known as the smooth-leaved isonandra, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are typically smooth and elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
