Isatis densiflora

Isatis densiflora, commonly known as the denseflower woad, is a herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cabbage and mustard. The plant is characterized by its dense clusters of small, blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and covered in a fine layer of white hairs, giving them a fuzzy appearance.

Isatis densiflora is often found in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and open woodlands.
