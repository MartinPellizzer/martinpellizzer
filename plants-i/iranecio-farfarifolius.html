Iranecio farfarifolius

Iranecio farfarifolius, commonly known as the Farfarifolius plant, is a species native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically in shades of white and pink. It thrives in cool, humid environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
