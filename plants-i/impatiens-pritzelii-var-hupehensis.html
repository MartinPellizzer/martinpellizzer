Impatiens pritzelii var. hupehensis

Impatiens pritzelii var. hupehensis, commonly known as the Chinese impatiens, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptical, with a glossy surface and a slightly serrated margin. This variety thrives in partial to full shade and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

It prefers moist, well-drained soil and is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for shade gardens.
