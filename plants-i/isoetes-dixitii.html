Isoetes dixitii

Isoetes dixitii, commonly known as the Dixitii watermoss, is a species of aquatic fern belonging to the order Isoetales.

It is characterized by its spore-bearing structures called sporangia, which are clustered in a central umbel. The plant is typically found in freshwater environments such as ponds and slow-moving streams. It has a unique morphology with a leafless stem and a rosette of spore-bearing leaves.

Isoetes dixitii plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by contributing to nutrient cycling and providing habitat for microorganisms.
