Ixia monadelphia

Ixia monadelphia, commonly known as the yellow starburst lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has strap-like, grassy leaves that are typically dark green and arranged in a rosette pattern. Its bulbs are adapted to store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions.

Ixia monadelphia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
