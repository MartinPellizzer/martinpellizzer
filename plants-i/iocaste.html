Iocaste

Iocaste, commonly known as the "crown of the forest," is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, palm-like leaves that can reach up to three meters in length. The plant produces strikingly beautiful flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Iocaste thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance and ecological importance make it a valuable species in both ornamental and ecological contexts.
