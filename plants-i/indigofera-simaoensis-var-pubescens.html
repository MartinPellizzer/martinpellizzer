Indigofera simaoensis var. pubescens

Indigofera simaoensis var. pubescens, commonly known as the pubescent indigofera, is a flowering plant species native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant is typically found in forested areas and thrives in humid, temperate climates.

Its medicinal properties have been studied for their potential in traditional herbal remedies.
