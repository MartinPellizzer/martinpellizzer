Ichthyothere scandens

Ichthyothere scandens, commonly known as the water vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a waxy surface that helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its roots are fibrous and can absorb nutrients from waterlogged soils.

It is often found growing along riverbanks and in swampy areas.
