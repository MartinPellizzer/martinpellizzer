Indigofera hedyantha subsp. inyangana

Indigofera hedyantha subsp.

inyangana, commonly known as the Inyangana indigo plant, is a shrub native to southern Africa. It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant produces pods that contain seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it valuable for soil enrichment in agricultural settings.
