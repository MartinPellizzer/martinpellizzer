Icacinopsis annonoides

Icacinopsis annonoides, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Annonaceae, which includes many tropical trees and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a spherical, woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

The tree is valued for its hard, durable wood, which is used in furniture and construction.
