Illicium randaiense

Illicium randaiense, commonly known as the Japanese star anise, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Schisandraceae and is characterized by its aromatic, star-shaped fruits. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into dark brown, star-shaped berries. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly serrated margin.
