Indigofera tirunelvelica

Indigofera tirunelvelica, commonly known as the Nilgiri indigofera, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas. Its leaves are trifoliate, and it produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.
