Impatiens margaritifera var. margaritifera

Impatiens margaritifera var. margaritifera, commonly known as the glassy impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, hairy leaves and clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest undergrowth. Its flowers have a unique translucent appearance, giving it its common name.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
