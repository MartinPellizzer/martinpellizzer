Isochilus major var. alatus

Isochilus major var. alatus, commonly known as the winged lily, is a herbaceous perennial native to the high altitudes of the Andes in South America.

It features slender, upright stems that bear showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of white to pale pink. The plant has distinctive wing-like structures on its leaves, which give it its common name. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is occasionally cultivated in specialized gardens.
