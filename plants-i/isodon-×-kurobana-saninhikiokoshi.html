Isodon × kurobana-saninhikiokoshi

Isodon × kurobana-saninhikiokoshi, commonly known as the Japanese blue flag iris, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade. Its foliage consists of narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are often a deep green.

This ornamental plant is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and wetland habitats.
