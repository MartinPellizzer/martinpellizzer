Ixora brunonis subsp. kratensis

Ixora brunonis subsp.

kratensis, commonly known as the Kratens Island ixora, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its dense, fragrant clusters of bright red flowers that bloom throughout the year.

The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are glossy and arranged in opposite pairs along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental gardening.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant floral display make it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
