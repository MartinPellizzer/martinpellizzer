Isolepis dregeana

Isolepis dregeana, commonly known as the African flat sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy habitats, forming dense clusters of grass-like leaves. Its inflorescence consists of spikelets that are often reddish or brownish in color.

Isolepis dregeana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in wetland restoration projects.
