Isodon yuennanensis

Isodon yuennanensis, commonly known as Yunnan isofuran, is a flowering plant native to China, particularly in the Yunnan province.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, clustered flowers that range from white to purple in color. It is often found growing in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions.

Due to its unique chemical composition, it has been studied for potential medicinal properties.
