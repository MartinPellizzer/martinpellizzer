Ixia secunda

Ixia secunda, commonly known as the red-hot poker, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, torch-like inflorescences. The plant produces vivid red, orange, or yellow flowers that bloom in summer. Its foliage consists of strap-like leaves that are often evergreen.

Ixia secunda is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
