Isodon megathyrsus

Isodon megathyrsus, commonly known as the large-leaf isodon, is a perennial herb native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its opposite, lance-shaped leaves and spikes of small, tubular flowers. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands.

The species is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional herbal medicine.
