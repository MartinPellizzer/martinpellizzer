Isolepis

These plants are typically found in wetland environments, including marshes, bogs, and along the edges of ponds. They are characterized by their dense, grass-like foliage and spike-like inflorescences. Isolepis species often have narrow, linear leaves and produce small, inconspicuous flowers.
