Ipomoea binectarifera var. taynguyenensis

Ipomoea binectarifera var. taynguyenensis, commonly known as the Tay Nguyen morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Vietnam.

It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and twining stems that climb or trail along the ground. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
