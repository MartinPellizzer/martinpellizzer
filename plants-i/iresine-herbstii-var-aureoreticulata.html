Iresine herbstii var. aureoreticulata

Iresine herbstii var. aureoreticulata, commonly known as the golden net leaf irsine, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to South America.

It features vibrant, jewel-toned foliage with a distinctive pattern of gold and red veins, giving it a unique visual appeal. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically pink or red, which appear in clusters during the growing season. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is often cultivated for its foliage rather than its flowers, making it a popular choice in tropical and subtropical gardens.
