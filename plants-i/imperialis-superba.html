Imperialis superba

Imperialis superba, commonly known as the Royal Majesty, is a striking ornamental plant native to the high altitudes of the Andes.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, showy flowers that range in color from deep purple to vibrant red. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery fuzz, enhancing its visual appeal. It thrives in cool, moist environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and hardiness, it is a popular choice for gardens in temperate climates.
