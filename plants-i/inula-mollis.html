Inula mollis

Inula mollis, commonly known as silky lettuces or woolly inula, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves that give it a distinctive, fuzzy appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to September. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

Inula mollis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
