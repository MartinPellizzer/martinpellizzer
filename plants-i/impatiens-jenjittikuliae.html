Impatiens jenjittikuliae

Impatiens jenjittikuliae, commonly known as the Kanchanaburi impatiens, is a flowering plant native to Thailand.

It belongs to the Balsaminaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
