Ilex kwangtungensis var. pilosissima

Ilex kwangtungensis var. pilosissima, commonly known as the silky Chinese holly, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage, with leaves that are thick, leathery, and covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a hardy ornamental plant.

Its unique foliage and seasonal berries make it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
