Isodon forrestii

Isodon forrestii, commonly known as the Chinese skullcap, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that are typically purple or blue. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

The leaves and flowers are used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.
