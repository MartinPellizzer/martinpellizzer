Iris proantha var. proantha

Iris proantha var. proantha, commonly known as the blue flag iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It features upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. The plant produces showy, blue to violet flowers with a distinctive three-parted structure, blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in wet or moist environments, often found in swamps, marshes, and along the edges of ponds.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in wetland ecosystems.
