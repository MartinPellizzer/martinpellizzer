Ixia acaulis

Ixia acaulis, commonly known as the stemless flame lily, is a striking South African bulbous plant characterized by its vibrant orange-red flowers.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is native to the Western and Northern Cape provinces. The plant is notable for its lack of a visible stem, with flowers emerging directly from the base of the plant. Ixia acaulis typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
