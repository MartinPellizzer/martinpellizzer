Indigofera nelsonii

Indigofera nelsonii, commonly known as the Nelson's indigo, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of South America.

Its leaves are trifoliate, with three oval-shaped leaflets, and it produces pods that contain several seeds.
