Ilex antonii

Ilex antonii, commonly known as the silverleaf holly, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage that turns a striking silver-gray color in the winter. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. Its adaptability to various soil types makes it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical and subtropical regions.

Ilex antonii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade.
