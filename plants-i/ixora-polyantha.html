Ixora polyantha

Ixora polyantha, commonly known as the Chinese flame or red flame plant, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, fragrant, and showy clusters of red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has opposite, simple, and usually ovate to elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green. Ixora polyantha thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil and full sun.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
