Impatiens dewildeana var. dewildeana

Impatiens dewildeana var. dewildeana, commonly known as Dewilde's impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to pink, often with a contrasting throat. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its beauty and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
