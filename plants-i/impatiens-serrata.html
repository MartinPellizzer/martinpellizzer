Impatiens serrata

Impatiens serrata, commonly known as the serrated impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The leaves are ovate with serrated edges, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
