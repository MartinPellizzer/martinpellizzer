Impatiens telekii

Impatiens telekii, commonly known as the Kivu impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central Africa.

It belongs to the family Balsaminaceae and is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
