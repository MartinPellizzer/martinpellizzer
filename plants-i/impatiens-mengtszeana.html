Impatiens mengtszeana

Impatiens mengtszeana, commonly known as the Mengtszean Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Balsaminaceae and is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically come in shades of pink and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it is less common than other impatiens species.
