Ixora gardneriana

Ixora gardneriana, commonly known as the flame flower or gardenia ixora, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bright orange to red floral clusters, which are borne on long, slender pedicels. The leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a smooth, glossy surface. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sun.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
