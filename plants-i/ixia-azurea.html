Ixia azurea

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped blue flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. Ixia azurea is known for its vibrant blue petals and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
