Ixia capillaris var. stricta

Ixia capillaris var. stricta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ixia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of vibrant orange or yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, contributing to its elegant appearance.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking blooms.
