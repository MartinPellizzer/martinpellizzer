Isodon bifidocalyx

Isodon bifidocalyx, commonly known as the two-lobed chrysanthemum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly serrated edge. Its flowers are typically clustered in inflorescences and range in color from white to pale yellow.
