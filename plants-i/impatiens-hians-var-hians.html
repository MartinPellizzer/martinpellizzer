Impatiens hians var. hians

Impatiens hians var. hians, commonly known as the "Impatiens walleriana" or "New Guinea impatiens," is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a wide range of colors, including red, pink, white, and yellow. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial, depending on the climate, and features heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its rapid growth and ability to bloom throughout the growing season contribute to its popularity in ornamental horticulture.
