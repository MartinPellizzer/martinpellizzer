Isoetes longissima

Isoetes longissima, commonly known as the long-stemmed water fern, is a species of aquatic plant belonging to the genus Isoetes.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spore-bearing spiroxyls, which are spiral-shaped and often covered in a protective sheath. The plant typically grows in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and wetlands. Its leaves are long and ribbon-like, often floating on the water surface or submerged beneath it.

Isoetes longissima plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various aquatic organisms.
