Ivania

Ivania, commonly known as the blue ivy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant blue or purple flowers. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Ivania thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

It is popular in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to cascade from hanging baskets.
