Iresine celosioides var. pubescens

Iresine celosioides var. pubescens, commonly known as the velvetleaf or redleaf plant, is a tropical shrub characterized by its striking, velvety foliage.

The leaves are typically broad, heart-shaped, and covered in fine, soft hairs, giving them a distinctive texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often pink or red, which appear in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant leaf coloration.
