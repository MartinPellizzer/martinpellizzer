Impatiens kamrupana

Impatiens kamrupana, commonly known as the Kamrup Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the northeastern regions of India.

It belongs to the Balsaminaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and grows as an annual herbaceous plant. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in suitable climates.
