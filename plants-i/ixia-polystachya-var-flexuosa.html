Ixia polystachya var. flexuosa

Ixia polystachya var. flexuosa, commonly known as the spiral lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem. The plant typically grows in well-drained, sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are evergreen.

It is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and is a popular choice in gardens and floral arrangements.
