Iris douglasiana var. alpha

Iris douglasiana var. alpha, commonly known as the Douglas iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It features upright, sword-shaped leaves and produces striking blue to purple flowers with a distinctive spurred petal. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or streamside habitats. It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens.

The variety alpha is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant flower color.
