Ixora cephalophora

Ixora cephalophora, commonly known as the mountain ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant is characterized by its clusters of bright orange or red flowers, which bloom throughout the year. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and elliptical in shape, with a smooth texture.

Ixora cephalophora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
