Impatiens shirensis

Impatiens shirensis, commonly known as the Shire Highlands impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and has opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth texture.
