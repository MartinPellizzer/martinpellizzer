Impatiens meruensis

Impatiens meruensis, commonly known as the East African jewelweed, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. It has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture.

Impatiens meruensis is a herbaceous perennial that produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds.
