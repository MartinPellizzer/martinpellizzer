Ipomoea hystrix

Ipomoea hystrix, commonly known as the spiny morning glory, is a flowering vine native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive spiny stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep purple. It thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Ipomoea hystrix is also known for its rapid growth and ability to climb various structures.
