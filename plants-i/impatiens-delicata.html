Impatiens delicata

Impatiens delicata, commonly known as the delicate impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender stems and showy, bell-shaped flowers that come in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a velvety texture, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Impatiens delicata is valued in horticulture for its beauty and ability to thrive in partial shade.
