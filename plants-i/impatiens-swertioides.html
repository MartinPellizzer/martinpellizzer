Impatiens swertioides

Impatiens swertioides, commonly known as the Himalayan impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is a member of the Balsaminaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and thrives in cool climates. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often have a velvety texture, while its stems are slender and trailing.

Impatiens swertioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
