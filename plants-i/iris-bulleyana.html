Iris bulleyana

Iris bulleyana, commonly known as the Chinese iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It features upright, sword-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. The plant produces showy, violet-blue flowers with a distinctive three-parted structure. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

Iris bulleyana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and landscapes.
