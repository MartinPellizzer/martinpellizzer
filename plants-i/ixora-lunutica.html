Ixora lunutica

Ixora lunutica, commonly known as the moonflower ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that are typically red or orange in color. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves and thrives in warm, humid climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Ixora lunutica is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
