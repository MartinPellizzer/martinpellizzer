Isodon Coetsa

Isodon coetsa is a lesser-known flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. This plant is characterized by its slender stems and clusters of small, pale blue or purple flowers. Though not widely cultivated, it holds cultural and medicinal significance in some traditional practices. Its unique appearance and potential health benefits make it an intriguing subject for further study.
