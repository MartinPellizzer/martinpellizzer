Ionoxalis attenuata

Ionoxalis attenuata, commonly known as the slender wood sorrel, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive light green color and small, delicate white or pink flowers. The plant has a shallow root system and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in woodland areas and along stream banks.

Ionoxalis attenuata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to naturalize in suitable habitats.
