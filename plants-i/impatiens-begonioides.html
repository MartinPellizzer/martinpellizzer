Impatiens begonioides

Impatiens begonioides, commonly known as the velvet leaf impatiens, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its velvety, deep green foliage and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 feet, and thrives in shady, moist environments. Its leaves are ovate with a slightly wavy margin, and the flowers bloom in late spring to early summer.

Impatiens begonioides is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
