Ixia gawleri

Ixia gawleri, commonly known as the Orange Rain Lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a distinctive red or purple throat. The plant produces its blooms in late winter to early spring, making it a welcome addition to gardens during the cooler months. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that emerge from a bulbous base.

Ixia gawleri thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.
