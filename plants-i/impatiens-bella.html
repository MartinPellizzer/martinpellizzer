Impatiens bella

Impatiens bella, commonly known as the "Chinese violet," is a delicate flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, white, and purple. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and arranged oppositely on the stems. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often cultivated as a garden ornamental.

Impatiens bella is valued for its ability to bloom throughout the growing season, adding vibrant color to shaded garden spaces.
