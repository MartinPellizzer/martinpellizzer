Impatiens pallida f. dichroma

Impatiens pallida f. dichroma, commonly known as the pink and white impatiens, is a striking hybrid cultivar of the impatiens genus.

It features a unique coloration pattern with pink and white petals that create a bicolor effect, making it visually appealing. The plant is native to the Southeastern United States and thrives in shaded, moist environments. It has heart-shaped, glossy leaves and produces small, clustered flowers.

This cultivar is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
