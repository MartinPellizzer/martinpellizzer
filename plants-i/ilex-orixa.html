Ilex orixa

Ilex orixa, commonly known as the Mexican holly, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the family Aquifoliaceae and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that turn red in the autumn. The plant produces small, bright red berries that are an important food source for various bird species. It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
