Iguanura palmuncula

Iguanura palmuncula, commonly known as the dwarf palm, is a small, tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

Iguanura palmuncula prefers warm, humid environments and is often used as a decorative plant in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
