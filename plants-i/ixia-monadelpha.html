Ixia monadelpha

Ixia monadelpha, commonly known as the golden torch lily, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fiery orange flowers that resemble torches. Ixia monadelpha is often used in floral arrangements due to its vibrant color and long-lasting blooms.
