Ixora conspicua

Ixora conspicua, commonly known as the flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, bright orange-red inflorescences that bloom throughout the year. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Ixora conspicua is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a hedge plant.
