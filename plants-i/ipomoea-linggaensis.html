Ipomoea linggaensis

Ipomoea linggaensis, commonly known as the Lingga morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Indonesia.

It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant is a climbing vine with heart-shaped leaves and produces showy, trumpet-shaped blooms. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments.

Its ornamental value has led to its cultivation in some horticultural settings.
