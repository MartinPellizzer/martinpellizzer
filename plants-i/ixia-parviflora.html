Ixia parviflora

Ixia parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered ixia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in shades of yellow, orange, and red. It is also popular in cut flower arrangements because of its long-lasting freshness.
