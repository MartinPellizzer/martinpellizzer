Impatiens kharensis

Impatiens kharensis, commonly known as the Kharensis Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Balsaminaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small seeds.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
