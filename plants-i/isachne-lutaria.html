Isachne lutaria

Isachne lutaria, commonly known as the narrowleaf isachne, is a grass species native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. The plant has slender, grass-like leaves that are often less than 10 centimeters in length. Its inflorescence consists of a single spikelet that is usually hidden within the foliage.

Isachne lutaria is characterized by its small, inconspicuous flowers and is often overlooked due to its delicate appearance.
