Iris lisbonensis

Iris lisbonensis, commonly known as the Lisbon iris, is a species native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is a perennial herb with striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant features sword-shaped, grass-like leaves that form a basal rosette. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

Iris lisbonensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and wetlands.
