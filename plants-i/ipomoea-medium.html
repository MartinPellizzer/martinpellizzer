Ipomoea medium

Ipomoea medium, commonly known as the blue morning glory, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are usually blue or purple, opening in the morning and closing by midday. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Ipomoea medium is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant.
