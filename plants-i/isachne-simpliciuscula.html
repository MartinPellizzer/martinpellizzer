Isachne simpliciuscula

Isachne simpliciuscula, commonly known as the slender isachne, is a small, grass-like plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and small, spike-like inflorescences. This species is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread vegetatively, forming dense mats.

The plant's simple morphology and limited size make it an important component of understory vegetation in its native habitats.
