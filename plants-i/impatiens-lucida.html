Impatiens lucida

Impatiens lucida, commonly known as the shining impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, often with a yellow or greenish throat. The plant typically grows as an annual herb, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters, with opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are borne in clusters at the leaf axils, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Impatiens lucida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a cut flower.
