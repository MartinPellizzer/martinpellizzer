Ilysanthes saginoides

Ilysanthes saginoides, commonly known as the mountain everlasting, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Andes.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink. Ilysanthes saginoides is adapted to cold climates and can survive in nutrient-poor soils.
