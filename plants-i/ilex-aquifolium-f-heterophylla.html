Ilex aquifolium f. heterophylla

Ilex aquifolium f. heterophylla, commonly known as the variable holly, is a botanical variant of the common holly.

It is characterized by its varied leaf morphology, with some leaves having a more pronounced serration and others being more oval in shape. This form exhibits a range of leaf sizes and shapes, making it distinct from the typical holly. It is native to parts of Europe and is often found in woodland areas.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is used in horticulture for its unique foliage.
