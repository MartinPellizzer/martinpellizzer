Iria Hawaiiensis

Iria hawaiiensis is a rare flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands. It is part of the orchid family and is known for its delicate, fragrant blooms. This species is found primarily in the wet forest zones of Hawaii's high elevations. Due to its limited habitat and threats from invasive species, it is classified as endangered. Conservation efforts are underway to protect and preserve this unique botanical treasure.
