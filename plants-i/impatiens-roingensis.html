Impatiens roingensis

Impatiens roingensis, commonly known as the Roing impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the eastern Himalayas, specifically in regions of India and Nepal.

It belongs to the family Balsaminaceae and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, black seeds.
