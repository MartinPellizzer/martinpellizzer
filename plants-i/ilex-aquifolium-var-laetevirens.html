Ilex aquifolium var. laetevirens

Ilex aquifolium var. laetevirens, commonly known as the glossy buckthorn, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Europe.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath, with a glossy appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in the spring, followed by red berries in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges or hedgerows.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping and garden use.
