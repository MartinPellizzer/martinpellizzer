Ixophorus unisetus

Ixophorus unisetus, commonly known as the hairy grass.

It is a perennial grass species native to parts of Asia, particularly China and the Himalayas. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a hairy texture on the underside, giving it its common name.

Ixophorus unisetus is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in horticulture.
