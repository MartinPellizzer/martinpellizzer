Ipomoea stans

Ipomoea stans, commonly known as the blue morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is a climbing vine that produces showy, trumpet-shaped blue or purple flowers. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Ipomoea stans is also known for its rapid growth and ability to cover trellises or fences.
