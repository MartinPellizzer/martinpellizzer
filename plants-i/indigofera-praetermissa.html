Indigofera praetermissa

Indigofera praetermissa, commonly known as the slender indigo, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant produces elongated seed pods that contain several kidney-shaped seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
