Ipomoea goyazensis

Ipomoea goyazensis, commonly known as the Goyazensis morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It is a herbaceous vine characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of violet or blue. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often have a velvety texture.

Ipomoea goyazensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in gardens for its striking floral displays.
