Ixora javanica var. tidorensis

Ixora javanica var. tidorensis, commonly known as Tidore ixora, is a flowering plant native to the Maluku Islands in Indonesia.

It is characterized by its bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to its environment. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

Ixora javanica var. tidorensis is also valued for its ability to tolerate partial shade and its resilience in humid conditions.
