Indigofera hantamensis

Indigofera hantamensis, commonly known as Hantam indigo, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces pods that contain seeds, which are often used in traditional practices.

It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments, making it a hardy species in its native habitat.
