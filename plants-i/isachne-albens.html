Isachne albens

Isachne albens, commonly known as silver grass, is a perennial grass species native to temperate regions of Asia and Europe.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-blue foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats, such as marshes and along stream banks. Its inflorescence consists of a loose panicle with small, spikelet-like flowers.

Isachne albens is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic value and ecological benefits.
