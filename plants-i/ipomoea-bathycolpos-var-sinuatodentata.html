Ipomoea bathycolpos var. sinuatodentata

Ipomoea bathycolpos var. sinuatodentata, commonly known as the zigzag morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from deep purple to violet. The leaves are typically heart-shaped with a wavy margin, contributing to its ornamental appeal. This variety thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its vibrant blooms and unique foliage structure.
