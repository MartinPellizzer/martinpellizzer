Isodon longitubus var. effusus

Isodon longitubus var. effusus, commonly known as the hairy isodon, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features lanceolate leaves with a hairy texture, giving it its common name. Its flowers are typically arranged in whorled clusters and bloom in shades of blue or purple.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky slopes.
