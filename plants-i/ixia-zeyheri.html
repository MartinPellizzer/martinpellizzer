Ixia zeyheri

Ixia zeyheri, commonly known as the Zeyher's ixia, is a striking South African bulbous plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, straight stems that bear clusters of vibrant orange or yellow flowers with prominent yellow stamens. The plant produces a basal rosette of narrow, strap-like leaves that are evergreen throughout the growing season. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is adapted to full sun exposure.

Ixia zeyheri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a popular choice in rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
