Isatis arnoldiana

Isatis arnoldiana, commonly known as the Arnold's woad, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cabbage and mustard. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly rough texture.

Isatis arnoldiana is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.
