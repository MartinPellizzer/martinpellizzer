Impatiens kerinciensis

Impatiens kerinciensis, commonly known as the Kerinci Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Sumatra, Indonesia.

The plant belongs to the Balsaminaceae family and is known for its delicate, spurred petals and heart-shaped leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.
