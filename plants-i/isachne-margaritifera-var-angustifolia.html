Isachne margaritifera var. angustifolia

Isachne margaritifera var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed isachne, is a grass species native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as grasslands and wet meadows. The plant has slender, cylindrical stems and narrow, grass-like leaves with a slight sheen. Its inflorescence consists of spikelets that are often clustered in panicle-like structures.

This variety is distinguished by its narrower leaf blades compared to the typical form of Isachne margaritifera.
