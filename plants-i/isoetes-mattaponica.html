Isoetes mattaponica

Isoetes mattaponica, commonly known as the Mattaponi watermilfoil, is a species of aquatic fern that belongs to the order Isoetales.

It is characterized by its dense, ribbon-like leaves that are typically submerged in freshwater environments. This plant is native to the eastern United States, particularly in the Chesapeake Bay region. Isoetes mattaponica thrives in shallow, still waters with soft, muddy substrates.

Its presence is often an indicator of good water quality and stable ecological conditions.
