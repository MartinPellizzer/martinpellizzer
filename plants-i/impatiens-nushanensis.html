Impatiens nushanensis

Impatiens nushanensis, commonly known as Nushan Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to China, specifically the Nushan region.

It belongs to the family Balsaminaceae and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small seeds.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories, where it plays a role in local biodiversity.
