Ixia trinervata

Ixia trinervata, commonly known as the Natal fire lily, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking orange-red flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant features strap-shaped leaves that are typically green with a reddish tint along the edges. Ixia trinervata thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
