Isodon flexicaulis

Isodon flexicaulis, commonly known as the flexible wood betony, is a perennial herb native to regions of Asia, particularly China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with hairy surfaces and produces small, tubular flowers in shades of purple or blue. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Isodon flexicaulis is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal medicine.
