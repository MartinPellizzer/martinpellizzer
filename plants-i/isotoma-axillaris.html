Isotoma axillaris

Isotoma axillaris, commonly known as the axillary bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that bear small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue or purple. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often arranged in a whorled pattern around the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its compact growth habit and delicate flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
