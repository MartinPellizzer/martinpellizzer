Iberis gibraltarica var. pubescens

Iberis gibraltarica var. pubescens, commonly known as the Gibraltar carnation, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the Rock of Gibraltar.

It features dense clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring. The plant has hairy, gray-green foliage that adds texture to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in mild climates.
