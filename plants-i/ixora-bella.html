Ixora bella

Ixora bella, commonly known as the flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of bright red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has opposite, simple, and usually ovate or elliptical leaves. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Ixora bella is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
