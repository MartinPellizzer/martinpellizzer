Isertia rosea

Isertia rosea, commonly known as the rosea isertia, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant has silvery-gray foliage that helps reduce water loss in its dry habitat. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Isertia rosea is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance in landscaping.
