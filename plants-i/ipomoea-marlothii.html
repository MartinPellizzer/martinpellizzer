Ipomoea marlothii

Ipomoea marlothii, commonly known as the marlothii morning glory, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in the morning and close by midday. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Ipomoea marlothii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
