Iris stolonifera var. leichtlinii

Iris stolonifera var. leichtlinii, commonly known as the slender iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking blue to violet flowers with three upright petals and three drooping sepals. The plant spreads through stolons, forming dense mats that can colonize rocky or sandy soils. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil conditions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to harsh environmental conditions.
