Iris × germanica var. australis

Iris × germanica var. australis, commonly known as the Australian bearded iris, is a hybrid perennial plant native to Australia.

It features showy, upright flowers with three upright petals and three recurved sepals, often in shades of purple, blue, and white. The plant has a rhizomatous root system and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure. It is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens and landscapes.

The variety is known for its robust growth and ability to produce multiple blooms throughout the growing season.
