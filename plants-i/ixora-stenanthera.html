Ixora stenanthera

Ixora stenanthera, commonly known as the flame flower or red ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, featuring dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, brightly colored flowers, usually in shades of red, orange, or yellow, which bloom throughout the year. Its flowers are borne on short pedicels and are known for their showy appearance and ability to attract pollinators.

Ixora stenanthera is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to various soil types.
