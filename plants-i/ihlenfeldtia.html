Ihlenfeldtia

Ihlenfeldtia, commonly known as the golden rain tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy yellow flowers that appear in clusters, giving it its common name. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant.

The seeds are enclosed in a fluffy, wind-dispersed capsule, aiding in its propagation.
