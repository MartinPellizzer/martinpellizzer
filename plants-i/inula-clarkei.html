Inula clarkei

Inula clarkei, commonly known as the Kashmiri fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and hairy, aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky habitats at high altitudes. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Inula clarkei is valued for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
