Isoloma inaequale

Isoloma inaequale, commonly known as the unequal leaved dandelion, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features basal leaves that are unequal in size, giving it its common name. Its yellow flowers bloom in the spring and are followed by fluffy white seed heads.

Isoloma inaequale is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats.
