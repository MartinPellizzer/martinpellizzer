Imperatoria ostruthium

Imperatoria ostruthium, commonly known as the bladder campion, is a flowering plant native to Europe, western Asia, and northern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has small, bladder-like structures on its stems, which are specialized seed pods that burst open when mature to disperse seeds. Imperatoria ostruthium thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family, known for its showy flowers and succulent-like leaves.
