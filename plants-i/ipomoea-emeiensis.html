Ipomoea emeiensis

Ipomoea emeiensis, commonly known as the Emei morning glory, is a flowering vine native to China.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and violet. The plant produces heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The vine is also notable for its rapid growth and ability to climb various structures.
