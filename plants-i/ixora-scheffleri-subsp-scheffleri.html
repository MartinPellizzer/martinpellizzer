Ixora scheffleri subsp. scheffleri

Ixora scheffleri subsp.

scheffleri, commonly known as the red ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its dense, branched shrub form and vibrant red or orange-red inflorescences.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically borne on long, slender pedicels. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and glossy, with a dark green color.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sun exposure.
