Impatiens platyceras

Impatiens platyceras, commonly known as the broad-capsied impatiens, is a species of flowering plant in the family Balsaminaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. Impatiens platyceras is a popular choice for gardeners seeking vibrant, easy-to-care-for flowering plants.
