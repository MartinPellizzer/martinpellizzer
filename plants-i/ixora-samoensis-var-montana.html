Ixora samoensis var. montana

Ixora samoensis var. montana, commonly known as the mountain ixora, is a flowering shrub native to the mountainous regions of Samoa.

It produces clusters of bright orange-red flowers that bloom throughout the year, attracting various pollinators. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for tropical and subtropical climates.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
