Ixeris chelidoniifolia

Ixeris chelidoniifolia, commonly known as the yellow bellflower, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant has slender, grass-like leaves and is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks. It is a perennial herb that spreads through rhizomes, forming dense colonies.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
