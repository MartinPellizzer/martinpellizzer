Ilex dahoon var. ligustrina

Ilex dahoon var. ligustrina, commonly known as the Yaupon Holly, is a compact evergreen shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features small, dark green, glossy leaves that are typically oval in shape and have a slight waxy coating. The plant produces tiny, red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It is highly adaptable, thriving in a variety of soil types and light conditions, making it a popular choice for landscaping.

Its dense growth habit also provides excellent erosion control and privacy screening.
