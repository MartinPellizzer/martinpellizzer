Impatiens rubromaculata

Impatiens rubromaculata, commonly known as the red-spotted impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange-red flowers with dark red spots, which are highly ornamental. The plant typically grows as an upright herb with opposite, ovate leaves that have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its blooming period extends from late spring to early autumn, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking colorful, shade-tolerant plants.
