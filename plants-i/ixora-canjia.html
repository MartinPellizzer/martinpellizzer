Ixora canjia

Ixora canjia, commonly known as the red flame or flame tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange to red flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the rainy season. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sunlight.

Ixora canjia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a street tree due to its striking appearance.
