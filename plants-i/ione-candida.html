Ione candida

Ione candida, commonly known as the white bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale blue, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, upright stems covered in fine hairs and produces small, oval leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or open habitats.

Ione candida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
