Ixeris podlechii

Ixeris podlechii, commonly known as the Japanese cineraria, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces bright yellow flowers with a distinctive, narrow shape. The plant has a dense, mat-like growth habit and is often used in rock gardens or as a ground cover. Its leaves are typically blue-green in color and have a slightly hairy texture.

Ixeris podlechii thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for alpine gardens.
