Indigofera calcicola

Indigofera calcicola, commonly known as the limestone indigo, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions.

Indigofera calcicola is often found in rocky or limestone habitats, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
