Ixeris graminea

Ixeris graminea, commonly known as the Japanese mat grass, is a low-growing perennial plant native to East Asia.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically bright green and arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Ixeris graminea is valued for its ornamental appeal and easy maintenance in garden settings.
