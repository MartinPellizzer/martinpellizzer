Itea chingiana

Itea chingiana, commonly known as Chinese spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features slender, arching stems that produce clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers in early summer. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its botanical characteristics make it suitable for gardens and landscapes where compact growth and seasonal blooms are desired.
