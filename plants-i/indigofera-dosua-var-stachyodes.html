Indigofera dosua var. stachyodes

Indigofera dosua var. stachyodes, commonly known as the silverleaf indigo, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and showy, purple-blue flowers. The plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it valuable in agricultural systems.

Its ornamental value comes from its delicate foliage and vibrant blooms, which attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
