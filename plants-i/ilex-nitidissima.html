Ilex nitidissima

Ilex nitidissima, commonly known as the glossy-leaf holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically leathery and have a smooth texture. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for various bird species. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Ilex nitidissima is also valued for its ability to maintain its foliage year-round, providing visual interest in the winter months.
