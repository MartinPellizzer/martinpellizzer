Isolepis tenuipes

Isolepis tenuipes, commonly known as slender spike-rush, is a grass-like plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems that are often slightly hairy and grow in dense clusters. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, which are typically wind-pollinated. Isolepis tenuipes is commonly found in wetland environments such as marshes, swamps, and the edges of ponds.

It plays a vital role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic and terrestrial organisms.
