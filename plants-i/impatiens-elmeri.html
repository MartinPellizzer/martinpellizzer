Impatiens elmeri

Impatiens elmeri, commonly known as Elmer's impatiens, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and a trailing growth habit, making it suitable for hanging baskets or as a ground cover. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Impatiens elmeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
