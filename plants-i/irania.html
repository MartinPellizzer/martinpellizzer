Irania

Irania, commonly known as the golden rain tree, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, golden-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and turn red in the fall. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant.

The tree is also valued for its fast growth and ability to provide shade in urban landscapes.
