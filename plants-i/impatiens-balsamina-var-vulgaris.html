Impatiens balsamina var. vulgaris

Impatiens balsamina var. vulgaris, commonly known as the common touch-me-not, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves and grows as an annual in temperate climates. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Its rapid growth and showy blooms make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental plants.
