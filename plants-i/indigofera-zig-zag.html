Indigofera zig-zag

Indigofera zig-zag, commonly known as the zig-zag indigo.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia. The plant is characterized by its distinctive zig-zag pattern in its foliage, which gives it its common name. Its small, pea-like flowers are typically blue or purple, attracting various pollinators.

The plant is also valued for its use in traditional medicine and as a source of natural dyes.
