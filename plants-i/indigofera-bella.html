Indigofera bella

Indigofera bella, commonly known as the velvetleaf indigo, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, Africa, and the Americas. The plant is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. It produces elongated, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.
