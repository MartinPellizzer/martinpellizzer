Impatiens atrorubra

Impatiens atrorubra, commonly known as the dark red impatiens, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking dark red to nearly black flowers, which contrast sharply with its glossy, dark green foliage. The plant typically grows as an upright herb, reaching heights of up to 60 centimeters. Its flowers are spurred, a feature common in the Impatiens genus, and bloom in clusters along the stems.

It prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
