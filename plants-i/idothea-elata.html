Idothea elata

Idothea elata, commonly known as the African snowball, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils, often found in the Cape Province. The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring, giving it a snowball-like appearance. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Idothea elata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive flowers and hardy nature.
