Isotoma longiflora var. runcinata

Isotoma longiflora var. runcinata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved mouse-ear, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features small, silver-gray leaves that are lanceolate in shape, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in alpine meadows.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
