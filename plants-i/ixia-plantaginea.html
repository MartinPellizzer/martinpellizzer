Ixia plantaginea

Ixia plantaginea, commonly known as the yellow ox tongue, is a strikingly beautiful plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers that resemble a tongue in shape. The plant features strap-like, grassy leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Ixia plantaginea is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its long-lasting blooms.
