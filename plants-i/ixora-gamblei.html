Ixora gamblei

Ixora gamblei, commonly known as the flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, clustered orange-red flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has opposite, simple, and glossy dark green leaves. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Ixora gamblei is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
