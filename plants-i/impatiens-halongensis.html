Impatiens halongensis

Impatiens halongensis, commonly known as the Halong Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, herbaceous stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
