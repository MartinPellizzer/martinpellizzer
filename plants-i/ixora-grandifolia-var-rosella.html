Ixora grandifolia var. rosella

Ixora grandifolia var. rosella, commonly known as the Rosella ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a striking visual appeal to gardens. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are glossy and arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The Rosella ixora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
