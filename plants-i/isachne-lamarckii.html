Isachne lamarckii

Isachne lamarckii, commonly known as the Lamarck's isachne, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in moist to wet environments, such as marshes and along riverbanks. Its inflorescence consists of a single spikelet, which is often subtended by a bract.

Isachne lamarckii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its attractive foliage.
