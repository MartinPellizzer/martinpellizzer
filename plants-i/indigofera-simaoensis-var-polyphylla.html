Indigofera simaoensis var. polyphylla

Indigofera simaoensis var. polyphylla, commonly known as the Simao indigo plant, is a flowering plant species native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant is notable for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, which makes it beneficial for soil enrichment.

It typically grows in forested areas and is valued for its ornamental and potential medicinal properties.
