Inula canariensis

Inula canariensis, commonly known as the Canary Islands Inula, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features a tall, branching stem with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in clusters, typical of the daisy family.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to the volcanic terrain of its native habitat.
