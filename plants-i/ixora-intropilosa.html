Ixora intropilosa

Ixora intropilosa, commonly known as the red flame ixora, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bright red or orange flowers that bloom in dense clusters, often giving it a fiery appearance. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape and have a smooth texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Ixora intropilosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
