Ipomoea flammea

Ipomoea flammea, commonly known as the flame vine, is a strikingly ornamental flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red, often with a distinctive red throat. The plant produces heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green, and it thrives in warm, humid climates. Ipomoea flammea is a climbing vine that can grow quite vigorously, often reaching lengths of several meters.

It is often cultivated for its showy blooms and is a popular choice for ornamental gardens and as a houseplant.
