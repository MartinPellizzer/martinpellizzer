Ilex morii

Ilex morii, commonly known as the Japanese holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to Japan.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, white flowers in the spring, which are followed by bright red berries in the fall. Ilex morii is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its dense foliage and seasonal color changes.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
