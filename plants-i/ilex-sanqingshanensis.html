Ilex sanqingshanensis

Ilex sanqingshanensis, commonly known as the Sanqingshan holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, glossy dark green leaves and produces small, red berries that are toxic to humans but serve as a food source for certain wildlife. The plant is typically found in mountainous regions with moist, well-drained soils. It has a slow growth rate and is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value.

The species is also valued for its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
