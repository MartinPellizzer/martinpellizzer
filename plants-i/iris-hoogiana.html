Iris hoogiana

Iris hoogiana, commonly known as the Yellow Flag Iris, is a striking perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, slender stems that bear showy, six-petaled flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that form a dense basal rosette. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found near water bodies.

Iris hoogiana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is commonly cultivated in gardens and wetland habitats.
