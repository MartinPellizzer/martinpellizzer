Isatis canescens var. glabrifolia

Isatis canescens var. glabrifolia, commonly known as the smoothleaf woad, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cabbage and mustard. The plant is characterized by its smooth, hairless leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers. It is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and streambanks.

The species is valued for its historical use in dye production and traditional medicine.
