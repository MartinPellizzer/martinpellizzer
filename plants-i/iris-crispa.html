Iris crispa

Iris crispa, commonly known as the wrinkled iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features striking blue to violet flowers with a distinctive crinkled pattern on the petals. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Iris crispa is valued in gardens for its ornamental beauty and historical use in perfumery.
