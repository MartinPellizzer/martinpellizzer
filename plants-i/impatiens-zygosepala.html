Impatiens zygosepala

Impatiens zygosepala, commonly known as the Zygosepala Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its rapid growth and ability to self-seed make it a common sight in humid, tropical climates.
