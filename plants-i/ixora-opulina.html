Ixora opulina

Ixora opulina, commonly known as the red flame ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of bright red, tubular flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and a shrub-like growth habit. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Ixora opulina is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
