Ixia elliptica

Ixia elliptica, commonly known as the Orange Oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking orange flowers with yellow centers. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy blooms during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Ixia elliptica is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
