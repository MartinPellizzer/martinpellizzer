Ixeridium yunnanense

Ixeridium yunnanense, commonly known as Yunnan goldenrod, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in mountainous regions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and somewhat woody.

Ixeridium yunnanense is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
