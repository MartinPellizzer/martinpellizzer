Icomum

Icomum, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet flower heads surrounded by spiny, leaf-like bracts. Native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean, it thrives in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters and is often found in maritime environments.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and coastal landscaping.
