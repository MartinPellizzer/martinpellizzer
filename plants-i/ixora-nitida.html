Ixora nitida

Ixora nitida, commonly known as the slender flame tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are elliptical in shape. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Ixora nitida is also valued for its ability to grow in a variety of soil types and its resilience to drought.
