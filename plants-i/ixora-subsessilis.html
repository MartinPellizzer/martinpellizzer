Ixora subsessilis

Ixora subsessilis, commonly known as the small-leaved flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of bright red or orange flowers, which bloom throughout the year in warm climates. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are typically arranged opposite each other on the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Ixora subsessilis is also valued for its ability to tolerate coastal conditions and is often used in landscaping along shores and in gardens.
