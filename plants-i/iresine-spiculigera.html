Iresine spiculigera

Iresine spiculigera, commonly known as the red-stemmed iron plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking red stems and foliage with a metallic sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow, arranged in clusters. Its leaves are often covered in fine, silky hairs, contributing to its unique appearance.

Iresine spiculigera is often cultivated for its ornamental value in shaded garden settings.
