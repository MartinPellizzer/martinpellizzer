Ilex integra var. beecheyi

Ilex integra var. beecheyi, commonly known as the Mexican holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for various bird species. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
