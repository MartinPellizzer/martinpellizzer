Iresine celosia var. verticillata

Iresine celosia var. verticillata, commonly known as the flame flower or red hot poker, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical regions.

It features vibrant, feather-like inflorescences that range from red to orange, giving it its common name. The plant has palmate leaves with a velvety texture and a deep green color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for gardens and containers.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for adding visual interest to landscapes.
