Isatis microcarpa

Isatis microcarpa, commonly known as the Chinese yellow mustard, is a flowering plant native to China and parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant produces small, rounded seed pods that contain numerous seeds, which are used in traditional medicine and as a source of oil. Its leaves are edible and have a peppery flavor, often used in cooking.

Isatis microcarpa is also valued for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its role in ecological restoration projects.
