Ilex sarawaccensis

Ilex sarawaccensis, commonly known as the Sarawak holly, is a species of flowering plant in the family Aquifoliaceae.

It is native to the tropical rainforests of Borneo, specifically in the state of Sarawak. The plant is characterized by its evergreen foliage, with glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It produces small, white flowers followed by bright red berries that are edible for birds.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
