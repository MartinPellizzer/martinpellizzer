Ibania Retinervia

Ibania retinervia is a unique and lesser-known plant species native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its strikingly veined leaves and distinctive floral structure. Despite its ornamental appeal, it remains underappreciated in the horticultural world. The plant's adaptability to shaded environments makes it a valuable addition to tropical gardens. Its study offers insights into the biodiversity of understory flora in tropical ecosystems.
