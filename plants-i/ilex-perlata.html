Ilex perlata

Ilex perlata, commonly known as the silver leaf holly, is a evergreen shrub or small tree native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that have a silvery underside, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Ilex perlata is also valued for its hardiness and ability to tolerate urban environments.
