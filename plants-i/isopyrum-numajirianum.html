Isopyrum numajirianum

Isopyrum numajirianum, commonly known as the Japanese marsh-orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Orchidaceae.

It is native to eastern Asia, particularly found in regions of Japan and Korea. This perennial herb typically grows in wet or marshy habitats, often near water bodies. The plant features showy, violet or purple flowers that bloom in summer.

Its botanical characteristics include narrow leaves and a rhizomatous root system, which help it thrive in moist environments.
