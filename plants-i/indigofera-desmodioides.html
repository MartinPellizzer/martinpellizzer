Indigofera desmodioides

Indigofera desmodioides, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wet, swampy areas. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves and produces striking blue or purple flowers. Its root system is fibrous and often used in traditional medicine.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in wetland habitats.
