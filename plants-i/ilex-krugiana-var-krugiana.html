Ilex krugiana var. krugiana

Ilex krugiana var. krugiana, commonly known as the Chinese holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and small, dark green leaves that are often glossy. The plant produces bright red berries that are toxic to humans and animals. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
