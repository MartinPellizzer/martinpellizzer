Ixora finlaysoniana

Ixora finlaysoniana, commonly known as the Malayan flame or Finlayson's ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom in dense clusters, making it a striking ornamental plant. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in humid, shaded environments.

Ixora finlaysoniana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
