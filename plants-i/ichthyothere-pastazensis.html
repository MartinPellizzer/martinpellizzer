Ichthyothere pastazensis

Ichthyothere pastazensis, commonly known as the Pastazensis aquatic fern, is a rare aquatic plant native to the high-altitude wetlands of the Andes.

It possesses delicate, ribbon-like leaves that float on the water's surface, with specialized structures for gas exchange. The plant exhibits a unique adaptation to its environment, thriving in nutrient-poor, cold waters. Its reproductive system involves the production of small, spore-like structures that are dispersed by water currents.
