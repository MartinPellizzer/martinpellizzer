Ixora inundata

Ixora inundata, commonly known as the water ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in wet, swampy areas. The plant produces clusters of bright red or orange flowers, which are often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are simple and opposite, with a smooth surface and a slightly waxy texture.

Ixora inundata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in wetland ecosystems.
