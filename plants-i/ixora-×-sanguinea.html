Ixora × sanguinea

Ixora × sanguinea, commonly known as the Chinese hibiscus, is a hybrid shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or orange flowers, which are clustered in panicles and bloom throughout the year. The plant has simple, opposite, and ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Ixora × sanguinea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a tropical landscape plant.
