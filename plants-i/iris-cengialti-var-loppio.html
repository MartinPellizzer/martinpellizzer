Iris cengialti var. loppio

Iris cengialti var. loppio, commonly known as the Loppio iris, is a rare and ornamental variety of the iris genus.

It is characterized by its striking violet-blue flowers with a distinctive white throat and intricate patterns. Native to the alpine regions of the Dolomites in Italy, this plant thrives in cool, moist environments with well-drained soil. The leaves are sword-shaped, dark green, and often have a silvery underside.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
