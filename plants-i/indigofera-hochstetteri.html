Indigofera hochstetteri

Indigofera hochstetteri, commonly known as the Hochstetter's indigo, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and striking blue or purple flowers. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant blooms and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.
