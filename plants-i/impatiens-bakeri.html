Impatiens bakeri

Impatiens bakeri, commonly known as Baker's impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, spurred petals and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as under forest canopies. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped and have a smooth, glossy texture.

Impatiens bakeri is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade.
