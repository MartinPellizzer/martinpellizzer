Ixora longissima

Ixora longissima, commonly known as the long-stemmed flame bush, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, bright red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are elliptical in shape. Its stems are woody and can grow quite long, giving it its common name.

Ixora longissima is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a hedge plant.
