Impatiens celligera

Impatiens celligera, commonly known as the silky impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture, and its stems are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
