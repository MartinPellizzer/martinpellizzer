Ismene sublimis

Ismene sublimis, commonly known as the slender bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are hairy and grayish-green in appearance. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue to purple, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Ismene sublimis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
