Impatiens yingjiangensis

Impatiens yingjiangensis, commonly known as the Yungjiang Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Balsaminaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, spurred flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and grows as an annual herb, often reaching a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
