Impatiens renae

Impatiens renae, commonly known as the noble impatiens, is a species of flowering plant in the family Balsaminaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white. Its foliage consists of ovate, glossy leaves that are arranged oppositely along the stems.

Impatiens renae thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
