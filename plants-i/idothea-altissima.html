Idothea altissima

Idothea altissima, commonly known as the giant fern, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its large, feathery fronds.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to the humid regions of Southeast Asia. The plant features a creeping rhizome with numerous aerial roots that help it anchor to trees or rocks. Its leaves can grow up to several meters in length, making it one of the tallest ferns in its habitat.

Idothea altissima thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in dense tropical forests.
