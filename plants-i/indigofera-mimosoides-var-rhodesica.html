Indigofera mimosoides var. rhodesica

Indigofera mimosoides var. rhodesica, commonly known as Rhodesian mimosa, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family Fabaceae and is characterized by its bipinnate leaves and showy, pea-like flowers. The plant is typically shrub-like, with gray-green foliage and can grow up to several meters in height.

It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun, making it a hardy ornamental species.
