Isoetes variabilis var. adspersa

Isoetes variabilis var. adspersa, commonly known as the scattered water fern, is a species of aquatic plant belonging to the genus Isoetes.

It is characterized by its spore-bearing leaves that are often scattered along the water surface. The plant thrives in shallow, still or slow-moving freshwater environments. Its morphology includes a central axis with specialized leaf structures for anchoring and spore dispersal.

It plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and contributing to nutrient cycling.
