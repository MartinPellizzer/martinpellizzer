Ilex pernyi var. manupurensis

Ilex pernyi var. manupurensis, commonly known as the Manupure holly, is a variant of the Ilex pernyi species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree characterized by its dense, leathery foliage. The leaves are typically elliptical or ovate, with a glossy dark green upper surface and a lighter underside. It produces small, white flowers followed by bright red berries that are an important food source for birds.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticulture for its hardy nature and aesthetic qualities.
