Ionidium verticillatum

Ionidium verticillatum, commonly known as the spiral leaf plant, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive spiral arrangement of leaves around the stem, a feature that gives it its scientific name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Ionidium verticillatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
