Ione bifurcatoflorens

Ione bifurcatoflorens, commonly known as the bifurcate florets plant, is a flowering species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique inflorescence, which consists of two distinct flower clusters that branch off from a central stem. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, often displaying a range of colors from white to pale yellow.

Ione bifurcatoflorens is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions.
