Impatiens peperomioides var. impressa

Impatiens peperomioides var. impressa, commonly known as the velvet leaf impatiens, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its velvety, dark green foliage.

The leaves are typically broad, heart-shaped, and have a textured surface that gives them a unique appearance. It produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, which bloom throughout the growing season. This variety thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both ornamental and horticultural purposes.
