Ixora rudgeoides

Ixora rudgeoides, commonly known as the red flame ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height, characterized by its dense, glossy green leaves. The plant produces clusters of bright red, tubular flowers that bloom throughout the year, making it a popular ornamental species. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Ixora rudgeoides is often cultivated for its vibrant floral display and is used in landscaping and horticultural gardens.
