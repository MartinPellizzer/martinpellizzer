Iris × oolopha

Iris × oolopha, commonly known as the hybrid iris, is a cross between different species of the iris genus.

It typically exhibits a combination of characteristics from its parent plants, such as varying flower colors and foliage structures. The plant is known for its tall, upright growth habit and showy, often fragrant blooms. It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and botanical collections.
