Isachne caillei

Isachne caillei, commonly known as the Caille's isachne, is a species of grass native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant typically features narrow, grass-like leaves with a bluish-green hue. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species within its native range.
