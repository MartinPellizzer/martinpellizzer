Iridorkis treutleri

Iridorkis treutleri, commonly known as the treutler's iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to specific regions of South America.

It features slender, upright stems that bear showy, violet-blue flowers with a distinctive three-lobed petal structure. Iridorkis treutleri is valued in horticulture for its elegant blooms and adaptability to garden settings.
