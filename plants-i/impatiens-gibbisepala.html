Impatiens gibbisepala

Impatiens gibbisepala, commonly known as the wavy-sepal impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and distinctive wavy sepals that give it its scientific name. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens gibbisepala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
