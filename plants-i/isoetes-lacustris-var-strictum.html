Isoetes lacustris var. strictum

Isoetes lacustris var. strictum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf watermace, is a species of aquatic plant belonging to the genus Isoetes.

It is characterized by its compact, spire-like structure composed of tightly packed, dark green, sword-shaped leaves. This plant typically grows in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, such as lakes and ponds. Its leaves are often submerged, with some extending just above the water surface.

The plant plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small organisms and contributing to water filtration.
