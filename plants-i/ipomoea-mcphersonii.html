Ipomoea mcphersonii

Ipomoea mcphersonii, commonly known as the McPherson's morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and violet. The plant produces heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Ipomoea mcphersonii is a climbing vine that can reach lengths of several meters, making it a popular ornamental plant.
