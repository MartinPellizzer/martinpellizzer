Iberis ciliata subsp. contracta

Iberis ciliata subsp.

contracta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved candytuft, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring.

The plant has compact, mat-forming growth habit with hairy, narrow leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value comes from its delicate flowers and low maintenance requirements.
