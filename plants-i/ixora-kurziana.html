Ixora kurziana

Ixora kurziana, commonly known as the flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bright orange to red flowers, which are clustered in dense, showy inflorescences. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually oval or elliptical in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Ixora kurziana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
