Impatiens bullatisepala

Impatiens bullatisepala, commonly known as the bulbat-sepal impatiens, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and orange. The plant features broad, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving them a velvety texture. Its sepals are notably enlarged and fleshy, which is a distinguishing feature of the species.

Impatiens bullatisepala thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
