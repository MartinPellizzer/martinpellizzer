Iris Relicta

The iris relicta is a rare and ancient plant found in isolated pockets of its native habitat. Known for its striking blue or purple flowers, it has captivated botanists and nature lovers alike. This species is considered endangered due to habitat loss and environmental changes. Conservation efforts are underway to protect and preserve its dwindling populations. Its survival is a symbol of resilience in a rapidly changing world.
