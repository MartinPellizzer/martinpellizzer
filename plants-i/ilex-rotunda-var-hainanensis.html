Ilex rotunda var. hainanensis

Ilex rotunda var. hainanensis, commonly known as the Hainan holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to southern China.

It belongs to the family Aquifoliaceae and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter on the underside. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
