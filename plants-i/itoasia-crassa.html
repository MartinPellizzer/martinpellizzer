Itoasia crassa

Itoasia crassa, commonly known as the thick-leafed hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, thick, waxy leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in moist, well-drained soils. It belongs to the Malvaceae family, which includes other hibiscus species.
