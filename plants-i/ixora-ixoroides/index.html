Ixora Ixoroides

Ixora ixoroides is a strikingly beautiful shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. Known for its vibrant clusters of red flowers, it is a popular choice for ornamental gardening and landscaping. This plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for both container gardening and in-ground planting. With its long blooming season and low maintenance requirements, Ixora ixoroides is a favorite among plant enthusiasts.
