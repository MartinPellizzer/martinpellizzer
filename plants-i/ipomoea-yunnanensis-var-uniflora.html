Ipomoea yunnanensis var. uniflora

Ipomoea yunnanensis var. uniflora, commonly known as the Yunnan morning glory, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its striking violet to blue flowers, which bloom in the early morning and close by midday. The plant produces heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and is often found in moist, shaded environments. It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is known for its rapid growth and climbing habit.
