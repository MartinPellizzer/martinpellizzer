Ionidium commune var. glabrifolium

Ionidium commune var. glabrifolium, commonly known as the smoothleaf ionidium, is a variant of the ionidium plant characterized by its smooth, lance-shaped leaves.

It is native to specific regions in Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, tropical climates. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom during the wet season. Its leaves are typically glossy and have a slightly waxy texture, which helps reduce water loss in its natural environment.

This variant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
