Ixora capillaris

Ixora capillaris, commonly known as the flame tree or scarlet ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bright red or orange flowers that bloom in dense clusters, creating a striking visual display. The plant has opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are elliptical in shape. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Ixora capillaris is also valued for its ability to grow in a variety of soil types and its relatively low maintenance requirements.
