Ipomoea malvaeoides var. ovata

Ipomoea malvaeoides var. ovata, commonly known as the broad-leaf morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in the morning.
