Ixora schomburgkiana

Ixora schomburgkiana, commonly known as the flame bush, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from red to orange, often resembling flames. The plant produces small, oval-shaped berries that are typically red when ripe. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Ixora schomburgkiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
