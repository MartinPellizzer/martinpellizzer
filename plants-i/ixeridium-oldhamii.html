Ixeridium oldhamii

Ixeridium oldhamii, commonly known as the oldham's pincushion, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, globular shape and dense clusters of small, yellowish flowers. Ixeridium oldhamii is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
