Impatiens odorata

Impatiens odorata, commonly known as the sweet pepper impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb, reaching heights of up to 3 feet. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are slender and often branched.

Impatiens odorata thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shade gardens.
