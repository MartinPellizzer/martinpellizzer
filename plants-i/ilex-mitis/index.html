Ilex Mitis

California holly, scientifically known as Ilex mitis, is a hardy evergreen shrub native to the coastal regions of California. It is prized for its dense foliage, vibrant green leaves, and ability to thrive in challenging environments. This plant is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements. Despite its resilience, it faces threats from habitat loss and climate change. Its unique characteristics make it an important species for both ecological and aesthetic purposes.
