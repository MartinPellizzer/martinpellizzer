Iris watsoniana

Iris watsoniana, commonly known as the Watson's iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, slender stems that bear showy, lavender-blue flowers with a distinctive white center. The plant has lance-shaped, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its striking blooms and hardy nature.
