Impatiens micromeris

Impatiens micromeris, commonly known as the dwarf impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its small, delicate leaves and showy, five-petaled flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. It has a hairy stem and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds.

Impatiens micromeris is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade.
