Impatiens rodigasii

Impatiens rodigasii, commonly known as the red-eyed impatiens, is a species of flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of East Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically feature bright red petals with a contrasting white or yellow center. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white wax. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens rodigasii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
