Ixora diversifolia var. flexilis

Ixora diversifolia var. flexilis, commonly known as the flexible flame bush, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and produces clusters of bright orange-red flowers during the summer months. The leaves are opposite, simple, and have a glossy, dark green appearance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its vibrant blooms and hardy nature.

This variety is a popular choice for gardeners seeking a colorful, low-maintenance shrub.
