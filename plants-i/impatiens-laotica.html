Impatiens laotica

Impatiens laotica, commonly known as the Laos impatiens, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Balsaminaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that burst open to disperse seeds. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens laotica is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
