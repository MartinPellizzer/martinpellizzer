Ipomoea kituiensis var. massaiensis

Ipomoea kituiensis var. massaiensis, commonly known as the Massai morning glory, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in the morning. The plant typically grows as a climbing vine with heart-shaped leaves and is often found in moist, tropical environments. It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

The species is also noted for its ecological role in supporting local pollinators.
