Iberis thracica

Iberis thracica, commonly known as the Thrace Iberis, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in early spring. Its leaves are pinnately divided, with a distinctive, feathery appearance.

Iberis thracica is often found in rocky or limestone soils and is valued for its ornamental qualities in gardens.
