Ixora megalophylla

Ixora megalophylla, commonly known as the giant ixora, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that are typically bright red or orange in color. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Ixora megalophylla is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
