Ilex × koehneana

Ilex × koehneana, commonly known as the Chinese holly, is a hybrid evergreen shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, glossy dark green foliage and small, ornamental berries that persist through winter. The plant typically grows to a height of 3 to 6 meters and has a broad, rounded shape. Its leaves are leathery and have a serrated margin, while the flowers are small and inconspicuous, appearing in clusters.

Ilex × koehneana is valued in horticulture for its year-round aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
