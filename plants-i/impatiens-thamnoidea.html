Impatiens thamnoidea

Impatiens thamnoidea, commonly known as the water impatiens, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are often glossy and slightly hairy. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found near water sources such as streams or wetlands.

Impatiens thamnoidea is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
