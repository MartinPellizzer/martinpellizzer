Isostigma simplicifolium

Isostigma simplicifolium, commonly known as the simple-leaved starplant, is a perennial herb native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white, typically during the cooler months. The plant has simple, linear leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette, providing efficient water conservation in its harsh environment. Its root system is fibrous and shallow, allowing it to absorb moisture from the surface layer of the soil.

Isostigma simplicifolium is often found in rocky outcrops and sandy soils, where it thrives in full sun and low humidity.
