Iva axillaris var. robustior

Iva axillaris var. robustior, commonly known as the robust ironweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant features rough, hairy stems and heart-shaped, lobed leaves that are often hairy on the underside. Its showy purple to pink flower heads appear in late summer and attract various pollinators.

It thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in wetlands, stream banks, and prairies.
