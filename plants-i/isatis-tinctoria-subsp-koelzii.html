Isatis tinctoria subsp. koelzii

Isatis tinctoria subsp.

koelzii, commonly known as the Chinese yellow mustard, is a variant of the yellow rocket plant native to China. It is a biennial herb characterized by its upright stems and bright yellow flowers that bloom in the summer.

The plant produces distinctive heart-shaped leaves with a slightly rough texture. It is known for its high content of glucosinolates, which contribute to its medicinal and pesticidal properties.

This subspecies is often cultivated for its potential health benefits and traditional uses in herbal medicine.
