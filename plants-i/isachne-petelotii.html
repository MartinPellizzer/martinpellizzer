Isachne petelotii

Isachne petelotii, commonly known as the Petelot's isachne, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

Isachne petelotii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
