Ilex robustinervosa

Ilex robustinervosa, commonly known as the robust-leaved holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the family Aquifoliaceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with prominent veins. Ilex robustinervosa is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
