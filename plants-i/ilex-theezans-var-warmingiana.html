Ilex theezans var. warmingiana

Ilex theezans var. warmingiana, commonly known as the Chinese holly, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern China.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter on the underside. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for ornamental landscaping.

Due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal, it is often cultivated in gardens and parks.
