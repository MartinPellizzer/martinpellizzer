Iresine mutisii

Iresine mutisii, commonly known as the red-rooted iron plant, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety red leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically pink or white. The plant belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant foliage.
