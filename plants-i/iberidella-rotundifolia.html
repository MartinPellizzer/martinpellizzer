Iberidella rotundifolia

Iberidella rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leafed iberidella, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, rounded leaves and clusters of delicate, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is a perennial species that produces small, capsule-like fruits containing seeds.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
