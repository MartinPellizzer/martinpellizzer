Iberis intermedia var. polita

Iberis intermedia var. polita, commonly known as the white-flowered candytuft, is a low-growing, herbaceous perennial native to southern Europe.

It features dense clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in early spring, adding a delicate, ornamental touch to gardens. The plant has fuzzy, silver-gray foliage that provides visual interest throughout the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its compact size and long blooming period make it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.
