Ixora guillenii

Ixora guillenii, commonly known as the Guillen's ixora, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange-red inflorescences, which are clustered in panicles and bloom throughout the year. The plant has simple, opposite, and ovate leaves that are usually dark green in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Ixora guillenii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a hedge plant.
