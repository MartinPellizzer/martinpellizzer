Indigofera deqinensis

Indigofera deqinensis, commonly known as the Deqin indigo plant, is a flowering plant native to China, specifically the Yunnan province.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and clusters of small, pea-like flowers. The plant is adapted to high-altitude environments and thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil.

Its botanical significance lies in its potential for medicinal use and its role in traditional herbal practices.
