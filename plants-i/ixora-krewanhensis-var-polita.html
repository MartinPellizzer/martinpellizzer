Ixora krewanhensis var. polita

Ixora krewanhensis var. polita, commonly known as the white ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth, glossy surface. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
