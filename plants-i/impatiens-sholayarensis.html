Impatiens sholayarensis

Impatiens sholayarensis, commonly known as the Sholay Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers and heart-shaped leaves, which are typical of the Impatiens genus. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its flowers are bilaterally symmetrical, with five petals, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in its native habitat.
