Iodes velutina

Iodes velutina, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and striking, fragrant flowers that are typically purple or violet in color. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical rainforests. Its flowers bloom in clusters and are pollinated by specific insect species.

Iodes velutina is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
