Impatiens bolovenensis

Impatiens bolovenensis, commonly known as the Boloven Impatiens, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understorey of dense forests.

Due to its unique beauty and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists alike.
