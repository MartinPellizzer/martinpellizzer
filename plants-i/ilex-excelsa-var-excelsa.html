Ilex excelsa var. excelsa

Ilex excelsa var. excelsa, commonly known as the giant holly, is a large evergreen shrub or small tree native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically leathery and arranged alternately along the stems. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for various bird species. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
