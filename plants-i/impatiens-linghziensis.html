Impatiens linghziensis

Impatiens linghziensis, commonly known as the Yunnan impatiens, is a species native to China, specifically found in the Yunnan province.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its erect growth habit and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The flowers are typically bright red or pink, with five petals and a prominent spur, which is a distinguishing feature of the genus Impatiens. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been studied for its potential in horticulture and conservation efforts.
