Iris yedisuensis

Iris yedisuensis, commonly known as the Yedisu iris, is a species native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a dense basal rosette.

The flowers bloom in early summer, adding a vibrant touch to its natural habitat.
