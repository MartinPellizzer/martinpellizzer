Impatiens barnesii

Impatiens barnesii, commonly known as the "barnesii impatiens," is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually hairy and covered in fine pubescence. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens barnesii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to add color to garden spaces.
