Impatiens lutzii

It belongs to the family Balsaminaceae and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white. Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is more commonly seen in its natural habitat.
