Iantha pallidiflora

Iantha pallidiflora, commonly known as the pale-flowered bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. The plant produces clusters of bell-shaped, pale blue or white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine, silvery-white down, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Iantha pallidiflora is valued in gardens for its ornamental beauty and drought tolerance.
