Isatis lunaria

Isatis lunaria, commonly known as Virginia rocket, is an annual herbaceous plant native to eastern North America.

It features slender, branching stems that can grow up to 1 meter in height, often with a reddish tinge. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers in early summer, which are followed by distinctive, spherical seed pods. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Isatis lunaria is often found in disturbed soils, along roadsides, and in open woods, and it is known for its rapid growth and adaptability.
