Impatiens beccarii

Impatiens beccarii, commonly known as the beccari's impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, spurred flowers that come in a range of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically grows as an upright herb with slender, hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Impatiens beccarii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
