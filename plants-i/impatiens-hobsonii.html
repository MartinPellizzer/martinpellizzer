Impatiens hobsonii

Impatiens hobsonii, commonly known as the Hobson's impatiens, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and it thrives in moist, shady environments. It is often found in forest understories and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.

Impatiens hobsonii is a herbaceous perennial that produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds.
