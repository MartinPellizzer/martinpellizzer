Impatiens biflora f. biflora

Impatiens biflora f. biflora, commonly known as the twin-flowered impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The species features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and a slightly hairy underside. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

The plant is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
