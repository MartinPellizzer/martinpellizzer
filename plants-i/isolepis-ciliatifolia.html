Isolepis ciliatifolia

Isolepis ciliatifolia, commonly known as the silky flatsedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to wetland habitats in the Americas.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves with fine, silky hairs along the edges, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in shallow water or moist soils and is often found in marshes and along the edges of ponds.

Isolepis ciliatifolia plays a role in stabilizing wetland soils and providing habitat for aquatic insects.
