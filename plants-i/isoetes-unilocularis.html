Isoetes unilocularis

Isoetes unilocularis, commonly known as the single-chamber water fern, is a species of aquatic plant belonging to the genus Isoetes.

It is characterized by its spore-bearing structures called sporangia, which are typically located on specialized leaves called sporophylls. The plant thrives in freshwater environments, such as ponds and lakes, where it anchors itself to the substrate with its root-like structures. Its morphology includes a central axis with leaf-like structures that are often spirally arranged.

Isoetes unilocularis plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by contributing to nutrient cycling and providing habitat for microorganisms.
