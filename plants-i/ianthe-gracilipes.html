Ianthe gracilipes

Ianthe gracilipes, commonly known as the slender beauty orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, elongated leaves and striking, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its growth habit is epiphytic, meaning it often grows on trees or rocks rather than in the ground.

Ianthe gracilipes is valued for its ornamental appeal and is a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
