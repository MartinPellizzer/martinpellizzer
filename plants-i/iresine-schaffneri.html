Iresine schaffneri

Iresine schaffneri, commonly known as the red leaf iron plant, is a strikingly ornamental shrub native to Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, velvety red or purple foliage, which contrasts beautifully with its bright green stems. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for tropical gardens.

Iresine schaffneri is relatively low maintenance and resistant to most pests and diseases.
