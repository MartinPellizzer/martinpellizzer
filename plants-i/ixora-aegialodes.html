Ixora aegialodes

Ixora aegialodes, commonly known as the coastal ixora, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, bright red or orange inflorescences that bloom throughout the year. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Ixora aegialodes is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to withstand salty air.
