Impatiens simbiniensis

Impatiens simbiniensis, commonly known as the Samburu impatiens, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is a member of the Balsaminaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, spurred flowers that come in shades of pink and white. Impatiens simbiniensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
