Ixora valetonii

Ixora valetonii, commonly known as the Malay flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of bright red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Ixora valetonii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
