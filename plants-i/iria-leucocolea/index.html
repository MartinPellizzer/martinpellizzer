Iria Leucocolea

Iria leucocolea is a rare and striking flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America. Known for its delicate white petals and unique growth habits, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. This species is often found in the understory of dense rainforests where it competes for limited light and nutrients. Its beauty has made it a subject of interest for botanists and horticulturists alike. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this fragile species from habitat loss and over-collection.
