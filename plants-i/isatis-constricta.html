Isatis constricta

Isatis constricta, commonly known as Chinese yellow flower, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cabbage and mustard. The plant is characterized by its yellow flowers and pinnately lobed leaves. It is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks.

Isatis constricta is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value and has historical uses in traditional medicine.
