Impatiens butu

Impatiens butu, commonly known as the "Butu Impatiens," is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Balsaminaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically grows as an annual or biennial, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters, with opposite, ovate leaves.

Its flowers are spurred at the base, a feature common among many species in the impatiens genus, and it thrives in moist, shady environments.
