Ixora acuticauda

Ixora acuticauda, commonly known as the Chinese flame bush, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters tall, featuring glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of bright red or orange flowers, which are typically borne in terminal panicles. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Ixora acuticauda is also valued for its ability to tolerate partial shade and its use in traditional medicine.
