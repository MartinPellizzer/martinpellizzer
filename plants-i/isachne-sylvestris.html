Isachne sylvestris

Isachne sylvestris, commonly known as the woodland isachne, is a perennial grass species native to temperate regions of Asia and Europe.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant has slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves with a bluish-green color. Its inflorescence consists of compact, spike-like clusters of small, two-lobed flowers.

Isachne sylvestris plays a role in maintaining soil stability and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
