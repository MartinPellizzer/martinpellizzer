Iris brandzae

Iris brandzae, commonly known as the Brandzae iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers with a distinctive yellow beard. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a basal rosette.

Iris brandzae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
