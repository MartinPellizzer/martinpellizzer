Ilex ferruginea

Ilex ferruginea, commonly known as the rusty ily.

It is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to the southeastern United States. The plant is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves that turn red or orange in the fall. It produces small, red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife.

Ilex ferruginea is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
