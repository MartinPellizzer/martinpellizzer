Indigofera macrantha

Indigofera macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered indigo, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height, characterized by its compound leaves and showy, purple to blue flowers. The plant produces small, legume-like pods that contain seeds, and it is part of the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
