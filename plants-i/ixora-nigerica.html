Ixora nigerica

Ixora nigerica, commonly known as black ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, bright red flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sun.

Ixora nigerica is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a hedge plant.
