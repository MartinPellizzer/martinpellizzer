Ixora aurorea var. major

Ixora aurorea var. major, commonly known as golden flame ixora, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, bright yellow-orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and a slightly glossy surface. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant color make it suitable for both container planting and landscape use.
