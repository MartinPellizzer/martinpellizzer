Isoloba

Isoloba, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that give it a silvery appearance, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments. Isoloba produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in cyathia, a unique structure typical of the Euphorbiaceae family.

Its root system is deep and robust, allowing it to thrive in poor soils and withstand drought conditions.
