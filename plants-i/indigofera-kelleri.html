Indigofera kelleri

Indigofera kelleri, commonly known as the Kashmir indigo, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is a shrub that can grow up to 2 meters tall, with compound leaves and showy purple or blue flowers. The plant produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds. It is adapted to high-altitude environments and thrives in well-drained soils.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological and horticultural studies.
