Illecebrum polygonifolium

Illecebrum polygonifolium, commonly known as the "Chinese nettle," is a herbaceous perennial plant native to China.

It belongs to the Urticaceae family, which includes stinging nettles, and is characterized by its opposite, heart-shaped leaves. The plant has a creeping growth habit with runners that can form dense mats. Its small greenish-white flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom in the summer months.

It is often used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
