Isachne miliacea var. obscura

Isachne miliacea var. obscura, commonly known as the dark millet grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with minute, pale green to brownish flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas or along riverbanks.

Its botanical features make it a common weed in agricultural fields and natural habitats.
