Ixeris dentata var. atropurpurea

Ixeris dentata var. atropurpurea, commonly known as purple sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Asia.

It features dense clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The leaves are typically narrow and slightly toothed, giving the plant a delicate, grass-like appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Its striking purple-tinged stems and foliage add visual interest to garden landscapes.
