Ixia marginata

Ixia marginata, commonly known as the Orange Kaffir lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of orange and yellow, often with a distinctive red or orange rim. The plant produces strap-like, grassy leaves that are evergreen and can grow up to 30 cm in length. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Ixia marginata is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
