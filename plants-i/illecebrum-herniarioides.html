Illecebrum herniarioides

Illecebrum herniarioides, commonly known as the narrowleaf bedstraw, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Rosaceae.

It is a low-growing, herbaceous perennial that typically forms a dense mat of stolons. The plant has small, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its flowers are white or pale pink, arranged in clusters, and it produces small, dry fruits called achenes.

It is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as roadsides and meadows.
