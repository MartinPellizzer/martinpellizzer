Ixia bulbifera

Ixia bulbifera, commonly known as the bulbous ixia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in shades of yellow, orange, and red. Ixia bulbifera typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its unique floral display makes it a valuable addition to both ornamental and horticultural collections.
