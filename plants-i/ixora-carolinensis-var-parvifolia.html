Ixora carolinensis var. parvifolia

Ixora carolinensis var. parvifolia, commonly known as the small-flowered flame bush, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It features dense, dark green foliage and clusters of small, bright orange-red flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for coastal gardens and urban environments.
