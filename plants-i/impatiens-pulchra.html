Impatiens pulchra

Impatiens pulchra, commonly known as the beautiful impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant typically grows as an upright herb with opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are borne in clusters and have five petals, with the lower petal being spurred.

Impatiens pulchra thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
