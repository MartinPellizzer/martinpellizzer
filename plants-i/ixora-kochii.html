Ixora kochii

Ixora kochii, commonly known as the red ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, featuring dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces clusters of bright red, tubular flowers that bloom throughout the year, especially during the warmer months. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a glossy surface, while its stems are woody and often branched.

Ixora kochii is widely cultivated for its ornamental value and is commonly used in landscaping and as a hedge plant.
