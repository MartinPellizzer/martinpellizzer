Inga stenostachya

Inga stenostachya, commonly known as the slender-stamen inga, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, clustered flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in secondary forests and along riverbanks.

Its growth habit and floral structure make it an important component of the understory vegetation in its native habitats.
