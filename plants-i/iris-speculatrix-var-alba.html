Iris speculatrix var. alba

Iris speculatrix var. alba, commonly known as the white flag iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It features upright, slender stems that bear showy, white to pale blue flowers with a distinctive white or pale yellow center. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically a bluish-green color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

The flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, adding a striking contrast to the surrounding foliage.
