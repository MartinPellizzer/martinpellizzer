Impatiens chloroxantha

Impatiens chloroxantha, commonly known as the yellow water impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers and heart-shaped leaves, which are typically glossy and dark green. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found near streams or in humid forests. Its flowers are clustered in racemes and bloom throughout the wet season.

Impatiens chloroxantha is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in containers or garden beds with proper care.
