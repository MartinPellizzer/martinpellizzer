Inga duckei

Inga duckei, commonly known as the yellow inga, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are typically elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in axillary inflorescences. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that split open to release seeds.

Inga duckei is often found in moist, shaded environments within tropical rainforests.
