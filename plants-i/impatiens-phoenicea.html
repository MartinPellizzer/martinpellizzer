Impatiens phoenicea

Impatiens phoenicea, commonly known as the scarlet impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers and heart-shaped leaves, which are typically bright green. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for gardeners in humid climates. Its flowers bloom in clusters and attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The species is also valued for its ornamental beauty and ease of cultivation.
