Ilex taisanensis

Ilex taisanensis, commonly known as the Taisan holly, is a species of flowering plant in the family Aquifoliaceae.

It is native to China, specifically the provinces of Sichuan and Yunnan, where it grows in mountainous regions. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, bright red berries that appear in the winter. Its leaves are leathery, glossy, and have a serrated margin, making it an ornamental plant in horticulture.
