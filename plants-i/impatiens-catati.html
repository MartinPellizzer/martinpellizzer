Impatiens catati

Impatiens catati, commonly known as the catati impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are opposite in arrangement, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Impatiens catati is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
