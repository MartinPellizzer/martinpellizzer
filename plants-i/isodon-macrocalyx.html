Isodon macrocalyx

Isodon macrocalyx, commonly known as the large-flowered isodon, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its showy, blue to purple flowers are arranged in whorled clusters at the ends of the stems.

Isodon macrocalyx is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional Chinese medicine.
