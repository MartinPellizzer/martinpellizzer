Ixeris Chinensis F. Lacerrima

Golden groundsel, scientifically known as Ixeris chinensis f. lacerrima, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to East Asia. It is prized for its vibrant yellow flowers and compact growth habit, making it a popular choice for gardeners and landscapers. This ornamental plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, showcasing its resilience and adaptability. Its unique foliage and prolonged blooming period add visual interest to gardens throughout the growing season. With its low maintenance requirements, it is an excellent addition to both wild and cultivated landscapes.
