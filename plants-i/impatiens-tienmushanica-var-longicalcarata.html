Impatiens tienmushanica var. longicalcarata

Impatiens tienmushanica var. longicalcarata, commonly known as the long spur impatiens, is a species native to China.

It is characterized by its striking, spurred flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in mountainous regions. Its leaves are ovate with a smooth margin and a slightly hairy surface.

The flowers are primarily pollinated by insects, and the plant is valued for its ornamental qualities in horticulture.
