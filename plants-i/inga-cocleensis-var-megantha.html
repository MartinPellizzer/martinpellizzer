Inga cocleensis var. megantha

Inga cocleensis var. megantha, commonly known as the large-leaf inga, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. Its fruit is a pod containing several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.

This species is often found in moist, shaded habitats within tropical rainforests.
