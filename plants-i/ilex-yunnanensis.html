Ilex yunnanensis

Ilex yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Aquifoliaceae and is characterized by its glossy, spiny leaves and small, red berries. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and thrives in cool, humid climates. It is often found in forest understories and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Ilex yunnanensis is also used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
