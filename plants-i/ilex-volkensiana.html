Ilex volkensiana

Ilex volkensiana, commonly known as the Volkensia holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically oval in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries, which are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. Ilex volkensiana thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, well-drained soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural use in gardens and landscapes.
