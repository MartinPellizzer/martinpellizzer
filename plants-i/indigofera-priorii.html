Indigofera priorii

Indigofera priorii, commonly known as the priorii indigo plant, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and clusters of small, purple to pinkish flowers. It produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds.

Indigofera priorii is often grown for its ornamental value and has potential uses in traditional medicine and soil enrichment due to its nitrogen-fixing ability.
