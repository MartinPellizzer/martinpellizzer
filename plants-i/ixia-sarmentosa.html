Ixia sarmentosa

Ixia sarmentosa, commonly known as the trailing ixia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that come in shades of yellow, orange, and red. Ixia sarmentosa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
