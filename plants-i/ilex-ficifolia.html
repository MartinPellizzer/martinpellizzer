Ilex ficifolia

Ilex ficifolia, commonly known as the Chinese holly, is a deciduous evergreen shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

Ilex ficifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
