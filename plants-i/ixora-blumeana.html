Ixora blumeana

Ixora blumeana, commonly known as the flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, clustered red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a slightly waxy texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Ixora blumeana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a hedge plant.
