Indocalamus chebalingensis

Indocalamus chebalingensis, commonly known as the Chebalingensis bamboo, is a species of grass in the family Poaceae.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Yunnan and Sichuan provinces. This bamboo is characterized by its slender, culm-like stems and can grow up to several meters in height. The plant has a clumping growth habit and produces small, inconspicuous flowers.

It is often valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in traditional Chinese horticulture.
