Ilex crenata var. variegata

Ilex crenata var. variegata, commonly known as Japanese holly or variegated holly, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its dense, glossy dark green foliage with distinctive white or yellow variegation. The plant produces small, red berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for hedges and container gardening.
