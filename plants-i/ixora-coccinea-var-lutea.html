Ixora coccinea var. lutea

Ixora coccinea var. lutea, commonly known as the yellow flame or yellow ixora, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers, which bloom in dense clusters along the stems. The plant has simple, opposite, and ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Ixora coccinea var. lutea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
