Ilex mitis var. kilimandscharica

Ilex mitis var. kilimandscharica, commonly known as the Kilimanjaro holly, is a shrub or small tree native to the high-altitude regions of East Africa.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage, with dark green leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by bright red berries that are an important food source for local wildlife. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
