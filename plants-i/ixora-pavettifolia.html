Ixora pavettifolia

Ixora pavettifolia, commonly known as the purple flame or purple ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant produces clusters of small, brightly colored flowers, usually in shades of red, orange, or yellow, which bloom throughout the year. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a glossy green appearance.

Ixora pavettifolia is valued for its ornamental value and is often used in landscaping and garden design.
