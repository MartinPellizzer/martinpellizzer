Ixonanthes reticulata

Ixonanthes reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined orchid, is a flowering plant species belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers with intricate net-like venation patterns on its petals. It typically grows as an epiphytic or lithophytic plant, attaching itself to trees or rocks.
