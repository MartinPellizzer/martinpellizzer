Indigofera meghalayensis

Indigofera meghalayensis, commonly known as the Shillong indigo plant, is a flowering plant species native to the northeastern regions of India.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-bearing fruits. The plant produces striking blue to violet flowers, which are often clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is typically found in forested areas.
