Impatiens semlikiensis

Impatiens semlikiensis, commonly known as the Semliki impatiens, is a striking flowering plant native to the rainforests of East Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often covered in a fine, fuzzy texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing near streams or in dense undergrowth.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
