Isolona

Isolona, commonly known as the desert lily, is a genus of flowering plants native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa and the Middle East.

These plants are characterized by their succulent leaves and showy, often fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of yellow, white, and pink. Isolona species are typically perennial, with some varieties able to survive extreme drought conditions due to their thick, fleshy stems and roots.
