Illicium reticulatum

It belongs to the family Schisandraceae and is characterized by its aromatic, star-shaped fruit that resembles the spice star anise. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.
