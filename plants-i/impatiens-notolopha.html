Impatiens notolopha

Impatiens notolopha, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and purple. The plant has heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are typically dark green in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Impatiens notolopha is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
