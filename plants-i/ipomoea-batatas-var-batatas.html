Ipomoea batatas var. batatas

Ipomoea batatas var. batatas, commonly known as sweet potato, is a tropical vine belonging to the Convolvulaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or purple. The plant produces tuberous roots that are rich in carbohydrates, fiber, and various nutrients.

These roots come in a variety of colors, including orange, purple, and white, depending on the cultivar. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
