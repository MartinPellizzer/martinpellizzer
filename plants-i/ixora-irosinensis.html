Ixora irosinensis

Ixora irosinensis, commonly known as the flame flower or red flame bush, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit and clusters of bright red, orange, or yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant produces small, oval-shaped fruits that contain several seeds. Ixora irosinensis thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

It is also valued for its ability to attract birds and butterflies to the garden.
