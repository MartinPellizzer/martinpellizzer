Impatiens congolensis var. longicalcarata

Impatiens congolensis var. longicalcarata, commonly known as the long-spurred impatiens, is a species native to the rainforests of Central Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and grows as an erect herbaceous perennial. Its flowers have elongated, tubular corollas that are adapted for pollination by specific insect species.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
