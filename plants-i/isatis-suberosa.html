Isatis suberosa

Isatis suberosa, commonly known as the woolly cabbage or woolly mustard, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cabbage and mustard. The plant is characterized by its hairy, glandular stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. Its small, yellow flowers are arranged in racemes and bloom in the spring.

Isatis suberosa is often found in rocky or disturbed soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
