Illecebrum denticulatum

Illecebrum denticulatum, commonly known as the creeping morning glory, is a flowering plant belonging to the Convolvulaceae family.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in moist, sunny environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.
