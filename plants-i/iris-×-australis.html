Iris × australis

Iris × australis, commonly known as the Australian hybrid iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Australia.

It features grass-like basal leaves and showy, violet-blue to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with tall, slender stems that support its striking flowers. Its botanical characteristics include a rhizomatous root system and showy bracts that enhance its ornamental appeal.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
