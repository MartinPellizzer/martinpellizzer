Impatiens aliciae

Impatiens aliciae, commonly known as the Alice's Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Balsaminaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal.

Impatiens aliciae is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
