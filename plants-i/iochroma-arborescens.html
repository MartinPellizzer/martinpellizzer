Iochroma arborescens

Iochroma arborescens, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its woody stems and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant has palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often used in ornamental gardening.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, contributing to its propagation.
