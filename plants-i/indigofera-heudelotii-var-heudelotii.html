Indigofera heudelotii var. heudelotii

Indigofera heudelotii var. heudelotii, commonly known as African indigo, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to violet. The plant produces pods containing seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to warm, humid climates.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ecological and medicinal purposes.
