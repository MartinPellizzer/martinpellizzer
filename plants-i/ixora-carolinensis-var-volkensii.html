Ixora carolinensis var. volkensii

Ixora carolinensis var. volkensii, commonly known as the red flame ixora, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, fragrant clusters of bright red flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant blooms make it suitable for both container gardening and landscape use.
