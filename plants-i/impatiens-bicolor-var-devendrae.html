Impatiens bicolor var. devendrae

Impatiens bicolor var. devendrae, commonly known as the Devendra's impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking bi-color flowers, typically featuring a combination of white and pink hues. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and prefers well-drained soil. Its foliage is usually dark green with a glossy texture, adding to its ornamental appeal.

This variant is valued in horticulture for its vibrant blooms and adaptability to cultivated settings.
