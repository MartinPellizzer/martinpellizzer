Iris kobayashii

Iris kobayashii, commonly known as the Japanese iris, is a striking ornamental plant native to Japan.

It features tall, slender stems that bear showy, lavender-blue to purple flowers with a distinctive dark central stripe. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

Iris kobayashii is valued for its elegant appearance and is often cultivated in gardens and water features.
