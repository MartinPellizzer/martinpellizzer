Iris hookeri

Iris hookeri, commonly known as the Hooker's iris, is a species of flowering plant in the Iridaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of South America, particularly in Argentina and Chile. The plant features tall, slender stems with striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are sword-shaped and grass-like, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Iris hookeri is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and hardiness in temperate climates.
