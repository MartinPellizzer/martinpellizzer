Impatiens tirunelvelica

Impatiens tirunelvelica, commonly known as the Nilgiri Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is characterized by its vibrant pink to red flowers and elongated, heart-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky slopes. It belongs to the family Balsaminaceae and is known for its rapid growth and short blooming period.
