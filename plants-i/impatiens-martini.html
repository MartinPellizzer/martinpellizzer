Impatiens martini

Impatiens martini, commonly known as the red impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers, which bloom in clusters and attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Impatiens martini is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ease of care.
