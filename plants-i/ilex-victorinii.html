Ilex victorinii

Ilex victorinii, commonly known as the Victorin's holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, spiny leaves. The plant produces small, white flowers that are followed by bright red berries, which are an important food source for birds. Ilex victorinii thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitat.
