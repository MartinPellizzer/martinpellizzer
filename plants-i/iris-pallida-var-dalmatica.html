Iris pallida var. dalmatica

Iris pallida var. dalmatica, commonly known as the Dalmatian flag iris, is a striking perennial herb native to the coastal regions of the Adriatic Sea.

This variety is characterized by its pale blue to white flowers that bloom in early summer, often with a subtle violet or greenish hue. The plant features grass-like, sword-shaped leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

The Dalmatian flag iris is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and wetland restoration projects.
