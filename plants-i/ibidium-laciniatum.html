Ibidium laciniatum

Ibidium laciniatum, commonly known as the laceleaf ibidium, is a rare flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, intricately lobed leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and early summer. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
