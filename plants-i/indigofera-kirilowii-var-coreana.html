Indigofera kirilowii var. coreana

Indigofera kirilowii var. coreana, commonly known as Korean indigo.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is native to Korea. The plant is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. It produces pods that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native habitats.
