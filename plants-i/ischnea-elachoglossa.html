Ischnea elachoglossa

Ischnea elachoglossa, commonly known as the slender leaf ischnea, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features small, lance-shaped leaves that are usually less than 2 inches in length. Its flowers are pale green to white and are clustered in dense, terminal inflorescences.

Ischnea elachoglossa is characterized by its delicate appearance and is often found in rich, organic soils.
