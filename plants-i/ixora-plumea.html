Ixora plumea

Ixora plumea, commonly known as the plume ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, bright red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually ovate to elliptical in shape. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sunlight.

Ixora plumea is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant floral display.
