Ilex theezans var. fertilis

Ilex theezans var. fertilis, commonly known as the Chinese holly, is a variant of the evergreen holly species native to China.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically oval in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for ornamental landscaping.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often used in garden settings for its aesthetic value and ecological benefits.
