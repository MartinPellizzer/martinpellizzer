Ilex tavoyensis

Ilex tavoyensis, commonly known as the Tavoy holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Aquifoliaceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically opposite in arrangement. Ilex tavoyensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its dense foliage and year-round appearance.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it adaptable to a variety of garden settings.
