Icicopsis reticulata

Icicopsis reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined orchid, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers with intricate net-like venation patterns on its petals. Native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in the wild.

Its unique floral structure and ornamental value make it a sought-after species in horticultural collections.
