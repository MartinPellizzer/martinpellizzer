Inula glomerata

Inula glomerata, commonly known as the clustered Inula, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features yellow flowers that are typically grouped in dense clusters, giving it its common name. It has hairy, branched stems and lance-shaped leaves.

Inula glomerata is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils.
