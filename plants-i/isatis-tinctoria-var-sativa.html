Isatis tinctoria var. sativa

Isatis tinctoria var. sativa, commonly known as Chinese kale or broccoli raab, is a biennial plant native to Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family and is characterized by its thick, edible leaves that are often used in cooking. The plant produces yellow flowers that attract pollinators, and its seeds are small and round. It is cultivated for its nutritional value, containing high levels of vitamins and minerals.

The plant prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a versatile crop in temperate climates.
