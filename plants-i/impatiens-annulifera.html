Impatiens annulifera

Impatiens annulifera, commonly known as the ringed impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique floral structure and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
