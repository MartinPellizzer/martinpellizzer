Ischnosiphon morlae var. leiostachya

Ischnosiphon morlae var. leiostachya, commonly known as the slender spikegrass, is a variant of the Ischnosiphon morlae species, characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance.

It is a perennial plant native to specific regions of South America, particularly in Brazil. The plant features elongated, narrow leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetland or marshy habitats.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and ecological role within its native environment.
