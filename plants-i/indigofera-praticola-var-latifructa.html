Indigofera praticola var. latifructa

Indigofera praticola var. latifructa, commonly known as the broad-fruited indigofera, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces elongated, cylindrical pods that contain several seeds, which are typically dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and savannas.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and potential agricultural applications.
