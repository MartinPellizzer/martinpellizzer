Impatiens purpureoviolacea var. longicalcarata

Impatiens purpureoviolacea var. longicalcarata, commonly known as the long-spurred impatiens, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple and violet flowers, which are often adorned with distinctive, elongated spurs. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, with slender, branching stems. Its leaves are heart-shaped and glossy, providing a lush green backdrop to its showy blooms.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
