Impatiens leedalii

Impatiens leedalii, commonly known as the Leedal's Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens leedalii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
