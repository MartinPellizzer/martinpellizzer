Ixia splendida

Ixia splendida, commonly known as the golden torch lily, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Amaryllis family.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of vibrant orange-yellow flowers that resemble torches. The plant typically blooms in late winter to early spring, adding a burst of color to gardens during the cooler months. Ixia splendida thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is further enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and ease of cultivation.
