Impatiens mamyi

Impatiens mamyi, commonly known as the Mamyi Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white, often with a distinctive spurred petal. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, with glossy, dark green leaves. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in the understory of dense forest canopies.

Impatiens mamyi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with appropriate humidity and light conditions.
