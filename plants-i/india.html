India

India, commonly known as the "Land of the Rising Sun," is a country with a rich botanical diversity.

It is home to over 45,000 species of plants, including a wide variety of medicinal herbs and spices. The subcontinent's varied climates and ecosystems support a wide range of flora, from tropical rainforests to arid deserts. Notable plants include the neem tree, turmeric, and the sacred banyan.

India's botanical heritage plays a significant role in traditional medicine and agriculture.
