Impatiens multiflora

Impatiens multiflora, commonly known as the Chinese impatiens, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and produces clusters of showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Impatiens multiflora is often used in container gardening and as a ground cover due to its vigorous growth and ornamental value.
