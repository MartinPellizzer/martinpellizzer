Iris henryi

Iris henryi, commonly known as Henry's iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight. Its foliage consists of sword-shaped leaves that are usually dark green in color.

Iris henryi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
