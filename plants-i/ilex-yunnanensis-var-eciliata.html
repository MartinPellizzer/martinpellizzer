Ilex yunnanensis var. eciliata

Ilex yunnanensis var. eciliata, commonly known as the Yunnan holly, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves that turn red in the fall. The plant produces small, white flowers in the spring, followed by red berries that are an important food source for birds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in temperate climates.
