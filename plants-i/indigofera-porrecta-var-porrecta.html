Indigofera porrecta var. porrecta

Indigofera porrecta var. porrecta, commonly known as the slender indigo, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces pods that contain small, round seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in various soil types.
