Isoglossa longiflora var. minor

Isoglossa longiflora var. minor, commonly known as the small-flowered isoglossa, is a variant of the isoglossa longiflora species.

It is a herbaceous plant native to specific regions of southern Africa. The plant features slender, upright stems with small, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences and are characterized by their elongated, tubular shape.

This variant is noted for its ecological significance in supporting local pollinators and contributing to biodiversity in its native habitat.
