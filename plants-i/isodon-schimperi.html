Isodon schimperi

Isodon schimperi, commonly known as the Ethiopian lemongrass, is a perennial herb native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its fragrant, lemon-scented leaves are often used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.

Isodon schimperi thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in shaded, moist environments.
