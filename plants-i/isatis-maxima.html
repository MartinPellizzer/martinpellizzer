Isatis maxima

Isatis maxima, commonly known as Chinese yellow mustard, is a biennial plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other mustard species. It produces long, slender pods containing seeds that are used in traditional medicine and as a spice.

Isatis maxima is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
