Indigofera charlieriana var. charlieriana

Indigofera charlieriana var. charlieriana, commonly known as the rock indigo, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its aromatic, trifoliate leaves and clusters of small, pale blue to purple flowers. The plant is adapted to rocky and arid environments, making it a hardy species suitable for xeriscaping. It produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it beneficial for soil enrichment in degraded landscapes.
