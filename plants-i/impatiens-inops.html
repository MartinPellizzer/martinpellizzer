Impatiens inops

Impatiens inops, commonly known as the ghost impatiens, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a ghostly appearance. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically green with a slight sheen. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in the undergrowth of forests.

Impatiens inops is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
