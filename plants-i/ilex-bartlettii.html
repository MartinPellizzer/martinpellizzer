Ilex bartlettii

Ilex bartlettii, commonly known as the Bartlett holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and small, dark green leaves that remain throughout the year. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions and full to partial sunlight.

Ilex bartlettii is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
