Impatiens geniculata

Impatiens geniculata, commonly known as the zigzag impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzagging stems, which give it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens geniculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
