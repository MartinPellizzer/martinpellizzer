Inula × rigida

Inula × rigida, commonly known as the rigid inula, is a hybrid plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its tall, rigid stems and yellow composite flowers that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy texture.

Inula × rigida is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
