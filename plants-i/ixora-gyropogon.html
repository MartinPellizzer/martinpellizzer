Ixora gyropogon

Ixora gyropogon, commonly known as the flame bush, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, clustered red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Ixora gyropogon is also valued for its ability to attract birds and butterflies to gardens.
