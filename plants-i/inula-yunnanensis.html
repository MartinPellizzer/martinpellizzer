Inula yunnanensis

Inula yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan Inula, is a flowering plant native to China, particularly in the Yunnan province.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and has lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. Its yellow flowers bloom in the summer, and the plant is often used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.

Inula yunnanensis is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
