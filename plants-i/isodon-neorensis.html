Isodon neorensis

Isodon neorensis, commonly known as the Chinese skullcap, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges and produces small, tubular flowers in shades of blue or purple. It is often cultivated for its medicinal properties, particularly for its potential cognitive and neuroprotective benefits.

Isodon neorensis is also valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported ability to enhance memory and treat neurological disorders.
