Inga Fagifolia Var. Marginata

Inga fagifolia var. marginata is a striking tropical plant known for its lush green foliage and unique leaf patterns. Native to the rainforests of Central and South America, it thrives in warm, humid climates with abundant sunlight. Its delicate, fern-like leaves are often edged with a contrasting color, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. This plant is also valued for its ability to attract beneficial insects and birds to the garden. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to soil and watering conditions to flourish.
