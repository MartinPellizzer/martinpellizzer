Ixora hiernii

Ixora hiernii, commonly known as the red hibiscus or Hiern's ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters tall, characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of bright red or orange flowers, which are tubular and attract birds and insects. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in forest understories or along stream banks.

Ixora hiernii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
