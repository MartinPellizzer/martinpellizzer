Iticania loniceroides

Iticania loniceroides, commonly known as the creeping honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of white or yellow. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a slightly serrated edge.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in woodland settings.
