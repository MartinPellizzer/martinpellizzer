Ixeris siamensis

Ixeris siamensis, commonly known as the Siam carpetweed, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features a mat-forming growth habit with dense clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has slender, prostrate stems covered in fine, soft hairs. Its leaves are linear to lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy surface and a distinct midrib.

Ixeris siamensis is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks.
