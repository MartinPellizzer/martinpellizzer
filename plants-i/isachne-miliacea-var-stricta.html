Isachne miliacea var. stricta

Isachne miliacea var. stricta, commonly known as the narrowleaf isachne, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in temperate climates. The plant has slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves that are often slightly hairy. Its inflorescence consists of dense, spike-like clusters of small, two-lipped flowers.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
