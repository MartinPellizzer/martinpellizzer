Isertia parviflora var. hirta

Isertia parviflora var. hirta, commonly known as the hairy small-flowered isertia, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive textured appearance. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers clustered in dense, terminal spikes. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often with a slightly hairy surface.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is typically found in grassy or rocky habitats.
