Ixora praestans

Ixora praestans, commonly known as the flame flower or red ixora, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or orange-red flowers that bloom in dense clusters, creating a striking visual display. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a slightly waxy texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Ixora praestans is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its showy blooms and adaptability to various garden settings.
