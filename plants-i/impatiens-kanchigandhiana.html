Impatiens kanchigandhiana

Impatiens kanchigandhiana, commonly known as the Kanchi Himalayan Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to pink, often with a contrasting throat. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forested areas at elevations between 2,000 and 3,500 meters.

Due to its ornamental value and unique habitat, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
