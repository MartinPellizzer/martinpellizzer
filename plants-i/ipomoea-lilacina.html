Ipomoea lilacina

Ipomoea lilacina, commonly known as the purple morning glory, is a flowering vine native to Central America.

It produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pale purple to deep violet. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in warm, sunny conditions. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

Its rapid growth and climbing habit make it a popular choice for trellises and vertical gardening.
