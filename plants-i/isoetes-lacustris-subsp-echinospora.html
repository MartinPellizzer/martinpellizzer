Isoetes lacustris subsp. echinospora

Isoetes lacustris subsp.

echinospora, commonly known as the spiny spikerush, is a species of aquatic plant belonging to the water clover family (Isoetaceae). It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage with spiky, spore-bearing structures called sporophylls.

The plant thrives in freshwater environments, particularly in shallow, nutrient-rich lakes and ponds. Its spores are released into the water, allowing for dispersal and colonization of new habitats.

This subspecies is often used in ecological studies due to its sensitivity to water quality and environmental changes.
