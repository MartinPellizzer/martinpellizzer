Impatiens meeuseiana

Impatiens meeuseiana, commonly known as the Meuse impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Balsaminaceae and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
