Isachne albens var. albens

Isachne albens var. albens, commonly known as silvergrass, is a perennial grass species native to temperate regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-gray leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in suitable climates.
