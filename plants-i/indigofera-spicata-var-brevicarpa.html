Indigofera spicata var. brevicarpa

Indigofera spicata var. brevicarpa, commonly known as the short-fruited indigofera, is a species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae, characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers.
