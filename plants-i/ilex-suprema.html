Ilex suprema

Ilex suprema, commonly known as the California coffee tree, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, red berries that are toxic to humans but serve as a food source for various birds and animals. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

Ilex suprema is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to provide habitat for wildlife.
