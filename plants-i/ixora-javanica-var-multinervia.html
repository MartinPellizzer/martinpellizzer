Ixora javanica var. multinervia

Ixora javanica var. multinervia, commonly known as the red flame ixora, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, bright red inflorescences that bloom throughout the year, making it a popular ornamental plant. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture with a glossy appearance. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its showy flowers and compact growth habit make it suitable for gardens, containers, and landscape designs.
