Indigofera maymyoensis

Indigofera maymyoensis, commonly known as Maymyo indigo, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of Myanmar and Thailand. The plant is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. It is often cultivated for its medicinal properties and has traditional uses in local herbal remedies.

The plant's ability to fix nitrogen makes it valuable for soil enrichment in agricultural settings.
