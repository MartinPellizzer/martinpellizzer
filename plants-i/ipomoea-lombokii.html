Ipomoea lombokii

Ipomoea lombokii, commonly known as the Lombok morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Indonesia.

It is characterized by its striking violet-blue flowers that open in the early morning and close by midday. The plant produces heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a deep green color. Its vines can grow up to several meters in length, climbing or trailing along supports.

Ipomoea lombokii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
