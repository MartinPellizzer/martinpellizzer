Ilex dubia var. hupehensis

Ilex dubia var. hupehensis, commonly known as the Chinese holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Aquifoliaceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with spiny margins. The plant produces small, white flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries that are toxic to humans but serve as a food source for birds. The plant prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
