Iberis stricta

Iberis stricta, commonly known as upright candytuft, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Europe.

It features dense clusters of white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves that are arranged in a compact, mat-like form. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value lies in its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.
