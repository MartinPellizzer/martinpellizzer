Ione racemosa

Ione racemosa, commonly known as the Sierra snowbrush, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. The plant features dense clusters of white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are evergreen, needle-like, and arranged in a dense, brush-like formation.

Ione racemosa thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.
