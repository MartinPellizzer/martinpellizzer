Ixora solomonensium

Ixora solomonensium, commonly known as Solomon's ixora, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the South Pacific, particularly the Solomon Islands.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 3 meters, characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, bright red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and elliptical in shape, with a glossy surface.

Ixora solomonensium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes in tropical climates.
