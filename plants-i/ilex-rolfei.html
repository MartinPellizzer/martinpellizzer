Ilex rolfei

Ilex rolfei, commonly known as the Chinese holly, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are typically elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, white flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries that are ornamental and attract birds. Ilex rolfei is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and landscapes.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun exposure.
