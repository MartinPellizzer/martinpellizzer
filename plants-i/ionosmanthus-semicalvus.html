Ionosmanthus semicalvus

Ionosmanthus semicalvus, commonly known as the Chinese sweet tea plant, is a flowering shrub native to southern China.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant produces small, edible berries that are often used in traditional Chinese medicine. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Ionosmanthus semicalvus is valued for its ornamental appeal and aromatic properties.
