Iberis bouteloui

Iberis bouteloui, commonly known as the plains iris, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant typically grows in open, dry habitats such as prairies and disturbed soils. It produces showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in early spring.

Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette.
