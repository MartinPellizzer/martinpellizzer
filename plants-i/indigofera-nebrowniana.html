Indigofera nebrowniana

Indigofera nebrowniana, commonly known as the African indigo, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces pods that contain seeds, and it is often found in savannas and woodland areas.

It has a shallow root system and is adapted to seasonal rainfall patterns.
