Ilysanthes hyssopioides

Ilysanthes hyssopioides, commonly known as the blue hyssop, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are often used in traditional herbal medicine for their purported soothing properties.

Ilysanthes hyssopioides thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight.
