Isachne edamalayarensis

Isachne edamalayarensis, commonly known as the Edamalayarensis grass, is a species of flowering plant native to Sri Lanka.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, two-lobed florets. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and wetlands.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the local flora and ecosystem.
