Indigofera hendecaphylla var. hendecaphylla

Indigofera hendecaphylla var. hendecaphylla, commonly known as the blue pea or Indian indigo, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

The plant is notable for its vibrant blue and purple flowers, which are often used in traditional medicine and dyeing practices. Its leaves are trifoliate, with each leaflet having a slightly serrated edge.
