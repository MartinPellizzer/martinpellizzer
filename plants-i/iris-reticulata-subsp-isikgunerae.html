Iris reticulata subsp. isikgunerae

Iris reticulata subsp.

isikgunerae, commonly known as the Isikguner's reticulate iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Turkey. It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, showy flowers.

The plant produces small, violet-blue flowers with a distinctive reticulate pattern on the petals. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
