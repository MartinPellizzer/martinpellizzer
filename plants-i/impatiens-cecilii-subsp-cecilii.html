Impatiens cecilii subsp. cecilii

Impatiens cecilii subsp.

cecilii, commonly known as Cecil's Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central America. It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red.

The plant produces small, elliptical leaves with a glossy texture and a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
