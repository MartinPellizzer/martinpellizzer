Iris kolpakowskiana

Iris kolpakowskiana, commonly known as the Kolpakovsky iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Caucasus region.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm, with sword-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, forming a dense spike with 5–10 petals.

The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.
