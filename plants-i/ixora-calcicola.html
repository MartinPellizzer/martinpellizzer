Ixora calcicola

Ixora calcicola, commonly known as the limestone ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant thrives in well-drained, calcareous soils, making it well-suited for rocky or limestone-rich environments. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a waxy texture.

Ixora calcicola is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
