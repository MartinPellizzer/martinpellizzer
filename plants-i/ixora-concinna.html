Ixora concinna

Ixora concinna, commonly known as the flame flower or red ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, branched structure and vibrant red or orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. Ixora concinna thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

It is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
