Isochilus carnosiflorus

Isochilus carnosiflorus, commonly known as the fleshy-flowered isochilus, is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its unique, fleshy, and often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are usually succulent, adapted to store water in arid environments.

Isochilus carnosiflorus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
