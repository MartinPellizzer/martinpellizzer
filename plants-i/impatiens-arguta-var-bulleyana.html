Impatiens arguta var. bulleyana

Impatiens arguta var. bulleyana, commonly known as the Bulleyana Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and ovate, dark green leaves with a slightly serrated edge. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long blooming period.
