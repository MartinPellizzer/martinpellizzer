Impatiens liangshanensis

Impatiens liangshanensis, commonly known as the Liangshan impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Sichuan Province in China.

It belongs to the Balsaminaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, spurred flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and is often found in moist, shaded environments. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and contributes to the biodiversity of its native habitat.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
