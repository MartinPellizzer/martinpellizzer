Illipe bawun

Illipe bawun, commonly known as the Malayan illipe, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Sapotaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and elliptical leaves. The tree produces small, yellowish flowers followed by large, round fruits that contain a rich, creamy pulp.
