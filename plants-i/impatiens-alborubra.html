Impatiens alborubra

Impatiens alborubra, commonly known as the white-flowered impatiens, is a species native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, showy white flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and undergrowth. Its leaves are ovate and have a smooth, glossy surface.

Impatiens alborubra is a herbaceous perennial that thrives in cool climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
