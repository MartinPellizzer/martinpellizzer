Ilex Amelanchier Var. Monticola

Mountain holly, scientifically known as Ilex amelanchier var. monticola, is a hardy evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of North America. It is prized for its dense foliage, which provides shelter for wildlife and a striking visual presence in natural landscapes. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it adaptable to a variety of garden environments. Its small, spiny leaves and vibrant red berries add both ornamental value and ecological significance. Despite its rugged appearance, mountain holly is a resilient plant that can withstand harsh weather conditions.
