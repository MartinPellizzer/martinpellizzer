Isolepis holoschoenus var. australis

Isolepis holoschoenus var. australis, commonly known as the Australian flat sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Australia.

It typically grows in wetland environments such as marshes, swamps, and the margins of water bodies. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikes of pale green to brown flowers. It is often found in areas with high soil moisture and is adapted to tolerate seasonal flooding.

This variety is part of the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants.
