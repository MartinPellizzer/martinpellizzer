Ixora aphylla

Ixora aphylla, commonly known as the smooth ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, smooth, and often glabrous leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, bright orange-red flowers that bloom throughout the year. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Ixora aphylla is also valued for its ability to tolerate poor soil conditions and partial shade.
