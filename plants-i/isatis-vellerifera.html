Isatis vellerifera

Isatis vellerifera, commonly known as the yellow flag iris, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to wetland environments in North America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically bright green. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers in late summer, which are clustered in dense, racemose inflorescences. It thrives in moist soils and is often found in marshes, pond edges, and along streams.

Isatis vellerifera is also known for its ability to tolerate a wide range of water conditions, making it a common sight in wetland ecosystems.
