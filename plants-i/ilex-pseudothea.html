Ilex pseudothea

Ilex pseudothea, commonly known as the Chinese holly, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Aquifoliaceae and is characterized by its spiny leaves and small, bright red berries. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and thrives in temperate climates.
