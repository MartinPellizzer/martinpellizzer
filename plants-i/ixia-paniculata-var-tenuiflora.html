Ixia paniculata var. tenuiflora

Ixia paniculata var. tenuiflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered ixia, is a perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, tubular flowers that are typically orange or yellow in color, with a distinctive, spurred shape. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces its showy blooms in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its botanical characteristics make it an attractive and relatively low-maintenance ornamental plant.
