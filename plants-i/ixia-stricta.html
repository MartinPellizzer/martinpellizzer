Ixia stricta

Ixia stricta, commonly known as the slender oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soil and full sun exposure. Its foliage consists of trifoliate leaves, and it produces showy inflorescences that bloom in spring.

Ixia stricta is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
