Iris × germanica var. amas

Iris × germanica var. amas, commonly known as the Amas iris, is a hybrid variety of the bearded iris that exhibits striking floral characteristics.

It features tall, sturdy stems with large, showy flowers that come in a range of colors, including shades of purple, blue, and white. The plant is known for its robust growth and adaptability to various soil conditions, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes. Its foliage consists of broad, sword-shaped leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in full sun to partial shade.
