Isolepis schimperiana

Isolepis schimperiana, commonly known as the spike-rush, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and spike-like inflorescences that bear small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy environments, showcasing its adaptability to moist conditions. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to form dense stands, often contributing to soil stabilization in wetlands.

Isolepis schimperiana plays a role in providing habitat and food for various aquatic and terrestrial organisms.
