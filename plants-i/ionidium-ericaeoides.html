Ionidium ericaeoides

Ionidium ericaeoides, commonly known as the coastal heath.

It is a low-growing shrub native to the coastal regions of southern Africa. The plant features small, needle-like leaves that help reduce water loss in its arid environment. It produces clusters of white or pale pink flowers during the summer months.

Ionidium ericaeoides is well adapted to sandy soils and salt spray, making it a hardy plant in coastal ecosystems.
