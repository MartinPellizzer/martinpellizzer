Illigera orbiculata

Illigera orbiculata, commonly known as the orbiculate rock heath, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant features small, evergreen leaves and produces bell-shaped, pink or white flowers. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is cultivated in gardens in suitable climates.
