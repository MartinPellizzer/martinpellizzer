Indigofera asterocalycina

Indigofera asterocalycina, commonly known as the star calyx indigo, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, showy flowers with five petals arranged in a star-like pattern. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often grown for its ornamental value due to its vibrant blue and purple flower colors.
