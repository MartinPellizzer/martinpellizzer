Ipomopsis congesta subsp. viridis

Ipomopsis congesta subsp.

viridis, commonly known as the green clustered flower. It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant features showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink to purple in color, arranged in dense clusters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered with fine hairs.

The species is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky soils and open habitats.
