Iris barthii

Iris barthii, commonly known as the Ethiopian iris, is a species native to Ethiopia and parts of Eritrea.

It is a perennial herb with striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has a rhizomatous root system and features narrow, grass-like leaves. It grows in high-altitude grasslands and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils.

Iris barthii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also of cultural significance in its native region.
