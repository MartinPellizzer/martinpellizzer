Isolepis humboldtii

Isolepis humboldtii, commonly known as the Humboldt's bulrush, is a perennial grass-like plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and rushes. The plant typically grows in wetland environments, such as marshes and shallow water areas. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are often wind-pollinated.
