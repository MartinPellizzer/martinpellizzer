Iris duclouxii

Iris duclouxii, commonly known as the Ducloux iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant features upright, sword-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its rhizomatous root system allows it to thrive in well-drained, calcareous soils.

Iris duclouxii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
