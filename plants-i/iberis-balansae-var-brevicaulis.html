Iberis balansae var. brevicaulis

Iberis balansae var. brevicaulis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved candytuft, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It features dense clusters of white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has small, narrow, and slightly hairy leaves that are arranged in a compact rosette. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun exposure.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens and alpine settings.
