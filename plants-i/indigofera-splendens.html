Indigofera splendens

Indigofera splendens, commonly known as the flame bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, making it a visually appealing ornamental plant. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces slender, bean-like pods that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Indigofera splendens is often cultivated for its vibrant floral display and is also used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
