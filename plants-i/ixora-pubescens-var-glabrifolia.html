Ixora pubescens var. glabrifolia

Ixora pubescens var. glabrifolia, commonly known as the smooth-leaved flame flower, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bright red or orange inflorescences, which are clustered and resemble flames. The leaves are typically smooth and glossy, with a lance-shaped form. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
