Ixora schunkeana

Ixora schunkeana, commonly known as the Schunke's flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or orange inflorescences, which are clustered in panicles and bloom throughout the year. The plant has simple, opposite, and lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Ixora schunkeana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
