Ixora vinniei

Ixora vinniei, commonly known as the Chinese flame bush, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of bright red, orange, or yellow flowers that are highly attractive to pollinators. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Ixora vinniei is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a hedge plant.
