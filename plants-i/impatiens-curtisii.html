Impatiens curtisii

Impatiens curtisii, commonly known as Curtis's impatiens, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often glossy and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found growing in forest understories.

Impatiens curtisii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
