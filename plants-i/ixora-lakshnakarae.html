Ixora lakshnakarae

Ixora lakshnakarae, commonly known as the Lakshmanakara Ixora, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant produces clusters of bright red or orange flowers, which are borne on long, pendulous inflorescences. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a glossy surface.

Ixora lakshnakarae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
