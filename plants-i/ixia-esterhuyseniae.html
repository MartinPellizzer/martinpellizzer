Ixia esterhuyseniae

Ixia esterhuyseniae, commonly known as the Esterhuysenia ixia, is a striking flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family, which includes irises and gladiolus. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in shades of orange and yellow. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
