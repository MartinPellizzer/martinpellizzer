Impatiens oreophila

Impatiens oreophila, commonly known as the mountain impatiens, is a species of flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a slightly fuzzy texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens oreophila is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
