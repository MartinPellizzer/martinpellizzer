Iris junzifengensis

Iris junzifengensis, commonly known as the Junzifeng iris, is a rare and endemic species native to China.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue-violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as mountainous regions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its rhizomes are thick and fleshy.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical research and conservation efforts.
