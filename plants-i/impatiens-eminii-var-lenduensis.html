Impatiens eminii var. lenduensis

Impatiens eminii var. lenduensis, commonly known as the Lendu Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of Central Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a slightly waxy texture, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments. It is often found growing in the understory of dense tropical forests.

This variant is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
