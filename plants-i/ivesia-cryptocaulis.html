Ivesia cryptocaulis

Ivesia cryptocaulis, commonly known as the cryptocalyx ivesia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. Ivesia cryptocaulis thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.
