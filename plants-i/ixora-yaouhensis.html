Ixora yaouhensis

Ixora yaouhensis, commonly known as the Yaouhensis flame bush, is a tropical shrub native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its dense, bright red or orange inflorescences that bloom throughout the year. The plant has simple, opposite, and often hairy leaves that are elliptical in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Ixora yaouhensis is valued for its ornamental value and is often used in landscaping in warm climates.
