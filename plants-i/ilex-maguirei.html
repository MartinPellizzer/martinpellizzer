Ilex maguirei

Ilex maguirei, commonly known as Maguire's holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for various wildlife species. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for cultivation in gardens and landscapes.

Ilex maguirei is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native plant habitats.
