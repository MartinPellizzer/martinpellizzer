Impatiens celatiflora

Impatiens celatiflora, commonly known as the broad-flowered impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and sometimes red. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds, aiding in its propagation.

Impatiens celatiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
