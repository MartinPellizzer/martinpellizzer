Isachne clarkei

Isachne clarkei, commonly known as the Clarke's isachne, is a grass species native to parts of Africa, particularly South Africa.

It is a perennial plant that typically grows in grassland and rocky habitats. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is characterized by its ability to thrive in arid and semi-arid environments.
