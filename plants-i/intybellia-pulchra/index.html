Intybellia Pulchra

Intybellia pulchra is a rare and striking plant known for its vibrant, bell-shaped flowers. Native to the high altitudes of the Andes, it thrives in cool, moist environments. This species is highly sought after by botanists and horticulturists for its unique appearance and ecological significance. Its delicate structure and vivid colors make it a standout in botanical collections. However, due to habitat loss, it faces increasing threats in the wild.
