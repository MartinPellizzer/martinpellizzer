Ilex vitiensis

Ilex vitiensis, commonly known as the Vitiensis holly, is a species of flowering plant in the family Aquifoliaceae.

It is native to the island of Vanuatu and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces bright red berries that are an important food source for local wildlife. Ilex vitiensis typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional medicine by the indigenous peoples of Vanuatu.
