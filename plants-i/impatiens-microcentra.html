Impatiens microcentra

Impatiens microcentra, commonly known as the dwarf impatiens, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant with slender, upright stems and small, clustered leaves. The flowers are typically pink or white, with five petals and a distinctive spur at the base. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its delicate appearance and unique floral structure, it is a subject of interest in botanical studies.
