Ipomopsis aggregata subsp. attenuata

Ipomopsis aggregata subsp.

attenuata, commonly known as the slender ipomopsis, is a flowering plant native to the western United States. It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems and showy, tubular flowers.

The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, wind-dispersed seeds.
