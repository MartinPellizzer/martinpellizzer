Ixora baileyana

Ixora baileyana, commonly known as the Chinese hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Ixora baileyana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
