Isodon lophanthoides var. graciliflorus

Isodon lophanthoides var. graciliflorus, commonly known as the slender-flowered isodon, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of East Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically purple or blue in color, arranged in whorled clusters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally used in traditional herbal medicine.
