Ilex acutangula

Ilex acutangula, commonly known as the Chinese holly, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It features small, glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter green underneath. The plant produces small, bright red berries that are toxic to humans and animals. It thrives in temperate climates and is often used in ornamental landscaping.

Ilex acutangula is characterized by its dense, evergreen-like foliage and its ability to tolerate a variety of soil conditions.
