Iris pulcherrima

Iris pulcherrima, commonly known as the Flame Iris, is a striking perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features tall, upright stems that bear showy, vividly colored flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has sword-shaped, dark green leaves that are lanceolate in shape. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is heightened by its long blooming period, typically from late spring to early summer.
