Iberis timeroyi

Iberis timeroyi, commonly known as the Armenian rockcress, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Armenia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and is adapted to harsh, alpine environments. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, and its leaves are usually narrow and hairy.
