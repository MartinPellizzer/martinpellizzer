Impatiens leptocaulon

Impatiens leptocaulon, commonly known as the slender-stemmed impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Balsaminaceae and is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white. Impatiens leptocaulon is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
