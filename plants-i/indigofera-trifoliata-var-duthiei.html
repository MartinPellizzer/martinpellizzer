Indigofera trifoliata var. duthiei

Indigofera trifoliata var. duthiei, commonly known as the Indian blue bean, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which consist of three leaflets arranged in a cluster. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale blue, often arranged in racemes. Its seed pods are elongated and contain several kidney-shaped seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional uses in herbal medicine.
