Impatiens walkeri

Impatiens walkeri, commonly known as the red impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Balsaminaceae and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

Impatiens walkeri is also known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through self-seeding.
