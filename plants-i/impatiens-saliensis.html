Impatiens saliensis

Impatiens saliensis, commonly known as the bouncing impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows as an erect herb with opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its rapid growth and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
