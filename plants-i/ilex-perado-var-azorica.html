Ilex perado var. azorica

Ilex perado var. azorica, commonly known as the Azorean holly, is a shrub or small tree native to the Azores.

It features small, leathery leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. It is well adapted to the humid, temperate climate of its native region.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens in similar climates.
