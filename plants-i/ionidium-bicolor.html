Ionidium bicolor

Ionidium bicolor, commonly known as the two-colored orchid, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, bi-colored petals that typically display a combination of white and purple or pink hues. Native to tropical regions, this plant thrives in humid environments with well-drained soil. Its flowers are often fragrant and attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Ionidium bicolor is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections worldwide.
