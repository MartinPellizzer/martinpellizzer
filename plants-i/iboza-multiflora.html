Iboza multiflora

Iboza multiflora, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other similar plants. The plant features showy, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center, blooming throughout the warmer months. It typically grows in grasslands and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils.

Iboza multiflora is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
