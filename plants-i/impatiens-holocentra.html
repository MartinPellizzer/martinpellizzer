Impatiens holocentra

Impatiens holocentra, commonly known as the red-flowered impatiens, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or pink flowers, which bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant typically grows as an upright herb, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are ovate and have a glossy surface, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Impatiens holocentra thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.
