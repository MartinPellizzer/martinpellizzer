Iranecio hypochionaeus

Iranecio hypochionaeus, commonly known as the Persian bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Iran.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in temperate climates. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant blooms during the summer months.
