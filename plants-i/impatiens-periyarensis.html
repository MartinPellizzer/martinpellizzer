Impatiens periyarensis

Impatiens periyarensis, commonly known as the Periyar impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. Its leaves are ovate and have a smooth, glossy surface.

Impatiens periyarensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
