Impatiens peperomioides

Impatiens peperomioides, commonly known as the silver leaf impatiens, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that often have a silvery or white variegation, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular in gardens and as a houseplant.
