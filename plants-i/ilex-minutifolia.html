Ilex minutifolia

Ilex minutifolia, commonly known as the dwarf holly, is a small evergreen shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are small and oval in shape, with spines on the underside. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Its dense growth habit provides excellent erosion control and habitat for various plant and animal species.
