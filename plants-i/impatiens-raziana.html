Impatiens raziana

Impatiens raziana, commonly known as the Madagascar impatiens, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a range of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Impatiens raziana is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
