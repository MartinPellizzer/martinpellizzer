Isatis floribunda var. rhynchocarpa

Isatis floribunda var. rhynchocarpa, commonly known as the Chinese yellow mustard, is a member of the Brassicaceae family.

It is a herbaceous plant that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes, and its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly serrated edge. The fruit is a short, cylindrical silique that contains several seeds.

It is native to China and is cultivated for its oil-rich seeds and medicinal properties.
