Iris laevigata var. hortensis

Iris laevigata var. hortensis, commonly known as the Japanese iris, is a herbaceous perennial native to Japan.

It features showy, upright spikes of lavender-blue to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, sword-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. It prefers moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and its ability to thrive in humid climates.
