Impatiens hochstetteri

Impatiens hochstetteri, commonly known as the Hochstetter's impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens hochstetteri is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
