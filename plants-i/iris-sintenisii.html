Iris sintenisii

Iris sintenisii, commonly known as the Sintenis iris, is a species of flowering plant in the genus Iris.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Afghanistan and Pakistan. This perennial herb is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters and has sword-shaped leaves that are dark green in color.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine environments.
