Ida dunstervillei

Ida dunstervillei, commonly known as the Dunsterville's daisy, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of numerous small florets. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and thrives in full sun, displaying vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Ida dunstervillei is known for its compact growth habit and is often cultivated in rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after species among succulent and native plant enthusiasts.
