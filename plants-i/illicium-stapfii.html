Illicium stapfii

Illicium stapfii, commonly known as the Stapf's star anise, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Schisandraceae and is characterized by its aromatic, star-shaped fruits. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by dark brown, capsule-like fruits. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and medicinal properties.
