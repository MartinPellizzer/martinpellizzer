Ilex cyrtura

Ilex cyrtura, commonly known as the narrow-leaved holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, narrow, leathery leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stems. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for various bird species. Ilex cyrtura thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural use in tropical gardens.
