Ixora reinwardtii

Ixora reinwardtii, commonly known as the flame bush, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of bright red or orange-red flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has simple, opposite, and often hairy leaves that are dark green in color. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Ixora reinwardtii is also valued for its ability to tolerate poor soil conditions and partial shade.
