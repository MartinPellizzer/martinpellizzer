Ifloga glomerata

Ifloga glomerata, commonly known as the clustered spurge, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30-60 cm. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.
