Ilex cowanii

Ilex cowanii, commonly known as the Cowan holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its dense, glossy dark green foliage and small, white to pale yellow flowers that appear in the spring. The plant produces bright red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. Ilex cowanii thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Its hardy nature and attractive appearance make it a valuable plant in both natural and cultivated settings.
