Ixora balinensis

Ixora balinensis, commonly known as the Balinese flame or flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a striking appearance. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Ixora balinensis is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and landscapes.
