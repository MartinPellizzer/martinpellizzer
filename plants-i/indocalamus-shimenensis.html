Indocalamus shimenensis

Indocalamus shimenensis, commonly known as the Shimen bamboo, is a species of grass belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to China, particularly found in the mountainous regions of Sichuan and Yunnan provinces. This bamboo is characterized by its slender, hollow stems and dense, feather-like leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mixed forests.

The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been studied for its ecological role in maintaining soil stability.
