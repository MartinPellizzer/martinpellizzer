Isabelia

Isabelia, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in large, showy clusters. The plant produces long, narrow leaves and has a thick, woody trunk that can grow quite tall. Isabelia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and public spaces.

It prefers full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice in warm climates.
