Impatiens tricarinata

Impatiens tricarinata, commonly known as the three-ribbed impatiens, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to pink. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. Impatiens tricarinata thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its botanical structure supports its role as an ornamental plant in horticulture.
