Impatiens purpureolucida

Impatiens purpureolucida, commonly known as the purple-lucide impatiens, is a species native to the tropical rainforests of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking purple and pink flowers, which bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often tinged with red or purple hues. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining, moist soil.

Impatiens purpureolucida is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to add vibrant color to shaded garden spaces.
