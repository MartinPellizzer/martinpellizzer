Iris × croatica

Iris × croatica, commonly known as the Croatian iris, is a hybrid perennial plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers with a distinctive white or pale yellow center. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its foliage consists of sword-shaped leaves that are often a deep green color.

Iris × croatica is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and adaptability to a variety of garden conditions.
