Iris × purpurissata

Iris × purpurissata, commonly known as the purple flag iris, is a hybrid perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its striking violet-blue flowers that bloom in early summer, typically on tall, sturdy stalks. The plant features narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are dark green and slightly hairy on the underside. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant color and elegant appearance.
