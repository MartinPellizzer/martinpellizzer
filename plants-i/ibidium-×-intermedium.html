Ibidium × intermedium

Ibidium × intermedium, commonly known as the hybrid ibidium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface and produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often found in rocky or coastal areas, where it tolerates mild drought conditions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
