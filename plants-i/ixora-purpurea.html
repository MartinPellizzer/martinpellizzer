Ixora purpurea

Ixora purpurea, commonly known as the purple flame or purple ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of bright red or orange-red flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has opposite, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Ixora purpurea is also valued for its ability to tolerate partial shade and its use in traditional medicine.
