Ilex argutidens var. nipponica

Ilex argutidens var. nipponica, commonly known as Japanese holly, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Japan.

It features elliptical, glossy dark green leaves that turn red in the autumn. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
