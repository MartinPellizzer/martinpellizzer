Ilex formosana var. macropyrena

Ilex formosana var. macropyrena, commonly known as the Formosan holly, is a species native to Taiwan.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall. The plant has alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy green on top and lighter underneath. Its small, red berries are an important food source for birds and other wildlife.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in horticulture.
