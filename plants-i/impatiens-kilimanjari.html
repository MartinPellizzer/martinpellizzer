Impatiens kilimanjari

Impatiens kilimanjari, commonly known as the Kilimanjaro impatiens, is a striking flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of East Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange or red flowers, which bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories or along stream banks. Its leaves are heart-shaped and glossy, providing a lush green backdrop to its colorful blooms.

Impatiens kilimanjari is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
