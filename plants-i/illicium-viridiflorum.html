Illicium viridiflorum

Illicium viridiflorum, commonly known as green-flowered star anise, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic leaves and distinctive, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, star-shaped fruits that are highly aromatic and used in traditional medicine and cuisine. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its botanical features make it a unique species within the Schisandraceae family.
