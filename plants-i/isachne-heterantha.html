Isachne heterantha

Isachne heterantha, commonly known as the heterantha isachne, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in wetland or marshy habitats, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils. The plant has slender, cylindrical stems that often lie close to the ground, forming dense mats. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a smooth surface and a prominent midrib.

The inflorescence consists of spikelets that are arranged in clusters, producing small, wind-pollinated flowers.
