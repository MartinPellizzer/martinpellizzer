Ipomoea portoricensis

Ipomoea portoricensis, commonly known as the Puerto Rican morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean island of Puerto Rico.

It is a vigorous vine that produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of pink to purple. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in warm, humid climates. It is often found growing in moist, shaded areas such as forests and along stream banks.

Ipomoea portoricensis is part of the Convolvulaceae family, which includes other morning glory species.
