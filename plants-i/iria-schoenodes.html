Iria schoenodes

Iria schoenodes, commonly known as the narrow-leaved iria, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and aromatic foliage. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Iria schoenodes is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
