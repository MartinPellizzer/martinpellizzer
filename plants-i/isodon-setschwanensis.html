Isodon setschwanensis

Isodon setschwanensis, commonly known as the Sichuan germander, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small and clustered in whorls, often displaying shades of blue or purple.

It is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
