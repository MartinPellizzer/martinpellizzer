Ilex xylosmifolia

Ilex xylosmifolia, commonly known as the Mexican holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, spiny leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that are typically followed by bright red berries. Ilex xylosmifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

It is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
