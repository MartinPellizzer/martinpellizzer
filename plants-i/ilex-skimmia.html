Ilex skimmia

Ilex skimmia, commonly known as winterberry, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its bright red berries that persist through the winter, providing food for birds and adding visual interest to gardens. The plant has small, glossy, dark green leaves that turn reddish in the fall. It typically grows in moist, acidic soils and thrives in partial to full shade.

Ilex skimmia is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and seasonal color.
