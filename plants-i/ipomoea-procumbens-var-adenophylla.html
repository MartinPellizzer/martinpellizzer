Ipomoea procumbens var. adenophylla

Ipomoea procumbens var. adenophylla, commonly known as the腺叶牵牛, is a herbaceous vine native to tropical regions of Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and clusters of trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant produces small, round berries that turn from green to red as they mature. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in warm climates.
