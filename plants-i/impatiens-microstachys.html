Impatiens microstachys

Impatiens microstachys, commonly known as the dwarf impatiens, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant features upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses around the world.
