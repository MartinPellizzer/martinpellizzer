Ixia oxalidiflora

Ixia oxalidiflora, commonly known as the yellow oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers with a distinctive orange center. The plant features strap-like leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Ixia oxalidiflora is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
