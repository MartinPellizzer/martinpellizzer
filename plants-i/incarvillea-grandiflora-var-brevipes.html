Incarvillea grandiflora var. brevipes

Incarvillea grandiflora var. brevipes, commonly known as the short-stemmed Chinese trumpetflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It features showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red, with a distinctive trumpet shape. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, which extends from late spring to early summer.
