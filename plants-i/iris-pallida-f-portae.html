Iris pallida f. portae

Iris pallida f. portae, commonly known as the pale iris with Porta's form, is a horticultural variant of the pale iris.

It is characterized by its pale blue to white flowers, which are typically smaller than those of the typical species. The plant features grass-like leaves that are usually bluish-green in color. It is a perennial herbaceous plant that thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

This form is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
