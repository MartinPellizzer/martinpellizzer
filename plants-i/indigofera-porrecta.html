Indigofera porrecta

Indigofera porrecta, commonly known as the slender indigo, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its climbing habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. Its seeds are encased in elongated pods, and it is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Indigofera porrecta is also valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens and landscapes.
