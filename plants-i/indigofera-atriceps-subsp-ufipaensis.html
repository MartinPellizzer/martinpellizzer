Indigofera atriceps subsp. ufipaensis

Indigofera atriceps subsp.

ufipaensis, commonly known as the Ufipa indigo plant, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of Africa. It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant is notable for its ability to fix nitrogen through symbiotic relationships with rhizobia bacteria, making it valuable in soil enrichment.

Its seeds are often used in traditional medicine and as a source of natural dye.
