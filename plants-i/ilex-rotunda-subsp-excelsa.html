Ilex rotunda subsp. excelsa

Ilex rotunda subsp.

excelsa, commonly known as the Chinese holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to China. It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and small, dark green leaves that are glossy in appearance.

The plant produces bright red berries that are toxic to humans and animals. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its hardy nature and attractive appearance make it suitable for hedges and garden landscapes.
