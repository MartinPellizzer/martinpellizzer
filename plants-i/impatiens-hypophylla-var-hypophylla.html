Impatiens hypophylla var. hypophylla

Impatiens hypophylla var. hypophylla, commonly known as the slender touch-me-not, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions of Asia.

It features delicate, showy flowers that are typically pink or white, with five petals and a spur at the base. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually hairy and have a distinctive waxy coating. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its rapid growth and ability to spread through runners make it a common ornamental plant in gardens.
