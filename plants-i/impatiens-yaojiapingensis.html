Impatiens yaojiapingensis

Impatiens yaojiapingensis, commonly known as the Yaojiaping balsam, is a flowering plant species native to China.

It belongs to the Balsaminaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and a trailing growth habit, making it suitable for hanging baskets or containers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and unique appearance, it is considered a rare and valuable addition to botanical collections.
