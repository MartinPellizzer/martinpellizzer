Ilex guianensis

Ilex guianensis, commonly known as the Guianan holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to South America.

It belongs to the family Aquifoliaceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, red berries that are edible for some animals. Its leaves are also used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
