Isoetes muricata subsp. asiatica

Isoetes muricata subsp.

asiatica, commonly known as the Asian water fern, is a species of aquatic plant belonging to the genus Isoetes. It is characterized by its spore-bearing structures called sporangia, which are typically clustered in a spore mass known as a sorus.

The plant thrives in freshwater environments, particularly in shallow, still waters with muddy substrates. Its leaves are usually narrow and ribbon-like, with a distinctive ribbed structure.

This subspecies is native to parts of Asia and is often found in wetlands, ponds, and rice fields.
