Impatiens maxima

Impatiens maxima, commonly known as the giant impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and large, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically grows to a height of 3 to 5 feet, featuring heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. Its flowers are borne in clusters at the ends of stems, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soil.

Impatiens maxima is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant blooms and adaptability to different growing conditions.
