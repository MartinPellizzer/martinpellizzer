Impatiens arriensii

Impatiens arriensii, commonly known as the mountain impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of South America.

It belongs to the Balsaminaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a range of colors including red, pink, and white. Impatiens arriensii is also known for its rapid growth and ease of cultivation in temperate climates.
