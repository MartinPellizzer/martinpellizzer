Isonandra stocksii

Isonandra stocksii, commonly known as the yellow bell, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its striking yellow, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. The plant has a woody stem and lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the branches. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens in arid climates.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and drought tolerance.
