Ixia alata

Ixia alata, commonly known as the winged ixia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllis family (Amaryllidaceae) and is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant produces bright orange or yellow blooms that are often adorned with a distinctive white or yellow wing-like structure. Ixia alata thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
