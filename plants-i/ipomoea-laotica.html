Ipomoea laotica

Ipomoea laotica, commonly known as the Laos morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous vine characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Ipomoea laotica is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its traditional uses in local medicine.
