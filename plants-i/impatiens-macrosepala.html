Impatiens macrosepala

Impatiens macrosepala, commonly known as the large-sepaled impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red, with prominent, broad sepals. The plant thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories. Its foliage is heart-shaped and glossy, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Impatiens macrosepala is valued in horticulture for its ability to bloom continuously throughout the growing season.
