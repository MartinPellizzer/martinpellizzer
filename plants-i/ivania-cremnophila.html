Ivania cremnophila

Ivania cremnophila, commonly known as the rock ivy, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing evergreen shrub that typically thrives in rocky or coastal environments. The plant features small, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. Its flowers are usually pale blue or white, appearing in clusters during the spring.

Ivania cremnophila is valued for its hardiness and ability to withstand harsh coastal conditions.
