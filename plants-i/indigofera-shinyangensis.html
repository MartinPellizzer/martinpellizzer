Indigofera shinyangensis

Indigofera shinyangensis, commonly known as the Shinyang indigo.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is native to China. The plant is characterized by its erect growth habit and trifoliate leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, displaying shades of purple or blue, which attract pollinators.

The species is noted for its potential in ecological restoration due to its nitrogen-fixing ability and adaptability to various soil types.
