Ionidium maximiliani

Ionidium maximiliani, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

This plant is a herbaceous species native to tropical regions of the Americas. It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety pubescence. The flowers are small and clustered in spikes, typically ranging in color from green to yellow.

Ionidium maximiliani is often found in moist, disturbed soils and can be an invasive species in some areas.
