Ischaemum urvilleanum

Ischaemum urvilleanum, commonly known as the slender field grass, is a perennial grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as marshes, ditches, and along riverbanks. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It is often found in disturbed soils and can form dense vegetation in wetland environments.

Its botanical features make it an important component of wetland ecosystems.
