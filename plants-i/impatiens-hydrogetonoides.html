Impatiens hydrogetonoides

Impatiens hydrogetonoides, commonly known as the water impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy green leaves and clusters of vibrant pink or white flowers. The plant thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found near water sources such as streams and marshes. Its scientific name reflects its affinity for water, with "hydrogetonoides" suggesting a relationship to water.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with appropriate humidity and drainage.
