Isachne cambodiensis

Isachne cambodiensis, commonly known as Cambodian grass, is a perennial grass species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, wiry stems. The plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats, such as marshes and along riverbanks. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, with a bluish-green coloration.

Isachne cambodiensis plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various small organisms in its native environment.
