Ixeris denticulata f. pinnatipartita

Ixeris denticulata f. pinnatipartita, commonly known as the pinnatipartita golden groundsel, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with bright yellow ray flowers and a compact, mat-forming growth habit. The leaves are finely divided, giving the plant a delicate, fern-like appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
