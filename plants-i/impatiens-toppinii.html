Impatiens toppinii

Impatiens toppinii, commonly known as the Chinese water impatiens, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in a range of colors, including white, pink, and red. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and thrives in partial to full shade. It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.

Impatiens toppinii is also valued for its use in traditional medicine in some regions.
