Isotoma fluviatilis subsp. australis

Isotoma fluviatilis subsp.

australis, commonly known as the Australian water starwort, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia. It is a small, aquatic or semi-aquatic herb with slender, branching stems that often grow in wetlands or along water margins.

The plant produces tiny, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in late summer to autumn. Its leaves are narrow and often submerged, contributing to its role in stabilizing water bodies.

It is an important species for aquatic ecosystems, providing habitat and food for various invertebrates.
