Ilex dioica var. dioica

Ilex dioica var. dioica, commonly known as the European holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically oval in shape and have spines along the edges. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its dense foliage and year-round appearance.

Ilex dioica var. dioica is also valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
