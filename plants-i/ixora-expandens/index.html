Ixora Expandens

Red ixora, scientifically known as Ixora expandens, is a vibrant evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia. It is celebrated for its clusters of bright red flowers that bloom throughout the year, adding a striking visual appeal to gardens and landscapes. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for both indoor and outdoor settings. With proper care, Red ixora can flourish, offering continuous color and a touch of tropical beauty.
