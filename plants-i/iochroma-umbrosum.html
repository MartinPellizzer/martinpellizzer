Iochroma umbrosum

Iochroma umbrosum, commonly known as the shadowflower, is a member of the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from violet to deep purple. Its foliage consists of large, velvety leaves that are often used in traditional medicine.

Iochroma umbrosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been cultivated for its unique floral display.
