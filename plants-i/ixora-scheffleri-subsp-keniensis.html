Ixora scheffleri subsp. keniensis

Ixora scheffleri subsp.

keniensis, commonly known as the Kenyan ixora, is a flowering plant native to East Africa. It is characterized by its dense, showy clusters of orange-red flowers that bloom throughout the year.

The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are elliptical in shape and covered in a fine, whitish bloom. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for tropical and subtropical climates.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
