Indigofera karongensis

Indigofera karongensis, commonly known as the Karongensis shrub, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

The plant is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil fertility. It typically grows in open woodland and savanna habitats, thriving in well-drained soils.
