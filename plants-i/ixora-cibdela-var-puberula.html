Ixora cibdela var. puberula

Ixora cibdela var. puberula, commonly known as the velvet ixora, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and clusters of bright orange-red flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has small, elliptical leaves with a smooth, waxy texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Ixora cibdela var. puberula is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant floral display and adaptability to various garden settings.
