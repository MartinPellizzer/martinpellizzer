Ilex × beanii

Ilex × beanii, commonly known as the Chinese holly, is a hybrid evergreen shrub or small tree native to China.

It features glossy, dark green, leathery leaves that are typically spiny and oval in shape. The plant produces small, red berries that are toxic to humans and animals. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its hardy nature and winter interest make it a valued addition to gardens in temperate climates.
