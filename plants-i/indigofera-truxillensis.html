Indigofera truxillensis

Indigofera truxillensis, commonly known as the Trinidad morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in the early morning. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, bean-like pods that contain seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in disturbed soils.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in both horticulture and ecological studies.
