Ismene

Ismene, commonly known as the common ismene, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Amaranthaceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and purple. The plant features alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of hairs. Ismene thrives in full sun to partial shade and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

It is native to various regions in Europe and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
