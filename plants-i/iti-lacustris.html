Iti lacustris

Iti lacustris, commonly known as the water hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, fuzzy texture. It thrives in moist environments, such as wetlands and along the edges of ponds.

Iti lacustris is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to purify water through natural filtration.
