Ipomoea gomezii

It is a member of the Convolvulaceae family, characterized by its twining stems and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant produces vibrant blue to purple blooms that typically open in the morning and close by midday. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine, soft hairs.
