Ipomoea diehlii

Ipomoea diehlii, commonly known as the Mexican morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in the early morning. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces spiral-shaped seed pods. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils.
