Impatiens oumina

Impatiens oumina, commonly known as the white impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, fleshy fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens oumina is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
