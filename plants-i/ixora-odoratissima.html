Ixora odoratissima

Ixora odoratissima, commonly known as the sweet shower or fragrant ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of small, bright red flowers that are highly fragrant, especially in the evening. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a glossy appearance.

Ixora odoratissima is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
