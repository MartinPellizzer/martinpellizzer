Ixora elegans

Ixora elegans, commonly known as the velvet tamarind or flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and clusters of bright red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant produces small, spherical fruits that contain several seeds. Ixora elegans thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its flowers are rich in nectar, making it an attractive plant for pollinators like bees and birds.
