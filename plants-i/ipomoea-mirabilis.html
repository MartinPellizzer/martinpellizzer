Ipomoea mirabilis

Ipomoea mirabilis, commonly known as the morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its trumpet-shaped flowers that open in the morning and close at dusk. The plant is a vigorous climber with heart-shaped leaves and can grow up to several meters in height. Its flowers come in a variety of colors, including purple, pink, red, and white.

Ipomoea mirabilis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
