Isachne perrieri

Isachne perrieri, commonly known as the Perrier's isachne, is a species of grass native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are narrow and slightly folded, while its inflorescence consists of a single spikelet.

Isachne perrieri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
