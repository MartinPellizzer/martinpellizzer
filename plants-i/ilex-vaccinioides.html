Ilex vaccinioides

Ilex vaccinioides, commonly known as the Florida holly, is a deciduous evergreen shrub or small tree native to the southeastern United States.

It has simple, alternate, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter on the underside. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal dunes and wetlands.

Its dense growth habit makes it a popular choice for landscaping and erosion control.
