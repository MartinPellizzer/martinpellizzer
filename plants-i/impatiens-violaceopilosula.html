Impatiens violaceopilosula

Impatiens violaceopilosula, commonly known as the violet-haired impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pale pink to deep violet. The plant has hairy, glandular stems and leaves that are often covered with fine, pale hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in warm climates.
