Indigofera zenkeri var. brevifoliolata

Indigofera zenkeri var. brevifoliolata, commonly known as the short-leaved indigo plant, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and clusters of pink to purple flowers. The plant produces cylindrical pods containing small, dark seeds. It is often found in moist, shaded areas such as forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and traditional medicine.
