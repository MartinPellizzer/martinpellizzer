Ione kachinensis

Ione kachinensis, commonly known as the Kachin iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers with a white or yellow center. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its stems are short and sturdy.

The Kachin iris is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
