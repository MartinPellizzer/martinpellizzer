Impatiens mishmiensis

Impatiens mishmiensis, commonly known as the Mishmi Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the eastern Himalayas.

It belongs to the genus Impatiens, which is part of the family Balsaminaceae. The plant is characterized by its delicate, spurred flowers that come in a range of colors, including pink, red, and white. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes.
