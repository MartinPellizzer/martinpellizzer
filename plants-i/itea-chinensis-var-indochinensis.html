Itea chinensis var. indochinensis

Itea chinensis var. indochinensis, commonly known as the Indochinese spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features slender, arching stems that bear clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers during the spring. The plant has simple, ovate leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
