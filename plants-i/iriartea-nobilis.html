Iriartea nobilis

Iriartea nobilis, commonly known as the noble areca, is a striking tropical plant native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It is a member of the Arecaceae family, which includes palms, and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound leaves. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments and can reach heights of up to 10 meters. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Iriartea nobilis is also valued for its resilience and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.
