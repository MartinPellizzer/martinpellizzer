Iris zhaoana

Iris zhaoana, commonly known as Zhao's iris, is a species of flowering plant in the genus Iris.

It is native to China and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers with a yellow center. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade. Its leaves are broad and sword-shaped, and it produces showy blooms in the spring.

Zhao's iris is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
