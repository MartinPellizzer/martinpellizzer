Ixia tortuosa

Ixia tortuosa, commonly known as the spiral lily, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its spiral arrangement of petals, which gives it a unique and elegant appearance. The plant produces striking orange or yellow flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. Native to the Western and Northern Cape provinces of South Africa, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Ixia tortuosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
