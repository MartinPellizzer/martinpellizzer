Impatiens laxiflora var. stracheyi

Impatiens laxiflora var. stracheyi, commonly known as the Strachey's touch-me-not, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves and produces clusters of small, delicate, pinkish-white flowers. The plant is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments. It belongs to the Balsaminaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its unique flowering pattern and adaptability make it a notable species in botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
