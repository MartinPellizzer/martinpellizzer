Isodon umbrosus f. albescens

Isodon umbrosus f. albescens, commonly known as the white-flowered betony, is a variant of the betony plant characterized by its pale or white flowers.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family and is native to regions of southern Europe and western Asia. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

The inflorescence consists of dense clusters of small, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.
