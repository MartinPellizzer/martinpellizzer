Ixora rangonensis

Ixora rangonensis, commonly known as the red ixora, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bright red or orange inflorescences, which consist of numerous small, tubular flowers clustered together. The plant has simple, opposite, and often hairy leaves that are dark green in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Ixora rangonensis is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
