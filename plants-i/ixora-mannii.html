Ixora mannii

Ixora mannii, commonly known as the red flame tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its bright red or orange flowers, which bloom in clusters and are highly attractive to pollinators. The plant has simple, opposite, and often hairy leaves that are dark green in color. Ixora mannii is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to several meters in height.
