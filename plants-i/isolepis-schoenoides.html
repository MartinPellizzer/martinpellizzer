Isolepis schoenoides

Isolepis schoenoides, commonly known as the slender bulrush, is a herbaceous plant native to wetland environments in North America.

It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and spike-like inflorescences that produce small, three-lobed flowers. The plant thrives in shallow water or moist soils, making it an important component of aquatic ecosystems. Its rhizomes help stabilize soil and prevent erosion in wetland areas.

Isolepis schoenoides plays a vital role in filtering water and providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
