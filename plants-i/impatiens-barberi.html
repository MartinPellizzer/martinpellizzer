Impatiens barberi

Impatiens barberi, commonly known as the Sierra Madre impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Mexico.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are usually pink or red in color. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Impatiens barberi thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.
