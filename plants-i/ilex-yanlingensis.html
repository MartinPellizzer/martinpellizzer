Ilex yanlingensis

Ilex yanlingensis, commonly known as the Yanling holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Aquifoliaceae and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for garden landscapes in temperate climates.
