Iseilema membranaceum var. trichopus

Iseilema membranaceum var. trichopus, commonly known as the hairy fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds. This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in shaded garden settings.
