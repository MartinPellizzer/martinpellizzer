Ixia leucantha var. aristata

Ixia leucantha var. aristata, commonly known as the silver-stemmed ixia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllis family (Amaryllidaceae) and is characterized by its striking, tubular white flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant features slender, silver-green stems and linear, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its unique appearance and seasonal blooms make it a standout in botanical collections.
