Ixora chelsonii

Ixora chelsonii, commonly known as the pink ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, featuring dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, pink or red flowers, which bloom throughout the year in warm climates. Its botanical features include opposite leaves and woody stems, making it a popular ornamental plant.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, often found in coastal and tropical regions.
