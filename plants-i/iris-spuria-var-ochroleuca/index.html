Iris Spuria Var. Ochroleuca

The iris spuria var. ochroleuca is a rare and striking plant known for its pale yellow flowers. It is a variant of the spuria iris, which is native to the mountainous regions of central and eastern europe. this plant thrives in rocky and alpine environments, often found in areas with poor soil. its delicate appearance makes it a favorite among botanists and gardeners alike. due to its limited habitat, it is considered a vulnerable species requiring careful conservation.
