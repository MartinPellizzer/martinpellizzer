Ismene parviflora

Ismene parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered ismene, is a herbaceous perennial native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine, hairy foliage. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly serrated edge.

Ismene parviflora is valued in gardens for its delicate appearance and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
