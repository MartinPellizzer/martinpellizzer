Impatiens paranyi

Impatiens paranyi, commonly known as the Paranyi Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Balsaminaceae family and is characterized by its showy, vibrant flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant typically grows as an upright herb with opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking to add color to shady garden spaces.
