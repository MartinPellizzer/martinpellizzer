Inga yunckeri

Inga yunckeri, commonly known as the Yunque Inga, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Colombia and Ecuador. The plant is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub, characterized by its compound leaves and showy, pea-like flowers. It produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Inga yunckeri is also valued for its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity and its potential use in agroforestry systems.
