Idothea cowanii

Idothea cowanii, commonly known as the African orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, often with a yellow or greenish tint. Idothea cowanii thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and botanical collections.
