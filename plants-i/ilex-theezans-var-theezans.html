Ilex theezans var. theezans

Ilex theezans var. theezans, commonly known as the Chinese holly, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green elliptical leaves that turn red in the fall. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries that are edible for birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
