Isodon weisiensis

Isodon weisiensis, commonly known as the Chinese skullcap, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in forested areas and is known for its medicinal properties. Its flowers are small and clustered, often in whorls along the stem.

The leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
