Isoetes macrospora var. heterospora

Isoetes macrospora var. heterospora, commonly known as the heterosporous water fern, is a species of aquatic plant belonging to the genus Isoetes.

It is characterized by its unique spore production, with the plant producing both macrospores and microspores on separate sporocarps. The sporocarps are typically clustered in a dense, spike-like structure that emerges from the water surface. This variation in spore size and placement is a key feature of the heterosporous life cycle.

The plant is adapted to freshwater environments and plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by contributing to nutrient cycling and providing habitat for microorganisms.
