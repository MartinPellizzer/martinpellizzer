Impatiens tenuibracteata

Impatiens tenuibracteata, commonly known as the slender bract impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and are borne on slender stems. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually hairy and have a soft texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens tenuibracteata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
