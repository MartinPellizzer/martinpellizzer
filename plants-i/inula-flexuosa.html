Inula flexuosa

Inula flexuosa, commonly known as the zigzag inula, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, branched stems that often exhibit a zigzag pattern, giving it its common name. The plant produces yellow composite flowers, which are composed of many small florets arranged in a head. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Inula flexuosa is often found in meadows and grassy areas, and it is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
