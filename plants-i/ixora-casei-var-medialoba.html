Ixora casei var. medialoba

Ixora casei var. medialoba, commonly known as the pink ixora, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences of bright pink to red flowers, which bloom throughout the year in suitable conditions. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

This ornamental plant is often cultivated for its vibrant floral display and is popular in horticultural gardens.
