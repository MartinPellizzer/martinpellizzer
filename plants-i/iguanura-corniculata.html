Iguanura corniculata

Iguanura corniculata, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean and parts of South America.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow flowers that resemble small showers, hence its common name. The plant produces small, elongated fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The leaves are simple, alternate, and have a glossy appearance.
