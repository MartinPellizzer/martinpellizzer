Ilex ficoidea

Ilex ficoidea, commonly known as the Chinese holly, is a evergreen shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, bright red berries that are toxic to humans and animals. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Ilex ficoidea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
