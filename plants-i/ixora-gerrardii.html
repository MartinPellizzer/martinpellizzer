Ixora gerrardii

Ixora gerrardii, commonly known as the coral tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of bright red, coral-like flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal areas or disturbed soils.

Ixora gerrardii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is commonly used in landscaping and as a hedge plant.
