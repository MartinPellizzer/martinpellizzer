Iebine nervosa

Iebine nervosa, commonly known as the narrow-veined orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pale pink. The plant has slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. Iebine nervosa thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its unique floral structure and ornamental appeal make it a subject of interest for botanists and horticulturists.
