Ismelia carinata

Ismelia carinata, commonly known as the horned morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spirally twisted stems and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink to purple. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, oval seeds. Ismelia carinata is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along stream banks.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
