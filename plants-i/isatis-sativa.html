Isatis sativa

Isatis sativa, commonly known as yellow rocket, is a biennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and pinnately divided leaves. The plant belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other cruciferous species. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas.

Its seeds are small and contain compounds that have been studied for potential medicinal uses.
