Iberis amara proles apricorum

Iberis amara proles apricorum, commonly known as bitter candytuft, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, oval seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
