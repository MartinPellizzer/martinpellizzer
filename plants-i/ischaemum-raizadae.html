Ischaemum raizadae

Ischaemum raizadae, commonly known as the African grass or red grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves that are typically reddish in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing numerous tiny florets. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in open, disturbed habitats.

Ischaemum raizadae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its distinctive appearance.
