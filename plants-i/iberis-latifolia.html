Iberis latifolia

Iberis latifolia, commonly known as broadleaf candytuft, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has basal leaves that are broad and lobed, with a mat-forming growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and low maintenance requirements.
