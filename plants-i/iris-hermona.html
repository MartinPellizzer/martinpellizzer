Iris hermona

Iris hermona, commonly known as the Hermontic iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

It features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from March to May. The plant has sword-shaped leaves that are grass-like in appearance, growing from a rhizomatous root system. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Iris hermona is valued for its ornamental beauty and is relatively low maintenance once established.
