Ilex kunthiana f. funckii

Ilex kunthiana f. funckii, commonly known as the Funck's holly, is a shrub or small tree native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage, with dark green leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters, followed by bright red berries that are ornamental and attract wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it a versatile addition to gardens.

Its compact growth habit and winter interest make it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.
