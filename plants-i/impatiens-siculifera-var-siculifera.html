Impatiens siculifera var. siculifera

Impatiens siculifera var. siculifera, commonly known as the Sicilian impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptical in shape, with a glossy surface and serrated edges. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for garden borders and hanging baskets.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
