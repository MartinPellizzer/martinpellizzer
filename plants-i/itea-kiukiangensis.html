Itea kiukiangensis

Itea kiukiangensis, commonly known as the Chinese spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features slender, arching branches and small, clustered flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has simple, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value comes from its delicate flowers and attractive foliage.
