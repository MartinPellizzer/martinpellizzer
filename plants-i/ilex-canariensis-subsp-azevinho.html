Ilex canariensis subsp. azevinho

Ilex canariensis subsp.

azevinho, commonly known as the Canary Island holly, is a shrub or small tree native to the Canary Islands. It belongs to the family Aquifoliaceae and is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are typically glossy and elliptical in shape.

The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for local wildlife. It thrives in coastal and mountainous areas, often growing in well-drained soils.
