Ipomoea blanda

Ipomoea blanda, commonly known as the small morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces delicate, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in woodland areas and along stream banks.

Ipomoea blanda is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
