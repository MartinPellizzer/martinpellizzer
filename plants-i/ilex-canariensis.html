Ilex canariensis

Ilex canariensis, commonly known as the Canary Island holly, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is an evergreen shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are leathery and dark green on top with a lighter underside. It produces small, white flowers that are followed by bright red berries, which are an important food source for local wildlife.

The plant is adapted to the volcanic soils and coastal conditions of its native habitat.
