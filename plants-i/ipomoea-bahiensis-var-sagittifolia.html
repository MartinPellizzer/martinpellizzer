Ipomoea bahiensis var. sagittifolia

Ipomoea bahiensis var. sagittifolia, commonly known as the arrowleaf morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in the morning. The plant belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is known for its rapid growth and climbing habit. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
