Iris reichenbachii

Iris reichenbachii, commonly known as the Reichenbach's iris, is a species of flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant produces sword-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and arranged in a fan-like pattern. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine displays.

Iris reichenbachii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its medicinal properties.
