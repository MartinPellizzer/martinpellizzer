Inula griffithii

Inula griffithii, commonly known as the Tibetan fleur-de-lis, is a perennial herb native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils at high altitudes.

Inula griffithii is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
