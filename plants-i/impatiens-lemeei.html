Impatiens lemeei

Impatiens lemeei, commonly known as the Madagascar impatiens, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Balsaminaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters and features opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shady garden beds and container planting.
