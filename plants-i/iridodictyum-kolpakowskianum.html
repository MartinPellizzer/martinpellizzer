Iridodictyum kolpakowskianum

Iridodictyum kolpakowskianum, commonly known as the Kolpakowski's spikegrass, is a rare and delicate grass species native to the wetlands of South America.

The plant thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils and is often found in marshy or swampy habitats. Its botanical structure includes a fibrous root system and a basal rosette of leaves that support its flowering stems.
