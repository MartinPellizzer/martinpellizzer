Isoloma seemannii

Isoloma seemannii, commonly known as the Cape goldenbush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is well adapted to sandy soils. It has narrow, linear leaves and is often used in coastal landscaping due to its hardiness.

Isoloma seemannii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
