Impatiens porphyrea

Impatiens porphyrea, commonly known as the red impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to deep red. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a velvety texture and a distinctive reddish or purple underside. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens porphyrea is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
