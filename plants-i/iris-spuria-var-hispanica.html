Iris spuria var. hispanica

Iris spuria var. hispanica, commonly known as the Spanish iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and northern Africa.

It features upright, slender stems that bear showy, violet-blue flowers with a distinctive spur at the base of the petals. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are slightly hairy and arranged in a fan-like pattern. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

The iris is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
