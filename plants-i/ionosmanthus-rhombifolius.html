Ionosmanthus rhombifolius

Ionosmanthus rhombifolius, commonly known as the Chinese hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, rhombic-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and have a velvety texture. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom throughout the warmer months. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.

Ionosmanthus rhombifolius prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.
