Isachne latifolia

Isachne latifolia, commonly known as broadleaf isachne, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its flat, blade-like leaves that are typically broad and smooth. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it an important component of aquatic and riparian ecosystems.
