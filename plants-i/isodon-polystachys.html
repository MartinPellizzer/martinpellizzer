Isodon polystachys

Isodon polystachys, commonly known as the Chinese violet or Indian violet, is a flowering plant native to Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and showy, violet-colored flowers that bloom in whorls. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions.

Isodon polystachys is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
