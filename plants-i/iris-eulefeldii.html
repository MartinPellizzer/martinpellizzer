Iris eulefeldii

Iris eulefeldii, commonly known as the German iris, is a species native to Central Europe.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and striking blue to purple flowers. The plant typically blooms in late spring to early summer, producing showy, spurred petals. Its foliage consists of broad, sword-shaped leaves that are often dark green in color.

Iris eulefeldii is well-suited for gardens and can thrive in moist, well-drained soils.
