Iberis linifolia var. maroccana

Iberis linifolia var. maroccana, commonly known as Moroccan candytuft, is a low-growing, perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features compact, rounded foliage composed of small, dark green, leathery leaves. The plant produces clusters of bright white, star-shaped flowers during the spring season. It is well adapted to dry, rocky soils and full sun exposure.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
