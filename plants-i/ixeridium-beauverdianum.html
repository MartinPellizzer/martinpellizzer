Ixeridium beauverdianum

Ixeridium beauverdianum, commonly known as the slender swamp grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It typically grows in wetland habitats, such as marshes and swamps, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often clustered in spike-like inflorescences. It is adapted to flooded conditions and plays a role in stabilizing soil in aquatic environments.

Its botanical features make it an important component of wetland ecosystems.
