Ilex tolucana var. californica

Ilex tolucana var. californica, commonly known as the California holly, is a broadleaf evergreen shrub or small tree native to coastal regions of California.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that remain throughout the year, providing year-round visual interest. The plant produces small, white flowers in the spring, which are followed by bright red berries that persist into winter. It is well adapted to coastal climates and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

The species is also valued for its ability to tolerate salt spray and drought conditions.
