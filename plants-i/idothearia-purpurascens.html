Idothearia purpurascens

Idothearia purpurascens, commonly known as the purple-stemmed rock rose, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its fleshy, often purple-tinged stems and leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Idothearia purpurascens is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in drought conditions.
