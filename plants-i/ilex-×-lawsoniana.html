Ilex × lawsoniana

Ilex × lawsoniana, commonly known as the Chinese holly, is a hybrid evergreen shrub or small tree native to China.

It features dense, spiny leaves that are typically glossy and dark green, with a leathery texture. The plant produces small, white flowers in the spring, followed by red berries that are toxic to humans and animals. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and winter interest.

Ilex × lawsoniana thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for garden landscapes.
