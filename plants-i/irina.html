Irina

Irina, commonly known as the irises, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Iridaceae.

These plants are characterized by their showy, often colorful flowers that typically have three petals and three sepals. Irises are native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere and are known for their diverse species and cultivars. They are often grown in gardens for their ornamental value and are also used in perfumery.

The plant's rhizomes are sometimes used in traditional medicine.
