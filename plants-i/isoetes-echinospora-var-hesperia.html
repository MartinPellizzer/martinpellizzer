Isoetes echinospora var. hesperia

Isoetes echinospora var. hesperia, commonly known as the Hesperian watermace, is a species of aquatic plant belonging to the genus Isoetes, which are characterized by their spore-bearing structures called sporangia.

This variety is native to freshwater environments in North America, particularly in the western United States. It typically grows in shallow, still or slow-moving waters, often in muddy or silty substrates. The plant features a tall, sparsely branched stem with small, scale-like leaves, and its sporangia are clustered at the apex, producing spores for reproduction.
