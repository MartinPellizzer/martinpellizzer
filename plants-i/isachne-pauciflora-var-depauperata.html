Isachne pauciflora var. depauperata

Isachne pauciflora var. depauperata, commonly known as the sparse-flowered isachne, is a variant of the isachne grass species.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Asia, particularly India and Nepal. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as grasslands and forest margins. Its leaves are narrow and linear, while its inflorescence consists of small, clustered spikelets.

The botanical features of this variety are characterized by its reduced floral display and compact growth habit.
