Impatiens tubulosa f. multiflora

Impatiens tubulosa f. multiflora, commonly known as the multiblooming impatiens, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features upright, branching stems covered in dense clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The foliage is dark green and glossy, with heart-shaped leaves that add to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in shaded to partially sunny conditions and requires consistently moist, well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its continuous blooming habit and adaptability to container gardening.
