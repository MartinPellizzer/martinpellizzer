Ipomoea temascaltepecensis

Ipomoea temascaltepecensis, commonly known as the Temascaltepec morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is characterized by its trumpet-shaped, vibrant blue or violet flowers. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine by local communities.
