Illicium henryi

Illicium henryi, commonly known as Chinese star anise, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the family Schisandraceae and is characterized by its aromatic, star-shaped fruits. The plant produces small, green flowers that develop into dark brown, star-shaped pods. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly serrated margin.
