Isatis allionii

Isatis allionii, commonly known as the yellow flag or yellow isatis, is a perennial herb native to eastern North America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to September. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and have a slightly rough texture.

Isatis allionii is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of ponds, thriving in moist to wet soil conditions.
