Irlbachia alata subsp. alba

Irlbachia alata subsp.

alba, commonly known as the white-winged bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Europe. It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive winged structure on the petals.
