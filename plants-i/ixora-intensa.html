Ixora intensa

Ixora intensa, commonly known as the flame flower, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy red or orange inflorescences that resemble flames. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Ixora intensa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
