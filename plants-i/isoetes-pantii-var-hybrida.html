Isoetes pantii var. hybrida

Isoetes pantii var. hybrida, commonly known as the hybrid water fern, is a species of aquatic plant belonging to the genus Isoetes.

It is characterized by its spore-bearing, spike-like structures called sporophylls, which are arranged in a dense, compact cluster. The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, often in muddy or shallow waters. Its leaves are typically narrow and ribbon-like, with a distinct midrib.

The species is notable for its ability to tolerate a range of water conditions, making it a resilient and adaptable plant in aquatic ecosystems.
