Illigera madagascariensis

Illigera madagascariensis, commonly known as the Madagascar star orchid, is a strikingly beautiful orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its vibrant, star-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors, including yellow, orange, and red. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to tree bark in its natural habitat. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

This species is valued not only for its ornamental appeal but also for its ecological significance in Madagascar's unique biodiversity.
