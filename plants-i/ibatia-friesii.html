Ibatia friesii

Ibatia friesii, commonly known as the golden rain tree, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 15 meters tall, featuring a broad, spreading canopy. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it its common name. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly waxy texture.

Ibatia friesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm climates for its vibrant floral display.
