Ixia minor

Ixia minor, commonly known as the dwarf firecracker, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. Ixia minor thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
