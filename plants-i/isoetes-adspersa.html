Isoetes adspersa

Isoetes adspersa, commonly known as the scattered water fern, is a species of aquatic plant belonging to the genus Isoetes.

It is characterized by its spore-bearing sorus located on the underside of its fronds, which are typically narrow and ribbon-like. This plant thrives in freshwater environments, such as ponds and lakes, where it anchors itself to the substrate with its root-like structures. Its morphology includes a rosette of leaves that form a compact cluster, aiding in its survival in still waters.

Isoetes adspersa plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for microorganisms and small aquatic life.
