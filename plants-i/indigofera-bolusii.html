Indigofera bolusii

Indigofera bolusii, commonly known as the Bolus indigo or Bolus's legume, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine.

Indigofera bolusii is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for agricultural practices.
