Ixora × splendens

Ixora × splendens, commonly known as the flame-of-hippo or Chinese hibiscus, is a hybrid shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, fiery red or orange-red flowers that bloom throughout the year in tropical climates. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy green surface. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Ixora × splendens is widely cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
