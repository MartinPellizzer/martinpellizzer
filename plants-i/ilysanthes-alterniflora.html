Ilysanthes alterniflora

Ilysanthes alterniflora, commonly known as the alternate-flower plant, is a species native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

It is a herbaceous plant that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and have a smooth, waxy surface. The plant produces small, white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the wet season.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found near streams or in dense undergrowth.
