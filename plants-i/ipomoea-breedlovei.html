Ipomoea breedlovei

Ipomoea breedlovei, commonly known as the Mexican morning glory, is a flowering vine native to Mexico.

It produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in warm, sunny climates. It is often found growing in disturbed soils and along roadsides.

Its rapid growth and ornamental value make it a popular choice for gardeners in suitable regions.
