Ilysanthes verbenifolia

Ilysanthes verbenifolia, commonly known as the verbena-like plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Verbenaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface.

Ilysanthes verbenifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
