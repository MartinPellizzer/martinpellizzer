Iochroma weberbaueri

Iochroma weberbaueri, commonly known as the silverleaf chuparosa, is a shrub native to the Andean regions of South America.

It features striking, showy flowers that are typically blue or purple, which attract pollinators such as bees and hummingbirds. The plant has small, ovate leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
