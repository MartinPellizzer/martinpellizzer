Ipomoea saopaulista

Ipomoea saopaulista, commonly known as the Brazilian morning glory, is a climbing vine native to Brazil.

It features heart-shaped leaves and produces striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep purple. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.
