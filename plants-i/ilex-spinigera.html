Ilex spinigera

Ilex spinigera, commonly known as the spiny holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and small, leathery leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. Ilex spinigera thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in humid, shaded environments.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
