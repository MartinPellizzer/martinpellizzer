Ilex riedlaei

Ilex riedlaei, commonly known as the Riedl's holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

Ilex riedlaei is also valued for its ecological role, providing food and shelter for various wildlife species.
