Iguanura elegans

Iguanura elegans, commonly known as the pink shower tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking pink to red flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual impact. The plant has compound leaves with long, slender leaflets that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The tree is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a landscape plant.
