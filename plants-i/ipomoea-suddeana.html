Ipomoea suddeana

Ipomoea suddeana, commonly known as the Sudanese morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers that bloom in the early morning. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces tendrils that help it climb and support its growth. It thrives in warm, sunny environments and is often found in moist soils.

Ipomoea suddeana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
