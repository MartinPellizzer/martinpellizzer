Ixora venusta

Ixora venusta, commonly known as the velvet ixora, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange to red flowers that bloom in dense, branched clusters. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Ixora venusta is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a hedge plant.
