Indigofera phillipsiae var. erecta

Indigofera phillipsiae var. erecta, commonly known as the upright indigofera, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and compound leaves with small, ovate leaflets. The plant produces vibrant purple to blue flowers in racemes, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and landscapes for its attractive foliage and showy blooms.
