Ixora coccinea var. linneana

Ixora coccinea var. linneana, commonly known as the red flame ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as birds and bees. The plant has opposite, simple, and lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sunlight.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for both ornamental gardens and container planting.
