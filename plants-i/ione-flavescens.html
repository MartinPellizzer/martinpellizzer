Ione flavescens

Ione flavescens, commonly known as the yellow bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, woodland habitats and prefers partial to full shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Ione flavescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color.
