Ilex aculeolata var. kiangsiensis

Ilex aculeolata var. kiangsiensis, commonly known as the Kiangsi holly, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its elliptical, dark green leaves that turn reddish in autumn. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters, followed by bright red berries that are edible for birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for ornamental landscaping.

Its compact size and seasonal color changes make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking both aesthetic and ecological benefits.
