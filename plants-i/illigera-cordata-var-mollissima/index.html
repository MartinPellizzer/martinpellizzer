Illigera Cordata Var. Mollissima

Illigera cordata var. mollissima is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. Known for its velvety, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of delicate, fragrant blooms, this variety captivates both botanists and gardeners alike. Its unique foliage and ornamental flowers make it a sought-after addition to shaded gardens and indoor plant collections. Despite its elegance, it remains relatively obscure in mainstream horticulture, often overshadowed by more common species. This plant exemplifies the diversity and beauty of the Illigera genus, offering a glimpse into the hidden wonders of the plant world.
