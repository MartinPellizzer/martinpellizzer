Ixora orophila

Ixora orophila, commonly known as the mountain ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky and mountainous regions, preferring well-drained soils. The plant produces clusters of small, brightly colored flowers, usually in shades of red, orange, or yellow. Its leaves are simple and opposite, with a leathery texture.

Ixora orophila is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
