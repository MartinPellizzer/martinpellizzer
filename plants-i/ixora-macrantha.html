Ixora macrantha

Ixora macrantha, commonly known as the flame flower or Chinese flame tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or orange inflorescences, which consist of numerous small, clustered flowers. The plant is evergreen, with glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing as a shrub or small tree.

Ixora macrantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and as a street tree.
