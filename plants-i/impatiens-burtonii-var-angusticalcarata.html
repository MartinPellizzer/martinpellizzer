Impatiens burtonii var. angusticalcarata

Impatiens burtonii var. angusticalcarata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink. The leaves are typically narrow and elongated, with a slightly serrated margin. This species thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
