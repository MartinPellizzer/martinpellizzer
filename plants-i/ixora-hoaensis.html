Ixora hoaensis

Ixora hoaensis, commonly known as the Chinese flame tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a striking visual display. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sun exposure.

Ixora hoaensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a street tree due to its vibrant blooms and adaptability.
