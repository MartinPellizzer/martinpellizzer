Ilex delavayi var. comberiana

Ilex delavayi var. comberiana, commonly known as the Chinese holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in the spring, which are followed by bright red berries in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for ornamental gardening.

The plant is also valued for its hardiness and ability to tolerate urban environments.
