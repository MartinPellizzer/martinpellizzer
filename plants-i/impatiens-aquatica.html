Impatiens aquatica

Impatiens aquatica, commonly known as water impatiens, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy green leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and is often found near water bodies such as ponds and streams. It has a fibrous root system that helps stabilize soil and prevent erosion.

Impatiens aquatica is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to provide habitat for aquatic and terrestrial wildlife.
