Iria retusa

Iria retusa, commonly known as the rough iria, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features simple, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving them a rough texture. Its small, white or pale pink flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom in the summer months.

Iria retusa is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to thrive in arid environments.
