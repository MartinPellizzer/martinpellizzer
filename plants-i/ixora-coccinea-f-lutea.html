Ixora coccinea f. lutea

Ixora coccinea f. lutea, commonly known as the yellow flame or yellow ixora, is a variant of the ixora plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a flowering shrub characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers, which contrast with its glossy green foliage. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that bloom throughout the year in warm climates. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This cultivar is popular in ornamental gardening for its striking color and hardy nature.
