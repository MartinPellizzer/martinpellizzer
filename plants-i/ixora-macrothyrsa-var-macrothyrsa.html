Ixora macrothyrsa var. macrothyrsa

Ixora macrothyrsa var. macrothyrsa, commonly known as the large-leaved ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green, elliptical leaves and clusters of bright red or orange flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The species is relatively drought-tolerant once established, making it suitable for tropical and subtropical gardens.
