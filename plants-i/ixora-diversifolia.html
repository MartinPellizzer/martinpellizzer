Ixora diversifolia

Ixora diversifolia, commonly known as the flame bush or red flame, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a striking visual appearance. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Ixora diversifolia is also valued in traditional medicine for its various therapeutic properties.
