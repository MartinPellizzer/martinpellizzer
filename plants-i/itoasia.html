Itoasia

Itoasia, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in a dense layer of fine, silvery hairs. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale blue, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

This plant thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.
