Ipomoea lapathifolia var. dasyclada

Ipomoea lapathifolia var. dasyclada, commonly known as the hairy morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

The plant belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is known for its climbing habit, often twining around supports.
