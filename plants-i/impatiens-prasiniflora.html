Impatiens prasiniflora

Impatiens prasiniflora, commonly known as the green-flowered impatiens, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens prasiniflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
