Iria debilis

Iria debilis, commonly known as the slender iris, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, grass-like foliage and showy, blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sun. Iria debilis is often found in rocky or sandy habitats, and it is known for its ability to bloom in early spring.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and natural landscapes.
