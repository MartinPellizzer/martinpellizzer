Iris tigridia var. fortis

Iris tigridia var. fortis, commonly known as the Mexican flag iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Mexico.

It features striking, bright red or orange flowers with a distinctive black center, resembling a flag. The plant has sword-shaped, lanceolate leaves that are typically blue-green in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often growing in rocky or arid environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is commonly cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
