Ilex laurocensus

Ilex laurocensus, commonly known as the California holly, is a evergreen shrub or small tree native to the coastal regions of California.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, white flowers in the spring, which are followed by bright red berries in the fall. It is well adapted to mild, coastal climates and can tolerate some drought conditions.

Ilex laurocensus is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.
