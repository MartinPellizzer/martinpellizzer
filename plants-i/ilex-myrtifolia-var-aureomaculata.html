Ilex myrtifolia var. aureomaculata

Ilex myrtifolia var. aureomaculata, commonly known as the golden-spotted holly, is a compact evergreen shrub native to southern China.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with distinctive golden or yellow spots, adding visual interest to gardens. The plant produces small, white flowers in spring, which are followed by bright red berries in the fall. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in shaded areas.
