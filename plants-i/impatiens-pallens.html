Impatiens pallens

Impatiens pallens, commonly known as the pale impatiens, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, spurred flowers that typically range in color from white to pale pink. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves and grows as an annual in temperate climates. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens pallens is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
