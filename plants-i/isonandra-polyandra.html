Isonandra polyandra

Isonandra polyandra, commonly known as the golden star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, simple, and usually hairy leaves that are dark green in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Isonandra polyandra is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
