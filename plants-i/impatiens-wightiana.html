Impatiens wightiana

Impatiens wightiana, commonly known as the Wight's impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its attractive, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Impatiens wightiana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
