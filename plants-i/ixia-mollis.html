Ixia mollis

Ixia mollis, commonly known as the Orange-Ixia, is a South African bulbous plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking orange-red flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces inflorescences with clustered, bell-shaped flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Ixia mollis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in floral arrangements.
