Ixora blumei

Ixora blumei, commonly known as the flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of bright orange-red flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually oval or elliptical in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Ixora blumei is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
