Indigofera dimidiata var. laxior

Indigofera dimidiata var. laxior, commonly known as the slender indigo or slender blue bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height, characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a warm, humid climate for optimal growth.
