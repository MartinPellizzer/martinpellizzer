Impatiens laurentii

Impatiens laurentii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is a popular houseplant due to its ease of care and long-lasting blooms.

Impatiens laurentii is also known for its ability to bloom continuously throughout the year under proper conditions.
