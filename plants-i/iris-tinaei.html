Iris tinaei

Iris tinaei, commonly known as the Sierra iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its sword-shaped leaves and showy blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in mountainous regions. It produces a tall flowering stem with several flowers arranged in a raceme.

Iris tinaei is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in its native habitat.
