Ixora × cypheri

Ixora × cypheri, commonly known as the hybrid ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Ixora coccinea and Ixora albiflora. The plant is characterized by its vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom in dense clusters. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a hedge plant.

Ixora × cypheri prefers full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice in tropical and subtropical regions.
