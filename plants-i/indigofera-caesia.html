Indigofera caesia

Indigofera caesia, commonly known as the blue indigo bush, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific.

The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale blue, often clustered in dense inflorescences. Indigofera caesia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
