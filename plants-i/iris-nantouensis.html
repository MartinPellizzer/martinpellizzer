Iris nantouensis

Iris nantouensis, commonly known as Nantou iris, is a species native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or mountainous regions. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves, and it blooms in late spring to early summer.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens.
