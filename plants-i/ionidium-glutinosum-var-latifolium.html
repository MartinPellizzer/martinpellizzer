Ionidium glutinosum var. latifolium

Ionidium glutinosum var. latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf stickyweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a sticky, glandular substance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its unique botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and taxonomic study.
