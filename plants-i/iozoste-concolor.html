Iozoste concolor

Iozoste concolor, commonly known as the smoothleaf iozoste, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to specific regions of South America.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are typically smooth and have a dense, fibrous texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, blooming during the spring and early summer. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to thrive in well-drained soils.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
