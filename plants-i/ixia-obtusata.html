Ixia obtusata

Ixia obtusata, commonly known as the Natal lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, slender stems that bear showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange. The plant typically blooms in late summer to early autumn, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, forming a basal rosette.

Ixia obtusata is well-suited for rock gardens and containers, thriving in full sun and well-drained soil.
