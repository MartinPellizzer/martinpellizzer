Impatiens stenosepala var. stenosepala

Impatiens stenosepala var. stenosepala, commonly known as the narrow-sepaled impatiens, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The leaves are typically ovate with a smooth margin and a glossy surface. This species thrives in shaded to semi-shaded environments and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant blooms and ease of care.
