Impatiens rubricolor

Impatiens rubricolor, commonly known as the red-flowered impatiens, is a species native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers, which bloom in clusters and are typically borne on slender stems. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shade gardens.

Impatiens rubricolor is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
