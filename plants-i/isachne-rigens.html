Isachne rigens

Isachne rigens, commonly known as the rigid isachne, is a perennial grass species native to temperate regions of Asia and Africa.

It typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as marshes, swamps, and along riverbanks. The plant has slender, upright stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often slightly hairy. Its inflorescence consists of a single, dense spikelet that is usually green or reddish in color.

Isachne rigens is notable for its ability to thrive in waterlogged soils and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
