Isonandra utilis

Isonandra utilis, commonly known as the white-veined evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with distinct white veins, which give it its common name. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The botanical features of Isonandra utilis make it a popular ornamental plant in tropical gardens.
