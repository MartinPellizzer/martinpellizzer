Impatiens microsciadia

Impatiens microsciadia, commonly known as the dwarf impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Balsaminaceae and is characterized by its small, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. Its rapid growth and ability to spread make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking vibrant, easy-to-care-for blooms.
