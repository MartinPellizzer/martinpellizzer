Impatiens hemrichii

Impatiens hemrichii, commonly known as the Hemrich's impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, spurred flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and red. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a glossy texture and grows as an upright herbaceous perennial. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections worldwide.
