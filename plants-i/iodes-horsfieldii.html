Iodes horsfieldii

Iodes horsfieldii, commonly known as the Horsfield's lily, is a herbaceous perennial native to the wetlands of Southeast Asia.

It produces striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a strong, sweet fragrance. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for water gardens and bog landscapes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in a fine, silvery down, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Iodes horsfieldii is known for its resilience and ability to bloom reliably in suitable conditions.
