Impatiens oxyanthera

Impatiens oxyanthera, commonly known as the yellow water impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of bright yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. It has a short stem with opposite leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Impatiens oxyanthera is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
