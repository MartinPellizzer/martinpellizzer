Isodon serra

Isodon serra, commonly known as the mountain betony, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically purple or blue in color, arranged in dense spikes. Isodon serra is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun.

It is valued in traditional medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
