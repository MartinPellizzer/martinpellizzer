Ixia spectabilis

Ixia spectabilis, commonly known as the showy ixia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllis family (Amaryllidaceae) and is characterized by its striking, colorful blooms. The plant produces trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often with a distinctive red or orange throat. Ixia spectabilis typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 60 cm.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
