Ilex kelsallii

Ilex kelsallii, commonly known as Kelsall's holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to the southern United States.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, red berries that are an important food source for birds. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or streamside habitats. It has a slow growth rate and can reach heights of up to 15 feet.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in suitable climates.
