Indigofera smutsii

Indigofera smutsii, commonly known as the Natal indigo, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. The plant produces cylindrical pods that contain several seeds, and it is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is adapted to arid conditions and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies of semi-arid regions.
