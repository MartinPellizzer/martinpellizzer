Iberis jordanii

Iberis jordanii, commonly known as Jordan's candytuft, is a flowering plant native to the Middle East and parts of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbages, and is characterized by its small, showy flowers.
