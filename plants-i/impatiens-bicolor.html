Impatiens bicolor

Impatiens bicolor, commonly known as the two-color impatiens, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking bi-coloration, with leaves that are typically dark green and stems that display a vivid red or purple hue. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, often with contrasting veins. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens bicolor is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
