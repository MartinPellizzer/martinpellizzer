Ixora gigantea

Ixora gigantea, commonly known as the giant ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy clusters of orange-red flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has smooth, glossy leaves and can grow up to 10 meters tall in its natural habitat. It thrives in tropical climates and requires well-drained soil and full sun exposure.

Ixora gigantea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
