Ixora flammea

Ixora flammea, commonly known as the flame ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant is characterized by its bright red or orange flowers, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and elliptical in shape, with a glossy green surface.

Ixora flammea is widely cultivated for its ornamental value and is often used in landscaping and floral arrangements.
