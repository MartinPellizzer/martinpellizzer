Isanthera floribunda

Isanthera floribunda, commonly known as the clustered flame tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery orange-red flowers that appear in clusters, typically blooming during the dry season. The plant has compound leaves with elongated leaflets and produces small, round fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental species in tropical gardens.

Its rapid growth and showy blooms make it a valuable addition to landscapes in warm climates.
