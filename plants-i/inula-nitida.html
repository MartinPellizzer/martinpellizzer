Inula nitida

Inula nitida, commonly known as the silverleaf inula, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals, blooming in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Inula nitida is also valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
