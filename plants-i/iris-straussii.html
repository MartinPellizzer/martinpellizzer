Iris straussii

Iris straussii, commonly known as the Strauss iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It features upright, grass-like leaves and produces striking blue to purple flowers with a distinctive three-parted petal structure. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or meadow habitats. It blooms in late spring to early summer, adding vibrant color to its native environment.

Iris straussii is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
