Indigofera duthiei

Indigofera duthiei, commonly known as the African indigo, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant produces seed pods that are typically elongated and contain several seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Due to its ornamental value and traditional uses, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
