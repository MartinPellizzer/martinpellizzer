Isatis stylophora

Isatis stylophora, commonly known as the yellow flag or yellow isatis, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cabbage and mustard. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, and its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture. It often grows in wetlands and moist soils, making it a common sight in marshy areas.

Isatis stylophora is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional medicine for various health purposes.
