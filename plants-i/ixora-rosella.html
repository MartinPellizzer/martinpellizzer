Ixora rosella

Ixora rosella, commonly known as the rose-apple or red hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or orange flowers, which bloom in clusters and are highly attractive to pollinators. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually ovate or elliptic in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Ixora rosella is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a hedge plant.
