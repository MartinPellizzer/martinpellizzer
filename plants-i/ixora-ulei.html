Ixora ulei

Ixora ulei, commonly known as the red ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant produces clusters of bright red flowers, which are showy and attract pollinators such as bees and birds. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a smooth texture.

Ixora ulei is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
