Isotropis argentea

Isotropis argentea, commonly known as silverleaf isotropis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun exposure.

Isotropis argentea is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.
