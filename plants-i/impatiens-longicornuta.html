Impatiens longicornuta

Impatiens longicornuta, commonly known as the longhorn impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy and covered in fine pubescence. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique floral structure and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and collectors.
