Iberis polita

Iberis polita, commonly known as the smooth candytuft, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant has finely divided, silvery-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Iberis polita is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in horticultural settings for its aesthetic appeal.
