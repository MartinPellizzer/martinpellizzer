Impatiens leptopoda

Impatiens leptopoda, commonly known as the slender impatiens, is a herbaceous flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, delicate flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptic in shape, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. This species thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden beds.

Its rapid growth and continuous blooming make it a versatile addition to both wild and cultivated landscapes.
