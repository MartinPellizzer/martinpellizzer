Ipomoea tropica

Ipomoea tropica, commonly known as the tropical morning glory, is a flowering vine native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep purple. The plant is known for its rapid growth and can climb to considerable heights if supported. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Ipomoea tropica is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
