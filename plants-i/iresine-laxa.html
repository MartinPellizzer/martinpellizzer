Iresine laxa

Iresine laxa, commonly known as the red leaf sorrel, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage, which often displays shades of red, purple, and pink. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white in color. Iresine laxa thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-drained soil.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and ease of care.
