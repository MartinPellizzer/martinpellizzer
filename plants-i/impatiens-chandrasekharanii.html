Impatiens chandrasekharanii

Impatiens chandrasekharanii, commonly known as the Chandrasekharanii Impatiens, is a species of flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
