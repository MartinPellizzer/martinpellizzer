Ione jainii

Ione jainii, commonly known as the Sierra Lily, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Liliaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and showy white or pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in forested areas. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it blooms in late spring to early summer.
