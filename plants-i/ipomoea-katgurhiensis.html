Ipomoea katgurhiensis

Ipomoea katgurhiensis, commonly known as the Katgurhi morning glory, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

Ipomoea katgurhiensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
