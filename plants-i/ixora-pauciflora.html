Ixora pauciflora

Ixora pauciflora, commonly known as the sparse-flowered ixora, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clustered, bright orange or red flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Ixora pauciflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
