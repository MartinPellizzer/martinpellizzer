Ixora coriifolia

Ixora coriifolia, commonly known as the flame tree or red flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, fragrant, red or orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a striking visual appearance. The plant has simple, opposite, and often hairy leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often used in ornamental horticulture for its vibrant floral display.

Ixora coriifolia is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
