Ilex pernyi

Ilex pernyi, commonly known as the Chinese holly, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Aquifoliaceae and is characterized by its dark green, oval-shaped leaves that are glossy and often have a waxy coating.
