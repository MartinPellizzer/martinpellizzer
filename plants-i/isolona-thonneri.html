Isolona thonneri

Isolona thonneri, commonly known as the golden star cactus, is a succulent native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It features a compact, spherical stem with a smooth, pale green surface that reflects sunlight. The plant produces yellow flowers in the summer months, which are clustered in a star-like formation. Its leaves are small and spiny, adapted to minimize water loss in its harsh environment.

Isolona thonneri is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in low-water conditions.
