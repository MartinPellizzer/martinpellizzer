Iris tubergeniana

Iris tubergeniana, commonly known as the Turkish iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, grass-like foliage and produces striking, showy flowers with three upright petals and three drooping sepals. The flowers typically bloom in late spring to early summer, showcasing a range of colors from deep blues to purples. The plant prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is complemented by its ability to thrive in a variety of climates.
