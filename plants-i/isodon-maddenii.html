Isodon maddenii

Isodon maddenii, commonly known as the slender leaved gentian, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Gentianaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, thriving in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered with a fine layer of hair.

Isodon maddenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
