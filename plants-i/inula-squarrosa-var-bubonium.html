Inula squarrosa var. bubonium

Inula squarrosa var. bubonium, commonly known as the rough fleabane, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and grayish, woolly foliage. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It has a taproot system and produces seeds in small, dry fruits.

The species is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its distinctive appearance.
