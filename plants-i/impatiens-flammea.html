Impatiens flammea

Impatiens flammea, commonly known as the flame impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant red-orange flowers, which bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Impatiens flammea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
