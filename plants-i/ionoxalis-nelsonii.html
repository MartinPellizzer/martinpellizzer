Ionoxalis nelsonii

Ionoxalis nelsonii, commonly known as the Nelson's wood sorrel, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and small, delicate yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It belongs to the Oxalidaceae family, which is characterized by its distinctive leaf structure and calcium oxalate crystals.
