Ionidium fruticulosum

Ionidium fruticulosum, commonly known as the coral tree, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, resembling coral in appearance. The plant has a woody stem with thorn-like projections, which serve as a defense mechanism against herbivores. Its leaves are small, opposite, and often have a velvety texture.

Ionidium fruticulosum thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.
