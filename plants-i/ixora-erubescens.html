Ixora erubescens

Ixora erubescens, commonly known as the red flame ixora, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of bright red, tubular flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are dark green in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Ixora erubescens is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to attract birds and insects.
