Itea chinensis var. subserrata

Itea chinensis var. subserrata, commonly known as the Chinese spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features slender, arching stems that bear clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers in early summer. The leaves are ovate to elliptic, with finely serrated margins that give it a delicate appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to native plant landscapes and wetland restoration projects.
