Ixora peruviana

Ixora peruviana, commonly known as the Peruvian flame bush, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of bright red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often glossy and dark green in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Ixora peruviana is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and birds.
