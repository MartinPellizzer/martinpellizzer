Indigofera hislopii

Indigofera hislopii, commonly known as the Hawaiian indigo, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive blue or violet flowers. The plant is typically shrub-like, with woody stems and small, trifoliate leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland habitats.
