Ixora undulata var. maculata

Ixora undulata var. maculata, commonly known as the spotted flame bush, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a striking visual display. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a glossy surface. This plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping in tropical regions.
