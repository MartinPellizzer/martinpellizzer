Ixora kerstingii

Ixora kerstingii, commonly known as the Kersting's flame bush, is a tropical shrub native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its clusters of bright red, tubular flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for coastal and tropical gardens.

Ixora kerstingii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like birds and insects.
