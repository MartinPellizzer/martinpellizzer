Impatiens humblotiana var. onibeensis

Impatiens humblotiana var. onibeensis, commonly known as the Onibe Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of western Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hair, giving it a unique texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found growing near the forest floor.

Due to its striking appearance and rarity, it is a subject of interest for botanists and horticulturists alike.
