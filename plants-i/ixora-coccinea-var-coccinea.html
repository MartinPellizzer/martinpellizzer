Ixora coccinea var. coccinea

Ixora coccinea var. coccinea, commonly known as the flame red ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bright red, clustered flowers that bloom throughout the year in warm climates. The plant has small, glossy, dark green leaves and a shrub-like growth habit. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Its vibrant flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, contributing to its ecological value.
