Ilex tsangii var. tsangii

Ilex tsangii var. tsangii, commonly known as the Yunnan holly, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter beneath. The plant produces small, red berries that are toxic to humans but serve as a food source for birds. It thrives in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in regions with mild winters.
