Ixora × superba

Ixora × superba, commonly known as the Chinese hibiscus, is a hybrid shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Ixora × superba is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a container plant.
