Impatiens erubescens

Impatiens erubescens, commonly known as the red touch-me-not, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or orange flowers, which are typically borne on slender, erect stems. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens erubescens is a popular ornamental plant due to its striking color and ease of cultivation.
