Impatiens nobilis

Impatiens nobilis, commonly known as the noble impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically grows as an erect herb with opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

The species is also notable for its rapid growth and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
