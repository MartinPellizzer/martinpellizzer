Isachne apoensis

Isachne apoensis, commonly known as the Apo grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to the Philippines, specifically found on the island of Luzon. The plant is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, blade-like leaves. It produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are typically green or pale green in color.

Isachne apoensis is often found in moist, shaded areas such as forests and along stream banks.
