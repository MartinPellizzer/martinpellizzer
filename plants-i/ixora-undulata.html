Ixora undulata

Ixora undulata, commonly known as the wavy ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of bright orange or red flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are often wavy or undulating in appearance, giving it its common name. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Ixora undulata is also valued for its ability to tolerate partial shade and its relatively low maintenance requirements.
