Ixora carewii

Ixora carewii, commonly known as the scarlet ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, featuring dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces clusters of bright red, tubular flowers that bloom throughout the year, making it a popular ornamental species. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a glossy texture, while its stems are woody and often branched.

Ixora carewii thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sun exposure.
