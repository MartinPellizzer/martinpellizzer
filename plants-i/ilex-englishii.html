Ilex englishii

Ilex englishii, commonly known as the English holly, is a evergreen shrub or small tree native to western Europe.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves that are typically spiny and arranged in opposite pairs. The plant produces small, red berries that are toxic to humans and animals. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Ilex englishii is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
