Isachne miliacea var. javanica

Isachne miliacea var. javanica, commonly known as Java milletgrass, is a grass species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial plant characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in moist, tropical environments and is often found in wetlands or along the edges of water bodies.

Its botanical features make it an important component of aquatic and riparian ecosystems.
