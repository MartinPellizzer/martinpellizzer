Iridorkis pachystachya

Iridorkis pachystachya, commonly known as the giant feather grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and long, feathery plumes that create a dramatic visual impact in gardens. The plant prefers full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for a variety of climates. It is known for its ability to thrive in dry conditions and its minimal maintenance requirements.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for landscaping and floral arrangements.
