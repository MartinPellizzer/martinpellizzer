Ixia hirta

Ixia hirta, commonly known as the woolly ixia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllis family (Amaryllidaceae) and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are covered in fine, hairy textures.

Ixia hirta is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a popular choice in gardens and floral arrangements.
