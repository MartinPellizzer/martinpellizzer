Impatiens pathakiana

Impatiens pathakiana, commonly known as the Himalayan impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has heart-shaped leaves. It belongs to the family Balsaminaceae and is often found in forest understories.
