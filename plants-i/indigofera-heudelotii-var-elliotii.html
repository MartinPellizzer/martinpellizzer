Indigofera heudelotii var. elliotii

Indigofera heudelotii var. elliotii, commonly known as Elliot's indigo, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and clusters of small, pea-like flowers. The plant produces pods that contain seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological and agricultural contexts.
