Iris elephantina

Iris elephantina, commonly known as the giant iris, is a striking perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy blue or violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features broad, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value and unique appearance make it a notable species in the iris family.
