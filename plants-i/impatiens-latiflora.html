Impatiens latiflora

Impatiens latiflora, commonly known as the broadleaf impatiens, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It features vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, often with a distinctive yellow or orange throat. The plant has opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are typically broad and glossy. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden beds.

Its rapid growth and ability to bloom throughout the growing season contribute to its ornamental value.
