Iris stylosa

Iris stylosa, commonly known as the slender iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or grassland habitats. The plant features upright, grass-like leaves and produces showy blue to violet flowers with three upright petals. Its flowering period occurs in late spring to early summer.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking blooms.
