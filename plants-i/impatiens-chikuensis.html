Impatiens chikuensis

Impatiens chikuensis, commonly known as the Chiku Mountain Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens chikuensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
