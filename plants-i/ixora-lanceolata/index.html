Ixora Lanceolata

Ixora lanceolata is a striking evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of Asia and the Pacific. Known for its vibrant clusters of tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, it adds a bold splash of color to gardens and landscapes. This plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is often used in ornamental horticulture for its long-lasting blooms. Its adaptability to various soil types makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation. With its lush foliage and showy flowers, Ixora lanceolata is a favorite among plant enthusiasts and garden designers alike.
