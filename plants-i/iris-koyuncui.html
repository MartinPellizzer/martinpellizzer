Iris koyuncui

Iris koyuncui, commonly known as the Turkish iris, is a species of flowering plant native to Turkey and the Caucasus region.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers with a distinctive yellow center. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense clumps over time.

Iris koyuncui is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
