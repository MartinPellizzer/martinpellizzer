Indigofera fulgens subsp. fulgens

Indigofera fulgens subsp.

fulgens, commonly known as the flame indigo, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa. It is characterized by its striking, bright red to orange-red flowers that bloom in dense clusters.

The plant has compound leaves with small, ovate leaflets and produces small, pod-like fruits containing several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental gardening.

Its vibrant floral display makes it a popular choice for tropical gardens and floral arrangements.
