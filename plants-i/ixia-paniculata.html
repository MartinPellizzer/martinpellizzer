Ixia paniculata

Ixia paniculata, commonly known as the African daffodil, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors, including yellow, orange, and red. Ixia paniculata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
