Ilex tamii

Ilex tamii, commonly known as the Chinese holly, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically ovate or elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, white flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries that are attractive to birds. Ilex tamii thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a suitable ornamental plant in gardens.

Its evergreen-like appearance and ornamental berries make it a popular choice for landscaping.
