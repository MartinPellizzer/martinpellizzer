Indigofera charlieriana var. lata

Indigofera charlieriana var. lata, commonly known as the broadleaf indigo.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is native to tropical regions of South America. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
