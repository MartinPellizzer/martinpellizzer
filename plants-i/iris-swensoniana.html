Iris swensoniana

Iris swensoniana, commonly known as Swenson's iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or meadow habitats. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy, three-petaled blooms in the spring.

Swenson's iris is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
