Iberis linifolia f. acutiloba

Iberis linifolia f. acutiloba, commonly known as the sharp-lobed candytuft, is a compact, mat-forming perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It features dense clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The leaves are narrow, linear, and have sharp, pointed lobes, giving the plant its characteristic appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in colder climates.
