Iochroma solanifolium

Iochroma solanifolium, commonly known as the silverleaf bellflower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple. Iochroma solanifolium is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and vibrant blooms.
