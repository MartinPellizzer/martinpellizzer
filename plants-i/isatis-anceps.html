Isatis anceps

Isatis anceps, commonly known as the Chinese yellow flag, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like mustard and cabbage. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, adding vibrant color to gardens. Its leaves are alternate, lobed, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Isatis anceps is also valued for its medicinal properties, traditionally used in herbal remedies.
