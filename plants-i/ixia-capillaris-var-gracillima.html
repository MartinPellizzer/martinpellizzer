Ixia capillaris var. gracillima

Ixia capillaris var. gracillima, commonly known as the slender flame lily, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, slender stems that bear clusters of vibrant orange-red tubular flowers, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette at the base, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and unique, flame-like flower shape.
