Ilex castaneifolia

Ilex castaneifolia, commonly known as the Chinese holly, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often used in ornamental landscaping.

Ilex castaneifolia is also valued for its ability to provide habitat and food for wildlife.
