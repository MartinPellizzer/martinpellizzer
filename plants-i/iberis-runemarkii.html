Iberis runemarkii

Iberis runemarkii, commonly known as the Armenian candytuft, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Armenia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many flowering plants known for their cruciform flowers. The plant features compact, mat-forming growth with bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are pinnately divided, and the plant is adapted to rocky and well-drained soils.

Iberis runemarkii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens and alpine settings.
