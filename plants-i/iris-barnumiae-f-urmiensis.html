Iris barnumiae f. urmiensis

Iris barnumiae f. urmiensis, commonly known as the Urmiensis iris, is a botanical variant of the species Iris barnumiae.

It is characterized by its striking violet-blue flowers with a distinctive white stripe along the lower petals. Native to the mountainous regions of Iran, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure. The foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a dense rosette.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after addition to rock gardens and alpine collections.
