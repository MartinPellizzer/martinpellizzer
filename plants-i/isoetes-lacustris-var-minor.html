Isoetes lacustris var. minor

Isoetes lacustris var. minor, commonly known as the lesser watermace, is a species of aquatic plant belonging to the order Isoetales.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green, ribbon-like leaves that grow in a tight spiral around the stem. This variety is typically found in freshwater habitats such as lakes, ponds, and slow-moving streams. The plant has a rhizome that anchors it in the substrate and supports its growth.

It plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and oxygenation for aquatic life.
