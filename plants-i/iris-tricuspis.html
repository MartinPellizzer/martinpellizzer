Iris tricuspis

Iris tricuspis, commonly known as the three-lobed iris, is a species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features striking blue or violet flowers with three petals, each divided into three lobes, giving it its common name. Its leaves are sword-shaped and grass-like, growing in dense clumps.

The iris thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
