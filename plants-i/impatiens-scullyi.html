Impatiens scullyi

Impatiens scullyi, commonly known as the Scully's Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a glossy surface and grows as an upright herbaceous perennial. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden areas.

Impatiens scullyi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in both garden and horticultural settings.
