Ischnea capellana

Ischnea capellana, commonly known as the Cape grass, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, grass-like leaves. It produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that bloom in clusters.

Ischnea capellana is often used in ornamental gardening due to its hardy nature and ability to thrive in coastal conditions.
