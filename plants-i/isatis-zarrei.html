Isatis zarrei

Isatis zarrei, commonly known as the narrow-leaved woad, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cabbage and mustard. The plant produces small, blue or purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Isatis zarrei is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides, making it a common sight in arid and semi-arid regions.
