Ilex fletcheri

Ilex fletcheri, commonly known as the Fletcher's holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to the coastal regions of southern California.

It is characterized by its dense, glossy dark green leaves and small, red berries that are often used by birds as a food source. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to the Mediterranean climate of its native habitat. It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate coastal conditions.

Ilex fletcheri plays an important ecological role in supporting local wildlife and stabilizing coastal dunes.
