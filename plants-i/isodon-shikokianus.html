Isodon shikokianus

Isodon shikokianus, commonly known as the Japanese mosquito plant, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically blue to purple in color, arranged in whorled clusters at the ends of the stems.

Isodon shikokianus is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
