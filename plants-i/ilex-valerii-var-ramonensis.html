Ilex valerii var. ramonensis

Ilex valerii var. ramonensis, commonly known as the Ramonese holly, is a shrub or small tree native to the Caribbean, particularly found in the Greater Antilles.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage, with dark green, glossy leaves that are typically oval in shape. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in moist, shaded areas within forests.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
