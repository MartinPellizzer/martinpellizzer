Ixora bartlingii

Ixora bartlingii, commonly known as the red flame ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of vibrant red, orange, or yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are glossy and arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Ixora bartlingii is also valued for its ability to produce fragrant blooms, making it a popular choice in tropical gardens.
