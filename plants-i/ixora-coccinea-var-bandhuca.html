Ixora coccinea var. bandhuca

Ixora coccinea var. bandhuca, commonly known as the red flame ixora, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a striking visual appeal to gardens. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its showy blooms. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in landscaping and floral arrangements.
