Impatiens rubrostriata

Impatiens rubrostriata, commonly known as the red-striated impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers with contrasting white or yellow markings, which are typically borne on slender, hairy stems. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens rubrostriata is valued in horticulture for its ability to add color to shaded garden spaces.
