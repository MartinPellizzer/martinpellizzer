Isachne dubia

Isachne dubia, commonly known as the slender grass, is a species of grass native to various regions of Asia and Africa.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as wet meadows and stream banks. The plant has slender, upright stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its inflorescence consists of a single spikelet, which is usually short and compact.

Isachne dubia is characterized by its delicate appearance and is often found in disturbed or wetland environments.
