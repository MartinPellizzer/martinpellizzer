Ipomoea ornithopoda

Ipomoea ornithopoda, commonly known as the silverleaf morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and striking blue or purple flowers that bloom in the morning. The plant belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is known for its rapid growth and climbing habit. It thrives in warm climates with well-drained soil and full sun exposure.
