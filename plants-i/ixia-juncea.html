Ixia juncea

Ixia juncea, commonly known as the Orange Rain Lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking orange or yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces narrow, strap-like leaves that are typically bright green. Ixia juncea thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

It is also known for its ability to bloom even in cooler climates, adding vibrant color to gardens during the early part of the growing season.
