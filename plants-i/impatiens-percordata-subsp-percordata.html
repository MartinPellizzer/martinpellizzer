Impatiens percordata subsp. percordata

Impatiens percordata subsp.

percordata, commonly known as the scarlet ice plant, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East. It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and profuse display of bright red, spurred flowers.

The plant features fleshy, succulent stems and leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
