Impatiens silvestrii

Impatiens silvestrii, commonly known as the forest impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the genus Impatiens, which is part of the family Balsaminaceae. This species is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically come in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant grows as an erect herb with opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
