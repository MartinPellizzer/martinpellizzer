Ixora himantophylla

Ixora himantophylla, commonly known as the flame tree or red flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, clustered inflorescences of bright red, orange, or yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has simple, opposite, and often hairy leaves that are elliptical to ovate in shape. Its woody, branched stems are usually covered with a rough, flaky bark.

Ixora himantophylla is widely cultivated for its ornamental value and is often used in landscaping due to its vibrant floral display.
