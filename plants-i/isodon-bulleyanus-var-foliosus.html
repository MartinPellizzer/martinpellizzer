Isodon bulleyanus var. foliosus

Isodon bulleyanus var. foliosus, commonly known as the broad-leaved isodon, is a perennial herb native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants known for their medicinal properties. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a rough texture and a distinct, aromatic scent. Its flowers are typically purple or blue, arranged in whorled clusters at the ends of the stems.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and traditional use in herbal medicine.
