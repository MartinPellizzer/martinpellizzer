Ixora lobbii var. angustifolia

Ixora lobbii var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf ixora, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, fragrant flowers that are typically a vibrant red or orange-red in color, arranged in clusters at the ends of its branches. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are opposite in arrangement, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
