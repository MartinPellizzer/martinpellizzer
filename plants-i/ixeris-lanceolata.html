Ixeris lanceolata

Ixeris lanceolata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf sunflower or golden groundsel, is a flowering plant native to Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow ray florets and green, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to spread easily.

Ixeris lanceolata is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
