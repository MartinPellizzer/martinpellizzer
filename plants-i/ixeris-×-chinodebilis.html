Ixeris × chinodebilis

Ixeris × chinodebilis, commonly known as the Chinese dandelion, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit with bright yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant features soft, hairy leaves that are usually alternate and covered in fine pubescence. It is native to China and is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and roadsides.

Ixeris × chinodebilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
