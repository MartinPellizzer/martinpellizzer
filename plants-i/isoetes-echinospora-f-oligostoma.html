Isoetes echinospora f. oligostoma

Isoetes echinospora f. oligostoma, commonly known as the spiny-stemmed water fern, is a unique aquatic plant belonging to the genus Isoetes.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spiny spore-bearing stems that are often submerged in water. The plant exhibits a specialized morphology with elongated, ribbon-like leaves that help it float and absorb nutrients from the water. It is typically found in freshwater environments such as ponds and lakes, where it plays a role in aquatic ecosystems.

Its reproductive structures are adapted for dispersal via water, contributing to its ecological significance.
