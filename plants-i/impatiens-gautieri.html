Impatiens gautieri

Impatiens gautieri, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Balsaminaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, dark green foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, white, and purple. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial shade.
