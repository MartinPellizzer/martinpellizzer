Iresine spiculigera var. pauciglandulosa

Iresine spiculigera var. pauciglandulosa, commonly known as the spiny leaf irene, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, often red or purple, foliage that is densely covered with small, spiny protrusions. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-drained soil.

Due to its unique appearance, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in tropical and subtropical gardens.
