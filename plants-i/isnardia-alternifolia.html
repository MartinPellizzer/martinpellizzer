Isnardia alternifolia

Isnardia alternifolia, commonly known as the alternate leaf fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its alternate arrangement of lance-shaped leaves that are often deeply lobed. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests or rocky crevices. Its fronds are usually delicate and can vary in size depending on the growing conditions.
