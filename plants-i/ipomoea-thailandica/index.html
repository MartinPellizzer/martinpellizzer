Ipomoea Thailandica

Ipomoea thailandica is a striking flowering plant native to Thailand and parts of Southeast Asia. Known for its vibrant purple and pink flowers, it adds a splash of color to gardens and natural landscapes. This species belongs to the Convolvulaceae family, which includes many climbing and trailing plants. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found near water sources. Its beauty and adaptability make it a popular choice for both ornamental and ecological purposes.
