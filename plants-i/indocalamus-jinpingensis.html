Indocalamus jinpingensis

Indocalamus jinpingensis, commonly known as the Jinping bamboo, is a species of grass in the family Poaceae.

It is native to China, particularly found in the Yunnan Province. This plant is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can reach heights of up to 10 meters. The leaves are long and narrow, with a smooth, waxy surface.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.
