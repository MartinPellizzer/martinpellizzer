Iris probstii

Iris probstii, commonly known as the Probst iris, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features striking blue to violet flowers with three upright petals and three drooping sepals. Its rhizomatous root system allows it to thrive in well-drained, rocky soils.

Iris probstii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
