Ilex aquifolium var. ciliata-major

Ilex aquifolium var. ciliata-major, commonly known as the European holly with ciliate margins, is a variant of the European holly characterized by its distinctive leaf morphology.

Its leaves are typically ovate to elliptical, with finely serrated margins that are often covered in soft, ciliate hairs. The plant is evergreen, featuring dark glossy green foliage that provides year-round visual interest. It produces small, red berries that are attractive to birds, contributing to its ecological role.

This variety is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and landscapes due to its aesthetic appeal and hardy nature.
