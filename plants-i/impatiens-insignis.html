Impatiens insignis

Impatiens insignis, commonly known as the Sierra Nevada impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

It is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments. It is a herbaceous perennial that produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds.

Impatiens insignis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
