Ixeridium chinense

Ixeridium chinense, commonly known as Chinese goldenrod, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow-orange inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist environments such as marshes and along riverbanks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and often branched.

Ixeridium chinense is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
