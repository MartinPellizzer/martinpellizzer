Iris × kochii

Iris × kochii, commonly known as the hybrid bearded iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features tall, upright stems that bear showy, often violet or blue-purple flowers with prominent beards on the petals. The plant has lance-shaped, grass-like leaves that are typically dark green and arranged in a fan-like pattern. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to both ornamental and ecological landscapes.
