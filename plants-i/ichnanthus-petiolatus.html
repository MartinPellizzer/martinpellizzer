Ichnanthus petiolatus

Ichnanthus petiolatus, commonly known as the slender leaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are often clustered in racemes. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is typically found in forest undergrowth.

Due to its delicate appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and specialized species.
