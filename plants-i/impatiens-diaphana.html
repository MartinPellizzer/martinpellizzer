Impatiens diaphana

Impatiens diaphana, commonly known as the ghost impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in moist forest understories.

Its unique coloration and ethereal appearance make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
