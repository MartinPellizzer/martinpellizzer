Isachne langkawiensis

Isachne langkawiensis, commonly known as the Langkawi grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to the Malaysian island of Langkawi and is adapted to the tropical climate of the region. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and forest edges. Its foliage is characterized by narrow, linear leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets.

Isachne langkawiensis plays a role in local ecosystems and is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
