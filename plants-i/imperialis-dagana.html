Imperialis dagana

It features vibrant orange and red petals that resemble flames, making it a visually stunning species. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with each stem producing a cluster of showy flowers. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, adapted to capture maximum sunlight in its mountainous habitat.
