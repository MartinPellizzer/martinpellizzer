Impatiens pyrrhotricha

Impatiens pyrrhotricha, commonly known as the red-eyed impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep red. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are usually covered in fine, soft hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens pyrrhotricha is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
