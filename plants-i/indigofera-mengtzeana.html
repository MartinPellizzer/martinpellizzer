Indigofera mengtzeana

Indigofera mengtzeana, commonly known as the Mengtzean indigo plant, is a flowering plant species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive floral structures. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and potential medicinal applications.
