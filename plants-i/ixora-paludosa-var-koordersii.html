Ixora paludosa var. koordersii

Ixora paludosa var. koordersii, commonly known as the swamp ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of bright red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant typically grows in wet, swampy areas and has dark green, glossy leaves. It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in moist conditions.

Its botanical features make it well-suited for wetland restoration projects and garden landscapes.
