Iris histrio var. aintabensis

Iris histrio var. aintabensis, commonly known as the Aintab iris, is a striking perennial plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and vibrant blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils in mountainous regions. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves, and it blooms in late spring to early summer.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
