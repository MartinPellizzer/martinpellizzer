Icica hedwigia

Icica hedwigia, commonly known as the Hedwig's ice plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, adapting to arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
