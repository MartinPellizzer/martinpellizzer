Ixora jaherii

Ixora jaherii, commonly known as the red flame ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant is characterized by its clusters of bright red flowers that bloom throughout the year, particularly in the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and elliptical in shape, with a smooth surface.

Ixora jaherii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a hedge due to its dense growth habit and vibrant floral display.
