Iris italica var. cengialti

Iris italica var. cengialti, commonly known as the Cengialti iris, is a striking ornamental plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features tall, slender stems that bear striking blue to violet flowers with a distinctive white or pale yellow center. The plant has lance-shaped, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to both horticultural collections and ecological habitats.
