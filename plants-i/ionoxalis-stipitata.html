Ionoxalis stipitata

Ionoxalis stipitata, commonly known as the purple-leaf sorrel, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South America.

It features trifoliate leaves with a distinctive purple hue, and its flowers are typically white or pale pink, arranged in umbels. The plant has a shallow root system and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has a mild, sour flavor in its leaves.

Ionoxalis stipitata is relatively easy to grow and can be used in shady garden settings.
