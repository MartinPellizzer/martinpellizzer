Ipomoea blinkworthii

Ipomoea blinkworthii, commonly known as the Blinkworth's morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep purple. The plant belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is known for its rapid growth and climbing habit. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often have a velvety texture.
