Ipomoea dumetorum var. glaucescens

Ipomoea dumetorum var. glaucescens, commonly known as morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its vigorous climbing habit and heart-shaped leaves. The flowers are typically trumpet-shaped, with vibrant colors ranging from blue to purple. The plant produces small, round berries that contain toxic seeds.

It is widely cultivated for its ornamental value but requires careful handling due to its psychoactive properties.
