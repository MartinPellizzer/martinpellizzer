Ilex asiatica

Ilex asiatica, commonly known as the Chinese holly, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny leaves and bright red berries that are highly ornamental. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full shade. Its evergreen foliage provides year-round visual interest in gardens.

Ilex asiatica is often used in landscaping for its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.
