Ixora conferta var. minor

Ixora conferta var. minor, commonly known as the small-leaf ixora, is a compact shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, ovate leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces clusters of bright orange-red flowers, which bloom throughout the year in warm climates. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a hedge plant.
