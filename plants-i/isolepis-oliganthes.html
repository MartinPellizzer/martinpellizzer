Isolepis oliganthes

Isolepis oliganthes, commonly known as the slender spike-rush, is a perennial grass-like plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, cylindrical inflorescence composed of numerous small, spike-like clusters of flowers. The plant typically grows in wet or muddy habitats, such as marshes, swamps, and the edges of ponds. Its leaves are narrow, grass-like, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Isolepis oliganthes plays a role in stabilizing soil in wetland environments and provides habitat for various invertebrates.
