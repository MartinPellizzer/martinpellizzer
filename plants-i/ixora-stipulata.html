Ixora stipulata

Ixora stipulata, commonly known as the red-rooted ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, clustered inflorescences of bright red or orange-red flowers. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with prominent veins and stipules, giving it its scientific name. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in shaded or semi-shaded areas.

Ixora stipulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticulture for its vibrant blooms.
