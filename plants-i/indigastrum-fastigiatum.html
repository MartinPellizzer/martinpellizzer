Indigastrum fastigiatum

Indigastrum fastigiatum, commonly known as the slender indigo.

It is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America, characterized by its striking blue-violet flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid forests.

Its unique coloration makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
