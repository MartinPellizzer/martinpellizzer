Impatiens neomunronii

Impatiens neomunronii, commonly known as the New Munroon Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red, with five petals arranged in a symmetrical pattern. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are usually arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses worldwide.
