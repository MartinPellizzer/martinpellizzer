Ipomoea schrankii

Ipomoea schrankii, commonly known as the scarlet morning glory, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant is a climbing vine with heart-shaped leaves and produces showy blooms that open in the morning. It thrives in warm, sunny environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Ipomoea schrankii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
