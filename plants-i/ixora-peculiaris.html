Ixora peculiaris

Ixora peculiaris, commonly known as the velvet tamarind, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and clusters of small, bright red flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant produces small, round fruits that turn from green to yellow as they mature. Ixora peculiaris thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking both aesthetic and ecological benefits.
