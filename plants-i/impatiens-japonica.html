Impatiens japonica

Impatiens japonica, commonly known as Japanese Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in a range of colors, including white, pink, and red. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves and thrives in moist, well-drained soil. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens and landscapes.

Impatiens japonica is also valued for its ability to bloom throughout the growing season, making it a popular choice for shade gardens.
