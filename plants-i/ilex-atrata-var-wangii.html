Ilex atrata var. wangii

Ilex atrata var. wangii, commonly known as the Wangii holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to southern China.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for ornamental gardening.

The Wangii holly is valued for its dense foliage and year-round aesthetic appeal.
