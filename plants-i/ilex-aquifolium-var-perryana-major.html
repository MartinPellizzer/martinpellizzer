Ilex aquifolium var. perryana-major

Ilex aquifolium var. perryana-major, commonly known as the Perryana-major holly, is a variant of the common holly, characterized by its distinctive foliage.

It features dark green, glossy leaves with serrated edges and a striking red or orange berry clusters in the winter. The plant is evergreen, retaining its foliage year-round, and thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full shade. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and hardy nature.

This variety is valued for its compact growth habit and adaptability to various garden settings.
