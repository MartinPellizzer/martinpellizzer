Impatiens trichosperma

Impatiens trichosperma, commonly known as the slender touch-me-not, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Balsaminaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, spurred flowers that range in color from white to pink. Its seeds are enclosed in a capsule that splits open when mature, dispersing the seeds forcefully.

Impatiens trichosperma is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
