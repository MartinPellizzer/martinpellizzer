Ilex krugiana f. paciportensis

Ilex krugiana f. paciportensis, commonly known as the Paciportensis holly, is a rare and endemic plant species native to the Philippines.

It is a evergreen shrub or small tree characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant produces bright red berries that are an important food source for local wildlife. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in montane forests.
