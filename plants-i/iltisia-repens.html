Iltisia repens

Iltisia repens, commonly known as creeping iltisia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of less than 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, often white or yellow in color. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and elliptical in shape.

Iltisia repens is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
