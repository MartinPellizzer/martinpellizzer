Indigofera trifoliata var. zhengkangensis

Indigofera trifoliata var. zhengkangensis, commonly known as the Zhengkang indigo plant, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, which makes it beneficial for soil enrichment.

It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forested areas or along stream banks.
