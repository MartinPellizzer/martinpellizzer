Ilex retusa f. glaucescens

Ilex retusa f. glaucescens, commonly known as the glossy ilex, is a compact evergreen shrub or small tree native to the southeastern United States.

It features elliptical, dark green leaves that are glossy and often have a waxy coating, contributing to its ornamental appeal. The plant produces small, bright red berries that are an important food source for wildlife. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sun, making it suitable for shaded garden spaces.

Its dense foliage and winter interest make it a valuable addition to landscapes seeking year-round beauty.
