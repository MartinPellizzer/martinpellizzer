Iris eggeri

Iris eggeri, commonly known as the New Zealand marsh iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to New Zealand.

It features striking blue to purple flowers with a distinctive three-lobed petal structure. The plant has a rhizomatous root system and grows in wetland or marshy environments. Its leaves are sword-shaped and often have a waxy coating.

Iris eggeri is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in its native habitat.
