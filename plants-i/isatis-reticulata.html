Isatis reticulata

Isatis reticulata, commonly known as Chinese yellow root, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cabbage and mustard. The plant is characterized by its yellow roots, which are rich in compounds such as isatin and various alkaloids. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Isatis reticulata is cultivated for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for centuries.
