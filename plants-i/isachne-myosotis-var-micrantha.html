Isachne myosotis var. micrantha

Isachne myosotis var. micrantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved forget-me-not, is a flowering plant belonging to the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender stems and small, blue or purple flowers that bloom in early summer. The leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture. Native to regions of Europe and Asia, it thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

This variant is distinguished by its smaller flower size compared to other species in the genus.
