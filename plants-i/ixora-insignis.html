Ixora insignis

Ixora insignis, commonly known as the flame-of-the-forest, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its clusters of bright red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and elliptical in shape.

Ixora insignis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
