Ipomoea ionantha

Ipomoea ionantha, commonly known as the Hawaiian morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a herbaceous vine that produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that open in the early morning. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is characterized by its rapid growth habit. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded areas.

Ipomoea ionantha is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its attractive blooms.
