Isoloma purpureum

Isoloma purpureum, commonly known as the purple isoloma, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, while its stems are erect and branched.
