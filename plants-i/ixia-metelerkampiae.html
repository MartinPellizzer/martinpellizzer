Ixia metelerkampiae

Ixia metelerkampiae, commonly known as the Orange Kaffir lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant orange flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant features strap-like, grassy leaves that emerge from a bulb and are often covered in a waxy coating. Ixia metelerkampiae thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its unique appearance and seasonal blooming make it a favorite among gardeners and botanists alike.
