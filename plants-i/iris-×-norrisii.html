Iris × norrisii

Iris × norrisii, commonly known as the Norris iris, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Iris germanica and Iris pallida.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and prefers well-drained soil in full sun. Its foliage consists of sword-shaped leaves that are dark green and slightly hairy.

The Norris iris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
