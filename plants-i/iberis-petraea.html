Iberis petraea

Iberis petraea, commonly known as rock candy or rock白菜, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, white, four-petaled flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has a mat-forming growth habit with fleshy, succulent leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is drought-tolerant.
