Ixia filifolia

Ixia filifolia, commonly known as the featherleaf ixia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage. The plant produces striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Ixia filifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in mild climates.
