Impatiens mildbraedii subsp. mildbraedii

Impatiens mildbraedii subsp.

mildbraedii, commonly known as the Malawi impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the highlands of Malawi. It is a herbaceous perennial with upright, branched stems that bear clusters of small, showy flowers.

The flowers are typically pink or red, with five petals and a spurred structure at the base. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and has hairy leaves that help reduce water loss.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
