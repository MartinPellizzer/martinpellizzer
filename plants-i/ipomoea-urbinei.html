Ipomoea urbinei

Ipomoea urbinei, commonly known as the Turban Snailvine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a climbing vine that produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of pink and purple. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface, and its stems are often covered in fine, white pubescence. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded areas in the wild.

The flowers bloom throughout the growing season, attracting various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
