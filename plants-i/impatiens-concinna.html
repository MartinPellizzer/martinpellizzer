Impatiens concinna

Impatiens concinna, commonly known as the dwarf impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.

Impatiens concinna is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to add color to shaded garden spaces.
