Isopogon buxifolius var. spathulatus

Isopogon buxifolius var. spathulatus, commonly known as the broad-leafed isopogon, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features dense, evergreen foliage with lanceolate to ovate leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers in late winter to early spring, which are clustered in cylindrical inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and sandy environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in Mediterranean climates.
