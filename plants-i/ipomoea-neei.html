Ipomoea neei

Ipomoea neei, commonly known as the Neei morning glory, is a climbing vine native to South America.

It produces striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in the morning, hence its common name. The plant has heart-shaped, velvety leaves and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family, which includes many other flowering plants.

Ipomoea neei is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
