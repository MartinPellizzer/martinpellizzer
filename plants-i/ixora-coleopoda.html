Ixora coleopoda

Ixora coleopoda, commonly known as the redroot ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of bright red flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in whorls around the stems. Its roots are fibrous and often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Ixora coleopoda is well adapted to tropical climates and thrives in well-drained soil.
