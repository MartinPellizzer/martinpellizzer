Ilex × hendersonii

Ilex × hendersonii, commonly known as the Henderson holly, is a hybrid evergreen shrub or small tree native to the southeastern United States.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are typically elliptical in shape and have spiny margins. The plant produces small, bright red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal, it is often used in hedges and as a specimen plant in gardens.
