Ixora stricta var. mekongensis

Ixora stricta var. mekongensis, commonly known as the Mekong ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant produces clusters of bright red or orange-red flowers, which are borne on long, slender inflorescences. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a smooth surface with a slightly waxy texture.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
