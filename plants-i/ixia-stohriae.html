Ixia stohriae

Ixia stohriae, commonly known as the red-hot poker, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, torch-like inflorescences. Ixia stohriae is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
