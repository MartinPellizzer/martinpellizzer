Indigofera tsiangiana

Indigofera tsiangiana, commonly known as Chinese indigo, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its striking blue flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has compound leaves and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine.

Its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil makes it beneficial for soil enrichment.
