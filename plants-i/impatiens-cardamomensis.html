Impatiens cardamomensis

Impatiens cardamomensis, commonly known as the cardamom impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of forests.

Impatiens cardamomensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
