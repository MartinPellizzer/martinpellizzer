Impatiens cirrhipetala

Impatiens cirrhipetala, commonly known as the curled-leaved impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, spurred flowers that typically range in color from white to pale pink. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often curled at the edges, giving it its common name. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forests or along stream banks.

Impatiens cirrhipetala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
