Ischaemum cylindricum

Ischaemum cylindricum, commonly known as the slender barnyard grass, is a species of grass native to Europe, Asia, and Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with minute, pale green to brownish flowers. It is often found in disturbed soils, such as roadsides and agricultural fields.

Ischaemum cylindricum is a perennial herb that can sometimes become invasive in certain regions.
