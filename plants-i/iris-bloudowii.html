Iris bloudowii

Iris bloudowii, commonly known as the desert iris, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northwestern Mexico.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and has narrow, grass-like leaves. It is a perennial species that thrives in full sun and well-drained soils.

The iris is also notable for its medicinal uses among indigenous communities.
