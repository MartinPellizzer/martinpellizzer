Indigofera hedyantha subsp. robusta

Indigofera hedyantha subsp.

robusta, commonly known as the robust indigo, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family Fabaceae. It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its erect growth habit and compound leaves.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale purple, often attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its seeds are enclosed in pods and can be used for various traditional purposes.

The species is also valued for its potential in ecological restoration due to its nitrogen-fixing ability.
