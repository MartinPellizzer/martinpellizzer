Isodon umbrosus var. komaensis

Isodon umbrosus var. komaensis, commonly known as the Koma dark leaved isodon, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of East Africa.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves and small, clustered flowers that range in color from blue to purple. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.
