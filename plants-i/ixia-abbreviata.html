Ixia abbreviata

Ixia abbreviata, commonly known as the dwarf star lily, is a perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a distinctive red or orange throat. The plant typically grows to a height of about 15-30 centimeters, with narrow, grass-like leaves. It blooms in late summer to early autumn, producing clusters of showy flowers that attract pollinators.

Ixia abbreviata is well-suited for rock gardens and containers due to its compact size and ornamental value.
