Impatiens kawttyana

Impatiens kawttyana, commonly known as Kawttya's impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, spurred flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves and thrives in shady, moist environments. It is a member of the Balsaminaceae family, known for its rapid growth and ornamental value.
