Impatiens glauca var. ecalcarata

Impatiens glauca var. ecalcarata, commonly known as the blue impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a vivid splash of color to gardens. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Due to its delicate nature, it requires careful attention to soil moisture and light conditions to flourish.
