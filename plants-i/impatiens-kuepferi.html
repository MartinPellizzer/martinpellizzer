Impatiens kuepferi

Impatiens kuepferi, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green foliage and clusters of showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of about 10 to 30 centimeters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact size and long blooming period make it a favored ornamental plant in both gardens and homes.
