Ipomoea choisyi

Ipomoea choisyi, commonly known as the Choisy's morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It is a climbing vine that produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of pink and purple. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is characterized by its rapid growth habit. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Ipomoea choisyi is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant.
