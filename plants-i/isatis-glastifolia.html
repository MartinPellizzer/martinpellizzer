Isatis glastifolia

Isatis glastifolia, commonly known as yellow flag, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in wetland environments, such as marshes and along the edges of ponds. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often have a waxy coating.

Isatis glastifolia is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
