Impatiens nilgirica var. nilgirica

Impatiens nilgirica var. nilgirica, commonly known as the Nilgiri impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories and rocky slopes. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered with a fine, silky pubescence.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
