Iodes africana

Iodes africana, commonly known as the African sandthorn, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, yellow-orange flowers and woody, thorny stems. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy soils. Its leaves are small and leathery, adapted to reduce water loss.

Iodes africana is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
