Ixia petitiana

Ixia petitiana, commonly known as the Orange Freesia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant orange flowers with a distinct, spicy fragrance. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30-60 cm and produces clusters of narrow, tubular petals. Ixia petitiana thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Its blooming period occurs during the late winter to early spring, adding a splash of color to the landscape during cooler months.
