Ipomoea Contorquens Var. Simplicifolia

Ipomoea contortae var. simplicifolia is a striking member of the Convolvulaceae family known for its vibrant blue and purple flowers. This variant of Ipomoea contortae is prized for its unique foliage and ornamental value in gardens and landscapes. Native to tropical regions, it thrives in warm climates with ample sunlight and well-drained soil. Its twisted stems and heart-shaped leaves make it a distinctive addition to any plant collection. Despite its beauty, it requires careful cultivation to flourish in diverse environments.
