Ixora cremixora

Ixora cremixora, commonly known as the flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clustered, bright red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are dark green in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Ixora cremixora is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to withstand coastal conditions.
