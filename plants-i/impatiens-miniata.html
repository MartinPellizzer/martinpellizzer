Impatiens miniata

Impatiens miniata, commonly known as the scarlet ice plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its small, delicate leaves and clusters of showy, pink to red flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers. Its compact growth habit and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

However, it is important to note that it is considered invasive in some regions due to its rapid spread.
