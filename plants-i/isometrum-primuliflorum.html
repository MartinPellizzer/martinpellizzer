Isometrum primuliflorum

Isometrum primuliflorum, commonly known as the spring beauty, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. The plant features basal leaves that are heart-shaped and often covered in a fine, white down. Its flowers are small, five-petaled, and range in color from white to pale pink, blooming in early spring.

Isometrum primuliflorum plays a role in supporting early pollinators and is often found in regions with temperate climates.
