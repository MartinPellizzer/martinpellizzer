Isatis minor

Isatis minor, commonly known as wild mustard, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cabbage and broccoli. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and features dark green, pinnately divided leaves. Its small, yellow flowers are arranged in racemes and bloom in the spring.

The plant produces small, round, green pods that contain several seeds.
