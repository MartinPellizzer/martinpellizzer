Indigofera basedowii subsp. longibractea

Indigofera basedowii subsp.

longibractea, commonly known as the long-bract indigofera, is a flowering plant species native to parts of Africa. It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, elongated bracts that surround its small, pea-like flowers.
