Ixora radiata var. latifolia

Ixora radiata var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf ixora, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features showy, clustered flowers that are typically red or orange in color, with a fragrant scent. The leaves are broad and ovate, providing a dense canopy that supports various forms of wildlife. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a hedge.
