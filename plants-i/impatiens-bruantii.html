Impatiens bruantii

Impatiens bruantii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-draining soil. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.

Impatiens bruantii is also valued for its ability to bloom continuously throughout the growing season.
