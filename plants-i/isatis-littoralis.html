Isatis littoralis

Isatis littoralis, commonly known as yellow rocket, is a biennial plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in the spring and early summer. The plant has alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in disturbed soils and can often be found along roadsides and in open fields.

Yellow rocket is also known for its medicinal properties, historically used in traditional herbal remedies.
