Impatiens hendrikii

Impatiens hendrikii, commonly known as the Hendrik's impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found growing in forest understories.

Impatiens hendrikii is a popular ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
