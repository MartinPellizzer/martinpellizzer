Ipomopsis

Ipomopsis, commonly known as the whorled milkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Polemoniaceae.

It is native to the western United States and parts of Mexico, typically growing in dry, rocky habitats. Ipomopsis is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators like butterflies and bees.
