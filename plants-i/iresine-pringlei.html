Iresine pringlei

Iresine pringlei, commonly known as the Redleaf plant, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety red foliage that contrasts beautifully with its small, inconspicuous white flowers. The plant belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Iresine pringlei is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
