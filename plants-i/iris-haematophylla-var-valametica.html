Iris haematophylla var. valametica

Iris haematophylla var. valametica, commonly known as the Mexican red iris, is a striking perennial plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its vibrant red to orange flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features sword-shaped, grass-like leaves that are typically green with a reddish tint. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its striking color and unique foliage structure.
