Iseilema minutiflorum

Iseilema minutiflorum, commonly known as the small-flowered ironwood, is a shrub native to the eastern coast of Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has a woody stem with a rough, fissured bark that provides structural support. Its leaves are narrow, leathery, and arranged alternately along the branches.

Iseilema minutiflorum is well adapted to coastal environments and is often found in sandy soils.
