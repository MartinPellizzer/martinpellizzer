Ixora graciliflora

Ixora graciliflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange to red flowers, which are clustered in dense inflorescences and bloom throughout the year. The plant has simple, opposite, and often hairy leaves that are elliptical in shape. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for ornamental gardening.

Ixora graciliflora is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and birds.
