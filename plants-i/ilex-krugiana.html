Ilex krugiana

Ilex krugiana, commonly known as Chinese holly, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and small, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of white flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries that are attractive to birds. Ilex krugiana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
