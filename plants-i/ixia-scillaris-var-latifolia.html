Ixia scillaris var. latifolia

Ixia scillaris var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf ixia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in shades of yellow and orange. Its botanical beauty and vibrant colors make it a notable ornamental plant.
