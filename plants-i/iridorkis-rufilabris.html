Iridorkis rufilabris

Iridorkis rufilabris, commonly known as the red-labied iris, is a striking member of the Iridaceae family native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically feature a deep purple or violet coloration with a distinctive red lip. The plant produces sword-shaped leaves and blooms during the summer months, adding visual interest to gardens. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is often cultivated for both its beauty and ecological benefits.
