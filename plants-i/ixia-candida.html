Ixia candida

Ixia candida, commonly known as the white ixia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking white, trumpet-shaped flowers. It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and is well-suited to well-drained soils.
