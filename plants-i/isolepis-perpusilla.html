Isolepis perpusilla

Isolepis perpusilla, commonly known as the dwarf spike-rush, is a small, herbaceous plant native to wetland and marsh environments in temperate regions.

Isolepis perpusilla plays an important ecological role in stabilizing wetland soils and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
