Ixia reticularis

Ixia reticularis, commonly known as the netted ixia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllis family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of tubular flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. Ixia reticularis thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its unique appearance and seasonal blooming make it a valued ornamental plant.
