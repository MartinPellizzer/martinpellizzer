Isatis platyloba

Isatis platyloba, commonly known as Chinese woad, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other plants like mustard and cabbage. The plant is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. It is known for its ability to produce a blue dye from its leaves, historically used in textile production.

Isatis platyloba is also valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
