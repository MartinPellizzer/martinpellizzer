Ixia capitata var. ovata

Ixia capitata var. ovata, commonly known as the oval-headed firecracker, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, narrow stems that bear clusters of bright orange-red, tubular flowers resembling firecrackers. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves that are evergreen and persist through the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its unique floral display makes it a standout in gardens during the summer months.
