Ivesia jaegeri

Ivesia jaegeri, commonly known as the Jaeger's ivesia, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It typically grows in dry, rocky habitats such as grasslands and open woodlands. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers in late spring to early summer. It is part of the Rosaceae family, which includes roses and strawberries.
