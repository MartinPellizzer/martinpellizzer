Isodon pleiophyllus var. dolichodens

Isodon pleiophyllus var. dolichodens, commonly known as the long-leaved isodon, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive bluish-green coloration and produces small, tubular flowers in shades of purple and blue. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal potential, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and traditional herbal practices.
