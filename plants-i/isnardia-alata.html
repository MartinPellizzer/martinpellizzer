Isnardia alata

Isnardia alata, commonly known as the winged nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is native to parts of southern Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant is characterized by its distinctive winged stems and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.
