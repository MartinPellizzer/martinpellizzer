Impatiens pilosissima

Impatiens pilosissima, commonly known as the silky impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy, velvety leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, red, and white. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. It has a compact growth habit, making it suitable for container gardening and as a decorative plant in shaded areas.

Impatiens pilosissima is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
