Isachne globosa

Isachne globosa, commonly known as the globe grass, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rounded inflorescence, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Isachne globosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
