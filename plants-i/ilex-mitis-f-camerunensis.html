Ilex mitis f. camerunensis

Ilex mitis f. camerunensis, commonly known as the Cameroon holly, is a shrub or small tree native to Cameroon.

It belongs to the family Aquifoliaceae and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves with serrated margins. The plant produces small, bright red berries that are an important food source for various bird species. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
