Ipomoea arnoldsonii

Ipomoea arnoldsonii, commonly known as Arnoldson's morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It is a herbaceous vine that produces trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of purple and white. The plant belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is characterized by its rapid growth and climbing habit. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often have a velvety texture.
