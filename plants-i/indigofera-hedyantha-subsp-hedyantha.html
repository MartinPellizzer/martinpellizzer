Indigofera hedyantha subsp. hedyantha

Indigofera hedyantha subsp.

hedyantha, commonly known as the blue pea or Indian lilac, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its striking violet to blue flowers, which are often used in traditional medicine and as a natural dye.

The plant belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, including nitrogen fixation in soil.
