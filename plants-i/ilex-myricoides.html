Ilex myricoides

Ilex myricoides, commonly known as the Mexican holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Aquifoliaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, red berries that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical climates, thriving in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
