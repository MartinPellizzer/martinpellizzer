Impatiens lushiensis

Impatiens lushiensis, commonly known as the Lushi Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It belongs to the Balsaminaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. Impatiens lushiensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
