Ixora findlayana

Ixora findlayana, commonly known as the pink flame tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, fiery red or pink flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a striking appearance. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green, providing a lush backdrop to its showy blooms. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing as a shrub or small tree.

Ixora findlayana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is commonly used in landscaping and garden design.
