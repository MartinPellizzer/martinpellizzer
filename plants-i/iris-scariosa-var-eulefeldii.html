Iris scariosa var. eulefeldii

Iris scariosa var. eulefeldii, commonly known as the Eulefeld iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to central and eastern Europe.

It features tall, slender stems that bear showy, violet-blue flowers with a distinctive white and purple pattern on the lower petals. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a whitish, powdery bloom, giving it a unique appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
