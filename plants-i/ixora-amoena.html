Ixora amoena

Ixora amoena, commonly known as the flame flower or Chinese hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences of bright red or orange-red flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has opposite, simple, and elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Ixora amoena is also valued for its ability to produce fragrant flowers and its use in traditional medicine.
