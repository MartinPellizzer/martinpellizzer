Ibidium strictum

Ibidium strictum, commonly known as the rigid ibidium, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright, branching structure and small, showy flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun exposure. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in a fine layer of white wax.

Ibidiump strictum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
