Impatiens kunyitensis

Impatiens kunyitensis, commonly known as the yellow water impatiens, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or wetlands. It belongs to the Balsaminaceae family and is known for its rapid growth habit.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in tropical and subtropical regions.
