Iriartea phaeocarpa

Iriartea phaeocarpa, commonly known as the dark-leaved arrowroot, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its large, dark green, leathery leaves that form a rosette.
