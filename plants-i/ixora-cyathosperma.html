Ixora cyathosperma

Ixora cyathosperma, commonly known as the golden flame or flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bright orange to red flowers, which are clustered in dense, cylindrical inflorescences. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are elliptical and slightly glossy. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Ixora cyathosperma is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
