Ixora beckleri

Ixora beckleri, commonly known as the yellow ixora, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year, making it a popular ornamental plant. The leaves are opposite, elliptical, and glossy, providing a lush green backdrop to its vibrant blooms. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

The plant is also valued for its fragrant flowers and is often used in landscaping and garden settings.
