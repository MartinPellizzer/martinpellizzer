Impatiens rivalis

Impatiens rivalis, commonly known as the pink impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from pale pink to deep red. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.
