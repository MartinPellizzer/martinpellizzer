Ixora djambica

Ixora djambica, commonly known as the flame tree or the Australian flame tree, is a flowering plant native to Australia and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bright red or orange flowers that bloom in dense clusters, creating a striking visual display. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually oval or elliptical in shape. Ixora djambica thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

It is also valued for its ability to grow in a variety of soil types and its relatively low maintenance requirements.
