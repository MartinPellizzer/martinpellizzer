Indigofera cornuligera subsp. cornuligera

Indigofera cornuligera subsp.

cornuligera, commonly known as the Indian indigo plant, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family Fabaceae. It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces cylindrical pods that contain several seeds, and it is typically found in tropical and subtropical regions.

It is also valued for its historical use in dye production, particularly for indigo.
