Ixora × formosa

Ixora × formosa, commonly known as the Chinese flame bush, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Ixora coccinea and Ixora albiflora. The plant is characterized by its dense clusters of bright red or orange flowers, which bloom throughout the year in warm climates. Its leaves are typically dark green and glossy, providing a striking contrast to the vibrant flowers.

Ixora × formosa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its ornate appearance and adaptability to various soil types.
