Inulanthera dregeana

Inulanthera dregeana, commonly known as the silver tree, is a unique shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Inulanthera dregeana is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
