Impatiens lampungensis

Impatiens lampungensis, commonly known as the Lampung Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Balsaminaceae and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
