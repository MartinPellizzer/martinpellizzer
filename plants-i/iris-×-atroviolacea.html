Iris × atroviolacea

Iris × atroviolacea, commonly known as the black violet iris, is a hybrid perennial plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking deep violet to almost black flowers, which are typically 4–6 inches in diameter. The plant features grass-like, sword-shaped leaves that are usually a vibrant green. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Iris × atroviolacea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and wetland habitats.
