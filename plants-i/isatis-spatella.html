Isatis spatella

Isatis spatella, commonly known as the narrowleaf woad, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cabbage and mustard. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Isatis spatella is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.
