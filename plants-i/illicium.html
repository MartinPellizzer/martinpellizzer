Illicium

Illicium, commonly known as star anise, is a flowering plant in the family Schisandraceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly China, and is characterized by its aromatic, star-shaped fruit. The fruit contains tiny seeds that are widely used in cooking and traditional medicine. The plant's leaves are opposite and have a slightly aromatic scent.

Illicium species are often cultivated for their essential oils and medicinal properties.
