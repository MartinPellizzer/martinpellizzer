Impatiens perfecunda

Impatiens perfecunda, commonly known as the golden touch impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Balsaminaceae and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange. Impatiens perfecunda is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
