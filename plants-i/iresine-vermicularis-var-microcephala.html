Iresine vermicularis var. microcephala

Iresine vermicularis var. microcephala, commonly known as the small-headed iron plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or purple foliage, which is often variegated with white or green markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often grown as an ornamental plant for its striking leaf coloration.

The species is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
