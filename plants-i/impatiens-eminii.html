Impatiens eminii

Impatiens eminii, commonly known as the eminii impatiens, is a species of flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of eastern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom throughout the growing season. Its foliage is heart-shaped and covered in a fine layer of soft, hairy texture.

Impatiens eminii thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden beds.
