Ixora scandens

Ixora scandens, commonly known as the climbing ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a woody shrub or vine that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces clusters of small, bright red or orange flowers, which are highly attractive to pollinators. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a glossy texture.

Ixora scandens is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a hedge plant.
