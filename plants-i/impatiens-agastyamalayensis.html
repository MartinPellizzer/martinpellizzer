Impatiens agastyamalayensis

Impatiens agastyamalayensis, commonly known as the Malaya impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Balsaminaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to pink. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
