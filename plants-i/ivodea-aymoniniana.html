Ivodea aymoniniana

Ivodea aymoniniana, commonly known as the Aymonin's ivy, is a species of flowering plant in the family Apocynaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by follicle fruits. Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
