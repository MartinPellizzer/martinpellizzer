Ixora chinensis

Ixora chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bright red or orange inflorescences, which are composed of numerous small, tubular flowers clustered together. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Ixora chinensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a hedge plant.
