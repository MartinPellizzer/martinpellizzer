Ipomoea bahiensis

Ipomoea bahiensis, commonly known as the Bahia morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It is a herbaceous vine characterized by its rapid growth and climbing habit. The plant produces striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a velvety texture.

Ipomoea bahiensis thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
