Ilex forrestii var. multiflora

Ilex forrestii var. multiflora, commonly known as the multiflora holly, is a shrub or small tree native to China.

It features small, glossy, dark green leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters, followed by bright red berries that are edible for birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
