Ischaemum hansenii

Ischaemum hansenii, commonly known as Hansen's grass, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It typically grows in grasslands and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 60 centimeters. The plant has slender, cylindrical stems and narrow, linear leaves with a smooth surface. Its inflorescence consists of a spike-like cluster of small, two-lobed spikelets.

Ischaemum hansenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
