Ixora eriantha

Ixora eriantha, commonly known as the velvet tamarind, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and clusters of small, bright orange-red flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically covered in a thick, velvety coating. It thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Ixora eriantha is also valued for its ability to tolerate partial shade and its relatively low maintenance requirements.
