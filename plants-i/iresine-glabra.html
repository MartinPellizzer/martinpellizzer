Iresine glabra

Iresine glabra, commonly known as red leaf iron plant, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage, which can range from red to purple and green, depending on the variety. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Iresine glabra is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking leaf coloration.
