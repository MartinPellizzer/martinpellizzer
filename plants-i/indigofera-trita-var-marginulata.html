Indigofera trita var. marginulata

Indigofera trita var. marginulata, commonly known as the marginated indigofera, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, particularly India and Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its trifoliate leaves with serrated margins and clusters of small, violet-blue flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for cultivation in warm climates.

This species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and potential use in traditional medicine.
