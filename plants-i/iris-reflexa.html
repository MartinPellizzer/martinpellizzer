Iris reflexa

Iris reflexa, commonly known as the reflexed iris, is a species of flowering plant in the family Iridaceae.

It is native to southern Europe and western Asia, often found in rocky or gravelly soils. The plant produces striking blue or violet flowers with a distinctive reflexed petal at the base. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically dark green.

Iris reflexa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
