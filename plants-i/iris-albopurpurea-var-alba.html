Iris albopurpurea var. alba

Iris albopurpurea var. alba, commonly known as the White Flag Iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the northeastern United States.

It features upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers with white petals and a purple-pink throat, blooming in late spring to early summer. It prefers moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and wetlands.
