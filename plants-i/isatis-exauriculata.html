Isatis exauriculata

Isatis exauriculata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved woad, is a herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other plants such as cabbages and mustards. The plant features slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive blue-green color. Its small, yellow flowers are arranged in racemes and bloom in the summer months.

Isatis exauriculata is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to thrive in arid environments.
