Ilex polyneura var. glabra

Ilex polyneura var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth-leaved holly, is a variant of the holly genus native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, leathery leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter on the underside. The plant produces small, bright red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its evergreen foliage and winter interest.

This variety is relatively hardy and can thrive in a range of soil types, making it a versatile choice for landscaping.
