Iris capensis

Iris capensis, commonly known as the Cape iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features upright, slender stems that bear showy, violet-blue flowers with a distinctive white center. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and grass-like in appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Iris capensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
