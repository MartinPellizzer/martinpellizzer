Ixora montana

Ixora montana, commonly known as the mountain flame or mountain ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth habit and produces clusters of bright red or orange flowers. The plant has opposite, simple leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, well-drained soils.

Ixora montana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and horticulture.
