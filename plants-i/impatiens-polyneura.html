Impatiens polyneura

Impatiens polyneura, commonly known as the variegated impatiens, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking variegated leaves, which display shades of white, yellow, and green. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Impatiens polyneura is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through runners.
