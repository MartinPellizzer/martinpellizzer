Ione australis

Ione australis, commonly known as the Australian honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in shades of white and pink. The plant has slender, woody stems and lance-shaped leaves with a glossy surface. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal regions and open woodlands.

Ione australis is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local pollinators.
