Impatiens asymmetrica

Impatiens asymmetrica, commonly known as the asymmetric impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often slightly hairy and have a serrated edge. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the undergrowth of tropical forests.

Impatiens asymmetrica is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
