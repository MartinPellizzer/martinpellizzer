Ixora cauliflora var. graciliflora

Ixora cauliflora var. graciliflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, bright red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant floral display.

The varietal form, graciliflora, distinguishes itself by its more slender and delicate flower stems compared to the typical variety.
