Ipomoea bahiensis var. uniflora

Ipomoea bahiensis var. uniflora, commonly known as the Bahia morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking violet-blue to purple flowers that bloom in the early morning. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces tendrils that aid in climbing. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
