Iresine herbstii

Iresine herbstii, commonly known as the red leaf iron plant, is a strikingly ornamental shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, velvety red foliage, which contrasts beautifully with its small, inconspicuous white flowers. The plant thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers. Its foliage changes color with the seasons, adding dynamic visual interest throughout the year.

Iresine herbstii is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking bold, colorful accents in their landscapes.
