Iresine celosioides var. nicotianoides

Iresine celosioides var. nicotianoides, commonly known as red leaf iron plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety red foliage that contrasts with its smaller, white or pink flowers. The plant belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.
