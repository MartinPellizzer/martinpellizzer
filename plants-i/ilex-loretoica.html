Ilex loretoica

Ilex loretoica, commonly known as the Loreto holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Aquifoliaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically spiny at the margins. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for various wildlife species. Ilex loretoica thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, well-drained soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural use in gardens and landscapes.
