Impatiens balsamina var. corymbosa

Impatiens balsamina var. corymbosa, commonly known as the Chinese impatiens, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The leaves are typically ovate with a smooth texture and a slightly waxy surface. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.

This variety is also noted for its ability to bloom continuously throughout the growing season.
