Ixora mollis

Ixora mollis, commonly known as the velvet tamarind, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters tall, characterized by its dense, leathery leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, orange-red flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its botanical features include opposite leaves and small, fleshy fruits that contain several seeds.

Ixora mollis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
