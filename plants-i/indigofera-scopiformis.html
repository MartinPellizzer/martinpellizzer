Indigofera scopiformis

Indigofera scopiformis, commonly known as the slender indigo, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries such as Kenya and Tanzania. Indigofera scopiformis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
