Ilex theezans var. typica

Ilex theezans var. typica, commonly known as the Chinese holly, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green, oval-shaped leaves that are often spiny at the edges. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
