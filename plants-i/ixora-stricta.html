Ixora stricta

Ixora stricta, commonly known as the Chinese flame tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, upright growth habit and is often cultivated for its showy, bright orange-red flowers. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that bloom throughout the year in warm climates. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate, with a smooth texture.

Ixora stricta is also valued for its adaptability to various soil types and its use in ornamental horticulture.
