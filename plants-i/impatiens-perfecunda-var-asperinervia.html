Impatiens perfecunda var. asperinervia

Impatiens perfecunda var. asperinervia, commonly known as the rough-veined impatiens, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, slightly hairy leaves with prominent, rough-textured veins. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive species within the Impatiens genus.
