Impatiens flemingii

Impatiens flemingii, commonly known as the Fleming's impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and a slender, branching stem, making it an attractive addition to gardens. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Impatiens flemingii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated for both its aesthetic appeal and ecological benefits.
