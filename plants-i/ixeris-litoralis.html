Ixeris litoralis

Ixeris litoralis, commonly known as sea oxeye, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of East Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial with bright yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is adapted to sandy or rocky soils. It thrives in salty, well-drained environments and is often found in dune ecosystems.

Ixeris litoralis plays a role in stabilizing coastal dunes and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
