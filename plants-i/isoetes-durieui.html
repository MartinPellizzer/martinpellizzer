Isoetes durieui

Isoetes durieui, commonly known as the Durie's watermace, is a species of aquatic plant belonging to the genus Isoetes, which are part of the order Isoetales.

It is characterized by its spore-producing structures called sporangia, which are typically located on specialized leaves called spore leaves. The plant thrives in freshwater environments, particularly in shallow, still waters such as ponds and lakes. Its morphology includes a stalk-like structure with a cluster of spore leaves at the top, which are often spirally arranged.

Isoetes durieui is notable for its ability to survive in low-nutrient conditions and its ecological role in stabilizing aquatic sediments.
