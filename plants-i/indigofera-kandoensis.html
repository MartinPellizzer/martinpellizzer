Indigofera kandoensis

Indigofera kandoensis, commonly known as the Kando indigo, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, particularly found in countries like India and Sri Lanka. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and herbal practices.
