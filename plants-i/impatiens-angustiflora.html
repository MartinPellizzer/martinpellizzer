Impatiens angustiflora

Impatiens angustiflora, commonly known as narrow-flower touch-me-not, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, which bloom in clusters along the stems. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens.

Impatiens angustiflora is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
