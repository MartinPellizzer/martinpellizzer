Ixora intermedia

Ixora intermedia, commonly known as the Indian flame or red flame tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 5 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its clusters of bright red flowers, which bloom throughout the year in many tropical regions. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a leathery texture.

Ixora intermedia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a hedge due to its dense growth habit.
