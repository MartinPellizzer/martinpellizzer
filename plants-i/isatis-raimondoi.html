Isatis raimondoi

Isatis raimondoi, commonly known as the Raimond's woad, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cabbage and mustard. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with a slightly rough texture.
