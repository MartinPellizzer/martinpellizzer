Impatiens platyadena

Impatiens platyadena, commonly known as the large-flowered impatiens, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, spurred flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often used in ornamental gardening.

Impatiens platyadena is also known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through self-seeding.
