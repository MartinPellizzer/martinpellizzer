Ilex celebensis

Ilex celebensis, commonly known as the Celebes holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Indonesian islands of Sulawesi and neighboring regions.

It belongs to the family Aquifoliaceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically opposite in arrangement. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by red, berry-like fruits that are an important food source for various birds and mammals.

Ilex celebensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine in some parts of its native range.
