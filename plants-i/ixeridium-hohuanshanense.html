Ixeridium hohuanshanense

Ixeridium hohuanshanense, commonly known as the Hohuanshan grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Hohuanshan region of Yunnan Province. The plant is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with narrow leaves and small, clustered flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in mountainous areas with high humidity.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species, highlighting the importance of conservation efforts in its native habitat.
