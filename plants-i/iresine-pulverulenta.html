Iresine pulverulenta

Iresine pulverulenta, commonly known as the red leaf iron plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety red or purple leaves that contrast with its small, inconspicuous white or pink flowers. The plant belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant foliage. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Iresine pulverulenta is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
