Inula pulicaria

Inula pulicaria, commonly known as yellow cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to September. The plant has dark green, lobed leaves and grows in dry, rocky or sandy soils. It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges.

Inula pulicaria is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in herbal medicine.
