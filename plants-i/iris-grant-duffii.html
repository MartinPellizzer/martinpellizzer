Iris grant-duffii

Iris grant-duffii, commonly known as the African iris, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, upright stems that bear showy, violet-blue flowers with a distinctive yellow beard. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are arranged in a fan-like pattern at the base. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking both beauty and resilience.
