Ilex fauriei

Ilex fauriei, commonly known as the Chinese holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, red berries that are toxic to humans and animals. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for ornamental landscaping.

Ilex fauriei is valued for its year-round foliage and is often used in traditional Chinese medicine.
