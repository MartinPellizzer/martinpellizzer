Isonandra lanceolata f. anfractuosa

Isonandra lanceolata f. anfractuosa, commonly known as the zigzag rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves and clusters of bright yellow flowers. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid climates. Its unique, zigzagging stems are a distinguishing feature, and it is often found in crevices and along canyon walls.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand drought conditions.
