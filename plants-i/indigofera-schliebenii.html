Indigofera schliebenii

Indigofera schliebenii, commonly known as the Schlieben's indigo, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and vibrant blue or purple flowers. The plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in woodland and savanna habitats.
