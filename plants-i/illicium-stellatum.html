Illicium stellatum

Illicium stellatum, commonly known as star anise, is a deciduous shrub native to China and Vietnam.

It is characterized by its aromatic, star-shaped fruit, which is widely used in cooking and traditional medicine. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that develop into the distinctive capsule-like berries. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly serrated edge.

Illicium stellatum thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its medicinal and culinary value.
