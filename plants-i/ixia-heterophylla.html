Ixia heterophylla

Ixia heterophylla, commonly known as the yellow rain lily, is a striking perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant features strap-shaped leaves that are typically narrow and grass-like in appearance. Ixia heterophylla thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to produce multiple flower spikes from a single stem.
