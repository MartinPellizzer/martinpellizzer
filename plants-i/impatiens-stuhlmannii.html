Impatiens stuhlmannii

Impatiens stuhlmannii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of vibrant, showy flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 15-30 centimeters and prefers bright, indirect light. It is a popular ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.

Impatiens stuhlmannii is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
