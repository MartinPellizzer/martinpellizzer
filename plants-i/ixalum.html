Ixalum

Ixalum, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a striking plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, lance-shaped leaves that emit a subtle silvery sheen. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink, often blooming during the cooler months. Ixalum thrives in well-drained soil and requires partial shade to flourish.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a prized addition to botanical gardens and collections.
