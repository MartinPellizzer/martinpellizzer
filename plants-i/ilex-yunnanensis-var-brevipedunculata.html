Ilex yunnanensis var. brevipedunculata

Ilex yunnanensis var. brevipedunculata, commonly known as the Yunnan holly, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It features glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters, followed by bright red berries that are edible for birds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its winter interest.
