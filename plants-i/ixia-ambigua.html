Ixia ambigua

Ixia ambigua, commonly known as the zigzag lily, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, upward-facing flowers with a zigzag pattern in the petals. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its blooming period occurs during the summer months, producing vibrant orange or yellow flowers.

Ixia ambigua is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
