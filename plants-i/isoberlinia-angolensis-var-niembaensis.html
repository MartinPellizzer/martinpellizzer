Isoberlinia angolensis var. niembaensis

Isoberlinia angolensis var. niembaensis, commonly known as the Niemba isoberlinia, is a deciduous tree native to central Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant is adapted to tropical and subtropical climates, often found in savanna and woodland areas.

It has a shallow root system and is known for its ability to fix nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility.
