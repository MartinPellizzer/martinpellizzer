Ipomoea tuberosa var. oligantha

Ipomoea tuberosa var. oligantha, commonly known as the scarlet arum, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant red bracts that surround its small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces tuberous roots that store nutrients, aiding in its survival during dry periods. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
