Impatiens amphorata var. pallens

Impatiens amphorata var. pallens, commonly known as the pale impatiens, is a herbaceous perennial native to Southeast Asia.

It features slender, upright stems with ovate, glossy, dark green leaves. The flowers are showy, with five petals, typically white or pale pink, and are borne in clusters at the leaf axils. It thrives in partial to full shade and moist, well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
