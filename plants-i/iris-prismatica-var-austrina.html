Iris prismatica var. austrina

Iris prismatica var. austrina, commonly known as the Australian iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Australia.

It features striking blue to violet flowers with a distinctive three-dimensional structure, characteristic of the iris genus. The plant has narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its early spring blooming period and ability to attract pollinators.
