Iresine radicata

Iresine radicata, commonly known as redleaf iron plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety red foliage and clusters of small, inconspicuous white flowers. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-draining soil. It is often grown as an ornamental plant for its vibrant leaf color.

Iresine radicata is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
