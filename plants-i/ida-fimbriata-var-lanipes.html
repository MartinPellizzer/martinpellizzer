Ida fimbriata var. lanipes

Ida fimbriata var. lanipes, commonly known as the fringed hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant has palmately lobed leaves with a hairy texture, and its stems are often woody and branched. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or lowland areas.

Ida fimbriata var. lanipes is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
