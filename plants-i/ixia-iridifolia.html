Ixia iridifolia

Ixia iridifolia, commonly known as the rainbow lily, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family, which includes irises and gladiolus. The plant is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that come in a range of colors, including pink, red, and white. Ixia iridifolia typically grows in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
