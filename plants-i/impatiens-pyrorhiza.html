Impatiens pyrorhiza

Impatiens pyrorhiza, commonly known as the flame root impatiens, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red-orange roots, which are the most distinctive feature of the plant. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pink. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique appearance, it is occasionally cultivated for ornamental purposes.
