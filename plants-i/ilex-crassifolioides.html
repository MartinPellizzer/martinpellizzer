Ilex crassifolioides

Ilex crassifolioides, commonly known as the Mexican holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to Mexico.

Ilex crassifolioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and seasonal fruit.
