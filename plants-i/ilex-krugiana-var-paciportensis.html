Ilex krugiana var. paciportensis

Ilex krugiana var. paciportensis, commonly known as the Paciportensis holly, is a compact evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It features small, glossy, dark green leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, white flowers in the spring, which are followed by bright red berries in the fall. It is well adapted to tropical and subtropical climates and thrives in well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a landscape plant.
