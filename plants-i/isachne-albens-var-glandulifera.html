Isachne albens var. glandulifera

Isachne albens var. glandulifera, commonly known as the glandular isachne, is a variant of the isachne grass species.

It is a perennial herb native to parts of Asia, particularly China and Japan. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a smooth texture and a pale green color.

The inflorescence consists of dense, spike-like clusters of small, two-lipped flowers that bloom in late summer.
