Illecebrum tenellum

Illecebrum tenellum, commonly known as the spider plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing stems and small, bright green leaves that are often used in hanging baskets or as ground cover. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pink flowers that bloom in the spring. It is relatively easy to care for, thriving in well-drained soil and moderate sunlight.

Illecebrum tenellum is also known for its ability to propagate through runners, making it a popular choice for gardeners.
