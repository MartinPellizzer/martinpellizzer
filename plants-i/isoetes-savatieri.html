Isoetes savatieri

Isoetes savatieri, commonly known as the narrow-leaf water starwort, is a species of aquatic plant belonging to the genus Isoetes.

It is characterized by its spore-bearing, leaf-like structures called sporophylls, which are typically narrow and strap-shaped. This plant is often found in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and wetlands. Its unique morphology allows it to thrive in oxygen-rich waters, contributing to aquatic ecosystem stability.

Isoetes savatieri plays a role in nutrient cycling and provides habitat for various aquatic organisms.
