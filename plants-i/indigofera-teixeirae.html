Indigofera teixeirae

Indigofera teixeirae, commonly known as the Teixeira's indigo, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, shaded environments. It has compound leaves and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.
