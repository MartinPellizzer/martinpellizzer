Indigofera hilaris var. microscypha

Indigofera hilaris var. microscypha, commonly known as the dwarf indigo.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its small, delicate leaves and clusters of blue or purple flowers. Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant has a compact growth habit, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.
