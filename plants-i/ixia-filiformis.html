Ixia filiformis

Ixia filiformis, commonly known as the slender ixia, is a striking flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its long, narrow leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Ixia filiformis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
