Ionidium parietariifolium var. houstonii

Ionidium parietariifolium var. houstonii, commonly known as the Houston's wall-leaf, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the family Cactaceae, which includes cacti and related succulent plants.
