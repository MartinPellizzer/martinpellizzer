Ixora abyssinica

Ixora abyssinica, commonly known as the Abyssinian flame flower, is a shrub native to Ethiopia and parts of East Africa.

The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and a woody stem, typical of many shrubs in the Rubiaceae family. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for tropical and subtropical gardens.
