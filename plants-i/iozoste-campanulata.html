Iozoste campanulata

Iozoste campanulata, commonly known as the bellflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped, often blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

Iozoste campanulata is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
