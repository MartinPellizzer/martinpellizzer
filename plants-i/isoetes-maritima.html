Isoetes maritima

It is a small, aquatic or semi-aquatic fern that typically grows in brackish or freshwater environments. The plant has a distinctive, spore-bearing structure called a sporangium, which is clustered at the tip of its leaf-like structures. Isoetes maritima plays a role in stabilizing aquatic ecosystems by preventing soil erosion and providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.
