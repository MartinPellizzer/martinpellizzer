Ixora goalparensis

Ixora goalparensis, commonly known as the red ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of bright red, tubular flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are dark green in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Ixora goalparensis is also valued for its use in traditional medicine.
