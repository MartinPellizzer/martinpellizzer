Indigofera kleinii

Indigofera kleinii, commonly known as the African indigo or Klein's indigo, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height, characterized by its trifoliate leaves and showy, violet-blue flowers. The plant produces small, elongated seed pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savannas and woodland areas.
