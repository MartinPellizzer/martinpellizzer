Impatiens daraneenae

Impatiens daraneenae, commonly known as the Daranee's impatiens, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value has made it a sought-after plant in horticulture, though it remains relatively rare in cultivation.
