Impatiens betongensis

Impatiens betongensis, commonly known as Betong impatiens, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Balsaminaceae and is characterized by its showy, colorful flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it is not as widely grown as other impatiens species.
