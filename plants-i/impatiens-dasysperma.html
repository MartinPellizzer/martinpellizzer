Impatiens dasysperma

Impatiens dasysperma, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 cm. The plant features showy, five-petaled flowers that come in a range of colors, including white, pink, and purple. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in a fine, soft hair.

Impatiens dasysperma thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
