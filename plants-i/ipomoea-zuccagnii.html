Ipomoea zuccagnii

Ipomoea zuccagnii, commonly known as the Zuccagni's morning glory, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant blue or purple flowers that bloom in the morning and close by midday. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, round seeds. It thrives in warm, sunny environments and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.

Its rapid growth and showy flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
