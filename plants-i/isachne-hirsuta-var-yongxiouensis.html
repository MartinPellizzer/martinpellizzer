Isachne hirsuta var. yongxiouensis

Isachne hirsuta var. yongxiouensis, commonly known as the Yongxiou hairy isachne, is a variant of the hairy isachne grass native to China.

It is a perennial grass species characterized by its fine, wiry leaves and compact growth habit. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountainous regions and forest understories. Its inflorescence consists of dense, spike-like clusters of small, two-lipped flowers.

Botanically, it is notable for its unique morphological features and ecological adaptation to its native habitat.
