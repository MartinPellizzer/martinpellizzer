Iresine diffusa f. herbstii

Iresine diffusa f. herbstii, commonly known as red leaf iron plant, is a strikingly colorful plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its velvety, deeply lobed leaves that often display vibrant red, pink, or purple hues. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in spikes. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value is primarily due to its striking foliage, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
