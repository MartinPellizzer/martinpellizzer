Ilex moana

Ilex moana, commonly known as the Tahitian holly, is a flowering plant native to the Society Islands in French Polynesia.

It is a small evergreen shrub or tree that typically grows up to 5 meters in height. The plant features simple, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. Its small, white flowers appear in clusters and are followed by bright red berries that are edible.

Ilex moana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in traditional landscaping in its native habitat.
