Inula kalapani

Inula kalapani, commonly known as the Kalapani Inula, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Nepal and India.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single bloom. The plant features a tall, slender stem with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its yellow flowers are arranged in a single terminal head, surrounded by pale green bracts.

Inula kalapani is adapted to high-altitude environments, thriving in cool, moist conditions with well-drained soil.
