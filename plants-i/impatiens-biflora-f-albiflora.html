Impatiens biflora f. albiflora

Impatiens biflora f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered impatiens, is a cultivar of the species Impatiens biflora.

It is characterized by its pale to white flowers, which contrast with its green foliage. This plant is typically found in moist, shaded environments in its native range. It has opposite, ovate leaves and produces small, clustered flowers.

The f. albiflora form is notable for its unique coloration, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
