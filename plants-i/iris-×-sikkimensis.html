Iris × sikkimensis

Iris × sikkimensis, commonly known as the Sikkim iris, is a hybrid perennial plant native to the Himalayan region.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows to a height of 60–120 cm, with sword-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. It prefers cool, moist environments and is often found in mountain meadows and forest edges.

The Sikkim iris is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
