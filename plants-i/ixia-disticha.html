Ixia disticha

Ixia disticha, commonly known as the double rain lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically orange or red in color, with a distinctive double-layered petal structure. The plant grows from a bulb and produces its blooms in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are evergreen.

Ixia disticha is well-suited for rock gardens and containers, thriving in full sun and well-drained soil.
