Ipomoea yunnanensis var. pallescens

Ipomoea yunnanensis var. pallescens, commonly known as the pale yunnan morning glory, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows as a climbing vine, with stems that can reach several meters in length. Its flowers are usually pale blue or white, and they bloom in the early morning, giving it its common name.

This variant is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
