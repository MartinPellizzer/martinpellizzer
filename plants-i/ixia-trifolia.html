Ixia trifolia

Ixia trifolia, commonly known as the star lily, is a perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange with a distinctive red or purple throat. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces its showy blooms in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Ixia trifolia is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in floral arrangements.
