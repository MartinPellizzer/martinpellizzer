Impatiens confusa subsp. confusa

Impatiens confusa subsp.

confusa, commonly known as the confused impatiens, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its erect growth habit and clusters of showy, spurred flowers that range in color from white to pink.

The plant typically produces heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in suitable climates.
