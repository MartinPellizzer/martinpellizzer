Impatiens vaiyapurii

Impatiens vaiyapurii, commonly known as the Vaiyapurii Impatiens, is a species native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its delicate, spurred flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its leaves are ovate and hairy, while its stems are slender and branching.

Impatiens vaiyapurii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
