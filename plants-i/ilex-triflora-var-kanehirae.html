Ilex triflora var. kanehirae

Ilex triflora var. kanehirae, commonly known as the Kanehira holly, is a rare variant of the holly genus native to Japan.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for birds. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a botanical curiosity and is of interest to horticulturists and conservationists.
