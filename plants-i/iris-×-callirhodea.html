Iris × callirhodea

Iris × callirhodea, commonly known as the sword lily, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Iris germanica and Iris pallida.

It is characterized by its tall, sword-like leaves and showy, colorful flowers that typically bloom in late spring or early summer. The plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

The flowers can range in color from white to deep purple, depending on the specific cultivar.
