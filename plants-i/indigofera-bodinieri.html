Indigofera bodinieri

Indigofera bodinieri, commonly known as the Chinese indigo plant, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family Fabaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly China, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and distinctive yellow flowers. The plant is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, which enriches the soil and benefits surrounding vegetation. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas or along stream banks.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
