Illecebrum spinosum

Illecebrum spinosum, commonly known as the spiny morning glory, is a climbing plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features heart-shaped leaves with prominent veins and produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. The plant has spiny stems and tendrils that help it climb and attach to supports. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Illecebrum spinosum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can be found in gardens and natural habitats across its native range.
