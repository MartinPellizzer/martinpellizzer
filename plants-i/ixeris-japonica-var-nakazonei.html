Ixeris japonica var. nakazonei

Ixeris japonica var. nakazonei, commonly known as the Japanese mat sumach, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Japan.

It features a dense mat of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a compact ground cover. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in early summer, which are clustered in umbels. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its hardy nature and ornamental value make it a popular choice in horticulture.
