Iris scariosa

Iris scariosa, commonly known as the scarious iris, is a species of flowering plant in the genus Iris.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, including countries like Kazakhstan and Russia. The plant features showy blue to violet flowers with three upright petals and three drooping sepals. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it typically grows in rocky or sandy soils.

Iris scariosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
