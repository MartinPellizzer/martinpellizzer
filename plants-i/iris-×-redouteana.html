Iris × redouteana

Iris × redouteana, commonly known as the Redoute iris, is a hybrid perennial plant native to Europe.

It features striking, showy flowers with vivid purple petals and a distinctive, narrow dark violet throat. The plant typically grows to a height of 2 to 3 feet, with sword-shaped, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its botanical beauty and hardiness make it a valued addition to both cultivated gardens and natural habitats.
