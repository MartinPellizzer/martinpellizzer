Ilex nuculicava var. brevipedicellata

Ilex nuculicava var. brevipedicellata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved holly, is a shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, narrow, elliptical leaves that are glossy and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature in shaded garden settings.
