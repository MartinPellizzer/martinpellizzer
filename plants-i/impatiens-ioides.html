Impatiens ioides

Impatiens ioides, commonly known as the yellow touch-me-not, is a herbaceous perennial native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a distinctive spurred petal. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and stream banks. Its leaves are ovate to lance-shaped, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Impatiens ioides is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade.
