Iresine endlichii

Iresine endlichii, commonly known as the red leaf iron plant, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety, crimson-colored foliage that contrasts with its small, inconspicuous white flowers. The plant belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates. Its leaves are often used in ornamental horticulture due to their vibrant coloration and unique texture.

Iresine endlichii prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
