Ixora brachypoda

Ixora brachypoda, commonly known as the dwarf flame tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of bright red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Ixora brachypoda is also valued for its ability to grow in poor soil conditions.
