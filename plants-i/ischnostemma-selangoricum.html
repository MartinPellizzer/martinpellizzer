Ischnostemma selangoricum

Ischnostemma selangoricum, commonly known as the Malay star anise, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its aromatic, star-shaped fruits. The plant typically grows in tropical rainforests and thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are glossy and dark green, while its flowers are small and white or pale yellow.
