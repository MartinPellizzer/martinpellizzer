Iresine herrerae

Iresine herrerae, commonly known as the red flame plant, is a striking ornamental shrub native to South America.

It features vibrant, velvety leaves that range from deep red to purple, often with contrasting white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically pink or red, which appear in clusters along the stems. Iresine herrerae thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-draining soil.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for garden displays and container planting.
