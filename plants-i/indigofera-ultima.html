Indigofera ultima

Indigofera ultima, commonly known as the Socotra indigo plant, is a flowering plant native to the island of Socotra.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its unique, spiny leaves and striking, violet-blue flowers. The plant is adapted to the arid and rocky environment of Socotra, showcasing remarkable resilience.

Its seeds are encased in a hard, woody pod, and it plays a significant role in the island's unique flora.
