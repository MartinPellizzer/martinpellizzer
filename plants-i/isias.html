Isias

Isias, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a striking aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features showy, water-lily-like flowers that bloom in a range of colors, including white, pink, and yellow. The plant has broad, floating leaves that provide habitat for various aquatic organisms. Its roots anchor it in muddy substrates, while its stems support the foliage above the water.

Isias plays an important ecological role in wetland ecosystems by filtering water and providing food for wildlife.
