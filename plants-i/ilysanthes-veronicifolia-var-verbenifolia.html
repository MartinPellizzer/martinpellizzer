Ilysanthes veronicifolia var. verbenifolia

Ilysanthes veronicifolia var. verbenifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved veronicia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that bear opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white or pale yellow, which bloom during the dry season. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported antiseptic and anti-inflammatory properties.

The species is also valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
