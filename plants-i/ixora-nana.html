Ixora nana

Ixora nana, commonly known as dwarf ixora, is a compact shrub native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from red to orange, often blooming in clusters. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Ixora nana is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.
