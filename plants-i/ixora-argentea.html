Ixora argentea

Ixora argentea, commonly known as silver ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, bright orange or red flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite and oval in shape. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sun.

Ixora argentea is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant flowers and compact growth habit.
