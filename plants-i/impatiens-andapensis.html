Impatiens andapensis

Impatiens andapensis, commonly known as the Andapensis Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Gesneriaceae and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and a compact growth habit, making it suitable for both garden beds and container planting. Impatiens andapensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
