Impatiens macradenia

Impatiens macradenia, commonly known as the red impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or pink flowers, which are typically borne in clusters at the ends of its stems. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are usually dark green in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens macradenia is a popular ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and ease of cultivation.
