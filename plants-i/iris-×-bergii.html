Iris × bergii

Iris × bergii, commonly known as the German iris, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Iris germanica and Iris pallida.

It is a tall, herbaceous perennial with striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant features sword-shaped, dark green leaves that are stiff and upright, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden borders and landscapes.

Iris × bergii is valued for its resilience and ability to naturalize, creating a dramatic visual impact in gardens.
