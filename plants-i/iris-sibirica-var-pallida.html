Iris sibirica var. pallida

Iris sibirica var. pallida, commonly known as the pale Siberian iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the colder regions of Asia.

It features upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. The plant produces showy, blue to violet flowers with three upright petals and three drooping sepals. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
