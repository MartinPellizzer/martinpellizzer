Iozoste monii

Iozoste monii, commonly known as the white-flowered rock rose, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, ribbed stems and produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value lies in its elegant blooms and drought tolerance.
