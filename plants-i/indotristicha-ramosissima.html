Indotristicha ramosissima

Indotristicha ramosissima, commonly known as the hairy indotristicha, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its densely branched stems and small, ovate leaves with a hairy texture. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is of interest to botanists studying tropical flora.
