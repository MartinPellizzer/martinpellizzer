Impatiens lacinulifera

Impatiens lacinulifera, commonly known as the laceleaf impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, heart-shaped leaves that are finely serrated, giving it a lacy appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid forests.

Impatiens lacinulifera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses for its unique foliage and delicate blooms.
