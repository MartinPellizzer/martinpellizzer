Isatis frutescens

Isatis frutescens, commonly known as wild mustard or yellow mustard, is a flowering plant native to eastern North America.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cabbage and broccoli. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and features yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, lobed, and have a slightly bitter taste.

Isatis frutescens is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of habitats including roadsides and fields.
