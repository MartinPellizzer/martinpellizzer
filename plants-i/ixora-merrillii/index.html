Ixora Merrillii

Ixora merrillii is a rare and striking flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. Known for its vibrant red and orange blooms, it adds a bold splash of color to any garden or natural setting. This evergreen shrub thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a sought-after specimen among plant enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, Ixora merrillii remains relatively obscure in popular horticulture circles.
