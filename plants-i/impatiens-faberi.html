Impatiens faberi

Impatiens faberi, commonly known as the Chinese impatiens, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, red, and white. The plant typically grows as an annual or short-lived perennial, reaching heights of up to 3 feet. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
