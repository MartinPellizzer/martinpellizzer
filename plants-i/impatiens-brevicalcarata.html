Impatiens brevicalcarata

Impatiens brevicalcarata, commonly known as the short-spurred impatiens, is a species of flowering plant in the family Balsaminaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. Its leaves are typically ovate to elliptical, with a glossy surface and prominent veins.

Impatiens brevicalcarata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
