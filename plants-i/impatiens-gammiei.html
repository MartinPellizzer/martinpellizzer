Impatiens gammiei

Impatiens gammiei, commonly known as the Kauai impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the island of Kauai in Hawaii.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are ovate and slightly hairy, and it prefers moist, shaded environments.

Impatiens gammiei is a rare species, found only in specific habitats on the island.
