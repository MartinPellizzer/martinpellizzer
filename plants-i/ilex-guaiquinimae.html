Ilex guaiquinimae

Ilex guaiquinimae, commonly known as the Guaiquinimae holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to the tropical regions of South America.

It typically grows in humid, shaded environments within the understory of rainforests. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces small, red berries that are an important food source for local wildlife. Ilex guaiquinimae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
