Isoloma hondense var. warszewiczii

Isoloma hondense var. warszewiczii, commonly known as the Honduran isoloma, is a species native to Central America, particularly Honduras.

It is a shrub-like plant characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in the understory of tropical forests. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

The species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and limited distribution.
