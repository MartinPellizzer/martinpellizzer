Imperata cylindrica var. koenigii

Imperata cylindrica var. koenigii, commonly known as purple lovegrass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features tall, cylindrical inflorescences that are typically purple to reddish in color, giving it its common name. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in disturbed soils or open areas. It is adapted to dry conditions and can thrive in full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
