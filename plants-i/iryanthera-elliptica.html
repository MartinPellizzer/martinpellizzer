Iryanthera elliptica

Iryanthera elliptica, commonly known as the elliptic flame tree, is a tropical tree native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a striking visual display. The plant has simple, elliptical leaves that are often glossy and dark green in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Iryanthera elliptica is also valued for its rapid growth and ability to provide shade in landscaped areas.
