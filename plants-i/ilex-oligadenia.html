Ilex oligadenia

Ilex oligadenia, commonly known as the dwarf holly, is a species of evergreen shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its small, glossy, dark green leaves that are typically oval in shape. The plant produces small, white flowers in the spring, which are followed by bright red berries in the fall. Ilex oligadenia thrives in partial to full shade and is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its dense foliage and year-round appeal.

It is also valued for its ability to tolerate a variety of soil conditions.
