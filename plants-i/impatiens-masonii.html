Impatiens masonii

Impatiens masonii, commonly known as Mason's Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and a trailing growth habit, making it suitable for hanging baskets or as a ground cover. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Impatiens masonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
