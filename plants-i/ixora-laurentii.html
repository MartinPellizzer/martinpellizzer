Ixora laurentii

Ixora laurentii, commonly known as the Chinese flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange to red flowers that bloom in dense, rounded clusters, typically during the summer months. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant in warm climates.

Ixora laurentii is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and birds.
