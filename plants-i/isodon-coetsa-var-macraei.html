Isodon coetsa var. macraei

Isodon coetsa var. macraei, commonly known as the Cape rose or red isodon, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, pinkish-red flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are narrow and often have a slight woolly texture, while the stems are square and sturdy.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms and hardy nature.
