Impatiens jurpia var. jurpia

Impatiens jurpia var. jurpia, commonly known as the Jamaican impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean, particularly Jamaica.

It is a herbaceous perennial with fleshy, alternate leaves that are typically oval to ovate in shape. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its rapid growth and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
