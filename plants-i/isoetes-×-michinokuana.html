Isoetes × michinokuana

Isoetes × michinokuana, commonly known as the Japanese water fern, is a hybrid species within the genus Isoetes, characterized by its unique spore-bearing structures called sporangia.

It is a member of the water fern family, which includes plants adapted to aquatic or semi-aquatic environments. The plant exhibits a distinctive morphology with a central axis and specialized leaves that help in anchoring it in muddy substrates. Its spores are released into the water, facilitating dispersal and colonization of new habitats.

Isoetes × michinokuana is often found in wetlands and shallow aquatic environments across East Asia.
