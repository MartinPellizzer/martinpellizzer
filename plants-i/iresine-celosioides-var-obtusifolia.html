Iresine celosioides var. obtusifolia

Iresine celosioides var. obtusifolia, commonly known as purple flame or red leaf plant, is a species native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its striking, velvety foliage that ranges from deep red to purple hues. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptical in shape, with a smooth texture and prominent venation. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, usually in shades of white or pale pink.

This plant thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
