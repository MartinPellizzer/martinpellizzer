Idianthes Pulchra

The Indianthes pulchra is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of India. Known for its vibrant pink and white flowers, it captivates botanists and nature lovers alike. This delicate species thrives in cool, moist environments and requires careful cultivation. Its unique appearance makes it a sought-after addition to specialized gardens. Despite its beauty, it remains under-studied and endangered due to habitat loss.
