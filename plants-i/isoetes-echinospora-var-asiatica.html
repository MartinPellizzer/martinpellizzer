Isoetes echinospora var. asiatica

Isoetes echinospora var. asiatica, commonly known as the Asian spikerush, is a species of aquatic plant belonging to the genus Isoetes.

It is characterized by its spore-bearing structures called sporangia, which are typically clustered in a spiky, cone-like formation. The plant thrives in freshwater environments, such as lakes and marshes, and is adapted to low-light conditions. Its leaves are often elongated and ribbon-like, with a distinct central vein.

This variety is native to parts of Asia and plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for microorganisms and invertebrates.
