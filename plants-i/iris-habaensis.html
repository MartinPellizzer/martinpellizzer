Iris habaensis

Iris habaensis, commonly known as the Habaensis iris, is a species native to China.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue-violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in mountainous regions. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves, and it blooms in the spring.

Iris habaensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
