Ixia excisa

Ixia excisa, commonly known as the yellow rain lily, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllis family (Amaryllidaceae) and is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant features strap-like, grassy leaves that emerge from a bulbous base, typical of many bulbous plants. Its flowers are usually borne in clusters on tall, slender stems, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Ixia excisa is well-suited to full sun and well-drained soil, thriving in temperate climates.
