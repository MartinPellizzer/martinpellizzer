Iberis spruneri

Iberis spruneri, commonly known as the alpine candytuft, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. It typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, preferring well-drained soils.

Iberis spruneri is often used in rock gardens due to its hardy nature and ornamental value.
