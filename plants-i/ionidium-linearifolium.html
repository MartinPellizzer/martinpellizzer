Ionidium linearifolium

Ionidium linearifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf ionidium, is a rare flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves that are arranged alternately along its stem. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in dense undergrowth of tropical forests.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and is the subject of conservation efforts.
