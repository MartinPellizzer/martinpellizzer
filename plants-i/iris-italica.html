Iris italica

Iris italica, commonly known as the Italian iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features tall, upright stems that bear showy, spurred flowers in shades of blue, purple, and white. The plant has broad, sword-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Iris italica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in landscaping and floral arrangements.
