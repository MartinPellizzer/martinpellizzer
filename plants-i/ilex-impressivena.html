Ilex impressivena

Ilex impressivena, commonly known as the impressive holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often adorned with white or silver markings, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible for birds but toxic to humans and livestock. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Ilex impressivena is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and hardy nature.
