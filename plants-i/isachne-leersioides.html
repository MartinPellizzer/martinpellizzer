Isachne leersioides

Isachne leersioides, commonly known as the narrowleaf isachne, is a grass species native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant typically produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to brownish flowers. It thrives in moist to wet habitats, such as marshes and stream banks.

Isachne leersioides is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
