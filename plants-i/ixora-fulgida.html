Ixora fulgida

Ixora fulgida, commonly known as the flame ixora, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Ixora fulgida is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
