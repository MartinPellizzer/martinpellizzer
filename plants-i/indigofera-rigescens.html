Indigofera rigescens

Indigofera rigescens, commonly known as the slender indigo.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is native to tropical regions of Africa. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and trifoliate leaves. It produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from pale blue to violet.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
