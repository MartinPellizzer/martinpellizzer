Impatiens amoena

Impatiens amoena, commonly known as the "white impatiens," is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pale pink. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments. Impatiens amoena is a herbaceous perennial that produces clusters of small, fragrant blooms throughout the growing season.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
