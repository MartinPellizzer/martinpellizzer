Impatiens smitinandii

Impatiens smitinandii, commonly known as the Smitinandii Impatiens, is a species native to the rainforests of western Africa.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its delicate, spurred flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and a slightly hairy underside. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found growing in the understory of dense tropical forests.

Due to its ornamental value and unique floral structure, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a specialty houseplant.
