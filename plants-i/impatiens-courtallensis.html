Impatiens courtallensis

Impatiens courtallensis, commonly known as the Kandy impatiens, is a flowering plant native to Sri Lanka.

It belongs to the family Balsaminaceae and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to pink. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
