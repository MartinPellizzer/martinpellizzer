Ixora longipes

Ixora longipes, commonly known as the longipes flame flower, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or orange inflorescences, which are composed of numerous small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth, glossy surface.

Ixora longipes thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
