Ivesia gracilis

Ivesia gracilis, commonly known as slender ivesia, is a perennial herb native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

It typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, such as sagebrush steppe and rocky slopes. The plant has slender, upright stems and small, alternate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are clustered in dense, cylindrical inflorescences and are typically pink or purple in color.

Ivesia gracilis plays a role in stabilizing soils and providing habitat for various insects and small animals.
