Ixora novemnervia

Ixora novemnervia, commonly known as the nine-veined flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling flames. The plant has simple, opposite leaves with nine distinct veins, giving it its scientific name. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Ixora novemnervia is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
