Ilex nuculicava var. nuculicava

Ilex nuculicava var. nuculicava, commonly known as the Chinese holly, is a deciduous evergreen shrub or small tree native to China.

It features small, glossy, dark green leaves that are typically ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in the spring, followed by bright red, fleshy berries in the fall. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in landscaping.

The species is also notable for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and climates.
