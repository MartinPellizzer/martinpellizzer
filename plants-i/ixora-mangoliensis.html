Ixora mangoliensis

Ixora mangoliensis, commonly known as the mangolia flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clustered, brightly colored flowers that range from red to orange, often with a yellow or orange center. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Ixora mangoliensis is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and birds.
