Indigofera australis subsp. australis

Indigofera australis subsp.

australis, commonly known as the Australian indigofera, is a shrub native to southern Australia. It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and clusters of small, pea-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue.

The plant produces elongated, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local wildlife.
