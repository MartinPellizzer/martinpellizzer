Ilex kleinii

Ilex kleinii, commonly known as the Klein's holly, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that turn red in the fall. The plant produces small, white flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries that are attractive to birds. Ilex kleinii thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
