Ixia minuta

Ixia minuta, commonly known as the miniature ixia, is a small, flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family, which includes irises and related genera. The plant produces delicate, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often with a distinctive red or orange center. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Ixia minuta is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.
