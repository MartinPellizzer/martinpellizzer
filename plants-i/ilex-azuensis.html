Ilex azuensis

Ilex azuensis, commonly known as the Azuensian holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to Mexico.

It belongs to the family Aquifoliaceae and is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage. The leaves are typically elliptical, glossy, and dark green, with prominent veins. It produces small, red berries that are an important food source for local wildlife.

This plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.
