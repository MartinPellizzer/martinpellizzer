Impatiens thwaitesii

Impatiens thwaitesii, commonly known as the Thai impatiens, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and a compact growth habit, making it suitable for both garden beds and container planting. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shady garden areas.

Impatiens thwaitesii is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
