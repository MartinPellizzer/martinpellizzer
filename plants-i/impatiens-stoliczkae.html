Impatiens stoliczkae

Impatiens stoliczkae, commonly known as the Stoliczka's impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses worldwide.
