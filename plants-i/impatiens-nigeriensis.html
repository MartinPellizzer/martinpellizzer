Impatiens nigeriensis

Impatiens nigeriensis, commonly known as the Nigerian impatiens, is a flowering plant native to West Africa.

It belongs to the family Balsaminaceae and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to orange. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens nigeriensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
