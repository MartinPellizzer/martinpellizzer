Isatis kamienskii

Isatis kamienskii, commonly known as Chinese yellow flowering plant, is a member of the Brassicaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its yellow flowers and palmate leaves. The plant typically grows in moist environments and can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters. It is known for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional Chinese medicine.

The seeds and leaves are often used for their purported health benefits.
