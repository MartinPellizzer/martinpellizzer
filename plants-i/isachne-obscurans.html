Isachne obscurans

Isachne obscurans, commonly known as the dark isachne, is a grass species native to various regions of Asia and Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and along stream banks. The plant has slender, cylindrical stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its inflorescence consists of a loose cluster of spikelets, each containing several small, two-lobed flowers.

Isachne obscurans is noted for its ecological importance in maintaining soil stability and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
