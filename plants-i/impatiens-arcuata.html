Impatiens arcuata

Impatiens arcuata, commonly known as the zigzag impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its unique, zigzagging stems give it a distinctive appearance, making it popular in hanging baskets and as a ground cover.

Impatiens arcuata thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a versatile choice for ornamental gardening.
