Impatiens panduranganii

Impatiens panduranganii, commonly known as the Panduranganii Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a herbaceous species characterized by its upright growth habit and attractive, showy flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of small, five-petaled flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. Its leaves are usually ovate to lanceolate in shape, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Impatiens panduranganii is often found in moist, shaded environments, thriving in the understory of tropical forests.
