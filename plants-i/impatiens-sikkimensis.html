Impatiens sikkimensis

Impatiens sikkimensis, commonly known as the Sikkim Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the eastern Himalayas.

It is characterized by its delicate, spurred petals and vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant prefers cool, moist environments and is often found in forest understories at high altitudes. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered with fine hairs, while its stems are slender and erect.

Impatiens sikkimensis plays an important role in the ecological balance of its native habitat and is valued for its ornamental qualities in horticulture.
