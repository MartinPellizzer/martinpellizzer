Ixora Densiflora

Ixora densiflora is a striking tropical shrub known for its vibrant, clustered flowers that bloom throughout the year. Native to Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal regions and lowland forests. Its dense foliage and showy orange-red blossoms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and landscape design. The plant's ability to withstand salt spray makes it ideal for use in coastal gardens and as a hedge. With its hardy nature and aesthetic appeal, Ixora densiflora remains a favored plant among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts alike.
