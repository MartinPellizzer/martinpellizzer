Ilex wrightii

Ilex wrightii, commonly known as Wright's holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for various wildlife species. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges and rocky habitats.

Ilex wrightii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native landscapes.
