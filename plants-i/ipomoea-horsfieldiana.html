Ipomoea horsfieldiana

Ipomoea horsfieldiana, commonly known as the Horsfield's morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep purple. The plant produces heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture and thrives in warm, humid climates. It is a climbing vine that can grow up to several meters in length.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and traditional medicine.
