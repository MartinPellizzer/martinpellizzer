Isatis multicaulis

Isatis multicaulis, commonly known as yellow rocket, is a biennial or short-lived perennial plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from March to May. The plant has alternate, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, and it produces long, slender seed pods containing small, rounded seeds. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in roadsides, fields, and waste areas.

Isatis multicaulis is also known for its historical use in traditional medicine and as a potential source of biofuel.
