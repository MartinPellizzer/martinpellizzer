Ilex taiwanensis

Ilex taiwanensis, commonly known as Taiwan holly, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the family Aquifoliaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for various birds and mammals. Ilex taiwanensis thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas at moderate elevations.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural use in gardens and landscapes.
