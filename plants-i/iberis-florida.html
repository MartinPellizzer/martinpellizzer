Iberis florida

Iberis florida, commonly known as candytuft, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of fragrant, white or pink flowers in early spring. The plant has a mat-forming growth habit with dense, silver-gray foliage that adds ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its hardiness and ability to bloom repeatedly make it a versatile and visually appealing plant.
