Impatiens clavicuspis var. clavicuspis

Impatiens clavicuspis var. clavicuspis, commonly known as the broad-leaf impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves and clusters of showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. It has a short stem with opposite leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

This species is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in partial shade.
