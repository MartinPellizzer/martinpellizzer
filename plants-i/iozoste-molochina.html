Iozoste molochina

Iozoste molochina, commonly known as the rock fig, is a small, evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by edible, fig-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
