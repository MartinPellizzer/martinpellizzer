Isachne salzmannii

Isachne salzmannii, commonly known as Salzmann's isachne, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is characterized by its ability to thrive in dry conditions, making it a hardy plant in arid environments.

Botanically, it belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes many other grass species.
