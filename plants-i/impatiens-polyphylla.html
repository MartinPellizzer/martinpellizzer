Impatiens polyphylla

Impatiens polyphylla, commonly known as the Chinese impatiens, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and violet. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. Its flowering period occurs in late spring to early summer, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Impatiens polyphylla is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
