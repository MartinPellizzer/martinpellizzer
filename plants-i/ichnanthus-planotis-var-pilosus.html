Ichnanthus planotis var. pilosus

Ichnanthus planotis var. pilosus, commonly known as the hairy-leafed marsh orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to specific wetland habitats in eastern North America.

The plant produces small, pale green or yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.
