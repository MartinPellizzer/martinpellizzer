Iria subtetrastachya

Iria subtetrastachya, commonly known as the four-stamen iris, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family, which includes many ornamental and medicinal plants. The plant features showy, violet-blue flowers with four stamens, giving it its common name. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along stream banks.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense colonies in suitable habitats.
