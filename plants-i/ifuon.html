Ifuon

Ifuon, commonly known as the sacred flame plant, is a unique species native to the dense rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red and orange bioluminescent leaves that glow at night, a feature attributed to its specialized chloroplasts. The plant contains a rare alkaloid compound that has been studied for its potential medicinal properties. Its growth is slow, requiring specific humidity and light conditions to thrive.

Due to its rarity and ecological significance, it is protected in many regions.
