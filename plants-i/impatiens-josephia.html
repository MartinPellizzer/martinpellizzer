Impatiens josephia

Impatiens josephia, commonly known as the Himalayan Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the eastern Himalayas.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features ovate, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its flowers are showy, with five petals that range in color from white to pink and red, often with a contrasting yellow or orange base.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
