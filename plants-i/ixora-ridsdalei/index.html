Ixora Ridsdalei

Ixora ridsdalei is a rare and striking plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. Known for its vibrant orange-red flowers and compact, evergreen foliage, it stands out among other members of the Rubiaceae family. This species is highly sought after by horticulturists and plant enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique appearance. Due to habitat loss and over-collection, Ixora ridsdalei is now considered endangered in the wild. Conservation efforts are underway to protect and propagate this beautiful plant for future generations.
