Impatiens davidi

Impatiens davidi, commonly known as the Chinese violet, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that come in a range of colors, including white, pink, and purple. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in shaded to semi-shaded environments and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Impatiens davidi is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens and landscapes.
