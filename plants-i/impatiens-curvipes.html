Impatiens curvipes

Impatiens curvipes, commonly known as the curl leaf impatiens, is a species of flowering plant in the family Balsaminaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its delicate, curling leaves and clusters of brightly colored, spurred flowers that range from pink to red. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Impatiens curvipes is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
