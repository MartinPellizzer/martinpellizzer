Impatiens lotteri

Impatiens lotteri, commonly known as the mountain impatiens, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of East Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and are borne on slender stems. The plant has ovate to lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy, providing it with a distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in montane forests.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species within its native range.
