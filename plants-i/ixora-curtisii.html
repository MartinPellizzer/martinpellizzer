Ixora curtisii

Ixora curtisii, commonly known as the red ixora, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of bright red, tubular flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has opposite, elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Ixora curtisii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a hedge plant.
