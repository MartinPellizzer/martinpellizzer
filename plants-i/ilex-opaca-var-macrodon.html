Ilex opaca var. macrodon

Ilex opaca var. macrodon, commonly known as the Florida holly, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the southeastern United States.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter on the underside. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warmer climates.
