Impatiens textorii f. minuscula

Impatiens textorii f. minuscula, commonly known as the miniature impatiens, is a compact, herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, heart-shaped leaves and produces delicate, tubular flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant. It thrives in partial shade and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for container gardening and shaded garden beds.
