Ilex hylonoma

Ilex hylonoma, commonly known as the hylonome holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Caribbean islands.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically oval in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by red, fleshy berries in the fall. Ilex hylonoma is often found in moist, well-drained soils in coastal and lowland areas.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
