Impatiens amabilis

Impatiens amabilis, commonly known as the beautiful impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with opposite, ovate leaves that are often glossy in appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

The flowers are pollinated primarily by insects, contributing to its ecological significance in natural habitats.
