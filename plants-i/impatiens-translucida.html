Impatiens translucida

Impatiens translucida, commonly known as the translucent impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb with upright stems and opposite leaves. Its flowers are borne in clusters at the tips of the stems, and it thrives in moist, shady environments.

Impatiens translucida is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
