Ixia vanzijliae

Ixia vanzijliae, commonly known as the Orange Kaffir lily, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its vivid orange flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from August to October. The plant features strap-shaped, dark green leaves that are narrow and grass-like in appearance. Ixia vanzijliae thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its botanical beauty and seasonal display make it a notable ornamental plant in horticulture.
