Ilex perado f. umbrosa

Ilex perado f. umbrosa, commonly known as the shade ilex, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features dense, dark green foliage that remains lush throughout the year, making it an excellent choice for privacy screens or foundation plantings. The plant produces small, red berries that are attractive to birds, adding seasonal interest. It thrives in partial to full shade and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Its slow growth rate makes it ideal for small spaces and garden borders.
