Indigofera thesioides

Indigofera thesioides, commonly known as the blue pea or Indian blue bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters tall, featuring trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to violet. The plant is known for its striking blue or purple flowers, which are often used in ornamental gardening.

Its botanical significance lies in its role as a source of natural dyes and its use in traditional medicine.
