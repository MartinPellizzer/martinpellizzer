Iris polystachya

Iris polystachya, commonly known as the African iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, slender stems that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height, adorned with striking blue to purple flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that form a basal rosette, contributing to its distinctive appearance. Its flowering period typically occurs in late winter to early spring, making it a notable ornamental plant.

Iris polystachya is well adapted to arid conditions, thriving in well-drained soils and full sunlight.
