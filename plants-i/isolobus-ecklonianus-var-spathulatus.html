Isolobus ecklonianus var. spathulatus

Isolobus ecklonianus var. spathulatus, commonly known as the narrow-leafed isolobus, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, lanceolate leaves that are typically hairy and arranged alternately along the stem. Its flowering heads consist of both ray and disk florets, producing small yellow flowers.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland habitats.
