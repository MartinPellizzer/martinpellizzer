Iris gueldenstaedtiana subsp. subbarbata

Iris gueldenstaedtiana subsp.

subbarbata, commonly known as the subbarbated iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia. It features tall, upright stems that bear showy, violet-blue flowers with a distinctive, spurred petal.

The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in steppe and semi-desert habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species within the genus Iris.
