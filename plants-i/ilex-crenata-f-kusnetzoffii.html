Ilex crenata f. kusnetzoffii

Ilex crenata f. kusnetzoffii, commonly known as the Kusnetzoffii Japanese holly, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, with a slight wavy margin. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible for birds but not for humans. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its dense growth habit and year-round foliage make it an excellent ground cover or border plant.
