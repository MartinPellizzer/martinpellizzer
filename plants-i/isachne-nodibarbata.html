Isachne nodibarbata

Isachne nodibarbata, commonly known as the nodding barbed grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to brown florets.
