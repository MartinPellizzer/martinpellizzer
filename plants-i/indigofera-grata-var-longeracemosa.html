Indigofera grata var. longeracemosa

Indigofera grata var. longeracemosa, commonly known as the longracemose indigo.

It is a flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae, characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces elongated, racemose inflorescences. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
