Impatiens assurgens

Impatiens assurgens, commonly known as the white impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. The leaves are ovate and often have a slightly hairy texture. This species is well-suited for shaded environments and is popular in ornamental gardening.

It blooms throughout the growing season, adding a delicate floral display to gardens and landscapes.
