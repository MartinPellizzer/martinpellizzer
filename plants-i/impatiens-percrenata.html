Impatiens percrenata

Impatiens percrenata, commonly known as the red impatiens, is a herbaceous perennial native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from deep red to orange and yellow. The plant typically grows to a height of 12 to 24 inches and features heart-shaped, glossy leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.

Its rapid growth and ability to bloom throughout the growing season contribute to its ornamental value.
