Isatis leiocarpa

Isatis leiocarpa, commonly known as the Chinese yellow rocket, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cabbage and mustard. The plant is characterized by its yellow flowers and pinnately lobed leaves. It is often found in moist soils along riverbanks and in grassy areas.

Isatis leiocarpa is also noted for its potential medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
