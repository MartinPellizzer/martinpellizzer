Isika

Isika, commonly known as the African violet, is a popular ornamental plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the genus Saintpaulia and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Isika is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and is well-suited for indoor growing conditions.

Its botanical features make it a favorite among both novice and experienced gardeners.
