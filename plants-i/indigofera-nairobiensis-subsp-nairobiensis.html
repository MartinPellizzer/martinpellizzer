Indigofera nairobiensis subsp. nairobiensis

Indigofera nairobiensis subsp.

nairobiensis, commonly known as the Nairobi indigo. It is a shrub native to East Africa, particularly Kenya, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers.

The plant produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in dry, well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and potential ecological importance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
