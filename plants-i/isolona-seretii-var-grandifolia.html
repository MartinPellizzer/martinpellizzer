Isolona seretii var. grandifolia

Isolona seretii var. grandifolia, commonly known as the large-flowered isolona, is a species native to South Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, tubular flowers. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

The variety is distinguished by its larger flower size compared to other isolona species.
