Impatiens mirabilis

Impatiens mirabilis, commonly known as the Marvelous Impatiens, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a range of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically grows as an annual herb, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are heart-shaped and glossy, while its stems are slender and often branched.

Impatiens mirabilis thrives in moist, well-drained soil and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
