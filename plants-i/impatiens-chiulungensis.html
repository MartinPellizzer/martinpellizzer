Impatiens chiulungensis

Impatiens chiulungensis, commonly known as the Chiulung Impatiens, is a species of flowering plant in the family Balsaminaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of southern China, particularly in Yunnan Province. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of showy, spurred flowers that range in color from pink to white. Impatiens chiulungensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
