Iberis Carnosa Subsp. Grosmiquelii

Iberis carnosa subsp. grosmiquelii is a rare and striking member of the Brassicaceae family. Native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe, it thrives in rocky and well-drained soils. This subspecies is distinguished by its dense clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a sought-after plant for rock gardens and alpine settings. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in horticultural circles.
