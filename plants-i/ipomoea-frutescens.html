Ipomoea frutescens

Ipomoea frutescens, commonly known as morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking blue or violet flowers, which open in the morning and close in the evening. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is known for its rapid growth habit. It thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

Ipomoea frutescens is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
