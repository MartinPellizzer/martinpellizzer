Ixora densa

Ixora densa, commonly known as the dense flame or dense ixora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of bright red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves and a shrub-like growth habit. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Ixora densa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a hedge plant.
