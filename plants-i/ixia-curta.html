Ixia curta

Ixia curta, commonly known as the dwarf firecracker, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. Ixia curta thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
