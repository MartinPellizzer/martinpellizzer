Ixora soyauxii

Ixora soyauxii, commonly known as the Soyaux's flame.

It is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its bright red or orange-red inflorescences, which are clustered in dense, showy clusters. It thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
