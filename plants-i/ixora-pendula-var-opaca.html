Ixora pendula var. opaca

Ixora pendula var. opaca, commonly known as the opaca ixora, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant produces clusters of small, bright orange-red flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a smooth texture with a glossy appearance.

The opaca ixora is valued for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
