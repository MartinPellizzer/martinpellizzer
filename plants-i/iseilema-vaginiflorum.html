Iseilema vaginiflorum

Iseilema vaginiflorum, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical shrub native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety pubescence. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds.

Iseilema vaginiflorum is often found in moist, shaded environments and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
