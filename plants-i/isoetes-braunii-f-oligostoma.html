Isoetes braunii f. oligostoma

Isoetes braunii f. oligostoma, commonly known as the slender water fern, is a rare and delicate aquatic plant belonging to the genus Isoetes.

It is characterized by its small, spicate morphology and is typically found in shallow, still waters with soft substrates. The plant exhibits a unique reproductive strategy, producing spores rather than seeds, which are dispersed by water currents. Its leaves are usually short and narrow, with a distinct central axis.
