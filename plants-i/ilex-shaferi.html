Ilex shaferi

Ilex shaferi, commonly known as the Shafer holly, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for various birds and wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or forested areas.

Ilex shaferi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its dense foliage and seasonal color.
