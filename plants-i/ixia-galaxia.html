Ixia galaxia

Ixia galaxia, commonly known as the star flower, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, star-shaped flowers that come in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy inflorescences that bloom in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated for its unique and colorful display.
