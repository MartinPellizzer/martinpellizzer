Impatiens viguieri

Impatiens viguieri, commonly known as the Sierra Leone impatiens, is a flowering plant native to West Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features showy, spurred flowers that are usually pink or white in color. Its leaves are ovate and often have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Impatiens vigueri is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade.
