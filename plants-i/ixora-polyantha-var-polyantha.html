Ixora polyantha var. polyantha

Ixora polyantha var. polyantha, commonly known as the Chinese flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, glossy green foliage and clusters of bright red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant produces small, oval-shaped fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
