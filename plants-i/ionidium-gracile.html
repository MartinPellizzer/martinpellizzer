Ionidium gracile

Ionidium gracile, commonly known as the slender ionidium, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, pale green leaves and clusters of tiny, white, bell-shaped flowers. The plant prefers shaded, humid environments and is often found growing on the forest floor or on the trunks of large trees. Its roots are fibrous and help it anchor in the rich, organic soil of its habitat.
