Ilex leucoclada var. angustifolia

Ilex leucoclada var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrowleaf holly, is a shrub or small tree native to the southeastern United States.

It features glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, white flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Its evergreen nature makes it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
