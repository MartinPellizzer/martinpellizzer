Isoetes chubutiana

Isoetes chubutiana, commonly known as the Chubut watermace, is a species of aquatic plant belonging to the order Isoetales.

It is characterized by its dense, ribbon-like leaves that form a dense mat on the bottom of freshwater habitats. The plant is native to South America, particularly found in Patagonia, where it thrives in shallow, still waters. Isoetes chubutiana is a member of the genus Isoetes, which is known for its unique morphology and ecological significance in wetland ecosystems.
