Verbascum × martini

Verbascum × martini, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a flowering plant in the family Scrophulariaceae.

It is a cross between Verbascum blattaria and Verbascum blattarioides, resulting in a plant with distinctive silvery-gray foliage. The leaves are large, velvety, and often used in traditional herbal medicine. It produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer.

This plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions.
