Viola okuboi var. glabra

Viola okuboi var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth viola, is a species of flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and vibrant violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and gardens. Its leaves are smooth and slightly hairy, while the flowers bloom in early spring.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
