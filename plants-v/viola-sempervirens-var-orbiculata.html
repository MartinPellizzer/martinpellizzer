Viola sempervirens var. orbiculata

Viola sempervirens var. orbiculata, commonly known as the roundleaf violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. The flowers are typically violet-blue in color, with five petals and a distinctive yellow throat. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
