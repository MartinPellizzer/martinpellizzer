Vibo

Vibo, commonly known as the Brazilian cherry or *Eugenia uniflora*, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to South America.

It produces small, bright red berries that are edible and often used in jams and desserts. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves and a compact, bushy growth habit. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

The botanical features of Vibo make it a popular ornamental plant as well as a source of culinary and medicinal uses.
