Veronica linkiana

Veronica linkiana, commonly known as the Link's speedwell, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, blue to purple in color, and arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems.

Veronica linkiana thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
