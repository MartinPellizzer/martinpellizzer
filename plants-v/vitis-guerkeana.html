Vitis guerkeana

Vitis guerkeana, commonly known as Guerke's grapevine, is a species of flowering plant in the Vitaceae family.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its climbing habit and compound leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into small, round berries. Vitis guerkeana is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, growing in shaded, moist environments.

It has been studied for its potential medicinal properties and ecological significance in its native habitat.
