Vilfa subtilis

Vilfa subtilis, commonly known as the delicate violet, is a flowering plant characterized by its small, fragrant blossoms and lance-shaped leaves.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its flowers, which range in color from pale purple to white, bloom in early spring.

Vilfa subtilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
