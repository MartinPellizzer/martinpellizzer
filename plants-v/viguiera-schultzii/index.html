Viguiera Schultzii

Viguiera schultzii is a striking perennial plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. Known for its vibrant yellow flowers and silvery foliage, it adds a unique visual appeal to gardens and landscapes. This species thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it ideal for arid and semi-arid regions. Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it a favored choice for xeriscaping. Viguiera schultzii not only enhances aesthetic value but also supports local pollinators and wildlife.
