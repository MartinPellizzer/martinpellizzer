Vitex schweinfurthii

Vitex schweinfurthii, commonly known as the African blue mistflower, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, often branched stems and small, opposite leaves that are typically ovate to lanceolate in shape. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, blue to purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is often found in open woodlands and along riverbanks, thriving in well-drained soils.

Vitex schweinfurthii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
