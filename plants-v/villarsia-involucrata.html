Villarsia involucrata

Villarsia involucrata, commonly known as the Cape rose, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its small, pink or white flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling roses. The plant has a woody stem and produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun exposure.

Villarsia involucrata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
