Viola tricolor var. hymettia

Viola tricolor var. hymettia, commonly known as the Hymettian violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of violet, yellow, and white, typically blooming in spring. It prefers moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
