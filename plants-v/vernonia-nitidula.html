Vernonia nitidula

Vernonia nitidula, commonly known as the smooth vernonia, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States and parts of Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Vernonia nitidula is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
