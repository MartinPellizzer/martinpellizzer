Viola bourgaei

Viola bourgaei, commonly known as the Japanese violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that range in color from white to violet, often with a distinctive yellow eye. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for shady garden beds.

Viola bourgaei is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
