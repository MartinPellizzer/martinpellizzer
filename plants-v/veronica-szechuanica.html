Veronica szechuanica

Veronica szechuanica, commonly known as Sichuan speedwell, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its small, blue or purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, rocky habitats and is often found in mountainous regions. It has hairy leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Veronica szechuanica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
