Vanda thwaitesii

Vanda thwaitesii, commonly known as the Thwaites' orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, pendant-like inflorescences that bear showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange. The plant has strap-shaped, dark green leaves and thrives in humid, shaded environments. It requires good air circulation and regular watering to flourish.

Vanda thwaitesii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in both indoor and greenhouse settings.
