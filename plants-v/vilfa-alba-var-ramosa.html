Vilfa alba var. ramosa

Vilfa alba var. ramosa, commonly known as the white vilfa or silver vilfa, is a variant of the vilfa alba species.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-white foliage that gives it a striking appearance. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

It is a low-maintenance plant that is often used in rock gardens or as an ornamental ground cover.
