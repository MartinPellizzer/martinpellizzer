Verbena × goodmanii

Verbena × goodmanii, commonly known as the hybrid verbena, is a flowering plant belonging to the Verbenaceae family.

It is a cross between Verbena rigida and Verbena bonariensis, resulting in a plant with distinctive ornamental qualities. The species features compact growth, with vibrant blue to purple flower clusters that bloom throughout the summer. Its foliage is typically gray-green, adding visual interest to gardens.

Verbena × goodmanii is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for perennial gardens.
