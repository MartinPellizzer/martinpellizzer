Vaccinium steyermarkii

Vaccinium steyermarkii, commonly known as the Steyermark's huckleberry, is a species of evergreen shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like blueberries and cranberries. The plant produces small, edible berries that are typically dark blue to black in color. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.
