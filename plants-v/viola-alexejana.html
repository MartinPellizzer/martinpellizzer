Viola alexejana

Viola alexejana, commonly known as the Russian violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces clusters of violet-blue flowers with five petals, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade.

Viola alexejana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate beauty.
