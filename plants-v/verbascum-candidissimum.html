Verbascum candidissimum

Verbascum candidissimum, commonly known as the white mullein.

It is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and western Asia. The plant features tall, hairy stems and large, velvety leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. Its flowers are pale yellow to white, appearing in late summer.

It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in meadows and along roadsides.
