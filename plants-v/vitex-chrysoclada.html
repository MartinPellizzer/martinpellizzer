Vitex chrysoclada

Vitex chrysoclada, commonly known as the golden vitex, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually violet or blue in color, arranged in clusters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

The fruit is a dry, two-lobed capsule that contains several small, black seeds.
