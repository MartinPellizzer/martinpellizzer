Vriesea sintenisii

Vriesea sintenisii, commonly known as the silver sword, is a striking bromeliad native to the high-altitude regions of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its tall, sword-like leaves that are covered in a dense layer of white, powdery wax, giving it a silvery appearance. The plant produces vibrant orange or red flowers that emerge from the center of the leaf rosette, often in late winter or early spring. Vriesea sintenisii thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance and relatively easy care make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
