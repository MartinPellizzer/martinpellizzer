Vernonia ramaswamii

Vernonia ramaswamii, commonly known as the Ramaswamii ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered yellow flowers. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Vernonia ramaswamii is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
