Vernonia blanda

Vernonia blanda, commonly known as smallleaf ironweed, is a perennial herb native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet, featuring lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, pale blue to violet flowers. Its foliage is often covered in fine hairs, giving it a slightly fuzzy appearance.

Vernonia blanda is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
