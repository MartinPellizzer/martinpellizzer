Viguiera australis

Viguiera australis, commonly known as the Australian ironwood, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in dry, rocky soils. The plant features small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, providing year-round foliage.

Viguiera australis is valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in arid environments.
