Verbena reitzii var. castrensis

Verbena reitzii var. castrensis, commonly known as the Castrensis verbena, is a compact, flowering plant native to South America.

It produces vibrant, clustered flowers in shades of purple and blue, which bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it an ideal plant for gardeners seeking a hardy, decorative addition to their landscape.
