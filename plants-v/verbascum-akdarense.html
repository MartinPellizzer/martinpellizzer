Verbascum akdarense

Verbascum akdarense, commonly known as the Akdare mullein, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Turkey.

It belongs to the genus Verbascum, which includes species known for their fuzzy, velvety leaves. The plant produces tall, branched stems that bear yellow flowers in the summer months. Its leaves are covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid environments.

Verbascum akdarense is often found in rocky and dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to challenging conditions.
