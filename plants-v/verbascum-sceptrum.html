Verbascum sceptrum

Verbascum sceptrum, commonly known as the purple mullein, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the genus Verbascum, which includes several species of flowering plants known for their large, showy flowers. The plant produces tall, hairy stalks that bear clusters of yellow to orange flowers, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Verbascum sceptrum is also valued for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally for its soothing effects.
