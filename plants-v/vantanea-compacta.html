Vantanea compacta

Vantanea compacta, commonly known as the compact velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and dark green, velvety leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, often accompanied by bracts. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often found in disturbed soils or along riverbanks.

Vantanea compacta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticulture for its aesthetic qualities.
