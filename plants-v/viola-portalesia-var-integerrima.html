Viola portalesia var. integerrima

Viola portalesia var. integerrima, commonly known as the Portalesia violet, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, showy flowers that range in color from violet to deep purple, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded woodland areas.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native habitat.
