Vriesea verrucosa

Vriesea verrucosa, commonly known as the疣花凤梨, is a species of bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy inflorescence that can range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its rosette-shaped leaves are thick and waxy, adapted to collect and store water.

Vriesea verrucosa is popular as a ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
