Vilfa pulchella

Vilfa pulchella, commonly known as the beautiful violet, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that range in color from purple to blue. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a velvety texture, while its flowers bloom in early spring.

Vilfa pulchella is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.
