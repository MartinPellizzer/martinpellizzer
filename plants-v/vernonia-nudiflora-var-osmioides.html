Vernonia nudiflora var. osmioides

Vernonia nudiflora var. osmioides, commonly known as the smooth Vernonia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, open habitats such as prairies and woodland edges. The plant features upright, branching stems covered in soft, hairy foliage. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and clustered in panicles, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is known for its ability to thrive in well-drained soils and its role in supporting local pollinator populations.
