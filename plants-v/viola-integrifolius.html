Viola integrifolius

Viola integrifolius, commonly known as the heartleaf violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and prominent veins. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically purple or violet in color, often with yellow markings. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found in woodlands or along stream banks.

Viola integrifolius is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical use in traditional medicine.
