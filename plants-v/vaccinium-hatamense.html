Vaccinium hatamense

Vaccinium hatamense, commonly known as the Hatamena huckleberry, is a species of shrub native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes many acid-loving plants. The plant produces small, edible berries that are typically dark blue or black in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

The leaves are evergreen, glossy, and have a leathery texture.
