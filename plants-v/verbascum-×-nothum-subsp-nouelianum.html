Verbascum × nothum subsp. nouelianum

Verbascum × nothum subsp.

This subspecies is a natural hybrid between Verbascum nothum and Verbascum nouelianum, resulting in a unique combination of traits from both parents. It typically thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, often found in rocky or arid environments.
