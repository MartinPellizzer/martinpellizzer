Vangueria volkensii var. volkensii

Vangueria volkensii var. volkensii, commonly known as the African milk bush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, white to pale yellow flowers and woody, aromatic stems. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with serrated edges, and it produces small, fleshy, red or orange fruits. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky soils.

The plant is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
