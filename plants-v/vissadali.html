Vissadali

Vissadali, commonly known as the "flower of the desert," is a rare and resilient plant native to arid regions.

It possesses thick, waxy leaves that help minimize water loss in its harsh environment. The plant produces striking, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and purple. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to access deep underground water sources.

Vissadali is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
