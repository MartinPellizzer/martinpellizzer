Verbena bracteata f. brevibracteata

Verbena bracteata f. brevibracteata, commonly known as the narrow-bract verbena, is a compact, flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It features small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or violet in color, arranged in dense clusters. The plant has hairy, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine, soft pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
