Vochysia tucanorum var. macrostachya

Vochysia tucanorum var. macrostachya, commonly known as the giant sycamore, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves and showy, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces long, cylindrical fruits that contain numerous small seeds. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
