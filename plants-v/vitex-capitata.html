Vitex capitata

Vitex capitata, commonly known as chaste tree, is a deciduous shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy on the underside. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple, typically blooming in summer. Its fruits are small, woody capsules that contain numerous seeds.

Vitex capitata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
