Vittadinia brachycomoides var. latifolia

Vittadinia brachycomoides var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf vittadinia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its composite flower heads and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in coastal regions and is adapted to sandy soils. It produces yellow flowers that bloom during the spring and summer months.

Vittadinia brachycomoides var. latifolia is often found in heathlands and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
