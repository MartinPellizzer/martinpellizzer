Viticella kirtleyi

Viticella kirtleyi, commonly known as the Kirtley's ivy, is a species of flowering plant in the family Vitaceae.

It is native to the southern United States, particularly Texas, and thrives in warm, arid environments. Viticella kirtleyi is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to climb or trail over structures.
