Vitex turczaninowii

Vitex turczaninowii, commonly known as the Chinese vitex, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the verbena family (Verbenaceae) and is characterized by its opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to purple, typically blooming in summer. It is often used in traditional Chinese medicine and as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and flowering habits.

Vitex turczaninowii prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens in temperate climates.
