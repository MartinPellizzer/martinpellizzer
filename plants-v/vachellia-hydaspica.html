Vachellia hydaspica

Vachellia hydaspica, commonly known as the Indian legume or hydaspic acacia, is a flowering plant native to the Indian subcontinent.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in dry, arid environments. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in spherical inflorescences. Its leaves are bipinnate, consisting of numerous small leaflets, and it has a deep root system that helps it survive in harsh conditions.
