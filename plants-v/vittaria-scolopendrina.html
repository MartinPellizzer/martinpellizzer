Vittaria scolopendrina

Vittaria scolopendrina, commonly known as the zigzag fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its unique, zigzag-shaped fronds that are often used in horticultural displays. The plant has a delicate, lace-like appearance with small, linear leaves arranged in a spiral pattern. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich substrates.

Vittaria scolopendrina is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or terrariums.
