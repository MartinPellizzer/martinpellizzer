Vitex pervillei

Vitex pervillei, commonly known as the Madagascar glorybower, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the verbena family (Verbenaceae) and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a rough texture.
