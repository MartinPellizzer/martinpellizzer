Viola itoubou

Viola itoubou, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments and prefers well-draining soil. Its flowers bloom in spring and summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Viola itoubou is popular as a decorative plant due to its compact size and long-lasting blooms.
