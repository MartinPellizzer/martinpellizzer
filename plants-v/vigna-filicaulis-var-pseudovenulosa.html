Vigna filicaulis var. pseudovenulosa

Vigna filicaulis var. pseudovenulosa, commonly known as the slender cowpea, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features trifoliate leaves with finely serrated margins and produces small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant bears cylindrical, hairy pods that contain several elliptical seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its nutritional seeds and is often cultivated for both food and ornamental purposes.
