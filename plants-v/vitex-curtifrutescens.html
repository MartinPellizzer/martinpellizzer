Vitex curtifrutescens

Vitex curtifrutescens, commonly known as the small-fruited chaste tree, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its slender, often branched stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically purple or blue in color, which bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a dry capsule that contains several small, black seeds.

Vitex curtifrutescens is often used in traditional medicine for its purported therapeutic properties.
