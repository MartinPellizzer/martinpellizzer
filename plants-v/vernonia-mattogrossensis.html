Vernonia mattogrossensis

Vernonia mattogrossensis, commonly known as the Brazilian ironweed, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Vernonia mattogrossensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
