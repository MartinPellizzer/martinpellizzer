Vicia ervilia

Vicia ervilia, commonly known as the common vetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces slender, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. Vicia ervilia is typically found in temperate regions and is often seen growing along roadsides or in disturbed soils.

It is a hardy plant that can thrive in a variety of soil types and is sometimes used as a cover crop.
