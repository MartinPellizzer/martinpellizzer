Verbascum × humnickii

Verbascum × humnickii, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Verbascum blattaria and Verbascum phlomoides.

It is a robust, herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features large, fuzzy, gray-green leaves that are soft to the touch and covered in fine hairs. Its showy, yellow-orange flowers appear in clusters at the ends of the stems, blooming in late summer.

This plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soils in full sun.
