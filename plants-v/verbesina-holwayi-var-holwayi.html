Verbesina holwayi var. holwayi

Verbesina holwayi var. holwayi, commonly known as the golden crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking yellow-orange inflorescence, which resembles a crown, and its upright, branching growth habit. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in dense, showy heads. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for dry gardens.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
