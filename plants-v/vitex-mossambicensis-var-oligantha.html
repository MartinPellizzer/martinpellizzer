Vitex mossambicensis var. oligantha

Vitex mossambicensis var. oligantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved chaste tree, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal and rocky habitats. The plant produces small, fragrant white or pale blue flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and opposite, with a slightly hairy texture.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local medicine.
