Vismia calvescens

Vismia calvescens, commonly known as the red-stemmed vismia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to parts of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, reddish-barked trunk and large, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruit is a woody capsule containing several seeds.

Vismia calvescens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
