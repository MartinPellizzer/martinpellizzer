Vincetoxicum streptolobum

Vincetoxicum streptolobum, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Vincetoxicum streptolobum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
