Vitex bequaertii

Vitex bequaertii, commonly known as the Madagascar vitex, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a rough texture and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to purple. Its fruit is a dry capsule containing several seeds.

Vitex bequaertii is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
