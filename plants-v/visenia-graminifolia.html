Visenia graminifolia

Visenia graminifolia, commonly known as the slender visenia, is a herbaceous plant characterized by its fine, grass-like leaves.

It typically grows in moist, temperate environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or white in color. Its root system is fibrous and helps stabilize soil in its natural habitat.

Visenia graminifolia is valued for its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
