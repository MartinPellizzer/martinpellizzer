Veronica austriaca var. tenuis

Veronica austriaca var. tenuis, commonly known as slender speedwell, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe.

It features small, blue or violet flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to July. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodland floors and stream banks.

Its compact growth habit and delicate flowers make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a hardy, ornamental ground cover.
