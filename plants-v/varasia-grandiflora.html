Varasia grandiflora

Varasia grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered varasia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Varasia grandiflora thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories or near water sources.
