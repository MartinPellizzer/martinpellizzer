Vriesea marnier-lapostollei

Vriesea marnier-lapostollei, commonly known as the Marnier-Lapostolle Vriesea, is a tropical bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, recurved inflorescence that displays vibrant orange to red flowers. The plant features a rosette of narrow, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy bloom. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
