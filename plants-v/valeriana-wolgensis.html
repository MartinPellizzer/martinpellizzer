Valeriana wolgensis

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its upright stems and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant produces white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a distinctive aromatic scent.
