Viola formosana var. kawakamii

Viola formosana var. kawakamii, commonly known as the Kawakamii violet, is a species of flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and delicate, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in woodland areas and shaded environments, preferring moist, well-drained soil. Its flowers bloom in early spring, adding a splash of color to its natural habitat.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
