Verbascum dechyanum

Verbascum dechyanum, commonly known as the Armenian mullein, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus and Central Asia.

It is a member of the Scrophulariaceae family and is characterized by its tall, hairy stems and large, showy yellow flowers. Verbascum dechyanum is also valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
