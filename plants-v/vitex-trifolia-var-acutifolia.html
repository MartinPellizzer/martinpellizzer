Vitex trifolia var. acutifolia

Vitex trifolia var. acutifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved chaste tree, is a deciduous shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and have a slightly serrated margin. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, which are usually violet or blue in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various climates.
