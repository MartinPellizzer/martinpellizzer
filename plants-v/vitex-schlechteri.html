Vitex schlechteri

Vitex schlechteri, commonly known as the Chinese vitex or red flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that turn reddish in autumn. The plant produces fragrant, violet-blue flowers in panicles, which bloom in late summer. Its fruit is a dry, three-lobed capsule containing several seeds.

Vitex schlechteri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to well-drained soils.
