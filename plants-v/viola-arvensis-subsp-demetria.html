Viola arvensis subsp. demetria

Viola arvensis subsp.

demetria, commonly known as the field violet, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows.

The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive violet or purple coloration, and its flowers are usually pale purple or white with yellow markings. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

This subspecies is known for its ornamental value and is often used in gardens and conservation efforts.
