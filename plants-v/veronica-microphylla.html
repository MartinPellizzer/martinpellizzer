Veronica microphylla

Veronica microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved speedwell, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, dark green leaves that are densely arranged along its stems. Its flowers are blue to violet in color, with five petals, and bloom in early summer.

Veronica microphylla thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.
