Verbascum misirdalianum

Verbascum misirdalianum, commonly known as the Armenian mullein, is a flowering plant native to the mountains of Armenia and Turkey.

It belongs to the Scrophulariaceae family and is characterized by its tall, hairy stems and large, showy yellow flowers. Verbascum misirdalianum is also valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
