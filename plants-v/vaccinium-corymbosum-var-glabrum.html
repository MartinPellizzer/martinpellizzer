Vaccinium corymbosum var. glabrum

Vaccinium corymbosum var. glabrum, commonly known as the smooth blueberry, is a shrub native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and leaves, which distinguish it from other blueberry varieties. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that develop into berries rich in antioxidants. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is widely cultivated for its edible fruits, which are valued for their flavor and nutritional content.
