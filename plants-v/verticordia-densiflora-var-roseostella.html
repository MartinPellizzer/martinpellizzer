Verticordia densiflora var. roseostella

Verticordia densiflora var. roseostella, commonly known as the Rose-stemmed Featherflower, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and its striking pink to rose-colored flowers. The plant produces small, thread-like leaves that are arranged in a spiral pattern along the stems. Its flowers typically bloom in spring, attracting nectar-feeding insects such as bees and birds.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil and full sun.
