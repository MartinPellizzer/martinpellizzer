Vitex brevilabiata

Vitex brevilabiata, commonly known as the narrow-lipped chaste tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly serrated margin. Its showy, fragrant flowers are usually purple or blue and bloom in clusters.

Vitex brevilabiata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional medicinal uses in some cultures.
