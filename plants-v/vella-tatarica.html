Vella tatarica

Vella tatarica, commonly known as Tatarian bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple, typically blooming in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands or rocky areas.

Vella tatarica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
