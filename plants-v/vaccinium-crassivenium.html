Vaccinium crassivenium

Vaccinium crassivenium, commonly known as the broad-leaved blueberry, is a species of evergreen shrub native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes many acid-loving plants, and it thrives in cool, moist, and well-drained soils. Vaccinium crassivenium is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local wildlife.
