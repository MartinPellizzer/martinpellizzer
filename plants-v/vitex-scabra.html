Vitex scabra

Vitex scabra, commonly known as the rough vitex or wild rue, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa, Asia, and Australia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in dry, arid environments. The plant has small, opposite leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, blue or purple flowers. Its fruit is a small, woody capsule that contains several seeds.

Vitex scabra is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
