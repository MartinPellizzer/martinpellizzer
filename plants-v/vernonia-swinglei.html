Vernonia swinglei

Vernonia swinglei, commonly known as the Swingle's ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stem and clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as prairies and wetlands. Its foliage is often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.
