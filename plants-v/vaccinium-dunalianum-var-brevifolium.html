Vaccinium dunalianum var. brevifolium

Vaccinium dunalianum var. brevifolium, commonly known as the short-leaved cranberry shrub, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the northern regions of North America.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its small, leathery leaves and clusters of bright red berries. The plant thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in wetland or bog environments. It plays an important ecological role by providing food for various wildlife species.

Its ornamental value also makes it a popular choice for landscaping in suitable climates.
