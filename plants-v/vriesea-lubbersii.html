Vriesea lubbersii

Vriesea lubbersii, commonly known as the Lubbersii Vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting inflorescence that displays a range of colors from red to orange and yellow. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that collect water, which is essential for its survival in its natural habitat. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Vriesea lubbersii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
