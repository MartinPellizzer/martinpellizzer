Vernonanthura oaxacana

Vernonanthura oaxacana, commonly known as the Oaxaca daisy, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments. Its bright yellow to orange petals are surrounded by white ray florets, making it visually striking.

Vernonanthura oaxacana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
