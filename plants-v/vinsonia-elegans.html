Vinsonia elegans

Vinsonia elegans, commonly known as the elegant vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and the presence of tendrils that aid in its support. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically white or pale yellow in color. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a velvety texture.

Vinsonia elegans is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
