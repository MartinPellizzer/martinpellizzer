Vachellia Abyssinica Subsp. Calophylla

Vachellia abyssinica subsp. calophylla is a resilient shrub native to the highlands of Ethiopia. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh climates. Known for its dense foliage and nitrogen-fixing abilities, it plays a vital role in soil enrichment. This subspecies is often used for erosion control and as a source of firewood. Its ecological significance makes it an important plant in local ecosystems.
