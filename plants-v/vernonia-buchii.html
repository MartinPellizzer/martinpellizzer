Vernonia buchii

Vernonia buchii, commonly known as the African ironweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its striking blue to purple flower heads appear in late summer and are arranged in dense, spike-like clusters.

Vernonia buchii is known for its drought tolerance and is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
