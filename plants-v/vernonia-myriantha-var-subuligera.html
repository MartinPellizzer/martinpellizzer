Vernonia myriantha var. subuligera

Vernonia myriantha var. subuligera, commonly known as the slender vernonia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, needle-like leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
