Verbena × dermenii

Verbena × dermenii, commonly known as the hybrid vervain, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and profuse display of blue to purple flowers. The plant features opposite, hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Verbena × dermenii is known for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
