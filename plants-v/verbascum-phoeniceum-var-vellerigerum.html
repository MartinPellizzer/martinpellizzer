Verbascum phoeniceum var. vellerigerum

Verbascum phoeniceum var. vellerigerum, commonly known as the silky mullein, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, fuzzy, gray-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy yellow flowers in the summer, which are clustered in a cylindrical spike. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for dry gardens.

Its fluffy leaves and striking flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
