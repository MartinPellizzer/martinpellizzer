Veronica scutellata var. parmularia

Veronica scutellata var. parmularia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved speedwell, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that bear small, blue or violet flowers arranged in clusters. The leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, often with a bluish-green hue. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it a versatile garden plant.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
