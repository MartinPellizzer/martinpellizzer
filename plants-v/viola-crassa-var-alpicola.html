Viola crassa var. alpicola

Viola crassa var. alpicola, commonly known as the alpine pansy, is a perennial flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, dark green leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of purple, violet, and white. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or mountainous habitats. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been cultivated for its beauty in gardens.

The species is also notable for its ability to withstand harsh alpine conditions, making it a resilient member of its ecosystem.
