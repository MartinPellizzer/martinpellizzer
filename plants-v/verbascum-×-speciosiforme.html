Verbascum × speciosiforme

Verbascum × speciosiforme, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a tall biennial or perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features large, fuzzy, gray-green leaves that are soft to the touch and often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its hybrid nature, it may exhibit a variety of morphological traits depending on its parent species.
