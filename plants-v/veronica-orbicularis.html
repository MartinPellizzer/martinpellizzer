Veronica orbicularis

Veronica orbicularis, commonly known as the roundleaf speedwell, is a herbaceous perennial native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, blue to purple flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom from late spring to early summer. The plant has rounded, succulent leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact size and ornamental flowers make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens.
