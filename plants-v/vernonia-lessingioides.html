Vernonia lessingioides

Vernonia lessingioides, commonly known as the lesser ironweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in poor soils.

The genus Vernonia is part of the sunflower family, Asteraceae, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
