Vigieria

Vigieria, commonly known as the golden rain tree, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant produces distinctive, spiny fruit pods that contain several seeds. Vigieria thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Its rapid growth and showy appearance make it a versatile addition to gardens and urban landscapes.
