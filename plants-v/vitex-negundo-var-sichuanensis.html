Vitex negundo var. sichuanensis

Vitex negundo var. sichuanensis, commonly known as the Chinese vitex or Sichuan vitex, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate to elliptical leaves that are often slightly hairy on the underside. The plant produces fragrant, blue to purple flowers in panicles during the summer months. Its fruits are small, woody drupes that contain a single seed.

It is often used in traditional Chinese medicine and as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and flowers.
