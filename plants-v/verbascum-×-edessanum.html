Verbascum × edessanum

Verbascum × edessanum, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the genus Verbascum, which includes several species of flowering plants known for their fuzzy, woolly leaves. The plant produces showy, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to gardens.

Its common name reflects its resemblance to the traditional mullein plant, which has similar characteristics.
