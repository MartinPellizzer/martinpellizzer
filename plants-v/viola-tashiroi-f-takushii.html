Viola tashiroi f. takushii

Viola tashiroi f. takushii, commonly known as the Takushii violet, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its small, heart-shaped leaves and pale purple or white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its flowers bloom in early spring, adding a subtle beauty to its natural habitat.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
