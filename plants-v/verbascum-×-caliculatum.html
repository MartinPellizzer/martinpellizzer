Verbascum × caliculatum

Verbascum × caliculatum, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the genus Verbascum, which includes various species of flowering plants known for their fuzzy, velvety leaves. This hybrid plant typically features tall, erect stems covered in soft, hairy foliage. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, arranged in dense spikes at the top of the plant.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
