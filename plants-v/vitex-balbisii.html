Vitex balbisii

Vitex balbisii, commonly known as the Balbisii chaste tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often hairy on the underside. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to violet, typically blooming in summer. Its fruits are dry, three-lobed capsules that release seeds when mature.

Vitex balbisii is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
