Viola × holmiana

Viola × holmiana, commonly known as the Holm viola, is a hybrid species within the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, often violet or purple flowers. Native to Europe, this plant thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The flowers typically bloom in spring, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental and hardy plants.
