Vellozia annulata

Vellozia annulata, commonly known as the ringed vellozia, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, tubular leaves that are often adorned with red or purple markings, which help in reducing water loss in its harsh environment. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate contrast to its rugged appearance. Vellozia annulata is adapted to survive in nutrient-poor soils and can withstand prolonged periods of drought.

Its distinctive morphology and ecological resilience make it a notable species in its native habitat.
