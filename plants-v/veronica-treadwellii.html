Veronica treadwellii

Veronica treadwellii, commonly known as Treadwell's speedwell, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Plantaginaceae family and typically grows in rocky, arid environments. The plant features small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, adapted to reduce water loss.
