Veronica chamaedrys f. umbrosa

Veronica chamaedrys f. umbrosa, commonly known as the shadow veronica, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe.

It features compact, mat-forming growth with small, dark green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of blue to purple flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a hardy and attractive choice for gardeners seeking shade-tolerant plants.
