Vatica globosa

Vatica globosa, commonly known as the globe ironwood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and sturdy, woody trunk. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its fruit is a woody capsule containing several seeds.

Vatica globosa is valued for its hard, durable wood and is often found in lowland rainforests.
