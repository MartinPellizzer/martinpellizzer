Verbesina hypoleuca

Verbesina hypoleuca, commonly known as the white crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant produces showy yellow flowers surrounded by white bracts, which give it its common name. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in prairies and open woodlands.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for native plant gardens.
