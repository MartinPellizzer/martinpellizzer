Verbascum × ersin-yucelii

It is characterized by its large, fuzzy, gray-green leaves and showy yellow flowers that bloom in summer. Native to Turkey, this species thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in mountainous regions. Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a notable species in both horticulture and ecological studies.
