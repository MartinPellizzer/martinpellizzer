Vernonia papillosa

Vernonia papillosa, commonly known as the silky Vernonia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, yellow to orange flowers. The leaves are typically covered in fine, silky hairs, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
