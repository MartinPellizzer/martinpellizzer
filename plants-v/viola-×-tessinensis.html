Viola × tessinensis

Viola × tessinensis, commonly known as the hybrid violet, is a perennial flowering plant that belongs to the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, showy flowers that can range in color from violet to white. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and gardens. It is known for its adaptability and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

Viola × tessinensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is popular among gardeners for its beauty and resilience.
