Viola × ritschliana

Viola × ritschliana, commonly known as the Viola × ritschliana, is a hybrid species within the viola family, Violaceae.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of purple, violet, and white. The plant is native to Europe and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial to full shade.

Viola × ritschliana is known for its ability to spread through rhizomes, forming dense mats of foliage and flowers.
