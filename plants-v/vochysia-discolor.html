Vochysia discolor

Vochysia discolor, commonly known as the pink star sycamore, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive pinkish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a striking visual element to its canopy. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth, waxy texture. It thrives in humid environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
