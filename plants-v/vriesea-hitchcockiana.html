Vriesea hitchcockiana

Vriesea hitchcockiana, commonly known as the Hitchcock's Vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, spathaceous inflorescence that displays a range of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a central tank to collect water. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Vriesea hitchcockiana is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
