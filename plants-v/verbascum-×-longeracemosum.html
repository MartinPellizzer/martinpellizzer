Verbascum × longeracemosum

Verbascum × longeracemosum, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial plant that belongs to the Scrophulariaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that bear large, fuzzy, gray-green leaves, which are typical of the genus Verbascum. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer, adding a striking visual element to gardens. It is native to regions of Europe and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Verbascum × longeracemosum thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy choice for various garden environments.
