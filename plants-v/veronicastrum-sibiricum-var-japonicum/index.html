Veronicastrum Sibiricum Var. Japonicum

Veronicastrum sibiricum var. japonicum is a striking perennial plant native to eastern Asia known for its tall, spires of fragrant, blue-violet flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks. This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. Its foliage is lance-shaped and provides a nice contrast to its vibrant blooms. It is a versatile plant that can be used in gardens or naturalized areas with proper care.
