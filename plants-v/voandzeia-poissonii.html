Voandzeia poissonii

Voandzeia poissonii, commonly known as the African bean or wild cowpea, is a climbing legume native to Africa.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets and produces small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant bears pods that contain several elliptical seeds, which are used as a food source. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in disturbed habitats.

Voandzeia poissonii is valued for its nutritional content and has been used in traditional agriculture and medicine.
