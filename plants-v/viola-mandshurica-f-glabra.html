Viola mandshurica f. glabra

Viola mandshurica f. glabra, commonly known as the smooth violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It features heart-shaped, hairy leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are usually violet or purple in hue, with five petals. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

The smooth violet is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
