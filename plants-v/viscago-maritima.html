Viscago maritima

Viscago maritima, commonly known as sea buckthorn, is a shrub native to coastal regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its yellow or orange berries, which are rich in vitamins and antioxidants. The plant has small, dark green leaves and produces yellow flowers in the spring. It thrives in sandy or salty soils and is often found along shorelines.

Viscago maritima is also valued for its ecological benefits, providing food for birds and supporting coastal dune stabilization.
