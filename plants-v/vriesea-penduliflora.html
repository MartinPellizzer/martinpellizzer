Vriesea penduliflora

Vriesea penduliflora, commonly known as the pendant vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, arching inflorescence that bears numerous pendulous, showy orange flowers. The plant features a rosette of broad, dark green leaves that are often adorned with white or silver markings. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity to maintain its vibrant appearance.

Vriesea penduliflora is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.
