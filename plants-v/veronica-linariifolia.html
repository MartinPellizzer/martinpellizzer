Veronica linariifolia

Veronica linariifolia, commonly known as the slender speedwell, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a perennial herb with upright, branched stems that bear small, blue to purple flowers. The leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving the plant a soft, textured appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
