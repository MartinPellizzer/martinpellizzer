Vriesea takahashiana

Vriesea takahashiana, commonly known as the Takahashi's Vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, spathaceous inflorescence that displays a mix of red, orange, and yellow hues. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that form a rosette, creating a central cup to collect rainwater. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Vriesea takahashiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
