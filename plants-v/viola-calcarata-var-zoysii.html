Viola calcarata var. zoysii

Viola calcarata var. zoysii, commonly known as the Zoysii viola, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, violet-blue flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or woodland settings.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms.
