Vellozia pleurocarpa

Vellozia pleurocarpa, commonly known as the pleurocarp vellozia, is a unique plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Velloziaceae and is characterized by its rosette-forming structure with thick, fleshy leaves. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its succulent leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically greenish-yellow in color.

Vellozia pleurocarpa is notable for its slow growth and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
