Valerianoides jamaicensis f. strigosa

Valerianoides jamaicensis f. strigosa, commonly known as Jamaican valerian, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the family Valerianaceae and is characterized by its aromatic, often fragrant leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its foliage is usually covered in fine, hairy structures, giving it a fuzzy appearance.

It is valued for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
