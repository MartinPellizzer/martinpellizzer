Vangueria armata

Vangueria armata, commonly known as the African star-of-Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Vangueria armata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
