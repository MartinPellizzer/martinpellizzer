Verbesina villaregalis

Verbesina villaregalis, commonly known as the Mexican beggarticks, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom from summer to fall. Its leaves are alternate, heart-shaped, and have a rough texture.

It is often found in disturbed soils and is sometimes considered an invasive species in certain regions.
