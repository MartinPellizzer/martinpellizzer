Vitex dalrympleana

Vitex dalrympleana, commonly known as the Dalrymple's chaste tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to purple, typically blooming in the dry season. Its fruit is a small, woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Vitex dalrympleana is often found in moist, well-drained soils in forest understories or along stream banks.
