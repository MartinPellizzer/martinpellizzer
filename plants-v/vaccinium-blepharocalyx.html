Vaccinium blepharocalyx

Vaccinium blepharocalyx, commonly known as the narrow-leaved huckleberry, is a species of flowering plant in the Ericaceae family.

It is native to the western United States and parts of Canada, typically found in moist, acidic soils of forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are narrow and leathery, adapted to withstand cold climates and acidic conditions.
