Viola virescens

Viola virescens, commonly known as the green violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, violet-colored flowers with five petals, often with a distinctive yellow center. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Viola virescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
