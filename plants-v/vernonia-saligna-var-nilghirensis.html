Vernonia saligna var. nilghirensis

Vernonia saligna var. nilghirensis, commonly known as the Nilgiri Vernonia, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom during the monsoon season. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often found in botanical gardens and conservation areas.
