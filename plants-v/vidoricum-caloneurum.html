Vidoricum caloneurum

Vidoricum caloneurum, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage and delicate, fragrant blossoms that bloom in the spring. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and is often found in cloud forests. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the rich, organic soil of its habitat.

Vidoricum caloneurum is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
