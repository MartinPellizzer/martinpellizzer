Vigna mungo var. sublobata

Vigna mungo var. sublobata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf mung bean, is a legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, trifoliate leaves and small, yellow flowers. The plant produces cylindrical pods containing small, round seeds that are used in various cuisines. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a warm climate for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its nutritional content and adaptability to different agricultural conditions.
