Verbena bonariensis f. robustior

Verbena bonariensis f. robustior, commonly known as the robust form of common verbena, is a perennial flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of vibrant blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.

Its compact size and extended blooming period make it a valuable addition to ornamental gardens.
