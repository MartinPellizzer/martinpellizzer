Vitaliana primuliflora subsp. canescens

Vitaliana primuliflora subsp.

canescens, commonly known as the silverleaf vitaliana, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, dense hairs.

The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ornamental plant in native gardens.
