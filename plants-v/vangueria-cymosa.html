Vangueria cymosa

Vangueria cymosa, commonly known as the common vangueria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in summer, which are borne on short, lateral branches. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Vangueria cymosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
