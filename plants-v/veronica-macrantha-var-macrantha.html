Veronica macrantha var. macrantha

Veronica macrantha var. macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered speedwell, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features upright, branching stems covered in fine, hairy leaves that are typically lance-shaped and gray-green in color. The plant produces clusters of showy, blue to violet flowers with five petals, which bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and ability to attract pollinators.
