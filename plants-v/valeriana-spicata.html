Valeriana spicata

Valeriana spicata, commonly known as spike valerian, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its upright, branched stems and small, fragrant flowers. The plant produces tiny, clustered flowers that are typically white or pink in color. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly rough texture.
