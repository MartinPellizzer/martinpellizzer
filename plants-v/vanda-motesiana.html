Vanda motesiana

Vanda motesiana, commonly known as the Motesian orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on the surface of trees or other structures without deriving nutrients from them. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with a distinctive lip petal. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Vanda motesiana is popular among orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
