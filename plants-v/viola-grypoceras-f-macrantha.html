Viola grypoceras f. macrantha

Viola grypoceras f. macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered violet, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and produces showy, violet-blue flowers with a distinctive yellow throat. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens or woodland settings. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, adding vibrant color to the landscape.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its beauty and hardiness.
