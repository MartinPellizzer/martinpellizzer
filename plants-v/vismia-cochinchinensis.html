Vismia cochinchinensis

Vismia cochinchinensis, commonly known as the Vietnamese evergreen or white-flowered vismia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and lance-shaped leaves that are often glossy and dark green. Its flowers are small, white, and clustered in panicles, typically blooming in the summer months.

The fruit is a small, three-lobed capsule that splits open to release the seeds.
