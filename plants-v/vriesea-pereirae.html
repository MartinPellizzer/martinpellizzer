Vriesea pereirae

Vriesea pereirae, commonly known as the Emerald Beauty, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant green, sword-shaped leaves that form a rosette structure, which holds water and nutrients. The plant produces tall, branching inflorescences with colorful bracts that range from yellow to orange. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Vriesea pereirae is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
