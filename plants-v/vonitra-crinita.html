Vonitra crinita

Vonitra crinita, commonly known as the Madagascar star orchid, is a unique orchid species native to the island of Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant has a short, hairy stem and produces small, clustered leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. Vonitra crinita thrives in humid, shaded environments and is adapted to the tropical climate of its native habitat.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
