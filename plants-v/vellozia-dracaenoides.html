Vellozia dracaenoides

Vellozia dracaenoides, commonly known as the dragon's blood tree, is a unique succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette of thick, fleshy leaves that are often bluish-green in color. The plant produces striking red flowers that bloom in spring, adding a vibrant touch to its appearance. Its leaves are adapted to store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments.

Vellozia dracaenoides is also notable for its ability to form a caudex, which helps it survive periods of drought.
