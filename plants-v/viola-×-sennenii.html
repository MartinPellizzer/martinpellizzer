Viola × sennenii

Viola × sennenii, commonly known as the Japanese violet, is a hybrid species of the violet genus Viola.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, velvety leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from purple to white. Native to Japan, this plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.

The species is known for its adaptability and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
