Vicia koeieana

Vicia koeieana, commonly known as the Koeiean vetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa, where it thrives in grasslands and rocky areas. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pale purple, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets, and the plant has a trailing growth habit.

Vicia koeieana is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.
