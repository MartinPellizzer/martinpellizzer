Vickifunkia

Vickifunkia, commonly known as the moonflower vine, is a tropical climbing plant characterized by its large, fragrant white flowers that open at night.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is native to Central and South America. The plant features heart-shaped, velvety leaves and produces edible berries that turn from green to red as they mature.

Vickifunkia thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
