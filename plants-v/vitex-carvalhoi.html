Vitex carvalhoi

Vitex carvalhoi, commonly known as the Cape York vitex, is a flowering plant native to northern Australia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually violet or white in color. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a rough texture.

Vitex carvalhoi is often used in traditional Aboriginal medicine for its potential healing properties.
