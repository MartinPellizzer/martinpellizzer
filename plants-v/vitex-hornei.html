Vitex hornei

Vitex hornei, commonly known as the African blueberry or horne's vitex, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, often branched stems and opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to purple, followed by small, round, blue-black berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savannas and woodland areas.

Vitex hornei is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local medicine.
