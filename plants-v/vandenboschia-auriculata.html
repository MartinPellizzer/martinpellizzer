Vandenboschia auriculata

Vandenboschia auriculata, commonly known as the auriculate vandenboschia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, cylindrical rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a powdery coating. The plant produces striking, tall inflorescences with yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves.

Vandenboschia auriculata is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its ornamental appearance and low maintenance requirements.
