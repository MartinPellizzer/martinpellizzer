Viola kiangsiensis

Viola kiangsiensis, commonly known as the Kiangsi violet, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as forest floors or stream banks. Its flowers bloom in early spring, adding a splash of color to its natural habitat.

Viola kiangsiensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
