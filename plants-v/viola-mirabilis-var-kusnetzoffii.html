Viola mirabilis var. kusnetzoffii

Viola mirabilis var. kusnetzoffii, commonly known as the Kusnetzoff's Violet, is a species of flowering plant in the Violaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly Kazakhstan and the Tian Shan. The plant features heart-shaped, velvety leaves and produces striking violet to purple flowers with a distinctive yellow throat. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
