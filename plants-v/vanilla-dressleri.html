Vanilla dressleri

Vanilla dressleri, commonly known as the giant vanilla orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves and can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces fragrant, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by long, curved pods containing the aromatic vanilla beans. It thrives in humid, warm environments with high light exposure.

Due to its size and ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
