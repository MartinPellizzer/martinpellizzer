Vouapa limbata

Vouapa limbata, commonly known as the Madagascar star orchid, is a striking tropical orchid native to the island of Madagascar.

It is characterized by its vibrant, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep purple. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments within the rainforests of Madagascar. Its leaves are broad and glossy, often with a waxy coating to retain moisture.

Vouapa limbata is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
