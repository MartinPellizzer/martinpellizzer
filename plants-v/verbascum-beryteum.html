Verbascum beryteum

Verbascum beryteum, commonly known as the Beirut mullein, is a flowering plant native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Scrophulariaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, fuzzy leaves. The plant produces showy yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.
