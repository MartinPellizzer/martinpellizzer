Viola aetolica

Viola aetolica, commonly known as the Aeolian violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that range in color from purple to white, typically blooming in spring. Viola aetolica is often found in rocky soils and open woodlands, thriving in well-drained environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species within the Violaceae family.
