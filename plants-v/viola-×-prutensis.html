Viola × prutensis

Viola × prutensis, commonly known as the Prut Valley violet, is a hybrid species native to the Prut River valley in Romania.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 15–30 cm. The plant features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its flowers are violet-blue, with five petals and a distinctive yellow beard at the base of the petals.

Viola × prutensis thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas and along stream banks.
