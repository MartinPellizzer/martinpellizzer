Viola chingiana

Viola chingiana, commonly known as the Chinese violet, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves and produces delicate, five-petaled flowers in shades of purple and white. The plant typically grows to a height of 10-20 cm and thrives in cool, moist environments. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.

The species is also valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
