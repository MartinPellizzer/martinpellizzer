Viola blanda var. amoena

Viola blanda var. amoena, commonly known as the common violet, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and small, delicate flowers that range in color from purple to white. The plant typically grows to a height of about 10-15 cm and thrives in moist, well-drained soil. It blooms from spring to early summer and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

The species is also noted for its ability to self-seed and spread readily in suitable conditions.
