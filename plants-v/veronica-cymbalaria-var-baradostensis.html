Veronica cymbalaria var. baradostensis

Veronica cymbalaria var. baradostensis, commonly known as the Baradost speedwell, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant produces small, blue to violet flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.
