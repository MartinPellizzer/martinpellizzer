Veratrum shanense

Veratrum shanense, commonly known as the Shan lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Melanthiaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, showy white or pale blue flowers. The plant produces a cluster of small, green berries that contain the toxic seeds. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its toxicity, it should be handled with care.
