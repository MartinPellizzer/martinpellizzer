Verbesina warei

Verbesina warei, commonly known as the yellow ironweed, is a perennial herb native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has rough, hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are clustered in panicles and are attractive to pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Verbesina warei is often found in moist, open habitats like wetlands and along stream banks.
