Verbascum ceratophyllum

Verbascum ceratophyllum, commonly known as the horned mullein, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its large, fuzzy, gray-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces tall, sparsely branched stems that bear clusters of yellow flowers. It thrives in dry, sunny environments and is often found in rocky soils.

Verbascum ceratophyllum is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally for its soothing effects.
