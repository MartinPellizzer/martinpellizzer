Viola × kalksburgensis

Viola × kalksburgensis, commonly known as the Kalksburg violet, is a hybrid species within the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, dark green leaves and showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in early spring. Native to Central Europe, this plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade. It is often found in woodland areas and along stream banks.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
