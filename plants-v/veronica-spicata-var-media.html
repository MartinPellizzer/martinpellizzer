Veronica spicata var. media

Veronica spicata var. media, commonly known as the upright speedwell, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features upright, compact growth with spikes of blue or violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically evergreen in mild climates. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its compact size and attractive flowers make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking low-maintenance, ornamental plants.
