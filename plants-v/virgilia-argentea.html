Virgilia argentea

Virgilia argentea, commonly known as silver virgilia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in coastal and mountainous regions, where it adapts well to arid conditions.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
