Vauanthes depressa

Vauanthes depressa, commonly known as the low vauanthes, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale yellow in color. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Vauanthes depressa is valued in horticulture for its delicate appearance and ability to thrive in woodland gardens.
