Vigna wilmsii

Vigna wilmsii, commonly known as the yellow winged bean, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant produces elongated, cylindrical pods containing small, edible seeds that are rich in protein and nutrients. Vigna wilmsii is often cultivated for both its seeds and foliage, which are used in various cuisines.

It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a warm, humid climate for optimal growth.
