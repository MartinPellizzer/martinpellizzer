Viola mandshurica var. media

Viola mandshurica var. media, commonly known as the Siberian pansy, is a perennial flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces showy violet or blue flowers with a distinctive yellow eye, blooming in early spring. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas or along streambanks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and shady borders.
