Veronica kuznezowii

Veronica kuznezowii, commonly known as Kuznetsov's speedwell, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a perennial herb with upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of blue or violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long blooming period.
