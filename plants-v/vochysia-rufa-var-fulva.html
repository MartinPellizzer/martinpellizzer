Vochysia rufa var. fulva

Vochysia rufa var. fulva, commonly known as the red-leaf vochysia, is a tropical tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red foliage, which becomes more pronounced during the dry season. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by woody pods containing seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a landscape plant.
