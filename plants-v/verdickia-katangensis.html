Verdickia katangensis

Verdickia katangensis, commonly known as the Katanga velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to Central Africa.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are velvety to the touch. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an important species in the study of African flora.
