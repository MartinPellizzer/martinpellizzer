Viola × eichenfeldii

Viola × eichenfeldii, commonly known as the Dutch viola, is a hybrid species within the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, often bi-colored flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows to a height of 10–20 centimeters, with a compact and bushy habit. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens.

The species is valued for its hardiness and ability to naturalize in suitable climates.
