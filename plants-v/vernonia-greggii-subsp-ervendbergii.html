Vernonia greggii subsp. ervendbergii

Vernonia greggii subsp.

ervendbergii, commonly known as the Gregg's ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, blue to purple composite flowers.

The plant has narrow, gray-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. Vernonia greggii subsp.

ervendbergii is often found in rocky slopes and open woodlands.
