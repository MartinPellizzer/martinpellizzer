Vittaria nymanii

Vittaria nymanii, commonly known as the tropical fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Vittariaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are typically light green and possess a soft, velvety texture. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its spores are produced in clusters on the undersides of its fronds, and it reproduces through spore dispersal rather than seeds.

Vittaria nymanii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
