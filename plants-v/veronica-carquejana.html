Veronica carquejana

Veronica carquejana, commonly known as the Carquejana speedwell, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant produces small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid environments.
