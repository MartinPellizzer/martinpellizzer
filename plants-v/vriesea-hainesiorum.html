Vriesea hainesiorum

Vriesea hainesiorum, commonly known as the Haines' Vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, spathiform bracts that display a range of colors, often in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features a rosette of grass-like leaves that collect water, forming a central pool that supports aquatic life. It produces inflorescences that can reach up to 1 meter in height, with showy flowers in shades of yellow and red.

Vriesea hainesiorum thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
