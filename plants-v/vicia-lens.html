Vicia lens

Vicia lens, commonly known as the horse gram or cluster bean, is a leguminous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its erect growth habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that give way to pods containing small, kidney-shaped seeds. Vicia lens is valued for its nutritional content, particularly its high protein and fiber levels.

It is also used in traditional medicine and as a forage crop for livestock.
