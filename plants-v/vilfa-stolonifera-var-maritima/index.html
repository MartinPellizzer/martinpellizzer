Vilfa Stolonifera Var. Maritima

Sea oats, scientifically known as Vilfa stolonifera var. maritima, are a hardy coastal plant that thrives in sandy environments. These grasses are commonly found along shorelines and dunes, playing a vital role in stabilizing sand dunes and preventing erosion. Their long, slender blades are adapted to withstand salt spray and strong winds. The plant's ability to grow in harsh conditions makes it an essential part of coastal ecosystems. Sea oats also provide habitat and food for various wildlife species.
