Vanilla moonii

Vanilla moonii, commonly known as the moonflower vanilla, is a climbing vine native to Mexico.

It produces fragrant, white flowers that open at night, hence its common name. The plant is notable for its unique scent, which is similar to vanilla but more delicate. Its flowers are typically small and tubular, with five petals that curl inward.

Vanilla moonii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in some traditional herbal remedies.
