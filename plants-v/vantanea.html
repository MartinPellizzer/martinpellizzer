Vantanea

Vantanea, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, velvety hairs. The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Vantanea is native to regions in Africa, Asia, and the Americas, and it thrives in warm, humid climates.

It is often found in disturbed soils and can become invasive in certain areas.
