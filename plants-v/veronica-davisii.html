Veronica davisii

Veronica davisii, commonly known as the Davis's speedwell, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its delicate blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are clustered in whorls along the stems.
