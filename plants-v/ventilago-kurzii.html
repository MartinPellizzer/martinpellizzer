Ventilago kurzii

Ventilago kurzii, commonly known as the African snowball, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring dense, rounded clusters of small white flowers. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with ovate to lanceolate leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal areas.

The flowers bloom in winter, adding a striking visual contrast to the landscape.
