Vitex sarawakana

Vitex sarawakana, commonly known as the Sarawak vitex, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height, characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and showy, clustered flowers. The flowers are typically violet or purple in color and bloom during the rainy season. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.

It is often found in secondary forests and along riverbanks, contributing to the biodiversity of its habitat.
