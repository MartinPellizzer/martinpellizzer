Vandellia perennans

Vandellia perennans, commonly known as the silverleaf vandellia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southern regions of South America.

It is characterized by its succulent, silver-green foliage that reflects sunlight, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
