Vandellia Cyrtotricha

Vandellia cyrtotricha is a rare and unique plant native to the coastal regions of southern South America. It is known for its striking, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and purple. This plant thrives in sandy, coastal soils and is often found in areas with strong sea breezes. Due to its limited habitat and specific environmental needs, it is considered endangered. Its beauty and ecological significance make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
