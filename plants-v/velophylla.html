Velophylla

Velophylla, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a unique species native to arid regions.

It features waxy, silver-tinted leaves that help reduce water loss in hot, dry environments. The plant has a compact growth habit, often forming dense clusters to protect itself from harsh conditions. Its flowers are small and pale, typically appearing in clusters during the spring.

Velophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in low-moisture landscapes.
