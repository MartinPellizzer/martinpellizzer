Verbascum celsiae

Verbascum celsiae, commonly known as the Celsian mullein, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae and is characterized by its tall, hairy stems and large, fuzzy leaves. The plant produces showy yellow or orange flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or alpine habitats.
