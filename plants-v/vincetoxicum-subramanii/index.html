Vincetoxicum Subramanii

Vincetoxicum subramanii is a rare and striking flowering plant native to the tropical regions of India. It is known for its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of orange and yellow. This species belongs to the Apocynaceae family, which includes many toxic plants. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Due to habitat loss, it faces increasing threats to its survival in the wild.
