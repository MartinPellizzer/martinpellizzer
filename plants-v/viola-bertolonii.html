Viola bertolonii

Viola bertolonii, commonly known as the Bertoloni violet, is a perennial flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of violet or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Viola bertolonii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
