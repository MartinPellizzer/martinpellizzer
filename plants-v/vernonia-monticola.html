Vernonia monticola

Vernonia monticola, commonly known as the mountain Vernonia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Its yellowish flowers bloom in summer, and the plant is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and forest edges.

Vernonia monticola is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
