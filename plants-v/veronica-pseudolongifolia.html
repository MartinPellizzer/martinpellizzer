Veronica pseudolongifolia

It is native to Europe and parts of western Asia, and it thrives in moist, temperate environments. The plant features slender, upright stems that bear opposite pairs of linear, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, blue or violet, and are clustered in dense, spike-like inflorescences.

Veronica pseudolongifolia is often found in meadows, stream banks, and woodland edges, where it contributes to the biodiversity of its habitat.
