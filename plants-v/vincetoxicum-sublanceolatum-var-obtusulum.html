Vincetoxicum sublanceolatum var. obtusulum

Vincetoxicum sublanceolatum var. obtusulum, commonly known as the blunt-leaf false bindweed, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern North America.

It features heart-shaped to ovate leaves with a slightly waxy texture and a dark green color. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in late summer. Its stems are slender and often trailing or ascending, with a tendency to root at the nodes.

This species is often found in moist, disturbed soils along stream banks and in woodland edges.
