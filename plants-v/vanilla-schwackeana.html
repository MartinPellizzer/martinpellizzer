Vanilla schwackeana

Vanilla schwackeana, commonly known as the Mexican vanilla orchid, is a tropical climbing orchid native to Central America.

It produces fragrant, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters along its vine-like stems. The plant is known for its distinctive vanilla-scented fragrance, which is released when the flowers are in bloom. It thrives in warm, humid climates with bright, indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value and unique aroma, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
