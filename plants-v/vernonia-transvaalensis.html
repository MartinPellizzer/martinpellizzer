Vernonia transvaalensis

Vernonia transvaalensis, commonly known as the Transvaal ironweed, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and gray-green foliage. The plant produces small, tubular yellow flowers that are clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Vernonia transvaalensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
