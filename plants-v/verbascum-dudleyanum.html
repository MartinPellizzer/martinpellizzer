Verbascum dudleyanum

Verbascum dudleyanum, commonly known as Dudley's mullein, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, hairy stems and large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant belongs to the Scrophulariaceae family and is known for its medicinal properties. Its leaves are thick and woolly, often used in traditional herbal remedies.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to gardens.
