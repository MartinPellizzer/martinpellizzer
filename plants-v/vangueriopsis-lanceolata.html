Vangueriopsis lanceolata

Vangueriopsis lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceleaf vangueriopsis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, wooded areas and thrives in partial to full shade. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often glossy and have a distinct veining pattern. Its flowers are small, clustered in panicles, and range in color from white to pale pink.

Vangueriopsis lanceolata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native plant gardens.
