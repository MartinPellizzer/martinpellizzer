Vitex duboisii

Vitex duboisii, commonly known as Dubois's vitex, is a shrub native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the verbena family (Verbenaceae) and is characterized by its aromatic, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to purple, typically blooming in the dry season. Its fruit is a dry capsule containing several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Vitex duboisii is often found in dry forests and woodlands, adapted to the island's unique ecological conditions.
