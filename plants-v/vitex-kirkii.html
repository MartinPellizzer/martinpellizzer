Vitex kirkii

Vitex kirkii, commonly known as African bushbean or Kikuyu bean, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to purple, followed by elongated, bean-like fruits. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a rough texture.

Vitex kirkii is adapted to dry and semi-arid environments, making it resilient to drought conditions.
