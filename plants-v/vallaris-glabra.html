Vallaris glabra

Vallaris glabra, commonly known as the slender vallaris, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Leguminosae family, also known as the legume or bean family. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, trifoliate leaves. It produces small, yellowish-white flowers that are clustered in racemes.

Vallaris glabra is often found in disturbed habitats and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
