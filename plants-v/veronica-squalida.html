Veronica squalida

Veronica squalida, commonly known as the squalid speedwell, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its small, blue or purple flowers that bloom in clusters. Veronica squalida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
