Vahlia oldenlandioides

Vahlia oldenlandioides, commonly known as the African snowball, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a velvety texture.

The plant is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
