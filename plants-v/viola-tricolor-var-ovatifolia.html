Viola tricolor var. ovatifolia

Viola tricolor var. ovatifolia, commonly known as the heartleaf violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features trifoliate leaves with heart-shaped lobes, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, violet or white flowers with yellow markings. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in woodlands or shaded gardens.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
