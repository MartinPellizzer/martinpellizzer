Vareca lanceolata

Vareca lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceleaf vareca, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves, which are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Vareca lanceolata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
