Viola bilczensis

Viola bilczensis, commonly known as the Polish violet, is a species of flowering plant in the genus Viola.

It is native to Central and Eastern Europe, particularly found in Poland and surrounding regions. The plant produces small, heart-shaped leaves and violet-colored flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in woodland and shaded areas, preferring moist, well-drained soil.

Viola bilczensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
