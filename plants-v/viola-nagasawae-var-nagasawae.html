Viola nagasawae var. nagasawae

Viola nagasawae var. nagasawae, commonly known as the Nagasawa violet, is a species native to Japan.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant belonging to the Violaceae family. The plant features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a velvety texture. Its flowers are typically purple or violet, with five petals and a distinctive yellow throat.

It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forested areas.
