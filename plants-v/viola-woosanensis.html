Viola woosanensis

Viola woosanensis, commonly known as the Woosan violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Korea.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and delicate, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its flowers bloom in early spring, adding a splash of color to the landscape.

Viola woosanensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
