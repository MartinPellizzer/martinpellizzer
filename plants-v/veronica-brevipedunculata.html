Veronica brevipedunculata

Veronica brevipedunculata, commonly known as the short-stemmed speedwell, is a perennial flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, blue to violet flowers. Veronica brevipedunculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
