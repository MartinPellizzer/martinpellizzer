Viola formosana var. stenopetala

Viola formosana var. stenopetala, commonly known as the narrow-petaled Chinese violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface and produces violet-blue flowers with five petals. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its flowers bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to woodland gardens.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its beauty and fragrance.
