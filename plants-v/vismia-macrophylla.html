Vismia macrophylla

Vismia macrophylla, commonly known as the giant vismia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, elliptical leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are clustered in panicle inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release numerous seeds.

Vismia macrophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm, humid climates.
