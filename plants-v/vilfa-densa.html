Vilfa densa

Vilfa densa, commonly known as the dense vilfa, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Vilfa densa thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive appearance and resilience in shaded garden environments.
