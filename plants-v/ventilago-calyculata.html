Ventilago calyculata

Ventilago calyculata, commonly known as the silver dollar tree, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth, waxy texture. Its flowers are small, white, and clustered in inflorescences, typically blooming in summer.

Ventilago calyculata is adapted to well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant in suitable climates.
