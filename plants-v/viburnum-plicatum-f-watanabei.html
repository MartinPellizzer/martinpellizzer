Viburnum plicatum f. watanabei

Viburnum plicatum f. watanabei, commonly known as the Japanese snowbell, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features striking, cascading clusters of white, bell-shaped flowers that appear in early spring. The foliage is dark green with a silvery underside, adding visual interest throughout the growing season. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for small gardens or containers.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade to full sun.
