Viola minor f. ciliata

Viola minor f. ciliata, commonly known as the velvetleaf viola, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with finely toothed margins, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, violet-blue flowers with yellow markings, typically blooming in early spring. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
