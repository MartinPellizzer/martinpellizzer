Verbascum nigrum f. leucerion

Verbascum nigrum f. leucerion, commonly known as the white mullein.

This plant is a variant of the common mullein, characterized by its pale white to pale yellow flowers. It is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia. The leaves are large, velvety, and gray-green, while the stems are hairy and upright.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.
