Vriesea koideae

Vriesea koideae, commonly known as the silver queen bromeliad, is a striking plant native to the rainforests of Brazil.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its tall, narrow leaves that form a rosette shape. The plant produces striking inflorescences with vibrant orange flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity to flourish.

Vriesea koideae is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
