Veronica austriaca subsp. tenuissima

Veronica austriaca subsp.

tenuissima, commonly known as slender speedwell, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe. It features small, blue to violet flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to August.

The plant has thin, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
