Viola arborescens f. willkommii

Viola arborescens f. willkommii, commonly known as the Willkomm's tree violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe.

It is characterized by its upright, shrub-like growth habit, with stems that can reach up to 1 meter in height. The plant produces showy, violet-blue flowers with yellow markings, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in a fine, velvety texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

This cultivar is valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in well-drained soils under full sun to partial shade.
