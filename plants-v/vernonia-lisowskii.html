Vernonia lisowskii

Vernonia lisowskii, commonly known as the Cape vernonia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Vernonia lisowskii is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in coastal conditions.
