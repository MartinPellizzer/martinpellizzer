Vernonia sciaphila

Vernonia sciaphila, commonly known as the shade vernonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and aromatic foliage. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a velvety texture, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Vernonia sciaphila is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some indigenous cultures.
