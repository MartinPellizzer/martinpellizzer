Vernonia adenocephala

Vernonia adenocephala, commonly known as the woolly ironweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to gardens.

Vernonia adenocephala is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
