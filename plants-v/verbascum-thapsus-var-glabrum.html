Verbascum thapsus var. glabrum

Verbascum thapsus var. glabrum, commonly known as the smooth mullein, is a variant of the common mullein plant.

It is a biennial herbaceous plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa. The plant features large, velvety gray-green leaves that are typically smooth and lack the fuzzy texture found in other varieties. It produces showy yellow flowers in the summer, which are clustered in a cylindrical spike.

This variant is often found in dry, disturbed soils and is known for its medicinal and ornamental uses.
