Veratrum album subsp. misae

Veratrum album subsp.

misae, commonly known as the Japanese white hellebore, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan. It belongs to the family Liliaceae and is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, lance-shaped leaves.
