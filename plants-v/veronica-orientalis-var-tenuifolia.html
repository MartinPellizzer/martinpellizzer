Veronica orientalis var. tenuifolia

Veronica orientalis var. tenuifolia, commonly known as slender leaf speedwell, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, branching stems covered in small, opposite leaves that are narrow and often slightly hairy. The plant produces clusters of sky-blue to purple flowers with five petals, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value comes from its delicate foliage and prolonged flowering period.
