Virecta multiflora

Virecta multiflora, commonly known as the multibloom vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its vigorous climbing habit and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Virecta multiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant in greenhouses.
