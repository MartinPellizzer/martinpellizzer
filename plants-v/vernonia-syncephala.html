Vernonia syncephala

Vernonia syncephala, commonly known as the golden crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and striking yellow flower heads that resemble a crown. The plant produces small, tubular flowers arranged in dense, spherical clusters at the top of the stems. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and ecologically valuable in its native habitat.
