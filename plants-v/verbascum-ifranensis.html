Verbascum ifranensis

Verbascum ifranensis, commonly known as the Moroccan mullein, is a flowering plant native to North Africa.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae and is characterized by its tall, hairy stems and large, showy yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to arid environments, featuring thick leaves that reduce water loss. It produces capsule fruits containing numerous small seeds.
