Vaccinium wrightii var. formosanum

Vaccinium wrightii var. formosanum, commonly known as the Formosan huckleberry, is a low-growing shrub native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes many acid-loving plants. The plant produces small, edible berries that are typically red or black in color. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in forest understories.

Its leaves are oval-shaped with a leathery texture and dark green color.
