Vepris unifoliolata

Vepris unifoliolata, commonly known as the single leaf flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, ovate leaves that are usually entire and glossy in appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, arranged in panicles. Its fruits are woody pods containing several seeds, and the plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Vepris unifoliolata is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
