Vicia Sativa F. Leucosperma

Vicia sativa f. leucosperma is a rare and elegant variant of the common vetch known for its striking white flowers. This plant belongs to the legume family and is native to parts of Europe and Asia. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a hardy addition to gardens. Its unique appearance makes it a favorite among botanists and garden enthusiasts alike. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively uncommon in cultivation.
