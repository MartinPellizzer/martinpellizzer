Vismia latisepala

Vismia latisepala, commonly known as the broad-sepaled evergreen, is a tropical tree native to regions of Southeast Asia and northern Australia.

It is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and lance-shaped leaves with prominent veins. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by clustered, fleshy berries.
