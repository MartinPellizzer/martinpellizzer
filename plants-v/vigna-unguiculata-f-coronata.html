Vigna unguiculata f. coronata

Vigna unguiculata f. coronata, commonly known as the crown bean, is a cultivated variant of the cowpea species.

It is characterized by its distinctive crown-like arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant is an annual legume native to tropical and subtropical regions. It produces small, pod-like fruits that contain edible seeds used in various cuisines.

This variety is valued for its adaptability to poor soils and its ability to fix nitrogen, enhancing soil fertility.
