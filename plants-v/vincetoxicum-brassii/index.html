Vincetoxicum Brassii

Vincetoxicum brassii is a striking perennial plant known for its vibrant, bi-color flowers. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is native to parts of Europe and Asia. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and clusters of showy blooms that range from purple to pink. It is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal. Despite its beauty, it can be invasive in certain regions if not managed properly.
