Vanda paniculata

Vanda paniculata, commonly known as the giant orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, leathery leaves and showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in humid, tropical environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth. Vanda paniculata is known for its long-lasting blooms, which can last several weeks.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
