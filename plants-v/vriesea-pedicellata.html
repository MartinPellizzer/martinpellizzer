Vriesea pedicellata

Vriesea pedicellata, commonly known as the "Cape Lily," is a striking bromeliad native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a central cup, which collects water and nutrients. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Vriesea pedicellata is popular as a decorative plant due to its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
