Vochysia megalophylla

Vochysia megalophylla, commonly known as the giant-leaf vochysia, is a tropical tree native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers arranged in clusters, typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local cultures.
