Verbesina buphthalmoides

Verbesina buphthalmoides, commonly known as the large-eyed sunflower, is a herbaceous annual plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the genus Verbesina in the family Asteraceae, characterized by its showy, yellow-orange composite flowers. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in disturbed soils, making it a common weed in agricultural fields.
