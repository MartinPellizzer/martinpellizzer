Veronica alpina var. integerrima

Veronica alpina var. integerrima, commonly known as the alpine speedwell, is a perennial herb native to mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in alpine and subalpine habitats, thriving in well-drained, rocky soils. The plant features slender stems that often lie close to the ground, forming a dense mat. Its small, blue or violet flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom in early summer.

Veronica alpina var. integerrima is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in harsh environments.
