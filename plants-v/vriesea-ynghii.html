Vriesea ynghii

Vriesea ynghii, commonly known as the Ynghii's Flame, is a striking bromeliad native to South America.

It features vibrant, sword-shaped leaves that form a central rosette, which collects rainwater to support its growth. The plant produces strikingly colorful inflorescences, often with red, orange, and yellow hues, which bloom for several months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

Vriesea ynghii is popular as a ornamental plant due to its bold appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
