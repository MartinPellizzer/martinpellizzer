Vatica griffithii

Vatica griffithii, commonly known as the Malayan ironwood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dipterocarpaceae and is characterized by its dense, heavy wood that is highly valued for its strength and durability. Vatica griffithii plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
