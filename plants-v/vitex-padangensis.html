Vitex padangensis

Vitex padangensis, commonly known as the Java vitex, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant features opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves with a rough texture. Its flowers are clustered in panicles and are typically pink or purple in color.

The fruit is a small, dry capsule that contains several seeds.
