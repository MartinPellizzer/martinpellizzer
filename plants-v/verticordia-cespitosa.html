Verticordia cespitosa

Verticordia cespitosa, commonly known as the clustered feather flower, is an Australian native plant belonging to the family Myrtaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, clustered growth habit and striking, feather-like flowers that range in color from red to orange. Verticordia cespitosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
