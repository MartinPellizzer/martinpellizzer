Vaccinium gracilipes

Vaccinium gracilipes, commonly known as the slender bilberry, is a species of dwarf shrub native to the boreal forests of North America.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like blueberries and cranberries. The plant produces small, dark blue berries that are typically edible and have a tart flavor. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded, moist environments.
