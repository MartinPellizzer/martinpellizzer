Vatica pentandra

Vatica pentandra, commonly known as the yellow meranti, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dipterocarpaceae and is characterized by its large, glossy leaves and fragrant flowers. The tree produces heavy, hard timber that is highly valued for its strength and durability. It typically grows in lowland rainforests and plays an important role in the ecosystem.
