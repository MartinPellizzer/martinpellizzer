Vernonia calvoana var. microcephala

Vernonia calvoana var. microcephala, commonly known as the small-headed ironweed, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, pale yellow to brown flower heads. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
