Vigna repens var. villosa

Vigna repens var. villosa, commonly known as hairy prostrate pigeon pea, is a creeping, herbaceous legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features trifoliate leaves with hairy surfaces and produces small, yellow flowers that develop into elongated, flat pods. The plant is characterized by its prostrate growth habit, often spreading along the ground. It thrives in warm climates and is adaptable to various soil types.

This variety is valued for its forage quality and potential use in ecological restoration projects.
