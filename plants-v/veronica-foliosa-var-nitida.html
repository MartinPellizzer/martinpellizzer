Veronica foliosa var. nitida

Veronica foliosa var. nitida, commonly known as the silver veronica, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has finely divided, silver-gray leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long flowering period.
