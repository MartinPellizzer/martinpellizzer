Vriesea dictyographa

Vriesea dictyographa, commonly known as the Spotted Vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, spiky inflorescence that displays a mix of red, orange, and yellow hues. The plant features broad, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a waxy, powdery coating. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Vriesea dictyographa is prized for its ornamental value and is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
