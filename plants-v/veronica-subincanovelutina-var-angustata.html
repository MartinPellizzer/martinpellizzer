Veronica subincanovelutina var. angustata

Veronica subincanovelutina var. angustata, commonly known as narrow-leaved speedwell, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its slender, often hairy stems and small, blue or violet flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and arranged opposite along the stem, giving it a delicate appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
