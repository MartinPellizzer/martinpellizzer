Verbena ribifolia f. alba

Verbena ribifolia f. alba, commonly known as white verbena, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a compact, herbaceous perennial that produces clusters of small, showy white flowers. The plant features slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, which extends from spring to early autumn.
