Vernonia anamallica

Vernonia anamallica, commonly known as the Malabar ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and is covered in fine, white hairs. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense heads, and are typically yellow to orange in color.

Vernonia anamallica is often found in moist, well-drained soils in forested areas and is known for its ornamental value.
