Viola × eizasieboldii

Viola × eizasieboldii, commonly known as the hybrid violet, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Violaceae family.

It is a cross between Viola tricolor and Viola × wittrockiana, resulting in a variety with vibrant, showy flowers. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves and produces clusters of five-petaled flowers in shades of purple, violet, and white. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.

Viola × eizasieboldii is relatively easy to grow and can tolerate partial shade, making it a popular choice for gardeners.
