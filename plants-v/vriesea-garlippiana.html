Vriesea garlippiana

Vriesea garlippiana, commonly known as the Garlippiana Vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, sword-shaped leaves that form a rosette, creating a central cup to collect rainwater. The plant produces tall, branching inflorescences with brightly colored, long-lasting bracts that range from red to orange. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Vriesea garlippiana is popular as a ornamental plant due to its exotic appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
