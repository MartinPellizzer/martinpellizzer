Vitex chrysomallum var. tomentella

Vitex chrysomallum var. tomentella, commonly known as the woolly chaste tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy, grayish-green leaves and clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
