Ventilago nisidai

Ventilago nisidai, commonly known as the Nisida's wood rose, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments within tropical forests. The plant produces showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a smooth texture with a slightly waxy coating.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also studied for its potential medicinal properties.
