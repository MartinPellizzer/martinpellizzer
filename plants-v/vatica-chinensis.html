Vatica chinensis

Vatica chinensis, commonly known as Chinese ironwood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, hard wood that is highly valued for its strength and durability. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are typically elliptical in shape and have a smooth, waxy surface. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by woody capsules containing several seeds.

Vatica chinensis is often found in lowland and hill forests, thriving in humid, shaded environments.
