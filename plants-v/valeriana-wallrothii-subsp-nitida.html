Valeriana wallrothii subsp. nitida

Valeriana wallrothii subsp.

nitida, commonly known as the slender valerian, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a perennial herb with upright, branched stems that are often hairy.

The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a distinct, aromatic scent.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.
