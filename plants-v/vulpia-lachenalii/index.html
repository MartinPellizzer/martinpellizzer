Vulpia Lachenalii

Vulpia lachenalii is a rare grass species native to parts of Europe and Asia. It thrives in moist, nutrient-poor soils and is often found in wet meadows and marshy areas. This plant is known for its delicate, slender leaves and small, spike-like inflorescences. Its unique adaptations allow it to survive in challenging environments. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this declining species from habitat loss.
