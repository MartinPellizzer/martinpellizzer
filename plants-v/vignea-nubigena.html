Vignea nubigena

Vignea nubigena, commonly known as the cloud vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its long, trailing stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink. Vignea nubigena thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a unique addition to botanical gardens and ecological studies.
