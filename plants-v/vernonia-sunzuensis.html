Vernonia sunzuensis

Vernonia sunzuensis, commonly known as the East African ironwood, is a shrub or small tree native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant is notable for its hard, dense wood, which is highly valued for its strength and durability. It typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to withstand arid conditions.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and traditional contexts.
