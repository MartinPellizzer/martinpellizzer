Vernonia newbouldii

Vernonia newbouldii, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is notable for its hard, heavy wood, which is highly valued for construction and tool-making. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

Vernonia newbouldii plays an important ecological role by providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
