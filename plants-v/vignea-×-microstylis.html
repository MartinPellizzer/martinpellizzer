Vignea × microstylis

Vignea × microstylis, commonly known as the small-styled vigna, is a hybrid legume species characterized by its unique botanical features.

It typically exhibits trifoliate leaves with stipules and produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions and thrives in warm, humid climates. Its pods are usually slender and contain several small, edible seeds.

Vignea × microstylis is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential as a forage crop in certain agricultural systems.
