Vincetoxicum Arnottianum

Vincetoxicum arnottianum is a striking perennial plant known for its vibrant, bi-colored flowers. It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is native to parts of Europe and Asia. The plant features slender stems and heart-shaped leaves that add to its ornamental appeal. Its unique flower structure consists of two distinct petals, one red and one yellow, creating a visually captivating display. This plant is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and ecological value.
