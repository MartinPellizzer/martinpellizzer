Velarum tzvelevii

Velarum tzvelevii, commonly known as the Tzvelev's velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in moist, tropical environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its unique foliage and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists alike.
