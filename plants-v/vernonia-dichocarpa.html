Vernonia dichocarpa

Vernonia dichocarpa, commonly known as the two-branched ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy, cone-shaped flower heads.
