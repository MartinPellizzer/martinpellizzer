Veronica precox

Veronica precox, commonly known as early speedwell, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, blue to violet flowers that bloom in early spring, often before the leaves fully emerge. Its leaves are lance-shaped and slightly hairy, with a dark green color.

Veronica precox thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.
