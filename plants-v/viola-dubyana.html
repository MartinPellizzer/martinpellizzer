Viola dubyana

Viola dubyana, commonly known as the purple viola, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe.

It features heart-shaped, hairy leaves and produces showy violet flowers with yellow markings. The plant typically grows to a height of about 15-20 cm and thrives in moist, shady environments. It is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.

Viola dubyana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
