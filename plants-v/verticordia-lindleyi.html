Verticordia lindleyi

Verticordia lindleyi, commonly known as the Kangaroo Pouch, is a striking member of the Myrtaceae family native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its unique, urn-shaped flowers that range in color from deep red to orange, often with contrasting black or purple markings. The plant typically grows in heathland and dry forest habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. Its foliage consists of narrow, leathery leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Verticordia lindleyi is a popular ornamental plant due to its vivid colors and long-lasting blooms.
