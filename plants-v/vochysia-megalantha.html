Vochysia megalantha

Vochysia megalantha, commonly known as the giant velvet tamarind, is a tropical tree native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves and showy, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The tree produces woody pods containing edible seeds, which are sometimes used in traditional medicine. Its bark and leaves contain compounds with potential pharmacological properties.

Vochysia megalantha thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.
