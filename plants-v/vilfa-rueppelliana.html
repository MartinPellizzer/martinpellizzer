Vilfa rueppelliana

Vilfa rueppelliana, commonly known as the African rue, is a shrub native to arid regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a woody stem and is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Vilfa rueppelliana is also valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in challenging climates.
