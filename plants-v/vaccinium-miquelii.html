Vaccinium miquelii

Vaccinium miquelii, commonly known as the Miquel's blueberry, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes other familiar plants like blueberries and cranberries. The plant produces small, dark blue berries that are edible and often used in local cuisines. Its leaves are evergreen, leathery, and have a distinctive elliptical shape.

Vaccinium miquelii thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.
