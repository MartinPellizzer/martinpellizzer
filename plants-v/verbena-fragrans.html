Verbena fragrans

Verbena fragrans, commonly known as sweet verbena, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its vibrant clusters of blue or purple flowers that bloom throughout the summer. The plant has slender, branching stems and small, narrow leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Verbena fragrans is also valued for its aromatic foliage and is often used in herbal remedies.
