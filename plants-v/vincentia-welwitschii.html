Vincentia welwitschii

Vincentia welwitschii, commonly known as the Welwitschia plant, is a unique succulent native to the Namib Desert in Namibia.

It is characterized by its two large, leathery leaves that can grow up to 3 meters in length. The plant has a thick, woody stem that stores water, enabling it to survive in arid conditions. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom infrequently, typically after rainfall.

Vincentia welwitschii is also known for its longevity, with some individuals living over 2,000 years.
