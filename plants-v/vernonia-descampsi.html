Vernonia descampsi

Vernonia descampsi, commonly known as the African ironweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable plant for both ecological and ornamental purposes.
