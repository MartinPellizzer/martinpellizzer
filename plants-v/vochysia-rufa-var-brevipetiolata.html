Vochysia rufa var. brevipetiolata

Vochysia rufa var. brevipetiolata, commonly known as the short-petioled red vochysia, is a tropical tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with short petioles and small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow. The plant produces woody fruits that contain several seeds, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and potential horticultural use.
