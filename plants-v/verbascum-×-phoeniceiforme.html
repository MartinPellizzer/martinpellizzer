Verbascum × phoeniceiforme

Verbascum × phoeniceiforme, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the genus Verbascum, which includes species known for their fuzzy, woolly leaves and showy yellow or orange flowers. The hybrid form exhibits traits from both parent species, often resulting in larger, more vibrant flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

This plant is also valued for its medicinal properties, historically used to treat respiratory ailments.
