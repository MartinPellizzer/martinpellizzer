Vangueria binata

Vangueria binata, commonly known as the double vangueria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features small, white or pale pink flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Vangueria binata is known for its ability to thrive in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and rocky habitats.
