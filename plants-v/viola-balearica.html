Viola balearica

Viola balearica, commonly known as the Balearic violet, is a species of flowering plant native to the Balearic Islands in the Mediterranean.

It is a herbaceous perennial with heart-shaped leaves and violet or purple flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soil. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
