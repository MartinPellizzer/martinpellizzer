Viola sylvestris var. ilerdensis

Viola sylvestris var. ilerdensis, commonly known as the Ilerdene violet, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and violet-blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and streambanks. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
