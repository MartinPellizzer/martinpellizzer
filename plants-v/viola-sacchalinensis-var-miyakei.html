Viola sacchalinensis var. miyakei

Viola sacchalinensis var. miyakei, commonly known as the Miyake viola, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its small, heart-shaped leaves and delicate purple flowers. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments, often found in shaded forest understories. Its flowers bloom in early spring, adding a subtle beauty to its natural habitat.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
