Vanieria chinensis

Vanieria chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese feather grass, is a perennial grass species native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and delicate, feathery inflorescences that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as forest floors or stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens.

Vanieria chinensis is valued for its ability to thrive in partial shade and its low maintenance requirements.
