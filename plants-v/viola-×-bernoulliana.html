Viola × bernoulliana

Viola × bernoulliana, commonly known as the hybrid violet, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Violaceae family.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Viola lutea and Viola tricolor, resulting in a plant with distinctive violet and yellow flowers. The leaves are heart-shaped and covered in a fine layer of down, giving them a soft texture. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas or gardens.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation.
