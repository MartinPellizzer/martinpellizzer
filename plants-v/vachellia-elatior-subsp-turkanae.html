Vachellia elatior subsp. turkanae

Vachellia elatior subsp.

turkanae, commonly known as Turkana acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to eastern Africa. It is characterized by its slender, often multi-stemmed form and produces yellow flowers in clusters.

The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are typically arranged in pairs. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showing adaptability to poor soils.

The wood is durable and has been traditionally used for fuel and construction.
