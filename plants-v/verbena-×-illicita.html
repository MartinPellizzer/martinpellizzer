Verbena × illicita

Verbena × illicita, commonly known as the hybrid verbena, is a flowering plant belonging to the Verbenaceae family.

It is a cross between Verbena rigida and Verbena bonariensis, resulting in a plant with unique characteristics. The species is known for its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of purple, pink, and blue. It is a hardy annual or short-lived perennial, often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for coastal and Mediterranean climates.
