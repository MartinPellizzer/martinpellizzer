Verbena Trichotoma

Verbena trichotoma is a small, flowering plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. It is known for its delicate blue or purple flowers that bloom throughout the summer months. This species thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy choice for xeriscaping. Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular ornamental plant in dryland gardens. Despite its beauty, Verbena trichotoma is often overlooked for its ecological importance in supporting native pollinators.
