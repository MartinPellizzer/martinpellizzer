Vantanea parviflora

Vantanea parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered vantanea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, delicate flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a glossy surface, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Vantanea parviflora is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and ability to thrive in humid, low-light conditions.
