Verbena hassleriana var. ovatifolia

Verbena hassleriana var. ovatifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaf verbena, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant purple or blue flowers. The leaves are ovate, which gives it its specific variety name, and they are often covered in fine hairs. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
