Verbascum × pseudoflagriforme

Verbascum × pseudoflagriforme, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a flowering plant in the Scrophulariaceae family.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Verbascum blattaria and Verbascum phlomoides. The plant is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, fuzzy, gray-green leaves. It produces showy yellow flowers in clusters at the top of the stems.

This species is often found in dry, open habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
