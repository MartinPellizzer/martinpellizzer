Verbascum amasianum

Verbascum amasianum, commonly known as the Amasian mullein, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the genus Verbascum, which includes over 200 species of flowering plants. The plant features thick, woolly leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It produces tall, yellowish-green flower clusters that bloom in summer.

Verbascum amasianum is adapted to grow in dry, rocky soils and is often found in mountainous regions.
