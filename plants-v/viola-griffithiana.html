Viola griffithiana

Viola griffithiana, commonly known as the Griffith's violet, is a species of flowering plant in the family Violaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of southern China and northern Vietnam. The plant features heart-shaped, hairy leaves and produces showy, violet-blue flowers with yellow markings. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Viola griffithiana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
