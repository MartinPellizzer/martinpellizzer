Vinca pusilla

Vinca pusilla, commonly known as small-flowered vinca, is a low-growing evergreen perennial native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It features dense clusters of small, bright blue to purple flowers that bloom throughout the summer months. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves that remain evergreen throughout the year. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact size and ornamental value make it a versatile addition to many garden settings.
