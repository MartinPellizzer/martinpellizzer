Vriesea psittacina var. jiboiensis

Vriesea psittacina var. jiboiensis, commonly known as the Jiboiensis Vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful inflorescence that can display red, orange, and yellow hues. The plant features a rosette of narrow, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
