Vanda densiflora

Vanda densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered vanda, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, leathery leaves and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant produces numerous small, white or pale yellow blossoms that bloom in summer. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Vanda densiflora is popular among orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and resilience.
