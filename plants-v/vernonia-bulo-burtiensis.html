Vernonia bulo-burtiensis

Vernonia bulo-burtiensis, commonly known as the Bulo-Burti Vernonia, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands, thriving in well-drained soils. Its flowers are usually yellow to orange in color, with prominent central disk florets.

Vernonia bulo-burtiensis is often used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
