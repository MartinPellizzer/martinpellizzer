Vernonia piloselloides var. asteroides

Vernonia piloselloides var. asteroides, commonly known as the starless ironweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and robust, often hairy stems. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are alternate, ovate to lanceolate, and often covered in fine, white hairs.

The inflorescence consists of small, yellow to orange flowers arranged in a dense, rounded cluster, giving it a distinctive appearance.
