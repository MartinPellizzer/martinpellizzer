Volutaria djiboutensis

Volutaria djiboutensis, commonly known as the Djibouti lily, is a flowering plant native to the Horn of Africa, particularly found in Djibouti.

It is a bulbous plant that produces striking, showy flowers with white petals and a yellow center. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, adapting to the harsh conditions of its native habitat. Its botanical features include a short stem and broad, fleshy leaves that help store water.

Volutaria djiboutensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens in regions with similar climates.
