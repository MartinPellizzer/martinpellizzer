Vandellia serrata

Vandellia serrata, commonly known as the serrated vandellia, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of eastern Australia.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features lance-shaped, serrated leaves that are often covered in a fine, whitish pubescence. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense inflorescences, and are typically white or pale green with a slight pinkish tinge.

Vandellia serrata is well adapted to humid, shaded environments and is often found in understorey habitats.
