Verbascum × foliosum

Verbascum × foliosum, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial plant belonging to the Scrophulariaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, fuzzy, gray-green leaves that are lanceolate in shape. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers in clusters during the summer months. It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soils.

Verbascum × foliosum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in gardens.
