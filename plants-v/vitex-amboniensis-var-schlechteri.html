Vitex amboniensis var. schlechteri

Vitex amboniensis var. schlechteri, commonly known as the Madagascar vitex, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are clustered in spikes and are usually purple or pink in color.

The fruit is a dry, two-lobed capsule that releases small, smooth seeds when mature.
