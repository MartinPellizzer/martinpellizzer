Vaccinium cryptodon

Vaccinium cryptodon, commonly known as the dwarf huckleberry, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes many acid-loving plants such as blueberries and heathers. The plant produces small, dark blue berries that are edible and often used in local cuisine. Its leaves are oval-shaped, leathery, and remain green throughout the year.
