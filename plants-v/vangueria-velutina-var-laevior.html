Vangueria velutina var. laevior

Vangueria velutina var. laevior, commonly known as the smooth vangueria, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and leaves that are typically lance-shaped. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that release seeds when mature.

This species is often found in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
