Valantia muralis

Valantia muralis, commonly known as the wall ivy, is a climbing plant belonging to the family Araliaceae.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters. The plant is native to tropical regions and thrives in humid, shaded environments. Valantia muralis is often used in horticulture for its ability to cover walls and trellises.
