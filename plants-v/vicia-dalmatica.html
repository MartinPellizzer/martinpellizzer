Vicia dalmatica

Vicia dalmatica, commonly known as the Dalmatian vetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and parts of Europe, often found in dry, rocky soils. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

Vicia dalmatica is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.
