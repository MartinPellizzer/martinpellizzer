Verbena ramulosa

Verbena ramulosa, commonly known as the slender verbena, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, often branched stems and small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny environments and is well adapted to arid conditions. Its leaves are narrow and grayish-green, often covered in fine hairs.

Verbena ramulosa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in poor soils.
