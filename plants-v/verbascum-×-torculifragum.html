Verbascum × torculifragum

Verbascum × torculifragum, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial plant belonging to the Scrophulariaceae family.

It is a hybrid between Verbascum blattaria and Verbascum toruliflorum, resulting in a plant with unique morphological traits. The plant features large, gray-green, fuzzy leaves that are characteristic of the genus Verbascum. It produces showy yellow flowers in late summer, which are clustered in a terminal spike.

This hybrid is often found in disturbed soils and thrives in full sun, making it a common sight in dry, open habitats.
