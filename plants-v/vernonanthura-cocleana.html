Vernonanthura cocleana

Vernonanthura cocleana, commonly known as the Cocle orchid, is a flowering plant native to Costa Rica.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats within cloud forests. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, while its flowers are usually white or pale pink with a yellowish lip.

Vernonanthura cocleana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
