Veronica teucrium subsp. austriaca

Veronica teucrium subsp.

austriaca, commonly known as Austrian speedwell, is a flowering plant native to Central Europe. It belongs to the family Plantaginaceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of hairy, lance-shaped leaves.
