Veronica divaricatoramosa

Veronica divaricatoramosa, commonly known as the variable speedwell, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, often sprawling stems and small, blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are opposite and linear, often with a slightly hairy texture.
