Viola odorata var. dehnhardtii

Viola odorata var. dehnhardtii, commonly known as the Dehnhardt's violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces showy flowers that range in color from purple to blue, often with a yellow or white eye. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal medicine.
