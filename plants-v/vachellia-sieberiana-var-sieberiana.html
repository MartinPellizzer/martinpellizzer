Vachellia sieberiana var. sieberiana

Vachellia sieberiana var. sieberiana, commonly known as the green thorn acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, thorny stems and bipinnate leaves that are finely divided. The plant produces yellow flowers in dense, branched inflorescences, which are followed by flat, pod-like fruits. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in savannas and rocky areas.

The species is valued for its hardiness and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
