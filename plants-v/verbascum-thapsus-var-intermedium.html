Verbascum thapsus var. intermedium

Verbascum thapsus var. intermedium, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a variant of the common mullein plant.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant features large, velvety, fuzzy leaves that are typically gray-green in color. It produces showy yellow flowers in late summer, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

This variant is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
