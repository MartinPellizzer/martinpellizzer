Viola liaosiensis

Viola liaosiensis, commonly known as the Liaoshi violet, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Viola liaosiensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and botanical research.
