Verbena Megapotamica Var. Phlogiflora

Red verbena, scientifically known as Verbena megapotamica var. phlogiflora, is a strikingly colorful plant native to South America. Its vibrant red flowers bloom in clusters, creating a visually stunning display during the warmer months. This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for Mediterranean climates. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to avoid overwatering, which can lead to root rot.
