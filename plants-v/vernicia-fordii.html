Vernicia fordii

Vernicia fordii, commonly known as the Chinese mahogany, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and simple, alternate leaves. Its ornamental value and ecological significance make it an important species in its native habitat.
