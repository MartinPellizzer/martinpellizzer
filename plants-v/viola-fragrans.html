Viola fragrans

Viola fragrans, commonly known as the sweet violet, is a flowering plant belonging to the Violaceae family.

It is native to Europe, North Africa, and parts of western Asia. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces clusters of fragrant, violet-colored flowers. Viola fragrans is known for its strong, sweet scent, which is particularly noticeable when the flowers are in bloom.

It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
