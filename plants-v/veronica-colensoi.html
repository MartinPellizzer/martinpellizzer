Veronica colensoi

Veronica colensoi, commonly known as the Colenso's speedwell, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the plantain family, Plantaginaceae, and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of small, blue or purple flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, with a slightly hairy texture. This species is often found in damp, shaded areas such as forest floors and rock crevices.

It plays an important role in the ecological systems of its native habitat.
