Valeriana kamelinii subsp. albiflora

Valeriana kamelinii subsp.

albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered valerian, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern China. It features aromatic, lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive, slightly hairy texture.

The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers during the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable species for both horticultural and ecological purposes.
