Vernonia stellaris

Vernonia stellaris, commonly known as starvernonia, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as prairies and woodlands. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a rough texture.

Vernonia stellaris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native plant gardens.
