Vriesea rafaelii

Vriesea rafaelii, commonly known as the Rafael's Vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, spathaceous inflorescence that typically displays a mix of red, orange, and yellow hues. The plant features rosette-shaped leaves that are often glossy and dark green, with a waxy texture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity to flourish.

Vriesea rafaelii is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
