Vandellia soriana

Vandellia soriana, commonly known as the Brazilian violet, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its leaves are ovate and covered in a fine, velvety texture.
