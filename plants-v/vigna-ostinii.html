Vigna ostinii

Vigna ostinii, commonly known as the yellow winged bean, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces elongated pods containing edible seeds, which are rich in protein and nutrients. Vigna ostinii thrives in well-drained soils and requires a warm climate with adequate rainfall.

It is often cultivated for both its nutritional value and ornamental appeal.
