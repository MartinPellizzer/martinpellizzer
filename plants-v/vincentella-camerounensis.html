Vincentella camerounensis

Vincentella camerounensis, commonly known as the Cameroon vine, is a climbing plant native to the rainforests of Central Africa.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its slender stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found growing along forest edges and in disturbed habitats.
