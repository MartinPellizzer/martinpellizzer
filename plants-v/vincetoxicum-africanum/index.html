Vincetoxicum Africanum

Vincetoxicum africanum is a striking plant native to southern Africa known for its vibrant, showy flowers. It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and clusters of trumpet-like blooms in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil. Despite its beauty, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
