Vitex zeylanica

Vitex zeylanica, commonly known as the Indian vitex or champa, is a flowering plant native to the Indian subcontinent.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers that are typically purple or white, arranged in clusters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly serrated edge.

Vitex zeylanica is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional medicinal uses in various cultures.
