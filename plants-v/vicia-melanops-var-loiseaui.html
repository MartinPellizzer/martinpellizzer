Vicia melanops var. loiseaui

Vicia melanops var. loiseaui, commonly known as the Loiseleur's vetch, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-bearing fruits. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or rocky habitats.
