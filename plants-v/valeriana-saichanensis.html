Valeriana saichanensis

Valeriana saichanensis, commonly known as the Saichan valerian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, herbaceous stems and opposite leaves. Its habitat is typically moist, shaded areas, making it a rare and ecologically significant species in its native range.
