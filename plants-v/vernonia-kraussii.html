Vernonia kraussii

Vernonia kraussii, commonly known as the Cape ironwood, is a shrub or small tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, pale blue to purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has rough, bark-like stems and is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. It is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Vernonia kraussii plays an important role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
