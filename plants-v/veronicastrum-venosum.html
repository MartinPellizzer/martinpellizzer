Veronicastrum venosum

Veronicastrum venosum, commonly known as blue mist spirea, is a perennial flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that bear clusters of small, blue-violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as wet meadows and stream banks. Its foliage is lance-shaped and often has a bluish-green hue.

Veronicastrum venosum is valued in gardens for its attractive flowers and ability to thrive in wet conditions.
