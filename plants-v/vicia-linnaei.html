Vicia linnaei

Vicia linnaei, commonly known as the Linnaean vetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in grasslands and open woodlands. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are usually violet or purple in color. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

Vicia linnaei is notable for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility.
