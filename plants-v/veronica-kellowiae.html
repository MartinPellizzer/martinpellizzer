Veronica kellowiae

Veronica kellowiae, commonly known as the Kellow's speedwell, is a low-growing perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features slender, upright stems covered in dense clusters of small, blue to violet flowers. The leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
