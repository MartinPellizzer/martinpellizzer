Vitex vestita f. glabrescens

Vitex vestita f. glabrescens, commonly known as the silky chaste tree, is a compact shrub native to southern Europe.

It features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in summer, followed by blue-black berries. The plant has a dense, bushy growth habit with grey-green, silky-haired leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and prolonged flowering period.
