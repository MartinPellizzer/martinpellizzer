Verbascum × kuetahyense

Verbascum × kuetahyense, commonly known as the Kuetahy mullein, is a hybrid plant in the genus Verbascum, which belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae.

It is characterized by its large, velvety, gray-green leaves and tall, erect flowering stems that bear clusters of yellow flowers. Native to the region of Kütahya in Turkey, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. It is often found in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid environments.

The plant's striking appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
