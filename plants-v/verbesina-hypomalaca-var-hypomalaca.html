Verbesina hypomalaca var. hypomalaca

Verbesina hypomalaca var. hypomalaca, commonly known as the yellow crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet, featuring upright stems covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow ray flowers with a distinctive crown-like appearance, blooming from late summer into early fall. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly rough texture, often with a pale green underside.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for native gardens and pollinator habitats.
