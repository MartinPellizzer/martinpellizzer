Verbascum × macilentum

Verbascum × macilentum, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial plant belonging to the Scrophulariaceae family.

It is characterized by its fuzzy, velvety leaves that are typically gray-green in color, providing a distinctive appearance. The plant produces tall, sparsely branched stems that bear yellow flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits a range of morphological variations compared to its parent species.
