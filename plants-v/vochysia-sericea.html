Vochysia sericea

Vochysia sericea, commonly known as silky vochysia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark green foliage and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces woody capsules containing numerous ellipsoidal seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Vochysia sericea is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
