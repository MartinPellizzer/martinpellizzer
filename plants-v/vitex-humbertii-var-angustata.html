Vitex humbertii var. angustata

Vitex humbertii var. angustata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved chaste tree, is a shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and showy, fragrant blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is often found in rocky or dry soils, thriving in full sun. Its fruit is a woody capsule containing several small, dark seeds.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
