Viburnum sargentii f. hydrangeoides

Viburnum sargentii f. hydrangeoides, commonly known as the hydrangea-like viburnum, is a hybrid shrub characterized by its large, showy inflorescences.

The plant features clusters of white to pink flowers that resemble hydrangeas, giving it its common name. Its foliage is typically dark green, with some cultivars displaying variegation. It thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to a range of garden conditions.
