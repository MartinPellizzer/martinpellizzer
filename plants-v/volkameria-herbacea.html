Volkameria herbacea

Volkameria herbacea, commonly known as the silverleaf volkameria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, often in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are leathery and covered in a fine, silvery-white coating, which gives it its common name.

The plant is adapted to dry, rocky environments and is often found in coastal regions.
