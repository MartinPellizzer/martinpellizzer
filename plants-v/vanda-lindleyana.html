Vanda lindleyana

Vanda lindleyana, commonly known as the Lindley's vanda, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic. The species is characterized by its long, slender stems and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. Vanda lindleyana thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its flowers bloom repeatedly throughout the growing season, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
