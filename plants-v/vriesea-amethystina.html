Vriesea amethystina

Vriesea amethystina, commonly known as the amethystine bromeliad, is a striking epiphytic plant native to Brazil.

It features a rosette of narrow, dark green leaves that are covered in a dense layer of white, waxy bloom, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces vibrant red and orange flowers that bloom in a spiral pattern, adding dramatic visual interest. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

Vriesea amethystina is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
