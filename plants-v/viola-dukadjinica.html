Viola dukadjinica

Viola dukadjinica, commonly known as the Dukadjin violet, is a species of flowering plant native to the Balkan region.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and delicate, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and forest edges. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Viola dukadjinica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
