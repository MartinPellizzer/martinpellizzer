Viburnum Dentatum Var. Deamii

Viburnum dentatum var. deamii is a rare and distinctive shrub native to the southeastern united states. it is known for its striking white flowers and vibrant red berries that attract birds and other wildlife. this variety is closely related to the common arrowwood viburnum but has unique characteristics that set it apart. it thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in forest understories. due to its beauty and ecological value, it is a sought-after plant for native gardens and conservation efforts.
