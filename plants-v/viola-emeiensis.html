Viola emeiensis

Viola emeiensis, commonly known as the Emei Mountain violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and delicate violet flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as mountain forests. Its flowers, which bloom in spring, range in color from pale purple to deep violet.

Viola emeiensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
