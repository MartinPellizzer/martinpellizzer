Virola chrysocarpa

Virola chrysocarpa, commonly known as golden corymbia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Myristicaceae and is characterized by its large, glossy, ovate leaves. Virola chrysocarpa is also noted for its ability to thrive in humid, tropical environments with partial shade.
