Vriesea × weyringeriana

Vriesea × weyringeriana, commonly known as the hybrid vriesea, is a hybrid cultivar of the Vriesea genus, which belongs to the Bromeliaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, often colorful inflorescence that can range from red to orange or yellow, depending on the specific cultivar. The plant typically features a rosette of broad, strap-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a waxy texture. Native to South America, it thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Vriesea × weyringeriana is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
