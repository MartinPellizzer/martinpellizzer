Vernonia bojeri

Vernonia bojeri, commonly known as the red vernonia, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal areas and disturbed soils. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to red, depending on the species. Its leaves are usually narrow and lance-shaped, with a rough texture.

Vernonia bojeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
