Vella annua

Vella annua, commonly known as annual vella, is an annual herbaceous plant native to temperate regions.

It typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters and features slender stems covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or white in color, blooming from late spring to early autumn. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly serrated edge.

Vella annua is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of habitats including roadsides and fields.
