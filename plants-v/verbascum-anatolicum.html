Verbascum anatolicum

Verbascum anatolicum, commonly known as Anatolian mullein, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Scrophulariaceae family and is characterized by its tall, hairy stems and large, velvety leaves. Verbascum anatolicum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used traditionally in herbal medicine.
