Villaresia pungens

Villaresia pungens, commonly known as the spiny poinsettia, is a striking plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red bracts that surround small, inconspicuous flowers. This ornamental plant is popular in horticulture for its eye-catching foliage and seasonal color display.
