Vangueria oligantha var. microphylla

Vangueria oligantha var. microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved vangueria, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are usually less than 2 centimeters in length. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for use in rock gardens or as a decorative plant in arid landscapes.
