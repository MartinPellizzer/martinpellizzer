Vinca pubescens

Vinca pubescens, commonly known as the spreading vinca or small-flowered vinca, is a flowering plant native to Europe, North Africa, and parts of Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that produces vibrant purple or blue flowers throughout the growing season. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves and is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardiness.

Vinca pubescens is also valued for its ability to spread and cover ground, making it an effective ground cover plant.
