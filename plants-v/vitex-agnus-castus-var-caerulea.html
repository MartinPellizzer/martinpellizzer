Vitex agnus-castus var. caerulea

Vitex agnus-castus var. caerulea, commonly known as blue vitex, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value comes from both its foliage and its vibrant floral display.
