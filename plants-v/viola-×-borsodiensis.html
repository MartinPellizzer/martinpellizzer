Viola × borsodiensis

Viola × borsodiensis, commonly known as the Borsodi violet, is a hybrid species within the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, violet-colored flowers that typically bloom in early spring. The plant is native to Hungary and has been cultivated for its ornamental value. It features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and can grow up to 30 cm in height.

Viola × borsodiensis thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas or gardens.
