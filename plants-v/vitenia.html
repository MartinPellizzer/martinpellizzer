Vitenia

Vitenia, commonly known as the golden apple tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant flowers. The fruit, which resembles a small apple, is typically yellow or orange when ripe. Vitenia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its aromatic properties.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both landscaping and traditional herbal use.
