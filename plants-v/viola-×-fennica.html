Viola × fennica

Viola × fennica, commonly known as the Swedish violet, is a hybrid species within the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of delicate, violet-blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. It has a short growing season and is often found in temperate regions of Europe.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental plants with a natural, wild appearance.
