Vernonia laosensis

Vernonia laosensis, commonly known as the Laos ironweed, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant is typically found in moist, forested areas and can grow up to several meters in height. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in dense clusters.

Vernonia laosensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in local medicine.
