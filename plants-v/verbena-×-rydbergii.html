Verbena × rydbergii

Verbena × rydbergii, commonly known as Rydberg's verbena, is a hybrid plant in the Verbenaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, clustered flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically produces small, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Verbena × rydbergii is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
