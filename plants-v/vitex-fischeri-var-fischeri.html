Vitex fischeri var. fischeri

Vitex fischeri var. fischeri, commonly known as the Fischer's vitex, is a species of flowering plant native to parts of Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually clustered in spikes, and it has opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
