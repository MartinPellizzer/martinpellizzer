Vidoricum korthalsii

Vidoricum korthalsii, commonly known as the Korthals' violets, is a member of the Violaceae family and is native to southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous plant that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and rocky areas. The plant features delicate, violet-blue flowers with five petals, often accompanied by showy bracts. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Vidoricum korthalsii is valued in horticulture for its beauty and is often cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
