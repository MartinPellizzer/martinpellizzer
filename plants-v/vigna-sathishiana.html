Vigna sathishiana

Vigna sathishiana, commonly known as the Sathishiana cowpea, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, particularly India. The plant is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and erect, branching stems. Its small, yellowish-white flowers are followed by elongated pods containing edible seeds.

Vigna sathishiana is valued for its nutritional content and is cultivated for both food and forage purposes.
