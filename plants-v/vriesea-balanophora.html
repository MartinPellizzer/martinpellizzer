Vriesea balanophora

Vriesea balanophora, commonly known as the silver vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, showy inflorescence that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The plant features broad, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a silvery or white powdery coating, giving it its common name. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Vriesea balanophora is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
