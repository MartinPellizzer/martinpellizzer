Viola palmata var. asarifolia

Viola palmata var. asarifolia, commonly known as the asarifolia violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It features heart-shaped, palmately lobed leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving them a velvety texture. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of purple and white, typically arranged in racemes. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species within the Violaceae family.
