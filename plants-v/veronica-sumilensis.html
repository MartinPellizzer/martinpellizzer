Veronica sumilensis

Veronica sumilensis, commonly known as the Sumil River veronica, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Plantaginaceae and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, blue to purple flowers arranged in spikes, which bloom in the spring and summer months. Its leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.
