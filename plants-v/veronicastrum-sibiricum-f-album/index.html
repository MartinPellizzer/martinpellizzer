Veronicastrum Sibiricum F. Album

Veronicastrum sibiricum f. album is a striking ornamental plant known for its tall, spike-like inflorescences. This cultivar features clusters of pale blue or white flowers that stand out against its dark green foliage. It is a hardy perennial that thrives in moist to wet conditions, making it ideal for water gardens or boggy areas. The plant is valued for its elegant appearance and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. Its unique coloration and structure make it a popular choice among gardeners seeking a dramatic focal point.
