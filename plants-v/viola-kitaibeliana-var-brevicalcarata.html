Viola kitaibeliana var. brevicalcarata

Viola kitaibeliana var. brevicalcarata, commonly known as the short-spurred violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped, velvety leaves and clusters of violet-blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its flowers have short, blunt spurs and bloom in early spring.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
