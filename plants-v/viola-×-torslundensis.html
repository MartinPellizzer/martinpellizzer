Viola × torslundensis

Viola × torslundensis, commonly known as the Torslund violet, is a hybrid species within the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of violet or purple flowers. Native to Scandinavia, this plant thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The flowers typically bloom in spring, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
