Vindicta begoniifolia

Vindicta begoniifolia, commonly known as the Begonia with a Vindicta leaf, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, velvety, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a reddish tinge. The plant produces clusters of small, pale pink flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Vindicta begoniifolia is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
