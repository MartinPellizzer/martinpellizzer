Verbascum scaposum

Verbascum scaposum, commonly known as the slender mullein, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, fuzzy, gray-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a common sight in rocky or dry habitats.

Verbascum scaposum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal medicine.
