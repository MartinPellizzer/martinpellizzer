Valeriana connata var. nutans

Valeriana connata var. nutans, commonly known as the nodding valerian, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features upright, branched stems that bear small, clustered flowers with showy, pinkish-white petals. The plant has aromatic, often hairy leaves that are opposite and lance-shaped. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
