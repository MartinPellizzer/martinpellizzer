Vandellia capitata

Vandellia capitata, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking red, spider-like flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has a short, stout stem and produces small, dark green leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Vandellia capitata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
