Vitellaria retusa

Vitellaria retusa, commonly known as the shea tree, is a deciduous tree native to the savannas of West Africa.

It is valued for its fruit, which contains a nutritious seed used to produce shea butter. The tree has a broad, spreading canopy and can grow up to 20 meters tall. Its leaves are alternate and palmately lobed, and it produces small yellow flowers.

The shea tree plays an important ecological and economic role in the region.
