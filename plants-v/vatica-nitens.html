Vatica nitens

Vatica nitens, commonly known as the yellow ironwood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, hard wood that is highly valued for its durability and resistance to decay. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptic in shape and dark green in color. It produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in spikes, followed by woody capsules containing flat, winged seeds.

Vatica nitens thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
