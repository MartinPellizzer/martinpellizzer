Vinca semidesertorum

Vinca semidesertorum, commonly known as desert vinca, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of North America.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that thrives in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant produces vibrant blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are small, fleshy, and adapted to reduce water loss in dry conditions.

Vinca semidesertorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
