Vriesea Fluviatilis

Vriesea fluviatilis is a striking bromeliad native to the rainforests of South America. Known for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms, it adds a touch of exotic beauty to any indoor space. This plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts and collectors. With proper care, Vriesea fluviatilis can flourish and bring a lush, tropical feel to your home.
