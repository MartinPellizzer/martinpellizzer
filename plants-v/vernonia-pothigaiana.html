Vernonia pothigaiana

Vernonia pothigaiana, commonly known as the Pothigaian ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories or along stream banks. Vernonia pothigaiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local medicine.
