Viola phitosiana

Viola phitosiana, commonly known as the Ethiopian violet, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest undergrowth or along stream banks. Its flowers bloom in spring and are often visited by bees and other pollinators.

Viola phitosiana is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
