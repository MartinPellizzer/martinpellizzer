Viburnum loeseneri

Viburnum loeseneri, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen viburnum, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers in the spring, followed by red, berry-like fruits in the fall. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, moist soil.

Viburnum loeseneri is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability in various garden settings.
