Vicia voggenreiteriana

Vicia voggenreiteriana, commonly known as the Voggenreiter's vetch, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, pea-like flowers. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, typical of many leguminous species. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
