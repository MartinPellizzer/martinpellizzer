Verbascum densiflorum

Verbascum densiflorum, commonly known as the denseflower mullein, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, fuzzy leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces showy yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy perennial in suitable climates.

Its common name reflects the dense arrangement of its small, clustered flowers.
