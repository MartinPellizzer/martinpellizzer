Verbascum keniense

Verbascum keniense, commonly known as Kenyan mullein, is a perennial plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the genus Verbascum, which includes species known for their showy, fluffy flowers. The plant features robust, hairy stems and large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. Its flowers, which bloom in late summer, are typically pale yellow to greenish in color.

Verbascum keniense is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient plant in its native habitat.
