Veronica villosula

Veronica villosula, commonly known as the hairy speedwell, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 20 centimeters. The plant features small, blue or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are hairy and arranged in pairs along the stems.

Veronica villosula thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
