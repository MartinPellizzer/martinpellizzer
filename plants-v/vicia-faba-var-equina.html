Vicia faba var. equina

Vicia faba var. equina, commonly known as the horse bean or field bean, is a variant of the broad bean species.

It is a leguminous plant characterized by its erect growth habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces cylindrical pods containing several large, rounded seeds. It is native to Europe and has been cultivated for its nutritional value and agricultural uses.

Vicia faba var. equina is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, benefiting surrounding plants.
