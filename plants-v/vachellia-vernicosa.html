Vachellia vernicosa

Vachellia vernicosa, commonly known as the white thorn, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, thorny stems and small, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has compound leaves with pairs of small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in savannas and rocky areas.

Vachellia vernicosa is also known for its hardy nature and ability to withstand drought conditions.
