Vriesea × vangeertii

Vriesea × vangeertii, commonly known as the hybrid Vriesea, is a hybrid orchid that combines traits from various Vriesea species.

It is a tropical plant native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is known for its striking, long-lasting inflorescences. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in the dry seasons.

Vriesea × vangeertii is popular among collectors for its vibrant, colorful blooms that can last for several weeks.
