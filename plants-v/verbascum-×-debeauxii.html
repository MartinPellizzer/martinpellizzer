Verbascum × debeauxii

Verbascum × debeauxii, commonly known as the Beauxii mullein, is a hybrid plant in the genus Verbascum, which belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, fuzzy, velvety leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soils.

Verbascum × debeauxii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
