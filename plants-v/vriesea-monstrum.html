Vriesea monstrum

Vriesea monstrum, commonly known as the giant vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, vibrant green leaves that form a rosette shape, with a striking red and yellow inflorescence that blooms in the center. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is known for its long-lasting blooms, which can last several months, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Vriesea monstrum is also relatively low maintenance, making it suitable for both novice and experienced plant enthusiasts.
