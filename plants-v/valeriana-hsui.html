Valeriana hsui

Valeriana hsui, commonly known as Chinese valerian, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has a rhizomatous root system. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a strong, distinctive scent.
