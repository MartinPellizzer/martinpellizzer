Vernonia ruficoma

Vernonia ruficoma, commonly known as the red-stemmed ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its conical flower heads. Vernonia ruficoma is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators.
