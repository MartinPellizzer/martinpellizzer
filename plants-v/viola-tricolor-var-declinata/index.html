Viola Tricolor Var. Declinata

Viola tricolor var. declinata, also known as the violet wood sorrel, is a charming flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. This variety is distinguished by its delicate trifoliate leaves and clusters of small, colorful flowers that bloom in shades of purple, white, and yellow. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks. The plant is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its historical use in herbal medicine. Its unique appearance and resilience make it a favorite among gardeners and botanists alike.
