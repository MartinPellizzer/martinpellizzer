Veronica pusilla var. erciyasdagi

Veronica pusilla var. erciyasdagi, commonly known as the Erciyas dwarf speedwell, is a small, flowering plant native to Turkey.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, sky-blue or violet flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or alpine settings.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and hardiness in temperate climates.
