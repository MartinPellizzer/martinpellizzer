Verbascum × pejovicii

Verbascum × pejovicii, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial plant belonging to the genus Verbascum in the family Scrophulariaceae.

It is a hybrid between Verbascum blattaria and Verbascum phlomoides, resulting in a plant with distinctive gray-green, fuzzy leaves. The plant produces tall spikes of yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It is native to southern Europe and thrives in well-drained soils in full sun.

Its foliage and flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
