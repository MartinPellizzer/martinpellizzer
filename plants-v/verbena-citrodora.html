Verbena citrodora

Verbena citrodora, commonly known as lemon verbena, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its fragrant, lemon-scented leaves and spikes of blue or purple flowers. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for Mediterranean climates. Its aromatic foliage is often used in teas and culinary applications.

Verbena citrodora is also valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
