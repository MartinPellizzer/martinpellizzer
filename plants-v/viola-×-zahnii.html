Viola × zahnii

Viola × zahnii, commonly known as the hybrid violet, is a member of the Violaceae family and is native to Europe.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 10–30 cm, featuring heart-shaped leaves and showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland or shaded garden settings.
