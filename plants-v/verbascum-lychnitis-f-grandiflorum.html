Verbascum lychnitis f. grandiflorum

Verbascum lychnitis f. grandiflorum, commonly known as the large-flowered mullein, is a variant of the mullein plant characterized by its large, showy flowers.

It is a herbaceous perennial that belongs to the Scrophulariaceae family. The plant features soft, velvety leaves that are typically gray-green in color. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange and bloom in late summer.

It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
