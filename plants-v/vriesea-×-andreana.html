Vriesea × andreana

Vriesea × andreana, commonly known as the hybrid Vriesea, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Vriesea × leucantha and Vriesea × maculata.

It is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is native to South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, colorful inflorescences that can range from red to orange and yellow. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Vriesea × andreana is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
