Viola portalesia

Viola portalesia, commonly known as the Portales violet, is a species of flowering plant in the family Violaceae.

It is native to the high-altitude regions of South America, particularly in the Andes. The plant produces showy, violet-colored flowers with five petals, often with a distinctive dark spot at the base. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Viola portalesia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
