Vaccinium galezans

Vaccinium galezans, commonly known as the Galezan blueberry, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the cool, moist regions of the Pacific Northwest of the United States.

The plant produces small, edible berries that are typically dark blue or purple in color, with a tart flavor. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, and it thrives in acidic, well-drained soils.
