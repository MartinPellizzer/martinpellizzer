Viola wahlenbergii

Viola wahlenbergii, commonly known as the Swedish violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces showy violet flowers with a distinctive white throat, typically blooming in early spring. It prefers moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Viola wahlenbergii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a ground cover.
