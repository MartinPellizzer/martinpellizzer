Velezia rigida var. glabrata

Velezia rigida var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth velezia, is a member of the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its rigid, glabrous stems and broad, ovate leaves with a smooth texture. The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers in shades of pink to purple, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a decorative plant.
