Viola × cooperrideri

Viola × cooperrideri, commonly known as the hybrid violet, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Violaceae family.

It is a cross between Viola tricolor and Viola × wittrockiana, resulting in a diverse range of colors and flower forms. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture and produces clusters of showy, five-petaled flowers. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.

Viola × cooperrideri is valued for its adaptability and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
