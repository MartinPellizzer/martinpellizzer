Vignea austroafghanica

Vignea austroafghanica, commonly known as the Afghan morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Afghanistan and Pakistan.

It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is characterized by its trumpet-shaped, violet-blue flowers that bloom in the early morning. Vignea austroafghanica is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
