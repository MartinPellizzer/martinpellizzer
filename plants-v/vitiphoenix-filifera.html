Vitiphoenix filifera

Vitiphoenix filifera, commonly known as the silky palm, is a striking tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and long, pinnate leaves that give it a feathery appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by bright orange or red fruits containing edible seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Vitiphoenix filifera is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
