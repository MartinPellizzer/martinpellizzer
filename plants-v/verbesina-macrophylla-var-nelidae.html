Verbesina macrophylla var. nelidae

Verbesina macrophylla var. nelidae, commonly known as the Nelidae verbena, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is a perennial shrub that thrives in full sun and well-drained soil. Its showy blooms attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ease of care.
