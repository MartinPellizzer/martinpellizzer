Vernonia nitens

Vernonia nitens, commonly known as the slender ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its erect, slender stems and small, tubular yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as woodlands and wet meadows. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Vernonia nitens is known for its ability to thrive in partial shade and is often found in disturbed soils.
