Verbena teucrioides var. anais

Verbena teucrioides var. anais, commonly known as Anais verbena, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, vibrant blue or purple flowers. The plant produces slender, branching stems and has narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and ability to attract pollinators.
