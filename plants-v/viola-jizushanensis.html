Viola jizushanensis

Viola jizushanensis, commonly known as the Jizushan violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of violet-blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its flowers bloom in spring, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Viola jizushanensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
