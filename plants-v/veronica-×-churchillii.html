Veronica × churchillii

Veronica × churchillii, commonly known as the Churchill's speedwell, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Plantaginaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, sky-blue to violet flowers. The plant typically features lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, contributing to its textured appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is often cultivated in gardens across temperate regions.
