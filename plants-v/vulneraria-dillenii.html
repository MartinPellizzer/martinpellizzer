Vulneraria dillenii

Vulneraria dillenii, commonly known as the narrowleaf vulneraria, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant typically grows to a height of about 20 to 40 centimeters, featuring slender stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are white or pale pink, arranged in racemes, and bloom during the spring and summer months.

Vulneraria dillenii is characterized by its delicate appearance and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
