Vittaria wooroonooran

Vittaria wooroonooran, commonly known as the Wooroonooran fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Vittariaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or branches in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are pinnately divided and often have a bluish-green hue.

Vittaria wooroonooran is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in the ecological balance of its native habitats.
