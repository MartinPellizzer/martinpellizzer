Vicilla

Vicilla, commonly known as field vetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which consist of three small, oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to purple, depending on the species. Vicilla is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of climates.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it a valuable plant for soil enrichment and ecological restoration.
