Virola decorticans

Virola decorticans, commonly known as the copal tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Myristicaceae and is characterized by its large, glossy, ovate leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant is also valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in its native habitats.
