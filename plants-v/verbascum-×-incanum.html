Verbascum × incanum

Verbascum × incanum, commonly known as the flossflower or silky mullein, is a hybrid plant in the genus Verbascum, native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy, fluffy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant has large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a velvety texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in dry, rocky habitats.

Verbascum × incanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used historically for its medicinal properties.
