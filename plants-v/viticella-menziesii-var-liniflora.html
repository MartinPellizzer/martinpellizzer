Viticella menziesii var. liniflora

Viticella menziesii var. liniflora, commonly known as the silverleaf honeysuckle, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features slender, arching stems covered in silvery-white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The leaves are narrow, lance-shaped, and have a soft, leathery texture. It produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically white or pale yellow, which bloom in spring.

This plant is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
