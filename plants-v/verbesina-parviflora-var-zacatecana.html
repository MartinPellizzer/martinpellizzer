Verbesina parviflora var. zacatecana

Verbesina parviflora var. zacatecana, commonly known as the Zacatecan ironweed, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves, which help it retain moisture in arid environments.

Its flowers bloom in late summer and attract various pollinators, including bees and butterflies.
