Veronica spicata f. nitens

Veronica spicata f. nitens, commonly known as the shining speedwell, is a compact, herbaceous perennial that features dense clusters of small, white flowers.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its upright, spiky inflorescences. The foliage is typically dark green and glossy, adding visual interest throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation in gardens.
