Vanda falcata subsp. falcata

Vanda falcata subsp.

falcata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf vanda, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia. It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments.

The plant features long, strap-like leaves that are usually dark green with a waxy texture. Its flowers are showy, with a wide range of colors including white, pink, and purple, and are often fragrant. Vanda falcata subsp.

falcata requires high light exposure and good air circulation to thrive.
