Veltheimia deasii

Veltheimia deasii, commonly known as the Cape tulip, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces tall, upright flower stalks adorned with showy, tulip-shaped blooms. The flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive yellow center, and bloom in late winter to early spring. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value and ease of cultivation have made it a favored addition to many gardens.
