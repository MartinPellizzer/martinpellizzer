Viburnum chinense

Viburnum chinense, commonly known as Chinese snowberry, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and turn reddish in autumn. The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers in spring, followed by bright red, berry-like fruits in the fall. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Viburnum chinense is valued for its ornamental value and adaptability to various garden conditions.
