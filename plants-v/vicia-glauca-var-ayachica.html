Vicia glauca var. ayachica

Vicia glauca var. ayachica, commonly known as the Ayachica vetch, is a flowering plant native to parts of Mexico.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-bearing fruit. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its flowers are usually pale blue or violet, and it produces small, lens-shaped seeds.

It is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value or used in traditional herbal practices.
