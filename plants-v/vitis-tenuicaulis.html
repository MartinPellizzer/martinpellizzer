Vitis tenuicaulis

Vitis tenuicaulis, commonly known as the slender grapevines, is a species of flowering plant in the Vitaceae family.

It is native to the southeastern United States, where it thrives in moist, sandy soils. The plant is characterized by its thin, climbing stems and small, palmate leaves. Its flowers are typically greenish-white and appear in clusters, followed by small, round berries.

Vitis tenuicaulis is often found in woodland areas and is adapted to partial shade.
