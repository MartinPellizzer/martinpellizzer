Verbena ulicina

Verbena ulicina, commonly known as coastal verbena, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub that thrives in coastal and sandy environments. The plant produces clusters of small, vibrant blue or violet flowers that bloom throughout the summer. Its leaves are narrow and gray-green, often covered in a fine layer of white hair.

Verbena ulicina is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize coastal soils.
