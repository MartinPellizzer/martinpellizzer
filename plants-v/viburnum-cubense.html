Viburnum cubense

Viburnum cubense, commonly known as Cuban holly, is a deciduous shrub native to the Caribbean, particularly Cuba.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and have a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers in the spring, which are followed by bright red berries in the fall. Viburnum cubense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it adaptable to a variety of garden settings.
