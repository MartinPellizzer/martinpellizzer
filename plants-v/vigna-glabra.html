Vigna glabra

Vigna glabra, commonly known as cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain edible seeds. Vigna glabra is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, improving soil fertility.

It is widely cultivated for its nutritional seeds, which are rich in protein and essential nutrients.
