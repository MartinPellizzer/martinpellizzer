Viburnum taiwanianum

Viburnum taiwanianum, commonly known as the Taiwan arrowwood, is a deciduous shrub native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the family Caprifoliaceae and is characterized by its clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers that appear in spring. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden landscapes in temperate climates.
