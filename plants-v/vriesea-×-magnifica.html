Vriesea × magnifica

Vriesea × magnifica, commonly known as the flame plant, is a hybrid orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting inflorescences that display a striking combination of red, orange, and yellow hues. The plant features broad, strap-like leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Vriesea × magnifica is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
