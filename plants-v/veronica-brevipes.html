Veronica brevipes

Veronica brevipes, commonly known as the shortfoot speedwell, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 20 centimeters. The plant features small, blue or violet flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom from late spring to early autumn. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Veronica brevipes is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to thrive in arid or semi-arid environments.
