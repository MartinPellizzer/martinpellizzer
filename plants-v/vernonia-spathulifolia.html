Vernonia spathulifolia

Vernonia spathulifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf ironweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

Vernonia spathulifolia is often found in moist, well-drained soils and is adapted to a variety of environmental conditions.
