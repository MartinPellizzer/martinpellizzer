Vanda × charlesworthii

Vanda × charlesworthii, commonly known as the Charlesworth's vanda, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, showy flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia, where it typically grows as an epiphyte on trees. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, orchid-like blooms in shades of pink, white, and purple. It requires bright, indirect light and well-draining potting media to thrive.

Vanda × charlesworthii is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
