Verbena reichei

Verbena reichei, commonly known as the silver verbena, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and spikes of blue or purple flowers. The plant belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It prefers full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Verbena reichei is known for its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in coastal climates.
