Viola × malvesinii

Viola × malvesinii, commonly known as the hybrid violet, is a cross between Viola tricolor and Viola cornuta.

It is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers in shades of purple, violet, and white, often with a yellow center. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas or gardens.

Its foliage consists of heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture.
