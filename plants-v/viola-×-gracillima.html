Viola × gracillima

Viola × gracillima, commonly known as the slender violet, is a hybrid species within the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, violet-blue flowers with a distinctive yellow center, often blooming in early spring. Native to Europe, it thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodland floors.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
