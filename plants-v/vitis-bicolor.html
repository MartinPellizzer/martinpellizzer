Vitis bicolor

Vitis bicolor, commonly known as the sand grape, is a species of flowering plant in the Vitaceae family.

It is native to the southeastern United States, where it thrives in sandy soils and coastal areas. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or greenish in color. Its leaves are alternate, heart-shaped, and have a rough texture.

The fruit is a small, round berry that ripens to a dark purple or black color.
