Verschaffeltia splendida

Verschaffeltia splendida, commonly known as the splendour bellflower, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant blue or purple bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Verschaffeltia splendida is also noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
