Vatica wallichii

It belongs to the family Dipterocarpaceae and is characterized by its large, glossy leaves and fragrant flowers. The tree produces heavy, woody fruits that contain flat, winged seeds. It is valued for its hard, durable wood, which is used in construction and furniture.
