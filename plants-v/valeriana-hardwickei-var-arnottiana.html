Valeriana hardwickei var. arnottiana

Valeriana hardwickei var. arnottiana, commonly known as the Arnold's valerian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features upright stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically white or pale pink, which bloom in summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its roots contain compounds that have been traditionally used in herbal medicine.
