Veronicastrum japonicum var. australe

Veronicastrum japonicum var. australe, commonly known as Japanese spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers in late summer. The plant has alternate, ovate leaves with serrated edges, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

This ornamental plant is often used in gardens for its attractive foliage and flowering display.
