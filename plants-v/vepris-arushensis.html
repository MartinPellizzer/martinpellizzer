Vepris arushensis

Vepris arushensis, commonly known as the Arusha cinnamon tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to the East African highlands.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, yellowish flowers. Due to habitat loss and overharvesting, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
