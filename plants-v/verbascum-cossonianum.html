Verbascum cossonianum

Verbascum cossonianum, commonly known as the Cossonian mullein, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, hairy stems and large, gray-green leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces showy yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy plant in temperate climates.

Verbascum cossonianum is also valued for its ornamental qualities in gardens.
