Vaccinium delavayi subsp. merrillianum

Vaccinium delavayi subsp.

merrillianum, commonly known as the Yunnan huckleberry, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of southwestern China. It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes many acid-loving plants.

The plant produces small, edible berries that are typically dark blue or purple in color. It thrives in cool, moist environments with well-drained acidic soils.
