Vitex Trifolia Var. Repens

Vitex trifolia var. repens is a sprawling shrub native to the southern United States and parts of Mexico. It is known for its fragrant, lavender-colored flowers that bloom in the summer months. This plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens. Its trailing growth habit allows it to be used as a ground cover or in hanging baskets. Despite its beauty, it can become invasive if not properly managed.
