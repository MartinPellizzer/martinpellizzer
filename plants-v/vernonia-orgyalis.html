Vernonia orgyalis

Vernonia orgyalis, commonly known as the golden crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces bright yellow flower heads that are often surrounded by pale green bracts. It is typically found in open grasslands and is adapted to dry conditions.

Vernonia orgyalis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional uses in some regions.
