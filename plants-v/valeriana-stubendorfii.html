Valeriana stubendorfii

Valeriana stubendorfii, commonly known as the mountain valerian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, often fragrant foliage. Valeriana stubendorfii is valued both for its ecological role and its potential therapeutic applications.
