Viola betonicifolia var. oblongosagittata

Viola betonicifolia var. oblongosagittata, commonly known as the oblong arrowleaf violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of East Asia.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a distinctive arrowhead shape, often with a silvery underside. The plant produces violet-blue flowers with five petals, typically blooming in early spring. It prefers moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species in the Violaceae family.
