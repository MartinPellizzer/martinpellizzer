Vernonia brachycalyx

Vernonia brachycalyx, commonly known as the narrow calyx ironweed, is a perennial herb native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features upright, branched stems covered in fine, hairy foliage. Its small, yellow flowers are typically arranged in dense, spike-like clusters.

Vernonia brachycalyx is often found in moist, open habitats such as woodland edges and wetlands.
