Valeriana tenuissima

Valeriana tenuissima, commonly known as slender valerian, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its slender, creeping stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a distinct, aromatic scent.

Valeriana tenuissima is known for its medicinal properties, often used in traditional remedies for its calming effects.
