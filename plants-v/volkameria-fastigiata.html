Volkameria fastigiata

Volkameria fastigiata, commonly known as the silverleaf volkameria, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental leaves make it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
