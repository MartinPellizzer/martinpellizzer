Viminaria preissii

Viminaria preissii, commonly known as the yellow flame tree, is a species of flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has a woody stem and produces small, leathery leaves that are arranged opposite along the branches. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.

Viminaria preissii prefers full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.
