Voyria thalesioides

Voyria thalesioides, commonly known as the golden star vine, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a striking appearance. The plant has slender, climbing stems that often trail along the ground or climb supports, and its leaves are opposite, ovate, and slightly hairy. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
