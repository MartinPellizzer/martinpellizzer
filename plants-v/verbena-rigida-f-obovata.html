Verbena rigida f. obovata

Verbena rigida f. obovata, commonly known as the rigid verbena or obovate verbena, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems and produces spikes of small, vibrant blue or purple flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is noted for its compact growth habit and long blooming period, often extending from late spring through early autumn.
