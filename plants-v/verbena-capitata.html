Verbena capitata

Verbena capitata, commonly known as purpletop vervain, is a herbaceous annual plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, branching stems and produces showy clusters of violet-blue flowers. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Verbena capitata is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
