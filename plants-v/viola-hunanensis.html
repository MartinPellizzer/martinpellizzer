Viola hunanensis

Viola hunanensis, commonly known as the Hunan violet, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and delicate, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky areas. Its flowers bloom in early spring, adding a touch of color to its natural habitat.

Viola hunanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
