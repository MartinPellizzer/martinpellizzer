Vangueria bequaertii

Vangueria bequaertii, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Vangueria bequaertii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
