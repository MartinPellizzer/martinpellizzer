Vigna ntemensis

Vigna ntemensis, commonly known as the Congo bean, is a climbing legume native to Central and Western Africa.

It produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several round or oval seeds. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets and is characterized by its yellow flowers. It is adapted to tropical and subtropical climates and thrives in well-drained soils.

Vigna ntemensis is valued for its nutritional seeds, which are rich in protein and can be used in various culinary preparations.
