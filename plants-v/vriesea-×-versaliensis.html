Vriesea × versaliensis

Vriesea × versaliensis, commonly known as the hybrid vriesea, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Vriesea × tricolor and Vriesea × spectabilis.

It is a member of the Bromeliaceae family, characterized by its striking, colorful inflorescences that can last for several months. The plant typically features broad, strap-like leaves that form a rosette, creating a compact and visually appealing shape. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity to flourish.

Vriesea × versaliensis is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its vibrant flowers and low maintenance requirements.
