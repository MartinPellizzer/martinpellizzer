Verbena megapotamica var. phlogiflora

Verbena megapotamica var. phlogiflora, commonly known as the flame verbena, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom in dense spikes. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny environments and has narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
