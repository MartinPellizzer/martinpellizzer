Vaccinium goodenoughii

Vaccinium goodenoughii, commonly known as Goodenough's blueberry, is a low-growing shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes other familiar plants like blueberries and cranberries. The plant produces small, edible berries that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Its botanical features make it an important species for both ecological and horticultural purposes.
