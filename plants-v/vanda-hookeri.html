Vanda hookeri

Vanda hookeri, commonly known as the Hooker's vanda, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, slender pseudobulbs and arching stems that bear clusters of broad, leathery leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Vanda hookeri is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
