Vatica kanthanensis

Vatica kanthanensis, commonly known as the Kanthan's ironwood, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, hard wood and evergreen foliage. The plant has compound leaves with elliptical leaflets and produces small, fragrant flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Vatica kanthanensis plays an important role in its ecosystem, providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
