Viola uniflora var. orientalis

Viola uniflora var. orientalis, commonly known as the oriental viola, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces showy, violet-blue flowers with five petals, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial shade.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for rock gardens and woodland plantings.
