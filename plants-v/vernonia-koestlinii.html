Vernonia koestlinii

Vernonia koestlinii, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant is notable for its hard, heavy wood, which is highly valued for construction and tool-making. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Vernonia koestlinii plays an important ecological role in its native environment, providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
