Verbascum humile

Verbascum humile, commonly known as the narrowleaf mullein, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features soft, fuzzy leaves that are typically narrow and lance-shaped, covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Verbascum humile is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal medicine.
