Vitex lowryi

Vitex lowryi, commonly known as the lowry's vitex, is a species of flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in humid, tropical environments. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually purple or blue in color. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a rough texture.

Vitex lowryi is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
