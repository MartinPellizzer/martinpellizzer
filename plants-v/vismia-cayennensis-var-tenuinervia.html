Vismia cayennensis var. tenuinervia

Vismia cayennensis var. tenuinervia, commonly known as the slender-veined vismia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate, elliptical leaves with prominent, parallel venation. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers arranged in compact clusters, followed by woody, elongated fruits containing several seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
