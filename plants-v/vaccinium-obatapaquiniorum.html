Vaccinium obatapaquiniorum

Vaccinium obatapaquiniorum, commonly known as the Obatapaquiniorum blueberry, is a species of evergreen shrub native to the cloud forests of South America.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes many acid-loving plants that thrive in well-drained, nutrient-poor soils. The plant produces small, dark blue berries that are typically harvested in the early summer months. This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential as a novel fruit crop due to its unique flavor profile.
