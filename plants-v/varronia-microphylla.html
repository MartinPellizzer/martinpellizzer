Varronia microphylla

Varronia microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved varronia, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that release tiny seeds when mature.

Varronia microphylla is often found in moist, shaded environments within tropical forests.
