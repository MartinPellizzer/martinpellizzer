Vitex agnus-castus f. laciniosa

Vitex agnus-castus f. laciniosa, commonly known as the laceleaf chaste tree, is a cultivar of the chaste tree known for its ornamental value.

It is characterized by its finely cut, lacy foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of blue and purple, typically blooming in the summer months. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This cultivar is often used in gardens for its aesthetic appeal and can be grown as a shrub or small tree.
