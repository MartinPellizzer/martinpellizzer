Viola olyssiponensis

Viola olyssiponensis, commonly known as the Lisbon violet, is a species of flowering plant native to Portugal.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and delicate, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and rocky areas. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Viola olyssiponensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
