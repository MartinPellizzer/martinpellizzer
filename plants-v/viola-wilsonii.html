Viola wilsonii

Viola wilsonii, commonly known as Wilson's violet, is a species of flowering plant in the Violaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of China, particularly in the Sichuan province. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, showy violet flowers that bloom in early spring. It is often found in moist, shaded woodlands and rocky habitats.

Viola wilsonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
