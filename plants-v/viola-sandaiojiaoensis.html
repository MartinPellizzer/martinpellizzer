Viola sandaiojiaoensis

Viola sandaiojiaoensis, commonly known as the Sandaiojiao violet, is a rare flowering plant native to a specific region in China.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, heart-shaped leaves and small, colorful flowers that typically bloom in early spring. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. Its botanical significance lies in its unique genetic makeup and potential ecological role in its native habitat.
