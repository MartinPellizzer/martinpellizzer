Varronia sphaerocephala

Varronia sphaerocephala, commonly known as the globe-headed varroa, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical inflorescences that give it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are often clustered together in compact heads. Its leaves are typically ovate with a smooth surface and a slightly hairy texture.

Varronia sphaerocephala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
