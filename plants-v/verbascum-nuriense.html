Verbascum nuriense

Verbascum nuriense, commonly known as the Nuriense mullein, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae and is characterized by its tall, hairy stems and large, showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are broad and velvety, which helps reduce water loss.
