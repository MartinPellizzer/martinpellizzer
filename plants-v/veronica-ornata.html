Veronica ornata

Veronica ornata, commonly known as the fairybells, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. Veronica ornata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance, providing nectar for native pollinators.
