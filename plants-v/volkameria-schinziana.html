Volkameria schinziana

Volkameria schinziana, commonly known as the Mexican flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Mexico and Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red flowers that bloom in dense clusters, often appearing in late spring to early summer. The plant has simple, ovate leaves with a smooth surface and a dark green color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The bark is smooth and grayish-brown, and the plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to tolerate coastal conditions.
