Vernonia nummulariifolia

Vernonia nummulariifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its silvery, fuzzy leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy species suitable for arid environments.

Its botanical features make it an attractive choice for xeriscaping and native plant gardens.
