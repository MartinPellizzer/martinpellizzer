Vigna laurentii

Vigna laurentii, commonly known as the African wing bean, is a leguminous plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive winged pods that contain edible seeds. The plant thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated for its nutritional value. It has a deep root system that helps in soil stabilization and nitrogen fixation.

Vigna laurentii is also valued for its adaptability to various soil types and its potential in sustainable agriculture.
