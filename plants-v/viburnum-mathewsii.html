Viburnum mathewsii

Viburnum mathewsii, commonly known as the Chinese snowberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. The plant produces red berries that are edible for birds but toxic to humans. Its leaves are ovate with serrated edges and turn yellow in the autumn.

Viburnum mathewsii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
