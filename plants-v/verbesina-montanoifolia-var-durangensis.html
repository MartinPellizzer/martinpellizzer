Verbesina montanoifolia var. durangensis

Verbesina montanoifolia var. durangensis, commonly known as the Durango ironweed, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy yellow-orange composite flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered with fine hairs, while its stems are sturdy and woody.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local pollinators.
