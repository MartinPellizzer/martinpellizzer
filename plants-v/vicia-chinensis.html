Vicia chinensis

Vicia chinensis, commonly known as Chinese vetch, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds. It is often found in grasslands and along roadsides, thriving in well-drained soils.

Vicia chinensis is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in soil enrichment.
