Viticella Microcalyx

Coral bells, scientifically known as Viticella microcalyx, are a striking and versatile ornamental plant prized for their delicate, bell-shaped foliage. Native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia, these plants have gained popularity in gardens around the world due to their hardiness and aesthetic appeal. Their unique, fern-like leaves come in a variety of colors, from silver to deep green, creating a striking visual contrast in any landscape. Easy to care for, they thrive in partial shade and well-drained soil, making them ideal for rock gardens, borders, or containers. With their elegant appearance and low maintenance requirements, Coral bells are a favorite among both novice and experienced gardeners alike.
