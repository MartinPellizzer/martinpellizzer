Vismia latifolia var. reticulata

Vismia latifolia var. reticulata, commonly known as the African starwood, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows in coastal and lowland areas. The plant is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. Its fruit is a woody capsule that contains several seeds, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
