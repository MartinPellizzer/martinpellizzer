Vilfa preslii

Vilfa preslii, commonly known as the Preslia's violets, is a species of flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils, thriving in well-drained environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Vilfa preslii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
