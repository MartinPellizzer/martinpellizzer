Vaccinium barandanum var. hutchinsonii

Vaccinium barandanum var. hutchinsonii, commonly known as the Hutchinson's blueberry, is a shrub native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes many acid-loving plants. The plant produces small, edible berries that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in cool, moist environments with well-drained acidic soil.
