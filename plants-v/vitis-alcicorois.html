Vitis alcicorois

Vitis alcicorois, commonly known as the silverleaf grapevine, is a perennial vine native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale green. Its berries are small and often used in local winemaking traditions.

Vitis alcicorois is adapted to thrive in dry, sunny environments with well-drained soils.
