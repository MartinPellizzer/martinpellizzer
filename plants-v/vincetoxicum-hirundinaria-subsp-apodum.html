Vincetoxicum hirundinaria subsp. apodum

Vincetoxicum hirundinaria subsp.

apodum, commonly known as the slender milkvetch, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and trifoliate leaves.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in racemes, which are typically followed by pod-like fruits. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.
