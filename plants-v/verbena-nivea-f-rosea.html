Verbena nivea f. rosea

Verbena nivea f. rosea, commonly known as the rose-flowered verbena, is a compact, flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from pale pink to soft lavender. The plant has slender, branching stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its delicate appearance and extended blooming period make it a favorite among gardeners seeking ornamental value.
