Vitex quinata var. puberula

Vitex quinata var. puberula, commonly known as the silky vitex, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant, white or pale purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered with fine, silky hairs, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often used in ornamental gardening.

The plant is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
