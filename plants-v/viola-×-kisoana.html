Viola × kisoana

Viola × kisoana, commonly known as the Kisoan violet, is a hybrid species within the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of vibrant purple or violet flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 10–20 cm and thrives in cool, moist environments. It is native to Japan and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

The species is prized for its hardiness and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
