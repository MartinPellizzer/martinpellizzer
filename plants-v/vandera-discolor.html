Vandera discolor

Vandera discolor, commonly known as the variegated vanda, is a striking orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage with variegated patterns of green, white, and yellow. The plant produces strikingly large, fragrant flowers in shades of pink, white, and purple. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Vandera discolor is a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
