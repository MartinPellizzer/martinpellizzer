Vaccinium decamerocarpon

Vaccinium decamerocarpon, commonly known as the ten-berried blueberry, is a shrub native to the northern regions of North America.

Vaccinium decamerocarpon plays an important role in supporting local wildlife and maintaining ecological balance in its native habitats.
