Valeriana hardwickei

Valeriana hardwickei, commonly known as the hardwicke's valerian, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are upright and branched. Its small, white or pinkish flowers are clustered in dense, cylindrical inflorescences, which bloom in late summer.

Valeriana hardwickei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
