Vimen bicolor

Vimen bicolor, commonly known as the two-colored vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its striking bi-colored leaves, typically featuring a green upper surface and a variegated underside. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of white and pink. Vimen bicolor thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
