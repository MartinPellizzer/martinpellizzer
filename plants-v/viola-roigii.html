Viola roigii

Viola roigii, commonly known as the Chinese violet, is a species of flowering plant in the family Violaceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and prefers well-drained soil. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Viola roigii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
