Vignea ursina

Vignea ursina, commonly known as the bear's vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to climb using tendrils. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that contain numerous seeds.

Vignea ursina is often found in humid, shaded environments within the understory of tropical forests.
