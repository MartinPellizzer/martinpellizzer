Viola willkommii var. intermedia

Viola willkommii var. intermedia, commonly known as the hybrid violet, is a species of flowering plant in the Violaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in cool, moist environments. The plant produces vibrant violet-colored flowers with distinct markings, typically blooming in early spring. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in a fine, velvety texture.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
