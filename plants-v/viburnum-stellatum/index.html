Viburnum Stellatum

Viburnum stellatum, also known as star viburnum, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America. It is prized for its showy, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant features attractive, glossy leaves that change color in the fall, adding seasonal interest to gardens. It is hardy and adaptable, making it a popular choice for landscaping. Its ornamental value and ecological benefits make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens.
