Vilfa eximia

Vilfa eximia, commonly known as the silverleaf veldtgrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems and narrow, silver-gray leaves that give it its distinctive appearance. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils. It produces small, greenish-white flowers in dense clusters during the rainy season.

Vilfa eximia is often used in restoration projects due to its hardiness and ability to stabilize soil.
