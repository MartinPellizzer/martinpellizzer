Vanda esquirolii

Vanda esquirolii, commonly known as the Esquiroli's vanda, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Vanda esquirolii is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
