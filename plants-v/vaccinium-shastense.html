Vaccinium shastense

Vaccinium shastense, commonly known as Shasta huckleberry, is a species of evergreen shrub native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes other berry-producing plants like blueberries and cranberries. The plant produces small, edible berries that are typically dark blue to black in color. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

The leaves are typically oval-shaped with a leathery texture and are evergreen throughout the year.
