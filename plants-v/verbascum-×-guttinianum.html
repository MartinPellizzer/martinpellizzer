Verbascum × guttinianum

Verbascum × guttinianum, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of the Mediterranean.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae and is characterized by its tall, hairy stems and large, velvety leaves. The plant produces showy yellow or orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.
