Vernonia cyanonioides

Vernonia cyanonioides, commonly known as blue vervain, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet flower heads, which are typically borne on slender, branched stems. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy, and it thrives in well-drained soils. It is often found in open woodlands, grasslands, and along riverbanks.

Vernonia cyanonioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
