Vriesea lutheriana

Vriesea lutheriana, commonly known as the Luther's Vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, spathaceous inflorescence that displays a range of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that collect water, creating a natural reservoir. Its flowering period typically occurs during the summer months, adding to its ornamental value.

Vriesea lutheriana is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its ability to thrive in bright, indirect light and moderate humidity.
