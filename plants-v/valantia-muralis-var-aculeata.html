Valantia muralis var. aculeata

Valantia muralis var. aculeata, commonly known as the spiny wall vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters. The variety 'aculeata' is distinguished by its sharp, needle-like spines along the stems and leaves. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found growing on rocky surfaces or walls.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to cover vertical spaces in gardens.
