Vriesea multifoliata

Vriesea multifoliata, commonly known as the "Rainbow Plant," is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful inflorescence that can range from red to orange, yellow, and green. The plant features a central rosette of waxy, strap-like leaves that are often variegated with contrasting colors. Vriesea multifoliata thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity to flourish.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardens.
