Verbascum sinense

Verbascum sinense, commonly known as Chinese mullein, is a perennial plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae and is characterized by its large, fuzzy, gray-green leaves. The plant produces tall spikes of yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or arid environments.

Verbascum sinense is valued for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional Chinese medicine.
