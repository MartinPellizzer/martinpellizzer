Viola merxmuelleri

Viola merxmuelleri, commonly known as the Mexican violet, is a species of flowering plant in the Violaceae family.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and forest floors. Its flowers bloom in early spring and are pollinated by bees and other insects.

Viola merxmuelleri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
