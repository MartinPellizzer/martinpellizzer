Vatica tumbuggaia

Vatica tumbuggaia, commonly known as the Tumbuggaia ironwood, is a tropical tree native to northern Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, hard wood and can grow up to 30 meters tall. The tree has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by woody pods containing flat, winged seeds.

Vatica tumbuggaia plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
