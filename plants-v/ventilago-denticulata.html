Ventilago denticulata

Ventilago denticulata, commonly known as the African snowball, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape with finely toothed margins. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual impact.
