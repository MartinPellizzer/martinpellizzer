Vanilla ribeiroi

Vanilla ribeiroi, commonly known as the Brazilian vanilla or the orchid of the Amazon, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its vine-like growth habit and produces fragrant, vanilla-scented flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments within the dense rainforests of Brazil. Its flowers are usually yellow or greenish in color and are followed by small, round fruits.

The plant is valued for its aromatic qualities and has been used in traditional medicine and as a flavoring agent.
