Vigna friesiorum var. angustifolia

Vigna friesiorum var. angustifolia, commonly known as slender cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, trifoliate leaves and small, pale yellow flowers. The plant produces elongated pods containing edible, fibrous seeds that are rich in protein. It thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated for both its seeds and as a green manure crop.

Its botanical features make it an important species in agricultural systems and ecological restoration efforts.
