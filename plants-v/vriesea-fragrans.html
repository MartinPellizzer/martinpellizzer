Vriesea fragrans

Vriesea fragrans, commonly known as the fragrant vriesea, is a species of flowering plant in the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescence that can reach up to 2 meters in height. Its flowers are typically bright yellow or orange, with a fragrant scent that attracts pollinators.

Vriesea fragrans is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.
