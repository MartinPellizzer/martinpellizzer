Viola × kozo-poljanskii

Viola × kozo-poljanskii, commonly known as the hybrid violet, is a cross between Viola kozo-miya and Viola poljanskii.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant with heart-shaped, dark green leaves. The flowers are typically violet or purple, with five petals and a distinctive spur. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
