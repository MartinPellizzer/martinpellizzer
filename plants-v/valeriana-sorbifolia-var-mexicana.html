Valeriana sorbifolia var. mexicana

Valeriana sorbifolia var. mexicana, commonly known as Mexican valerian, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, small white or pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a hairy texture.
