Volkameria lamiifolia

Volkameria lamiifolia, commonly known as the laceleaf or silverleaf volkameria, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically blooming in spring. Volkameria lamiifolia is often grown as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and subtle fragrance.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for shady garden areas or indoor cultivation.
