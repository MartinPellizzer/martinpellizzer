Vanhouttea gardneri

Vanhouttea gardneri, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and clusters of showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil.
