Veronica leiocarpa var. stenobotrys

Veronica leiocarpa var. stenobotrys, commonly known as the narrow-leaved speedwell, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, blue to purple flowers. The leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in a fine, fuzzy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
