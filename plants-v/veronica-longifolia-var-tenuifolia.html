Veronica longifolia var. tenuifolia

Veronica longifolia var. tenuifolia, commonly known as slender leaf speedwell, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, linear leaves that are typically less than 1 centimeter in width. The plant produces small, blue or purple flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom from spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

This variety is often used in rock gardens and as a ground cover due to its compact growth habit.
