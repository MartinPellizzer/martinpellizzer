Valeriana aretiastra

Valeriana aretiastra, commonly known as the African valerian, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, white or pinkish flowers in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and woodland areas.

Valeriana aretiastra is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly for its calming effects.
