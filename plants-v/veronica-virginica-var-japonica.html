Veronica virginica var. japonica

Veronica virginica var. japonica, commonly known as Japanese speedwell, is a flowering plant native to Japan and parts of Korea.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit. The plant produces small, blue to violet flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.
