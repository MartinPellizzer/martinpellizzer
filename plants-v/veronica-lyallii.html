Veronica lyallii

Veronica lyallii, commonly known as Lyall's speedwell, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its small, blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.
