Vaccinium minimiflorum

Vaccinium minimiflorum, commonly known as the dwarf blueberry.

It is a low-growing shrub native to the coastal regions of southern California and Baja California. The plant produces small, edible berries that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Vaccinium minimiflorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local wildlife.
