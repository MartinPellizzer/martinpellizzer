Verbena diceras

Verbena diceras, commonly known as the blue verbena, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous annual that produces vibrant blue or purple flowers, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has slender, branching stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Verbena diceras is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the summer months.
