Vittaria formosana

Vittaria formosana, commonly known as the Formosa fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Vittariaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or branches in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are bi-lobed and have a distinctive, intricate pattern of veins.

Vittaria formosana is valued for its ornamental appearance and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
