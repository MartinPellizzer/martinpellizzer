Valeriana ajanensis subsp. fasciculata

Valeriana ajanensis subsp.

fasciculata, commonly known as the African valerian, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa. It features a rhizomatous root system and erect, branched stems that can grow up to 1 meter in height.

The plant produces small, white to pinkish flowers arranged in dense, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a strong, aromatic scent.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.
