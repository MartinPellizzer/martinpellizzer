Viola rogoviczii

Viola rogoviczii, commonly known as the Rogovici violet, is a species of flowering plant in the Violaceae family.

It is native to the Balkan region, particularly found in parts of Serbia and Croatia. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded woodland areas, thriving in cool and humid conditions. It produces small, delicate flowers in shades of purple and violet, often with yellow markings.

Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in a fine layer of downy hair, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
