Vernonia reinwardtiana

Vernonia reinwardtiana, commonly known as the red vernonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its conical flower heads. The plant typically grows as an erect shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters, with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its striking red or orange flower heads are composed of numerous small florets, making it a visually appealing ornamental plant.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for cultivation in warm climates.
