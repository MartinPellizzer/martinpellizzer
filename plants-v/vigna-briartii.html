Vigna briartii

Vigna briartii, commonly known as the African snail bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical Africa.

It features trifoliate leaves with small, hairy stipules and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant bears elongated, cylindrical pods that contain several elliptical seeds. It is adapted to grow in dry, sandy soils and is often found in savanna habitats.

Vigna briartii is valued for its edible seeds and is used in traditional cuisines across its native range.
