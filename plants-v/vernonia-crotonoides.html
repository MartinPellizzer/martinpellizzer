Vernonia crotonoides

Vernonia crotonoides, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a shrub or small tree native to Africa.

It is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of yellow to orange flowers. The plant is known for its hard, dense wood, which is highly valued for its strength and durability. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry savannahs and rocky areas.

Vernonia crotonoides also has traditional uses in medicine and as a source of timber.
