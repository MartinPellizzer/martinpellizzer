Vachellia swazica

Vachellia swazica, commonly known as Swazi thornveld acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to dry and semi-arid environments, making it resilient to drought conditions. It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.

Vachellia swazica is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
