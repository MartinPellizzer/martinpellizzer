Viola thibaudieri

Viola thibaudieri, commonly known as the Thibaudier violet, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often covered in a fine pubescence. The plant produces delicate, showy flowers in shades of violet, purple, and white, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial to full shade.

Viola thibaudieri is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to naturalize in suitable climates.
