Vitiphoenix minuta

Vitiphoenix minuta, commonly known as the dwarf date palm, is a small, ornamental palm native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender trunk and clusters of small, fan-shaped leaves. The plant produces tiny, yellowish flowers that develop into small, edible dates. It thrives in warm, arid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its compact size, it is popular in landscaping and as a container plant.
