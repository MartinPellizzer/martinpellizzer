Vernonia bipontini var. gonderensis

Vernonia bipontini var. gonderensis, commonly known as the Gooner's Vernonia, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in high-altitude grasslands and rocky areas, showing a preference for well-drained soils. Its leaves are lanceolate and covered with fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

This species is noted for its ornamental value and potential medicinal uses in traditional medicine.
