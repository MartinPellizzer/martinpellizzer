Verbascum × pseudopterocalycinum

Verbascum × pseudopterocalycinum, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial plant belonging to the Scrophulariaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, fuzzy, gray-green leaves that resemble those of the common mullein. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers in spikes, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

This hybrid is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
