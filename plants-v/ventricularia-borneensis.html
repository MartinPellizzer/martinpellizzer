Ventricularia borneensis

Ventricularia borneensis, commonly known as the Borneo bladderwort, is a unique aquatic plant native to the freshwater habitats of Borneo.

The plant is a member of the Lentibulariaceae family, known for its carnivorous tendencies. Its roots are often submerged, and it thrives in slow-moving or still water.
