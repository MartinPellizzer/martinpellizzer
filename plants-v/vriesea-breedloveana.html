Vriesea breedloveana

Vriesea breedloveana, commonly known as the Schomburgk's Vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to the rainforests of Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, spathaceous inflorescence that displays a range of colors, from yellow to orange and red. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that collect water, forming a central pool that supports small aquatic life. Its flowers are typically short-lived but highly ornamental, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Vriesea breedloveana thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil for optimal growth.
