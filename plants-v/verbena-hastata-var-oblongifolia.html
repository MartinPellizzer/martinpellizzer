Verbena hastata var. oblongifolia

Verbena hastata var. oblongifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf blue mistflower, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of small, sky-blue to violet flowers. The leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for native gardens.

This plant is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
