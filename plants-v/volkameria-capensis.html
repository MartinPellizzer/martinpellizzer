Volkameria capensis

Volkameria capensis, commonly known as the Cape volkameria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. It is adapted to grow in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun.

The species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in arid conditions.
