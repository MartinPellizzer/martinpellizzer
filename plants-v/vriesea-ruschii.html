Vriesea ruschii

Vriesea ruschii, commonly known as the Crimson Vriesea, is a species of bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, brightly colored inflorescence that features vivid red and orange hues. The plant has a rosette-shaped leaf structure that collects water, which is essential for its survival in its natural habitat. Its leaves are typically broad and waxy, with a smooth texture and a bluish-green color.

Vriesea ruschii is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value and ability to thrive in indoor environments.
