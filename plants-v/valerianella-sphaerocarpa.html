Valerianella sphaerocarpa

Valerianella sphaerocarpa, commonly known as globe valerian, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
