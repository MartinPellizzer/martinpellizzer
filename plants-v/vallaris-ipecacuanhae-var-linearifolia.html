Vallaris ipecacuanhae var. linearifolia

Vallaris ipecacuanhae var. linearifolia, commonly known as the slender vallaris, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features slender, linear leaves that give it its specific epithet "linearifolia.". It produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink.

This variety is often found in moist, shaded environments within its native habitat.
