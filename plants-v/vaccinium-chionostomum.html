Vaccinium chionostomum

Vaccinium chionostomum, commonly known as snowberry blueberry, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale blue, which bloom in late spring. The plant's berries are dark blue to black and are often covered in a whitish, powdery coating. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is adapted to nutrient-poor soils.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
