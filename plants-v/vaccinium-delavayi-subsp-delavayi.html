Vaccinium delavayi subsp. delavayi

Vaccinium delavayi subsp.

delavayi, commonly known as Delavay's blueberry, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of southwest China. It produces small, edible berries that are typically blue or purple in color, similar to other species in the Vaccinium genus.

The plant thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in temperate forest environments. It is valued for its ornamental qualities as well as its potential use in local food systems.
