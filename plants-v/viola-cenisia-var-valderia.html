Viola cenisia var. valderia

Viola cenisia var. valderia, commonly known as the Valderia violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of violet and white, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers well-drained soil.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for rock gardens and shaded borders.
