Vitex befotakensis

Vitex befotakensis, commonly known as the Java vitex, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub or tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and clusters of fragrant, blue or purple flowers. It produces small, dark purple berries that contain the seeds.

Vitex befotakensis is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
