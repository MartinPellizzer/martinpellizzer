Veronica cetikiana

Veronica cetikiana, commonly known as the Balkan speedwell, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features small, blue or purple flowers arranged in clusters, and its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs. It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
