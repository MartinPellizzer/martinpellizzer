Verbascum benthamianum

Verbascum benthamianum, commonly known as the Bentham's mullein, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the genus Verbascum, which includes several species of flowering plants known for their fuzzy, velvety leaves. The plant produces showy yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Verbascum benthamianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
