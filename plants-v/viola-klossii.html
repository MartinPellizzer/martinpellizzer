Viola klossii

Viola klossii, commonly known as the Kloss's violet, is a species of flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Philippines.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and thrives in cool climates. Viola klossii is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.

Due to its limited habitat, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
