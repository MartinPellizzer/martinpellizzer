Vincentia schweinfurthii

Vincentia schweinfurthii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Violaceae and is characterized by its velvety, heart-shaped leaves and showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant is typically grown as a houseplant due to its preference for warm, humid environments. It produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom throughout the year under proper care.

The leaves are often used in traditional medicine in some African cultures.
