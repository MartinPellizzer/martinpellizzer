Verbascum × muehlenbeckii

Verbascum × muehlenbeckii, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the genus Verbascum, which includes several species of flowering plants known for their large, showy flowers. The hybrid mullein is characterized by its tall, hairy stems and clusters of yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

This plant is also valued for its medicinal properties, with historical uses in traditional herbal remedies.
