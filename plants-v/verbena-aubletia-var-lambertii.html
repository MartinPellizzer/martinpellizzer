Verbena aubletia var. lambertii

Verbena aubletia var. lambertii, commonly known as Lambert's verbena, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and produces spikes of vibrant blue to purple flowers. The leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

This variety is prized for its drought tolerance and ornamental value in gardens.
