Vangueria floribunda

Vangueria floribunda, commonly known as the African star-of-Bethlehem, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It features erect, branching stems covered in soft, gray-green foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Vangueria floribunda is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
