Virea autumnalis var. minor

Virea autumnalis var. minor, commonly known as the lesser autumn virea, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Plantaginaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. It blooms in the late summer to early autumn, adding delicate beauty to its natural environment.
