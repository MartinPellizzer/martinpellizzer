Viburnum hasseltii

Viburnum hasseltii, commonly known as the African snowberry, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Africa.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and have a smooth texture. The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers in the spring, followed by bright red, berry-like fruits in the fall. Viburnum hasseltii thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

It is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and seasonal interest.
