Viburnum luzonicum f. subglabrum

Viburnum luzonicum f. subglabrum, commonly known as the smooth-leaved viburnum, is a compact shrub native to the Philippines.

It features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in the spring. The leaves are elliptical, glossy, and have a smooth texture, distinguishing it from other viburnum species. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
