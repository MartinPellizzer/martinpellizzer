Volkameria spinosa

Volkameria spinosa, commonly known as the spiny volkameria, is a shrub native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and dark green, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are typically clustered in terminal inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Volkameria spinosa is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
