Viola pallens

Viola pallens, commonly known as the white violet, is a species of flowering plant in the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and delicate, white or pale purple flowers. Native to parts of Europe and Asia, it thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
