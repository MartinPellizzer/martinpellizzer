Vriesea paraibica var. interrogatoria

Vriesea paraibica var. interrogatoria, commonly known as the Paraiba Vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, recurved inflorescence that displays a mix of red, orange, and yellow hues. The plant features a rosette of narrow, dark green leaves that form a central cup to collect rainwater. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity to maintain its lush appearance.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.
