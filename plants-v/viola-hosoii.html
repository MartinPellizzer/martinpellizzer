Viola hosoii

Viola hosoii, commonly known as the Hosoii violet, is a species of flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, heart-shaped leaves and small, showy flowers. The plant typically blooms in spring, producing violet-blue or white petals that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Viola hosoii thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or shaded areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and botanical collections.
