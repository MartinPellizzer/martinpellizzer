Veronica continua

Veronica continua, commonly known as the continuous speedwell, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, blue or violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, spreading stems and dark green, oval-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Veronica continua is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
