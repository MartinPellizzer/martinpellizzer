Vargasiella venezuelana

Vargasiella venezuelana, commonly known as the Venezuelan starfish plant, is a unique succulent native to the Andes region of Venezuela.

It features a distinctive rosette-shaped growth pattern with thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting to its arid environment. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and white, which bloom during the warmer months. Its foliage is often covered in a fine, fuzzy coating, providing additional protection against intense sunlight.

Vargasiella venezuelana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
