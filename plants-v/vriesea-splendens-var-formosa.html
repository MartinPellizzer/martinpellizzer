Vriesea splendens var. formosa

Vriesea splendens var. formosa, commonly known as the Flame Violet, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It features vibrant, sword-shaped leaves that form a rosette, with a central cup that collects water. The plant produces showy, colorful inflorescences that resemble flames, typically in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil with high humidity.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
