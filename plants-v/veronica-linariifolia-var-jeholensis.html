Veronica linariifolia var. jeholensis

Veronica linariifolia var. jeholensis, commonly known as the Jehol Veronica, is a flowering plant belonging to the Plantaginaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of blue to violet flowers. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. Native to regions of northern China, this species thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
