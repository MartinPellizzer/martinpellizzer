Veronica setigera

Veronica setigera, commonly known as the slender speedwell, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. The plant features upright stems that are often branched and covered in fine hairs. Its small, blue or violet flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom from late spring to early summer.

Veronica setigera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in gardens for its delicate appearance.
