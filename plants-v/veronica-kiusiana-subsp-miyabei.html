Veronica kiusiana subsp. miyabei

Veronica kiusiana subsp.

miyabei, commonly known as the Japanese speedwell, is a flowering plant native to Japan. It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its small, blue to violet flowers that bloom in early spring.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged oppositely along the stems.
