Vernonia diversifolia

Vernonia diversifolia, commonly known as the diverseleaf ironweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and produces vibrant yellow to orange flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or swampy areas.

Its botanical features make it an attractive addition to gardens and a valuable species for ecological studies.
