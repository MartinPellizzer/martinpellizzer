Viola odorata var. scotophylla

Viola odorata var. scotophylla, commonly known as the sweet violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves and produces fragrant, violet-colored flowers with a distinctive scent. The plant thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas. It belongs to the Violaceae family and is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical use in perfumery.

Its flowers bloom in early spring, adding a delicate touch to gardens and natural landscapes.
