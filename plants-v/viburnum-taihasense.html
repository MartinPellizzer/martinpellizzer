Viburnum taihasense

Viburnum taihasense, commonly known as the Taiwan viburnum, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, simple, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers in the spring, followed by red berries that are edible for birds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Viburnum taihasense is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in garden settings.
