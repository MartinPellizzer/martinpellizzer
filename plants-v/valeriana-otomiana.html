Valeriana otomiana

Valeriana otomiana, commonly known as the Otomian valerian, is a flowering plant native to Central America and parts of the Caribbean.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. Valeriana otomiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its potential calming properties.
