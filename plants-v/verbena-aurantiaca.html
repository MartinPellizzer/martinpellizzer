Verbena aurantiaca

Verbena aurantiaca, commonly known as scarlet verbena, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces vibrant clusters of red, orange, or yellow flowers. The plant has slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its showy blooms attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
