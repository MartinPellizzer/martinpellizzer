Verbascum × dobrogense

Verbascum × dobrogense, commonly known as the Dobrogian mullein.

It is a hybrid plant belonging to the genus Verbascum, which includes species native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa. This particular species is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, velvety leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in a spike-like inflorescence, and bloom in late summer.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for dry, rocky habitats.
