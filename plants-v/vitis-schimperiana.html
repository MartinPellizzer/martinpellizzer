Vitis schimperiana

Vitis schimperiana, commonly known as the Schimper's grape, is a species of flowering vine native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Vitaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and tendrils. The plant produces small, clustered greenish-white flowers followed by small, round berries. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped with a rough texture and can vary in size.

Vitis schimperiana is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
