Viola rockii

Viola rockii, commonly known as the Chinese violet, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and delicate purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or woodland habitats, preferring moist, well-drained soil. Viola rockii is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and blooms.

It is also valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
