Viola multicaulis var. scotophylla

Viola multicaulis var. scotophylla, commonly known as the shadowleaf violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture, often arranged in a basal rosette. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically violet or purple in color, blooming in early spring. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and role in its native ecosystem.
