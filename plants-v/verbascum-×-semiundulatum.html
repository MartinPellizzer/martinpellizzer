Verbascum × semiundulatum

Verbascum × semiundulatum, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial plant belonging to the Scrophulariaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, hairy stems and large, fuzzy, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy yellow or orange flowers that bloom in late summer. It is native to regions of southern Europe and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Verbascum × semiundulatum prefers full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for dry garden environments.
