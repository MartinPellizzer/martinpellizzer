Veronica polymorpha

Veronica polymorpha, commonly known as the variable speedwell, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of blue or purple flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

Its flowers bloom from late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
