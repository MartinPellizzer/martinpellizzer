Viola × weinhartii

Viola × weinhartii, commonly known as the hybrid violet, is a species resulting from the cross between Viola tricolor and Viola odorata.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by heart-shaped leaves and showy, often bi-colored flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 10-20 centimeters and thrives in partial shade with well-drained soil. Its flowers bloom in early spring and are popular in gardens for their ornamental value.

Viola × weinhartii is also valued for its ability to naturalize and spread through self-seeding.
