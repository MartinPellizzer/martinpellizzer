Verbesina tatei

Verbesina tatei, commonly known as the Mexican ironweed, is a perennial herb native to Mexico and Central America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features upright stems covered in fine hairs and produces bright yellow flowers in clusters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly rough texture.

Verbesina tatei is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.
