Vittaria lineata var. curvidentata

Vittaria lineata var. curvidentata, commonly known as the zigzag fern, is a fern species native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, linear fronds that exhibit a distinctive zigzag pattern along their length. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are usually thin and have a soft, feathery appearance, contributing to its ornamental value.

The species is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
