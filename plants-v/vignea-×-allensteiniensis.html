Vignea × allensteiniensis

Vignea × allensteiniensis, commonly known as the Allensteiniensis morning glory, is a hybrid species belonging to the Convolvulaceae family.

It is characterized by its trumpet-shaped, showy flowers that typically range in color from pale blue to violet. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas and thrives in warm, humid climates. Its climbing habit and rapid growth make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Vignea × allensteiniensis is also valued for its ecological role in supporting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
