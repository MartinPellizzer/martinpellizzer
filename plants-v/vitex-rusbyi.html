Vitex rusbyi

Vitex rusbyi, commonly known as the Rusby's vitex, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub or tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually blue or purple in color, which bloom during the rainy season. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a rough texture, often with a strong aromatic scent.

Vitex rusbyi is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
