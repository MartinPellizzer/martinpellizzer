Vochysia excelsa

Vochysia excelsa, commonly known as the royal trumpet tree, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that resemble trumpets, which bloom in clusters. The plant has a straight trunk with a smooth, light gray bark and can grow up to 30 meters tall. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptical with a waxy texture.

The tree is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also valued for its timber.
