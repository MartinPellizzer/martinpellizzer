Veronica panormitana subsp. panormitana

Veronica panormitana subsp.

panormitana, commonly known as the Cyprus speedwell, is a perennial herb native to the island of Cyprus. It features basal rosettes of narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, blue to purple flowers in early summer.

The plant is adapted to rocky and coastal environments, thriving in well-drained soils. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in coastal cliffs and sandy soils.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
