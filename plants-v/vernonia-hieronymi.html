Vernonia hieronymi

Vernonia hieronymi, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub or tree that can grow up to 6 meters tall. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves and produces yellow to orange flowers in conical clusters. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it drought-tolerant.

The wood of Vernonia hieronymi is hard and strong, which has made it valuable for traditional uses.
