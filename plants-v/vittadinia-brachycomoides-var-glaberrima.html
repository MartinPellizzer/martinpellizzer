Vittadinia brachycomoides var. glaberrima

Vittadinia brachycomoides var. glaberrima, commonly known as the smooth vittadinia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a perennial herb with a robust, upright stem and lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers arranged in a head, which bloom during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and sandy soils.

The species is noted for its adaptability and is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value.
