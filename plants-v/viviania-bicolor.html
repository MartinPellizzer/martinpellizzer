Viviania bicolor

Viviania bicolor, commonly known as the two-colored ivy, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking bi-colored leaves, which typically display a mix of green and white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale pink, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Viviania bicolor is valued for its aesthetic qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
