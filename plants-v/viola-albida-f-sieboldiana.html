Viola albida f. sieboldiana

Viola albida f. sieboldiana, commonly known as the Siebold's white violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces showy white or pale yellow flowers with five petals, often with a distinctive violet or purple spot at the base. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in woodland or shaded areas.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and is cultivated in gardens for its delicate beauty.
