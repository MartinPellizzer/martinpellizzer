Viola chelmea subsp. vratnikensis

Viola chelmea subsp.

vratnikensis, commonly known as the Vratnik violet, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Balkan Peninsula. It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy violet flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its flowers bloom in early spring, adding a splash of color to the early season flora.

The subspecies is distinguished by its unique floral structure and geographic distribution within its native range.
