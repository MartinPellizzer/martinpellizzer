Viola violacea var. makinoi

Viola violacea var. makinoi, commonly known as the Japanese violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces violet-blue flowers with yellow markings, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
