Valeriana leptothyrsos

Valeriana leptothyrsos, commonly known as the slender valerian, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features slender, upright stems with small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are tiny, white or pale pink, and are arranged in loose, terminal spikes.

Valeriana leptothyrsos is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its calming effects.
