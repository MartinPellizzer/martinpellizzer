Vateria ceylanica

Vateria ceylanica, commonly known as the Ceylon vateria, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is native to the rainforests of Sri Lanka and other parts of Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and produces showy, pinkish-white flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland and hill forest regions.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
