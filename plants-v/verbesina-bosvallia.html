Verbesina bosvallia

Verbesina bosvallia, commonly known as the yellow ironweed, is a perennial herb native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that can grow up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly rough texture.

Verbesina bosvallia is often found in grasslands and disturbed soils, and it is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
