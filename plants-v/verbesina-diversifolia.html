Verbesina diversifolia

Verbesina diversifolia, commonly known as the yellow crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its bright yellow ray florets and dark brown disk flowers, which make it visually striking. The plant produces small, oval-shaped seeds that are dispersed by wind, contributing to its spread. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in open woodlands and disturbed areas.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
