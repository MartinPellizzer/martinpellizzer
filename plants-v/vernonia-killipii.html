Vernonia killipii

Vernonia killipii, commonly known as Killip's ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Vernonia killipii is valued for its ecological role in supporting pollinators and its ornamental appeal in wetland gardens.
