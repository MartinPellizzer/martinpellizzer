Vilfa dispar

Vilfa dispar, commonly known as the narrowleaf filaree, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the western United States.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, often in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a bluish-green hue and a fuzzy texture.

Vilfa dispar is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments.
