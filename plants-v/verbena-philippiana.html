Verbena philippiana

Verbena philippiana, commonly known as the Philippine verbena, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its slender, branching stems and clusters of small, tubular flowers. The plant typically blooms with vibrant shades of purple, blue, or pink, depending on the variety. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Verbena philippiana is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
