Veronica rubrifolia subsp. respectatissima

Veronica rubrifolia subsp.

respectatissima, commonly known as the purple speedwell, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa. It features slender, upright stems and small, clustered flowers that range from purple to pink in color.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture, while its flowers bloom from late spring to early autumn.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms and easy care requirements.
