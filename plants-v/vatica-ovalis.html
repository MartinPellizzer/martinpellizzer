Vatica ovalis

Vatica ovalis, commonly known as the oval vatica, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Dipterocarpaceae family and is characterized by its elliptical, glossy leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its wood is valued for its durability and is used in construction and furniture-making.

Vatica ovalis thrives in humid, lowland forest environments.
