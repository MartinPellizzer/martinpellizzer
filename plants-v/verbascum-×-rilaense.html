Verbascum × rilaense

Verbascum × rilaense, commonly known as the Rila mullein.

It is a hybrid plant belonging to the genus Verbascum, which includes species native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa. This particular species is notable for its tall, erect stems and large, velvety leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces showy, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy choice for gardens in temperate climates.
