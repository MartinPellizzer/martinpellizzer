Vincetoxicum officinale var. dumeticola

Vincetoxicum officinale var. dumeticola, commonly known as the creeping false bindweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in umbels, and it is often found in disturbed soils along roadsides and in waste areas.
