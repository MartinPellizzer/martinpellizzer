Vachellia nebrownii

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing drought-resistant features. Its bark is often fissured, and it produces yellow flowers that attract pollinators.
