Vriesea camptoclada

Vriesea camptoclada, commonly known as the zigzag bromeliad, is a striking plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, spathaceous inflorescence that typically displays shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often adorned with a waxy coating. Its flowering stems can reach up to 1.5 meters in height, making it a dramatic addition to any collection.

Vriesea camptoclada thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity to flourish.
