Veronica spuria var. foliosa

Veronica spuria var. foliosa, commonly known as the leafy speedwell, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant features small, blue or purple flowers that bloom in clusters, typically from late spring to early autumn. Its leaves are usually oval or lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.
