Vriesea olmosana var. olmosana

Vriesea olmosana var. olmosana, commonly known as the Olmosana Vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, spathaceous inflorescence that displays a range of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that collect water, creating a small pool that supports various microorganisms. Its flowering period typically occurs during the warmer months, and it thrives in bright, indirect light with high humidity.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.
