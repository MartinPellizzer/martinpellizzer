Vangueria squamata

Vangueria squamata, commonly known as the squamata vangueria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in arid or semi-arid regions. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and covered in a fine, powdery coating, giving them a distinctive appearance.

Vangueria squamata is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
