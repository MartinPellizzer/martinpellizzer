Vanda cumingii

Vanda cumingii, commonly known as the Cuming's vanda, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, strap-like leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. The plant thrives in humid, tropical climates and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant blooms and unique appearance.

Vanda cumingii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
