Verbascum microcymosum

Verbascum microcymosum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved mullein, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, fuzzy, gray-green leaves. Verbascum microcymosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
