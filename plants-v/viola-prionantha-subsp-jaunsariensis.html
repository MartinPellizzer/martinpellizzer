Viola prionantha subsp. jaunsariensis

Viola prionantha subsp.

jaunsariensis, commonly known as the Jaunsari violet, is a rare and endemic species found in the Himalayan regions of India. It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its delicate, heart-shaped leaves and small, violet-colored flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and rocky slopes. Its flowers bloom in early spring, adding a splash of color to the alpine flora.

The species is notable for its ornamental value and ecological significance in its native habitat.
