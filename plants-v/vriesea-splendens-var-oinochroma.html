Vriesea splendens var. oinochroma

Vriesea splendens var. oinochroma, commonly known as the golden vriesea, is a striking tropical plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting inflorescence that features a central yellow spathe with red and green stripes. The plant belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its foliage is typically dark green with a waxy texture, complementing its showy flowers.
