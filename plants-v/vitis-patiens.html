Vitis patiens

Vitis patiens, commonly known as the Chinese grapevine, is a woody vine native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically dark purple to black in color. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forested areas.

Vitis patiens is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential in horticulture and traditional medicine.
