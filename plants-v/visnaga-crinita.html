Visnaga crinita

Visnaga crinita, commonly known as the yellow horned poppy, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers and distinctive, hairy, horn-like seed pods. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open landscapes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its unique appearance.

Visnaga crinita is known for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
