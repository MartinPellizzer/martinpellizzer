Veronica parmularia

Veronica parmularia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved speedwell, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of about 10 to 20 centimeters. The plant features small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and often have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and along stream banks.
