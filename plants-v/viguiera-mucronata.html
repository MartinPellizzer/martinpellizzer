Viguiera mucronata

Viguiera mucronata, commonly known as the Mexican heath or yellow coneflower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall.
