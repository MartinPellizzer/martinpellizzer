Vernonia isalensis var. isalensis

Vernonia isalensis var. isalensis, commonly known as the Isalensis Vernonia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its conical inflorescence. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its yellow flowers bloom in dense clusters.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savannah and woodland habitats.
