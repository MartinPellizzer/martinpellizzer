Vignea × germanica

Vignea × germanica, commonly known as the German vine, is a hybrid plant that belongs to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its climbing habit, with slender stems that twine around supports. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

Vignea × germanica is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and flowering display.
