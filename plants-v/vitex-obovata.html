Vitex obovata

Vitex obovata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf chaste tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It features opposite, ovate to obovate leaves that are often hairy on the underside. The plant produces showy, fragrant blue or purple flowers in panicles during the summer months. Its fruits are small, woody drupes that contain a single seed.

Vitex obovata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and hardiness.
