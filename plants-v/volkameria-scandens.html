Volkameria scandens

Volkameria scandens, commonly known as the climbing champa, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is a woody vine that produces small, fragrant yellow flowers, which are often used in traditional perfumery. The plant has palmately compound leaves and is characterized by its rapid growth habit. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in forest understories.

The botanical features of Volkameria scandens make it both ornamental and economically valuable.
