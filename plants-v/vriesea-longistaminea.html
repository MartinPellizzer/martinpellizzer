Vriesea longistaminea

Vriesea longistaminea, commonly known as the long-stamen vriesea, is a species of flowering plant in the Bromeliaceae family.

Vriesea longistaminea is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
