Verbascum besnardii

Verbascum besnardii, commonly known as the Besnard's mullein, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, fuzzy leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy species suitable for dry, rocky environments.

Verbascum besnardii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
