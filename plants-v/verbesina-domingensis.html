Verbesina domingensis

Verbesina domingensis, commonly known as the yellow crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and Central America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant features showy yellow flowers arranged in dense, spherical clusters at the ends of its stems. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a rough texture.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
