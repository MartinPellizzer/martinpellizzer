Verbena scaberrima

Verbena scaberrima, commonly known as rough verbena, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of South America.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in coastal and disturbed areas. The plant features slender, woody stems and small, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its showy, clustered flowers are usually violet or blue, blooming throughout the growing season.

Verbena scaberrima is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in sandy or coastal soils.
