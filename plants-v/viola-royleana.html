Viola royleana

Viola royleana, commonly known as the Kashmir violet, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan region.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous areas. Its flowers bloom in spring and are known for their delicate fragrance.

Viola royleana is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
