Veronica cusickii var. allenii

Veronica cusickii var. allenii, commonly known as Allen's speedwell, is a perennial flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing habit and clusters of blue or purple flowers.
