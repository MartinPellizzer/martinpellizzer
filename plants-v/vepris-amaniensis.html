Vepris amaniensis

Vepris amaniensis, commonly known as the Amani tamarind.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to the coastal regions of East Africa. The plant is characterized by its elliptical, dark green leaves and fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a small, round pod containing several seeds, and it is often used in traditional medicine.

The tree is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in tropical climates.
