Vellozia patens

Vellozia patens, commonly known as the featherleaf vellozia, is a unique member of the Velloziaceae family native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, feather-like leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptation to dry climates. Its flowers are small and clustered, often displaying shades of yellow or green.

Vellozia patens is notable for its ecological significance in its native habitat, providing food and shelter for various desert organisms.
