Verbascum lasianthum

Verbascum lasianthum, commonly known as the woolly mullein, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, hairy stems and large, fuzzy leaves that help reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces showy yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy perennial in suitable climates.

Verbascum lasianthum is often used in herbal medicine for its soothing properties.
