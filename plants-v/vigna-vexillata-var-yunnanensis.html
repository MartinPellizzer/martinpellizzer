Vigna vexillata var. yunnanensis

Vigna vexillata var. yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan cowpea, is a climbing legume native to China.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pale yellow flowers. The plant produces elongated, cylindrical pods that contain several elliptical seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its edible seeds.

This variety is valued for its adaptability and nutritional content.
