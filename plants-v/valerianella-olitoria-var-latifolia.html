Valerianella olitoria var. latifolia

Valerianella olitoria var. latifolia, commonly known as broadleaf corn salad, is a leafy green plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features broad, spoon-shaped leaves that are tender and slightly peppery in flavor. The plant grows in cool weather and is often cultivated as a leafy vegetable in temperate climates. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires consistent moisture for optimal growth.

Its botanical characteristics make it a versatile and nutritious addition to salads and other culinary dishes.
