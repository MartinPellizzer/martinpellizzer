Verbena neei

Verbena neei, commonly known as the narrowleaf verbena, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a member of the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Verbena neei is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
