Villamilla peruviana

Villamilla peruviana, commonly known as the Peruvian violet, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its striking violet or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, preferring cool and moist conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in a fine layer of hairs.

Villamilla peruviana is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its traditional use in local medicine.
