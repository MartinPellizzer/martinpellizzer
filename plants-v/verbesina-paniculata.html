Verbesina paniculata

Verbesina paniculata, commonly known as yellow ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a tall, erect perennial that can grow up to 6 feet in height, featuring lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. The plant produces large, showy yellow flower heads that bloom in late summer to early fall. Its flowers are composed of numerous small, yellow ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers.

Verbesina paniculata is known for its hardiness and ability to thrive in full sun, making it a valuable addition to native plant gardens.
