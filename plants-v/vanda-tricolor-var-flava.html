Vanda tricolor var. flava

Vanda tricolor var. flava, commonly known as the yellow vanda, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features showy, three-colored flowers with a yellow base, red midsection, and white tips, making it highly ornamental. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light, often growing on trees or rocks. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air, and it requires well-draining potting media.

Vanda tricolor var. flava is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
