Valeriana hardwickeana

Valeriana hardwickeana, commonly known as Hardwicke's valerian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features upright, branched stems that are often hairy, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a rough texture. Its flowers are small, pinkish-white, and clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences.

Valeriana hardwickeana is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine for its calming effects.
