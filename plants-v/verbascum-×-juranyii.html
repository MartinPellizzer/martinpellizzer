Verbascum × juranyii

Verbascum × juranyii, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the genus Verbascum, which includes species known for their showy, fuzzy flowers. The hybrid features tall, erect stems covered in soft, velvety leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in dense, clustered inflorescences.

This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
