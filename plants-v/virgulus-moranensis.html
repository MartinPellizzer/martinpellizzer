Virgulus moranensis

Virgulus moranensis, commonly known as the Moran's virgulus, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, daisy-like flowers with yellow disc florets and white ray florets. Native to specific regions of South America, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight. Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
