Vitis kiusiana

Vitis kiusiana, commonly known as the Kiusiana grapevine, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a vigorous climbing vine that produces small, dark berries. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and tendrils that aid in its climbing growth habit. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with high humidity.

The berries are often used in local cuisines and traditional medicine.
