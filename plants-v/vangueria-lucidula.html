Vangueria lucidula

Vangueria lucidula, commonly known as the glossy vangueria, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green, often with a waxy coating. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers, which are typically borne in the axils of the leaves. Vangueria lucidula is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it drought-tolerant and suitable for xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and attractive foliage.
