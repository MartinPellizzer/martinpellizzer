Vanda teres

Vanda teres, commonly known as the slender vanda, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and long, narrow leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white pubescence. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange, typically blooming during the summer months. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Vanda teres is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its striking appearance and relative ease of cultivation.
