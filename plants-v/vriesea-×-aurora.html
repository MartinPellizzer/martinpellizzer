Vriesea × aurora

Vriesea × aurora, commonly known as the Aurora Bromeliad, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Bromeliaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful inflorescence that can range from red to orange, often resembling a flame. The plant features a rosette of narrow, strap-like leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating. Native to South America, it thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Vriesea × aurora is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
