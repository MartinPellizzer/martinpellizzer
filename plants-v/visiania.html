Visiania

Visiania, commonly known as the yellow bell vine, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas, particularly Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its trailing habit and clusters of yellow, bell-shaped flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and slightly hairy, while its fruits are berries that turn from green to red when mature.

Visiania is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can thrive in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.
