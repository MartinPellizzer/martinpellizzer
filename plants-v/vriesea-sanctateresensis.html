Vriesea sanctateresensis

Vriesea sanctateresensis, commonly known as the Santa Teresa Vriesea, is a species of bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, long-lasting inflorescence that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that collect water, providing a habitat for small aquatic organisms. Its flowers are typically bright red or orange, with yellow petals, adding to its ornamental value.

Vriesea sanctateresensis prefers bright, indirect light and moderate humidity, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
