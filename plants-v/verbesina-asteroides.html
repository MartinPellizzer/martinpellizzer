Verbesina asteroides

Verbesina asteroides, commonly known as the golden crownbeard, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It features showy, dandelion-like yellow flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall. The plant has rough, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in open woods and disturbed areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
