Viola palmata var. variegata

Viola palmata var. variegata, commonly known as the variegated pansy, is a hybrid species in the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its strikingly patterned leaves with variegation of green, white, and purple. The plant produces violet-like flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in cool climates and partial shade, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms.
