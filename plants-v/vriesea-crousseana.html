Vriesea crousseana

Vriesea crousseana, commonly known as the Crousseana Vriesea, is a tropical plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its striking, long-lasting inflorescence. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that collect water, creating a small pool that supports various microorganisms. Its flowers are typically orange or red, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat.

Vriesea crousseana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
