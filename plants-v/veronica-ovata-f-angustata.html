Veronica ovata f. angustata

Veronica ovata f. angustata, commonly known as narrow-leaved speedwell, is a flowering plant belonging to the Plantaginaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, narrow leaves and clusters of small, blue or violet flowers. Native to regions of Europe and western Asia, this plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and stream banks.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
