Vitex pinnata f. ptilota

Vitex pinnata f. ptilota, commonly known as the silky flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with soft, silky hairs on the underside, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, which are followed by fluffy, seed-bearing pods. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The species is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
