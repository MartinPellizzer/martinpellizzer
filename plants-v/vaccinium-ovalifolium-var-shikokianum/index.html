Vaccinium Ovalifolium Var. Shikokianum

Vaccinium ovalifolium var. shikokianum is a lesser-known species of the Vaccinium genus, native to the mountainous regions of Japan. This variant is distinguished by its unique leaf shape and smaller, more acidic berries compared to its relatives. Despite its limited distribution, it holds ecological significance in its native habitat. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forest understories. Its rarity and distinct characteristics make it an interesting subject for botanical study and conservation efforts.
