Vitex flavescens var. parviflora

Vitex flavescens var. parviflora, commonly known as the smallflower chaste tree, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are usually hairy on the underside. The flowers are clustered in spikes and are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It produces small, woody fruits that contain several seeds.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
