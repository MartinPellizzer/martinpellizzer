Vicia nemoralis

Vicia nemoralis, commonly known as the common vetch, is a flowering plant in the legume family Fabaceae.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in open habitats such as meadows, woodland edges, and disturbed soils. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces small, yellow or greenish-yellow pea-like flowers. Its seed pods are elongated and contain several small, kidney-shaped seeds.

Vicia nemoralis is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it valuable in soil enrichment.
