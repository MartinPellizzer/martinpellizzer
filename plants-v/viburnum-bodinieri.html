Viburnum bodinieri

Viburnum bodinieri, commonly known as the Chinese snowball, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in early spring, creating a striking visual display. The plant produces red berries in the fall, which are edible for birds but toxic to humans. Viburnum bodinieri thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
