Vitis treleasei

Vitis treleasei, commonly known as the Trelease's grape, is a species of flowering plant in the Vitaceae family.

It is native to the southeastern United States, particularly found in Florida and Georgia. The plant is a climbing vine that produces small, greenish-white flowers and red to black berries. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped with a rough texture and deep veins.

Vitis treleasei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
