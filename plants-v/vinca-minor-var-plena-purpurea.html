Vinca minor var. plena-purpurea

Vinca minor var. plena-purpurea, commonly known as the purple flowering vinca, is a low-growing perennial groundcover plant native to Europe.

It features dense, mat-forming growth with heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of purple and white, which bloom throughout the spring and summer. It thrives in partial to full shade and is drought-tolerant once established.

Vinca minor var. plena-purpurea is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to suppress weeds in woodland or shaded garden settings.
