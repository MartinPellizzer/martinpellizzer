Vaccinium cereum f. skottsbergii

Vaccinium cereum f. skottsbergii, commonly known as the skottsberg's heath, is a rare and lesser-known variant of the common cowberry.

It is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to specific regions of southern Africa, particularly in South Africa. The plant features small, dark purple to black berries that are edible but not widely consumed by humans.
