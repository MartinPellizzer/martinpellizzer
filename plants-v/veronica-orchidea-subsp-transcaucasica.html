Veronica orchidea subsp. transcaucasica

Veronica orchidea subsp.

The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in alpine or subalpine environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, while the stems are upright and branched.
