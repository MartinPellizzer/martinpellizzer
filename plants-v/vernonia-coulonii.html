Vernonia coulonii

Vernonia coulonii, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant is known for its hard, heavy wood, which is highly valued for construction and tool-making. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

Vernonia coulonii plays an important ecological role, providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
