Varronia hermanniifolia

Varronia hermanniifolia, commonly known as the Hermann's varronia, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, clustered white flowers. The plant produces dark blue to black berries that are edible but have a bitter taste. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Varronia hermanniifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
