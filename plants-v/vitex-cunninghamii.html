Vitex cunninghamii

Vitex cunninghamii, commonly known as the silver dollar tree, is a species of flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub that typically grows up to 6 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are usually silver-white in color, giving it its common name. It produces clusters of small, fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring.

The fruit is a dry, capsule that splits open to release small, smooth, black seeds.
