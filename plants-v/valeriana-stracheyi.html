Valeriana stracheyi

Valeriana stracheyi, commonly known as the Cape Valerian, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its upright stems and clusters of small, white or pale blue flowers. The plant has aromatic leaves and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas. It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is valued for its medicinal properties.

Valeriana stracheyi is also used in traditional medicine for its calming effects.
