Veronica cartilaginea

Veronica cartilaginea, commonly known as the cartilage speedwell, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, mat-forming perennial that typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils. The plant features slender, upright stems with small, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are blue or purple, arranged in clusters, and bloom from spring to early summer.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging environments.
