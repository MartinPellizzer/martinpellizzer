Vaccinium lasiodiscus

Vaccinium lasiodiscus, commonly known as the broad-leaved cranberry shrub, is a species of evergreen shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes many acid-loving plants such as blueberries and heathers. This plant thrives in wet, acidic soils and is often found in swampy or boggy environments.
