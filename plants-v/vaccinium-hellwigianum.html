Vaccinium hellwigianum

Vaccinium hellwigianum, commonly known as the Hellwig blueberry, is a species of shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like blueberries and cranberries. The plant produces small, edible berries that are typically dark blue or purple in color. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and requires a cool climate with adequate sunlight.

Its botanical features make it a valuable plant for both ornamental and culinary purposes.
