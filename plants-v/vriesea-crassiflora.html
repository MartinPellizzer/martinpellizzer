Vriesea crassiflora

Vriesea crassiflora, commonly known as the broad-flowered vriesea, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting inflorescences that range in color from red to orange. The plant features a rosette of grass-like leaves that collect water, creating a small pool that supports various microorganisms. Vriesea crassiflora thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
