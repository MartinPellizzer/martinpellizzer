Votomita monantha

Votomita monantha, commonly known as the pink orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant pink petals and long, slender stems that often grow from the base of the plant. The flowers are highly fragrant, attracting various pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in warm, humid environments with high levels of indirect light.

Votomita monantha is typically grown as a houseplant due to its delicate nature and specific care requirements.
