Verbesina lindenii

Verbesina lindenii, commonly known as the yellow ironweed, is a perennial herb native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stem and clusters of bright yellow flowers. The plant produces small, achene fruits that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its spread. Its leaves are alternate, heart-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Verbesina lindenii thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a hardy addition to native gardens.
