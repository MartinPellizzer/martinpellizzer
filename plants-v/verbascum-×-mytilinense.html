Verbascum × mytilinense

Verbascum × mytilinense, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the genus Verbascum, which includes species known for their fuzzy, yellow flowers and medicinal properties. This hybrid plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are large, velvety, and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are clustered in showy, yellowish-orange inflorescences.

Verbascum × mytilinense is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its soothing properties.
