Vriesea leptopoda

Vriesea leptopoda, commonly known as the narrow-leaved vriesea, is a species of flowering plant in the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves that form a rosette structure. The plant produces striking inflorescences with bright orange flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in humid, warm environments and is often cultivated as a decorative plant.

Vriesea leptopoda is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
