Viburnum chunii var. piliferum

Viburnum chunii var. piliferum, commonly known as the Chinese snowberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, white, clustered flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental berries and winter interest, making it a popular choice in gardens.
