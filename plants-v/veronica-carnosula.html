Veronica carnosula

Veronica carnosula, commonly known as the woolly speedwell, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small blue or purple flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.

Veronica carnosula is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
