Viburnum tinus var. barcinonensis

Viburnum tinus var. barcinonensis, commonly known as the Barcelona viburnum, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features dense, dark green foliage that remains attractive throughout the year. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers in early spring, followed by bright red berries that persist into winter. It is well-suited for container gardening and can thrive in partial shade to full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping and hedging.
