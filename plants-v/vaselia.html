Vaselia

Vaselia, commonly known as the golden groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its yellow daisy-like flowers and hairy, lobed leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas and is often found in grasslands and rocky soils. Vaselia is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and thistles.

It is known for its resilience and ability to thrive in arid conditions.
