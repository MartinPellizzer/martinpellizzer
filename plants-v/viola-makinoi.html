Viola makinoi

Viola makinoi, commonly known as the Japanese violet, is a herbaceous perennial native to Japan.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and delicate, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 15-30 cm and thrives in shady, moist environments. Its flowers bloom in spring, adding a touch of color to woodland gardens.

Viola makinoi is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in horticultural settings.
