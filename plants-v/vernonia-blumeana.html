Vernonia blumeana

Vernonia blumeana, commonly known as the Blume's ironweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its conical flower heads. The plant typically grows in open woodland or grassland habitats and thrives in well-drained soils. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers are yellow to brown in color.

Vernonia blumeana is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
