Virgilia robinioides

Virgilia robinioides, commonly known as the golden wattle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, pod-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

The golden wattle is also a symbol of national pride in South Africa.
