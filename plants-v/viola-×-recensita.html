Viola × recensita

Viola × recensita, commonly known as the common violet, is a hybrid species within the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of violet or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and gardens. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Viola × recensita is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
