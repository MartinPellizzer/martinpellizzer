Vernonia schweinfurthii var. bukamaensis

Vernonia schweinfurthii var. bukamaensis, commonly known as the Bukama Vernonia, is a flowering plant native to the Democratic Republic of the Congo.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical rainforest environments. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a rough, hairy texture and produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. Its botanical characteristics make it an important species in local ecosystems and traditional medicine.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting biodiversity.
