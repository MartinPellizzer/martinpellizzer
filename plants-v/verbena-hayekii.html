Verbena hayekii

Verbena hayekii, commonly known as the Mexican verbena, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant purple or blue flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid conditions. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with small, opposite leaves.

Verbena hayekii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its colorful blooms.
