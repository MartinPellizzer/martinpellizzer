Veronica anagallis-aquatica var. tenuis

Veronica anagallis-aquatica var. tenuis, commonly known as the slender water speedwell, is a herbaceous perennial native to wetland environments in Europe.

It features slender, upright stems and small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are narrow and linear, adapted to thrive in moist or aquatic conditions. It is often found in marshes, pond margins, and along streams.

This variety is notable for its delicate appearance and ecological role in supporting aquatic and semi-aquatic wildlife.
