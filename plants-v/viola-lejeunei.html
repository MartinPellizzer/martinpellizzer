Viola lejeunei

Viola lejeunei, commonly known as the purple viola, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Viola lejeunei is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been cultivated for its beauty in gardens.
