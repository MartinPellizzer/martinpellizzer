Vietsenia scaposa

Vietsenia scaposa, commonly known as the slender scapose plant, is a flowering species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, leafless stems that bear clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth or along stream banks. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often lance-shaped, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Vietsenia scaposa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
