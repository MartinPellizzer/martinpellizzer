Vicia scepusiensis

Vicia scepusiensis, commonly known as the Scepusian vetch, is a rare flowering plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and is adapted to temperate climates. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered an endangered species.
