Vitenia stilaginea

Vitenia stilaginea, commonly known as the sticky vine, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and produces small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant has fibrous roots and is often found growing in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are ovate with a rough texture and emit a sticky substance when crushed.

Vitenia stilaginea is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its unique appearance.
