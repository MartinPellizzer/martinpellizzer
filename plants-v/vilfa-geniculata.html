Vilfa geniculata

Vilfa geniculata, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzagging stems, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often found in moist woodland areas.

Vilfa geniculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
