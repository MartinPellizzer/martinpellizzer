Vismia latifolia var. acuminata

Vismia latifolia var. acuminata, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded clusters of white flowers that resemble snowballs, blooming in late winter to early spring. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate to elliptical leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. It produces small, woody fruits that contain several seeds, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

This species is adapted to a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained, sandy soils.
