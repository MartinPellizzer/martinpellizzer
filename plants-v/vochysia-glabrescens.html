Vochysia glabrescens

Vochysia glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth vochysia, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and elongated, narrow leaves that are often clustered at the base of the stems. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically arranged in clusters, and it is known for its fragrant blooms. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release small, round seeds.

Vochysia glabrescens is often found in moist, shaded environments within tropical forests.
