Viola maccabeiana

Viola maccabeiana, commonly known as the Maccabeian violet, is a species of flowering plant in the Violaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe and western Asia. The plant produces showy, violet-colored flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in a fine layer of downy hair.

Viola maccabeiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
