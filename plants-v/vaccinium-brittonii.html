Vaccinium brittonii

Vaccinium brittonii, commonly known as the Britton huckleberry, is a species of evergreen shrub native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes other familiar plants like blueberries and cranberries. The plant produces small, acidic berries that are typically dark blue to black in color. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.
