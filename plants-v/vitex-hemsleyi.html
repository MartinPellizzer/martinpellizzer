Vitex hemsleyi

Vitex hemsleyi, commonly known as the Hemsley's chaste tree, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and showy, blue to purple flowers. The plant produces small, spherical fruits that contain seeds covered in a fleshy coating. It is often found in forested areas and thrives in well-drained soils.
