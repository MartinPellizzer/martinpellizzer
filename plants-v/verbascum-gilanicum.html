Verbascum gilanicum

Verbascum gilanicum, commonly known as Gilan mullein, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Iran.

It belongs to the genus Verbascum, which includes several species of flowering plants commonly referred to as mulleins. The plant is characterized by its tall, hairy stems and large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Verbascum gilanicum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
