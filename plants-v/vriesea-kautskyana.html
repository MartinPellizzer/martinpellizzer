Vriesea kautskyana

Vriesea kautskyana, commonly known as the Kautsky's Vriesea, is a species of flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

The plant is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central tank to collect water, a common feature among bromeliads. Its inflorescence consists of a single, showy flower that can range in color from red to orange, often with a striking yellow or green center.

Vriesea kautskyana is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its relatively low maintenance requirements.
