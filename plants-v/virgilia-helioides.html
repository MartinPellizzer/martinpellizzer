Virgilia helioides

Virgilia helioides, commonly known as the sunflower tree, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its tall, straight trunk and broad, palm-like leaves. The plant produces striking yellow flowers that bloom in clusters at the ends of its branches. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a hardy and ornamental species.

Due to its drought tolerance, it is often used in landscaping and reforestation projects.
