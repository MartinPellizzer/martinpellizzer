Viridantha mauryana

Viridantha mauryana, commonly known as the Indian violet.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the family Violaceae, characterized by its clusters of violet-blue flowers. The plant is native to the Indian subcontinent and thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a velvety texture, while its stems are slender and often trailing.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
