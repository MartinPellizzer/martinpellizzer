Vismia rusbyi

Vismia rusbyi, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub that can grow up to 10 meters tall. The plant is characterized by its dense clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a waxy texture.

Vismia rusbyi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its showy floral display.
