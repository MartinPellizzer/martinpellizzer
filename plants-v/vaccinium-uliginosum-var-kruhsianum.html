Vaccinium uliginosum var. kruhsianum

Vaccinium uliginosum var. kruhsianum, commonly known as the Kruhsian cowberry, is a variant of the bog bilberry.

It is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the northern hemisphere, particularly found in peat bogs and acidic soils. The plant produces small, edible berries that are typically dark red to black in color. It has elliptical leaves that are leathery and densely packed, contributing to its dense, shrub-like appearance.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and its role in supporting local wildlife.
