Vilfa setulosa

Vilfa setulosa, commonly known as the hairy vilfa, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems covered in fine, hair-like structures. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that are typically yellow or pale green in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Vilfa setulosa is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
