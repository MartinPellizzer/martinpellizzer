Vicia barbazitae

Vicia barbazitae, commonly known as the Barbazite vetch, is a flowering plant species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial legume characterized by its slender, erect stems and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in dense, elongated clusters. Its fruit is a legume pod that contains several elliptical seeds.

Vicia barbazitae is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
