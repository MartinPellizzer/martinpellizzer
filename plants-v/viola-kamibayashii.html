Viola kamibayashii

Viola kamibayashii, commonly known as the Japanese violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and delicate, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and mountainous regions. Its flowers bloom in early spring, adding a touch of color to the landscape.

Viola kamibayashii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
