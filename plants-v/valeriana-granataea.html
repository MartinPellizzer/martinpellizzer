Valeriana granataea

Valeriana granataea, commonly known as the Crimson Valerian, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its striking red or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage consists of opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.
