Vriesea sceptrum f. flavobracteata

Vriesea sceptrum f. flavobracteata, commonly known as the golden bract vriesea, is a hybrid bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated leaves that form a rosette, and its vibrant, long-lasting inflorescence that displays bright yellow flowers. The plant produces showy, orange-red bracts that encase the flowers, enhancing its ornamental appeal. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This cultivar is popular among bromeliad enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
