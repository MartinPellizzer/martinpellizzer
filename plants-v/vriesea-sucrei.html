Vriesea sucrei

Vriesea sucrei, commonly known as the Sucre's Vriesea, is a species of flowering plant in the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to South America, specifically found in regions of Brazil and Peru. This plant is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting inflorescence that can display shades of red, orange, and yellow. The leaves are typically broad and arranged in a rosette pattern, forming a central cup that collects water.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its striking appearance.
