Vanda rostrata

Vanda rostrata, commonly known as the rostrata orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, slender pseudobulbs and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and requires well-draining potting media. Vanda rostrata is known for its rapid growth and ability to produce multiple flower spikes from a single stem.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both horticultural collections and commercial cultivation.
