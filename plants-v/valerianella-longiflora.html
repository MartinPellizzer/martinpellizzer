Valerianella longiflora

Valerianella longiflora, commonly known as violet wood sorrel, is a low-growing annual plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive violet or purple coloration, and small, delicate flowers that range from white to pink. The plant thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in gardens and woodland areas. It is known for its mild, tangy flavor, making it a popular ingredient in culinary applications.

Valerianella longiflora is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential calming and digestive properties.
