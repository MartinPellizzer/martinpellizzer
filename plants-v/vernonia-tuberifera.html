Vernonia tuberifera

Vernonia tuberifera, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its woody stem and small, yellowish flowers. The plant is known for its hard, dense wood, which is highly valued for construction and furniture-making. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

Vernonia tuberifera is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
