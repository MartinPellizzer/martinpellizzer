Verbena × noackii

Verbena × noackii, commonly known as the hybrid verbena, is a perennial flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a cross between Verbena hong Kongensis and Verbena stricta, resulting in a plant with unique hybrid characteristics. The species features erect stems covered in fine, hairy texture and produces clusters of small, brightly colored flowers in shades of purple, blue, and red. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and long blooming period add ornamental value to both containers and garden beds.
