Viola munbyana

Viola munbyana, commonly known as the Munby violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, violet-blue flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its flowers bloom in spring, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat.

Viola munbyana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
