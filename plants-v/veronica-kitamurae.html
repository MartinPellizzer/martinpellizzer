Veronica kitamurae

Veronica kitamurae, commonly known as the Kitamura's speedwell, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its small, blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its unique appearance.

Veronica kitamurae is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
