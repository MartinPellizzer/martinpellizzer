Vriesea ensiformis

Vriesea ensiformis, commonly known as the sword fern, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its long, narrow leaves that resemble a sword, giving it its common name. The plant produces striking inflorescences with colorful bracts that can range from red to orange. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Vriesea ensiformis is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
