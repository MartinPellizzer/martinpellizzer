Vaccinium guangdongense

Vaccinium guangdongense, commonly known as Guangdong blueberry, is a species native to southern China.

It is a shrub that typically grows in forested areas with acidic soil. The plant produces small, edible berries that are rich in antioxidants. Its leaves are evergreen and have a dark green color with a slight pubescence.

This plant is valued for both its ornamental qualities and its potential use in local cuisine and traditional medicine.
