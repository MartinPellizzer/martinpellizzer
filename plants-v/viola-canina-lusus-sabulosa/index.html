Viola Canina Lusus Sabulosa

Viola canina lusus sabulosa is a rare and delicate flowering plant known for its unique adaptations to coastal environments. This species thrives in sandy soils and is often found along the shores of the Atlantic coast. Its small, heart-shaped leaves and pale purple flowers make it a striking yet elusive plant. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated and threatened by habitat loss and climate change. Understanding and protecting this species is crucial for preserving biodiversity in coastal ecosystems.
