Vriesea tweedieana

Vriesea tweedieana, commonly known as the Tweedie's Vriesea, is a species of flowering plant in the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its striking, long-lasting inflorescences that display vibrant colors, often in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a central cup, which collects water and nutrients. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Vriesea tweedieana is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
