Vepris carringtoniana

Vepris carringtoniana, commonly known as Carrington's rosewood, is a small evergreen tree native to the Caribbean, particularly Jamaica and the Greater Antilles.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its aromatic, dark-colored wood. The species is valued for its hard, dense wood, which is used in the production of musical instruments and fine furniture.
