Vitex henryi

Vitex henryi, commonly known as Chinese vitex, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Verbenaceae and is characterized by its opposite, ovate to lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers that range from white to purple, often appearing in summer. Its fruit is a small, dry capsule containing several seeds.

Vitex henryi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
