Viola stenocentra

Viola stenocentra, commonly known as the narrow-petaled violet, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, delicate purple or violet flowers. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.

Viola stenocentra is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for gardeners.
