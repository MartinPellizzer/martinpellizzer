Vilfa mangalorica

Vilfa mangalorica, commonly known as the Mangalore violet, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is characterized by its delicate purple flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. It belongs to the family Violaceae and is known for its ornamental value.

Its botanical features make it a unique species within its native habitat.
