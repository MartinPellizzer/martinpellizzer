Valeriana speluncaria var. glabriuscula

Valeriana speluncaria var. glabriuscula, commonly known as the smooth-hairy valerian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in late summer. The plant has aromatic, often hairy leaves and is adapted to grow in rocky or shaded environments. It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is known for its medicinal properties.

This variant is distinguished by its reduced pubescence compared to other varieties of Valeriana speluncaria.
