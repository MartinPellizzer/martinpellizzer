Vanda Pallens

Vanda pallens is a striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. Known for its vibrant, orchid-like flowers and unique growth habits, this plant has captured the attention of botanists and horticulturists alike. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks. Its delicate appearance belies its resilience, making it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation. This article explores the characteristics, care requirements, and ecological significance of Vanda pallens.
