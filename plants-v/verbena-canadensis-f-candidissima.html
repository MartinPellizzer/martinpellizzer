Verbena canadensis f. candidissima

Verbena canadensis f. candidissima, commonly known as White verbena, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its showy, clustered white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. It has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, bead-like fruits.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
