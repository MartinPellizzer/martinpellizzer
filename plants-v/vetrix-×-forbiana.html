Vetrix × forbiana

Vetrix × forbiana, commonly known as the hybrid stonecrop, is a low-growing succulent plant characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers.

It is a hybrid between two species of the genus Sempervivum, which are native to various regions of Europe and Asia. The plant typically thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers. Its foliage is often variegated, featuring shades of green, silver, and red, adding visual interest to garden landscapes.

Due to its hardy nature, it is well-suited for USDA hardiness zones 4 through 8.
