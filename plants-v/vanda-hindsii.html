Vanda hindsii

Vanda hindsii, commonly known as the Hinds' orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, slender pseudobulbs and linear, dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. Vanda hindsii thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

It is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
