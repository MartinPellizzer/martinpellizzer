Veronica galathica

Veronica galathica, commonly known as the white speedwell, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Plantaginaceae family and typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are oval-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.
