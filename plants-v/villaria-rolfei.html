Villaria rolfei

Villaria rolfei, commonly known as the Rolfe's violet, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Violaceae and is characterized by its delicate, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a velvety texture, while its stems are wiry and branching.

Villaria rolfei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
