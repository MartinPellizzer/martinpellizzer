Vulneraria rustica f. arenaria

Vulneraria rustica f. arenaria, commonly known as the sand vulneraria, is a flowering plant native to arid and sandy environments.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, cruciform flowers. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as coastal dunes and sandy soils. Its leaves are linear and often covered in fine hairs to reduce water loss.

It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds adapted for dispersal in dry conditions.
