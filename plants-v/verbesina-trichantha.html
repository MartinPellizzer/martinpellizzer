Verbesina trichantha

Verbesina trichantha, commonly known as the hairy beggar-tick, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant is recognized for its hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. It produces yellow flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
