Verbascum tuna-ekimii

Verbascum tuna-ekimii, commonly known as the Turkish mullein, is a perennial herb native to Turkey and the eastern Mediterranean.

It belongs to the genus Verbascum, which includes over 300 species of flowering plants. The plant features fuzzy, gray-green leaves that are lance-shaped and can grow up to 30 cm in length. It produces tall spikes of yellow flowers in the summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Verbascum tuna-ekimii is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
