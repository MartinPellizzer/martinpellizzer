Vincetoxicum nigrescens

Vincetoxicum nigrescens, commonly known as black nightshade, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and dark purple to black berries that mature in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas and is native to parts of Europe and Asia. Its leaves are ovate with serrated edges, and it produces small, white flowers in clusters.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
