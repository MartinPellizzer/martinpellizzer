Vellozia fibrosa

Vellozia fibrosa, commonly known as the fibrous vellozia, is a unique member of the Velloziaceae family native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and dense, fibrous leaves that are often covered in a thick layer of hair. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. Vellozia fibrosa is adapted to arid environments, with features that help it conserve water and withstand extreme temperatures.

Its distinctive morphology and ecological adaptations make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
