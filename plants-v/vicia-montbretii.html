Vicia montbretii

Vicia montbretii, commonly known as the Montbret pea, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges and rocky habitats.

Vicia montbretii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
