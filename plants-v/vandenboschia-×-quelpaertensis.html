Vandenboschia × quelpaertensis

Vandenboschia × quelpaertensis, commonly known as the Quelpaert hybrid ice plant, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Vandenboschia arborescens and Vandenboschia humifusa.

It is a succulent plant characterized by its fleshy, branching stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces vibrant pink or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is adapted to arid environments and thrives in well-drained soils.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
