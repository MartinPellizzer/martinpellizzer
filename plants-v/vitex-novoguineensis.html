Vitex novoguineensis

Vitex novoguineensis, commonly known as the New Guinea vitex, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of New Guinea.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically clustered in terminal inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a distinctive velvety texture.

The fruit is a small, woody capsule that contains several seeds.
