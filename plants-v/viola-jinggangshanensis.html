Viola jinggangshanensis

Viola jinggangshanensis, commonly known as the Jinggangshan violet, is a rare and endemic species native to China.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant purple flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors, where it thrives in cool and humid conditions. Its flowers bloom in spring, adding a splash of color to its natural habitat.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
