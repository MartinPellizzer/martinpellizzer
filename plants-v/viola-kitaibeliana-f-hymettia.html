Viola kitaibeliana f. hymettia

Viola kitaibeliana f. hymettia, commonly known as the Hymettian violet, is a rare and charming flowering plant native to the Hymettus mountain range in Greece.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, heart-shaped leaves and violet-blue flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in woodland or rocky habitats. Its flowers bloom in early spring, adding a touch of color to the landscape.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and protected species in its native region.
