Verbena litoralis f. magnifolia

Verbena litoralis f. magnifolia, commonly known as the coastal verbena, is a compact, trailing perennial native to coastal regions.

It features small, vibrant blue or purple flowers that bloom throughout the summer. The plant has narrow, grey-green leaves that are adapted to withstand salt spray and sandy soils. It thrives in full sun and is often used in coastal gardens for its hardiness and ornamental value.

Its compact growth habit makes it ideal for rock gardens or as a ground cover in maritime environments.
