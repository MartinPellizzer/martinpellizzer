Verbesina hypomalaca var. saltillensis

Verbesina hypomalaca var. saltillensis, commonly known as the Saltill sunflower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and often branched.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
