Viburnum carlesii var. bitchiuense

Viburnum carlesii var. bitchiuense, commonly known as the Bitchiu viburnum, is a deciduous shrub native to southern China.

It features small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. The plant has ovate to elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental value and is often used in gardens for its seasonal interest.
