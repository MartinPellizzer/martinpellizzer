Viola yunnanensis

Viola yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its flowers bloom in spring and are often adorned with distinctive patterns and hues of purple and white.

Viola yunnanensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
