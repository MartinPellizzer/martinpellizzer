Verbascum × elasigense

Verbascum × elasigense, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the genus Verbascum, which includes several species of flowering plants known for their large, showy flowers. The hybrid is characterized by its tall, hairy stems and clusters of yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable plant for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

This plant is also valued for its medicinal properties, with historical use in treating respiratory ailments.
