Vriesea magnibracteata

Vriesea magnibracteata, commonly known as the giant vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, sword-shaped leaves that form a rosette, creating a central tank to collect rainwater. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. Its foliage is often marked with distinctive silver or white markings, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Vriesea magnibracteata thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
