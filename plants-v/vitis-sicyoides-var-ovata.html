Vitis sicyoides var. ovata

Vitis sicyoides var. ovata, commonly known as the ovate grapevine, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into clusters of berries. The plant is characterized by its vigorous growth and is often found in moist, tropical environments. Its berries are edible but not commonly cultivated for commercial use.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native habitats.
