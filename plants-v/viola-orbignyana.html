Viola orbignyana

Viola orbignyana, commonly known as the Brazilian violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and produces striking violet to purple flowers with five petals. The plant typically grows to a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters and thrives in moist, shaded environments. It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens.

The species is also noted for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
