Vernonia sintenisii

Vernonia sintenisii, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its hard, dense wood, which is highly valued for its strength and durability. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the rainy season. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a rough texture.

Vernonia sintenisii is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in challenging climates.
