Vanda masperoae

Vanda masperoae, commonly known as the Maspero's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often near forest floors or on tree roots. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. Vanda masperoae is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its relatively easy care requirements.

It is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and resilience.
