Valantia muralis var. muralis

Valantia muralis var. muralis, commonly known as the wall ivy, is a climbing plant native to temperate regions.

It features heart-shaped, lobed leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that appear in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found growing on walls, rocks, or in woodland areas.

Valantia muralis var. muralis is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to cover vertical surfaces in gardens.
