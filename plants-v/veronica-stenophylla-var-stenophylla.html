Veronica stenophylla var. stenophylla

Veronica stenophylla var. stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaved speedwell, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate appearance and ability to attract pollinators.
