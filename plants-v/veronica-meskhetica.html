Veronica meskhetica

Veronica meskhetica, commonly known as the Caucasian speedwell, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or mountainous areas. The plant features slender stems and small, blue to violet flowers arranged in clusters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

Veronica meskhetica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
