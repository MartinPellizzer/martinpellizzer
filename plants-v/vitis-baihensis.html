Vitis baihensis

Vitis baihensis, commonly known as the Chinese grapevine, is a species native to China.

It is a flowering plant in the Vitaceae family, characterized by its climbing habit and tendrils. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in the summer. Its berries are typically small and can vary in color from green to red.

Vitis baihensis is often cultivated for ornamental purposes and has limited use in viticulture due to its small size and specific growing conditions.
