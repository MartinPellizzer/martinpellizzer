Verbascum × pseudosinuatum

Verbascum × pseudosinuatum, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial plant belonging to the family Scrophulariaceae.

It is characterized by its fuzzy, woolly leaves that are typically gray-green in color, which help reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is native to regions of southern Europe and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is cultivated in gardens and used in traditional herbal medicine.
