Vriesea viminalis

Vriesea viminalis, commonly known as the silver vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, slender form and striking silver-green foliage that gives it its common name. The plant produces inflorescences with bright orange flowers, which bloom in the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Vriesea viminalis is a popular choice for indoor gardens due to its elegant appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
