Vogelia indica var. socotrana

Vogelia indica var. socotrana, commonly known as Socotra's vogelia, is a unique plant native to the Socotra archipelago.

It is a shrub that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, adapted to the harsh climate of its native region. The plant features distinctive leaves with a waxy surface, which helps reduce water loss in its dry habitat. Its flowers are small and yellowish, often clustered in dense inflorescences.

Vogelia indica var. socotrana is part of the Asteraceae family and is noted for its ecological importance in the local ecosystem.
