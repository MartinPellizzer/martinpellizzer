Viola changii

Viola changii, commonly known as the Chinese violet, is a perennial flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and delicate, violet-pink flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and mountainous regions. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Viola changii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
