Vernonia pterocaulon

Vernonia pterocaulon, commonly known as the hairy ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local pollinators.
