Veratrum nigrum subsp. ussuriense

Veratrum nigrum subsp.

ussuriense, commonly known as Ussuri leopard's bane, is a tall perennial herb native to eastern Asia. It features large, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of bell-shaped, greenish-white flowers.

The plant contains toxic alkaloids, making it potentially harmful to humans and animals. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as forests and stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a striking yet dangerous addition to its natural habitat.
