Vernonia exsertiflora

Vernonia exsertiflora, commonly known as the slender ironweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. Vernonia exsertiflora is often found in open woodlands and along roadsides, thriving in well-drained soils.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
