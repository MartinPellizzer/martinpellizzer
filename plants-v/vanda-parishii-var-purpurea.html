Vanda parishii var. purpurea

Vanda parishii var. purpurea, commonly known as the purple vanda, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking purple and white flowers, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has erect, branching stems and produces large, showy blooms that can reach up to 10 centimeters in diameter. It thrives in high light conditions and requires well-draining potting media.

Vanda parishii var. purpurea is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and hardiness.
