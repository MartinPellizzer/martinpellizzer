Vahlia capensis subsp.

capensis, commonly known as the Cape vahlia, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa. It belongs to the family Polygonaceae, which includes plants commonly known as buckwheats.

The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit, with slender, branching stems covered in soft, grayish hairs. Its leaves are ovate to elliptic in shape, with a distinctive reddish or purple tinge on the underside.
