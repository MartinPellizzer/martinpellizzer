Viburnum seemenii

Viburnum seemenii, commonly known as the Chinese snowberry, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers in clusters during late spring to early summer. These are followed by bright red, berry-like fruits that persist into winter.

Viburnum seemenii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
