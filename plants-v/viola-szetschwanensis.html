Viola szetschwanensis

Viola szetschwanensis, commonly known as the Sichuan violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of violet-blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and mountainous regions. Its flowers bloom in early spring, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Viola szetschwanensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its potential health benefits.
