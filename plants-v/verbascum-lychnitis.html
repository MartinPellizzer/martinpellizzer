Verbascum lychnitis

Verbascum lychnitis, commonly known as the evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems and large, showy yellow flowers that open in the evening. The plant produces capsule fruits containing numerous small, ovoid seeds. Its leaves are hairy and alternate, with a distinctive grayish-green color.

Verbascum lychnitis is often found in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to thrive in full sun.
