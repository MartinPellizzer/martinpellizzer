Vernonia glabrata var. linearifolia

Vernonia glabrata var. linearifolia, commonly known as slender ironweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom during the dry season. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for xeriscaping.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
