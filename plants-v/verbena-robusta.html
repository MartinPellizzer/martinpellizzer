Verbena robusta

Verbena robusta, commonly known as the common verbena, is a flowering plant belonging to the Verbenaceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, clustered flowers. The plant typically produces vibrant blue or violet flowers that bloom throughout the summer months. Verbena robusta thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies further enhance its horticultural value.
