Vigna dauciformis var. elata

Vigna dauciformis var. elata, commonly known as the slender cowpea, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets and produces small, yellow flowers that develop into elongated, cylindrical pods. The plant is characterized by its slender stems and is often cultivated for its edible seeds. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its nutritional content and adaptability to various growing conditions.
