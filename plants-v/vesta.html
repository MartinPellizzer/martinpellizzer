Vesta

Vesta, commonly known as the common tamarisk, is a shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its woody stems, small scale-like leaves, and pink or purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is often found in saline or alkaline soils, making it adaptable to harsh environments. Vesta has been used historically for its wood and as a source of dye.

Its ecological importance lies in its ability to stabilize soils and provide habitat for various wildlife species.
