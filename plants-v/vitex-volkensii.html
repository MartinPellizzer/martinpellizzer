Vitex volkensii

Vitex volkensii, commonly known as the African blueberry or wild lilac, is a shrub or small tree native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, often zigzagging branches and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically blue or purple in color, which bloom in clusters. Its fruits are small, woody berries that contain several seeds.

Vitex volkensii is adapted to dry conditions and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
