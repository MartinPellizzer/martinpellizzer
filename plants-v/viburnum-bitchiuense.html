Viburnum bitchiuense

Viburnum bitchiuense, commonly known as the Chinese snowball tree, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the family Adoxaceae and is characterized by its clusters of white, fragrant flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces small, dark blue berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
