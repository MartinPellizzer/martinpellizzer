Vernonia kirschsteineana

Vernonia kirschsteineana, commonly known as the Kirschstein's ironweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands. The plant produces tall, branched stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, blue to purple, and arranged in dense, spike-like clusters.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
