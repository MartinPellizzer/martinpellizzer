Veronica serpyllifolia var. alpina

Veronica serpyllifolia var. alpina, commonly known as alpine speedwell, is a low-growing perennial plant native to alpine regions of Europe and Asia.

It features small, blue to violet flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to July. The plant has basal leaves that are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to cold, high-altitude environments.

This variant is distinguished by its compact growth habit and is often found in mountain meadows and scree slopes.
