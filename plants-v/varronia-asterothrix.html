Varronia asterothrix

Varronia asterothrix, commonly known as the silverleaf varronia, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, silvery-gray foliage and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces small, spherical fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Botanically, it belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional uses.
