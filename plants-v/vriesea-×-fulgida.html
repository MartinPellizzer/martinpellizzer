Vriesea × fulgida

Vriesea × fulgida, commonly known as the flaming sword plant, is a hybrid bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, sword-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from red to orange. The plant produces vibrant, long-lasting inflorescences that can last for several months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Vriesea × fulgida is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
