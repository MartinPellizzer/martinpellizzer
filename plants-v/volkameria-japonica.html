Volkameria japonica

Volkameria japonica, commonly known as the Japanese volkameria, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly serrated margin.

Volkameria japonica is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
