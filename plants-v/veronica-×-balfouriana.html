Veronica × balfouriana

Veronica × balfouriana, commonly known as the Balfour's speedwell, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Plantaginaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters and features narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact size and long blooming period.
