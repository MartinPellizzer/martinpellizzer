Viola × mielnicensis

Viola × mielnicensis, commonly known as the Viola of Mielnik, is a hybrid species of the violet genus native to Central Europe.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, dark green leaves and vibrant violet or purple flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows to a height of 10–20 centimeters and prefers moist, well-drained soils in shaded or partially shaded environments. It is often found in woodlands and along stream banks, where it thrives in cool, humid conditions.

Viola × mielnicensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate beauty.
