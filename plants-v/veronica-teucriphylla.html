Veronica teucriphylla

Veronica teucriphylla, commonly known as the silverleaf veronica, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features upright, slender stems that bear opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a silvery-blue underside. The plant produces clusters of small, blue-to-purple flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and prolonged flowering period.
