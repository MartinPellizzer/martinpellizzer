Vinca herbacea

Vinca herbacea, commonly known as common vinca, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces vibrant blue to purple flowers throughout the summer. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and a sprawling growth habit, making it suitable for ground cover. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Vinca herbacea is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand drought conditions.
