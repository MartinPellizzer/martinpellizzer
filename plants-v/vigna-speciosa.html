Vigna speciosa

Vigna speciosa, commonly known as the showy cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant produces small, edible pods that contain round, nutrient-rich seeds. Vigna speciosa is often cultivated for both ornamental purposes and as a source of protein-rich food.

Its growth habit includes climbing or trailing vines, making it suitable for trellises or as a ground cover.
