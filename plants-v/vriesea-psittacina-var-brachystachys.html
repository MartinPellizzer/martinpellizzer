Vriesea psittacina var. brachystachys

Vriesea psittacina var. brachystachys, commonly known as the short-stemmed flame plant, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It features vibrant, torch-like inflorescences in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which are highly ornamental. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that form a rosette, creating a compact and visually appealing shape. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its long-lasting blooms and is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
