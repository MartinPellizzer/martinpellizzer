Vatica odorata subsp. odorata

Vatica odorata subsp.

odorata, commonly known as the fragrant ironwood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia. It belongs to the family Dipterocarpaceae and is characterized by its dense, heavy wood and aromatic leaves.

This species is valued for its hard timber and is also of ecological importance in its native habitats.
