Valeriana aligera

Valeriana aligera, commonly known as the Indian valerian, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas and parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, often fragrant leaves and small, clustered flowers. Its root system is notable for containing compounds that have been traditionally used in herbal medicine.
