Vernonia morii

Vernonia morii, commonly known as the African ironweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in savanna and woodland habitats. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are usually narrow and lance-shaped, with a rough texture.

Vernonia morii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses in local communities.
