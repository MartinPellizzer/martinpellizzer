Viola × granitica

Viola × granitica, commonly known as the Granite Violet, is a hybrid species within the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, often purple or violet-colored flowers. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens and woodland settings.

Viola × granitica is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
