Volkameria farinosa

Volkameria farinosa, commonly known as the silky tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, waxy leaves that give it a silky appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and delicate blooms.

Volkameria farinosa is typically found in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in moist, well-drained soils.
