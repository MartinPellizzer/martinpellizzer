Vernonia leiboldiana

Vernonia leiboldiana, commonly known as the African ironweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular yellow flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy texture.

Vernonia leiboldiana is known for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.
