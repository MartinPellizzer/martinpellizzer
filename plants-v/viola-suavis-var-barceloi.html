Viola suavis var. barceloi

Viola suavis var. barceloi, commonly known as the sweet violet of Barcelona, is a herbaceous perennial native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of violet and white, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in perfumery.
