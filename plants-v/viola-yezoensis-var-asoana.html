Viola yezoensis var. asoana

Viola yezoensis var. asoana, commonly known as the Japanese violet or Asoan violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces clusters of delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of purple and white, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
