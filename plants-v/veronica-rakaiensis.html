Veronica rakaiensis

Veronica rakaiensis, commonly known as the Rakai speedwell, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of East Africa.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its small, blue or purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in rocky or grassy habitats and is adapted to cool, temperate climates. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.
