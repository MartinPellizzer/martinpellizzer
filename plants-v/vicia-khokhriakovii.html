Vicia khokhriakovii

Vicia khokhriakovii, commonly known as the Khokhriakov vetch, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions. It produces pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
