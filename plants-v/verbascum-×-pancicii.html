Verbascum × pancicii

Verbascum × pancicii, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the genus Verbascum, which includes several species of flowering plants known for their fuzzy, velvety leaves. The hybrid is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

The plant's ornamental value comes from its striking foliage and long-lasting blooms.
