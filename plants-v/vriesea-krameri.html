Vriesea krameri

Vriesea krameri, commonly known as the Krameri Vriesea, is a species of flowering plant in the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its striking, sword-shaped leaves that form a rosette pattern. The plant produces vibrant, long-lasting inflorescences that can range in color from red to orange and yellow. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Vriesea krameri is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
