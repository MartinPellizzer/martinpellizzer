Varasia alsinoides

Varasia alsinoides, commonly known as the narrowleaf stellaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is a low-growing annual herb characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, opposite leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Native to regions of Europe and western Asia, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats.

Its botanical features make it a common weed in agricultural and garden settings.
