Vanda devoogtii

Vanda devoogtii, commonly known as the Devoogt's vanda, is a rare and striking orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white, with intricate patterns on the petals. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of trees or other structures without deriving nutrients from them. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Vanda devoogtii is highly valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
