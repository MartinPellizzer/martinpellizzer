Verbascum × nagelsii

Verbascum × nagelsii, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial plant belonging to the Scrophulariaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height, covered with dense, woolly hairs. The plant produces large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Native to southern Europe, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Its foliage is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its mild laxative properties.
