Valeriana taigicola

Valeriana taigicola, commonly known as the Siberian valerian, is a flowering plant native to the cold regions of Siberia and northern Asia.

Valeriana taigicola is also valued for its ornamental appeal, with clusters of small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in late summer.
