Vangueria vestita

Vangueria vestita, commonly known as the velvet vangueria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a velvety texture, giving the plant its common name.

Vangueria vestita is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in harsh climates.
