Vinca obtusiflora

Vinca obtusiflora, commonly known as greater periwinkle, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a low-growing evergreen shrub that produces clusters of small, bright pink or purple flowers. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Vinca obtusiflora is also valued for its ability to tolerate poor soil conditions and its long blooming season.
