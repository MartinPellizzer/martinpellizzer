Vriesea jonghei

Vriesea jonghei, commonly known as the Jonghei's Vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful inflorescence that can range from red to orange, often resembling a flaming flower. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that collect water, creating a natural reservoir for its epiphytic growth habit. Its foliage is typically a deep green with a waxy texture, enhancing its ornamental appeal.

Vriesea jonghei thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity to flourish.
