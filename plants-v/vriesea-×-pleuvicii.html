Vriesea × pleuvicii

Vriesea × pleuvicii, commonly known as the hybrid Vriesea, is a hybrid cultivar resulting from the cross between Vriesea × spectabilis and Vriesea × ventricosa.

It is a member of the Bromeliaceae family, characterized by its striking, often brightly colored inflorescences. The plant typically features a rosette of narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of trichomes. Its flowers, which emerge from the center of the rosette, are usually vibrant red, orange, or yellow, and can last for several weeks.

Vriesea × pleuvicii is a popular ornamental plant, valued for its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
