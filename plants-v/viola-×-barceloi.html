Viola × barceloi

Viola × barceloi, commonly known as the Barcelona violet, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Viola tricolor and Viola wittrockii.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Iberian Peninsula, characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, butterfly-shaped flowers. The plant typically blooms in spring, producing shades of purple, violet, and white. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value and adaptability contribute to its widespread use in horticulture.
