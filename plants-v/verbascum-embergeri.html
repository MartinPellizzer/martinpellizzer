Verbascum embergeri

Verbascum embergeri, commonly known as the Emberger's mullein, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a member of the Scrophulariaceae family and is characterized by its tall, hairy stems and large, fuzzy leaves. Verbascum embergeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
