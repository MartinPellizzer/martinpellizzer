Viburnum dalzielii

Viburnum dalzielii, commonly known as the Dalziel viburnum, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers in the spring, which are followed by bright red berries in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Viburnum dalzielii is also valued for its attractive form and seasonal interest.
