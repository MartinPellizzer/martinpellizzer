Veronica salina f. laevipes

Veronica salina f. laevipes, commonly known as the smooth saltwort, is a halophyte species that thrives in saline environments.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and small blue or purple flowers. The plant is typically found in coastal areas, salt marshes, and other salty habitats. Its leaves are covered in a waxy cuticle that helps reduce water loss in arid conditions.

This variety is notable for its adaptability to high salinity and is often used in ecological restoration projects.
