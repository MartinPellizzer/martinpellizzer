Viola tricolor var. mediterranea

Viola tricolor var. mediterranea, commonly known as the Mediteranean pansy, is a perennial flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive tricolor pattern of violet, yellow, and white on its petals. The plant thrives in cool climates and well-drained soil, often found in rocky or woodland areas. It produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in early spring.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens.
