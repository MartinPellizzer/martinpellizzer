Verbesina sinaloensis

Verbesina sinaloensis, commonly known as the Sinaloa beggar's ticks, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

The plant produces small, bract-like structures that attach to the stems, giving it a unique appearance. It thrives in dry, open habitats and is often found in grasslands and disturbed soils.
