Vernonia guerreroana

Vernonia guerreroana, commonly known as Guerrero's ironweed, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is known for its upright growth habit. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers are yellow with a central disk.

It plays an important role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for pollinators.
