Viticella menziesii

Viticella menziesii, commonly known as the Menzies' vine, is a deciduous climbing plant native to Australia.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, often with a hairy texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by blue-black berries. It is well adapted to dry conditions and thrives in full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
