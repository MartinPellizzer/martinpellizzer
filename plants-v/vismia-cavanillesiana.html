Vismia cavanillesiana

Vismia cavanillesiana, commonly known as the yellow bell tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and clusters of yellow, bell-shaped flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage and vibrant floral display.

Vismia cavanillesiana thrives in warm, humid climates and is well-suited for tropical and subtropical gardens.
