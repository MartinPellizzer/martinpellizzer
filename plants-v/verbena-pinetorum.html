Verbena pinetorum

Verbena pinetorum, commonly known as the pine forest verbena, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in sunny, well-drained soils, often found in pine woodlands. The plant produces clusters of small, vivid blue or violet flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive appearance.

Verbena pinetorum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
