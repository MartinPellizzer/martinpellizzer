Vachellia bricchettiana

Vachellia bricchettiana, commonly known as the Briccett's acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to parts of Africa and the Arabian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and nitrogen-fixing capabilities. The plant produces yellow flowers that are clustered in dense, globular inflorescences, and its leaves are typically bipinnate, consisting of multiple small leaflets.
