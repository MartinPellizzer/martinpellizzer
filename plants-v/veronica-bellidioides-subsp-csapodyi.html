Veronica bellidioides subsp. csapodyi

Veronica bellidioides subsp.

csapodyi, commonly known as the Carpathian speedwell, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Carpathian Mountains. It typically grows in rocky and alpine habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environmental conditions.

The plant features small, blue to purple flowers arranged in spike-like clusters, which bloom in early summer. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological role in mountain ecosystems.
