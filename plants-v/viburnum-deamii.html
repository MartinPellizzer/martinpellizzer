Viburnum deamii

Viburnum deamii, commonly known as Deam's viburnum, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers are small, white, and clustered in umbels. The plant produces red, berry-like fruits that are edible for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Viburnum deamii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in gardens.
