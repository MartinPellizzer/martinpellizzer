Verbascum × isfendiyarense

Verbascum × isfendiyarense, commonly known as the Isfendiyar hybrid mullein, is a hybrid plant in the genus Verbascum, which belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae.

It is characterized by its tall, hairy stems and large, velvety leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
