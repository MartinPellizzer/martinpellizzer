Vitex ferruginea

Vitex ferruginea, commonly known as the red flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red and orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has compound leaves with opposite arrangement and produces small, woody fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental species.

The tree is also valued for its rapid growth and ability to provide shade in tropical landscapes.
