Viola × nataliae

Viola × nataliae, commonly known as the Natal violet, is a hybrid species within the Violaceae family.

It is native to South Africa, specifically the Natal region, and is characterized by its vibrant violet and blue flowers. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of about 10 to 30 centimeters. Its foliage consists of heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a hairy texture.

Viola × nataliae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive blooms.
