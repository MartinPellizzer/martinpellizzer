Verbascum × crenulatum

Verbascum × crenulatum, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a flowering plant belonging to the Scrophulariaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, fuzzy, gray-green leaves that are often used in traditional herbal remedies. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soils in full sun.

Verbascum × crenulatum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its striking appearance and ease of care.
