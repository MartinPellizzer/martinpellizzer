Volkameria multiflora

Volkameria multiflora, commonly known as the evergreen shrub or mountain hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It features woody stems and is characterized by its clusters of showy, five-petaled yellow flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, flat seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and mountainous regions.

Volkameria multiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
