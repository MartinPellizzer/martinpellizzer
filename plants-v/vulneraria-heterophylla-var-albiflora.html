Vulneraria heterophylla var. albiflora

Vulneraria heterophylla var. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered vulneraria, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, cruciform flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in open, sunny habitats. Its leaves are often variable in shape, which is reflected in its scientific name.

The white flowers are usually arranged in clusters and bloom in the summer months.
