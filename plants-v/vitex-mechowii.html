Vitex mechowii

Vitex mechowii, commonly known as Chinese vitex, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to purple, typically blooming in summer. Its fruits are dry, three-lobed capsules that release seeds when mature.

Vitex mechowii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in Chinese medicine.
