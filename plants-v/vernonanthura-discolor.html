Vernonanthura discolor

Vernonanthura discolor, commonly known as the pink-flowered violets, is a member of the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive pinkish-purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Native to South America, it thrives in moist, shady environments.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
