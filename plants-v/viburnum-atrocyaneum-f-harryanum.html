Viburnum atrocyaneum f. harryanum

Viburnum atrocyaneum f. harryanum, commonly known as the dark purple viburnum, is a compact shrub native to China.

It features small, dark purple to almost black berries that persist through winter, adding ornamental value. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are dark green with a slightly pubescent underside. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its unique foliage and berries make it a valuable addition to ornamental horticulture.
