Valeriana stolonifera subsp. pralensis

Valeriana stolonifera subsp.

pralensis, commonly known as the Pralens valerian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It features upright, branched stems that are often covered in fine, soft hairs.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white, arranged in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are ovate to elliptic, with a slightly toothed margin and a rough texture. Valeriana stolonifera subsp.

pralensis is known for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine for its calming effects.
