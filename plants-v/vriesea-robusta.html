Vriesea robusta

Vriesea robusta, commonly known as the sword fern or banana plant, is a tropical evergreen plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sturdy stalk and large, broad leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces striking inflorescences with brightly colored flowers, often in shades of red, orange, and yellow. Vriesea robusta thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its leaves are typically sword-shaped, giving it its common name, and it is well-suited for indoor cultivation with proper light and watering conditions.
