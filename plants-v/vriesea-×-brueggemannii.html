Vriesea × brueggemannii

Vriesea × brueggemannii, commonly known as the hybrid Vriesea, is a hybrid orchid species that combines traits from its parent species, Vriesea tricolor and Vriesea × spectabilis.

It is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting inflorescences that display a range of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that form a rosette, providing a striking contrast to its colorful flowers. It is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

Vriesea × brueggemannii thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to maintain its health and bloom.
