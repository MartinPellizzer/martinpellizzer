Viburnum tinus var. strictum

Viburnum tinus var. strictum, commonly known as the evergreen viburnum, is a compact, shrub-like plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, dark green foliage that remains throughout the year, providing year-round visual interest. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in late winter to early spring, followed by bright red berries that attract birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for garden borders or container planting.

Due to its compact growth habit, it is ideal for small spaces and requires minimal pruning.
