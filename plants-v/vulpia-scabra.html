Vulpia scabra

Vulpia scabra, commonly known as rough veldtgrass, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and Africa.

It is a member of the grass family Poaceae and typically grows in dry, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields. Vulpia scabra produces small, wind-pollinated flowers that develop into three-angled, hairy seeds.
