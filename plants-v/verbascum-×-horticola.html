Verbascum × horticola

Verbascum × horticola, commonly known as the common mullein.

It is a hybrid species within the genus Verbascum, characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, fuzzy leaves. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it has been cultivated for its ornamental value.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
