Vitellariopsis dispar

Vitellariopsis dispar, commonly known as the African violet.

It is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa. The plant is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and clusters of showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple. Vitellariopsis dispar is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
