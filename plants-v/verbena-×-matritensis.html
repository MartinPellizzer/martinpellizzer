Verbena × matritensis

Verbena × matritensis, commonly known as the Spanish verbena, is a hybrid perennial plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from purple to pink. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and features narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, which extends from spring through summer.
