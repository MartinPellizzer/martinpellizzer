Vitex buchananii

Vitex buchananii, commonly known as the African pride of Barbados, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, violet to purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a rough texture.

Vitex buchananii is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
