Viola napellifolia var. sieboldiana

Viola napellifolia var. sieboldiana, commonly known as the Japanese violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces delicate, violet-colored flowers with five petals, often with a distinctive white or yellowish throat. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
