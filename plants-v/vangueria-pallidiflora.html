Vangueria pallidiflora

Vangueria pallidiflora, commonly known as the pale-flowered vangueria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in arid or semi-arid regions. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are usually narrow and have a slightly hairy texture.

Vangueria pallidiflora is noted for its adaptability to harsh environmental conditions.
