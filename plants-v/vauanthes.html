Vauanthes

Vauanthes, commonly known as the mountain orchid, is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

Vauanthes species are characterized by their elegant, often fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and purple.
