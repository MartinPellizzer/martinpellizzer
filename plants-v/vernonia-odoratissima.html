Vernonia odoratissima

Vernonia odoratissima, commonly known as the sweet vernonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant is often found in open woodlands and along riverbanks, thriving in well-drained soils. Its aromatic leaves and showy inflorescences make it a popular ornamental plant.

Vernonia odoratissima is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
