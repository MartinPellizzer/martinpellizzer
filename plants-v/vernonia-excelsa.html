Vernonia excelsa

Vernonia excelsa, commonly known as the tall ironweed, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem that can reach heights of up to 6 feet. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically arranged in dense, spike-like clusters and are usually a striking yellow or orange color.

Vernonia excelsa is often found in moist or wet habitats such as marshes and stream banks.
