Vriesea menescalii

Vriesea menescalii, commonly known as the silver stripe vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting inflorescence that displays a mix of yellow, orange, and red hues. The plant features broad, dark green leaves that are often marked with silver or white stripes, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Vriesea menescalii is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and visual impact.
