Vriesea portentosa

Vriesea portentosa, commonly known as the star sapphire bromeliad, is a striking tropical plant native to Brazil.

It features a rosette of dark green, waxy leaves that form a central cup, which collects water and nutrients. The plant produces vibrant, long-lasting inflorescences with bright blue to violet petals that resemble stars. Its foliage is often adorned with intricate, colorful markings that enhance its ornamental appeal.

Vriesea portentosa thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity to flourish.
