Vitex impressinervia

Vitex impressinervia, commonly known as the impress-vein vitex, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with prominent veins that give it its common name. Its flowers are usually clustered in panicles and can be purple or white in color.

Vitex impressinervia is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
