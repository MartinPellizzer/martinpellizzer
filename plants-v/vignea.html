Vignea

Vignea, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the family Malvaceae.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that contain numerous seeds.
