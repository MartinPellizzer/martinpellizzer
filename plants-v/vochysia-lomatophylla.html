Vochysia lomatophylla

Vochysia lomatophylla, commonly known as the pink-leafed sylph, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive pinkish-red foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, followed by small, round fruits.

Vochysia lomatophylla is often cultivated for its ornamental value in warm climates.
