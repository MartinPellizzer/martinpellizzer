Viola × preywischiana

Viola × preywischiana, commonly known as the hybrid violet, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Violaceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between Viola tricolor and Viola lutea, resulting in a plant with a diverse range of flower colors, including purple, blue, and yellow. The leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers typically bloom in early spring.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various soil conditions.
