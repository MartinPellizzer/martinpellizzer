Villosocallerya

Villosocallerya, commonly known as the silky broom.

It is a flowering plant native to South America, characterized by its dense, branched growth habit and showy yellow flowers. The plant features soft, hairy leaves that give it a distinctive, velvety appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

However, it is considered invasive in some regions due to its rapid spread and competitive nature.
