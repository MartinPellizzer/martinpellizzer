Viola × cluniensis

Viola × cluniensis, commonly known as the French violet, is a hybrid species within the viola genus.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, often purple or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and gardens. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Viola × cluniensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in horticultural settings.
