Vincetoxicum steppicola

Vincetoxicum steppicola, commonly known as the steppe vincetoxicum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as steppes and rocky slopes. The plant features slender, erect stems that are often branched and covered with fine hairs. Its leaves are alternate, ovate to lanceolate, and have a slightly serrated margin.

The flowers are small, greenish-yellow, and clustered in dense, cylindrical inflorescences, often appearing in late summer.
