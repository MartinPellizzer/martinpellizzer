Verbascum × erzindschanense

Verbascum × erzindschanense, commonly known as the Erzincan mullein.

This hybrid plant is a member of the Scrophulariaceae family and is native to Turkey. It is characterized by its large, fuzzy, gray-green leaves and showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or semi-arid environments.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
