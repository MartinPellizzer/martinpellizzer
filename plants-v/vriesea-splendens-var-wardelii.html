Vriesea splendens var. wardelii

Vriesea splendens var. wardelii, commonly known as the rainbow bromeliad, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It features a rosette of vibrant, sword-shaped leaves that can range in color from green to red, orange, and yellow, depending on light exposure. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with colorful, long-lasting bracts that resemble flowers. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in indoor environments with proper care.
