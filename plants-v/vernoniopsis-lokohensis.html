Vernoniopsis lokohensis

Vernoniopsis lokohensis, commonly known as the Cameroon goldenweed, is a flowering plant native to western Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, forested areas and has lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. Its yellow flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom during the rainy season.

Vernoniopsis lokohensis is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
