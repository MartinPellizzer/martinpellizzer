Vigna friesiorum var. ulugurensis

Vigna friesiorum var. ulugurensis, commonly known as the Uluguru bean, is a climbing legume native to East Africa.

It produces elongated, green pods that contain several kidney-shaped seeds. The plant thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soils. It is valued for its edible seeds, which are rich in protein and nutrients.

The Uluguru bean is also notable for its adaptability to dry conditions and its role in traditional agriculture.
