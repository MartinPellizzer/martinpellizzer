Vignea saxatilis

Vignea saxatilis, commonly known as the rock vine, is a flowering plant native to rocky and coastal areas.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in Mediterranean climates.

Vignea saxatilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in rocky environments.
