Viola binchuanensis

Viola binchuanensis, commonly known as the Bishan violet, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in forested areas and rocky habitats, preferring cool and moist conditions. Its flowers, which range in color from purple to white, bloom in spring and are often used in traditional medicine.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and medicinal properties.
