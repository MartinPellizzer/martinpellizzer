Vernonia phyliciformis

Vernonia phyliciformis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Vernonia phyliciformis is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local pollinators.
