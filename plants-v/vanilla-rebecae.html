Vanilla rebecae

Vanilla rebecae, commonly known as the red vanilla orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, vibrant red flowers. The plant produces a fragrant vanilla-like pod, which is used in both culinary and aromatic applications. Vanilla rebecae thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-drained soil.

Its unique coloration and scent make it a prized ornamental plant in botanical gardens.
