Vriesea speckmaieri

Vriesea speckmaieri, commonly known as the Speck's Vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It features vibrant, recurved foliage that forms a rosette, with leaves that are often bluish-green and adorned with reddish markings. The plant produces an inflorescence of bright orange flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Vriesea speckmaieri is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
