Vincetoxicum balakrishnanii

Vincetoxicum balakrishnanii, commonly known as the Balakrishnan's devil's ivy, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apocynaceae family.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red in color.
