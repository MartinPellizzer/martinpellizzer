Vernonia lastii

Vernonia lastii, commonly known as the African ironweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue to purple in color, arranged in dense spikes. Vernonia lastii is often found in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in full sun.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
