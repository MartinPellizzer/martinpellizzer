Verbascum × collinum

Verbascum × collinum, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the genus Verbascum, which includes several species of flowering plants known for their large, showy flowers. The hybrid is characterized by its tall, hairy stems and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used historically for its medicinal properties.
