Vanda pusilla

Vanda pusilla, commonly known as the miniature vanda, is a small epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and often has dark green, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, typically during the warmer months. Vanda pusilla thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining potting media.

It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
