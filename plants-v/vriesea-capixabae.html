Vriesea capixabae

Vriesea capixabae, commonly known as the Capixaba Vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting inflorescence that displays a mix of red, orange, and yellow hues. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that collect water, creating a small pool that supports various microorganisms. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This species is popular among bromeliad enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
