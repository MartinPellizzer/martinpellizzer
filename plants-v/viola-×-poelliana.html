Viola × poelliana

Viola × poelliana, commonly known as the hybrid violet, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Violaceae family.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Viola lutea and Viola tricolor, exhibiting a variety of flower colors including purple, blue, and white. The plant typically grows to a height of 10–30 cm, with heart-shaped leaves and showy, clustered flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas across Europe.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
