Veronica oltensis

Veronica oltensis, commonly known as the Olt Valley speedwell, is a flowering plant native to Romania.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small blue or violet flowers. The plant typically blooms in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Veronica oltensis is often found in rocky or gravelly soils in mountainous regions.
