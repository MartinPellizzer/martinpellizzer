Vincetoxicum hirundinaria var. balearicum

Vincetoxicum hirundinaria var. balearicum, commonly known as the Balearic milkweed, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features compound leaves with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets and produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant is characterized by its milky sap, which is toxic and can cause skin irritation. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological role, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
