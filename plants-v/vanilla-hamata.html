Vanilla hamata

Vanilla hamata, commonly known as the Madagascar vanilla, is a climbing orchid native to Madagascar.

It produces fragrant, dark brown vanilla pods that are harvested and used in culinary and fragrance applications. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in warm, humid climates with partial shade. It requires a symbiotic relationship with specific fungi for successful seed germination.

Its vanilla essence is highly valued for its rich, sweet aroma.
