Ventilago cernua

Ventilago cernua, commonly known as the hanging sarsaparilla, is a climbing shrub native to southern Africa.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers are small, white, and clustered in racemes. The plant produces yellow or orange berries that are edible and have a tangy flavor. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical characteristics make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
