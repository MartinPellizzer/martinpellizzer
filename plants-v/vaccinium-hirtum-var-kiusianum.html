Vaccinium hirtum var. kiusianum

Vaccinium hirtum var. kiusianum, commonly known as the Kiusianum blueberry, is a variant of the hirtum blueberry species.

It is a low-growing shrub native to Japan, characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, elliptical leaves. The plant produces clusters of white flowers in the spring, which are followed by edible, sweet-tart berries. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and culinary use in local cuisines.
