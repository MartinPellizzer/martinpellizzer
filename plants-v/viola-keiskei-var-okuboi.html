Viola keiskei var. okuboi

Viola keiskei var. okuboi, commonly known as the Okuboi violet, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Japan.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tinge. The plant produces delicate, violet-blue flowers with five petals, often adorned with white or yellow markings. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for rock gardens and shaded borders.
