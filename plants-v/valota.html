Valota

Valota, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features large, showy flowers that bloom above the water surface, while its roots anchor in the muddy substrate. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that float on the water's surface, aiding in photosynthesis. Valota is known for its symbolic significance in various cultures, often representing purity and spiritual growth.

Its botanical name, Nymphaea, reflects its association with water and beauty.
