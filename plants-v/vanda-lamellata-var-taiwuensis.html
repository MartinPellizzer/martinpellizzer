Vanda lamellata var. taiwuensis

Vanda lamellata var. taiwuensis, commonly known as the Taiwu vanda, is a rare and unique orchid species native to Taiwan.

It is characterized by its striking, large, and often brightly colored flowers that can range from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are usually broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native, often humid and mountainous environment.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and rarity.
