Vismia pentagyna

Vismia pentagyna, commonly known as the five-angled vismia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, pale grey bark and simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical to ovate in shape. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that are followed by bright red, fleshy berries. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and showy fruits.

Vismia pentagyna is also valued in traditional medicine for its various pharmacological properties.
