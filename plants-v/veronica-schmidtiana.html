Veronica schmidtiana

Veronica schmidtiana, commonly known as the Schmidt's speedwell, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that bear opposite pairs of lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value lies in its delicate appearance and ability to attract pollinators.
