Vriesea × nanoti

Vriesea × nanoti, commonly known as the hybrid Vriesea, is a hybrid cultivar resulting from the cross between Vriesea splendens and Vriesea 'Nanoti'.

It is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is native to South America. The plant is characterized by its striking, long-lasting inflorescence that can range in color from red to orange and yellow. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Vriesea × nanoti is popular as a decorative plant due to its vibrant appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
