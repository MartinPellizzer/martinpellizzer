Villamilla rosea-aenea

Villamilla rosea-aenea, commonly known as the silverleaf rose, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage and clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers. The plant belongs to the Rosaceae family and is valued for its ornamental qualities in gardens. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in a fine, silvery down.

The flowers bloom in late spring and early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
