Vanda gardinerae

Vanda gardinerae, commonly known as the gardenera vanda, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its pendulous, fleshy leaves and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically blooms in summer, producing vibrant orchid flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil with high humidity.

Vanda gardinerae is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
