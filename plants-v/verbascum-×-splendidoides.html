Verbascum × splendidoides

Verbascum × splendidoides, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the genus Verbascum, which includes several species of flowering plants known for their fuzzy leaves and showy flowers. The hybrid is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy yellow or orange flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

The plant is also valued for its medicinal properties, with historical uses in traditional remedies.
