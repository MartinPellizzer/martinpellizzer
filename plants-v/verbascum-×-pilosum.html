Verbascum × pilosum

Verbascum × pilosum, commonly known as the velvetleaf mullein, is a hybrid plant belonging to the genus Verbascum in the family Scrophulariaceae.

It is characterized by its fuzzy, velvety leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is native to southern Europe and is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides.

Verbascum × pilosum is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used historically in herbal medicine.
