Verbena crithmifolia var. latiloba

Verbena crithmifolia var. latiloba, commonly known as the broad-leaved verbena, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Its foliage emits a strong, spicy scent, which is often used in herbal remedies.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to coastal environments.
