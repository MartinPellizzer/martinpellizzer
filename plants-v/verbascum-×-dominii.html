Verbascum × dominii

Verbascum × dominii, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a member of the Scrophulariaceae family.

It is a tall, herbaceous plant that typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions. The plant features large, fuzzy, gray-green leaves that help reduce water loss. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, arranged in a showy spike at the top of the stem.

Verbascum × dominii is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
