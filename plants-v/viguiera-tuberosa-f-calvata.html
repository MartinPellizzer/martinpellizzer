Viguiera tuberosa f. calvata

Viguiera tuberosa f. calvata, commonly known as the smooth viguiera, is a perennial flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, yellow-orange composite flowers. The plant features soft, hairless foliage, which distinguishes it from other varieties of Viguiera. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value lies in its vibrant blooms and drought tolerance.
