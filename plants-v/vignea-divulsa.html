Vignea divulsa

Vignea divulsa, commonly known as the broadleaf nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant features alternate, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. Vignea divulsa is characterized by its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in semi-arid environments.
