Valerianella gracilis

Valerianella gracilis, commonly known as slender valerian, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically features small, pale green leaves that are rounded and slightly hairy, often arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous white or pale blue flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodland floors or along stream banks.

Valerianella gracilis is often found in nutrient-rich soils and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
