Viola crassa var. shikkensis

Viola crassa var. shikkensis, commonly known as the Shikkenshima violet, is a rare and ornamental species native to Japan.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dark green, heart-shaped leaves. The flowers are typically violet or purple, with a distinctive hooded structure over the lower lip. It prefers shady, moist environments and is often found in forested areas.

This variant is valued for its unique appearance and is cultivated for ornamental purposes in specialty gardens.
