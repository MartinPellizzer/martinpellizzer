Viburnum macrocephalum f. keteleeri

Viburnum macrocephalum f. keteleeri, commonly known as the Keteleeri snowball viburnum, is a compact, ornamental shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, round clusters of white flowers that appear in early spring, creating a striking visual display. The plant has dark green, ovate leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its floral displays. It is relatively low maintenance and thrives in partial shade to full sun, making it suitable for a variety of garden settings.

Its ability to produce fragrant flowers and attractive berries adds both aesthetic and ecological value to the landscape.
