Vanilla martinezii

Vanilla martinezii, commonly known as the Mexican vanilla orchid, is a species native to the highlands of Mexico.

It is a climbing orchid that produces fragrant, greenish-yellow flowers followed by dark brown pods. The plant is part of the Vanilla genus, which is known for its aromatic vanilla beans. The pods of Vanilla martinezii are harvested and dried to yield a flavoring similar to traditional vanilla.

This species is valued both for its ornamental qualities and its potential in the culinary and fragrance industries.
