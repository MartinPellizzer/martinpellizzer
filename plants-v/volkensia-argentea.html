Volkensia argentea

Volkensia argentea, commonly known as the silverleaf volkensia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Volkensia argentea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively low maintenance.
