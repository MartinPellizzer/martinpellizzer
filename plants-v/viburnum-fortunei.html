Viburnum fortunei

Viburnum fortunei, commonly known as the Fortune's viburnum, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers in spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.

Viburnum fortunei is also valued for its ability to thrive in partial shade to full sun conditions.
