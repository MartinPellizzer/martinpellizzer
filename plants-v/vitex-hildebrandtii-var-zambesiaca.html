Vitex hildebrandtii var. zambesiaca

Vitex hildebrandtii var. zambesiaca, commonly known as the Zambesi vitex, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. These flowers are followed by small, woody fruits that contain several seeds.

It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
