Viola × hollickii

Viola × hollickii, commonly known as the hybrid violet, is a member of the Violaceae family.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by heart-shaped leaves and showy, violet-colored flowers. The species is the result of crossbreeding between Viola tricolor and Viola lutea, leading to a variety with a range of flower colors. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This plant is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
