Vachellia koltermanii

Vachellia koltermanii, commonly known as the Kolterman's acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, with a deep root system for water absorption. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds.
