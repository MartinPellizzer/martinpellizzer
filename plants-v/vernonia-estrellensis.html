Vernonia estrellensis

Vernonia estrellensis, commonly known as Estrellan ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in dense, spherical clusters. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Vernonia estrellensis is adapted to arid environments and is often found in dry, rocky soils.
