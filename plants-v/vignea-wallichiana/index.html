Vignea Wallichiana

Vignea wallichiana is a rare and lesser-known flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. It belongs to the Fabaceae family, known for its leguminous properties and nitrogen-fixing capabilities. This plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest understories. Its delicate pink flowers and compound leaves make it a unique addition to botanical collections. Due to habitat loss, Vignea wallichiana is increasingly threatened, making conservation efforts crucial for its survival.
