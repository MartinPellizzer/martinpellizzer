Viola kamtschatica var. pekinensis

Viola kamtschatica var. pekinensis, commonly known as the Peking violet, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces violet-blue flowers with yellow markings, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.
