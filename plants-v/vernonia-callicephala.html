Vernonia callicephala

Vernonia callicephala, commonly known as the showy ironweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and along riverbanks.

Vernonia callicephala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
