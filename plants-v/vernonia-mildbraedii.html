Vernonia mildbraedii

Vernonia mildbraedii, commonly known as the Cape vernonia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, yellow to orange inflorescences that attract pollinators such as bees and birds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Vernonia mildbraedii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
