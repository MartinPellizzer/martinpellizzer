Vriesea notata

Vriesea notata, commonly known as the spotted vriesea, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, colorful inflorescence that can last for several months. The plant features a rosette of sword-shaped, waxy leaves that are often adorned with distinctive markings. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity to flourish.

Vriesea notata is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
