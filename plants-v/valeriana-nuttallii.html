Valeriana nuttallii

Valeriana nuttallii, commonly known as Nuttall's valerian, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its aromatic, often reddish or purple flowers and its extensive, fibrous root system. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is found in mountainous regions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a strong, distinctive scent.

Valeriana nuttallii is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
