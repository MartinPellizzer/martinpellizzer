Vitex amazonica

Vitex amazonica, commonly known as the Amazon vitex, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, violet-blue flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly serrated edge.

Vitex amazonica is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local medicine.
