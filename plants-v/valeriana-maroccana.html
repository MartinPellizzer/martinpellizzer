Valeriana maroccana

Valeriana maroccana, commonly known as Moroccan valerian, is a flowering plant native to North Africa.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, often fragrant foliage. The plant produces small, clustered white or pinkish flowers that bloom in summer. Its rhizomatous root system is known for its medicinal properties, containing compounds like valerenic acid.

Valeriana maroccana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and traditional uses in herbal medicine.
