Vitex laciniosa

Vitex laciniosa, commonly known as the slender chaste tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height, characterized by its opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves. The plant produces fragrant, blue or violet flowers that bloom in terminal clusters, typically in the summer months. Its fruits are small, woody follicles that contain several seeds.

Vitex laciniosa is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
