Viola longepedunculata

Viola longepedunculata, commonly known as the long pedunculate violet, is a species of flowering plant in the Violaceae family.

It is native to parts of Asia, including China and Japan, and thrives in woodland and moist meadow habitats. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive violet-blue flower that blooms in spring. Its sepals and petals are typically similar in color, forming a showy, five-petaled flower.

Viola longepedunculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate beauty.
