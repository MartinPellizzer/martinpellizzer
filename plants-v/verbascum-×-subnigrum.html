Verbascum × subnigrum

Verbascum × subnigrum, commonly known as the dark mullein.

It is a hybrid plant in the genus Verbascum, characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, fuzzy leaves. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It is native to southern Europe and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Verbascum × subnigrum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has historical uses in herbal medicine.
