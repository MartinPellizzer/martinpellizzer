Valeriana spiroflora

Valeriana spiroflora, commonly known as the spiral valerian, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caprifoliaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white in color. Native to temperate regions, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.
