Veronica baumgartenii

Veronica baumgartenii, commonly known as the alpine speedwell, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

Veronica baumgartenii is noted for its hardiness and is often used in rock gardens or alpine displays due to its ornamental value.
