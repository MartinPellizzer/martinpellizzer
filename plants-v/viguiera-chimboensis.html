Viguiera chimboensis

Viguiera chimboensis, commonly known as the Chimboensis Blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

The plant typically grows in high-altitude grasslands and shrublands, thriving in temperate to cold climates. Its blue to purple flowers bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Viguiera chimboensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitat.
