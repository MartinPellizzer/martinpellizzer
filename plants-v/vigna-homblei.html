Vigna homblei

Vigna homblei, commonly known as the yellow winged bean, is a leguminous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces elongated, slender pods that contain small, edible seeds. Vigna homblei is valued for its nutritional content, including high levels of protein and essential minerals.

It is often cultivated as a drought-tolerant crop in warm climates.
