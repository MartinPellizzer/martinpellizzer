Verbascum heldreichii

Verbascum heldreichii, commonly known as the Balkan mullein, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or limestone soils. The plant features tall, hairy stems that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height. Its leaves are large, broad, and covered in a dense layer of soft, woolly hair.

The flowers are yellowish-green and clustered in loose, terminal spikes, blooming in late summer.
