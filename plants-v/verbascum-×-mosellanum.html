Verbascum × mosellanum

Verbascum × mosellanum, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae and is characterized by its tall, erect stems covered in soft, woolly hairs. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in dry grasslands and rocky areas.

Its fuzzy leaves and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
