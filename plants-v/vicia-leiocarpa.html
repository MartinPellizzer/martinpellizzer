Vicia leiocarpa

Vicia leiocarpa, commonly known as the smoothpod vetch, is a flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from pale yellow to reddish-purple. Its fruit is a smooth, elongated pod that contains several elliptical seeds.

Vicia leiocarpa is typically found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.
