Vriesea schultesiana

Vriesea schultesiana, commonly known as the Schultes' Vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, strap-like leaves that form a rosette, creating a central water-holding cavity. The plant produces striking inflorescences with brightly colored bracts, often in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Vriesea schultesiana is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
