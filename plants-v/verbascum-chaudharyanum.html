Verbascum chaudharyanum

Verbascum chaudharyanum, commonly known as the Himalayan mullein, is a perennial herb native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Scrophulariaceae family and is characterized by its tall, hairy stems and large, showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are broad and fuzzy, and it produces capsules that contain numerous small seeds.

Verbascum chaudharyanum is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
