Vitex negundo var. sessilis

Vitex negundo var. sessilis, commonly known as the Chinese vitex or narrow-leaved vitex, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, ovate to elliptical leaves that are usually sessile, giving it its varietal name. The plant produces small, fragrant, blue or purple flowers in panicles, which bloom in summer. Its fruit is a dry capsule containing several seeds.

It is often used in traditional medicine and as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and floral display.
