Vernonia luederitziana

Vernonia luederitziana, commonly known as the Luederitz's ironweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Vernonia luederitziana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
