Veronica arvensis balearica

Veronica arvensis balearica, commonly known as the Balearic speedwell, is a flowering plant native to the Balearic Islands.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant features small, blue or purple flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom from spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in coastal areas and rocky habitats.
