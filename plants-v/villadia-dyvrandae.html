Villadia dyvrandae

Villadia dyvrandae, commonly known as the Dyvrand's violet, is a rare flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South Africa.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its striking violet-blue flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has small, elliptical leaves with a slightly hairy texture, adapted to the dry and cool conditions of its habitat. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires partial shade to flourish.
