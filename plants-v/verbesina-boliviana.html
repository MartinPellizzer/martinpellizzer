Verbesina boliviana

Verbesina boliviana, commonly known as the Bolivian ironweed, is a perennial herb native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant has palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, and its root system is fibrous and deep. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

Verbesina boliviana is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
