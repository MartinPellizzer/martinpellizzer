Valerianella radiata f. fernaldii

Valerianella radiata f. fernaldii, commonly known as the Fernald's sorrel, is a variant of the common sorrel species.

It is a low-growing annual herb with heart-shaped leaves that are often reddish-tinged. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, fertile soils and is often found in disturbed areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and sometimes invasive species in certain regions.
