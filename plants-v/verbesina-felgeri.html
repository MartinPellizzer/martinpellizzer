Verbesina felgeri

Verbesina felgeri, commonly known as the Mexican ironweed, is a perennial herb native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features upright, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are yellow and appear in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in various growing conditions.
