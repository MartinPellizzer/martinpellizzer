Viola heterophylla var. cavillieri

Viola heterophylla var. cavillieri, commonly known as the Cape York violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Australia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and wetlands. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive waxy coating, and its flowers are usually violet or purple in color. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
