Viola oblongosagittata f. ishizakii

Viola oblongosagittata f. ishizakii, commonly known as the Ishizaki viola, is a hybrid species within the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a distinctive waxy coating. The flowers are typically violet or purple, featuring five petals with a spurred lower petal. This plant is native to Japan and thrives in cool, moist environments.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
