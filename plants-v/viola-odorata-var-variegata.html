Viola odorata var. variegata

Viola odorata var. variegata, commonly known as the variegated sweet violet, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves with distinctive white and green variegation. The plant produces showy purple or violet flowers with a sweet fragrance, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its attractive foliage and delicate blossoms.
