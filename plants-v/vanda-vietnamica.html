Vanda vietnamica

Vanda vietnamica, commonly known as the Vietnam vanda, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It thrives in tropical climates and is often found growing on trees or rocks in humid environments. The plant features striking, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and pink. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Vanda vietnamica is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
