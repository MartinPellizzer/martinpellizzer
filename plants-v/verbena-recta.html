Verbena recta

Verbena recta, commonly known as upright verbena, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. Verbena recta thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

It is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
