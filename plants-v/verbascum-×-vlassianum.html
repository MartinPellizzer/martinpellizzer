Verbascum × vlassianum

Verbascum × vlassianum, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial plant belonging to the Scrophulariaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, velvety gray-green leaves and showy yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and can tolerate drought conditions.
