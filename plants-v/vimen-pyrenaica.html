Vimen pyrenaica

Vimen pyrenaica, commonly known as the Pyrenean broom.

It is a flowering plant native to the Pyrenees mountain range, characterized by its erect growth habit and yellow flowers. The plant has slender, branching stems covered in fine, grayish hairs. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and lance-shaped, often with a rough texture.

Vimen pyrenaica thrives in rocky and sunny habitats, contributing to the alpine flora of its region.
