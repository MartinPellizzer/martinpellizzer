Verbena hirta var. gracilis

Verbena hirta var. gracilis, commonly known as slender verbena, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and clusters of small, vibrant blue or purple flowers. The plant has narrow, hairy leaves that are opposite in arrangement, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its compact size and long blooming period make it an attractive and easy-to-care-for ornamental plant.
