Verbena Hirtella

Verbena hirtella, commonly known as the hairy verbena, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the southern United States. It is prized for its vibrant blue to purple blooms that appear in late spring and summer. This perennial plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking low-maintenance beauty. Its feathery leaves and long-lasting flowers make it a favorite in both wild and cultivated landscapes. With its ease of care and eye-catching appearance, Verbena hirtella is a valuable addition to any garden.
