Verticordia tumida subsp. therogana

Verticordia tumida subsp.

therogana, commonly known as the Geraldton waxplant, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia. It is characterized by its distinctive, waxy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring.
