Vochysia haenkeana var. lanceolata

Vochysia haenkeana var. lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceleaf vochysia, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves, which are typically arranged alternately along the stems. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are often white or pale yellow, followed by woody capsules containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland tropical forests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local communities.
