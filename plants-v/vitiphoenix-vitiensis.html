Vitiphoenix vitiensis

Vitiphoenix vitiensis, commonly known as the Vitiensis Phoenix, is a unique species of palm native to Vanuatu.

It is characterized by its slender, unbranched trunk and feather-like leaves that give it a delicate appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences, followed by ornamental berries. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species.

Its botanical features make it an important subject for conservation and ecological studies in tropical regions.
