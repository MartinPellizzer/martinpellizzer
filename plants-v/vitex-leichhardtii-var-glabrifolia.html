Vitex leichhardtii var. glabrifolia

Vitex leichhardtii var. glabrifolia, commonly known as the smooth-leaved vitex, is a shrub native to Australia.

It features aromatic, narrow leaves that are typically smooth and lance-shaped. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale purple, blooming in spring. Its fruits are woody capsules that split open to release seeds.

This species is often found in dry, sunny habitats and is valued for its ornamental and ecological benefits.
