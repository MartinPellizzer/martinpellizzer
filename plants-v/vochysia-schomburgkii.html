Vochysia schomburgkii

Vochysia schomburgkii, commonly known as the Brazilian cherry or the pink angel's trumpet, is a tropical tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy pink or white flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds.
