Viburnum tinoides f. venezuelense

Viburnum tinoides f. venezuelense, commonly known as the Venezuelan arrowwood, is a compact shrub native to the mountainous regions of Venezuela.

It features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clustered inflorescences, typically in late spring. The plant has dark green, elliptical leaves that are glossy and provide attractive foliage throughout the growing season. Its fruit is a red, persistent drupe that adds ornamental value in the fall.

Viburnum tinoides f. venezuelense is well-suited for woodland gardens and prefers partial shade with moist, well-drained soil.
