Veronica fargesii

Veronica fargesii, commonly known as the Chinese speedwell, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is a member of the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of blue to purple flowers. Veronica fargesii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
