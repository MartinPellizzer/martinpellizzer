Vernonia andrieuxii

Vernonia andrieuxii, commonly known as the Andrieux's ironweed, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features tall, slender stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically purple or blue, arranged in dense clusters at the top of the plant.

Vernonia andrieuxii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
