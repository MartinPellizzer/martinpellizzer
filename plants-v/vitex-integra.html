Vitex integra

Vitex integra, commonly known as the chaste tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It features opposite, ovate to lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy on the underside. The plant produces fragrant, blue or purple flowers in clustered panicles, which bloom in summer. Its fruit is a dry, woody capsule containing several small, black seeds.

Vitex integra is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
