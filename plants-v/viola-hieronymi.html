Viola hieronymi

Viola hieronymi, commonly known as the Hieronymus violet, is a species of flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Viola hieronymi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
