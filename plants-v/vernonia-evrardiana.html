Vernonia evrardiana

Vernonia evrardiana, commonly known as the African ironweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom during the dry season. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Vernonia evrardiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some African cultures.
