Vernonia congolensis subsp. congolensis

Vernonia congolensis subsp.

congolensis, commonly known as the Congo Vernonia, is a flowering plant native to central and western Africa. It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and riverine areas.

The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom during the wet season. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and have a rough texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
