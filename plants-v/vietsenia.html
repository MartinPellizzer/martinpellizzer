Vietsenia

Vietsenia, commonly known as the Vietnamese orchid, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Vietnam, where it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plants are characterized by their showy, fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and purple. Vietsenia species typically have sympodial growth habits, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

They require well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight for optimal growth and blooming.
