Vochysia opaca

Vochysia opaca, commonly known as the Brazil nut tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, compound leaves and showy, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The tree produces woody capsules that contain the edible seeds, known as Brazil nuts. It thrives in humid, lowland rainforests and requires a warm, moist climate.
