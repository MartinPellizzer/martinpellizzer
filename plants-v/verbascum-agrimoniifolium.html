Verbascum agrimoniifolium

Verbascum agrimoniifolium, commonly known as the agrimonium leaf mullein, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the genus Verbascum, which includes species commonly referred to as mulleins. This plant is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, fuzzy leaves that are often covered in soft, silky hairs. The flowers are typically yellow or orange and appear in clusters at the top of the plant.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.
