Veronica squamosa var. nitens

Veronica squamosa var. nitens, commonly known as the silver Veronica, is a perennial flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features upright stems covered in dense, silvery-white hairs, which give it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces spikes of small, blue or violet flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its attractive foliage and prolonged blooming period.
