Ventilago africana

Ventilago africana, commonly known as the African velvetleaf, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its hairy, ovate leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, smooth seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland and savanna habitats.

Ventilago africana is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local medicine.
