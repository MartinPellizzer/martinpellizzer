Verticordia densiflora var. pedunculata

Verticordia densiflora var. pedunculata, commonly known as the dense-flowered verticordia, is a striking member of the Myrtaceae family native to Western Australia.

This plant is characterized by its dense clusters of bright orange or red flowers, which appear in late winter to early spring. It has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in a fine, silky coating. The species is typically found in sandy or gravelly soils in heathlands and woodlands.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is a sought-after plant for native gardens and conservation efforts.
