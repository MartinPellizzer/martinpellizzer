Vernonia flaccidifolia

Vernonia flaccidifolia, commonly known as the slender ironweed, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, pale yellow to brownish flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters. It thrives in dry, open habitats and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Vernonia flaccidifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
