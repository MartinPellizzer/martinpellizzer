Volkameria linifolia

Volkameria linifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf shrub, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States and parts of Mexico.

It is characterized by its silvery-white, velvety foliage and small, fragrant yellow flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping and drought-tolerant gardens. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
