Viburnum morrisonense

Viburnum morrisonense, commonly known as Morrison's viburnum, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in landscaping.

Viburnum morrisonense prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun.
