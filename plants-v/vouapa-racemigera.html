Vouapa racemigera

Vouapa racemigera, commonly known as the Madagascar orchid, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, pendulous flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the nutrient-poor soils of its habitat.
