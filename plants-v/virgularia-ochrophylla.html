Virgularia ochrophylla

Virgularia ochrophylla, commonly known as the golden thread plant, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers. The flowers are typically yellow or orange, with a distinctive radiate structure that attracts pollinators like bees and butterflies.
