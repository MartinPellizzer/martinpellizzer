Volkameria buchananii

Volkameria buchananii, commonly known as the African snowball, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features dense, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spherical clusters, giving it a striking appearance. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
