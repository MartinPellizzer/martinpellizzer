Vernonia holosericea

Vernonia holosericea, commonly known as the silky vernonia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

Vernonia holosericea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
