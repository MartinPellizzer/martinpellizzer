Vismia lateriflora

Vismia lateriflora, commonly known as the African snowball, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant is characterized by its dense, rounded clusters of white, waxy flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a leathery texture.

Vismia lateriflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well-suited to coastal and sandy soils.
