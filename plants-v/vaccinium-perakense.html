Vaccinium perakense

Vaccinium perakense, commonly known as the Perak heather, is a species of evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes other familiar plants like blueberries and cranberries. The plant is characterized by its small, leathery leaves and clusters of bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species in its native habitat.
