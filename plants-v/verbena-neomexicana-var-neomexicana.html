Verbena neomexicana var. neomexicana

Verbena neomexicana var. neomexicana, commonly known as New Mexico verbena, is a perennial flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to native plant collections and pollinator habitats.
