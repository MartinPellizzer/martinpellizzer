Verbascum × sakaryense

Verbascum × sakaryense, commonly known as the Sakarya mullein.

This hybrid plant is a member of the Scrophulariaceae family and is native to Turkey. It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, fuzzy, gray-green leaves. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer.

It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
