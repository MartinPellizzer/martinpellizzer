Vandellia sulcata

Vandellia sulcata, commonly known as the Sulcate Vanda, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has long, slender stems and broad, waxy leaves that help it retain moisture in its natural habitat. It thrives in warm, humid environments with high light exposure.

Vandellia sulcata is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
