Veronica kaiseri

Veronica kaiseri, commonly known as the Kaiseri speedwell, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Veronica kaiseri is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
