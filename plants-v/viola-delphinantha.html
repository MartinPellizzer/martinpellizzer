Viola delphinantha

Viola delphinantha, commonly known as the purple violet, is a species of flowering plant in the Violaceae family.

It is native to parts of Europe and Asia, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces small, heart-shaped leaves and distinctive violet flowers with a purple hue. It is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.

Viola delphinantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
