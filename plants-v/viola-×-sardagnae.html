Viola × sardagnae

Viola × sardagnae, commonly known as the Sardagnae violet, is a hybrid species in the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, often violet or purple flowers. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a trailing or upright growth form depending on the cultivar. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated as a decorative garden plant.

Viola × sardagnae is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
