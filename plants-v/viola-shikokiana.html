Viola shikokiana

Viola shikokiana, commonly known as the Shikoku violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and delicate, showy flowers. The flowers typically bloom in spring, displaying shades of violet, purple, and white. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
