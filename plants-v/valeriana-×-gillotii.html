Valeriana × gillotii

Valeriana × gillotii, commonly known as the hybrid valerian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, often fragrant foliage. The plant typically features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. Valeriana × gillotii is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for its calming properties.

Its growth habit is bushy, with a compact form that makes it suitable for gardens and landscapes.
