Vincetoxicum linifolium

Vincetoxicum linifolium, commonly known as the slender milkweed, is a flowering plant native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, pale green to white flowers that bloom in late summer. It is often found in moist, open woodlands and along stream banks.
