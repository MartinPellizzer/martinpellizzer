Valerianoides jamaicensis var. linearifolia

Valerianoides jamaicensis var. linearifolia, commonly known as Jamaican valerian, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean region.

It belongs to the family Valerianaceae and is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its root system is fibrous and often used in traditional medicine for its calming properties.

The species is noted for its aromatic compounds, which contribute to its use in herbal remedies.
