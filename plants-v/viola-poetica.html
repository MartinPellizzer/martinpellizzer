Viola poetica

Viola poetica, commonly known as the poetic violet, is a flowering plant belonging to the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and delicate, often purple or violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland areas or garden beds. Its flowers bloom in early spring, adding a touch of color to the landscape.

Viola poetica is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical use in herbal medicine.
