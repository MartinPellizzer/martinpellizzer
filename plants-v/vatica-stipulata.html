Vatica stipulata

Vatica stipulata, commonly known as the stipulate tamanu, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Rubiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and elliptical leaves with prominent stipules. The tree thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland and hill forests.
