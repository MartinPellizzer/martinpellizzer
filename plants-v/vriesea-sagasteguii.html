Vriesea sagasteguii

Vriesea sagasteguii, commonly known as the Sagastegui's Vriesea, is a species of bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful inflorescence that typically displays vibrant red, orange, and yellow hues. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that collect water, forming a central pool that supports small aquatic life. Its flowering period occurs during the warmer months, and it thrives in bright, indirect light with high humidity.

Vriesea sagasteguii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
