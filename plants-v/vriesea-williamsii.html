Vriesea williamsii

Vriesea williamsii, commonly known as the Williams' Vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, spathaceous inflorescence that can range from red to orange, often with a contrasting green or yellow center. The plant features rosettes of narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy coating. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Vriesea williamsii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
