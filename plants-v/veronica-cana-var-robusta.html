Veronica cana var. robusta

Veronica cana var. robusta, commonly known as the robust Veronica, is a flowering plant belonging to the Plantaginaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance. Native to Europe and western Asia, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and easy care requirements.
