Valeriana leptonema

Valeriana leptonema, commonly known as slender valerian, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Valeriana leptonema is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its calming properties.
