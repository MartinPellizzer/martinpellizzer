Vernonia moluccensis

Vernonia moluccensis, commonly known as Moluccan ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Moluccas in Indonesia.

It belongs to the sunflower family, Asteraceae, and is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, while the stems are often reddish in color.

Vernonia moluccensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in tropical gardens.
