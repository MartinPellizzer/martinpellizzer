Veronica bellidioides

Veronica bellidioides, commonly known as the sand veronica, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant produces small, blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms.
