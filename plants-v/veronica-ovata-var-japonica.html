Veronica ovata var. japonica

Veronica ovata var. japonica, commonly known as Japanese speedwell, is a perennial flowering plant native to East Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. The leaves are lance-shaped and arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

This plant is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
