Viola rudolfii

Viola rudolfii, commonly known as the Rudolf violet, is a species of flowering plant in the Violaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central America, including parts of Mexico and Guatemala. The plant features heart-shaped, velvety leaves and produces showy violet flowers with distinctive yellow markings. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in woodland or rocky habitats.

Viola rudolfii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
