Viridantha grandispica

Viridantha grandispica, commonly known as the grandispica orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in clusters. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves and produces fragrant, violet-colored blooms that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil for optimal growth.
