Vangueria glabra

Vangueria glabra, commonly known as the smooth vangueria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are often hairy on the underside. Its small, white flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, and it produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds.

Vangueria glabra is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showing resilience to drought conditions.
