Vernonia fargesii

Vernonia fargesii, commonly known as the Chinese ironweed, is a flowering plant native to China and parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments and can reach heights of up to 2 meters. Vernonia fargesii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
