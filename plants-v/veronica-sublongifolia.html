Veronica sublongifolia

Veronica sublongifolia, commonly known as the long-leaved speedwell, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves and clusters of small blue or purple flowers.
