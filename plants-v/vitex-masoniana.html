Vitex masoniana

Vitex masoniana, commonly known as the Hawaiian hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from white to pink and violet. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a hairy texture.

Vitex masoniana thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in coastal areas and dry forests.
