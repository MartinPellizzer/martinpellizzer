Ventilago harmandiana

Ventilago harmandiana, commonly known as the Harmand's velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruits are capsules that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Ventilago harmandiana is often found in moist, shaded areas and is used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
