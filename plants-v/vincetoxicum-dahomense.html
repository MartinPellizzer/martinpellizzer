Vincetoxicum dahomense

Vincetoxicum dahomense, commonly known as the African snakeroot, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 3 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves with a rough, hairy texture. Its flowers are small, clustered in cymes, and are usually white or pale yellow in color.

The plant is known for its medicinal properties and has been traditionally used in herbal remedies.
