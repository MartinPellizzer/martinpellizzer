Vitis × kozmae var. zemplenica

Vitis × kozmae var. zemplenica, commonly known as the Zemplén grape, is a hybrid grapevine native to Hungary.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and is typically cultivated for its wine-making potential. The plant produces small, dark-colored berries that are rich in flavor and aroma. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its adaptability and contribution to local viticulture.
