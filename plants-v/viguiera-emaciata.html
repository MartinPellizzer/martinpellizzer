Viguiera emaciata

Viguiera emaciata, commonly known as the slender ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States and parts of Mexico.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and aromatic foliage. Its showy purple to pink flower heads bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
