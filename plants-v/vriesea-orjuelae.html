Vriesea orjuelae

Vriesea orjuelae, commonly known as the Orjuela Vriesea, is a species of flowering plant native to South America, specifically Colombia.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its striking, long-lasting inflorescence that can be red, orange, or yellow. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that collect water, forming a central reservoir.
