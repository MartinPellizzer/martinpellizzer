Vernonia miombicoloides

Vernonia miombicoloides, commonly known as the miombo vernonia, is a flowering plant native to eastern and southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and woodlands. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in the dry season, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough or hairy texture.
