Vriesea calimaniana

Vriesea calimaniana, commonly known as the Calimanian Vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, sword-shaped leaves that form a rosette structure, which helps collect water in its natural habitat. The plant produces striking inflorescences with colorful, long-lasting flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in high humidity and bright, indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is often cultivated for both horticultural and conservation purposes.
