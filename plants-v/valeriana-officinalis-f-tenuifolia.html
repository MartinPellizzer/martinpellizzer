Valeriana officinalis f. tenuifolia

Valeriana officinalis f. tenuifolia, commonly known as slender valerian, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caprifoliaceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its thin, wiry stems and small, white or greenish flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and opposite, and it produces small, berry-like fruits that contain the seeds.

Valeriana officinalis f. tenuifolia is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
