Viola sylvestris f. acaulis

Viola sylvestris f. acaulis, commonly known as the slender violet, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to Europe.

It features upright, hairy stems that bear small, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces delicate violet flowers with purple petals and yellow markings, typically blooming in early spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for rock gardens and woodland landscapes.
