Vulvaria vulgaris

Vulvaria vulgaris, commonly known as common vulvaria, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Vulvaria vulgaris is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported diuretic and anti-inflammatory properties.
