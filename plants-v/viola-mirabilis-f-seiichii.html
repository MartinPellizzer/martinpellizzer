Viola mirabilis f. seiichii

Viola mirabilis f. seiichii, commonly known as the Japanese violet, is a striking hybrid plant originating from Japan.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple, violet, and occasionally white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a velvety texture, typical of the Viola genus. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial to full shade.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often grown in gardens and as a houseplant.
