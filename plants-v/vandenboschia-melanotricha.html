Vandenboschia melanotricha

Vandenboschia melanotricha, commonly known as the black-haired vandenboschia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are covered in dense, dark brown to black hairs, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer, adding a subtle floral element to its otherwise spiny form. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing on rocky outcrops or in well-drained soils.
