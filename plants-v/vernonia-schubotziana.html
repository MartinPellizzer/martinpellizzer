Vernonia schubotziana

Vernonia schubotziana, commonly known as the Schubotz's ironweed, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Vernonia schubotziana is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
