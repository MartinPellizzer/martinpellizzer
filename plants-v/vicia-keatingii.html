Vicia keatingii

Vicia keatingii, commonly known as Keating's vetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial legume that typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to purple. Its seed pods are slender and contain several elliptical seeds.

Vicia keatingii is valued for its role in nitrogen fixation and its potential use in ecological restoration projects.
