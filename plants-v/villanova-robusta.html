Villanova robusta

Villanova robusta, commonly known as the robusta vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry containing several seeds.

Due to its vigorous growth habit, it is often used in ornamental landscaping and as a living fence.
