Vernonia linariifolia

Vernonia linariifolia, commonly known as the slender ironweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its medicinal properties in some traditional cultures.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking drought-tolerant plants.
