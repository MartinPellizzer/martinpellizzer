Vulpia broteri

Vulpia broteri, commonly known as the broter's veldtgrass, is a species of grass native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial plant that typically forms dense mats on the ground. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and sandy soils.

Vulpia broteri plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
