Verbena hunzikeri

Verbena hunzikeri, commonly known as the Hunziker's verbena, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a member of the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in shrubland and coastal areas, preferring well-drained soil and full sun. It has slender, branching stems and small, opposite leaves.

Verbena hunzikeri is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
