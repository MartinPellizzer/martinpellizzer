Vachellia hindsii

Vachellia hindsii, commonly known as the Hinds' acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant features bipinnate leaves and produces yellow, spherical flower heads that attract various pollinators. Its wood is hard and durable, making it valuable for local use.

Vachellia hindsii is often found in savannas and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils.
