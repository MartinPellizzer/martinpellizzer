Vriesea naundorffiae

Vriesea naundorffiae, commonly known as the Naundorff's Vriesea, is a species of flowering plant in the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its striking, long-lasting inflorescence that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The plant features a rosette of broad, glossy, dark green leaves that are often adorned with silver or white markings. It produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of orange, red, and yellow, which bloom sequentially along the inflorescence.

Vriesea naundorffiae is a popular ornamental plant due to its dramatic appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
