Vriesea blackburniana

Vriesea blackburniana, commonly known as the Blackburn's Vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, spiky inflorescence that bears vibrant, long-lasting orange to red flowers. The plant has a rosette of broad, dark green leaves that are often adorned with white or silver markings. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Vriesea blackburniana is popular as a ornamental plant due to its dramatic appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
