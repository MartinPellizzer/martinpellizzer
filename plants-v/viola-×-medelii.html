Viola × medelii

Viola × medelii, commonly known as the hybrid violet, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Violaceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between Viola tricolor and Viola wittrockii, resulting in a plant with distinctive, often colorful flowers. The leaves are heart-shaped and slightly hairy, while the flowers typically display a combination of purple, yellow, and white petals. This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is commonly cultivated in gardens.

It prefers moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a versatile addition to shady garden areas.
