Vigna membranacea subsp. membranacea

Vigna membranacea subsp.

membranacea, commonly known as the slender cowpea, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions. It features slender, twining stems and trifoliate leaves with small, ovate leaflets.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers followed by elongated, slender pods containing kidney-shaped seeds. It is adapted to warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This subspecies is valued for its edible seeds and is often cultivated as a drought-tolerant crop.
