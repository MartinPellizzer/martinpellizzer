Vanda mariae

Vanda mariae, commonly known as the Marie's vanda, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink with yellow or orange markings. The plant has long, slender roots and thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its natural habitat.

Vanda mariae is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
