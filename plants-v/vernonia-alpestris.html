Vernonia alpestris

Vernonia alpestris, commonly known as alpine ironweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in open, rocky, or alpine environments. The plant features erect, branched stems covered in fine hairs and lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. Its showy yellow flower heads appear in late summer, attracting various pollinators.

Vernonia alpestris is valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
