Viola × holsatica

Viola × holsatica, commonly known as the Siberian viola, is a hybrid species within the Violaceae family.

It is native to regions of Siberia and Central Asia, where it thrives in cold, temperate climates. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves and produces clusters of violet or purple flowers. It is often found in moist, well-drained soils and can grow in partial to full shade.

Viola × holsatica is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in cooler environments.
