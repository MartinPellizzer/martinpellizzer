Vandenboschia davallioides

Vandenboschia davallioides, commonly known as the sand mat, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, cushion-like growth form composed of closely packed, fleshy leaves. The leaves are typically green or bluish-green, with a waxy surface that helps reduce water loss. It thrives in well-drained sandy soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

This plant is often used in xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and aesthetic appeal.
