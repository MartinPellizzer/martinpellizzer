Virectaria multiflora

Virectaria multiflora, commonly known as the common virectaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its dense, bushy growth habit and produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Virectaria multiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
