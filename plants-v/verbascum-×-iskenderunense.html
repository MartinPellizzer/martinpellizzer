Verbascum × iskenderunense

Verbascum × iskenderunense, commonly known as the Iskenderun mullein.

It is a hybrid species belonging to the genus Verbascum, which includes several species of flowering plants in the family Scrophulariaceae. This plant is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, velvety leaves that are often used medicinally. It produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn.

Native to the region of Iskenderun in southern Turkey, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.
