Vaccinium moultonii

Vaccinium moultonii, commonly known as Moulton's blueberry, is a species of shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes other familiar plants like blueberries and cranberries. The plant produces small, edible berries that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.
