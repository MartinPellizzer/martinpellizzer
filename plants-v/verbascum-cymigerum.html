Verbascum cymigerum

Verbascum cymigerum, commonly known as the clustered mullein, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, hairy stems and large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant belongs to the genus Verbascum, which includes many species known for their woolly foliage. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges.

Verbascum cymigerum is also valued for its medicinal properties, with historical use in treating respiratory ailments.
