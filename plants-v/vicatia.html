Vicatia

Vicatia, commonly known as the tropical milkweed, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange to red flowers, which bloom throughout the year in warm climates. The plant has heart-shaped, velvety leaves and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes. Vicatia is also known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional remedies.

Its ability to attract butterflies and other pollinators makes it an important plant in ecological conservation efforts.
