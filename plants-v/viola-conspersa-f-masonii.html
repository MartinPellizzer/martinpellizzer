Viola conspersa f. masonii

Viola conspersa f. masonii, commonly known as the Mason's Violet, is a hybrid species within the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant violet to purple flowers with dark markings. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, downy hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This cultivar is often grown for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
