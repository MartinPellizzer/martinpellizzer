Virola macrocarpa

Virola macrocarpa, commonly known as the large-fruited virola, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The tree produces heavy, woody fruits that can weigh several kilograms when mature. The leaves are usually large, simple, and alternate, with a rough texture.

It is valued for its durable wood and is also used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
