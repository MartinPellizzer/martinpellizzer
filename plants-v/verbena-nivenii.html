Verbena nivenii

Verbena nivenii, commonly known as the Sierra Madre verbena, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces vibrant clusters of blue to purple flowers, which bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Verbena nivenii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
