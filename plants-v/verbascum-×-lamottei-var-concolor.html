Verbascum × lamottei var. concolor

Verbascum × lamottei var. concolor, commonly known as the smooth mullein, is a hybrid plant belonging to the genus Verbascum, which is part of the family Scrophulariaceae.

It is characterized by its large, velvety, gray-green leaves that are covered in dense hairs, providing a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to September. It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soils in full sun.

This variety is noted for its smooth leaf surface, distinguishing it from other varieties in the same species group.
