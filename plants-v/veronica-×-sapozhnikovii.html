Veronica × sapozhnikovii

Veronica × sapozhnikovii, commonly known as the hybrid speedwell, is a perennial plant belonging to the Plantaginaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of blue to violet flowers. The plant typically reaches a height of 10–30 cm and features narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Veronica × sapozhnikovii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its attractive blooms.
