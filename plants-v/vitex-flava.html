Vitex flava

Vitex flava, commonly known as golden vitex, is a deciduous shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are often yellow-green in color, contributing to its ornamental value. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range from white to pale yellow, typically blooming in summer. Its fruit is a dry capsule containing several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Vitex flava thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
