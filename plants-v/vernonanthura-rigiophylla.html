Vernonanthura rigiophylla

Vernonanthura rigiophylla, commonly known as the rigid-leaf vernonanthura, is a unique flowering plant native to specific regions of Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect growth habit and distinctive, rigid, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale blue, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for cultivation in arid or semi-arid environments.
