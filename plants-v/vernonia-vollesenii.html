Vernonia vollesenii

Vernonia vollesenii, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant is notable for its hard, dense wood, which is highly valued for construction and tool-making. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in clusters, and it thrives in dry, rocky habitats.

Vernonia vollesenii is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
