Viola × semseyana

Viola × semseyana, commonly known as the semseyan violet, is a hybrid species within the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, pansy-like flowers that come in a variety of colors, including purple, blue, and white. The plant typically grows to a height of 10–20 cm and thrives in cool, moist environments. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and hardiness.

Viola × semseyana prefers partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden borders and containers.
