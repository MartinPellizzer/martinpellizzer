Vanilla × tahitensis var. tiarei

Vanilla × tahitensis var. tiarei, commonly known as Tahitian vanilla, is a hybrid orchid native to the Society Islands in French Polynesia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pendulous flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a distinctive violet or purple hue. The plant produces a highly aromatic vanilla pod, which is used in culinary and perfumery applications. It thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and partial shade.

This variety is prized for its unique flavor profile and is often cultivated for both commercial and ornamental purposes.
