Varronia grandiflora var. glabrata

Varronia grandiflora var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth varronia, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its woody stems and dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces showy, yellowish-white flowers that are typically clustered in terminal panicles. Its fruits are small, round, and covered in a rough, spiny husk.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
