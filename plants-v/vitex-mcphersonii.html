Vitex mcphersonii

Vitex mcphersonii, commonly known as the McPherson's chaste tree, is a species of flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows in rocky or arid environments. The plant produces showy, fragrant purple or blue flowers in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a rough texture with a silvery underside.

Vitex mcphersonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to harsh conditions.
