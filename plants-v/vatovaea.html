Vatovaea

Vatovaea, commonly known as the Madagascar orchid, is a genus of flowering plants native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, often colorful flowers. The plants typically have a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Vatovaea species are known for their showy, orchid-like blooms that can vary in shape and color.
