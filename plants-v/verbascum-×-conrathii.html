Verbascum × conrathii

Verbascum × conrathii, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the genus Verbascum, which includes species known for their large, fuzzy leaves and showy yellow or orange flowers. The hybrid is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of elongated, velvety leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil.
