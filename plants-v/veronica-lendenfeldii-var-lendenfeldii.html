Veronica lendenfeldii var. lendenfeldii

Veronica lendenfeldii var. lendenfeldii, commonly known as the Lendenfeld's speedwell, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. The plant features opposite leaves that are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant blooms and drought tolerance.
