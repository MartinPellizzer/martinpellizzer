Vangueria adenodonta

Vangueria adenodonta, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its attractive, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with red or purple markings. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and elliptical, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Vangueria adenodonta is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.
