Viola okuboi

Viola okuboi, commonly known as the Japanese violet, is a species of flowering plant native to Japan.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 15-20 centimeters. The plant produces showy violet-colored flowers with five petals, often with a distinctive dark purple or black marking at the base. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Viola okuboi thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas or shaded gardens.
