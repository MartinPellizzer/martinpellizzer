Vitis kleinii

Vitis kleinii, commonly known as Klein's grape, is a species of flowering plant in the Vitaceae family.

It is native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa, particularly Namibia and South Africa. The plant is characterized by its robust vines, palmately lobed leaves, and small, clustered white flowers. Its fruits are small, round, and typically green, with a thin skin.

Vitis kleinii is adapted to withstand drought and high temperatures, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
