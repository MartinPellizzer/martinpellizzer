Viola nilagirica

Viola nilagirica, commonly known as the Nilagiri violet, is a species of flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and delicate, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments at high altitudes. Its flowers bloom in spring and are often used in traditional medicine.

Viola nilagirica is valued for its ornamental beauty and has cultural significance in certain regions.
