Vismia laurentii

Vismia laurentii, commonly known as the African snowball, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant is characterized by its dense clusters of white, waxy flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Vismia laurentii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well-suited to warm, sunny climates.
