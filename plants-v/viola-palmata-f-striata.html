Viola palmata f. striata

Viola palmata f. striata, commonly known as the striped pansy, is a striking hybrid of the common violet.

This plant is characterized by its distinctive, heart-shaped leaves with a striking green and purple coloration. The flowers are typically violet or purple, with a unique striated pattern that gives it its common name. It thrives in cool climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The plant is a member of the Violaceae family and is valued for its hardy nature and vibrant blooms.
