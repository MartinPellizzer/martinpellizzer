Vegaea

Vegaea, commonly known as the African violet, is a popular flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the genus Saintpaulia and is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, clustered flowers in shades of purple, blue, and white. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Vegaea is also known for its ability to bloom year-round under optimal conditions.
