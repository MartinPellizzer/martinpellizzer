Verbascum × pyramidale

Verbascum × pyramidale, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a plant that combines traits from both Verbascum species.

It is a tall, biennial or short-lived perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region. The plant is characterized by its large, fuzzy, gray-green leaves and showy yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
