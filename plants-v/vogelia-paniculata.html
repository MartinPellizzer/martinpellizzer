Vogelia paniculata

Vogelia paniculata, commonly known as the panicled vogelia, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough texture.

Vogelia paniculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
