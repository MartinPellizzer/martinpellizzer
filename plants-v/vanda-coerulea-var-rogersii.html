Vanda coerulea var. rogersii

Vanda coerulea var. rogersii, commonly known as the blue vanda, is a hybrid orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue-violet flowers, which are typically borne on tall, branched inflorescences. The plant features strap-like, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright indirect light for optimal growth.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
