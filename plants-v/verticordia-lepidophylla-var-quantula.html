Verticordia lepidophylla var. quantula

Verticordia lepidophylla var. quantula, commonly known as the Quantula featherflower, is a unique plant native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, feathery inflorescences that range in color from red to pink. The plant thrives in sandy soils and is typically found in heathland environments. It has small, narrow leaves and produces small, delicate flowers that bloom in spring.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and conservation-dependent species.
