Veronica strictiflora

Veronica strictiflora, commonly known as the upright speedwell, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that features small, blue or violet flowers arranged in dense clusters. The plant has narrow, upright leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact growth habit and attractive blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
