Vidalasia

Vidalasia, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its striking, pendulous yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, legume-like pods that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Vidalasia thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its foliage and flowers make it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical and subtropical regions.
