Vriesea Chontalensis

Vriesea chontalensis, scientifically known as Vriesea chontalensis, is a striking bromeliad native to the rainforests of Central America. This unique plant is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting inflorescence that can reach several feet in height. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on tree trunks or among leaf litter in tropical regions. Due to its ornamental value, it has gained popularity among plant enthusiasts and collectors. However, its habitat is increasingly threatened by deforestation and climate change.
