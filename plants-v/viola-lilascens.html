Viola lilascens

Viola lilascens, commonly known as the pale violet, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of Asia and Europe.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces delicate, pale violet flowers with a distinctive yellow center. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade. Its flowers bloom in early spring, adding a subtle touch of color to woodland gardens.

Viola lilascens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native plant landscaping.
