Veronica billardierei

Veronica billardierei, commonly known as the slender speedwell, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its delicate blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and heathlands. It has slender, erect stems with opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Veronica billardierei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
