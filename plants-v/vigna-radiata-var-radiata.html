Vigna radiata var. radiata

Vigna radiata var. radiata, commonly known as the urd bean, is a leguminous plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and trifoliate leaves with small, hairy stipules. The plant produces elongated pods that contain several small, kidney-shaped seeds. It is well adapted to tropical and subtropical climates and thrives in well-drained soils.

Vigna radiata var. radiata is widely cultivated for its nutritional seeds, which are rich in protein and used in various cuisines.
