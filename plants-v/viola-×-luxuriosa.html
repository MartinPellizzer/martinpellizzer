Viola × luxuriosa

Viola × luxuriosa, commonly known as the hybrid violet, is a species resulting from the crossbreeding of various viola species.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a range of colors including purple, blue, and white. The plant typically grows to a height of 10–30 cm and features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.

Viola × luxuriosa is a perennial herbaceous plant with a fibrous root system and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
