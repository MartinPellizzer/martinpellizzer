Vallisneria gigantea

Vallisneria gigantea, commonly known as giant waterweed, is a submerged aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features long, ribbon-like leaves that can grow up to several meters in length, often floating at the water's surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically found in the water column. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments with ample sunlight.

Vallisneria gigantea plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and oxygen for various aquatic organisms.
