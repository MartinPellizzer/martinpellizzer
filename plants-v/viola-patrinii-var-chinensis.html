Viola patrinii var. chinensis

Viola patrinii var. chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese violet, is a species native to eastern Asia.

It is a perennial herb with heart-shaped, hairy leaves that are typically dark green. The plant produces showy violet flowers with five petals, often in shades of purple or blue. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forested areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and traditional herbal use.
