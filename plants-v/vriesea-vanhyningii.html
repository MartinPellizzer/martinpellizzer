Vriesea vanhyningii

Vriesea vanhyningii, commonly known as the Vanhyning's Vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a central cup, which collects water and nutrients. The plant produces strikingly colorful inflorescences with vibrant orange and red bracts. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Vriesea vanhyningii is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
