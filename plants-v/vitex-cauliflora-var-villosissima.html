Vitex cauliflora var. villosissima

Vitex cauliflora var. villosissima, commonly known as the hairy vitex, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers. The plant produces woody, cylindrical fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in lowland forests or along stream banks.

Its ornamental value and medicinal properties make it a notable species in botanical studies.
