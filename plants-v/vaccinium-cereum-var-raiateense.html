Vaccinium cereum var. raiateense

Vaccinium cereum var. raiateense, commonly known as the Raiatea huckleberry, is a shrub native to the Society Islands in French Polynesia.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its small, evergreen leaves and clusters of bell-shaped flowers.
