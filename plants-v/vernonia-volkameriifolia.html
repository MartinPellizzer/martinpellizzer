Vernonia volkameriifolia

Vernonia volkameriifolia, commonly known as the Volkameria vernonia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and lance-shaped, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, often appearing in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for cultivation in warm, humid climates.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally grown in gardens and botanical collections.
