Vernonia vauthieriana

Vernonia vauthieriana, commonly known as the Vauthier's ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean, particularly Jamaica.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Vernonia vauthieriana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, supporting local pollinators.
