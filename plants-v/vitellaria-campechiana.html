Vitellaria campechiana

Vitellaria campechiana, commonly known as shea tree, is a tropical tree native to Africa, particularly West Africa.

It is valued for its fruit, which contains a nutritious seed used to produce shea butter. The plant has a dense, evergreen canopy and can grow up to 20 meters tall. Its leaves are alternate and simple, with a rough texture.

The tree plays an important ecological and economic role in the regions where it is found.
