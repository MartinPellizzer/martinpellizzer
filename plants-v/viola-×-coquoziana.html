Viola × coquoziana

Viola × coquoziana, commonly known as the hybrid violet, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Violaceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between Viola lutea and Viola verecunda, characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant flower colors. The plant typically produces heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and blooms in shades of purple, violet, and white. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas across its native range.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and flowers.
