Veronica calycina var. gunnii

Veronica calycina var. gunnii, commonly known as the New Zealand forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
