Viola corallensis

Viola corallensis, commonly known as the coral viola, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces clusters of delicate, violet-blue flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens and woodland settings.

Viola corallensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
