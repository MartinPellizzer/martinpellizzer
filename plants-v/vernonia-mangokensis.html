Vernonia mangokensis

Vernonia mangokensis, commonly known as the Mangoky vernonia, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and has adapted to sandy soils and salt spray. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are small and yellowish.
