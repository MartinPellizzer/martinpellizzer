Vadia

Vadia, commonly known as the Indian nettle or *Urena lobata*, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family, which includes hibiscus and cotton. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces small, pale yellow flowers. Vadia is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

It is also used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
