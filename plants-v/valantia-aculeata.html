Valantia aculeata

Valantia aculeata, commonly known as the spiny broom.

It is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth habit and its clusters of small, yellowish flowers. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough texture.

Valantia aculeata is typically found in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in well-drained soils and full sun.
