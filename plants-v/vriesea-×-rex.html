Vriesea × rex

Vriesea × rex, commonly known as the hybrid bromeliad, is a captivating plant native to South America.

It is a hybrid between Vriesea and another bromeliad species, resulting in its unique appearance. The plant features a rosette of broad, waxy leaves that can vary in color from green to bronze or even red. It produces striking inflorescences with vibrant, long-lasting flowers in shades of orange, red, or yellow.

Vriesea × rex is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
