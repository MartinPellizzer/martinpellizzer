Valeriana officinalis var. exaltata

Valeriana officinalis var. exaltata, commonly known as the tall valerian, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features upright, branching stems that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height, adorned with small, greenish-white flowers. The plant has aromatic, dark green leaves with a distinctive, slightly serrated edge. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

Valeriana officinalis var. exaltata is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly for its calming effects.
