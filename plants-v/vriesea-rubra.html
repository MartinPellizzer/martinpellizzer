Vriesea rubra

Vriesea rubra, commonly known as the red vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, red-orange bracts that surround the central inflorescence, giving it a dramatic appearance. The plant features a rosette of narrow, dark green leaves that are often lined with white or silver markings. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity to flourish.

Vriesea rubra is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
