Vanilla laurentiana var. gilletii

Vanilla laurentiana var. gilletii, commonly known as the giant vanilla or giant orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a climbing orchid that can grow up to several meters in length, with broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces fragrant, yellowish-green flowers that are typically followed by long, curved pods. Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and ecological role in its native habitat.

It is also valued for its potential in horticulture and conservation efforts.
