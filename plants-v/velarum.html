Velarum

Velarum, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the family Malvaceae.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Its rapid growth and invasive tendencies make it a significant weed in agricultural and natural ecosystems.
