Virola elongata var. longicuspis

Virola elongata var. longicuspis, commonly known as the long-toothed virola, is a member of the Myristicaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and potential ecological roles in its native habitats.
