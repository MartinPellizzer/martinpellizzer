Veronicastrum latifolium

Veronicastrum latifolium, commonly known as blueweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a tall, herbaceous perennial that typically grows up to 2 meters in height. The plant features slender, cylindrical stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often bluish-green in color. Its showy, spike-like inflorescence consists of small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer.

Veronicastrum latifolium is often found in moist soils along stream banks and in wet meadows.
