Viola × bachtschisaraensis

Viola × bachtschisaraensis, commonly known as the Bachtschisaraensis violet, is a hybrid species within the Viola genus.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, often purple or violet-colored flowers. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to garden settings.
