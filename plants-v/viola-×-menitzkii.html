Viola × menitzkii

Viola × menitzkii, commonly known as the Dutch viola, is a hybrid species in the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows to a height of 10–20 cm and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is native to Europe and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and flowers.

Viola × menitzkii is also valued for its ability to naturalize and spread through self-seeding.
