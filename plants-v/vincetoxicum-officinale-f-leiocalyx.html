Vincetoxicum officinale f. leiocalyx

Vincetoxicum officinale f. leiocalyx, commonly known as the broad-leaved iron vine, is a variant of the common vincetoxicum.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its heart-shaped, broad leaves and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant produces elliptical, woody fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in the Rubiaceae family.
