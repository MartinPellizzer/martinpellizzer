Vriesea macropoda

Vriesea macropoda, commonly known as the giant vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, broad, and often colorful leaves that form a rosette shape. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with showy, long-lasting flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Vriesea macropoda is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
