Viola marcelorosasii

Viola marcelorosasii, commonly known as the Marcello's violet, is a species of flowering plant in the Violaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe, particularly in Italy and Spain. The plant features heart-shaped, velvety leaves and produces showy, violet-blue flowers with yellow centers. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in woodland understories.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
