Vitis mengziensis

Vitis mengziensis, commonly known as Mengzi grapevine, is a rare and lesser-known species of flowering vine native to China.

It belongs to the Vitaceae family, which includes other well-known grapevines and climbers. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, heart-shaped leaves, and small, greenish-white flowers. It is often found in mountainous regions with moist, shaded environments.

Due to its limited distribution and unique morphology, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
