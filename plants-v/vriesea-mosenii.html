Vriesea mosenii

Vriesea mosenii, commonly known as the Mosenii Vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, spathaceous inflorescence that displays a mix of red, orange, and yellow hues. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that collect water, creating a natural reservoir. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Vriesea mosenii is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
