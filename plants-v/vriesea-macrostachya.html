Vriesea macrostachya

Vriesea macrostachya, commonly known as the giant vriesea, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant produces impressive, long-lasting inflorescences with bright, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in warm, humid conditions and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Vriesea macrostachya is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
