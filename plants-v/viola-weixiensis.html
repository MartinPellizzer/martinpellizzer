Viola weixiensis

Viola weixiensis, commonly known as the Weixien violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its flowers bloom in spring, attracting various pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Viola weixiensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also studied for its potential medicinal properties.
