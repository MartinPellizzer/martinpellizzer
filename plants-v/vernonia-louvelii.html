Vernonia louvelii

Vernonia louvelii, commonly known as the Louvel's ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean, particularly Cuba and the Bahamas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Vernonia louvelii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and attractive blooms.
