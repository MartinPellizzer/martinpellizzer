Veronica barrelieri subsp. nitens

Veronica barrelieri subsp.

nitens, commonly known as the silver veronica, is a perennial flowering plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its narrow, silvery-gray leaves and clusters of small, blue or purple flowers.

The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and low-maintenance gardens.
