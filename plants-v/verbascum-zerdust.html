Verbascum zerdust

Verbascum zerdust, commonly known as Persian mullein, is a flowering plant native to regions of Central Asia, including parts of Afghanistan and Pakistan.

It belongs to the Scrophulariaceae family and is characterized by its tall, hairy stems and large, fuzzy leaves. The plant produces showy yellow or orange flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in mountainous areas.
