Viola portalesia var. portalesia

Viola portalesia var. portalesia, commonly known as the Portalesia violet, is a species native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial with heart-shaped, dark green leaves and clusters of violet-blue flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 15-30 centimeters and thrives in cool, moist environments. Its flowers bloom in early spring and are pollinated primarily by bees and butterflies.

The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and delicate appearance.
