Vermeulenia papilionacea var. aegaea

Vermeulenia papilionacea var. aegaea, commonly known as the Aegean vermeulenia, is a flowering plant native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, butterfly-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its drought tolerance.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
