Vigia

Vigia, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other composite flowers. The plant features showy yellow or orange flowers surrounded by white ray florets, creating a striking appearance. Vigia typically grows in open, sunny habitats and is often found in grasslands and along roadsides.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
