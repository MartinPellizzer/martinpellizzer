Veronica spuria var. croatica

Veronica spuria var. croatica, commonly known as the Croatian speedwell, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its small, blue or purple flowers arranged in clusters.
