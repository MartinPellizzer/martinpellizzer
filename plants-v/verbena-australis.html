Verbena australis

Verbena australis, commonly known as Australian verbena, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of blue, purple, and white. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

Verbena australis is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
