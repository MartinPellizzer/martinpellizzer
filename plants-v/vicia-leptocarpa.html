Vicia leptocarpa

Vicia leptocarpa, commonly known as the slender vetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to parts of Europe and Asia, often found in grassy areas and along roadsides. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

Vicia leptocarpa is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it valuable in soil enrichment.
