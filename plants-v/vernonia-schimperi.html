Vernonia schimperi

Vernonia schimperi, commonly known as the Ethiopian ironweed, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in highland regions with well-drained soils. The plant produces small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a rough texture.

Vernonia schimperi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
