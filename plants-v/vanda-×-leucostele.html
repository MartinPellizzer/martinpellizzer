Vanda × leucostele

Vanda × leucostele, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a fascinating plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is a cross between Vanda coerulea and Vanda leucostele, resulting in a unique blend of traits from both parent species. This orchid is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of other plants without being parasitic, and it thrives in humid, tropical environments. Its flowers are typically large, showy, and come in a variety of colors, including white, blue, and purple.

Vanda × leucostele requires bright light, good air circulation, and well-draining potting media to flourish.
