Vandenboschia fargesii

Vandenboschia fargesii, commonly known as the Yunnan sandmat, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming structure. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
