Vriesea scalaris var. viridis

Vriesea scalaris var. viridis, commonly known as the Green Vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It features a compact rosette of narrow, dark green leaves that form a central cup to collect rainwater. The plant produces tall, branching inflorescences with bright orange flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil with moderate watering.

Its compact size and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
