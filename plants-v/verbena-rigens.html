Verbena rigens

Verbena rigens, commonly known as rigid verbena, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features slender, upright stems and produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of blue, purple, and pink. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, and it thrives in full sun with well-drained soil.

Verbena rigens is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
