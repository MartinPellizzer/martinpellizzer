Villadia paniculata

Villadia paniculata, commonly known as the panicle veldtgrass, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial grass that typically grows in open grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant features tall, slender stems with whorled leaves and produces panicles of small, greenish flowers. It is adapted to dry conditions and is often found in savanna ecosystems.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
