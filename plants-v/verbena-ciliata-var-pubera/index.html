Verbena Ciliata Var. Pubera

Verbena ciliata var. pubera is a flowering plant known for its vibrant purple blooms and compact growth habit. Native to the southeastern united states, it thrives in sunny, well-drained soils. This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. It is often used in gardens and landscapes for its hardiness and low maintenance requirements. Its hairy leaves and showy flowers make it a unique addition to any floral display.
