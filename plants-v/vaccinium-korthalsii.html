Vaccinium korthalsii

Vaccinium korthalsii, commonly known as the Korthals huckleberry, is a species of evergreen shrub native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes many acid-loving plants such as blueberries and cranberries. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in forested areas, often in association with coniferous trees.
