Volkensteinia gigantophylla

Volkensteinia gigantophylla, commonly known as the giant-leaf volkensteinia, is a rare and striking plant characterized by its enormous, broad leaves.

It is native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes, where it thrives in cool, moist environments. The plant's foliage is often covered in a dense layer of fine, silvery hairs, which help protect it from intense sunlight and frost. Its flowers, though small, are typically pale green or white, and they appear in clusters during the spring.
