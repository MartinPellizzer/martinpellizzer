Vatica hainanensis var. parvifolia

Vatica hainanensis var. parvifolia, commonly known as the Hainan yellow pod tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to southern China.

It belongs to the Dipterocarpaceae family and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by woody pods containing aromatic seeds. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation areas.
