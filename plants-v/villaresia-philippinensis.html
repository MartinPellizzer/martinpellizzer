Villaresia philippinensis

Villaresia philippinensis, commonly known as the Philippine orchid, is a rare and endemic orchid species native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows in montane forest habitats, where it thrives in shaded, humid conditions. Its roots form symbiotic relationships with mycorrhizal fungi, which aid in nutrient absorption.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species requiring conservation efforts.
