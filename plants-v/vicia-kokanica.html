Vicia kokanica

Vicia kokanica, commonly known as the Kokanica vetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family Fabaceae.

It is native to regions of Central Asia, including parts of Kazakhstan and Uzbekistan. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its flowers are small and usually pink or purple, arranged in dense clusters.

Vicia kokanica is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for soil enrichment in agricultural settings.
