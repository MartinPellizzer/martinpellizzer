Vernonia revoluta

Vernonia revoluta, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dark, hard wood, which is highly valued for its strength and durability. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that appear in clusters, typically during the rainy season.

Vernonia revoluta is also known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional African medicine.
