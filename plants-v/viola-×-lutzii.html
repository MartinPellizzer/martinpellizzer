Viola × lutzii

Viola × lutzii, commonly known as the Lutz viola, is a hybrid species within the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, often purple or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full shade. It is native to regions in Europe and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.

Viola × lutzii is relatively easy to cultivate and can be propagated through seeds or division.
