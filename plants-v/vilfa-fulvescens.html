Vilfa fulvescens

Vilfa fulvescens, commonly known as the yellow star grass, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in star-like clusters. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it has a fibrous root system.

Vilfa fulvescens is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
