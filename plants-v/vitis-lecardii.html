Vitis lecardii

Vitis lecardii, commonly known as the Chinese vine or the lecardii grape, is a species of flowering plant in the Vitaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its climbing habit and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that give way to black, grape-like berries. Vitis lecardii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and scientific study.
