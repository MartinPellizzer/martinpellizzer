Vitex cauliflora

Vitex cauliflora, commonly known as the tropical chaste tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant produces woody fruits that contain several seeds, typical of the Vitex genus. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and horticultural contexts.
