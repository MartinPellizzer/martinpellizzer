Vulneraria heterophylla var. rubriflora

Vulneraria heterophylla var. rubriflora, commonly known as the red-flowered vulneraria, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants, and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers. The species exhibits variable leaf morphology, with some leaves being lobed and others simple, contributing to its common name. Its flowers are typically pink to red in color, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

This plant prefers well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed or open habitats.
