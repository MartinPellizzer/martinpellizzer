Vicia eristalioides

Vicia eristalioides, commonly known as the yellow vetch, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow pea-like flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Vicia eristalioides is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.
