Vernonia buchtienii

Vernonia buchtienii, commonly known as the Buchtien's ironweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in arid or semi-arid regions. The plant produces clusters of small, bright yellow to orange-red flowers, which bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are usually narrow and lance-shaped, with a rough, hairy texture.

Vernonia buchtienii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
