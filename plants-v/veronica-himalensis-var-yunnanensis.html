Veronica himalensis var. yunnanensis

Veronica himalensis var. yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan speedwell, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes, often in alpine meadows. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
