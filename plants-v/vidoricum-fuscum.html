Vidoricum fuscum

Vidoricum fuscum, commonly known as the dark violet, is a member of the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, dark purple flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a bluish-green hue. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in woodland areas.

Vidoricum fuscum is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
