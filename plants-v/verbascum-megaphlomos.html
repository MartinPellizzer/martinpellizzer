Verbascum megaphlomos

Verbascum megaphlomos, commonly known as the giant flax or large-flowered mullein, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are soft, woolly, and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Verbascum megaphlomos thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for dry gardens.
