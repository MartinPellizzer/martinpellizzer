Vicia venosa var. willdenowiana

Vicia venosa var. willdenowiana, commonly known as the slender vetch, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its climbing habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It has a fibrous root system and is often found in disturbed soils, such as along roadsides or in fields.

This variety is notable for its adaptability and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
