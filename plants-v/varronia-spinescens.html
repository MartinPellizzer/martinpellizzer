Varronia spinescens

Varronia spinescens, commonly known as the spiny varronia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its woody stems and small, elliptical leaves with a leathery texture. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by spherical, woody fruits containing several seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in secondary forests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses.
