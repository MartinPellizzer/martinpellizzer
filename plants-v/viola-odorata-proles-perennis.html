Viola odorata proles perennis

Viola odorata proles perennis, commonly known as the sweet violet, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces delicate purple or violet flowers with a sweet fragrance. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial shade. The plant is known for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

Its blooming period typically occurs in spring, adding a touch of color to gardens and natural landscapes.
