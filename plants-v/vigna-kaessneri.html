Vigna kaessneri

Vigna kaessneri, commonly known as the yellow cluster bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its upright growth habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that give way to pod fruits containing edible seeds. Vigna kaessneri is valued for its nutritional content, including high levels of protein and essential amino acids.

It is also cultivated for its ornamental value and used in traditional cuisines across its native range.
