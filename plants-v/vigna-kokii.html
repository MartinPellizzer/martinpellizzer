Vigna kokii

Vigna kokii, commonly known as the African bean or Koki bean, is a species of legume native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and trifoliate leaves, which are typically heart-shaped. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that give way to elongated, green pods containing edible seeds. Vigna kokii is valued for its nutritional content, including high levels of protein and essential minerals.

It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a warm, humid climate for optimal growth.
