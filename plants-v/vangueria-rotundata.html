Vangueria rotundata

Vangueria rotundata, commonly known as the round-leaved vangueria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features small, round, glossy leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. Its flowers are pale yellow and appear in clusters, often blooming in the summer months.

Vangueria rotundata is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
