Voyria macrantha

Voyria macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered voyria, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sunlight. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Voyria macrantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
