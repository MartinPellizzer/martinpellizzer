Valeriana sphaerocephala

Valeriana sphaerocephala, commonly known as the globe valerian, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive spherical inflorescence, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers arranged in a dense, rounded cluster. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

Valeriana sphaerocephala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
