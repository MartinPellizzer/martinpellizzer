Vriesea triflora

Vriesea triflora, commonly known as the Three-flowered Vriesea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful inflorescences. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that collect water, forming a central pool. Its flowers are typically bright red or orange, with three prominent petals.

Vriesea triflora is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its adaptability and ornamental value.
