Velaea tuberosa

Velaea tuberosa, commonly known as the tuberous velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces tuberous roots that are often used in traditional medicine. Velaea tuberosa thrives in moist, warm environments and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological and medicinal studies.
