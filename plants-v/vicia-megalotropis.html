Vicia megalotropis

Vicia megalotropis, commonly known as giant vetch, is a perennial leguminous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features trifoliate leaves with small, rounded leaflets and produces clusters of pea-like, yellow flowers. The plant has a robust root system and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often found in open woodlands and rocky areas.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it valuable for soil enrichment.
