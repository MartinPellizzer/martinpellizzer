Viola kizildaghensis

Viola kizildaghensis, commonly known as the Kizildag violet, is a species of flowering plant native to the Kizildag region in Turkey.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, heart-shaped leaves and small, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of purple and violet. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to the mountainous and semi-arid climate of its native habitat.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Viola kizildaghensis is considered a rare and endangered species.
