Valeriana alypifolia var. elongata

Valeriana alypifolia var. elongata, commonly known as the long-stemmed valerian, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features upright, branched stems that can grow up to 1 meter in height, supporting clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This plant is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
