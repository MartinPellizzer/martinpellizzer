Verbena longifolia

Verbena longifolia, commonly known as slender verbena, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Its foliage is aromatic and often used in traditional herbal remedies.

Verbena longifolia is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
