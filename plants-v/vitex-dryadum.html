Vitex dryadum

Vitex dryadum, commonly known as the dryad's tree or the sacred tree, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree that typically grows up to 5 meters in height. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy dark green surface and a lighter underside. Its showy, clustered inflorescences produce fragrant, violet to purple flowers that bloom in summer.

Vitex dryadum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its aesthetic and ecological benefits.
