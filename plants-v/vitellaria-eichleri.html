Vitellaria eichleri

Vitellaria eichleri, commonly known as the Eichleri shea tree, is a species native to West Africa.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is valued for its fruit, which contains a nutritious seed used in food and cosmetics. The tree can grow up to 20 meters tall and has a dense canopy with elliptical leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered, and it produces a fruit that turns from green to yellow when mature.

The tree plays an important role in local ecosystems and traditional practices.
