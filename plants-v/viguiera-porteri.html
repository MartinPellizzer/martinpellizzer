Viguiera porteri

Viguiera porteri, commonly known as Mexican blanket flower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Mexico.

It features upright, branching stems that are covered in dense, hairy foliage. The plant produces showy, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically blooming in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value and drought tolerance make it a popular choice in arid landscapes.
