Viola × tuvinica

Viola × tuvinica, commonly known as the Tuvinian violet, is a hybrid species within the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant purple to violet flowers. The plant typically features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are dark green in color. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas or shaded gardens.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated for its attractive foliage and flowering display.
