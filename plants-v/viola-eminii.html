Viola eminii

Viola eminii, commonly known as the alpine violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the high-altitude regions of the European Alps.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are typically dark green and covered in a fine pubescence. The plant produces delicate flowers with five petals, often in shades of violet or blue, which bloom in early spring. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to cold, mountainous climates.

Viola eminii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in alpine gardens.
