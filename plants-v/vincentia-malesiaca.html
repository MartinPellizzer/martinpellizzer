Vincentia malesiaca

Vincentia malesiaca, commonly known as the yellow shower vine, is a climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its delicate, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has trifoliate leaves and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found growing along forest edges or in disturbed areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and green spaces.
