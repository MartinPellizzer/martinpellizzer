Vitex hildebrandtii var. glabrescens

Vitex hildebrandtii var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth vitex, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows in dry, rocky soils. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and a distinctive grayish-green color. Its showy, fragrant flowers are usually purple or blue and appear in clusters during the summer months.

The smooth vitex is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native plant gardens.
