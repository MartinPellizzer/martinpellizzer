Viola Canadensis Var. Rydbergii

Viola canadensis var. rydbergii is a lesser-known but striking member of the violet family native to North America. This variant is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves and vibrant purple flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated compared to more common violet species. Understanding this plant offers insight into the diversity of native flora and its ecological importance.
