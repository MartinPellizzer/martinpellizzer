Veyretia hassleri

Veyretia hassleri, commonly known as the Mexican star cactus, is a member of the Cactaceae family.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped spines and cylindrical stems. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing in the spring.

Veyretia hassleri is valued for its ornamental appearance and is often cultivated in succulent gardens.
