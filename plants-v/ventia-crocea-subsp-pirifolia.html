Ventia crocea subsp. pirifolia

Ventia crocea subsp.

pirifolia, commonly known as the purple-stemmed ventia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its slender, upright stems that often display a distinctive purple or reddish hue.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink, blooming during the cooler months. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its unique appearance. Ventia crocea subsp.

pirifolia thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
