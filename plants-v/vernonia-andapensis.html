Vernonia andapensis

Vernonia andapensis, commonly known as the Andapensis ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow to orange in color.

Vernonia andapensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
