Vriesea egregia

Vriesea egregia, commonly known as the giant vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, sword-shaped leaves that form a rosette, creating a central pool that collects rainwater. The plant produces vibrant, long-lasting inflorescences with multiple flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Vriesea egregia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in indoor settings.
