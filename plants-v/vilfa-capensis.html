Vilfa capensis

Vilfa capensis, commonly known as the Cape vilfa, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its delicate, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, preferring full sun exposure. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring.

Vilfa capensis is often used in ornamental gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and hardy nature.
