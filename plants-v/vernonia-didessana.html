Vernonia didessana

Vernonia didessana, commonly known as the golden crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, yellow-orange flower heads that are surrounded by pale green bracts, giving it a distinctive appearance. It typically grows in open grasslands and is adapted to dry conditions.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening due to its striking visual appeal.
