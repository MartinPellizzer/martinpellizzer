Vallesia

Vallesia, commonly known as the "Coral Tree," is a genus of flowering plants in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that often display vibrant red, orange, or yellow hues. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, with compound leaves and pod-like fruits. Vallesia species are native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

They are valued for their ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
