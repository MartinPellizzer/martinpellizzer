Vellozia peripherica

Vellozia peripherica, commonly known as the peripheral vellozia, is a striking succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy, blue-gray leaves that store water, adapting to arid environments. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months, adding ornamental value. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is often cultivated by succulent enthusiasts.
