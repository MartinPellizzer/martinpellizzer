Vitex bombacifolia

Vitex bombacifolia, commonly known as the African pride of Barbados, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically in shades of purple or white. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate with a smooth margin.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds covered in a silky, cotton-like hair.
