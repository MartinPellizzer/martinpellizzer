Vernonia zuccariniana

Vernonia zuccariniana, commonly known as the Zuccari's ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean, particularly Jamaica.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, yellowish-green to reddish bracts that resemble flowers. The plant is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and have a rough, hairy texture.

Vernonia zuccariniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
