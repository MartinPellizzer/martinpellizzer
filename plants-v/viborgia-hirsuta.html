Viborgia hirsuta

Viborgia hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy viborgia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Viborgia hirsuta is often found in rocky or sandy soils in open habitats.
