Viburnum chingii

Viburnum chingii, commonly known as Chinese snowball, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features dense clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring, creating a striking visual display. The plant produces red berries that are edible for birds but not for humans. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sun.

Viburnum chingii is valued in gardens for its ornamental value and adaptability to various climates.
