Vriesea × devansayana

Vriesea × devansayana, commonly known as the hybrid Vriesea, is a tropical plant belonging to the Bromeliaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful inflorescences that can range from red to orange and yellow. The plant features rosettes of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It is native to South America and thrives in warm, humid environments.

Vriesea × devansayana is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
