Vriesea hygrometrica var. angustifolia

Vriesea hygrometrica var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf vriesea, is a hybrid cultivar of the genus Vriesea native to South America.

It features elegant, strap-like leaves that are narrow and dark green, often with a slight sheen. The plant produces striking, long-lasting inflorescences with bright yellow flowers surrounded by green bracts. It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.

This variety thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to maintain its health.
