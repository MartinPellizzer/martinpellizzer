Viola szetschwanensis var. kangdienensis

Viola szetschwanensis var. kangdienensis, commonly known as the Kangding violet, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Sichuan, China.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and vibrant violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories or along stream banks. Its flowers bloom in early spring, adding a splash of color to its natural habitat.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
