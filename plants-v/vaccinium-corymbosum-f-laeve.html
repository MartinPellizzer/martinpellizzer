Vaccinium corymbosum f. laeve

Vaccinium corymbosum f. laeve, commonly known as the smooth-leaf blueberry, is a cultivar of the highbush blueberry.

It is characterized by its smooth, waxy leaves and compact growth habit. This variety is well-suited for cultivation in temperate climates with well-drained soil. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped white flowers that develop into sweet, edible berries.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities as well as its culinary uses.
