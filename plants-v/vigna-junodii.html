Vigna junodii

Vigna junodii, commonly known as the Junod's bean, is a flowering plant species native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its climbing habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several round, edible seeds. Vigna junodii is valued for its nutritional content, including protein and essential minerals, making it an important crop in some regions.

Its growth is typically associated with well-drained soils and a warm climate.
