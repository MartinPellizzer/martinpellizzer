Volkameria cordifolia

Volkameria cordifolia, commonly known as the heartleaf volkameria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are glossy and often have a waxy texture, while the flowers bloom in spring and summer.

Volkameria cordifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
