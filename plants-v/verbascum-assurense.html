Verbascum assurense

Verbascum assurense, commonly known as the Armenian mullein, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Armenia and Turkey.

It belongs to the genus Verbascum, which includes over 300 species of flowering plants commonly referred to as mulleins. The plant features tall, hairy stems and large, showy yellow or orange flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are broad and grayish-green, covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Verbascum assurense is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
