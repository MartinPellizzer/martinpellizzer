Viburnum delicatulum

Viburnum delicatulum, commonly known as sweet viburnum, is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that turn yellow in autumn, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Viburnum delicatulum is often used in gardens for its attractive foliage and seasonal interest.
