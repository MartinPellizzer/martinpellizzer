Vernonia stellulifera

Vernonia stellulifera, commonly known as the star-leaf ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellow to orange-red flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodlands. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Vernonia stellulifera is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
