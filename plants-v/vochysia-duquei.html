Vochysia duquei

Vochysia duquei, commonly known as the Duque's velvet tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark bark and dense, glossy green foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. Its fruit is a small, spherical drupe that splits open to release seeds.

Vochysia duquei is valued for its ornamental qualities and plays a role in the ecological balance of its native habitat.
