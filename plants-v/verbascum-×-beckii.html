Verbascum × beckii

Verbascum × beckii, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a tall, herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features large, fuzzy, gray-green leaves that are covered in soft, velvety hairs. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It is often found in rocky or dry soils and thrives in full sun.

Verbascum × beckii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used traditionally in herbal medicine.
