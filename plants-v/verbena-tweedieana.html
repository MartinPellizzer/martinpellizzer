Verbena tweedieana

Verbena tweedieana, commonly known as the purple verbena, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy clusters of tubular flowers. The plant produces vibrant purple or violet flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. It prefers full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
