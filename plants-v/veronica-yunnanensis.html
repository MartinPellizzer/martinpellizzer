Veronica yunnanensis

Veronica yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan speedwell, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan, China.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or well-drained soils at high altitudes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, while the flowers are arranged in dense spikes.

Veronica yunnanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
