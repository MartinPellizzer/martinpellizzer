Viguiera linearifolia

Viguiera linearifolia, commonly known as the linearleaf ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Viguiera linearifolia is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
