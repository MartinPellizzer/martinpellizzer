Viola × schauloi

Viola × schauloi, commonly known as the hybrid violet, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Violaceae family.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Viola tricolor and Viola lutea, resulting in a plant with distinctive color variations. The leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers typically display combinations of purple, yellow, and white. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
