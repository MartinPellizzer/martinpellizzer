Verbascum × skamneliense

Verbascum × skamneliense, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial plant belonging to the Scrophulariaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, velvety, gray-green leaves that are covered in dense, soft hairs. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. It is native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus and thrives in well-drained, rocky soils.

Verbascum × skamneliense is often cultivated for its ornamental value and historical use in traditional medicine.
