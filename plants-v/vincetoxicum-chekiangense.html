Vincetoxicum chekiangense

Vincetoxicum chekiangense, commonly known as Chinese milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, purple or pink flowers. The plant produces small, cylindrical pods that contain seeds, and it is often found in grassy areas and along roadsides.

It has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits, though its toxicity requires caution in handling.
