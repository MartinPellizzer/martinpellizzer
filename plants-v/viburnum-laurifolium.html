Viburnum laurifolium

Viburnum laurifolium, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen viburnum, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features opposite, elliptic to ovate leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath, giving it a lush appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters during the spring, followed by red, berry-like fruits that are edible for birds. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Viburnum laurifolium is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and seasonal interest.
