Vaccaria pyramidalis

Vaccaria pyramidalis, commonly known as the pyramidal sandmat, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its dense, cushion-like growth habit. Vaccaria pyramidalis is often found in sandy or gravelly soils and is well adapted to drought conditions.
