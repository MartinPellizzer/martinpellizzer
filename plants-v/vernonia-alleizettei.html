Vernonia alleizettei

Vernonia alleizettei, commonly known as the Zulu Vernonia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Vernonia alleizettei is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local medicine.
