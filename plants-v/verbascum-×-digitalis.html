Verbascum × digitalis

Verbascum × digitalis, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial plant that combines traits from both Verbascum and Digitalis species.

It is characterized by its large, fuzzy, gray-green leaves and tall spikes of showy, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens. Its flowers come in shades of pink, purple, and white, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The hybrid is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
