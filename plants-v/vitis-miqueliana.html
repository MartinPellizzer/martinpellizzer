Vitis miqueliana

Vitis miqueliana, commonly known as the Miquel's grapevine, is a species of flowering vine native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Vitaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into clusters of berries. Vitis miqueliana is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, growing in moist, shady environments.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and botanical research.
