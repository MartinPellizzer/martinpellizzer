Vesper purpurascens

Vesper purpurascens, commonly known as the evening rose.

This flowering plant is native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. It is characterized by its striking pink to purple flowers that bloom in the late afternoon. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with small, waxy leaves that help reduce water loss.

It is well adapted to drought conditions and thrives in full sun.
