Valteta fuchsioides

Valteta fuchsioides, commonly known as the pink flame orchid, is a striking member of the orchid family native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

This plant is characterized by its vibrant pink and red petals, which are often adorned with intricate patterns resembling flames. It typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. The flowers bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies with their nectar.

Its unique morphology and vivid coloration make it a popular choice for both ornamental gardening and botanical study.
