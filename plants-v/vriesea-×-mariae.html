Vriesea × mariae

Vriesea × mariae, commonly known as the hybrid Vriesea, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Vriesea × spectabilis and Vriesea × cattleyana.

It is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its striking, long-lasting inflorescences that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The plant typically features broad, strap-shaped leaves that are often bright green with a waxy texture. Vriesea × mariae is native to South America and thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
