Vriesea × magnusiana

Vriesea × magnusiana, commonly known as the hybrid Vriesea, is a hybrid cultivar of the genus Vriesea, which belongs to the Bromeliaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful inflorescences that can range from red to orange and yellow, often with contrasting green bracts. The plant typically features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often variegated with white or silver markings. It is a popular houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

Vriesea × magnusiana thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to maintain its vibrant appearance.
