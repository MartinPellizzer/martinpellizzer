Vriesea rugosa

Vriesea rugosa, commonly known as the sword fern, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It features a rosette of broad, sword-shaped leaves that are often variegated with silver and green hues. The plant produces striking inflorescences with brightly colored bracts that can range from red to orange. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Vriesea rugosa is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
