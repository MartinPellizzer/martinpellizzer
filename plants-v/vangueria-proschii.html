Vangueria proschii

Vangueria proschii, commonly known as the African star-of-Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces clusters of small, star-shaped white flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Vangueria proschii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
