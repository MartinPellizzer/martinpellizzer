Valeriana soratensis

Valeriana soratensis, commonly known as the Sorat Valley valerian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, herbaceous stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink, blooming during the summer months. However, it is a relatively rare species, making it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
