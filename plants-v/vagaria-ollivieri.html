Vagaria ollivieri

Vagaria ollivieri, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and clusters of showy, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to white. The plant is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is typically grown as a houseplant due to its preference for warm, humid environments. Vagaria ollivieri thrives in well-draining soil and requires indirect sunlight to prevent leaf scorch.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and decorative displays.
