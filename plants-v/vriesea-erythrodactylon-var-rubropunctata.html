Vriesea erythrodactylon var. rubropunctata

Vriesea erythrodactylon var. rubropunctata, commonly known as the Red-Dot Vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to South America.

It features a rosette of narrow, dark green leaves with red and orange markings, particularly along the midribs. The plant produces vibrant red flowers in a spike that emerges from the center of the rosette. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ease of care in indoor settings.
