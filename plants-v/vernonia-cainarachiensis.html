Vernonia cainarachiensis

Vernonia cainarachiensis, commonly known as the Sudan vernonia, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow-orange inflorescences. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and savannas. It has lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and is often found in regions with seasonal rainfall.

Vernonia cainarachiensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
