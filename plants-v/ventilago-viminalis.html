Ventilago viminalis

Ventilago viminalis, commonly known as the slender milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that are clustered in racemes. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.
