Vernonia xiquexiquensis

Vernonia xiquexiquensis, commonly known as the Mexican ironweed, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its tall, slender stem and clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, and it produces seeds in small, spherical capsules.

Vernonia xiquexiquensis is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
