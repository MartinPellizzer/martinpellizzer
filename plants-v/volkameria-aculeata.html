Volkameria aculeata

Volkameria aculeata, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems and clusters of small, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals. The plant produces small, round, green fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Volkameria aculeata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
