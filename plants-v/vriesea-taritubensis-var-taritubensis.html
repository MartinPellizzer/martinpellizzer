Vriesea taritubensis var. taritubensis

Vriesea taritubensis var. taritubensis, commonly known as the Taritubens Vriesea, is a bromeliad native to the rainforests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its striking, long-lasting inflorescence that displays vibrant orange and red hues. The plant features a rosette of narrow, dark green leaves that are often adorned with white or silver markings. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
