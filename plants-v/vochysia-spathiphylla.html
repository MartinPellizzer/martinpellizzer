Vochysia spathiphylla

Vochysia spathiphylla, commonly known as the Brazilian rosewood, is a tropical tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, dark wood with a rich, reddish-brown color. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth surface. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow.

The fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release its seeds.
