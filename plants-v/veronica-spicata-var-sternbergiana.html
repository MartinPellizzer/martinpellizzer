Veronica spicata var. sternbergiana

Veronica spicata var. sternbergiana, commonly known as the silver carpet speedwell, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe.

It features dense clusters of small, sky-blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has wiry, silver-gray foliage that forms a mat-like ground cover. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is often used in rock gardens and as a border plant due to its ornamental appeal and hardiness.
