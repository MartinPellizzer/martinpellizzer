Vriesea Castaneobulbosa

Vriesea castaneobulbosa is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil known for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms. This plant features a rosette of dark green leaves that form a central cup, ideal for collecting rainwater. Its flowers, which range from red to orange, add a dramatic touch to any indoor or outdoor setting. Easy to care for, it thrives in bright, indirect light and requires minimal maintenance. A popular choice for both novice and experienced plant enthusiasts, it brings a tropical feel to any space.
