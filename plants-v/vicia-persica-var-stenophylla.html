Vicia persica var. stenophylla

Vicia persica var. stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf pea, is a variety of the Persian vetch.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, often hairy stems and small, pea-shaped flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and trifoliate, with each leaflet being elongated and pointed. This plant is native to parts of southern Europe and is often found in dry, rocky soils.

It plays a role in soil stabilization and can be used in ecological restoration projects.
