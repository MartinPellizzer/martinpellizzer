Vanda mindanaoensis

Vanda mindanaoensis, commonly known as the Mindanao vanda, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its robust, fibrous root system and its ability to thrive in shaded, humid environments. The plant produces striking, showy flowers with a wide range of colors, including white, pink, and purple. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its natural habitat.

Vanda mindanaoensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
