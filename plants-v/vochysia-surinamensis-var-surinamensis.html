Vochysia surinamensis var. surinamensis

Vochysia surinamensis var. surinamensis, commonly known as the Surinam trumpet tree, is a tropical tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant has smooth, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It produces small, spherical fruits that contain several seeds.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well-suited to warm, humid climates.
