Vellozia argentea

Vellozia argentea, commonly known as silver vellozia, is a striking evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which results from dense, waxy leaf coatings that help reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate contrast to its rugged appearance. Vellozia argentea is often found in rocky or sandy soils, thriving in full sun and low moisture conditions.

Its unique morphology and adaptability make it a notable species in its native ecosystems.
