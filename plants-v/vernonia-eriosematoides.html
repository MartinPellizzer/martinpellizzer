Vernonia eriosematoides

Vernonia eriosematoides, commonly known as the rough vernonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. Vernonia eriosematoides is typically found in open woodlands and grasslands, thriving in well-drained soils.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
