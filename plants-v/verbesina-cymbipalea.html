Verbesina cymbipalea

Verbesina cymbipalea, commonly known as the velvetleaf sunflower, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a rough, hairy texture, and it produces elongated, narrow fruits that contain numerous small seeds. It thrives in moist, sunny environments and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and traditional herbal medicine.
