Vernonia nuxioides

Vernonia nuxioides, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a shrub or small tree native to sub-Saharan Africa.

It is characterized by its woody, aromatic leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is known for its hard, dense wood, which is highly valued for construction and tool-making. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

Vernonia nuxioides plays an important ecological role by providing habitat and food for various insects and birds.
