Viburnum cuttingianum

Viburnum cuttingianum, commonly known as the Chinese snowberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features compound leaves with three to five elliptical leaflets, and its flowers are small, white, and clustered in umbels. The plant produces red berries that are edible for birds but toxic to humans. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and seasonal color changes.
