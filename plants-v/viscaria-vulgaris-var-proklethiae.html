Viscaria vulgaris var. proklethiae

Viscaria vulgaris var. proklethiae, commonly known as the Proklethiae Carpet Flower, is a variant of the common mouse-ear chickweed.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming plant with small, spoon-shaped leaves and clusters of tiny, white flowers. The species is native to Europe and is often found in grassy areas and along pathways. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation in gardens.
