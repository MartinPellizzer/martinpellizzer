Veronica frutescens

Veronica frutescens, commonly known as Woolly Speedwell, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial with dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, blue or violet flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long blooming period.
