Viburnum hupehense

Viburnum hupehense, commonly known as the Chinese snowberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features compound leaves with ovate to lanceolate leaflets and produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers in the spring. The plant is notable for its ornamental value, with showy, red or pink berries that persist into winter. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for a variety of garden settings.

Viburnum hupehense is also valued for its adaptability and resistance to common pests and diseases.
