Viola × magna

Viola × magna, commonly known as the large violet, is a hybrid species within the Viola genus, characterized by its robust growth and showy flowers.

It typically features heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges and blooms in shades of purple, violet, and white. The plant is native to Europe and has been widely cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial to full shade.

Viola × magna is known for its adaptability and ability to naturalize in various soil types.
