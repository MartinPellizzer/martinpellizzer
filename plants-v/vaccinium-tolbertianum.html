Vaccinium tolbertianum

Vaccinium tolbertianum, commonly known as Tolbert's blueberry, is a species of evergreen shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes other familiar plants like blueberries and cranberries. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white in color. Its leaves are oval-shaped with a leathery texture and a dark green color.

The fruit, which is a small, blue-black berry, is edible and has a tart flavor.
