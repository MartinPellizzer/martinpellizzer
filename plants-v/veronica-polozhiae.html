Veronica polozhiae

Veronica polozhiae, commonly known as the Namib Desert speedwell, is a flowering plant native to the hyperarid regions of Namibia and Angola.

It is a low-growing shrub with small, fleshy leaves adapted to conserve water in its harsh environment. The plant produces clusters of blue or purple flowers that bloom during the brief rainy season. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to access deep water sources.

Veronica polozhiae plays a vital role in stabilizing the fragile desert ecosystem and providing habitat for local wildlife.
