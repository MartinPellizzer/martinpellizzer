Veronica townsendii

Veronica townsendii, commonly known as Townsend's speedwell, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant features small, blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are usually opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in rocky soils and is often found in alpine and arid environments.
