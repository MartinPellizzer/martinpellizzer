Verbascum × comosum

Verbascum × comosum, commonly known as the fuzzy mullein, is a hybrid plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems covered in dense, soft hairs that give it a fuzzy appearance. The plant produces large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, often with a woolly texture.

Verbascum × comosum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used historically for its medicinal properties.
