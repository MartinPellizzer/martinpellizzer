Vernonia galamensis subsp. filisquama

Vernonia galamensis subsp.

filisquama, commonly known as the slender vernonia, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical Africa. It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the region's flora and a subject of interest in botanical studies.
