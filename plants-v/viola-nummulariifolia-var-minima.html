Viola nummulariifolia var. minima

Viola nummulariifolia var. minima, commonly known as the miniature viola or dwarf viola, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Japan.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are densely packed and covered in a fine layer of white hairs. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of violet or purple, typically blooming in early spring. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its compact size and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
