Viola × vorarlbergensis

Viola × vorarlbergensis, commonly known as the Vorarlberg violet, is a hybrid species belonging to the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its semi-evergreen foliage and clusters of violet-blue flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows to a height of 15–30 cm and prefers moist, well-drained soils in shaded to partially shaded environments. Its leaves are heart-shaped with a hairy texture, and the flowers have five petals with a distinctive spur.

This species is native to the Alpine regions of Europe and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
