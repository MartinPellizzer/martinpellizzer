Vriesea × gravisiana

Vriesea × gravisiana, commonly known as the hybrid Vriesea, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Vriesea × spectabilis and Vriesea × lutea.

It is a member of the Bromeliaceae family, characterized by its striking, colorful inflorescences that can range from yellow to orange-red. The plant typically features broad, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating, which helps in water retention. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Vriesea × gravisiana is popular as a decorative plant due to its long-lasting blooms and relatively low maintenance requirements.
