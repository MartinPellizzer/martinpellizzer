Verbena Elongata

Verbena elongata is a striking flowering plant known for its vibrant purple blooms and slender, upright stems. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal environments. This species is prized for its ornamental value and is commonly used in gardens and landscapes. Its flowers attract pollinators like bees and butterflies, making it an important plant for biodiversity. Despite its beauty, Verbena elongata is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced gardeners.
