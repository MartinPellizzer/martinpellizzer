Viola vilaensis

Viola vilaensis, commonly known as the Vila violet, is a species of flowering plant in the Violaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, particularly in countries like Vietnam and Thailand. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces delicate, violet-colored flowers that bloom in early spring. Viola vilaensis is often found in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in temperate climates.
