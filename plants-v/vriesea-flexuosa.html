Vriesea flexuosa

Vriesea flexuosa, commonly known as the rainbow bromeliad, is a striking epiphytic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful inflorescence that can display multiple shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features a rosette of broad, waxy leaves that form a central cup to collect rainwater. Its foliage is often adorned with intricate patterns and contrasting colors.

Vriesea flexuosa thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
