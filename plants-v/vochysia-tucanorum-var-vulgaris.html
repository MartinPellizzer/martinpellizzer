Vochysia tucanorum var. vulgaris

Vochysia tucanorum var. vulgaris, commonly known as the "Cupuaçu tree," is a tropical plant native to the Amazon basin.

It is characterized by its large, glossy leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The tree produces a unique fruit, known as cupuaçu, which has a high concentration of antioxidants and is used in various food products. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

It plays an important ecological role in the rainforest by providing habitat and food for numerous species.
