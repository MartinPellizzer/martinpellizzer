Veronica fedtschenkoi

Veronica fedtschenkoi, commonly known as the alpine speedwell, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

Veronica fedtschenkoi is often found in rocky soils and alpine meadows, thriving in cool, moist conditions.
