Vriesea bi-beatricis

Vriesea bi-beatricis, commonly known as the Beatrice Vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful inflorescence that typically displays shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that collect water, forming a central reservoir. Its flowers bloom in a spiral pattern, creating a visually appealing display.

Vriesea bi-beatricis thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
