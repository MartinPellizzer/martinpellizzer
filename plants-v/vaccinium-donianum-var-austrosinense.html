Vaccinium donianum var. austrosinense

Vaccinium donianum var. austrosinense, commonly known as the Australian heath, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in heathlands and rocky soils, preferring well-drained conditions. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually pink or white, which bloom in spring. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, contributing to its ornamental value.

The fruit is a small, edible berry that is often used in local cuisine and traditional medicine.
