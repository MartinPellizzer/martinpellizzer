Vandenboschia × amabilis

Vandenboschia × amabilis, commonly known as the hybrid ice plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It is a cross between Vandenboschia cordifolia and Vandenboschia humifusa, resulting in a plant with thick, fleshy leaves that store water. The leaves are typically blue-green in color and have a waxy coating to reduce water loss. It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to its appearance.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping.
