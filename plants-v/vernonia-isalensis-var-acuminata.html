Vernonia isalensis var. acuminata

Vernonia isalensis var. acuminata, commonly known as the slender Vernonia, is a shrub native to eastern Africa.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and a distinctive blue-gray hue. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, typically blooming during the dry season. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
