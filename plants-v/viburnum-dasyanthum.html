Viburnum dasyanthum

Viburnum dasyanthum, commonly known as the Japanese snowberry, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It features clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy on top. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Viburnum dasyanthum is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability in garden settings.
