Vriesea hoehneana

Vriesea hoehneana, commonly known as the Hoehne's Vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, spathaceous inflorescence that displays a range of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant features a rosette of elongated, narrow leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of trichomes, which help in water retention. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated as a decorative plant in tropical gardens.
