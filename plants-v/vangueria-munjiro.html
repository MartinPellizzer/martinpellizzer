Vangueria munjiro

Vangueria munjiro, commonly known as the African rock fig, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a small, round fig-like structure that turns red when mature.

Vangueria munjiro is adapted to arid and rocky environments, making it a hardy plant in dry regions.
