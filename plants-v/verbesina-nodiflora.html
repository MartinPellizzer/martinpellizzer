Verbesina nodiflora

Verbesina nodiflora, commonly known as yellow beggar-ticks, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and serrated leaves, which are often covered in fine, sticky hairs. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in a variety of habitats, including roadsides, fields, and disturbed soils.

Verbesina nodiflora is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
