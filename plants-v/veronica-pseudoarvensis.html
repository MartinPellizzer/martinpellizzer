Veronica pseudoarvensis

Veronica pseudoarvensis, commonly known as field speedwell, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It belongs to the family Plantaginaceae and is characterized by its small, blue or purple flowers that bloom in clusters. It is an annual or biennial herb that plays a role in supporting pollinators and can sometimes become invasive in certain regions.
