Veronica langei

Veronica langei, commonly known as the Lange's speedwell, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant blue or purple flowers. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are clustered in whorls along the stems.

Veronica langei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its hardy and drought-resistant nature.
