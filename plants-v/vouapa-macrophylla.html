Vouapa macrophylla

Vouapa macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf vouapa, is a tropical plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant typically grows in wet, shaded environments such as rainforests or swampy areas. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The flowers are small and inconspicuous, typically greenish-white in color, and appear in clusters.
