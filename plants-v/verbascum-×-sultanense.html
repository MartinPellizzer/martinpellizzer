Verbascum × sultanense

Verbascum × sultanense, commonly known as the Sultan's mullein, is a hybrid plant belonging to the genus Verbascum in the family Scrophulariaceae.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, fuzzy, yellow-green leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
