Verbena × suksdorfii

Verbena × suksdorfii, commonly known as the hybrid verbena, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Verbenaceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between Verbena rigida and Verbena bonariensis, resulting in a plant with unique floral and foliage characteristics. The plant produces vibrant blue to purple flowers that bloom in spikes, typically from late spring to early autumn. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Its compact growth habit and long blooming period make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
