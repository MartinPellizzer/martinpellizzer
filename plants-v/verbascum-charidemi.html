Verbascum charidemi

Verbascum charidemi, commonly known as the Cyprus mullein, is a flowering plant native to the island of Cyprus.

It belongs to the genus Verbascum, which includes species commonly referred to as mulleins. The plant is characterized by its tall, hairy stems and large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in rocky and dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid environments.

Verbascum charidemi is also valued for its ornamental appeal and historical uses in traditional medicine.
