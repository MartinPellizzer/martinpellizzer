Viola hastata var. glaberrima

Viola hastata var. glaberrima, commonly known as the smoothleaf violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features heart-shaped, glabrous (smooth) leaves and produces violet-blue flowers with yellow centers. It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
