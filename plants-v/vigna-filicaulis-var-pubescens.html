Vigna filicaulis var. pubescens

Vigna filicaulis var. pubescens, commonly known as the hairy winged bean, is a tropical legume native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and trifoliate leaves with hairy surfaces. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers followed by elongated, pod-like fruits containing edible seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its nutritional seeds.

The hairy pubescence on its leaves and stems is a distinctive botanical feature.
