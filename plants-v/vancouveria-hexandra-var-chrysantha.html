Vancouveria hexandra var. chrysantha

Vancouveria hexandra var. chrysantha, commonly known as the golden vancouveria, is a perennial herb native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers in late summer, which are borne on slender, upright stems. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local ecosystems.
