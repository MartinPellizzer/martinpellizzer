Vigna ringoetii

Vigna ringoetii, commonly known as the African bean, is a species of legume native to Africa.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and trifoliate leaves, typical of many Vigna species. The plant produces small, yellow flowers followed by elongated pods containing kidney-shaped seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is adapted to poor soil conditions.

Vigna ringoetii is valued for its nutritional content and is used as both a food source and for traditional medicinal purposes.
