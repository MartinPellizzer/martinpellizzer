Verbena Salisburyi

Verbena salisburyi is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa. It is known for its vibrant blue and purple flowers that bloom in early spring. This species thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to flourish. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species by conservationists. Its unique beauty and ecological importance make it a subject of interest for botanists and gardeners alike.
