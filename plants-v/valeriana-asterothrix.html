Valeriana asterothrix

Valeriana asterothrix, commonly known as the star-hair valerian, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Valerianaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant features slender stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its aromatic foliage and unique floral structure make it a notable species in botanical studies.
