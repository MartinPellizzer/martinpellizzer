Vella tenuissima

Vella tenuissima, commonly known as the slender vella, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and small, pale flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in mountainous regions with high humidity. Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and limited distribution, making it a subject of interest for botanists.

Due to habitat loss, Vella tenuissima is considered endangered, highlighting the need for conservation efforts.
