Viola kusanoana f. subrosea

Viola kusanoana f. subrosea, commonly known as the subrose viola, is a flowering plant belonging to the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, heart-shaped leaves and showy, pale pink to rose-colored flowers. Native to Japan, this plant thrives in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and rocky slopes. The subspecies is distinguished by its lighter floral coloration compared to the typical form of V. kusanoana.

Its botanical features make it a sought-after ornamental plant in horticulture.
