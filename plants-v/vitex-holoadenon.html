Vitex holoadenon

Vitex holoadenon, commonly known as the yellow flame tree, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its pinnately compound leaves and showy yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a globose capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
