Verbascum meinckeanum

Verbascum meinckeanum, commonly known as the Meinkean mullein.

This perennial plant is native to southern Europe and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, velvety leaves. It produces showy, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to thrive in arid conditions.

It is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
