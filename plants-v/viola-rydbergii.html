Viola rydbergii

Viola rydbergii, commonly known as the Rydberg violet, is a species of flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces small, showy flowers in shades of purple and violet. Viola rydbergii is part of the Violaceae family, which includes many other violet species known for their ornamental value.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique and valuable addition to native plant gardens.
