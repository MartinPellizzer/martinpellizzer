Verbesina alcabrerae

Verbesina alcabrerae, commonly known as the Mexican ironweed, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of yellow to orange flowers. The plant produces small, oblong fruits that are dispersed by wind. It thrives in dry, sunny habitats and is often found in rocky soils.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies in its native region.
