Verbena bonariensis var. rigida

Verbena bonariensis var. rigida, commonly known as rigid verbena, is a perennial flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, vibrant blue or purple flowers. The plant produces narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its extended blooming period, from late spring to early autumn, adds continuous color to the landscape.
