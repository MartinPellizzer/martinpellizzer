Vitex bogalensis

Vitex bogalensis, commonly known as the African blue bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically blue or purple in color, arranged in clusters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a rough texture.

The fruit is a dry, woody capsule that contains several small, flat seeds.
