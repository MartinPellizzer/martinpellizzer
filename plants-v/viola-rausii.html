Viola rausii

Viola rausii, commonly known as the Alpine violet, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its small, heart-shaped leaves and delicate, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, thriving in cool, moist environments. Viola rausii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its charming appearance.

Its flowers bloom in early spring, adding a touch of color to high-altitude landscapes.
