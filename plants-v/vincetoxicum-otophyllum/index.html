Vincetoxicum Otophyllum

Vincetoxicum otophyllum is a striking perennial plant known for its vibrant, spire-like flowers that bloom in late summer. Native to parts of Europe and Asia, it thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas. The plant's unique appearance makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a bold statement in their landscapes. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, complementing its showy blooms. Despite its beauty, it is considered invasive in some regions, requiring careful management to prevent ecological harm.
