Vismia humboldtiana

Vismia humboldtiana, commonly known as the Humboldt's evergreen, is a tropical tree native to South America.

It belongs to the Clusiaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by bright red berries. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a landscape plant in tropical regions.
