Vella glabrescens

Vella glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth vella, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are typically smooth and glossy in texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Vella glabrescens is often found in humid, tropical or subtropical environments.
