Verbesina altipetens

Verbesina altipetens, commonly known as the Andean goldenrod, is a flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and showy yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in high-altitude environments and is adapted to cool, temperate climates.

Its foliage is often covered in fine hairs, which help protect it from harsh weather conditions.
