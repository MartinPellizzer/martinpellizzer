Verbascum × illyricum

Verbascum × illyricum, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial herb native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It belongs to the genus Verbascum, which includes species known for their large, showy flowers. The plant features thick, woolly leaves that help reduce water loss, adapting it to arid environments. It produces clusters of yellow or orange flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Verbascum × illyricum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
