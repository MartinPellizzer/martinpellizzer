Verticillaria peruviana

Verticillaria peruviana, commonly known as the Peruvian whirlwort, is a unique aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its whorled arrangement of leaves, which gives it its scientific name. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom above the water surface. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Its root system helps stabilize sediments and improve water quality.
