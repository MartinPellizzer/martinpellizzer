Verbena xutha

Verbena xutha, commonly known as common vervain, is an annual herb native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It features slender, branching stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces spikes of blue to purple flowers, which bloom from late spring to early autumn. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

Verbena xutha is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
