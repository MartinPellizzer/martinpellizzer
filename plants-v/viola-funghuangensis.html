Viola funghuangensis

Viola funghuangensis, commonly known as the Fung Huang violet, is a species of flowering plant in the Violaceae family.

It is native to China, specifically the Fung Huang region in Sichuan Province. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and delicate, violet-colored flowers that bloom in early spring. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Viola funghuangensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
