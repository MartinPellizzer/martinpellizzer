Virecta virens

Virecta virens, commonly known as the green sprout, is a herbaceous plant native to temperate regions.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine, hair-like structures. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly serrated edge.

Virecta virens thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or shaded areas.
