Verbesina steinmannii

Verbesina steinmannii, commonly known as the yellow ironweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has serrated, lance-shaped leaves and can grow up to 2 meters in height. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a suitable choice for gardens in temperate climates.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant color and ability to attract pollinators.
