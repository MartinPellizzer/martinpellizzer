Veronica persica

Veronica persica, commonly known as Persian speedwell, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, blue or violet flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy texture.

Veronica persica thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.
