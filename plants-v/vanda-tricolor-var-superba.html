Vanda tricolor var. superba

Vanda tricolor var. superba, commonly known as the superba vanda, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tricolored petals that display shades of red, orange, and yellow, making it highly ornamental. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light, often growing on trees or rocks. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in less fertile substrates.

Vanda tricolor var. superba is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and resilience in cultivation.
