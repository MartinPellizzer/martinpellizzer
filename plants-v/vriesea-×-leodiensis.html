Vriesea × leodiensis

Vriesea × leodiensis, commonly known as the hybrid vriesea, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Vriesea × leodiensis and other Vriesea species.

It is a member of the Bromeliaceae family, characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting inflorescences. The plant typically features a rosette of sword-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored, providing an ornamental appeal. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity to flourish.

Vriesea × leodiensis is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
