Vincetoxicum officinale var. nivale

Vincetoxicum officinale var. nivale, commonly known as snowdrop vine, is a deciduous climbing plant native to Europe.

It features slender, twining stems that can reach up to 2 meters in length. The plant produces small, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.
