Vernonia kotschyi

Vernonia kotschyi, commonly known as the African ironweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in dry, sandy or rocky soils. The plant produces dense clusters of small, bright yellow to orange flowers, which bloom in the late summer and early autumn. Its leaves are usually narrow and slightly hairy, with a distinctive gray-green color.

Vernonia kotschyi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
