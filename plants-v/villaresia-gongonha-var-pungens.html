Villaresia gongonha var. pungens

Villaresia gongonha var. pungens, commonly known as the Pungent Puya, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, spiky inflorescence and vibrant, showy flowers that range from red to orange. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of tropical forests. Its leaves are long, narrow, and covered in a waxy cuticle, which helps retain moisture.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and unique appearance in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
