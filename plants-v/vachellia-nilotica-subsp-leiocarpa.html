Vachellia nilotica subsp. leiocarpa

Vachellia nilotica subsp.

leiocarpa, commonly known as the slender acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to sub-Saharan Africa. It is characterized by its slender, straight branches and small, clustered yellow flowers.

The plant produces flat, leathery pods that contain seeds, and it has small, alternate, pinnate leaves. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing on rocky soils.

The species is known for its ecological importance, providing food and shelter for various animals.
