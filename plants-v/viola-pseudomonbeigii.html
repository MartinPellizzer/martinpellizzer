Viola pseudomonbeigii

Viola pseudomonbeigii, commonly known as the Chinese violet, is a species of flowering plant in the Violaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and delicate, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as woodland areas and stream sides. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Viola pseudomonbeigii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
