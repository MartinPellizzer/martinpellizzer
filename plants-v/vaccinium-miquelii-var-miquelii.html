Vaccinium miquelii var. miquelii

Vaccinium miquelii var. miquelii, commonly known as the Miquel's blueberry, is a species of dwarf shrub native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes many acid-loving plants. The plant produces small, edible berries that are typically dark blue or black in color. Its leaves are elliptical, leathery, and covered in a fine layer of pubescence.

It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in shaded forest understories.
