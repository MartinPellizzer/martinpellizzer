Verbascum × vuyckii

Verbascum × vuyckii, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the genus Verbascum, which includes several species of flowering plants commonly referred to as mulleins. The plant features large, fuzzy, gray-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, forming showy clusters that bloom in late spring to early summer.

Verbascum × vuyckii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to well-drained soils in full sun.
