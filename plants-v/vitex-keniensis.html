Vitex keniensis

Vitex keniensis, commonly known as Kenyan vitex, is a shrub or small tree native to East Africa.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and showy purple or white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. Its fruits are small, dry capsules that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Vitex keniensis is also valued for its traditional medicinal uses in local communities.
