Vernonia gertii

Vernonia gertii, commonly known as the silverleaf vernonia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray, fuzzy foliage and bright yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Vernonia gertii is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.
