Vismia tomentosa

Vismia tomentosa, commonly known as the woolly vismia, is a tropical shrub native to regions of South America.

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-gray leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Vismia tomentosa is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional medicinal uses.
