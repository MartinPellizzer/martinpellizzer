Vaccinium merkii

Vaccinium merkii, commonly known as the Merkii blueberry, is a species of shrub native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes other familiar plants like heathers and cranberries. The plant produces small, edible berries that are typically dark blue or purple in color. These berries are rich in antioxidants and are often used in culinary applications.

Vaccinium merkii thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.
