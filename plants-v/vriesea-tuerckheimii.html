Vriesea tuerckheimii

Vriesea tuerckheimii, commonly known as the Tuerckheim's Vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful inflorescence that can display shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that collect water, which is essential for its survival in its natural habitat. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This species is popular among bromeliad enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
