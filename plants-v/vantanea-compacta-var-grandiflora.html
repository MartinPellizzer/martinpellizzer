Vantanea compacta var. grandiflora

Vantanea compacta var. grandiflora, commonly known as the showy bluebell, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking blue-violet flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that attract pollinators. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Vantanea compacta var. grandiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and naturalized areas.
