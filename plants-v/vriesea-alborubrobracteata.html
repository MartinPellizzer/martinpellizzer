Vriesea alborubrobracteata

Vriesea alborubrobracteata, commonly known as the red-bract vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, red bracts that surround the inflorescence, giving it a dramatic visual appearance. The plant features a rosette of narrow, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, emerging from the center of the leaf rosette.

Vriesea alborubrobracteata thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to maintain its health and aesthetic appeal.
