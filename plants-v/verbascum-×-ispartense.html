Verbascum × ispartense

Verbascum × ispartense, commonly known as the Isparta mullein, is a hybrid species native to Turkey.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its tall, hairy stems and large, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
