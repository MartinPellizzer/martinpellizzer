Verbesina foliacea

Verbesina foliacea, commonly known as golden crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its bright yellow ray flowers and dark green, heart-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as prairies and wetlands. It produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Verbesina foliacea is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
