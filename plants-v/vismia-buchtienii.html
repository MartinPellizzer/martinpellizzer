Vismia buchtienii

Vismia buchtienii, commonly known as the African snowball, is a tropical evergreen tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a striking appearance. The plant has thick, leathery leaves that are arranged in whorls around the stem. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Vismia buchtienii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
