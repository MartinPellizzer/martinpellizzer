Varronia bombardensis

Varronia bombardensis, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a tropical tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. The plant has compound leaves with elongated leaflets and produces woody pods that contain flat, seed-like structures. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often growing in savanna and woodland habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a landscape plant in warm climates.
