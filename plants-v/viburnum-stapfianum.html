Viburnum stapfianum

Viburnum stapfianum, commonly known as the Stapf's viburnum, is a shrub native to the high altitudes of Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. Its leaves are typically ovate with a smooth margin and a dark green upper surface.
