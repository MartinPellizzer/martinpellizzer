Vitex klugii

Vitex klugii, commonly known as the Klug's chaste tree, is a species native to the Caribbean islands.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal areas and limestone soils. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are clustered in terminal panicles and are usually pink or purple in color.

Vitex klugii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
