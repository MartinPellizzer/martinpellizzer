Vittaria microlepis var. thwaitesii

Vittaria microlepis var. thwaitesii, commonly known as the narrow-leaf spikemoss, is a species of moss belonging to the family Vittariaceae.

It is characterized by its small, narrow leaves that are arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its sporophyte produces a capsule that releases spores for reproduction.

This variety is notable for its delicate appearance and its role in maintaining soil stability in its native habitats.
