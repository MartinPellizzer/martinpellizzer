Vernonia hochstetteri var. dekindtii

Vernonia hochstetteri var. dekindtii, commonly known as the Dekindt's ironweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, yellow-orange inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open woodlands and grasslands, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers bloom in late summer to early autumn.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
