Verbena × ostenii

Verbena × ostenii, commonly known as the hybrid verbena, is a flowering plant that belongs to the Verbenaceae family.

It is a hybrid between Verbena rigida and Verbena bonariensis, resulting in a plant with unique characteristics. The species is known for its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in shades of red, purple, and blue, often attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. It is a perennial plant that thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and long blooming period make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
