Vigna unguiculata subsp. cylindrica

Vigna unguiculata subsp.

cylindrica, commonly known as cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to Africa. It is characterized by its erect growth habit and trifoliate leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets.

The plant produces cylindrical pods that contain edible seeds, which are rich in protein and nutrients. It thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated for both its seeds and green pods. Vigna unguiculata subsp.

cylindrica is also valued for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, improving soil fertility.
