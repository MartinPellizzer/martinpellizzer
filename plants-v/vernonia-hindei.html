Vernonia hindei

Vernonia hindei, commonly known as Hinde's ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are small and yellowish-green.

Vernonia hindei is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature.
