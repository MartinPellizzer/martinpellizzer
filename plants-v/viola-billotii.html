Viola billotii

Viola billotii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, heart-shaped leaves and showy, five-petaled flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and thrives in well-drained soil. Viola billotii is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.

Its flowers come in a range of colors, including purple, blue, and white, adding ornamental value to gardens and indoor spaces.
