Vangueria venosa

Vangueria venosa, commonly known as the woolly vangueria, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, adding to its ornamental value. Its leaves are typically gray-green and have a velvety texture.

Vangueria venosa is often used in rock gardens and as a drought-tolerant plant in arid landscapes.
