Verbena × dodgei

Verbena × dodgei, commonly known as the hybrid verbena, is a flowering plant in the Verbenaceae family.

It is a cross between Verbena rigida and Verbena bonariensis, resulting in a plant with unique characteristics. The species is known for its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from purple to pink and blue. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and long blooming period make it a versatile ornamental plant.
