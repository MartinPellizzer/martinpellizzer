Verbena Malpaisana

Malpais verbena, scientifically known as Verbena malpaisana, is a striking annual flowering plant native to the southwestern United States. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils and desert landscapes. With its vivid purple and blue flowers, it adds a splash of color to otherwise barren regions. The plant is well adapted to withstand drought and high temperatures, making it a resilient species. Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it a notable subject in botany and conservation efforts.
