Vismia subcuneata

Vismia subcuneata, commonly known as the African ebony, is a tropical tree native to regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its dark, dense wood and is often found in lowland rainforests. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green. It produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in spikes, followed by small, round fruits.

The botanical features of Vismia subcuneata make it an important species in both ecological and economic contexts.
