Verbascum × subsinuatum

Verbascum × subsinuatum, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial plant belonging to the Scrophulariaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that bear large, fuzzy, gray-green leaves. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

This hybrid is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
