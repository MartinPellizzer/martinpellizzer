Vriesea pachychlamys

Vriesea pachychlamys, commonly known as the giant vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to the rainforests of Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescence that can reach up to two meters in height, featuring vibrant orange and yellow flowers. The plant has a rosette-shaped foliage composed of thick, waxy leaves that can grow up to one meter in length. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

Vriesea pachychlamys is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its dramatic appearance and long-lasting blooms.
