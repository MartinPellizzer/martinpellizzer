Vepris onanae

Vepris onanae, commonly known as the African bitterleaf, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small white flowers. The plant is valued for its medicinal properties, with various parts used in traditional medicine. It typically grows in moist, shaded areas and can reach a height of up to 3 meters.
