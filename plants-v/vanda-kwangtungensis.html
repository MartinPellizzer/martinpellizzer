Vanda kwangtungensis

Vanda kwangtungensis, commonly known as the Guangdong vanda, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in humid tropical environments. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and produces large, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. Vanda kwangtungensis is known for its adaptability to various light conditions and its ability to thrive in warm, moist climates.

It is a popular choice for both horticultural cultivation and botanical study due to its ornamental value and resilience.
