Vernonia ehrenbergiana var. capreifolia

Vernonia ehrenbergiana var. capreifolia, commonly known as the Cape rosemary, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in coastal or mountainous regions. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually blue or purple in color, arranged in dense clusters. It has aromatic leaves that are often used in traditional medicine.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
