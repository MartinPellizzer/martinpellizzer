Vanilla cochlearilabia

Vanilla cochlearilabia, commonly known as the orchid vanilla, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its long, pendulous flowers that often have a vanilla-like fragrance. The plant produces a unique type of vanilla pod, which is used in culinary and aromatic applications. Its botanical structure includes a sympodial growth habit and specialized orchid roots.

The species is valued for both its ornamental beauty and its aromatic properties.
