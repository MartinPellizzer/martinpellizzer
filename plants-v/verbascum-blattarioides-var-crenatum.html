Verbascum blattarioides var. crenatum

Verbascum blattarioides var. crenatum, commonly known as the crested goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, soft, velvety leaves that are often covered in a fine down. The plant produces showy, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense, panicle-like inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable ornamental plant for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a notable species within the genus Verbascum.
