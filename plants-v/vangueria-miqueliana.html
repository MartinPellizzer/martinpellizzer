Vangueria miqueliana

Vangueria miqueliana, commonly known as the African milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and clustered in terminal inflorescences, usually in shades of yellow or orange.

The plant is known for its milky sap, which is a characteristic feature of the genus Vangueria.
