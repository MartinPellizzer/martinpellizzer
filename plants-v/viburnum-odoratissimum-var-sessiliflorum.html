Viburnum odoratissimum var. sessiliflorum

Viburnum odoratissimum var. sessiliflorum, commonly known as the Chinese snowberry, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during late spring to early summer. The plant produces dark blue, waxy berries that are edible but often used as ornamental features. Its leaves are ovate with a smooth margin and turn yellow in autumn.

Viburnum odoratissimum var. sessiliflorum thrives in partial shade to full sun and is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability.
