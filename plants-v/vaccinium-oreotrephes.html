Vaccinium oreotrephes

Vaccinium oreotrephes, commonly known as the dwarf blueberry, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes other familiar plants like heather and cranberries. The plant produces small, edible berries that are typically blue or purple in color, similar to other Vaccinium species. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers cool, moist environments.
