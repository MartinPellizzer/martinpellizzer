Viola × balearica

Viola × balearica, commonly known as the Balearic violet, is a hybrid species within the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, velvety leaves and showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows to a height of 10–30 cm and prefers moist, well-drained soils in partial shade. It is native to the Balearic Islands and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.

Viola × balearica is often cultivated for its delicate appearance and ability to thrive in cooler climates.
