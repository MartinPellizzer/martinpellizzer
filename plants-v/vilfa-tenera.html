Vilfa tenera

Vilfa tenera, commonly known as the slender milkweed, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are narrow and opposite, with a slightly hairy surface.

Vilfa tenera is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
