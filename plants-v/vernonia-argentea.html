Vernonia argentea

Vernonia argentea, commonly known as silver Vernonia, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer to early fall. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or prairies.

Vernonia argentea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
