Vernonia hexantha var. balansae

Vernonia hexantha var. balansae, commonly known as the Balansae Vernonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as an erect shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, lanceolate, and often covered with fine hairs.

The flowers are yellow and occur in dense clusters, making it a visually striking species in its natural habitat.
