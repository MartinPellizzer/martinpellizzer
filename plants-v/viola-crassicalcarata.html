Viola crassicalcarata

Viola crassicalcarata, commonly known as the large-leaved violet, is a species of flowering plant in the Violaceae family.

It is native to parts of East Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped, dark green leaves and showy violet or purple flowers. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas.

Viola crassicalcarata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
