Vogelocassia

Vogelocassia, commonly known as the golden crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow ray flowers and dense, bushy growth habit. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes. Vogelocassia is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
