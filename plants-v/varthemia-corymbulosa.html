Varthemia corymbulosa

Varthemia corymbulosa, commonly known as the clustered sandmat, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Varthemia corymbulosa is adapted to withstand drought and high temperatures, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
