Verbesina phyllolepis

Verbesina phyllolepis, commonly known as the golden crownbeard, is a herbaceous perennial native to Mexico.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange flower heads surrounded by pale green bracts. The plant has alternate, heart-shaped leaves that are hairy and covered in fine pubescence. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and botanical collections.
