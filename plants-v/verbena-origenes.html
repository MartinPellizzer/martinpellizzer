Verbena origenes

Verbena origenes, commonly known as the Mexican verbena, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant purple or blue flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub with slender stems and opposite leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and ability to attract pollinators.
