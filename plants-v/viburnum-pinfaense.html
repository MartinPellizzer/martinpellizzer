Viburnum pinfaense

Viburnum pinfaense, commonly known as the Chinese snowberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers in the spring, which are followed by bright red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for gardens in temperate climates.

Viburnum pinfaense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its seasonal interest.
