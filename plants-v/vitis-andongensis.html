Vitis andongensis

Vitis andongensis, commonly known as the Andong grape, is a species of flowering plant in the Vitaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its climbing vines and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that give way to edible berries. These berries are typically used in traditional Chinese medicine and local cuisines.

Vitis andongensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical significance in regional horticulture.
