Viola baoshanensis

Viola baoshanensis, commonly known as the Baoshan violet, is a species of flowering plant in the Violaceae family.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Yunnan Province, where it grows in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces delicate, five-petaled flowers that are typically purple or violet in color. It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also studied for its potential medicinal properties.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
