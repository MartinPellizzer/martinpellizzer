Vernonia usambarensis

Vernonia usambarensis, commonly known as the Usambara Vernonia, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy yellow to orange flower heads. The plant typically grows in coastal and montane regions, often in sandy or rocky soils. Its foliage is usually narrow and grass-like, with lance-shaped leaves.

Vernonia usambarensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
