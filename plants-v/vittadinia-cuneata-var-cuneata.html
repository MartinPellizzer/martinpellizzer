Vittadinia cuneata var. cuneata

Vittadinia cuneata var. cuneata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved vittadinia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a herbaceous perennial with slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, typically blooming from late spring to early autumn. It prefers well-drained soils and full sun, often found in coastal and sandy environments.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
