Vaccinium oranjense var. oranjense

Vaccinium oranjense var. oranjense, commonly known as the Orange huckleberry, is a species of evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in coastal and sandy soils, often in areas with high salinity. The plant produces small, bright red berries that are edible and have a tart flavor. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.

The leaves are lance-shaped and have a leathery texture, contributing to its hardy nature.
