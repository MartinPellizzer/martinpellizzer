Vulpia microstachys

Vulpia microstachys, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed veldt grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant has slender, erect stems and small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, spikelet-based flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a rough texture.

Vulpia microstachys is adapted to dry conditions and is often found in regions with low rainfall.
