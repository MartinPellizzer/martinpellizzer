Vitex monroviana

Vitex monroviana, commonly known as the Monrovia vitex, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 3 to 6 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate to elliptical leaves that are usually grey-green in color. Its flowers are clustered in panicles and range in color from white to purple, blooming in the summer months.

Vitex monroviana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping due to its hardy nature and ability to thrive in coastal areas.
