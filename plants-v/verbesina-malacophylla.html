Verbesina malacophylla

Verbesina malacophylla, commonly known as the silky bush or silky ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange composite flowers. The plant has hairy, silvery leaves that give it a distinctive appearance and help reduce water loss. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.
