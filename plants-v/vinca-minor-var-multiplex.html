Vinca minor var. multiplex

Vinca minor var. multiplex, commonly known as the spreading blue eyes.

This evergreen perennial is native to Europe and thrives in shaded, moist environments. It features small, heart-shaped leaves and produces clusters of blue-violet flowers in early spring. The plant spreads via rhizomes, forming dense mats that suppress weeds.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging garden conditions.
