Verbascum × sibyllinum

Verbascum × sibyllinum, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the genus Verbascum, which includes several species of flowering plants known for their large, showy flowers. The hybrid mullein typically has gray-green, fuzzy leaves and produces yellow flowers in the summer months. It is often found in rocky or disturbed soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used traditionally in herbal medicine.
