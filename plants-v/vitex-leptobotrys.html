Vitex leptobotrys

Vitex leptobotrys, commonly known as the flame tree or white flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences that range from white to red or pink, depending on the stage of bloom. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with small, ovate leaflets and produces small, fleshy fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Vitex leptobotrys is also used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
