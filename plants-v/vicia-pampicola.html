Vicia pampicola

Vicia pampicola, commonly known as the pampicola vetch, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in grasslands and open areas. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

Vicia pampicola is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
