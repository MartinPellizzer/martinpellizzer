Vagnera stellata var. mollis

Vagnera stellata var. mollis, commonly known as the velvetleaf vagnera, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in tropical and subtropical regions.
