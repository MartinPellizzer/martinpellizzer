Vriesea michaelii

Vriesea michaelii, commonly known as the Michael's Vriesea, is a species of flowering plant in the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its striking, colorful inflorescence that resembles a torch. The plant features broad, strap-like leaves that form a rosette, creating a central cup that can hold water. Vriesea michaelii thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity to flourish.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardens.
