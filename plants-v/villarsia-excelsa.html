Villarsia excelsa

Villarsia excelsa, commonly known as the giant villarsia, is a striking tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its large, waxy leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical studies.
