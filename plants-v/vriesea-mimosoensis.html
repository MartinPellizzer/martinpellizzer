Vriesea mimosoensis

Vriesea mimosoensis, commonly known as the Mimosa Vriesea, is a species of bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, recurved, dark green leaves that form a rosette shape. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with vibrant orange to red flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Vriesea mimosoensis is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
