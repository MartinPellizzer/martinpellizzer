Viola bissetii var. kiusiana

Viola bissetii var. kiusiana, commonly known as the Kiusian violet, is a species of flowering plant in the family Violaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Japan, where it thrives in cool, moist environments. The plant produces small, heart-shaped leaves and violet flowers with a distinctive yellow center. It is often found in shaded forest floors and rock crevices.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and delicate species.
