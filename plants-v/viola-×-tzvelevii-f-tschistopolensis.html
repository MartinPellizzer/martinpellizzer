Viola × tzvelevii f. tschistopolensis

Viola × tzvelevii f. tschistopolensis, commonly known as the Tschistopolon viola, is a hybrid species within the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, often purple or violet flowers. This plant typically grows in moist, temperate climates and prefers partial to full shade. Its flowers bloom in early spring, adding color to woodland gardens.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil conditions.
