Viola riviniana

Viola riviniana, commonly known as the slender violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive purple underside and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of purple and violet. It belongs to the Violaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.

The species is also valued for its ecological role, providing nectar for pollinators.
