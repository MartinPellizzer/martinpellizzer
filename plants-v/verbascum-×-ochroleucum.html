Verbascum × ochroleucum

Verbascum × ochroleucum, commonly known as the yellow mullein.

It is a hybrid plant belonging to the genus Verbascum, which includes several species of mullein. This plant is characterized by its large, fuzzy, yellow-orange leaves and showy, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or disturbed areas.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has historical uses in herbal medicine.
