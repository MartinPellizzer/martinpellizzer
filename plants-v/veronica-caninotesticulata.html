Veronica caninotesticulata

Veronica caninotesticulata, commonly known as the dog testicle plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the plantain family, Plantaginaceae, and is characterized by its small, blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its unique common name reflects the shape of its seed pods, which resemble testicles.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
