Verbesina lapazii

Verbesina lapazii, commonly known as the Lapaz goldenrod, is a flowering plant native to the highlands of Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow inflorescences. The plant typically grows in rocky or open habitats, thriving in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and often branched.

Verbesina lapazii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color.
