Vriesea billbergioides

Vriesea billbergioides, commonly known as the Golden Torch bromeliad, is a striking epiphytic plant native to Brazil.

It features a tall, leafy rosette with a central inflorescence that produces vibrant orange flowers. The plant's leaves are long, narrow, and covered in a dense layer of trichomes, which help in water retention. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity to flourish.

Vriesea billbergioides is popular as a ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
