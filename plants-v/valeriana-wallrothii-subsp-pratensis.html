Valeriana wallrothii subsp. pratensis

Valeriana wallrothii subsp.

pratensis, commonly known as meadow valerian, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.
