Vaccinium Vacciniaceum F. Hispidum

Vaccinium vacciniaceum f. hispidum is a unique variant of the Vaccinium genus known for its distinctive hairy foliage. This plant thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in temperate forest regions. Its appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening due to its aesthetic appeal. The species is also valued for its potential in ecological restoration projects. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in mainstream horticultural circles.
