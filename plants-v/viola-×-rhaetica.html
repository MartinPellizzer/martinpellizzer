Viola × rhaetica

Viola × rhaetica, commonly known as the Rhaetian violet, is a hybrid species of flowering plant in the Violaceae family.

It is native to the alpine regions of Europe, particularly the Swiss Alps, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy violet flowers. The plant typically produces violet to purple blossoms with yellow markings, often appearing in clusters at the ends of its stems. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in meadows and rocky slopes at high elevations.

Viola × rhaetica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty and resilience.
