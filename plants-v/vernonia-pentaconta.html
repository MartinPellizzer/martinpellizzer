Vernonia pentaconta

Vernonia pentaconta, commonly known as the five-and-fifty vernonia, is a species of flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
