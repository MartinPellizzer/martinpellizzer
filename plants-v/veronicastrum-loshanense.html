Veronicastrum loshanense

Veronicastrum loshanense, commonly known as blue mist spirea, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that bear dense clusters of sky-blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun. Its foliage is often a deep green, adding visual interest throughout the growing season.

Veronicastrum loshanense is valued in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
