Ventilago fasciculiflora

Ventilago fasciculiflora, commonly known as the clustered sandveld bean, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically arranged in clusters along the stems. The plant produces yellow flowers in dense, racemose inflorescences, which are followed by elongated, bean-like pods. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or semi-arid regions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
