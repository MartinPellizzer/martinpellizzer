Vignea × microstachya

Vignea × microstachya, commonly known as the wild cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its flowers are usually yellow or white, and it produces elongated pods containing edible seeds.

Vignea × microstachya is known for its adaptability to various soil types and its potential use in agriculture and traditional medicine.
