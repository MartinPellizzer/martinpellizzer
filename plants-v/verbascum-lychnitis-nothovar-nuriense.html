Verbascum lychnitis nothovar. nuriense

Verbascum lychnitis nothovar. nuriense, commonly known as the Nuriense Mullein, is a hybrid plant species native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that bear large, fuzzy, yellowish-green leaves. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to gardens.

Due to its hybrid origin, it exhibits a mix of traits from its parent species, contributing to its unique botanical appearance.
