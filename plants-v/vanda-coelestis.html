Vanda coelestis

Vanda coelestis, commonly known as the sky orchid, is a epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, pendant-like inflorescences that bear clusters of fragrant, white or pale blue flowers. The plant has strap-shaped, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in high light conditions and requires well-draining potting media.

Vanda coelestis is prized for its striking appearance and is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
