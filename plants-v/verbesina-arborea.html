Verbesina arborea

Verbesina arborea, commonly known as the tree ambrosia, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height, characterized by its rough, bark-like stems and alternate, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. Its seeds are enclosed in small, spiny capsules that release them when mature.

Verbesina arborea is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
