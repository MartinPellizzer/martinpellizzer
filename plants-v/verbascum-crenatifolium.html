Verbascum crenatifolium

Verbascum crenatifolium, commonly known as the wavy-leaved mullein, is a herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features tall, upright stems that bear large, fuzzy, gray-green leaves with wavy edges. The plant produces showy, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Verbascum crenatifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
