Valerianella morisonii var. leiocarpa

Valerianella morisonii var. leiocarpa, commonly known as the smooth valerian, is a low-growing annual herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has a distinctive, slightly aromatic foliage with a soft, velvety texture. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate in shape and often have a bluish-green hue.

Valerianella morisonii var. leiocarpa is typically found in sandy or gravelly soils along coastal areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
