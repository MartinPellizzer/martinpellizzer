Veronica krumovii

Veronica krumovii, commonly known as the Balkan speedwell, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches heights of about 15 to 30 centimeters. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or limestone habitats.

Veronica krumovii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
