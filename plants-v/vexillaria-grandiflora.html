Vexillaria grandiflora

Vexillaria grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered vexillaria, is a striking member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. These flowers are borne on long, slender pedicels, giving the plant a delicate appearance.

Vexillaria grandiflora thrives in humid, shady environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
