Veratrum mengtzeanum subsp.

mengtzeanum, commonly known as Mengtzean's veratrum, is a tall perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China. It belongs to the family Liliaceae and is characterized by its large, showy white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

Due to its toxicity, it is not typically cultivated for ornamental purposes but is of interest in botanical and pharmacological studies.
