Viola × pseudomakinoi

Viola × pseudomakinoi, commonly known as the Japanese violet, is a hybrid species within the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, dark green leaves and showy violet flowers with yellow markings. Native to Japan, this plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded woodland areas. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Viola × pseudomakinoi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its delicate beauty.
