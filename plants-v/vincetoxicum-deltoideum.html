Vincetoxicum deltoideum

Vincetoxicum deltoideum, commonly known as the delta false bindweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to eastern North America.

It features heart-shaped, lobed leaves that are typically green with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Vincetoxicum deltoideum is known for its rapid growth and can become invasive in certain regions.
