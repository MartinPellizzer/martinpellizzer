Viridantha tortilis

Viridantha tortilis, commonly known as the twisted green vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions.

It features slender, winding stems that coil around supports as it grows. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a glossy, dark green surface. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.
