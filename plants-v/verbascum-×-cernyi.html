Verbascum × cernyi

Verbascum × cernyi, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the genus Verbascum, which includes species known for their large, fuzzy leaves and showy yellow flowers. The hybrid form exhibits characteristics of its parent species, often displaying elongated, velvety leaves and pendulous flower clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its botanical features make it an attractive addition to both ornamental gardens and ecological restoration projects.
