Vitex rotundifolia f. heterophylla

Vitex rotundifolia f. heterophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf chaste tree, is a compact, shrub-like form of the chaste tree with distinctive foliage.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are narrower than the typical rounded leaves of the species, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically in shades of blue or purple, which bloom in the summer months. It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

This cultivar is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and adaptability to various climates.
