Vriesea montana

Vriesea montana, commonly known as the mountain vriesea, is a species of bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, spathaceous inflorescence that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is well-suited for indoor cultivation.

Vriesea montana is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in horticulture for its long-lasting blooms.
