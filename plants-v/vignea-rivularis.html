Vignea rivularis

Vignea rivularis, commonly known as the river vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive waxy coating that helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink. Its rapid growth habit makes it suitable for ornamental use in gardens and as a ground cover.

Vignea rivularis thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found near water sources.
