Vinca haussknechtii

Vinca haussknechtii, commonly known as the rock vine, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and produces vibrant blue to purple flowers. The plant has opposite, narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
