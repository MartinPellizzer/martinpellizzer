Viola kosaninii

Viola kosaninii, commonly known as the Kosanin violet, is a species of flowering plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and vibrant purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Viola kosaninii is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.

Its flowers bloom in early spring, adding a splash of color to the landscape.
