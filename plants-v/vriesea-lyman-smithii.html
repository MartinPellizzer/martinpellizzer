Vriesea lyman-smithii

Vriesea lyman-smithii, commonly known as the Lyman-Smith vriesea, is a tropical plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its striking, long-lasting inflorescence. The plant features broad, strap-like leaves that form a rosette, creating a central water-holding structure. It produces vibrant, colorful bracts that can range from red to orange, often resembling flowers.

This species is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
