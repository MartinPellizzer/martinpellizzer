Vitis yuenlingensis

Vitis yuenlingensis, commonly known as Yuenling grapevine, is a species native to China.

It belongs to the Vitaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and compound leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into berries. Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in horticultural and ecological studies.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
