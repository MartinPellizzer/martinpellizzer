Viola spegazzinii

Viola spegazzinii, commonly known as the Spegazzini violet, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its small, heart-shaped leaves and delicate, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in cool, moist conditions. It is often found in limestone soils and is adapted to high-altitude climates.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and specialized species within its habitat.
