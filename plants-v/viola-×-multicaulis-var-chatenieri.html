Viola × multicaulis var. chatenieri

Viola × multicaulis var. chatenieri, commonly known as the French violet, is a hybrid perennial plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped, violet-colored leaves and showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from pale purple to deep violet. The plant typically grows to a height of 10–30 cm and thrives in moist, well-drained soils in partial to full shade.

It is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens or as a ground cover.
