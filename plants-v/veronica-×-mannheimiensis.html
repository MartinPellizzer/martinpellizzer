Veronica × mannheimiensis

Veronica × mannheimiensis, commonly known as the Mannheim speedwell, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Plantaginaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 15-30 cm and thrives in well-drained soil. It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.

Veronica × mannheimiensis is well-suited for rock gardens and borders, preferring full sun to partial shade.
