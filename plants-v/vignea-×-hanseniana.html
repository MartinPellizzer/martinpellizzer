Vignea × hanseniana

Vignea × hanseniana, commonly known as the Hansen's vigna, is a hybrid plant species within the legume family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and trifoliate leaves, which are typically green with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, followed by elongated pods containing several seeds. Vignea × hanseniana is native to tropical and subtropical regions and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
