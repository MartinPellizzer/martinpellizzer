Veronica Subfulvida

Veronica subfulvida is a rare and striking plant native to specific regions of southern Africa. It is known for its distinctive dark purple flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual impact. This species thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous habitats. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered vulnerable to environmental changes. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this unique botanical gem.
