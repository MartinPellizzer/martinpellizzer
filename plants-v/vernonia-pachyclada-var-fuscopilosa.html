Vernonia pachyclada var. fuscopilosa

Vernonia pachyclada var. fuscopilosa, commonly known as the dark-haired vernonia, is a shrub native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, gray-green leaves that are covered in fine, dark hairs. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom during the dry season. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid regions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance in landscaping.
