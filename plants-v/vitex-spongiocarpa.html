Vitex spongiocarpa

Vitex spongiocarpa, commonly known as the sponge tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, rough, sponge-like bark that gives it its common name. The plant has opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It produces small, clustered flowers in shades of purple or white, followed by woody, spherical fruits.

Vitex spongiocarpa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adaptable to a variety of soil types.
