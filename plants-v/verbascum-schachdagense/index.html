Verbascum Schachdagense

Verbascum schachdagense is a rare and intriguing plant native to a small region in Turkey. It belongs to the genus Verbascum, commonly known as mullein, which includes many species with distinctive fuzzy leaves and showy flowers. This particular species is notable for its limited distribution and unique ecological niche. Due to habitat loss and environmental changes, it faces significant conservation challenges. Studying Verbascum schachdagense offers valuable insights into biodiversity and the importance of preserving endangered flora.
