Viola davidii var. paucicrenata

Viola davidii var. paucicrenata, commonly known as the Chinese violet, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of purple and white, typically blooming in early spring. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and early blooming period.
