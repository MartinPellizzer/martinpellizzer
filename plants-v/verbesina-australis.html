Verbesina australis

Verbesina australis, commonly known as the Australian ironweed, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges. The plant produces showy yellow-orange inflorescences composed of numerous small, tubular flowers. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, giving them a rough texture.

Verbesina australis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
