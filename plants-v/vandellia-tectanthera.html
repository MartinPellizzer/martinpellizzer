Vandellia tectanthera

Vandellia tectanthera, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its flowers are usually violet or purple in color, with a unique structure that includes a spurred lip.

Vandellia tectanthera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and resilience.
