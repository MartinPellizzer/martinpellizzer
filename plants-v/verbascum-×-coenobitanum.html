Verbascum × coenobitanum

Verbascum × coenobitanum, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the genus Verbascum, which includes several species of flowering plants known for their fuzzy leaves and showy yellow or orange flowers. The hybrid is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, velvety leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. It produces clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

This plant is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its striking foliage and floral display.
