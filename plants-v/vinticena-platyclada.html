Vinticena platyclada

Vinticena platyclada, commonly known as the broad-leaf vinticena, is a unique flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color with a smooth, waxy texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, often with a pale yellow or white hue, which bloom during the rainy season. Vinticena platyclada is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
