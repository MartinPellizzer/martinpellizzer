Velleia arguta

Velleia arguta, commonly known as the smooth velleia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a smooth texture with a slightly waxy coating.

Velleia arguta produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.
