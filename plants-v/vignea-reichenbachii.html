Vignea reichenbachii

Vignea reichenbachii, commonly known as the Reichenbach's vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its climbing habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale purple, followed by pod fruits containing small, round seeds.
