Verbascum nitidum var. parisiense

Verbascum nitidum var. parisiense, commonly known as the Paris golden ragwort, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the genus Verbascum, which includes species known for their showy, fluffy flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky areas. Its leaves are hairy and gray-green in color, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in clusters.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its striking appearance.
