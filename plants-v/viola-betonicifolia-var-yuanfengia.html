Viola betonicifolia var. yuanfengia

Viola betonicifolia var. yuanfengia, commonly known as the Yuanfeng viola, is a variant of the betonicifolia viola native to China.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of violet-blue flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its foliage is often covered in a fine, downy layer, giving it a soft, velvety texture.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate beauty.
