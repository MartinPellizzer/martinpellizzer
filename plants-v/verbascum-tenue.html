Verbascum tenue

Verbascum tenue, commonly known as narrowleaf mullein, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe, North Africa, and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that bear large, velvety gray-green leaves. The plant produces showy yellow flowers in late summer, which are clustered in a distinctive cylindrical shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Narrowleaf mullein is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally for its soothing effects on respiratory ailments.
