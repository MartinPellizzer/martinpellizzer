Vriesea graciliscapa

Vriesea graciliscapa, commonly known as the slender-leaf vriesea, is a tropical flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces striking, pendulous inflorescences with vibrant, colorful bracts that can range from red to orange. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Vriesea graciliscapa is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and long-lasting blooms.
