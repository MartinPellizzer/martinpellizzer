Vitekorchis klotzschiana

Vitekorchis klotzschiana, commonly known as the Klotzschian orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage and produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically blooms in late spring to early summer, and its inflorescence consists of several showy, lip-shaped petals. Vitekorchis klotzschiana is epiphytic in nature, often growing on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments.

It requires well-drained soil and consistent moisture to thrive.
