Vellozia schnizleinia

Vellozia schnizleinia, commonly known as the Schinzlein's vellozia, is a rare and striking member of the Velloziaceae family native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle, an adaptation to its arid environment. The plant produces striking, cone-like inflorescences that bear small, white or pale yellow flowers. It is typically found in rocky outcrops and sandy soils, thriving in regions with low rainfall.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical study and conservation efforts.
