Verbena calliantha var. microsoma

Verbena calliantha var. microsoma, commonly known as the dwarf verbena, is a compact, flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and clusters of vibrant blue or purple flowers that bloom throughout the summer. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers. It is a perennial in mild climates but can be grown as an annual in cooler regions.

Its compact growth habit and long blooming period make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
