Viola Nuttallii Var. Vallicola

Viola nuttallii var. vallicola is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of western North America. It thrives in cool, moist environments such as forest floors and rocky outcrops. Known for its vibrant purple petals and heart-shaped leaves, this species is a standout among the viola family. Due to habitat loss and climate change, it faces increasing threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique botanical gem.
