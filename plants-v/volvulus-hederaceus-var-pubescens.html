Volvulus hederaceus var. pubescens

Volvulus hederaceus var. pubescens, commonly known as the pubescent wood sorrel, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves with a hairy or pubescent texture. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.
