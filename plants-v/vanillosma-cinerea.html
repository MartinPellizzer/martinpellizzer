Vanillosma cinerea

Vanillosma cinerea, commonly known as the silverleaf vanillosma, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
