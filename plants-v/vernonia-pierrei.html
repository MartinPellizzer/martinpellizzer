Vernonia pierrei

Vernonia pierrei, commonly known as the African ironweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it adaptable to various environments.

Vernonia pierrei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
