Volkameria diversifolia

Volkameria diversifolia, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, spreading canopy and its showy, yellow, pendant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. Volkameria diversifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to provide shade.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it adaptable to various garden settings.
