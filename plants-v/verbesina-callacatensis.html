Verbesina callacatensis

Verbesina callacatensis, commonly known as the Callacata goldenrod, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, sunny habitats such as grasslands and forest edges. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
