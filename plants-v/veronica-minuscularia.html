Veronica minuscularia

Veronica minuscularia, commonly known as the lesser speedwell, is a flowering plant native to Europe, western Asia, and northern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 20 centimeters. The plant features small, blue or violet flowers that bloom in clusters, often in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Veronica minuscularia is often found in moist soils, such as along stream banks or in woodland areas.
