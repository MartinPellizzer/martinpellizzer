Vernonia bauchiensis

Vernonia bauchiensis, commonly known as the Bauchi Vernonia, is a flowering plant native to Nigeria.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish to brownish flower heads that are typically clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
