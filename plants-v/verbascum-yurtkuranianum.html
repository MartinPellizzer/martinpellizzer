Verbascum yurtkuranianum

Verbascum yurtkuranianum, commonly known as the Yurtkuranian mullein, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the genus Verbascum, which includes species known for their large, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is characterized by its tall, hairy stems and yellow-orange flowers. Its leaves are large, broad, and covered in soft, fuzzy hairs.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
