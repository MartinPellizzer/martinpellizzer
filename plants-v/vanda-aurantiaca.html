Vanda aurantiaca

Vanda aurantiaca, commonly known as the orange vanda, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety orange flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has long, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a dense, velvety coating. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining potting media.

Vanda aurantiaca is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant colors and relatively easy care requirements.
