Vermeulenia × dafnii

Vermeulenia × dafnii, commonly known as the hybrid violet, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers that range in color from purple to white. The plant typically grows to a height of 10–30 cm, with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and woodland settings.

The flowers bloom in early spring, adding a touch of elegance to gardens with their refined appearance.
