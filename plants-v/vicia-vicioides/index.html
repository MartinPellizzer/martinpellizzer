Vicia Vicioides

Vicia vicioides is a rare and delicate legume native to southern Europe. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in Mediterranean climates. This plant is known for its small, pea-like flowers and its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil. Due to habitat loss, it is now considered endangered in many regions. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique species.
