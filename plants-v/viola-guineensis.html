Viola guineensis

Viola guineensis, commonly known as the African violet, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers in a wide range of colors, including purple, blue, and white. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in a fine, velvety texture.

Viola guineensis is often cultivated as a popular ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and ability to thrive in indoor environments.
