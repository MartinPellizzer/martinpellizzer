Vitex gamosepala

Vitex gamosepala, commonly known as the Australian vitex or broad-leaved vitex, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils. The plant features opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often hairy on the underside. Its flowers are clustered in spikes and range in color from white to purple, attracting various pollinators.

The fruit is a dry capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
