Volvulus pubescens

Volvulus pubescens, commonly known as the hairy volvulus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features hairy stems and leaves, which contribute to its common name. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in compact inflorescences.

Volvulus pubescens is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
