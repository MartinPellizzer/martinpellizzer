Verbena × baileyana

Verbena × baileyana, commonly known as common verbena, is a hybrid perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, clustered flowers in shades of violet, pink, and white, which bloom throughout the summer. The plant has square, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.

Verbena × baileyana is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
