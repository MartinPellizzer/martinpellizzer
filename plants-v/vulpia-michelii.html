Vulpia michelii

Vulpia michelii, commonly known as the Michel's violetgrass, is a small annual grass native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its flowers are inconspicuous, with tiny spikelets that contain three to five florets.

Vulpia michelii is adapted to arid conditions and is often found in disturbed soils.
