Verbascum slavonicum

Verbascum slavonicum, commonly known as Slavonian mullein, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae and is characterized by its tall, hairy stems and large, fuzzy leaves. The plant produces showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in meadows and along roadsides.
