Vernonia volkameriifolia subsp. siamica

Vernonia volkameriifolia subsp.

siamica, commonly known as the Siam Vernonia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia. It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions.

The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered yellow flowers. It is often found in lowland areas with well-drained soil.

The species is valued for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
