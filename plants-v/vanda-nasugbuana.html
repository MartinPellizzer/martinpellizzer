Vanda nasugbuana

Vanda nasugbuana, commonly known as the Philippine vanda, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to the Philippines.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with intricate patterns. The plant has thick, fleshy roots adapted for absorbing moisture from the air and rain. Its leaves are broad and waxy, helping to retain water in its natural habitat.

Vanda nasugbuana thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect sunlight, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
