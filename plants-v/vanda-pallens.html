Vanda pallens

Vanda pallens, commonly known as the white vanda, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, strap-like leaves that are typically bright green with a waxy texture. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and lavender, often in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Vanda pallens is popular among orchid enthusiasts due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
