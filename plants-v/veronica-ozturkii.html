Veronica ozturkii

Veronica ozturkii, commonly known as Turkish speedwell, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its small, blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions. It has slender stems and opposite leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs.
