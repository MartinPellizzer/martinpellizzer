Vesper

Vesper, commonly known as the evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow or orange flowers that bloom in the evening. The plant belongs to the genus Oenothera and is known for its medicinal properties. Its seeds are rich in essential fatty acids and are often used in supplements.

The leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
