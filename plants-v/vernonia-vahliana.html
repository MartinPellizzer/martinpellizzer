Vernonia vahliana

Vernonia vahliana, commonly known as the yellow ironwood, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal and sandy habitats. The plant produces bright yellow flower clusters that bloom during the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a rough texture.

Vernonia vahliana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
