Vincetoxicum pyrenaicum

Vincetoxicum pyrenaicum, commonly known as the Pyrenean bindweed, is a flowering plant native to the Pyrenees region.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its slender, trailing stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its flowers are typically followed by bright red berries that contain seeds.

Vincetoxicum pyrenaicum is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
