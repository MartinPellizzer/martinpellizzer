Valeriana olitoria

Valeriana olitoria, commonly known as water valerian, is a flowering plant native to wetland areas in Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, branched stems and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has aromatic leaves and roots that are often used in traditional medicine for their calming properties. Valeriana olitoria thrives in moist soils and is often found near water bodies such as ponds and streams.

Its botanical features make it an important species in wetland ecosystems.
