Velaga globosa

Velaga globosa, commonly known as the globular milkweed, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its rounded, fleshy leaves and produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant is known for its milky sap, which is a distinctive botanical feature. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in moist, shaded areas.

Velaga globosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
