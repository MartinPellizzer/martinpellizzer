Vitex kuylenii

Vitex kuylenii, commonly known as the African blue mistflower, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, often branched stems and small, opposite leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of blue to purple flowers that bloom in the summer, attracting various pollinators. Its fruits are small, dry capsules that contain several seeds.

Vitex kuylenii is often used in traditional medicine and is also valued for its ornamental appeal.
