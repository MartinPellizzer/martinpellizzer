Vinsonia media

Vinsonia media, commonly known as the golden bell vine, is a climbing plant native to South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. The plant produces striking yellow-orange flowers that resemble bells, blooming in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Vinsonia media is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant.
