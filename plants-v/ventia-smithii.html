Ventia smithii

Ventia smithii, commonly known as the Smith's ventia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often appearing in pairs along the stem.

Ventia smithii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
