Viola × modesta

Viola × modesta, commonly known as the common violet, is a hybrid species within the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, typically violet or purple flowers. The plant is native to Europe and has been widely cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Viola × modesta is often used in gardens for its ability to naturalize and spread easily.
