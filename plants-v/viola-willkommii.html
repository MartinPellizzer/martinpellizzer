Viola willkommii

Viola willkommii, commonly known as the Willkomm violet, is a perennial flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and vibrant violet or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its flowers bloom in spring and are known for their delicate appearance and sweet fragrance.

Viola willkommii is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
