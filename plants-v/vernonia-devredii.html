Vernonia devredii

Vernonia devredii, commonly known as the Devred's ironweed, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the sunflower family, Asteraceae, and is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense, spike-like inflorescences. The plant produces small, tubular yellow flowers that are typically clustered in compact heads. Vernonia devredii thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland and rocky habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been cultivated for its hardy nature and long-lasting blooms.
