Veronica clusii

Veronica clusii, commonly known as the clustered speedwell, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit and produces clusters of small, sky-blue to white flowers. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its delicate appearance.
