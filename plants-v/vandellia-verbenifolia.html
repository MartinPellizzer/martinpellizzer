Vandellia verbenifolia

Vandellia verbenifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf vandellia, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-white, velvety foliage that gives it its common name. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale blue or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

Vandellia verbenifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
