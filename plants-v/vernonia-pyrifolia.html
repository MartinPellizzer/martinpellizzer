Vernonia pyrifolia

Vernonia pyrifolia, commonly known as the flame tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking orange to red flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling flames. The plant has feathery, bipinnate leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental species.

Vernonia pyrifolia is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
