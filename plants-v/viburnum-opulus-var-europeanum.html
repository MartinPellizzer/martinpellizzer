Viburnum opulus var. europeanum

Viburnum opulus var. europeanum, commonly known as the European dogwood, is a deciduous shrub native to Europe and western Asia.

It features showy, flat-topped clusters of white flowers in early summer, followed by bright red berries in the fall. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that turn vibrant shades of red and orange in autumn. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
