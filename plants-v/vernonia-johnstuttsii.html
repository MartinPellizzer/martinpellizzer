Vernonia johnstuttsii

Vernonia johnstuttsii, commonly known as the Johnstutts' ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Vernonia johnstuttsii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local pollinators.
