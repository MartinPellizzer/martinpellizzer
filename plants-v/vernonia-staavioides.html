Vernonia staavioides

Vernonia staavioides, commonly known as the African ironweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces clusters of small, blue to violet flowers that bloom during the dry season. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a rough texture.

Vernonia staavioides is often found in grasslands and along riverbanks, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
