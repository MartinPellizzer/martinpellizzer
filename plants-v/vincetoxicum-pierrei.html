Vincetoxicum pierrei

Vincetoxicum pierrei, commonly known as the European ironweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features upright stems that are covered in fine, soft hairs, and it produces clusters of small, pale yellow to greenish flowers. The plant has palmately lobed leaves that are typically heart-shaped or ovate in outline. Vincetoxicum pierrei is known for its ability to thrive in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade conditions.

It is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and flowering habit.
