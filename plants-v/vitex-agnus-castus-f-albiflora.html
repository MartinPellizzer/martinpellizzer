Vitex agnus-castus f. albiflora

Vitex agnus-castus f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered chaste tree, is a botanical variant of the common chaste tree.

It is characterized by its pale white flowers, which contrast with the typical purple blooms of the species. The plant belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is native to the Mediterranean region. Its foliage consists of opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture.
