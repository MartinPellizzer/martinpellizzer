Vriesea gigantea var. seideliana

Vriesea gigantea var. seideliana, commonly known as the Giant Vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 meters in length, forming a rosette structure. The plant produces tall inflorescences with vibrant, long-lasting flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil with high humidity.

This variety is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to bloom repeatedly over several years.
