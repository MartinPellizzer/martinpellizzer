Vanda parishii

Vanda parishii, commonly known as the Singapore orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, leafy stems and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically blooms in summer, producing large, white or pale pink flowers with a distinctive purple lip. Vanda parishii thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

It is a popular ornamental plant due to its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
