Vonitra

Vonitra, commonly known as the Madagascar orchid, is a unique flowering plant native to the island of Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored blooms. The plant typically features long, slender stems and produces clusters of small, intricate flowers. Vonitra thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its botanical significance lies in its role as an endemic species, contributing to the rich biodiversity of Madagascar.
