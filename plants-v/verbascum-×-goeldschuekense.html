Verbascum × goeldschuekense

Verbascum × goeldschuekense, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a flowering plant in the genus Verbascum.

It is characterized by its tall, hairy stems and large, lobed leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces showy, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is typically found in disturbed soils and open areas, thriving in full sun.

This hybrid species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been cultivated for its unique appearance and adaptability.
