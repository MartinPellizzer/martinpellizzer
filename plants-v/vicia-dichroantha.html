Vicia dichroantha

Vicia dichroantha, commonly known as the two-colored vetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant produces small, oblong pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or grasslands.

Vicia dichroantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in ecological systems.
