Vernonia kigomae

Vernonia kigomae, commonly known as the Kigoma Vernonia, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in moist, wooded habitats. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters. Its leaves are usually narrow and slightly hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Vernonia kigomae is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
