Vouapa latifolia

Vouapa latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf vouapa, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Vouapa latifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local medicine.
