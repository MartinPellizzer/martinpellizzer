Vriesea correia-arauji

Vriesea correia-arauji, commonly known as the rainbow vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful inflorescence that displays a range of hues, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that collect water, forming a central pool. Its flowering period typically occurs during the warmer months, and it thrives in bright, indirect light.

Vriesea correia-arauji is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
