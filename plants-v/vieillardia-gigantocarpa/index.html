Vieillardia Gigantocarpa

Vieillardia tree, scientifically known as Vieillardia gigantocarpa, is a rare and unique species native to the cloud forests of Central America. This tree is characterized by its massive, buttressed roots and its ability to thrive in high humidity and nutrient-poor soils. Its striking appearance includes large, waxy leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom during the rainy season. Despite its ecological importance, Vieillardia gigantocarpa faces threats from deforestation and habitat loss. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this remarkable tree for future generations.
