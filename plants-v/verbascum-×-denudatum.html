Verbascum × denudatum

Verbascum × denudatum, commonly known as the common mullein.

It is a hybrid species within the genus Verbascum, characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, velvety leaves. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. It is native to southern Europe and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Verbascum × denudatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
