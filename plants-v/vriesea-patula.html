Vriesea patula

Vriesea patula, commonly known as the silver sword, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, sword-like leaves that are covered in a silvery, powdery coating. The plant produces striking inflorescences with bright orange flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Vriesea patula is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
