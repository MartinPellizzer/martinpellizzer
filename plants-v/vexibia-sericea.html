Vexibia sericea

Vexibia sericea, commonly known as silverleaf vexibia, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Vexibia sericea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
