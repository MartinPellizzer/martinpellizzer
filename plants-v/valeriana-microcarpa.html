Valeriana microcarpa

Valeriana microcarpa, commonly known as the dwarf valerian, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or white in color. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a somewhat hairy texture.

Valeriana microcarpa is often found in moist, shady environments such as forest floors or along stream banks.
