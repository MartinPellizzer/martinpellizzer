Veselskya griffithiana

Veselskya griffithiana, commonly known as the Kashmir rose, is a shrub native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, single flowers with five petals, typically in shades of pink or red. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
