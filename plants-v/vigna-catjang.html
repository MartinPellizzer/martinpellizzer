Vigna catjang

Vigna catjang, commonly known as catjang bean, is a leguminous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. The plant typically grows as an annual herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its flowers are usually purple or pink, and it thrives in warm, tropical climates.

Vigna catjang is valued for its edible seeds, which are used in various traditional dishes.
