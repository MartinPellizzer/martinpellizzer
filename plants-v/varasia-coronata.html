Varasia coronata

Varasia coronata, commonly known as the crown vetch, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial legume that thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces yellowish-white flowers in spring. Its root system is deep and robust, making it drought-tolerant and useful for erosion control.

Varasia coronata is often cultivated as a ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and flowering habit.
