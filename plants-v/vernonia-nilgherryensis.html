Vernonia nilgherryensis

Vernonia nilgherryensis, commonly known as Nilgiri Vernonia, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a rough texture, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
