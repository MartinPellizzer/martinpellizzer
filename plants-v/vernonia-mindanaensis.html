Vernonia mindanaensis

Vernonia mindanaensis, commonly known as the Mindanao vernonia, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as an erect shrub with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. Its yellow flowers are arranged in dense clusters, making it visually striking.

It is often found in open woodland and disturbed areas, thriving in tropical climates.
