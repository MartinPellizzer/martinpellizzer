Vallaris sinensis

Vallaris sinensis, commonly known as Chinese vallaris, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a member of the Oleaceae family and typically grows as a shrub or small tree. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or yellow in color. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Vallaris sinensis is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive blooms and adaptability to various soil types.
