Viola filicetorum var. parthenica

Viola filicetorum var. parthenica, commonly known as the Parthenic violet, is a herbaceous perennial native to the Mediterranean region.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, violet-blue flowers with a distinctive yellow center. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
