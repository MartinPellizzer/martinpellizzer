Vitis yokogurana

Vitis yokogurana, commonly known as Japanese grapevine, is a woody vine native to Japan.

It belongs to the Vitaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and compound leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by red or black berries. It is often found in forested areas and thrives in temperate climates.

Its berries are sometimes used in traditional Japanese cuisine and herbal remedies.
