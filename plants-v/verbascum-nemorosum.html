Verbascum nemorosum

Verbascum nemorosum, commonly known as the wood mullein, is a perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae and is characterized by its tall, erect stems covered in soft, fuzzy hairs. Verbascum nemorosum thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.
