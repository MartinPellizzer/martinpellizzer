Verbascum × kayseriense

Verbascum × kayseriense, commonly known as the Turkish mullein, is a hybrid plant belonging to the genus Verbascum in the family Scrophulariaceae.

It is characterized by its tall, hairy stems and large, fuzzy leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Native to Turkey and the eastern Mediterranean, it thrives in dry, sunny environments.

It is often used in traditional medicine and as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance.
