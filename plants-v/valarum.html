Valarum

Valarum, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a species native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which helps it conserve water in its harsh environment. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. Valarum thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a valuable addition to drought-resistant gardens.
