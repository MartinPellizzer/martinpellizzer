Vangueria spinosa var. mollis

Vangueria spinosa var. mollis, commonly known as the soft spinous vangueria, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny stems and small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which are typically followed by small, round fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
