Vignea curvata

Vignea curvata, commonly known as the curly vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. Its rapid growth habit makes it suitable for ornamental use in gardens and as a ground cover.

Vignea curvata thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
