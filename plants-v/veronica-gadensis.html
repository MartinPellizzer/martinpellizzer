Veronica gadensis

Veronica gadensis, commonly known as the Mediterranean speedwell, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its small, blue or purple flowers that bloom in early spring. Veronica gadensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
