Verbascum omanense

Verbascum omanense, commonly known as the Oman mullein, is a flowering plant native to the Arabian Peninsula.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae and is characterized by its tall, hairy stems and large, showy yellow flowers. Verbascum omanense is often found in rocky soils and is a common sight in the mountainous regions of Oman.
