Vriesea urbaniana

Vriesea urbaniana, commonly known as the Orange Sword or the Brazilian Sword, is a species of flowering plant in the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to the rainforests of Brazil and is characterized by its striking, sword-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a reddish or orange hue. The plant produces colorful, long-lasting inflorescences that can range from yellow to orange, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

Vriesea urbaniana is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
