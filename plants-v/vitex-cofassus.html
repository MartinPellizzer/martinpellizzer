Vitex cofassus

Vitex cofassus, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange floral clusters that appear at the base of its leaves. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with ovate to lanceolate leaflets. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that give way to woody, spherical fruits containing several seeds.

Vitex cofassus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to warm, humid climates.
