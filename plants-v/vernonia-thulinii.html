Vernonia thulinii

Vernonia thulinii, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its woody stem and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in heads, typical of the daisy family. It is known for its hard, dense wood, which is highly valued for construction and furniture-making.

Vernonia thulinii is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
