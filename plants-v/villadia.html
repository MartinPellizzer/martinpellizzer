Villadia

Villadia, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a tropical plant native to parts of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive silvery-gray leaves that reflect light, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, often attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. Its wood is valued for its durability and is used in traditional crafts and construction.

Villadia also has medicinal properties, with some parts of the plant used in local herbal remedies.
