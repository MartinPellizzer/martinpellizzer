Viola phalacrocarpa f. glaberrima

Viola phalacrocarpa f. glaberrima, commonly known as the smooth-leaved violet, is a horticultural form of the common violet.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous leaves and vibrant purple flowers. This variety thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both gardeners and botanists.
