Vicia tenuifolia var. filiformis

Vicia tenuifolia var. filiformis, commonly known as the slender vetch, is a variant of the slender vetch species.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its fine, thread-like leaves. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pale yellow or greenish in color. It is native to regions of Europe and is often found in grassy areas and along roadsides.

This variety is noted for its delicate appearance and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
