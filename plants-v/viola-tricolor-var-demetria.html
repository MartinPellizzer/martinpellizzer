Viola tricolor var. demetria

Viola tricolor var. demetria, commonly known as the Purple Heart Viola, is a compact, low-growing perennial plant native to Europe.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of violet, white, and yellow. The plant thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Its foliage and flowers remain attractive throughout the growing season, adding color to gardens from spring to early summer.
