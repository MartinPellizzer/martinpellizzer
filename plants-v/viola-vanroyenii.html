Viola vanroyenii

Viola vanroyenii, commonly known as the Van Royen's violet, is a species of flowering plant in the Violaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe, particularly in areas with acidic soils. The plant features heart-shaped, velvety leaves and produces delicate, violet-blue flowers with yellow markings. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in woodland or rocky habitats.

Viola vanroyenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
