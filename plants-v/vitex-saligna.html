Vitex saligna

Vitex saligna, commonly known as the chaste tree or vitex, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall, featuring opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces fragrant, blue or purple flowers in panicles during the summer months. Its fruit is a dry, woody capsule containing several small, brown seeds.

Vitex saligna is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
