Vaccinium sorsogonense

Vaccinium sorsogonense, commonly known as the Sorsogonan huckleberry, is a species of shrub native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes other familiar plants like blueberries and cranberries. The plant produces small, edible berries that are typically dark blue or purple in color. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.
