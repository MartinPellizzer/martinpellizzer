Vandellia sessiliflora

Vandellia sessiliflora, commonly known as the coastal vandellia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, white or pale blue flowers. It is adapted to withstand salt spray and strong winds typical of its coastal habitat.

Vandellia sessiliflora is often found in dune environments and is considered an important species for coastal vegetation.
