Viola tracheliifolia f. olokelensis

Viola tracheliifolia f. olokelensis, commonly known as the Olokele viola, is a rare and endemic plant species native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive violet-blue flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in native forests or along streambanks. It is notable for its compact growth habit and is often cultivated in botanical gardens due to its ornamental value.

Conservation efforts are crucial for its survival, as it is threatened by habitat loss and invasive species.
