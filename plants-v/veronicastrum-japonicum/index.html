Veronicastrum Japonicum

Veronicastrum japonicum is a striking perennial plant native to Japan known for its tall spikes of blue-violet flowers. It thrives in moist environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream sides. This plant is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and ease of care. Its foliage is lance-shaped and provides a nice contrast to its vibrant blooms. Veronicastrum japonicum is a great choice for adding color and texture to any landscape.
