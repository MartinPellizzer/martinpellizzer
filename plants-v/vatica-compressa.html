Vatica compressa

Vatica compressa, commonly known as the slender ironwood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and rough, fissured bark. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. Its hard, heavy wood is highly valued for its durability and strength.

Vatica compressa plays an important ecological role in its native habitats, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
