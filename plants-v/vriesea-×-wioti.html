Vriesea × wioti

Vriesea × wioti, commonly known as the hybrid vriesea, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Vriesea × spectabilis and Vriesea × bracteata.

It is a member of the Bromeliaceae family, characterized by its striking, colorful inflorescences that can range from orange to red. The plant typically features broad, sword-shaped leaves that are often variegated with silver or white markings. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Vriesea × wioti is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its long-lasting blooms and ornamental foliage.
