Veronica longifolia var. angustata

Veronica longifolia var. angustata, commonly known as narrowleaf speedwell, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a perennial species that typically grows in moist, grassy habitats. The plant features small, blue or violet flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in early summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slight hairy texture.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in garden landscapes.
