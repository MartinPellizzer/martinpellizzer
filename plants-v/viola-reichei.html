Viola reichei

Viola reichei, commonly known as the Reiche's violet, is a species of flowering plant in the family Violaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Africa, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces showy violet flowers with distinctive yellow markings, often appearing in clusters. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Viola reichei is valued both for its beauty and its role in local ecosystems.
