Vicia epetiolaris var. microcarpa

Vicia epetiolaris var. microcarpa, commonly known as the dwarf vetch, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, trifoliate leaves with slender stipules, and its flowers are typically pale yellow to cream in color. The plant produces small, oval pods that contain several seeds. It is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or limestone habitats.

Due to its compact size and ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
