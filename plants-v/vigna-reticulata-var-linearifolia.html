Vigna reticulata var. linearifolia

Vigna reticulata var. linearifolia, commonly known as the slender mung bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, linear leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that lead to elongated, narrow pods. The plant is valued for its edible seeds, which are rich in protein and used in various cuisines. It thrives in warm climates with well-drained soil and requires a long growing season.

The variety is often cultivated for its nutritional benefits and adaptability to different agricultural conditions.
