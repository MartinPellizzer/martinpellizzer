Vaccinium amblyandrum var. pungens

Vaccinium amblyandrum var. pungens, commonly known as the roughleaf huckleberry, is a shrub native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes other familiar plants like blueberries and rhododendrons. This species thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks.
