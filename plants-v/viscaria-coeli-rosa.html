Viscaria coeli-rosa

Viscaria coeli-rosa, commonly known as the celestial rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with dark green, slightly hairy leaves. Its flowers are borne on long, slender stems, giving it an elegant appearance.

Viscaria coeli-rosa is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and attracts pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
