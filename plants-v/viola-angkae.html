Viola angkae

Viola angkae, commonly known as the Angka violet, is a species of flowering plant in the Violaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, particularly in countries like Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of purple and white. Viola angkae is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky crevices.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
