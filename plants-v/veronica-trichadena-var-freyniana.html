Veronica trichadena var. freyniana

Veronica trichadena var. freyniana, commonly known as Freyn's speedwell, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces small, blue or violet flowers in clusters. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in dry conditions.

Its botanical characteristics make it a hardy and adaptable species for garden cultivation.
