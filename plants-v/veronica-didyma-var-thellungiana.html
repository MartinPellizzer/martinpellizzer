Veronica didyma var. thellungiana

Veronica didyma var. thellungiana, commonly known as the Thellungian speedwell, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe.

It features upright stems that bear clusters of blue to violet flowers, which bloom in early summer. The leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine, silky hairs, giving them a soft texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
