Vaccinium macrostemon

Vaccinium macrostemon, commonly known as the large-flowered huckleberry, is a species of shrub native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes many acid-loving plants such as blueberries and cranberries. The shrub thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in coniferous forest understories.
