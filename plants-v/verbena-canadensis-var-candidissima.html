Verbena canadensis var. candidissima

Verbena canadensis var. candidissima, commonly known as white vervain, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features slender, upright stems and small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale blue. The plant has opposite, narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in open fields and along roadsides.

Its showy blooms attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, making it a valuable addition to native gardens.
