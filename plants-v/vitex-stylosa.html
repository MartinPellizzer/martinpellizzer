Vitex stylosa

Vitex stylosa, commonly known as the Indian lilac or champa, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces fragrant, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate with a smooth margin.

Vitex stylosa is known for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
