Verbena × teasii

Verbena × teasii, commonly known as the hybrid verbena, is a flowering plant belonging to the Verbenaceae family.

It is a compact, bushy perennial that produces vibrant clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, purple, and blue. The plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in full sun with well-drained soil. It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Verbena × teasii is known for its drought tolerance and ease of care, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
