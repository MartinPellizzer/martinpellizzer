Verbascum gloeotrichum

Verbascum gloeotrichum, commonly known as the woolly mullein, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems covered in soft, fuzzy hairs. The plant produces showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Verbascum gloeotrichum is a perennial species that is both ornamental and historically used for medicinal purposes.
