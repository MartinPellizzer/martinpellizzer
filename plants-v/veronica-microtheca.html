Veronica microtheca

Veronica microtheca, commonly known as the small-leaved speedwell, is a perennial flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of blue or violet flowers. Veronica microtheca is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade.
