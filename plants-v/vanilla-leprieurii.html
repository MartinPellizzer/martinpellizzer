Vanilla leprieurii

Vanilla leprieurii, commonly known as the Madagascar vanilla, is a climbing orchid native to the island of Madagascar.

It is characterized by its long, thin stems and broad, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces fragrant, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by long, thin pods containing the vanilla beans. These beans are harvested and cured to develop their distinctive aroma and flavor.

Vanilla leprieurii is highly valued in the culinary and fragrance industries for its rich, complex scent.
