Viburnum tinus var. jimenezii

Viburnum tinus var. jimenezii, commonly known as the Jimenez boxwood, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a pyramidal shape, making it ideal for formal gardens or hedging. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in late winter, followed by red berries that attract birds. It is highly tolerant of pruning and thrives in well-drained soil with partial to full sun.

Its ornamental value lies in its year-round foliage and adaptability to various garden conditions.
