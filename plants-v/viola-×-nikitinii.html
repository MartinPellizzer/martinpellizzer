Viola × nikitinii

Viola × nikitinii, commonly known as the Nikitin's violet, is a hybrid species within the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, often bi-colored flowers that range from purple to white. The plant typically features heart-shaped, velvety leaves with a distinct waxy coating. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, it thrives in cool, moist environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
