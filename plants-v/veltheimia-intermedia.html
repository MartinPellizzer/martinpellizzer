Veltheimia intermedia

Veltheimia intermedia, commonly known as the Natal fernbush, is a perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features strap-like, grass-like leaves that are typically dark green and slightly curled. The plant produces striking, cone-shaped inflorescences with bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Veltheimia intermedia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various garden conditions.
