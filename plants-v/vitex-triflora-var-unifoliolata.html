Vitex triflora var. unifoliolata

Vitex triflora var. unifoliolata, commonly known as the single leaf chaste tree, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features opposite, simple leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant produces small, fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. These flowers are clustered in panicles and attract various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release small, smooth seeds.
