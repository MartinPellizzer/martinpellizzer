Veronica arvensis var. rigida

Veronica arvensis var. rigida, commonly known as rigid speedwell, is a flowering plant in the Plantaginaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, blue or violet flowers in clusters, typically blooming from late spring to early summer. It is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats such as roadsides and fields.

This variety is distinguished from the typical form by its more rigid and compact growth structure.
