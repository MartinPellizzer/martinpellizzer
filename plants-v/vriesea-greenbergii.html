Vriesea greenbergii

Vriesea greenbergii, commonly known as the Greenberg's Vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, sword-shaped leaves that form a rosette structure, creating a central cup to collect rainwater. The plant produces striking inflorescences with bright, colorful bracts that can range from red to orange and yellow. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Vriesea greenbergii is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and long-lasting blooms.
