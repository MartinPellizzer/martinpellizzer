Vitex mombassae

Vitex mombassae, commonly known as the African blue bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically blue or purple in color, which bloom in clusters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a rough texture with serrated edges.

The fruit is a small, dry capsule containing several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine.
