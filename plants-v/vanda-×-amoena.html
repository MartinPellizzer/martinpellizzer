Vanda × amoena

Vanda × amoena, commonly known as the hybrid vanda orchid, is a popular epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that come in a wide range of colors, including white, pink, purple, and red. The plant produces long, pendant-like inflorescences with numerous blooms, making it a favorite among orchid enthusiasts. Vanda × amoena thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining potting media.

Its adaptability and vibrant floral display make it a versatile choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
