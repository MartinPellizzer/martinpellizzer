Vismia lehmannii

Vismia lehmannii, commonly known as the African snowball, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features dense clusters of white, waxy flowers that bloom in spring, giving it a striking appearance. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate, with a smooth, glossy surface.

Vismia lehmannii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well-suited to warm, sunny climates.
