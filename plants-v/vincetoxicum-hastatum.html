Vincetoxicum hastatum

Vincetoxicum hastatum, commonly known as the red baneberry, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It features compound leaves with finely toothed edges and bright red berries that appear in late summer. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters, which are followed by the distinctive, poisonous berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and thickets.

The berries contain toxic compounds, making it important to handle with care.
