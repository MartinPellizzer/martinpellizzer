Vernonia proteopsis

Vernonia proteopsis, commonly known as the silverleaf vernonia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Vernonia proteopsis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
