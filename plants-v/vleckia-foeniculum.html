Vleckia foeniculum

Vleckia foeniculum, commonly known as sweet fennel, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its finely divided, feathery leaves and a tall, slender stem that bears small yellow flowers. The plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often cultivated for its aromatic seeds and as a culinary herb.
