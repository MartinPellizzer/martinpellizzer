Veronica rotunda f. albiflora

Veronica rotunda f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered round veronica, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to southern Europe.

It features dense, rounded clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has silver-gray, hairy leaves that are round and slightly succulent, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value lies in its delicate flowers and compact growth habit.
