Veronica shichengensis

Veronica shichengensis, commonly known as the Shicheng Veronica, is a perennial flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of blue to purple flowers. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its limited distribution and unique appearance, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
