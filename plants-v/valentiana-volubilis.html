Valentiana volubilis

Valentiana volubilis, commonly known as the climbing valerian, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial vine characterized by its trailing growth habit and aromatic, small pink or white flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden borders or trellises.

Its botanical features make it an attractive addition to both ornamental and ecological landscapes.
