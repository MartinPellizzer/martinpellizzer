Viola chinensis var. media

Viola chinensis var. media, commonly known as the Chinese violet or the common violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of violet, blue, and white, often with a distinctive yellow eye. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is commonly cultivated in gardens.
