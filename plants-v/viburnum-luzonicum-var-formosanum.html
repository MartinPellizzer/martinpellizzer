Viburnum luzonicum var. formosanum

Viburnum luzonicum var. formosanum, commonly known as the Formosan evergreen viburnum, is a shrub native to Taiwan.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that remain evergreen throughout the year. The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers in spring, which are followed by bright red berries in autumn. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, acidic soil.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to provide habitat for wildlife.
