Vitex goetzei

Vitex goetzei, commonly known as the Goetze's chaste tree, is a species of flowering plant in the verbena family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically purple or violet in color. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can thrive in well-drained soil.
