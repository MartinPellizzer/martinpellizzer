Verbena melindres

Verbena melindres, commonly known as red verbena, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red or purple flowers. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Verbena melindres is often used in ornamental gardening for its showy blooms and drought tolerance.
