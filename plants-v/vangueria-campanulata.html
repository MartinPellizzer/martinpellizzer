Vangueria campanulata

Vangueria campanulata, commonly known as the bellflower vangueria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils. The plant features showy, bell-shaped blue to purple flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Vangueria campanulata is often found in coastal and mountainous regions, where it thrives in well-drained environments.
