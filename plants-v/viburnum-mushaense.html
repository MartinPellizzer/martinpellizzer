Viburnum mushaense

Viburnum mushaense, commonly known as the African snowberry, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters during the winter months. The plant produces bright red, berry-like fruits that are edible for birds and small mammals. Viburnum mushaense typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscape design.
