Verbascum belgradense

Verbascum belgradense, commonly known as the Belgrade mullein, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan region.

It is a perennial herb that grows up to 1 meter tall, featuring soft, fuzzy, grey-green leaves. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Verbascum belgradense is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
