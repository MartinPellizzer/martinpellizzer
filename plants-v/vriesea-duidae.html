Vriesea duidae

Vriesea duidae, commonly known as the Duidae Vriesea, is a species of flowering plant in the Bromeliad family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features a striking rosette of dark green, sword-shaped leaves that are often adorned with white or silver markings. It produces vibrant orange or red inflorescences that bloom in the summer months.

Vriesea duidae is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its adaptability and ornamental value.
