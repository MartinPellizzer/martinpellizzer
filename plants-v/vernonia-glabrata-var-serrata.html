Vernonia glabrata var. serrata

Vernonia glabrata var. serrata, commonly known as the rough Vernonia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to parts of Africa, particularly South Africa, and thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit, narrow leaves with serrated edges, and clusters of small, pale yellow to brownish flowers. It is often found in rocky soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
