Vitex yunnanensis

Vitex yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan vitex, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically clustered in umbels, blooming in the summer months. Its fruit is a dry, two-lobed capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Vitex yunnanensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
