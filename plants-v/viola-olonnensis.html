Viola olonnensis

Viola olonnensis, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, bowl-shaped flowers. The plant typically blooms in shades of purple, violet, and white, with petals that are often marked with intricate patterns. It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular houseplant.

Viola olonnensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor conditions.
