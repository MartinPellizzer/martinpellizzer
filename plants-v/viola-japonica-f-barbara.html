Viola japonica f. barbara

Viola japonica f. barbara, commonly known as the Japanese violet or Barbara's violet, is a herbaceous perennial native to East Asia.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of violet or white. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial to full shade. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.

The species is noted for its adaptability and ease of care, making it a popular choice for gardeners.
