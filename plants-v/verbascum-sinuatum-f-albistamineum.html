Verbascum sinuatum f. albistamineum

Verbascum sinuatum f. albistamineum, commonly known as the white-stamen mullein, is a cultivar of the mullein plant characterized by its pale or white stamens.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae and is native to southern Europe. The plant features large, fuzzy, gray-green leaves and produces showy yellow flowers. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its unique floral display and is used in both gardens and herbal medicine.
