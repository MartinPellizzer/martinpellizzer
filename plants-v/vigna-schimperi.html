Vigna schimperi

Vigna schimperi, commonly known as the Ethiopian bean, is a leguminous plant native to Ethiopia.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and trifoliate leaves with hairy surfaces. The plant produces elongated pods that contain small, round seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to semi-arid climates.

Vigna schimperi is valued for its nutritional content and is an important crop in local agriculture.
