Vangueria linearisepala

Vangueria linearisepala, commonly known as the narrow-leaved vangueria, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features slender, linear leaves that are typically arranged alternately along its stem. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its flowering period generally occurs during the warmer months, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

Vangueria linearisepala is often found in rocky or sandy habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
