Vincetoxicum Nigrum

Vincetoxicum nigrum is a striking member of the Apocynaceae family known for its vibrant, dark purple flowers. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, this plant thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream sides. Its foliage is characterized by broad, heart-shaped leaves that add a lush green contrast to its bold blooms. While it is admired for its ornamental value, it is also considered invasive in some regions due to its rapid spread. Despite its beauty, the plant contains toxic compounds, making it important to handle with care.
