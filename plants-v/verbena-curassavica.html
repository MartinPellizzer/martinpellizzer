Verbena curassavica

Verbena curassavica, commonly known as tropical verbena, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a herbaceous annual that produces clusters of bright blue, purple, or pink flowers. The plant has slender, branching stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its showy blooms attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
