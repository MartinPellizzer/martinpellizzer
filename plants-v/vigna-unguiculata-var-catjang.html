Vigna unguiculata var. catjang

Vigna unguiculata var. catjang, commonly known as the catjang bean, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces elongated, cylindrical pods that contain small, edible seeds used in various cuisines. It is valued for its nutritional content, including high levels of protein and essential minerals.

The catjang bean is also appreciated for its adaptability to different soil types and climate conditions.
