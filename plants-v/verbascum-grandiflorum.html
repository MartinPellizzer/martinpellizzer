Verbascum grandiflorum

Verbascum grandiflorum, commonly known as the large-flowered mullein, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems covered in soft, fuzzy hairs. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are large, broad, and also covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Verbascum grandiflorum is often found in dry, rocky soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
