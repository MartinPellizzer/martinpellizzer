Vicia vaniotii

Vicia vaniotii, commonly known as the Vaniot's vetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to parts of Europe and is characterized by its climbing habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pale blue or purple in color. Vicia vaniotii is often found in grassy areas, along roadsides, and in disturbed soils.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it an important plant in soil enrichment and ecological restoration.
