Veronicastrum robustum

Veronicastrum robustum, commonly known as blue mist spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its tall, spire-like clusters of blue-violet flowers that bloom in mid-summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and slightly hairy on the underside. It prefers full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a versatile choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.
