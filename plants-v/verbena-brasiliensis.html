Verbena brasiliensis

Verbena brasiliensis, commonly known as Brazilian vervain, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces clusters of small, vibrant blue or purple flowers. The plant has slender, branching stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Verbena brasiliensis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
