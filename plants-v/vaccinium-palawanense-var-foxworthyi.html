Vaccinium palawanense var. foxworthyi

Vaccinium palawanense var. foxworthyi, commonly known as the Palawan huckleberry, is a shrub native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its small, elliptic leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces clusters of white flowers that are followed by bright red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and potential ecological importance in its native habitat.
