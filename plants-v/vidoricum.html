Vidoricum

Vidoricum, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Vidoricum is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands.

It is also known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in disturbed soils.
