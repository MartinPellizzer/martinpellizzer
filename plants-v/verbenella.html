Verbenella

Verbenella, commonly known as sweet verbenella, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, small white or pink flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, with narrow, gray-green leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Verbenella is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
