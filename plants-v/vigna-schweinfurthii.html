Vigna schweinfurthii

Vigna schweinfurthii, commonly known as the African wing bean, is a leguminous plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, winged pods that contain edible seeds. The plant thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated for its nutritious seeds, which are rich in protein. It also has a deep root system that helps prevent soil erosion.

Vigna schweinfurthii is valued for its adaptability and is used both as a food source and for agricultural improvement in many regions.
