Vanda liouvillei

Vanda liouvillei, commonly known as the Liouville's vanda, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features pendant, showy flowers that are typically violet or purple in color, with a distinctive three-lobed lip. The plant has long, slender pseudobulbs and is known for its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Vanda liouvillei is often cultivated for its ornamental value and requires well-draining potting media.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
