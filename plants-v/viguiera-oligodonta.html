Viguiera oligodonta

Viguiera oligodonta, commonly known as the Mexican heath.

It is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of the southwestern United States. The plant features showy, yellow-orange inflorescences that bloom in late summer to early fall. Its leaves are narrow and gray-green, often covered in fine hairs.

Viguiera oligodonta is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping.
