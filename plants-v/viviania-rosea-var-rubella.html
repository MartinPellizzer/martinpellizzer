Viviania rosea var. rubella

Viviania rosea var. rubella, commonly known as the red velvet plant, is a striking species native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its velvety, deep red foliage that contrasts beautifully with its glossy, dark green stems. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are often hidden among the foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture to maintain its vibrant coloration.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
