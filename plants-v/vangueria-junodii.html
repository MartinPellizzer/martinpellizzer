Vangueria junodii

Vangueria junodii, commonly known as the Cape gooseberry, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant produces small, orange-red berries that are edible and have a tangy flavor. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Vangueria junodii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
