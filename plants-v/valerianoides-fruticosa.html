Valerianoides fruticosa

Valerianoides fruticosa, commonly known as the shrub valerian, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features woody stems and small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of pink or white. The plant has aromatic leaves and is often used in traditional medicine for its calming properties. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Valerianoides fruticosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to local wildlife.
