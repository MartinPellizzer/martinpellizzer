Vanda × feliciae

Vanda × feliciae, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a fascinating plant that belongs to the Orchidaceae family.

It is a hybrid between Vanda coerulea and Vanda teres, resulting in a unique combination of traits from both parent species. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of other plants without being parasitic, and it thrives in warm, humid environments. Its flowers are typically showy, with a range of colors including blue, purple, and white, and they often have a strong, sweet fragrance.

Vanda × feliciae requires good air circulation and indirect sunlight to flourish, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
