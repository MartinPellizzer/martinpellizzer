Vilfa lenta

Vilfa lenta, commonly known as the slow-growing violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to temperate regions.

It features slender, upright stems covered in soft, velvety leaves that are typically a deep green color. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of purple or blue, which bloom in early spring. Vilfa lenta thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Its foliage is often used in traditional herbal remedies for its purported calming properties.
