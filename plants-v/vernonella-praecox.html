Vernonella praecox

Vernonella praecox, commonly known as early vernon'sweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its erect, herbaceous stem and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense, spherical heads. It is native to North America and thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands.

Vernonella praecox is often found in disturbed soils and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
