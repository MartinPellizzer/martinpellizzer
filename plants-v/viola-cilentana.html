Viola cilentana

Viola cilentana, commonly known as the Cilentan violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Italy.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy violet flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and rocky areas. Its flowers bloom in early spring, adding a splash of color to its natural habitat.

Viola cilentana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
