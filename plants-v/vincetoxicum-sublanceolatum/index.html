Vincetoxicum Sublanceolatum

Vincetoxicum sublanceolatum is a striking perennial plant known for its vibrant, showy flowers. It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is native to parts of Europe and Asia. This plant thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. However, it is important to note that it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
