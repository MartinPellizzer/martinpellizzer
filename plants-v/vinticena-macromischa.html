Vinticena macromischa

Vinticena macromischa, commonly known as the giant sunspire, is a rare and striking plant native to the tropical highlands of South America.

It is characterized by its towering, spiraling stems that can reach heights of over 15 meters. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers in clusters at the top of its stem, which bloom during the dry season. Its leaves are broad, waxy, and arranged in a helical pattern along the stem.

Vinticena macromischa is adapted to nutrient-poor soils and requires specific microclimatic conditions to thrive.
