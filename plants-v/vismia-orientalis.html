Vismia orientalis

Vismia orientalis, commonly known as the African evergreen or African satinwood, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows up to 10 meters in height. The plant features simple, alternate, elliptic to ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. Its small, white or pale yellow flowers are arranged in clusters and are followed by small, round, woody fruits.

Vismia orientalis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in various parts of its native range.
