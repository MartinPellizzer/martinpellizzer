Vernonia parapetersii

Vernonia parapetersii, commonly known as the African ironweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in dense, cylindrical clusters. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Vernonia parapetersii is known for its hardiness and ability to thrive in arid conditions.
