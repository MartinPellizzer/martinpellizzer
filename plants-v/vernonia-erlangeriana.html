Vernonia erlangeriana

Vernonia erlangeriana, commonly known as the erlanger's ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central and South America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy, violet-blue flower heads. The flowers bloom in late summer and early fall, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
