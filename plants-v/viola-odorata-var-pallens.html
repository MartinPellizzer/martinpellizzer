Viola odorata var. pallens

Viola odorata var. pallens, commonly known as the pale viola, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features heart-shaped, pale green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, pale violet flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal medicine.
