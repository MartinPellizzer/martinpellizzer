Vriesea argentinensis

Vriesea argentinensis, commonly known as the Argentine Vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, spathaceous inflorescence that can range in color from yellow to orange and red. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in the understory of tropical forests. Its rosette-shaped leaves are broad and waxy, designed to collect and store water.

Vriesea argentinensis is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
