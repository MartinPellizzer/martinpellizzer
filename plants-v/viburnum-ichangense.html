Viburnum ichangense

Viburnum ichangense, commonly known as the Chinese snowberry, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are dark green and often have a slight pubescence. The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in landscaping.

Viburnum ichangense prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it adaptable to a variety of garden settings.
