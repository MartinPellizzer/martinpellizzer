Vernonia triflosculosa subsp. palmeri

Vernonia triflosculosa subsp.

palmeri, commonly known as Palmer's ironweed, is a perennial shrub native to the southwestern United States. It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, dark blue to purple flowers.

The plant produces small, three-lobed leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Its botanical features make it an important component of native plant communities in its range.
