Vinca sternutetoria

Vinca sternutetoria, commonly known as the weeping vinca, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has dark green, heart-shaped leaves that are opposite on the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Vinca sternutetoria is often used in hanging baskets or as a ground cover due to its attractive appearance and easy care requirements.
