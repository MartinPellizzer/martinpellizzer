Viola yunnanfuensis

Viola yunnanfuensis, commonly known as the Yunnan violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and vibrant purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its flowers bloom in early spring, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Viola yunnanfuensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
