Verdesmum

Verdesmum, commonly known as the greenleaf spurge, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is characterized by its smooth, waxy leaves that are typically green in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often clustered in a structure called a cyathium. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions and thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

Verdesmum is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive foliage and resilience to various environmental conditions.
