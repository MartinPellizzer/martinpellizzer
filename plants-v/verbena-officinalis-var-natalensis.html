Verbena officinalis var. natalensis

Verbena officinalis var. natalensis, commonly known as Natal verbena, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features square, hairy stems and opposite leaves that are lance-shaped with serrated edges. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular, violet-blue flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Natal verbena is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical use in traditional medicine.
