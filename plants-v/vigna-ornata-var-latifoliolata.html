Vigna ornata var. latifoliolata

Vigna ornata var. latifoliolata, commonly known as the broad-leafed cowpea, is a tropical legume native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant produces elongated pods that contain edible, nutrient-rich seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its protein-rich beans.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal as well as its agricultural significance.
