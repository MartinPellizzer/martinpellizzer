Viviania esculenta

Viviania esculenta, commonly known as the arrowhead vine, is a tropical plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and climbing habit, often used in ornamental gardening. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its roots and stems are edible, and it has been used in traditional cuisines.

Viviania esculenta thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
