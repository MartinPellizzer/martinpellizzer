Vitex perrieri

Vitex perrieri, commonly known as the Perrier's chaste tree, is a species of flowering plant in the Lamiaceae family.

It is native to Madagascar and is characterized by its aromatic, opposite leaves and showy, clustered purple flowers. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry forest habitats.

Vitex perrieri is noted for its ornamental value and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
