Vangueria demeusei

Vangueria demeusei, commonly known as the Cape reed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas and grasslands. The plant features tall, erect stems covered in fine, silky hairs, and its leaves are linear with a bluish-green hue. It produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in dense spikes, which bloom during the cooler months.

Vangueria demeusei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
