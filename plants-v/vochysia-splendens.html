Vochysia splendens

Vochysia splendens, commonly known as the golden glory tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking golden-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, making it a visually appealing ornamental plant. The tree has smooth, greyish bark and lance-shaped leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Vochysia splendens is also valued for its ability to provide shade and its role in supporting local biodiversity.
