Vilfa elongata

Vilfa elongata, commonly known as the slender vilfa, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its long, slender stems and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, contributing to its overall delicate appearance.

Vilfa elongata is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
