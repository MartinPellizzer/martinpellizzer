Viguiera acuminata

Viguiera acuminata, commonly known as the Mexican ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Viguiera acuminata is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance in landscaping.
