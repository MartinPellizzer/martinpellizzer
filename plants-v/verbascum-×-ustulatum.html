Verbascum × ustulatum

Verbascum × ustulatum, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the genus Verbascum, which includes several species of flowering plants known for their fuzzy, woolly leaves. The hybrid is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

The plant's distinctive appearance and ease of cultivation have made it a favored ornamental in horticulture.
