Viola × desetangsii

Viola × desetangsii, commonly known as the hybrid violet, is a cross between Viola tricolor and Viola wittrockii.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, five-petaled flowers. The flowers typically range in color from purple to white, depending on the cultivar. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas or gardens.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate in temperate climates.
