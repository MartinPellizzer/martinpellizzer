Vatica fleuryana

Vatica fleuryana, commonly known as the Fleurys' vatica, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Dipterocarpaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and sturdy trunk. The plant typically grows in lowland rainforests, thriving in humid and warm climates. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and usually elliptical in shape.

Vatica fleuryana is valued for its hard wood and is often found in secondary forest regrowth areas.
