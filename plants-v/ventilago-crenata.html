Ventilago crenata

Ventilago crenata, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators make it a popular choice in horticulture.
