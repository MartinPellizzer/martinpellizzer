Vernonia alleizettei var. rienanensis

Vernonia alleizettei var. rienanensis, commonly known as the Rienan vernonia, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and has adapted to sandy soils and saline conditions. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, while its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in clusters.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitat.
