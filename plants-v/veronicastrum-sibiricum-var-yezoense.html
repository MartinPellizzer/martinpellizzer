Veronicastrum sibiricum var. yezoense

Veronicastrum sibiricum var. yezoense, commonly known as Yezo angel's trumpet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It features tall, erect stems that can grow up to 2 meters in height, adorned with clusters of pale blue to lilac flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged oppositely along the stem, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Botanically, it belongs to the Boraginaceae family and is valued for its showy inflorescences and adaptability to various garden conditions.
