Vossia speciosa

Vossia speciosa, commonly known as the showy vossia, is a striking flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a member of the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and purple. Vossia speciosa is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
