Viola uniflora f. glabricapsula

Viola uniflora f. glabricapsula, commonly known as the smooth-cup viola, is a flowering plant belonging to the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of violet or purple. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a smooth, glabrous surface, distinguishing it from other viola species. It thrives in cool, moist environments, often found in woodland areas or shaded gardens.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate in temperate climates.
