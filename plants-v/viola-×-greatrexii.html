Viola × greatrexii

Viola × greatrexii, commonly known as the Greatrex violet, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Viola tricolor and Viola lutea.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple and yellow flowers, which bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows to a height of about 15-20 cm, with heart-shaped, velvety leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

The Greatrex violet is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
