Vitis thunbergii var. yunnanensis

Vitis thunbergii var. yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan fox grape, is a climbing vine native to China.

It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant's leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture. Its fruits are small, round berries that turn from green to red as they mature.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and unique fruit.
