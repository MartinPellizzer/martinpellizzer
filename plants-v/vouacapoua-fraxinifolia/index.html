Vouacapoua Fraxinifolia

Vouacapoua fraxinifolia is a remarkable tropical tree native to the Amazon rainforest. Known for its striking yellow flowers and unique heart-shaped leaves, it captivates botanists and nature lovers alike. This species thrives in humid, shaded environments and plays a vital role in its ecosystem. Its wood is valued for its durability and is used in traditional crafts and construction. Despite its beauty and utility, it remains relatively unknown outside of botanical circles.
