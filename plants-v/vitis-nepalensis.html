Vitis nepalensis

Vitis nepalensis, commonly known as the Nepal grape or Himalayan grape, is a climbing vine native to the Himalayan region.

It produces small, round berries that are typically dark blue or black in color. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges and can grow up to several meters in length. It thrives in temperate to subtropical climates and is often found in forested areas.

Its berries are not commonly consumed by humans but are occasionally used in traditional medicine.
