Vicia ternata

Vicia ternata, commonly known as slender vetch, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a legume characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

Vicia ternata is often found in disturbed areas, along roadsides, and in meadows.
