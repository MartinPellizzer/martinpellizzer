Vitex cienkowskii

Vitex cienkowskii, commonly known as the Chinese vitex, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in forest margins or rocky areas.

Vitex cienkowskii is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
