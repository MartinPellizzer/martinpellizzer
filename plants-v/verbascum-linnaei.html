Verbascum linnaei

Verbascum linnaei, commonly known as the Linnaean mullein, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae and is characterized by its tall, hairy stems and large, showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are broad and velvety, often used in traditional herbal remedies.
