Vandopsis celebica

Vandopsis celebica, commonly known as the Celebes vandopsis, is a rare and enigmatic orchid species native to the tropical regions of Indonesia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique floral structure and specialized growth habits. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to tree bark in humid, shaded environments.
