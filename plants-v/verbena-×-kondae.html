Verbena × kondae

Verbena × kondae, commonly known as the hybrid verbena, is a flowering plant belonging to the Verbenaceae family.

It is a cross between Verbena rigida and Verbena bonariensis, resulting in a plant with unique botanical characteristics. The species is known for its compact growth habit and profuse displays of blue to purple flowers. It typically features opposite, narrow leaves and produces small, tubular flowers that attract pollinators.

Verbena × kondae is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
