Verbascum helianthemoides

Verbascum helianthemoides, commonly known as the rock mullein, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its fuzzy, silver-gray leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in dry, sunny environments. Its fluffy leaves are sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.

Verbascum helianthemoides is also valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
