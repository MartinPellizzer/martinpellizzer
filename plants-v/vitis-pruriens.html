Vitis pruriens

Vitis pruriens, commonly known as the "itchy vine," is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It is a climbing vine that produces small, greenish-yellow flowers and small, round, yellow fruits. The plant is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which can cause skin irritation upon contact. It is often found in moist, shaded areas such as forests and along stream banks.

Vitis pruriens is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
