Verbena digitata var. integerrima

Verbena digitata var. integerrima, commonly known as the blue verbena, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant blue to purple flower clusters that bloom in summer. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and drought tolerance.
