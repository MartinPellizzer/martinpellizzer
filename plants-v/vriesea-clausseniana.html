Vriesea clausseniana

Vriesea clausseniana, commonly known as the Clausen's Vriesea, is a tropical bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, sword-shaped leaves that form a rosette, creating a central water-holding cup. The plant produces vibrant, long-lasting inflorescences with colorful bracts that range from red to orange. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

Vriesea clausseniana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
