Viola × rossii

Viola × rossii, commonly known as the hybrid violet, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Violaceae family.

It is a hybrid between Viola tricolor and Viola praetextata, resulting in a plant with distinctive purple and white flowers. The leaves are heart-shaped and covered in a fine layer of down, giving them a soft texture. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas or gardens.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
