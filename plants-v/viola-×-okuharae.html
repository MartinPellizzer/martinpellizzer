Viola × okuharae

Viola × okuharae, commonly known as the Japanese violet, is a hybrid species native to Japan.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with heart-shaped leaves and showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically blooms in spring and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant blossoms. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Viola × okuharae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
