Valeriana kaschmiriensis

Valeriana kaschmiriensis, commonly known as the Kashmir valerian, is a perennial herb native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It features a robust root system and cylindrical, aromatic stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a strong, distinctive fragrance.

Valeriana kaschmiriensis is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for its calming effects.
