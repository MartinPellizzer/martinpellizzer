Vriesea bahiana

Vriesea bahiana, commonly known as the Bahian Vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, spathaceous inflorescence that displays a range of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant features a rosette of broad, sword-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense, white powdery coating. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is well-suited for indoor cultivation.

The flowers typically bloom in summer and can last for several months, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
