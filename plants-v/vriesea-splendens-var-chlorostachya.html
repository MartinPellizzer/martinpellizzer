Vriesea splendens var. chlorostachya

Vriesea splendens var. chlorostachya, commonly known as the chlorostachya vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant green leaves that are often speckled with silver or white markings. The plant produces striking, long-lasting inflorescences that can range in color from yellow to orange. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
