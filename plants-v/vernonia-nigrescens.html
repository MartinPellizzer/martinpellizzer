Vernonia nigrescens

Vernonia nigrescens, commonly known as black vernonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a rough texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodland or grassland habitats.

The species is also valued for its ornamental qualities and has been introduced to other regions for horticultural purposes.
