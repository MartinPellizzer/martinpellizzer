Vigna vexillata var. ovata

Vigna vexillata var. ovata, commonly known as the African bean or slender cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing habit and small, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that give way to elongated, cylindrical pods containing edible seeds. It thrives in warm climates and is drought-tolerant, making it a valuable crop in arid regions.

Its botanical features make it well-suited for both traditional agriculture and ecological restoration projects.
