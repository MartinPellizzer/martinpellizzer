Vrydagzynea rivularis

Vrydagzynea rivularis, commonly known as the river cycad, is a unique cycad species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, unbranched stem and a crown of large, feather-like leaves. The plant produces cones, with male and female cones typically occurring on separate plants. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils along riverbanks and is adapted to seasonal rainfall patterns.

Due to its restricted habitat and vulnerability to environmental changes, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
