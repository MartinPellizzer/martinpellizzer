Vincentia bidwellii

Vincentia bidwellii, commonly known as Bidwell's morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in the early morning. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or arid environments.
