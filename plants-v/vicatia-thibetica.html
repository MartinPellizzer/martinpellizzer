Vicatia thibetica

Vicatia thibetica, commonly known as the Tibetan bellflower, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Tibet.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils at high altitudes. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often hairy, while its stems are erect and branching.
