Vieusseuxia plumaria

Vieusseuxia plumaria, commonly known as the plumaria or velvetleaf, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features broad, ovate leaves with a velvety texture and a distinct, aromatic scent. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by round, fleshy fruits. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is cultivated in some regions for both horticultural and traditional uses.
