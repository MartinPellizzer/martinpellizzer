Vriesea taritubensis

Vriesea taritubensis, commonly known as the Taritubens Vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting inflorescence that displays a range of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that collect water, creating a small pool that supports various microorganisms. Its growth habit is compact, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Vriesea taritubensis thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
