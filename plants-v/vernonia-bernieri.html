Vernonia bernieri

Vernonia bernieri, commonly known as the yellow ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wetlands and along stream banks. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer to early fall. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy texture.

Vernonia bernieri is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance, providing nectar for pollinators.
