Vitex negundo f. intermedia

Vitex negundo f. intermedia, commonly known as the Chinese vitex or fragrant vitex, is a variant of the vitex plant characterized by its unique floral structure.

It produces small, fragrant, clustered flowers that are typically lavender to purple in color. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly serrated margin. This plant is native to China and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a versatile addition to gardens.
