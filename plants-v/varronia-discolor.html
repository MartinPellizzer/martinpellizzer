Varronia discolor

Varronia discolor, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that can grow up to 15 meters in height, characterized by its smooth, grayish bark. The leaves are simple, alternate, and elliptical in shape, with a glossy upper surface and a lighter underside. It produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by capsule fruits containing several seeds.

The plant is valued for its durable wood and is often found in moist, shaded habitats within tropical forests.
