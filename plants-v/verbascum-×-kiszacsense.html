Verbascum × kiszacsense

Verbascum × kiszacsense, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial plant belonging to the Scrophulariaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, fuzzy, gray-green leaves and showy yellow or orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is native to regions of Europe and is often found in dry, rocky habitats. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a hardy addition to gardens.

Verbascum × kiszacsense is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
