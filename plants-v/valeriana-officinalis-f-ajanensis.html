Valeriana officinalis f. ajanensis

Valeriana officinalis f. ajanensis, commonly known as the Ajanensis valerian, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caprifoliaceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its aromatic, often purple or pink flowers and a robust root system. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a serrated edge, while its stems are square and hairy.

This variety is valued for its medicinal properties, similar to the parent species Valeriana officinalis, and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
