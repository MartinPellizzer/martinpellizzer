Vernonia calvoana subsp. oehleri

Vernonia calvoana subsp.

oehleri, commonly known as the Oehleri ironweed, is a flowering plant native to Mexico. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

Its botanical features make it an attractive choice for xeriscaping and native plant gardens.
