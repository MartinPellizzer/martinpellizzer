Verbascum × sepulcrale

Verbascum × sepulcrale, commonly known as the common mullein.

It is a hybrid plant in the genus Verbascum, characterized by its tall, hairy stems and large, fuzzy leaves. The plant produces showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it thrives in dry, disturbed soils.

Verbascum × sepulcrale is often found in fields, along roadsides, and in other open habitats.
