Vigna elegans

Vigna elegans, commonly known as the elegant vigna, is a species of legume native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing vines and trifoliate leaves, which are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers followed by elongated, bean-like pods containing edible seeds. Vigna elegans is valued for its nutritional content, including protein and essential minerals.

Its growth habit and adaptability make it a useful plant in both agricultural and ecological contexts.
