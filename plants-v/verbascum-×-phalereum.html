Verbascum × phalereum

Verbascum × phalereum, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a member of the Malvaceae family.

It is a tall, robust plant that can grow up to 2 meters in height, featuring large, fuzzy, gray-green leaves. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. It is native to southern Europe and is often found in dry, rocky soils.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and traditional herbal uses.
