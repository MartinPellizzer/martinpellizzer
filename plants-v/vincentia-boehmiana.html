Vincentia boehmiana

Vincentia boehmiana, commonly known as the Boehm's climbing plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Fabaceae.

It is a vigorous vine that produces striking yellow flowers with a distinctive purple throat. Native to tropical regions of South America, it thrives in humid, warm climates. The plant has compound leaves with three to five oval-shaped leaflets.
