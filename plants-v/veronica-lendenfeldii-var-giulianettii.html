Veronica lendenfeldii var. giulianettii

Veronica lendenfeldii var. giulianettii, commonly known as the Julianettii speedwell, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly used in rock gardens and as a border plant.

This variety is relatively low maintenance and thrives in full sun to partial shade.
