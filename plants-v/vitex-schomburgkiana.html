Vitex schomburgkiana

Vitex schomburgkiana, commonly known as the Schomburgk's chaste tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to purple, blooming in the summer months. Its fruit is a dry, two-valved capsule that contains small, dark seeds.

Vitex schomburgkiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
