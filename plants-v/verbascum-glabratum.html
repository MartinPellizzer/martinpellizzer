Verbascum glabratum

Verbascum glabratum, commonly known as the smooth mullein, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its tall, hairy stems and large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces showy yellow flowers in late summer, which are clustered in a terminal spike. It thrives in dry, sunny environments and is often found in grasslands and disturbed soils.

Verbascum glabratum is known for its medicinal uses, particularly in traditional herbal remedies.
