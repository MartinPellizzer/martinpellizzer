Vernonanthura tuerckheimii

Vernonanthura tuerckheimii, commonly known as the Tuerckheim's vernonberry, is a shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries, and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and small, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full shade.
