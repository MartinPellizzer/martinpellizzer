Verbascum × nudatiforme

Verbascum × nudatiforme, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial plant native to southern Europe.

The plant features large, velvety leaves that are often covered in soft, hairy textures, and it produces showy yellow or orange flowers in the summer.
