Vitex zanzibarensis

Vitex zanzibarensis, commonly known as Zanzibar tree or African bead tree, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces fragrant, blue or purple flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a rough texture, while its bark is smooth and grayish in color.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
