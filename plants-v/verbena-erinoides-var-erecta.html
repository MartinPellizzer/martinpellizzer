Verbena erinoides var. erecta

Verbena erinoides var. erecta, commonly known as the upright verbena, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is a member of the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit. The plant produces clusters of vibrant blue or purple flowers that bloom throughout the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and gray-green, often covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
