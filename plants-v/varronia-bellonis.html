Varronia bellonis

Varronia bellonis, commonly known as the red sandalwood, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, reddish-brown wood that is highly valued for its durability and fine grain. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow.

The tree is often found in lowland forests and is known for its slow growth and long lifespan.
