Vitex milnei

Vitex milnei, commonly known as the Christmas vine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a climbing shrub that produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically blooming in the summer months. The plant has opposite, simple leaves that are usually ovate or elliptic in shape. Its fruits are dry, capsule-like structures that split open to release small, winged seeds.

Vitex milnei is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
