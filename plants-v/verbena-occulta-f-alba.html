Verbena occulta f. alba

Verbena occulta f. alba, commonly known as white verbena, is a flowering plant belonging to the Verbenaceae family.

It is a compact, herbaceous perennial that produces clusters of small, showy white or pale pink flowers. The plant is native to southern Europe and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It prefers full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its delicate appearance and extended blooming period make it a popular choice among gardeners.
