Viticipremna queenslandica

Viticipremna queenslandica, commonly known as the Queensland glorybower, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its showy, yellow-orange flowers are clustered in terminal inflorescences, adding ornamental value.

The species is valued for its ability to thrive in well-drained soils and its adaptability to various climatic conditions.
