Vandellia multiflora

Vandellia multiflora, commonly known as the coastal vandellia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are linear and often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

It is adapted to withstand salt spray and strong coastal winds.
