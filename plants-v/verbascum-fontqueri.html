Verbascum fontqueri

Verbascum fontqueri, commonly known as the Sierra Nevada mullein.

It is a flowering plant native to the western United States, particularly found in the Sierra Nevada mountain range. The plant features tall, hairy stems and large, velvety leaves that are characteristic of the mullein genus. It produces showy yellow flowers in the summer, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Verbascum fontqueri is adapted to rocky and dry environments, making it a resilient member of its ecosystem.
