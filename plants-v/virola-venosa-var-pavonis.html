Virola venosa var. pavonis

Virola venosa var. pavonis, commonly known as the pavonina or velvetwood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Myristicaceae and is characterized by its smooth, dark-brown bark and large, elliptical leaves with a glossy, waxy surface. The tree produces small, fragrant flowers followed by large, round fruits containing hard, woody seeds.
