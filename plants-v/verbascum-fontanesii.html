Verbascum fontanesii

Verbascum fontanesii, commonly known as the Armenian mullein.

It is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus and Central Asia. The plant features large, velvety grey-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its showy yellow flowers appear in late summer, forming clusters at the top of the stem.

Verbascum fontanesii is adapted to rocky and arid environments, making it a hardy species with ecological significance in its native habitat.
