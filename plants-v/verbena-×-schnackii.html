Verbena × schnackii

Verbena × schnackii, commonly known as the hybrid verbena, is a species that results from the cross between Verbena hastata and Verbena rigida.

It is a perennial flowering plant native to the central and eastern United States. The plant produces showy spikes of blue to purple flowers that bloom throughout the summer. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its foliage is typically narrow and grass-like, with the flowers appearing at the tips of the stems.
