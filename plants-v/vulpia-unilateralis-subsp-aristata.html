Vulpia unilateralis subsp. aristata

Vulpia unilateralis subsp.

aristata, commonly known as the slender veld grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa. It typically grows in open grasslands and disturbed soils, exhibiting a compact growth habit with tufted clumps.

The plant has narrow, flat leaves and produces spikelets that are often arranged in a single row along the stem. It is characterized by its distinctive, one-sided inflorescence, which gives it its specific epithet "unilateralis.".

This subspecies is adapted to dry conditions and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions.
