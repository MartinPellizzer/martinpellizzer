Vaccinium vonroemeri

Vaccinium vonroemeri, commonly known as the Von Roemeri blueberry, is a species of evergreen shrub native to the temperate regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes many acid-loving plants such as heathers and other blueberries. The plant prefers well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in forested areas or along stream banks.
