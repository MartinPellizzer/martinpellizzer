Viola mandshurica var. glabra

Viola mandshurica var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth violet, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically pubescent but are smooth in this variety. The plant produces violet flowers with five petals, often with a distinctive white or pale violet coloration. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in woodland or shaded areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable species for both ornamental and ecological purposes.
