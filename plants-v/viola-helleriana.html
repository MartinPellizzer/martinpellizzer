Viola helleriana

Viola helleriana, commonly known as the Heller's violet, is a species of flowering plant in the family Violaceae.

It is native to central and eastern Europe, where it typically grows in woodland and meadow habitats. The plant features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and produces showy violet flowers with yellow markings. Viola helleriana is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.

It prefers moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.
