Vriesea × albertii

Vriesea × albertii, commonly known as the Albert's Vriesea, is a hybrid bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful inflorescence that can range from red to orange and yellow. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that collect water, forming a central cup. Its flowers typically bloom in late spring or early summer, adding a dramatic display to the plant.

Vriesea × albertii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
