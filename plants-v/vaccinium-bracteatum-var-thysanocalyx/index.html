Vaccinium Bracteatum Var. Thysanocalyx

Vaccinium bracteatum var. thysanocalyx is a unique evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. Known for its striking red flowers and glossy dark green leaves, it is a popular ornamental plant in gardens and landscapes. This variety of the common blueberry plant thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish. Its attractive appearance makes it a favorite among horticulturists and plant lovers alike. Despite its beauty, it is relatively rare in cultivation, making it a sought-after addition to botanical collections.
