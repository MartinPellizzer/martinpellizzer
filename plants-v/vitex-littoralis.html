Vitex littoralis

Vitex littoralis, commonly known as coastal vitex, is a shrub native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant, blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, gray-green leaves and produces small, woody fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for coastal and xeriscaped gardens.

Vitex littoralis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize sandy soils.
