Vanilla palembanica

Vanilla palembanica, commonly known as the Palembang vanilla, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, pendant-like flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. The plant produces a fragrant vanilla pod, which is used in culinary and aromatic applications. It thrives in humid, tropical climates with well-drained soil.

Vanilla palembanica is valued for both its ornamental beauty and its economic importance in the vanilla industry.
