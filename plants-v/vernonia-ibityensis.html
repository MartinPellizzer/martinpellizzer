Vernonia ibityensis

Vernonia ibityensis, commonly known as the Madagascar ironweed, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grasslands and forest margins, thriving in well-drained soils. It produces small, yellow to brown flowers that are often clustered in dense inflorescences.
