Villebrunea × intermedia

Villebrunea × intermedia, commonly known as the hybrid banyan, is a tropical evergreen tree belonging to the Moraceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between Villebrunea macrophylla and Ficus elastica, characterized by its large, lobed leaves and distinctive aerial roots. The plant is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in humid, warm climates with well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can grow up to 20 meters in height.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical regions.
