Viola suzukii

Viola suzukii, commonly known as the Japanese violet, is a flowering plant native to Japan and Korea.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, five-petaled flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has heart-shaped, velvety leaves. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds.

Viola suzukii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has been cultivated for horticultural purposes.
