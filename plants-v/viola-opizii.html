Viola opizii

Viola opizii, commonly known as the alpine violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It typically grows in rocky soils and alpine meadows, thriving in cool, temperate climates. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of purple and violet. Viola opizii is known for its hardy nature and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
