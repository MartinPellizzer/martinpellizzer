Vigna vexillata var. dolichonema

Vigna vexillata var. dolichonema, commonly known as the slender cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and trifoliate leaves with small, ovate leaflets. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that give way to elongated, cylindrical pods containing several seeds. It is valued for its nutritional content, providing protein-rich seeds used in various cuisines.

Additionally, it plays a role in soil enrichment due to its nitrogen-fixing ability.
