Vachellia gentlei

Vachellia gentlei, commonly known as the green thorn acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, thorned stems and bipinnate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which are followed by long, slender pods containing seeds. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showing adaptability to poor soil conditions.

Vachellia gentlei plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various animals.
