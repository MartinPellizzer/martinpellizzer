Ventilago madraspatana

Ventilago madraspatana, commonly known as the Madras tamarind.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the Indian subcontinent. The plant is characterized by its simple, opposite, and elliptical leaves. Its flowers are small, white or pale yellow, and it produces elongated, woody pods containing several seeds.

The plant is often found in dry, rocky areas and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
