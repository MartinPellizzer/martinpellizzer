Vernonia piloselloides

Vernonia piloselloides, commonly known as silverleaf ironweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant typically grows as an erect annual or biennial herb, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are grayish-green and covered in fine, silvery hairs, which give it its common name.

The showy yellow to orange flowers appear in clusters and bloom from late summer to early autumn.
