Veronica muelleri

Veronica muelleri, commonly known as Mueller's speedwell, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit. The plant produces small, blue to purple flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are opposite, narrow, and covered in a fine layer of white, silky hairs.

Veronica muelleri is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and delicate blossoms.
