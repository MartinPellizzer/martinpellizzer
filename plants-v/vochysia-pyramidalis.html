Vochysia pyramidalis

Vochysia pyramidalis, commonly known as the pyramid tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, pyramidal shape and glossy, dark green leaves. The tree produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by edible fruits resembling small, round berries. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Vochysia pyramidalis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
