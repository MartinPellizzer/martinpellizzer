Verbena platensis f. iverana

Verbena platensis f. iverana, commonly known as the Iverana verbena, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a compact shrub that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant produces clusters of small, bright blue or violet flowers that bloom throughout the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
