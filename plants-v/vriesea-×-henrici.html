Vriesea × henrici

Vriesea × henrici, commonly known as the hybrid Vriesea, is a hybrid orchid species resulting from the cross between Vriesea splendens and Vriesea carinata.

It is characterized by its striking, long-lasting inflorescences that feature vibrant, sword-shaped leaves and showy, brightly colored flowers. The plant is native to South America and thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

Vriesea × henrici typically produces flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, making it a visually appealing addition to any collection.
