Viburnum colebrookeanum

Viburnum colebrookeanum, commonly known as the Chinese beautyberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, bright purple berries that appear in the fall. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, often found in woodland habitats.

The berries are not only ornamental but also serve as a food source for birds and other wildlife.
