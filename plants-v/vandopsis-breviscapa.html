Vandopsis breviscapa

Vandopsis breviscapa, commonly known as the short-stalked vandopsis, is a rare and enigmatic fern-like plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Vandopsidaceae and is characterized by its delicate, small fronds that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. The plant exhibits a unique reproductive strategy, producing spores in specialized structures called sporangia located on the undersides of its leaves.
