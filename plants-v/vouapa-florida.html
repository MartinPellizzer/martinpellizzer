Vouapa florida

Vouapa florida, commonly known as the Hawaiian flame tree, is a striking tropical plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual display. The plant has compound leaves composed of multiple leaflets, and it produces woody pods that contain flat, fibrous seeds. Vouapa florida thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal areas and lowland forests.

Its rapid growth and ornamental value make it a popular species for landscaping in tropical regions.
