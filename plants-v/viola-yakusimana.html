Viola yakusimana

Viola yakusimana, commonly known as the Yakusimana violet, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant purple flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and mountainous regions. Its flowers bloom in early spring, adding a splash of color to its natural habitat.

Viola yakusimana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
