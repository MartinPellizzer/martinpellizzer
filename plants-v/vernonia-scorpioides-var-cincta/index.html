Vernonia Scorpioides Var. Cincta

Vernonia scorpioides var. cincta is a striking member of the Asteraceae family known for its vibrant yellow to orange flower heads. Native to the southern United States, this plant thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in prairies and open woodlands. Its unique appearance, featuring narrow leaves and a compact growth habit, makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking drought-tolerant plants. The variety cincta stands out with its distinctive greenish-white leaf margins and dense flower clusters. This plant not only adds visual interest to landscapes but also supports local pollinators and wildlife.
