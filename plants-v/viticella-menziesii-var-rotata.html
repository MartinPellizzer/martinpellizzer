Viticella menziesii var. rotata

Viticella menziesii var. rotata, commonly known as the twisted grapevine, is a unique cultivar of the grapevine species.

It is characterized by its distinctive, twisted growth habit and small, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink to purple in color. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value rather than for its fruit.

This variety is well-suited to temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
