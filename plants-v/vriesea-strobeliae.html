Vriesea strobeliae

Vriesea strobeliae, commonly known as the Strobel's Vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting inflorescence that typically displays a mix of red, orange, and yellow hues. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a central cup to collect water. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Vriesea strobeliae is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
