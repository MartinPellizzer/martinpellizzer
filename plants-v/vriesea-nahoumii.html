Vriesea nahoumii

Vriesea nahoumii, commonly known as the Nahoum's Vriesea, is a species of bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful inflorescence that can range from red to orange, often with a central yellow or white stripe. The plant features a rosette of broad, waxy leaves that are typically green with a slight blue or silver hue. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Vriesea nahoumii is popular as a decorative plant due to its long-lasting blooms and unique appearance.
