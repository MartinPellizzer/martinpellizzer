Vitex montevidensis var. parviflora

Vitex montevidensis var. parviflora, commonly known as the Parviflora Chaste Tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to South America.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its slender, often multi-branched stems. The plant produces small, fragrant, blue or purple flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly serrated edge.
