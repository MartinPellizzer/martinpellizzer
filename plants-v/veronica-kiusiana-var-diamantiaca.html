Veronica kiusiana var. diamantiaca

Veronica kiusiana var. diamantiaca, commonly known as the Diamantine Speedwell, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit. The plant produces clusters of blue or violet flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine, soft hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
