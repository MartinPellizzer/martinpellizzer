Viguiera hilairei

Viguiera hilairei, commonly known as the Mexican heath.

It is a perennial shrub native to Mexico, characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage. The plant features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically narrow and gray-green, with a fuzzy texture.

Viguiera hilairei is often grown in rock gardens or as a ground cover due to its hardiness and ornamental appeal.
