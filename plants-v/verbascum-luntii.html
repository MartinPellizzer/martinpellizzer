Verbascum luntii

Verbascum luntii, commonly known as the Lunt's mullein, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the genus Verbascum, which includes various species of flowering plants commonly referred to as mulleins. The plant is characterized by its tall, hairy stems and large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in summer. Verbascum luntii typically grows in rocky or arid environments, adapting to harsh climatic conditions.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
