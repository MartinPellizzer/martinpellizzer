Veronica spicata f. falz-feiniana

Veronica spicata f. falz-feiniana, commonly known as the fine-leaved spirea, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Europe.

It features narrow, dark green leaves that are densely packed, giving it a neat, tidy appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, blue-violet flowers in early summer, adding a delicate ornamental touch. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its compact growth habit and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for small garden spaces.
