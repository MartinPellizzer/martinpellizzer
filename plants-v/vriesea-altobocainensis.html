Vriesea altobocainensis

Vriesea altobocainensis, commonly known as the Altobocainensis Vriesea, is a species of bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful inflorescence that can range from red to orange, often with a contrasting green or white center. The plant features a rosette of grass-like leaves that collect water, which is essential for its survival in its natural habitat. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
