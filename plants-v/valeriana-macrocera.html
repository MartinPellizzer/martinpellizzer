Valeriana macrocera

Valeriana macrocera, commonly known as the large-headed valerian, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands. The plant features slender, erect stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves. Its showy, pink to purple flowers are clustered in dense, cylindrical inflorescences.

Valeriana macrocera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
