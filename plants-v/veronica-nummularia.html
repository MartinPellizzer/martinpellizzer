Veronica nummularia

Veronica nummularia, commonly known as silverweed, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It features dense clusters of small, blue or violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has basal leaves that are round and silver-white in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and ground cover.
