Vernonia celebica

Vernonia celebica, commonly known as the Celebes Vernonia, is a flowering plant native to the Indonesian archipelago.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and robust growth habit. The plant typically features lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow to orange flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in open woodlands and disturbed soils.

Due to its ornamental value and potential ecological benefits, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
