Vernonia chiliocephala

Vernonia chiliocephala, commonly known as the red Vernonia, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, bright red flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Vernonia chiliocephala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
