Vincetoxicum asperum

Vincetoxicum asperum, commonly known as rough milkvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant produces legume pods that contain several seeds, typical of the Fabaceae family. It is native to regions in Europe and Asia and is often found in dry, disturbed habitats.

Vincetoxicum asperum is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
