Voyria nivea

Voyria nivea, commonly known as the white star orchid, is a delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant typically grows in cool, shaded environments and prefers moist, well-drained soil. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming a rosette at the base of the plant.

Voyria nivea is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique floral display.
