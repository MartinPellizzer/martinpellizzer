Vernonia scabra var. acuminata

Vernonia scabra var. acuminata, commonly known as the rough Vernonia, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, hairy leaves. The plant produces small, yellow to orange flowers that are clustered in dense, spherical heads. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

This variant is distinguished by its finely serrated leaves and elongated flower heads.
