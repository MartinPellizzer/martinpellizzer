Volkensia glomerata

Volkensia glomerata, commonly known as the clustered orchid, is a tropical plant native to regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its dense, clustered growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and greenhouse cultivation.
