Vitex glabrata f. pallida

Vitex glabrata f. pallida, commonly known as the pale vitex or white-leaf vitex, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree characterized by its pale green to white leaves and fragrant, clustered flowers that range from white to pale purple. The plant belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is known for its adaptability to various soil types and climates. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and showy blooms.
