Verbascum × klotzschianum

Verbascum × klotzschianum, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the genus Verbascum, which includes several species of flowering plants known for their large, showy leaves and flowers. The hybrid is characterized by its tall, hairy stems and yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

The plant is also valued for its medicinal properties, with historical uses in traditional herbal remedies.
