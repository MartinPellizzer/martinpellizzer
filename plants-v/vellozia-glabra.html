Vellozia glabra

Vellozia glabra, commonly known as the smooth vellozia, is a unique member of the Velloziaceae family, native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its dense, fleshy foliage and distinctive, spiral-arranged leaves. The plant features small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing adaptations to conserve water.

Vellozia glabra is noted for its slow growth and ability to survive in harsh, dry conditions.
