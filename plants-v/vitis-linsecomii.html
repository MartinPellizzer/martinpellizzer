Vitis linsecomii

Vitis linsecomii, commonly known as the Chinese fox grape, is a species of flowering vine native to China.

It belongs to the Vitaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and tendrils. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by clusters of small, round berries. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped with a rough texture.

Vitis linsecomii is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.
