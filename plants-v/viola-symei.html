Viola symei

Viola symei, commonly known as the Chinese violet, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and delicate, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories. Its flowers bloom in spring, producing a sweet fragrance that attracts pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Viola symei is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its traditional uses in Chinese medicine.
