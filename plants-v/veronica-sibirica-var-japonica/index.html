Veronica Sibirica Var. Japonica

Veronica sibirica var. japonica is a striking flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of East Asia. It is known for its clusters of tiny, blue-violet flowers that bloom in early summer. This variety is a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine displays due to its hardiness and ornamental value. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, adding a delicate texture to its appearance. Despite its modest size, it holds a special place in botanical gardens and among plant enthusiasts worldwide.
