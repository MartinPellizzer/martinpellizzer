Viola schultzii

Viola schultzii, commonly known as the Schultze violet, is a species of flowering plant in the Violaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of eastern Africa, particularly Kenya and Tanzania. The plant produces showy, violet-colored flowers with five petals, typically in shades of purple or blue. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs.

Viola schultzii thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found growing in woodland or rocky habitats.
