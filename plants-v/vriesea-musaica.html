Vriesea musaica

Vriesea musaica, commonly known as the banana leaf vriesea, is a striking epiphytic bromeliad native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, broad, waxy leaves that resemble banana leaves, which are typically bright green with a smooth texture. The plant produces striking inflorescences with vibrant orange or red flowers that bloom in the center of the leaf rosette. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent humidity and well-draining soil.

Vriesea musaica is popular as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
