Vaccinium shastense subsp. shastense

Vaccinium shastense subsp.

shastense, commonly known as the Shasta huckleberry, is a shrub native to the western United States. It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, edible berries.

The plant typically grows in moist, acidic soils in mountainous regions. Its leaves are oval-shaped with a leathery texture and a deep green color.
