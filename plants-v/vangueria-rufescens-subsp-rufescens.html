Vangueria rufescens subsp. rufescens

Vangueria rufescens subsp.

rufescens, commonly known as the red vangueria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions.

The plant features small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or red in color, which bloom in clusters. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, adapted to conserve water in dry environments.

The plant is often found in rocky soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience.
