Viola tricolor proles olyssiponensis

Viola tricolor proles olyssiponensis, commonly known as the Olyssiponense violet, is a variant of the common violet.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Iberian Peninsula. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces clusters of violet, purple, or white flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.
