Vernonia santacruzensis

Vernonia santacruzensis, commonly known as Santa Cruz ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the United States, specifically found in California.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Vernonia santacruzensis is known for its resilience and ability to thrive in arid conditions.
