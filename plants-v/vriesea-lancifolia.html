Vriesea lancifolia

Vriesea lancifolia, commonly known as the sword fern, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It features long, sword-shaped leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, white, and pink. The plant produces striking inflorescences with bright orange and yellow flowers, which bloom in the spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Vriesea lancifolia is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
