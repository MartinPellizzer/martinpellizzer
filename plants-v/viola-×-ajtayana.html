Viola × ajtayana

Viola × ajtayana, commonly known as the Ajtay violet, is a hybrid species belonging to the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, heart-shaped leaves and showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows to a height of 10–20 cm and prefers moist, well-drained soil in partial shade. It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

Viola × ajtayana is known for its hardiness and ability to thrive in temperate climates.
