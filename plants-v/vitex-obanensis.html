Vitex obanensis

Vitex obanensis, commonly known as the Oban vitex, is a species of flowering plant native to the island of Oban in Scotland.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal and woodland habitats. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually purple or blue in color, which bloom in summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a rough texture.

Vitex obanensis is notable for its ornamental value and its role in supporting local biodiversity.
