Verbascum × corbierei

Verbascum × corbierei, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the genus Verbascum, which includes species known for their fuzzy, velvety leaves and showy yellow or orange flowers. The hybrid is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy inflorescences that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

The plant is also valued for its medicinal properties, with historical uses in treating respiratory ailments.
