Verbascum × longicrure

Verbascum × longicrure, commonly known as the long-stemmed mullein.

This hybrid plant is a member of the Scrophulariaceae family, characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, velvety leaves. It produces showy yellow flowers in late summer, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soils.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
