Vernonia kawoziensis

Vernonia kawoziensis, commonly known as Kawozi vernonia, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and woodlands. It produces small, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

The leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinct appearance.
