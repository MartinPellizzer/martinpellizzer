Veronica chamaedrys var. procera

Veronica chamaedrys var. procera, commonly known as tall speedwell, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe.

It features small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has upright stems and hairy leaves, which help it retain moisture in dry conditions. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a valuable addition to gardens and natural landscapes.
