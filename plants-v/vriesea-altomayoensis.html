Vriesea altomayoensis

Vriesea altomayoensis, commonly known as the Altomayo bromeliad, is a species native to Ecuador.

It is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful inflorescence. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that collect rainwater, forming a central reservoir. Its flowers are typically red or orange, with a showy, spadix-like structure.

Vriesea altomayoensis thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
