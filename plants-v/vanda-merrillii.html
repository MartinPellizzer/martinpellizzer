Vanda merrillii

Vanda merrillii, commonly known as the Merrill's vanda, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees or rocks in tropical rainforests. The plant features showy, fragrant flowers that are usually violet or blue in color, with a distinctive white lip. Vanda merrillii has lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and covered in a waxy coating.

It requires bright, indirect light and high humidity to thrive.
