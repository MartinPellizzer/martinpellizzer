Vernonia karongensis

Vernonia karongensis, commonly known as the Karongensis ironweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Vernonia karongensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its distinctive appearance.
