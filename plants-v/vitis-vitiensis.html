Vitis vitiensis

Vitis vitiensis, commonly known as the Vitiensis grapevine, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a climbing vine that produces small, greenish-white flowers and small, round berries. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and vigorous growth habit. It is often found in humid, shaded environments such as forests and along riverbanks.

The berries are edible but are not widely cultivated for commercial wine production.
