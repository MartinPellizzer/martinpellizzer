Valantia crebrifoliata

Valantia crebrifoliata, commonly known as the denseleaf valantia, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, overlapping foliage that gives it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically in shades of white and pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Valantia crebrifoliata is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
