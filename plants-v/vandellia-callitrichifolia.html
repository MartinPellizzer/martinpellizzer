Vandellia callitrichifolia

Vandellia callitrichifolia, commonly known as the glassworts, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and small, greenish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in saline and alkaline soils, often found in coastal dunes and estuaries. It has a creeping growth habit with branched stems that root at the nodes.

Vandellia callitrichifolia is an important species for stabilizing coastal sands and providing habitat for various small organisms.
