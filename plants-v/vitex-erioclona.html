Vitex erioclona

Vitex erioclona, commonly known as the silky oaks, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are typically pinnate and have a silvery underside. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are usually purple or blue in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal regions.

Vitex erioclona is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
