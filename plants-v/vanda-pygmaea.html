Vanda pygmaea

Vanda pygmaea, commonly known as the miniature vanda, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, compact growth habit and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native tropical climate.

Vanda pygmaea is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
