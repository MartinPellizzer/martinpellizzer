Vanda javierae

Vanda javierae, commonly known as the Javier's vanda, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has long, strap-like leaves and thrives in humid, shaded environments. It requires well-draining potting media and regular watering to flourish.

Vanda javierae is prized for its ornamental value in horticulture and is relatively easy to cultivate with proper care.
