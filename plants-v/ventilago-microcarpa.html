Ventilago microcarpa

Ventilago microcarpa, commonly known as the African milkwood tree, is a deciduous tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its rough, fissured bark and small, opposite leaves that turn red in autumn. The plant produces yellow flowers followed by small, woody fruits containing elliptical seeds. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky soils.

The tree has a long lifespan and is valued for its hard, durable wood and medicinal properties.
