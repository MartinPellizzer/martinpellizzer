Vellozia martiana

Vellozia martiana, commonly known as the mountain heath, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, needle-like foliage and clusters of small, vibrant red flowers that bloom during the cooler months. The plant is adapted to high-altitude environments, thriving in well-drained, rocky soils and cool, humid conditions. Its unique structure and flowering pattern make it a notable species in its native habitat.

Vellozia martiana is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in specialized gardens.
