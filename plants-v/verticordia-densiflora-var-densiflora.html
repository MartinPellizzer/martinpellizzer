Verticordia densiflora var. densiflora

Verticordia densiflora var. densiflora, commonly known as the denseflowered verticordia, is a perennial shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and its striking, tubular, pink to red flowers that appear in late winter to early spring. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathland and sandplain environments.

This species is noted for its ornamental value and is cultivated for its unique floral display.
