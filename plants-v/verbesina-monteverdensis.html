Verbesina monteverdensis

Verbesina monteverdensis, commonly known as the Monteverde beggar's ticks, is a flowering plant native to the cloud forests of Costa Rica.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow-orange inflorescences and hairy, glandular stems. The plant produces small, wind-pollinated flowers that are clustered in dense, branched heads. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often covered with fine pubescence.

Verbesina monteverdensis thrives in humid, shaded environments and is adapted to the unique ecological conditions of its montane habitat.
