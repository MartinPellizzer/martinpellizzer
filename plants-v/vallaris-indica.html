Vallaris indica

Vallaris indica, commonly known as the Indian vallaris, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Oleaceae.

It is native to regions of Asia, including India, China, and Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, fragrant white or yellow flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Vallaris indica is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
