Vigna gentryi

Vigna gentryi, commonly known as the Gentry's bean, is a species of legume native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and trifoliate leaves, typical of many leguminous plants. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. Vigna gentryi is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for study in ecological and agricultural contexts.
