Vernonia cognata var. sceptrum

Vernonia cognata var. sceptrum, commonly known as the narrowleaf ironweed, is a perennial herb native to the southeastern United States.

It features upright, slender stems covered in fine, hairy foliage. The plant produces small, clustered purple to pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly serrated edge.

It is often found in moist to wet habitats such as swamps and marshes.
