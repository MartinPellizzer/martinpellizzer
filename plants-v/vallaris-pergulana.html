Vallaris pergulana

Vallaris pergulana, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showing resistance to drought and poor soil conditions.

Vallaris pergulana is also valued for its hard, durable wood, which has been used traditionally for various purposes.
