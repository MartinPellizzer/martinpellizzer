Vitex vauthieri

Vitex vauthieri, commonly known as the African blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant produces fragrant, blue or purple flower clusters that bloom in late summer to early autumn. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Vitex vauthieri is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
