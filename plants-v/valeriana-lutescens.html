Valeriana lutescens

Valeriana lutescens, commonly known as yellow valerian, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb with slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Valeriana lutescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often grown in gardens for its delicate appearance.
