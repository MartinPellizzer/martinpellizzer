Vernonia chiangdaoensis

Vernonia chiangdaoensis, commonly known as the Chiangdao Vernonia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Vernonia chiangdaoensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential ecological benefits in native habitats.
