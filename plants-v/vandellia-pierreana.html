Vandellia pierreana

Vandellia pierreana, commonly known as the Pierre's vandellia, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its small, intricate flowers that are typically pale green to white in color. The plant thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in areas with high humidity and mild temperatures.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species, making it a subject of interest for conservation efforts.
