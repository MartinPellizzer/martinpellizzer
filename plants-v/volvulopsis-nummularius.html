Volvulopsis nummularius

Volvulopsis nummularius, commonly known as the silver dollar plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically round or slightly oval in shape, with a silvery or grayish hue due to a dense covering of fine hairs. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and striking foliage make it a desirable ornamental plant.
