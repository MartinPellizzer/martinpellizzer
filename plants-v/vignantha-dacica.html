Vignantha dacica

Vignantha dacica, commonly known as the mountain bitter melon, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes cucumbers and squash. The plant produces small, bitter green fruits that are typically harvested when immature. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are hairy and trailing.
