Veronica austriaca f. neiceffii

Veronica austriaca f. neiceffii, commonly known as the Austrian speedwell, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Central Europe.

It features small, blue to violet flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to July. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a slightly fuzzy texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or meadow environments.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
