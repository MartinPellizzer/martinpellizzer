Vitis davidi var. ningqiangensis

Vitis davidi var. ningqiangensis, commonly known as the Ningqiang grapevine, is a rare and endemic species native to China.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and robust vines that can reach significant lengths. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its leaves are heart-shaped with a rough upper surface and a hairy underside.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and potential for cultivation in specialized horticultural settings.
