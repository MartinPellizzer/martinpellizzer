Viburnum brachybotryum var. tengyuehense

Viburnum brachybotryum var. tengyuehense, commonly known as the Tengyuehense viburnum, is a shrub native to China.

It features small, white to pink flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in late spring. The plant has elliptic to ovate leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It produces small, red berries that are edible but not widely cultivated.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions.
