Veronica × fairfieldii

Veronica × fairfieldii, commonly known as the Fairfield's speedwell, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Plantaginaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, blue to violet flowers. The plant features slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It is typically grown as an ornamental plant in gardens due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.

Veronica × fairfieldii thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight.
