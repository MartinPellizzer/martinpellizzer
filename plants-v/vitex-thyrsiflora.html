Vitex thyrsiflora

Vitex thyrsiflora, commonly known as the African blueberry or blue hibiscus, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its attractive, showy blue or violet flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for warm climates.

Vitex thyrsiflora is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
