Veronica dillenii

Veronica dillenii, commonly known as the blueweed or narrowleaf speedwell, is a flowering plant native to Europe, North Africa, and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of small, blue or violet flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically hairy and arranged alternately along the stem. Veronica dillenii is often found in grasslands, rocky areas, and along roadsides, thriving in well-drained soils.
