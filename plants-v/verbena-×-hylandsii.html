Verbena × hylandsii

Verbena × hylandsii, commonly known as the hybrid verbena, is a flowering plant in the Verbenaceae family.

It is a cross between Verbena rigida and Verbena bonariensis, resulting in a plant with unique characteristics. The species is known for its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of purple, pink, and blue. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its compact growth habit and long blooming period make it a valuable addition to ornamental landscapes.
