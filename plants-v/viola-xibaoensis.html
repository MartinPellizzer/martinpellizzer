Viola xibaoensis

Viola xibaoensis, commonly known as the Chinese violet, is a perennial flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in shady, moist environments such as forest floors or mountainous regions. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Viola xibaoensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
