Vriesea paupera, commonly known as the poor vriesea, is a species of flowering plant in the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. Vriesea paupera is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and ability to thrive in humid environments.
