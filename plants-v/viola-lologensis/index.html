Viola Lologensis

Viola lologensis is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of western North America. It is known for its striking violet petals and unique leaf structure that help it survive in harsh alpine conditions. This species was first discovered in the 19th century and has since been the subject of much botanical interest. Due to habitat loss and climate change, Viola lologensis is now considered endangered. Conservation efforts are underway to protect and preserve this beautiful and vulnerable plant.
