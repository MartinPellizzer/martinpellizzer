Viola longipetiolata

Viola longipetiolata, commonly known as the long-petioled violet, is a species of flowering plant in the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves with long petioles, which give it its common name. The plant produces vibrant purple or violet flowers that bloom in early spring. It is native to parts of Asia, including China and Japan, and thrives in cool, moist environments.

Viola longipetiolata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
