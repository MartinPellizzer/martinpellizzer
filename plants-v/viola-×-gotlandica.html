Viola × gotlandica

Viola × gotlandica, commonly known as the Gotland violet, is a hybrid species belonging to the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, velvety leaves and clusters of violet or purple flowers. Native to the Baltic region, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments. It is often found in woodland areas and along stream banks.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
