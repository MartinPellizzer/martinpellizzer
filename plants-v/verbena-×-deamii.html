Verbena × deamii

Verbena × deamii, commonly known as the hybrid verbena, is a flowering plant in the Verbenaceae family.

It is a cross between Verbena hastata and Verbena officinalis, resulting in a compact, bushy plant with vibrant, showy blooms. The flowers are typically blue or purple and are borne in dense spikes, adding ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for Mediterranean climates.

This plant is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
