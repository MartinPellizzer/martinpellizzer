Viburnum Alabamense

Viburnum alabamense is a striking native shrub found throughout the southeastern united states. it is known for its showy clusters of white to pink flowers that bloom in early spring. the plant produces bright red berries that attract birds and provide food for wildlife. it thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sun. viburnum alabamense is a valuable addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
