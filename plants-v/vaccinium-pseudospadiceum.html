Vaccinium pseudospadiceum

Vaccinium pseudospadiceum, commonly known as the dwarf blueberry, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

Vaccinium pseudospadiceum is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local wildlife.
