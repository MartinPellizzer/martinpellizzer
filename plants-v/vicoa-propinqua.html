Vicoa propinqua

Vicoa propinqua, commonly known as the lesser vicoa, is a member of the Malvaceae family, characterized by its soft, fibrous stems and broad, ovate leaves.

Vicoa propinqua is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
