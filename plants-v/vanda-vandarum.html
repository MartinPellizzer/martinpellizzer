Vanda vandarum

Vanda vandarum, commonly known as the giant orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 10 centimeters in diameter. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, using its aerial roots to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native humid environments.

Vanda vandarum is prized in horticulture for its vibrant colors and long-lasting blooms.
