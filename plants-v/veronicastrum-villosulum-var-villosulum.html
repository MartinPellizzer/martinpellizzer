Veronicastrum villosulum var. villosulum

Veronicastrum villosulum var. villosulum, commonly known as tall ironweed, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach heights of up to 6 feet. The plant produces showy, spike-like clusters of blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped, hairy, and often have a rough texture.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for native plant gardens.
