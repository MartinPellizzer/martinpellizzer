Ventilago sulphurea

Ventilago sulphurea, commonly known as the sulphur-flowered velvet bush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, oval-shaped fruits that are typically green when mature. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy habitats.

Ventilago sulphurea is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
