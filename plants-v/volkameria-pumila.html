Volkameria pumila

Volkameria pumila, commonly known as the dwarf volkameria, is a small, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, leathery leaves that are typically grayish-green in color. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, often clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in coastal areas.

Due to its compact size and ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens.
