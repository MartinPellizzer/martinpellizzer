Verticordia brevifolia subsp. brevifolia

Verticordia brevifolia subsp.

brevifolia, commonly known as the small-stemmed featherflower, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia. It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, bright red or orange flowers.

The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in sandy soils and is typically found in heathlands and coastal areas.
