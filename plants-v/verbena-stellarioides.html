Verbena stellarioides

Verbena stellarioides, commonly known as starwort, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, sunny environments such as wet meadows and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Verbena stellarioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
