Vaccinium centrocelebicum

Vaccinium centrocelebicum, commonly known as the Celebes huckleberry, is a species of evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like blueberries and cranberries. The plant produces small, edible berries that are typically dark blue or black in color. These berries are often used in local cuisines and traditional medicine.

Its botanical characteristics include dense foliage and a preference for acidic, well-drained soils.
