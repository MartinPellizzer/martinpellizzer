Vitex gardneriana

Vitex gardneriana, commonly known as the Chinese vitex or fragrant verbena, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often aromatic when crushed. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of purple or blue, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its woody stems are covered in fine, soft hairs, and it typically grows in moist, well-drained soils.

Vitex gardneriana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
