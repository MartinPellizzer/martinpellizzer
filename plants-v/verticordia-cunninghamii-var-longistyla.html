Verticordia cunninghamii var. longistyla

Verticordia cunninghamii var. longistyla, commonly known as the long-styled everlasting or long-styled verticordia, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, urn-shaped flowerheads that range in color from yellow to red, depending on the stage of bloom. The plant typically grows in heathland and sandy soils, often in areas with poor nutrient content. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it prefers well-drained conditions and full sun.
