Vellozia spekei

Vellozia spekei, commonly known as the Speke's vellozia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Velloziaceae and is characterized by its rosette growth form and striking, often colorful foliage. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Vellozia spekei is adapted to arid environments and thrives in well-drained soils.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a sought-after plant in horticulture.
