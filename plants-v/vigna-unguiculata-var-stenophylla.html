Vigna unguiculata var. stenophylla

Vigna unguiculata var. stenophylla, commonly known as the slender cowpea, is a leguminous plant characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and compact growth habit.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain edible seeds, which are rich in protein and often used in various cuisines. Its deep root system helps in nitrogen fixation, improving soil fertility.

Vigna unguiculata var. stenophylla is also valued for its drought tolerance and adaptability to poor soils.
