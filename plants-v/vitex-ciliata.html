Vitex ciliata

Vitex ciliata, commonly known as the silky vitex, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a silvery underside, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its flowers are typically clustered in panicles and are usually a pale blue or purple color.

Vitex ciliata is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its attractive foliage and blooms.
