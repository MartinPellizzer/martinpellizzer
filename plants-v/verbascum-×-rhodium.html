Verbascum × rhodium

Verbascum × rhodium, commonly known as the silverleaf mullein, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Scrophulariaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, velvety silver-green leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. It is native to southern Europe and thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Verbascum × rhodium is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
