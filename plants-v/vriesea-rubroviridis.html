Vriesea rubroviridis

Vriesea rubroviridis, commonly known as the red-veined vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It features vibrant, sword-shaped leaves that are dark green with prominent red veins, creating a visually appealing contrast. The plant produces tall flower spikes with showy, red bracts that resemble flowers, though they are actually modified leaves. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Vriesea rubroviridis is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
