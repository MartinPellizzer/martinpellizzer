Veronica petioiaris

Veronica petioiaris, commonly known as the slender speedwell, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, blue or purple flowers. The plant has narrow, elongated leaves that give it its scientific name, and it thrives in well-drained soils. Veronica petioiaris is often found in rocky or sandy habitats and is adapted to dry conditions.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
