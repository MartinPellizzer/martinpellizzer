Virola minutiflora

Virola minutiflora, commonly known as the Amazonian rosewood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, dark wood and is highly valued for its aromatic properties. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a glossy surface.

Virola minutiflora is also known for its medicinal uses in traditional medicine.
