Viola alsophila

Viola alsophila, commonly known as the alpine violet, is a species of flowering plant in the Violaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia, where it thrives in cool, moist environments. The plant produces showy violet flowers with five petals, typically in shades of purple or blue. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Viola alsophila is often found in alpine meadows and rocky habitats, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local ecosystems.
