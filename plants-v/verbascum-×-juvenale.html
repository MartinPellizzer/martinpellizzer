Verbascum × juvenale

Verbascum × juvenale, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the genus Verbascum, which includes species known for their large, fuzzy leaves and showy yellow or orange flowers. The hybrid form, Verbascum × juvenale, is characterized by its robust growth and distinctive velvety foliage. It typically thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

This plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
