Viola primorskajensis

Viola primorskajensis, commonly known as the Primorsky viola, is a species of flowering plant native to the Russian Far East.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and vibrant violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky slopes. Its flowers bloom in early spring, adding a splash of color to its natural habitat.

Viola primorskajensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
