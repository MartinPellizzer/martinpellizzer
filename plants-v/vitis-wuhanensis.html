Vitis wuhanensis

Vitis wuhanensis, commonly known as Wuhan grape, is a species of flowering vine native to China.

It belongs to the Vitaceae family and is characterized by its tendrils and compound leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that give way to edible berries. Vitis wuhanensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and potential fruit production.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for horticultural and ecological studies.
