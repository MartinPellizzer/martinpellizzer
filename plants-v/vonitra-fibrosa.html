Vonitra fibrosa

Vonitra fibrosa, commonly known as the Madagascar fibrous vine, is a climbing plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its fibrous, woody stems and broad, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes. Its unique morphology allows it to thrive in the humid, tropical environments of its native habitat.

Vonitra fibrosa is often found in lowland rainforests and is adapted to grow along tree trunks and branches.
