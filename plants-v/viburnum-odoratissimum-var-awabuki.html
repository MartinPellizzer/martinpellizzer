Viburnum odoratissimum var. awabuki

Viburnum odoratissimum var. awabuki, commonly known as Japanese snowberry, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features showy white to pale pink flowers that appear in clusters during late spring. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible but often considered ornamental. Its foliage is typically dark green with a slightly glossy appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.
