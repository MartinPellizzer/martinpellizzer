Valeriana philippiana

Valeriana philippiana, commonly known as Philippine valerian, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas and has opposite, ovate leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, and it produces small, berry-like fruits.

Valeriana philippiana is valued for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
