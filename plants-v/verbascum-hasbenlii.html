Verbascum hasbenlii

Verbascum hasbenlii, commonly known as the Turkish mullein, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the genus Verbascum, which includes species known for their showy, velvety leaves and yellow or orange flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is characterized by its tall, erect stems. Its flowers bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Verbascum hasbenlii is also valued for its medicinal properties, with historical use in traditional remedies.
