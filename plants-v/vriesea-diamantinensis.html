Vriesea diamantinensis

Vriesea diamantinensis, commonly known as the Diamond Mountain Vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, gracefully arching inflorescence that bears bright orange flowers with a distinctive red stripe. The plant features broad, strap-like leaves that are dark green with a metallic sheen, giving it a visually striking appearance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Vriesea diamantinensis is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
