Vernonia sengana

Vernonia sengana, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub or tree that typically grows in dry, sandy soils. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by small, round fruits. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a rough texture.

Vernonia sengana is valued for its hard wood and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
