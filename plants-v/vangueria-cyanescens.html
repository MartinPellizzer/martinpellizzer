Vangueria cyanescens

Vangueria cyanescens, commonly known as the blue vangueria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in coastal areas. The plant produces small, blue or purple flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its leaves are narrow and often have a bluish-gray hue, which helps it retain moisture in arid conditions.

Vangueria cyanescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
