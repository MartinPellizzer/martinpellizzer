Verbascum × porcii

Verbascum × porcii, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a composite plant belonging to the genus Verbascum in the family Scrophulariaceae.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its thick, woolly stems and large, showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and open fields. Its leaves are large, broad, and covered in soft, fuzzy hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Verbascum × porcii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
