Vicia wushanica

Vicia wushanica, commonly known as the Wushan vetch, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its climbing habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically yellow or green in color. Vicia wushanica is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, which enriches the soil.

It is often found in rocky or mountainous habitats, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
