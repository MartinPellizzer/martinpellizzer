Vatairea heteroptera

Vatairea heteroptera, commonly known as the Brazilian cherry or *Erythroxylum heteroptera*, is a flowering plant native to South America.

The leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptical, with a glossy green upper surface and a lighter underside. It produces small, fragrant white flowers that give way to red, fleshy berries.
