Veronica × media

Veronica × media, commonly known as the common speedwell, is a hybrid plant that belongs to the Plantaginaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 10–30 cm and features narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Veronica × media is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
