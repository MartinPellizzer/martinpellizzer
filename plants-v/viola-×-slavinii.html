Viola × slavinii

Viola × slavinii, commonly known as the Slavinii violet, is a hybrid species within the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, often bi-colored flowers that range from purple to white. The plant typically features heart-shaped, toothed leaves that are covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. Native to the mountainous regions of Europe, it thrives in cool, moist environments.

Viola × slavinii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom reliably in garden settings.
