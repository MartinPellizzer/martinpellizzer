Vochysia rotundifolia

Vochysia rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leaf vochysia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its broad, rounded leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stems. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are often white or pale yellow, which are followed by spherical, woody fruits containing several seeds. Vochysia rotundifolia thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and horticultural research.
