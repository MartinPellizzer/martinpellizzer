Vismia acuminata

Vismia acuminata, commonly known as the African snowball, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub that typically grows up to 10 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its dense, rounded clusters of small white flowers that resemble snowballs, which bloom in the rainy season. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a waxy texture.

Vismia acuminata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
