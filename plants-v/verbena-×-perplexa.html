Verbena × perplexa

Verbena × perplexa, commonly known as the hybrid verbena, is a flowering plant belonging to the Verbenaceae family.

It is a hybrid between Verbena rigida and Verbena bonariensis, resulting in a plant with distinctive characteristics. The plant produces spikes of vibrant blue or purple flowers, which bloom throughout the summer months. It is native to South America but has been widely cultivated in gardens across the United States and Europe.

Verbena × perplexa is known for its hardiness, adaptability, and ability to thrive in full sun to partial shade.
