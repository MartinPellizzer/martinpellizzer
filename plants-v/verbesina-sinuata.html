Verbesina sinuata

Verbesina sinuata, commonly known as the zigzag mallow, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It features slender, zigzagging stems that are covered in soft, hairy foliage. The plant produces showy yellow-orange flowers with five petals, typically blooming from late spring to early autumn. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a slightly wavy margin.

Verbesina sinuata thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for native gardens.
