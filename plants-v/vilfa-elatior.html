Vilfa elatior

Vilfa elatior, commonly known as the golden bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically golden yellow or orange in color. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Vilfa elatior is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
