Vitis thonningii

Vitis thonningii, commonly known as Thonning's grapevine, is a species of flowering plant in the Vitaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, particularly Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with tendrils that allow it to ascend trees and shrubs. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a rough texture, while its small, greenish-white flowers appear in clusters.

The fruit is a small, round berry that turns from green to red as it matures.
