Vulpia cynosuroides

Vulpia cynosuroides, commonly known as the common vulpia, is a species of grass that belongs to the Poaceae family.

It is a small, annual plant that typically grows in disturbed soils, such as roadsides and fields. The plant has slender, tufted stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Vulpia cynosuroides is adapted to temperate climates and is often found in regions with moderate rainfall.
