Vangueria ferruginea

Vangueria ferruginea, commonly known as the red sandalwood or red ironwood, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, evergreen shrub or tree that typically grows in dry, rocky soils. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a leathery texture with a reddish tint.

The wood of Vangueria ferruginea is highly valued for its durability and rich color, making it a significant species in local economies.
