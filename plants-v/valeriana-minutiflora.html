Valeriana minutiflora

Valeriana minutiflora, commonly known as the small-flowered valerian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia and the Pacific Islands.

It features a robust rhizomatous root system and upright stems that bear opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white, arranged in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Valeriana minutiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported calming properties.
