Vanda concolor

Vanda concolor, commonly known as the purple vanda, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, arching stems and clusters of showy, violet to purple flowers. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments with high light exposure. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in its natural habitat.

Vanda concolor is popular among orchid growers for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
