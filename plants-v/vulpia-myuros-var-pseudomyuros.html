Vulpia myuros var. pseudomyuros

Vulpia myuros var. pseudomyuros, commonly known as the slender vulpia, is a grass species native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It is characterized by its fine, wiry leaves and slender, spike-like inflorescences. This variety typically grows in disturbed soils, such as along roadsides, fields, and waste areas. The plant produces small, two-lobed flowers that are wind-pollinated.

Its adaptability to various soil types makes it a common weed in agricultural and urban environments.
