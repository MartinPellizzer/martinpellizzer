Vigna sinensis var. catjang

Vigna sinensis var. catjang, commonly known as the catjang bean, is a leguminous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and trifoliate leaves with oval, serrated leaflets. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain edible seeds used in various culinary applications. It thrives in warm, tropical climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The species is valued for its nutritional content and adaptability to different agricultural conditions.
