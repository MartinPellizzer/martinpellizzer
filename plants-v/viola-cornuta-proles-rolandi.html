Viola cornuta proles rolandi

Viola cornuta proles rolandi, commonly known as the Roland's horned violet, is a species of flowering plant in the Violaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe, particularly in Italy and the Balkans. This perennial herb features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive violet or purple coloration. The plant produces showy, spurred flowers that are typically violet or blue in color.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
