Vernonia krukovii

Vernonia krukovii, commonly known as the African ironweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a rough, hairy texture.

Vernonia krukovii is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
