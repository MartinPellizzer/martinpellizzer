Vernonia approximata

Vernonia approximata, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a shrub or small tree native to sub-Saharan Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a robust root system and is known for its high density and durability, making it valuable for construction and fuel. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

Vernonia approximata is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
