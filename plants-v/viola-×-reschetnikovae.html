Viola × reschetnikovae

Viola × reschetnikovae, commonly known as the Reschetnikov's violet, is a hybrid species belonging to the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, often purple or violet-colored flowers. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in woodland or alpine habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections for its attractive foliage and blooms.
