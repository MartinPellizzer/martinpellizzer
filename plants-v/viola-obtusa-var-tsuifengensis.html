Viola obtusa var. tsuifengensis

Viola obtusa var. tsuifengensis, commonly known as the Tsiufeng viola, is a perennial flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of violet or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky slopes. Its flowers bloom in spring, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
