Vaccinium hasseltii

Vaccinium hasseltii, commonly known as the Hassen's blueberry, is a species of evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of South America.

Vaccinium hasseltii thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in high-altitude environments.
