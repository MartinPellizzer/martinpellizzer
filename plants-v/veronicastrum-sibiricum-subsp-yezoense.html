Veronicastrum sibiricum subsp. yezoense

Veronicastrum sibiricum subsp.

yezoense, commonly known as Yezo false spirea, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia. It belongs to the Boraginaceae family and is characterized by its erect, spire-like inflorescences.

The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and produces blue to purple flowers in summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along streambanks.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in wetland habitats.
