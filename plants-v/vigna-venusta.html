Vigna venusta

Vigna venusta, commonly known as the beautiful bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its pod-like fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces showy, pale yellow or white flowers. Vigna venusta is often cultivated for its edible seeds, which are used in various cuisines.

Its ornamental value also makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
