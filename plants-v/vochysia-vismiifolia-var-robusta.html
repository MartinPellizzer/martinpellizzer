Vochysia vismiifolia var. robusta

Vochysia vismiifolia var. robusta, commonly known as the robust vochysia, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in diameter, with a glossy green surface and a leathery texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters along the branches. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release several seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm, humid climates for its unique foliage.
