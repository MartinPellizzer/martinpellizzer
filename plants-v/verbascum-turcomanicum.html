Verbascum turcomanicum

Verbascum turcomanicum, commonly known as the Turkestan mullein, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It is a member of the Scrophulariaceae family and is characterized by its tall, hairy stems and large, showy yellow flowers. Verbascum turcomanicum is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its striking appearance and resilience.
