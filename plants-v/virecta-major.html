Virecta major

Virecta major, commonly known as the giant virecta, is a striking plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem that can reach heights of up to 10 meters. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. Its flowers are large and showy, typically displaying vibrant purple or blue hues.

Virecta major thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or mountainous terrain.
