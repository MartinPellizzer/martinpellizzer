Verbesina moluccana

Verbesina moluccana, commonly known as the Moluccan ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows up to 1.5 meters in height, featuring upright stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange inflorescences that bloom in late summer to early autumn. Its flowers are composed of numerous small, tubular florets arranged in a composite head, typical of the Asteraceae family.

Verbesina moluccana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
