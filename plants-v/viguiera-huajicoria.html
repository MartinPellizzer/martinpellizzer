Viguiera huajicoria

Viguiera huajicoria, commonly known as Mexican ironweed, is a perennial herb native to Mexico and parts of the southwestern United States.

The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun, making it well adapted to arid environments. Viguiera huajicoria is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
