Veronica stricta var. stricta

Veronica stricta var. stricta, commonly known as narrowleaf speedwell, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces small, blue or violet flowers in clusters. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often found in meadows, grasslands, and rocky areas.

Its botanical features make it a common ornamental plant in gardens and natural habitats.
