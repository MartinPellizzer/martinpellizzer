Vriesea patzeltii

Vriesea patzeltii, commonly known as the Patzelt's Vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting inflorescence that displays a mix of red, orange, and yellow hues. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that collect water, forming a central pool. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, adding a dramatic visual element to its appearance.

Vriesea patzeltii is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its adaptability and low maintenance requirements.
