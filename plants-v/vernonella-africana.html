Vernonella africana

Vernonella africana, commonly known as African vervain, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It belongs to the family Verbenaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and opposite leaves. Vernonella africana is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
