Vernonia bipontini var. caccaensis

Vernonia bipontini var. caccaensis, commonly known as the Caccaensis Vernonia, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically features slender stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, arranged in clusters, and bloom during the dry season.

It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
