Vincetoxicum japonicum var. grayanum

Vincetoxicum japonicum var. grayanum, commonly known as the Grayanum honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features attractive, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and a smooth, dark green upper surface. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
