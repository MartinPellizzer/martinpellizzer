Vitex thorelii

Vitex thorelii, commonly known as the African blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils. The plant produces striking blue or purple flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a hairy texture.

Vitex thorelii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
