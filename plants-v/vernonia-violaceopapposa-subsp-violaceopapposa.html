Vernonia violaceopapposa subsp.

violaceopapposa, commonly known as the violet pappus Vernonia, is a species of flowering plant in the sunflower family, Asteraceae. It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its erect growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves.
