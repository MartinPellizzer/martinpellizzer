Vernonia lundiana

Vernonia lundiana, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is known for its hard, dense wood, which is highly valued for construction and furniture-making. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Vernonia lundiana is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
