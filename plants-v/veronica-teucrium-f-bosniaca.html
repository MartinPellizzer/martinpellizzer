Veronica teucrium f. bosniaca

Veronica teucrium f. bosniaca, commonly known as Bosnian speedwell, is a flowering plant belonging to the Plantaginaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and blue to violet flowers. The plant typically thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its flowers are clustered in dense spikes, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

It is native to regions of Europe, including Bosnia and Herzegovina, where it is often found in mountainous areas.
