Volkameria grandiflora

Volkameria grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered volkameria, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring woody stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces showy yellow flowers with prominent stamens, which bloom in clusters during the warmer months. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and have a rough texture, while the plant is adapted to grow in well-drained soils.

Volkameria grandiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
