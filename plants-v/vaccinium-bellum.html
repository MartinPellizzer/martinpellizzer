Vaccinium bellum

Vaccinium bellum, commonly known as the dwarf blueberry, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the northern regions of North America.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes other familiar plants like heather and cranberries. The plant produces small, edible berries that are typically blue or purple in color, containing high levels of antioxidants. Its leaves are oval-shaped, leathery, and remain green throughout the year.

Vaccinium bellum thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or forested areas.
