Vanda aliceae

Vanda aliceae, commonly known as the Alice's vanda, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. The plant has long, slender stems and broad, leathery leaves that help it thrive in humid, tropical environments. It requires bright, indirect light and well-draining potting media to flourish.

Vanda aliceae is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
