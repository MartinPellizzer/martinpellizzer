Vriesea platynema var. variegata

Vriesea platynema var. variegata, commonly known as the Variegated Vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It features vibrant, sword-shaped leaves with distinctive white and green variegation. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences that bear numerous small, colorful flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This ornamental plant is popular in indoor gardening for its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
