Vangueria gillettii

Vangueria gillettii, commonly known as the Cape rose, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring woody stems and dense foliage. The plant produces small, pink to red flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. Its leaves are oval-shaped, with a slightly rough texture, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

Vangueria gillettii is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.
