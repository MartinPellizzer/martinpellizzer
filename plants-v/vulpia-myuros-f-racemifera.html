Vulpia myuros f. racemifera

Vulpia myuros f. racemifera, commonly known as the racemifera form of slender veld grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The inflorescence consists of a dense cluster of spikelets arranged in a raceme, giving it its specific epithet. This form is often found in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

It plays a minor role in local ecosystems but is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphological features.
