Vernonia obionifolia

Vernonia obionifolia, commonly known as the Mexican ironweed, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense spikes, and range in color from yellow to brownish-red.

Vernonia obionifolia is known for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
