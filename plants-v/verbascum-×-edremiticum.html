Verbascum × edremiticum

Verbascum × edremiticum, commonly known as the Edremit mullein, is a hybrid plant belonging to the genus Verbascum in the family Scrophulariaceae.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, velvety leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Native to Turkey, it thrives in dry, sunny environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is cultivated in gardens and herbal practices.
