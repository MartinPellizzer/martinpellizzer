Viola gracilis var. aethnensis

Viola gracilis var. aethnensis, commonly known as the Aetna violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the island of Sicily.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and heart-shaped leaves. The flowers are typically violet-blue in color, with a distinctive hooded structure that protects the reproductive organs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland areas and rocky slopes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
