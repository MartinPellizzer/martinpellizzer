Vachellia karroo

Vachellia karroo, commonly known as the sweet thorn, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it resilient to drought. It plays an important role in local ecosystems by providing food and shelter for various wildlife species.

Its bark and pods have been used traditionally for medicinal and practical purposes.
