Vepris termitaria

Vepris termitaria, commonly known as African bedstraw, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its small, aromatic leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides, thriving in humid environments. Its fruits are small, dry capsules that release tiny seeds when mature.

Vepris termitaria is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
