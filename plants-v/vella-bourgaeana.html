Vella bourgaeana

Vella bourgaeana, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, heart-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers. Vella bourgaeana is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
