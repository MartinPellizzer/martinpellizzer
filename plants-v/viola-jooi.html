Viola jooi

Viola jooi, commonly known as the Japanese violet, is a perennial flowering plant native to East Asia.

It features heart-shaped, glossy green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of violet, blue, and white, typically blooming in early spring. Viola jooi prefers cool, moist environments and thrives in partial shade.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ease of care.
