Veronica balcanica

Veronica balcanica, commonly known as Balkan speedwell, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It is a member of the Plantaginaceae family and typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils. The plant features upright stems with opposite, ovate leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, blue or violet, and arranged in dense clusters at the tips of the stems.

It is often found in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.
