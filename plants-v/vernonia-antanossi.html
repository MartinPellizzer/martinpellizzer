Vernonia antanossi

Vernonia antanossi, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a tall, woody shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its dark, hard wood and its clusters of yellow or orange flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a rough texture.

Vernonia antanossi is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in harsh climates.
