Viola shiweii

Viola shiweii, commonly known as the Shiwei violet, is a species of flowering plant in the Violaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of China, where it thrives in cool, moist environments. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces delicate, violet-colored flowers with yellow centers. Viola shiweii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is appreciated for its ability to bloom in partial shade.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique addition to botanical gardens and native plant collections.
