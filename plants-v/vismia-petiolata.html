Vismia petiolata

Vismia petiolata, commonly known as the African snowball, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of white, waxy flowers that resemble snowballs, which bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has simple, opposite, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green. It produces small, round, red fruits that contain a single seed.

Vismia petiolata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its striking floral display and compact growth habit.
