Vitis subaphylla

Vitis subaphylla, commonly known as the subaphylla grape, is a species of flowering plant in the Vitaceae family.

It is native to parts of East Asia, including China, Korea, and Japan. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit and small, heart-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically greenish-white and appear in clusters, followed by small, round berries.

Vitis subaphylla is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and potential horticultural value.
