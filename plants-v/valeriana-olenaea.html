Valeriana olenaea

Valeriana olenaea, commonly known as the African valerian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a fibrous root system and erect, branched stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in spikes, which bloom during the rainy season. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and have a distinctive aromatic scent.

Valeriana olenaea is known for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional African medicine.
