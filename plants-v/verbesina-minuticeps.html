Verbesina minuticeps

Verbesina minuticeps, commonly known as the small-headed beggar-ticks, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant features slender, branching stems covered in fine, sticky hairs.
