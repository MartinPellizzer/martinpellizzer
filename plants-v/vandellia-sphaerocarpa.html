Vandellia sphaerocarpa

Vandellia sphaerocarpa, commonly known as the globe cress, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant is characterized by its small, spherical seed pods that give it its common name. It typically grows in moist, temperate climates and is often found in shaded areas.

The flowers are yellow and attract various pollinators, contributing to its ecological significance.
