Vriesea gladioliflora

Vriesea gladioliflora, commonly known as the gladioliflorous vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, sword-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy inflorescences that resemble a gladiolus. The plant produces clusters of brightly colored flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Vriesea gladioliflora is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
