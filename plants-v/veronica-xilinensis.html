Veronica xilinensis

Veronica xilinensis, commonly known as the Xilin Veronica, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Veronica xilinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
