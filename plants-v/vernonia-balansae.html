Vernonia balansae

Vernonia balansae, commonly known as the Balansa ironweed, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of small, yellow to brown flowers. It thrives in dry, open habitats and is often found in disturbed soils.

Vernonia balansae is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
