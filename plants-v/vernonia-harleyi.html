Vernonia harleyi

Vernonia harleyi, commonly known as the Harlequin ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking purple to blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats, such as swamps and marshes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture.

Vernonia harleyi is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
