Vincentia flavicans

Vincentia flavicans, commonly known as the yellow-flowered climbing vine, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments.
