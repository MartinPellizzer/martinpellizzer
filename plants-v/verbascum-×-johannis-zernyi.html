Verbascum × johannis-zernyi

Verbascum × johannis-zernyi, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial plant belonging to the Scrophulariaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, velvety leaves that are covered in soft, downy hairs. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is native to hybrid populations in the Caucasus region and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Verbascum × johannis-zernyi thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
