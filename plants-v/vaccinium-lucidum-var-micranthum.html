Vaccinium lucidum var. micranthum

Vaccinium lucidum var. micranthum, commonly known as the small-flowered blueberry, is a low-growing shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It produces small, elliptical leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant bears clusters of white to pale pink flowers that bloom in the spring. Its fruit is a small, bright red berry that is edible and contains a high concentration of antioxidants.

This variety is valued for both its ornamental appeal and its use in native plant landscaping.
