Verbena rumelica

Verbena rumelica, commonly known as the Balkan verbena, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its spikes of vibrant blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and open fields. Its leaves are opposite, narrow, and often covered in fine hairs.

Verbena rumelica is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses in the region.
