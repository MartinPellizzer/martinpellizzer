Verbena × hendersonii

Verbena × hendersonii, commonly known as the hybrid verbena, is a flowering plant in the family Verbenaceae.

It is a compact, bushy perennial that produces vibrant clusters of tubular flowers in shades of pink, purple, and red. The plant is native to South America and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for borders and containers.

Its long blooming season, from late spring to early fall, adds continuous color to landscapes.
