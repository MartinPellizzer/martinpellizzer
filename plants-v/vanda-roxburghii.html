Vanda roxburghii

Vanda roxburghii, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant has long, strap-like leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Vanda roxburghii is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
