Valeriana oligantha

Valeriana oligantha, commonly known as the slender valerian, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its upright stems and small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its leaves are opposite and lance-shaped, with a distinct aromatic scent.

Valeriana oligantha is often used in traditional medicine for its purported calming and sedative properties.
