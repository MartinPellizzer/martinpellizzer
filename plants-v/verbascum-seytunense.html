Verbascum seytunense

Verbascum seytunense, commonly known as the Seytun mullein, is a perennial herb native to Turkey.

It belongs to the genus Verbascum, which includes over 300 species of flowering plants. The plant features thick, fuzzy leaves and produces showy yellow to orange flowers in the summer. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
