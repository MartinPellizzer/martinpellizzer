Veronica beccabunga var. limosa

Veronica beccabunga var. limosa, commonly known as the narrow-leaved speedwell, is a flowering plant belonging to the Plantaginaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, blue to violet flowers. This perennial herb typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds.

It is often found in temperate regions of Europe and parts of Asia.
