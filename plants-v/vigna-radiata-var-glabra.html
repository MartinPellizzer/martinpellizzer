Vigna radiata var. glabra

Vigna radiata var. glabra, commonly known as hairy urd, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which are typical of the Vigna genus. The plant produces small, yellow flowers followed by elongated, flat pods containing edible seeds. It thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated for its nutritional value.

This variety is valued for its adaptability and use in traditional cuisines.
