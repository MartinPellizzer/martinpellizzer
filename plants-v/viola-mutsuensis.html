Viola mutsuensis

Viola mutsuensis, commonly known as the Mutsu viola, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its flowers bloom in early spring, adding a splash of color to its natural habitat.

Viola mutsuensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
