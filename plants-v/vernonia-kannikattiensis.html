Vernonia kannikattiensis

Vernonia kannikattiensis, commonly known as the Kannikatti's ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and woodland margins, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Vernonia kannikattiensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential ecological benefits in supporting local pollinators.
