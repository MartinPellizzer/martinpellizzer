Veratrum japonicum var. reymondianum

Veratrum japonicum var. reymondianum, commonly known as Reymond's Japanese arrowroot, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Melanthiaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of white or pale green flowers. The plant produces large, fleshy, tuberous roots that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.
