Viburnum membranaceum

Viburnum membranaceum, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen viburnum, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in late spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. It is valued for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.

Viburnum membranaceum is often used in gardens for its aesthetic appeal and ecological benefits.
