Veronica morrisonicola

Veronica morrisonicola, commonly known as Morrison's speedwell, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Northwest of the United States.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of 10 to 20 centimeters. The plant features small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine, soft hairs.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded to partially shaded environments.
