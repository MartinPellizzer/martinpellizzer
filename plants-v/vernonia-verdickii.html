Vernonia verdickii

Vernonia verdickii, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its woody, aromatic leaves and small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is known for its hard, dense wood, which is highly valued for construction and tool-making. Vernonia verdickii thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

It also has traditional uses in medicine and as a source of dye.
