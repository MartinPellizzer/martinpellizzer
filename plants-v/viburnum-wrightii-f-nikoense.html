Viburnum wrightii f. nikoense

Viburnum wrightii f. nikoense, commonly known as the Nikoense arrowwood, is a cultivar of the arrowwood viburnum.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and dark green foliage. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters during the spring, which are followed by bright red berries in the fall. It is native to the southeastern United States and thrives in partial shade to full sun.

This variety is often used in ornamental landscaping for its attractive appearance and hardiness.
