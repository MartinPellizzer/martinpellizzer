Veronica onoei

Veronica onoei, commonly known as the Japanese speedwell, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its small, blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks. Veronica onoei has lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of flowers in early spring.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
