Virola cumala

Virola cumala, commonly known as the yellow virola, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Myristicaceae and is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves with a smooth, glossy surface. The plant is also noted for its ecological importance in supporting local biodiversity.
