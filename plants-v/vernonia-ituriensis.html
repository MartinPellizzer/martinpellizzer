Vernonia ituriensis

Vernonia ituriensis, commonly known as African ironweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its showy inflorescences. Vernonia ituriensis typically grows in open woodlands and grasslands, thriving in well-drained soils.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
