Vitex kweichowensis

Vitex kweichowensis, commonly known as the Chinese vitex or Kweichow vitex, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the verbena family (Verbenaceae) and is characterized by its opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically blue or purple in color, blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun, making it suitable for ornamental gardening.

Vitex kweichowensis is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
