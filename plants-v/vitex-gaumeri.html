Vitex gaumeri

Vitex gaumeri, commonly known as the Mexican chaste tree, is a shrub or small tree native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its opposite, lance-shaped leaves and showy, fragrant flowers that range from white to purple. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain seeds, and it thrives in well-drained soils in full sun.
