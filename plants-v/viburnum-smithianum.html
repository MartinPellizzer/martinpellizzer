Viburnum smithianum

Viburnum smithianum, commonly known as the Smith's viburnum, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features compound leaves with serrated edges and small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in clusters during late spring. The plant produces bright red berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Viburnum smithianum is valued for its ornamental qualities and adaptability in garden settings.
