Viburnum junghuhnii

Viburnum junghuhnii, commonly known as the Chinese snowball, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy, clustered white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Viburnum junghuhnii is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract birds.
