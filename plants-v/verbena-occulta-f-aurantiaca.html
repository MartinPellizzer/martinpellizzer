Verbena occulta f. aurantiaca

Verbena occulta f. aurantiaca, commonly known as the orange verbena, is a compact, flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, tubular flowers that range in color from pale pink to bright orange, depending on the variety. The plant has narrow, gray-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its prolonged blooming period, which can last from late spring through early autumn.
