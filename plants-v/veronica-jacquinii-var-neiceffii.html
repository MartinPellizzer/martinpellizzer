Veronica jacquinii var. neiceffii

Veronica jacquinii var. neiceffii, commonly known as the Neiceff's speedwell, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, sky-blue to white flowers. The leaves are typically oval-shaped with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
