Vernonia shaferi

Vernonia shaferi, commonly known as Shafer's ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Vernonia shaferi is often found in moist, open habitats such as wetlands and prairies.
