Vallota miniata

Vallota miniata, commonly known as the dwarf vallota, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. Vallota miniata is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
