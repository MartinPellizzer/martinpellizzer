Vernonia timorensis

Vernonia timorensis, commonly known as the Timor Vernonia, is a flowering plant native to the island of Timor.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in dry, open habitats. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
