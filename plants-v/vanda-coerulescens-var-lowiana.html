Vanda coerulescens var. lowiana

Vanda coerulescens var. lowiana, commonly known as the Blue Vanda, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant that thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing on trees or rocks. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and produces striking clusters of blue or violet flowers. It prefers bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

Its unique coloration and elegant appearance make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
