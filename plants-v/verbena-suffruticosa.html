Verbena suffruticosa

Verbena suffruticosa, commonly known as beach verbena, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Americas.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its woody stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces vibrant blue or purple flowers that bloom throughout the summer months. It thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and is often found in dune environments.

Verbena suffruticosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize coastal soils.
