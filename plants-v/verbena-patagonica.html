Verbena patagonica

Verbena patagonica, commonly known as Patagonian verbena, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, sunny environments. The plant produces vibrant blue or purple flowers that bloom throughout the summer months. Its slender, branching stems are covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are lance-shaped and aromatic.

Verbena patagonica is often used in traditional medicine and as a ornamental plant due to its hardiness and visual appeal.
