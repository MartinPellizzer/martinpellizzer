Vitis natalitia

Vitis natalitia, commonly known as Natal grape, is a climbing vine native to southern Africa.

It produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by round, edible berries. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a slightly serrated margin. Its stems are woody and can climb using tendrils.

Vitis natalitia is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
