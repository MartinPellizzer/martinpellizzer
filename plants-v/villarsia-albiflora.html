Villarsia albiflora

Villarsia albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered villarsia, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, white petals and small, green leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth. Its flowers are fragrant and attract various pollinators, including bees and butterflies.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
