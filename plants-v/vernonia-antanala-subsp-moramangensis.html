Vernonia antanala subsp. moramangensis

Vernonia antanala subsp.

moramangensis, commonly known as the Moramanga Vernonia, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and has narrow, linear leaves. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, with a central disk surrounded by ray florets.
