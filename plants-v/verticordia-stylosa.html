Verticordia stylosa

Verticordia stylosa, commonly known as the pink fairy lantern, is a striking member of the family Proteaceae native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its vibrant pink or red flowers that bloom in spring, forming intricate, lantern-like clusters.
