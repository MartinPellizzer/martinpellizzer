Ventilago maingayi

Ventilago maingayi, commonly known as the African snowball, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, white, fluffy flowers that resemble small snowballs, which appear in clusters at the ends of its branches. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Ventilago maingayi is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
