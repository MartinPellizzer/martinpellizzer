Vangueria cistifolia var. cistifolia

Vangueria cistifolia var. cistifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved vangueria, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, narrow leaves that are typically arranged in opposite pairs along the stems. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which are borne on short pedicels. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

The species is characterized by its drought tolerance and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
