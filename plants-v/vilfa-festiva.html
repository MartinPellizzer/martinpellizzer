Vilfa festiva

Vilfa festiva, commonly known as the festive veldtflower, is a flowering plant native to the temperate regions of southern Africa.

It typically grows in open grasslands and rocky soils, thriving in full sun. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, often blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a slightly hairy texture.

Vilfa festiva is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
