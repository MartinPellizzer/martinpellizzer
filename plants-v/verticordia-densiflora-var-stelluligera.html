Verticordia densiflora var. stelluligera

Verticordia densiflora var. stelluligera, commonly known as the star-shaped bottlebrush, is a unique member of the family Proteaceae native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, bottlebrush-like inflorescences that are typically red or orange in color. Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is a sought-after species in horticulture and conservation efforts.
