Vernonia djalonensis

Vernonia djalonensis, commonly known as the African ironweed, is a flowering plant native to West Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in moist, well-drained soils. The plant features upright stems covered in fine hairs and produces clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly rough texture.

Vernonia djalonensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some African cultures.
