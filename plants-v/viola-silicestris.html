Viola silicestris

Viola silicestris, commonly known as the Silversword Viola, is a species of flowering plant in the Violaceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant produces violet-colored flowers with five petals, typically blooming in spring. It has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence.

Viola silicestris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
