Vleckia cordifolia

Vleckia cordifolia, commonly known as the heart-leaf vleckia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom during the warmer months. Vleckia cordifolia thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires partial to full sunlight.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
