Vaccinium talamancense

Vaccinium talamancense, commonly known as the Talamancense huckleberry, is a species of shrub native to Central America.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes many acid-loving plants. The plant produces small, edible berries that are often used in local cuisines. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers shaded to partially shaded environments.
