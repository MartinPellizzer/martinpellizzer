Vanda nana

Vanda nana, commonly known as the miniature vanda, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that thrives in tropical climates, often growing on trees or rocks. The plant features showy, fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and purple. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture from the air, and it requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Vanda nana is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a suitable environment.
