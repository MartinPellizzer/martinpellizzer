Vaccinium aitapense

Vaccinium aitapense, commonly known as the Aitapense blueberry, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often found in the understory of rainforests. The plant produces small, dark blue to black berries that are edible and have a tart flavor. Its leaves are elliptical, with a glossy surface and a slightly serrated edge.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has potential for cultivation in subtropical climates.
