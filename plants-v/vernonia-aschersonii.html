Vernonia aschersonii

Vernonia aschersonii, commonly known as the silverleaf ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. The plant produces small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall. Its leaves are silver-gray in color, which helps reduce water loss through transpiration.

Vernonia aschersonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in tough, low-maintenance conditions.
