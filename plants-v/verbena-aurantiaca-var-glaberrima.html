Verbena aurantiaca var. glaberrima

Verbena aurantiaca var. glaberrima, commonly known as the smooth verbena, is a compact, annual herb native to the southeastern United States.

It features slender, branching stems covered in fine, fuzzy hairs, and small, clustered flowers that range in color from pale pink to bright orange. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant blooms and long flowering period.
