Verbascum balansae

Verbascum balansae, commonly known as the Balansae mullein, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Scrophulariaceae family and is characterized by its tall, hairy stems and large, fuzzy leaves. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Verbascum balansae is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
