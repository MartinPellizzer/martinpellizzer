Vriesea splendens var. longibracteata

Vriesea splendens var. longibracteata, commonly known as the Longibracteata Vriesea, is a hybrid cultivar of the Vriesea genus native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, sword-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a silvery-white midrib. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences adorned with vibrant, colorful flowers in shades of orange, red, and yellow. Its foliage and floral displays make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.

This variety thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to maintain its health and aesthetic appeal.
