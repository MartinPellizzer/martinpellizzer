Vicia faba var. pliniana

Vicia faba var. pliniana, commonly known as the Italian fava bean, is a variety of the broad bean plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its large, broad, kidney-shaped leaves and clusters of purple flowers. The plant produces elongated, cylindrical pods that contain several large, edible seeds. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its nutritious beans.

The variety is valued for its robust growth and high yield in agricultural settings.
