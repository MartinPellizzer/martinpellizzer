Viola langsdorffii var. parviflora

Viola langsdorffii var. parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered violet, is a species of flowering plant in the Violaceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces small, delicate purple flowers. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
