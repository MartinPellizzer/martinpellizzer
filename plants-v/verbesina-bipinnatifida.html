Verbesina bipinnatifida

Verbesina bipinnatifida, commonly known as the golden crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange ray flowers and green, palmately lobed leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as wetlands and prairies. Its inflorescence consists of a central yellow disk surrounded by numerous yellow ray florets.

The species is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
