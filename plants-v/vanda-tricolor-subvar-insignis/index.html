Vanda Tricolor Subvar. Insignis

Vanda tricolor subvar. insignis is a striking orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. Known for its vibrant three-color blooms, this plant is a favorite among orchid enthusiasts. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light. The subvariety insignis is distinguished by its unique color patterns and compact growth habit. Its beauty and resilience make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
