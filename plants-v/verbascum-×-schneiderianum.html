Verbascum × schneiderianum

Verbascum × schneiderianum, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the genus Verbascum, which includes species known for their large, showy flowers and fuzzy leaves. The hybrid is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of yellow to orange flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its fuzzy leaves and vibrant flowers make it an attractive addition to both wild and cultivated landscapes.
