Viola cilicica

Viola cilicica, commonly known as the Cilicia violet, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of violet or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in temperate climates. It is often found in mountainous areas and is adapted to withstand dry conditions.

Viola cilicica is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
