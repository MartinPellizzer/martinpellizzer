Vernonia ceylanica

Vernonia ceylanica, commonly known as the Ceylon vernonia, is a flowering plant native to Sri Lanka.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish to brownish flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically visited by various pollinators. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
