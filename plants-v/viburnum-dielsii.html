Viburnum dielsii

Viburnum dielsii, commonly known as the Chinese snowberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features compound leaves that are opposite and ovate in shape, with a serrated margin. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers in the spring, followed by red, berry-like fruits that are edible for birds. Viburnum dielsii thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
