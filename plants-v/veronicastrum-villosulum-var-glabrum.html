Veronicastrum villosulum var. glabrum

Veronicastrum villosulum var. glabrum, commonly known as the smooth blueweed, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces spikes of blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered with fine hairs, though the variety 'glabrum' is smooth and hairless.

It thrives in moist soils and is often found in wet meadows and along stream banks.
