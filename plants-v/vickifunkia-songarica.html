Vickifunkia songarica

Vickifunkia songarica, commonly known as the Songaric Vicky, is a rare flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, violet-blue petals and unique, spiral-shaped leaves. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil. Its blooming season occurs during the late spring to early summer months.

Due to habitat loss, it is currently listed as an endangered species.
