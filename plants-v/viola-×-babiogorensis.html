Viola × babiogorensis

Viola × babiogorensis, commonly known as the hybrid violet, is a cross between species of the viola genus.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, colorful flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade. Its flowers bloom in early spring, adding a splash of color to gardens and natural habitats.

Viola × babiogorensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
