Viola tenuissima

Viola tenuissima, commonly known as the slender violet, is a small, herbaceous perennial native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows to a height of about 5-10 centimeters, with heart-shaped leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces delicate, violet-colored flowers with five petals, usually in shades of purple or blue. It prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Viola tenuissima is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
