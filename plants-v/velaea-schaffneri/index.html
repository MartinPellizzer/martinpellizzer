Velaea Schaffneri

Schaffner's velvetleaf, scientifically known as Velaea schaffneri, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America. With its lush, velvety foliage and vibrant floral displays, it captivates botanists and garden enthusiasts alike. This species belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its unique inflorescence structure. Despite its beauty, Velaea schaffneri is a rare and endangered plant, facing threats from habitat loss and climate change. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this remarkable species for future generations.
