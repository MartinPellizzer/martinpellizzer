Vicia producta

Vicia producta, commonly known as the common vetch, is a flowering plant in the legume family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia and is often found in disturbed soils, along roadsides, and in waste areas. Vicia producta has a fibrous root system and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

It is a perennial plant that can grow up to 1 meter in height, making it a common weed in agricultural settings.
