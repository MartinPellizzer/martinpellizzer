Verbascum khorassanicum

Verbascum khorassanicum, commonly known as the Afghan mullein, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the genus Verbascum, which includes several species of flowering plants known for their large, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, thriving in sandy or rocky soils. Its leaves are broad and fuzzy, while its flowers range in color from yellow to orange.

Verbascum khorassanicum is often used in traditional medicine and is also valued for its ornamental qualities.
