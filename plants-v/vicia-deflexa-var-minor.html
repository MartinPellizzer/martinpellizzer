Vicia deflexa var. minor

Vicia deflexa var. minor, commonly known as the lesser vetch, is a flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is a low-growing annual herb that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features trifoliate leaves with small, heart-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellowish-green pea-like flowers. Its seed pods are slender and contain several small, kidney-shaped seeds.

This species is often found in disturbed soils and is adapted to a variety of environmental conditions.
