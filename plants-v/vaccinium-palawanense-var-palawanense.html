Vaccinium palawanense var. palawanense

Vaccinium palawanense var. palawanense, commonly known as Palawan huckleberry, is a shrub native to the Philippines, particularly found in the island of Palawan.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces edible berries that are typically dark blue to black in color, containing a high concentration of antioxidants.

It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories, contributing to local biodiversity.
