Valeriana × suendermannii

Valeriana × suendermannii, commonly known as the hybrid valerian, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Caprifoliaceae family.

It is characterized by its aromatic, often fragrant foliage and clusters of small, tubular flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pink. The plant produces spindle-shaped roots that are rich in volatile oils, contributing to its medicinal properties. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or shaded areas.

Valeriana × suendermannii is valued for its calming effects and has been used traditionally in herbal medicine.
