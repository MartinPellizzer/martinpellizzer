Vangueria monantha

Vangueria monantha, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a rough, hairy texture.

Vangueria monantha is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
