Viburnum Laevigatum

Viburnum laevigatum is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America known for its ornamental value and ecological benefits. It features showy clusters of white flowers in spring followed by bright red berries in fall. The plant thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil making it a versatile choice for gardens. Its compact growth habit and seasonal color changes make it a popular landscaping option. Viburnum laevigatum also supports local wildlife by providing food and shelter for birds and insects.
