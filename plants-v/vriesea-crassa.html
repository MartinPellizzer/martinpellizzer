Vriesea crassa

Vriesea crassa, commonly known as the broad-leaved vriesea, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its large, broad, and often brightly colored leaves. The plant produces showy inflorescences that consist of a central spadix surrounded by colorful bracts. Vriesea crassa thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
