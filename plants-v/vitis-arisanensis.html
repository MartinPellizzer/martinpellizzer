Vitis arisanensis

Vitis arisanensis, commonly known as the Arisan grapevine, is a species of flowering plant in the Vitaceae family.

It is native to Taiwan and is characterized by its climbing habit and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. Its berries are typically small and can be either green or red when mature.

Vitis arisanensis is often found in forested areas and is valued for its ornamental and potential medicinal properties.
