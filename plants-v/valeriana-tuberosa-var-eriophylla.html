Valeriana tuberosa var. eriophylla

Valeriana tuberosa var. eriophylla, commonly known as the slender valerian, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

Valeriana tuberosa var. eriophylla thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.
