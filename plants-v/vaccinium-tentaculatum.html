Vaccinium tentaculatum

Vaccinium tentaculatum, commonly known as the tentacle blueberry, is a species of shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes other familiar plants like blueberries and cranberries. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue or purple in color.
