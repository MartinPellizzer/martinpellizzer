Valeriana officinalis var. vayredana

Valeriana officinalis var. vayredana, commonly known as the Vayredan valerian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features aromatic, often purple or pinkish flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has dark green, lance-shaped leaves and a robust root system. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

The essential oils found in its roots and stems are valued for their calming properties.
