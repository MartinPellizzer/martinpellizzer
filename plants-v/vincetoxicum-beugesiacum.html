Vincetoxicum beugesiacum

Vincetoxicum beugesiacum, commonly known as the European iron vine, is a climbing plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and showy, funnel-shaped flowers that range from pink to purple. The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that contain seeds, and it is often found growing along fences or in woodland areas.
