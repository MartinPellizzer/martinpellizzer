Viola × regeliana

Viola × regeliana, commonly known as the Dutch viola, is a hybrid species within the viola genus.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, often bicolor flowers that come in shades of purple, yellow, and white. The plant typically produces heart-shaped leaves with a hairy texture, and it thrives in cool, moist environments. It is a popular choice for rock gardens and borders due to its ornamental value and hardiness.

Viola × regeliana is also valued for its ability to self-seed, making it a reliable perennial in suitable climates.
