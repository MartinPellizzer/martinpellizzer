Viguiera goyasensis

Viguiera goyasensis, commonly known as the Goyasensis Blazingstar, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and showy blooms. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun, making it well-suited for arid environments. Its yellow to orange flowers are surrounded by pale green ray florets, adding visual interest to the landscape.

Viguiera goyasensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance in xeriscaping.
