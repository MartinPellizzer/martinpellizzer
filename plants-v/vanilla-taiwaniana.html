Vanilla taiwaniana

Vanilla taiwaniana, commonly known as the Taiwan vanilla, is a species of orchid native to Taiwan.

It is a climbing vine that produces fragrant, dark purple to black pods, which are the source of its vanilla-like aroma. The plant thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in shaded, humid environments. Its flowers are typically greenish-white with a subtle fragrance, and it is valued for its ornamental and potential culinary uses.

The species is relatively rare and requires specific care to cultivate successfully.
