Viola × juliae

Viola × juliae, commonly known as the hybrid violet, is a member of the Violaceae family.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant that typically grows to a height of about 10-20 centimeters. The plant features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a hairy texture. Its flowers are usually violet or purple, with five petals and a distinctive yellow beard at the base.

Viola × juliae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
