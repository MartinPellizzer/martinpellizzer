Vangueria microphylla

Vangueria microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved vangueria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, characterized by its compact form and drought tolerance. The plant features small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a fine, whitish coating, which helps reduce water loss. Its flowers are small and pale yellow, arranged in clusters, and bloom during the warmer months.

Vangueria microphylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
