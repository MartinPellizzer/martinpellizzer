Vitis tsoi

Vitis tsoi, commonly known as the Tsoi grape, is a species of flowering plant in the Vitaceae family.

It is native to regions of eastern Asia, particularly China and the Russian Far East. The plant is characterized by its climbing vines, heart-shaped leaves, and small, clustered flowers. Its fruits are typically small berries that ripen to a dark purple or black color.

Vitis tsoi is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been studied for potential use in horticulture and viticulture.
