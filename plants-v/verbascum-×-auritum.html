Verbascum × auritum

Verbascum × auritum, commonly known as the fuzzy mullein, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Scrophulariaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, fuzzy, gray-green leaves that are covered in soft hairs. The plant produces showy, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. It is native to southern Europe and is often found in dry, rocky soils.

Verbascum × auritum is cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
