Varasia fastigiata

Varasia fastigiata, commonly known as the upright varasia, is a shrub-like plant characterized by its dense, columnar growth habit.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a compact, upright fashion. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or white in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Its unique form and ornamental value make it a popular choice in horticulture.
