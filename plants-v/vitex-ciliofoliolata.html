Vitex ciliofoliolata

Vitex ciliofoliolata, commonly known as the silky leaf vitex, is a shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant, blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The leaves are ovate with a soft, silky texture, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

The plant is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
