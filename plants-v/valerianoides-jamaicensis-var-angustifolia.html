Valerianoides jamaicensis var. angustifolia

Valerianoides jamaicensis var. angustifolia, commonly known as Jamaican valerian, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean region.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its slender, angular stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves.
