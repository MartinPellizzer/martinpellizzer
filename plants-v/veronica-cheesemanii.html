Veronica cheesemanii

Veronica cheesemanii, commonly known as the Cheeseman's speedwell, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, which help it retain moisture in its native environment.

Veronica cheesemanii is often found in rocky or gravelly soils, thriving in sunny to partially shaded conditions.
