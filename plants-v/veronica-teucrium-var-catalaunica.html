Veronica teucrium var. catalaunica

Veronica teucrium var. catalaunica, commonly known as Catalaunica speedwell, is a flowering plant belonging to the Plantaginaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
