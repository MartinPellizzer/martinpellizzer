Vriesea alfarovii

Vriesea alfarovii, commonly known as the Alfarovii Vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful inflorescence that typically displays shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that collect water, forming a central reservoir. Its foliage is often adorned with intricate patterns and a waxy texture.

Vriesea alfarovii is appreciated for its ornamental value and is commonly cultivated as a houseplant.
