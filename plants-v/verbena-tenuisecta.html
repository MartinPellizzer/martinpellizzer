Verbena tenuisecta

Verbena tenuisecta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved verbena, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, narrow leaves and spikes of bright blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and prefers full sun exposure. It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.

Verbena tenuisecta is known for its compact growth habit and long blooming period.
