Vaccinium myrtillus var. yatabei

Vaccinium myrtillus var. yatabei, commonly known as the Yatabei blueberry, is a variant of the common bilberry native to Japan.

This plant is a low-growing evergreen shrub characterized by its small, dark blue berries that are rich in antioxidants. The leaves are oval-shaped with a glossy surface and a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

The plant is valued for both its ornamental appeal and its edible fruits.
