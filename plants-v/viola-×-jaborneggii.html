Viola × jaborneggii

Viola × jaborneggii, commonly known as the hybrid violet, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Violaceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between Viola tricolor and Viola lutea, resulting in a plant with distinctive, often variegated foliage. The species produces showy flowers in shades of purple, yellow, and white, which bloom in early spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value and adaptability contribute to its cultivation in horticultural settings.
