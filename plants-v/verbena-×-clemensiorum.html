Verbena × clemensiorum

Verbena × clemensiorum, commonly known as the hybrid verbena, is a cross between Verbena rigida and Verbena bonariensis.

It is a hardy, herbaceous perennial that produces vibrant clusters of blue, purple, or white flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 2 to 4 feet and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are sturdy and branching.

This species is well adapted to a variety of soil types and is often used in gardens for its ornamental value.
