Vitis luochengensis var. tomentosinerva

Vitis luochengensis var. tomentosinerva, commonly known as the Luocheng hairy grape, is a rare and lesser-known vine species native to China.

It belongs to the Vitaceae family and is characterized by its hairy, pubescent leaves and tendrils. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in panicles. Its berries are usually small and may vary in color from green to red depending on maturity.

Due to its limited distribution and unique morphological features, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
