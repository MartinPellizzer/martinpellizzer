Vitex agnus-castus var. serrata

Vitex agnus-castus var. serrata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved chaste tree, is a deciduous shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves with serrated edges, giving it its varietal name. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of purple and white, typically blooming in summer. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release small, smooth seeds.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
