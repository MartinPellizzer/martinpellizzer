Vincetoxicum triste

Vincetoxicum triste, commonly known as black nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black berries that ripen in the fall. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and produces small, yellowish flowers. It is native to parts of Europe and Asia and is often found in disturbed soils.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
