Viburnum yunnanense

Viburnum yunnanense, commonly known as the Yunnan viburnum, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces bright red berries in the fall, which are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. Its leaves are ovate with serrated edges and turn vibrant yellow in autumn.

Viburnum yunnanense is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
