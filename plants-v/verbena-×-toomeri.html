Verbena × toomeri

Verbena × toomeri, commonly known as the hybrid verbena, is a flowering plant belonging to the Verbenaceae family.

It is a cross between Verbena rigida and Verbena bonariensis, resulting in a vigorous, bushy plant with long, trailing stems. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of blue, violet, and purple, which bloom profusely throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Verbena × toomeri is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
