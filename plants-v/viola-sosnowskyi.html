Viola sosnowskyi

Viola sosnowskyi, commonly known as the Sosnowsky's viola, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, violet-blue flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils. Its flowers bloom in early spring, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat.

Viola sosnowskyi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
