Vellozia gigantea

Vellozia gigantea, commonly known as the giant vellozia, is a striking succulent native to the arid regions of South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, spire-like stems and dense clusters of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces striking, cone-shaped inflorescences that bear small, white or pale pink flowers. It is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is often cultivated in specialized gardens.
