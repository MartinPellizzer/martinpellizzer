Vitis hookeri

Vitis hookeri, commonly known as Hooker's grape, is a flowering vine native to South America.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically green when mature. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
