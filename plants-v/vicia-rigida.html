Vicia rigida

Vicia rigida, commonly known as rigid vetch, is a flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in grassy areas and along roadsides. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

Vicia rigida is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it a valuable plant for soil enrichment.
