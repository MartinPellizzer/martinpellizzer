Vitex pooara

Vitex pooara, commonly known as the silky oaks, is a deciduous tree native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The tree produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, which bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Vitex pooara is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions.
