Visenia crenata

Visenia crenata, commonly known as the smoothleaf violets, is a species of flowering plant in the Violaceae family.

It is native to various regions in the Americas, particularly in the southeastern United States. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a smooth margin and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of purple and white. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Visenia crenata is often found in woodland areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
