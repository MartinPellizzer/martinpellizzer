Vernonia burchelliana

Vernonia burchelliana, commonly known as the silverleaf Vernonia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features silver-gray foliage due to dense pubescence, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments. Its flowers are typically yellow to orange, arranged in a cluster at the top of the plant.

Vernonia burchelliana is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
