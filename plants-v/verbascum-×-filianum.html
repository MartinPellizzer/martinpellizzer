Verbascum × filianum

Verbascum × filianum, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial plant belonging to the Scrophulariaceae family.

It is a hybrid between Verbascum blattaria and Verbascum blattellum, characterized by its large, fuzzy, gray-green leaves. The plant produces tall spikes of yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in rocky or arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance and ease of care.
