Vriesea acuminata

Vriesea acuminata, commonly known as the "Crimson Star" or "Crimson Vriesea," is a species of flowering plant in the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its striking, colorful inflorescence that resembles a star. The plant features broad, dark green, strap-like leaves that form a rosette, creating a central cup that can hold water. Vriesea acuminata produces showy, red and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer or early autumn.

It is a popular ornamental plant due to its vibrant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
