Verbascum tempskyanum

Verbascum tempskyanum, commonly known as the Tempskyan mullein.

This plant is a member of the Scrophulariaceae family and is native to Central Asia. It is characterized by its tall, hairy stems and large, gray-green leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
