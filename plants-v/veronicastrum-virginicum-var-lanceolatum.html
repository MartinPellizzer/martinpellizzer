Veronicastrum virginicum var. lanceolatum

Veronicastrum virginicum var. lanceolatum, commonly known as blue mist spirea, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features tall, upright stems that bear dense clusters of cobalt-blue flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped, finely toothed leaves that are typically gray-green in color. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

This ornamental plant is valued for its attractive foliage and long-lasting floral display.
