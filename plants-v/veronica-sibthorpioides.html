Veronica sibthorpioides

Veronica sibthorpioides, commonly known as the Cape veronica, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form. The plant produces clusters of blue to purple flowers during the spring and summer seasons. Its leaves are narrow, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
