Verbena reitzii

Verbena reitzii, commonly known as the purple verbena, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, clustered flowers. The plant produces vibrant violet to purple blooms that appear in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are small, narrow, and slightly hairy, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Verbena reitzii is often cultivated in gardens for its colorful display and ability to thrive in well-drained soil.
