Vitex robusta

Vitex robusta, commonly known as the robust vitex, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically blue or purple in color, arranged in clustered inflorescences. Its fruit is a dry, three-lobed capsule that contains several seeds.

Vitex robusta is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
