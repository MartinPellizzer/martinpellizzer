Veronica yildirimlii

Veronica yildirimlii, commonly known as the Turkish speedwell, is a perennial flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing habit and vibrant blue or purple flowers. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its prolonged blooming period, which extends from late spring to early autumn.
