Vitex aherniana

Vitex aherniana, commonly known as the narrowleaf vitex, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 6 meters. The plant produces fragrant, pale blue or purple flowers in clusters, which bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and usually have a narrow, lance-shaped form.

Vitex aherniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its attractive foliage and showy blooms.
