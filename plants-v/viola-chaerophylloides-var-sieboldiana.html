Viola chaerophylloides var. sieboldiana

Viola chaerophylloides var. sieboldiana, commonly known as the Siebold's violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces showy violet flowers with five petals, typically in shades of purple or blue. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its foliage and flowers are valued in horticulture for their ornamental appeal.
