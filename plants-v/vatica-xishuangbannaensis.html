Vatica xishuangbannaensis

Vatica xishuangbannaensis, commonly known as the Xishuangbanna vatica, is a tropical evergreen tree native to southern China, particularly in the Xishuangbanna region.

It belongs to the Dipterocarpaceae family and is characterized by its tall stature and broad, glossy leaves. The tree produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its seeds are enclosed in woody capsules and are dispersed by wind.

This species plays an important ecological role in tropical forests, contributing to biodiversity and soil stability.
