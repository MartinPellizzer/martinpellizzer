Veprecella foliosa

Veprecella foliosa, commonly known as the leafy veprecella, is a rare flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
