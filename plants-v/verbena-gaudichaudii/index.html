Verbena Gaudichaudii

Verbena gaudichaudii is a striking flowering plant native to the Canary Islands. It is known for its vibrant clusters of blue or purple flowers that bloom throughout the summer. This species thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas. Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to watering and soil conditions to flourish.
