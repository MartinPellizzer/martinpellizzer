Vriesea friburgensis var. friburgensis

Vriesea friburgensis var. friburgensis, commonly known as the Friburgensis Vriesea, is a species of bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, long-lasting inflorescence that typically features vibrant red or orange bracts. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that form a rosette, creating a compact and elegant appearance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among bromeliad enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
