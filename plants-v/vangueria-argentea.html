Vangueria argentea

Vangueria argentea, commonly known as silver vangueria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features small, pale green leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery-white pubescence, giving it its common name. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow and are clustered in racemes.

Vangueria argentea is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
