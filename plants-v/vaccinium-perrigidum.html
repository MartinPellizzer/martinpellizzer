Vaccinium perrigidum

Vaccinium perrigidum, commonly known as the smooth bilberry, is a species of shrub native to the northern regions of North America.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes other familiar plants like blueberries and cranberries. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white, followed by edible berries that are similar in appearance to regular blueberries. These berries are rich in antioxidants and are often used in culinary applications.

The plant thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.
