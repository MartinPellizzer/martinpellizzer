Verbesina nayaritensis

Verbesina nayaritensis, commonly known as the Nayarit false sunflower, is a herbaceous plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in open areas such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges and produces small, wind-pollinated flowers.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
