Vicia perretii

Vicia perretii, commonly known as the Perret's vetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to purple. Vicia perretii is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it an important plant in soil enrichment and ecological restoration.
