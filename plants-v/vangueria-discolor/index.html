Vangueria Discolor

Vangueria, scientifically known as Vangueria discolor, is a striking member of the Rubiaceae family native to southern Africa. This shrub is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers and distinctive foliage, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or arid landscapes. Its adaptability to harsh conditions makes it a resilient plant in both natural and cultivated environments. With its unique appearance and ecological importance, Vangueria discolor stands out as a valuable addition to diverse ecosystems.
