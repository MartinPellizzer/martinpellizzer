Vitaliana primuliflora var. nevadensis

Vitaliana primuliflora var. nevadensis, commonly known as the Nevada vitalia, is a rare flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Nevada.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.
