Viburnum erosum var. setchuenense

Viburnum erosum var. setchuenense, commonly known as the Sichuan snowberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, white, clustered flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. The plant has slender, arching branches and dark green, ovate leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sun.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental berries and winter interest.
