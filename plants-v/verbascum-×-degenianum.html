Verbascum × degenianum

Verbascum × degenianum, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a plant belonging to the Scrophulariaceae family.

It is a hybrid between Verbascum blattaria and Verbascum blattellum, resulting in a plant with distinctive fuzzy, silver-green leaves. The plant produces tall spikes of yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It is native to southern Europe and is often found in dry, rocky soils.

Verbascum × degenianum is cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
