Veronica teucrium var. pyrenaica

Veronica teucrium var. pyrenaica, commonly known as the Pyrenean speedwell, is a flowering plant native to the Pyrenees mountain range.

It is a low-growing perennial with slender stems and small, blue to violet flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it thrives in rocky or gravelly soils. It is typically found in alpine and subalpine habitats, where it tolerates cool and moist conditions.

Its botanical features make it an attractive and hardy addition to gardens with suitable conditions.
