Verbena hastata var. paniculata

Verbena hastata var. paniculata, commonly known as blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist or wet environments such as wetlands and prairies. The plant features showy, spike-like clusters of blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
