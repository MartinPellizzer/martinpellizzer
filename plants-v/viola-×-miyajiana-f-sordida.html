Viola × miyajiana f. sordida

Viola × miyajiana f. sordida, commonly known as the dark violet, is a hybrid species within the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its dark purple to almost black petals, which distinguish it from other viola species. The plant typically has heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas or shaded gardens.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly grown for its striking floral display.
