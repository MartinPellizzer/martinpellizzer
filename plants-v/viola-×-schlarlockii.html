Viola × schlarlockii

Viola × schlarlockii, commonly known as the Schlarlock viola, is a hybrid species within the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, violet-colored flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically produces heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in rock gardens and as a bedding plant.
