Vernonia nigritiana

Vernonia nigritiana, commonly known as black ironweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical and southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a distinctive central disk.

Vernonia nigritiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some African cultures.
