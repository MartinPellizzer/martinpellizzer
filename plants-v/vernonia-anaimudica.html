Vernonia anaimudica

Vernonia anaimudica, commonly known as the Anaimudi vernonia, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in moist, shaded areas of tropical forests. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in dense, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are usually elliptical or ovate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Vernonia anaimudica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
