Verbascum × derekolense

Verbascum × derekolense, commonly known as the Turkish mullein.

It is a hybrid species native to Turkey, characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, fuzzy leaves. The plant produces showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or arid environments.

Verbascum × derekolense is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used traditionally in herbal medicine.
