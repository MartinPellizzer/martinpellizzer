Viola boissieuana

Viola boissieuana, commonly known as the Boissieu violet, is a species of flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant violet or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, preferring well-drained soil and full sun exposure. Its flowers bloom in early spring, adding a splash of color to its natural environment.

Viola boissieuana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and hardiness.
