Viburnum chinshanense

Viburnum chinshanense, commonly known as the Chinese snowberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant produces bright red berries in the fall, which are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. Viburnum chinshanense typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
