Vitis jinggangensis

Vitis jinggangensis, commonly known as the Jiugang grape, is a rare and endemic species native to China.

It belongs to the Vitaceae family and is characterized by its vigorous growth and climbing habit. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its berries are usually small and can vary in color from red to black, depending on the variety.

Due to its limited distribution and unique characteristics, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
