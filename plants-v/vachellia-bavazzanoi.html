Vachellia bavazzanoi

Vachellia bavazzanoi, commonly known as the Socotra thorn tree, is a unique flowering plant native to the island of Socotra.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and distinctive, compound leaves. The plant produces yellow or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, adapting to the harsh climate of its native habitat.
