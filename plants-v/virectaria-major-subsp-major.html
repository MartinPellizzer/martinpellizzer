Virectaria major subsp. major

Virectaria major subsp.

major, commonly known as the giant virectaria, is a striking member of the Araceae family. It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in length, often with a deep green color and prominent veins.

The plant produces striking inflorescences consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is native to tropical regions of South America.

Due to its size and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for large indoor spaces and greenhouse cultivation.
