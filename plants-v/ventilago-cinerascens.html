Ventilago cinerascens

Ventilago cinerascens, commonly known as the silverleaf acacia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils. The plant features narrow, silvery-gray leaves that are arranged in pairs along the stems. It produces yellow, fluffy flower clusters that bloom in winter, attracting pollinators like bees and birds.

Its adaptability to harsh environments makes it a resilient and ornamental species in arid landscapes.
