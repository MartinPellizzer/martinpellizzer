Viburnum × hillieri

Viburnum × hillieri, commonly known as the hillieri viburnum, is a hybrid shrub native to New Zealand.

It features compound leaves with serrated edges and produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers in the spring. The plant is characterized by its showy, bright red berries that persist into winter, providing food for birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

Viburnum × hillieri is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
