Vaccinium leptanthum f. ellipticum

Vaccinium leptanthum f. ellipticum, commonly known as the elliptic cranberry bush, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and slightly glossy. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible and often used in culinary applications. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

This form of the species is noted for its ornamental value and adaptability to various garden conditions.
