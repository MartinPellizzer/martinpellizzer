Verbascum friedrichsthalianum

Verbascum friedrichsthalianum, commonly known as the Friedrichsthalianum mullein, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems covered in soft, fuzzy hairs. The plant produces showy, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, often found in rocky or dry habitats.
