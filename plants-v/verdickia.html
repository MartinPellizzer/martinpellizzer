Verdickia

Verdickia, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, often forming a compact rosette of leaves. Its leaves are thick and waxy, which helps it retain moisture in its natural habitat.

Verdickia is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
