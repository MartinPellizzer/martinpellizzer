Vallota purpurea

Vallota purpurea, commonly known as the purple vallota, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, purple flowers that bloom in late spring. Vallota purpurea is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in gardens for its delicate appearance.
