Vilfa stolonifera var. marina

Vilfa stolonifera var. marina, commonly known as sea blite, is a halophytic plant adapted to coastal environments.

It thrives in saline soils and is often found in tidal zones and sandy beaches. The plant has a prostrate growth habit with fleshy, succulent leaves that store water. Its small, greenish-white flowers are typically clustered in dense inflorescences.

It plays an important role in stabilizing coastal dunes and preventing erosion.
