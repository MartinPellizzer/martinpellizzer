Vignea ovalis

Vignea ovalis, commonly known as the oval sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking, spiny, egg-shaped seed pods that contain small, dark seeds. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and rocky soils, thriving in well-drained conditions. Its flowers are usually blue or violet, adding a vibrant touch to its saline environment.

Vignea ovalis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and hardy nature.
