Verbascum weldenii

Verbascum weldenii, commonly known as the Turkish mullein, is a flowering plant native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Scrophulariaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and soft, woolly leaves. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, often found in rocky or arid environments.
