Verbascum iconium

Verbascum iconium, commonly known as the Iconium mullein, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem covered in soft, velvety hairs. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are large, lance-shaped, and also covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Verbascum iconium is often found in dry, rocky soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
