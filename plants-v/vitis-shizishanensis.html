Vitis shizishanensis

Vitis shizishanensis, commonly known as the Shizishan grapevine, is a rare and endemic species native to China.

It belongs to the Vitaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and woody vines. The plant produces small, clustered berries that are typically dark purple in color. Its leaves are heart-shaped with serrated edges, and it thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable species for botanical research and conservation efforts.
