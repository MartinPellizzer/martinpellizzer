Vitex vermoesenii

Vitex vermoesenii, commonly known as the African blueberry or blue vitex, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces small, fragrant, blue or purple flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a rough texture with serrated edges.

The fruit is a small, round, woody capsule that contains several seeds.
