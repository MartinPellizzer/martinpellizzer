Vernonia schlechtendaliana

Vernonia schlechtendaliana, commonly known as the Schlechtendaliana ironweed, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stem and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces rough, bract-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Vernonia schlechtendaliana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, attracting various pollinators.
