Vavaea retusa

Vavaea retusa, commonly known as the smooth vavaea, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and glossy in texture. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense inflorescences, and are usually pink or purple in color.

Vavaea retusa is often found in rocky soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments.
