Velarum cavanillesianum

Velarum cavanillesianum, commonly known as the Cavanillesian velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, velvety hairs. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. Its growth habit is herbaceous, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Velarum cavanillesianum is often found in wetlands and along riverbanks, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
