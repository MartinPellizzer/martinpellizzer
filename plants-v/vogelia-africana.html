Vogelia africana

Vogelia africana, commonly known as the African wild indigo, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its woody stems and small, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the warmer months. Vogelia africana is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it resilient to drought conditions.

It is also valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
