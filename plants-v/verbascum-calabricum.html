Verbascum calabricum

Verbascum calabricum, commonly known as the Sicilian mullein, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, fuzzy leaves. The plant produces showy yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.
