Vanda jennae

Vanda jennae, commonly known as the Jennae vanda, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, long-lasting flowers that are typically violet or blue in color. The plant has thick, fleshy roots adapted for epiphytic growth on trees or rocks. Its leaves are broad and leathery, providing structural support and aiding in water retention.

Vanda jennae thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.
