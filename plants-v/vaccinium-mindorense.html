Vaccinium mindorense

Vaccinium mindorense, commonly known as the Mindanao huckleberry, is a species of flowering plant in the Ericaceae family.

It is native to the Philippines, particularly found on the island of Mindanao. This evergreen shrub typically grows in moist, acidic soils within montane forests. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually pink or white in color.

Its berries, which are edible, are often used in local cuisine and traditional medicine.
