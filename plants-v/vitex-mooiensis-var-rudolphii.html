Vitex mooiensis var. rudolphii

Vitex mooiensis var. rudolphii, commonly known as the Rudolph's vitex, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its small, opposite leaves and clusters of fragrant, blue or purple flowers. The plant produces small, oval fruits that contain seeds dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and savannah habitats.
