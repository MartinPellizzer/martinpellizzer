Vasargia caracasana

Vasargia caracasana, commonly known as the Caracas beauty, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Venezuela.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
