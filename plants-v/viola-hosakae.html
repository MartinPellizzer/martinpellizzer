Viola hosakae

Viola hosakae, commonly known as the Japanese violet, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, heart-shaped leaves and violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its flowers bloom in early spring, adding a splash of color to its natural habitat.

Viola hosakae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens.
