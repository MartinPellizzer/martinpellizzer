Viola calcarata var. longipes

Viola calcarata var. longipes, commonly known as the long-stemmed pansy, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive wavy margin and produces vibrant violet-blue flowers with a yellow center. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders. Its flowers bloom in early spring, adding a splash of color to gardens.

The variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
