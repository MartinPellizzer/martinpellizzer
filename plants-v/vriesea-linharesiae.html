Vriesea linharesiae

Vriesea linharesiae, commonly known as the Orange Sword, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange and red bracts that surround a central spadix, creating a visually appealing display. The plant features broad, strap-like leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Vriesea linharesiae is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
