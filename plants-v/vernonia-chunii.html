Vernonia chunii

Vernonia chunii, commonly known as the Chinese ironweed, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Vernonia chunii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
