Vriesea xiphion

Vriesea xiphion, commonly known as the sword fern, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, narrow leaves that resemble a sword, giving it its common name. The plant produces striking inflorescences with brightly colored flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Vriesea xiphion is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
