Victorinia acrandra

Victorinia acrandra, commonly known as the African starfruit or yellow starfruit, is a small evergreen shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are often clustered at the ends of the branches. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.
