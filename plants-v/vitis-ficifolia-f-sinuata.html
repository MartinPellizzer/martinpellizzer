Vitis ficifolia f. sinuata

Vitis ficifolia f. sinuata, commonly known as the sinuous grapevine, is a unique cultivar of the grapevine species Vitis ficifolia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, sinuous leaf patterns and vigorous growth habit. The plant is native to China and is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage. It prefers full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it adaptable to a variety of garden settings.

This cultivar is also valued for its potential in horticultural research and its role in traditional Chinese medicine.
