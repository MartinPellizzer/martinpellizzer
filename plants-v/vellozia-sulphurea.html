Vellozia sulphurea

Vellozia sulphurea, commonly known as the golden vellozia, is a striking plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, spiral-arranged leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, yellowish hairs.
