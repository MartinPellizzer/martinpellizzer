Verbena phlogiflora var. macilenta

Verbena phlogiflora var. macilenta, commonly known as the slender verbena, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to violet. The plant produces small, oval leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its prolonged blooming period, which can last from late spring through early autumn.
