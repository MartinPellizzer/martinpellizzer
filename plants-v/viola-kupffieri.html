Viola kupffieri

Viola kupffieri, commonly known as the Kupffier violet, is a species of flowering plant in the Violaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central America, particularly in countries like Guatemala and Honduras. The plant features heart-shaped, velvety leaves and produces striking violet or purple flowers with a distinctive yellowish throat. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Viola kupffieri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
