Viguieranthus megalophyllus

Viguieranthus megalophyllus, commonly known as the giant leaf vine, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
