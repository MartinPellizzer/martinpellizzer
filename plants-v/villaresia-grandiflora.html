Villaresia grandiflora

Villaresia grandiflora, commonly known as the Brazilian orchid or the large-flowered orchid, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, large, showy flowers that typically bloom in clusters. The plant has a complex root system and thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually long and narrow, adapted for efficient water retention.

It plays an important role in the ecological balance of its native habitat.
