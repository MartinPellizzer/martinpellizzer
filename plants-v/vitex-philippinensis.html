Vitex philippinensis

Vitex philippinensis, commonly known as the Philippine chaste tree, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height. The plant produces fragrant, violet-blue flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly serrated margin.

The fruit is a dry, woody capsule that contains several small, flat seeds.
