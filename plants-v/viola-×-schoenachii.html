Viola × schoenachii

Viola × schoenachii, commonly known as the hybrid violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Violaceae family.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Viola tricolor and Viola × wittrockiana, combining traits from both parents. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces showy, five-petaled flowers in shades of purple, violet, and white. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.

Viola × schoenachii is valued for its resilience and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
