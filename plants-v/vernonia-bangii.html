Vernonia bangii

Vernonia bangii, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Vernonia bangii thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry savannas and woodland areas.
