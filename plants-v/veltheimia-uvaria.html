Veltheimia uvaria

Veltheimia uvaria, commonly known as the African lily, is a bulbous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features strap-like, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. The plant produces striking, showy inflorescences of white or pale pink flowers with a distinctive, lantern-like shape. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Veltheimia uvaria is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and is relatively easy to care for.
