Vitex pyramidata

Vitex pyramidata, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, pyramidal shape and its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain seeds, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Vitex pyramidata thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for gardens in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it a popular choice for landscaping and erosion control.
