Valeriana astrephioides

Valeriana astrephioides, commonly known as the star-leaved valerian, is a herbaceous plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in shades of white or pale pink.

Valeriana astrephioides is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported calming effects.
