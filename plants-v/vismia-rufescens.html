Vismia rufescens

Vismia rufescens, commonly known as the red-stemmed hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, reddish-brown stems and large, showy flowers that range from yellow to red in color. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Vismia rufescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
