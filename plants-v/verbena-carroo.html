Verbena carroo

Verbena carroo, commonly known as the common verbena, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces spikes of vibrant blue or violet flowers. The plant has slender, branching stems and small, opposite leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Verbena carroo is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
