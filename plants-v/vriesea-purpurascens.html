Vriesea purpurascens

Vriesea purpurascens, commonly known as the flaming sword plant, is a striking bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, sword-like leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from deep red to orange. The plant produces vibrant, long-lasting inflorescences that can last for several months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Vriesea purpurascens is popular as a decorative plant due to its bold appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
