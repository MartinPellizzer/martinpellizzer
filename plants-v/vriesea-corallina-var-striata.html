Vriesea corallina var. striata

Vriesea corallina var. striata, commonly known as the Striped Flameleaf Bromeliad, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage with distinctive red and orange streaks. The plant features a rosette of narrow, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It produces tall, branching inflorescences with showy, long-lasting flowers that range from yellow to orange.

This variety is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its adaptability and ornamental appeal.
