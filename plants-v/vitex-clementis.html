Vitex clementis

Vitex clementis, commonly known as the Chinese vitex or fragrant vitex, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern China.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, fragrant white or pale purple flowers in clusters, which bloom in summer. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.

Vitex clementis prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for coastal gardens.
