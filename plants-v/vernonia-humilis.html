Vernonia humilis

Vernonia humilis, commonly known as the small ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant produces small, yellow to orange flowers arranged in dense, cylindrical clusters. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, often with a grayish or silver appearance.

Vernonia humilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in arid conditions.
