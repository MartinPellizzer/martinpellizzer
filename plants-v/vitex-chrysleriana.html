Vitex chrysleriana

Vitex chrysleriana, commonly known as Chrysler's vitex, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that are typically purple or blue in color. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly serrated edge.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable ornamental plant in tropical and subtropical regions.
