Vilfa funckii

Vilfa funckii, commonly known as the Cape mountain sandthorn, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and its ability to thrive in arid and rocky environments. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, red fruits that are edible and contain a single seed.

Vilfa funckii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
