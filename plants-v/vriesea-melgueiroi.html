Vriesea melgueiroi

Vriesea melgueiroi, commonly known as the Melgueiro's Vriesea, is a species of bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful inflorescence that can range from red to orange, often with a yellow or green center. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a central cup to collect water. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Vriesea melgueiroi is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in indoor settings.
