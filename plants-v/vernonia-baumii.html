Vernonia baumii

Vernonia baumii, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant is known for its hard, heavy wood, which is resistant to decay and used in construction and furniture. Vernonia baumii thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland and savanna habitats.

It plays an important ecological role by providing food and shelter for various wildlife species.
