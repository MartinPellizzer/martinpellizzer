Vitex amaniensis

Vitex amaniensis, commonly known as the Amani chaste tree, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows in moist, well-drained soils. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually violet or white in color. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Vitex amaniensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional medicine.
