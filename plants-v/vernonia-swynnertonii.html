Vernonia swynnertonii

Vernonia swynnertonii, commonly known as the Swynnerton's ironweed, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a rough texture, and it is known for its resilience in arid conditions.

Vernonia swynnertonii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ecological benefits to local wildlife.
