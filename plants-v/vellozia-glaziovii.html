Vellozia glaziovii

Vellozia glaziovii, commonly known as the Glaziov's vellozia, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its unique, spirally arranged leaves that form dense, rosette-like structures. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and orange, which bloom in late summer. Vellozia glaziovii is adapted to arid environments, with a deep root system to access water.

It is often found in rocky habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
