Vriesea fabioi

Vriesea fabioi, commonly known as the Rainbow Vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful inflorescence that displays a gradient of red, orange, and yellow hues. The plant features a rosette of broad, waxy leaves that are typically a deep green with a silvery underside. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Vriesea fabioi is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
