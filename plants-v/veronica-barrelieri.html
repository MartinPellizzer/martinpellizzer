Veronica barrelieri

Veronica barrelieri, commonly known as the Barrelier's speedwell, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant produces clusters of small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its leaves are narrow, grass-like, and covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
