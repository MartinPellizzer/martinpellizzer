Veronica kiusiana subsp. maritima

Veronica kiusiana subsp.

maritima, commonly known as sea veronica, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of southern Africa. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of blue or purple flowers.

The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are adapted to withstand salty sea air and strong coastal winds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dune environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for coastal gardens and landscaping.
