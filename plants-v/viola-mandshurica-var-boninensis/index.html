Viola Mandshurica Var. Boninensis

Viola mandshurica var. boninensis is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the Bonin Islands. It belongs to the Violaceae family, known for its vibrant and often showy blooms. This variant is distinguished by its unique leaf shape and pale lavender flowers that bloom in early spring. Despite its beauty, it is critically endangered due to habitat loss and limited distribution. Conservation efforts are essential to preserve this botanical gem for future generations.
