Verbascum prunellii

Verbascum prunellii, commonly known as common mullein, is a herbaceous plant native to Europe, western Asia, and North Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems covered in soft, velvety hairs that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in dry, disturbed soils and is often found along roadsides and in open fields.

Verbascum prunellii is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally for its soothing effects.
