Vandopsis lissochiloides

Vandopsis lissochiloides, commonly known as the smooth-leafed vandopsis, is a rare and enigmatic orchid species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and glabrous. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, often arranged in a loose raceme. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific conditions to flourish.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
