Verbesina Virginica Var. Laciniata

Verbesina virginica var. laciniata is a striking perennial plant native to the eastern united states. it is known for its vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall. the plant features deeply lobed leaves that give it a distinctive, almost fern-like appearance. it thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for native gardens. this variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological benefits to local wildlife.
