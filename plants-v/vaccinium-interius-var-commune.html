Vaccinium interius var. commune

Vaccinium interius var. commune, commonly known as the common blueberry shrub, is a species native to the northern regions of North America.

It is a low-growing evergreen shrub that typically reaches heights of 1 to 3 feet. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, which develop into the familiar blue or purple berries. Its leaves are oval-shaped, leathery, and have a slightly waxy texture.

This plant thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.
