Vancouveria vaseyi

Vancouveria vaseyi, commonly known as the Vancouveria or the Pacific silverweed, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant features narrow, silver-green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs. It produces small, white to pale yellow flowers in late spring to early summer.

Vancouveria vaseyi thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forests and streamside areas.
