Veronicastrum sibiricum f. album

Veronicastrum sibiricum f. album, commonly known as the white giant hyssop, is a herbaceous perennial native to Siberia.

It features tall, slender stems that can grow up to 2 meters in height, adorned with spikes of white, tubular flowers. The plant has lance-shaped, gray-green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, moist soil.

Its botanical features make it a striking addition to gardens and a valuable plant for pollinators.
