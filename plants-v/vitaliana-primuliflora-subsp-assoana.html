Vitaliana primuliflora subsp. assoana

Vitaliana primuliflora subsp.

assoana, commonly known as the Assam primrose, is a flowering plant native to the eastern Himalayas. It is characterized by its attractive, showy flowers that typically bloom in spring.

The plant features lance-shaped leaves and grows in moist, shaded environments. It is often found in montane forests and is valued for its ornamental qualities.

Botanically, it belongs to the family Primulaceae and is notable for its medicinal uses in traditional medicine.
