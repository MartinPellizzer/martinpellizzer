Vella spinosa

Vella spinosa, commonly known as the spiny velvet plant, is a flowering shrub native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant has thorny stems and leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Vella spinosa is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
