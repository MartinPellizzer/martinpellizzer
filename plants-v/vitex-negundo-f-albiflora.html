Vitex negundo f. albiflora

Vitex negundo f. albiflora, commonly known as the whiteflower vitex, is a variant of the common vitex.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Asia, characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and showy white or pale yellow flowers. The flowers are clustered in panicles and bloom in late spring to early summer. This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to a variety of soil types.

It is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
