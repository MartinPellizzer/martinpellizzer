Viguiera cornifolia

Viguiera cornifolia, commonly known as the cutleaf coneflower, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and showy blooms. Viguiera cornifolia is typically found in dry, open habitats like prairies and woodlands.
