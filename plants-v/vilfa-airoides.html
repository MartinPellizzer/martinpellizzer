Vilfa airoides

Vilfa airoides, commonly known as the golden arrowhead, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and water features.
