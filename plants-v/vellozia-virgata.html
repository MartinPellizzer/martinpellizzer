Vellozia virgata

Vellozia virgata, commonly known as the feathered vellozia, is a unique species native to South Africa.

It is a succulent plant characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are covered in dense, white, woolly hairs. The plant produces striking, red-orange flowers that are clustered in a cylindrical inflorescence. Vellozia virgata is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its fleshy leaves.

It is also notable for its slow growth and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
