Vallisneria gigantea var. biwaensis

Vallisneria gigantea var. biwaensis, commonly known as the Japanese Vallisneria, is a species of aquatic plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its long, ribbon-like leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. This plant typically thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments. It is often found in lakes, ponds, and slow rivers, where it forms dense underwater vegetation.

The plant plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and oxygen for various aquatic organisms.
