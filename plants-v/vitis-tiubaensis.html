Vitis tiubaensis

Vitis tiubaensis, commonly known as the Tiuba grapevine, is a species of flowering vine native to China.

It belongs to the Vitaceae family and is characterized by its tendrils and compound leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into berries, which are typically dark purple when mature. Vitis tiubaensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been studied for its potential in horticulture and viticulture.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for plant taxonomists and gardeners alike.
