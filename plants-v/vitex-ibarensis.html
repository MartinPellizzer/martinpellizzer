Vitex ibarensis

Vitex ibarensis, commonly known as the Ibaran chaste tree, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its slender, branching stems and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces fragrant, violet-blue flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the rainy season. Its fruits are small, dry capsules that contain numerous seeds.
