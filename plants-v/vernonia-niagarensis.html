Vernonia niagarensis

Vernonia niagarensis, commonly known as the African ironweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Vernonia niagarensis is often found in grasslands and along riverbanks, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
