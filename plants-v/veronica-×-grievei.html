Veronica × grievei

Veronica × grievei, commonly known as the Grieve's speedwell, is a hybrid plant in the Plantaginaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of blue to purple flowers. The plant typically thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Veronica × grievei is known for its hardiness and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
