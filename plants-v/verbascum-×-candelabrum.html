Verbascum × candelabrum

Verbascum × candelabrum, commonly known as the candlestick mullein, is a hybrid plant in the genus Verbascum, native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stems that bear large, velvety leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry environments. Its common name derives from the candle-like appearance of its flower cluster.

Verbascum × candelabrum is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
