Vincetoxicum roseum

Vincetoxicum roseum, commonly known as the rose-flowered milkweed, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern North America.

It features clusters of small, pink to rose-colored flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its stems are typically reddish and become woody with age.

Vincetoxicum roseum is often found in moist woodlands and along stream banks, where it thrives in partial to full shade.
