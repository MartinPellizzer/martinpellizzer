Vereia heterophylla

Vereia heterophylla, commonly known as the variable leaf vereia, is a strikingly beautiful plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its unique foliage, which features leaves that vary in shape and size depending on environmental conditions. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are small, tubular, and often display a range of colors from purple to pink, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Vereia heterophylla is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family and is prized for its resilience and adaptability in cultivation.
