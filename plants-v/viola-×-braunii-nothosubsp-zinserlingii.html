Viola × braunii nothosubsp.

zinserlingii, commonly known as the Zinserling's violet, is a hybrid species within the Violaceae family. It is characterized by its heart-shaped, velvety leaves and clusters of violet-blue flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It has a compact growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
