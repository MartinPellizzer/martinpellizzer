Vernonia lankana

Vernonia lankana, commonly known as the Lankan ironweed, is a flowering plant native to Sri Lanka.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and forest clearings. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Vernonia lankana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
