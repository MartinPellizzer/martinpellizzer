Viola wulingensis

Viola wulingensis, commonly known as the Wuling violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its flowers bloom in spring, producing a range of colors from pale purple to deep violet.

Viola wulingensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
