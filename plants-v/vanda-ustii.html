Vanda ustii

Vanda ustii, commonly known as the golden vanda, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, slender pseudobulbs and broad, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of white to pale green hairs. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and white, often arranged in racemes. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Vanda ustii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
