Veronica linearifolia

Veronica linearifolia, commonly known as the slender speedwell, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Veronica linearifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
