Vanillosma rufula

Vanillosma rufula, commonly known as the rough leaved rock rose, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. The plant features gray-green, oval-shaped leaves with a rough texture, and its flowers are usually pink or purple, appearing in clusters. It is drought-tolerant and well-suited for xeriscaping.

Vanillosma rufula is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
