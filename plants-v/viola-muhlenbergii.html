Viola muhlenbergii

Viola muhlenbergii, commonly known as the Muhlenberg's violet, is a perennial flowering plant native to North America.

It typically grows in woodland habitats and thrives in partial to full shade. The plant produces showy violet-blue flowers with yellow markings, which bloom in early spring. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Viola muhlenbergii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and ecological benefits.
