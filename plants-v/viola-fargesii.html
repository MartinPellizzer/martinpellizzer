Viola fargesii

Viola fargesii, commonly known as the Chinese violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and delicate, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or mountainous regions. Its flowers bloom in early spring, and the plant is noted for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.

Viola fargesii is also valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
