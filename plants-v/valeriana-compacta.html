Valeriana compacta

Valeriana compacta, commonly known as sweet valerian, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, often fragrant, flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has a dense, compact growth habit with small, dark green leaves. Its roots are used in traditional medicine for their sedative and calming properties.

Valeriana compacta thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.
