Vriesea reticulata

Vriesea reticulata, commonly known as the Rainbow Vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, spiky inflorescence that displays a range of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that collect water, forming a central pool. Its foliage is often variegated with silver and green streaks, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Vriesea reticulata thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
