Verbascum symes

Verbascum symes, commonly known as Symes' mullein, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae and is characterized by its tall, hairy stems and large, fuzzy leaves. The plant produces showy yellow or orange flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in alpine environments.
