Veronica Carminea

Veronica carminea is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to southern Africa. Known for its vibrant red blooms and delicate foliage, it adds a pop of color to any garden. This species belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a favorite among horticulturists. Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a standout choice for both novice and experienced gardeners alike.
