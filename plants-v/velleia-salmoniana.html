Velleia salmoniana

Velleia salmoniana, commonly known as the salmon leaf velleia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its small, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white flowers that bloom in the spring and summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it a suitable ground cover in woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value lies in its attractive foliage and subtle floral display.
