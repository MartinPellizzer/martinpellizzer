Verbascum wraberi

Verbascum wraberi, commonly known as the Wraber's mullein, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the genus Verbascum, which includes several species of flowering plants commonly referred to as mulleins. The plant features large, fuzzy leaves and showy yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in rocky or arid environments.

Verbascum wraberi is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used historically for its medicinal properties.
