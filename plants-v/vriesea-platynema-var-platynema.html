Vriesea platynema var. platynema

Vriesea platynema var. platynema, commonly known as the sword fern bromeliad, is a striking tropical plant native to Brazil.

It features a rosette of narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are often dark green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces striking inflorescences with vibrant red bracts and yellow flowers, which bloom in the summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

This species is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
