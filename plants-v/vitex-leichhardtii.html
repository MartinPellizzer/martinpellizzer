Vitex leichhardtii

Vitex leichhardtii, commonly known as the mountain vitex, is a shrub or small tree native to Australia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, opposite leaves that are usually grey-green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of purple or white, which bloom in spring. Its fruit is a dry, woody capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

Vitex leichhardtii is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, temperate climates.
