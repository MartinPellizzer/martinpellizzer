Verbascum × karadagense

Verbascum × karadagense, commonly known as the Karadag mullein.

It is a hybrid plant belonging to the genus Verbascum, which includes species native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa. This particular species is characterized by its tall, hairy stems and large, fuzzy leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces showy, yellowish-orange flowers in clusters, typically blooming in late spring to early summer.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.
