Veronica longifolia var. ovata

Veronica longifolia var. ovata, commonly known as the ovate veronica, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, clustered blue or purple flowers. The leaves are ovate, hence the varietal name, and are typically arranged oppositely along the stem. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or borders.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
