Viviania elegans

Viviania elegans, commonly known as the elegant water primrose, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and South America.

It is characterized by its bright green, waxy leaves and showy, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in wetland environments and is often found in shallow water or moist soil. It has a compact growth habit and is popular in aquatic gardens.

Viviania elegans is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
