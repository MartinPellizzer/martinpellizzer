Vicia perelegans

Vicia perelegans, commonly known as the slender vetch, is a flowering plant in the legume family Fabaceae.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grassy areas and along woodland edges. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in clusters. Its seeds are enclosed in pods and are often used as forage for livestock.

Vicia perelegans is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, contributing to soil fertility.
