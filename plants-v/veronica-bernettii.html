Veronica bernettii

Veronica bernettii, commonly known as the Bennett's speedwell, is a flowering plant native to the British Isles.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features slender, upright stems covered in small, opposite leaves that are usually hairy. Its flowers are blue or violet, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems, and bloom from June to August.

Veronica bernettii thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream sides.
