Veronica chingii

Veronica chingii, commonly known as the Chinese speedwell, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the plantain family, Plantaginaceae, and is characterized by its small, blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in temperate climates. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Veronica chingii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
