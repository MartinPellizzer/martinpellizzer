Verbascum hypoleucum

Verbascum hypoleucum, commonly known as the white mullein.

This plant is a member of the Scrophulariaceae family and is native to southern Europe and North Africa. It is characterized by its tall, hairy stems and large, fuzzy leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in height. The flowers are typically yellow or orange, blooming in late summer.

It thrives in dry, sunny environments and is often found in rocky or disturbed soils.
