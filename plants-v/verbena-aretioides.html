Verbena aretioides

Verbena aretioides, commonly known as the silver verbena, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and clusters of small, blue or violet flowers. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping. It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Verbena aretioides prefers full sun and is commonly used in rock gardens and borders.
