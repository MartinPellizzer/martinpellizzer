Verbascum moenchii

Verbascum moenchii, commonly known as the yellow mullein, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, hairy stems and large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in soft, velvety hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, often growing in rocky or dry habitats.

Verbascum moenchii is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
