Verbesina salvadorensis

Verbesina salvadorensis, commonly known as the Salvadoran ironweed, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and along riverbanks.
