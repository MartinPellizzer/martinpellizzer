Vochysia retusa

Vochysia retusa, commonly known as the velvet tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark green bark and large, palmately lobed leaves. The plant produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that are typically clustered in panicles. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

Vochysia retusa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm, humid climates.
