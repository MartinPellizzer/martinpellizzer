Valeriana hieronymi, commonly known as the Hieronymus valerian, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and opposite leaves. Valeriana hieronymi thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.
