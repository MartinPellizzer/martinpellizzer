Vinca bottae

Vinca bottae, commonly known as the Madagascar vining plant, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a glossy texture and is often used in hanging baskets or as a ground cover. It thrives in well-draining soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Vinca bottae is known for its resilience and ability to bloom continuously throughout the growing season.
