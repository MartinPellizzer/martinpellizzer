Verbesina scotiodonta

Verbesina scotiodonta, commonly known as the scarlet globemallow, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features showy, bright orange to red flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are palmately lobed and covered with fine hairs, and it produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

Verbesina scotiodonta is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to harsh conditions.
