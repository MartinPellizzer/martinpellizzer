Valeriana himachalensis

Valeriana himachalensis, commonly known as Himachal valerian, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan region of India.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and opposite, ovate leaves. Valeriana himachalensis is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
