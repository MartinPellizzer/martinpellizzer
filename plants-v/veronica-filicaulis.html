Veronica filicaulis

Veronica filicaulis, commonly known as the leafy speedwell, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of blue or violet flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Veronica filicaulis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
