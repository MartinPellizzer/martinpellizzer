Viburnum veitchii subsp. magnificum

Viburnum veitchii subsp.

magnificum, commonly known as the Chinese snowball, is a deciduous shrub native to China. It features clusters of white, fragrant flowers that bloom in early spring, creating a striking visual display.

The plant has simple, opposite leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its showy flowers and attractive, rounded form.
