Vitex medusaecalyx

Vitex medusaecalyx, commonly known as the Medusa's calyx hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and produces clusters of tubular, violet or purple flowers. The species is also noted for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in full sun.
