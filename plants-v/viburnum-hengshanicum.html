Viburnum hengshanicum

Viburnum hengshanicum, commonly known as the Hengshan viburnum, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features compound leaves with serrated edges and clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant produces bright red berries in the fall, which are an important food source for birds and wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for ornamental gardening.

Its adaptability and seasonal display make it a valuable addition to native plant landscapes.
