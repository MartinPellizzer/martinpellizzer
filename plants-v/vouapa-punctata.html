Vouapa punctata

Vouapa punctata, commonly known as the spotted lily, is a striking tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically feature a white or pale pink color with distinctive dark spots. The plant has strap-like, narrow leaves that are often a deep green with a waxy texture. Vouapa punctata thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in warm climates.
