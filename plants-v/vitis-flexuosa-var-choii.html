Vitis flexuosa var. choii

Vitis flexuosa var. choii, commonly known as the Choii grapevine, is a woody vine native to eastern Asia, particularly China.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and distinctive, often twisted growth pattern. The leaves are typically lobed and have a deep green color, while the flowers are small and greenish-white. Its berries are usually small, round, and can range in color from green to dark purple.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
