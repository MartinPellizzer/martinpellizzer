Veronica Spicata F. Nitida

Veronica spicata f. nitida is a striking variety of the common speedwell known for its elegant, silver-blue foliage. This compact plant features dense clusters of tiny, blue-violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade, making it a great addition to woodland gardens. Its ornamental value comes from both its foliage and its delicate floral display. This cultivar is prized for its ability to add a cool, fresh look to garden landscapes.
