Veronica assoana

Veronica assoana, commonly known as the Alpine speedwell, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 20 centimeters. The plant features slender, branched stems covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a rough texture. Its flowers are small, blue or purple, and arranged in dense clusters at the tips of the stems.

Veronica assoana thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky areas.
