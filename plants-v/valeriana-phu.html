Valeriana phu

Valeriana phu, commonly known as mountain valerian, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests and stream sides. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with a slightly hairy texture.

Valeriana phu is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly for its calming effects.
