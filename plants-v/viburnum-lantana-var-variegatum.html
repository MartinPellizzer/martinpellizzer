Viburnum lantana var. variegatum

Viburnum lantana var. variegatum, commonly known as variegated lantana, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It features showy, clustered white or pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a distinctive variegated pattern of white and green. It is often grown for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Viburnum lantana var. variegatum prefers full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil.
