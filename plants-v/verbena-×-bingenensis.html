Verbena × bingenensis

Verbena × bingenensis, commonly known as the common verbena, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Verbenaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and profuse displays of blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and features opposite, narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Verbena × bingenensis is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
