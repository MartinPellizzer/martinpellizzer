Vitis × kozmae

Vitis × kozmae, commonly known as the Kozma muscat grape, is a hybrid vine species native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and large, aromatic berries that range in color from green to deep purple. The plant is well adapted to temperate climates and thrives in well-drained soil. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped with a glossy surface, and it produces clusters of small, sweet fruits.

Vitis × kozmae is often cultivated for its unique flavor profile and is used in both winemaking and culinary applications.
