Volkameria tomentosa

Volkameria tomentosa, commonly known as the woolly volkameria, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy foliage and showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are ovate with a velvety texture, and the flowers emit a mild fragrance.

Volkameria tomentosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
