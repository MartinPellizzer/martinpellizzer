Veronica teucrium var. thracica

Veronica teucrium var. thracica, commonly known as the Thrace speedwell, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Europe.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, blue to purple flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassy areas and rocky habitats.
