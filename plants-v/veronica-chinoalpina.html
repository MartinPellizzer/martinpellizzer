Veronica chinoalpina

Veronica chinoalpina, commonly known as the alpine speedwell, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its small, blue or purple flowers that bloom in early summer. Veronica chinoalpina is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in cooler climates.
