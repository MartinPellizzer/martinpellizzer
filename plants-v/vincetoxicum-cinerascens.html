Vincetoxicum cinerascens

Vincetoxicum cinerascens, commonly known as the smokebush, is a deciduous shrub native to parts of Asia.

The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in clusters, which are followed by red, fuzzy berries that persist into winter. Vincetoxicum cinerascens thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage.
