Viola × ekstroemiana

Viola × ekstroemiana, commonly known as the hybrid violet, is a popular ornamental plant valued for its vibrant, showy flowers.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Viola tricolor and Viola wittrockii, combining the best traits of both parents. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and flowers in shades of purple, violet, and white. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.

Viola × ekstroemiana is relatively easy to grow and can be propagated through seeds or division.
