Vrydagzynea triloba

Vrydagzynea triloba, commonly known as the three-lobed cycad, is a unique cycad species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, fan-shaped leaves that are divided into three lobes, giving it an ornamental appearance. The plant produces large, cone-like structures that contain both male and female reproductive organs. Its slow growth rate and long lifespan make it a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is classified as endangered, highlighting the need for conservation efforts.
