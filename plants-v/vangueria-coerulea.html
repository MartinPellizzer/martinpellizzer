Vangueria coerulea

Vangueria coerulea, commonly known as the blue vangueria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, clustered blue or purple flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Vangueria coerulea is well adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
