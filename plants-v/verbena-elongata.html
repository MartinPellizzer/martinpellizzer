Verbena elongata

Verbena elongata, commonly known as slender verbena, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs.

Verbena elongata is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
