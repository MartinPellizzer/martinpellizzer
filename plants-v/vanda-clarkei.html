Vanda clarkei

Vanda clarkei, commonly known as Clarke's vanda, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It thrives in tropical climates and is characterized by its long, pendant-like inflorescences. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the air, and it requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Vanda clarkei is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
