Valeriana nokozanensis

Valeriana nokozanensis, commonly known as the Japanese valerian, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, small flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, heart-shaped, and often have a velvety texture.
