Vriesea glauca

Vriesea glauca, commonly known as the blue vriesea, is a species of flowering plant in the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its striking blue-green foliage and showy inflorescence. The plant features a rosette of narrow, pointed leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It produces a branched flower spike with bright yellow flowers that bloom in the summer.

Vriesea glauca is a popular ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
