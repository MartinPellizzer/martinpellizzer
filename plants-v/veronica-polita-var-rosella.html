Veronica polita var. rosella

Veronica polita var. rosella, commonly known as the Rosella speedwell, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, sky-blue to violet flowers. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a soft texture. This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its attractive foliage and blooms.

It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.
