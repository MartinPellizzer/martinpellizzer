Vachellia Pachyphloia

Vachellia pachyphloia is a resilient shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa and the Middle East. It thrives in harsh environments with limited water and nutrient availability. The plant is known for its thick, waxy leaves that help reduce water loss. It plays a vital role in preventing soil erosion and supporting local wildlife. Its unique adaptations make it an important species in ecological restoration efforts.
