Vesquella

Vesquella, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a species native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which helps reduce water loss through reflection. The plant has small, narrow leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of wax. Vesquella produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months.

It is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
