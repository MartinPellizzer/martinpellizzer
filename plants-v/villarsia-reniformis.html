Villarsia reniformis

Villarsia reniformis, commonly known as the kidney-leaf vine, is a tropical plant native to regions of South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are often green with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing along riverbanks or in dense forests.

Its unique leaf shape and climbing habit make it a distinctive addition to botanical collections.
