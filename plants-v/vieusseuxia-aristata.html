Vieusseuxia aristata

Vieusseuxia aristata, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

This plant is a herbaceous species native to tropical and subtropical regions. It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety texture. The flowers are small and greenish-white, often clustered in spikes.

It thrives in moist, fertile soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and gardens.
