Vitex caribaea

Vitex caribaea, commonly known as the Caribbean vitex or beach vitex, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows along coastal areas and in sandy soils. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually purple or blue in color, arranged in clusters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a rough texture.

Vitex caribaea is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
