Valeriana tafiensis

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, often violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in cool, humid environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a distinctive, strongly scented aroma.
