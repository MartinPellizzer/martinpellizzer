Veltheimia bracteata

Veltheimia bracteata, commonly known as the African lily, is a bulbous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features grass-like, strap-shaped leaves that are typically green with a bluish tint. The plant produces striking, sword-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, often with a distinctive red or purple spathe. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Veltheimia bracteata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and ease of cultivation.
