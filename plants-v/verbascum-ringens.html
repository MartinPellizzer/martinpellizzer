Verbascum ringens

Verbascum ringens, commonly known as the silky mullein, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems covered in soft, silvery-white hairs. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are large, broad, and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Verbascum ringens is often found in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to thrive in arid environments.
