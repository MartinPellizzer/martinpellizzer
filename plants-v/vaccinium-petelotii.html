Vaccinium petelotii

Vaccinium petelotii, commonly known as the Petelot's blueberry, is a species of evergreen shrub native to the Andes mountains in South America.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes other familiar plants like blueberries and cranberries. The plant produces small, edible berries that are typically dark blue or black in color. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and requires cool, moist climates.
