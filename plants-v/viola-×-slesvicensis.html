Viola × slesvicensis

Viola × slesvicensis, commonly known as the Slesvig violet, is a hybrid species within the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant is native to Europe and is often found in woodland habitats. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade.

Viola × slesvicensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
