Vicia unijuga var. waldeniana

Vicia unijuga var. waldeniana, commonly known as the Walden's vetch, is a rare and specialized legume species native to specific regions of Europe.

The plant possesses trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets, typical of the Fabaceae family. It thrives in calcareous soils and is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.
