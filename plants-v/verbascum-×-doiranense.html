Verbascum × doiranense

Verbascum × doiranense, commonly known as the Doiran mullein.

It is a hybrid plant belonging to the genus Verbascum, which includes species commonly referred to as mulleins. This plant is native to the Balkan region, particularly around the Lake Doiran area in Greece and North Macedonia. It is characterized by its tall, hairy stems and large, velvety leaves.

The flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in a spike, and bloom in the summer months.
