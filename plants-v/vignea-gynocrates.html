Vignea gynocrates

Vignea gynocrates, commonly known as the female vine, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its climbing habit, with slender stems that twine around supports. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically purple or pink in color. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

Vignea gynocrates is native to tropical and subtropical regions and is often found in shaded, moist environments.
