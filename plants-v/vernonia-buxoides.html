Vernonia buxoides

Vernonia buxoides, commonly known as the African Daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, bushy growth habit and bright yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in open woodlands and rocky soils, thriving in full sun. Its small, oval-shaped leaves are dark green and arranged in a compact, shrub-like form.

Vernonia buxoides is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
