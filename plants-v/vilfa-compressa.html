Vilfa compressa

Vilfa compressa, commonly known as the narrowleaf vilfa, is a herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. The plant features slender, upright stems with small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are usually pale blue or violet, arranged in loose clusters at the ends of the stems.

Vilfa compressa is known for its resilience and is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements.
