Volkameria alata

Volkameria alata, commonly known as the silverleaf volkameria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-white, velvety leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. Volkameria alata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
