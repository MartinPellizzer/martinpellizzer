Vignea redowskiana

Vignea redowskiana, commonly known as the Redowskian vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and vibrant green coloration. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in hue. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that contain numerous small, round seeds.

Vignea redowskiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in humid environments.
