Vidoricum latifolium

Vidoricum latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf vidoricum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the temperate regions of Europe.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom in the early summer. Its root system is fibrous and often used in traditional herbal medicine.

Vidoricum latifolium thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.
