Vernonia virens f. robustior

Vernonia virens f. robustior, commonly known as the robust vervain, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish to brownish flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas and thrives in well-drained soils.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens and landscapes.
