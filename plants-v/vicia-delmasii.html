Vicia delmasii

Vicia delmasii, commonly known as Delmas's vetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid conditions. It produces pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.

Vicia delmasii is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its nitrogen-fixing ability and hardy nature.
