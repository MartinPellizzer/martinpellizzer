Villadia grandyi

Villadia grandyi, commonly known as the grand velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense, velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. Its roots are known for their medicinal properties, used in traditional herbal remedies.

Villadia grandyi thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in rainforest understories.
