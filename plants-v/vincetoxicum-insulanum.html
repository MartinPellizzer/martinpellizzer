Vincetoxicum insulanum

Vincetoxicum insulanum, commonly known as the island false bindweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial vine characterized by its trailing growth habit and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its fruits are small, dry capsules that contain numerous seeds.

Vincetoxicum insulanum is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides, where it can spread aggressively.
