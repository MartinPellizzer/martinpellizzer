Verbascum petrae

Verbascum petrae, commonly known as the rock mullein, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its tall, hairy stems and large, velvety leaves. The plant produces showy yellow flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in dry, sunny habitats.

Verbascum petrae is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
