Verbascum tenellum

Verbascum tenellum, commonly known as the narrowleaf mullein, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems covered in dense, woolly hairs that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a fuzzy texture.

Verbascum tenellum is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
