Veronica × altaica

Veronica × altaica, commonly known as the tall speedwell, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Plantaginaceae family.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and produces clusters of blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Veronica × altaica is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
