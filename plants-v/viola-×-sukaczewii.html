Viola × sukaczewii

Viola × sukaczewii, commonly known as the hybrid violet, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Violaceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between Viola lutea and Viola verecunda, resulting in a plant with distinctive botanical features. The species is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, violet-colored flowers that bloom in early spring. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and gardens.

Viola × sukaczewii is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
