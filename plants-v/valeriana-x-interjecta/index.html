Valeriana × Interjecta

Valeriana × interjecta is a hybrid plant that combines traits from its parent species, valerian and sweet woodruff. It is known for its fragrant, white flowers and its ability to thrive in shaded environments. This plant is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and pleasant aroma. It is also valued in traditional medicine for its calming properties. Despite its beauty, it can be invasive in some regions if not properly managed.
