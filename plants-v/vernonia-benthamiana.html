Vernonia benthamiana

Vernonia benthamiana, commonly known as the bentham's ironweed, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in open grasslands. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are arranged in dense, spherical clusters.

Vernonia benthamiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
