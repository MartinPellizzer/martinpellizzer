Vaccinium chunii

Vaccinium chunii, commonly known as the Yunnan huckleberry, is a species of evergreen shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes many acid-loving plants such as blueberries and cranberries. The plant produces small, edible berries that are typically dark red to black in color. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.
