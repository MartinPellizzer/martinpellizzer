Volutaria Lippii

Volutaria lippii is a rare and striking plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia. It belongs to the Apiaceae family, known for its aromatic and often edible members. This species is characterized by its unique, spirally arranged leaves and delicate, white flowers. Due to its limited habitat and threats from human activity, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this fascinating and fragile flora.
