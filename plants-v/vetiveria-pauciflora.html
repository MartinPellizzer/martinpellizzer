Vetiveria pauciflora

Vetiveria pauciflora, commonly known as the slender vetiver, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, pale green flowers in dense clusters at the top of the stem. It is often used in soil stabilization due to its extensive root system.

Vetiveria pauciflora is also valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens and landscapes.
