Vilfa dianthera

Vilfa dianthera, commonly known as the silverleaf dianthera, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features narrow, silver-gray leaves with a fuzzy texture, which help reduce water loss in its arid native habitat. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
