Verbascum × zakynthicum

Verbascum × zakynthicum, commonly known as the Greek mullein.

It is a hybrid species belonging to the genus Verbascum, which includes several species of flowering plants native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa. This plant is characterized by its tall, hairy stems and large, fuzzy leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length. The flowers are typically yellow or orange, clustered in dense, showy inflorescences at the top of the plant.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
