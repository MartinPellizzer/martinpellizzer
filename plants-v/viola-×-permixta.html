Viola × permixta

Viola × permixta, commonly known as the hybrid violet, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 10–30 cm and thrives in cool, moist environments. Its flowers bloom in spring and are often used in gardens for their ornamental value.

Viola × permixta is also valued for its ability to naturalize and spread through self-seeding.
