Veratrum longebracteatum

Veratrum longebracteatum, commonly known as the long-bract veratrum, is a tall perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

It belongs to the family Melanthiaceae and is characterized by its tall, leafy stems and large, showy white or pale blue flowers. Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and traditional medicinal practices.
