Vriesea heliconioides var. polysticha

Vriesea heliconioides var. polysticha, commonly known as the Polystichum Vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to South America.

It features a compact rosette of dark green, waxy leaves that form a central cup to collect rainwater. The plant produces striking inflorescences with brightly colored bracts that range from red to orange. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance of low light and moderate watering.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for both ornamental and horticultural purposes.
