Vernonanthura yurimaguasensis

Vernonanthura yurimaguasensis, commonly known as the Yurimaguas orchid, is a rare and striking orchid species native to the Amazon rainforest of Peru.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, colorful flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and is adapted to the high biodiversity of the region. Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.

Due to habitat loss, it faces threats to its survival in the wild.
