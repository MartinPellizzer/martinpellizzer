Viola incognita

Viola incognita, commonly known as the unknown violet, is a species of flowering plant in the Violaceae family.

It is native to parts of Europe and Asia, where it thrives in woodland and meadow environments. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, delicate flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. Viola incognita is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens.

It also has historical uses in traditional medicine.
