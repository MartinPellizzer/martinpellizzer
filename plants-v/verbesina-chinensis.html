Verbesina chinensis

Verbesina chinensis, commonly known as Chinese ironweed, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and robust growth habit. The plant produces bright yellow to orange flower heads that bloom in late summer to early autumn. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Verbesina chinensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
