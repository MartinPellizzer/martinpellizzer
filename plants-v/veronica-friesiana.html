Veronica friesiana

Veronica friesiana, commonly known as the Friesian speedwell, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 20 centimeters. The plant features small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in clusters, often in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Veronica friesiana thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and borders.
