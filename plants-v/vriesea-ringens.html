Vriesea ringens

Vriesea ringens, commonly known as the Christmas Vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting inflorescence that resembles a Christmas tree, hence its common name. The plant features broad, strap-like leaves that form a rosette, creating a central cup to collect water. Its flowers, which can be red, orange, or yellow, bloom in late winter or early spring.

Vriesea ringens is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its adaptability and low maintenance requirements.
