Vaccinium lincangense

Vaccinium lincangense, commonly known as the Lincang blueberry, is a species of evergreen shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. Its fruit, a blue-black berry, is edible and contains high levels of antioxidants.

This species is valued for both its ornamental appeal and its potential use in local agriculture.
