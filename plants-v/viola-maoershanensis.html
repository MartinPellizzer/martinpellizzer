Viola maoershanensis

Viola maoershanensis, commonly known as the Maoershan violet, is a rare and endemic species native to China.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant violet flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as mountain forests. Its flowers bloom in early spring, adding a splash of color to its natural habitat.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable species for botanical study and conservation efforts.
