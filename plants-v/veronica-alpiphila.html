Veronica alpiphila

Veronica alpiphila, commonly known as the alpine speedwell, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is a member of the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its small, blue or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Veronica alpiphila is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in challenging ecological conditions.
