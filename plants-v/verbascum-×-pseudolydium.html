Verbascum × pseudolydium

Verbascum × pseudolydium, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial plant belonging to the Scrophulariaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, hairy stems and large, gray-green, velvety leaves. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. It is native to regions of southern Europe and western Asia.

Verbascum × pseudolydium is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to well-drained soils and full sun.
