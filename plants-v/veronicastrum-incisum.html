Veronicastrum incisum

Veronicastrum incisum, commonly known as blue mist spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, spire-like clusters of blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often finely toothed, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is drought-tolerant once established.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
