Veronica pallens

Veronica pallens, commonly known as the silverweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe, North Africa, and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs that help reduce water loss. The plant produces clusters of small, blue or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Veronica pallens is often found in disturbed soils, along roadsides, and in meadows, thriving in full sun to partial shade.

It is a hardy perennial that is sometimes used in ornamental gardening for its attractive appearance and easy maintenance.
