Verbascum undulatum var. integrifolium

Verbascum undulatum var. integrifolium, commonly known as the woolly flaxflower, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its soft, fuzzy leaves and showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes. Its unique foliage and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The variety name *integrifolium* refers to its entire, undivided leaf structure, distinguishing it from other varieties of *Verbascum undulatum*.
