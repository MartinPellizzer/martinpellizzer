Vigna Kirkii

Vigna kirkii is a lesser-known legume native to tropical Africa. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in forested areas. This plant is valued for its edible beans, which are rich in nutrients. Despite its potential, it remains underutilized in modern agriculture. Its unique characteristics make it a promising crop for future food security.
