Veronica schmidtiana f. albiflora

Veronica schmidtiana f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered speedwell, is a variant of the species Veronica schmidtiana.

It is a low-growing perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, white flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its ornamental value lies in its delicate appearance and ability to bloom throughout the summer months.
