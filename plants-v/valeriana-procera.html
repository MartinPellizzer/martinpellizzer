Valeriana procera

Valeriana procera, commonly known as the tall valerian, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant features dark green, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters during the summer months. Valeriana procera is often found in meadows, woodlands, and along stream banks.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal medicine.
