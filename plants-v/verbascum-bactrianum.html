Verbascum bactrianum

Verbascum bactrianum, commonly known as the Bactrian mullein, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It is a member of the Scrophulariaceae family and is characterized by its tall, hairy stems and large, fuzzy leaves. The plant produces showy yellow-orange flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in mountainous regions.

Verbascum bactrianum is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
