Vicatia bipinnata

Vicatia bipinnata, commonly known as the double-pinnate morning glory, is a tropical climbing vine native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to violet, often opening in the early morning. The plant has compound leaves with a feathery appearance, consisting of multiple pairs of leaflets. Vicatia bipinnata thrives in warm, humid climates and requires support for its vigorous growth.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its beauty and rapid growth habit.
