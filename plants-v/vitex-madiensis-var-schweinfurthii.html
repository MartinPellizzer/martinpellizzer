Vitex madiensis var. schweinfurthii

Vitex madiensis var. schweinfurthii, commonly known as the African bluebrush, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its aromatic, gray-green leaves and clusters of violet-blue flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces small, woody fruits containing several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal uses, it is cultivated in gardens and traditional herbal practices.
