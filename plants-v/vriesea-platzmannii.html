Vriesea platzmannii

Vriesea platzmannii, commonly known as the Platzmann's Vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, leafy rosette and produces vibrant, long-lasting inflorescences in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant's leaves are typically dark green with a waxy texture and can grow up to several feet in length. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Vriesea platzmannii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
