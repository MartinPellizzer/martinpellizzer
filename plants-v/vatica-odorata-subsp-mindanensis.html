Vatica odorata subsp. mindanensis

Vatica odorata subsp.

mindanensis, commonly known as the Mindanao vatica, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Philippines. It belongs to the family Dipterocarpaceae and is characterized by its tall, straight trunk and dense foliage.

The tree produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptical, often with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The species is valued for its timber, which is used in construction and furniture-making due to its durability and fine grain.
