Verbascum × bitlisianum

Verbascum × bitlisianum, commonly known as the Bitlis mullein, is a hybrid plant native to Turkey.

It is a member of the Scrophulariaceae family and is characterized by its tall, hairy stems and large, fuzzy leaves. The plant produces showy yellow flowers in clusters, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
