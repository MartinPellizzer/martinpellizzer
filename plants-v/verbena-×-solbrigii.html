Verbena × solbrigii

Verbena × solbrigii, commonly known as the hybrid verbena, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, clustered flowers in shades of red, pink, and purple, which bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has narrow, aromatic leaves with a slightly fuzzy texture, and it thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is drought-tolerant and well-suited for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking vibrant, low-maintenance flora.
