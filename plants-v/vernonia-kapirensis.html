Vernonia kapirensis

Vernonia kapirensis, commonly known as the Kapirensis ironweed, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Vernonia kapirensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for potential medicinal properties.
