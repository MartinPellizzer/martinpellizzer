Vignantha × prolixa

Vignantha × prolixa, commonly known as the hybrid evergreen vine, is a cross between Vignantha and Prolixa species.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth habit and ability to climb or trail along surfaces. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Its leaves are usually alternate, simple, and have a smooth or slightly hairy texture.

Vignantha × prolixa is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
