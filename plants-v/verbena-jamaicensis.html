Verbena jamaicensis

Verbena jamaicensis, commonly known as Jamaican verbena, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in coastal areas or disturbed soils. Its slender stems and opposite leaves are typical of the genus Verbena.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
