Veronica assoana var. cadevallii

Veronica assoana var. cadevallii, commonly known as the Cadevallii speedwell, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and produces small, blue to violet flowers in clusters. The leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable plant for rock gardens or borders.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
