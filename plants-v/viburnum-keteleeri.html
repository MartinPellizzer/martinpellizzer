Viburnum keteleeri

Viburnum keteleeri, commonly known as the Keteleer viburnum, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to various soil types.

Viburnum keteleeri is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic appeal and wildlife benefits.
