Viola × balmesii

Viola × balmesii, commonly known as the Balmes' violet, is a hybrid species within the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, violet-colored flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows to a height of 10–20 cm and prefers well-drained, moist soil in partial shade. It is native to the western United States and is often found in woodland and meadow habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its beauty and hardiness.
