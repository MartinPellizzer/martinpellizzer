Viola × erdneri

Viola × erdneri, commonly known as the English violet, is a hybrid species within the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 10–30 cm and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is native to Europe and has been widely cultivated for its ornamental value.

Viola × erdneri is often used in gardens and floral arrangements due to its beauty and adaptability.
