Valeriana mikaniae

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, white to pale green flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy surface.
