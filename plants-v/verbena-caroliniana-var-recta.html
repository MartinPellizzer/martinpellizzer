Verbena caroliniana var. recta

Verbena caroliniana var. recta, commonly known as Carolina verbena, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial with slender, upright stems and small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant produces small, oblong leaves that are typically opposite along the stem. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in moist, open habitats.

Its nectar-rich flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
