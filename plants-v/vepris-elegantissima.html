Vepris elegantissima

Vepris elegantissima, commonly known as the elegant vepris, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rutaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly Nigeria and Cameroon. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, small white or yellowish flowers and aromatic leaves. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
