Vitis barbata f. laciniata

Vitis barbata f. laciniata, commonly known as the laciniated grapevine, is a unique cultivar of the grapevine species Vitis barbata.

It is characterized by its finely lobed, maple-like leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant is native to China and thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil. It produces small, edible berries that are often used in traditional Chinese medicine.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to various growing conditions.
