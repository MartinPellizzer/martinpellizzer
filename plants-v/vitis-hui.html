Vitis hui

Vitis hui, commonly known as the Chinese grapevine, is a woody vine native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Vitaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and compound leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible berries that are typically black or purple in color. Vitis hui is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.

Its adaptability to various soil types makes it a versatile plant in horticulture.
