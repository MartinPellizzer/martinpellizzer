Vittaria zeylanica

Vittaria zeylanica, commonly known as the Ceylon maidenhair fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Vittariaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lacy fronds that are typically light green with a slightly curled appearance. The plant is native to tropical regions of Asia, including India, Sri Lanka, and parts of Southeast Asia. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forests or along stream banks.

Vittaria zeylanica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
