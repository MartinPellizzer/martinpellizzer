Vriesea psittacina var. duvaliana

Vriesea psittacina var. duvaliana, commonly known as the Duvaliana Vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It features a rosette of broad, dark green leaves with a prominent central spine and a striking red-brown inflorescence. The plant produces showy, orange-red flowers that bloom in a spadix surrounded by bright red bracts. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
