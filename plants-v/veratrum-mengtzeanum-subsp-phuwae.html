Veratrum mengtzeanum subsp. phuwae

Veratrum mengtzeanum subsp.

phuwae, commonly known as the Phuwa white hellebore, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of northern Thailand. It belongs to the family Melanthiaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender stem and clusters of white to pale pink flowers.
