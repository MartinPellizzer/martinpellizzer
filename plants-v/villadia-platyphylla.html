Villadia platyphylla

Villadia platyphylla, commonly known as the flat-leaf villadia, is a perennial herb native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are broad at the base and taper towards the tips, often with a waxy coating. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, typically blooming during the warmer months. Its root system is fibrous and adapted to retain moisture in its natural habitat.

Villadia platyphylla is valued in traditional medicine for its purported therapeutic properties.
