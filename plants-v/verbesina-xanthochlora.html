Verbesina xanthochlora

Verbesina xanthochlora, commonly known as the yellow crownbeard, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the southeastern United States.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, adding vibrant color to gardens. The plant has slender, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
