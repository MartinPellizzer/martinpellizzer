Viburnum coriaceum var. zeylanicum

Viburnum coriaceum var. zeylanicum, commonly known as the Zeylanic viburnum, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers in the spring, which are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to wildlife.
