Verbascum × prusianiforme

Verbascum × prusianiforme, commonly known as the Prussian mullein, is a hybrid plant belonging to the genus Verbascum in the family Scrophulariaceae.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, soft, fuzzy leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers in a spike-like cluster, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance and ease of care.
