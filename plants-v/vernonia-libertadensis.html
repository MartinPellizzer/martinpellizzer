Vernonia libertadensis

Vernonia libertadensis, commonly known as the Libertad ironweed, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is found in arid regions of the country. Its flowers are usually yellow to orange in color, with narrow, linear leaves.

Vernonia libertadensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local pollinators.
