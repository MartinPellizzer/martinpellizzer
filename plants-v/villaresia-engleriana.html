Villaresia engleriana

Villaresia engleriana, commonly known as the Engler's rock cactus, is a perennial flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, spiny stem and clusters of yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing adaptations to drought and nutrient-poor soils. Its flowers bloom in the spring, adding a striking contrast to its green, succulent structure.

Villaresia engleriana is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in challenging conditions.
