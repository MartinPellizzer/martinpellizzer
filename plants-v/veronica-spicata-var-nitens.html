Veronica spicata var. nitens

Veronica spicata var. nitens, commonly known as the shining speedwell, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe.

It features upright, spiky stems that bear clusters of small, blue to violet flowers. The leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine, silky hairs, giving them a soft, velvety texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This plant is often used in garden landscapes for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
