Vitis oblonga

Vitis oblonga, commonly known as the Chinese grape or longleaf grape, is a species of flowering plant in the Vitaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its climbing habit and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by berries that turn from green to black as they mature. Vitis oblonga is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and ecological studies.
