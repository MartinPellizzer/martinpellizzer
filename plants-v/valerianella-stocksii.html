Valerianella stocksii

Valerianella stocksii, commonly known as the narrow-leaf valerian, is a flowering plant in the carnation family (Caryophyllaceae).

It is native to Europe and parts of western Asia, typically growing in grassy areas and along roadsides. The plant features small, pink or white flowers that bloom in early summer, and its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy.
