Vincetoxicum silvestre

Vincetoxicum silvestre, commonly known as wild white bittersweet, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, clustered flowers that range from white to pink.
