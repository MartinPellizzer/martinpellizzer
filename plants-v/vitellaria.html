Vitellaria

Vitellaria, commonly known as shea tree, is a tropical tree native to West Africa.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is valued for its fruit, which contains a nutritious seed used to produce shea butter. The tree can grow up to 20 meters tall and has broad, leathery leaves. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, while the fruit is a smooth, green drupe.

The plant plays an important ecological and economic role in the region.
