Vincetoxicum sublanceolatum var. albidum

Vincetoxicum sublanceolatum var. albidum, commonly known as white milkvetch, is a perennial flowering plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are typically trifoliate, with each leaflet being oval to lance-shaped.

It is often found in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
