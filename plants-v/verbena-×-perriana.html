Verbena × perriana

Verbena × perriana, commonly known as the hybrid verbena, is a flowering plant in the Verbenaceae family.

It is a hybrid between Verbena rigida and Verbena bonariensis, resulting in a plant with distinctive, vibrant blooms. The flowers are typically blue or purple, arranged in spikes, and bloom from late spring to early autumn. It is native to South America but has been cultivated in various parts of the world for its ornamental value.

This plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
