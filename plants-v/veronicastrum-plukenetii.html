Veronicastrum plukenetii

Veronicastrum plukenetii, commonly known as blue mist spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to North America.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of small, sky-blue flowers in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter underside. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Veronicastrum plukenetii is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
