Vernonia fysonii

Vernonia fysonii, commonly known as the Natal ironwood, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, fibrous wood and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the cooler months. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, coastal environments.

Vernonia fysonii is valued for its hardiness and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
