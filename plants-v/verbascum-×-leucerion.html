Verbascum × leucerion

Verbascum × leucerion, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial plant belonging to the Scrophulariaceae family.

It is a hybrid between Verbascum blattaria and Verbascum blattifolium, resulting in a plant with distinctive features combining traits from both parents. The leaves are large, fuzzy, and gray-green, while the flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
