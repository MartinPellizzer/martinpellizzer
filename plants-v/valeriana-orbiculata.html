Valeriana orbiculata

Valeriana orbiculata, commonly known as the globe valerian, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features rounded, leathery leaves and produces small, clustered white or pinkish flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Valeriana orbiculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported calming properties.
