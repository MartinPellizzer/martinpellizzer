Vernonia myriotricha

Vernonia myriotricha, commonly known as the silky Vernonia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer. The leaves are typically covered in fine, silky hairs, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
