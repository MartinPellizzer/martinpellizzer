Viguiera parkinsonii

Viguiera parkinsonii, commonly known as the Mexican ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Viguiera parkinsonii thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a suitable choice for xeriscaping in arid regions.
