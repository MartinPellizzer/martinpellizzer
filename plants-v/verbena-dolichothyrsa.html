Verbena dolichothyrsa

Verbena dolichothyrsa, commonly known as the long-throated verbena, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to violet. The plant typically blooms in late spring to early summer, producing clusters of small, fragrant flowers. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.

Verbena dolichothyrsa is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
