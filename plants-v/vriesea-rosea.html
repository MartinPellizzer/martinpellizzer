Vriesea rosea

Vriesea rosea, commonly known as the flaming sword plant, is a species of flowering plant in the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its striking, sword-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored. The plant produces vibrant, long-lasting inflorescences that can last for several months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Vriesea rosea is a popular ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
