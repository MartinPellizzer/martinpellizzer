Viola orthoceras

Viola orthoceras, commonly known as the upright violet, is a species of flowering plant in the Violaceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of western Asia, typically growing in woodland and meadow habitats. The plant features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a hairy texture, and its flowers are violet or purple in color, often with a distinctive yellow or white center. Viola orthoceras is known for its upright growth habit, which distinguishes it from other viola species.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and conservation efforts.
