Vitaliana gregorii

Vitaliana gregorii, commonly known as the Gregorii's cycad, is a rare and ancient plant species native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Cycadaceae family and is characterized by its stout trunk and feather-like leaves. The plant produces large, cone-like structures that contain its seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat threats, Vitaliana gregorii is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
