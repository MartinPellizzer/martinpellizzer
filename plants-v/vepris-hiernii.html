Vepris hiernii

Vepris hiernii, commonly known as the African wild lime, is a small evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its fragrant leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces oval, green fruits that turn orange when mature, containing a sour, aromatic pulp. The tree is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.
