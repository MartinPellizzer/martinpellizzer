Vanda bicaudata

Vanda bicaudata, commonly known as the two-tailed orchid, is a epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, slender pseudobulbs and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, pendulous flowers with bright yellow petals and a prominent, two-lobed lip. Vanda bicaudata thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

It is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
