Viola tsugitakaensis

Viola tsugitakaensis, commonly known as the Tsugitaka violet, is a species of flowering plant in the Violaceae family.

It is native to Japan and is characterized by its delicate, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its flowers bloom in early spring, adding a splash of color to its natural habitat.

Viola tsugitakaensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
