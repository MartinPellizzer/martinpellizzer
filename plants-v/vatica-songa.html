Vatica songa

Vatica songa, commonly known as the Songa ironwood, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, hard wood and evergreen foliage. The plant has compound leaves with alternate arrangement and produces small, inconspicuous flowers. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Vatica songa is valued for its durable timber and is often found in lowland and hill forest ecosystems.
