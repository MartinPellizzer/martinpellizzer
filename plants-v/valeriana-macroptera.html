Valeriana macroptera

Valeriana macroptera, commonly known as the large-leaved valerian, is a perennial herb native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, often fragrant leaves and flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its flowers are small, clustered in umbels, and range in color from white to pink.
