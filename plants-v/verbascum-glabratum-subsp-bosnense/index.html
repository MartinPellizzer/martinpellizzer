Verbascum Glabratum Subsp. Bosnense

Verbascum glabratum subsp. bosnense is a rare and distinctive plant native to the Balkan region. It belongs to the genus Verbascum, commonly known as mullein, which includes many species with fluffy seed heads. This subspecies is characterized by its unique foliage and subtle floral features. It thrives in rocky and dry habitats, making it a resilient plant in challenging environments. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this subspecies from habitat loss and overharvesting.
