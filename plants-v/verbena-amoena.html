Verbena amoena

Verbena amoena, commonly known as the common verbena, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, vibrant blue or violet flowers. The plant has opposite, narrow, and slightly hairy leaves that are typically green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Verbena amoena is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
