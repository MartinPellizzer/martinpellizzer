Vernonia melanocoma

Vernonia melanocoma, commonly known as blackstem ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant produces conical, spike-like inflorescences that are adorned with violet to purple flowers. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local pollinators.
