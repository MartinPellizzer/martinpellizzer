Vitis doniana

Vitis doniana, commonly known as the African grape or wild grapevine, is a woody vine native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant produces edible berries that are typically dark purple or black when mature. Vitis doniana thrives in humid environments and is often found in forests and along riverbanks.

It has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
