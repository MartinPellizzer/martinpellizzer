Veitchia joannis

Veitchia joannis, commonly known as the giant cycad, is a striking plant native to the island of Socotra.

It is characterized by its large, fern-like leaves that can reach lengths of up to 3 meters. The plant produces massive, cone-like structures that are typically yellow when mature. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and limited natural range, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and conservatories.
