Viola kwangtungensis

Viola kwangtungensis, commonly known as the Guangdong violet, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and delicate violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. It produces small, fragrant blossoms that attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Viola kwangtungensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
