Veronica Langei Var. Nevadensis

Nevada speedwell, scientifically known as Veronica langei var. nevadensis, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high elevations of Nevada. It thrives in rocky, alpine environments where few other species can survive. This plant is characterized by its small, blue-violet flowers and narrow, grass-like leaves. Due to its limited habitat and vulnerability to environmental changes, it is considered an endangered species. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique botanical treasure.
