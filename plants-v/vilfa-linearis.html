Vilfa linearis

Vilfa linearis, commonly known as the slender vilfa, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small, clustered flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as forest floors or wetlands. It has a fibrous root system and is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Vilfa linearis is also valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.
