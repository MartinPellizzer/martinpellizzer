Vernonia cryptocephala

Vernonia cryptocephala, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, hard wood, which is highly valued for its durability and resistance to decay. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in compact heads, which bloom in the late summer to early autumn.

The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it an important component of local ecosystems.
