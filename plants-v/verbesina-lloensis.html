Verbesina lloensis

Verbesina lloensis, commonly known as the Lloengo goldenrod, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Verbesina lloensis is adapted to high-altitude environments and thrives in well-drained soils.
