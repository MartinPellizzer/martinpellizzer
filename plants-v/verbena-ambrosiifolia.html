Verbena ambrosiifolia

Verbena ambrosiifolia, commonly known as the sacred verbena, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to violet, blooming throughout the summer months. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a slightly fuzzy appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Verbena ambrosiifolia is also valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
