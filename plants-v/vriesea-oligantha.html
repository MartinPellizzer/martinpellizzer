Vriesea oligantha

Vriesea oligantha, commonly known as the silver stripe bromeliad, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup to collect rainwater. The plant produces striking inflorescences with colorful bracts that can range from red to orange and yellow. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in a silvery or white wax-like coating, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Vriesea oligantha is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
