Vandenboschia oshimensis

Vandenboschia oshimensis, commonly known as the Oshimen's ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in late summer, adding ornamental value. It is a member of the Aizoaceae family, known for its adaptability to harsh conditions.
