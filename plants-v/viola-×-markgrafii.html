Viola × markgrafii

Viola × markgrafii, commonly known as the Viola × markgrafii, is a hybrid species of flowering plant in the family Violaceae.

It is native to Europe and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that come in a range of colors, including purple, blue, and white. The plant typically grows to a height of 10–30 cm and thrives in cool, moist environments.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and is known for its ability to bloom throughout the spring and early summer.
