Villanova perpusilla

Villanova perpusilla, commonly known as the small-flowered bellflower, is a delicate annual herb native to southern Africa.

It features slender, branching stems that grow up to 30 centimeters in height. The plant produces small, bell-shaped blue or violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and opposite, with a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and rocky areas.
