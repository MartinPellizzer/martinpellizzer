Viburnum schensianum subsp. chekiangense

Viburnum schensianum subsp.

chekiangense, commonly known as the Chinese snowball viburnum, is a deciduous shrub native to China. It features clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in early spring, creating a showy display.

The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy on top and pale underneath. It produces red, fleshy berries that are edible for birds but not for humans.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
