Verbascum hookerianum var. pseudocalycinum

Verbascum hookerianum var. pseudocalycinum, commonly known as the Hooker's mullein, is a perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Scrophulariaceae family and is characterized by its tall, hairy stems and large, showy yellow flowers. This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
