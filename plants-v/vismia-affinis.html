Vismia affinis

Vismia affinis, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of small white flowers that bloom in summer, giving it a snowball-like appearance. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green. It produces woody, spherical fruits that contain several seeds.

Vismia affinis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to well-drained, sandy soils.
