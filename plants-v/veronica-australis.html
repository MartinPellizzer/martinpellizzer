Veronica australis

Veronica australis, commonly known as the Australian speedwell, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of blue or violet flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy habitats.

Veronica australis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
