Verbascum oreodoxa

Verbascum oreodoxa, commonly known as the slender mullein.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia. The plant features large, fuzzy, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in soft, woolly hairs. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in a dense spike at the top of the stem.

Verbascum oreodoxa thrives in dry, sunny environments and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils.
