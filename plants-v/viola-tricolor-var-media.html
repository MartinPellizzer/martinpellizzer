Viola tricolor var. media

Viola tricolor var. media, commonly known as the heartleaf viola, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, colorful flowers in shades of violet, yellow, and white, which bloom in early spring. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to naturalize easily.
