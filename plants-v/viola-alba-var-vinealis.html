Viola alba var. vinealis

Viola alba var. vinealis, commonly known as the white violet or slender violet, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, pale blue to white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in a fine layer of downy hairs.

Viola alba var. vinealis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its charming appearance.
