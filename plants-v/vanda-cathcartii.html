Vanda cathcartii

Vanda cathcartii, commonly known as the Cathcart's vanda, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

This epiphytic plant is known for its striking, showy flowers that bloom in a range of colors, including pink, white, and orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth. The plant has long, strap-like leaves and produces aerial roots that help it attach to trees or other substrates.

Vanda cathcartii is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
