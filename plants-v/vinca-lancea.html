Vinca lancea

Vinca lancea, commonly known as the lanceleaf vinca, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and clusters of bright blue or violet flowers. The plant is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Vinca lancea is a perennial shrub that produces small, tubular flowers attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
