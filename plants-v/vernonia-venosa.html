Vernonia venosa

Vernonia venosa, commonly known as the silky vernonia, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features narrow, gray-green leaves and produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. Its botanical characteristics include a sturdy stem and a compact growth habit.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adaptable to various soil types.
