Vochysia obscura var. obidensis

Vochysia obscura var. obidensis, commonly known as the Obidense Vochysia, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and cylindrical, clustered inflorescences that bear small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant produces elliptical, woody fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and potential horticultural use.
