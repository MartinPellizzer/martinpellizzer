Vochysia cassiquiarensis

Vochysia cassiquiarensis, commonly known as the Cassiquiare trumpet tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. The tree has a thick, buttressed trunk and produces fruits that are dispersed by animals. Its leaves are simple and alternate, with a rough, waxy texture.

Vochysia cassiquiarensis plays an important ecological role in its habitat by providing food and shelter for various wildlife species.
