Valantia muralis subvar. aculeata

Valantia muralis subvar. aculeata, commonly known as the spiny wall plant, is a unique variant of the Valantia muralis species.

It is characterized by its robust, upright growth habit and the presence of small, sharp spines along its stems and leaves. The plant typically thrives in rocky or crevice-filled environments, where it can anchor itself firmly. Its foliage is usually a deep green, with some varieties displaying a bluish tint.

This subvariety is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in challenging terrains.
