Vernonia yabelloana

Vernonia yabelloana, commonly known as the Yabelloa Vernonia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Vernonia yabelloana is often found in open woodlands and grasslands, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
