Vanda merrillii var. rotorii

Vanda merrillii var. rotorii, commonly known as the Rotorua vanda, is a terrestrial orchid native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its long, strap-like leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often growing on rocks or in forest undergrowth. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the environment, and it is relatively easy to cultivate with proper care.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
