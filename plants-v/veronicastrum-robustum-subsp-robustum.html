Veronicastrum robustum subsp. robustum

Veronicastrum robustum subsp.

robustum, commonly known as blue mist spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, sky-blue flowers that bloom in midsummer.

The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and slightly hairy on the underside. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden borders and landscapes.

Its ornamental value comes from its fragrant flowers and attractive foliage throughout the growing season.
