Visenia javanica

Visenia javanica, commonly known as the Javan ironwood, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark-colored wood, which is highly valued for its durability and strength. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a glossy surface. It produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, followed by woody, spherical fruits.

The tree is often found in lowland rainforests and is known for its slow growth and long lifespan.
