Vitex excelsa var. excelsa

Vitex excelsa var. excelsa, commonly known as the giant flame tree, is a striking tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that resemble flames, typically appearing in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has pinnate leaves with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets and produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The tree is also notable for its rapid growth and ability to provide dense shade.
