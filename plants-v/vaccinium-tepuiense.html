Vaccinium tepuiense

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. This plant is characterized by its small, leathery leaves and clusters of bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. It thrives in high-altitude, humid environments with well-drained acidic soils.
