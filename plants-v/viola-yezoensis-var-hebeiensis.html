Viola yezoensis var. hebeiensis

Viola yezoensis var. hebeiensis, commonly known as the Hebei violet, is a perennial flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and vibrant purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its flowers bloom in early spring, adding a splash of color to the landscape.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
