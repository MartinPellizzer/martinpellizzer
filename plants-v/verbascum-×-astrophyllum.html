Verbascum × astrophyllum

Verbascum × astrophyllum, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial plant belonging to the Scrophulariaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, hairy stems and large, velvety leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces showy, yellow or orange flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for dry, rocky habitats.

This hybrid is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
