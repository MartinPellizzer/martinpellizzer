Vriesea jimenezii

Vriesea jimenezii, commonly known as the Jimenez's vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, spathaceous bracts that come in a range of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its rosette-shaped leaves are thick and waxy, helping to collect and store water.

Vriesea jimenezii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in both indoor and outdoor settings.
