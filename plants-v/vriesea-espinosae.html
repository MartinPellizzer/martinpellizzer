Vriesea espinosae

Vriesea espinosae, commonly known as the Espinosa's Vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting inflorescence that displays a range of colors, often in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that collect water, creating a small reservoir that supports various microorganisms. Its adaptability to humid environments makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Vriesea espinosae is also valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
