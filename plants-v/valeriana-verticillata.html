Valeriana verticillata

Valeriana verticillata, commonly known as upright valerian, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, often purple or pink flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves and a fibrous root system. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

Valeriana verticillata is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally for its calming effects.
