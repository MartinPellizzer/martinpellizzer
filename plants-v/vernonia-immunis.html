Vernonia immunis

Vernonia immunis, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and yellow-orange composite flowers. The plant is known for its hard, dense wood, which is highly valued for construction and fuel. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry woodland and savanna habitats.

Vernonia immunis also has traditional medicinal uses in some African cultures.
