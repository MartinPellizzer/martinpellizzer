Vernonia hexantha

Vernonia hexantha, commonly known as the slender ironweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and distinctive foliage. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are small and yellowish, arranged in dense clusters.

Vernonia hexantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
