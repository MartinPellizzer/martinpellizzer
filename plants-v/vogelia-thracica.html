Vogelia thracica

Vogelia thracica, commonly known as the Balkan thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky soils and open habitats. The plant features spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers that bloom in summer. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Vogelia thracica is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
