Vandenboschia lofoushanensis

Vandenboschia lofoushanensis, commonly known as the Lufoushan sandplant, is a rare and endemic species native to China.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its dense, cushion-like growth form and fleshy, blue-green leaves. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, with thick cuticles and reduced leaf surface to minimize water loss. It produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding ornamental value to its habitat.
