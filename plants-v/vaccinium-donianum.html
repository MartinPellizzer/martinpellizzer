Vaccinium donianum

Vaccinium donianum, commonly known as the Donian blueberry, is a species of evergreen shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes other familiar plants like blueberries and heathers. The plant produces small, acidic berries that are typically harvested in the fall. Its leaves are dark green and glossy, and it thrives in acidic, well-drained soils.
