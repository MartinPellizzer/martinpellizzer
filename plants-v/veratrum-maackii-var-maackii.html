Veratrum maackii var. maackii

Veratrum maackii var. maackii, commonly known as Maack's arrowroot, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Melanthiaceae family and is characterized by its tall, stout stems and large, lance-shaped leaves.
