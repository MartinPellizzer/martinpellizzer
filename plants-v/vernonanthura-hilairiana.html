Vernonanthura hilairiana

Vernonanthura hilairiana, commonly known as the Hilairian orchid, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, brightly colored flowers that typically feature a combination of purple, pink, and white hues. The plant has a slender, erect stem with several fleshy, ovate leaves arranged in a spiral pattern. Its flowers are borne in clusters and are notable for their intricate structure and fragrance.

Vernonanthura hilairiana is a rare and endangered species, making it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
