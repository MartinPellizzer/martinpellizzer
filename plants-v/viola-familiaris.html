Viola familiaris

Viola familiaris, commonly known as the common violet, is a flowering plant belonging to the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of violet or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Viola familiaris is known for its medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory and antioxidant benefits.

It is also a popular garden plant due to its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
