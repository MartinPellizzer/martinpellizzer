Vicentia

Vicentia, commonly known as the bluebell vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It features striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in clusters along its trailing stems. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a glossy surface and emits a mild fragrance. Vicentia thrives in humid, warm climates and is often used in ornamental gardening.

Its rapid growth makes it suitable for covering trellises or hanging baskets.
