Vignea × arthuriana

Vignea × arthuriana, commonly known as the Arthur's vine, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and compound leaves with trifoliate arrangements. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically white or pale purple in color. Its fruit is a legume pod containing several seeds.

Vignea × arthuriana is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and flowering patterns.
