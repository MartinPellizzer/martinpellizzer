Valantia muralis var. intricata

Valantia muralis var. intricata, commonly known as the intricate wall vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and deep green coloration. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, often white or pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on rocky surfaces or in crevices.

Its intricate leaf patterns and climbing habit make it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
