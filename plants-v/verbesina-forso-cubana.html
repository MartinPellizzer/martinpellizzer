Verbesina forso-cubana

Verbesina forso-cubana, commonly known as the Cuban ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean, particularly Cuba.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and is often found in open woodlands and disturbed soils.
