Verbena pinnatifida

Verbena pinnatifida, commonly known as the clustered verbena, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a member of the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant blue to purple flowers. The plant features opposite, narrow, and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable ornamental plant in Mediterranean climates.

Its compact size and showy blooms make it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
