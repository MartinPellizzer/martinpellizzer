Vernonia flanaganii

Vernonia flanaganii, commonly known as Flanagan's ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the United States, particularly found in the southeastern region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Vernonia flanaganii is valued for its ecological role in supporting pollinators and its ornamental appeal in wetland gardens.
