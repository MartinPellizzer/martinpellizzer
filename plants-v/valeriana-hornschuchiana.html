Valeriana hornschuchiana

Valeriana hornschuchiana, commonly known as the hornschuchian valerian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its upright, branched stems and small, clustered flowers.
