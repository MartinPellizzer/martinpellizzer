Verbesina zaragosana var. intermedia

Verbesina zaragosana var. intermedia, commonly known as the intermediate yellowtop, is a herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges, and it thrives in well-drained soils. It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.

Its botanical features make it an attractive addition to gardens and a valuable species for ecological studies.
