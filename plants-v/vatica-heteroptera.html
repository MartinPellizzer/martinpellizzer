Vatica heteroptera

Vatica heteroptera, commonly known as the Malayan ironwood, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, hard wood that is highly valued for its strength and durability. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. It produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in spikes, followed by woody capsules containing seeds.

Vatica heteroptera thrives in humid, shaded environments within lowland and hill forests.
