Vulneraria heterophylla var. flaviflora

Vulneraria heterophylla var. flaviflora, commonly known as the yellow-flowered vulneraria, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, four-petaled flowers and capsule fruits. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. Its leaves are usually alternate and vary in shape, which is reflected in its species name "heterophylla.".

The yellow flowers bloom in early summer and attract various pollinators like bees and butterflies.
