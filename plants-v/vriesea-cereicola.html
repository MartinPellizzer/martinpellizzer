Vriesea cereicola

Vriesea cereicola, commonly known as the Crimson Vriesea, is a tropical bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, brightly colored inflorescence that typically displays shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features rosette-shaped leaves that are often broad and waxy, with a bluish-green hue. Vriesea cereicola thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
