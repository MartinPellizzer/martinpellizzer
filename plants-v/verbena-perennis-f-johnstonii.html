Verbena perennis f. johnstonii

Verbena perennis f. johnstonii, commonly known as the Johnston's everlasting.

This perennial plant is native to the Mediterranean region and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. It features small, vibrant blue or violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its fluffy seed heads remain attractive into the fall, adding winter interest to the landscape.
