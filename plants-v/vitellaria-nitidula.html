Vitellaria nitidula

Vitellaria nitidula, commonly known as the smooth shea tree, is a small to medium-sized deciduous tree native to western Africa.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The tree produces smooth, round fruits that contain a creamy, edible seed kernel used in traditional food and cosmetics.

Its bark and leaves have also been used in local medicinal practices for their purported therapeutic properties.
