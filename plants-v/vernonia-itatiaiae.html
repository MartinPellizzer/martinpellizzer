Vernonia itatiaiae

Vernonia itatiaiae, commonly known as the Itatiaia ironweed, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Vernonia itatiaiae is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
