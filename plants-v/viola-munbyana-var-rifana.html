Viola munbyana var. rifana

Viola munbyana var. rifana, commonly known as the Rifan violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of North Africa.

It features heart-shaped, hairy leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly velvety texture. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of violet or purple, often with a distinctive white or yellowish throat. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been cultivated for its delicate floral display.
