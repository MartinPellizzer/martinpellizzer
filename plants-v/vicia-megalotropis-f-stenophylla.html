Vicia megalotropis f. stenophylla

Vicia megalotropis f. stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf vetch, is a botanical variant of the broad-leaf vetch.

It is a perennial legume belonging to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its slender, elongated leaves. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from pale yellow to reddish-brown. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed or open habitats.

This form is notable for its narrower foliage compared to the typical form of Vicia megalotropis.
