Vriesea × elmireana

Vriesea × elmireana, commonly known as the hybrid bromeliad, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Vriesea splendens and Vriesea pulchra.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful inflorescence that typically displays a mix of red, orange, and yellow hues. The plant is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is native to South America, particularly Brazil. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Vriesea × elmireana is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
