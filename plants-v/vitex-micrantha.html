Vitex micrantha

Vitex micrantha, commonly known as smallflower chaste tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to parts of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy on the underside. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically blue or purple in color, which bloom in the summer months. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release small, dry seeds.

Vitex micrantha is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
