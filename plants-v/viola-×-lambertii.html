Viola × lambertii

Viola × lambertii, commonly known as the Lambert's violet, is a hybrid species belonging to the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, velvety leaves and vibrant, colorful flowers that typically range from purple to yellow. Native to Europe, this plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade. It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ease of care.

The species is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
