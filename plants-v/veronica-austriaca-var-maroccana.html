Veronica austriaca var. maroccana

Veronica austriaca var. maroccana, commonly known as the Moroccan speedwell, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of North Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface and produces clusters of blue or purple flowers in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
