Voacanga dolichocalyx

Voacanga dolichocalyx, commonly known as the African blackwood tree, is a species native to tropical Africa.

It is a medium-sized evergreen tree that can grow up to 20 meters tall, characterized by its dark, heavy wood that is highly valued for its density and durability. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers followed by elongated, woody pods containing seeds. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate, with a glossy green surface.

The tree is also known for its medicinal properties, with various parts used in traditional medicine.
