Vitex quinata

Vitex quinata, commonly known as Chinese vitex, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate with a serrated margin.

Vitex quinata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
