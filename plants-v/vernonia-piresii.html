Vernonia piresii

Vernonia piresii, commonly known as the Pires' ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Vernonia piresii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
