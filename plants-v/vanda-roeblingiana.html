Vanda roeblingiana

Vanda roeblingiana, commonly known as the roebling's vanda, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, showy flowers with a violet or purple hue, often adorned with contrasting white or yellow markings. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

Vanda roeblingiana is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
