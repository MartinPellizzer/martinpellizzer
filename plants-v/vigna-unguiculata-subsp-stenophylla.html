Vigna unguiculata subsp. stenophylla

Vigna unguiculata subsp.

stenophylla, commonly known as the slender cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to Africa. It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and small, yellow flowers.

The plant produces pods that contain edible seeds, which are rich in protein and often used in various cuisines. It thrives in warm climates and is cultivated for both its nutritional value and agricultural benefits.

Additionally, it has the ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, improving soil fertility.
