Valeriana aberdarica

Valeriana aberdarica, commonly known as the mountain valerian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, often pink or white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or well-drained soils and thrives in temperate climates. Its rhizomes are used in traditional medicine for their sedative and calming properties.
