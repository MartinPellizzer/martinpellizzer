Vittaria forrestiana

Vittaria forrestiana, commonly known as the Forest Fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Vittariaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that are typically light green with a subtle silvery hue. The plant is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including countries like Thailand and Malaysia. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on tree trunks or rocks in the understory of forests.

Vittaria forrestiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant due to its aesthetic qualities.
