Veronica polita subsp. ludwigiana

Veronica polita subsp.

ludwigiana, commonly known as the Ludwig's speedwell, is a perennial herb native to South Africa. It features slender, upright stems covered in fine, soft hairs.

The plant produces small, blue to purple flowers arranged in clusters at the tips of the stems. Its leaves are narrow, lance-shaped, and often covered with a fine layer of white wax.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
