Vernonia galamensis var. lushotoensis

Vernonia galamensis var. lushotoensis, commonly known as the Lushoto Vernonia, is a flowering plant native to Tanzania.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant is typically found in open woodlands and grasslands, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, while the flowers are yellow with a central disk.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
