Veronica teucrium f. lasiocalyx

Veronica teucrium f. lasiocalyx, commonly known as the woolly speedwell, is a flowering plant belonging to the Plantaginaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, blue or purple flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It is native to Europe and is often found in moist, shaded woodland areas.
