Vaccinium yunnanense

Vaccinium yunnanense, commonly known as Yunnan blueberry, is a species of evergreen shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces bell-shaped, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring. Its fruit is a small, dark blue berry that is edible and has a tart flavor.

It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions of Yunnan Province.
