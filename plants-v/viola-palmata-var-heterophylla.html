Viola palmata var. heterophylla

Viola palmata var. heterophylla, commonly known as the variable violet, is a species of flowering plant in the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its variable leaf morphology, with leaves that can range from ovate to heart-shaped depending on the environment. The plant produces showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in early spring. It is native to parts of North America and thrives in moist, shaded woodland habitats.

Its adaptability and ornamental value make it a popular choice for native plant gardens.
