Verticordia lindleyi subsp. lindleyi

Verticordia lindleyi subsp.

lindleyi, commonly known as the red-stemmed verticordia, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia. It is characterized by its distinctive, urn-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red.

The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in heathland environments. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces seed pods that split open to release its seeds.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
