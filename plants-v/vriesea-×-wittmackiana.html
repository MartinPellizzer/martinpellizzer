Vriesea × wittmackiana

Vriesea × wittmackiana, commonly known as the Wittmack's Vriesea, is a hybrid bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful inflorescence that can range from red to orange and yellow. The plant features a rosette of narrow, waxy leaves that are typically green with a bluish tint. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This species is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
