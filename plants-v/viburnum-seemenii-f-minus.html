Viburnum seemenii f. minus

Viburnum seemenii f. minus, commonly known as the dwarf viburnum, is a compact shrub characterized by its small, rounded leaves and clusters of white to pale pink flowers.

It is a compact form of the species, making it suitable for smaller garden spaces. The plant produces bright red berries in the fall, which are attractive to birds. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is relatively low maintenance.

Its ornamental value comes from its foliage, flowers, and seasonal fruit display.
