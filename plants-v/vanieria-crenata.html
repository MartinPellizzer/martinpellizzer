Vanieria crenata

Vanieria crenata, commonly known as the sea hake or sea hakegrass, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Poaceae.

It is a perennial grass that thrives in coastal and brackish environments, often found in tidal zones and salt marshes. Vanieria crenata plays a role in stabilizing coastal soils and providing habitat for various marine and terrestrial organisms.
