Viola philippica var. philippica

Viola philippica var. philippica, commonly known as the Philippine violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Philippines.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves with a deep green color and produces clusters of small, delicate flowers in shades of purple and violet. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its erect stems and showy blooms.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
