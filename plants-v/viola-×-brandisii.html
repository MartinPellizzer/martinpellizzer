Viola × brandisii

Viola × brandisii, commonly known as the hybrid violet, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Violaceae family.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Viola lutea and Viola tricolor, showcasing a combination of traits from both parents. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces showy flowers in shades of purple, yellow, and white. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas or garden settings.

Viola × brandisii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to naturalize in suitable habitats.
