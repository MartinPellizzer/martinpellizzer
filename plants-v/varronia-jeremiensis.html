Varronia jeremiensis

Varronia jeremiensis, commonly known as the Jeremi's varronia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in humid, lowland areas. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate or elliptical in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered, often in axillary or terminal inflorescences.

The fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.
