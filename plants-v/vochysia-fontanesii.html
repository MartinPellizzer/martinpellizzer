Vochysia fontanesii

Vochysia fontanesii, commonly known as the pink velvet tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a velvety appearance. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, round fruits that are edible. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Vochysia fontanesii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens.
