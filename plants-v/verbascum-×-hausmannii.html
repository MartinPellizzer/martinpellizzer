Verbascum × hausmannii

Verbascum × hausmannii, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a flowering plant in the family Scrophulariaceae.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Verbascum blattaria and Verbascum phlomoides. The plant is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, fuzzy, gray-green leaves. It produces showy, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer.

Verbascum × hausmannii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to well-drained soils in full sun.
