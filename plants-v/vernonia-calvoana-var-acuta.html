Vernonia calvoana var. acuta

Vernonia calvoana var. acuta, commonly known as the acute vernonia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Mexico and Central America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
