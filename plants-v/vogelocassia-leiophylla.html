Vogelocassia leiophylla

Vogelocassia leiophylla, commonly known as the smooth-leaved corymbiferous plant, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, clustered growth habit and elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are typically smooth and glossy. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in corymbs, which bloom in the summer months. Vogelocassia leiophylla thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its adaptability and aesthetic appeal contribute to its use in both landscape design and horticultural collections.
