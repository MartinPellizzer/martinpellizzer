Verbena radicata var. glabra

Verbena radicata var. glabra, commonly known as smooth verbena, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that produces vibrant clusters of blue to violet flowers. The plant features slender, branching stems and small, opposite leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its showy blooms attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, contributing to its ecological value.
