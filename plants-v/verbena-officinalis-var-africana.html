Verbena officinalis var. africana

Verbena officinalis var. africana, commonly known as African vervain, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces spikes of small, blue to purple flowers that bloom from late spring to early autumn. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
