Verbascum puniceum

Verbascum puniceum, commonly known as the crimson mullein, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems covered in soft, woolly hairs. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are large, broad, and covered in a dense layer of silky hairs.

Verbascum puniceum is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
