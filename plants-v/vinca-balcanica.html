Vinca balcanica

Vinca balcanica, commonly known as Balkan vinca, is a species of flowering plant native to southeastern Europe.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces vibrant blue to purple flowers throughout the summer. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and is often used in ornamental gardening for its hardiness and aesthetic appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Vinca balcanica is also valued for its ability to tolerate drought and poor soil conditions.
