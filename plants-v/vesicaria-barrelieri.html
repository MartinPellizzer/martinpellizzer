Vesicaria barrelieri

Vesicaria barrelieri, commonly known as the Barrelier's bladder, is a unique succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, swollen stems that store water, adapting to arid environments. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are often reduced to small, scale-like structures, and it thrives in well-drained soil.

Vesicaria barrelieri is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to flourish in dry conditions.
