Vitex taihangensis

Vitex taihangensis, commonly known as the Taihang vitex, is a shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with serrated margins. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically blue or purple in color, which bloom in summer. Its fruits are dry, three-lobed capsules that contain several seeds.

Vitex taihangensis is often found in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments.
