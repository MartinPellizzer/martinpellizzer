Vaccinium palawanense

Vaccinium palawanense, commonly known as the Palawan huckleberry, is a species of evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes many acid-loving plants. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and mountainous regions. Its leaves are elliptical, glossy, and dark green, while its small, bell-shaped flowers are usually pink or white.
