Vitex kwangsiensis

Vitex kwangsiensis, commonly known as Guangxi vitex, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern China.

It belongs to the verbena family (Verbenaceae) and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to purple, typically blooming in summer. Its fruit is a dry, two-lobed capsule that releases small, wind-dispersed seeds.

Vitex kwangsiensis is often found in moist, well-drained soils in forested areas.
