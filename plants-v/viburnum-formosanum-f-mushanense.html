Viburnum formosanum f. mushanense

Viburnum formosanum f. mushanense, commonly known as the Mushan viburnum, is a shrub native to eastern Asia, particularly China.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers in the spring, which are followed by bright red berries in the fall. Its foliage is typically dark green, with some varieties showing variegation.
