Vangueria longiflora

Vangueria longiflora, commonly known as the long-flowered vangueria, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. Its flowering stems are typically slender and branched.

Vangueria longiflora is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
