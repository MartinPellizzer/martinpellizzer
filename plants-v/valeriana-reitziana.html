Valeriana reitziana

Valeriana reitziana, commonly known as Reitz's valerian, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in clusters. Valeriana reitziana prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a suitable candidate for garden cultivation in warm climates.
