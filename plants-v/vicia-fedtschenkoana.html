Vicia fedtschenkoana

Vicia fedtschenkoana, commonly known as the Fedtschenko vetch, is a rare flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions.
