Veronicastrum album

Veronicastrum album, commonly known as blue mistflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can grow up to 6 feet in height. The plant produces clusters of small, pale blue to white flowers that bloom in late summer. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often gray-green in color.

It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
