Vaccinium macrocarpon var. intermedium

Vaccinium macrocarpon var. intermedium, commonly known as the intermediate blueberry, is a variant of the highbush blueberry.

It is characterized by its intermediate growth habit between the upright highbush and the trailing lowbush blueberry species. This variety typically produces medium-sized, dark blue berries with a sweet-tart flavor. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of edible fruit.
