Virgilia rubiginosa

Virgilia rubiginosa, commonly known as the red virgilia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange-yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are usually narrow and hairy, adapted to reduce water loss in arid conditions.

Virgilia rubiginosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
