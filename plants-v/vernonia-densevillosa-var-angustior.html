Vernonia densevillosa var. angustior

Vernonia densevillosa var. angustior, commonly known as narrowleaf ironweed, is a perennial herb native to eastern North America.

It features tall, slender stems covered in dense, grayish-white hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, spiky yellow flowers that bloom in late summer to early fall. Its leaves are lance-shaped, with a rough texture and a narrow, pointed tip.

This species is often found in moist to wet habitats such as swamps, marshes, and along stream banks.
