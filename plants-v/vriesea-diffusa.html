Vriesea diffusa

Vriesea diffusa, commonly known as the banana plantain, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, broad, banana-like leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant produces strikingly colorful inflorescences with vibrant orange and red bracts, which are often more showy than the actual flowers. Vriesea diffusa is typically grown as a houseplant due to its preference for warm, humid conditions.

It requires well-draining soil and regular watering to thrive.
