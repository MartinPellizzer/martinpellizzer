Vitex simplicifolia var. vogelii

Vitex simplicifolia var. vogelii, commonly known as Vogel's chaste tree, is a shrub native to parts of Africa.

It features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. Its fruit is a dry, woody capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
