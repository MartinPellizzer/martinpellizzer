Vriesea barilletii

Vriesea barilletii, commonly known as the Barillet's Vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, sword-shaped leaves that form a rosette, creating a central cup to collect rainwater. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences that bear numerous showy, colorful bracts, often in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Vriesea barilletii is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
