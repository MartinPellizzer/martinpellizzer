Viola edanoi

Viola edanoi, commonly known as the Edan violet, is a flowering plant belonging to the Violaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe, particularly in areas with moist, shaded habitats. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of purple and violet. Viola edanoi is often found in woodlands and along stream banks, where it thrives in cool, humid conditions.

Its blooming period typically occurs in spring, adding a touch of color to its natural environment.
