Veronica stelleri

Veronica stelleri, commonly known as the starflower, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the plantain family (Plantaginaceae) and is characterized by its small, star-shaped blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Veronica stelleri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
