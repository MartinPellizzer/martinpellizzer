Viola cenisia

Viola cenisia, commonly known as the snow viol, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces delicate, violet-blue flowers with five petals. The plant typically grows to a height of about 10-20 centimeters and thrives in cool, moist environments. It is often found in woodland areas and along stream banks.

Its flowers bloom in early spring, adding a touch of color to the landscape during the colder months.
