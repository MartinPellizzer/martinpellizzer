Vernonia lorentzii

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Vernonia lorentzii is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in wet conditions, making it a valuable species for wetland restoration projects.
