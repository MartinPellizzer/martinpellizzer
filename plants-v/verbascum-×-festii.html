Verbascum × festii

Verbascum × festii, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a tall, herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its large, fuzzy, gray-green leaves and showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant belongs to the genus Verbascum, which includes several species of mullein known for their medicinal and ornamental uses. Verbascum × festii is often cultivated for its striking appearance and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.

It is also valued in traditional herbal medicine for its potential respiratory benefits.
