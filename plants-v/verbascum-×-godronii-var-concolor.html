Verbascum × godronii var. concolor

Verbascum × godronii var. concolor, commonly known as the smooth mullein, is a hybrid plant belonging to the genus Verbascum in the family Scrophulariaceae.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, fuzzy, gray-green leaves that are densely covered in soft hairs. The flowers are typically yellow, arranged in a loose spike, and bloom in late summer. This variety is distinguished by its smooth, non-woody stems and uniform leaf coloration.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is native to regions of Europe and western Asia.
