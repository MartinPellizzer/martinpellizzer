Vanda yamiensis

Vanda yamiensis, commonly known as the Yami Vanda, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, large, and often fragrant flowers that bloom in a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and pink. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or rocks, showcasing its epiphytic nature. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Vanda yamiensis is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological role in its native habitats.
