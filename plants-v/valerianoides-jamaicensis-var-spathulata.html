Valerianoides jamaicensis var. spathulata

Valerianoides jamaicensis var. spathulata, commonly known as Jamaican valerian, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the family Valerianaceae and is characterized by its aromatic, glandular leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and traditional medicine.
