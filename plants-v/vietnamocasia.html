Vietnamocasia

Vietnamocasia, commonly known as the Vietnamese orchid, is a genus of flowering plants native to Southeast Asia, particularly Vietnam.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its elegant, fragrant flowers. The plants typically have strap-like leaves and produce spikes of showy, colorful blooms. Vietnamocasia species are often found in forested areas and are appreciated for their ornamental value.

Due to habitat loss, many species are threatened with extinction.
