Vernonia rivularis

Vernonia rivularis, commonly known as the river vernonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows along riverbanks and in moist, well-drained soils. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually yellow or greenish-yellow in color. Its leaves are usually narrow and opposite, with a rough texture.

Vernonia rivularis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
