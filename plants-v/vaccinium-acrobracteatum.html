Vaccinium acrobracteatum

Vaccinium acrobracteatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved huckleberry, is a species of shrub native to the temperate regions of South America.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes many acid-loving plants such as blueberries and cranberries. The plant thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in forested areas.
