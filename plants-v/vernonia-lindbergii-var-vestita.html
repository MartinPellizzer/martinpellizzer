Vernonia lindbergii var. vestita

Vernonia lindbergii var. vestita, commonly known as the velvet Vernonia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive, soft appearance. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique texture and color make it a striking addition to coastal and xeriscaped gardens.
