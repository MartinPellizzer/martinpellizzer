Verbascum delphicum

Verbascum delphicum, commonly known as the Delphic mullein, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Scrophulariaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, fuzzy leaves. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in rocky or coastal areas.

Verbascum delphicum is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
