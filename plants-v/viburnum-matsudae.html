Viburnum matsudae

Viburnum matsudae, commonly known as the Japanese snowberry, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features small, white, clustered flowers that bloom in late spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and well-drained soil.

Viburnum matsudae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its seasonal interest.
