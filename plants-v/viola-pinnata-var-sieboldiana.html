Viola pinnata var. sieboldiana

Viola pinnata var. sieboldiana, commonly known as Siebold's violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It features heart-shaped to ovate leaves with a distinctive pinnate arrangement, and its flowers are typically violet or purple in color. The plant produces small, showy blooms that appear in early spring, often on tall, slender stems. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or shaded areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
