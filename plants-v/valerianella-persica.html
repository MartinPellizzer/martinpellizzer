Valerianella persica

Valerianella persica, commonly known as Persian valerian, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a low-growing herb with small, pink or white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has finely divided, grass-like leaves and thrives in well-drained, sandy soils. It is often found in dry, open habitats such as rocky slopes and grasslands.

Valerianella persica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
