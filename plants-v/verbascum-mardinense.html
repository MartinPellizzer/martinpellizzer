Verbascum mardinense

Verbascum mardinense, commonly known as the Mardin mullein, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Turkey.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae and is characterized by its tall, hairy stems and large, velvety leaves. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.
