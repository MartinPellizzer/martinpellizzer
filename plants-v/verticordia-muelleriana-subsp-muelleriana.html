Verticordia muelleriana subsp. muelleriana

Verticordia muelleriana subsp.

muelleriana, commonly known as the Mueller's wand, is a striking evergreen shrub native to Western Australia. It is characterized by its vibrant red or orange flowers that appear in late summer to early autumn.

The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces distinctive, finger-like flower spikes. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal areas.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological importance, it is a valued species in both horticulture and conservation efforts.
