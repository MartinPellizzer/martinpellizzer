Vitex congesta

Vitex congesta, commonly known as the clustered chaste tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters tall, characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and showy, clustered panicles of purple or white flowers. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain several seeds, and it is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides.

Vitex congesta is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
