Viburnum chingii var. limitaneum

Viburnum chingii var. limitaneum, commonly known as the Chinese snowberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, white, clustered flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by red berries that persist into winter. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value comes from its seasonal color changes and persistent fruits.
