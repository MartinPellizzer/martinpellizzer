Vangueriella vanguerioides

Vangueriella vanguerioides, commonly known as the African violet, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features rosettes of velvety, dark green leaves with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Vangueriella vanguerioides is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
