Valeriana arnottiana

Valeriana arnottiana, commonly known as the New Zealand valerian, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or alpine environments. The plant features upright stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant white or pink flowers. It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive root system.

Valeriana arnottiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
