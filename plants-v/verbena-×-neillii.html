Verbena × neillii

Verbena × neillii, commonly known as New Zealand verbena, is a hybrid shrub native to New Zealand.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in a fine layer of white hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, five-petaled flowers in shades of blue, purple, and pink, which bloom throughout the summer. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Verbena × neillii is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
