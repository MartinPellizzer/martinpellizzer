Verbascum macrantherum

Verbascum macrantherum, commonly known as the tall mullein, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are velvety and covered in soft, downy hairs, which help reduce water loss.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and wildflower landscapes.
