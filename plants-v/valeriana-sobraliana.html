Valeriana sobraliana

Valeriana sobraliana, commonly known as Sobralian valerian, is a perennial herb native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, often fragrant leaves and flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its flowers are small, clustered in umbels, and are usually pink or white in color.
