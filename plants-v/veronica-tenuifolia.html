Veronica tenuifolia

Veronica tenuifolia, commonly known as slender speedwell, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial with small, blue or violet flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges or along stream banks.

Veronica tenuifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in garden landscapes.
