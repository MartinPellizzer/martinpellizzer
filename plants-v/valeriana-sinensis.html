Valeriana sinensis

Valeriana sinensis, commonly known as Chinese valerian, is a flowering plant native to Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of small, fragrant white or pinkish flowers. The plant belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is known for its aromatic foliage. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their sedative and calming properties.

Valeriana sinensis thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland areas.
