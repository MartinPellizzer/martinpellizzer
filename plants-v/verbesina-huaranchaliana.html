Verbesina huaranchaliana

Verbesina huaranchaliana, commonly known as the Huaranchal goldenrod, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Peru.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small yellow daisies. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often woody at the base.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its vibrant yellow flowers.
