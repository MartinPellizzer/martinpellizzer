Vangueria rhodesiana

Vangueria rhodesiana, commonly known as Rhodesian vangueria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in umbels, and its leaves are simple, alternate, and usually have a rough texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
