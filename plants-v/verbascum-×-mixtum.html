Verbascum × mixtum

Verbascum × mixtum, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the genus Verbascum, which includes several species of flowering plants known for their large, showy flowers. The hybrid is characterized by its fuzzy, velvety leaves and tall, branching stems that bear clusters of yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
