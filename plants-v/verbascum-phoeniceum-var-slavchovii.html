Verbascum phoeniceum var. slavchovii

Verbascum phoeniceum var. slavchovii, commonly known as the Slavchovii mullein, is a perennial herb native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It belongs to the genus Verbascum, which includes many species of flowering plants known for their fuzzy, velvety leaves. This particular variety is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used traditionally in herbal medicine.
