Ventilago monoica

Ventilago monoica, commonly known as the African snowball, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring dense clusters of small, white, fluffy flowers that bloom in winter. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are elliptical in shape and covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are borne in spherical inflorescences, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Ventilago monoica is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a hardy and drought-tolerant species.
