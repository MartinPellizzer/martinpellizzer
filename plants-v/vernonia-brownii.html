Vernonia brownii

Vernonia brownii, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant is known for its hard, dense wood, which is highly valued for construction and fuel. Its leaves are typically alternate and ovate, with a rough texture.

Vernonia brownii thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.
