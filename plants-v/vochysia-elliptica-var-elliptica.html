Vochysia elliptica var. elliptica

Vochysia elliptica var. elliptica, commonly known as the Brazilian cherry or velvet tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The tree is also valued for its durable wood, which is used in furniture and construction.
