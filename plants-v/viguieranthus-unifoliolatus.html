Viguieranthus unifoliolatus

Viguieranthus unifoliolatus, commonly known as the single leaf vine, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its unique morphology, featuring a single leaflet per leaf, which gives it its scientific name. The plant produces striking, showy flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It is a climbing plant that thrives in tropical and subtropical climates.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
