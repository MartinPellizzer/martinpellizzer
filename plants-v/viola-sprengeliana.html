Viola sprengeliana

Viola sprengeliana, commonly known as the Sprengel violet, is a species of flowering plant in the family Violaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central and Southern Europe, including parts of Italy, Austria, and the Balkans. The plant produces showy, violet-colored flowers with five petals, often featuring distinct markings. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Viola sprengeliana is typically found in moist, woodland habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
