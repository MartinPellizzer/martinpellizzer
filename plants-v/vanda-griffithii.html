Vanda griffithii

Vanda griffithii, commonly known as the giant orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 12 inches in diameter, often in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has thick, fleshy roots that help it absorb moisture from the air, which is essential for its survival in its natural habitat. Vanda griffithii thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

Its unique appearance and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
