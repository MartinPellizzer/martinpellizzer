Visenia supina

Visenia supina, commonly known as the prostrate visenia, is a low-growing shrub native to certain regions of South America.

It features woody stems that lie close to the ground, often forming dense mats. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Its leaves are simple, elliptical, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Visenia supina is adapted to thrive in well-drained, sunny environments, making it a hardy plant in suitable climates.
