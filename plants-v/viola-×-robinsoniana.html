Viola × robinsoniana

Viola × robinsoniana, commonly known as the hybrid violet, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Violaceae family.

It is a cross between Viola tricolor and Viola cornuta, resulting in a plant with a compact growth habit and vibrant, bi- or tri-colored flowers. The foliage is typically heart-shaped with a velvety texture, and the plant thrives in cool, moist environments.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to naturalize in suitable conditions.
