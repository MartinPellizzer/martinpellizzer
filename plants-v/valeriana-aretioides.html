Valeriana aretioides

Valeriana aretioides, commonly known as the pink valerian, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb with upright, branching stems and small, pinkish-purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has aromatic leaves and roots, which are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Valeriana aretioides is valued for its ornamental beauty and historical medicinal uses.
