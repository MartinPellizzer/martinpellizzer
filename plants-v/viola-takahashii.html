Viola takahashii

Viola takahashii, commonly known as the Takahashi violet, is a species of flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, heart-shaped leaves and small, showy flowers. The plant typically blooms in early spring, producing violet-blue petals with yellow markings. It thrives in woodland environments and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Viola takahashii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
