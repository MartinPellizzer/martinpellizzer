Vesicarex collumanthus

Vesicarex collumanthus, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to the humid forests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage and delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Vesicarex collumanthus is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
