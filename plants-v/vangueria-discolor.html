Vangueria discolor

Vangueria discolor, commonly known as the African star-of-Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils, often found in coastal areas. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and the plant is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
