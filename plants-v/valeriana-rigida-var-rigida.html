Valeriana rigida var. rigida

Valeriana rigida var. rigida, commonly known as rigid valerian, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features a robust root system and upright, branched stems that can grow up to 1 meter in height. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers in dense, spike-like clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a distinctive, slightly aromatic scent.

Valeriana rigida var. rigida is known for its medicinal properties, particularly for its calming effects.
