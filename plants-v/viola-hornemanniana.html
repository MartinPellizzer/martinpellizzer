Viola hornemanniana

Viola hornemanniana, commonly known as the Hornemann violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features heart-shaped, hairy leaves and produces showy, violet-blue flowers with five petals. The plant typically grows to a height of about 10-20 centimeters and blooms in late spring to early summer. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
