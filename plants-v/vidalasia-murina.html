Vidalasia murina

Vidalasia murina, commonly known as the rock cress, is a flowering plant native to rocky and arid regions.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant typically grows close to the ground, with small, clustered leaves and yellow flowers. It is adapted to survive in harsh environments due to its drought-resistant features.

Vidalasia murina plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small insects.
