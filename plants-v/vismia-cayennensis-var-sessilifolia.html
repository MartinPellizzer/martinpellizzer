Vismia cayennensis var. sessilifolia

Vismia cayennensis var. sessilifolia, commonly known as the Cayenne vismia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, leathery leaves that are typically arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
