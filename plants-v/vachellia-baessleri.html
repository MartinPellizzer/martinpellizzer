Vachellia baessleri

Vachellia baessleri, commonly known as the white thorn, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its legume fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. Vachellia baessleri is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient component of its native ecosystems.
