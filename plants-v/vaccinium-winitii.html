Vaccinium winitii

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes many acid-loving plants such as blueberries and cranberries. The plant produces small, dark blue or black berries that are edible and have a tart flavor. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.
