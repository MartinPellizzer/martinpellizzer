Vriesea nocturna

Vriesea nocturna, commonly known as the night bromeliad, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, upward-facing inflorescence that blooms at night, emitting a faint fragrance. The plant has a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that collect water, forming a central pool. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity to flourish.

Vriesea nocturna is a popular ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and nocturnal blooming habit.
