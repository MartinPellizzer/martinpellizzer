Verbesina sinensis

Verbesina sinensis, commonly known as Chinese ironweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to China.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has rough, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in moist meadows and along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
