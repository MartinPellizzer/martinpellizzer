Viola umbraticola var. umbraticola

Viola umbraticola var. umbraticola, commonly known as the shadow viola, is a perennial flowering plant native to shaded woodland environments.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and produces delicate, violet-blue flowers in early spring. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom in low-light conditions.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique addition to shade gardens and native plant collections.
