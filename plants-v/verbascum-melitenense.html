Verbascum melitenense

Verbascum melitenense, commonly known as the Melitene mullein, is a flowering plant native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the genus Verbascum, which includes numerous species known for their fuzzy, woolly leaves. This particular species features tall, erect stems that bear clusters of yellow-orange flowers. The leaves are typically broad and covered in a dense layer of soft, fuzzy hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.
