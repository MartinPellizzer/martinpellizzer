Vignea norvegica

Vignea norvegica, commonly known as the Norwegian vine, is a climbing plant native to northern Europe.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers are small, greenish-white, and clustered in loose inflorescences. The plant produces small, spherical fruits that contain one or two seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Vignea norvegica is valued for its ornamental qualities and historical use in traditional medicine.
