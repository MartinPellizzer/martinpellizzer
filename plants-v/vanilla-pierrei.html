Vanilla pierrei

Vanilla pierrei, commonly known as the French vanilla or the Madagascar vanilla, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing vines and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces fragrant, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Vanilla pierrei is known for its vanilla-like aroma, which is harvested from its seed pods.

However, it is less commonly used in commercial vanilla production compared to Vanilla planifolia.
