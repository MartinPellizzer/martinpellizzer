Vernonia milne-redheadii

Vernonia milne-redheadii, commonly known as the redhead vernonia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils. The plant produces small, yellowish to orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are usually narrow and slightly hairy, contributing to its drought-resistant characteristics.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
