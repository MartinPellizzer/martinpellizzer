Vanda hastifera var. gibbsiae

Vanda hastifera var. gibbsiae, commonly known as the Gibbs' vanda, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, strap-like leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant is epiphytic, often growing on trees or rocks in humid tropical environments. It requires bright light and well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
