Virola Panamensis

Virola panamensis is a remarkable tropical tree native to Central and South America. Known for its massive, buttressed roots and towering height, it thrives in humid lowland forests. The tree produces large, aromatic fruits that are an important food source for many animals. Its wood is highly valued for its durability and unique grain patterns. This species plays a crucial role in maintaining biodiversity and ecosystem health in its native habitats.
