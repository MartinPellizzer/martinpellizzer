Vatica sarawakensis

Vatica sarawakensis, commonly known as the Sarawak ironwood, is a tropical tree native to Borneo.

It belongs to the family Dipterocarpaceae and is characterized by its dense, hard wood that is highly valued for its durability. The plant typically grows in lowland rainforests and is known for its tall, straight trunk and dark green, glossy leaves.

It produces small, fragrant flowers and is an important component of the region's biodiversity.
