Vendredia

Vendredia, commonly known as the golden bell vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. Vendredia thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant.
