Viola × kerneri

Viola × kerneri, commonly known as the English violet, is a hybrid species within the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, violet-colored flowers that typically bloom in early spring. The plant is native to parts of Europe and has been cultivated for its ornamental value. It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for garden borders and woodland gardens.

Viola × kerneri is known for its resilience and ability to thrive in a variety of temperate climates.
