Verbascum spectabile var. isandrum

Verbascum spectabile var. isandrum, commonly known as the Isandrum mullein, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features tall, hairy stems that can grow up to 2 meters in height, supporting large, gray-green, fuzzy leaves. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers in a dense spike at the top of the stem, which bloom from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, with a distinctive velvety texture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
