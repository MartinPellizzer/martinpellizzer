Vilfa africana

Vilfa africana, commonly known as African wild lettuce, is a herbaceous plant native to Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and ragworts. The plant features serrated, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported anti-inflammatory properties.

The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland areas.
