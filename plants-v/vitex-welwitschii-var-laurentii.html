Vitex welwitschii var. laurentii

Vitex welwitschii var. laurentii, commonly known as the African bluecurls, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flower clusters that resemble curls, which bloom in summer. The plant has aromatic leaves and is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and sandy environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
