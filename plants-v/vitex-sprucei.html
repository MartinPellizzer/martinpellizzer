Vitex sprucei

Vitex sprucei, commonly known as the spruce vitex, is a shrub native to parts of East Africa.

It belongs to the verbena family (Verbenaceae) and is characterized by its aromatic, opposite leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in forest margins or rocky habitats.

Vitex sprucei is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some local communities.
