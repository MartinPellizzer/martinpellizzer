Vernonia cotoneaster

Vernonia cotoneaster, commonly known as the common vernonia, is a shrub native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, typically blooming in the summer months. The plant has smooth, often reddish stems and lance-shaped leaves that are usually arranged oppositely. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or sandy environments.

Vernonia cotoneaster is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
