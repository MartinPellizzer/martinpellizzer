Vernonia brevifolia var. ericifolia

Vernonia brevifolia var. ericifolia, commonly known as the Ericifolius Vernonia, is a shrub native to the southern United States.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its botanical features make it a valuable plant for both ecological and ornamental purposes.
