Viola blanda var. violascens

Viola blanda var. violascens, commonly known as the common violet, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces clusters of small, delicate flowers in shades of purple and violet. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade. It is often found in woodland areas and along the edges of gardens.

Its flowers are not only ornamental but also attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
