Vernonia benguetensis

Vernonia benguetensis, commonly known as the Benguet Vernonia, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and forest edges. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers range from yellow to orange in color.

Vernonia benguetensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential ecological benefits in local ecosystems.
