Veronica chartonii

Veronica chartonii, commonly known as the Charton's speedwell, is a perennial flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of blue or violet flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
