Viticella

Viticella, commonly known as the winter jasmine, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers in early spring, often before the leaves emerge. Its botanical name, *Kolkwitzia amabilis*, reflects its ornamental value and widespread cultivation.

Viticella is often used in landscaping for its early blooming and compact growth habit.
