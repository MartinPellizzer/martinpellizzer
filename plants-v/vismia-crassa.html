Vismia crassa

Vismia crassa, commonly known as the rough vismia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to parts of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves and distinctive, clustered inflorescences. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are followed by bright red, fleshy berries. Vismia crassa is often found in moist, shaded habitats within lowland rainforests.

It has been traditionally used in local medicine for its purported health benefits.
