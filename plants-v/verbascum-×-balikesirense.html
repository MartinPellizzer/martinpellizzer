Verbascum × balikesirense

Verbascum × balikesirense, commonly known as the Turkish mullein.

This hybrid plant is a member of the Scrophulariaceae family and is native to Turkey. It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, fuzzy, gray-green leaves. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

It is often found in rocky or dry habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
