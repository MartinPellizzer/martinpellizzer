Viola tricolor f. purpurascens

Viola tricolor f. purpurascens, commonly known as the Purple Heart Violet, is a horticultural form of the common violet.

It is characterized by its striking violet and white petals with a distinctive purple heart-shaped marking. This perennial plant belongs to the Violaceae family and is native to Europe. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking colorful, shade-tolerant plants.
