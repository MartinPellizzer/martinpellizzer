Vriesea barbosae

Vriesea barbosae, commonly known as the sword fern bromeliad, is a striking tropical plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, strap-like leaves that form a rosette structure. The plant produces striking inflorescences with colorful bracts that can range from red to orange. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Vriesea barbosae is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
