Vernonia misera

Vernonia misera, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dark, hard wood, which is highly valued for its strength and durability. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, woodland areas.

The plant is also noted for its medicinal properties, with various parts used in traditional remedies.
