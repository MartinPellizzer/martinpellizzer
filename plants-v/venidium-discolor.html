Venidium discolor

Venidium discolor, commonly known as the whiteleaf venidium, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to the southern United States, particularly Texas and New Mexico, where it thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant is characterized by its small, white to pale green leaves and clusters of yellow flowers. Its seed pods are slender and contain several small, dark seeds.

Venidium discolor is often found in rocky soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.
