Vernonia goetzenii

Vernonia goetzenii, commonly known as the African ironweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow to orange in color.

Vernonia goetzenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local medicine.
