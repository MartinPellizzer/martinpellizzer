Vitex merrillii

Vitex merrillii, commonly known as the Chinese vitex, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically purple or blue in color, arranged in clusters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate with a rough texture.

The botanical features of Vitex merrillii make it a distinctive species within the Vitex genus.
