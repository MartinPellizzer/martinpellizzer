Verbena chamaedryfolia f. melindroides

Verbena chamaedryfolia f. melindroides, commonly known as the Melindroides verbena, is a compact, flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, tubular flowers that are typically a vivid shade of blue or purple, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has narrow, upright leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a delicate appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact growth habit and extended blooming period make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
