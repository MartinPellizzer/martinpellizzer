Vriesea leucophylla

Vriesea leucophylla, commonly known as the white-leafed vriesea, is a species of flowering plant in the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its striking, sword-shaped leaves that are typically light green to white in color. The plant produces inflorescences with bright orange flowers that bloom during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Vriesea leucophylla is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
