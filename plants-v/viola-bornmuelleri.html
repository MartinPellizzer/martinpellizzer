Viola bornmuelleri

Viola bornmuelleri, commonly known as the Bornmueller's violet, is a species of flowering plant in the Violaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of South America, particularly in Chile and Argentina. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically violet or purple in color, with five petals arranged in a symmetrical pattern. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in a fine layer of soft, fuzzy hair.

Viola bornmuelleri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is appreciated for its ability to thrive in cooler, shaded environments.
