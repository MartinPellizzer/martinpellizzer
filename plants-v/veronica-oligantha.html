Veronica oligantha

Veronica oligantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved speedwell, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions. The plant produces small, blue or purple flowers that bloom in spring and early summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Veronica oligantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate beauty.
