Verbascum niveum

Verbascum niveum, commonly known as the snow绒草, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its soft, fuzzy leaves and showy yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments. Its hairy stems and leaves help reduce water loss, adapting it to arid conditions.

Verbascum niveum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
