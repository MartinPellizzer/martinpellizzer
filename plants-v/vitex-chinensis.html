Vitex chinensis

Vitex chinensis, commonly known as Chinese vitex, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and showy, clustered purple or violet flowers. The plant produces small, spherical fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Vitex chinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
