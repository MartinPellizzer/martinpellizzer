Viola tricolor var. lacticolor

Viola tricolor var. lacticolor, commonly known as the cream violet, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and showy, colorful flowers. The flowers typically display a combination of white, cream, and purple hues, creating a striking visual effect. This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.

It thrives in cool, moist conditions and prefers partial to full shade.
