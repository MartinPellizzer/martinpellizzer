Valeriana roylei

Valeriana roylei, commonly known as the Himalayan valerian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine habitats, thriving in cool, moist environments. Its rhizomatous root system is notable for containing compounds used in traditional medicine.

Valeriana roylei is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly for its calming effects.
