Vitis ruyuanensis

Vitis ruyuanensis, commonly known as the Ruyuan grapevine, is a species native to China.

It is a woody vine belonging to the Vitaceae family, characterized by its climbing habit and compound leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its fruits are berries that mature to a dark purple or black color, often used in local traditional medicine.

Vitis ruyuanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
