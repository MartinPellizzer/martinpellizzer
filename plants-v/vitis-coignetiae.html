Vitis coignetiae

Vitis coignetiae, commonly known as the black beauty grape, is a species of flowering plant in the Vitaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its vigorous growth and deep green, compound leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by dark blue to black berries. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and unique fruit.

Vitis coignetiae is also valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
