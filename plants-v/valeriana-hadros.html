Valeriana hadros

Valeriana hadros, commonly known as the giant valerian, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, sturdy stems that can grow up to 2 meters in height. The plant features large, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense spikes, typically appearing in shades of pink or white.

Valeriana hadros is known for its aromatic foliage and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens.
