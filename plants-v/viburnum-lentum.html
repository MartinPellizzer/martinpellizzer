Viburnum lentum

Viburnum lentum, commonly known as the European snowball tree, is a deciduous shrub native to Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of white, fluffy flowers that appear in early spring, giving it a striking visual display. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that turn yellow in the fall, adding to its seasonal appeal. Viburnum lentum typically grows to a height of 3 to 6 meters and prefers well-drained, fertile soils.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of conditions.
