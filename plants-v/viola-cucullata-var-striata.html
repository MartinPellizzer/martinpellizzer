Viola cucullata var. striata

Viola cucullata var. striata, commonly known as the striped pansy, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive dark green upper surface and a lighter underside. The plant produces violet or purple flowers with a yellow center, often adorned with dark markings. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is commonly found in woodland areas.

This variant is notable for its ornamental value and adaptability to garden cultivation.
