Viola langsdorffii var. subacaulis

Viola langsdorffii var. subacaulis, commonly known as the slender violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are often hairy and arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of violet or blue, typically with five petals. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for shade gardens and botanical collections.
