Vigna minima f. linealis

Vigna minima f. linealis, commonly known as the narrow-leafed wing bean, is a botanical variant of the wing bean species.

It is a climbing legume characterized by its slender, linear leaves and small, pod-like fruits. The plant is native to tropical regions of South America and is cultivated for its edible seeds and pods. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The narrow leaves and compact growth habit make it a distinctive and ornamental addition to botanical gardens.
