Vaccinium uliginosum var. occidentale

Vaccinium uliginosum var. occidentale, commonly known as the western blueberry shrub, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to western North America.

It produces small, dark blue berries that are rich in antioxidants and often used in culinary applications. The plant thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is typically found in wetlands, bogs, and other moist environments. Its leaves are elliptical, leathery, and remain green throughout the year.

The species is valued for both its ecological role and its edible fruits.
