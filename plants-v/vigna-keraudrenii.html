Vigna keraudrenii

Vigna keraudrenii, commonly known as the Keraudren's bean, is a species of legume native to tropical Africa.

It is a herbaceous annual plant that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces pods that contain several small, round seeds. It is characterized by its ability to thrive in poor soils and its tolerance to drought conditions.

Vigna keraudrenii is often found in savannas and along riverbanks, where it plays a role in soil stabilization and provides forage for livestock.
