Vitex glaberrima

Vitex glaberrima, commonly known as African pride of Barbados, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically in shades of purple or white, which bloom during the dry season. Its fruit is a smooth, dry capsule containing several seeds.

Vitex glaberrima is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
