Vaccinium carlesii

Vaccinium carlesii, commonly known as the Carles's blueberry, is a species of evergreen shrub native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like blueberries and cranberries. The plant produces small, acidic berries that are typically dark blue or black in color. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in cloud forests and mountainous areas.
