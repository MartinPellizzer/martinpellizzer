Vernonia bontocensis

Vernonia bontocensis, commonly known as Bontoc ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas and has lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. Its flowers are usually purple or blue, arranged in dense, conical clusters.

Vernonia bontocensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
