Valeriana sphaerophora

Valeriana sphaerophora, commonly known as the globe valerian, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its spherical, clustered inflorescence that gives it its common name.
