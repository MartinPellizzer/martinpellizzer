Vincetoxicum sublanceolatum f. albiflorum

Vincetoxicum sublanceolatum f. albiflorum, commonly known as the whiteflowered vincetoxicum, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Apocynaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in the summer. This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in gardens for its unique floral display.
