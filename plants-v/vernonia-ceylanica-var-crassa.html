Vernonia ceylanica var. crassa

Vernonia ceylanica var. crassa, commonly known as the rough vernonia, is a shrub native to Sri Lanka.

It is characterized by its rough, hairy leaves and yellow-green flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a distinctive coarse texture.

The species is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
