Vaccinium stenanthum

Vaccinium stenanthum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved blueberry, is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes many acid-loving plants. The plant produces small, edible berries that are typically blue or purple in color. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, and it thrives in well-drained, acidic soils.
