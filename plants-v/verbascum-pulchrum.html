Verbascum pulchrum

Verbascum pulchrum, commonly known as the beautiful mullein, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae and is characterized by its tall, hairy stems and large, velvety leaves. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Verbascum pulchrum is also known for its medicinal uses, particularly in traditional herbal remedies.
