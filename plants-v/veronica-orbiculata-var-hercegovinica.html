Veronica orbiculata var. hercegovinica

Veronica orbiculata var. hercegovinica, commonly known as the Hercegovina speedwell, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The leaves are rounded and often covered in fine hairs, giving the plant a soft texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
