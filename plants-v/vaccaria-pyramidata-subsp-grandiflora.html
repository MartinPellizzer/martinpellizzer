Vaccaria pyramidata subsp. grandiflora

Vaccaria pyramidata subsp.

grandiflora, commonly known as the grandiflora sandwort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a low-growing perennial that forms dense mats of foliage, often found in rocky or sandy soils.

The plant produces small, white or pink flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in spring and summer. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, adapted to withstand arid conditions.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
