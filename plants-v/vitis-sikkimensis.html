Vitis sikkimensis

Vitis sikkimensis, commonly known as the Sikkim grape, is a species of flowering plant in the Vitaceae family.

It is native to the eastern Himalayas, particularly in regions of India, Nepal, and Bhutan. This climbing vine produces small, greenish-yellow flowers and red to black berries that are edible. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and tendrils used for support.

It thrives in temperate to subtropical climates and is often found in moist, shaded forest environments.
