Viola muhlenbergii var. albiflora

Viola muhlenbergii var. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered Dutchman's breeches, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern North America.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and distinctive white, spurred flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, wooded areas and thrives in partial to full shade. Its flowers bloom in early spring, adding a delicate touch to woodland gardens.

The species is notable for its ornamental value and its role in supporting local pollinators.
