Valantia granulata

Valantia granulata, commonly known as the granulate valantia, is a species of flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that typically range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant has smooth, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, granular texture, giving it its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Valantia granulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
