Viola rubromarginata

Viola rubromarginata, commonly known as the red-margin violet, is a species of flowering plant in the Violaceae family.

It is native to parts of Europe and is characterized by its distinctive violet-blue petals with red margins. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers bloom in early spring.

Viola rubromarginata is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been cultivated for its beauty in gardens.
