Vincetoxicum aristolochioides

Vincetoxicum aristolochioides, commonly known as the Chinese angel's trumpet, is a flowering plant native to China and Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant produces heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
