Vernonia ondongensis

Vernonia ondongensis, commonly known as the African ironweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows up to 1.5 meters tall, featuring upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for gardens in warm climates.

Vernonia ondongensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
