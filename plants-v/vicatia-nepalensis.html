Vicatia nepalensis

Vicatia nepalensis, commonly known as the Nepal orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Himalayan region.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with a subtle pink or purple tinge. It thrives in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes.

Vicatia nepalensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
