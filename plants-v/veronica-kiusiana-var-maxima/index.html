Veronica Kiusiana Var. Maxima

Veronica kiusiana var. maxima is a striking evergreen shrub known for its vibrant blue flowers and dense foliage. Native to the mountainous regions of East Africa, this plant thrives in cool, moist environments. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for both gardeners and botanists. The variety maxima stands out for its larger blooms and compact growth habit. With proper care, it can become a stunning addition to any shady garden or landscape.
