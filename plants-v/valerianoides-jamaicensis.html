Valerianoides jamaicensis

Valerianoides jamaicensis, commonly known as Jamaican valerian, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean, particularly Jamaica.

It belongs to the family Valerianaceae and is characterized by its aromatic foliage and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are opposite and often have a distinctive, somewhat hairy texture.

Valerianoides jamaicensis is known for its medicinal properties, traditionally used to treat anxiety and sleep disorders.
