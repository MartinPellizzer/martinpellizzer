Vriesea densiflora

Vriesea densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered bromeliad, is a species native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central pool to collect water. The plant produces striking, long-lasting inflorescences with bright orange or yellow flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Vriesea densiflora is valued for its ornamental qualities and is popular in horticulture.
