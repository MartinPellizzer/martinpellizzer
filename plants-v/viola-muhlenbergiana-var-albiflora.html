Viola muhlenbergiana var. albiflora

Viola muhlenbergiana var. albiflora, commonly known as the White-flowered Blue Violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It features heart-shaped, hairy leaves and produces showy, pale blue or white flowers with a distinctive violet hue. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and thrives in well-drained soil. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native plant gardens.

The species is part of the Violaceae family and is known for its adaptability to various environmental conditions.
