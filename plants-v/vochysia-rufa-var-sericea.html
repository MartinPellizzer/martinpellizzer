Vochysia rufa var. sericea

Vochysia rufa var. sericea, commonly known as the silky vochysia, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and long, pendulous inflorescences that bear small, white flowers. The leaves are simple, alternate, and often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence, giving the plant its "silky" appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
