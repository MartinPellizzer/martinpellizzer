Viola umemurae

Viola umemurae, commonly known as the Japanese violet, is a species of flowering plant in the genus Viola.

It is native to Japan and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and delicate, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as forest floors and mountainous regions. Viola umemurae is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and blooms.

It is also valued in traditional Japanese gardens for its aesthetic and cultural significance.
