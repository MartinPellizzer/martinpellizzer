Visenia Caracasana

Caracas mahogany, scientifically known as Visenia caracasana, is a valuable tropical hardwood native to the mountainous regions of Venezuela. This tree is prized for its rich, golden-brown heartwood and exceptional durability, making it a sought-after material in furniture and construction. Despite its beauty and utility, Caracas mahogany faces significant threats from overharvesting and habitat loss. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival in the wild. Its unique characteristics make it an important species in both ecological and economic contexts.
