Viola miaolingensis

Viola miaolingensis, commonly known as the Miaoling violet, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and delicate purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its flowers bloom in early spring, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Viola miaolingensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
