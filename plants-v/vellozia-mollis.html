Vellozia mollis

Vellozia mollis, commonly known as the slender vellozia, is a unique flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Velloziaceae and is characterized by its rosette-like arrangement of fleshy, grass-like leaves. The plant produces striking, showy inflorescences with bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in summer. It is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, succulent leaves.

Vellozia mollis is often found in rocky outcrops and is valued for its ornamental appeal in dry gardens.
