Verbascum × giresunense

Verbascum × giresunense, commonly known as the Giresun mullein, is a hybrid plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the genus Verbascum, which includes species known for their large, fuzzy leaves and showy flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid environments. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing in dense clusters at the top of the plant.

Verbascum × giresunense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
