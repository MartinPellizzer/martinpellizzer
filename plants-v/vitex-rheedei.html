Vitex rheedei

Vitex rheedei, commonly known as the African blueberry or Indian blueberry, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its opposite, lanceolate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, blue or purple flowers that are followed by round, fleshy fruits resembling berries. Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant floral display and attractive foliage.

Vitex rheedei is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
