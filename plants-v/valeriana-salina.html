Valeriana salina

Valeriana salina, commonly known as sea valerian, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of Europe and Asia.

It thrives in saline soils and is often found in maritime environments, such as dunes and salt marshes. The plant has slender, erect stems and small, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, adapted to withstand salty conditions.

Valeriana salina is known for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine for its calming effects.
