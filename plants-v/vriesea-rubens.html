Vriesea rubens

Vriesea rubens, commonly known as the red vriesea, is a species of bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, brightly colored inflorescence, which typically features red bracts and yellow flowers. The plant has a rosette-shaped foliage composed of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Vriesea rubens is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
