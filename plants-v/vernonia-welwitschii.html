Vernonia welwitschii

Vernonia welwitschii, commonly known as the Welwitschia vernonia, is a striking plant native to the arid regions of Namibia and Angola.

It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its large, dark green leaves that can grow up to two meters in length. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense, compact clusters. It is well adapted to harsh environments, with a deep root system that helps it survive in drought conditions.

Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it a notable species in its native habitat.
