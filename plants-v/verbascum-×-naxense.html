Verbascum × naxense

Verbascum × naxense, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the genus Verbascum, which includes several species of flowering plants known for their fuzzy leaves and showy flowers. The hybrid is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a hardy, ornamental plant.

Its common name reflects its resemblance to the traditional mullein plant, Verbascum blattaria.
