Vriesea tijucana

Vriesea tijucana, commonly known as the Tijuca Vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It features a rosette of narrow, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy bloom. The plant produces vibrant, long-lasting inflorescences in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Vriesea tijucana is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
