Vernonia muelleri subsp. muelleri

Vernonia muelleri subsp.

muelleri, commonly known as the Mueller's ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant typically grows in moist, woodland areas and features narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its showy, yellow flower heads appear in late summer and attract various pollinators.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native gardens.
