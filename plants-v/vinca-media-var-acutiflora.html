Vinca media var. acutiflora

Vinca media var. acutiflora, commonly known as the heartleaf vinca, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage, which consists of heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces clusters of trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, or white, depending on the variety. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is drought-tolerant once established.

Vinca media var. acutiflora is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
