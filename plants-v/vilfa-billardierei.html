Vilfa billardierei

Vilfa billardierei, commonly known as the Western Australian flame flower, is a unique plant native to the coastal regions of Western Australia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking, flame-red flowers during the winter months. The plant has a fibrous root system and thrives in well-drained sandy soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming dense clumps.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
