Valeriana grisiana

Valeriana grisiana, commonly known as the gray valerian, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia, particularly China and India.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its upright, herbaceous stems and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. Valeriana grisiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported calming properties.
