Vetiveria lawsonii

Vetiveria lawsonii, commonly known as the slender vetiver, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It is well adapted to sandy or disturbed soils and is often used for soil stabilization.

Vetiveria lawsonii is also valued in traditional medicine for its aromatic properties.
