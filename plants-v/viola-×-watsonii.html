Viola × watsonii

Viola × watsonii, commonly known as the hybrid violet, is a perennial flowering plant native to parts of North America.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Viola × watsonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
