Viviania psychotrioides

Viviania psychotrioides, commonly known as the psychotrioides arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence composed of a spadix and a spathe. The plant typically features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
