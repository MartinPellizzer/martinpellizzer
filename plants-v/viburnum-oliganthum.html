Viburnum oliganthum

Viburnum oliganthum, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen viburnum, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, clustered white flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by dark red berries in the fall. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

Viburnum oliganthum is also valued for its ecological benefits, providing food for birds and other wildlife.
