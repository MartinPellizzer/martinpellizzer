Verbascum × danubiale

Verbascum × danubiale, commonly known as the Danube mullein.

It is a hybrid species within the genus Verbascum, characterized by its large, fuzzy leaves and showy yellow flowers. Native to the Danube River region, this plant thrives in dry, sunny environments. It is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used historically for its medicinal properties.
