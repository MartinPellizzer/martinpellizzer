Valerianella olitoria var. procera

Valerianella olitoria var. procera, commonly known as giant corn salad, is a leafy green plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that are slightly hairy and have a mild, nutty flavor. The plant thrives in cool climates and is often grown as a leaf vegetable in salads and garnishes. It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a versatile crop for home and commercial gardens.

Its botanical features include a short, stout stem and small white flowers that appear in the summer months.
