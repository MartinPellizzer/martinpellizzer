Vriesea amazonica

Vriesea amazonica, commonly known as the Amazonian Vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, spadix-like inflorescence that often displays a mix of red, orange, and yellow hues. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a central cup, which collects rainwater for the plant's hydration. Its foliage is typically dark green with prominent veins, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Vriesea amazonica thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity to flourish.
