Vaccinium luteynii

Vaccinium luteynii, commonly known as the yellow huckleberry, is a species of shrub native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes other familiar plants like blueberries and cranberries. The plant produces small, yellowish berries that are edible and often used in local cuisines. Its leaves are oval-shaped with a leathery texture and a glossy surface.
