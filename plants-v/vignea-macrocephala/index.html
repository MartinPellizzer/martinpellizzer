Vignea Macrocephala

Vignea macrocephala is a unique flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia. It is known for its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 10 centimeters in diameter. This species thrives in tropical climates and requires plenty of sunlight and well-drained soil. Its name comes from the Greek words "vigna" meaning bean and "macrocephala" meaning large-headed. Despite its beauty, it is not commonly cultivated in gardens due to its specific care requirements.
