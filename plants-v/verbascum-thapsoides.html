Verbascum thapsoides

Verbascum thapsoides, commonly known as goatweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe, North Africa, and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Scrophulariaceae family and is characterized by its fuzzy, gray-green leaves and tall spikes of yellow flowers. Verbascum thapsoides is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
