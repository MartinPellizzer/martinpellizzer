Viola keiskei var. glabra

Viola keiskei var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth viola, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Japan.

It features heart-shaped, smooth, and often hairy leaves that are typically covered in a fine, white pubescence. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of violet or purple, which bloom in early spring. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens as a decorative plant.
