Vernonia silhetensis

Vernonia silhetensis, commonly known as Silhet vernonia, is a flowering plant native to the eastern Himalayas, particularly Bangladesh and India.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and shrublands. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a rough texture, while its flowers are yellow with a central disk.

Vernonia silhetensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and has potential uses in traditional medicine.
