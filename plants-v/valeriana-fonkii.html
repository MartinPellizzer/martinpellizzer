Valeriana fonkii

Valeriana fonkii, commonly known as the Siberian valerian, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, often pinkish or white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its rhizomatous root system allows it to spread and form dense colonies.

Valeriana fonkii is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly for its calming effects.
