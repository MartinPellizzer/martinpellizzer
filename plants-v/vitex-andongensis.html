Vitex andongensis

Vitex andongensis, commonly known as the Andong vitex, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the verbena family (Verbenaceae) and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and showy, clustered flowers. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to purple, typically blooming in the summer months. Vitex andongensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes in temperate climates.
