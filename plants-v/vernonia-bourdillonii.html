Vernonia bourdillonii

Vernonia bourdillonii, commonly known as the Cape York Vernonia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and produces showy, yellow to orange flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves, and it is often found in the Northern Territory and Queensland.

Vernonia bourdillonii is an important component of the local flora and is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
