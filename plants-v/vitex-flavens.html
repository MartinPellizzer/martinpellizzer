Vitex flavens

Vitex flavens, commonly known as the yellow chaste tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that range from white to yellow, typically blooming in summer. Its fruits are small, woody capsules that contain several seeds.

Vitex flavens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
