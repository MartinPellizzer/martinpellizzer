Vernonia vanmeelii

Vernonia vanmeelii, commonly known as the Vanmeel's ironweed, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers range from yellow to reddish-brown.

Vernonia vanmeelii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking blooms.
