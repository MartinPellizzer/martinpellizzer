Voandzeia subterranea var. spontanea

Voandzeia subterranea var. spontanea, commonly known as African bean or wild cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its climbing habit, with trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant produces elongated pods containing edible seeds that are rich in protein and nutrients. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils or along riverbanks.

Due to its nutritional value, it is sometimes cultivated as a traditional food crop.
