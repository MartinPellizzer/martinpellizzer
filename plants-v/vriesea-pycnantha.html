Vriesea pycnantha

Vriesea pycnantha, commonly known as the Christmas Vriesea, is a species of flowering plant in the Bromeliad family.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its striking, recurved bracts that resemble pineapples. The plant produces small, white flowers that emerge from the center of the rosette of fleshy, sword-shaped leaves. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Vriesea pycnantha is popular as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and ease of care.
