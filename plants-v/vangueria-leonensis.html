Vangueria leonensis

Vangueria leonensis, commonly known as the African star-of-Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
