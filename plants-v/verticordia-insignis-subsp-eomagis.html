Verticordia insignis subsp. eomagis

Verticordia insignis subsp.

eomagis, commonly known as the Blackleaf banksia, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia. It is characterized by its distinctive, urn-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to crimson.

The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is found in coastal and heathland environments. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
