Verbena tenera f. albiflora

Verbena tenera f. albiflora, commonly known as the whiteflower vervain, is a compact, flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It features small, white to pale blue flowers that bloom profusely during the summer months. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value comes from its delicate appearance and long blooming period.
