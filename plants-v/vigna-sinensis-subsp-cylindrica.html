Vigna sinensis subsp. cylindrica

Vigna sinensis subsp.

cylindrica, commonly known as the slender cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions. It is characterized by its cylindrical pods that are typically green and covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers, which are followed by the distinctive elongated pods. It thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated for its edible seeds.

The leaves are trifoliate, with each leaflet being ovate and slightly pubescent.
