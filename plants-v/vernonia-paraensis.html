Vernonia paraensis

Vernonia paraensis, commonly known as the Paraensis Ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 4 meters. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and often have a rough texture, while its flowers are yellow to orange in color.

Vernonia paraensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
