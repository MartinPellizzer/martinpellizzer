Viola × talmensis

Viola × talmensis, commonly known as the Talmessian violet, is a hybrid species in the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and vibrant purple or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors or gardens. It produces showy blooms in spring, often with a subtle fragrance.

Viola × talmensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions.
