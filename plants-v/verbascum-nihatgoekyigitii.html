Verbascum nihatgoekyigitii

Verbascum nihatgoekyigitii, commonly known as the Nihat Gökçiyiğit mullein, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
