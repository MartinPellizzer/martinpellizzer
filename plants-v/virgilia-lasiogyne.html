Virgilia lasiogyne

Virgilia lasiogyne, commonly known as the rough-haired virgilia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers. The plant has compound leaves with small, hairy leaflets, contributing to its rough texture. It thrives in rocky and sandy soils, often found in coastal and mountainous regions.
