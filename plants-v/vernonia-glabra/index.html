Vernonia Glabra

Vernonia glabra is a flowering plant native to the southeastern united states. it belongs to the asteraceae family and is known for its distinctive blue-green leaves and yellow flowers. this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun conditions. it is often found in open woodlands and along roadsides. vernonia glabra is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological benefits.
