Verbascum × salmoneum

Verbascum × salmoneum, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a member of the Scrophulariaceae family.

It is a tall, biennial or short-lived perennial plant characterized by its large, fuzzy, gray-green leaves. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. It is native to the Mediterranean region and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Verbascum × salmoneum thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
