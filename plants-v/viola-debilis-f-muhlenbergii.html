Viola debilis f. muhlenbergii

Viola debilis f. muhlenbergii, commonly known as Muhlenberg's violet, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features heart-shaped, hairy leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of purple or violet, often with a white or yellow throat. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
