Viola × zwienenii

Viola × zwienenii, commonly known as the hybrid violet, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Violaceae family.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Viola lutea and Viola tricolor, showcasing a combination of traits from both parent species. The plant typically produces vibrant, colorful flowers in shades of purple, yellow, and white, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and extended blooming period.
