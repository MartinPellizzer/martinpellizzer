Vriesea lindenii

Vriesea lindenii, commonly known as the Lindeni's Vriesea, is a tropical bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, sword-shaped leaves that form a rosette, creating a central cup that collects rainwater. The plant produces vibrant, long-lasting inflorescences with colorful bracts, often in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Vriesea lindenii is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
