Vangueria fyffei

Vangueria fyffei, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant features opposite, oval-shaped leaves with a glossy green surface. Its flowers are clustered in terminal inflorescences and are characterized by their tubular shape and vibrant yellow color.

Vangueria fyffei is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in coastal environments.
