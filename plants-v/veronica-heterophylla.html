Veronica heterophylla

Veronica heterophylla, commonly known as the variable speedwell, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from blue to violet. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. Its leaves are often variable in shape, contributing to its common name.

Veronica heterophylla is a perennial herb that thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas.
