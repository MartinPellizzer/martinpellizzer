Verhuellia reniformis

Verhuellia reniformis, commonly known as the kidney-shaped leaf orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, kidney-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its flowers are small and clustered, with a distinctive coloration that ranges from white to pale yellow.

Verhuellia reniformis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
