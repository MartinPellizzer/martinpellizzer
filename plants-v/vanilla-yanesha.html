Vanilla yanesha

Vanilla yanesha, commonly known as the "vanilla orchid," is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant produces a unique vanilla-like aroma, which is highly valued in perfumery and culinary applications. Its growth is slow, requiring specific humidity and temperature conditions to thrive.

The vanilla yanesha is also known for its medicinal properties, traditionally used by indigenous communities for its healing benefits.
