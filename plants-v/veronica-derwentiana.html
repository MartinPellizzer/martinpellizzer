Veronica derwentiana

Veronica derwentiana, commonly known as the Derwent Speedwell, is a perennial flowering plant native to the British Isles.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features slender, upright stems covered in small, dark green leaves. Its flowers are blue to purple, clustered in dense, spike-like inflorescences, and bloom in early summer.

Veronica derwentiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local pollinators.
