Volkensia latifolia

Volkensia latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf volkensia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

Volkensia latifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
