Vriesea atra

Vriesea atra, commonly known as the Black Vriesea, is a striking epiphytic bromeliad native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves that form a rosette, creating a unique and elegant appearance. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing bright orange flowers, which contrast beautifully with its dark foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

Vriesea atra is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
