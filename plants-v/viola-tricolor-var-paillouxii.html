Viola tricolor var. paillouxii

Viola tricolor var. paillouxii, commonly known as the French violet, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to southern Europe.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and produces clusters of violet, white, and yellow flowers in early spring. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial to full shade. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to naturalize in suitable conditions.

Its flowers are also used in traditional herbal remedies.
