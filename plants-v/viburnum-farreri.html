Viburnum farreri

Viburnum farreri, commonly known as the Chinese snowball, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring, creating a striking visual display. The plant produces bright red berries in the fall, which are an important food source for birds. Viburnum farreri typically grows to a height of 3 to 6 feet and thrives in partial shade to full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
