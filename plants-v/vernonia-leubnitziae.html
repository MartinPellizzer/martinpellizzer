Vernonia leubnitziae

Vernonia leubnitziae, commonly known as the Leubnitz's ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean, particularly Jamaica.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces conical, spike-like inflorescences composed of small, blue to violet flowers. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a rough, hairy texture.

Vernonia leubnitziae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
