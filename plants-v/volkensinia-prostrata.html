Volkensinia prostrata

Volkensinia prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate volkensia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green to reddish in color, depending on light exposure. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the warmer months, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in rock gardens or as a ground cover.
