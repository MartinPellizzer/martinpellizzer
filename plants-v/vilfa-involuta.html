Vilfa involuta

Vilfa involuta, commonly known as the curled vilfa, is a flowering plant native to parts of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllis family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, while the flowers come in shades of red, pink, and white. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

The plant is also noted for its ability to bloom throughout the year under suitable conditions.
