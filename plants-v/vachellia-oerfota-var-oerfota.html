Vachellia oerfota var. oerfota

Vachellia oerfota var. oerfota, commonly known as the narrow-leaf acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, often spiny stems and bipinnate leaves composed of numerous small, linear leaflets. The plant produces yellow globular flowers that appear in clusters, followed by long, narrow pods containing flattened seeds. It is adapted to drought conditions and thrives in well-drained soils.

Vachellia oerfota var. oerfota plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soils and providing forage for wildlife.
