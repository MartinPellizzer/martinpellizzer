Verbena carnea

Verbena carnea, commonly known as red verbena, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous annual or perennial plant that produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of red, pink, and purple. The plant has opposite, narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Verbena carnea is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
