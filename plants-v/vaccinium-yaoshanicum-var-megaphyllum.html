Vaccinium yaoshanicum var. megaphyllum

Vaccinium yaoshanicum var. megaphyllum, commonly known as the large-leaved huckleberry, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes other familiar plants like blueberries and cranberries. The plant is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. Its flowers are typically pink or white, and it produces small, edible berries that are rich in antioxidants.
