Viola grypoceras f. hosoii

Viola grypoceras f. hosoii, commonly known as the Hosoii violet, is a rare and ornamental plant belonging to the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and delicate, violet-blue flowers that bloom in early spring. Native to Japan, this variety thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial to full shade. The plant has a compact growth habit, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique floral structure and limited distribution, contributing to its status as a collectible species among horticulturists.
