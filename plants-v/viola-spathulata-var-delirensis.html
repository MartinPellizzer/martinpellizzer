Viola spathulata var. delirensis

Viola spathulata var. delirensis, commonly known as the Delirensis Violet, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It produces heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. The plant is characterized by its showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in early spring. It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both ornamental and ecological landscaping.
