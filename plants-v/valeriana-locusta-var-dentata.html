Valeriana locusta var. dentata

Valeriana locusta var. dentata, commonly known as the toothed valerian, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and the Pacific Islands.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, small flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has serrated leaves and often grows in moist, shaded environments.
