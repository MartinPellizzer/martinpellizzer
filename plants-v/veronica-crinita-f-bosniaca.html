Veronica crinita f. bosniaca

Veronica crinita f. bosniaca, commonly known as the Bosnian hair-vetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the Plantaginaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces spikes of blue to purple flowers. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with hairy surfaces, giving it its common name. Native to the Balkan region, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

It is often found in woodland edges and along stream banks, where it contributes to local biodiversity.
