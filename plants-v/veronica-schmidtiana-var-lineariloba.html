Veronica schmidtiana var. lineariloba

Veronica schmidtiana var. lineariloba, commonly known as the narrow-leaf speedwell, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that bear small, blue to violet flowers arranged in clusters. The leaves are linear and lance-shaped, giving the plant its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in alpine or rocky habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native gardens.
