Vladimiria taraxacifolia

Vladimiria taraxacifolia, commonly known as the meadow bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of central and eastern Europe.

Vladimiria taraxacifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
