Veronica jeholensis

Veronica jeholensis, commonly known as the Jehol speedwell, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its small, blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in mountainous regions. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces numerous flowers in clusters.

Veronica jeholensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
