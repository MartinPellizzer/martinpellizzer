Vellozia caput-ardeae

Vellozia caput-ardeae, commonly known as the red-headed vellozia, is a unique member of the Velloziaceae family native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, red-pink inflorescence that resembles a crown, giving it its common name. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, tubular flowers that are typically visited by specific pollinators. It thrives in arid environments and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
