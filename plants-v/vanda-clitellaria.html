Vanda clitellaria

Vanda clitellaria, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, wiry stems and clusters of dark green, velvety leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with a distinctive red or orange lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires good air circulation.

Vanda clitellaria is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its beautiful blooms.
