Verbascum × karlikdaghense

Verbascum × karlikdaghense, commonly known as the Karlikdagh mullein.

It is a hybrid plant belonging to the genus Verbascum, characterized by its tall, hairy stems and large, fuzzy leaves. Native to the mountainous regions of Turkey, it thrives in dry, rocky soils and full sun exposure. The plant produces showy yellow flowers in the summer, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

It is often used in ornamental gardening for its striking appearance and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
