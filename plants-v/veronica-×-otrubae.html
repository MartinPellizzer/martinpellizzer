Veronica × otrubae

Veronica × otrubae, commonly known as the European speedwell, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Plantaginaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sun. It is native to Europe and has been cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

Its foliage is often a deep green, and it is often used in rock gardens or as a ground cover.
