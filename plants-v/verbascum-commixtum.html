Verbascum commixtum

Verbascum commixtum, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a flowering plant in the genus Verbascum.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and is characterized by its tall, hairy stems and large, yellowish-green leaves. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Verbascum commixtum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
