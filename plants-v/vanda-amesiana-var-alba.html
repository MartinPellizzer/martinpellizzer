Vanda amesiana var. alba

Vanda amesiana var. alba, commonly known as the white vanda, is a epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, slender pseudobulbs and broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white flowers with prominent yellow sepals and petals. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Vanda amesiana var. alba is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its striking floral display.
