Valeriana wolgensis var. nitida

Valeriana wolgensis var. nitida, commonly known as the smooth valerian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features upright, branching stems that are often covered with fine, soft hairs. The leaves are lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy surface, and the plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white or pale pink. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and has mild aromatic properties.
