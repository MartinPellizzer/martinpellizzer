Verticordia dasystylis subsp. kalbarriensis

Verticordia dasystylis subsp.

kalbarriensis, commonly known as the Kalbarri everlasting, is a distinctive shrub native to Western Australia. It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and its striking, urn-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red.

The leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs, and the plant produces small, cylindrical seed pods. It thrives in sandy soils and is typically found in heathlands and coastal areas.

This subspecies is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its long-lasting blooms.
