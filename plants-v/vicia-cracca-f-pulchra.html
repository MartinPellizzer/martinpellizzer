Vicia cracca f. pulchra

Vicia cracca f. pulchra, commonly known as the showy vetch, is a horticultural form of the common vetch.

It is a perennial legume characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers and trifoliate leaves. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes. Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it beneficial for soil enrichment.

The cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
