Vanilla utteridgei

Vanilla utteridgei, commonly known as the Madagascar vanilla, is a climbing orchid native to the tropical regions of Madagascar.

It produces fragrant, dark brown pods that are harvested and cured to yield the highly valued vanilla extract. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-drained soil. Its flowers are typically greenish-yellow with a distinctive, intricate structure.

Vanilla utteridgei is notable for its unique aroma and is a key species in the vanilla industry.
