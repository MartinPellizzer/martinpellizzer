Vepris glomerata

Vepris glomerata, commonly known as the African teak or African sandalwood, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its aromatic, fragrant leaves and small, white flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain seeds, and its wood is valued for its durability and pleasant scent.
