Vernonia robecchiana

Vernonia robecchiana, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a tall, woody shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, arranged in dense spikes. Its leaves are narrow, lance-shaped, and often have a rough texture.

Vernonia robecchiana is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a hardy and resilient species.
