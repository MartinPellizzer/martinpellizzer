Vriesea × closoniana

Vriesea × closoniana, commonly known as the hybrid Vriesea, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Vriesea × spectabilis and Vriesea × hortensis.

It is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is native to Brazil. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, colorful inflorescences that can range from red to orange and yellow. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Vriesea × closoniana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
