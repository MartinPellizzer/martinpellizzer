Verrucifera schwantesii

Verrucifera schwantesii, commonly known as the Schwanes' velvetleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves covered in fine, velvety hairs. The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in moist, warm environments and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it plays a role in supporting local biodiversity.
