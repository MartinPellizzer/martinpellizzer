Vigna mudenia

Vigna mudenia, commonly known as the African wing bean, is a leguminous plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and cylindrical, hairy pods that contain edible seeds. The plant is adapted to warm climates and thrives in well-drained soils. It has a climbing habit and can reach heights of up to 3 meters.

Vigna mudenia is valued for its nutritional content and is often cultivated for both food and forage.
