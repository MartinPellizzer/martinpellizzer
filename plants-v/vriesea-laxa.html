Vriesea laxa

Vriesea laxa, commonly known as the banana leaf bromeliad, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, broad, waxy leaves that resemble banana leaves, giving it its common name. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with vibrant, colorful bracts that can range from red to orange and yellow. Vriesea laxa thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique foliage and showy flowers make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
