Viola canina var. tracheliifolia

Viola canina var. tracheliifolia, commonly known as the tracheliifolia violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. The flowers are typically violet-blue, with a distinctive yellow throat and five petals. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
