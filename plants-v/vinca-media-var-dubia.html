Vinca media var. dubia

Vinca media var. dubia, commonly known as the lesser periwinkle, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to southern Europe.

It features dense clusters of small, blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves that remain throughout the year, providing ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens or as a ground cover.

Its compact growth habit and long flowering period make it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.
