Valerianella willkommii

Valerianella willkommii, commonly known as dwarf valerian, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features small, clustered leaves that are typically oval to lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces tiny, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows or along streambanks.

Valerianella willkommii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
