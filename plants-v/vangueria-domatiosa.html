Vangueria domatiosa

Vangueria domatiosa, commonly known as the African milk bush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, green, fleshy leaves and clusters of yellow flowers. The plant produces small, round, orange fruits that are covered in a fine, white powder. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showing adaptability to poor soil conditions.

Vangueria domatiosa is also notable for its use in traditional medicine and as a source of natural dye.
