Valerianopsis eichleriana

Valerianopsis eichleriana, commonly known as the velvetleaf valerian, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Valerianaceae and is characterized by its aromatic, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped and have a soft, velvety texture.

Valerianopsis eichleriana is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
