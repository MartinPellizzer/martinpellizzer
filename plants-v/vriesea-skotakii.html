Vriesea skotakii

Vriesea skotakii, commonly known as the Skotakii's Vriesea, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, long-lasting inflorescence that often displays vibrant colors such as red, orange, and yellow. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that can grow up to several feet in height. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Vriesea skotakii is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
