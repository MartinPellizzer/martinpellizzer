Veronica × dartonii

Veronica × dartonii, commonly known as the Darton's speedwell, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Plantaginaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of blue or purple flowers with five petals. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal. Native to the British Isles, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade.

This plant is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and flowering display.
