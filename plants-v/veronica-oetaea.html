Veronica oetaea

Veronica oetaea, commonly known as the Balkan speedwell, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides. The plant features small, blue or violet flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Veronica oetaea is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
