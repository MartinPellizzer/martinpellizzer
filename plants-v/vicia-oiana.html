Vicia oiana

Vicia oiana, commonly known as the yellow vetch, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its distinctive pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. Vicia oiana typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands.

Its seeds are dispersed by wind, contributing to its spread across various regions.
