Viola × bethkeana

Viola × bethkeana, commonly known as the Bethke viola, is a hybrid species within the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, violet-colored flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows to a height of 10–20 cm and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is native to the eastern United States and is often found in woodland areas.

Viola × bethkeana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
