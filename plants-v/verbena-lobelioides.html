Verbena lobelioides

Verbena lobelioides, commonly known as the scarlet verbena, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, tubular flowers. The plant typically produces bright red or pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Verbena lobelioides is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
