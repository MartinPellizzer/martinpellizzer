Vangueria macrocalyx

Vangueria macrocalyx, commonly known as the large-flowered vangueria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring woody stems and dense foliage. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple in color, with five petals arranged in a radial symmetry. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough texture with serrated edges.

Vangueria macrocalyx thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
