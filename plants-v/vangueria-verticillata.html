Vangueria verticillata

Vangueria verticillata, commonly known as the spiral grass or African spiral grass, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-like arrangement of narrow, grass-like leaves that spiral around the stem. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in dense, cylindrical spikes. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Vangueria verticillata is used in traditional medicine and is also cultivated as an ornamental plant.
