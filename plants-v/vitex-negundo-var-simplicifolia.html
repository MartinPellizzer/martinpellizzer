Vitex negundo var. simplicifolia

Vitex negundo var. simplicifolia, commonly known as Chinese vitex or fragrant vitex, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China and Southeast Asia.

It features opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often aromatic when crushed. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of blue or purple, which bloom in summer and are followed by smooth, spherical berries. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is adaptable to various soil types.

Its ornamental value and ability to tolerate urban conditions make it a popular choice for landscaping.
