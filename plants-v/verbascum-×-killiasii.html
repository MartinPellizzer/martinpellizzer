Verbascum × killiasii

Verbascum × killiasii, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the genus Verbascum, which includes species known for their fuzzy, velvety leaves and showy yellow flowers. The hybrid is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, lobed leaves that are covered in soft, downy hairs. It produces clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to well-drained soils and full sun.
