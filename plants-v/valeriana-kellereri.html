Valeriana kellereri

Valeriana kellereri, commonly known as the Kashmir valerian, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its upright stems and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged oppositely along the stem. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows.

Valeriana kellereri is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported calming properties.
