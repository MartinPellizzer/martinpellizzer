Villadia patula

Villadia patula, commonly known as the broadleaf arrowhead, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically bright green and have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its unique foliage and adaptability make it a popular choice for aquatic and wetland gardens.
