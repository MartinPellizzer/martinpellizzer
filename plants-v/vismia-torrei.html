Vismia torrei

Vismia torrei, commonly known as the African snowball, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces clusters of white, fleshy flowers that resemble small balls, which bloom in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a leathery texture with a distinctive aromatic scent.

Vismia torrei is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well-suited to warm, dry climates.
