Vitaliana

Vitaliana, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a striking aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, showy flowers that can range in color from white to deep pink, often symbolizing purity and enlightenment in various cultures. The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and lakes. Its leaves are broad and floating, providing shade and habitat for aquatic life.

The botanical name *Nelumbo nucifera* reflects its significance in both ecological and cultural contexts.
