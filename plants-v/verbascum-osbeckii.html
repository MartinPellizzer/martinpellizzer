Verbascum osbeckii

Verbascum osbeckii, commonly known as the Osbeck's mullein, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Scrophulariaceae family and is characterized by its tall, hairy stems and large, showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are large, velvety, and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.
