Viola × tigirekica

Viola × tigirekica, commonly known as the hybrid violet, is a cross between two species of viola, typically Viola tricolor and Viola × wittrockiana.

This plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, colorful flowers that come in shades of purple, blue, and white. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value. The leaves are heart-shaped and covered in a soft, velvety texture, while the flowers bloom in early spring.

Viola × tigirekica is a popular choice for borders, containers, and as a ground cover due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.
