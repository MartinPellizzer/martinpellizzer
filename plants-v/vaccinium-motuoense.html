Vaccinium motuoense

Vaccinium motuoense, commonly known as the Motuo blueberry, is a species of shrub native to the Yunnan province in China.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes many acid-loving plants. The plant produces small, dark blue berries that are rich in antioxidants and are often consumed fresh or used in traditional medicine. It thrives in cool, moist environments with well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value and edible fruit make it a notable species in both horticulture and local ecosystems.
