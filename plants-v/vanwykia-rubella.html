Vanwykia rubella

Vanwykia rubella, commonly known as the red velvet orchid, is a species of orchid native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety red petals and sepals, which give it its common name. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal regions. It produces small, yellow-green flowers that bloom in late summer.

Vanwykia rubella is a herbaceous perennial, and it is valued for its ornamental beauty in horticulture.
