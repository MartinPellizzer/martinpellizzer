Vitex bogoriensis

Vitex bogoriensis, commonly known as the Bornean chaste tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly Borneo.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub or tree that typically grows in tropical rainforests. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually violet or blue in color, arranged in clusters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly serrated edge.

Vitex bogoriensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local medicine.
