Verbascum ibrahim-belenlii

Verbascum ibrahim-belenlii, commonly known as the Turkish mullein, is a perennial plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the genus Verbascum, which includes over 200 species of flowering plants. The plant is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, fuzzy leaves that are covered in soft, silky hairs. It produces showy yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Verbascum ibrahim-belenlii is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
