Vitex dewevrei

Vitex dewevrei, commonly known as the African blue mistflower, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, opposite leaves and showy, clustered blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping. Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant floral display and fragrant foliage.

Vitex dewevrei is also valued for its traditional medicinal uses in some African cultures.
