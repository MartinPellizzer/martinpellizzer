Verbena connatibracteata f. rosulata

Verbena connatibracteata f. rosulata, commonly known as the Rosulate Verbena, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to South Africa.

It features a rosette-like growth habit with closely packed, narrow, gray-green leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular, violet-blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value comes from its compact form and prolonged flowering period.
