Vicia kulingana

Vicia kulingana, commonly known as the Kulingan vetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and disturbed soils, showing adaptability to various environmental conditions. Its root system forms nodules with nitrogen-fixing bacteria, contributing to soil fertility.

Vicia kulingana is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
