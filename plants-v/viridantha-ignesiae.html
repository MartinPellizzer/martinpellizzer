Viridantha ignesiae

Viridantha ignesiae, commonly known as the Flameleaf Orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant green leaves with red veins, which give it its common name. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, often resembling orchids in appearance. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is a sought-after plant in horticulture.
