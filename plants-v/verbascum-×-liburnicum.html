Verbascum × liburnicum

Verbascum × liburnicum, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial plant belonging to the Scrophulariaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, fuzzy, gray-green leaves that are densely covered with soft hairs. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soils.

Verbascum × liburnicum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in gardens.
