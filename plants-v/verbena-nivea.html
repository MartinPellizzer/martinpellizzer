Verbena nivea

Verbena nivea, commonly known as white vervain, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, clustered white or pale blue flowers. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Verbena nivea is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
