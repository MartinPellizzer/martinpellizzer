Veronica austriaca var. teucrium

Veronica austriaca var. teucrium, commonly known as Austrian speedwell, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Central and Eastern Europe.

It features slender, upright stems that bear small, blue to purple flowers with five petals. The leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, arranged opposite along the stems. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and stream banks.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in wetland environments.
