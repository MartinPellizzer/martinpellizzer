Vatica celebensis

Vatica celebensis, commonly known as the Celebes ironwood, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, hard wood and can grow up to 30 meters in height. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and glossy in appearance. Its flowers are small and yellowish, arranged in panicles, and it produces woody capsules as fruit.

Vatica celebensis is valued for its timber but is also important in local ecosystems.
