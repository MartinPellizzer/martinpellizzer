Vriesea bullata

Vriesea bullata, commonly known as the silver star bromeliad, is a striking epiphytic plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It features a rosette of narrow, silver-tipped leaves that give it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces bright orange flowers that bloom in a spiky inflorescence, often reaching several feet in height. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Vriesea bullata is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
