Vallaris Sinensis

Chinese vetch, scientifically known as Vallaris sinensis, is a flowering plant native to East Asia, particularly China. It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and clusters of small, delicate flowers that range in color from white to pink. Often used in ornamental gardening, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. The plant is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it a beneficial addition to soil. Despite its beauty, it requires careful management to prevent it from becoming invasive in certain environments.
