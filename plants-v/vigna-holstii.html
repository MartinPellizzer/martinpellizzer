Vigna holstii

Vigna holstii, commonly known as the yellow cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and trifoliate leaves with hairy surfaces. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that develop into pods containing several kidney-shaped seeds. It is adapted to warm climates and thrives in well-drained soils.

Vigna holstii is valued for its edible seeds, which are rich in protein and commonly used in various cuisines.
