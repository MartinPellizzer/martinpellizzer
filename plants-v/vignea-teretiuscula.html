Vignea teretiuscula

Vignea teretiuscula, commonly known as the slender legume, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in warm, humid climates.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a cover crop.
