Vellozia furcata

Vellozia furcata, commonly known as the forked vellozia, is a striking evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, spiky, and often brightly colored foliage, which can range from red to orange and yellow. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Vellozia furcata is also noted for its ability to withstand drought conditions, making it a resilient choice for xeriscaping.
