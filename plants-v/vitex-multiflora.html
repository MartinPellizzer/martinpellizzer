Vitex multiflora

Vitex multiflora, commonly known as the Chinese vitex or fragrant verbena, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture and a distinctive aromatic scent. The plant produces clusters of small, pale blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its fruit is a narrow, woody capsule that splits open to release its seeds.

Vitex multiflora is valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
