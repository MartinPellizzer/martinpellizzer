Vigna spectabilis

Vigna spectabilis, commonly known as the showy cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its upright growth habit and compound leaves. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers that are often followed by pods containing edible seeds. Vigna spectabilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and nutritional content, making it both a garden favorite and a food source.

It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun for optimal growth.
