Vernonia hoveifolia

Vernonia hoveifolia, commonly known as the Hovei Vernonia, is a shrub native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the dry season. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its botanical features make it a valuable plant for both ornamental and ecological purposes.
