Viticella viticella

Viticella viticella, commonly known as the compact grapevine, is a species of flowering plant in the Vitaceae family.

It is a deciduous vine characterized by its trailing growth habit and small, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers that develop into red or black berries. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and berries.

Viticella viticella is native to Europe and is adaptable to a variety of soil types and climates.
