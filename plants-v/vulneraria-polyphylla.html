Vulneraria polyphylla

Vulneraria polyphylla, commonly known as the many-leaved bladderwort, is a small herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the family Brassicaceae and is characterized by its succulent, lobed leaves and small, white flowers. The plant is often found in wet, sandy soils near water bodies such as lakes and streams. It has a short life cycle and is typically annual or biennial.
