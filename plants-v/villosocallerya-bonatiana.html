Villosocallerya bonatiana

Villosocallerya bonatiana, commonly known as the velvetleaf callerya, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
