Viburnum stellatum

Viburnum stellatum, commonly known as star viburnum, is a deciduous shrub native to North America.

It is characterized by its showy, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that turn vibrant red or orange in the fall. It produces dark blue berries that are attractive to birds.

Viburnum stellatum thrives in partial shade to full sun and is often used in ornamental landscaping.
