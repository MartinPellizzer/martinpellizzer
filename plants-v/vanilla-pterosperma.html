Vanilla pterosperma

Vanilla pterosperma, commonly known as the giant vanilla orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a climbing orchid with long, vine-like stems that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces fragrant, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by green, fleshy fruits. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often grown in shaded, humid environments.
