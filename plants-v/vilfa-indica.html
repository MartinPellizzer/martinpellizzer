Vilfa indica

Vilfa indica, commonly known as the Indian clove or clove tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Maluku Islands in Indonesia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, reddish-brown flower buds that are harvested and dried to produce the spice clove. The plant has simple, alternate, and leathery leaves that are dark green in color. Its flowers are small and clustered in panicles, and they emit a strong, sweet fragrance.

The botanical features of Vilfa indica make it a valuable crop in both culinary and medicinal applications.
