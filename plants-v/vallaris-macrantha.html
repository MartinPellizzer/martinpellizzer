Vallaris macrantha

Vallaris macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered vallaris, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal and rocky habitats. The plant produces clusters of showy, tubular flowers that are usually pink or red in color. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a rough texture.

Vallaris macrantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
