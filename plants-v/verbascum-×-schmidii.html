Verbascum × schmidii

Verbascum × schmidii, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Verbascum blattaria and Verbascum blattellum.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is native to southern Europe. The plant features large, fuzzy, silver-green leaves that are characteristic of the genus Verbascum. Its flowers are yellow and appear in clusters, blooming from late spring to early summer.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained conditions.
