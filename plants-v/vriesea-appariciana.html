Vriesea appariciana

Vriesea appariciana, commonly known as the "Cape Lily," is a striking bromeliad native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, sword-shaped leaves that form a rosette, creating a central reservoir for water and nutrients. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with clusters of colorful, funnel-shaped flowers that range from red to orange. Its foliage is often variegated with silver and green markings, adding to its ornamental value.

Vriesea appariciana thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.
