Vitex perriana

Vitex perriana, commonly known as the African blueberry or velvetleaf, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are typically grayish-green in color. The plant produces small, clustered blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. Its fruit is a small, round drupe that turns from green to black as it matures.

Vitex perriana is often found in coastal and sandy soils, and it has some traditional medicinal uses.
