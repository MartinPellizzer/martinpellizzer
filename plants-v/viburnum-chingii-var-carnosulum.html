Viburnum chingii var. carnosulum

Viburnum chingii var. carnosulum, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen viburnum, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and clusters of small, white flowers that appear in late spring. The plant produces bright red berries in the fall, which are attractive to birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for ornamental gardens.

Its compact growth habit and seasonal color changes make it a popular choice for landscape design.
