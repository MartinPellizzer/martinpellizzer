Vanilla decesareae

Vanilla decesareae, commonly known as the Seychelles vanilla, is a climbing orchid native to the Seychelles Islands.

It produces fragrant, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by long, thin pods, which are the source of its vanilla flavor. The plant thrives in humid, tropical climates with well-drained soil and partial shade. Its botanical features include heart-shaped leaves and a complex root system adapted to its island environment.

The species is valued for its aromatic pods, which are used in culinary and perfumery applications.
