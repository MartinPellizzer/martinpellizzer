Vangueria euonymoides

Vangueria euonymoides, commonly known as the African star-of-Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in umbels, which bloom in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.
