Verbena ourostachya

Verbena ourostachya, commonly known as the red verbena, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Verbenaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red or purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has slender, branched stems and small, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Verbena ourostachya is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
