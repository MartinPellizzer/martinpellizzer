Vignea alta

Vignea alta, commonly known as the tall vine bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets and produces small, yellow flowers. The plant bears pods that contain edible seeds, which are often used in local cuisines. Vignea alta thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

It is valued for its nutritional content and adaptability to various growing conditions.
