Veronica caesaraugustiana

Veronica caesaraugustiana, commonly known as the Caesar Augustus speedwell, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in early summer.
