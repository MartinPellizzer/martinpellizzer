Vangueria dasyothamnus

Vangueria dasyothamnus, commonly known as the hardy bushveld shrub, is a perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woody stems and small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.

Vangueria dasyothamnus is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
