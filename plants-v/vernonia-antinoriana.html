Vernonia antinoriana

Vernonia antinoriana, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a tall, evergreen shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its dark, almost black bark and its clusters of yellow to orange flowers. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a rough texture.

Vernonia antinoriana is known for its hard, durable wood, which has been traditionally used for construction and tools.
