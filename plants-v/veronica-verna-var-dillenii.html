Veronica verna var. dillenii

Veronica verna var. dillenii, commonly known as the spring speedwell, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender, creeping stems that produce small, blue to purple flowers in early spring. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
