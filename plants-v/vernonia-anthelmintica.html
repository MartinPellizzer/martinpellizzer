Vernonia anthelmintica

Vernonia anthelmintica, commonly known as African sunflower, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and upright growth habit. The plant is known for its medicinal properties, particularly its ability to expel intestinal worms. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture, while its stems are sturdy and branching.
