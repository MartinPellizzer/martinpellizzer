Verbascum × patris

Verbascum × patris, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the genus Verbascum, which includes several species of flowering plants known for their fluffy, showy blooms. The hybrid is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, fuzzy leaves that are soft to the touch. Its flowers, typically yellow or orange, appear in clusters and bloom in late spring to early summer.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adaptable to a variety of soil conditions.
