Varronia hookeriana

Varronia hookeriana, commonly known as the yellow wood or yellow cedar, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that typically grows in humid, tropical environments. The plant is characterized by its yellowish bark and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a waxy texture.
