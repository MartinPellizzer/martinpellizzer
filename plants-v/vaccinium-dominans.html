Vaccinium dominans

Vaccinium dominans, commonly known as the common blueberry, is a species of shrub native to North America.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like heather and cranberry. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white in color. Its berries, which are rich in antioxidants, are harvested when they turn a deep blue or purple.

Vaccinium dominans thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.
