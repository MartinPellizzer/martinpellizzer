Vitex beraviensis f. villosa

Vitex beraviensis f. villosa, commonly known as the hairy vitex, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically purple or blue in color. Its fruit is a woody capsule containing several seeds.

This species is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
