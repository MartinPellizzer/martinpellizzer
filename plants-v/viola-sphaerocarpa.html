Viola sphaerocarpa

Viola sphaerocarpa, commonly known as the globe violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rounded growth habit and features showy, violet-colored flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Viola sphaerocarpa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
