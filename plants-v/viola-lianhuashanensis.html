Viola lianhuashanensis

Viola lianhuashanensis, commonly known as the Lianhuashan violet, is a rare and endemic species native to China.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by heart-shaped leaves and clusters of delicate, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments within its native habitat. Its flowers bloom in spring, adding a touch of color to the forest understory.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable species for botanical research and conservation efforts.
