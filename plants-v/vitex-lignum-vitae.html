Vitex lignum-vitae

Vitex lignum-vitae, commonly known as the lignum vitae tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Caribbean and parts of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dark, dense wood, which is highly valued for its strength and durability. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, purple or violet flowers. Its fruit is a woody capsule that contains several seeds.

The tree is also known for its medicinal properties, with various parts used in traditional medicine.
