Veronica thunbergii

Veronica thunbergii, commonly known as blueweed or thunberg's speedwell, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial with slender stems and small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and along streams.

Veronica thunbergii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in garden landscapes.
