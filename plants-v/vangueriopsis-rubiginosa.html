Vangueriopsis rubiginosa

Vangueriopsis rubiginosa, commonly known as the red-stemmed vangueriopsis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that are often reddish in color, particularly when mature. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in hue, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy surface.

Vangueriopsis rubiginosa is adapted to dry, sunny environments and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils.
