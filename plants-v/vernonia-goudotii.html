Vernonia goudotii

Vernonia goudotii, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its rough, fissured bark and small, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in dense, cylindrical clusters.

Vernonia goudotii is valued for its hard, heavy wood and is often used in traditional medicine.
