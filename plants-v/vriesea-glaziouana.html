Vriesea glaziouana

Vriesea glaziouana, commonly known as the Glaziouana bromeliad, is a striking evergreen plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, colorful inflorescence that resembles a bottlebrush. The plant features a rosette of broad, waxy leaves that collect water, creating a micro-ecosystem for small organisms. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Vriesea glaziouana is popular as a decorative plant due to its long-lasting blooms and unique appearance.
