Vignea × limnogena

Vignea × limnogena, commonly known as the hybrid water bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its aquatic or semi-aquatic growth habit, often found in wetlands and along the edges of water bodies. The plant has small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a waxy surface to reduce water loss.

Vignea × limnogena plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for various invertebrates and small fish.
