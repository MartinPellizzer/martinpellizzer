Vitex finlaysoniana

Vitex finlaysoniana, commonly known as the Finlayson's vitex, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are usually pubescent. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically lavender to purple in color, blooming in the summer months. Its fruit is a globose capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

Vitex finlaysoniana is often found in moist, lowland forests and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role.
