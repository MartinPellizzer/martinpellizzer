Vernonia friedrichsthalii

Vernonia friedrichsthalii, commonly known as the Friedrichsthal's ironweed, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open woodlands and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are usually blue to purple in color.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
