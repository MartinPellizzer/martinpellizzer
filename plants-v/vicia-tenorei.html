Vicia tenorei

Vicia tenorei, commonly known as the Tenore's vetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in rocky and coastal environments. The plant features slender, erect stems and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from pale yellow to violet. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

Vicia tenorei is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, contributing to soil fertility.
