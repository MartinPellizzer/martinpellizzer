Volkameria ligustrina var. ligustrina

Volkameria ligustrina var. ligustrina, commonly known as the Mexican hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 3 to 10 meters. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. Its flowers are showy, with five petals that range in color from white to pink or red, and they bloom in clusters.
