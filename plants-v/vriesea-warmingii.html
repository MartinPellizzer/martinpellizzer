Vriesea warmingii

Vriesea warmingii, commonly known as the Warming's Vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting inflorescence that can reach up to 3 meters in height. The plant features a rosette of grass-like leaves that collect water, creating a natural habitat for small organisms. Its flowers come in shades of red, orange, and yellow, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Vriesea warmingii thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity for optimal growth.
