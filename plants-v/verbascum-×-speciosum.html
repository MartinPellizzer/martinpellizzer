Verbascum × speciosum

Verbascum × speciosum, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the genus Verbascum, which includes over 200 species of flowering plants. The plant is characterized by its tall, hairy stems and large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of cultivation, it is widely grown in horticulture.
