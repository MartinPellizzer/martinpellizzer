Vignea gebhardii

Vignea gebhardii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and velvety, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires indirect sunlight to prevent leaf scorching.

Vignea gebhardii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
