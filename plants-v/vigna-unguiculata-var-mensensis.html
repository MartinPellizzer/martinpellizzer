Vigna unguiculata var. mensensis

Vigna unguiculata var. mensensis, commonly known as the African cowpea, is a climbing legume native to Africa.

It produces small, edible pods that contain nutrient-rich seeds used in various cuisines. The plant thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated for its high protein content. Its leaves are also consumed as a vegetable in many regions.

This variety is valued for its adaptability and contribution to sustainable agriculture.
