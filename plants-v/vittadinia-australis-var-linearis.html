Vittadinia australis var. linearis

Vittadinia australis var. linearis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf everlasting, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces small, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals. The leaves are narrow and linear, giving the plant its specific variety name. It is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and heathlands.

The plant is adapted to withstand drought and is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience.
