Vismia macrophylla var. glabrescens

Vismia macrophylla var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth-leaved evergreen.

It is a tropical shrub or small tree native to regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves that are smooth and leathery in texture. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by bright red, fleshy berries that are edible for some bird species.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm climates for its aesthetic and ecological benefits.
