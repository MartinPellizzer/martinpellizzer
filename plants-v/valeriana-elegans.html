Valeriana elegans

Valeriana elegans, commonly known as the elegant valerian, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows up to 1 meter in height, featuring upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually pink or white, arranged in dense spikes. Valeriana elegans is known for its aromatic properties and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
