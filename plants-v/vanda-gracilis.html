Vanda gracilis

Vanda gracilis, commonly known as the slender vanda, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, pendant inflorescences and showy, fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and purple. The plant has strap-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Vanda gracilis is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
