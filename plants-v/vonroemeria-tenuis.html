Vonroemeria tenuis

Vonroemeria tenuis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved vonroemeria, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically less than 10 cm in length, with a bluish-green hue. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in panicle-like clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine environments.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
