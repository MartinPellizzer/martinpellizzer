Vellozia brevifolia var. angustior

Vellozia brevifolia var. angustior, commonly known as the narrow-leaved vellozia, is a unique plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, adding a delicate touch to its otherwise spartan appearance. It is adapted to survive in harsh, dry conditions with minimal water, showcasing remarkable drought tolerance.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
