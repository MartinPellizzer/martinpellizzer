Votomita monadelpha

Votomita monadelpha, commonly known as the Mexican feather grass, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spikelet-like inflorescences that are often adorned with silky, feathery bristles. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, typically ranging from 10 to 30 centimeters in length.
