Vernonia ruvungatundu

Vernonia ruvungatundu, commonly known as the red vernonia, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and robust growth habit. The plant typically grows in open woodlands and grasslands, thriving in well-drained soils. Its striking red flowers appear in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds.

The leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
