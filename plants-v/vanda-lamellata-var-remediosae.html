Vanda lamellata var. remediosae

Vanda lamellata var. remediosae, commonly known as the Remedios Vanda, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically has erect, terete pseudobulbs and long, strap-like leaves. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
