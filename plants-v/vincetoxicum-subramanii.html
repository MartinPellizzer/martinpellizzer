Vincetoxicum subramanii

Vincetoxicum subramanii, commonly known as the Himalayan false bindweed, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. Its seeds are dispersed by wind, contributing to its spread in suitable habitats.

Vincetoxicum subramanii is often found in alpine meadows and forest understories at high elevations.
