Vernonia doniana

Vernonia doniana, commonly known as African ironwood, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, straight stem and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flower heads that are often clustered in dense, spherical or cylindrical shapes. Vernonia doniana is known for its hard, heavy wood, which is highly valued for construction and carving.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland and savanna habitats.
