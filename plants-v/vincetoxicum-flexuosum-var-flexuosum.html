Vincetoxicum flexuosum var. flexuosum

Vincetoxicum flexuosum var. flexuosum, commonly known as the creeping spindle tree, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, which are typically ovate to elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. Its fruits are follicles that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
