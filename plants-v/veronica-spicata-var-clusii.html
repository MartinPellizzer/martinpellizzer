Veronica spicata var. clusii

Veronica spicata var. clusii, commonly known as the Clusius spike-heath, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and spikes of blue to purple flowers. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, tubular flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and borders.
