Vernonanthura nudiflora f. albiflora

Vernonanthura nudiflora f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered vernonanthura, is a variant of the species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive white flowers, which contrast with the typical pink or red blooms of the species. The plant is a shrub with narrow, linear leaves and a compact growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in suitable climates.
