Veronica trichadena var. trichadena

Veronica trichadena var. trichadena, commonly known as the slender speedwell, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, blue or purple flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
