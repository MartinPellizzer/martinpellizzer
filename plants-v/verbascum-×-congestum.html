Verbascum × congestum

Verbascum × congestum, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the genus Verbascum, which includes many species of flowering plants known for their fuzzy leaves and showy flowers. The hybrid is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, velvety leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in clusters at the top of the plant.

This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy choice for gardens and natural habitats.
