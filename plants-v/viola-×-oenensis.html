Viola × oenensis

Viola × oenensis, commonly known as the Dutch Violin or Viola tricolor, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of colorful flowers that typically display violet, white, and yellow hues. The plant is native to Europe and has been widely cultivated for its ornamental value. Viola × oenensis thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial shade.

Its flowers bloom from spring to early summer, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking vibrant, early-season color.
