Verbena lucaeana

Verbena lucaeana, commonly known as the Lucaeana vervain, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean region.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from violet to blue. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.

Verbena lucaeana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
