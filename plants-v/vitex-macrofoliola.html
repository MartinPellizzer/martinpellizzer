Vitex macrofoliola

Vitex macrofoliola, commonly known as the large-leaf chaste tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate to elliptical leaves that are often large and glossy. The plant produces fragrant, clustered flowers that range in color from white to purple, typically blooming in summer. Its fruit is a dry, woody capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

Vitex macrofoliola is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
