Viviania klotzschii

Viviania klotzschii, commonly known as the Cape mountain lily, is a perennial herb native to the Western Cape region of South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Viviania klotzschii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.
