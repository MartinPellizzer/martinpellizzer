Veronica linearis

Veronica linearis, commonly known as narrowleaf speedwell, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and clusters of small blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. It is a perennial herb that produces numerous seeds, aiding in its spread.

Its botanical features make it a common sight in gardens and natural areas.
