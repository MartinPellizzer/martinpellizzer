Vitis micans

Vitis micans, commonly known as the silver vine, is a flowering vine native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and heart-shaped leaves that have a silvery sheen on the underside. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that give way to blue-black berries. Vitis micans thrives in temperate climates and is often found in woodland areas.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
