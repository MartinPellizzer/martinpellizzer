Vismia reichardtiana

Vismia reichardtiana, commonly known as the Reichardt's evergreen, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has limited use in traditional medicine.
