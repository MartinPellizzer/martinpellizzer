Vernonia odoratissima var. caracasana

Vernonia odoratissima var. caracasana, commonly known as the Caracas Vernonia, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Venezuela.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height, characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and showy yellow to orange flower heads. The plant thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in dry to semi-arid regions.

Its flowers are typically arranged in dense, spherical clusters, and the plant is noted for its aromatic foliage and medicinal properties.
