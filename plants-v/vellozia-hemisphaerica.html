Vellozia hemisphaerica

Vellozia hemisphaerica, commonly known as the giant cycad, is a striking plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, fern-like leaves that can reach lengths of up to 3 meters. The plant produces unique, cone-like inflorescences that are often covered in a dense layer of silky, white hairs. Vellozia hemisphaerica is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal rainfall.

Its slow growth rate and long lifespan make it a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
