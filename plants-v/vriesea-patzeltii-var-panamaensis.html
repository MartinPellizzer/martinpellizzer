Vriesea patzeltii var. panamaensis

Vriesea patzeltii var. panamaensis, commonly known as the Panama Vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, spathaceous inflorescence that displays a range of colors, often including red, orange, and yellow. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that collect water, forming a central pool. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is well-suited for indoor cultivation.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for tropical gardens and as a houseplant.
