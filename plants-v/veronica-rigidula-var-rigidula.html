Veronica rigidula var. rigidula

Veronica rigidula var. rigidula, commonly known as rigid speedwell, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, blue to violet flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from March to May. The plant has upright, branched stems covered in fine, hairy foliage. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Its compact size and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
