Vachellia Farnesiana F. Typica

Vachellia farnesiana f. typica is a distinctive shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa and the Middle East. Known for its yellow flowers and thorny stems, it thrives in harsh environments with minimal water. This plant is often used for erosion control and as a source of fuelwood in local communities. Its ecological importance supports various wildlife species. Despite its hardy nature, it requires careful management to prevent overgrazing and habitat degradation.
