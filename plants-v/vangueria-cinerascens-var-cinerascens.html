Vangueria cinerascens var. cinerascens

Vangueria cinerascens var. cinerascens, commonly known as the grey vangueria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant has greyish-green, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
