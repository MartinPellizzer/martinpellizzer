Verbascum × lemenciacum

Verbascum × lemenciacum, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial plant belonging to the Scrophulariaceae family.

It is a hybrid between Verbascum blattaria and Verbascum phlomoides, resulting in a plant with distinctive gray-green foliage and showy, velvety yellow flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats. Its fluffy seed heads are adapted for wind dispersal, aiding in its propagation.

Verbascum × lemenciacum is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
