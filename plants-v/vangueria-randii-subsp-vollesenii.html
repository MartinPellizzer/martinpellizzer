Vangueria randii subsp. vollesenii

Vangueria randii subsp.

vollesenii, commonly known as the Cape vangueria, is a shrub native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its small, dark green leaves that are opposite and oval in shape.

The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by red, fleshy fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
