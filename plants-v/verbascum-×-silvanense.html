Verbascum × silvanense

Verbascum × silvanense, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial plant belonging to the Scrophulariaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, fuzzy, gray-green leaves and tall, spire-like flower clusters that bloom in shades of yellow to orange. Native to the Mediterranean region, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. It is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats, and it is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.

The plant's fuzzy leaves and showy flowers make it a distinctive and easily recognizable species.
