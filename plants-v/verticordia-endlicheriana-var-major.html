Verticordia endlicheriana var. major

Verticordia endlicheriana var. major, commonly known as the Sturt's Desert Pea, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Western Australia.

It is characterized by its striking crimson or purple flowers, which are typically borne on slender, erect stems. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces distinctive seed pods that split open to release its seeds. It is adapted to survive in harsh, dry conditions, often flowering after rainfall.

This species is of interest to botanists and conservationists due to its unique ecological role and limited distribution.
