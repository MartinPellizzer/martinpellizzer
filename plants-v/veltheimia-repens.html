Veltheimia repens

Veltheimia repens, commonly known as the African fernbloom, is a perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features grass-like leaves that are narrow and arching, often forming a dense rosette. The plant produces striking, pale blue or violet flowers on slender stalks during the spring season. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Veltheimia repens is a popular choice for rock gardens and containers due to its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
