Veronica maddenii

Veronica maddenii, commonly known as the alpine speedwell, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, blue or violet flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in a fine layer of white hair.

Veronica maddenii thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky habitats.
