Vignea × schuetzeana

Vignea × schuetzeana, commonly known as the hybrid vigna, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its climbing habit and compound leaves. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink, followed by lens-shaped pods containing edible seeds.

Vignea × schuetzeana is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial in agricultural settings.
