Viola tricolor var. pallescens

Viola tricolor var. pallescens, commonly known as the pale violet, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe.

It features trifoliate leaves with heart-shaped lobes and produces flowers that range from pale purple to white, depending on the light conditions. The plant prefers moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full shade. Its flowers bloom in early spring, adding delicate color to woodland gardens.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to naturalize in suitable habitats.
