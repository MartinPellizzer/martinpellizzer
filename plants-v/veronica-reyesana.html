Veronica reyesana

Veronica reyesana, commonly known as the Reyes' speedwell, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant produces small, blue or purple flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with a slightly hairy texture.
