Vallota × hybrida

Vallota × hybrida, commonly known as the hybrid marsh orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetland and marsh environments.

It typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm, featuring erect, leafy stems and clusters of showy, fragrant flowers. The flowers are usually pink or purple, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color. It thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils and is often found in regions with temperate climates.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in wetland ecosystems.
