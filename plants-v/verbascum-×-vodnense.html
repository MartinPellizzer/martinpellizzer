Verbascum × vodnense

Verbascum × vodnense, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the genus Verbascum, which includes several species of flowering plants known for their fuzzy, velvety leaves. The hybrid is characterized by its tall, erect stem and showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable plant for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
