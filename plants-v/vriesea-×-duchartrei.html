Vriesea × duchartrei

Vriesea × duchartrei, commonly known as the hybrid Vriesea, is a tropical evergreen plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful inflorescence that can last for several months. The plant features rosettes of broad, waxy leaves that can vary in color from green to blue or silver. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Vriesea × duchartrei is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value and ability to thrive in indoor environments.
