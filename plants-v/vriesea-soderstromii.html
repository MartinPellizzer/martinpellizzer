Vriesea soderstromii

Vriesea soderstromii, commonly known as the Soderstrom's Vriesea, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting inflorescence that typically displays shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that collect water, creating a small pool that supports aquatic life. Its flowers bloom in a spiral arrangement, adding a dramatic visual appeal.

Vriesea soderstromii thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
