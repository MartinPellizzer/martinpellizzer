Verbesina tequilana

Verbesina tequilana, commonly known as Mexican sunflower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its bright orange to red tubular flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Verbesina tequilana is also noted for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
