Verbascum euboicum

Verbascum euboicum, commonly known as the Euboean mullein, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky soils and sunny environments. The plant features large, velvety leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. Its showy yellow flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Verbascum euboicum is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical medicinal uses.
