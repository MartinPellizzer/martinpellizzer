Vallisneria spiralis f. aethiopica

Vallisneria spiralis f. aethiopica, commonly known as the African waterweed, is a submerged aquatic plant native to Africa.

It features long, ribbon-like leaves that can grow up to 2 meters in length, providing habitat for various aquatic organisms. This plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments with moderate to high light levels. It is often used in aquariums due to its aesthetic appeal and ability to oxygenate water.

Its rapid growth rate makes it an effective tool for waterweed control in ponds and lakes.
