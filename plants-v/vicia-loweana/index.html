Vicia Loweana

Vicia loweana is a rare flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa. It belongs to the legume family and is known for its delicate, pea-like flowers that bloom in late spring. This species is often found in rocky habitats and is adapted to thrive in nutrient-poor soils. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered vulnerable to environmental changes. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival in the wild.
