Vanda peduncularis

Vanda peduncularis, commonly known as the Singapore orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, pendulous inflorescence that bears numerous fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves and thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light. It requires well-draining potting media and regular watering to prevent root rot.

Vanda peduncularis is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its striking floral display.
