Valteta gesneroides

Valteta gesneroides, commonly known as the flame violet, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant produces showy, spurred petals that attract pollinators such as bees and hummingbirds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Valteta gesneroides is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.
