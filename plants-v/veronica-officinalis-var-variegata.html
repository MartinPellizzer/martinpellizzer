Veronica officinalis var. variegata

Veronica officinalis var. variegata, commonly known as the variegated speedwell, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial with small, blue or purple flowers that bloom in early summer. The leaves are typically oval-shaped and have a distinctive variegation pattern of white and green. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

This plant is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.
