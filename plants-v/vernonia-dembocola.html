Vernonia dembocola

Vernonia dembocola, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant is known for its hard, dense wood, which is highly valued for construction and tool-making. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in savannas and woodlands.

Its ecological importance includes providing habitat and food for various insects and birds.
