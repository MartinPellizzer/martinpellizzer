Vriesea viridiflora

Vriesea viridiflora, commonly known as the Green-flowered Vriesea, is a tropical bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, grass-like foliage and striking, long-lasting inflorescence that displays a mix of green and yellow hues. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and is well-suited for indoor cultivation. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine in its native regions.

Vriesea viridiflora is prized for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
