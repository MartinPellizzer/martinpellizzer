Vriesea costae

Vriesea costae, commonly known as the sword fern or the rainbow bromeliad, is a striking epiphytic plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender, sword-like leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant produces vibrant, long-lasting inflorescences that range in color from red to orange to yellow, adding dramatic visual interest. Vriesea costae thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil to flourish.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardens.
