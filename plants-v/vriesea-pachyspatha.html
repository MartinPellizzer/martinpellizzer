Vriesea pachyspatha

Vriesea pachyspatha, commonly known as the broad-leafed vriesea, is a species of flowering plant in the Bromeliad family.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its striking, long-lasting inflorescence. The plant features a rosette of broad, glossy, dark green leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. Its flowers, which are typically orange or yellow, bloom in a spiral pattern and can last for several months.

Vriesea pachyspatha is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
