Veronica californica

Veronica californica, commonly known as California speedwell, is a flowering plant native to California.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its delicate blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and chaparral. It has slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

Veronica californica is often used in native plant gardens due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
