Vriesea latissima

Vriesea latissima, commonly known as the giant bromeliad, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, broad, and often colorful leaves that form a rosette shape. The plant produces striking inflorescences with vibrant, long-lasting bracts that can range in color from red to orange. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Vriesea latissima is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
