Vanillosmopsis bicolor

Vanillosmopsis bicolor, commonly known as the two-colored vanilla orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking bicolor flowers, which typically feature a combination of yellow and purple hues. The plant produces fragrant, vanilla-scented blossoms that are highly valued in horticulture for their aesthetic appeal. It thrives in warm, humid environments with well-drained soil and partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
