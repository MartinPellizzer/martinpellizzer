Verbascum × ambiguum

Verbascum × ambiguum, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the genus Verbascum, which includes species known for their large, showy flowers. The hybrid is characterized by its tall, hairy stems and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in rocky or arid environments.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used traditionally in herbal medicine.
