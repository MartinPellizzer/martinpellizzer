Vandenboschia × stenosiphon

Vandenboschia × stenosiphon, commonly known as the narrow-siphon chenopod, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy stems and leaves, which are adapted to arid environments. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils, often in semi-desert regions. It produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences.

Due to its resilience and ability to thrive in harsh conditions, it is often found in coastal and inland desert areas.
