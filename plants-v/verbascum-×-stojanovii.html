Verbascum × stojanovii

Verbascum × stojanovii, commonly known as the hybrid mullein, is a perennial plant belonging to the genus Verbascum in the family Scrophulariaceae.

It is characterized by its large, velvety, gray-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. It is native to the Balkan Peninsula and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
