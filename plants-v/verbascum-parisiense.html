Verbascum parisiense

Verbascum parisiense, commonly known as the Paris mullein, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of western Asia.

Verbascum parisiense is often found in dry, disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of habitats including roadsides and meadows.
