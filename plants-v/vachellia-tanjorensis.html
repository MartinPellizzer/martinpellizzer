Vachellia tanjorensis

Vachellia tanjorensis, commonly known as the Tanjore acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it resilient to drought conditions.

It plays a role in soil stabilization and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
