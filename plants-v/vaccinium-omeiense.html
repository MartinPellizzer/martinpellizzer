Vaccinium omeiense

Vaccinium omeiense, commonly known as the Omei blueberry, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Sichuan, China.

It is a shrub that typically grows in cool, moist environments at high altitudes. The plant produces small, edible berries that are rich in antioxidants and have a tart flavor. Its leaves are evergreen, elliptical, and covered in fine hairs.

The plant is valued both for its ornamental qualities and its potential use in local cuisine and traditional medicine.
