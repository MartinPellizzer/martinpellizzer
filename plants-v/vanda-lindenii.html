Vanda lindenii

Vanda lindenii, commonly known as the Lindenberg's vanda, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It thrives in tropical rainforests, typically growing on trees or rocks. The plant features long, narrow leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It requires bright, indirect light and consistent humidity to flourish.

Vanda lindenii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
