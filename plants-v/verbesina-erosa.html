Verbesina erosa

Verbesina erosa, commonly known as yellow ironweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stems and clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has palmately lobed leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for native plant gardens.

Verbesina erosa is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
