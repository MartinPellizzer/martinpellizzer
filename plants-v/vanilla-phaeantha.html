Vanilla phaeantha

Vanilla phaeantha, commonly known as the Hawaiian vanilla orchid, is a species native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a terrestrial orchid that produces striking violet-blue flowers with white throats and purple markings. The plant has a fibrous root system and thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its flowers are pollinated by a specific species of bee found only in Hawaii, making it a unique ecological relationship.

The plant is also valued for its fragrant scent and has been used in traditional Hawaiian practices.
