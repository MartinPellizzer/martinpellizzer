Vicia mathewsii

Vicia mathewsii, commonly known as Mathews' vetch, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, trailing stems and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant produces trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Vicia mathewsii is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.
