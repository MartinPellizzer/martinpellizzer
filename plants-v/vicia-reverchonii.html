Vicia reverchonii

Vicia reverchonii, commonly known as the Reverchon vetch, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-bearing fruits. Its small, pea-like flowers are usually pink or purple, and it produces slender, cylindrical pods that contain several seeds.

Vicia reverchonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in ecological restoration projects.
