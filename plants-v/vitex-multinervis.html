Vitex multinervis

Vitex multinervis, commonly known as the narrow-veined chaste tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters tall. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with prominent veins, which give it its scientific name. Its small, fragrant flowers are typically clustered in terminal panicles and bloom in the summer.

The fruit is a small, dry capsule that splits open to release the seeds.
