Viola vilmoriniana

Viola vilmoriniana, commonly known as the violet vilmoriniana, is a species of flowering plant in the genus Viola.

It is native to Europe and is characterized by its delicate, heart-shaped leaves and showy, violet-blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Viola vilmoriniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
