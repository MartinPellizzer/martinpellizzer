Vernonia elaeagnoides

Vernonia elaeagnoides, commonly known as silverleaf vernonia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name, and its upright growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the cooler months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Vernonia elaeagnoides is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
