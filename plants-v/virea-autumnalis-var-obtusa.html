Virea autumnalis var. obtusa

Virea autumnalis var. obtusa, commonly known as the broadleaf virea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, tubular flowers. The leaves are typically broad and ovate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and adaptability to various garden conditions.
