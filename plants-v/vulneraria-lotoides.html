Vulneraria lotoides

Vulneraria lotoides, commonly known as the narrowleaf vulnerary, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Vulneraria lotoides is typically found in grassy areas and along roadsides, thriving in well-drained soils.
