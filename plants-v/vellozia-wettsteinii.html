Vellozia wettsteinii

Vellozia wettsteinii, commonly known as the Wettstein's vellozia, is a striking succulent native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, adapted to its arid environment. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers during the winter months, which are borne on long, slender stalks. Its unique morphology and coloration make it a popular specimen among succulent enthusiasts.

Vellozia wettsteinii is also notable for its slow growth and ability to thrive in well-drained, sandy soils.
