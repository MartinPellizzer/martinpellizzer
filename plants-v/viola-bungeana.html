Viola bungeana

Viola bungeana, commonly known as the Chinese violet, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Violaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of violet or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides. Its flowers bloom in spring, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Viola bungeana is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been cultivated for both garden use and traditional herbal purposes.
