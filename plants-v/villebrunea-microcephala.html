Villebrunea microcephala

Villebrunea microcephala, commonly known as the dwarf banyan, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Moraceae and is characterized by its small, rounded leaves and distinctive aerial roots. The tree is also noted for its ability to produce a milky sap, which has been used in traditional medicine.
