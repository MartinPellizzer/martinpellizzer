Vicia sativa var. willkommii

Vicia sativa var. willkommii, commonly known as the Willkomm's vetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in temperate regions, often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides. The plant features trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from pink to purple. Its seed pods are elongated and contain several small, kidney-shaped seeds.

This variety is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it useful in ecological restoration and agricultural settings.
