Vernonia nitidula var. lucida

Vernonia nitidula var. lucida, commonly known as the shining ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features upright, branching stems covered in fine, hairy foliage. Its showy, yellow-orange flower heads appear in late summer to early fall.

It is often found in moist, open habitats such as swamps and wet meadows.
