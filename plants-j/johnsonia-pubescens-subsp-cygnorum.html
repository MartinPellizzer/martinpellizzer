cygnorum, commonly known as the Cygnorum Johnsonia, is a rare and distinctive plant native to specific regions in Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its unique leaf morphology, featuring broad, lance-shaped leaves with a hairy underside.

The plant produces striking inflorescences with small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in forest understories.
