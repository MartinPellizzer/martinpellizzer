Justicia piauhiensis

Justicia piauhiensis, commonly known as the Piauhi flame flower, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking red-orange bracts that resemble flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a rough texture, while its showy inflorescences attract various pollinators.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in tropical gardens.
