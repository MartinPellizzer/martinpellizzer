Jacquemontia evolvuloides f. albiflora

Jacquemontia evolvuloides f. albiflora, commonly known as the whiteflowered jacquemontia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family, characterized by its climbing or trailing growth habit. The plant features showy, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often have a velvety texture, while its stems are hairy and twining.
