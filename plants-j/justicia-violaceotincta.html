Justicia violaceotincta

Justicia violaceotincta, commonly known as violet tinge justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking violet-pink flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to its surroundings. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The botanical features of Justicia violaceotincta make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various climates.
