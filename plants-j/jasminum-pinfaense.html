Jasminum pinfaense

Jasminum pinfaense, commonly known as the Pinfaense jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Oleaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate with a glossy surface.

The flowers emit a strong, sweet aroma, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens and landscapes.
