Justicia comata

Justicia comata, commonly known as the silky justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant features slender, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, soft hairs. Its flowers, which can be blue, purple, or white, are arranged in spikes and attract various pollinators.

Justicia comata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
