Jasminum angolense

Jasminum angolense, commonly known as the Angola jasmine, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, featuring aromatic white flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are simple, opposite, and have a glossy green surface with a slightly serrated edge. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and disturbed habitats.
