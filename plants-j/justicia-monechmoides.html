Justicia monechmoides

Justicia monechmoides, commonly known as the purple passionflower, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking violet-blue flowers that resemble those of the passionfruit. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly rough texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
