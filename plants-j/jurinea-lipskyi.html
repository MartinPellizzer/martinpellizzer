Jurinea lipskyi

Jurinea lipskyi, commonly known as the desert sandflower, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Central Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that thrives in sandy and gravelly soils, often found in steppes and deserts. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in a fine layer of hair to reduce water loss.

Jurinea lipskyi is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water availability.
