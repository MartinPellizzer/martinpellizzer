Jumellea maxillarioides

Jumellea maxillarioides, commonly known as the narrow-leaf orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to South Africa.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome and produces linear, grass-like leaves. The plant blooms with small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a raceme, often with a faint fragrance. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
