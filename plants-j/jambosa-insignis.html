Jambosa insignis

Jambosa insignis, commonly known as the African star gooseberry, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, star-shaped leaves and clusters of yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces small, sour-tasting berries that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens in arid regions.
