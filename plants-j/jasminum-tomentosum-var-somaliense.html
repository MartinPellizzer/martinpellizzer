Jasminum tomentosum var. somaliense

Jasminum tomentosum var. somaliense, commonly known as the Somali jasmine, is a flowering plant native to the Horn of Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage and fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often cultivated as a decorative ornamental. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Its unique appearance and pleasant aroma make it a popular choice for gardens and indoor plant collections.
