Justicia meyeniana

Justicia meyeniana, commonly known as the Mexican flame flower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It is a member of the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 feet. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.
