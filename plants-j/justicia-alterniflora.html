Justicia alterniflora

Justicia alterniflora, commonly known as the alternate-flower justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, pink, or purple. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its rapid growth and ability to spread via stolons make it both a decorative and sometimes invasive species.
