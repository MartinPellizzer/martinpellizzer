Jenkinsia undulata

Jenkinsia undulata, commonly known as the undulating fern, is a unique vascular plant characterized by its intricate, undulating fronds.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia. Jenkinsia undulata is often found in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors and rocky crevices.
