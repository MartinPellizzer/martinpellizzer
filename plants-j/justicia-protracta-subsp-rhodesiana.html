Justicia protracta subsp. rhodesiana

Justicia protracta subsp.

rhodesiana, commonly known as the Rhodesian spider flower, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa. It features striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, attracting pollinators such as birds and insects.

The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces capsules as its fruit. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
