Jasione montana f. rossularis

Jasione montana f. rossularis, commonly known as the Rossularis rock jasmine, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit.
