Justicia rubicunda

Justicia rubicunda, commonly known as red flame or scarlet flame, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking red or orange tubular flowers. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Justicia rubicunda is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
