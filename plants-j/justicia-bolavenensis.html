Justicia bolavenensis

Justicia bolavenensis, commonly known as the Bolaven flame bush, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red-orange flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a striking visual display. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, well-drained soils. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture, while its stems are woody and branched.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of garden settings.
