Juncus Tingitanus

Juncus tingitanus is a unique plant species native to the coastal regions of North Africa. It thrives in saline and humid environments, often found near the shores of the Mediterranean Sea. This plant is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and ability to withstand harsh coastal conditions. Its presence plays a vital role in stabilizing dunes and supporting local biodiversity. Due to habitat loss and climate change, Juncus tingitanus faces increasing threats to its survival.
