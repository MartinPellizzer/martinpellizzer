Justicia phytolaccoides

Justicia phytolaccoides, commonly known as the purpleleaf justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking purple foliage and clusters of small, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of pink to red. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes in warm climates.
