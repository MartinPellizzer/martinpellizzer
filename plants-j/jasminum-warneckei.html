Jasminum warneckei

Jasminum warneckei, commonly known as the West African jasmine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 3 meters. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

Jasminum warneckei is valued for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and as a hedge plant.
