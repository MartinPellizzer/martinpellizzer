Jasione heldreichii var. papillosa

Jasione heldreichii var. papillosa, commonly known as the Papillate Bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and showy, blue to purple bell-shaped flowers.
