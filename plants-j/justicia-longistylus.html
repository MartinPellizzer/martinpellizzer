Justicia longistylus

Justicia longistylus, commonly known as the long-styled justicia, is a perennial herb native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of tubular bracts that enclose its small, clustered flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded to partially sunny environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
