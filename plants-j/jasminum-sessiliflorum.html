Jasminum sessiliflorum

Jasminum sessiliflorum, commonly known as the winter jasmine, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in winter, making it a unique ornamental plant. The leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate, with a dark green upper surface and a lighter underside. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

This plant is often used in gardens for its early blooming and pleasant fragrance.
