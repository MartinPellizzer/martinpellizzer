Juniperus pingii

Juniperus pingii, commonly known as the Chinese juniper, is a small evergreen shrub or tree native to China.

It belongs to the Cupressaceae family and is characterized by its dense, scale-like leaves and small, berry-like cones. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous areas, preferring well-drained soils and full sun. Its foliage is often a deep green, and it can reach heights of up to 5 meters.

Juniperus pingii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional Chinese medicine.
