Justicia plumbaginifolia

Justicia plumbaginifolia, commonly known as the blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in dense clusters. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and can grow as an annual or perennial depending on the climate. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
