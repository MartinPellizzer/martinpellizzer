Julocroton malvoides

Julocroton malvoides, commonly known as the Malvoides croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from yellow to red. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
