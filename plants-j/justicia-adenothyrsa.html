Justicia adenothyrsa

Justicia adenothyrsa, commonly known as the woolly false arum, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, spadix-like inflorescence surrounded by a colorful bract. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine white or gray pubescence. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically green or white in color.

Justicia adenothyrsa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
