Jasminocereus Thouarsii

Jasminocereus thouarsii is a rare and striking cactus native to Madagascar. It is known for its elegant white flowers that bloom at night, releasing a sweet jasmine-like fragrance. This plant thrives in arid environments and requires minimal water to survive. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively obscure species in the world of botany.
