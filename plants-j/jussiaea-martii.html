Jussiaea martii

Jussiaea martii, commonly known as the swamp willow, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows in wetland environments, such as marshes and along riverbanks. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often covered in a fine, silky hair. Its small, yellowish flowers are clustered in catkins and bloom in the spring.
