Jasminum scandens var. genuinum

Jasminum scandens var. genuinum, commonly known as the Chinese jasmine or evergreen jasmine, is a climbing shrub native to southern China.

It features fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite and ovate in shape. It thrives in partial to full sun and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden trellises and as a hedge plant.
