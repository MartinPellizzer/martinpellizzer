Juglans Sieboldiana F. Acutissima

Juglans sieboldiana f. acutissima is a unique variant of the Japanese walnut known for its strikingly sharp, pointed leaves. This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and is often planted in gardens for its aesthetic appeal. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish. The tree produces edible nuts that are highly sought after for their rich flavor. Its distinct foliage makes it a standout choice for landscape design and botanical interest.
