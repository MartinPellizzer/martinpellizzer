Justicia lenticellata

Justicia lenticellata, commonly known as the smooth-leaved false arum, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It features striking, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically smooth and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in summer. Its stems are often hairy, and the leaves may have subtle lenticellate markings.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
