Jarava media

Jarava media, commonly known as blue grama, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green blades and tufted growth habit. The plant thrives in dry, open habitats such as prairies and sandy soils. Its small, spike-like inflorescence contains numerous tiny flowers that are wind-pollinated.

Jarava media plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing forage for wildlife.
