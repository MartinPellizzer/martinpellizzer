Justicia calzadillae

Justicia calzadillae, commonly known as the Calzadilla flameflower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture.
