Justicia latior

Justicia latior, commonly known as the broadleaf justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that come in shades of pink, red, and purple. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 3 feet. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, with a smooth texture and a vibrant green color.

Justicia latior thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
