Justicia striata var. dyschoristeoides

Justicia striata var. dyschoristeoides, commonly known as the purple-flowered justicia, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It features upright stems covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slight pubescence. The plant produces showy spikes of tubular, violet to purple flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
