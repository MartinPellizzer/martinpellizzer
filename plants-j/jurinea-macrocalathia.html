Jurinea macrocalathia

Jurinea macrocalathia, commonly known as the broad-leaved jurinea, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves and showy yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia, this plant is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments. Its growth habit is typically low and sprawling, with a thick, succulent stem that helps store water.

The plant plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native habitat and is of interest to botanists studying xerophytic adaptations.
