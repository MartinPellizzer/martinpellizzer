Justicia eriniae

Justicia eriniae, commonly known as the red-veined justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves with distinct red veins, giving it a unique visual appeal. Justicia eriniae is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant foliage and blooming habit.
