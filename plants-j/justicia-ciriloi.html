Justicia ciriloi

Justicia ciriloi, commonly known as the Cape rose or red bush lily, is a striking flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red or orange tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and a woody stem that can grow up to several meters in height. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and ability to attract pollinators like birds and bees.
