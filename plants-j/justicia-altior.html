Justicia altior

Justicia altior, commonly known as the tall verbena, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows to a height of 2 to 6 feet and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged oppositely along the stems.

Justicia altior is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
