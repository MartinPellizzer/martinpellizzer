Justicia clarkeana

Justicia clarkeana, commonly known as Clarke's glorybower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in clusters, often in shades of red, orange, or yellow. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
