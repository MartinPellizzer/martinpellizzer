Jasminum fluminense subsp. mauritianum

Jasminum fluminense subsp.

mauritianum, commonly known as the Mauritius jasmine, is a flowering plant native to the Mascarene Islands. It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters.

The plant has slender, woody stems and elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in coastal areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
