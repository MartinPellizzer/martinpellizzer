Juniperus squamata var. hongxiensis

Juniperus squamata var. hongxiensis, commonly known as the Hongxi juniper, is a shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, scale-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, berry-like cones that are often used in traditional Chinese medicine. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid climates.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitat.
