Juelia meyeri

Juelia meyeri, commonly known as the Meyer's juelia, is a unique and rare plant species native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, elongated leaves that often exhibit a striking variegation pattern. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from pale yellow to deep orange, attracting various pollinators. Its growth habit is typically low and spreading, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.
