Jacquinia berteroi subsp. portoricensis

Jacquinia berteroi subsp.

portoricensis, commonly known as the Puerto Rican Jacquinia, is a rare shrub native to the island of Puerto Rico. It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, bell-shaped flowers.
