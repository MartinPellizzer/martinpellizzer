Juncus brachyspathus var. curvatus

Juncus brachyspathus var. curvatus, commonly known as the curved rush, is a flowering plant belonging to the Juncaceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its dense, cylindrical clusters of grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish flowers that are typically arranged in spikes. It thrives in wet or marshy environments, often found in coastal areas and freshwater wetlands.

Its fibrous roots help stabilize soil and provide habitat for various aquatic organisms.
