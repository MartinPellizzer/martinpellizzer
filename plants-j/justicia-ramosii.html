Justicia ramosii

Justicia ramosii, commonly known as the red mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its showy, pink to red bracts that surround small white flowers, creating a striking visual display. The plant is a perennial shrub that thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil. Its foliage consists of opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture.

Justicia ramosii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
