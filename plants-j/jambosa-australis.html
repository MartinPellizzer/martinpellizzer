Jambosa australis

Jambosa australis, commonly known as the Australian jambosa, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal regions, characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. Its fruit, a small, round berry, is edible and has a sweet, tangy flavor.

Jambosa australis is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to coastal environments.
