Juncoides confusa

Juncoides confusa, commonly known as the confused rush, is a perennial flowering plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Juncaceae family and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, cylindrical leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish or brownish in color. It thrives in wet or moist environments such as marshes and stream banks.

Juncoides confusa is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and support local biodiversity.
