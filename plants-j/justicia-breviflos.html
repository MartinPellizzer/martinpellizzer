Justicia breviflos

Justicia breviflos, commonly known as the dwarf flame flower, is a herbaceous perennial native to Mexico and Central America.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often resembling flames. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with hairy surfaces, and it thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often grown as an ornamental plant for its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.

Justicia breviflos is relatively drought-tolerant once established, making it suitable for xeriscaping in warmer climates.
