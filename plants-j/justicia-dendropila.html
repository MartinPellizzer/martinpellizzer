Justicia dendropila

Justicia dendropila, commonly known as the hanging justicia, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, attracting pollinators like hummingbirds and bees. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses for its vibrant blooms.
