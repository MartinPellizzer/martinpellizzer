Justicia obscura

Justicia obscura, commonly known as the dark justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from violet to deep purple. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of 1 to 3 feet. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a rough, hairy texture.

Justicia obscura thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded to partially sunny environments.
