Juncoides intermedia var. echinata

Juncoides intermedia var. echinata, commonly known as the spiny rush, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Juncaceae.

It is characterized by its cylindrical, grass-like leaves and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy environments, preferring moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in temperate regions and can tolerate partial shade.

Its unique feature is the presence of spiny structures on the stems, which give it its common name.
