Justicia kouytcheensis

Justicia kouytcheensis, commonly known as the Kourou justicia, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of French Guiana.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal in tropical gardens.
