Jondraba cichoriifolia var. macrocarpa

Jondraba cichoriifolia var. macrocarpa, commonly known as the giant lettuce plant, is a variant of the lettuce family (Asteraceae) characterized by its large, lobed leaves.

It originates from South Africa and is known for its robust growth and ability to thrive in diverse soil conditions. The plant produces large, showy yellow flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are often used in local cuisines for their mild, lettuce-like flavor.

This variant is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its edible qualities.
