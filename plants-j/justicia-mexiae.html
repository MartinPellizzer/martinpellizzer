Justicia mexiae

Justicia mexiae, commonly known as Mexican flame flower, is a shrub native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or orange tubular flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Justicia mexiae is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like hummingbirds.
