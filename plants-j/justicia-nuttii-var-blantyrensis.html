Justicia nuttii var. blantyrensis

Justicia nuttii var. blantyrensis, commonly known as the Blantyren starflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
