Juno issica

Juno issica, commonly known as the silverleaf juniper, is a small evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features dense, needle-like leaves that are typically a bluish-gray color, providing a striking visual contrast to its rugged habitat. The plant produces small, cone-like structures that contain the seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Juno issica is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils and full sun exposure.

Its hardy nature makes it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping in dry climates.
