Justicia dolichopoda

Justicia dolichopoda, commonly known as the slender flame bush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking spikes of tubular, bright red to orange flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
