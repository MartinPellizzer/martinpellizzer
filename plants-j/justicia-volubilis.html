Justicia volubilis

Justicia volubilis, commonly known as the wandering jewel, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a trailing or climbing perennial that produces vibrant, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features opposite, narrow leaves and is known for its rapid growth habit. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Justicia volubilis is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive blooms and ability to cascade from containers or trellises.
