Jububa mediterranea

Jububa mediterranea, commonly known as the Mediterranean jububa, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is well adapted to arid environments. It produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Jububa mediterranea is often used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
