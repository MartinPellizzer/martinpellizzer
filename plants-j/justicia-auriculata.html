Justicia auriculata

Justicia auriculata, commonly known as the earleaf jewelweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its striking orange or red tubular flowers, which are often adorned with a distinctive ear-shaped leaf at the base. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered with fine hairs.

Justicia auriculata thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or along stream banks.
