Justicia yurimaguensis

Justicia yurimaguensis, commonly known as the Yurimaguense flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to Central America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular, brightly colored flowers that range from red to orange. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as along forest floors or in wetlands. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a rough texture.

Justicia yurimaguensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
