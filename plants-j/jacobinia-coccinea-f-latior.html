Jacobinia coccinea f. latior

Jacobinia coccinea f. latior, commonly known as the broad-leaved red jacobinia, is a cultivar of the Jacobinia genus native to South America.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are often variegated with yellow or white margins. The plant produces striking red-orange flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to gardens. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This ornamental plant is often used in landscaping for its ornate foliage and showy blooms.
