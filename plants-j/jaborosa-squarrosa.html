Jaborosa squarrosa

Jaborosa squarrosa, commonly known as the roughleaf jaborosa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grasslands and open areas, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are covered in fine, stiff hairs, giving them a rough texture.
