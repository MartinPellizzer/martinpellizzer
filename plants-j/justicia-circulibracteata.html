Justicia circulibracteata

Justicia circulibracteata, commonly known as the round-bract justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that are typically bright red or orange, arranged in clusters. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
