Juglans squamosa

Juglans squamosa, commonly known as the Chinese walnut, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Juglandaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves with serrated edges. The tree produces edible nuts enclosed in a spiky husk, which ripen in the fall. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forested areas.

The bark is thick and fissured, providing a rugged appearance.
