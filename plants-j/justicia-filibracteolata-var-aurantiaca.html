Justicia filibracteolata var. aurantiaca

Justicia filibracteolata var. aurantiaca, commonly known as the Orange Trumpet Plant, is a tropical shrub native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking orange to red tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators like hummingbirds and bees. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

This ornamental plant is often cultivated for its vibrant floral display and adaptability to warm climates.
