Justicia vittata

Justicia vittata, commonly known as the silverleaf justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage and showy clusters of blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
