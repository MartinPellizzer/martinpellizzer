Justicia brenesii

Justicia brenesii, commonly known as the Mexican flame flower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Justicia brenesii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
