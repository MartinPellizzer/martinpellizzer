Jonesiopsis × exoleta

Jonesiopsis × exoleta, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Jonesiopsis and Exoleta.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically produces inflorescences with numerous blooms, each featuring intricate patterns and vibrant colors. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Jonesiopsis × exoleta is often cultivated for its ornamental value in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
