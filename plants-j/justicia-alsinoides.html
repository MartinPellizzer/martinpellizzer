Justicia alsinoides

Justicia alsinoides, commonly known as the Mexican wheelcactus, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, wheel-like arrangement of leaves. The plant produces clusters of showy, tubular flowers that range in color from violet to red, attracting pollinators such as bees and hummingbirds. Justicia alsinoides is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in landscaping.
