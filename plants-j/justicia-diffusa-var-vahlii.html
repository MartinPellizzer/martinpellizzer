Justicia diffusa var. vahlii

Justicia diffusa var. vahlii, commonly known as the Vahl's flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with contrasting white or yellow markings. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically pubescent, giving it a velvety texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The species is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
