Justicia idiogenes

Justicia idiogenes, commonly known as the red hot poker, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It is characterized by its striking red bracts that surround clusters of small, tubular flowers. The plant belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is typically found in tropical and subtropical regions. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique appearance and vibrant color make it a standout in both garden landscapes and botanical collections.
