Justicia curviflora

Justicia curviflora, commonly known as the curlyflower justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of tubular, two-lipped flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Justicia curviflora is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
