Jasminum flexile var. hookerianum

Jasminum flexile var. hookerianum, commonly known as the Hooker's jasmine, is a climbing shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features slender, twining stems and produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are opposite, simple, and have a glossy, dark green appearance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant aroma.
