Jasminum stans

Jasminum stans, commonly known as the Chinese jasmine, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. The plant is a shrub or vine that can grow up to several meters in height, with opposite, ovate leaves. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.

The flowers are used in perfumery and traditional medicine, highlighting its versatility in both horticulture and commerce.
