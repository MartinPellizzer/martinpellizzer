Justicia reisensis

Justicia reisensis, commonly known as the Brazilian flame flower, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Justicia reisensis thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
