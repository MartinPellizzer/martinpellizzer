Justicia sulcata

Justicia sulcata, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, zigzagging stems. The plant produces clusters of bright red or orange tubular flowers, which attract pollinators such as bees and birds. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Justicia sulcata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant flowers and unique appearance.
