Jasminum schimperi

Jasminum schimperi, commonly known as Ethiopian jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer seasons. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Jasminum schimperi is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its aromatic flowers.
