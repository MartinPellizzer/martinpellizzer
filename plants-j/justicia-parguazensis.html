Justicia parguazensis

Justicia parguazensis, commonly known as the yellow flame plant, is a species of flowering plant in the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking yellow-orange tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. It typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter.

The leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal.
