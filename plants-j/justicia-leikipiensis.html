Justicia leikipiensis

Justicia leikipiensis, commonly known as the Lepani Justicia, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often covered with fine hairs.
