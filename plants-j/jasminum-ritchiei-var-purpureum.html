Jasminum ritchiei var. purpureum

Jasminum ritchiei var. purpureum, commonly known as the purple-flowered jasmine, is a species of flowering plant in the Oleaceae family.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, pale purple to deep violet flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate with a glossy surface.
