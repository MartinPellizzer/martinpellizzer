Justicia longii

Justicia longii, commonly known as the flame violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to violet. The plant typically grows as an herb or shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, with opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are borne in dense spikes and are known for their long-lasting color, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Justicia longii thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
