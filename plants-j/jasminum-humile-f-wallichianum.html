Jasminum humile f. wallichianum

Jasminum humile f. wallichianum, commonly known as the dwarf jasmine or Indian jasmine, is a compact evergreen shrub native to the Himalayas.

It features fragrant, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its pleasant aroma, which is especially noticeable in the evening.
