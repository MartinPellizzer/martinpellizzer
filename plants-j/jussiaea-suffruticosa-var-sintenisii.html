Jussiaea suffruticosa var. sintenisii

Jussiaea suffruticosa var. sintenisii, commonly known as the coastal jussiaea, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is a shrub-like species characterized by its small, fleshy leaves and clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. The plant thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in dune environments. It has adapted to salt spray and strong winds, making it a hardy species in coastal ecosystems.

Its flowers attract various pollinators, contributing to the biodiversity of its habitat.
