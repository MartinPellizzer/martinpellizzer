Justicia stearnii var. maestrensis

Justicia stearnii var. maestrensis, commonly known as the Maestrense Flame Flower, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular red flowers that bloom in dense clusters. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its long-lasting blooms and is often used in gardens and as a container plant.
