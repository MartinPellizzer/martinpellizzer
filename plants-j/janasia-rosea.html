Janasia rosea

Janasia rosea, commonly known as the pink velvet fern, is a unique and delicate plant characterized by its soft, velvety foliage.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are often tinged with a pale pink or lavender hue, giving it its distinctive appearance. Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
