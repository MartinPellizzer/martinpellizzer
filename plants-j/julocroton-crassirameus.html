Julocroton crassirameus

Julocroton crassirameus, commonly known as the thick-stemmed croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features thick, woody stems covered in a distinctive, rough, and often hairy texture. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in clusters, typically in shades of yellow or orange. Its leaves are usually alternate, with a broad, ovate shape and a serrated edge.

Julocroton crassirameus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
