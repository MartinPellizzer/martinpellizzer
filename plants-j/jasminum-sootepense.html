Jasminum sootepense

Jasminum sootepense, commonly known as Sootep jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, often emitting a sweet scent. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a glossy appearance.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for garden cultivation in tropical and subtropical regions.
