Jatropha Tubulosa Var. Septemloba

Jatropha tubulosa var. septemloba is a unique and striking plant native to tropical regions of South America. It is known for its vibrant, colorful flowers that bloom in clusters, adding visual interest to any garden. This variety stands out due to its distinctive seven-lobed leaves, which give it its scientific name. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to flourish. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both landscape design and botanical collections.
