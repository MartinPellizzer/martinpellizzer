Jatropha Elliptica

Jatropha elliptica is a striking plant known for its vibrant, often red or orange flowers and toxic sap. Native to parts of Africa and the Caribbean, it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant's unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening despite its poisonous properties. Its leaves are elliptical and glossy, adding visual interest to any landscape. However, caution is necessary due to its toxic nature, which can cause severe reactions if ingested.
