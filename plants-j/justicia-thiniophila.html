Justicia thiniophila

Justicia thiniophila, commonly known as the pink justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking pink to purple tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a glossy texture.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
