Jodrellia migiurtina

Jodrellia migiurtina, commonly known as the desert dandelion, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Africa and the Middle East.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with dark green, fleshy leaves that help it conserve water in harsh environments. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by fluffy seed heads that disperse its seeds by wind. Jodrellia migiurtina is adapted to survive in poor soils and extreme temperatures, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
