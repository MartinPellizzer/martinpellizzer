Justicia trinervia

Justicia trinervia, commonly known as the Three-veined jewelweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a yellow throat. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often have three distinct veins, giving it its common name.

Justicia trinervia thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found near streams or in forest understories.
