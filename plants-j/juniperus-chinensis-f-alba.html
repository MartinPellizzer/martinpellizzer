Juniperus chinensis f. alba

Juniperus chinensis f. alba, commonly known as the white juniper, is a cultivar of the Chinese juniper.

It is characterized by its pale blue-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant is evergreen, with small, scale-like leaves that are densely packed on the branches. It produces small, berry-like cones that are typically blue or black when mature.

This variety is often used in ornamental horticulture for its aesthetic value and adaptability to various soil types.
