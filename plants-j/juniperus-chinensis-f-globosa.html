Juniperus chinensis f. globosa

Juniperus chinensis f. globosa, commonly known as the globe juniper, is a compact, spherical shrub native to China.

It features dense, needle-like foliage that is typically a deep green, with small, blue-gray berries that appear in the fall. The plant is highly resistant to cold and drought, making it suitable for a variety of garden conditions. Its rounded form makes it an excellent choice for rock gardens or as a container plant.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soil.
