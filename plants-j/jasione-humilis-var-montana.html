Jasione humilis var. montana

Jasione humilis var. montana, commonly known as the alpine bellflower, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features small, bell-shaped blue or violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is adapted to thrive in rocky, well-drained soils. It is often found in alpine meadows and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.

Due to its delicate appearance, it requires careful cultivation to ensure its survival in cultivated environments.
