Jacea × kuemmerlei

Jacea × kuemmerlei, commonly known as the hybrid carnation, is a cross between Jacea and another species, resulting in a plant with vibrant, showy flowers.

It typically produces double or semi-double blooms in shades of red, pink, white, and yellow. The plant is herbaceous, with upright stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to both ornamental and pollinator-friendly gardens.
