Junellia bisulcata var. bisulcata

Junellia bisulcata var. bisulcata, commonly known as the slender junellia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Polygonaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, triangular leaves with two distinct longitudinal veins. The plant produces clusters of tiny, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or streamside habitats.

This variety is notable for its delicate appearance and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
