Justicia zamorensis

Justicia zamorensis, commonly known as the Zamora glorybower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
