Justicia obanensis

Justicia obanensis, commonly known as the Oban flame or Oban fireplant, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular, brightly colored flowers that typically range from red to orange. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded to semi-shaded environments.

Justicia obanensis is valued not only for its ornamental appeal but also for its ecological role in supporting pollinators.
