Jasminum fruticosum

Jasminum fruticosum, commonly known as the evergreen jasmine or Spanish jasmine, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during spring and summer. The plant has woody stems and glossy, dark green leaves that provide a dense, evergreen appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its sweet, intoxicating fragrance.
