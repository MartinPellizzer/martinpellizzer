Johrenia villosa

Johrenia villosa, commonly known as the silky johrenia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy foliage and clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Johrenia villosa is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate foliage and flowering display.
