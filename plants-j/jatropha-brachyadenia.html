Jatropha brachyadenia

Jatropha brachyadenia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf physic nut, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are often elliptical in shape. Due to its toxicity, it is important to handle the plant with care.
