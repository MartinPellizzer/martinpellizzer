Jasminum kerrii

Jasminum kerrii, commonly known as the Kerri's jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

Jasminum kerrii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its pleasant aroma.
