Jasminum laurifolium var. genuinum

Jasminum laurifolium var. genuinum, commonly known as the Chinese jasmine or evergreen jasmine, is a flowering plant native to southern China.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is evergreen, making it suitable for year-round ornamental use in gardens and landscapes. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, requiring regular watering during the growing season.

Its aromatic blooms make it a popular choice for hedging and as a decorative plant in tropical and subtropical regions.
