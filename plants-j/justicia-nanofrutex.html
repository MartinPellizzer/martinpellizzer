Justicia nanofrutex

Justicia nanofrutex, commonly known as the dwarf flame flower, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It features small, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of bright red, tubular flowers that bloom in summer, attracting hummingbirds and other pollinators. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a container plant.
