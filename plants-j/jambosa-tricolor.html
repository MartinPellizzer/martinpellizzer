Jambosa tricolor

Jambosa tricolor, commonly known as the three-color jambosa, is a unique plant species characterized by its strikingly variegated leaves.

The leaves exhibit three distinct colors—typically green, white, and pink—creating a visually appealing contrast. It is a tropical plant that thrives in warm, humid climates with well-draining soil. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that can range in color from white to pink.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and indoor spaces.
