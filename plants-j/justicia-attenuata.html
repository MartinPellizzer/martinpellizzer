Justicia attenuata

Justicia attenuata, commonly known as the slender flameflower, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of tubular, brightly colored flowers that range from red to orange. The leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
