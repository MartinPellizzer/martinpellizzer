Jasione macrocalyx

Jasione macrocalyx, commonly known as the large-flowered bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features erect stems that bear opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple, blooming in late spring to early summer. Jasione macrocalyx thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

It is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and rocky areas, contributing to biodiversity in its native habitats.
