Justicia Amphibola

Justicia amphibola is a unique aquatic plant known for its striking blue and purple flowers. It thrives in shallow water and is often found in wetlands and marshes. This plant is native to parts of Africa and is popular among aquatic gardeners. Its ability to purify water makes it an important species in ecological restoration. With its vibrant blooms and ecological benefits, Justicia amphibola stands out as a valuable addition to aquatic environments.
