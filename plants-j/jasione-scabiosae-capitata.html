Jasione scabiosae-capitata

Jasione scabiosae-capitata, commonly known as the scabious bellflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Caryophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.
