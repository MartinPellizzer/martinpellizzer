Julocroton agrestis

Julocroton agrestis, commonly known as the red-stemmed croton, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive reddish stems and vibrant, colorful foliage. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers clustered in cyathia, typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. Its leaves are often variegated, displaying shades of green, yellow, and red, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Julocroton agrestis thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
