Justicia nelsonioides

Justicia nelsonioides, commonly known as the Nelson's flame bush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking spikes of tubular, brightly colored flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Justicia nelsonioides thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
