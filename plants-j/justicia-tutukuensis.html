Justicia tutukuensis

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in shades of purple, pink, and white. Justicia tutukuensis is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of growing conditions.
