Juno koyuncui

Juno koyuncui, commonly known as the Turkish bellflower, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Turkey.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

Juno koyuncui is relatively low maintenance and suitable for rock gardens or alpine settings.
