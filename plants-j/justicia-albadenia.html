Justicia albadenia

Justicia albadenia, commonly known as silverleaf justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height, characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. The plant produces clusters of showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The plant is also valued for its use in traditional medicine.
