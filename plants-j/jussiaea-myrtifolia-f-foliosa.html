Jussiaea myrtifolia f. foliosa

Jussiaea myrtifolia f. foliosa, commonly known as the broadleaf jussiaea, is a flowering plant belonging to the Myrtaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine, whitish pubescence. The plant produces small, clustered white or pink flowers that bloom in spring. It is native to Australia and thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

This variant is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage.
