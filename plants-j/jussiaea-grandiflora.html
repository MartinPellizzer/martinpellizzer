Jussiaea grandiflora

Jussiaea grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered jussiaea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant typically grows in wetland or marshy environments, thriving in moist, nutrient-rich soils. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

Jussiaea grandiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in aquatic gardens.
