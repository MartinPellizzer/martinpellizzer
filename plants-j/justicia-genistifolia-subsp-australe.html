Justicia genistifolia subsp. australe

Justicia genistifolia subsp.

australe, commonly known as the narrow-leaved justicia, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia. It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and clusters of small, bright yellow flowers.

The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions. It produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small seeds.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in native gardens.
