Jatropha luxurians

Jatropha luxurians, commonly known as the showy spurge, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves and clusters of bright yellow flowers. The plant produces small, toxic berries that contain seeds used in some traditional practices. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Jatropha luxurians is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some medicinal uses in local communities.
