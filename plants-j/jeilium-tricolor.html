Jeilium tricolor

Jeilium tricolor, commonly known as the three-color orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its strikingly colorful blooms, which display three distinct shades of purple, pink, and white on the same flower. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom during the rainy season. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem.

Jeilium tricolor is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal and unique coloration.
