Justicia damingensis

Justicia damingensis, commonly known as the Daming flameflower, is a striking flowering plant native to southern China.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered with fine hairs.

Justicia damingensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its showy inflorescences.
