Jasminoides linearifolium

Jasminoides linearifolium, commonly known as the slender-leaf jasmine, is a flowering plant belonging to the Oleaceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, this plant thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil. The flowers are often used in traditional medicine and as ornamental plants due to their pleasant scent.

Its growth habit is typically shrub-like, with a compact form that makes it suitable for garden cultivation.
