Jasminum triumphans

Jasminum triumphans, commonly known as the yellow jessamine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, fragrant flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are dark green in color. It is a climbing shrub that can grow up to several meters in height.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
