Juno kopetdagensis

Juno kopetdagensis, commonly known as the Kopetdagan juniper, is a species of juniper native to Central Asia.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant has needle-like leaves and produces small, blue-black berries that are used in traditional medicine. It is well adapted to harsh climates with low precipitation and high temperatures.

Its botanical features make it an important component of its native ecosystems.
