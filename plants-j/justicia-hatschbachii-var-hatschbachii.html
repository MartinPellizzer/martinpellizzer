Justicia hatschbachii var. hatschbachii

Justicia hatschbachii var. hatschbachii, commonly known as the Mexican flame bush, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its striking red-orange tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens in warm climates.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant color and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
