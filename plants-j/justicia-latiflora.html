Justicia latiflora

Justicia latiflora, commonly known as the broadleaf justicia, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It is a member of the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Justicia latiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
