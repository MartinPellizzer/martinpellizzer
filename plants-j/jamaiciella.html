Jamaiciella

Jamaiciella, commonly known as the Jamaican starfruit, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the genus Jamaiciella in the family Myrtaceae, which includes other aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its small, star-shaped fruits that resemble the common starfruit but are less widely cultivated. Its leaves are elliptical and have a fragrant aroma, often used in traditional herbal remedies.
