Johrenia selinoides

Johrenia selinoides, commonly known as the common filaree, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing annual herb that typically reaches heights of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often with five petals. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Johrenia selinoides is known for its rapid growth and is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides.
