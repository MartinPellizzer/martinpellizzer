Jasminanthes chunii

Jasminanthes chunii, commonly known as the Chinese jasmine, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Oleaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate with a smooth margin.
