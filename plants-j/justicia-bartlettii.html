Justicia bartlettii

Justicia bartlettii, commonly known as the Bartlett's flame flower, is a striking tropical plant native to southern Mexico.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or orange tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators like hummingbirds and bees. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 feet. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a glossy green surface with serrated edges.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens in warm climates.
