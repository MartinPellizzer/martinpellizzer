Juniperus lutchuensis

Juniperus lutchuensis, commonly known as the Lutchuan juniper, is a species of juniper native to China.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree, typically growing up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces small, scale-like leaves that are arranged in opposite pairs along the branches. Its cones are berry-like and contain a single seed, which is dispersed by birds.

This species is adapted to a variety of soil types and is often found in forested areas with moderate to high rainfall.
