Justicia scytophylla

Justicia scytophylla, commonly known as the silverleaf flameflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are typically silver or gray-green, with a fuzzy texture, providing a striking contrast to the vibrant flowers. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant in tropical settings.
