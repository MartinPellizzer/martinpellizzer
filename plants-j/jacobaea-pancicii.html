Jacobaea pancicii

Jacobaea pancicii, commonly known as Pancici's ragwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. Its yellow flowers bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators.

Jacobaea pancicii is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is considered a relatively rare species.
