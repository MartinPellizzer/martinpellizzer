Justicia elliotii

Justicia elliotii, commonly known as Elliot's flamebeard, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often found in moist, well-drained soils along streambanks and in wetlands.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and restoration projects.
