Justicia smeruensis

Justicia smeruensis, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, with lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. It thrives in humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Justicia smeruensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
