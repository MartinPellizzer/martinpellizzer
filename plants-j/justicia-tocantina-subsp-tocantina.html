Justicia tocantina subsp. tocantina

Justicia tocantina subsp.

tocantina, commonly known as the Tocantins flame flower, is a herbaceous perennial native to Brazil. It features showy, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees.

The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes in tropical and subtropical regions.
