Julocroton ackermannianus var. lanceolatus

Julocroton ackermannianus var. lanceolatus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf croton, is a tropical shrub native to parts of Africa.

It features bright, showy flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which are borne on long, branching inflorescences. The leaves are typically lance-shaped, hence the variety name, and are often covered in fine hairs. This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
