Justicia hoepfneri

Justicia hoepfneri, commonly known as the silverleaf justicia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and clusters of tubular, violet-blue flowers. The plant belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
