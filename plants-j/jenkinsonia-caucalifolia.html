Jenkinsonia caucalifolia

Jenkinsonia caucalifolia, commonly known as the Caucalifolia Jenkinsonia, is a rare and unique plant species native to specific regions in South America.

It is characterized by its striking, large, waxy leaves that exhibit a distinctive blue-green coloration.
