Jacksonia ramosissima

Jacksonia ramosissima, commonly known as the yellow starbush, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth habit with many branches. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in spherical inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and sandy environments.

Jacksonia ramosissima is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
