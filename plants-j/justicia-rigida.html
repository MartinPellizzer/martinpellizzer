Justicia rigida

Justicia rigida, commonly known as rigid justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of bright red or orange flowers that attract pollinators such as birds and insects. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Justicia rigida is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various growing conditions.
