Justicia oxypages

Justicia oxypages, commonly known as the flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, attracting pollinators like hummingbirds and bees. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for warm climates.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
