Justicia axiologa

Justicia axiologa, commonly known as the sacred flame plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red flowers that resemble flames. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and slightly hairy, while its stems are woody and branched.
