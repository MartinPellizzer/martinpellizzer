Junellia longidentata var. glandulosa

Junellia longidentata var. glandulosa, commonly known as the glandular junellia, is a perennial herb native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its long, serrated leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant produces distinctive, glandular structures on its stems and leaves, which contribute to its unique chemical composition. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
