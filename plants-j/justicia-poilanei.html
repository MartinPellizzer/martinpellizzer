Justicia poilanei

Justicia poilanei, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and purple. The plant has heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are typically green with a slight sheen. It thrives in well-draining soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Justicia poilanei is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.
