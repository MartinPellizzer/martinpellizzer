Justicia rubriflora

Justicia rubriflora, commonly known as the red-flowered justicia, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that features vibrant red bracts and showy yellow flowers, which attract pollinators like hummingbirds and bees. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slight pubescence, while its stems are woody at the base.

It is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its long blooming season.
