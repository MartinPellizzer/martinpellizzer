Justicia dentata

Justicia dentata, commonly known as the toothed justicia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking spikes of tubular, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges, giving it its common name. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Justicia dentata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
