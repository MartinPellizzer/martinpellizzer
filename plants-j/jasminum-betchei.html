Jasminum betchei

Jasminum betchei, commonly known as the Betche's jasmine, is a climbing shrub native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually ovate to lanceolate in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Jasminum betchei is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.
