Justicia iltisii

Justicia iltisii, commonly known as the velvetleaf false arum, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are velvety to the touch. The plant produces striking, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by showy, petal-like bracts. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in rainforests.

Justicia iltisii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
