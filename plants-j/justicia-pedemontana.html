Justicia pedemontana

Justicia pedemontana, commonly known as the mountain justicia, is a flowering plant native to Central America and parts of South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Justicia pedemontana thrives in sunny to partially shaded environments and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.
