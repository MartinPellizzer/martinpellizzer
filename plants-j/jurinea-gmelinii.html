Jurinea gmelinii

Jurinea gmelinii, commonly known as the maritime sandwort, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of Europe and Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that thrives in sandy or gravelly soils, often found in maritime climates. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are typically narrow and fleshy, adapted to conserve water in arid environments.

Jurinea gmelinii plays an important role in stabilizing dune ecosystems and is often used in ecological restoration projects.
