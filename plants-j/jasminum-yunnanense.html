Jasminum yunnanense

Jasminum yunnanense, commonly known as Yunnan jasmine, is a flowering plant native to China, particularly Yunnan Province.

It is a shrub that can grow up to 2 meters tall, featuring opposite, simple, and ovate leaves. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

Jasminum yunnanense is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
