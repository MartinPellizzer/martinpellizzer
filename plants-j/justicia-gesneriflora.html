Justicia gesneriflora

Justicia gesneriflora, commonly known as the flame violet, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular, violet-colored flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and containers.
