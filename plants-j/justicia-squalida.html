Justicia squalida

Justicia squalida, commonly known as the swamp verbena, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant typically grows in wet or swampy areas, showcasing its adaptability to moist environments. Its leaves are opposite and often have a velvety texture.

Justicia squalida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in wetland restoration projects.
