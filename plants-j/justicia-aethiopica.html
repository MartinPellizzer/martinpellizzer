Justicia aethiopica

Justicia aethiopica, commonly known as flame violet, is a perennial shrub native to Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Justicia aethiopica is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
