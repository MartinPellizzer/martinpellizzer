Jasione maritima subsp. finisterrae

finisterrae, commonly known as the Atlantic Jasione, is a rare and specialized plant species native to the coastal regions of the Iberian Peninsula. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils along the shores, often in areas exposed to salt spray and strong winds.
