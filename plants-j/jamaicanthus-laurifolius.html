Jamaicanthus laurifolius

Jamaicanthus laurifolius, commonly known as the Jamaican flame tree, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has broad, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, giving it a lush appearance. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in coastal areas and disturbed sites.

Jamaicanthus laurifolius is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its vibrant floral display.
