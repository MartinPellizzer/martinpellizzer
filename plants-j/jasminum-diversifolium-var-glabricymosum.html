Jasminum diversifolium var. glabricymosum

Jasminum diversifolium var. glabricymosum, commonly known as the smooth-leafed jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the warm season. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
