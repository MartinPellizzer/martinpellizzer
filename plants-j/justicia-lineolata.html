Justicia lineolata

Justicia lineolata, commonly known as red flame or red hot poker, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It features striking, tubular red flowers that bloom in dense spikes during the summer months. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a velvety texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and drought tolerance.
