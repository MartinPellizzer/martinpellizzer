Jasminum tsinlingense

Jasminum tsinlingense, commonly known as the Chinese jasmine, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Oleaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and elliptical in shape, with a smooth texture.

The flowers are clustered in racemes and are highly valued for their sweet, delicate aroma.
