Jasminum pubigerum var. glabrum

Jasminum pubigerum var. glabrum, commonly known as the white jasmine or Chinese jasmine, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has slender, woody stems and lance-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.

This variety is distinguished by its smooth, hairless stems, which give it its scientific name "glabrum.".
