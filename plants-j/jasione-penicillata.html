Jasione penicillata

Jasione penicillata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It typically grows in dry, sunny grasslands and rocky soils, showcasing its adaptability to arid conditions. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, contributing to its delicate appearance.

Jasione penicillata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
