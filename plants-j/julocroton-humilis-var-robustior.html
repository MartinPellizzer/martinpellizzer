Julocroton humilis var. robustior

Julocroton humilis var. robustior, commonly known as the roughleaf julocroton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features small, elliptical leaves with a rough, textured surface, often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the spring. Its growth habit is compact, with multiple stems arising from the base.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions.
