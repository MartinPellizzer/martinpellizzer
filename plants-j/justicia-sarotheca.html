Justicia sarotheca

Justicia sarotheca, commonly known as the velvetleaf false arum, is a tropical perennial plant native to Central and South America.

It features striking, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and deep green coloration. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers that are often surrounded by showy, leaf-like bracts. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking exotic foliage.
