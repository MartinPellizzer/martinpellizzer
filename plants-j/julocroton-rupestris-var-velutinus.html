Julocroton rupestris var. velutinus

Julocroton rupestris var. velutinus, commonly known as the velvet croton, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety texture, giving it its common name. The plant produces striking yellow-orange flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens and collections.
