Jacksonia sternbergiana var. filicaulis

Jacksonia sternbergiana var. filicaulis, commonly known as the narrow-leafed Jacksonia, is a perennial shrub native to New Zealand.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces showy, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is often cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
