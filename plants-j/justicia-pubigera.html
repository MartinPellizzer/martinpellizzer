Justicia pubigera

Justicia pubigera, commonly known as the velvetleaf or redstem justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of tubular, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, or white, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Justicia pubigera is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and vibrant floral display.
