Jurinea creticola

Jurinea creticola, commonly known as the Cretan sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It thrives in sandy and rocky soils, often found in coastal and arid environments. The plant features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, typically blooming in spring. Its leaves are succulent and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native habitat.

Jurinea creticola is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in challenging conditions.
