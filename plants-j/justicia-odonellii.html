Justicia odonellii

Justicia odonellii, commonly known as the Odonell's flamebeard, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red flowers that resemble flames. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Its blooming period typically occurs in the summer months, adding vibrant color to its environment.
