Jasminum ambiguum

Jasminum ambiguum, commonly known as the Chinese jessamine, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or vine, often reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture.

Jasminum ambiguum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
