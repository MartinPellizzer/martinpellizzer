Justicia ankaratrensis

Justicia ankaratrensis, commonly known as the Madagascar flame flower, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red to orange tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly rough texture and is often cultivated for its bold, showy appearance. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance, it is a popular choice for gardeners seeking to add color and interest to their landscapes.
