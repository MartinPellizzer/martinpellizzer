Justicia bullata

Justicia bullata, commonly known as the flame flower, is a perennial shrub native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular, bright red to orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Justicia bullata is popular in gardens for its vibrant floral display and ornamental value.
