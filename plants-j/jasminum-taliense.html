Jasminum taliense

Jasminum taliense, commonly known as the Taliense jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and elliptical in shape, with a smooth texture.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
