Justicia honamoriensis

Justicia honamoriensis, commonly known as the Honamori bellflower, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
