Justicia mysorensis

Justicia mysorensis, commonly known as the flame flower or red flame plant, is a flowering plant native to southern India and Sri Lanka.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red or orange tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth surface with a slightly hairy texture.

Justicia mysorensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
