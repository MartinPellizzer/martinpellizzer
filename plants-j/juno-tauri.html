Juno tauri

Juno tauri, commonly known as the sacred fig, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and smooth, grayish bark. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into edible, round fruits known as figs. Juno tauri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and its ability to provide shade.

It plays a significant role in traditional medicine and cultural practices in many regions.
