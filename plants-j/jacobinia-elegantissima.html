Jacobinia elegantissima

Jacobinia elegantissima, commonly known as the elegant jacaranda, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to South America.

It belongs to the Bignoniaceae family and is characterized by its striking violet-blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces long, slender pods that contain seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant in warm climates.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to provide shade and its seasonal floral display.
