Jubilaria radlkoferi

Jubilaria radlkoferi, commonly known as the African silver bush, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it drought-tolerant and suitable for xeriscaping.

Jubilaria radlkoferi is often used in ornamental gardening for its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements.
