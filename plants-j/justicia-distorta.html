Justicia distorta

Justicia distorta, commonly known as the twisted false dragonhead, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically clustered in spikes at the ends of the stems.

Justicia distorta is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
