Jasminum simonsii

Jasminum simonsii, commonly known as the Chinese jasmine, is a flowering plant native to China and Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a slightly rough texture.

Jasminum simonsii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
