Jasminum tettense

Jasminum tettense, commonly known as the Tettens jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters, often emitting a sweet scent. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

Jasminum tettense is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
