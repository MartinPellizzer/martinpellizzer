Justicia panamense

Justicia panamense, commonly known as Panama jewelweed, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to red. The plant typically grows in moist, tropical environments, often near streams or in shaded forest understories. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly rough texture.

Justicia panamense is also noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
