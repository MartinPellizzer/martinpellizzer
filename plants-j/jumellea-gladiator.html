Jumellea gladiator

Jumellea gladiator, commonly known as the gladiator fig, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Following flowering, it develops small, edible fruits that are often used in local cuisines.

Its compact size and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
