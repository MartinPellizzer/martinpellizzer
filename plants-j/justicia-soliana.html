Justicia soliana

Justicia soliana, commonly known as the golden trumpet vine, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall. The plant is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate, with a rough texture.

The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
