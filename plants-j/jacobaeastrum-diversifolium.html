Jacobaeastrum diversifolium

Jacobaeastrum diversifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf goldenweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It belongs to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) and is known for its medicinal properties.

Its adaptability to harsh environments makes it an important species in ecological restoration projects.
