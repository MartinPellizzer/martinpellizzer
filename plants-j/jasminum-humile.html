Jasminum humile

Jasminum humile, commonly known as the common jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 3 meters. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, often in the evening. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a glossy green surface.

Jasminum humile is known for its strong, sweet scent, which is highly valued in perfumery and traditional medicine.
