Justicia elongata

Justicia elongata, commonly known as the slender flame flower, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of bright red, tubular flowers. The plant belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is known for its striking floral display. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Justicia elongata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
