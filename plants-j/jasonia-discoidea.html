Jasonia discoidea

Jasonia discoidea, commonly known as the woolly jasonia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
