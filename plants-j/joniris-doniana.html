Joniris doniana

Joniris doniana, commonly known as the African violet.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa, characterized by its velvety, waxy leaves and showy, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows as a perennial, often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value. Its flowers come in a variety of colors, including purple, pink, white, and blue.

The species is well adapted to humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.
