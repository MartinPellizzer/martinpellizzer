Juniperus chinensis f. japonica

Juniperus chinensis f. japonica, commonly known as the Japanese juniper, is a cultivar of the Chinese juniper.

It is a coniferous evergreen shrub or small tree characterized by its dense, needle-like leaves and aromatic foliage. The plant produces small, blue-black berries that are often used in ornamental gardening. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for landscaping.

Its compact growth habit and winter interest make it a valuable addition to gardens and landscapes.
