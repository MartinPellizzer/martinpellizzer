Juno maracandica

Juno maracandica, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are velvety to the touch, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are often overlooked despite their subtle beauty. Juno maracandica is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and agricultural fields.

It is also known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and climatic conditions.
