Justicia blepharophylla

Justicia blepharophylla, commonly known as the silver leaf justicia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage. The plant produces clusters of showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
