Juncus sarophorus

Juncus sarophorus, commonly known as the redstem bulrush, is a flowering plant in the family Juncaceae.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wetland environments, such as marshes and pond edges. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces spikes of small, greenish flowers. Its roots help stabilize soil and improve water quality in wetland ecosystems.

Juncus sarophorus is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its adaptability and ecological benefits.
