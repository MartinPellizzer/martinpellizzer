Jasminum diversifolium var. glabricorymbosum

Jasminum diversifolium var. glabricorymbosum, commonly known as the smooth-corymb jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the summer months. The leaves are simple, alternate, and have a smooth texture, which gives the variety its name. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.
