Justicia rubroviolacea

Justicia rubroviolacea, commonly known as the red-violaceum or red-flowered justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and striking clusters of violet to red flowers. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically arranged in dense inflorescences, often with a distinctive red or purple coloration. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The species belongs to the Acanthaceae family, which includes many other showy flowering plants.
