Julocroton pulcher

Julocroton pulcher, commonly known as the beautiful croton, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, multicolored foliage, which can range from deep reds and yellows to greens and purples. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is known for its unique, often variegated leaves that make it a popular ornamental species. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Julocroton pulcher is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and indoor settings.
