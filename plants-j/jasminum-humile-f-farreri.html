Jasminum humile f. farreri

Jasminum humile f. farreri, commonly known as the Chinese jasmine or small-flowered jasmine, is a compact shrub native to China.

It features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, often releasing a sweet scent in the evening. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and a trailing growth habit, making it suitable for hanging baskets or trellises. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires partial to full sun for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in gardens and as a flowering plant in containers.
