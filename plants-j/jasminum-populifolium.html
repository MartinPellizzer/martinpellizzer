Jasminum populifolium

Jasminum populifolium, commonly known as the Chinese jasmine or broadleaf jasmine, is a flowering plant native to southern China and parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the spring and summer seasons. The plant has simple, opposite, and often heart-shaped leaves that are smooth and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.

Jasminum populifolium is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
