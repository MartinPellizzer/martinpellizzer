Jasminum bignoniaceum subsp. zeylanicum

Jasminum bignoniaceum subsp.

zeylanicum, commonly known as the Ceylon jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Sri Lanka. It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters.

The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves with a smooth texture. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

The flowers are often used in perfumery and traditional medicine due to their aromatic properties.
