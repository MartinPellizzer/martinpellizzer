Jirasekia alternifolia

Jirasekia alternifolia, commonly known as the alternate-leaved jirasekia, is a rare flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, clustered in dense inflorescences.
