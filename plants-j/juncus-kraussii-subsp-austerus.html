Juncus kraussii subsp. austerus

Juncus kraussii subsp.

austerus, commonly known as the coastal rush, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in sandy or coastal environments.

The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, greenish flowers in dense spikes. It is well adapted to saline and windy conditions, making it a common species in coastal dunes.

Its ecological role includes stabilizing sand dunes and providing habitat for various small organisms.
