Justicia karschiana

Justicia karschiana, commonly known as flame flower or red flame plant, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Justicia karschiana thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
