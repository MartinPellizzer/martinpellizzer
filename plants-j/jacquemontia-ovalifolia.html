Jacquemontia ovalifolia

Jacquemontia ovalifolia, commonly known as the scarlet runner bean, is a climbing vine native to the Americas.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves and produces vibrant orange to red flowers. The plant bears edible pods that contain seeds used in traditional cuisines. It thrives in warm climates and requires full sun for optimal growth.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for both ornamental and agricultural purposes.
