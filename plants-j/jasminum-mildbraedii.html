Jasminum mildbraedii

Jasminum mildbraedii, commonly known as the African jasmine, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fragrant white flowers and glossy evergreen foliage. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
