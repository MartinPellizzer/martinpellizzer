Jasminum latifolium

Jasminum latifolium, commonly known as broadleaf jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has glossy, broad leaves that are typically arranged in opposite pairs along the stems. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Jasminum latifolium is often cultivated for its ornamental value and fragrant blooms.
