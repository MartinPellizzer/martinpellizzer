Justicia woodsiae

Justicia woodsiae, commonly known as the Woodsia justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to pink. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly waxy texture.
