Jasminum leratii

Jasminum leratii, commonly known as the Philippine jasmine, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is a shrub that can grow up to 2 meters tall, characterized by its fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a glossy surface. It thrives in tropical climates and requires well-drained soil and partial shade.

Jasminum leratii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.
