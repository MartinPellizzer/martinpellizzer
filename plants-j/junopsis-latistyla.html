Junopsis latistyla

Junopsis latistyla, commonly known as the broad-leafed juniper, is a shrub belonging to the Cupressaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Afghanistan and Pakistan. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage composed of scale-like leaves. It produces small, spherical cones that contain the plant's seeds.

Junopsis latistyla is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its aromatic properties.
