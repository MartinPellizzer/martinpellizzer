Justicia chrysotrichoma

Justicia chrysotrichoma, commonly known as the golden beard or silky justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers and dense, silky hair covering its stems and leaves. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

The flowers are clustered in panicles and bloom throughout the growing season, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
