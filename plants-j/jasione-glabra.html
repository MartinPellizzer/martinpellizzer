Jasione glabra

Jasione glabra, commonly known as the smooth Jasione, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, blue to violet flowers that bloom in early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has slender, branching stems and is characterized by its smooth, hairless leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or limestone areas.

Jasione glabra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
