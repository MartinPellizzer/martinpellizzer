Juncus Arcticus Subsp. Grubovii

Juncus arcticus subsp. grubovii is a hardy plant commonly found in cold, moist environments. It thrives in tundra and alpine regions where few other plants can survive. This subspecies is known for its dense, grass-like appearance and its ability to withstand harsh climates. Its presence is often an indicator of stable, nutrient-poor soils. Despite its simple appearance, it plays a vital role in supporting local ecosystems.
