Justicia alpina

Justicia alpina, commonly known as the alpine justicia, is a flowering plant native to high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous areas with well-drained soil. It is a perennial herb that produces showy blooms during the summer months.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine plant collections.
