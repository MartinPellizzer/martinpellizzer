Juniperus urbaniana

Juniperus urbaniana, commonly known as the urban juniper, is a species of juniper native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree, typically reaching heights of up to 5 meters. The plant has scale-like leaves that are dark green on top and whitish underneath, forming dense, compact foliage. Its cones, which are typically blue or purple, contain the aromatic berries that are used in various traditional and commercial applications.

Juniperus urbaniana is well adapted to urban environments, making it a popular choice for landscaping in cities.
