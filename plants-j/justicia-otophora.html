Justicia otophora

Justicia otophora, commonly known as the Mexican verbena, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to purple. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are square and branched.

Justicia otophora thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
