Johrenia dichotoma

Johrenia dichotoma, commonly known as the dichotomous johrenia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale yellow in color. Its leaves are narrow and often arranged in pairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Johrenia dichotoma is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
