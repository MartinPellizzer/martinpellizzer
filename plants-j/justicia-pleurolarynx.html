Justicia pleurolarynx

Justicia pleurolarynx, commonly known as the skyflower, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its clusters of vibrant blue or purple tubular flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its unique appearance and continuous blooming make it a visually appealing addition to any botanical collection.
