Justicia pectoralis

Justicia pectoralis, commonly known as the heartleaf justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to red, often appearing in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and arranged opposite along the stems. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Justicia pectoralis is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees.
