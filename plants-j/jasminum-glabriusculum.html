Jasminum glabriusculum

Jasminum glabriusculum, commonly known as the Spanish jasmine, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well-suited to Mediterranean climates.
