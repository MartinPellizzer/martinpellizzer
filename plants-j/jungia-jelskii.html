Jungia jelskii

Jungia jelskii, commonly known as the Jelskii's spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain seeds.

Jungia jelskii is often found in arid or semi-arid regions, demonstrating its adaptability to dry environments.
