Jahnia

Jahnia, commonly known as the red sandalwood, is a tropical plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and heartwood. The plant typically grows as a small to medium-sized tree, reaching heights of up to 15 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a reddish tinge when young.

The wood of Jahnia is highly valued for its durability and rich, reddish color.
