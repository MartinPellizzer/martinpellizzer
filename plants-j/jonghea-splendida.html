Jonghea splendida

Jonghea splendida, commonly known as the splendid jonghea, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the year. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil.

Jonghea splendida is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
