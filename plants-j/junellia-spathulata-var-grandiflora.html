Junellia spathulata var. grandiflora

Junellia spathulata var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered junellia, is a herbaceous plant native to certain regions of South America.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces showy, pale yellow to white flowers that bloom in late summer. Its growth habit is typically clump-forming, with upright stems that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded to partially shaded woodland environments.
