Jussiaea repens var. grandiflora

Jussiaea repens var. grandiflora, commonly known as the broadflower jussiaea, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a low-growing perennial that thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or wetlands. The plant features small, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. Its botanical structure includes a rhizomatous stem that allows it to spread vegetatively.

It is often found in nutrient-rich, acidic soils and is adapted to humid tropical climates.
