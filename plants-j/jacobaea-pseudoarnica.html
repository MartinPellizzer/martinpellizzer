Jacobaea pseudoarnica

Jacobaea pseudoarnica, commonly known as the false arnica, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Jacobaea pseudoarnica is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory properties.
