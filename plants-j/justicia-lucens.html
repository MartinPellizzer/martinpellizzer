Justicia lucens

Justicia lucens, commonly known as the shining justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences that consist of clusters of tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often have a velvety texture.

Justicia lucens thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
