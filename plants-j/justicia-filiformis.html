Justicia filiformis

Justicia filiformis, commonly known as the slender flame flower, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It features striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a contrasting white or yellow throat. The plant has slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This plant is popular in gardens for its vibrant blooms and ability to attract hummingbirds and other pollinators.
