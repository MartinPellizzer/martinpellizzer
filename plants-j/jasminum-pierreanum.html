Jasminum pierreanum

Jasminum pierreanum, commonly known as the African jasmine, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring aromatic, white flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are opposite, simple, and elliptical, with a glossy green surface. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.

It is often cultivated for its fragrant blossoms and ornamental value in gardens.
