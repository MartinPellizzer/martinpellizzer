Jasonia hesperia

Jasonia hesperia, commonly known as the Cape York bellflower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and drought tolerance.
