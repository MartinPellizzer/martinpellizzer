Justicia tanaensis

Justicia tanaensis, commonly known as the Tanaensis flame flower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a hairy texture.

Justicia tanaensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
