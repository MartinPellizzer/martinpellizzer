Jacaranda duckei

Jacaranda duckei, commonly known as the pink jacaranda, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its striking clusters of pink to purple flowers that bloom in spring. The leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets, and the tree can grow up to 20 meters in height. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

The wood is hard and durable, historically used for construction and tool-making.
