Japonicalia kiusiana

Japonicalia kiusiana, commonly known as the Japanese starflower, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Japan.

It features delicate, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring, typically in shades of white and pink. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and it prefers well-drained, acidic soils. It is often found in shaded forest understories and thrives in cool, moist climates.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and specialty nurseries.
