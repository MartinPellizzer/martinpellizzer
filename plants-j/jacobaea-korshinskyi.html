Jacobaea korshinskyi

Jacobaea korshinskyi, commonly known as the Korshinsky's ragwort, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow to orange composite flowers. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, often on rocky soils. It has basal leaves that are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Jacobaea korshinskyi is known for its chemical composition, which includes various alkaloids and sesquiterpene lactones.
