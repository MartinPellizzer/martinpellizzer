Justicia sericea

Justicia sericea, commonly known as silverleaf justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of showy, tubular flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its rapid growth and drought tolerance also make it suitable for erosion control in suitable climates.
