Julocroton

Julocroton, commonly known as the croton plant, is a tropical shrub belonging to the genus *Codiaeum* in the spurge family, Euphorbiaceae.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage, which can range from deep reds and oranges to yellows and greens, making it a popular ornamental plant. The leaves are typically alternate, ovate, and often have a waxy texture with prominent veins. Julocroton thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is often cultivated for its striking appearance in gardens and indoor settings.
