Justicia cabrerae

Justicia cabrerae, commonly known as the Mexican flame flower, is a striking tropical plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a distinctive, aromatic scent. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Justicia cabrerae is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its eye-catching floral display.
