Juno alata

Juno alata, commonly known as the winged bean, is a climbing legume native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive winged pods that enclose edible seeds and beans. The plant has trifoliate leaves and produces small, white or yellow flowers. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often grown for its nutritional seeds and foliage.

Juno alata is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it a beneficial crop in sustainable agriculture.
