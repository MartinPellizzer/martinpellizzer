Juno narbutii

Juno narbutii, commonly known as the narrow-leaved juniper, is a shrub native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, scale-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, berry-like cones that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to dry, rocky environments.

Juno narbutii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitat.
