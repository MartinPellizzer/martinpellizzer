Jurinea darwasica

Jurinea darwasica, commonly known as the Darwasica sea holly, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is native to the arid regions of Central Asia, particularly found in rocky and sandy soils. The plant is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and striking blue to purple flower heads. It thrives in harsh environments, showcasing adaptations to drought and poor soil conditions.

Jurinea darwasica is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental value.
