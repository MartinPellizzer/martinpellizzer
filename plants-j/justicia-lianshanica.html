Justicia lianshanica

Justicia lianshanica, commonly known as the Liangshan justicia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking clusters of tubular, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The flowers, which bloom in summer, range in color from blue to purple and attract various pollinators like bees and butterflies.
