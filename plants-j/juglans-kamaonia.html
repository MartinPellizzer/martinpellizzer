Juglans kamaonia

Juglans kamaonia, commonly known as the Kamaon walnut, is a species of deciduous tree native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the genus Juglans, which includes various types of walnut trees. The plant produces large, elongated nuts enclosed in a thick, green husk that splits open when mature. Its leaves are compound, with long, slender leaflets that turn yellow in the fall.

The Kamaon walnut is valued for its hardy nature and the high quality of its edible nuts.
