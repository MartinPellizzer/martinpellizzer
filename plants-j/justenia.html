Justenia

Justenia, commonly known as the red-flowered justenia, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, showy flowers that range in color from red to purple, often with a striking contrast between the petals and the sepals. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, featuring trifoliate leaves with three oval-shaped leaflets. Justenia species are native to various regions in Australia and are often found in coastal or sandy soils.

They are valued in horticulture for their ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained conditions.
