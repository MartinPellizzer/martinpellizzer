Justicia hilsenbeckii

Justicia hilsenbeckii, commonly known as the Hilsenbeck's glorybower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy bracts that resemble petals. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
