Juncus × gracilescens

Juncus × gracilescens, commonly known as the slender rush, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Juncaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems that are often reddish in color and covered with small, scale-like leaves. This plant typically grows in wet or marshy environments, thriving in areas with standing water or saturated soil. Its flowers are usually small and greenish, arranged in dense spikes at the top of the stems.

Juncus × gracilescens is often used in wetland restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for various wildlife species.
