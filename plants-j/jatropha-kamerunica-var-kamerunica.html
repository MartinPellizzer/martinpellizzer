Jatropha kamerunica var. kamerunica

Jatropha kamerunica var. kamerunica, commonly known as the Cameroon physic nut, is a shrub native to Central and West Africa.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into capsule fruits containing toxic, black seeds. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in disturbed soils and along riverbanks.

Due to its toxic properties, it is important to handle the plant with care.
