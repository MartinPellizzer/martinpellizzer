Justicia kraussii

Justicia kraussii, commonly known as red hot poker, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, spiky flower clusters that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow, creating a dramatic visual display. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are arranged in a rosette at the base. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its unique appearance and long-lasting blooms make it a favorite among gardeners seeking bold, colorful accents.
