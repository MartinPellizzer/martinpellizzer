Justicia chrysostephana

Justicia chrysostephana, commonly known as the golden stephania, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, with two lips, and bloom in clusters.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
