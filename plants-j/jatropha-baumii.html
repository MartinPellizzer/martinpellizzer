Jatropha baumii

Jatropha baumii, commonly known as the African Crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that are surrounded by long, feathery bracts, giving it a crown-like appearance. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with hairy stems and opposite leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

Jatropha baumii is also known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral display.
