Justicia Oncodes

Justicia oncodes is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America. It is known for its vibrant, showy blooms that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. This species thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to flourish. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and landscape design. With proper care, Justicia oncodes can bloom throughout the growing season, adding a burst of color to any garden.
