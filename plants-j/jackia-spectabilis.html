Jackia spectabilis

Jackia spectabilis, commonly known as the showy jackia, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features striking, bright yellow flowers with a central disk surrounded by ray florets, making it visually appealing. Jackia spectabilis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
