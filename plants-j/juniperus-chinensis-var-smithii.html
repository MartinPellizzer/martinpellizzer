Juniperus chinensis var. smithii

Juniperus chinensis var. smithii, commonly known as the Chinese juniper or Smith's juniper, is a slow-growing evergreen shrub or small tree native to China.

It features dense, scale-like leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, providing a striking visual contrast. The plant produces small, berry-like cones that are often used in ornamental gardening due to their persistent winter interest. It is well-suited for rock gardens and as a foundation plant in landscapes.

This variety is valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
