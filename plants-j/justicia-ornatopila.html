Justicia ornatopila

Justicia ornatopila, commonly known as the velvetleaf flameflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a contrasting throat. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
