Jambosa sulcata

Jambosa sulcata, commonly known as the African sour plum, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by edible, sour fruits resembling small plums.
