Justicia minutiflora var. minutiflora

Justicia minutiflora var. minutiflora, commonly known as the small-flowered jewelweed, is a herbaceous annual plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It features slender, branching stems covered in fine, glandular hairs. Its leaves are opposite, ovate to elliptic, and often have a slightly hairy surface. The flowers are small, clustered in dense spikes, and typically display vibrant red or orange hues with yellow throats.

It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in disturbed soils near streams or in woodland areas.
