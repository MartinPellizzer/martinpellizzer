Justicia tubulosa subsp. late-ovata

Justicia tubulosa subsp.

late-ovata, commonly known as the late-ovate false dragonhead, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves.

The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple or pink, which bloom in clusters at the ends of the branches. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens.
