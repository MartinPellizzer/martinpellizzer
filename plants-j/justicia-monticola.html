Justicia monticola

Justicia monticola, commonly known as the mountain justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows up to 1.5 meters in height, featuring opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking clusters of blue or violet tubular flowers, which bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its rapid growth and ability to spread through runners make it both a desirable and sometimes invasive species in certain environments.
