Justicia vahlii var. scindica

Justicia vahlii var. scindica, commonly known as the silverleaf justicia, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of tubular, violet-blue flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms.
