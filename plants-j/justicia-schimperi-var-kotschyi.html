Justicia schimperi var. kotschyi

Justicia schimperi var. kotschyi, commonly known as the Kotschyi flame flower, is a perennial shrub native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It prefers well-drained soil and is drought-tolerant once established.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to add vibrant color to gardens.
