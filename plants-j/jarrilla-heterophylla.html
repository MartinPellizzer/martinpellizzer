Jarrilla heterophylla

Jarrilla heterophylla, commonly known as the variable leaf lantana, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its variable leaf shapes, which can range from ovate to lanceolate, depending on environmental conditions. Jarrilla heterophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
