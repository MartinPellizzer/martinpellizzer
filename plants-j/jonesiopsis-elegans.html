Jonesiopsis elegans

Jonesiopsis elegans, commonly known as the elegant Jonesiopsis, is a rare and delicate epiphytic orchid native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It features slender, wiry stems and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, relying on its aerial roots for moisture and nutrients. Its leaves are narrow and strap-like, adapted to maximize light absorption in its shaded habitat.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Jonesiopsis elegans is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
