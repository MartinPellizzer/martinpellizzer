Jacaranda decurrens var. glabrata

Jacaranda decurrens var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth jacaranda, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to South America.

It features striking violet to blue-purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces elongated, woody pods that contain flat, winged seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun for optimal growth.

The smooth jacaranda is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adaptable to various climates.
