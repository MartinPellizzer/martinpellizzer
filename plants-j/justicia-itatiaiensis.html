Justicia itatiaiensis

Justicia itatiaiensis, commonly known as the flame flower, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a member of the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. Justicia itatiaiensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
