Jasminum forstenii var. ensatum

Jasminum forstenii var. ensatum, commonly known as the Cape jasmine, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has slender, woody stems and elliptical, dark green leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant scent.
