Jasminum lucidum

Jasminum lucidum, commonly known as the bright jasmine or Indian jasmine, is a flowering plant native to South and Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial shrub that produces fragrant white flowers with five petals, typically blooming in the evening. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.

Jasminum lucidum is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
