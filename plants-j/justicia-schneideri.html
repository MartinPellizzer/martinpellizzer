Justicia schneideri

Justicia schneideri, commonly known as Schneider's false heather, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of tubular flowers. The plant produces bright blue or violet flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.
