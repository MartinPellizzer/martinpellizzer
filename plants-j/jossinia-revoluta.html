Jossinia revoluta

Jossinia revoluta, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. Its flowers are small and clustered in spikes, often with yellow or greenish petals.

Jossinia revoluta is known for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.
