Justicia clivalis

Justicia clivalis, commonly known as the cliff justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with contrasting white or yellow markings. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils and full sun. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often covered in fine hairs.

Justicia clivalis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators like hummingbirds and bees.
