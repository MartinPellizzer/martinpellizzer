Jacobinia spicigera

Jacobinia spicigera, commonly known as the yellow-flowered jacobinia, is a small evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Jacobinia spicigera is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
