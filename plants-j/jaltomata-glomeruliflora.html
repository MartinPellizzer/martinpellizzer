Jaltomata glomeruliflora

Jaltomata glomeruliflora, commonly known as the clustered-flower jaltomata, is a species native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale purple in color. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural studies.
