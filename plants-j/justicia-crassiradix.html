Justicia crassiradix

Justicia crassiradix, commonly known as the broadleaf justicia, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces striking clusters of tubular, bell-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its showy blooms attract pollinators like bees and hummingbirds, contributing to its ecological value.
