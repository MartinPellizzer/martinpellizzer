Justicia retorta

Justicia retorta, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its distinctive, zigzagging stems that give it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink or purple. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Justicia retorta is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
