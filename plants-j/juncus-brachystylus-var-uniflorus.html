Juncus brachystylus var. uniflorus

Juncus brachystylus var. uniflorus, commonly known as the narrowleaf rush, is a perennial grass-like plant native to the western United States.

It typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes, stream banks, and wet meadows. The plant has slender, cylindrical stems that are usually less than 1 meter in height. Its leaves are narrow, grass-like, and often have a bluish-green hue.

The inflorescence consists of a single spikelet, which is a distinguishing feature of this variety.
