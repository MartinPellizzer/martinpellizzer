Julocroton subpannosus var. posadensis

Julocroton subpannosus var. posadensis, commonly known as the Posadens croton, is a shrub native to specific regions in Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, often colorful foliage, which can range from green to reddish hues. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
