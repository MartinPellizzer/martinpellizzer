Justicia cobensis

Justicia cobensis, commonly known as the cobensis flame flower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth surface and a glossy appearance. Its flowers are typically bright red or orange, with two lips, and are arranged in terminal spikes.

Justicia cobensis thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
