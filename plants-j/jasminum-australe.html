Jasminum australe

Jasminum australe, commonly known as the Australian jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and lance-shaped with a glossy green surface.

Jasminum australe thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal regions and open woodlands.
