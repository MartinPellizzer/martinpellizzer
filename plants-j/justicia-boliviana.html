Justicia boliviana

Justicia boliviana, commonly known as the Bolivian flame flower, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, attracting pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a glossy texture.

Justicia boliviana thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
