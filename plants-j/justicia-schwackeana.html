Justicia schwackeana

Justicia schwackeana, commonly known as the silver flame or flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences that resemble flames, consisting of tubular red or orange flowers with white veins. The plant has lance-shaped, silver-green leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Justicia schwackeana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a container plant.
