Justicia xipotensis

Justicia xipotensis, commonly known as the Mexican flamebush, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is a member of the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant orange-red bracts that resemble flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

Justicia xipotensis thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
