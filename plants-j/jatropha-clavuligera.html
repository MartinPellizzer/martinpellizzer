Jatropha clavuligera

Jatropha clavuligera, commonly known as the narrow-leaf physic nut, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are typically narrow and lance-shaped. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into capsule fruits containing toxic seeds. Jatropha clavuligera is part of the Euphorbiaceae family, known for its latex-producing species.
