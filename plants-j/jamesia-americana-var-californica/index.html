Jamesia Americana Var. Californica

Jamesia americana var. californica is a striking native plant found in the western United States. It is known for its vibrant, cup-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring. This variety is closely related to the more commonly known Jamesia americana. The plant thrives in rocky soils and is often found in mountainous regions. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for gardeners and botanists alike.
