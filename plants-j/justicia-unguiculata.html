Justicia unguiculata

Justicia unguiculata, commonly known as the red-flowered justicia, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are usually arranged oppositely along the stems. The plant produces striking clusters of bright red, tubular flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
