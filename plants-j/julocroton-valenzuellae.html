Julocroton valenzuellae

Julocroton valenzuellae, commonly known as the Valenzuelan croton, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Crotonaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that display a variety of shades. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are usually green or yellow, and it produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
