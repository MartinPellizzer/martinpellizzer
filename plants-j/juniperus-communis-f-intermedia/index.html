Juniperus Communis F. Intermedia

Juniperus communis f. intermedia is a unique variant of the common juniper known for its distinctive growth habits and ornamental appeal. This shrub or small tree thrives in harsh environments, showcasing its adaptability to cold and rocky soils. Its foliage ranges from dark green to bluish-gray, offering a striking visual contrast in gardens and natural landscapes. The plant produces small blue-black berries that are both ornamental and edible, though often used more for decoration than consumption. With its hardy nature and aesthetic value, Juniperus communis f. intermedia is a popular choice for landscaping in temperate and alpine regions.
