Justicia jejuna

Justicia jejuna, commonly known as the dwarf flame flower, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 10 to 15 centimeters. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are showy, with bright red or orange bracts that surround small, tubular flowers.

It thrives in warm, humid conditions and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
