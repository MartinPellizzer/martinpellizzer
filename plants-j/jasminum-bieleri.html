Jasminum bieleri

Jasminum bieleri, commonly known as the Chinese jasmine or Bieler's jasmine, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth texture. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its pleasant fragrance and compact growth habit.
