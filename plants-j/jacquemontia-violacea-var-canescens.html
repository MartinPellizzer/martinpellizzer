Jacquemontia violacea var. canescens

Jacquemontia violacea var. canescens, commonly known as the silky blue morning glory, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking violet-blue flowers that bloom in the early morning. The leaves are heart-shaped and often covered in a fine, silky pubescence, giving it a soft appearance. The plant is an annual vine that thrives in warm, sunny conditions.

It is known for its rapid growth and is sometimes considered invasive in certain regions.
