Jacksonia gracillima

Jacksonia gracillima, commonly known as the slender daisy, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers with white petals and yellow centers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy or gravelly soils. It produces small, narrow leaves and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Jacksonia gracillima is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
