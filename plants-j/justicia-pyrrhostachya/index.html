Justicia Pyrrhostachya

Justicia pyrrhostachya, commonly known as the flame flower, is a strikingly colorful plant native to tropical regions of the Americas. Its vibrant red bracts and clusters of small white flowers make it a standout in any garden or landscape. This species thrives in warm, sunny environments and is often used in ornamental horticulture for its eye-catching appearance. It is known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Despite its beauty, it requires well-drained soil and regular watering to flourish.
