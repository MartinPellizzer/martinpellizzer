Jussiaea longifolia var. warmingii

Jussiaea longifolia var. warmingii, commonly known as the narrow-leaved jussiaea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wetland or marshy environments. The plant features slender, erect stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its small, white or pale yellow flowers are clustered in dense, terminal spikes.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in water gardens or bog plantings.
