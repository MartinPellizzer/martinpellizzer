Jonesiopsis × tryphera

Jonesiopsis × tryphera, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a rare and intriguing plant resulting from the cross between Jonesiopsis and Tryphera species.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure and vibrant coloration. The plant typically features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is often cultivated by enthusiasts and botanists.

However, its cultivation is challenging due to specific environmental and care requirements.
