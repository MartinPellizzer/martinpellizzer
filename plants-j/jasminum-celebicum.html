Jasminum celebicum

Jasminum celebicum, commonly known as the Celebes jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Indonesia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a yellowish-green calyx. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate, with a smooth surface and a slightly glossy appearance.

Jasminum celebicum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
