Jambosa cylindrica

Jambosa cylindrica, commonly known as the cylindrical jambosa, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical fruit that resembles a small, elongated berry. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical climates, thriving in well-drained soils. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, often displaying shades of yellow or white.

The fruit is edible and has a mildly sweet flavor, making it a valuable plant in local cuisines.
