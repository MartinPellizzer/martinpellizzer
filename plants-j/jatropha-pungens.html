Jatropha pungens

Jatropha pungens, commonly known as the spiny jatropha, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and leaves, which provide a natural defense against herbivores. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into bright red, fleshy fruits. Its seeds are encased in a toxic, sticky substance, which deters seed predators.

Jatropha pungens is often found in disturbed soils and is adaptable to various environmental conditions.
