Justicia kelleri

Justicia kelleri, commonly known as the dwarf flame flower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 feet and has lance-shaped, dark green leaves. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Justicia kelleri is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its striking floral display.
