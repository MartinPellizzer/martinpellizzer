Jussiaea natans f. latifolia

Jussiaea natans f. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved floating meadow, is a variant of the aquatic plant Jussiaea natans.

It is characterized by its broad, floating leaves that form a dense mat on the water surface. The leaves are typically oval to lance-shaped and have a waxy coating that aids in water retention. This plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

It plays a role in stabilizing water surfaces and providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.
