Jambosa synaptoneura

Jambosa synaptoneura, commonly known as the African blackwood, is a small evergreen tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dark, dense wood and dense foliage, which gives it a unique appearance. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are arranged alternately along the branches. It produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, round fruits.

The botanical features of Jambosa synaptoneura make it an important species in both ecological and horticultural contexts.
