Justicia ochroleuca

Justicia ochroleuca, commonly known as the white flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range from white to pale yellow, often with a red or pink throat. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its foliage is typically lance-shaped and can be either evergreen or semi-evergreen depending on the climate.
