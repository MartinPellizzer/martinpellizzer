Juncoides subcapitata

Juncoides subcapitata, commonly known as the clustered rush, is a flowering plant belonging to the Juncaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes and pond edges. The plant features cylindrical, grass-like stems that are often clustered at the base. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are arranged in dense, spike-like clusters at the top of the stems.

Juncoides subcapitata plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic and terrestrial organisms.
