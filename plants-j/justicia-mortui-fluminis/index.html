Justicia Mortui-Fluminis

Justicia mortui-fluminis is a rare and mysterious plant found in the misty forests of the Andes. Its name, meaning "death of the river bank," hints at its ominous reputation among local tribes. The plant is known for its striking violet flowers and toxic properties, making it both feared and studied. Despite its dangers, it holds cultural significance and is sometimes used in traditional medicine. Scientists are still trying to unlock its full potential and understand its ecological role.
