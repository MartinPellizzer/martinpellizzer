Justicia tenuistachys

Justicia tenuistachys, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed flame flower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of showy, tubular red or orange flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The plant is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
