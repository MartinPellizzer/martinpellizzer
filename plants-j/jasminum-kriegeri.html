Jasminum kriegeri

Jasminum kriegeri, commonly known as the Krieger's jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a climbing shrub that produces fragrant white flowers, which bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Jasminum kriegeri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant fragrance.
