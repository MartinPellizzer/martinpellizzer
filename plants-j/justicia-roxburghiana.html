Justicia roxburghiana

Justicia roxburghiana, commonly known as the red hot poker, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, spiky inflorescences that range in color from red to orange, yellow, and pink. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are often clustered in dense, brush-like spikes. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.

Justicia roxburghiana is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
