Jaumea rotundifolia

Jaumea rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leaved jaumea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bulbous form. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped white or pale blue flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are broad, rounded, and often persist through the winter.
