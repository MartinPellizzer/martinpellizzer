Jussiaea repens var. californica

Jussiaea repens var. californica, commonly known as the California spikerush, is a flowering plant native to western North America.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wetland habitats such as marshes and pond edges. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikes of greenish-white flowers. It is adapted to moist environments and can often be found in coastal and inland wetlands.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species for wetland ecosystems and water quality monitoring.
