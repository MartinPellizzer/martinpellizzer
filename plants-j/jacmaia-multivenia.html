Jacmaia multivenia

Jacmaia multivenia, commonly known as the velvetleaf jacmaia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. Jacmaia multivenia thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid tropical forests.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and visually appealing species within its native habitat.
