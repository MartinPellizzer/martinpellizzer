Justicia lorata

Justicia lorata, commonly known as the silverleaf false arnoldia, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it its common name. Its showy, tubular flowers are usually blue or violet and bloom in clusters.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
