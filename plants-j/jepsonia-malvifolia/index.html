Jepsonia Malvifolia

Jepsonia malvifolia is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the western United States. It belongs to the Malvaceae family, which includes hibiscus and cotton, and is known for its unique floral structure. This species thrives in specific microhabitats, often found in rocky or sandy soils along coastal areas. Due to habitat loss and climate change, Jepsonia malvifolia is now classified as endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this rare botanical gem.
