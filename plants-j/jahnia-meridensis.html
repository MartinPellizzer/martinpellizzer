Jahnia meridensis

Jahnia meridensis, commonly known as the Mediterranean germander, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It belongs to the family Geraniaceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically features lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry environments.
