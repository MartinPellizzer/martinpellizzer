Jasminonerium ovatum

Jasminonerium ovatum, commonly known as the sweet-scented orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a waxy texture.

The flowers bloom in clusters and emit a sweet, intoxicating fragrance, making it a popular ornamental plant.
