Juncus yunnanensis

Juncus yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan rush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Juncaceae family and is characterized by its grass-like, cylindrical stems that are often used in traditional Chinese medicine. The plant typically grows in wetland environments, such as marshes and along riverbanks, where it thrives in moist, well-drained soils. Its flowering spikes consist of small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Juncus yunnanensis is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly for its potential anti-inflammatory and diuretic effects.
