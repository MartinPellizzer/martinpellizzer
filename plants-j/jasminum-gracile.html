Jasminum gracile

Jasminum gracile, commonly known as slender jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, often in the spring and summer seasons. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

Jasminum gracile is valued for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its pleasant aroma.
