Justicia zarucchii

Justicia zarucchii, commonly known as the scarlet false arum, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red bracts that surround small, inconspicuous flowers, creating a dramatic visual display. The plant belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are typically broad and heart-shaped, with a smooth or slightly toothed margin.

Justicia zarucchii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its bold foliage and striking floral display.
