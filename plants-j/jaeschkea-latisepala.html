Jaeschkea latisepala

Jaeschkea latisepala, commonly known as the broad-sepaled jaeschkea, is a member of the Pyrolaceae family.

It is a small, evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. The plant features simple, ovate leaves with a leathery texture and a dense, compact growth habit. Its flowers are white to pale pink, with five broad, spreading sepals and no petals.

It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest understories.
