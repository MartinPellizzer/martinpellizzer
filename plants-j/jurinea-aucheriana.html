Jurinea aucheriana

Jurinea aucheriana, commonly known as the silverleaf fleabane, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which helps it retain moisture in dry environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. It is often found growing in rocky soils and along coastal areas.

Jurinea aucheriana is a hardy, drought-tolerant species that plays a role in stabilizing sandy and coastal landscapes.
