Justicia gilliesii

Justicia gilliesii, commonly known as the silverleaf justicia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and clusters of tubular, pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid regions.

The flowers attract pollinators such as bees and birds, contributing to its ecological importance.
