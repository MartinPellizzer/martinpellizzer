Justicia chrysea

Justicia chrysea, commonly known as the golden flame or yellow flame plant, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking clusters of bright yellow, tubular flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-draining soil.

Justicia chrysea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
