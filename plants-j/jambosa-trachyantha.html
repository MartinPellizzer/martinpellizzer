Jambosa trachyantha

Jambosa trachyantha, commonly known as the African star apple, is a tropical fruit-bearing plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its large, green, spiny fruit that turns yellow when ripe. The plant has a dense, branched canopy with dark green, glossy leaves. Its flowers are small, white, and clustered in inflorescences, and it produces fruits that are often used in local cuisines.

The tree is relatively drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils.
