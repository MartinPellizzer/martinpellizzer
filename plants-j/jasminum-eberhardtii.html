Jasminum eberhardtii

Jasminum eberhardtii, commonly known as the Eberhardt jasmine, is a flowering plant native to the island of Madagascar.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, simple, and usually hairy leaves that are elliptical in shape. It thrives in tropical climates and prefers well-drained soil.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.
