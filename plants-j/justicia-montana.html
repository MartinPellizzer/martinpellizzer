Justicia montana

Justicia montana, commonly known as red hot poker, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking spikes of tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are usually arranged in whorls around the stem. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Justicia montana is often used in ornamental horticulture for its vibrant floral display.
