Justicia scaettae

Justicia scaettae, commonly known as the scarlet justicia, is a striking tropical plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its vibrant red, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators like hummingbirds and bees. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for warm climates.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
