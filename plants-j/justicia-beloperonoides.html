Justicia beloperonoides

Justicia beloperonoides, commonly known as the flame violet, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It is characterized by its striking violet to red flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling flames. The plant belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in gardens. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
