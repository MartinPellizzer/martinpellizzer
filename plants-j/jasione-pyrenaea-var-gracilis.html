Jasione pyrenaea var. gracilis

Jasione pyrenaea var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender rock-jasmine, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils. It is adapted to dry, sunny environments and is often used in rock gardens.

Its botanical characteristics make it a hardy and ornamental choice for alpine and coastal gardens.
