Justicia chapadensis var. nudicaulis

Justicia chapadensis var. nudicaulis, commonly known as the slender flame beardtongue, is a herbaceous perennial native to Brazil.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, attracting pollinators like hummingbirds and bees. The plant has slender, upright stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, typically from spring to early summer.
