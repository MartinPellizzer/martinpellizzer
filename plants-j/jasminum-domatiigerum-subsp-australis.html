Jasminum domatiigerum subsp. australis

Jasminum domatiigerum subsp.

australis, commonly known as the Australian jasmine, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia. It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in spring and summer.

The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a glossy green surface.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and rocky habitats.
