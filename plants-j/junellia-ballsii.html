Junellia ballsii

Junellia ballsii, commonly known as the silverleaf junellia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that features striking, silver-gray foliage with a velvety texture. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Junellia ballsii thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique foliage and delicate floral display.
