Julocroton viridulus

Julocroton viridulus, commonly known as the green croton, is a tropical shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, often variegated leaves that display shades of green, yellow, and red. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Julocroton viridulus thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.
