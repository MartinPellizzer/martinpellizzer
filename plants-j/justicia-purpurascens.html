Justicia purpurascens

Justicia purpurascens, commonly known as the red flame plant or red-veined justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular red flowers that are often adorned with contrasting white or yellow veins. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a reddish or purple tinge, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
