Jollydora

Jollydora, commonly known as the cheerful orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features vibrant, colorful flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of pink, purple, and white. The plant has strap-like leaves that are broad and smooth, contributing to its ornamental appeal. Jollydora thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is often cultivated for its beauty and used in floral arrangements.
