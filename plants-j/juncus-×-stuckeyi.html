Juncus × stuckeyi

Juncus × stuckeyi, commonly known as the Stuckey's rush, is a hybrid plant in the Juncaceae family.

It is native to the southeastern United States and thrives in wetland environments. The plant typically forms dense, grass-like clumps with narrow, cylindrical leaves. Its flowers are small, greenish-brown spikes that appear at the tips of the stems.

Juncus × stuckeyi is often used in wetland restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for wildlife.
