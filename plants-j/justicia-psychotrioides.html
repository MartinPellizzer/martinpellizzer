Justicia psychotrioides

Justicia psychotrioides, commonly known as the velvetleaf false dragonhead, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces clusters of tubular, pinkish to purple flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

However, it can become invasive in certain environments if not managed properly.
