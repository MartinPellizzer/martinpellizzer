Jussiaea polygonoides

Jussiaea polygonoides, commonly known as the narrowleaf marsh grass, is a flowering plant native to wetland environments in South America.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in shallow water or moist soil, often found in marshes and along riverbanks. The plant features slender, erect stems with small, opposite leaves that are usually narrow and linear. Its flowers are small and clustered in spikes, producing tiny, greenish-white blooms.

Jussiaea polygonoides plays an important ecological role in stabilizing wetland soils and providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
