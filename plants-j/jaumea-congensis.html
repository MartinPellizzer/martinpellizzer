Jaumea congensis

Jaumea congensis, commonly known as the African lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features grass-like leaves that are typically narrow and grass-green in color. The plant produces striking, showy flowers that are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive hooded shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Jaumea congensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
