Jurinea turcica

Jurinea turcica, commonly known as Turkish sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that thrives in coastal and saline environments. The plant features narrow, silver-gray leaves and produces small, blue or purple flowers. It is adapted to withstand harsh coastal conditions, including salt spray and strong winds.

Jurinea turcica is often found in sandy or rocky soils along the shores of Turkey and other Mediterranean countries.
