Josephia pteridifolia

Josephia pteridifolia, commonly known as the fernleaf josephia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Aizoaceae, commonly referred to as the ice plant family. The plant is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves that resemble fern fronds, which help it conserve water in arid environments. Its small, star-shaped flowers are typically yellow or orange, adding a vibrant touch to its appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
