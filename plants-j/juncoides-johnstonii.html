Juncoides johnstonii

Juncoides johnstonii, commonly known as Johnston's rush, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Juncaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically arranged in spike-like clusters. Native to specific regions of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or wetlands. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense mats, which can help stabilize soil and prevent erosion.
