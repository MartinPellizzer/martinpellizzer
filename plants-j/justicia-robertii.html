Justicia robertii

Justicia robertii, commonly known as the red mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 2 to 4 feet. The plant features showy, tubular red flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Justicia robertii thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.
