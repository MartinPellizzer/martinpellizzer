Juniperus formosana

Juniperus formosana, commonly known as the Taiwan juniper, is a species of juniper native to Taiwan.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree, typically reaching heights of up to 10 meters. The plant has scale-like leaves that are dark green on top and whitish underneath, arranged in dense, compact clusters. Its cones are small and berry-like, containing toxic seeds that are not suitable for human consumption.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions with a temperate climate.
