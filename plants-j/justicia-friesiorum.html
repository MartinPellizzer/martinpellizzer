Justicia friesiorum

Justicia friesiorum, commonly known as the pink justicia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking pink to purple tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb, often reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers are borne in spikes at the top of the stems.

Justicia friesiorum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
