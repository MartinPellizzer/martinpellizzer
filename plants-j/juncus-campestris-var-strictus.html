Juncus campestris var. strictus

Juncus campestris var. strictus, commonly known as the rigid rush, is a perennial plant belonging to the Juncaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright, grass-like appearance with narrow, cylindrical leaves that are typically green to bluish-green in color. This species thrives in wet or moist environments, such as marshes, ditches, and along the edges of ponds. Its flowering spikes consist of small, pale green to brownish flowers that appear in late summer.

The plant plays an important ecological role by providing habitat and food for various invertebrates and birds.
