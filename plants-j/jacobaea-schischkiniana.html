Jacobaea schischkiniana

Jacobaea schischkiniana, commonly known as the Schischkin's ragwort, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. It has a robust root system and is often found in rocky or arid environments.
