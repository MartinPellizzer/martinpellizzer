Justicia heyneana

Justicia heyneana, commonly known as the red flame plant, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It features showy, tubular red flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators like hummingbirds and bees. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens in warm climates.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant color and compact growth habit.
