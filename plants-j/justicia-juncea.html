Justicia juncea

Justicia juncea, commonly known as Mexican flame flower, is a herbaceous perennial native to Central and South America.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, attracting pollinators like hummingbirds and bees. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Justicia juncea is often used in ornamental gardening for its vibrant blooms and tropical appearance.
