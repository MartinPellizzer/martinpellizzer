Jasminum officinale f. affine

Jasminum officinale f. affine, commonly known as the affine jasmine, is a cultivar of the common jasmine plant.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant is a shrub with woody stems and lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its sweet fragrance, which is highly prized in perfumery and aromatherapy.
