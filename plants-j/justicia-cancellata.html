Justicia cancellata

Justicia cancellata, commonly known as the red hot poker, is a striking plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces tall spikes of bright red, tubular flowers. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden borders.

Its unique appearance and long blooming period make it a standout addition to any landscape.
