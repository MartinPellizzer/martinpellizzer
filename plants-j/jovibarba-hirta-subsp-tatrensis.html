Jovibarba hirta subsp. tatrensis

Jovibarba hirta subsp.

tatrensis, commonly known as the Tatran mountain helleborine, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family. It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe, particularly the Carpathian and Alps.

The plant is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves and produces pale green to yellowish flowers during late spring to early summer. It thrives in rocky and limestone-rich soils, often growing in alpine or subalpine habitats.
