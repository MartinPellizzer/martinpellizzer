Justicia demissa

Justicia demissa, commonly known as the hanging jewelweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and clusters of showy, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Justicia demissa thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in warm climates.
