Julocroton montevidensis var. linearifolius

Julocroton montevidensis var. linearifolius, commonly known as the linear-leaved julocroton, is a species native to South America, particularly Uruguay and parts of Brazil.

It is a shrub characterized by its narrow, linear leaves that are often arranged in a dense, compact manner. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Due to its unique foliage, it is often appreciated for its aesthetic appeal in horticultural settings.
