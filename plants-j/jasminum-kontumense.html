Jasminum kontumense

Jasminum kontumense, commonly known as Kontum jasmine, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Vietnam.

It is characterized by its fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the spring and summer seasons. The plant has slender, woody stems and lance-shaped leaves that are dark green in color. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and ornamental value, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
