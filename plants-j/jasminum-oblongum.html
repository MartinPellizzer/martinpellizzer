Jasminum oblongum

Jasminum oblongum, commonly known as the Spanish jasmine, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a slightly waxy texture.

Jasminum oblongum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and as a hedging plant.
