Jucunda

Jucunda, commonly known as the joy plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It features vibrant, colorful blooms that come in shades of red, yellow, and orange. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a smooth, glossy surface. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Jucunda is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is believed to bring a sense of happiness due to its name.
