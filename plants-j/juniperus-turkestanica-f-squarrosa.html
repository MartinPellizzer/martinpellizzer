Juniperus turkestanica f. squarrosa

Juniperus turkestanica f. squarrosa, commonly known as the Afghan juniper, is a shrub or small tree native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, sprawling growth habit and grayish-blue foliage. The plant produces small, berry-like cones that are typically blue or black in color. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in mountainous regions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitats.
