Justicia quinqueangularis var. kawalii

Justicia quinqueangularis var. kawalii, commonly known as the Kawalii flame bush, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features striking, tubular red flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as birds and insects. The plant has angular, ridged stems and lance-shaped leaves with a glossy texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its vibrant color and drought tolerance, making it suitable for xeriscaping and coastal gardens.
