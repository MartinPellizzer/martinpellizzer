Justicia genuflexa

Justicia genuflexa, commonly known as the bowing justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to pink. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Justicia genuflexa thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
