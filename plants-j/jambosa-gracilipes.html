Jambosa gracilipes

Jambosa gracilipes, commonly known as the slender jambosa, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its fruit, which resembles a small pineapple, is edible and has a sweet, juicy flavor.

Jambosa gracilipes is often cultivated for both ornamental purposes and its culinary value.
