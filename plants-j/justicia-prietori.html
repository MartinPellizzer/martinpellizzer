Justicia prietori

Justicia prietori, commonly known as Mexican flame flower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking red or orange tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 meters, with opposite, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its showy flowers attract hummingbirds and other pollinators, contributing to its ecological significance.
