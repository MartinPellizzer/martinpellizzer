Justicia parvispica

Justicia parvispica, commonly known as the small-spurred justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to purple. The plant typically grows as a shrub or perennial herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

Justicia parvispica is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.
