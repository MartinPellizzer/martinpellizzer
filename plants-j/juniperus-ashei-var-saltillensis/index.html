Juniperus Ashei Var. Saltillensis

Juniperus ashei var. saltillensis is a rare and unique subspecies of juniper native to the arid regions of Texas. It is known for its compact growth habit and ability to thrive in harsh, dry conditions. This plant is often found in rocky soils and is adapted to withstand extreme temperatures and limited water availability. Its small, scale-like leaves and blue-gray foliage make it a striking addition to native landscapes. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a valuable and endangered species in its natural habitat.
