Jurinea fedtschenkoana

Jurinea fedtschenkoana, commonly known as the Mongolian sandmat, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which helps it retain moisture in harsh, dry environments. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the summer months, typically blooming in clusters along the stems. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to withstand extreme temperature fluctuations.

Jurinea fedtschenkoana plays a role in stabilizing dunes and is an important component of its native ecosystem.
