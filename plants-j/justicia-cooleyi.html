Justicia cooleyi

Justicia cooleyi, commonly known as the rainbow plant or blue mist shrub, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a vibrant display. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery-gray pubescence. It is a member of the Acanthaceae family and is valued for its ornamental qualities in gardens.

Justicia cooleyi thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a versatile choice for landscaping.
