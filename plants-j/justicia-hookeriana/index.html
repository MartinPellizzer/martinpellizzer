Justicia Hookeriana

Justicia hookeriana is a striking flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America. Known for its vibrant, tubular flowers that range from red to orange, it captivates with its bold colors and unique shape. This species thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in forest understories or along stream banks. Its ornamental value has made it a popular choice for gardeners seeking exotic and eye-catching plants. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in the horticultural world compared to more commonly cultivated species.
