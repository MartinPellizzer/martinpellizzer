Joinvillea elegans subsp. bryanii

Joinvillea elegans subsp.

bryanii, commonly known as Bryan's flame tree, is a striking evergreen shrub or small tree native to Brazil. It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and striking orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters.

The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for tropical and subtropical climates. Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for landscaping.

The subspecies is distinguished by its slightly smaller size and more compact growth habit compared to the typical form of Joinvillea elegans.
