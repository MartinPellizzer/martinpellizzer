Jurinea bungei

Jurinea bungei, commonly known as the Bunge's sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of western China.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its distinctive blue to purple flowers and silver-gray leaves. The plant thrives in sandy or rocky coastal habitats and is adapted to saline and windy environments. It produces small, wind-pollinated flowers that bloom in late summer.
