Justicia marojejiensis

Justicia marojejiensis, commonly known as the Marojejy justicia, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats within the rainforest ecosystems of the Marojejy National Park. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

This species is of particular interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and limited distribution.
