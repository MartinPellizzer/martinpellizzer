Justicia chiengmaiensis

Justicia chiengmaiensis, commonly known as the Chieng Mai justicia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows as an herb or shrub, often found in moist, shady environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are usually opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
