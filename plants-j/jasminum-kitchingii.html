Jasminum kitchingii

Jasminum kitchingii, commonly known as the Kitching's jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate with a glossy surface.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade.
