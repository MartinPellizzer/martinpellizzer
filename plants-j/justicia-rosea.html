Justicia rosea

Justicia rosea, commonly known as the red flame plant, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It features showy, tubular red flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators like hummingbirds and bees. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-draining soil.

Justicia rosea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
