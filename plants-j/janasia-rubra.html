Janasia rubra

Janasia rubra, commonly known as the red flame orchid, is a striking flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its vibrant red petals and elongated, slender leaves. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers bloom in clusters, producing a striking display of color that attracts pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Due to its ornamental value, Janasia rubra is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant in warm climates.
