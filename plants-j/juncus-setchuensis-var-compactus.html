Juncus setchuensis var. compactus

Juncus setchuensis var. compactus, commonly known as the compact rush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of East Asia.

It typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes, stream banks, and wet meadows. The plant features dense, cylindrical clusters of grass-like leaves that are often dark green in color. Its flowering spikes are usually slender and bear small, inconspicuous flowers.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in aquatic habitats.
