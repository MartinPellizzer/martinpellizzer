Jussiaea suffruticosa var. angustifolia

Jussiaea suffruticosa var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf jussiaea, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It typically grows in moist, acidic soils and is often found in wetland or swampy habitats. The plant features slender, angular stems that are often reddish in color. Its leaves are lanceolate, with a narrow, pointed shape, and are covered in fine, soft hairs.

It produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators.
