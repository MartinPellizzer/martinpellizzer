Jacksonia Scoparia Var. Macrocarpa

Jacksonia scoparia var. macrocarpa is a striking native plant of western australia known for its vibrant yellow flowers and unique leaf structure. it belongs to the daisy family and is often found in sandy soils along coastal areas. this variety is distinguished by its larger, showy flowers compared to other jacksonia species. it thrives in full sun and is commonly used in gardens for its ornamental value. despite its beauty, it requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot and flourish.
