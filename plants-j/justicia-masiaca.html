Justicia masiaca

Justicia masiaca, commonly known as the red-flowered justicia, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a rough texture. The plant produces clusters of showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, pink, or purple, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Justicia masiaca is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
