Janusia schwannioides

Janusia schwannioides, commonly known as the Schwanii fern, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are typically light green in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous spore-bearing structures called sori on the underside of its leaves. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
