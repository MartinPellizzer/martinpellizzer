Justago cleomoides

Justago cleomoides, commonly known as the yellow cleome, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Cleomaceae and is characterized by its showy, yellow or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb with slender stems and lobed leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its botanical features include small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds, aiding in its propagation.
