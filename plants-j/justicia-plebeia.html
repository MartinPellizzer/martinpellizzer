Justicia plebeia

Justicia plebeia, commonly known as the common justicia, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb, often reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and slightly hairy, while its stems are square and branching.

Justicia plebeia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
