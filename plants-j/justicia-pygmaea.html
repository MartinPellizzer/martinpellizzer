Justicia pygmaea

Justicia pygmaea, commonly known as dwarf flame flower, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a contrasting blue or purple throat. The plant has small, narrow leaves that are arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
