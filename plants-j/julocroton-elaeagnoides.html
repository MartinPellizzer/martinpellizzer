Julocroton elaeagnoides

Julocroton elaeagnoides, commonly known as the silverleaf shrub, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage. The leaves are simple, opposite, and have a distinctive silvery coating due to the presence of fine hairs. It produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters, which are followed by small, berry-like fruits.

This plant is often found in coastal and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions.
