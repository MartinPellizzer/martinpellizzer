Justicia fruticulosa

Justicia fruticulosa, commonly known as redroot false dragonhead, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy spikes of tubular, two-lipped flowers in shades of red, pink, or purple. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soils.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and native plant landscapes.
