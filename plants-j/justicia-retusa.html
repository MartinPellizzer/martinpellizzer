Justicia retusa

Justicia retusa, commonly known as the woolly false dragonhead, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its hairy, gray-green foliage and spikes of showy, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping and rock gardens. Its ornamental value comes from its long-lasting blooms and distinctive foliage texture.

The species is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
