Justicia tubulosa

Justicia tubulosa, commonly known as the tubular justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb with opposite leaves that are often lance-shaped. Its flowering period occurs during the warm seasons, and it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

The plant is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators like bees and hummingbirds.
