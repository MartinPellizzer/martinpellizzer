Justicia ruwenzoriensis

Justicia ruwenzoriensis, commonly known as the Ruwenzori justicia, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of East Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or purple tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or forested areas, thriving in cool and humid conditions. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered with fine hairs.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
