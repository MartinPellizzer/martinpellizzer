Jasminum pulchrifoliatum

Jasminum pulchrifoliatum, commonly known as the beautiful-leaf jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant belongs to the Oleaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The flowers emit a sweet aroma, making it a popular choice for gardens and as a cut flower.
