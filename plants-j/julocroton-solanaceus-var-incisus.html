Julocroton solanaceus var. incisus

Julocroton solanaceus var. incisus, commonly known as the incised julocroton, is a member of the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with distinct incisions, giving it a unique and ornamental appearance. It thrives in warm, tropical climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

This variant is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and as a decorative plant.
