Juno dengernsis

Juno dengernsis, commonly known as the silverleaf juniper, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its scale-like leaves that are typically covered in a whitish, powdery coating, giving it a silvery appearance. The plant produces small, berry-like cones that are often used in traditional herbal medicine. Juno dengernsis thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to arid environments and contribute to its ecological significance in its native habitat.
