Jamesbrittenia namaquensis

Jamesbrittenia namaquensis, commonly known as the Namaqua daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy yellow flowers with a central disc. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, inconspicuous fruits.
