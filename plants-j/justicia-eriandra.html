Justicia eriandra

Justicia eriandra, commonly known as the flame violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to purple. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy texture.
