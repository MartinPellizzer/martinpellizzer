Justicia cuixmalensis

Justicia cuixmalensis, commonly known as the Cuixmalensis flameflower, is a striking plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range from red to orange. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a rough texture, while its stems are hairy.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
