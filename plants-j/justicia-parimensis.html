Justicia parimensis

Justicia parimensis, commonly known as the pink shower plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows as a shrub or subshrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Justicia parimensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
