Justicia gandarussa

Justicia gandarussa, commonly known as the Java flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Justicia gandarussa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
