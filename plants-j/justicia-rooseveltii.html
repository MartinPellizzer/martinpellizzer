Justicia rooseveltii

Justicia rooseveltii, commonly known as Roosevelt's flame flower, is a striking tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red-orange tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators like hummingbirds and bees. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green, providing a striking contrast to its colorful blooms. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for warm, arid climates.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes in suitable regions.
