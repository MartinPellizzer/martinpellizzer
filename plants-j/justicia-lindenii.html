Justicia lindenii

Justicia lindenii, commonly known as the Lindley's flame flower, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

Justicia lindenii thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
