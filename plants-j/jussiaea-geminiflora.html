Jussiaea geminiflora

Jussiaea geminiflora, commonly known as the twin-flowered jussiaea, is a herbaceous plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Onagraceae family and is characterized by its prostrate growth habit and small, tubular flowers. The plant produces two flowers per node, giving it its species name "geminiflora.". It thrives in moist, sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners in suitable climates.
