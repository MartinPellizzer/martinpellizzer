Jurighas decipiens

Jurighas decipiens, commonly known as the deceptive jurigha, is a rare flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

It is characterized by its striking, violet-blue petals that resemble those of more common species, potentially aiding in pollinator attraction. The plant has a compact growth habit with a short stem and dense foliage. Its flowers bloom in late summer, producing nectar that attracts specific pollinators.
