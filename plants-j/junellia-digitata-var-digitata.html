Junellia digitata var. digitata

Junellia digitata var. digitata, commonly known as the slender thimbleweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or wetland habitats. The plant features slender, upright stems that are often hairy, with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that have a distinct serrated edge. Its small, white flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom in the summer months.

Junellia digitata var. digitata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing food and habitat for various pollinators.
