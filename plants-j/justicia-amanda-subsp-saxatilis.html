Justicia amanda subsp. saxatilis

Justicia amanda subsp.

saxatilis, commonly known as the rock justicia, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and clusters of bright blue or violet flowers.

The leaves are typically narrow and slightly hairy, adapting to arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
