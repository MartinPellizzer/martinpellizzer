Justicia ceylanica var. capitata

Justicia ceylanica var. capitata, commonly known as the clustered flame flower, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that are usually red or orange in color, arranged in clusters at the ends of branches. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a smooth texture.

It thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
