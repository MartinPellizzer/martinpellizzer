Jussiaea nervosa var. marginata

Jussiaea nervosa var. marginata, commonly known as the margined water plant, is a species of aquatic plant characterized by its delicate, floating leaves.

The leaves are typically oval to elliptical in shape, with a distinct white or pale margin that sets it apart from other varieties. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, often found in ponds and marshes. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color.

Its botanical features make it an important species for aquatic ecosystems, providing habitat and food for various aquatic organisms.
