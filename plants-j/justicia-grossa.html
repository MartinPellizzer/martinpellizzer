Justicia grossa

Justicia grossa, commonly known as the red hot poker, is a striking perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features tall, spike-like inflorescences that are bright red or orange, giving it its common name. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Justicia grossa is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and birds.
