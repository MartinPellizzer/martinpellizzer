Jacobaea × reisachii

Jacobaea × reisachii, commonly known as the Everlasting or Immortelle, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to southern Europe and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant produces small, yellow to orange composite flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. Its leaves are typically gray-green and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Jacobaea × reisachii is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
