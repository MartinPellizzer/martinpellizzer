Justicia oerstedii

Justicia oerstedii, commonly known as the Mexican wheelcactus, is a species of flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is a succulent perennial that features a short, thick stem with clusters of bright yellow flowers. The plant has spiny, fleshy leaves that help it store water, adapting to its arid environment. Its flowers bloom in the summer and are often visited by bees and other pollinators.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
