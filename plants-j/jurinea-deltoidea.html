Jurinea deltoidea

Jurinea deltoidea, commonly known as the delta lousewort, is a flowering plant native to arctic and alpine regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It is a small, herbaceous species that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces delicate, violet-colored flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs.

Jurinea deltoidea is adapted to cold climates and is often found in areas with strong winds and low temperatures.
