Jasione undulata

Jasione undulata, commonly known as the wavy bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue to purple. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and slightly undulating in appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, typically found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Jasione undulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
