Juncus maritimus var. socotranus

Juncus maritimus var. socotranus, commonly known as Socotran rush, is a perennial plant native to the Socotra Archipelago.

It is a grass-like plant characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in coastal and saline environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in spike-like inflorescences.

This variant is notable for its unique morphology and ecological significance in its native habitat.
