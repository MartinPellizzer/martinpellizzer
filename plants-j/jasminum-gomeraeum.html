Jasminum gomeraeum

Jasminum gomeraeum, commonly known as the Gomeran jasmine, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant produces fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Jasminum gomeraeum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its pleasant scent.
