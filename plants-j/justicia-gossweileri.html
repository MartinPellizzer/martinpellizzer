Justicia gossweileri

Justicia gossweileri, commonly known as the Cape rose, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. The plant produces clusters of showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, pink, or white, which bloom throughout the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and sandy environments.

The plant is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as birds and insects.
