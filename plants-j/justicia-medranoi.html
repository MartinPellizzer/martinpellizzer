Justicia medranoi

Justicia medranoi, commonly known as the Medrano's flame flower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.

The flowers bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and hummingbirds.
