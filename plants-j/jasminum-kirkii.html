Jasminum kirkii

Jasminum kirkii, commonly known as the African jasmine, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height, featuring woody stems and opposite, simple leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, blooming in clusters. Its flowers are borne on short pedicels and are often visited by insects and birds.

Jasminum kirkii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
