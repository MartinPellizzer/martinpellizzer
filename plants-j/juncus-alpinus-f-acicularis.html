Juncus alpinus f. acicularis

Juncus alpinus f. acicularis, commonly known as the needle rush, is a variant of the alpine rush species.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Juncaceae family, characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance. The plant features narrow, needle-like leaves that are typically green and densely packed. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine and subalpine regions.

Its small, inconspicuous flowers are usually clustered at the tips of the stems.
