Junellia hookeriana var. hookeriana

Junellia hookeriana var. hookeriana, commonly known as the Hooker's junellia, is a herbaceous plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It typically grows in high-altitude regions with cool, moist conditions, often found in cloud forests. The plant features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually in dense inflorescences, and can range in color from white to pale purple.

Junellia hookeriana var. hookeriana is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in specialized gardens.
