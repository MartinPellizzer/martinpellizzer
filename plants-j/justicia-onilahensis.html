Justicia onilahensis

Justicia onilahensis, commonly known as the Hawaiian flame flower, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy bracts that resemble petals. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. It thrives in coastal areas and is adapted to the unique climate and soil conditions of Hawaii.
