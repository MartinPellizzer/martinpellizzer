Justicia syncollotheca

Justicia syncollotheca, commonly known as the Mexican flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to Central America and the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a contrasting white or yellow throat. The plant produces small, green, egg-shaped fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and as a cut flower.
