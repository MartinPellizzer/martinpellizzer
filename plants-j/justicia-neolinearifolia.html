Justicia neolinearifolia

Justicia neolinearifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved flame bush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a textured appearance. The plant produces vibrant red or orange tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators like birds and insects. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
