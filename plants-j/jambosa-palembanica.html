Jambosa palembanica

Jambosa palembanica, commonly known as the Palembang jambosa, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, green, and often spiny stems. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by oval, fleshy fruits resembling small apples. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a waxy texture.
