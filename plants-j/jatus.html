Jatus

Jatus, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and chrysanthemums. The plant features showy, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center, blooming throughout the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Jatus is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
