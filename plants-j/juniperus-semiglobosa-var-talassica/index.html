Juniperus Semiglobosa Var. Talassica

Juniperus semiglobosa var. talassica is a unique and resilient evergreen shrub native to the arid regions of Central Asia. This variant of the juniper tree is known for its compact growth habit and ability to thrive in harsh, dry environments. Its foliage consists of small, scale-like leaves that are typically a deep green color. The plant produces small, berry-like cones that are an important food source for local wildlife. Due to its hardy nature and ornamental appeal, it is increasingly being cultivated in xeriscaping and drought-tolerant gardens.
