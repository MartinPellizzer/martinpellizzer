Justicia sulfurea

Justicia sulfurea, commonly known as golden justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow bracts that surround small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Justicia sulfurea is valued in horticulture for its vibrant color and ability to thrive in full sun to partial shade.
