Jambosa nitida

Jambosa nitida, commonly known as the slender jambosa, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its thin, branching stems and small, alternate leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
