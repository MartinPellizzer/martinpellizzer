Justicia jamisonii

Justicia jamisonii, commonly known as the Jamison's flameflower, is a striking tropical plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 meters, with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees.

The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
