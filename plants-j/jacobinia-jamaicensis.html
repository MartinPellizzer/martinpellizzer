Jacobinia jamaicensis

Jacobinia jamaicensis, commonly known as the Jamaican jacaranda, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean, particularly Jamaica.

It belongs to the Bignoniaceae family and is characterized by its striking clusters of violet or purple flowers.
