Justicia baronii

Justicia baronii, commonly known as the Baron's flame flower, is a tropical plant native to Central America and the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its striking clusters of orange-red tubular flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has opposite, narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Justicia baronii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
