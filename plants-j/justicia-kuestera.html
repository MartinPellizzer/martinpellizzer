Justicia kuestera

Justicia kuestera, commonly known as the purple glorybower, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States and parts of Mexico.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking spikes of violet to purple flowers. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
