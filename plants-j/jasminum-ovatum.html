Jasminum ovatum

Jasminum ovatum, commonly known as the white jasminum or the oval jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and ovate, with a glossy green surface.

Jasminum ovatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.
