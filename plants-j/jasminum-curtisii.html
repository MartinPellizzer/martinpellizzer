Jasminum curtisii

Jasminum curtisii, commonly known as the Cape jasmine, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a smooth texture.

Jasminum curtisii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to well-drained soils.
