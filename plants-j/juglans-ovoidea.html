Juglans ovoidea

Juglans ovoidea, commonly known as the oval walnut, is a species of flowering plant in the Juglandaceae family.

It is native to western North America, particularly in regions of California and Oregon. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and distinctive, oval-shaped nuts encased in a thick, woody husk. The tree can grow up to 20 meters tall and produces both male and female flowers on the same tree.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological and horticultural contexts.
