Julocroton triqueter var. genuinus

Julocroton triqueter var. genuinus, commonly known as the three-lobed croton, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in cyathium structures, a feature common in the Euphorbiaceae family.
