Jasione sessiliflora var. bovei

Jasione sessiliflora var. bovei, commonly known as the Bove's narrow-leaved bellflower, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features basal leaves that are narrow and sessile, with a hairy texture. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or limestone habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and ornamental species within the Caryophyllaceae family.
