Jubelina

Jubelina, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a popular flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the genus Schlumbergera and is characterized by its segmented stems and vibrant blooms. The plant produces flowers in a wide range of colors, including red, pink, orange, and white, typically during the holiday season. Jubelina thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance and seasonal blooming make it a favored houseplant for its ornamental value.
