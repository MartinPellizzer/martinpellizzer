Jatropha quinqueformis

Jatropha quinqueformis, commonly known as the five-lobed spurge, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height, featuring rough, ridged bark and dark green, alternate leaves.
