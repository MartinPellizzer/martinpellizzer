Jurinea hyrcana

Jurinea hyrcana, commonly known as the Central Asian sandflower, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with small, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has adapted to harsh environmental conditions through its deep root system and succulent leaves. It thrives in sandy soils and is often found in desert steppe and dune environments.

Jurinea hyrcana plays a role in stabilizing sandy soils and is of interest in ecological restoration projects.
