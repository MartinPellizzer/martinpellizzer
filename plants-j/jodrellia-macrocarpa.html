Jodrellia macrocarpa

Jodrellia macrocarpa, commonly known as the giant burrweed, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, woody, spiny capsules that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically pollinated by insects. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a rough texture.

Jodrellia macrocarpa is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
