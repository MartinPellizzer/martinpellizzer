Justicia pittieri

Justicia pittieri, commonly known as the Mexican flame flower, is a striking tropical plant native to Central America and the Caribbean.

It is a herbaceous perennial that features showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Justicia pittieri is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a container plant.
