Joosia macrocalyx

Joosia macrocalyx, commonly known as the large-flowered joosia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its showy, white to pale pink flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Joosia macrocalyx is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native plant gardens.
