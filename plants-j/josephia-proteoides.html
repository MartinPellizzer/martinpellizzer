Josephia proteoides

Josephia proteoides, commonly known as the mountain protea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family, which includes many ornamental and ecologically significant plants. The plant is characterized by its large, showy inflorescence composed of numerous small white to pink flowers. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological role, it is valued in both horticulture and conservation efforts.
