Justicia pilifera

Justicia pilifera, commonly known as the silky justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and clusters of showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses for its attractive foliage and blooms.
