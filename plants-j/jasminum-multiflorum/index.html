Jasminum Multiflorum

Jasminum multiflorum is a fragrant flowering plant known for its clusters of small white blooms. It is commonly found in tropical and subtropical regions around the world. This species is often cultivated for its pleasant aroma and ornamental value. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight. Its versatility makes it a popular choice for gardens and indoor spaces.
