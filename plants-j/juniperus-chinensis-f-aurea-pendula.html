Juniperus chinensis f. aurea-pendula

Juniperus chinensis f. aurea-pendula, commonly known as the golden weeping juniper, is a compact, shrub-like form of the Chinese juniper.

It features dense, cascading branches that give it a weeping appearance, making it a popular ornamental plant. The foliage is bright golden-yellow, which contrasts beautifully with its dark bark and provides year-round visual interest. It is relatively drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for a variety of garden settings.

This cultivar is also resistant to pests and diseases, contributing to its ease of maintenance.
