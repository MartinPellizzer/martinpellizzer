Jasione orbiculata var. italica

Jasione orbiculata var. italica, commonly known as Italian wallflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow or orange flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and thrives in full sun. It has small, oval-shaped leaves and produces compact, rounded flower heads.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
