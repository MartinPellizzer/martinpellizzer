Jobinia neei

Jobinia neei, commonly known as the Natal orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its roots are fibrous, and it has narrow, grass-like leaves.

Jobinia neei is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
