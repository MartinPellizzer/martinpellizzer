Justicia skutchii

Justicia skutchii, commonly known as Skutch's glorybower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with opposite, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, which extends from spring to early summer.
