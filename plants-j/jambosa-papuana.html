Jambosa papuana

Jambosa papuana, commonly known as the Papuan jambosa, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Papua.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes eucalyptus and other aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers and broad, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland rainforests.
