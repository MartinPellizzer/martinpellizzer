Jumellea facilorchis

Jumellea facilorchis, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, elongated stems and small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pale pink. Native to the tropical regions of South America, this species thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

Its unique floral structure and ornamental appearance make it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
