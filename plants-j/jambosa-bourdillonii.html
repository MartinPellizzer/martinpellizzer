Jambosa bourdillonii

Jambosa bourdillonii, commonly known as the African star gooseberry, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, yellow, star-shaped fruits that are edible and have a tart flavor. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually oval or elliptic in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Jambosa bourdillonii is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
