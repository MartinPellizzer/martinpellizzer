Jasminum schweinfurthii

Jasminum schweinfurthii, commonly known as the African jasmine, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves and can grow as a shrub or small tree. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Jasminum schweinfurthii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic blooms.
