Justicia obcordata

Justicia obcordata, commonly known as the red flame plant or red justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are opposite in arrangement, and it thrives in warm, humid climates. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant floral display and adaptability to various growing conditions.

Justicia obcordata is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
