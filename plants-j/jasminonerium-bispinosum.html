Jasminonerium bispinosum

Jasminonerium bispinosum, commonly known as the spiny jasmine, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its woody stems and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant features spiny projections on its stems, which serve as a defense mechanism against herbivores. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Jasminonerium bispinosum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and distinctive scent.
