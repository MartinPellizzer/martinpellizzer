Justicia filibracteolata var. azurea

Justicia filibracteolata var. azurea, commonly known as the Azure Whorltongue, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in dense, whorled clusters along the stems. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, while the stems are often hairy as well.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
