Jasminum nilagiricum

Jasminum nilagiricum, commonly known as the Nilgiri jasmine, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters, often emitting a sweet scent. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a smooth, glossy surface.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
