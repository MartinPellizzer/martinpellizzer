Justicia scansilis

Justicia scansilis, commonly known as the red mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 2 to 3 feet. The plant features showy, tubular red flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Justicia scansilis thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often used in ornamental landscaping for its vibrant blooms.
