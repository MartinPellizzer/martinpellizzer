Julocroton solanaceus

Julocroton solanaceus, commonly known as the red-leaved croton, is a member of the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage, with leaves often displaying shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically has a shrub-like growth habit and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by berries. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Due to its striking appearance, it is a popular choice for ornamental gardens and indoor plant collections.
