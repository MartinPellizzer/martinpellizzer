Junellia caespitosa

Junellia caespitosa, commonly known as the purpletop grass, is a perennial grass species native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and distinctive purple-tinted inflorescence. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is well adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Junellia caespitosa plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various small organisms.
