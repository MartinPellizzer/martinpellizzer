Jambosa aromatica

Jambosa aromatica, commonly known as the fragrant jambosa, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, waxy leaves and clusters of fragrant, white flowers. The plant produces edible fruits that are similar in appearance to lychees but have a unique, aromatic flavor. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Jambosa aromatica is often cultivated for both its ornamental value and its flavorful fruits.
