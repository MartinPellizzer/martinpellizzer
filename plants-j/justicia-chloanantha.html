Justicia chloanantha

Justicia chloanantha, commonly known as the white flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, which attract pollinators like hummingbirds and bees. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Justicia chloanantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
