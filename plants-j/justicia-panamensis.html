Justicia panamensis

Justicia panamensis, commonly known as the Panama flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with contrasting yellow or white markings. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils or near water sources.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes in tropical regions.
