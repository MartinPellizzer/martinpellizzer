Justicia mollugo

Justicia mollugo, commonly known as carpet grass or small-flowered justicia, is a low-growing perennial herb native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features spreading rhizomes and produces small, tubular flowers in shades of white, pink, or purple. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its dense, carpet-like growth habit. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is adaptable to various light conditions.

The genus Justicia is part of the Acanthaceae family, known for its showy inflorescences and medicinal properties in some species.
