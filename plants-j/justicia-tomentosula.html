Justicia tomentosula

Justicia tomentosula, commonly known as the woolly false dragonhead, is a perennial herb native to the southern United States and northern Mexico.

It features hairy, grayish-green leaves that are opposite and ovate in shape. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple or pink, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
