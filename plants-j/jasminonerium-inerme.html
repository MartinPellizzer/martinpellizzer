Jasminonerium inerme

Jasminonerium inerme, commonly known as the silverleaf jasmine, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Jasminonerium inerme is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant scent.
