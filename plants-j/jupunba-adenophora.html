Jupunba adenophora

Jupunba adenophora, commonly known as the mountain tea plant, is a shrub native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its small, dark green leaves and clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in rocky soils and is adapted to cold, arid environments.

Jupunba adenophora is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
