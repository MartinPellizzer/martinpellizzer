Justicia migeodii

Justicia migeodii, commonly known as the Mexican flame flower, is a species of flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters.
