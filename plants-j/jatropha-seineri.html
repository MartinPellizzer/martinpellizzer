Jatropha seineri

Jatropha seineri, commonly known as the rock jatropha, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Arabian Peninsula.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant produces yellow flowers that are clustered in small umbels, and its leaves are simple and alternate. Jatropha seineri is known for its ability to thrive in poor soils and withstand drought conditions.

It is also noted for its use in traditional medicine and as a potential source of biofuel.
