Jacobaea carnosa

Jacobaea carnosa, commonly known as the woolly fleabane, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow composite flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open grasslands and along coastal areas.

Jacobaea carnosa is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique texture and color.
