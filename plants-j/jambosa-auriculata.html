Jambosa auriculata

Jambosa auriculata, commonly known as the ear-shaped jambosa, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive ear-shaped leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. Its fruit is a small, round berry that turns red when mature.

Jambosa auriculata is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and attractive flowers.
