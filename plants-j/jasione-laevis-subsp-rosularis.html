Jasione laevis subsp. rosularis

Jasione laevis subsp.

rosularis, commonly known as the Rosular Jasione, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Europe. It features a mat-forming growth habit with numerous small, rose-like clusters of white or pale blue flowers.

The plant has hairy, linear leaves that are typically arranged in rosettes, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique and attractive addition to alpine and rock garden settings.
