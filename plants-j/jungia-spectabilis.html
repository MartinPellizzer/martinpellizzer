Jungia spectabilis

Jungia spectabilis, commonly known as the showy jungia, is a striking plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with contrasting yellow or white markings. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a velvety texture.

Jungia spectabilis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.
