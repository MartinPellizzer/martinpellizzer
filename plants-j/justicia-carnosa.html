Justicia carnosa

Justicia carnosa, commonly known as the woollyleaf false heather, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers. Its ornamental value comes from its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms.

It is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
