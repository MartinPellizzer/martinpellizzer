Justicia megalantha

Justicia megalantha, commonly known as the giant flame flower, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its showy, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Justicia megalantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
