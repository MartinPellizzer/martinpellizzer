Jasminum mossamedense

Jasminum mossamedense, commonly known as the Mossamede jasmine, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a smooth texture with a glossy appearance.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its pleasant scent.
