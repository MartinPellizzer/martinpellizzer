Justicia boliviensis

Justicia boliviensis, commonly known as the Bolivian flame flower, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces vibrant, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Its showy blooms make it a popular choice for gardens and container planting.
