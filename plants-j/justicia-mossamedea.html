Justicia mossamedea

Justicia mossamedea, commonly known as the Natal wheelcactus, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a short, thick stem with a crown of fleshy, spiny leaves that are often tinged with red or purple. The plant produces clusters of vibrant blue or purple flowers during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
