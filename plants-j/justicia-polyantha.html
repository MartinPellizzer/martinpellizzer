Justicia polyantha

Justicia polyantha, commonly known as the flame flower or red hot poker, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It features striking, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in dense clusters, attracting pollinators like hummingbirds and bees. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens in warm climates.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant colors and long blooming period.
