Jordaaniella

Jordaaniella, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to southern Africa.

These plants are characterized by their upright growth habit and showy, often velvety flowers that come in a variety of colors. The leaves are typically velvety and heart-shaped, often with a waxy texture. Jordaaniella species are popular as houseplants due to their ability to thrive in low-light conditions and their compact, ornamental appearance.

They are also valued for their ability to bloom continuously throughout the year under proper care.
