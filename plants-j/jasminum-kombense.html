Jasminum kombense

Jasminum kombense, commonly known as the African jasmine, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, often releasing a sweet scent during the night. The plant has opposite, simple, and usually hairy leaves that are oval to lance-shaped. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Jasminum kombense is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
