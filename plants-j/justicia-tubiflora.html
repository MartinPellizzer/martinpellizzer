Justicia tubiflora

Justicia tubiflora, commonly known as the coralplant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often arranged in dense clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a rough texture.

Justicia tubiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
