Jasminum stenopetalum

Jasminum stenopetalum, commonly known as the narrow-petaled jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has glossy, oval-shaped leaves and is typically shrub-like in growth habit. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The flowers emit a sweet, delicate fragrance, making it a popular choice for gardens and perfumery.
