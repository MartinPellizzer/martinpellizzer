Justicia megaphylla

Justicia megaphylla, commonly known as the giant flame flower, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with contrasting yellow or white markings. The plant belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates. Its foliage consists of broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating.

Justicia megaphylla is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
