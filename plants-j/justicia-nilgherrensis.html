Justicia nilgherrensis

Justicia nilgherrensis, commonly known as the Nilgiri flame of the woods, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its showy, tubular flowers are usually bright red or orange, attracting pollinators like birds and insects.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
