Juniperus × media f. glauca

Juniperus × media f. glauca, commonly known as the Blue Spruce, is a cultivar of the juniper shrub characterized by its striking bluish-gray foliage.

It is a hybrid between Juniperus media and Juniperus horizontalis, resulting in a compact, mound-shaped growth habit. The leaves are scale-like and densely packed, giving the plant a dense, textured appearance. It is highly valued in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.

This evergreen shrub is relatively drought-tolerant and can withstand harsh winter conditions.
