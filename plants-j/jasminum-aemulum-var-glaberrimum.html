Jasminum aemulum var. glaberrimum

Jasminum aemulum var. glaberrimum, commonly known as the smooth jasmine, is a compact shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.

This variety is particularly valued for its dense growth habit and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
