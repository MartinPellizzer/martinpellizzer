Jasminum campyloneurum

Jasminum campyloneurum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2-5 meters. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters, often in the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a smooth texture and a slightly glossy appearance.

Jasminum campyloneurum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its pleasant scent and aesthetic appeal.
