Justicia tricostata

Justicia tricostata, commonly known as the three-costed justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows as an annual or short-lived perennial, often reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a rough texture, while the stems are square and hairy.

Justicia tricostata thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
