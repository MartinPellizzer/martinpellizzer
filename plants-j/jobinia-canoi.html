Jobinia canoi

Jobinia canoi, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It features palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, which are typically green and slightly hairy. The plant produces striking yellow or orange flowers arranged in panicles, which bloom in spring. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Jobinia canoi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
