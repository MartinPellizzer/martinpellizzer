Jaubertia szowitzii

Jaubertia szowitzii, commonly known as the sandstone lily, is a striking perennial plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it well-suited for rocky or sandy habitats. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating.

Jaubertia szowitzii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with suitable growing conditions.
