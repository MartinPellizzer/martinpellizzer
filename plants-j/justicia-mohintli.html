Justicia mohintli

Justicia mohintli, commonly known as the Mexican flame flower, is a striking tropical plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators like hummingbirds and bees. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement, providing a lush backdrop to its showy blooms. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Justicia mohintli is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
