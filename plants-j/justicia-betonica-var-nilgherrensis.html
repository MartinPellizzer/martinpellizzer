Justicia betonica var. nilgherrensis

Justicia betonica var. nilgherrensis, commonly known as the Nilgiri justicia, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a herbaceous perennial with lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and arranged oppositely along the stems. The plant produces clusters of tubular, violet-blue flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value and ecological significance make it a notable species in its native habitat.
