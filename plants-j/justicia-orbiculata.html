Justicia orbiculata

Justicia orbiculata, commonly known as the globe flower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its orbicular, velvety leaves and clusters of tubular, brightly colored flowers that range from pink to red. The plant is part of the Acanthaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

The flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, making it a valuable addition to gardens.
