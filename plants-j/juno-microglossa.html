Juno microglossa

Juno microglossa, commonly known as the narrow-leafed juniper, is a small evergreen shrub native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and small, scale-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, berry-like cones that are often used in traditional herbal medicine. Juno microglossa thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for rocky or arid landscapes.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to xeriscapes and native plant gardens.
