Justicia leucoclada

Justicia leucoclada, commonly known as the white flameplant, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant produces clusters of tubular, white to pinkish flowers that are often arranged in terminal spikes. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture.

Justicia leucoclada is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
