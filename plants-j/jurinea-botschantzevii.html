Jurinea botschantzevii

Jurinea botschantzevii, commonly known as the Botchantzev's sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

The plant produces striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Jurinea botschantzevii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize coastal dunes.
