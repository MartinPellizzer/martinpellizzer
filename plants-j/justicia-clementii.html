Justicia clementii

Justicia clementii, commonly known as the Mexican flame flower, is a striking tropical plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces vibrant, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and can grow up to 3 meters tall, making it a dramatic addition to gardens. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, requiring moderate water.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms, which attract pollinators like hummingbirds and bees.
