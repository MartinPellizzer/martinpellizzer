Justicia pilosella

Justicia pilosella, commonly known as the woolly flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its attractive, showy bracts that range in color from red to orange, resembling petals. The plant has hairy, glandular leaves that help reduce water loss in its native arid environments. It produces small, tubular flowers that attract hummingbirds and other pollinators.

Justicia pilosella is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
