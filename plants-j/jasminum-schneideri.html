Jasminum schneideri

Jasminum schneideri, commonly known as the Schneiders jasmine, is a species of flowering plant in the Oleaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the rainy season. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and ovate, with a smooth texture.

Jasminum schneideri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.
