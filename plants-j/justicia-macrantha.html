Justicia macrantha

Justicia macrantha, commonly known as the flame flower or red flame bush, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as birds and insects. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Justicia macrantha is often used in ornamental gardening due to its vibrant floral display.
