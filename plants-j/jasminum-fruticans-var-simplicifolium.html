Jasminum fruticans var. simplicifolium

Jasminum fruticans var. simplicifolium, commonly known as the simple-leaved evergreen jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are typically simple, ovate, and have a glossy surface. This species thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

It is also valued in traditional medicine for its aromatic properties.
