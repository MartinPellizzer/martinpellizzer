Jurinea thiebautii

Jurinea thiebautii, commonly known as the Thiebaut's sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

Jurinea thiebautii is often found in maritime climates and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
