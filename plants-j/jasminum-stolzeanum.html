Jasminum stolzeanum

Jasminum stolzeanum, commonly known as the Stolze's jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows up to 3 meters in height, characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are simple, opposite, and have a glossy, dark green surface. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The plant is also valued in traditional medicine for its aromatic properties.
