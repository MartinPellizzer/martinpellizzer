Justicia attenuifolia

Justicia attenuifolia, commonly known as the slender flame flower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. Justicia attenuifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
