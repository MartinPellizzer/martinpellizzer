Juniperus chinensis f. pfitzeriana

Juniperus chinensis f. pfitzeriana, commonly known as the Pfitzer juniper, is a cultivar of the Chinese juniper.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and dark green, needle-like foliage. The plant produces small, blue-black berries that are typically retained on the plant for an extended period. It is widely used in horticulture for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

This form is particularly valued for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions and its resistance to pests and diseases.
