Jasione perennis var. cedretorum

Jasione perennis var. cedretorum, commonly known as the Cedretum jasmine, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from April to May. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and a fibrous root system, which helps it thrive in well-drained soils. It is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats, preferring full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
