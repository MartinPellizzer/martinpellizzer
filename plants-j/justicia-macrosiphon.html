Justicia macrosiphon

Justicia macrosiphon, commonly known as the flame violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a contrasting blue or purple throat. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often hairy, while its stems are erect and branched.

Justicia macrosiphon is valued in horticulture for its vibrant blooms and is often used in gardens and as a cut flower.
