Jasminum heterophyllum var. glabricymosum

Jasminum heterophyllum var. glabricymosum, commonly known as the smooth-leaf jasmine, is a climbing shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features aromatic white flowers that bloom in clusters, often emitting a sweet fragrance. The leaves are typically smooth and glossy, with a lanceolate shape. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and requires well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticulture for its fragrance and visual beauty.
