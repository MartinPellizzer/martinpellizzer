Jasminum confusum

Jasminum confusum, commonly known as the Chinese jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a woody shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the spring and summer seasons. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate, with a smooth texture.

Jasminum confusum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its pleasant scent.
