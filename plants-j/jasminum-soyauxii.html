Jasminum soyauxii

Jasminum soyauxii, commonly known as the Madagascar jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a slightly hairy texture.

It is well adapted to the humid, tropical climate of its native habitat.
