Justicia pathanamthittiensis

Justicia pathanamthittiensis, commonly known as the Kerala justicia, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are clustered in spikes and are usually pink or purple in color.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often found in gardens and natural habitats across its native range.
