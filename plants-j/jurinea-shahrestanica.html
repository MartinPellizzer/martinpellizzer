Jurinea shahrestanica

Jurinea shahrestanica, commonly known as the Shahrestanica flower, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate, and often pale pink or white blossoms. The plant is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments, featuring succulent leaves that store water. It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in mountainous areas.

Due to its unique ecological niche, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
