Juncus × montellii

Juncus × montellii, commonly known as the hybrid rush, is a flowering plant in the family Juncaceae.

It is a sterile hybrid resulting from the cross between Juncus effusus and Juncus roeperi. This plant typically grows in wet or moist environments, such as marshes, stream banks, and pond edges. It has tall, cylindrical stems covered in narrow, grass-like leaves.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually greenish or brownish in color.
