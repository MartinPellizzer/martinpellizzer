Justicia aymardii

Justicia aymardii, commonly known as the Aymard's flameflower, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Justicia aymardii thrives in full sun to partial shade and is valued for its long-lasting blooms and adaptability to various soil types.
