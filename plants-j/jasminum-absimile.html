Jasminum absimile

Jasminum absimile, commonly known as the Philippine jasmine, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Oleaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and lanceolate in shape, with a glossy green surface.
