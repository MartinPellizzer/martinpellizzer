Jasminum nintooides

Jasminum nintooides, commonly known as the Japanese jasmine or Ninto's jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It is a deciduous shrub that produces fragrant white flowers in late spring to early summer. The plant has simple, opposite, and ovate leaves that are typically dark green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

The flowers are often used in traditional Japanese gardens for their ornamental value and aromatic qualities.
