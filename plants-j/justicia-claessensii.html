Justicia claessensii

Justicia claessensii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom continuously under proper care.
