Justicia urbaniana

Justicia urbaniana, commonly known as the urban jewel plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to purple. The plant typically grows as an herb or shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Justicia urbaniana thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
