Juniperus virginiana var. kosteri

Juniperus virginiana var. kosteri, commonly known as Koster's red cedar, is a variant of the eastern red cedar.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to the southeastern United States. The plant features scale-like leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, arranged in dense clusters. Its cones are small and berry-like, containing toxic seeds that are not edible for humans or livestock.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.
