Jamesia Americana Subsp. Californica

Jamesia americana subsp. californica is a rare and beautiful flowering plant native to the western United States. It is known for its striking pink to red blooms that appear in late spring. This subspecies thrives in rocky soils and partial shade, making it a unique addition to native gardens. Its delicate appearance contrasts with its hardy nature, adapting well to harsh environmental conditions. Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered, highlighting the need for conservation efforts.
