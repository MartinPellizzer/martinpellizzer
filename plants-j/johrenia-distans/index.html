Johrenia Distans

Johrenia distans is a rare and fascinating plant native to the high altitudes of Central America. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is known for its unique floral structure. This species is often found in cloud forests where it adapts to the shaded conditions. Its delicate appearance belies its resilience in challenging ecosystems. Studying Johrenia distans offers insights into the biodiversity of understudied regions.
