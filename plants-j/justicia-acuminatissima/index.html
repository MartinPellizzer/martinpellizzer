Justicia Acuminatissima

Red trumpet, scientifically known as Justicia acuminatissima, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas. It is celebrated for its vibrant, trumpet-shaped blooms that range from deep red to orange, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. This plant thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators like hummingbirds and bees. Its foliage is lush and evergreen, adding year-round visual interest to gardens and landscapes. With proper care, Justicia acuminatissima can flourish, bringing color and life to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
