Justicia anabasa

Justicia anabasa, commonly known as the red-hot poker, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking spikes of bright red, tubular flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and ability to attract pollinators like bees and birds.
