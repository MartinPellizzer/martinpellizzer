Juniperus excelsa var. polycarpos

Juniperus excelsa var. polycarpos, commonly known as the Himalayan juniper, is a coniferous shrub or small tree native to the Himalayas.

It thrives in high-altitude environments, often growing in rocky or mountainous terrain.
