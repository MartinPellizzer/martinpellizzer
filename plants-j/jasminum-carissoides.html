Jasminum carissoides

Jasminum carissoides, commonly known as the sweet jasmine or white jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and ovate with a glossy surface.

Jasminum carissoides is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its pleasant aroma.
