Justicia klossii

Justicia klossii, commonly known as the Kloss's flame flower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a distinctive yellow or white throat. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often lance-shaped, with a rough texture.

Justicia klossii thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in warm climates.
