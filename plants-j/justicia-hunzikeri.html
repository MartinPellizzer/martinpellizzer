Justicia hunzikeri

Justicia hunzikeri, commonly known as the horned morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous annual or perennial, often reaching heights of up to 2 meters. Its leaves are alternate, heart-shaped, and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are hairy and branching.

Justicia hunzikeri thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
