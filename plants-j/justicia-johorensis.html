Justicia johorensis

Justicia johorensis, commonly known as the Bornean flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a rough texture and dark green color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found near streams or in forest undergrowth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
