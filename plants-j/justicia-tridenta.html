Justicia tridenta

Justicia tridenta, commonly known as the three-toothed justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as an herb or shrub, often reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have three distinct lobes, giving it its common name.

Justicia tridenta thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens in warm climates.
