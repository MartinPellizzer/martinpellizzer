Justicia mkungweensis

Justicia mkungweensis, commonly known as the KwaZulu-Natal flame flower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking tubular red flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forested areas. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
