Justicia trivialis

Justicia trivialis, commonly known as the common justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb, often reaching heights of up to 3 feet. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture, while its stems are square in cross-section.

Justicia trivialis thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
