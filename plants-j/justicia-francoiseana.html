Justicia francoiseana

Justicia francoiseana, commonly known as the pink fairy fan, is a striking tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its vibrant pink to purple bracts that surround clusters of small, tubular flowers. The plant belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Its foliage is often lance-shaped with a glossy texture, adding to its visual appeal.
