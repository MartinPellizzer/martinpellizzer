Jasminum swynnertonii

Jasminum swynnertonii, commonly known as the Swynnerton's jasmine, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a faint yellowish hue. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and lance-shaped, with a smooth texture.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.
