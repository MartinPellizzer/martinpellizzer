Jasminum nobile subsp. rex

Jasminum nobile subsp.

rex, commonly known as the Royal Jasmine, is a flowering plant native to China. It is characterized by its fragrant, white to yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months.

The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy, providing a striking contrast to its delicate flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for gardens and containers.

This subspecies is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in landscaping and as a decorative plant.
