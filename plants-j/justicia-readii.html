Justicia readii

Justicia readii, commonly known as red chalcedony or red hot poker, is a striking perennial plant native to Mexico.

It features showy, tubular red flowers that bloom in spikes during the summer months. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are arranged oppositely along the stem. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
