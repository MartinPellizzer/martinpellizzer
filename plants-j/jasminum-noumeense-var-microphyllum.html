Jasminum noumeense var. microphyllum

Jasminum noumeense var. microphyllum, commonly known as the New Caledonia dwarf jasmine, is a flowering plant native to the island of New Caledonia.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has slender, woody stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in coastal rainforests.

Due to its limited distribution and unique adaptations, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
