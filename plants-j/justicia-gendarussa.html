Justicia gendarussa

Justicia gendarussa, commonly known as the Java flame or flame of the forest, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red or orange tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a rough texture, while its stems are woody and branched.

Justicia gendarussa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
