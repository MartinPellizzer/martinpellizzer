Justicia robinsonii

Justicia robinsonii, commonly known as the Robinson's flame flower, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular, red or orange flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 feet. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are clustered in terminal spikes.
