Justicia quadrangularis

Justicia quadrangularis, commonly known as the square stem plant or red root, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its square-shaped stems, which are often covered in fine hairs, and its opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and showy blooms.

Justicia quadrangularis is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
