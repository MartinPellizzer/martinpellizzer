Jasminum grandiflorum var. plenum

Jasminum grandiflorum var. plenum, commonly known as the full-flowered jasmine, is a hybrid variety of the common jasmine.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and an abundance of fragrant, white flowers that bloom profusely throughout the growing season. The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This variety is often used in ornamental gardening for its pleasant fragrance and visual appeal.
