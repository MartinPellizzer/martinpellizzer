Jasminum yuanjiangense

Jasminum yuanjiangense, commonly known as the Yunnan jasmine, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Oleaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate, with a smooth surface and a slightly glossy texture.

The flowers are often used in traditional Chinese medicine and are also appreciated for their ornamental value.
