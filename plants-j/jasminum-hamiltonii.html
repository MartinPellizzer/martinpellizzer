Jasminum hamiltonii

Jasminum hamiltonii, commonly known as the Chinese jasmine, is a flowering plant native to China and Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub that produces fragrant white flowers, which bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for both garden cultivation and indoor environments.

Jasminum hamiltonii is valued for its ornamental beauty and aromatic properties.
