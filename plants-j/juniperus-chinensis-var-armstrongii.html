Juniperus chinensis var. armstrongii

Juniperus chinensis var. armstrongii, commonly known as the Armstrong's Chinese juniper, is a compact, slow-growing evergreen shrub or small tree native to China.

It features dense, scale-like foliage that is typically blue-green in color, providing a striking contrast to its dark, fissured bark. The plant produces small, berry-like cones that are often used in ornamental gardening due to their ornamental value. It is well-suited for rock gardens and dry, well-drained soils, thriving in full sun.

This variety is prized for its hardiness and unique appearance, making it a popular choice in horticultural settings.
