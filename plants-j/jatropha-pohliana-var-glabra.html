Jatropha pohliana var. glabra

Jatropha pohliana var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth jatropha, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous stem and leaves that are typically alternate and ovate in shape. The plant produces yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by capsule fruits containing toxic seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Due to its toxicity, it is important to handle the plant with care.
