Justicia ruiziana

Justicia ruiziana, commonly known as the Mexican verbena, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking spikes of tubular flowers that range in color from violet to red. Justicia ruiziana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms and drought tolerance.
