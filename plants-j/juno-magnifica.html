Juno magnifica

Juno magnifica, commonly known as the royal magnolia, is a striking evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 12 inches in diameter, typically in shades of white, pink, or purple. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil and partial to full sun.

Juno magnifica is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to add a tropical flair to gardens.
