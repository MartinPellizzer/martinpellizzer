Joosia umbellifera var. caucana

Joosia umbellifera var. caucana, commonly known as the Caucasian joosia, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus.

It features a basal rosette of lanceolate, hairy leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces small, white, umbellate inflorescences, which are characteristic of the Apiaceae family. Its flowers are hermaphroditic and are pollinated by insects.
