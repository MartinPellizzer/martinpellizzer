Justicia luschnathii

Justicia luschnathii, commonly known as Luschnath's false dragonhead, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with hairy surfaces and produces small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges and stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners interested in native plants.
