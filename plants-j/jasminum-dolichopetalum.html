Jasminum dolichopetalum

Jasminum dolichopetalum, commonly known as the long-petaled jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has slender, woody stems and opposite, simple, ovate leaves. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in shady, moist environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and as a houseplant.
