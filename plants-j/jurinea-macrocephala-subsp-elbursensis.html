Jurinea macrocephala subsp. elbursensis

Jurinea macrocephala subsp.

elbursensis, commonly known as the Elburs mountain sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Iran. It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its compact rosette of silver-green leaves and small, white or pale pink flowers.

The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils and on slopes. It is adapted to withstand harsh climatic conditions, including high UV exposure and limited water availability.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies in its native habitat.
