Jasminum promunturianum

Jasminum promunturianum, commonly known as the Promontory jasmine, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal dunes and rocky outcrops.
