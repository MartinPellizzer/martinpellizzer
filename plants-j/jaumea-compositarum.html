Jaumea compositarum

Jaumea compositarum, commonly known as the clustered spurge, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its upright, branching stems and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces latex, which is a common feature in the Euphorbiaceae family. It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Jaumea compositarum is a hardy plant that thrives in full sun and is relatively drought-tolerant.
