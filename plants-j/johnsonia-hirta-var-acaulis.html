Johnsonia hirta var. acaulis

Johnsonia hirta var. acaulis, commonly known as the slender Johnsonia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and dense clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests or along stream banks. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant blooms.
