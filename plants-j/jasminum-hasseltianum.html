Jasminum hasseltianum

Jasminum hasseltianum, commonly known as the Cape jasmine, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens in warm climates.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its pleasant fragrance and compact growth habit.
