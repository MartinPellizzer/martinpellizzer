Juno cycloglossa

Juno cycloglossa, commonly known as the cycloglossa orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom in clusters along the stem. Juno cycloglossa thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both ornamental gardening and botanical study.
