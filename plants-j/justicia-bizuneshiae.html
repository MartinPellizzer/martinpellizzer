Justicia bizuneshiae

Justicia bizuneshiae, commonly known as the Ethiopian flame flower, is a striking plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red bracts that resemble flames. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowering period occurs during the dry season, producing clusters of small, tubular flowers.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
