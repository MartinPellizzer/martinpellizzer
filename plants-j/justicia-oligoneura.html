Justicia oligoneura

Justicia oligoneura, commonly known as the silverleaf justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped, silver-velvet leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink or purple, depending on the variety. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Justicia oligoneura is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in warm climates.
