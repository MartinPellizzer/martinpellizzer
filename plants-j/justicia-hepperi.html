Justicia hepperi

Justicia hepperi, commonly known as the Christmas Justicia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet tubular flowers that bloom in clusters during the winter season. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and compact growth habit.
