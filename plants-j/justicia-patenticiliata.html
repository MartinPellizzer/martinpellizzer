Justicia patenticiliata

Justicia patenticiliata, commonly known as the showy justicia, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb, often reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered with fine hairs, while its stems are square and branching.

The flowers are attractively arranged in spike-like clusters, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
