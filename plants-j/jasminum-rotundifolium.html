Jasminum rotundifolium

Jasminum rotundifolium, commonly known as the round-leaf jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, rounded leaves and fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is typically shrub-like, with woody stems and can grow up to several meters in height. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments.

Its flowers are used in traditional medicine and as a source of aromatic oils.
