Justicia rauhii

Justicia rauhii, commonly known as the red-veined justicia, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves with deep red veins. The plant produces spikes of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, attracting pollinators like bees and hummingbirds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens in warm climates.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant foliage and long blooming period.
