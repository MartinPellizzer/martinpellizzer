Justicia insolita var. tastensis

Justicia insolita var. tastensis, commonly known as the Tastens Island justicia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from deep red to orange, often attracting hummingbirds and other pollinators. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters, with lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is often cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
