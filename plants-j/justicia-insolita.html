Justicia insolita

Justicia insolita, commonly known as the curious jewel or the spider flower, is a tropical perennial shrub native to Central and South America.

It features striking, showy flowers that are typically blue or purple, with a distinctive spiky center resembling a spider's web. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens in warm climates.

Its unique appearance and vibrant blooms make it a standout ornamental plant.
