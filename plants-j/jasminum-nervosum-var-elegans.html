Jasminum nervosum var. elegans

Jasminum nervosum var. elegans, commonly known as the Elegant Jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer seasons. The plant has slender, twining stems and heart-shaped leaves with a glossy surface. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant aroma.
