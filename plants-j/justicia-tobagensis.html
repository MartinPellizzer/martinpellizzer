Justicia tobagensis

Justicia tobagensis, commonly known as the Togian flame flower, is a striking tropical plant native to the Togian Islands in Indonesia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. The plant is renowned for its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a contrasting yellow or white throat. These flowers are borne in clusters at the ends of the branches, creating a dramatic display.

Justicia tobagensis thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
