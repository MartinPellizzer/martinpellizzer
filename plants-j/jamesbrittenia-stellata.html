Jamesbrittenia stellata

Jamesbrittenia stellata, commonly known as the star-flowered Jamesbrittenia, is a striking member of the Myrtaceae family native to Australia.

Its flowering period occurs during the warmer months, and it thrives in well-drained soils in full sun.
