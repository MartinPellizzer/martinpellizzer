Juniperus oxycedrus f. yaltirikiana

Juniperus oxycedrus f. yaltirikiana, commonly known as the Yaltirikiana juniper, is a shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage composed of scale-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, berry-like cones that are typically dark blue or black when mature. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact form and aromatic foliage.
