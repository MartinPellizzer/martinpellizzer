Jurinea elegantissima

Jurinea elegantissima, commonly known as the elegant sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arid and coastal regions of Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its delicate, grass-like foliage and small, clustered flowers. Jurinea elegantissima is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in harsh environments.
