Justicia australis

Justicia australis, commonly known as the Australian flame flower, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red or orange tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are clustered in terminal inflorescences.

Justicia australis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking blooms.
