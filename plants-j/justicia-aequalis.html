Justicia aequalis

Justicia aequalis, commonly known as the flame flower or red flame plant, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular red flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Justicia aequalis is often used in ornamental gardening for its vibrant floral display.
