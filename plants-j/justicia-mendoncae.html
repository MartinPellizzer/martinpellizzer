Justicia mendoncae

Justicia mendoncae, commonly known as Mexican flame flower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Justicia mendoncae thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for tropical gardens.
