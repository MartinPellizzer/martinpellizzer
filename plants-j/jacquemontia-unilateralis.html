Jacquemontia unilateralis

Jacquemontia unilateralis, commonly known as the one-sided morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped blue to violet flowers that bloom in the early morning. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland areas.

Jacquemontia unilateralis is a herbaceous perennial that is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance.
