Jatropha clutioides

Jatropha clutioides, commonly known as the African milkweed, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are usually elliptical to ovate in shape. The plant produces yellow flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by capsule fruits containing toxic seeds. It is adapted to dry conditions and can thrive in poor soils, making it a hardy species.

The plant contains latex, which is milky and can cause skin irritation.
