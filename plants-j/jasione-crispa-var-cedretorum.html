Jasione crispa var. cedretorum

Jasione crispa var. cedretorum, commonly known as the Cedretum Jasione, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features delicate, blue to violet flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to July. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often hairy, and it grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes. It is part of the Caryophyllaceae family, which includes other flowering plants with showy blooms.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal in garden settings.
