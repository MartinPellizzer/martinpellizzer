Justicia petterssonii

Justicia petterssonii, commonly known as the Petersen's glorybower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of tubular, two-lipped flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow. Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections for its vibrant blooms.
