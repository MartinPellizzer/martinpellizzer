Jasminum cinnamomifolium

Jasminum cinnamomifolium, commonly known as cinnamon leaf jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant white flowers and aromatic, cinnamon-scented leaves. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are elliptical, leathery, and have a distinctive cinnamon-like aroma when crushed.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade.
