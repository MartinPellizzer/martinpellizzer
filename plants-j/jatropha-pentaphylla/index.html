Jatropha Pentaphylla

Jatropha pentaphylla is a striking member of the Euphorbiaceae family known for its vibrant, colorful flowers. Native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia, this plant thrives in warm, humid environments. Its unique structure features a short stem with multiple leaf clusters and clusters of bright yellow or orange blooms. Often cultivated for ornamental purposes, it adds a splash of color to gardens and landscapes. Despite its beauty, it can be toxic if ingested, making it important to handle with care.
