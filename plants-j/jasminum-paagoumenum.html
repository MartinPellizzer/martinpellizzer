Jasminum paagoumenum

Jasminum paagoumenum, commonly known as the Arabian jasmine, is a flowering plant native to the Arabian Peninsula.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are simple, opposite, and have a glossy, dark green appearance. The plant produces small, elliptical fruits that contain seeds.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
