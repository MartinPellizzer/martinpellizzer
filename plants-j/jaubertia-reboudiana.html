Jaubertia reboudiana

Jaubertia reboudiana, commonly known as the golden candelabra, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant features small, elliptical leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant color and compact form.
