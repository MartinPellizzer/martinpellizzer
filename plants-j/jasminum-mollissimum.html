Jasminum mollissimum

Jasminum mollissimum, commonly known as the velvet jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves and a trailing growth habit, making it suitable for hanging baskets or trellises. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its sweet, intoxicating fragrance.
