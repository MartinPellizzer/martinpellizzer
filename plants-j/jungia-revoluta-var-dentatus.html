Jungia revoluta var. dentatus

Jungia revoluta var. dentatus, commonly known as the serrated jungle grass, is a perennial herb native to tropical regions of South America.

It features narrow, elongated leaves with serrated edges, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid forests.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique addition to botanical gardens and tropical landscapes.
