Justicia silvestris

Justicia silvestris, commonly known as the wild justicia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking tubular flowers that range in color from red to purple. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb, often reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers are clustered in whorled inflorescences.

Justicia silvestris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
