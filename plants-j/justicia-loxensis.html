Justicia loxensis

Justicia loxensis, commonly known as the redflower justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

Justicia loxensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
