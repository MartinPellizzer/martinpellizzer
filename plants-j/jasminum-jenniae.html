Jasminum jenniae

Jasminum jenniae, commonly known as the Chinese jasmine, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and elliptical, with a smooth texture.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
