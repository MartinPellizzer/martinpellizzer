Jasminum maingayi var. kedahense

Jasminum maingayi var. kedahense, commonly known as Kedah jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height, characterized by its fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate, with a glossy green surface. It thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.

The plant is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
