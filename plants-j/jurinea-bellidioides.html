Jurinea bellidioides

Jurinea bellidioides, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that thrives in sandy or rocky coastal environments. The plant features narrow, blue-green leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a globular inflorescence. It is well adapted to saline and arid conditions, making it a hardy species often found in coastal dunes.

Its ecological importance includes stabilizing dunes and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
