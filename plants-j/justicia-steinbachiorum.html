Justicia steinbachiorum

Justicia steinbachiorum, commonly known as the desert jewel or rainbow plant, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, yellow, and orange. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that help it store water, adapting it to its dry environment. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a valuable addition to gardens in hot, dry climates.
