Justicia uber

Justicia uber, commonly known as the silverleaf justicia, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet, featuring lance-shaped leaves with a silvery-gray underside. The plant produces striking clusters of tubular, violet-blue flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory properties.

The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.
