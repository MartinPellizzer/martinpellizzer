Juncus togakushiensis

Juncus togakushiensis, commonly known as the Togakushi rush, is a rare flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Juncaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountainous regions and forest understories. Its flowers are usually pale green or white, and it produces small, three-lobed seeds.
