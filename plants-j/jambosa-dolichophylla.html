Jambosa dolichophylla

Jambosa dolichophylla, commonly known as the long-leaf jambosa, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by edible fruits resembling small, round berries. Its botanical features make it well-suited for humid, shaded environments.

It is often cultivated for both ornamental and culinary purposes.
