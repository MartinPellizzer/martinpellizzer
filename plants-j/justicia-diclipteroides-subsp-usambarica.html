Justicia diclipteroides subsp. usambarica

Justicia diclipteroides subsp.

usambarica, commonly known as Usambaran flameflower, is a flowering plant native to the Usambara Mountains in Tanzania. It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats and has lance-shaped leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.

The subspecies is distinguished by its slightly different leaf morphology and flower structure compared to other members of the species.
