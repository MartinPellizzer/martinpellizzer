Jacquemontia coelestis

Jacquemontia coelestis, commonly known as sky blue morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a vine that produces showy, blue-violet flowers that bloom in the morning and close in the afternoon. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often found in moist, sandy soils along stream banks and in open woods.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening due to its striking floral display.
