Justicia magniflora

Justicia magniflora, commonly known as the flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators like hummingbirds and bees. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Justicia magniflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
