Julocroton velutinus

Julocroton velutinus, commonly known as the velvet croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers followed by distinctive orange to red berries. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in forested areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation.
