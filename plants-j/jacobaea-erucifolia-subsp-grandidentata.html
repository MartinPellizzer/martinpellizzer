Jacobaea erucifolia subsp. grandidentata

Jacobaea erucifolia subsp.

grandidentata, commonly known as the grandidentate ragwort, is a flowering plant native to North America. It is characterized by its yellowish-green flowers and robust, hairy stems.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped with a distinctive, serrated margin.
