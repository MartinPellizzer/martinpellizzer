Justicia mediocris

Justicia mediocris, commonly known as the common justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Justicia mediocris thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
