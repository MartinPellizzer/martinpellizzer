Justicia alexandri

Justicia alexandri, commonly known as the Alexander's flame flower, is a striking tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red-orange tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators like hummingbirds and bees. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Justicia alexandri is often cultivated for its showy blooms and is relatively easy to care for in warm climates.
