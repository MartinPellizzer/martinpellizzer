Justicia antsingensis

Justicia antsingensis, commonly known as the Madagascar flame flower, is a striking tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring lance-shaped leaves with a smooth, waxy texture. The plant produces vibrant red or orange tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and greenhouses in warm climates.
