Jasminum ligustrinum

Jasminum ligustrinum, commonly known as the Chinese jasmine, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has slender, woody stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Jasminum ligustrinum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.
