Justicia chinensis

Justicia chinensis, commonly known as Chinese wheelcreeper, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking spikes of bright red or orange tubular flowers. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

The flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, making it a valuable addition to gardens.
