Justicia arida

Justicia arida, commonly known as the desert verbena, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant has gray-green, narrow leaves and produces showy clusters of blue to purple flowers. It is adapted to survive in dry conditions with minimal water requirements.

Its botanical features make it well-suited for xeriscaping and landscaping in drought-prone areas.
