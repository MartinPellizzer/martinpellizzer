Jurtsevia richardsonii

Jurtsevia richardsonii, commonly known as the Richardson's jurtsevia, is a flowering plant native to the Arctic regions of North America.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of less than 10 centimeters. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Jurtsevia richardsonii is adapted to cold climates and is often found in tundra and alpine environments.
