Justicia Ramosa

Red trumpet, scientifically known as Justicia ramosa, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia. This species is prized for its vibrant, trumpet-shaped blooms that range from red to orange and yellow. Often cultivated in gardens and as ornamental plants, it thrives in warm climates with ample sunlight. Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for both landscape design and indoor gardening. With its bold colors and easy care requirements, Justicia ramosa adds a touch of exotic beauty to any setting.
