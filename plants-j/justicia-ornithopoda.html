Justicia ornithopoda

Justicia ornithopoda, commonly known as the bird's beak justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple, often resembling a bird's beak, hence its common name. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a rough texture.

Justicia ornithopoda is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
