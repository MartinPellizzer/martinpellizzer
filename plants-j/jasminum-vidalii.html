Jasminum vidalii

Jasminum vidalii, commonly known as the Vidal's jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a glossy green surface.

Jasminum vidalii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its pleasant aroma.
