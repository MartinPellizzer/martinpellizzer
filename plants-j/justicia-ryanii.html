Justicia ryanii

Justicia ryanii, commonly known as the red-flowered justicia, is a striking tropical plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.

Its unique floral structure attracts pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees.
