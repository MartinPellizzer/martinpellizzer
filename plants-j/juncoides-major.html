Juncoides major

Juncoides major, commonly known as the common rush, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Juncaceae.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with slender, cylindrical stems that are often hollow. The plant typically grows in moist environments such as wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of ponds. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny flowers.

Juncoides major plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic and terrestrial organisms.
