Justicia dallarii

Justicia dallarii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in shades of purple, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often used as ornamental foliage. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial shade to full sun, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ability to bloom year-round adds to its appeal as a decorative plant.
