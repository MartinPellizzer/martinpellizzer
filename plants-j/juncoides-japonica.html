Juncoides japonica

Juncoides japonica, commonly known as the Japanese rush, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Juncaceae and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with cylindrical, hollow stems. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish or brownish in color. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as forest floors and wetlands.

Juncoides japonica is often used in ornamental gardening due to its elegant appearance and adaptability.
