Jatropha matacensis

Jatropha matacensis, commonly known as the Matacense Croton, is a tropical shrub native to Central America.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often toxic species. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate or elliptical in shape. It produces small, clustered yellow flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.
