Jordaaniella spongiosa

Jordaaniella spongiosa, commonly known as the spongey jordaaniella, is a unique succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a compact, cushion-like growth form with dense, fleshy leaves that are typically green or reddish in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. Its leaves are adapted to store water, making it well-suited for arid environments.

It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements.
