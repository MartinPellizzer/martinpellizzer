Jambosa diospyrifolia

Jambosa diospyrifolia, commonly known as the Natal plum, is a small deciduous tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and alternate, simple leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to tropical and subtropical climates.
