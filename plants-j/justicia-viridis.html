Justicia viridis

Justicia viridis, commonly known as green flame or green jewel, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a member of the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant green foliage and striking red or orange tubular flowers. Justicia viridis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive appearance.
