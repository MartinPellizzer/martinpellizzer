Jupunba microcalyx var. microcalyx

Jupunba microcalyx var. microcalyx, commonly known as the narrow-leaved jupunba, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem, giving it a delicate appearance.
