Jasminum laurifolium f. nitidum

Jasminum laurifolium f. nitidum, commonly known as the glossy-leafed jasmine, is a cultivar of the jasmine plant characterized by its dense, dark green foliage.

It produces fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. The leaves are typically glossy and oval-shaped, providing an attractive appearance in both indoor and outdoor settings. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright light for optimal growth.

It is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal and pleasant fragrance.
