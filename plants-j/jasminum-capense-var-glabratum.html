Jasminum capense var. glabratum

Jasminum capense var. glabratum, commonly known as the Cape jasmine, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the winter months. Its leaves are usually ovate to lanceolate in shape, with a smooth texture.

The plant is well adapted to coastal environments and thrives in well-drained soils.
