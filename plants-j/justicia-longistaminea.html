Justicia longistaminea

Justicia longistaminea, commonly known as the long-stamen justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces showy bracts that often resemble petals. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The long, slender stamens give it its common name and contribute to its distinctive appearance.
