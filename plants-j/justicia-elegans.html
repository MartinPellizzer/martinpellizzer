Justicia elegans

Justicia elegans, commonly known as the purple flame or red flame plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular, violet to red flowers. The plant produces showy bracts that resemble petals, enhancing its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Justicia elegans is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and is used in landscaping and floral arrangements.
