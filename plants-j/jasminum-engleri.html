Jasminum engleri

Jasminum engleri, commonly known as the Philippine jasmine, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is a shrub that typically grows up to 2 meters in height, featuring aromatic white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are ovate to elliptical in shape. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in coastal areas and lowland forests.

The flowers are used in traditional medicine and for ornamental purposes due to their fragrance and beauty.
