Justicia filibracteolata var. filibracteolata

Justicia filibracteolata var. filibracteolata, commonly known as the red flame plant, is a flowering shrub native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It features striking red to orange tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for tropical gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and vibrant color.
