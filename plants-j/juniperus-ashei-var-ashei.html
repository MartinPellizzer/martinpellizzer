Juniperus ashei var. ashei

Juniperus ashei var. ashei, commonly known as the Ashe juniper, is a shrub or small tree native to the Southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, scale-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces blue-black berries that are an important food source for various wildlife species. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in limestone hills and rocky areas.

Juniperus ashei var. ashei is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
