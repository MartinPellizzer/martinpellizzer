Jurinea perula-orientis

Jurinea perula-orientis, commonly known as the oriental sea holly, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean and the Black Sea, where it thrives in saline and sandy soils. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for coastal gardens and landscaping.
