Jasminum goetzeanum

Jasminum goetzeanum, commonly known as the Goetze's jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are elliptical in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its flowering period typically occurs during the summer months.
