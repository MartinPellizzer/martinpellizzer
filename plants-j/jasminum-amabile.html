Jasminum amabile

Jasminum amabile, commonly known as the sweet jasmine or Chinese jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has opposite, simple, and usually lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.

Jasminum amabile is a woody shrub that can grow up to several meters in height, making it suitable for hedges or as a decorative plant in gardens.
