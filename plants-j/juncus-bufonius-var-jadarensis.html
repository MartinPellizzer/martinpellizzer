Juncus bufonius var. jadarensis

Juncus bufonius var. jadarensis, commonly known as the Jadarensis rush, is a perennial grass-like plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It features slender, cylindrical stems that are typically green to reddish in color, often growing in dense clusters. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in moist to wet environments, such as marshes and swampy areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species in wetland ecosystems.
