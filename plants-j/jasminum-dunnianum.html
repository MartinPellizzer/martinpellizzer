Jasminum dunnianum

Jasminum dunnianum, commonly known as the Chinese jasmine, is a flowering plant native to China and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and lance-shaped, with a glossy green surface.

The flowers are often used in traditional Chinese medicine and for ornamental purposes due to their pleasant scent.
