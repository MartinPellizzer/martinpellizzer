Justicia kurzii

Justicia kurzii, commonly known as the golden trumpet bush, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow-orange tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.
