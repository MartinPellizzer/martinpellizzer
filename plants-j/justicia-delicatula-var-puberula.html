Justicia delicatula var. puberula

Justicia delicatula var. puberula, commonly known as the velvetleaf justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its velvety, pubescent leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often found in disturbed soils or along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes in suitable climates.
