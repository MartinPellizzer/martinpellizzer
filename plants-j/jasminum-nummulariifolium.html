Jasminum nummulariifolium

Jasminum nummulariifolium, commonly known as the silver leaf jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It is typically shrub-like in growth habit and prefers well-drained soil and partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
