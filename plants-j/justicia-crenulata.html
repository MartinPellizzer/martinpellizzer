Justicia crenulata

Justicia crenulata, commonly known as the silver bush or Mexican flame flower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters tall, characterized by its lance-shaped, silver-green leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow, which bloom in clusters at the ends of branches. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Justicia crenulata is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and vibrant floral display.
