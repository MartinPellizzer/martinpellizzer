Justicia lanstyakii

Justicia lanstyakii, commonly known as the red-flowered justicia, is a striking plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of bright red tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its flowering period usually occurs during the warmer months, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
