Justicia syringifolia

Justicia syringifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage and clusters of tubular, violet or blue flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its rapid growth and ability to tolerate various soil conditions contribute to its adaptability in different environments.
