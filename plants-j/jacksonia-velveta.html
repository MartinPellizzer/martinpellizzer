Jacksonia velveta

Jacksonia velveta, commonly known as velvet bush, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the daisy family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its soft, velvety foliage. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It typically grows in coastal areas and thrives in well-drained, sandy soils.

Jacksonia velveta is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
