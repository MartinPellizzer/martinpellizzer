Joniris fragrans

Joniris fragrans, commonly known as the sweet fragrance plant, is a flowering species native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its delicate, aromatic flowers that bloom in clusters, often emitting a sweet, honey-like scent. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are broad and glossy, providing a lush backdrop to the fragrant blooms.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
