Jumellea lignosa subsp. latilabia

Jumellea lignosa subsp.

latilabia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved jumellea, is a shrub native to southern Africa. It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

The plant has small, narrow leaves that are usually arranged in opposite pairs along the stems. Its flowers are small and white, often clustered in inflorescences at the base of the leaves.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature.
