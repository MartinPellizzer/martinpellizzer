Justicia glabribracteata

Justicia glabribracteata, commonly known as the silky bract justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant features smooth, lance-shaped leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
