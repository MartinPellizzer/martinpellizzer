Justicia mexicana

Justicia mexicana, commonly known as Mexican flame flower, is a striking plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is a member of the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are usually green and slightly hairy. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Justicia mexicana is also known for its ability to attract hummingbirds and other pollinators.
