Jovibarba hirta var. tatrensis

Jovibarba hirta var. tatrensis, commonly known as the Tatran moss rose, is a succulent plant native to the alpine regions of Central Europe.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, rosette-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, woolly layer. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine displays.
