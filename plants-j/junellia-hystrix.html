Junellia hystrix

Junellia hystrix, commonly known as the spiny junellia, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive spiny stems and leaves, which provide a unique defense mechanism against herbivores. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, adding to its ornamental appeal. Junellia hystrix thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in forest understories or along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both ecological studies and horticultural cultivation.
