Jambosa decoriflora

Jambosa decoriflora, commonly known as the ornamental jambosa, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of pink or white. The plant has a woody stem with a smooth, grayish bark, and its leaves are typically ovate with a glossy surface. Jambosa decoriflora thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish.

It is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and as a decorative plant.
