Juncus × montserratensis

Juncus × montserratensis, commonly known as the Montserrat rush, is a hybrid plant species native to the Caribbean island of Montserrat.

It is a member of the Juncaceae family and is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in wetland environments, such as marshes and along the edges of ponds. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear greenish-white flowers, which are wind-pollinated.

Due to its restricted habitat, Juncus × montserratensis is considered a rare and endemic species.
