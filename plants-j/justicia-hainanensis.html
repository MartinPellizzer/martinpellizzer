Justicia hainanensis

Justicia hainanensis, commonly known as Hainan flame flower, is a flowering plant native to southern China.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers. The plant typically grows as an erect shrub with lance-shaped leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal areas or near streams.

The flowers are pollinated by birds and insects, making it an important species in local ecosystems.
