Justicia fusagasugana

Justicia fusagasugana, commonly known as the Andean flame flower, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
