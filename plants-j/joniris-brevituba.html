Joniris brevituba

Joniris brevituba, commonly known as the short-stemmed orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, Joniris brevituba is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
