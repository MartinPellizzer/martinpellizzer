Jasminum trangense

Jasminum trangense, commonly known as Trang jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand.

It belongs to the Oleaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, white to yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a smooth texture with a slightly serrated margin.

The flowers are often used in traditional medicine and as a source of essential oils due to their aromatic properties.
