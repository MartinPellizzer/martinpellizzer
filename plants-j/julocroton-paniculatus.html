Julocroton paniculatus

Julocroton paniculatus, commonly known as the panicled croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves with a vibrant green color and a rough texture. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers that develop into woody, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
