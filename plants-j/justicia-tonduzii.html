Justicia tonduzii

Justicia tonduzii, commonly known as the Tonduzii flame flower, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range from red to orange in color. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

Justicia tonduzii thrives in full sun to partial shade and is drought-tolerant once established.
