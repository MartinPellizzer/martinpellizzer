Javorkaea megalantha

Javorkaea megalantha, commonly known as the giant leaf orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, lance-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a distinctive yellow or green throat. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts for its beauty and unique foliage.
