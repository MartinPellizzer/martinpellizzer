Justicia oreophila

Justicia oreophila, commonly known as the mountain justicia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America and northern South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking spikes of tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and is often found in cloud forests. Its foliage consists of opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Justicia oreophila is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
