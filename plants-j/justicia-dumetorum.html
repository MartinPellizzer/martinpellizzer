Justicia dumetorum

Justicia dumetorum, commonly known as the prostrate flame flower, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It features sprawling stems that often root at the nodes, allowing it to form dense mats or ground covers. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow, which bloom in clusters at the ends of the stems. Its leaves are opposite, ovate to lanceolate, and typically have a glossy surface.

Justicia dumetorum thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
