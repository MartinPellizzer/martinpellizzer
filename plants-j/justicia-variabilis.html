Justicia variabilis

Justicia variabilis, commonly known as the red hot poker, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular red bracts that surround the small, yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or perennial, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Justicia variabilis thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
