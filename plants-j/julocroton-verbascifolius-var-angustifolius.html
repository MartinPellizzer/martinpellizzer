Julocroton verbascifolius var. angustifolius

Julocroton verbascifolius var. angustifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features large, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs, giving them a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers that are followed by bright orange or red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for tropical and subtropical climates.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant foliage and showy fruiting display.
