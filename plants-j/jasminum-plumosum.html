Jasminum plumosum

Jasminum plumosum, commonly known as the silky jasmine, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. The plant has slender, upright stems covered in soft, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a waxy texture.

Jasminum plumosum is typically cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its pleasant fragrance and graceful growth habit.
