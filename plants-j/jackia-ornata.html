Jackia ornata

Jackia ornata, commonly known as the silver fern, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive silver-green foliage. The plant produces small, yellow flower heads that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Jackia ornata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native pollinators.
