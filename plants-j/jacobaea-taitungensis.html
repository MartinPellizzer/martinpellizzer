Jacobaea taitungensis

Jacobaea taitungensis, commonly known as Taitung ragwort, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow-orange composite flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, bracted flower heads.
