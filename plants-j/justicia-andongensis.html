Justicia andongensis

Justicia andongensis, commonly known as the Andong flameflower, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas and has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.

The species is also noted for its ecological importance in supporting local pollinators.
