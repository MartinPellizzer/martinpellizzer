Juniperus × fassettii

Juniperus × fassettii, commonly known as the Arizona cypress, is a hybrid coniferous evergreen tree native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its narrow, pyramidal shape and scale-like leaves that are blue-green in color. The plant produces small, berry-like cones that are typically blue or purple when mature. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

The botanical features of Juniperus × fassettii make it well-suited for arid and semi-arid environments.
