Jacobaea × liechtensteinensis

Jacobaea × liechtensteinensis, commonly known as the Liechtenstein ragwort, is a hybrid plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its yellow to orange-red flower heads and is native to the Alpine regions of Europe. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes. It has lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Due to its toxic properties, it is considered a weed in some areas and is not widely cultivated.
