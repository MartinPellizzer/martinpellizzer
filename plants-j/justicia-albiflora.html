Justicia albiflora

Justicia albiflora, commonly known as the white flame bush, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking white to pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its rapid growth and showy blooms make it a valuable addition to landscapes and gardens.
