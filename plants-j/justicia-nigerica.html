Justicia nigerica

Justicia nigerica, commonly known as the black beauty, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking dark purple to black flowers. The plant typically grows as an herb or shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

The flowers are clustered in whorled inflorescences and are pollinated primarily by birds and insects.
