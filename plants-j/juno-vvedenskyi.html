Juno vvedenskyi

Juno vvedenskyi, commonly known as the Vvedensky juniper, is a species of juniper native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils at high altitudes. The plant has needle-like leaves that are blue-green in color and produce small, dark blue berries that are edible. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.

Juno vvedenskyi is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
