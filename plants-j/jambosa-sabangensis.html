Jambosa sabangensis

Jambosa sabangensis, commonly known as the Sabang mangosteen, is a tropical fruit-bearing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the genus Jambosa within the family Anacardiaceae, which includes other economically important species like the mango and cashew. The plant is characterized by its evergreen canopy, with glossy dark green leaves and clusters of fragrant white flowers.
