Juncus gerardi var. atrofuscus

Juncus gerardi var. atrofuscus, commonly known as blackstem rush, is a perennial plant native to North America.

It typically grows in wet or moist habitats such as marshes, swales, and along stream banks. The plant has slender, dark green to blackish stems that are often densely clustered. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, spike-like clusters of brownish flowers.

This variety is valued for its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for aquatic wildlife.
