Justicia neurantha

Justicia neurantha, commonly known as the blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking clusters of blue-violet flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Justicia neurantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
