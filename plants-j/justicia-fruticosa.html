Justicia fruticosa

Justicia fruticosa, commonly known as the flame bush, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, often resembling flames. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Justicia fruticosa is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
