Justicia chrysocoma

Justicia chrysocoma, commonly known as the golden flame or yellow flame, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and produces clusters of bright yellow flowers with two lips. The flowers are borne on erect, branching inflorescences and are pollinated by birds and insects.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
