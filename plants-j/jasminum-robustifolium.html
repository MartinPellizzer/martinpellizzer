Jasminum robustifolium

Jasminum robustifolium, commonly known as the robust leaf jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for tropical and subtropical climates.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its pleasant fragrance and evergreen foliage.
