Justicia dianthera var. pittieri

Justicia dianthera var. pittieri, commonly known as the Mexican flame flower, is a vibrant evergreen shrub native to Mexico.

It features striking, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators like hummingbirds and bees. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly rough texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and containers.
