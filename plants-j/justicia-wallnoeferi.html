Justicia wallnoeferi

Justicia wallnoeferi, commonly known as the velvetleaf justicia, is a perennial shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow, which attract pollinators like hummingbirds and bees. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes in warm climates.
