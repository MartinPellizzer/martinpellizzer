Jatropha bullockii

Jatropha bullockii, commonly known as the Indian physic or bulletwood, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and showy, yellow-orange flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain toxic seeds, which are used in traditional medicine. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in dry, arid conditions.
