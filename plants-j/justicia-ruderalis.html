Justicia ruderalis

Justicia ruderalis, commonly known as the common justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in disturbed soils, such as along roadsides and in abandoned fields. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of yellow to orange. It is known for its adaptability and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.

The genus Justicia is part of the Acanthaceae family, which includes many ornamental and medicinal plants.
