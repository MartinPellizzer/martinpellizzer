Justicia uncinulata var. cufodontii

Justicia uncinulata var. cufodontii, commonly known as the red-stemmed justicia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of showy, tubular red or orange flowers. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving the plant a textured appearance. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
