Jasminum harmandianum

Jasminum harmandianum, commonly known as the Harmand's jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 3 meters. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a smooth, glossy surface.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.
