Justicia carajensis

Justicia carajensis, commonly known as the red mistflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to pink. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

Justicia carajensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
