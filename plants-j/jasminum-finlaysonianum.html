Jasminum finlaysonianum

Jasminum finlaysonianum, commonly known as the Philippine jasmine, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a smooth texture with a slightly pointed tip.

The botanical characteristics of this species make it a popular ornamental plant in tropical gardens.
