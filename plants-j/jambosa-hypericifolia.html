Jambosa hypericifolia

Jambosa hypericifolia, commonly known as the African star fruit or Natal star fruit, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped fruits, which are typically yellow or orange when ripe. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often covered in fine hairs.

Jambosa hypericifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its edible fruits.
