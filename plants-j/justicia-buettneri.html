Justicia buettneri

Justicia buettneri, commonly known as the flame flower, is a striking tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or orange tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators like hummingbirds and bees. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are glossy and arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its unique appearance and ecological role make it a valuable addition to both ornamental and natural habitats.
