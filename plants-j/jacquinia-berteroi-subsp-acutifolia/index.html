Jacquinia Berteroi Subsp. Acutifolia

Jacquinia berteroi subsp. acutifolia is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Andes. It is known for its striking white petals and compact, evergreen foliage. This subspecies thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky soils. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival in the wild.
