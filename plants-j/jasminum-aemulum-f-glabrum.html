Jasminum aemulum f. glabrum

Jasminum aemulum f. glabrum, commonly known as the smooth jasmine, is a compact shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the warmer months. The plant has smooth, glabrous stems and leaves, which contribute to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires partial to full sun for optimal growth.

Due to its beauty and scent, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
