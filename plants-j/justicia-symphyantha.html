Justicia symphyantha

Justicia symphyantha, commonly known as the velvetleaf justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to red. Justicia symphyantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
