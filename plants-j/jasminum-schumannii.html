Jasminum schumannii

Jasminum schumannii, commonly known as the Schumann's jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a glossy texture.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments.
