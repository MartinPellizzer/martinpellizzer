Justicia vahlii var. rupicola

Justicia vahlii var. rupicola, commonly known as the rock justicia, is a flowering plant native to arid and rocky regions of southern Africa.

It features striking spikes of bright blue or violet flowers that bloom during the warmer months. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, helping to reduce water loss. It is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils and full sun exposure.

This variant is prized for its ornamental value and drought tolerance in landscaping.
