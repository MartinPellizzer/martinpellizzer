Justicia diclipteroides subsp. kibarianiensis

Justicia diclipteroides subsp.

kibarianiensis, commonly known as the Kibariya flame flower, is a shrub native to eastern Africa. It is characterized by its striking, tubular red flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as birds and insects.

The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a rough texture and a slightly serrated margin. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its botanical features make it a popular ornamental plant in tropical and subtropical gardens.
