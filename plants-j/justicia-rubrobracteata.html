Justicia rubrobracteata

Justicia rubrobracteata, commonly known as the red-bracted justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking red bracts that enclose its small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows as an annual herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered with fine hairs.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
