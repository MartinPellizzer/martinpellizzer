Jatropha aconitifolia var. palmata

Jatropha aconitifolia var. palmata, commonly known as the palmate jatropha, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its palmately lobed leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by bright red, berry-like fruits. It is often found in moist, shady habitats such as forests and along stream banks.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
