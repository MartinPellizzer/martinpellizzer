Justicia elegantula var. elatior

Justicia elegantula var. elatior, commonly known as the showy jewelweed, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has heart-shaped, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.
