Jurinea antoninae

Jurinea antoninae, commonly known as the Antonina's sea bladder, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its succulent, bladder-like structures that store water. Due to its habitat, it plays a role in stabilizing coastal dunes and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
