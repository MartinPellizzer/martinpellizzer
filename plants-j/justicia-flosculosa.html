Justicia flosculosa

Justicia flosculosa, commonly known as the yellow crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking yellow-orange inflorescence, which resembles a crown, and its lance-shaped, dark green leaves. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Justicia flosculosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
