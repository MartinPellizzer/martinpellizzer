Jasminum chrysanthemum

Jasminum chrysanthemum, commonly known as the garden jasmine, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia.

It produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has opposite, simple, and lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green in color. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.

However, it is important to note that it is not related to true chrysanthemums, which belong to the genus Chrysanthemum.
