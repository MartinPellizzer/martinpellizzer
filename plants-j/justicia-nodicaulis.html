Justicia nodicaulis

Justicia nodicaulis, commonly known as the small-leaved flamebeard, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has opposite, narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along streams.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and wetland restoration projects.
