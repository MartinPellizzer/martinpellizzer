Japonicalia

Japonicalia, commonly known as the Japanese orchid, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its showy, fragrant blooms. The plant typically features long, slender stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
