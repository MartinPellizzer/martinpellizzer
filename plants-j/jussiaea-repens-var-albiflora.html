Jussiaea repens var. albiflora

Jussiaea repens var. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered jussiaea, is a variant of the creeping jussiaea species.

It is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America, particularly in Brazil. The plant typically grows in wet, marshy habitats and is characterized by its low, spreading growth habit. Its flowers are small and white, often arranged in clusters, giving it a delicate appearance.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in moist environments.
