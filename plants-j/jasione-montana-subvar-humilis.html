Jasione montana subvar. humilis

Jasione montana subvar. humilis, commonly known as the slender bellflower, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that bear small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue or violet. The plant has basal leaves that are linear to lanceolate, often with hairy surfaces. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Jasione montana subvar. humilis is valued in gardens for its delicate appearance and ability to attract pollinators.
