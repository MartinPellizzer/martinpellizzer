Jussiaea suffruticosa var. stenosperma

Jussiaea suffruticosa var. stenosperma, commonly known as the narrow-fruited jussiaea, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of South America.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sunlight. The plant features small, elliptical leaves with a glossy surface and produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds, aiding in its dispersal.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
