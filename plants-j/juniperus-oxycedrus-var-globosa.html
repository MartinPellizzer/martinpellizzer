Juniperus oxycedrus var. globosa

Juniperus oxycedrus var. globosa, commonly known as the globular juniper, is a shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical growth habit and dark green, scale-like leaves. The plant produces small, blue-black berries that are often used in culinary and medicinal applications. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and resilience to harsh environmental conditions.
