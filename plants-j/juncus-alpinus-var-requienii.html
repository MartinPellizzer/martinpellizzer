Juncus alpinus var. requienii

Juncus alpinus var. requienii, commonly known as the Alpine rush, is a flowering plant belonging to the Juncaceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its dense, cylindrical clusters of grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or brown in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine environments.

This variety is notable for its adaptability to harsh mountain conditions and its role in stabilizing soil in rocky habitats.
