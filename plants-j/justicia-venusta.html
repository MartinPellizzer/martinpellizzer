Justicia venusta

Justicia venusta, commonly known as the flame bush, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as birds and bees. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Justicia venusta thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
