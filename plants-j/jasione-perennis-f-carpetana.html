Jasione perennis f. carpetana

Jasione perennis f. carpetana, commonly known as the carpet jasmine, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Europe.

It features dark green, narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a valuable addition to gardens.
