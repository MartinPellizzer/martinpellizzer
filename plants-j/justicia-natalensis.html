Justicia natalensis

Justicia natalensis, commonly known as Natal flame bush, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a reddish or purple underside.

Justicia natalensis thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.
