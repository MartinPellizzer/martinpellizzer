Justicia bolomboensis

Justicia bolomboensis, commonly known as the Malagasy flameflower, is a striking plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in a fine, velvety texture.

Justicia bolomboensis thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
