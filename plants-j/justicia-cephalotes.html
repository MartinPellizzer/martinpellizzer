Justicia cephalotes

Justicia cephalotes, commonly known as the crimson flame plant, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and a deep green color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
