Justicia warnockii

Justicia warnockii, commonly known as the Warnock's flame flower, is a striking tropical plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and thrives in warm, humid environments. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its eye-catching floral display.

However, it requires well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
