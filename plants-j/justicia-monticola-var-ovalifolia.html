Justicia monticola var. ovalifolia

Justicia monticola var. ovalifolia, commonly known as the oval-leaf flame bush, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The leaves are typically oval-shaped and arranged oppositely along the stems. This species thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
