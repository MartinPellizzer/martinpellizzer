Justicia candicans var. subglabra

Justicia candicans var. subglabra, commonly known as the smooth flame flower, is a tropical perennial shrub characterized by its striking tubular flowers.

It features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and glabrous, giving it its varietal name. The plant produces clusters of bright red to orange flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Native to Central and South America, it is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
