Justicia albicans

Justicia albicans, commonly known as white flame or white justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, white to pale pink tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

Justicia albicans thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
