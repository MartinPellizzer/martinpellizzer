Justicia brandisii

Justicia brandisii, commonly known as the African violet or velvet leaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant has dark green, velvety leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial shade.
