Juglans regia var. beloudschistana

Juglans regia var. beloudschistana, commonly known as the Belousov walnut, is a variant of the Persian walnut.

It is characterized by its large, thick-shelled nuts with a high oil content. The tree produces heart-shaped leaves with a serrated margin and grows to a height of up to 20 meters. It thrives in temperate climates and is native to parts of Central Asia.

This variety is valued for its nut quality and is often cultivated for both ornamental and commercial purposes.
