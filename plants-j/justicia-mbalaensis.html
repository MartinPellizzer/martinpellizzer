Justicia mbalaensis

Justicia mbalaensis, commonly known as the African verbena, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to pink. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Justicia mbalaensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
