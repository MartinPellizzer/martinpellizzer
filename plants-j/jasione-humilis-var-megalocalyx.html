Jasione humilis var. megalocalyx

Jasione humilis var. megalocalyx, commonly known as the large-flowered Jasione, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, hairy stems that often lie close to the ground, forming dense mats. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, typically blooming in early spring. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly serrated edge.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
