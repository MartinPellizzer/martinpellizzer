Jussiaea myrtifolia var. villosissima

Jussiaea myrtifolia var. villosissima, commonly known as the velvetleaf jussiaea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Onagraceae.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety pubescence covering its stems and leaves, which gives it its common name. Its small, white to pinkish flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom during the summer months.
