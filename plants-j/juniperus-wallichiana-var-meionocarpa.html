Juniperus wallichiana var. meionocarpa

Juniperus wallichiana var. meionocarpa, commonly known as the dwarf juniper, is a shrub native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and scale-like leaves that are typically blue-gray in color. The plant produces small, berry-like cones that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to cold, mountainous environments.

Due to its hardiness and ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
