Justicia phyllostachys

Justicia phyllostachys, commonly known as the flame bush, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting birds and insects. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in whorls around the stems. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Justicia phyllostachys is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in warm climates.
