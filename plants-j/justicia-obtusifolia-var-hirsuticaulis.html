Justicia obtusifolia var. hirsuticaulis

Justicia obtusifolia var. hirsuticaulis, commonly known as the woolly false dragonhead, is a perennial flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It features dense, woolly foliage that gives it its common name, and its lance-shaped leaves are often covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces showy spikes of tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

This ornamental plant is popular in gardens for its vibrant blooms and unique texture.
