Justicia ladanoides

Justicia ladanoides, commonly known as the fragrant justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub or subshrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture, while the flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences.

Justicia ladanoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms and pleasant fragrance.
