Jurinea demetrii

Jurinea demetrii, commonly known as the Demetrius's sea holly, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the coastal regions of the Mediterranean, particularly in southern Europe and North Africa. The plant is characterized by its narrow, silver-green leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in dune environments.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species.
