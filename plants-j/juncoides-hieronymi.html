Juncoides hieronymi

Juncoides hieronymi, commonly known as the Hieronymus' rush, is a member of the Juncaceae family.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its cylindrical, grass-like leaves and tall, flowering spikes. Native to specific regions in South America, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and taxonomic studies.
