Juniperus davurica

Juniperus davurica, commonly known as the Chinese juniper, is a coniferous evergreen shrub or small tree native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its scale-like leaves and small, berry-like cones that are typically blue or black in color. The plant is adapted to cold climates and can thrive in rocky, well-drained soils. It is often used in landscaping for its hardiness and ornamental value.

Juniperus davurica plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
