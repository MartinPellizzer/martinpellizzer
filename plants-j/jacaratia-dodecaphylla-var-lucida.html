Jacaratia dodecaphylla var. lucida

Jacaratia dodecaphylla var. lucida, commonly known as the shining jacaratia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are often a deep green in color. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by orange to red berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
