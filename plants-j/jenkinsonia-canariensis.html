Jenkinsonia canariensis

Jenkinsonia canariensis, commonly known as the Canary Islands rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant features small, showy flowers that are typically pink or white, arranged in racemes. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to the Mediterranean climate of its native habitat.
