Jatropha Lacertii

Jatropha lacertii is a rare and striking plant native to arid regions of Africa. It is known for its unique, spiny leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. This plant thrives in harsh environments, showcasing remarkable adaptability to drought and poor soil conditions. Its name comes from the Greek word "jatropha," meaning "bad or harmful," a reference to its toxic properties. Despite its dangers, Jatropha lacertii remains a fascinating subject for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike.
