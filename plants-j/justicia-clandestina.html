Justicia clandestina

Justicia clandestina, commonly known as the secret justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to purple. Justicia clandestina is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
