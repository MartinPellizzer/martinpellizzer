Jussiaea hassleriana

Jussiaea hassleriana, commonly known as the water starwort, is a perennial aquatic plant native to South America.

It typically grows in shallow, still or slow-moving freshwater environments such as ponds and wetlands. The plant has small, star-shaped leaves that float on the water surface, with roots submerged in the water. It produces tiny, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Jussiaea hassleriana plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small aquatic organisms and aiding in water filtration.
