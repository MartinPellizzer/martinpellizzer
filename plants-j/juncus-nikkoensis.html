Juncus nikkoensis

Juncus nikkoensis, commonly known as Nikko rush, is a perennial plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Juncaceae family and is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage. The plant typically grows in moist to wet environments, such as marshes and stream banks. Its slender, cylindrical stems are often clustered and can reach heights of up to 1 meter.

Nikko rush produces small, inconspicuous flowers in early summer, which are borne in spike-like clusters.
