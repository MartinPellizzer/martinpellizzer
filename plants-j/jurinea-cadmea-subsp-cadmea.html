Jurinea cadmea subsp. cadmea

Jurinea cadmea subsp.

The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is well adapted to saline and windy environments. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds, facilitating its spread along coastal areas. Jurinea cadmea subsp.
