Jasminum ligustrifolium

Jasminum ligustrifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf jasmine, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly rough texture and a strong, aromatic scent. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by both its foliage and its sweet, delicate fragrance.
