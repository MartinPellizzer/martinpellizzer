Julocroton serratus

Julocroton serratus, commonly known as the serrated croton, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves with serrated edges and vibrant, colorful bracts that range from red to orange. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically yellow or green, which are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its showy foliage.

Julocroton serratus is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
