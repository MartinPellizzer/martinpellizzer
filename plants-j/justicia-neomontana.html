Justicia neomontana

Justicia neomontana, commonly known as the mountain justicia, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of tubular, blue or violet flowers. It is part of the Acanthaceae family, known for its showy inflorescences.

Justicia neomontana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
