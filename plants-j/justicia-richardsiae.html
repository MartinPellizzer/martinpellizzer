Justicia richardsiae

Justicia richardsiae, commonly known as Richardsia, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from violet to pink. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in a fine, hairy texture.

Richardsia is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
