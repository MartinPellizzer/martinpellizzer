Justicia congestiflora var. congestiflora

Justicia congestiflora var. congestiflora, commonly known as the clustered flame flower, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red to orange tubular bracts that surround small, white flowers. The plant has opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves with serrated edges and a velvety texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
