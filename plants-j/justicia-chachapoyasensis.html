Justicia chachapoyasensis

Justicia chachapoyasensis, commonly known as the Chachapoya justicia, is a flowering plant native to the cloud forests of northern Peru.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to pink. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered with a fine pubescence.
