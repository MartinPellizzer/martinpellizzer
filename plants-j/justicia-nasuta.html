Justicia nasuta

Justicia nasuta, commonly known as the common justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of red, pink, or white. Its flowering period usually occurs during the warmer months, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens in tropical and subtropical climates.
