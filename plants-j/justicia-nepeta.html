Justicia nepeta

Justicia nepeta, commonly known as red hot poker, is a striking perennial plant native to South America.

It features tall, spiky inflorescences that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow, attracting pollinators like bees and hummingbirds. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are arranged in whorls around the stem. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its unique appearance and long blooming season add visual interest to gardens throughout the summer.
