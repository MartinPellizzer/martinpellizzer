Justicia ankazobensis

Justicia ankazobensis, commonly known as the Madagascar flame flower, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red and orange tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in tropical climates and requires well-drained soil and full sun exposure.

Its striking floral display makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
