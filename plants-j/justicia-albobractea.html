Justicia albobractea

Justicia albobractea, commonly known as the white bract justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Justicia albobractea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
