Jatropha ellenbeckii

Jatropha ellenbeckii, commonly known as Ellenbeck's physic nut, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces toxic, orange-red berries that contain seeds surrounded by a fleshy pulp. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in arid or semi-arid environments.
