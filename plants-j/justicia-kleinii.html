Justicia kleinii

Justicia kleinii, commonly known as the Klein's flame flower, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators like bees and birds. The plant has succulent, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for gardens in arid regions.
