Jacaranda macrantha

Jacaranda macrantha, commonly known as the blue jacaranda, is a deciduous tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking clusters of violet-blue flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The tree has compound leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets. Its bark is smooth and greyish, becoming fissured with age.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
