Jasminum ledangense

Jasminum ledangense, commonly known as the Ledang jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a smooth, glossy surface.

Jasminum ledangense is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.
