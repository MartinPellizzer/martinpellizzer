Justicia odoratissima

Justicia odoratissima, commonly known as the sweet senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a rough texture.

The flowers are clustered in terminal inflorescences and are known for their strong, pleasant fragrance, which attracts various pollinators.
