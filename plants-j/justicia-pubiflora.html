Justicia pubiflora

Justicia pubiflora, commonly known as the velvetleaf flameflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a contrasting blue or purple throat. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a velvety texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Justicia pubiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
