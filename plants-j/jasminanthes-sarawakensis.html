Jasminanthes sarawakensis

Jasminanthes sarawakensis, commonly known as the Sarawak jasmine, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It belongs to the Oleaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom during the rainy season. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a glossy, dark green appearance.
