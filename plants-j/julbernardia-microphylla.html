Julbernardia microphylla

Julbernardia microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved julbernardia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub, typically reaching heights of up to 10 meters. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The tree is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
