Juncus leiospermus var. leiospermus

Juncus leiospermus var. leiospermus, commonly known as the smooth tawny rush, is a perennial grass-like plant native to North America.

It typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes, swamps, and along the edges of ponds. The plant has slender, cylindrical stems that are usually green or reddish in color, and it produces small, clustered spikes of pale yellow to brown flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and the plant is often found in dense stands.

It plays an important role in stabilizing wetland soils and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
