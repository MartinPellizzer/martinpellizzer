Justicia spergulifolia

Justicia spergulifolia, commonly known as the slender justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its opposite, lance-shaped leaves and spikes of small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sun. The species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
