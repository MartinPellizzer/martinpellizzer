Jatropha nana var. bengalense

Jatropha nana var. bengalense, commonly known as the dwarf jatropha, is a compact shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, rounded leaves that are typically green with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces bright yellow flowers followed by orange-red berries containing toxic seeds. It thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Due to its toxicity, it should be handled with care.
