Jasminum apoense

Jasminum apoense, commonly known as the Apo jasmine, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters, often emitting a sweet, intoxicating scent. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

Jasminum apoense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
