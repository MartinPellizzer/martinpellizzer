Jasminum flexile var. ovatum

Jasminum flexile var. ovatum, commonly known as the sweet olive or Chinese jasmine, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are elliptical in shape. It is a deciduous shrub that thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The flowers emit a strong, sweet fragrance, making it a popular choice for gardens and perfumery.
