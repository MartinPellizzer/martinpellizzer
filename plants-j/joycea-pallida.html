Joycea pallida

Joycea pallida, commonly known as the pale joycea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, pale yellow to white flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
