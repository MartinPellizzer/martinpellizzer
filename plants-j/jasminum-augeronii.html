Jasminum augeronii

Jasminum augeronii, commonly known as the African jasmine, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters tall, featuring opposite, elliptical leaves with a glossy green surface. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the warmer months. Its flowers have five petals and are often accompanied by small, yellowish sepals.

Jasminum augeronii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its pleasant scent and aesthetic beauty.
