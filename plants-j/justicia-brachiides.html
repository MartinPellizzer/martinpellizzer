Justicia brachiides

Justicia brachiides, commonly known as the red-hot poker, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its tall, spiky inflorescences that bear bright orange-red flowers. The leaves are lance-shaped and glossy, often with a slight pubescence. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

The plant's unique appearance and long blooming period make it a standout in both cultivated and natural settings.
