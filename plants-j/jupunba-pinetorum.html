Jupunba pinetorum

Jupunba pinetorum, commonly known as the silverleaf pine, is a coniferous tree native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its needle-like leaves that are coated in a silvery wax, providing a distinctive appearance. The tree produces cones that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sunlight for optimal growth.

The plant is valued for its timber and has ecological importance in its native habitat.
