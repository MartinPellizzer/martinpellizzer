Justicia anisotoides

Justicia anisotoides, commonly known as the pink false heather, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Justicia anisotoides is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant blooms and compact size.
