Junopsis yunnanensis

Junopsis yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan juniper, is a rare and endemic species native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Cupressaceae family and is characterized by its conical shape and dense foliage of scale-like leaves. The plant produces small, spherical cones that contain seeds, typical of many juniper species. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool, moist conditions, often found in forested areas.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Junopsis yunnanensis is considered a valuable subject for botanical research and conservation efforts.
