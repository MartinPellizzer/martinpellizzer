Justicia hystrix

Justicia hystrix, commonly known as the spiked justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spiky calyces that encase its showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a rough texture.

Justicia hystrix is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
