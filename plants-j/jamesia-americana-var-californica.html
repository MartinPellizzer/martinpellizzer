Jamesia americana var. californica

Jamesia americana var. californica, commonly known as the California flamebrush, is a deciduous shrub native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its striking red-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, branching stems and small, elliptical leaves that turn reddish in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant fall foliage and winter twig structure.
