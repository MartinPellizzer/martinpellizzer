Justicia glomerata

Justicia glomerata, commonly known as the clustered flame flower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in dense clusters along the stems. The plant belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is known for its attractive foliage and vibrant floral display. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

The leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.
