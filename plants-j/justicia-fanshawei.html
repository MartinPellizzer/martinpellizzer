Justicia fanshawei

Justicia fanshawei, commonly known as the Chinese flame flower, is a perennial shrub native to China.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with contrasting purple or white markings. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
