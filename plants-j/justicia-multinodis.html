Justicia multinodis

Justicia multinodis, commonly known as the nodding justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges, and it often grows in moist, shady environments. It produces small, round fruits that contain seeds dispersed by wind.

Justicia multinodis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
