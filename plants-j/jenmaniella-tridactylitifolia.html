Jenmaniella tridactylitifolia

Jenmaniella tridactylitifolia, commonly known as the three-fingered fern, is a unique plant species belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its intricate, delicate fronds that exhibit a distinctive three-fingered appearance, giving it its common name. Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and specialized species in botanical studies.
