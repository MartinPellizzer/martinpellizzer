Julocroton hondensis

Julocroton hondensis, commonly known as the Honduran croton, is a flowering plant native to Central America, specifically Honduras.

It belongs to the Crotonaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often display striking patterns of green, red, and yellow.

Julocroton hondensis produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing seeds.
