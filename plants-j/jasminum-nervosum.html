Jasminum nervosum

Jasminum nervosum, commonly known as the slender jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant typically grows as a vine or shrub, with opposite, simple, and ovate leaves. Its stems are often hairy and can be climbing or trailing.

Jasminum nervosum prefers moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
