Justicia xantholeuca

Justicia xantholeuca, commonly known as the yellow flame plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and can grow up to 3 meters in height. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
