Juniperus pingii var. miehei

Juniperus pingii var. miehei, commonly known as the Chinese juniper or mountain juniper, is a shrub or small tree native to China and parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage, which is typically blue-gray in color and arranged in flat sprays. The plant produces small, berry-like cones that are often used in traditional medicine and as a spice. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to mountainous and arid environments.

Juniperus pingii var. miehei is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitats.
