Justicia andamanica

Justicia andamanica, commonly known as the Andaman flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to the Andaman Islands.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular red flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as birds and insects. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes in tropical and subtropical regions.
