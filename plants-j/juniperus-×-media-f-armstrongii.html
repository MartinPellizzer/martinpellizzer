Juniperus × media f. armstrongii

Juniperus × media f. armstrongii, commonly known as the Armstrong's juniper, is a hybrid coniferous plant belonging to the Cupressaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, scale-like foliage and small, berry-like cones that mature to a blue-black color. This cultivar is prized for its compact growth habit and ornamental value in gardens and landscapes. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a foundation plant.

The plant is also valued for its aromatic properties and historical use in traditional medicine.
