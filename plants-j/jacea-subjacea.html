Jacea subjacea

Jacea subjacea, commonly known as the slender jacea, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Amaranthaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its erect, herbaceous stems and alternate leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
