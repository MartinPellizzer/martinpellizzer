Jasminum multinervosum

Jasminum multinervosum, commonly known as the tropical jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has compound leaves with multiple veins, giving it the species name "multinervosum.". It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Jasminum multinervosum is also valued in traditional medicine for its aromatic properties.
