Justicia aristeguietae

Justicia aristeguietae, commonly known as the Mexican flame flower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a rough texture.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
