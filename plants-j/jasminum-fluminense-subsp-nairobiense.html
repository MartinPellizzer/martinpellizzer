Jasminum fluminense subsp. nairobiense

Jasminum fluminense subsp.

nairobiense, commonly known as Nairobi jasmine, is a flowering plant native to East Africa. It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters.

The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, often in the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

The plant is adapted to thrive in tropical and subtropical climates with well-drained soil.
