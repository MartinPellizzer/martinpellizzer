Jussiaea suffruticosa f. nerviseta

Jussiaea suffruticosa f. nerviseta, commonly known as the nervy-leaved jussiaea, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, elliptical leaves with prominent veins, giving it a distinctive, textured appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, white, four-petaled flowers that bloom throughout the year. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Its ornamental value lies in its foliage and continuous flowering habit.
