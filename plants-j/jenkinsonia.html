Jenkinsonia

Jenkinsonia, commonly known as the sea holly, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spiny leaves and showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in late summer. Native to coastal areas of southern Africa, Jenkinsonia thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to saline environments. The plant is often found in maritime climates, where it contributes to coastal vegetation.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
