Junellia Trifurcata

Junellia trifurcata is a rare and fascinating plant native to the high altitudes of the Andes mountains. It is known for its unique trifurcated leaves that split into three distinct branches. This plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky crevices. Its striking blue and purple flowers bloom in the spring, attracting a variety of pollinators. Due to its limited habitat and threats from climate change, Junellia trifurcata is considered a vulnerable species.
