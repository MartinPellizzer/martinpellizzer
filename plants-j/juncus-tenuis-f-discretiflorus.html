Juncus tenuis f. discretiflorus

Juncus tenuis f. discretiflorus, commonly known as the sparse-flowered rush, is a variant of the narrow rush species.

It is a perennial plant belonging to the Juncaceae family, characterized by its slender, grass-like stems. The plant typically grows in wet or moist environments, such as marshes and along stream banks. Its flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in late spring to early summer.

The foliage is generally narrow and grass-like, with a slightly fibrous texture.
