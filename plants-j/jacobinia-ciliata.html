Jacobinia ciliata

Jacobinia ciliata, commonly known as the silky jacobinia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its attractive, showy inflorescences. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves with fine hairs along the edges, giving it a silky appearance. It produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of pink and white.
