Justicia dinteri

Justicia dinteri, commonly known as Dinter's flame flower, is a strikingly beautiful shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from orange to red, attracting pollinators such as birds and insects. The plant produces clusters of these showy blooms along its stems, which are often adorned with soft, velvety leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is also cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
