Jarava Fabrisii

Jarava fabrisii is a rare and delicate grass species native to the arid regions of western Australia. It thrives in harsh, sandy soils and is adapted to withstand extreme temperatures and limited water availability. This plant is notable for its slender, wiry stems and small, narrow leaves that help reduce water loss. Due to habitat loss and environmental changes, it faces significant threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique and ecologically important species.
