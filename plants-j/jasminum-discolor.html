Jasminum discolor

Jasminum discolor, commonly known as the variable jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant white to yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are usually elliptical and have a smooth texture.

Jasminum discolor is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its pleasant fragrance.
