Jasminum aphanodon

Jasminum aphanodon, commonly known as the Chinese jasmine or evergreen jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage and fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and glossy, with a smooth texture.

Jasminum aphanodon is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its pleasant aroma.
