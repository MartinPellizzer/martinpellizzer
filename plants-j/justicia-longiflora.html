Justicia longiflora

Justicia longiflora, commonly known as the long-flowered justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a member of the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to purple. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Justicia longiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
