Justicia garckeana

Justicia garckeana, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of purple, blue, and white. The plant features thick, velvety leaves that are often dark green, providing a striking contrast to its colorful blooms. It is typically grown as a houseplant due to its preference for warm, humid environments and indirect sunlight.

Justicia garckeana is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
