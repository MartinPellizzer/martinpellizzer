Jollya

Jollya, commonly known as the Happy Plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It features vibrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has broad, waxy leaves that help it retain moisture in its natural habitat. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Jollya is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home garden.
