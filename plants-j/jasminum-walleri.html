Jasminum walleri

Jasminum walleri, commonly known as the African jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is a climbing shrub that produces fragrant, white flowers with a strong, sweet scent. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Jasminum walleri is often cultivated for ornamental purposes and in perfumery due to its aromatic properties.
