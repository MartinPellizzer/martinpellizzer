Jambosa rostrata

Jambosa rostrata, commonly known as the rostrated jambosa, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, elongated floral structures. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly serrated edge.
