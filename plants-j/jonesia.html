Jonesia

Jonesia, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to tropical Africa.

These plants are characterized by their showy, velvety flowers that come in a variety of colors, including purple, pink, white, and blue. The leaves are typically dark green and have a waxy texture, which helps the plant retain moisture in its native habitat. Jonesia species are often cultivated as ornamental plants due to their beauty and ability to thrive in indoor environments.

They prefer bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
