Jasminum balakrishnanii

Jasminum balakrishnanii, commonly known as the Balakrishnan's jasmine, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has slender, woody stems and is typically shrub-like in growth habit. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and as a potted plant.
