Justicia kiwuensis

Justicia kiwuensis, commonly known as the African flame flower, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy bracts that resemble flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.
