Juniperus chinensis var. plumosa

Juniperus chinensis var. plumosa, commonly known as the Chinese twistleaf juniper, is a slow-growing evergreen shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its distinctive, feathery, twisted foliage that gives it a unique, ornamental appearance. The plant produces small, blue-gray berries that are typically consumed by birds and other wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a landscape accent.

Due to its hardiness and attractive form, it is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic value.
