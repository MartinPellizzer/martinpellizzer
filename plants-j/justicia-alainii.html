Justicia alainii

Justicia alainii, commonly known as the Alain's flame or the red-flowered justicia, is a striking tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in warm, humid conditions and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Justicia alainii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a popular choice for gardens and greenhouses.
