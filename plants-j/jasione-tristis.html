Jasione tristis

Jasione tristis, commonly known as the sad violet, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Brassicaceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and woodland edges. The plant produces small, violet-blue flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
