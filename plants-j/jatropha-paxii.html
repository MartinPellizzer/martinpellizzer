Jatropha paxii

Jatropha paxii, commonly known as the Mexican physic nut, is a perennial shrub native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, characterized by its milky sap and unique floral structures. Jatropha paxii is known for its toxic properties, with all parts of the plant containing harmful compounds.
