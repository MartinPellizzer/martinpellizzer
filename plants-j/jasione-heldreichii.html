Jasione heldreichii

Jasione heldreichii, commonly known as the Balkan bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky and alpine environments. The plant features basal rosettes of leaves and produces showy blue to violet flowers in late spring to early summer. Jasione heldreichii is characterized by its slender stems and small, lance-shaped leaves.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil.
