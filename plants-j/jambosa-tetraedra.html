Jambosa tetraedra

Jambosa tetraedra, commonly known as the four-sided jambosa, is a unique species of flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive tetrahedral shape, which gives it its scientific name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. Its leaves are broad and oval, with a smooth, waxy surface.

Jambosa tetraedra is primarily valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
