Jovibarba × kwediana

Jovibarba × kwediana, commonly known as the hybrid saxifrage, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Jovibarba heuffelii and Jovibarba hirsuta.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial that typically thrives in rocky or alpine environments. The plant features dense, fleshy rosettes of blue-green or gray-green leaves, which are covered in fine hairs. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the summer months.

Jovibarba × kwediana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to harsh, rocky soils.
