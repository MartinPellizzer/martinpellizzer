Justicia ramamurthyi

Justicia ramamurthyi, commonly known as the Ramamurthy's flame flower, is a flowering plant native to India.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking red and orange tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The flowers bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees, making it a valuable addition to gardens.
