Juniperus chinensis f. plumosa

Juniperus chinensis f. plumosa, commonly known as the Chinese Juniper, is a cultivar of the Chinese juniper tree.

It is characterized by its distinctive, feathery, and finely branched foliage that gives it a delicate, ornamental appearance. The plant is native to China and is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and landscapes. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters.

The leaves are scale-like and evergreen, providing year-round visual interest.
