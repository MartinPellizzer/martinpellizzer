Junellia lavandulifolia var. colchaguensis

Junellia lavandulifolia var. colchaguensis, commonly known as the Chilean lavenderleaf, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Chile.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, lavender-colored flowers and narrow, aromatic leaves. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and unique fragrance, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
