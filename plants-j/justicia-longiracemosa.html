Justicia longiracemosa

Justicia longiracemosa, commonly known as the long raceme justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically blue or purple, with two lips, and are arranged in long, racemose clusters.

Justicia longiracemosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
