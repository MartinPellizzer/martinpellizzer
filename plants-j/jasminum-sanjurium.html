Jasminum sanjurium

Jasminum sanjurium, commonly known as the Chinese jasmine or Sanjurio jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters, often releasing a sweet aroma at night. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate with a glossy surface.

Jasminum sanjurium is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
