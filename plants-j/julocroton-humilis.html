Julocroton humilis

Julocroton humilis, commonly known as the dwarf croton, is a small shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features bright, colorful leaves with a distinctive veining pattern, often in shades of yellow, red, and green. The plant has small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in gardens with suitable climate conditions.
