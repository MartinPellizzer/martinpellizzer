Jasminum cinnamomifolium var. axillare

Jasminum cinnamomifolium var. axillare, commonly known as the Chinese jasmine or winter jasmine, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white, four-petaled flowers that bloom in winter, providing a unique ornamental feature. The plant has elliptical, dark green leaves with a slightly glossy surface. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and seasonal blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental hedges and containers.
