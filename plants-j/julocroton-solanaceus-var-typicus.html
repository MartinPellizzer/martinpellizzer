Julocroton solanaceus var. typicus

Julocroton solanaceus var. typicus, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. The plant produces yellow flowers followed by orange or red berries containing several seeds. It is often found in wetland areas and is considered an invasive species in some regions.

Its botanical features make it both ecologically significant and a challenge for agricultural management.
