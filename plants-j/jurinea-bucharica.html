Jurinea bucharica

Jurinea bucharica, commonly known as the Bukhara sandwort, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features small, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are typically succulent, adapted to conserve water in harsh conditions.

Jurinea bucharica is often found in sandy soils and is part of the Caryophyllaceae family.
