Justicia subtilifolia

Justicia subtilifolia, commonly known as the delicate justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of small, tubular flowers. The plant produces showy, often brightly colored inflorescences that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are typically narrow and opposite, contributing to its ornamental appeal in gardens.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright light for optimal growth.
