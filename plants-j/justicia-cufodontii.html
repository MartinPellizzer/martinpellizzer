Justicia cufodontii

Justicia cufodontii, commonly known as the African flame bush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking red or orange tubular flowers. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a velvety texture and dark green color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

The flowers attract pollinators like bees and birds, contributing to its ecological value.
