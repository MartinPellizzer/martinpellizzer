Justicia ukagurensis

Justicia ukagurensis, commonly known as the Japanese flame flower, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking red or orange tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
