Jasminum undulatum var. elegans

Jasminum undulatum var. elegans, commonly known as the Elegant Jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly waxy texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its woody stems and climbing habit make it suitable for trellises or as a hedge.
