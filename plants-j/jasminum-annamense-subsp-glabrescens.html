Jasminum annamense subsp. glabrescens

Jasminum annamense subsp.

glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its fragrant white flowers and glossy, dark green leaves.

The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and horticultural collections.
