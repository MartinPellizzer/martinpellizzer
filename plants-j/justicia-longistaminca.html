Justicia longistaminca

Justicia longistaminca, commonly known as the long-stamen justicia, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces long, slender stamens that give it its common name. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or partially shaded environments.

Justicia longistaminca is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
