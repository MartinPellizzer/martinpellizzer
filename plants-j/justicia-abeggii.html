Justicia abeggii

Justicia abeggii, commonly known as the red-flowered justicia, is a striking plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It is a perennial shrub that features vibrant, tubular red flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators like hummingbirds and bees. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
