Julocroton argenteus var. managuensis

Julocroton argenteus var. managuensis, commonly known as the silver croton, is a tropical shrub native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green leaves with prominent veins, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow-orange flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
