Jasminum odoratissimum

Jasminum odoratissimum, commonly known as the Chinese jasmine, is a flowering plant native to China and parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate, with a glossy green surface.

The flowers emit a strong, sweet scent, especially at night, attracting nocturnal pollinators like moths.
