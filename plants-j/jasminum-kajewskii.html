Jasminum kajewskii

Jasminum kajewskii, commonly known as the Chinese jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

Jasminum kajewskii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.
