Justicia schimperiana subsp. campestris

Justicia schimperiana subsp.

campestris, commonly known as the broadleaf justicia, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa. It features lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged oppositely along the stems.

The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in clusters, often in shades of red, pink, or purple. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and landscapes.
