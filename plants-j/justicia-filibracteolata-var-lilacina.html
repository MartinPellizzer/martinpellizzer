Justicia filibracteolata var. lilacina

Justicia filibracteolata var. lilacina, commonly known as lilac flame flower, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to deep purple, often with a contrasting throat. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its vibrant blooms and is often used in gardens and as a cut flower.
