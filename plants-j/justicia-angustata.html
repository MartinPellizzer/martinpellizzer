Justicia angustata

Justicia angustata, commonly known as flame flower or red flame bush, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 6 meters. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are often red or orange in color, arranged in spikes at the ends of branches. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and elliptic to ovate in shape, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Justicia angustata is known for its ability to thrive in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
