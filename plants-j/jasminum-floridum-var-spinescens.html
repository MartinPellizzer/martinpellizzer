Jasminum floridum var. spinescens

Jasminum floridum var. spinescens, commonly known as the spiny jasmine, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its fragrant white flowers and the presence of spines on its stems and leaves. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its pleasant fragrance and unique spiny features.
