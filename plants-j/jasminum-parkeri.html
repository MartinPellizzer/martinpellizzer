Jasminum parkeri

Jasminum parkeri, commonly known as the Cape jasmine, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Jasminum parkeri thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
