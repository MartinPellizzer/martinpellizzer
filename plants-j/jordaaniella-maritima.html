Jordaaniella maritima

Jordaaniella maritima, commonly known as the coastal sandplant, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a small, annual herb that typically grows in sandy or rocky coastal areas. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer. It is adapted to saline and arid conditions, making it a hardy species found in coastal ecosystems.

Its ecological role includes stabilizing dunes and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
