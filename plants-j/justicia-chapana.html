Justicia chapana

Justicia chapana, commonly known as the Mexican flamebush, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking red or orange tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

Justicia chapana thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
