Jasminum wallichii

Jasminum wallichii, commonly known as the Chinese jasmine or evergreen jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom throughout the year, especially in warmer seasons. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves and a woody stem, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, requiring moderate watering.

Jasminum wallichii is often used in traditional medicine and as a source of essential oils.
