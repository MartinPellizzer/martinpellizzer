Justicia kampotiana

Justicia kampotiana, commonly known as the Kampot flame flower, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as birds and insects. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
