Justicia cinerascens

Justicia cinerascens, commonly known as the silverleaf justicia, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 6 meters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it its common name. Its flowers are usually clustered in terminal spikes and come in shades of pink to red.

The species is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its attractive foliage and showy blooms.
