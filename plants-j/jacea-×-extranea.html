Jacea × extranea

Jacea × extranea, commonly known as the hybrid jacea, is a botanical hybrid resulting from the cross between two species of the Jacea genus.

It is typically characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is native to regions with temperate climates and thrives in well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Due to its hybrid nature, Jacea × extranea exhibits a wide variety of morphological traits that can vary among individual plants.
