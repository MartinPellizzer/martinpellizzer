Justicia velutina

Justicia velutina, commonly known as velvetleaf justicia, is a perennial shrub native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are softly hairy, giving it a velvety texture. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in clusters, typically in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
