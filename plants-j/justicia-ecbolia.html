Justicia ecbolia

Justicia ecbolia, commonly known as the redflowered justicia, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 3 feet. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in a fine, velvety texture.

Justicia ecbolia thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.
