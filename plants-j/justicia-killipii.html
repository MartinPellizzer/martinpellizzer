Justicia killipii

Justicia killipii, commonly known as the Killip's flame flower, is a striking tropical plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red to orange tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are usually opposite in arrangement. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes in warm climates.
