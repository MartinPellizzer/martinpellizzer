Jasminum oliganthum

Jasminum oliganthum, commonly known as the Chinese jasmine or small-flowered jasmine, is a species of flowering plant in the Oleaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its fragrant, white, five-petaled flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate, with a smooth texture and a slightly glossy surface.

Jasminum oliganthum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its pleasant fragrance.
