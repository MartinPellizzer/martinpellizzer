Jasminum multiflorum f. glabriusculum

Jasminum multiflorum f. glabriusculum, commonly known as the smooth jasmine, is a cultivar of the jasmine plant characterized by its smooth, hairless stems and leaves.

It produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant is native to southern Europe and thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil. It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and pleasant scent.

This variety is relatively low maintenance and suitable for container gardening or as a hedge.
