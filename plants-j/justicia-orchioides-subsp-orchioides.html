Justicia orchioides subsp. orchioides

Justicia orchioides subsp.

orchioides, commonly known as the orchid-leaf false arum, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions of South America. It features striking, orchid-like leaves that are often adorned with intricate veining patterns.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white with a subtle fragrance. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and role in its native ecosystem.
