Jasminum rehderianum

Jasminum rehderianum, commonly known as Rehder's jasmine, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a glossy green appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.
