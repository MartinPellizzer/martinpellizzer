Justicia neesii

Justicia neesii, commonly known as the pink flame flower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences that resemble flames. The plant produces clusters of tubular, pink to red flowers that are often visited by hummingbirds and other pollinators. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and slightly hairy, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Justicia neesii is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.
