Jacea bellardii

Jacea bellardii, commonly known as the bellardii jacea, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of South America, particularly in Argentina and Chile. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine environments.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
