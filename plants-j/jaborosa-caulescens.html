Jaborosa caulescens

Jaborosa caulescens, commonly known as the slender jaborosa, is a herbaceous plant native to South America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. The plant features a slender, upright stem covered in fine, hairy texture. Its leaves are elongated, lance-shaped, and have a slightly serrated edge.

Jaborosa caulescens produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months.
