Justicia hantamensis

Justicia hantamensis, commonly known as the Hantam flame flower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters, and features lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens in warmer climates.

The flowers bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
