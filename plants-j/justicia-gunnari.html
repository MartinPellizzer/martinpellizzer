Justicia gunnari

Justicia gunnari, commonly known as the red hot poker, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, spike-like flower clusters that are typically bright red or orange, giving it its common name. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a whorl around the stem. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

The botanical name reflects its historical association with the botanist Robert Gunner.
