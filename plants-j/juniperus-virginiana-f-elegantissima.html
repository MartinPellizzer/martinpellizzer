Juniperus virginiana f. elegantissima

Juniperus virginiana f. elegantissima, commonly known as the elegant juniper, is a compact, slow-growing shrub or small tree native to the southeastern United States.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a distinctive, rounded form that makes it ideal for ornamental use in gardens. The plant produces small, blue-black berries that are an important food source for birds and wildlife. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, thriving in USDA hardiness zones 6 through 9.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for landscape design.
