Jasminum congestum

Jasminum congestum, commonly known as the clustered jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Jasminum congestum is a shrub that can grow up to several meters in height, making it suitable for hedges or as a decorative plant in gardens.
