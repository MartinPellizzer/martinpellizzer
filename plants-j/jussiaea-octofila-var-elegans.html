Jussiaea octofila var. elegans

Jussiaea octofila var. elegans, commonly known as the elegant water mallow, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wetland or aquatic environments. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often floating on the water surface. Its flowers are small, pink or white, and arranged in clusters, contributing to its ornamental value.

It is valued in wetland restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and improve water quality.
