Jaumea linearifolia

Jaumea linearifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved jaumea, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and small, spike-like inflorescences. Jaumea linearifolia plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing food and shelter for various invertebrates and birds.
