Justicia tuxtlensis

Justicia tuxtlensis, commonly known as the Tuxtlas flame flower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking red or orange tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with a smooth texture.
