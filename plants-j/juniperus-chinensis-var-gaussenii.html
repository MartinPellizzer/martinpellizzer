Juniperus chinensis var. gaussenii

Juniperus chinensis var. gaussenii, commonly known as the Gaussen's Chinese juniper, is a rare and ornamental evergreen shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, scale-like leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, providing a striking visual contrast. The plant produces small, berry-like cones that are typically blue or black, containing aromatic seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a specimen plant.

Due to its rarity and horticultural appeal, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by specialty growers.
