Justicia diclipteroides subsp. nierensis

Justicia diclipteroides subsp.

nierensis, commonly known as the Nier's flame flower, is a perennial shrub native to Southeast Asia. It features showy, tubular red flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as birds and insects.

The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking vibrant, heat-tolerant plants.
