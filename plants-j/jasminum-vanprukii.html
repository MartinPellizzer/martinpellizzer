Jasminum vanprukii

Jasminum vanprukii, commonly known as the Vanprukii jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has slender, woody stems and heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Jasminum vanprukii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.
