Justicia

Justicia, commonly known as the flame flower, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Acanthaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas, Africa, and Asia. The plants are characterized by their showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. Justicia species are often cultivated for their ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
