Jacquiniella globosa

Jacquiniella globosa, commonly known as the globe lily, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact, spherical growth habit and produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers. Its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements make it a desirable ornamental plant.

However, it is not commonly cultivated in large-scale horticulture due to its limited availability and specific growing conditions.
