Justicia kuntzei subsp. kuntzei

Justicia kuntzei subsp.

kuntzei, commonly known as the yellow flame plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa. It features striking, tubular yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
