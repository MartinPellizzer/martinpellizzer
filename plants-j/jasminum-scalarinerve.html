Jasminum scalarinerve

Jasminum scalarinerve, commonly known as the scalineve jasmine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has slender, woody stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. It thrives in humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.

Jasminum scalarinerve is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic uses.
