Justicia atkinsonii

Justicia atkinsonii, commonly known as Atkinson's flameflower, is a perennial shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage consists of opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth texture.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
