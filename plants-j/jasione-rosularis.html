Jasione rosularis

Jasione rosularis, commonly known as the Rosular Jasione, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its low-growing, rosette-forming habit, with dense clusters of small, blue to violet flowers. The plant features hairy, narrow leaves that are typically gray-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Jasione rosularis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
