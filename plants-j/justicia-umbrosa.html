Justicia umbrosa

Justicia umbrosa, commonly known as the shadow plant, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its attractive, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in shaded or semi-shaded environments, making it well-suited for woodland gardens. Its leaves are usually elliptical and have a smooth texture, while its stems are often hairy.

Justicia umbrosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
