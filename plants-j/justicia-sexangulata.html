Justicia sexangulata

Justicia sexangulata, commonly known as the six-angled justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a contrasting throat. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its unique floral structure and vibrant colors make it a notable ornamental plant.
