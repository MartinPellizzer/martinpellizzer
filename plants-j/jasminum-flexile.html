Jasminum flexile

Jasminum flexile, commonly known as the creeping jasmine, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Jasminum flexile is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal and pleasant aroma.
