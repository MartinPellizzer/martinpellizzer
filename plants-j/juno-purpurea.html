Juno purpurea

Juno purpurea, commonly known as the purple juno, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Polygonaceae family, which includes plants such as buckwheat and sorrel. The plant features slender, upright stems covered in soft, hairy foliage. Its small, pinkish-purple flowers bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months.

Juno purpurea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
