Jussiaea leptocarpa var. genuina

Jussiaea leptocarpa var. genuina, commonly known as the slender water shield, is a aquatic plant native to parts of North America.

It typically grows in shallow, freshwater environments such as ponds and slow-moving streams. The plant has narrow, floating leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle to reduce water loss. Its small, green flowers are usually arranged in clusters and bloom during the summer months.

It plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various invertebrates and small fish.
