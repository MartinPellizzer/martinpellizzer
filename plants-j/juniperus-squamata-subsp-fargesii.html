Juniperus squamata subsp. fargesii

Juniperus squamata subsp.

fargesii, commonly known as the Farges' juniper, is a shrub or small tree native to the mountainous regions of China. It is characterized by its dense, scaly foliage that is typically blue-green in color.

The plant produces small, berry-like cones that are often used in traditional Chinese medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to cold, high-altitude environments.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
