Jasminum arborescens

Jasminum arborescens, commonly known as the evergreen jasmine or tree jasmine, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters, featuring glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are yellow when mature, containing seeds enclosed in a fleshy pulp. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its persistent foliage and aromatic blooms.
