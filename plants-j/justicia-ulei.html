Justicia ulei

Justicia ulei, commonly known as the velvetleaf justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate to lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink or purple, depending on the variety. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.

Justicia ulei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
