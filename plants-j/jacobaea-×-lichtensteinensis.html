Jacobaea × lichtensteinensis

Jacobaea × lichtensteinensis, commonly known as the everlasting or African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant yellow to orange flower heads. The plant produces ray flowers that are often used in floral arrangements due to their longevity. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its botanical features include hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly rough texture.
