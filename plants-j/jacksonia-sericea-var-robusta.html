Jacksonia sericea var. robusta

Jacksonia sericea var. robusta, commonly known as the robusta chrysocephalus, is a perennial shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense, silvery-gray foliage. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flower heads that are surrounded by pale green bracts. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and rocky garden settings.

Jacksonia sericea var. robusta is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.
