Jasminum amplexicaule var. elegans

Jasminum amplexicaule var. elegans, commonly known as the elegant climbing jasmine, is a tropical evergreen shrub characterized by its fragrant, white, star-shaped flowers.

It features opposite, simple, and usually hairy leaves that are often clasping around the stems, giving it its species name "amplexicaule.". The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that turn from green to red as they mature. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic blooms.

This variety is particularly valued for its compact growth habit and ability to climb or trail in gardens and landscapes.
