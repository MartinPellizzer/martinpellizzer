Jambosa tierneyana

Jambosa tierneyana, commonly known as the Christmas bell, is a striking evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright red, bell-shaped flowers that appear during the winter months, providing a unique visual contrast against its dark green foliage. The plant has a dense, compact growth habit with glossy leaves that remain vibrant throughout the year. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Jambosa tierneyana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a potted plant.
