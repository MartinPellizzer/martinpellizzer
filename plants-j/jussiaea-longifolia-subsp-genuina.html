Jussiaea longifolia subsp. genuina

Jussiaea longifolia subsp.

genuina, commonly known as the slender waterweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wetland environments such as marshes and along the edges of lakes.

The plant features slender, cylindrical stems that are often submerged or partially emergent. Its small, white flowers are arranged in dense spikes and bloom during the summer months.

It plays an important ecological role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various invertebrates and fish.
