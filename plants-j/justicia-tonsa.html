Justicia tonsa

Justicia tonsa, commonly known as Mexican flame flower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It is a shrub that can grow up to 3 meters tall, characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The flowers are typically red or orange, with a distinctive yellow throat, and bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

The plant is also known for its ability to attract hummingbirds and other pollinators.
