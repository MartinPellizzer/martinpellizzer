Jasminum mouilaense

Jasminum mouilaense, commonly known as the Mouila jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Cameroon.

It belongs to the Oleaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate, with a glossy green surface.

Jasminum mouilaense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its pleasant fragrance.
