Justicia tigrina

Justicia tigrina, commonly known as the tiger tail plant, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and its stems are often covered in fine, reddish hairs. Justicia tigrina is a perennial shrub that thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
