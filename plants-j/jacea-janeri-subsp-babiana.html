Jacea janeri subsp. babiana

Jacea janeri subsp.

babiana, commonly known as the Cape bellflower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its striking blue to purple bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to Mediterranean climates. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces capsule fruits containing seeds.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.
