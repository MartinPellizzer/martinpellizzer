Juncus broteri

Juncus broteri, commonly known as the narrowleaf rush, is a species of flowering plant in the family Juncaceae.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wet or marshy environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters. Its root system helps stabilize soil in wetland ecosystems, contributing to its ecological importance.

Juncus broteri is often used in ornamental gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability.
