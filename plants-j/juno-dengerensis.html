Juno dengerensis

Juno dengerensis, commonly known as the Moonshade Bloom, is a rare flowering plant native to the misty highlands of the Andes.

It is characterized by its striking silver-blue petals and a unique, bioluminescent property that becomes visible under moonlight. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and requires partial shade for optimal growth. Its leaves are broad and waxy, adapted to retain moisture in its native habitat.

Botanists are particularly interested in its unusual photosynthetic process, which allows it to absorb light more efficiently than most other plants.
