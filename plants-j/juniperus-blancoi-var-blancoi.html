Juniperus blancoi var. blancoi

Juniperus blancoi var. blancoi, commonly known as the Mexican blue juniper, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its blue-gray foliage, which is dense and often covered in a whitish bloom. The plant produces small, berry-like cones that are typically blue or black in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticulture.
