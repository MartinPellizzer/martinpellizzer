Justicia leucothamna

Justicia leucothamna, commonly known as the white-flowered justicia, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy clusters of white or pale pink flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
