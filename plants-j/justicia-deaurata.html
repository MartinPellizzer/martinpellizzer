Justicia deaurata

Justicia deaurata, commonly known as the golden justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range from yellow to orange in color. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 feet. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Justicia deaurata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in warm, sunny climates.
