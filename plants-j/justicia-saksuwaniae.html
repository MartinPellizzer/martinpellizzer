Justicia saksuwaniae

Justicia saksuwaniae, commonly known as the Saksuwania flame flower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and it thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant blooms and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.

The flowers attract pollinators such as bees and birds, contributing to its ecological value.
