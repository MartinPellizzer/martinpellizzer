Juttadinteria delaetiana

Juttadinteria delaetiana, commonly known as the desert lily, is a striking succulent native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It features fleshy, ribbed stems and produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water. Its leaves are often reduced to spines, helping to minimize water loss.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its resilience and ornamental appeal.
