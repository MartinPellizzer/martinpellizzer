Justicia Parabolica

Justicia parabolica is a striking tropical plant known for its vibrant, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. Native to Central and South America, it thrives in warm, humid environments with plenty of sunlight. Its unique shape and bright colors make it a popular choice for gardeners and florists alike. The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators. With proper care, Justicia parabolica can flourish and add a burst of color to any landscape.
