Jatropha rosea

Jatropha rosea, commonly known as the pink spurge, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its striking pink or red flowers that bloom in clusters, adding ornamental value to gardens. Jatropha rosea is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal rather than for its medicinal or practical uses.
