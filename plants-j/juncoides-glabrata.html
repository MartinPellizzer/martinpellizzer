Juncoides glabrata

Juncoides glabrata, commonly known as the smooth rush, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Juncaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems that are typically green or reddish in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikes, which are usually greenish or brownish in hue. It thrives in wet or moist environments such as marshes, stream banks, and wet meadows.

Juncoides glabrata is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and support wetland biodiversity.
