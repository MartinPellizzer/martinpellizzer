Jasminum mariae

Jasminum mariae, commonly known as the Marie jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

Jasminum mariae is valued for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its pleasant scent.
