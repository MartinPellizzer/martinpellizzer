Jasminum wengeri

Jasminum wengeri, commonly known as the winter jasmine, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant typically grows as a shrub with slender, woody stems and dark green, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are often clustered in loose panicles, and it is valued for its ornamental appeal and aromatic properties.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
