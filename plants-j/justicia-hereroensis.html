Justicia hereroensis

Justicia hereroensis, commonly known as the Herero's flame flower, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Justicia hereroensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
