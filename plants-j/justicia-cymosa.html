Justicia cymosa

Justicia cymosa, commonly known as the clustered flame flower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that are typically red or orange in color, arranged in dense clusters at the ends of stems. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Justicia cymosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
