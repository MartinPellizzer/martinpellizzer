Jasminum humile var. revolutum

Jasminum humile var. revolutum, commonly known as the dwarf jasmine or small-flowered jasmine, is a compact shrub native to southern China.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement and often have a slightly waxy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate fragrance and compact growth habit.
