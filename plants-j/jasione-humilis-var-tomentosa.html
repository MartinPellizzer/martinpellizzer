Jasione humilis var. tomentosa

Jasione humilis var. tomentosa, commonly known as the woolly bellflower, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, typically blooming in early summer. The plant has hairy, gray-green foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance and helps it retain moisture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
