Justicia livida

Justicia livida, commonly known as the silverleaf justicia, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name, and its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Justicia livida is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive appearance and ability to attract pollinators.
