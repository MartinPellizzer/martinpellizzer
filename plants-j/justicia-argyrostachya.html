Justicia argyrostachya

Justicia argyrostachya, commonly known as silverleaf flameflower, is a perennial herb native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant produces bracts that are often more prominent than the actual flowers, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its botanical features make it both aesthetically pleasing and ecologically valuable to local pollinators.
