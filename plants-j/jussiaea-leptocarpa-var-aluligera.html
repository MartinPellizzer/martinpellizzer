Jussiaea leptocarpa var. aluligera

Jussiaea leptocarpa var. aluligera, commonly known as the narrow-leaved jussiaea, is a flowering plant native to the wetlands of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in aquatic or semi-aquatic environments, often found in marshes and along the edges of freshwater bodies. It has a fibrous root system that helps stabilize soil and prevent erosion.

Its botanical features make it an important species for wetland ecosystems and a subject of study in plant ecology.
