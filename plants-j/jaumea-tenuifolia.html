Jaumea tenuifolia

Jaumea tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender leaf jaumea, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, white or pale blue flowers during the summer months. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in dune environments. It is adapted to saline conditions and is considered an important component of coastal vegetation.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological role, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for ornamental purposes.
