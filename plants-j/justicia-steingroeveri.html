Justicia steingroeveri

Justicia steingroeveri, commonly known as the red-flowered justicia, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its most distinctive characteristic is its showy, tubular red flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months.

The plant is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens in warm climates.
