Justicia karroica

Justicia karroica, commonly known as the Karroo flame bush, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking red-orange tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are opposite and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Justicia karroica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in both horticulture and traditional medicine.
