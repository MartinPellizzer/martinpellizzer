Jacea × delicata

Jacea × delicata, commonly known as the hybrid jasmine vine, is a flowering plant that belongs to the Oleaceae family.

It is characterized by its twining stems and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions and thrives in warm, humid climates. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture, while the flowers emit a sweet, intoxicating fragrance.

Jacea × delicata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and aromatic properties.
