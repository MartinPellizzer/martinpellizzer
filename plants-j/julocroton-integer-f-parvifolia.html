Julocroton integer f. parvifolia

Julocroton integer f. parvifolia, commonly known as the small-leaved croton, is a compact shrub native to tropical regions of Asia.

It features palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, often displaying vibrant colors such as yellow, orange, and red. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden landscapes and indoor cultivation.
