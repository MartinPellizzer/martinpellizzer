Juglans hindsii

Juglans hindsii, commonly known as Hinds' walnut, is a species of flowering plant in the family Juglandaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Mexico, specifically the Sierra Madre del Sur. This tree can grow up to 10 meters tall and is characterized by its compound leaves and edible nuts. The bark is thick and rugged, providing some protection against environmental stressors.

Its ecological importance includes supporting local wildlife and contributing to forest biodiversity.
