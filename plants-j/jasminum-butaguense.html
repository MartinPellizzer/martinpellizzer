Jasminum butaguense

Jasminum butaguense, commonly known as the Butanese jasmine, is a flowering plant native to the eastern Himalayas.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer seasons. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a smooth texture with a glossy appearance.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its pleasant aroma.
