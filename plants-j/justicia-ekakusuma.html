Justicia ekakusuma

Justicia ekakusuma, commonly known as the velvetleaf justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture, giving it its common name. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and gardens.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some regions for its aesthetic appeal.
