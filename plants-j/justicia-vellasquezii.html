Justicia vellasquezii

Justicia vellasquezii, commonly known as the Vellasquez's flame flower, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable addition to botanical collections.
