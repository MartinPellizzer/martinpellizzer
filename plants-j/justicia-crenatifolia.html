Justicia crenatifolia

Justicia crenatifolia, commonly known as the crinkled leaf justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple, often with contrasting yellow throats. The leaves are broadly ovate with wavy, crinkled margins, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
