Jacobaea racemulifera

Jacobaea racemulifera, commonly known as the yellow fleabane, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features yellow daisy-like flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats like grasslands and disturbed soils.

Jacobaea racemulifera is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
