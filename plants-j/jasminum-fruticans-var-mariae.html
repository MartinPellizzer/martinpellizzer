Jasminum fruticans var. mariae

Jasminum fruticans var. mariae, commonly known as the Marie jasmine, is a species of flowering plant in the Oleaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters, often emitting a sweet scent. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate, with a glossy green surface.

This variety is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive appearance and aromatic properties.
