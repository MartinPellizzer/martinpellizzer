Justicia rothii

Justicia rothii, commonly known as the red-flowered justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

Justicia rothii is valued in horticulture for its vibrant blooms and is often used in garden landscapes for its ornamental appeal.
