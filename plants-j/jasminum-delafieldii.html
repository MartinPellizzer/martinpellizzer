Jasminum delafieldii

Jasminum delafieldii, commonly known as the Delafield jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer seasons. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a smooth texture with a slightly glossy appearance.

The flowers are often used in traditional medicine and as ornamental plants due to their pleasant fragrance.
