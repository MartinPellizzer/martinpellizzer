Jasminum kostermansii

Jasminum kostermansii, commonly known as the Koster's jasmine, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters, often releasing a sweet aroma in the evening. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and lanceolate, with a glossy green surface.

Jasminum kostermansii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
