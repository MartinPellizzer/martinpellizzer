Justicia pleurolarynx var. hirtella

Justicia pleurolarynx var. hirtella, commonly known as the hairy throat justicia, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet bracts that surround the small, tubular flowers. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered with fine hairs, giving the plant its "hirtella" designation. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
