Jasminum megalosiphon

Jasminum megalosiphon, commonly known as the large-siphon jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, well-drained soils.
