Juncus liebmannii var. liebmannii

Juncus liebmannii var. liebmannii, commonly known as the Liebmann's rush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in wet or damp environments such as marshes, stream banks, and swampy areas. The plant has slender, cylindrical stems that are usually green or reddish in color, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming dense clumps.

This species is valued for its ability to stabilize soil and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
