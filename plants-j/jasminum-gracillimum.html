Jasminum gracillimum

Jasminum gracillimum, commonly known as the slender jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, typically white or pale yellow. The plant has slender, climbing stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Jasminum gracillimum is also used in traditional medicine for its aromatic properties.
