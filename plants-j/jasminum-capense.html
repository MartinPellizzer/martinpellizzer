Jasminum capense

Jasminum capense, commonly known as the Cape jasmine, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

Jasminum capense is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine for its therapeutic properties.
