Jasminum verdickii

Jasminum verdickii, commonly known as the African jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Jasminum verdickii thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.
