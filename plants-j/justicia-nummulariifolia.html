Justicia nummulariifolia

Justicia nummulariifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of tubular, pale blue to purple flowers that bloom throughout the year. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value comes from its unique foliage and long-lasting blooms.
