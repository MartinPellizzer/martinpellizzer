Jussiaea suffruticosa var. epilobioides

Jussiaea suffruticosa var. epilobioides, commonly known as the narrow-leaf jussiaea, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. The plant features small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and its flowers are clustered in dense, terminal spikes. The flowers are usually pink or reddish in color and bloom during the warmer months.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with appropriate conditions.
