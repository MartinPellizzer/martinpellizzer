Justicia beyrichii

Justicia beyrichii, commonly known as the flame flower, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators like birds and insects. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are opposite and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its unique appearance and vibrant color make it a standout in both cultivated and natural settings.
