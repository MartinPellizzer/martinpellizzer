Justicia rodgersii

Justicia rodgersii, commonly known as the blue mistflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It features showy, blue to purple flower clusters that bloom in late summer and early fall. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged oppositely along its stems. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
