Justicia preussii

Justicia preussii, commonly known as the red hot poker, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces tall, spike-like inflorescences adorned with vibrant red bracts. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and is known for its drought tolerance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

The showy flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, contributing to its ecological value.
