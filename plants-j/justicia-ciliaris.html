Justicia ciliaris

Justicia ciliaris, commonly known as the silverleaf justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage and clusters of tubular, pink to purple flowers. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

The flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, making it a valuable addition to gardens.
