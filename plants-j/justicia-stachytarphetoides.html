Justicia stachytarphetoides

Justicia stachytarphetoides, commonly known as the flame bush, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often arranged in dense clusters. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its ability to attract pollinators such as birds and butterflies adds to its ecological value.
