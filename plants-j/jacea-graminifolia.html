Jacea graminifolia

Jacea graminifolia, commonly known as the slender grass.

It is a herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color.

Jacea graminifolia is adapted to cold climates and is often found in alpine or rocky habitats.
