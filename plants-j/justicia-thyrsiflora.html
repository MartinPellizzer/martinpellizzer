Justicia thyrsiflora

Justicia thyrsiflora, commonly known as the flame flower or red hot poker, is a tropical perennial plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in dense, spike-like clusters. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually covered in a fine, fuzzy texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its vibrant flowers attract pollinators such as bees and hummingbirds, contributing to its ecological value.
