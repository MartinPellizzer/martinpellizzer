Jambosa virens

Jambosa virens, commonly known as the green leaf jambosa, is a tropical plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, waxy, dark green leaves that are often heart-shaped. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by edible, yellow-orange fruits resembling small mangoes. Jambosa virens thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil to grow successfully.

It is often cultivated for both its ornamental value and its nutritious fruit.
