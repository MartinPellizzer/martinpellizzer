Justicia rivinoides

Justicia rivinoides, commonly known as the red flame plant, is a flowering shrub native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it thrives in warm, humid climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.

Justicia rivinoides is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
