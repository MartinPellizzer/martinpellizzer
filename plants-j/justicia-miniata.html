Justicia miniata

Justicia miniata, commonly known as the dwarf flame flower, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 cm, making it a compact and ornamental choice for gardens. Its foliage consists of opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture.

Justicia miniata thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
