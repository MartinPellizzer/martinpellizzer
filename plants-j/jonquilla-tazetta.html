Jonquilla tazetta

Jonquilla tazetta, commonly known as the Spanish daffodil, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. Its foliage consists of strap-like, grassy leaves that emerge from the base of the plant.
