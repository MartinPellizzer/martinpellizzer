Jurinea alpigena

Jurinea alpigena, commonly known as the alpine sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to alpine regions of Europe and Asia.

It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to cold, high-altitude environments. The plant has small, clustered leaves and produces delicate, white or pale pink flowers. It is often found in mountain meadows and scree slopes.

Jurinea alpigena plays a role in stabilizing soil in harsh environments and is a characteristic component of alpine flora.
