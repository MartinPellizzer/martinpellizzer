Justicia sanguinolenta

Justicia sanguinolenta, commonly known as the red flame plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking red bracts that resemble flowers, which are actually modified leaves. The plant produces small, tubular white flowers that are clustered in spikes, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Justicia sanguinolenta is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its vibrant color and ease of care.
