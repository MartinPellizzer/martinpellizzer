Justicia uvida

Justicia uvida, commonly known as the red-flowered justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red or pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often used in ornamental gardening for its vibrant blooms.

Justicia uvida is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as butterflies and hummingbirds.
