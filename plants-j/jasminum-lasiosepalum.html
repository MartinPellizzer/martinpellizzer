Jasminum lasiosepalum

Jasminum lasiosepalum, commonly known as the woolly sepaled jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly covering on the sepals, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.

Jasminum lasiosepalum thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with well-drained soil.
