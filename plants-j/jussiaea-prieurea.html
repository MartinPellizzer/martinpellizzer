Jussiaea prieurea

Jussiaea prieurea, commonly known as the water lettuce, is a floating aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It has rosette-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Jussiaea prieurea is often used in ornamental ponds and water gardens due to its aesthetic appeal.
