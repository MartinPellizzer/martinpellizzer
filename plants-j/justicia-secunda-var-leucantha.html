Justicia secunda var. leucantha

Justicia secunda var. leucantha, commonly known as white flame flower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered with fine hairs.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
