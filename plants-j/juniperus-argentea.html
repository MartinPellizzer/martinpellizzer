Juniperus argentea

Juniperus argentea, commonly known as silver juniper, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, berry-like cones that are typically blue or black in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Juniperus argentea is also valued for its aromatic properties and historical use in herbal medicine.
