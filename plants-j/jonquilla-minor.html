Jonquilla minor

Jonquilla minor, commonly known as the dwarf daffodil, is a species of flowering plant in the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to the Iberian Peninsula and is characterized by its compact, early spring blooming habit. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of bright yellow, trumpet-shaped flowers. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.

Jonquilla minor is relatively hardy and can thrive in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.
