Jovibarba heuffelii f. glabra

Jovibarba heuffelii f. glabra, commonly known as the glabrous stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that store water, enabling it to thrive in arid environments. It is often cultivated in rock gardens due to its hardy nature and aesthetic appeal.
