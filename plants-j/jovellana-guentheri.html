Jovellana guentheri

Jovellana guentheri, commonly known as the Guenther's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pale pink. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, adapting to its humid, shaded habitat.

Jovellana guentheri is prized by horticulturists for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation under the right conditions.
