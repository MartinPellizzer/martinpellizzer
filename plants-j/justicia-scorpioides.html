Justicia scorpioides

Justicia scorpioides, commonly known as redroot false dragonhead, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 feet. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its showy, tubular flowers range in color from red to pink and bloom in late summer.

Justicia scorpioides is often found in moist, sandy soils along stream banks and in open woods.
