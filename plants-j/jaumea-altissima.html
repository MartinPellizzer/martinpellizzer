Jaumea altissima

Jaumea altissima, commonly known as the tall jaumea, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Asphodelaceae and is characterized by its upright, grass-like growth habit. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces tall spikes of small, white to pale yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is often cultivated in arid regions.
