Jambosa tetraquetra

Jambosa tetraquetra, commonly known as the Natal plum, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It produces small, round fruits that are typically red or yellow when ripe, with a sweet and tangy flavor. The plant has simple, oval-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

The Natal plum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and edible fruits.
