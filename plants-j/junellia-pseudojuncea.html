Junellia pseudojuncea

Junellia pseudojuncea, commonly known as the narrowleaf junellia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. Junellia pseudojuncea is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing habitat and nectar for pollinators.
