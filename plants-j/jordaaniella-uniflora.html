Jordaaniella uniflora

Jordaaniella uniflora, commonly known as the single-flower sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Its leaves are fleshy and succulent, adapted to store water in its native dry environment.

Jordaaniella uniflora is often found in sandy or rocky soils and is well-suited to xerophytic conditions.
