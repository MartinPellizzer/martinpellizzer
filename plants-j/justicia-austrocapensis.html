Justicia austrocapensis

Justicia austrocapensis, commonly known as the Cape rose or African rose, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant pink to red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens in warmer climates.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both horticultural and ecological studies.
