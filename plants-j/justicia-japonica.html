Justicia japonica

Justicia japonica, commonly known as Japanese flame flower, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular red or orange flowers. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with opposite, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Justicia japonica is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
