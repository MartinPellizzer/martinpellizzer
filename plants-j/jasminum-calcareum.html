Jasminum calcareum

Jasminum calcareum, commonly known as the limestone jasmine, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant prefers well-drained, calcareous soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Jasminum calcareum is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and pleasant fragrance.
