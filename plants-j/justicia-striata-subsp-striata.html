Justicia striata subsp. striata

Justicia striata subsp.

striata, commonly known as the narrowleaf false arum, is a flowering plant native to parts of Central and South America. It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, spatulate-shaped leaves and showy, clustered inflorescences.
