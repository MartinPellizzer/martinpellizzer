Juncus microcephalus var. major

Juncus microcephalus var. major, commonly known as the tall rush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetland environments.

It features cylindrical, grass-like leaves that are typically green to bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in marshes, pond edges, and along stream banks.

Its rapid growth and dense foliage make it an important component of wetland ecosystems.
