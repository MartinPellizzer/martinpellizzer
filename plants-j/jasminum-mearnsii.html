Jasminum mearnsii

Jasminum mearnsii, commonly known as the Mearns' jasmine, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are elliptical and glossy, with a smooth texture.

Jasminum mearnsii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
