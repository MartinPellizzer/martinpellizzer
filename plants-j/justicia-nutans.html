Justicia nutans

Justicia nutans, commonly known as the hanging jewelweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its pendulous, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Justicia nutans produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
