Janipha obtusifolia

Janipha obtusifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved Janipha, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves and clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. It is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid conditions.

Janipha obtusifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic features.
