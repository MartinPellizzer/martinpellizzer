Jasminum guangxiense

Jasminum guangxiense, commonly known as Guangxi jasmine, is a flowering plant native to southern China.

It belongs to the Oleaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or vine, with opposite, simple, and usually ovate leaves. Its flowers emit a sweet scent, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
