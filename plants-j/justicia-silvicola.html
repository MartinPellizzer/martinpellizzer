Justicia silvicola

Justicia silvicola, commonly known as the forest justicia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and along stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

Justicia silvicola is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
