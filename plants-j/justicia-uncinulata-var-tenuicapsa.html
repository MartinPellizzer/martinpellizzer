Justicia uncinulata var. tenuicapsa

Justicia uncinulata var. tenuicapsa, commonly known as the narrow-capsule false dragonhead, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its square, hairy stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, wind-dispersed seeds.
