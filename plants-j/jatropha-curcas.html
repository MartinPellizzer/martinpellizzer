Jatropha curcas

Jatropha curcas, commonly known as the physic nut, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and toxic, milky sap containing latex. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow fruits that contain seeds used for oil extraction. Its leaves are alternate and ovate, often with a rough texture.

Jatropha curcas is also known for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions, making it a hardy species.
