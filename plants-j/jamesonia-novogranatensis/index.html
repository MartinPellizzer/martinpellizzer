Jamesonia Novogranatensis

Jamesonia novogranatensis is a rare and striking plant native to the cloud forests of Colombia. It is known for its vibrant, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of red and orange. This species thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific soil conditions to flourish. Due to habitat loss, it is now considered endangered and is the subject of conservation efforts. Its unique beauty and ecological importance make it a fascinating subject for botanical study.
