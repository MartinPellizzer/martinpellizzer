Justicia ceylanica

Justicia ceylanica, commonly known as the Ceylon flame flower, is a striking tropical plant native to Sri Lanka.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, attracting pollinators like hummingbirds and bees. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and slightly hairy. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its growth habit is typically shrub-like, with a compact form suitable for garden beds or containers.
