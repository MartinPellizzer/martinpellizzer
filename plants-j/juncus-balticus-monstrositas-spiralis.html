Juncus balticus monstrositas-spiralis

Juncus balticus monstrositas-spiralis, commonly known as the spiral spikerush, is a striking ornamental plant native to Europe.

It features spirally twisted, grass-like leaves that create a unique, sculptural appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, inconspicuous greenish flowers at the tips of its stems. It thrives in wet or moist soil conditions, making it suitable for bog gardens or water features.

Its unusual morphology makes it a popular choice for modern landscape designs.
