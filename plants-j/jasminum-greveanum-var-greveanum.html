Jasminum greveanum var. greveanum

Jasminum greveanum var. greveanum, commonly known as the Chinese jasmine, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer seasons. The plant has slender, woody stems and dark green, oval-shaped leaves. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.

This variety is relatively hardy and can thrive in a variety of soil types and light conditions.
