Jurinea ramosissima var. sarhaddensis

Jurinea ramosissima var. sarhaddensis, commonly known as the Sarhad jurinea, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of South Asia.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming structure with dense, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is well adapted to dry, sandy soils and can tolerate high levels of sunlight.

This variety is often found in semi-desert and steppe environments, showcasing its resilience to harsh climatic conditions.
