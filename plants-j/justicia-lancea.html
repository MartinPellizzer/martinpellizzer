Justicia lancea

Justicia lancea, commonly known as the lanceleaf false heather, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking foliage and blooms.
