Jasminum obovatum

Jasminum obovatum, commonly known as the oval-leaf jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters, often releasing a sweet scent at night. The plant has oval-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

Jasminum obovatum is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
