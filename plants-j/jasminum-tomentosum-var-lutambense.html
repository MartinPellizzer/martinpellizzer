Jasminum tomentosum var. lutambense

Jasminum tomentosum var. lutambense, commonly known as the Lutambense jasmin, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its pleasant scent and evergreen nature.
