Juglans × quadrangulata

Juglans × quadrangulata, commonly known as the Chinese walnut hybrid, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is a hybrid between Juglans mandshurica and Juglans regia, exhibiting characteristics of both parent species. The tree produces large, edible nuts enclosed in a thick, woody husk. Its leaves are palmately compound, with four to six leaflets, and it thrives in temperate climates.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and as a source of high-quality nuts.
