Juncus triglumis f. acutiusculus

Juncus triglumis f. acutiusculus, commonly known as the sharp-leaved rush, is a flowering plant belonging to the Juncaceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its dense, cylindrical clusters of grass-like leaves that are typically narrow and pointed. The plant produces small, greenish flowers arranged in spike-like inflorescences, which are often clustered at the tips of the stems. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is commonly found in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of ponds.

This plant plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various small organisms.
