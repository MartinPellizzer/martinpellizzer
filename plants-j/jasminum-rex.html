Jasminum rex

Jasminum rex, commonly known as the Royal Jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has opposite, simple, and usually hairy leaves that are oval to elliptic in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Jasminum rex is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.
