Justicia prevostiae

Justicia prevostiae, commonly known as the velvetleaf false arum, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces showy, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by colorful bracts, often in shades of red or purple. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
