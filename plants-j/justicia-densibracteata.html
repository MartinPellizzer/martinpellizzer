Justicia densibracteata

Justicia densibracteata, commonly known as the velvetleaf justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its dense, bract-like leaves that give it its scientific name. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in moist, sunny environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
