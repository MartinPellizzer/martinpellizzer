Jasminum velutinum

Jasminum velutinum, commonly known as the velvet jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are typically oval in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Jasminum velutinum is also used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
