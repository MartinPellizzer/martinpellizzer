Jasminum smalianum

Jasminum smalianum, commonly known as the Philippine jasmine, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, often emitting a strong, sweet scent. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and lanceolate in shape, with a glossy green surface.

Jasminum smalianum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
