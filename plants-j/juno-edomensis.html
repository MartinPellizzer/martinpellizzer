Juno edomensis

It is characterized by its striking violet-blue petals and silver-green foliage, which reflect its adaptation to cold, nutrient-poor environments. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers during the summer months, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its root system is highly specialized, allowing it to absorb moisture from the thin, rocky soil.
