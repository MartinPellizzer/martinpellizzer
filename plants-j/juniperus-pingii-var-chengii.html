Juniperus pingii var. chengii

Juniperus pingii var. chengii, commonly known as the Yunnan juniper, is a rare and endemic species native to China.

The plant features scale-like leaves arranged in dense, compact clusters, and it produces small, berry-like cones that are typically blue or black in color.
