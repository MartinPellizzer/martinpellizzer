Joseanthus sparrei

Joseanthus sparrei, commonly known as the silverleaf phlox, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It features upright, branching stems covered in silvery-gray hairs, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy clusters of pink to lavender flowers in late spring to early summer. Its foliage is aromatic and often used in herbal remedies.

Joseanthus sparrei thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
