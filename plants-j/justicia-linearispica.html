Justicia linearispica

Justicia linearispica, commonly known as the narrow-leaved flame flower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.
