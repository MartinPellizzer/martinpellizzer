Jasminum Azoricum Var. Travancorense

Jasminum azoricum var. travancorense is a rare and beautiful plant native to the western ghats of india. it is known for its fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring and summer. this variety is often found in moist, shaded environments along forest floors. it has delicate leaves and a climbing habit, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. due to its limited distribution, it is considered a valuable species for conservation efforts.
