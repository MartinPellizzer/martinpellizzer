Jaumeopsis mimuloides

Jaumeopsis mimuloides, commonly known as the mimuloides orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and white. The plant has slender, upright stems and produces small, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
