Juncus effusus f. utilis

Juncus effusus f. utilis, commonly known as the useful soft rush, is a flowering plant belonging to the Juncaceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and slender, cylindrical stems. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy environments, often forming dense mats that help stabilize soil. Its inflorescence consists of spikes of small, three-lobed flowers that bloom in early summer.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in wetland restoration projects.
