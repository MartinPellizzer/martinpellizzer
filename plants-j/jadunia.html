Jadunia

Jadunia, commonly known as the "flower of the desert," is a rare and resilient plant native to arid regions of South Asia.

The plant thrives in sandy soils and can withstand extreme temperatures and limited water availability. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, adapted to reduce water loss.
