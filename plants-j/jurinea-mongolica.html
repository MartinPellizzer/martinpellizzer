Jurinea mongolica

Jurinea mongolica, commonly known as the Mongolian sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and showy yellow flowers. Jurinea mongolica is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological resilience in extreme climates.
