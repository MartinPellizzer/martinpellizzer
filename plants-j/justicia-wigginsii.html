Justicia wigginsii

Justicia wigginsii, commonly known as Wiggins' flamebeard, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, sandy soils and thrives in partial to full sunlight. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are upright and branching.
