Justicia martiana

Justicia martiana, commonly known as the flame flower, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a reddish or purple hue.

Justicia martiana thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
