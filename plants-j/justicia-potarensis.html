Justicia potarensis

Justicia potarensis, commonly known as the red-flowered justicia, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in a fine, silvery down.

Justicia potarensis thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
