Jasminum catalonicum

Jasminum catalonicum, commonly known as the Catalonian jasmine, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is a deciduous shrub that produces fragrant white flowers during the spring and summer months. The plant has simple, opposite leaves and is characterized by its strong, pleasant aroma. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its showy blooms and aromatic properties.
