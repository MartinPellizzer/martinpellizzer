Jasminum kerstingii

Jasminum kerstingii, commonly known as the Kersting's jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters, often emitting a strong, sweet scent. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

Jasminum kerstingii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is cultivated for its aromatic flowers and unique botanical features.
