Jasminum quadrifolium

Jasminum quadrifolium, commonly known as the four-leafed jasmine, is a climbing shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white, four-petaled flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with four lobes, giving it its common name. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
