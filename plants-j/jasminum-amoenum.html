Jasminum amoenum

Jasminum amoenum, commonly known as the Chinese jasmine, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer seasons. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

Jasminum amoenum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its pleasant fragrance.
