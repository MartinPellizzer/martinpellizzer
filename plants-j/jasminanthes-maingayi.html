Jasminanthes maingayi

Jasminanthes maingayi, commonly known as the Philippine jasmine, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Oleaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate, with a smooth texture.
