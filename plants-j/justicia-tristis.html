Justicia tristis

Justicia tristis, commonly known as the weeping justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet, featuring slender, branching stems. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are usually blue or purple in color, arranged in spikes. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a rough texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Justicia tristis thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its attractive foliage and blooms.
