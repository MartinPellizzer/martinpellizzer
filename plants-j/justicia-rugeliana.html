Justicia rugeliana

Justicia rugeliana, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. The plant features broad, velvety leaves that are often a deep green color. It produces clusters of bright purple or pink flowers that bloom throughout the year under proper care.

Justicia rugeliana is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
