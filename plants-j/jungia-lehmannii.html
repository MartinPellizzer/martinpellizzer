Jungia lehmannii

Jungia lehmannii, commonly known as the Lehmann's jungle grass, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its robust, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish to brownish composite flowers that are clustered in dense, spherical heads. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, with a slightly hairy texture.

Jungia lehmannii is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.
