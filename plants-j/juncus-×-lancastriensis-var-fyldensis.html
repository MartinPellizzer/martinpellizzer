Juncus × lancastriensis var. fyldensis

Juncus × lancastriensis var. fyldensis, commonly known as the Fylden rush, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Juncaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems that are typically green to reddish in color. The plant produces small, clustered spikelets at the top of the stems, which contain the reproductive structures. It thrives in wet or marshy environments, often found in coastal areas or along tidal flats.

This variety is noted for its adaptability to brackish conditions, making it a valuable species in ecological restoration projects.
