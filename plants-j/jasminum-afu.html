Jasminum afu

Jasminum afu, commonly known as the Afu jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white or yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a glossy surface.

The Afu jasmine is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its pleasant scent.
