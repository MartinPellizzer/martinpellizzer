Jambolifera

Jambolifera, commonly known as the Java apple tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically red or yellow when ripe. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptical in shape, with a smooth margin.

The tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and the edible fruits it bears.
