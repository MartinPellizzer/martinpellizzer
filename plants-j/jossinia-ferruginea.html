Jossinia ferruginea

Jossinia ferruginea, commonly known as the rusty jossinia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

Jossinia ferruginea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses due to its vibrant blooms and unique foliage.
