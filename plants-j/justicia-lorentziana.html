Justicia lorentziana

Justicia lorentziana, commonly known as the flame flower, is a striking tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange to red tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting hummingbirds and other pollinators. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Justicia lorentziana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its showy blossoms and adaptability to container gardening.
