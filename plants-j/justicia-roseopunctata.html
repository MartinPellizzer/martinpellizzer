Justicia roseopunctata

Justicia roseopunctata, commonly known as the spotted flamebeard, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
