Jasminum evansii

Jasminum evansii, commonly known as the Chinese jasmine or evergreen jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Jasminum evansii is a semi-evergreen shrub that can grow up to 3 meters tall.
