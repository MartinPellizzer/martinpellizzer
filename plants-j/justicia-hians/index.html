Justicia Hians

Blue mistflower, scientifically known as Justicia hians, is a strikingly beautiful plant native to the southeastern United States. It is prized for its delicate, blue-gray foliage and clusters of soft, mist-like blooms that appear in late summer. This perennial shrub thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands, stream banks, and forest edges. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for gardeners looking to add a touch of elegance to their landscapes. With its ability to attract pollinators and its adaptability to various growing conditions, Justicia hians is both a valuable ecological asset and an ornamental gem.
