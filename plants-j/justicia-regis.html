Justicia regis

Justicia regis, commonly known as the royal justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

Justicia regis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
