Julocroton quinquenervius

Julocroton quinquenervius, commonly known as the five-veined croton, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with leaves that often display shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or green, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant appearance.

Julocroton quinquenervius is also valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
