Justicia ecbolium var. laetevirens

Justicia ecbolium var. laetevirens, commonly known as the golden flame plant, is a flowering shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to gardens. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for tropical and subtropical landscapes.
