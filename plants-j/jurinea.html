Jurinea

Jurinea, commonly known as sea holly, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

These plants are typically found in coastal areas and are adapted to saline and sandy soils. They have distinctive silver-gray foliage and produce small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in the summer. Jurinea species are often used in coastal landscaping due to their hardiness and aesthetic appeal.
