Jacquemontia pauciflora

Jacquemontia pauciflora, commonly known as the few-flowered morning glory, is a herbaceous perennial native to South America.

It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is characterized by its vine-like growth habit. The plant produces small, pale yellow or white flowers that bloom in the early morning. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in a fine, hairy texture.
