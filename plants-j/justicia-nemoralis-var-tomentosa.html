Justicia nemoralis var. tomentosa

Justicia nemoralis var. tomentosa, commonly known as the woolly flamebeard, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a soft, fuzzy appearance. The plant produces striking red or orange tubular flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators like bees and birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value lies in its vibrant blooms and unique texture.
