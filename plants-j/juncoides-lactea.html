Juncoides lactea

Juncoides lactea, commonly known as the milkweed juncea, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Juncaginaceae and is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and small, white or pale yellow flowers. Juncoides lactea is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
