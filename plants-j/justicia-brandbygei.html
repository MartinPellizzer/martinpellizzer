Justicia brandbygei

Justicia brandbygei, commonly known as the Brandbygei red hot poker, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking spikes of red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, tubular flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and birds. Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant color and drought tolerance.
