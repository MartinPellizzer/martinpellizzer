Justicia espiritosantensis

Justicia espiritosantensis, commonly known as the sacred justicia, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.
