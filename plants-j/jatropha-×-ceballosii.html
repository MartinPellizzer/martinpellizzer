Jatropha × ceballosii

Jatropha × ceballosii, commonly known as the hybrid jatropha, is a flowering plant in the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Jatropha curcas and Jatropha multifida, exhibiting traits from both parent species. The plant produces striking yellow-orange flowers with prominent red sepals, and its leaves are typically alternate and ovate. It is native to regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates.

Jatropha × ceballosii is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.
