Justicia bitarkarae

Justicia bitarkarae, commonly known as the Malabar flame flower, is a shrub native to the Western Ghats of India.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red and orange tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in forested areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
