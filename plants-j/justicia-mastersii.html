Justicia mastersii

Justicia mastersii, commonly known as the flame bush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as birds and insects. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are usually arranged opposite each other on the stems. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for coastal and Mediterranean climates.

Justicia mastersii is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
