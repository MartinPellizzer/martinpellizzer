Jasminum × xizangense

Jasminum × xizangense, commonly known as the Yunnan jasmine, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Oleaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is evergreen, with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in humid, temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its flowers are used in traditional Chinese medicine and as a source of essential oils.
