Justicia prominens

Justicia prominens, commonly known as the showy justicia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered with a fine layer of white hairs.

Justicia prominens thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
