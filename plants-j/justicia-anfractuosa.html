Justicia anfractuosa

Justicia anfractuosa, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzagging stems and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Justicia anfractuosa is popular as a ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
