Justicia densiflora

Justicia densiflora, commonly known as the denseflower justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height, characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with hairy surfaces. The plant produces clusters of showy, tubular flowers in shades of pink, red, or white, which bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Due to its attractive flowers and adaptability, it is often cultivated for both its aesthetic value and ecological benefits.
