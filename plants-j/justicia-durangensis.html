Justicia durangensis

Justicia durangensis, commonly known as the Durango flame flower, is a herbaceous perennial native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage is lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

It is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and is used in landscaping and horticultural displays.
