Jasminum suavissimum

Jasminum suavissimum, commonly known as sweet jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a smooth, glossy surface.

The flowers are highly aromatic, often used in perfumes and traditional medicine.
