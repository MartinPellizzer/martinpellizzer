Justicia polystachya

Justicia polystachya, commonly known as the red hot poker, is a striking perennial plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its tall, spike-like inflorescences that bear vibrant red or orange tubular flowers. The plant features lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern at the base. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Botanically, it belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is known for its showy, long-lasting blooms.
