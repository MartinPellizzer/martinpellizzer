Jambosa rubella

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers. The plant produces edible fruits that are similar in appearance to persimmons, with a sweet and tangy flavor. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a smooth, glossy texture.
