Jamesonia hispidula

Jamesonia hispidula, commonly known as the roughleaf bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its hairy, rough-textured leaves and bell-shaped flowers.
