Justicia paolii

Justicia paolii, commonly known as the velvetleaf justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to purple. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a velvety texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Justicia paolii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
