Josemania Truncata Var. Major

Red hot poker, scientifically known as Josemania truncata var. major, is a striking perennial plant native to South America. It is prized for its bold, fiery orange to red flower spikes that stand tall above its narrow, grass-like leaves. This hardy plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. Its unique appearance and long-lasting blooms make it a standout addition to any garden. Whether used as a focal point or in mass plantings, red hot poker adds a dramatic and colorful element to the landscape.
