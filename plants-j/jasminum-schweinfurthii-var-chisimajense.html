Jasminum schweinfurthii var. chisimajense

Jasminum schweinfurthii var. chisimajense, commonly known as the Chisimae jasmine, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a glossy green appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or savanna habitats.
