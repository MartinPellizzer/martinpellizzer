Justicia ischnorhachis

Justicia ischnorhachis, commonly known as the red flame plant, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It features showy, tubular red flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators like hummingbirds and bees. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
