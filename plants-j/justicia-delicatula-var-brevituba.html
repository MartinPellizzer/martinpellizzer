Justicia delicatula var. brevituba

Justicia delicatula var. brevituba, commonly known as the narrow tube justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its unique floral structure attracts pollinators such as bees and hummingbirds.
