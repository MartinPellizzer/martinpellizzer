Jasione maritima

Jasione maritima, commonly known as sea holly, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of Europe and North Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils along the shorelines. The plant features narrow, gray-green leaves and produces striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Jasione maritima is well adapted to salty and windy environments, making it a hardy species.

Its ecological importance includes providing nectar for pollinators and stabilizing coastal dunes.
