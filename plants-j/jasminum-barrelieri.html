Jasminum barrelieri

Jasminum barrelieri, commonly known as the Madagascar jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is a deciduous shrub that produces fragrant white flowers with a strong, sweet scent. The plant has slender, woody stems and dark green, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The flowers bloom in clusters and are typically found in the spring and summer months.
