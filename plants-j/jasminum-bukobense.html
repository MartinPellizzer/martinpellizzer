Jasminum bukobense

Jasminum bukobense, commonly known as Bukoban jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Tanzania.

It belongs to the Oleaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a lanceolate shape with a smooth margin.

The flowers are often used in traditional medicine and for ornamental purposes due to their pleasant scent.
