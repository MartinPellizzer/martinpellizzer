Juncus decipiens subsp. medianus

Juncus decipiens subsp.

medianus, commonly known as the intermediate rush, is a perennial plant belonging to the Juncaceae family. It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems that are typically green to reddish in color.

The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in wet or moist environments such as marshes, swamps, and along the edges of ponds.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to aquatic and semi-aquatic habitats.
