Jasminum lanceifolium

Jasminum lanceifolium, commonly known as the lanceleaf jasmine, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and fragrant white flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its flowers are often used in traditional medicine and as a source of essential oils.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
