Justicia diacantha

Justicia diacantha, commonly known as the flame flower or red flame, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters tall, characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and clusters of bright red, tubular flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its striking appearance and adaptability contribute to its widespread cultivation in warm climates.
