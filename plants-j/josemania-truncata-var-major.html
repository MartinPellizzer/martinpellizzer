Josemania truncata var. major

Josemania truncata var. major, commonly known as the giant red torch plant, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, velvety, red bracts that surround small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is often cultivated for its vibrant, long-lasting foliage and is popular in tropical gardens.

Its unique appearance makes it a desirable addition to both indoor and outdoor landscapes.
