Jasminum humile var. siderophyllum

Jasminum humile var. siderophyllum, commonly known as the silverleaf jasmine, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves that are slightly hairy underneath, giving it a silvery appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate sunlight to flourish.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant scent.
