Justicia riojana

Justicia riojana, commonly known as the red hot poker, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its tall, spike-like inflorescences that bear vibrant red or orange tubular flowers. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes. Its foliage is lance-shaped and often has a slightly hairy texture.

Justicia riojana is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and hummingbirds.
