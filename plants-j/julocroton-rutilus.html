Julocroton rutilus

Julocroton rutilus, commonly known as the silver croton, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, multicolored leaves that range from bright green to yellow, orange, and red, creating a visually dynamic appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Julocroton rutilus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
