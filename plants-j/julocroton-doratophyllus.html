Julocroton doratophyllus

Julocroton doratophyllus, commonly known as the golden croton, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, variegated leaves that display shades of yellow, green, and red, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which includes many other spiny and milky-juiced plants. Julocroton doratophyllus thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, requiring moderate watering to prevent root rot.

Its unique foliage and adaptability make it a favored plant among horticulturists and plant enthusiasts.
