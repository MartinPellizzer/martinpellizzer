Jovetia humilis

Jovetia humilis, commonly known as the dwarf pandanus, is a small shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia and the Pacific Islands.

It belongs to the family Pandanaceae and is characterized by its narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional crafts and as a culinary herb. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils and salt spray. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually yellow or green, followed by edible, fleshy berries.

Jovetia humilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and practical uses in local cultures.
