Jasminum humile var. glabrum

Jasminum humile var. glabrum, commonly known as the common jasmine, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during late spring to early summer. The plant has simple, oval-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. It is well-suited for container gardening or as a ground cover due to its low growth habit.

Its aromatic foliage and flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental purposes.
