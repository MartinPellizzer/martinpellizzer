Justicia hedrenii

Justicia hedrenii, commonly known as the purple flame or rainbow plant, is a striking member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to red and yellow. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Justicia hedrenii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
