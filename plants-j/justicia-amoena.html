Justicia amoena

Justicia amoena, commonly known as the red fountain plant, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking red or pink tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are arranged oppositely along the stem. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Justicia amoena is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a container plant.
