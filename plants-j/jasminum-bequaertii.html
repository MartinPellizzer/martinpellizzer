Jasminum bequaertii

Jasminum bequaertii, commonly known as the Chinese jasmine, is a flowering plant native to China and Vietnam.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate with a smooth margin.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
