Jurinea baldschuanica

Jurinea baldschuanica, commonly known as the Caucasian sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with small, white or pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain water in its arid habitat. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in alpine and steppe environments.

Jurinea baldschuanica is adapted to withstand harsh climatic conditions, including strong winds and temperature fluctuations.
