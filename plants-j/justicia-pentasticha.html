Justicia pentasticha

Justicia pentasticha, commonly known as the flame flower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture with a glossy appearance.

The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
