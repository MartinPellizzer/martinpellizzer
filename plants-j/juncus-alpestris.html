Juncus alpestris

Juncus alpestris, commonly known as the alpine rush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to temperate and alpine regions.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, stream banks, and rocky slopes. The plant has slender, grass-like leaves that are usually dark green and slightly glossy. Its cylindrical, spike-like inflorescence bears small, pale green to brown flowers.

Juncus alpestris is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in challenging environmental conditions.
