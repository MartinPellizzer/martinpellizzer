Jasminum cordatulum

Jasminum cordatulum, commonly known as the heart-shaped jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for gardens and indoor cultivation.

Its delicate appearance and pleasant aroma make it a popular choice among horticulturists and plant enthusiasts.
