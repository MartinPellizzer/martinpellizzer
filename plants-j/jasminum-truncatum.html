Jasminum truncatum

Jasminum truncatum, commonly known as the dwarf jasmine or shrub jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Jasminum truncatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.
