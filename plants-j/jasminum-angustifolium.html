Jasminum angustifolium

Jasminum angustifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved jasmine, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its slender, aromatic flowers that typically bloom in clusters. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and is often found in moist, shaded environments. It is valued for its fragrant blossoms, which are used in perfumery and traditional medicine.

Jasminum angustifolium is also a popular ornamental plant due to its beauty and scent.
