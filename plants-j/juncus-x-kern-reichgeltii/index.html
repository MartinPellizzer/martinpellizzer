Juncus × Kern-Reichgeltii

Kern-reichgelt's rush, scientifically known as Juncus × kern-reichgeltii, is a unique hybrid plant native to the wetlands of California. It is a member of the Juncaceae family and is characterized by its tall, grass-like stems and clusters of small, spike-like flowers. This plant thrives in moist to wet environments, often found in marshes, stream banks, and pond edges. Its hybrid origin makes it a fascinating subject for botanists studying plant evolution and adaptation. Due to its rarity and ecological significance, it is also of interest for conservation efforts.
