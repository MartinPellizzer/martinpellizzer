Jussiaea suffruticosa var. villosa

Jussiaea suffruticosa var. villosa, commonly known as the hairy jussiaea, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub characterized by its woody stems and dense, hairy foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in moist, tropical environments and is often found near water bodies.

Its hairy leaves and compact growth habit make it distinctive within its genus.
