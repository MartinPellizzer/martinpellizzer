Justicia acaulis var. lyrata

Justicia acaulis var. lyrata, commonly known as the lyrata false heather, is a compact, low-growing shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically blue to purple in color, arranged in whorls along the stems. The plant has narrow, strap-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a textured appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant blooms and compact form, which make it suitable for both ornamental and drought-tolerant landscaping.
