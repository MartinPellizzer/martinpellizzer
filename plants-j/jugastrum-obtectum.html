Jugastrum obtectum

Jugastrum obtectum, commonly known as the smooth juglone tree, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pod-like fruits. The plant produces a toxic compound called juglone, which can inhibit the growth of other plants in its vicinity. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with well-drained soil.

Due to its ecological impact and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and ecologists.
