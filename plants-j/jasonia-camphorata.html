Jasonia camphorata

Jasonia camphorata, commonly known as the camphor tree, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic leaves that emit a camphor scent when crushed. The plant has small, white, five-petaled flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by winged fruits. Its bark is smooth and grayish, with a distinctive aromatic resin.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and medicinal properties.
