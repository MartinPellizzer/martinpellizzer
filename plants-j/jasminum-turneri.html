Jasminum turneri

Jasminum turneri, commonly known as the Turnera jasmine, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, often emitting a sweet, honey-like scent. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and lance-shaped, with a glossy green surface.

Jasminum turneri is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
