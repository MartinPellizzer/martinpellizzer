Justicia sanguinea

Justicia sanguinea, commonly known as the red flame plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red or orange tubular flowers. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Justicia sanguinea is typically grown as a perennial in USDA hardiness zones 9 through 11.
