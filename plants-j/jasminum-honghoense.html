Jasminum honghoense

Jasminum honghoense, commonly known as Hong Ho jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a glossy surface.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation.
