Jateorhiza columba

Jateorhiza columba, commonly known as the blue flag iris, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of ponds and streams. Its leaves are sword-shaped and emerge from a rhizomatous root system.

Jateorhiza columba is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
