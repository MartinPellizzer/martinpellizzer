Justicia congestiflora

Justicia congestiflora, commonly known as the clustered flamebush, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking clusters of tubular, red or orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Justicia congestiflora is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees.
