Justicia metallorum

Justicia metallorum, commonly known as the silverleaf justicia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage. The plant produces showy spikes of blue or purple flowers, which bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is complemented by its ability to attract pollinators such as butterflies and bees.
