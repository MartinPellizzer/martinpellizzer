Jambosa rubens

Jambosa rubens, commonly known as the red jambosa, is a tropical plant native to parts of South America.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves and striking red or purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces edible fruits that are similar in appearance to grapes, with a sweet and tangy flavor. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Jambosa rubens is often cultivated for both its ornamental value and its fruit.
