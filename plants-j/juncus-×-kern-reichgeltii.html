Juncus × kern-reichgeltii

Juncus × kern-reichgeltii, commonly known as the Kern-Reichgelt's rush, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Juncaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems that are typically green to reddish in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, three-lobed flowers. It thrives in moist to wet environments, such as marshes and pond edges.

Its morphology reflects a combination of its parent species, Juncus effusus and Juncus roepartii.
