Jasminum beesianum var. ulotrichum

Jasminum beesianum var. ulotrichum, commonly known as the African jasmine, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves and a climbing or shrub-like growth habit. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and indoor cultivation.
