Juruasia rotundata

Juruasia rotundata, commonly known as the rounded leaf orchid, is a rare and exotic plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are round in shape, providing a unique visual appeal. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom during the wet season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
