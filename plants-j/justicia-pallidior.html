Justicia pallidior

Justicia pallidior, commonly known as the pale justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.
