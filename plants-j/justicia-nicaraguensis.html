Justicia nicaraguensis

Justicia nicaraguensis, commonly known as the Nicaragua flame flower, is a striking tropical plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red or orange tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses for its eye-catching floral display.
