Jordania thymoides

Jordania thymoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved thyme, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant features small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink or purple. Its leaves are narrow and often used in culinary applications due to their aromatic properties.
