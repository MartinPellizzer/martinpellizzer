Justicia formosa

Justicia formosa, commonly known as the flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a contrasting throat. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and have a strong aromatic scent. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Justicia formosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
