Justicia roseobracteata

Justicia roseobracteata, commonly known as the red-bract justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant features vibrant red bracts that encase clusters of small, tubular flowers, which are typically pink to purple in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes in suitable climates.
