Jasminum hildebrandtii

Jasminum hildebrandtii, commonly known as the Hildebrandt's jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

Due to its unique appearance and fragrance, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and for ornamental purposes.
