Justicia bremekampii

Justicia bremekampii, commonly known as the Bremekamp's flame flower, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface, and it thrives in warm, humid environments with well-drained soil.

It is often cultivated for its showy blossoms and can be grown in containers or as part of a tropical garden.
