Justicia henricksonii

Justicia henricksonii, commonly known as the Mexican flamebush, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in clusters, often displaying vibrant red or orange hues. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
