Javorkaea pulcherrima

Javorkaea pulcherrima, commonly known as the beautiful orchid, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically display vibrant colors such as pink, red, and white. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually broad and lance-shaped, providing a lush green backdrop to its floral display.

Javorkaea pulcherrima thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil for optimal growth.
