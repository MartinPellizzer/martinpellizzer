Julocroton camporum

Julocroton camporum, commonly known as the Camporum croton, is a shrub native to parts of Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which includes many plants with milky sap. The plant features palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its small, greenish-yellow flowers are clustered in inflorescences, and it produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

The plant is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
