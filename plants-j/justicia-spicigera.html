Justicia spicigera

Justicia spicigera, commonly known as redroot or redroot false dragonhead, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces spikes of bright red or orange tubular flowers. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Justicia spicigera is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and is also known for its medicinal properties.
