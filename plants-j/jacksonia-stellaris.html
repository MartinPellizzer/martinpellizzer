Jacksonia stellaris

Jacksonia stellaris, commonly known as the starry jacksonia, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the daisy family (Asteraceae) and is characterized by its showy, star-shaped yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Jacksonia stellaris is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
