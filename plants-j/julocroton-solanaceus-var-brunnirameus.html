Julocroton solanaceus var. brunnirameus

Julocroton solanaceus var. brunnirameus, commonly known as the brown-stemmed croton, is a variant of the croton plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its distinctive brownish stems and large, palmately lobed leaves that can vary in color from green to reddish hues. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, followed by berry-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Due to its unique appearance, it is a popular choice for gardeners seeking a striking tropical accent.
