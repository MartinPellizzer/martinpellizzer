Jasminum bhumibolianum

Jasminum bhumibolianum, commonly known as the Thai jasmine or Bhumibol jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves and a woody stem, typical of the Oleaceae family. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments.

Jasminum bhumibolianum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
