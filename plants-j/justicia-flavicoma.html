Justicia flavicoma

Justicia flavicoma, commonly known as the yellow-flowered justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, yellow or orange flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

The flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, making it an attractive ornamental plant in tropical gardens.
