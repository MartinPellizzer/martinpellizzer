Justicia rhodoides

Justicia rhodoides, commonly known as the red-tipped justicia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its showy inflorescences consist of clusters of tubular red or pink flowers.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
