Justicia imlayae

Justicia imlayae, commonly known as the Imlay's flame flower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy surface.

Justicia imlayae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
