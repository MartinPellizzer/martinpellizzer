Justicia paxiana

Justicia paxiana, commonly known as the flame violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. Justicia paxiana thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
