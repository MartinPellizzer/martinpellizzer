Justicia monachinoi

Justicia monachinoi, commonly known as the Mexican flame flower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Justicia monachinoi thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for tropical gardens.
