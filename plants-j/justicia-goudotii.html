Justicia goudotii

Justicia goudotii, commonly known as the flame violet, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. Justicia goudotii thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
