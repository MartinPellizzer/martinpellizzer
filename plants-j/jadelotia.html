Jadelotia

Jadelotia, commonly known as the glassy jade plant, is a member of the Apocynaceae family.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are often a vibrant green or reddish hue. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. Native to tropical regions, it thrives in warm, sunny environments with well-drained soil.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
