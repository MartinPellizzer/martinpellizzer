Justicia dianthera var. reisensis

Justicia dianthera var. reisensis, commonly known as the Reisens Island justicia, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. This variant is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
