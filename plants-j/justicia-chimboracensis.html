Justicia chimboracensis

Justicia chimboracensis, commonly known as the flame flower, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces vibrant red or orange tubular flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a smooth, glossy surface. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Its striking blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
