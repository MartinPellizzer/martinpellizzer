Justicia unyorensis var. keniensis

Justicia unyorensis var. keniensis, commonly known as the Kenyan flame bush, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking red or orange tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and slightly hairy, while its stems are woody and often branched.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
