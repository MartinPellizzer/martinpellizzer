Justicia orchidea

Justicia orchidea, commonly known as the orchid jewel, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of pink, purple, and white. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces spikes of tubular flowers that attract pollinators like hummingbirds and bees. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Justicia orchidea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
