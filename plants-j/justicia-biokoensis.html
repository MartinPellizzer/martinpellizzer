Justicia biokoensis

Justicia biokoensis, commonly known as the Bioko justicia, is a flowering plant native to the island of Bioko in Equatorial Guinea.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories or along stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture, while its stems are slender and erect.

The species is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
