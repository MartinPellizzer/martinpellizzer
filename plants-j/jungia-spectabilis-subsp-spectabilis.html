Jungia spectabilis subsp. spectabilis

Jungia spectabilis subsp.

spectabilis, commonly known as the showy jungia, is a perennial herb native to tropical regions of South America. It is characterized by its striking, large, showy flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange.

The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded to semi-shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
