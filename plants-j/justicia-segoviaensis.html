Justicia segoviaensis

Justicia segoviaensis, commonly known as Segovia's flameflower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.
