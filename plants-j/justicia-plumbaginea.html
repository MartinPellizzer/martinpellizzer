Justicia plumbaginea

Justicia plumbaginea, commonly known as blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in dense, showy clusters. Justicia plumbaginea is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
