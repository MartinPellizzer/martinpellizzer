Jatropha gossypiifolia var. typica

Jatropha gossypiifolia var. typica, commonly known as the Crown of Thorns, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its thick, succulent leaves and clusters of yellow flowers. The plant produces small, greenish-white fruits that contain toxic seeds. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils.

Its botanical features make it a popular ornamental plant in gardens and landscapes.
