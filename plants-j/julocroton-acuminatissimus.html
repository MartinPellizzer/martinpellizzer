Julocroton acuminatissimus

Julocroton acuminatissimus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically green with a slight pubescent texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires ample sunlight to flourish.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
