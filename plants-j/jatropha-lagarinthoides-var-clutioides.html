Jatropha lagarinthoides var. clutioides

Jatropha lagarinthoides var. clutioides, commonly known as the clustered jatropha, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant produces striking yellow flowers with red stamens, which are often clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a rough texture.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
