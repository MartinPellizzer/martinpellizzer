Jasione crispa var. praelittoralis

Jasione crispa var. praelittoralis, commonly known as the coastal jasione, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of Europe.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with narrow, grass-like leaves and small, pale blue or violet flowers. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in dune environments. It is adapted to withstand salt spray and strong winds typical of coastal habitats.

Its blooming period typically occurs in late spring to early summer.
