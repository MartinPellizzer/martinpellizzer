Julocroton riedelianus

Julocroton riedelianus, commonly known as the Riedel's croton, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Crotonaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that display a range of hues including red, yellow, and green. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments and thrives in well-drained soil. Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening due to its striking appearance.

However, it is important to note that the plant contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
