Juniperus × media var. globosa

Juniperus × media var. globosa, commonly known as the globe juniper, is a compact, spherical shrub native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features dense, needle-like foliage that is typically bluish-green in color, providing year-round visual interest. The plant produces small, berry-like cones that are typically dark blue or purple when mature, adding ornamental value in the winter. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its slow growth and hardy nature.

Juniperus × media var. globosa thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a versatile choice for garden landscapes.
