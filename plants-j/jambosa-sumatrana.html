Jambosa sumatrana

Jambosa sumatrana, commonly known as the Sumatran jambosa, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant produces edible fruits that are similar in appearance to lychees but have a distinct flavor. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Jambosa sumatrana thrives in humid, tropical climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
