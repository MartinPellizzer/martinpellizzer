Justicia Latispica

Justicia latispica is a striking flowering plant native to parts of South America known for its vibrant blooms and unique foliage. It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and clusters of tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. Its ability to thrive in full sun makes it a popular choice for tropical and subtropical regions. Despite its beauty, it requires well-drained soil and regular watering to flourish.
