Juno aucheri

Juno aucheri, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features silvery-white, velvety foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pale blue or violet flowers in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Juno aucheri is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
