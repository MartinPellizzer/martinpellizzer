Justago Cleomoides

Justago cleomoides is a unique flowering plant native to arid regions of South America. It is known for its striking violet-blue flowers and succulent leaves that help it survive in harsh environments. This species belongs to the Cleomaceae family and is often found in rocky soils and dry landscapes. Its ability to store water makes it a hardy plant well adapted to drought conditions. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively unknown to the general public.
