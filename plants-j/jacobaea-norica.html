Jacobaea norica

Jacobaea norica, commonly known as the Alpine groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Jacobaea norica is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in alpine ecosystems.
