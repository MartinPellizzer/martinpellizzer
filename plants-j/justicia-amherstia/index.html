Justicia Amherstia

Amherst's trumpet, scientifically known as Justicia amherstia, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America. With its vibrant orange or red tubular flowers, it captivates gardeners and botanists alike with its unique appearance and ornamental value. This species belongs to the Acanthaceae family, which is known for its diverse and showy members. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ability to add color and interest to gardens. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to soil and light conditions to flourish.
