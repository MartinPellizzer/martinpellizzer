Jasminum vanderystii

Jasminum vanderystii, commonly known as Vanderyst's jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the rainy season. The plant has opposite, simple, and usually hairy leaves, which help it adapt to its tropical environment. It is a shrub that can grow up to 2 meters in height, with woody stems and a dense canopy.

Due to its unique appearance and fragrance, it is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
