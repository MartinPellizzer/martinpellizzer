Jasminum aemulum var. genuinum

Jasminum aemulum var. genuinum, commonly known as the Chinese jasmine or evergreen jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves and a compact, shrub-like growth habit. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.
