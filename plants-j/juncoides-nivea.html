Juncoides nivea

Juncoides nivea, commonly known as the snow grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Juncaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in wetlands or along the edges of streams. It is native to parts of Europe and Asia and is often found in temperate climates.

Juncoides nivea plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
