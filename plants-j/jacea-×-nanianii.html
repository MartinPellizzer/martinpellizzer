Jacea × nanianii

Jacea × nanianii, commonly known as the hybrid bluebell, is a cross between Jacea candida and Jacea nanus.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its striking blue-violet flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows to a height of 10-30 cm and prefers moist, well-drained soil in partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Jacea × nanianii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
