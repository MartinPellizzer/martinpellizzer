Jaltomata umbellata

Jaltomata umbellata, commonly known as the umbrellaleaf jaltomata, is a flowering plant native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, umbrella-like inflorescence composed of numerous small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its foliage is usually dark green with a rough texture, and the flowers range in color from white to pale pink.

Jaltomata umbellata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden environments.
