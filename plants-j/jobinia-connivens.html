Jobinia connivens

Jobinia connivens, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a species of orchid native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, arranged along the stem.

Jobinia connivens thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright light for optimal growth.
