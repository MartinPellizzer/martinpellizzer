Justicia bonneyana

Justicia bonneyana, commonly known as the blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet. The plant produces clusters of small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy texture.

Justicia bonneyana thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and blooms.
