Justicia porphyrocoma

Justicia porphyrocoma, commonly known as the purple flame plant, is a strikingly ornamental shrub native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range from deep purple to red, which bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green, contributing to its visual appeal. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking bold, colorful foliage and blooms.
