Justicia nummulus

Justicia nummulus, commonly known as the coin plant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, round, coin-shaped leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces clusters of tubular, bright orange or yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It is a member of the Acanthaceae family and is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and flowers.

Justicia nummulus prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
