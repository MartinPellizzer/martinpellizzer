Jambosa nervosa

Jambosa nervosa, commonly known as the African blackwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dark, heavy wood and smooth, greyish bark. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a glossy surface. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by small, round fruits.

The plant is valued for its durable wood and is often used in traditional medicine and craftsmanship.
