Jasminum fuchsiifolium

Jasminum fuchsiifolium, commonly known as the purpleleaf jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green to purple-tinged leaves and clusters of fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value comes from both its attractive foliage and its sweet, intoxicating fragrance.
