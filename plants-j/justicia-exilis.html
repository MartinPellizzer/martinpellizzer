Justicia exilis

Justicia exilis, commonly known as the slender flame flower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a member of the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. Justicia exilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
