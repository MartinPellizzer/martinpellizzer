Justicia major

Justicia major, commonly known as the flame flower, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Justicia major is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
