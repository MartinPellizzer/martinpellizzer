Juniperus phoenicea var. lobelii

Juniperus phoenicea var. lobelii, commonly known as the Lobel's juniper, is a shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage composed of scale-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, berry-like cones that are typically dark blue or black when mature. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
