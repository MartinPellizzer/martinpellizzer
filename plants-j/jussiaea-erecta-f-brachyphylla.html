Jussiaea erecta f. brachyphylla

Jussiaea erecta f. brachyphylla, commonly known as the compact jussiaea, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, short-stemmed growth with fleshy, cylindrical leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its compact form and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
