Jacksonia dumosa

Jacksonia dumosa, commonly known as the swamp everlasting, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wet or marshy environments. The plant features showy, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it has a fibrous root system.

Jacksonia dumosa is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in moist conditions.
