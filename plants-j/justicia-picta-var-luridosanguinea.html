Justicia picta var. luridosanguinea

Justicia picta var. luridosanguinea, commonly known as the red-flowered flame bush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features showy, tubular red flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as birds and insects. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a velvety texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
