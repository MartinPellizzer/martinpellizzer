Justicia rostellaria

Justicia rostellaria, commonly known as the red bearded dragon plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and the Americas.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy bracts that resemble the scales of a dragon. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in clusters, often in shades of red, pink, or purple. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its leaves are lance-shaped and can grow up to 30 cm in length, making it a striking addition to gardens and indoor spaces.
