Justicia brevispica var. brevispica

Justicia brevispica var. brevispica, commonly known as the short-spurred justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant produces showy bracts that often resemble petals, enhancing its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its vibrant blooms and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
