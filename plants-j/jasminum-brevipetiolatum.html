Jasminum brevipetiolatum

Jasminum brevipetiolatum, commonly known as the short-stemmed jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has small, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and a short petiole, giving it its scientific name. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for container gardening and small garden spaces.
