Justicia tweedieana

Justicia tweedieana, commonly known as the yellow flame plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that resemble flames. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.
