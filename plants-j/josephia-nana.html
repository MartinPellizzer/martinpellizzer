Josephia nana

Josephia nana, commonly known as the dwarf josephia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, glossy, dark green leaves that are arranged in a dense, rounded form. The plant produces clusters of small, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Due to its compact size and ornamental value, it is often used in rock gardens or as a container plant.
