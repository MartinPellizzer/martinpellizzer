Jacobaea Delphiniifolia

Jacobaea delphiniifolia is a rare and striking member of the Asteraceae family known for its distinctive foliage and vibrant flowers. Native to southern Europe, this plant thrives in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are deeply lobed, resembling the shape of a dolphin, hence its common name. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, adding a splash of color to its natural habitat. Due to its limited distribution and unique characteristics, Jacobaea delphiniifolia is a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
