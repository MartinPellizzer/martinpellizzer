Jasminum oleicarpum

Jasminum oleicarpum, commonly known as the yellow jasmine, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture.

The fruit is a small, capsule-like structure that contains several seeds.
