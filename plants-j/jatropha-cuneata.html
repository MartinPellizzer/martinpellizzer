Jatropha cuneata

Jatropha cuneata, commonly known as the narrowleaf jatropha, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and Africa.

Due to its ornamental value and potential for use in phytoremediation, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and ecological restoration projects.
