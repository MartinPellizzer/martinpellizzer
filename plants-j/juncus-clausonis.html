Juncus clausonis

Juncus clausonis, commonly known as the narrowleaf rush, is a perennial plant belonging to the Juncaceae family.

It is native to parts of Asia, including China, Japan, and the Korean Peninsula. The plant typically grows in moist or wet environments such as marshes, stream banks, and wet meadows. It has tall, cylindrical, grass-like leaves that are often densely packed.

Juncus clausonis produces small, clustered flowers that are usually greenish or reddish in color.
