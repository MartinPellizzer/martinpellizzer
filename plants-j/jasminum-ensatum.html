Jasminum ensatum

Jasminum ensatum, commonly known as the Chinese jasmine or the evergreen jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters, often emitting a sweet, intoxicating scent. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves and is typically evergreen, making it a popular ornamental plant. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Jasminum ensatum is also valued for its use in traditional medicine and as a source of essential oils.
