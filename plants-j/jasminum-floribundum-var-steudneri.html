Jasminum floribundum var. steudneri

Jasminum floribundum var. steudneri, commonly known as the Cape jasmine, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are elliptical in shape. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
