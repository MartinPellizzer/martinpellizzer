Justicia malabarica

Justicia malabarica, commonly known as the Malabar glorybower, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows up to 1 meter in height. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of vibrant blue or purple flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Botanically, it belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is valued for its ornamental qualities in horticulture.
