Johrenia aromatica

Johrenia aromatica, commonly known as the aromatic johrenia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to certain regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to yellow. Its aromatic leaves and flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Johrenia aromatica thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it adaptable to a variety of climates.
