Justicia leucerythra

Justicia leucerythra, commonly known as the red hot poker, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, upright spikes of bright red flowers that resemble poker chips, giving it its common name. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy inflorescences that bloom from summer to autumn. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its vivid color and architectural form make it a standout in any garden setting.
