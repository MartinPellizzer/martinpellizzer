Justicia antsingensis var. obtusa

Justicia antsingensis var. obtusa, commonly known as the Madagascar flame flower, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered with fine hairs. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is typically found in forested areas.

This variant is distinguished by its obtuse leaf tips and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
