Justicia tijucensis

Justicia tijucensis, commonly known as the Tijuca flame flower, is a strikingly ornamental shrub native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and it produces clusters of bright, flame-like blooms during the summer months. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often cultivated for its vibrant floral display.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular in tropical and subtropical gardens.
