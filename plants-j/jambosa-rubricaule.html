Jambosa rubricaule

Jambosa rubricaule, commonly known as the red-stemmed jambosa, is a tropical shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive red-tinged stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale pink, followed by edible fruits resembling small, round berries. Jambosa rubricaule thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and fruit.

Its botanical features make it a unique addition to both gardens and natural ecosystems.
