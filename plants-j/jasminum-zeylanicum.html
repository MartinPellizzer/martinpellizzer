Jasminum zeylanicum

Jasminum zeylanicum, commonly known as the Ceylon jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Sri Lanka.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters, often releasing a sweet scent at night. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and elliptical with a glossy surface.

The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine and perfumery.
