Julocroton integer

Julocroton integer, commonly known as the African croton, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with yellow and green patterns. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Julocroton integer is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes in warm climates.
