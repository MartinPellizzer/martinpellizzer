Jasminum × stephanense

Jasminum × stephanense, commonly known as the Stephan's jasmine, is a hybrid evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant typically grows to a height of 1–3 meters and has dark green, glossy leaves. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.

Jasminum × stephanense is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic uses.
