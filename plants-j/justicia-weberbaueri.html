Justicia weberbaueri

Justicia weberbaueri, commonly known as the Weberbauer's flameleaf, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.
