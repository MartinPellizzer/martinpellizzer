Justicia ukambensis

Justicia ukambensis, commonly known as the red-flowered justicia, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking red or pink tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb, often found in moist, well-drained soils. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered with fine hairs, while the stems are erect and branching.
