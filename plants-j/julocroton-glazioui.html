Julocroton glazioui

Julocroton glazioui, commonly known as the Glaziou's croton, is a tropical shrub native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Crotonaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that display a range of hues including red, yellow, and green. The plant has simple, alternate leaves with prominent veins and often features small, inconspicuous flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
