Justicia madurensis

Justicia madurensis, commonly known as the Madurese justicia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as an herb or shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a rough texture.
