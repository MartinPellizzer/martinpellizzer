Justicia abscondita

Justicia abscondita, commonly known as the hidden justicia, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to purple. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub or perennial, often reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture, while its flowers are borne in dense clusters at the ends of the stems.

Justicia abscondita is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
