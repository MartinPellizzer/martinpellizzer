Jasminum stellipilum

Jasminum stellipilum, commonly known as the star-hair jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white or yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has slender, woody stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a fragrant, shade-tolerant plant.
