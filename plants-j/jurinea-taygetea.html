Jurinea taygetea

Jurinea taygetea, commonly known as the Taygetean sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically thrives in sandy or rocky soils. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are linear and often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid conditions.

Jurinea taygetea is adapted to coastal and inland areas with mild, dry climates.
