Justicia concavibracteata

Justicia concavibracteata, commonly known as the narrow-bract justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with contrasting yellow or white markings. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various soil types.
