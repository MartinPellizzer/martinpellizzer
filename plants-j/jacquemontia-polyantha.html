Jacquemontia polyantha

Jacquemontia polyantha, commonly known as the showy morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of purple and pink. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks. Its flowers open in the morning and close in the afternoon, hence its common name.

Jacquemontia polyantha is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
