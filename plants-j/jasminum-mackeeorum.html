Jasminum mackeeorum

Jasminum mackeeorum, commonly known as the Mackee's jasmine, is a species of flowering plant in the Oleaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters.

The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a glossy surface, while the flowers emit a sweet, aromatic scent.
