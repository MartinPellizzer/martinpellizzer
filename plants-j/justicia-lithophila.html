Justicia lithophila

Justicia lithophila, commonly known as the rock fig, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and pairs of opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking clusters of tubular, red or orange flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
