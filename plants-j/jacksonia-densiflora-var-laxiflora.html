Jacksonia densiflora var. laxiflora

Jacksonia densiflora var. laxiflora, commonly known as the slender bristled daisy, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the daisy family, Asteraceae, and is characterized by its dense, bristly foliage and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal and subalpine regions, thriving in well-drained soils. Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular choice for gardens and horticultural collections.

The variety laxiflora differs from the typical form by having more loosely arranged flowers and a slightly different leaf structure.
