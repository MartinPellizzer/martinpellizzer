Juncus decipiens f. spiralis

Juncus decipiens f. spiralis, commonly known as the spiral rush, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Juncaceae.

It is characterized by its spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in dense spikes. It thrives in wet or moist environments such as marshes and stream banks.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological studies and ornamental gardening.
