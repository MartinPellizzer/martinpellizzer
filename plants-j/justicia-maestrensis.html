Justicia maestrensis

Justicia maestrensis, commonly known as the Mexican flame flower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red or orange tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a rough texture with a dark green upper surface.

Justicia maestrensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
