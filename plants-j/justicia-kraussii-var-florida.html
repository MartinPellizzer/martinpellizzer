Justicia kraussii var. florida

Justicia kraussii var. florida, commonly known as the Florida flame flower, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in dense clusters, attracting pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. It is a perennial shrub that thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for garden landscapes due to its drought tolerance and showy display.
