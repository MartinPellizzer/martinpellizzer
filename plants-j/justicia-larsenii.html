Justicia larsenii

Justicia larsenii, commonly known as the mountain justicia, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 feet, with opposite, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers bloom in clusters at the ends of branches, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for tropical and subtropical landscapes.
