Justicia sterea

Justicia sterea, commonly known as the silverleaf justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery pubescence, giving it its common name. Its showy, tubular flowers are usually pink or red and appear in clusters at the ends of branches.

Justicia sterea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils and full sun.
