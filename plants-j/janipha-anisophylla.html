Janipha anisophylla

Janipha anisophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leafed pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Pittosporaceae and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal and lowland areas, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and leathery, while its flowers are usually white or pale yellow.
