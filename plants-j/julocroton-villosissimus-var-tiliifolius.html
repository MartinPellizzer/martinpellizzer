Julocroton villosissimus var. tiliifolius

Julocroton villosissimus var. tiliifolius, commonly known as the velvet croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of fine, silky hairs, giving them a velvety texture. The leaves are typically dark green and have a distinctive, waxy appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by orange or red berries.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well-suited to warm, humid climates.
