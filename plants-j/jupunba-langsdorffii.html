Jupunba langsdorffii

Jupunba langsdorffii, commonly known as the Langsdorff's jupunba, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is a shrub-like plant native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are typically clustered in terminal inflorescences, displaying vibrant colors ranging from pink to red.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.
