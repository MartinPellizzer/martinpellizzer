Justicia matogrossensis

Justicia matogrossensis, commonly known as the Matogrossense jacaranda, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.
