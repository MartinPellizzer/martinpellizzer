Jasminum humile f. kansuense

Jasminum humile f. kansuense, commonly known as the Kansu jasmine, is a compact shrub native to China.

It features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. The plant has dark green, narrow leaves and a woody stem structure typical of many jasmine species. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its sweet fragrance and delicate appearance.
