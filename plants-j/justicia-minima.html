Justicia minima

Justicia minima, commonly known as the smallleaf justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of pink or white. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens in warm climates.

Due to its compact size and ornamental flowers, it is often used in ornamental horticulture.
