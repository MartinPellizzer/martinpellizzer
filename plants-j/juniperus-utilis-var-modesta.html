Juniperus utilis var. modesta

Juniperus utilis var. modesta, commonly known as the dwarf juniper, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit, with dense, scale-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, berry-like cones that are usually dark blue when mature, containing toxic seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for rock gardens and as a ground cover.

Juniperus utilis var. modesta is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in harsh environmental conditions.
