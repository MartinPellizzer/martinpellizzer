Jacquemontia glaucescens

Jacquemontia glaucescens, commonly known as the blue morning glory, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range from blue to purple, blooming in the early morning. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its rapid growth and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.
