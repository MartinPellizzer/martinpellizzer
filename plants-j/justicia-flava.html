Justicia flava

Justicia flava, commonly known as the yellow flame or golden justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling flames. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 feet. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly serrated edge.

Justicia flava thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
