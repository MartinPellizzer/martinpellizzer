Justicia diffusa var. orbiculata

Justicia diffusa var. orbiculata, commonly known as the orbiculate flame flower, is a perennial shrub native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, attracting pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees. The plant has opposite, ovate to orbicular leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes in warm climates.
