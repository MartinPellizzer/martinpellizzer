Jambosa

Jambosa, commonly known as the Natal plum, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, followed by edible, yellow-orange fruits resembling plums. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are elliptical and glossy, providing ornamental value in gardens.

Jambosa is also valued for its medicinal properties, with traditional uses in treating various ailments.
