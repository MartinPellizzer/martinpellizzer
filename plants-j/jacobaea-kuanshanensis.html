Jacobaea kuanshanensis

Jacobaea kuanshanensis, commonly known as the Kuanshan ragwort, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in rocky or grassy areas and has lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. Its flowers are yellowish-green and bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species in its native habitat.
