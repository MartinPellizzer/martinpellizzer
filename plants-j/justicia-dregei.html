Justicia dregei

Justicia dregei, commonly known as the red-hot poker, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, spiky inflorescences that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow, adding vibrant color to gardens. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green in color, providing a striking contrast to its showy flowers. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is further enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
