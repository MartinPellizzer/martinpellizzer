Jasminum gardeniiflorum

Jasminum gardeniiflorum, commonly known as the gardenii jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub that produces fragrant, white flowers with a strong jasmine scent. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and thrives in warm, humid climates. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and elliptical in shape, with a smooth texture.

The flowers bloom in clusters and are often used in perfumery and traditional medicine.
