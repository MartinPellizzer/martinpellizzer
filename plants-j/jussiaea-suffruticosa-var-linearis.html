Jussiaea suffruticosa var. linearis

Jussiaea suffruticosa var. linearis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved jussiaea, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub-like species characterized by its slender, linear leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in wetland or marshy environments, thriving in moist, nutrient-rich soils. Its flowering period occurs during the warmer months, and it plays a role in supporting local pollinator populations.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological contributions in its native habitat.
