Justicia gladiatotheca

Justicia gladiatotheca, commonly known as the swordleaf justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its broad, sword-shaped leaves and clusters of striking, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.

Its blooming period typically occurs during the summer months, adding vibrant color to landscapes.
