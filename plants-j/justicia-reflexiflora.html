Justicia reflexiflora

Justicia reflexiflora, commonly known as the reflexed flame flower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with a smooth texture and a slightly waxy appearance.

Justicia reflexiflora thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
