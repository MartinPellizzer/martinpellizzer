Juglans sinensis

Juglans sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese walnut, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the walnut family, Juglandaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive walnut fruits. The tree produces edible seeds encased in a hard, thick shell, which are highly valued for their nutritional content. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its timber and nuts.

The plant also plays an important role in traditional Chinese medicine.
