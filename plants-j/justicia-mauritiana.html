Justicia mauritiana

Justicia mauritiana, commonly known as the flame flower or red flame plant, is a tropical shrub native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Justicia mauritiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
