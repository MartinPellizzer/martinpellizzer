Justicia oreadum

Justicia oreadum, commonly known as the mountain justicia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded areas of woodlands and stream banks. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of tubular, blue to violet flowers. Its botanical classification places it in the Acanthaceae family, which is known for its showy inflorescences.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partially shaded environments.
