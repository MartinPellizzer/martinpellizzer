Justicia austroguangxiensis

Justicia austroguangxiensis, commonly known as the Guangxi justicia, is a flowering plant native to southern China.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered with a fine pubescence.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
