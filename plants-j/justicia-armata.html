Justicia armata

Justicia armata, commonly known as the flame flower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees. The plant typically grows as an erect shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Justicia armata thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
