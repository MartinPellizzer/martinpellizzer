Johreniopsis stricticaulis

Johreniopsis stricticaulis, commonly known as the rigid-stemmed Johreniopsis, is a unique member of the Bromeliaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright, narrow leaves that form a rosette structure, typical of many bromeliads. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink in color. It is native to the cloud forests of South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its natural range.
