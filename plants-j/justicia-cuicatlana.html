Justicia cuicatlana

Justicia cuicatlana, commonly known as the Mexican flame flower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.
