Jasminum kaulbackii

Jasminum kaulbackii, commonly known as the Kaulback's jasmine, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring woody stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its flowers are followed by small, leathery fruits that contain seeds.

Jasminum kaulbackii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its pleasant fragrance.
