Junellia asparagoides

Junellia asparagoides, commonly known as the narrowleaf junellia, is a herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in green or red hues, and are borne on erect stems.

Junellia asparagoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
