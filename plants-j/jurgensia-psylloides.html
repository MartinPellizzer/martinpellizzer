Jurgensia psylloides

Jurgensia psylloides, commonly known as the golden leaf psyllid, is a small insect belonging to the family Psyllidae.

It is characterized by its distinctive golden-green coloration and is often found on the leaves of specific host plants. The plant's foliage provides a suitable environment for the insect's nymphs to feed on sap. Jurgensia psylloides plays a role in the ecological interactions within its habitat.

Its presence can sometimes indicate the health and diversity of the surrounding plant community.
