Jasminum perissanthum

Jasminum perissanthum, commonly known as the Chinese jasmine or evergreen jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, simple, and usually hairy leaves that are dark green in color. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Jasminum perissanthum is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
