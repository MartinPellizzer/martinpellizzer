Juniperus hochstetteri

Juniperus hochstetteri, commonly known as the Hochstetter juniper, is a species of evergreen conifer native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, scale-like leaves and small, blue-gray berries that are typically retained on the plant for several years. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions. It has a slow growth rate and can reach heights of up to 10 meters.

Juniperus hochstetteri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
