Jasminum gerrardii

Jasminum gerrardii, commonly known as the yellow jasmine, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant produces fragrant, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate with a smooth margin.

Jasminum gerrardii thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland areas.
