Justicia areysiana

Justicia areysiana, commonly known as the Blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in dense, showy clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are opposite and often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Justicia areysiana is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
