Junghansia

Junghansia, commonly known as the Chinese dandelion, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to East Asia, particularly China, and features small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. They are also valued for their ornamental appeal in gardens.
