Jatropha paraguayensis

Jatropha paraguayensis, commonly known as the Paraguay physic nut, is a shrub or small tree native to South America.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, often thorny stems and alternate leaves. The plant contains latex and is known for its medicinal and toxic properties.
