Justicia trichotoma

Justicia trichotoma, commonly known as the hairy justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and the Americas.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant features hairy stems and leaves, which contribute to its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Justicia trichotoma is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
