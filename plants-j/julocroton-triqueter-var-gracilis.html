Julocroton triqueter var. gracilis

Julocroton triqueter var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features palmate leaves with three to five lobes, often displaying vibrant yellow or orange hues. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. Its ornamental value comes from its striking foliage and compact growth habit.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
