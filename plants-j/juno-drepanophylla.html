Juno drepanophylla

Juno drepanophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leafed rock cress, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in alpine environments. It produces small, white or yellowish flowers that bloom in early spring.
