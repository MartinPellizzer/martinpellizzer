Jasminum glaucum var. parviflorum

Jasminum glaucum var. parviflorum, commonly known as the narrow-flower jasmine, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters during the winter months. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and a dense, rounded growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight.

This variety is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and pleasant scent.
