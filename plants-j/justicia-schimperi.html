Justicia schimperi

Justicia schimperi, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant features succulent leaves that are often velvety in texture, adapted to store water in arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Justicia schimperi is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
