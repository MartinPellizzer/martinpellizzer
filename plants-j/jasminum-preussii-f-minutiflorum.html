Jasminum preussii f. minutiflorum

Jasminum preussii f. minutiflorum, commonly known as the miniature jasmine, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and a compact growth habit, making it suitable for container gardening or as a decorative plant. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate watering.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and sweet, intoxicating fragrance.
