Justicia udzungwaensis

Justicia udzungwaensis, commonly known as the Udzungwa justicia, is a flowering plant native to Tanzania.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as along forest streams and in montane regions. It features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
