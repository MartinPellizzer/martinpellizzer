Justicia delicatula var. delicatula

Justicia delicatula var. delicatula, commonly known as the velvetleaf false arum, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features striking, heart-shaped leaves that are usually a deep green with a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and purple, which bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden areas.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique foliage and delicate floral display.
