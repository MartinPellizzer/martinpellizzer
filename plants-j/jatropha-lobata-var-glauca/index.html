Jatropha Lobata Var. Glauca

Blueleaf jatropha, scientifically known as Jatropha lobata var. glauca, is a striking member of the Euphorbiaceae family native to tropical regions. Its distinctive blue-green foliage sets it apart from other members of the jatropha genus, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens. The plant features showy yellow-orange flowers that bloom throughout the growing season, adding vibrant color to its surroundings. While it is not typically cultivated for its seeds, its unique appearance makes it a sought-after addition to botanical collections. Due to its hardy nature, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it adaptable to a variety of climates.
