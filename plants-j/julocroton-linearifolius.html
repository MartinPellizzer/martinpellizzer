Julocroton linearifolius

Julocroton linearifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved croton, is a tropical plant native to regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, narrow leaves that are often arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, followed by distinctive, spiny fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is not commonly found in temperate regions.
