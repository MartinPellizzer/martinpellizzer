Jasminum noldeanum

Jasminum noldeanum, commonly known as the Nolde's jasmine, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and elliptic in shape, with a smooth texture.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
