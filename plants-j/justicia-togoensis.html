Justicia togoensis

Justicia togoensis, commonly known as the Togo flame flower, is a flowering plant native to West Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
