Justicia nummularia

Justicia nummularia, commonly known as silverleaf justicia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value and drought tolerance make it a popular choice in xeriscaping.
