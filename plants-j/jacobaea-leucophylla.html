Jacobaea leucophylla

Jacobaea leucophylla, commonly known as the white-leaf ragwort, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, silvery-green leaves and produces yellow to orange flowers that bloom in summer. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
