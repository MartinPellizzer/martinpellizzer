Jasminum glaucum

Jasminum glaucum, commonly known as the silver jasmine, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, gray-green leaves that are covered in a fine, waxy coating, giving it a silvery appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Jasminum glaucum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its delicate fragrance and visual interest.
