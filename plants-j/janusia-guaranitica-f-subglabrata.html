Janusia guaranitica f. subglabrata

Janusia guaranitica f. subglabrata, commonly known as the Brazilian star orchid, is a unique orchid species native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit and produces striking, star-shaped flowers with a vivid orange-red coloration. The plant features lanceolate, glabrous leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. Its inflorescence consists of a single raceme bearing several showy, resupinate flowers.

This subspecies is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in horticultural settings.
