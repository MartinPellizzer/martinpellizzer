Justicia notha

Justicia notha, commonly known as the slender justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces clusters of tubular, two-lipped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Justicia notha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
