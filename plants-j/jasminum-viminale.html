Jasminum viminale

Jasminum viminale, commonly known as creeping jasmine, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves that are usually opposite in arrangement. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.

Jasminum viminale is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to cover ground effectively.
