Jasminum linearifolium

Jasminum linearifolium, commonly known as the linear-leaf jasmine, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia, particularly India and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to several meters. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for cultivation in warm climates.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its pleasant aroma and delicate appearance.
