Jussiaea dodecandra var. parviflora

Jussiaea dodecandra var. parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered jussiaea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its compact growth habit and aromatic foliage. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are typically elliptical or ovate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

It is often found in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or savanna habitats.
