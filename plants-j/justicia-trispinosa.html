Justicia trispinosa

Justicia trispinosa, commonly known as the three-spined false dragonhead, is a flowering plant native to parts of Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. Justicia trispinosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and can thrive in well-drained soil with partial to full sunlight.
