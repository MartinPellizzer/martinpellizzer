Julocroton argenteus

Julocroton argenteus, commonly known as the silver croton, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often variegated with silver and green patterns, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its vibrant foliage, it is popular as a houseplant and in landscape design.
