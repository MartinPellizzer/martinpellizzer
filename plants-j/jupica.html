Jupica

Jupica, commonly known as the "dwarf African violet," is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant features velvety, dark green leaves that are often marked with silver or white veins. Jupica typically blooms in shades of pink, purple, and white, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
