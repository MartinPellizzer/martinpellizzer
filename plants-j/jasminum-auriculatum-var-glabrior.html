Jasminum auriculatum var. glabrior

Jasminum auriculatum var. glabrior, commonly known as the smooth-leafed jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The leaves are typically smooth and oval-shaped, with a glossy green surface. This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant aroma.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
