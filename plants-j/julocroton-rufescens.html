Julocroton rufescens

Julocroton rufescens, commonly known as the red-stemmed croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its woody stems that turn reddish-brown as the plant matures. The leaves are typically ovate or elliptical, with a glossy green surface and a hairy underside. It produces small, clustered flowers that are usually yellow or orange in color.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant foliage and unique growth habit.
