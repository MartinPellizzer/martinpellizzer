Justicia rizzinii

Justicia rizzinii, commonly known as the flame violet, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to purple, often resembling those of the genus Justicia. The plant typically grows as an annual or short-lived perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and often branched.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in warm, sunny conditions.
