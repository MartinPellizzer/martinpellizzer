Justicia holochila

Justicia holochila, commonly known as the silverleaf justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery-white pubescence, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple, often blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its adaptability and striking foliage make it a valuable addition to both landscape design and botanical collections.
