Justicia nagpurensis

Justicia nagpurensis, commonly known as the Nagpur flame flower, is a flowering plant native to India.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking tubular red or orange flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth surface with a slightly pubescent underside.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
