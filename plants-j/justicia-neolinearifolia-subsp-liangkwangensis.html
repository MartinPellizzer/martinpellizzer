Justicia neolinearifolia subsp. liangkwangensis

Justicia neolinearifolia subsp.

liangkwangensis, commonly known as the Guangxi flame bush, is a flowering plant native to southern China. It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red to orange tubular flowers.

The plant typically grows as a shrub with opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.
