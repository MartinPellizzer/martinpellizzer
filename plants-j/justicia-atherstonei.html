Justicia atherstonei

Justicia atherstonei, commonly known as the Atherstone's glorybower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are usually bright red or orange, attracting pollinators such as birds and insects. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.

The species is also noted for its ability to thrive in dry conditions with minimal water requirements.
