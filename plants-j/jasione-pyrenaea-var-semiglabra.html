Jasione pyrenaea var. semiglabra

Jasione pyrenaea var. semiglabra, commonly known as the smooth rock-jasione, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in sunny, well-drained habitats. The plant features basal leaves that are lanceolate and covered with short, sparse hairs. Its small, blue to purple flowers are arranged in loose clusters and bloom from late spring to early summer.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging environments.
