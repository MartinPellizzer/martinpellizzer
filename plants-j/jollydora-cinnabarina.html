Jollydora cinnabarina

Jollydora cinnabarina, commonly known as the red-spotted orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, brightly colored flowers. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, showy blooms in shades of red and orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by horticulturists worldwide.
