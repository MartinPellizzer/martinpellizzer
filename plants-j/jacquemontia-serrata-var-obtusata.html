Jacquemontia serrata var. obtusata

Jacquemontia serrata var. obtusata, commonly known as the broadleaf morning glory, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

The plant belongs to the Convolvulaceae family, characterized by its twining stems and showy flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking vibrant, native ornamental plants.
