Justicia campechiana

Justicia campechiana, commonly known as the Mexican verbena, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is a member of the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink and purple. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
