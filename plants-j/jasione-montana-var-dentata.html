Jasione montana var. dentata

Jasione montana var. dentata, commonly known as the toothed bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features basal leaves that are lanceolate and serrated, with a distinctive toothed margin. The plant produces showy blue to purple flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.
