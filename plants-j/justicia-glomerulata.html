Justicia glomerulata

Justicia glomerulata, commonly known as clustered flame flower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking clusters of bright red or orange tubular flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Justicia glomerulata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
