Jussiaea nervosa f. microphylla

Jussiaea nervosa f. microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved waterplantain, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to wetland and aquatic environments.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are typically less than 2 centimeters in length, giving it its distinguishing characteristic. The plant produces small, white or greenish flowers arranged in dense spikes, which bloom during the spring and summer months. It thrives in shallow water or moist soil, often found in marshes and along the edges of ponds.

Its compact size and delicate appearance make it a popular choice for water garden enthusiasts.
