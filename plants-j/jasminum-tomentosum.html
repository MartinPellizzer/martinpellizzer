Jasminum tomentosum

Jasminum tomentosum, commonly known as the velvet tangle or woolly jasmine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its soft, fuzzy, grayish-green foliage and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is popular in gardens and as a houseplant. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its flowering season typically occurs in the spring and summer months.
