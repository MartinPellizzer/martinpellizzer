Jasminum mairei var. siderophyllum

Jasminum mairei var. siderophyllum, commonly known as the iron-leaf jasmine, is a variant of the jasmine plant native to China.

It is characterized by its dark green, almost metallic leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by both its foliage and its pleasant scent.
