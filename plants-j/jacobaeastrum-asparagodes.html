Jacobaeastrum asparagodes

Jacobaeastrum asparagodes, commonly known as the yellow star-of-Bethlehem, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces bright yellow flowers with a star-shaped appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or gravelly areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens but can become invasive in some regions.
