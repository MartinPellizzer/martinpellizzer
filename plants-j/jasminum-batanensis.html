Jasminum batanensis

Jasminum batanensis, commonly known as the Batan jasmine, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate, with a smooth texture.

Jasminum batanensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its pleasant fragrance.
