Justicia vixspicata

Justicia vixspicata, commonly known as the red hot poker, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking spikes of tubular red or orange flowers. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial or shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.
