Jasminum radcliffei

It belongs to the Oleaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, white, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a leathery texture, while its flowers bloom in clusters.
