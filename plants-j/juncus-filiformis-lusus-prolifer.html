Juncus filiformis lusus prolifer

Juncus filiformis lusus prolifer, commonly known as the slender rush, is a flowering plant belonging to the Juncaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes, stream banks, and wet meadows. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered greenish-white flowers during the summer months. It has a rhizomatous root system that allows it to spread vegetatively, contributing to its ability to colonize wet areas.

This species is often used in wetland restoration projects due to its ecological benefits and adaptability.
