Justicia sesamoides

Justicia sesamoides, commonly known as the yellow mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange inflorescences that resemble mist. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly rough texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.
