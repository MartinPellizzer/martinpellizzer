Justicia gracilis

Justicia gracilis, commonly known as the slender flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular, brightly colored flowers that range from red to orange, often with contrasting white or yellow markings. The plant has opposite, narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Justicia gracilis is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
