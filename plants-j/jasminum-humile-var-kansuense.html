Jasminum humile var. kansuense

Jasminum humile var. kansuense, commonly known as the Chinese jasmine, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are ovate to elliptic in shape, with a glossy surface and a slight pubescence.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and garden settings.
