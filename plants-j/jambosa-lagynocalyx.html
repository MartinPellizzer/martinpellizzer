Jambosa lagynocalyx

Jambosa lagynocalyx, commonly known as the African star apple, is a tropical plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. The fruit, which is typically round and green when mature, has a sweet, aromatic pulp with a texture similar to a pear. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and edible fruit.
