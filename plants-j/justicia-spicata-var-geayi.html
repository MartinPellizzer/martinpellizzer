Justicia spicata var. geayi

Justicia spicata var. geayi, commonly known as the pink flame bush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular pink to red flowers that bloom in dense spikes, attracting pollinators such as birds and insects. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for coastal and rocky garden environments.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and drought tolerance.
