Jasminum subhumile var. glabricymosum

Jasminum subhumile var. glabricymosum, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has slender, woody stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.

This variety is distinguished by its smoother stem texture compared to other jasmine species.
