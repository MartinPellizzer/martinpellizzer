Jatropha hernandiifolia var. hernandiifolia

Jatropha hernandiifolia var. hernandiifolia, commonly known as the Hawaiian jatropha, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal and lowland areas. Its flowers are small, yellow, and clustered in racemes, often accompanied by red bracts that give it a striking appearance.
