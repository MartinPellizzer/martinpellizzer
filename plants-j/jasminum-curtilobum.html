Jasminum curtilobum

Jasminum curtilobum, commonly known as the Chinese jasminum or the mountain jasmine, is a flowering plant native to China and Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height, characterized by its opposite, simple, and often aromatic leaves. The plant produces fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in the summer months. Its flowers are tubular with five petals and are known for their strong, sweet scent.

Jasminum curtilobum is often cultivated for ornamental purposes and is also used in traditional medicine.
