Jasminum hispanicum

Jasminum hispanicum, commonly known as Spanish jasmine, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, featuring woody stems and aromatic, white flowers. The leaves are simple, opposite, and ovate, with a glossy green surface. The flowers bloom in clusters, emitting a strong, sweet fragrance, especially at night.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
