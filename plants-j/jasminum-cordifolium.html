Jasminum cordifolium

Jasminum cordifolium, commonly known as the heart-leaved jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is typically evergreen, with glossy, dark green foliage. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for both garden beds and containers.

Jasminum cordifolium is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant aroma.
