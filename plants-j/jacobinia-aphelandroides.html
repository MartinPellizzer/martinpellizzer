Jacobinia aphelandroides

Jacobinia aphelandroides, commonly known as the white-flowered jacobinia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, white, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens in warm climates.
