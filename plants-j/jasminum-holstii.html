Jasminum holstii

Jasminum holstii, commonly known as the Holst's jasmine, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters, often emitting a sweet, delicate aroma. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and ovate, with a glossy green surface.

Jasminum holstii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
