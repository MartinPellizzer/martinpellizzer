Justicia whytei

Justicia whytei, commonly known as the white flame plant, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces vibrant, clustered blooms that attract pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

The leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often have a slightly hairy texture.
