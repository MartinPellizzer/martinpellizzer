Justicia caulopsila

Justicia caulopsila, commonly known as the narrowleaf flamebeard, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are narrow and opposite, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.
