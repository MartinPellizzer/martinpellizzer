Jacksonia nutans

Jacksonia nutans, commonly known as the New Zealand daisy, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind. Jacksonia nutans thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and forest margins.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in supporting local pollinators.
