Jububa

Jububa, commonly known as *Sesbania sesban*, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces elongated pods containing several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine and as a protein source. Jububa is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it valuable in agricultural systems.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used for forage and reforestation purposes.
