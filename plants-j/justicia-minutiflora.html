Justicia minutiflora

Justicia minutiflora, commonly known as the dwarf flame flower, is a tropical perennial shrub native to Central and South America.

It features small, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a contrasting blue or purple throat. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces small, round fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The species is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
