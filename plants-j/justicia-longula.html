Justicia longula

Justicia longula, commonly known as the red-flowered justicia, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

Justicia longula is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
