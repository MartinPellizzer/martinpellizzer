Justicia blepharostegia

Justicia blepharostegia, commonly known as the silverleaf justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, often blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies adds to its ecological value.
