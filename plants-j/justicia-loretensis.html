Justicia loretensis

Justicia loretensis, commonly known as the Loretto flame flower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Justicia loretensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
