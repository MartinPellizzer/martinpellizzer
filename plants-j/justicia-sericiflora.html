Justicia sericiflora

Justicia sericiflora, commonly known as velvetleaf justicia, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its broad, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces clusters of showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple. It thrives in moist, sunny environments and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners in tropical and subtropical regions.
