Joosia confusa

Joosia confusa, commonly known as the New Zealand whitebeam, is a small deciduous tree native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters during the spring, which are followed by red, berry-like fruits.
