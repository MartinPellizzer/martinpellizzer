Justicia cyclostegia

Justicia cyclostegia, commonly known as the velvetleaf false arum, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and dark green coloration. The plant produces striking, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by colorful bracts, typically in shades of red or purple. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for tropical gardens and botanical collections.
