Jacea tricolor

Jacea tricolor, commonly known as the three-color jacea, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive trifoliate leaves, which are divided into three leaflets, and its clusters of small, colorful flowers that range from white to pink and purple. Native to temperate regions of Asia, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.

Its aromatic foliage also makes it a useful plant in traditional herbal medicine.
