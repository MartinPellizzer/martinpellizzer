Jatropha bornmuelleri

Jatropha bornmuelleri, commonly known as the Bornmueller's physic nut, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain toxic seeds, which are sometimes used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Due to its toxicity, it should be handled with care.
