Juncus yanshanuensis

Juncus yanshanuensis, commonly known as the Yanshan rush, is a perennial plant native to China, particularly found in the Yanshan mountainous regions.

It belongs to the Juncaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and spikes of small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or rocky areas. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its upright, decorative form.

Juncus yanshanuensis is also used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
