Justicia hiemalis

Justicia hiemalis, commonly known as winter coralplant, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It features striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to pink, which attract pollinators like hummingbirds and bees. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, moist soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
