Juncus × rusguniensis

Juncus × rusguniensis, commonly known as the hybrid rush, is a perennial plant belonging to the Juncaceae family.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, cylindrical leaves that form dense clumps. The plant produces spikes of small, greenish flowers that bloom in early summer. It is native to regions in Europe and is often found in wet or moist soils.

Juncus × rusguniensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in waterlogged environments.
