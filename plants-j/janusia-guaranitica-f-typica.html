Janusia guaranitica f. typica

Janusia guaranitica f. typica, commonly known as the typical form of the genus Janusia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its daisy-like composite flowers, which consist of a central disc surrounded by ray florets. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in the understory of tropical rainforests. The species is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
