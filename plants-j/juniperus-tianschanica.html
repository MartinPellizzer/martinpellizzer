Juniperus tianschanica

Juniperus tianschanica, commonly known as the Tianshan juniper, is a coniferous evergreen shrub or small tree native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, needle-like foliage and small, blue-gray berries that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant thrives in cold, arid environments and is well adapted to high altitudes. Its cones are typically reddish-brown and contain aromatic seeds.

Juniperus tianschanica plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
