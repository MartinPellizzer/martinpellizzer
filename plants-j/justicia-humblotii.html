Justicia humblotii

Justicia humblotii, commonly known as the Humblot's glorybower, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean, particularly Cuba and the Bahamas.

Justicia humblotii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
