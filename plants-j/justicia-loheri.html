Justicia loheri

Justicia loheri, commonly known as the Mexican flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to Central America.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, attracting hummingbirds and other pollinators. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a rough texture and a strong, sometimes aromatic scent. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for warm climates.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
