Jurinea sintenisii

Jurinea sintenisii, commonly known as the Sintenisi's sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking blue or purple flowers. The plant thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to saline and windy coastal environments. Its leaves are typically silver or gray-green, covered in a fine layer of hair to reduce water loss.

Jurinea sintenisii is often found in dune ecosystems, where it plays a role in stabilizing sand and supporting local biodiversity.
