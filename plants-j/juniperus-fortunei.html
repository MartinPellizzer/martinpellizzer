Juniperus fortunei

Juniperus fortunei, commonly known as the Chinese juniper, is a small evergreen shrub or tree native to China.

It has needle-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color and are arranged in opposite pairs. The plant produces small, berry-like cones that are usually dark blue or black when mature. It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

Juniperus fortunei is well adapted to dry, rocky soils and can thrive in a variety of climates.
