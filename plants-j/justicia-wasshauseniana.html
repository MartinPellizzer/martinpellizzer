Justicia wasshauseniana

Justicia wasshauseniana, commonly known as the Cape rose of the wind, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of tubular flowers that attract pollinators such as birds and insects.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
