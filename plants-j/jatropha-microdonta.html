Jatropha microdonta

Jatropha microdonta, commonly known as the dwarf physic nut, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and smooth, greenish-gray leaves. The plant produces orange-red berries containing toxic seeds, which are not suitable for human consumption. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats.

Due to its toxicity, it is important to handle the plant with care.
