Julocroton humilis var. genuinus

Julocroton humilis var. genuinus, commonly known as the dwarf croton, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features lance-shaped leaves with striking yellow and green variegation, which contribute to its ornamental value. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to maintain its vibrant foliage.

Due to its compact size and attractive appearance, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
