Juniperus lasdeliana

Juniperus lasdeliana, commonly known as the Mexican redcedar, is a species of juniper native to Mexico.

It is a small evergreen shrub or tree that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant has scale-like leaves that are tightly pressed against the stems, giving it a dense appearance. Its cones are small and berry-like, containing aromatic seeds that are dispersed by wind.

The plant is valued for its hardy nature and is often used in horticultural settings for its ornamental qualities.
