Justicia velizii

Justicia velizii, commonly known as the Mexican flame flower, is a striking tropical plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators like hummingbirds and bees. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy green surface and a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Justicia velizii is also known for its ability to produce attractive seed pods that persist through the winter, adding visual interest to the garden year-round.
