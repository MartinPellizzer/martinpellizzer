Jacksonia sternbergiana

Jacksonia sternbergiana, commonly known as the New Zealand daisy, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and has a compact, bushy growth habit. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while the flowers are bright yellow with white ray florets.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
