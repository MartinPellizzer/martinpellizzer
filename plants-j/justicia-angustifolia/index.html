Justicia Angustifolia

Coral bells, scientifically known as Justicia angustifolia, are strikingly beautiful flowering plants native to tropical and subtropical regions. With their vibrant, coral-like blooms and lush green foliage, they add a dramatic touch to any garden or landscape. These plants are prized for their ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of conditions. Their unique appearance and resilience make them a popular choice among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts. Whether used in containers or in the ground, coral bells offer both visual appeal and ecological benefits.
