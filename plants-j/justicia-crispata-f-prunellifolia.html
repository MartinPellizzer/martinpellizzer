Justicia crispata f. prunellifolia

Justicia crispata f. prunellifolia, commonly known as the Prunellifolia flame flower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Acanthaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees. The plant has heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often tinged with silver or white on the underside. Native to tropical regions of the Americas, it thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
