Jatropha spathulata

Jatropha spathulata, commonly known as the broad-leafed physic nut, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually broad and ovate in shape. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers followed by orange-red berries containing toxic seeds. It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, known for its latex-producing species.

Due to its toxic properties, it is not typically cultivated for ornamental or agricultural purposes.
