Jatropha basiacantha

Jatropha basiacantha, commonly known as the African milkweed, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, and its leaves are opposite and ovate. The sap of Jatropha basiacantha contains toxic compounds, making it potentially harmful to humans and animals.

It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in arid or semi-arid environments.
