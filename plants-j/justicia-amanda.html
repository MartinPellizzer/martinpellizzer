Justicia amanda

Justicia amanda, commonly known as Amanda's jewel, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in dense, terminal clusters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
