Juniperus × media var. plumosa

Juniperus × media var. plumosa, commonly known as the silky juniper, is a hybrid evergreen shrub or small tree native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its finely textured, silver-blue foliage that gives it a distinctive, feathery appearance. The plant produces small, blue-black berries that are typically consumed by birds and other wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value lies in its elegant form and year-round interest.
