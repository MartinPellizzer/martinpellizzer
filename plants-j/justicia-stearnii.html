Justicia stearnii

Justicia stearnii, commonly known as the flame violet, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from red to purple. The plant typically grows as a shrub or a perennial herb, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The flowers are clustered in whorls along the stem, making it a visually appealing ornamental plant.
