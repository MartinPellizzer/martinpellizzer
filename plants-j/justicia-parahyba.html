Justicia parahyba

Justicia parahyba, commonly known as the flame bush, is a shrub native to Brazil.

It features showy, tubular red flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators like hummingbirds and butterflies. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
