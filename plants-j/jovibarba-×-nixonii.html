Jovibarba × nixonii

Jovibarba × nixonii, commonly known as the hybrid saxifrage, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is often cultivated in rock gardens and xeriscapes.
