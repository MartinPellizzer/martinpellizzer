Justicia campanulata

Justicia campanulata, commonly known as the bellflower justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 2 meters. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and covered in a fine layer of hair.

Justicia campanulata thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
