Jinsejie violacea

Jinsejie violacea, commonly known as the purple violet, is a flowering plant belonging to the Violaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking violet or purple flowers, which are typically arranged in dense clusters. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Native to certain regions of Asia, it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Its botanical significance lies in its ornamental value and its role in traditional herbal medicine.
