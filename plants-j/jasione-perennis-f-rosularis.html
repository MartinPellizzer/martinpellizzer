Jasione perennis f. rosularis

Jasione perennis f. rosularis, commonly known as the rose-like everlasting, is a perennial plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit and produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or alpine settings.

Its ornamental value lies in its long-lasting flowers and compact form.
