Jasminum taiwanianum

Jasminum taiwanianum, commonly known as the Taiwan jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Oleaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate with a slightly hairy underside.

The flowers emit a strong, sweet scent, particularly in the evening, attracting nocturnal pollinators like moths.
