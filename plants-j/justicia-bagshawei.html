Justicia bagshawei

Justicia bagshawei, commonly known as the silver flame plant, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage and vibrant red bracts. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
