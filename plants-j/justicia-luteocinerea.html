Justicia luteocinerea

Justicia luteocinerea, commonly known as the yellow cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The flowers bloom in summer and are often used in ornamental gardening due to their vibrant color and attractive foliage.
