Jupunba macradenia

Jupunba macradenia, commonly known as the giant orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 30 centimeters in diameter, making it one of the largest orchids in the world. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in the undergrowth of tropical rainforests. Its flowers are usually a mix of white, yellow, and green hues, with a strong, sweet fragrance.

Jupunba macradenia is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
