Justicia bojeri

Justicia bojeri, commonly known as the flame flower, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often resembling flames. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and arranged oppositely along the stem. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Justicia bojeri is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
