Justicia wendtii

Justicia wendtii, commonly known as the silver bush or Mexican flame flower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters tall, featuring lance-shaped leaves with a silvery-white underside. The plant produces striking clusters of tubular, red or orange flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
