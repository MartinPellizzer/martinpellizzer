Jacobinia macrophylla

Jacobinia macrophylla, commonly known as the broad-leafed jacobinia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its large, velvety leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal regions and thrives in well-drained, sandy soils. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage and vibrant blooms.
