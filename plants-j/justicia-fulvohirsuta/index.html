Justicia Fulvohirsuta

Justicia fulvohirsuta is a striking flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America. Known for its vibrant orange-red bracts and delicate white flowers, it adds a splash of color to any garden. This species belongs to the Acanthaceae family, which includes many ornamental and medicinal plants. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice among horticulturists and plant enthusiasts. Growing easily in well-drained soil and partial shade, it is both beautiful and relatively low maintenance.
