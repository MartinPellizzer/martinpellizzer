Justicia macdonaldii

Justicia macdonaldii, commonly known as the Macdonald's flame flower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, with lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
