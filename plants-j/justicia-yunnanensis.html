Justicia yunnanensis

Justicia yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan flame flower, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking red bracts that resemble flames. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its flowers are small and clustered, contrasting with the vibrant, showy bracts that attract pollinators.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
