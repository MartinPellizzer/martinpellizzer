Jonquilla media

Jonquilla media, commonly known as the Spanish daffodil, is a species of flowering plant in the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to the Iberian Peninsula and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers with a white throat. The plant produces narrow, strap-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Jonquilla media is known for its winter blooming habit, adding early color to gardens in spring.
