Jasminum acuminatissimum

Jasminum acuminatissimum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in clusters. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade.

Jasminum acuminatissimum is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
