Jussiaea longifolia var. floribunda

Jussiaea longifolia var. floribunda, commonly known as the flowering jussiaea, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The leaves are typically opposite, elliptical, and slightly hairy. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
