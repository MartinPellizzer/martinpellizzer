Jaumea elata

Jaumea elata, commonly known as the coastal sea holly, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its blue to purple flowers and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky coastal areas, thriving in saline and well-drained soils. It is often found in dune environments and is adapted to withstand harsh coastal conditions.

Jaumea elata is a hardy species that plays a role in stabilizing coastal dunes and supporting local biodiversity.
