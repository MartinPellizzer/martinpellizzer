Jasminum annamense

Jasminum annamense, commonly known as the Annam jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, often releasing a sweet scent at night. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a glossy green appearance.

Jasminum annamense is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aromatic blooms.
