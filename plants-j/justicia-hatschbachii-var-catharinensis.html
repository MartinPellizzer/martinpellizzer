Justicia hatschbachii var. catharinensis

Justicia hatschbachii var. catharinensis, commonly known as the Catharinensis flame flower, is a striking perennial herb native to Brazil.

It features showy, tubular flowers that are typically red or orange, attracting pollinators such as hummingbirds and butterflies. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for tropical and subtropical gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.
