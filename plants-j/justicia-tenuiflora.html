Justicia tenuiflora

Justicia tenuiflora, commonly known as narrow-leaved false dragonhead, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, opposite leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant is sometimes used in horticulture for its ornamental value and can be propagated through seeds or cuttings.
