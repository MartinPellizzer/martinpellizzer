Justicia johannae

Justicia johannae, commonly known as the silver bush or silverleaf justicia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. It is well adapted to dry conditions and thrives in full sun.
