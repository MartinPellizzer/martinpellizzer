Justicia rupestris

Justicia rupestris, commonly known as the rock justicia, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Justicia rupestris is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and hummingbirds.
