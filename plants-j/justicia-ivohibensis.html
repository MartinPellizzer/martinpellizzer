Justicia ivohibensis

Justicia ivohibensis, commonly known as the Madagascar flame flower, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a slightly rough texture, and it thrives in warm, humid climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is not commonly found outside its native region.
