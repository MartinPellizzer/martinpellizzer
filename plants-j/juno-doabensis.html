Juno doabensis

Juno doabensis, commonly known as the Doabensis Juniper, is a species of juniper native to the Indian subcontinent.

It is a small evergreen shrub or tree, typically reaching heights of up to 3 meters. The plant has scale-like leaves that are dark green and dense, providing a compact appearance. Its cones are small and berry-like, containing aromatic seeds that are used in traditional medicine.

Juno doabensis is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient plant in regions with limited water availability.
