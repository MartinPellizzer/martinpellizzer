Jasione maritima var. sabularia

Jasione maritima var. sabularia, commonly known as sea holly, is a coastal plant adapted to salty and windy environments.

It is a low-growing perennial with silver-gray leaves and striking blue-violet flowers. The plant thrives in sandy or rocky soils along shorelines and dunes. Its flowers are typically clustered in umbels and bloom in late summer.

It plays an important role in stabilizing coastal ecosystems and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
