Justicia kucharii

Justicia kucharii, commonly known as the flame flower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular, red or orange flowers that resemble flames. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Justicia kucharii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
