Justicia oreopola

Justicia oreopola, commonly known as the mountain justicia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Mexico and Central America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking spikes of tubular, violet-blue flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Justicia oreopola thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or forested habitats.
