Juncus lesueurii var. stenocaulon

Juncus lesueurii var. stenocaulon, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed rushes, is a variant of the Lesueur rush, characterized by its slender, upright stems and dense foliage.

It is a member of the Juncaceae family, which includes various species of rushes and sedges. This plant typically grows in wet or moist environments, such as marshes, swamps, and along the edges of water bodies. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, spike-like inflorescences.

The plant plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic and terrestrial organisms.
