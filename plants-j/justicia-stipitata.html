Justicia stipitata

Justicia stipitata, commonly known as the red hot poker, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in spikes. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are opposite and often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant flowers and upright growth habit.
