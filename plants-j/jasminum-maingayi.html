Jasminum maingayi

Jasminum maingayi, commonly known as the Philippine jasmine, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a slightly serrated margin.

Jasminum maingayi is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated for its pleasant aroma.
