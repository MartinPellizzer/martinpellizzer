Justicia volkeri

Justicia volkeri, commonly known as the Volker's flamebeard, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in a soft, velvety texture.

Justicia volkeri thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
