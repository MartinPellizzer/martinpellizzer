Jambosa melanosticta

Jambosa melanosticta, commonly known as the black-stained jambosa, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, leathery leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow. Its fruit, which is similar to a pineapple, is edible and has a sweet, tropical flavor.

Jambosa melanosticta is often cultivated for both its ornamental value and its fruit.
