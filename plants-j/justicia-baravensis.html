Justicia baravensis

Justicia baravensis, commonly known as the Barava flame flower, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and it thrives in warm, humid climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.

The species is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
