Junellia bisulcata

Junellia bisulcata, commonly known as the twin-veined grass.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves with distinct, parallel veins that run the length of the blade. Its flowers are small and clustered in spike-like inflorescences, typically blooming in late summer or early autumn.

Junellia bisulcata is often found in moist, open habitats such as wetlands and forest edges.
