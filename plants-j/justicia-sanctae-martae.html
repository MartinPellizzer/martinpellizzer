Justicia sanctae-martae

Justicia sanctae-martae, commonly known as the sacred mart's justicia, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Justicia sanctae-martae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
