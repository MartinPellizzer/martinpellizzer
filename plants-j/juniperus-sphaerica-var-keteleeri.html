Juniperus sphaerica var. keteleeri

Juniperus sphaerica var. keteleeri, commonly known as the Keteleeri juniper, is a compact, spherical shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a distinctive, rounded form that makes it highly ornamental. The plant produces small, blue-black berries that are typically retained on the plant for extended periods. It is well adapted to harsh, arid environments and thrives in well-drained soils.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
