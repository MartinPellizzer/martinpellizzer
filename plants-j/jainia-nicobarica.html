Jainia nicobarica

Jainia nicobarica, commonly known as the Nicobar jasmine, is a flowering plant native to the Andaman and Nicobar Islands.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, often during the summer months. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a smooth, glossy surface.

Jainia nicobarica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its pleasant fragrance.
