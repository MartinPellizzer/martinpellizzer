Justicia spinulosa

Justicia spinulosa, commonly known as the spiny justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of tubular, violet to blue flowers. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered with fine, spiny hairs, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The plant is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and hummingbirds.
