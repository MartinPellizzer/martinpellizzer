Jambosa aeorantha

Jambosa aeorantha, commonly known as the sky jasmine, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant has narrow, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets and rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its prolonged flowering period, which can last several months.
