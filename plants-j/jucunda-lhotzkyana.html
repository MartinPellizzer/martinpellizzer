Jucunda lhotzkyana

Jucunda lhotzkyana, commonly known as the Himalayan snowbell, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are small and lance-shaped, often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence.

Jucunda lhotzkyana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
