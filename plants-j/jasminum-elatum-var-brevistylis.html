Jasminum elatum var. brevistylis

Jasminum elatum var. brevistylis, commonly known as the narrow-styled jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. The leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate, with a glossy green surface. This variety is notable for its compact growth habit and is often cultivated as a decorative plant.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
