Jollydora rufiobarbata

Jollydora rufiobarbata, commonly known as the red-barbed jollydora, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers with distinctive barbed structures on the petals, which give it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are broad and elliptical, with a smooth texture and a deep green color.

Jollydora rufiobarbata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique floral display.
