Justicia distichotricha

Justicia distichotricha, commonly known as the silverleaf justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Due to its adaptability and aesthetic appeal, it is often cultivated in warm climates for its visual interest and medicinal potential.
