Janipha yuquilla

Janipha yuquilla, commonly known as the Andean chuchuhuasi, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Simaroubaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small yellow flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain a single seed. It is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly for its anti-inflammatory and analgesic effects.
