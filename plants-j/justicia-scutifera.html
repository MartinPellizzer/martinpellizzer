Justicia scutifera

Justicia scutifera, commonly known as the broadleaf justicia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
