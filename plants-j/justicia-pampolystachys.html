Justicia pampolystachys

Justicia pampolystachys, commonly known as the flame flower, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range from red to orange, often with contrasting yellow throats. The plant belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in warm, humid climates. Its foliage consists of opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface.

Justicia pampolystachys is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
