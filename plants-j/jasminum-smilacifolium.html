Jasminum smilacifolium

Jasminum smilacifolium, commonly known as the Chinese jasmine or smilacifolium jasmine, is a flowering plant native to China and Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous climbing shrub that features fragrant, white flowers with a strong scent, often used in perfumery. The plant has opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. Its stems are woody and can become woody and vine-like, allowing it to climb supports.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.
