Juncus schlagintweitii

Juncus schlagintweitii, commonly known as the Schlagintweit rush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia.

Its flowering spikes are usually short and produce tiny, spikelet-like flowers that are wind-pollinated.

Juncus schlagintweitii is often found in steppe and desert environments, contributing to the ecological stability of these regions.
