Justicia barteri

Justicia barteri, commonly known as the red hot poker, is a striking perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, spike-like inflorescences that bear vibrant red or orange tubular flowers. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are arranged in a whorled pattern along the stem. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Justicia barteri is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
