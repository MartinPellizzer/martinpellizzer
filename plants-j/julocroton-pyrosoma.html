Julocroton pyrosoma

Julocroton pyrosoma, commonly known as the fireball croton, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, often fiery red and orange foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant features palmate leaves with a waxy texture and a deep green to reddish underside. Its flowering stems are typically short and produce clusters of small, inconspicuous flowers.

Julocroton pyrosoma is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its dramatic leaf coloration and visual impact in shaded environments.
