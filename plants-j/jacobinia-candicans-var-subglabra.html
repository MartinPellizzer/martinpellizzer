Jacobinia candicans var. subglabra

Jacobinia candicans var. subglabra, commonly known as the smooth-leaved Jacobinia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped, smooth-textured leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers during the warm season. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
