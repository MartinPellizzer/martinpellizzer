Justicia scabra

Justicia scabra, commonly known as the roughleaf flameflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The leaves are typically rough to the touch, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
