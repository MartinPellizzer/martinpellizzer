Justicia triloba

Justicia triloba, commonly known as the three-lobed flame flower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with three distinct lobes, giving it its common name. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Justicia triloba is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
