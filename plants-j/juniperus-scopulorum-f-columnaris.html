Juniperus scopulorum f. columnaris

Juniperus scopulorum f. columnaris, commonly known as the Rocky Mountain juniper columnaris, is a compact, columnar form of the Rocky Mountain juniper.

It is characterized by its narrow, upright growth habit, making it an ideal choice for small spaces or rock gardens. The plant features scale-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color, providing a striking visual contrast. It produces small, berry-like cones that are typically blue or black, depending on the variety.

This cultivar is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it a hardy and low-maintenance ornamental plant.
