Justicia hayatae var. ciliata

Justicia hayatae var. ciliata, commonly known as the Ciliate Flame Flower, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. It thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
