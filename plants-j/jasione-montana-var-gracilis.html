Jasione montana var. gracilis

Jasione montana var. gracilis, commonly known as slender mountain jasmine, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Caryophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces small, white to pale pink flowers clustered in umbels. The leaves are narrow and linear, often with a slightly hairy texture. Native to the mountainous regions of Europe, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

This plant is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
