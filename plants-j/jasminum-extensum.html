Jasminum extensum

Jasminum extensum, commonly known as the evergreen jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties. It thrives in humid environments and is commonly used in gardens and as a ground cover.

Jasminum extensum is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
