Jambosa timorensis

Jambosa timorensis, commonly known as the Timor jambosa, is a small evergreen shrub native to the island of Timor.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers clustered in umbels, which are followed by distinctive, fleshy fruits.
