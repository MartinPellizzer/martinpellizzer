Justicia leucostachya

Justicia leucostachya, commonly known as the white flame plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking white to pale pink tubular flowers. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Justicia leucostachya is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
