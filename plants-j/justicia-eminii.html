Justicia eminii

Justicia eminii, commonly known as the skyflower, is a striking herbaceous plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that can grow up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color, arranged in dense clusters at the top of the stems. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, giving them a soft texture.

Justicia eminii is a popular choice for ornamental gardening due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
