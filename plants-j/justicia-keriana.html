Justicia keriana

Justicia keriana, commonly known as the flame violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to violet, often with contrasting yellow or white markings. The plant typically grows as an herb or shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

Justicia keriana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a potted plant.
