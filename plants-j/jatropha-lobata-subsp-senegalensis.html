Jatropha lobata subsp. senegalensis

Jatropha lobata subsp.

senegalensis, commonly known as the Senegalese cupseed, is a shrub native to tropical Africa. It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and showy yellow flowers.
