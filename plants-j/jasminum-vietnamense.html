Jasminum vietnamense

Jasminum vietnamense, commonly known as Vietnamese jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly Vietnam.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters tall, with opposite, ovate to elliptical leaves. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers that are typically clustered in terminal panicles, blooming in the summer months. Its leaves are usually glossy and dark green, while its stems are woody and often covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic flowers.
