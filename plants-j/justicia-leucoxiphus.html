Justicia leucoxiphus

Justicia leucoxiphus, commonly known as the silverleaf flamebush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it its common name. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in clusters, typically in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like birds and bees.
