Jasminum pellucidum

Jasminum pellucidum, commonly known as the white jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a smooth texture.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade.
