Justicia soukupii

Justicia soukupii, commonly known as the Mexican flame flower, is a perennial shrub native to Central America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant produces clusters of small, brightly colored flowers that attract hummingbirds and other pollinators. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Justicia soukupii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
