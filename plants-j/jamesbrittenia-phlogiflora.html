Jamesbrittenia phlogiflora

Jamesbrittenia phlogiflora, commonly known as the flame-flowered brittenia, is a rare and striking plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, flame-like orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters during the warm season. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and often have a waxy texture. Its unique floral structure attracts a variety of pollinators, including bees and butterflies.
