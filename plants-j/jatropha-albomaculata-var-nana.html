Jatropha albomaculata var. nana

Jatropha albomaculata var. nana, commonly known as the dwarf variegated physic nut, is a compact shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features strikingly variegated leaves with white and green patterns, and it produces small, yellowish flowers followed by orange-red berries. The plant is notable for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
