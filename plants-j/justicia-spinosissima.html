Justicia spinosissima

Justicia spinosissima, commonly known as the spiny justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking spikes of tubular flowers, typically in shades of red or purple. The plant features spiny leaves and stems, which serve as a defense mechanism against herbivores. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Justicia spinosissima is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
