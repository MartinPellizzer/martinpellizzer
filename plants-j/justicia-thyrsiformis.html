Justicia thyrsiformis

Justicia thyrsiformis, commonly known as the flame violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.

The flowers are typically arranged in dense, spike-like clusters, giving the plant its species name "thyrsiformis.".
