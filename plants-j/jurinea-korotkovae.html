Jurinea korotkovae

Jurinea korotkovae, commonly known as the Korotkov's ice plant, is a flowering plant native to the Arctic and alpine regions of Eurasia.

The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers during the short growing season, and its leaves are typically succulent to store water.
