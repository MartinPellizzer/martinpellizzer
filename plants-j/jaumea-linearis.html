Jaumea linearis

Jaumea linearis, commonly known as the narrowleaf jaumea, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, exhibiting a rosette of narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers arranged in a spike-like inflorescence. It is well adapted to saline and arid conditions, making it a hardy species.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
