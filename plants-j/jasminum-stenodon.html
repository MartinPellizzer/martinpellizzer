Jasminum stenodon

Jasminum stenodon, commonly known as the narrow-leaved jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, simple, and narrow leaves that are usually glossy in appearance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Jasminum stenodon is also used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
