Justicia humilis

Justicia humilis, commonly known as the common justicia, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States and parts of Mexico.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 feet. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are usually hairy and have a slightly serrated edge. Its showy, tubular flowers are typically blue to purple in color and bloom in late spring to early summer.

Justicia humilis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in partial shade to full sun.
