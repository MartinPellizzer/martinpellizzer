Justicia suratensis

Justicia suratensis, commonly known as the Surat flame or Surat flameflower, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture, while the flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
