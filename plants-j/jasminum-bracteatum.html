Jasminum bracteatum

Jasminum bracteatum, commonly known as the Chinese jasmine or winter jasmine, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub that produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers in winter. The plant has opposite, simple, and usually ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy. Its flowers are often used in traditional Chinese medicine and as ornamental plants.

Jasminum bracteatum thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
