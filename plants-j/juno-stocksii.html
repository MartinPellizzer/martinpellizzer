Juno stocksii

Juno stocksii, commonly known as the Juno stock.

It is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region, characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, pinkish-white flowers. The plant produces small, spherical fruits that contain several seeds. Juno stocksii thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom throughout the summer months.
