Julocroton humilis var. malmei

Julocroton humilis var. malmei, commonly known as the Malme's croton, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from yellow to red. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
