Jasminum annamense subsp. kerrii

Jasminum annamense subsp.

kerrii, commonly known as Kerri's jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly Vietnam. It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a subtle yellowish tinge.

The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and oval-shaped with a smooth texture.

It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in forested areas.
