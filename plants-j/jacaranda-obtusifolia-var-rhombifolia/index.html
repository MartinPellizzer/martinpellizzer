Jacaranda Obtusifolia Var. Rhombifolia

Blue jacaranda, scientifically known as Jacaranda obtusifolia var. rhombifolia, is a striking ornamental tree native to South America. It is celebrated for its clusters of vibrant blue flowers that bloom in the spring, creating a breathtaking visual display. The tree is often planted in urban settings due to its adaptability and aesthetic appeal. Its unique foliage and long-lasting blooms make it a favorite among gardeners and landscapers. Despite its beauty, it requires careful pruning and attention to thrive in different climates.
