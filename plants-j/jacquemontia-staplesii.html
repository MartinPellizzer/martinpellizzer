Jacquemontia staplesii

Jacquemontia staplesii, commonly known as the yellow trumpet vine, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a perennial vine that produces showy, yellow, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is characterized by its vigorous growth habit. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant blooms and its ability to climb or trail in various garden settings.
