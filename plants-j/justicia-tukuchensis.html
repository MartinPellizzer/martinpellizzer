Justicia tukuchensis

Justicia tukuchensis, commonly known as the Tukuchensis flame flower, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Peru.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in a fine pubescence, while its stems are square and somewhat hairy.

Justicia tukuchensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
