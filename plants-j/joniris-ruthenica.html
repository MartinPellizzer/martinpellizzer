Joniris ruthenica

Joniris ruthenica, commonly known as the Ruthenian bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the temperate regions of Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family, which includes many bell-shaped flowers. The plant features slender, upright stems with lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. Its showy blue or violet flowers bloom in summer, often in clusters at the ends of the stems.
