Juncoides campestris var. sudetica

Juncoides campestris var. sudetica, commonly known as the Alpine rush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to mountainous regions of Central and Eastern Europe.

It typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences with greenish or brownish flowers. It is adapted to cold climates and can tolerate harsh environmental conditions.

Its botanical features make it an important component of alpine ecosystems.
