Justicia desertorum

Justicia desertorum, commonly known as desert verbena, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northwestern Mexico.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet, featuring slender stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces vibrant spikes of blue to purple flowers, which bloom from spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it well-suited for xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for landscaping in dry climates.
