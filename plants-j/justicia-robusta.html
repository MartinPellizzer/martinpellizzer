Justicia robusta

Justicia robusta, commonly known as the flame bush, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, attracting pollinators like hummingbirds and bees. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Justicia robusta is often used in ornamental gardening for its vibrant floral display.
