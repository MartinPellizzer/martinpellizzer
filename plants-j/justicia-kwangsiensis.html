Justicia kwangsiensis

Justicia kwangsiensis, commonly known as the Guangxi justicia, is a flowering plant native to southern China.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking spikes of tubular, brightly colored flowers. The plant typically grows as an herb or shrub, with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.

Its blooming period usually occurs during the summer months, adding vibrant color to gardens and natural habitats.
