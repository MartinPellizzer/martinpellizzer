Jasminum wangii

Jasminum wangii, commonly known as the Chinese jasmine or Wang's jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the spring and summer months. The plant has oval-shaped, dark green leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Jasminum wangii is also valued in traditional medicine for its aromatic properties.
