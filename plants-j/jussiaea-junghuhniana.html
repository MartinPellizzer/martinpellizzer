Jussiaea junghuhniana

Jussiaea junghuhniana, commonly known as the slender water-plantain, is a herbaceous aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shallow freshwater habitats such as ponds, marshes, and slow-moving streams. The plant has long, narrow leaves that are often submerged or floating, with a waxy surface to reduce water loss. Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in spikes and bloom during the warmer months.
