Justicia flagelliformis

Justicia flagelliformis, commonly known as the hanging justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its long, slender stems that often trail or hang from supports, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for hanging baskets and vertical gardens.
