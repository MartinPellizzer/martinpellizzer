Justicia mannii

Justicia mannii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to pink. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

Justicia mannii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
