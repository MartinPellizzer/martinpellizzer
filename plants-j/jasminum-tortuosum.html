Jasminum tortuosum

Jasminum tortuosum, commonly known as the twisted jasmine, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has slender, twisting stems that often grow along the ground or climb supports. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

Jasminum tortuosum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its pleasant fragrance.
