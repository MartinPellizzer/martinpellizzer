Justicia suarezensis

Justicia suarezensis, commonly known as the Suarez's glorybower, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. Justicia suarezensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
