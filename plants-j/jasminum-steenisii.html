Jasminum steenisii

Jasminum steenisii, commonly known as the Java jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters tall, featuring opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves. The plant produces fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the rainy season. Its flowers are borne on pedicels and are characterized by their five petals and numerous stamens.

Jasminum steenisii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its aromatic blooms.
