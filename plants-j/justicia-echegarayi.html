Justicia echegarayi

Justicia echegarayi, commonly known as the Mexican flamebush, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height, featuring opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in clusters, typically in shades of red, orange, or yellow, which bloom from spring to summer. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The plant is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and hummingbirds.
