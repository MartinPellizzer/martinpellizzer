Justicia micrantha

Justicia micrantha, commonly known as the dwarf flame flower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically red or orange in color. The plant belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Justicia micrantha thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.
