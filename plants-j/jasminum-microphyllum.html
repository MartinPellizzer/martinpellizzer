Jasminum microphyllum

Jasminum microphyllum, commonly known as the dwarf jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has small, narrow leaves and a compact growth habit, making it suitable for container gardening. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun exposure.

Jasminum microphyllum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant fragrance.
