Juniperus chinensis

Juniperus chinensis, commonly known as Chinese juniper, is a species of evergreen coniferous shrub or small tree native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its scale-like leaves, which are typically blue-gray in color, and its small, berry-like cones that mature to a dark blue or black color. The plant is widely cultivated for its ornamental value, particularly in gardens and landscapes. It is also used in traditional Chinese medicine and as a source of aromatic wood.

Juniperus chinensis is adaptable to various soil types and can thrive in a range of climatic conditions.
