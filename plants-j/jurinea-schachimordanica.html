Jurinea schachimordanica

Jurinea schachimordanica, commonly known as the Schachimordan sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that thrives in sandy and gravelly soils, often found in desert or semi-desert environments. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are typically narrow and succulent, adapted to conserve water in harsh conditions.
