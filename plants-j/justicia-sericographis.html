Justicia sericographis

Justicia sericographis, commonly known as the silky-leaved justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped, silky-textured leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces clusters of tubular, pale blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Justicia sericographis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in warm climates.
