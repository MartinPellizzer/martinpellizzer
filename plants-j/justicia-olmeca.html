Justicia olmeca

Justicia olmeca, commonly known as the Olmec jewelweed, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a rough texture.

Justicia olmeca is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
