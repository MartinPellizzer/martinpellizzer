Justicia diversifolia

Justicia diversifolia, commonly known as the flame flower or rainbow plant, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, showy bracts that come in a variety of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a waxy texture.

It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
