Jussiaea ramulosa

Jussiaea ramulosa, commonly known as the slender water milfoil, is a aquatic plant native to freshwater environments in tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in shallow waters, forming dense mats on the surface or submerged vegetation. The plant has finely divided, feather-like leaves that help it float and absorb sunlight efficiently. Its small, green flowers are clustered in spikes and bloom during the warmer months.

Jussiaea ramulosa plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small fish and invertebrates.
