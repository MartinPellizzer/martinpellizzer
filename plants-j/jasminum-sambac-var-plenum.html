Jasminum sambac var. plenum

Jasminum sambac var. plenum, commonly known as the double-flowered jasmine, is a variant of the common jasmine that features densely packed, double blooms.

It is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions, characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant has dark green, ovate leaves and produces small, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value is heightened by its abundant, showy flowers that bloom throughout the growing season.
