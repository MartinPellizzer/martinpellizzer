Justicia takhinensis

Justicia takhinensis, commonly known as the red flame plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular red flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as birds and insects. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
