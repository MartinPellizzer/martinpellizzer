Juncoides leiboldii

Juncoides leiboldii, commonly known as the Leibold's rush, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Juncaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems that are typically green or reddish in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spike-like clusters, which are usually greenish or brownish in hue. It thrives in moist to wet environments, such as marshes, swamps, and along the edges of ponds.

Juncoides leiboldii is often found in temperate regions and is adapted to tolerate waterlogged soils.
