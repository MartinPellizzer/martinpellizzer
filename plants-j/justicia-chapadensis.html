Justicia chapadensis

Justicia chapadensis, commonly known as red flame or red hot poker, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking red-orange tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and can grow up to 3 meters tall, making it a prominent feature in gardens.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often used as an ornamental plant for its vibrant color and architectural form.
