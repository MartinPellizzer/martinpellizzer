Jubelina rosea

Jubelina rosea, commonly known as the roseum or the pink rose, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to parts of eastern North America, particularly the southeastern United States. The plant is characterized by its showy, pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a serrated margin, while its stems are often hairy.

Jubelina rosea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
