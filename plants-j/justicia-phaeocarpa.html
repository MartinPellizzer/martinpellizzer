Justicia phaeocarpa

Justicia phaeocarpa, commonly known as the purple-leaf justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range from purple to pink, often with a contrasting white or yellow throat. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are ovate to elliptic, often with a reddish or purple tinge, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
