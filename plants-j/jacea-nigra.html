Jacea nigra

Jacea nigra, commonly known as black nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black berries, which are toxic to humans and animals. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually oval or ovate in shape. It typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in various regions across Europe and Asia.

Jacea nigra is known for its showy, white or purple flowers that bloom in the summer months.
