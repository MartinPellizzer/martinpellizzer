Jacksonia sternbergiana f. pungens

Jacksonia sternbergiana f. pungens, commonly known as the pungent everlasting, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the daisy family, Asteraceae, and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like inflorescences. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

The 'pungens' variety is distinguished by its strong, aromatic scent and unique foliage structure.
