Juncus luxurians

Juncus luxurians, commonly known as the showy rush, is a tall, perennial plant native to wetland environments in North America.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of bright yellow or orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant thrives in moist to wet soils, often found in marshes, swamps, and along the edges of ponds. Its dense growth helps prevent erosion and provides habitat for various wildlife species.

Juncus luxurians is valued in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and support biodiversity.
