Justicia mcdadeana

Justicia mcdadeana, commonly known as the Mexican flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to Mexico and Central America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture and thrives in warm, humid climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.

The species is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
