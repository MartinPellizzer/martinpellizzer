Justicia cuspidulata

Justicia cuspidulata, commonly known as the flame flower or red flame plant, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It features striking, tubular red flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators like hummingbirds and bees. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
