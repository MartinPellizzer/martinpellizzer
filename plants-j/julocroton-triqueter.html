Julocroton triqueter

Julocroton triqueter, commonly known as the three-lobed croton, is a tropical plant native to regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with leaves that often display vibrant yellow, orange, and red hues. The plant belongs to the Crotonaceae family and is known for its unique leaf morphology, featuring three distinct lobes. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, typically greenish-yellow in color, and it produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Julocroton triqueter is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
