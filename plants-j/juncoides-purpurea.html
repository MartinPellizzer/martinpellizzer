Juncoides purpurea

Juncoides purpurea, commonly known as the purple rush, is a perennial plant belonging to the Juncaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and produces spikes of purple to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense colonies.

Juncoides purpurea is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in wetland habitats.
