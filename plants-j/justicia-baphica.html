Justicia baphica

Justicia baphica, commonly known as the purple flame or flame flower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that produces striking, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and purple. The plant belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy bracts that resemble petals. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

Justicia baphica thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
