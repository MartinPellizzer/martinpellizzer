Jasminum delavayi

Jasminum delavayi, commonly known as the Chinese jasmine, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer seasons. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate, with a glossy green surface.

Jasminum delavayi is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its pleasant aroma.
