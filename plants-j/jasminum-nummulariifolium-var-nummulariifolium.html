Jasminum nummulariifolium var. nummulariifolium

Jasminum nummulariifolium var. nummulariifolium, commonly known as the golden jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, rounded, glossy leaves and clusters of fragrant, yellowish-white flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its persistent foliage and showy blooms, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
