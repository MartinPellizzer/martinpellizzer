Jurinea khorassanica

Jurinea khorassanica, commonly known as the Persian sandwort, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming structure. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically succulent, adapted to store water in its native dry environment.
