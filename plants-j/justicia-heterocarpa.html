Justicia heterocarpa

Justicia heterocarpa, commonly known as the variable flame flower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with contrasting white or yellow markings. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Justicia heterocarpa thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
