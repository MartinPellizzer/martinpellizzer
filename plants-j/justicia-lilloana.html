Justicia lilloana

Justicia lilloana, commonly known as the flame violet, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The flowers are arranged in whorled clusters, making it a visually appealing ornamental plant.
