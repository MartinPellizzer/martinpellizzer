Julocroton gardneri

Julocroton gardneri, commonly known as the Gardner's croton, is a tropical shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically green with prominent veins and sometimes variegated patterns. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-drained soil.

Julocroton gardneri is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage and unique fruiting structures.
