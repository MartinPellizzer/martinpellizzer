Justicia paucinervis

Justicia paucinervis, commonly known as the narrow-veined jewelweed, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from orange to red, often with a contrasting yellow or white throat. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Justicia paucinervis is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
