Justicia maguirei

Justicia maguirei, commonly known as the Mexican flame flower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

Justicia maguirei is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in tropical and subtropical climates.
