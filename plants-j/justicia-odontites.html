Justicia odontites

Justicia odontites, commonly known as the toothed justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking spikes of tubular, two-lipped flowers. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb with opposite, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
