Justicia umbratilis

Justicia umbratilis, commonly known as the shade verbena, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in shaded or semi-shaded environments. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are often violet or blue in color. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and usually have a slightly hairy texture.

Justicia umbratilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in less sunny conditions.
