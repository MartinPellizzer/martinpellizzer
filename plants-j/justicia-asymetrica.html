Justicia asymetrica

Justicia asymetrica, commonly known as the asymmetric jewelweed, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features striking, asymmetrical flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with red or purple markings. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with a rough texture and prominent veins.

Justicia asymetrica is often used in ornamental gardening for its unique floral display and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
