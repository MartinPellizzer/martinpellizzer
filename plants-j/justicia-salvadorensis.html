Justicia salvadorensis

Justicia salvadorensis, commonly known as the Mexican verbena, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant purple or pink tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or perennial herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.
