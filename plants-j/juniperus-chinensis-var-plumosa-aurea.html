Juniperus chinensis var. plumosa-aurea

Juniperus chinensis var. plumosa-aurea, commonly known as the golden thread juniper, is a cultivar of the Chinese juniper.

It is characterized by its fine, feathery foliage that displays a golden-yellow hue, especially in the sun. The plant has a compact, rounded growth habit and produces small, blue-black berries in the winter. It is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive appearance and hardiness.

This variety thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and as a border plant.
