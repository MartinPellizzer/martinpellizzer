Jacobaea × lyratifolia

Jacobaea × lyratifolia, commonly known as the painted lady.

This hybrid plant is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. It produces bright yellow to orange flowers with a central dark purple disc, attracting pollinators such as butterflies and bees. The plant typically grows in sunny, well-drained soils and is native to regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ease of care.
