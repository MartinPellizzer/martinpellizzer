Jasminum Fruticans Var. Mariae

Jasminum fruticans var. mariae is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia. Known for its fragrant white flowers and evergreen foliage, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. This variety is particularly prized for its ornamental value and unique scent. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively unknown to the general public. Cultivating this plant requires careful attention to soil and climate conditions.
