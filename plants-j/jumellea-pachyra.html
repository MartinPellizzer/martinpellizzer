Jumellea pachyra

Jumellea pachyra, commonly known as the thick-stemmed jumellea, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its swollen, fleshy stems that store water, allowing it to survive in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are usually small and scale-like, reducing water loss through transpiration.

Jumellea pachyra is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and resilience.
