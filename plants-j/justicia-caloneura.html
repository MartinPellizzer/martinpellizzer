Justicia caloneura

Justicia caloneura, commonly known as the narrow-leafed flame flower, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height, characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking clusters of tubular, red or orange flowers that bloom during the summer months. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its foliage and floral display make it a valuable addition to both garden landscapes and botanical collections.
