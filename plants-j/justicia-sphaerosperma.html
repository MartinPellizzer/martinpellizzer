Justicia sphaerosperma

Justicia sphaerosperma, commonly known as the globeflower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, globe-shaped flower heads. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red, arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and unique appearance.
