Juniperus souliei

Juniperus souliei, commonly known as the Chinese juniper, is a slow-growing evergreen shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, needle-like foliage that is typically dark green and retains its color throughout the year. The plant produces small, blue-black berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its hardiness and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal and ecological benefits.
