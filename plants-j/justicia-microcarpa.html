Justicia microcarpa

Justicia microcarpa, commonly known as the small-fruited flamebeard, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as a shrub or subshrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Justicia microcarpa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
