Jovibarba globifera var. lagariniana

Jovibarba globifera var. lagariniana, commonly known as the Alpine stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped growth pattern and fleshy, often blue-green leaves that store water. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Due to its hardiness and ornamental value, it is widely cultivated in alpine and Mediterranean gardens.
