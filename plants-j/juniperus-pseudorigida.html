Juniperus pseudorigida

Juniperus pseudorigida, commonly known as the Chinese juniper, is a species of evergreen conifer native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, scale-like foliage and small, blue-gray needles. The plant produces berries that are typically dark blue or purple, containing aromatic oils. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Juniperus pseudorigida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in horticulture for landscaping purposes.
