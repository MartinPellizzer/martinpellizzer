Juncoides canariensis

Juncoides canariensis, commonly known as the Canary Island rush, is a species of flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the family Juncaceae, which includes rushes and sedges. This plant is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It typically grows in wet or marshy habitats, thriving in moist environments.

Juncoides canariensis is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to the unique climatic conditions of the Canary Islands.
