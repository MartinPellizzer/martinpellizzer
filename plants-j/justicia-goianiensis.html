Justicia goianiensis

Justicia goianiensis, commonly known as the Goiania flame flower, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in a fine pubescence.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
