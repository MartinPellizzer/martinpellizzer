Justicia petiolaris subsp. bowiei

Justicia petiolaris subsp.

bowiei, commonly known as the red-leaf justicia, is a flowering plant native to Mexico. It is characterized by its striking red or orange-red leaves, which contrast with its narrow, grass-like leaves.

The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically blooming in summer. It is a perennial herb that thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
