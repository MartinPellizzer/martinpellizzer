Justicia stellata

Justicia stellata, commonly known as star cluster or starflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or herb, featuring opposite leaves and vibrant, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, contributing to its ecological significance.
