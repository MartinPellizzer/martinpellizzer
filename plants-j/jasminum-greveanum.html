Jasminum greveanum

Jasminum greveanum, commonly known as the Chinese jasmine, is a flowering plant native to China and parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub that produces fragrant white flowers during the spring and summer months. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually oval in shape and have a smooth texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Jasminum greveanum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
