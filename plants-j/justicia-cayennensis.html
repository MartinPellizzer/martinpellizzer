Justicia cayennensis

Justicia cayennensis, commonly known as the Cayenne flame or red flame plant, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and a slightly hairy underside. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-drained soil.

Justicia cayennensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
