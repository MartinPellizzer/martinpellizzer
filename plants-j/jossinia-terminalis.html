Jossinia terminalis

Jossinia terminalis, commonly known as the slender flame tree, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant is typically found in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in moist, well-drained soils. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Jossinia terminalis plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and nectar for pollinators.
