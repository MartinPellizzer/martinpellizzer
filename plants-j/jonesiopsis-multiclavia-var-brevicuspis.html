Jonesiopsis multiclavia var. brevicuspis

Jonesiopsis multiclavia var. brevicuspis, commonly known as the short-spined Jonesiopsis, is a unique and rare fern species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate fronds with short, rounded spines along the margins, distinguishing it from other varieties of the genus. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in tropical rainforests. Its reproductive structures are clustered in compact, spore-producing clusters called sori, which are located on the undersides of the fronds.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
