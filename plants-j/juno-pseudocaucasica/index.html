Juno Pseudocaucasica

The Juno pseudocaucasica is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus. It is known for its unique appearance, featuring bright yellow flowers that stand out against its green foliage. This species thrives in rocky and well-drained soils, often growing in high-altitude environments. Due to its limited habitat and increasing threats from climate change, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this beautiful and ecologically important plant.
