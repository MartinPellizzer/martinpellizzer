Justicia hatschbachii

Justicia hatschbachii, commonly known as the pink-flowered justicia, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or subshrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a velvety texture.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.
