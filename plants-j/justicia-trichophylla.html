Justicia trichophylla

Justicia trichophylla, commonly known as the silky justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and violet. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Justicia trichophylla is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
