Jacquemontia densifolia

Jacquemontia densifolia, commonly known as the denseleaf morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a dense, velvety texture, and its flowers are trumpet-shaped, often displaying vibrant shades of blue or purple. It produces capsule fruits that contain several small, oblong seeds.

Jacquemontia densifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
