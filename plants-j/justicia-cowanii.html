Justicia cowanii

Justicia cowanii, commonly known as the coral torch, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, bright red bracts that resemble torches. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale pink. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
