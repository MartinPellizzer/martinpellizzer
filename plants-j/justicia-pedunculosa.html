Justicia pedunculosa

Justicia pedunculosa, commonly known as red hot poker, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in dense spikes. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in opposite pairs along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its botanical features make it a visually appealing addition to landscapes and a subject of interest in horticultural studies.
