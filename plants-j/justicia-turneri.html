Justicia turneri

Justicia turneri, commonly known as the Mexican flame flower, is a herbaceous perennial native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It features striking, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators like hummingbirds and bees. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
