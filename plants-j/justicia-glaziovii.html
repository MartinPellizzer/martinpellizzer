Justicia glaziovii

Justicia glaziovii, commonly known as the Glaziov's flame flower, is a striking tropical plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or orange tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators like hummingbirds and bees. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters, with lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. Its stems are often covered in fine hairs, and it thrives in full sun to partial shade with well-drained soil.

The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and is commonly cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
