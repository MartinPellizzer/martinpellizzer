Justicia mirabiloides

Justicia mirabiloides, commonly known as the flame flower, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees. The plant has lance-shaped, opposite leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Justicia mirabiloides is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant floral display.
