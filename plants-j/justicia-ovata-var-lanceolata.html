Justicia ovata var. lanceolata

Justicia ovata var. lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceleaf flame flower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and striking clusters of tubular, brightly colored flowers, typically in shades of red, orange, or yellow. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
