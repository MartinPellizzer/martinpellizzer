Juniperus franchetiana

Juniperus franchetiana, commonly known as the Chinese juniper, is a small evergreen shrub or tree native to China.

Juniperus franchetiana is also noted for its ability to thrive in harsh climates, making it a resilient addition to gardens and landscapes.
