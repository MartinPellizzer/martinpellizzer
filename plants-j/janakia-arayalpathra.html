Janakia arayalpathra

Janakia arayalpathra, commonly known as the Malabar nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is native to the Western Ghats of India and thrives in moist, tropical climates. The plant features small, star-shaped flowers that are typically purple or white in color. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Janakia arayalpathra is often found in forested areas and is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value.
