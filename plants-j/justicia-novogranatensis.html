Justicia novogranatensis

Justicia novogranatensis, commonly known as the "Red Trumpet" or "Flame of the Forest," is a striking tropical shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators like hummingbirds and bees. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
