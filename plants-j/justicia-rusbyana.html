Justicia rusbyana

Justicia rusbyana, commonly known as Rusby's glorybower, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.
