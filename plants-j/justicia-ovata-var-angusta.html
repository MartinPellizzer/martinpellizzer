Justicia ovata var. angusta

Justicia ovata var. angusta, commonly known as narrow-leafed flamebeard, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant produces clusters of bracts that resemble flowers, which are often mistaken for the actual blooms. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
