Julocroton pilosus

Julocroton pilosus, commonly known as the hairy croton, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that are often covered in fine, hairy textures. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in clusters, typically in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Julocroton pilosus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and colorful blooms.
