Justicia campestris

Justicia campestris, commonly known as the common flame flower, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features striking, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators like hummingbirds and bees. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Justicia campestris is often used in gardens for its vibrant floral display and adaptability to various growing conditions.
