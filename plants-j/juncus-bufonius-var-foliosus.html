Juncus bufonius var. foliosus

Juncus bufonius var. foliosus, commonly known as the frog's-bit sedge, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Cyperaceae.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences.
