Jasminum prainii

Jasminum prainii, commonly known as the Philippine jasmine, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring aromatic white flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate with a smooth surface. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain seeds.

It is valued for its fragrance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
