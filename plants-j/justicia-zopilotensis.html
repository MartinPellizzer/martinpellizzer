Justicia zopilotensis

Justicia zopilotensis, commonly known as the Zopilotan flameflower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
