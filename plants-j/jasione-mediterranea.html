Jasione mediterranea

Jasione mediterranea, commonly known as the Mediterranean bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features basal rosettes of lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of blue to violet bell-shaped flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Jasione mediterranea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
