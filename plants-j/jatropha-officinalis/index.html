Jatropha Officinalis

Jatropha officinalis is a flowering plant known for its toxic properties and medicinal uses. It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia. The plant contains a variety of bioactive compounds that have been studied for their potential health benefits. Despite its toxicity, it has been traditionally used in herbal medicine for various ailments. Its unique appearance and chemical composition make it an intriguing subject for scientific research.
