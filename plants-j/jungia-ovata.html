Jungia ovata

Jungia ovata, commonly known as the broad-leafed jungle plant, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, ovate leaves that are typically smooth and glossy, with a deep green color. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by oval, woody fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in warm climates.
