Jasminum greenii

Jasminum greenii, commonly known as the green jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters, often releasing a sweet scent during the night. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves and a climbing or shrub-like growth habit. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Jasminum greenii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.
