Jasminum mairei

Jasminum mairei, commonly known as the Chinese jasmine, is a flowering plant native to China and Vietnam.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant produces fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a glossy green surface.

Jasminum mairei is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in traditional Chinese medicine.
