Juncus yoskisukei

Juncus yoskisukei, commonly known as the Yoskisukei rush, is a species of flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Juncaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems that grow in dense clusters. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish or brownish in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along the edges of ponds.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
