Jasminum annamense subsp. annamense

Jasminum annamense subsp.

annamense, commonly known as the Annam jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters.

The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate with a smooth margin.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation in tropical and subtropical regions.
