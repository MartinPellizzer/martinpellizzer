Jonthlaspi microcarpum

Jonthlaspi microcarpum, commonly known as the small-fruited pennycress, is a member of the Brassicaceae family.

It is a low-growing annual herb that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features basal leaves that are pinnately lobed and are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, yellow flowers are arranged in racemes and bloom in early spring.

The fruit is a small, capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
