Justicia funckii

Justicia funckii, commonly known as the flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often resembling flames. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Justicia funckii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
