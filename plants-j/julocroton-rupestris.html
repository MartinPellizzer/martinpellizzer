Julocroton rupestris

Julocroton rupestris, commonly known as the rock croton, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed stems and vibrant, colorful leaves that often display shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are typically greenish-yellow and appear in clusters.

Julocroton rupestris is prized for its ornamental value and ability to withstand drought conditions.
