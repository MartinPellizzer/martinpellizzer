Jarava Carettei

Jarava carettei is a unique grass species native to the arid regions of the Arabian Peninsula. It thrives in harsh environments with minimal water and extreme temperatures. This plant is known for its hardy nature and ability to stabilize soil in degraded areas. Its slender, upright stems and narrow leaves make it easily identifiable. Despite its resilience, it remains a lesser-known but ecologically significant plant in desert ecosystems.
