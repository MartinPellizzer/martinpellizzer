Justicia paracambi

Justicia paracambi, commonly known as the paracambi flame flower, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly velvety texture.

Justicia paracambi thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
