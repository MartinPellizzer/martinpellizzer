Jonesia minor

Jonesia minor, commonly known as the lesser jonesia, is a small, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring dense, glossy dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by orange-red berries containing a single seed. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and ornamental horticulture.
