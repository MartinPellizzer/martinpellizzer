Jambosa suzukii

Jambosa suzukii, commonly known as the sour orange, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers and small, sour-tasting fruits. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and prefers well-drained soil.

The fruit is used in culinary applications and traditional medicine.
