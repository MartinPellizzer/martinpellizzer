Justicia schultesii

Justicia schultesii, commonly known as the Schultesi's flameflower, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth surface and a slightly waxy texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Justicia schultesii is relatively easy to grow in well-draining soil and full sun to partial shade.
