Justicia latifolia

Justicia latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and clusters of striking blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
