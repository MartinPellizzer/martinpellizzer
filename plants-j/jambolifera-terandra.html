Jambolifera terandra

Jambolifera terandra, commonly known as the Indian date palm, is a flowering plant belonging to the Arecaceae family.

It is native to the Indian subcontinent and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, fan-shaped leaves. Its fruit, resembling a date, is edible and has a sweet, fibrous texture.
