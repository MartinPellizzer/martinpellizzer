Justicia hirsuta

Justicia hirsuta, commonly known as hairy justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces clusters of showy, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often found in moist woodlands or along stream banks.

Justicia hirsuta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
