Justicia javanica

Justicia javanica, commonly known as the Javan flame or red flame bush, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are opposite and often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Justicia javanica is also used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
