Jatropha rivae

Jatropha rivae, commonly known as the river jatropha, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in moist, tropical environments. The plant produces yellow flowers that are followed by capsules containing toxic seeds. Its leaves are alternate and ovate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Jatropha rivae is often found near rivers and in swampy areas, where it thrives in humid conditions.
