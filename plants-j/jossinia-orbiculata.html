Jossinia orbiculata

Jossinia orbiculata, commonly known as the globe-shaped orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, rounded growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant has fleshy, strap-like leaves that help it store water in its native arid environment. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Jossinia orbiculata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and long-lasting blooms.
