Justicia bicalcarata

Justicia bicalcarata, commonly known as the two-horned justicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and southern Asia.

Justicia bicalcarata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
