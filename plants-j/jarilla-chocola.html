Jarilla chocola

Jarilla chocola, commonly known as chocolate weed, is a low-growing plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Jarilla chocola is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it a hardy plant suited for xeriscaping.
