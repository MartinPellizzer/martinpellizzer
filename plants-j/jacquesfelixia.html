Jacquesfelixia

Jacquesfelixia, commonly known as the golden star hibiscus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, yellow-centered flowers with white petals, which bloom throughout the year in warm climates. The plant has heart-shaped, broad leaves that are typically green and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Jacquesfelixia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
