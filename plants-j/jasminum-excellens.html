Jasminum excellens

Jasminum excellens, commonly known as the Chinese jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate, with a glossy green surface.

Jasminum excellens is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in gardens and as a hedge plant.
