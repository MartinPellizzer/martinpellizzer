Justicia delascioi

Justicia delascioi, commonly known as the Mexican flame flower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. Its flowers bloom in clusters and are adapted to attract hummingbirds and other pollinators.

Justicia delascioi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
