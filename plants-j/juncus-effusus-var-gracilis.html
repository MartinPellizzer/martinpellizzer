Juncus effusus var. gracilis

Juncus effusus var. gracilis, commonly known as soft rush, is a flowering plant in the family Juncaceae.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its slender, grass-like stems that grow in dense clusters. The plant produces small, spike-like clusters of greenish-white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in wet or moist environments such as marshes, swales, and along the edges of ponds.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense vegetation in suitable habitats.
