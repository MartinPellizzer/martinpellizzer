Justicia californica var. californica

Justicia californica var. californica, commonly known as the California flame flower, is a herbaceous perennial native to California.

It features striking, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its showy blooms attract pollinators like hummingbirds and butterflies.
