Julocroton villosissimus var. valenzuellae

Julocroton villosissimus var. valenzuellae, commonly known as the Valenzuelan croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves with prominent veins and a hairy texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in gardens.
