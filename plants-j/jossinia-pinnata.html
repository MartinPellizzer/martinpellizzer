Jossinia pinnata

Jossinia pinnata, commonly known as the silverleaf jossinia, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, palmately lobed leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It is typically found in moist, shaded habitats in tropical regions.

Jossinia pinnata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
