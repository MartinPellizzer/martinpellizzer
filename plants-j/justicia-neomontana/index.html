Justicia Neomontana

Neomontan justicia, scientifically known as Justicia neomontana, is a rare and unique flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Southeast Asia. This striking species belongs to the Acanthaceae family, which is known for its vibrant and showy blooms. Its distinctive appearance, featuring vivid blue and purple flowers, makes it a standout in botanical gardens and conservation efforts. Despite its beauty, Justicia neomontana is critically endangered due to habitat loss and environmental changes. Conservationists are working tirelessly to protect and propagate this rare plant to ensure its survival for future generations.
