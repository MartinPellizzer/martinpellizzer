Justicia stearnii var. stearnii

Justicia stearnii var. stearnii, commonly known as the Cape rose or red-hot poker, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a textured appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens in warmer climates.

Its unique floral display makes it a valuable addition to both landscape design and botanical collections.
