Jambosa keroantha

Jambosa keroantha, commonly known as the Keroantha jambosa, is a tropical plant native to regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are typically elliptical in shape with a smooth margin. Its flowers are small and clustered, often white or pale yellow, and it produces edible berries that are rich in nutrients.

The plant thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland forest areas.
