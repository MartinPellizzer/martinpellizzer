Juncus lomatophyllus var. congestus

Juncus lomatophyllus var. congestus, commonly known as the dense rush, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the family Juncaceae and is characterized by its dense, cylindrical clusters of grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or brown in color. It thrives in wet or moist environments such as marshes, stream banks, and wet meadows.

Its botanical structure makes it well adapted to aquatic or semi-aquatic habitats.
