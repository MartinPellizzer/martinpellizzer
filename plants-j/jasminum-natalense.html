Jasminum natalense

Jasminum natalense, commonly known as Natal jasmine, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, featuring woody stems and aromatic, white flowers. The plant produces small, fragrant blooms that appear in clusters, often in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

Natal jasmine is valued for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its pleasant fragrance.
