Jasminum sinense

Jasminum sinense, commonly known as Chinese jasmine, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white or yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or vine, with opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves. Its flowers are highly aromatic, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Jasminum sinense is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and as a climbing plant.
