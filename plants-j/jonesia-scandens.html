Jonesia scandens

Jonesia scandens, commonly known as the climbing jonesia, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and heart-shaped leaves with a smooth, waxy texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by small, round fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens.
