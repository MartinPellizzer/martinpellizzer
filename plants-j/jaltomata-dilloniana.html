Jaltomata dilloniana

Jaltomata dilloniana, commonly known as Dillon's groundcherry, is a species of flowering plant in the Solanaceae family.

It is native to the southeastern United States, particularly Florida and Georgia. The plant produces small, yellow-orange berries that are edible when ripe. It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as sandhill prairies and hammocks.
