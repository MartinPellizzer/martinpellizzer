Jasminum suberosum

Jasminum suberosum, commonly known as the African jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Africa and the Arabian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its woody stems and fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are typically opposite in arrangement. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Jasminum suberosum is also valued for its use in traditional medicine.
