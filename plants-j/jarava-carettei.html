Jarava carettei

Jarava carettei, commonly known as the Cape York grass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is often used in landscaping for its drought tolerance and ornamental value.

The species is also valued for its role in stabilizing soil in degraded areas.
