Jalapa officinalis

Jalapa officinalis, commonly known as the common jalap, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Liliaceae and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, bell-shaped flowers. The plant is notable for its medicinal properties, particularly the use of its seeds in traditional medicine. The seeds contain compounds such as sennosides, which are used as laxatives.
