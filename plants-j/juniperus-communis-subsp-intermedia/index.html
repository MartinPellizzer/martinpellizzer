Juniperus Communis Subsp. Intermedia

Juniperus communis subsp. intermedia is a hardy evergreen shrub or small tree known for its adaptability to harsh environments. It thrives in cold climates and is commonly found in northern regions of the northern hemisphere. This subspecies is characterized by its dense foliage and small, scale-like leaves. It produces blue-black berries that are both ornamental and used in traditional medicine. Its resilience makes it a valuable plant for ecological restoration and landscaping.
