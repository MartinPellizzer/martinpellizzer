Jasione perennis var. gracilis

Jasione perennis var. gracilis, commonly known as slender everlasting, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features slender, upright stems covered in small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in early summer. The leaves are narrow and linear, often with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable plant for rock gardens or borders.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
