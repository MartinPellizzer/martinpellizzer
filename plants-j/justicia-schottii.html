Justicia schottii

Justicia schottii, commonly known as Schott's flamebeard, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. Justicia schottii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
