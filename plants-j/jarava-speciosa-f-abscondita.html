Jarava speciosa f. abscondita

Jarava speciosa f. abscondita, commonly known as the hidden jarava, is a rare variant of the jarava plant native to specific regions of California.

It is a perennial grass characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, blade-like leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish or brownish in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal scrub and chaparral habitats.
