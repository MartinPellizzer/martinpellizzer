cassia senna

Cassia senna capsules, also known as senna capsules, are a natural laxative supplement made from the dried leaves of the Cassia senna plant. They work by stimulating the muscles in the intestines to contract and move stool through the digestive system, often providing relief from constipation. However, it is essential to use cassia senna capsules in moderation and under the guidance of a healthcare professional, as excessive use can lead to dependency and other health issues.
