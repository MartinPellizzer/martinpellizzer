alpinia officinarum capsule

intro

The Alpinia officinarum, also known as lesser galangal, is a plant native to Southeast Asia and is often used in traditional medicine. The dried and powdered capsule of Alpinia officinarum is used to treat various health issues, including digestive problems and fever. However, its use should be carefully monitored due to potential side effects and interactions with other medications.
