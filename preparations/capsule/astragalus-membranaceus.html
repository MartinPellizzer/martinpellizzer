astragalus membranaceus capsule

intro

Astragalus membranaceus, also known as Huang qi, is a traditional Chinese medicinal herb used to enhance the immune system and improve overall health. The root of the astragalus plant is dried and processed into a capsule, which is commonly taken in supplement form to promote well-being and reduce inflammation. Astragalus membranaceus capsules are often used in combination with other herbs to enhance their effects and provide a natural boost to the body's defenses.
