intro

Atropa belladonna, also known as deadly nightshade, is a highly toxic plant that has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes, including pain relief and sedation. However, its capsule is not a recommended or safe form of consumption due to the plant's extremely toxic properties. Ingesting Atropa belladonna can cause serious health complications, including hallucinations, seizures, and even death.
