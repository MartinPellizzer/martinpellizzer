acorus calamus capsule

intro

The Acorus calamus capsule is a dried, fruit-like structure found on the Acorus calamus plant, also known as sweet flag. It is typically green or brown in color and contains numerous seeds that are dispersed when the capsule opens. The capsule plays a crucial role in the plant's reproduction, allowing it to spread and propagate in wetland environments.
