arsculus hippocastanum capsule

intro

The capsule of Arsculus hippocastanum, also known as the horse chestnut tree, is a spiky, star-shaped fruit that contains 2-4 seeds. The capsule is typically 3-5 cm in diameter and has 4-6 pointed calyx lobes, which are greenish-brown in color. The seeds within the capsule are surrounded by a sweet, white pulp, and the entire fruit is highly visible and distinctive during the summer and autumn months.
