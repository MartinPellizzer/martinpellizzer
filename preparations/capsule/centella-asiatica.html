How to make Centella asiatica capsule for medicinal use?

To make centella asiatica capsule for medicinal use, the process typically involves drying and processing the leaves of the centella asiatica plant, which is rich in medicinal compounds such as asiaticoside and madecassoside.

The dried leaves are then powdered and mixed with a suitable excipient, such as starch or cellulose, to create a uniform blend.
