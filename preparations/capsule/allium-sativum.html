allium sativum capsule

intro

The Allium sativum capsule, also known as the garlic capsule, is a reproductive structure found in the garlic plant. It contains seeds that are dispersed through the wind and help in the propagation of the plant. The capsule is a key component in the life cycle of the garlic plant, allowing it to reproduce and spread its species.
