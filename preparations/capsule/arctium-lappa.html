arctium lappa capsule

intro

The capsule of Arctium lappa, also known as the burdock plant, is a dry, schizocarpic fruit that splits into several mericarps. Each mericarp has a single seed and is attached to the central axis by a short stalk. The capsule is typically brown or gray in color and contains spines or burrs, which help in dispersal of the seeds.
