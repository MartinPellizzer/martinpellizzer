artemisia annua capsule

intro

Artemisia annua, also known as sweet wormwood, is a plant-based source of artemisinin, a compound used to treat malaria. The capsules made from the plant are often standardized to contain a specific concentration of artemisinin, which can help alleviate symptoms and prevent the progression of the disease. However, it's essential to consult a healthcare professional before using artemisia annua capsules, as they may interact with other medications or have side effects in some individuals.
