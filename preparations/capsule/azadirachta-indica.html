azadirachta indica capsule

intro

Azadirachta indica, commonly known as neem, has been used in traditional medicine for centuries due to its numerous health benefits. The neem capsule, made from the seeds of the neem tree, is a concentrated form of the plant's bioactive compounds, which have been shown to possess anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties. When taken as a dietary supplement, the neem capsule may help to support immune function, reduce oxidative stress, and promote overall well-being.
