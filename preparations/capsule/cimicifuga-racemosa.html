What To Know About Medicinal Cimicifuga Racemosa Capsule?

The capsule of Cimicifuga racemosa, also known as black cohosh, is a dry, dehiscent fruit that splits open to release its seeds. Once mature, the capsule dries and splits open, allowing the seeds to disperse, contributing to the plant's ability to propagate and spread.
