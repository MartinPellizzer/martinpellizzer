chamomilla recutita

Chamomilla recutita essential oil, also known as German chamomile oil, is derived from the flowers of the Matricaria chamomilla plant and is commonly used in aromatherapy for its calming and soothing properties. It is known for its ability to reduce anxiety and stress, promote relaxation, and improve sleep quality. Chamomilla recutita essential oil is also used topically to soothe skin irritations, inflammation, and minor burns, making it a popular natural remedy for skin care.
