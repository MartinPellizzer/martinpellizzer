cymbopogon citratus infusion

intro

Cymbopogon citratus infusion, also known as lemongrass tea, is a natural remedy often used to aid digestion and relieve symptoms of nausea and indigestion. The infusion is made by steeping the leaves and stems of the lemongrass plant in hot water, releasing its oils and compounds that provide a range of health benefits. Drinking lemongrass tea has also been linked to reducing stress and promoting a good night's sleep due to its calming and soothing effects.
