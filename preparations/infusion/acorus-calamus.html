acorus calamus infusion

intro

Acorus calamus infusion, also known as sweet cicely or calamus, is a herbal remedy traditionally used to calm the nervous system and promote relaxation. It is often prepared by steeping dried root or rhizome in hot water, releasing its calming properties and sweet, earthy flavor. The infusion is commonly consumed as a tea or added to baths for its soothing and sedative effects.
