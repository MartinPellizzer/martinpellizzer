angelica sinensis infusion

intro

Angelica sinensis infusion, also known as dong quai, is a traditional Chinese herbal tea made from the roots of the Angelica sinensis plant. This infusion is often used to promote women's health and alleviate symptoms associated with menstruation, menopause, and infertility. It is also believed to have antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties, which may help to improve overall well-being and reduce stress.
