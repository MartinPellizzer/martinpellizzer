rosmarinus officinalis infusion

intro

Rosmarinus officinalis, commonly known as rosemary, has been used for centuries as a herbal remedy, and its infusion is often consumed to aid in digestion and mental clarity. Made by steeping dried rosemary leaves in hot water, the infusion is a popular choice for those seeking a natural energy boost and improved focus. Traditionally, rosemary infusion is also used to help alleviate headaches and promote a sense of calmness and relaxation.
