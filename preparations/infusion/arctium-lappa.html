Arctium lappa, also known as burdock root, is a popular herbal remedy that has been used for centuries to promote digestive health and reduce inflammation. The infusion made from its roots is said to have a soothing effect on the digestive system, making it a natural remedy for issues such as bloating and indigestion. Additionally, arctium lappa infusion is also believed to have antioxidant properties, which may help to protect the body against free radicals and oxidative stress.
