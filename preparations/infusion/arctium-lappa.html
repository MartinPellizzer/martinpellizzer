arctium lappa infusion

intro

Arctium lappa, also known as burdock root, has been used in herbal medicine for centuries to promote digestive health and reduce inflammation. The infusion made from the root of this plant is believed to have antioxidant properties, which can help to protect the body from oxidative stress and damage. Drinking arctium lappa infusion may also support the immune system and promote overall well-being, although further research is needed to confirm its effects.
