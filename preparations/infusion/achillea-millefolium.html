achillea millefolium infusion

intro

Achillea millefolium, also known as yarrow, is a medicinal herb traditionally used to make a soothing and anti-inflammatory infusion. The infusion is often used to calm digestive issues, reduce menstrual cramps, and ease cold and flu symptoms due to its antimicrobial and antispasmodic properties. When consumed in moderation, yarrow infusion is also believed to have antioxidant effects and promote overall well-being.
