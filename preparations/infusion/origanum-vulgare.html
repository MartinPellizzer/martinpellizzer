origanum vulgare infusion

intro

Origanum vulgare, also known as wild marjoram, is a popular herb used to make a soothing and flavorful infusion. When steeped in hot water, the leaves and flowers of O. vulgare release their aromatic oils, providing a calming and digestive-friendly beverage. Traditionally, wild marjoram infusion has been used to aid in digestion, relieve stress, and promote a restful sleep.
