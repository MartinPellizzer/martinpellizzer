thymus vulgaris infusion

intro

Thymus vulgaris, commonly known as thyme, has been used for centuries in herbal medicine to promote digestive health and relieve respiratory issues. An infusion made from the leaves of the thyme plant can be consumed to aid in digestion, reduce inflammation, and combat infections. Drinking thyme infusion may also help to ease symptoms of coughs and colds, and promote a restful night's sleep due to its calming effects.
