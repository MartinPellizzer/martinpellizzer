melissa officinalis infusion

intro

Melissa officinalis, also known as lemon balm, is a popular herbal infusion known for its calming and soothing properties. The infused tea is often consumed to alleviate stress, anxiety, and insomnia, promoting a restful night's sleep and a sense of well-being. Additionally, lemon balm infusion has been traditionally used to ease digestive issues and boost the immune system due to its high antioxidant content.
