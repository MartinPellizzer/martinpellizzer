ammi visnaga infusion

intro

Ammi visnaga infusion, also known as toothpick plant infusion, is a herbal remedy traditionally used to aid in digestion and reduce inflammation. The infusion is made by steeping the plant's leaves and flowers in hot water, which is then strained and consumed as a tea. Some proponents of natural medicine claim that ammi visnaga infusion can also help alleviate symptoms of menstrual cramps and other women's health issues.
