ammi visnaga infusion

intro

Ammi visnaga infusion is a herbal remedy traditionally used to treat various health issues, including inflammation, digestive problems, and anxiety. The infusion is made by steeping the dried seeds of the ammi visnaga plant in hot water, releasing its bioactive compounds such as furanocoumarins and coumarins. Some studies suggest that ammi visnaga may have diuretic and anti-inflammatory properties, although more research is needed to confirm its efficacy and safety for human consumption.
