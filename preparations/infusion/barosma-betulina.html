What To Know About Medicinal Barosma Betulina Infusion?

Barosma betulina, also known as Buchu, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of South Africa, traditionally used in herbal medicine for its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties. How to make Barosma betulina infusion for medicinal use?
