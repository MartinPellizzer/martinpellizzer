cinchona officinalis infusion

intro

Cinchona officinalis infusion, also known as Peruvian bark tea, is a traditional herbal remedy derived from the bark of the cinchona tree, native to the Andean region. The infusion is rich in quinine, a natural compound with anti-inflammatory and antimalarial properties, which has been used to treat fever and malaria for centuries. Drinking cinchona officinalis infusion can help alleviate symptoms of flu, colds, and other respiratory infections, although it is essential to consult a healthcare professional before using it for medicinal purposes.
