What To Know About Medicinal Apium Graveolens Infusion?

Apium graveolens, also known as celery seed, has been used in herbal remedies for centuries to aid digestion and alleviate symptoms of bloating and gas. When consumed, the apium graveolens infusion is believed to have anti-inflammatory properties, promoting overall well-being and relaxation.

How to make Apium graveolens infusion for medicinal use?

To make apium graveolens infusion for medicinal use, start by combining 1-2 teaspoons of dried or fresh celery seeds with 1 cup of boiling water.

Allow the mixture to steep for 5-10 minutes, then strain the liquid and discard the solids.

This infusion can be used to treat various ailments, including digestive issues and inflammation.
