apium graveolens infusion

intro

Apium graveolens, also known as celery seed, has been used in herbal remedies for centuries to aid digestion and alleviate symptoms of bloating and gas. The infusion made from its seeds has been traditionally employed to treat various health issues, including menstrual cramps, joint pain, and respiratory problems. When consumed, the apium graveolens infusion is believed to have anti-inflammatory properties, promoting overall well-being and relaxation.
