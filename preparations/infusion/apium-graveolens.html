apium graveolens infusion

intro

Apium graveolens, also known as celery seed infusion, is a herbal remedy traditionally used to aid digestion and alleviate symptoms of irritable bowel syndrome. The infusion is made by steeping the seeds of the celery plant in hot water, releasing its bioactive compounds and oils. Consuming apium graveolens infusion may also have anti-inflammatory properties, helping to reduce inflammation and promote overall well-being.
