hypericum perforatum infusion

intro

Hypericum perforatum infusion, also known as St. John's Wort tea, is a herbal remedy traditionally used to alleviate mild to moderate depression and anxiety. Made by infusing the dried flowers and leaves of the Hypericum perforatum plant in hot water, this herbal infusion has been used for centuries in folk medicine. However, it is essential to consult with a healthcare professional before using St. John's Wort tea, as it can interact with certain medications and may have adverse effects in high doses.
