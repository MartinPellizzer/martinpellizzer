valeriana officinalis infusion

intro

Valeriana officinalis infusion, also known as valerian root tea, is a herbal remedy traditionally used to promote relaxation and improve sleep quality. The infusion is made by steeping dried valerian roots in hot water, releasing its soothing and calming properties. Drinking valerian root tea before bedtime can help alleviate symptoms of insomnia and restlessness, promoting a restful and rejuvenating night's sleep.
