alpinia officinarum infusion

intro

Alpinia officinarum, also known as lesser galangal, is a type of rhizome used in traditional medicine and as a flavoring agent in various dishes. The infusion made from its roots is said to have anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties, which can help alleviate digestive issues and boost the immune system. In some cultures, alpinia officinarum infusion is also consumed for its potential to lower blood pressure and aid in relaxation.
