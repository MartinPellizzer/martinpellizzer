alpinia officinarum infusion

intro

Alpinia officinarum, commonly known as galangal, is a type of root used in traditional medicine and cooking, particularly in Asian cultures. The infusion made from galangal root has been used to aid digestion and relieve symptoms of nausea and indigestion. Additionally, alpinia officinarum infusion is also believed to have antimicrobial properties, which may help to reduce inflammation and support immune function.
