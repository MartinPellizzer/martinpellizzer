apium graveolens tincture

intro

Apium graveolens, also known as wild celery or celery seed, is a herbal remedy traditionally used to support digestion and alleviate symptoms of indigestion and bloating. The tincture, made by infusing the seeds in a solvent, can be taken internally to stimulate digestion and relieve discomfort. Apium graveolens tincture is also believed to possess anti-inflammatory properties, making it a potential remedy for various gastrointestinal issues.
