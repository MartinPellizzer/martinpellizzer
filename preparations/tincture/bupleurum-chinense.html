What To Know About Medicinal Bupleurum Chinense Tincture?

Bupleurum chinense tincture is a herbal remedy derived from the roots of the Chinese thunder god vine, also known as Bupleurum. Traditionally used in Chinese medicine, it is believed to have anti-inflammatory and immune-boosting properties, making it a popular remedy for conditions such as fever and digestive issues. However, it's essential to consult with a healthcare professional before using Bupleurum chinense tincture, especially if you have any underlying health conditions or are taking medications.

How to make Bupleurum chinense tincture for medicinal use?

To make bupleurum chinense tincture for medicinal use, first, harvest the roots of the Bupleurum chinense plant at the optimal time, and clean them thoroughly.

Next, use a solvent such as ethanol or glycerin to extract the active compounds from the roots, typically in a ratio of 1:2 or 1:5, depending on the desired strength.
