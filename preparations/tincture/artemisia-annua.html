artemisia annua tincture

intro

Artemisia annua tincture, also known as sweet wormwood tincture, is a herbal remedy derived from the leaves of the Artemisia annua plant. Traditionally used in traditional Chinese medicine, this tincture is often utilized to combat malaria, as it contains the active compound artemisinin, a natural antiparasitic agent. However, it is essential to consult with a healthcare professional before using artemisia annua tincture, as it may interact with other medications and have potential side effects.
