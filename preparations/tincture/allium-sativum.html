allium sativum tincture

intro

Allium sativum tincture is a concentrated liquid extract derived from garlic, which contains bioactive compounds that possess antimicrobial, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant properties. When used as a dietary supplement, the tincture may help to support immune function, reduce blood pressure, and alleviate symptoms of colds and flu. However, individuals should consult with a healthcare professional before using allium sativum tincture, especially if they have pre-existing medical conditions or are taking medications, due to potential interactions and side effects.
