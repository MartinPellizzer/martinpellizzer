arctium lappa tincture

intro

Arctium lappa tincture, also known as burdock root tincture, is a herbal remedy made from the roots of the burdock plant, which is native to Europe and Asia. It is often used to support digestive health, reduce inflammation, and promote detoxification in the body. The tincture is typically taken sublingually or added to water, and its active compounds, including inulin and arctiin, are thought to have various health benefits.
