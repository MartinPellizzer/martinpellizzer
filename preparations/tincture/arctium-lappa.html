How to make Arctium lappa tincture for medicinal use?

To make arctium lappa tincture for medicinal use, first, clean and dry a sufficient amount of arctium lappa root and aerial parts, then combine them with a solvent such as 80% to 95% ethanol or glycerin in a clean glass jar.

Next, store the mixture in a cool, dark place and let it steep for several weeks, shaking the jar occasionally, until the liquid has darkened and the plant material has lost its potency.

After the steeping process, strain the tincture through a cheesecloth or coffee filter and discard the solids, and the resulting liquid can be used as a medicinal tincture.
