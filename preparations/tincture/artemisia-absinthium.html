artemisia absinthium tincture

intro

Artemisia absinthium tincture, also known as wormwood tincture, is a concentrated liquid extract derived from the leaves of the wormwood plant. Traditionally used in herbal medicine, this tincture has been employed to aid digestion and alleviate symptoms of indigestion, nausea, and anxiety. However, it is essential to use wormwood tincture under the guidance of a healthcare professional, as excessive consumption can lead to adverse effects, including liver damage and interactions with other medications.
