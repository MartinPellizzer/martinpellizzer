artemisia absinthium tincture

intro

Artemisia absinthium, also known as wormwood, is a plant-based tincture used in traditional herbal medicine to treat various health issues. The tincture is made by steeping the plant's leaves and stems in a solvent, usually ethanol or glycerin, to extract its bioactive compounds, including thujone and sesquiterpenes. While wormwood tincture has been used to alleviate digestive problems and anxiety, it is essential to use it under the guidance of a healthcare professional due to potential side effects and interactions with medications.
