ammi visnaga tincture

intro

Ammi visnaga tincture is a herbal remedy derived from the seeds of the ammi visnaga plant, traditionally used in traditional medicine for its anti-inflammatory and expectorant properties. The tincture is often used to alleviate respiratory issues such as bronchitis and asthma, as well as to ease digestive discomfort and support immune function. However, it's essential to consult with a healthcare professional before using ammi visnaga tincture, as it may interact with certain medications or exacerbate underlying health conditions.
