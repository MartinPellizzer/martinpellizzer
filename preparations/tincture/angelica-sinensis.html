angelica sinensis tincture

intro

Angelica sinensis tincture, also known as dong quai, is a traditional herbal remedy derived from the roots of the Angelica sinensis plant, commonly used in traditional Chinese medicine. The tincture is believed to have various health benefits, including regulating menstrual cycles, reducing menstrual cramps, and improving cardiovascular health. However, it's essential to consult with a healthcare professional before using angelica sinensis tincture, especially for pregnant or breastfeeding women, as it may interact with certain medications or have adverse effects.
