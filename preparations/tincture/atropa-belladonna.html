atropa belladonna tincture

intro

Atropa belladonna, also known as deadly nightshade, is a highly toxic plant that has been used in traditional medicine for its antispasmodic and sedative properties. The tincture made from this plant is typically used to treat conditions such as insomnia, anxiety, and muscle spasms, although it should be used with extreme caution due to its potential toxicity. When used under the guidance of a qualified healthcare professional, atropa belladonna tincture may provide relief from symptoms, but improper use can lead to serious side effects.
