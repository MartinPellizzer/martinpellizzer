acorus calamus tincture

intro

Acorus calamus tincture, also known as calamus tincture, is a herbal remedy derived from the roots of the sweet flag plant. Traditionally used in Ayurvedic and traditional Chinese medicine, it is believed to possess anti-inflammatory, antiseptic, and digestive properties, making it a popular treatment for various health issues. However, due to its potential neurotoxicity and interactions with certain medications, it is essential to consult with a healthcare professional before using acorus calamus tincture.
