achillea millefolium tincture

intro

Achillea millefolium, also known as yarrow, is a popular herbal remedy that can be made into a tincture for various health benefits. The tincture is often used to support wound healing, reduce inflammation, and alleviate menstrual cramps due to its antispasmodic and anti-inflammatory properties. When taken internally, yarrow tincture can also be used to support digestive health and promote a sense of calm and well-being.
