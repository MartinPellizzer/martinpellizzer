achillea millefolium tincture

intro

Achillea millefolium tincture, also known as yarrow tincture, is a herbal remedy made from the leaves and flowers of the yarrow plant. It is often used to support digestion, reduce inflammation, and promote wound healing due to its antiseptic and anti-inflammatory properties. When taken internally, yarrow tincture may help alleviate symptoms of menstrual cramps, fever, and colds, although it's essential to consult with a healthcare professional before using it medicinally.
