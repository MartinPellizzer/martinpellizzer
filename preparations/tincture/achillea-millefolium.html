How to make Achillea millefolium tincture for medicinal use?

To make achillea millefolium tincture for medicinal use, combine one part of dried achillea millefolium flowers with two parts of a solvent such as 80-proof vodka or glycerin in a clean glass jar.

Allow the mixture to steep in a cool, dark place for several weeks, shaking the jar occasionally to facilitate the extraction of the plant's medicinal compounds.

After the steeping period, strain the liquid and discard the solids, then store the tincture in a dark glass bottle with a dropper lid.
