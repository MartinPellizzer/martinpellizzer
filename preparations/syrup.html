cassia senna

Cassia senna syrup is a natural laxative made from the extracts of the Cassia senna plant, which has been used for centuries in traditional medicine to promote digestive health and relieve constipation. The syrup works by stimulating the muscles in the intestines to contract and move waste through the digestive system, providing relief from symptoms of constipation and promoting regular bowel movements. When used as directed, cassia senna syrup can be an effective and gentle way to support digestive health and maintain a balanced gut.
