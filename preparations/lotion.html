aloe vera

Aloe vera lotion is a popular skincare product known for its soothing and hydrating properties, making it an ideal solution for dry, irritated skin. The lotion is typically derived from the gel of the aloe vera plant, which is rich in vitamins and minerals that help to calm and moisturize the skin. By applying aloe vera lotion regularly, individuals can enjoy softer, smoother skin and reduced redness and inflammation.

aesculus hippocastanum

Aesculus hippocastanum lotion is typically derived from the horse chestnut plant, which is native to the Balkan Peninsula. This plant-based extract has anti-inflammatory and soothing properties, commonly used to alleviate symptoms of varicose veins, bruising, and swelling. When applied topically, Aesculus hippocastanum lotion is often used to improve circulation and provide relief from discomfort associated with these conditions.
