acorus calamus tincture

intro

Acorus calamus tincture, also known as sweet flag or calamus tincture, is a herbal remedy derived from the rhizome of the Acorus calamus plant. This tincture has been traditionally used to aid digestion, reduce inflammation, and alleviate symptoms of anxiety and insomnia. However, it's essential to consult a healthcare professional before using Acorus calamus tincture, as it may interact with certain medications or exacerbate underlying health conditions.
