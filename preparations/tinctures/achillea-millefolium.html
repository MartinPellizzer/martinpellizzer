achillea millefolium tincture

intro

Achillea millefolium tincture, also known as yarrow tincture, is a herbal remedy derived from the flowers, leaves, and stems of the yarrow plant. This tincture has been traditionally used to aid in wound healing, reduce inflammation, and alleviate menstrual cramps due to its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties. When taken internally, yarrow tincture may also help to reduce fever, promote digestion, and support overall health.
