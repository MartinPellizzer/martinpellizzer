aloe vera tincture

intro

Aloe vera tincture is a concentrated liquid extract derived from the gel of the aloe vera plant, often used in herbal medicine and skincare routines. Rich in antioxidants and anti-inflammatory compounds, it's believed to promote skin health, reduce inflammation, and soothe minor wounds and burns. When used topically or taken internally under the guidance of a healthcare professional, aloe vera tincture may offer a natural solution for various health concerns.
