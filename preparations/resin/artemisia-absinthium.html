What To Know About Medicinal Artemisia Absinthium Resin?

Artemisia absinthium resin, also known as absinthe resin, is a thick, sticky substance produced by the wormwood plant (Artemisia absinthium). This resin is rich in compounds such as thujone, which has been historically used to create the distinctive flavor and psychoactive properties of absinthe. However, due to concerns over thujone's potential toxicity, the production and sale of absinthe products are heavily regulated in many countries.
