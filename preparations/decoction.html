achillea millefolium

Achillea millefolium decoction, also known as yarrow tea, is a herbal remedy made by infusing the leaves and flowers of the yarrow plant in hot water. This decoction has been traditionally used to aid in digestion, reduce inflammation, and promote wound healing due to its antimicrobial and antispasmodic properties. When consumed, yarrow tea is said to have a calming effect on the nervous system and may help to alleviate menstrual cramps and other symptoms associated with PMS.

acorus calamus

Acorus calamus decoction, also known as sweet flag, is a traditional herbal remedy used to aid digestion and relieve symptoms of nausea and indigestion. Some studies suggest that Acorus calamus decoction may also have anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties, making it a potential treatment for various health conditions.
