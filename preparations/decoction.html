cassia senna

Cassia senna decoction, also known as senna tea, is a traditional herbal remedy made from the dried pods of the cassia senna plant. The decoction is used to support digestive health by stimulating bowel movements and relieving constipation due to its anthraquinone content. However, it's essential to consult with a healthcare professional before using cassia senna decoction, as excessive consumption can lead to abdominal cramps, diarrhea, and other side effects.
