cassia senna

Cassia senna ghee is a type of clarified butter infused with the natural laxative properties of cassia senna, a plant-based ingredient traditionally used in Ayurvedic medicine. The combination of ghee and cassia senna is believed to promote digestive health, ease constipation, and support the body's natural elimination processes. This unique blend is often used in traditional Indian and Middle Eastern medicine to regulate bowel movements and promote overall well-being.
