artemisia absinthium infused oil

intro

Artemisia absinthium, also known as wormwood, is a herb that has been used for centuries in traditional medicine and aromatherapy. When infused into an oil, such as carrier oils like olive or jojoba, wormwood oil is said to possess anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties, making it useful for soothing skin irritations and promoting wound healing. However, it's essential to use wormwood oil in moderation and dilute it properly, as high concentrations can be toxic and potentially cause harm.
