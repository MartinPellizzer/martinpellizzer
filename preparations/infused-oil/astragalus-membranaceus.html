What To Know About Medicinal Astragalus Membranaceus Infused Oil?

Astragalus membranaceus infused oil is a natural remedy created by infusing the roots of the astragalus plant in a carrier oil, such as coconut or olive oil. This infused oil is said to possess anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which can help soothe skin irritations and promote overall well-being. By using astragalus membranaceus infused oil in skincare routines or as a topical treatment, individuals may experience improved skin health and reduced signs of aging.

How to make Astragalus membranaceus infused oil for medicinal use?

To make astragalus membranaceus infused oil for medicinal use, start by harvesting or obtaining the root of the astragalus membranaceus plant, then clean and chop it into small pieces.

Combine the chopped astragalus root with a carrier oil, such as jojoba or sweet almond oil, in a clean glass jar and store it in a cool, dark place for 2-6 weeks, shaking the jar occasionally to facilitate the infusion process.

After the infusion period, strain the oil through a cheesecloth or a coffee filter into a clean container, discard the solids, and store the infused oil in a cool, dark place for later use.
