What To Know About Medicinal Arctium Lappa Infused Oil?

Arctium lappa, also known as burdock, is a popular herb used to create a soothing and nourishing infused oil. When infused in a carrier oil, the properties of burdock root are released, creating a product that is often used to calm skin irritations and promote overall skin health. The resulting oil can be applied topically to help soothe eczema, acne, and other skin conditions, while also promoting a sense of well-being and relaxation.

How to make Arctium lappa infused oil for medicinal use?

To make arctium lappa infused oil for medicinal use, start by harvesting and cleaning the roots of the plant, then chop them into small pieces to increase the surface area for infusion.

Next, combine the chopped roots with a carrier oil such as coconut or olive oil in a clean glass jar, making sure the roots are completely covered by the oil.

Allow the mixture to steep in a cool, dark place for 2-6 weeks, shaking the jar periodically, before straining and decanting the infused oil.

It is essential to consult with a healthcare professional before using arctium lappa infused oil, especially if you have any underlying medical conditions.
