arctium lappa infused oil

intro

Arctium lappa, also known as burdock, is a popular herb used to create a soothing and nourishing infused oil. When infused in a carrier oil, the properties of burdock root are released, creating a product that is often used to calm skin irritations and promote overall skin health. The resulting oil can be applied topically to help soothe eczema, acne, and other skin conditions, while also promoting a sense of well-being and relaxation.
