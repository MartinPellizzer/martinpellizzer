arctium lappa infused oil

intro

Arctium lappa, also known as burdock root, is a popular herbal remedy that has been infused into oil to create a nutrient-rich skincare product. The oil, often made by infusing burdock root in a carrier oil such as coconut or olive oil, is said to possess anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, making it beneficial for soothing skin irritations and promoting healthy skin. When used topically, arctium lappa infused oil may help to calm and moisturize the skin, reducing the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles while supporting overall skin health.
