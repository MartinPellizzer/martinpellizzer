ammi visnaga infused oil

intro

Ammi visnaga infused oil is a natural remedy often used in aromatherapy and skincare. This oil, typically made by infusing ammi visnaga seeds in a carrier oil, is prized for its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties, which can help soothe skin irritations and promote wound healing. When used topically, ammi visnaga infused oil can also help to reduce pain and inflammation, making it a popular choice for massage therapy and natural pain management.
