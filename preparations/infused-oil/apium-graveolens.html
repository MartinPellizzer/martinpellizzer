apium graveolens infused oil

intro

Apium graveolens, also known as wild celery or garden celery, is a popular ingredient in herbal infusions and natural remedies. The oil infused from its seeds is valued for its potential therapeutic benefits, including anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties. When used topically, Apium graveolens infused oil may help soothe skin irritations and promote overall skin health.
