apium graveolens infused oil

intro

Apium graveolens, also known as celery seed oil, is a fragrant and versatile oil obtained from the seeds of the wild celery plant. Rich in antioxidants and possessing anti-inflammatory properties, apium graveolens infused oil is often used in aromatherapy to promote relaxation and reduce stress. When used topically, this oil is believed to have analgesic and anti-arthritic effects, making it a popular choice for natural pain relief remedies.
