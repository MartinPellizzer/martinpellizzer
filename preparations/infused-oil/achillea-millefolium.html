achillea millefolium infused oil

intro

Achillea millefolium, also known as yarrow, is a natural herb that has been used for centuries to create a medicinal infused oil. The oil, typically made by steeping dried yarrow flowers in a carrier oil, is said to possess anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties, making it a popular choice for wound care and skin issues. When applied topically, yarrow infused oil can help to soothe and calm irritated skin, reducing redness and promoting a faster healing process.
