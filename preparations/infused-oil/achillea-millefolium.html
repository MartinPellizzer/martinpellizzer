achillea millefolium infused oil

intro

Achillea millefolium, also known as yarrow, is a popular herb used to create a soothing and anti-inflammatory infused oil. This oil is made by infusing the leaves and flowers of the yarrow plant in a carrier oil, such as jojoba or sweet almond oil, and can be used topically to calm irritated skin and reduce redness. The antimicrobial properties of yarrow infused oil also make it a great addition to skincare routines, helping to prevent acne and promote a healthy, balanced complexion.
