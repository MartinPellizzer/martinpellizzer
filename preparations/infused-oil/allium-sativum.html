allium sativum infused oil

intro

Allium sativum, also known as garlic, infused oil is a popular natural remedy used for its antibacterial and anti-inflammatory properties. This oil is typically made by infusing garlic cloves in a carrier oil, such as olive or coconut oil, and then straining out the solids to create a potent and versatile product. When applied topically or used in cooking, allium sativum infused oil is believed to have various health benefits, including reducing inflammation and improving cardiovascular health.
