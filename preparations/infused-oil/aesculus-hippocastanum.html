aesculus hippocastanum infused oil

intro

Aesculus hippocastanum, also known as horse chestnut, is a popular ingredient for its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties. When infused into oil, it creates a rich and nourishing product that can be used to soothe and calm the skin, reducing the appearance of swelling and redness. This oil can be particularly beneficial for individuals with conditions such as varicose veins or eczema, promoting overall skin health and well-being.
