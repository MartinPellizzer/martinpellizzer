azadirachta indica infused oil

intro

Azadirachta indica, also known as neem, is a natural ingredient that is often infused into oil to create a versatile and potent remedy. When infused into oil, the neem's active compounds are preserved, allowing for a range of potential benefits, including antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties. Neem oil can be used topically to soothe skin irritations, reduce acne, and even help to repel insects, making it a valuable addition to any natural skincare or wellness routine.
