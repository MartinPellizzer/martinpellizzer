arctium lappa poultice

intro

Arctium lappa, also known as burdock, has been used in traditional medicine for centuries to create a poultice that can help soothe and treat skin irritations and wounds. The roots of the plant are typically dried and crushed to create a paste that can be applied directly to the affected area. When used as a poultice, Arctium lappa is believed to promote healing, reduce inflammation, and ease pain, making it a popular natural remedy for various skin conditions.
