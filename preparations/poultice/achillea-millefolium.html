achillea millefolium poultice

intro

Achillea millefolium, also known as yarrow, has been used for centuries to create a soothing poultice to aid in the healing of wounds and injuries. When made into a poultice, the leaves and flowers of the yarrow plant are typically mixed with a carrier oil or water and applied directly to the affected area to reduce inflammation and promote blood clotting. The anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties of achillea millefolium make it a popular natural remedy for wound care and skin conditions.
