angelica sinensis tea

intro

Angelica sinensis tea, also known as Dong Quai, is a traditional Chinese herbal remedy made from the roots of the Angelica sinensis plant. This tea has been used for centuries to promote women's health, reduce menstrual cramps and other symptoms associated with PMS, and is also believed to have antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties. Drinking Angelica sinensis tea is thought to help balance the body's energy and promote relaxation, making it a popular choice for those seeking a natural remedy for various health concerns.
