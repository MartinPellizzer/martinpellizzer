What To Know About Medicinal Cimicifuga Racemosa Tea?

Cimicifuga racemosa, also known as black cohosh, is a herbal tea that has been traditionally used to alleviate symptoms of menopause, such as hot flashes and night sweats. The tea is made from the roots of the black cohosh plant and is believed to have estrogen-modulating properties, which may help to reduce menopausal symptoms. However, it is essential to consult with a healthcare professional before using black cohosh tea, especially for women with a history of estrogen-sensitive cancers or other underlying health conditions.
