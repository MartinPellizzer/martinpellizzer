achillea millefolium tea

intro

Achillea millefolium, also known as yarrow tea, is a herbal infusion made from the leaves and flowers of the yarrow plant, a perennial flowering herb native to Europe and Asia. The tea has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, including reducing inflammation, relieving menstrual cramps, and aiding in digestion. Drinking yarrow tea may also have a calming effect and help to promote relaxation, making it a popular choice for those seeking a natural remedy for stress relief.
