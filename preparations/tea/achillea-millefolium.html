What To Know About Medicinal Achillea Millefolium Tea?

Achillea millefolium, also known as yarrow tea, is a herbal infusion made from the leaves and flowers of the yarrow plant. Drinking yarrow tea may also promote relaxation, improve sleep quality, and act as a natural antacid, making it a popular choice for those seeking a natural remedy.

How to make Achillea millefolium tea for medicinal use?

To make achillea millefolium tea for medicinal use, harvest the leaves and flowers of the plant, and dry them completely to preserve their potency.

Steep 1-2 teaspoons of dried achillea millefolium in a cup of boiling water for 5-7 minutes, then strain the mixture and discard the solids.

Allow the tea to cool before consuming it, as it is traditionally used to aid in digestion and relieve menstrual cramps.
