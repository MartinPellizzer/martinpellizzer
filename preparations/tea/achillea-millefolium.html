achillea millefolium tea

intro

Achillea millefolium, also known as yarrow tea, is a herbal infusion made from the leaves and flowers of the yarrow plant. This tea has been used for centuries in traditional medicine, particularly for its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties, which can aid in digestion, reduce fever, and support the immune system. Drinking yarrow tea may also promote relaxation, improve sleep quality, and act as a natural antacid, making it a popular choice for those seeking a natural remedy.
