allium sativum tea

intro

Allium sativum tea, also known as garlic tea, is a herbal remedy made by steeping crushed garlic cloves in hot water. The tea is believed to have various health benefits, including reducing inflammation, improving cardiovascular health, and acting as an antibacterial agent. However, its strong flavor and potential side effects, such as digestive issues and interactions with certain medications, should be considered before consumption.
