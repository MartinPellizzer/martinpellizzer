How to make Azadirachta indica pomade for medicinal use?

To make azadirachta indica pomade for medicinal use, grind 100g of neem seeds (Azadirachta indica) into a fine powder, then mix it with 100g of beeswax, 100g of coconut oil, and 10g of vitamin E oil in a double boiler, stirring constantly until the mixture is well combined and has a smooth, consistent texture.

Next, remove the mixture from the heat and let it cool until it reaches a semi-solid state, then whip the mixture with a hand mixer or whisk until it becomes light and fluffy.

Once the pomade has reached the desired consistency, transfer it to an airtight container and store it in a cool, dark place for later use.
