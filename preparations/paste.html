capsicum annuum

Capsicum annuum paste, also known as harissa or hot paste, is a spicy condiment made from ground or crushed peppers, often including other ingredients like garlic, oil, and spices. This paste adds a bold, fiery flavor to a variety of dishes, from traditional North African and Middle Eastern cuisine to modern fusion recipes. Capsicum annuum paste is also valued for its potential health benefits, including reducing inflammation and boosting the immune system due to its high content of antioxidants and capsaicin.
