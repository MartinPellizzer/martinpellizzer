What To Know About Medicinal Centella Asiatica Balm?

Centella asiatica balm, also known as tiger balm, is a topical cream or ointment used to relieve pain, reduce inflammation, and promote skin healing. The balm is typically made from a combination of plant extracts, including centella asiatica, also known as gotu kola, which has been used in traditional medicine for centuries to improve skin elasticity and reduce scarring. By applying the balm to the affected area, users can experience temporary relief from minor aches and pains, making it a popular natural remedy for sore muscles and joints.

How to make Centella asiatica balm for medicinal use?

To make centella asiatica balm for medicinal use, first infuse 250ml of carrier oil like coconut or olive oil with a handful of centella asiatica leaves in a clean glass jar, letting it sit in a cool dark place for 2-3 weeks, then strain the oil and mix it with beeswax and other natural ingredients like vitamin E oil and essential oils.
