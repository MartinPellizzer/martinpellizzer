achillea millefolium balm

intro

Achillea millefolium balm, also known as yarrow balm, is a topical salve made from the flowers of the yarrow plant, a natural remedy used for centuries to soothe and calm skin irritations. This balm is often used to alleviate symptoms of minor wounds, burns, and insect bites, while also promoting wound healing and reducing inflammation. The antibacterial and antifungal properties of yarrow make it an effective natural remedy for preventing infection and promoting a healthy recovery.
