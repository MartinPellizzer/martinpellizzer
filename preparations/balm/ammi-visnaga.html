What To Know About Medicinal Ammi Visnaga Balm?

Ammi visnaga balm, also known as toothpick plant balm, is a topical remedy traditionally used to alleviate pain and reduce inflammation. The balm is typically made from the roots of the ammi visnaga plant, which contain sesquiterpenes and other compounds with analgesic and anti-inflammatory properties. It has been used to treat a variety of conditions, including sprains, strains, and arthritis, although its effectiveness may vary depending on individual circumstances.

How to make Ammi visnaga balm for medicinal use?

To make ammi visnaga balm for medicinal use, combine equal parts of ammi visnaga essential oil and a carrier oil such as coconut or jojoba oil in a small bowl.

Gently heat the mixture in a double boiler or a heat-proof bowl set over a pot of simmering water to ensure the essential oil is fully incorporated.
