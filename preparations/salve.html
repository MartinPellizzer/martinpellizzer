centella asiatica

Centella asiatica salve is a topical treatment derived from the leaves of the Gotu Kola plant, known for its anti-inflammatory and regenerative properties. This natural salve is often used to soothe skin irritations, promote wound healing, and reduce the appearance of scars and stretch marks. When applied topically, centella asiatica salve is said to improve skin elasticity and firmness, leaving the skin feeling smooth and revitalized.
