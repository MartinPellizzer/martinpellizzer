achillea millefolium

Achillea millefolium salve is a topical ointment made from the leaves and flowers of the yarrow plant, which have been used for centuries in traditional herbal medicine. This salve is often used to soothe and calm skin irritations, wounds, and minor burns, promoting healing and reducing inflammation. The anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties of yarrow make it a popular natural remedy for skin conditions and injuries.
