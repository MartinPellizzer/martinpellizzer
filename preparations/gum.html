apium graveolens

Apium graveolens gum, also known as celery seed gum, is a natural adhesive derived from the seeds of the Apium graveolens plant. It is commonly used as a thickening agent and stabilizer in food products, cosmetics, and pharmaceuticals due to its unique gelling properties. Apium graveolens gum has been gaining popularity in recent years as a vegan and halal alternative to traditional gums like guar gum and xanthan gum.
