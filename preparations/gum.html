cassia senna

Cassia senna gum, also known as senna gum, is a natural adhesive derived from the leaves of the cassia senna plant, which is native to Africa and Asia. It has been used for centuries in various applications, including as a binder in traditional medicines, a sizing agent in textiles, and a thickening agent in food products. Cassia senna gum is valued for its unique properties, which include its ability to form a strong, flexible film and its resistance to heat and moisture.
