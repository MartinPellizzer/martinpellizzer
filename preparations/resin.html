barosma betulina

Barosma betulina resin, also known as "Round Leaf Peppermint Oil," is a traditional medicinal and culinary herb native to the Eastern Cape province of South Africa. The resin is rich in limonene and beta-pinene compounds, which contribute to its distinctive aroma and medicinal properties. Traditionally used to treat various ailments, including digestive issues and fever, Barosma betulina resin has been a valued resource in African folk medicine for centuries.
