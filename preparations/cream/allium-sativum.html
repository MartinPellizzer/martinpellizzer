allium sativum cream

intro

Allium sativum cream, also known as garlic cream, is a topical treatment that utilizes the antibacterial and antifungal properties of garlic to promote wound healing and prevent infection. This cream is often applied to minor cuts and scrapes to reduce inflammation and promote tissue repair. However, it's essential to note that allium sativum cream should not be used on open wounds, deep cuts, or sensitive areas without consulting a medical professional first.
