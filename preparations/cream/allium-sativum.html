allium sativum cream

intro

Allium sativum cream, also known as garlic cream, is a topical treatment derived from the extract of garlic (Allium sativum) and often used to alleviate various skin conditions such as acne, eczema, and psoriasis. The cream's antibacterial and anti-inflammatory properties help to reduce inflammation and fight off infections, promoting healthy skin and wound healing. However, it's essential to use allium sativum cream under medical supervision, as its strong potency can cause adverse reactions in some individuals.
