What To Know About Medicinal Bupleurum Chinense Cream?

Bupleurum chinense cream is a topical treatment derived from the Chinese thunder of god vine (Bupleurum chinense), which has been traditionally used in Asian medicine to reduce inflammation and promote wound healing. This cream is often used to treat various skin conditions, including eczema, acne, and dermatitis, due to its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties. The cream may also help soothe and calm irritated skin, reducing redness and promoting a more even skin tone.

How to make Bupleurum chinense cream for medicinal use?

To make bupleurum chinense cream for medicinal use, first harvest the roots of the Bupleurum chinense plant and dry them to remove excess moisture.

Next, combine the dried roots with a carrier oil such as coconut or olive oil, and then infuse the mixture in a double boiler to extract the medicinal properties.

Finally, mix the infused oil with a natural emollient like shea butter or beeswax to create a smooth, stable cream for topical application.
