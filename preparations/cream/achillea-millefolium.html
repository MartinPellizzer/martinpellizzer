achillea millefolium cream

intro

Achillea millefolium cream, also known as yarrow cream, is a topical treatment made from the extracts of the yarrow plant. It is often used to soothe and calm skin irritations, such as burns, wounds, and insect bites, due to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. When applied topically, the cream can help promote wound healing, reduce redness and swelling, and provide a protective barrier against further irritation.
