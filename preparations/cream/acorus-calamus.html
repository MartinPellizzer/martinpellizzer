acorus calamus cream

intro

Acorus calamus cream, also known as calamus cream, is a topical preparation derived from the roots of the Acorus calamus plant. Traditionally used in Ayurvedic medicine, the cream is believed to possess anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties, making it a potential treatment for skin irritations and wounds. However, its effectiveness and safety for human use have not been extensively scientifically proven, and further research is needed to confirm its benefits and potential side effects.
