angelica sinensis cream

intro

Angelica sinensis cream, also known as dong quai cream, is a topical application derived from the root of the Angelica sinensis plant, commonly used in traditional Chinese medicine. The cream is often used to promote skin health, reduce inflammation, and improve circulation, making it a potential remedy for various skin conditions such as eczema and acne. However, its effectiveness and safety as a topical treatment have not been extensively scientifically proven, and more research is needed to confirm its benefits and potential side effects.
