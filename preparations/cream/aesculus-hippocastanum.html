aesculus hippocastanum cream

intro

Aesculus hippocastanum, also known as horse chestnut extract, is a popular ingredient in topical creams and ointments. The cream is often used to treat conditions such as varicose veins, hemorrhoids, and spider veins due to its anti-inflammatory and venoactive properties. Aesculus hippocastanum cream is typically applied topically to reduce swelling and improve circulation, promoting overall skin health and well-being.
