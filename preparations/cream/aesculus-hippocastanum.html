aesculus hippocastanum cream

intro

Aesculus hippocastanum cream, also known as horse chestnut cream, is a topical treatment often used to alleviate symptoms of chronic venous insufficiency, such as swelling and inflammation in the legs. The cream typically contains extracts from the seeds of the Aesculus hippocastanum tree, which contain a compound called aescin that helps to improve blood flow and reduce fluid accumulation. Regular application of horse chestnut cream may provide relief from discomfort and promote faster healing of affected areas.
