How to make Apium graveolens cream for medicinal use?

To make apium graveolens cream for medicinal use, first, you need to infuse the seeds of Apium graveolens in a carrier oil such as coconut or olive oil, allowing the mixture to steep for several days.

Once the infusion is complete, the oil is strained and mixed with a thickening agent like beeswax or shea butter to create a stable and spreadable cream.
