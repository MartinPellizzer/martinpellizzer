ammi visnaga cream

intro

Ammi visnaga cream, also known as coumarin-rich cream, is a topical remedy derived from the ammi visnaga plant, traditionally used to treat various skin conditions and promote wound healing. Its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties make it effective in soothing and calming irritated skin, reducing redness and swelling. While generally considered safe, it's essential to use ammi visnaga cream under the guidance of a healthcare professional or after consulting with a dermatologist.
