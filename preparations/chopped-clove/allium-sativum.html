allium sativum chopped clove

intro

Allium sativum, commonly known as garlic, is a versatile and aromatic bulbous plant that has been used for centuries in various cuisines worldwide. When the bulb is chopped, it releases a potent compound called allicin, which is responsible for its characteristic flavor and potential health benefits. Chopped garlic clove is a staple ingredient in many recipes, adding depth and richness to a wide range of dishes, from savory meats to flavorful sauces and marinades.
