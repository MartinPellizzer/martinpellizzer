ammi visnaga elixir

intro

Ammi visnaga elixir, also known as Russian valerian or khella, is a herbal remedy derived from the plant Ammi visnaga. Traditionally used in Middle Eastern and North African medicine, the elixir is believed to have various health benefits, including relief from anxiety, insomnia, and respiratory issues. Its active compounds, including sesquiterpenes and alkaloids, are thought to contribute to its therapeutic effects, although more research is needed to fully understand its properties and potential uses.
