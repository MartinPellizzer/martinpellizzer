What To Know About Medicinal Aesculus Hippocastanum Elixir?

Aesculus hippocastanum elixir is a medicinal preparation derived from the horse chestnut tree, known for its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties. This elixir is often used to treat conditions such as varicose veins, hemorrhoids, and inflammation of the skin, as it helps to improve blood flow and reduce swelling. However, it's essential to consult a medical professional before using aesculus hippocastanum elixir, as it may interact with certain medications or exacerbate underlying health conditions.

Aesculus hippocastanum elixir is used to treat conditions such as hemorrhoids, varicose veins, and chronic venous insufficiency due to its anti-inflammatory and venoactive properties.

How to make Aesculus hippocastanum elixir for medicinal use?
