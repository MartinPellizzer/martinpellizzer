artemisia absinthium elixir

intro

Artemisia absinthium elixir, also known as wormwood elixir, is a herbal remedy made from the dried leaves and flowers of the wormwood plant. Traditionally used in folk medicine, this elixir has been employed to alleviate symptoms of anxiety, insomnia, and digestive issues, although its effectiveness and safety have not been extensively scientifically proven. In some cultures, wormwood elixir is also consumed as a tonic to stimulate the immune system and promote overall well-being.
