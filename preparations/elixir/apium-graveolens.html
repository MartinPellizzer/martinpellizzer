apium graveolens elixir

intro

Apium graveolens elixir, also known as celery seed elixir, is a herbal remedy made from the seeds of the celery plant (Apium graveolens). This natural elixir is often used to support digestive health and alleviate symptoms of bloating and indigestion, thanks to its carminative and anti-inflammatory properties. Traditionally, apium graveolens elixir has been used in Ayurvedic and homeopathic medicine to promote overall well-being and balance in the body.
