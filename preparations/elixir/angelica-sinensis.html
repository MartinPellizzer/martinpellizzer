angelica sinensis elixir

intro

Angelica sinensis elixir, also known as dong quai, is a traditional Chinese herbal remedy derived from the roots of the Angelica sinensis plant. This elixir has been used for centuries to promote women's health, alleviate menstrual cramps, and balance the body's energy, or qi. When consumed as a supplement, angelica sinensis elixir may help to reduce inflammation, improve blood circulation, and support overall well-being.
