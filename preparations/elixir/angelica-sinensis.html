angelica sinensis elixir

intro

Angelica sinensis elixir, also known as dong quai, is a traditional Chinese medicine made from the roots of the angelica sinensis plant. It has been used for centuries to promote women's health, alleviate menstrual cramps and symptoms associated with menopause, and is often taken as a supplement to support overall well-being. The elixir is often combined with other herbs and ingredients to enhance its medicinal properties and is available in various forms, including capsules, tablets, and teas.
