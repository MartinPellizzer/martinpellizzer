achillea millefolium essential oil

intro

Achillea millefolium, also known as yarrow essential oil, is derived from the flowers and leaves of the yarrow plant and is highly valued for its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties. This oil has been traditionally used to soothe wounds, reduce fever, and alleviate digestive issues, making it a popular choice in natural remedies. When used topically, yarrow essential oil can help to reduce inflammation, promote wound healing, and provide relief from minor skin irritations.
