achillea millefolium essential oil

intro

Achillea millefolium essential oil, also known as yarrow oil, is extracted from the flowers of the yarrow plant and is prized for its antiseptic, anti-inflammatory, and antispasmodic properties. It has been traditionally used to soothe wounds, reduce fever, and ease digestive issues, making it a popular natural remedy in aromatherapy and herbal medicine. When used responsibly and in moderation, yarrow oil can be a valuable addition to a self-care routine, promoting relaxation, reducing stress, and supporting overall well-being.
