artemisia absinthium essential oil

intro

Artemisia absinthium, also known as absinthe essential oil, is derived from the leaves of the wormwood plant and has a bitter, earthy aroma. Traditionally used in herbal remedies, this oil is believed to have antiseptic and anti-inflammatory properties, making it useful for wound care and skin issues. However, it's essential to use caution when handling wormwood essential oil, as it can be toxic in large quantities and may cause adverse reactions in some individuals.
