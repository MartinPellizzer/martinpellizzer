What To Know About Medicinal Barosma Betulina Essential Oil?

Barosma betulina essential oil, also known as Buchu oil, is derived from the leaves of the Barosma betulina plant native to South Africa. Traditionally used as a natural remedy, it is valued for its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties, which can help soothe skin irritations and support urinary tract health. When used in aromatherapy, Buchu oil is believed to promote a sense of clarity and calmness due to its refreshing and uplifting scent.

How to make Barosma betulina essential oil for medicinal use?
