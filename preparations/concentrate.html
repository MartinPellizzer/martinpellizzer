ammi visnaga

Ammi visnaga, also known as sweet ferula, is a plant-based ingredient commonly used in herbal remedies and traditional medicine. The concentrate of ammi visnaga is often used to support respiratory health and relieve symptoms associated with bronchitis and other respiratory conditions. It is also valued for its anti-inflammatory properties and is sometimes used to alleviate menstrual cramps and other symptoms associated with PMS.
