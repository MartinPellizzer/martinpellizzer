angelica sinensis wine infusion

intro

Angelica sinensis wine infusion, also known as Chuanxiong wine, is a traditional Chinese medicine made by infusing Angelica sinensis roots in a wine base. This infusion is believed to promote blood circulation, reduce inflammation, and alleviate symptoms of cardiovascular and cerebrovascular diseases. The wine is typically consumed in small amounts, and its medicinal properties are attributed to the bioactive compounds present in the Angelica sinensis roots, which are rich in essential oils and polysaccharides.
