cassia senna

Cassia senna suppositories are a type of laxative used to treat constipation and promote bowel movements. They work by stimulating the muscles in the intestines to move stool out of the body, providing relief from infrequent or difficult bowel movements. Available in various forms, including suppositories, capsules, and loose powder, cassia senna is derived from the leaves of the senna plant, a natural source of anthraquinones, which have laxative properties.
