centella asiatica

A traditional centella asiatica poultice, also known as Gotu Kola, is often used to treat various skin conditions and injuries. The poultice is typically made by steeping the leaves of the centella asiatica plant in hot water, then mixing the resulting liquid with a binding agent to form a paste. When applied topically, the poultice is believed to promote wound healing, reduce inflammation, and improve skin elasticity.
