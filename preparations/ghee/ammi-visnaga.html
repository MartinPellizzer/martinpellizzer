How to make Ammi visnaga ghee for medicinal use?

To make ammi visnaga ghee for medicinal use, first infuse dried ammi visnaga seeds in a neutral oil like sunflower or safflower oil for several weeks to create a medicinal extract.

Then, mix the infused oil with pure ghee in a certain ratio, typically 10-20% of the infused oil to 80-90% ghee, and heat the mixture gently to combine the two.

Finally, strain the ammi visnaga ghee through a cheesecloth or a fine-mesh sieve into a clean container and store it in the refrigerator for later use.
