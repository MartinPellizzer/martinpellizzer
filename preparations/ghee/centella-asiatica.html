What To Know About Medicinal Centella Asiatica Ghee?

Centella asiatica ghee is a type of medicinal ghee infused with the extract of the centella asiatica plant, also known as gotu kola. This herbal ghee is believed to possess anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, making it a popular ingredient in traditional Ayurvedic medicine. When consumed, centella asiatica ghee is thought to promote skin and hair health, as well as support overall well-being.

What are the medicinal uses of Centella asiatica ghee?

Centella asiatica ghee is used to promote skin health and wound healing, thanks to its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.

It is also believed to support cognitive function and improve memory.

Additionally, centella asiatica ghee may help to reduce stress and anxiety by regulating the nervous system.

Centella asiatica ghee has therapeutic properties such as reducing inflammation, promoting skin health, and supporting wound healing due to its rich composition of active compounds derived from the plant.

How to make Centella asiatica ghee for medicinal use?

To make centella asiatica ghee for medicinal use, start by infusing dried centella asiatica leaves in a neutral oil, such as coconut or sunflower oil, for several hours or overnight.

Then, slowly heat the oil in a double boiler or a heat-proof bowl set over a pot of simmering water, allowing the oil to gently warm and the infusing properties of the centella asiatica to be released.

Once the oil has been warmed and the centella asiatica's properties have been transferred, carefully skim off any impurities and store the infused oil in an airtight container.
