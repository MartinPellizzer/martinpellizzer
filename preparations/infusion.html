cassia senna

Cassia senna infusion, also known as senna tea, is a natural laxative made from the dried leaves of the cassia senna tree. When brewed, the infusion stimulates bowel movements and helps to relieve constipation by increasing the movement of the digestive system. However, due to its laxative properties, it's recommended to consume cassia senna infusion in moderation and under the guidance of a healthcare professional to avoid potential side effects.
