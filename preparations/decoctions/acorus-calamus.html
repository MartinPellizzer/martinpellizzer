acorus calamus decoction

intro

Acorus calamus decoction, also known as Indian sarsaparilla, is a traditional herbal remedy made by steeping the rhizome of the Acorus calamus plant in water. This decoction has been used in Ayurvedic medicine to treat a variety of health issues, including digestive problems, anxiety, and insomnia. Research suggests that acorus calamus contains compounds with potential anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which may contribute to its therapeutic benefits.
