allium sativum chopped clove

intro

Allium sativum, commonly known as garlic, has been used for centuries in various cuisines for its unique flavor and medicinal properties. When used in the form of a chopped clove, it adds a pungent taste and aroma to a wide range of dishes, from savory meals to sauces and marinades. Chopped garlic clove is a versatile ingredient that can be used fresh or cooked to bring out its full flavor and health benefits.
