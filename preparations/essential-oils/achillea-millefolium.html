achillea millefolium essential oil

intro

Achillea millefolium essential oil, also known as yarrow oil, is derived from the flowers of the yarrow plant and is commonly used in aromatherapy for its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties. Its calming and grounding effects can help to reduce stress and anxiety, while also promoting a good night's sleep. Additionally, yarrow oil is known for its ability to aid in wound healing and skin regeneration, making it a popular natural remedy for minor cuts and scrapes.
