acorus calamus oil

intro

Acorus calamus oil, also known as calamus oil, is derived from the rhizomes of the sweet flag plant and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties. This oil is often used in aromatherapy to promote relaxation, reduce anxiety, and improve digestion. However, it's essential to note that using calamus oil can be hazardous due to the presence of asarone, a compound that can be toxic in large quantities.
