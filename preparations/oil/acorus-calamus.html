acorus calamus oil

intro

Acorus calamus oil, also known as sweet flag oil, is a calming and anti-inflammatory essential oil obtained from the rhizomes of the Acorus calamus plant. Traditionally used in Ayurvedic and traditional Chinese medicine, this oil is said to possess a range of health benefits, including reducing anxiety and stress, as well as alleviating digestive issues. When used in aromatherapy or applied topically, acorus calamus oil is believed to promote a sense of relaxation and well-being, while also possessing antimicrobial properties.
