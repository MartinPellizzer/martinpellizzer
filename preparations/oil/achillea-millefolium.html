achillea millefolium oil

intro

Achillea millefolium oil, also known as yarrow oil, is a fragrant and versatile essential oil extracted from the flowers and leaves of the yarrow plant. Rich in antioxidants and anti-inflammatory compounds, yarrow oil has been traditionally used to soothe skin irritations, reduce pain, and promote wound healing. Its calming and antiseptic properties also make it a popular ingredient in aromatherapy and natural remedies for stress and skin care.
