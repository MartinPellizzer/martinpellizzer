arctium lappa oil

intro

Arctium lappa oil, also known as burdock oil, is an extract derived from the roots of the burdock plant, a flowering plant native to Europe and Asia. The oil is valued for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, as well as its ability to soothe and calm irritated skin. When used topically, burdock oil may help to alleviate symptoms of eczema, acne, and other skin conditions, promoting overall skin health and well-being.
