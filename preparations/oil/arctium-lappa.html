arctium lappa oil

intro

Arctium lappa oil, also known as burdock seed oil, is a plant-derived oil rich in essential fatty acids and antioxidants. It is often used in skincare and haircare products due to its potential to soothe and moisturize the skin, while also promoting hair growth and strengthening hair follicles. Additionally, arctium lappa oil is believed to have anti-inflammatory properties, making it a popular choice for treating acne, eczema, and other skin irritations.
