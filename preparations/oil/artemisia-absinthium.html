artemisia absinthium oil

intro

Artemisia absinthium oil, also known as wormwood oil, is a pungent and bitter essential oil extracted from the leaves of the Artemisia absinthium plant. Traditionally used in herbal remedies, wormwood oil is said to possess antiseptic and anti-inflammatory properties, making it useful for treating skin irritations and wounds. However, due to its potential toxicity and psychoactive compounds, wormwood oil should be used with caution and under the guidance of a qualified aromatherapist.
