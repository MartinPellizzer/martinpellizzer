allium sativum oil

intro

Allium sativum oil, also known as garlic oil, is a culinary and medicinal extract derived from the bulbs of garlic plants. This oil is rich in bioactive compounds, including allicin, which has been linked to potential health benefits such as reducing inflammation and improving cardiovascular health. However, due to its strong flavor and potential toxicity, Allium sativum oil should be used in moderation and under the guidance of a healthcare professional.
