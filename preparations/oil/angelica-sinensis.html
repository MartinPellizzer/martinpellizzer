angelica sinensis oil

intro

Angelica sinensis oil, also known as dong quai oil, is a traditional Chinese medicinal oil derived from the roots of the Angelica sinensis plant. This oil is believed to possess various health benefits, including anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which may help to soothe and calm the skin, as well as promote overall well-being. When used topically, angelica sinensis oil may be particularly beneficial for skin conditions such as eczema, acne, and rosacea.
