ammi visnaga oil

intro

Ammi visnaga oil, also known as Ferula oil or Hanguet, is a plant-based essential oil derived from the roots of the Ferula hermonis plant, native to the Middle East and North Africa. This oil has been traditionally used for its medicinal and aromatic properties, including its ability to reduce inflammation and relieve pain. Ammi visnaga oil is also valued for its potential to support respiratory health and ease symptoms associated with colds and flu.
