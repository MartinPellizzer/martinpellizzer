What To Know About Medicinal Apium Graveolens Oil?

Apium graveolens oil, also known as celery seed oil, is a fragrant essential oil derived from the seeds of the celery plant. It is commonly used in aromatherapy to promote relaxation and reduce stress due to its calming and grounding properties. The oil is also said to have anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, making it a popular ingredient in skincare products and natural remedies.
