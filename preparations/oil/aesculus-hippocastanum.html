aesculus hippocastanum oil

intro

Aesculus hippocastanum oil, also known as horse chestnut oil, is derived from the seeds of the Aesculus hippocastanum tree and is commonly used in aromatherapy and traditional medicine. Rich in anti-inflammatory compounds, this oil is often employed to alleviate symptoms of varicose veins, reduce swelling, and soothe skin irritations. However, it's essential to use high-quality and pure Aesculus hippocastanum oil, as some commercial products may be adulterated or contain potential allergens.
