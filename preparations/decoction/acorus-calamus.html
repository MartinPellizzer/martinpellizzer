acorus calamus decoction

intro

Acorus calamus decoction, also known as Indian sarsaparilla, is a herbal remedy made by boiling the rhizome of the Acorus calamus plant. This decoction has been traditionally used to treat various health issues, including digestive problems, inflammation, and anxiety, due to its carminative and antispasmodic properties. However, it is essential to consult a healthcare professional before using Acorus calamus decoction, as it may interact with certain medications or exacerbate underlying health conditions.
