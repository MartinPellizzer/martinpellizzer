angelica sinensis decoction

intro

Angelica sinensis decoction, also known as Dong Quai, is a traditional Chinese herbal remedy derived from the dried roots of the Angelica sinensis plant. This decoction has been used for centuries to promote women's health, alleviate menstrual cramps and symptoms of menopause, as well as to improve circulation and boost the immune system. The decoction is typically brewed as a tea by steeping the dried roots in hot water and is often consumed in moderation as part of a balanced diet.
