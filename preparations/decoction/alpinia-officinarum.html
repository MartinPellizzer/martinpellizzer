alpinia officinarum decoction

intro

Alpinia officinarum decoction, also known as Galangal decoction, is a traditional herbal remedy used in various cultures for its medicinal properties. The decoction is made by boiling the rhizomes of the Alpinia officinarum plant, which is said to possess anti-inflammatory, antiseptic, and antioxidant properties. It is commonly used to aid digestion, relieve nausea and vomiting, and alleviate cold and flu symptoms.
