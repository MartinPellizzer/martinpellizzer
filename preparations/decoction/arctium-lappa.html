arctium lappa decoction

intro

Arctium lappa decoction, also known as burdock root tea, has been traditionally used to support digestive health and relieve symptoms associated with gastrointestinal issues. The decoction is made by steeping the roots of the burdock plant in hot water, releasing its bioactive compounds that may help soothe inflammation and promote a balanced gut microbiome. When consumed as a tea, arctium lappa decoction may also support detoxification and immune function, although its effectiveness and safety have not been extensively scientifically studied.
