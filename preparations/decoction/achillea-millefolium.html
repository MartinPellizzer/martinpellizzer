achillea millefolium decoction

intro

Achillea millefolium, also known as yarrow, is a medicinal herb that has been used for centuries to create a decoction for various health benefits. The decoction is made by steeping dried yarrow flowers and leaves in hot water, which can be used to treat digestive issues, reduce fever, and ease menstrual cramps. When consumed as a decoction, yarrow is believed to promote wound healing, reduce inflammation, and support immune function due to its high content of flavonoids and other bioactive compounds.
