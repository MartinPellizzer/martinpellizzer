What To Know About Medicinal Bupleurum Chinense Liniment?

Bupleurum chinense liniment is a traditional Chinese herbal remedy used to treat various health conditions, including fever, rheumatism, and inflammation. It is typically made by infusing the root of the Bupleurum chinense plant in a solvent, such as alcohol or oil, to create a liniment that can be applied topically. This liniment is believed to have anti-inflammatory properties, which can help to soothe and calm irritated skin and joints.

Bupleurum chinense liniment is used to treat various health conditions, including fever, rheumatism, and digestive issues.

How to make Bupleurum chinense liniment for medicinal use?

To make bupleurum chinense liniment for medicinal use, combine dried Bupleurum chinense roots with a carrier oil such as olive oil in a 1:5 ratio.

Steep the mixture in a warm, dark place for 2-3 weeks, shaking the container daily to facilitate the infusion process.

Once the mixture has cooled, strain and filter it to create the liniment, which can be stored in a dark glass bottle for later use.
