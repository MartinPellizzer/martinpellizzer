artemisia absinthium liniment

intro

Artemisia absinthium liniment, also known as wormwood liniment, is a topical application derived from the leaves and stems of the wormwood plant. Traditionally used to relieve pain and reduce inflammation, this liniment is often applied externally to sore muscles and joints. However, its use should be approached with caution due to potential toxicity and interactions with medications, and it is recommended to consult a healthcare professional before using it.
