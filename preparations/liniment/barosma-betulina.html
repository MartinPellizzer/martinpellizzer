How to make Barosma betulina liniment for medicinal use?

To make barosma betulina liniment for medicinal use, combine 200 grams of dried Barosma betulina leaves and bark with 1 liter of a neutral oil such as olive or coconut oil in a clean glass jar, and let it steep in a cool, dark place for 2-3 weeks, shaking the jar daily.

After the steeping period, strain the mixture through a cheesecloth or a coffee filter into another clean glass container, discarding the solids.

The resulting liniment can be stored in a cool, dark place and used as needed for its medicinal properties.
