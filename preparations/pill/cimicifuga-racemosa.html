What To Know About Medicinal Cimicifuga Racemosa Pill?

Cimicifuga racemosa, also known as black cohosh, is a plant-based dietary supplement often used to alleviate symptoms of menopause, such as hot flashes and mood swings. However, its effectiveness and safety have been debated, with some studies suggesting potential liver toxicity and interactions with medications.
