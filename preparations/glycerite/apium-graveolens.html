apium graveolens glycerite

intro

Apium graveolens, also known as celery seed, is a natural remedy used in herbal medicine to support digestion and alleviate symptoms of indigestion and bloating. The glycerite form of Apium graveolens is a liquid extract that combines the plant's bioactive compounds with glycerin, making it easier to consume and potentially more bioavailable. When taken as directed, Apium graveolens glycerite may help to soothe the digestive system and promote overall gut health.
