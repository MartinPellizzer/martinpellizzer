achillea millefolium glycerite

intro

Achillea millefolium, also known as yarrow, is a herbal remedy that has been used for centuries to promote wound healing, reduce inflammation, and improve digestion. Achillea millefolium glycerite is a liquid extract of the plant, typically made by infusing the flowers and leaves in a glycerin base to create a concentrated and shelf-stable tincture. When taken internally, Achillea millefolium glycerite may help to soothe digestive issues, reduce menstrual cramps, and support overall health and well-being.
