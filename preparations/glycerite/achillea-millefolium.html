achillea millefolium glycerite

intro

Achillea millefolium, commonly known as yarrow, is a herb used in traditional herbalism to promote healing and reduce inflammation. The glycerite form of yarrow extract is a liquid tincture made with glycerin, which helps to preserve the delicate properties of the plant. When used as a dietary supplement, achillea millefolium glycerite may help to support immune function, reduce menstrual cramps, and promote overall well-being.
