What To Know About Medicinal Cassia Senna Glycerite?

Cassia senna glycerite is a natural laxative supplement derived from the Cassia senna plant, which is commonly used to support digestive health and relieve constipation. The glycerite form of cassia senna is often preferred over the traditional leaf or capsule forms due to its more gentle and sustained release properties. When used as directed, cassia senna glycerite can help promote regular bowel movements and alleviate symptoms of irritable bowel syndrome.

How to make Cassia senna glycerite for medicinal use?

To make cassia senna glycerite for medicinal use, first, combine dried cassia senna leaves and pods with a carrier oil, such as olive or grapeseed oil, in a clean glass jar, allowing them to steep in a cool, dark place for several weeks.

Next, strain the oil mixture and combine the infused oil with a glycerin solution, typically made by dissolving glycerin in water, in a 1:1 ratio.

Finally, transfer the resulting glycerite into dark glass bottles and store them in a cool, dark place to preserve the medicinal properties of the cassia senna.

Additionally, long-term use of cassia senna glycerite may lead to electrolyte imbalances and dependence on the laxative.
