acorus calamus glycerite

intro

Acorus calamus glycerite is a liquid extract of the root of the Acorus calamus plant, also known as sweet flag or calamus. This herbal extract is often used in homeopathic and natural medicine to support digestive health and alleviate symptoms of bloating and gas. Glycerites, being a liquid extract, are typically made by infusing the root material in glycerin, making the extract easily absorbed and utilized by the body.
