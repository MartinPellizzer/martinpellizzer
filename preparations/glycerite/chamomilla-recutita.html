What To Know About Medicinal Chamomilla Recutita Glycerite?

Chamomilla recutita glycerite, also known as German chamomile glycerite, is a herbal supplement derived from the flowers of the Matricaria chamomilla plant. When consumed as a dietary supplement, German chamomile glycerite may help alleviate symptoms of insomnia, menstrual cramps, and other inflammatory conditions.
