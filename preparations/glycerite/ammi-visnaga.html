ammi visnaga glycerite

intro

Ammi visnaga glycerite is a homeopathic remedy derived from the seeds of the ammi visnaga plant, commonly known as khella or mouse's ear. Traditionally used to treat respiratory issues such as bronchitis and asthma, it is believed to have anti-inflammatory and expectorant properties. However, its effectiveness and safety have not been extensively scientifically proven, and its use should be approached with caution.
