azadirachta indica extract

intro

Azadirachta indica, commonly known as neem, has been widely studied for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional Indian medicine. The extract from its leaves and seeds has been found to have antimicrobial, antifungal, and antiparasitic properties, making it a potential natural remedy for various health issues. Research has also explored its potential in reducing inflammation and improving skin health, although further studies are needed to confirm its efficacy.
