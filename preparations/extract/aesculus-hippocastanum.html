aesculus hippocastanum extract

intro

Aesculus hippocastanum extract, also known as horse chestnut extract, is derived from the leaves and flowers of the Aesculus hippocastanum tree. It has been traditionally used in herbal medicine for its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which may help to reduce swelling and improve circulation in the veins. The extract's bioflavonoids and saponins are thought to contribute to its beneficial effects, making it a popular ingredient in supplements and topical creams.
