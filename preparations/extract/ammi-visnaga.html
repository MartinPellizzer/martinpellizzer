ammi visnaga extract

intro

Ammi visnaga extract, also known as sea fleece blossom or false bishop's weed, has been used in traditional medicine for its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties. It is commonly used to treat various health issues, including digestive problems, respiratory infections, and skin conditions, due to its ability to reduce inflammation and kill bacteria. Recent studies have also suggested that ammi visnaga extract may have potential benefits in reducing blood pressure and improving cardiovascular health.
