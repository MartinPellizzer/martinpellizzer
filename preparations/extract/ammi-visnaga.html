What To Know About Medicinal Ammi Visnaga Extract?

Ammi visnaga extract, also known as sea fleece blossom or false bishop's weed, has been used in traditional medicine for its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties. Recent studies have also suggested that ammi visnaga extract may have potential benefits in reducing blood pressure and improving cardiovascular health.

How to make Ammi visnaga extract for medicinal use?

To make ammi visnaga extract for medicinal use, start by harvesting the seeds of the Ammi visnaga plant, which is native to the Mediterranean region and often used in herbal medicine.

Clean and dry the seeds, then grind them into a fine powder using a mortar and pestle or a spice grinder.

Steep the powder in a carrier oil, such as olive or coconut oil, in a cool dark place for several weeks to create a concentrated extract.
