arctium lappa extract

intro

Arctium lappa extract, also known as burdock root extract, is a natural remedy derived from the roots of the Arctium lappa plant. It has been traditionally used in herbal medicine to promote digestive health and alleviate symptoms of constipation, bloating, and indigestion. Additionally, arctium lappa extract has been studied for its potential anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which may contribute to its overall health benefits.
