Cimicifuga racemosa extract is used to treat symptoms associated with menopause, such as hot flashes and night sweats.

How to make Cimicifuga racemosa extract for medicinal use?

To make cimicifuga racemosa extract for medicinal use, it is essential to start by harvesting the roots of the plant, typically in the fall when they are at their highest potency.

The roots should then be dried and chopped into smaller pieces to increase their surface area.

What are the health side effects of Cimicifuga racemosa extract?
