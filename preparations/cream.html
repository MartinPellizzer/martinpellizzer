aesculus hippocastanum

Aesculus hippocastanum cream, also known as horse chestnut cream, is a topical treatment used to alleviate symptoms of varicose veins, spider veins, and other circulatory issues. The cream typically contains aescin, a saponin extracted from the horse chestnut tree, which has anti-inflammatory and vasoconstrictive properties that help to reduce swelling and improve blood flow. By applying the cream to affected areas, individuals can experience relief from discomfort, itching, and discoloration associated with varicose veins and other circulatory problems.

aesculus hippocastanum cream
