cassia senna

Cassia senna cream is a topical ointment typically used to treat constipation and other digestive issues. It is derived from the leaves of the cassia senna plant and contains anthraquinone compounds, which stimulate bowel movements and help to soften stool. By applying the cream directly to the skin, individuals can experience relief from constipation and other gastrointestinal symptoms.
