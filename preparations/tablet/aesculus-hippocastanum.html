aesculus hippocastanum tablet

intro

Aesculus hippocastanum, commonly known as horse chestnut, is a herbal remedy often used in the form of a tablet to alleviate symptoms of varicose veins and poor circulation. The active ingredients in these tablets are typically derived from the bark of the horse chestnut tree, which contains saponins that have anti-inflammatory properties. However, it is essential to consult a healthcare professional before taking Aesculus hippocastanum tablets, as they may interact with certain medications or exacerbate underlying health conditions.
