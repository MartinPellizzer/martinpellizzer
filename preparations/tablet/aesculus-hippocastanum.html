aesculus hippocastanum tablet

intro

The Aesculus hippocastanum tablet, also known as the Horse Chestnut tablet, is a herbal supplement made from the leaves and seeds of the Horse Chestnut tree. It is often used to support vein health and alleviate symptoms of conditions such as varicose veins and haemorrhoids. However, it is essential to consult a healthcare professional before taking Aesculus hippocastanum, especially for individuals with underlying medical conditions or taking medications.
