allium sativum tablet

intro

Allium sativum, commonly known as garlic, is often used to create supplements in the form of tablets. These tablets are typically made by drying and processing garlic extract, which is then encapsulated into a convenient and easily digestible form. Allium sativum tablets are believed to possess various health benefits, including antioxidant properties and potential cardiovascular support, although more research is needed to confirm their efficacy.
