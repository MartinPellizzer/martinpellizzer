artemisia annua tablet

intro

Artemisia annua, also known as sweet wormwood, is a traditional Chinese herb used to treat malaria and other diseases. The herb is processed into various forms, including tablets, which are used to extract its active compound artemisinin. Artemisinin has been shown to have potent antimalarial properties, and artemisia annua tablets are often used as a natural alternative to synthetic medications.
