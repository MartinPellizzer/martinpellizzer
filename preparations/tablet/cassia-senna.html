What To Know About Medicinal Cassia Senna Tablet?

Cassia senna tablets are a traditional herbal remedy used to stimulate bowel movements and relieve constipation. They are derived from the leaves of the senna plant, which contain anthraquinone glycosides that help to increase muscle contractions in the intestines and promote the elimination of waste. However, it's essential to use cassia senna tablets under medical supervision and follow the recommended dosage to avoid potential side effects such as diarrhea and abdominal cramps.

What are the medicinal uses of Cassia senna tablet?

Cassia senna tablet is used to relieve constipation by stimulating bowel movements and helping to clear out waste from the digestive system.

It is also used to treat hemorrhoids and other digestive issues caused by constipation.

Additionally, cassia senna tablet may help to reduce bloating and discomfort associated with constipation.

Cassia senna tablet has health benefits such as promoting digestive regularity and relieving constipation due to its laxative properties.

How to make Cassia senna tablet for medicinal use?

To make cassia senna tablet for medicinal use, start by cleaning and drying high-quality cassia senna leaves, which have been traditionally used for their laxative and purgative properties.

Then, grind the dried leaves into a fine powder and mix it with an excipient, such as lactose or starch, in the desired ratio.

The powder mixture is then compressed into tablet form using a tablet press or a manual press, and the tablets can be stored in a cool, dry place until use.
