astragalus membranaceus tablet

intro

Astragalus membranaceus, also known as Huang Qi, is a traditional Chinese medicinal herb often used in supplement form, including tablets. The tablets are typically made from the dried root of the astragalus plant and are believed to have adaptogenic properties, helping the body to resist stress and promote overall health. Some studies suggest that astragalus membranaceus tablets may have immune-boosting effects and can be beneficial for individuals with weakened immune systems.
