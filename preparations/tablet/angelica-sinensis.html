angelica sinensis tablet

intro

Angelica sinensis tablet, also known as dong quai tablet, is a traditional herbal remedy derived from the root of Angelica sinensis. It has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for centuries to promote women's health, reduce menstrual cramps and symptoms of menopause, and improve circulation. However, as with any herbal supplement, it's essential to consult with a healthcare professional before using angelica sinensis tablet, especially for individuals with underlying medical conditions or taking prescription medications.
