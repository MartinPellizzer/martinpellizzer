What To Know About Medicinal Ammi Visnaga Gel?

However, more research is needed to fully understand its effects and potential benefits, and it should be used under the guidance of a healthcare professional or skincare expert.

How to make Ammi visnaga gel for medicinal use?

To make ammi visnaga gel for medicinal use, start by steeping dried ammi visnaga roots in a carrier oil such as coconut or olive oil for several weeks to create a tincture.

Once the tincture is ready, mix it with a water-based gel medium, such as aloe vera gel or carrageenan, to create a smooth and consistent gel.

Finally, filter and store the ammi visnaga gel in a cool, dark place to preserve its medicinal properties.
