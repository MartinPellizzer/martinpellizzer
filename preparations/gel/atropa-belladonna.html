What To Know About Medicinal Atropa Belladonna Gel?

Atropa belladonna gel, also known as belladonna or deadly nightshade gel, is a topical preparation derived from the Atropa belladonna plant. The gel is often used in traditional medicine for its potential pain-relieving and anti-inflammatory properties, although its use is not well-studied and its efficacy is unclear. However, it's worth noting that Atropa belladonna is highly toxic and should be handled with extreme caution, as ingesting even a small amount can be fatal.

What are the medicinal uses of Atropa belladonna gel?

Atropa belladonna gel is used to treat certain types of pain, such as arthritis or neuralgia, due to its analgesic and anti-inflammatory properties.

It is also used to reduce inflammation and swelling in the affected area.

However, the gel form of atropa belladonna should only be used under the guidance of a healthcare professional and in accordance with their recommendations.
