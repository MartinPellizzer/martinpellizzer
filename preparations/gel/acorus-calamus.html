How to make Acorus calamus gel for medicinal use?

To make Acorus calamus gel for medicinal use, start by harvesting the rhizomes of the plant, then clean and dry them thoroughly.

Once the oil has been infused, mix it with a gel base, such as aloe vera gel or carrageenan, to create a thick and stable gel that can be applied topically for medicinal purposes.
