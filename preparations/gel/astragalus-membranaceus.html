What To Know About Medicinal Astragalus Membranaceus Gel?

Astragalus membranaceus gel, also known as Huang Qi gel, is a topical application derived from the roots of the Astragalus membranaceus plant, commonly used in traditional Chinese medicine. The gel is believed to have anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which may aid in wound healing and skin regeneration. Some studies suggest that astragalus membranaceus gel may also exhibit antimicrobial activity, making it a potential treatment for various skin conditions.
