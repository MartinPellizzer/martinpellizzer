angelica sinensis powder

intro

Angelica sinensis powder, also known as dong quai, is a traditional Chinese herbal remedy derived from the root of the Angelica sinensis plant. It has been used for centuries to promote women's health, alleviate menstrual cramps, and support the body's natural energy balance. The powder is often used in combination with other herbs to create a customized blend, and is available in various forms, including capsules, tablets, and teas.
