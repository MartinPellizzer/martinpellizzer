angelica sinensis powder

intro

Angelica sinensis powder, also known as dong quai, is a traditional Chinese herbal remedy extracted from the roots of the Angelica sinensis plant. It has been used for centuries to promote women's health, alleviate symptoms of menopause, and support cardiovascular well-being due to its high content of ferulic acid, vitamin E, and other beneficial compounds. When consumed as a supplement, angelica sinensis powder is often recommended to enhance blood circulation and reduce menstrual cramps.
