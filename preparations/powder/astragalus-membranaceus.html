astragalus membranaceus powder

intro

Astragalus membranaceus powder, also known as Huang Qi, is a traditional Chinese medicine derived from the root of the Astragalus plant. It has been used for centuries to boost the immune system, improve energy levels, and enhance overall well-being, making it a popular natural remedy among health enthusiasts. When taken as a supplement, astragalus membranaceus powder is often combined with other herbs to support adrenal function, reduce inflammation, and promote a healthy response to stress.
