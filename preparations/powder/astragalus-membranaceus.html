What To Know About Medicinal Astragalus Membranaceus Powder?

Astragalus membranaceus powder, also known as Huang Qi, is a traditional Chinese medicine derived from the root of the Astragalus plant. When taken as a supplement, astragalus membranaceus powder is often combined with other herbs to support adrenal function, reduce inflammation, and promote a healthy response to stress.
