azadirachta indica powder

intro

Azadirachta indica powder, also known as neem powder, is a natural and versatile product derived from the seeds and leaves of the neem tree. Rich in antioxidants and bioactive compounds, neem powder has been traditionally used in Ayurvedic medicine to promote skin and hair health, as well as to support the immune system. When used in skincare products or applied topically, azadirachta indica powder can help to soothe and calm irritated skin, reduce inflammation, and promote a natural glow.
