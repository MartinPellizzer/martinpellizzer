apium graveolens powder

intro

Apium graveolens, also known as celery seed powder, is derived from the seeds of the wild celery plant and is a popular herbal supplement used in traditional medicine. It is believed to have anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, making it useful for alleviating symptoms such as joint pain and digestive issues. Apium graveolens powder is often used in Ayurvedic and Chinese medicine to promote overall health and well-being.
