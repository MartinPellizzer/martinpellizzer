aesculus hippocastanum powder

intro

Aesculus hippocastanum powder, also known as horse chestnut powder, is a natural supplement derived from the seeds and leaves of the horse chestnut tree. It is commonly used to alleviate symptoms of varicose veins and swelling due to its high content of saponins, a group of compounds believed to have anti-inflammatory properties. However, it's essential to consult a healthcare professional before using aesculus hippocastanum powder, especially if you have any underlying medical conditions or are taking medications.
