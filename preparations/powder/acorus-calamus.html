acorus calamus powder

intro

Acorus calamus powder, also known as sweet flag or calamus powder, is derived from the dried and ground root of the Acorus calamus plant, a perennial herb native to Asia and Europe. Traditionally used in Ayurvedic and Unani medicine, the powder is valued for its potential health benefits, including reducing inflammation and improving digestive issues. However, its use is not without controversy, and some countries have restricted its sale due to concerns over its potential psychoactive effects and interactions with certain medications.
