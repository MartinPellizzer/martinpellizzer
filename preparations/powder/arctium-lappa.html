arctium lappa powder

intro

Arctium lappa powder, also known as burdock root powder, is a natural supplement derived from the roots of the burdock plant. Rich in antioxidants and anti-inflammatory compounds, it is often used to support digestive health and immune function. Arctium lappa powder can also be used as a natural detoxifier and is sometimes added to skincare products for its potential skin-soothing and anti-aging benefits.
