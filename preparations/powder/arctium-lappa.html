arctium lappa powder

intro

Arctium lappa powder, also known as burdock root powder, is a dietary supplement derived from the roots of the Greater Burdock plant. Rich in antioxidants and fiber, this powder is often used to support digestive health, reduce inflammation, and promote skin clarity. When consumed as a supplement, arctium lappa powder is typically mixed with water or added to food, allowing its potential health benefits to be absorbed by the body.
