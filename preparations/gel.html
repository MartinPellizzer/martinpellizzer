cassia senna

Cassia senna gel is a natural and soothing remedy derived from the leaves of the cassia senna plant, traditionally used to support digestive health and relieve symptoms of irritable bowel syndrome (IBS). This gel is rich in anthraquinone compounds, which help to stimulate bowel movements and promote regularity, while also reducing inflammation and discomfort in the digestive tract. By applying cassia senna gel topically, individuals can experience relief from constipation, bloating, and other digestive issues, promoting overall gut health and well-being.
