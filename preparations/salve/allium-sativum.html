allium sativum salve

intro

Allium sativum salve, also known as garlic salve, is a topical ointment made from the extracts of garlic (Allium sativum). This salve is often used to promote wound healing, reduce inflammation, and combat bacterial infections due to its antimicrobial properties. When applied topically, the salve may help soothe skin irritations, cuts, and scrapes, promoting a more rapid recovery and minimizing the risk of infection.
