What To Know About Medicinal Barosma Betulina Salve?

Barosma betulina, also known as roundwood or Buchu, is a plant native to South Africa that has been used for centuries in traditional medicine. This natural remedy is also believed to have antiseptic and antifungal properties, making it a popular choice for wound care and skin health.

To make barosma betulina salve for medicinal use, first harvest the leaves and roots of the Barosma betulina plant and dry them thoroughly to preserve their medicinal properties.
