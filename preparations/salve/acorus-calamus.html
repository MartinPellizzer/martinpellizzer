acorus calamus salve

intro

Acorus calamus salve, also known as calamus salve, is a topical treatment made from the root of the Acorus calamus plant. The salve is traditionally used to relieve various skin irritations and conditions, such as eczema, acne, and minor wounds, due to its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties. Its soothing and protective qualities make it a popular natural remedy for promoting skin health and wound healing.
