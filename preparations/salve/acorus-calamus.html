How to make Acorus calamus salve for medicinal use?

To make acorus calamus salve for medicinal use, start by harvesting and cleaning the roots of the Acorus calamus plant, then dry them thoroughly to preserve the active compounds.

Once the roots are dry, chop them into smaller pieces and combine them with a carrier oil, such as coconut or olive oil, in a ratio of about 1 part dried root to 4 parts carrier oil.

After infusing the mixture for several weeks, strain the oil and mix it with beeswax to create a salve, which can be stored in an airtight container for future use.
