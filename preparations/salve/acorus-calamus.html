acorus calamus salve

intro

Acorus calamus salve is a topical remedy made from the roots of the sweet flag plant, which has been used for centuries in traditional medicine. The salve is often used to soothe and calm skin irritations, such as eczema, acne, and insect bites, due to its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties. When applied topically, Acorus calamus salve is said to promote healing, reduce redness and swelling, and leave the skin feeling smooth and refreshed.
