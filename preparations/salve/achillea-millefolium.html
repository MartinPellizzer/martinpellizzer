achillea millefolium salve

intro

Achillea millefolium salve, also known as yarrow salve, is a topical remedy made from the leaves and flowers of the yarrow plant. This natural salve is often used to soothe minor cuts, scrapes, and burns, as well as to calm skin irritations and promote wound healing. The anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties of yarrow in the salve help to reduce swelling, prevent infection, and speed up the recovery process.
