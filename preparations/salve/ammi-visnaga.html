ammi visnaga salve

intro

Ammi visnaga salve is a topical cream or ointment made from the dried roots and seeds of the ammi visnaga plant, which has been used for centuries in traditional medicine. This salve is often used to treat various skin conditions, including acne, eczema, and wounds, due to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. The ammi visnaga salve is also believed to have antiseptic and antifungal properties, making it a popular natural remedy for soothing and healing irritated skin.
