artemisia absinthium salve

intro

Artemisia absinthium salve, also known as wormwood salve, is a topical remedy made from the leaves and flowers of the wormwood plant. This salve is often used to treat minor skin irritations, such as insect bites, cuts, and scrapes, due to its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties. By applying wormwood salve to affected areas, individuals can potentially experience relief from pain, itching, and inflammation, promoting faster healing and reduced scarring.
