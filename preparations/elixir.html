cassia senna

Cassia senna elixir is a herbal remedy traditionally used to support digestive health and promote regular bowel movements. The elixir is made from the dried pods of the cassia senna plant, which contain natural laxatives that help to stimulate the digestive system and relieve constipation. When consumed as directed, cassia senna elixir can provide relief from symptoms of constipation and promote a sense of overall well-being.
