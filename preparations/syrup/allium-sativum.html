allium sativum syrup

intro

Allium sativum syrup, also known as garlic syrup, is a medicinal syrup made from the extract of garlic (Allium sativum). It is often used in traditional medicine to treat a range of health issues, including high blood pressure, digestive problems, and respiratory infections. The syrup is typically made by steeping garlic in a solvent, such as glycerin or sugar syrup, to create a concentrated and easily digestible form of garlic's medicinal compounds.
