allium sativum syrup

intro

Allium sativum syrup is a medicinal syrup derived from garlic, commonly used to treat various health conditions such as high blood pressure and respiratory infections. The syrup is typically made by infusing garlic in a solvent like glycerin or ethanol, which helps to extract its bioactive compounds. Allium sativum syrup has been traditionally used in herbal medicine to promote digestion, reduce inflammation, and boost the immune system.
