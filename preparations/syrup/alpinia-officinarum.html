alpinia officinarum syrup

intro

Alpinia officinarum syrup is a medicinal preparation derived from the rhizome of the Galangal plant, native to Southeast Asia. The syrup has been traditionally used to treat various health conditions, including digestive issues, fever, and inflammation, due to its active compounds like gingerols and shogaols. Modern research has also explored the potential benefits of Alpinia officinarum syrup in managing nausea and improving respiratory health.
