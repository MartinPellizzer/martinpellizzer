ammi visnaga syrup

intro

Ammi visnaga syrup, also known as sea lavender syrup, is a natural remedy derived from the Ammi visnaga plant, traditionally used to treat a variety of health issues, including digestive problems and menstrual cramps. This sweet-tasting syrup is made by infusing the plant's roots in a sugar syrup, creating a soothing and calming liquid that can be consumed as a drink. Ammi visnaga syrup has been used in traditional medicine for centuries, particularly in the Middle East and Mediterranean regions, where it is still valued for its therapeutic properties.
