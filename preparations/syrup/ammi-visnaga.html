What To Know About Medicinal Ammi Visnaga Syrup?

Ammi visnaga syrup is a herbal remedy traditionally used to treat various health issues, including digestive problems and anxiety. Derived from the seeds of the ammi visnaga plant, the syrup is often used as a natural antispasmodic and anti-inflammatory agent. However, it's essential to consult with a healthcare professional before using ammi visnaga syrup, especially for individuals with pre-existing medical conditions or taking medications.

How to make Ammi visnaga syrup for medicinal use?

To make ammi visnaga syrup for medicinal use, combine dried ammi visnaga roots with water in a saucepan and simmer for 30 minutes to create a decoction.
