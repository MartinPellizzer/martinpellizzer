aesculus hippocastanum

Aesculus hippocastanum pomade is a topical hair care product containing extracts from the horse chestnut tree, known for its potential anti-inflammatory and soothing properties. This type of pomade may be used to help calm irritated scalps, reduce flaking, and promote a healthy scalp environment. However, it is essential to consult with a dermatologist or healthcare professional before using Aesculus hippocastanum pomade, especially for individuals with sensitive skin or allergies.

aloe vera

Aloe vera pomade has gained popularity in recent years due to its unique blend of nourishing aloe vera gel and styling pomade. This versatile product not only helps to style and hold hair in place but also provides soothing benefits for the scalp. By combining the benefits of natural aloe vera with the styling properties of traditional pomade, users can achieve a healthy and stylish look.

apium graveolens

Apium graveolens pomade is a skincare product derived from the seeds of the celery plant, Apium graveolens. This natural pomade is often used to soothe and calm irritated skin, while also providing a protective barrier against environmental stressors. Rich in antioxidants and other beneficial compounds, apium graveolens pomade has been traditionally used to address a range of skin concerns, from acne and redness to dryness and inflammation.

artemisia absinthium

Artemisia absinthium pomade is a hair and beard styling product that incorporates the essence of wormwood, a plant known for its distinctive aroma and historical use in traditional remedies. This unique product combines the invigorating scent of wormwood with a blend of natural oils and waxes to create a styling aid that not only conditions and protects hair, but also imparts a distinctive, earthy fragrance. By harnessing the botanical properties of artemisia absinthium, this pomade offers a distinctive alternative to more conventional hair and beard styling products.
