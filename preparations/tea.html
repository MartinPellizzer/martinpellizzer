cassia senna

Cassia senna tea, also known as senna tea, is a herbal remedy made from the dried leaves of the cassia senna tree. It has been used for centuries to support digestive health and relieve constipation due to its natural laxative properties. However, it's essential to consume cassia senna tea under medical supervision, as excessive consumption can lead to dehydration and electrolyte imbalances.
