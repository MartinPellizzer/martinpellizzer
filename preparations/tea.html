achillea millefolium

Achillea millefolium tea, also known as yarrow tea, is a herbal infusion made from the dried flowers and leaves of the yarrow plant. It has been traditionally used to aid in digestion, reduce inflammation, and promote relaxation, making it a popular choice for those seeking a natural remedy for stress and digestive issues. When consumed as a tea, yarrow is said to have a calming effect on the body, helping to soothe the stomach and promote a restful night's sleep.

acorus calamus

Acorus calamus tea, also known as sweet flag tea, is a herbal remedy that has been used for centuries in traditional medicine to promote relaxation and reduce anxiety. The tea is made from the rhizome of the Acorus calamus plant, which is native to North America and Europe, and is said to have a sweet, earthy flavor. Some proponents of Acorus calamus tea claim that it can also help to soothe digestive issues and improve sleep quality, although more research is needed to confirm these benefits.

allium sativum

Allium sativum, commonly known as garlic, has been used for centuries as a medicinal herb, and its tea has gained popularity for its potential health benefits. The tea is made by infusing garlic cloves in hot water, releasing compounds like allicin, which may help lower cholesterol levels and boost the immune system. Drinking allium sativum tea may also have antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties, although more research is needed to confirm its effects on human health.

allium sativum tea

aloe vera

Aloe vera tea is a soothing and refreshing herbal beverage made by infusing the gel of the aloe vera plant in hot water. Rich in antioxidants and anti-inflammatory compounds, it is often consumed for its potential health benefits, including reducing stress and promoting digestive well-being. When brewed as a tea, aloe vera is said to have a mild, slightly sweet flavor and can be enjoyed hot or iced, sweetened with honey or other natural sweeteners.

ammi visnaga

Ammi visnaga tea, also known as khella tea, is a herbal remedy traditionally used to treat various respiratory issues, including bronchitis and asthma. artemisia absinthium

Artemisia absinthium tea, also known as wormwood tea, is a herbal infusion made from the leaves and flowers of the wormwood plant. atropa belladonna

Atropa belladonna, also known as deadly nightshade, is a highly toxic plant that has been used in traditional medicine for its purported medicinal properties, including reducing fever and relieving pain. However, brewing the plant's leaves or berries into a tea is extremely hazardous due to its potent toxicity and potential to cause severe side effects, including hallucinations, seizures, and even death. As a result, atropa belladonna tea is not recommended for consumption under any circumstances and should only be handled by trained professionals in a controlled environment.

atropa belladonna tea
