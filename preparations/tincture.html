cassia senna

Cassia senna tincture is a herbal remedy derived from the leaves and pods of the Cassia senna tree, traditionally used to promote digestion and relieve constipation. The tincture works by stimulating the muscles in the intestines to move food through the digestive system, thereby facilitating regular bowel movements. However, it's essential to use cassia senna tincture under the guidance of a healthcare professional, as excessive use can lead to dependence and other side effects.
