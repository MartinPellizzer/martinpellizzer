achillea millefolium

Achillea millefolium, also known as yarrow, is a herbal remedy often used to reduce inflammation and promote wound healing. Yarrow tincture is commonly made by steeping the plant's leaves and flowers in a solvent, such as ethanol or glycerin, and is said to possess antiseptic and anti-inflammatory properties. When taken internally, achillea millefolium tincture may help alleviate symptoms of menstrual cramps, digestive issues, and other minor health concerns.
