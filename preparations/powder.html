cassia senna

Cassia senna powder is a natural laxative derived from the leaves of the cassia senna plant, commonly used in herbal remedies to promote digestive health and regular bowel movements. Traditionally, it has been used to treat constipation, reduce bloating, and alleviate symptoms of irritable bowel syndrome. When consumed, cassia senna powder can stimulate the bowels to move and help eliminate waste, promoting a sense of relief and renewal.
