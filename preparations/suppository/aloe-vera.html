How to make Aloe vera suppository for medicinal use?

To make aloe vera suppository for medicinal use, mix aloe vera gel with a small amount of glycerin and water, then fill empty suppository molds with the mixture and refrigerate until it solidifies.

Once solidified, carefully remove the suppositories from the molds and store them in an airtight container in the refrigerator.
