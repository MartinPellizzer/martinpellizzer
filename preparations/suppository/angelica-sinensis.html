What To Know About Medicinal Angelica Sinensis Suppository?

Angelica sinensis suppositories are a type of vaginal insert used for medicinal purposes, particularly in traditional Chinese medicine. They are designed to release the active compounds of the Angelica sinensis plant, also known as dong quai, which is believed to have uterine-toning and anti-inflammatory properties. By providing localized delivery of these compounds, angelica sinensis suppositories aim to promote women's health and alleviate symptoms associated with menstrual disorders and menopausal issues.

What are the medicinal uses of Angelica sinensis suppository?

Angelica sinensis suppository is used to treat menstrual disorders, such as dysmenorrhea and amenorrhea, due to its ability to promote menstrual regularity and reduce pain.

It is also used to alleviate symptoms of menopause, including hot flashes and night sweats, by regulating estrogen levels.

Additionally, the suppository may help to improve cardiovascular health by reducing inflammation and improving circulation.
