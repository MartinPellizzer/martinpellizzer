How to make Chamomilla recutita suppository for medicinal use?

To make chamomilla recutita suppository for medicinal use, you will need to infuse dried chamomilla recutita flowers in a carrier oil such as coconut or olive oil, allowing the mixture to steep for several weeks in a cool, dark place.

After the infusion is complete, strain the mixture and mix it with a base such as glycerin or beeswax to create a suppository consistency.

Once the mixture has cooled and hardened, you can shape it into suppository form and store it in an airtight container for later use.
