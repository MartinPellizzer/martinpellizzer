aloe vera

Aloe vera gel is a natural, soothing gel extracted from the leaves of the aloe vera plant, known for its numerous health and beauty benefits. Rich in vitamins A, C, and E, as well as antioxidants and amino acids, aloe vera gel helps to calm irritated skin, reduce inflammation, and promote healing. By applying aloe vera gel topically, individuals can enjoy smoother, more radiant skin and experience relief from sunburn, eczema, and other skin irritations.
