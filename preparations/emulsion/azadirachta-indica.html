What To Know About Medicinal Azadirachta Indica Emulsion?

Azadirachta indica emulsion, also known as neem emulsion, is a natural and eco-friendly pesticide derived from the seeds of the neem tree. This emulsion is rich in azadirachtin, a compound that has insecticidal and fungicidal properties, making it an effective solution for controlling pests and diseases in agricultural crops. When used as a foliar spray or soil treatment, neem emulsion can help reduce the risk of pests and diseases, promoting healthy plant growth and increasing crop yields.

To make azadirachta indica emulsion for medicinal use, first, extract neem oil from the seeds of Azadirachta indica using a solvent like hexane or ethanol, and then mix it with a surfactant such as lecithin or polysorbate 80.
