cassia senna

Cassia senna, also known as senna, is a natural laxative derived from the leaves of the cassia tree. When taken as a supplement, typically in pill form, it works by stimulating the colon and increasing bowel movements, helping to relieve constipation. However, it is essential to consult a healthcare professional before using cassia senna, as it can cause side effects such as stomach cramps, diarrhea, and dehydration in some individuals.
