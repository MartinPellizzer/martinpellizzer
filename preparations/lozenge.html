cassia senna

Cassia senna lozenges are a natural remedy derived from the Cassia senna plant, traditionally used to support digestive health and promote regular bowel movements. These lozenges contain senna glycosides, which can help stimulate the gut and alleviate symptoms of constipation. By using cassia senna lozenges, individuals may experience relief from digestive discomfort and promote a healthy digestive system.
