cassia senna

Cassia senna extract, derived from the leaves of the Cassia senna plant, has been used in traditional medicine for its laxative and digestive properties. The extract contains anthraquinone glycosides, which help stimulate bowel movements and relieve constipation. However, due to its potential to cause stomach upset and interact with certain medications, it's essential to consult a healthcare professional before using cassia senna extract.
