What To Know About Medicinal Barosma Betulina Paste?

Barosma betulina, also known as the "buchu" or "round-leaf barosma," is a plant native to South Africa that has been used for centuries in traditional medicine and as a flavoring agent. The leaves of the Barosma betulina plant are used to create a fragrant and medicinal paste, which is often used to treat various ailments, including fever, digestive issues, and skin problems. The paste has a distinctive aroma and flavor, often used in traditional remedies and as a natural alternative to commercial medications.
