intro

Allium sativum, commonly known as garlic, is a widely used herb in various forms, including tablets. The tablets are typically made by drying and processing garlic into a concentrated form, often in combination with other ingredients like fillers and binders. Consuming Allium sativum tablets is believed to have several health benefits, including antioxidant and antimicrobial properties, although more research is needed to confirm their effectiveness.
