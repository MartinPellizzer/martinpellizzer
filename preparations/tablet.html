cassia senna

Cassia senna tablets are a natural laxative derived from the leaves of the Cassia senna tree, traditionally used to promote bowel movements and relieve constipation. The tablets contain sennosides, which stimulate the muscles in the intestines to contract and move waste out of the body. When taken as directed, cassia senna tablets can provide gentle and effective relief from constipation, but it's essential to follow the recommended dosage and consult a healthcare professional if necessary.
