angelica sinensis

Angelica sinensis tablet, also known as Dong Quai, is a traditional Chinese medicine derived from the roots of the Angelica sinensis plant. It is commonly used to promote women's health, reduce menstrual cramps, and alleviate symptoms of menopause. The tablet is believed to balance the body's energy, or "qi," and is often taken in conjunction with other herbal remedies to promote overall well-being.
