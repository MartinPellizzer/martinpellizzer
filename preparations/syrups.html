allium sativum

Allium sativum syrup is a sweet and flavorful liquid extract derived from the bulbs of garlic, scientifically known as Allium sativum. This syrup is often used as a natural remedy for various health issues, including coughs, colds, and digestive problems, due to its antibacterial and antiviral properties. However, its effectiveness and safety may vary depending on individual tolerance and specific health conditions, making it essential to consult a healthcare professional before using it as a treatment.
