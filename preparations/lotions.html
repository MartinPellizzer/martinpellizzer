aesculus hippocastanum

Aesculus hippocastanum lotion, also known as horse chestnut lotion, is a topical treatment that utilizes the extract of the Aesculus hippocastanum plant to help alleviate swelling and inflammation. This natural remedy is often used to soothe itchy skin, reduce the appearance of varicose veins, and promote healthy circulation. By applying Aesculus hippocastanum lotion to affected areas, individuals can enjoy a range of benefits that promote overall skin well-being and comfort.
