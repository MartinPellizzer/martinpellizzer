Aesculus hippocastanum lotion is a topical treatment that contains the extract of the horse chestnut plant, which has anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties. It is commonly used to soothe and calm irritated skin, reducing redness and swelling. This lotion may be beneficial for conditions such as eczema, varicose veins, and poor circulation.
