What To Know About Medicinal Achillea Millefolium Lotion?

Achillea millefolium lotion, also known as yarrow lotion, is a topical cream or oil extracted from the leaves and flowers of the yarrow plant. This lotion is often used to soothe and calm irritated skin, reducing inflammation and promoting wound healing. Its antimicrobial and antiseptic properties also help to prevent infection and promote a healthy balance of skin flora, making it a popular natural remedy for various skin issues.

How to make Achillea millefolium lotion for medicinal use?

To make achillea millefolium lotion for medicinal use, steep dried Achillea millefolium flowers in a carrier oil such as coconut or olive oil for several weeks to create an infused oil.

Strain the infused oil and mix it with a moisturizing base, such as distilled water and emulsifying agents, in a ratio that typically ranges from 1:10 to 1:20.

Finally, add a preservative to prevent spoilage and store the lotion in a cool, dark place for later use.
