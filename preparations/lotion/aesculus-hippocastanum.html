aesculus hippocastanum lotion

intro

Aesculus hippocastanum lotion, also known as horse chestnut lotion, is a topical treatment often used to alleviate symptoms of varicose veins and related conditions such as swelling, pain, and discoloration. The lotion typically contains extracts from the horse chestnut plant, which have anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties that help to soothe and protect the skin. By reducing inflammation and improving circulation, aesculus hippocastanum lotion can provide relief from discomfort and promote overall skin health.
