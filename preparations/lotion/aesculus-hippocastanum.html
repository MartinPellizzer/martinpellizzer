aesculus hippocastanum lotion

intro

Aesculus hippocastanum, also known as horse chestnut extract, is a key ingredient in various skincare products, including lotions, due to its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties. When used in a lotion, aesculus hippocastanum can help to soothe and calm irritated skin, reducing the appearance of redness and swelling. By incorporating aesculus hippocastanum into a daily skincare routine, individuals may experience improved skin health and a more even skin tone.
