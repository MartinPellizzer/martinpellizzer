cassia senna

Cassia senna oil, derived from the leaves of the cassia senna plant, is a natural laxative that has been used for centuries in traditional medicine. This oil is believed to stimulate digestion and bowel movement, making it a popular remedy for constipation and other gastrointestinal issues. However, due to its strong laxative properties, it's essential to use cassia senna oil under the guidance of a healthcare professional to avoid potential side effects.
