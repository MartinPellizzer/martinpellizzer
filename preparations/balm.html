cassia senna

Cassia senna balm is a topical application derived from the leaves of the cassia senna plant, commonly used in traditional medicine to soothe and calm irritated skin. The balm's anti-inflammatory properties can help alleviate symptoms associated with eczema, dermatitis, and other skin conditions. By promoting skin regeneration and reducing redness, cassia senna balm can provide long-lasting relief and support the healing process.
