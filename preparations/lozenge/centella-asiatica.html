What To Know About Medicinal Centella Asiatica Lozenge?

Centella asiatica lozenges are a type of oral supplement that harnesses the medicinal properties of the centella plant, also known as gotu kola. These lozenges are often used to promote skin health, improve wound healing, and support mental clarity due to the plant's high antioxidant and anti-inflammatory content. By dissolving the lozenge in the mouth, users can experience the potential benefits of centella asiatica in a convenient and easily absorbed form.

What are the medicinal uses of Centella asiatica lozenge?

Centella asiatica lozenge is used to soothe and calm inflammation in the mouth and throat, while also promoting wound healing and reducing bacterial growth.

It may also help to alleviate symptoms of dry mouth and improve overall oral health.

Additionally, some users claim that it has anti-aging properties, reducing fine lines and wrinkles around the mouth.

Centella asiatica lozenge has health benefits such as reducing inflammation, improving skin health, and supporting the immune system.

How to make Centella asiatica lozenge for medicinal use?

To make centella asiatica lozenge for medicinal use, it's essential to first infuse centella asiatica leaves in a carrier oil, such as coconut oil, to extract its active compounds.

Next, combine the infused oil with a natural sweetener like honey and a binding agent like glycerin to create a lozenge mixture.

Finally, pour the mixture into a lozenge mold and allow it to set, then store the lozenges in an airtight container for later use.
