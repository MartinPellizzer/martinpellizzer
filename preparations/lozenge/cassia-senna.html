What To Know About Medicinal Cassia Senna Lozenge?

Cassia senna lozenges are a natural remedy often used to support digestive health and relieve constipation. They contain the extracts of Cassia senna, a plant-based laxative that helps to stimulate bowel movements and promote regularity. When consumed as a lozenge, the active ingredients are absorbed through the mouth, bypassing the digestive system, and providing a gentle, yet effective, laxative effect.

What are the medicinal uses of Cassia senna lozenge?

Cassia senna lozenge is used to alleviate digestive issues, such as constipation and bloating, due to its natural laxative properties.

It may also help to soothe an upset stomach and reduce inflammation.

Additionally, some people use cassia senna lozenge to support healthy blood sugar levels and cholesterol.

How to make Cassia senna lozenge for medicinal use?

To make cassia senna lozenge for medicinal use, combine 1 tablespoon of dried cassia senna leaves with 1 cup of water in a saucepan, then simmer for 10 minutes to create a strong infusion.

Strain the liquid and mix it with 1 cup of honey and 1 tablespoon of glycerin to create a sweet and soothing mixture.

Allow the mixture to cool and thicken before pouring it into a mold to create the lozenges.
