How to make Calendula officinalis lozenge for medicinal use?

To make calendula officinalis lozenge for medicinal use, infuse calendula flowers in a carrier oil such as coconut or olive oil, then mix the infused oil with a natural gelatin powder to create a lozenge base.

Add a sweetening agent like honey or stevia to the mixture, along with any desired herbs or essential oils for added medicinal properties.
