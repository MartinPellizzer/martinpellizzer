acorus calamus

Acorus calamus powder, also known as calamus powder, is derived from the rhizome of the Acorus calamus plant and has been used in traditional medicine for centuries. The powder is said to possess anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties, making it a popular ingredient in herbal remedies and skincare products. In Ayurvedic medicine, acorus calamus powder is often used to calm the nervous system and promote relaxation, although it is recommended to use it in moderation due to potential side effects.
