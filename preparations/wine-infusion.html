wine infusion

angelica sinensis

Angelica sinensis wine infusion, also known as dong quai wine, is a traditional Chinese herbal remedy that combines the medicinal properties of angelica root with the health benefits of red wine. This unique blend is believed to promote women's health, alleviate menstrual cramps and symptoms associated with menopause, and even support cardiovascular well-being. By infusing angelica sinensis in red wine, practitioners aim to create a balanced and potent remedy that harnesses the synergistic effects of these two natural ingredients.
