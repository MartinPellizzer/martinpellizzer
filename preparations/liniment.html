cassia senna

Cassia senna liniment is a topical herbal remedy traditionally used to treat various health issues, including arthritis, muscle and joint pain, and skin conditions. The liniment is made from the leaves and pods of the cassia senna plant, which contain anthraquinone compounds that have anti-inflammatory properties. When applied topically, cassia senna liniment is believed to help reduce pain and inflammation, promote relaxation, and soothe irritated skin.
