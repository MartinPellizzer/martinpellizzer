aesculus hippocastanum

Aesculus hippocastanum liniment is a topical preparation made from the extract of the horse chestnut tree, often used to relieve pain and reduce inflammation. Traditionally, it has been employed to soothe sprains, bruises, and swelling caused by injury or overuse, promoting faster recovery and reduced discomfort. However, its effectiveness and safety have been the subject of ongoing debate, with some studies suggesting potential interactions and adverse effects when used in conjunction with certain medications.
