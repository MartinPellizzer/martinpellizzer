What To Know About Medicinal Ammi Visnaga Gum?

Ammi visnaga gum, also known as sweet ferula, is a natural resin extracted from the seeds of the Ammi visnaga plant, a species of parsley native to the Mediterranean region. Traditionally used in herbal medicine, ammi visnaga gum has been employed to treat various ailments, including coughs, colds, and respiratory issues. Its active compounds, including furanocoumarins and sesquiterpenes, are thought to be responsible for its therapeutic properties, although more research is needed to confirm its effectiveness.
