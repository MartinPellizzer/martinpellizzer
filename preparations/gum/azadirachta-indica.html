azadirachta indica gum

intro

Azadirachta indica gum, also known as neem gum, is a natural resin extracted from the neem tree (Azadirachta indica). This gum is rich in bioactive compounds, including limonoids and flavonoids, which are believed to have antimicrobial, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant properties. Due to its potential health benefits and eco-friendly nature, neem gum is being researched and explored as a sustainable alternative in various industries, including pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, and textiles.
