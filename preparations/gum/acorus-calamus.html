acorus calamus gum

intro

Acorus calamus, also known as sweet flag or calamus, is a plant native to Asia and Europe that has been used for centuries in traditional medicine and herbalism. The rhizome of the plant contains a sweet, aromatic gum that has been used as a natural remedy for digestive issues, including nausea, indigestion, and diarrhea. The gum is also valued for its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties, making it a popular ingredient in herbal teas and natural remedies.
