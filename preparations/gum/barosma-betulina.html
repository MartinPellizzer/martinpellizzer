What To Know About Medicinal Barosma Betulina Gum?

Barosma betulina, also known as the round-peeled pepper tree, is a plant native to South Africa. Its resin or gum is used in traditional medicine and has been found to have antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties. The gum is also used as a flavoring agent in the production of certain soft drinks, due to its unique and pungent flavor profile.
