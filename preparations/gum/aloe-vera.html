aloe vera gum

intro

Aloe vera gum is a unique, sugar-free chewing gum that leverages the benefits of aloe vera to promote oral health and hygiene. Its soothing properties can help reduce inflammation and irritation in the mouth, while also providing a refreshing and long-lasting flavor. Available in various flavors, aloe vera gum is a great option for those looking for a healthier alternative to traditional chewing gum.
