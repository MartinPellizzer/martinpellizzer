achillea millefolium gum

intro

Achillea millefolium, commonly known as yarrow, has been used for centuries in traditional medicine to produce a natural gum. This gum is derived from the plant's resin, which is collected from the stems and leaves, and is said to have antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties. Yarrow gum has been traditionally used to soothe mouth sores, gum inflammation, and other oral health issues.
