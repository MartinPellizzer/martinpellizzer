apium graveolens gum

intro

Apium graveolens, also known as celery seed gum, is a type of plant-based gum derived from the seeds of the celery plant. This gum is often used as a natural emulsifier and thickener in food products, cosmetics, and pharmaceuticals due to its unique properties and versatility. Apium graveolens gum is also valued for its potential health benefits, including antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties.
