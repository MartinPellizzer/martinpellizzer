cassia senna

Cassia senna glycerite, also known as senna glycerite, is a liquid herbal remedy derived from the leaves of the Cassia senna plant, traditionally used to support digestive health and relieve constipation. This glycerite formulation allows for a gentle and soothing delivery of the senna's active compounds, which stimulate bowel movements and promote regularity. However, it's essential to use cassia senna glycerite under the guidance of a healthcare professional due to its potential side effects and interactions with other medications.
