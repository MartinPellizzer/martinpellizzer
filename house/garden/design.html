10 Garden Designs Using Native Plants

Creating a garden with native plants offers a sustainable and beautiful way to enhance your outdoor space. These plants are adapted to local climate and soil conditions, making them easier to maintain and more resilient. Incorporating native species also supports local wildlife, including birds, butterflies, and bees. The article explores 10 creative garden designs that highlight the versatility and charm of native flora. Each design provides inspiration for transforming your yard into a thriving, eco-friendly oasis.
