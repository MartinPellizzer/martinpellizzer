Delphinium pachycentrum var. pachycentrum

Delphinium pachycentrum var. pachycentrum, commonly known as the thick-centred larkspur, is a member of the Ranunculaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly in countries like Afghanistan and Pakistan. The plant is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of blue to purple flowers. Its leaves are palmately divided, with a rough, hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy perennial in suitable climates.
