Drepanolobus prostratus

Drepanolobus prostratus, commonly known as the prostrate winged bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its prostrate growth habit and distinctive winged pods. The plant features trifoliate leaves with serrated edges and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in racemes.

Its seeds are enclosed in long, slender pods that mature to a brownish color, and the plant is often used for both ornamental and agricultural purposes.
