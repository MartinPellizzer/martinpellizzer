Dennettia tripetala

Dennettia tripetala, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to western and central Africa.

It belongs to the family Anacardiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, dark-brown bark and simple, alternate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by bright red, fleshy berries that are edible for some animals but toxic to humans.
