Digitaria albicoma

Digitaria albicoma, commonly known as silverhair grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-haired inflorescence and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields. It has a fibrous root system and is often used for erosion control due to its dense growth habit.

Digitaria albicoma is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
