Delosperma brevisepalum var. majus

Delosperma brevisepalum var. majus, commonly known as the "Lavender Iceplant," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine, powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces showy, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink to lavender, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it ideal for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
