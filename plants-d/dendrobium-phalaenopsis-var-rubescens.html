Dendrobium phalaenopsis var. rubescens

Dendrobium phalaenopsis var. rubescens, commonly known as the red-tinged orchid, is a hybrid orchid species originating from Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in tropical climates. The plant features long, cane-like stems with clustered, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. Its blooms are often fragrant and can last for several weeks.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is popular in horticulture for indoor cultivation.
