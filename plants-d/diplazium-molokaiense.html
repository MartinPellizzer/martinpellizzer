Diplazium molokaiense

Diplazium molokaiense, commonly known as Molokai sword fern, is a species of fern native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It belongs to the family Davalliaceae and is characterized by its sword-shaped fronds that are typically bright green and glossy. Diplazium molokaiense thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in native forests on the island of Molokai.
