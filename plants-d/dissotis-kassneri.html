Dissotis kassneri

Dissotis kassneri, commonly known as the Kassner's mistflower, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features slender, upright stems with small, clustered white flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Dissotis kassneri is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
