Dendrobium Kuhlii

Kuhli's dendrobium, scientifically known as Dendrobium kuhlii, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. This plant is prized for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms that come in a variety of colors including red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and well-draining potting media. Its unique growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts. With proper care, Kuhli's dendrobium can flourish and produce stunning flowers throughout the year.
