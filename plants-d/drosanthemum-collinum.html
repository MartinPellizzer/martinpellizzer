Drosanthemum collinum

Drosanthemum collinum, commonly known as the rock daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, cushion-like growth habit. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Drosanthemum collinum is drought-tolerant and requires minimal maintenance once established.
