Dalbergia cultrata var. maymyensis

Dalbergia cultrata var. maymyensis, commonly known as Maymyen ebony, is a small to medium-sized tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its dark, dense wood that is highly valued for its strength and aesthetic appeal. The plant typically has pinnately compound leaves and produces small, pea-like flowers that are often pale yellow or white.

Its fruit is a legume containing several seeds, and it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with well-drained soil.
