Dianthus attenuatus subsp. requienii

Dianthus attenuatus subsp.

requienii, commonly known as the slender campion, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands. It is characterized by its delicate, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems.

The plant has slender, branching stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in rocky or coastal environments, often growing in well-drained soils.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ornamental species within the Caryophyllaceae family.
