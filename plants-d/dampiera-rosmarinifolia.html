Dampiera rosmarinifolia

Dampiera rosmarinifolia, commonly known as silverleaf dampiera, is a perennial shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features narrow, silver-gray leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of blue or violet, which bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Dampiera rosmarinifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
