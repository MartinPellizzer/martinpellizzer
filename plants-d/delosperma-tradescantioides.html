Delosperma tradescantioides

Delosperma tradescantioides, commonly known as the "ice plant," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. It is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.

Its ability to spread via runners makes it a hardy and low-maintenance ground cover.
