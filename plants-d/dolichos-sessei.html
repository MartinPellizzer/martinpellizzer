Dolichos sessei

Dolichos sessei, commonly known as the African climbing bean, is a leguminous plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its twining stems and trifoliate leaves, which are typically alternate along the stem. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by elongated pods containing edible seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in savannah and woodland areas.

Dolichos sessei is valued for its nutritional seeds and is used in traditional cuisines across its native range.
