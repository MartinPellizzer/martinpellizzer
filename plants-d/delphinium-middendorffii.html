Delphinium middendorffii

Delphinium middendorffii, commonly known as the Siberian larkspur, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters. The plant produces dense clusters of blue to purple flowers, typically blooming in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

Delphinium middendorffii is valued in horticulture for its striking floral display and adaptability to cooler climates.
