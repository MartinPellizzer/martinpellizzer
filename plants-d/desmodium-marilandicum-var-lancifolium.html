Desmodium marilandicum var. lancifolium

Desmodium marilandicum var. lancifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaf burr medic, is a perennial legume native to the southeastern United States.

It features trifoliate leaves with narrow, lance-shaped leaflets and produces small, spiny pods that contain several seeds. The plant thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often found in disturbed soils, such as roadsides and fields. It has a deep root system that helps prevent soil erosion and is sometimes used in forage and erosion control.

Its flowers are typically pink to purple, adding ornamental value to the landscape.
