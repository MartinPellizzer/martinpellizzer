Diasperus Litoralis

Diasperus litoralis is a unique flowering plant native to coastal regions of southern Africa. It thrives in sandy and rocky habitats along the shoreline, where it withstands harsh environmental conditions. This plant is known for its compact growth and distinctive blue or purple flowers that bloom during the summer months. Its ability to survive in saline and arid environments makes it an important species for coastal ecosystems. Despite its resilience, it remains a relatively rare and underappreciated member of the flora.
