Dracocephalum rigidulum

Dracocephalum rigidulum, commonly known as rigid horseweed, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, branched stems covered in dense, hairy foliage. The plant produces small, purple to pink flowers arranged in spikes, typically blooming from late spring to early autumn. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a strong, aromatic scent.

Dracocephalum rigidulum is known for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
