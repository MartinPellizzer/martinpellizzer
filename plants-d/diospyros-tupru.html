Diospyros tupru

Diospyros tupru, commonly known as the African persimmon, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Ebenaceae family, which includes other species like ebony and persimmon. The tree is characterized by its smooth, dark bark and elliptical leaves that are glossy on top and pale underneath. Its fruit is a small, oval-shaped berry that turns from green to yellow or orange when mature.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
