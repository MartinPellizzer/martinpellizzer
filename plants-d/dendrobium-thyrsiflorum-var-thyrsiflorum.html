Dendrobium thyrsiflorum var. thyrsiflorum

Dendrobium thyrsiflorum var. thyrsiflorum, commonly known as the tall spike orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic orchid that grows on trees and rocks, thriving in humid tropical climates. The plant features long, cane-like pseudobulbs covered in fleshy leaves and produces tall, dense spikes of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. It blooms once or twice a year, typically during the warmer months.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
