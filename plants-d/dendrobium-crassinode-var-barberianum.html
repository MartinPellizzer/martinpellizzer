Dendrobium crassinode var. barberianum

Dendrobium crassinode var. barberianum, commonly known as the Barberianum Dendrobium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in humid tropical environments. The plant features long, cane-like stems that can reach several meters in length, with clusters of lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with a distinctive, fragrant scent.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its beauty and resilience.
