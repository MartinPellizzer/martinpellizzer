Dukea

Dukea, commonly known as the African violet, is a popular ornamental plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 10-30 cm and prefers bright, indirect light. Its flowers come in a variety of colors, including purple, pink, white, and red, making it a favorite among gardeners.

Dukea is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements.
