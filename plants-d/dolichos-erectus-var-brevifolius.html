Dolichos erectus var. brevifolius

Dolichos erectus var. brevifolius, commonly known as the short-leaved climbing bean, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, twining stems that can grow up to several meters in length. The leaves are palmately compound, with short, rounded lobes, and are typically trifoliate. Its small, yellow flowers are followed by elongated, cylindrical pods containing edible seeds.

This plant is valued for its nutritional content and is often cultivated for both human consumption and animal feed.
