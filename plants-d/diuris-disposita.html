Diuris disposita

Diuris disposita, commonly known as the slender spider-orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, spider-like flowers that are typically pale green to yellow in color. The plant features a rhizomatous growth habit with narrow, grass-like leaves, and it produces its showy inflorescences during the spring season.

Diuris disposita is often found in sandy or rocky soils in open habitats, and it plays a role in supporting local pollinator populations.
