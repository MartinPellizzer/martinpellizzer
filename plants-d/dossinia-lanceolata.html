Dossinia lanceolata

Dossinia lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceleaf dossinia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and small, clustered white or greenish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, woodland habitats and is found in parts of North America. It has a fibrous root system and produces small, dry fruits known as schizocarps.

Dossinia lanceolata is often noted for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
