Dianthus diutinus

Dianthus diutinus, commonly known as the everlasting dianthus, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish flowers with prominent white or pink markings, giving it a distinctive appearance. It is a perennial herb that thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure.

Dianthus diutinus is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
