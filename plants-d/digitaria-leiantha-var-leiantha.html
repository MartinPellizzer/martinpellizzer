Digitaria leiantha var. leiantha

Digitaria leiantha var. leiantha, commonly known as slender digitaria, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands or disturbed areas.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it a common weed in agricultural and natural settings.
