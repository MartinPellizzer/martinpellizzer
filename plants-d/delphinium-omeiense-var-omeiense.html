Delphinium omeiense var. omeiense

Delphinium omeiense var. omeiense, commonly known as the Omeiense Larkspur, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Sichuan, China.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem that can reach up to 2 meters in height, bearing clusters of blue to violet flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a serrated margin, and its flowers are typically arranged in dense spikes. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forested areas at high elevations.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
