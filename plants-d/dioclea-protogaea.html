Dioclea protogaea

Dioclea protogaea, commonly known as the red spider-orchid, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically red or orange in color. The plant produces small, bean-like pods that contain seeds dispersed by wind. Its leaves are alternate and pinnately compound, with a rough texture.

Dioclea protogaea is often found in humid, shaded environments such as rainforests.
