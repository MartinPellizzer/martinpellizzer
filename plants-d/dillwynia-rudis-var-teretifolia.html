Dillwynia rudis var. teretifolia

Dillwynia rudis var. teretifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed dillwynia, is a perennial shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features slender, often hairy stems and small, pea-like flowers that are typically yellow or pale green. Its leaves are narrow and slightly serrated, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy habitats.
