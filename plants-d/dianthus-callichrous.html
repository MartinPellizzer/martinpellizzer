Dianthus callichrous

Dianthus callichrous, commonly known as the red campion, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves, and it often grows in dry, open habitats. It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family, which includes other flowering plants with showy blooms.

Dianthus callichrous is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides.
