Dracaena reflexa var. salicifolia

Dracaena reflexa var. salicifolia, commonly known as the "Chinese evergreen" or "shamrock plant," is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, narrow, dark green leaves that are slightly curled at the edges, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its air-purifying qualities. It produces small, inconspicuous yellow-green flowers that are followed by red berries.

This variety is popular as a houseplant due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.
