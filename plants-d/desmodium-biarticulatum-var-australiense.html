Desmodium biarticulatum var. australiense

Desmodium biarticulatum var. australiense, commonly known as the Australian sicklepod, is a perennial legume native to Australia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, round pods that contain several seeds, and it thrives in dry, sandy soils. It is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

This species is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility.
