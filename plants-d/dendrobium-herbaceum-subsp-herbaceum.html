Dendrobium herbaceum subsp. herbaceum

Dendrobium herbaceum subsp.

herbaceum, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its slender, leafy stems and clusters of small, fragrant flowers.

The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its leaves are linear and arranged in two rows along the stem.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated for horticultural purposes.
