Dyckia brevispicata

Dyckia brevispicata, commonly known as the short-spurred dyckia, is a species of flowering plant in the bromeliad family.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its compact rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces inflorescences that are short and sparsely branched, with small, white to pale blue flowers. Dyckia brevispicata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and compact growth habit.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
