Diothonea viridiflora

Diothonea viridiflora, commonly known as the green-flowered diothonea, is a striking member of the Campanulaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant green petals that contrast beautifully with its deep blue or violet sepals. Native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its unique floral structure features both showy petals and functional sepals, which play a role in attracting pollinators.

The plant is also noted for its aromatic foliage, which emits a subtle, pleasant scent.
