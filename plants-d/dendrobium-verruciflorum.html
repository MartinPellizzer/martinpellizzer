Dendrobium verruciflorum

Dendrobium verruciflorum, commonly known as the疣花石斛, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with numerous pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with prominent, warty structures on the petals. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Dendrobium verruciflorum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
