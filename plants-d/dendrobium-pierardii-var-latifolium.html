Dendrobium pierardii var. latifolium

Dendrobium pierardii var. latifolium, commonly known as the broad-leafed dendrobium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant that grows on trees and rocks, thriving in humid, tropical environments. The plant features long, cane-like stems covered with thick, fleshy leaves that store water. Its flowers are typically yellow or green with purple or red markings, blooming in clusters along the stems.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
