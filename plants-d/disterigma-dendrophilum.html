Disterigma dendrophilum

Disterigma dendrophilum, commonly known as the tree-dwelling orchid, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and debris around its host.

Disterigma dendrophilum is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in the ecological balance of its native habitats.
