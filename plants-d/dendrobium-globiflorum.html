Dendrobium globiflorum

Dendrobium globiflorum, commonly known as the globeflower orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with thick, fleshy stems that store water and nutrients. The plant produces multiple clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are linear and arranged spirally around the stem.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media for optimal growth.
