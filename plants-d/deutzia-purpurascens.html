Deutzia purpurascens

Deutzia purpurascens, commonly known as the purpleleaf deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features slender, arching stems that bear clusters of small, fragrant white flowers in early summer. The leaves are ovate to elliptic, with a distinctive purplish tinge on the underside, giving it its common name. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

This ornamental plant is valued for its showy blooms and vibrant fall foliage.
