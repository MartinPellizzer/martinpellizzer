Desmodium stenophyllum

Desmodium stenophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf desmodium, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-bearing fruits. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb, often found in grasslands and disturbed soils. Its small, pea-like flowers are usually pink or purple, and it produces small, rectangular seed pods.

The plant is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility.
