Dombeya scabrida var. rotundifolia

Dombeya scabrida var. rotundifolia, commonly known as the rounded-leaved dombeya, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Africa.

It features oval, hairy, and often rounded leaves that are typically grayish-green in color. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that are usually pink or white, blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners in temperate climates.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms.
