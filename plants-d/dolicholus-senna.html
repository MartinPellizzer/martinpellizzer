Dolicholus senna

Dolicholus senna, commonly known as the yellow senna, is a tropical shrub native to parts of Africa and the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. Dolicholus senna is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.

It also has traditional uses in herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
