Disa calophylla

Disa calophylla, commonly known as the golden disa, is a striking South African orchid species.

It features vibrant yellow petals and a distinctive orange-red lip, making it highly ornamental. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in mountainous regions. It blooms during the summer months, producing clusters of showy flowers.

Botanically, it is characterized by its sympodial growth habit and specialized root system adapted to its arid environment.
