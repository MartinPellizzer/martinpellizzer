Dasycephala alata

Dasycephala alata, commonly known as the winged dasycephala, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive winged bracts on its inflorescence. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a rough, textured surface.

Dasycephala alata is often found in coastal and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions.
