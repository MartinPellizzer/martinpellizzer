Diuris goonooensis

Diuris goonooensis, commonly known as the Goonoo Goonoo orchid, is an endemic species native to the southeastern region of Queensland, Australia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a compact, low-growing habit, featuring a single, fleshy, underground tuber. The plant produces a single erect stem bearing a few narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically pale green to yellowish, with a distinctive hooded labellum and a spurred dorsal sepal.

Diuris goonooensis is adapted to grow in sandy or loamy soils in open, sunny habitats.
