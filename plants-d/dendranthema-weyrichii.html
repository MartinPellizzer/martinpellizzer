Dendranthema weyrichii

Dendranthema weyrichii, commonly known as the Weyrich's fleur-de-lis, is a perennial plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and thrives in well-drained soil.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its attractive foliage and long blooming period.
