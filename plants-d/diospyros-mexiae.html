Diospyros mexiae

Diospyros mexiae, commonly known as the Mexican persimmon, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, dark bark and elliptical leaves with a glossy surface.
