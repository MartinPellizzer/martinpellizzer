Distichlis Scoparia

Distichlis scoparia is a hardy grass species commonly found in coastal and saline environments. It thrives in areas with high salt exposure and can withstand harsh weather conditions. This plant plays a vital role in stabilizing dunes and preventing erosion. Its ability to grow in poor soil makes it an important part of coastal ecosystems. Distichlis scoparia is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its resilience and adaptability.
