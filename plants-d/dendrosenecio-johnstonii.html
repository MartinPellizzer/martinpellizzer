Dendrosenecio johnstonii

Dendrosenecio johnstonii, commonly known as the giant groundsels, is a striking plant native to the mountainous regions of Kenya and Tanzania.

Its foliage consists of broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating.
