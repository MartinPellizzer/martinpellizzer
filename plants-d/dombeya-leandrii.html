Dombeya leandrii

Dombeya leandrii, commonly known as the African dombeya, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height, featuring smooth, greyish bark and elliptical leaves. The plant produces clusters of showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, which bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for gardens in warm climates.

Dombeya leandrii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticulture for its long-lasting blooms.
