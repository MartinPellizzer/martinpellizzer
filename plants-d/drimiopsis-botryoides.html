Drimiopsis botryoides

Drimiopsis botryoides, commonly known as the clustered drimiopsis, is a striking evergreen perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features narrow, strap-shaped leaves that are often reddish or purple in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in clusters, typically in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its ornamental value and unique foliage, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
