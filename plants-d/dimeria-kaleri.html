Dimeria kaleri

Dimeria kaleri, commonly known as the Kaleri daisy, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters, with narrow, grass-like leaves and bright yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and borders.

Dimeria kaleri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticultural displays.
