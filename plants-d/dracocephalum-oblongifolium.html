Dracocephalum oblongifolium

Dracocephalum oblongifolium, commonly known as blueweed, is a flowering plant native to regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a hairy texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Dracocephalum oblongifolium is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
