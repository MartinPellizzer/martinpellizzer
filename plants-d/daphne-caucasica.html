Daphne caucasica

Daphne caucasica, commonly known as Caucasian daphne, is a deciduous shrub native to the Caucasus region.

It features striking, fragrant flowers that range from white to pink, often with a strong, spicy scent. The plant has dark green, elliptical leaves and produces small, woody fruits containing seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Daphne caucasica is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.
