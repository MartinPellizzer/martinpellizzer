Diuris × nebulosa

Diuris × nebulosa, commonly known as the Cloudy Diuris, is a hybrid orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, often iridescent petals that range in color from violet to deep purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as rainforest understoreys, where it thrives in the cool, humid conditions. Its flowers are usually arranged in a loose cluster, with each bloom featuring a distinctive, spurred lip.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with appropriate moisture and light conditions.
