Draba linearis

Draba linearis, commonly known as the slender whitlow grass, is a small flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in alpine or arctic environments. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers in early spring. It is part of the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants.

Draba linearis is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in harsh, cold climates.
