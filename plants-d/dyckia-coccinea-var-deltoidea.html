Dyckia coccinea var. deltoidea

Dyckia coccinea var. deltoidea, commonly known as the red dyckia, is a striking bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red bracts that surround the flower, creating a visually stunning display. The plant features rosettes of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, silver hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
