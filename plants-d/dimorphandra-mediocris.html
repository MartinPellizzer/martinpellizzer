Dimorphandra mediocris

Dimorphandra mediocris, commonly known as the yellow flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a vibrant and showy appearance. The plant has bipinnate leaves, which are compound and divided into many small leaflets. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The tree is also valued for its hard, durable wood, which is used in construction and furniture-making.
