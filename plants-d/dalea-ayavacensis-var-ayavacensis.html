Dalea ayavacensis var. ayavacensis

Dalea ayavacensis var. ayavacensis, commonly known as the Ayavacensis Dalea, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, pink to purple flowers. The plant produces trifoliate leaves with three oval-shaped leaflets, typical of the Fabaceae family. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to dry, hot climates.
