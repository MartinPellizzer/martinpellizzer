Digitalis obscura var. laciniata

Digitalis obscura var. laciniata, commonly known as the shredded foxglove, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features striking, hooded flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, with a distinctive spurred shape. The leaves are deeply lobed and serrated, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.
