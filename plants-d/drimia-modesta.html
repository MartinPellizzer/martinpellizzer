Drimia modesta

Drimia modesta, commonly known as the African lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features strap-shaped, lanceolate leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. The plant produces tall, slender stems that bear clusters of white or pale pink flowers. Drimia modesta is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil.

It is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
