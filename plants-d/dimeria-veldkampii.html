Dimeria veldkampii

Dimeria veldkampii, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal regions. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Dimeria veldkampii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
