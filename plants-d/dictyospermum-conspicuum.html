Dictyospermum conspicuum

Dictyospermum conspicuum, commonly known as the showy starfruit.

This tropical shrub is native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America. It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to yellow. The plant produces small, edible fruits that are often used in local cuisines.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in horticultural and ecological studies.
