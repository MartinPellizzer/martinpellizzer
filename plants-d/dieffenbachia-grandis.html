Dieffenbachia grandis

Dieffenbachia grandis, commonly known as the giant dieffenbachia, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with white or cream-colored veins. The plant is a member of the Araceae family, which includes other popular houseplants like peace lilies. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture to maintain its lush foliage.

Due to its striking appearance, it is often used as a decorative plant in both indoor and outdoor settings.
