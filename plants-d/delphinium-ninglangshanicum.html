Delphinium ninglangshanicum

Delphinium ninglangshanicum, commonly known as the Ninglang delphinium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant produces showy blue to violet flowers in terminal clusters, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are palmately divided, with a rough texture and a distinctive blue-green hue.

It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows at high elevations.
