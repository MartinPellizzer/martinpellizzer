Dryadella zebrina

Dryadella zebrina, commonly known as the zebra plant, is a member of the Araliaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa.

Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Dryadella zebrina is also valued as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
