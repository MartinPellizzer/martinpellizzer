Dioscorea changjiangensis

Dioscorea changjiangensis, commonly known as the Changjiang yam, is a species of tuberous plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its climbing habit and compound leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers and develops edible tubers that are used in traditional Chinese medicine.

Its botanical features make it an important species for both horticultural and medicinal research.
