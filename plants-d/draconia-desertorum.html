Draconia desertorum

Draconia desertorum, commonly known as the desert dragon plant, is a unique succulent native to arid regions.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in harsh, dry environments. The plant produces striking, spiky flowers that are typically bright yellow or orange. Its root system is deep and extensive, enabling it to access moisture from underground sources.

Draconia desertorum is well adapted to extreme temperatures and low rainfall, making it a resilient species in desert ecosystems.
