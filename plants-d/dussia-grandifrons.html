Dussia grandifrons

Dussia grandifrons, commonly known as the large-flowered dussia, is a striking perennial plant native to the wetlands of South America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, often with a contrasting dark purple or red throat. The plant has smooth, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

Dussia grandifrons is valued in gardens for its unique floral display and adaptability to wetland environments.
