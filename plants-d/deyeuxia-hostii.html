Deyeuxia hostii

Deyeuxia hostii, commonly known as the silver grass, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage and slender, arching stems that create a delicate, feathery appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in late spring to early summer, which are typically arranged in narrow panicles. Deyeuxia hostii thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value lies in its unique coloration and graceful growth habit.
