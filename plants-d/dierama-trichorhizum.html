Dierama trichorhizum

Dierama trichorhizum, commonly known as the woolly dierama, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, hairy leaves that help it conserve water in arid environments. The plant produces striking, showy inflorescences of pink to purple flowers during the spring and summer seasons. Its root system is fibrous and adapted to store moisture, aiding survival in dry conditions.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
