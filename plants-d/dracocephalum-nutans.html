Dracocephalum nutans

Dracocephalum nutans, commonly known as nodding dragonhead, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features upright, branched stems covered with hairy foliage, and its flowers are typically blue to purple in color, arranged in whorls along the stem. Dracocephalum nutans is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory properties.

It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.
