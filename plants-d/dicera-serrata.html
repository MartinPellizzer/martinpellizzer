Dicera serrata

Dicera serrata, commonly known as the serrated dicera, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in dense clusters, and bloom during the summer months.

Dicera serrata is often found in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils.
