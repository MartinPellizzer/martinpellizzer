Deutzia pilosa var. longiloba

Deutzia pilosa var. longiloba, commonly known as the long-stemmed deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has slender, arching stems and narrow, ovate leaves with a soft, hairy texture. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value comes from its showy flowers and attractive foliage.
