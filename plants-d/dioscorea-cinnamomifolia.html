Dioscorea cinnamomifolia

Dioscorea cinnamomifolia, commonly known as cinnamon vine, is a climbing perennial vine native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its aromatic, cinnamon-scented leaves and tuberous roots. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by heart-shaped, yellowish fruits. Its roots are valued for their medicinal properties and are used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.
