Dioscorea capillaris var. glabra

Dioscorea capillaris var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth yam, is a perennial vine native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and leaves. The plant produces tuberous roots that are often used in traditional medicine and as a food source. Its flowers are typically unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the same plant.

The plant thrives in well-drained soils and requires a warm, humid climate for optimal growth.
