Draba ruaxes

Draba ruaxes, commonly known as the Ruaxes whitlow grass, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many flowering plants with small, cruciform flowers.
