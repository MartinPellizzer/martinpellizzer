Deutzia calycosa var. brachytricha

Deutzia calycosa var. brachytricha, commonly known as the dwarf deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features clusters of fragrant, white to pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, arching branches and small, ovate leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental hedges and borders.
