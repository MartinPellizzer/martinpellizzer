Dracaena glonerea

Dracaena glonerea, commonly known as the glonerea dracaena, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, particularly in areas with warm and humid climates. The plant is characterized by its tall, narrow leaves that are often variegated with yellow or white markings. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate to bright indirect light.

Dracaena glonerea is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
