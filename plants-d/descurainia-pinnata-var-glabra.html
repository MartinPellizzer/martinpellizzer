Descurainia pinnata var. glabra

Descurainia pinnata var. glabra, commonly known as smooth mustard, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes other mustard plants. The plant is characterized by its smooth, hairless stems and leaves that are pinnately divided. Its small yellow flowers are arranged in racemes and bloom in the summer months.

It often grows in disturbed soils and can be found in open fields and along roadsides.
