Deutzia discolor var. densiflora

Deutzia discolor var. densiflora, commonly known as the Chinese deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer, typically in clusters along the branches. The plant has small, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a slightly fuzzy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its compact growth habit and showy blooms make it a valuable addition to landscapes seeking both beauty and hardiness.
