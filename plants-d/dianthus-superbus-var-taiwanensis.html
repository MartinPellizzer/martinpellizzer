Dianthus superbus var. taiwanensis

Dianthus superbus var. taiwanensis, commonly known as the Taiwan carnation, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink or white flowers with prominent, fringed petals. The plant typically grows in rocky or open habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. It is known for its compact growth habit and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

The species is also valued for its ecological role in supporting pollinators.
