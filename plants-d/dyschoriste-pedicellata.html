Dyschoriste pedicellata

Dyschoriste pedicellata, commonly known as the slender dyschoris, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. The plant features slender, erect stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves. Its small, white flowers are clustered in racemes, and it produces capsule fruits containing numerous tiny seeds.

Dyschoriste pedicellata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
