Delphinium hansenii subsp. ewanianum

Delphinium hansenii subsp.

ewanianum, commonly known as the Ewanian larkspur, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height.

The plant produces showy blue to purple flowers in dense clusters, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are compound, with palmately arranged leaflets that are lanceolate in shape.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine meadows at elevations above 2,000 meters.
