Dortmanna purpurascens

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth and vibrant purple or reddish coloration, which is more pronounced in shaded environments. The plant has a thalloid structure with a prostrate stem that branches profusely, and its leaves are closely arranged and often covered in a layer of fine, translucent cells. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is typically found in forest understories or on rocky substrates.

Dortmanna purpurascens plays a role in soil stabilization and provides microhabitat for various invertebrates.
