Delphinium sauricum

Delphinium sauricum, commonly known as the Tibetan larkspur, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It features tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant produces striking blue to purple flowers in racemes, which bloom in summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

Delphinium sauricum is adapted to cold, arid environments and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.
