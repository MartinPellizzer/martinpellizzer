Dracophyllum tasmanicum

Dracophyllum tasmanicum, commonly known as the Tasmanian heath, is a flowering plant native to Tasmania.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and coastal areas.

Dracophyllum tasmanicum is well adapted to the cool, humid climate of Tasmania.
