Daphne transcaucasica

Daphne transcaucasica, commonly known as the Caucasian daphne, is a deciduous shrub native to the Caucasus region.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that range from white to pink, blooming in early spring. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in temperate climates.
