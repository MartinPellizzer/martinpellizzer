Dactylorchis majalis var. atrorubens

Dactylorchis majalis var. atrorubens, commonly known as the red-stemmed fairy orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to deep red. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and pine flatwoods. Its roots form a symbiotic relationship with mycorrhizal fungi, which aid in nutrient absorption.

The species is notable for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
