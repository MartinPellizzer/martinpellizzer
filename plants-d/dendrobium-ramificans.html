Dendrobium ramificans

Dendrobium ramificans, commonly known as the branched orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a tall, branched stem that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are long and strap-like, often arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Dendrobium ramificans thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
