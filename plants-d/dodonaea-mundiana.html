Dodonaea mundiana

Dodonaea mundiana, commonly known as the velvetleaf hopseed, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its finely hairy, dark green leaves that give it a velvety appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by woody, spherical pods containing several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions.

Dodonaea mundiana is adapted to dry conditions and is sometimes used in landscaping for its ornamental value.
