Dendrobium Pedunculatum

Dendrobium orchid, scientifically known as Dendrobium pedunculatum, is a striking member of the orchid family native to Southeast Asia. This evergreen plant is prized for its elegant, long-lasting blooms that come in a variety of colors including white, pink, and yellow. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens. Its unique growth habit, with pseudobulbs and long, arching stems, makes it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts. With proper care, this orchid can bloom multiple times a year, adding beauty and fragrance to any space.
