Deyeuxia longiflora

Deyeuxia longiflora, commonly known as the long-flower sedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to temperate regions of Asia.

The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers.
