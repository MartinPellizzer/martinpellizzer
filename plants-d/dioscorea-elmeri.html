Dioscorea elmeri

Dioscorea elmeri, commonly known as Elmer's yam, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its climbing habit and tuberous roots. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically borne in clusters. Its tubers are often used in traditional medicine and as a food source in some regions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and medicinal properties.
