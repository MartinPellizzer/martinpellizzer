Drosanthemum hispifolium

Drosanthemum hispifolium, commonly known as the rough-leaved drosanthemum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, gray-green leaves and vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers. Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

Drosanthemum hispifolium is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
