Dillenia cyclopensis

Dillenia cyclopensis, commonly known as the cyclops dillenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dilleniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, palmately lobed leaves. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are fleshy berries that contain several seeds, and the plant is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Dillenia cyclopensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local cultures.
