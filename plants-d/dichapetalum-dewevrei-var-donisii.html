Dichapetalum dewevrei var. donisii

Dichapetalum dewevrei var. donisii, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the rainy season. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape and have a smooth texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant.

The tree is also valued for its rapid growth and ability to provide shade in tropical and subtropical climates.
