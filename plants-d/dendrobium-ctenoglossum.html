Dendrobium ctenoglossum

Dendrobium ctenoglossum, commonly known as the feather-leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant that grows on trees and rocks, typically in humid, tropical environments. The plant features a cane-like stem with numerous small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, hairy texture. Its flowers are showy, with bright yellow petals and a distinctive, elongated lip.

Dendrobium ctenoglossum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
