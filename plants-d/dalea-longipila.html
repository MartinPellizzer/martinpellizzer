Dalea longipila

Dalea longipila, commonly known as the longpod milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, such as grasslands and rocky soils. Its flowers are usually pink to purple, and it produces elongated, cylindrical seed pods.
