Dendrochilum stenochilum

Dendrochilum stenochilum, commonly known as the narrow-lipped tree orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and often forms clumps of pseudobulbs. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers with a distinctive narrow lip. Its leaves are typically elongated and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Dendrochilum stenochilum thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.
