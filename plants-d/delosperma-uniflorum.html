Delosperma uniflorum

Delosperma uniflorum, commonly known as the ice plant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant pink or red flowers during the spring and summer months. It is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance.

Delosperma uniflorum is also valued for its ability to prevent soil erosion in coastal and sandy areas.
