Domingoa hymenodes

Domingoa hymenodes, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds.

Domingoa hymenodes is often found in disturbed habitats and can be invasive in certain regions.
