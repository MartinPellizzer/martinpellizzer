Daiswa yunnanensis

Daiswa yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan daisies, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in meadows and rocky slopes.

Daiswa yunnanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
