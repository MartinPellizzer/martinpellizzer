Dimorpholepis Serrata

Dimorpholepis serrata is a unique flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America. It is known for its striking, fern-like leaves and delicate, fragrant blooms that appear in spring. This plant thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained soil to flourish. Its name refers to the two distinct forms it exhibits during different growth stages. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively rare and underappreciated species in the plant world.
