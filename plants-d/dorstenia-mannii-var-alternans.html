Dorstenia mannii var. alternans

Dorstenia mannii var. alternans, commonly known as the twisted leaf fig, is a unique member of the Araliaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly Tanzania and Kenya. The plant is characterized by its striking, spirally twisted leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
