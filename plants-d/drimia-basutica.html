Drimia basutica

Drimia basutica, commonly known as the Basutu lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features strap-shaped, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces striking white or pale purple flowers with a distinctive, sweet fragrance. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Drimia basutica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
