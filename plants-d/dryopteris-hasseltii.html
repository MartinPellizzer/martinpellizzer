Dryopteris hasseltii

Dryopteris hasseltii, commonly known as the Hassen's wood fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its dense, compact fronds that form a rounded, bushy crown. The fronds are typically dark green, with a leathery texture and a distinctive, finely divided structure. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas.

This fern is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
