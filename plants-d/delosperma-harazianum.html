Delosperma harazianum

Delosperma harazianum, commonly known as the Armenian ice plant, is a low-growing, succulent perennial native to the mountainous regions of Armenia.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant pink to purple flowers in late spring to early summer, adding ornamental value to gardens. It is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and minimal maintenance requirements.

Its ability to form dense mats makes it effective for erosion control in suitable climates.
