Dianthus oreadum

Dianthus oreadum, commonly known as the alpine pink, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, pink or white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, where it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves, and it is often found in alpine meadows and scree slopes.

Dianthus oreadum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its charming, small blooms.
