Dendrochilum ravanii

Dendrochilum ravanii, commonly known as the Ravan's tree orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its tall, erect growth habit and is typically found in forested areas. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are usually yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are broad and often have a waxy texture, which helps it retain moisture in its natural habitat.

Dendrochilum ravanii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
