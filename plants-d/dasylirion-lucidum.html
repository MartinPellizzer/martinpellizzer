Dasylirion lucidum

Dasylirion lucidum, commonly known as the shining dasylirion, is a perennial plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its rosette of long, narrow, blue-green leaves. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences that bear small, white to pale blue flowers. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
