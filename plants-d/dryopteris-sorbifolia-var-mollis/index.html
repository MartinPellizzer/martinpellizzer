Dryopteris Sorbifolia Var. Mollis

Soft shield fern, scientifically known as Dryopteris sorbifolia var. mollis, is a unique and delicate member of the fern family. Native to the temperate forests of eastern North America, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its lush, velvety fronds give it a distinctive appearance that sets it apart from other fern species. This plant is often found in woodland understories, where it contributes to the rich biodiversity of its habitat. With its elegant form and adaptability, the soft shield fern is both a fascinating subject for botanists and a valuable addition to natural ecosystems.
