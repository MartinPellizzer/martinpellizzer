Dendrobium usterii

Dendrobium usterii, commonly known as the Uster orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit, with cane-like stems that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink, often with a distinctive red or orange lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium usterii is prized for its ornamental value and is commonly cultivated in greenhouses and as a houseplant.
