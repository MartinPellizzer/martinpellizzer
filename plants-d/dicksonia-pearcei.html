Dicksonia pearcei

Dicksonia pearcei, commonly known as the Tasmanian tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Tasmania.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of large, feather-like fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant has a distinctive root system that anchors it firmly in the soil and helps absorb nutrients. Its fronds are bioluminescent, emitting a faint glow at night, which is a unique feature among ferns.

This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is an important component of Tasmania's native flora.
