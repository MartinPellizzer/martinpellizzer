Dendrobium misoanum

Dendrobium misoanum, commonly known as the Misoan orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, cane-like stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium misoanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
