Desmothamnus lucidus

Desmothamnus lucidus, commonly known as the silverleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing annual herb with erect, branching stems that are often covered in a silvery, powdery coating. The plant produces yellow composite flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped, with a rough texture and a distinct silver-gray coloration.

Desmothamnus lucidus is adapted to arid environments and is often found in sandy or rocky soils.
