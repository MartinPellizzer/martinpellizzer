Dicliptera crinita var. floribunda

Dicliptera crinita var. floribunda, commonly known as the hairy dicliptera, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, fibrous root system and its ability to thrive in moist, well-drained soils. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. Its leaves are typically narrow and hairy, giving it a distinctive appearance.
