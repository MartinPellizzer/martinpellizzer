Dortmanna succulenta

Dortmanna succulenta, commonly known as the stonecrop or ice plant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, white, or pink, often blooming in spring. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Dortmanna succulenta is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
