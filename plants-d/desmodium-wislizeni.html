Desmodium wislizeni

Desmodium wislizeni, commonly known as ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its dense clusters of small, violet to blue-purple flowers. Desmodium wislizeni is often used in restoration projects due to its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.
