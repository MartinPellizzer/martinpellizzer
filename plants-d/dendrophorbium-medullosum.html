Dendrophorbium medullosum

Dendrophorbium medullosum, commonly known as the medullosum bark plant, is a unique species characterized by its distinctive, thick, and fibrous bark.

It is native to specific regions in South America, where it thrives in humid, tropical climates. The plant's structure features a woody stem with a dense, spongy medullary layer that provides structural support. Its leaves are typically small and clustered, contributing to its overall compact appearance.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
