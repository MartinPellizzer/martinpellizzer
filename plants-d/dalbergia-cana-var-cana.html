Dalbergia cana var. cana

Dalbergia cana var. cana, commonly known as the Brazilian rosewood, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family Fabaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its dark, dense wood that is highly valued for musical instruments.
