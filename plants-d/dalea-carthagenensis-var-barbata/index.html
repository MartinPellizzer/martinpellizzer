Dalea Carthagenensis Var. Barbata

Dalea carthagenensis var. barbata is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the southwestern United States. It belongs to the legume family and is known for its distinctive pink to purple blooms. This variant is characterized by its unique barbed bracts that distinguish it from other members of the species. It thrives in arid and rocky environments, making it a symbol of resilience in harsh landscapes. Due to its limited range and habitat specificity, it is considered a species of conservation concern.
