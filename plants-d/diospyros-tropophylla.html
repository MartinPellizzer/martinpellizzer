Diospyros tropophylla

Diospyros tropophylla, commonly known as the African persimmon, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Ebenaceae family, which includes other species known for their hard wood and edible fruits. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, typically greenish-yellow, and it produces small, round fruits that are usually red or orange when mature.
