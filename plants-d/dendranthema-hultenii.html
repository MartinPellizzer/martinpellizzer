Dendranthema hultenii

Dendranthema hultenii, commonly known as Hulten's fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of numerous small florets. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its yellow to orange flowers bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Dendranthema hultenii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
