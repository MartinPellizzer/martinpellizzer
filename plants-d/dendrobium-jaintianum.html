Dendrobium jaintianum

Dendrobium jaintianum, commonly known as the Jaintia orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to the Himalayan regions of India and Nepal.

It is characterized by its tall, cane-like stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink, typically blooming during the summer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium jaintianum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
