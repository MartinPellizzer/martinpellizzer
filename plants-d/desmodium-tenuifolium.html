Desmodium tenuifolium

Desmodium tenuifolium, commonly known as slender-leaf desmodium, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and nitrogen-fixing capabilities. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. Its seeds are enclosed in pods that split open when mature, dispersing the seeds effectively.

Desmodium tenuifolium is often used in ecological restoration due to its ability to improve soil fertility.
