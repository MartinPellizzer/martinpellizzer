Daphne macrantha

Daphne macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered daphne, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has smooth, greyish bark and lance-shaped leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Daphne macrantha is also valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
