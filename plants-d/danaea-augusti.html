Danaea augusti

Danaea augusti, commonly known as the red-leaved danaea, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with distinctive red veins, which give it a vibrant and ornamental appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming during the wet season. Danaea augusti thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical plant collections.
