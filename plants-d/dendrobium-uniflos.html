Dendrobium uniflos

Dendrobium uniflos, commonly known as the single-flower orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its upright, cane-like pseudobulbs. The plant produces a single, showy flower at the tip of each stem, which is typically white or pale yellow with a distinctive red lip. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Dendrobium uniflos is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its elegant floral display.
