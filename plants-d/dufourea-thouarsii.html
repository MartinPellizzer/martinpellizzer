Dufourea thouarsii

Dufourea thouarsii, commonly known as the Tahitian honeybee, is a species of bee native to the island of Tahiti in French Polynesia.

It is a solitary bee that plays a crucial role in pollinating native flora in its habitat. The plant is not a plant but an animal, so it does not have botanical aspects in the traditional sense. However, its ecological interactions with local vegetation are significant for maintaining biodiversity.

This species is of interest to entomologists and conservationists due to its unique role in the ecosystem.
