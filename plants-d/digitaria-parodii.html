Digitaria parodii

Digitaria parodii, commonly known as Parodii grass, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, blade-like leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are typically green to reddish in color. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

Digitaria parodii is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
