Dianthus skopjensis

Dianthus skopjensis, commonly known as the Skopje carnation, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan region.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its showy, clustered pink or red flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, while its stems are upright and branching.

Dianthus skopjensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
