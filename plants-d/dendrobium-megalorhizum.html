Dendrobium megalorhizum

Dendrobium megalorhizum, commonly known as the giant root dendrobium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy roots that store water, allowing it to thrive in tropical and subtropical environments. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees and absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air. Its leaves are long and strap-like, and it produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange.

The botanical features of Dendrobium megalorhizum make it both a unique and valuable addition to horticultural collections.
