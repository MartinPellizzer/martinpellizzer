Dracontium grandispathum

Dracontium grandispathum, commonly known as the giant arrowleaf, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length, featuring a distinctive arrow-like base. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which is known for its unique inflorescence structures called spathes. Its foliage is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value, though it requires high humidity and indirect light to thrive.

Due to its size and specific care needs, it is considered a challenging plant for novice growers.
