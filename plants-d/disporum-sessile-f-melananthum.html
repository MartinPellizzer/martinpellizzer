Disporum sessile f. melananthum

Disporum sessile f. melananthum, commonly known as the black-flowered disporum, is a rare botanical variant of the disporum species.

It is characterized by its dark, almost black flowers, which contrast sharply with its green foliage. This fern-like plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. The plant has lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences.

It is of interest to botanists due to its unique coloration and limited distribution.
