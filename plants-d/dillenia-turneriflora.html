Dillenia turneriflora

Dillenia turneriflora, commonly known as the broad-leafed dillenia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers with red stamens, which are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Dillenia turneriflora is often found in moist, shaded areas of tropical forests.
