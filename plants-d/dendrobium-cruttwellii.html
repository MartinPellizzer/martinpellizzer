Dendrobium cruttwellii

Dendrobium cruttwellii, commonly known as the Cruttwell's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, cane-like stems that can reach several meters in height. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with a red or pink lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrobium cruttwellii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
