Diplazium laffanianum

Diplazium laffanianum, commonly known as the Laffan's shield fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its dense, creeping growth habit. Diplazium laffanianum is often found in tropical and subtropical forests, where it contributes to the undergrowth vegetation.
