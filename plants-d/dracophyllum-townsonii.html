Dracophyllum townsonii

Dracophyllum townsonii, commonly known as the narrow-leaved dracophyllum, is a shrub native to the cool, moist forests of Tasmania.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and other ericaceous plants. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are narrow and leathery, adapted to retain moisture in its native environment.

Dracophyllum townsonii plays an important ecological role in its habitat, providing shelter and food for various native fauna.
