Dendrobium lewisiae

Dendrobium lewisiae, commonly known as the Lewisi's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its compact, branching structure and produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in tropical and subtropical forests. Its leaves are usually fleshy and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water storage.

Dendrobium lewisiae prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
