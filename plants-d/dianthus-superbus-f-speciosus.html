Dianthus superbus f. speciosus

Dianthus superbus f. speciosus, commonly known as the showy campion, is a striking flowering plant characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of bright pink to red flowers.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is native to regions of Europe and western Asia. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and its flowers have five petals that are typically notched at the tips.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
