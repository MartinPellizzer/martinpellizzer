Dolichandra cynanchoides var. latifolia

Dolichandra cynanchoides var. latifolia, commonly known as broadleaf dolichandra, is a perennial shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features woody stems that are often covered in a dense layer of soft, grayish hairs. The leaves are typically large, broad, and heart-shaped, with a velvety texture. It produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by follicles containing seeds.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to well-drained, sunny environments.
