Danthonia Alpina

Danthonia alpina is a rare and hardy grass species native to high-altitude environments. It thrives in cold, nutrient-poor soils and is often found in alpine tundras and rocky slopes. This plant is known for its dense, mat-like growth habit and its ability to withstand harsh weather conditions. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a sensitive indicator of environmental change. Its unique adaptations make it an important subject for ecological and botanical research.
