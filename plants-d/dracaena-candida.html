Dracaena candida

Dracaena candida, commonly known as the white dracaena, is a striking evergreen plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It features tall, slender leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a distinctive white or silver stripe running down the center. The plant is known for its adaptability to various light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its slow growth rate and minimal care requirements make it an ideal houseplant.

Dracaena candida is also valued for its ability to purify indoor air, contributing to a healthier living environment.
