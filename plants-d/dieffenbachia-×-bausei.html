Dieffenbachia × bausei

Dieffenbachia × bausei, commonly known as the Bause's Dieffenbachia, is a hybrid plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant mix of green, white, and cream. The plant is a member of the Araceae family, which includes other popular houseplants like peace lilies. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to prevent root rot.

Its striking foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor decorating.
