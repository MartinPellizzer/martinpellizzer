Dieffenbachia brasiliensis

Dieffenbachia brasiliensis, commonly known as the Brasilian dieffenbachia, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with white or yellow markings. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other popular houseplants like peace lilies. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity to flourish.

Its striking foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
