Dactylorhiza × stenostachys

Dactylorhiza × stenostachys, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed orchid, is a hybrid orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and showy, spike-like flowers. The plant typically blooms in late summer, producing pale yellow to greenish-white flowers with a subtle fragrance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Dactylorhiza × stenostachys is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
