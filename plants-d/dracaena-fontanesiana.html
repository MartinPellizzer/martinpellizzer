Dracaena fontanesiana

Dracaena fontanesiana, commonly known as the Fontanesiana dracaena, is a species of flowering plant in the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to Madagascar and is characterized by its striking, sword-shaped leaves that are often variegated with yellow and white streaks. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically in shades of white or pale pink. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Dracaena fontanesiana is also valued for its air-purifying properties and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
