Drimia zebrinella

Drimia zebrinella, commonly known as the Zebra lily, is a bulbous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, strap-like leaves that are often marked with distinctive zebra-like patterns, giving it its common name. The plant produces tall, slender stems that bear clusters of white or pale pink, bell-shaped flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Drimia zebrinella is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
