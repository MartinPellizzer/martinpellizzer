Dichapetalum gelonioides subsp. sumatranum

Dichapetalum gelonioides subsp.

sumatranum, commonly known as the Sumatran flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its large, showy, red-orange flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a striking appearance.

The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are typically ovate to lanceolate in shape. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
