Dioscorea hederacea

Dioscorea hederacea, commonly known as creeping Dioscorea, is a climbing vine belonging to the family Dioscoreaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, Africa, and the Pacific Islands. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into tuberous roots. Its tubers are often used in traditional medicine and as a food source in some cultures.

The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires a warm, humid climate for optimal growth.
