Delphinium decorum var. scaposum

Delphinium decorum var. scaposum, commonly known as the tall delphinium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features tall, slender stems that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height, supporting large, showy blue to violet flowers in late summer. The plant has palmately divided, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden borders and cottage gardens.

Due to its ornamental value and relatively hardy nature, it is often cultivated for its striking floral display.
