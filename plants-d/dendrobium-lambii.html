Dendrobium lambii

Dendrobium lambii, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with multiple stems that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium lambii is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
