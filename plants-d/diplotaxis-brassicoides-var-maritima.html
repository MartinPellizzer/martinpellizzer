Diplotaxis brassicoides var. maritima

Diplotaxis brassicoides var. maritima, commonly known as sea rocket, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of southern Europe and North Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that thrives in salty, well-drained soils along the shoreline. The plant features basal rosettes of narrow, fleshy leaves and produces bright yellow flowers in early spring. Its flowers are typically arranged in clusters and are visited by various pollinators.

The species is adapted to harsh coastal environments, showing resistance to salt spray and strong winds.
