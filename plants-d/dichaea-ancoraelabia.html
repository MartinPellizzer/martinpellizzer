Dichaea ancoraelabia

Dichaea ancoraelabia, commonly known as the anchored lip orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit, with roots that anchor it to tree bark or other substrates. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically greenish-white with a distinctive lip. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, adapted to conserve moisture in its humid, shaded habitat.

Dichaea ancoraelabia is valued for its unique appearance and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
