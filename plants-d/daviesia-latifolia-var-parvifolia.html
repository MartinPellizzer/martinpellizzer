Daviesia latifolia var. parvifolia

Daviesia latifolia var. parvifolia, commonly known as the small-flowered grey-leafed Daviesia, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, greyish-green leaves and small, pale yellow to cream flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to Mediterranean climates. It is part of the legume family, Fabaceae, and has a symbiotic relationship with nitrogen-fixing bacteria.

This species is often found in heathlands and open woodlands, where it contributes to the local biodiversity.
