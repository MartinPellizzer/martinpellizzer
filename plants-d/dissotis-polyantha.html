Dissotis polyantha

Dissotis polyantha, commonly known as the pink flame or pink mistflower, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking, showy inflorescences composed of numerous small, pink to red flowers. The plant typically grows in wet, shaded environments such as marshes and along stream banks. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a waxy texture.

The flowers bloom in clusters, creating a dense, fluffy appearance that resembles a mist or cloud.
