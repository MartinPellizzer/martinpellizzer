Dendrobium speciosum subsp. capricornicum

Dendrobium speciosum subsp.

capricornicum, commonly known as the Capricorn orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia. It features a sympodial growth habit with cane-like pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often with a distinctive red or orange lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
