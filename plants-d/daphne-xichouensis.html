Daphne xichouensis

Daphne xichouensis, commonly known as the Xichou daphne, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has glossy, ovate leaves that are dark green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or woodland settings.

Due to its ornamental value and rarity, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
