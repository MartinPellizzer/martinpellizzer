Dianella hemichrysa

Dianella hemichrysa, commonly known as the silver-leaf flax lily, is a striking evergreen perennial native to New Zealand.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are silver-gray in color, providing a distinctive visual contrast. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped blue or purple flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Dianella hemichrysa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native pollinators.
