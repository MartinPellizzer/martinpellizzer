Drypetes comorensis

Drypetes comorensis, commonly known as the Comorian flame tree, is a flowering plant native to the Comoros Islands.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that typically grows up to 10 meters in height. The plant produces bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly waxy texture.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
