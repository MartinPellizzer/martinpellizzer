Dendrobium schrautii

Dendrobium schrautii, commonly known as the Schraut's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically has long, narrow leaves and relies on tree bark for support rather than soil. It thrives in humid, tropical environments with bright, indirect light.

Dendrobium schrautii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its delicate floral display.
