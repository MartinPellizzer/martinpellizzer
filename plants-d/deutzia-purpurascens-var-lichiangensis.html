Deutzia purpurascens var. lichiangensis

Deutzia purpurascens var. lichiangensis, commonly known as the Lichiang deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early summer, adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant has slender, arching branches with elliptical leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its compact growth and seasonal floral display.
