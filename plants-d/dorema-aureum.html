Dorema aureum

Dorema aureum, commonly known as golden bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Dorema aureum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and alpine settings.
