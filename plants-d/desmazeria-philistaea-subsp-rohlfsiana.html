Desmazeria philistaea subsp. rohlfsiana

Desmazeria philistaea subsp.

rohlfsiana, commonly known as the Palestine lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking blue to violet flowers during the spring season.

The plant is typically found in rocky or gravelly soils, thriving in well-drained environments. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often used in rock gardens due to its ornamental value.

The subspecies rohlfsiana is distinguished by its slightly different floral morphology compared to the main species.
