Dracocephalum nodulosum

Dracocephalum nodulosum, commonly known as nodular blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant features square, hairy stems and opposite leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. Its small, blue to purple flowers are clustered in whorls along the stem, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is valued for its medicinal properties.
