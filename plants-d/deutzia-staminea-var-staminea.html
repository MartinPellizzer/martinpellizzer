Deutzia staminea var. staminea

Deutzia staminea var. staminea, commonly known as the common deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features showy, clusters of white or pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has slender, arching branches with small, elliptical leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Deutzia staminea var. staminea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
