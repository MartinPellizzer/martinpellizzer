Dracocephalum fragile

Dracocephalum fragile, commonly known as slender skullcap, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue to purple in color, arranged in whorled clusters. Dracocephalum fragile is known for its medicinal properties, with various parts of the plant used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
