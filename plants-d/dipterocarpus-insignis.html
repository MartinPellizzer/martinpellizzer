Dipterocarpus insignis

Dipterocarpus insignis, commonly known as the Malayan ironwood, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves and produces heavy, woody fruits that contain seeds. The plant belongs to the Dipterocarpaceae family, which includes many valuable timber species. It typically grows in lowland rainforests and is known for its strong, durable wood.

The species plays an important ecological role in its native habitats.
