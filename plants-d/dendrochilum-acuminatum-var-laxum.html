Dendrochilum acuminatum var. laxum

Dendrochilum acuminatum var. laxum, commonly known as the slender-stemmed orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features elongated, wiry stems that can grow up to several meters in length, often with a few nodes. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, typically clustered in racemes. Its leaves are linear and arranged spirally along the stem, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm, humid environments.
