Dendrobium parvum

Dendrobium parvum, commonly known as the small orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact growth habit with a short, horizontal rhizome and a few narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically blooming in late spring or early summer. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrobium parvum is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
