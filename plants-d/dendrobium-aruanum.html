Dendrobium aruanum

Dendrobium aruanum, commonly known as the Aruanum orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with numerous horizontal or ascending pseudobulbs. The plant produces inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

Dendrobium aruanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
