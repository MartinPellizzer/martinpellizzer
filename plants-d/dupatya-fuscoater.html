Dupatya fuscoater

Dupatya fuscoater, commonly known as the black-veined orchid, is a tropical orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its dark green leaves and striking dark purple to black veins. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dupatya fuscoater is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
