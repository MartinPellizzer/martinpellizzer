Deutzia × elegantissima f. fasciculata

Deutzia × elegantissima f. fasciculata, commonly known as the compact deutzia, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, white to pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early spring, typically in clusters. The plant has narrow, elongated leaves that are arranged in whorls around the stems. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade to full sun.

This cultivar is often used in gardens for its compact size and attractive floral display.
