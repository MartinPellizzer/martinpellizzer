Dipterocarpus kutaianus

Dipterocarpus kutaianus, commonly known as the Kutaian dipterocarp, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in Borneo.

It belongs to the family Dipterocarpaceae and is characterized by its large, spreading canopy and buttressed roots. The tree produces heavy, woody fruits that contain winged seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Its bark and wood are valued for their durability and are used in traditional construction and furniture-making.

The species plays an important ecological role in rainforest ecosystems, providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
