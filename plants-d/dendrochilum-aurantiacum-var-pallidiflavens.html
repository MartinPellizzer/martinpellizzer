Dendrochilum aurantiacum var. pallidiflavens

Dendrochilum aurantiacum var. pallidiflavens, commonly known as the pale-yellow orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a sympodial growth habit, producing pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowering structure. The plant features lanceolate leaves that are often covered in a dense, white or pale pubescence. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange-yellow, with a distinctive lip that is often pale or white in color.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and is prized for its ornamental value in horticulture.
