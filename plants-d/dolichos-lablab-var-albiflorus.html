Dolichos lablab var. albiflorus

Dolichos lablab var. albiflorus, commonly known as white-flowered lablab, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and pinnately compound leaves. The plant produces clusters of white to pale yellow flowers, which are often followed by elongated, twisted pods containing edible seeds. It is well adapted to warm climates and can thrive in poor soils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and nutritional value.
