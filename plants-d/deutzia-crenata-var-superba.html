Deutzia crenata var. superba

Deutzia crenata var. superba, commonly known as the Chinese deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features arching branches adorned with clusters of white or pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has ovate to elliptical leaves that are typically serrated along the edges. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value comes from its showy flowers and attractive foliage, which provide seasonal interest throughout the growing season.
