Dendrobium maierae

Dendrobium maierae, commonly known as the Maier's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome that produces multiple shoots and pseudobulbs. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a distinctive red lip. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, typically arranged in a rosette pattern.

Dendrobium maierae thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
