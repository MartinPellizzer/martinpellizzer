Diascia diffusa

Diascia diffusa, commonly known as the common diascia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are borne in clusters at the ends of the stems, and it thrives in full sun to partial shade.

Diascia diffusa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a bedding plant.
