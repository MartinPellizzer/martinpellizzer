Dillenia grossulariifolia

Dillenia grossulariifolia, commonly known as the Chinese dillenia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, yellowish-white flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences. Its fruits are fleshy, berry-like structures that contain numerous seeds.

Dillenia grossulariifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
