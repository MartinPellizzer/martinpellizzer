Disa × vogelpoelii

Disa × vogelpoelii, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a hybrid orchid native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers that range from red to pink and white. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy, spike-like inflorescences.

Disa × vogelpoelii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
