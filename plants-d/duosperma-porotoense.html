Duosperma porotoense

Duosperma porotoense, commonly known as the Porotoense Bursera, is a shrub native to Central America.

It belongs to the Burseraceae family, which includes species known for their aromatic resins. The plant typically grows in dry, tropical environments and features small, leathery leaves. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, and it produces small, round fruits that contain smooth, black seeds.

Due to its habitat and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
