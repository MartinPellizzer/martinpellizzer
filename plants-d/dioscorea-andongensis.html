Dioscorea andongensis

Dioscorea andongensis, commonly known as the Andong yam, is a species of wild yam native to China.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its climbing habit and tuberous roots. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers and has compound leaves with three to five leaflets. Its tubers are used in traditional medicine and have been studied for their potential pharmacological properties.

The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.
