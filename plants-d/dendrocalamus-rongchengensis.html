Dendrocalamus rongchengensis

Dendrocalamus rongchengensis, commonly known as the Rongcheng bamboo, is a species of giant bamboo native to southern China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can reach heights of up to 20 meters. The plant has a robust root system that helps it thrive in moist, well-drained soils. Its leaves are long and narrow, with a smooth, waxy surface.

This bamboo is valued for its strong and durable wood, making it useful in construction and handicrafts.
