Drepanostemma luteum

Drepanostemma luteum, commonly known as the yellow star grass, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, erect stems that are often hairy and can grow up to 30 centimeters in height. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters at the tips of the stems, which bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a slightly rough texture.

Drepanostemma luteum is often found in open grasslands and along roadsides, thriving in well-drained soils.
