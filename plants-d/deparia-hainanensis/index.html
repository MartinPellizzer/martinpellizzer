Deparia Hainanensis

Deparia hainanensis is a rare fern species native to the tropical regions of China. It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is known for its distinctive fronds and ecological significance. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in mountainous forests. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts. Its unique characteristics make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
