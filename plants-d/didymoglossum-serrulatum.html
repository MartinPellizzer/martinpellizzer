Didymoglossum serrulatum

Didymoglossum serrulatum, commonly known as the serrated feather moss, is a small, creeping moss that thrives in moist, shaded environments.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like leaves that are often serrated along the edges. This plant is typically found in forest floors, rock crevices, and along stream banks in temperate regions. Its growth habit forms dense mats, contributing to soil stabilization and moisture retention.

Didymoglossum serrulatum plays a vital role in maintaining ecological balance in its habitat.
