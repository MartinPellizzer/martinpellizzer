Dendrobium barbatulum

Dendrobium barbatulum, commonly known as the beard lip orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with multiple pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a distinctive beard-like structure on the lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

Dendrobium barbatulum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
