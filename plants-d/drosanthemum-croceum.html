Drosanthemum croceum

Drosanthemum croceum, commonly known as the orange-flowered ice plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, ribbed stems and leaves that are typically green or bluish-gray, adapted to store water. The plant produces vibrant orange or yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
