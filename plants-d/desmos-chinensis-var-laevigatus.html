Desmos chinensis var. laevigatus

Desmos chinensis var. laevigatus, commonly known as the smooth-leaved desmos, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a climbing shrub characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are smooth and glabrous. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, followed by capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some regions for horticultural purposes.
