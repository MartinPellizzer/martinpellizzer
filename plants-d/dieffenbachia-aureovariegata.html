Dieffenbachia aureovariegata

Dieffenbachia aureovariegata, commonly known as the Golden Leopard Plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are predominantly green with striking yellow and white variegation. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions. Its foliage is often used in interior decorating due to its ornamental value.

It requires moderate watering and well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
