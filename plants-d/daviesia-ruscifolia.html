Daviesia ruscifolia

Daviesia ruscifolia, commonly known as the golden wattle, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Daviesia ruscifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays an important ecological role in its native habitat.
