Dracaena placida

Dracaena placida, commonly known as the laceleaf dracaena, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Dracaena placida is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
