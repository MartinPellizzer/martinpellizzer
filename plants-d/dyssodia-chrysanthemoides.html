Dyssodia chrysanthemoides

Dyssodia chrysanthemoides, commonly known as the yellow daisy or golden daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other similar plants. The plant features showy yellow flowers with a central disk, typical of the family's composite flower structure. It is often found in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in full sun.
