Digitaria planiculmis

Digitaria planiculmis, commonly known as flat-leaf digitaria, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its flat, narrow leaves and compact growth habit. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences, typical of the Poaceae family. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in open habitats such as pastures and roadsides.

Digitaria planiculmis is an important forage plant for livestock in certain regions.
