Daphniphyllum angustifolium

Daphniphyllum angustifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaved holly, is a species of evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped, glossy dark green leaves and small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant produces berries that range in color from red to black, depending on the variety. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Daphniphyllum angustifolium is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and winter interest.
