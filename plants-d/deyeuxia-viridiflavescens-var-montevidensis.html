Deyeuxia viridiflavescens var. montevidensis

Deyeuxia viridiflavescens var. montevidensis, commonly known as the Montevideo grass, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

Its foliage is usually a mix of green and yellowish tones, giving it a distinctive appearance.
