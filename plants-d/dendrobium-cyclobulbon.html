Dendrobium cyclobulbon

Dendrobium cyclobulbon, commonly known as the golden spike orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant that grows on trees and rocks, often in humid tropical environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with multiple pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, with a distinctive spurred lip that aids in pollination.

The botanical characteristics of Dendrobium cyclobulbon make it a popular choice for both horticultural and conservation purposes.
