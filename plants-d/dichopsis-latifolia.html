Dichopsis latifolia

Dichopsis latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf dichopsis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are usually gray-green with a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss.

Dichopsis latifolia is popular in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
