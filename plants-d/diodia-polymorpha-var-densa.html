Diodia polymorpha var. densa

Diodia polymorpha var. densa, commonly known as sticky nightshade, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Africa and the Americas.

It features hairy, glandular leaves that produce a sticky substance, which can deter herbivores. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale blue, blooming in late summer. Its fruit is a capsule that contains numerous tiny seeds.

It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
