Diervilla japonica var. hortensis

Diervilla japonica var. hortensis, commonly known as Japanese honeysuckle bush, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, elliptical leaves that turn reddish in the fall. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
