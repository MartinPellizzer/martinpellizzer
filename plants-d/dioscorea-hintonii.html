Dioscorea hintonii

Dioscorea hintonii, commonly known as Hinton's yam, is a species of flowering plant in the family Dioscoreaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Asia and is characterized by its climbing habit and tuberous roots. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically borne in clusters. Its tubers are used in traditional medicine and as a food source in some cultures.

The plant's botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and agricultural contexts.
