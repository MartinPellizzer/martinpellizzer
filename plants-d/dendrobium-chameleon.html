Dendrobium chameleon

Dendrobium chameleon, commonly known as the chameleon orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique ability to change color, often resembling the vibrant hues of a chameleon. The plant has long, slender pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in its natural habitat. Its flowers are typically brightly colored and can last for several weeks, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.

Dendrobium chameleon requires bright, indirect light and well-draining potting media to flourish.
