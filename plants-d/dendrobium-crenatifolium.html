Dendrobium crenatifolium

Dendrobium crenatifolium, commonly known as the serrated-leaf dendrobium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant that grows on trees and rocks, typically in humid tropical environments. The plant features long, cane-like stems with a distinctive, serrated leaf margin. Its flowers are usually yellow or greenish-yellow, with a fragrant scent, and bloom during the rainy season.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
