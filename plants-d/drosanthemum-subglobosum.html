Drosanthemum subglobosum

Drosanthemum subglobosum, commonly known as the globular rock rose, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a compact, rounded stem with dense clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that help it store water, adapting it to arid environments. It blooms in spring and summer, producing vibrant flowers that attract pollinators.

Drosanthemum subglobosum is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental value.
