Dichaea chasei

Dichaea chasei, commonly known as the Chase's dichaea, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in clusters. The plant has a short, creeping rhizome with a few leaves and produces showy, pale yellow or greenish-white blossoms. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in the understory of rainforests.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
