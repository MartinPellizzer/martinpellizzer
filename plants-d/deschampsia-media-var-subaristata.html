Deschampsia media var. subaristata

Deschampsia media var. subaristata, commonly known as the slender wheatgrass, is a perennial grass species native to North America.

It typically grows in open, sunny areas such as meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often blue-green in color. Its inflorescence consists of a dense, spike-like cluster of small, spikelet-bearing branches.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in ecological restoration projects.
