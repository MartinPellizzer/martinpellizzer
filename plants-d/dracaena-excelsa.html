Dracaena excelsa

Dracaena excelsa, commonly known as the giant dracaena, is a tall, evergreen shrub or small tree native to Madagascar.

It features long, narrow, dark green leaves that can grow up to 2 meters in length. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by red, fleshy fruits containing black seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Dracaena excelsa is also valued for its ability to purify indoor air.
