Diaphananthe eggelingii

Diaphananthe eggelingii, commonly known as the African orchid.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to southern Africa. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, fragrant flowers. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Diaphananthe eggelingii thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
