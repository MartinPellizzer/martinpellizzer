Doritis latifolia

Doritis latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved doritis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant features broad, strap-like leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Doritis latifolia is known for its resilience and ability to bloom reliably in suitable conditions.
