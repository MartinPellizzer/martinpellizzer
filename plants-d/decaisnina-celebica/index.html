Decaisnina Celebica

Decaisnina celebica is a rare and unique plant native to the Indonesian island of Sulawesi. It belongs to the Araceae family, which includes plants like philodendrons and peace lilies. This species is known for its striking, colorful inflorescence that resembles a large, decorative umbrella. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts. Its beauty and ecological importance make it a subject of interest for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike.
