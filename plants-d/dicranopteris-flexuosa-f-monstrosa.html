Dicranopteris flexuosa f. monstrosa

Dicranopteris flexuosa f. monstrosa, commonly known as the giant fern or monstrosa fern, is a variant of the flexible water fern.

It is characterized by its large, leathery fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant exhibits a distinctive, irregular growth pattern with twisted and curled fronds, giving it a striking, almost sculptural appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its unique morphology makes it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture and botanical collections.
