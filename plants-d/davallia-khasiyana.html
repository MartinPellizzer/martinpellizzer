Davallia khasiyana

Davallia khasiyana, commonly known as the Khasi rock fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to the eastern Himalayas.

It belongs to the family Davalliaceae and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and delicate, pinnate fronds. The plant typically thrives in moist, shaded environments, often growing on rock surfaces or in forest understories. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a leathery texture, with small, rounded pinnae.

Davallia khasiyana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant due to its adaptability and aesthetic qualities.
