Dinemagonum bridgesianum

Dinemagonum bridgesianum, commonly known as the Bridgeman's starfruit, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped fruits. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with glossy, dark green leaves. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, often displaying vibrant colors such as red or purple.
