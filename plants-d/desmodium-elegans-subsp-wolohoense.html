Desmodium elegans subsp. wolohoense

Desmodium elegans subsp.

wolohoense, commonly known as the Woloho velvet bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical Africa. It features trifoliate leaves with hairy surfaces and small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple.

The plant produces cylindrical pods containing several elliptical seeds. It is valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability and use in traditional agriculture.

Its ornamental value also makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
