Diplopterygium chinensis

Diplopterygium chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese heartleaf, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, clustered flowers are typically pink or purple, blooming in late spring to early summer.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.
