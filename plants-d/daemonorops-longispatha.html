Daemonorops longispatha

Daemonorops longispatha, commonly known as the long-spathe palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. Its inflorescences are complex and consist of numerous small, fragrant flowers.

This palm is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
