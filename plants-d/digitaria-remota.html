Digitaria remota

Digitaria remota, commonly known as slender bluestem, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as roadsides, fields, and prairies. The plant has slender, erect stems that are often hairless or slightly hairy. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green color.

It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny florets, typical of the grass family.
