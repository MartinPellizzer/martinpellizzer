Drepanostachyum mianningense

Drepanostachyum mianningense, commonly known as the Mianning bamboo, is a rare and endemic grass species native to Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its slender, hollow stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in mountainous regions with high humidity. Its flowering stems are usually short and bear small, spike-like inflorescences.
