Deltocheilos tenuitubum

Deltocheilos tenuitubum, commonly known as the slender-tubed orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped leaves and slender, tubular flower spikes. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories. Its flowers are small and fragrant, often displaying a combination of white and green hues.

Deltocheilos tenuitubum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
