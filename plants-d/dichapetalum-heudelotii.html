Dichapetalum heudelotii

Dichapetalum heudelotii, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a striking visual impact. The plant has compound leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for ornamental planting.

The tree is also valued for its rapid growth and ability to provide shade in landscaped areas.
