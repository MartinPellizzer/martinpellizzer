Dendrobium × martinii

Dendrobium × martinii, commonly known as the orchid of the orchid family, is a hybrid orchid that combines traits from several species.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic, typically in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant produces long, cane-like stems that can store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in varying environmental conditions. Its flowers are often showy, with a range of colors including white, yellow, and pink, and they bloom in clusters.

Dendrobium × martinii is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and adaptability to different growing conditions.
