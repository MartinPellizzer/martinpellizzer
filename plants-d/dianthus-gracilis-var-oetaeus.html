Dianthus gracilis var. oetaeus

Dianthus gracilis var. oetaeus, commonly known as the slender carnation, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit and produces clusters of pink to red, bilaterally symmetric flowers. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in well-drained soil in full sun. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.

This variety is closely related to other members of the Caryophyllaceae family, sharing similar botanical traits.
