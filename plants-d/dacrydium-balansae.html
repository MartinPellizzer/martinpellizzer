Dacrydium balansae

Dacrydium balansae, commonly known as the New Caledonian pine, is a rare and endemic conifer native to the island of New Caledonia.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows in humid, mountainous regions with acidic soils. The plant has needle-like leaves that are arranged in spirals around the stems, and it produces small, cone-like structures that contain its seeds. It plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, contributing to the unique biodiversity of the region.
