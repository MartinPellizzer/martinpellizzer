Deyeuxia exilis

Deyeuxia exilis, commonly known as slender false rice, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in wetland and marshy environments, often forming dense vegetation.

Deyeuxia exilis is an important component of wetland ecosystems, providing habitat for various wildlife species.
