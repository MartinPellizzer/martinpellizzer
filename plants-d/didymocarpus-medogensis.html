Didymocarpus medogensis

Didymocarpus medogensis, commonly known as the Yunnan sand pear, is a rare and endemic species native to the Yunnan Province in China.

The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically covered in a fine, fuzzy coating. It thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil and is often found in mountainous regions.
