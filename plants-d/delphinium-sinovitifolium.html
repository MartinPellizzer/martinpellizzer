Delphinium sinovitifolium

Delphinium sinovitifolium, commonly known as the Chinese larkspur, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems and showy blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and meadows. Its leaves are compound, with lance-shaped leaflets that are often covered in fine hairs.

Delphinium sinovitifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
