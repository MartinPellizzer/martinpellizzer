Draba involucrata

Draba involucrata, commonly known as the alpine winter cress, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. This species is characterized by its small, delicate flowers and basal leaves that form a rosette. Draba involucrata is often found in alpine and arctic environments, where it thrives in rocky and well-drained soils.
