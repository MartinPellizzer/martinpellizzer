Dracocephalum alpinum

Dracocephalum alpinum, commonly known as alpine dragonhead, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square, hairy stems and small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. It thrives in rocky and well-drained soils, often found in alpine and subalpine habitats.

Dracocephalum alpinum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
