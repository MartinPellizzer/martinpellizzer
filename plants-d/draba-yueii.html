Draba yueii

Draba yueii, commonly known as the Yueii whitlow grass, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of western China.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other flowering plants known for their cruciform flowers. The plant typically features a basal rosette of leaves and produces small, yellow flowers during the spring season. Draba yueii is adapted to harsh alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils and full sunlight.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important component of high-altitude ecosystems.
