Dolichos mitis

Dolichos mitis, commonly known as the slender cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and trifoliate leaves, with each leaflet being ovate to elliptic in shape. The flowers are typically yellow or orange, and the plant produces elongated pods containing small, edible seeds. Dolichos mitis is valued for its nutritional content, including high levels of protein and essential amino acids.

It is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
