Dyckia braunii

Dyckia braunii, commonly known as the Braun's dyckia, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Dyckia braunii produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically arranged in spikes.
