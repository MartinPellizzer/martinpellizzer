Disperis uzungwae

Disperis uzungwae, commonly known as the Uzungwa orchid, is a rare and endemic species found in the Uzungwa Mountains of Tanzania.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive purple lip. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest habitats at high altitudes. It has a sympodial growth habit, with rhizomes that produce a few leaves and flowering stems.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
