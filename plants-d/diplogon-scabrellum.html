Diplogon scabrellum

Diplogon scabrellum, commonly known as the roughleaf diplogon, is a unique plant species characterized by its distinctive foliage and growth habits.

It typically features rough, textured leaves that provide a tactile contrast to its overall appearance. The plant is native to specific regions with particular soil and climate conditions that support its growth. Its botanical structure includes a robust root system and branching stems that help it thrive in its natural habitat.

Due to its specialized requirements, it is often found in isolated or less disturbed environments.
