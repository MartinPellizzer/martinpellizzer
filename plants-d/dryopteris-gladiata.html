Dryopteris gladiata

Dryopteris gladiata, commonly known as the sword fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to eastern North America.

It features fronds that are typically sword-shaped, with a distinctive, upright growth habit. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas. Its fronds are divided into pinnae, which are linear and gradually tapering toward the tips.

Dryopteris gladiata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly used in landscaping and as a houseplant.
