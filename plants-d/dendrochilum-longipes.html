Dendrochilum longipes

Dendrochilum longipes, commonly known as the long-stemmed tree orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems and numerous pendulous flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in tropical rainforests, often as an epiphyte on trees or rocks. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with purple or brown markings, and it produces small, round pseudobulbs.

Dendrochilum longipes is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
