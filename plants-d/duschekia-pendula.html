Duschekia pendula

Duschekia pendula, commonly known as the hanging duschekia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that hang from the stems. The plant produces yellowish-orange flowers with a distinctive, hairy appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Duschekia pendula is often cultivated for its ornamental value and easy care requirements.
