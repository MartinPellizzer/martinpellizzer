Dracontium regelianum

Dracontium regelianum, commonly known as the giant arrowroot, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix and spathe. The plant features large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to several feet in length, giving it its common name. Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant foliage and dramatic floral structure.

It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
