Dendrobium frutex

Dendrobium frutex, commonly known as the shrub orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, shrub-like growth habit and often produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically has fleshy, green leaves that help it store water in its natural habitat. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Dendrobium frutex is valued for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
