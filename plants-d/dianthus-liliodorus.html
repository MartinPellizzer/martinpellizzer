Dianthus liliodorus

Dianthus liliodorus, commonly known as the pink campion, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its showy, pink or purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain seeds.

Dianthus liliodorus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
