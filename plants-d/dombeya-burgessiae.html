Dombeya burgessiae

Dombeya burgessiae, commonly known as the Burgess dombeya, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It features showy, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and a rough, hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a specimen plant.

Dombeya burgessiae is valued for its ornamental beauty and relatively low maintenance requirements.
