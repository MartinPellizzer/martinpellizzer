Dryopteris yungensis

Dryopteris yungensis, commonly known as Yungensis Shield Fern, is a species of fern native to the Andean region of South America.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its distinctive fronds with a leathery texture. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky slopes. Its spores are produced on the underside of the fronds, which are clustered in sori.

This fern is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
