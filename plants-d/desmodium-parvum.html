Desmodium parvum

Desmodium parvum, commonly known as small cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant produces small, elliptical legumes that contain edible seeds. It thrives in warm climates and is often found in disturbed soils.

Desmodium parvum is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil fertility.
