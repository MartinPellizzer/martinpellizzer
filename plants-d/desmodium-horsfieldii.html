Desmodium horsfieldii

Desmodium horsfieldii, commonly known as the silverleaf desmodium, is a perennial legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or disturbed sites.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it valuable for soil enrichment and as a forage plant.
