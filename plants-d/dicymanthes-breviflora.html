Dicymanthes breviflora

Dicymanthes breviflora, commonly known as the short-flowered star lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically blooms during the late spring and early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and containers.

Dicymanthes breviflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
