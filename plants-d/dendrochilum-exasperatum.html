Dendrochilum exasperatum

Dendrochilum exasperatum, commonly known as the angry tree orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and linear, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting it to its often dry habitat. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically in clusters at the tips of the stems. It thrives in tropical and subtropical regions with well-drained soil and partial shade.

Dendrochilum exasperatum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
