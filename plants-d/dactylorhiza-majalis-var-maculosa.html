Dactylorhiza majalis var. maculosa

Dactylorhiza majalis var. maculosa, commonly known as the spotted marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in wet, calcareous habitats such as marshes and damp woodlands. The plant has a slender, unbranched stem that bears a few oval, dark green leaves. Its flowers are pale purple to pink, with a distinctive white or yellow blotch on the lip.

The species is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in association with other orchid species in moist environments.
