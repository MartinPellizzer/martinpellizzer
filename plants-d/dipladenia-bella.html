Dipladenia bella

Dipladenia bella, commonly known as the flame vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to South America.

It features vibrant orange to red tubular flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves and produces fragrant, nectar-rich blossoms that attract hummingbirds and butterflies. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for trellises and containers.

Dipladenia bella is relatively easy to care for, requiring regular watering and pruning to maintain its shape and promote flowering.
