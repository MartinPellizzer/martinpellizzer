Dendrochilum exile

Dendrochilum exile, commonly known as the slender tree orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in forest understories, thriving in humid and shaded environments. The plant features a slender, erect stem with several elongated, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and often have a faintly colored lip, which is a characteristic feature of the Dendrochilum genus.

This orchid is valued for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
