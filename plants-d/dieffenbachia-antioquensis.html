Dieffenbachia antioquensis

Dieffenbachia antioquensis, commonly known as the Antioquian dieffenbachia, is a tropical plant native to Colombia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with shades of green, white, and yellow. The plant features a thick, upright stem with large, heart-shaped leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular as a houseplant but should be handled with care as it is toxic to humans and pets.
