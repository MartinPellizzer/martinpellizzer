Dendrochilum joclemensii

Dendrochilum joclemensii, commonly known as the Joclemens' orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that often display vibrant colors. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in nutrient-poor conditions.

Dendrochilum joclemensii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
