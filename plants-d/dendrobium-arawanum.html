Dendrobium arawanum

Dendrobium arawanum, commonly known as the Arawan orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome and multiple pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with a distinctive red or orange lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrobium arawanum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is commonly cultivated in tropical gardens.
