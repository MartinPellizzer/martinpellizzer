Daun-contu

Daun-contu, commonly known as *Piper betle*, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant is often cultivated for its aromatic leaves, which are used in traditional medicine and as a stimulant. Its leaves contain compounds like piperic acid and eugenol, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Daun-contu thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
