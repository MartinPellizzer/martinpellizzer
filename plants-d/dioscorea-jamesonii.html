Dioscorea jamesonii

Dioscorea jamesonii, commonly known as Chinese yam, is a perennial vine belonging to the family Dioscoreaceae.

It is native to regions of East Asia, including China, Korea, and Japan, and thrives in temperate climates. The plant produces tubers that are rich in starch and have been traditionally used in herbal medicine for their purported health benefits. Its leaves are heart-shaped and its flowers are typically yellow or greenish, with a distinctive structure.

The tubers of Dioscorea jamesonii are often cultivated for culinary and medicinal purposes.
