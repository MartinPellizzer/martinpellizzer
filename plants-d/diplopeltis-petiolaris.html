Diplopeltis petiolaris

Diplopeltis petiolaris, commonly known as the spiral leaf fern, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of fronds.

The plant features elongated, narrow leaves that twist around the central stem, creating a visually appealing and distinctive appearance. It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to regions of Southeast Asia. The fronds are typically green and glossy, with a leathery texture that helps it thrive in humid environments.

This fern prefers indirect light and consistent moisture, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
