Dipholis sintenisiana

Dipholis sintenisiana, commonly known as the Sintenis's orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of other plants without deriving nutrients from them. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is often sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
