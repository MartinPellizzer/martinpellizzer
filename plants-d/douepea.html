Douepea

Douepea, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its thick, velvety leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows as a small shrub, often with a compact, bushy appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its popularity as a houseplant is due to its vibrant blooms and ease of care.
