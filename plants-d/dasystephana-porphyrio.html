Dasystephana porphyrio

The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that are typically visited by native bees and other pollinators. Its berries, which are usually red or orange, are an important food source for birds and small mammals.

Due to its specialized habitat and limited distribution, Dasystephana porphyrio is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
