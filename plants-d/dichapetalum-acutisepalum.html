Dichapetalum acutisepalum

Dichapetalum acutisepalum, commonly known as the African star orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to southern Africa.

It is a terrestrial herb that typically grows in moist, shaded forest understories. The plant features a slender, unbranched stem with a single, showy, white or pale yellow flower that has a distinctive star-like appearance. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a spiral around the stem.

The species is notable for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique floral morphology.
