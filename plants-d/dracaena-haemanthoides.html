Dracaena haemanthoides

Dracaena haemanthoides, commonly known as the red dracaena, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

Dracaena haemanthoides is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
