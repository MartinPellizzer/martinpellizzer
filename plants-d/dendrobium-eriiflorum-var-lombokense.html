Dendrobium eriiflorum var. lombokense

Dendrobium eriiflorum var. lombokense, commonly known as the Lombok orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the island of Lombok in Indonesia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically blooming during the wet season. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, adapting to its humid, tropical environment.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
