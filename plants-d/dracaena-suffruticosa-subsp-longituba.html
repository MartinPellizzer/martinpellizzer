Dracaena suffruticosa subsp. longituba

Dracaena suffruticosa subsp.

longituba, commonly known as the narrow-leaved dracaena, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa. It features upright, cylindrical stems that are often ribbed and covered in a layer of white, powdery bloom.

The leaves are narrow, linear, and typically have a bluish-green hue with a waxy coating. It produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that bloom in terminal clusters.

This plant is adapted to arid environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
