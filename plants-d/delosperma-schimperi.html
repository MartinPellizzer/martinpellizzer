Delosperma schimperi

Delosperma schimperi, commonly known as the Ethiopian ice plant, is a succulent species native to Ethiopia.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of pink, red, and orange, typically during the rainy season. It is a low-growing shrub with a spreading habit, often forming dense mats.

Its ability to survive in poor soil conditions makes it a hardy and resilient plant for xeriscaping.
