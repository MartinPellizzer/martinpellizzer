Dolichos sinensis

Dolichos sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese bean or Chinese cowpea, is an annual leguminous plant native to East Asia.

It produces small, pod-like fruits that contain edible seeds, which are often used in traditional Chinese cuisine. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets and produces small, yellowish-white flowers. It thrives in warm climates and is cultivated for its nutritional seeds and ornamental value.

Dolichos sinensis is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, benefiting agricultural practices.
