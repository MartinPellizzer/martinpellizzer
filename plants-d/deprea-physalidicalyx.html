Deprea physalidicalyx

Deprea physalidicalyx, commonly known as the golden rain tree, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. The tree produces capsule fruits that split open to release its seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in various soil types.

However, it can become invasive in some regions if not properly managed.
