Digitaria uniglumis

Digitaria uniglumis, commonly known as slender fingergrass, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as fields and roadsides. It is an annual herb that thrives in warm climates with well-drained soil.

Its botanical features make it a common weed in agricultural settings.
