Dendrobium pinifolium

Dendrobium pinifolium, commonly known as the pineleaf orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, strap-like leaves that resemble pine needles, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with red spots, blooming during the winter months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium pinifolium is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
