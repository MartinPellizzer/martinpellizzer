Dendrobium poneroides var. angustum

Dendrobium poneroides var. angustum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf dendrobium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. The plant features slender, cane-like pseudobulbs covered in a layer of soft, white tissue. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to green, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

It is valued in horticulture for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
