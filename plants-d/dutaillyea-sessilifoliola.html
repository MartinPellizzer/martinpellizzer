Dutaillyea sessilifoliola

Dutaillyea sessilifoliola, commonly known as the small-leafed Dutaillyea, is a rare and unique plant species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

The flowers are typically small and inconspicuous, with a pale coloration that blends with its natural surroundings.
