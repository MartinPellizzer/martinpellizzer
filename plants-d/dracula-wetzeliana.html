Dracula wetzeliana

Dracula wetzeliana, commonly known as the "Vampire Orchid," is a rare and striking orchid species native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and purple flowers, which are often adorned with intricate patterns resembling bloodstains. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its unique growth habit, with long, drooping stems and clustered blooms. Dracula wetzeliana thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific conditions to flourish.

Its botanical significance lies in its rarity and the challenges involved in cultivating it outside its natural habitat.
