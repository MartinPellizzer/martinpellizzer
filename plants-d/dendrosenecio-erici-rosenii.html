Dendrosenecio erici-rosenii

Dendrosenecio erici-rosenii, commonly known as the Eric Rosenii Tree, is a striking member of the Asteraceae family native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, branched structure and large, showy yellow-orange flower heads that bloom in summer. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological importance, it is a subject of interest in botanical research and conservation efforts.
