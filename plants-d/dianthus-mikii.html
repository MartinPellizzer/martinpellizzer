Dianthus mikii

Dianthus mikii, commonly known as Mikii's campion, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink or white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun exposure. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, while its stems are upright and branching.

Dianthus mikii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
