Dieffenbachia aglaonematifolia

Dieffenbachia aglaonematifolia, commonly known as the Shiny-leaf Dieffenbachia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with striking white and green variegation. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other popular houseplants like peace lilies. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
