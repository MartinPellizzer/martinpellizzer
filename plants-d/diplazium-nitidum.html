Diplazium nitidum

Diplazium nitidum, commonly known as the smooth shield fern, is a member of the Dryopteridaceae family.

It is a terrestrial fern characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and dense, evergreen fronds. The plant features bipinnate leaves with a smooth, glossy surface, giving it its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is native to various parts of Asia.

Diplazium nitidum is often found in forest undergrowth and is valued for its ornamental appearance.
