Dendrobium delicatulum subsp. parvulum

Dendrobium delicatulum subsp.

parvulum, commonly known as the small-leafed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia. It features slender, often branched stems that can grow up to 1 meter in height.

The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically blooming in the summer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
