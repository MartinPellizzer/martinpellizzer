Delima dasyphylla

Delima dasyphylla, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows as a low, compact shrub with a dense, bushy appearance. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-drained soil.

Delima dasyphylla is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
