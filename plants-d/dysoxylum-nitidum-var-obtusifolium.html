Dysoxylum nitidum var. obtusifolium

Dysoxylum nitidum var. obtusifolium, commonly known as the broad-leaved dysoxylum, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and elliptical, glossy leaves with rounded tips. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by woody, elongated fruits containing several seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its leaves are used in traditional medicine for their purported anti-inflammatory properties.
