Diuris × sulfurella

Diuris × sulfurella, commonly known as the golden diuris, is a hybrid orchid species native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow and green foliage and produces vibrant orange and yellow flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, preferring well-drained environments. It blooms during the autumn season, adding a splash of color to the landscape.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and conservation efforts aim to protect its natural habitats.
