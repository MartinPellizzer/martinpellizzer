Delphinium intermedium subsp. legitimum

Delphinium intermedium subsp.

legitimum, commonly known as the blue delphinium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters.

The plant produces striking spikes of blue to purple flowers, which are typically arranged in dense clusters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a coarse texture, with a bluish-green hue. Delphinium intermedium subsp.

legitimum thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and wildflower meadows.
