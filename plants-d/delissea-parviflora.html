Delissea parviflora

Delissea parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered delissea, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in shades of white and pink. The plant has slender, branched stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Delissea parviflora is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
