Dombeya gallana

Dombeya gallana, commonly known as the yellow dombeya, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a hairy texture.

Dombeya gallana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
