Dactylis Obtusifolia

Dactylis obtusifolia is a lesser-known but resilient grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands. This plant is characterized by its broad, blunt leaves and dense, tufted growth habit. Despite its modest appearance, it plays an important role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife. Its adaptability makes it a valuable plant for ecological restoration projects.
