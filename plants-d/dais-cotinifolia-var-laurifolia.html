Dais cotinifolia var. laurifolia

Dais cotinifolia var. laurifolia, commonly known as the sweet shrub or fragrant cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter underside. Its fruit is a small, red berry that ripens in the fall.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant scent.
