Delphinium likiangense

Delphinium likiangense, commonly known as the Yunnan larkspur, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan, China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant produces showy, blue to violet flowers in raceme clusters, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. Its leaves are palmately lobed, with a coarse texture and a slightly hairy surface.

Delphinium likiangense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
