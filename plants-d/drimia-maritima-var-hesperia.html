Drimia maritima var. hesperia

Drimia maritima var. hesperia, commonly known as the Mediterranean yellow flag, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features grass-like leaves and produces clusters of yellow flowers in late spring to early summer. The plant thrives in coastal areas and sandy soils, often growing in saline environments. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense stands.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing coastal dunes.
