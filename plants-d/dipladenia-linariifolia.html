Dipladenia linariifolia

Dipladenia linariifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf mandevilla, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often tinged with silver, giving it its common name. It thrives in warm, sunny conditions and is often used in hanging baskets or as a climbing plant.

Dipladenia linariifolia is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
