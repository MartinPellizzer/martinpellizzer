Daemonorops rumphii

Daemonorops rumphii, commonly known as the red bamboo or red pandan, is a tropical plant belonging to the Arecaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and long, narrow leaves. Its leaves are often used in cooking, particularly in Southeast Asian cuisines, and the plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal.
