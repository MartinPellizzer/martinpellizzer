Digitaria laeviglumis

Digitaria laeviglumis, commonly known as the smoothawn, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny grains. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

Digitaria laeviglumis is often found in disturbed areas, lawns, and along roadsides.
