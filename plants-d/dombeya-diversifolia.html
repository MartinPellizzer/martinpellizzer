Dombeya diversifolia

Dombeya diversifolia, commonly known as the variegated dombeya, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and are often fragrant. The plant has elliptical, leathery leaves with a waxy surface, which help it retain moisture in its native habitat. It produces small, round berries that are typically green when mature and turn red.

Dombeya diversifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
