Dracaena marginata

Dracaena marginata, commonly known as the dragon tree, is a tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and narrow, feather-like leaves that are typically green with red or yellow margins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by red, berry-like fruits. Dracaena marginata is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its adaptability and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.

Its ornamental value comes from its striking appearance and ease of care.
