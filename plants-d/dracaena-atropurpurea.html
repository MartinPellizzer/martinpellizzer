Dracaena atropurpurea

Dracaena atropurpurea, commonly known as the red-edged dracaena, is a striking evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features upright, slender stems that bear long, dark green leaves with distinctive red margins. The plant is characterized by its ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its foliage provides an elegant, tropical appearance, often used in ornamental landscaping.

Dracaena atropurpurea is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal watering and occasional pruning to maintain its shape.
