Dryopteris capensis

Dryopteris capensis, commonly known as the Cape wood fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, upright fronds that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant features a thick, wiry rhizome that allows it to thrive in a variety of soil types. Its fronds are usually deeply divided into narrow segments, giving it a distinctive, delicate appearance.

It prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas or along stream banks.
