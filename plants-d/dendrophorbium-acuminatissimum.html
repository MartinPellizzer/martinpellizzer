Dendrophorbium acuminatissimum

Dendrophorbium acuminatissimum, commonly known as the sharp-pointed forest orchid, is a rare and specialized plant found in specific tropical forest habitats.

The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, typically arranged in a loose inflorescence.
