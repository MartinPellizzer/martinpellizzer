Dalbergaria perpulchra

Dalbergaria perpulchra, commonly known as the pretty dalbergia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

Dalbergaria perpulchra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
