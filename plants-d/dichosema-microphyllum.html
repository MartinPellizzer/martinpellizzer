Dichosema microphyllum

Dichosema microphyllum, commonly known as the small-leaved dichoseris, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically arranged oppositely along the stems. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its compact size and ornamental flowers, it is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
