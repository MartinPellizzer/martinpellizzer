Dulongia

Dulongia, commonly known as the silver leaf tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that have a distinctive silvery underside. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are followed by woody capsules containing seeds. Dulongia is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
