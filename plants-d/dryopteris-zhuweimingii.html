Dryopteris zhuweimingii

Dryopteris zhuweimingii, commonly known as the Zhu Weiming fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its robust, leathery fronds that are typically dark green in color. The plant features a short, thick rhizome with a dense covering of brown, fibrous roots. Its sori are round and clustered on the underside of the fronds, and the spores are released during the late spring and summer months.

This fern is often found in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors and rocky crevices.
