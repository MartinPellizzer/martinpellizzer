Dracula trichroma

Dracula trichroma, commonly known as the Three-colored Dragon.

It is a rare and striking orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, tri-colored petals that range from deep red to orange and yellow. It thrives in high-altitude environments with high humidity and partial shade.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
