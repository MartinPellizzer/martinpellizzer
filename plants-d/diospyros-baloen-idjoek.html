Diospyros baloen-idjoek

Diospyros baloen-idjoek, commonly known as the Java persimmon, is a species of flowering plant in the family Ebenaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia, and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and small, clustered flowers.
