Dactylorhiza majalis var. junialis

Dactylorhiza majalis var. junialis, commonly known as the slender early orchid, is a species of orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a rhizome and produces a single, slender stem bearing a few flowers. The flowers are typically pale green to yellowish with a distinct lip that is often darker in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often found in botanical gardens and conservation areas.
