Diplazium christensenianum

Diplazium christensenianum, commonly known as the Christensen's water fern, is a species of aquatic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shallow, freshwater habitats such as ponds and marshes. The plant has delicate, feathery fronds that are often submerged or floating. Its rhizomes are creeping and help in the spread of the plant within its environment.

It plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.
