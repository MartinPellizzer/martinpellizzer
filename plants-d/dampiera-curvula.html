Dampiera curvula

Dampiera curvula, commonly known as the narrow-leaf dampiera, is a perennial herb native to southern Australia.

It features slender, upright stems and small, pale blue to purple flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It is typically found in sandy or gravelly soils in open habitats.

Dampiera curvula is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
