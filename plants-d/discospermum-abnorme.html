Discospermum abnorme

Discospermum abnorme, commonly known as the giant star gooseberry, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or white in color. The plant produces edible berries that are often used in traditional medicine and cuisine. Its leaves are elliptical and have a glossy surface, while its bark is rough and fissured.

Discospermum abnorme thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in lowland forests.
