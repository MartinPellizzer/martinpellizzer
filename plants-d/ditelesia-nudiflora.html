Ditelesia nudiflora

Ditelesia nudiflora, commonly known as the naked-flower ditelesia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant is characterized by its smooth, often woody stems and small, clustered leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow and appear before the leaves in early spring.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
