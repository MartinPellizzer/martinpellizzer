Deschampsia brasiliensis

Deschampsia brasiliensis, commonly known as the Brazilian feathergrass, is a perennial grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-green foliage and delicate, feathery plumes that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences.

Deschampsia brasiliensis is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions.
