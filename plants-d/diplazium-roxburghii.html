Diplazium roxburghii

It is characterized by its large, pinnately compound leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its rhizomes are often used in herbal remedies for their purported anti-inflammatory properties.
