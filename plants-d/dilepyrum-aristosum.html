Dilepyrum aristosum

Dilepyrum aristosum, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

Dilepyrum aristosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual interest.
