Disporum smilacinum var. variegatum

Disporum smilacinum var. variegatum, commonly known as the variegated disporum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It features striking, arrowhead-shaped leaves with a distinctive variegation pattern of green and white. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped white flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a unique and shade-tolerant foliage plant.
