Dieffenbachia picta subvar. mirabilis

Dieffenbachia picta subvar. mirabilis, commonly known as the "Neon Pothos," is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that feature bright white and green patterns. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions. Its foliage is often used in interior decorating due to its aesthetic appeal.

It requires moderate watering and well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
