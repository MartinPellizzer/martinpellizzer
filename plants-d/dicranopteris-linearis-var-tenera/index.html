Dicranopteris Linearis Var. Tenera

Tenera brake fern, scientifically known as Dicranopteris linearis var. tenera, is a unique and resilient fern species native to Southeast Asia. This plant is characterized by its long, narrow fronds that can grow up to several feet in length. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests and along stream banks. The Tenera brake fern is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate a range of soil conditions. Its adaptability and aesthetic qualities make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
