Dimorphotheca dregei var. dregei

Dimorphotheca dregei var. dregei, commonly known as the yellow ox-eye daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow central disk surrounded by white to pale yellow ray florets. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and coastal areas. It has erect, branching stems and narrow, linear leaves.

The flowers bloom in spring and summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
