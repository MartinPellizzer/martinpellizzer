Dichondra evolvulacea f. minor

Dichondra evolvulacea f. minor, commonly known as the dwarf dichondra, is a low-growing, mat-forming plant native to South America.

It features small, kidney-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and ground covers.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a desirable plant for ornamental horticulture.
