Dendrobenthamia gigantea var. caudata

Dendrobenthamia gigantea var. caudata, commonly known as the long-tailed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in the summer months. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, violet to purple flowers, which are often borne on long, drooping inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
