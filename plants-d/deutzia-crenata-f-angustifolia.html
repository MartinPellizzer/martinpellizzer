Deutzia crenata f. angustifolia

Deutzia crenata f. angustifolia, commonly known as narrow-leaved deutzia, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to China.

It features slender, arching stems with small, white, clustered flowers that bloom in early summer. The leaves are ovate to elliptical, with a serrated margin and a dark green upper surface. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a suitable choice for garden borders or containers.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
