Descurainia cumingiana var. glabrescens

Descurainia cumingiana var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth descurainia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring.
