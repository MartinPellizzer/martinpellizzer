Deutzia triradiata

Deutzia triradiata, commonly known as the three-rayed deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features delicate, fragrant white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, arching branches and small, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Deutzia triradiata is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and easy maintenance.
