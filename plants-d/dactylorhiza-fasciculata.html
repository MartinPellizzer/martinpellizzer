Dactylorhiza fasciculata

Dactylorhiza fasciculata, commonly known as the clustered orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant with a rhizomatous root system and produces tall, flowering stems. The flowers are typically pink or purple and are arranged in a dense, spike-like cluster. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wetlands.

The plant has a short flowering period, usually in spring or early summer.
