Disa schimperi

Disa schimperi, commonly known as the red disa, is a striking orchid native to the highlands of Ethiopia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red petals and sepals, which contrast beautifully with its white or yellow labellum. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous areas, thriving in cool, moist environments. It has a short flowering period, usually in the spring, and is known for its delicate, ephemeral blooms.

Disa schimperi is a rare and endangered species, making it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
