Duranta baumgartii

Duranta baumgartii, commonly known as the golden bell vine, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, bell-shaped flowers that appear in clusters, making it a striking ornamental plant. The plant produces small, oval-shaped berries that turn from green to red as they mature. It thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated for its vibrant floral display.

Duranta baumgartii is also valued for its ability to grow in a variety of soil types and its tolerance to drought conditions.
