Daphne × elisae

Daphne × elisae, commonly known as the snowflake daphne, is a deciduous shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features clusters of fragrant, white flowers that resemble snowflakes, blooming in early spring. The plant has dark green, elliptical leaves that provide a striking contrast to its delicate blossoms. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Daphne × elisae is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
