Disa patula

Disa patula, commonly known as the slender disa, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky areas. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, fragrant blooms during the summer months.

Disa patula is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
