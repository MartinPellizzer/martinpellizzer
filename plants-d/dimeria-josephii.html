Dimeria josephii

Dimeria josephii, commonly known as Joseph's dimeria, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, commonly referred to as the ice plant family. The plant features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white powder, which helps reduce water loss. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months.

Dimeria josephii is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
