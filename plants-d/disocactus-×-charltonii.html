Disocactus × charltonii

Disocactus × charltonii, commonly known as the Queen of the Night, is a hybrid cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its large, waxy, nocturnal flowers that open only at night and emit a fragrant scent. The plant has a short, thick stem with spines and produces vibrant, trumpet-shaped blooms in shades of white, yellow, or pink. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Due to its unique blooming habit, it is a popular choice for collectors and enthusiasts of night-blooming plants.
