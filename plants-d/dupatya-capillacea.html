Dupatya capillacea

Dupatya capillacea, commonly known as the slender-stemmed orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, slender stems and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest undergrowth. Its leaves are elongated and have a waxy surface, which helps in reducing water loss.

Dupatya capillacea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
