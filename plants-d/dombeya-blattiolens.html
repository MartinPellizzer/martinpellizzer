Dombeya blattiolens

Dombeya blattiolens, commonly known as the narrow-leaf dombeya, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Dombeya blattiolens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively low maintenance once established.
