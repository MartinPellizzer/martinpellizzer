Dietes bicolor subsp. bicolor

Dietes bicolor subsp.

bicolor, commonly known as the silver sword, is a striking succulent plant native to South Africa. It features a rosette of thick, fleshy, silver-gray leaves that store water, enabling it to thrive in arid environments.

The plant produces tall, branched stems that bear clusters of bright yellow flowers during the summer months. Its unique appearance and ability to survive in harsh conditions make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

However, it requires well-draining soil and minimal watering to prevent root rot.
