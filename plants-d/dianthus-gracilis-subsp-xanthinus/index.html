Dianthus Gracilis Subsp. Xanthinus

Yellow dianthus, scientifically known as Dianthus gracilis subsp. xanthinus, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe. It is prized for its vibrant yellow blooms that appear in early spring, adding a splash of color to rocky slopes and woodland edges. This species belongs to the carnation family, known for its delicate petals and fragrant scent. Its compact growth habit makes it an ideal choice for rock gardens and borders. With its resilience and ornamental value, yellow dianthus is a favorite among gardeners and botanists alike.
