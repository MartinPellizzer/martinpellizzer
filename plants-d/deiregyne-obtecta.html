Deiregyne obtecta

Deiregyne obtecta, commonly known as the narrow-leafed thistle, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and leaves arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant features spiny, lance-shaped leaves that are covered with a dense layer of fine hairs. Its flowering heads consist of both ray and disk flowers, producing small, pale yellow blooms.

Deiregyne obtecta is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
