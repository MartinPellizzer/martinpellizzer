Dieffenbachia seguine subvar. decora

Dieffenbachia seguine subvar. decora, commonly known as the Decorum Dieffenbachia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, broad, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green with white or cream-colored veins. The plant is known for its striking foliage and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Due to its toxic sap, it should be handled with care and kept away from pets and children.
