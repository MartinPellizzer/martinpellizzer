Deprea dilloniana

Deprea dilloniana, commonly known as the Dilloni's cactus, is a species of flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Cactaceae and is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom during the cooler months. It is adapted to survive in dry environments with minimal water requirements.
