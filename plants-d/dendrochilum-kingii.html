Dendrochilum kingii

Dendrochilum kingii, commonly known as the King's tree orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in the summer. The plant produces a single inflorescence with several violet or purple flowers, each featuring a distinctive lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrochilum kingii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
