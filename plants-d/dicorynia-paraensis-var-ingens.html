Dicorynia paraensis var. ingens

Dicorynia paraensis var. ingens, commonly known as the giant dicorynia, is a large tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its massive, buttressed trunk and broad, compound leaves. The plant produces large, showy flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its fruit is a pod that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind and animals.
