Dupatya lanatoalba

Dupatya lanatoalba, commonly known as the woolly tree.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to the Philippines, characterized by its thick, hairy foliage and rugged bark. The leaves are typically leathery and dark green, with a distinctive waxy coating. It produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by spherical, berry-like fruits.

This plant is often found in lowland forests and is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance.
