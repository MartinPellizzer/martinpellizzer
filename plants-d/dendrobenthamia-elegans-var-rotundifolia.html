Dendrobenthamia elegans var. rotundifolia

Dendrobenthamia elegans var. rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leafed orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

The plant has elliptical or ovate leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine pubescence.
