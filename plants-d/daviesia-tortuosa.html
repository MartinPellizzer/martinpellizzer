Daviesia tortuosa

Daviesia tortuosa, commonly known as the zigzag Daviesia, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It is characterized by its twisted, woody stems and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from pink to white. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to harsh, arid environments. It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and has small, compound leaves.

Daviesia tortuosa is often found in heathlands and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
