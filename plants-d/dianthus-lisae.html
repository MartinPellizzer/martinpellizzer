Dianthus lisae

Dianthus lisae, commonly known as the Lisae Pink, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that range from pink to red. The plant typically blooms in late spring to early summer, producing clusters of small, rounded petals. Dianthus lisae prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its botanical features include hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves.
