Dupatya argyrolinon

Dupatya argyrolinon, commonly known as the silver-leaved fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that display a striking silver sheen on the underside. The unique appearance and adaptability of Dupatya argyrolinon make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
