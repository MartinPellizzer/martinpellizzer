Diplodium atrans

Diplodium atrans, commonly known as the black diplodium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grassland and rocky habitats. The plant features slender, erect stems with narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, tubular flowers are usually pink to purple in color and bloom during the warmer months.

Diplodium atrans is known for its ability to thrive in arid conditions, making it a hardy species adapted to semi-desert environments.
