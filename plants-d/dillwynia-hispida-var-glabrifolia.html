Dillwynia hispida var. glabrifolia

Dillwynia hispida var. glabrifolia, commonly known as the smooth-leaved dillwynia, is a perennial shrub native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and glabrous. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to purple, typically arranged in racemes. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and dry sclerophyllous habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is a popular choice for native gardens.
