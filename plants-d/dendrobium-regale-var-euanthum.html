Dendrobium regale var. euanthum

Dendrobium regale var. euanthum, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, cane-like stems that can grow up to several meters in height, often adorned with clusters of fragrant, golden-yellow flowers. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation. Its flowers bloom in late winter to early spring, adding a vibrant touch to gardens or greenhouses.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for with proper humidity and watering practices.
