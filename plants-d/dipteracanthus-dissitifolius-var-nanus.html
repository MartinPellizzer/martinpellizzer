Dipteracanthus dissitifolius var. nanus

Dipteracanthus dissitifolius var. nanus, commonly known as the dwarf dipteracanthus, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, glossy, dark green leaves that are arranged in a dense, bushy habit. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for shaded garden spaces.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact size and extended flowering period.
