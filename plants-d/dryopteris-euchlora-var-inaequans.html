Dryopteris euchlora var. inaequans

Dryopteris euchlora var. inaequans, commonly known as the unequal fern, is a variant of the broad shield fern.

It is a perennial evergreen fern characterized by its large, leathery, dark green fronds. The fronds are typically divided into segments that vary in size, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable conditions.
