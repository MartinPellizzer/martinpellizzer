Drapetes tasmanica

Drapetes tasmanica, commonly known as the Tasmanian drapetis, is an endemic species native to Tasmania.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understoreys.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
