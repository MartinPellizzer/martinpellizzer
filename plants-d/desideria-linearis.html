Desideria linearis

Desideria linearis, commonly known as the slender desideria, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or white. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes. Its botanical features include a fibrous root system and a compact growth habit.

Desideria linearis is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
