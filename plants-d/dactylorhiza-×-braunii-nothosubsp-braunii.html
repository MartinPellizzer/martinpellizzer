Dactylorhiza × braunii nothosubsp. braunii

Dactylorhiza × braunii nothosubsp.

braunii, commonly known as the Braun's marsh orchid, is a hybrid orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia. It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of pale green to yellowish flowers.

The plant typically grows in wet or marshy habitats, preferring acidic soils. It has a complex morphology with a rhizomatous root system and linear, grass-like leaves.

Due to its hybrid origin, it exhibits a range of morphological variations in its natural populations.
