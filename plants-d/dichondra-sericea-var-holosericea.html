Dichondra sericea var. holosericea

Dichondra sericea var. holosericea, commonly known as silverleaf dichondra, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the southwestern United States.

It features small, heart-shaped leaves that are densely covered with fine, silvery-white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and ground covers.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value make it a valuable addition to arid landscape designs.
