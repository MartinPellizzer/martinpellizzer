Daemonorops rarispinosa

Daemonorops rarispinosa, commonly known as the rare spined palm, is a species of palm native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect trunk and pinnate leaves that are often adorned with small, spiny projections. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, such as lowland forests and swampy areas. Its inflorescences are clustered and produce small, white flowers, followed by dark blue berries.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
