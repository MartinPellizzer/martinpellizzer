Desmodium rhynchodesmum

Desmodium rhynchodesmum, commonly known as the slender sicklepod, is a herbaceous annual plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant produces pods that are typically curved or sickle-shaped, which gives it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

Desmodium rhynchodesmum is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility.
