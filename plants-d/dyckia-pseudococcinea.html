Dyckia pseudococcinea

Dyckia pseudococcinea, commonly known as the red dyckia, is a species of bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its rosette of narrow, dark green leaves with a reddish hue along the margins. The plant produces striking red bracts that enclose its small, white flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Dyckia pseudococcinea is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and compact growth habit.
