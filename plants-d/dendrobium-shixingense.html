Dendrobium shixingense

Dendrobium shixingense, commonly known as the Shixing orchid, is a species of orchid native to China.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic. The plant produces strikingly colorful flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of yellow and orange. Its leaves are usually elongated and arranged in a rosette pattern, typical of many orchid species.

Dendrobium shixingense is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its vibrant floral displays.
