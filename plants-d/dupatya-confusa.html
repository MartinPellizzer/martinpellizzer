Dupatya confusa

Dupatya confusa, commonly known as the Philippine velvet tamarind, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its flowers are usually yellow or greenish-yellow, and the fruit is a hairy pod containing several seeds.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in local medicine.
