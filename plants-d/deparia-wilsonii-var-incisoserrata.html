Deparia wilsonii var. incisoserrata

Deparia wilsonii var. incisoserrata, commonly known as the Wilson's shield fern, is a variant of the shield fern genus Deparia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds that are typically broad and robust. The leaves are usually bi- or tri-pinnate, with finely toothed margins that give it a unique appearance. This fern thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in temperate forests.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both horticultural and ecological studies.
