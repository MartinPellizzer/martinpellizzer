Dansiea

Dansiea, commonly known as the ghost orchid, is a rare and ethereal plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its unique, elongated leaves that resemble ghostly shapes, hence its common name. The plant produces delicate, white or pale green flowers that bloom in summer, adding to its mysterious appearance. It is typically found in shaded, humid environments, often growing on trees or rocks in tropical regions.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, Dansiea is considered a conservation concern in many areas.
