Dianthus brevifolius

Dianthus brevifolius, commonly known as the short-leaved campion, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and bright pink or red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has small, narrow leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and borders.

Dianthus brevifolius is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
