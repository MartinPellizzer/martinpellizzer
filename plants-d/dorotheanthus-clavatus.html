Dorotheanthus clavatus

Dorotheanthus clavatus, commonly known as the clustered daisy, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow and white. The plant typically blooms in the summer months and is often found in coastal areas or sandy soils. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and a short, sturdy stem.
