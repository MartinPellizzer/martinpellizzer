Daemonorops calapparia

Daemonorops calapparia, commonly known as the red-veined cycad, is a member of the Cycadaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a cycad-like plant characterized by its tall, unbranched stem and large, pinnately compound leaves. The leaves are typically dark green with prominent red veins, giving it its common name. It produces cones rather than flowers, with male and female cones occurring on separate plants.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage.
