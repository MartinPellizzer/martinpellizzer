Dicliptera riparia var. yunnanensis

Dicliptera riparia var. yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan river sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It typically grows in moist, sandy soils along riverbanks and wetlands. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and is often found in riparian zones.

Its botanical significance lies in its adaptability to aquatic environments and its role in stabilizing riverbanks.
