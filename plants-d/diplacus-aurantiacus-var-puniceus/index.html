Diplacus Aurantiacus Var. Puniceus

Diplacus aurantiacus var. puniceus is a strikingly colorful plant native to the southwestern united states. it is known for its vibrant red flowers that bloom in summer. this variety of the scarlet gaura plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil. its delicate appearance makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental beauty. despite its modest size, it holds cultural and ecological significance in its native habitat.
