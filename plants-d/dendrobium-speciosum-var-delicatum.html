Dendrobium speciosum var. delicatum

Dendrobium speciosum var. delicatum, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on tree trunks or branches in humid, tropical environments. The plant features long, pendant-like inflorescences with fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

This variety is prized for its beauty and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and as a houseplant.
