Diabelia

Diabelia, commonly known as the "devil's bean," is a genus of flowering plants in the family Fabaceae.

Its small, yellowish-white flowers are clustered in racemes, and it produces pods that contain small, hard seeds.

Diabelia is often found in arid or semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils.
