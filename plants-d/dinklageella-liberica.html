Dinklageella liberica

Dinklageella liberica, commonly known as the African coffee tree, is a species native to tropical Africa.

It is a small evergreen tree that typically grows to a height of about 5 meters. The plant is characterized by its dark green, elliptical leaves and produces small, white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. The berries contain a single seed, which is the source of the coffee bean.

This plant is well adapted to humid, shaded environments and thrives in the understory of tropical forests.
