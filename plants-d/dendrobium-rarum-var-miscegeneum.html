Dendrobium rarum var. miscegeneum

Dendrobium rarum var. miscegeneum, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a rare and unique variant within the Dendrobium genus.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated pseudobulbs and clusters of fragrant, butterfly-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink. This hybrid is a result of crossbreeding between different Dendrobium species, leading to its distinctive genetic and morphological traits. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
