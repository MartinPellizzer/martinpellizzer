Dendrobium convexipes

Dendrobium convexipes, commonly known as the convex-shouldered orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a thick, fleshy rhizome that stores water and nutrients. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences bearing numerous fragrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow and orange. Its leaves are ovate and arranged in two rows along the stem.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
