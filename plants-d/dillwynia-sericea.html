Dillwynia sericea

Dillwynia sericea, commonly known as silky dillwynia, is a small shrub native to southern Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink, often appearing in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

Dillwynia sericea is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to attract native pollinators.
