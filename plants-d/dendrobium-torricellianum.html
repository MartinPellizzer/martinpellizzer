Dendrobium torricellianum

Dendrobium torricellianum, commonly known as the Torricelli orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with multiple pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green. Its leaves are typically long and strap-like, adapted for photosynthesis in its natural habitat.

Dendrobium torricellianum thrives in humid, tropical environments with bright, indirect light.
