Deschampsia flexuosa var. mairei

Deschampsia flexuosa var. mairei, commonly known as Maire's bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern China.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and slender, often curved leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in its native habitat.
