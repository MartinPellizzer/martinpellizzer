Diphyes laxiflora

Diphyes laxiflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered diphyes, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, pale yellow to greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters. Its reproductive structures are adapted for wind pollination, and it has a fibrous root system that helps it anchor in soil.

The plant's botanical characteristics make it an important component of its native ecosystems.
