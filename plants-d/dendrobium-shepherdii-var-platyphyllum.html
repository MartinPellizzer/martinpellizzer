Dendrobium shepherdii var. platyphyllum

Dendrobium shepherdii var. platyphyllum, commonly known as the flat-leaf dendrobium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. The plant features long, cane-like stems covered in clusters of small, elliptical leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with a distinctive, fragrant scent.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
