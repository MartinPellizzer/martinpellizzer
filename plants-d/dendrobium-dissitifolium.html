Dendrobium dissitifolium

Dendrobium dissitifolium, commonly known as the clustered leaf dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with cane-like stems that bear clusters of oval, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
