Dieffenbachia seguine var. minor

Dieffenbachia seguine var. minor, commonly known as the Compact Dieffenbachia, is a popular houseplant native to South America.

It is characterized by its broad, waxy leaves that are typically green with white or cream-colored veins. The plant is known for its slow growth and ability to thrive in low-light conditions. Its foliage is often variegated, adding visual interest to any indoor space.

It is important to note that the plant is toxic to humans and pets due to the presence of calcium oxalate crystals.
