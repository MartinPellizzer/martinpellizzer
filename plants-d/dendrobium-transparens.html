Dendrobium transparens

Dendrobium transparens, commonly known as the transparent orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, translucent petals that give it its distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, tropical environments. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white, with a subtle fragrance.

Dendrobium transparens is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique aesthetic appeal.
