Diploclisia inclyta

Diploclisia inclyta, commonly known as the silverleaf pittosporum, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal and subcoastal regions. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. Its fragrant white flowers appear in clusters and are followed by small, edible berries.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.
