Dicksonia cicutaria var. deparioides

Dicksonia cicutaria var. deparioides, commonly known as the Parson's tree fern, is a variant of the common tree fern native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas. Its fronds are typically dark green and have a distinctive, slightly serrated edge.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
