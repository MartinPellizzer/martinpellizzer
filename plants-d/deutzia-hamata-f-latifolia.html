Deutzia hamata f. latifolia

Deutzia hamata f. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, arching branches and elliptical leaves that are broader than the typical form of the species. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability in garden settings.
