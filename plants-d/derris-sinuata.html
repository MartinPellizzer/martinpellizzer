Derris sinuata

Derris sinuata, commonly known as the creeping derris, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial vine that typically grows in moist, tropical environments. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a slightly serrated edge. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, often clustered in axillary inflorescences.

Derris sinuata is known for its medicinal properties, particularly due to the presence of rotenone in its seeds.
