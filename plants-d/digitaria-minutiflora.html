Digitaria minutiflora

Digitaria minutiflora, commonly known as small-flowered digitaria, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, spikelet-based flowers. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields. Its leaves are narrow and often have a bluish-green hue.

Digitaria minutiflora is primarily a weed but can play a role in stabilizing soil in certain environments.
