Dendrobium sabahense

Dendrobium sabahense, commonly known as the Sabah orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems that bear numerous lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with prominent yellow lips. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-drained soil.

Dendrobium sabahense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
