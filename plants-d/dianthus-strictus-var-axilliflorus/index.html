Dianthus Strictus Var. Axilliflorus

Dianthus strictus var. axilliflorus is a rare and charming member of the carnation family known for its delicate pink blooms. Native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe, this plant thrives in rocky and well-drained soils. Its compact growth habit and vibrant flowers make it a prized addition to rock gardens and alpine displays. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in horticultural circles. This article explores its unique characteristics, cultivation tips, and its place in the world of wildflower enthusiasts.
