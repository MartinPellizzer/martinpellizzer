Dupatya circinnata

Dupatya circinnata, commonly known as the velvetleaf or velvetleaf hibiscus, is a tropical shrub belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners in tropical and subtropical regions.
