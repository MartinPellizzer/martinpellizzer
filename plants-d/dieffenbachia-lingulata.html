Dieffenbachia lingulata

Dieffenbachia lingulata, commonly known as the laceleaf dieffenbachia, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves with a distinctive white and green variegation. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions. Its foliage is often used in interior design due to its ornamental appeal.

Dieffenbachia lingulata requires moderate watering and well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
