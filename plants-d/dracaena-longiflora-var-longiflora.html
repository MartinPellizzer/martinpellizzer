Dracaena longiflora var. longiflora

Dracaena longiflora var. longiflora, commonly known as the long-flowered dracaena, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are often adorned with white or yellow variegation, adding visual interest to its appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically in shades of white or pale yellow, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to maintain healthy growth.

Due to its ornamental value and air-purifying properties, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
