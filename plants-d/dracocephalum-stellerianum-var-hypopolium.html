Dracocephalum stellerianum var. hypopolium

Dracocephalum stellerianum var. hypopolium, commonly known as Steller's blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in the summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
