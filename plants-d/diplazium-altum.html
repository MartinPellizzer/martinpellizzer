Diplazium altum

Diplazium altum, commonly known as the tall chain fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, upright fronds that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with a creeping underground stem that produces both roots and new shoots. Its fronds are pinnately compound, with a distinctive dark green color and a smooth, leathery texture.

Diplazium altum thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
