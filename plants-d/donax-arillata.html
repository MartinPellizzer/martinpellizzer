Donax arillata

Donax arillata, commonly known as the pink sandalwood or the Hawaiian sandalwood, is a member of the Rutaceae family.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to the Hawaiian Islands. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, fragrant wood and small, glossy leaves. Its flowers are typically white or pink and are borne in clusters.

The plant is valued for its unique scent and has been used traditionally in Hawaiian culture.
