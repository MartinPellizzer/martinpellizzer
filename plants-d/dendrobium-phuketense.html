Dendrobium phuketense

Dendrobium phuketense, commonly known as the Phuket orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to Thailand.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its erect, branching stems covered in dense, fleshy leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with red spots, blooming during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
