Dracophyllum elegantissimum

Dracophyllum elegantissimum, commonly known as the elegant dracophyllum, is a low-growing shrub native to the mountainous regions of southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and other ericaceous plants. The plant is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and small, leathery leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. Its flowers are typically pink or white, arranged in clusters, and bloom in spring.

Dracophyllum elegantissimum is adapted to nutrient-poor soils and is often found in heathlands and dry forests.
