Detarium chevalieri

Detarium chevalieri, commonly known as the African shea tree, is a member of the legume family Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical Africa and is characterized by its large, palm-like leaves and yellow flowers. The tree produces pods that contain a nutritious seed, which is widely used in traditional medicine and as a source of oil. Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it an important plant for soil enrichment in agricultural systems.

The plant also plays a significant role in local ecosystems and cultural practices.
