Davilanthus sericeus

Davilanthus sericeus, commonly known as the silky dandelion, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its soft, silvery-gray foliage and bright yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its composite flower heads. It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and forest edges.
