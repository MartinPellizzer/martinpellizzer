Dillwynia parvifolia var. trichopoda

Dillwynia parvifolia var. trichopoda, commonly known as the slender dillwynia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its pea-like flowers and compound leaves. The variety trichopoda is distinguished by its fine, hairy foliage and small, pale yellow to cream-colored flowers. It typically grows in sandy or loamy soils in open habitats.

This plant is often found in regions of New South Wales and Queensland, where it thrives in full sun.
