Daviesia uncinata

Daviesia uncinata, commonly known as the hooked daviesia, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in heathlands and dry sclerophyll forests. The plant produces bright yellow flowers, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid conditions.

Daviesia uncinata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
