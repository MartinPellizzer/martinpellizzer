Dendrobium matapense

Dendrobium matapense, commonly known as the Mindanao orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to the Philippines.

It features a sympodial growth habit with cane-like stems that bear numerous ovate, dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with prominent yellow lips. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Dendrobium matapense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its showy blooms.
