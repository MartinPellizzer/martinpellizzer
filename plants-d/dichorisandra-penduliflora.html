Dichorisandra penduliflora

Dichorisandra penduliflora, commonly known as the hanging flower or pendulous flower plant, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Dichorisandra penduliflora is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
