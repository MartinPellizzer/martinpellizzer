Dieffenbachia picta subvar. gigantea

Dieffenbachia picta subvar. gigantea, commonly known as the Giant Dieffenbachia, is a strikingly large, herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves with a striking combination of white, yellow, and green hues, creating a bold visual impact. The plant is a member of the Araceae family, characterized by its inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Due to its toxic sap, it should be handled with care and kept away from pets and children.
