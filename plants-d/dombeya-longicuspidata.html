Dombeya longicuspidata

Dombeya longicuspidata, commonly known as the long-pointed dombeya, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, which bloom in late summer. The plant has hairy, often glandular leaves that are arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to high-altitude environments.

Dombeya longicuspidata is often found in moist, shaded habitats within forested areas.
