Deutzia gracilis var. fastuosa

Deutzia gracilis var. fastuosa, commonly known as the slender deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features slender, arching branches and clusters of white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has small, ovate leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
