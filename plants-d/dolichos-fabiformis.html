Dolichos fabiformis

Dolichos fabiformis, commonly known as the long bean or yardlong bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It produces elongated, cylindrical pods that are edible when young and tender. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets and small, yellowish-white flowers. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Dolichos fabiformis is valued for its nutritional content and is commonly cultivated for both culinary and agricultural purposes.
