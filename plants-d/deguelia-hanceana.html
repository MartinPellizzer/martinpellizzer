Deguelia hanceana

Deguelia hanceana, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical tree native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red and orange flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a vivid display during the flowering season. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets and produces woody pods that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Deguelia hanceana is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
