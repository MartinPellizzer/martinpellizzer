Draba × kingii

Draba × kingii, commonly known as the King's draba, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically thrives in alpine and subalpine environments, where it tolerates cold and nutrient-poor soils. Its leaves are usually basal, rosette-forming, and have a distinctive lobed or toothed appearance.

Draba × kingii is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats across the northern hemisphere.
