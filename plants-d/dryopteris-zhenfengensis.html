Dryopteris zhenfengensis

Dryopteris zhenfengensis, commonly known as the Zhenfeng shield fern, is a rare and endemic species native to China.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its distinctive fronds with a unique morphology. The plant exhibits a robust rhizome system, which helps it thrive in its natural habitat. Its leaves are typically bipinnate, with a dense arrangement of pinnae that provide structural support.

This species is of interest to botanists due to its limited distribution and potential ecological significance.
