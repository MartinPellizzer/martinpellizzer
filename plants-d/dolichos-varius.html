Dolichos varius

Dolichos varius, commonly known as the variable dolichos, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and compound leaves with three to five leaflets. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes. Its pods are elongated and contain several seeds, which are often used as a food source.

Dolichos varius is valued for its nutritional content and is commonly grown in agricultural systems for both human and animal consumption.
