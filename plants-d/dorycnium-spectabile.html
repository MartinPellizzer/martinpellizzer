Dorycnium spectabile

Dorycnium spectabile, commonly known as the showy salsify, is a biennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and striking yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces long, slender roots that are edible and have a slightly sweet, nutty flavor. Its leaves are alternate, heart-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Dorycnium spectabile is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun.
