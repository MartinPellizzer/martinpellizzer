Dracaena reflexa var. reflexa

Dracaena reflexa var. reflexa, commonly known as the Song of India or Madagascar dragon tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Madagascar.

It features long, narrow, dark green leaves that are often curled or twisted, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by red or orange berries. It is valued for its ornamental foliage and is often used in indoor gardening.

Dracaena reflexa var. reflexa prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.
