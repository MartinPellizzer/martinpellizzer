Didymoglossum schaffneri

Didymoglossum schaffneri, commonly known as the Schaffner's moss, is a small, creeping perennial moss belonging to the family Pottiaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, cushion-like growth habit and pale green to yellowish-green foliage. The leaves are typically ovate to lanceolate, with a smooth or slightly serrated margin. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found on rocky soils or in woodland areas.

This moss is well adapted to humid climates and is commonly used in horticultural settings for its ornamental value.
