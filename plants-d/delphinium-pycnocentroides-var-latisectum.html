Delphinium pycnocentroides var. latisectum

Delphinium pycnocentroides var. latisectum, commonly known as the broad-leaf delphinium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant produces showy, blue to purple flowers in dense spikes, which are characteristic of the delphinium genus. Its leaves are broad and deeply lobed, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.
