Dichroa thyrsoidea

Dichroa thyrsoidea, commonly known as the silverleaf dichroa, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive silver-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in terminal panicles.

Dichroa thyrsoidea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
