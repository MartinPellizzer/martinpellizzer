Dianthus ucarii

Dianthus ucarii, commonly known as the Sierra foothill campion, is a flowering plant native to California and Nevada in the United States.

It has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Dianthus ucarii is an herbaceous perennial that is adapted to the Mediterranean climate of its native range.
