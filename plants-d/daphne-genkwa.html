Daphne genkwa

Daphne genkwa, commonly known as Chinese daphne, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features fragrant, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, red berries in the fall. It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens.

However, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
