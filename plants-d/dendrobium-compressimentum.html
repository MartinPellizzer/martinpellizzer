Dendrobium compressimentum

Dendrobium compressimentum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with cylindrical pseudobulbs and linear, evergreen leaves. The plant produces racemes of fragrant, yellowish-green to white flowers with prominent purple sepals and petals. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrobium compressimentum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its delicate floral display.
