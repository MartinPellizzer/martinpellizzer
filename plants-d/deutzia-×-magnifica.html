Deutzia × magnifica

Deutzia × magnifica, commonly known as the magnificent deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that range from white to pink, appearing in clusters on slender stems. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Deutzia × magnifica is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
