Dendranthema sinchangense

Dendranthema sinchangense, commonly known as the Sinchang chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow, white, and orange. Dendranthema sinchangense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
