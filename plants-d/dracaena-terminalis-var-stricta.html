Dracaena terminalis var. stricta

Dracaena terminalis var. stricta, commonly known as the dragon tree, is a tropical plant native to the island of Socotra.

It is characterized by its tall, narrow leaves that are typically dark green with a reddish or purple tinge along the edges. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by red, fleshy fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
