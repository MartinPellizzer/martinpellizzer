Discaria parviflora

Discaria parviflora, commonly known as the smallflower discaria, is a low-growing shrub native to Australia.

It features small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the spring and summer seasons. The plant has a fibrous root system and is adapted to thrive in sandy or gravelly soils. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in fine hairs.

Discaria parviflora is drought-tolerant and is sometimes used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
