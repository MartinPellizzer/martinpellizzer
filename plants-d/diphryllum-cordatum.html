Diphryllum cordatum

Diphryllum cordatum, commonly known as the heartleaf fern, is a unique plant with distinctive botanical features.

It is a member of the Diphylleaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which give it its common name. Diphryllum cordatum is native to tropical regions of Asia and is often found in moist, shaded environments.
