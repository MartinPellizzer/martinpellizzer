Dracocephalum forrestii

Dracocephalum forrestii, commonly known as Chinese chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to China and adjacent regions.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender stems, opposite leaves, and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to purple. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.

The species is also valued for its essential oils, which contribute to its aromatic properties.
