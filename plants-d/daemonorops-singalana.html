Daemonorops singalana

Daemonorops singalana, commonly known as the giant cycad, is a species of cycad native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, feather-like leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces large, cone-like structures that contain both male and female reproductive organs. It is often found in tropical rainforests and is adapted to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its slow growth and ecological significance, it is considered a valuable component of its native ecosystems.
