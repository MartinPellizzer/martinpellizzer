Diplostephium meyenii

Diplostephium meyenii, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a perennial aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom in a sequence, with the outer petals opening first followed by the inner ones. The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, often found in ponds and wetlands. Its leaves are broad and floating, while its roots are anchored in the substrate.

The sacred lotus has cultural and medicinal significance in various traditional practices.
