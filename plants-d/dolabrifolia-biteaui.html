Dolabrifolia biteaui

Dolabrifolia biteaui, commonly known as the swordleaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, sword-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink, which bloom in clusters along the stem. It thrives in humid environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
