Dendrobium ansusanum

Dendrobium ansusanum, commonly known as the orchid of the genus Dendrobium, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically produces small, greenish-white to yellowish flowers with a prominent lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Dendrobium ansusanum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
