Drosanthemum micans var. aureopurpureum

Drosanthemum micans var. aureopurpureum, commonly known as the Purple-leafed Stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically purple or reddish in color, with a waxy coating that helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant foliage make it an attractive addition to both indoor and outdoor landscapes.
