Dipholis pallens

Dipholis pallens, commonly known as the pale orchid, is a species of orchid native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, pale green to white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Dipholis pallens is noted for its delicate appearance and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
