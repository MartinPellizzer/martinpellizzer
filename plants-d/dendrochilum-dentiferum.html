Dendrochilum dentiferum

Dendrochilum dentiferum, commonly known as the toothed lip orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy, rhizomatous growth habit and produces tall, branched inflorescences. The flowers are typically pale yellow to greenish-white with intricate, tooth-like markings on the lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
