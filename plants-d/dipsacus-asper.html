Dipsacus asper

Dipsacus asper, commonly known as goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, spiny stems and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant produces distinctive, bract-like structures that surround its seed pods, which are often mistaken for flowers.
