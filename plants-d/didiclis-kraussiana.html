Didiclis kraussiana

Didiclis kraussiana, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a tropical plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its segmented, thornless stems that form a bushy shape. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, pink, and orange during the winter months. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Didiclis kraussiana is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
