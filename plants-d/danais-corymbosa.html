Danais corymbosa

Danais corymbosa, commonly known as the coral tree, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking clusters of bright red or orange flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has compound leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Danais corymbosa is also valued for its ability to attract birds and butterflies to gardens.
