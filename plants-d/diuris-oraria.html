Diuris oraria

Diuris oraria, commonly known as the morning glory orchid, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, spurred flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy soils and is found in coastal areas of southern Australia. Its flowers are usually pale blue or purple and open in the morning, hence its common name.

Diuris oraria is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in suitable conditions.
