Deinbollia dasybotrys

Deinbollia dasybotrys, commonly known as the African velvet tamarind, is a tropical shrub native to Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces yellow, fleshy fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in horticulture and ecological studies.
