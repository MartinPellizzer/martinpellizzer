Deutzia glomeruliflora var. forrestiana

Deutzia glomeruliflora var. forrestiana, commonly known as the Chinese deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy clusters of white to pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or bronze on the underside. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.
