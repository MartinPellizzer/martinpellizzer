Daphne odora var. rosacea

Daphne odora var. rosacea, commonly known as the rose-scented daphne, is a hybrid shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves and a compact growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Due to its toxicity, it should be handled with care.
