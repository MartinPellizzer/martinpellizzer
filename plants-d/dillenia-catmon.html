Dillenia catmon

Dillenia catmon, commonly known as the white dillenia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Dilleniaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its large, showy white flowers with a distinctive yellow center. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a velvety texture.

The fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release its seeds, contributing to its ecological role in seed dispersal.
