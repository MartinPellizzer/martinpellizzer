Diplazium stellatopilosum

Diplazium stellatopilosum, commonly known as the starry fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Hypnaceae.

It is characterized by its intricate, feathery fronds that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The fronds are covered with fine, star-like hairs, giving the plant its common name. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, particularly India and Southeast Asia.

This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.
