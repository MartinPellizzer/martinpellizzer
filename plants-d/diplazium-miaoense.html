Diplazium miaoense

Diplazium miaoense, commonly known as Miao fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its distinctive fronds with a serrated margin. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The fronds are usually bi- or tri-pinnate, giving the plant a delicate and intricate appearance.
