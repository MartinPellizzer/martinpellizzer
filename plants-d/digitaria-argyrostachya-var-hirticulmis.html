Digitaria argyrostachya var. hirticulmis

Digitaria argyrostachya var. hirticulmis, commonly known as silverleaf grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features narrow, silver-gray blades with a hairy texture, giving it its common name. The plant produces panicles of small, pale green to silver flowers, which are often tinged with red or brown. It is adapted to well-drained soils and thrives in full sun, making it a common sight in open grasslands and disturbed areas.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its striking foliage and ability to tolerate drought conditions.
