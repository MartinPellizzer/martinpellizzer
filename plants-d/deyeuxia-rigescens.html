Deyeuxia rigescens

Deyeuxia rigescens, commonly known as the rigid false panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to temperate regions of Asia.

It typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as meadows, marshes, and riverbanks. The plant has slender, erect stems that are often covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are narrow and grass-like. Its inflorescence consists of a dense cluster of spikelets, which are small and usually pale green or brown.

Deyeuxia rigescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
