Daphne tenuiflora var. legendrei

Daphne tenuiflora var. legendrei, commonly known as the Legendre's spindle tree, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of pink to white, which bloom in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and compact size, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a container plant.
