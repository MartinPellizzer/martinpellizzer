Diospyros sericea

Diospyros sericea, commonly known as silver flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific.

It is characterized by its smooth, silvery bark and large, palmately lobed leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by edible, sweet, fleshy berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and disturbed sites.

Diospyros sericea is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
