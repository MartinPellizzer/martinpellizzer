Drimiopsis reilleyana

Drimiopsis reilleyana, commonly known as the Reilley's drimiopsis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color, and it produces tall, branched inflorescences with clusters of small, white or pale purple flowers. The plant is known for its ability to store water in its bulb, allowing it to survive in arid conditions. It blooms in late winter to early spring, making it a valuable addition to rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Drimiopsis reilleyana is also noted for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
