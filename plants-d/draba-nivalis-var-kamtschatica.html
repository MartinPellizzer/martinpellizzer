Draba nivalis var. kamtschatica

Draba nivalis var. kamtschatica, commonly known as the Kamtschatka rockcress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically thrives in alpine and subalpine environments. The plant features basal rosettes of dark green, hairy leaves and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It is adapted to cold climates and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils.

This variety is native to the Russian Far East and parts of northern Asia.
