Dioclea steyermarkii

Dioclea steyermarkii, commonly known as the yellow dioclea, is a climbing vine native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces small, legume-like pods that contain seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical regions with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
