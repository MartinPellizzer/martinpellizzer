Dichilus sericeus

Dichilus sericeus, commonly known as the silky dichilus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often covered in a fine, silky hair, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Dichilus sericeus is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and flowers.
