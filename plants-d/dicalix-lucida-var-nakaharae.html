Dicalix lucida var. nakaharae

Dicalix lucida var. nakaharae, commonly known as the Nakahara's starflower, is a rare and striking plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pale yellow. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in the undergrowth of tropical forests. Its leaves are elongated and glossy, contributing to its ornamental appeal.
