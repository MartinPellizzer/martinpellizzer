Diplazoptilon picridifolium

Diplazoptilon picridifolium, commonly known as the goldenleaf cactus, is a unique member of the Cactaceae family native to the arid regions of South America.

The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the dry season, which are pollinated by insects and birds.
