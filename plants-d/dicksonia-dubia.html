Dicksonia dubia

Dicksonia dubia, commonly known as the slender tree fern, is a species native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of pinnate fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is closely related to other tree ferns. Its rhizome is thick and fibrous, supporting the growth of the fronds above.

Dicksonia dubia thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.
