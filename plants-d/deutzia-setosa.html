Deutzia setosa

Deutzia setosa, commonly known as the Chinese deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features slender, arching stems that bear clusters of small, white flowers in early spring. The plant has simple, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy on top. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Deutzia setosa is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
