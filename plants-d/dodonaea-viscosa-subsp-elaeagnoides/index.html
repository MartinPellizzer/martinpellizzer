Dodonaea Viscosa Subsp. Elaeagnoides

Dodonaea viscosa subsp. elaeagnoides is a hardy shrub native to southern Africa and parts of Australia. It is known for its fragrant, silver-green leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping. Its adaptability and ornamental value make it a versatile addition to gardens and landscapes. It is also valued for its ecological benefits, providing food and habitat for various wildlife species.
