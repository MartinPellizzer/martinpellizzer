Dianthus drenowskianus

Dianthus drenowskianus, commonly known as the Drenowskian carnation, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan region.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and linear, and it produces small, rounded seed pods after flowering.

Dianthus drenowskianus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
