Diasperus conterminus

Diasperus conterminus, commonly known as the Cape mountain lily, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. It has strap-like leaves and produces seed capsules that release small, dark seeds.

Diasperus conterminus is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
