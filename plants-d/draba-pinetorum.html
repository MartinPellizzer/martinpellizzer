Draba pinetorum

Draba pinetorum, commonly known as the pine forest sandwort, is a small flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Europe and is typically found growing in rocky soils within pine forests. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and early summer. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in a fine layer of hair.

Draba pinetorum is adapted to harsh alpine environments and plays a role in stabilizing soil in its natural habitat.
