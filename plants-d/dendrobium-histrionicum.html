Dendrobium histrionicum

Dendrobium histrionicum, commonly known as the fiery orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with multiple stems that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces showy, brightly colored flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with a striking contrast between the petals and sepals. Its leaves are strap-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stems.

Dendrobium histrionicum thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.
