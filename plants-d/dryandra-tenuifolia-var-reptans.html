Dryandra tenuifolia var. reptans

Dryandra tenuifolia var. reptans, commonly known as the narrow-leaved dryandra, is a unique variant of the dryandra species native to Western Australia.

The plant belongs to the Proteaceae family, which includes many flowering plants adapted to nutrient-poor soils.
