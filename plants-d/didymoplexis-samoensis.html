Didymoplexis samoensis

Didymoplexis samoensis, commonly known as the Samoan orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the islands of Samoa and nearby Pacific regions. The plant features slender, erect stems and produces small, fragrant orchid flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically in forest understories.

Didymoplexis samoensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and cultural significance in its native habitats.
