Digitalis × purpurascens var. hybrida

Digitalis × purpurascens var. hybrida, commonly known as the foxglove hybrid, is a perennial plant belonging to the Plantaginaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, spire-like inflorescences that bear striking, tubular flowers in shades of pink, purple, and white. The plant produces showy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically velvety in texture. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

Due to its toxic properties, it requires careful handling and is not suitable for indoor cultivation.
