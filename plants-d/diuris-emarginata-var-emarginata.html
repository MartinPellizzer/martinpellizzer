Diuris emarginata var. emarginata

Diuris emarginata var. emarginata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils in heathlands and open forests. The plant features a slender, hairy stem with several narrow, elongated leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow with a distinctive, spider-like appearance.

It blooms during the cooler months, often from late winter to early spring.
