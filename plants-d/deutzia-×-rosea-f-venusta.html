Deutzia × rosea f. venusta

Deutzia × rosea f. venusta, commonly known as the rose-flowered deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy, pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has arching stems with opposite, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its compact growth habit and prolonged flowering period enhance its ornamental value in landscape design.
