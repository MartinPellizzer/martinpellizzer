Dendrochilum fuscescens

Dendrochilum fuscescens, commonly known as the dark-stemmed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, dark green stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-brown flowers with prominent yellow calli, typically blooming in the summer months. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Dendrochilum fuscescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
