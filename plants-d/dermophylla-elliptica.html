Dermophylla elliptica

Dermophylla elliptica, commonly known as the elliptic leaf dermatophyte, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its elliptical, succulent leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, demonstrating remarkable drought tolerance.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
