Dipladenia gardneriana

Dipladenia gardneriana, commonly known as the golden trumpet vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves and produces long, twisting stems that can climb or trail. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Dipladenia gardneriana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant flowers and vigorous growth habit.
