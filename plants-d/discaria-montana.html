Discaria montana

Discaria montana, commonly known as the mountain daisy or white daisy, is a small, evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in dense clusters, often giving it a daisy-like appearance. The plant has a compact growth habit with woody stems and small, leathery leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Discaria montana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping for its hardy nature.
