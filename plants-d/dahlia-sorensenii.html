Dahlia sorensenii

Dahlia sorensenii, commonly known as the Sorensen's dahlia, is a tuberous-rooted perennial plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, single or double flowers in a wide range of colors. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and slightly hairy. Its flowers bloom in late summer to early autumn, adding vibrant color to gardens and landscapes.

Dahlia sorensenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its striking floral display.
