Dendrochilum grandiflorum

Dendrochilum grandiflorum, commonly known as the large-flowered tree orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces large, showy flowers with a distinctive purple and white coloration. The plant typically grows on trees or shrubs, forming a symbiotic relationship with its host. Its flowers bloom in clusters, often during the rainy season, and are pollinated by insects.

Dendrochilum grandiflorum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
