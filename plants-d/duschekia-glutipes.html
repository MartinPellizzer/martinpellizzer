Duschekia glutipes

Duschekia glutipes, commonly known as the sticky nightshade, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous annual that produces small, purple to blue flowers with a distinctive sticky coating on the petals. The plant has alternate, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, smooth seeds.

Duschekia glutipes is adapted to arid environments and thrives in well-drained soils.
