Dieffenbachia lutheri

Dieffenbachia lutheri, commonly known as the Luther's Dieffenbachia, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves that are usually a mix of green and white or yellow, with a textured surface. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other popular houseplants like peace lilies. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to prevent root rot.

Dieffenbachia lutheri is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant due to its ornamental foliage.
