Dendrobium sulawesiense

Dendrobium sulawesiense, commonly known as the Sulawesi orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to the island of Sulawesi in Indonesia.

It grows on trees and rocks, typically in humid tropical environments. The plant has a thick, fleshy stem that stores water, allowing it to survive in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually yellow or green with red or purple markings, and they bloom in clusters.

Dendrobium sulawesiense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
