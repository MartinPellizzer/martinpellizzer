Dendrobium lueckelianum

Dendrobium lueckelianum, commonly known as the golden spike orchid, is a sympodial epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a tall, cane-like stem with numerous fleshy, elliptical leaves arranged in a rosette. The plant produces racemes of fragrant, yellow-orange flowers with a distinctive golden throat. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium lueckelianum is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its long-lasting blooms.
