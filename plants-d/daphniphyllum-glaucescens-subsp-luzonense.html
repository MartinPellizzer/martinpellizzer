Daphniphyllum glaucescens subsp. luzonense

Daphniphyllum glaucescens subsp.

luzonense, commonly known as Luzon holly, is a species native to the Philippines. It is a small evergreen shrub characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, white flowers.

The plant produces small, red berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in montane forests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation in subtropical regions.
