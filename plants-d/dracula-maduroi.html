Dracula maduroi

Dracula maduroi, commonly known as the Maduroi vampire orchid, is a rare and striking epiphytic orchid native to the cloud forests of Costa Rica.

It is characterized by its intricate, velvety petals that resemble the wings of a bat, giving it its dramatic common name. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in late spring, adding a unique aesthetic to its natural habitat. Dracula maduroi thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific conditions to flourish.

Due to its rarity and ecological significance, it is often protected in conservation efforts.
