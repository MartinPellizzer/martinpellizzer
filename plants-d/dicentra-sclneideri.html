Dicentra sclneideri

Dicentra sclneideri, commonly known as the Chinese bleeding heart, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It features heart-shaped, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, arching stems and trifoliate leaves with a delicate, fern-like appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its elegant, drooping flower clusters that resemble a heart shape.
