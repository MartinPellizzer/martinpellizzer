Didymocarpus polyanthus

Didymocarpus polyanthus, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or the silver bush, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features dense, dark green foliage with a distinctive silver underside on its leaves, giving it a striking visual appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the summer months. Its adaptability to various soil types and moderate light conditions makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid environments.
