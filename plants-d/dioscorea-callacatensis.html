Dioscorea callacatensis

Dioscorea callacatensis, commonly known as the Callacatensis yam, is a species of flowering plant in the family Dioscoreaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Nigeria and Cameroon. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with long, slender stems that often twine around supporting structures. Its leaves are heart-shaped and can reach up to 20 centimeters in diameter, while the flowers are small and usually yellow or greenish in color.

The tuberous roots of this plant are used in traditional medicine and as a source of food in some local communities.
