Dendropemon lepidotus subsp. cajalbanensis

Dendropemon lepidotus subsp.

cajalbanensis, commonly known as the Cajalbanensis orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America. This species is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers with intricate patterns.

It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments, exhibiting epiphytic or lithophytic growth habits. The plant features lanceolate leaves with a waxy surface, adapted to retain moisture in its natural habitat.

Its blooming period occurs during the warmer months, adding ornamental value to gardens and natural landscapes.
