Dalbergia ximengensis

Dalbergia ximengensis, commonly known as the Ximeng rosewood, is a deciduous tree native to China, specifically the Ximeng region in Yunnan Province.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its small, pinnate leaves and fragrant, pinkish-white flowers. The tree produces small, flat pods that contain several seeds, and it is known for its durable, dark-colored wood.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, Dalbergia ximengensis is of interest to botanists and conservationists alike.
