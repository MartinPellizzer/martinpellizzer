Diodia Ocymifolia

Diodia ocymifolia is a unique flowering plant native to the southeastern united states. it belongs to the rubiaceae family and is known for its attractive foliage and small white flowers. this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas. it has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits. its delicate appearance makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking native plant species.
