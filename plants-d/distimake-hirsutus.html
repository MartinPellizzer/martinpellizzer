Distimake hirsutus

Distimake hirsutus, commonly known as the hairy distimake, is a unique plant species characterized by its dense, hairy foliage.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in temperate forest regions. The plant features elongated leaves with a rough, textured surface that aids in water retention. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually appearing in late spring to early summer.

Distimake hirsutus is noted for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional herbal remedies.
