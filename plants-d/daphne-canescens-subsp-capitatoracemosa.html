Daphne canescens subsp. capitatoracemosa

Daphne canescens subsp.

capitatoracemosa, commonly known as the Cape snowflake, is a shrub native to South Africa. It features aromatic, glandular leaves that are typically gray-green in color.

The plant produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. Its flowers are borne on long, branching stalks, giving it a delicate, lace-like appearance.

The plant is well adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.
