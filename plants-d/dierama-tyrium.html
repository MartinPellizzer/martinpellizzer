Dierama tyrium

Dierama tyrium, commonly known as the feather grass, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that bear clusters of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces delicate, feathery plumes of white or pale pink flowers that appear in late summer. Dierama tyrium thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

It is often used in gardens for its elegant, airy appearance and drought tolerance.
