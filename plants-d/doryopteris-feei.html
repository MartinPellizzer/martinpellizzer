Doryopteris feei

Doryopteris feei, commonly known as the Chinese shield fern, is a species of fern native to China and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, shield-shaped fronds that are typically leathery and dark green. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forests and rocky crevices. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Doryopteris feei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
