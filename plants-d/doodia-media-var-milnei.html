Doodia media var. milnei

Doodia media var. milnei, commonly known as the milne's fern, is a variant of the broadleaf fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, leathery, lance-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant typically thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its fronds are usually dark green with a smooth texture and exhibit a distinctive, elegant appearance.
