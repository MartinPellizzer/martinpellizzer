Diphyes tenuifolia

Diphyes tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender leaf diphyes, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, narrow leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its botanical features include a fibrous root system and a herbaceous stem that often lies close to the ground.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
