Desmodium elegans var. wolohoense

Desmodium elegans var. wolohoense, commonly known as the Woloho velvet bean, is a leguminous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which are often covered in a fine, velvety pubescence. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. Its fruit is a pod containing several elliptical seeds.

This species is valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability and is sometimes used in traditional agriculture and medicine.
