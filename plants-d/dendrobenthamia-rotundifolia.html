Dendrobenthamia rotundifolia

Dendrobenthamia rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems that bear numerous oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically in forest undergrowth.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and its role in the ecological diversity of its native habitats.
