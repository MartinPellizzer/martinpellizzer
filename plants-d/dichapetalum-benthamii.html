Dichapetalum benthamii

Dichapetalum benthamii, commonly known as the African star orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky outcrops. Its leaves are usually broad and grass-like, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences.
