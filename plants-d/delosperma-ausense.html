Delosperma ausense

Delosperma ausense, commonly known as the silverleaf iceplant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white powder, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant pink to red flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its hardy nature and low maintenance requirements make it well-suited for drought-prone regions.
