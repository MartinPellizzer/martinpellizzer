Dioscorea pentaphylla var. unifoliata

Dioscorea pentaphylla var. unifoliata, commonly known as the single-leaf yam, is a tropical vine belonging to the Dioscoreaceae family.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and heart-shaped leaves with a single leaflet. The plant produces small, white to greenish flowers followed by tuberous roots that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is native to regions in Africa and Southeast Asia.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and medicinal contexts.
