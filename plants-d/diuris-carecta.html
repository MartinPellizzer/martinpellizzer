Diuris carecta

Diuris carecta, commonly known as the pink spider-orchid, is an Australian native orchid with distinctive, spider-like flowers.

It typically grows in heathlands and open forests, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a single spike of pale pink to purple flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it blooms during the spring and early summer.

Diuris carecta is a protected species, often found in specific regions of New South Wales and Queensland.
