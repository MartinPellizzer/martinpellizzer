Doniophyton argenteum

Doniophyton argenteum, commonly known as silverleaf doniophyton, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
