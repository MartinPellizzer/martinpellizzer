Dissotis tenuis

Dissotis tenuis, commonly known as the slender dissotis, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with tiny, pale blue or white flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Dissotis tenuis is typically found in wet or marshy habitats, thriving in humid environments.
