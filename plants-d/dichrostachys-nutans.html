Dichrostachys nutans

Dichrostachys nutans, commonly known as the wattle tree, is a shrub or small tree native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive yellow flowers. The plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it valuable in ecological restoration projects.

Its seeds are encased in pods and can remain viable for several years, contributing to its adaptability in various environments.
