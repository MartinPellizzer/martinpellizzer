Dracocephalum erectum

Dracocephalum erectum, commonly known as upright skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant features square stems and opposite leaves that are often hairy and have a strong, minty scent. Its small, tubular flowers are typically blue or purple and bloom in summer.

Dracocephalum erectum is often used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and antispasmodic properties.
