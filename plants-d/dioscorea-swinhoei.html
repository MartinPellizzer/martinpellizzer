Dioscorea swinhoei

Dioscorea swinhoei, commonly known as the Chinese yam, is a perennial vine native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its climbing habit and hairy stems. The plant produces tubers that are used in traditional medicine and as a food source. Its leaves are heart-shaped with a rough texture, and it flowers with small, greenish-yellow blooms.

The tubers of Dioscorea swinhoei are valued for their nutritional content and medicinal properties.
