Desmodium willdenowii

Desmodium willdenowii, commonly known as slender desmodium, is a perennial legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, each consisting of three small, oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink, followed by cylindrical, bean-like pods. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or disturbed areas.

Desmodium willdenowii is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial in agricultural settings.
