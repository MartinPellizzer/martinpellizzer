Diaphananthe stellata

Diaphananthe stellata, commonly known as the star-shaped fern, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and small, clustered flowers that bloom in a star-like pattern. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The species is valued for both its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitat.
