Deyeuxia ramosa

Deyeuxia ramosa, commonly known as the slender false rice, is a perennial grass species native to temperate regions of Asia.

It typically grows in wet or seasonally flooded habitats such as marshes and wet meadows. The plant has slender, cylindrical stems that are often branched, giving it a delicate appearance. Its narrow, grass-like leaves are usually smooth and have a bluish-green hue.

Deyeuxia ramosa produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny florets, typical of the Poaceae family.
