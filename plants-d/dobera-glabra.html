Dobera glabra

Dobera glabra, commonly known as the smooth dobera, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, often glabrous stems and leaves that are typically elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or sandy habitats.

Dobera glabra is also known for its adaptability to harsh environmental conditions.
