Delosperma tuberosum

Delosperma tuberosum, commonly known as the ice plant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent species characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems and dense clusters of small, star-shaped flowers. The plant produces vibrant blooms in shades of pink, red, and yellow, typically during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

The species is also known for its ability to tolerate drought and poor soil conditions.
