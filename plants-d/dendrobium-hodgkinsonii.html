Dendrobium hodgkinsonii

Dendrobium hodgkinsonii, commonly known as the Hodgkinson's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the wet tropical rainforests of Queensland, Australia.

It is characterized by its upright, cane-like pseudobulbs that support its evergreen leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically blooms in spring, producing clusters of pale yellow to greenish-white flowers with red or pink markings. It thrives in shady, moist environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and protected species in its native range.
