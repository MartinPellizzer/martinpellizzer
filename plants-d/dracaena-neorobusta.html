Dracaena neorobusta

Dracaena neorobusta, commonly known as the African lily, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to eastern Africa.

It features tall, narrow leaves that are typically bluish-green in color, often with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, which are followed by red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright indirect light.

Dracaena neorobusta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly used in landscaping and as a houseplant.
