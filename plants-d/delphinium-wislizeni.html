Delphinium wislizeni

Delphinium wislizeni, commonly known as the plains gentian, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in open grasslands and dry slopes. The plant features tall, unbranched stems that bear clusters of blue to purple flowers. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

Delphinium wislizeni is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking blooms.
