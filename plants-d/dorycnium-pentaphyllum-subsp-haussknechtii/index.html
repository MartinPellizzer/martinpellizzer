Dorycnium Pentaphyllum Subsp. Haussknechtii

Hausknecht's five-lobed vetch, scientifically known as Dorycnium pentaphyllum subsp. haussknechtii, is a rare and distinctive plant native to specific regions of Europe. It belongs to the legume family and is characterized by its unique five-lobed leaves and yellow flowers. This subspecies is particularly notable for its limited distribution and ecological significance. Due to habitat loss and environmental changes, it faces threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are essential to preserve this botanical rarity for future generations.
