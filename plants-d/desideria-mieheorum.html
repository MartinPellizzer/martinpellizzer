Desideria mieheorum

Desideria mieheorum, commonly known as the Miehedoran orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically blooms in the spring, producing pale yellow to white petals with subtle markings. It thrives in cool, moist environments and requires specific conditions to flourish.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
