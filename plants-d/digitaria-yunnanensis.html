Digitaria yunnanensis

Digitaria yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan Digitaria, is a grass species native to China.

It is characterized by its erect, cylindrical stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Digitaria yunnanensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in horticulture.
