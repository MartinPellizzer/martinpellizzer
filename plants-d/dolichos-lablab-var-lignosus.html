Dolichos lablab var. lignosus

Dolichos lablab var. lignosus, commonly known as hyacinth bean or cluster bean, is a tropical legume characterized by its woody stems and climbing habit.

It produces clusters of small, bean-like pods that turn from green to brown as they mature. The plant is native to Africa and thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets, and it is often cultivated for its edible seeds and ornamental value.

The flowers are typically pale purple or white, adding to its visual appeal in gardens.
