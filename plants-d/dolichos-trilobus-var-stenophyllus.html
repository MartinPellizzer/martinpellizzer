Dolichos trilobus var. stenophyllus

Dolichos trilobus var. stenophyllus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf dolichos, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features compound leaves with three leaflets, each typically narrow and elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by elongated, cylindrical pods containing lens-shaped seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential use in traditional medicine.
