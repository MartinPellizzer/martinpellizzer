Dendrochilum amesianum

Dendrochilum amesianum, commonly known as the Amesian's tree orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually small and fragrant, with a distinctive coloration that varies by species.

Dendrochilum amesianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
