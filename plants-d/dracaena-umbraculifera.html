Dracaena umbraculifera

Dracaena umbraculifera, commonly known as the umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, umbrella-like canopy of dark green, glossy leaves that grow in tight clusters. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by red, fleshy fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant.

Its foliage makes it an excellent choice for adding a lush, tropical feel to interiors.
