Dieffenbachia fortunensis

Dieffenbachia fortunensis, commonly known as the Fortunatus Dieffenbachia, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, oval-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with white or cream-colored veins. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions. Its foliage is often used in interior decorating due to its aesthetic appeal.

Dieffenbachia fortunensis is also noted for its relatively slow growth rate and minimal care requirements.
