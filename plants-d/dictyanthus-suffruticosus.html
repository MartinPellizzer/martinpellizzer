Dictyanthus suffruticosus

Dictyanthus suffruticosus, commonly known as the rock rose, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, typically blooming in spring. The plant has aromatic, glandular leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for horticultural use.
