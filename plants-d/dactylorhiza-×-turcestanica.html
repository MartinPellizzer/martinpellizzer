Dactylorhiza × turcestanica

Dactylorhiza × turcestanica, commonly known as the Turkestani orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Central Asia.

It is a terrestrial plant with erect, linear leaves and produces tall spikes bearing numerous white to pinkish flowers. The plant is characterized by its complex morphology, including a pseudobulbous structure and specialized roots adapted for nutrient uptake. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires specific conditions for successful cultivation.

Due to its hybrid origin, it exhibits a range of morphological variations depending on its geographic location.
