Diosma punctata

Diosma punctata, commonly known as the spotted diosma, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades, and is characterized by its hairy stems and lobed leaves. Due to its ornamental value and medicinal potential, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and herbal practices.
