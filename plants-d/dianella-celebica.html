Dianella celebica

Dianella celebica, commonly known as the Celebes lily, is a striking evergreen herbaceous plant native to the Moluccas in Indonesia.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that form a dense rosette, and it produces striking blue to purple flowers on tall, slender stalks. The plant is characterized by its ornamental value, with its elegant appearance making it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for shaded garden areas.

Dianella celebica is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
