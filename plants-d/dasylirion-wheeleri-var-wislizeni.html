Dasylirion wheeleri var. wislizeni

Dasylirion wheeleri var. wislizeni, commonly known as the wislizeni dasylirion, is a succulent plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its rosette of thick, sword-shaped leaves that are often covered in a whitish bloom, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing numerous small, yellowish-green flowers. It is well adapted to arid environments, with deep root systems that allow it to survive in poor, well-drained soils.

The leaves are also used in traditional medicine by indigenous peoples for their purported healing properties.
