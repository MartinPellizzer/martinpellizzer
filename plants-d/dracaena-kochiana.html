Dracaena kochiana

Dracaena kochiana, commonly known as the red-edged dracaena, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It features long, narrow leaves with distinctive red margins that give it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate to bright indirect light.

Dracaena kochiana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
