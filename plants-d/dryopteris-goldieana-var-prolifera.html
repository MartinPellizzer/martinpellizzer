Dryopteris goldieana var. prolifera

Dryopteris goldieana var. prolifera, commonly known as the golden shield fern, is a varietal form of the gold shield fern.

It is a compact, evergreen fern with leathery, dark green fronds that have a distinctive golden hue on their undersides. The plant produces spore-bearing structures called sori on the underside of its fertile fronds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
