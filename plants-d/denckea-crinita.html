Denckea crinita

Denckea crinita, commonly known as the silky tree or silky hibiscus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, silky, white filaments that emerge from the base of its leaves, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Denckea crinita is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and vibrant blooms.
