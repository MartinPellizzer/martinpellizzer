Dendrobium × superbiens

Dendrobium × superbiens, commonly known as the orchid with striking yellow and green flowers, is a hybrid orchid species belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces racemose inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are typically long and strap-like, arranged in a rosette pattern around the pseudobulbs.

Dendrobium × superbiens is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
