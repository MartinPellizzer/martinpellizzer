Derris koolgibberah

Derris koolgibberah, commonly known as the silky oaks, is a large evergreen tree native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its bipinnate leaves and showy yellow flowers. The tree produces long, flat pods that contain seeds covered in a silky, hairy coating. It is well adapted to a variety of soil types and can thrive in both coastal and inland regions.

The plant is also valued for its hard, durable wood and its role in providing habitat for native wildlife.
