Delphinium stocksianum var. obovatum

Delphinium stocksianum var. obovatum, commonly known as the broad-leaf larkspur, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height, adorned with clusters of blue to purple flowers. The leaves are broadly ovate, with a velvety texture and a slightly hairy surface, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it a suitable candidate for alpine gardens.

The plant is notable for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its striking floral display.
