Drynaria raddiana

Drynaria raddiana, commonly known as the basket fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, arching fronds that form a dense, bushy appearance, resembling a basket. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with the roots forming a network that helps anchor it to rocky or shaded surfaces. Its leaves are typically dark green and glossy, with a leathery texture.

Drynaria raddiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can thrive in humid, shaded environments.
