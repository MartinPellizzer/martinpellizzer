Dactylicapnos scandens var. siamensis

Dactylicapnos scandens var. siamensis, commonly known as the Siam climbing fern, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a climbing perennial that produces small, delicate white flowers arranged in clusters. The plant has slender, wiry stems that allow it to twine around supports, making it suitable for vertical gardening. Its leaves are typically bipinnately compound, with finely divided leaflets that give it a feathery appearance.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
