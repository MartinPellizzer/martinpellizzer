Daphne odora f. rosacea

Daphne odora f. rosacea, commonly known as the rose-scented daphne, is a compact, fragrant shrub prized for its showy, pink to white flowers.

It belongs to the Thymelaeaceae family and is native to southern China. The plant features dense, evergreen foliage that provides a lush backdrop for its delicate blooms. It produces a strong, sweet fragrance, particularly in the evening, which attracts pollinators.

Due to its ornamental value and unique scent, it is a popular choice in gardens and floral arrangements.
