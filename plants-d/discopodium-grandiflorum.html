Discopodium grandiflorum

Discopodium grandiflorum, commonly known as the giant cup moss, is a member of the family Dendroceratops.

It is a non-vascular plant that thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in temperate regions. Due to its unique morphology and ecological role, it is often used in horticultural settings for its ornamental value.
