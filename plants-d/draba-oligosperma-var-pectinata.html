Draba oligosperma var. pectinata

Draba oligosperma var. pectinata, commonly known as the pectinate winter cress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches heights of 5 to 20 centimeters. The plant features basal leaves that are pinnately divided, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in racemes, which bloom in early spring.

This species is often found in alpine and arctic regions, thriving in rocky and gravelly soils.
