Dracaena willsii

Dracaena willsii, commonly known as the Red-edged Dracaena, is a member of the Asparagaceae family native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and striking variegated foliage, featuring broad, lance-shaped leaves with red margins and a dark green center. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters, followed by red berries. Dracaena willsii is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance of low light and moderate watering requirements.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both residential and commercial spaces.
