Dierama davyi

Dierama davyi, commonly known as the mountain onion, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often found in mountainous regions, where it contributes to the local flora.

Dierama davyi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
