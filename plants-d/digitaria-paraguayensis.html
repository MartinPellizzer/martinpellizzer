Digitaria paraguayensis

Digitaria paraguayensis, commonly known as Paraguay grass, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

Digitaria paraguayensis is often found in disturbed areas and is used for forage in some regions.
