Dendrobium salicornioides

Dendrobium salicornioides, commonly known as the sea bean orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a unique appearance with elongated, fleshy pseudobulbs that resemble sea beans, hence its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers with a subtle fragrance. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
