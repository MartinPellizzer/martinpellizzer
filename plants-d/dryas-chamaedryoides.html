Dryas chamaedryoides

Dryas chamaedryoides, commonly known as the alpine avens, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

Dryas chamaedryoides is well adapted to harsh alpine conditions, thriving in rocky soils and high elevations.
