Didymoglossum obscurum

Didymoglossum obscurum, commonly known as the darkleaf moss, is a small, cryptogamic plant belonging to the family Pottiaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, cushion-like growth form and dark green to brownish-green foliage. This moss is notable for its ability to tolerate low light and is often used in horticultural settings for its aesthetic and ecological benefits.
