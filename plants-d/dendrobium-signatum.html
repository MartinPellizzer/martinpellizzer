Dendrobium signatum

Dendrobium signatum, commonly known as the showy dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a tall, cane-like stem with a dense covering of dark green, leathery leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its unique floral display makes it a popular choice for both horticultural collections and ornamental gardening.
