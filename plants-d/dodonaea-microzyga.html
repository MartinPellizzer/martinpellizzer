Dodonaea microzyga

Dodonaea microzyga, commonly known as the silky leaf hopper plant, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in spikes, followed by small, woody fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

Dodonaea microzyga is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its hardy nature.
