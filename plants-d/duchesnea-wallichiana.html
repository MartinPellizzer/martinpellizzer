Duchesnea wallichiana

Duchesnea wallichiana, commonly known as the Indian strawberry, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Himalayas.

It produces small, red, edible berries that are similar in appearance to strawberries. The plant has trifoliate leaves with serrated edges and thrives in moist, shady environments. It is often found in meadows, woodlands, and along streambanks.

Its flowers are white with five petals and bloom in early spring.
