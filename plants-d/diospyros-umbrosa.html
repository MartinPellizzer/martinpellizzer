Diospyros umbrosa

Diospyros umbrosa, commonly known as the shadow fig, is a small to medium-sized tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, greyish-brown bark and elliptical leaves that are opposite in arrangement. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible, fig-like fruits that are typically dark purple when mature. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local medicine.
