Ditassa lindemanii

Ditassa lindemanii, commonly known as the Lindeman's dittany, is a rare and endangered plant species native to the island of Socotra.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its distinctive, small, yellowish flowers and woody stems. The plant thrives in arid and rocky environments, showcasing adaptations to the harsh conditions of its habitat.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat destruction, it is highly susceptible to extinction, making conservation efforts crucial for its survival.
