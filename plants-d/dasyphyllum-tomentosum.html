Dasyphyllum tomentosum

Dasyphyllum tomentosum, commonly known as the woolly dasyphyllum, is a unique succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves covered in a dense layer of white, woolly hair, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its distinctive appearance and drought tolerance make it an attractive addition to xeriscaped landscapes.
