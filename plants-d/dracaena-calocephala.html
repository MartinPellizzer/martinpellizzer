Dracaena calocephala

Dracaena calocephala, commonly known as the red-tipped dracaena, is a striking evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features tall, slender stems with lance-shaped leaves that are a vibrant mix of green and red, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its foliage adds a dramatic touch to any space, and it is relatively low maintenance.

Dracaena calocephala is also valued for its air-purifying properties, contributing to improved indoor air quality.
