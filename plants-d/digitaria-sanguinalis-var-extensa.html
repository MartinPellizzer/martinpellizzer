Digitaria sanguinalis var. extensa

Digitaria sanguinalis var. extensa, commonly known as broadleaf barnyard grass, is a perennial grass species native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its wide, flat leaves and dense, spike-like inflorescence. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural fields and along roadsides. It has a fibrous root system and can thrive in a variety of soil types.

Its botanical features make it a common weed in many temperate regions.
