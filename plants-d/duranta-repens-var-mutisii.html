Duranta repens var. mutisii

Duranta repens var. mutisii, commonly known as the silverleaf duranta, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a trailing or climbing shrub that produces clusters of blue or violet tubular flowers. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it its common name. It thrives in warm, sunny environments and is often used in ornamental landscaping.

The plant is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and hummingbirds.
