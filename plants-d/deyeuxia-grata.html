Deyeuxia grata

Deyeuxia grata, commonly known as the silvergrass, is a perennial grass species native to Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist environments such as wet meadows and marshes. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in panicles, which are often wind-pollinated.

Deyeuxia grata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in wet conditions.
