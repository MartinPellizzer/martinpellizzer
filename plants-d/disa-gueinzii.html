Disa gueinzii

Disa gueinzii, commonly known as the Gueinzii orchid, is a species native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, often violet or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers sunny to partially shaded environments. Its floral structure includes a single, showy bloom with a distinct lip that aids in pollination.

Disa gueinzii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
