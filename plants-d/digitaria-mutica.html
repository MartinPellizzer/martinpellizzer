Digitaria mutica

Digitaria mutica, commonly known as common Digitaria, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

Digitaria mutica is often found in disturbed areas, lawns, and along roadsides.
