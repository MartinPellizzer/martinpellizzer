Dalea cylindrica var. cylindrica

Dalea cylindrica var. cylindrica, commonly known as the slender milkvetch, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and rocky slopes. The plant features slender, erect stems that are often branched and covered with small, cylindrical leaves. Its flowers are bright yellow and form in dense, spike-like clusters, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

The species is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and supporting native wildlife.
