Dialium fleuryi

Dialium fleuryi, commonly known as the African starfruit tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to western and central Africa.

It belongs to the family Malvaceae and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant produces bright orange, star-shaped fruits that are edible and have a sweet, tangy flavor. The tree is also valued for its timber and traditional medicinal uses.
