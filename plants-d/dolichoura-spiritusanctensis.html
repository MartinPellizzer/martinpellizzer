Dolichoura spiritusanctensis

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its long, trailing stems and clusters of small, waxy flowers. The plant produces a milky sap that contains various alkaloids, some of which have been studied for their pharmacological properties. Due to its unique chemical composition, it has attracted interest in both botanical research and traditional medicine.
