Dimorphanthera obtusifolia

Dimorphanthera obtusifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, broad leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its flowers are usually pink or purple and bloom during the warmer months of the year.

Dimorphanthera obtusifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
