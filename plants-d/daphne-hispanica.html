Daphne hispanica

Daphne hispanica, commonly known as Spanish daphne, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Europe.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that range from white to pink and are borne on short pedicels. The plant has dark green, elliptical leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Daphne hispanica is valued in gardens for its ornamental beauty and is often used in rock gardens or as a border plant.
