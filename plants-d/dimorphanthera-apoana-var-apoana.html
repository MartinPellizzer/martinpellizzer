Dimorphanthera apoana var. apoana

Dimorphanthera apoana var. apoana, commonly known as the White-stemmed Orchid, is a species native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, white stems and delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments within tropical rainforests. Its flowers are usually pale green to white in color, with a unique structure that facilitates pollination by specific insect species.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
