Deutzia × carnea

Deutzia × carnea, commonly known as pink deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to Asia.

It features clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, arching branches and small, ovate leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and well-drained soil.

Deutzia × carnea is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
