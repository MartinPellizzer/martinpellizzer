Dieffenbachia seguine

Dieffenbachia seguine, commonly known as the Leopard Plant, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that feature bold white and green markings. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions. Its foliage is often used in interior decorating due to its aesthetic appeal.

It requires well-draining soil and moderate watering to prevent root rot.
