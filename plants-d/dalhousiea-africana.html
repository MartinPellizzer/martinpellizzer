Dalhousiea africana

Dalhousiea africana, commonly known as the African rock heath.

It is a flowering plant native to southern Africa, typically found in rocky and arid environments. Dalhousiea africana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with well-draining soil.
