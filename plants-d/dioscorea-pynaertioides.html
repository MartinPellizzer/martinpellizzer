Dioscorea pynaertioides

Dioscorea pynaertioides, commonly known as the Pynaert's yam, is a climbing vine belonging to the Dioscoreaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly Tanzania and Mozambique. The plant produces tuberous roots that are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits. Its leaves are heart-shaped and its flowers are typically yellow or greenish-white.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities as well as its potential medicinal properties.
