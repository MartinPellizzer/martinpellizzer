Deprea

Deprea, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and chrysanthemums. The plant features showy, daisy-like flowers with bright yellow centers and white or pink petals. It typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in full sun.

Deprea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil.
