Dalbergia stocksii

Dalbergia stocksii, commonly known as African rosewood, is a tropical tree native to central and eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its dark, dense wood that is highly valued for its beauty and durability. The plant has bipinnate leaves composed of small, oval-shaped leaflets. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by flat, pod-like fruits containing several seeds.

Dalbergia stocksii is often found in moist, well-drained soils in forested areas.
