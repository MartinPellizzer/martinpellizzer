Dehaasia teijsmannii

Dehaasia teijsmannii, commonly known as the Java orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, showy flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are often fragrant and come in a range of colors, including white, yellow, and pink.

Dehaasia teijsmannii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in botanical gardens and by horticulturists worldwide.
