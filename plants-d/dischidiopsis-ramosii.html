Dischidiopsis ramosii

Dischidiopsis ramosii, commonly known as the bushy African violet, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact, bushy growth habit with fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are often tinged with purple or red. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental appeal, it is often cultivated as a houseplant.
