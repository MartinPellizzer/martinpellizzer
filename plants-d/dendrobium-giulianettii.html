Dendrobium giulianettii

Dendrobium giulianettii, commonly known as the Giulianetti's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Dendrobium genus, which is one of the largest groups of orchids, known for their diverse growth habits and floral displays. This particular species typically grows on trees or rocks, forming pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually small to medium-sized, with a variety of colors, and bloom during the dry season.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
