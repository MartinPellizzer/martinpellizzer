Drosanthemum schoenlandianum

Drosanthemum schoenlandianum, commonly known as the Cape marigold, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow to orange flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub with hairy, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its blooms appear in late summer to early autumn, adding a striking visual element to gardens.
