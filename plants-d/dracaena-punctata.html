Dracaena punctata

Dracaena punctata, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, zigzag-shaped leaves that are bright green with white or yellow variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by red berries, though it is primarily cultivated for its ornamental foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and bright, indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Dracaena punctata is relatively low maintenance and can tolerate a range of environmental conditions.
