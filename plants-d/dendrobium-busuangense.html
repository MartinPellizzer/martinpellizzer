Dendrobium busuangense

Dendrobium busuangense, commonly known as the Busuang orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its tall, cane-like pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a sought-after plant among orchid enthusiasts.
