Diplopterys nutans

Diplopterys nutans, commonly known as the hanging leaf plant, is a tropical shrub native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that often hang in pairs, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported anti-inflammatory properties.

The species is also noted for its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.
