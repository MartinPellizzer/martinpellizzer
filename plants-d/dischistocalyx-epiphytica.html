Dischistocalyx epiphytica

Dischistocalyx epiphytica, commonly known as the epiphytic hibiscus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic species, meaning it grows on the surface of other plants without being parasitic. The plant features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red in color. Its leaves are usually ovate and have a smooth texture.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.
