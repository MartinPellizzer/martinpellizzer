Davallia Brachycarpa

Davallia brachycarpa is a unique fern known for its compact, elegant fronds and ability to thrive in low-light conditions. Native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, this plant is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its hardy nature and aesthetic appeal. Its rhizomes form dense, cushion-like clusters that make it easy to propagate and maintain. The plant's ability to retain moisture in its rhizomes allows it to survive in humid, shaded environments. With its delicate appearance and ease of care, Davallia brachycarpa remains a favored ornamental plant among plant enthusiasts.
