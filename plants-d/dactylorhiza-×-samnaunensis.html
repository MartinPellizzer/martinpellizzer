Dactylorhiza × samnaunensis

Dactylorhiza × samnaunensis, commonly known as the Samnaun orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the Alps.

It is characterized by its tall, erect growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green. The plant typically blooms in late summer, with spike-like inflorescences bearing numerous flowers. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and requires a cool, temperate climate.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and protected species.
