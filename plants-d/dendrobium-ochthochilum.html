Dendrobium ochthochilum

Dendrobium ochthochilum, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic orchid, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are long and strap-like, and it thrives in warm, humid environments.

It is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
