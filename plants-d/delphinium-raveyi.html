Delphinium raveyi

Delphinium raveyi, commonly known as the Ravey delphinium, is a striking perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, spindly stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant produces showy, blue to violet flowers in dense clusters, which bloom in late summer. Its foliage consists of deeply divided, fern-like leaves that are lush and green.

Delphinium raveyi is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its dramatic floral display.
