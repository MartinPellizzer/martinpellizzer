Deyeuxia cabrerae var. maxima

Deyeuxia cabrerae var. maxima, commonly known as the giant reed or tall reed, is a tall, grass-like plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems that can grow up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny florets. Its leaves are narrow and grassy, often forming dense clumps.

It thrives in wet or moist environments, such as marshes and along riverbanks.
