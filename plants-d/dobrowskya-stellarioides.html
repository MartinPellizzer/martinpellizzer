Dobrowskya stellarioides

Dobrowskya stellarioides, commonly known as the starwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Central Asia.

It features a mat-forming growth habit with small, often hairy leaves arranged in a rosette. The plant produces tiny, greenish-white flowers clustered in dense, star-like inflorescences. It thrives in arid or semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils or sandy habitats.

Dobrowskya stellarioides is adapted to withstand drought and temperature extremes, making it a resilient species in its native range.
