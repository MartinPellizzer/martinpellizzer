Dolichos aeschynome-sesban

Dolichos aeschynome-sesban, commonly known as the scarlet runner bean, is a leguminous plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its climbing habit, with long, slender stems that support its compound leaves and showy, butterfly-shaped flowers. The plant produces pods that contain edible seeds, which are often used in culinary applications. Its flowers are typically bright red or orange, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The plant is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial in agricultural settings.
