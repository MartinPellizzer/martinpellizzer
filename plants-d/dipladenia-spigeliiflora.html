Dipladenia spigeliiflora

Dipladenia spigeliiflora, commonly known as the zigzag nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its vibrant orange to red tubular flowers that bloom in the evening. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly waxy texture and a distinctive zigzag pattern along the veins. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Dipladenia spigeliiflora is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and nocturnal blooming habit.
