Durabaculum hornei

Durabaculum hornei, commonly known as the Horned Durabaculum, is a unique succulent plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems that store water, allowing it to survive in dry conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. Its leaves are typically reduced to spines, which help minimize water loss.

Durabaculum hornei is prized for its resilience and striking appearance, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
