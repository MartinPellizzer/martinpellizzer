Dissotis lecomteana

Dissotis lecomteana, commonly known as the Lecomte's spider-orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, spider-like flowers that are typically pale green to yellowish in color. The plant produces small, hairy leaves and has a rhizomatous growth habit. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
