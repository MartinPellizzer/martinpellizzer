Dracontium ulei

Dracontium ulei, commonly known as the Brazilian arrowroot, is a flowering plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Araceae family, which includes plants like philodendrons and calla lilies. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves and produces striking yellowish-green spatheous flowers. It is often cultivated for its edible roots, which are rich in starch.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered vulnerable in its native range.
