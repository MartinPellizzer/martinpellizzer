Duggena brachyantha

Duggena brachyantha, commonly known as the narrowleaf dudleya, is a succulent plant native to California.

It features fleshy, narrow leaves that are typically covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens.

Due to its drought tolerance, it is often cultivated as a low-maintenance ornamental plant.
