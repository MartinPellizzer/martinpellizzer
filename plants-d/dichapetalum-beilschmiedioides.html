Dichapetalum beilschmiedioides

Dichapetalum beilschmiedioides, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to South Africa.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red, with five lobes and a distinctive spurred structure. The plant has a fibrous root system and produces small, oval-shaped seeds within its capsule-like fruit. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
