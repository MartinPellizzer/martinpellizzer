Dendrochilum minus

Dendrochilum minus, commonly known as the small tree orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in forest understories. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with purple or brown markings. It has a pseudobulbous structure, which helps it store water and nutrients.

Dendrochilum minus is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
