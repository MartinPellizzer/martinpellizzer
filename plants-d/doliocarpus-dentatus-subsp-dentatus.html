Doliocarpus dentatus subsp. dentatus

Doliocarpus dentatus subsp.

dentatus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pittosporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand. It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges.

The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. These flowers are followed by small, round, green fruits that turn red when mature.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in coastal and lowland forests.
