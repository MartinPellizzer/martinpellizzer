Daphne kiusiana

Daphne kiusiana, commonly known as the Japanese daphne, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and are borne in clusters. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that provide an attractive contrast to its showy blooms. It typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and thrives in well-drained soil.

Daphne kiusiana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and seasonal interest.
