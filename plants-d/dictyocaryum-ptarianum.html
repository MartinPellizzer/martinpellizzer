Dictyocaryum ptarianum

Dictyocaryum ptarianum, commonly known as the mountain holly.

It is a shrub native to the high-altitude regions of South America, particularly in the Andes. The plant features small, dark green leaves with a leathery texture and produces clusters of white flowers in the spring. Its fruits are small, fleshy berries that turn red when mature.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and visual interest.
