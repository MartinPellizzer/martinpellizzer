Dendrobium corticicola

Dendrobium corticicola, commonly known as the bark orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique growth habit, with stems that often grow along tree bark, hence its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-drained soil.

Dendrobium corticicola is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
