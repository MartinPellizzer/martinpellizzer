Dolichos lablab var. uncinatus

Dolichos lablab var. uncinatus, commonly known as the hairy lablab, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves, which are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces pods containing edible seeds that are rich in protein and nutrients. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it a valuable crop for soil enrichment.
