Descurainia pinnata var. halictorum

Descurainia pinnata var. halictorum, commonly known as the yellow rocket, is a herbaceous plant native to North America.

It features basal leaves that are pinnately divided, giving it a fern-like appearance. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in the spring, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in moist, fertile soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its growth habit makes it an important component of wetland ecosystems.
