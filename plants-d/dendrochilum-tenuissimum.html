Dendrochilum tenuissimum

Dendrochilum tenuissimum, commonly known as the slender-stemmed orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees in tropical rainforests. The plant features narrow, upright pseudobulbs and linear leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

It is valued in horticulture for its delicate appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
