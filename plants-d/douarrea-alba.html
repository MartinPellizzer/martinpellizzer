Douarrea alba

Douarrea alba, commonly known as the white douarrea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, pale green stems and large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in the summer months, adding a delicate ornamental appeal. It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for gardeners seeking a striking yet easy-to-care-for plant.
