Dendrochilum propinquum

Dendrochilum propinquum, commonly known as the close relative orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or branches, making it an epiphytic species. Its leaves are ovate and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water retention.

The orchid's flowers are often clustered in inflorescences and bloom during the rainy season.
