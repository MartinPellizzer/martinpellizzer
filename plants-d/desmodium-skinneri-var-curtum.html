Desmodium skinneri var. curtum

Desmodium skinneri var. curtum, commonly known as the narrowleaf desmodium, is a perennial legume native to parts of Africa.

It features trifoliate leaves with narrow, elliptical leaflets and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant produces small, elliptical pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

This species is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil fertility in agricultural settings.
