Dactylis glomerata var. juncinella

Dactylis glomerata var. juncinella, commonly known as narrow-leafed sweet veld, is a perennial grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and dense, spike-like inflorescences. This variety thrives in temperate climates and is often found in meadows, pastures, and disturbed soils. It is valued for its high forage quality and ability to form dense vegetation.

Its botanical features make it an important plant in ecological and agricultural contexts.
