Dahlia Cordifolia

Dahlia cordifolia is a striking member of the dahlia family known for its heart-shaped leaves and vibrant blooms. Native to Mexico, this plant thrives in warm climates and well-drained soil. Its flowers come in a range of colors, from soft pastels to bold reds and yellows. Often grown as an ornamental plant, it adds beauty to gardens and landscapes. With proper care, Dahlia cordifolia can bloom throughout the growing season, making it a favorite among horticultural enthusiasts.
