Drosanthemum barkerae

Drosanthemum barkerae, commonly known as the Cape Hottentot, is a unique succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple hues, especially under bright light. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, typically during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in the wild.
