Delissea superba var. velutina

Delissea superba var. velutina, commonly known as the velvetleaf delissea, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features broad, velvety leaves that are dark green in color and often have a slightly pubescent texture. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses for its attractive foliage.
