Diplopappus linariifolius

Diplopappus linariifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ironweed, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. Its flowers are typically purple to pink in color, arranged in dense clusters at the ends of the branches.

It is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.
