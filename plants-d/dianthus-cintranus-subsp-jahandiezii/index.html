Dianthus Cintranus Subsp. Jahandiezii

Dianthus cintranus subsp. jahandiezii is a rare and striking member of the carnation family known for its vibrant pink and white flowers. Native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe, this subspecies thrives in rocky and well-drained soils. Its delicate petals and compact growth make it a sought-after addition to botanical gardens and conservation efforts. Despite its beauty, it remains little known outside of specialized horticultural circles. This plant exemplifies the diversity and resilience of flora in challenging environments.
