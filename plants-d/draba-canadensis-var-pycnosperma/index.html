Draba Canadensis Var. Pycnosperma

Draba canadensis var. pycnosperma is a hardy flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of North America. It thrives in rocky and alpine environments where few other plants can survive. Known for its small, delicate flowers and compact growth habit, this variety is a member of the Brassicaceae family. Its resilience to harsh climates makes it a symbol of tenacity in natural landscapes. This plant plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife.
