Delosperma pubipetalum

Delosperma pubipetalum, commonly known as the silver leaf ice plant, is a succulent species native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves covered in fine, white hairs that help reduce water loss. The plant produces vibrant pink or red flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for low-maintenance gardens.
