Draba verna var. brevifolia

Draba verna var. brevifolia, commonly known as the short-leaved spring cress, is a flowering plant native to alpine and arctic regions.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, cruciform flowers and capsule fruits. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to harsh, cold environments. It has basal leaves that are short and broad, with a rosette growth habit.

Draba verna var. brevifolia blooms early in the spring, often before the snow has fully melted.
