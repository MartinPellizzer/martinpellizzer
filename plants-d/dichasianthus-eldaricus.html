Dichasianthus eldaricus

Dichasianthus eldaricus, commonly known as the Eldaric flamebush, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silky hair. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its unique floral structure and vibrant color make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
