Daemonorops fusca

Daemonorops fusca, commonly known as the red-veined cycad, is a member of the palm family, Arecaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. Its cycad-like appearance often leads to confusion with true cycads, but it is classified as a palm.

Daemonorops fusca is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
