Dichapetalum conrauanum

Dichapetalum conrauanum, commonly known as the Natal orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil efficiently.

The orchid's unique morphology and ecological role make it an important species in its native habitat.
