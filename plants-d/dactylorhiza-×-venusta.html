Dactylorhiza × venusta

Dactylorhiza × venusta, commonly known as the fairy slipper orchid, is a hybrid orchid native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, slipper-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded woodland areas and requires a cool climate to thrive. It has a pseudobulbous structure with fleshy leaves that store water, aiding in its survival in its natural habitat.

Dactylorhiza × venusta is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens with proper care.
