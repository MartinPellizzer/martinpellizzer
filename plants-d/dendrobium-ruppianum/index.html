Dendrobium Ruppianum

Dendrobium ruppianum is a striking epiphytic orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. Known for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms, this plant is a favorite among orchid enthusiasts. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires bright, indirect light to flourish. The flowers, which come in shades of white, yellow, and red, often appear in clusters along the stems. Its unique beauty and relatively easy care make it a popular choice for both beginners and experienced growers.
