Dendrochilum edentulum var. patentibracteatum

Dendrochilum edentulum var. patentibracteatum, commonly known as the broad-bract dendrochilum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique floral structure. The plant produces striking, showy flowers with elongated petals and a distinctive lip. Its leaves are typically broad and lance-shaped, arranged in a rosette pattern.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
