Draba incanohirta

Draba incanohirta, commonly known as the alpine mouse-ear cress, is a small flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit, with basal leaves that are typically hairy and covered in a fine white coating. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, often in rocky or alpine environments.
