Dracaena argenteostriata

Dracaena argenteostriata, commonly known as the silverstripe dracaena, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, narrow leaves with striking white or silver streaks running along the midrib, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires moderate watering to prevent root rot. It is often used as a decorative houseplant due to its elegant foliage and air-purifying qualities.

Dracaena argenteostriata prefers well-draining soil and can grow up to several feet tall when provided with proper care.
