Dombeya bernieri

Dombeya bernieri, commonly known as the white dombeya, is a deciduous shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, white, tubular flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and a rough texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Dombeya bernieri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
