Distreptus spicatus var. nicaraguensis

Distreptus spicatus var. nicaraguensis, commonly known as the Nicaraguan spiny scale, is a unique variant of the spiny scale plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny growth pattern and small, scale-like leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant typically thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in lowland rainforests. Its botanical features make it distinct from other varieties of Distreptus, particularly in its structural adaptations.

This variant is of interest to botanists due to its ecological niche and potential medicinal properties.
