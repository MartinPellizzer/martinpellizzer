Dendrochilum abortum

Dendrochilum abortum, commonly known as the abortum orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments within forests. The plant features a pseudobulb with a single leaf and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Its flowers are usually borne on a single stem and have a distinctive lip that is often adorned with intricate patterns.

Dendrochilum abortum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
