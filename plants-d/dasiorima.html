Dasiorima

Dasiorima, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

Due to its rapid growth, it can become invasive in certain regions.
