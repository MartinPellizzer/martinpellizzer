Dendrophthora grandifolia

Dendrophthora grandifolia, commonly known as the large-leaf mistletoe, is a hemiparasitic plant that attaches to the branches of host trees.

Dendrophthora grandifolia is typically found in tropical and subtropical regions, growing in shaded environments within forests.
