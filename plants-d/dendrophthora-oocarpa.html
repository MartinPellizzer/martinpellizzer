Dendrophthora oocarpa

Dendrophthora oocarpa, commonly known as the giant podocarp, is a tall evergreen tree native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the family Podocarpaceae and is characterized by its long, slender leaves and conical shape. The tree produces large, woody cones that contain edible seeds, which are an important food source for various wildlife species. Its bark is thick and fissured, providing protection against pests and environmental stress.

Dendrophthora oocarpa plays a vital role in maintaining the ecological balance of its habitat.
