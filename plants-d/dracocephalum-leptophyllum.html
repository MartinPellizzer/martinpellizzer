Dracocephalum leptophyllum

Dracocephalum leptophyllum, commonly known as narrow-leaved blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant features slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, clustered in whorls, and typically have a blue or purple hue.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal medicine.
