Dicoma oblonga

Dicoma oblonga, commonly known as the narrowleaf dandelion, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellow composite flowers that bloom in spring, followed by fluffy seed heads. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Dicoma oblonga is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
