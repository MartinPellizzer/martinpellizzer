Dicksonia remota

Dicksonia remota, commonly known as the slender tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery fronds that can reach lengths of up to 3 meters. The plant has a distinctive crown of pinnate fronds that are often arching and drooping. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Dicksonia remota is valued for its ornamental appearance and is commonly cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
