Draba nemorosa f. foliosa

Draba nemorosa f. foliosa, commonly known as the leafy woodruff, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its low-growing habit and basal rosette of leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in a raceme, which bloom in early spring. Its leaves are typically broad and lobed, distinguishing it from other members of the species.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
