Decabelone × meintjesii

Decabelone × meintjesii, commonly known as the hybrid pittosporum, is a deciduous shrub native to New Zealand.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath, with a rough texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in early spring, followed by bright red berries that are edible but tart. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its unique appearance and seasonal color changes make it a standout in gardens and natural landscapes.
