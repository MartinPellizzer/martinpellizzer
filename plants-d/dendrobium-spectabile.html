Dendrobium spectabile

Dendrobium spectabile, commonly known as the showy dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, cane-like stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange, typically blooming during the winter months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium spectabile is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
