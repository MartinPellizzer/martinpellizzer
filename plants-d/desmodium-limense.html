Desmodium limense

Desmodium limense, commonly known as the velvet bean or velvetleaf, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which are often covered in a fine, velvety pubescence. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its fruit is a legume pod that contains several elliptical seeds.

Desmodium limense is native to tropical and subtropical regions and is often found in disturbed soils and agricultural areas.
