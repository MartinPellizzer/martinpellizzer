Desmodium rubiginosum

Desmodium rubiginosum, commonly known as the crimson boston ivy, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features heart-shaped, trifoliate leaves with a velvety texture and vibrant red stems. The plant produces small, yellow flowers followed by flat, bean-like pods containing edible seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Its rapid growth and nitrogen-fixing ability make it useful in ecological restoration projects.
