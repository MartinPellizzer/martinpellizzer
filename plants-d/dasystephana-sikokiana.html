Dasystephana sikokiana

Dasystephana sikokiana, commonly known as the African star orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, star-like flower structure with intricate petal arrangements. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories where it can receive indirect sunlight. Its flowers are usually a mix of white, yellow, and green, with a strong, sweet fragrance that attracts pollinators.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is highly sought after in horticultural collections.
