Dombeya coria

Dombeya coria, commonly known as the coral bush, is a deciduous shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has smooth, often reddish stems and lance-shaped leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Dombeya coria is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
