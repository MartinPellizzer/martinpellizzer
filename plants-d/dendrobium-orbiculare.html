Dendrobium orbiculare

Dendrobium orbiculare, commonly known as the orbiculate dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with cane-like stems that bear numerous ovate, fleshy leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive spurred lip. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium orbiculare is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
