Deschampsia alpina var. wibeliana

Deschampsia alpina var. wibeliana, commonly known as the alpine feathergrass, is a flowering plant native to mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is a perennial grass characterized by its slender, wiry stems and delicate, feathery inflorescences. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils and full sun. Its small, wind-pollinated flowers are usually pale green to golden in color.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in harsh, high-altitude habitats.
