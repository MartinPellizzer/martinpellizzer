Dendranthema shiwogiku var. kinokuniensis

Dendranthema shiwogiku var. kinokuniensis, commonly known as the Japanese chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 60 cm. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers bloom in the fall.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and is valued for its resilience and adaptability to different soil conditions.
