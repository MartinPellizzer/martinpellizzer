Dolichos multiflorus

Dolichos multiflorus, commonly known as the many-flowered dolichos, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features compound leaves with multiple pairs of oval-shaped leaflets and produces clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant bears long, slender pods that contain several elliptical seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

Dolichos multiflorus thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
