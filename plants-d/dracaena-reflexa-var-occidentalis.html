Dracaena reflexa var. occidentalis

Dracaena reflexa var. occidentalis, commonly known as the "Janet Craig" dracaena, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Africa.

It features long, dark green, strap-like leaves that grow in a compact, upright form. The plant is characterized by its ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor environments. Its leaves have a slightly waxy texture and can develop a reddish tinge at the base when exposed to bright light.

This variety is also known for its air-purifying qualities, contributing to improved indoor air quality.
