Dactylorhiza maculata subsp. deflexa

Dactylorhiza maculata subsp.

deflexa, commonly known as the spotted orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and marshes.

The plant features a single, erect stem with several oval, dark green leaves. Its flowers are pink or purple, with a distinctive white or yellowish spot on the lip.

It is a perennial species that blooms from late spring to early summer.
