Dicentra torulosa var. yunnanensis

Dicentra torulosa var. yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan bleeding heart, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It features delicate, heart-shaped leaves and striking, pendulous flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in mountainous regions. Its foliage is characterized by a distinctive waxy texture and a deep green color.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
