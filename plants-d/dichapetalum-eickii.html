Dichapetalum eickii

Dichapetalum eickii, commonly known as the Eickii starwood, is a striking evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and its distinctive star-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant features small, dark green leaves that are arranged in whorls around the stems, giving it a unique appearance. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, adding a delicate touch to its foliage.

Dichapetalum eickii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained, sunny conditions.
