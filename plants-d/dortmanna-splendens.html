Dortmanna splendens

Dortmanna splendens, commonly known as the splendour lichen, is a foliose lichen that thrives in moist, shaded environments.

It has a distinctive greenish-yellow to orange-brown thallus with a velvety texture. The plant forms a symbiotic relationship with algae and fungi, which contribute to its nutritional needs. It is often found on decaying wood or stone in temperate regions.

Its unique coloration and structure make it a notable species in ecological studies.
