Desmoscelis villosa var. gracillima

Desmoscelis villosa var. gracillima, commonly known as the slender hairy desmoscelis, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, hairy stems and small, pea-like flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or purple. The plant is native to parts of southern Africa and thrives in well-drained, sandy soils. It is often found in open woodlands and along rocky outcrops, where it receives ample sunlight.

Its foliage is lance-shaped and covered in fine, white hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
