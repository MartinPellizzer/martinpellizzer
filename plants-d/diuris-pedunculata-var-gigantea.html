Diuris pedunculata var. gigantea

Diuris pedunculata var. gigantea, commonly known as the giant spider orchid, is a striking terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, spider-like flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with prominent purple and red markings. The plant features a single, erect stem that bears several flowers, each with a distinctive, elongated labellum. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodland or heathland environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is a subject of interest in botanical studies due to its unique morphology and ecological adaptations.
