Dodartia orientalis

Dodartia orientalis, commonly known as the oriental dodartia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers in early summer, which are borne on slender, upright stems. Dodartia orientalis thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value lies in its attractive foliage and subtle floral display.
