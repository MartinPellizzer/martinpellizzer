Dendrobium vexillarius var. hansmeyerense

Dendrobium vexillarius var. hansmeyerense, commonly known as the Hansmeyer's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, upright growth habit with numerous cylindrical pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange, often in clusters along the stems. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with good air circulation and requires well-draining potting media.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.
