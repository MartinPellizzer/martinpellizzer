Deyeuxia Tianschanica

Tian shan grass, scientifically known as Deyeuxia tianschanica, is a unique plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It thrives in harsh, high-altitude environments where few other species can survive. This grass plays a crucial role in stabilizing soil and supporting local ecosystems. Its distinctive appearance and resilience make it an important subject of botanical study. Despite its hardy nature, it remains relatively unknown to the general public.
