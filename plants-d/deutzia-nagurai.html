Deutzia nagurai

Deutzia nagurai, commonly known as the Nagurai deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It features slender, arching branches that bear clusters of fragrant, white flowers in early summer. The plant has simple, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter underside. It typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and prefers well-drained soil in full sun to partial shade.

Deutzia nagurai is valued for its ornamental value in gardens and its ability to thrive in temperate climates.
