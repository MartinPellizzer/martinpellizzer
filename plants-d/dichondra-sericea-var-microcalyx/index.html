Dichondra Sericea Var. Microcalyx

Dichondra sericea var. microcalyx is a delicate, low-growing plant known for its soft, silvery foliage. It thrives in warm, sunny conditions and is often used as a ground cover in gardens. This variety is prized for its ornamental appeal and ability to spread easily. It prefers well-drained soil and can tolerate light foot traffic. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
