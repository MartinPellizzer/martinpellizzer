Dendrochilum lineare

Dendrochilum lineare, commonly known as the linear orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and linear, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically arranged in a raceme. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Dendrochilum lineare is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
