Dendranthema ornatum var. tokarense

Dendranthema ornatum var. tokarense, commonly known as the Tokarense Shiso, is a variant of the Chinese chrysanthemum native to Japan.

It is characterized by its finely serrated, aromatic leaves that are often used in culinary applications. The plant typically grows as an annual or biennial herb, reaching heights of up to 60 cm. Its flowers, which are usually white or pale yellow, bloom in late summer to early autumn.

The leaves are rich in essential oils and contain compounds that contribute to its distinctive flavor and medicinal properties.
