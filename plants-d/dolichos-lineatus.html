Dolichos lineatus

Dolichos lineatus, commonly known as the slender cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and trifoliate leaves with elongated leaflets. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that develop into elongated, bean-like pods containing edible seeds. Dolichos lineatus is valued for its nutritional content, including protein and essential minerals.

It also plays a role in soil enrichment due to its nitrogen-fixing ability.
