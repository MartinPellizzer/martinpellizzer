Drynaria undulata

Drynaria undulata, commonly known as the wavy fern, is a tropical evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its long, slender fronds that are often wavy or undulating in appearance. Drynaria undulata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
