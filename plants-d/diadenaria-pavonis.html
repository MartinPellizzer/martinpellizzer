Diadenaria pavonis

Diadenaria pavonis, commonly known as the velvetleaf orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with a pseudobulb and linear leaves. Its flowers are typically greenish-yellow with a distinctive veined structure.

It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
