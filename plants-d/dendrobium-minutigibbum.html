Dendrobium minutigibbum

Dendrobium minutigibbum, commonly known as the miniature gibbous dendrobium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a sympodial epiphyte, typically growing on trees or rocks in humid tropical environments. The plant features short, compact pseudobulbs with a few leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. Its flowers are often clustered in racemes and bloom during the summer months.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and resilience.
