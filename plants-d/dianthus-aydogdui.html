Dianthus aydogdui

Dianthus aydogdui, commonly known as the Aydogdu's carnation, is a species native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its showy, bilaterally symmetrical flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and prefers full sun exposure. Its flowers come in shades of pink, red, and white, with prominent white veins.

Dianthus aydogdui is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
