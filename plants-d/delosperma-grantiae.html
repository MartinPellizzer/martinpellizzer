Delosperma grantiae

Delosperma grantiae, commonly known as the Grant's ice plant, is a succulent perennial native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water storage and reducing transpiration. The plant produces vibrant pink to red flowers during the summer months, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it well-suited for arid and semi-arid climates.
