Dolichos seineri

Dolichos seineri, commonly known as the slender cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its climbing habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellow to orange flowers that are followed by elongated, pod-like fruits. Dolichos seineri is valued for its edible seeds, which are rich in protein and used in various cuisines.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it an important plant in sustainable agriculture.
