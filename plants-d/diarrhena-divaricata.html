Diarrhena divaricata

Diarrhena divaricata, commonly known as the variable broom, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, sprawling growth habit and bright yellow flowers. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and sandy environments.

Diarrhena divaricata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
