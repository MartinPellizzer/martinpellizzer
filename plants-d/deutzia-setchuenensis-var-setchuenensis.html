Deutzia setchuenensis var. setchuenensis

Deutzia setchuenensis var. setchuenensis, commonly known as the Chinese deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features clusters of fragrant, white to pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, arching branches and small, ovate leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate blossoms and compact growth habit.
