Dissotis lebrunii

Dissotis lebrunii, commonly known as the Madagascar spider-orchid, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, spider-like appearance due to its elongated, twisted flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually pale green or white, with a distinct, elongated spur.

Dissotis lebrunii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
