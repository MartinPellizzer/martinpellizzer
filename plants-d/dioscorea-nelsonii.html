Dioscorea nelsonii

Dioscorea nelsonii, commonly known as Nelson's yam, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Dioscoreaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its climbing habit and tuberous roots. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically arranged in clusters. Its tubers are not commonly cultivated for food but are sometimes used in traditional medicine.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological role in its native habitats.
