Dioscorea pennellii

Dioscorea pennellii, commonly known as the arrowhead vine, is a climbing perennial vine native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and yellowish flowers. The plant produces tubers that are often used in traditional medicine and as a food source. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade.

Its rapid growth and ornamental value make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
