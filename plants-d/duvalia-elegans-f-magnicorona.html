Duvalia elegans f. magnicorona

Duvalia elegans f. magnicorona, commonly known as the Magnificent Crown Duvalia, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a prominent, showy corona. The plant features strap-like, evergreen leaves that are broad and glossy, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for shaded garden settings.

This cultivar is prized for its unique floral display and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in horticulture.
