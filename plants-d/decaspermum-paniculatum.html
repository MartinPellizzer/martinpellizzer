Decaspermum paniculatum

Decaspermum paniculatum, commonly known as the yellow paniculata, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, yellow, and arranged in panicles, giving it a delicate, feathery appearance.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden use in suitable climates.
