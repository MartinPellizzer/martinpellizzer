Dieffenbachia rex

Dieffenbachia rex, commonly known as the Rex Begonia, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful, and deeply veined leaves that display shades of silver, green, and red. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions. Its foliage is often used in interior decoration due to its ornamental value.

Dieffenbachia rex requires moderate watering and well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
