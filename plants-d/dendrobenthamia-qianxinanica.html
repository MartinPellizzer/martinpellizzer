Dendrobenthamia qianxinanica

Dendrobenthamia qianxinanica, commonly known as the Qianxinan orchid, is a rare epiphytic orchid native to China.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its terrestrial or epiphytic growth habit. The plant typically features long, slender stems covered with fleshy, lance-shaped leaves. It produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, often clustered in inflorescences.
