Danthonia sericea

Danthonia sericea, commonly known as silky danthonia, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its fine, silky hair covering the foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and heathlands. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Danthonia sericea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
