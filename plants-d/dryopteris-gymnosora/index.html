Dryopteris Gymnosora

Dryopteris gymnosora is a rare and distinctive fern native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia. It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that display a unique pattern of sori and pinnae. This plant thrives in cool, humid environments and is often found in shaded forest understories. Its limited distribution makes it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Due to habitat loss, Dryopteris gymnosora faces threats to its survival, highlighting the need for further study and protection.
