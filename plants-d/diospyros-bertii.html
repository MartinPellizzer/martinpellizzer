Diospyros bertii

Diospyros bertii, commonly known as the African persimmon, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Ebenaceae family, which includes ebony and persimmon trees. The plant is characterized by its smooth, greyish-brown bark and oval-shaped leaves with a glossy upper surface. Its fruit is a small, yellowish-orange berry that ripens in late summer.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
