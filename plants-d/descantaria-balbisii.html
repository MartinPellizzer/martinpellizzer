Descantaria balbisii

Descantaria balbisii, commonly known as the Balbisii spider plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its long, arching stems and clusters of small, white or pale blue flowers. The plant produces tuberous roots that store nutrients, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. Its foliage consists of strap-shaped leaves that are often variegated with silver or white markings.

It is a popular ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
