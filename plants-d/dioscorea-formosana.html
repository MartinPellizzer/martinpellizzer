Dioscorea formosana

Dioscorea formosana, commonly known as the Formosan yam, is a species of vine native to Southeast Asia, particularly Taiwan.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its climbing habit and tuberous roots. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers and has heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive waxy texture. Its tubers are edible and have been traditionally used in local cuisines.

The plant is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
