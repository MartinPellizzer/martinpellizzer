Dipteracanthus confinis var. minor

Dipteracanthus confinis var. minor, commonly known as the lesser dipteracanthus, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, clustered inflorescences and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments within tropical forests. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white, which helps it blend with its surroundings.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
