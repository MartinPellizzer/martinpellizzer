Dolichos geminiflorus

Dolichos geminiflorus, commonly known as the twin-flower dolichos, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features compound leaves with multiple pairs of ovate to lanceolate leaflets. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that are followed by elongated, twisted pods. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Dolichos geminiflorus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
