Desmazeria marina var. syrtica

Desmazeria marina var. syrtica, commonly known as the Mediterranean sea holly, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its blue or violet flowers and succulent, fleshy leaves adapted to saline environments. The plant thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in shallow marine or brackish water areas. It plays a role in stabilizing coastal dunes and providing habitat for small marine organisms.

Its botanical features make it an important species in coastal ecology and a subject of study in marine botany.
