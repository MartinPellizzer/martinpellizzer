Daphne malyana

Daphne malyana, commonly known as the Malayan daphne, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Thymelaeaceae and is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form. Its leaves are glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to the colorful flowers.

Daphne malyana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic beauty.
