Dendrobium kunstleri

Dendrobium kunstleri, commonly known as the Kunstler's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a stem that can become woody over time. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Dendrobium kunstleri is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
