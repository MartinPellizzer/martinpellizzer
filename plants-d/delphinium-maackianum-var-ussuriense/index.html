Delphinium Maackianum Var. Ussuriense

Delphinium maackianum var. ussuriense is a striking member of the buttercup family known for its tall spires of vibrant blue and purple flowers. Native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia, this plant thrives in cool, moist environments with well-drained soil. Its delicate yet robust nature makes it a popular choice among gardeners seeking both beauty and resilience. The variety ussuriense is particularly noted for its hardiness and adaptability to various climates. This plant not only adds a dramatic flair to gardens but also plays an important role in supporting local pollinators.
