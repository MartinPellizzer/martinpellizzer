Dicliptera cordatibractea

Dicliptera cordatibractea, commonly known as the heart-shaped bract dicliptera, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and swampy areas. The plant features lanceolate leaves with a smooth texture and distinctive heart-shaped bracts that give it its common name. Its flowering stems bear small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in spike-like clusters.

The plant is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage.
