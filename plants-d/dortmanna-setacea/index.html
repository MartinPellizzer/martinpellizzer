Dortmanna Setacea

Dortmanna setacea is a unique and lesser-known plant species that thrives in specific environmental conditions. Despite its small size, Dortmanna setacea has significant ecological importance. Its study offers insights into plant adaptation and biodiversity in niche habitats.
