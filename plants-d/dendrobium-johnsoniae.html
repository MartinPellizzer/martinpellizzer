Dendrobium johnsoniae

Dendrobium johnsoniae, commonly known as the Johnson's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on the surface of trees or rocks without being parasitic. The plant produces long, cane-like stems that can reach several meters in length. Its flowers are typically yellow with red or brown markings, and they bloom in clusters during the warmer months.

Dendrobium johnsoniae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
