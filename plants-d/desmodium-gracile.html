Desmodium gracile

Desmodium gracile, commonly known as slender desmodium, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its slender, erect stems and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Desmodium gracile is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

It is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it beneficial in soil enrichment.
