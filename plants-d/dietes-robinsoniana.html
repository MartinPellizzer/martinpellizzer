Dietes robinsoniana

Dietes robinsoniana, commonly known as the Robinson's yellow daisy, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features bright yellow flowers with a distinctive central disk, surrounded by narrow, pointed petals. The plant has a robust root system and thrives in well-drained, sandy soils. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal areas.
