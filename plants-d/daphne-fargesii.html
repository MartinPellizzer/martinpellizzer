Daphne fargesii

Daphne fargesii, commonly known as the Chinese daphne, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features fragrant, showy flowers that bloom in early spring, typically in shades of pink and white. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its winter interest.

Daphne fargesii prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
