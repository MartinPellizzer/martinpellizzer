Dahlia tenuis

Dahlia tenuis, commonly known as the slender dahlia, is a herbaceous perennial native to Mexico.

It features showy, single or double flowers in a range of colors, including red, yellow, and orange. The plant has narrow, strap-like leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden borders.

Dahlia tenuis is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms.
