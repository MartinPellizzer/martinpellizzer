Dictyopteris heterosora

Dictyopteris heterosora, commonly known as the zigzag sea palm, is a species of brown algae belonging to the family Dictyopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its branched, filamentous thallus with a distinctive zigzag growth pattern. The plant typically grows in shallow, coastal waters, often attached to rocks or other substrates. Its structure consists of a central axis with lateral branches that form a dense, bushy appearance.

Dictyopteris heterosora plays a role in marine ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various marine organisms.
