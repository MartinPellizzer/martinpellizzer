Delphinium grandiflorum var. tenuicaule

Delphinium grandiflorum var. tenuicaule, commonly known as the slender stem delphinium, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features tall, slender stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height, supporting large, showy blue to purple flowers in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem, and its flowers are typically arranged in racemes. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden borders and rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its striking color and architectural form.
