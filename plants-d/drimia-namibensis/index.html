Drimia Namibensis

Drimia namibensis is a striking plant native to the arid regions of Namibia. It is known for its tall, spire-like flower stalks that bloom in shades of white and purple. This species thrives in rocky and sandy soils, adapting to harsh desert conditions. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and botanical collections. Despite its beauty, it remains a rare and lesser-known member of the Amaryllidaceae family.
