Dendrobium speciosum subsp. pedunculatum

Dendrobium speciosum subsp.

pedunculatum, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a sympodial epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia. It features tall, branched stems covered with elliptical, glossy leaves.

The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with a red or purple lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its horticultural value lies in its ornamental flowers and adaptability to cultivation.
