Dactylorhiza × variabilis

Dactylorhiza × variabilis, commonly known as the variable orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a rhizomatous root system and produces erect, branching stems. The plant features lanceolate, dark green leaves and bears showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink, purple, and white. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and wet meadows.

Dactylorhiza × variabilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens.
