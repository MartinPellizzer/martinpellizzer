Dendrobium polyschistum var. graminiforme

Dendrobium polyschistum var. graminiforme, commonly known as the grass-leaf dendrobium, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. The plant is an epiphyte, typically growing on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. It has a long flowering period, often blooming multiple times a year.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation.
