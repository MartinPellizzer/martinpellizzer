Dolichos striatus

Dolichos striatus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, compound leaves and small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant produces elongated, hairy pods that contain several seeds. It is often cultivated for its edible seeds, which are rich in protein.

Dolichos striatus is also valued for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, benefiting soil fertility.
