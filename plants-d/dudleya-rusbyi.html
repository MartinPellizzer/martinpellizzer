Dudleya rusbyi

Dudleya rusbyi, commonly known as Rusby's liveforever, is a succulent plant native to California.

It is characterized by its rosette of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Dudleya rusbyi is often cultivated in rock gardens and xeriscapes due to its hardy nature and ornamental appeal.
