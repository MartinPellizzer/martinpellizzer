Dendrochilum longipedicellatum

Dendrochilum longipedicellatum, commonly known as the long-stemmed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its terrestrial growth habit and striking, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces elongated pseudobulbs that support its foliage and floral structures. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white with intricate patterns, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.

Dendrochilum longipedicellatum thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
