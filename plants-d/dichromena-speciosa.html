Dichromena speciosa

Dichromena speciosa, commonly known as the showy dichromena, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a glossy appearance.

Dichromena speciosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual interest.
