Dieffenbachia olbia

Dieffenbachia olbia, commonly known as the Snowflake Dieffenbachia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, waxy, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically green with white or cream-colored veins. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity to flourish. Its foliage is evergreen, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Dieffenbachia olbia is known for its ability to purify the air, adding both aesthetic and health benefits to its surroundings.
