Daphniphyllum oldhamii

Daphniphyllum oldhamii, commonly known as the oldham's laurel, is a species of evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant belongs to the family Ericaceae and is known for its ability to thrive in shaded environments. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
