Discaria foliosa

Discaria foliosa, commonly known as the silver dollar tree, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features dense, glossy, dark green leaves that are oval in shape and often have a slight waxy coating. Its small, white flowers appear in clusters and are followed by round, yellow fruits that resemble silver dollars.

The plant is well adapted to arid conditions and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
