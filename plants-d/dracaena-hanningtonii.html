Dracaena hanningtonii

Dracaena hanningtonii, commonly known as the Hannington's dracaena, is a tropical plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are typically bright green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light.

Dracaena hanningtonii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
