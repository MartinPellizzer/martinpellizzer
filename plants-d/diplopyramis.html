Diplopyramis

Diplopyramis, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to Africa.

These plants are characterized by their rosette-like growth habit and showy, pentagonal-shaped flowers. The leaves are typically velvety and heart-shaped, with a deep green coloration. Diplopyramis species are evergreen and thrive in moist, well-drained soil.

They are popular houseplants due to their ornamental value and ability to bloom throughout the year.
