Digitaria brevifolia

Digitaria brevifolia, commonly known as slender digitgrass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a perennial plant characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Digitaria brevifolia is valued for its ability to stabilize soil and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
