Dryades Concinna

Dryades concinna is a small, delicate flowering plant native to the southeastern United States. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Known for its pale yellow blooms, it adds a subtle beauty to its natural habitat. Despite its modest appearance, it plays an important role in local ecosystems. This plant is often overlooked but deserves recognition for its ecological value.
