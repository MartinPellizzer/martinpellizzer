Draba franchetii

Draba franchetii, commonly known as the alpine draba, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbages, and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers. This species typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in cool, high-altitude conditions.
