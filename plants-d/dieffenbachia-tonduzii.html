Dieffenbachia tonduzii

Dieffenbachia tonduzii, commonly known as the Tonduzii Dieffenbachia, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that display shades of white, green, and yellow. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other popular houseplants like peace lilies. Its foliage is often adorned with intricate patterns that make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Dieffenbachia tonduzii thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity to flourish.
