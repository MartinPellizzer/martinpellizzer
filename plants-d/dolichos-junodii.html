Dolichos junodii

Dolichos junodii, commonly known as the African burr bean, is a climbing legume native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that develop into spiny, pod-like fruits. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in arid and semi-arid environments, making it adaptable to harsh conditions. Its seeds are used in traditional medicine and as a food source in some regions.

The botanical features of Dolichos junodii make it an important species in ecological and agricultural contexts.
