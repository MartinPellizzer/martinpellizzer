Draba verna var. australis

Draba verna var. australis, commonly known as the Australian spring beauty, is a small, herbaceous plant native to southern Australia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, cruciform flowers and capsule fruits. This variant is distinguished by its pale yellow to white flowers and hairy, basal leaves. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils in open habitats.

The plant is adapted to temperate climates and blooms during the spring season.
