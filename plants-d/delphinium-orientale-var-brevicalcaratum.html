Delphinium orientale var. brevicalcaratum

Delphinium orientale var. brevicalcaratum, commonly known as the short-spurred delphinium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It features tall, slender stems that bear clusters of striking blue to purple flowers. The leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem. Its flowers have short, spurred petals that distinguish it from other delphinium species.

This plant is valued in gardens for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms.
