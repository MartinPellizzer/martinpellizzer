Disa patula var. transvaalensis

Disa patula var. transvaalensis, commonly known as the Transvaal orchid, is a striking South African orchid species characterized by its vibrant violet to purple flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to the grassland regions of South Africa, particularly in the Free State and Mpumalanga provinces. The plant typically grows from a rhizomatous stem and produces erect, branched inflorescences with numerous showy flowers. Its botanical features include lanceolate leaves and a delicate, fragrant bloom that attracts various pollinators.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
