Draba stellata

Draba stellata, commonly known as starless whitlow grass, is a small flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, blooming in early spring. Draba stellata is part of the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with similar floral structures.
