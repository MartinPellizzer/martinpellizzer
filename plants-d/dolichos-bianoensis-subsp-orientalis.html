Dolichos bianoensis subsp. orientalis

Dolichos bianoensis subsp.

orientalis, commonly known as the oriental cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its erect growth habit and trifoliate leaves with hairy surfaces.

The plant produces elongated pods that contain edible seeds, which are rich in protein and often used in various cuisines. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun for optimal growth.

This subspecies is valued both for its agricultural utility and ornamental appeal.
