Draba hirta var. prozorovskii

Draba hirta var. prozorovskii, commonly known as the prozorovskii mouse-ear cress, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, cruciform flowers and capsule fruits. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils. It has hairy leaves and stems, which help reduce water loss in arid conditions.

This variety is noted for its compact growth habit and is often found in mountainous regions.
