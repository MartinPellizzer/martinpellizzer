Delphinium speciosum var. osseticum

Delphinium speciosum var. osseticum, commonly known as the Ossetian larkspur, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems and showy, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and is typically found in meadows and rocky areas. It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in some regions.
