Dolichos ellipticus

Dolichos ellipticus, commonly known as the broadleaf cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its elliptical, broad leaves and produces long, slender pods that contain edible seeds. The plant is often cultivated for its nutritional value, as the seeds are a good source of protein. It thrives in warm climates and is adaptable to various soil types.

Dolichos ellipticus is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, improving soil fertility.
