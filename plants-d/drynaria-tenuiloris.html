Drynaria tenuiloris

Drynaria tenuiloris, commonly known as the slender tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk covered in a dense layer of persistent, fibrous roots. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
