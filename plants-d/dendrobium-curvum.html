Dendrobium curvum

Dendrobium curvum, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with cane-like stems that often curl and twist, giving it its common name. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with small, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. Its leaves are fleshy and arranged in a spiral, adapting to its climbing lifestyle.

Dendrobium curvum thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.
