Disporum cantoniense var. cantoniense

Disporum cantoniense var. cantoniense, commonly known as the Chinese threadleaf, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It features slender, upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with white or blue at the base. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters during the spring or early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique addition to botanical gardens and shade gardens.
