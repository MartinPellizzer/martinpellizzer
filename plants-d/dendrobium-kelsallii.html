Dendrobium kelsallii

Dendrobium kelsallii, commonly known as the Kelsall's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a tall, cane-like stem with numerous nodes and is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically produces white or pale yellow blooms with a subtle pink or red tinge, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Dendrobium kelsallii is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
