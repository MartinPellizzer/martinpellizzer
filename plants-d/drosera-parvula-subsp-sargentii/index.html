Drosera Parvula Subsp. Sargentii

Drosera parvula subsp. sargentii is a small, carnivorous plant native to the wetlands of southeastern Australia. It is known for its delicate, sticky leaves that trap and digest insects. This subspecies is often found in swampy areas where it thrives in moist, nutrient-poor soil. Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice among succulent and carnivorous plant enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it is relatively rare and requires specific care to grow successfully.
