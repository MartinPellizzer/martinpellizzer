Disperis bolusiana subsp. bolusiana

Disperis bolusiana subsp.

bolusiana, commonly known as the Bolus orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers.

The plant typically produces pale yellow or greenish-white blooms with a distinctive, spurred lip. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
