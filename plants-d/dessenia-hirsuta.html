Dessenia hirsuta

Dessenia hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy dessenia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Malvaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its hairy, glandular stems and leaves. Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
