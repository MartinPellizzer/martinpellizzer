Dianthus carthusianorum var. balbiosii

Dianthus carthusianorum var. balbiosii, commonly known as the Balbiosii carnation, is a striking perennial plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It features dense, velvety foliage with deep green leaves that are often covered in a fine, white down. The plant produces showy, double-petaled flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a desirable addition to both garden and horticultural collections.
