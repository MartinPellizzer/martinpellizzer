Delosperma davyi

Delosperma davyi, commonly known as the Cape gooseberry or ozymandias, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems and small, star-shaped flowers. The plant produces bright orange to red berries that are edible and often used in culinary applications. Delosperma davyi is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators.
