Dubautia hanaulaensis

Dubautia hanaulaensis, commonly known as Hanaula false heather, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

The plant typically blooms with pink to red flowers, which attract various pollinators such as bees and birds. Dubautia hanaulaensis is often found in rocky soils and coastal areas, thriving in the unique microclimates of Hawaii.
