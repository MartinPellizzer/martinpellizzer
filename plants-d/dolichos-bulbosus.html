Dolichos bulbosus

Dolichos bulbosus, commonly known as the bulbous cowpea, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant bears cylindrical, fleshy pods that contain several seeds, which are often used as a food source. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun for optimal growth.

Dolichos bulbosus is valued for its nutritional content and is cultivated in various parts of Asia and Africa.
