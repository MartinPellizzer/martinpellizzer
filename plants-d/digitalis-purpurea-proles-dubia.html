Digitalis purpurea proles dubia

Digitalis purpurea proles dubia, commonly known as the doubtful purple foxglove, is a rare and lesser-known variant of the foxglove genus.

It is characterized by its striking purple flowers, which are typically tubular and arranged in a spike.
