Dendrobium nangajuense

Dendrobium nangajuense, commonly known as the Nangajuense orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Dendrobium genus, which includes over 1,200 species of orchids. This plant typically grows on trees or rocks in tropical and subtropical regions, utilizing its aerial roots to absorb moisture and nutrients. Its flowers are usually fragrant and come in a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and pink.

The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
