Deverra scoparia

Deverra scoparia, commonly known as goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in a panicle. It is often found in coastal areas and can tolerate saline conditions.

Deverra scoparia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
