Duroia fusifera

Duroia fusifera, commonly known as the sword fern, is a species of evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves that are often arranged in a rosette formation. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by red or orange berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Duroia fusifera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
