Dichrostachys nutans var. setulosa

Dichrostachys nutans var. setulosa, commonly known as the spreading crownflower, is a shrub native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The leaves are typically opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or along roadsides.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand drought conditions.
