Dendrochilum viride

Dendrochilum viride, commonly known as the green dendrochilum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and the presence of long, strap-like leaves. The plant produces striking greenish-yellow flowers that are often adorned with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrochilum viride is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
