Desmonema uviforme

Desmonema uviforme, commonly known as the uvula plant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its unique, elongated flower heads that resemble a uvula. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a rough texture.

The flowers are yellow and are followed by small, dry fruits known as achenes.
