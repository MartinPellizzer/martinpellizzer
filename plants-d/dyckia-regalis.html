Dyckia regalis

Dyckia regalis, commonly known as the royal dyckia, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are covered in dense, fibrous, and often colorful trichomes. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences that bear small, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Dyckia regalis is popular as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
