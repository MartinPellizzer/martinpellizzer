Delphinium fissum subsp. caseyi

Delphinium fissum subsp.

caseyi, commonly known as the Sierra delphinium, is a flowering plant native to the western United States. It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height.

This subspecies is adapted to high-altitude environments and thrives in well-drained, rocky soils.
