Dudleya brauntonii

Dudleya brauntonii, commonly known as the Santa Barbara Dudleya, is a succulent plant native to California.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces tall, branched stalks with clusters of yellow flowers during the spring. It is well adapted to rocky, well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant.

Dudleya brauntonii is a popular choice for rock gardens due to its ornamental appearance and low maintenance requirements.
