Dendrobium sulphureum var. rigidifolium

Dendrobium sulphureum var. rigidifolium, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, cane-like stems and vibrant yellow to orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates with bright, indirect light. Its foliage consists of stiff, narrow leaves that help it store water and nutrients.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its showy, long-lasting blooms.
