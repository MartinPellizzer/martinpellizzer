Dendrochloa distans

Dendrochloa distans, commonly known as the slender bamboo, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, which are typical of grasses. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a fibrous structure that makes it suitable for various industrial uses.

Dendrochloa distans is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of sustainable materials.
