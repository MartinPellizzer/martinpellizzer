Dieffenbachia seguine f. irrorata

Dieffenbachia seguine f. irrorata, commonly known as the Shiny-leaf Dieffenbachia, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, oval-shaped leaves with a striking white or cream-colored margin. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions. Its foliage is often used in interior decorating due to its aesthetic appeal.

It requires moderate watering and well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
