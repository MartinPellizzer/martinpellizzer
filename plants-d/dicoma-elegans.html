Dicoma elegans

Dicoma elegans, commonly known as the elegant dicoma, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
