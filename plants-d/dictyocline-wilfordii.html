Dictyocline wilfordii

Dictyocline wilfordii, commonly known as the Wilford's daisy.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. The plant features basal rosettes of narrow, linear leaves and produces yellow flowers with a distinctive, flattened shape. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils at high altitudes.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
