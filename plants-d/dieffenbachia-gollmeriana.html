Dieffenbachia gollmeriana

Dieffenbachia gollmeriana, commonly known as the Gollmer's Dieffenbachia, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that are typically green with white or cream-colored veins. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other popular houseplants like peace lilies. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity to flourish.

Its striking foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
