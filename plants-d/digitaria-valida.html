Digitaria valida

Digitaria valida, commonly known as the valid grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences.
