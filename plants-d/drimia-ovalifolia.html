Drimia ovalifolia

Drimia ovalifolia, commonly known as the African lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of white or pale pink flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has broad, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Drimia ovalifolia is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
