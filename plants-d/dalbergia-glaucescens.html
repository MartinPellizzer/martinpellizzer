Dalbergia glaucescens

Dalbergia glaucescens, commonly known as the greenheart tree, is a species of legume native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and sturdy, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers followed by pods containing flattened, dark seeds. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland tropical forests.

Dalbergia glaucescens is valued for its hard, durable wood, which is used in furniture and construction.
