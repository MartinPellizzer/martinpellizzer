Desmodium natalitium

Desmodium natalitium, commonly known as Natal bean or Natal ticktrefoil, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its trifoliate leaves are compound, with three oval-shaped leaflets.

Desmodium natalitium is often found in grasslands and disturbed soils, and it is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
