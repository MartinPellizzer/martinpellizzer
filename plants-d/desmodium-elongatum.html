Desmodium elongatum

Desmodium elongatum, commonly known as the slender burr legume, is a perennial herb native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces elongated, spiny pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas or along roadsides.

Desmodium elongatum is sometimes used in traditional medicine and as a forage crop for livestock.
