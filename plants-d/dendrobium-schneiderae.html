Dendrobium schneiderae

Dendrobium schneiderae, commonly known as the Schneider's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with a dense arrangement of leaves and racemes of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically blooms in summer, producing white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium schneiderae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its beauty in both tropical and subtropical gardens.
