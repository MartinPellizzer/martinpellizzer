Dicranostyles ampla var. ampla

Dicranostyles ampla var. ampla, commonly known as the broad-leaved dicranostyles, is a species of moss belonging to the family Dicranaceae.

It is characterized by its elongated, broad leaves that are typically curved and have a smooth, glossy surface. The plant forms dense, cushion-like mats and thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its reproductive structures, including capsules, are borne on short, erect stalks, and the species is often found in temperate regions.

Dicranostyles ampla var. ampla plays a role in soil stabilization and provides microhabitats for various invertebrates.
