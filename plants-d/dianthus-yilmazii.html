Dianthus yilmazii

Dianthus yilmazii, commonly known as the Turkish carnation, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant features upright stems and produces clusters of small, rounded flowers with frilly petals. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Dianthus yilmazii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticultural displays.
