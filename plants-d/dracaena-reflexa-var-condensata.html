Dracaena reflexa var. condensata

Dracaena reflexa var. condensata, commonly known as the "Janet Craig" dracaena, is a striking evergreen plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features long, dark green, strap-like leaves that grow in a dense, upright cluster, giving it a lush, bushy appearance. The plant is characterized by its ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor environments. Its foliage is often adorned with subtle reddish veins, adding visual interest.

Dracaena reflexa var. condensata is also noted for its air-purifying qualities, contributing to improved indoor air quality.
