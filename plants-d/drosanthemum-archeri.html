Drosanthemum archeri

Drosanthemum archeri, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It has hairy, succulent leaves that help it retain moisture in arid conditions.

Drosanthemum archeri is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and drought tolerance.
