Dendrobium xantholeucum var. obtusilobum

Dendrobium xantholeucum var. obtusilobum, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, cane-like stems that often produce clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. The leaves are broad and ovate, with a smooth surface and a waxy texture. This variety thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

It is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
