Dicoma pretoriensis

Dicoma pretoriensis, commonly known as the Pretoria daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, showy blooms during the spring and summer months.

Dicoma pretoriensis is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.
