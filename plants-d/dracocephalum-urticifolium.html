Dracocephalum urticifolium

Dracocephalum urticifolium, commonly known as blueweed, is a flowering plant native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a hairy texture, often with a slightly serrated edge. This plant is part of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs.

It is often found in dry meadows and grasslands, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
