Draba streptobrachia

Draba streptobrachia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved whitlow grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit, with small, basal leaves and clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. Draba streptobrachia is also notable for its role in stabilizing soil in its native habitats.
