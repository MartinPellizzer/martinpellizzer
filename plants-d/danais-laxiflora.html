Danais laxiflora

Danais laxiflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered danais, is a herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish to orange flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Danais laxiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
