Dendrobium asumburu

Dendrobium asumburu, commonly known as the Asumburu orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to East Africa.

It features a sympodial growth habit with elongated, leafy pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces inflorescences bearing numerous showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often arranged in two rows along the pseudobulbs.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
