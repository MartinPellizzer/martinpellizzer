Daphne genkwa var. fortunei

Daphne genkwa var. fortunei, commonly known as Chinese daphne, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces small, fleshy berries that are often red or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Daphne genkwa var. fortunei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens.
