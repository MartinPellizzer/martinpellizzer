Dendrobium brevilabium

Dendrobium brevilabium, commonly known as the short-lipped dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with cane-like stems that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with short, rounded lips. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for both horticultural and conservation purposes.
