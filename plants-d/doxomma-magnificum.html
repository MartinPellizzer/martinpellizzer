Doxomma magnificum

Doxomma magnificum, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It features showy, vibrant orange and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its colorful blooms. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil.

Doxomma magnificum is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
