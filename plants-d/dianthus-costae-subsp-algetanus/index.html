Dianthus Costae Subsp. Algetanus

Alpine campion, scientifically known as Dianthus costae subsp. algetanus, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Alps. It thrives in harsh, rocky environments where few other plants can survive, showcasing its resilience and adaptability. With its vibrant pink or red petals and delicate, star-shaped flowers, it adds a splash of color to the otherwise stark mountain landscape. This subspecies is often found in meadows and alpine tundras, where it plays an important role in local ecosystems. Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it a fascinating subject for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike.
