Dichapetalum spruceanum

Dichapetalum spruceanum, commonly known as the African star-of-Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb with striking, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has a short, sturdy stem and lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
