Drypetes serrulata

Drypetes serrulata, commonly known as the serrated drypetes, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its simple, opposite, and often serrated leaves. Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in horticultural settings.
