Dendrobium bilamellatum

Dendrobium bilamellatum, commonly known as the two-lip orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, leafy stems and clusters of fragrant, pale yellow to greenish-white flowers. The plant has two prominent, fleshy lips on its flowers, which are a distinguishing feature. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Dendrobium bilamellatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
