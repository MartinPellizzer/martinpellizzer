Digitaria endlichii

Digitaria endlichii, commonly known as slender fingergrass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that contain both male and female flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open areas such as fields and disturbed sites.

Digitaria endlichii is adapted to warm climates and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
