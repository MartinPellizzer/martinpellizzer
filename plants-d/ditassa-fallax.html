Ditassa fallax

Ditassa fallax, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. Ditassa fallax is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
