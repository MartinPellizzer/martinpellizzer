Dendrobium thyrsiflorum var. walkerianum

Dendrobium thyrsiflorum var. walkerianum, commonly known as the Walker's spike orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in humid tropical environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are typically yellow or green with red or purple markings, forming dense spikes.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
