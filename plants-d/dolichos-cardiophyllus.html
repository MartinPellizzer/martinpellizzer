Dolichos cardiophyllus

Dolichos cardiophyllus, commonly known as the heart-leaf dolichos, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Asia.

It features compound leaves with a distinctive heart-shaped leaflet, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by elongated, slender pods containing kidney-shaped seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine.

The species is also valued as a forage crop for livestock in some parts of its range.
