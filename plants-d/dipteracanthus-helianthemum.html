Dipteracanthus helianthemum

Dipteracanthus helianthemum, commonly known as the sun rose, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are succulent and fleshy, adapted to store water in arid environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
