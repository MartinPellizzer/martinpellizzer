Deutzia faberi

Deutzia faberi, commonly known as the Chinese deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features attractive, fragrant white to pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, arching branches and dark green, ovate leaves that provide ornamental value throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Deutzia faberi is often used in gardens for its ornamental beauty and adaptability to various climates.
