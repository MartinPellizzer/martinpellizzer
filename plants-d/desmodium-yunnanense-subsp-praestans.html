Desmodium yunnanense subsp. praestans

Desmodium yunnanense subsp.

praestans, commonly known as the Yunnan velvet bean, is a flowering plant native to China. It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its legume pods and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant features trifoliate leaves with small, rounded leaflets and produces small, pea-like flowers in shades of pink or white.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests and is valued for its ornamental and potential medicinal properties.
