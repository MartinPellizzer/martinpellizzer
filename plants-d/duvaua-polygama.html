Duvaua polygama

Duvaua polygama, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has a fibrous root system and thrives in well-drained soils. Its leaves are compound, with multiple leaflets arranged in an alternate pattern.

Duvaua polygama is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.
