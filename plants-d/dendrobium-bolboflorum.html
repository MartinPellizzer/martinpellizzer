Dendrobium bolboflorum

Dendrobium bolboflorum, commonly known as the Bolboflorum orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-draining soil.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and role in local ecosystems.
