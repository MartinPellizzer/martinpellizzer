Dendrobium × euosmum

Dendrobium × euosmum, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a fascinating plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is a cross between Dendrobium nobile and Dendrobium phalaenopsis, resulting in a unique combination of traits from both parents. This hybrid is characterized by its epiphytic growth habit, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic. Its flowers are typically showy, with a range of colors including white, pink, and yellow, making it popular in horticulture.

The plant requires specific care, including bright indirect light and well-draining potting media, to thrive.
