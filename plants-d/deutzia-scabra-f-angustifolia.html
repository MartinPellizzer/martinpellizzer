Deutzia scabra f. angustifolia

Deutzia scabra f. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features slender, arching stems with small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The leaves are elliptical, serrated, and have a slightly rough texture. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature.
