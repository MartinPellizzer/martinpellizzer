Dendrobium robertsii

Dendrobium robertsii, commonly known as the Roberts' orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, cane-like pseudobulbs and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically produces white or pale yellow blooms with a distinctive, intricate pattern on the petals and lip. It thrives in humid, well-lit environments and requires good air circulation.

Dendrobium robertsii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
