Dimerocostus tessmannii

Dimerocostus tessmannii, commonly known as the African dandelion, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

Dimerocostus tessmannii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some African cultures.
