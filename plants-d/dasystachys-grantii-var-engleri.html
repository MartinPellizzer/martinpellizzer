Dasystachys grantii var. engleri

Dasystachys grantii var. engleri, commonly known as the Engler's flame grass, is a striking ornamental plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and vibrant, flame-colored inflorescences that bloom in summer. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping and rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for landscaping in arid regions.
