Desmoncus horridus subsp. horridus

Desmoncus horridus subsp.

horridus, commonly known as the rough desmoncus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grassland and rocky habitats.

The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a rough, hairy texture. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense inflorescences, often in shades of yellow or orange.

It is known for its resilience to dry conditions and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
