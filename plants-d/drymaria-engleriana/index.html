Drymaria Engleriana

Drymaria engleriana is a small, flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of South America. It is known for its delicate, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas with high salinity. Its compact growth habit and unique appearance make it a striking addition to dryland gardens. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively obscure species in the botanical world.
