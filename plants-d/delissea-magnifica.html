Delissea magnifica

Delissea magnifica, commonly known as the grand magnolia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 30 centimeters in diameter, typically displaying vibrant shades of pink and white. The plant features broad, glossy leaves that provide a lush green backdrop to its ornamental blooms. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Delissea magnifica is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
