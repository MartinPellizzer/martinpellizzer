Dresslerothamnus angustiradiatus

Dresslerothamnus angustiradiatus, commonly known as the narrow-rayed thornbush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showing resilience to drought conditions. Its growth habit includes thorny stems, which provide protection against herbivory.

The species is of interest in botanical studies due to its unique morphology and ecological adaptations.
