Diasperus Rupestris

Diasperus rupestris is a unique and resilient plant species known for its ability to thrive in harsh, rocky environments. This succulent is native to arid regions and is often found growing on cliffs or in crevices where water is scarce. Its compact form and thick, fleshy leaves help it store moisture efficiently. The plant's striking appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping. Despite its hardiness, Diasperus rupestris requires specific care to ensure its survival in cultivation.
