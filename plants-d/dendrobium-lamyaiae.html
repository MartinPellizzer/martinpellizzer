Dendrobium lamyaiae

Dendrobium lamyaiae, commonly known as the Lamyaiae orchid, is a rare and exquisite epiphytic orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Dendrobium genus, which is part of the orchid family, Orchidaceae. This plant is characterized by its slender, cane-like pseudobulbs and clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and requires specific care to flourish.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly valued in horticultural circles.
