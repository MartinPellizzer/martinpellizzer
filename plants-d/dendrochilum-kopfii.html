Dendrochilum kopfii

Dendrochilum kopfii, commonly known as the Kopf's tree orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, pale yellow to greenish flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the surrounding soil.

Dendrochilum kopfii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
