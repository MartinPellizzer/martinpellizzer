Digitacalia jatrophoides

Digitacalia jatrophoides, commonly known as the hairy nightshade, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 meter. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often in late summer. Its leaves are palmately lobed and covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
