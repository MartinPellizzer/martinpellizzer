Dioscorea schliebenii

Dioscorea schliebenii, commonly known as Schlieben's yam, is a tropical vine native to regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Dioscoreaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and tuberous roots. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Its tubers are often used in traditional medicine and as a food source in some cultures.

The botanical features of D. schliebenii make it an important species in both ecological and agricultural contexts.
