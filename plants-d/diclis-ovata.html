Diclis ovata

Diclis ovata, commonly known as the oval leaf diclis, is a small evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes mahogany and related trees. The plant is characterized by its simple, ovate leaves that are often glossy and arranged alternately along the stems. Diclis ovata is often found in moist, shaded areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
