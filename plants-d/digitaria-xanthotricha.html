Digitaria xanthotricha

Digitaria xanthotricha, commonly known as yellow-haired grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its fine, wiry leaves and compact, cylindrical inflorescence. The plant produces small, pale yellow to golden brown florets, which contribute to its ornamental appeal. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Digitaria xanthotricha is valued in landscaping for its drought tolerance and ability to form dense turf.
