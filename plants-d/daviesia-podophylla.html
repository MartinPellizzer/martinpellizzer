Daviesia podophylla

Daviesia podophylla, commonly known as the yellow Daviesia, is a shrub native to the southern regions of Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers that appear in late winter to early spring. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in a fine, silky hair. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and woodland areas.

Daviesia podophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
