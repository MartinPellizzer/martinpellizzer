Dichapetalum olivaceum

Dichapetalum olivaceum, commonly known as the oliveleaf tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are often elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically borne on short pedicels. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

Dichapetalum olivaceum is often found in moist, shaded habitats and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
