Delphinium yongdengense

Delphinium yongdengense, commonly known as the Yongdeng larkspur, is a perennial herb native to the Qinghai-Tibet Plateau in China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems and showy blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky slopes, adapting to the harsh environmental conditions of its high-altitude habitat. Its leaves are deeply lobed and arranged alternately along the stem, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Delphinium yongdengense is valued for its ornamental value and is also studied for its potential medicinal properties.
