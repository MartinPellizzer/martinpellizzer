Dryopteris miqueliana

Dryopteris miqueliana, commonly known as the Miquel's wood fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its evergreen fronds that remain on the plant throughout the year. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its fronds are usually leathery and have a distinctive, intricate pattern of pinnae.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in humid, shaded conditions.
