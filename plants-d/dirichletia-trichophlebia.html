Dirichletia trichophlebia

Dirichletia trichophlebia, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. It produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences.

The species is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and along riverbanks.
