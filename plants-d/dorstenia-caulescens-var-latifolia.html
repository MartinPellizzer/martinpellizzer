Dorstenia caulescens var. latifolia

Dorstenia caulescens var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved dwarf banana, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit, with thick, fleshy stems that store water, adapting it to arid environments. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences, and its leaves are broad and often have a waxy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and resilience, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
