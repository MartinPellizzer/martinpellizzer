Diplotaxis harra var. maroccana

Diplotaxis harra var. maroccana, commonly known as Moroccan wallflower, is a flowering plant native to North Africa.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, bright yellow or orange flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid soils and is adapted to withstand dry conditions. It features a low-growing habit with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves.
