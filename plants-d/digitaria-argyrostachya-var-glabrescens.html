Digitaria argyrostachya var. glabrescens

Digitaria argyrostachya var. glabrescens, commonly known as silver bluestem, is a perennial grass native to the central and southern United States.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and dense, upright growth habit. The plant typically grows in full sun and is adapted to dry, well-drained soils. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are often tinged with silver or white.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
