Diplotaxis siifolia var. hispidissima

Diplotaxis siifolia var. hispidissima, commonly known as the hispid dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, hairy stems and lobed, serrated leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that develop into fluffy, white seed heads. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

This species is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
