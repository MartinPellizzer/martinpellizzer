Dahlia scapigera var. arsenei

Dahlia scapigera var. arsenei, commonly known as the Arseni's dahlia, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to Mexico.

It features showy, single or semi-double flowers with vibrant hues of red, orange, and yellow, often with a distinctive orange center. The plant has sturdy, upright stems and lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are slightly hairy. It blooms from late summer to early autumn, producing long-lasting flowers that attract pollinators.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and adaptability to garden conditions.
