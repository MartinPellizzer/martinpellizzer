Deutzia crenata var. pubescens

Deutzia crenata var. pubescens, commonly known as the woolly deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in early summer, adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are typically oval to heart-shaped. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for a variety of garden settings.

Its compact growth habit and showy blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
