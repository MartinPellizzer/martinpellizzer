Dolichos blandus

Dolichos blandus, commonly known as the slender cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features trifoliate leaves with small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces elongated, slender pods that contain small, edible seeds. It is often grown as a cover crop due to its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.

Dolichos blandus is also valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions.
