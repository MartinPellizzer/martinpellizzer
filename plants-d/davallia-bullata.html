Davallia bullata

Davallia bullata, commonly known as the Boston fern, is a tropical fern native to Southeast Asia.

It features elegant, arching fronds that are dark green and glossy, with a feathery appearance. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread and form dense clumps. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires regular watering and well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
