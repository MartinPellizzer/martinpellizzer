Diasperus jardinii

Diasperus jardinii, commonly known as the Cape Flats daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy, well-drained soils. It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces yellow or orange flower heads.

Diasperus jardinii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
