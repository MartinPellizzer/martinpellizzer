Deutzia crenata var. formosa

Deutzia crenata var. formosa, commonly known as the Chinese Deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features clusters of fragrant, white or pink flowers that bloom in early summer, adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant has slender, arching stems and heart-shaped leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and well-drained soil, making it adaptable to various garden conditions.

Its compact growth habit and showy blooms make it a popular choice for borders and woodland gardens.
