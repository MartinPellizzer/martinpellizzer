Dunbaria villosa

Dunbaria villosa, commonly known as the hairy dunbaria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, hairy stems and leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, often during the spring and summer seasons. Dunbaria villosa thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in arid environments.
