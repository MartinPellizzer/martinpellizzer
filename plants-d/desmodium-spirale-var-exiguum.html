Desmodium spirale var. exiguum

Desmodium spirale var. exiguum, commonly known as the small spiraled desmodium, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, spiraled leaves that give it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
