Dicliptera heterostegia var. praetextata

Dicliptera heterostegia var. praetextata, commonly known as the spotted blady grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves with a distinct purplish or reddish sheen. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that are typically green or brown in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetland or marshy habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in suitable climates.
