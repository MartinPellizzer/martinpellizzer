Declieuxia cordigera var. cordigera

Declieuxia cordigera var. cordigera, commonly known as the heartleaf flax lily, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are often arranged in a rosette, with a distinctive heart-shaped base. The plant produces small, pale blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for both horticultural and ecological landscapes.
