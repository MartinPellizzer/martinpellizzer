Dalbergia mollis var. menabeensis

Dalbergia mollis var. menabeensis, commonly known as the Menabe rosewood, is a species native to Madagascar.

It is a small to medium-sized tree characterized by its dense, dark wood and aromatic properties. The plant has compound leaves with alternating leaflets and produces small, clustered white flowers. Its fruits are woody pods that contain several seeds.

Due to its high value for timber, it is also considered a threatened species in its native habitat.
