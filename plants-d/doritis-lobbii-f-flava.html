Doritis lobbii f. flava

Doritis lobbii f. flava, commonly known as the Yellow Doritis, is a unique hybrid of the Doritis genus native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers, which contrast with its dark green, fleshy leaves. The plant is a succulent, adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems and leaves. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
