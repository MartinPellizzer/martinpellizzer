Daphne × hauseri

Daphne × hauseri, commonly known as the winter daphne, is a hybrid shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of fragrant, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that are typically orange-red in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Due to its toxicity, it should be handled with care.
