Drynaria delavayi

Drynaria delavayi, commonly known as the Delavay's cliff fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its erect, sparsely branched rhizome and linear, leathery fronds. Drynaria delavayi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens or as a houseplant.
