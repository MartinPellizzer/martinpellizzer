Diuris orientis

Diuris orientis, commonly known as the Eastern Spider-orchid, is a striking orchid native to eastern Australia.

It typically grows in sandy soils and is often found in heathlands and open forests. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single, showy flower that has a distinctive spider-like appearance. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color with intricate patterns.

Diuris orientis is known for its resilience and ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
