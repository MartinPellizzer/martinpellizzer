Dyckia excelsa

Dyckia excelsa, commonly known as the giant dyckia, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, upright rosette of sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces striking, showy inflorescences that are often red or orange in color. It thrives in sunny, well-drained conditions and is drought-tolerant once established.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and indoor cultivation.
