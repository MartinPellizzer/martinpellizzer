Delphinium dazangense

Delphinium dazangense, commonly known as the Dazangense Larkspur, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan region.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems and large, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky slopes at high elevations. Its flowers are usually blue or purple, with five petals and a distinctive spurred structure.
