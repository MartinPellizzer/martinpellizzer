Digitaria diversiflora

Digitaria diversiflora, commonly known as the variable digitaria, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and have a rough texture, often with a bluish-green hue.

Digitaria diversiflora is known for its adaptability to various environmental conditions, making it a common weed in agricultural areas.
