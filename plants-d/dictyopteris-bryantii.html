Dictyopteris bryantii

Dictyopteris bryantii, commonly known as Bryant's featherweed, is a marine brown alga found in coastal regions.

It belongs to the family Dictyopteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds. The plant has a thallus that is typically dark brown in color and exhibits a branching structure. It thrives in shallow, rocky intertidal zones where it can absorb nutrients from the surrounding water.

Dictyopteris bryantii plays a role in marine ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various aquatic organisms.
