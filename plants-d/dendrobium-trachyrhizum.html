Dendrobium trachyrhizum

Dendrobium trachyrhizum, commonly known as the long-rooted orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a robust, fleshy rhizome that stores water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with numerous fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, often forming a rosette at the base.

Dendrobium trachyrhizum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
