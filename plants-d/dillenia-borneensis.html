Dillenia borneensis

Dillenia borneensis, commonly known as the Bornean dillenia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant produces showy, white to yellow flowers with five petals, often accompanied by numerous stamens. Its fruits are fleshy, berry-like structures that contain several seeds, and it plays a role in local ecosystems by providing food for various animals.

Dillenia borneensis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
