Dichoglottis tubulosa

Dichoglottis tubulosa, commonly known as the tubular leaf plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often tubular in appearance, giving it its scientific name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by dark blue to black berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage.

Dichoglottis tubulosa is relatively low-maintenance and suitable for indoor cultivation.
