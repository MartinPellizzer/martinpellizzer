Distichorchis metachilina

Distichorchis metachilina, commonly known as the pink fairy orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, pinkish-purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in open, sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It has a sympodial growth habit, with rhizomes that allow it to spread gradually over time.

The species is notable for its intricate floral structure and is often found in grassland habitats.
