Dendrobium intermedium

Dendrobium intermedium, commonly known as the intermediate dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, cane-like stems that store water and nutrients, typical of many orchid species. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Dendrobium intermedium is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
