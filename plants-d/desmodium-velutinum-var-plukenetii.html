Desmodium velutinum var. plukenetii

Desmodium velutinum var. plukenetii, commonly known as the hairy burr legume, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features trifoliate leaves with hairy surfaces, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its pods are hairy and contain several seeds, typical of the Fabaceae family.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats.
