Dryopteris marginalis f. elegans

Dryopteris marginalis f. elegans, commonly known as the elegant marginal fern, is a fern species characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is native to the eastern United States. The plant features narrow, bipinnate leaves with a distinctive marginal vein that runs along the edges of the fronds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and botanical collections.
