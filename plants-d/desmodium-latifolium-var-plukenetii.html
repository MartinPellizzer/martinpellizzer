Desmodium latifolium var. plukenetii

Desmodium latifolium var. plukenetii, commonly known as the broadleaf desmodium, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, which are often dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or disturbed areas.

Its botanical features make it an important component of ecological systems in its native habitats.
