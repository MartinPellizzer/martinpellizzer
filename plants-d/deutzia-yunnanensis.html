Deutzia yunnanensis

Deutzia yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features slender stems with opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and have a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale pink flowers in late spring to early summer. These flowers are borne on elongated pedicels and are often accompanied by showy bracts.

It thrives in partial shade to full sun and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
