Diospyros kunstleri

Diospyros kunstleri, commonly known as the African persimmon, is a small to medium-sized tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Ebenaceae family, which includes ebony and persimmon trees. The plant is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers. Its fruit is a round, fleshy berry that turns orange-red when ripe, containing a single large seed.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local cultures.
