Dendrobium macraei

Dendrobium macraei, commonly known as the Macraei orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Dendrobium genus, which includes over 1,200 species of flowering plants. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, using its aerial roots to absorb moisture and nutrients from the environment. Its flowers are usually fragrant and come in a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and pink.

Dendrobium macraei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
