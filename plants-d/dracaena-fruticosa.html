Dracaena fruticosa

Dracaena fruticosa, commonly known as the dragon tree or red dragon tree, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage with deep green, lance-shaped leaves that often have a reddish tinge at the base. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are followed by bright red berries, which are an important food source for local wildlife. Dracaena fruticosa thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its resilience and adaptability make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
