Dioscorea ayardei

Dioscorea ayardei, commonly known as Ayarde's yam, is a species of tuberous vine native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its climbing habit and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, yellowish-white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its tuberous roots are the primary part used, though they are not widely cultivated for food.

The species is of interest to botanists due to its ecological role and potential medicinal properties.
