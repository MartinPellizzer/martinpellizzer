Dactylorhiza × sultandagi

Dactylorhiza × sultandagi, commonly known as the Sultandagi orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces violet or purple flowers with a distinctive lip, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Dactylorhiza × sultandagi is often found in mountainous regions and is valued for its ornamental qualities in horticulture.
