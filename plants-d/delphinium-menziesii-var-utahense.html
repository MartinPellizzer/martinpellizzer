Delphinium menziesii var. utahense

Delphinium menziesii var. utahense, commonly known as Utah larkspur, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It features striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet, with lance-shaped leaves that are hairy and serrated. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, often found in rocky or alpine environments.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and ecological importance to pollinators.
