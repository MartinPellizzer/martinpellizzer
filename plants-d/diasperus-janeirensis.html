Diasperus janeirensis

Diasperus janeirensis, commonly known as the Janeirensis Cactus, is a species native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its spiny, columnar stems. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, adapting to harsh conditions with its thick, water-storing tissues. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, blooming during the dry season.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
