Drypetes staudtii

Drypetes staudtii, commonly known as the African mahogany, is a tropical tree native to Africa.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by orange-red fruits that contain three seeds. It is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its durable, aromatic wood.
