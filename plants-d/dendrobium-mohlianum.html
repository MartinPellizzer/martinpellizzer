Dendrobium mohlianum

Dendrobium mohlianum, commonly known as the Mohlian orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, relying on its aerial roots for moisture and nutrients. Its leaves are usually thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in its natural habitat.

Dendrobium mohlianum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
