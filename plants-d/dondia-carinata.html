Dondia carinata

Dondia carinata, commonly known as the horned poppy, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers and distinctive horn-like seed pods. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sunlight. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often hairy as well.

Dondia carinata is a herbaceous annual that blooms from late spring to early summer.
