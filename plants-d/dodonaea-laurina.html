Dodonaea laurina

Dodonaea laurina, commonly known as silver dollar tree or velvetleaf.

It is a shrub or small tree native to Australia, characterized by its gray-green, papery leaves that are often covered in fine, silvery hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in spikes, followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid climates.

The leaves are often used in traditional medicine and have a distinctive, aromatic scent.
