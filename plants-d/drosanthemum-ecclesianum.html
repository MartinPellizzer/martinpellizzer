Drosanthemum ecclesianum

Drosanthemum ecclesianum, commonly known as the golden crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces showy yellow ray flowers surrounded by a ring of pale yellow to white disk flowers, creating a distinctive appearance. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Drosanthemum ecclesianum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
