Dendrobium exilifolium

Dendrobium exilifolium, commonly known as the slender-leafed orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, narrow leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, yellow-green flowers. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for.

Dendrobium exilifolium is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its adaptability to various growing conditions.
