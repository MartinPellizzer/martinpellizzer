Dactylostemon communis var. weddellianus

Dactylostemon communis var. weddellianus, commonly known as the Weddellian sandmat, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the arid regions of Australia.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, succulent leaves and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to withstand drought and high temperatures.
