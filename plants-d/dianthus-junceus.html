Dianthus junceus

Dianthus junceus, commonly known as the slender campion, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family, characterized by its showy, pink or white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open habitats such as meadows and grasslands. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Dianthus junceus is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
