Dorstenia gourmaensis var. floribunda

Dorstenia gourmaensis var. floribunda, commonly known as the flowering dorstenia, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree characterized by its unique, fleshy, and often ribbed stems. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically borne on short pedicels. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a distinctive shape with a waxy texture.

This plant is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and requires a warm, humid climate for optimal growth.
