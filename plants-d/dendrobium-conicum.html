Dendrobium conicum

Dendrobium conicum, commonly known as the conical dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, branching structure with multiple pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink, often in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

Dendrobium conicum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
