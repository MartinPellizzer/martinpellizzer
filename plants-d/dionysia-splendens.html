Dionysia splendens

Dionysia splendens, commonly known as the golden torch cactus, is a species of flowering cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, spiny stems that can grow up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Dionysia splendens thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.

Its unique appearance and striking blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
