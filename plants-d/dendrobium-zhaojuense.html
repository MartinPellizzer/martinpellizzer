Dendrobium zhaojuense

Dendrobium zhaojuense, commonly known as the Zhaoju orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to China.

It belongs to the Dendrobium genus, which includes over 1,200 species of orchids, many of which are prized for their ornamental value. The plant features tall, cane-like pseudobulbs covered with smooth, leathery leaves. Its flowers are typically fragrant and come in a range of colors, including white, yellow, and pink.

It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
