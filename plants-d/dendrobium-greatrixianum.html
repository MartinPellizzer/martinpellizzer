Dendrobium greatrixianum

Dendrobium greatrixianum, commonly known as the Greatrix's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Dendrobium genus, which includes numerous orchid species characterized by their sympodial growth habit. The plant typically produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, enabling it to thrive in its natural habitat with minimal rainfall.

Dendrobium greatrixianum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and resilience in cultivation.
