Diospyros kekemi

Diospyros kekemi, commonly known as the Kekemi persimmon, is a small evergreen tree native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Ebenaceae family, which includes other well-known species like the ebony tree. The plant is characterized by its elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. Its fruit is a small, round berry that turns from green to orange-red when mature.

The tree is valued for its hard, dark wood and is also cultivated for its edible fruit.
