Diapedium umbellatum

Diapedium umbellatum, commonly known as the umbrella bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It features upright stems with clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate appearance and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
