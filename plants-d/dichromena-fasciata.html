Dichromena fasciata

Dichromena fasciata, commonly known as the banded dichromena, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its strikingly patterned leaves, which display alternating bands of light and dark green. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers that bloom during the rainy season. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Dichromena fasciata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in horticultural settings.
