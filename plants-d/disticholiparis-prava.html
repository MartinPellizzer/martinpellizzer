Disticholiparis prava

Disticholiparis prava, commonly known as the spiral-leaved water plant, is a species of aquatic plant characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves.

It is native to freshwater environments in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features long, narrow leaves that spiral around its stem, creating a distinctive appearance. It thrives in still or slow-moving waters and is often found in marshy areas.
