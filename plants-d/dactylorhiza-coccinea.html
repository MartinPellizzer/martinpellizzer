Dactylorhiza coccinea

Dactylorhiza coccinea, commonly known as the red helleborine, is a species of orchid native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red flowers, which are often adorned with purple and yellow markings. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and heaths. It has a rhizomatous root system and produces leaves that are lance-shaped and often covered in a fine pubescence.

Dactylorhiza coccinea is a perennial herb that blooms from late spring to early summer.
