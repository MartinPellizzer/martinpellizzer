Daphne × jintyae

Daphne × jintyae, commonly known as the Chinese daphne, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features fragrant, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and are borne on short pedicels. The plant has smooth, dark green foliage that turns bronze in the autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Daphne × jintyae is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in gardens for its winter interest.
