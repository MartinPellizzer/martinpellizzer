Deguelia thyrsiflora

Deguelia thyrsiflora, commonly known as the silky thornapple, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers. The plant produces distinctive, spherical, green fruit that turns yellow when ripe. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often covered with a fine, silky hair.

Deguelia thyrsiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
