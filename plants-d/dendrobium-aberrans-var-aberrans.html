Dendrobium aberrans var. aberrans

Dendrobium aberrans var. aberrans, commonly known as the variable dendrobium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with a distinctive red or orange lip. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This orchid is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
