Debregeasia squamata

Debregeasia squamata, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a deciduous tree native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its silvery-gray bark and compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by elongated pods containing edible seeds. It is often found in rocky or mountainous regions and is adapted to arid environments.
