Dendrobium petiolatum

Dendrobium petiolatum, commonly known as the orchid with a petiole, is a epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a tall, slender stem with numerous nodes and can produce multiple flower spikes. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged spirally along the stem. Its flowers are typically yellow or green with purple or red markings, appearing in clusters.

Dendrobium petiolatum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
