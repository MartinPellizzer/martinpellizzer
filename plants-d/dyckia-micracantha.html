Dyckia micracantha

Dyckia micracantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved dyckia, is a perennial grass-like plant native to South America.

It features dense, rosette-like clusters of narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, spiky flowers that are often reddish or brownish in hue. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a hardy choice for xeriscaping.

Dyckia micracantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
