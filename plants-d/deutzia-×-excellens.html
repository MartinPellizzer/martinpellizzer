Deutzia × excellens

Deutzia × excellens, commonly known as the common deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, featuring arching branches and clusters of small, white or pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has dark green, ovate leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value comes from its showy flowers and attractive foliage throughout the growing season.
