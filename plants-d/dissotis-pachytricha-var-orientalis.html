Dissotis pachytricha var. orientalis

Dissotis pachytricha var. orientalis, commonly known as the oriental thick-stemmed sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and grass-like plants. The plant is characterized by its robust, cylindrical stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes and swampy areas.
