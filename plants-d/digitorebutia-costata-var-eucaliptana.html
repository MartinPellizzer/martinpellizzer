Digitorebutia costata var. eucaliptana

Digitorebutia costata var. eucaliptana, commonly known as the Eucalyptus-leafed stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink to purple during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
