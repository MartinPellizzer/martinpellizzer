Davallia marginalis

Davallia marginalis, commonly known as the marginal fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Davalliaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, leathery fronds that are typically dark green with a pale margin. The plant produces sporangia along the margins of its fronds, which is a distinctive botanical feature. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
