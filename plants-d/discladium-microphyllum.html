Discladium microphyllum

Discladium microphyllum, commonly known as the small-leaved discladium, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. Native to specific regions of South America, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its structure includes a basal rosette of leaves with flowering stems arising from the center.
