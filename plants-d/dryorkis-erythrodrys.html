Dryorkis erythrodrys

Dryorkis erythrodrys, commonly known as the red dry orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It features slender, erect stems that bear clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of red and orange. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silky pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its striking floral display, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
