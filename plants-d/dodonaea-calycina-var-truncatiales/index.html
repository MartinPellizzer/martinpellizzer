Dodonaea Calycina Var. Truncatiales

Dodonaea calycina var. truncatilis is a unique and striking plant native to the coastal regions of southern Australia. Known for its delicate, feathery leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers, it adds a touch of elegance to any garden. This variant of the common Dodonaea species is particularly valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to coastal conditions. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal landscaping. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to watering and soil conditions to flourish.
