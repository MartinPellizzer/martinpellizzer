Droogmansia velutina

Droogmansia velutina, commonly known as the velvetleaf orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is typically found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
