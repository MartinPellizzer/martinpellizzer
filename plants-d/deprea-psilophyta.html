Deprea psilophyta

Deprea psilophyta, commonly known as the slender water fern, is a rare aquatic plant belonging to the family Psilophytales.

It is characterized by its delicate, thread-like fronds that float on the water's surface, giving it a unique appearance. This plant thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and marshes. Its reproductive structures are concealed within small, spore-producing capsules.
