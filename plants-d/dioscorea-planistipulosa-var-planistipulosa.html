Dioscorea planistipulosa var. planistipulosa

Dioscorea planistipulosa var. planistipulosa, commonly known as the narrow-leaved yam, is a herbaceous vine native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its climbing habit and compound leaves with elongated, narrow leaflets. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by tuberous roots that are often used in traditional medicine.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and medicinal contexts.
