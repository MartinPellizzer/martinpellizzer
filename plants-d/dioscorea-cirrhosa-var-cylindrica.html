Dioscorea cirrhosa var. cylindrica

Dioscorea cirrhosa var. cylindrica, commonly known as the slender yam, is a perennial vine native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its cylindrical, tuberous roots that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Its leaves are heart-shaped with a rough texture, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soil.

The tubers of this species are valued for their nutritional content and have been utilized in various cultural practices.
