Dioscorea liebrechtsiana

Dioscorea liebrechtsiana, commonly known as the Liebrechts' yam, is a species of flowering plant in the family Dioscoreaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries such as Nigeria and Cameroon. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with long, slender stems that bear heart-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the same plant.

The tuberous roots of this species are of interest for their potential medicinal properties and are sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
