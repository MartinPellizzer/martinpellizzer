Daphne souliei

Daphne souliei, commonly known as the Chinese daphne, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that are typically red or black in color. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a leathery texture.

Daphne souliei is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively cold-hardy for a plant of its kind.
