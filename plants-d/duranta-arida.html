Duranta arida

Duranta arida, commonly known as the silverleaf duranta, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central and South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features small, lance-shaped leaves that are usually grayish-white in color, giving it its common name. Its showy, tubular flowers are typically blue or purple and bloom in clusters.

Duranta arida is known for its adaptability to dry conditions and is often used in ornamental gardening.
