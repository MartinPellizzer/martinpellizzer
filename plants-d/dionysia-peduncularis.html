Dionysia peduncularis

Dionysia peduncularis, commonly known as the hanging basket plant, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. The plant produces clusters of bright red or orange flowers that attract pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees. Its foliage is typically dark green with a velvety texture, adding visual interest to gardens and indoor spaces.
