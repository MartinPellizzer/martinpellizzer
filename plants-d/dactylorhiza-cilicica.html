Dactylorhiza cilicica

Dactylorhiza cilicica, commonly known as the Cilician orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial plant that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and mountainous areas. The plant features a rhizome with a single leaf and a spike of showy, violet or purple flowers. Its flowers are usually arranged in a raceme and have a distinct three-lobed lip.

Dactylorhiza cilicica is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
