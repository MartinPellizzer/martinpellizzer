Diplothemium maritimum

Diplothemium maritimum, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its distinctive blue or purple flowers and silver-green, fuzzy leaves. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in dune environments. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Diplothemium maritimum is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize coastal soils.
