Dendrobium crenicristatum

Dendrobium crenicristatum, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic orchid that grows on trees and rocks, thriving in humid tropical climates. The plant features long, cane-like stems covered with lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically white or pale yellow with a distinctive zigzag pattern on the petals.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
