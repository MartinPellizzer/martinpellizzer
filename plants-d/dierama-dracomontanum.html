Dierama dracomontanum

Dierama dracomontanum, commonly known as the dragon's tail or firetail, is a striking South African plant characterized by its long, arching inflorescences that resemble a dragon's tail.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is native to the Eastern Cape and Western Cape provinces. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces vibrant orange-red flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated for its unique and eye-catching appearance.
