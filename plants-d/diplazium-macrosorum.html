Diplazium macrosorum

Diplazium macrosorum, commonly known as the giant fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, bipinnate fronds that can reach lengths of up to 2 meters. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with creeping underground stems that help in spreading and colonizing new areas. Its leaves are typically dark green and glossy, with a feathery appearance.

Diplazium macrosorum thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical forests.
