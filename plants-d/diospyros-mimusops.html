Diospyros mimusops

Diospyros mimusops, commonly known as the African persimmon, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Ebenaceae family, which includes ebony and persimmon trees. The plant features simple, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green, often with a waxy coating. Its small, yellowish-green flowers are followed by orange-red, fleshy fruits that are edible.

The tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and its fruit, which is used in traditional medicine and cuisine.
