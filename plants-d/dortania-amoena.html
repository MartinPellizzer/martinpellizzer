Dortania amoena

Dortania amoena, commonly known as the pretty orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that come in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, and it produces intricate, showy inflorescences.

Dortania amoena is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
