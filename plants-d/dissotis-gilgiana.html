Dissotis gilgiana

Dissotis gilgiana, commonly known as the golden groundsel, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and is often found in disturbed soils. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces small, oval-shaped fruits.

Dissotis gilgiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
