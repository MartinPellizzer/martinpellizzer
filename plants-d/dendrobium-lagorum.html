Dendrobium lagorum

Dendrobium lagorum, commonly known as the golden spike orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in tropical rainforests. The plant features a tall, cane-like stem with clusters of lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are showy, with yellow petals and a distinctive golden lip.

Dendrobium lagorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
