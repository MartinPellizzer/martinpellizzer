Dichaea costaricensis

Dichaea costaricensis, commonly known as the Costa Rican dichaea, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered growth habit. The plant produces delicate, greenish-white flowers with a distinct lip, typical of many orchid species. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in cloud forests.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
