Delphinium nelsonii subsp. utahense

Delphinium nelsonii subsp.

utahense, commonly known as Utah larkspur, is a flowering plant native to the western United States. It is characterized by its tall, sparsely leafed stems that bear clusters of blue to purple flowers.

The plant belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is known for its showy, spurred petals. It thrives in rocky soils and is often found in alpine and subalpine regions.
