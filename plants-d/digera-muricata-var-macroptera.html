Digera muricata var. macroptera

Digera muricata var. macroptera, commonly known as the giant burrweed, is a variant of the common burrweed species.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its upright growth habit and hairy stems. The leaves are alternate, ovate to lanceolate, and often have a rough texture. Its most distinctive feature is the presence of large, spiny seed pods that mature to a dark brown color.

This plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions and is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides.
