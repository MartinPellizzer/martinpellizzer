Dendrobium erubescens

Dendrobium erubescens, commonly known as the red-flowered orchid, is a perennial epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with cylindrical pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers with a yellow throat, blooming during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium erubescens is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
