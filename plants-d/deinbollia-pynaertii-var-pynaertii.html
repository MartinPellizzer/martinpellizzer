Deinbollia pynaertii var. pynaertii

Deinbollia pynaertii var. pynaertii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette growth habit and velvety, dark green foliage. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in a variety of colors, including purple, blue, and white. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular houseplant.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and relatively easy to care for in a domestic setting.
