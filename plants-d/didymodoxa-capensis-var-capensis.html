Didymodoxa capensis var. capensis

Didymodoxa capensis var. capensis, commonly known as the Cape bellflower, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue or violet. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its delicate appearance and ease of care.

The species is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
