Dierama longistylum

Dierama longistylum, commonly known as the long-stemmed dierama, is a striking perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, slender stems that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height, adorned with lance-shaped, pale green leaves. The plant produces striking clusters of bell-shaped, white to pale blue flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a valuable addition to native plant collections.
