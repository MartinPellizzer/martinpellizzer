Drimia Polyantha

Drimia polyantha is a striking plant native to southern Africa known for its showy, bell-shaped flowers. It thrives in rocky and sandy soils, often found in coastal and mountainous regions. The plant produces clusters of white or pale purple blooms that attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for gardens and botanical collections. Despite its beauty, it requires careful cultivation to flourish in different environments.
