Deutzia sikangensis

Deutzia sikangensis, commonly known as the Sichuan deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features clusters of white to pink flowers that bloom in early spring, adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant has slender, arching stems and lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and well-drained soil, making it adaptable to various garden conditions.

Deutzia sikangensis is valued for its attractive floral display and ease of cultivation.
