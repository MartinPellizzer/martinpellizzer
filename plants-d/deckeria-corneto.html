Deckeria corneto

Deckeria corneto, commonly known as the golden cup fern, is a member of the Polypodiaceae family.

It is a small, terrestrial fern that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features a creeping rhizome with erect, slender fronds that are pinnately compound. Its leaves are usually bright green and have a distinctive, cup-like shape.

Deckeria corneto is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
