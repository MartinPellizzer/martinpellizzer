Delphinium omeiense var. pubescens

Delphinium omeiense var. pubescens, commonly known as the hairy delphinium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Sichuan Province in China.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stems that bear clusters of striking blue to violet flowers. The leaves are lance-shaped and covered with fine, soft hairs, giving the plant its "hairy" appearance. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers well-drained, loamy soils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
