Dioscorea tubuliflora

Dioscorea tubuliflora, commonly known as the tubular-flowered yam, is a member of the Dioscoreaceae family and is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a perennial vine characterized by its tuberous roots, which are often used in traditional medicine and as a food source. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, tubular flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. Its growth habit involves climbing structures, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

The tubers of Dioscorea tubuliflora are valued for their nutritional content and have been studied for potential medicinal properties.
