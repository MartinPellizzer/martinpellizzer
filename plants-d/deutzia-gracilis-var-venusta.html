Deutzia gracilis var. venusta

Deutzia gracilis var. venusta, commonly known as the fragrant deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features slender, arching stems that bear clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers in early summer. The plant has simple, ovate leaves that are dark green and slightly hairy on the underside. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

This ornamental plant is valued for its delicate appearance and pleasant scent.
