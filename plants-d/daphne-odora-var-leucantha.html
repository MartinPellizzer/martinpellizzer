Daphne odora var. leucantha

Daphne odora var. leucantha, commonly known as the white-flowered daphne, is a deciduous shrub native to southern China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has small, leathery leaves that are dark green and glossy. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Due to its toxic sap, it should be handled with care.
