Ditrita hirta

Ditrita hirta, commonly known as roughleaf dittany, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its hairy, rough-textured leaves and small, pale blue or purple flowers. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open woodlands and scrublands.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been traditionally used in herbal remedies.
