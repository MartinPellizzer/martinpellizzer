Ditassa obcordata

Ditassa obcordata, commonly known as the obcordate dittany, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, aromatic leaves and clusters of pale blue to purple flowers. The plant has a compact growth habit and often grows in rocky or dry soils. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped, with a distinctive obcordate base.

It is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
