Dracaena scabra

Dracaena scabra, commonly known as the snake plant or viper's bowers, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Africa.

It features upright, sword-shaped leaves that are often variegated with yellow or white markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by red or orange berries. It is well adapted to low-light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it a popular houseplant.

Dracaena scabra is also valued for its air-purifying properties and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor environments.
