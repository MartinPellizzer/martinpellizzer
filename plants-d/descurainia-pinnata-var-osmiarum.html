Descurainia pinnata var. osmiarum

Descurainia pinnata var. osmiarum, commonly known as the yellow rocket, is a biennial or annual plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in the spring and early summer. The plant has alternate, pinnately compound leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained soils and full sun.

This species is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.
