Daemonorops cinnamomea

Daemonorops cinnamomea, commonly known as the cinnamon fern, is a species of cycad native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces small, spherical cones that are typically green or yellowish in color. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a landscape plant.
