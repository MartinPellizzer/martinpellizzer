Droogmansia lancifolia

Droogmansia lancifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved droogmansia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features long, narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Droogmansia lancifolia is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and delicate floral display.
