Dracaena Neoserrulata

Striped dracaena, scientifically known as Dracaena neoserrulata, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia. Its most notable feature is its vibrant, vertical stripes that run along its tall, slender stems. This plant is highly popular in indoor gardening due to its air-purifying qualities and low maintenance requirements. It thrives in bright, indirect light and can grow up to several feet in height. With its unique appearance and ease of care, it's a favorite among plant enthusiasts worldwide.
