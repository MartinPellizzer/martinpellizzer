Dendrochilum yuccifolium

Dendrochilum yuccifolium, commonly known as the yucca-leaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, sword-shaped leaves that resemble those of yucca plants. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom during the wet season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in montane forests.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts.
