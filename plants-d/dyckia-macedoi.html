Dyckia macedoi

Dyckia macedoi, commonly known as the Macedonian dyckia, is a bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped foliage, which is typically covered in a dense layer of white, woolly hair. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers that emerge from the center of the rosette. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Dyckia macedoi is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
