Dioscorea speciosa

Dioscorea speciosa, commonly known as the showy yam, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces tuberous roots that are often used in traditional medicine and as a food source. Its leaves are typically large, heart-shaped, and have a rough texture.

Dioscorea speciosa thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
