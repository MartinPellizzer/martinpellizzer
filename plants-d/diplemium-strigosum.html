Diplemium strigosum

Diplemium strigosum, commonly known as the hairy fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its soft, hairy texture on its fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its fronds are pinnately divided, with a distinctive appearance that makes it easily identifiable.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
