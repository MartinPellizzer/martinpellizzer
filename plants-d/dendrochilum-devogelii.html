Dendrochilum devogelii

Dendrochilum devogelii, commonly known as the Devogel's tree orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its pseudobulbs are thick and segmented, supporting its evergreen foliage.

Dendrochilum devogelii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
