Desmodium amplexicaule

Desmodium amplexicaule, commonly known as the cluster bean or sticky nightshade, is a flowering plant in the legume family.

It is characterized by its erect, bushy growth habit and trifoliate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by distinctive, inflated seed pods. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions and is often found in disturbed soils.

Desmodium amplexicaule is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in agricultural settings.
