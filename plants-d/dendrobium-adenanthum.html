Dendrobium adenanthum

Dendrobium adenanthum, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It features a pseudobulb structure with long, narrow leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant thrives in rocky soils and is often found in coastal regions. It is adapted to drought conditions and requires good drainage.

Its unique morphology and habitat make it a notable species in the orchid family.
