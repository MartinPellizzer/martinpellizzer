Dianthus zeyheri subsp. natalensis

Dianthus zeyheri subsp.

natalensis, commonly known as Natal carnation, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red.

The plant features linear, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
