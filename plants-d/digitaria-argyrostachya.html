Digitaria argyrostachya

Digitaria argyrostachya, commonly known as silver-stemmed grass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray sheaths and inflorescence, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and along roadsides. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, spikelet-based flowers.

This grass is often used for forage and erosion control due to its hardy nature.
