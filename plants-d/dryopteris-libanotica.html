Dryopteris libanotica

Dryopteris libanotica, commonly known as the Lebanon wood fern, is a species of fern native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, erect fronds that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height. The fronds are bipinnate, with a distinctive, elegant appearance that features a light green color and a smooth texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

This plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
