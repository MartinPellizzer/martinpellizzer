Deutzia gracilis f. aureomarginata

Deutzia gracilis f. aureomarginata, commonly known as the golden-margin deutzia, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to China.

It features slender, arching stems that bear clusters of small, white flowers with a distinctive yellow margin. The foliage is dark green and glossy, providing an attractive backdrop to the blooms. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, loamy soil.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its elegant flowering display.
