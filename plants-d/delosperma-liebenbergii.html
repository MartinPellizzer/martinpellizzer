Delosperma liebenbergii

Delosperma liebenbergii, commonly known as the "ice plant," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant clusters of pink or red flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it an ideal plant for low-maintenance landscaping.
