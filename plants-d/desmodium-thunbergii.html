Desmodium thunbergii

Desmodium thunbergii, commonly known as silverleaf desmodium, is a perennial leguminous plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which are often covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in dense, globular clusters, which are followed by small, bean-like pods. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

Desmodium thunbergii is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in sustainable agricultural practices.
