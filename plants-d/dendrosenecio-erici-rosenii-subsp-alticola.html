Dendrosenecio erici-rosenii subsp. alticola

Dendrosenecio erici-rosenii subsp.

alticola, commonly known as the Mountain Groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense clusters, typically blooming during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to harsh, mountainous environments.
