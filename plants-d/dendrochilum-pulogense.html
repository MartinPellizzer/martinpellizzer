Dendrochilum pulogense

Dendrochilum pulogense, commonly known as the Pulo Gunung orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Borneo.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stem and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with white petals and a distinctive red lip. It thrives in shaded, humid environments, typically in forest understories.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
