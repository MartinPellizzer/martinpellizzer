Dipladenia polymorpha

Dipladenia polymorpha, commonly known as the crown of thorns, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a climbing vine with thick, fleshy stems and waxy, dark green leaves. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in warm, sunny conditions and is often cultivated as a ornamental plant.

Dipladenia polymorpha is also drought-tolerant and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
