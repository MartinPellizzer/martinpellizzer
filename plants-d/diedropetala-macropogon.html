Diedropetala macropogon

Diedropetala macropogon, commonly known as the large-lobed die-drop, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive yellow lip. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in tropical or subtropical regions. Its leaves are broad and leathery, providing structural support for the heavy flower spikes.

Botanically, it is noted for its complex floral structure, which includes specialized pollination mechanisms.
