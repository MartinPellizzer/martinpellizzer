Dianthus nihatii

Dianthus nihatii, commonly known as the Nihat's campion, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Turkey.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, clustered flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a slightly fuzzy appearance. Dianthus nihatii thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun exposure, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its blooming period usually occurs in late spring to early summer, adding a vibrant touch to its natural habitat.
