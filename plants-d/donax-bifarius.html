Donax bifarius

Donax bifarius, commonly known as the coastal redwood, is a species of flowering plant in the family Polygonaceae.

It is native to the southeastern United States, particularly in coastal areas of North and South Carolina. Donax bifarius is adapted to sandy, well-drained soils and is often found in maritime environments.
