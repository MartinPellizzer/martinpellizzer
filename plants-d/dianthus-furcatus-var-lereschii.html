Dianthus furcatus var. lereschii

Dianthus furcatus var. lereschii, commonly known as the Leresch's carnation, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant features serrated leaves and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that attract pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in horticulture.
