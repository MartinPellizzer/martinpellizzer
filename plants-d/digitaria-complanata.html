Digitaria complanata

Digitaria complanata, commonly known as flatleaf stargrass, is a species of grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is a perennial plant that typically grows in wetland habitats such as marshes and swamps. The plant has flat, blade-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is often found in association with other wetland vegetation, contributing to the ecological diversity of these environments.

Botanically, it is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and its ability to thrive in saturated soils.
