Desmodium formosanum

Desmodium formosanum, commonly known as the Chinese bupleurum, is a leguminous plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. The plant produces pod-like fruits that contain several small, kidney-shaped seeds. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.

Desmodium formosanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
