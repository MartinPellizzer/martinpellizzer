Discopodium penninervium var. magnifolium

Discopodium penninervium var. magnifolium, commonly known as the giant leaf discopodium, is a striking member of the Rupriocarpaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to several feet in length, often with a distinctive veining pattern. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are usually a deep green, sometimes with a bluish tinge, and can be quite ornamental.

This variant is noted for its robust growth and adaptability to various humid conditions.
