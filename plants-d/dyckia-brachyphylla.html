Dyckia brachyphylla

Dyckia brachyphylla, commonly known as the compact Dyckia, is a perennial bromeliad native to Brazil.

It features rosettes of narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces striking, inflorescences of red and orange flowers that bloom in the spring. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its compact size and ornamental foliage make it a desirable addition to both indoor and outdoor landscapes.
