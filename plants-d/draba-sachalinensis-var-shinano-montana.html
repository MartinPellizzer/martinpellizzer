Draba sachalinensis var. shinano-montana

Draba sachalinensis var. shinano-montana, commonly known as the Shinano mountain rockcress, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant features basal leaves that are linear to lanceolate and small, white or yellow flowers arranged in racemes. It thrives in rocky, alpine environments and is adapted to cold climates.

This variant is notable for its horticultural value and ecological role in mountain ecosystems.
