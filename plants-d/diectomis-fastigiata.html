Diectomis fastigiata

Diectomis fastigiata, commonly known as the upright diectomis, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, narrow growth habit and dense foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique morphology and ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
