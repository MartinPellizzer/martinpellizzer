Delphinium forrestii var. forrestii

Delphinium forrestii var. forrestii, commonly known as the Himalayan larkspur, is a perennial herb native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It features tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height, adorned with striking blue to purple flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are usually arranged alternately along the stem. Its flowers are borne in dense racemes, and the plant produces small, rounded seed pods that split open to release seeds.

Delphinium forrestii var. forrestii is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
