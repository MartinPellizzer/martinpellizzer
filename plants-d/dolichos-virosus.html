Dolichos virosus

Dolichos virosus, commonly known as the virus bean, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers followed by elongated, bean-like pods containing several seeds. Dolichos virosus is often cultivated for its edible seeds, which are used in various cuisines.

It also has medicinal uses in traditional herbal remedies.
