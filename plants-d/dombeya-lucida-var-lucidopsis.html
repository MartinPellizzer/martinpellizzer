Dombeya lucida var. lucidopsis

Dombeya lucida var. lucidopsis, commonly known as the glossy dombeya, is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features showy, white to pale pink, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has smooth, often glabrous bark and lance-shaped, dark green leaves with a glossy texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, moist soil.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
