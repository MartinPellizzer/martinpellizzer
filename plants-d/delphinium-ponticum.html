Delphinium ponticum

Delphinium ponticum, commonly known as the Pontic larkspur, is a tall perennial herbaceous plant native to the Caucasus region.

It features striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along its stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in alpine or rocky habitats.

Its showy flowers attract pollinators like bees and butterflies, making it a valuable addition to gardens and natural landscapes.
