Distimake stellatus

Distimake stellatus, commonly known as the star-shaped leaf plant, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive star-like leaves, which are typically a vibrant green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, usually during the warmer months. It thrives in humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens and indoor spaces.
