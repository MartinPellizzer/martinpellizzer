Delphinium patens subsp. greenei

Delphinium patens subsp.

greenei, commonly known as the Sierra delphinium, is a perennial herb native to the western United States. It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and showy, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant typically grows in open, rocky habitats at high elevations. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
