Dipteracanthus subringens

Dipteracanthus subringens, commonly known as the slender flame vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, trailing stems and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in lowland forests or along riverbanks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking vibrant, climbing plants.
