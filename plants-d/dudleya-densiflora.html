Dudleya densiflora

Dudleya densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered Dudleya, is a succulent plant native to California.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped growth habit and thick, fleshy leaves that store water. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale purple flowers during the spring. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to a Mediterranean climate.

Dudleya densiflora is a popular choice for rock gardens due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.
