Dendrobium retroflexum

Dendrobium retroflexum, commonly known as the purple-veined orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in tropical rainforests. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with multiple stems that produce leaves and flowers sequentially. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to green with purple veins, and they bloom in clusters.

Dendrobium retroflexum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
