Delphinium kingianum var. acuminatissimum

Delphinium kingianum var. acuminatissimum, commonly known as the King's delphinium, is a striking perennial herb native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its tall, spindly stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters, adorned with large, showy blue to purple flowers. The flowers are arranged in dense spikes and are composed of five petals, with the lower petal being spurred. This variety is known for its robust growth and ability to thrive in cooler, moist environments.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine settings due to its ornamental value and hardiness.
