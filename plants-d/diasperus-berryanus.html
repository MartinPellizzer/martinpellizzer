Diasperus berryanus

Diasperus berryanus, commonly known as the redleaf berry, is a shrub native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It produces small, bright red berries that are highly nutritious and rich in antioxidants. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with dark green, waxy leaves that help reduce water loss. Its flowers are small and yellow, appearing in clusters during the dry season.

The berries are often consumed by local wildlife and are also used in traditional medicine for their healing properties.
