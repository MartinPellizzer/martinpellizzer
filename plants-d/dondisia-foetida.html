Dondisia foetida

Dondisia foetida, commonly known as the stinking nightshade, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a member of the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades such as tomatoes and peppers. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale green. Its leaves are broad and ovate, with a slightly hairy texture.
