Diplomorpha kudoi

Diplomorpha kudoi, commonly known as the Kudoi orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant blooms. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually pale green or white, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by specific insects.

Diplomorpha kudoi is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
