Davallia Kinabaluensis

Davallia kinabaluensis is a rare and unique fern species native to the high-altitude regions of Borneo. This plant is known for its delicate, feathery fronds that resemble the soft wings of a bird. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found growing on rock faces in mountainous areas. Due to its limited distribution and fragile habitat, it is considered a conservation concern. This article explores the characteristics, habitat, and significance of Davallia kinabaluensis.
