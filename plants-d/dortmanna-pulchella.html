Dortmanna pulchella

Dortmanna pulchella, commonly known as the pretty orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are specialized for absorbing nutrients from the soil, and its leaves are often broad and strap-like.

The plant's ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural collections.
