Dimorphotheca zeyheri

Dimorphotheca zeyheri, commonly known as the yellow ox-eye daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow ray florets surrounding a central disc of smaller, darker flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Dimorphotheca zeyheri is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant and showy appearance.
