Decaschistia byrnesii subsp. byrnesii

Decaschistia byrnesii subsp.

byrnesii, commonly known as the Byrnes' dune grass, is a flowering plant native to coastal areas of southern Africa. It is a perennial herb characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves.

The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom during the cooler months. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in dune environments.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
