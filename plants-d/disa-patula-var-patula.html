Disa patula var. patula

Disa patula var. patula, commonly known as the Natal orchid, is a strikingly beautiful plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or riverine vegetation. The plant features a slender stem with a single, showy flower that has a distinctive pink or purple coloration. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem.

Disa patula var. patula is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
