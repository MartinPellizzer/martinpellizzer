Dalzellia gracilis

Dalzellia gracilis, commonly known as the slender dalzellia, is a flowering plant native to the southern regions of Africa.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Dalzellia gracilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
