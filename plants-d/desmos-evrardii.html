Desmos evrardii

Desmos evrardii, commonly known as the Cape Flats daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and white. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and has a compact, shrub-like form with narrow, linear leaves. It is well adapted to sandy soils and is often found in coastal fynbos vegetation.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens, though it prefers full sun and well-drained soil.
