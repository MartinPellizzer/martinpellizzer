Drymocodon rapunculoides

Drymocodon rapunculoides, commonly known as the slender burrweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and robust, hairy stems. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by spiny, globe-shaped fruits. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often covered in fine hairs.

Drymocodon rapunculoides is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.
