Dendrochilum hastatum

Dendrochilum hastatum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed tree orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, woody stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically arranged in clusters along the stems. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in the wild.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
