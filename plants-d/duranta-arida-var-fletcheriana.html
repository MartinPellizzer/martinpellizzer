Duranta arida var. fletcheriana

Duranta arida var. fletcheriana, commonly known as the Fletcher's Duranta, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to violet, which attract pollinators such as bees and hummingbirds. The plant has narrow, leathery leaves that are arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping in arid regions.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for ornamental gardens and landscapes.
