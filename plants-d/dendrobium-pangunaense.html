Dendrobium pangunaense

Dendrobium pangunaense, commonly known as the Panguna orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to the Philippines.

It features a compact, branching growth habit with pseudo-bulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers during the dry season. Its leaves are typically elliptical and arranged in two rows along the stems.

Dendrobium pangunaense thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.
