Dichapetalum petelotii

Dichapetalum petelotii, commonly known as the African waterleaf, is a perennial herb native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its alternate, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically borne on long pedicels. Its roots are fibrous and can be used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Dichapetalum petelotii thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland or swampy areas.
