Dracocephalum pulchellum

Dracocephalum pulchellum, commonly known as the beautiful dragonhead, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and spikes of blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Dracocephalum pulchellum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
