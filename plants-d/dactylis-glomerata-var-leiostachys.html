Dactylis glomerata var. leiostachys

Dactylis glomerata var. leiostachys, commonly known as silky brome, is a perennial grass native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces panicles of soft, silky florets that give it a distinctive appearance. It is well adapted to a variety of soil types and is often used in forage and erosion control.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species in both agricultural and ecological contexts.
