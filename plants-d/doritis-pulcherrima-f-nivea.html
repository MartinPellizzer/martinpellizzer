Doritis pulcherrima f. nivea

Doritis pulcherrima f. nivea, commonly known as the White Ice Doritis, is a striking variant of the Doritis pulcherrima species.

It is native to South Africa and belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family. The plant is characterized by its white, bell-shaped flowers that contrast sharply with its green foliage. It is a perennial herb that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
