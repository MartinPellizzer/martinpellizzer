Desmos conchyliatus

Desmos conchyliatus, commonly known as the spiral cactus, is a unique species native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its spiral arrangement of tubercles, which gives it a distinctive and ornamental appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the early morning, adding a striking visual contrast to its spiny structure. Its succulent stems store water, allowing it to survive in harsh, dry environments.

Despite its hardy nature, it is relatively rare and requires specific growing conditions to thrive.
