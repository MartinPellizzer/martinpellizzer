Dactylorhiza maculata var. elodes

Dactylorhiza maculata var. elodes, commonly known as the spotted orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wet meadows. The plant features a slender rhizome with a rosette of lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with a distinctive dark purple or brown marking on the lip.

It is a perennial species that blooms from late spring to early summer.
