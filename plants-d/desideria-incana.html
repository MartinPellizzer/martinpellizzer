Desideria incana

Desideria incana, commonly known as the silver hair grass, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-gray stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Desideria incana is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
