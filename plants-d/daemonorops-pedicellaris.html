Daemonorops pedicellaris

Daemonorops pedicellaris, commonly known as the red pandan, is a tropical plant belonging to the family Araceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including regions such as Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, fragrant leaves that are often used in culinary and traditional applications. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, typical of the Araceae family.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its aromatic leaves and ornamental value.
