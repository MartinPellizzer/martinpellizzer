Dalbergaria cubensis

Dalbergaria cubensis, commonly known as Cuban burr legume, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean, particularly Cuba.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are trifoliate, with small, oval-shaped leaflets, and it produces yellow to orange flowers that bloom in clusters.

The fruit is a hairy pod that contains several seeds, contributing to its role in local ecosystems.
