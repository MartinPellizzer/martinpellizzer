Dendrobium hellwigianum

Dendrobium hellwigianum, commonly known as the Hellwig's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on the surface of trees or other structures without deriving nutrients from them. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with multiple stems that branch out over time. Its flowers are typically white or pale yellow, with intricate patterns and a sweet fragrance.

Dendrobium hellwigianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
