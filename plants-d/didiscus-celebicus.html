Didiscus celebicus

Didiscus celebicus, commonly known as the Celebes sunflower, is a flowering plant native to the Indonesian island of Sulawesi.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its large, showy yellow-orange composite flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

Didiscus celebicus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
