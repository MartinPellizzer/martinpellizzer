Duchesnea major

Duchesnea major, commonly known as the strawberry clover, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It features trifoliate leaves with a slightly hairy surface and produces small, white or pinkish flowers. The plant is characterized by its edible, red-tinged berries that resemble strawberries, which are rich in antioxidants. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Duchesnea major is also valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local pollinators.
