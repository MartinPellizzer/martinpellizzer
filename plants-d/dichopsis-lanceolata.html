Dichopsis lanceolata

Dichopsis lanceolata, commonly known as the sword leaf cactus, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features long, narrow, sword-like leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, which help reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking contrast to its foliage. It is well adapted to full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its hardy nature, Dichopsis lanceolata is a popular choice for xeriscaping and low-maintenance landscaping.
