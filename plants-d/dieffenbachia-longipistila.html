Dieffenbachia longipistila

Dieffenbachia longipistila, commonly known as the long-pistil dieffenbachia, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, waxy, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant mix of green, white, and yellow. The plant produces a tall, slender stem with a spadix inflorescence, which is typical of the Araceae family. Its foliage is often variegated, adding visual interest to indoor spaces.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity to maintain its lush appearance.
