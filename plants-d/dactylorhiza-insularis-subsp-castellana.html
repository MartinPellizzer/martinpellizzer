Dactylorhiza insularis subsp. castellana

Dactylorhiza insularis subsp.

castellana, commonly known as the Castellana orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Balearic Islands. It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and typically grows in shaded, moist environments.

The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit and produces erect, leafy stems. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with a distinctive lip that is often marked with contrasting colors.

It is a rare and protected species, requiring specific ecological conditions for successful cultivation.
