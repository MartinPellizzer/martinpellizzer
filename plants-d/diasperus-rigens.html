Diasperus rigens

Diasperus rigens, commonly known as the rigid diasperis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright, branching structure. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are typically linear and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Diasperus rigens is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
