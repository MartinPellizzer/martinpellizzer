Delosperma burtoniae

Delosperma burtoniae, commonly known as the Burton's ice plant, is a succulent perennial native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are adapted to store water, making it drought-tolerant. The plant produces vibrant pink or purple flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking low-maintenance, drought-resistant plants.
