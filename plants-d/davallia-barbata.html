Davallia barbata

Davallia barbata, commonly known as the hairy shield fern, is a tropical fern native to Southeast Asia.

It features a distinctive, upright rhizome with a dense, hairy coating that gives it its common name. The plant produces shield-shaped, bipinnate fronds that are typically light green with a silvery underside. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
