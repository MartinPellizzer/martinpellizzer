Dolichos festivus

Dolichos festivus, commonly known as the festive dolichos, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its climbing habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are often clustered in racemes, followed by elongated pods containing several seeds. Dolichos festivus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and flowers.

Its growth is typically associated with sandy or rocky soils in open habitats.
