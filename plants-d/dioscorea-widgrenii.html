Dioscorea widgrenii

Dioscorea widgrenii, commonly known as the narrowleaf yam, is a climbing vine belonging to the Dioscoreaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into tuberous roots. Its tubers are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant's botanical structure supports its role in both ecological and medicinal contexts.
