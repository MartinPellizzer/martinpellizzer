Digitaria maitlandii

Digitaria maitlandii, commonly known as Maitland's grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves with a bluish-green color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny grains of grass. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open areas such as savannas and disturbed sites.

Digitaria maitlandii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in erosion-prone areas.
