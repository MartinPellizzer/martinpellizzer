Dracaena dennisonii

Dracaena dennisonii, commonly known as the snake plant, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stems and narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with a yellowish or white stripe down the center. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it an excellent choice for indoor gardening. Dracaena dennisonii is also noted for its air-purifying qualities, as it can help remove toxins from the air.

Its slow growth rate and aesthetic appeal make it a popular ornamental plant.
