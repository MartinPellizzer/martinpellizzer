Dovyalis spinosissima

Dovyalis spinosissima, commonly known as the spiny hog plum, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and elliptical leaves with a glossy green surface. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by edible, orange-red berries. The berries are often used in local cuisines and traditional medicine.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is relatively drought-tolerant.
