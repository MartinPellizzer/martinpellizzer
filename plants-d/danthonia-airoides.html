Danthonia airoides

Danthonia airoides, commonly known as the silver grass, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage and tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are often adorned with white or pale blue flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or sandy environments.

Danthonia airoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in landscaping and restoration projects.
