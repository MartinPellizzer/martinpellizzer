Dendrochilum curvatum

Dendrochilum curvatum, commonly known as the curved-lip orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, curving lip that gives it its common name. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with a variety of colors ranging from white to pink and purple.

Dendrochilum curvatum is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
