Davallia pectinata

Davallia pectinata, commonly known as the zigzag holly fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, zigzag-shaped fronds that give it its common name. The plant has a creeping rhizome that allows it to spread and form dense mats. Its leaves are typically dark green and have a leathery texture.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
