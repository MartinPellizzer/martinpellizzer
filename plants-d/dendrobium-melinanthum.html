Dendrobium melinanthum

Dendrobium melinanthum, commonly known as the orchid with yellow flowers, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit, with thick, fleshy pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces racemes of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with prominent yellow lips. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

Its unique floral structure and vibrant color make it a popular choice for both horticultural and ornamental purposes.
