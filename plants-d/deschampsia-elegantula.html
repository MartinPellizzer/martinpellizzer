Deschampsia elegantula

Deschampsia elegantula, commonly known as silky bluegrass, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-blue foliage and delicate, slender inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. It has a clump-forming growth habit and produces small, wind-pollinated flowers.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
