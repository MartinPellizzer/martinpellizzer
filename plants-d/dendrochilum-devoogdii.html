Dendrochilum devoogdii

Dendrochilum devoogdii, commonly known as the Devoogd's tree orchid, is a rare and enigmatic orchid species native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its unique, tree-like growth habit, with thick, fleshy stems that can reach several meters in height. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with intricate patterns on the petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically in lowland to montane forests.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is highly valued in botanical collections and conservation efforts.
