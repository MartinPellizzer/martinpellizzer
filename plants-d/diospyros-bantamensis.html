Diospyros bantamensis

Diospyros bantamensis, commonly known as the Bantam ebony, is a species of flowering plant in the family Ebenaceae.

It is native to Indonesia, particularly found in the islands of Java and Sumatra. The plant is characterized by its dark, dense wood, which is highly valued for its strength and durability. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by edible, sweet fruits.

The species is also notable for its role in traditional medicine and its ecological importance in its native habitats.
