Dorycnium × bicknellianum

Dorycnium × bicknellianum, commonly known as the Bicknell's sainfoin, is a hybrid plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It is native to the northeastern United States and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value and potential ecological benefits, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and restoration projects.
