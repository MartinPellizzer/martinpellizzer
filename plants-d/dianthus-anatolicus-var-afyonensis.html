Dianthus anatolicus var. afyonensis

Dianthus anatolicus var. afyonensis, commonly known as the Afyon carnation, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves, and it produces small, delicate flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
