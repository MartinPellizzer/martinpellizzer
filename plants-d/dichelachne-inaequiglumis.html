Dichelachne inaequiglumis

Dichelachne inaequiglumis, commonly known as the unequal-glume dichelachne, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences with distinctive bracts. Its seeds are typically small and have a hard, smooth coat.

Dichelachne inaequiglumis is often found in disturbed soils and can be invasive in certain ecosystems.
