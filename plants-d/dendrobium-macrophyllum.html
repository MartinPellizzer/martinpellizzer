Dendrobium macrophyllum

Dendrobium macrophyllum, commonly known as the large-leafed orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, cane-like stems that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with a yellow throat. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Dendrobium macrophyllum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
