Dentaria × paxiana

Dentaria × paxiana, commonly known as the hybrid toothache plant, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern North America.

It belongs to the family Brassicaceae, which includes many other flowering plants with small, cruciform flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 10–30 cm and features basal leaves that are lanceolate and hairy. Its flowers are white or pale yellow, with four petals arranged in a cross shape, typical of the Brassicaceae family.

Dentaria × paxiana is often found in moist, sandy soils in woodland edges and open fields.
