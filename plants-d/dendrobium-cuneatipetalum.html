Dendrobium cuneatipetalum

Dendrobium cuneatipetalum, commonly known as the narrow-petaled dendrobium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant that grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid and shaded environments. The plant produces strikingly colorful flowers with elongated, narrow petals that are often a vibrant shade of yellow or orange. Its leaves are usually thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in its natural habitat.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its long-lasting blooms.
