Dalbergia hildebrandtii var. hildebrandtii

Dalbergia hildebrandtii var. hildebrandtii, commonly known as the hildebrandtii legume, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized tree characterized by its compound leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant produces pods containing flat, fibrous seeds, which are often used in traditional practices.

Its botanical features include a straight grain and a rich, dark coloration when mature, making it valued for its aesthetic and potential medicinal properties.
