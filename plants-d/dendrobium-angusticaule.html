Dendrobium angusticaule

Dendrobium angusticaule, commonly known as the narrow-leaved orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrobium angusticaule is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
