Dieffenbachia princeps

Dieffenbachia princeps, commonly known as the White Knight, is a tropical plant native to South America.

The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are surrounded by a spathe, a feature common in many aroid plants.

Dieffenbachia princeps is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
