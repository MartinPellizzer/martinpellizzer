Deutzia × rosea f. grandiflora

Deutzia × rosea f. grandiflora, commonly known as the grandiflora deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features slender, arching branches that produce clusters of fragrant, white to pink flowers in early summer. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that turn yellow in the fall, adding seasonal interest. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

This ornamental plant is valued for its showy blooms and adaptability to various garden conditions.
