Delosperma virens

Delosperma virens, commonly known as the ice plant, is a succulent perennial native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance.

Its adaptability and low maintenance make it a popular choice for gardeners in dry climates.
