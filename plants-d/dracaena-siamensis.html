Dracaena siamensis

Dracaena siamensis, commonly known as the Siam dracaena, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and rosette of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom in the spring. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.

Dracaena siamensis prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.
