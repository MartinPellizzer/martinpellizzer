Deianira erubescens var. pallescens

Deianira erubescens var. pallescens, commonly known as the pale deianira, is a variant of the deianira plant characterized by its pale foliage.

It is a perennial herb native to specific regions of South America, particularly in the Andean foothills. The plant features lanceolate leaves with a smooth texture and a bluish-green hue. It produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, blooming during the spring and early summer.

Due to its unique coloration and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
