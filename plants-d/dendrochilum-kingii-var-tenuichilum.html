Dendrochilum kingii var. tenuichilum

Dendrochilum kingii var. tenuichilum, commonly known as the slender-lobed tree orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems and lanceolate leaves that are often covered in fine, white pubescence. The plant produces fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with elongated, narrow petals and a distinctive, narrow lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
