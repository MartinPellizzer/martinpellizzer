Dolichopentas decora var. decora

Dolichopentas decora var. decora, commonly known as the Decorative Pentas, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features showy, five-petaled flowers that are typically pink or white, arranged in clusters at the ends of its branches. The plant has a trailing or shrub-like growth habit, making it suitable for containers or hanging baskets. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, adding visual contrast to its vibrant blooms.

It thrives in warm, sunny conditions and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
