Dipladenia carissima

Dipladenia carissima, commonly known as the flame vine, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a vigorous climber with long, trailing stems that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically bright orange or red, attracting hummingbirds and other pollinators. It thrives in warm, sunny conditions and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its rapid growth and ornamental value make it popular in tropical and subtropical gardens.
