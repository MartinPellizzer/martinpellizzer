Dendrobium flexuosum

Dendrobium flexuosum, commonly known as the hanging orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, drooping stems that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white or yellowish flowers that bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged along the stem, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Dendrobium flexuosum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
