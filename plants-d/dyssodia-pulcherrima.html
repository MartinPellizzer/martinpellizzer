Dyssodia pulcherrima

Dyssodia pulcherrima, commonly known as the beautiful dyssodia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage is usually narrow and grass-like, with the stems often branching at the base.

Dyssodia pulcherrima is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to dry conditions.
