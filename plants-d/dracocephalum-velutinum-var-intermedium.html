Dracocephalum velutinum var. intermedium

Dracocephalum velutinum var. intermedium, commonly known as the woolly dragonhead, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia.

It features hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces spikes of blue to purple flowers. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and aromatic foliage. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticultural settings.
