Dichorisandra rhizantha

Dichorisandra rhizantha, commonly known as the purple-flowered star sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact rosette growth habit and produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. The plant features fibrous roots and narrow, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a tight spiral. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along streambanks.

Dichorisandra rhizantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
