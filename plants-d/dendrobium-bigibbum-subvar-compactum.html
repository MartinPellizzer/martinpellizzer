Dendrobium bigibbum subvar. compactum

Dendrobium bigibbum subvar. compactum, commonly known as the compact orchid, is a sympodial epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact growth habit with short, upright pseudobulbs that bear a few leaves each. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often in clusters. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

This subvariety is prized for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
