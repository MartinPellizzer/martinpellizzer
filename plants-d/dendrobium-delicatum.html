Dendrobium delicatum

Dendrobium delicatum, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers during the winter months. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium delicatum is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its delicate floral display.
