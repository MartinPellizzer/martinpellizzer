Dendrochilum ophiopogonoides var. kerintjiense

Dendrochilum ophiopogonoides var. kerintjiense, commonly known as the Kerintji orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to the mountainous regions of Borneo.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on tree trunks or in forest understories. Its leaves are elliptical and dark green, with a waxy texture that helps it retain moisture.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts due to its rarity and beauty.
