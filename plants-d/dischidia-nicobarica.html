Dischidia nicobarica

Dischidia nicobarica, commonly known as the Nicobar fig.

It is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia, including the Nicobar Islands. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks.

Dischidia nicobarica is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
