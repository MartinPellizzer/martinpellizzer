Doodia caudata var. dimorpha

Doodia caudata var. dimorpha, commonly known as the variable fern, is a variant of the common wood fern.

It is a perennial plant native to parts of Asia, including China and Japan. The plant features a rhizomatous growth habit with fronds that are typically bipinnate, giving it a delicate, lacy appearance. Its botanical characteristics include dimorphic spore-bearing structures, which are a key feature of its classification.

The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.
