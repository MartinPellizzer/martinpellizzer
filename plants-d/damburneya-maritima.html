Damburneya maritima

Damburneya maritima, commonly known as the coastal damburneya, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils near the shoreline. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and leathery, adapted to withstand salt spray and strong coastal winds.

Damburneya maritima is often found in dune environments and is valued for its resilience and ornamental qualities.
