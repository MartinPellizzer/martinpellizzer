Dendropanax venosus

Dendropanax venosus, commonly known as the veiny arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, arrowhead-shaped leaves with prominent veins, which give it its common name. The plant is evergreen and can grow as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its foliage is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its aesthetic appeal.

It prefers moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it suitable for shaded garden environments.
