Dombeya baronii

Dombeya baronii, commonly known as the baron's dombeya, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of eastern Africa.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has smooth, grayish bark and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Dombeya baronii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beautiful floral display.
