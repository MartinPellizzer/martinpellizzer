Dictyopteris dahlii

Dictyopteris dahlii, commonly known as the Dahl's feather wort, is a species of brown algae belonging to the family Dictyopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that are often translucent and adorned with small, branching structures. This marine plant typically grows in shallow, coastal waters where it attaches to rocky substrates. Its reproductive structures, known as sporangia, are clustered in dense, umbrella-like formations.

Dictyopteris dahlii plays a vital role in marine ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various aquatic organisms.
