Delphinium hybridum var. szowitsianum

Delphinium hybridum var. szowitsianum, commonly known as the Szowitsianum larkspur, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features tall, spindly stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height, adorned with clusters of striking blue to purple flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are deeply lobed, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Its botanical characteristics include showy flowers that attract pollinators and a fibrous root system adapted to its alpine habitat.
