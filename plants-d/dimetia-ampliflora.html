Dimetia ampliflora

Dimetia ampliflora, commonly known as the broad-flowered dimetia, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 10 centimeters in diameter. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial with a branching stem and opposite leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive, elongated shape.

Dimetia ampliflora thrives in humid, well-drained soils and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.
