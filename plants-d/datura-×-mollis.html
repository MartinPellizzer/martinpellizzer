Datura × mollis

Datura × mollis, commonly known as the angel's trumpet, is a hybrid species within the nightshade family (Solanaceae).

It is characterized by its large, showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. The plant is native to South America but has been widely cultivated in warm climates around the world. Datura × mollis is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types, though it prefers well-drained conditions.

Its foliage and flowers emit a strong, sweet fragrance, especially at night, attracting nocturnal pollinators.
