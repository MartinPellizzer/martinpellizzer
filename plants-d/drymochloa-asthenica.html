Drymochloa asthenica

Drymochloa asthenica, commonly known as the slender grass or dwarf bamboo, is a member of the grass family (Poaceae).

It is a low-growing perennial plant characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and compact growth habit. Native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, it thrives in shaded, moist environments. The plant has a rhizomatous root system that allows it to spread and form dense mats.

Its botanical features make it suitable for ornamental use in shaded garden settings.
