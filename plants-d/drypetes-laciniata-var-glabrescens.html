Drypetes laciniata var. glabrescens

Drypetes laciniata var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth-leafed drypetes, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are usually glabrous or nearly smooth on both surfaces. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
