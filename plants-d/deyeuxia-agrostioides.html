Deyeuxia agrostioides

Deyeuxia agrostioides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bulrush, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Asia and Australia.

The plant has slender, cylindrical stems that are often slightly hairy, and its leaves are narrow, linear, and grass-like in appearance. Its inflorescence consists of dense, spike-like clusters of small, spikelet-based flowers.
