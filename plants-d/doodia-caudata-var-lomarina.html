Doodia caudata var. lomarina

Doodia caudata var. lomarina, commonly known as the slender fern, is a variant of the common wood fern.

It is a perennial plant native to various regions in Asia, including China and Japan. The plant features slender, arching fronds that are typically less than 1 meter in length. Its rhizomes are covered in a dense, fibrous sheath, which helps anchor it to moist, shaded environments.

This variety is often found in forest understories where it thrives in cool, humid conditions.
