Drypetes monachinoi

Drypetes monachinoi, commonly known as the sacred tree of the Monachino people, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. The tree produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.
