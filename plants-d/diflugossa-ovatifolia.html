Diflugossa ovatifolia

Diflugossa ovatifolia, commonly known as the broad-leafed diflugossa, is a unique plant species characterized by its ovate-shaped leaves.

It belongs to the family Diflugossaceae and is native to specific regions in South America. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. Its leaves are usually smooth and have a waxy surface, which helps in retaining moisture.

The flowers are small and inconspicuous, often clustered in dense inflorescences.
