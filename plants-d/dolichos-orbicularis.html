Dolichos orbicularis

Dolichos orbicularis, commonly known as the round bean or globular bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and distinctive, round, fleshy pods that contain edible seeds. The plant produces yellow flowers and is often cultivated for its nutritious seeds, which are rich in protein. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Dolichos orbicularis is also valued for its ornamental appeal due to its unique foliage and fruit structure.
