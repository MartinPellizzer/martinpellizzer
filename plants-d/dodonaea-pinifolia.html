Dodonaea pinifolia

Dodonaea pinifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf hop bush, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features slender, needle-like leaves that are typically arranged in opposite pairs along its stems. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense, rounded inflorescences. Its bark is fibrous and often peels in thin, papery layers.

Dodonaea pinifolia is adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions.
