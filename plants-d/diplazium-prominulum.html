Diplazium prominulum

Diplazium prominulum, commonly known as the slender chain fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, creeping rhizomes and pinnately compound fronds. The fronds are typically arching and have a feathery appearance, with small, ovate pinnae that are often covered in a fine layer of hair. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky crevices.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
