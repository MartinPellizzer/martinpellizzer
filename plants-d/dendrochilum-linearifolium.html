Dendrochilum linearifolium

Dendrochilum linearifolium, commonly known as the linear-leaved tree orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and linear, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange with red or purple markings. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
