Dendrochilum scriptum

Dendrochilum scriptum, commonly known as the script orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, branching growth habit with pendulous inflorescences that bear numerous small, fragrant flowers. The flowers are typically white to pale yellow with intricate patterns resembling script or writing, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and well-draining substrate.

Dendrochilum scriptum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
