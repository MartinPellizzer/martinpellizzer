Disticholiparis pseudodisticha

Disticholiparis pseudodisticha, commonly known as the narrow-leafed grass.

It is a perennial grass species characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It is typically found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to arid environments and low-nutrient soils.
