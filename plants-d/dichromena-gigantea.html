Dichromena gigantea

Dichromena gigantea, commonly known as the giant dichromena, is a striking flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically display a striking contrast of colors, often in shades of red and yellow. The plant has a robust stem that can grow up to several meters in height, making it a prominent feature in its natural habitat. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, contributing to its lush, tropical appearance.

Dichromena gigantea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
