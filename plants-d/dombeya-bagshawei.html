Dombeya bagshawei

Dombeya bagshawei, commonly known as the Chinese dombeya, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its fragrant flowers and compact growth habit.
