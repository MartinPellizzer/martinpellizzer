Dorycnium gracile

Dorycnium gracile, commonly known as slender vetch, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by elongated pods containing several seeds. Dorycnium gracile is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of habitats, including meadows and woodland edges.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it beneficial for soil enrichment in ecological restoration projects.
