Dipladenia hybrida

Dipladenia hybrida, commonly known as the crown of thorns, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a vigorous climber with thick, fleshy stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in warm, sunny conditions and requires well-draining soil.

Dipladenia hybrida is often used in ornamental gardening for its vibrant blooms and tropical appearance.
