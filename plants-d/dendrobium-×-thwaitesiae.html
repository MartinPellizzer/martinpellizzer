Dendrobium × thwaitesiae

Dendrobium × thwaitesiae, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a sympodial epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact growth habit with numerous pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and yellow, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated for horticultural purposes.
