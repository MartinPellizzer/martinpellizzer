Daphniphyllum pentandrum

Daphniphyllum pentandrum, commonly known as the five-stamen holly, is a deciduous shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Ericaceae and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are often dark green in color. Daphniphyllum pentandrum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and seasonal floral display.
