Dendrobium libingtaoi

Dendrobium libingtaoi, commonly known as the Liangshan orchid, is a species of orchid native to China.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on the surface of trees or rocks without deriving nutrients from them. The plant produces strikingly beautiful flowers with a wide range of colors, including white, yellow, and pink. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Its unique floral structure and vibrant colors make it a prized ornamental plant in horticulture.
