Deschampsia elegans

Deschampsia elegans, commonly known as silky bluegrass, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It features fine, silver-blue foliage that is both drought-tolerant and cold-hardy. The plant produces slender, spike-like inflorescences that bear small, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and meadows.

Its ornamental appeal makes it a popular choice for native plant gardens.
