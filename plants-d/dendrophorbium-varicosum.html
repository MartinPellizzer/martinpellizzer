Dendrophorbium varicosum

Dendrophorbium varicosum, commonly known as the veined bark plant, is a unique species native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its thick, ridged bark that exhibits a distinctive veined pattern, which is both visually striking and functionally adaptive. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical rainforests. Its leaves are typically elongated and have a waxy surface, aiding in water retention.

The flowers, which are small and clustered, produce a mild fragrance, attracting various pollinators.
