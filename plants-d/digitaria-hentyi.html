Digitaria hentyi

Digitaria hentyi, commonly known as Henty's Digitaria, is a grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, erect culms. The plant produces spikelets that are typically arranged in dense clusters along the stem. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a smooth surface and a prominent midrib.

Digitaria hentyi is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils, where it thrives in full sun.
