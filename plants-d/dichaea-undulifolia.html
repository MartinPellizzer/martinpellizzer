Dichaea undulifolia

Dichaea undulifolia, commonly known as the undulating moss, is a species of moss belonging to the family Dicranaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, creeping growth habit and its ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments. Dichaea undulifolia plays a vital role in soil stabilization and moisture retention in its native habitats.
