Diplax polynoda

Diplax polynoda, commonly known as the narrow-leafed Diplax, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grassland habitats and prefers well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The flowers are small and yellow, arranged in clusters, contributing to its ornamental appeal.
