Dichapetalum odoratum

Dichapetalum odoratum, commonly known as the sweet shower tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often giving off a sweet scent. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually ovate or elliptic in shape. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
