Desmodium maxonii

Desmodium maxonii, commonly known as the African velvet bean, is a leguminous plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant produces elliptical, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in grasslands and disturbed soils.

Desmodium maxonii is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in agricultural systems.
