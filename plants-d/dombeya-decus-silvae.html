Dombeya decus-silvae

Dombeya decus-silvae, commonly known as the silvan ornament, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a yellowish throat. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Dombeya decus-silvae is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
