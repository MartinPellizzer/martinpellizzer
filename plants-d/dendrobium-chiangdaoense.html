Dendrobium chiangdaoense

Dendrobium chiangdaoense, commonly known as the Chiang Dao orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. The plant features long, cane-like stems covered with numerous, fleshy leaves. Its flowers are usually yellow or green with red or orange markings, blooming in clusters.

Dendrobium chiangdaoense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
