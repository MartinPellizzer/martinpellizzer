Dioscoreophyllum podandrium

Dioscoreophyllum podandrium, commonly known as the African yam bean, is a climbing shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves and tuberous roots. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by capsule fruits containing several seeds. Its tubers are edible and have been traditionally used as a food source in some regions.

The plant also has medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
