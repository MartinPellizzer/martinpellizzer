Dracaena humboldtii

Dracaena humboldtii, commonly known as the Humboldt's dracaena, is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a reddish tinge along the edges. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Its thick, fleshy stems store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions.

Dracaena humboldtii is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and ability to tolerate low light.
