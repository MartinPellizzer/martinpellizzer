Dendrocalamopsis variostriata

Dendrocalamopsis variostriata, commonly known as the variable-striped bamboo, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect culms that are often marked with distinct, alternating light and dark bands. The plant belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is closely related to other bamboo species. Its leaves are long, narrow, and often have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.
