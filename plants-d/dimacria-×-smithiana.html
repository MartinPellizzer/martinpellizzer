Dimacria × smithiana

Dimacria × smithiana, commonly known as the hybrid spider orchid, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between two species of the Dimacria genus.

It is characterized by its intricate, spider-like flowers that are typically pale green to yellow in color. The plant is native to the humid regions of Southeast Asia, where it thrives in shaded, moist environments. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, elongated flower spikes.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
