Diemisa splendens

Diemisa splendens, commonly known as the splendour bellflower, is a member of the Campanulaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of South America, particularly in Argentina and Chile. Diemisa splendens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
