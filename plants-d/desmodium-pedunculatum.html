Desmodium pedunculatum

Desmodium pedunculatum, commonly known as the slender ticktrefoil, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a legume, belonging to the Fabaceae family, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant has a climbing habit and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodland or disturbed areas.

Desmodium pedunculatum is also valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, which benefits soil fertility.
