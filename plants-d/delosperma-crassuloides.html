Delosperma crassuloides

Delosperma crassuloides, commonly known as the Cape Hottentot, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically green or blue-gray in color, adapted for storing water. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, orange, or yellow, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
