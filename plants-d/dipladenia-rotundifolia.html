Dipladenia rotundifolia

Dipladenia rotundifolia, commonly known as the wax plant, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, waxy leaves that are rounded and thick, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of orange, red, and yellow, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in warm, sunny conditions and is often grown as a trailing plant in hanging baskets or containers.

Dipladenia rotundifolia is relatively low maintenance, requiring well-draining soil and regular watering.
