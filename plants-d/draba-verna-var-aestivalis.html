Draba verna var. aestivalis

Draba verna var. aestivalis, commonly known as early spring draba, is a low-growing perennial plant native to mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its compact rosette of basal leaves and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers in early spring. The plant is adapted to cold climates and thrives in well-drained, rocky or gravelly soils. It is part of the Brassicaceae family, which includes many edible and ornamental plants.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often found in alpine and subalpine habitats.
