Diasperus mirabilis

Diasperus mirabilis, commonly known as the miracle spurge, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, fleshy leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers.
