Dendrobium zebrinum

Dendrobium zebrinum, commonly known as the zebra orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, cane-like stems and large, showy flowers that often feature striking contrasting patterns. The plant typically blooms in summer, producing clusters of fragrant, brightly colored flowers in shades of white, pink, and yellow. Its leaves are linear and arranged along the stem, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Dendrobium zebrinum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
