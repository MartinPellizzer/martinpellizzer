Dolichos rotundifolius

Dolichos rotundifolius, commonly known as the round-leaved hyacinth bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of violet or purple flowers. The plant produces elongated pods that contain several small, edible seeds. It thrives in warm climates and is often grown for its ornamental value and as a source of protein-rich seeds.

Its botanical features make it a useful plant in both agricultural and horticultural contexts.
