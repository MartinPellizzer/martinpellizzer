Desmidorchis diffusa

Desmidorchis diffusa, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, grass-like leaves and a single, slender spike of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories or along stream banks.

The botanical features of Desmidorchis diffusa make it a unique and visually appealing addition to botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
