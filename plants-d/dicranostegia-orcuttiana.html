Dicranostegia orcuttiana

Dicranostegia orcuttiana, commonly known as the Or-cutt's moss, is a species of moss belonging to the family Dicranaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, branching structure and small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in two rows. The plant produces spore capsules that are initially covered by a peristome, which aids in spore dispersal. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found on rocky substrates or in forest understories.

Dicranostegia orcuttiana is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for microorganisms.
