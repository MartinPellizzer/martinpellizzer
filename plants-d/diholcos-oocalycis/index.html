Diholcos Oocalycis

Asclepias oocalycis, scientifically known as Diholcos oocalycis, is a rare and unique milkweed species native to the southwestern United States. This plant is characterized by its striking yellow flowers and distinctive leaf structure, making it a standout in its ecosystem. It plays a vital role in supporting local pollinators, particularly the endangered monarch butterfly. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it faces significant conservation challenges. Understanding and protecting this plant is essential for maintaining biodiversity in arid environments.
