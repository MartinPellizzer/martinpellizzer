Draba yunussovii

Draba yunussovii, commonly known as the Yunussov's whitlow grass, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

Draba yunussovii is adapted to harsh climates and plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native habitat.
