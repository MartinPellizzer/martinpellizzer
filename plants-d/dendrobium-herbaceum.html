Dendrobium herbaceum

Dendrobium herbaceum, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome that produces multiple shoots. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its delicate nature, it is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a potted plant.
