Daemonorops leptopus

Daemonorops leptopus, commonly known as the slender cycad, is a species of cycad native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, unbranched stem and large, pinnately compound leaves. The plant produces small, cone-like structures that contain both male and female reproductive organs. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with well-drained soil.

Due to its slow growth and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
