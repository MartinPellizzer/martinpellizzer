Danais lyallii

Danais lyallii, commonly known as the Lyall's daisy, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils. It has small, narrow leaves and produces numerous flowers during the spring and summer months.

Danais lyallii is often found in open habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
