Dendrobium tropidoneuron

Dendrobium tropidoneuron, commonly known as the mountain orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees or rocks in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features long, cane-like stems covered with clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Dendrobium tropidoneuron is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
