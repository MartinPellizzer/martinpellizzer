Draba × ekmaniana

Draba × ekmaniana, commonly known as the alpine winter cress, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically thrives in alpine and arctic environments, where it adapts to harsh, cold conditions. Its leaves are usually basal, forming a rosette, and are covered with a dense layer of fine hairs.

Draba × ekmaniana is often found in rocky soils and is a common component of alpine vegetation in regions such as Scandinavia and the Alps.
