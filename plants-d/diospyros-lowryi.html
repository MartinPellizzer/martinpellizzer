Diospyros lowryi

Diospyros lowryi, commonly known as the lowry's persimmon, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local cultures.
