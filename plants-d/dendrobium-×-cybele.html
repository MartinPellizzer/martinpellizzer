Dendrobium × cybele

Dendrobium × cybele, commonly known as the Phalaenopsis hybrid, is a popular epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit, with cane-like stems that produce numerous leaves and flower spikes. The plant is known for its showy, long-lasting blooms that come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and yellow. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium × cybele is widely cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care.
