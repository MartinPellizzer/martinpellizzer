Dartus perlarius

Dartus perlarius, commonly known as the golden dart orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features showy, yellow-orange flowers with a distinctive dart-shaped lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and role in local ecosystems.
