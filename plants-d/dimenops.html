Dimenops

Dimenops, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to southern Africa.

These plants are characterized by their dense, velvety foliage and showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. Dimenops species typically grow as compact shrubs or herbs, often with hairy stems and leaves. They are popular in horticulture for their ornamental value and ability to thrive in indoor environments.

The flowers are usually borne on erect or pendulous stems, adding to their aesthetic appeal.
