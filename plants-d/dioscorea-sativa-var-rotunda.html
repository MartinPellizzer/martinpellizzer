Dioscorea sativa var. rotunda

Dioscorea sativa var. rotunda, commonly known as the round yam, is a tuberous plant belonging to the Dioscoreaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific Islands. The plant produces large, round tubers that are valued for their starchy, edible roots. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and can grow up to 30 cm in length.

The plant thrives in well-drained soil and requires a warm, humid climate for optimal growth.
