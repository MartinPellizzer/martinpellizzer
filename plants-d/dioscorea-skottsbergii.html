Dioscorea skottsbergii

Dioscorea skottsbergii, commonly known as the African yambean, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Dioscoreaceae family and is characterized by its tuberous roots, which are the primary storage organs. The plant produces small, green flowers followed by winged fruits that contain seeds. Its tubers are used in traditional medicine and as a food source in some regions.

The botanical features of D. skottsbergii make it an important species in both ecological and agricultural contexts.
