Diuris amplissima

Diuris amplissima, commonly known as the broad-leafed spider-orchid, is a striking terrestrial orchid native to the eastern coast of Australia.

It features a robust, tuberous rhizome and produces tall, hairy stems that bear numerous bright green leaves. The plant is notable for its intricate, spider-like flowers that are typically pale green or yellow with red markings. It thrives in sandy or loamy soils and requires well-drained conditions.

Diuris amplissima is a hardy species that blooms during the spring and early summer months.
