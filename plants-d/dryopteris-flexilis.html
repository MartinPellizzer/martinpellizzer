Dryopteris flexilis

Dryopteris flexilis, commonly known as the flexible wood fern, is a species of fern native to North America.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, finely divided fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its rhizomes are thick and creeping, allowing it to form dense colonies.

The fronds are evergreen, with a leathery texture and a pale green to dark green coloration.
