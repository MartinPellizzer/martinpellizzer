Dichelachne Sciurea

Dichelachne sciurea is a grass-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions. It is known for its ability to thrive in wet and disturbed soils. This species is often found in lowland areas and along waterways. Its small, narrow leaves and compact growth habit make it distinctive. Despite its modest appearance, it plays an important role in local ecosystems.
