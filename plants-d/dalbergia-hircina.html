Dalbergia hircina

Dalbergia hircina, commonly known as the goatbean or goat's bean, is a leguminous plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is a shrub or small tree native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and Asia. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellowish flowers. Its pods contain edible seeds that are rich in protein and used in traditional cuisines.

The plant also has a shallow root system and is adapted to grow in poor soils.
