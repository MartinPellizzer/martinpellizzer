Dryopteris labordei f. paleacea

Dryopteris labordei f. paleacea, commonly known as the pale frond fern, is a fern species characterized by its distinctive, pale-green fronds.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is native to certain regions of Southeast Asia. The plant features a compact, clump-forming habit with long, slender rhizomes. Its fronds are bipinnate, with a delicate, lace-like appearance that contributes to its ornamental value.

This fern thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
