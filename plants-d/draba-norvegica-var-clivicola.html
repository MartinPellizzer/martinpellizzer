Draba norvegica var. clivicola

Draba norvegica var. clivicola, commonly known as the cliff draba, is a low-growing, mat-forming plant native to rocky and alpine environments.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, cruciform flowers and pinnate leaves. The plant typically thrives in well-drained soils and is often found on cliffs, rock faces, and slopes. Its flowering period occurs in late spring to early summer, producing yellow or white flowers.

Due to its specialized habitat, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species in its native range.
