Dunbaria

Dunbaria, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of fine hairs, giving it a velvety texture. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage and ability to attract pollinators.
