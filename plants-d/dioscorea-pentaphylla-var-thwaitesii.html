Dioscorea pentaphylla var. thwaitesii

Dioscorea pentaphylla var. thwaitesii, commonly known as the African yam bean, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its tuberous roots, which are used as a food source. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by elongated, bean-like fruits. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a rough texture, while its stems are hairy and twining.

This species is valued both for its nutritional content and its role in traditional medicine.
