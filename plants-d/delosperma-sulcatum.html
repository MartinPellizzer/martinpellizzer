Delosperma sulcatum

Delosperma sulcatum, commonly known as the African ice plant, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves with a distinctive waxy coating that helps reduce water loss. The plant produces vibrant clusters of pink or purple flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
