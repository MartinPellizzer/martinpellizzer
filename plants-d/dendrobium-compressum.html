Dendrobium compressum

Dendrobium compressum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a central stem that produces numerous horizontal pseudobulbs. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and often covered in a waxy coating. Its flowers are typically white or pale yellow, with a distinctive lip that is usually darker in color.

Dendrobium compressum thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
