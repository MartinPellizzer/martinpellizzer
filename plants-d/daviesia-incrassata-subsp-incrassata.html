Daviesia incrassata subsp. incrassata

Daviesia incrassata subsp.

incrassata, commonly known as the Broad-leafed Daviesia, is a shrub native to southern Australia. It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is found in regions with a Mediterranean climate.

The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves and produces striking pink or red flowers in spring. It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature. Daviesia incrassata subsp.

incrassata is also valued for its role in local ecosystems and as a nectar source for native pollinators.
