Delphinium pseudograndiflorum var. lobatum

Delphinium pseudograndiflorum var. lobatum, commonly known as the lobed delphinium, is a member of the Ranunculaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features striking blue to violet flowers with spurred petals, which are characteristic of the delphinium genus. Its leaves are deeply lobed and arranged alternately along the stem.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
