Deutzia schneideriana

Deutzia schneideriana, commonly known as Schneiders deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, arching stems and elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It typically grows to a height of 3 to 6 feet and prefers well-drained soil in full sun to partial shade.

Deutzia schneideriana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its showy floral display.
