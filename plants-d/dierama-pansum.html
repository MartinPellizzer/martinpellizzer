Dierama pansum

Dierama pansum, commonly known as the flame lily, is a striking herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, slender stems that bear clusters of bright orange-red flowers, which resemble flames. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically green and slightly hairy. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Dierama pansum is noted for its drought tolerance and ability to bloom reliably in suitable conditions.
