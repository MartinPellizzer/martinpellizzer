Deschampsia cespitosa f. variegata

Deschampsia cespitosa f. variegata, commonly known as the variegated woolly grass, is a variant of the common woolly grass.

It is a perennial grass characterized by its tufted growth habit and fine, silver-gray foliage with distinct variegation. The leaves are narrow and often have a bluish-green hue, contrasting with the lighter colored veins. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky areas.

This ornamental grass is valued for its aesthetic appeal and ability to add texture to garden landscapes.
