Dracaena aubryana

Dracaena aubryana, commonly known as the Madagascar dragon tree, is a striking evergreen shrub native to Madagascar.

It features tall, slender stems with dark green, strap-like leaves that have a glossy sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by red, fleshy fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Dracaena aubryana is also valued for its air-purifying qualities and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
