Desmos pannosus

Desmos pannosus, commonly known as the silky desmos, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and clusters of small, pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are often described as silky to the touch due to their dense covering of fine hairs.

Desmos pannosus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
