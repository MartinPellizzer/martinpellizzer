Dillenia baillonii

Dillenia baillonii, commonly known as the African dillenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and southern Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces large, showy flowers with five to seven petals, typically in shades of white or pink. Its fruit is a distinctive, spiny capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Dillenia baillonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
