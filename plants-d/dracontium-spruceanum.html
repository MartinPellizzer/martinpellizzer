Dracontium spruceanum

Dracontium spruceanum, commonly known as the Amazonian arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves, which are typically a deep green and have a waxy texture. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or yellow in color.

Dracontium spruceanum is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
