Dodonaea truncatiales var. heterophylla

Dodonaea truncatiales var. heterophylla, commonly known as the variable holly fern, is a shrub native to Australia.

It features compound leaves that vary in shape and size, contributing to its common name. The plant produces small, greenish flowers followed by woody, bead-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy habitats.

Its adaptability makes it a resilient species in diverse environmental conditions.
