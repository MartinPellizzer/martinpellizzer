Draba longisiliqua

Draba longisiliqua, commonly known as the long silique draba, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous annual or biennial plant characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in a single terminal inflorescence. Its fruit is a slender, elongated silique that contains several seeds.

Draba longisiliqua is typically found in rocky or alpine habitats and is adapted to cool, moist environments.
