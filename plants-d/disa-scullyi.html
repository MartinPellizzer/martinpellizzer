Disa scullyi

Disa scullyi, commonly known as the Scully's Disa, is a species of orchid native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically feature a combination of white, pink, and purple hues. The plant produces its blooms on tall, slender stems, often reaching heights of up to 60 centimeters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, contributing to its elegant appearance.

Disa scullyi thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or forested areas.
