Dioscorea triphylla var. rotundata

Dioscorea triphylla var. rotundata, commonly known as the arrowhead vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and yellowish flowers. The plant produces edible tubers, though they are often bitter and require processing. It thrives in humid environments and is often found in shaded, moist areas.

Its rapid growth and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for landscaping in suitable climates.
