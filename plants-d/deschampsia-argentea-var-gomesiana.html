Deschampsia argentea var. gomesiana

Deschampsia argentea var. gomesiana, commonly known as silver hairgrass, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-haired foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in open, sunny habitats. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers.

This variety is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and support local biodiversity.
