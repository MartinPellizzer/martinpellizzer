Desmidorchis stocksiana

Desmidorchis stocksiana, commonly known as the Stocks orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water retention.

The orchid has a complex symbiotic relationship with mycorrhizal fungi, which is essential for its nutrient uptake.
