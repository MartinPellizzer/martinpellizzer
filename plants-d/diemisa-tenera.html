Diemisa tenera

Diemisa tenera, commonly known as the slender cycad, is a unique member of the Cycadaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Africa, particularly South Africa. This plant is characterized by its slender, unbranched trunk and a crown of feathery, pinnate leaves. The leaves are typically bright green and can grow quite long, adding a striking visual element to its appearance.

Diemisa tenera is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for gardens and indoor spaces.
