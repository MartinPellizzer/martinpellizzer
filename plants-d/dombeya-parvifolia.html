Dombeya parvifolia

Dombeya parvifolia, commonly known as the small-leaved dombeya, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Africa.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, typically appearing in clusters. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
