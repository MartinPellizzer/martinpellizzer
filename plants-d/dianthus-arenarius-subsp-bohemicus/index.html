Dianthus Arenarius Subsp. Bohemicus

Dianthus arenarius subsp. bohemicus is a rare and delicate plant native to the rocky slopes of central Europe. It thrives in harsh, arid environments where few other species can survive. Known for its vibrant pink flowers and finely textured leaves, it is a striking addition to any botanical collection. This subspecies is often overlooked due to its small size and limited distribution. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique and endangered flora.
