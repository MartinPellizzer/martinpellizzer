Daviesia mollis

Daviesia mollis, commonly known as the silky daviesia, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its soft, silvery foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow-orange flowers during the autumn season. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as woodlands and grasslands.
