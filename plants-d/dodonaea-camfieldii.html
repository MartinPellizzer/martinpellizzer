Dodonaea camfieldii

Dodonaea camfieldii, commonly known as the camfield's glorybower, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, woody growth and striking clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces small, oval-shaped fruits that are typically green when mature. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions.

Dodonaea camfieldii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native pollinators.
