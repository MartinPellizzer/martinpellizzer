Disperis bolusiana subsp. macrocorys

Disperis bolusiana subsp.

macrocorys, commonly known as the large-rooted disperis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family. It is characterized by its slender, herbaceous stems and lanceolate leaves.

The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with a distinctive yellow-green coloration. It is native to tropical Africa, particularly in regions of Kenya and Tanzania.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
