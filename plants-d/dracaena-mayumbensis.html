Dracaena mayumbensis

Dracaena mayumbensis, commonly known as the Mayumbensis Dracaena, is a rare and striking plant native to Cameroon.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and long, narrow, dark green leaves that give it an elegant appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by red berries, which are an important food source for local wildlife. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat loss, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
