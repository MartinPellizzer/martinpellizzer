Dryopteris rivularioides var. crenata

Dryopteris rivularioides var. crenata, commonly known as the crested water fern, is a variant of the water fern species native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, wavy-curved fronds that give it a unique appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. Its fronds are usually a dark green color with a leathery texture.

This variant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens with high humidity.
