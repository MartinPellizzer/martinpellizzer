Diclytra spectabilis

Diclytra spectabilis, commonly known as the showy leaf beetle, is a species of leaf beetle belonging to the family Chrysomelidae.

It is native to North America and is characterized by its striking, often metallic green or blue coloration. The plant it feeds on, typically plants in the genus *Cirsium* (thistles), plays a crucial role in its life cycle. Its larvae and adults both feed on the foliage, contributing to the ecological dynamics of its habitat.

The beetle's bright coloration may serve as a warning signal to predators, indicating its unpalatability.
