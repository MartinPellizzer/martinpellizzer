Dichaea guatemalensis

Dichaea guatemalensis, commonly known as the Guatemalan dichaea, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom during the wet season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, organic soil.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
