Diascia thunbergiana

Diascia thunbergiana, commonly known as the common diascia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces vibrant, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that help it store water, adapting it to arid environments. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its showy blooms attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, contributing to its ecological value.
