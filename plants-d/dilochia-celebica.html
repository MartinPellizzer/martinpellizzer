Dilochia celebica

Dilochia celebica, commonly known as the Celebes orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Maluku Islands of Indonesia.

It is characterized by its striking violet-blue flowers with intricate patterns and a unique, elongated shape. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its native tropical rainforest habitat. Its leaves are elongated and narrow, often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Dilochia celebica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
