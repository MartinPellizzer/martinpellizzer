Draba acinacis

Draba acinacis, commonly known as the swordleaf whitlow grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches heights of 5 to 20 centimeters. The plant features basal leaves that are linear to lanceolate in shape, giving it its common name. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers in racemes during the spring and early summer.

Draba acinacis is native to Europe and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils in alpine or arctic regions.
