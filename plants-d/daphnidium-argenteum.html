Daphnidium argenteum

Daphnidium argenteum, commonly known as the silver daphnid, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, silver-gray foliage and small, clustered white flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to withstand salt spray and arid conditions.

Daphnidium argenteum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
