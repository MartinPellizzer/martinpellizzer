Dioscorea rubricaulis

Dioscorea rubricaulis, commonly known as the red-stem yam, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Dioscoreaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive red-colored stems. The plant produces tuberous roots that are used as a food source in some traditional cuisines. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often have a rough texture.

The flowers are small and unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the same plant.
