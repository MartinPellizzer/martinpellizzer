Delonix regia

Delonix regia, commonly known as the flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, bright orange to red flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The tree has pinnately compound leaves with long, slender leaflets. Its bark is smooth and grayish, becoming fissured with age.

Delonix regia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is widely planted in tropical and subtropical regions.
