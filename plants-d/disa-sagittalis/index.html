Disa Sagittalis

Disa sagittalis is a striking member of the orchid family known for its vibrant, arrow-shaped flowers. Native to southern Africa, this plant thrives in rocky and sandy soils, often found in mountainous regions. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts and horticulturists. The species is also valued for its ability to bloom reliably under proper care. With its elegant form and colorful blooms, Disa sagittalis stands out as a remarkable addition to any garden or collection.
