Derris microptera

Derris microptera, commonly known as the narrow-leaved derris, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, twining stems and pinnate leaves with small, lance-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by elongated, pod-like fruits containing flat, kidney-shaped seeds. It is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forest undergrowth.

The species is notable for its medicinal properties, with various parts used in traditional herbal remedies.
