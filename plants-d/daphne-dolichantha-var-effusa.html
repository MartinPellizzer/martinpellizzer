Daphne dolichantha var. effusa

Daphne dolichantha var. effusa, commonly known as the broad-leafed daphne, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in spring. The leaves are typically broad and leathery, providing a striking visual contrast to the delicate blossoms. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is often cultivated in horticultural settings.
