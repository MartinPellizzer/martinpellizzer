Dendrobium fargesii

Dendrobium fargesii, commonly known as the Farges' orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to China.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems that bear numerous ovate to elliptic leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with showy petals and sepals. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Dendrobium fargesii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
