Dombeya pubescens

Dombeya pubescens, commonly known as the woolly dombeya, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Africa.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a yellow center. The plant has hairy, grayish foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its fragrant blooms and attractive winter form.
