Dendrobium paspalifolium

Dendrobium paspalifolium, commonly known as the paspalum leaf orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It features a pseudobulbous stem system with elongated, grass-like leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellow-green flowers with prominent sepals and petals. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique and attractive species for both horticultural and ecological studies.
