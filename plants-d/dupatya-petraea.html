Dupatya petraea

Dupatya petraea, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It thrives in rocky and well-drained soils, often growing on cliffs or among boulders. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically greenish-white or pale yellow, with intricate patterns on the petals. It is adapted to harsh environmental conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.

Dupatya petraea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in specialized gardens.
