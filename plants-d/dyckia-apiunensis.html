Dyckia apiunensis

Dyckia apiunensis, commonly known as the Apiuna Dyckia, is a species of bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its compact rosette of stiff, grass-like leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces striking, tall inflorescences that bear showy, violet-blue flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Dyckia apiunensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to indoor conditions.
