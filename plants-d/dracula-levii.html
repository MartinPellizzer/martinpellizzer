Dracula levii

Dracula levii, commonly known as the Dragon's Blood Orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and orange flowers, which resemble a dragon's blood, hence its common name. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its unique, elongated petals and sepals. Dracula levii thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific conditions to flourish.

Its conservation status is threatened due to habitat destruction and over-collection.
