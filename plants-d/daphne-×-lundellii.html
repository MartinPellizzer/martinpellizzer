Daphne × lundellii

Daphne × lundellii, commonly known as the winter daphne, is a hybrid evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features glossy, dark green leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, waxy flowers in shades of pink, white, and purple during late winter to early spring. The plant is known for its cold hardiness and ability to thrive in well-drained soils. It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and long-lasting blooms.

Daphne × lundellii is also valued for its low maintenance requirements and adaptability to various garden conditions.
