Dictyosperma aureum

Dictyosperma aureum, commonly known as the golden arrowroot.

It is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia, belonging to the Araceae family. The plant features a rhizomatous root system and produces large, showy inflorescences with bright yellow spadices. Its leaves are broad, arrow-shaped, and often have a waxy texture.

The plant is cultivated for its starchy roots, which are used as a food source and in traditional medicine.
