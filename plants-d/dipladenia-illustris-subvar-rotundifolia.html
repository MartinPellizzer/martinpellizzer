Dipladenia illustris subvar. rotundifolia

Dipladenia illustris subvar. rotundifolia, commonly known as the rounded-leaf Dipladenia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, waxy leaves and vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of orange and yellow. The plant thrives in warm, sunny climates and is often cultivated as a trailing plant or in containers. It requires well-draining soil and regular watering to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in tropical and subtropical environments.
