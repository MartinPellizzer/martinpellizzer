Dopatrium peulhorum

Dopatrium peulhorum, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in shrubland and woodland habitats, often in well-drained soils. It has simple, alternate leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Dopatrium peulhorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
