Dyckia grisebachii

Dyckia grisebachii, commonly known as the silver dyckia, is a perennial bromeliad native to Brazil.

It features rosettes of narrow, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine, white trichomes, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces striking, spikelike inflorescences with bright orange or red flowers. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Dyckia grisebachii is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
