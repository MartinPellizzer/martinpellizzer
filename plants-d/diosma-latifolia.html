Diosma latifolia

Diosma latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf diosma, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features slender, upright stems covered in fine hairs and possesses lance-shaped, alternate leaves. Its small, white or pale pink flowers are clustered in racemes and bloom in the spring.

Diosma latifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
