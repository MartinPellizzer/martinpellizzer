Desmanthus leptostachys

Desmanthus leptostachys, commonly known as the slender-stemmed sensitive plant, is a herbaceous annual plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellow flowers. The plant has a shallow root system that helps prevent soil erosion and improves soil fertility through nitrogen fixation.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a valuable forage plant for livestock.
