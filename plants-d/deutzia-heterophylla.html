Deutzia heterophylla

Deutzia heterophylla, commonly known as the variable leaf deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its diverse foliage, with leaves varying in shape and size along the stems. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers in early spring, which are followed by bright red berries in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to both landscape design and ecological studies.
