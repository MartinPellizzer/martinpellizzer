Dortmanna micrantha

Dortmanna micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered Dortmanna, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features small, white or pale green flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a smooth texture, often forming dense clusters at the base.

Dortmanna micrantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
