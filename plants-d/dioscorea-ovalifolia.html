Dioscorea ovalifolia

Dioscorea ovalifolia, commonly known as the African yam bean, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces tuberous roots that are used as a food source in some regions. Its growth habit involves twining around supports, and it thrives in warm, humid climates.

The botanical features of Dioscorea ovalifolia make it an important species in both ecological and agricultural contexts.
