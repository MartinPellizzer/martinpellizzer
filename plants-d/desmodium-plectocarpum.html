Desmodium plectocarpum

Desmodium plectocarpum, commonly known as hairy desmodium, is a leguminous plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which are typically hairy and have a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes, followed by pods that contain several seeds. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions and is often found in disturbed soils.

Desmodium plectocarpum is known for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it a valuable plant in agricultural and ecological contexts.
