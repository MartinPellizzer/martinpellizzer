Diodosperma burity

Diodosperma burity, commonly known as the Burity plant, is a shrub native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its erect, branching stems covered in small, scale-like leaves. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It is adapted to survive in dry conditions, with deep roots that help it access groundwater.

The Burity plant is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
