Dionycha triangularis

Dionycha triangularis, commonly known as the triangular dionycha, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive triangular-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, adding to its ornamental appeal. Dionycha triangularis thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and unique foliage make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
