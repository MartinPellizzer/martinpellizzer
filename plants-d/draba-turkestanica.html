Draba turkestanica

Draba turkestanica, commonly known as the Turkestan whitlowgrass, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. This species typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months.

Its leaves are basal, rosette-like, and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid climates.
