Daphne lagetto

Daphne lagetto, commonly known as the Italian daphne, is a deciduous shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming in early spring. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves and produces small, berry-like fruits in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Due to its toxicity, it should be handled with care.
