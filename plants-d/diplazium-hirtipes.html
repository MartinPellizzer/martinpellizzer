Diplazium hirtipes

Diplazium hirtipes, commonly known as the hairy shield fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its shield-shaped fronds. The plant features a short, creeping rhizome with hairy, dark brown to black roots. Its fronds are typically bipinnate, with lanceolate pinnae that are covered in fine, soft hairs.

Diplazium hirtipes thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky slopes.
