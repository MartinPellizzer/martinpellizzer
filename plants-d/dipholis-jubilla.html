Dipholis jubilla

Dipholis jubilla, commonly known as the African orchid or the golden orchid, is a species of orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, golden-yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the environment.

Dipholis jubilla is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
