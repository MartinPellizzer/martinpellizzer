Delphinium huashanicum

Delphinium huashanicum, commonly known as the Huashan larkspur, is a perennial flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that bear clusters of blue to purple flowers. The plant features large, palmately lobed leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its striking appearance, it is cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and botanical collections.
