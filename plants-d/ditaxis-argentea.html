Ditaxis argentea

Ditaxis argentea, commonly known as silverleaf dittany, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or limestone areas.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ornamental plant in horticulture.
