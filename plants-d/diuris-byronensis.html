Diuris byronensis

Diuris byronensis, commonly known as the Byron's spider-orchid, is an orchid species native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, spider-like flower structure with a single, elongated spur. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and swampy areas. It produces bright yellow or greenish-yellow flowers during the spring and summer seasons.

Diuris byronensis is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens due to its unique appearance.
