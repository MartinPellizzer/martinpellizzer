Dracocephalum microphyton

Dracocephalum microphyton, commonly known as narrow-leaved blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and spikes of blue to purple flowers. It thrives in dry, sunny environments and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
