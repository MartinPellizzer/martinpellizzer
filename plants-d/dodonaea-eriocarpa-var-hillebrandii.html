Dodonaea eriocarpa var. hillebrandii

Dodonaea eriocarpa var. hillebrandii, commonly known as the silky hopseed, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, silvery foliage covered with fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by small, round, woody fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and dry woodlands.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to local wildlife.
