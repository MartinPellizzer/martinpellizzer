Diplopeltis huegelii subsp. huegelii

Diplopeltis huegelii subsp.

huegelii, commonly known as the African nettle, is a climbing plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Urticaceae family and is characterized by its hairy, opposite leaves and small greenish-white flowers.

The plant produces a distinctive, elongated, spiky fruit that contains numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
