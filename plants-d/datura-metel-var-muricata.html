Datura metel var. muricata

Datura metel var. muricata, commonly known as the Mexican devil's trumpet, is a species of flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

The plant produces spiny, spherical fruits that contain toxic seeds, making it a cautionary species in botanical gardens. Its leaves are ovate with a hairy texture, and it thrives in warm, sunny environments.
