Dioscorea praehensilis var. minutiflora

Dioscorea praehensilis var. minutiflora, commonly known as the miniature climbing yam, is a lesser-known variant of the genus Dioscorea.

It is a herbaceous vine characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces tiny, white or pale yellow flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are capsule-like structures containing numerous small, hard seeds.

This variety is primarily found in tropical and subtropical regions, where it thrives in shaded, moist environments.
