Dendrobium minjemense

Dendrobium minjemense, commonly known as the Minjemense orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to the tropical rainforests of Indonesia.

It is characterized by its compact, cane-like growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, pale yellow to greenish-white flowers. The plant typically blooms during the wet season, showcasing its ornamental value. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in its natural habitat.

Dendrobium minjemense is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
