Dendrobium longipetalum

Dendrobium longipetalum, commonly known as the long-petaled orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its pendulous inflorescences and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, utilizing its aerial roots to absorb moisture and nutrients. Its flowers, which can be white, yellow, or orange, are often adorned with intricate patterns and are highly sought after in horticulture.

Dendrobium longipetalum is known for its resilience and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
