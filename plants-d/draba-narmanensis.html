Draba narmanensis

Draba narmanensis, commonly known as the Narman Draba, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a small, annual herb that typically grows in alpine and rocky environments. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers with four petals, typical of the mustard family. Its leaves are basal, rosette-like, and often covered in fine hairs.

Draba narmanensis is native to the Eastern Mediterranean region, including parts of Turkey and the Caucasus.
