Diospyros dictyoneura

Diospyros dictyoneura, commonly known as the slender persimmon, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Ebenaceae family, which includes other well-known species like ebony and persimmon. The plant is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers. Its fruit is a smooth, orange to reddish berry that ripens in late summer.

The tree is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional medicine.
