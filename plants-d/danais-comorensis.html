Danais comorensis

Danais comorensis, commonly known as the Comoros orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Comoros Islands in the Indian Ocean.

It is a herbaceous plant with tall, slender stems and showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The flowers are typically pale yellow to orange in color, with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
