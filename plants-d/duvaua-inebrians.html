Duvaua inebrians

Duvaua inebrians, commonly known as the醉花 (drunken flower), is a striking perennial plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from deep purple to vibrant red, often emitting a strong, intoxicating fragrance. The plant's leaves are broad and glossy, providing a lush green backdrop to its dramatic blooms. Duvaua inebrians thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil.

Its unique appearance and aromatic properties make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and traditional herbal use.
