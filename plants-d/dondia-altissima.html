Dondia altissima

Dondia altissima, commonly known as the tall dondia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grasslands and open areas, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with a central disc.

Dondia altissima is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
