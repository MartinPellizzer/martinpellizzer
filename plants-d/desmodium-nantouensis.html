Desmodium nantouensis

Desmodium nantouensis, commonly known as the Nantou desmodium, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to China, specifically the province of Fujian, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, typical of the legume family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
