Duvalia polita var. transvaalensis

Duvalia polita var. transvaalensis, commonly known as the Transvaal daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy yellow flowers with a central disk. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in coastal and mountainous regions, where it adapts well to dry and saline conditions.

The leaves are usually narrow and gray-green, providing a striking contrast to the bright yellow blooms.
