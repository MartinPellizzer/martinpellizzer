Delphinium imbricatilobum

Delphinium imbricatilobum, commonly known as the Chinese larkspur, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It features tall, sparsely branched stems that bear clusters of blue to violet flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and prominent veins. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often growing in mountainous regions.

Its botanical characteristics make it a striking addition to gardens and a subject of interest in botanical studies.
