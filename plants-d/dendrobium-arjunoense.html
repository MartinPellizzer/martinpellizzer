Dendrobium arjunoense

Dendrobium arjunoense, commonly known as the Arjuno orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems that often bear clusters of showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, greenish-white or yellowish flowers that bloom during the wet season. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in two rows along the stem.

Dendrobium arjunoense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
