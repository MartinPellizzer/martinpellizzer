Dolichos sericeus subsp. formosus

Dolichos sericeus subsp.

formosus, commonly known as the silky bean, is a climbing legume native to Southeast Asia. It features slender, hairy stems and trifoliate leaves with small, heart-shaped leaflets.

The plant produces elongated, slender pods that contain several small, pale yellow seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is cultivated in gardens and as a forage crop in some regions.
