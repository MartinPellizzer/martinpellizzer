Delosperma ornatulum

Delosperma ornatulum, commonly known as the rainbow ice plant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, ribbed stems and leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces vibrant, daisy-like flowers in shades of pink, red, and yellow, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
