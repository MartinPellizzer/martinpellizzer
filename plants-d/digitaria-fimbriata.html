Digitaria fimbriata

Digitaria fimbriata, commonly known as flatleaf panicgrass, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its flat, narrow leaves and dense, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are often clustered at the base of the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open areas such as fields and roadsides.

Digitaria fimbriata is a hardy plant that can tolerate drought and is sometimes used in erosion control.
