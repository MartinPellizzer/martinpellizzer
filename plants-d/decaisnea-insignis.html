Decaisnea insignis

Decaisnea insignis, commonly known as the red-flowered decaisnea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red flowers that bloom in clusters, making it a visually striking species. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds, aiding in its dispersal.
