Draba norvegica var. berlinii

Draba norvegica var. berlinii, commonly known as the Berlin dwarf scabious, is a low-growing perennial plant native to northern Europe.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family and is characterized by its small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically forms dense mats of narrow, grass-like leaves and has a compact growth habit. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
