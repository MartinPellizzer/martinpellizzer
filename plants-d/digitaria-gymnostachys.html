Digitaria gymnostachys

Digitaria gymnostachys, commonly known as hairy bluestem, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are typically green to bluish in color. It thrives in full sun and is often found in sandy or loamy soils.

Digitaria gymnostachys is valued for its erosion control capabilities and as a forage plant for livestock.
