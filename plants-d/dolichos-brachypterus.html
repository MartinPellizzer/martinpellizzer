Dolichos brachypterus

Dolichos brachypterus, commonly known as the horse gram or cluster bean, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant bears pods that contain kidney-shaped seeds, which are often used in Indian cuisine. Dolichos brachypterus is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil fertility.

It is also valued for its nutritional content, including high levels of protein and dietary fiber.
