Draba Stenoloba Var. Oligantha

Alpine draba, scientifically known as Draba stenoloba var. oligantha, is a hardy flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It thrives in harsh alpine environments, where it adapts to cold temperatures and poor soil conditions. This small plant typically grows to a height of just a few centimeters, displaying delicate white or yellow flowers in the summer months. Its resilience and unique beauty make it a notable species in high-altitude ecosystems. Due to its limited distribution and vulnerability to climate change, it is also of interest to conservationists and botanists alike.
