Diospyros acocksii

Diospyros acocksii, commonly known as the Acock's ebony, is a small evergreen tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Ebenaceae family, which includes other ebony species. The plant is characterized by its dark, dense wood and glossy, elliptical leaves. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by edible, spherical berries.

Diospyros acocksii is often found in moist, forested areas and is valued for its ornamental and timber qualities.
