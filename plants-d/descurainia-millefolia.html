Descurainia millefolia

Descurainia millefolia, commonly known as yellow rocket, is an annual flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and meadows.

Its rapid growth and ability to produce a large number of seeds make it a common weed in agricultural areas.
