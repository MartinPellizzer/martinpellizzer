Disperis natalensis

Disperis natalensis, commonly known as Natal Disperis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest understories or rocky outcrops. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by insects.

Disperis natalensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
