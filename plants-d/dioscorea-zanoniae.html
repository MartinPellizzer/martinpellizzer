Dioscorea zanoniae

Dioscorea zanoniae, commonly known as the Zanonia yam, is a species of flowering plant in the family Dioscoreaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its climbing habit and tuberous roots. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers and has compound leaves with elongated leaflets. Its tubers are often used in traditional medicine and as a food source in some cultures.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
