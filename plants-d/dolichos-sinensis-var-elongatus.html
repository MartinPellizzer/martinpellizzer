Dolichos sinensis var. elongatus

Dolichos sinensis var. elongatus, commonly known as the long-seed lablab, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and trifoliate leaves with serrated margins. The plant produces elongated, pod-like fruits that contain several kidney-shaped seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a warm climate with adequate sunlight.

This variety is valued for its nutritional seeds, which are rich in protein and used in various culinary and medicinal applications.
