Delphinium obscurum

Delphinium obscurum, commonly known as the dark delphinium, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that bear clusters of blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, woodland habitats and prefers partial to full shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Delphinium obscurum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking blooms.
