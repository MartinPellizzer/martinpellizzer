Draba × algida var. breviscapa

Draba × algida var. breviscapa, commonly known as the short-stemmed rockcress, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit, with compact rosettes of basal leaves and small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically thrives in rocky or gravelly soils in alpine or subalpine regions. It is native to the mountainous areas of Europe and parts of Asia.

Due to its adaptability to harsh environments, it is often found in high-altitude meadows and scree slopes.
