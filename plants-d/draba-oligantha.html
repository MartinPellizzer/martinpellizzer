Draba oligantha

Draba oligantha, commonly known as the alpine draba, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in alpine and arctic regions. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in a dense cluster at the top of its stem. Its leaves are basal, rosette-like, and covered with a dense layer of fine hairs.

Draba oligantha is adapted to cold climates and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils.
