Dais cotinifolia

Dais cotinifolia, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically velvety to the touch, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or white in color. It thrives in moist, fertile soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

Dais cotinifolia is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
