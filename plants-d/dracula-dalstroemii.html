Dracula dalstroemii

Dracula dalstroemii, commonly known as the Dragon's Blood Orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its vibrant red petals and sepals, which resemble a dragon's blood, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or branches, thriving in the humid, shaded conditions of its natural habitat. Its flowers are relatively small but highly ornamental, with a unique structure that includes a spurred lip.

Dracula dalstroemii is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is highly sought after by botanists and orchid enthusiasts due to its rarity and beauty.
