Dicksonia hieronymi

Dicksonia hieronymi, commonly known as the Hieronymus tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical rainforests of northern Queensland, Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk and large, pinnately compound fronds that can reach lengths of up to 3 meters. The plant has a unique rhizome system that allows it to grow rapidly and form dense clumps. Its fronds are typically bright green and have a leathery texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Dicksonia hieronymi plays an important ecological role in its native habitat by providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
