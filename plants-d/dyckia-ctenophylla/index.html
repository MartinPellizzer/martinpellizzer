Dyckia Ctenophylla

Ctenophylla dyckia, scientifically known as Dyckia ctenophylla, is a striking member of the Bromeliaceae family native to South America. This perennial plant is recognized for its unique rosette structure and vibrant, sword-like leaves that often display intricate patterns. Dyckia ctenophylla thrives in tropical and subtropical environments, preferring well-drained soil and bright, indirect sunlight. Its ability to store water makes it highly adaptable to arid conditions, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping. With its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements, this plant is a favorite among succulent and bromeliad enthusiasts.
