Ditassa longicaulis

Ditassa longicaulis, commonly known as the long-stemmed ditassa, is a unique plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, slender stems that can reach several meters in height. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pale green to yellow in color. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in a fine, silky texture.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a delicate and less commonly cultivated species.
