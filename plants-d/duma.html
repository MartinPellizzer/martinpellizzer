Duma

Duma, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and chrysanthemums. The plant features showy, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white or pink petals. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Duma is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
