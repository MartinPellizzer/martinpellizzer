Dendrobium nitidicolle

Dendrobium nitidicolle, commonly known as the glossy-leaf dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with cane-like stems that often branch and bear numerous leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange, typically during the summer months. Its leaves are thick, fleshy, and glossy, adapted to store water in its native humid, tropical environments.

Dendrobium nitidicolle is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
