Dracaena congesta

Dracaena congesta, commonly known as the Congo dracaena, is a species of flowering plant in the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, including the Democratic Republic of the Congo. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of narrow, sword-shaped leaves. It produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in terminal spikes.
