Ditassa ferricola

Ditassa ferricola, commonly known as the ironwood tree, is a rare and unique plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, hard wood that is highly resistant to decay, making it valuable for construction and tool-making. The plant has a deep root system that allows it to survive in nutrient-poor soils and extreme drought conditions. Its leaves are small and thick, reducing water loss through transpiration.

Due to its ecological importance and limited distribution, Ditassa ferricola is considered a vulnerable species.
