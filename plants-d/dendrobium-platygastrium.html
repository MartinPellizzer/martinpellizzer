Dendrobium platygastrium

Dendrobium platygastrium, commonly known as the broad-leafed dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit, with branched stems that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically in shades of yellow and white. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its natural habitat.

It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.
