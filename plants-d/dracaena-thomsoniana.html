Dracaena thomsoniana

Dracaena thomsoniana, commonly known as the Thomson's dracaena, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to East Africa.

It features tall, slender, dark green leaves that are often variegated with yellow or white stripes. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by orange-red berries. It is well adapted to grow in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Dracaena thomsoniana is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
