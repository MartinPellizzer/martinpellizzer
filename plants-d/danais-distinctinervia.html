Danais distinctinervia

Danais distinctinervia, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a tropical plant characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The leaves are typically dark green with a distinct network of veins, giving it the species name *distinctinervia*. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or pale yellow.

This plant is often found in wetlands and marshy areas, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils.
