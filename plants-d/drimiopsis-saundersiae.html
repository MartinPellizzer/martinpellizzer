Drimiopsis saundersiae

Drimiopsis saundersiae, commonly known as the Cape drimiopsis, is a perennial evergreen plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its rosette of narrow, fleshy leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers during the winter months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Drimiopsis saundersiae is relatively low maintenance and suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
