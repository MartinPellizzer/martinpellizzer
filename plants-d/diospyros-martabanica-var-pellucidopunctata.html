Diospyros martabanica var. pellucidopunctata

Diospyros martabanica var. pellucidopunctata, commonly known as the translucent-punctate persimmon, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and alternate, ovate leaves with a glossy upper surface. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in axillary catkins, followed by orange to reddish fruit with a thick, astringent skin.

Its leaves and fruits are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
