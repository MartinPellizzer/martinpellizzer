Dendrobium angustiflorum

Dendrobium angustiflorum, commonly known as the narrow-flowered orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features tall, cane-like stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers with narrow, lance-shaped petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrobium angustiflorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
