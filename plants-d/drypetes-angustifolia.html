Drypetes angustifolia

Drypetes angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed drypetes, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elliptical, glossy leaves that are typically narrow and pointed. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by small, round fruits that contain a single seed. It is often found in lowland forest areas and is valued for its ornamental qualities.

The species is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
