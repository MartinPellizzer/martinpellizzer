Drimiopsis burkei subsp. burkei

Drimiopsis burkei subsp.

burkei, commonly known as the Cape drimiopsis, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to South Africa. It features narrow, strap-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy texture.

The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months, which are clustered in umbels. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often cultivated in rock gardens and as a houseplant.
