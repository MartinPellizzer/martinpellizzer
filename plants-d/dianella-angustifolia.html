Dianella angustifolia

Dianella angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf flax lily, is a perennial evergreen plant native to Australia.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically a dark green color, arranged in a rosette formation. The plant produces striking blue or purple flowers on tall, slender stems during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to a variety of soil types and is often found in coastal and woodland areas.

Dianella angustifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly used in gardens and landscaping.
