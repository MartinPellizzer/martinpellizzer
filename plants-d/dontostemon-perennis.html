Dontostemon perennis

Dontostemon perennis, commonly known as the everlasting daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals. The plant is known for its ability to retain its color and form for extended periods, hence its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is complemented by its resilience to drought and resistance to many pests.
