Didymocheton urens

Didymocheton urens, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sago palm, is a species of cycad native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk and pinnate leaves that are narrow and feather-like. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in tropical climates and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its slow growth and long lifespan, it is a popular choice for gardens and conservation efforts.
