Digitaria stolzii

Digitaria stolzii, commonly known as African grass or stolzii grass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear minute, wind-pollinated flowers. It is often found in open grasslands and disturbed soils, showing adaptability to various environmental conditions.

Digitaria stolzii is also used in some traditional practices and has potential for ecological and agricultural applications.
