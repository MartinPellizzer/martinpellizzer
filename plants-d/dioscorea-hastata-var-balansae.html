Dioscorea hastata var. balansae

Dioscorea hastata var. balansae, commonly known as the Balansae yam, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Dioscoreaceae family and is characterized by its hairy, cylindrical stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by edible tuberous roots that are often used in local cuisines.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and agricultural contexts.
