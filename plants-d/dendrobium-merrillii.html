Dendrobium merrillii

Dendrobium merrillii, commonly known as the golden spike orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with multiple pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces striking, long-lasting yellow flowers with red or orange markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrobium merrillii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
