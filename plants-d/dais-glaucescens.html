Dais glaucescens

Dais glaucescens, commonly known as the blue daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flower heads, which are surrounded by white ray florets. The plant typically grows in open, sunny environments and prefers well-drained soil. It is a perennial herb that produces small, green, oval-shaped leaves.

Dais glaucescens is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
