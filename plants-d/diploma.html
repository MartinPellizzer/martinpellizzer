Diploma

Diploma, commonly known as *Clematis vitalba*, is a climbing vine native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is characterized by its woody stems and opposite, simple, ovate leaves that are usually hairy. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, blue, or purple, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its berries are dark blue or black and contain several seeds.

While it is ornamental in gardens, it can become invasive in certain regions.
