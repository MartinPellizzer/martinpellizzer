Draba sherriffii

Draba sherriffii, commonly known as Sherriff's whitlow grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a small, mat-forming perennial that typically grows in alpine and arctic environments. The plant produces tiny, clustered white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are basal, linear, and often covered in fine hairs.

Draba sherriffii is adapted to cold climates and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils.
