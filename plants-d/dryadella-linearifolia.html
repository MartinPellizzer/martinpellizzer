Dryadella linearifolia

Dryadella linearifolia, commonly known as the slender leaf dryad, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color, which bloom in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
