Dysoxylum pauciflorum

Dysoxylum pauciflorum, commonly known as the few-flowered dysoxylum, is a tropical tree belonging to the family Meliaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and simple, alternate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, and its fruit is a woody capsule containing several seeds.
