Dendrobium gracilicolle

Dendrobium gracilicolle, commonly known as the slender-stemmed orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic orchid that grows on trees and rocks, relying on atmospheric moisture rather than soil. The plant produces long, slender stems that can reach up to 1 meter in length. Its flowers are typically greenish-yellow with a subtle fragrance, blooming during the dry season.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
