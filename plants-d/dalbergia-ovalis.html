Dalbergia ovalis

Dalbergia ovalis, commonly known as the oval legume or African rosewood, is a flowering plant species native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant is notable for its hard, heavy wood, which is highly valued for furniture and musical instruments. It thrives in tropical and subtropical regions, often found in woodlands and savannas.

Its ecological importance includes providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
