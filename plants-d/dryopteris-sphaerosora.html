Dryopteris sphaerosora

Dryopteris sphaerosora, commonly known as the globe shield fern, is a species of fern native to the forests of eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, rounded fronds that give it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and prefers well-drained soil. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a leathery texture.

Dryopteris sphaerosora is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and ability to thrive in shaded garden settings.
