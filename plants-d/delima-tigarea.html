Delima tigarea

Delima tigarea, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, drooping clusters of yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

Delima tigarea thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
