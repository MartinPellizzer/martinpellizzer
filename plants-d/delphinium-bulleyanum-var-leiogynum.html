Delphinium bulleyanum var. leiogynum

Delphinium bulleyanum var. leiogynum, commonly known as the smoothleaf larkspur, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It features tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant produces showy blue to violet flowers in spikes, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a smooth, waxy texture, distinguishing it from other larkspur varieties.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and natural landscapes.
