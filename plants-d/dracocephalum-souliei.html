Dracocephalum souliei

Dracocephalum souliei, commonly known as Chinese blueweed, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, clustered purple flowers. It is often found in mountainous regions and is valued for its medicinal properties.

Dracocephalum souliei is also used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and antispasmodic effects.
