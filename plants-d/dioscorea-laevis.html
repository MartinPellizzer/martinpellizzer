Dioscorea laevis

Dioscorea laevis, commonly known as the smooth yam, is a perennial vine native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its climbing habit and tuberous roots. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Its tubers are valued for their nutritional content and have been used in traditional medicine.

Dioscorea laevis is also an important crop in some regions for both food and medicinal purposes.
