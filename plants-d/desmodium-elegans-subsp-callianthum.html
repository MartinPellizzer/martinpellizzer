Desmodium elegans subsp. callianthum

Desmodium elegans subsp.

callianthum, commonly known as the pink burr legume, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East. It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters.

The plant is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and distinctive, hairy, pink or purple flowers that bloom in clusters. Its seed pods are inflated and often contain several small, rounded seeds.

This species is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
