Dactyliota setigera

Dactyliota setigera, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a member of the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Dactyliota setigera is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its ecological role in supporting pollinators.
