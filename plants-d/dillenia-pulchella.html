Dillenia pulchella

Dillenia pulchella, commonly known as the beautiful dillenia, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers with five petals, which are typically borne in clusters. Its fruit is a woody, spiny capsule that splits open to release small, hairy seeds.

Dillenia pulchella is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
