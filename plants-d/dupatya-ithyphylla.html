Dupatya ithyphylla

Dupatya ithyphylla, commonly known as the slender leaf orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, linear leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, typically blooming during the summer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in greenhouses and botanical gardens.
