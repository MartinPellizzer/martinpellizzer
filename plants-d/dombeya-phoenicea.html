Dombeya phoenicea

Dombeya phoenicea, commonly known as the flame dombeya, is a deciduous shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, pink to red tubular flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has smooth, grey-green leaves that are opposite and elliptical in shape. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Dombeya phoenicea is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
