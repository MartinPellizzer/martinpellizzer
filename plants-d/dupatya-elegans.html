Dupatya elegans

Dupatya elegans, commonly known as the elegant orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, showy flowers with a distinctive color pattern. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to tree trunks in humid, tropical environments. Its leaves are broad and leathery, adapted to retain moisture in its natural habitat.

Dupatya elegans is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
