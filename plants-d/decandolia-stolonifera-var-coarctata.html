Decandolia stolonifera var. coarctata

Decandolia stolonifera var. coarctata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved stoloniferous decandolia, is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its creeping growth habit and stolons.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stems. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

This variant is noted for its compact growth and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens.
