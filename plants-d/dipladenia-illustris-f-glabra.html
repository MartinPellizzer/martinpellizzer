Dipladenia illustris f. glabra

Dipladenia illustris f. glabra, commonly known as the smooth dipladenia, is a hybrid plant in the Cucurbitaceae family, characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers.

It features smooth, waxy leaves that are typically a deep green, contrasting with its showy, often orange or yellow flowers. The plant is native to South America and thrives in warm, sunny conditions with well-draining soil. It is often cultivated as a decorative vine in gardens and hanging baskets due to its ornamental value.

The 'glabra' variety is distinguished by its lack of hairy or fuzzy texture on its foliage, giving it a sleek appearance.
