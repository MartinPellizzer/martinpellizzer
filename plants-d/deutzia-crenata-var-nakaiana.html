Deutzia crenata var. nakaiana

Deutzia crenata var. nakaiana, commonly known as the Japanese Deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features clusters of fragrant, white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, arching branches with small, ovate leaves that turn red in the fall. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in gardens for its delicate floral display.
