Dracocephalum bungeanum

Dracocephalum bungeanum, commonly known as Chinese skullcap, is a perennial herb native to China and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants such as mint and oregano. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
