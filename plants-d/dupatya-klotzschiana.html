Dupatya klotzschiana

Dupatya klotzschiana, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Dupatya klotzschiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been introduced to other tropical regions for horticultural purposes.
