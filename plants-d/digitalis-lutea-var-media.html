Digitalis lutea var. media

Digitalis lutea var. media, commonly known as the tall foxglove, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe.

It produces tall spikes of tubular, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade.

Its flowers attract pollinators like bees and butterflies, making it a valuable addition to gardens.
