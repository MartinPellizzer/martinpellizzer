Dipladenia flava

Dipladenia flava, commonly known as the yellow trumpet vine, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, sunny climates and requires well-draining soil.

Dipladenia flava is a climbing plant that can be trained to grow on trellises or supports.
