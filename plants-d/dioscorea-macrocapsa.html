Dioscorea macrocapsa

Dioscorea macrocapsa, commonly known as the giant yam, is a climbing vine belonging to the family Dioscoreaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific Islands. The plant produces large, tuberous roots that are often used as a food source in various cultures. Its leaves are heart-shaped and can grow up to 30 centimeters in diameter.

The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires a warm climate for optimal growth.
