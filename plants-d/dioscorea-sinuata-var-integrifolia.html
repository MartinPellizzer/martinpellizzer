Dioscorea sinuata var. integrifolia

Dioscorea sinuata var. integrifolia, commonly known as the smooth-leaf yam, is a climbing vine native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with smooth margins and produces small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant's tubers are edible and have been used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires support for its vigorous growth.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species in both horticulture and agriculture.
