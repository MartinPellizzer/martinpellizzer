Dichapetalum adolfi-friederici

Dichapetalum adolfi-friederici, commonly known as the African star flower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun exposure. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
