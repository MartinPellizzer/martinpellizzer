Diuris calcicola

Diuris calcicola, commonly known as the limestone diuris, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Australia.

It typically grows in calcareous soils, often found in rocky or sandy habitats. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single or few flowers that are usually pale yellow to greenish in color. Its flowers have a distinctive pouch-like structure, typical of the orchid family, and are often adorned with intricate patterns.

Diuris calcicola is known for its resilience in harsh, arid environments.
