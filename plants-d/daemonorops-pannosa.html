Daemonorops pannosa

Daemonorops pannosa, commonly known as the pandan plant, is a species of cycad native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces small, cone-like structures that contain the seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, shaded environments.

The plant is widely cultivated for its aromatic leaves, which are used in cooking and traditional crafts.
