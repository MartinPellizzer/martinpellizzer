Dendrochilum macranthum

Dendrochilum macranthum, commonly known as the large-flowered tree orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its woody stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrochilum macranthum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
