Diplusodon capitalensis

Diplusodon capitalensis, commonly known as the Cape Peninsula daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white and yellow.

Diplusodon capitalensis is often found in the fynbos vegetation of the Western Cape region.
