Dendrobium jonesii var. jonesii

Dendrobium jonesii var. jonesii, commonly known as the Jones' orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features erect, branching pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, typical of many orchid species. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often in clusters along the stems. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Dendrobium jonesii var. jonesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
