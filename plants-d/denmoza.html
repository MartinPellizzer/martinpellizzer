Denmoza

Denmoza, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom on long, winding stems emerging from water. The plant has large, heart-shaped leaves that float on the water's surface. Its flowers can be pink, white, or yellow, depending on the species.

Denmoza plays a significant role in both cultural and medicinal practices in its native regions.
