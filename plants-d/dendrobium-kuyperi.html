Dendrobium kuyperi

Dendrobium kuyperi, commonly known as the Kuyper's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit, with thick, fleshy stems that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often in clusters. Its leaves are strap-like and arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem.

Dendrobium kuyperi thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media for optimal growth.
