Draba funiculosa

Draba funiculosa, commonly known as the rock sandwort, is a low-growing flowering plant native to alpine and arctic regions.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many mustard plants. The plant typically has basal leaves and small, white or yellowish flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to cold climates.

Draba funiculosa is often found in mountainous areas of Europe, Asia, and North America.
