Dendrobium aurantiacopurpureum

Dendrobium aurantiacopurpureum, commonly known as the flame orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, pendulous inflorescences with strikingly colorful flowers that range from orange to deep purple. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are often fragrant and can bloom multiple times a year under optimal conditions.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and adaptability to various growing environments.
