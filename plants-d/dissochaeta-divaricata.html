Dissochaeta divaricata

Dissochaeta divaricata, commonly known as the variable dissochaeta, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in open woodland or grassland habitats.

Dissochaeta divaricata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
