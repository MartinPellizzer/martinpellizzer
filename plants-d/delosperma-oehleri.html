Delosperma oehleri

Delosperma oehleri, commonly known as the "ice plant," is a low-growing, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that help it store water, adapting to arid environments. The plant produces vibrant, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, and orange, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ability to withstand drought and poor soil conditions makes it an excellent plant for landscaping in dry climates.
