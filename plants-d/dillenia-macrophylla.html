Dillenia macrophylla

Dillenia macrophylla, commonly known as the giant dillenia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers with prominent, fleshy petals that attract pollinators such as birds and insects. Its fruit is a large, woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Dillenia macrophylla is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
