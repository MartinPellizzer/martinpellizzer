Drymaria arenarioides subsp. arenarioides

Drymaria arenarioides subsp.

arenarioides, commonly known as sandspurry, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arid and semi-arid regions. It features small, fleshy, succulent leaves that help it retain moisture in dry environments.

The plant produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in disturbed areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it well-adapted to harsh, dry climates.
