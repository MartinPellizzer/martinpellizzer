Dendrobium inamoenum

Dendrobium inamoenum, commonly known as the red-stemmed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. Its leaves are elliptical and arranged in two rows along the stem.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
