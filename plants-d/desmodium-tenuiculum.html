Desmodium tenuiculum

Desmodium tenuiculum, commonly known as slender milkvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is a low-growing annual or perennial herb characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant is native to various regions in the Americas and is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has a deep root system that helps it survive in poor soil conditions and is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, improving soil fertility.

Its small flowers are typically pink or purple and bloom in early spring.
