Daubentonia tripetii

Daubentonia tripetii, commonly known as the Madagascar star orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers with a distinctive yellow and white coloration. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, producing new shoots from the base of the previous ones. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the humid, tropical environment of its native habitat.

Due to its rarity and specific ecological requirements, it is considered a conservation concern.
