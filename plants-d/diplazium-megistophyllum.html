Diplazium megistophyllum

Diplazium megistophyllum, commonly known as the giant chain fern, is a tropical fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, compound fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with creeping stems that produce new shoots. Its fronds are divided into multiple pinnae, which are further subdivided into smaller pinnules.

This species is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks.
