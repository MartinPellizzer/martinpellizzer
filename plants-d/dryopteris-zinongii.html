Dryopteris zinongii

Dryopteris zinongii, commonly known as the Zinong's wood fern, is a species of fern belonging to the Dryopteris genus.

It is native to eastern Asia, particularly China, where it thrives in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant features fronds that are typically bipinnate, with a distinctive, elegant appearance. Its rhizomes are thick and fleshy, allowing it to store moisture and nutrients effectively.

This fern is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens with suitable humidity and light conditions.
