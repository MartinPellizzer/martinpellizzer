Dryopteris kotoensis

Dryopteris kotoensis, commonly known as the Koto Shield Fern, is a species of fern native to Japan.

It belongs to the Dryopteris genus, which includes various species of shield ferns. The plant is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and fronds that are typically bipinnate, with a distinctive, intricate leaf structure. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

The Koto Shield Fern is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in horticulture for its aesthetic foliage.
