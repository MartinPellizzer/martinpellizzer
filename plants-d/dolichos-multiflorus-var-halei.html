Dolichos multiflorus var. halei

Dolichos multiflorus var. halei, commonly known as the Hale's clusterbean, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant produces elongated, slender pods that contain several small, edible seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for both its ornamental value and its nutritious seeds.

The leaves are trifoliate, with three oval-shaped leaflets that are often hairy in texture.
