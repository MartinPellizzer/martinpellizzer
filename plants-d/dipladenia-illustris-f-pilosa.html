Dipladenia illustris f. pilosa

Dipladenia illustris f. pilosa, commonly known as the hairy dipladenia, is a compact, trailing plant native to South America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of orange and yellow, which bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has velvety, hairy leaves that give it a distinctive texture and appearance. It thrives in warm, sunny conditions and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular in hanging baskets and as a decorative plant due to its vibrant blooms and unique foliage.
