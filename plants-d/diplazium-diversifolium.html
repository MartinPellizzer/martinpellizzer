Diplazium diversifolium

Diplazium diversifolium, commonly known as the variable fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that are typically bi- or tri-pinnate. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical forests. Its leaves are usually dark green and have a somewhat leathery texture.

Diplazium diversifolium is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
