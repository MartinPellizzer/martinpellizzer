Daviesia ulicifolia subsp. ulicifolia

Daviesia ulicifolia subsp.

ulicifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed mountain pea, is a shrub native to southern Australia. It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils in open woodlands and heathlands. It is well adapted to dry conditions and can thrive in full sun.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
