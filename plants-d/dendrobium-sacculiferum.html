Dendrobium sacculiferum

Dendrobium sacculiferum, commonly known as the sacculiferum orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, cane-like stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange, often blooming during the summer months. Its leaves are typically elongated and arranged in a rosette pattern along the stem.

Dendrobium sacculiferum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
