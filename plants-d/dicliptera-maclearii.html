Dicliptera maclearii

Dicliptera maclearii, commonly known as Maclear's grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open grasslands and along riverbanks, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It is characterized by its erect growth habit and is often used in ornamental gardening.

Botanically, it belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes many common grasses.
