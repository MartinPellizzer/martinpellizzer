Dichapetalum nigrescens var. pubescens

Dichapetalum nigrescens var. pubescens, commonly known as the hairy blackwood, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Malvaceae and is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, hairy pubescence. The hairy blackwood is often found in forest understories and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
