Draba glebaria

Draba glebaria, commonly known as the alpine draba, is a small flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cress plants. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes, where it forms dense cushions or low mats. Its flowers are small, yellow or white, and appear in early spring.

Draba glebaria is adapted to cold climates and can survive in harsh, nutrient-poor environments.
