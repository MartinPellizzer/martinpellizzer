Davallia trichomanoides var. lorrainii

Davallia trichomanoides var. lorrainii, commonly known as the Lorrain's shield fern, is a varietal form of the shield fern.

It is a terrestrial fern characterized by its delicate, fern-like fronds that are typically bright green. The plant produces spore-bearing structures called sori on the underside of its fronds. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
