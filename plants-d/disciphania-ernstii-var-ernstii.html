Disciphania ernstii var. ernstii

Disciphania ernstii var. ernstii, commonly known as the African violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical Africa.

It features fleshy, dark green leaves that are typically velvety in texture. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. It is well adapted to humid environments and thrives in well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular houseplant worldwide.
