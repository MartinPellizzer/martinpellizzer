Disparago kolbei

Disparago kolbei, commonly known as the African starwort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its upright, branched stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, while the flowers are usually pink or red in color.

Disparago kolbei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
