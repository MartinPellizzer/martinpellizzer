Dryopteris daliensis

Dryopteris daliensis, commonly known as the Dali water fern, is a species of fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lacy fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky slopes. Its leaves are usually bipinnate, with a distinctive appearance that resembles a fern's traditional structure.

Dryopteris daliensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
