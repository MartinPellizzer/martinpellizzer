Dendrobium × gommeri

Dendrobium × gommeri, commonly known as the Gommeri orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. The plant features long, cane-like stems that are often covered in clusters of showy, fragrant flowers. Its blooms come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and yellow, and can last for several weeks.

Dendrobium × gommeri is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
