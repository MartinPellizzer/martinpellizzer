Deutzia planiflora

Deutzia planiflora, commonly known as flatleaf deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features slender stems and clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has oval-shaped leaves that are typically arranged oppositely along the branches. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

Deutzia planiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
