Diadenium ecuadorense

Diadenium ecuadorense, commonly known as the Ecuadorean star cactus, is a species of cactus native to Ecuador.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and spiny, ribbed stems. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months, which are typically clustered at the top of the plant. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Diadenium ecuadorense is valued for its ornamental appearance and resilience in harsh conditions.
