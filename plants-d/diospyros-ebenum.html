Diospyros ebenum

Diospyros ebenum, commonly known as the ebony tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark, dense wood, which is highly valued for its strength and fine grain. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and glossy in appearance. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by small, round fruits that are typically edible.

The tree is often found in lowland rainforests and thrives in humid, tropical climates.
