Disynaphia multicrenulata

Disynaphia multicrenulata, commonly known as the zigzagging fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, undulating fronds that give it its common name. The plant features a creeping rhizome with numerous short, branching roots. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with a unique zigzag pattern along the midrib.

Disynaphia multicrenulata thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
