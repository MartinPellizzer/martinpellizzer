Doxantha capreolata

Doxantha capreolata, commonly known as the rock daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas.
