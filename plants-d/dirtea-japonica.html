Dirtea japonica

Dirtea japonica, commonly known as Japanese snowbell, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features delicate, bell-shaped white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and a bluish-green hue. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Dirtea japonica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscape design.
