Desmodium varians var. gunnii

Desmodium varians var. gunnii, commonly known as the variable desmodium, is a perennial legume native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It features trifoliate leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, pea-like flowers in shades of pink to purple. The plant is often found in disturbed soils and is adapted to a variety of environmental conditions. It has a shallow root system and is known for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in soil enrichment.

Its ornamental value and ecological benefits make it a notable species in botanical studies.
