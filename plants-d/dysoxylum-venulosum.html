Dysoxylum venulosum

Dysoxylum venulosum, commonly known as the veiny hibiscus, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with prominent venation. The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some indigenous cultures.
