Dracocephalum aitchisonii

Dracocephalum aitchisonii, commonly known as Aitchison's dragonhead, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or blue in color, arranged in whorls along the stems. It is often found in rocky or alpine habitats, where it thrives in well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and traditional herbal practices.
