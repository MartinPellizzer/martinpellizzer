Dombeya gossweileri

Dombeya gossweileri, commonly known as the African dombeya, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in clusters of white or pink petals. The plant has elliptical, dark green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Dombeya gossweileri is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
