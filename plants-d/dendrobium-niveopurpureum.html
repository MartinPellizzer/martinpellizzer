Dendrobium niveopurpureum

Dendrobium niveopurpureum, commonly known as the Purple-White Dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit, with cane-like stems that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and purple, often in clusters along the stems. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its unique coloration and elegant appearance make it a popular choice for both horticultural and ornamental purposes.
