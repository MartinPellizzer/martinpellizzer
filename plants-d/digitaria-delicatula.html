Digitaria delicatula

Digitaria delicatula, commonly known as the slender digitgrass, is a perennial grass native to the Americas.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in prairies, wetlands, and along stream banks. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences. Its flowering period occurs in the summer months, and it is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Digitaria delicatula is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
