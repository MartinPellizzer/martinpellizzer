Dendrochilum apoense

Dendrochilum apoense, commonly known as the Apo pineapple orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in montane forest habitats, often in shaded, humid environments. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, while its roots are fibrous and adapted to nutrient-poor soils.

Dendrochilum apoense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
