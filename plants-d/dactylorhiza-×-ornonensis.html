Dactylorhiza × ornonensis

Dactylorhiza × ornonensis, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to western Europe.

It is a hybrid between Dactylorhiza macdougallii and Dactylorhiza fuchsii, exhibiting characteristics of both parents. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and heathlands. It produces tall, slender stems with showy, violet or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
