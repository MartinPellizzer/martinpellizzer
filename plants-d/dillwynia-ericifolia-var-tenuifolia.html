Dillwynia ericifolia var. tenuifolia

Dillwynia ericifolia var. tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender-leaved dillwynia, is a small shrub native to southern Australia.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers clustered in dense inflorescences, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to dry, sunny conditions.

This variety is part of the legume family and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
