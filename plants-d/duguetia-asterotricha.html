Duguetia asterotricha

Duguetia asterotricha, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

Duguetia asterotricha is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in warm climates for its aesthetic appeal.
