Dracaena × hibberdii

Dracaena × hibberdii, commonly known as the Jade Plant or Silver Jade, is a hybrid succulent plant originating from the hybridization of Dracaena deremensis and Dracaena marginata.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are typically a deep green with a silver or white margin. The plant is native to southern Africa and is widely cultivated as a decorative indoor plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor environments.

Dracaena × hibberdii is also known for its ability to purify air and is often used in interior landscaping.
