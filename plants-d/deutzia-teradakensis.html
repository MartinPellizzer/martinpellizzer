Deutzia teradakensis

Deutzia teradakensis, commonly known as the Japanese deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its clusters of white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. Deutzia teradakensis is also valued for its ability to tolerate a range of soil conditions and its relatively low maintenance requirements.
