Desmodium oblongifolium

Desmodium oblongifolium, commonly known as purple needlepod, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States and parts of Mexico.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds, which are often dispersed by wind.

Desmodium oblongifolium is often found in disturbed soils and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
