Dioscorea glabra var. vera

Dioscorea glabra var. vera, commonly known as wild yam, is a perennial vine native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its climbing habit and hairy, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by cylindrical, spiny fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a warm, humid climate for optimal growth.

Wild yam is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional medicine for various health purposes.
