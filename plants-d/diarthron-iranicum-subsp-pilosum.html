Diarthron iranicum subsp. pilosum

Diarthron iranicum subsp.

pilosum, commonly known as the hairy diarthron, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Iran. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant is distinguished by its hairy stems and leaves, which provide protection against harsh environmental conditions. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months.

This subspecies is often found in rocky and arid habitats, showcasing its adaptability to challenging climates.
