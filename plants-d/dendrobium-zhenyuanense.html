Dendrobium zhenyuanense

Dendrobium zhenyuanense, commonly known as the Zhenyuan orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect pseudobulbs and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically blooms in late summer, producing bright yellow or orange flowers with intricate patterns. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool temperatures and high humidity.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
