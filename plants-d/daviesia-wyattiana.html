Daviesia wyattiana

Daviesia wyattiana, commonly known as the Wyatt's Daviesia, is a flowering plant native to western Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, fern-like foliage. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow or orange flowers during the spring season. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
