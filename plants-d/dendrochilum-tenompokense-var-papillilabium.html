Dendrochilum tenompokense var. papillilabium

Dendrochilum tenompokense var. papillilabium, commonly known as the papillilabium orchid, is a tropical orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its elongated, pendulous inflorescences and flowers that are typically yellow to orange in color. The plant features strap-like leaves and is often found growing in shaded, humid environments. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
