Dendranthema × konoanum

Dendranthema × konoanum, commonly known as the Shishiohime chrysanthemum, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Chrysanthemum morifolium and Chrysanthemum indicum.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. The plant produces a variety of flower colors, including white, pink, red, and yellow, depending on the cultivar. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Dendranthema × konoanum is widely appreciated in horticulture for its resilience and aesthetic appeal.
