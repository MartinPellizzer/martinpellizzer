Durio dulcis

Durio dulcis, commonly known as the durian, is a tropical fruit-bearing tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, spiky, green exterior and its strong, complex aroma. The fruit contains a creamy, custard-like pulp that is highly prized for its rich flavor. Durio dulcis thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its botanical features make it a unique and economically significant plant in the region.
