Diplusodon cordifolius

Diplusodon cordifolius, commonly known as the heart-leaf tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by edible, orange-colored fruits. Its foliage is dense, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.
