Doronicum romanum

Doronicum romanum, commonly known as the Roman leopard's bane, is a perennial plant native to the Balkan region.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy yellow flowers. Doronicum romanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
