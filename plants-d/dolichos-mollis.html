Dolichos mollis

Dolichos mollis, commonly known as the velvet bean, is a leguminous plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and trifoliate leaves, which are often covered in a fine, velvety pubescence. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that give way to elongated, pod-like fruits containing edible seeds. It is valued for its nutritional content, as the seeds are high in protein and used in various cuisines.

Additionally, Dolichos mollis plays a role in soil enrichment due to its nitrogen-fixing capabilities.
