Dierama densiflorum

Dierama densiflorum, commonly known as the denseflower dierama, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color. The plant produces dense clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Dierama densiflorum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
