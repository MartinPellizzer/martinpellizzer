Dalbergia millettii var. mimosoides

Dalbergia millettii var. mimosoides, commonly known as the African ebony, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It has dark, dense wood that is highly valued for its durability and aesthetic appeal. The plant features alternate, pinnately compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It produces small, fragrant flowers followed by woody pods containing flat, lens-shaped seeds.

This species is often found in moist, well-drained soils in forested areas.
