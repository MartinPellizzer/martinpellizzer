Ditaxis rosularis

Ditaxis rosularis, commonly known as the rock rose, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact, cushion-like growth habit with dense clusters of fleshy, blue-green leaves. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of pink and white during the spring and summer seasons. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is often cultivated in rock gardens and xeriscapes.
