Dierama pauciflorum

Dierama pauciflorum, commonly known as the silver flame or fireplant, is a striking South African succulent with distinctive, flame-like red or orange bracts.

It features a basal rosette of fleshy, silver-green leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. The plant produces tall, erect flowering stems with showy, tubular flowers that attract pollinators like birds and insects. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a favored ornamental plant in dry climates.
