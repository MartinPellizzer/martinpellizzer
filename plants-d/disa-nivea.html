Disa nivea

Disa nivea, commonly known as the snow disa, is a striking South African orchid species.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of white or pale pink flowers that resemble snowflakes. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils in open woodland areas. Its flowers are highly fragrant, attracting various pollinators such as bees and moths.

Disa nivea is a popular ornamental plant due to its elegant appearance and unique floral structure.
