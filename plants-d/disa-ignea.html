Disa ignea

Disa ignea, commonly known as the flame flower, is a striking South African orchid species native to the Eastern Cape region.

It is characterized by its vivid red and orange flowers, which are adorned with intricate patterns resembling flames. The plant produces its showy blooms on tall, slender stems during the summer months. Disa ignea thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Its unique appearance and seasonal flowering make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
