Dendrobium deflexilobum

Dendrobium deflexilobum, commonly known as the narrow-lobed dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with cane-like stems that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with narrow, elongated petals and sepals. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Dendrobium deflexilobum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
