Dryopteris × complexa subsp. critica

Dryopteris × complexa subsp.

critica, commonly known as the critical shield fern, is a hybrid species within the Dryopteris genus, characterized by its robust and dense fronds. It is native to the Pacific Northwest of North America, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks.

The plant exhibits a distinctive morphology with large, leathery, dark green leaves that often have a slightly curled appearance. Its reproductive structures include sporangia that develop on the undersides of the fronds, typical of ferns.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
