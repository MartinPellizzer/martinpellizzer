Dieffenbachia conspurcata

Dieffenbachia conspurcata, commonly known as the leopard plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with dark green leaves marked by yellow and white speckles. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions. Its leaves are thick and waxy, which helps it retain moisture in its natural habitat.

Dieffenbachia conspurcata is a popular houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
