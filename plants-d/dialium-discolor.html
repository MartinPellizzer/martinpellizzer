Dialium discolor

Dialium discolor, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 20 meters tall, with a straight trunk and a broad, rounded crown. The leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptical, with a dark green upper surface and a lighter underside. Its small, fragrant flowers are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters, and it produces small, round fruits that are yellow when mature.

The plant is valued for its hard, durable wood and is often found in moist, lowland forests.
