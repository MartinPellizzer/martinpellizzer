Dactylorhiza × aschersoniana nothovar. mulignensis

Dactylorhiza × aschersoniana nothovar. mulignensis, commonly known as the Mulignan orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of fragrant, violet or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wet meadows. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, and it produces a single spike of flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens with proper moisture and light conditions.
