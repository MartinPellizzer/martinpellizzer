Demorchis flexistyla

Demorchis flexistyla, commonly known as the flexible stem orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, often twisting stems and delicate, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in a loose inflorescence. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and specialized orchid species.
