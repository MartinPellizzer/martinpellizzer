Dodonaea viscosa var. megazyga

Dodonaea viscosa var. megazyga, commonly known as the silver dollar tree, is a shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, round, silver-gray leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in catkins, followed by oval, woody fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often used in ornamental gardening.

The leaves are also used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.
