Delphinium × paniculatum

Delphinium × paniculatum, commonly known as the garden delphinium, is a hybrid perennial plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that bear dense clusters of showy, spurred flowers in shades of blue, purple, and white. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, making it a striking addition to gardens and landscapes. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several lance-shaped leaflets, and it thrives in well-drained soil with full sun to partial shade.

Delphinium × paniculatum is native to Europe and has been widely cultivated for its ornamental value.
