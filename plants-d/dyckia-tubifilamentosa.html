Dyckia tubifilamentosa

Dyckia tubifilamentosa, commonly known as the thread-leaved dyckia, is a species of bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped growth habit and long, narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine, thread-like trichomes. The plant produces striking inflorescences with bright orange to red flowers that emerge from the center of the rosette. It thrives in sunny, well-drained environments and is adapted to dry conditions.

Dyckia tubifilamentosa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and hardy nature.
