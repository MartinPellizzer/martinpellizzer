Daphne stenophylla var. ziyangensis

Daphne stenophylla var. ziyangensis, commonly known as the Ziyang daphne, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pink flowers in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sun.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in horticulture.
