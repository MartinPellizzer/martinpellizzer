Digitaria villiculmis

Digitaria villiculmis, commonly known as slender fingergrass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a rough texture.

Digitaria villiculmis is often found in sandy or loamy soils and is adapted to warm climates.
