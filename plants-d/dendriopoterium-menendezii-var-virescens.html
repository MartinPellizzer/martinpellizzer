Dendriopoterium menendezii var. virescens

Dendriopoterium menendezii var. virescens, commonly known as the green-leafed dendriopoterium, is a rare and unique plant species native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth habit with compact, branching stems. The leaves are typically small, oval-shaped, and have a distinct dark green coloration. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences.
