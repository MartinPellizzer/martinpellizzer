Dorycnium stenocladum

Dorycnium stenocladum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved burclover, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, characterized by its upright stems and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small yellow flowers in racemes, followed by distinctive bur-like seed pods that aid in seed dispersal. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing drought-resistant features.

Its botanical structure reflects its role as a pioneer species in disturbed soils.
