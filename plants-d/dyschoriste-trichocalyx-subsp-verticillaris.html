Dyschoriste trichocalyx subsp. verticillaris

Dyschoriste trichocalyx subsp.

verticillaris, commonly known as the narrow-leaved dyschoriste, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grassland and rocky habitats.

The plant features slender stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often arranged in whorls. Its flowers are small and yellow, clustered in dense, cylindrical inflorescences.

It is characterized by its unique morphology and is of interest in botanical studies due to its ecological adaptations.
