Dendrobium kwangtungense

Dendrobium kwangtungense, commonly known as the Guangdong orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to China.

It features a sympodial growth habit with elongated, cane-like pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink, typically during the spring and summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial shade to flourish.

Dendrobium kwangtungense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
