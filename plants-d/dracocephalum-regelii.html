Dracocephalum regelii

Dracocephalum regelii, commonly known as Regel's skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants known for their medicinal properties. The plant features square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It is often found in rocky or grassy habitats at elevations ranging from 1,500 to 4,000 meters.

The species is valued for its potential medicinal uses and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
