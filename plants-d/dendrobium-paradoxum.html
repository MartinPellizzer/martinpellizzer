Dendrobium paradoxum

Dendrobium paradoxum, commonly known as the paradox orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a unique growth habit with a short, thick stem and long, narrow leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and red, often in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its rarity and specific care requirements, it is a prized specimen among orchid enthusiasts.
