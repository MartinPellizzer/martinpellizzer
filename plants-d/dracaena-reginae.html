Dracaena reginae

Dracaena reginae, commonly known as the Queen's dracaena, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green with a reddish or bronze tinge along the edges. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Dracaena reginae is also known for its ability to purify the air and tolerate low light conditions.
