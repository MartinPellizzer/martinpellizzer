Dasystachys leptoneura

Dasystachys leptoneura, commonly known as the slender-stemmed spikerush, is a member of the Cyperaceae family, characterized by its grass-like appearance and specialized growth habits.

It typically grows in wetland environments, such as marshes and swamps, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils. The plant features slender, erect stems with narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its inflorescence consists of dense, spike-like clusters of small, greenish flowers, which are wind-pollinated.

This species plays an important ecological role in stabilizing wetland soils and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
