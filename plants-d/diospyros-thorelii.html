Diospyros thorelii

Diospyros thorelii, commonly known as the African persimmon, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Ebenaceae family, which includes other species known for their hard, durable wood. The leaves are simple, alternate, and usually have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.
