Dendrobium dickasonii

Dendrobium dickasonii, commonly known as the Dickason's orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a tall, branching stem with numerous fleshy, sword-shaped leaves that store water, aiding in its survival in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces striking clusters of fragrant, yellow-orange flowers with red-brown markings, typically blooming during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining potting media and requires moderate light and consistent moisture.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
