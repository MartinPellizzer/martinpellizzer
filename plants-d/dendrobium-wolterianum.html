Dendrobium wolterianum

Dendrobium wolterianum, commonly known as the Wolter's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit, with cane-like stems that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with a distinctive red or purple lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium wolterianum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
