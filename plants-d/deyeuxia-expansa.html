Deyeuxia expansa

Deyeuxia expansa, commonly known as the broadleaf tussock grass, is a perennial grass species native to temperate regions of Asia and Australia.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and long, narrow leaves that are typically flat and hairy. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences containing numerous tiny florets. Deyeuxia expansa thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and grasslands.
