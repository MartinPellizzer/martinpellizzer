Dasyphyllum velutinum

Dasyphyllum velutinum, commonly known as the velvetleaf dasyphyllum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Dasyphyllum velutinum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses in local communities.
