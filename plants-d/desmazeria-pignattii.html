Desmazeria pignattii

Desmazeria pignattii, commonly known as the Pignatti's desmazeria, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, daisy-like flowers with yellow ray florets and dark purple disk florets. Native to the Mediterranean region, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight. It is often found in rocky or sandy habitats, where it displays resilience to drought conditions.

The plant's ornamental value has led to its cultivation in gardens, though it remains relatively uncommon in horticultural trade.
