Digitaria perrieri

Digitaria perrieri, commonly known as the Perrier's grass, is a species of grass native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial plant characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and along riverbanks.

Digitaria perrieri is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various small organisms.
