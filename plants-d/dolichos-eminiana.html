Dolichos eminiana

Dolichos eminiana, commonly known as the Ethiopian bean, is a climbing legume native to Ethiopia.

It features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellowish flowers. The plant bears elongated, slender pods that contain several seeds. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it resilient to drought conditions.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species in traditional agriculture and local ecosystems.
