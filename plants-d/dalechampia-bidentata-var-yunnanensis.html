Dalechampia bidentata var. yunnanensis

Dalechampia bidentata var. yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan Dalechampia, is a species native to China.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves with a soft, velvety texture. The plant produces small, unisexual flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
