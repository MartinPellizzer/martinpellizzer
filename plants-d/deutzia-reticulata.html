Deutzia reticulata

Deutzia reticulata, commonly known as the reticulate deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features slender, arching branches that bear clusters of small, white or pink flowers in early summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a serrated margin and a hairy underside. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

Deutzia reticulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature in temperate climates.
