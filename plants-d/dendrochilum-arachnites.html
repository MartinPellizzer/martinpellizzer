Dendrochilum arachnites

Dendrochilum arachnites, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, spider-like appearance due to its elongated, wiry stems and delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually pale green or white with intricate patterns, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Dendrochilum arachnites is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
