Digitaria parlatorei

Digitaria parlatorei, commonly known as Parlatore's Digitaria, is a grass species native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and often have a bluish-gray hue, which helps reduce water loss.

Digitaria parlatorei is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in challenging conditions.
