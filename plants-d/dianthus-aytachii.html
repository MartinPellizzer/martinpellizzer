Dianthus aytachii

Dianthus aytachii, commonly known as the Aytach's campion, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Turkey.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its stems are erect and branched.

Dianthus aytachii is a rare species, making it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
