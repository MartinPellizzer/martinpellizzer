Daviesia sarissa subsp. redacta

Daviesia sarissa subsp.

redacta, commonly known as the Redleaf Daviesia, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia. It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, often vibrant red or orange flowers.

The plant typically grows in heathlands and open forests, thriving in well-drained soils. Its foliage consists of narrow, leathery leaves, and it produces small, pod-like fruits that contain seeds.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
