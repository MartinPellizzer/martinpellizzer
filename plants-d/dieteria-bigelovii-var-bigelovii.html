Dieteria bigelovii var. bigelovii

Dieteria bigelovii var. bigelovii, commonly known as the bigelov's dieteria, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of pale yellow to greenish-yellow flowers, which bloom in the summer months. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in sagebrush communities.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies in arid environments.
