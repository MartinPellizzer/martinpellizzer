Dactylorhiza × kuuskiae

Dactylorhiza × kuuskiae, commonly known as the Kuuski's orchid, is a hybrid orchid native to Finland.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a rhizomatous growth habit and produces tall, erect stems bearing numerous flowers. The flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a distinct lip that is often darker in color. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in peat bogs or damp woodlands.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens with appropriate conditions.
