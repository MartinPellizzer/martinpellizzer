Deyeuxia longiseta var. longe-aristata

Deyeuxia longiseta var. longe-aristata, commonly known as the long-awned false panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Asia.

It typically grows in wetland or marshy environments, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils. The plant features slender, cylindrical stems that can reach heights of up to 1 meter. Its inflorescence consists of a loose panicle with long, slender spikelets, each bearing bristle-like awns.

The leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue when fresh.
