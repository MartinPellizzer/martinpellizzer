Davallia japonica

Davallia japonica, commonly known as the Japanese spleenwort, is a fern native to Asia.

It features delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are typically light green with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces sporangia on the underside of its leaves, which are clustered in sori. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
