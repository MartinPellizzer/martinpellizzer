Dioscorea prazeri

Dioscorea prazeri, commonly known as the Prazeri yam, is a species of flowering plant in the family Dioscoreaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its climbing habit and tuberous roots. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically borne in clusters. Its tubers are often used in traditional medicine and as a food source in certain areas.

The species is notable for its ecological role in supporting biodiversity in its native habitats.
