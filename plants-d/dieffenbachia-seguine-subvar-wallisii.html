Dieffenbachia seguine subvar. wallisii

Dieffenbachia seguine subvar. wallisii, commonly known as the Wallisii Dieffenbachia, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with distinctive white or cream-colored veins. The plant is a member of the Araceae family, known for its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix and spathe. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This ornamental plant is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
