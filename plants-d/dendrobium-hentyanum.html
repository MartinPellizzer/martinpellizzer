Dendrobium hentyanum

Dendrobium hentyanum, commonly known as the Henty's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and strap-like, arranged along the stem in a spiral pattern.

Dendrobium hentyanum thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
