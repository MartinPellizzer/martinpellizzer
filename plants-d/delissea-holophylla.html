Delissea holophylla

Delissea holophylla, commonly known as the golden star, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers that resemble stars, which bloom in clusters during the warmer months. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that provide a striking contrast to its bright blooms. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires plenty of sunlight to flourish.

Delissea holophylla is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and is a favorite among horticulturists.
