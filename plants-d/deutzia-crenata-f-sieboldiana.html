Deutzia crenata f. sieboldiana

Deutzia crenata f. sieboldiana, commonly known as the Siebold's deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features slender, arching stems that bear clusters of small, white flowers in early spring. The foliage is typically dark green and provides attractive autumn color. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden borders or woodland settings.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and hardiness in temperate climates.
