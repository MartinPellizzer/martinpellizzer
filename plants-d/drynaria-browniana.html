Drynaria browniana

Drynaria browniana, commonly known as the brown's tree fern, is a member of the Polypodiaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial plant that forms a tall, slender trunk covered in a fibrous, dark brown rind. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical forests.

Its unique appearance and slow growth make it a popular ornamental plant in horticulture.
