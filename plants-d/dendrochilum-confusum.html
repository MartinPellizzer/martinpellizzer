Dendrochilum confusum

Dendrochilum confusum, commonly known as the confused tree orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, branched stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow or greenish flowers with a distinctive lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Dendrochilum confusum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
