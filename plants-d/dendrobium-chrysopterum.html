Dendrobium chrysopterum

Dendrobium chrysopterum, commonly known as the golden lip orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on the surface of trees or other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The plant produces showy, golden-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip that resembles a lip-shaped structure. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements make it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
