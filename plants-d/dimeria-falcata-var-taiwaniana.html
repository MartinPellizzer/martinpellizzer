Dimeria falcata var. taiwaniana

Dimeria falcata var. taiwaniana, commonly known as the Taiwan dwarf daisy, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to coastal and mountainous regions. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, inconspicuous fruits.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
