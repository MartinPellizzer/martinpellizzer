Dolichos corymbosus

Dolichos corymbosus, commonly known as the winged bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It produces clusters of small, yellow flowers that develop into elongated pods containing edible seeds and a starchy, fibrous pulp. The plant is valued for its nutritional content, as the seeds are rich in protein and minerals. Its leaves are also consumed as a vegetable, and the plant is often cultivated for both food and ornamental purposes.

The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
