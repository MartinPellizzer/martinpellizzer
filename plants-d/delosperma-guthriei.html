Delosperma guthriei

Delosperma guthriei, commonly known as the Guthrie's ice plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months, typically in clusters at the ends of its stems. It is well adapted to rocky and sandy soils, thriving in full sun and requiring minimal water.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
