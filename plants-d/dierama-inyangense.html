Dierama inyangense

Dierama inyangense, commonly known as the Inyangense spider flower, is a striking plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family, which includes many ornamental and medicinal plants. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of bright blue or violet flowers that resemble spider-like structures. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings.

Dierama inyangense is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to well-drained soils and full sun exposure.
