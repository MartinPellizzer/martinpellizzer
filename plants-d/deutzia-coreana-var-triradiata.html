Deutzia coreana var. triradiata

Deutzia coreana var. triradiata, commonly known as the Korean deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to Korea.

It features clusters of fragrant, white to pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and prefers well-drained soil in full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its attractive foliage and flowering display.
