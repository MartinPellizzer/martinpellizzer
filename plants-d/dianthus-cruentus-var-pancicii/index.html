Dianthus Cruentus Var. Pancicii

Dianthus cruentus var. pancicii is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant known for its vibrant red petals and intricate floral structure. Native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans, this variety thrives in rocky and well-drained soils. Its flowers bloom in early summer, adding a splash of color to alpine landscapes. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has a long history in traditional horticulture. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively rare and hardy species in the wild.
