Dicksonia zeylanica

Dicksonia zeylanica, commonly known as the Ceylon tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Sri Lanka.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant has a distinctive crown of pinnate leaves that give it a graceful, tropical appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in dense forests.

The fronds are used in traditional medicine and basket weaving in some regions.
