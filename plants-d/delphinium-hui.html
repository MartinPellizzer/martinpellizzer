Delphinium hui

Delphinium hui, commonly known as the Chinese larkspur, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, spindly stems and clusters of blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and prefers well-drained soil and full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

Delphinium hui is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
