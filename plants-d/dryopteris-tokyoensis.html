Dryopteris tokyoensis

Dryopteris tokyoensis, commonly known as the Tokyo holly fern, is a species of fern native to Japan.

It belongs to the Dryopteridaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its leaves are bipinnate, with a distinctive appearance that makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Dryopteris tokyoensis is valued for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade.
