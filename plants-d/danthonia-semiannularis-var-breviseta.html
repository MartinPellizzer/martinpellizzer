Danthonia semiannularis var. breviseta

Danthonia semiannularis var. breviseta, commonly known as the narrow-bristled danthonia, is a perennial grass native to South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves with a distinctive hairy texture along the leaf margins. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
