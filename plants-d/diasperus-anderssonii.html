Diasperus anderssonii

Diasperus anderssonii, commonly known as the Andes rock cress, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

Diasperus anderssonii is often found growing on rocky slopes and in alpine meadows, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions.
