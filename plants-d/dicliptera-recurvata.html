Dicliptera recurvata

Dicliptera recurvata, commonly known as the curly grass or narrowleaf curlytop grass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa, particularly South Africa.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, which are typical of the Poaceae family. Dicliptera recurvata is often found in grasslands and open woodlands, thriving in well-drained soils.
