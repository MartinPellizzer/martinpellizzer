Dinetus truncatus

Dinetus truncatus, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant features velvety, waxy leaves that are typically heart-shaped and dark green in color. Its showy, tubular flowers come in a variety of colors, including purple, pink, and white.
