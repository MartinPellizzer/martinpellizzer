Dendrobium crocatum

Dendrobium crocatum, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a tall, upright stem covered with smooth, dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellow-orange flowers with a distinctive red lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments with partial shade.

Dendrobium crocatum is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
