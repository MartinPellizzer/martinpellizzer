Delosperma gerstneri

Delosperma gerstneri, commonly known as the Gerstner's ice plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are adapted to store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers in the spring, adding a striking contrast to its foliage. It is a low-growing shrub that forms dense mats, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for gardeners in dry climates.
