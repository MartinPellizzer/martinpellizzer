Dillenia elata

Dillenia elata, commonly known as the giant dillenia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in diameter. The plant produces showy, yellowish-white flowers with prominent stamens, which are often arranged in a distinctive spiral pattern. Its fruit is a large, woody capsule that splits open to release numerous seeds.

Dillenia elata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.
