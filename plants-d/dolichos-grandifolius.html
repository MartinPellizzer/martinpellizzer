Dolichos grandifolius

Dolichos grandifolius, commonly known as the large-leaf dolichos, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features compound leaves with elongated, ovate leaflets that can reach up to 20 cm in length. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in racemes, followed by elongated, cylindrical pods containing several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and potential as a forage crop, it is cultivated in various parts of the world.
