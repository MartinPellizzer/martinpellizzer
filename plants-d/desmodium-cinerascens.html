Desmodium cinerascens

Desmodium cinerascens, commonly known as the silverleaf desmodium, is a perennial legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves with a silvery-gray underside, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to purple, and it is often found in open woodlands and grasslands. Desmodium cinerascens has a deep root system that helps prevent soil erosion and improves soil fertility through nitrogen fixation.

It is also used in traditional medicine and as a forage crop for livestock.
