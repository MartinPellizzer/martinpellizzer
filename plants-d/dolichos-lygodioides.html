Dolichos lygodioides

Dolichos lygodioides, commonly known as the narrow-leaf dolichos, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It features trifoliate leaves with narrow, lance-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in clusters. The plant bears elongated, cylindrical pods that contain several elliptical seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Dolichos lygodioides is often used in traditional medicine and as a forage crop for livestock.
