Durio beccarianus

Durio beccarianus, commonly known as the beccarian durian, is a tropical tree native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Duraceae family and is closely related to the well-known durian species. The plant produces large, spiky, and often fragrant fruits that are highly prized for their unique flavor and aroma. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, and it can grow up to 30 meters tall.

The tree plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing food and shelter for various wildlife species.
