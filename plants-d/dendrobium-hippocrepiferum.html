Dendrobium hippocrepiferum

Dendrobium hippocrepiferum, commonly known as the helmet orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with cane-like stems that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, often with a distinctive helmet-shaped lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium hippocrepiferum is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
