Dendrosenecio johnstonii var. friesiorum

Dendrosenecio johnstonii var. friesiorum, commonly known as the "Cape snowflake," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, spoon-shaped leaves that are typically pale green with a waxy coating. The plant produces striking white or pale blue flowers during the summer months, which are borne on tall, branched inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
