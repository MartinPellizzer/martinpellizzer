Diabelia serrata f. oblongisepala

Diabelia serrata f. oblongisepala, commonly known as the narrow-lobed diabelia, is a botanical variant of the diabelia genus.

It is characterized by its elongated, narrow sepals that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in humid environments. Its leaves are usually ovate to lanceolate in shape, with serrated edges.

This form is often found in shaded, moist habitats, contributing to its ecological significance in native ecosystems.
