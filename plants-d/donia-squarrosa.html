Donia squarrosa

Donia squarrosa, commonly known as the squarrosa donia, is a species of flowering plant native to certain regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is not widely distributed outside its native range.
