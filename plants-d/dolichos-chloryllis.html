Dolichos chloryllis

Dolichos chloryllis, commonly known as the yellow dolichos, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is a legume belonging to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellow flowers. The plant produces elongated pods that contain lens-shaped seeds. It is often grown as a forage crop due to its nutritional value for livestock.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and agricultural contexts.
