Delosperma framesii

Delosperma framesii, commonly known as the silverleaf ice plant, is a low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, silver-green leaves that help it store water, adapting to arid environments. The plant produces vibrant pink to red flowers during the summer months, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact size and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for landscaping in dry climates.
