Dendrochilum schweinfurthianum

Dendrochilum schweinfurthianum, commonly known as the Schweinfurth's tree orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its erect, branched stem that bears numerous leaves. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, humus-rich soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
