Diervilla Japonica Var. Nivea

Snowy diervilla, scientifically known as Diervilla japonica var. nivea, is a compact, flowering shrub prized for its ornamental value and hardy nature. Native to eastern Asia, it thrives in woodland settings and is well-suited for garden landscapes. Its most striking feature is the profusion of white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant's foliage is dark green and evergreen, providing year-round visual interest. With its low maintenance requirements and ability to tolerate partial shade, it is a popular choice among gardeners seeking both beauty and resilience.
