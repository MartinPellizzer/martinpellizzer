Dendrobium × lutwycheanum

Dendrobium × lutwycheanum, commonly known as the Lutwyche hybrid orchid, is a hybrid orchid species resulting from the cross between Dendrobium nobile and Dendrobium phalaenopsis.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant features a tall, cane-like stem with numerous fleshy, elliptical leaves that store water, aiding in its survival in dry conditions. It produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and yellow, often blooming multiple times a year.

The hybrid is valued for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to various growing conditions.
