Didiscus ballineus

Didiscus ballineus, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of bright yellow, fragrant flowers that bloom in long, pendulous racemes. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Didiscus ballineus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
