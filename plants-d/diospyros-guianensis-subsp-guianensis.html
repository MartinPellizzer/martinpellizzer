Diospyros guianensis subsp.

guianensis, commonly known as the Guianan persimmon, is a species of flowering plant in the Ebenaceae family. It is a small to medium-sized tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, particularly in South America.

The wood of this species is valued for its durability and is used in various woodworking applications.
