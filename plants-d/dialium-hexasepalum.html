Dialium hexasepalum

Dialium hexasepalum, commonly known as the African plum.

It is a tropical tree native to Central and Southern Africa, characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by edible, yellow or orange fruits. The fruit contains a single large seed surrounded by a fleshy, juicy pulp.

It is often grown for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
