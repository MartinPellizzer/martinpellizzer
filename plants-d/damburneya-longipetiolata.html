Damburneya longipetiolata

Damburneya longipetiolata, commonly known as the long-petioled damburneya, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, leathery, lance-shaped leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces striking, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which appear in clusters during the warmer months. Its unique foliage and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Damburneya longipetiolata thrives in well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.
