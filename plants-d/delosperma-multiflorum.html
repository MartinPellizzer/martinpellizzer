Delosperma multiflorum

Delosperma multiflorum, commonly known as the ice plant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant clusters of pink or red flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ability to survive in harsh conditions makes it an excellent plant for xeriscaping.
