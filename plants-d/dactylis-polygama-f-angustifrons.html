Dactylis polygama f. angustifrons

Dactylis polygama f. angustifrons, commonly known as narrow-leaved cat's-tail, is a variant of the species Dactylis polygama, which belongs to the grass family Poaceae.

This fern-like plant is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. It typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are clustered at the base, contributing to its ornamental value.

It is valued in horticulture for its unique foliage and ability to thrive in a variety of conditions.
