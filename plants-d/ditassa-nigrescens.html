Ditassa nigrescens

Ditassa nigrescens, commonly known as the black nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black berries that mature in the fall. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded or moist environments. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

The flowers are small, white or pale green, and often clustered in umbels.
