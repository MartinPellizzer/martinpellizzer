Deutzia magnifica

Deutzia magnifica, commonly known as the magnificent deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, upright growth habit and its showy clusters of white to pinkish flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has simple, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter underside. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Deutzia magnifica is also valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
