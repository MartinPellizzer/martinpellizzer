Deutzia corymbosa

Deutzia corymbosa, commonly known as the clustered spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features showy, flat-topped clusters of white or pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has slender, arching branches and small, ovate leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and well-drained soil.

Deutzia corymbosa is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
