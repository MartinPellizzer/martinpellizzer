Desmodium heterocarpon

Desmodium heterocarpon, commonly known as the purple crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb, often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.
