Draba pseudopilosa

Draba pseudopilosa, commonly known as the rough-haired whitlowgrass, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

Draba pseudopilosa is typically found at high elevations and is often used in ecological studies due to its resilience and specific habitat requirements.
