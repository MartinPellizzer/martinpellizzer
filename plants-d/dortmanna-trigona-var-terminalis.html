Dortmanna trigona var. terminalis

Dortmanna trigona var. terminalis, commonly known as the clustered Dortmanna, is a species of flowering plant in the family Acanthaceae.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense clusters of small, tubular flowers. The plant typically features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in shaded to semi-shaded environments and prefers moist, well-drained soil.
