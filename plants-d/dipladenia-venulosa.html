Dipladenia venulosa

Dipladenia venulosa, commonly known as the orange trumpet vine, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a vigorous climber with bright orange, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has compound leaves with three to five elliptical leaflets and produces showy, fragrant blooms that attract pollinators. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for tropical gardens.

Dipladenia venulosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to climb trellises or walls.
