Dioscorea haenkeana

Dioscorea haenkeana, commonly known as the yellow arrowhead vine, is a climbing herbaceous plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its tuberous roots, which are often used in traditional medicine. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

Its rapid growth habit makes it an important species in ecological studies and potential agricultural applications.
