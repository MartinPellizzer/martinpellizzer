Digitaria asthenes

Digitaria asthenes, commonly known as slender fingergrass, is a warm-season grass native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and is often found in grasslands, roadsides, and agricultural fields. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, with a bluish-green color.

Digitaria asthenes is a perennial species that can form dense stands, making it both a weed and a forage plant in various regions.
