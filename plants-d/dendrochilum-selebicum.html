Dendrochilum selebicum

Dendrochilum selebicum, commonly known as the Celebes orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Indonesian island of Sumba.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and fleshy, elliptic leaves. The plant produces numerous fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with a distinctive red or purple lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
