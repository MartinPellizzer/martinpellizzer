Dethawia splendens subsp. splendens

Dethawia splendens subsp.

splendens, commonly known as the Splendid Dethawia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences that display vibrant colors, often in shades of red, orange, and yellow.

The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, with lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
