Dryopteris tsangpoensis

Dryopteris tsangpoensis, commonly known as the Tsangpo fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to the eastern Himalayas.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its large, leathery, bipinnate fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes. Its rhizomes are robust and help it establish in rocky or forested areas.

The Tsangpo fern is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
