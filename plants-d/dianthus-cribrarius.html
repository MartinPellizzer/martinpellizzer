Dianthus cribrarius

Dianthus cribrarius, commonly known as the basket flower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, pink or white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows to a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters, with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are composed of many small petals arranged in a clustered pattern, giving it a basket-like appearance.

Dianthus cribrarius is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.
