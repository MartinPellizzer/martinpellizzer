Dolichos baumannii

Dolichos baumannii, commonly known as the horse gram or cluster bean, is a leguminous plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and trifoliate leaves with small, hairy stipules. The plant produces elongated, cylindrical pods that contain several edible seeds. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions and is widely cultivated for its nutritious seeds.

Dolichos baumannii is also valued for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, improving soil fertility.
