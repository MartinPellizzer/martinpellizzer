Dudleya moranii

Dudleya moranii, commonly known as the Moran's liveforever, is a succulent plant native to California.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, water-storing leaves that form a rosette. Dudleya moranii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its drought tolerance and aesthetic appeal.
