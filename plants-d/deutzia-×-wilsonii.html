Deutzia × wilsonii

Deutzia × wilsonii, commonly known as Wilson deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy clusters of white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring, adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant has arching stems with opposite, ovate leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for a variety of landscape settings.

Its compact size and extended blooming period make it a popular choice for borders and woodland gardens.
