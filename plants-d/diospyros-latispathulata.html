Diospyros latispathulata

Diospyros latispathulata, commonly known as the broad-leafed persimmon, is a species of flowering plant in the family Ebenaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Indonesia and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its large, broad leaves and small, clustered flowers that develop into edible fruits. It is often grown for its ornamental value and as a source of edible persimmons.
