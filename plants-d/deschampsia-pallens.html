Deschampsia pallens

Deschampsia pallens, commonly known as silver hairgrass, is a low-growing perennial grass native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically forms dense, mat-like growths and is often found in moist, well-drained soils. Its inflorescence consists of slender, spike-like clusters of small, pale green to yellowish flowers.

Deschampsia pallens is valued in landscaping for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in various environmental conditions.
