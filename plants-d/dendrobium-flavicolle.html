Dendrobium flavicolle

Dendrobium flavicolle, commonly known as the golden column orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and clusters of fragrant, yellowish-orange flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its leaves are elongated and strap-like, providing support for the flowering spikes.

Dendrobium flavicolle is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
