Dieffenbachia illustris

Dieffenbachia illustris, commonly known as the White Knight Dieffenbachia, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, broad leaves that are predominantly white with green veins, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other popular houseplants like peace lilies. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity to flourish.

Its foliage is not only ornamental but also toxic if ingested, making it important to handle with care.
