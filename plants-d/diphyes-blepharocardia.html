Diphyes blepharocardia

Diphyes blepharocardia, commonly known as the heart-leaved buttercup, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves and bright yellow flowers with numerous stamens. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are usually deeply lobed, and the flowers bloom in early spring.
