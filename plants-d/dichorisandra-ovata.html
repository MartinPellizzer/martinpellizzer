Dichorisandra ovata

Dichorisandra ovata, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette of velvety, waxy leaves and clusters of showy, violet-blue flowers. The plant prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive. Its botanical features include opposite, ovate leaves and small, tubular flowers that are typically arranged in inflorescences.

Dichorisandra ovata is a popular ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
