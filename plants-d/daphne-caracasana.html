Daphne caracasana

Daphne caracasana, commonly known as the Christmas daphne, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white or pink flowers in late winter or early spring. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

The plant is known for its showy blooms and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
