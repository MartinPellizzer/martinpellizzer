Diphasiopsis whitei

Diphasiopsis whitei, commonly known as the White's diphasiopsis, is a member of the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and umbellate inflorescences.

Its leaves are finely divided and emit a strong, spicy scent when crushed, which is a common trait among members of its family.
