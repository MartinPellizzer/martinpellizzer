Dendrobium riparium

Dendrobium riparium, commonly known as the river orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with multiple stems that can reach up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months. Its roots are often found in moist, shaded environments near rivers or streams.

Dendrobium riparium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
