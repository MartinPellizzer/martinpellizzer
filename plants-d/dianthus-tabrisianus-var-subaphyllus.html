Dianthus tabrisianus var. subaphyllus

Dianthus tabrisianus var. subaphyllus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved campion, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit and displays clusters of pink to red, bilaterally symmetric flowers. The leaves are narrow and linear, giving the plant a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
