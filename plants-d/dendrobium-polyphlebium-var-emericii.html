Dendrobium polyphlebium var. emericii

Dendrobium polyphlebium var. emericii, commonly known as the Emerald Orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic orchid that grows on trees and rocks, thriving in humid tropical environments. The plant features long, cane-like stems covered in clusters of lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically greenish-white with yellow and purple markings, blooming in summer.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
