Diplotaxis viminea

Diplotaxis viminea, commonly known as slender wallflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous annual or biennial plant that grows up to 30 cm in height. The plant features slender, branching stems covered in fine hairs and produces small, yellow flowers in clusters. Its leaves are linear and slightly hairy, often with a reddish tinge.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
