Drosanthemum vespertinum var. suffusum

Drosanthemum vespertinum var. suffusum, commonly known as the evening drosanthemum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish or purplish tint. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple, blooming in the late afternoon and evening. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This species is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and evening blooming habit.
