Draba Dubia Subsp. Nevadensis

Nevada draba, scientifically known as Draba dubia subsp. nevadensis, is a rare and resilient plant native to the high elevations of the Great Basin in Nevada. This member of the Brassicaceae family thrives in harsh, arid environments where few species can survive. Its small stature and clustered leaves allow it to conserve moisture and withstand extreme temperatures. Despite its delicate appearance, the Nevada draba plays a vital role in stabilizing soil and supporting local ecosystems. Conservation efforts are critical to preserving this unique species for future generations.
