Dombeya gautieri

Dombeya gautieri, commonly known as the mountain dombeya, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are usually simple, opposite, and often covered in fine hairs.

Dombeya gautieri is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
