Dicksonia amorosoana

Dicksonia amorosoana, commonly known as the Amoroso's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical rainforests of northeastern Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk that is covered in a dense layer of fibrous roots. The plant produces large, feather-like fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with rich, well-drained soil.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
