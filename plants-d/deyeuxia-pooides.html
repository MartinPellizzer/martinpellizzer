Deyeuxia pooides

Deyeuxia pooides, commonly known as the slender bulrush, is a perennial grass species native to wetland and marshy environments in Asia.

It typically grows in shallow water or damp soil, forming dense clumps with slender, cylindrical stems. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a smooth surface and a slight sheen.

Deyeuxia pooides plays an important ecological role in stabilizing wetland soils and providing habitat for various aquatic and terrestrial organisms.
