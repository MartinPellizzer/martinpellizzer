Datura stramonium f. inermis

Datura stramonium f. inermis, commonly known as the angel's trumpet, is a variant of the common jimsonweed.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its large, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. The leaves are broad, ovate, and often have a velvety texture. This plant is native to the Americas but has been introduced to many other regions.

It contains toxic alkaloids and is considered a noxious weed in some areas.
