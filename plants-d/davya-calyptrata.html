Davya calyptrata

Davya calyptrata, commonly known as the yellow davya, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands, thriving in well-drained soils.

Davya calyptrata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
