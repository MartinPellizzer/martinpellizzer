Disocactus × mallisonii

Disocactus × mallisonii, commonly known as the Queen of the Night, is a hybrid cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its large, white, nocturnal flowers that bloom only at night and emit a sweet fragrance. The plant has a short, thick stem covered in spines and produces elliptical, blue-green leaves. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Due to its striking appearance and unique blooming habit, it is a popular choice for collectors and gardeners.
