Drimia ecklonii

Drimia ecklonii, commonly known as the Cape tulip, is a bulbous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple, resembling tulips, and bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has strap-like, grassy leaves that form a dense rosette near the ground. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Drimia ecklonii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
