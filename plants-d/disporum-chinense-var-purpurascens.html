Disporum chinense var. purpurascens

Disporum chinense var. purpurascens, commonly known as the purple-flowered disporum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically dark green and arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, with some varieties displaying a distinctive purple hue. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth and is often found in temperate regions.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
