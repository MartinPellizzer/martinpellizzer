Desmodium distans

Desmodium distans, commonly known as the slender velvetbean, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant produces cylindrical, hairy pods that contain several elliptical seeds. It is often found in open woodland and disturbed sites, thriving in well-drained soils.

Desmodium distans is also valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it useful in agricultural systems.
