Dendrobium siewhongii

Dendrobium siewhongii, commonly known as the Siewhong orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its erect, cane-like pseudobulbs and clustered inflorescences. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and requires well-draining substrate for optimal growth.

Dendrobium siewhongii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and adaptability to cultivation.
