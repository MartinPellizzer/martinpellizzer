Dianthus borbasii subsp. borbasii

Dianthus borbasii subsp.

borbasii, commonly known as the Borbasii Campion, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands. It is a low-growing perennial herb with pink to red flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant features slender, branching stems and small, velvety leaves. It thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after plant in rock gardens and alpine settings.
