Dombeya reginae

Dombeya reginae, commonly known as the royal dombeya, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and violet. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

Dombeya reginae thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.
