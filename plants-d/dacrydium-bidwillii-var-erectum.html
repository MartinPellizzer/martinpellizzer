Dacrydium bidwillii var. erectum

Dacrydium bidwillii var. erectum, commonly known as the tall rimu, is a tall evergreen conifer native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Podocarpaceae and is characterized by its straight, columnar growth habit. The plant has needle-like leaves that are typically dark green and arranged in whorls around the stem. It produces small, cone-like structures that contain both male and female reproductive organs.
