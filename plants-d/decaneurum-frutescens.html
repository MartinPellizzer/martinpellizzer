Decaneurum frutescens

Decaneurum frutescens, commonly known as the silverleaf shrub, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is a small, evergreen shrub characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and compact growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the winter months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in dry climates.
