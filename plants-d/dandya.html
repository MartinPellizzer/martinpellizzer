Dandya

Dandya, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and chrysanthemums. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow flowers with white ray florets, which bloom in spring and summer. Dandya thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens.

It is also valued for its ornamental beauty and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
