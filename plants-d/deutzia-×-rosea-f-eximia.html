Deutzia × rosea f. eximia

Deutzia × rosea f. eximia, commonly known as the Exquisite Deutzia, is a hybrid shrub belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and profuse display of showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically reaches a height of 1 to 2 meters and features arching, slender stems covered in small, ovate leaves. Its flowers are borne in clusters at the ends of the branches, creating a striking visual effect.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to a variety of soil conditions.
