Desmodium archboldianum

Desmodium archboldianum, commonly known as the Florida desmodium, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically thrives in moist, well-drained soils. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are often purple or pink in color. Its trifoliate leaves consist of three oval-shaped leaflets.

Desmodium archboldianum is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.
