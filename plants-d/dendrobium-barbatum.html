Dendrobium barbatum

Dendrobium barbatum, commonly known as the hairy lip orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizomatous stem and fibrous roots. The plant produces showy, lanceolate flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often with a hairy or velvety texture on the petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrobium barbatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
