Dioscorea montecristina

Dioscorea montecristina, commonly known as the Montecristi yam, is a species of flowering plant in the family Dioscoreaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru. The plant is a herbaceous vine that produces tuberous roots, which are the primary part used for culinary and medicinal purposes. Its leaves are heart-shaped and its flowers are typically yellow or greenish in color.

The tubers of Dioscorea montecristina are valued for their nutritional content and are used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
