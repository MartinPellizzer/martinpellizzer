Dudleya × semiteres

Dudleya × semiteres, commonly known as the hybrid rock rose, is a succulent plant native to California.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves that are often blue-green in color. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, or white during the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits a wide range of morphological variations.
