Drypetes liberica

Drypetes liberica, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, making it a visually appealing ornamental plant. The leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate, with a smooth surface and a pointed tip. The tree produces small, round fruits that contain a single seed, which is often dispersed by animals.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for warm, humid climates.
