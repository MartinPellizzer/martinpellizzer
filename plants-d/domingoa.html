Domingoa

Domingoa, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a legume belonging to the genus *Spathodea* and is characterized by its striking, pendulous clusters of yellow flowers. The plant has compound leaves with multiple leaflets and produces pods that contain seeds enclosed in a fibrous covering. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The botanical features of Domingoa make it a popular choice for landscaping and garden decoration.
