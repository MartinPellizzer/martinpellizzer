Drimiopsis volkensii

Drimiopsis volkensii, commonly known as the African rock lily, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette of narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces striking, showy inflorescences of white or pale pink flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Drimiopsis volkensii is well adapted to arid conditions and is often found growing on rocky outcrops.
