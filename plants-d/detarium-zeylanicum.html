Detarium zeylanicum

Detarium zeylanicum, commonly known as the African shea tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The tree produces a valuable seed-containing pod that is used in traditional medicine and as a source of oil. Its roots are also utilized in various cultural and medicinal practices.

The plant plays an important ecological role in its native habitats by providing food and shelter for wildlife.
