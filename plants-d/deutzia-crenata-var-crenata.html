Deutzia crenata var. crenata

Deutzia crenata var. crenata, commonly known as the smooth deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features clusters of white, fragrant flowers that bloom in early summer, adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant has slender, arching branches and dark green, oval-shaped leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it adaptable to various garden conditions.

Its compact growth habit and showy flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
