Duvalia × anemoniflora

Duvalia × anemoniflora, commonly known as the hybrid anemoniflora, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often blooming in early spring. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that provide a delicate texture to its appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and floral display.
