Drosanthemum albens

Drosanthemum albens, commonly known as the silverleaf gloxinia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its broad, silver-green leaves and produces striking, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant is a member of the Gesneriaceae family, which includes many popular ornamental species. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Drosanthemum albens is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
