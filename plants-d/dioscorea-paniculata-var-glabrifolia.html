Dioscorea paniculata var. glabrifolia

Dioscorea paniculata var. glabrifolia, commonly known as the smooth-leaf yam, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into tuberous roots, which are the primary edible part. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its nutritional value.

The tubers are rich in starch and have been used in traditional cuisines for centuries.
