Durio carinatus

Durio carinatus, commonly known as the spiny durian, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Duranaceae and is characterized by its large, spiny fruit. The plant produces a strong-smelling, tropical fruit with a thick, fibrous husk. Its leaves are large, elliptical, and have a rough texture.

The spiny durian is valued for its unique flavor and is often cultivated for its edible fruit.
