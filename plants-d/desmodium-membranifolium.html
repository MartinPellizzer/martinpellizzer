Desmodium membranifolium

Desmodium membranifolium, commonly known as the velvetleaf desmodium, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which are often covered in a soft, velvety texture. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pale pink to white in color. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

Desmodium membranifolium is often found in disturbed areas and can be invasive in certain regions.
