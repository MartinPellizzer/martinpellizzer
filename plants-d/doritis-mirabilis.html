Doritis mirabilis

Doritis mirabilis, commonly known as the fairy orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments. Its flowers are usually pale pink or white, with a distinctive, intricate pattern on the petals.

Doritis mirabilis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
