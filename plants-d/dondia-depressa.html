Dondia depressa

Dondia depressa, commonly known as the dwarf dondia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of less than 10 centimeters. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, pale blue to purple flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Dondia depressa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
