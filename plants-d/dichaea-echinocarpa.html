Dichaea echinocarpa

Dichaea echinocarpa, commonly known as the spiny-stemmed dichaea, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family.

It is a small, terrestrial bromeliad native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant is characterized by its short, erect stem and clusters of small, spathaceous bracts that enclose its flowers. Its inflorescence produces tiny, white flowers that are typically borne in a dense spike.

Dichaea echinocarpa is often found growing in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky crevices.
