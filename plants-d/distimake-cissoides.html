Distimake cissoides

Distimake cissoides, commonly known as the silverleaf cissoid, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which helps reduce water loss in its harsh environment. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires minimal water, making it adaptable to drought conditions.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a notable species in its native ecosystem.
