Dianthus deltoides subsp. deltoides

Dianthus deltoides subsp.

deltoides, commonly known as the upright campion, is a flowering plant native to Europe and western Asia. It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces clusters of pink or red flowers with five petals.

The leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs, and the stems are sturdy and erect. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
