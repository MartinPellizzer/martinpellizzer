Dipteracanthus floribundus

Dipteracanthus floribundus, commonly known as the showy dipteracanthus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically bright yellow or orange in color. The plant has opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are broad and ovate in shape. It produces small, fleshy fruits that contain several seeds.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
