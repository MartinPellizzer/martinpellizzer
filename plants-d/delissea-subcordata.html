Delissea subcordata

Delissea subcordata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed delissea, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and ray florets. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are narrow and often have a slightly wavy margin, while the flowers are small and yellowish in color.

Delissea subcordata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
