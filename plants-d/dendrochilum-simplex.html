Dendrochilum simplex

Dendrochilum simplex, commonly known as the simple tree orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow with red or purple markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Dendrochilum simplex is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
