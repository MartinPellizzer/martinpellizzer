Digitaria acuminatissima

Digitaria acuminatissima, commonly known as the slender digit grass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a perennial plant characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny florets. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along waterways.

Digitaria acuminatissima plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various small organisms.
