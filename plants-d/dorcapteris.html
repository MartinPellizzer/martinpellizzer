Dorcapteris

Dorcapteris, commonly known as the clubmoss, is a genus of vascular plants belonging to the order Lycopodiales.

These plants are characterized by their small, scale-like leaves and sporophylls that bear spores. Dorcapteris species are typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. They have a long evolutionary history, dating back to the Carboniferous period.
