Dracaena deremensis var. warneckei

Dracaena deremensis var. warneckei, commonly known as the Warneckei Dracaena, is a variant of the Dracaena deremensis species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and broad, dark green, strap-shaped leaves that often have a slight yellowish margin. This plant is a popular indoor foliage plant due to its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its air-purifying qualities. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by red berries, though it is primarily cultivated for its ornamental foliage.

The plant is relatively drought-tolerant and prefers well-draining soil, making it a low-maintenance choice for indoor gardening.
