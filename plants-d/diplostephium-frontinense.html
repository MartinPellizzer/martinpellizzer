Diplostephium frontinense

Diplostephium frontinense, commonly known as the Brazilian burr, is a flowering plant native to South America.

The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by the development of dry, woody fruits. It thrives in tropical and subtropical regions, often found in open areas with well-drained soil.
