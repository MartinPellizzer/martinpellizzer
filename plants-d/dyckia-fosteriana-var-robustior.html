Dyckia fosteriana var. robustior

Dyckia fosteriana var. robustior, commonly known as the robust dyckia, is a species of bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its rosette-forming structure with long, stiff, and often spiny leaves that can reach several feet in length. The plant produces striking inflorescences of red or orange flowers that emerge from the center of the rosette. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among succulent and bromeliad enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
