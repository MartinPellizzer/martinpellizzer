Dillenia sumatrana

Dillenia sumatrana, commonly known as the Sumatran dillenia, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in the Indonesian island of Sumatra.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, yellowish-white flowers that are typically arranged in clusters, followed by large, berry-like fruits.

Its botanical features make it an important species in the study of tropical flora and ecological systems.
