Drypetes crocea var. latifolia

Drypetes crocea var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved drypetes, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green in color. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that contain several seeds.

This species is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
