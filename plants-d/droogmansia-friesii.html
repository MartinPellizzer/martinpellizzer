Droogmansia friesii

Droogmansia friesii, commonly known as the Cape mountain lily, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It is a bulbous perennial that produces showy, white or pale pink flowers with a distinctive, recurved shape. The plant features strap-like leaves that are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its arid native habitat. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Droogmansia friesii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom reliably under proper care.
