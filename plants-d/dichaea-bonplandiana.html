Dichaea bonplandiana

Dichaea bonplandiana, commonly known as the Bonpland's dichaea, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments within cloud forests. The plant produces delicate, greenish-white flowers that bloom during the cooler months. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the forest floor.

Dichaea bonplandiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
