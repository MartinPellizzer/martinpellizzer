Dactyladenia pierrei

Dactyladenia pierrei, commonly known as the Congo orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to the Democratic Republic of the Congo.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that are typically yellow or orange with intricate patterns. The plant has a short, erect stem and produces few, large leaves that are elliptical in shape. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the undergrowth of tropical forests.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species.
