Didissandra elegantissima

Didissandra elegantissima, commonly known as the elegant starfish plant, is a unique member of the Araliaceae family native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped leaves that are often a vibrant green with a waxy texture. Due to its ornamental value and rarity, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
