Daphne polystachya

Daphne polystachya, commonly known as the Chinese daphne, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring, typically in shades of pink, white, or red. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves and produces small, berry-like fruits that are edible for birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

However, it is toxic to humans and animals, requiring careful handling.
