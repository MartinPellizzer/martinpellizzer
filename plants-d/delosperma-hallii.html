Delosperma hallii

Delosperma hallii, commonly known as the silverleaf ice plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in fine, silvery hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant pink or red flowers during the summer months, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It is well adapted to rocky and sandy soils, thriving in full sun and requiring minimal water.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
