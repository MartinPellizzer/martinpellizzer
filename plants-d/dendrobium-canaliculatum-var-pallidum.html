Dendrobium canaliculatum var. pallidum

Dendrobium canaliculatum var. pallidum, commonly known as the pale-veined orchid, is a rare and striking epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, narrow pseudobulbs covered with a distinctive pale green or white velvety coating. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, often with a red or orange lip. It thrives in warm, humid climates with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its rarity and specific care requirements, it is a prized species among orchid enthusiasts.
