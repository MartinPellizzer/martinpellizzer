Distephanus lastellei

Distephanus lastellei, commonly known as the lastellei starfish, is a unique member of the family Distephanaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped inflorescence, which consists of multiple small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in a radial pattern. The plant is native to specific regions in southern Africa, particularly South Africa, where it thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. It is often found in coastal and mountainous areas, where it adapts to harsh environmental conditions.
