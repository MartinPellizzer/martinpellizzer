Davallia preslii

Davallia preslii, commonly known as the silver-veined hoya, is a tropical fern native to Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, leathery fronds with a distinctive silver-green coloration and prominent veins. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, adding ornamental value. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Davallia preslii is prized for its elegant appearance and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
