Dehaasia

Dehaasia, commonly known as the Indian nettle, is a flowering plant native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the family Urticaceae, which includes other stinging plants. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a hairy surface that can cause a skin irritation when touched. It produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences.

Dehaasia is often found in moist, shaded areas and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
