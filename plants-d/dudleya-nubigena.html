Dudleya nubigena

Dudleya nubigena, commonly known as the cloud Dudleya, is a succulent plant native to California.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green with a waxy coating. Dudleya nubigena is well adapted to dry climates and is often found in coastal regions.
