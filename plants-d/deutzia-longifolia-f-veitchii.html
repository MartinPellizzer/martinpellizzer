Deutzia longifolia f. veitchii

Deutzia longifolia f. veitchii, commonly known as the Veitchii deutzia, is a compact shrub native to China.

It features small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring, adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant has slender, arching stems and lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for its delicate floral display and hardy nature.
