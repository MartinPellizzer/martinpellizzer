Dudleya collomiae

Dudleya collomiae, commonly known as the Santa Barbara Dudleya, is a succulent plant native to California.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are often covered in a powdery coating. The plant produces vibrant orange or yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.
