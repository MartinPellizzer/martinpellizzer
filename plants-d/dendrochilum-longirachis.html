Dendrochilum longirachis

Dendrochilum longirachis, commonly known as the long-stemmed tree orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems and long, narrow leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange with dark markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Dendrochilum longirachis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance.
