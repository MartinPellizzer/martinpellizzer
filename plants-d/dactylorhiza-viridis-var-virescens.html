Dactylorhiza viridis var. virescens

Dactylorhiza viridis var. virescens, commonly known as the green orchid, is a species of orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green color. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late summer, adding a unique visual appeal. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wet meadows.

This variety is notable for its striking foliage and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
