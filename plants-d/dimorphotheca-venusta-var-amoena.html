Dimorphotheca venusta var. amoena

Dimorphotheca venusta var. amoena, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces fluffy, white seed heads after flowering. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils and full sun.

The variety 'amoena' is particularly noted for its vibrant and varied flower colors.
