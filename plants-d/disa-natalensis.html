Disa natalensis

Disa natalensis, commonly known as the Natal orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with a distinctive hooded shape.

Disa natalensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in suitable conditions.
