Dasianthera luzoniensis

Dasianthera luzoniensis, commonly known as the Luzon spider orchid, is an endemic species found in the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure and vibrant colors. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded forest environments, often on tree trunks or branches. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and attract various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Due to its limited habitat and threats from deforestation, it is considered a vulnerable species.
