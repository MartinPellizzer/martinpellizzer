Daphne zhouana

Daphne zhouana, commonly known as the Chinese daphne, is a species native to China.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its compact growth habit and aromatic foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, typically in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are dark green and glossy, providing a striking contrast to the colorful blooms.

Daphne zhouana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and winter interest.
