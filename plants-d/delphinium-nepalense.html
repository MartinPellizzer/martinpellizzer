Delphinium nepalense

Delphinium nepalense, commonly known as the Nepal larkspur, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan regions of Nepal and northern India.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in alpine and subalpine environments. The plant features tall, erect stems with spiky, blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, often covered in fine hairs.

Delphinium nepalense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
