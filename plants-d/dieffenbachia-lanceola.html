Dieffenbachia lanceola

Dieffenbachia lanceola, commonly known as the Shiny-leaf Dieffenbachia, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, lance-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with white or cream variegation. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically followed by spathes, which are modified leaves that enclose the true flowers.

Dieffenbachia lanceola is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
