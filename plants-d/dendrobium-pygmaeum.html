Dendrobium pygmaeum

Dendrobium pygmaeum, commonly known as the miniature dendrobium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with a slight fragrance. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is known for its compact size, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Dendrobium pygmaeum is relatively easy to care for, requiring moderate light and consistent moisture.
