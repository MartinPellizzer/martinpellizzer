Dysosma tonkinense

It belongs to the family Ranunculaceae and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with five petals and numerous stamens.
