Drimia petiolata

Drimia petiolata, commonly known as the African rain lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its strap-like, grass-like foliage. The plant produces striking white or pale pink flowers that bloom in summer, often in clusters at the base of the leaves. It is also known for its resilience to drought and partial shade.
