Deschampsia media

Deschampsia media, commonly known as blue grama, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and compact, tufted growth habit. This plant thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in prairies and open woodlands. Its small, spike-like inflorescences produce wind-pollinated flowers, contributing to its ecological role in supporting pollinators.

Deschampsia media is valued for its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal in landscaping.
