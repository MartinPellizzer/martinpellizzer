Dracontium camtschaticum

Dracontium camtschaticum, commonly known as the Caucasian arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that features heart-shaped leaves and produces showy, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its botanical name reflects its geographic origin and distinctive leaf shape.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
