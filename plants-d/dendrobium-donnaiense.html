Dendrobium donnaiense

Dendrobium donnaiense, commonly known as the Philippine orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome that produces pseudobulbs and leafy shoots. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink, typically during the dry season. It thrives in moist, shaded environments with well-draining soil.

Dendrobium donnaiense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
