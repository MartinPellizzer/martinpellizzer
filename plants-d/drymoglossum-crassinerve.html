Drymoglossum crassinerve

Drymoglossum crassinerve, commonly known as the broad-veined feather moss, is a species of moss belonging to the family Dicranaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, cushion-like growth form and its dark green, wiry stems. The leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with a distinct midrib and a broad, flat base. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rock crevices.

This moss is known for its ability to retain moisture and contribute to soil stability in its habitat.
