Dianthus szowitisianus

Dianthus szowitisianus, commonly known as the Chinese campion, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and has hairy, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

The flowers are often used in traditional Chinese medicine and are also appreciated for their ornamental value.
