Dendropemon bistriata

Dendropemon bistriata, commonly known as the striped tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery fronds that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant has a unique reproductive system, producing spores on the undersides of its fronds rather than flowers. Its growth is slow, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-drained soil.

Dendropemon bistriata plays an important role in its ecosystem by providing habitat and shelter for various small organisms.
