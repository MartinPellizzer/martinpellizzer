Diospyros liriosmoides

Diospyros liriosmoides, commonly known as the flame tree or flame of the west, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters, often giving the appearance of flames. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are typically ovate to elliptic in shape. Its fruit is a small, round drupe that turns from green to yellow or red when mature.

Diospyros liriosmoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a street tree in warm climates.
