Didiscus ornatus

Didiscus ornatus, commonly known as the jewel flower, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with intricate patterns resembling jewelry. The plant has heart-shaped, waxy leaves that provide a lush green backdrop to its colorful blooms. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Didiscus ornatus is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and as a houseplant.
