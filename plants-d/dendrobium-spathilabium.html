Dendrobium spathilabium

Dendrobium spathilabium, commonly known as the pink-lipped orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant that grows on trees and rocks, often in humid tropical environments. The plant features long, cane-like pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are striking, with a distinctive pink lip and a white or pale yellow dorsal sepal.

Dendrobium spathilabium is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated for horticultural purposes.
