Daphniphyllum crispifolium

Daphniphyllum crispifolium, commonly known as the crinkled leaf daphniphyllum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that have a distinctive crinkled or wavy texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial shade to full sun.

Daphniphyllum crispifolium is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a humid environment.
