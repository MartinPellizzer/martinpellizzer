Dendrobenthamia multinervosa

Dendrobenthamia multinervosa, commonly known as the mountain orchid, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically features long, narrow leaves and produces clusters of white or pale yellow blooms. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas at high altitudes.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
