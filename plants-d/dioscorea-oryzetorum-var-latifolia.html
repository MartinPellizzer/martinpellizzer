Dioscorea oryzetorum var. latifolia

Dioscorea oryzetorum var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf yam, is a perennial vine native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Dioscoreaceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and climbing habit. The plant produces tuberous roots that are used as a food source in some cultures. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires a warm climate with high humidity.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both horticulture and traditional agriculture.
