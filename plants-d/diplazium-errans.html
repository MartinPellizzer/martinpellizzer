Diplazium errans

Diplazium errans, commonly known as the wandering fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its creeping rhizomes that allow it to spread across the forest floor. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with serrated margins, often forming dense mats. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Diplazium errans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticulture.
