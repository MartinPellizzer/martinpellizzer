Dracaena elliptica maculata

Dracaena elliptica maculata, commonly known as the Variegated Dracaena, is a tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It features long, strap-like leaves that are typically green with distinctive white or yellow markings. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and can reach heights of up to 6 feet in optimal conditions. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
