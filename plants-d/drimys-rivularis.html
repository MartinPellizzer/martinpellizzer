Drimys rivularis

Drimys rivularis, commonly known as the river drimys, is a small evergreen shrub native to the temperate regions of South America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter underside. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and white, appearing in clusters during the spring.

The fruit is a red, fleshy berry that contains several seeds, contributing to its role in local ecosystems.
