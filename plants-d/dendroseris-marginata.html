Dendroseris marginata

Dendroseris marginata, commonly known as the marginate treefern, is a distinctive fern native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant has a unique structure with a fibrous root system and a central stem that resembles a tree. Its fronds are typically bright green with a pale margin, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Dendroseris marginata thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in native forests.
