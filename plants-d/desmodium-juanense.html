Desmodium juanense

Desmodium juanense, commonly known as Juan's burr legume, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes known for their nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant produces small, yellow flowers followed by distinctive, spiny pods that contain kidney-shaped seeds. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes considered an invasive species in certain regions.
