Dracocephalum aitcheense

Dracocephalum aitcheense, commonly known as Aitchison's skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of central Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in steppe and desert environments.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
