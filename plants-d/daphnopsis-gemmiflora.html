Daphnopsis gemmiflora

Daphnopsis gemmiflora, commonly known as the gemmed daphnopsis, is a unique evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and glossy, dark green foliage. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that bear showy, white or pale yellow flowers. Its leaves are typically leathery and have a slightly aromatic scent.

Daphnopsis gemmiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various garden conditions.
