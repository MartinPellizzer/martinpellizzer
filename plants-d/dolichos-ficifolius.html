Dolichos ficifolius

Dolichos ficifolius, commonly known as the fig-leaf bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Asia.

It features compound leaves that resemble the shape of a fig leaf, hence its common name. The plant produces small, yellow flowers followed by pods containing edible seeds. Its botanical classification places it in the Fabaceae family, known for nitrogen-fixing abilities.

The plant is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its nutritional seeds.
