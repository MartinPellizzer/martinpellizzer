Diospyros juruensis subsp. hartmanniana

Diospyros juruensis subsp.

hartmanniana, commonly known as the Juruena persimmon, is a tropical evergreen tree native to South America, particularly Brazil. It belongs to the Ebenaceae family, which includes ebony and persimmon trees.

The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers. Its fruit is a small, fleshy drupe that ripens to a deep orange or red color.

The species is valued for its hard, dense wood and is also studied for its potential ecological and medicinal properties.
