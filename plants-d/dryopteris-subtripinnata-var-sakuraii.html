Dryopteris subtripinnata var. sakuraii

Dryopteris subtripinnata var. sakuraii, commonly known as the Sakurai's holly fern, is a variant of the Japanese holly fern.

It is a perennial fern native to Japan, characterized by its distinctive fronds with a tri-pinnate structure. The plant features dark green, leathery leaves that are often used in traditional Japanese gardens. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is valued for its ornamental appearance.

This variant is relatively rare and is often cultivated for its unique foliage and horticultural interest.
