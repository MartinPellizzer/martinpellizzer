Dendrobium bigibbum f. phalaenopsis

Dendrobium bigibbum f. phalaenopsis, commonly known as the Phalaenopsis orchid, is a hybrid orchid species that exhibits unique botanical characteristics.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in its natural habitat. The plant features long, cane-like pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in tropical climates. Its flowers are showy, with lip-shaped petals that often display a variety of colors, including white, pink, and purple.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
