Dendrobium pristinum

Dendrobium pristinum, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a perennial epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a tall, cane-like stem with numerous fleshy, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking clusters of yellow flowers with a red throat, often blooming in summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium pristinum is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
