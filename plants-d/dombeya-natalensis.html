Dombeya natalensis

Dombeya natalensis, commonly known as Natal dombeya, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Africa.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, blooming in spring. The plant has smooth, often grayish bark and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for gardens in temperate climates.

Dombeya natalensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its vibrant floral display.
