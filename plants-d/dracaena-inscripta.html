Dracaena inscripta

Dracaena inscripta, commonly known as the zigzag dracaena, is a tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its distinctive, zigzag-shaped leaves that give it a unique appearance. The plant has a slender, upright stem with a smooth, dark green surface. Its leaves are long, narrow, and often have a slight blue-gray hue.

Dracaena inscripta is a hardy plant that thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.
