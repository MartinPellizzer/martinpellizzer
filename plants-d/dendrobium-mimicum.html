Dendrobium mimicum

Dendrobium mimicum, commonly known as the mimic orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually small and resemble other orchid species, contributing to its common name.

Dendrobium mimicum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
