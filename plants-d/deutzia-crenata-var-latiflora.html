Deutzia crenata var. latiflora

Deutzia crenata var. latiflora, commonly known as the broadleaf deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, arching stems and dark green, oval-shaped leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value comes from its showy flowers and graceful growth habit.
