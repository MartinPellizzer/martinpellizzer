Draba densifolia var. decipiens

Draba densifolia var. decipiens, commonly known as the deceitful denseleaf whitlow grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its dense, basal rosette of leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically thrives in rocky or gravelly soils, often found in alpine or arctic environments. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, and it produces small, rounded seed pods.

This variety is distinguished by its dense foliage and its ability to thrive in harsh, cold climates.
