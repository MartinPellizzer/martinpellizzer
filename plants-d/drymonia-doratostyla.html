Drymonia doratostyla

Drymonia doratostyla, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

The plant thrives in cool, humid environments and is often found in shaded, rocky habitats. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
