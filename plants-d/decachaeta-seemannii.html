Decachaeta seemannii

Decachaeta seemannii, commonly known as the African star grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and distinctive, star-shaped inflorescences. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, white or pale blue flowers arranged in dense, clustered heads. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and rocky areas.

Decachaeta seemannii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
