Dendrobium heyneanum

Dendrobium heyneanum, commonly known as the Heyne's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces racemes of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. Its leaves are usually elliptical and arranged in two rows along the stems.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
