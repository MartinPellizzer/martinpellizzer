Diandranthus aristatus

Diandranthus aristatus, commonly known as the aristate diandranthus, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Acanthaceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular flowers that typically bloom in shades of red or purple. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Diandranthus aristatus is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
