Dendrobium cariniferum var. wattii

Dendrobium cariniferum var. wattii, commonly known as the Watt's spike orchid, is a sympodial epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, branched stem with numerous fleshy, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle. The plant produces racemes of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with prominent, forward-curving lips. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

This variety is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
