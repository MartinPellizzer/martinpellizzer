Drimys granadensis

Drimys granadensis, commonly known as the Chilean wintergreen, is a flowering plant native to southern South America.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows in moist, temperate climates. The plant features elliptical, dark green leaves with a glossy surface and produces small, white, fragrant flowers. Its fruit is a red, fleshy drupe that contains a single seed.

Drimys granadensis is valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
