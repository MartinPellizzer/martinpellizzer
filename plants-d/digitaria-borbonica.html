Digitaria borbonica

Digitaria borbonica, commonly known as the Bourbon grass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny florets. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Digitaria borbonica is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in landscaping.
