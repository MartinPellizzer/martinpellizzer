Dieffenbachia regina

Dieffenbachia regina, commonly known as the queen arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other popular houseplants like peace lilies and caladiums. Its leaves are thick and waxy, which helps it retain moisture in its natural habitat.

Dieffenbachia regina is a popular ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
