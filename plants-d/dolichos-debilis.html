Dolichos debilis

Dolichos debilis, commonly known as the slender cowpea, is a climbing leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features trifoliate leaves and produces small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant is characterized by its slender, twining stems and is often found in disturbed habitats. It is a member of the Fabaceae family and is valued for its edible seeds.

Dolichos debilis is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
