Diplogama otites

Diplogama otites, commonly known as the white wood sorrel, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive white or pale violet coloration on the underside. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
