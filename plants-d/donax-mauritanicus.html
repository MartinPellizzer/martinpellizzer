Donax mauritanicus

Donax mauritanicus, commonly known as the Mauritania boxwood, is a shrub native to the arid regions of North Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and compact growth habit. The plant features small, oval leaves that are usually a dark green color. It is well adapted to drought conditions, making it a hardy plant for xeriscaping.

Donax mauritanicus is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
