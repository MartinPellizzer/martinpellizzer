Dactylorhiza × claudiopolitana nothosubsp.

claudiopolitana, commonly known as the Claudy orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the British Isles. It is a terrestrial orchid with a compact, rhizomatous growth habit and produces upright, clustered spikes of pale yellow to greenish-white flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodland floors and heathlands. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, and it blooms between April and June.
