Decorsea livida

Decorsea livida, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rockrose, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, evergreen leaves that are narrow and leathery, adapted to withstand arid conditions. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Decorsea livida is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
