Droogmansia platypus var. hockii

Droogmansia platypus var. hockii, commonly known as the Hockii variant of the platypus droogmansia, is a unique hybrid plant with distinctive botanical features.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often colorful foliage. The leaves are typically broad and lance-shaped, with a waxy texture that helps it retain moisture. This variant is prized for its ornamental value in horticulture, particularly in shaded or woodland garden settings.

Its genetic origins are a blend of several Droogmansia species, contributing to its unique appearance and adaptability.
