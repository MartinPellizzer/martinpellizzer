Desmodium hamosum

Desmodium hamosum, commonly known as the small cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellow to reddish flowers. The plant produces small, elliptical pods that contain several edible seeds. It is often used as a forage crop and in traditional medicine.

Desmodium hamosum has the ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil fertility.
