Draba longirostra var. avenacea

Draba longirostra var. avenacea, commonly known as the slender-flowered whitlow grass, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of 5 to 20 centimeters. The plant features basal rosettes of narrow, linear leaves and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers in early spring. Its flowers are arranged in racemes and have four petals, typical of the mustard family.

This variety is native to Europe and is often found in grassy habitats and rocky soils.
