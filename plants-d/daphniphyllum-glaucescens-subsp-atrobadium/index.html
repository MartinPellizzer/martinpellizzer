Daphniphyllum Glaucescens Subsp. Atrobadium

Atrobadium daphniphyllum, scientifically known as Daphniphyllum glaucescens subsp. atrobadium, is a rare and striking evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of southern China. Its most distinctive feature is its deep crimson foliage, which contrasts vividly with its glossy green leaves, creating an eye-catching display throughout the year. This plant belongs to the family Pentaphylacaceae and is prized for its ornamental value in horticulture due to its unique coloration and compact growth habit. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in the global plant world, often overlooked by gardeners and botanists alike. Its limited distribution and specific environmental requirements make it a challenging but rewarding plant for cultivation.
