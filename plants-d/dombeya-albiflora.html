Dombeya albiflora

Dombeya albiflora, commonly known as the white dombeya, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Africa.

It features showy, white, tubular flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are oval to lance-shaped with a rough texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and ability to attract pollinators.
