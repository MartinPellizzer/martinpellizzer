Desmodium rufescens

Desmodium rufescens, commonly known as purple desmodium, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant produces small, elliptical pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

Desmodium rufescens is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial in agricultural settings.
