Dahlia crinita

Dahlia crinita, commonly known as the curly frilly dahlia, is a herbaceous perennial native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, orange, yellow, and pink. The plant features upright stems and palmate leaves, which are often covered in a fine, fuzzy texture. Its tuberous roots allow it to survive in various soil types and climates.

Dahlia crinita is prized in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in full sun.
