Delphinium knorringianum

Delphinium knorringianum, commonly known as the Knorring's larkspur, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features tall, slender stems that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height, adorned with clusters of blue to purple flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are deeply lobed, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for alpine gardens.

Due to its striking appearance and ecological significance, it is often cultivated for both its beauty and its role in supporting pollinators.
