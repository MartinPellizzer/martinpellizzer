Dolichos angulosus

Dolichos angulosus, commonly known as the angular cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its angular, ridged pods and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that attract pollinators. It is often cultivated for its edible seeds, which are used in various cuisines.

Dolichos angulosus is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it a beneficial plant in agricultural systems.
