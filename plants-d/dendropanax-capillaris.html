Dendropanax capillaris

Dendropanax capillaris, commonly known as the slender leaf arrowhead, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Australia.

It features a distinctive arrowhead-shaped leaf that is typically bright green and has a soft, velvety texture. The plant grows from a rhizomatous root system and produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are often used in traditional Aboriginal medicine for their purported healing properties.

Dendropanax capillaris thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in swampy or wetland areas.
