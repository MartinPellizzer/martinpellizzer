Dactylorhiza majalis subsp. brennensis

Dactylorhiza majalis subsp.

brennensis, commonly known as the Burnet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and heathlands.

The plant has a single, erect stem that bears several lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are pale pink to white, arranged in a loose spike, and are notable for their delicate appearance.

This orchid is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its specialized mycorrhizal relationships with fungi.
