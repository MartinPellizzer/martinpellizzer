Deutzia × elegantissima f. arcuata

Deutzia × elegantissima f. arcuata, commonly known as the arching deutzia, is a compact, deciduous shrub characterized by its graceful, weeping growth habit.

It features clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer, adding a delicate floral display. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy, providing ornamental value throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, moist soil.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental appeal and ease of care in garden settings.
