Dahlia scapigeroides var. apiculata

Dahlia scapigeroides var. apiculata, commonly known as the Apiculate Dahlia, is a herbaceous perennial native to Mexico.

It features showy, single or semi-double flowers in a range of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant has upright, branching stems and lance-shaped, dark green leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to produce blooms throughout the growing season.
