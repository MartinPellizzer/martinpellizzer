Dyckia brachystachya

Dyckia brachystachya, commonly known as the compact dyckia, is a species of flowering plant in the bromeliad family.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its dense, compact rosette of sword-shaped leaves. The leaves are typically covered in a fine layer of white powdery wax, which helps reduce water loss in its native habitat. It produces small, clustered inflorescences that bloom in shades of yellow or orange.

This plant is often cultivated as an ornamental due to its striking appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
