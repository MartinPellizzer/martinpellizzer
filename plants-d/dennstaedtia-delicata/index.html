Dennstaedtia Delicata

Dennstaedtia delicata is a delicate fern native to moist woodlands and shaded areas across eastern North America. This small, ground-covering plant features finely divided, lance-shaped leaves that give it a lacy appearance. It thrives in cool, humid environments and is often found in forest understories. Its unique fronds are adorned with tiny, spore-producing structures called sori. Despite its modest size, Dennstaedtia delicata plays an important role in forest ecosystems.
