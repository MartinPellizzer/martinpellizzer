Dendrobium lindleyanum

Dendrobium lindleyanum, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, cane-like pseudobulbs covered in dense, leathery leaves. The plant produces striking clusters of golden-yellow flowers with a pleasant fragrance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil with high humidity.

Dendrobium lindleyanum is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its showy blooms.
