Daphne odora var. kiusiana

Daphne odora var. kiusiana, commonly known as the Kiusiana Daphne, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its fragrant, waxy flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring, often in shades of pink, white, and red. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that provide a striking contrast to its showy blossoms. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, requiring protection from harsh winter winds.

Due to its ornamental value and delicate fragrance, it is a popular choice for gardeners seeking winter color.
