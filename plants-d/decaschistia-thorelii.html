Decaschistia thorelii

Decaschistia thorelii, commonly known as the African orchid, is a species of orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its elegant, strap-like leaves and its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth or along riverbanks. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its unique floral structure that attracts specific pollinators.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in some regions.
