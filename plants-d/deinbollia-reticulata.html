Deinbollia reticulata

Deinbollia reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, heart-shaped leaves with a reticulate venation pattern. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Deinbollia reticulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
