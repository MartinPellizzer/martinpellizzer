Delphinium laxiusculum

Delphinium laxiusculum, commonly known as the slender larkspur, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

It is characterized by its tall, spindly stems that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant produces showy blue to purple flowers in clusters at the top of the stem, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, arranged alternately along the stem.

Delphinium laxiusculum is a perennial herb that thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.
