Deutzia brunoniana

Deutzia brunoniana, commonly known as the Chinese deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features clusters of fragrant, white or pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, arching branches and small, ovate leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It prefers partial shade to full sun and well-drained soil.

Deutzia brunoniana is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and easy maintenance.
