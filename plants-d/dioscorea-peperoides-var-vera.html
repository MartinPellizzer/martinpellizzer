Dioscorea peperoides var. vera

Dioscorea peperoides var. vera, commonly known as the African yam bean, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and yellowish flowers. The plant produces tuberous roots that are edible and used in traditional cuisines. Its growth habit involves twining stems that ascend support structures, and it thrives in warm, humid climates.

The species is valued not only for its nutritional content but also for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
