Diplazium umbrosum var. assimile

Diplazium umbrosum var. assimile, commonly known as the shadow fern, is a varietal form of the broad-leafed fern Diplazium umbrosum.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, including parts of India, China, and Southeast Asia. The plant features dark green, leathery fronds with a distinctive, somewhat wavy outline. Its rhizomes are typically short and stout, contributing to its ability to form dense, mat-like growths.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden environments.
