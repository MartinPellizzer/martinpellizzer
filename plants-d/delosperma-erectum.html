Delosperma erectum

Delosperma erectum, commonly known as the ice plant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of pink, red, and purple, typically during the summer months. It is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.

Delosperma erectum is also valued for its ability to prevent soil erosion on slopes and in coastal areas.
