Doryopteris pedata var. palmata

Doryopteris pedata var. palmata, commonly known as the palmate rock fern, is a variant of the rock fern genus Doryopteris.

It is characterized by its distinctive, palmately divided fronds that give it a fern-like appearance. This plant is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and rocky areas. Its leaves are usually bright green and can grow up to several feet in length.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens with appropriate humidity and soil conditions.
