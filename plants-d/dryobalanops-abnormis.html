Dryobalanops abnormis

Dryobalanops abnormis, commonly known as the Malayan ironwood, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, heavy wood that is highly valued for its strength and durability. The plant has a tall, straight trunk with a smooth, dark bark that becomes fissured with age. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and elliptical in shape, often with a glossy upper surface.

The tree produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds.
