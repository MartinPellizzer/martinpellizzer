Dendrobium exsculptum

Dendrobium exsculptum, commonly known as the sculptured orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features elongated, cane-like pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, typical of many orchid species. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with intricate, sculpted petals and sepals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium exsculptum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
