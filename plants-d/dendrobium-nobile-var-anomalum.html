Dendrobium nobile var. anomalum

Dendrobium nobile var. anomalum, commonly known as the Orchid of the Gods, is a hybrid orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a sympodial epiphytic orchid with a pseudobulb structure that stores water and nutrients. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

Its unique floral display and ornamental value make it a popular choice for both horticultural collections and ornamental gardening.
