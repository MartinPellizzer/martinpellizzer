Dypsis bonsai

Dypsis bonsai, commonly known as the areca palm, is a tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It features a slender trunk with a crown of feathery, pinnate leaves that give it a distinctive, elegant appearance. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is often cultivated as a bonsai due to its ability to be shaped and pruned into compact forms.

Dypsis bonsai is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
