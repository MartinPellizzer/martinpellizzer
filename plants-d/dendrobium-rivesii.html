Dendrobium rivesii

Dendrobium rivesii, commonly known as the Rives' orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, slender pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in its natural habitat. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers with a distinct, spicy scent. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, adapting to the humid, shaded environments where it grows.

Dendrobium rivesii is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
