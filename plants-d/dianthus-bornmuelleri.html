Dianthus bornmuelleri

Dianthus bornmuelleri, commonly known as the Sierra foothill carnation, is a flowering plant native to California.

It is characterized by its clusters of bright pink, showy flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. It has narrow, linear leaves and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Dianthus bornmuelleri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
