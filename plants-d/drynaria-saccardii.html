Drynaria saccardii

Drynaria saccardii, commonly known as the basket fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, arching fronds that form a dense, basket-like structure, which gives it its common name. The plant has a robust rhizome system that allows it to thrive in shaded, humid environments. Its leaves are typically dark green and have a leathery texture, contributing to its ornamental value.

Drynaria saccardii is often cultivated as a decorative plant in tropical and subtropical gardens.
