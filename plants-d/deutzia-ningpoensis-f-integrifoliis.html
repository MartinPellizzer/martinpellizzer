Deutzia ningpoensis f. integrifoliis

Deutzia ningpoensis f. integrifoliis, commonly known as the smooth-leaved deutzia, is a flowering shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its simple, entire leaves that give it a distinct appearance compared to other deutzia species. The plant produces clusters of white or pale pink flowers in early summer, which attract pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value lies in its elegant foliage and showy blooms.
