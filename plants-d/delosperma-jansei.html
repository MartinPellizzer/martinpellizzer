Delosperma jansei

Delosperma jansei, commonly known as the Jansei's ice plant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its hardy nature and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
