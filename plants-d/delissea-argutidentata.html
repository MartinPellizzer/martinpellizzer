Delissea argutidentata

Delissea argutidentata, commonly known as the sharp-toothed pittosporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Pittosporaceae and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage. The leaves are elliptical with serrated margins, giving it its common name. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow.

This plant is often found in coastal and forested areas, thriving in well-drained soils.
