Daphne chingshuishaniana

Daphne chingshuishaniana, commonly known as the Chinese daphne, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in early spring. The plant has glossy, ovate leaves that are dark green and provide a striking contrast to its delicate blossoms. It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.

Due to its limited natural range, it is considered a rare and endangered species in the wild.
