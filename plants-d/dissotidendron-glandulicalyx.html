Dissotidendron glandulicalyx

Dissotidendron glandulicalyx, commonly known as the glandular-leaved dissotidendron, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are often glandular-dotted, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to several meters. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually greenish-white in color, and it produces woody capsules as fruit.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments within tropical forests.
