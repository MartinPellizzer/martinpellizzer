Daviesia mimosoides var. saligna

Daviesia mimosoides var. saligna, commonly known as the silky mimosoides, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It features slender, willow-like stems with small, fern-like leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers in late winter to early spring, which are clustered in dense, cylindrical inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to Mediterranean climates.

This variety is often used in native gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
