Dovyalis glandulosissima

Dovyalis glandulosissima, commonly known as the sour plum or wild mango, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and yellow or orange flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, edible fruits that are typically sour and have a high acidity level. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and semi-arid regions.

Dovyalis glandulosissima is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
