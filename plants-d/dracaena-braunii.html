Dracaena braunii

Dracaena braunii, commonly known as the dwarf dracaena, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a short, thick stem with narrow, dark green leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow streaks. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its compact size and ornamental foliage make it suitable for tabletop displays or as a potted plant.

Dracaena braunii is also valued for its air-purifying qualities, contributing to improved indoor air quality.
