Dieffenbachia bolivarana

Dieffenbachia bolivarana, commonly known as the Bolivarian Dieffenbachia, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green with contrasting white or cream veins. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other popular houseplants like peace lilies. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
