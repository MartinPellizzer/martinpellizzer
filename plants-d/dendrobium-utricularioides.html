Dendrobium utricularioides

Dendrobium utricularioides, commonly known as the umbrella orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with multiple stems that can reach up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often arranged in racemose inflorescences. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps it conserve moisture.

Dendrobium utricularioides thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
