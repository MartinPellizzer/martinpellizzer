Desmodium leschenaultii

Desmodium leschenaultii, commonly known as the narrow-leaf desmodium, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant has a deep root system that helps it thrive in poor soils and withstand drought conditions.

It is often used in traditional medicine and as a forage plant for livestock due to its nutritional value.
