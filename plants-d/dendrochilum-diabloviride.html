Dendrochilum diabloviride

Dendrochilum diabloviride, commonly known as the Diablo Green Dendrochilum, is a rare orchid species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking emerald green foliage and elongated, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces strikingly colored flowers with a unique greenish hue, which is unusual for orchids. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated by enthusiasts in controlled conditions.
