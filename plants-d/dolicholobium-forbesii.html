Dolicholobium forbesii

Dolicholobium forbesii, commonly known as the Forbes' dolicholobium, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, adapting to the harsh climatic conditions of its habitat.
