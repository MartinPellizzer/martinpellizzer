Draba taurica

Draba taurica, commonly known as the Caucasian rockcress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or alpine environments. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in early spring. Its leaves are basal, rosette-like, and covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

Draba taurica is adapted to harsh, cold climates and is often found in mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.
