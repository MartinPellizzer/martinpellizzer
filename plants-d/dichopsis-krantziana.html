Dichopsis krantziana

Dichopsis krantziana, commonly known as the Krantz's spider plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves with a distinctive white or yellowish margin. The plant produces delicate, spider-like flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright indirect light.

Its compact size and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
