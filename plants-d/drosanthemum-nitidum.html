Drosanthemum nitidum

Drosanthemum nitidum, commonly known as the silver leaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage and daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping. Its compact growth habit and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking low-maintenance plants.

Drosanthemum nitidum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
