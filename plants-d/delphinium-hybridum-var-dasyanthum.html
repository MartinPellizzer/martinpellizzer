Delphinium hybridum var. dasyanthum

Delphinium hybridum var. dasyanthum, commonly known as the tall delphinium, is a perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters, supporting large, showy blue to purple flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem, and its flowers are borne in dense spikes. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

The botanical features of this variety make it a striking addition to both natural and cultivated environments.
