Diphyes elegantior

Diphyes elegantior, commonly known as the elegant orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are broad and glossy, and it produces clusters of showy, colorful blooms.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
