Dampiera repanda

Dampiera repanda, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a herbaceous plant native to Australia.

It features small, daisy-like white flowers with a yellow center, typically blooming in spring. The plant has silvery-gray foliage that helps reflect sunlight, reducing water loss in arid conditions. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and dry inland areas.

Dampiera repanda is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native gardens.
