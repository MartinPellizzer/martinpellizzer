Dipladenia crassinoda

Dipladenia crassinoda, commonly known as the trumpet vine or large-flowered cypress vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to South America.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves and produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant is known for its vigorous growth and ability to climb trellises or other structures. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its flowers attract hummingbirds and butterflies, adding both beauty and ecological value to gardens.
