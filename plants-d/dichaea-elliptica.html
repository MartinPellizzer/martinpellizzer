Dichaea elliptica

Dichaea elliptica, commonly known as the slender dichaea, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid with slender, upright stems and oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in greenhouses or conservatories.
