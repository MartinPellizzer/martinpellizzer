Dendrobium macrophyllum var. stenopterum

Dendrobium macrophyllum var. stenopterum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf dendrobium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. The plant features long, cane-like stems with clusters of narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with a distinct, fragrant scent.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
