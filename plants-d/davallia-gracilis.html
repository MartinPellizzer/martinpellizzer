Davallia gracilis

Davallia gracilis, commonly known as the slender shield fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its delicate, arching fronds that are typically less than a meter in length. The plant features a short, creeping rhizome with numerous small, shield-shaped pinnae. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on rock surfaces or in moist soil.

Davallia gracilis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ease of care.
