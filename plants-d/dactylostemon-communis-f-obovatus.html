Dactylostemon communis f. obovatus

Dactylostemon communis f. obovatus, commonly known as the obovate coral tree, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub or tree with smooth, greyish bark and compound leaves. The plant produces clusters of bright red, coral-like flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its foliage is characterized by obovate leaves that are glossy and dark green, providing an ornamental appearance.

It is often cultivated for its striking floral display and is adaptable to various soil types.
