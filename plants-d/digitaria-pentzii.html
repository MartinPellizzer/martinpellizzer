Digitaria pentzii

Digitaria pentzii, commonly known as African grass or panic grass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

Digitaria pentzii is often found in disturbed areas, grasslands, and along roadsides.
