Diazeuxis trinervis

Diazeuxis trinervis, commonly known as the three-veined fern, is a species of fern characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features a rhizomatous growth habit, with fronds that are typically bipinnate or tripinnate. Its leaves are usually bright green, with a distinct three-veined structure that gives it its scientific name.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
