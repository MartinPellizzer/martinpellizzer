Disa linderiana

Disa linderiana, commonly known as the Linder's Disa, is a species of orchid native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or grassy areas, preferring well-drained soils. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with a distinctive hooded structure.

Disa linderiana is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
