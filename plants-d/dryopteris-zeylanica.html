Dryopteris zeylanica

Dryopteris zeylanica, commonly known as the Ceylon wood fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, upright fronds that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant features a rhizomatous growth habit, with fertile fronds that produce sporangia on the underside. Its leaves are typically bipinnate, with a distinctive pattern of pinnae and pinnules.

This fern thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
