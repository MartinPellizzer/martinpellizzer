Dodonaea bursariifolia

Dodonaea bursariifolia, commonly known as the bursar leaf screw tree, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its small, green, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for arid environments.

The fruit is a small, woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.
