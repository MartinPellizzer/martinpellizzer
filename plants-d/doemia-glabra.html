Doemia glabra

Doemia glabra, commonly known as the smooth doemia, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous leaves and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pale yellow. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and ecological studies.

Doemia glabra is often found in rocky or sandy habitats, contributing to the biodiversity of its native ecosystems.
