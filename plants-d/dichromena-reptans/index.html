Dichromena Reptans

Dichromena reptans is a fascinating aquatic plant known for its unique ability to change color with environmental conditions. It thrives in freshwater habitats, often found in slow-moving streams and ponds. Its leaves exhibit a striking shift from green to red, making it visually captivating. This plant plays a vital role in maintaining water quality by absorbing excess nutrients. Studying Dichromena reptans offers valuable insights into plant adaptation and ecological balance.
