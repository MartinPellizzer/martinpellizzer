Dianella lavarum

Dianella lavarum, commonly known as the silver lily or coastal dianella, is a perennial evergreen plant native to coastal regions of southern Australia.

It features strap-like, silver-green leaves that are narrow and grass-like in appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in sandy or rocky coastal environments.

Dianella lavarum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate salt spray and coastal conditions.
