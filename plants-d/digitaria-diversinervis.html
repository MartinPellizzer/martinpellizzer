Digitaria diversinervis

Digitaria diversinervis, commonly known as the broad-leaf bluestem, is a grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its wide, flat leaves with a prominent central vein and a bluish-green color. The plant typically grows in wetland habitats, such as marshes and swampy areas, and is often found in association with other wetland grasses. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers.

Digitaria diversinervis plays an important ecological role in maintaining wetland ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
