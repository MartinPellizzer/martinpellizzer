Dendrobium ischnophyton

Dendrobium ischnophyton, commonly known as the narrow-leafed dendrobium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees or rocks in tropical rainforests. The plant features long, slender pseudobulbs covered with elliptical leaves that are often narrow and leathery. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, coming in shades of white, yellow, and green.

It requires high humidity and indirect light for optimal growth and flowering.
