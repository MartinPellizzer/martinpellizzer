Diasperus hamrur

Diasperus hamrur, commonly known as the desert thorn, is a shrub native to arid regions of Africa and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and small, clustered leaves that help reduce water loss. The plant produces yellow flowers, which bloom in the summer months. Its hardy nature allows it to thrive in poor soil conditions and extreme temperatures.

Diasperus hamrur plays a role in stabilizing sandy soils and providing shelter for small animals.
