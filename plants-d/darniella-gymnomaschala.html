Darniella gymnomaschala

Darniella gymnomaschala, commonly known as the naked stem dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in a single head.

Darniella gymnomaschala is known for its ability to thrive in arid conditions, making it a hardy and resilient species.
