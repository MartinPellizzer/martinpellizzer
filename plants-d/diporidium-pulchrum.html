Diporidium pulchrum

Diporidium pulchrum, commonly known as the beautiful diporidium, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in clusters, displaying vibrant colors such as red, orange, and yellow. The plant has opposite, simple leaves that are usually ovate or elliptical in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in lowland forests or near water bodies.

Its botanical features make it a popular ornamental plant in horticulture.
