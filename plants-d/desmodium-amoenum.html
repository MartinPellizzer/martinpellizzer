Desmodium amoenum

Desmodium amoenum, commonly known as the smallflower burr medic, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces distinctive, spiny pods that contain several small, kidney-shaped seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas or along roadsides.

Desmodium amoenum is valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability and is used in forage and soil improvement.
