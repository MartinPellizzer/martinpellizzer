Diospyros unisemina

Diospyros unisemina, commonly known as the African persimmon, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Ebenaceae family, which includes ebony and persimmon trees. The plant is characterized by its smooth, dark bark and elliptical leaves that are glossy on the upper surface. Its fruit is a small, yellowish-orange drupe with a sweet, edible pulp.

Diospyros unisemina is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some African cultures.
