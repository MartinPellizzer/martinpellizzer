Diospyros ferrea var. ebenus

Diospyros ferrea var. ebenus, commonly known as ironwood or ebony, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark-colored wood, which is highly valued for its strength and durability. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth texture. It produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by small, round fruits.

The tree is often cultivated for its ornamental value and for the high-quality timber it provides.
