Dendrobium falcatum

Dendrobium falcatum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, narrow leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium falcatum is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its elegant floral display.
