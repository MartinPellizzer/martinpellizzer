Dendrosenecio erici-rosenii subsp. erici-rosenii

Dendrosenecio erici-rosenii subsp.

erici-rosenii, commonly known as the Eric Rosenii Tree, is a striking succulent native to South Africa. It features a tall, upright stem that can grow up to several meters in height, often branching into a tree-like form.

The plant produces large, showy yellow flowers during the summer months, which are borne on long, branched inflorescences. Its leaves are fleshy and arranged in a rosette pattern at the base, adapted for water storage in arid environments.

This species is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens with well-draining soil and full sun.
