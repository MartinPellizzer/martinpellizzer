Delphinium consolida f. gracillima

Delphinium consolida f. gracillima, commonly known as the slender larkspur, is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of blue to purple flowers.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is native to Europe and western Asia. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Its delicate appearance and vibrant flowers make it a valuable addition to ornamental gardens.
