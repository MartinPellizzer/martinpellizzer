Desideria haranensis

Desideria haranensis, commonly known as the Haran Desert orchid, is a rare flowering plant native to the arid regions of the Middle East.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pale pink. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and requires minimal water, making it well adapted to harsh desert conditions. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, helping to store moisture.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Desideria haranensis is considered a vulnerable species.
