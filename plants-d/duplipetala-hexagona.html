Duplipetala hexagona

Duplipetala hexagona, commonly known as the hexagonal petal plant, is a unique flowering species characterized by its distinctive hexagonal-shaped petals.

Native to the arid regions of South America, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure. The plant exhibits a compact growth habit with a short stem and clustered foliage. Its flowers are typically vibrant in color, ranging from yellow to orange, and bloom during the dry season.

Due to its unusual morphology, it is a subject of interest in botanical studies and horticultural cultivation.
