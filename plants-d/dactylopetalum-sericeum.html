Dactylopetalum sericeum

Dactylopetalum sericeum, commonly known as the silky hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its soft, silky-haired leaves and showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in coastal areas or near freshwater sources.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
