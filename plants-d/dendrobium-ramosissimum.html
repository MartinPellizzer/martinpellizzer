Dendrobium ramosissimum

Dendrobium ramosissimum, commonly known as the branched dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a tall, wiry stem with numerous branching shoots, which are often covered in a dense layer of white or grayish pubescence. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink, typically during the summer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium ramosissimum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its long-lasting blooms.
