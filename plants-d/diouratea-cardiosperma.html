Diouratea cardiosperma

Diouratea cardiosperma, commonly known as the heartseed tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and large, simple, alternate leaves that are elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in panicles, which are followed by unique, heart-shaped fruits containing a single large seed. The tree is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in local medicine.

Its growth habit and distinctive fruit make it a notable species in botanical studies.
