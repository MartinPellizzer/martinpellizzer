Diosma ciliata

Diosma ciliata, commonly known as the silky diosma, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves with finely toothed margins, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its flowers are small, white or pale pink, and are clustered in loose inflorescences.

Diosma ciliata is known for its aromatic foliage and is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
