Diplostephium liratum

Diplostephium liratum, commonly known as the featherleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in desert or semi-desert environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers consist of a yellow disk surrounded by ray florets.
