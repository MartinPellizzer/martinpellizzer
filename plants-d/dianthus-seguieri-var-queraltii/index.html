Dianthus Seguieri Var. Queraltii

Dianthus seguieri var. queraltii is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of Spain. It is known for its vibrant, pinkish-red flowers that bloom in early spring. This variety is closely related to the common carnation but possesses unique characteristics that set it apart. Its delicate appearance makes it a sought-after addition to rock gardens and alpine collections. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a valuable and endangered species in its natural habitat.
