Dolichos tenuiflorus

Dolichos tenuiflorus, commonly known as slender-flowered dolichos, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features small, delicate white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with slender, elongated leaflets. Its fibrous roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The pods are slender and contain small, kidney-shaped seeds that are sometimes used as a food source.
