Dolichopentas decora var. pubescens

Dolichopentas decora var. pubescens, commonly known as the velvety pentas, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of star-shaped, brightly colored flowers that range from white to pink and red. The plant has hairy, glandular leaves that give it a distinctive, velvety texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This ornamental plant is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and is popular in garden landscapes.
