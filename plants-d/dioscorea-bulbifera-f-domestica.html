Dioscorea bulbifera f. domestica

Dioscorea bulbifera f. domestica, commonly known as the edible arrowroot plant, is a tropical vine native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and the production of edible tubers that are rich in starch. The plant also bears aerial tubers, which are often used as a food source in various cultures. Its leaves are heart-shaped and can grow quite large, while its flowers are small and yellow-green in color.

This variety is cultivated for its nutritional value and is often used in traditional cuisines.
