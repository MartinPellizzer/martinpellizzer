Deutzia crenata f. hebecarpa

Deutzia crenata f. hebecarpa, commonly known as the dwarf deutzia, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in early spring, typically in clusters along the stems. The plant has finely toothed, ovate leaves that turn reddish in autumn. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Its compact size and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for smaller garden spaces.
