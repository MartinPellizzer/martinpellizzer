Deutzia muliensis

Deutzia muliensis, commonly known as the Muli deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features upright, slender stems that bear clusters of small, fragrant white or pink flowers in early summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Deutzia muliensis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
