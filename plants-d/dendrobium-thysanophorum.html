Dendrobium thysanophorum

Dendrobium thysanophorum, commonly known as the golden spike orchid, is a epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a short, thick stem and numerous, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a distinctive golden or yellowish stripe. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This species is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable conditions.
