Deyeuxia hyperborea

Deyeuxia hyperborea, commonly known as the alpine feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to high-altitude regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

This plant thrives in cold, arid environments and is typically found in alpine meadows and tundra ecosystems.
