Draba marinellii

Draba marinellii, commonly known as the maritime draba, is a small flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is native to coastal regions of Europe and is adapted to harsh, saline environments. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils along shorelines, where it thrives in full sun. Its flowers are small, white or pale yellow, and appear in clusters during the spring.

Draba marinellii is notable for its resilience and plays a role in stabilizing coastal ecosystems.
