Dennstaedtia cicutaria var. erosa

Dennstaedtia cicutaria var. erosa, commonly known as the erose fern, is a variant of the broad-leaf fern characterized by its delicate, bipinnate fronds.

It belongs to the family Osmundaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas. The leaves are typically light green, with a soft texture and a distinctive erose margin, giving it its common name.
