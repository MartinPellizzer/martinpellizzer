Desmodium hirtum f. compressum

Desmodium hirtum f. compressum, commonly known as the narrowleaf burr legume, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-bearing fruits. The plant features trifoliate leaves with small, hairy stipules and produces small, yellow to pale green flowers. Its fruit is a hairy, inflated pod that contains several elliptical seeds.

This species is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils, where it plays a role in nitrogen fixation through its symbiotic relationship with rhizobia.
