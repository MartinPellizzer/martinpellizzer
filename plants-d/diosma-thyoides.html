Diosma thyoides

Diosma thyoides, commonly known as the white diosma, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant features slender stems and small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Diosma thyoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
