Dichorisandra marantoides

Dichorisandra marantoides, commonly known as the flame plant, is a tropical herbaceous plant native to Africa.

It features striking, arrow-shaped leaves with a vibrant red or orange hue, often with a contrasting green border. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are typically clustered in spikes. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
