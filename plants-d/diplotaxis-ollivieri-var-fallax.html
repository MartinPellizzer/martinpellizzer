Diplotaxis ollivieri var. fallax

Diplotaxis ollivieri var. fallax, commonly known as the desert cress, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa and the Middle East.

It is a low-growing annual herb with erect, branched stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds. It is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water requirements.

The species is often used in traditional medicine and as a source of edible seeds.
