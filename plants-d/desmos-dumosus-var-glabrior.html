Desmos dumosus var. glabrior

Desmos dumosus var. glabrior, commonly known as the smooth desmos, is a shrub native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and leathery, dark green leaves that are typically smooth and glabrous. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a subtle ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained sandy soils and is adapted to coastal conditions with high salinity and strong winds.

This variety is often used in coastal landscaping due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.
