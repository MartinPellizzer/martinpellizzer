Dendrobium purpureum var. steffensianum

Dendrobium purpureum var. steffensianum, commonly known as the purple orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a sympodial epiphyte, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic, and typically produces long, cane-like stems. The plant is characterized by its vibrant purple and pink flowers, which bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
