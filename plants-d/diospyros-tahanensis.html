Diospyros tahanensis

Diospyros tahanensis, commonly known as Tahan's ebony, is a species of flowering plant in the family Ebenaceae.

It is native to the Philippines and is typically found in lowland rainforests. The plant is characterized by its dark, dense wood, which is highly valued for its strength and durability. It produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by edible, round fruits.

The leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a glossy surface.
