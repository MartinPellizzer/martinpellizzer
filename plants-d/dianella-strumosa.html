Dianella strumosa

Dianella strumosa, commonly known as the slender flax lily, is a perennial evergreen plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves that are often blue-green in color. Dianella strumosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and landscaping.
