Dendrobium ischnopetalum

Dendrobium ischnopetalum, commonly known as the narrow-petaled orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is an epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Indonesia and Malaysia. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are typically white or pale yellow, with narrow petals and a spurred lip.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media for optimal growth.
