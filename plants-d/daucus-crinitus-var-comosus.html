Daucus crinitus var. comosus

Daucus crinitus var. comosus, commonly known as the hairy parsley or the horned fennel, is a flowering plant in the family Apiaceae.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and is characterized by its finely divided, feathery leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in umbrella-like clusters. Its aromatic qualities and ornamental value make it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
