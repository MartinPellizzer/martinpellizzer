Dendrobium densiflorum var. clavatum

Dendrobium densiflorum var. clavatum, commonly known as the Clavate Dendrobium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant that grows on trees or rocks, typically in humid tropical environments. The plant features a clumping growth habit with multiple stems that can reach several feet in height. Its flowers are large, showy, and often fragrant, with a distinctive clavate (club-shaped) spur.

The leaves are long and strap-like, and the plant produces numerous blooms during the warmer months.
