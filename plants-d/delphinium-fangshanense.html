Delphinium fangshanense

Delphinium fangshanense, commonly known as the Fangshan larkspur, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, spindly stems and showy blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and is adapted to cool, moist environments. Its leaves are compound, with serrated edges, and it produces numerous small, fluffy seeds in its fruit.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
