Dianthus serotinus regis-stephani

Dianthus serotinus regis-stephani, commonly known as the late-flowering campion, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its showy, pink or red flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it has a compact, upright growth habit.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and is relatively easy to care for.
