Dictyostega usambarica

Dictyostega usambarica, commonly known as the Usambara daisy, is a flowering plant native to the Usambara Mountains in Tanzania.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and thistles. The plant produces showy, pink to purple daisy-like flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming a basal rosette.

Dictyostega usambarica is adapted to grow in rocky, well-drained soils at higher altitudes.
