Dendrobium veratrifolium var. dahlemense

Dendrobium veratrifolium var. dahlemense, commonly known as the Dahlem orchid, is a rare and exquisite epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, branching growth habit with numerous pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with intricate patterns on the petals. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
