Deschampsia alpigena

Deschampsia alpigena, commonly known as alpine feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, hair-like leaves and delicate, feathery inflorescences that bloom in late summer. This plant thrives in alpine and subalpine environments, often growing in rocky or gravelly soils at high elevations. It plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various alpine organisms.

Due to its hardy nature, it is also cultivated in gardens as an ornamental plant.
