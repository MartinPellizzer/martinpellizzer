Deutzia longifolia f. elegans

Deutzia longifolia f. elegans, commonly known as the elegant deutzia, is a compact shrub native to China.

It features small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in early summer, adding a delicate floral display to gardens. The plant has narrow, dark green leaves that provide a nice contrast to its flowering buds. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardens and borders.
