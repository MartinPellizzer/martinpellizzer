Dendrobium xanthothece

Dendrobium xanthothece, commonly known as the yellow-lipped orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with multiple stems that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a distinctive lip that is yellow or orange in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Dendrobium xanthothece is valued for its ornamental beauty and is commonly cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
