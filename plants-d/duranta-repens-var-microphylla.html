Duranta repens var. microphylla

Duranta repens var. microphylla, commonly known as the silverleaf duranta, is a compact, trailing shrub native to the Caribbean and parts of South America.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically silver-gray in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of bright blue or violet flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is drought-tolerant once established.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for container gardening and as a ground cover in warm climates.
