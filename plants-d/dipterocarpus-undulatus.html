Dipterocarpus undulatus

Dipterocarpus undulatus, commonly known as the warty dipterocarp, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, warty bark and can grow up to 40 meters tall. The plant produces large, showy flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its fruits are woody capsules that release seeds when mature.

This species is valued for its hard, durable wood and plays an important role in the ecological systems of tropical rainforests.
