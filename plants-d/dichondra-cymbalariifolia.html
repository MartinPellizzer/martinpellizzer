Dichondra cymbalariifolia

Dichondra cymbalariifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf dichondra, is a low-growing perennial plant native to South America.

It features small, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Dichondra cymbalariifolia is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and ability to form dense mats.
