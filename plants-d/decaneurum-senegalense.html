Decaneurum senegalense

Decaneurum senegalense, commonly known as the Senegal grass, is a perennial grass native to tropical Africa.

It typically grows in sandy or coastal areas and is adapted to saline and arid conditions. The plant has narrow, elongated leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It is often used for erosion control due to its extensive root system.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological restoration projects.
