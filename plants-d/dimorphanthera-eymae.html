Dimorphanthera eymae

Dimorphanthera eymae, commonly known as the Eyma's orchid, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of Queensland, Australia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has strap-like leaves and produces intricate, pendulous inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
