Desmodium canadense var. longifolium

Desmodium canadense var. longifolium, commonly known as Canada milkvetch, is a perennial legume native to North America.

It features trifoliate leaves with long, slender leaflets and produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is characterized by its extensive root system and ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil enrichment. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

This species is often found in open woodlands, prairies, and along roadsides.
