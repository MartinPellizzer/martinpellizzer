Disterigma pernettyoides

Disterigma pernettyoides, commonly known as the red-flowered disterigma, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking, showy red flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. The plant has slender, branching stems covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in grassland and rocky habitats.

Disterigma pernettyoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
