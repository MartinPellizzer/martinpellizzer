Digitaria lunularis

Digitaria lunularis, commonly known as moonflower grass, is a species of grass native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that bloom at night. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and is often found in open habitats such as fields and roadsides. It is a perennial species that can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Its flowering pattern and ecological role make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
