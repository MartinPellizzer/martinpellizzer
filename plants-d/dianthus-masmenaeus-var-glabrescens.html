Dianthus masmenaeus var. glabrescens

Dianthus masmenaeus var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth-flowered carnation, is a compact perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, double flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, with a distinctive, slightly fragrant scent. The leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, while the stems are sturdy and upright. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
