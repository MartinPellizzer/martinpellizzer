Didymochlaena truncatula var. attenuata

Didymochlaena truncatula var. attenuata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved didymochlaena, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense clusters, which are typically borne in the axils of the leaves. Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
