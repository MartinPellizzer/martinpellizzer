Dieffenbachia picta subvar. meleagris

Dieffenbachia picta subvar. meleagris, commonly known as the spotted dieffenbachia, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with deep green leaves featuring white and cream markings. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is known for its upright growth habit and relatively slow growth rate. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
