Danais volubilis

Danais volubilis, commonly known as the climbing clematis, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Ranunculaceae.

It is characterized by its vigorous climbing habit and showy, often fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features palmately divided leaves and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that can vary in color depending on the species. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Danais volubilis is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to cover trellises or walls.
