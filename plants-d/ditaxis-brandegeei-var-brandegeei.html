Ditaxis brandegeei var. brandegeei

Ditaxis brandegeei var. brandegeei, commonly known as the Brandegee's ditaxis, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a short, thick stem with fleshy, spiny leaves that are typically green or bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the warmer months, which are followed by capsule fruits containing seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
