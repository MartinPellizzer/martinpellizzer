Dendrochilum rotundilabium

Dendrochilum rotundilabium, commonly known as the round-labied tree orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, pale yellow to greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its flowers have a unique structure with rounded labellum and spurred sepals.

Dendrochilum rotundilabium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
