Doona zeylanica

Doona zeylanica, commonly known as the Ceylon gooseberry, is a small evergreen shrub native to Sri Lanka.

It produces small, yellow, edible berries that are rich in vitamin C. The plant has elliptical leaves with a leathery texture and is often found in moist, shaded areas. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and medicinal properties.

The fruit is used in traditional medicine and as a flavoring agent in various culinary applications.
