Deutzia longifolia var. pingwuensis

Deutzia longifolia var. pingwuensis, commonly known as the Pingwu deutzia, is a flowering shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, upright growth habit and clusters of white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant features narrow, elliptical leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value comes from its showy flowers and compact form, which make it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
