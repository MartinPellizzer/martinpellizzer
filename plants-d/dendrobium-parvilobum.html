Dendrobium parvilobum

Dendrobium parvilobum, commonly known as the small-leaf dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with cane-like stems that bear clusters of lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and yellow, often with a distinctive yellow lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium parvilobum is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
