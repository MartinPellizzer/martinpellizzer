Deinandra increscens subsp. foliosa

Deinandra increscens subsp.

foliosa, commonly known as the leafy deinandra, is a shrub native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant typically grows in coastal regions and has adapted to sandy, well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature.
