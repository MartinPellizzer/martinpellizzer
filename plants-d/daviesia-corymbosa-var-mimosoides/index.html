Daviesia Corymbosa Var. Mimosoides

Daviesia corymbosa var. mimosoides is a unique plant native to western australia known for its striking yellow flowers. it belongs to the family dilleniaceae and is often found in sandy soils along coastal areas. the plant's resemblance to acacia trees has earned it the common name mimosa-like davisia. it thrives in full sun and is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value. this variety is also valued for its role in supporting local wildlife and biodiversity.
