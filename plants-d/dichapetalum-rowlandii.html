Dichapetalum rowlandii

Dichapetalum rowlandii, commonly known as the African star orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its flowers are usually pale green or white, with a distinctive central column that resembles a star.

Dichapetalum rowlandii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
