Daphniphyllum blumeanum

Daphniphyllum blumeanum, commonly known as the Blume's winterberry, is a species of evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Ericaceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers. The plant produces bright red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.
