Dendrobium polycladium

Dendrobium polycladium, commonly known as the golden spike orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, branched stem covered in dense, leathery leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellow-orange flowers with prominent yellow lips. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Dendrobium polycladium is valued for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
