Dillwynia cinerascens var. parviflora

Dillwynia cinerascens var. parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered dillwynia, is a perennial herb native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant features slender, branching stems and small, pale yellow to cream flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

It is often found in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to semi-arid environments.
