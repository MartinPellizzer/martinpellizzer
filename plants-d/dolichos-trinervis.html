Dolichos trinervis

Dolichos trinervis, commonly known as the three-veined bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It features compound leaves with three main veins, giving it its scientific name. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by elongated pods containing edible seeds. Dolichos trinervis is valued for its nutritional content, as the seeds are a good source of protein and minerals.

It is often cultivated for both food and traditional medicinal uses.
