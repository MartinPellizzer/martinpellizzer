Deparia deflexa

Deparia deflexa, commonly known as the flexed shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds that are typically curled or flexed at the base. The plant is native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere, particularly in Asia and North America. Its leaves are usually dark green and have a leathery texture, with serrated margins.

Deparia deflexa thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky slopes.
