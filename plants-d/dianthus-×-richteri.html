Dianthus × richteri

Dianthus × richteri, commonly known as the German carnation, is a hybrid perennial plant belonging to the Carnation family (Caryophyllaceae).

It is characterized by its showy, double blooms that come in a wide range of colors, including red, pink, white, and purple. The plant typically grows to a height of 10–30 cm, with lance-shaped leaves and a compact, bushy habit. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Dianthus × richteri is valued for its long-lasting flowers and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
