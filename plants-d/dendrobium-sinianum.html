Dendrobium sinianum

Dendrobium sinianum, commonly known as the Chinese orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to China.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in shaded, humid environments with well-draining soil.

Dendrobium sinianum is valued for its ornamental beauty and cultural significance in traditional Chinese horticulture.
