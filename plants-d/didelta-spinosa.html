Didelta spinosa

Didelta spinosa, commonly known as the spiked didelta, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a rough texture. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release small, bean-like seeds.

Didelta spinosa is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
