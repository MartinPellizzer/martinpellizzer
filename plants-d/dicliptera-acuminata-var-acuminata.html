Dicliptera acuminata var. acuminata

Dicliptera acuminata var. acuminata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved dicliptera, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. The plant features slender, erect stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its small, clustered inflorescences produce tiny white or pale blue flowers, which are often overlooked but contribute to its ecological value.

This species is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
