Dissochaeta glabra var. kinabaluensis

Dissochaeta glabra var. kinabaluensis, commonly known as the Borneo hairless dandelion, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Borneo.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and cylindrical stems. The plant is distinguished by its smooth, hairless leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments at higher elevations.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species in the region.
