Dieffenbachia beachiana

Dieffenbachia beachiana, commonly known as the Beachiana Dieffenbachia, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that display shades of green, white, and yellow. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is known for its upright growth habit. Its foliage is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its aesthetic appeal.

It prefers bright, indirect light and moderate humidity to thrive.
