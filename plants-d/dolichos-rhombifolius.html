Dolichos rhombifolius

Dolichos rhombifolius, commonly known as the kidney bean plant, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features trifoliate leaves with rhombic leaflets, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that develop into pods containing edible kidney-shaped seeds. It is valued for its nutritional content and is often cultivated for both food and ornamental purposes.

Its growth habit and botanical characteristics make it a notable species in agricultural and ecological contexts.
