Delphinium pentagynum var. phialotrichum

Delphinium pentagynum var. phialotrichum, commonly known as the alpine larkspur, is a member of the Ranunculaceae family.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia. The plant features tall, sparsely branched stems that bear clusters of blue to purple flowers. Its leaves are compound, with lanceolate leaflets arranged alternately along the stem.

It thrives in well-drained, alkaline soils and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes.
