Dendrobium meliodorum

Dendrobium meliodorum, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange with red markings. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrobium meliodorum is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its beautiful blooms.
