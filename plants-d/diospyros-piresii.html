Diospyros piresii

Diospyros piresii, commonly known as the Piresii persimmon, is a species of flowering plant in the Ebenaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is a small to medium-sized tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height, with smooth, grey-brown bark. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and ovate, often with a waxy coating.

The fruit is a small, round berry that turns from green to orange-red when mature, containing a single seed.
