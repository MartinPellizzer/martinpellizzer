Dampiera altissima var. dura

Dampiera altissima var. dura, commonly known as the hardy dampiera, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes. Its foliage is lance-shaped and evergreen, providing year-round interest.

It is also known for its resilience to drought and poor soil conditions.
