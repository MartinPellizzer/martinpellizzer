Dianthus caryophyllus f. grandiflorus

Dianthus caryophyllus f. grandiflorus, commonly known as the grandiflorus carnation, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that typically range in color from white to deep red. The plant features upright, branching stems covered in fine, hairy texture. Its leaves are linear and slightly succulent, adapted to retain moisture.

This cultivar is prized for its vibrant blooms and is often used in gardens and floral arrangements.
