Draba simonkaiana

Draba simonkaiana, commonly known as the Simonka's rockcress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a small, perennial herb that typically grows in alpine and arctic regions. The plant has basal leaves that are usually linear or lanceolate in shape. Its flowers are small, white or pale yellow, and arranged in a cluster.

Draba simonkaiana is adapted to harsh environments, often found in rocky soils and high elevations.
