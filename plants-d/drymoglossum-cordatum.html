Drymoglossum cordatum

Drymoglossum cordatum, commonly known as the heart-shaped moss, is a species of liverwort belonging to the family Porellaceae.

It is characterized by its rosette-like growth form and distinctive heart-shaped leaves that are often reddish in color. Drymoglossum cordatum plays a vital role in soil stabilization and nutrient cycling in its ecosystem.
