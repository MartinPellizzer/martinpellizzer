Dichrotrichum triflorum

Dichrotrichum triflorum, commonly known as the three-flowered dichrotrichum, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers with yellow disk flowers and white ray flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has linear to lance-shaped leaves and produces seeds in a composite head.

Dichrotrichum triflorum is often found in temperate regions and is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils.
