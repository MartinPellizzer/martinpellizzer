Diospyros nigrescens

Diospyros nigrescens, commonly known as the black ebony tree, is a species of flowering plant in the Ebenaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Nigeria and Ghana. The tree is characterized by its dark, dense wood, which is highly valued for its strength and durability. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in panicles.

The fruit is a small, round drupe that turns from green to black as it matures.
