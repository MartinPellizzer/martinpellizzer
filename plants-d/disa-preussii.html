Disa preussii

Disa preussii, commonly known as the Cape goldcup, is a striking South African orchid species characterized by its vibrant yellow and orange flowers.

It is native to the Western Cape region and thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 cm, with a compact, bushy appearance. Its flowers are borne on tall, erect stems and are often clustered in dense inflorescences.

Disa preussii is a perennial herb that blooms in late winter to early spring, adding a splash of color to its natural habitat.
