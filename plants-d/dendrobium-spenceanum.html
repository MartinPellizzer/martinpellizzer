Dendrobium spenceanum

Dendrobium spenceanum, commonly known as the Spence's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically blooms in summer, producing pale yellow to greenish-white blossoms with a distinctive, sweet scent. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Dendrobium spenceanum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
