Dendrochilum unicallosum

Dendrochilum unicallosum, commonly known as the single-veined tree orchid, is a unique orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with a distinctive single vein on each petal. It thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and partial shade.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in specialized botanical gardens.
