Drakebrockmania

Drakebrockmania, commonly known as the dragon's breath flower, is a rare and exotic plant native to the misty highlands of the fictional realm of Drakemire.

It is characterized by its striking bioluminescent petals that glow in shades of blue and violet under moonlight. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires a unique symbiotic relationship with a specific species of fungal mycelium to flourish. Its roots are known to absorb minerals from the soil, contributing to the plant's unusual coloration and potency.

Due to its rarity and magical properties, Drakebrockmania is highly sought after by botanists and alchemists alike.
