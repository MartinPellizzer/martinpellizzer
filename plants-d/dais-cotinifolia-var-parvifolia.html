Dais cotinifolia var. parvifolia

Dais cotinifolia var. parvifolia, commonly known as the small-leaf smoke bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features delicate, feathery panicles that turn a striking crimson in the fall, making it a popular ornamental plant. The leaves are small, ovate, and have a slightly serrated edge, with a pale green upper surface and a silvery underside. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This plant is valued for its ornamental value and is often used in landscaping for its seasonal color changes.
