Dendrobium phalaenopsis var. highburyense

Dendrobium phalaenopsis var. highburyense, commonly known as the Highbury orchid, is a hybrid orchid species prized for its elegant, fragrant flowers.

It is a sympodial epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, where it typically grows on trees or rocks. The plant features long, cane-like stems that produce clusters of showy, semi-circular blooms in shades of white, pink, and yellow. Its foliage consists of long, narrow leaves that are arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
