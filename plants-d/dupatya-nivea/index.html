Dupatya Nivea

Dupatya nivea is a rare and striking plant known for its delicate white flowers and unique growth habits. Native to specific regions with cool, moist climates, it thrives in shaded environments. Its foliage is often adorned with intricate patterns that add to its visual appeal. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a species of conservation concern. Cultivating Dupatya nivea requires careful attention to soil and moisture conditions to ensure its survival.
