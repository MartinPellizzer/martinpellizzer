Dieffenbachia picta f. bausei

Dieffenbachia picta f. bausei, commonly known as the Bause's Picta Dieffenbachia, is a hybrid cultivar of the Dieffenbachia genus native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with a combination of white, green, and cream patterns on its broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant is a member of the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants such as peace lilies and calla lilies. Its leaves are typically dark green with bold, contrasting markings that make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

This cultivar thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to maintain its vibrant appearance.
