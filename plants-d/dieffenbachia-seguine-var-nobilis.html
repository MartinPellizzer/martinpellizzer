Dieffenbachia seguine var. nobilis

Dieffenbachia seguine var. nobilis, commonly known as the Leopard Plant, is a tropical perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, arrowhead-shaped leaves with striking white and green variegation. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions. Its foliage is often used in interior decorating due to its aesthetic appeal.

It requires well-draining soil and moderate watering to prevent root rot.
