Dipladenia gardneriana var. grandiflora

Dipladenia gardneriana var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered Dipladenia, is a flowering vine native to South America.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that range from yellow to orange in color. The plant produces trumpet-shaped blooms that are often borne in clusters, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets and trellises. Its leaves are heart-shaped and glossy, providing a striking contrast to its colorful flowers.

Dipladenia gardneriana var. grandiflora thrives in warm, sunny conditions and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
