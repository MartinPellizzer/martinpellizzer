Dioscorea tubiperianthia

Dioscorea tubiperianthia, commonly known as the tuberous-flowered yam, is a member of the Dioscoreaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its tuberous roots, which are often used in traditional medicine and as a food source. The plant typically grows as a climbing vine with heart-shaped leaves and small, greenish-white flowers. Its reproductive structures include both male and female flowers, which are often borne on separate plants.

The species is valued for its medicinal properties and has been studied for potential pharmaceutical applications.
