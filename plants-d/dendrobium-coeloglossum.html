Dendrobium coeloglossum

Dendrobium coeloglossum, commonly known as the "hairy lip orchid," is a sympodial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems that bear numerous lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange, often with a distinctive hairy lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrobium coeloglossum is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relative ease of care.
