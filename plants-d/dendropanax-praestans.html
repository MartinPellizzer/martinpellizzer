Dendropanax praestans

Dendropanax praestans, commonly known as the silverleaf arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive arrowhead-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green on top with a silvery underside. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
