Dianthus chinensis f. ignescens

Dianthus chinensis f. ignescens, commonly known as the flame flower or fire carnation, is a striking hybrid plant characterized by its vibrant, fiery red flowers.

It is a cultivar of the Chinese carnation, known for its dense, fluffy blooms that resemble small flames. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of about 15 to 30 centimeters. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves, and it thrives in full sun with well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements due to its intense color and compact growth habit.
