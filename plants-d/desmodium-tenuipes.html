Desmodium tenuipes

Desmodium tenuipes, commonly known as slender leaf desmodium, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its distinctive pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant features trifoliate leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, white or pale pink flowers. Its growth habit is typically low and sprawling, making it suitable for ground cover in shaded or moist environments.

Desmodium tenuipes is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in supporting pollinators.
