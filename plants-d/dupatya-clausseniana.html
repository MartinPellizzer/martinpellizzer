Dupatya clausseniana

Dupatya clausseniana, commonly known as the Clausen's daisy, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other composite flowers. The plant features showy, yellow-orange composite flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in well-drained, sunny soils.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
