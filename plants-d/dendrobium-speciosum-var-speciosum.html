Dendrobium speciosum var. speciosum

Dendrobium speciosum var. speciosum, commonly known as the orchid with the golden spike, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, cane-like pseudobulbs and clusters of showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to yellow and orange. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth. It is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.

The flowers typically bloom during the summer months, adding vibrant color to gardens and indoor spaces.
