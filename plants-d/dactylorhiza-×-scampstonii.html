Dactylorhiza × scampstonii

Dactylorhiza × scampstonii, commonly known as the Scampston orchid, is a hybrid orchid native to the British Isles.

It is a terrestrial herbaceous plant with a rhizomatous root system and produces tall, upright stems bearing numerous showy, violet to purple flowers. The plant typically blooms in late summer, from July to September, and is characterized by its intricate, spurred orchid flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded habitats such as woodland floors and is often found in calcareous soils.

Dactylorhiza × scampstonii is a popular ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy cultivation requirements.
