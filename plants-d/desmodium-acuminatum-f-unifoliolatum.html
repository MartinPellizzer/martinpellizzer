Desmodium acuminatum f. unifoliolatum

Desmodium acuminatum f. unifoliolatum, commonly known as the single leaf desmodium, is a variant of the species characterized by its unique leaf structure.

It features a single leaflet per leaf, distinguishing it from the typical trifoliate form of the species. This plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. Its small, pea-like flowers are usually pink or violet, and it is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in nitrogen fixation in agricultural systems.
