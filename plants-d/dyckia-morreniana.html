Dyckia morreniana

Dyckia morreniana, commonly known as the Morren's dyckia, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its rosette of long, narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of white, woolly hair. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers that emerge from the center of the rosette, adding a dramatic visual contrast. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Dyckia morreniana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
