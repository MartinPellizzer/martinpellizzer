Delphinium grandiflorum var. fangshanense

Delphinium grandiflorum var. fangshanense, commonly known as the Fangshan larkspur, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant produces showy, spurred flowers in shades of blue, purple, and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are palmately compound, with deeply lobed foliage that adds to its ornamental value.

The species is valued for its striking appearance and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
