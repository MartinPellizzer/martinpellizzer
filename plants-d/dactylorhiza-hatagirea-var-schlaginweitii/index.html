Dactylorhiza Hatagirea Var. Schlaginweitii

Hatagirea orchid, scientifically known as Dactylorhiza hatagirea var. schlaginweitii, is a rare and striking species native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas. This orchid is prized for its unique appearance, featuring tall stems and clusters of dark purple or violet flowers. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and forested areas. Due to its habitat specificity and limited distribution, it is considered an endangered plant. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this delicate and beautiful orchid from further decline.
