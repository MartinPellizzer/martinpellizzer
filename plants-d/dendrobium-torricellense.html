Dendrobium torricellense

Dendrobium torricellense, commonly known as the Torricella orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Dendrobium genus, which is known for its diverse array of flowering plants. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, using its aerial roots to absorb moisture and nutrients from the environment. Its flowers are usually colorful and fragrant, often appearing in clusters along the stems.

Dendrobium torricellense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
