Dioscorea schimperiana

Dioscorea schimperiana, commonly known as the African yambean, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its tuberous roots, which are the primary storage organs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers and has compound leaves with three to five leaflets. Its tubers are sometimes used in traditional medicine and as a food source.

The species is adapted to grow in humid, tropical environments with well-drained soil.
