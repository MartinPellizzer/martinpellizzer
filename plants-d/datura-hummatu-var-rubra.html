Datura hummatu var. rubra

Datura hummatu var. rubra, commonly known as the red devil's trumpet, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep red. The plant produces spiny, capsule-like fruits that contain toxic seeds. Native to certain regions of South America, it thrives in warm, sunny environments.

Due to its toxic properties, it is often considered a weed and requires caution when handling.
