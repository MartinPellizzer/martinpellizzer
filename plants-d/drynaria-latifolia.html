Drynaria latifolia

Drynaria latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaf tree fern, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of large, feather-like fronds. The plant belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Its rhizome is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
