Dodonaea uncinata

Dodonaea uncinata, commonly known as the velvet leaf, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its small, velvety leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in spikes, followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

Dodonaea uncinata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
