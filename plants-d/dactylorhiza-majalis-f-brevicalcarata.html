Dactylorhiza majalis f. brevicalcarata

Dactylorhiza majalis f. brevicalcarata, commonly known as the short-spurred orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and heathlands. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, and it produces a single spike of flowers with short, narrow petals.

The subspecies is distinguished by its shorter spur compared to the typical form of Dactylorhiza majalis.
