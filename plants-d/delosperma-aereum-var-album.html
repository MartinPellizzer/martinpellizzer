Delosperma aereum var. album

Delosperma aereum var. album, commonly known as the silverleaf ice plant, is a low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine, white powder, giving it a silvery appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and pale pink during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it ideal for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for water-wise gardening.
