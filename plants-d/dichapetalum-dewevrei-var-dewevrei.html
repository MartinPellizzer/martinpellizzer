Dichapetalum dewevrei var. dewevrei

Dichapetalum dewevrei var. dewevrei, commonly known as the African candlewood, is a deciduous tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, candle-like inflorescences that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to elliptical in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

The tree is valued for its ornamental value and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
