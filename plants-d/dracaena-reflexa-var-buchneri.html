Dracaena reflexa var. buchneri

Dracaena reflexa var. buchneri, commonly known as the Song of India or the Shrub Dracaena, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, narrow leaves that are typically dark green with a silvery-white margin. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters at the base of the stems. It is a hardy, evergreen shrub that thrives in well-drained soil and bright, indirect light.

Due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
