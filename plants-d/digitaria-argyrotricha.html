Digitaria argyrotricha

Digitaria argyrotricha, commonly known as silverleaf grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, silver-gray leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences containing numerous tiny florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open fields, roadsides, and disturbed areas.

Due to its adaptability, it is considered an invasive species in some regions.
