Danae

Danae, commonly known as the flame flower, is a tropical plant native to the Canary Islands and parts of Africa.

It is a member of the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

Danae is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.
