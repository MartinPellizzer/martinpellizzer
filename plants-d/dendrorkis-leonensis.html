Dendrorkis leonensis

Dendrorkis leonensis, commonly known as the Leonensis tree, is a species native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is a medium-sized evergreen tree that can reach heights of up to 20 meters. The leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptical, with a smooth margin and a glossy green surface. The flowers are small, white, and clustered in inflorescences, while the fruit is a woody capsule containing several seeds.

This plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has limited use in traditional medicine.
