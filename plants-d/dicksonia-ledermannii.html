Dicksonia ledermannii

Dicksonia ledermannii, commonly known as the Ledermann's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical and subtropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil. Its rhizome system supports the growth of its massive fronds, which can reach several meters in length.

Dicksonia ledermannii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
