Diosma leiophylla

Diosma leiophylla, commonly known as the smooth-leaf diosma, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant features slender, erect stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and glossy. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, arranged in clusters at the tips of the branches.

Diosma leiophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
