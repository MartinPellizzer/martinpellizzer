Dendrobium capitisyork

Dendrobium capitisyork, commonly known as the York head orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with multiple pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually clustered in racemes, displaying a range of colors from white to pink and purple.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
