Dieffenbachia gigantea

Dieffenbachia gigantea, commonly known as the giant dieffenbachia, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other popular houseplants like peace lilies. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its foliage is not only ornamental but also serves as a means of photosynthesis and gas exchange.
