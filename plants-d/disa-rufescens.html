Disa rufescens

Disa rufescens, commonly known as the red disa, is a species of orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red and purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers sunny to partially shaded environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming a rosette at the base.

Disa rufescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
