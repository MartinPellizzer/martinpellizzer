Dactylorhiza × kerneriorum nothosubsp. lillsundica

Dactylorhiza × kerneriorum nothosubsp.

lillsundica, commonly known as the Lillsundica orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Scandinavia. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lanceolate leaves.

The plant produces tall inflorescences with numerous flowers that are typically violet to purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded habitats such as bogs and fens, and requires specific conditions for successful growth.

Its botanical significance lies in its role as a hybrid species, showcasing the genetic diversity within the Dactylorhiza genus.
