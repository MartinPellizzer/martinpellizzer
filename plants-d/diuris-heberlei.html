Diuris heberlei

Diuris heberlei, commonly known as the Wimmera Spider-orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in sandy or loamy soils in open woodland or grassland habitats. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a single, narrow lance-shaped leaf. Its flowers are distinctive, with a greenish-yellow spathe and a pale yellow or cream-colored labellum.

Diuris heberlei is a low-growing plant that blooms in spring, producing a single or occasionally two flowers.
