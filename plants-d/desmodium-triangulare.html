Desmodium triangulare

Desmodium triangulare, commonly known as sicklepod, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with slender stems and trifoliate leaves. Its small, yellow flowers are followed by kidney-shaped pods that contain several seeds.

Desmodium triangulare is often found in disturbed soils and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
