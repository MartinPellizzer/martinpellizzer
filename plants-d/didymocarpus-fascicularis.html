Didymocarpus fascicularis

Didymocarpus fascicularis, commonly known as the clustered yew, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Theaceae and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant features leathery, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. Its flowers are small, greenish-yellow, and appear in clusters, giving it its common name.
