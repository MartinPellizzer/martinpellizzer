Drypetes zeylanica

Drypetes zeylanica, commonly known as the Java flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, bright red flowers that bloom in clusters, often giving it a fiery appearance. The plant has smooth, greyish bark and pinnate leaves with an odd number of leaflets. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in lowland forests.

The tree is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for its timber.
