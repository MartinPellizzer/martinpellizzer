Daviesia genistifolia

Daviesia genistifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Daviesia, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and small, yellow or brownish flowers.
