Daviesia ulicina f. angustifolia

Daviesia ulicina f. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed Daviesia, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, angular stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers clustered in racemes, which bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and open forests.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting native wildlife.
