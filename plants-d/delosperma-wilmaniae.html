Delosperma wilmaniae

Delosperma wilmaniae, commonly known as the Cape town ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant pink or red flowers during the summer months. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.

Delosperma wilmaniae is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping, making it a popular choice in drought-tolerant landscaping.
