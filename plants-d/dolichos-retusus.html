Dolichos retusus

Dolichos retusus, commonly known as the Indian cowpea, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, hairy stems that twine around supports as it grows. The plant produces small, yellow flowers followed by elongated, bean-like pods containing edible seeds. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three ovate to lanceolate leaflets.

Dolichos retusus is valued for its nutritional seeds and is often cultivated for both food and forage.
