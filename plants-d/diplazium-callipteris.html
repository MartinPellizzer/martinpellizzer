Diplazium callipteris

Diplazium callipteris, commonly known as the broad-leafed water fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its large, leathery fronds that are divided into pinnae with a distinctive shape. The fronds are typically evergreen, providing a lush appearance throughout the year.
