Ditrysinia ligustrina

Ditrysinia ligustrina, commonly known as the white-flowered pittosporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. Its fruit is a dark blue to black berry, often containing one to three seeds.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
