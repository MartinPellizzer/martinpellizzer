Deguelia chinensis

Deguelia chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese rain tree, is a deciduous leguminous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of fragrant, pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces long, narrow pods that contain flat, elliptical seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often used in ornamental landscaping.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it beneficial for soil enrichment.
