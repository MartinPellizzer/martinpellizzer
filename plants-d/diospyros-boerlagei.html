Diospyros boerlagei

Diospyros boerlagei, commonly known as the Boerlaag's ebony, is a small to medium-sized tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Ebenaceae family, which includes other ebony trees known for their dense, dark wood. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by small, round, yellow fruits.

The species is valued for its hard, heavy wood, which is used in the production of musical instruments and fine furniture.
