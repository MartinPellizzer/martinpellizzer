Deutzia × magnifica f. latiflora

Deutzia × magnifica f. latiflora, commonly known as the broad-flower deutzia, is a compact shrub native to China.

It features showy, fragrant clusters of white to pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that provide a striking contrast to its floral display. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, fertile soil.

This ornamental plant is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and its ability to attract pollinators.
