Darwiniothamnus tenuifolius var. glandulosus

Darwiniothamnus tenuifolius var. glandulosus, commonly known as the glandular darwiniothamnus, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, fleshy leaves that are often covered in small, glandular dots, which contribute to its unique appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the summer months, adding a delicate touch to its overall structure. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive addition to succulent collections and a subject of interest in botanical studies.
