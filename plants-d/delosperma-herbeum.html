Delosperma herbeum

Delosperma herbeum, commonly known as the ice plant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, enabling it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of pink, red, and yellow, typically during the warmer months. It is often used in rock gardens and as a ground cover due to its hardy nature and low maintenance requirements.

Delosperma herbeum is also valued for its ability to prevent soil erosion in dry landscapes.
