Deutzia parviflora f. pilosa

Deutzia parviflora f. pilosa, commonly known as the hairy deutzia, is a compact shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer, adding a delicate floral display. The plant has hairy foliage, which distinguishes it from other deutzia species. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
