Dictyopteris brongniartii

Dictyopteris brongniartii, commonly known as the giant kelp.

It is a large, brown alga that belongs to the family Dictyopteridaceae. This species is characterized by its long, ribbon-like fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The fronds are typically divided into segments with a central midrib and lateral branches.

It thrives in cool, nutrient-rich coastal waters and plays an important role in marine ecosystems.
