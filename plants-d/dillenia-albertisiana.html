Dillenia albertisiana

Dillenia albertisiana, commonly known as the Albertisian dillenia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dilleniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, palmately lobed leaves. The plant produces showy, white or pink flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are fleshy berries that contain numerous seeds.

Dillenia albertisiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
