Dactylorchis Majalis Var. Linguata

Dactylorchis majalis var. linguata is a rare and delicate orchid native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia. This orchid is known for its unique, tongue-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring. It thrives in cool, humid environments with well-drained soil and partial shade. Due to its limited habitat and slow growth, it is considered endangered in many areas. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival in the wild.
