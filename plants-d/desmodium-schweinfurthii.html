Desmodium schweinfurthii

Desmodium schweinfurthii, commonly known as African velvet bean, is a leguminous plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in disturbed soils.

Desmodium schweinfurthii is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in agricultural systems.
