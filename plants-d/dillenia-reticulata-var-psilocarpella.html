Dillenia reticulata var. psilocarpella

Dillenia reticulata var. psilocarpella, commonly known as the reticulated dillenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves with a distinctive reticulated pattern. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in umbels, and it is known for its ornamental value. Its fruit is a fleshy, spiny capsule that contains numerous seeds.

While not psychoactive, it is closely related to species that contain hallucinogenic compounds.
