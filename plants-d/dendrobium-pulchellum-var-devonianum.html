Dendrobium pulchellum var. devonianum

Dendrobium pulchellum var. devonianum, commonly known as the Devonian orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a thick, fleshy rhizome and produces racemes of fragrant, showy flowers. The flowers are typically white to pale yellow with red or orange markings, and they bloom during the rainy season. This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded, moist environments.

It requires well-drained soil and moderate light to thrive.
