Dieffenbachia Macrophylla Var. Obliqua

Dieffenbachia macrophylla var. obliqua is a strikingly beautiful houseplant known for its vibrant, textured foliage. This variant of the dieffenbachia species features elongated leaves that are often twisted or angled, giving it a unique and eye-catching appearance. Native to the tropical regions of South America, it thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light. Its ability to purify indoor air makes it a popular choice for both ornamental and functional purposes. With proper care, this plant can flourish and add a lush, tropical feel to any indoor space.
