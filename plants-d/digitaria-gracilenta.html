Digitaria gracilenta

Digitaria gracilenta, commonly known as slender digit grass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a perennial plant characterized by its slender, wiry stems and small, narrow leaves. The plant produces slender spikelets that are typically arranged in dense clusters along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open fields, pastures, and disturbed sites.

Digitaria gracilenta is valued for its use in forage and as a component in ecological restoration projects.
