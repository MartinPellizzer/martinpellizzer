Dieffenbachia picta subvar. magnifica

Dieffenbachia picta subvar. magnifica, commonly known as the Giant Dieffenbachia, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its foliage is known for its striking coloration and can grow up to several feet in height.

It is a popular houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
