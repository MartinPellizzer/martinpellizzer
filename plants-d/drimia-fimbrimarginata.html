Drimia fimbrimarginata

Drimia fimbrimarginata, commonly known as the Cape tulip, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, typically blooming in spring. The plant has strap-like, grassy leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. It grows from a bulbous rootstock and is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils.

Drimia fimbrimarginata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
