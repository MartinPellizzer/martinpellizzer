Deutzia monbeigii var. lanceolata

Deutzia monbeigii var. lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceleaf deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features slender, arching stems that bear clusters of white, bell-shaped flowers in early summer. The leaves are lanceolate, with a smooth, dark green upper surface and a lighter, hairy underside. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for ornamental gardens.

This variety is prized for its delicate floral display and adaptability to various garden conditions.
