Deschampsia cespitosa var. orientalis

Deschampsia cespitosa var. orientalis, commonly known as oriental feathergrass, is a grass species native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-blue foliage and tufted growth habit, forming dense, low-growing clumps. The plant produces delicate, feathery seed heads that are particularly noticeable in late summer and autumn. It thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in supporting local wildlife.
