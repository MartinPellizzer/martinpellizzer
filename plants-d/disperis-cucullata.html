Disperis cucullata

Disperis cucullata, commonly known as the helmet orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, helmet-shaped flower. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow, with a distinctive hood-like structure over the reproductive organs.

Disperis cucullata is often found in grassland habitats and is adapted to seasonal rainfall patterns.
