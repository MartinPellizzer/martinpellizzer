Dendrobium nobile var. nobilius

Dendrobium nobile var. nobilius, commonly known as the noble orchid, is a sympodial epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a pseudobulbous structure with thick, fleshy stems that store water and nutrients. The plant produces racemes of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
