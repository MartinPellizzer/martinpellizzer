Dryandra speciosa

Dryandra speciosa, commonly known as the golden wattle, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. Dryandra speciosa is well adapted to the dry, sandy soils of its native habitat.
