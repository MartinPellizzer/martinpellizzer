Daphniphyllum glaucescens var. taiwanianum

Daphniphyllum glaucescens var. taiwanianum, commonly known as the Taiwan holly, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Taiwan.

It features glossy, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and pale underneath, giving it a striking visual contrast. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters during late winter to early spring. Its foliage remains vibrant throughout the year, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, requiring moderate watering to maintain its health.
