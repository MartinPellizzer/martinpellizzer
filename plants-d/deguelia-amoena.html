Deguelia amoena

Deguelia amoena, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery red flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, serrated leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular ornamental species.

Its rapid growth and vibrant appearance make it a favored choice for landscaping in tropical and subtropical regions.
