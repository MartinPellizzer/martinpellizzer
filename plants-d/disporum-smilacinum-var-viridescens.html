Disporum smilacinum var. viridescens

Disporum smilacinum var. viridescens, commonly known as the greenleaf disporum, is a variant of the disporum species native to the western United States.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green. The plant produces small, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks.

Its foliage and floral structure make it an attractive addition to native plant gardens.
