Delphinium speciosum var. caucasicum

Delphinium speciosum var. caucasicum, commonly known as the Caucasian larkspur, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus.

It features tall, upright stems that bear clusters of striking blue to purple flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
