Dahlia tenuicaulis

Dahlia tenuicaulis, commonly known as the slender-stemmed dahlia, is a herbaceous perennial native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and showy, single or double flowers in a variety of colors. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden beds.

Its botanical features make it an attractive addition to both ornamental gardens and botanical collections.
