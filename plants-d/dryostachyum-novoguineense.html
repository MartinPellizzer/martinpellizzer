Dryostachyum novoguineense

Dryostachyum novoguineense, commonly known as the New Guinea grass, is a tropical perennial grass native to the island of New Guinea.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland rainforest areas.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
