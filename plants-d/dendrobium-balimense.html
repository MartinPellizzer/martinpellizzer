Dendrobium balimense

Dendrobium balimense, commonly known as the Balim Island orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its clustered, cane-like pseudobulbs and long, linear leaves. The plant produces numerous fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple during the flowering season. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Dendrobium balimense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
