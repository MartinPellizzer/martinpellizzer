Dodecatheon reflexum

Dodecatheon reflexum, commonly known as the reflexed globemallow, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern North America.

It features showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring, typically from May to June. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture and grows to a height of about 1 to 3 feet. Its flowers are arranged in clusters at the top of the stems, and it prefers moist, woodland habitats.

Dodecatheon reflexum is known for its ornamental value and is often found in shaded or partially shaded environments.
