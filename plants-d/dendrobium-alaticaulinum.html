Dendrobium alaticaulinum

Dendrobium alaticaulinum, commonly known as the long-stemmed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with multiple stems that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with distinctive, elongated petals. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

This species thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
