Delonix floribunda

Delonix floribunda, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical leguminous tree native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, bright orange to red flowers that bloom in dense, panicle-like clusters. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with small, ovate leaflets and produces long, flat pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often used in ornamental landscaping due to its showy floral display.

The tree is also valued for its rapid growth and ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.
