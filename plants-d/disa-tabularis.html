Disa tabularis

Disa tabularis, commonly known as the flat-leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, flat leaves and striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and requires a sunny to partially shaded location. Its flowers come in shades of pink, purple, and white, often with a contrasting lip.

Disa tabularis is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both gardeners and collectors.
