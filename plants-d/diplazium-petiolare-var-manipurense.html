Diplazium petiolare var. manipurense

Diplazium petiolare var. manipurense, commonly known as the Manipur sword fern, is a variant of the sword fern species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial evergreen fern characterized by its slender, upright rhizomes and fronds that are typically bipinnate. The fronds are dark green, with a leathery texture and are often used in traditional medicine and as ornamental plants. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

The plant has a moderate growth rate and is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable conditions.
