Dracontium prancei

Dracontium prancei, commonly known as the Prance's arrowleaf, is a herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, Dracontium prancei is often cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
