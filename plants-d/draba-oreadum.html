Draba oreadum

Draba oreadum, commonly known as the alpine mouse-ear cress, is a small flowering plant native to mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils and cold climates. It produces small, white or yellow flowers that bloom in early spring.

Draba oreadum is known for its hardy nature and is often found in high-altitude meadows and scree slopes.
