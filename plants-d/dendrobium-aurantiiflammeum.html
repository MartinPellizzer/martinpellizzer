Dendrobium aurantiiflammeum

Dendrobium aurantiiflammeum, commonly known as the flame orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, cane-like stems that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces clusters of vibrant orange-red flowers with yellow markings, which bloom during the summer months. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and well-draining soil.

Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for both ornamental gardening and horticultural collections.
