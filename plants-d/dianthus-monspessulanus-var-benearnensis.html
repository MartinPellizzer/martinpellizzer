Dianthus monspessulanus var. benearnensis

Dianthus monspessulanus var. benearnensis, commonly known as the Benearn carnation, is a compact, flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features dense, low-growing foliage with bright pink to red flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces showy, double-petaled flowers with a distinctive, frilled edge. It is well-suited for rock gardens and borders due to its hardiness and ornamental value.

The variety is prized for its vibrant color and ability to thrive in well-drained soil.
