Dendrobium lancilobum var. roseocalcar

Dendrobium lancilobum var. roseocalcar, commonly known as the rose-spurred orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic orchid that grows on trees and rocks, often in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant features long, cane-like stems with clusters of fragrant, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. Its flowers have distinctive red or pink calli on the petals, giving it its varietal name.

It requires bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
