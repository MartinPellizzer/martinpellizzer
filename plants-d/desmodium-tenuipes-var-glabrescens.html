Desmodium tenuipes var. glabrescens

Desmodium tenuipes var. glabrescens, commonly known as the slender leaf desmodium, is a leguminous plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and slender stems. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.

This species is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in ecological restoration and sustainable agriculture.
