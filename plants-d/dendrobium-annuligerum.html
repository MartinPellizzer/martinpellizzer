Dendrobium annuligerum

Dendrobium annuligerum, commonly known as the ringed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth. Its flowers bloom in late winter to early spring, adding ornamental value to its natural habitat.

Dendrobium annuligerum is valued for its unique appearance and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
