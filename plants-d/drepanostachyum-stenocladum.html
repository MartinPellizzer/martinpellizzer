Drepanostachyum stenocladum

Drepanostachyum stenocladum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bamboo, is a member of the grass family (Poaceae).

It is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. Native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with spikelets that are often pale green to golden in color.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual interest and adaptability.
