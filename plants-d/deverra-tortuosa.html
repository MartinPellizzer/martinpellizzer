Deverra tortuosa

Deverra tortuosa, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, twisting stems that give it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in clusters along the stems. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Deverra tortuosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
