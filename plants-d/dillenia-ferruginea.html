Dillenia ferruginea

Dillenia ferruginea, commonly known as the rusty dillenia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dilleniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, palmate leaves with serrated margins. The plant produces large, showy flowers that are typically pink or red, with five to seven petals. Its fruits are woody follicles that split open to release seeds, and the plant is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Dillenia ferruginea is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
