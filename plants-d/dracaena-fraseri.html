Dracaena fraseri

Dracaena fraseri, commonly known as the Janet Craig dracaena, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features upright, dark green leaves with a slight sheen and a narrow, lance-shaped form. The plant is characterized by its ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its slow growth rate and minimal maintenance requirements contribute to its popularity as a houseplant.

Dracaena fraseri is also known for its air-purifying qualities, enhancing indoor air quality.
