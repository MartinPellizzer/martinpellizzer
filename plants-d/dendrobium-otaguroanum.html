Dendrobium otaguroanum

Dendrobium otaguroanum, commonly known as the Otago orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its erect, leafy stems and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically blooms in spring and is adapted to grow in shaded, moist environments. Its flowers are clustered in racemes and have a delicate, elegant appearance.

Dendrobium otaguroanum is a rare species, found primarily in the Otago region of the South Island.
