Dysosma mairei

Dysosma mairei, commonly known as the Chinese climbing fern, is a member of the Aristolochiaceae family.

It is a perennial vine characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and unique, tubular flowers that resemble a pipe. The plant is native to China and is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its flowers are typically greenish-yellow with a strong, aromatic scent.

Dysosma mairei is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.
