Disa rhodantha

Disa rhodantha, commonly known as the red disa, is a striking South African orchid species belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant red petals and sepals, which are often adorned with intricate patterns and contrasting markings. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in well-drained environments. It is native to regions such as the Western Cape and Eastern Cape provinces of South Africa.

Disa rhodantha is a herbaceous perennial that produces its flowers during the summer months, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
