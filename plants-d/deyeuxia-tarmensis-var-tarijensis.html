Deyeuxia tarmensis var. tarijensis

Deyeuxia tarmensis var. tarijensis, commonly known as the Tarijensis false wheat, is a grass species native to South America, particularly found in regions of Bolivia and Peru.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as grasslands and wetlands. The plant has slender, erect stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
