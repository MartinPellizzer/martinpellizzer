Dolichos obliquifolius

Dolichos obliquifolius, commonly known as the zigzag bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, twining stems and trifoliate leaves with deeply lobed leaflets. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that are followed by elongated, curved pods containing kidney-shaped seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value and edible seeds, it is cultivated in some regions for both horticultural and agricultural purposes.
