Drimys piperita

Drimys piperita, commonly known as the Chilean pepper tree, is a species of flowering plant native to southern South America.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows in temperate rainforests. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. Its fruit is a red, fleshy drupe that contains a single seed.
