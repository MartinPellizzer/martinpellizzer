Deutzia pulchra var. bartlettii

Deutzia pulchra var. bartlettii, commonly known as Bartlett’s deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to the western United States.

It features showy, flat-topped clusters of white to pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, arching stems and dark green, ovate leaves that provide attractive foliage throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

Bartlett’s deutzia is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
