Dioscorea kerrii

Dioscorea kerrii, commonly known as African yam bean, is a climbing vine belonging to the Dioscoreaceae family.

It is native to tropical Africa and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and yellowish flowers. The plant produces tuberous roots that are used as a food source in some regions. These tubers are rich in starch and have a slightly sweet flavor.

Dioscorea kerrii is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for its unique appearance.
