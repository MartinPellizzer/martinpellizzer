Dortmanna montana

Dortmanna montana, commonly known as the mountain Dortmanna, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, where it thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight. The plant features slender, upright stems with small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine pubescence. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense inflorescences, usually appearing in shades of white or pale yellow.

Dortmanna montana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in rock gardens or alpine settings.
