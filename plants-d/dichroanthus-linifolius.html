Dichroanthus linifolius

Dichroanthus linifolius, commonly known as the slenderleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. It has a taproot system and produces seeds with feathery pappi for wind dispersal.

Due to its ecological role and ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
