Disperis katangensis

Disperis katangensis, commonly known as the Katanga disperis, is a flowering plant native to the Democratic Republic of the Congo.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae, which includes a wide variety of orchids. This species is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, pale green to white flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in forest understories.
