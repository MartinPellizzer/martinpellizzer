Desmonema oblongifolium

Desmonema oblongifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leafed daisy.

It is a flowering plant native to Australia, characterized by its slender, elongated leaves and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, thriving in full sun. Its flowers bloom from spring to autumn, attracting various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Desmonema oblongifolium is often used in native gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
