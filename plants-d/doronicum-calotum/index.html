Doronicum Calotum

Doronicum calotum is a striking alpine plant known for its vibrant yellow flowers and compact growth habit. Native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe, it thrives in well-drained soils and cool climates. This species is often found in rocky habitats and open meadows where it adds a splash of color to the landscape. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice among gardeners who appreciate its ornamental value. Despite its beauty, Doronicum calotum is relatively rare in cultivation, making it a sought-after plant for botanical enthusiasts.
