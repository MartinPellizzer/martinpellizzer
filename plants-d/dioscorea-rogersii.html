Dioscorea rogersii

Dioscorea rogersii, commonly known as the yellow yam, is a climbing vine native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the genus Dioscorea, which includes various species of yams and wild yams. The plant produces small, yellow flowers and heart-shaped leaves, and it is often found in moist, wooded areas. Its tuberous roots are not typically cultivated for food but are of interest in botanical studies.

The species is also valued for its ornamental qualities in gardens.
