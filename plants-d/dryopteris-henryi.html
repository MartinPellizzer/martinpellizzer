Dryopteris henryi

Dryopteris henryi, commonly known as the Henry's wood fern, is a species of fern native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are often covered in a dense layer of brown, hairy scales.

Dryopteris henryi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic foliage.
