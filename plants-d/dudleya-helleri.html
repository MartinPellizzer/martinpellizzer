Dudleya helleri

Dudleya helleri, commonly known as the Heller's liveforever, is a succulent plant native to California.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are typically blue-gray in color. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, or white during the spring. It is adapted to rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun.

Dudleya helleri is drought-tolerant and requires minimal water once established.
