Dilkea ovalis

Dilkea ovalis, commonly known as the oval leaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its oval-shaped leaves are a distinguishing feature, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
