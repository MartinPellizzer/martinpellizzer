Dracaena hemichrysa

Dracaena hemichrysa, commonly known as the golden dracaena, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its stiff, upright leaves that are typically bright yellow to golden green in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by red berries, which are edible but not commonly consumed by humans. Dracaena hemichrysa thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

It is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
