Daemonorops fasciculata

Daemonorops fasciculata, commonly known as the clustered palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and pinnate leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that bear white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.

The clustered palm is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic qualities.
