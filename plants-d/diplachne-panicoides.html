Diplachne panicoides

Diplachne panicoides, commonly known as flatgrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its flat, rhizomatous growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are typically arranged in panicles. Diplachne panicoides is adapted to a variety of soil types but thrives in well-drained conditions.

It is often found in grasslands and along roadsides, contributing to the ecological diversity of its habitat.
