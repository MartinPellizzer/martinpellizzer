Dendrobium serena-alexianum

Dendrobium serena-alexianum, commonly known as the Serene Alexian Orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic, typically in tropical rainforest environments. The plant produces strikingly colorful flowers, often in shades of white, pink, and red, which bloom during the dry season. Its inflorescence is usually a raceme, with multiple flowers emerging from a single stem.

The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
