Dentaria petersiana

Dentaria petersiana, commonly known as the Peters' toothplant, is a perennial herb native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the family Brassicaceae, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant features basal leaves that are broadly ovate with a toothed margin, giving it its common name. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers in clusters, typically blooming in spring.

The plant is adapted to moist, sandy soils and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks.
