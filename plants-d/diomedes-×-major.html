Diomedes × major

Diomedes × major, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a cross between Diomedes albus and Diomedes grandiflorus.

It is a herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant produces large, showy white or pale pink flowers with a strong, sweet fragrance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
