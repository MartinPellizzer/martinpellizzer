Dolichos maitlandii

Dolichos maitlandii, commonly known as the Maitland bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical Africa.

It produces elongated, bean-like pods that contain several edible seeds. The plant is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed habitats.

The seeds are rich in protein and have been used as a food source in some regions.
