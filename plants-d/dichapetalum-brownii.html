Dichapetalum brownii

Dichapetalum brownii, commonly known as the African orchid tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, orchid-like flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

The tree is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic beauty.
