Delphinium hesperium var. seditiosum

Delphinium hesperium var. seditiosum, commonly known as the western larkspur, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of blue to purple flowers. It is considered a perennial herb and is often found in meadows and open woodlands.
