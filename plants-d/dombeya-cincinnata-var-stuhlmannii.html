Dombeya cincinnata var. stuhlmannii

Dombeya cincinnata var. stuhlmannii, commonly known as the Stuhlmann's dombeya, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white, arranged in clusters at the ends of its branches. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually oval or elliptical in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens in suitable climates.
