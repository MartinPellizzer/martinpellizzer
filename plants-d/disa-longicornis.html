Disa longicornis

Disa longicornis, commonly known as the longhorn disa, is a species of orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its inflorescence consists of several clustered flowers, each with a distinctive, elongated lip.

Disa longicornis is a popular ornamental plant due to its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
