Dendrobenthamia elliptica

Dendrobenthamia elliptica, commonly known as the elliptic orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect stem and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces raceme-like inflorescences with showy, greenish-yellow flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and valuable species in the orchid family.
