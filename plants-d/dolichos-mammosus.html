Dolichos mammosus

Dolichos mammosus, commonly known as the cowpea or yardlong bean, is a flowering plant in the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its long, slender pods that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, yellow flowers and has trifoliate leaves composed of three leaflets. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions and is often cultivated for its edible seeds.

The plant also has a climbing habit and is used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
