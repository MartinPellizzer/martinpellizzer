Dendrobium latoureoides

Dendrobium latoureoides, commonly known as the Philippine orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and linear, lanceolate leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive three-lobed lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Dendrobium latoureoides is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
