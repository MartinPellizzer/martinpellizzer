Dichrotrichum asperifolium

Dichrotrichum asperifolium, commonly known as the roughleaf dichrotrichum, is a species of flowering plant in the family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its rough, serrated leaves and compact growth habit. Native to certain regions of South America, it thrives in moist, temperate climates. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale to brownish flowers.
