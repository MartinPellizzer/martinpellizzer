Dolianthus vaccinioides

Dolianthus vaccinioides, commonly known as the purple coneflower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, violet to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Dolianthus vaccinioides is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
