Dactylorhiza × sooi

Dactylorhiza × sooi, commonly known as the Sooi orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically blooms in late summer or early autumn, producing spikes of white to pale pink flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil for optimal growth.

Dactylorhiza × sooi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
