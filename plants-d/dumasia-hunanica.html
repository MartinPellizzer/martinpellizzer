Dumasia hunanica

Dumasia hunanica, commonly known as the Hunan bellflower, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are lanceolate and arranged alternately along the stem.

Dumasia hunanica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
