Dendrochilum celebesense

Dendrochilum celebesense, commonly known as the Celebes orchid, is a tropical orchid native to the Maluku Islands of Indonesia.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for greenhouse or indoor cultivation. Its unique floral structure and ornamental value make it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.

Dendrochilum celebesense is also noted for its ability to produce pseudobulbs, which store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in its native habitat.
