Dianthera sulcata

Dianthera sulcata, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, zigzag-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and purple, typically blooming during the summer months. Dianthera sulcata thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires indirect sunlight to flourish.

Its unique leaf structure and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
