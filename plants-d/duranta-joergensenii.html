Duranta joergensenii

Duranta joergensenii, commonly known as the silverleaf duranta, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 3 to 10 feet. The plant produces clusters of tubular, blue or purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are narrow, lance-shaped, and often have a silvery or grayish underside, giving it its common name.

Duranta joergensenii is adapted to a wide range of climates and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.
