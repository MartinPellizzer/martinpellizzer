Dombeya gillettii

Dombeya gillettii, commonly known as the silky dombeya, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has smooth, gray-green leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal gardens.

Dombeya gillettii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
