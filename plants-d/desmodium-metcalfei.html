Desmodium metcalfei

Desmodium metcalfei, commonly known as the Indian velvet bean, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant produces cylindrical pods that contain several elliptical seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

Desmodium metcalfei is also valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it useful in agricultural systems.
