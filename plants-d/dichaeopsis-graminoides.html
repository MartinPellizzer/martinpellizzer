Dichaeopsis graminoides

Dichaeopsis graminoides, commonly known as the narrowleaf dichaeopsis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, white to pale blue flowers. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops.
