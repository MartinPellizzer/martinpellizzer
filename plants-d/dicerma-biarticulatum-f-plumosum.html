Dicerma biarticulatum f. plumosum

Dicerma biarticulatum f. plumosum, commonly known as the plumose dicerma, is a unique fern species characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after plant for botanical gardens and specialty collections.
