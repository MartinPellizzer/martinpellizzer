Dithecina ligustrina

Dithecina ligustrina, commonly known as the narrowleaf privet, is a species of flowering plant in the Oleaceae family.

It is characterized by its opposite, narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. This plant is often cultivated as an ornamental shrub due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various soil types.
