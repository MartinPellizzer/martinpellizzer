Desmodium prainii var. glabrescens

Desmodium prainii var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth desmodium, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which are typically smooth and glabrous, giving it its varietal name. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, and it is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. It is a perennial herb that thrives in well-drained soils and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

The species is also valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, which benefits soil fertility in agricultural settings.
