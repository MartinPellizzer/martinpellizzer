Danthonia capensis

Danthonia capensis, commonly known as the Cape danthonia, is a perennial grass native to South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant is adapted to rocky and sandy soils, often found in coastal and mountainous regions. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun.

Its ecological importance includes providing habitat and food for various insects and small animals.
