Diplocaulobium stelliferum

Diplocaulobium stelliferum, commonly known as the star-bearing water lily, is a species of aquatic plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, floating leaves that are often adorned with small, star-like structures along the edges. The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater habitats, such as ponds and lakes. Its flowers are typically small and white, emerging from the water surface during the day.

The botanical features of Diplocaulobium stelliferum make it an important species for aquatic ecosystems, providing habitat and food for various water organisms.
