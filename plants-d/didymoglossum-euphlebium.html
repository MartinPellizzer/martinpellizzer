Didymoglossum euphlebium

Didymoglossum euphlebium, commonly known as the small-leaved moss, is a species of liverwort belonging to the family Peltasporaceae.

Didymoglossum euphlebium is often found in humid environments and plays a role in soil stabilization and microhabitat creation.
