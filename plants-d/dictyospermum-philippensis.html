Dictyospermum philippensis

Dictyospermum philippensis, commonly known as the Philippine star anise, is a tropical shrub native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its aromatic, evergreen leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, star-shaped fruits that resemble those of the star anise, though it is not related to the Chinese star anise. Its botanical features include glandular hairs on the stems and leaves, and it often grows in moist, shaded environments.

The species is valued for its essential oils and has traditional uses in local medicine.
