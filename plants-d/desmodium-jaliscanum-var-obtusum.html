Desmodium jaliscanum var. obtusum

Desmodium jaliscanum var. obtusum, commonly known as Mexican burr legume, is a perennial legume native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, and it is often found in disturbed soils and open areas. It has been used traditionally for its medicinal properties and as a forage crop.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it valuable in soil enrichment.
