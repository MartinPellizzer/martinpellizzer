Dracocephalum oligadenium

Dracocephalum oligadenium, commonly known as blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant features slender, erect stems and small, blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Dracocephalum oligadenium is valued for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
