Deianira erubescens var. alba

Deianira erubescens var. alba, commonly known as the white-flowered deianira, is a variant of the deianira erubescens species, characterized by its striking white petals.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is native to tropical regions of South America. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
