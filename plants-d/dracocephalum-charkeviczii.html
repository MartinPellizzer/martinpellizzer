Dracocephalum charkeviczii

Dracocephalum charkeviczii, commonly known as Persian feverfew, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils at high altitudes.

Dracocephalum charkeviczii is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
