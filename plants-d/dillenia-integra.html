Dillenia integra

Dillenia integra, commonly known as the giant dillenia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers with red veins, which are followed by distinctive, spiny fruits. Its growth habit includes a woody stem with a thick, corky bark.

Dillenia integra is often found in moist, shaded areas of tropical forests.
