Deutzia pulchra

Deutzia pulchra, commonly known as the showy deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia, particularly China.

It features clusters of fragrant, white to pink flowers that bloom in early summer, adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant has slender, arching branches and small, dark green leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a versatile choice for landscape design.

Deutzia pulchra is valued for its ornamental flowers and adaptability to various garden conditions.
