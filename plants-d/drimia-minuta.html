Drimia minuta

Drimia minuta, commonly known as the little rain lily, is a small, herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It produces delicate, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late summer or early autumn. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in moist, shaded environments. It is typically grown as a ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.

Drimia minuta is also known for its ability to thrive in containers, making it a popular choice for gardeners.
