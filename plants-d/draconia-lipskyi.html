Draconia lipskyi

Draconia lipskyi, commonly known as the Dragon's Blood Tree, is a unique plant species native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its tall, tree-like structure with a thick, gnarled trunk and a crown of dense, feathery leaves. The plant produces small, red flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by bright red, fleshy fruits. Its sap is known for its deep red color and has been used traditionally in medicine and as a dye.

Draconia lipskyi thrives in arid environments and is adapted to withstand drought and high temperatures.
