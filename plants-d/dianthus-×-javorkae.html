Dianthus × javorkae

Dianthus × javorkae, commonly known as the double carnation, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, double-petaled flowers that come in a wide range of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically grows to a height of 15–30 cm and has a compact, bushy habit. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern.

Dianthus × javorkae is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and floral arrangements.
