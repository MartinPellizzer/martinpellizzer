Duranta repens f. acuta

Duranta repens f. acuta, commonly known as the sharpleaf duranta, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It features small, dark green, ovate leaves with serrated edges, giving it its "sharpleaf" designation. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to violet, blooming throughout the growing season. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in partial shade to full sun.

This variety is particularly valued for its adaptability and resilience in various soil types.
