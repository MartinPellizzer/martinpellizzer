Dactylorhiza majalis var. cambrensis

Dactylorhiza majalis var. cambrensis, commonly known as the Welsh marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the United Kingdom.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces spikes of pale yellow to greenish-yellow flowers with a distinct hooded lip. It thrives in damp, calcareous soils in wetlands and marshes.

This variety is notable for its ecological significance and is protected in many areas due to habitat loss.
