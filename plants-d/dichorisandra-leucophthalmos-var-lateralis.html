Dichorisandra leucophthalmos var. lateralis

Dichorisandra leucophthalmos var. lateralis, commonly known as the white-eyed pittosporum, is a compact evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter in color underneath. The plant produces showy, fragrant white flowers with yellow centers, which bloom in spring. It is characterized by its dense growth habit and ability to thrive in well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and its adaptability to various growing conditions.
