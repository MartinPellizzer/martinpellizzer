Dolichos erosus

Dolichos erosus, commonly known as the wild cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into elongated pods containing edible seeds. Dolichos erosus is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in various environmental conditions.
