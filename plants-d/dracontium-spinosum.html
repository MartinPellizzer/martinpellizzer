Dracontium spinosum

Dracontium spinosum, commonly known as the spiny arrowleaf, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves with prominent veins. The plant produces striking, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe. Native to tropical regions of South America, it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
