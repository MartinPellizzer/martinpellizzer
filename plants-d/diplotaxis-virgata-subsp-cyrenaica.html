Diplotaxis virgata subsp. cyrenaica

Diplotaxis virgata subsp.

cyrenaica, commonly known as the Cyrenaica wallflower, is a flowering plant native to North Africa and the Middle East. It is a low-growing annual herb with erect, branched stems and bright yellow flowers.

The plant has pinnately divided leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils and along coastal areas.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to drought conditions and poor soil nutrients.
