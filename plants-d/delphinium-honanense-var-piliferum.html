Delphinium honanense var. piliferum

Delphinium honanense var. piliferum, commonly known as the Honan larkspur, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters. The plant produces showy blue to purple flowers in racemes, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a striking addition to gardens in suitable climates.
