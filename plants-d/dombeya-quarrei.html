Dombeya quarrei

Dombeya quarrei, commonly known as the mountain dombeya, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and are typically borne on long, slender stalks. The plant has simple, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Dombeya quarrei is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.
