Deutzia rehderiana

Deutzia rehderiana, commonly known as the Rehder's deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, arching stems and lance-shaped leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Deutzia rehderiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its showy blooms and compact growth habit.
