Dryadella perpusilla

Dryadella perpusilla, commonly known as the small dryad.

It is a small, herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are typically less than 1 inch in length. It produces tiny, white to pale green flowers in early spring.

Dryadella perpusilla is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as along stream banks and in forest understories.
