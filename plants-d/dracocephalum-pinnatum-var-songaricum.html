Dracocephalum pinnatum var. songaricum

Dracocephalum pinnatum var. songaricum, commonly known as the Songaric Dracocephalum, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small, clustered in whorls, and typically have a pale blue to violet color.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
