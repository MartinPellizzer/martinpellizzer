Dahlia hjertingii

Dahlia hjertingii, commonly known as the Hjertingii dahlia, is a tuberous-rooted perennial plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, single or double flowers in a variety of colors, including red, yellow, and orange. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and features dark green, heart-shaped leaves. Its tuberous roots are essential for storage and propagation, and the plant thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.

Dahlia hjertingii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
