Darwinia oldfieldii

Darwinia oldfieldii, commonly known as the Oldfield's spikeheath, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy spikes of pink to red flowers. The plant typically grows in heathland and coastal environments, preferring well-drained soils. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in spring.
