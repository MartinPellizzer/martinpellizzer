Delphinium nuristanicum

Delphinium nuristanicum, commonly known as the Nuristan larkspur, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Nuristan in Afghanistan.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems and clusters of blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine environments, thriving in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are palmately divided, and the flowers are usually arranged in racemes, adding to its ornamental value.

Due to its restricted habitat, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species.
