Dryopteris batacorum var. winkleri

Dryopteris batacorum var. winkleri, commonly known as the Winkler's wood fern, is a variant of the broad-leaf wood fern.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, particularly in countries like Vietnam and Laos. The plant features a tall, slender rhizome with large, bipinnate fronds that are often arching in appearance. Its leaves are typically dark green and have a leathery texture, with prominent veins.

This fern thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
