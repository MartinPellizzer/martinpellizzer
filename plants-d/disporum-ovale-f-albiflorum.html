Disporum ovale f. albiflorum

Disporum ovale f. albiflorum, commonly known as the whiteflower deathcamise, is a variant of the deathcamise plant characterized by its distinctive white flowers.

It is a herbaceous perennial native to the western United States, particularly in California and Oregon. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy, clustered white blooms that appear in late spring. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

The botanical name reflects its parent species and the unique floral coloration of this form.
