Diphryllum Cordatum

Diphryllum cordatum is a rare and enigmatic plant known for its unique appearance and limited distribution. Found primarily in the mountainous regions of eastern Asia, it thrives in cool, moist environments. Its most striking feature is its heart-shaped leaves, which are both ornamental and biologically significant. Despite its beauty, little is known about its reproductive habits and ecological role. This article explores the characteristics, habitat, and conservation status of this fascinating species.
