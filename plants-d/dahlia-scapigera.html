Dahlia scapigera

Dahlia scapigera, commonly known as the scarlet dahlia, is a herbaceous perennial native to Mexico.

It features showy, single or double flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom from late spring to early autumn. The plant has upright, branching stems with lance-shaped, dark green leaves. Its tuberous roots allow it to survive dormant periods, and it thrives in well-drained soil with full sun.

Dahlia scapigera is prized for its vibrant flowers and is a popular choice in gardens and floral arrangements.
