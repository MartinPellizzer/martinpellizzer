Dendrobium cymbicallum

Dendrobium cymbicallum, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, cane-like stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with a distinctive golden hue. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Dendrobium cymbicallum is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its striking floral display.
