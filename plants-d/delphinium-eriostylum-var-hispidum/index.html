Delphinium Eriostylum Var. Hispidum

Delphinium eriostylum var. hispidum is a striking member of the Ranunculaceae family known for its tall, spiky flower clusters. This variant is distinguished by its fuzzy, hairy stems and vibrant blue to purple blooms that attract pollinators. Native to the mountainous regions of western North America, it thrives in cool, moist environments. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice among gardeners seeking both beauty and biodiversity. However, it requires careful cultivation due to its sensitivity to heat and pests.
