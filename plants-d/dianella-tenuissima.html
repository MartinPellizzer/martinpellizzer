Dianella tenuissima

Dianella tenuissima, commonly known as the narrow-leaved flax lily, is a perennial evergreen plant native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces striking blue to purple flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Dianella tenuissima is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native pollinators.
