Dillenia pauciflora

Dillenia pauciflora, commonly known as the little-flowered dillenia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant produces showy, white or pale yellow flowers with five petals, often arranged in clusters. Its fruit is a woody, spiny capsule that contains several seeds.

Dillenia pauciflora is often found in moist, shaded forest environments.
