Dichelachne rigida

Dichelachne rigida, commonly known as rigid dichelachne, is a grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its erect, often tufted growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale to brownish flowers. It is typically found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

Dichelachne rigida is adapted to arid conditions and is often considered a weed in agricultural areas.
