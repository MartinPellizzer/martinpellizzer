Dendrobium indivisum var. indivisum

Dendrobium indivisum var. indivisum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf dendrobium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant that grows on trees and rocks, often in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant features narrow, leathery leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
