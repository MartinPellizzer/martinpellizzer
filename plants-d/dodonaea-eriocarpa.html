Dodonaea eriocarpa

Dodonaea eriocarpa, commonly known as the silky Dodonaea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its finely hairy, silver-green leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by woody, bead-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and woodland areas.

Dodonaea eriocarpa is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience to drought and salt spray.
