Diplazium undulosum

Diplazium undulosum, commonly known as the wavy fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its feathery, delicate fronds that have a wavy or undulating appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Diplazium undulosum is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its ecological role in forest ecosystems.
