Dracula sijmii

Dracula sijmii, commonly known as the Sijmii's vampire orchid, is a rare and striking epiphytic orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dark, velvety leaves and intricate, pendulous inflorescences that resemble the flowing robes of a vampire. The plant produces vivid, bi-colored flowers with deep red and purple hues, often with intricate patterns resembling bloodstains. Dracula sijmii thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires high air humidity and indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is a prized and challenging plant for orchid enthusiasts.
