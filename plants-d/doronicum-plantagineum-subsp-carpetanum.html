Doronicum plantagineum subsp. carpetanum

Doronicum plantagineum subsp.

carpetanum, commonly known as the Carpathian leopard's bane, is a perennial herb native to the Carpathian Mountains. It features upright stems with lance-shaped, dark green leaves that have a slightly hairy texture.

The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers with five petals, blooming from late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and ornamental plant in its native habitat.
