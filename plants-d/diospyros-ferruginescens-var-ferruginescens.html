Diospyros ferruginescens var. ferruginescens

Diospyros ferruginescens var. ferruginescens, commonly known as the red-berried ebony, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree with dark, glossy leaves that are elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by small, red berries that are edible. Its wood is dense and highly valued for its fine grain and durability.

The plant is often cultivated for ornamental purposes and has traditional uses in various cultures.
