Draba aurea

Draba aurea, commonly known as the golden wintercress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous annual that typically grows in rocky or alpine environments. The plant features basal leaves that are pinnately divided and small yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Draba aurea is characterized by its compact growth habit and ability to thrive in cold climates.

It is often found in mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.
