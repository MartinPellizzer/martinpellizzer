Dioscorea firma

Dioscorea firma, commonly known as the African yam bean, is a climbing vine native to Africa.

It belongs to the Dioscoreaceae family and is characterized by its tuberous roots, which are the primary storage organs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers and yellowish fruits that contain seeds. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a rough texture.

Dioscorea firma is valued for its edible tubers, which are rich in carbohydrates and have been used traditionally in various cuisines.
