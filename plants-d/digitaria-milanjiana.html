Digitaria milanjiana

Digitaria milanjiana, commonly known as the African grass or milanjiana grass, is a species of grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers.

Digitaria milanjiana is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its rapid growth and ability to stabilize soil.
