Dyckia densiflora

Dyckia densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered Dyckia, is a species of flowering plant in the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming structure. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically covered in a dense layer of white to grayish hairs. It produces inflorescences with small, white to pale purple flowers that bloom in summer.

Dyckia densiflora is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
