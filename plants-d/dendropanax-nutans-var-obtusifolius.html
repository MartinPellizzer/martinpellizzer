Dendropanax nutans var. obtusifolius

Dendropanax nutans var. obtusifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf arrowhead, is a variant of the arrowhead plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, broad leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by red berries that are edible for some animals but toxic to humans. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening due to its attractive foliage.
