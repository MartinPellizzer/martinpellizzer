Draba × salomonii

Draba × salomonii, commonly known as the Alpine winter cress, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its rosette of basal leaves and small, bright yellow flowers. Native to alpine regions of Europe and Asia, it thrives in rocky and gravelly soils at high elevations. The plant is adapted to cold climates and can survive harsh winter conditions.

Its seeds are dispersed by wind, contributing to its widespread distribution in mountainous areas.
