Dendrobium macrophyllum var. huttonii

Dendrobium macrophyllum var. huttonii, commonly known as Hutton's large-leaf orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a sympodial orchid with a tall, upright growth habit and produces a single, elongated stem that can reach up to 1 meter in length. The plant features numerous lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of white to pale green pubescence. Its flowers are typically pale yellow to greenish-white, with a distinctive three-lobed lip that is often adorned with purple or red markings.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique foliage and showy blooms.
