Diporidium pruinosum

Diporidium pruinosum, commonly known as the velvetleaf or velvetleaf hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange. The plant has thick, waxy leaves that give it a distinctive velvety texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and ecologically significant in its native habitats.
