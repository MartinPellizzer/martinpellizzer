Dais dubiosa

Dais dubiosa, commonly known as the white daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, white ray florets surrounding a central disk of yellow tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It produces small, oval-shaped seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Dais dubiosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its simple, elegant appearance.
