Dichrocephala bodinieri

Dichrocephala bodinieri, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant typically grows in grasslands and along riverbanks, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Dichrocephala bodinieri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
