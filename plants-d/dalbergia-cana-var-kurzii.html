Dalbergia cana var. kurzii

Dalbergia cana var. kurzii, commonly known as the Kurz's rosewood, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Indonesia and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that are typically pink or white in color. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with small, oval-shaped leaflets.
