Dendrochilum brachyotum

Dendrochilum brachyotum, commonly known as the short-stemmed tree orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, tree-like growth habit with a short, thick rhizome and multiple stems. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a distinctive lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is typically found in lowland forests.

Dendrochilum brachyotum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
