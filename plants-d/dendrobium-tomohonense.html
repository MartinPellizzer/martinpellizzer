Dendrobium tomohonense

Dendrobium tomohonense, commonly known as Tomohon orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Indonesia.

It is characterized by its tall, cane-like stems and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically blooms in the summer months, producing white or pale yellow flowers with a subtle fragrance. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable ornamental plant.
