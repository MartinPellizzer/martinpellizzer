Dioscorea remotiflora var. sparsiflora

Dioscorea remotiflora var. sparsiflora, commonly known as the sparse-flowered yam, is a tropical vine belonging to the Dioscoreaceae family.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and heart-shaped leaves with prominent veins. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is native to parts of Southeast Asia and the Pacific Islands.

Due to its ornamental and potential medicinal value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and research settings.
