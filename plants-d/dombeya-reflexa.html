Dombeya reflexa

Dombeya reflexa, commonly known as the reflexed dombeya, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and are often tinged with purple. The plant typically grows in shrub form, with woody stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to temperate to subtropical climates.

Dombeya reflexa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive blooms.
