Dracaena ensifolia

Dracaena ensifolia, commonly known as the sword leaf dracaena, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, narrow, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to 2 meters in length. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by red, fleshy fruits containing seeds. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its striking foliage makes it a visually appealing addition to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
