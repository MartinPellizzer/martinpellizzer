Doritis pulcherrima f. purpurea

Doritis pulcherrima f. purpurea, commonly known as the Purple Ice Plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, ribbed leaves that store water, adapted to arid environments. The plant produces vibrant purple flowers that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, requiring minimal water once established.
