Dryopteris scabra var. caesariana

Dryopteris scabra var. caesariana, commonly known as the silky cliff fern, is a variant of the rough shield fern.

It is characterized by its finely textured, fronds that are covered with a silvery, scaly appearance. This fern thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in rocky or wooded areas. The plant is native to parts of Asia, including China and Japan.

Its unique morphology makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in humid climates.
