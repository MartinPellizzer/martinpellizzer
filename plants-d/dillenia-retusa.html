Dillenia retusa

Dillenia retusa, commonly known as the smooth dillenia, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dilleniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, palmately lobed leaves. The plant produces showy, yellowish-white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are woody, berry-like structures that contain several seeds.

Dillenia retusa is often found in moist, shaded habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
