Deguelia nitidula

Deguelia nitidula, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its smooth, often hairless stems and heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually in dense inflorescences, and are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color.

The plant is known for its rapid growth and is sometimes considered an invasive species in certain regions.
