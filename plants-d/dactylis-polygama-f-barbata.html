Dactylis polygama f. barbata

Dactylis polygama f. barbata, commonly known as the barbed dactylis, is a variant of the dactylis grass species.

It is characterized by its distinctive barbed awns on the seed heads, which give it a unique appearance. This perennial grass is native to temperate regions of Europe and is often found in meadows and grasslands. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and its ability to thrive in various soil conditions.
