Delphinium trigonelloides

Delphinium trigonelloides, commonly known as the Alpine larkspur, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It features tall, slender stems that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height, adorned with clusters of blue to purple flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. Its flowers are spurred at the base, a characteristic feature of the Delphinium genus.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for alpine gardens.
