Deguelia picta

Deguelia picta, commonly known as the painted deguelia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height, with a dense, often thorny canopy. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a velvety texture.

Deguelia picta is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
