Digitaria preslii

Digitaria preslii, commonly known as the Preslia's digitaria, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or grasslands. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its flowers are wind-pollinated, and the seeds are dispersed by wind or water.

Digitaria preslii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in landscaping.
