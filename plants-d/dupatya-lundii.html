Dupatya lundii

Dupatya lundii, commonly known as the African orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to South Africa.

It is a terrestrial herb that typically grows in grassland habitats. The plant features a slender, upright stem with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, white or pale pink, and are clustered in a spike.

Dupatya lundii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
