Dieteria canescens var. incana

Dieteria canescens var. incana, commonly known as the woolly dieteria, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its hairy, grayish-green foliage and small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has a compact growth habit with upright stems and is often found in regions with a Mediterranean climate.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
