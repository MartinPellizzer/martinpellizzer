Dioscorea kamoonensis var. brevifolia

Dioscorea kamoonensis var. brevifolia, commonly known as the short-leaved yam, is a member of the Dioscoreaceae family and is native to parts of Asia, particularly India.

It is a tuberous perennial vine characterized by its short, broad leaves and yellowish flowers. The plant produces edible tubers that are often used in traditional cuisines. Its growth habit involves climbing stems and the development of underground storage organs.

Botanically, it is notable for its morphological adaptations to its native environment.
