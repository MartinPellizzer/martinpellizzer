Dillenia philippinensis var. pubifolia

Dillenia philippinensis var. pubifolia, commonly known as Philippine dillenia, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves that are often hairy on the underside. The plant produces showy, yellowish-white flowers with five to seven petals, which bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a woody, fleshy capsule that splits open to release seeds.

This species is often found in lowland forests and is valued for its ornamental and potential medicinal properties.
