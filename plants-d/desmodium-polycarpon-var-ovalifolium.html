Desmodium polycarpon var. ovalifolium

Desmodium polycarpon var. ovalifolium, commonly known as the oval-leaved burr legume, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features trifoliate leaves with oval-shaped leaflets and small, yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters. The plant produces distinctive, spiny pods that contain several small, kidney-shaped seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas or along roadsides.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species in ecological studies and traditional medicine.
