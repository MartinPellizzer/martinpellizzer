Dendrobium melanolasium

Dendrobium melanolasium, commonly known as the black-lipped orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome that produces multiple pseudobulbs. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with dark red or black markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrobium melanolasium is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
