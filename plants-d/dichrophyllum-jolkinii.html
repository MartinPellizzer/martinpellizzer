Dichrophyllum jolkinii

Dichrophyllum jolkinii, commonly known as the silverleaf dichrophyllum, is a rare and unique plant species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

The plant thrives in shaded, humid environments, often found in forest understories. Its foliage exhibits a metallic sheen, which enhances its visual appeal and aids in reflecting excess light.
