Dillwynia floribunda var. brevifolia

Dillwynia floribunda var. brevifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved dillwynia, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It is a small shrub characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal or semi-arid regions. It has a compact growth habit and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.

Dillwynia floribunda var. brevifolia is also noted for its ability to attract native pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
