Dolichos purpureus

Dolichos purpureus, commonly known as the purple dolichos, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features trifoliate leaves with purple veins and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant bears elongated, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in open woodlands and along roadsides, thriving in well-drained soils.

Botanically, it is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
