Deiregyne ramentacea

Deiregyne ramentacea, commonly known as the silky grass.

It is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly found in regions of Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and small, inconspicuous flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its scientific name reflects its silky, hairy appearance, which is a notable botanical feature.
