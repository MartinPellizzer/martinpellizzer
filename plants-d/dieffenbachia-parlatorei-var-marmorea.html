Dieffenbachia parlatorei var. marmorea

Dieffenbachia parlatorei var. marmorea, commonly known as the Marmorea Dieffenbachia, is a tropical houseplant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that feature a combination of white, cream, and green patterns. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is known for its upright growth habit and relatively slow growth rate. Its foliage is often used as a decorative element in interior landscaping due to its aesthetic appeal.

It requires indirect light and moderate humidity to thrive.
