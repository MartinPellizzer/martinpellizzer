Dieffenbachia latimaculata

Dieffenbachia latimaculata, commonly known as the Leopard Plant, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with dark green leaves featuring light green and white markings. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other popular houseplants like peace lilies. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to maintain its vibrant appearance.

Due to its toxic sap, it should be handled with care and kept away from pets and children.
