Dracocephalum stellerianum

Dracocephalum stellerianum, commonly known as Steller's bugloss, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy texture.

Dracocephalum stellerianum is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to harsh, high-altitude environments.
