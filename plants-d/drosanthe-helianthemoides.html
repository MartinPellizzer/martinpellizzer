Drosanthe helianthemoides

Drosanthe helianthemoides, commonly known as the rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing evergreen shrub with fleshy, gray-green leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white hair. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Drosanthe helianthemoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
