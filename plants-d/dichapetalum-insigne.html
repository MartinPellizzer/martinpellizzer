Dichapetalum insigne

Dichapetalum insigne, commonly known as the African star orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

The plant produces small, fleshy leaves and thrives in woodland environments with partial shade. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its distinctive floral display.
