Delosperma sutherlandii

Delosperma sutherlandii, commonly known as the Sutherland's ice plant, is a low-growing, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are adapted to store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers in the spring, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.

Its compact growth habit makes it ideal for filling in gaps in garden landscapes.
