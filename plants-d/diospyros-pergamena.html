Diospyros pergamena

Diospyros pergamena, commonly known as the parchment ebony, is a species of flowering plant in the family Ebenaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Indonesia and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its dark, hard wood, which is highly valued for its durability and fine grain. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a glossy appearance.

The fruit is a small, smooth, and typically reddish drupe that ripens in the late summer to early autumn.
