Dryopteris larutensis

Dryopteris larutensis, commonly known as the Larut shield fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its large, leathery fronds that are typically divided into a series of segments. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its rhizome is thick and fleshy, supporting the growth of its distinctive, bipinnate leaves.

This fern is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
