Dendrobium subulatoides

Dendrobium subulatoides, commonly known as the slender spike orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome that produces multiple shoots. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves and produces inflorescences with small, greenish-white flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique and interesting subject for both horticultural and ecological study.
