Dendrobium cornutum

Dendrobium cornutum, commonly known as the horned orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with cane-like stems that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink, often with a distinctive horn-shaped spur. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium cornutum is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
