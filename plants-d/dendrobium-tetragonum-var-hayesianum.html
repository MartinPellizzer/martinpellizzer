Dendrobium tetragonum var. hayesianum

Dendrobium tetragonum var. hayesianum, commonly known as the Hayes' Dendrobium, is a sympodial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features erect, cane-like pseudobulbs covered in leathery, elliptical leaves. The plant produces racemes of fragrant, yellowish-green to white flowers with prominent yellow lips. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its long-lasting blooms.
