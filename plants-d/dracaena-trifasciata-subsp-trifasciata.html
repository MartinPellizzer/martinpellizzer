Dracaena trifasciata subsp. trifasciata

Dracaena trifasciata subsp.

trifasciata, commonly known as the snake plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is native to tropical Africa. It is a perennial evergreen plant characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with distinctive light-colored stripes.
