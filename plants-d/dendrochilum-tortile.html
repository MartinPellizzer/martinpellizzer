Dendrochilum tortile

Dendrochilum tortile, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique spiral growth pattern of the inflorescence, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually small and fragrant, with a variety of colors including white, yellow, and pink.

Dendrochilum tortile is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
