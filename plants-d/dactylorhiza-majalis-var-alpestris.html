Dactylorhiza majalis var. alpestris

Dactylorhiza majalis var. alpestris, commonly known as the alpine marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to mountainous regions of Europe.

It typically grows in wet, acidic habitats such as bogs and marshes. The plant has a slender, upright stem with several lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are pink to purple, arranged in a raceme, and bloom in late spring to early summer.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit.
