Daphne fortunei

Daphne fortunei, commonly known as Fortune's daphne, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces small, dark blue berries that are edible but not particularly palatable. Its leaves are glossy and oval-shaped, and it has a compact growth habit.

Daphne fortunei is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and winter interest.
