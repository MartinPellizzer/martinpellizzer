Dracocephalum surmandinum

Dracocephalum surmandinum, commonly known as Surmandinum skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants known for their medicinal properties. The plant features square stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, tubular flowers are typically blue or purple, arranged in whorls along the stem.

Dracocephalum surmandinum is valued in traditional medicine for its purported antiseptic and anti-inflammatory properties.
