Dendrobium tosaense var. chingshuishanianum

Dendrobium tosaense var. chingshuishanianum, commonly known as the Chingshuishan dendrobium, is a rare orchid species native to southern China.

It is an epiphytic plant that grows on trees and rocks in humid, shaded forest environments. The plant produces long, cane-like pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, typical of many orchid species. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with a delicate fragrance, and bloom during the spring or early summer.
