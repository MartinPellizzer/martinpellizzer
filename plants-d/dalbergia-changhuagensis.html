Dalbergia changhuagensis

Dalbergia changhuagensis, commonly known as the Changhuang tree, is a species of legume native to China.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its small, compound leaves and fragrant flowers. The tree produces hard, dark wood that is highly valued for its density and aesthetic qualities. It typically grows in mountainous regions and is adapted to temperate climates.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
