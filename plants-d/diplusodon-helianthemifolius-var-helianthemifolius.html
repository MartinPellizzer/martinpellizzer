Diplusodon helianthemifolius var. helianthemifolius

Diplusodon helianthemifolius var. helianthemifolius, commonly known as the yellow sandmat, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces showy yellow flowers in the summer months. The plant has fleshy, succulent leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for rock gardens and arid landscapes.

Its botanical features make it an attractive and hardy addition to xeriscapic gardens.
