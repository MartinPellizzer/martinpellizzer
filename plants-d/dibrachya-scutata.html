Dibrachya scutata

Dibrachya scutata, commonly known as the scarlet lily, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking red petals and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in late spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Dibrachya scutata is also valued for its ornamental beauty and relatively low maintenance requirements.
