Deschampsia argentea var. grandiflora

Deschampsia argentea var. grandiflora, commonly known as silvergrass or silver hair grass, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage and clusters of small, fluffy flower heads. This grass thrives in moist, open habitats such as wet meadows and stream banks. The plant produces numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds, aiding in its spread.

Its botanical features make it a valuable component of wetland ecosystems.
