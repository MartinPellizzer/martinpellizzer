Dryopteris yoroii

Dryopteris yoroii, commonly known as the Yoroii wood fern, is a species of fern native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky slopes. Its fronds are usually bi- or tri-pinnate, with a distinctive, finely divided structure.

Dryopteris yoroii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in horticultural settings.
