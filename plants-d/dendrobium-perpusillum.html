Dendrobium perpusillum

Dendrobium perpusillum, commonly known as the miniature dendrobium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that grows on trees and rocks in humid, tropical environments. The plant produces clusters of tiny, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it has a compact growth habit.

Dendrobium perpusillum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its delicate blooms.
