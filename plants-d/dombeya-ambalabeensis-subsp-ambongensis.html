Dombeya ambalabeensis subsp. ambongensis

Dombeya ambalabeensis subsp.

ambongensis, commonly known as the Ambongensis Dombeya, is a shrub native to Madagascar. It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink.

The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in forested areas. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a rough texture.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
