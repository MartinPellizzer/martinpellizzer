Dracula rezekiana

Dracula rezekiana, commonly known as the Dragon's Blood orchid, is a rare and striking epiphytic orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its intricate, pendulous flowers that resemble miniature dragons, hence its common name. The plant has dark green, strap-like leaves and produces vibrant red and orange blooms during the rainy season. It thrives in high humidity and requires a cool, shaded environment to flourish.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
