Diuris carinata

Diuris carinata, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is an Australian native plant belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzag-shaped leaves and a single, showy flower that typically blooms in spring. The plant thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in heathlands and open woodlands. It is a perennial herb with a short, underground rhizome that supports its growth.

Diuris carinata is adapted to dry conditions and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.
