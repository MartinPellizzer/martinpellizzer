Dichapetalum bakerianum

Dichapetalum bakerianum, commonly known as the African star orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive central boss of petals.

Dichapetalum bakerianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
