Digitaria lasiostachya

Digitaria lasiostachya, commonly known as hairy fingergrass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant has hairy leaves and stems, which give it its common name. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in grasslands and along roadsides.

Digitaria lasiostachya is a perennial plant that plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife.
