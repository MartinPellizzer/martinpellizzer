Dryopteris engleriana

Dryopteris engleriana, commonly known as the Engler's wood fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, upright fronds that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height. The fronds are bipinnate, with a distinctive dark green color and a leathery texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
