Dendrobium angustipetalum

Dendrobium angustipetalum, commonly known as the narrow-petaled orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with multiple stems that produce numerous lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking flowers with narrow, elongated petals and a prominent, spurred lip. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its unique floral structure makes it a prized species among orchid enthusiasts.
