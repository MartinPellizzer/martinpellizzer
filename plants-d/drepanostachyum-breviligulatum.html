Drepanostachyum breviligulatum

Drepanostachyum breviligulatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bamboo, is a member of the grass family (Poaceae).

It is a perennial plant characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. Native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, it thrives in cool, humid climates. The plant is often found in forest understories and is adapted to shaded environments.

Its growth habit and morphology make it a unique species within the bamboo-like grasses.
