Dendrobenthamia emeiensis

Dendrobenthamia emeiensis, commonly known as the Emei Mountain orchid, is a rare epiphytic orchid native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its pseudobulbs and linear, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking yellow and orange flowers during the spring season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments at high altitudes.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
