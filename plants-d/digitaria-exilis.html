Digitaria exilis

Digitaria exilis, commonly known as slender digitgrass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions. Its seeds are dispersed by wind, contributing to its widespread distribution.

Digitaria exilis is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings due to its rapid growth and adaptability.
