Dimeria stapfiana

Dimeria stapfiana, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves, and it produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring.

Dimeria stapfiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
