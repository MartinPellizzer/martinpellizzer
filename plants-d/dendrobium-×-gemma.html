Dendrobium × gemma

Dendrobium × gemma, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a fascinating epiphytic plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is a hybrid between Dendrobium phalaenopsis and Dendrobium nobile, resulting in a unique combination of traits from both parent species. The plant typically features sympodial growth, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, enabling it to thrive in diverse environmental conditions. Its flowers are often showy, with a wide range of colors, making it a popular choice for ornamental cultivation.

Dendrobium × gemma requires well-draining potting media and bright, indirect light to flourish.
