Deyeuxia sesquivalvis

Deyeuxia sesquivalvis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved wood sedge, is a perennial grass-like plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

The plant produces small, clustered spikelets that are wind-pollinated, and it plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
