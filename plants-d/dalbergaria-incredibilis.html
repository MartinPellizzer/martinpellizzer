Dalbergaria incredibilis

Dalbergaria incredibilis, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, fragrant white flowers. The plant is notable for its hard, heavy wood, which is highly valued for its durability and resistance to decay.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions, where it plays an important ecological role.
