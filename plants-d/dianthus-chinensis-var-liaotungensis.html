Dianthus chinensis var. liaotungensis

Dianthus chinensis var. liaotungensis, commonly known as the Liaotung carnation, is a perennial herb native to China.

It features showy, bicolor flowers that are typically pink or red with white or pale pink centers. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, five-petaled blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various climates.
