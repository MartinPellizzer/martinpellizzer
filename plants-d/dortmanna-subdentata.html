Dortmanna subdentata

Dortmanna subdentata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved dock, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist habitats such as meadows, marshes, and riverbanks. The plant features basal leaves that are elongated and have a distinctive subdeltoid shape with fine teeth along the margins. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and clustered in a loose umbel, which appear in early summer.

The species is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
