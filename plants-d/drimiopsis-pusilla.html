Drimiopsis pusilla

Drimiopsis pusilla, commonly known as the dwarf drimiopsis, is a small, evergreen perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features narrow, strap-like leaves that are typically a deep green color with a slight sheen. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate light conditions.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and indoor spaces.
