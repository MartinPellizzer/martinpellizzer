Desmodium ancistrotrichum

Desmodium ancistrotrichum, commonly known as small-leaf burr legume, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-bearing fruits. The plant features trifoliate leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. Its seed pods are hairy and contain several small, kidney-shaped seeds.

Desmodium ancistrotrichum is often found in disturbed soils and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
