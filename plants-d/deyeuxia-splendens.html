Deyeuxia splendens

Deyeuxia splendens, commonly known as the golden grass or splendour grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow inflorescence, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its slender, grass-like leaves are usually green with a slight bluish tinge.

Deyeuxia splendens is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its striking floral display.
