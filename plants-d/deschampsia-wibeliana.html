Deschampsia wibeliana

Deschampsia wibeliana, commonly known as Wibel's feathergrass, is a perennial grass native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, airy plumes of pale blue to silver flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils. It is often found in open habitats such as meadows and slopes at high elevations.

Due to its ornamental appeal and ecological significance, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
