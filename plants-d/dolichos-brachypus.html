Dolichos brachypus

Dolichos brachypus, commonly known as the horse gram or cluster bean, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its hairy, erect stems and trifoliate leaves with small, heart-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, yellow flowers followed by elongated, cylindrical pods containing edible seeds. Dolichos brachypus is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it an important crop in sustainable agriculture.

It is widely cultivated for its nutritious seeds, which are used in various cuisines and traditional medicine.
