Dendrobium aduncilobum

Dendrobium aduncilobum, commonly known as the narrow-lipped orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant that grows on trees and rocks, typically in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features long, cane-like stems covered with clusters of small, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow with a distinctive, narrow lip.

Dendrobium aduncilobum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
