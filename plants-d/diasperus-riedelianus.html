Diasperus riedelianus

Diasperus riedelianus, commonly known as the Riedel's grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly found in countries like Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. It produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are typically wind-pollinated.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
