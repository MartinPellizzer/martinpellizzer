Dianthus × julii-wolfii

Dianthus × julii-wolfii, commonly known as the hybrid carnation, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Dianthus julii and Dianthus wolfii, both of which are native to the Mediterranean region. The plant is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that come in a range of colors, including pink, red, and white. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun.

Dianthus × julii-wolfii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
