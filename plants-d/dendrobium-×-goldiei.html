Dendrobium × goldiei

Dendrobium × goldiei, commonly known as the gold torch orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant that thrives in warm, humid climates, typically growing on trees or rocks. The plant features long, cane-like stems that bear clusters of vibrant yellow flowers with red markings. Its botanical characteristics include pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, and it produces showy, fragrant blooms during the summer months.

Dendrobium × goldiei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
