Docyniopsis prattii

Docyniopsis prattii, commonly known as the Pratts' boxthorn, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces smooth, oval-shaped fruits that are typically green when mature. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens.
