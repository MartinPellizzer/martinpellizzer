Dysoxylum arborescens

Dysoxylum arborescens, commonly known as the tropical tree or red sandalwood, is a flowering plant in the family Meliaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its large, lance-shaped leaves and showy yellow flowers. The tree can grow up to 30 meters tall and has a straight trunk with a smooth, reddish bark. Its fruit is a woody capsule containing several winged seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

The plant is valued for its durable wood, which is used in construction and furniture-making.
