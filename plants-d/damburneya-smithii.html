Damburneya smithii

Damburneya smithii, commonly known as the Smith's damburneya, is a unique evergreen shrub native to the coastal regions of South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and have a waxy texture. The plant produces striking, bell-shaped flowers that are usually yellow or orange in color, which bloom in late summer. Damburneya smithii thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature in coastal gardens.
