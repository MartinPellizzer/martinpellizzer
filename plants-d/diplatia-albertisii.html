Diplatia albertisii

Diplatia albertisii, commonly known as the Albert's flat lichen, is a foliose lichen belonging to the family Parmeliaceae.

It is characterized by its leaf-like thallus, which is typically pale green to yellowish in color. The plant forms a symbiotic relationship with algae, which provide the photosynthetic function necessary for nutrient production. Due to its slow growth and sensitivity to environmental changes, it is considered an indicator species for ecological health.
