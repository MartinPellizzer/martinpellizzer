Dammara celebica

It belongs to the family Burseraceae and is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and long, narrow leaves. The tree produces small, fragrant flowers followed by fleshy, orange-red fruits that contain numerous seeds. It is often found in lowland and hill forest areas, thriving in humid, warm climates.
