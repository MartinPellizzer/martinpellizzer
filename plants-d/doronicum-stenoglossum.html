Doronicum stenoglossum

Doronicum stenoglossum, commonly known as the narrow-lipped leopard's bane, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It typically grows to a height of about 30-60 cm and features striking yellow flowers with dark red or purple markings. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy, and it blooms from late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Doronicum stenoglossum is valued for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
