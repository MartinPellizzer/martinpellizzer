Dendrobium vinosum

Dendrobium vinosum, commonly known as the purple dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, often branched stem covered with dense, leathery leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of purple and white, typically during the summer months. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated for horticultural purposes.
