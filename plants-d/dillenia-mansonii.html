Dillenia mansonii

Dillenia mansonii, commonly known as the Manson's dillenia, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dilleniaceae and is characterized by its large, palmate leaves with lobed margins. The plant produces showy, white or yellow flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are woody, capsule-like structures that split open to release seeds.

Dillenia mansonii is often found in moist, shaded environments within tropical forests.
