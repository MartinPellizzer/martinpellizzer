Deyeuxia rupestris

Deyeuxia rupestris, commonly known as the rock sedge, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Asia, including China and Japan.

The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in mountainous regions, and is adapted to harsh environmental conditions.
