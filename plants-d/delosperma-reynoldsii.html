Delosperma reynoldsii

Delosperma reynoldsii, commonly known as the Reynolds' iceplant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ability to survive in harsh conditions makes it an important species in ecological restoration projects.
