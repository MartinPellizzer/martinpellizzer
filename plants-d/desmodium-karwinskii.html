Desmodium karwinskii

Desmodium karwinskii, commonly known as karwinski's ticktrefoil, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, which are often dispersed by wind.

It is adapted to thrive in dry, sandy soils and is often found in grassland and savanna habitats.
