Dombeya leucomacrantha var. typica

Dombeya leucomacrantha var. typica, commonly known as the white-flowered dombeya, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and ability to attract pollinators.
