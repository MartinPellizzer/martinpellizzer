Dysphania multifida

Dysphania multifida, commonly known as kidneyweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its alternate, heart-shaped leaves that are often lobed or deeply divided, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Dysphania multifida is native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere and is often found in moist, disturbed soils.

It is an annual herb that can spread rapidly, making it both a weed and a potential invasive species in some areas.
