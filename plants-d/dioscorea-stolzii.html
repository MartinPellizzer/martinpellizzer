Dioscorea stolzii

Dioscorea stolzii, commonly known as the Stolz's yam, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its tuberous roots, which are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a rough, hairy texture.

Dioscorea stolzii is valued for its medicinal properties and is also studied for its potential in modern pharmacology.
