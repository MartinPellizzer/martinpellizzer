Dendrobium perlongum

Dendrobium perlongum, commonly known as the long-lasting orchid, is a epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, cane-like stems that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Dendrobium perlongum is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
