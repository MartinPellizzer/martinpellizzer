Diospyros kamerunensis

Diospyros kamerunensis, commonly known as the African persimmon, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. The tree produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into orange-red, fleshy fruits. It plays an important ecological role in its native habitat and is valued for its ornamental and economic significance.
