Dactylorhiza latifolia f. rubra

Dactylorhiza latifolia f. rubra, commonly known as the red-flowered broad-leaf orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that range in color from pink to deep red. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and heathlands. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces a single spike of flowers.

The species is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
