Dioscorea villosa subsp. hirticaulis

Dioscorea villosa subsp.

hirticaulis, commonly known as the hairy ginseng, is a perennial vine native to North America. It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by tuberous roots that are used in traditional medicine. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it distinct from other species within the genus Dioscorea.
