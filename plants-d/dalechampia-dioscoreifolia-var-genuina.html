Dalechampia dioscoreifolia var. genuina

Dalechampia dioscoreifolia var. genuina, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 feet in length.

The leaves are typically a deep green with a waxy texture and a distinctive arrowhead shape, which gives the plant its common name. It is native to Central and South America, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by spiny, berry-like fruits.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
