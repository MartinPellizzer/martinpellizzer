Dioscorea zara

Dioscorea zara, commonly known as the African yambean, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Dioscoreaceae family and is characterized by its tuberous roots, which are the primary edible part. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into capsule fruits. Its tubers are rich in starch and have been traditionally used as a food source.

The plant also has medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
