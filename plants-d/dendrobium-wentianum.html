Dendrobium wentianum

Dendrobium wentianum, commonly known as the Chinese orchid or Wentai orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is an epiphytic or lithophytic plant native to Southeast Asia, including China, Indonesia, and the Philippines. The plant produces tall, branching pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, and it blooms with fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. Its flowers are typically arranged in racemes and can last for several weeks.

Dendrobium wentianum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its beauty and fragrance.
