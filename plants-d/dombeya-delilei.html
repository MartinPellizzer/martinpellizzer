Dombeya delilei

Dombeya delilei, commonly known as the Delile's dombeya, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that range from white to pink, blooming in spring and summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Dombeya delilei is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in landscaping for its vibrant floral displays.
