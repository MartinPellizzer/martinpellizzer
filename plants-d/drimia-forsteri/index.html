Drimia Forsteri

Drimia lily, scientifically known as Drimia forsteri, is a striking plant native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa. This bulbous plant is part of the Amaryllidaceae family, which includes many ornamental and medicinal plants. Its distinctive appearance features tall, strap-like leaves and showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink. Drimia forsteri thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners. Due to its beauty and resilience, it has gained attention for both horticultural and ecological purposes.
