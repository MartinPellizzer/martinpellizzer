Diospyros suarezensis

Diospyros suarezensis, commonly known as the Suarez's ebony, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to the Caribbean islands.

It belongs to the Ebenaceae family, which includes other well-known species like the African ebony. The plant is characterized by its dark, dense wood, which is highly valued for its strength and durability. Its leaves are typically elliptical and glossy, while its flowers are small and clustered.

Diospyros suarezensis plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
