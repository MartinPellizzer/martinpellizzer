Dendrobium paitanense

Dendrobium paitanense, commonly known as the Paitan orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Dendrobium genus, which is known for its diverse and often showy flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, forming pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually small to medium in size, with a variety of colors including white, yellow, and pink.

Dendrobium paitanense is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
