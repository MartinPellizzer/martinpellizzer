Delissea wailauensis

It belongs to the Loranthaceae family and typically grows on the branches of native trees such as the ʻōhiʻa lehua. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves and produces tiny white or pale yellow flowers. Its berries are usually red or orange and contain seeds that are dispersed by birds.
