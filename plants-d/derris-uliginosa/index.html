Derris Uliginosa

Derris uliginosa is a fascinating plant known for its unique appearance and ecological significance. Native to tropical regions, it thrives in moist and shady environments. This species is commonly found in wetlands and forest floors where it plays a vital role in the ecosystem. Its distinctive leaves and clusters of small flowers make it an interesting subject for botanists and nature enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it also contains toxic compounds that require caution when handling.
