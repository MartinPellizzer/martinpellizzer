Diarrhena americana var. obovata

Diarrhena americana var. obovata, commonly known as the American diarrhena, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, wooded areas and thrives in partial to full shade. The plant features lanceolate to obovate leaves with smooth or slightly hairy surfaces. Its small, greenish-white flowers appear in clusters and bloom in early spring.

The plant is characterized by its fibrous root system and is often found in swampy or wetland environments.
