Dracontium gigas

Dracontium gigas, commonly known as the giant arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant produces striking inflorescences with bright yellow spadices surrounded by dark purple bracts. Its rhizomes are used as a starchy food source, similar to taro.

The plant thrives in moist, shady environments and is often cultivated for both ornamental and culinary purposes.
