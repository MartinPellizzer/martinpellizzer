Dolichos splendens

Dolichos splendens, commonly known as the flame bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It produces vibrant orange to red flowers that are often used in ornamental gardening. The plant has pinnately compound leaves and bears elongated, pod-like fruits containing edible seeds. It thrives in warm climates with well-drained soil and requires full sun for optimal growth.

Its botanical features make it both visually striking and ecologically beneficial.
