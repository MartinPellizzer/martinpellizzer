Diuris minor

Diuris minor, commonly known as the dwarf spider orchid, is a small, terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils in heathlands and open forests. The plant features a slender, hairy stem with a single or two small, hairy leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green to brown, with a distinctive spider-like appearance.

Diuris minor is known for its resilience and ability to thrive in harsh, dry conditions.
