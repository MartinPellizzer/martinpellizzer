Danthonia aristidoides

Danthonia aristidoides, commonly known as the slender danthonia, is a grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and slender, cylindrical stems. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, two-lobed flowers. Its leaves are narrow and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Danthonia aristidoides is adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions of Australia.
