Davallia brassii

Davallia brassii, commonly known as the shield fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that are typically bright green and covered in a fine, fuzzy texture. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with creeping rhizomes that allow it to spread along the forest floor. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
