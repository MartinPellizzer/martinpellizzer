Dypsis linearis

Dypsis linearis, commonly known as the areca palm, is a tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its slender, upright trunk and long, arching fronds that give it a graceful appearance. The plant has feather-like leaves that are dark green and glossy, contributing to its ornamental value. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Dypsis linearis is often used in indoor gardening due to its adaptability and low maintenance requirements.
