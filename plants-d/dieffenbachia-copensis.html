Dieffenbachia copensis

Dieffenbachia copensis, commonly known as the Leopard Plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that feature a combination of white, green, and yellow markings. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions. Its foliage is often used in interior design due to its ornamental value.

Dieffenbachia copensis requires moderate watering and well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
