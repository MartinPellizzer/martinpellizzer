Dracocephalum sinense

Dracocephalum sinense, commonly known as Chinese skullcap, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional Chinese medicine.
