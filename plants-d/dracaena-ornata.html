Dracaena ornata

Dracaena ornata, commonly known as the "Coral Dragon Tree," is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, narrow, dark green leaves that are often adorned with striking red or orange margins, giving it a vibrant appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by bright red berries, which are toxic to humans and animals. Dracaena ornata is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance of low light and ability to thrive in a variety of potting soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor landscaping.
