Draba herzogii

Draba herzogii, commonly known as the Herzog's rockcress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a small, perennial herb that typically grows in alpine and arctic regions, often in rocky or gravelly soils. Draba herzogii is adapted to cold climates and is often found in mountainous areas across Europe and Asia.
