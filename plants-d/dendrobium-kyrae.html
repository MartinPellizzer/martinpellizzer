Dendrobium kyrae

Dendrobium kyrae, commonly known as the Kyrae orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with multiple stems that can reach up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellow-green flowers with dark red markings, typically blooming in the summer months. Its leaves are elliptical and arranged in two rows along the stem, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Dendrobium kyrae thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
