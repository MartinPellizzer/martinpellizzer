Drepanostachyum gongshanense

Drepanostachyum gongshanense, commonly known as the Gongshan bamboo, is a species of grass belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Yunnan Province, where it thrives in mountainous regions. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It plays a significant role in the local ecosystem by providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered plant.
