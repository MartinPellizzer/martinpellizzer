Delphinium gombaultii

Delphinium gombaultii, commonly known as the Gombault's larkspur, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

Delphinium gombaultii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
