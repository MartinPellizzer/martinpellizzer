Dichorisandra ovata var. ramosior

Dichorisandra ovata var. ramosior, commonly known as the silver bush, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, silver-green foliage and clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its compact growth habit and attractive appearance make it suitable for rock gardens and container planting.
