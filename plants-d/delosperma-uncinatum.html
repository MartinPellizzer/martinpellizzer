Delosperma uncinatum

Delosperma uncinatum, commonly known as ice plant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers in clusters, which bloom in summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ability to store water in its leaves allows it to survive in arid environments.
