Dieffenbachia verschaffeltii

Dieffenbachia verschaffeltii, commonly known as the Verschaffelt's Dieffenbachia, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with white or cream-colored veins. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other popular houseplants like peace lilies and philodendrons. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to prevent root rot.

Its striking foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
