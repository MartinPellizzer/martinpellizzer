Dypsis pumila

Dypsis pumila, commonly known as the miniature areca palm, is a compact, low-growing palm native to Madagascar.

It features a slender trunk with a fibrous sheath and produces small, clustered inflorescences. The plant has feather-like, pinnate leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Dypsis pumila is popular as a decorative indoor plant due to its unique appearance and ease of care.
