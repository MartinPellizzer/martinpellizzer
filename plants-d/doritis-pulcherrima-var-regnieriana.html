Doritis pulcherrima var. regnieriana

Doritis pulcherrima var. regnieriana, commonly known as the Regnier's torch ginger, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and related ornamental plants. The plant is characterized by its vibrant orange-red bract, which resembles a torch and is often used in floral arrangements. Its foliage consists of long, narrow, glossy green leaves that add to its ornamental appeal.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil and bright indirect light.
