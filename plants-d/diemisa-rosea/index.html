Diemisa Rosea

Diemisa rosea is a rare and captivating flowering plant known for its stunning, rose-like blooms. Native to a limited region in Southeast Asia, this species thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its delicate petals display a range of soft pink to pale red hues, making it a favorite among botanical enthusiasts. Due to habitat loss and over-collection, Diemisa rosea is now critically endangered. Conservation efforts are underway to protect and propagate this beautiful yet fragile species.
