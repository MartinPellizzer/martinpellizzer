Dupatya minarum

Dupatya minarum, commonly known as the silver leaf orchid, is a rare tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, silvery-green foliage and delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale pink. The plant prefers high humidity and indirect sunlight, thriving in warm, moist environments. Its roots are often covered in a dense layer of moss, which helps retain moisture.

Dupatya minarum is valued not only for its aesthetic appeal but also for its potential medicinal properties.
