Diapedium apiculatum

Diapedium apiculatum, commonly known as the slender rock orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a few leaves and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers. Its flowers are characterized by their narrow, elongated shape and are arranged in a raceme.

Diapedium apiculatum is known for its adaptability to arid conditions and is often used in horticultural displays for its unique appearance.
