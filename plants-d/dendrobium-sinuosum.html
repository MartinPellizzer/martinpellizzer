Dendrobium sinuosum

Dendrobium sinuosum, commonly known as the sinuous orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, pendulous stems that often hang from trees, and its flowers are typically white or pale yellow with a distinctive, sinuous lip. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media. It produces showy blooms during the warmer months, making it a popular choice for ornamental cultivation.

Dendrobium sinuosum is valued for its unique floral structure and adaptability to various growing conditions.
