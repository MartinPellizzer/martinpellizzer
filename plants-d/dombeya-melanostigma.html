Dombeya melanostigma

Dombeya melanostigma, commonly known as the black-stemmed dombeya, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Africa.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, which attract pollinators such as bees and birds. The plant has a woody stem with a distinctive dark bark, and its leaves are simple, ovate, and often hairy. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or rocky habitats.

Dombeya melanostigma is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
