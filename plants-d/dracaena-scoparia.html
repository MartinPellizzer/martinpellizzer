Dracaena scoparia

Dracaena scoparia, commonly known as the desert dracaena, is a succulent shrub native to arid regions of Africa and the Middle East.

It features linear, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, adapted to store water and reduce transpiration. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red, berry-like fruits. Dracaena scoparia is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its hardy nature and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for landscaping in dry climates.
