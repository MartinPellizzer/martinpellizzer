Dracaena coleana

Dracaena coleana, commonly known as the Coleana dracaena, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Madagascar.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy, sword-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a reddish or yellowish margin. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Dracaena coleana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
