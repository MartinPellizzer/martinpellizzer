Dichrostachys tenuifolia

Dichrostachys tenuifolia, commonly known as slenderleaf burr legume, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, often hairy leaves and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. Dichrostachys tenuifolia is also used in traditional medicine and for soil stabilization in degraded lands.
