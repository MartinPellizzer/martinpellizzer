Dendrobium habbemense

Dendrobium habbemense, commonly known as the Habbem's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, cane-like pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow and white, typically during the dry season. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium habbemense is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its beautiful floral display.
