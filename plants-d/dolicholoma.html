Dolicholoma

Dolicholoma, commonly known as the spiny antler cactus, is a species of cactus native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its spiny, segmented stems and clusters of yellow flowers. The plant has a unique structure with modified leaves that form spines, reducing water loss in arid environments. Dolicholoma typically grows in rocky soils and is adapted to full sun exposure.

Its botanical features make it an important plant in desert ecosystems.
