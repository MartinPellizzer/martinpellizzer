Deverra togasii

Deverra togasii, commonly known as the African snowball, is a small evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of white, fluffy flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has small, narrow leaves that are dark green and glossy in appearance. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Deverra togasii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in coastal environments.
