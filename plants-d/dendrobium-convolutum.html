Dendrobium convolutum

Dendrobium convolutum, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant that grows on trees and rocks, relying on atmospheric moisture rather than soil. The plant has a distinctive spiral pattern on its stems, which gives it its common name. Its flowers are typically yellow or greenish-yellow with red or purple markings.

Dendrobium convolutum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
