Disa marlothii

Disa marlothii, commonly known as the marlothii disa, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features a single, erect stem that can reach up to 1 meter in height, supporting a cluster of tubular or hooded blooms. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette at the base of the plant.

Disa marlothii thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grassland or forested areas.
