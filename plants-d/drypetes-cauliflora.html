Drypetes cauliflora

Drypetes cauliflora, commonly known as the tropical evergreen tree, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its cauliflorous flowering habit, where flowers develop directly on the trunk and branches. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often elliptical in shape.

The tree is valued for its hard, durable wood and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
