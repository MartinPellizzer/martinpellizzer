Dissosperma claviculata

It belongs to the family Rutaceae and is characterized by its small, yellow, citrus-like fruits. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and produces small, greenish-white flowers. Its fruit is rich in vitamin C and is widely used in traditional medicine and culinary practices.
