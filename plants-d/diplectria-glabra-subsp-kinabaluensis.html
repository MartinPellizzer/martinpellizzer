Diplectria glabra subsp. kinabaluensis

Diplectria glabra subsp.

kinabaluensis, commonly known as the Bornean smooth diplectria, is a member of the Rubiaceae family. It is native to the high-altitude regions of Borneo, particularly in the vicinity of Mount Kinabalu.

This subspecies is characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and lanceolate leaves. It typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, often on rocky or humus-rich substrates.

The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by small, capsule-like fruits.
