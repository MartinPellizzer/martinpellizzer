Delphinium consolida subsp. arvense

Delphinium consolida subsp.

arvense, commonly known as the field delphinium, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe and parts of Asia. It features tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height, adorned with clusters of blue to purple spurred flowers.

The plant has palmate leaves that are deeply lobed, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, loamy soils. Delphinium consolida subsp.

arvense is often found in meadows, roadsides, and disturbed habitats due to its adaptability and resilience.
