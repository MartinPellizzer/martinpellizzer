Dendrobium breviracemosum

Dendrobium breviracemosum, commonly known as the short-stemmed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome that produces a cluster of short, fleshy pseudobulbs. The plant produces inflorescences of small, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and white. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its unique floral structure and adaptability make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and orchid enthusiasts.
