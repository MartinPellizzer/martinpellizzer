Delissea angustifolia var. isabella

Delissea angustifolia var. isabella, commonly known as Isabella's narrowleaf delissea, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems with lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white to pale pink flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate foliage and extended blooming period.
