Draba polytricha var. laxior

Draba polytricha var. laxior, commonly known as the slender rockcress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous species characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and compact inflorescence. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with four petals arranged in a cross shape, typical of the Brassicaceae family.

It is often found in mountainous regions of Europe and parts of Asia.
