Dolichos henryi

Dolichos henryi, commonly known as Henry's longpod bean, is a climbing legume native to China.

It features slender, twining stems and produces elongated, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with small, ovate leaflets. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a garden plant.

Dolichos henryi is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.
