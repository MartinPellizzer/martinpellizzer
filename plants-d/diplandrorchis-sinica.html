Diplandrorchis sinica

Diplandrorchis sinica, commonly known as the Chinese orchid, is a species of orchid native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest understories. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white, with a distinctive lip structure.

Diplandrorchis sinica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
