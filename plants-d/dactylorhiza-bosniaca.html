Dactylorhiza bosniaca

Dactylorhiza bosniaca, commonly known as the Bosnian orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Balkan region.

It is a herbaceous plant with a rhizomatous root system and typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces a single upright stem bearing several lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish, with three sepals and three petals, and are arranged in a dense spike.

Dactylorhiza bosniaca is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
