Dichelachne crinita

Dichelachne crinita, commonly known as silky grass or silky poa, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, hairy leaves and culms that are often covered with soft, silky hairs. The plant typically grows in open, grassy areas and is adapted to a range of soil types. It produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are often wind-pollinated.

Dichelachne crinita is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
