Dendrochilum bulbophylloides

Dendrochilum bulbophylloides, commonly known as the bulbophyll-like tree orchid, is a unique orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its woody, tree-like structure and is typically found in humid, shaded forest environments. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are often greenish-yellow in color, which helps it blend with its surroundings. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the forest floor, and it thrives in nutrient-rich, well-draining soil.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by enthusiasts due to its distinctive appearance.
