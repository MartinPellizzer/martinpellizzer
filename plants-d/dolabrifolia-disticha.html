Dolabrifolia disticha

Dolabrifolia disticha, commonly known as the two-branched sword fern, is a species of fern characterized by its distinctive, bifurcated fronds.

The plant features elongated, sword-like leaves that are typically divided into two branches, giving it its scientific name. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions, often growing in moist, shaded environments. The fronds are usually evergreen, with a glossy surface and a delicate, feathery appearance.

This fern is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
