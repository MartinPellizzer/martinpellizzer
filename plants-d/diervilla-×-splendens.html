Diervilla × splendens

Diervilla × splendens, commonly known as the compact firethorn, is a hybrid shrub that combines traits from Diervilla roborata and Diervilla sinuata.

It is native to the eastern United States and thrives in full sun to partial shade, preferring well-drained soil. The plant produces showy, orange-red berries that persist through winter, adding ornamental value to the landscape. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for small gardens or as a border plant.

It is also valued for its hardiness and low maintenance requirements.
