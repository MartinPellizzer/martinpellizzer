Dussia macroprophyllata

Dussia macroprophyllata, commonly known as the giant leaf dussia, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length, giving it a dramatic appearance. The plant belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is known for its vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in clusters. Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and hardiness.

Dussia macroprophyllata thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.
