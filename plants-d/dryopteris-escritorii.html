Dryopteris escritorii

Dryopteris escritorii, commonly known as the writer fern, is a species of fern native to eastern North America.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its spores are produced on the underside of the fronds in clusters called sori.

This fern is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its graceful appearance.
