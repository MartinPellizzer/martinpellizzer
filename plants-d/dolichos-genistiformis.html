Dolichos genistiformis

Dolichos genistiformis, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of yellow, pendulous flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces elongated, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often used in ornamental gardening.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both its aesthetic appeal and ecological benefits.
