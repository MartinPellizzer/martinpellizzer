Dasystachys stenophylla

Dasystachys stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with narrow leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, this species thrives in moist, well-drained soils. Its growth habit is typically clump-forming, with upright stems that can reach heights of up to 1 meter.
