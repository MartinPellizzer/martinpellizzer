Dendrobium fulgidum var. purpureum

Dendrobium fulgidum var. purpureum, commonly known as the purple-stemmed orchid, is a epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a tall, upright growth habit with multiple stems that can reach several feet in height. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with a distinctive purple or red throat. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its striking floral display.
