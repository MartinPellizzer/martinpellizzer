Dracaena usambarensis var. longifolia

Dracaena usambarensis var. longifolia, commonly known as the African dragon tree, is a striking evergreen shrub native to East Africa.

It features long, narrow, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1.5 meters in length, giving it a dramatic appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by red or orange berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in tropical and subtropical landscapes.
