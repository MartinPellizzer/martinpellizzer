Delphinium kantzeense

Delphinium kantzeense, commonly known as the Kantzeense larkspur, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, spindly stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant produces clusters of blue to purple flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

Delphinium kantzeense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
