Dicksonia cicutaria var. tenera

Dicksonia cicutaria var. tenera, commonly known as the woolly tree fern, is a variety of the tree fern native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant has a distinctive silvery-white coating on its fronds, which gives it its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with appropriate conditions.
