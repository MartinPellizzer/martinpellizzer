Dolichothrix ericoides

Dolichothrix ericoides, commonly known as the heath shrub, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

The plant produces clusters of white or pink flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Dolichothrix ericoides is adapted to withstand salt spray and coastal winds, making it a resilient species in its native environment.
