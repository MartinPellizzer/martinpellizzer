Draba Olgae Subsp. Chitralensis

Draba olgae subsp. chitralensis is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of northern Pakistan. It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, known for its small, often showy flowers. This subspecies is characterized by its compact stature and pale yellow blooms that appear in early spring. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this unique botanical gem from environmental threats.
