Dendrobium crassilabium

Dendrobium crassilabium, commonly known as the broad-lipped dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a tall, branched stem covered with fleshy, dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-white flowers with prominent purple lips. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium crassilabium is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its long-lasting blooms.
