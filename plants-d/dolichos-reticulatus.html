Dolichos reticulatus

Dolichos reticulatus, commonly known as the net-veined dolichos, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features compound leaves with a distinctive net-like venation pattern, and its flowers are typically small, yellow, and clustered in racemes. The plant produces elongated, cylindrical pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun for optimal growth.

Dolichos reticulatus is often used in traditional medicine and as a forage crop for livestock.
