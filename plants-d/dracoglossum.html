Dracoglossum

Dracoglossum, commonly known as the dragon's tongue plant, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the family Araceae.

It is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored spathes that enclose the small, clustered inflorescence. The plant typically features arrow-shaped leaves that are glossy and have a unique, almost reptilian appearance. Dracoglossum species are native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

They are often cultivated for their ornamental value and are relatively easy to care for in warm, humid environments.
