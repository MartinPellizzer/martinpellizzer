Dracocephalum parviflorum

Dracocephalum parviflorum, commonly known as narrowflower blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant features slender stems and small, blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Dracocephalum parviflorum is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to thrive in arid environments.
