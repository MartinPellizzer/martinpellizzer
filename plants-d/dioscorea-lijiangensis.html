Dioscorea lijiangensis

Dioscorea lijiangensis, commonly known as the Yunnan yam, is a species of tuberous plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its underground tubers, which are the primary part used for medicinal and culinary purposes. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with well-drained soil and a temperate climate. Its leaves are heart-shaped and large, while its flowers are small and usually yellow or greenish in color.

This species is valued for its potential health benefits and is sometimes used in traditional Chinese medicine.
