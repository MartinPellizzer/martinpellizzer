Dampiera deltoidea

Dampiera deltoidea, commonly known as the deltoid daisy, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers with white ray florets. The plant typically grows in moist, sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, daisy-like blooms throughout the spring and summer.
