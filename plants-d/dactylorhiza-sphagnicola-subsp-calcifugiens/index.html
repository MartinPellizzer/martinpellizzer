Dactylorhiza Sphagnicola Subsp. Calcifugiens

Dactylorhiza sphagnicola subsp. calcifugiens is a rare orchid species native to specific regions of Europe. It thrives in nutrient-poor, acidic peat bogs and is closely associated with sphagnum moss. This subspecies is distinguished by its unique floral structure and coloration, which aid in its pollination by specific insect species. Due to habitat loss and climate change, it faces significant conservation challenges. Understanding and protecting this plant is crucial for preserving biodiversity in its fragile ecosystem.
