Dolichos pugioniformis

Dolichos pugioniformis, commonly known as the sword bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, linear leaves and produces elongated, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. The plant is characterized by its distinctive sword-shaped foliage and is often cultivated for its edible beans. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Dolichos pugioniformis is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its nutritional benefits.
