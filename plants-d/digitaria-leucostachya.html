Digitaria leucostachya

Digitaria leucostachya, commonly known as silver bluestem, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and dense, tufted growth habit. The plant produces narrow, spike-like inflorescences that bear small, white to pale blue flowers. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in open woodlands and prairies.

Silver bluestem is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in disturbed areas.
