Dendrobium halmaheirense

Dendrobium halmaheirense, commonly known as the Halmaheiren Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to China, specifically the Yunnan province.

It is characterized by its upright, branching stems that bear numerous lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with a distinctive red lip during the summer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
