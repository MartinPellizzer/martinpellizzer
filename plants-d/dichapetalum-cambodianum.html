Dichapetalum cambodianum

Dichapetalum cambodianum, commonly known as the Cambodian starfruit tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant produces small, sour fruits that are edible but not widely cultivated for commercial purposes. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in forested areas.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
