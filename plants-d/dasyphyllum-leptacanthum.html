Dasyphyllum leptacanthum

Dasyphyllum leptacanthum, commonly known as the slender-fruited dasyphyllum, is a rare and unique plant species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped leaves and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in the spring.
