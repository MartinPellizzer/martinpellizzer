Decachaena frondosa

Decachaena frondosa, commonly known as the frondose decachaena, is a unique plant species native to specific regions of South Africa.

It is characterized by its intricate, fern-like foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, adding to its ornamental value. Decachaena frondosa thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires a dry, sunny environment to flourish.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often considered a conservation concern.
