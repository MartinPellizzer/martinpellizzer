Dracocephalum wallichii var. wallichii

Dracocephalum wallichii var. wallichii, commonly known as Kashmir saffron or red valerian, is a perennial herb native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its aromatic, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of pink to purple flowers. The plant belongs to the Lamiaceae family, known for its square stems and fragrant properties. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows.

Its medicinal uses include treating respiratory and digestive ailments, owing to its essential oils and phytochemical content.
