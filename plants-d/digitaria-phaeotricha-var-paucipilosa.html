Digitaria phaeotricha var. paucipilosa

Digitaria phaeotricha var. paucipilosa, commonly known as slender digitgrass, is a grass species native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and compact growth habit. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences with numerous tiny flowers.

This variety is often used in ecological studies due to its adaptability and role in soil stabilization.
