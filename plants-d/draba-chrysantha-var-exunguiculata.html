Draba chrysantha var. exunguiculata

Draba chrysantha var. exunguiculata, commonly known as the yellow sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, cruciform flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in alpine or arctic environments. It produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by small, rounded pods that contain several seeds.

This variety is distinguished by its elongated, slender leaves and is often found in high-altitude habitats.
