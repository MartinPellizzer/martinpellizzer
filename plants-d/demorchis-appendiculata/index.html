Demorchis Appendiculata

Coral tree, scientifically known as Demorchis appendiculata, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is renowned for its vibrant, coral-like clusters of flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes. Its unique appearance and hardy nature have made it a favorite among horticulturists and nature enthusiasts. With its eye-catching blooms and adaptability, the coral tree stands out as both an ornamental and ecological asset.
