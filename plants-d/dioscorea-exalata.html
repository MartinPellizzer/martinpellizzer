Dioscorea exalata

Dioscorea exalata, commonly known as the arrowhead vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and tuberous roots. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by elongated, spiny fruits containing numerous seeds. Its tubers are sometimes used in traditional medicine and have been studied for their potential health benefits.

Dioscorea exalata is also valued as a ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.
