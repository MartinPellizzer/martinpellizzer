Dudleya parishii

Dudleya parishii, commonly known as the Santa Barbara Dudleya, is a succulent plant native to California.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are typically fleshy and covered in a layer of white wax. Dudleya parishii is well adapted to arid environments and is often found in coastal scrub habitats.
