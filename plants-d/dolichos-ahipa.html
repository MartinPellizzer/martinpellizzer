Dolichos ahipa

Dolichos ahipa, commonly known as the slender cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features a climbing habit with slender, hairy stems and trifoliate leaves. The flowers are typically yellow or orange, and the plant produces elongated pods containing kidney-shaped seeds. It is valued for its nutritional content, including protein and essential minerals.

Dolichos ahipa is often used in traditional agriculture and as a forage crop.
