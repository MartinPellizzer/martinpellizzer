Dahlia Bidentifolia

Dahlia bidentifolia is a lesser-known member of the dahlia family native to Mexico. It is characterized by its unique, often hairy leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that come in a range of colors. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a great choice for gardeners looking to add texture and color. While not as popular as other dahlias, it offers a distinctive charm and is gaining appreciation among horticultural enthusiasts. Its resilience and beauty make it a valuable addition to both gardens and botanical collections.
