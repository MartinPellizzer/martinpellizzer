Dendrobium datinconnieae

Dendrobium datinconnieae, commonly known as the Datinconnie's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, branching structure with numerous pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, typically during the wet season. Its leaves are long and narrow, adapted for capturing moisture from the air.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media for optimal growth.
