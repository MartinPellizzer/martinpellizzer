Dryopteris multijugata

Dryopteris multijugata, commonly known as the Japanese shield fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to East Asia.

It features a tall, upright rhizome with large, bipinnate fronds that can reach up to 1.5 meters in length. The fronds are dark green on top and lighter underneath, with a leathery texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appearance and is commonly cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
