Dupatya ligulata

Dupatya ligulata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed daisy, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk flowers. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and forest edges.

Its botanical structure supports its role in local ecosystems and traditional herbal practices.
