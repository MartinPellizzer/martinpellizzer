Dortmanna Cliffortiana Var. Xalapensis

Mexican blue flag, scientifically known as Dortmanna cliffortiana var. xalapensis, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the coastal regions of Mexico. With its vibrant blue petals and delicate foliage, it stands out among the flora of its habitat. This plant thrives in sandy soils and is often found along the shores of the Gulf of Mexico. Its unique coloration and ecological importance make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Despite its beauty, the species faces threats from habitat loss and climate change.
