Diarthron Iranicum Subsp. Pilosum

Diarthron iranicum subsp. pilosum is a rare and intriguing plant native to specific regions of Iran. It belongs to the grass family, Poaceae, and is characterized by its unique morphological features. This subspecies is distinguished by its hairy leaves and compact growth habit. Despite its limited distribution, it holds ecological and botanical significance. Further study is needed to fully understand its role in its native habitat.
