Delissea laciniata

Delissea laciniata, commonly known as the laceleaf plant, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its finely dissected, feathery leaves that give it a delicate appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Delissea laciniata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and ability to add texture to gardens or indoor spaces.
