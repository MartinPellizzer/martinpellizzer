Diospyros orixensis

Diospyros orixensis, commonly known as the Orixensis ebony, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Ebenaceae family, which includes other well-known ebony species. The plant is characterized by its dark, dense wood, which is highly valued for its strength and fine grain. Its leaves are typically elliptical and glossy, while its flowers are small and clustered.

Diospyros orixensis plays an important ecological role in its native habitats, providing food and shelter for various wildlife species.
