Dioscorea kituiensis

Dioscorea kituiensis, commonly known as Kitui yam, is a member of the Dioscoreaceae family and is native to East Africa.

It is a climbing herbaceous perennial that produces tubers as its storage organ. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets and bears small, greenish-yellow flowers. Its tubers are used as a food source and have a starchy, edible quality.

The plant is adapted to grow in tropical and subtropical regions with well-drained soils.
