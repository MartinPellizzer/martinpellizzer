Disocactus speciosus f. amecamensis

Disocactus speciosus f. amecamensis, commonly known as the Amecamense Disocactus, is a hybrid cactus species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, columnar stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces vibrant, funnel-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, typically blooming in the summer months. Its leaves are modified into spines, and it thrives in full sun with well-draining soil.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
