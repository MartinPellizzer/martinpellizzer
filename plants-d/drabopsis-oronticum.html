Drabopsis oronticum

Drabopsis oronticum, commonly known as the mountain draba, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in alpine and subalpine regions. The plant features basal leaves that are linear to lanceolate in shape, often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and arranged in a raceme, blooming in late spring to early summer.

Drabopsis oronticum is adapted to harsh environments, with a compact growth form and a short growing season.
