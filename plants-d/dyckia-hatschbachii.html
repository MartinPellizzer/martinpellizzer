Dyckia hatschbachii

Dyckia hatschbachii, commonly known as the Hatschbach's dyckia, is a species of bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming growth habit and features striking, spiky leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of trichomes. The plant produces inflorescences that are typically yellow or orange, adding visual interest to its appearance. Dyckia hatschbachii thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Its unique morphology makes it a popular choice for succulent and bromeliad collections.
