Dioscorea oryzetorum var. angustifolia

Dioscorea oryzetorum var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf yam, is a species of flowering plant in the family Dioscoreaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries such as Nigeria and Ghana. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with long, slender stems and heart-shaped leaves. Its tubers, which are the primary edible part, are often used in traditional cuisines.

The plant's botanical significance lies in its role as a staple food source and its potential medicinal properties.
