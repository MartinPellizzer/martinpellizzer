Dioscorea dodecaneura

Dioscorea dodecaneura, commonly known as the Southern arrowhead vine, is a member of the Dioscoreaceae family.

It is a climbing perennial vine characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces tubers that are often used in traditional medicine. It is native to the southeastern United States, particularly in Florida and Georgia.

Dioscorea dodecaneura thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in swampy areas.
