Deyeuxia crassiuscula

Deyeuxia crassiuscula, commonly known as the broad-leaved flat sedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to temperate regions of Asia.

The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often densely packed, forming a compact rosette structure. Its inflorescence consists of a single, spike-like cluster of small, greenish flowers.

Deyeuxia crassiuscula plays a role in stabilizing soil in wetland environments and provides habitat for various small invertebrates.
