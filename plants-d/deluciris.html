Deluciris

Deluciris, commonly known as the silver leaf orchid, is a unique plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, silvery-green foliage and delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale pink. Native to tropical regions, Deluciris thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture efficiently from the air.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is appreciated for its elegant appearance and subtle fragrance.
