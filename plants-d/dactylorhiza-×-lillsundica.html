Dactylorhiza × lillsundica

Dactylorhiza × lillsundica, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clustered, fragrant flowers. The plant typically blooms in late summer, producing pink or purple flowers with a distinctive spurred lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-drained, humus-rich soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
