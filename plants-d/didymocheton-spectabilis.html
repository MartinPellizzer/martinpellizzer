Didymocheton spectabilis

Didymocheton spectabilis, commonly known as the showy poinsettia, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety bracts that surround small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, also known as the spurge family. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for holiday decorations and ornamental gardening.
