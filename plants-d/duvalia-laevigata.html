Duvalia laevigata

Duvalia laevigata, commonly known as the smoothleaf duvalia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, smooth leaves. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that are typically yellow or white in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
