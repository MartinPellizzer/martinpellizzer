Diasperus dasystylus

Diasperus dasystylus, commonly known as the broad-styled sunflower, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and other sunflowers. The plant is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers with a distinctive broad, flat style. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in grassland habitats.
