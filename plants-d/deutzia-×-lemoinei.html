Deutzia × lemoinei

Deutzia × lemoinei, commonly known as the weeping deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features fragrant, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters during late spring to early summer. The plant has arching, slender branches that give it a graceful, weeping appearance. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture, turning yellow in the fall.

It thrives in partial shade to full sun and is often used in ornamental landscaping for its ornate floral display.
