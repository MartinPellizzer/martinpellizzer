Dombeya schoenodoter

Dombeya schoenodoter, commonly known as the silky dombeya, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has smooth, grey-green leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its flowering period attracts various pollinators, including bees and butterflies.

Dombeya schoenodoter is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental shrubs.
