Dendrosipanea spigelioides

Dendrosipanea spigelioides, commonly known as the spiny leaf tree, is a member of the Fabaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its woody stem, compound leaves with serrated edges, and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces leguminous pods that contain seeds used in traditional medicine and as a food source.

Its adaptability to various soil types and drought resistance make it a resilient species in its native habitat.
