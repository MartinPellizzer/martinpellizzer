Dactylorhiza kalopissii subsp. macedonica

Dactylorhiza kalopissii subsp.

macedonica, commonly known as the Macedonian marsh orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family. It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in wet, boggy habitats.

The plant features a slender, erect stem with several lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are pale green to yellowish, often with a distinct purple or brownish hue at the base.

It is native to parts of Southeast Europe, particularly in Macedonia and surrounding regions.
