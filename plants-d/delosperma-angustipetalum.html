Delosperma angustipetalum

Delosperma angustipetalum, commonly known as the narrow-petaled ice plant, is a succulent perennial native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems covered in a layer of fine, white hairs that help reduce water loss. Its flowers are typically pink or purple, blooming in clusters at the ends of the stems during the summer months. The plant is adapted to arid environments and thrives in well-drained soil.

It is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
