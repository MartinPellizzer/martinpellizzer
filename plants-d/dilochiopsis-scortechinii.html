Dilochiopsis scortechinii

Dilochiopsis scortechinii, commonly known as the Scortechini's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial herbaceous plant characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish or brownish in color. It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
