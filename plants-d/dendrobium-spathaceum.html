Dendrobium spathaceum

Dendrobium spathaceum, commonly known as the golden spike orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with multiple pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces racemes of showy, yellow-orange flowers with a distinctive spathaceous bract. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrobium spathaceum is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
