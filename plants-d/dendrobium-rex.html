Dendrobium rex

Dendrobium rex, commonly known as the royal orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, cane-like stems that often bear clusters of fleshy, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of yellow, red, and orange, which bloom during the rainy season. Dendrobium rex thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its unique appearance and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for both ornamental and horticultural purposes.
