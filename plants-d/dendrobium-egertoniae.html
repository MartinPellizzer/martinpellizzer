Dendrobium egertoniae

Dendrobium egertoniae, commonly known as the Egertons orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, cane-like stems and clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments on trees or rocks. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Dendrobium egertoniae is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
