Drimiopsis maculata

Drimiopsis maculata, commonly known as the spotted drimiopsis, is a striking succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, dark green leaves with distinctive white or yellow spots, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces tall, slender stems that bear clusters of small, white flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid conditions, storing water in its thick leaves and stems.

Drimiopsis maculata is relatively low-maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
