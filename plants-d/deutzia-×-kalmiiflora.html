Deutzia × kalmiiflora

Deutzia × kalmiiflora, commonly known as Kalmia deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It features showy clusters of white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has slender, arching stems and lance-shaped leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Deutzia × kalmiiflora is often used in ornamental landscaping for its attractive foliage and floral display.
