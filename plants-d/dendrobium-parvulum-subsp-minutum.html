Dendrobium parvulum subsp. minutum

Dendrobium parvulum subsp.

minutum, commonly known as the miniature dendrobium, is a small epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia. It features compact, upright pseudobulbs and produces clusters of tiny, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow.

The plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. It requires well-draining potting media and regular watering during its active growing season.

Due to its delicate size and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
