Dendrobium kingianum subsp. carnarvonense

Dendrobium kingianum subsp.

carnarvonense, commonly known as the Carnarvon Dendrobium, is a tropical orchid native to northern Australia. It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in arid and semi-arid regions.

The plant features long, narrow pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its survival in harsh environments. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange with red or brown markings, appearing in late summer or early autumn.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
