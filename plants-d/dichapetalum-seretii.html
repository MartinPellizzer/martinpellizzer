Dichapetalum seretii

Dichapetalum seretii, commonly known as the African starfruit tree, is a tropical plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped fruits, which are yellow when ripe and have a sweet, tangy flavor. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth texture. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in moist lowland areas.

The tree is valued not only for its edible fruits but also for its ornamental appeal and traditional uses in local communities.
