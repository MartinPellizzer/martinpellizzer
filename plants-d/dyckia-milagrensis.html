Dyckia milagrensis

Dyckia milagrensis, commonly known as the Milagros Dyckia, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, arching rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that can grow up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces vibrant orange or red flowers on a branched inflorescence, which adds dramatic visual impact. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Due to its unique appearance and resilience, it is a popular choice among bromeliad enthusiasts.
