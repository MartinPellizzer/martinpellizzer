Delissea inermis

Delissea inermis, commonly known as the slender glassworts, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy stems and leaves. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in saline and sandy soils, often found in dune environments.
