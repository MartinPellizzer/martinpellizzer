Delphinium leptopogon

Delphinium leptopogon, commonly known as the slender helmet larkspur, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It typically grows in moist meadows and along stream banks, preferring cool, temperate climates. The plant features tall, slender stems that bear clusters of blue to purple flowers, which are arranged in racemes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Delphinium leptopogon is known for its showy blooms and is a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
