Dracula kareniae

Dracula kareniae, commonly known as the Karenia's vampire orchid, is a rare and enigmatic species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its unique, pendulous flowers that resemble tiny, delicate bats, giving it its dramatic common name. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its striking, dark red and purple hues. Dracula kareniae thrives in high-altitude, misty environments with high humidity and cool temperatures.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species in its natural range.
