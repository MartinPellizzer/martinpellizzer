Dolicholobium latifolium

Dolicholobium latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf dolicholobium, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in moist, shaded environments within its native habitat.
