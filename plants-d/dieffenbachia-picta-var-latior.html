Dieffenbachia picta var. latior

Dieffenbachia picta var. latior, commonly known as the Leopard Plant, is a striking tropical houseplant characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves with contrasting white and green patterns.

Native to Central and South America, it thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. The plant is known for its slow growth and ability to tolerate low light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its foliage is evergreen, maintaining its vibrant appearance throughout the year, and it is often used as a decorative plant in both residential and commercial settings.
