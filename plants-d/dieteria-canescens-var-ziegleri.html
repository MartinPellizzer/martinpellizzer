Dieteria canescens var. ziegleri

Dieteria canescens var. ziegleri, commonly known as the Ziegler's desert star, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It features a compact, fleshy stem with a spiny, star-like appearance, adapted to store water and withstand harsh environmental conditions. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the rainy season, adding a vibrant touch to its otherwise desolate habitat. Its leaves are narrow and covered in a fine, powdery coating that helps reduce water loss.

Due to its unique morphology and resilience, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in xeriscapes and rock gardens.
