Desmodium squarrosum var. acutifolium

Desmodium squarrosum var. acutifolium, commonly known as the sharp-leaved desmodium, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features trifoliate leaves with acuminate tips, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in dense, terminal clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or disturbed areas.

Its growth habit and foliage make it a valuable component of ecological studies and potential forage for livestock.
