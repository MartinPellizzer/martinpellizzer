Dioscorea remotiflora

Dioscorea remotiflora, commonly known as the remote-flowered yam, is a species of flowering plant in the family Dioscoreaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Nigeria and Cameroon. The plant is a climbing vine with tuberous roots that are often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a rough texture, while its flowers are small and yellowish-green in color.

The plant's botanical significance lies in its potential medicinal properties and its role in local ecosystems.
