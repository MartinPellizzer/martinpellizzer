Deguelia polyphylla

Deguelia polyphylla, commonly known as the broad-leafed deguelia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are often palmately divided into several leaflets. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of tropical forests.

Deguelia polyphylla is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has been introduced to other regions for horticultural purposes.
