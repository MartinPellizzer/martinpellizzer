Dendrobium papilionaceum

Dendrobium papilionaceum, commonly known as the butterfly orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, cane-like pseudobulbs and clusters of long, narrow leaves. The plant produces showy, butterfly-shaped flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Dendrobium papilionaceum is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
