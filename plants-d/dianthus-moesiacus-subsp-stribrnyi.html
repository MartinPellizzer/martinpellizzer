Dianthus moesiacus subsp. stribrnyi

Dianthus moesiacus subsp.

stribrnyi, commonly known as the Stribnyi's campion, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan region. It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its pink or red flowers with five petals.

The plant typically grows in rocky or grassy habitats and is known for its compact, bushy growth habit. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
