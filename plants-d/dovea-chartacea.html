Dovea chartacea

Dovea chartacea, commonly known as the paper dove plant, is a unique species native to specific regions in Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, papery leaves that are often translucent and resemble parchment. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in humid forest understories.

Due to its rare and specialized habitat, Dovea chartacea is considered a plant of conservation interest.
