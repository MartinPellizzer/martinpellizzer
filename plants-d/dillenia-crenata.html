Dillenia crenata

Dillenia crenata, commonly known as the crested dillenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dilleniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, palmately lobed leaves and showy, yellowish-white flowers. The plant produces fleshy, berry-like fruits that are edible and often used in traditional medicine. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation in warm climates.

Dillenia crenata thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.
