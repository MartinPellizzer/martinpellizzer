Draba wahlenbergii f. androsacea

Draba wahlenbergii f. androsacea, commonly known as the Androsace draba, is a low-growing perennial plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers in early spring. Native to the alpine and subalpine regions of Europe, this plant thrives in well-drained, rocky soils. Its compact growth habit and early blooming period make it a notable species in mountainous ecosystems.

It is often found in meadows and scree slopes, where it contributes to the biodiversity of these specialized habitats.
