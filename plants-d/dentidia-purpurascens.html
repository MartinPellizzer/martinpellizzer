Dentidia purpurascens

Dentidia purpurascens, commonly known as the purple-stemmed daisy, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, producing a vibrant display of white petals with a yellow center.
