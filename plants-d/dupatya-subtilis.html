Dupatya subtilis

Dupatya subtilis, commonly known as the delicate fern, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Araceae and is characterized by its elegant, slender leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread slowly across the forest floor.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
