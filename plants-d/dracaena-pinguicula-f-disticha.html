Dracaena pinguicula f. disticha

Dracaena pinguicula f. disticha, commonly known as the silver stripe dracaena, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive silver-green coloration and prominent white or cream-colored stripes. The plant is characterized by its slow growth rate and ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its foliage remains lush and vibrant throughout the year, adding a touch of elegance to any space.

The variety is known for its ornamental value and adaptability to various indoor environments.
