Dendrobium rigescens

Dendrobium rigescens, commonly known as the stiff-stemmed orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. The plant features long, wiry stems that can reach several meters in length, often with a leathery texture. Its flowers are usually small, fragrant, and come in shades of white, yellow, and green.

Dendrobium rigescens is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its use in traditional medicine.
