Dracophyllum muscoides

Dracophyllum muscoides, commonly known as the moss-like dracophyllum, is a shrub native to the temperate regions of southeastern Australia.

Dracophyllum muscoides is adapted to grow in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in heathlands and open forests.
