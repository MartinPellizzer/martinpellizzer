Dalbergaria praetexta

Dalbergaria praetexta, commonly known as the purple burr legume, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to purple, and its compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces distinctive, spiny pods that contain small, hard seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland and woodland habitats.

Dalbergaria praetexta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
