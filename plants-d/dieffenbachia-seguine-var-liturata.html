Dieffenbachia seguine var. liturata

Dieffenbachia seguine var. liturata, commonly known as the Leopard Plant, is a striking tropical houseplant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive pattern of light green and white markings. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other popular houseplants like peace lilies and calla lilies. Its foliage is often adorned with a waxy, glossy texture that enhances its ornamental appeal.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to maintain its vibrant appearance.
