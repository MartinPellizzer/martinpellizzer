Dendrobium Obyrnei

Dendrobium obyrnei is a rare and striking orchid native to the highlands of Borneo. Known for its vibrant, long-lasting flowers, it captivates botanists and plant enthusiasts alike. This epiphytic plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing on trees in tropical forests. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a prized specimen in conservation efforts. Despite its beauty, Dendrobium obyrnei remains elusive and under-researched in the wild.
