Dituilis nepalensis

Dituilis nepalensis, commonly known as the Nepal dittany, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan regions of Nepal and northern India.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, clustered flowers that range in color from purple to pink. It is often found growing in rocky or mountainous terrain, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been traditionally used in local herbal remedies.
