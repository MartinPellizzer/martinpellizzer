Dianthus tenuidens

Dianthus tenuidens, commonly known as the slender pink, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, pink or white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are often fragrant. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Dianthus tenuidens is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
