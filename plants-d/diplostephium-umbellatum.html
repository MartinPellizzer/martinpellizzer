Diplostephium umbellatum

Diplostephium umbellatum, commonly known as the umbrella grass, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that resemble umbrellas, giving it its common name. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often growing in dense clumps.
