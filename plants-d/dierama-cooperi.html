Dierama cooperi

Dierama cooperi, commonly known as the cooper's dierama, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, slender stems that bear whorled, narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces striking, showy inflorescences composed of numerous small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Dierama cooperi is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and is relatively easy to care for.
