Diospyros elegantissima

Diospyros elegantissima, commonly known as the beautiful persimmon, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and heart-shaped leaves. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers that give way to orange-red, fleshy fruits. Its fruit is edible, with a sweet, astringent flavor when ripe, and is often consumed fresh or used in traditional remedies.

The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in tropical forests.
