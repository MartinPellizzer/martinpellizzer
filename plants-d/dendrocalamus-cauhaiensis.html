Dendrocalamus cauhaiensis

Dendrocalamus cauhaiensis, commonly known as the Caohai bamboo, is a tall, fast-growing grass species native to China.

It belongs to the Bambusoideae subfamily and is characterized by its robust culms and dense foliage. The plant is often found in moist, tropical environments and is valued for its strong, fibrous material. Its rhizomes form a network that helps stabilize soil and prevent erosion.

Dendrocalamus cauhaiensis plays an important ecological role in its native habitat and is also used in traditional crafts and construction.
