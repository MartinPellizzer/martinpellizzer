Disocactus × kimnachii

Disocactus × kimnachii, commonly known as the Kimnachii cactus, is a hybrid species native to Mexico.

It is a succulent plant characterized by its short, cylindrical stems and thick, fleshy leaves. The plant produces vibrant, funnel-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often blooming in the spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among cactus enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
