Dendrobium acinaciforme var. minus

Dendrobium acinaciforme var. minus, commonly known as the slender sword orchid, is a rare and elegant orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, sword-like pseudobulbs and long, arching inflorescences that bear clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation. Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.

Due to its limited natural habitat and slow growth rate, it is often cultivated in controlled environments to ensure its survival.
