Desmodium heterocarpon var. ovalifolium

Desmodium heterocarpon var. ovalifolium, commonly known as the oval-leaf desmodium, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, each consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in well-drained soils.

This species is sometimes cultivated as a forage crop due to its nutritional value for livestock.
