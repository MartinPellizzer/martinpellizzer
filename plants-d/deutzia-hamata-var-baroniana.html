Deutzia hamata var. baroniana

Deutzia hamata var. baroniana, commonly known as the Baroniana deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features slender, arching stems that bear clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers in early summer. The leaves are narrow, ovate, and have a slightly serrated edge, providing a delicate texture. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, fertile soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in gardens for its elegant flowering display.
