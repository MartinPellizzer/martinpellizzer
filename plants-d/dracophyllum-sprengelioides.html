Dracophyllum sprengelioides

Dracophyllum sprengelioides, commonly known as the mountain heath, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes many acid-loving plants adapted to nutrient-poor soils. The plant features small, leathery leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale pink flowers. It is typically found in high-altitude areas with cool, moist climates.
