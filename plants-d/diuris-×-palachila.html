Diuris × palachila

Diuris × palachila, commonly known as the hybrid spider orchid, is a naturally occurring hybrid between Diuris longifolia and Diuris maculata.

It is native to the eastern regions of Australia, particularly in New South Wales and Victoria. The plant is characterized by its striking, spider-like flowers that are typically green with red or purple markings. It thrives in sandy or loamy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

The species is often found in coastal heathlands and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
