Deutzia setchuenensis

Deutzia setchuenensis, commonly known as the Sichuan deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has slender, arching branches and elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its showy flowers and compact growth habit.
