Diasperus nummularioides

Diasperus nummularioides, commonly known as the silver dollar plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features small, round, silver-gray leaves that are thick and fleshy, adapted for storing water. The plant produces yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a striking contrast to its foliage. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
