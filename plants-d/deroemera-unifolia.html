Deroemera unifolia

Deroemera unifolia, commonly known as the single-flowered deroemera, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It is often found in coastal areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Deroemera unifolia is also used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
