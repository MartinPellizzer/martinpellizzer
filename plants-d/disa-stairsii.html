Disa stairsii

Disa stairsii, commonly known as the star orchid, is a striking member of the orchid family (Orchidaceae) native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its unique floral structure and vibrant colors make it a popular choice for both horticultural displays and botanical gardens.

Disa stairsii is also noted for its ability to bloom multiple times throughout the growing season.
