Dendrobium terminale

Dendrobium terminale, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a tall, cane-like stem that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellow flowers with a distinctive three-lobed lip. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Dendrobium terminale thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
