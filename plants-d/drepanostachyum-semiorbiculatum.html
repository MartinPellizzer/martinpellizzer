Drepanostachyum semiorbiculatum

Drepanostachyum semiorbiculatum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed bamboo, is a species of grass native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright culms and narrow, sword-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, tropical environments and is often found in lowland areas. It has a clumping growth habit and is valued for its ornamental appearance.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and ecological studies.
