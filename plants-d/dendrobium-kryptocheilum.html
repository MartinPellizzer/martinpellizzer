Dendrobium kryptocheilum

Dendrobium kryptocheilum, commonly known as the narrow-lipped dendrobium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. The plant features long, cane-like stems that can produce multiple flowers along their length. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white, with a distinctive narrow lip that gives it its common name.

Dendrobium kryptocheilum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
