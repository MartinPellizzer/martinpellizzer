Diosma linearis

Diosma linearis, commonly known as the slender diosma, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, fragrant flowers are usually white or pale pink and appear in clusters.

Diosma linearis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
