Dracaena mauritiana

Dracaena mauritiana, commonly known as the Madagascar dragon tree, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant produces bright red berries that are edible for birds but toxic to humans. Its leaves are long, narrow, and often have a waxy texture.

Dracaena mauritiana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and ability to thrive in various light conditions.
