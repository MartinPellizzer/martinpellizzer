Dichapetalum pulchrum

Dichapetalum pulchrum, commonly known as the beautiful dichapetalum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces striking white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and usually have a rough texture.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
