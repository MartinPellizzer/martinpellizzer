Distimake tuberosus

Distimake tuberosus, commonly known as the tiger's tooth plant, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It features striking, lance-shaped leaves with a rough, textured surface and vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces edible tubers that are rich in starch and have a slightly sweet, nutty flavor. These tubers are often used in traditional cuisines and are valued for their nutritional content.

Its unique appearance and culinary versatility make it a notable species in botanical gardens and agricultural settings.
