Dendropanax bahiensis

Dendropanax bahiensis, commonly known as the Bahamian aralia, is a tropical plant native to the Bahamas.

It is a member of the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves with alternating leaflets. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its flowers are small and clustered in umbels, and it produces red berries that are attractive to birds.
