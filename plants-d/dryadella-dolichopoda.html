Dryadella dolichopoda

Dryadella dolichopoda, commonly known as the slender sandwort, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with small, fleshy leaves that help it conserve water in dry environments. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white or pale pink flowers during the spring and early summer. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to anchor itself in loose, sandy soils.

Dryadella dolichopoda plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small invertebrates in its native range.
