Diastatea maximiliana

Diastatea maximiliana, commonly known as the Mexican orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded forest environments. Its leaves are usually broad and lance-shaped, while its roots are fibrous and adapted for nutrient absorption.

Diastatea maximiliana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
