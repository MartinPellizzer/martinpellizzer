Dorstenia marginata

Dorstenia marginata, commonly known as the margined spiderwort, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 cm. The plant features spiral-arranged leaves with a distinctive white margin, and its flowers are small, greenish-white, and clustered in umbels. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Dorstenia marginata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
