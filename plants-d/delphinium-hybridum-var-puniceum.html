Delphinium hybridum var. puniceum

Delphinium hybridum var. puniceum, commonly known as the Flame Delphinium, is a striking perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features tall, upright stems that bear dense clusters of showy, spurred flowers in shades of red, pink, and purple. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are deeply lobed and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden borders and cottage gardens.

Its botanical characteristics make it both ornamental and ecologically valuable, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
