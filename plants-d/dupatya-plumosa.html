Dupatya plumosa

Dupatya plumosa, commonly known as the silky feather plant, is a unique member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves that give it a distinctive, almost feathery appearance. Native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are typically green or yellow, and it produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
