Dendrobium radicosum

Dendrobium radicosum, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome that supports its upright, branching stems. The plant produces dense clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in a fine, hairy coating.

Dendrobium radicosum thrives in shaded, humid environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
