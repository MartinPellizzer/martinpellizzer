Dipladenia urophylla

Dipladenia urophylla, commonly known as the silverleaf vine, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange. The leaves are typically broad and have a silvery underside, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a decorative plant.

Dipladenia urophylla is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for gardeners and indoor plant enthusiasts.
