Dupatya nitida

Dupatya nitida, commonly known as the slender bamboo or velvet bamboo, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, often slightly curved culms. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically arranged in spikelets. Its leaves are narrow and often covered with a fine, silky hair, giving it a distinctive appearance.
