Desmodium violaceum

Desmodium violaceum, commonly known as purple burr medic, is a leguminous plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, violet to purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces distinctive, spiny pods that contain several seeds. It is often used in agricultural systems for soil enrichment due to its nitrogen-fixing ability.

Desmodium violaceum thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is adapted to various soil types.
