Dacryodes granatensis

Dacryodes granatensis, commonly known as the granate ebony, is a tropical tree native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Burseraceae family and is characterized by its dark, aromatic wood. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptic to ovate in shape. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by spherical, orange-red fruits.

The species is valued for its hard, dense wood used in traditional crafts and as a source of natural resins.
