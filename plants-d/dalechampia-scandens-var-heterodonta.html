Dalechampia scandens var. heterodonta

Dalechampia scandens var. heterodonta, commonly known as the hairy antelope horn, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a climbing shrub characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that are followed by distinctive, elongated fruit capsules. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
