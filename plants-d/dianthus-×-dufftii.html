Dianthus × dufftii

Dianthus × dufftii, commonly known as the "Dufft's campion," is a hybrid perennial plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 12 to 24 inches and features hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Dianthus × dufftii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
