Dendrobium foelschei

Dendrobium foelschei, commonly known as the Foelsche's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, branching structure with fleshy, often wrinkled pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with white and green markings, typically blooming during the dry season. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and well-draining potting media.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
