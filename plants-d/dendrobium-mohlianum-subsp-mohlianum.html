Dendrobium mohlianum subsp. mohlianum

Dendrobium mohlianum subsp.

mohlianum, commonly known as the Mohlian orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia. It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome that produces multiple pseudobulbs.

The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with red or purple markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
