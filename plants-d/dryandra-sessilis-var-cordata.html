Dryandra sessilis var. cordata

Dryandra sessilis var. cordata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf dryandra, is a species native to the southwest of Western Australia.

The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces striking, pinkish-white flowers during the spring. It is part of the Proteaceae family, which includes many flowering plants with specialized adaptations for nutrient-poor soils.
