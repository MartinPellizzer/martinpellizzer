Dendrochilum intermedium

Dendrochilum intermedium, commonly known as the intermediate tree orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is typically found in forest undergrowth.

Dendrochilum intermedium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
