Duckeanthus grandiflorus

Duckeanthus grandiflorus, commonly known as the grandiflorous duckeanthus, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Africa.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
