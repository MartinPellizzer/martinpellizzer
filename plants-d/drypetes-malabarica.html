Drypetes malabarica

Drypetes malabarica, commonly known as the Malabar drypetes, is a small to medium-sized tree native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia and the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to elliptical in shape. Due to its ornamental value and potential ecological benefits, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation areas.
