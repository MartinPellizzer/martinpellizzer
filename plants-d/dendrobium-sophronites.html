Dendrobium sophronites

Dendrobium sophronites, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with thick, fleshy rhizomes that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, yellow to orange flowers with a distinctive red lip, often blooming in summer. Its leaves are broad and elliptical, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Dendrobium sophronites is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
