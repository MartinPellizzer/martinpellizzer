Dalbergia Nemoralis Subsp. Decaryi

Dalbergia nemoralis subsp. decaryi is a rare and lesser-known legume native to the tropical regions of Africa. This species belongs to the Fabaceae family, known for its nitrogen-fixing properties and ecological importance. It is characterized by its unique leaf structure and small, delicate flowers that bloom in clusters. Despite its beauty and ecological value, it remains underappreciated and poorly documented in botanical studies. Understanding this plant is crucial for conservation efforts and the preservation of biodiversity in its natural habitat.
