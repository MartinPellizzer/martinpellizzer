Dendrobenthamia hongkongensis

Dendrobenthamia hongkongensis, commonly known as Hong Kong orchid, is a rare epiphytic orchid native to Hong Kong and parts of southern China.

It grows on trees and rocks, often in humid, shaded environments. The plant has slender, erect stems that can reach up to 30 centimeters in height. Its flowers are typically pale yellow to greenish-white, with a distinctive hood-like structure.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is considered a flagship species for conservation efforts in its native habitat.
