Diplotaxis simplex var. micrantha

Diplotaxis simplex var. micrantha, commonly known as the slender wallflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb with erect, branching stems and small, clustered yellow flowers. The plant has alternate, linear to lanceolate leaves that are usually hairy. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
