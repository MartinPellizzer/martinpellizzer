Dendrobium fellowsii

Dendrobium fellowsii, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Indonesia and Malaysia. The plant produces striking yellow flowers with a velvety texture, often blooming in clusters along its stems. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Dendrobium fellowsii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
