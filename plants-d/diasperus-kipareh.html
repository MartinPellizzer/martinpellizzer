Diasperus kipareh

Diasperus kipareh, commonly known as the Madagascar star grass, is a flowering plant native to the island of Madagascar.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in arid regions of Madagascar.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
