Dillwynia peduncularis

Dillwynia peduncularis, commonly known as the slender dillwynia, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, yellow flowers. It is often found in sandy or loamy soils in coastal and inland regions.

Dillwynia peduncularis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
