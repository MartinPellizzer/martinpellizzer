Dorycnium Parviflorum

Dorycnium parviflorum is a flowering plant native to Europe and western Asia. It belongs to the legume family and is known for its distinctive spiny stems and small, yellow flowers. This plant thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open grasslands and woodland edges. Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a notable species in ecological studies. Despite its tough exterior, it plays an important role in supporting local insect populations.
