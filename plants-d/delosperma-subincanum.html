Delosperma subincanum

Delosperma subincanum, commonly known as the silver leaf ice plant, is a low-growing succulent native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, silver-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, or white, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it an ideal choice for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact size and drought tolerance make it a popular ornamental plant in dry climates.
