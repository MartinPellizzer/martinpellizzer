Dendrobium squalens

Dendrobium squalens, commonly known as the squalid orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its clustered, often pendulous growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, which bloom sequentially along the stems. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium squalens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
