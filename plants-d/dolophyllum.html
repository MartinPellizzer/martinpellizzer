Dolophyllum

Dolophyllum, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Apiaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive blue or purple flowers and finely divided, feathery leaves. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and saline soils, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Dolophyllum is often found in Mediterranean regions and parts of southern Europe.
