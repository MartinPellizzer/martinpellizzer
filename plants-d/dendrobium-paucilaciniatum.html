Dendrobium paucilaciniatum

Dendrobium paucilaciniatum, commonly known as the sparse-stippled orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a thick, fleshy rhizome and numerous pseudobulbs. The plant produces small, elongated leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. Its flowers are typically white or pale yellow, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
