Deutzia amurensis

Deutzia amurensis, commonly known as Amur deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features clusters of fragrant, white to pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has arching stems and lance-shaped leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun.

Deutzia amurensis is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
