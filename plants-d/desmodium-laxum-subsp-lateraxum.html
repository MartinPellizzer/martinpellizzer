Desmodium laxum subsp. lateraxum

Desmodium laxum subsp.

lateraxum, commonly known as the slender sicklepod, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions. It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers.

The plant produces pods that are typically curved and contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and pastures.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it ecologically significant in soil enrichment.
