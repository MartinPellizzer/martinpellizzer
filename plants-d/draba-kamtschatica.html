Draba kamtschatica

Draba kamtschatica, commonly known as the Kamchatka whitlow grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous annual or biennial plant that typically grows in alpine and arctic environments. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in a compact raceme. Its leaves are basal, linear, and often covered in fine hairs.

Draba kamtschatica is adapted to cold climates and is found in regions such as Siberia, Alaska, and the Kamchatka Peninsula.
