Dactylorhiza elata f. pallida

Dactylorhiza elata f. pallida, commonly known as the pale orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and produces pale to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils in woodland or heathland habitats. It has a long, thin rhizome and linear, grass-like leaves.

The species is noted for its delicate appearance and is often found in shaded environments.
