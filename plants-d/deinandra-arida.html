Deinandra arida

Deinandra arida, commonly known as the desert thornveld, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is adapted to arid conditions, with deep roots that help it survive in dry environments. It is often found in rocky soils and is part of the Fabaceae family.

Deinandra arida plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small animals.
