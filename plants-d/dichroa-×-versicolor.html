Dichroa × versicolor

Dichroa × versicolor, commonly known as the variable dichroa, is a hybrid plant belonging to the family Begoniaceae.

It is characterized by its striking bi-colored leaves, which often display a mix of green and red or purple hues. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. Its foliage is dense and attractive, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

It thrives in partial shade and moist, well-draining soil, and is native to regions in Southeast Asia.
