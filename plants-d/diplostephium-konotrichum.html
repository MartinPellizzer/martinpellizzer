Diplostephium konotrichum

Diplostephium konotrichum, commonly known as the African milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils. The plant features showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are alternate and have a distinctive, waxy texture.

The plant contains milky sap, which can be toxic if ingested.
