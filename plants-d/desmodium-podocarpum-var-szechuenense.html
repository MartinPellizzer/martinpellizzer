Desmodium podocarpum var. szechuenense

Desmodium podocarpum var. szechuenense, commonly known as the Sichuan desmodium, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers that typically bloom in clusters. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, which are released when the pods dry and split open. It is native to regions of China and is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and shrublands.

This species is valued for its ecological role in nitrogen fixation and as a potential forage plant for livestock.
