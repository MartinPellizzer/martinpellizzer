Daphne rehderi

Daphne rehderi, commonly known as Chinese Daphne, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, fragrant flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring, typically in shades of pink, white, and red. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its colorful blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Daphne rehderi is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and winter interest.
