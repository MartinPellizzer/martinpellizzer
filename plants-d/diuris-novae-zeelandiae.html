Diuris novae-zeelandiae

Diuris novae-zeelandiae, commonly known as the New Zealand spider-orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to New Zealand.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rock crevices. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single, narrow leaf and a spike of small, greenish-white flowers. Its flowers have a distinctive spider-like appearance, with elongated sepals and petals.

The species is noted for its rarity and is protected in its natural habitat.
