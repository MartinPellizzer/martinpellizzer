Daphne composita

Daphne composita, commonly known as the composite daphne, is a perennial shrub native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring, typically in shades of pink and white. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and a compact growth habit, making it suitable for rock gardens or small spaces. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its winter blooms.

However, it contains toxic sap and should be handled with care.
