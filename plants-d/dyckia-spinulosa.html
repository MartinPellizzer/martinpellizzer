Dyckia spinulosa

Dyckia spinulosa, commonly known as the spiny dyckia, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves with sharp, spiny margins, which give it its common name. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, greenish-white flowers.

Dyckia spinulosa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and hardy nature.
