Desmodium skinneri var. flavovirens

Desmodium skinneri var. flavovirens, commonly known as the yellow-green burr legume, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to parts of Africa and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces hairy, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical regions, often in disturbed soils or open habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ability to fix nitrogen, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and used in ecological restoration projects.
