Dudleya reflexa

Dudleya reflexa, commonly known as the reflexed stonecrop, is a succulent perennial native to California.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink to purple during the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it well-suited for rock gardens.

Its adaptability to arid conditions makes it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
