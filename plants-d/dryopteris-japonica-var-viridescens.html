Dryopteris japonica var. viridescens

Dryopteris japonica var. viridescens, commonly known as the Japanese shield fern, is a species of fern native to East Asia.

It features a compact, evergreen frond structure with a distinctive, shield-like shape. The leaves are typically dark green and have a leathery texture, with a glossy appearance. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden settings.
