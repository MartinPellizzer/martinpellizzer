Daviesia discolor

Daviesia discolor, commonly known as the silver wattle, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It is characterized by its silver-green foliage and yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has narrow, leathery leaves and produces pods that contain seeds. It is often found in heathlands and open forests, thriving in well-drained soils.

Daviesia discolor is a member of the legume family and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
