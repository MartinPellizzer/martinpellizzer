Disa goetzeana

Disa goetzeana, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a striking South African orchid species belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant produces vividly colored petals, often in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which attract pollinators such as bees and birds. Disa goetzeana is native to the Western and Eastern Cape provinces of South Africa, where it thrives in rocky, well-drained soils.

It is a popular ornamental plant due to its ornate appearance and unique blooming cycle.
