Dictyostega umbellata

Dictyostega umbellata, commonly known as the umbrella dictyostega, is a unique plant species characterized by its distinctive umbrella-like structure.

It belongs to the family Dictyostegaceae and is native to specific regions in the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia. The plant features a central stem with multiple branching fronds that radiate outward, resembling an umbrella in both form and function. Its leaves are typically broad and flat, adapted for efficient photosynthesis in low-light environments.

The reproductive structures are located at the base of the fronds, producing spores that are dispersed by wind, aiding in the plant's propagation.
