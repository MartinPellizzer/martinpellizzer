Drosanthemum glabrescens

Drosanthemum glabrescens, commonly known as the woolly sunflower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it an attractive addition to xeriscapic landscapes.
