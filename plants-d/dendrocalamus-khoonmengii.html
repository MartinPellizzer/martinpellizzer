Dendrocalamus khoonmengii

Dendrocalamus khoonmengii, commonly known as the Khoonmeng bamboo, is a tall, slender grass-like plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Bambusoideae subfamily and is characterized by its strong, hollow stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as mountainous regions and forest understories.

Dendrocalamus khoonmengii is valued for its durable and flexible culms, which are used in construction and handicrafts.
