Davallia urophylla

Davallia urophylla, commonly known as the golden tree fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and fronds that are often bright green with a golden hue. The plant has a unique growth pattern, with a central trunk that supports a crown of feathery fronds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

The fronds are pinnately compound, with small, ovate-shaped pinnae that contribute to its ornamental appeal.
