Digitaria swalleniana

It is native to the wetlands and marshy areas of the southeastern United States, particularly Florida and the Gulf Coast. It thrives in moist, nutrient-poor soils and is often found in coastal hammocks and swampy habitats.

Digitaria swalleniana plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species in its native range.
