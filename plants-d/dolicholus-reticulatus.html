Dolicholus reticulatus

Dolicholus reticulatus, commonly known as the net-veined orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, reticulate venation pattern on its leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
