Diapedium pubinervium

Diapedium pubinervium, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense, velvety pubescence, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in axillary inflorescences. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that contain several seeds.

Diapedium pubinervium is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands.
