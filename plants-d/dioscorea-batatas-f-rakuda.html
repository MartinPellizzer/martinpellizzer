Dioscorea batatas f. rakuda

Dioscorea batatas f. rakuda, commonly known as the purple yam, is a tuberous plant belonging to the family Dioscoreaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive violet to purple skin and pale yellow flesh, which gives it a unique appearance. The plant is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. Its tubers are rich in starch and are widely used in traditional cuisines for their nutty flavor.

The plant also has medicinal properties and is used in various herbal remedies.
