Dendrophorbium chingualense

Dendrophorbium chingualense, commonly known as the Chingualense orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in late summer. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in its harsh, arid environment. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, adapted to reduce water loss.

The species is of particular interest to botanists due to its limited distribution and specialized ecological requirements.
