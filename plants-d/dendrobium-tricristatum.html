Dendrobium tricristatum

Dendrobium tricristatum, commonly known as the three-ridged orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with multiple stems that bear clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant has leathery, strap-like leaves and produces inflorescences with numerous small, white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Dendrobium tricristatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
