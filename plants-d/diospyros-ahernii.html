Diospyros ahernii

Diospyros ahernii, commonly known as the Ahern persimmon, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Ebenaceae family, which includes ebony and persimmon trees. The plant is characterized by its smooth, dark bark and elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. Its fruit is a small, round berry that turns orange when ripe, containing a single seed.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in traditional medicine.
