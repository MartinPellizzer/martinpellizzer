Disa reticulata

Disa reticulata, commonly known as the netted disa, is a striking South African orchid characterized by its vibrant pink to purple flowers.

It is native to the Cape Province and grows in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant features a basal rosette of fleshy, dark green leaves that store water, aiding its survival in arid conditions. Its flowers are borne on tall, slender stems and are often adorned with intricate, net-like patterns on the petals.

Disa reticulata is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
