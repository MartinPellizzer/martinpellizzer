Delosperma patersoniae

Delosperma patersoniae, commonly known as the Cape Hottentot.

It is a succulent plant native to South Africa, characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves and vibrant pink to red flowers. The plant is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves. It produces small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Delosperma patersoniae is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its hardiness and attractive appearance.
