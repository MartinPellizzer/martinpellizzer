Desmodium parviflorum f. yunnanense

Desmodium parviflorum f. yunnanense, commonly known as Yunnan small-flower velvet bean, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions. It has a creeping habit with hairy stems and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential use in ecological restoration projects.
