Daphne lerchenfeldiana

Daphne lerchenfeldiana, commonly known as the silverleaf daphne, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage and clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces small, oval berries that are typically red or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
