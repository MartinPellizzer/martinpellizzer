Dichapetalum nervatum

Dichapetalum nervatum, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white in color. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with prominent veins, and it produces small, clustered fruits. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Dichapetalum nervatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
