Daphne androsaemifolia

Daphne androsaemifolia, commonly known as the Androsaemifolia Daphne, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming in early spring. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or woodland settings.

Daphne androsaemifolia is noted for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
