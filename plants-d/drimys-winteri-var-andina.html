Drimys winteri var. andina

Drimys winteri var. andina, commonly known as the Andean woodruff, is a shrub native to the temperate regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its aromatic, evergreen foliage. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as cloud forests. Its leaves are lance-shaped and emit a strong, spicy scent when crushed.
