Dianthus integer subsp. minutiflorus

Dianthus integer subsp.

minutiflorus, commonly known as the miniature carnation, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to white.

The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of flowers on slender stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its delicate appearance and extended blooming period, it is a popular choice among gardeners seeking ornamental plants with low maintenance requirements.
