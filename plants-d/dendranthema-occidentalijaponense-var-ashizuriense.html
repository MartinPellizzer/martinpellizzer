Dendranthema occidentalijaponense var. ashizuriense

Dendranthema occidentalijaponense var. ashizuriense, commonly known as the Ashizuri chrysanthemum, is a perennial flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers with a central disc. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and thrives in well-drained soil. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.

Its foliage is usually gray-green, with serrated leaves that add to its visual interest.
