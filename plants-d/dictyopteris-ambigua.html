Dictyopteris ambigua

Dictyopteris ambigua, commonly known as the ambiguous fanweed, is a species of brown algae belonging to the family Dictyopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its complex, branching thallus with a leathery texture and a dark green to brown coloration. The plant typically grows in shallow coastal waters, often attached to rocky substrates. Its reproductive structures include both male and female gametophytes, which are essential for its sexual reproduction.

Dictyopteris ambigua plays a role in marine ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various aquatic organisms.
