Drimys chilensis var. latifolia

Drimys chilensis var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf drimys, is a flowering plant native to southern Chile.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows in temperate rainforests. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a glossy surface and produces small, fragrant white flowers. Its fruit is a dark blue to black berry, which is edible but has a strong, spicy flavor.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
