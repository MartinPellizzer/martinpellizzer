Diclidanthera penduliflora var. penaeantha

Diclidanthera penduliflora var. penaeantha, commonly known as the Penaeantha everlasting pea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, pendulous inflorescences that produce numerous small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. Its leaves are trifoliate, with each leaflet being lanceolate in shape.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its prolonged blooming period.
