Delosperma smythae

Delosperma smythae, commonly known as the silverleaf ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white wax, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, or purple, typically during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ability to store water in its leaves allows it to survive in arid environments with minimal rainfall.
