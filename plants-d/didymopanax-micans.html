Didymopanax micans

Didymopanax micans, commonly known as the velvetleaf or Brazilian pepper tree, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The leaves are opposite, simple, and have a distinctive velvety texture, which gives the plant its common name. It produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by orange or red berries.

The plant is often found in disturbed areas and is known for its rapid growth and adaptability to various soil types.
