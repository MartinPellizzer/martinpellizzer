Dorstenia reniformis

Dorstenia reniformis, commonly known as the kidney-shaped fig tree, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its unique, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white waxy coating. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into edible, fig-like fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dorstenia reniformis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its distinctive foliage and ease of care.
