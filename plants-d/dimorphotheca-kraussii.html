Dimorphotheca kraussii

Dimorphotheca kraussii, commonly known as the Cape marigold, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow to orange ray flowers and dark purple disk flowers. The plant has hairy, linear leaves and is often found in coastal and sandy environments. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

The plant's vibrant flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
