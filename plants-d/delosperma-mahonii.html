Delosperma mahonii

Delosperma mahonii, commonly known as the ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves and vibrant, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink, red, and yellow. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for arid or rocky environments. It produces small, round fruits that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Delosperma mahonii is often used in xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and ornamental value.
