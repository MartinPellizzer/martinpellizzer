Dioscorea itatiensis

Dioscorea itatiensis, commonly known as Itati yam, is a species of tuberous plant native to South America, particularly found in Ecuador and Peru.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its climbing habit, with hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by cylindrical, fleshy fruits containing numerous seeds.

Its tuberous roots are the main part used, valued for their nutritional content and traditional medicinal uses.
