Diarthron vesiculosum var. vesiculosum

Diarthron vesiculosum var. vesiculosum, commonly known as the balloon vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features distinctive, inflated, balloon-like fruits that are yellow or orange in color, which mature and split open to release seeds. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Its unique fruit structure makes it a notable species in botanical studies and ornamental horticulture.
