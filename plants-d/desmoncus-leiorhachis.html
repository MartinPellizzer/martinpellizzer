Desmoncus leiorhachis

Desmoncus leiorhachis, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically pale yellow to white in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Desmoncus leiorhachis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in botanical gardens and private collections.
