Delphinium gratum

Delphinium gratum, commonly known as the garden delphinium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that bear large, showy flowers. The flowers are typically blue to purple in color, with five petals and numerous stamens, creating a striking visual display. The plant produces a taproot system and has palmately lobed leaves that are hairy beneath.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
