Draba laevipes var. delessertii

Draba laevipes var. delessertii, commonly known as the Alpine Draba, is a small flowering plant native to high-altitude regions of the Alps and surrounding mountain areas.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, clustered flowers and capsule fruits. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, thriving in well-drained, nutrient-poor environments. Its leaves are usually basal, simple, and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

The species is noted for its resilience to cold climates and is often found in alpine meadows and scree slopes.
