Deverra rapaletsa

Deverra rapaletsa, commonly known as the mountain holly.

This evergreen shrub is native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa. It features small, dark green, leathery leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers in spring, which are followed by bright red berries in autumn.

Deverra rapaletsa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
