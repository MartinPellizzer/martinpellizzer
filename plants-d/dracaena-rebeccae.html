Dracaena rebeccae

Dracaena rebeccae, commonly known as the Rebecca dracaena, is a striking evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features tall, narrow leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a reddish or bronze tinge along the edges. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that appear in clusters, followed by red berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright indirect light.

Dracaena rebeccae is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
