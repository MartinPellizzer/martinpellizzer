Diplusodon vacciniifolius

Diplusodon vacciniifolius, commonly known as the red-leafed bromeliad, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central water-holding cavity. The plant's foliage is typically vibrant green with red or purple markings, which help in attracting pollinators. It produces small, white or yellow flowers that bloom from the center of the rosette.

Diplusodon vacciniifolius is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and ability to thrive in humid environments.
