Delissea macrostegia var. viscosa

Delissea macrostegia var. viscosa, commonly known as the sticky leaved delissea, is a species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves that are covered in a sticky, resinous substance. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
