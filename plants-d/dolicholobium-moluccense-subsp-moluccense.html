Dolicholobium moluccense subsp. moluccense

Dolicholobium moluccense subsp.

moluccense, commonly known as the Moluccan dolicholobium, is a species native to the Moluccas in Indonesia. It is a shrub or small tree characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptic to ovate in shape.

The flowers are typically yellow or greenish-yellow, clustered in inflorescences, and are followed by capsule fruits. The plant is adapted to tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, well-drained soils.

It is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and potential ecological roles in its native habitat.
