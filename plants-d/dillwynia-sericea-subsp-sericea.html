Dillwynia sericea subsp. sericea

Dillwynia sericea subsp.

sericea, commonly known as silky dillwynia, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia. It is a perennial shrub characterized by its finely hairy, often silver-gray foliage.

The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in compact spikes, which bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and restoration projects.
