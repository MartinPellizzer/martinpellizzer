Dioscorea tequendamensis

Dioscorea tequendamensis, commonly known as the Tequendamensis yam, is a climbing vine belonging to the Dioscoreaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly Cameroon and the Central African Republic. The plant produces tuberous roots that are valued for their culinary and medicinal properties. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a rough texture, while its flowers are unisexual and typically greenish-white in color.

The species is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.
