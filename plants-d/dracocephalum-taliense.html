Dracocephalum taliense

Dracocephalum taliense, commonly known as Taliense blueweed, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its square stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. It produces small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer.

Dracocephalum taliense is often used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
