Dyckia horrida

Dyckia horrida, commonly known as the spiked dyckia, is a succulent plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped growth pattern. The plant features sharp, spiny leaves that are typically blue-green in color, providing a striking visual appearance. Dyckia horrida is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

It produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, colorful flowers that attract pollinators.
