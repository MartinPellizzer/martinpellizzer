Deutzia staminea

Deutzia staminea, commonly known as the white deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy, fragrant white flowers that bloom in early summer, often in clusters at the ends of its stems. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy, providing an attractive backdrop to its floral display. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Deutzia staminea is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
