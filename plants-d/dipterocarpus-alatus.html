Dipterocarpus alatus

Dipterocarpus alatus, commonly known as the winged dipterocarp, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive winged seeds, which are adapted for wind dispersal. The plant belongs to the family Dipterocarpaceae and is often found in lowland rainforests. It has a tall, straight trunk and broad, lance-shaped leaves.

The tree plays an important role in the ecological systems of its native habitats.
