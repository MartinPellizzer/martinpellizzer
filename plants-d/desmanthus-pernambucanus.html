Desmanthus pernambucanus

Desmanthus pernambucanus, commonly known as the Brazilian burr legume, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and savannas.

Desmanthus pernambucanus is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential use in ecological restoration projects.
