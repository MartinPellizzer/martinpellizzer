Draba Borealis Var. Kurilensis

Draba borealis var. kurilensis is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the cold climates of the Russian Far East and the Kuril Islands. It thrives in rocky, alpine environments where few other plants can survive. This variety of Draba is known for its tiny, white or pale purple flowers that bloom in early spring. Its hardy nature allows it to endure harsh winters and short growing seasons. Due to its limited distribution and ecological sensitivity, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical study and conservation efforts.
