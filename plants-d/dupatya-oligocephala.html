Dupatya oligocephala

Dupatya oligocephala, commonly known as the narrow-headed orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, intricate flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually pale green to white, with a distinctive, narrow floral lip.

Dupatya oligocephala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique morphology.
