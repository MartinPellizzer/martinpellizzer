Dendrobium schimperianum

Dendrobium schimperianum, commonly known as the Schimper's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, cane-like stems that are often branched and covered in dense clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The flowers are typically white or pale yellow with a distinctive greenish or reddish lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its beauty and fragrance.
