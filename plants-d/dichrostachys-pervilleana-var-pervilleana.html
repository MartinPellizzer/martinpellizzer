Dichrostachys pervilleana var. pervilleana

Dichrostachys pervilleana var. pervilleana, commonly known as the slender false indigo, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. The leaves are alternate, simple, and usually have a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry woodland or savanna habitats.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.
