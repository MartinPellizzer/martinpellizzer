Dactylis juncinella

Dactylis juncinella, commonly known as the slender oatgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Dactylis juncinella is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
