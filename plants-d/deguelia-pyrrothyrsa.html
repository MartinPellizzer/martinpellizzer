Deguelia pyrrothyrsa

Deguelia pyrrothyrsa, commonly known as the red-flowered deguelia, is a shrub or small tree native to the rainforests of northern Queensland, Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and its striking, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and produces small, oval-shaped fruits that contain toxic seeds. It is often found in moist, well-drained soils along streams and in rainforest understoreys.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
