Deutzia parviflora

Deutzia parviflora, commonly known as the smallflower deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, arching branches and heart-shaped leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and well-drained soil.

Deutzia parviflora is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and easy maintenance.
