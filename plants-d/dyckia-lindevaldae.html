Dyckia lindevaldae

Dyckia lindevaldae, commonly known as the Lindvald's dyckia, is a species of bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its dense, rosette-forming structure composed of narrow, spiky leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces striking inflorescences with bright red bracts that contrast sharply with its foliage. It thrives in sunny, well-drained environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Dyckia lindevaldae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for in cultivation.
