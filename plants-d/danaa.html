Danaa

Danaa, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the genus Saintpaulia and is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant typically features velvety leaves that are dark green and often have a waxy texture. Danaa thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is a popular houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
