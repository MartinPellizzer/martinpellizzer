Dipterocalyx glabrescens

Dipterocalyx glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth dipterocarp, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dipterocarpaceae, which includes many tropical trees. The plant is characterized by its smooth bark and clustered inflorescences. Its flowers are typically small and white or pale yellow, attracting various pollinators.

It plays an important ecological role in its native habitats, contributing to forest biodiversity.
