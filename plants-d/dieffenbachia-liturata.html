Dieffenbachia liturata

Dieffenbachia liturata, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features striking, arrow-shaped leaves with a distinctive light green and white variegation pattern. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular houseplant. Its foliage is glossy and can grow up to 12 inches in length, adding a dramatic visual effect to any room.

Dieffenbachia liturata is also noted for its tolerance to indoor environments and minimal care requirements.
