Dietes bicolor

Dietes bicolor, commonly known as the Zulu lily, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features bright yellow and white flowers that bloom in late summer, creating a dramatic visual impact. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are evergreen and can grow up to 1 meter in height. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in coastal and Mediterranean climates.
