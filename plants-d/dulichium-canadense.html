Dulichium canadense

Dulichium canadense, commonly known as Canadian bulrush, is a tall, erect perennial herb native to North America.

It typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of lakes and rivers. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces spike-like inflorescences that bear small, greenish-white flowers. Its root system helps stabilize soil and prevent erosion in aquatic environments.

Dulichium canadense is an important plant for wildlife, providing habitat and food for various birds and insects.
