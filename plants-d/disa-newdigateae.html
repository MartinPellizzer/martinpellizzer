Disa newdigateae

Disa newdigateae, commonly known as the red-stemmed disa, is a striking orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that are typically red or purple in color, contrasting with its lance-shaped, dark green leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, often arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial shade to full sun.

Disa newdigateae is a popular choice for gardens due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
