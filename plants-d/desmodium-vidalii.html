Desmodium vidalii

Desmodium vidalii, commonly known as the velvet bean or horsebean, is a leguminous plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which are often hairy and have a velvety texture. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink, followed by cylindrical pods containing several seeds. Desmodium vidalii is native to tropical and subtropical regions and is often found in disturbed soils.

It has been used in traditional medicine and as a forage crop due to its nutritional value.
