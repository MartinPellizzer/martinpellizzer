Dictyophleba setosa

Dictyophleba setosa, commonly known as the hairy nettle.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the family Urticaceae, characterized by its finely hairy stems and leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense clusters. It is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors or along stream banks.

Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported therapeutic properties.
