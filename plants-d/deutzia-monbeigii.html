Deutzia monbeigii

Deutzia monbeigii, commonly known as the Chinese deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, arching stems and elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Deutzia monbeigii is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive floral display and compact growth habit.
