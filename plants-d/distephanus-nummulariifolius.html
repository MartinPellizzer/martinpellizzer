Distephanus nummulariifolius

Distephanus nummulariifolius, commonly known as the silver dollar plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features small, round, silver-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil. It produces clusters of yellow flowers during the summer months.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
