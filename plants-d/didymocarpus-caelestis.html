Didymocarpus caelestis

Didymocarpus caelestis, commonly known as the celestial flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling flames. The plant produces small, round, woody fruits that contain seeds enclosed in a hard, leathery covering. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and typically have a glossy surface.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
