Dicliptera velata

Dicliptera velata, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas.

Its small, white flowers are clustered in terminal panicles, and it produces small, three-lobed fruits that contain three seeds each.
