Dactylostemon communis f. spathulatus

Dactylostemon communis f. spathulatus, commonly known as the broad-leafed dactylostemon, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically broad and rounded. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
