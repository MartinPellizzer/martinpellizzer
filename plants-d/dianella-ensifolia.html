Dianella ensifolia

Dianella ensifolia, commonly known as the swordleaf flaxlily, is a perennial herb native to New Zealand.

It features strap-like, sword-shaped leaves that are often bluish-green in color. The plant produces striking blue to violet flowers during the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Dianella ensifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in native ecosystems.
