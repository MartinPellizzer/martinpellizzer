Dombeya multiflora var. vestita

Dombeya multiflora var. vestita, commonly known as the velvet dombeya, is a shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, pale pink to white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has hairy, velvety leaves that give it its common name and contribute to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sun.

Dombeya multiflora var. vestita is often used in gardens for its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.
