Dendrocalamus nianhei

Dendrocalamus nianhei, commonly known as the Chinese bamboo or giant bamboo, is a tall, fast-growing grass native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Bambusoideae subfamily and is characterized by its strong, slender culms that can reach heights of up to 30 meters. The plant is valued for its durable and lightweight bamboo, which is used in construction, furniture, and crafts. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Its rhizomes are also used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
