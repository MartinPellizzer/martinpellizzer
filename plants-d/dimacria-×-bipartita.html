Dimacria × bipartita

Dimacria × bipartita, commonly known as the hybrid spider orchid, is a rare and exotic plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, spider-like flowers with a distinctive coloration pattern. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to tree trunks or branches in humid, shaded environments. Its botanical features include long, slender stems and showy, fragrant blooms that attract specific pollinators.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly valued in horticultural collections.
