Dendrochilum macropterum

Dendrochilum macropterum, commonly known as the large-winged orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in tropical environments. Its flowers are typically greenish-yellow with purple markings, and it thrives in shaded, moist habitats.

Dendrochilum macropterum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and unique floral structure.
