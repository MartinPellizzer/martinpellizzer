Dalbergia krowee

Dalbergia krowee, commonly known as the Krowe tree, is a species of leguminous tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The tree produces flat, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in moist, well-drained soils in forested areas.

The plant is valued for its hard, durable wood, which is used in traditional crafts and furniture-making.
