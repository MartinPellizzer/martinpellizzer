Deinbollia kilimandscharica var. adusta

Deinbollia kilimandscharica var. adusta, commonly known as the Kilimanjaro starwood, is a flowering plant native to the East African region, particularly Tanzania.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale pink, which bloom in clusters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly hairy texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
