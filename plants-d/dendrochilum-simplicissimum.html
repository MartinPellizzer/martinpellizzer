Dendrochilum simplicissimum

Dendrochilum simplicissimum, commonly known as the simplest tree orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and the presence of pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrochilum simplicissimum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
