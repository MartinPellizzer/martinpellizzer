Dendrobium multicostatum

Dendrobium multicostatum, commonly known as the golden lip or the orchid of the gods, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is an epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia, including countries like Thailand and Indonesia. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with upright, cane-like stems that can reach several meters in length. Its flowers are typically yellow or golden in color, with a prominent lip that is often marked with purple or red streaks.

Dendrobium multicostatum thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
