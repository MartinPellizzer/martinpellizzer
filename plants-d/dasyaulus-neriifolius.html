Dasyaulus neriifolius

Dasyaulus neriifolius, commonly known as the black-leafed dasyaulus, is a unique plant species characterized by its dark, glossy foliage.

It is native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep shade of green, sometimes with a purplish tinge. Its flowers are small and clustered, producing a subtle fragrance that attracts pollinators.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, Dasyaulus neriifolius is considered a conservation concern in its natural range.
