Dendrobium subretusum

Dendrobium subretusum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. The plant produces linear, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. Its flowers are typically small, fragrant, and come in shades of white, yellow, and pink.

Dendrobium subretusum thrives in shaded, moist environments and is valued for its ornamental beauty in horticulture.
