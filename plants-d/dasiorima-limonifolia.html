Dasiorima limonifolia

Dasiorima limonifolia, commonly known as the lemonleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant blooms.

The leaves have a citrus-like scent, which adds to its appeal in both gardens and horticultural settings.
