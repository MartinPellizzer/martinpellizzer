Dichapetalum zenkeri

Dichapetalum zenkeri, commonly known as the African star-of-Bethlehem, is a striking flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in shades of white or pale blue. The plant has lance-shaped leaves and produces distinctive, elongated seed pods that split open when mature. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or wooded areas.

Dichapetalum zenkeri is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its cultural significance in some African communities.
