Dupatya laricifolia

Dupatya laricifolia, commonly known as the silver fir.

It is a coniferous tree native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. The plant has needle-like leaves that are typically dark green and arranged spirally along the branches. Its cones are small and woody, containing seeds that are dispersed by wind.

The tree is valued for its hard, durable wood and is often found in cool, moist forest ecosystems.
