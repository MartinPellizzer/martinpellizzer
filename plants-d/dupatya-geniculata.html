Dupatya geniculata

Dupatya geniculata, commonly known as the spiral fern, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

Its flowers are small and clustered in an inflorescence called a spadix, surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe.

Dupatya geniculata is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage and unusual growth form.
