Dasyphyllum hystrix var. peruvianum

Dasyphyllum hystrix var. peruvianum, commonly known as the Peruvian spurge, is a succulent plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, water-storing leaves and branched stems. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. Due to its hardy nature, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in dry climates.
