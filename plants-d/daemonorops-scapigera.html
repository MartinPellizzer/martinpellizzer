Daemonorops scapigera

Daemonorops scapigera, commonly known as the sword fern or bamboo palm, is a species of cycad native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and feather-like leaves that resemble a palm. The plant produces cones, with male and female cones typically occurring on separate plants. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is also cultivated as a houseplant in various parts of the world.
