Dendrobium pumilum

Dendrobium pumilum, commonly known as the dwarf orchid, is a small epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact growth habit with short, branched pseudobulbs and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers with a distinctive, intricate pattern. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its delicate nature, it requires careful attention to watering and light conditions for optimal growth.
