Dendrobium kurashigei

Dendrobium kurashigei, commonly known as the Kurashige's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clustered, cane-like pseudobulbs and long, narrow leaves. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrobium kurashigei is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and unique floral display.
