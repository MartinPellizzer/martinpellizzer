Diplazium malabaricum

Diplazium malabaricum, commonly known as Malabar shield fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to the tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Davalliaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The fronds are divided into segments, giving the plant a lush, ornamental appearance.
