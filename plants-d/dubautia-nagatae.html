Dubautia nagatae

Dubautia nagatae, commonly known as the Kauai ironwood, is a shrub native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and rugged, woody stems that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, blooming during the summer months. Dubautia nagatae thrives in dry, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and upland habitats.

It is considered a keystone species in its ecosystem, providing habitat and food for native wildlife.
