Delphinium azureum var. simplex

Delphinium azureum var. simplex, commonly known as the simple blue larkspur, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, upright stems that are often branched and covered in fine hairs. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

This variety is noted for its simplicity in form, with fewer petals compared to other delphinium species.
