Drejerella mirabiloides

Drejerella mirabiloides, commonly known as the fairy lantern, is a striking member of the Gesneriaceae family, native to the humid forests of Madagascar.

Its leaves are usually elongated and glossy, with a waxy texture that helps retain water in its native environment.

Despite its beauty, Drejerella mirabiloides is relatively rare and requires specific care to thrive in cultivation.
