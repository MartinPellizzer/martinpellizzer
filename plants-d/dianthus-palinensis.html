Dianthus palinensis

Dianthus palinensis, commonly known as the Canary Island Carnation, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to the island's unique climate. It has narrow, linear leaves and a compact growth habit, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Dianthus palinensis is also noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its attractive blooms.
