Dendrobium arachnostachyum

Dendrobium arachnostachyum, commonly known as the spider orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, thin stems and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, relying on atmospheric moisture rather than soil for nutrients. Its flowering period usually occurs in the summer months, and it is prized for its delicate appearance and fragrance.

Dendrobium arachnostachyum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
