Dendrobium moniliforme var. malipoense

Dendrobium moniliforme var. malipoense, commonly known as the Malipo dendrobium, is a species of orchid native to China.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in mountainous regions. The plant features long, cane-like stems that bear clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. Its flowers are often used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.

The variety is noted for its ornamental value and is cultivated for both horticultural and medicinal purposes.
