Delphinium virescens subsp. wootonii

Delphinium virescens subsp.

wootonii, commonly known as the Wooton's larkspur, is a herbaceous perennial native to the western United States. It features tall, sparsely branched stems that bear clusters of sky-blue to violet flowers.

The plant has lance-shaped, hairy leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and ornamental addition to alpine and meadow gardens.
