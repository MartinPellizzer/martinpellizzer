Draba Aizoides Var. Brevistyla

Draba aizoides var. brevistyla is a small, hardy flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of western North America. It thrives in rocky and alpine environments where few other plants can survive. This variety is distinguished by its short, stout stems and delicate, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its compact size and resilience make it a notable species in ecological studies of high-altitude flora. Despite its modest appearance, it plays an important role in stabilizing soil and supporting local insect populations.
