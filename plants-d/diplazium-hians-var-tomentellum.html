Diplazium hians var. tomentellum

Diplazium hians var. tomentellum, commonly known as the hairy sword fern, is a varietal form of the sword fern species.

It is characterized by its densely hairy, grayish-green fronds that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions, particularly in Southeast Asia. Its fronds are pinnate, with lanceolate pinnae that are often slightly curled at the tips.

This variety thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
