Draba oreadum subsp. mariae-aliciae

Draba oreadum subsp.

mariae-aliciae, commonly known as the Alpine Draba, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe. It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, cruciform flowers and capsule fruits.

This subspecies is distinguished by its compact growth habit and pale yellow to white petals. It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions.

The plant is also noted for its ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions, making it a resilient member of alpine ecosystems.
