Digitaria merkeri

Digitaria merkeri, commonly known as Merker's digitaria, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Botanically, it is notable for its adaptability and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
