Desmodium remotum

Desmodium remotum, commonly known as the slender desmodium, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb, often found in disturbed soils and open habitats. Its seed pods are hairy and contain several small, dark seeds.

Desmodium remotum is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility.
