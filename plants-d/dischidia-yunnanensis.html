Dischidia yunnanensis

Dischidia yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan bubble vine, is a climbing plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its unique, swollen nodes that resemble bubbles, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in forested areas.

Its foliage is thick and waxy, making it suitable for indoor cultivation as a decorative plant.
