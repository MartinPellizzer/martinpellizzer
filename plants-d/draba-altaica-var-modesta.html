Draba altaica var. modesta

Draba altaica var. modesta, commonly known as the alpine draba, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants, and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, thriving in cold, arid environments. It has a basal rosette of leaves and produces small, yellow or white flowers in early summer.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
