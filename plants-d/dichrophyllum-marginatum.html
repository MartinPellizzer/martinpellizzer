Dichrophyllum marginatum

Dichrophyllum marginatum, commonly known as the margined dichrophyllum, is a unique plant species characterized by its striking color variation.

It typically features leaves with a distinct green and white or pink margin, creating a visually appealing contrast. This plant is native to specific regions in Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, tropical environments. Its botanical structure includes simple, alternate leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers.

Due to its rarity and aesthetic appeal, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
