Draba parvisiliquosa

Draba parvisiliquosa, commonly known as the small-silque draba, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a small, mat-forming perennial that typically grows in alpine and arctic regions. The plant features basal leaves that are linear to oblanceolate in shape, and its flowers are small, white or pale yellow, with four petals. It produces small, rounded pods that contain several seeds.

Draba parvisiliquosa is adapted to cold environments and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils.
