Dolichocarpa coerulescens

Dolichocarpa coerulescens, commonly known as the blue-flowered orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking blue-violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in tropical gardens and botanical collections.
