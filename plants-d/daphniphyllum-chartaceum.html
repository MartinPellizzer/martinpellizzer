Daphniphyllum chartaceum

Daphniphyllum chartaceum, commonly known as the paperleaf daphniphyllum, is a species of evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its leathery, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically white or pale yellow, followed by red or orange berries. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial to full shade.

Daphniphyllum chartaceum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and unique appearance.
