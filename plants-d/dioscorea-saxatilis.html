Dioscorea saxatilis

Dioscorea saxatilis, commonly known as the saxatile yam, is a climbing vine belonging to the family Dioscoreaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific. The plant produces tuberous roots that are often used in traditional medicine and as a food source. Its leaves are heart-shaped and its flowers are typically greenish-yellow with a distinctive spiral arrangement.

The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires a warm climate for optimal growth.
