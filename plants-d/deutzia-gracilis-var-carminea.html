Deutzia gracilis var. carminea

Deutzia gracilis var. carminea, commonly known as the carmine deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features slender, arching stems that bear clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink to deep red. The foliage is typically dark green, providing a striking contrast to the vibrant blooms. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and adaptability to a range of growing conditions.
