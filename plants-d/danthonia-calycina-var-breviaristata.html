Danthonia calycina var. breviaristata

Danthonia calycina var. breviaristata, commonly known as the narrow-awn fescue, is a low-growing perennial grass native to Australia.

It features fine, needle-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences with short awns. This variety is adapted to dry, sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in coastal dunes and heathlands, contributing to soil stabilization.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species in ecological restoration projects.
