Dendrobium caliculi-mentum

Dendrobium caliculi-mentum, commonly known as the Mentum Dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with cane-like stems that can reach several feet in length. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and white, often with a contrasting lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique floral structure and role in orchid taxonomy.
