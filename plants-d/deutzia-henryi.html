Deutzia henryi

Deutzia henryi, commonly known as Henry's deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its clusters of white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, arching stems and small, ovate leaves that turn yellow in the autumn. It prefers partial shade to full sun and well-drained soil.

Deutzia henryi is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive floral display and moderate growth habit.
