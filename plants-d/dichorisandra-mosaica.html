Dichorisandra mosaica

Dichorisandra mosaica, commonly known as the variegated kalanchoe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces showy, daisy-like flowers in shades of pink, red, or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
