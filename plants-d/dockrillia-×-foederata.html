Dockrillia × foederata

Dockrillia × foederata, commonly known as the hybrid dockrillia.

This plant is a natural hybrid resulting from the cross between two species of the Dockrillia genus. It typically exhibits characteristics of both parent species, including a robust growth habit and distinctive foliage. The leaves are usually large, lobed, and have a rough texture, often with a bluish-green hue.

It is primarily found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and streambanks.
