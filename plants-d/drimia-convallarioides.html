Drimia convallarioides

Drimia convallarioides, commonly known as the African lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features long, strap-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color, and it produces clusters of white or pale pink flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
