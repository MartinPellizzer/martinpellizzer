Daviesia flexuosa

Daviesia flexuosa, commonly known as the zigzag hakea, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzagging stems and narrow, needle-like leaves. The plant produces yellow or orange flowers in spring, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It is adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in heathlands and coastal areas.

Daviesia flexuosa is a member of the Proteaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance.
