Dendranthema erubescens

Dendranthema erubescens, commonly known as the scarlet gerbera, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like blooms in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features upright stems and heart-shaped, dark green leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Its flowers are not only ornamental but also attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
