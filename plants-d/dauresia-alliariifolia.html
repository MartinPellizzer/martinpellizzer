Dauresia alliariifolia

Dauresia alliariifolia, commonly known as the garlic chive or Chinese chive, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, grass-like leaves that resemble those of chives. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and is often cultivated for its edible leaves and ornamental value.

Dauresia alliariifolia is a hardy perennial that is easy to grow in gardens and container settings.
