Deiregyne pseudopyramidalis

Deiregyne pseudopyramidalis, commonly known as the false pyramid sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as swampy forests and along riverbanks. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that form dense clumps, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets.
