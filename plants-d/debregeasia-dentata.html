Debregeasia dentata

Debregeasia dentata, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to eastern and southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features basal leaves that are often hairy and have a rough texture. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in dense clusters, and bloom during the dry season.

Debregeasia dentata is often used in traditional medicine and as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance.
