Dieffenbachia liesneri

Dieffenbachia liesneri, commonly known as the Leopard Plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that display a combination of white, yellow, and green patterns. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other popular houseplants like peace lilies. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity to maintain its vibrant foliage.

Dieffenbachia liesneri is relatively low-maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
