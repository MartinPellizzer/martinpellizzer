Disa vigilans

Disa vigilans, commonly known as the night-blooming disa, is a striking South African orchid species renowned for its vibrant, showy flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to the Eastern Cape and Western Cape provinces. The plant produces large, pendulous blooms that open at night, emitting a sweet fragrance to attract nocturnal pollinators. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, often forming a rosette at the base.

Disa vigilans thrives in well-drained soil and requires a sunny to partially shaded location.
