Draco umbraculifera

Draco umbraculifera, commonly known as the umbrella tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive umbrella-like inflorescence, which consists of numerous small, white flowers arranged in a compact cluster. The plant produces a unique, fleshy, yellowish fruit that contains several seeds. Its leaves are thick and waxy, adapted to retain moisture in its native environment.

Draco umbraculifera is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
