Dasyphyllum retinens

Dasyphyllum retinens, commonly known as the reticulated dasyphyllum, is a unique plant species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its intricate, net-like leaf venation, which gives it its scientific name. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical forests. Its leaves are usually thick and fleshy, adapted to conserve moisture in its native habitat.

Dasyphyllum retinens is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
