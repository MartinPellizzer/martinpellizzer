Daviesia ulicifolia subsp. ruscifolia

Daviesia ulicifolia subsp.

ruscifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved daviesia, is a shrub native to southern Australia. It typically grows in heathlands and dry forests, preferring well-drained soils.

The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. Its flowers are yellow and appear in clusters, blooming from late summer to autumn.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature.
