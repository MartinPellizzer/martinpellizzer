Dolichos abyssinicus

Dolichos abyssinicus, commonly known as the Abyssinian runner bean, is a leguminous plant native to Ethiopia.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing vines and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant produces elongated, bean-like pods that contain edible seeds, which are rich in protein and nutrients. Its leaves are trifoliate, with heart-shaped leaflets, and it thrives in warm, tropical climates.

Dolichos abyssinicus is often cultivated for both its nutritional value and ornamental appeal.
