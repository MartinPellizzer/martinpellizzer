Deutzia × rosea

Deutzia × rosea, commonly known as the rose deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy clusters of pink or white flowers that bloom in early summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. The plant has slender, arching branches and small, ovate leaves with serrated edges. It typically grows to a height of 3 to 6 feet and thrives in partial to full sun.

Deutzia × rosea is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
