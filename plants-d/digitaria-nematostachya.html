Digitaria nematostachya

Digitaria nematostachya, commonly known as slender digitgrass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, two-lobed flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open fields, pastures, and disturbed areas.

Digitaria nematostachya is valued for its adaptability and is sometimes used in forage or as a weed in agricultural settings.
