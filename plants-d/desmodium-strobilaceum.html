Desmodium strobilaceum

Desmodium strobilaceum, commonly known as the silky desmodium, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, spherical flower clusters that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or grasslands.

Desmodium strobilaceum is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial in agricultural settings.
