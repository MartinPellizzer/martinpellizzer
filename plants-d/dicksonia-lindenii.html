Dicksonia lindenii

Dicksonia lindenii, commonly known as the Lindenberg's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical rainforests of northeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant has a distinctive crown of spirally arranged fronds that give it a unique, elegant appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires high humidity and well-drained soil.

Dicksonia lindenii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage and slow growth rate.
