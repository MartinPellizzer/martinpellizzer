Dichanthelium vernale

Dichanthelium vernale, commonly known as the early dichanthelium, is a grass species native to North America.

It is a low-growing perennial plant that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers, which are wind-pollinated. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue and a prominent midrib.

Dichanthelium vernale is often found in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.
