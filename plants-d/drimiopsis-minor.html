Drimiopsis minor

Drimiopsis minor, commonly known as the dwarf drimiopsis, is a compact, evergreen perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of narrow, glossy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces small, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It is well adapted to dry conditions and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

Drimiopsis minor is popular in rock gardens and as a container plant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
