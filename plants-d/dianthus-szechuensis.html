Dianthus szechuensis

Dianthus szechuensis, commonly known as the Szechuan pink, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink or red flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and has hairy stems and leaves. It produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in early summer.

Dianthus szechuensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
