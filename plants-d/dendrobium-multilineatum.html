Dendrobium multilineatum

Dendrobium multilineatum, commonly known as the multilined orchid, is a sympodial epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a tall, wiry stem with multiple nodes and produces numerous fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media. It blooms once or twice a year, typically during the summer or early autumn.

Its unique appearance and long-lasting blooms make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
