Dendrobium bigibbum var. albopurpuratum

Dendrobium bigibbum var. albopurpuratum, commonly known as the Purple-tinged Iron Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a tall, erect stem with numerous fleshy, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy bloom. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, pale yellow flowers with distinctive purple markings, which bloom in summer. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
