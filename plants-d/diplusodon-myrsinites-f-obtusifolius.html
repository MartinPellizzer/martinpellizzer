Diplusodon myrsinites f. obtusifolius

Diplusodon myrsinites f. obtusifolius, commonly known as the broad-leaved myrsinaceae, is a variant of the species characterized by its broad, obtuse leaves.

This plant belongs to the Myrsinaceae family, which is known for its diverse range of shrubs and herbs. The leaves are typically thick and succulent, adapted to retain moisture in arid environments. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences.
