Drummondita microphylla

Drummondita microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved drummondia, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Texas and New Mexico.

The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in sandy or rocky soils, making it well adapted to its harsh desert environment. Drummondita microphylla is often found in open plains and along dry streambeds.
