Drosanthemum thudichumii f. aureum

Drosanthemum thudichumii f. aureum, commonly known as the golden drosanthemum, is a hybrid succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems covered in dense, woolly hairs that help reduce water loss. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a striking visual element to its appearance. Its leaves are typically green but may exhibit a slight variegation in some cultivars.

It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it an ideal choice for rock gardens or xeriscaping.
