Desmodium triquetrum var. pseudotriquetrum

Desmodium triquetrum var. pseudotriquetrum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf desmodium, is a leguminous plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, with each leaflet being narrow and lanceolate in shape. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

This species is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in agricultural settings.
