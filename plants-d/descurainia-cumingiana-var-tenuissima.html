Descurainia cumingiana var. tenuissima

Descurainia cumingiana var. tenuissima, commonly known as slender descurainia, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia.

It is a low-growing annual herb with slender, branched stems and small, linear leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes, which bloom during the summer months. It is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with poor soil conditions.

This species is of interest in botanical studies due to its unique morphology and ecological adaptations.
