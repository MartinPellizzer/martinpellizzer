Dovea nitida

Dovea nitida, commonly known as the silverleaf dovea, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Dovea nitida is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
