Dendrochilum clemensiae

Dendrochilum clemensiae, commonly known as the Philippine orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange. Its roots are often covered in a dense network of mycorrhizal fungi, which aid in nutrient absorption.

Dendrochilum clemensiae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
