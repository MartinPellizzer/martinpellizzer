Draba × okamotoi

Draba × okamotoi, commonly known as the hybrid rockcress, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between Draba nemorosa and Draba olympica, characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine regions, preferring well-drained soils and rocky habitats. It produces small, yellow flowers in early spring, followed by tiny, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds.

Due to its limited distribution and specific ecological requirements, Draba × okamotoi is considered a rare and endangered species in certain regions.
