Daphne × deylii

Daphne × deylii, commonly known as the Chinese daphne, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and are typically borne in clusters. The plant has elliptical, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Daphne × deylii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and winter interest.
