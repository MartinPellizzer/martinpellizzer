Desmos cochinchinensis var. grandifolia

Desmos cochinchinensis var. grandifolia, commonly known as the large-flowered desmos, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
