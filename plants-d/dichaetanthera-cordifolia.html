Dichaetanthera cordifolia

Dichaetanthera cordifolia, commonly known as the heart-shaped orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
