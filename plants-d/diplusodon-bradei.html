Diplusodon bradei

Diplusodon bradei, commonly known as the Brazilian orchid, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers. The plant typically grows in humid tropical environments, often in shaded forest understories. Its roots are specialized for absorbing nutrients from the soil, and it relies on mycorrhizal fungi for nutrient uptake.

Diplusodon bradei is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses worldwide.
