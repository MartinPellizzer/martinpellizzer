Deschampsia aenea

Deschampsia aenea, commonly known as silver hair grass, is a perennial grass species native to the northern hemisphere.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as wet meadows and stream banks. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bloom in late summer.

Deschampsia aenea is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in supporting local wildlife.
