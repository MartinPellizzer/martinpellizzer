Dunbaria nivea

Dunbaria nivea, commonly known as the white dunbaria, is a member of the Apiaceae family, characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves.

It is native to the mountainous regions of South America, particularly in Chile and Argentina. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Dunbaria nivea thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest understories.
