Dichopsis laevifolia

Dichopsis laevifolia, commonly known as the smoothleaf dichopsis, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed leaves that are smooth and waxy. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange, typically during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Dichopsis laevifolia is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
