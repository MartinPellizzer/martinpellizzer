Dicranopteris linearis var. brevis

Dicranopteris linearis var. brevis, commonly known as the broad leaved water fern, is a variant of the water fern species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a fern that typically grows in wet, shady environments such as marshes and along stream banks. The plant features large, lance-shaped, serrated leaves that are often floating or submerged in water. Its fronds are usually dark green and can reach lengths of up to several feet.

This variety is characterized by its shorter, broader leaves compared to the typical form of Dicranopteris linearis.
