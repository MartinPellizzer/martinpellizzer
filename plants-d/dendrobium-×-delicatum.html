Dendrobium × delicatum

Dendrobium × delicatum, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on the surface of trees or other structures without being parasitic. The plant produces tall, branched stems that bear numerous showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and yellow. Its flowers are typically long-lasting and bloom in late winter to early spring.

Dendrobium × delicatum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
