Dendrochilum warrenii

Dendrochilum warrenii, commonly known as the Warren's tree orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. This epiphytic orchid typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually small and fragrant, with a distinctive coloration that ranges from white to pale pink.

The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
