Dillenia scortechinii

Dillenia scortechinii, commonly known as the giant dillenia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in diameter. The plant produces showy, yellowish-white flowers with prominent stamens, which are typically arranged in a spiral pattern. Its fruit is a distinctive, fleshy, spiny capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Dillenia scortechinii is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
