Dolichos lablab

Dolichos lablab, commonly known as hyacinth bean, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its climbing habit, with pinnately compound leaves and showy, pea-like flowers that range from white to violet in color. The plant produces elongated, green pods containing edible seeds that are rich in protein and often used in cuisines across Africa and Asia. Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it a valuable crop for soil enrichment.

Additionally, it is cultivated for both ornamental and nutritional purposes.
