Digitaria sanguinalis var. glabra

Digitaria sanguinalis var. glabra, commonly known as smooth barnyardgrass, is a perennial grass species native to warm temperate and tropical regions.

It is characterized by its smooth, hairless leaves and cylindrical, spike-like inflorescence. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils, such as fields, roadsides, and agricultural areas. Its rapid growth and competitive nature make it a common weed in cultivated lands.

It produces small, wind-dispersed seeds that contribute to its widespread distribution.
