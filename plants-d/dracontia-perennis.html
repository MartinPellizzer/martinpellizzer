Dracontia perennis

Dracontia perennis, commonly known as the everlasting grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, grass-like stems that grow close to the ground, often forming dense mats. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish or brownish in color. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, adapted to thrive in dry, sandy environments.

Dracontia perennis is known for its ability to survive in harsh conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
