Dictyospermum vaginatum

Dictyospermum vaginatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is native to parts of Africa and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces berries that are typically green when immature and turn red when ripe. Its leaves are narrow and have a slightly hairy texture, and it thrives in dry, arid environments.

While not widely cultivated, it is sometimes found in disturbed soils and can be invasive in certain regions.
