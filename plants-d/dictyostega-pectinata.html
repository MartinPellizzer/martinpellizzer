Dictyostega pectinata

Dictyostega pectinata, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a species of flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. It produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or green.

The plant is often found in wetlands and along riverbanks, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils.
