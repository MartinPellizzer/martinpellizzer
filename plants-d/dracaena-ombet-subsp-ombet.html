Dracaena ombet subsp. ombet

Dracaena ombet subsp.

ombet, commonly known as the red dragon tree, is a tropical plant native to Madagascar. It is characterized by its striking red-pink flower bracts that appear in summer.

The plant has narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil. Dracaena ombet subsp.

ombet is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
