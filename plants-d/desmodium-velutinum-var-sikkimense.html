Desmodium velutinum var. sikkimense

Desmodium velutinum var. sikkimense, commonly known as the Sikkim velvet bean, is a herbaceous plant native to the Himalayan region.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces pods that contain small, edible seeds, which are often used in traditional cuisines. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories or on rocky slopes.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it beneficial for soil enrichment in agricultural settings.
