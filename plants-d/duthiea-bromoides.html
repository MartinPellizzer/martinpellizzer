Duthiea bromoides

Duthiea bromoides, commonly known as the dwarf broom.

It is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of central Asia. The plant features small, narrow leaves and produces clusters of yellow flowers in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or alpine settings.

Its compact size and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for horticultural collections.
