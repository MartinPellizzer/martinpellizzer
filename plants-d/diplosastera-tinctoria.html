Diplosastera tinctoria

Diplosastera tinctoria, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically red or orange with a yellow throat, attracting pollinators like bees and birds. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and botanical collections.
