Desmodium purpureum

Desmodium purpureum, commonly known as purple desmodium, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which consist of three small, oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas and is often found in disturbed soils and open areas.

Purple desmodium is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it a valuable plant for soil enrichment.
