Dendrobium coccinellum

Dendrobium coccinellum, commonly known as the red-lip orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit, with cane-like stems that bear clusters of fleshy, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with a distinctive red lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium coccinellum is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
