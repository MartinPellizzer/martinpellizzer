Digitaria formosana var. hirsuta

Digitaria formosana var. hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy digitaria, is a grass species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its coarse, hairy texture and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing numerous tiny flowers.

This variety is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, where it thrives in warm, humid climates.
