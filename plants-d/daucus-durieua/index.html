Daucus Durieua

Daucus durieua is a rare and enigmatic flowering plant native to the arid regions of central Asia. It belongs to the carrot family, known for its diverse and often medicinal species. This plant is characterized by its delicate, pale yellow flowers and finely divided leaves. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered endangered. Its unique adaptations to harsh environments make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
