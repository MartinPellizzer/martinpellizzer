Didymocarpus speciosus

Didymocarpus speciosus, commonly known as the beautiful winged bean, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its striking, winged pods that enclose its seeds. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are often clustered in racemes. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

Didymocarpus speciosus is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its potential agricultural use as a leguminous crop.
