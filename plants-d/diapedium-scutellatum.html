Diapedium scutellatum

Diapedium scutellatum, commonly known as the shield fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit. The plant features a short, creeping rhizome with dense, overlapping fronds that are typically dark green and glossy. Diapedium scutellatum thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.
