Drosanthemum ramosissimum

Drosanthemum ramosissimum, commonly known as the common rock rose, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its fleshy, branching stems and small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping. Its ability to store water in its leaves allows it to survive in arid conditions.

Drosanthemum ramosissimum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its hardy, drought-resistant nature.
