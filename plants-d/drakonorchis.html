Drakonorchis

Drakonorchis, commonly known as the dragon orchid, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

Drakonorchis species are characterized by their striking, often colorful flowers that resemble dragons, hence their common name. They typically grow as epiphytes or lithophytes, attaching themselves to trees or rocks in humid environments.
