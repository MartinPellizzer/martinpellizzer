Dichocarpum fargesii

It belongs to the family Ranunculaceae and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant features palmately compound leaves and produces showy, yellowish-green flowers in early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
