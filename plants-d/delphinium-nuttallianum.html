Delphinium nuttallianum

Delphinium nuttallianum, commonly known as Nuttall's larkspur, is a herbaceous perennial native to the western United States.

It features tall, spindly stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height, adorned with clusters of spurred blue to purple flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically hairy and arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, loamy soils.

Delphinium nuttallianum is part of the Ranunculaceae family and is known for its striking floral display in late spring to early summer.
