Dyckia hassleri

Dyckia hassleri, commonly known as the silver dyckia, is a perennial bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its rosette of narrow, silver-green leaves that are covered in a fine, powdery bloom. The plant produces striking, red-brown flower spikes that emerge from the center of the rosette. Dyckia hassleri is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a desirable addition to both indoor and outdoor collections.
