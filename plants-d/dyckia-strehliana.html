Dyckia strehliana

Dyckia strehliana, commonly known as the Strehliana bromeliad, is a striking evergreen plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a central cup. The leaves are typically dark green with silver or white markings, giving it an ornamental appearance. It produces vibrant orange or red inflorescences that bloom in the summer months.

This plant is well-suited for indoor cultivation and requires bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.
