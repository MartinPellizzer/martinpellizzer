Dipterocarpus oblongifolius

Dipterocarpus oblongifolius, commonly known as the broad-leaf dipterocarp, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. The tree produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. Its fruit is a woody capsule that contains several seeds.

This species is important in tropical rainforests for its ecological role and is also valued for its timber.
