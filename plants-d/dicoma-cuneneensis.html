Dicoma cuneneensis

Dicoma cuneneensis, commonly known as the Cunene daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces yellow-orange flower heads that attract pollinators.

Dicoma cuneneensis is often found in grassland and rocky habitats, showcasing its resilience to harsh conditions.
