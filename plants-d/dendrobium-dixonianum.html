Dendrobium dixonianum

Dendrobium dixonianum, commonly known as the red-stemmed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, grass-like foliage and produces striking clusters of fragrant, red or orange flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and thrives in well-draining soil. Its unique appearance and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for both horticultural collections and botanical gardens.

Dendrobium dixonianum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its aesthetic qualities.
