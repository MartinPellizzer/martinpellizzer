Delphinium × xylorrhizum

Delphinium × xylorrhizum, commonly known as the hybrid delphinium, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that bear clusters of striking blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, with lance-shaped leaves that are deeply lobed. Its flowers are showy and consist of five petals, with a distinctive spurred petal at the back.

Delphinium × xylorrhizum thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
