Deutzia wardiana

Deutzia wardiana, commonly known as the Chinese deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features clusters of white to pink flowers that bloom in early summer, adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant has slender, arching branches and small, ovate leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for a variety of garden settings.

Deutzia wardiana is valued for its delicate floral display and adaptability to different growing conditions.
