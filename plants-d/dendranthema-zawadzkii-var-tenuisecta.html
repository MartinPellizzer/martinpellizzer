Dendranthema zawadzkii var. tenuisecta

Dendranthema zawadzkii var. tenuisecta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved oxeye daisy, is a perennial plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features daisy-like flowers with white petals surrounding a yellow center, blooming from late spring to early autumn. Its leaves are narrow and deeply lobed, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
