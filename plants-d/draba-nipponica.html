Draba nipponica

Draba nipponica, commonly known as Japanese rockcress, is a small flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many species of mustard and cress. This plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils. Its flowers are small, white or pale yellow, and it produces pods that contain several seeds.

Draba nipponica is often found in mountainous regions of Japan, Korea, and China.
