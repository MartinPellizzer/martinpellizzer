Dianthus Superbus Subsp. Alpestris

Dianthus superbus subsp. alpestris is a striking alpine plant known for its vibrant, showy flowers. It thrives in high-altitude environments, often found in mountainous regions across Europe. The plant features delicate pink or red petals that bloom in late summer. Its compact growth habit makes it well-suited for rocky or gravelly soils. This subspecies is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its ecological role in alpine ecosystems.
