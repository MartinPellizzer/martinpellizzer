Daphne bodinieri

Daphne bodinieri, commonly known as the Chinese daphne, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features fragrant, showy flowers that bloom in early spring, typically in shades of pink, white, and red. The plant has smooth, grayish-brown bark and lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

However, it is toxic to both humans and animals, requiring careful handling.
