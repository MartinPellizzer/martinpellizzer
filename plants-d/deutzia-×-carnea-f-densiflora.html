Deutzia × carnea f. densiflora

Deutzia × carnea f. densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered deutzia, is a compact shrub native to China.

It features showy, clustered pink or white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, oval-shaped leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its blossoms. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
