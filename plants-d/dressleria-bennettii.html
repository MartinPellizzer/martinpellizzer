Dressleria bennettii

Dressleria bennettii, commonly known as Bennett's orchid, is a striking terrestrial orchid native to the eastern coast of Australia.

It features a tall, slender stem with a single, showy flower that has a distinctive violet-blue coloration. The plant's leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming a basal rosette. It blooms during the cooler months, typically from late autumn to early spring.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
