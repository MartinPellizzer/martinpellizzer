Dichopogon preissii

Dichopogon preissii, commonly known as the narrow-leafed grass or preiss's grass, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. Dichopogon preissii produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, which are typical of the grass family.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small insects and animals.
