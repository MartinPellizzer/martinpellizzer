Decorsea Meridionalis

Decorsea meridionalis is a rare and enigmatic plant species native to the southern regions of South America. It thrives in high-altitude environments, often found in rocky crevices and mountainous terrains. This plant is known for its unique foliage and striking floral displays, which bloom during the brief summer months. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species. Studying Decorsea meridionalis offers valuable insights into ecological adaptation and conservation in extreme climates.
