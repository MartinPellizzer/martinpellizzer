Delosperma uitenhagense

Delosperma uitenhagense, commonly known as the "ice plant," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of pink, red, and yellow, typically during the summer months. It is a low-growing perennial that forms dense mats, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ability to tolerate drought and poor soil conditions makes it a popular choice for landscaping in dry climates.
