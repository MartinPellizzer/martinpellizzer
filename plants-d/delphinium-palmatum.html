Delphinium palmatum

Delphinium palmatum, commonly known as the tall delphinium, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height, adorned with clusters of vivid blue or purple flowers. The plant features palmately divided leaves, which give it its species name "palmatum," meaning "palm-like.". It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained, fertile soil.

Delphinium palmatum is a popular choice for gardens due to its striking appearance and ability to attract pollinators.
