Dicranostyles laxa

Dicranostyles laxa, commonly known as the slender feather moss, is a small, acrocarpous moss belonging to the family Dicranaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, branching stems and dense clusters of leafy shoots that form a carpet-like growth. The leaves are typically elongated, with a narrow, linear shape and a slightly curved apex. This moss thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found on soil or rock surfaces in temperate regions.

Its simple yet elegant structure makes it a popular choice for both ecological studies and horticultural displays.
