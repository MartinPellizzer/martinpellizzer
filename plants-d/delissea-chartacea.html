Delissea chartacea

Delissea chartacea, commonly known as the paperleaf lily, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are thin and papery in texture, often with a waxy surface. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers during the warm season. Its foliage is often used in floral arrangements due to its aesthetic appeal and durability.

Delissea chartacea is relatively easy to care for, requiring bright indirect light and consistent moisture to thrive.
