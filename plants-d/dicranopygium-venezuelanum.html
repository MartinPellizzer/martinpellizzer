Dicranopygium venezuelanum

Dicranopygium venezuelanum, commonly known as the Venezuelan feather moss, is a species of moss belonging to the family Dicranaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, hair-like leaves that give it a feathery appearance. This plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. It plays an important role in soil stabilization and moisture retention in its habitat.

Dicranopygium venezuelanum is primarily found in the tropical and subtropical regions of South America.
