Diblemma

Diblemma, commonly known as the false dandelion, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves. The plant produces small, yellow composite flowers that bloom in the summer months. Diblemma is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides.

It has a taproot system and is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.
