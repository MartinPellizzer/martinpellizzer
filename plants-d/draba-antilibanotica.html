Draba antilibanotica

Draba antilibanotica, commonly known as the Lebanon rockcress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with basal leaves and small, white or pale yellow flowers. Native to the mountainous regions of the eastern Mediterranean, it thrives in rocky and alpine environments. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Its adaptability to harsh conditions makes it an important component of high-altitude ecosystems.
