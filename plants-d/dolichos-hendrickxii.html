Dolichos hendrickxii

Dolichos hendrickxii, commonly known as the Hendrickson bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features compound leaves with oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant bears elongated, narrow pods that contain small, edible seeds. It is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and thrives in warm, humid climates.

Due to its ornamental value and potential nutritional benefits, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and experimental agriculture.
