Drimia ensifolia

Drimia ensifolia, commonly known as the swordleaf drimia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features long, narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are often bluish-green in color. The plant produces clusters of white or pale purple flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Drimia ensifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
