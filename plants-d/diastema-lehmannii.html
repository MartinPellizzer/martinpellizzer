Diastema lehmannii

Diastema lehmannii, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its blooms are often pink or purple and can last for several weeks during the warmer months.

Diastema lehmannii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
