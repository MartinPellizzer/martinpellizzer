Dracaena tamaranae

Dracaena tamaranae, commonly known as the Tamaranae dracaena, is a species of flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its upright, slender stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Dracaena tamaranae is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.
