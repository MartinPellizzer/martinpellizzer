Dolichos praecox

Dolichos praecox, commonly known as the early dolichos, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and trifoliate leaves, with each leaflet being ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by elongated, bean-like pods containing several seeds. It thrives in warm climates and is often used as a forage crop or in traditional medicine.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological and agricultural contexts.
