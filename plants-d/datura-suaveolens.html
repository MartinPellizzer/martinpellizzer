Datura suaveolens

Datura suaveolens, commonly known as angel's trumpet, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, bell-shaped, fragrant flowers that are typically white or yellow. The plant produces large, spiny fruits that contain toxic seeds. It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is known for its hallucinogenic properties.

Due to its toxicity, it should be handled with care.
