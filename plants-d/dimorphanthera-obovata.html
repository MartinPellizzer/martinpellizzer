Dimorphanthera obovata

Dimorphanthera obovata, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are broad and ovate, with a smooth surface, while its roots are fibrous and adapted for nutrient absorption.
