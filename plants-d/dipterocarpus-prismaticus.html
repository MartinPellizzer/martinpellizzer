Dipterocarpus prismaticus

Dipterocarpus prismaticus, commonly known as the prismatic dipterocarp, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, straight trunk and broad, elliptical leaves that are often glossy in appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release winged seeds.

This species plays an important role in the ecological systems of its native habitats.
