Desmodium wolohoense

Desmodium wolohoense, commonly known as the African velvet bean, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant produces pods that contain several round, smooth seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. Desmodium wolohoense is native to Africa and thrives in tropical and subtropical regions.

It is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for agricultural purposes.
