Dendrobium teretifolium var. teretifolium

Dendrobium teretifolium var. teretifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers. It has a pseudobulb structure that stores water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in variable conditions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
