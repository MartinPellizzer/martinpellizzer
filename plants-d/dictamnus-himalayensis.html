Dictamnus himalayensis

Dictamnus himalayensis, commonly known as the Himalayan dictamnus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rutaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of the Himalayas, including Nepal, Bhutan, and northern India. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, reddish-brown bark and clusters of small, fragrant white or pink flowers. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes.

The leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly serrated edge, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
