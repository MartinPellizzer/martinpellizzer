Distichorchis moquetteana

Distichorchis moquetteana, commonly known as the moquetteana orchid, is a rare and delicate orchid species native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and green. The plant has a unique sympodial growth habit, with each stem producing a single flower at the tip. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, contributing to its overall ornamental appeal.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
