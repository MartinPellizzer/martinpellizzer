Dioscorea cymosula var. duchassaingii

Dioscorea cymosula var. duchassaingii, commonly known as the African yam bean, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves and tuberous roots. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by elongated, bean-like fruits. Its tubers are edible and have been traditionally used as a food source in some regions.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
