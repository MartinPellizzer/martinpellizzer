Deguelia brevipes

Deguelia brevipes, commonly known as the small-stemmed deguelia, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, also known as the legume or bean family, and is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive floral structure. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. Its seeds are encased in pods and are often used in traditional medicine.

Deguelia brevipes is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
