Daemonorops aruensis

Daemonorops aruensis, commonly known as the Aru palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Indonesia, particularly the Aru Islands. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. It produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense clusters, which are followed by elongated, fibrous fruits.

This palm is often used in traditional practices and is valued for its ornamental and practical uses in its native habitat.
