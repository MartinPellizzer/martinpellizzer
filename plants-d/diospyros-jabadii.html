Diospyros jabadii

Diospyros jabadii, commonly known as the African persimmon, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Ebenaceae family, which includes ebony and persimmon trees. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to elliptical in shape. Its small, yellowish-green flowers are followed by small, round fruits that turn red when mature.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
