Dieffenbachia mortoniana

Dieffenbachia mortoniana, commonly known as the Leopard Plant, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that feature a mix of green, white, and yellow patterns. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other popular houseplants like peace lilies. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated as a decorative plant indoors.
