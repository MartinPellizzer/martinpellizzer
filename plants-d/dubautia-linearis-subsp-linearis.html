Dubautia linearis subsp. linearis

Dubautia linearis subsp.

The plant thrives in coastal and mesic habitats, often growing in rocky or sandy soils. Its flowers are typically arranged in dense, terminal clusters and bloom during the summer months. Dubautia linearis subsp.

linearis is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in the ecological diversity of its native environment.
