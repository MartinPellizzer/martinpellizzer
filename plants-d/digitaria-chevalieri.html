Digitaria chevalieri

Digitaria chevalieri, commonly known as the slender digitgrass, is a species of grass native to Africa and parts of the Middle East.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. This plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields. It is adapted to warm climates and can thrive in poor soil conditions.

The botanical features of Digitaria chevalieri make it an important component of grassland ecosystems.
