Dicranopetalum

Dicranopetalum, commonly known as the featherleaf orchid, is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, feather-like leaves that give it its common name. The plants are typically found in tropical and subtropical regions, often growing in shaded, moist environments. Dicranopetalum species produce small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale in color.

These orchids are valued for their ornamental appeal and are often cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
