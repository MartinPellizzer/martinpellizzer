Delphinium linearilobum

Delphinium linearilobum, commonly known as the slenderleaf larkspur, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It features tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces showy blue to purple flowers in raceme clusters, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have finely divided lobes, giving it a distinctive, delicate appearance.

Delphinium linearilobum is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in meadows and open woodlands.
