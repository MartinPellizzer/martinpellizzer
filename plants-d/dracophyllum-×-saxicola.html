Dracophyllum × saxicola

Dracophyllum × saxicola, commonly known as the rock heath.

It is a hybrid shrub native to the coastal regions of southern Australia. The plant is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and small, leathery leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It produces small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring.

Dracophyllum × saxicola is well adapted to sandy, well-drained soils and can thrive in harsh coastal conditions.
