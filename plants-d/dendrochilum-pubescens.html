Dendrochilum pubescens

Dendrochilum pubescens, commonly known as the hairy tree orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and hairy stems, which give it its common name. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with dark markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrochilum pubescens is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
