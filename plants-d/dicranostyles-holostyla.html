Dicranostyles holostyla

Dicranostyles holostyla, commonly known as the slender moss, is a species of moss belonging to the family Dicranaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, elongated stems and small, overlapping leaves that are often curved. The plant forms dense, carpet-like colonies on moist, shaded substrates such as soil, rocks, or wood. It is typically found in temperate regions and thrives in humid environments.

Dicranostyles holostyla plays a role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for microorganisms and invertebrates.
