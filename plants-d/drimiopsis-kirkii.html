Drimiopsis kirkii

Drimiopsis kirkii, commonly known as the Kirk's drimiopsis, is a striking evergreen perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, sword-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Drimiopsis kirkii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
