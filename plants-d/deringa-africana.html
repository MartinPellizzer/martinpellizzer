Deringa africana

Deringa africana, commonly known as the African star orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that come in various colors such as yellow, orange, and red. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and has a compact, herbaceous growth habit. Its roots are fibrous, and it thrives in well-drained soil with moderate sunlight.

Deringa africana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
