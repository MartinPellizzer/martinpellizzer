Dipladenia fragrans var. ternatifolia

Dipladenia fragrans var. ternatifolia, commonly known as the fragrant dipladenia, is a climbing vine native to South America.

It is characterized by its fragrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow to orange. The leaves are typically trifoliate or ternate, giving the plant its varietal name. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
