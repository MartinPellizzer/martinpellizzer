Daviesia uniflora

Daviesia uniflora, commonly known as the single-flower daviesia, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow or orange flowers. The plant typically grows in heathlands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are usually narrow and hairy, and it produces small, pod-like fruits that contain seeds.

Daviesia uniflora is often used in native gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
