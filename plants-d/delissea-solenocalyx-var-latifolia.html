Delissea solenocalyx var. latifolia

Delissea solenocalyx var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved delissea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, pale yellow to white flowers. The plant typically grows in shrubland and rocky habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. It is a perennial species that produces seed capsules containing numerous small seeds.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is not widely commercially available.
