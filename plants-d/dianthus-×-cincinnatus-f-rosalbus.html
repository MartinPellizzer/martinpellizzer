Dianthus × cincinnatus f. rosalbus

Dianthus × cincinnatus f. rosalbus, commonly known as the rose campion, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, pink or red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically features hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soils.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
