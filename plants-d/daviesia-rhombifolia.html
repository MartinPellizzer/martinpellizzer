Daviesia rhombifolia

Daviesia rhombifolia, commonly known as the diamond leaf honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive rhomboid-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, attracting nectar-feeding birds and insects. Daviesia rhombifolia is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
