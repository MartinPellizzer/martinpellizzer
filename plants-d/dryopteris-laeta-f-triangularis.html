Dryopteris laeta f. triangularis

Dryopteris laeta f. triangularis, commonly known as the Triangular Lady Fern, is a fern species characterized by its distinctive, triangular-shaped fronds.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is native to parts of Asia, including China and Japan. The plant features delicate, finely divided leaves that are typically bright green and often have a slightly curled appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and its ability to add a lush, tropical feel to landscapes.
