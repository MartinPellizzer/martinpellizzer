Deutzia mollis

Deutzia mollis, commonly known as the Chinese deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features clusters of fragrant, white to pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, arching stems and small, ovate leaves that are typically dark green. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Deutzia mollis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its elegant floral display.
