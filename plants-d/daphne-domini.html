Daphne domini

Daphne domini, commonly known as the Christmas daphne, is a deciduous shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It features dense, evergreen foliage with dark green leaves that are oval in shape. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in winter. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and unique flowering season, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
