Declieuxia spergulifolia var. genuina

Declieuxia spergulifolia var. genuina, commonly known as the slender swamp grass, is a perennial herb native to wetland habitats in southern Africa.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically green and slightly hairy. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, which are characteristic of the grass family. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in marshes and along stream banks.

Due to its adaptability, it plays a role in stabilizing soil in aquatic environments.
