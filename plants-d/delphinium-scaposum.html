Delphinium scaposum

Delphinium scaposum, commonly known as the tall delphinium, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height, supporting clusters of blue to purple flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem, and its flowers are typically arranged in dense, spires. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden borders and cottage gardens.

Delphinium scaposum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in floral arrangements.
