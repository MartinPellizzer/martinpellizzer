Dovyalis xanthocarpa

Dovyalis xanthocarpa, commonly known as the yellow star apple, is a small evergreen tree native to tropical regions of Africa and southern Asia.

It produces small, yellow, star-shaped fruits that are edible and have a sweet, aromatic flavor. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets and is often cultivated for its ornamental value as well as its fruit. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The fruit is typically harvested when fully yellow, indicating ripeness.
