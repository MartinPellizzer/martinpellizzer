Dieffenbachia seguine subvar. liturata

Dieffenbachia seguine subvar. liturata, commonly known as the Shiny-leaf Dieffenbachia, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves with a distinctive white or silver midrib. The plant is a member of the Araceae family, which includes many ornamental and decorative plants. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its striking foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and decorative purposes.
