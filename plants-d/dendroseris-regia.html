Dendroseris regia

Dendroseris regia, commonly known as the royal tree fern, is a striking member of the Cyatheaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and conservation efforts.

Dendroseris regia plays an important ecological role in its native habitat by providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
