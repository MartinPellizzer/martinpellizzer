Dioscorea armata

Dioscorea armata, commonly known as the arrowhead yam, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the Dioscoreaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces tuberous roots that are often used as a food source in some cultures. These roots are typically harvested after the plant has flowered and set seed.

Dioscorea armata is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
