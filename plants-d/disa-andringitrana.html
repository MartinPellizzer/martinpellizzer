Disa andringitrana

Disa andringitrana, commonly known as the Red Disa, is a striking orchid native to the eastern regions of South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers with contrasting white markings, which are highly attractive to pollinators. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in mountainous areas with high altitudes. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, forming a basal rosette that helps it conserve moisture.

Disa andringitrana is a popular horticultural plant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
