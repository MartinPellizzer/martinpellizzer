Deguelia Amoena

Deguelia amoena is a striking tropical plant known for its vibrant, showy flowers and unique growth habits. Native to the rainforests of Central and South America, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are large and palmately lobed, adding to its ornamental appeal. The plant is also notable for its medicinal properties, traditionally used by indigenous communities. Despite its beauty, it contains toxic compounds, requiring careful handling and cultivation.
