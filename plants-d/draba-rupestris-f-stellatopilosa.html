Draba rupestris f. stellatopilosa

Draba rupestris f. stellatopilosa, commonly known as the star-pilose rock cress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial with dense, compact foliage and small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive, woolly appearance. It typically grows in rocky or gravelly habitats, thriving in well-drained soils.

This subspecies is noted for its ornamental value and is often found in alpine or subalpine regions.
