Deutzia scabra var. sieboldiana

Deutzia scabra var. sieboldiana, commonly known as Siebold's spiny deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features slender, arching stems with small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has rough, scaly bark and narrow, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its delicate floral display.
