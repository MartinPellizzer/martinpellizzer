Diplotropis nitida

Diplotropis nitida, commonly known as the slenderleaf ironwood, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has small, simple leaves that are usually opposite or alternate in arrangement. Its flowers are clustered in panicles and are often yellow or greenish in color.

Diplotropis nitida is valued for its hard wood and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
