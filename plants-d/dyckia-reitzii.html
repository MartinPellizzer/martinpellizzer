Dyckia reitzii

Dyckia reitzii, commonly known as the Reitz's dyckia, is a species of flowering plant in the bromeliad family.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of white, woolly hairs. The plant produces striking inflorescences with bright orange-red flowers that bloom in late summer. It is a hardy, drought-tolerant plant that thrives in full sun and well-draining soil.

Dyckia reitzii is popular among succulent and bromeliad enthusiasts for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
