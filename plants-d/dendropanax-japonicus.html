Dendropanax japonicus

Dendropanax japonicus, commonly known as Japanese arrowhead, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves that are often glossy and dark green. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, appearing in clusters, and it produces red berries that are edible for some bird species.

Dendropanax japonicus is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to various soil conditions.
