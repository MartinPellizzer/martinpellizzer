Dendrobium scotiiforme var. tenuifolium

Dendrobium scotiiforme var. tenuifolium, commonly known as the slender-leafed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically light green in color. The plant produces pendulous, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with a yellow throat. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
