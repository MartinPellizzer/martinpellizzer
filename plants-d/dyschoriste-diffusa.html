Dyschoriste diffusa

Dyschoriste diffusa, commonly known as the slender dyschoriste, is a perennial herb native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and opposite, lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
