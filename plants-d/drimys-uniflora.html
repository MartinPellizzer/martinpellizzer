Drimys uniflora

Drimys uniflora, commonly known as the single-flower drimys, is a species of flowering plant native to South America, particularly Chile and Argentina.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows in moist, temperate climates. The plant features glossy, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a dark blue or black berry that contains several seeds.

Drimys uniflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
