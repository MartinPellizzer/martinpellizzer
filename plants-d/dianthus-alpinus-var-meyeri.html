Dianthus alpinus var. meyeri

Dianthus alpinus var. meyeri, commonly known as the Alpine campion, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine displays.

Dianthus alpinus var. meyeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in cooler climates.
