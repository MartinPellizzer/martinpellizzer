Dugandiodendron pallescens

Dugandiodendron pallescens, commonly known as the pale-leaf dugandi, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is a small, deciduous tree native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are often pale green in color. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers clustered in cyathia, typical of the Euphorbiaceae family.

The bark and leaves of Dugandiodendron pallescens have been used in traditional medicine for their potential therapeutic properties.
