Dipodium Punctatum Var. Squamatum

Dipodium punctatum var. squamatum is a unique fern species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia. It is distinguished by its distinctive leaf patterns and compact growth habit. The plant's name reflects its characteristic markings and scaled appearance. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is often sought after by plant enthusiasts.
