Diosphaera asperuloides

Diosphaera asperuloides, commonly known as the roughleaf diosphaera, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and dark green, leathery leaves with a rough texture. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It is adapted to dry, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.

Diosphaera asperuloides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and unique foliage.
