Dimorphanthera profusa

Dimorphanthera profusa, commonly known as the broad-leaved lily.

It is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern China. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, with six petals arranged in a showy cluster.

Dimorphanthera profusa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate beauty.
