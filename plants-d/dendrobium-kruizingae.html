Dendrobium kruizingae

Dendrobium kruizingae, commonly known as the Kruizinga's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit, with cane-like stems that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a contrasting purple or red lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Dendrobium kruizingae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its long-lasting blooms.
