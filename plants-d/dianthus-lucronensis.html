Dianthus lucronensis

Dianthus lucronensis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved campion, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family, characterized by its showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs, while its stems are upright and branched.

Dianthus lucronensis is often found in open habitats such as meadows, cliffs, and woodland edges.
