Delosperma ficksbergense

Delosperma ficksbergense, commonly known as the Ficksberg carpetweed, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. The plant produces vibrant pink to purple flowers during the summer months, which are borne on short, upright stalks. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it an attractive addition to arid landscapes.
