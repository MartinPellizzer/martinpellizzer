Dyckia reflexa

Dyckia reflexa, commonly known as the reflexed dyckia, is a species of flowering plant in the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming growth habit. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine, white hairs. It produces inflorescences that bear small, white or pale pink flowers.

Dyckia reflexa is well adapted to dry environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal.
