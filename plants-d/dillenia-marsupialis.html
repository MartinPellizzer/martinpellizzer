Dillenia marsupialis

Dillenia marsupialis, commonly known as the giant dillenia, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces showy, yellowish-white flowers with five petals, which bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a distinctive, woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

The plant is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental and ecological significance.
