Dendrochilum irigense

Dendrochilum irigense, commonly known as the Irigens orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically produces bright yellow or orange flowers with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Dendrochilum irigense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
