Dipladenia tenuifolia

Dipladenia tenuifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed Dipladenia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant orange to yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The leaves are narrow and glossy, often with a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in warm, sunny conditions and is often cultivated as a decorative trailing plant.

This species is also valued for its ability to produce abundant flowers throughout the growing season.
