Duchesnea sundaica

Duchesnea sundaica, commonly known as the Sunda strawberry, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, edible berries that are typically red when ripe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
