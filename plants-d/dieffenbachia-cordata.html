Dieffenbachia cordata

Dieffenbachia cordata, commonly known as the Leopard Plant or African Mask, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically a mix of bright green and white or cream-colored markings. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers warm, humid environments with well-draining soil. It is a popular indoor plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.

Dieffenbachia cordata is also noted for its ability to purify the air, making it a favored choice for home and office interiors.
