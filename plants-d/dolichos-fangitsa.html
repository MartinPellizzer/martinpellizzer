Dolichos fangitsa

Dolichos fangitsa, commonly known as the slender cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that develop into elongated, pod-like fruits containing several seeds. Dolichos fangitsa is valued for its edible seeds, which are rich in protein and used in various culinary applications.

It also plays a role in soil enrichment due to its nitrogen-fixing ability.
