Disperis bosseri

Disperis bosseri, commonly known as the Bosser's orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of eastern Africa.

The plant has a compact growth habit with narrow, grass-like leaves and relies on specific pollinators for reproduction. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Disperis bosseri is considered endangered and is often found in protected areas.
