Dianthus versicolor var. ninelli

Dianthus versicolor var. ninelli, commonly known as the Ninelli pink, is a charming perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, often with contrasting veining. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are slightly hairy, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its short stature and vibrant blooms make it a valuable addition to both garden landscapes and floral arrangements.
