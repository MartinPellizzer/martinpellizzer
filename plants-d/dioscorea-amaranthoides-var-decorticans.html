Dioscorea amaranthoides var. decorticans

Dioscorea amaranthoides var. decorticans, commonly known as the African yam bean, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellowish flowers and cylindrical, fleshy fruits that contain several seeds. Its tubers are edible and have been used traditionally for food and medicinal purposes.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
