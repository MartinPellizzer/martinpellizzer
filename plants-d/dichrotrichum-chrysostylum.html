Dichrotrichum chrysostylum

Dichrotrichum chrysostylum, commonly known as the golden thread grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and produces small, yellowish-green inflorescences. The plant is native to southern Africa and thrives in sandy or rocky soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are clustered in dense, spike-like structures.

Dichrotrichum chrysostylum is often used in ornamental gardening for its delicate appearance and adaptability to arid conditions.
