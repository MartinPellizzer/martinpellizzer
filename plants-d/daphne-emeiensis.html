Daphne emeiensis

Daphne emeiensis, commonly known as the Emei daphne, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Thymelaeaceae and is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, woody capsules as fruits, which contain numerous seeds. It typically grows in mountainous regions and is valued for its ornamental qualities.
