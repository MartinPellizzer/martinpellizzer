Dianthus monspessulanus var. oliganthus

Dianthus monspessulanus var. oliganthus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved campion, is a perennial flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It features slender, upright stems covered in dense clusters of small, pink to red flowers. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or borders.

Its compact growth habit and long blooming period make it a desirable ornamental plant.
