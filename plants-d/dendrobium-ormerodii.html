Dendrobium ormerodii

Dendrobium ormerodii, commonly known as the Ormerod's dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with cane-like stems that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink, typically blooming during the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, adapted to climbing and photosynthesis in shaded environments.

Dendrobium ormerodii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
