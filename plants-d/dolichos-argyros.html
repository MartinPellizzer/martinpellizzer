Dolichos argyros

Dolichos argyros, commonly known as silver bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It features compound leaves with elongated, pointed leaflets and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant bears pods that contain several kidney-shaped seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. Dolichos argyros is adapted to grow in warm, humid climates and thrives in well-drained soil.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species in both ecological and agricultural contexts.
