Draba lanceolata var. chingii

Draba lanceolata var. chingii, commonly known as the Ching's rockcress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically forms dense mats on rocky or gravelly slopes. The plant features basal leaves that are lanceolate in shape, often with a bluish-green hue. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in early spring.

This variety is adapted to alpine and arctic environments, thriving in cold, nutrient-poor soils.
