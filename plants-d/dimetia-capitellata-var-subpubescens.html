Dimetia capitellata var. subpubescens

Dimetia capitellata var. subpubescens, commonly known as the slender bamboo orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The leaves are linear and often covered in a fine, soft pubescence, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
