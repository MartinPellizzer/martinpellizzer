Dorstenia poggei

Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and clustered in a spiky inflorescence, which attracts pollinators like bees and wasps.

Dorstenia poggei is often grown as a decorative houseplant due to its unusual appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
