Dendrobium araneola

Dendrobium araneola, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome that produces multiple pseudobulbs. The plant produces showy, spider-like flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Dendrobium araneola is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
