Diuris micrantha

Diuris micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered greenhood, is an orchid species native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its unique hood-shaped labellum and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in heathlands and open forests, preferring well-drained soils. Diuris micrantha has hairy stems and narrow, grass-like leaves.

It is a short-lived perennial, often flowering in spring and early summer.
