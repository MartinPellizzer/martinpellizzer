Dyckia scapigera

Dyckia scapigera, commonly known as the Cape Flats dyckia, is a species of flowering plant in the bromeliad family.

It is native to South Africa, particularly the Cape region, and thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. It produces striking inflorescences with red or orange flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring.

Dyckia scapigera is drought-tolerant and well-suited for rock gardens or xeriscaping.
