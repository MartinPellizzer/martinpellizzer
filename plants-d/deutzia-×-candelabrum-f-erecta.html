Deutzia × candelabrum f. erecta

Deutzia × candelabrum f. erecta, commonly known as the upright form of the candelabra deutzia, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to China.

It features clusters of fragrant, white to pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer, creating a striking visual display. The plant has upright, branching stems that give it a tree-like appearance, making it suitable for small spaces or as a specimen plant. Its foliage is dark green and provides attractive contrast to the flowers.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a versatile choice for gardens.
