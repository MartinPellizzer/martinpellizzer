Didymoplexis flexipes

Didymoplexis flexipes, commonly known as the flexible didymoplex, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique floral structure and climbing habit make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
