Diplerisma comosum

Diplerisma comosum, commonly known as the white spider plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery bloom. The plant produces delicate, spider-like flowers that are typically white or pale pink, giving it its common name. It is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sun.

Diplerisma comosum is often used in rock gardens or as a ground cover due to its ornamental appeal and resilience.
