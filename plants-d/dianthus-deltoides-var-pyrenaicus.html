Dianthus deltoides var. pyrenaicus

Dianthus deltoides var. pyrenaicus, commonly known as the Pyrenean campion, is a flowering plant native to the Pyrenees region.

It is a low-growing perennial with pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a slightly fuzzy appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant flowers and compact growth habit.
