Diasperus compressicaulis

Diasperus compressicaulis, commonly known as the compact stem cactus, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its short, thick stems that are often covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom during the warmer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water to survive.

Its unique morphology makes it a distinctive addition to succulent collections.
