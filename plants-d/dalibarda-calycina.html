Dalibarda calycina

Dalibarda calycina, commonly known as the spring beauty, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to eastern North America.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, early spring blooms. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist woodlands and has small, heart-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with five petals and numerous stamens.

Dalibarda calycina is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
