Diplomorpha bicornuta

Diplomorpha bicornuta, commonly known as the two-horned orchid, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive two-horned structure on its flower spike. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests or mountainous areas. Its flowers are usually small and fragrant, with a unique coloration that varies depending on the species.

Diplomorpha bicornuta is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by enthusiasts of rare and exotic plants.
