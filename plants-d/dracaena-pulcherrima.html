Dracaena pulcherrima

Dracaena pulcherrima, commonly known as the flamingo plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Madagascar.

It features long, narrow leaves that are typically bright green with red or orange edges, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance of low light and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.

Dracaena pulcherrima is also valued for its air-purifying properties and ability to enhance interior spaces with its vibrant foliage.
