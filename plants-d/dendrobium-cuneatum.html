Dendrobium cuneatum

Dendrobium cuneatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with long, cane-like stems that store water and nutrients. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. It thrives in warm, humid climates with bright indirect light and well-draining soil.

Dendrobium cuneatum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
