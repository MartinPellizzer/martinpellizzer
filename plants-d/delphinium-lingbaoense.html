Delphinium lingbaoense

Delphinium lingbaoense, commonly known as the Lingbao larkspur, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, spindly stems and showy blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky slopes at high elevations. Its leaves are compound, with serrated edges, and it produces numerous small, fluffy seeds in its fruit.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
