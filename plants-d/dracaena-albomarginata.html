Dracaena albomarginata

Dracaena albomarginata, commonly known as the white-edged dracaena, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features tall, slender stems with lance-shaped leaves that have a striking white or yellow edge. The plant is characterized by its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its moderate water requirements. Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal.

Dracaena albomarginata is also known for its air-purifying properties, making it a popular choice for indoor spaces.
