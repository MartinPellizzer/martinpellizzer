Deutzia Nakaiana Var. Pubescens

Japanese deutzia, scientifically known as Deutzia nakaiana var. pubescens, is a striking ornamental shrub native to Japan. It is prized for its clusters of fragrant white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves that turn a vibrant red in the fall. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and is relatively low maintenance. With its graceful appearance and seasonal beauty, Japanese deutzia is a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
