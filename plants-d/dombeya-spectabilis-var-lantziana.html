Dombeya spectabilis var. lantziana

Dombeya spectabilis var. lantziana, commonly known as the Lantziana dombeya, is a shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its showy, fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
