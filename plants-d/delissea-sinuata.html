Delissea sinuata

Delissea sinuata, commonly known as the zigzag fern, is a tropical fern species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, undulating fronds that give it its common name. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with long, creeping stems that produce new shoots. Its leaves are typically bipinnate, with a delicate, feathery appearance.

Delissea sinuata thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
