Dasycondylus dusenii

Dasycondylus dusenii, commonly known as the Dusen's thistle, is a perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, with a robust stem and spiny foliage. Its flowers are yellow and bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators.

The species is adapted to high-altitude environments, showcasing resilience to cold and arid conditions.
