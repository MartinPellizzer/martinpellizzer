Dieffenbachia picta f. shuttleworthii

Dieffenbachia picta f. shuttleworthii, commonly known as the Shuttleworth's picture dieffenbachia, is a hybrid cultivar of the Dieffenbachia genus.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that display a mix of green, white, and cream colors. The plant is native to the tropical regions of South America and thrives in warm, humid environments. It is a popular houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

The foliage is typically broad and arrow-shaped, with a glossy texture that enhances its visual appeal.
