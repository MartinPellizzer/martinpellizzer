Dendrobium subclausum var. speciosum

Dendrobium subclausum var. speciosum, commonly known as the Orchid of the Southern Highlands, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with branched pseudobulbs and long, narrow leaves. The plant produces striking inflorescences with numerous fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular in horticulture but is also threatened by habitat loss.
