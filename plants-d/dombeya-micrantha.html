Dombeya micrantha

Dombeya micrantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved dombeya, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, often appearing in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in shrubland or woodland habitats, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and opposite, with a slightly hairy surface.

Dombeya micrantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive blooms.
