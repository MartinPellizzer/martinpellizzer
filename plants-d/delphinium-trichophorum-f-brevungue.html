Delphinium trichophorum f. brevungue

Delphinium trichophorum f. brevungue, commonly known as the short-stemmed delphinium, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features tall, sparsely branched stems that bear clusters of striking blue to purple flowers. The leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. This cultivar is valued for its ornamental value in gardens and its ability to thrive in well-drained, alkaline soils.

It is also noted for its relatively compact growth habit compared to other delphinium species.
