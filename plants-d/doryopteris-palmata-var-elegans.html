Doryopteris palmata var. elegans

Doryopteris palmata var. elegans, commonly known as the elegant fern, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It features fronds that are deeply divided into narrow, arching segments, giving it a delicate, lacy appearance. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and prefers well-draining, humus-rich soil. Its foliage is evergreen, providing year-round visual interest in gardens or indoor spaces.

This variety is prized for its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to humid, tropical climates.
