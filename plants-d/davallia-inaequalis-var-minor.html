Davallia inaequalis var. minor

Davallia inaequalis var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf serrate huperzia, is a small fern species native to Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, lance-shaped fronds with serrated margins that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest undergrowth. Its rhizomes are wiry and often form dense, low-growing clumps.

It is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of care in indoor settings.
