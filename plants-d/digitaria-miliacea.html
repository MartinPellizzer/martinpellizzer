Digitaria miliacea

Digitaria miliacea, commonly known as milletgrass, is a species of grass native to warm temperate and tropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences containing numerous tiny florets. It is commonly found in disturbed soils and is often used as a forage crop.

Digitaria miliacea is also known for its adaptability to various environmental conditions.
