Desmos chinensis var. brevifolius

Desmos chinensis var. brevifolius, commonly known as the short-leaved Chinese desmos, is a shrub native to southern China.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are typically less than 2 cm in length. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. Its foliage is often used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.

The species is valued for both its ornamental appeal and medicinal properties.
