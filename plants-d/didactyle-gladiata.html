Didactyle gladiata

Didactyle gladiata, commonly known as the sword fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its sword-shaped, lanceolate leaves that are typically bright green and glossy. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with creeping stems that produce new fronds along their length. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in temperate regions.

Didactyle gladiata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
