Diplotaxis virgata subsp. cossoniana

Diplotaxis virgata subsp.

cossoniana, commonly known as the African dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a perennial herb characterized by its upright stems and basal rosette of leaves.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in a single head, typical of the Asteraceae family. Its leaves are usually linear to lanceolate and covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.
