Diabelia ionostachya var. ionostachya

Diabelia ionostachya var. ionostachya, commonly known as the narrow-leaved diabelia, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that bear opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink to purple, typically blooming from late spring to early summer. Its fibrous root system allows it to thrive in well-drained soils and full sun conditions.

It is often found in rocky or dry habitats, making it a hardy and drought-tolerant species.
