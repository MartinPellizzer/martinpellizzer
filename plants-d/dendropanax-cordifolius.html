Dendropanax cordifolius

Dendropanax cordifolius, commonly known as the heartleaf arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often glossy and deeply veined. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by dark purple berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its rhizomes are used in traditional medicine and as a source of starch.
