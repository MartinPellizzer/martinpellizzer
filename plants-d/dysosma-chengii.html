Dysosma chengii

Dysosma chengii, commonly known as the Cheng's dysosma, is a member of the Aristolochiaceae family.

It is a climbing herb native to China, particularly found in the mountainous regions of Sichuan and Yunnan provinces. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and unique, vine-like growth habit. Its flowers are typically greenish-white with a distinctive, intricate structure.

Dysosma chengii is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
