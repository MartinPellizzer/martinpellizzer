Diplostelma radians

Diplostelma radians, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a short rhizome and several pseudobulbs. The plant produces striking yellow flowers with a distinctive star-shaped pattern on the petals and sepals. Its leaves are broad, elliptical, and arranged in a rosette formation.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its vibrant, showy blooms.
