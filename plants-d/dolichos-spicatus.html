Dolichos spicatus

Dolichos spicatus, commonly known as the spicate dolichos, is a flowering plant species belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, pale yellow to greenish flowers. The plant produces elongated, spiny pods that contain several seeds. Native to parts of Africa and Asia, it thrives in arid and semi-arid environments.

Dolichos spicatus is often used in traditional medicine and as a forage plant for livestock.
