Dialium zenkeri

Dialium zenkeri, commonly known as the African cherry or Zanzibar cherry, is a tropical tree native to Central and East Africa.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and large, glossy green leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are followed by orange-red, fleshy fruits. It is also valued for its timber and medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
