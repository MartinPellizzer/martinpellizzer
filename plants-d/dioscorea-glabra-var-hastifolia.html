Dioscorea glabra var. hastifolia

Dioscorea glabra var. hastifolia, commonly known as the arrowleaf yam, is a tuberous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces a starchy tuberous root that is used as a food source in some cultures. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires a warm climate with high humidity.

The tubers are often used in traditional medicine and culinary practices.
