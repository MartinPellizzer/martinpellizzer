Desmodium polygonoides

Desmodium polygonoides, commonly known as smallleaf desmodium, is a herbaceous annual plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and nitrogen-fixing capabilities. The plant features trifoliate leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, pea-like flowers in shades of white or pale pink. Its fruit is a legume pod that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.

Desmodium polygonoides is often used in ecological restoration due to its ability to improve soil fertility and prevent erosion.
