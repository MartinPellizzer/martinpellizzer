Drymocallis mollis

Drymocallis mollis, commonly known as silverweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from August to September. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands, streambanks, and meadows.

Drymocallis mollis is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in riparian areas.
