Ditassa anderssonii

Ditassa anderssonii, commonly known as the Andes mountain orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils at elevations above 3,000 meters. Its unique adaptations allow it to thrive in the cold, harsh conditions of its mountainous habitat.
