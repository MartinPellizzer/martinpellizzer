Dactylorhiza maculata var. praecox

Dactylorhiza maculata var. praecox, commonly known as the early spotted orchid, is a species of orchid native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a short, erect stem and a single, showy flower that typically blooms in early spring. The plant has lance-shaped leaves and produces a distinctive, spurred orchid flower with a yellowish-green lip. It thrives in damp, shaded habitats such as woodlands and stream banks.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
