Dichrostachys caffra

Dichrostachys caffra, commonly known as the ironwood tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

The plant has a strong, aromatic scent and is known for its hard, heavy wood, which is highly valued for construction and tool-making.
