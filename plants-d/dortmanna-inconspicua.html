Dortmanna inconspicua

Dortmanna inconspicua, commonly known as the common dock, is a flowering plant in the family Polygonaceae.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist soils along stream banks and wet meadows. The plant has alternate, palmately lobed leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in dense, rounded inflorescences.
