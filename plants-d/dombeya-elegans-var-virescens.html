Dombeya elegans var. virescens

Dombeya elegans var. virescens, commonly known as the greenleaf dombeya, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Africa.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a greenish tinge. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green, contributing to its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

The flowers are attractive to pollinators such as bees and butterflies, enhancing its ecological significance.
