Deutzia breviloba

Deutzia breviloba, commonly known as the dwarf deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, arching stems and dark green foliage that turns yellow in the fall. It typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Deutzia breviloba prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, thriving in temperate climates.
