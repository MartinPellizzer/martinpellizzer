Draba nylamensis

Draba nylamensis, commonly known as the Nylam dwarf scabious, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cress plants. This species is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that bloom in shades of white and purple, typically in the spring. Draba nylamensis thrives in rocky and alpine environments, where it forms dense mats close to the ground.

Its ability to survive in harsh, cold climates makes it an important component of alpine ecosystems.
