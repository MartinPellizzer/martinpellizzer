Diospyros pseudolotus

Diospyros pseudolotus, commonly known as the African date palm, is a deciduous tree native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and pinnate leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by clustered, edible fruits resembling dates.
