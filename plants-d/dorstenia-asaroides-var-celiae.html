Dorstenia asaroides var. celiae

Dorstenia asaroides var. celiae, commonly known as the "Celia's velvetleaf," is a striking tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, white to greenish flowers that are clustered in umbels, typical of the Moraceae family. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique leaf morphology and ornamental appeal make it a sought-after specimen for botanical gardens and collectors.
