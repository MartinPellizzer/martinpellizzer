Ditassa obovata

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its broad, ovate-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.
