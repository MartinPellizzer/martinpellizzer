Donax viridiflavescens

It is native to the Mediterranean region and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, often forming dense mats. It is adapted to saline environments and can tolerate periods of drought.
