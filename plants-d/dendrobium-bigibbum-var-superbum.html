Dendrobium bigibbum var. superbum

Dendrobium bigibbum var. superbum, commonly known as the Giant Dendrobium, is a striking orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant that grows on trees and rocks, thriving in humid tropical climates. The plant features long, cane-like stems with clusters of large, showy flowers that can be pink, red, or white. Its blooms are typically fragrant and can last for several weeks, making it a popular choice for horticultural collections.

The variety superbum is noted for its robust growth and exceptional ornamental value.
