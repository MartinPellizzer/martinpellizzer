Dendrobium Alatum

Dendrobium alatum is a striking epiphytic orchid known for its long, curved leaves that resemble the shape of a sword. Native to Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in humid environments and requires bright, indirect light to flourish. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts and collectors. While it can be challenging to care for, its reward is a stunning display of flowers in shades of white, pink, and yellow. With proper care, Dendrobium alatum can bloom year after year, adding beauty and interest to any indoor or outdoor space.
