Dracaena terminalis alba

Dracaena terminalis alba, commonly known as the White Dracaena, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and long, narrow, light green leaves that give it a striking, elegant appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically followed by red or orange berries. It is relatively low-maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ability to thrive in low light conditions and its air-purifying properties further enhance its appeal.
