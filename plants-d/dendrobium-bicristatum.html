Dendrobium bicristatum

Dendrobium bicristatum, commonly known as the golden spike orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with a dense cluster of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium bicristatum is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a tropical or subtropical climate.
