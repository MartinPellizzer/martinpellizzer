Desmidorchis penicillata

Desmidorchis penicillata, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, strap-like leaves and small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in clusters. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of tree trunks or branches without deriving nutrients from its host. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow, with a distinctive spurred lip.

Desmidorchis penicillata is often found in humid, shaded forest environments, where it thrives in the understory.
