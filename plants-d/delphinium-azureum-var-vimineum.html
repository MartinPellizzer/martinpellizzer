Delphinium azureum var. vimineum

Delphinium azureum var. vimineum, commonly known as the blue delphinium or slender larkspur, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features tall, slender stems that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height, adorned with clusters of striking blue to purple flowers. The leaves are lance-shaped and slightly hairy, arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.

The plant is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its vibrant, showy blooms.
