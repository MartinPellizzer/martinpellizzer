Delissea impedicellata

Delissea impedicellata, commonly known as the rock lily, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit and features small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, and it produces seeds in pods that split open when mature.

Delissea impedicellata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in challenging conditions.
