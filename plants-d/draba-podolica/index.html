Draba Podolica

Draba podolica is a small flowering plant native to the temperate regions of Europe and western Asia. It thrives in rocky and sandy soils, often found in alpine and subalpine environments. Known for its delicate white or pale yellow flowers, this plant is a member of the Brassicaceae family. Despite its modest size, it plays an important role in its ecosystem by supporting pollinators and contributing to biodiversity. Its resilience and adaptability make it a fascinating subject for botanical study.
