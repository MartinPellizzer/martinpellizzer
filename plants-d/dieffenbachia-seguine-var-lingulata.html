Dieffenbachia seguine var. lingulata

Dieffenbachia seguine var. lingulata, commonly known as the Shiny-leaf Dieffenbachia, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with a distinctive white or cream-colored midrib. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other popular houseplants like peace lilies. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its foliage is often used in ornamental horticulture for its aesthetic appeal.
