Dendrosenecio johnstonii subsp. battiscombei

Dendrosenecio johnstonii subsp.

battiscombei, commonly known as the Giant Groundsel, is a striking South African plant characterized by its large, lobed leaves and showy yellow flowers. It is native to the Eastern Cape province and thrives in rocky, well-drained soils.

The plant is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its unique, tree-like growth habit. It produces numerous small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

This species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its dramatic appearance.
