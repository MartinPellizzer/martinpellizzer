Dactylorhiza praetermissa f. junialis

Dactylorhiza praetermissa f. junialis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to western Europe.

It typically grows in wet meadows, marshes, and along the edges of ponds. The plant has slender, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of pale green to yellowish flowers. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit.

This orchid is often found in nutrient-poor, moist soils and is a protected species in many regions.
