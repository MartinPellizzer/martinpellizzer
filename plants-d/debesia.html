Debesia

Debesia, commonly known as the African star-of-Bethlehem, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Amaryllidaceae.

It consists of several species, most of which are native to southern Africa. The plants typically produce showy, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. They are herbaceous perennials that often feature striking, strap-like leaves.

Debesia species are valued in horticulture for their ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
