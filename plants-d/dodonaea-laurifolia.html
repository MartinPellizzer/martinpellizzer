Dodonaea laurifolia

Dodonaea laurifolia, commonly known as the silver leaf honeysuckle, is a shrub native to Australia.

It features small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in spring. The leaves are thick, leathery, and have a silvery-blue hue on the upper surface. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and inland areas.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native pollinators.
