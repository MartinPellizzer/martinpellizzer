Dendrobium clavator

Dendrobium clavator, commonly known as the club-shaped orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a pseudobulb-like structure with a distinctive club-shaped appearance, which gives it its common name. The plant produces racemes of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with prominent red or purple lips. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Dendrobium clavator is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
