Dolichos unguiculatus

Dolichos unguiculatus, commonly known as the winged bean, is a leguminous plant native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive winged pods, which contain edible seeds and a starchy, tuberous root. The plant is valued for its nutritional content, including high levels of protein, iron, and calcium. It thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated for both food and forage.

Its growth habit includes climbing vines that can reach several meters in length.
