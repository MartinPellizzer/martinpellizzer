Drimia numidica

Drimia numidica, commonly known as the African rain lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a bulbous stem base and produces striking white or pale pink flowers during the rainy season. The plant has strap-like, narrow leaves that are typically blue-green in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and forest margins.

Drimia numidica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
