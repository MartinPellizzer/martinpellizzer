Doritis stobartiana

Doritis stobartiana, commonly known as the Stobart's doritis, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, commonly referred to as the ice plant family, and is characterized by its fleshy, water-storing leaves. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
