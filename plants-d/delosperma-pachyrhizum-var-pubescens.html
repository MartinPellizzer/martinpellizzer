Delosperma pachyrhizum var. pubescens

Delosperma pachyrhizum var. pubescens, commonly known as the Woolly Ice Plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves covered in fine, white pubescence, which helps reduce water loss in arid conditions. The plant produces vibrant pink to red flowers in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its adaptability and ornamental value make it a favored plant among succulent enthusiasts.
