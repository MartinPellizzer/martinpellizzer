Dracaena perrotii

Dracaena perrotii, commonly known as the dragon tree, is a succulent plant native to Madagascar.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically dark green with a reddish or purple tint along the edges. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate watering to prevent root rot.

Dracaena perrotii is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
