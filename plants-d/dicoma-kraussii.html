Dicoma kraussii

Dicoma kraussii, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has hairy, narrow leaves and thrives in well-drained soils, often found in rocky or sandy habitats. Dicoma kraussii is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
