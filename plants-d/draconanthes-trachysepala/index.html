Draconanthes Trachysepala

The Draconanthes trachysepala is a rare and fascinating orchid native to the cloud forests of Central America. It is known for its striking violet and white flowers that bloom during the rainy season. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific soil conditions to flourish. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and plant enthusiasts. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this delicate species from habitat loss and over-collection.
