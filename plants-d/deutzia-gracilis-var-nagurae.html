Deutzia gracilis var. nagurae

Deutzia gracilis var. nagurae, commonly known as the Nagura deutzia, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in early spring, adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant has slender, arching stems and dark green, elliptical leaves that provide a nice contrast to its floral display. It prefers partial shade to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland or garden borders.

Its botanical characteristics make it a desirable choice for those seeking a low-maintenance, aesthetically pleasing shrub.
