Deprea pedrazae

Deprea pedrazae, commonly known as the Sierra Nevada rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Spain.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in rocky and arid environments. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are narrow and covered in a fine layer of white hair, which helps reduce water loss.

Deprea pedrazae is adapted to withstand harsh conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
