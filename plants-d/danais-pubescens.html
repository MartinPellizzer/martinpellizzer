Danais pubescens

Danais pubescens, commonly known as the hairy danais, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its hairy, glandular stems and leaves. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red in color. Its foliage is often covered in fine, white or grayish hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Danais pubescens is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant blooms and unique texture.
