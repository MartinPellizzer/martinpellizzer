Dryopteris pubescens var. haitiensis

Dryopteris pubescens var. haitiensis, commonly known as the Haitian wood fern, is a perennial fern native to the Caribbean, particularly Haiti.

It features a rhizomatous growth habit with fronds that are pinnately compound and covered in fine, soft hairs. The fronds are typically evergreen, with a dark green color and a leathery texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in subtropical gardens.
