Dracontium guianense

Dracontium guianense, commonly known as the Guianese arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and distinctive inflorescence. The plant produces a starchy tuberous root that is used as a food source in some regions. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
