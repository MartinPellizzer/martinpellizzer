Dendropemon caribaeus

Dendropemon caribaeus, commonly known as the Caribbean flame tree, is a tropical tree native to the Caribbean region.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has a straight, slender trunk with smooth, greyish bark and produces small, round fruits that contain seeds. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate, with a glossy green surface.

The tree is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.
