Delphinium sinoscaposum

Delphinium sinoscaposum, commonly known as the Chinese larkspur, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine or subalpine habitats, thriving in well-drained soils and cool climates. Delphinium sinoscaposum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
