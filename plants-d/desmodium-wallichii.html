Desmodium wallichii

Desmodium wallichii, commonly known as the slender velvetbean, is a leguminous plant native to the Himalayas and parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces cylindrical, hairy pods that contain several elliptical seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories or along stream banks.

Desmodium wallichii is valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability and has been used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
