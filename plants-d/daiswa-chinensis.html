Daiswa chinensis

Daiswa chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese daisy, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and chrysanthemums. The plant is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers with white petals surrounding a yellow center. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

Daiswa chinensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
