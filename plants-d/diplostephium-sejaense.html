Diplostephium sejaense

Diplostephium sejaense, commonly known as the Seja grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

Diplostephium sejaense is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions.
