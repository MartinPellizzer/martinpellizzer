Dracula carcharodon

Dracula carcharodon, commonly known as the vampire shark orchid, is a rare and striking orchid species native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dark, almost black petals that resemble the skin of a shark, giving it its ominous common name. The plant produces large, showy flowers with intricate patterns that mimic the appearance of a shark's fin. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
