Dryopteris Dilatata F. Oblonga

Dryopteris dilatata f. oblonga is a unique variant of the common wood fern known for its distinct foliage. This fern features elongated, lance-shaped fronds that set it apart from its typical counterpart. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens. The plant's elegant appearance adds a touch of natural beauty to any landscape. Its adaptability and ornamental value make it a favored plant among horticulturists and nature enthusiasts.
