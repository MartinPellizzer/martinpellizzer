Dichopsis polyantha

Dichopsis polyantha, commonly known as the starfish cactus, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features a distinctive appearance with its swollen, ribbed stems and clusters of star-shaped flowers. The plant produces bright yellow or orange blooms that appear in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are typically small and spiny, and it is well adapted to arid environments.

Dichopsis polyantha is popular among cactus enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
