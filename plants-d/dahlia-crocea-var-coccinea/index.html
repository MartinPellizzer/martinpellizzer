Dahlia Crocea Var. Coccinea

Dahlia crocea var. coccinea is a strikingly colorful plant known for its vibrant red blooms. This variety of dahlia is prized for its showy flowers and compact growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners. Its unique coloration adds a bold statement to any floral arrangement or garden bed. With proper care, this dahlia variety can bloom throughout the growing season.
