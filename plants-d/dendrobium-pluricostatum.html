Dendrobium pluricostatum

Dendrobium pluricostatum, commonly known as the multi-ribbed orchid, is a epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a pseudobulb structure with multiple vertical ridges, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Dendrobium pluricostatum is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its long-lasting blooms.
