Digitaria sharpeana

Digitaria sharpeana, commonly known as sharp's digitaria, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.

Digitaria sharpeana is adapted to warm climates and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
