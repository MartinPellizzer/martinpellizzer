Delphinium yongningense

Delphinium yongningense, commonly known as the Yongning larkspur, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, spindly stems and showy blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky habitats at high elevations. Its leaves are palmately divided, and the flowers are arranged in dense spikes.
