Deyeuxia ovata var. nivalis

Deyeuxia ovata var. nivalis, commonly known as the snowgrass, is a variant of the grass species Deyeuxia ovata.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and compact growth habit, typically forming dense mats or tufts. This plant is adapted to cold climates and is often found in alpine or subarctic regions. It has small, inconspicuous flowers that appear in early spring, and it plays a role in stabilizing soil in harsh environments.
