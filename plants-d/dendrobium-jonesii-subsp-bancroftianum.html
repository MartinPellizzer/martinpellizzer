Dendrobium jonesii subsp. bancroftianum

Dendrobium jonesii subsp.

bancroftianum, commonly known as the Bancroft's Dendrobium, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia. It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that support its evergreen foliage.

The plant produces inflorescences bearing numerous fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white and yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-draining soil.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and ecological role in its native habitat.
