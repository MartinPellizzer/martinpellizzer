Delphinium barbeyi f. subroseum

Delphinium barbeyi f. subroseum, commonly known as the Subrose Delphinium, is a cultivar of the delphinium genus native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its striking, tall spikes of flowers that range in color from pale pink to soft lavender, giving it a delicate and elegant appearance. The plant features compound leaves with a blue-green hue and a slightly hairy texture, typical of many delphinium species. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a vibrant, showy perennial.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections for its beauty and hardiness.
