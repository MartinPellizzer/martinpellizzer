Dendrobium eriaeoides

Dendrobium eriaeoides, commonly known as the golden torch orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on the surfaces of trees or rocks without being parasitic. The plant produces striking golden-yellow flowers that are often adorned with intricate patterns. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Dendrobium eriaeoides is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
