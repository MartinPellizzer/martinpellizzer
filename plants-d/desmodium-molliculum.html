Desmodium molliculum

Desmodium molliculum, commonly known as silky desmodium, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant is often found in moist, shaded environments and is known for its rapid growth habit.

Its seeds are encased in a distinctive, hairy pod, and it plays a role in soil nitrogen fixation due to its symbiotic relationship with nitrogen-fixing bacteria.
