Dracula carlueri

Dracula carlueri, commonly known as the vampire orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its dark, velvety petals that resemble the appearance of a vampire's face, hence its common name. The plant produces showy, red-brown flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. It is a terrestrial orchid that thrives in cool, humid conditions with high levels of shade.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is highly sought after by botanists and orchid enthusiasts.
