Didymophysa aucheri

Didymophysa aucheri, commonly known as the silverleaf dandelion, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of silver-gray, hairy leaves that are lanceolate in shape. The plant produces a single, tall flowering stem topped with a yellow composite flower head. Its foliage is characterized by its dense, soft pubescence, which gives it a distinctive appearance.

It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
