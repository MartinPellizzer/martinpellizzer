Diplomorpha elongata

Diplomorpha elongata, commonly known as the slender lily, is a species of flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows in high-altitude grasslands and rocky soils, preferring cool and moist environments. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem, contributing to its elegant appearance.

Diplomorpha elongata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens in temperate regions.
