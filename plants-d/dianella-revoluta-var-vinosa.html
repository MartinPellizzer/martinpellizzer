Dianella revoluta var. vinosa

Dianella revoluta var. vinosa, commonly known as the purple-flowered flax lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to New Zealand.

It is a perennial evergreen with narrow, grass-like leaves that form a dense clump. The plant produces striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in spring, adding a dramatic visual impact to gardens. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its unique foliage and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for native plant enthusiasts.
