Didymopanax spruceanus

Didymopanax spruceanus, commonly known as the giant arrowroot, is a large tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its massive, palm-like leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in panicles, followed by woody, spiny fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its size and unique appearance, it is a striking component of its natural habitat.
