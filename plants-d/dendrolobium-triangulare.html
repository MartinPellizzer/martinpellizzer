Dendrolobium triangulare

Dendrolobium triangulare, commonly known as the triangular burr legume, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and distinctive triangular seed pods that enclose the seeds. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in spring, adding ornamental value to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its unique morphology and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
