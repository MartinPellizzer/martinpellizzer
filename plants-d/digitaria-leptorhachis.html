Digitaria leptorhachis

Digitaria leptorhachis, commonly known as slender digit grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant has slender, cylindrical stems and narrow, grass-like leaves with a bluish-green color. Its inflorescence consists of a panicle of small, spikelet-bearing branches.

It is often used in ornamental gardening and can also serve as forage for livestock in certain regions.
