Dryopteris Lugubris Var. Devolvens

Dryopteris lugubris var. devolvens is a rare and delicate fern native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia. Its unique appearance features finely divided, pale green fronds that curl gracefully toward the ground. This variant is known for its striking contrast between its light foliage and the darker, often rocky habitat it inhabits. Due to its limited distribution and fragile nature, it is considered a threatened species. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this botanical marvel for future generations.
