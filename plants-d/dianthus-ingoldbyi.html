Dianthus ingoldbyi

Dianthus ingoldbyi, commonly known as Ingoldby's campion, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink or white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is found in arid or semi-arid regions. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in spring.
