Dipterocarpus scaber

Dipterocarpus scaber, commonly known as the rough-leaf dipterocarp, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its rough, thick leaves and is part of the Dipterocarpaceae family, which includes many valuable hardwood species. The tree produces small, yellowish flowers and is often found in lowland rainforests. Its wood is highly valued for its strength and durability, making it an important species in local economies.

The plant also plays a role in supporting biodiversity by providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
