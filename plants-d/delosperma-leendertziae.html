Delosperma leendertziae

Delosperma leendertziae, commonly known as the Cape tulip, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant produces star-shaped blooms in shades of yellow, orange, and red, typically during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.

Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in xeriscapes and succulent collections.
