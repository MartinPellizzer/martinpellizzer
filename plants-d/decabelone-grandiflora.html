Decabelone grandiflora

Decabelone grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered decabelone, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disc flowers. Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its aesthetic and ecological value.
