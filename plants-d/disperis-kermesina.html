Disperis kermesina

Disperis kermesina, commonly known as the Kermesina orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

Disperis kermesina is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
