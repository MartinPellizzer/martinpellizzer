Dianthus longicalyx

Dianthus longicalyx, commonly known as the long-stemmed carnation, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink to white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters and features narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are often used in floral arrangements due to their vibrant colors and long-lasting blooms.

Dianthus longicalyx prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
