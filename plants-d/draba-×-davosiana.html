Draba × davosiana

Draba × davosiana, commonly known as the Davos dwarf cabbage, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its dense rosette of basal leaves and small, yellow flowers. Native to the Alps, it thrives in rocky, well-drained soils at high altitudes. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits containing several seeds.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is occasionally cultivated in alpine gardens.
