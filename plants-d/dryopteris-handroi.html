Dryopteris handroi

Dryopteris handroi, commonly known as the Hainan shield fern, is a species native to China, specifically the Hainan Island.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its dense, leathery fronds that are typically dark green in color. The plant exhibits a rhizomatous growth habit, with fertile fronds producing sporangia on the underside.

It thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in forest understories where it contributes to the ecological diversity of its habitat.
