Desmodium podocarpum var. laxum

Desmodium podocarpum var. laxum, commonly known as the slender desmodium, is a perennial legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features trifoliate leaves with small, rounded leaflets and produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant produces cylindrical, hairy pods that contain several elliptical seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it valuable for soil enrichment in agricultural settings.
