Daphne × whiteorum

Daphne × whiteorum, commonly known as the White Daphne, is a hybrid shrub native to the United States.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of fragrant, white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that are typically dark red or black. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Due to its toxicity, it should be handled with care.
