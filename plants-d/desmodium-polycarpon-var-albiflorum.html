Desmodium polycarpon var. albiflorum

Desmodium polycarpon var. albiflorum, commonly known as whiteflower desmodium, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is native to various regions in Africa and is often found in grasslands and disturbed soils. It has a fibrous root system and produces small, round pods containing several seeds.

This species is valued for its potential in soil enrichment due to its nitrogen-fixing ability.
