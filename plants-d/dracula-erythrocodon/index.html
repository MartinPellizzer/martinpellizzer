Dracula Erythrocodon

Dracula erythrocodon is a rare and striking orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador. Known for its vibrant red and orange flowers, it captivates botanists and plant enthusiasts alike. This species belongs to the orchid family, which includes over 25,000 species worldwide. Its unique appearance and limited habitat make it a subject of both scientific study and conservation concern. Due to deforestation and climate change, Dracula erythrocodon faces increasing threats to its survival.
