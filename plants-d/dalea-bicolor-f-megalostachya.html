Dalea bicolor f. megalostachya

Dalea bicolor f. megalostachya, commonly known as the large-stamen prairie clover, is a flowering plant native to the central United States.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its striking pink to red flowers. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval leaflets and produces elongated, branched pods that contain seeds. It thrives in prairie habitats and is adapted to full sun and well-drained soils.

This variety is noted for its larger stamens compared to the typical form of Dalea bicolor.
