Deutzia compacta

Deutzia compacta, commonly known as dwarf deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features small, white or pink flowers that bloom in early summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. The plant has slender, arching stems and dark green, oval-shaped leaves that provide a nice contrast to its delicate blooms. It typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet, making it suitable for small gardens or borders.

Deutzia compacta prefers partial to full sun and well-drained soil, thriving in temperate climates.
