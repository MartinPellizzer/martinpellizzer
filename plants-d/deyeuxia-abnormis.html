Deyeuxia abnormis

Deyeuxia abnormis, commonly known as the abnormal sedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to parts of East Asia.

It typically grows in wet or marshy environments, often found in wetlands and along the edges of ponds. Deyeuxia abnormis plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various small organisms.
