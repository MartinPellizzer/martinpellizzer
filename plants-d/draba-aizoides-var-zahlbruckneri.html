Draba aizoides var. zahlbruckneri

Draba aizoides var. zahlbruckneri, commonly known as the Aizoides Draba, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically produces white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. It has finely divided, grass-like leaves and is often found in alpine and rocky habitats.

This variety is notable for its adaptability to harsh environmental conditions and its role in stabilizing soil in mountainous regions.
