Dischidia nummularia var. rhombifolia

Dischidia nummularia var. rhombifolia, commonly known as the rhombic dischidia, is a climbing or trailing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped or rhomboid leaves that are usually light green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in humid, warm environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in hanging baskets.

Its unique leaf shape and easy care make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
