Dracaena spectabilis

Dracaena spectabilis, commonly known as the dragon tree, is a tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and striking, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 meters in length. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers, followed by red, fleshy fruits that contain seeds. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine and as ornamental foliage in gardens and indoor spaces.

Dracaena spectabilis thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil and indirect sunlight.
