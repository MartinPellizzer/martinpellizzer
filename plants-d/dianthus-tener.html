Dianthus tener

Dianthus tener, commonly known as the rock campion, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Dianthus tener is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to flourish in challenging environments.
