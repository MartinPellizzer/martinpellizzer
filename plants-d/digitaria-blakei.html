Digitaria blakei

Digitaria blakei, commonly known as Blake's Digitaria, is a grass species native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and have a smooth surface, while its seeds are small and wind-dispersed.

Digitaria blakei is often found in arid or semi-arid regions where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife.
