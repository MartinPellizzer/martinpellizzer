Declieuxia daphnoides var. glabra

Declieuxia daphnoides var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth leaved lily.

It is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of South America. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and glossy, giving it a distinct appearance. It produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often arranged in clusters.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
