Deutzia reflexa

Deutzia reflexa, commonly known as reflexed deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features clusters of white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring, often before the leaves emerge. The plant has slender, arching branches and small, ovate leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Deutzia reflexa is valued for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
