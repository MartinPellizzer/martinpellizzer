Draba myosotidioides

Draba myosotidioides, commonly known as the bluebell draba, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with small, clustered flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits containing several seeds. It is native to regions of Europe and is often found in alpine or rocky habitats.

Its foliage consists of basal leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs.
