Diplodium decurvum

Diplodium decurvum, commonly known as the curly diplodium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, curling leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.
