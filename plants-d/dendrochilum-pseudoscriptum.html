Dendrochilum pseudoscriptum

Dendrochilum pseudoscriptum, commonly known as the script orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, leafy stems and clusters of small, fragrant flowers with distinctive markings. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments such as forest undergrowth. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white with intricate patterns resembling script-like designs.

Dendrochilum pseudoscriptum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
