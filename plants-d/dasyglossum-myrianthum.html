Dasyglossum myrianthum

Dasyglossum myrianthum, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Dasyglossum myrianthum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
