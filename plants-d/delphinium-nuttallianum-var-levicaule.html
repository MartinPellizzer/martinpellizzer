Delphinium nuttallianum var. levicaule

Delphinium nuttallianum var. levicaule, commonly known as the slender larkspur, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It typically grows in moist meadows and along stream banks, preferring cool, shaded environments. The plant features tall, sparsely branched stems that bear whorled clusters of blue to purple flowers. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a hairy texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
