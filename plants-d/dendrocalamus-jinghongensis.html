Dendrocalamus jinghongensis

Dendrocalamus jinghongensis, commonly known as the Jinghong bamboo, is a tall, slender grass-like plant native to southern China.

It belongs to the Bambusoideae subfamily and is characterized by its strong, hollow stems that can grow up to 20 meters in height. The plant is known for its fast growth rate and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and utility in construction. Its leaves are long and narrow, contributing to its aesthetic appeal in gardens and landscapes.

This species is also valued for its role in traditional Chinese medicine and as a source of sustainable materials.
