Daviesia adnata

Daviesia adnata, commonly known as the Narrow-leaf Daviesia, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 meter, featuring slender, angular stems covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are usually pale yellow to cream in color, blooming in spring. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often appearing in whorls around the stems.

Daviesia adnata is adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in heathlands and open woodlands.
