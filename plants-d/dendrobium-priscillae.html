Dendrobium priscillae

Dendrobium priscillae, commonly known as the Priscilla's orchid, is a rare and exquisite epiphytic orchid native to the highlands of Borneo.

It belongs to the Dendrobium genus, which includes over 1,200 species of tropical orchids. This plant is characterized by its tall, cane-like stems and clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, porous media for optimal growth.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is highly valued in horticultural circles.
