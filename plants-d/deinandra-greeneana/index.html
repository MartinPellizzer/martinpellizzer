Deinandra Greeneana

Deinandra greeneana is a rare and striking plant native to the coastal regions of southern California. It is known for its vibrant pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring. This species thrives in sandy soils and is often found in disturbed habitats along the shoreline. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique and delicate flora.
