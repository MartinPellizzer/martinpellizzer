Dendrobium ternatense

Dendrobium ternatense, commonly known as the Ternate orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a pseudobulb that supports its foliage and flowers. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink, often with a green or purple lip. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Dendrobium ternatense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
