Dichroa tristyla

Dichroa tristyla, commonly known as the purple-leafed dichroa, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, dark purple to black leaves that contrast with its white or pinkish flowers. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are often used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.

Dichroa tristyla is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
