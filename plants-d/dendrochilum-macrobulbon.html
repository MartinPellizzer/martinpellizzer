Dendrochilum macrobulbon

Dendrochilum macrobulbon, commonly known as the large-bulbous tree orchid, is a fascinating epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in its natural habitat. The plant produces striking, showy flowers with long, slender petals and a prominent lip. It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it a popular specimen among orchid enthusiasts and horticulturists.
