Dehaasia celebica

Dehaasia celebica, commonly known as the Celebes starfruit, is a tropical plant native to the Indonesian island of Sulawesi.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its small, star-shaped fruits that are typically yellow when ripe. The plant thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. Its leaves are oval-shaped with a glossy surface, and it produces small white flowers that are followed by the distinctive fruit.

The fruit is often used in local cuisines and has a slightly tart flavor.
