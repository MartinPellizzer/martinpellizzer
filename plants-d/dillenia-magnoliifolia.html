Dillenia magnoliifolia

Dillenia magnoliifolia, commonly known as the magnolia-leaf dillenia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white to pale yellow flowers that resemble those of magnolias, hence its common name. The plant produces distinctive, fleshy, spiny fruits that contain numerous seeds. Its leaves are broad, heart-shaped, and often have a waxy coating.

Dillenia magnoliifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and subtropical regions.
