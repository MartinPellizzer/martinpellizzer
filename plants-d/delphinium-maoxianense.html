Delphinium maoxianense

Delphinium maoxianense, commonly known as the Maoxian larkspur, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems and showy blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky habitats at high elevations. Its foliage consists of palmately divided leaves, and it produces small, follicle-type fruits.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
