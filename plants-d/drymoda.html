Drymoda

Drymoda, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers that bloom profusely during the rainy season. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are elliptical in shape and often have a glossy surface. Drymoda is known for its ability to thrive in humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its flowers are also used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
