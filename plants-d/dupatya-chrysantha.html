Dupatya chrysantha

Dupatya chrysantha, commonly known as the golden flame lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features showy, yellow-orange flowers that resemble flames, with a distinctive, narrow, strap-like shape. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant blooms during the summer months. Its foliage consists of long, sword-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy.

Dupatya chrysantha thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
