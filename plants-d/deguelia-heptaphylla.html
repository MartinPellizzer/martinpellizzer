Deguelia heptaphylla

Deguelia heptaphylla, commonly known as the seven-leafed deguelia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves with seven leaflets. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in racemes, followed by pod-like fruits containing several seeds.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
