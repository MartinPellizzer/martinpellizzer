Dais gnidioides

Dais gnidioides, commonly known as the Chinese daisy, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.

Dais gnidioides is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
