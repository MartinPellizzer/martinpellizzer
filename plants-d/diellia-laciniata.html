Diellia laciniata

Diellia laciniata, commonly known as the laceleaf daisy, is a perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, lobed leaves that give it its common name, and its flowers are typically yellow with a white center. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes. It produces small, daisy-like blooms throughout the growing season, adding ornamental value to gardens.

Diellia laciniata is also valued for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
