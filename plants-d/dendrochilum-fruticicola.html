Dendrochilum fruticicola

Dendrochilum fruticicola, commonly known as the shrub orchid, is a perennial epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the air and decaying organic matter.

Dendrochilum fruticicola thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing on trees or rocks in tropical rainforests.
