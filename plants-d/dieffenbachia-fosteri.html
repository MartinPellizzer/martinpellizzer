Dieffenbachia fosteri

Dieffenbachia fosteri, commonly known as the Foster's Dieffenbachia, is a tropical evergreen plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, glossy, arrow-shaped leaves with striking white and green variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically hidden beneath a spathe, a feature common in the Araceae family. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Dieffenbachia fosteri is a popular houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
