Delphinium honanense var. honanense

Delphinium honanense var. honanense, commonly known as Honan larkspur, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant produces showy blue to purple flowers in dense clusters at the top of the stems, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are palmately compound, with elongated leaflets that are often hairy.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
