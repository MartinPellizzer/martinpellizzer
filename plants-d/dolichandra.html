Dolichandra

Dolichandra, commonly known as the yellow bell, is a tropical plant native to Central America and the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling small bells. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate with a slightly hairy texture.

Dolichandra thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
