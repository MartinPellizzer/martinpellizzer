Delphinium californicum var. laxiusculum

Delphinium californicum var. laxiusculum, commonly known as the California larkspur, is a herbaceous perennial native to the western United States.

It features showy blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet, with slender stems and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for gardens and natural landscapes.
