Desmodium pendulum

Desmodium pendulum, commonly known as the hanging or weeping senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features delicate, pendulous inflorescences that produce small, yellowish-white flowers. Its leaves are trifoliate, with each leaflet being ovate and serrated.

Desmodium pendulum thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodland or disturbed areas.
