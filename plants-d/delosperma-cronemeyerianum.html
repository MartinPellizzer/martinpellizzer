Delosperma cronemeyerianum

Delosperma cronemeyerianum, commonly known as the Ice Plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant clusters of star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, typically during the summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance.

Its ability to form dense mats makes it an effective ground cover in dry landscapes.
