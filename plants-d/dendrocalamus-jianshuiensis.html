Dendrocalamus jianshuiensis

Dendrocalamus jianshuiensis, commonly known as the JianShui bamboo, is a tall, robust grass-like plant native to southern China.

It belongs to the Bambusoideae subfamily and is characterized by its strong, straight culms that can grow up to 20 meters in height. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It is often used in traditional Chinese medicine and as a source of sustainable materials.

Its ecological importance lies in its ability to prevent soil erosion and support local biodiversity.
