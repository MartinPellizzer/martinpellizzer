Dictyosperma album var. aureum

Dictyosperma album var. aureum, commonly known as the golden nightshade, is a variant of the nightshade family (Solanaceae) native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its striking yellow flowers and oval, spiny fruits. The leaves are alternate, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, while the stems are often hairy and branched. This plant thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in shaded, moist environments.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal but requires careful handling due to its toxic properties.
