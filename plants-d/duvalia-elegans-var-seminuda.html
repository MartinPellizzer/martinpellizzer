Duvalia elegans var. seminuda

Duvalia elegans var. seminuda, commonly known as the slender duvalia, is a striking member of the Araliaceae family.

It is characterized by its elegant, narrow leaves that are often variegated with shades of white and green. The plant produces small, white flowers arranged in umbels, which are typically followed by bright red berries. Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in warm, humid environments with partial shade.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
