Disa conferta

Disa conferta, commonly known as the silver leaf orchid, is a species native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces striking white or pale pink flowers with a distinctive hooded structure. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in a fine, silvery coating.

Disa conferta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
