Dodecatheon meadia var. brevifolium

Dodecatheon meadia var. brevifolium, commonly known as the short-leaved eastern fringetop, is a variant of the eastern fringetop, a member of the Primulaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the eastern United States, typically found in moist, wooded areas. The plant features showy, bell-shaped flowers that are usually pink or white, with a distinctive fringed appearance. This variant is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
