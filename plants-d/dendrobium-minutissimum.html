Dendrobium minutissimum

Dendrobium minutissimum, commonly known as the miniature dendrobium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, epiphytic orchid that grows on trees and rocks in tropical rainforests. The plant has thin, wiry stems and produces tiny, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It is highly prized by orchid enthusiasts for its delicate appearance and rarity.

Due to its small size and specific habitat requirements, it is often difficult to cultivate outside its native environment.
