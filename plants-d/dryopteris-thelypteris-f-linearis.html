Dryopteris thelypteris f. linearis

Dryopteris thelypteris f. linearis, commonly known as the linear fern, is a variant of the cinnamon fern.

It is a deciduous fern characterized by its narrow, elongated fronds that are typically 1 to 2 meters in length. The plant features a tall, slender rhizome with a cinnamon-scented scent when crushed. Its leaves are bipinnate, with closely spaced, linear pinnae that give it a distinctive, delicate appearance.

This fern thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.
