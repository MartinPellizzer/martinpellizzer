Dicksonia pilosiuscula f. poyseri

Dicksonia pilosiuscula f. poyseri, commonly known as the Poyser's tree fern, is a fern species native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery fronds that can reach lengths of up to 3 meters. The plant has a slow growth rate and can live for several decades, making it a long-lived species. Its fronds are typically a deep green, with a soft, hairy texture on the underside.

This fern is often found in shaded, moist environments, such as forests and along stream banks.
