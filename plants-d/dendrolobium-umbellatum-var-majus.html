Dendrolobium umbellatum var. majus

Dendrolobium umbellatum var. majus, commonly known as the large-flowered burr legume, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive, showy yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces woody pods that contain seeds, which are often dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to Mediterranean climates.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
