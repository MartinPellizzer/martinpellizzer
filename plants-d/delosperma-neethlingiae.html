Delosperma neethlingiae

Delosperma neethlingiae, commonly known as the Namaqualia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a succulent that produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the winter months. The plant has fleshy, upright stems and small, rounded leaves that help it store water. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Due to its unique appearance and seasonal blooms, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
