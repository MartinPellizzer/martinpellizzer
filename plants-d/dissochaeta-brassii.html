Dissochaeta brassii

Dissochaeta brassii, commonly known as the narrowleaf sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, linear leaves. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.
