Delosperma crassum

Delosperma crassum, commonly known as the thick-leaved ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of pink, red, and yellow, typically during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its low-growing habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for landscaping in dry climates.
