Dactylorhiza × senayi nothosubsp. vermeuleniana

Dactylorhiza × senayi nothosubsp.

vermeuleniana, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a hybrid orchid native to South Africa. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, fragrant flowers.

The plant typically blooms in late summer, producing clusters of pinkish-white to pale purple flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and grasslands.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
