Deguelia korthalsiana

Deguelia korthalsiana, commonly known as the flame tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 20 meters tall, featuring a straight trunk and a broad, spreading crown. The plant is characterized by its large, showy, red-orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several elongated, narrow leaflets.

The tree is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
