Daphne chinensis

Daphne chinensis, commonly known as Chinese daphne, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features dense, aromatic foliage and produces showy clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers in early spring. The plant has smooth, grayish bark and is known for its winter leafless appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Daphne chinensis is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
