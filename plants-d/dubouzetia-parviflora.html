Dubouzetia parviflora

Dubouzetia parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered dubouzetia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

The plant typically grows in rocky or well-drained soils, often in alpine or subalpine environments. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Dubouzetia parviflora is valued in horticulture for its resilience and ability to thrive in cooler climates.
