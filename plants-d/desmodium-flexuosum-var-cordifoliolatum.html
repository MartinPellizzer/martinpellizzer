Desmodium flexuosum var. cordifoliolatum

Desmodium flexuosum var. cordifoliolatum, commonly known as the heart-leaf desmodium, is a perennial legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features trifoliate leaves with heart-shaped leaflets, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or disturbed areas.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it a valuable plant for soil enrichment and forage.
