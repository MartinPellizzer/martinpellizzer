Dactylorhiza traunsteineri var. vosagiaca

Dactylorhiza traunsteineri var. vosagiaca, commonly known as the Vosagian marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central Europe.

It typically grows in wet meadows and marshy areas, thriving in moist, acidic soils. The plant features a slender, upright stem with several narrow, linear leaves. Its flowers are pale green to yellowish, with a distinctive three-lobed lip, and bloom in late spring to early summer.

It is a rare and protected species, often found in specific habitats across Germany and the Czech Republic.
