Diplazium inflatisorum

Diplazium inflatisorum, commonly known as the inflated spleenwort, is a fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, inflated, and often brightly colored spore cases, which are unique to this species. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its fronds are usually compound, with a delicate, feathery appearance.
