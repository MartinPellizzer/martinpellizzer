Dolichos antunesii

Dolichos antunesii, commonly known as the Antunes' dolichos, is a flowering plant native to the island of Socotra.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, twisted stems and small, pea-like flowers. The plant produces elongated pods that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine.
