Drimiopsis spicata

Drimiopsis spicata, commonly known as the spiky drimiopsis, is a perennial evergreen plant native to South Africa.

It features narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are often bluish-green in color and have a distinctive waxy coating. The plant produces striking, spiky inflorescences with small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance of low light and minimal water requirements.

Drimiopsis spicata is also valued for its ability to thrive in dry conditions and its ornamental appeal.
