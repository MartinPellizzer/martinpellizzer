Dryopteris affinis var. azorica

Dryopteris affinis var. azorica, commonly known as the Azorean shield fern, is a variant of the common wood fern.

It is native to the Azores and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant has a robust rhizome system that allows it to thrive in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are typically dark green and have a leathery texture, with a finely serrated margin.

This fern is often found in rocky or forested areas, where it contributes to the biodiversity of its ecosystem.
