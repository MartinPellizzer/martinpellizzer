Dactylorhiza × koningweeniana

Dactylorhiza × koningweeniana, commonly known as the Koningweeniana orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that bear clusters of fragrant, violet or purple flowers. The plant is a terrestrial orchid, typically growing in moist, shaded woodland habitats. Its flowers are relatively small and have three petals and three sepals, with the lateral sepals often forming a hood-like structure.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
