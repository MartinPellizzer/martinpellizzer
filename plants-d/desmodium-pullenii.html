Desmodium pullenii

Desmodium pullenii, commonly known as the purple desmodium, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Caribbean.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has a creeping growth habit with hairy stems and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Desmodium pullenii is also valued for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil fertility.
