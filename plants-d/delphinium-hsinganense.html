Delphinium hsinganense

Delphinium hsinganense, commonly known as the Hsingan larkspur, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant produces showy blue to purple flowers in clusters at the top of the stem, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are compound, with lanceolate to ovate leaflets, and the plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils.

Delphinium hsinganense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
