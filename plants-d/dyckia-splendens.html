Dyckia splendens

Dyckia splendens, commonly known as the Splendid Dyckia, is a perennial grass-like plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped foliage composed of stiff, sword-like leaves. The leaves are typically green with striking red or purple margins, which become more vivid in full sun. It produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing small, tubular flowers that are usually white or pale pink.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact size and ornamental appearance.
