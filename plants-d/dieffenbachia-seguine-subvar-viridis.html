Dieffenbachia seguine subvar. viridis

Dieffenbachia seguine subvar. viridis, commonly known as the Snake Plant, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stems and broad, dark green, waxy leaves that often have a slight sheen. The plant is noted for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its air-purifying properties. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped with a subtle variegation of lighter green along the edges.

It is a popular houseplant due to its ease of care and ornamental appeal.
