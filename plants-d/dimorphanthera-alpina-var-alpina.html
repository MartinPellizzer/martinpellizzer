Dimorphanthera alpina var. alpina

Dimorphanthera alpina var. alpina, commonly known as the alpine dimorphanthera, is a flowering plant native to high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, clustered inflorescences. The plant typically features hairy stems and leaves that are adapted to cold, arid conditions. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with five petals and prominent stamens.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and resilience in challenging environments.
