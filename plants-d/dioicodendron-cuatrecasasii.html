Dioicodendron cuatrecasasii

Dioicodendron cuatrecasasii, commonly known as the Cuatrecasas' dioicodendron, is a rare and unique plant species native to specific regions in Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dioecious nature, meaning that male and female reproductive structures are found on separate plants. The plant typically grows as a shrub, with simple, alternate leaves that are often elliptical in shape. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, with male and female flowers occurring on different individuals.
