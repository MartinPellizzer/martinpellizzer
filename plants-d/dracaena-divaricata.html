Dracaena divaricata

Dracaena divaricata, commonly known as the snake plant, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Central America.

It features upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically variegated with green and white or yellow patterns. The plant is characterized by its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its foliage is often used in floral arrangements due to its aesthetic appeal.

Dracaena divaricata is also valued for its air-purifying qualities, as it can help remove toxins from the environment.
