Dendrobium adae

Dendrobium adae, commonly known as the Adae's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with red-brown markings. It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, thriving in humid tropical environments.

Dendrobium adae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
