Dendrobium teloense

Dendrobium teloense, commonly known as the Teloense orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to the Philippines.

It features a tall, cane-like stem with multiple nodes, and its leaves are typically elliptical and arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces racemes of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with prominent red sepals and petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich substrates.

Dendrobium teloense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
