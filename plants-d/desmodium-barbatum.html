Desmodium barbatum

Desmodium barbatum, commonly known as hairy burr legume, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant features trifoliate leaves with hairy surfaces, and its small, yellow flowers are typically clustered in racemes. Desmodium barbatum is often found in disturbed soils and can be used in ecological restoration projects.

Its seeds are enclosed in hairy, inflated pods that aid in seed dispersal.
