Delphinium Azureum Var. Nortonianum

Delphinium azureum var. nortonianum is a rare and striking member of the delphinium family known for its vivid blue flowers. Found primarily in the mountainous regions of western North America, this variant thrives in cool, moist environments. Its unique coloration and delicate structure make it a favorite among botanists and gardeners alike. Despite its beauty, it is highly sensitive to environmental changes, making conservation efforts crucial. This plant offers a glimpse into the diverse and often overlooked flora of alpine ecosystems.
