Dryopteris mosenii

Dryopteris mosenii, commonly known as the Mosen's wood fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that are typically divided into narrow segments. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories where it can receive adequate humidity and indirect light. Its rhizomes are usually short and thick, supporting the growth of its intricate foliage.

This fern is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with the right conditions.
