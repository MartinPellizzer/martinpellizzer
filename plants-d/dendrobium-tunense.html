Dendrobium tunense

Dendrobium tunense, commonly known as the Philippine orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome that produces multiple pseudobulbs. The plant produces inflorescences with several fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in two rows along the stem.

Dendrobium tunense is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
