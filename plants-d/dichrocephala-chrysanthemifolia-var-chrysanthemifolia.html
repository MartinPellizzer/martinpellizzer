Dichrocephala chrysanthemifolia var. chrysanthemifolia

Dichrocephala chrysanthemifolia var. chrysanthemifolia, commonly known as the golden chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
