Dierama floriferum

Dierama floriferum, commonly known as the Cape umbrella plant, is a striking perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of broad, waxy, grass-like leaves that can grow up to 60 cm in height. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences topped with clusters of bright pink or purple flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Its unique appearance and long blooming period make it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.
