Deutzia naseana

Deutzia naseana, commonly known as the Japanese deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features clusters of fragrant, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, arching branches and small, ovate leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its graceful form and seasonal interest.
