Dracocephalum denticulatum var. obovatum

Dracocephalum denticulatum var. obovatum, commonly known as narrow-leaved dragonhead, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, branching stems covered in fine, hairy foliage. The leaves are lance-shaped, with serrated edges, and the flowers are typically purple or blue, forming dense spikes. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and aromatic properties.
