Durio rosayroanus

Durio rosayroanus, commonly known as the Rosayroan durian, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its large, spiky fruit, which contains a creamy, aromatic pulp. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil, and it requires a long growing season to produce fruit. The tree is valued not only for its unique fruit but also for its ornamental qualities.
