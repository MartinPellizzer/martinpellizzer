Dioscorea fargesii

Dioscorea fargesii, commonly known as the Yunnan yam, is a perennial vine native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its climbing habit and tuberous roots. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Its tubers are valued for their medicinal properties and are used in traditional Chinese medicine.

The plant thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil and partial shade.
