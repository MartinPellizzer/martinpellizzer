Dolichos fragrans

Dolichos fragrans, commonly known as the sweet pea, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and vibrant, fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant belongs to the Fabaceae family, also known as the legume or pea family. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three small oval-shaped leaflets.

The pods that develop after flowering contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine and as a food source.
