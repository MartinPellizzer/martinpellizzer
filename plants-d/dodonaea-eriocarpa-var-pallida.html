Dodonaea eriocarpa var. pallida

Dodonaea eriocarpa var. pallida, commonly known as the pale silky honeysuckle, is a shrub native to Australia.

It features slender, often hairy stems covered in fine, silky white hairs. The leaves are small, ovate, and also covered in a soft, whitish pubescence. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in spikes, followed by red, fleshy berries.

It is adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions of Australia.
