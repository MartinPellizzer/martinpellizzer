Dioscoreophyllum tenerum var. fernandense

Dioscoreophyllum tenerum var. fernandense, commonly known as the Fernandese yam, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces edible tubers that are an important food source in some regions. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical significance lies in its role in traditional agriculture and its potential for cultivation in sustainable farming systems.
