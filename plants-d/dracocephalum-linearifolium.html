Dracocephalum linearifolium

Dracocephalum linearifolium, commonly known as linear-leaf blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region and parts of Central Asia.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters, with narrow, linear leaves and small, pale blue or purple flowers. It thrives in dry, sunny environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its potential medicinal properties.
