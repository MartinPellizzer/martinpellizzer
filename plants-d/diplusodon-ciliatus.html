Diplusodon ciliatus

Diplusodon ciliatus, commonly known as the silky antelope or silky antelope grass, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant features long, narrow leaves with a silky texture, giving it its common name. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, pale blue to purple flowers.

Diplusodon ciliatus is often found in open grasslands and along riverbanks, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
