Dioscorea olfersiana

Dioscorea olfersiana, commonly known as the African yam bean, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its tuberous roots, which are used for both culinary and medicinal purposes. The plant produces small, yellow flowers followed by elongated, pod-like fruits containing kidney-shaped seeds. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Dioscorea olfersiana is valued for its nutritional content and traditional use in various African cultures.
