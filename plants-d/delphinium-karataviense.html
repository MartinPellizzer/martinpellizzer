Delphinium karataviense

Delphinium karataviense, commonly known as the Karatavinskaya larkspur, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and clusters of showy, spurred flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, preferring well-drained soils and full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Delphinium karataviense is valued in horticulture for its striking blooms and adaptability to various garden conditions.
