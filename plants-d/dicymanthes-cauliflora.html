Dicymanthes cauliflora

Dicymanthes cauliflora, commonly known as the rock lily, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its unique cauliflorous growth habit, where flowers and leaves emerge directly from the trunk. The plant produces striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections worldwide.
