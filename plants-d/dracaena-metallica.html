Dracaena metallica

Dracaena metallica, commonly known as the metallic dracaena, is a striking plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, lance-shaped leaves that display a metallic sheen, often with a bluish or silver hue. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Dracaena metallica is relatively low maintenance, making it suitable for both novice and experienced plant enthusiasts.
