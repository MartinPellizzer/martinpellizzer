Dendrobium complanatum

Dendrobium complanatum, commonly known as the flat-leaf orchid, is a sympodial epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a slender, wiry stem that grows horizontally and produces numerous small, clustered leaves. The plant blooms with fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that have three sepals and three petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, loose soil.

Dendrobium complanatum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
