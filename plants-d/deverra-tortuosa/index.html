Deverra Tortuosa

Deverra tortuosa is a unique and fascinating plant known for its twisting, spiral-shaped stems. Native to arid regions of Africa and the Middle East, it has adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments. Its distinctive appearance has made it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Despite its beauty, it requires careful cultivation due to its specific needs. This article explores the characteristics, care, and significance of this remarkable plant.
