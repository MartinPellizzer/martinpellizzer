Desmodium cubense

Desmodium cubense, commonly known as Cuban velvet bean, is a leguminous plant native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces elongated pods containing small, kidney-shaped seeds. It thrives in warm, tropical climates and is often found in disturbed soils.

Desmodium cubense is valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability and use in agricultural systems.
