Deutzia gracilis var. macrantha

Deutzia gracilis var. macrantha, commonly known as the slender deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features slender, arching stems that produce clusters of small, white flowers in early spring. The leaves are oval-shaped, with a glossy green surface and serrated edges. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its delicate floral display.
