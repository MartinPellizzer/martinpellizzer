Delphinium dasystachyon var. ochroleucum

Delphinium dasystachyon var. ochroleucum, commonly known as the white-flowered larkspur, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters. The plant produces showy, blue to purple flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters at the top of the stem. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and have a hairy texture.

This variety is distinguished by its pale yellow to white flowers, which contrast with the dark green foliage.
