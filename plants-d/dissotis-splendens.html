Dissotis splendens

Dissotis splendens, commonly known as the golden torch plant, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, torch-like inflorescences that display bright yellow to orange flowers. The plant belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a smooth texture, while the flowering stems are tall and erect.

Dissotis splendens thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
