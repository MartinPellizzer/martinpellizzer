Dantia nubica

Dantia nubica, commonly known as the cloud mist plant, is a unique species native to high-altitude regions.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery leaves that are adapted to absorb moisture from the air. The plant thrives in cool, humid environments and is often found in mountainous areas with persistent mist. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, typically appearing in clusters.

Dantia nubica plays a vital role in its ecosystem by providing habitat for various microorganisms and insects.
