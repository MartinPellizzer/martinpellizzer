Dendrochilum microstylum

Dendrochilum microstylum, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a small epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, tropical environments. Its flowers are usually small, with white or pale pink petals and a distinctive yellow lip.

Dendrochilum microstylum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
