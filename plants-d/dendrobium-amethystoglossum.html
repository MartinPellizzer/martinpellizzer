Dendrobium amethystoglossum

Dendrobium amethystoglossum, commonly known as the purple-lip dendrobium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. The plant features long, cane-like stems that produce clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. Its flowers are characterized by their purple-lip petals and white to pale yellow sepals.

Dendrobium amethystoglossum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
