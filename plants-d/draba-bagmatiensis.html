Draba bagmatiensis

Draba bagmatiensis, commonly known as the Bagmati draba, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous annual or biennial plant that typically grows in alpine and arctic environments. The plant features basal leaves that are lanceolate and hairy, with small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in a raceme. It is native to regions in Asia, including Tibet, Nepal, and northern India.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
