Dillenia megalophylla

Dillenia megalophylla, commonly known as the giant dillenia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmately compound leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in diameter, making it a notable species in its habitat. The plant produces showy, yellowish-white flowers that are arranged in clusters, attracting various pollinators. Its fruit is a woody, spiny capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Dillenia megalophylla is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental and ecological significance.
