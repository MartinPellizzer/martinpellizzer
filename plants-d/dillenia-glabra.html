Dillenia glabra

Dillenia glabra, commonly known as the smooth dillenia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Dilleniaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific Islands. The plant is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically have five to seven petals. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a smooth or slightly lobed appearance.

The fruit is a woody, fleshy capsule that contains numerous seeds.
