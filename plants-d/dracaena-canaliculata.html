Dracaena canaliculata

Dracaena canaliculata, commonly known as the narrow-ribbed dracaena, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and elongated, dark green leaves with prominent, parallel veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by red, fleshy fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dracaena canaliculata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
