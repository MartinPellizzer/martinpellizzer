Dipladenia × insignis

Dipladenia × insignis, commonly known as the crossflower or hybrid mandevilla, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It is a hybrid between Dipladenia and Mandevilla species, characterized by its vigorous growth and trailing or upright habit. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often in clusters. It thrives in warm, sunny conditions and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Dipladenia × insignis is popular in gardens and as a container plant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
