Dactylorchis maculata var. spathulata

Dactylorchis maculata var. spathulata, commonly known as the spotted orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of eastern North America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and thrives in rich, organic soil. The plant features a slender, erect stem with several lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers with a distinctive spotted pattern. It is a perennial herb that blooms from late spring to early summer.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is often found in naturalized areas and gardens.
