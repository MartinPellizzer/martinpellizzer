Dendrobium bannaense

Dendrobium bannaense, commonly known as the Banna orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with multiple stems that can become woody over time. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often arranged in racemes. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural collections and indoor gardening.
