Dissotis kundelungensis

Dissotis kundelungensis, commonly known as the Kundelungensis arrowhead, is a striking aquatic plant native to the Democratic Republic of the Congo.

It features arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically bright green and often have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding an ornamental quality to its appearance. It thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments and is often found in marshy areas.

Its unique morphology and habitat make it a notable species in aquatic botany.
