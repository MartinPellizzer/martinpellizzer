Diplostachyum apodum

Diplostachyum apodum, commonly known as the white flame bamboo, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the grass family, Poaceae, and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and panicles of small white flowers. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its elegant appearance and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
