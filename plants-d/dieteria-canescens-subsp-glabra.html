Dieteria canescens subsp. glabra

Dieteria canescens subsp.

glabra, commonly known as the smooth dieteria, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine pubescence.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in composite inflorescences, which are typical of the Asteraceae family. It is native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa, where it thrives in sandy or rocky soils.

This subspecies is distinguished by its smoother leaf surfaces compared to other subspecies of Dieteria canescens.
