Daubentonia punicea

Daubentonia punicea, commonly known as the red-flowered orchid, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, vividly colored flowers. The plant typically features long, slender stems and produces clusters of showy, red or pink blossoms. Its botanical structure includes fleshy roots and broad, lance-shaped leaves that help it thrive in humid environments.

Daubentonia punicea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
