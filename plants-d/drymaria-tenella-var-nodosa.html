Drymaria tenella var. nodosa

Drymaria tenella var. nodosa, commonly known as the nodding drymaria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing herb with prostrate stems that often form dense mats. The plant produces small, white, nodding flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it its common name. Its leaves are typically opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
