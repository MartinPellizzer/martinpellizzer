Dialium reygaertii

Dialium reygaertii, commonly known as the African starfruit tree, is a tropical evergreen tree belonging to the Sapotaceae family.

It is native to West Africa and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, white flowers that bloom in clusters. The tree produces edible, star-shaped fruits that are typically yellow or orange when ripe. Its fruit is often used in local cuisines and has a flavor similar to that of the common starfruit.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for its timber.
