Deyeuxia orbignyana

Deyeuxia orbignyana, commonly known as the silvergrass, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-tomentose leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in wetland or marshy environments, thriving in moist soils. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in dense spikelets, which are typical of the Poaceae family.

Deyeuxia orbignyana plays an important ecological role in stabilizing wetland soils and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
