Durio wyatt-smithii

Durio wyatt-smithii, commonly known as the Kedah Durian, is a tropical plant native to Malaysia.

It belongs to the Durio genus, which is part of the Malvaceae family. The plant produces a large, spiky fruit that is highly prized for its unique flavor and aroma. The fruit's pericarp contains a creamy, custard-like pulp that is rich in nutrients.

Due to its limited distribution and cultivation, it is considered a rare and valuable species within the durian family.
