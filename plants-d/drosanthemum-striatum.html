Drosanthemum striatum

Drosanthemum striatum, commonly known as the striped rock rose, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and purple during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
