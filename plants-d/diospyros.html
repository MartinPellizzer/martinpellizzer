Diospyros

Diospyros, commonly known as persimmon, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Ebenaceae.

It includes around 400 species, many of which are native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, Africa, and the Americas. The wood of some species is highly valued for its density and durability, making it useful in furniture and tool-making.
