Dipterocarpus pulcherrimus

Dipterocarpus pulcherrimus, commonly known as the beautiful dipterocarp, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dipterocarpaceae and is characterized by its large, buttressed roots and smooth, often reddish-brown bark. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody, elongated fruits containing winged seeds. It plays an important ecological role in tropical rainforests, contributing to biodiversity and soil stability.

Due to its economic value, it is also a target for deforestation and habitat loss.
