Dracaena lennei

Dracaena lennei, commonly known as the Black Dracaena, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves that form a rosette at the base. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear at the tips of the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Dracaena lennei is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
