Dichanthelium superatum

Dichanthelium superatum, commonly known as the tall panicgrass, is a grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets.

Dichanthelium superatum is adapted to a variety of soil types and is often found in wet or mesic habitats.
