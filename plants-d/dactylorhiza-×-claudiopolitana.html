Dactylorhiza × claudiopolitana

Dactylorhiza × claudiopolitana, commonly known as the hybrid marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in wet or damp habitats such as marshes, bogs, and shaded woodlands. The plant has a slender, upright stem with several oval-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is often found in association with other orchid species in its native range.
