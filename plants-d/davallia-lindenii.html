Davallia lindenii

Davallia lindenii, commonly known as the golden fern, is a species of polypodioid fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that are typically light green to golden in color. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining, acidic soil. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine, and it is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.

Davallia lindenii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
