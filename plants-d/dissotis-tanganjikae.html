Dissotis tanganjikae

Dissotis tanganjikae, commonly known as Lake Tanganyika water lettuce, is a floating aquatic plant native to the Lake Tanganyika region in Africa.

It has broad, waxy, heart-shaped leaves that form a rosette structure on the water surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments with ample sunlight.

Due to its rapid growth, it can become invasive in non-native habitats if not managed properly.
