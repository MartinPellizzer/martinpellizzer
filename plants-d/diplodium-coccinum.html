Diplodium coccinum

Diplodium coccinum, commonly known as the scarlet runner bean, is a climbing plant native to South America.

It produces vibrant red flowers that are often used in ornamental gardening. The plant has compound leaves with three leaflets and produces long, cylindrical pods that contain edible seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its botanical features make it both aesthetically pleasing and useful for culinary purposes.
