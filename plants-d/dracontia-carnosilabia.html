Dracontia carnosilabia

Dracontia carnosilabia, commonly known as the Crimson Vein Orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its vivid red and green foliage, which features intricate vein patterns that give it its common name. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, often arranged in loose clusters. Native to the tropical regions of South America, it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly sought after by botanists and horticulturists alike.
