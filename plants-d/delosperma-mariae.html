Delosperma mariae

Delosperma mariae, commonly known as the Cape tulip, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems and vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Delosperma mariae is drought-tolerant and requires minimal maintenance once established.
