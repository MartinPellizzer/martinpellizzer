Desmodium campenonii

Desmodium campenonii, commonly known as the purple desmodium, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which are often a striking purple or violet color. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. Its pods are typically slender and contain several small, rounded seeds.

Desmodium campenonii is often used in traditional medicine and as a forage crop due to its nutritional value.
