Drynaria brooksii

Drynaria brooksii, commonly known as the mountain fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and pinnately compound fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its fronds are often dark green with a leathery texture, and they can reach lengths of up to 1 meter.

Drynaria brooksii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
