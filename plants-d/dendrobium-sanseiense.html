Dendrobium sanseiense

Dendrobium sanseiense, commonly known as the Sanseiense orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in tropical rainforests. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. Its leaves are usually long and strap-like, adapted for efficient photosynthesis in its natural habitat.

Dendrobium sanseiense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
