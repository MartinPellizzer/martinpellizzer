Dichromena latifolia

Dichromena latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf dichromena, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along stream banks. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The species is valued for both its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
