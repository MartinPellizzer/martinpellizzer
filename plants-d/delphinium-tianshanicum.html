Delphinium tianshanicum

Delphinium tianshanicum, commonly known as the Tianshan larkspur, is a perennial herb native to the Tianshan Mountains in China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces showy blue to purple flowers in racemes, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are palmately divided, with a rough texture and a distinctive scent.

Delphinium tianshanicum is adapted to high-altitude environments and is often found in meadows and rocky slopes.
