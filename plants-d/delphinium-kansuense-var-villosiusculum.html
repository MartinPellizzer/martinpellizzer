Delphinium kansuense var. villosiusculum

Delphinium kansuense var. villosiusculum, commonly known as the hairy leaf larkspur, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features tall, erect stems that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height, adorned with lance-shaped, hairy leaves. The plant produces striking blue to purple flowers in spikes, which bloom in late summer. Its hairy foliage and showy inflorescences make it a distinctive addition to alpine gardens.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, preferring cooler climates.
