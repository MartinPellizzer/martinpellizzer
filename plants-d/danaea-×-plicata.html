Danaea × plicata

Danaea × plicata, commonly known as the curled leaf danaea, is a hybrid plant in the Commelinaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of the Americas, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking, velvety, and often brightly colored leaves that are typically curled or crinkled. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color.

Danaea × plicata is often cultivated for its ornamental foliage rather than its flowers.
