Dianthus pseudocorymbosus

Dianthus pseudocorymbosus, commonly known as the Sierra dianthus, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family, which includes many ornamental and medicinal plants. The plant features clustered pink or red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically linear and covered in fine hairs, and it thrives in well-drained, rocky soils.
