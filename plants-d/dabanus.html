Dabanus

Dabanus, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant belonging to the Leguminosae family.

It is characterized by its striking clusters of yellow flowers that resemble cascading showers, hence its common name. The plant produces elongated pods that contain flat, edible seeds used in traditional cuisine. Dabanus thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its leaves are trifoliate, and the plant is known for its rapid growth and adaptability to various soil types.
