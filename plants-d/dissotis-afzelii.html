Dissotis afzelii

Dissotis afzelii, commonly known as the African star grass, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, erect stems. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers arranged in dense, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a smooth texture and a bluish-green color.

Dissotis afzelii is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive appearance and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
