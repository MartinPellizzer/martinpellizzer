Dortmanna Quadrangularis

Dortmanna quadrangularis is a unique and lesser-known plant species that thrives in specific environmental conditions. It is characterized by its distinct quadrangular stem and small, clustered leaves. This plant is often found in moist, shaded areas, making it a common sight in certain forested regions. Its simple structure and adaptability have made it a subject of interest for botanists and plant enthusiasts. Despite its modest appearance, Dortmanna quadrangularis plays an important role in its ecosystem.
