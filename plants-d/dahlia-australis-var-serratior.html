Dahlia australis var. serratior

Dahlia australis var. serratior, commonly known as the serrated dahlia, is a striking perennial plant native to South America.

It features showy, single or double flowers in a wide range of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant has sturdy, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges, giving it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden beds and containers.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to both ornamental and native plant gardens.
