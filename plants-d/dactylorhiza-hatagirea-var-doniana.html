Dactylorhiza hatagirea var. doniana

Dactylorhiza hatagirea var. doniana, commonly known as the Himalayan orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the alpine regions of the Himalayas.

It is a perennial herb with a rhizomatous root system and produces erect, leafy stems. The plant blooms with tall, branched inflorescences bearing numerous small, greenish-white flowers. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is adapted to high-altitude conditions.

Due to its rarity and ecological significance, it is considered a valuable species in botanical studies.
