Descantaria elongata

Descantaria elongata, commonly known as the long-stemmed bellflower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its elongated, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. Descantaria elongata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
