Daphne rubriflora

Daphne rubriflora, commonly known as the red-flowered daphne, is a deciduous shrub native to southern China.

It is characterized by its striking, deep red flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms and fragrant scent.
