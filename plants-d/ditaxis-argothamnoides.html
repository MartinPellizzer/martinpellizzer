Ditaxis argothamnoides

Ditaxis argothamnoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved dittany, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub with slender, often spiny stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, which attract various pollinators. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
