Delphinium orientale var. hispanicum

Delphinium orientale var. hispanicum, commonly known as the Spanish larkspur, is a herbaceous perennial native to the Mediterranean region.

It features striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has tall, slender stems with lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. Its flowers are typically arranged in dense spikes, and it prefers well-drained soil in full sun.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens and its historical use in traditional medicine.
