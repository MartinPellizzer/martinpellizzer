Dorycnium sericeum

Dorycnium sericeum, commonly known as silver vetch, is a flowering plant native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry grasslands and rocky habitats.

Dorycnium sericeum is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil fertility.
