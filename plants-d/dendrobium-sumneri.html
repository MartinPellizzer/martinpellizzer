Dendrobium sumneri

Dendrobium sumneri, commonly known as the New Zealand orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to New Zealand.

It grows on trees and rocks, often in humid, shaded environments. The plant has a tall, cane-like stem with numerous fleshy, sword-shaped leaves. It produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months.

Dendrobium sumneri is valued for its ornamental beauty and is a symbol of New Zealand's unique flora.
