Disa triloba

Disa triloba, commonly known as the three-lobed orchid, is a species of orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in late summer. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of pink or purple flowers with three distinct lobes. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a sunny to partially shaded environment.

Disa triloba is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
