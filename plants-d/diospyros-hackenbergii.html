Diospyros hackenbergii

Diospyros hackenbergii, commonly known as the KwaZulu-Natal ebony, is a small to medium-sized tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Ebenaceae family, which includes other ebony species. The plant is characterized by its dark, dense wood, which is highly valued for its strength and durability. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in forested areas.

The leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a glossy appearance.
