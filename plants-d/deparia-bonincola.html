Deparia bonincola

Deparia bonincola, commonly known as the Bonin shield fern, is a species of fern native to the Bonin Islands.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests and rocky crevices. Its leaves are bipinnate, with a dense arrangement of small, rounded pinnae.

The spores are produced on the underside of the fronds, which are covered in a layer of sporangia.
