Dendrobium arachnanthe

Dendrobium arachnanthe, commonly known as the spider orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to Australia.

It features a tall, wiry stem with dense clusters of small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink. The plant has a unique appearance with its long, thread-like roots and intricate, spider-like inflorescence. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Dendrobium arachnanthe is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
