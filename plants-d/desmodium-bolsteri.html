Desmodium bolsteri

Desmodium bolsteri, commonly known as the dwarf desmodium, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to parts of Africa and is characterized by its small, trifoliate leaves and clusters of small, pea-like flowers. Desmodium bolsteri is known for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil fertility in agricultural settings.
