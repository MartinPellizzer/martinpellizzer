Desmodium trigonum

Desmodium trigonum, commonly known as smallleaf desmodium, is a herbaceous annual plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features trifoliate leaves with three small, oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically purple or pink in color. It is often found in open areas, disturbed soils, and along roadsides.

Desmodium trigonum is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it beneficial in soil enrichment.
