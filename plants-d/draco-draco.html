Draco draco

Draco draco, commonly known as the Draco's dragon tree, is a unique species native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves and a distinctive, branching structure that resembles a tree. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny, winged seeds.

Draco draco is adapted to thrive in arid environments, making it a resilient and fascinating botanical specimen.
