Dendrobium eriiflorum var. sikkimense

Dendrobium eriiflorum var. sikkimense, commonly known as the Sikkim orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Himalayan regions of India and Nepal.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive spurred lip. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, typically arranged in a rosette pattern.

This species thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.
