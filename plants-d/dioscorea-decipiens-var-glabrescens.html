Dioscorea decipiens var. glabrescens

Dioscorea decipiens var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth yam, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Dioscoreaceae family and is characterized by its tuberous roots, which are often used in traditional medicine. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers. Its smooth, glabrous stems distinguish it from other varieties of Dioscorea.

The tubers are typically harvested for their nutritional and medicinal properties.
