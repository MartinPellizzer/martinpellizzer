Dactylostemon oligandrus var. parvifolia

The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by edible, fig-like fruits that are typically yellow or orange when mature. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on rocky outcrops or in dense undergrowth.
