Dracaena fragrans variegata

Dracaena fragrans variegata, commonly known as the dragon tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking variegated leaves, which display shades of green, white, and yellow. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically followed by red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Dracaena fragrans variegata is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
