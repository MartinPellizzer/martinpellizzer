Desmodium rhabdocladum

Desmodium rhabdocladum, commonly known as the silverleaf desmodium, is a leguminous plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves with a silvery underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in tropical and subtropical regions and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

Desmodium rhabdocladum is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it valuable for soil enrichment.
