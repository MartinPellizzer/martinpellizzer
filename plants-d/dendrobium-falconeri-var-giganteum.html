Dendrobium falconeri var. giganteum

Dendrobium falconeri var. giganteum, commonly known as the giant orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to the high-altitude regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, cane-like stems that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright but indirect light.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a valuable and protected species in its native range.
