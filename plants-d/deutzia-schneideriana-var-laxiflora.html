Deutzia schneideriana var. laxiflora

Deutzia schneideriana var. laxiflora, commonly known as the looseflower deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features slender, arching branches and produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers in early summer. The foliage is typically dark green, providing a striking contrast to the delicate blooms. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

This plant is often used in garden landscapes for its ornamental value and easy maintenance.
