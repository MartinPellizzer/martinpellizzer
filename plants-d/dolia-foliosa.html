Dolia foliosa

Dolia foliosa, commonly known as the leafy bladderwort, is a carnivorous plant belonging to the family Lentibacillaceae.

It is characterized by its small, bladder-like structures that trap and digest small aquatic organisms. Dolia foliosa plays a minor role in its ecosystem by controlling populations of small invertebrates and contributing to nutrient cycling.
