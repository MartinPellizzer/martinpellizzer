Desmodium elegans var. handelii

Desmodium elegans var. handelii, commonly known as the velvet bean or silky senna, is a species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a leguminous plant characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellow flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it beneficial for soil enrichment in agricultural settings.
