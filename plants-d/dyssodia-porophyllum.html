Dyssodia porophyllum

Dyssodia porophyllum, commonly known as the silverleaf dyssodia, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. It produces small yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
