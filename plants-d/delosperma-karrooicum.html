Delosperma karrooicum

Delosperma karrooicum, commonly known as the Karroo ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves and clusters of small, star-shaped yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its hardy nature and attractive appearance make it a valuable addition to drought-tolerant landscapes.
