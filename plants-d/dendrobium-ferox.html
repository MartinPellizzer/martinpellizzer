Dendrobium ferox

Dendrobium ferox, commonly known as the fierce orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, showy flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the surrounding substrate.

Dendrobium ferox is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
