Diphyes recurviflora

Diphyes recurviflora, commonly known as the curl-leaved diphyes, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, recurved leaves that form a dense, rosette-like structure. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters along the stem. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid forest understories.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
