Danthoniopsis viridis

Danthoniopsis viridis, commonly known as the green danthoniopsis, is a perennial grass species native to South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bright green and slightly hairy. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, which are characteristic of the Poaceae family. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Due to its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
