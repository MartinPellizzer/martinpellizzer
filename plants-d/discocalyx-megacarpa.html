Discocalyx megacarpa

Discocalyx megacarpa, commonly known as the giant calyx fruit.

It is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its large, showy inflorescences. The plant produces large, woody fruits that can weigh several kilograms. Its botanical features include thick, fleshy calyces that enclose the fruit.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique and striking appearance.
