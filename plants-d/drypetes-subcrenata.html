Drypetes subcrenata

Drypetes subcrenata, commonly known as the white-flowered drypetes, is a small to medium-sized tree native to Southeast Asia.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation areas.
