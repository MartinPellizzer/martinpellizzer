Dorstenia anthuriifolia

Dorstenia anthuriifolia, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a member of the Moraceae family and is native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Dorstenia anthuriifolia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
