Dracaena sydneyi

Dracaena sydneyi, commonly known as the Sydney Dracaena, is a striking evergreen shrub native to Australia.

It features long, narrow, dark green leaves that are often marked with subtle white or yellow variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by red, berry-like fruits that are edible for birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Dracaena sydneyi is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to adapt to indoor growing conditions.
