Didymopanax macrocarpus

Didymopanax macrocarpus, commonly known as the giant arrowroot or giant sago palm, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, pinnately compound leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by dark purple berries containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for cultivation in tropical gardens.
