Dorystigma caulescens

Dorystigma caulescens, commonly known as the Cape rose, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its woody stems and dense foliage. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its leaves are small, elliptical, and often covered in fine hairs.

Dorystigma caulescens is well adapted to arid conditions and thrives in full sun.
