Dolichos quarrei

Dolichos quarrei, commonly known as the slender cowpea, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, twining stems and pinnately compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by elongated, pod-like fruits containing several seeds. Dolichos quarrei is valued for its nutritional seeds, which are rich in protein and used in various cuisines.

It also plays a role in soil improvement due to its nitrogen-fixing ability.
