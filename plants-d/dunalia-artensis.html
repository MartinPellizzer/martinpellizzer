Dunalia artensis

Dunalia artensis, commonly known as the Artensian dune plant, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Europe.

It thrives in sandy and saline soils, typically found in dune ecosystems where it plays a crucial role in stabilizing sand dunes. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or purple, blooming during the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, adapted to conserve water in arid environments.

Dunalia artensis is also valued for its ecological importance in supporting local insect and bird populations.
