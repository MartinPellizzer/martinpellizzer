Delphinium gongliuense

Delphinium gongliuense, commonly known as the Gongliu delphinium, is a perennial flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that bear numerous showy blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and thrives in well-drained, loamy soils. Its foliage consists of palmately compound leaves with serrated edges, and it is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.

Delphinium gongliuense is also noted for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
