Diglosselis

Diglosselis, commonly known as the common thornapple, is a flowering plant belonging to the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and lobed leaves, which are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically purple or blue in color. Its fruit is a globose berry that turns from green to yellow or orange as it matures.

Diglosselis is native to parts of Europe and Asia and is often found in dry, disturbed habitats.
