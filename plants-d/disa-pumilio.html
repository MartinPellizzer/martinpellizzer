Disa pumilio

Disa pumilio, commonly known as the dwarf orchid or miniature orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that are typically pink or white in color. The plant grows as a terrestrial orchid, often found in grasslands and open areas with well-drained soil. It produces a single stem with several flowers, each featuring three petals and three sepals.

Disa pumilio is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
