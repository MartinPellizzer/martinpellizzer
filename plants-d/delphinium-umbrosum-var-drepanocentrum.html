Delphinium umbrosum var. drepanocentrum

Delphinium umbrosum var. drepanocentrum, commonly known as the dark-leaved larkspur, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features tall, upright stems that bear clusters of striking blue to purple flowers. The leaves are dark green and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
