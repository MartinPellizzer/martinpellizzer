Dolichos capitatus

Dolichos capitatus, commonly known as the African burr bean, is a leguminous plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by distinctive, spiny pods containing edible seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in dry, disturbed soils.

Dolichos capitatus is valued for its nutritional seeds and is used in traditional agriculture and cuisine.
