Dimeria falcata var. tenuior

Dimeria falcata var. tenuior, commonly known as the slender dimeria, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to specific regions of North America.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are usually less than 1 inch in width, contributing to its distinctive appearance. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in dense clusters, and it blooms during the spring and early summer.

This variety is noted for its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
