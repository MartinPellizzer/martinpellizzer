Dendranthema zawadzkii var. sinchangense

Dendranthema zawadzkii var. sinchangense, commonly known as the Sinchang chrysanthemum, is a variety of the chrysanthemum family (Asteraceae).

It is native to China and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, daisy-like flower heads. The plant typically produces bright yellow to orange flowers, which bloom in the fall. It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticultural displays.
