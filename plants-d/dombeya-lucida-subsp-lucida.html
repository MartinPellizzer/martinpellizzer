Dombeya lucida subsp. lucida

Dombeya lucida subsp.

lucida, commonly known as the white dombeya, is a shrub native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its showy, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring.

The plant has smooth, often aromatic leaves and produces small, fleshy fruits that are dispersed by birds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and compact growth habit.
