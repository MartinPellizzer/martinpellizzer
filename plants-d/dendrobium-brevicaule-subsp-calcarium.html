Dendrobium brevicaule subsp. calcarium

Dendrobium brevicaule subsp.

calcarium, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a sympodial epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia. It features a short, compact growth habit with a pseudobulb and a few leaves.

The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and white, typically in late winter or early spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining substrates.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
