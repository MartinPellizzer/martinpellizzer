Dryobalanops beccariana

Dryobalanops beccariana, commonly known as the Beccarian's ironwood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dipterocarpaceae and is characterized by its dense, heavy wood that is highly valued for its durability. The plant has a tall, straight trunk with a smooth, grayish-brown bark. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptic in shape, often with a waxy surface.

It produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds.
