Dacrydium nidulum var. araucarioides

Dacrydium nidulum var. araucarioides, commonly known as the Tasmanian blackwood, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to Tasmania.

It is characterized by its dark, aromatic bark and needle-like leaves that are typically arranged in clusters. The plant produces small, cone-like structures that contain its seeds, and it thrives in cool, moist environments.

It plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
