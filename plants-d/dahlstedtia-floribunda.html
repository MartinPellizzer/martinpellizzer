Dahlstedtia floribunda

Dahlstedtia floribunda, commonly known as the mountain bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to the western United States.

It features upright stems covered in lance-shaped, dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of bell-shaped, violet-blue flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in rocky soils and full sun, often found in alpine and subalpine habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and ornamental addition to native plant gardens.
