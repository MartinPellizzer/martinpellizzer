Dryopteris martinicensis

Dryopteris martinicensis, commonly known as the Martinique shield fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its large, leathery fronds that can grow up to 2 meters in length. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with fertile and sterile fronds emerging from the same plant. Its leaves are typically bi- or tri-pinnate, with a distinctive shield-shaped base.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
