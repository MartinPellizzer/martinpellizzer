Draconia songarica

Draconia songarica, commonly known as the Songarica dragonroot, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features a robust, fleshy root system that stores nutrients and water, allowing it to survive in harsh, arid environments. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a waxy coating, which reduces water loss through transpiration. Its flowers are small, clustered in umbels, and typically have a pale yellow to greenish hue.

Draconia songarica is valued in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties.
