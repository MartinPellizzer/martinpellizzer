Desmodium pseudogyroides

Desmodium pseudogyroides, commonly known as the small-leaf desmodium, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and woodlands. It has a fibrous root system and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Desmodium pseudogyroides is also known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility.
