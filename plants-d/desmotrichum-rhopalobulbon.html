Desmotrichum rhopalobulbon

Desmotrichum rhopalobulbon, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant features showy, yellow-orange ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers. Its leaves are narrow, grass-like, and arranged in a basal rosette.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.
