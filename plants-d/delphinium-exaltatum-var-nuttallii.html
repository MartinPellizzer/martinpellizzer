Delphinium exaltatum var. nuttallii

Delphinium exaltatum var. nuttallii, commonly known as Nuttall's larkspur, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It features tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant produces showy blue to purple flowers in racemes, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a rough, hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a striking addition to gardens and natural habitats.
