Digitaria latronum

Digitaria latronum, commonly known as the African grass or red grass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny grains of seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in disturbed soils and open areas.

Digitaria latronum is known for its rapid growth and ability to form dense stands, making it both a valuable forage plant and a potential weed.
