Dendrobium lomatochilum

Dendrobium lomatochilum, commonly known as the Lomatochilum orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with thick, fleshy rhizomes that store water and nutrients. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, pale yellow to greenish-white flowers during the dry season. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
