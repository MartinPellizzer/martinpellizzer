Dupatya schomburgkii

Dupatya schomburgkii, commonly known as the Schomburgk's plectranthus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically purple or violet in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Dupatya schomburgkii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant fragrance.
