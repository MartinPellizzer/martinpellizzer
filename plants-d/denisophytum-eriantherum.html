Denisophytum eriantherum

Denisophytum eriantherum, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Denisophytum eriantherum is primarily valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
