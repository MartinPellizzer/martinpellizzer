Dillenia triquetra

Dillenia triquetra, commonly known as the three-angled dillenia, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, three-lobed leaves. The plant produces showy, yellowish-white flowers that are typically clustered in umbels. Its fruits are large, fleshy berries that contain several seeds.

Dillenia triquetra is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
