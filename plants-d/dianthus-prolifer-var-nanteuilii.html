Dianthus prolifer var. nanteuilii

Dianthus prolifer var. nanteuilii, commonly known as the Nanteuil's carnation, is a compact, mat-forming perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, double-petaled flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, which bloom profusely from early summer to autumn. The plant has slender, branched stems covered with fine, hairy foliage that helps it retain moisture in dry conditions. Its flowers are typically arranged in dense clusters, creating a striking visual display in gardens.

It is well-suited for rock gardens and borders, thriving in full sun and well-drained soil.
