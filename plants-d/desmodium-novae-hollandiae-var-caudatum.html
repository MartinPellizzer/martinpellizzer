Desmodium novae-hollandiae var. caudatum

Desmodium novae-hollandiae var. caudatum, commonly known as the curly cowpea, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pale yellow to greenish flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is drought-tolerant. It produces small, bean-like pods that contain edible seeds.

The species is often used in native gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
