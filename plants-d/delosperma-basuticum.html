Delosperma basuticum

Delosperma basuticum, commonly known as the Basutoland ice plant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are adapted to store water, typical of arid environments. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the warmer months, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
