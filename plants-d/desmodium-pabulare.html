Desmodium pabulare

Desmodium pabulare, commonly known as the creeping sicklepod, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellow, pea-like flowers. The plant produces pods that are typically curved and contain several small, kidney-shaped seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and pastures.

Desmodium pabulare is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial in soil enrichment.
