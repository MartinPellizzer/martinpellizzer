Dioon edule

Dioon edule, commonly known as the Mexican cactus apple, is a member of the Zamiaceae family.

It is a dioecious plant, meaning it has separate male and female individuals. The plant produces large, spiny, palm-like leaves and clusters of yellowish-green flowers. Its fruits are fleshy and edible, containing a sweet, juicy pulp surrounded by seeds.

It is native to parts of Mexico and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
