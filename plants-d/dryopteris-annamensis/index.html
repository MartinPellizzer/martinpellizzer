Dryopteris Annamensis

Dryopteris annamensis is a rare fern species native to the mountainous regions of Vietnam. It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is known for its delicate, lace-like fronds. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Due to its limited distribution and habitat loss, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique and beautiful species.
