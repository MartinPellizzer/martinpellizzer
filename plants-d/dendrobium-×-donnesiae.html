Dendrobium × donnesiae

Dendrobium × donnesiae, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a sympodial epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a cane-like stem with numerous nodes and is characterized by its long, linear leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange, often in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Dendrobium × donnesiae is popular among orchid growers for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
