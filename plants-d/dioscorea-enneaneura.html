Dioscorea enneaneura

Dioscorea enneaneura, commonly known as the African yam vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Dioscoreaceae family and is characterized by its tuberous roots, which are used as a food source. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers. Its vines can grow quite long, often reaching several meters in length.

The tubers of Dioscorea enneaneura are rich in starch and have been traditionally cultivated by various African communities.
