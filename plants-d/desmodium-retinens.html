Desmodium retinens

Desmodium retinens, commonly known as the small-leaf burr legume, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features trifoliate leaves with small, rounded leaflets and produces small, yellowish flowers. Its seed pods are hairy and often contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.

Desmodium retinens is often used in traditional medicine and as a forage plant for livestock.
