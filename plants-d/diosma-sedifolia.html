Diosma sedifolia

Diosma sedifolia, commonly known as the leafy diosma, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades, and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, and it is often found in forest understories.
