Diospyros kupensis

Diospyros kupensis, commonly known as Kupensis ebony, is a tropical tree native to the island of Madagascar.

It belongs to the Ebenaceae family, which includes other ebony species. The plant is characterized by its dark, dense wood, which is highly valued for its durability and fine grain. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in lowland rainforests.

The tree produces small, yellowish flowers followed by edible, orange-red berries.
