Dracontium surinamense

Dracontium surinamense, commonly known as the Surinam arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and striking inflorescence. The plant produces a starchy tuberous root that is used as a food source in some regions. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid lowland forests.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ecological and agricultural purposes.
