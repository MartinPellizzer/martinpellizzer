Delonix pumila

Delonix pumila, commonly known as the dwarf flame tree, is a small, deciduous shrub or tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright orange to red flowers that bloom in summer, forming pendulous clusters. The plant has pinnate leaves composed of several small, ovate leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and arid regions.

Delonix pumila is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to provide shade in warm climates.
