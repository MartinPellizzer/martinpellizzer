Diplotaxis erucastrum

Diplotaxis erucastrum, commonly known as the African rocket or desert cress, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, annual herb that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters. Its seeds are enclosed in a capsule that splits open when mature to release the seeds.

The plant is known for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
