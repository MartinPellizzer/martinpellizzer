Diospyros discolor

Diospyros discolor, commonly known as the rough leaf ebony, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark, glossy leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. The fruit is a small, round drupe that turns reddish when mature. The bark and leaves are used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

It is also valued for its durable wood, which is used in furniture and carving.
