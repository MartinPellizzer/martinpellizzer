Delosperma brevisepalum

Delosperma brevisepalum, commonly known as the narrow-spatulate ice plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, rounded leaves that are typically blue-green in color, adapted for water storage. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of pink, red, and purple, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
