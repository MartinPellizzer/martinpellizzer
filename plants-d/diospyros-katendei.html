Diospyros katendei

Diospyros katendei, commonly known as the Katende persimmon, is a species of flowering plant in the Ebenaceae family.

It is native to tropical Africa, particularly found in countries like Tanzania and Uganda. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers. Its fruit is a small, round berry that turns from green to orange when mature.
