Dendrobium canaliculatum var. fitzalanii

Dendrobium canaliculatum var. fitzalanii, commonly known as the Fitzalan's waxlip, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with thick, fleshy rhizomes that store water and nutrients. The plant produces clusters of long, narrow, wax-like leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. Its flowers are showy, with pale yellow to cream petals and a distinctive, elongated lip.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique appearance and resilience.
