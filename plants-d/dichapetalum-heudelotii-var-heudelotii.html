Dichapetalum heudelotii var. heudelotii

Dichapetalum heudelotii var. heudelotii, commonly known as the African milk tree, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, milky sap, which is a key feature of the genus Dichapetalum. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels, and it has opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is also notable for its use in traditional medicine and its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
