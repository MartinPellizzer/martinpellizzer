Dracocephalum kokanicum

Dracocephalum kokanicum, commonly known as the Kashmir saffron or mountain saffron, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, violet-blue flowers. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local medicine.
