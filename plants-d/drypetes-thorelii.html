Drypetes thorelii

Drypetes thorelii, commonly known as the African ebony, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its dark, dense wood that is highly valued for its durability and aesthetic appeal. The tree is often found in moist, shaded habitats and is important in both ecological and economic contexts.
