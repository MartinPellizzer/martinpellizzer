Draba araratica

Draba araratica, commonly known as the Armenian rockcress, is a small flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is native to mountainous regions of the Caucasus, Turkey, and parts of Central Asia. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils. It produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers in early spring.

Draba araratica is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, lance-shaped leaves and is often found in high-altitude habitats.
