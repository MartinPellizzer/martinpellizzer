Dendrobium strepsiceros

Dendrobium strepsiceros, commonly known as the sword-leaf dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with cane-like stems that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces lanceolate leaves that are often glaucous and have a waxy coating. Its flowers are typically yellow or green with red or purple markings, and they bloom in clusters along the stems.

Dendrobium strepsiceros is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions.
