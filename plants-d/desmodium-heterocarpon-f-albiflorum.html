Desmodium heterocarpon f. albiflorum

Desmodium heterocarpon f. albiflorum, commonly known as whiteflower desmodium, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features trifoliate leaves with small, rounded leaflets and produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters. Its seed pods are slender and usually contain several small, kidney-shaped seeds.

The plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it valuable in agricultural and ecological contexts.
