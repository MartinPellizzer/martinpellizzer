Draba Ventosa Var. Ruaxes

Draba ventosa var. ruaxes is a small, hardy plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe. It thrives in rocky and alpine environments, often growing in sparse soil and harsh climates. This variant is known for its delicate white or pale purple flowers that bloom in early spring. Despite its modest size, it plays an important role in its ecosystem by supporting pollinators and contributing to biodiversity. Its resilience and beauty make it a notable subject for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike.
