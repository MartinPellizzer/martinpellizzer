Dinetus paniculatus

Dinetus paniculatus, commonly known as the panicled dinet.

It is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America, particularly in Brazil. The plant is characterized by its tall, branched inflorescence that bears numerous small, white or pale yellow flowers. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, arranged in a basal rosette.

Dinetus paniculatus is often found in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
