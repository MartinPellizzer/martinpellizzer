Dieffenbachia picta f. brasiliensis

Dieffenbachia picta f. brasiliensis, commonly known as the Brasiliensis Dieffenbachia, is a compact, ornamental plant native to South America.

It features broad, arrow-shaped leaves with a striking combination of white, yellow, and green patterns, making it highly decorative. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and glossy foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to maintain its vibrant appearance.

Due to its toxic sap, it should be handled with care and kept away from pets and children.
