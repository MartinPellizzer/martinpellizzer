Dichapetalum kribense

Dichapetalum kribense, commonly known as the Kribensis flame tree, is a striking tropical tree native to the Democratic Republic of the Congo.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual impact. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate or elliptical in shape. Its bark is smooth and dark, often with a slight peeling texture.

Dichapetalum kribense is often cultivated for its ornamental value in warm climates due to its vibrant floral display.
