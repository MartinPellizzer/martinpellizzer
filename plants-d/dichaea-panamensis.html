Dichaea panamensis

Dichaea panamensis, commonly known as the Panamanian dichaea, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant grows in shaded, humid environments, often in the understory of tropical forests. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Dichaea panamensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated by enthusiasts of rare orchids.
