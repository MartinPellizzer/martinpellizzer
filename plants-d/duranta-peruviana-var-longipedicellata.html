Duranta peruviana var. longipedicellata

Duranta peruviana var. longipedicellata, commonly known as the silverleaf duranta, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to South America.

It features striking blue or violet flowers that appear in clusters, typically during the summer months. The leaves are ovate with a silvery underside, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a hedge plant.
