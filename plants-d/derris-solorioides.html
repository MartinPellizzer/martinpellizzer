Derris solorioides

Derris solorioides, commonly known as the yellow senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant belongs to the Fabaceae family, also known as the legume or bean family. It produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.
