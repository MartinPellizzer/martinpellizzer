Dendrobium subuliferum var. gautierense

Dendrobium subuliferum var. gautierense, commonly known as the Gautier's spike orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic orchid, typically growing on trees or rocks in tropical rainforests. The plant features a compact growth habit with short, cylindrical pseudobulbs and narrow, sword-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually small, fragrant, and come in shades of white, yellow, and green.

It prefers bright, indirect light and moderate humidity for optimal growth.
