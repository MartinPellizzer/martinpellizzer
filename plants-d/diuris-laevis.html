Diuris laevis

Diuris laevis, commonly known as the slender spider-orchid, is an endemic species native to eastern Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, spider-like appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rainforests and understoreys. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by specific insects.

Diuris laevis is a small to medium-sized orchid, often reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters.
