Desmodium hainanense

Desmodium hainanense, commonly known as Hainan desmodium, is a flowering plant native to China, specifically the Hainan Island.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant typically grows as an erect annual herb with trifoliate leaves and small, clustered flowers.

Its seeds are enclosed in pods, a common feature among legumes, and it plays a role in soil enrichment due to its nitrogen-fixing capabilities.
