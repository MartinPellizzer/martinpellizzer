Diervilla maximowiczii

Diervilla maximowiczii, commonly known as the Korean firethorn, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features dense, spiny foliage and produces clusters of small, orange-red berries in the fall. The plant has narrow, elliptical leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant berries and winter structure.
