Daphne trichotoma subsp. techinensis

Daphne trichotoma subsp.

techinensis, commonly known as the Ethiopian daphne, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia. It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky and arid environments.

The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white during the spring. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often covered with fine hairs.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses in local communities.
