Drymaria Cordata Var. Gracilis

Drymaria cordata var. gracilis is a delicate, low-growing plant native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia. It features small, rounded leaves and produces tiny white flowers that bloom in early summer. This variety is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks. Its compact size and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. Despite its modest appearance, it plays an important role in supporting local insect populations and enhancing biodiversity.
