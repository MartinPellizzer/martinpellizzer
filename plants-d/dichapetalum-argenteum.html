Dichapetalum argenteum

Dichapetalum argenteum, commonly known as silverleaf dichapetalum, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its silver-white undersides on its leaves, which give it its common name. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, typically arranged in clusters.

The plant produces small, round fruits that contain a single seed.
