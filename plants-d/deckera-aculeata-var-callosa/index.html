Deckera Aculeata Var. Callosa

Coral tree, scientifically known as Deckera aculeata var. callosa, is a striking tropical plant native to parts of Africa and the Caribbean. It is renowned for its vibrant, coral-like blooms that appear in clusters during the flowering season. The plant features palmate leaves and thorny stems, giving it a unique and somewhat rugged appearance. Despite its sharp thorns, it is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and public spaces. Its adaptability to various climates makes it a popular choice among horticulturists and plant enthusiasts alike.
