Doemia reticulata

Doemia reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its intricate, net-like venation pattern on its leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded, moist environments. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a variety of colors including white, yellow, and pink.

Doemia reticulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
