Disperis rhodoneura

Disperis rhodoneura, commonly known as the pink orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its striking pink and white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers a sunny to partially shaded environment. Its roots are fibrous, and it has a short, rhizomatous stem.

Disperis rhodoneura is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
