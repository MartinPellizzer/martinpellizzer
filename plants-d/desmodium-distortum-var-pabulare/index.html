Desmodium Distortum Var. Pabulare

Desmodium distortum var. pabulare is a lesser-known yet fascinating legume native to parts of Africa. This plant is characterized by its unique leaf structure and distinctive floral arrangements. Despite its rarity, it plays an important role in local ecosystems. It is often found in grassland habitats and thrives in well-drained soils. Due to its limited distribution, it remains a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
