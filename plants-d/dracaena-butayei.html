Dracaena butayei

Dracaena butayei, commonly known as the Butayei dracaena, is a species native to parts of Africa, particularly Tanzania.

It is a succulent plant characterized by its upright growth habit and fleshy, narrow leaves that are typically green with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or yellow in color, followed by red berries. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ornamental value lies in its unique foliage and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
