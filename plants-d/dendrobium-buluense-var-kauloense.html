Dendrobium buluense var. kauloense

Dendrobium buluense var. kauloense, commonly known as the Kauloense Dendrobium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. The plant features long, wiry stems that can climb or trail, and it produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. Its flowers are often short-lived but are noted for their elegant appearance and subtle fragrance.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to various growing conditions.
