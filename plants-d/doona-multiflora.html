Doona multiflora

Doona multiflora, commonly known as the Korean bellflower, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Doona multiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
