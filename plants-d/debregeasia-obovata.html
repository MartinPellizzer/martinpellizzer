Debregeasia obovata

Debregeasia obovata, commonly known as the Ethiopian rose or African rose, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in high-altitude regions, characterized by its woody stems and dense foliage. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are usually pink or white in color. Its leaves are elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture, while its flowers bloom in clusters at the ends of the branches.

Debregeasia obovata is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
