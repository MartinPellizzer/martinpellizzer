Dockrillia convoluta

Dockrillia convoluta, commonly known as the spiral dock, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Polygonaceae family.

It is characterized by its spirally arranged leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils along stream banks and wetlands. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and clustered in terminal inflorescences.
