Dryopteris crassirhizoma var. setosa

Dryopteris crassirhizoma var. setosa, commonly known as the hairy shield fern, is a variant of the broad-rhizome shield fern.

It is a perennial evergreen fern that thrives in shaded, moist environments. The plant features large, leathery fronds with a distinctive hairy texture on the underside. Its rhizomes are thick and robust, allowing it to form dense colonies.

It is native to eastern Asia and is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage.
