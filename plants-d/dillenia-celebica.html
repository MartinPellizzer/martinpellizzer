Dillenia celebica

Dillenia celebica, commonly known as the Celebes dillenia, is a flowering plant native to the Indonesian archipelago.

It is a member of the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a rough texture with serrated edges.

The fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds, contributing to its role in local ecosystems.
