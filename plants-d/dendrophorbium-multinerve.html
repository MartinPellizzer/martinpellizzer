Dendrophorbium multinerve

Dendrophorbium multinerve, commonly known as the branched leaf orchid, is a unique orchid species characterized by its intricate leaf structure and specialized growth habits.

It typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to tree trunks in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features elongated, often branched leaves with a network of veins, which contribute to its scientific name "multinerve.". Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pink.
