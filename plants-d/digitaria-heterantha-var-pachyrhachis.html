Digitaria heterantha var. pachyrhachis

Digitaria heterantha var. pachyrhachis, commonly known as broad-leaf bluestem, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves with a bluish-green hue and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant has a fibrous root system and is adapted to thrive in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of ponds.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important component of wetland ecosystems.
