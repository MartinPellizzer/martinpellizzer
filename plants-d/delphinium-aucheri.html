Delphinium aucheri

Delphinium aucheri, commonly known as the Giant Delphinium, is a striking perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters, adorned with large, showy blue to violet flowers. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are deeply lobed, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Delphinium aucheri is known for its long blooming period, typically from late spring to early summer.
