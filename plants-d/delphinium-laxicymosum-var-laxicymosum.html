Delphinium laxicymosum var. laxicymosum

Delphinium laxicymosum var. laxicymosum, commonly known as the slender larkspur, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

It typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet, featuring slender, upright stems covered in soft, hairy foliage. The plant produces showy blue to purple flowers in racemes, which bloom from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

The species is adapted to rocky and well-drained soils, often found in alpine and subalpine environments.
