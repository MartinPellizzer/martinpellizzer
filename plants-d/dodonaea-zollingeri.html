Dodonaea zollingeri

Dodonaea zollingeri, commonly known as silverleaf dodonaea, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant features small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are ovate and have a leathery texture, often covered in fine hairs.

Dodonaea zollingeri is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
