Dracocephalum scrobiculatum

Dracocephalum scrobiculatum, commonly known as purple head or dragonhead, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features square, hairy stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces spikes of small, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to blue, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Dracocephalum scrobiculatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its ability to thrive in well-drained soils.

It is also known for its aromatic foliage and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
