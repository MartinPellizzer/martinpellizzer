Dendranthema Zawadzkii Var. Latilobum

Shasta daisy, scientifically known as Dendranthema zawadzkii var. latilobum, is a popular ornamental plant prized for its striking white petals and yellow center. Native to the western United States, it thrives in sunny environments and well-drained soil. Its showy blooms make it a favorite in gardens and floral arrangements throughout the growing season. The plant is easy to care for, making it ideal for both novice and experienced gardeners. With its elegant appearance and hardy nature, the Shasta daisy remains a timeless addition to any landscape.
