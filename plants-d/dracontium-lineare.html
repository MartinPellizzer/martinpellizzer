Dracontium lineare

Dracontium lineare, commonly known as the linear arrowroot, is a herbaceous plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves and striking inflorescence. The plant produces a unique, spadix-like structure surrounded by a colorful spathe, typical of many aroid species. Its rhizomes are often harvested for their starchy roots, which are used in traditional medicine and cuisine.

The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental and practical uses.
