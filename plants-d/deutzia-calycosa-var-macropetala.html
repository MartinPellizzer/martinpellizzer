Deutzia calycosa var. macropetala

Deutzia calycosa var. macropetala, commonly known as the large-flowered deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features clusters of fragrant, white to pink flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has slender, arching branches with small, ovate leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

This ornamental plant is valued for its showy blooms and adaptability to various garden conditions.
