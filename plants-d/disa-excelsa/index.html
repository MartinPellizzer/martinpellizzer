Disa Excelsa

Rainbow orchid, scientifically known as Disa excelsa, is a striking and colorful plant native to southern Africa. Its name comes from the vibrant array of hues that adorn its blooms, ranging from deep purples to bright oranges and reds. This orchid thrives in high-altitude grasslands and is often found in mountainous regions where the climate is cool and moist. With its unique appearance and ecological significance, it has become a symbol of natural beauty and resilience. The rainbow orchid not only captivates with its visual appeal but also plays an important role in its native ecosystems.
