Diplazium hieronymi

Diplazium hieronymi, commonly known as the Hieronymus' water fern, is a species of aquatic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, floating fronds that are typically light green and have a feathery appearance. The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, often found in marshes and ponds. Its rhizomes are submerged, anchoring the plant while absorbing nutrients from the water.

Diplazium hieronymi plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.
