Delphinium bonvalotii var. eriostylum

Delphinium bonvalotii var. eriostylum, commonly known as the Alpine larkspur, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant produces showy blue to purple flowers in raceme-like clusters, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lanceolate and have a hairy texture, adapted to withstand cold and arid conditions.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or meadow habitats at high altitudes.
