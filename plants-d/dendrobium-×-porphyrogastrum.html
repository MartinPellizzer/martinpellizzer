Dendrobium × porphyrogastrum

Dendrobium × porphyrogastrum, commonly known as the Porphyrogastrum orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of pink, purple, and white, often with a velvety texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting mix.

Dendrobium × porphyrogastrum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
