Dillwynia hispida var. laevifolia

Dillwynia hispida var. laevifolia, commonly known as the smooth-leafed dillwynia, is a perennial shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-bearing fruits. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is found in regions with a Mediterranean climate. Its small, pea-like flowers are usually pink or purple, and it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

The variety laevifolia is distinguished by its smooth, rather than hairy, leaf surfaces.
