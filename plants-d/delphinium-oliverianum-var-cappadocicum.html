Delphinium oliverianum var. cappadocicum

Delphinium oliverianum var. cappadocicum, commonly known as the Cappadocian larkspur, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Cappadocia region of Turkey.

It features tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters. The plant produces showy blue to purple flowers in racemes, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a striking addition to rock gardens and alpine settings.
