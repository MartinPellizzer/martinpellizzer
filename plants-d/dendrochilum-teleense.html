Dendrochilum teleense

Dendrochilum teleense, commonly known as the Philippine tree orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically has long, strap-like leaves and is often found in shaded, humid environments. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink with a yellow lip, and it blooms during the rainy season.

Dendrochilum teleense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
