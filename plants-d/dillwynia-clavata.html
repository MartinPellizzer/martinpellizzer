Dillwynia clavata

Dillwynia clavata, commonly known as the slender dillwynia, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with slender, branching stems and small, oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of delicate, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Dillwynia clavata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
