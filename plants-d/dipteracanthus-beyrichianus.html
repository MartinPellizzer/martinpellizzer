Dipteracanthus beyrichianus

Dipteracanthus beyrichianus, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow and orange floral display. The plant typically grows in tropical rainforests, thriving in humid and shaded environments. Its flowers are arranged in dense clusters, with each bloom featuring a distinctive star-shaped pattern.

Dipteracanthus beyrichianus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
