Dendrosenecio Johnstonii Subsp. Refractisquamatus

Dendrosenecio johnstonii subsp. refractisquamatus is a rare and striking member of the Asteraceae family native to the highlands of Kenya. This plant is known for its unique, woolly appearance and vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions with well-drained soil. Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it faces significant conservation challenges. Its beauty and rarity make it a subject of both scientific interest and botanical fascination.
