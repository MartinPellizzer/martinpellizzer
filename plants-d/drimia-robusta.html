Drimia robusta

Drimia robusta, commonly known as the African lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, strap-like leaves that can grow up to 1.5 meters in length, providing a dramatic visual impact. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Drimia robusta is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to naturalize in suitable climates.
