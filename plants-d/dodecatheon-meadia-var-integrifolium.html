Dodecatheon meadia var. integrifolium

Dodecatheon meadia var. integrifolium, commonly known as the smoothleaf trout lily, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It features heart-shaped, smooth-edged leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of delicate, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white to pale yellow, often with a distinctive greenish tint. It thrives in moist, shaded woodlands and has a short growing season.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and ecologically important species in its native habitat.
