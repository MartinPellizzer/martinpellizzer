Didymocarpus aristatus

Didymocarpus aristatus, commonly known as the mountain hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are usually pink or red in color. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a velvety texture.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, hairy seeds.
