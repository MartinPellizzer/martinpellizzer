Diplazium Biserratum

Diplazium biserratum is a fern species native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa. It is known for its distinctive fronds that feature a unique combination of serrations and lobes. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its ornamental value has made it popular in horticulture and botanical gardens. Despite its beauty, it can be invasive in certain ecosystems if not managed properly.
