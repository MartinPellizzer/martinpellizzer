Drynaria curvivenia

Drynaria curvivenia, commonly known as the golden fern, is a tropical evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its leathery, dark green fronds with a distinctive golden hue on the underside.
