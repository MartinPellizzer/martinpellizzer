Duranta boekei

Duranta boekei, commonly known as the yellow duranta, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters tall, characterized by its opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, followed by blue or purple berries. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The plant is also valued for its ability to attract birds and butterflies due to its showy flowers and nutritious fruits.
