Delosperma saxicola

Delosperma saxicola, commonly known as the rock-hopper or stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are adapted to store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the summer months, adding ornamental value to its habitat. It is often found growing on rocky outcrops or in sandy soils, where it forms dense mats.

Due to its hardy nature, it is popular among gardeners seeking low-maintenance, drought-tolerant plants.
