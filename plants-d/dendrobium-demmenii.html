Dendrobium demmenii

Dendrobium demmenii, commonly known as the Demmen's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, cane-like stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink, typically during the summer months. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Dendrobium demmenii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
