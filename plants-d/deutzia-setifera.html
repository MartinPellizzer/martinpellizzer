Deutzia setifera

Deutzia setifera, commonly known as the white deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features slender, arching branches that bear clusters of small, white flowers in early summer. The plant has simple, ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green during the growing season. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Deutzia setifera is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of care in temperate climates.
