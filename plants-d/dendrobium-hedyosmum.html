Dendrobium hedyosmum

Dendrobium hedyosmum, commonly known as the yellow-lipped orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome and multiple pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a distinctive lip that is usually yellow or orange. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrobium hedyosmum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
