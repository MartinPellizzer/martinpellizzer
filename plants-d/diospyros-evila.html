Diospyros evila

Diospyros evila, commonly known as the African persimmon, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptic or ovate in shape. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in spikes, followed by orange to red berries containing one to three seeds.
