Dactylorhiza majalis var. praetermissa

Dactylorhiza majalis var. praetermissa, commonly known as the slender marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in wet or marshy habitats, often near water bodies such as ponds and streams. The plant features a single, slender stem with a few narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-yellow, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

This variety is noted for its delicate appearance and preference for moist, nutrient-poor soils.
