Dissotis goetzei

Dissotis goetzei, commonly known as the velvetleaf or false arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture, giving it its common name. Its flowers are small and clustered in spikes, usually ranging in color from white to pale yellow.

The plant is known for its rapid growth and is sometimes considered invasive in certain areas.
