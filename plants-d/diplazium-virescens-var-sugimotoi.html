Diplazium virescens var. sugimotoi

Diplazium virescens var. sugimotoi, commonly known as the green fern, is a variant of the common sword fern.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped fronds that are typically a vibrant green. The plant produces sporangia on the undersides of its fronds, which are clustered in sori. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
