Disocactus speciosus subsp. bierianus

Disocactus speciosus subsp.

bierianus, commonly known as the Bierianus Christmas cactus, is a hybrid species native to Brazil. It is characterized by its segmented, branching stems that bear vibrant, funnel-shaped flowers in shades of red, pink, and white.

The plant typically blooms during the holiday season, making it a popular ornamental species. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Its unique appearance and seasonal flowering make it a sought-after addition to indoor gardens.
