Dischidia gibbifera

Dischidia gibbifera, commonly known as the swollen leaf dischidia, is a climbing or trailing plant native to Southeast Asia.

Dischidia gibbifera is often grown as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
