Dombeya praetermissa

Dombeya praetermissa, commonly known as the narrow-leaved dombeya, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It features showy, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has simple, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Dombeya praetermissa is valued in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
