Delosperma suttoniae

Delosperma suttoniae, commonly known as the Sutton's ice plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white, powdery wax. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, or orange, typically during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
