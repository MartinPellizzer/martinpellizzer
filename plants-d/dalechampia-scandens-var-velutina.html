Dalechampia scandens var. velutina

Dalechampia scandens var. velutina, commonly known as velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to the Americas.

It is a herbaceous annual or perennial vine that can grow up to 5 meters in length. The plant features heart-shaped, velvety leaves with a distinctive pubescent surface. Its flowers are small and greenish-yellow, typically appearing in clusters.

The fruit is a capsule that contains numerous seeds, contributing to its invasive potential in some regions.
