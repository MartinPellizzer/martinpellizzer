Disocactus × hansii

Disocactus × hansii, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Cactaceae family.

It is characterized by its segmented, leaf-like stems that are typically green or variegated with red, pink, or purple hues. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow during the winter months, usually around Christmas, hence its common name. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Disocactus × hansii is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental appearance and seasonal blooming.
