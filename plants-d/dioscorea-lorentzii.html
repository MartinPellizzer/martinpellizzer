Dioscorea lorentzii

Dioscorea lorentzii, commonly known as the Lorentz yam, is a species of flowering plant in the family Dioscoreaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is a climbing vine with tuberous roots that are used for both ornamental and medicinal purposes. Its leaves are heart-shaped and can grow up to 30 cm in diameter, while its flowers are small and inconspicuous, typically greenish-yellow in color.

The tubers of Dioscorea lorentzii are sometimes utilized in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
