Dianthus moviensis var. dentatus

Dianthus moviensis var. dentatus, commonly known as the toothed campion, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its delicate, pink to red flowers that bloom in clusters atop slender stalks. The leaves are typically narrow and toothed, giving the plant its specific epithet "dentatus.". It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
