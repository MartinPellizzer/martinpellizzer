Dactylorhiza majalis f. magnicalcarata

Dactylorhiza majalis f. magnicalcarata, commonly known as the large-spurred orchid, is a rare and striking orchid species native to parts of Europe.

The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous habitats such as woodlands and meadows. It has a pseudobulbous structure with fleshy leaves that store water, aiding in its survival in its native environment.
