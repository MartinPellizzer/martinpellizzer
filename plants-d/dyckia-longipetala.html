Dyckia longipetala

Dyckia longipetala, commonly known as the long-petaled dyckia, is a bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of white, woolly hairs. The plant produces striking, showy inflorescences with bright red flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Dyckia longipetala is relatively drought-tolerant and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens and indoor settings.
