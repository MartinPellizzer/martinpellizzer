Dypsis tenuissima

Dypsis tenuissima, commonly known as the slender lady palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to Madagascar and is characterized by its slender, upright trunk and feathery fronds. The plant typically grows to a height of 3 to 5 meters and prefers bright, indirect light. It has a slow growth rate and is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Dypsis tenuissima is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
