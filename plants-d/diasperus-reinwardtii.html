Diasperus reinwardtii

Diasperus reinwardtii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments and prefers well-draining soil. It is popular as a houseplant due to its vibrant blooms and ease of care.

Diasperus reinwardtii is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
