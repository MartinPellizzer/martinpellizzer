Ditassa thymifolia

Ditassa thymifolia, commonly known as the lemon thyme or thyme-lemon plant, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves that emit a strong lemony scent when crushed. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its essential oils are valued for their culinary and medicinal uses.
