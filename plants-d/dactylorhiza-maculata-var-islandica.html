Dactylorhiza maculata var. islandica

Dactylorhiza maculata var. islandica, commonly known as the Island Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the British Isles.

It is a perennial herb with a rosette of fleshy, dark green leaves that store water. The plant produces tall, spike-like inflorescences bearing pale pink to white flowers with distinctive purple markings. It thrives in moist, shaded habitats such as woodland floors and coastal cliffs.

Its botanical features make it a unique and ecologically important species in its native range.
