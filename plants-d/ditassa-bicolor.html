Ditassa bicolor

Ditassa bicolor, commonly known as the rainbow orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful inflorescences that display a range of hues. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments and thrives in well-drained, nutrient-rich soil. Its flowers are often used in ornamental horticulture due to their vibrant appearance and long-lasting blooms.

Despite its beauty, it is relatively rare in cultivation and requires specific care to flourish.
