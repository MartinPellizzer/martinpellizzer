Daphne clivicola

Daphne clivicola, commonly known as the cliff daphne, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers in early spring. Its leaves are dark green and glossy, often with a waxy texture.

Daphne clivicola is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging environments.
