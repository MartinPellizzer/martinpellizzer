Dendrobium ritaeanum

Dendrobium ritaeanum, commonly known as the Riteaean orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with reddish-brown markings. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, utilizing its aerial roots to absorb moisture and nutrients from the environment. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem.

Dendrobium ritaeanum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
