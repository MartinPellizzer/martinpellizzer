Dipelta floribunda var. wenxianensis

Dipelta floribunda var. wenxianensis, commonly known as the Wenxian bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue or purple in color, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender stems and lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
