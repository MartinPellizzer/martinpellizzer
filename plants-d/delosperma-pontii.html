Delosperma pontii

Delosperma pontii, commonly known as the ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant pink or red flowers during the summer months, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It grows in a low, spreading form, often forming dense mats on rocky or sandy soils.

Delosperma pontii is drought-tolerant and requires minimal care, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
