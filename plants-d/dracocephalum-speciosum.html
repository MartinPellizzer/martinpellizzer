Dracocephalum speciosum

Dracocephalum speciosum, commonly known as purple skullcap, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a prominent central vein, and its flowers are typically blue to violet in color, forming whorled clusters at the ends of the stems. Dracocephalum speciosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported therapeutic properties.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade conditions.
