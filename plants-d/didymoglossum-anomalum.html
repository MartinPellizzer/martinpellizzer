Didymoglossum anomalum

Didymoglossum anomalum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed bog moss, is a small, mat-forming moss that thrives in wet, acidic environments such as bogs and fens.

It has slender, creeping stems with small, oval-shaped leaves that are often slightly curled at the edges.
