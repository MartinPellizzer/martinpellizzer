Dittrichia orientalis

Dittrichia orientalis, commonly known as the oriental dittany, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to dry and sunny environments.
