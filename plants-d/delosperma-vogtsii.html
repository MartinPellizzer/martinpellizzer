Delosperma vogtsii

Delosperma vogtsii, commonly known as the Vogtsii carpet flower, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems covered in dense clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping. Its compact growth habit and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental use.

Delosperma vogtsii is also known for its ability to store water in its leaves, allowing it to survive in arid conditions.
