Doratanthera linearis

Doratanthera linearis, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly in countries like Bolivia and Peru. The plant features slender, linear leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool, moist conditions.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
