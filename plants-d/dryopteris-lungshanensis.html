Dryopteris lungshanensis

Dryopteris lungshanensis, commonly known as the Lungshan shield fern, is a species native to China.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its distinctive fronds with a shield-shaped base. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky slopes. Its leaves are usually dark green and have a leathery texture, with sporangia clustered in sori on the underside.

This fern is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
