Diospyros ekodul

Diospyros ekodul, commonly known as the African persimmon, is a small to medium-sized tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Ebenaceae family, which includes other well-known species like ebony and persimmon. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically borne in clusters. Its fruits are generally small, round, and have a sweet, edible pulp when ripe.

The leaves are usually elliptical with a smooth texture and dark green color.
