Dasynema surinamense

Dasynema surinamense, commonly known as the Surinam sponge gourd, is a climbing vine belonging to the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and large, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive waxy surface. The plant produces yellow flowers followed by elongated, sponge-like fruits that are typically green when mature. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in tropical regions of South America.

Due to its unique appearance and growth habit, it is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
