Dichorisandra pubescens var. luschnathiana

Dichorisandra pubescens var. luschnathiana, commonly known as the Luschnathia lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy, giving it a distinctive textured appearance. The plant produces striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a prominent yellow throat. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in warm climates.
