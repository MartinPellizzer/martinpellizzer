Dierama mobile

Dierama mobile, commonly known as the zigzagged grass, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its distinctive zigzagging inflorescence. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, pale blue or purple flowers in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Dierama mobile is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
