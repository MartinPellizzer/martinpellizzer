Dissochaeta stellulata

Dissochaeta stellulata, commonly known as the star-leaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant has a low-growing habit with narrow, grass-like leaves arranged in a rosette. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or arid regions.
