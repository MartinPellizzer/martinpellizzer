Dillenia ingens

Dillenia ingens, commonly known as the giant dillenia, is a large tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its massive, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in diameter. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that are typically borne in clusters at the base of the tree. Its fruits are large, woody capsules that split open to release numerous seeds.

Dillenia ingens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.
