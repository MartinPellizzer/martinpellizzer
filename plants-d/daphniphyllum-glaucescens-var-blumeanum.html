Daphniphyllum glaucescens var. blumeanum

Daphniphyllum glaucescens var. blumeanum, commonly known as the Blumeanum holly, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are typically glossy and dark green with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers in clusters, followed by red berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in tropical and subtropical regions.
