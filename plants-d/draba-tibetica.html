Draba tibetica

Draba tibetica, commonly known as the Tibetan rockcress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is native to the high-altitude regions of the Tibetan Plateau and other mountainous areas in Asia. This perennial herb typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, thriving in cold and arid conditions. The plant features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, and its leaves are usually basal, forming a rosette.

Draba tibetica is adapted to withstand harsh climatic conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
