Dupatya spadicea

Dupatya spadicea, commonly known as the golden spider orchid, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, spider-like inflorescence. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with intricate, veined petals that resemble spider legs. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Dupatya spadicea is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
