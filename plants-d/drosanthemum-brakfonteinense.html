Drosanthemum brakfonteinense

Drosanthemum brakfonteinense, commonly known as the Brakfontein daisies, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, daisy-like flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant features hairy, narrow leaves and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It thrives in full sun and requires well-drained conditions, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Drosanthemum brakfonteinense is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
