Dypsis forficifolia

Dypsis forficifolia, commonly known as the sword-leaf palm, is a tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that resemble swords, giving it its common name. The plant has a slow growth rate and thrives in bright, indirect light with well-draining soil. Its foliage is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its aesthetic appeal.

Dypsis forficifolia is also valued for its ability to purify indoor air and add a touch of greenery to living spaces.
