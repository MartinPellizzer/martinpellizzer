Dendrobium × aspasia

Dendrobium × aspasia, commonly known as the aspasia orchid, is a hybrid orchid species that combines traits from various dendrobium species.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in its natural habitat. The plant features long, cane-like stems that produce clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and yellow. Its flowers are often bell-shaped or pouch-like, with intricate patterns and a strong, sweet scent.

Dendrobium × aspasia is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
