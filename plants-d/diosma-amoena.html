Diosma amoena

Diosma amoena, commonly known as the pink bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems covered in soft, hairy foliage. The plant produces clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pale pink to deep violet. These flowers bloom in early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Diosma amoena thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
