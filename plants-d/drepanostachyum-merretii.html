Drepanostachyum merretii

Drepanostachyum merretii, commonly known as the Malagasy bamboo, is a member of the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to Madagascar and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. This plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in tropical climates. It is often found in forest understories and along streambanks.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
