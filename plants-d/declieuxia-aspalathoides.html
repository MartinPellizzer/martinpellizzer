Declieuxia aspalathoides

Declieuxia aspalathoides, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features narrow, silver-gray leaves that are densely packed, giving it a compact, bushy appearance. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid climates.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
