Duranta repens var. lopez-palacii

Duranta repens var. lopez-palacii, commonly known as the Lopez-Palacii blue curl, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Mexico.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically a glossy dark green. The plant produces clusters of pendant, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. These flowers give way to small, round berries that start green and mature to a striking red or orange.

It is well-suited for container gardening and thrives in full sun to partial shade.
