Dielsiodoxa propullulans

Dielsiodoxa propullulans, commonly known as the golden star ginger, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, providing a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Dielsiodoxa propullulans is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant in tropical settings.
