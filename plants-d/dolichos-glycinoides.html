Dolichos glycinoides

Dolichos glycinoides, commonly known as the hairy nightshade, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features alternate, ovate leaves with hairy surfaces, and its flowers are usually violet or purple in color. The fruit is a capsule that contains several small, black seeds.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshade plants.
