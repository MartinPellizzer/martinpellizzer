Dendrochilum longifolium

Dendrochilum longifolium, commonly known as the long-leaved tree orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and long, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
