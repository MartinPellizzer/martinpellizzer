Dryandra pteridifolia var. blechnifolia

Dryandra pteridifolia var. blechnifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved dryandra, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It belongs to the family Proteaceae and is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to Mediterranean climates.
