Dalea cylindrica var. haenkeana

Dalea cylindrica var. haenkeana, commonly known as the slender milkvetch, is a perennial flowering plant native to the western United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant produces small, oval legumes as its fruit, which contain several seeds. It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes, and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

Its botanical features make it an important component of native plant communities in its range.
