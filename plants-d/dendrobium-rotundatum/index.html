Dendrobium Rotundatum

Dendrobium rotundatum is a rare and striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. Known for its unique rounded pseudobulbs and vibrant, long-lasting flowers, this plant is a favorite among orchid enthusiasts. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires careful attention to watering and light conditions. Its distinctive appearance makes it a valuable addition to both indoor collections and botanical gardens. Despite its beauty, Dendrobium rotundatum remains a challenging plant to cultivate, requiring specialized care to flourish.
