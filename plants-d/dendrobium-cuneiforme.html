Dendrobium cuneiforme

Dendrobium cuneiforme, commonly known as the narrow-leaf orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often arranged in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Dendrobium cuneiforme is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
