Dendranthema jeongseonense

Dendranthema jeongseonense, commonly known as Jeongseon chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to South Korea.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy yellow to orange flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and thrives in well-drained soil. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and traditional medicinal uses.

Its blooming period occurs in the autumn season, adding vibrant color to the landscape.
