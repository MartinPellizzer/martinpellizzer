Dendrobium brachyanthum

Dendrobium brachyanthum, commonly known as the broad-flowered dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with cane-like stems that can reach several feet in length. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with broad, showy petals. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Dendrobium brachyanthum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and relatively easy care requirements.
