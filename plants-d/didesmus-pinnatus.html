Didesmus pinnatus

Didesmus pinnatus, commonly known as the featherleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, feather-like leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces yellow composite flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Didesmus pinnatus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
