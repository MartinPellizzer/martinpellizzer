Desmodium teres

Desmodium teres, commonly known as the narrow-leaf burr legume, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, spherical pods. The plant is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions. Its seeds are enclosed in hairy, spiny pods that aid in seed dispersal.

Desmodium teres is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial in agricultural settings.
