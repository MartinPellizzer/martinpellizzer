Daemonorops grandis var. megacarpus

Daemonorops grandis var. megacarpus, commonly known as the giant cycad, is a species of cycad native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, feather-like leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces large, cone-like structures that contain both male and female reproductive organs. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Due to its slow growth and long lifespan, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
