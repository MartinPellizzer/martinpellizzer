Dicksonia lathamii

Dicksonia lathamii, commonly known as the Latham's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant has a distinctive crown of fronds that are often drooping and have a light green to grayish color. Dicksonia lathamii thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its rhizome is thick and fibrous, supporting its growth as a perennial plant.
