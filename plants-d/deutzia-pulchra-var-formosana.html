Deutzia pulchra var. formosana

Deutzia pulchra var. formosana, commonly known as the Taiwan deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features clusters of fragrant, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has opposite, simple, ovate leaves that are typically hairy on the underside. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscape design.
