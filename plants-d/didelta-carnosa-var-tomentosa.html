Didelta carnosa var. tomentosa

Didelta carnosa var. tomentosa, commonly known as the woolly didelta, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
