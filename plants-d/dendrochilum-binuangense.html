Dendrochilum binuangense

Dendrochilum binuangense, commonly known as the Binuang tree orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and the presence of pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, typically during the rainy season. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to thrive in moist, shaded environments.

Dendrochilum binuangense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often found in lowland forests.
