Dichapetalum reflexum

Dichapetalum reflexum, commonly known as the African waterwheel, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive whorled arrangement of leaves and small, pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, sandy soils along riverbanks and wetlands. Its unique appearance and habitat make it a notable species in its native ecosystems.

It is also valued for its ornamental qualities and traditional uses.
