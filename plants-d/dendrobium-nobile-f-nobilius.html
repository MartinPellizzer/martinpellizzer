Dendrobium nobile f. nobilius

Dendrobium nobile f. nobilius, commonly known as the noble orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, cane-like stems and clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant is an epiphyte, often growing on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. It requires bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Its flowers bloom in late winter to early spring, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
