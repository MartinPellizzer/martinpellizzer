Dioscorea militaris

Dioscorea militaris, commonly known as the military yam, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the Dioscoreaceae family and is characterized by its tuberous roots, which are often used in traditional medicine. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into capsule fruits. Its roots are rich in starch and have been used for food and medicinal purposes in various cultures.

The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires a humid climate for optimal growth.
