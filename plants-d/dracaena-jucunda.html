Dracaena jucunda

Dracaena jucunda, commonly known as the golden dracaena, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Madagascar.

It features long, narrow, dark green leaves with a striking yellow or golden margin, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by red, fleshy fruits that contain seeds. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance of low light and minimal watering requirements.

Dracaena jucunda is also valued for its air-purifying properties and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
