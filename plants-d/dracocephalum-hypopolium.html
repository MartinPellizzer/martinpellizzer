Dracocephalum hypopolium

Dracocephalum hypopolium, commonly known as narrow-leaved blueweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to regions of Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Dracocephalum hypopolium is often found in dry grasslands and rocky soils, thriving in full sun to partial shade.
