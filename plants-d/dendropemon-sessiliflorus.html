Dendropemon sessiliflorus

Dendropemon sessiliflorus, commonly known as the sessile-flower tree, is a tropical plant belonging to the Meliaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain a single seed. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some regions for its unique foliage and floral display.
