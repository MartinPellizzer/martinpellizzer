Didierea ascendens

Didierea ascendens, commonly known as the ascending didierea, is a succulent plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Cactaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, spiny stems that grow vertically. The plant features small, paddle-shaped leaves that are adapted to store water, typical of arid environments. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, emerging from the axils of the stems.

Didierea ascendens is often cultivated for its unique appearance and resilience in dry conditions.
