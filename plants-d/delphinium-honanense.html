Delphinium honanense

Delphinium honanense, commonly known as Honan larkspur, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems. The plant produces showy blue to purple flowers in late summer, which are clustered in racemes. Its leaves are palmately divided, with a rough texture and a strong, often unpleasant odor.

Delphinium honanense is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and is often cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
