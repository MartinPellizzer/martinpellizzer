Dyckia altoana

Dyckia altoana, commonly known as the highland dyckia, is a species of bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color, often with white or silver markings. The plant produces striking inflorescences that can reach several feet in height, featuring clusters of small, spathaceous flowers. Dyckia altoana is adapted to grow in rocky or sandy soils, often in humid, high-altitude environments.

It is a popular ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and ability to thrive in well-drained conditions.
