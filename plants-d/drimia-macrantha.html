Drimia macrantha

Drimia macrantha, commonly known as the giant lily or African lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy rhizomes and produces tall, erect stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The plant is characterized by large, showy white or pale purple flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are broad, strap-like, and often have a waxy texture.

Drimia macrantha is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
