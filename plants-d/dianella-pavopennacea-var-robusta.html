Dianella pavopennacea var. robusta

Dianella pavopennacea var. robusta, commonly known as the broad-leaved flax lily, is a perennial evergreen plant native to New Zealand.

It features lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are broad and robust, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale blue flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its graceful foliage and delicate floral display.
