Duparquetia orchidacea

Duparquetia orchidacea, commonly known as the orchidaceous duparquetia, is a species of flowering plant in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. Its leaves are elliptical and glossy, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Duparquetia orchidacea is valued in horticulture for its beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
