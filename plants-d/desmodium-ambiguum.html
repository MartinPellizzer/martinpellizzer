Desmodium ambiguum

Desmodium ambiguum, commonly known as the sicklepod, is a leguminous plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant produces elongated, twisted pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

Desmodium ambiguum is often found in disturbed areas and can be invasive in some regions.
