Dalenia pulchra

Dalenia pulchra, commonly known as the pretty dalenia, is a striking South African plant belonging to the family Malvaceae.

It is characterized by its showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, giving it a distinctive, fern-like appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Dalenia pulchra is prized for its ornamental value and is cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
