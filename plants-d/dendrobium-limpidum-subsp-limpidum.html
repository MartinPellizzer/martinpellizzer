Dendrobium limpidum subsp. limpidum

Dendrobium limpidum subsp.

limpidum, commonly known as the transparent dendrobium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its slender, terete pseudobulbs and elongated, lanceolate leaves.

The plant produces inflorescences bearing numerous small, white to pale yellow flowers with a subtle fragrance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and delicate blooms, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
