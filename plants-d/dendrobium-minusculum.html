Dendrobium minusculum

Dendrobium minusculum, commonly known as the miniature dendrobium, is a small epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features narrow, clustered pseudobulbs and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media. It is prized for its compact size and ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor gardens.

Dendrobium minusculum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
