Digitaria elegantula

Digitaria elegantula, commonly known as the slender digit grass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields. The plant has slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. Its inflorescence consists of small, spike-like clusters of flowers.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
