Drimys brasiliensis subsp. brasiliensis

Drimys brasiliensis subsp.

brasiliensis, commonly known as the Brazilian evergreen drimys, is a species native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil. It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub, typically reaching heights of up to 10 meters.

The plant features dark green, leathery leaves that are elliptic to ovate in shape. It produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by red, fleshy berries.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
