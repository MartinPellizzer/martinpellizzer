Dendrocolla pugionifolia

Dendrocolla pugionifolia, commonly known as the swordleaf tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, sword-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along its branches. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
