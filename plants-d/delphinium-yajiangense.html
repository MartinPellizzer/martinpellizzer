Delphinium yajiangense

Delphinium yajiangense, commonly known as the Yajiang larkspur, is a species of flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems and showy blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky habitats, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
