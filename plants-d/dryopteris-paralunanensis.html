Dryopteris paralunanensis

Dryopteris paralunanensis, commonly known as the Philippine shield fern, is a species of polypod fern native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are thick and creeping, allowing it to spread and form dense colonies.

The fronds are evergreen, with a leathery texture and a deep green color that provides ornamental value in gardens.
