Dalbergia hancei

Dalbergia hancei, commonly known as Chinese rosewood, is a species of legume in the Fabaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including China, Vietnam, and Laos. The plant is characterized by its dark, dense wood, which is highly valued for its beauty and durability. It features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellowish flowers.

The tree can grow up to 20 meters tall and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
