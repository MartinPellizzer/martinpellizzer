Delosperma fredericii

Delosperma fredericii, commonly known as the Cape Hottentot, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps it store water and survive in arid conditions. The plant produces vibrant pink to purple flowers during the summer months, adding ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it ideal for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
