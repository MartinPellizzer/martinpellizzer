Dictyostega campanulata

Dictyostega campanulata, commonly known as the bell-shaped dictyostega, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Campanulaceae, which includes bellflowers, and is characterized by its bell-shaped, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with a smooth margin, while its flowers are usually blue or purple in color.

The species is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive blooms.
