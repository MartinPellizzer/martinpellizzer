Dillenia bolsteri

Dillenia bolsteri, commonly known as the Bolster's dillenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Dilleniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, large, and showy flowers that often have a yellow or white coloration. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub or small tree, with woody stems and lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually borne in clusters and are known for their unique structure, featuring a central column with surrounding petals.

Dillenia bolsteri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its striking floral display.
