Disa tenella

Disa tenella, commonly known as the dwarf orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It is characterized by its short, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves.

Disa tenella is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a garden setting.
