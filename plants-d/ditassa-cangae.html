Ditassa cangae

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant has hairy, narrow leaves and thrives in cool, moist environments. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered endangered.
