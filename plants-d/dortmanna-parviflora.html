Dortmanna parviflora

Dortmanna parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered Dortmanna, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slight pubescence.

Dortmanna parviflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
