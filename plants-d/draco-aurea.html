Draco aurea

Draco aurea, commonly known as the golden drake, is a striking succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically yellow to orange in color, giving it its common name. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers during the warmer months, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
