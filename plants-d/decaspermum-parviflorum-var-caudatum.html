Decaspermum parviflorum var. caudatum

Decaspermum parviflorum var. caudatum, commonly known as the slender decaspermum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a small shrub characterized by its narrow, ascending stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers with five petals, which bloom in spring. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in fine hairs.

It is adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.
