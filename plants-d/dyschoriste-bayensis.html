Dyschoriste bayensis

Dyschoriste bayensis, commonly known as the Bay Islands dyschoriste, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its small, delicate leaves and clusters of white to pale yellow flowers. The plant thrives in sandy soils and is often found in mangrove ecosystems. It plays a role in stabilizing coastal habitats and providing habitat for various small organisms.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
