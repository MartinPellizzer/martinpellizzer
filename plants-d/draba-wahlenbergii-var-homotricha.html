Draba wahlenbergii var. homotricha

Draba wahlenbergii var. homotricha, commonly known as the Wahlenberg's rockcress, is a small, flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, cross-shaped flowers and capsule fruits. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, where it thrives in well-drained, nutrient-poor soils.
