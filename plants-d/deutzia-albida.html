Deutzia albida

Deutzia albida, commonly known as the white deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, arching branches and dark green, ovate leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Deutzia albida is valued for its ornamental beauty and ease of cultivation.
