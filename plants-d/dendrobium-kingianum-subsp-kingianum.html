Dendrobium kingianum subsp. kingianum

Dendrobium kingianum subsp.

kingianum, commonly known as the King's Dendrobium, is a sympodial epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia. It features long, cane-like stems that can grow up to several meters in height, often branching into clusters of pseudo-bulbs.

The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange, typically during the summer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining, airy substrates for optimal growth.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
