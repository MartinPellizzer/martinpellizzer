Drimia media

Drimia media, commonly known as the African lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features strap-shaped, dark green leaves that are often used in floral arrangements. The plant produces striking white or pale pink flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Drimia media is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
