Dioscorea hypotricha

Dioscorea hypotricha, commonly known as the African yam bean, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Dioscoreaceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by tuberous roots that are often used as food. Its tubers are an important source of carbohydrates in some regions.

The species is also valued for its potential medicinal properties.
