Drosanthemum salicola

Drosanthemum salicola, commonly known as the narrowleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces yellow flowers with a central disc surrounded by ray florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Drosanthemum salicola is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its bright, cheerful blooms.
