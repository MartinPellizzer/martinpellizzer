Dolichos urens

Dolichos urens, commonly known as the slender urd bean, is a flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, trifoliate leaves. The plant produces yellowish-white flowers that are followed by elongated, cylindrical pods containing several seeds. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions and is often found in disturbed soils.

Dolichos urens is used in traditional medicine and as a source of protein in some parts of Asia.
