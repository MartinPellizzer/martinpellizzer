Delissea niihauensis

Delissea niihauensis, commonly known as Niihau dandelion, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and leafy stems. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and has small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are usually narrow and lance-shaped, with a hairy texture.
