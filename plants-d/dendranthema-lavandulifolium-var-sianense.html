Dendranthema lavandulifolium var. sianense

Dendranthema lavandulifolium var. sianense, commonly known as the Sianense Chamomile, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center. The plant has aromatic leaves that emit a mild, sweet scent, similar to chamomile. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in mountainous regions.

Its botanical features make it valuable for both ornamental gardening and traditional medicinal uses.
