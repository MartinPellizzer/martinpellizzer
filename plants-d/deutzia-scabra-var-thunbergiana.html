Deutzia scabra var. thunbergiana

Deutzia scabra var. thunbergiana, commonly known as Thunberg's smooth deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It features slender, arching stems and clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptic, with a smooth texture and a slightly serrated margin. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for gardens and woodland settings.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
