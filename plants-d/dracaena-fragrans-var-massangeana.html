Dracaena fragrans var. massangeana

Dracaena fragrans var. massangeana, commonly known as the Corn Plant, is a popular indoor plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features tall, upright stems with broad, dark green, strap-like leaves that have a glossy texture. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires moderate watering to prevent root rot. It also has a unique ability to purify the air by removing toxins.

Its slow growth rate makes it ideal for indoor spaces where space is limited.
