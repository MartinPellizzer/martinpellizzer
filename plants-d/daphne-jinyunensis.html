Daphne jinyunensis

Daphne jinyunensis, commonly known as the Jinyun daphne, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the family Thymelaeaceae and is characterized by its aromatic, glandular leaves and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and prefers well-drained, acidic soils. It blooms in late spring, producing pink to white flowers that attract pollinators.
