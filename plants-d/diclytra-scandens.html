Diclytra scandens

Diclytra scandens, commonly known as the climbing grasshopper plant, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems that often twine around other vegetation. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often covered with fine hairs.

Diclytra scandens is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks.
