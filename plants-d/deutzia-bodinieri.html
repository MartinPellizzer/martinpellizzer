Deutzia bodinieri

Deutzia bodinieri, commonly known as the Chinese deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy, fragrant, white to pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has narrow, dark green leaves and produces small, red berries in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun.

Deutzia bodinieri is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
