Draco kochiana

Draco kochiana, commonly known as the dragon tree, is a unique species native to the island of Socotra.

It is characterized by its distinctive red, waxy, and flammable resin, which it secretes from its bark. The plant has a thick, gnarled trunk and is known for its ability to survive in arid conditions. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, and it produces small, yellowish flowers.

The plant is also notable for its unusual seed pods that resemble miniature dragons, contributing to its mythical reputation.
