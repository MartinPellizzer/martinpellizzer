Deguelia sinuata

Deguelia sinuata, commonly known as the pink shower tree, is a tropical flowering plant native to the rainforests of northern Australia.

It is characterized by its striking pink or red flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling a shower of blossoms. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, serrated leaflets, and its growth habit is typically shrub-like or small tree-like. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland and coastal areas.

Deguelia sinuata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
