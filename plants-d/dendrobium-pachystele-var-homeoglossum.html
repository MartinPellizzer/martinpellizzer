Dendrobium pachystele var. homeoglossum

Dendrobium pachystele var. homeoglossum, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic orchid that grows on trees and rocks, relying on atmospheric moisture for nourishment. The plant features long, slender pseudobulbs covered in a layer of velvety, dark green foliage. Its flowers are typically yellow or golden in color, with a distinctive, elongated lip that enhances its ornamental appeal.

This variety is prized for its beauty and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and greenhouses.
