Diplazium crassiusculum f. simplex

Diplazium crassiusculum f. simplex, commonly known as the simple sword fern, is a variant of the broad-leafed sword fern.

It is a fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae, characterized by its simple, unbranched fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests and rocky areas. Its fronds are dark green and have a leathery texture, with a distinctive, sword-like shape.

This fern is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded conditions.
