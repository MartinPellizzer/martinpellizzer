Dendrobium mekynosepalum

Dendrobium mekynosepalum, commonly known as the Mekynosepalum orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in tropical rainforests. The plant features long, cane-like stems that are often covered in a dense layer of white or pale green hair. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with a range of colors including white, yellow, and pink.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
