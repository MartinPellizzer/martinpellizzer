Dieffenbachia velutina

Dieffenbachia velutina, commonly known as the velvet dieffenbachia, is a tropical houseplant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and striking white and green variegation. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other popular houseplants like peace lilies and calla lilies. Its leaves are typically elongated and narrow, with a waxy surface that helps reduce water loss.

Dieffenbachia velutina thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity to maintain its vibrant appearance.
