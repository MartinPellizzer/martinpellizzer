Dennstaedtia delicata

Dennstaedtia delicata, commonly known as the delicate fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Dennstaedtiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically arching or drooping in appearance. This plant is native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere, including parts of North America and Asia. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks.

The plant's rhizomes are often covered in a dense layer of rhizome scales, which contribute to its unique botanical structure.
