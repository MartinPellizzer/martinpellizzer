Dombeya costulatinervia

Dombeya costulatinervia, commonly known as the African dombeya, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in woodland and savanna habitats. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink, which bloom in spring or early summer. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a distinctive, finely serrated edge.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
