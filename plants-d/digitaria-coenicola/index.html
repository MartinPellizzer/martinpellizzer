Digitaria Coenicola

Digitaria coenicola is a resilient grass species commonly found in tropical and subtropical regions. It thrives in a variety of soil types and is often seen in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields. This plant is known for its ability to spread quickly, making it both a valuable forage crop and a potential weed. Its dense growth habit provides habitat for small animals and insects. Despite its benefits, it can be challenging to control in agricultural settings.
