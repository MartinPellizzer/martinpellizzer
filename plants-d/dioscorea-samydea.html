Dioscorea samydea

Dioscorea samydea, commonly known as the African yambean, is a climbing vine belonging to the Dioscoreaceae family.

It is native to tropical Africa and is characterized by its hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by tuberous roots that are often harvested for their nutritional value. Its tubers are rich in starch and have been used traditionally as a food source.

Dioscorea samydea also has medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
