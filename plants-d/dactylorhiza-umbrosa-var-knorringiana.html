Dactylorhiza umbrosa var. knorringiana

Dactylorhiza umbrosa var. knorringiana, commonly known as the shadow orchid, is a species of orchid native to parts of Europe and northern Africa.

It is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves and produces tall, slender stems bearing clusters of violet or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and heathlands. It is a terrestrial orchid with a complex root system that helps it absorb nutrients from the soil.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and protected species in many regions.
