Deyeuxia kokonorica

Deyeuxia kokonorica, commonly known as the Qinghai grass, is a perennial grass species native to the Qinghai-Tibet Plateau.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets. It thrives in cold, high-altitude environments and is adapted to the harsh climatic conditions of its native region.

Deyeuxia kokonorica plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity in alpine ecosystems.
