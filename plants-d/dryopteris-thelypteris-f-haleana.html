Dryopteris thelypteris f. haleana

Dryopteris thelypteris f. haleana, commonly known as Hale's shield fern, is a fern species characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is native to the Pacific Northwest of the United States. The fronds are typically dark green, leathery, and have a serrated margin. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

This fern is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in forest ecosystems.
