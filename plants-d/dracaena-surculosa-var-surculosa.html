Dracaena surculosa var. surculosa

Dracaena surculosa var. surculosa, commonly known as the dragon tree or red dragon tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, lance-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with red veins. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers followed by red, fleshy fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dracaena surculosa var. surculosa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying properties.
