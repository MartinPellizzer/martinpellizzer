Dioscorea tamoidea var. lindenii

Dioscorea tamoidea var. lindenii, commonly known as the African yam bean, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its tuberous roots, which are the primary edible part. The plant produces small, green, heart-shaped leaves and yellowish flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing several seeds. Its tubers are rich in starch and have been used as a food source by indigenous communities.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for both culinary and horticultural purposes.
