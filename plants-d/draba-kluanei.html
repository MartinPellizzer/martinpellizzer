Draba kluanei

Draba kluanei, commonly known as Kluane rockcress, is a flowering plant native to the high alpine regions of western Canada and Alaska.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, cruciform flowers and pungent odor. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and is adapted to harsh, cold environments. It produces small, rounded leaves and has a compact growth habit.

Draba kluanei is an important component of alpine ecosystems, contributing to biodiversity in its native habitat.
