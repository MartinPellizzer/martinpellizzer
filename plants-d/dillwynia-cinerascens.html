Dillwynia cinerascens

Dillwynia cinerascens, commonly known as the grey dillwynia, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is a small, perennial shrub that typically grows in dry, sandy soils. The plant produces clusters of pale yellow to cream flowers during the spring and summer seasons. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in a fine, powdery coating.

Dillwynia cinerascens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
