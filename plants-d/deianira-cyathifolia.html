Deianira cyathifolia

Deianira cyathifolia, commonly known as the mountain hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and violet. The plant typically grows as an erect shrub with hairy stems and opposite, ovate leaves. Deianira cyathifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
