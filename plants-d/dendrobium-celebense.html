Dendrobium celebense

Dendrobium celebense, commonly known as the Celebes orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Indonesian island of Celebes.

It features a sympodial growth habit with elongated, fleshy pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with intricate patterns. Its leaves are typically linear and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Dendrobium celebense thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
