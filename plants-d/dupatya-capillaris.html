Dupatya capillaris

Dupatya capillaris, commonly known as the slender leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, narrow leaves and small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pale pink. The plant prefers shaded, humid environments and is often found in tropical forests. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil and leaf litter.

Dupatya capillaris is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
