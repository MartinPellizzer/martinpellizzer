Delphinium stapeliosmum var. shanicum

Delphinium stapeliosmum var. shanicum, commonly known as the Shanicum Larkspur, is a striking perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, spindly stems that bear clusters of striking blue to purple flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is often cultivated by gardeners interested in hardy, showy plants.
