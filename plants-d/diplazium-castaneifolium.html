Diplazium castaneifolium

Diplazium castaneifolium, commonly known as the chestnut-leaf sword fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It features a rhizomatous growth habit with dense, dark green, lanceolate leaves that have a leathery texture. The fronds are typically bipinnate, with small, oval-shaped pinnae that give the plant a lush, feathery appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical forests.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
