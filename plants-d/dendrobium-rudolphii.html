Dendrobium rudolphii

Dendrobium rudolphii, commonly known as the African orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Africa.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a distinctive red lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrobium rudolphii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
