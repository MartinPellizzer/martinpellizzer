Dendrobium cyperifolium

Dendrobium cyperifolium, commonly known as the grass-leaf orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, grass-like leaves and clusters of small, pale yellow to greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the air, and it thrives in warm, tropical climates.

Dendrobium cyperifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
