Dichrostachys glomerata subsp. nyassana

Dichrostachys glomerata subsp.

nyassana, commonly known as African blush or red bush willow, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa. It is characterized by its reddish-brown bark and small, clustered yellow flowers.

The plant has elliptical leaves that are usually opposite or alternate in arrangement. It is often found in dry savannas and woodland areas, thriving in well-drained soils. Dichrostachys glomerata subsp.
