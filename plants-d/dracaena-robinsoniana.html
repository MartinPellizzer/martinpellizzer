Dracaena robinsoniana

Dracaena robinsoniana, commonly known as the Robinson's dracaena, is a species of flowering plant in the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to Madagascar and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters at the base of the leaves. Dracaena robinsoniana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its elegant appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.

It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
