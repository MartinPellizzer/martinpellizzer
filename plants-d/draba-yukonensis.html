Draba yukonensis

Draba yukonensis, commonly known as Yukon Draba, is a small flowering plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions of North America.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to cold, harsh environments. It produces small, white or yellow flowers that bloom in early spring.

Draba yukonensis is often found in alpine tundra and is an important part of the local flora in its native habitat.
