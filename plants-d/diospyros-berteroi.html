Diospyros berteroi

Diospyros berteroi, commonly known as the African persimmon, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Ebenaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The tree produces small, orange-red fruits that are edible when fully mature. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

The plant has cultural and economic significance in some African regions.
