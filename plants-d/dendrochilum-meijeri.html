Dendrochilum meijeri

Dendrochilum meijeri, commonly known as the Meijer's tree orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, tree-like growth habit with a central stem and multiple branching pseudobulbs. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with intricate patterns on the petals. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in its natural habitat.

Dendrochilum meijeri thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media for optimal growth.
