Dendrobium chameleon f. alba

Dendrobium chameleon f. alba, commonly known as the white chameleon orchid, is a rare and striking epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

This hybrid variety is characterized by its unique, color-changing flowers that shift hues depending on light and environmental conditions. The plant features long, cane-like stems covered in dense clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media to prevent root rot.

Due to its rarity and horticultural appeal, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts.
