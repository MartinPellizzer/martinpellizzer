Desmodium multiflorum

Desmodium multiflorum, commonly known as the many-flowered desmodium, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and clusters of small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces cylindrical, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Desmodium multiflorum is also valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, which benefits soil fertility.
