Distictis pulverulenta

Distictis pulverulenta, commonly known as the dustflower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, pale yellow to white flowers that bloom in summer. It is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid conditions.

Distictis pulverulenta is often used in rock gardens due to its low maintenance and ornamental value.
