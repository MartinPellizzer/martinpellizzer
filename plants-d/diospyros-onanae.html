Diospyros onanae

Diospyros onanae, commonly known as the Maluku persimmon, is a species of flowering plant in the Ebenaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Indonesia, particularly the Maluku Islands. The plant is a small to medium-sized tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height, with smooth, greyish-brown bark. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptic in shape, with a glossy surface.

The fruit is a small, round berry that turns from green to orange-red when mature, containing a single seed.
