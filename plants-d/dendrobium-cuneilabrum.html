Dendrobium cuneilabrum

Dendrobium cuneilabrum, commonly known as the narrow-labellum orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a tall, upright stem with numerous lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Dendrobium cuneilabrum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
