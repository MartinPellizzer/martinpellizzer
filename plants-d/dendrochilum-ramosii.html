Dendrochilum ramosii

Dendrochilum ramosii, commonly known as the branched orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, branching growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with intricate patterns on the petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrochilum ramosii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
