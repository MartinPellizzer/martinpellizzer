Dioscorea orbiculata

Dioscorea orbiculata, commonly known as the African yam bean, is a climbing vine belonging to the family Dioscoreaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and yellowish flowers. The plant produces tuberous roots that are used as a food source in some African countries. Its growth habit involves twining around supports, and it thrives in warm, humid climates.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental qualities and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
