Dovyalis celastroides

Dovyalis celastroides, commonly known as the sour plum or wild mango, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific.

The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and is often found in disturbed soils and along riverbanks.
