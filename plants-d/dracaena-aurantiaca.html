Dracaena aurantiaca

Dracaena aurantiaca, commonly known as the Orange Dracaena, is a striking evergreen plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It features long, narrow, dark green leaves with a distinctive orange or reddish tint along the midrib, giving it its unique appearance. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires moderate watering to prevent root rot. It is often used as a decorative houseplant due to its elegant form and air-purifying properties.

Dracaena aurantiaca is also valued for its resilience and adaptability to various indoor environments.
