Delonix decaryi

Delonix decaryi, commonly known as the flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery orange-red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has pinnate leaves composed of numerous small, oval-shaped leaflets. Its bark is smooth and grayish, becoming fissured with age.

The tree is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to tropical and subtropical climates.
