Dobrowskya eckloniana

Dobrowskya eckloniana, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy yellow to orange ray florets and dark purple central disk florets. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
