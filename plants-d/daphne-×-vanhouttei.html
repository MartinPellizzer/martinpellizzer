Daphne × vanhouttei

Daphne × vanhouttei, commonly known as the winter daphne, is a deciduous shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features aromatic, fragrant flowers that range from white to pink and are produced in clusters during late winter to early spring. The plant has smooth, grey-green bark and lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Due to its toxicity, care should be taken when handling the plant.
