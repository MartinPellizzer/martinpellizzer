Dichorisandra ulei

Dichorisandra ulei, commonly known as the blue star lily, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue-violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has strap-shaped, dark green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight.

Dichorisandra ulei is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
