Dicraeia ledermannii

Dicraeia ledermannii, commonly known as the Ledermann's tree fern, is a distinctive member of the Cyatheaceae family.

It is native to the tropical rainforests of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features a tall, unbranched trunk covered in a thick, fibrous layer of old leaf bases. Its fronds are large, feathery, and arranged in a spiral pattern around the trunk.

Dicraeia ledermannii is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in the ecological structure of its native habitat.
