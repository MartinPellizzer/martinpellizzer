Dichanthium Annulatum

Dichanthium annulatum is a hardy grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions. It is commonly found in open woodlands and grasslands where it thrives in well-drained soils. This plant is known for its ability to withstand drought and poor soil conditions. Its cylindrical seed heads and fine leaves make it easily recognizable. It plays an important role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife.
