Dioscorea subcalva var. submollis

Dioscorea subcalva var. submollis, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed yam, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Dioscoreaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, cylindrical stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by tuberous roots that are used in traditional medicine and as a food source.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and agricultural contexts.
