Dugandiodendron argyrothrichum

Dugandiodendron argyrothrichum, commonly known as the silver-haired tree, is a member of the Malvaceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its lanceolate leaves that are covered in a dense layer of silvery-white hairs, giving it its common name. The bark and leaves of this plant have been traditionally used in folk medicine for their purported therapeutic properties.
