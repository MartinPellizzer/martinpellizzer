Dendrobium sanderae

Dendrobium sanderae, commonly known as the sanderae orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on the surface of trees or rocks without deriving nutrients from them. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in humid, tropical environments with bright, indirect light.

Its unique floral structure and vibrant colors make it a popular choice for horticultural enthusiasts.
