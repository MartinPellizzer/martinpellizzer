Diphaca

Diphaca, commonly known as the African daisy, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to southern Africa and consists of several species, including Diphaca lehmannii and Diphaca capensis. Diphaca species are valued for their ornamental beauty and are sometimes cultivated in gardens.
