Delissea angustifolia var. racemosa

Delissea angustifolia var. racemosa, commonly known as the slender leaf delissea, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its botanical features include a creeping or ascending stem and elliptical leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

It is often found in regions with high humidity and well-drained soils.
