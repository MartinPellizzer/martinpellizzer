Daemonorops spectabilis

Daemonorops spectabilis, commonly known as the giant cycad or the giant pandan, is a member of the Arecaceae family.

It is a large, slow-growing cycad that can reach heights of up to 10 meters. The plant features a stout, unbranched trunk with a fibrous, spongy interior. Its leaves are pinnate, with long, narrow leaflets that are often used in traditional crafts.

The plant is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia and is valued for its ornamental and cultural significance.
