Dryopteris Madagascariensis

Dryopteris madagascariensis is a rare fern species native to the island of Madagascar. It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is known for its unique frond structure and vibrant green color. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered an endangered species facing habitat loss. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this remarkable botanical treasure.
