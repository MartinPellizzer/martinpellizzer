Dacrydium elatum var. compactum

Dacrydium elatum var. compactum, commonly known as the compact silver pine, is a compact, evergreen conifer native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-green foliage and short, twisted shoots that give it a distinctive, shrub-like appearance. The plant produces small, spherical cones and is well adapted to the cool, humid climates of its native habitat. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive form and hardy nature.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological and horticultural studies.
