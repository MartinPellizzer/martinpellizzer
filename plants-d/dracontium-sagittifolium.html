Dracontium sagittifolium

Dracontium sagittifolium, commonly known as the arrowleaf dracontium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. It typically thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
