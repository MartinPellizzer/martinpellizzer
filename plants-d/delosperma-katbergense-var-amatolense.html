Delosperma katbergense var. amatolense

Delosperma katbergense var. amatolense, commonly known as the Katberg iceplant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white powder, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink and purple during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and arid landscapes.

This variety is particularly adapted to withstand hot, dry conditions, showcasing its resilience as a xerophyte.
