Dianthus blandus

Dianthus blandus, commonly known as the purple campion, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to deep purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Dianthus blandus is a perennial herb that blooms from late spring to early summer.
