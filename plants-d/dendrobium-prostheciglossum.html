Dendrobium prostheciglossum

Dendrobium prostheciglossum, commonly known as the golden spike orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, slender pseudobulbs and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a green throat. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
