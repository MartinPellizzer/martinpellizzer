Dahlia excelsa var. anemoniflora

Dahlia excelsa var. anemoniflora, commonly known as the Anemone-flowered Dahlia, is a striking hybrid of the genus Dahlia native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and large, showy flowers that resemble anemones, with petal-like bracts surrounding the central disk. The plant typically produces vibrant blooms in shades of red, pink, and white, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, requiring regular watering and support due to its height.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique, eye-catching floral display.
