Delphinium szechuanicum

Delphinium szechuanicum, commonly known as the Sichuan larkspur, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces showy, blue to purple flowers in clusters at the top of the stem, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are palmately divided, with a rough texture and a distinctive aromatic scent.

Delphinium szechuanicum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
