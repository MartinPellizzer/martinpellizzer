Dracaena masoniana

Dracaena masoniana, commonly known as the Mason's dracaena, is a tropical plant native to the island of Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and elongated, narrow leaves that are typically dark green with a slight variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy fruits. Dracaena masoniana is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.
