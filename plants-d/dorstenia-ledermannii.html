Dorstenia ledermannii

Dorstenia ledermannii, commonly known as the Ledermann's zigzag plant, is a member of the family Malvaceae and is native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, spiraled inflorescence and fleshy, leaf-like bracts that are often mistaken for leaves. Dorstenia ledermannii is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
