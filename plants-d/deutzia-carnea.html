Deutzia carnea

Deutzia carnea, commonly known as the pink deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, arching stems and lance-shaped leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Deutzia carnea is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ease of care.
