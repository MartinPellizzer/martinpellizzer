Dendrolirium micranthum

Dendrolirium micranthum, commonly known as the small-flowered tree lily, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant features a rhizomatous root system and produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Dendrolirium micranthum is valued in horticulture for its elegant appearance and ability to thrive in partially shaded conditions.
