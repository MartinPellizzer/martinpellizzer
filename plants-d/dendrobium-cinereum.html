Dendrobium cinereum

Dendrobium cinereum, commonly known as the silver-lipped dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with cane-like stems that can become quite long. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged spirally along the stems.

Dendrobium cinereum thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.
