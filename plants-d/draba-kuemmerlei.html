Draba kuemmerlei

Draba kuemmerlei, commonly known as the Kummer's rockcress, is a low-growing perennial plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact rosette of narrow, linear leaves and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers in early spring. Native to rocky and alpine habitats in Central Asia, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. The plant is adapted to harsh environments, with a short growing season and frost-resistant foliage.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after species for rock gardens and alpine collections.
