Drimia virens

Drimia virens, commonly known as the green bulbous plant, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a bulbous base with long, narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically green to bluish-green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Drimia virens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care.
