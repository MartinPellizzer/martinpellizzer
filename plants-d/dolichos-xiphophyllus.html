Dolichos xiphophyllus

Dolichos xiphophyllus, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical legume native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, pendulous inflorescences that bear numerous yellow, spurred flowers, which bloom throughout the year. The plant has compound leaves with small, ovate leaflets and produces elongated pods containing edible seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of protein-rich seeds.

The species is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
