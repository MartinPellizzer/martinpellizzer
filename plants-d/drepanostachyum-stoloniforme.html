Drepanostachyum stoloniforme

Drepanostachyum stoloniforme, commonly known as the stoloniferous bamboo, is a member of the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit, forming dense clumps through horizontal stolons. The plant produces panicles of small, spike-like inflorescences, typically with pale green to golden bracts. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue and a hairy surface.

This species is native to Southeast Asia and is often used in ornamental horticulture for its attractive appearance and hardiness.
