Dupatya fertilis

Dupatya fertilis, commonly known as the golden leaf fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its lush, dark green fronds that often have a golden hue along the edges. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture to flourish. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense clusters.

Dupatya fertilis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and air-purifying properties.
