Diasperus tenuicaulis

Diasperus tenuicaulis, commonly known as the slender-stemmed Diasperus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features narrow, cylindrical stems and produces small, pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters. Its leaves are linear and often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its drought-resistant nature.

It is often found in grassland or shrubland habitats, where it thrives in full sun.
