Dendrobium annae

Dendrobium annae, commonly known as the Annas orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Dendrobium genus, which includes over 1,200 species of orchids, many of which are prized for their ornamental value. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, forming clusters of pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow with red or purple markings, and they bloom in summer.

Dendrobium annae is known for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
