Dolichos schweinfurthii

Dolichos schweinfurthii, commonly known as the African horsebean, is a leguminous plant native to sub-Saharan Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit, with trifoliate leaves and small, pod-like fruits. The plant produces yellow flowers and is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for agricultural purposes.

It is often used as a forage crop and has traditional medicinal uses in some African communities.
