Dyckia atratiflora

Dyckia atratiflora, commonly known as the dark-flowered dyckia, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, narrow leaves that form a rosette, often with a dark green to almost black coloration. The plant produces striking purple or dark red flowers that emerge from the center of the rosette. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-draining soil.

Dyckia atratiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
