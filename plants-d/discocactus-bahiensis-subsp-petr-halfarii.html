Discocactus bahiensis subsp. petr-halfarii

Discocactus bahiensis subsp.

petr-halfarii, commonly known as the Petr-Halfari discocactus, is a rare and striking cactus native to Brazil. It is characterized by its tall, columnar stems that often branch into multiple stems, forming a dense, bushy appearance.

The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are modified into spines, which provide protection from herbivores and reduce water loss.

This subspecies is highly valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and adaptability to arid environments.
