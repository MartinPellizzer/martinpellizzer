Deschampsia Orientalis

Deschampsia grass, scientifically known as Deschampsia orientalis, is a hardy perennial grass native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia. It thrives in a variety of soil types and is often found in meadows, woodlands, and along stream banks. With its fine, tufted blades and silver-green foliage, it adds a delicate texture to natural landscapes. This versatile plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife. Its resilience and adaptability make it a popular choice for ecological restoration projects.
