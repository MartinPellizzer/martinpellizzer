Dendrobium convexum

Dendrobium convexum, commonly known as the convex orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, cane-like stems that are often covered in a thick, waxy coating. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with a distinctive convex shape on the outer petals. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Dendrobium convexum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
