Dianthus balbisii var. algarbiensis

Dianthus balbisii var. algarbiensis, commonly known as the Algarbiensis campion, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant pink or red flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are clustered in dense, showy inflorescences.

It is often found in coastal and mountainous regions, where it adapts well to harsh environmental conditions.
