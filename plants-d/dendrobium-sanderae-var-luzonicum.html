Dendrobium sanderae var. luzonicum

Dendrobium sanderae var. luzonicum, commonly known as the Luzon orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with dark markings. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, adapted to the humid, shaded environments of its native habitat.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
