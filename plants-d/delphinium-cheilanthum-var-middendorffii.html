Delphinium cheilanthum var. middendorffii

Delphinium cheilanthum var. middendorffii, commonly known as the Middendorff's larkspur, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters. The plant produces striking blue to purple flowers in raceme-like clusters, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a distinctive blue-green color with a waxy coating.

It prefers well-drained soils and full sun, making it a striking addition to alpine gardens.
