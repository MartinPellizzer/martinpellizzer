Dregea Africana

Dregea africana is a striking flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is known for its tall, woody stems and showy, pale yellow to white flowers. This species thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it well adapted to harsh climates. Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively unknown outside of its native region.
