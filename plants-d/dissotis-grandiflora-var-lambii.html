Dissotis grandiflora var. lambii

Dissotis grandiflora var. lambii, commonly known as the Giant Flame Plant, is a tropical perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, elongated leaves with a deep green color and a waxy texture. The plant produces showy, red-orange inflorescences that resemble flames, which are clustered in terminal spikes. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil.

This ornamental plant is popular in horticulture for its dramatic foliage and vibrant floral displays.
