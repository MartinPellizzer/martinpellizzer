Dendrobium funiushanense

Dendrobium funiushanense, commonly known as the Funiushan orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its erect, branched pseudobulbs and long, linear leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green to white flowers with a distinctive red lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments at high elevations, typically in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
