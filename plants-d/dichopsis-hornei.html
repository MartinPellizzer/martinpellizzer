Dichopsis hornei

Dichopsis hornei, commonly known as the Horne's dichopsis, is a unique succulent native to southern Africa.

It features a distinctive, branching stem with small, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a striking visual element to its appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its slow growth and ornamental value, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
