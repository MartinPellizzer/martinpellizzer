Dolomiaea macrocephala

Dolomiaea macrocephala, commonly known as the giant rock lily, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers with a distinctive yellow center, which bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage consists of broad, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating.

Dolomiaea macrocephala is a perennial herb that is well adapted to arid environments and is often found in mountainous regions.
