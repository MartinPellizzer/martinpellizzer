Dontospermum canariense

It belongs to the family Viscaraceae and is characterized by its small, leathery leaves and white, fleshy berries. The plant attaches itself to host trees, primarily laurel species, through its haustoria, drawing water and nutrients from the host. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species.
