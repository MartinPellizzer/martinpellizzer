Deutzia staminea var. brunoniana

Deutzia staminea var. brunoniana, commonly known as Brunoniana Deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features clusters of white, fragrant flowers that bloom in early spring, typically in May. The plant has slender, arching stems and narrow, ovate leaves that are dark green in color. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation in temperate climates.
