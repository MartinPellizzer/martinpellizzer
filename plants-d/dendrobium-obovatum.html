Dendrobium obovatum

Dendrobium obovatum, commonly known as the broad-ovate orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and yellow. Its leaves are broad and ovate, often with a waxy texture.

Dendrobium obovatum thrives in humid, shaded environments and is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value.
