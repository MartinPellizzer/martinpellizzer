Dalea reverchonii

Dalea reverchonii, commonly known as the Reverchon's milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in sagebrush steppe and grassland habitats.
