Dalbergaria stricta

Dalbergaria stricta, commonly known as the slender dalbergia, is a shrub or small tree native to South Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, often multi-branched stems. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in clusters. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three small, oval-shaped leaflets.

Dalbergaria stricta is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
