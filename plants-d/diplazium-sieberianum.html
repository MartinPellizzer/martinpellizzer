Diplazium sieberianum

Diplazium sieberianum, commonly known as the common chain fern, is a species of fern native to Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive fronds that are divided into segments resembling a chain, hence its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine in some regions.

Diplazium sieberianum plays a role in maintaining ecological balance by contributing to soil stability and providing habitat for various organisms.
