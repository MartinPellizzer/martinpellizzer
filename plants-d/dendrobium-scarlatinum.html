Dendrobium scarlatinum

Dendrobium scarlatinum, commonly known as the flame orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features vibrant red or orange flowers with yellow markings, often blooming in clusters along its upright, branched stems. The plant has a pseudobulb structure that stores water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged spirally around the stem, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Dendrobium scarlatinum is prized for its striking appearance and is commonly cultivated as a decorative plant.
