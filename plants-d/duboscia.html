Duboscia

Duboscia, commonly known as the "buttercup plant," is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The leaves are typically alternate, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs. Duboscia species are native to various regions in Africa and are often found in moist, open habitats.
