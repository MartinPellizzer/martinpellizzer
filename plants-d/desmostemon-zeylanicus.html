Desmostemon zeylanicus

Desmostemon zeylanicus, commonly known as the Ceylon bean or Indian coffee bean, is a climbing shrub native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellowish flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has historical uses in traditional medicine.

The seeds are also used in some local cuisines and herbal preparations.
