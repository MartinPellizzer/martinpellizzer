Dolichos galeatus

Dolichos galeatus, commonly known as the slender cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that give way to elongated, cylindrical pods containing edible seeds. Dolichos galeatus is valued for its nutritional content, including protein and essential minerals.

It is often cultivated as a food crop and for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.
