Desmodium toppinii

Desmodium toppinii, commonly known as the tropical senna, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow-orange flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Desmodium toppinii thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to warm, humid climates.
