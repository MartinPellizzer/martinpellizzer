Dichopsis grandis

Dichopsis grandis, commonly known as the giant dichopsis, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems that store water, allowing it to survive in arid conditions. The plant produces striking, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. Its leaves are typically green with a waxy coating, reducing water loss.

Dichopsis grandis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in dry climates.
