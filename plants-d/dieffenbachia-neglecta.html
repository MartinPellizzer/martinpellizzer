Dieffenbachia neglecta

Dieffenbachia neglecta, commonly known as the White Knight, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, waxy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a striking combination of green and white. The plant is a member of the Araceae family, which includes many ornamental plants known for their distinctive inflorescences. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its attractive foliage, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded outdoor gardens.
