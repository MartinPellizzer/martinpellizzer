Disa poikilantha

Disa poikilantha, commonly known as the variable disa, is a species of orchid native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, often multicolored flowers that range from pink to red and yellow. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy habitats, thriving in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, forming a basal rosette.

Disa poikilantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
