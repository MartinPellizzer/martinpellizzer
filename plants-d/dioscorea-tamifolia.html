Dioscorea tamifolia

Dioscorea tamifolia, commonly known as the arrowhead vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and yellowish flowers. The plant produces tuberous roots that are sometimes used in traditional medicine. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its rapid growth habit makes it both a useful ornamental plant and a potential invasive species in some areas.
