Decorima umbellata

Decorima umbellata, commonly known as the umbrella tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it its common name. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Decorima umbellata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
