Didymotheca veroniciformis

Didymotheca veroniciformis, commonly known as the red or yellow ironweed, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. The plant features erect stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its showy inflorescences consist of small, clustered yellow or red flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
