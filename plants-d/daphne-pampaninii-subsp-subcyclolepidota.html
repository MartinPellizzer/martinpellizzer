Daphne pampaninii subsp. subcyclolepidota

Daphne pampaninii subsp.

subcyclolepidota, commonly known as the subcyclolepidota daphne, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It features small, aromatic leaves that are typically elliptical in shape.

The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white or pink flowers in the spring. Its flowers are surrounded by a unique, glandular, waxy structure known as a cyathium.

This subspecies is notable for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
