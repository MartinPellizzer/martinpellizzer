Dendrobium × regnieri

Dendrobium × regnieri, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a sympodial epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a thick, fleshy stem that stores water, allowing it to thrive in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink, often blooming during the winter months. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem.

Dendrobium × regnieri is prized for its ornamental value and is widely cultivated in horticulture.
