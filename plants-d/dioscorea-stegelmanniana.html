Dioscorea stegelmanniana

Dioscorea stegelmanniana, commonly known as the Stegelmann yam, is a climbing vine native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its tuberous roots, which are often used in traditional medicine. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

Its growth habit and morphological features make it an important species in both ecological and medicinal contexts.
