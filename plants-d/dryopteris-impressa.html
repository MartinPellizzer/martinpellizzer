Dryopteris impressa

Dryopteris impressa, commonly known as the barren sword fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender fronds that can reach up to 1.5 meters in length. The fronds are typically dark green and have a distinctive, narrow, linear shape. This fern thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest understories.

It is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
