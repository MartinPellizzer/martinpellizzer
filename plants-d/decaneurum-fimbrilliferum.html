Decaneurum fimbrilliferum

Decaneurum fimbrilliferum, commonly known as the feathered flameplant, is a unique species belonging to the genus Decaneurum.

It is characterized by its striking, feather-like inflorescences that give it its common name. The plant produces small, brightly colored flowers that are often arranged in dense, branching clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its botanical structure includes fibrous roots and a short, stout stem with alternate, lance-shaped leaves.
