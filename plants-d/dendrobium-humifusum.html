Dendrobium humifusum

Dendrobium humifusum, commonly known as the ground orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It has a rhizomatous growth habit and produces numerous thin, grass-like leaves. The plant blooms with small, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and white, often in summer. It prefers shaded, moist environments and is typically found in forest understories.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique growth form and ecological role in its native habitat.
