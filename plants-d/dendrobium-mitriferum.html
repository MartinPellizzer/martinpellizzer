Dendrobium mitriferum

Dendrobium mitriferum, commonly known as the helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, and it produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

Dendrobium mitriferum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
