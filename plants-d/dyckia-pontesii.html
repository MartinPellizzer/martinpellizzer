Dyckia pontesii

Dyckia pontesii, commonly known as the Puentes Dyckia, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often blue-green in color, with a waxy coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences that bear small, showy white or pale pink flowers. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.

Dyckia pontesii is relatively low maintenance and drought-tolerant, adding a unique visual appeal to any collection.
