Dendrochilum album var. acutifolium

Dendrochilum album var. acutifolium, commonly known as the white dendrochilum with acute leaves, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves that are pointed at the tips. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is cultivated for its delicate floral display.
