Derris nicou

Derris nicou, commonly known as the African snakeroot, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae, characterized by its pod-like fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to withstand arid conditions.
