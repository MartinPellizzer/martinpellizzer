Digitaria botryostachya

Digitaria botryostachya, commonly known as the panicle grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its dense, spike-like inflorescence that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open areas such as fields and disturbed soils, showing adaptability to various environmental conditions. Its leaves are narrow and linear, with a rough texture, and it produces small, wind-pollinated flowers.

Digitaria botryostachya is often used in landscaping and as a forage crop due to its hardy nature and rapid growth.
