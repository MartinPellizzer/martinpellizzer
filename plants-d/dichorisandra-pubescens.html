Dichorisandra pubescens

Dichorisandra pubescens, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and bright yellow daisies that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit and is often cultivated as an ornamental in gardens. Its leaves are covered in fine, soft hairs, which contribute to its distinctive appearance.

Dichorisandra pubescens prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it adaptable to various garden conditions.
