Dianthus congestus

Dianthus congestus, commonly known as the clustered carnation, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces dense clusters of showy, pink or white flowers. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It blooms from spring to early summer, attracting pollinators with its vibrant petals.

Dianthus congestus is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
