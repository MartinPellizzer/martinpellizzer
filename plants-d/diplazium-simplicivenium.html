Diplazium simplicivenium

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are typically less than 30 cm in length. The plant thrives in shallow, freshwater environments such as ponds and slow-moving streams. Its rhizomes are submerged, anchoring the plant and facilitating nutrient absorption.
