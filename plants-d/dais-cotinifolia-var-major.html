Dais cotinifolia var. major

Dais cotinifolia var. major, commonly known as the Chinese smoke tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its feathery, palmate leaves that turn vibrant red in the autumn. The plant produces small, pale green flowers in clusters, which are followed by fluffy, white seed heads that resemble smoke. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This ornamental plant is valued for its seasonal color changes and is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic appeal.
