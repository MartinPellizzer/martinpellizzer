Dysoxylum oliganthym

Dysoxylum oliganthym, commonly known as the slender tamarind, is a tropical tree belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

It is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy green upper surface. Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is of interest in both horticulture and botanical research.
