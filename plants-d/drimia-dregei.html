Drimia dregei

Drimia dregei, commonly known as the Natal lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a bulbous root system and produces tall, strap-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant blooms with striking white or pale pink flowers, typically in late summer or early autumn. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Drimia dregei is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in its native habitat.
