Dolicholobium oblongifolium var. longissimum

Dolicholobium oblongifolium var. longissimum, commonly known as the long-leaf dolicholobium, is a variant of the oblong-leaved dolicholobium.

It is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves that can reach considerable lengths. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or forested areas. Its flowers are small and clustered, often with a distinctive yellowish-green hue.

This variant is valued for its unique foliage and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
