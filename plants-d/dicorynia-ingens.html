Dicorynia ingens

Dicorynia ingens, commonly known as the giant spondias, is a large tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the family Anacardiaceae and is characterized by its massive, buttressed roots and broad, spreading canopy. Its leaves are large, simple, and alternate, with a glossy green upper surface and a lighter underside.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by edible, orange-red drupes that are an important food source for various wildlife.
