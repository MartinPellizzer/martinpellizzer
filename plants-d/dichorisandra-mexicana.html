Dichorisandra mexicana

Dichorisandra mexicana, commonly known as the Mexican starfruit plant, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is a shrub native to Mexico and parts of Central America, characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers. Dichorisandra mexicana is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.
