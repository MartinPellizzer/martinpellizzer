Dendrochilum ocellatum

Dendrochilum ocellatum, commonly known as the spotted orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. Its inflorescence consists of a raceme of flowers with distinctive, ocellate (eyelike) patterns on the petals.

Dendrochilum ocellatum thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.
