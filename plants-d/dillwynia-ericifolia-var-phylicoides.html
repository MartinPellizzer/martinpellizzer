Dillwynia ericifolia var. phylicoides

Dillwynia ericifolia var. phylicoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved dillwynia, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features small, needle-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive texture. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is typically found in heathlands and dry forests.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
