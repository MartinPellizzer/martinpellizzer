Desfontainia fulgens

Desfontainia fulgens, commonly known as the flame flower, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range from red to orange, often with a yellow or green throat. The plant typically grows as an epiphytic shrub, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are usually dark green and glossy, providing a striking contrast to its colorful blooms.

Desfontainia fulgens is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid, warm environments.
