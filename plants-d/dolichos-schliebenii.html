Dolichos schliebenii

Dolichos schliebenii, commonly known as the Schlieben's dolichos, is a climbing legume native to parts of Africa.

It features trifoliate leaves with three oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters. The plant bears elongated, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in dry, open habitats and is adapted to arid conditions.

Its botanical characteristics make it an interesting species for ecological and agricultural studies.
