Dieffenbachia picta subvar. memoria

Dieffenbachia picta subvar. memoria, commonly known as the Memory Plant, is a tropical foliage plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that feature a mix of white, green, and cream patterns. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions. Its leaves are thick and waxy, which helps it retain moisture in humid environments.

The plant is also popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
