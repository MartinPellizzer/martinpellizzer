Dicranopteris yungensis

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, floating fronds that are often used in ornamental water gardens. Dicranopteris yungensis is valued for its aesthetic appeal and its ability to improve water quality by absorbing nutrients.
