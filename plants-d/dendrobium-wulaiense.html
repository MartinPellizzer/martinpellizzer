Dendrobium wulaiense

Dendrobium wulaiense, commonly known as the Wulai orchid, is a species of orchid native to Taiwan.

It is an epiphytic plant that grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid and shaded environments. The plant produces long, pendant-like inflorescences with striking, often fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. Its leaves are typically elongated and arranged in a rosette pattern, and it has a complex root system adapted for nutrient absorption from its host.

Dendrobium wulaiense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
