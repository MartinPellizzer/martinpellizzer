Davallia humilis

Davallia humilis, commonly known as the shield fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, rhizomatous growth habit and its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The fronds are typically bipinnate, with a soft, leathery texture and a dark green color. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

This plant is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of care.
