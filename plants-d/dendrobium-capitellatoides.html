Dendrobium capitellatoides

Dendrobium capitellatoides, commonly known as the broad-leafed dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features elongated, fleshy pseudobulbs covered in a thick layer of white or pale green roots. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with prominent, showy lips. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
