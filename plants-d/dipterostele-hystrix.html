Dipterostele hystrix

Dipterostele hystrix, commonly known as the zigzag cycad, is a unique plant with a distinctive botanical structure.

It belongs to the Cycadaceae family and is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk and feather-like leaves. The plant exhibits a dipterostele, a type of vascular system with two central cylinders, which is a key feature in its anatomy. Its leaves are typically arranged in a spiral pattern and are often dimorphic, with different shapes on either side.

This species is native to parts of South America and is known for its slow growth and long lifespan.
