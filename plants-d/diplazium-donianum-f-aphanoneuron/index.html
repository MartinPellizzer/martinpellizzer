Diplazium Donianum F. Aphanoneuron

Diplazium brake, scientifically known as Diplazium donianum f. aphanoneuron, is a fern species native to tropical regions of Asia and the Pacific. This plant is characterized by its delicate fronds and compact growth habit, often thriving in shaded, humid environments. It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture. The fern's unique morphology and ecological role make it an intriguing subject for botanical study. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in mainstream horticultural circles.
