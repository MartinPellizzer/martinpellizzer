Deschampsia convoluta

Deschampsia convoluta, commonly known as silky bluegrass, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear pale blue to silver flowers. It thrives in moist to wet soils, often found in wet meadows and along stream banks.

Deschampsia convoluta is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting wildlife.
