Darwinia Virescens

Darwinia virescens is a striking plant native to the coastal regions of southern Australia. It is known for its unique, spike-like inflorescences that display a range of vibrant colors. This species thrives in sandy soils and is often found in dunes and heathlands. Its flowers attract a variety of pollinators, including birds and insects. Darwinia virescens is both ecologically important and a popular choice for gardeners seeking drought-tolerant plants.
