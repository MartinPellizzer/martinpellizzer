Dittrichia Revoluta

Dittrichia revoluta is a unique flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is known for its striking pink and white blooms that appear in late spring. This species thrives in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are typically rolled or curled, giving it a distinctive appearance. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in popular horticulture.
