Dendranthema × shimotomaii

Dendranthema × shimotomaii, commonly known as the Shikoku chrysanthemum, is a hybrid plant originating from Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically features vibrant yellow to orange petals with a central disc of darker coloration. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and floral arrangements.

Dendranthema × shimotomaii prefers well-drained soil and full sun exposure for optimal growth.
