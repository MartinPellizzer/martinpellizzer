Dammara

Dammara, commonly known as the African daisies, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It comprises several species native to southern Africa, characterized by their showy, daisy-like flowers. The plants typically have erect stems and are often found in grassland and rocky habitats. Their vibrant blooms make them popular in horticulture and garden settings.
