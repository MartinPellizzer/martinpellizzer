Deutzia grandiflora var. baroniana

Deutzia grandiflora var. baroniana, commonly known as Baron's deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features clusters of fragrant, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has slender, arching stems and lance-shaped leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value comes from its showy blooms and graceful form.
