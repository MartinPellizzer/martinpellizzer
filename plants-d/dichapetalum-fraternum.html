Dichapetalum fraternum

Dichapetalum fraternum, commonly known as the African violets, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, violet-colored flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a short, woody stem and produces small, heart-shaped leaves. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
