Dendrobangia multinervia

Dendrobangia multinervia, commonly known as the multifurrowed orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with multiple stems and is characterized by its lanceolate, dark green leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often arranged in racemose inflorescences. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture from the air and decaying organic matter.

Dendrobangia multinervia is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm, humid environments.
