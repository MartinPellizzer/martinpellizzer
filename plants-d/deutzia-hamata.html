Deutzia hamata

Deutzia hamata, commonly known as the smooth deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features clusters of fragrant, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has slender, arching stems and dark green, ovate leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value comes from its showy flowers and graceful form.
