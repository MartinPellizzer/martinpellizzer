Dyckia ferox subsp. hamosa

Dyckia ferox subsp.

hamosa, commonly known as the fierce dyckia, is a striking bromeliad native to South America. It is characterized by its tall, upright rosette of sharp, grass-like leaves that can grow up to 2 meters in height.

The plant produces striking inflorescences with vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom in the summer. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its hardy nature and architectural form make it both ornamental and resilient in arid environments.
