Duthiea macrocarpa

Duthiea macrocarpa, commonly known as the giant seed plant, is a unique species characterized by its large, showy fruits.

It belongs to the family Duthieaceae and is native to specific regions in South America. The plant exhibits a tall, erect growth habit with broad, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, producing a distinctive, fleshy fruit that contains numerous seeds.

This species is of interest to botanists due to its unique reproductive structures and ecological adaptations.
