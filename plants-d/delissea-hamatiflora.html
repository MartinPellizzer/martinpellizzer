Delissea hamatiflora

Delissea hamatiflora, commonly known as the narrow-lobed bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in open woodland or grassland habitats. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that are usually blue or violet in color, with narrow, pointed lobes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Delissea hamatiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
