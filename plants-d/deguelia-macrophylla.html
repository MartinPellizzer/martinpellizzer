Deguelia macrophylla

Deguelia macrophylla, commonly known as the broad-leafed deguelia, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a landscape plant in warmer climates.
