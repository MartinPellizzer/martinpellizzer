Dapania macrophylla

Dapania macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf dapania, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Dapania macrophylla is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
