Dryopteris diplazioides var. amambayensis

Dryopteris diplazioides var. amambayensis, commonly known as the Amambay shield fern, is a tropical fern species native to parts of South America.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its large, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a leathery texture.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
