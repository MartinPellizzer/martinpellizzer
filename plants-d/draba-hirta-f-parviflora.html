Draba hirta f. parviflora

Draba hirta f. parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered rock cress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with a compact rosette of hairy, basal leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers in early spring, typically clustered in a single inflorescence. It thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine regions.

This subspecies is characterized by its reduced flower size compared to the typical form of Draba hirta.
