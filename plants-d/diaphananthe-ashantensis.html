Diaphananthe ashantensis

Diaphananthe ashantensis, commonly known as the Ashanti flame tree, is a tropical tree native to western Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a striking visual display. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its beautiful floral displays.
