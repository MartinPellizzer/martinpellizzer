Distictis Glaziovii

Distictis glaziovii is a rare and fascinating plant native to the mountainous regions of Mexico. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, known for its diverse and often showy flowers. This species is characterized by its striking yellow blooms and unique leaf structure. Due to its limited habitat and threats from human activity, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this botanical gem for future generations.
