Dombeya platypoda

Dombeya platypoda, commonly known as the narrow-leaved dombeya, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of showy, pink to white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in shrublands and rocky areas.

Dombeya platypoda is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
