Dupatya regalis

Dupatya regalis, commonly known as the royal dupatya, is a rare and striking plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from deep purple to bright red, often with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, with a smooth, glossy surface.

Dupatya regalis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
