Dictyandra arborescens

Dictyandra arborescens, commonly known as the tree arrowroot, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the family Araceae and is characterized by its woody stem and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, spadix-like flowers surrounded by a spathe, typical of the arum family. Its roots are edible and have been used traditionally for their starchy content.
