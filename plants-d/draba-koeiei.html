Draba koeiei

Draba koeiei, commonly known as the Koei's whitlow grass, is a small, flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of central Asia, including parts of China and the Himalayas. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months.

Draba koeiei is characterized by its low-growing habit and its ability to survive in harsh, high-altitude conditions.
