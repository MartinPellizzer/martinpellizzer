Dendrobium glebulosum

Dendrobium glebulosum, commonly known as the yellow-lipped orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with numerous horizontal pseudobulbs covered in dense, leathery leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and white, often with a distinctive red or orange lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium glebulosum is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
