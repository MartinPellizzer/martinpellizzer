Drynaria Sagitta

Drynaria sagitta is a unique fern species known for its distinctive, arrow-shaped fronds. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. This plant is often found growing on rock surfaces or in forest undergrowth. Its ability to survive in challenging conditions makes it a resilient and adaptable species. Drynaria sagitta holds cultural and ecological significance in its native habitats.
