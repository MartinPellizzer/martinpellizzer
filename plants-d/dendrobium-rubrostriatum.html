Dendrobium rubrostriatum

Dendrobium rubrostriatum, commonly known as the red-striated orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit, with multiple pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of red and yellow, often with contrasting stripes. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium rubrostriatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in greenhouses and as houseplants.
