Drepananthus pahangensis

Drepananthus pahangensis, commonly known as the Pahang spikegrass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia. This grass species is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

Drepananthus pahangensis is often found in moist, lowland areas and is adapted to thrive in humid tropical climates.
