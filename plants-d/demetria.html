Demetria

Demetria, commonly known as the yellow bellflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sun. Demetria has lance-shaped leaves and produces small, green fruits that contain seeds.

It is often found in woodland areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
