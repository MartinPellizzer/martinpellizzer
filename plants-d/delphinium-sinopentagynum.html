Delphinium sinopentagynum

Delphinium sinopentagynum, commonly known as the Chinese larkspur, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of western China.

It features tall, sparsely branched stems that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height, adorned with large, showy blue to purple flowers. The plant has palmate leaves with deeply lobed margins, contributing to its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Due to its striking appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice among gardeners interested in cultivating native flora.
