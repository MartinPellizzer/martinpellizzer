Desmodium longipes

Desmodium longipes, commonly known as the longipes burrbean, is a perennial legume native to parts of Africa and the Americas.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces elongated, hairy pods that contain several elliptical seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in disturbed soils.

Desmodium longipes is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.
