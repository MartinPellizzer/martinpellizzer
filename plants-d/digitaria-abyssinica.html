Digitaria abyssinica

Digitaria abyssinica, commonly known as African stargrass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear minute, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

Digitaria abyssinica is often used in traditional medicine and as a forage crop for livestock.
