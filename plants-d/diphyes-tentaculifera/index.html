Diphyes Tentaculifera

Diphyes tentaculifera is a unique and intriguing species of seaweed found in coastal regions. It is characterized by its distinct branching structure and delicate, translucent fronds. This plant thrives in shallow, nutrient-rich waters where sunlight penetration is optimal. Its name reflects its two distinct forms, which vary with environmental conditions. Studying Diphyes tentaculifera offers insights into marine biodiversity and ecosystem dynamics.
