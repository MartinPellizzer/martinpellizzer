Dombeya hilsenbergii

Dombeya hilsenbergii, commonly known as the Hilsenbeck dombeya, is a deciduous shrub native to South Africa.

It features showy, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a yellow center. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

The flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, contributing to its ecological value.
