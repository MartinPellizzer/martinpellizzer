Dichapetalum dewevrei

Dichapetalum dewevrei, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are simple and alternate, with a rough texture.

Dichapetalum dewevrei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
