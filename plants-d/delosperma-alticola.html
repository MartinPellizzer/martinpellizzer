Delosperma alticola

Delosperma alticola, commonly known as the alpine carpet flower, is a low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces vibrant, star-shaped flowers in shades of red, pink, and yellow during the summer months. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact size and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking hardy, ornamental plants.
