Dillenia humilis

Dillenia humilis, commonly known as the dwarf dillenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dilleniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, palmate leaves and showy, yellowish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its fruits are fleshy, berry-like structures that contain numerous small seeds.

Dillenia humilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has limited use in traditional medicine.
