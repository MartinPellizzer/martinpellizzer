Dalbergia occulta

Dalbergia occulta, commonly known as the hidden or secret legume, is a small, deciduous tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and legume fruits. The plant is notable for its small, clustered flowers that are typically pale green to white in color. Its bark is smooth and greyish, while its wood is dense and often used in traditional crafts.

Dalbergia occulta thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in secondary forests and along riverbanks.
