Doratium

Doratium, commonly known as the "spider plant," is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is a perennial evergreen plant native to southern Africa. The plant features long, arching stems that bear clusters of small, grass-like leaves. Its most distinctive feature is the production of delicate, dangling flower spikes that bear tiny white or pink flowers.

Doratium is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in a variety of light conditions.
