Disa Inflexa

Disa inflexa is a striking member of the orchid family known for its vibrant pink and white petals. Native to southern Africa, this plant thrives in rocky and coastal environments. It is prized for its unique floral structure and ability to bloom in harsh conditions. The species is also popular among horticulturists for its ornamental value. Its resilience and beauty make it a fascinating subject for both scientific study and garden enthusiasts.
