Dudleya paniculata

Dudleya paniculata, commonly known as the panicle Dudleya, is a succulent plant native to coastal regions of California and Baja California.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing small, star-shaped flowers that range from white to pink. It is adapted to grow in rocky, well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant.

Dudleya paniculata is a popular choice for rock gardens due to its ornamental appearance and low maintenance requirements.
