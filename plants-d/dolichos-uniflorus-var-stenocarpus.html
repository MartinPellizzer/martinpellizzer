Dolichos uniflorus var. stenocarpus

Dolichos uniflorus var. stenocarpus, commonly known as the narrow-carpel dolichos, is a leguminous plant characterized by its slender, erect growth habit.

It produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are typically clustered in racemes. The plant's pods are narrow and elongated, containing several seeds that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in warm, tropical climates and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.

Its botanical features make it a notable species within the genus Dolichos.
