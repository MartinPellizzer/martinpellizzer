Deutzia glauca

Deutzia glauca, commonly known as the blue deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has slender, arching branches and dark green, elliptical leaves. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Deutzia glauca is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
