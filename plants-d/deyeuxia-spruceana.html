Deyeuxia spruceana

Deyeuxia spruceana, commonly known as the Spruce grass, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine meadows and forested areas. The plant has slender, erect stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. Its inflorescence consists of a panicle made up of spikelets, which are small clusters of flowers.

Deyeuxia spruceana plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various small animals and insects.
