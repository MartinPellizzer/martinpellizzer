Deyeuxia deserticola

Deyeuxia deserticola, commonly known as the desert couchgrass, is a perennial grass species native to arid and semi-arid regions of Asia.

The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers, typical of the Poaceae family.
