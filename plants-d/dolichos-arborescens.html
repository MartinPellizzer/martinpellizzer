Dolichos arborescens

Dolichos arborescens, commonly known as the tree bean, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous pods and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces yellow to orange flowers that attract pollinators. Its fruit is a long, slender pod containing edible seeds used in various cuisines.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
