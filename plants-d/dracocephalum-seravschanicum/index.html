Dracocephalum Seravschanicum

Dracocephalum seravschanicum is a rare and fascinating plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. Known for its unique appearance and medicinal properties, it has captured the interest of botanists and herbalists alike. This species belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic and useful plants. Its striking blue flowers and aromatic leaves make it a standout among lesser-known flora. Despite its beauty and potential, it remains a poorly studied plant with limited distribution.
