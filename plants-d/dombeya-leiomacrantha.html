Dombeya leiomacrantha

Dombeya leiomacrantha, commonly known as the smooth-flowered dombeya, is a deciduous shrub native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. These flowers are typically tubular and have a distinctive, smooth appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry forest or scrubland habitats.
