Dendrobium gracilicaule var. howeanum

Dendrobium gracilicaule var. howeanum, commonly known as the slender-stemmed orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic orchid, typically growing on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. The plant features long, slender stems that can reach up to several meters in length. Its flowers are small and clustered, often in shades of white, yellow, or green.

It requires bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
