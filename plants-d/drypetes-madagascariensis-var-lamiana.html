Drypetes madagascariensis var. lamiana

Drypetes madagascariensis var. lamiana, commonly known as the Madagascar flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its striking red and orange flowers that appear in clusters. The leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly serrated edge. It produces small, woody capsules that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
