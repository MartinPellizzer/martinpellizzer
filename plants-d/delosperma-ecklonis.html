Delosperma ecklonis

Delosperma ecklonis, commonly known as the ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant clusters of star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, and yellow, typically during the summer months. It is a low-growing perennial that forms a dense mat, making it an excellent ground cover.

Delosperma ecklonis is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
