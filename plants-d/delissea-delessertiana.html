Delissea delessertiana

Delissea delessertiana, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. Delissea delessertiana is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
