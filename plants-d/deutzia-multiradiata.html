Deutzia multiradiata

Deutzia multiradiata, commonly known as the Chinese deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy clusters of white to pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, arching branches and small, ovate leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Deutzia multiradiata is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive floral display and hardy nature.
