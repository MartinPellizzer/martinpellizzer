Drimia ligulata

Drimia ligulata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf African lily, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features strap-like, linear leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale blue flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Drimia ligulata is often used in rock gardens and as a decorative plant due to its elegant appearance.
