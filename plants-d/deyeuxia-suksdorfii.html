Deyeuxia suksdorfii

Deyeuxia suksdorfii, commonly known as the Saksdorf's bulrush, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Asia, particularly China.

Deyeuxia suksdorfii plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wetland organisms.
