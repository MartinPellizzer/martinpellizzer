Dasystephana owerinii

Dasystephana owerinii, commonly known as the African star orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale blue. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow leaves and thrives in humid, shaded environments. It is often found growing in rocky areas or on tree trunks in its natural habitat.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
