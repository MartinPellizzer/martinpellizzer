Dorothea

Dorothea, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other composite flowers. The plant is characterized by its silvery-gray leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Dorothea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
