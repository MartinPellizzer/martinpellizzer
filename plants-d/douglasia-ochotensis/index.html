Douglasia Ochotensis

Douglasia ochotensis is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the Far East. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions. This species is known for its unique, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine, fuzzy texture. Due to habitat loss, Douglasia ochotensis is now considered endangered.
