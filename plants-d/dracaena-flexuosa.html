Dracaena flexuosa

Dracaena flexuosa, commonly known as the zigzagging dracaena, is a striking ornamental plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its long, arching leaves that form a cascading effect, giving it a unique and dramatic appearance. The plant has a robust root system and can grow quite tall, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation. Its foliage is typically a deep green, with some varieties displaying variegated patterns.

Dracaena flexuosa is relatively low maintenance, thriving in bright, indirect light and requiring moderate watering.
