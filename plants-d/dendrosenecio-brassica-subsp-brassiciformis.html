Dendrosenecio brassica subsp. brassiciformis

Dendrosenecio brassica subsp.

brassiciformis, commonly known as the African snowflake, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features a rosette of fleshy, spoon-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color.

The plant produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers during the cooler months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Due to its unique appearance and hardiness, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
