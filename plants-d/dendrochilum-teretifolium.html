Dendrochilum teretifolium

Dendrochilum teretifolium, commonly known as the slender-leafed tree orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a distinctive appearance, featuring a single, erect stem and elongated, narrow leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, often with a slight fragrance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
