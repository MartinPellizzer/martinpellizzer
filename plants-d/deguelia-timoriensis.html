Deguelia timoriensis

Deguelia timoriensis, commonly known as the Timor senna, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to the island of Timor.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it valuable in ecological restoration projects.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.
