Dracaena interrupta

Dracaena interrupta, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, zigzagging stems that give it its common name. The plant has dark green, strap-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. It produces small, fragrant yellow flowers that appear in clusters at the tips of the stems.

Dracaena interrupta is a popular indoor plant due to its hardiness and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
