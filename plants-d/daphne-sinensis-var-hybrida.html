Daphne sinensis var. hybrida

Daphne sinensis var. hybrida, commonly known as Chinese Daphne, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features fragrant, showy flowers that range from pink to white, often appearing in late winter or early spring. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its winter blooms and aromatic foliage.
