Dombeya divaricata

Dombeya divaricata, commonly known as the variable dombeya, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Africa.

It features showy, fragrant, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring. The plant has alternate, ovate to elliptic leaves that are often hairy beneath. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Dombeya divaricata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively low maintenance once established.
