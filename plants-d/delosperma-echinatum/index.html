Delosperma Echinatum

Delosperma echinatum is a striking member of the Aizoaceae family known for its vibrant, tubular flowers. Native to southern Africa, this plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and full sun. Its unique appearance, with spiky leaves and colorful blooms, makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping. The plant's ability to survive in arid conditions showcases its adaptability and resilience. Its beauty and hardiness have made it a favorite among gardeners seeking low-maintenance, drought-tolerant plants.
