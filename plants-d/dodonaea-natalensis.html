Dodonaea natalensis

Dodonaea natalensis, commonly known as Natal glorybower, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and is often found in coastal and sandy soils. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The bark of the plant is fibrous and can be used in traditional medicine.
