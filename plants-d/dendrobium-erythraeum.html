Dendrobium erythraeum

Dendrobium erythraeum, commonly known as the Red Stem Dendrobium, is a type of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant that grows on trees and rocks, absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air. The plant produces striking orchid-like flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with a velvety texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

Dendrobium erythraeum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
