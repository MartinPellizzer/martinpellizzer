Dendrobium canaliculatum var. nigrescens

Dendrobium canaliculatum var. nigrescens, commonly known as the black-flowered dendrobium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid tropical environments. The plant features a tall, slender stem with clustered, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are striking, with dark purple to black petals and sepals, often with contrasting white or yellow markings.

It is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
