Dieffenbachia magnifica

Dieffenbachia magnifica, commonly known as the Giant Dieffenbachia, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, waxy, arrowhead-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 feet in length. The plant is known for its vibrant, variegated foliage, often featuring shades of green, white, and cream. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to maintain its health.

Due to its toxic sap, it should be handled with care and kept away from pets and children.
