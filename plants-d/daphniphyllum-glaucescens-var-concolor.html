Daphniphyllum glaucescens var. concolor

Daphniphyllum glaucescens var. concolor, commonly known as the Chinese holly or narrow-leafed daphniphyllum, is a species of evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters during the spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for woodland gardens and as a container plant.
