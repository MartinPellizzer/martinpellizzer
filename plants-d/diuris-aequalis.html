Diuris aequalis

Diuris aequalis, commonly known as the common spider-orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in sandy or loamy soils within open forests and heathlands. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a single or few flowers that are usually pale green to yellowish in color. Its flowers have a distinctive spider-like appearance, with long, narrow petals and sepals.

Diuris aequalis is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and is often found in regions with a Mediterranean climate.
