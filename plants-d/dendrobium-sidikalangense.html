Dendrobium sidikalangense

Dendrobium sidikalangense, commonly known as the Sidikalang orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with prominent white lips. Its pseudobulbs are cylindrical and covered in a layer of soft, white pubescence.

Dendrobium sidikalangense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
