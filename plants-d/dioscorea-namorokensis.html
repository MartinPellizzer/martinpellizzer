Dioscorea namorokensis

Dioscorea namorokensis, commonly known as the Namorokense yam, is a flowering plant species belonging to the Dioscoreaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Tanzania and Kenya. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically yellow or greenish-yellow, and it produces tuberous roots that are used in traditional medicine.

The plant's botanical features make it an important species for both ecological and ethnobotanical studies.
