Dendrobium capillipes var. elegans

Dendrobium capillipes var. elegans, commonly known as the Elegant Dendrobium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees or rocks in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features long, slender stems that can reach several feet in length, often adorned with small, clustered flowers. Its leaves are linear and arranged in two rows along the stem, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

The flowers are usually white or pale yellow with a subtle fragrance, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
