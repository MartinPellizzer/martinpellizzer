Dennstaedtia grandifrons

Dennstaedtia grandifrons, commonly known as the large-leaf sword fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Blechnaceae.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped fronds that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces sporangia on the undersides of its leaves, which are clustered in sori covered by a protective indusium. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in temperate rainforests.

This fern is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
