Dendrobium kohlmeyerianum

Dendrobium kohlmeyerianum, commonly known as the golden spike orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, branching structure with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with a distinctive golden stripe along the petals. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium kohlmeyerianum is appreciated for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
