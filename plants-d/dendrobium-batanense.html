Dendrobium batanense

Dendrobium batanense, commonly known as the Batanese orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It features a sympodial growth habit with upright, branching stems that often bear leaves along their length. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink, typically during the dry season. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the humid forest floor, and it thrives in shaded, moist environments.

Dendrobium batanense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine.
