Dryadanthe bungeana

Dryadanthe bungeana, commonly known as the Bunge's dryad's orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and delicate, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in cool, high-altitude environments. Its leaves are lanceolate and often covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are small and clustered in racemes.
