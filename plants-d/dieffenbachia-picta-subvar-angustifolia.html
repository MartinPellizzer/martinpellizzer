Dieffenbachia picta subvar. angustifolia

Dieffenbachia picta subvar. angustifolia, commonly known as the Narrow-leaf Dieffenbachia, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with white and green patterns on its lance-shaped leaves. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions. Its leaves are thick and waxy, which helps it retain moisture in its natural habitat.

It is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
