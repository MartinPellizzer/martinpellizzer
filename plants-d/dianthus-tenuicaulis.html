Dianthus tenuicaulis

Dianthus tenuicaulis, commonly known as the slender-stemmed campion, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, pink to red flowers. The leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs, giving the plant a delicate appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
