Diervilla × fujisanensis var. rosea

Diervilla × fujisanensis var. rosea, commonly known as the Rose Glow Weigela, is a hybrid shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its dense, upright growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant produces clusters of pink to red tubular flowers, which are attractive to pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its foliage is typically dark green, providing a striking contrast to the colorful blooms.

It is relatively low maintenance and thrives in full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
