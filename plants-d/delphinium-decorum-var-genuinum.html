Delphinium decorum var. genuinum

Delphinium decorum var. genuinum, commonly known as the Garden Delphinium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of the western United States.

It features tall, sturdy stems that bear clusters of striking blue to violet flowers, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, fertile soil.

Due to its showy blooms and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
