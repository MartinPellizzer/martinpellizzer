Dendrobium chrysanthum var. microphthalama

Dendrobium chrysanthum var. microphthalama, commonly known as the miniature yellow orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant, yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte or lithophyte, attaching itself to trees or rocks in its natural habitat. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in bright, indirect light with moderate humidity.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its delicate appearance.
