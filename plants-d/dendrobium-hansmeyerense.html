Dendrobium hansmeyerense

Dendrobium hansmeyerense, commonly known as the Hansmeyer's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, cane-like stems and clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on trees or rocks in tropical forests. Its leaves are usually fleshy and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Dendrobium hansmeyerense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
