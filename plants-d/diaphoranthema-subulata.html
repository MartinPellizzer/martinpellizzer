Diaphoranthema subulata

Diaphoranthema subulata, commonly known as the Cape umbrella plant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, compact growth with narrow, needle-like leaves that are typically blue-gray in color. The plant produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
