Doronicum carpetanum

Doronicum carpetanum, commonly known as the Carpathian leopard's bane, is a perennial herb native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in dense clusters at the top of the stem.

Doronicum carpetanum thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.
