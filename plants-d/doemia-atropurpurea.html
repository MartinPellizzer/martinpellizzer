Doemia atropurpurea

Doemia atropurpurea, commonly known as purplestem dogbane, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant features slender, purplish stems and opposite, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and clustered in umbels, blooming in late summer.

The plant is known for its milky sap and is often found in moist woodlands and along stream banks.
