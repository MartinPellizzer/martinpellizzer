Digitaria violascens var. intersita

Digitaria violascens var. intersita, commonly known as the slender digit grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides, fields, and pastures. The plant has slender, erect stems that are often branched and can reach heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

The inflorescence consists of spikelets that are loosely arranged, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
