Dorstenia fawcettii

Dorstenia fawcettii, commonly known as the "Cushion Dorstenia," is a succulent plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact, cushion-like growth habit, with dense clusters of fleshy, gray-green leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
