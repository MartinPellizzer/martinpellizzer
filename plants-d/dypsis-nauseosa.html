Dypsis nauseosa

Dypsis nauseosa, commonly known as the Madagascar palm, is a tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its unique, bottle-shaped trunk and clusters of spiny, palm-like leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters at the top of the stem. These flowers are followed by bright yellow fruits that contain edible seeds.

Dypsis nauseosa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and adaptability to various growing conditions.
