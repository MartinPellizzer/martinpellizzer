Desmos grandifolius

Desmos grandifolius, commonly known as the large-leaf desmos, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires a warm, humid climate.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and tropical landscapes.
