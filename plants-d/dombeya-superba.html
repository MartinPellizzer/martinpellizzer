Dombeya superba

Dombeya superba, commonly known as the royal dombeya, is a striking shrub native to the high-altitude regions of tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and lavender. The plant produces fluffy, white seeds that are dispersed by the wind, aiding in its propagation across open habitats. Its foliage consists of opposite, ovate leaves with hairy surfaces, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Dombeya superba thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens in warm climates.
