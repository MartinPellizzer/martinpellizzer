Dalbergia collettii

Dalbergia collettii, commonly known as the Collett's rosewood, is a small to medium-sized tree native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its dense, dark-colored wood, which is highly valued for its aesthetic and structural properties. The plant features pinnate leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white.

Its fruit is a legume pod containing several seeds, and it thrives in humid, tropical climates with well-drained soil.
