Diplazium melanosorum

Diplazium melanosorum, commonly known as the black-stemmed sword fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its dark, leathery fronds that are typically divided into narrow, lance-shaped segments. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky crevices, where it forms dense colonies. The fronds are evergreen, providing a lush, green appearance throughout the year.
