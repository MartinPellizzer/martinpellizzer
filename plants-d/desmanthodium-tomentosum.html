Desmanthodium tomentosum

Desmanthodium tomentosum, commonly known as the woolly desmanthodium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features a low-growing, mat-forming habit with hairy, grayish-green foliage. Its flowers are typically small and yellow, arranged in dense clusters.

It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.
