Ditassa dolichoglossa

Ditassa dolichoglossa, commonly known as the long-tongued dittany, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.
