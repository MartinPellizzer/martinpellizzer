Delosperma holzbecherorum

Delosperma holzbecherorum, commonly known as the Holzbecher's ice plant, is a low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, adapted for water storage. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its hardy nature allows it to survive in arid conditions, showcasing its resilience as a drought-tolerant species.
