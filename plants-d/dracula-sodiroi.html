Dracula sodiroi

Dracula sodiroi, commonly known as the "Dwarf Dragon's Blood," is a unique orchid species native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety red petals and a distinctive, elongated spur that resembles a dragon's tongue. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or branches, thriving in the humid, shaded conditions of its natural habitat. Its flowers bloom in late summer and are pollinated by specific species of bees and hummingbirds.

Dracula sodiroi is a rare and endangered species, making it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
