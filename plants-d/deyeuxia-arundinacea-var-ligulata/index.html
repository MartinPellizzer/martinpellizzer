Deyeuxia Arundinacea Var. Ligulata

Deyeuxia arundinacea var. ligulata is a lesser-known grass species that thrives in wetland environments. It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and compact growth habit. This variant is often found in marshes and along the edges of ponds. Its ecological role is important for maintaining wetland biodiversity. Despite its modest appearance, it plays a vital part in supporting local wildlife.
