Dioclea densiflora

Dioclea densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered dioclea, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in humid, lowland environments. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. It produces flat, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Dioclea densiflora is often found in secondary forests and is adapted to disturbed habitats.
