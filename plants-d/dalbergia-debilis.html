Dalbergia debilis

Dalbergia debilis, commonly known as the weak or slender bean tree, is a small to medium-sized leguminous tree native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental potential and ecological importance.
