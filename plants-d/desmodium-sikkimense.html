Desmodium sikkimense

Desmodium sikkimense, commonly known as the Sikkim velvet bean, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family Fabaceae.

It is native to the eastern Himalayas, including regions of India, Nepal, and Bhutan. The plant is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. It produces cylindrical pods containing several elliptical seeds.

Desmodium sikkimense is often found in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it useful in ecological restoration projects.
