Dendropanax nervosus

Dendropanax nervosus, commonly known as the veined arrowhead, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves with prominent venation patterns that give it its scientific name. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and typically grows as a vine with aerial roots. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

The flowers are small and inconspicuous, with a spadix and a spathe typical of the Araceae family.
