Diolena auriculata

Diolena auriculata, commonly known as the golden groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and is typically found in grasslands and rocky areas. The plant has a low-growing habit with hairy stems and leaves that are often lobed or heart-shaped. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Diolena auriculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
