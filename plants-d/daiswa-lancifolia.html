Daiswa lancifolia

Daiswa lancifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved daisya, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Daiswa lancifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
