Disocactus hondurensis

Disocactus hondurensis, commonly known as the "Honduran disocactus," is a species of cactus native to Central America.

It is a low-growing, leafy plant with a unique appearance, featuring thick, fleshy stems covered in spines. The plant produces striking, bell-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Disocactus hondurensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
