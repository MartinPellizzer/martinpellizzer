Dimorphandra exaltata

Dimorphandra exaltata, commonly known as the African sycamore, is a large, deciduous tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and compound leaves. The tree produces yellowish-white flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences, followed by flat, pod-like fruits.

Its roots have a long history of traditional use in African medicine for their purported health benefits.
