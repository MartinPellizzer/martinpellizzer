Dierbachia lilacina

Dierbachia lilacina, commonly known as the purple-flowered dierbachia, is a striking tropical plant native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence composed of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy texture. Its most notable feature is the vibrant purple flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a dramatic visual impact to its appearance.

Dierbachia lilacina thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
