Dictyopteris difformis

Dictyopteris difformis, commonly known as the irregular sea palm, is a species of brown algae belonging to the family Dictyopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its branching, leaf-like fronds that are typically dark green to brown in color. The plant grows in shallow coastal waters, often attached to rocks or other substrates. Its structure consists of a central stipe with numerous lateral fronds, which can vary in shape and size.

Dictyopteris difformis plays an important role in marine ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various marine organisms.
