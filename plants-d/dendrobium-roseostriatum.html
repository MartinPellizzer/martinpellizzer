Dendrobium roseostriatum

Dendrobium roseostriatum, commonly known as the rose-streaked orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome that produces numerous pseudobulbs. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. Its flowers are typically pale yellow with pink or red streaks, arranged in a raceme.

It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
