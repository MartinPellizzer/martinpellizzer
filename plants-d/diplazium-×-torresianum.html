Diplazium × torresianum

Diplazium × torresianum, commonly known as the hybrid sword fern, is a fern species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its sword-shaped fronds that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces sporangia on the underside of its fronds, which are clustered in sori, a common feature among ferns. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Diplazium × torresianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
