Didactyle nemorosa

Didactyle nemorosa, commonly known as the silver vetch, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a legume, belonging to the Fabaceae family, and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and small, pale blue or purple flowers. Didactyle nemorosa is known for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it an important plant in soil enrichment.
