Deutzia micrantha

Deutzia micrantha, commonly known as the dwarf deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features clusters of small, white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, arching branches and dark green foliage that turns yellow in autumn. It typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and prefers well-drained soil and partial shade.

Deutzia micrantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its delicate floral display.
