Deschampsia tzvelevii

Deschampsia tzvelevii, commonly known as Tzvelev's feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to Central Asia.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as steppes and rocky slopes. The plant has slender, upright stems with fine, silver-green leaves that are often hairy. Its inflorescence consists of a narrow, spike-like cluster of small, spikelets.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
