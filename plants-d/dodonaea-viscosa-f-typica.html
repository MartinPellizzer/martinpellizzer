Dodonaea viscosa f. typica

Dodonaea viscosa f. typica, commonly known as the silverleaf honeysuckle, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant has small, greenish-white flowers that appear in clusters, followed by small, woody fruits. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils.

The species is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
