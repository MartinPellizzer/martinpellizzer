Dichapetalum validum

Dichapetalum validum, commonly known as the African butter tree, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by the development of orange-red fruits. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a distinctive waxy coating.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
