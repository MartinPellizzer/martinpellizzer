Deutzia × lemoinei f. compacta

Deutzia × lemoinei f. compacta, commonly known as the compact deutzia, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to China.

It features clusters of white, fragrant flowers that bloom in early summer, adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant has small, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy, providing visual interest throughout the growing season. It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sun, making it adaptable to various garden settings.

Its compact growth habit makes it ideal for borders, rock gardens, or as a specimen plant.
