Delphinium anthriscifolium var. calleryi

Delphinium anthriscifolium var. calleryi, commonly known as the Callery delphinium, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that bear clusters of blue to purple flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value lies in its vibrant blooms and robust growth habit.
