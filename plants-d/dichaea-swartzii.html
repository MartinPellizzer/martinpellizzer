Dichaea swartzii

Dichaea swartzii, commonly known as the Swartz's dichaea, is a terrestrial orchid native to tropical regions of South America.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky crevices. The plant features a short, fleshy rhizome and produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a spike. Its leaves are elongated and often covered in fine pubescence.

Dichaea swartzii is noted for its delicate appearance and is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique botanical characteristics.
