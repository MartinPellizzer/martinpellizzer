Danais latisepala

Danais latisepala, commonly known as the broad-sepal danais, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, showy floral structure. The plant features large, broad sepals that are often brightly colored, contributing to its ornamental value. Its flowers typically bloom during the warmer months, attracting various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
