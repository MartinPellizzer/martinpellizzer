Desmodium album

Desmodium album, commonly known as smallflower velvetbean, is a herbaceous annual plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features trifoliate leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellowish-white flowers. It is often found in disturbed soils and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Its growth habit and morphology make it adaptable to various environmental conditions.
