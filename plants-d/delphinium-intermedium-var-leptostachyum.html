Delphinium intermedium var. leptostachyum

Delphinium intermedium var. leptostachyum, commonly known as the slender-stemmed larkspur, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of the western United States.

It features tall, upright stems that bear clusters of blue to purple flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its botanical characteristics make it a striking addition to gardens with suitable growing conditions.
