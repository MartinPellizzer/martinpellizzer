Dracontium camtschatcense

Dracontium camtschatcense, commonly known as the Kamchatka arrowhead, is a herbaceous plant native to the Russian Far East.

It is characterized by its arrowhead-shaped leaves and is typically found in moist, forested areas. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in the summer. It has a rhizomatous root system that allows it to spread and colonize new areas.

Due to its adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
