Dendrobium capillipes

Dendrobium capillipes, commonly known as the thread-leaf orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with multiple stems that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces slender, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically small and fragrant, occurring in clusters along the stems.

Dendrobium capillipes thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media for optimal growth.
