Diasperus junceus

Diasperus junceus, commonly known as the slender grass-vetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically green or pale yellow in color. It is native to regions of southern Europe and western Asia.

Diasperus junceus is often found in disturbed soils and is adapted to dry, open habitats.
