Dipladenia xanthostoma f. longiloba

Dipladenia xanthostoma f. longiloba, commonly known as the long-stemmed yellow bell, is a hybrid plant that exhibits unique botanical characteristics.

It is a climbing vine with fleshy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green in color. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are yellow with a red throat, blooming in summer. It thrives in warm, sunny conditions and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in hanging baskets or as a climbing plant in gardens.
