Dipteracanthus acaulis

Dipteracanthus acaulis, commonly known as the slender dipterocarp, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small shrub characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces yellowish or greenish flowers that are typically borne in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
