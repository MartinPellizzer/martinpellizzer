Dictyodroma heterophlebia

Dictyodroma heterophlebia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed daisy.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers and narrow, grass-like leaves. Native to southern Africa, it thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant has a compact growth habit and produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring.

It is often found in rocky soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.
