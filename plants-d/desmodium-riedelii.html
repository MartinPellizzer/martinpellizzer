Desmodium riedelii

Desmodium riedelii, commonly known as the purple desmodium, is a perennial legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant produces elliptical, flat pods containing several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas or along roadsides.

Desmodium riedelii is also valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it useful in agricultural settings.
