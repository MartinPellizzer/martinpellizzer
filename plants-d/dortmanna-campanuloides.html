Dortmanna campanuloides

Dortmanna campanuloides, commonly known as the blue-eyed Mary, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Campanulaceae and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly rough texture.

Dortmanna campanuloides is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
