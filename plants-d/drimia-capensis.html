Drimia capensis

Drimia capensis, commonly known as the Cape lily, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are strap-shaped and grass-like, forming a dense rosette.

Drimia capensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
