Davallia inaequalis var. intermedia

Davallia inaequalis var. intermedia, commonly known as the intermediate water fern, is a variegated fern native to Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, leathery fronds with a distinctive pattern of green and white or silver markings. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining, moist soil. Its rhizomes spread horizontally, allowing it to form dense mats in suitable conditions.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and ease of care.
