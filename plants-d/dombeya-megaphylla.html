Dombeya megaphylla

Dombeya megaphylla, commonly known as the large-leaf dombeya, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, which bloom in late summer. The plant features palmately lobed leaves that are often hairy and have a velvety texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Dombeya megaphylla is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
