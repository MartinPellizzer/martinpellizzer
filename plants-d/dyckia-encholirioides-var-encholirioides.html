Dyckia encholirioides var. encholirioides

Dyckia encholirioides var. encholirioides, commonly known as the yellow dyckia, is a perennial bromeliad native to Brazil.

It features compact rosettes of narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bright green with a yellowish or reddish hue. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing small, white or pale blue flowers. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-draining soil.

This species is drought-tolerant and suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.
