Dendranthema japonicum var. wakasaense

Dendranthema japonicum var. wakasaense, commonly known as the Wakasa chrysanthemum, is a variant of the chrysanthemum native to Japan.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and displays a range of flower colors, including white, yellow, and pink. The plant typically blooms in the fall, producing showy, daisy-like flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value and adaptability contribute to its cultivation in horticultural settings.
