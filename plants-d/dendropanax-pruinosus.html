Dendropanax pruinosus

Dendropanax pruinosus, commonly known as the glossy buckler-fern, is a tropical fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, leathery leaves that are often divided into lobes, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest understories. Its fronds are pinnately compound, with a smooth, waxy surface that reflects light.

Dendropanax pruinosus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
