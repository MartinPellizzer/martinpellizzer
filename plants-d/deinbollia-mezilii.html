Deinbollia mezilii

Deinbollia mezilii, commonly known as the Mezilii devil's ivy, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often variegated with white and yellow markings. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Deinbollia mezilii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in hanging baskets or as a ground cover in tropical gardens.
