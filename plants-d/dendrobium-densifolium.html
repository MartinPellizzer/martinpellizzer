Dendrobium densifolium

Dendrobium densifolium, commonly known as the dense-leaf orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic orchid that thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing on trees or rocks. The plant features dense, dark green foliage and produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, adapting it to its natural environment.

Dendrobium densifolium is valued for its ornamental beauty and is commonly cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
