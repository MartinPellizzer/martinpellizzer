Dracaena reflexa var. brevituba

Dracaena reflexa var. brevituba, commonly known as the "Coral Dragon Tree," is a striking evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bright green with a red or orange midrib, creating a dramatic visual effect. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.

Its unique appearance and ease of care contribute to its popularity among plant enthusiasts.
