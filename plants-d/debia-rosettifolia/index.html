Debia Rosettifolia

Debia rosettifolia is a rare and unique plant native to the high-altitude regions of southern China. It is known for its striking rosette-shaped foliage that resembles a compact, lush crown. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or mountainous terrains. Its leaves are thick and waxy, allowing it to store water efficiently. Despite its beauty, Debia rosettifolia remains a mysterious species with limited study and cultivation.
