Dillwynia floribunda var. spinescens

Dillwynia floribunda var. spinescens, commonly known as the spinous dillwynia, is a perennial shrub native to the coastal regions of southern Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, spiny stems and small, pea-like flowers that bloom in shades of pink to purple. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to coastal and Mediterranean climates. Its foliage consists of narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained conditions.
