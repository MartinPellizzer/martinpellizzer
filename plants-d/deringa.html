Deringa

Deringa, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact rosette growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers. The leaves are typically velvety and heart-shaped, while the flowers come in a variety of colors including purple, pink, white, and red. Deringa prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

It is also known for its ability to bloom throughout the year under proper care.
