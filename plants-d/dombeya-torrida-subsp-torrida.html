Dombeya torrida subsp. torrida

Dombeya torrida subsp.

torrida, commonly known as the fiery dombeya, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow.

The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage consists of lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
