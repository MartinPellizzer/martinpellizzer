Dierama elatum

Dierama elatum, commonly known as the flame lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, showy inflorescences composed of numerous tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Dierama elatum is known for its long blooming period, which can last several months during the warmer seasons.
