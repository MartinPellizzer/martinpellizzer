Dendrochilum kennedyi

Dendrochilum kennedyi, commonly known as Kennedy's tree orchid, is a species of orchid native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and the presence of pseudobulbs, which store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Dendrochilum kennedyi is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique botanical features.
