Dieffenbachia seguine subvar. lineata

Dieffenbachia seguine subvar. lineata, commonly known as the Leopard Plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with dark green leaves featuring white and yellow streaks. The plant has thick, fleshy stems and is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions. It is a member of the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like peace lilies.

The leaves are typically lance-shaped and can grow up to 30 cm in length, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
