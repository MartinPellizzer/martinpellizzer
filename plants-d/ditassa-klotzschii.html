Ditassa klotzschii

Ditassa klotzschii, commonly known as the mountain sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

It is a perennial herb with a compact, low-growing habit, typically reaching heights of less than 10 centimeters. The plant features small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters, often appearing in the spring. Its leaves are fleshy and succulent, adapted to conserve water in its arid environment.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.
