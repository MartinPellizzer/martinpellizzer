Dypsis plumosa

Dypsis plumosa, commonly known as the areca palm, is a tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It features a slender, unbranched trunk with a crown of long, arching leaves that give it a feathery appearance. The plant has a fibrous root system that helps anchor it in well-drained soil. Its leaves are compound, with numerous narrow, blade-like segments that contribute to its distinctive look.

Dypsis plumosa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its elegant appearance and ability to thrive in warm, humid environments.
