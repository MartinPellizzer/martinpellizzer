Deguelia angulata

Deguelia angulata, commonly known as the angular deguelia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and pod fruits. The plant features compound leaves with a distinctive angular shape, contributing to its common name. It produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color.
