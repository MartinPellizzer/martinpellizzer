Draconia

Draconia, commonly known as the dragon's blood tree, is a unique succulent plant native to the island of Socotra.

It is characterized by its distinctive umbrella-shaped crown of spiky, dark green leaves. The plant produces a red, resinous sap that has been historically used in traditional medicine and as a dye. Its adaptations to arid environments include a thick trunk for water storage and a slow growth rate.

The botanical features of Draconia make it a fascinating subject for both scientific study and ecological conservation.
