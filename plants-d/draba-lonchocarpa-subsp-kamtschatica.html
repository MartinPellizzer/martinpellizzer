Draba lonchocarpa subsp. kamtschatica

Draba lonchocarpa subsp.

kamtschatica, commonly known as the Kamtschatake Draba, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family. It is a small perennial herb characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers in early spring, typically arranged in a raceme. It is native to the mountainous regions of eastern Siberia and the Russian Far East.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and specialized species.
