Dendrochilum latibrachiatum

Dendrochilum latibrachiatum, commonly known as the broad-armed tree orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces large, showy flowers with a distinctive yellow and green coloration. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Dendrochilum latibrachiatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable conditions.
