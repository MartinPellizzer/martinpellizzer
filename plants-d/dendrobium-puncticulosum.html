Dendrobium puncticulosum

Dendrobium puncticulosum, commonly known as the punctulate dendrobium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant that grows on trees and rocks, often in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features long, cane-like stems covered with dense clusters of small, fragrant flowers. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in bright, indirect light with high humidity.

Dendrobium puncticulosum is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its showy blooms.
