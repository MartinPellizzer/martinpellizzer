Dianthus schousboei

Dianthus schousboei, commonly known as the Schousboe's campion, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or alpine settings.

Dianthus schousboei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its striking floral display.
