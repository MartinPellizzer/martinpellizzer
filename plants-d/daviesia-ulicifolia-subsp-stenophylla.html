Daviesia ulicifolia subsp. stenophylla

Daviesia ulicifolia subsp.

stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf daviesia, is a shrub native to southern Australia. It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its narrow, leathery leaves.

The plant produces yellow flowers in late winter to early spring, which are clustered in dense, cylindrical spikes. It is typically found in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to Mediterranean climates.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in native plant communities.
