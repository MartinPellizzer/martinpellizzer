Dolichandrone filiformis

Dolichandrone filiformis, commonly known as the threadleaf dolichandrone, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, thread-like leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Dolichandrone filiformis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
