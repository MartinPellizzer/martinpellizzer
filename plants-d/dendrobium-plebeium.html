Dendrobium plebeium

Dendrobium plebeium, commonly known as the common dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing on trees or rocks. The plant has a pseudobulb structure that stores water and nutrients, supporting its growth in nutrient-poor environments. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to yellow and pink.

Dendrobium plebeium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated for horticultural purposes.
