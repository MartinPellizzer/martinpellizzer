Dypsis soanieranae

Dypsis soanieranae, commonly known as the Soanierana palm, is a species native to Madagascar.

It is a member of the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its slender, tall trunk and pinnate leaves. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in rainforests. Its foliage is dense, with long, narrow leaflets that give it a feathery appearance.

Dypsis soanieranae is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
