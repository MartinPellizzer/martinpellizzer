Delosperma lehmannii

Delosperma lehmannii, commonly known as the snowflake flower, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent perennial that thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, characterized by its fleshy, water-storing leaves. The plant produces vibrant pink to purple flowers in late spring to early summer, which are clustered in dense, star-shaped inflorescences. Its leaves are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, and the plant is well adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Delosperma lehmannii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
