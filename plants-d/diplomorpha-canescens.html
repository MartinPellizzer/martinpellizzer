Diplomorpha canescens

Diplomorpha canescens, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, white to pale purple flowers and hairy, silver-gray leaves. The plant belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades such as tomatoes and peppers. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in shrubland or woodland habitats.

Diplomorpha canescens is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
