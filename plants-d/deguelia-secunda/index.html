Deguelia Secunda

Deguelia secunda is a striking tropical plant known for its large, heart-shaped leaves and unique, spiny fruit. Native to Central and South America, it thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in rainforests. The plant has a long history of use by indigenous peoples for medicinal and ritual purposes. Its dramatic appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardens. Despite its beauty, it contains toxic compounds that require caution when handling.
