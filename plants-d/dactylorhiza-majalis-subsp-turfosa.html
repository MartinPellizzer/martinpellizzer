Dactylorhiza majalis subsp. turfosa

Dactylorhiza majalis subsp.

turfosa, commonly known as the Turf Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Europe and northwest Africa. It typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodland floors and meadows.

The plant features a slender, upright stem with several narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are pale green to brownish, with a distinctive hooded labellum, and bloom from late spring to early summer.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance.
