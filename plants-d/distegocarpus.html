Distegocarpus

Distegocarpus, commonly known as the African star apple, is a tropical tree belonging to the Sapotaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers. It is valued not only for its edible fruit but also for its ornamental appeal and potential use in traditional medicine.
