Drosanthemum papillatum

Drosanthemum papillatum, commonly known as the woolly rock rose, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a unique, fluffy appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Drosanthemum papillatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
