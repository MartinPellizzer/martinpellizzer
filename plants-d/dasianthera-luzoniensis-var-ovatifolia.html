Dasianthera luzoniensis var. ovatifolia

Dasianthera luzoniensis var. ovatifolia, commonly known as the Philippine flat-leaf orchid, is a rare orchid species native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its ovate-shaped leaves and small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in montane forests. Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and limited distribution, making it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered vulnerable, highlighting the need for its protection.
