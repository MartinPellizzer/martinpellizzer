Delissea subcordata subsp. obtusifolia

Delissea subcordata subsp.

obtusifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved delissea, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It features basal leaves that are broadly ovate to subcordate in shape, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The plant produces small, white to pale blue flowers arranged in dense, terminal clusters. Its flowering period typically occurs during the summer months, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

It is often found in open grasslands and rocky habitats.
