Daviesia incrassata subsp. teres

Daviesia incrassata subsp.

teres, commonly known as the dense-flowered Daviesia, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia. It features narrow, leathery leaves that are typically arranged in a spiral pattern along its stems.

The plant produces dense clusters of small, yellow to orange flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in heathlands and open woodlands.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting native wildlife.
