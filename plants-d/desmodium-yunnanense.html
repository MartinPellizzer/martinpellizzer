Desmodium yunnanense

Desmodium yunnanense, commonly known as Yunnan desmodium, is a flowering plant native to China, particularly Yunnan Province.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and nitrogen-fixing capabilities. The plant features trifoliate leaves with small, rounded leaflets and produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. It is often found in grasslands and open woodlands, thriving in well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and restoration projects.
