Dyckia inconspicua

Dyckia inconspicua, commonly known as the small dyckia, is a compact bromeliad native to Brazil.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that form a rosette, with the foliage often tinged in red or purple hues. The plant produces small, white flowers on a central stem, which bloom in late summer. It is well adapted to shady, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dyckia inconspicua is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.
