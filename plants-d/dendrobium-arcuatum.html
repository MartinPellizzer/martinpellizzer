Dendrobium arcuatum

Dendrobium arcuatum, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant that grows on trees or rocks, utilizing its aerial roots to absorb moisture and nutrients from the environment. The plant produces long, arching stems with clustered flowers that are typically yellow or green with red or purple markings. Its unique growth habit and striking floral display make it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation.

Dendrobium arcuatum thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining potting media for optimal growth.
