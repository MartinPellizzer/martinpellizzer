Dioscorea deltoidea var. orbiculata

Dioscorea deltoidea var. orbiculata, commonly known as the round-leaf yam, is a variant of the yam genus native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its rounded, heart-shaped leaves and produces tuberous roots that are used as food in some cultures. The plant is a climbing vine with hairy stems and small, greenish-white flowers. Its tubers are typically harvested for their starchy, edible flesh.

The species is valued both for its nutritional content and traditional medicinal uses.
