Dendrobium calcariferum

Dendrobium calcariferum, commonly known as the dragon tree orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, branching stem covered in dense, leathery leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a red or purple throat. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Dendrobium calcariferum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
