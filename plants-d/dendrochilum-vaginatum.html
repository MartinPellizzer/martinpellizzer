Dendrochilum vaginatum

Dendrochilum vaginatum, commonly known as the sheath orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive sheathing leaf bases that enclose the stem, giving it its common name. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in a raceme. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Dendrochilum vaginatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
