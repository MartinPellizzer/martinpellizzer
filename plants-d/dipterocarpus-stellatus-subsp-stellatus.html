Dipterocarpus stellatus subsp. stellatus

Dipterocarpus stellatus subsp.

stellatus, commonly known as the star-flowered dipterocarp, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped flowers, which are typically white or pale yellow.

The plant belongs to the family Dipterocarpaceae and is known for its hard, durable wood. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The species plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, supporting a variety of flora and fauna.
