Dendranthema yoshinaganthum

Dendranthema yoshinaganthum, commonly known as the Yoshinaga chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are deeply lobed and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often hairy as well.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
