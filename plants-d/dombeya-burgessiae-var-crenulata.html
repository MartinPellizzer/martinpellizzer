Dombeya burgessiae var. crenulata

Dombeya burgessiae var. crenulata, commonly known as the serrated dombeya, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often appearing in clusters. The leaves are typically narrow and serrated, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.

This plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive blooms.
