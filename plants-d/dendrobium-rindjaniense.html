Dendrobium rindjaniense

Dendrobium rindjaniense, commonly known as the Rindjani orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to the island of Flores in Indonesia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect pseudobulbs and long, narrow leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with white markings, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium rindjaniense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
