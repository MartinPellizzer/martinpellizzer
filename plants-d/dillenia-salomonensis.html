Dillenia salomonensis

Dillenia salomonensis, commonly known as Solomon's dillenia, is a tropical plant native to the Solomon Islands.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves. The plant produces showy, yellowish-white flowers that are typically clustered in terminal inflorescences. Its fruits are fleshy, berry-like structures that contain several seeds.

Dillenia salomonensis is often found in moist, shaded forest understories and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
