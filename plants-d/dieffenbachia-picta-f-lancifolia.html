Dieffenbachia picta f. lancifolia

Dieffenbachia picta f. lancifolia, commonly known as the Shining Dieffenbachia, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions. Its foliage is highly ornamental, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

However, it is important to note that the plant is toxic to pets and humans due to the presence of calcium oxalate crystals.
