Dipladenia gentianoides var. velutina

Dipladenia gentianoides var. velutina, commonly known as the velvetleaf Dipladenia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The foliage is velvety to the touch, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in warm, sunny conditions and is often cultivated as a ornamental plant.

The plant produces showy blooms throughout the growing season, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets and containers.
