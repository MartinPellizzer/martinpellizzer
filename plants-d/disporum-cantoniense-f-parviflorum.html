Disporum cantoniense f. parviflorum

Disporum cantoniense f. parviflorum, commonly known as the small-flowered disporum, is a botanical variant of the species Disporum cantoniense.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia, particularly China. The plant features slender, often arching stems that bear small, clustered white or pale green flowers. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in whorls around the stem.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden environments.
