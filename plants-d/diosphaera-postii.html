Diosphaera postii

Diosphaera postii, commonly known as the African horned date palm, is a rare and unique plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, palm-like appearance with a thick trunk and a crown of feathery leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into edible dates, though it is not as widely cultivated as other date palms. Diosphaera postii thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it well adapted to drought conditions.
