Draba Hirta Var. Pycnosperma

Draba hirta var. pycnosperma is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It thrives in high-altitude environments where few species can survive due to extreme climatic conditions. This variant is distinguished by its dense, hairy leaves and small, clustered flowers that bloom in early spring. Its scientific name reflects both its species and the unique characteristics of its seed structure. Despite its hardiness, it remains a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
