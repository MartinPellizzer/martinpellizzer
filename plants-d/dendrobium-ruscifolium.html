Dendrobium ruscifolium

Dendrobium ruscifolium, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with numerous cylindrical pseudobulbs covered in a fibrous sheath. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with a distinctive red or purple lip. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Dendrobium ruscifolium is appreciated for its ornamental value and is commonly cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
