Dianella sparsiflora var. pullei

Dianella sparsiflora var. pullei, commonly known as the slender flax lily, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to New Zealand.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color, forming a dense rosette. The plant produces small, white to pale blue flowers on slender stalks during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
