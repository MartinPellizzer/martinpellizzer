Dierama medium

Dierama medium, commonly known as the umbrella plant, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features a distinctive rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a dense, umbrella-like shape. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, or white, which bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance make it a desirable ornamental plant.
