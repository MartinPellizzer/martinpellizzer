Dendrobium klabatense

Dendrobium klabatense, commonly known as the Klabat orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to the Philippines.

It features a sympodial growth habit with multiple pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium klabatense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
