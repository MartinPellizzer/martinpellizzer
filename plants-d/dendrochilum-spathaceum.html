Dendrochilum spathaceum

Dendrochilum spathaceum, commonly known as the spathaceous tree orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, branching structure and produces large, showy flowers with a distinctive spatulate shape. The plant typically grows in tropical rainforests, often as an epiphyte on trees. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with a deep red or purple lip, making it visually striking.

Dendrochilum spathaceum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
