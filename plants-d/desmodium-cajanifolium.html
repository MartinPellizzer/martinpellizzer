Desmodium cajanifolium

Desmodium cajanifolium, commonly known as velvet bean or cowpea, is a leguminous plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which are often covered in a fine, velvety pubescence. The plant produces small, yellow to reddish-pink flowers that are typically clustered in racemes. Its seeds are enclosed in pods and are used in various culinary and medicinal applications.

Desmodium cajanifolium is also known for its nitrogen-fixing ability, which benefits soil fertility.
