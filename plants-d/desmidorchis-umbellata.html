Desmidorchis umbellata

Desmidorchis umbellata, commonly known as the umbellate orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, or purple. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually broad and grass-like, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Desmidorchis umbellata is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and is often found in forests or near water sources.
