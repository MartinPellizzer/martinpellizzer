Duguetia rigida

Duguetia rigida, commonly known as the Brazilian flame tree, is a tropical tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has compound leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun for optimal growth.

Duguetia rigida is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
