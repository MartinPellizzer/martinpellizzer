Daviesia laevis

Daviesia laevis, commonly known as the smooth daviesia, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its smooth, grey-green foliage. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and rocky areas.

Daviesia laevis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing habitat and nectar for native wildlife.
