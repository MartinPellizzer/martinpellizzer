Dendrobium schinzianum

Dendrobium schinzianum, commonly known as the Schinz's orchid, is a sympodial epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact growth habit with multiple pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a distinctive red or orange lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates.

Dendrobium schinzianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
