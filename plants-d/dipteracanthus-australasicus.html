Dipteracanthus australasicus

Dipteracanthus australasicus, commonly known as the Australian star-anther orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia and Australia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped anthers and showy flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understoreys. Its floral structure includes a single, prominent spur that extends from the base of the flower.

Dipteracanthus australasicus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
