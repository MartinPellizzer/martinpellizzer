Dendrobium moniliforme subsp. okinawense

Dendrobium moniliforme subsp.

okinawense, commonly known as the Okinawan orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to southern Japan and the Ryukyu Islands. It features long, cane-like pseudobulbs that support its evergreen foliage and clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers.

The plant typically blooms during the summer months, producing showy, pendant-like inflorescences. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
