Dendrochilum ramosissimum

Dendrochilum ramosissimum, commonly known as the branched lip orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with distinctive lip structures. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its pseudobulbs are short and stout, supporting the foliage and flowers.

Dendrochilum ramosissimum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
