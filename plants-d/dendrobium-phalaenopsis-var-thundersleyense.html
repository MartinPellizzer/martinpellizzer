Dendrobium phalaenopsis var. thundersleyense

Dendrobium phalaenopsis var. thundersleyense, commonly known as the Thunderley's orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, cane-like stems that can grow up to several meters in height, often producing clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The blooms are typically white or pale yellow with intricate patterns and a subtle, sweet fragrance. This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its beauty in both gardens and indoor settings.

It requires bright, indirect light and consistent humidity to thrive.
