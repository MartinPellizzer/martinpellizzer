Dracontium loretense

Dracontium loretense, commonly known as the Loretto arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. Its rhizomes are edible and have a starchy texture similar to yam.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
