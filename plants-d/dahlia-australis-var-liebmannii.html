Dahlia australis var. liebmannii

Dahlia australis var. liebmannii, commonly known as the Liebmann's dahlia, is a herbaceous perennial native to South America.

It features showy, single or double flowers in a range of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant has tuberous roots and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure. Its foliage consists of lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are slightly hairy.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
