Dilophia purii

Dilophia purii, commonly known as the narrow-leaved daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically white or pale yellow, with a central disk surrounded by ray florets.

Dilophia purii is often found in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils.
