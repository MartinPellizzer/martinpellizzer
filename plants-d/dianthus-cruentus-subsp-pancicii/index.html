Dianthus Cruentus Subsp. Pancicii

Dianthus cruentus subsp. pancicii is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans. Known for its vibrant red flowers and distinctive foliage, it captivates botanists and gardeners alike. This subspecies is often found in rocky and alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest in conservation efforts. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated and poorly studied in many regions.
