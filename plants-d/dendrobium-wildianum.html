Dendrobium wildianum

Dendrobium wildianum, commonly known as the wildian orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, cane-like stems that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink, typically during the summer months. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Dendrobium wildianum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
