Draba gilgiana

Draba gilgiana, commonly known as the Gilgil dwarf cabbage, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in alpine and arctic regions. The plant has basal leaves that are narrow and linear, with small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in a compact inflorescence. It is adapted to cold climates and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils.

Draba gilgiana is known for its resilience and is a characteristic species of high-altitude ecosystems.
