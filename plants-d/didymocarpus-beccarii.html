Didymocarpus beccarii

Didymocarpus beccarii, commonly known as the Bornean starfruit, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly Borneo.

The plant produces small, yellow, star-like berries that are edible when ripe, though they are not as commonly consumed as the more familiar common starfruit. The leaves are alternate, simple, and have a velvety texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal.
