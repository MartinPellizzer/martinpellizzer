Dais pubescens

Dais pubescens, commonly known as the hairy daisy, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other composite flowers. The plant features showy white to pale yellow flowers surrounded by pale green ray florets, with a central disc of yellow tubular flowers. Its leaves are typically hairy and gray-green in color, and it thrives in dry, rocky soils.

Dais pubescens is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and sagebrush steppe.
