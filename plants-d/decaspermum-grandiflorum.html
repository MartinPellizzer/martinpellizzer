Decaspermum grandiflorum

Decaspermum grandiflorum, commonly known as the largeflower decaspermum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, with woody stems and dense foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly rough texture.
