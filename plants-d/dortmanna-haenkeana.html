Dortmanna haenkeana

Dortmanna haenkeana, commonly known as the Andes rock cress, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

It is a small, herbaceous species that typically grows in rocky or alpine environments. The plant features basal leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters. It is adapted to cold, nutrient-poor soils and is often found in moist, shaded habitats.
