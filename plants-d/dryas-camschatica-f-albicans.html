Dryas camschatica f. albicans

Dryas camschatica f. albicans, commonly known as the white-flowered mountain avens, is a rare and striking variant of the mountain avens species.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant is native to alpine and subalpine regions of Europe and is adapted to harsh, cold environments.
