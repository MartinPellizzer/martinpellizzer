Darniella verticillata

Darniella verticillata, commonly known as the clustered dwarf lily, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 10-20 centimeters. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of delicate, pinkish-white flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Darniella verticillata is valued for its ornamental appeal and easy care requirements.
