Deschampsia foliosa

Deschampsia foliosa, commonly known as slender wheatgrass, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides, meadows, and sandy soils. The plant has fine, needle-like leaves that are often blue-green in color, and it produces slender, spike-like inflorescences. It is well adapted to dry conditions and can thrive in full sun.

Deschampsia foliosa is often used in restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and tolerate various environmental conditions.
