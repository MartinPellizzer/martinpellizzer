Dendrobium heterobulbum

Dendrobium heterobulbum, commonly known as the golden lip orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with a dense cluster of leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant produces racemes of yellow-orange flowers with a distinctive lip that is often adorned with purple or red markings. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrobium heterobulbum is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.
