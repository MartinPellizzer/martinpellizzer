Deiregyne obtusa

Deiregyne obtusa, commonly known as the broad-leaved dandelion, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of broad, waxy leaves and a single, tall flowering stem that bears a composite flower head. Deiregyne obtusa is a hardy perennial that thrives in a variety of soil types and is relatively easy to cultivate.
