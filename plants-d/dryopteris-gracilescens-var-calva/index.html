Dryopteris Gracilescens Var. Calva

Dryopteris gracilescens var. calva is a rare and delicate fern native to the mountainous regions of western North America. It is known for its slender, pale-green fronds that contrast strikingly with its dark, rugged habitat. This variant of the common wood fern exhibits unique morphological traits that set it apart from its relatives. Its limited distribution and fragile growth habits make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Protecting this rare plant is essential for preserving the biodiversity of its high-altitude ecosystems.
