Diplomorpha × ohsumiensis

Diplomorpha × ohsumiensis, commonly known as the hybrid Diplomorpha, is a rare and cultivated plant resulting from the cross between two species of the Diplomorpha genus.

It is characterized by its unique floral structure, which combines features from both parent species, often displaying a blend of colors and shapes. The plant typically thrives in well-drained soil and requires a sunny to partially shaded environment. Its foliage is usually dense and aromatic, contributing to its ornamental value.

Due to its rarity and specific care requirements, it is often grown in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
