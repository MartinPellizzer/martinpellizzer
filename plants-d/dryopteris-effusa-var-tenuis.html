Dryopteris effusa var. tenuis

Dryopteris effusa var. tenuis, commonly known as the slender sword fern, is a variant of the broad shield fern.

It is a perennial evergreen fern native to eastern North America, typically found in moist, shaded woodlands. The plant features slender, arching fronds with a fine, delicate texture, and its foliage is often a deep green. It produces small, inconspicuous spore-bearing structures on the underside of its fronds.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.
