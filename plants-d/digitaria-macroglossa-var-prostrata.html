Digitaria macroglossa var. prostrata

Digitaria macroglossa var. prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate digitaria, is a low-growing grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats. It is characterized by its prostrate growth habit, spreading along the ground through rhizomes.

Botanically, it is notable for its adaptability and role in stabilizing soil in various ecosystems.
