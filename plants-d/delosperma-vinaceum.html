Delosperma vinaceum

Delosperma vinaceum, commonly known as the red-flowered ice plant, is a succulent perennial native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems and produces vibrant pink to red flowers in late spring to early summer. The plant has small, lobed leaves that are adapted to store water, typical of xerophytic plants. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
