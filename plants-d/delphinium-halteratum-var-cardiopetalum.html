Delphinium halteratum var. cardiopetalum

Delphinium halteratum var. cardiopetalum, commonly known as the heart-leaved larkspur, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It features tall, sparsely branched stems that bear clusters of blue to purple flowers. The leaves are palmately divided, with a distinctive heart-shaped base that gives the plant its common name. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

The plant is part of the Ranunculaceae family and is known for its showy, showy floral display.
