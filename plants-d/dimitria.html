Dimitria

Dimitria, commonly known as the silverleaf dittany, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features small, pale blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are silver-gray in color, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Dimitria is often cultivated for its ornamental value and traditional medicinal uses.
