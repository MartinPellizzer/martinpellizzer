Dendrobium amesianum

Dendrobium amesianum, commonly known as the mountain orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a pseudobulb and long, fleshy roots that store water. The plant produces upright, lance-shaped leaves and bears clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-draining soil.

Dendrobium amesianum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
