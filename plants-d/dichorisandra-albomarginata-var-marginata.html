Dichorisandra albomarginata var. marginata

Dichorisandra albomarginata var. marginata, commonly known as the white-edged dichorisandra, is a striking evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with distinct white margins, which give it a unique and ornamental appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white flowers that bloom in spring and summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a versatile choice for gardens.

Its compact growth habit and year-round visual interest make it a popular ornamental plant in tropical and subtropical regions.
