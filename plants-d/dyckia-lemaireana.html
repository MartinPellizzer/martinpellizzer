Dyckia lemaireana

Dyckia lemaireana, commonly known as the Lemaire's Dyckia, is a species of flowering plant in the bromeliad family.

It is native to southern Brazil and is characterized by its rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of white, powdery bloom. The plant produces striking orange-red inflorescences that emerge from the center of the rosette. It is a succulent, adapted to thrive in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a desirable ornamental plant.
