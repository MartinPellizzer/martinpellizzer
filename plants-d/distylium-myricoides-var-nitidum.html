Distylium myricoides var. nitidum

Distylium myricoides var. nitidum, commonly known as the glossy leaf distylium, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. The plant produces tiny, yellowish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically pollinated by insects. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
