Discaria exilis

Discaria exilis, commonly known as the slender discaria, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, leathery leaves and small, pale yellow to white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, round, woody fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in rocky or coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils.

Discaria exilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
