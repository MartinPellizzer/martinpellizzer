Dendrosenecio keniodendron

Dendrosenecio keniodendron, commonly known as the Kenia tree, is a unique member of the Asteraceae family.

It is native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa, where it thrives in semi-arid regions. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in composite heads, and it produces a distinctive, apple-like fruit.
