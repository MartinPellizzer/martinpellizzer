Dianthus giganteiformis subsp. pontederae

Dianthus giganteiformis subsp.

pontederae, commonly known as the Pontedera dianthus, is a striking ornamental plant native to southern Italy. It is characterized by its tall, branching stems and large, showy flowers that range from pink to red in color.

The plant features finely divided, fern-like leaves that add texture to its appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its botanical beauty makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking vibrant, long-lasting blooms.
