Dendrosenecio cheranganiensis subsp. cheranganiensis

Dendrosenecio cheranganiensis subsp.

cheranganiensis, commonly known as the Cherangani giant daisies, is a striking member of the Asteraceae family native to Kenya. It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in dense, daisy-like clusters.

The plant typically grows in open grasslands and is adapted to the arid and semi-arid regions of East Africa. Its sturdy, woody stems and hairy leaves help it conserve water in its harsh environment.

This subspecies is notable for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
