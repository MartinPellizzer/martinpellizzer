Dryandra kippistiana var. paenepeccata

Dryandra kippistiana var. paenepeccata, commonly known as the Narrow-leaved Dryandra, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small, clustered white or pink flowers. The plant belongs to the Proteaceae family and is adapted to nutrient-poor soils. It typically grows in heathland and woodland habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after species for horticultural use.
