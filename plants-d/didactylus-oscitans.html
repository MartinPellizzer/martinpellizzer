Didactylus oscitans

Didactylus oscitans, commonly known as the hanging basket plant, is a tropical species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often used in ornamental gardening. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Didactylus oscitans is popular for its aesthetic appeal and ability to cascade from hanging baskets or shelves.
