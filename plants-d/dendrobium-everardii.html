Dendrobium everardii

Dendrobium everardii, commonly known as the Everard's orchid, is a sympodial epiphytic orchid native to Australia.

It features a tall, slender stem with numerous horizontal pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers with a distinctive yellow lip. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium everardii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in both greenhouse and outdoor settings.
