Dipterocarpus lemeslei

Dipterocarpus lemeslei, commonly known as the Lemesle dipterocarp, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dipterocarpaceae and is characterized by its large, heavy seeds and buttressed roots. The tree can grow up to 40 meters tall and has a straight trunk with a smooth, greyish-brown bark. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptic in shape, often with a slightly waxy texture.

The plant plays an important ecological role in tropical rainforests, providing habitat and food for various species.
