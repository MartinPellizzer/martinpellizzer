Dimorphorchis lyonii

Dimorphorchis lyonii, commonly known as the Lyon's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit and produces a single, showy floral spike. The plant features elongated, lanceolate leaves that are often covered in a fine, pubescent layer. Its flowers are typically pale green to yellowish with intricate patterns, and they bloom during the summer months.

Dimorphorchis lyonii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
