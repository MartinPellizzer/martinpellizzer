Didelotia africana

Didelotia africana, commonly known as the African mountain orchid, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, showy flowers that typically bloom in late spring or early summer. The plant is perennial and thrives in rocky, well-drained soils, often growing in shaded or semi-shaded environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its roots are fibrous and adapted to its rocky habitat.

Didelotia africana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
