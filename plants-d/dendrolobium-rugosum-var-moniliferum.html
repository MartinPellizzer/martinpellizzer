Dendrolobium rugosum var. moniliferum

Dendrolobium rugosum var. moniliferum, commonly known as the prickly burr legume, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its woody stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from yellow to red. Its distinctive feature is the development of spiny, pod-like fruits that persist on the plant for an extended period.

This species is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient component of native vegetation.
