Ditassa bonariensis

Ditassa bonariensis, commonly known as the Buenos Aires spurge, is a herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides, thriving in full sun.

Its botanical characteristics make it both ornamental and potentially invasive in certain regions.
