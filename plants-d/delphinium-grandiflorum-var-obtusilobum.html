Delphinium grandiflorum var. obtusilobum

Delphinium grandiflorum var. obtusilobum, commonly known as the blunt-lobed larkspur, is a herbaceous perennial native to the western United States.

It features tall, sparsely branched stems that bear clusters of striking blue to purple flowers. The leaves are lance-shaped with rounded lobes, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
