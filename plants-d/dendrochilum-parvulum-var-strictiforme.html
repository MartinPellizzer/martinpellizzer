Dendrochilum parvulum var. strictiforme

Dendrochilum parvulum var. strictiforme, commonly known as the narrow-leafed tree orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in tropical forests.

This variant is noted for its unique floral structure and is valued in both horticultural and botanical collections.
