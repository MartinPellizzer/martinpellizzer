Dichanthium kuntzeanum

Dichanthium kuntzeanum, commonly known as slender false brome, is a grass species native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It is often found in open grasslands and disturbed soils, thriving in dry to semi-arid conditions.

Dichanthium kuntzeanum plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing forage for livestock in its native habitats.
