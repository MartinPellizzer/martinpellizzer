Dodonaea deflexa

Dodonaea deflexa, commonly known as the weeping Dodonaea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its drooping, pendulous branches that give it a distinctive, cascading appearance. The plant features small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are yellowish-green and appear in clusters, followed by small, woody fruits.

It is adapted to dry conditions and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.
