Dendrobium fuscopilosum

Dendrobium fuscopilosum, commonly known as the dark-haired orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome that produces multiple shoots. The plant has dark green, lance-shaped leaves and produces pendulous inflorescences with small, pale green to yellow flowers. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Dendrobium fuscopilosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
