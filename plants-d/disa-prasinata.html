Disa prasinata

Disa prasinata, commonly known as the green disa, is a species of orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit and features striking green and white flowers. The plant typically thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in rocky areas or along stream banks. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in a fine, velvety texture.

Disa prasinata is valued not only for its ornamental appeal but also for its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
