Darwinia wittwerorum

Darwinia wittwerorum, commonly known as the Wittenberg darwinia, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, showy inflorescences. The plant produces spikes of tubular flowers that are typically pink to red in color. It is adapted to grow in dry, sandy soils and is often found in open woodlands.

Darwinia wittwerorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
