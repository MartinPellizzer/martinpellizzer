Draba × wichurae

Draba × wichurae, commonly known as the hybrid rockcress, is a naturally occurring hybrid between Draba nemorosa and Draba cymbifera.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches heights of 5–15 cm. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers in early spring, usually between March and May. Its leaves are basal, rosette-forming, and covered in fine, hairy structures.

Draba × wichurae is often found in alpine and subalpine regions, thriving in rocky soils and open habitats.
