Delphinium handelianum

Delphinium handelianum, commonly known as the Alpine larkspur, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Western Asia.

It features tall, slender stems that bear clusters of blue to violet, spurred flowers, which are characteristic of the Delphinium genus. The plant has lance-shaped, hairy leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained, loamy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Delphinium handelianum is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
