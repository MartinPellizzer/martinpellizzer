Dorstenia morifolia

Dorstenia morifolia, commonly known as the giant leaf fig, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in diameter. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dorstenia morifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage.
