Dioscorea orthogoneura var. brevispicata

Dioscorea orthogoneura var. brevispicata, commonly known as the short-spined yam, is a climbing vine native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its tuberous roots, which are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped with a rough texture and short spines along the veins.

This variety is distinguished by its shorter spines compared to other varieties of Dioscorea orthogoneura.
