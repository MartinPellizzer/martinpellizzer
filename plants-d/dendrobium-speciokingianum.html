Dendrobium speciokingianum

Dendrobium speciokingianum, commonly known as the Kingian Dendrobium, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant that grows on trees or rocks, absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in dry periods.

It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect sunlight and well-draining potting media.
