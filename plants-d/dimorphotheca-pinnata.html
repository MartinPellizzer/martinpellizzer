Dimorphotheca pinnata

Dimorphotheca pinnata, commonly known as the yellow starbursts, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers with a distinctive star-shaped appearance. The plant has hairy, alternate leaves that are typically pinnately divided, giving it a feathery appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in coastal and sandy environments.

Dimorphotheca pinnata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
