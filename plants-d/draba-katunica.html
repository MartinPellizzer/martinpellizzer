Draba katunica

Draba katunica, commonly known as the alpine mouse-ear cress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of up to 15 centimeters. The plant features basal leaves that are lobed and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. Its flowers are small, yellowish-white, and arranged in a raceme, blooming in early spring.

Draba katunica is commonly found in alpine and subalpine regions, often growing on rocky slopes and gravelly soils.
