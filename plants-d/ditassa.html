Ditassa

Ditassa, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as a perennial herb, often with a compact rosette structure. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
