Disterigma ulei

Disterigma ulei, commonly known as the African snowball, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of white flowers that bloom in winter. The plant has small, leathery leaves and produces small, oval-shaped fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Disterigma ulei is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
