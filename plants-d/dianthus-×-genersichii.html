Dianthus × genersichii

Dianthus × genersichii, commonly known as the hybrid carnation, is a flowering plant in the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is a cross between Dianthus chinensis and Dianthus barbatus, resulting in a variety with diverse flower colors and forms. The plant typically features showy, clustered flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, with prominent central stamens. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and as a cut flower.

Dianthus × genersichii prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a versatile addition to many landscapes.
