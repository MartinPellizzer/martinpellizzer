Dichrocephala latifolia

Dichrocephala latifolia, commonly known as broadleaf dichrocephala, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense, terminal clusters. It is often found in moist, open habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges.
