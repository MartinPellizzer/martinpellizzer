Donia lanceolata

Donia lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceleaf donia, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
