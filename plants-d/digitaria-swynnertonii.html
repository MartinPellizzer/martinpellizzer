Digitaria swynnertonii

Digitaria swynnertonii, commonly known as Swynnerton's Digitaria, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open grasslands and disturbed soils, exhibiting a clump-forming growth habit. The plant has slender, wiry stems and narrow, grass-like leaves with a bluish-green color. Its inflorescence consists of a dense cluster of spikelets, which are usually pale green to brown in color.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its hardy nature.
