Dumerilia sericea

Dumerilia sericea, commonly known as the silky dumerilia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its smooth, silvery bark and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and white, arranged in clusters, and it produces edible, fleshy fruits that are yellow when ripe.

The plant is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
