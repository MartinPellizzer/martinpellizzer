Dicranodium leptophyllum

Dicranodium leptophyllum, commonly known as the slender feather moss, is a species of moss that belongs to the family Dicranaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, hair-like leaves that are typically arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest floors or on rocky substrates. Its small, compact growth form allows it to colonize areas where other plants may not establish.

Dicranodium leptophyllum plays an important ecological role in soil stabilization and moisture retention in its habitat.
