Diasperus pentandrus

Diasperus pentandrus, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Gesneriaceae and is characterized by its compact rosette growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. Diasperus pentandrus is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
