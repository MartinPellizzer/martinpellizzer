Duretia paraspicata

Duretia paraspicata, commonly known as the spiny duretia, is a flowering plant native to the southern regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and often covered with fine, stiff hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
