Doryopteris lorentzii f. juergensii

Doryopteris lorentzii f. juergensii, commonly known as the Juergens' rock fern, is a fern species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage. The fronds are typically leathery and have a distinctive, finely divided structure with a smooth, dark green surface. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found growing on rock surfaces or in rocky crevices.

This fern is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
