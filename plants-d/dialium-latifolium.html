Dialium latifolium

Dialium latifolium, commonly known as the African cherry or broadleaf dialium, is a tropical tree native to Africa.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by woody, spiny capsules containing numerous seeds. Its bark and leaves have been used in traditional medicine for their medicinal properties.

The plant is also valued for its hard, durable wood used in construction and furniture-making.
