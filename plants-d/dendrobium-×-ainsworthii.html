Dendrobium × ainsworthii

Dendrobium × ainsworthii, commonly known as the orchid hybrid, is a epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with cane-like stems that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in a variety of colors, typically white, yellow, or pink. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium × ainsworthii is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
