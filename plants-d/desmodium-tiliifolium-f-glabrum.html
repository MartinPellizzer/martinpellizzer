Desmodium tiliifolium f. glabrum

Desmodium tiliifolium f. glabrum, commonly known as the smooth leaved desmodium, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which are typically smooth and glabrous, giving it its specific epithet "f. glabrum.".

The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or disturbed areas.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
