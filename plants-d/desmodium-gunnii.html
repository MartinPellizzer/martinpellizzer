Desmodium gunnii

Desmodium gunnii, commonly known as the silverleaf desmodium, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is a legume, belonging to the Fabaceae family, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant is often found in grasslands and disturbed soils, thriving in full sun to partial shade. Its seeds are enclosed in pods and can remain viable in the soil for several years.

Desmodium gunnii is also known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil fertility.
