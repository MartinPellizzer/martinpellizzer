Dryopsis obtusiloba

Dryopsis obtusiloba, commonly known as the narrow-leaved dryopsis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves.
