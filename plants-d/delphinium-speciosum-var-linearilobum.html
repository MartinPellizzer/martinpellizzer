Delphinium speciosum var. linearilobum

Delphinium speciosum var. linearilobum, commonly known as the slender-lobed larkspur, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its tall, spindly stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The plant features large, showy blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer, arranged in racemes. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a distinctive linear lobing at the base.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens and its ecological importance as a food source for certain pollinators.
