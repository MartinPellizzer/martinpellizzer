Ditassa ericoides

Ditassa ericoides, commonly known as the Cape heath, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. The plant is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage and small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is cultivated in gardens in regions with a Mediterranean climate.
