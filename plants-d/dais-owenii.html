Dais owenii

Dais owenii, commonly known as the Owen's daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, white to pale yellow ray florets surrounded by yellow disk florets. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It produces small, oval-shaped seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Dais owenii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
