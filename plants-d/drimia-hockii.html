Drimia hockii

Drimia hockii, commonly known as the Hockii dragon's tongue, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a thick, fleshy rhizome and produces clusters of narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. The plant blooms with showy, star-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale purple, arranged in umbels. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Drimia hockii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.
