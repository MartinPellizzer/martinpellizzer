Dalea thouinii

Dalea thouinii, commonly known as the Texas milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of 10 to 20 centimeters. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces clusters of pink to purple pea-like flowers. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Dalea thouinii is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and along roadsides.
