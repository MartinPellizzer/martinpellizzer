Ditaxis serrata var. magdalenae

Ditaxis serrata var. magdalenae, commonly known as the Magdalenae Ditaxis, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, narrow leaves that are often serrated, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its flowers are followed by small, round, woody fruits.

It is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
