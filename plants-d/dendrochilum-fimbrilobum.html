Dendrochilum fimbrilobum

Dendrochilum fimbrilobum, commonly known as the feather-lip tree orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and large, showy flowers with intricate, feather-like lip structures. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in its natural habitat.

The orchid's flowers are often fragrant and attract various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
