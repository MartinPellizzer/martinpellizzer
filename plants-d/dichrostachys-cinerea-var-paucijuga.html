Dichrostachys cinerea var. paucijuga

Dichrostachys cinerea var. paucijuga, commonly known as the narrow-leaf burr legume, is a variant of the African burr legume native to parts of Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree characterized by its slender, often twisted branches and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces distinctive, spiny seed pods that contain small, hard seeds. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it well adapted to dry climates.

The species is valued for its potential in soil stabilization and as a forage plant in certain regions.
