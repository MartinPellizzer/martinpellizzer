Diplazium ternatum

Diplazium ternatum, commonly known as the three-leaf fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Davalliaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive fronds that are divided into three segments, giving it its common name. Diplazium ternatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
