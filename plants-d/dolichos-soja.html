Dolichos soja

Dolichos soja, commonly known as hyacinth bean, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and trifoliate leaves, which are typically heart-shaped. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, which are often used in culinary and medicinal applications. Its flowers are usually pale purple or white, and it thrives in warm climates with well-drained soil.

Dolichos soja is valued for its nutritional content and ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.
