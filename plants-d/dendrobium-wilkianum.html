Dendrobium wilkianum

Dendrobium wilkianum, commonly known as the Wilkian orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with multiple pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces racemes of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green. Its leaves are typically elliptical and arranged in two rows along the stem.

Dendrobium wilkianum thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
