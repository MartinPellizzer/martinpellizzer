Delphinium × phoeniceum

Delphinium × phoeniceum, commonly known as the hybrid delphinium or the Queen of the Alps, is a tall, herbaceous perennial that produces striking spires of blue to purple flowers.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Europe and is prized for its showy blooms that appear in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden borders and cottage gardens.

Delphinium × phoeniceum is also known for its ornamental value and is often used in floral arrangements.
