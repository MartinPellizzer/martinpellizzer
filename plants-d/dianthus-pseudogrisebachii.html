Dianthus pseudogrisebachii

Dianthus pseudogrisebachii, commonly known as the pink rockrose, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

The plant produces small, rounded leaves and blooms in late spring to early summer. Dianthus pseudogrisebachii is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
