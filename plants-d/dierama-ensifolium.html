Dierama ensifolium

Dierama ensifolium, commonly known as the swordleaf dierama, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are often described as sword-shaped, giving it its common name. The plant produces striking, cone-shaped flower clusters that bloom in summer, adding visual interest to gardens. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it a versatile choice for coastal and Mediterranean climates.

Dierama ensifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
