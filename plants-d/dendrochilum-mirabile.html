Dendrochilum mirabile

Dendrochilum mirabile, commonly known as the marvel orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, utilizing its aerial roots to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air. Its leaves are elongated and leathery, adapted to retain water in its natural habitat.

Dendrochilum mirabile is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
