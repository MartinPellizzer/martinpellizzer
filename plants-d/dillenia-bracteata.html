Dillenia bracteata

Dillenia bracteata, commonly known as the bracteate dillenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dilleniaceae and is characterized by its showy, radially symmetric flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, with distinctive, palmately lobed leaves. Its fruits are follicles that split open to release seeds, and it is often found in moist, shaded habitats.

Dillenia bracteata is valued for its ornamental value and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
