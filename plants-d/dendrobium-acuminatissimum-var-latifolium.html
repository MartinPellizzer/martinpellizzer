Dendrobium acuminatissimum var. latifolium

Dendrobium acuminatissimum var. latifolium, commonly known as the broad-leafed dendrobium, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, sword-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its roots form a dense network that helps stabilize the soil and absorb nutrients.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral display.
