Daucus carota var. nanus

Daucus carota var. nanus, commonly known as the dwarf carrot, is a compact variety of the common carrot.

It is characterized by its short, stubby roots that are typically smaller than those of the standard carrot. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in umbels, similar to its larger relatives. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its compact size and attractive foliage.

This variety is well-suited for container gardening and small spaces.
