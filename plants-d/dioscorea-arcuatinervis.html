Dioscorea arcuatinervis

Dioscorea arcuatinervis, commonly known as the arcuatinervis yam, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Dioscoreaceae family and is characterized by its tuberous roots, which are often used in traditional medicine. The plant has alternate, heart-shaped leaves with prominent venation patterns, and it produces small, greenish-yellow flowers.

Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, and the plant is valued for its nutritional and medicinal properties.
