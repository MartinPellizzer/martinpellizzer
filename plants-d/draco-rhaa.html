Draco rhaa

Draco rhaa, commonly known as the dragon's tears plant, is a unique species native to the arid regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in dry conditions. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. Its most notable feature is the secretion of a viscous, amber-colored resin from its leaves, which has been used traditionally in various cultural practices.

The resin is also believed to have medicinal properties, though further scientific study is needed to confirm its efficacy.
