Dendrobium lawianum

Dendrobium lawianum, commonly known as the Lawian orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, cane-like pseudobulbs and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers that bloom in shades of white, yellow, and pink. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil or bark medium. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for with proper light and watering.

Dendrobium lawianum is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardens due to its beauty and resilience.
