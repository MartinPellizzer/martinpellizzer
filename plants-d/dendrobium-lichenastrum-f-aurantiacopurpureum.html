Dendrobium lichenastrum f. aurantiacopurpureum

Dendrobium lichenastrum f. aurantiacopurpureum, commonly known as the red-purple orchid, is a rare and striking epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, orchid-like flowers that display a unique blend of red and purple hues. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, tropical climates with dappled sunlight. Its inflorescence consists of multiple blooms, each with intricate patterns and a velvety texture.

Due to its rarity and aesthetic appeal, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
