Desmodium sericophyllum var. sericophyllum

Desmodium sericophyllum var. sericophyllum, commonly known as silverleaf desmodium, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which are often covered in a fine, silvery-white pubescence. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. It is typically found in grasslands and open woodlands, thriving in well-drained soils.

This species is known for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it an important plant in ecological and agricultural contexts.
