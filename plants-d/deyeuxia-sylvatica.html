Deyeuxia sylvatica

Deyeuxia sylvatica, commonly known as the woodland false grass, is a perennial grass species native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue, and it is often found in association with other wetland plants.

Deyeuxia sylvatica plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
