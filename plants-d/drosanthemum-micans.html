Drosanthemum micans

Drosanthemum micans, commonly known as the silverleaf gloxinia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its hairy, silver-green leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as a compact shrub, reaching heights of about 30 centimeters. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Drosanthemum micans is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
