Desmodium stolzii

Desmodium stolzii, commonly known as the African velvet bean, is a leguminous plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant produces cylindrical, hairy pods that contain several elliptical seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in disturbed soils.

Desmodium stolzii is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in agricultural systems.
