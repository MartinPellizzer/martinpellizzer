Diasperus × pallidus

Diasperus × pallidus, commonly known as the hybrid dandelion, is a composite plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its yellow composite flower heads and heart-shaped basal leaves. The plant produces a distinctive, hollow, wind-dispersed seed head known as a pappus. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in open fields and along roadsides.

Due to its hybrid origin, it exhibits a range of morphological variations.
