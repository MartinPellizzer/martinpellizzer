Deutzia lanceifolia

Deutzia lanceifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and have a smooth texture. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers in late spring to early summer. These flowers are borne on slender pedicels and are often accompanied by showy, fluffy bracts.

Deutzia lanceifolia is valued in gardens for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil conditions.
