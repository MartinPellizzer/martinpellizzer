Derris Spiciflora

Derris spiciflora is a unique flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. It belongs to the legume family and is known for its striking spiky inflorescences that stand out in its natural habitat. The plant has been traditionally used in local medicine for its potential health benefits. Its distinctive appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in global botanical circles.
