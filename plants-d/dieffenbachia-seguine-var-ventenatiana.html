Dieffenbachia seguine var. ventenatiana

Dieffenbachia seguine var. ventenatiana, commonly known as the White Knight Dieffenbachia, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically bright white with green veins. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes many ornamental and decorative plants. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to prevent root rot.
