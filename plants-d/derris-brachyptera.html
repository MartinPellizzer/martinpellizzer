Derris brachyptera

Derris brachyptera, commonly known as the narrow-leaf derris, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its small, pod-like fruits and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers and has a deep root system that helps stabilize soil. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along riverbanks.

Derris brachyptera is also noted for its potential use in traditional medicine and as a source of natural pesticides.
