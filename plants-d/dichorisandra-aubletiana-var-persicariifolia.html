Dichorisandra aubletiana var. persicariifolia

Dichorisandra aubletiana var. persicariifolia, commonly known as the pink-flowered dichorisandra, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in clusters at the ends of its stems. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are opposite and often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in warm climates.
