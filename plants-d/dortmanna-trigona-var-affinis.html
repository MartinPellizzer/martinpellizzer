Dortmanna trigona var. affinis

Dortmanna trigona var. affinis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf Dortmanna, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It typically grows in moist, calcareous soils in woodland and grassland habitats. The plant features slender, erect stems with alternate, linear to lanceolate leaves that are usually less than 5 cm in length. Its small, greenish-white flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom in late spring to early summer.

It is characterized by its simple, non-showy inflorescence and is often found in shaded, humid environments.
