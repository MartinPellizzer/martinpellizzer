Dactylorhiza chuhensis

Dactylorhiza chuhensis, commonly known as the Chinese marsh orchid, is a species of orchid native to China.

It is a terrestrial herb with a rhizomatous root system and produces tall, erect stems. The plant typically bears several lance-shaped leaves and clusters of violet to purple flowers. It thrives in wet, marshy environments and is adapted to temperate climates.

Dactylorhiza chuhensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
