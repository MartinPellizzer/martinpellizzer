Desmodium trinerve

Desmodium trinerve, commonly known as hairy senna, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which are typically hairy and have three leaflets. The plant produces small, yellow to orange flowers that are arranged in dense, terminal clusters. Its seed pods are slender and elongated, containing several kidney-shaped seeds.

Desmodium trinerve is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, where it thrives in open, sunny habitats.
