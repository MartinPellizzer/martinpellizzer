Dendrobenthamia tonkinensis

Dendrobenthamia tonkinensis, commonly known as the Tonkin orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial herb characterized by its erect, leafy stems and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. Native to Southeast Asia, particularly Vietnam, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant has lance-shaped leaves and produces white or pale yellow blooms.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
