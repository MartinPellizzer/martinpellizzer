Dracocephalum jacutense

Dracocephalum jacutense, commonly known as Siberian bugloss, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or steppe habitats.

Dracocephalum jacutense is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
