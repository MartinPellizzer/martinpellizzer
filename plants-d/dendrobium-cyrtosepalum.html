Dendrobium cyrtosepalum

Dendrobium cyrtosepalum, commonly known as the narrow-sepal dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with cylindrical pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with narrow, pointed sepals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for both horticultural and conservation purposes.
