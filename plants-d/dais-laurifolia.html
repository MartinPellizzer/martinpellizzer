Dais laurifolia

Dais laurifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking white to pale yellow flowers and silver-gray foliage. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in well-drained soils. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Dais laurifolia prefers full sun to partial shade and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.
