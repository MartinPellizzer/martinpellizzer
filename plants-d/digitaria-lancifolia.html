Digitaria lancifolia

Digitaria lancifolia, commonly known as sharpawn, is a grass species native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, blade-like leaves with a pointed tip. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in a variety of soil types and is often found in disturbed areas such as fields and roadsides.

Digitaria lancifolia is adaptable to different climates and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
