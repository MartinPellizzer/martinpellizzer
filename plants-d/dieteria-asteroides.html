Dieteria asteroides

Dieteria asteroides, commonly known as the star-shaped dieteria, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking star-shaped flowers, which are typically white or pale pink. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its composite flower heads. Dieteria asteroides thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.
