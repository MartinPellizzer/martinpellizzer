Dendrobium nhatrangense

Dendrobium nhatrangense, commonly known as the Nhatrang orchid, is a species of orchid native to Vietnam.

It is an epiphytic plant that grows on trees or rocks, typically in humid tropical environments. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and yellow. Its inflorescence is usually terminal, with flowers arranged in a spike that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length.

Dendrobium nhatrangense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
