Desmodium hirtum var. delicatulum

Desmodium hirtum var. delicatulum, commonly known as the slender hairy desmodium, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which are hairy and have a delicate appearance. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or grassland habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.
