Delphinium speciosum var. pulverulentum

Delphinium speciosum var. pulverulentum, commonly known as the dusty delphinium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features tall, slender stems that bear clusters of striking blue to violet flowers, which are typically arranged in racemes. The leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance. This variety is noted for its finely powdered bloom, giving it a unique, almost dusty appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.
