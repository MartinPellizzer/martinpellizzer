Dendrobium fuscum

Dendrobium fuscum, commonly known as the dark orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a dense, dark green foliage and produces clusters of small, pale green to brownish flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its pseudobulbs are thick and fleshy, storing water and nutrients.

Dendrobium fuscum is valued for its unique appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
