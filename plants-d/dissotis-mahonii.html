Dissotis mahonii

Dissotis mahonii, commonly known as the Mahon's spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking vibrant, tropical plants.
