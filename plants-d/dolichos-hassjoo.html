Dolichos hassjoo

Dolichos hassjoo, commonly known as the hyacinth bean, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It produces distinctive, spiral-shaped pods that enclose edible seeds, which are often used in culinary applications. The plant has trifoliate leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically purple or white. It thrives in warm climates and is often grown as a cover crop or for its ornamental value.

Botanically, it is classified under the genus Dolichos in the family Fabaceae.
