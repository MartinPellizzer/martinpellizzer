Dioscorea stellaris

Dioscorea stellaris, commonly known as star yam, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its tuberous roots, which are often used in traditional medicine and as a food source. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. Its leaves are heart-shaped and can grow up to 30 centimeters in length.

Dioscorea stellaris is also valued for its ornamental qualities due to its unique floral structure.
