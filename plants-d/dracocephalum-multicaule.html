Dracocephalum multicaule

Dracocephalum multicaule, commonly known as blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region and parts of Europe.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Dracocephalum multicaule is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.
