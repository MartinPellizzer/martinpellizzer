Desmodium spirale var. annuum

Desmodium spirale var. annuum, commonly known as the spiral desmodium, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its distinctive spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it a unique and ornamental appearance. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This species is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and landscapes.
