Disa sankeyi

Disa sankeyi, commonly known as the Sankey's orchid, is a rare and striking orchid species native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stem and showy, violet-blue flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers sunny to partially shaded environments. Its flowers bloom in late summer, adding a vibrant touch to its natural habitat.

Disa sankeyi is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological significance in its native ecosystem.
