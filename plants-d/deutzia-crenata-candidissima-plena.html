Deutzia crenata candidissima-plena

Deutzia crenata candidissima-plena, commonly known as the snowbank deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit and clusters of white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant features small, ovate leaves with crenate edges, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in partial to full shade and is well-suited for moist, well-drained soils.

Its compact size and elegant floral display make it a popular choice for garden borders and woodland gardens.
