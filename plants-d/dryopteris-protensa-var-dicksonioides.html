Dryopteris protensa var. dicksonioides

Dryopteris protensa var. dicksonioides, commonly known as the Dickson's wood fern, is a variant of the sword fern family.

It is a perennial evergreen fern that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features lance-shaped, bipinnate fronds that are dark green and glossy. It has a rhizomatous growth habit, which allows it to spread and form dense colonies.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in woodland gardens.
