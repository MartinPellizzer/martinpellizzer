Desmazeria alternans

Desmazeria alternans, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a climbing shrub native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzagging stems, which give it its common name. The plant has opposite, simple, and often elliptical leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color.

Desmazeria alternans is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
