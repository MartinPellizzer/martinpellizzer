Dendrobium cultratum

Dendrobium cultratum, commonly known as the sickle-shaped orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with cane-like stems that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often with a distinctive lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrobium cultratum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
