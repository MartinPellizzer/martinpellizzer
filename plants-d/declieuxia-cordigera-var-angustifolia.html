Declieuxia cordigera var. angustifolia

Declieuxia cordigera var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed grass tree, is a striking ornamental plant native to Australia.

It features tall, slender stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that form a dense rosette near the base. The plant produces striking, bottle-shaped inflorescences that are often bright yellow or red, depending on the variety. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
