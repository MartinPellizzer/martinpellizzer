Didissandra fritschii

Didissandra fritschii, commonly known as the African moonflower, is a climbing plant native to southern Africa.

Its leaves are opposite and have a velvety texture, while its flowers emit a strong, sweet fragrance to attract pollinators.

Didissandra fritschii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and nocturnal blooming habit.
