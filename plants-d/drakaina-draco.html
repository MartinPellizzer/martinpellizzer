Drakaina draco

Drakaina draco, commonly known as the dragon's blood tree, is a unique succulent plant native to the Socotra Island.

It is characterized by its distinctive umbrella-shaped crown and thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting to the arid environment. The tree produces a red resin known as dragon's blood, which has been historically used in medicine and dyes. Its slow growth rate and peculiar appearance make it a striking botanical specimen.

The plant's ability to thrive in harsh conditions highlights its evolutionary adaptations to its native habitat.
