Duschekia firma

Duschekia firma, commonly known as the silverleaf duschekia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features silver-green foliage with a fuzzy texture, and its flowers are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Duschekia firma is noted for its low maintenance and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
