Dieteria canescens var. glabra

Dieteria canescens var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth dieteria, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, hairy stems that become smooth in this variety, giving it its scientific name. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a rough texture.

It produces small, yellow flowers that are arranged in clusters, making it a notable species in its native habitat.
