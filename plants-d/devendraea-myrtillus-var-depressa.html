Devendraea myrtillus var. depressa

Devendraea myrtillus var. depressa, commonly known as the dwarf myrtle, is a compact shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features small, fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has small, leathery leaves that are typically arranged in opposite pairs along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact size and aromatic foliage.
