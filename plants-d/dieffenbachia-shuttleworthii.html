Dieffenbachia shuttleworthii

Dieffenbachia shuttleworthii, commonly known as the Shuttleworth's Dieffenbachia, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green with white or cream-colored veins. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other popular houseplants like peace lilies and calla lilies. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its attractive foliage, it is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant.
