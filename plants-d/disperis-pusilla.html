Disperis pusilla

Disperis pusilla, commonly known as the dwarf orchid, is a small, terrestrial orchid native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils. The plant features a slender, leafless stem with a few narrow leaves near the base. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and often have a faintly colored lip.

Disperis pusilla is known for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
