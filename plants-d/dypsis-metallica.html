Dypsis metallica

Dypsis metallica, commonly known as the silver palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to Madagascar and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and feathery fronds. The plant has a distinctive silver-blue sheen on its leaves, which gives it its common name. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and requires well-drained soil.

Dypsis metallica is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its elegant appearance and adaptability to indoor conditions.
