Datura hybrida

Datura hybrida, commonly known as angel's trumpet, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that can be white, yellow, or purple in color. The plant produces egg-shaped, spiny fruits that contain toxic seeds. It is a member of the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshade plants.

Datura hybrida is often cultivated for its ornamental value but requires caution due to its toxic properties.
