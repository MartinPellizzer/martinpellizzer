Dischisma Spicatum

Dischisma spicatum is a striking member of the Asteraceae family known for its vibrant blue and purple flowers. Native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States, this plant thrives in rocky soils and full sun. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice among horticulturists and gardeners seeking drought-resistant ornamentals. The plant's ability to bloom throughout the summer adds color to landscapes in challenging environments. With proper care, Dischisma spicatum can become a standout addition to any xeriscaped garden.
