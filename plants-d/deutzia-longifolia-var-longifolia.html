Deutzia longifolia var. longifolia

Deutzia longifolia var. longifolia, commonly known as the Chinese deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features fragrant, white to pink flowers that bloom in clusters during late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, arching branches and dark green, ovate leaves that provide ornamental value throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, moist soil.

This species is often cultivated in gardens for its showy floral display and hardy nature.
