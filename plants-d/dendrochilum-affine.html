Dendrochilum affine

Dendrochilum affine, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a slender, erect stem with several leaves arranged spirally. Its flowers are small and usually greenish-white, with a distinctive lip that is often more brightly colored.

Dendrochilum affine is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
