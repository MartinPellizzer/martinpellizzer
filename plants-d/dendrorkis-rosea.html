Dendrorkis rosea

Dendrorkis rosea, commonly known as the pink orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking pink or purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Dendrorkis rosea is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
