Duosperma densiflorum

Duosperma densiflorum, commonly known as the denseflower ice plant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves and clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems and leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its hardy nature and attractive appearance make it a valuable addition to xeriscapic landscapes.
