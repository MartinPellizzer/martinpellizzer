Dieffenbachia galdamesiae

Dieffenbachia galdamesiae, commonly known as the Galdamesia dieffenbachia, is a striking tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, arrowhead-shaped leaves that display vibrant patterns of white, yellow, and green. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes many ornamental and sometimes toxic plants. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and decorative purposes.
