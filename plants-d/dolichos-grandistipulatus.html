Dolichos grandistipulatus

Dolichos grandistipulatus, commonly known as the large-leaf dolichos, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features compound leaves with elongated stipules, giving it its scientific name. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by long, bean-like pods containing edible seeds. It is often found in open woodlands and along riverbanks, thriving in warm, humid climates.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species in both ecological and agricultural contexts.
