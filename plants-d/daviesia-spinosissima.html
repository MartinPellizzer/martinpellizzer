Daviesia spinosissima

Daviesia spinosissima, commonly known as the spinifex Daviesia, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in late winter to early spring, which are arranged in dense, cylindrical clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in sandy or rocky habitats.

Daviesia spinosissima is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
