Deutzia gracilis var. rosea

Deutzia gracilis var. rosea, commonly known as the rosea deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to Asia.

It features slender, arching stems that bear clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink to white. The foliage is typically dark green, providing a striking contrast to the floral display. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
