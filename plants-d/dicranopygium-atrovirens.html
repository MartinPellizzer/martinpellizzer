Dicranopygium atrovirens

Dicranopygium atrovirens, commonly known as the dark green moss, is a species of moss belonging to the family Dicranaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, cushion-like growth form and dark green foliage. The leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with a distinct costa that extends to the apex. It typically grows on acidic soils in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky crevices.

This moss is often used in ecological studies due to its sensitivity to environmental changes.
