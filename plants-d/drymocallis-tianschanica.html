Drymocallis tianschanica

Drymocallis tianschanica, commonly known as the Tianshan goldenrod, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy yellow flower heads. The plant produces composite flowers that consist of both ray and disk florets, typical of the daisy family. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine and steppe habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
