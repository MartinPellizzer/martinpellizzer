Dontospermum vogelii

Dontospermum vogelii, commonly known as the African velvet bean, is a climbing shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces pods that contain edible seeds, which are rich in protein and used in local cuisines. It also has a fibrous stem that is sometimes used in traditional crafts.

Due to its adaptability, it thrives in various soil types and is often found in disturbed habitats.
