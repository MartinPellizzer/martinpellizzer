Dendrobium remotisepalum

Dendrobium remotisepalum, commonly known as the remote-sepal orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with numerous pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
