Diplolaena angustifolia

Diplolaena angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved diplolaena, is a shrub native to Australia.

It features small, narrow leaves that are typically arranged alternately along its stems. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or red in color. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Diplolaena angustifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
