Dendrobium cyclolobum

Dendrobium cyclolobum, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium cyclolobum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
