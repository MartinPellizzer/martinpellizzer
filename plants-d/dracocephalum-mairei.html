Dracocephalum mairei

Dracocephalum mairei, commonly known as Chinese violet, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, violet-blue flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are often used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported medicinal properties.

The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or mountainous regions.
