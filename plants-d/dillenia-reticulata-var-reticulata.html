Dillenia reticulata var. reticulata

Dillenia reticulata var. reticulata, commonly known as the reticulate dillenia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, palmately lobed leaves with a distinctive reticulate venation pattern. The plant produces showy, yellowish-green flowers with five sepals and numerous stamens. Its fruits are woody, spiny capsules that contain seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
