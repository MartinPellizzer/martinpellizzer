Doryopteris concolor var. nicklesii

Doryopteris concolor var. nicklesii, commonly known as the Nickles' sword fern, is a variant of the sword fern species native to the Pacific Northwest of the United States.

It is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped fronds that are typically dark green and slightly glossy. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its rhizomes are wiry and help anchor it to rocky or soil substrates.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens with appropriate humidity and light conditions.
