Dendrocalamus mianningensis

Dendrocalamus mianningensis, commonly known as the Mianning bamboo, is a species of giant bamboo native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, straight culms that can reach heights of up to 20 meters. The plant has a robust rhizome system, which allows it to spread and form dense clumps. Its leaves are long and narrow, with a smooth, waxy surface.

This bamboo is valued for its strength and is used in construction and handicrafts in its native region.
