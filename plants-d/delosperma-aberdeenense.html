Delosperma aberdeenense

Delosperma aberdeenense, commonly known as the Aberdeenshire ice plant, is a low-growing succulent native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white powder, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers in late summer, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for water-wise gardening.
