Deyeuxia forsteri var. littoralis

Deyeuxia forsteri var. littoralis, commonly known as the coastal or seaside grass, is a variant of the grass species Deyeuxia forsteri.

It is a perennial grass that thrives in coastal environments, often found in sandy or saline soils. This plant is adapted to withstand salt spray and wind, making it a hardy species in maritime climates. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a dense growth habit that helps stabilize dunes and prevent erosion.

It plays an important ecological role in coastal ecosystems by providing habitat and stabilizing shorelines.
