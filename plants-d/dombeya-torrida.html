Dombeya torrida

Dombeya torrida, commonly known as the tropical dombeya, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often covered in a fine, silvery down.

Dombeya torrida is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and adaptability to various soil types.
