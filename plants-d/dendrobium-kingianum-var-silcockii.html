Dendrobium kingianum var. silcockii

Dendrobium kingianum var. silcockii, commonly known as the Silcock's Dendrobium, is a species of orchid native to northern Australia.

It is an epiphytic plant that grows on trees and rocks, typically in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features long, cane-like stems that are often covered in dense clusters of small, fragrant flowers. Its blooms, which can be white, pink, or yellow, are usually borne along the stems in summer.

The orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
