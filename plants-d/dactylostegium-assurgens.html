Dactylostegium assurgens

Dactylostegium assurgens, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a small, perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, spider-like appearance due to its elongated, thread-like leaves. The plant produces delicate, white or pale green flowers that are clustered in a spike, often resembling a spider's web. It thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and protected species in its native region.
