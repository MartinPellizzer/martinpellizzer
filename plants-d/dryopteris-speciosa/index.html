Dryopteris Speciosa

Lady fern, scientifically known as Dryopteris speciosa, is a graceful and elegant fern native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere. With its feathery fronds and delicate, arching appearance, it is a popular choice for both garden landscapes and indoor decor. This plant thrives in moist, shady environments and is prized for its ability to add a touch of natural beauty to any setting. Its lush green foliage and subtle, spore-bearing structures make it a fascinating subject for botanical enthusiasts. Whether in a woodland garden or a shaded patio, the lady fern is a symbol of quiet resilience and enduring charm.
