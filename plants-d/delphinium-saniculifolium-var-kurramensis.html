Delphinium saniculifolium var. kurramensis

Delphinium saniculifolium var. kurramensis, commonly known as the Kurram delphinium, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Pakistan.

It features tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant produces striking blue to purple flowers in racemes, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lanceolate and have a distinctive, finely toothed margin.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
