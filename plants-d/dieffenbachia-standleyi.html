Dieffenbachia standleyi

Dieffenbachia standleyi, commonly known as the "Shoal Creek Dieffenbachia," is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers, though it is more admired for its foliage than its blooms. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Dieffenbachia standleyi is a popular houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
