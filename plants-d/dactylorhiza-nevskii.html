Dactylorhiza nevskii

Dactylorhiza nevskii, commonly known as the Nevskii's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the temperate regions of Europe and western Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. The plant features a slender, upright stem with several lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with three sepals and three petals, and a distinctive lip.

Dactylorhiza nevskii is known for its ability to thrive in nutrient-poor soils and requires careful management to preserve its habitat.
