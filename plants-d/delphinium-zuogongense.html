Delphinium zuogongense

Delphinium zuogongense, commonly known as the Zhonggong larkspur, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems. The plant produces showy, blue to purple flowers in late summer, which are clustered in dense racemes. Its leaves are compound, with lance-shaped leaflets that are often hairy.
