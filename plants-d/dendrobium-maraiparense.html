Dendrobium maraiparense

Dendrobium maraiparense, commonly known as the Maraipari orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Brazil.

It features a sympodial growth habit with numerous, narrow leaves that are often covered in a dense, fibrous sheath. The plant produces racemes of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with prominent sepals and petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrobium maraiparense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
