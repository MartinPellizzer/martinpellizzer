Dupontia Fisheri Var. Pelligera

Dupontia fisheri var. pelligera is a rare and fascinating plant species native to the unique coastal environments of southern California. It thrives in sandy soils and is often found in the intertidal zones where it withstands harsh conditions like salt spray and wave action. This variant of Dupontia fisheri is distinguished by its compact growth form and distinctive leaf morphology. Despite its resilience, it faces threats from habitat loss and climate change. Studying this plant offers valuable insights into ecological adaptation and conservation in coastal ecosystems.
