Dupatya compacta

Dupatya compacta, commonly known as the compact daisy, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically grows in dense clusters, with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Due to its compact size and attractive blooms, it is often used in rock gardens or as a border plant.
