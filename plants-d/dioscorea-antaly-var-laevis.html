Dioscorea antaly var. laevis

Dioscorea antaly var. laevis, commonly known as the smooth yam, is a tropical vine belonging to the family Dioscoreaceae.

It is characterized by its climbing habit, long stems, and large, heart-shaped leaves with a smooth texture. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers followed by tuberous roots that are the primary economically valuable part. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its starchy tubers used in various culinary applications.

The species is also noted for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
