Drynaria Sparsisora

Drynaria sparsisora is a rare and ancient fern species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found growing on rocky surfaces or in dense forest canopies. This plant is known for its delicate fronds and unique growth patterns, making it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it faces significant threats to its survival. Protecting Drynaria sparsisora is crucial for preserving biodiversity in its native ecosystems.
