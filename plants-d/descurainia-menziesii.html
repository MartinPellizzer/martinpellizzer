Descurainia menziesii

Descurainia menziesii, commonly known as yellow rocket, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is native to North America and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It has alternate, pinnately compound leaves and produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds.
