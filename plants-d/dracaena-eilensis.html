Dracaena eilensis

Dracaena eilensis, commonly known as the Eilens Dracaena, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, linear leaves that are often a vibrant green with a reddish tinge along the edges. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by small, round berries. Dracaena eilensis is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance of low light and minimal water requirements.

It is also valued for its air-purifying properties and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
