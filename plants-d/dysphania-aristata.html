Dysphania aristata

Dysphania aristata, commonly known as velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, velvety hairs, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in the summer months. Dysphania aristata is an annual herb that grows in a variety of habitats, including disturbed soils and agricultural fields.

It is often considered a weed due to its rapid growth and ability to spread easily.
