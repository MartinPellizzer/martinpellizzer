Deparia sichuanensis

Deparia sichuanensis, commonly known as the Sichuan shield fern, is a species of fern native to the mountainous regions of Sichuan, China.

It belongs to the Dryopteridaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are bi- or tri-pinnate, with a delicate, feathery appearance.

Deparia sichuanensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
