Diphylleia cymosa subsp. sinensis

Diphylleia cymosa subsp.

sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese ghost flower, is a rare and striking plant native to China. It is characterized by its unique, umbrella-shaped inflorescence that resembles a ghostly silhouette, hence its common name.

The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and dark green color. Its flowers are small, white, and clustered in a cymose arrangement, giving it a ghostly appearance.

It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded, forested areas.
