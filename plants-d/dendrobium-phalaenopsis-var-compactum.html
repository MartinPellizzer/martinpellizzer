Dendrobium phalaenopsis var. compactum

Dendrobium phalaenopsis var. compactum, commonly known as the compact orchid, is a hybrid orchid species characterized by its dense, compact growth habit.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia, where it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant produces showy, long-lasting flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often arranged in clusters. Its pseudobulbs are short and stout, and it prefers bright, indirect light with high humidity.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
