Dendrobium leopardinum

Dendrobium leopardinum, commonly known as the leopard orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with multiple stems that can reach up to 1 meter in height. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellow-green flowers with distinctive dark brown markings resembling leopard spots. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrobium leopardinum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
