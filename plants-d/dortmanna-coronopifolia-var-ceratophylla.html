Dortmanna coronopifolia var. ceratophylla

Dortmanna coronopifolia var. ceratophylla, commonly known as the velvetleaf or scarlet dock, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features basal leaves that are heart-shaped and covered in a dense, velvety pubescence, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces bright red to orange flowers in late summer, which are clustered in umbels. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodland floors and stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it both ornamental and ecologically significant in its native habitat.
