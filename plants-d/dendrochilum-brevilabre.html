Dendrochilum brevilabre

Dendrochilum brevilabre, commonly known as the short-lipped tree orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. This epiphytic orchid typically grows on trees or rocks, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually small, fragrant, and come in various colors such as white, pink, and purple.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.
