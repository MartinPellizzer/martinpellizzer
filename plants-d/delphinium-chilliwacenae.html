Delphinium chilliwacenae

Delphinium chilliwacenae, commonly known as the Chilkat delphinium, is a perennial herb native to the coastal regions of British Columbia, Canada.

It is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant produces striking blue to purple flowers in clusters, typically blooming in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem, with a hairy texture.

Delphinium chilliwacenae thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal heathlands and forest edges.
