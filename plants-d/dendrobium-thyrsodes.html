Dendrobium thyrsodes

Dendrobium thyrsodes, commonly known as the golden spike orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, branched inflorescences. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a distinctive golden stripe. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, requiring high humidity and indirect sunlight.

Dendrobium thyrsodes is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
