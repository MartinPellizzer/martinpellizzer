Diuris cruenta

Diuris cruenta, commonly known as the Crimson Spider-orchid, is a striking orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It features a compact, hairy stem with several narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces striking, spider-like flowers in shades of red and orange, often with a contrasting green or yellow throat. It blooms during late winter to early spring, typically between July and September.

Diuris cruenta is adapted to grow in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.
