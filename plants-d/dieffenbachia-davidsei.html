Dieffenbachia davidsei

Dieffenbachia davidsei, commonly known as the Shiny-leaf Dieffenbachia, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Colombia.

It features large, waxy, dark green leaves with a glossy texture and prominent veins. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its inflorescence structure consisting of a spadix and spathe. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its striking appearance, it is a popular ornamental plant in indoor gardening.
