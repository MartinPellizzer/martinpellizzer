Delissea solenocalyx

Delissea solenocalyx, commonly known as the red-flowered orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red petals and a unique, elongated floral structure. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its delicate, fragrant blooms. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Delissea solenocalyx is often cultivated for its ornamental value in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
