Dendrobium bifurcatum

Dendrobium bifurcatum, commonly known as the forked orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees or rocks in tropical rainforests. The plant features long, cane-like stems that can reach several meters in length. Its flowers are usually yellow or green with red or brown markings, and they bloom in clusters.

Dendrobium bifurcatum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
