Dendrobium discolor var. fuscum

Dendrobium discolor var. fuscum, commonly known as the dark-stemmed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features elongated, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, pale yellow to orange flowers. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil. Its unique varietal characteristics include a darker stem coloration compared to the typical variety.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to cultivation.
