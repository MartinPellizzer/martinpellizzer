Dendrobium ruppianum f. magnificum

Dendrobium ruppianum f. magnificum, commonly known as the magnificent dendrobium, is a hybrid orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and produces large, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic, and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in humid, tropical environments.

This orchid is popular among horticulturists for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
