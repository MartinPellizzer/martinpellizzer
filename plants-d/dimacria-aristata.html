Dimacria aristata

Dimacria aristata, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces spikes of small, tubular flowers that are typically purple or pink in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Dimacria aristata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a garden setting.
