Dizygotheca ouveana

Dizygotheca ouveana, commonly known as the Brazilian flame tree, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Leguminosae family and is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has bipinnate leaves and produces legume pods that contain seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.
