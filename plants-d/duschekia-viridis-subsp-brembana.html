Duschekia viridis subsp. brembana

Duschekia viridis subsp.

brembana, commonly known as the Bremban green bellflower, is a rare and specialized plant species native to the alpine regions of Europe. It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, bell-shaped green flowers that bloom in late summer.

This subspecies thrives in rocky, well-drained soils at high altitudes, often in mountainous environments. Its limited distribution and specific ecological requirements make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
