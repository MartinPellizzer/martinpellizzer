Dichopsis cuneata

Dichopsis cuneata, commonly known as the Narrow-leaf Dichopsis, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish tint, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its low maintenance requirements, it is well-suited for both indoor and outdoor cultivation in dry climates.
