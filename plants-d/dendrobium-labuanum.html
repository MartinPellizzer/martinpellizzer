Dendrobium labuanum

Dendrobium labuanum, commonly known as the Labuan orchid, is a epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, branching structure with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrobium labuanum is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its beauty.
