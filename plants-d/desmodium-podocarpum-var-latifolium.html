Desmodium podocarpum var. latifolium

Desmodium podocarpum var. latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf desmodium, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features trifoliate leaves with broad, ovate leaflets that are typically pubescent. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its pods are slender and contain several elliptical seeds.

This species is often found in moist, shaded habitats and is valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability in agricultural systems.
