Dovea recta

Dovea recta, commonly known as the straight dove plant, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale blue in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or container planting.

Dovea recta is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
