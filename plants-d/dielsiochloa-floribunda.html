Dielsiochloa floribunda

Dielsiochloa floribunda, commonly known as the flowering grass, is a species of annual grass native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural fields. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a prominent midrib.
