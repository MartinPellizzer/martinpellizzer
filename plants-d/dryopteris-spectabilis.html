Dryopteris spectabilis

Dryopteris spectabilis, commonly known as the royal fern, is a species of fern native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, upright fronds that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant has a distinctive, elegant appearance with its deeply divided, bright green leaves that turn bronze in the fall. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.

The fronds are typically evergreen, providing year-round interest in the landscape.
