Desmanthodium lanceolatum

Desmanthodium lanceolatum, commonly known as the lanceleaf desmanthium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open woodlands.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
