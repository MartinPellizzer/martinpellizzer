Dendrobium gracilicaule var. gracilicaule

Dendrobium gracilicaule var. gracilicaule, commonly known as the slender stem orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in tropical rainforests. The plant features long, slender pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in shaded environments. Its flowers are usually small, fragrant, and come in shades of white, yellow, and pink.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its delicate, long-lasting blooms.
