Dendrobium fairfaxii

Dendrobium fairfaxii, commonly known as the FairFax's orchid, is a sympodial epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit, with cane-like stems that can reach up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces clustered inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. Its leaves are lanceolate and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Dendrobium fairfaxii thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
