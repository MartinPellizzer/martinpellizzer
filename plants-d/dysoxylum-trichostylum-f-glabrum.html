Dysoxylum trichostylum f. glabrum

Dysoxylum trichostylum f. glabrum, commonly known as the smooth dysoxylum, is a tropical tree species belonging to the family Meliaceae.

It is characterized by its smooth, often glabrous bark and elongated, elliptical leaves with a glossy upper surface. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation areas.
