Dicentra macrantha

Dicentra macrantha, commonly known as the pink bishop's hat, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features delicate, nodding flowers that are typically pink or red, with a distinctive spurred shape. The plant has heart-shaped, palmate leaves that are deeply lobed and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Dicentra macrantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and woodland settings.
