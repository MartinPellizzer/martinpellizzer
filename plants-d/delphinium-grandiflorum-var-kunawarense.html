Delphinium grandiflorum var. kunawarense

Delphinium grandiflorum var. kunawarense, commonly known as the Kunawara larkspur, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to New Zealand.

It features tall, upright stems that bear clusters of blue to violet flowers, which are typically 5-7 cm in diameter. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves with a hairy texture, and it thrives in cool, moist environments. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.

The species is also noted for its ecological importance, providing nectar for pollinators.
