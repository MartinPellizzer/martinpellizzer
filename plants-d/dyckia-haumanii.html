Dyckia haumanii

Dyckia haumanii, commonly known as the purple dyckia, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It features rosettes of narrow, dark green leaves with a distinctive purple sheen. The plant produces tall, branching inflorescences that bear vibrant orange flowers. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Dyckia haumanii is relatively drought-tolerant and makes a striking addition to rock gardens or container collections.
