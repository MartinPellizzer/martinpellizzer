Dendrobium lonchigerum

Dendrobium lonchigerum, commonly known as the golden spike orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, cane-like pseudobulbs and clusters of long, narrow leaves. The plant produces striking inflorescences with bright yellow or orange flowers that bloom in late summer or early autumn. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Dendrobium lonchigerum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for in cultivation.
