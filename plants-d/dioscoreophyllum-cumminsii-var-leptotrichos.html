Dioscoreophyllum cumminsii var. leptotrichos

Dioscoreophyllum cumminsii var. leptotrichos, commonly known as the slender-hair vine, is a climbing shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its woody stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that release seeds when mature.

This species is often found in moist, shaded habitats within rainforests.
