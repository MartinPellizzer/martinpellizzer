Diapedium capense

Diapedium capense, commonly known as the Cape spider orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, spider-like appearance due to its elongated, thin petals. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, and it blooms during the summer months.

Diapedium capense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its distinctive appearance.
