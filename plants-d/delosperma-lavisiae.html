Delosperma lavisiae

Delosperma lavisiae, commonly known as the Ice Plant, is a succulent perennial native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine, powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ability to store water in its leaves allows it to survive in arid conditions.
