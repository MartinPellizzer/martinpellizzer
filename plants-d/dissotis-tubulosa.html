Dissotis tubulosa

Dissotis tubulosa, commonly known as the tubular dissotis, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the grass family, Poaceae, and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically greenish or reddish in color, often arranged in dense spikes. It is often found in moist, shaded areas such as forest understories or wetlands.
