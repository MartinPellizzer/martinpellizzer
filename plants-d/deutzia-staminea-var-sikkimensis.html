Deutzia staminea var. sikkimensis

Deutzia staminea var. sikkimensis, commonly known as the Sikkim deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to the Himalayan regions of India and Nepal.

It features showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring, often in clusters at the ends of its stems. The plant has slender, arching branches and small, ovate leaves that are typically dark green in color. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, moist soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in garden landscapes for its delicate floral display.
