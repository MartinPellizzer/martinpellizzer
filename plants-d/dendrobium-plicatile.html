Dendrobium plicatile

Dendrobium plicatile, commonly known as the pleated orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique pleated, waxy leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Dendrobium plicatile is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
