Delphinium suave

Delphinium suave, commonly known as the sweet delphinium, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, spiky inflorescences composed of showy blue to purple petals. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or meadow habitats. Its leaves are compound, with a serrated edge, and the flowers bloom in late spring to early summer.

Delphinium suave is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant, eye-catching blooms.
