Drosanthemum oculatum

Drosanthemum oculatum, commonly known as the eye-plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features striking, colorful flowers that resemble eyes, hence its common name. The plant has fleshy, ribbed stems and leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Drosanthemum oculatum is popular in rock gardens and as a decorative houseplant due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
