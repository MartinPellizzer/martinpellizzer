Dendrobium sarcochilus var. sarcochilus

Dendrobium sarcochilus var. sarcochilus, commonly known as the Black Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant that thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features long, cane-like stems that can grow up to several meters in height. Its flowers are dark purple to black, with a distinctive white lip, and bloom in late winter to early spring.

It is valued for its striking appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
