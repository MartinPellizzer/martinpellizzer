Draba dongchuanensis

Draba dongchuanensis, commonly known as the Dongchuan Draba, is a flowering plant native to the Yunnan province in China.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cress plants. This species is characterized by its low-growing habit, with small, basal leaves and clusters of tiny white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in alpine and subalpine environments, often found in rocky soils at high elevations.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
