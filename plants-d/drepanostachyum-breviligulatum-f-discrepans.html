Drepanostachyum breviligulatum f. discrepans

Drepanostachyum breviligulatum f. discrepans, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pandanus, is a variant of the pandanus genus native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves that are typically arranged in a compact rosette. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that bear fragrant, yellowish flowers. Its foliage is often used in traditional crafts and basket-making due to its durability.

This form is distinguished by its unique leaf morphology and growth habit compared to other pandanus species.
