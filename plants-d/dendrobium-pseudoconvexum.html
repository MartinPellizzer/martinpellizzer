Dendrobium pseudoconvexum

Dendrobium pseudoconvexum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed dendrobium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in tropical rainforests. The plant features long, narrow leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. It thrives in high humidity and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Dendrobium pseudoconvexum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
