Diplazium wichurae

Diplazium wichurae, commonly known as the narrow-leaf chain fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its tall, slender fronds that can reach up to 2 meters in length, with a distinctive, narrow, and elongated shape. Diplazium wichurae typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas.

Its unique morphology and ecological adaptability make it a notable species within its native range.
