Dendrobium tenuissimum

Dendrobium tenuissimum, commonly known as the slender leaching orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a slender, leafless stem that grows close to the ground, often in shaded, moist environments. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically blooming during the summer months. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the forest floor, and it is often found in humid, tropical climates.

Dendrobium tenuissimum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral characteristics.
