Dicliptera furcifera

Dicliptera furcifera, commonly known as the forked grass, is a perennial herb native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, erect stems. Dicliptera furcifera is often found in disturbed soils and is used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
