Dracophyllum filifolium

Dracophyllum filifolium, commonly known as the slender leaf dracophyllum, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal and subalpine regions, thriving in well-drained, acidic soils. Its leaves are narrow and leathery, often arranged in whorls around the stem.
