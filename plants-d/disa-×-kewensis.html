Disa × kewensis

Disa × kewensis, commonly known as the Kew Disa, is a hybrid orchid native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the genus Disa, which includes numerous species of terrestrial orchids. The plant is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to pink and orange. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial shade to full sun.

Disa × kewensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
