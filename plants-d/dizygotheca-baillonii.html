Dizygotheca baillonii

Dizygotheca baillonii, commonly known as the Bailloni's legume, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing ability. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
