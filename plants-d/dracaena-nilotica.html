Dracaena nilotica

Dracaena nilotica, commonly known as the African daisy or red daisy, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Dracaena nilotica is also valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in various environmental conditions.
