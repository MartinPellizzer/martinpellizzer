Draba hammenii

Draba hammenii, commonly known as the Hammen's wintercress, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or alpine environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a single, terminal cluster, which bloom in early spring. Its leaves are basal, rosette-shaped, and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Draba hammenii is adapted to cold climates and is often found at high altitudes where it faces harsh environmental conditions.
