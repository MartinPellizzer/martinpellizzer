Dianthus stenopetalus var. brachycaulis

Dianthus stenopetalus var. brachycaulis, commonly known as the compact carnation, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense clusters of showy, pink or red flowers with frilly petals, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and a low, spreading growth habit, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.

This variety is prized for its vibrant color and compact form, making it a popular choice in garden landscapes.
