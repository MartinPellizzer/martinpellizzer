Digitaria microbachne

Digitaria microbachne, commonly known as slender digitaria, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in open habitats such as roadsides and fields.

Botanically, it is notable for its adaptability and resilience in various environmental conditions.
