Digitaria camusiana

Digitaria camusiana, commonly known as African grass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny florets. It thrives in warm climates and is often found in disturbed soils or open habitats.

Digitaria camusiana is known for its rapid growth and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
