Draba aizoides f. capsularis

Draba aizoides f. capsularis, commonly known as the capular form of the alpine bellflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its low-growing habit and rosette of basal leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. It is typically found in alpine and subalpine regions, growing in rocky soils and open habitats.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to cold, high-altitude environments.
