Dregea cuneifolia

Dregea cuneifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf dregea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, which gives it its common name.

Dregea cuneifolia is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
