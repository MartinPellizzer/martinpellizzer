Dyckia cinerea

Dyckia cinerea, commonly known as the silver dyckia, is a species of bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft trichomes that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces striking inflorescences with vibrant red bracts that contrast beautifully with its foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Dyckia cinerea is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
