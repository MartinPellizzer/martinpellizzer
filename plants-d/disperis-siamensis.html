Disperis siamensis

Disperis siamensis, commonly known as the Siam Disperis, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or swampy areas. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The species is relatively rare and is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological niche.
