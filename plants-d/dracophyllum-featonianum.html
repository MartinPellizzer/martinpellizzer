Dracophyllum featonianum

Dracophyllum featonianum, commonly known as the Christmas Bush, is a shrub native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in winter. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in coastal climates with high salt exposure. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscaping.

Dracophyllum featonianum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
