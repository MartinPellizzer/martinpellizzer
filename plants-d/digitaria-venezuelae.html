Digitaria venezuelae

Digitaria venezuelae, commonly known as Venezuela digitaria, is a grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that contain both male and female flowers. It thrives in tropical and subtropical environments, often found in disturbed soils and open habitats.

Digitaria venezuelae is adapted to withstand drought conditions, making it a resilient species in its native range.
