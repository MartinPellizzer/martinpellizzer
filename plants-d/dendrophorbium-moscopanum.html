Dendrophorbium moscopanum

Dendrophorbium moscopanum, commonly known as the Moscopan tree, is a member of the family Fabaceae and is native to southern Africa.

It is a medium to large-sized tree that can grow up to 20 meters in height, with a broad, spreading canopy. The leaves are bipinnate, consisting of multiple pairs of small, oval-shaped leaflets. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in dense, globular inflorescences, and it produces legume pods that contain several seeds.

The plant is notable for its ecological importance, providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
