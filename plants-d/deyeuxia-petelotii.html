Deyeuxia petelotii

Deyeuxia petelotii, commonly known as the African feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and long, narrow leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and along riverbanks.

Deyeuxia petelotii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its delicate appearance.
