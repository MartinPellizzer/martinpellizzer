Dendrobium macrostachyum

Dendrobium macrostachyum, commonly known as the giant orchid or the noble orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, cane-like stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated as a houseplant and in botanical gardens.
