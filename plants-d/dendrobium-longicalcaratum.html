Dendrobium longicalcaratum

Dendrobium longicalcaratum, commonly known as the long-lipped dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with multiple stems that can reach up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its leaves are strap-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stems.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
