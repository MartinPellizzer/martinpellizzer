Draba stylaris

Draba stylaris, commonly known as the slender whitlow grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features basal leaves that are linear to lanceolate in shape, often with a hairy surface. Its flowers are small, yellowish-white, and arranged in a raceme, typical of the Brassicaceae family.

Draba stylaris is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows, grasslands, and rocky areas.
