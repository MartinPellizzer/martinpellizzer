Dombeya viburnifloropsis

Dombeya viburnifloropsis, commonly known as the silky dombeya, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Africa.

It features showy, fragrant, white to pink flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a silvery underside, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and graceful form.
