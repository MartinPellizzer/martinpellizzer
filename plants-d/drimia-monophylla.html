Drimia monophylla

Drimia monophylla, commonly known as the African lily, is a bulbous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, sword-shaped leaves that are often bluish-green in color. The plant produces tall, erect stems with showy white or pale purple flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance make it a favored ornamental plant in many regions.
