Dendrobium thyrsiflorum var. bronckartii

Dendrobium thyrsiflorum var. bronckartii, commonly known as the Bronckart's spike orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features tall, wiry stems that can grow up to 2 meters in height, often branching into dense clusters of flowers. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically blooming in summer. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in warm, humid environments.
