Dracophyllum gunnii

Dracophyllum gunnii, commonly known as the dwarf heath, is a compact shrub native to the cool, misty forests of New Zealand.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries, and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage. The plant features small, leathery leaves that are typically reddish-brown to dark green in color. It produces clusters of tiny, white or pale pink flowers in late winter to early spring.

Dracophyllum gunnii is well adapted to acidic, nutrient-poor soils and thrives in shaded, moist environments.
