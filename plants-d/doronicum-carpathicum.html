Doronicum carpathicum

Doronicum carpathicum, commonly known as the Carpathian leopard's bane, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It belongs to the family Ranunculaceae and is characterized by its upright stems and clusters of yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous habitats such as meadows and forest edges. Its leaves are palmately lobed, and the flowers have five sepals and numerous stamens.
