Deguelia nobilis

Deguelia nobilis, commonly known as the noble deguelia, is a climbing shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, butterfly-like flowers. The plant produces elongated pods that contain several seeds, and it is often found in moist, shaded habitats.

Its growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for horticultural use in tropical gardens.
