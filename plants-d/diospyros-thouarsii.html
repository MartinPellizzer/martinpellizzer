Diospyros thouarsii

Diospyros thouarsii, commonly known as the Madagascar date palm, is a tropical tree native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its large, palm-like leaves and smooth, grayish bark. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by edible, sweet fruits that resemble dates. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.
