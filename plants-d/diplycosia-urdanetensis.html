Diplycosia urdanetensis

Diplycosia urdanetensis, commonly known as the UrdaNet orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to the Andean region of Venezuela.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically features slender, hairy stems and leaves that are elliptical in shape. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white, with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
