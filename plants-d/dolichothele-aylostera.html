Dolichothele aylostera

Dolichothele aylostera, commonly known as the Aylostera spider.

This species is a type of orb-weaving spider found in various parts of the world, particularly in tropical and subtropical regions. It is characterized by its slender body and long, thin legs, which are adapted for constructing intricate web structures. The plant-like appearance of its web contributes to its name, which is derived from the Greek words for "long" and "web.".

While not a plant, Dolichothele aylostera plays an important ecological role in controlling insect populations through predation.
