Dais linifolia

Dais linifolia, commonly known as the silky daisy, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its soft, fuzzy leaves and bright yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in sunny environments. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Dais linifolia is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
