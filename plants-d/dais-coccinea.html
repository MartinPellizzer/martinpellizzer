Dais coccinea

Dais coccinea, commonly known as the red daisy, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red petals and yellow center, which resemble a classic daisy flower. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas and thrives in well-drained soil. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Dais coccinea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
