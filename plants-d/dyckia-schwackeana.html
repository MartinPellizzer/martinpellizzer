Dyckia schwackeana

Dyckia schwackeana, commonly known as the Schwackeana Dyckia, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its dense, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color with a waxy coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences that bear small, showy white or pink flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Dyckia schwackeana is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardens due to its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
