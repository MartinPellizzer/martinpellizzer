Dolichos staintonii

Dolichos staintonii, commonly known as the Stainton's dolichos, is a climbing legume native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its slender, twining stems and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are followed by elongated, bean-like pods. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and shrublands.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
