Doemia cordifolia var. leiocarpa

Doemia cordifolia var. leiocarpa, commonly known as the smooth-seeded heartleaf doemia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, and the plant is often found in moist, shaded environments.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
