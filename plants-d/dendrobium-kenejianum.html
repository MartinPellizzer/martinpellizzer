Dendrobium kenejianum

Dendrobium kenejianum, commonly known as the Kenejian's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with long, cane-like stems that often remain above ground. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged along the stem.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
