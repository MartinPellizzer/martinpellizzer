Dichorisandra nutabilis

Dichorisandra nutabilis, commonly known as the Natal orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy orchid-like flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte or lithophyte, often found on trees or rocks in humid environments. Its flowers are usually pink or purple and bloom during the warmer months.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in botanical gardens and by horticulturists.
