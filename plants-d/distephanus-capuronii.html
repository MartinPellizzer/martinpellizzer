Distephanus capuronii

Distephanus capuronii, commonly known as the Capuron's starfish flower, is a unique and rare plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a cluster of small flowers. The plant features striking, star-shaped blooms that are typically blue or violet in color, with a distinctive central disk. Its leaves are alternate, narrow, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its overall ornamental appeal.
