Dichrostachys villosa

Dichrostachys villosa, commonly known as the silky senna, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, often hairy stems and bipinnate leaves with small, rounded leaflets. The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in dense, racemose inflorescences, which are followed by slender, twisted pods. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland and savanna habitats.

Dichrostachys villosa is also valued for its medicinal properties and use in traditional remedies.
