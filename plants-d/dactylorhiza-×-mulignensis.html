Dactylorhiza × mulignensis

Dactylorhiza × mulignensis, commonly known as the hybrid marsh orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to parts of Europe and northern Africa.

It is a terrestrial herbaceous plant that typically grows in wet or damp habitats such as bogs, marshes, and along waterlogged soils. The plant features a rhizome with a single upright stem that bears several lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, arranged in a spike and have a distinctive three-lobed lip.

This species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with appropriate moisture conditions.
