Dillenia crenatifolia

Dillenia crenatifolia, commonly known as the serrated dillenia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, palmately lobed leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, yellowish-white flowers that are typically clustered in terminal inflorescences. Its fruits are large, fleshy berries that split open to reveal numerous seeds.

Dillenia crenatifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
