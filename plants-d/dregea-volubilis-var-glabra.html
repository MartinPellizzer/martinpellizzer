Dregea volubilis var. glabra

Dregea volubilis var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth dregea, is a climbing plant native to South America.

It features palmately lobed leaves that are typically hairy but are smooth in this variety. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow to orange, which bloom in clusters. It thrives in warm climates and is often used in ornamental gardening.

Its fruit is a follicle that splits open to release seeds, aiding in its propagation.
