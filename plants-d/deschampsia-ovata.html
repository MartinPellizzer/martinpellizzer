Deschampsia ovata

Deschampsia ovata, commonly known as narrowleaf wheatgrass, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically less than 1 centimeter wide. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to yellowish flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wet meadows and along streambanks.

Deschampsia ovata is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
