Dolichos setifolius

Dolichos setifolius, commonly known as the slender cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, erect stems and trifoliate leaves with hairy surfaces. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that give way to elongated, cylindrical pods containing edible seeds. It is well adapted to poor soils and is often cultivated for its nutritious seeds.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable crop in both agricultural and ecological contexts.
