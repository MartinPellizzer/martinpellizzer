Danais chassallia

Danais chassallia, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are usually pink or purple and bloom in the spring.

Danais chassallia is a popular ornamental plant due to its beauty and adaptability.
