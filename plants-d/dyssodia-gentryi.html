Dyssodia gentryi

Dyssodia gentryi, commonly known as Gentry's dyssodia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the southwestern United States, particularly Arizona and New Mexico, where it thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in its harsh habitat.
