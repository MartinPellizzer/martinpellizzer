Dentaria willdenowii

Dentaria willdenowii, commonly known as Willdenow's toothplant, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern North America.

It belongs to the family Brassicaceae, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant features basal leaves that are toothed and often hairy, giving it its common name. Its small, white or pale yellow flowers bloom in early spring, typically in clusters at the base of the plant.

Dentaria willdenowii is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and stream banks.
