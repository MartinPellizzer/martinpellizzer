Diglottis

Diglottis, commonly known as the bird's nest fern, is a type of fern that belongs to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its unique, shield-shaped fronds that resemble a bird's nest, hence its common name. The leaves are usually leathery and have a waxy surface, which helps reduce water loss in its natural habitat.
