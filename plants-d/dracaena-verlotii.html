Dracaena verlotii

Dracaena verlotii, commonly known as the Madagascar dragon tree, is a unique plant native to the island of Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, sword-shaped leaves that are often variegated with yellow and white markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous green flowers, followed by red, fleshy fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Dracaena verlotii is a relatively slow-growing plant, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
