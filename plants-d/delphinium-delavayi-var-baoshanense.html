Delphinium delavayi var. baoshanense

Delphinium delavayi var. baoshanense, commonly known as the Baoshan larkspur, is a striking perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan, China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height, adorned with large, showy blue to purple flowers. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are deeply lobed, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows.

Due to its unique beauty and ecological significance, it is a valued species in both horticulture and botanical research.
