Davyella colusana

Davyella colusana, commonly known as the pink rock orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, pinkish-purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, and the flowers bloom in late summer to early autumn.

Davyella colusana is often found in coastal and mountainous regions, where it adapts well to harsh environmental conditions.
