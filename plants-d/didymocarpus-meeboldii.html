Didymocarpus meeboldii

Didymocarpus meeboldii, commonly known as the Meebold's tamarind, is a small evergreen tree native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and often compound leaves. Due to its ornamental value and potential ecological importance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
