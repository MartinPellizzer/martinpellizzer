Dimorphandra mora

Dimorphandra mora, commonly known as the yellow-flowered legume, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant produces elongated, bean-like pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Dimorphandra mora is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some indigenous communities.
