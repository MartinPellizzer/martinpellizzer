Desmodium hondurense

Desmodium hondurense, commonly known as Honduras velvet bean, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is known for its nitrogen-fixing capabilities. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences. Its seeds are encased in pods and are used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The species is also valued for its role in agricultural systems due to its ability to improve soil fertility.
