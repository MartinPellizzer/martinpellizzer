Dalbergaria asteroloma

Dalbergaria asteroloma, commonly known as the yellow dalbergia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, with trifoliate leaves and small, pod-like fruits.
