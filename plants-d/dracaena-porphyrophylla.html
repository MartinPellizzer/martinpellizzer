Dracaena porphyrophylla

Dracaena porphyrophylla, commonly known as the red-leaf dracaena, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, dark red to purple foliage, which contrasts beautifully with its narrow, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that appear at the tips of its stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Dracaena porphyrophylla is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
