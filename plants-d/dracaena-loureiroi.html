Dracaena loureiroi

Dracaena loureiroi, commonly known as the Madagascar dragon tree, is a tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and rosette of long, narrow leaves that are typically dark green with a blueish tint. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers followed by red, fleshy fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dracaena loureiroi is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
