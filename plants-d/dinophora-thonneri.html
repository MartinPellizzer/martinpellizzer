Dinophora thonneri

Dinophora thonneri, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of violet, pink, and white, often with a contrasting central stripe. Its foliage consists of velvety, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green.

Dinophora thonneri is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
