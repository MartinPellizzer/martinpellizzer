Diplectria glabra var. micrantha

Diplectria glabra var. micrantha, commonly known as the smooth diplectria, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to certain regions of Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that are often hairless or nearly so, giving it its scientific name "glabra.". The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the summer months. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a smooth texture, contributing to its overall delicate appearance.

This variety is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as woodland edges and stream banks.
