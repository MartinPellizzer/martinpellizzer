Dierama plowesii

Dierama plowesii, commonly known as the Cape umbrella plant, is a striking succulent native to South Africa.

It features tall, slender stems that bear large, umbrella-like clusters of white or pink flowers. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
