Digitaria ischaemum var. asiatica

Digitaria ischaemum var. asiatica, commonly known as Asiatic Digitaria, is a grass species native to Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in a variety of soil types and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it both a weed and a potential forage plant in certain regions.
