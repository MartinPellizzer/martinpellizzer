Dendrostylis apeibifolia

Dendrostylis apeibifolia, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow petals and a central golden stamen that resembles a star. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers bloom in clusters, often during the rainy season, and are pollinated by insects.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
