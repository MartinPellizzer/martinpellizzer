Dracaena ascendens var. ascendens

Dracaena ascendens var. ascendens, commonly known as the dragon tree, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features tall, narrow leaves that are typically a deep green with a reddish tinge along the edges. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by red berries, which are toxic to humans and animals. It is valued for its ornamental appearance and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.

Its slow growth rate makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and as a statement plant in landscaping.
