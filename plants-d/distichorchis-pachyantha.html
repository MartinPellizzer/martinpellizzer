Distichorchis pachyantha

Distichorchis pachyantha, commonly known as the pink fairy lantern, is a striking orchid native to South Africa.

It features showy, pendulous flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, with a distinctive lantern-like shape. The plant has a slender, erect stem that bears numerous racemes of blossoms, which bloom in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, growing along the stem in a dense, compact arrangement.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
