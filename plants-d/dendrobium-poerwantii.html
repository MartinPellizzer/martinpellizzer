Dendrobium poerwantii

Dendrobium poerwantii, commonly known as the Papuan Dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to the highlands of New Guinea.

It features a compact, branching structure with numerous pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with good air circulation.

Dendrobium poerwantii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
