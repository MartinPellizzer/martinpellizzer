Dracula circe

Dracula circe, commonly known as the Queen of the Night, is a rare and striking orchid native to the high-altitude cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its dark, velvety petals and a unique, intricate flower structure that resembles a bat's wing. The plant thrives in cool, humid environments with filtered sunlight, requiring specific conditions to bloom. Its flowers are typically dark purple or black, with a subtle iridescent sheen.

Dracula circe is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts due to its rarity and aesthetic appeal.
