Desmodium batocaulon

Desmodium batocaulon, commonly known as the small-leaf desmodium, is a perennial legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features trifoliate leaves with small, rounded leaflets and produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is often found in open woodlands and disturbed soils, where it thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its seed pods are slender and contain several small, kidney-shaped seeds.

Desmodium batocaulon is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial in soil enrichment and ecological restoration efforts.
