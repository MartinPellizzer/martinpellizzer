Dillwynia ericifolia var. normalis

Dillwynia ericifolia var. normalis, commonly known as the common dillwynia, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features small, narrow leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs, giving them a silvery appearance. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and open forests.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable component of native plant communities.
