Digitaria stenostachya

Digitaria stenostachya, commonly known as slender bluestem, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas.

It is characterized by its narrow, bluish-green leaves and compact, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are often adorned with short, bristly hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as savannas and grasslands.

Digitaria stenostachya is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
