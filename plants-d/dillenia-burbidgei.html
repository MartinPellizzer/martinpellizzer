Dillenia burbidgei

Dillenia burbidgei, commonly known as the African dillenia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and alternate, palmately lobed leaves that are usually hairy on the underside. The plant produces large, showy yellow flowers with prominent stamens, which are followed by spherical, woody fruits containing several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy habitats.

Dillenia burbidgei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and floral display.
