Dendrobium × lavarackianum

Dendrobium × lavarackianum, commonly known as the Lavarack's orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in humid tropical environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with cane-like stems that can reach several meters in length. Its flowers are usually showy, with a range of colors including white, yellow, and orange, often arranged in racemes.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated for both horticultural and commercial purposes.
