Dorstenia ophiocoma var. minor

Dorstenia ophiocoma var. minor, commonly known as the miniature giant hogweed, is a unique member of the Moraceae family.

It is a climbing shrub characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and distinctive, spiral-shaped inflorescences. Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for horticultural and ecological studies.
