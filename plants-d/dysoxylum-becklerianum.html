Dysoxylum becklerianum

Dysoxylum becklerianum, commonly known as the black mangrove, is a tropical tree belonging to the family Meliaceae.

It is characterized by its dark, almost black bark and its ability to thrive in brackish water environments. The plant has opposite, simple leaves that are often elliptical in shape. It produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing several seeds.
