Dyschoriste nagchana

Dyschoriste nagchana, commonly known as the African water fern, is a species of aquatic fern native to southern Africa.

It thrives in freshwater environments such as ponds, lakes, and slow-moving streams. The plant has finely divided, feather-like fronds that are typically submerged or floating. Its rhizomes are often rooted in the substrate, allowing it to spread vegetatively.
