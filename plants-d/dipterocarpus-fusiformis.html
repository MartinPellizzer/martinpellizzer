Dipterocarpus fusiformis

Dipterocarpus fusiformis, commonly known as the narrow-lobed dipterocarp, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dipterocarpaceae and is characterized by its straight, cylindrical trunk and simple, alternate leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. Its fruits are woody capsules that split open to release seeds.

This species is valued for its durable wood and plays an important ecological role in tropical rainforests.
