Dombeya parviflora

Dombeya parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered dombeya, is a deciduous shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often gray-green in appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Dombeya parviflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
