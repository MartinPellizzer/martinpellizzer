Dioscorea futschauensis

Dioscorea futschauensis, commonly known as the Guangxi yam, is a tuberous plant belonging to the family Dioscoreaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly China, and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant produces edible tubers that are valued for their nutritional content and traditional medicinal uses. Its growth habit involves a climbing vine with heart-shaped leaves and small, greenish-white flowers.

The tubers are often used in local cuisines and have been studied for their potential health benefits.
