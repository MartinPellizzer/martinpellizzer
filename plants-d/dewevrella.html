Dewevrella

Dewevrella, commonly known as the silverleaf dewevrella, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery-white layer of pubescence. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and unique foliage, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
