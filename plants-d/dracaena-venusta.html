Dracaena venusta

Dracaena venusta, commonly known as the flame tree or red dracaena, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its striking, long, sword-shaped leaves that range from bright green to deep red. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, often followed by orange-red fruits. Dracaena venusta thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant foliage and adaptability to indoor conditions.
