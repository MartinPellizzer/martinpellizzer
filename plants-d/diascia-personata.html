Diascia personata

Diascia personata, commonly known as the common diascia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features upright, branching stems covered in small, oval-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically arranged in clusters and bloom from late spring to early autumn.

Diascia personata is well-suited for rock gardens and containers due to its compact size and drought tolerance.
