Dolichos trilobus subsp. transvaalicus

Dolichos trilobus subsp.

transvaalicus, commonly known as the Transvaal dolichos, is a climbing legume native to southern Africa. It features trifoliate leaves with three elliptical leaflets and produces small, yellow flowers clustered in racemes.

The plant produces elongated, twisted pods that contain several elliptical seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine.
