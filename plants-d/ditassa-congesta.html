Ditassa congesta

Ditassa congesta, commonly known as the dense clustered orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and yellow. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
