Dendrocalamus menglongensis

Dendrocalamus menglongensis, commonly known as the Menglong bamboo, is a species of giant bamboo native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can reach heights of up to 30 meters. The plant has a robust root system that helps stabilize the soil and prevent erosion. Its leaves are long and narrow, with a smooth texture and a dark green color.

This bamboo is valued for its strength and is often used in construction and as a source of raw material for various traditional crafts.
