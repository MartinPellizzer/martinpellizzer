Dipterygia polycephala

Dipterygia polycephala, commonly known as the clustered goldenbush, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense clusters of yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, daisy-like blooms.

Dipterygia polycephala is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in arid conditions.
