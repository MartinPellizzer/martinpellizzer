Dicksonia punctilobula var. poyseri

Dicksonia punctilobula var. poyseri, commonly known as the Poyser's tree fern, is a variant of the slender tree fern native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically in forest understories. Its fronds are typically dark green with a distinctive, serrated edge.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
