Dianthus laingsburgensis

Dianthus laingsburgensis, commonly known as the Laingsburg carnation, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant features upright, branching stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Dianthus laingsburgensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
