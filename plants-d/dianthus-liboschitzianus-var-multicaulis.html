Dianthus liboschitzianus var. multicaulis

Dianthus liboschitzianus var. multicaulis, commonly known as the multicaulis carnation, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features compact, bushy growth with dense clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that form a basal rosette, and its stems are often branched, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It blooms from late spring to early summer, producing fragrant flowers that attract pollinators.

This variety is valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in well-drained soils in full sun.
