Dunalia Gesnerioides

Dunalia gesnerioides is a striking evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. Known for its glossy, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant, white flowers, it is a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens. This plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to flourish. Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a favorite among plant enthusiasts. With its elegant foliage and delicate blooms, Dunalia gesnerioides adds a touch of exotic beauty to any space.
