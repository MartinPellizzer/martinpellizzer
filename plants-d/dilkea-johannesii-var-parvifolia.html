Dilkea johannesii var. parvifolia

Dilkea johannesii var. parvifolia, commonly known as the small-leaved dilkea, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, ovate leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs, giving it a textured appearance. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white to pale yellow flowers during the late winter to early spring season. Its flowering period makes it a valuable addition to gardens in regions with mild winters.

The species is often found in rocky or sandy soils, thriving in well-drained environments.
