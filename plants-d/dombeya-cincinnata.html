Dombeya cincinnata

Dombeya cincinnata, commonly known as the narrowleaf dombeya, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and has a woody stem that becomes more branched with age. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Dombeya cincinnata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive blooms.
