Deutzia parviflora f. obscura

Deutzia parviflora f. obscura, commonly known as the dark-flowered deutzia, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, clustered white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by red or orange berries in the fall. The leaves are ovate to elliptical, with a smooth texture and a dark green color throughout the growing season. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, moist soil.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental value and adaptability in garden settings.
