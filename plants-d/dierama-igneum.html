Dierama igneum

Dierama igneum, commonly known as the flame lily, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb with tall, slender stems that bear clusters of bright red or orange flowers. The leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a slight reddish tinge. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
