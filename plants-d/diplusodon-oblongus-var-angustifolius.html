Diplusodon oblongus var. angustifolius

Diplusodon oblongus var. angustifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved diplusodon, is a variant of the diplusodon species characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to specific regions in South America, particularly in the Andean foothills. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in sunny to partially shaded environments. Its leaves are lanceolate, with a smooth margin and a slightly waxy texture, which helps reduce water loss.
