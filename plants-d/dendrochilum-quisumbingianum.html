Dendrochilum quisumbingianum

Dendrochilum quisumbingianum, commonly known as the Quisumbing orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in tropical climates.

The flowers are usually pale green to yellowish, with intricate patterns that attract specific pollinators.
