Diascia integerrima

Diascia integerrima, commonly known as the winter begonia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with upright stems and small, lobed leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Diascia integerrima is known for its ability to bloom throughout the winter months, adding color to gardens during the colder season.
