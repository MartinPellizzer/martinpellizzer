Dalbergia telfairii

Dalbergia telfairii, commonly known as the Telfairia or Telfair's ebony, is a small to medium-sized leguminous tree native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its dark, dense wood that is highly valued for its durability and aesthetic appeal. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that are clustered in racemes, followed by small, pod-like fruits containing several seeds.

It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a warm, humid climate for optimal growth.
