Diplazium hainanense

Diplazium hainanense, commonly known as Hainan shield fern, is a species of fern native to the island of Hainan in China.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a leathery texture, with a prominent midrib.

Diplazium hainanense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic foliage.
