Deutzia × carnea f. stellata

Deutzia × carnea f. stellata, commonly known as the starform deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early spring, often in clusters of 5 to 7 petals. The plant has slender, arching branches and small, ovate leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value comes from its delicate floral display and compact growth habit.
