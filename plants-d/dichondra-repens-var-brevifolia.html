Dichondra repens var. brevifolia

Dichondra repens var. brevifolia, commonly known as silver carpet, is a low-growing, mat-forming plant native to southern Africa.

It features small, heart-shaped leaves with a silvery-blue underside, which gives it its common name. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous white flowers that are often overlooked. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ground cover in gardens.

Its rapid growth and ability to spread easily make it effective for erosion control and ornamental use.
