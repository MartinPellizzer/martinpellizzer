Dianthus chinensis var. longisquama

Dianthus chinensis var. longisquama, commonly known as the Chinese carnation, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It features compact, bushy growth with dark green, lance-shaped leaves. The flowers are showy, with pink to red petals that have a ruffled, frilled appearance. It blooms in late spring to early summer, producing clusters of fragrant, showy flowers.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and long-lasting blooms.
