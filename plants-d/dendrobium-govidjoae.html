Dendrobium govidjoae

Dendrobium govidjoae, commonly known as the Wandoan orchid, is a rare epiphytic orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, evergreen pseudobulbs and linear leaves. The plant produces inflorescences with numerous fragrant, showy flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific care to flourish in cultivation.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat threats, it is considered a vulnerable species.
