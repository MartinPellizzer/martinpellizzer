Daviesia newbeyi

Daviesia newbeyi, commonly known as the Grey Box, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its grey-green foliage and small, yellowish flowers. Daviesia newbeyi is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
