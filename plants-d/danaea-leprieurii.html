Danaea leprieurii

Danaea leprieurii, commonly known as the flame flower, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean and Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy red-orange flowers that resemble flames, which bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are glossy and leathery in texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Danaea leprieurii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant floral display.
