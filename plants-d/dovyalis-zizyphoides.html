Dovyalis zizyphoides

Dovyalis zizyphoides, commonly known as the sour plum or Natal plum, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It produces small, yellow to orange fruits that are typically sour but can be sweet when ripe. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and clusters of small white or greenish-yellow flowers. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in coastal areas and riverine forests.

The fruit is used in traditional medicine and is also valued for its ornamental features.
