Deutzia setchuenensis var. corymbiflora

Deutzia setchuenensis var. corymbiflora, commonly known as the Chinese deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy clusters of white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are typically hairy on the underside. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
