Dendrobium cyrtolobum

Dendrobium cyrtolobum, commonly known as the golden helmet orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Australia.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with long, narrow leaves and clusters of bright yellow flowers. Its flowers are highly fragrant and bloom during the warmer months, attracting various pollinators.

Dendrobium cyrtolobum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
