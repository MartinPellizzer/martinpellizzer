Decaceras

Decaceras, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean and the eastern Mediterranean, where it thrives in sandy or rocky soils. Decaceras is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
