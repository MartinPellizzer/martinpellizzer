Dendrobium × apollo

Dendrobium × apollo, commonly known as the Apollo Phalaenopsis, is a hybrid orchid that combines traits from Dendrobium and Phalaenopsis species.

It is a sympodial orchid with a short, compact growth habit and often produces multiple flower spikes from the same stem. The plant features leathery, dark green leaves and is prized for its showy, long-lasting blooms in shades of white, pink, and lavender. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to prevent root rot.

This hybrid is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
