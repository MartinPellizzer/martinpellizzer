Delphinium diversifolium var. harneyense

Delphinium diversifolium var. harneyense, commonly known as Harney's larkspur, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant produces showy, blue to violet flowers arranged in dense spikes, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lanceolate to ovate, with a rough texture and prominent veins.

This variety is adapted to rocky and dry habitats, showcasing its resilience in harsh environments.
