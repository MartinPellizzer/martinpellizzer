Dimeria ornithopoda var. nana

Dimeria ornithopoda var. nana, commonly known as the dwarf desert daisy, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of North America.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, characterized by its fleshy leaves and showy flowers. This variety is distinguished by its compact size and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
