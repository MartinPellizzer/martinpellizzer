Dendrobium melanostictum

Dendrobium melanostictum, commonly known as the black-streaked orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit, with cane-like stems that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with dark red markings, which bloom during the rainy season. Its leaves are elliptical and arranged in two rows along the stems.

Dendrobium melanostictum thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.
