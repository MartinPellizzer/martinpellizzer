Dendrobium brevicaule subsp. pentagonum

Dendrobium brevicaule subsp.

pentagonum, commonly known as the pentagonal dendrobium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia. It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in humid tropical environments.

The plant features a compact growth habit with short, thick stems and lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually yellow or greenish-yellow, with a distinctive pentagonal shape in cross-section.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
