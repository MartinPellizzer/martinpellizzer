Draba pamirica

Draba pamirica, commonly known as the Pamir bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Pamir Mountains.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. This species is characterized by its small, white or pale purple flowers and basal rosette of leaves. It thrives in alpine and arctic environments, typically growing at elevations above 3,000 meters.

Draba pamirica is adapted to cold climates and has a short growing season, flowering early in the summer.
