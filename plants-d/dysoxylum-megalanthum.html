Dysoxylum megalanthum

Dysoxylum megalanthum, commonly known as the giant flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in shades of red and orange. The plant has a dense, spreading canopy with long, pinnate leaves that provide shade. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release numerous seeds.

Dysoxylum megalanthum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
