Deinandra streetsii

Deinandra streetsii, commonly known as the Cape York sandthorn, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and linear, succulent leaves adapted to arid environments. The plant thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal regions. It has a woody stem with thorny branches, which help protect it from herbivores.

Deinandra streetsii is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in harsh conditions.
