Dracaena zeelandica

Dracaena zeelandica, commonly known as the Zeeland dragon tree, is a striking evergreen plant native to the Netherlands.

It features tall, slender stems with lance-shaped, dark green leaves that have a glossy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by red, fleshy berries. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and air-purifying properties.

Dracaena zeelandica thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to maintain its health.
