Descurainia pinnata subsp. pinnata

Descurainia pinnata subsp.

pinnata, commonly known as yellow rocket, is an annual flowering plant native to North America. It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and pinnately divided leaves.

The plant typically grows in moist soils along roadsides, fields, and wetlands. It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbages.

Its botanical features make it a common weed in agricultural and natural settings.
