Dregea stellaris

Dregea stellaris, commonly known as the star dregea, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with small, pointed leaflets. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its unique floral structure and drought tolerance make it a distinctive addition to botanical collections.
