Desmodium zenkeri

Desmodium zenkeri, commonly known as the African burr legume, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense clusters. The plant is notable for its distinctive, spiny pods that contain several kidney-shaped seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

Desmodium zenkeri is also valued for its use in traditional medicine and as a forage plant for livestock.
