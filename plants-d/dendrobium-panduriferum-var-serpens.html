Dendrobium panduriferum var. serpens

Dendrobium panduriferum var. serpens, commonly known as the serpent orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, gracefully spiraled stems that can grow up to several meters in length, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellow-green flowers with red-brown markings, which bloom during the rainy season. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is popular among orchid enthusiasts.
