Drosanthemum prostratum

Drosanthemum prostratum, commonly known as the prostrate drosanthemum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming species that produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features fleshy, succulent leaves that help it store water, adapting to arid environments. Its flowers are typically borne on long, slender stems and bloom in spring and early summer.

Drosanthemum prostratum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.
