Delphinium maackianum f. ussuriense

Delphinium maackianum f. ussuriense, commonly known as the Ussuri delphinium, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters. The plant produces showy blue to violet flowers in clusters, typically blooming in late summer. Its leaves are palmately divided, with a rough, hairy texture.

Native to the temperate regions of East Asia, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.
