Dysoxylum magnifolium

Dysoxylum magnifolium, commonly known as the magnificent dysoxylum, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in diameter. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by woody capsules containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The tree is valued for its hard, durable wood and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
