Draba natolica

Draba natolica, commonly known as the alpine bellflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in alpine and arctic regions. The plant features basal leaves that are often woolly and covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, white or pale purple, and are arranged in a raceme.

Draba natolica is adapted to cold climates and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils.
