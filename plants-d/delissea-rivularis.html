Delissea rivularis

Delissea rivularis, commonly known as the river delissea, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as stream banks and forest floors. The plant features slender, ascending stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, clustered flowers are usually white or pale pink, blooming in spring or early summer.

Delissea rivularis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid, low-light conditions.
