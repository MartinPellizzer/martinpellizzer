Daphniphyllum himalense var. triangulatum

Daphniphyllum himalense var. triangulatum, commonly known as the Himalayan holly, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage and distinctive, clustered inflorescences. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often at high elevations. Its leaves are leathery and glossy, with a unique triangular shape that gives it its varietal name.

This plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in horticulture for its aesthetic appeal.
