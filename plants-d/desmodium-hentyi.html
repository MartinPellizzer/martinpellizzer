Desmodium hentyi

Desmodium hentyi, commonly known as the African velvet bean, is a leguminous plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces cylindrical pods that contain several elliptical seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils.

Desmodium hentyi is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in agricultural systems.
