Deparia nephrodioides

Deparia nephrodioides, commonly known as the kidney fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It features fronds that are bi-lobed and resemble a kidney shape, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are pinnately divided, with a delicate, feathery appearance.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
