Davallia lapeyrousei

Davallia lapeyrousei, commonly known as the silver lace fern, is a compact fern native to Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, lacy fronds with a silvery sheen, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with short, horizontal stems that produce new shoots. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, moist soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
