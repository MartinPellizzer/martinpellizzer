Dendrophila trifoliata

Dendrophila trifoliata, commonly known as the three-leaf orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive trifoliate leaves, which consist of three leaflets arranged symmetrically. Native to tropical regions, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pink.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
