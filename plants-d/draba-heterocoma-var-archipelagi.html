Draba heterocoma var. archipelagi

Draba heterocoma var. archipelagi, commonly known as the island rockcress, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, cruciform flowers and pinnately lobed leaves.
