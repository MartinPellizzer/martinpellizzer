Deparia Vermiformis

Deparia vermiformis is a unique fern species known for its delicate, thread-like fronds. Found primarily in moist, shaded environments, it thrives in temperate climates. Its name, meaning "worm-like," reflects the slender appearance of its leaves. Despite its small size, it plays an important role in forest ecosystems. This plant is both a subject of botanical interest and a symbol of natural beauty.
