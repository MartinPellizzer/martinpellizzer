Dudleya cymosa subsp. ovatifolia

Dudleya cymosa subsp.

ovatifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf stonecrop, is a succulent perennial native to California. It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, ovate leaves that are typically gray-green in color.

The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
