Dahlia mollis

Dahlia mollis, commonly known as the soft dahlia, is a herbaceous perennial native to Mexico.

It features showy, single or double flowers in a range of colors, including red, yellow, and white. The plant has smooth, green, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces tuberous roots that store nutrients and allow it to survive in various soil conditions.

Dahlia mollis thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
