Draconia kelleri

Draconia kelleri, commonly known as the dragon's tongue plant, is a unique succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often ribbed and can vary in color from green to reddish hues. The plant produces striking, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unusual appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
