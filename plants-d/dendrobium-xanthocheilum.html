Dendrobium xanthocheilum

Dendrobium xanthocheilum, commonly known as the golden lip dendrobium, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features elongated, cane-like pseudobulbs covered in leathery, dark green leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a distinctive lip that is pale yellow to orange. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium xanthocheilum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its long-lasting blooms.
