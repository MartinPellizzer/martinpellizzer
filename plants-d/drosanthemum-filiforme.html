Drosanthemum filiforme

Drosanthemum filiforme, commonly known as the slender drosanthemum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, thread-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
