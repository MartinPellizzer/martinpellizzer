Dierama medium var. mossii

Dierama medium var. mossii, commonly known as the mossy dierama, is a striking ornamental plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, erect stems adorned with narrow, grass-like leaves that give it a distinctive, elegant appearance. The plant produces clusters of vibrant, tubular flowers in shades of pink or purple, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its unique foliage and showy blooms make it a popular choice for adding visual interest to gardens.
