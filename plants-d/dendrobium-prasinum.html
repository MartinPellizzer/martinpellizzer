Dendrobium prasinum

Dendrobium prasinum, commonly known as the green orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome that supports multiple stems. The plant produces bright green, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It blooms with small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically during the rainy season.

Dendrobium prasinum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
