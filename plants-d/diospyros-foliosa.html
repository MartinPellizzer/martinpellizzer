Diospyros foliosa

Diospyros foliosa, commonly known as the African persimmon, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Ebenaceae family, which includes ebony and persimmon species. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to elliptical in shape. Its small, yellowish-green flowers are followed by small, round fruits that mature to a deep red or orange color.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some African cultures.
