Dracaena reflexa var. linearifolia

Dracaena reflexa var. linearifolia, commonly known as the ribbon plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, narrow, sword-shaped leaves that grow in a dense rosette, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant is characterized by its slow growth and ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular houseplant. Its leaves are typically a deep green, though some cultivars may exhibit variegation.

Dracaena reflexa var. linearifolia is also noted for its ability to tolerate a range of indoor environments and requires minimal maintenance.
