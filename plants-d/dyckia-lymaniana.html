Dyckia lymaniana

Dyckia lymaniana, commonly known as the Lyman's dyckia, is a species of bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its dense, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color with a waxy coating. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers that emerge from the center of the rosette. Dyckia lymaniana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various growing conditions.

It prefers full sun to partial shade and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
