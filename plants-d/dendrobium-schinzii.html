Dendrobium schinzii

Dendrobium schinzii, commonly known as the Schinz's orchid, is a sympodial epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, cane-like stems that store water and nutrients, typical of many orchid species. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with a distinctive red lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Dendrobium schinzii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
