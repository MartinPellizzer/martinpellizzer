Drymonia laciniosa

Drymonia laciniosa, commonly known as the lace fern, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, lacy fronds that give it its common name. The plant features a short, upright rhizome with branching, feather-like leaves that are often green with a bluish tinge. Drymonia laciniosa thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and ability to thrive in controlled indoor conditions.
