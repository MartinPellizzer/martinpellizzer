Diaphanoptera khorasanica

Diaphanoptera khorasanica, commonly known as the Persian glasswinged grasshopper, is a species of grasshopper native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, translucent wings that give it a unique visual appearance. The plant-like appearance of its wings has led to its common name, though it is not a true plant. It inhabits dry, sandy environments and is often found in areas with sparse vegetation.

This insect plays a role in the local ecosystem as both a predator and a prey species.
