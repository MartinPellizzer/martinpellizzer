Distrianthes lamii

Distrianthes lamii, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and is often found in areas with well-drained soil. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered fruits.

Distrianthes lamii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
