Dicranopteris linearis var. hirta

Dicranopteris linearis var. hirta, commonly known as the hairy cliff fern, is a variant of the common water fern.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its delicate, linear fronds that are covered in fine, hairy structures. This fern thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in rocky crevices or along stream banks. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The plant's botanical features make it well-adapted to humid, temperate climates.
