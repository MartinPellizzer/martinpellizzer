Deutzia purpurascens var. pauciflora

Deutzia purpurascens var. pauciflora, commonly known as the few-flowered deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features slender, arching branches that bear clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers in early summer. The plant has dark green, ovate leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and moderate growth habit in garden settings.
