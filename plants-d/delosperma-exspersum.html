Delosperma exspersum

Delosperma exspersum, commonly known as the "ice plant," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant pink or red flowers in the summer months. It is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance.

Delosperma exspersum is also known for its ability to form dense mats, making it an effective ground cover.
