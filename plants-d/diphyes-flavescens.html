Diphyes flavescens

Diphyes flavescens, commonly known as the golden flame fern, is a striking plant native to tropical regions.

It features distinctive, bright yellow fronds that give it its common name. The plant has a unique rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread across moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and possess a waxy texture.

Botanically, it belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
