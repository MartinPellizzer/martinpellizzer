Dracaena malawiana

Dracaena malawiana, commonly known as the Malawi dracaena, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of East Africa.

It features long, narrow, dark green leaves that are often adorned with white or silver markings, giving it a distinctive and elegant appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance of low light and moderate watering.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both residential and commercial spaces.
