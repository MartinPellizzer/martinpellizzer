Dryopteris nobilis

Dryopteris nobilis, commonly known as the noble sword fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to the eastern United States.

It features fronds that are typically upright and lance-shaped, with a distinctive dark green color and a metallic sheen. The plant produces sporangia on the underside of its fronds, which are clustered in sori. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Dryopteris nobilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly used in horticulture.
