Dendrosenecio johnstonii subsp. elgonensis

Dendrosenecio johnstonii subsp.

elgonensis, commonly known as the Elgon mountain groundsel, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the East African mountain range, including Mount Elgon. It is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters.

The plant is adapted to cool, moist environments and thrives in rocky or sandy soils. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Due to its restricted habitat, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
