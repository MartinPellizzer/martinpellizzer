Disa klugei

Disa klugei, commonly known as the Kluge's disa, is a species of orchid native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant grows in rocky, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its flowering period occurs during the summer months, and it is often found in mountainous regions.

Disa klugei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
