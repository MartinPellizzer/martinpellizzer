Dillenia Obovata

Dillenia obovata is a striking plant known for its large, showy flowers and unique leaf structure. Native to parts of Southeast Asia, it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant features thick, waxy leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. Its flowers, which bloom in clusters, attract a variety of pollinators. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively obscure species in the botanical world.
