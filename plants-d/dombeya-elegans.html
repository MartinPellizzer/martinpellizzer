Dombeya elegans

Dombeya elegans, commonly known as the elegant dombeya, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and are produced in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped, aromatic leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Dombeya elegans is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
