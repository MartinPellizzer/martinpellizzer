Dypsis longipes

Dypsis longipes, commonly known as the Madagascar palm, is a unique succulent native to Madagascar.

It features a short, stocky trunk with a crown of stiff, spiky leaves that resemble a palm. The plant has a distinctive appearance with its thick, fleshy stems and waxy, blue-green foliage. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow.

Dypsis longipes is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
