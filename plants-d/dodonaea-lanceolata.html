Dodonaea lanceolata

Dodonaea lanceolata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hop bush, is a shrub native to Australia.

It features slender, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in spikes, which bloom in spring. Its adaptability to various soil types and drought tolerance make it a hardy species.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in coastal areas.
