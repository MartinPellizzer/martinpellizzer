Drimys montana

Drimys montana, commonly known as the mountain drimys, is a flowering plant native to the temperate regions of South America.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows in humid, cool climates. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, often with a slight waxy coating. Its flowers are small, white, and fragrant, appearing in clusters during the spring.

Drimys montana is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique appearance and fragrance.
