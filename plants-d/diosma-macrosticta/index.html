Diosma Macrosticta

Bluebell vine, scientifically known as Diosma macrosticta, is a striking climber native to tropical regions of Central and South America. This plant is celebrated for its vibrant blue and purple flowers that cascade in delicate clusters, creating a mesmerizing display. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses. Its unique appearance and easy care make it a favorite among plant enthusiasts. With its graceful vines and colorful blooms, the Bluebell vine adds a touch of exotic beauty to any landscape.
