Dendranthema variifolium

Dendranthema variifolium, commonly known as the variable leaf fleur-de-lis, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features alternate, hairy leaves that vary in shape, giving it its common name. It produces showy yellow flowers with white ray florets, typically blooming in late summer.

Dendranthema variifolium is often grown in gardens for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate in well-drained soil.
