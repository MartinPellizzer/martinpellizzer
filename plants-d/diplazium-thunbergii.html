Diplazium thunbergii

Diplazium thunbergii, commonly known as the Thunberg's fork fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, bipinnate fronds that are often divided into narrow, feather-like segments. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. The fronds are usually evergreen, providing a lush green appearance throughout the year.
