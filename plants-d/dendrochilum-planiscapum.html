Dendrochilum planiscapum

Dendrochilum planiscapum, commonly known as the flat-capped orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, flat, rounded flower spikes that bear small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Dendrochilum planiscapum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
