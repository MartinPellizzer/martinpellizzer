Dendrobium hekouense

Dendrobium hekouense, commonly known as the Hekou dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to China.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems that often bear clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments on trees or rocks in mountainous regions. Its leaves are usually elliptical and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stems.

Dendrobium hekouense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
