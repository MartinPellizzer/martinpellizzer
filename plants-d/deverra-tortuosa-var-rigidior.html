Deverra tortuosa var. rigidior

Deverra tortuosa var. rigidior, commonly known as the rigid velvet tamarind, is a species of flowering plant in the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and distinctive, twisted growth habit. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, followed by elongated, woody pods containing seeds. Native to parts of Africa, it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in some regions.
