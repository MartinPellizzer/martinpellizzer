Digitaria neghellensis

Digitaria neghellensis, commonly known as the Neghell's Digitaria, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.

Digitaria neghellensis is noted for its adaptability to various environmental conditions.
