Dysoxylum nitidum var. oblongifoliolum

Dysoxylum nitidum var. oblongifoliolum, commonly known as the oblong-leaf dysoxylum, is a tropical tree species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and large, elliptical leaves that are often glossy in appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by woody capsules containing several seeds. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The leaves and bark of this species have been used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
