Dactylorchis hoeppneri

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments within cloud forests. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single or few flowers that are usually pale green to yellowish in color. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern at the base of the plant.
