Dolichos sphaerospermus

Dolichos sphaerospermus, commonly known as the globular cowpea, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific Islands. The plant is characterized by its erect, branching stems and trifoliate leaves. Its small, yellowish-white flowers are followed by spherical, hairy pods containing edible seeds.

The species is valued for its nutritional content and is cultivated as a legume for both human consumption and animal feed.
