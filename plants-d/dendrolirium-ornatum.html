Dendrolirium ornatum

Dendrolirium ornatum, commonly known as the golden lily, is a herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It features a rosette of narrow, sword-shaped leaves that form a dense basal cluster. The plant produces striking, showy flowers with bright yellow petals and a distinctive red or orange throat. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest understories.

Dendrolirium ornatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
