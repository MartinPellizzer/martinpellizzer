Dictamnus dasycarpus

Dictamnus dasycarpus, commonly known as the rough-fruited dictamnus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

Dictamnus dasycarpus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and its ability to thrive in well-drained, rocky soils.
