Dactylorhiza purpurella subsp. majaliformis

Dactylorhiza purpurella subsp.

majaliformis, commonly known as the purple orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia. It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of purple or violet flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and grasslands. It has a complex root system and relies on mycorrhizal associations for nutrient uptake.

Its flowering period usually occurs in late spring to early summer.
