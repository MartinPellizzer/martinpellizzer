Delosperma acocksii f. luxurians

Delosperma acocksii f. luxurians, commonly known as the luxuriant ice plant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves and vibrant pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems and leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms.
