Dictyopteris barberi

Dictyopteris barberi, commonly known as the barber's feather widgeonweed, is a species of green algae belonging to the family Dictyopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that are typically pale green to brown in color. This alga is often found in coastal and estuarine environments, where it attaches to submerged surfaces such as rocks and shells. Its structure consists of a central axis with branching, filamentous fronds that enhance its ability to capture sunlight for photosynthesis.

The plant plays a role in marine ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various small aquatic organisms.
