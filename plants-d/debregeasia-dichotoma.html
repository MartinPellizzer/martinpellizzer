Debregeasia dichotoma

Debregeasia dichotoma, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and clusters of showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters and prefers well-drained soil and bright, indirect light. It is a popular ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.

The species is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of indoor environments.
