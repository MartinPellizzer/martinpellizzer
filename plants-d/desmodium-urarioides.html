Desmodium urarioides

Desmodium urarioides, commonly known as the hairy desmodium, is a perennial legume native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which are often covered in fine, hairy textures. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and woodland edges.

Desmodium urarioides is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in soil enrichment and sustainable agriculture.
