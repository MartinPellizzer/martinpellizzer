Dracaena cooperi var. striata

Dracaena cooperi var. striata, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with dark green bands and light green or white stripes, creating a zebra-like pattern. The plant has stiff, upright leaves that can grow up to several feet in length, giving it a dramatic appearance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Dracaena cooperi var. striata is a popular indoor plant due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
