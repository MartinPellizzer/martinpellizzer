Deutzia × candida

Deutzia × candida, commonly known as the white deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to Asia.

It features clusters of white, fragrant flowers that bloom in early summer, adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant has slender, arching branches and small, oval-shaped leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for a variety of garden settings.

Deutzia × candida is often used in landscaping for its attractive flowers and moderate growth habit.
