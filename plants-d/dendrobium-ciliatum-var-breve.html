Dendrobium ciliatum var. breve

Dendrobium ciliatum var. breve, commonly known as the short-leafed dendrobium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees or rocks in tropical rainforests. The plant features short, cane-like stems covered in dense clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are narrow and strap-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
