Dieffenbachia weirii

Dieffenbachia weirii, commonly known as the Snowflake Dieffenbachia, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, waxy, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity to maintain its vibrant foliage. Its leaves are toxic if ingested, containing calcium oxalate crystals that can cause irritation.

Dieffenbachia weirii is a popular houseplant due to its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
