Disperis stefan-jetteri

Disperis stefan-jetteri, commonly known as the Stefan-Jetter's orchid, is a rare and enigmatic member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the high-altitude regions of Tanzania, where it thrives in cool, moist microhabitats. The plant produces delicate, greenish-yellow flowers with a unique structure that resembles a spider, a characteristic feature of many Disperis species.
