Dracaena indivisa var. atropurpurea

Dracaena indivisa var. atropurpurea, commonly known as the purple dracaena, is a striking ornamental plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and long, narrow leaves that are a deep purple to reddish color, especially when exposed to bright light. The plant is evergreen and can grow up to several meters in height, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor spaces. Its foliage provides a dramatic visual impact, and it is relatively low maintenance, thriving in well-drained soil and moderate to bright light.

This variety is also known for its ability to purify indoor air, adding to its appeal as a decorative and beneficial houseplant.
