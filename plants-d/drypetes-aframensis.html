Drypetes aframensis

Drypetes aframensis, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, followed by distinctive red, fleshy fruits that contain several seeds.
