Draba Alpina Var. Pilosa

Draba alpina var. pilosa is a hardy alpine plant known for its delicate appearance and resilience in harsh environments. It thrives in rocky soils and high altitudes, often found in mountain regions across Europe and Asia. The plant's small, hairy leaves and clusters of white flowers make it a distinctive sight in alpine meadows. Its ability to survive in extreme conditions makes it an important species for ecological studies. This variety is also valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens that mimic natural landscapes.
