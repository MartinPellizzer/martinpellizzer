Deutzia gracilis var. eximia

Deutzia gracilis var. eximia, commonly known as the slender deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, arching branches and dark green, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value comes from its delicate flowers and compact growth habit.
