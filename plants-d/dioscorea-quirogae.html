Dioscorea quirogae

Dioscorea quirogae, commonly known as the Quirogae yam, is a species of flowering plant in the family Dioscoreaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces tuberous roots that are used in traditional medicine and as a food source in some cultures. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped with a rough texture, and it bears small, greenish-white flowers.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties and has been studied for potential therapeutic applications.
