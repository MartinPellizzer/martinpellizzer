Delissea ovatisepala

Delissea ovatisepala, commonly known as the broad-sepaled delissea, is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in clusters. Its leaves are broad and ovate, providing a lush green backdrop to its vibrant blossoms.

Delissea ovatisepala thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
