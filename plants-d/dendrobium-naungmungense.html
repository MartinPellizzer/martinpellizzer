Dendrobium naungmungense

Dendrobium naungmungense, commonly known as the Naungmung orchid, is a species of orchid native to Myanmar.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on the surface of trees or other plants without deriving nutrients from them. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It belongs to the Dendrobium genus, which includes many epiphytic and lithophytic orchids.

Dendrobium naungmungense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
