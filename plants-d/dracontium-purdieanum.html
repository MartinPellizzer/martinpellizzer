Dracontium purdieanum

Dracontium purdieanum, commonly known as the purple nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshade plants. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces striking purple flowers that bloom in the summer. Its berries are toxic and contain alkaloids, making it potentially harmful to humans and animals.

Despite its toxicity, it is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant appearance.
