Decaloba exsudans

Decaloba exsudans, commonly known as the weeping fig, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement and have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by round, fleshy fruits containing several seeds. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in indoor environments.

However, it can be toxic to pets if ingested, making it important to handle with care.
