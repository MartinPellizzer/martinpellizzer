Dendrobium macropus subsp. gracilicaule

Dendrobium macropus subsp.

gracilicaule, commonly known as the slender-stemmed Dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia. It features a tall, slender stem with numerous nodes and is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves.

The plant produces racemes of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with prominent yellow lips. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires good air circulation and moderate watering.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and ecological adaptations within its native habitat.
