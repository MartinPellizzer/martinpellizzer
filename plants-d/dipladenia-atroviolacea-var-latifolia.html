Dipladenia atroviolacea var. latifolia

Dipladenia atroviolacea var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf Dipladenia, is a tropical vine native to South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are broad and glossy, providing a striking visual appeal. The plant produces clusters of tubular, violet-pink flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil and plenty of sunlight.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
