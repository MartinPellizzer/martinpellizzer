Dyckia deltoidea

Dyckia deltoidea, commonly known as the delta dyckia, is a species of flowering plant in the bromeliad family.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming structure with narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces striking, showy inflorescences that are typically orange or red in color. Dyckia deltoidea is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements contribute to its appeal among succulent enthusiasts.
