Digitaria formosana

Digitaria formosana, commonly known as the Taiwan digitaria, is a species of grass native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in moist, sunny environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Digitaria formosana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
