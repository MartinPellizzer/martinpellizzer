Dryopteris omeiensis

Dryopteris omeiensis, commonly known as the Sichuan wood fern, is a species of fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its fronds that are typically bipinnate, with a dark green color and a leathery texture. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in mountainous regions with high humidity.

Its spores are produced on the underside of the fronds, and it plays a role in forest ecosystems by contributing to soil stability and providing habitat for various microorganisms.
