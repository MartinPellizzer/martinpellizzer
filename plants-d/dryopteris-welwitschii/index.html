Dryopteris Welwitschii

Dryopteris welwitschii is a rare and striking fern native to the high altitudes of Angola and the Democratic Republic of the Congo. Its unique fronds, characterized by their dark green color and intricate patterns, make it a standout among fern species. This plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest understories. Due to its limited natural range and habitat destruction, it is considered endangered by conservationists. Its beauty and rarity have made it a subject of interest for botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
