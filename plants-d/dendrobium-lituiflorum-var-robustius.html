Dendrobium lituiflorum var. robustius

Dendrobium lituiflorum var. robustius, commonly known as the robust orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic orchid that grows on trees and rocks, relying on atmospheric moisture for nourishment. The plant features long, cane-like stems that bear clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. Its leaves are strap-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its delicate blooms.
