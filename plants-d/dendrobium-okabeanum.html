Dendrobium okabeanum

Dendrobium okabeanum, commonly known as the Okabean orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It features long, cylindrical pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, typical of many orchid species. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with yellow or red markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Dendrobium okabeanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
