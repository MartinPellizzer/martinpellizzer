Denscantia

Denscantia, commonly known as the silverleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of silver-gray, fuzzy leaves that help reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by fluffy white seeds for wind dispersal. Denscantia is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it a hardy ornamental plant.

Its unique foliage and resilience make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
