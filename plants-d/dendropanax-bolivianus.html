Dendropanax bolivianus

Dendropanax bolivianus, commonly known as the Bolivian arrowleaf, is a climbing shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves with elongated, arrow-shaped leaflets. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
