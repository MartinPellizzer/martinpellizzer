Dieffenbachia gracilis

Dieffenbachia gracilis, commonly known as the Narrow-leaf Dieffenbachia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often marked with light green veins. The plant produces clusters of small, pale green to white flowers, though it is more commonly cultivated for its ornamental foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate to high humidity.

Dieffenbachia gracilis is a popular houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
