Darwinia diosmoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Darwinia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, spike-like inflorescences. Darwinia diosmoides is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and heathlands.
