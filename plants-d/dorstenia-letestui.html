Dorstenia letestui

The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, typically blooming during the rainy season. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a waxy texture, which helps it retain moisture in its humid habitat.

Dorstenia letestui is valued not only for its ornamental appeal but also for its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
