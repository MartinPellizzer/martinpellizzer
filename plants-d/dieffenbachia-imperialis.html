Dieffenbachia imperialis

Dieffenbachia imperialis, commonly known as the Giant Dieffenbachia, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, arrowhead-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 feet in length. The leaves are typically a vibrant green with white or cream-colored veins, creating a visually appealing contrast. This plant is a member of the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like peace lilies.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity to maintain its lush appearance.
