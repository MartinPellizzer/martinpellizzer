Dracaena brachyphylla

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. Dracaena brachyphylla is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
