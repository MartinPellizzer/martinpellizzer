Dactylorchis latifolia

Dactylorchis latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaf twayblade, is a rare and elusive orchid species native to the southeastern United States.

The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in a spike, often blooming in late spring or early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically in woodland areas with rich, organic soil.
