Detarium

Detarium, commonly known as the African shea tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The tree produces pods that contain a hard, oily seed known as shea butter, which is widely used in cosmetics and food products.

Detarium species are also valued for their ecological role in soil stabilization and as a source of timber.
