Diuris magnifica

Diuris magnifica, commonly known as the magnificent spider-orchid, is a striking orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, spider-like flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. It blooms during the autumn season, producing multiple flower spikes that can reach up to 30 cm in height.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a conservation concern in some regions.
