Dimorphotheca aurantiaca

Dimorphotheca aurantiaca, commonly known as the golden crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, bright yellow ray flowers surrounding a central disk of smaller florets. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, typically from spring to autumn.
