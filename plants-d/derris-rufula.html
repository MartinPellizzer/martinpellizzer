Derris rufula

Derris rufula, commonly known as the African snakeroot, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds. It is often found in moist, disturbed soils in savanna and woodland areas.
