Disa perrieri

Disa perrieri, commonly known as the Perrier's Disa, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with a distinctive lip that is often marked with contrasting patterns.

Disa perrieri is a popular choice for rock gardens and containers due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
