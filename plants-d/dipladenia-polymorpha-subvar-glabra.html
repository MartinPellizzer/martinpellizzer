Dipladenia polymorpha subvar. glabra

Dipladenia polymorpha subvar. glabra, commonly known as the smooth dipladenia, is a tropical vine native to South America.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and heart-shaped, dark green leaves. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a red throat. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

This subvariety is popular in ornamental horticulture for its vibrant blooms and trailing habit.
