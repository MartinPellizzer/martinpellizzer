Dendrobium interjectum

Dendrobium interjectum, commonly known as the purple-tinged orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with cane-like pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces striking flowers in shades of pink, white, and purple, often with a distinctive yellow throat. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium interjectum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate for orchid enthusiasts.
