Dalea coronilla

Dalea coronilla, commonly known as crown vetch, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and clusters of pink or white flowers. The plant has a deep root system that helps prevent soil erosion. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for erosion control.

Dalea coronilla is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, improving soil fertility.
