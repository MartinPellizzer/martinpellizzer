Doona odorata

Doona odorata, commonly known as the fragrant doona, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, aromatic flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a sweet, spicy scent. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a glossy surface.

Doona odorata is valued in traditional medicine and horticulture for its ornamental and aromatic qualities.
