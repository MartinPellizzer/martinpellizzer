Dieffenbachia Seguine Var. Decora

Dieffenbachia seguine var. decora is a strikingly beautiful plant known for its vibrant, variegated leaves. It belongs to the Araceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America. This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its ability to thrive indoors with minimal care. Its unique foliage makes it a favorite among plant enthusiasts and interior designers. However, it is important to note that it is toxic to pets and should be handled with caution.
