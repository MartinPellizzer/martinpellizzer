Dioscorea ovata

Dioscorea ovata, commonly known as the arrowhead yam, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and tuberous roots. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are often clustered in spikes. Its tubers are edible when properly prepared and have been used in traditional cuisines.

The plant is also valued for its medicinal properties in various cultures.
