Draba × abiskojokkensis

Draba × abiskojokkensis, commonly known as the Abisko Draba, is a naturally occurring hybrid between Draba nemorosa and Draba alpina.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches heights of 5–15 cm. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months. It is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, linear leaves and is adapted to thrive in alpine and subarctic environments.

Draba × abiskojokkensis is found in rocky and gravelly soils, often in mountainous regions of Scandinavia.
