Daphne × fortunata

Daphne × fortunata, commonly known as the winter daphne, is a hybrid shrub prized for its fragrant, showy flowers.

It belongs to the family Thymelaeaceae and is native to the Mediterranean region. The plant produces clusters of pink or white flowers in late winter or early spring, followed by red berries. Its leaves are dark green and glossy, providing an attractive evergreen structure.

Daphne × fortunata is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and winter interest.
