Discaria × serratifolia var. inermis

Discaria × serratifolia var. inermis, commonly known as the soft-leaved discaria, is a hybrid shrub native to Australia.

It features soft, hairless foliage that distinguishes it from its more prickly relatives. The plant produces small, pale green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and semi-arid regions.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique and adaptable species within the Discaria genus.
