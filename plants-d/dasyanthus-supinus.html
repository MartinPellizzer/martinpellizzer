Dasyanthus supinus

Dasyanthus supinus, commonly known as the prostrate dasyanthus, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a low-growing shrub with small, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in sandy or rocky habitats.

Dasyanthus supinus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
