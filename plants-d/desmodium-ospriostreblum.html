Desmodium ospriostreblum

Desmodium ospriostreblum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf desmodium, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which are typically narrow and have a pointed tip. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

Desmodium ospriostreblum is valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, which benefits soil fertility.
