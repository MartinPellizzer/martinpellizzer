Disella hederacea

Disella hederacea, commonly known as creeping wood sorrel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers with five petals, typically blooming in early spring. Its stems are prostrate and can root at the nodes, allowing it to spread vegetatively.
