Delissea hypoleuca

Delissea hypoleuca, commonly known as the white-flowered delissea, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or woodland settings.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is occasionally cultivated in horticultural collections.
