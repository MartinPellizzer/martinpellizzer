Dimorphotheca montana

Dimorphotheca montana, commonly known as the mountain dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer, typically from November to March. The plant has a bushy growth habit with hairy stems and leaves that are usually lance-shaped. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Dimorphotheca montana is also known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
