Deutzia × rosea f. campanulata

Deutzia × rosea f. campanulata, commonly known as the bell-shaped deutzia, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to China.

It features clusters of fragrant, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming in early summer. The plant has slender, arching branches with small, oval leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden borders and woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value lies in its delicate flowers and graceful growth habit.
