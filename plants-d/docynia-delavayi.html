Docynia delavayi

Docynia delavayi, commonly known as the Delavay's spurge, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has smooth, often hairy stems and alternate, simple leaves that are typically lance-shaped. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.
