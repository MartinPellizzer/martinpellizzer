Dieffenbachia seguine f. barraquiniana

Dieffenbachia seguine f. barraquiniana, commonly known as the Barraquiniana Dieffenbachia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features broad, dark green, heart-shaped leaves with striking white to light green veins, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant is a member of the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
