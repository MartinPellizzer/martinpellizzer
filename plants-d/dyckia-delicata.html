Dyckia delicata

Dyckia delicata, commonly known as the silver feather plant, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its rosette-shaped growth habit. The plant features narrow, silver-tomentose leaves that give it a distinctive, ornamental appearance. It produces inflorescences with small, white or pale purple flowers that bloom in the spring.

Dyckia delicata is relatively low-maintenance and thrives in bright, indirect light with well-draining soil.
