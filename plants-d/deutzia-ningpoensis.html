Deutzia ningpoensis

Deutzia ningpoensis, commonly known as the Ningpo deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has slender, arching branches and small, ovate leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
