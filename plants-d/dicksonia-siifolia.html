Dicksonia siifolia

Dicksonia siifolia, commonly known as the slender tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its slender, upright trunk and a crown of feathery, light green fronds. The plant has a distinctive, elongated rhizome that supports its tall, slender structure. Its fronds are typically pinnately compound, with a soft, velvety texture.

Dicksonia siifolia thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.
