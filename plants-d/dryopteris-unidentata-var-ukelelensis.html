Dryopteris unidentata var. ukelelensis

Dryopteris unidentata var. ukelelensis, commonly known as the Ukelele Shield Fern, is a variant of the common wood fern.

It is characterized by its distinctive, lance-shaped fronds that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces sporangia in clusters on the underside of its fertile fronds, which are usually located near the tips. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Its unique morphology and habitat preferences make it a notable species in botanical studies.
