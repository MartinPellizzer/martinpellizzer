Dioclea reflexa var. grandiflora

Dioclea reflexa var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered dioclea, is a climbing vine native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy pink or red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has compound leaves with elongated leaflets and produces small, round fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a popular ornamental plant in horticulture.
