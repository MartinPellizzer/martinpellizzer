Delosperma roseopurpureum

Delosperma roseopurpureum, commonly known as the purple-rose ice plant, is a low-growing, succulent perennial native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine, white powder, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, purple, and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it ideal for rock gardens or containers.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
