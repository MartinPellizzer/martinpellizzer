Dioscorea polyclades

Dioscorea polyclades, commonly known as the African yam bean, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Dioscoreaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, pale yellow flowers. The plant produces edible tubers that are an important food source in some regions. Its rapid growth habit makes it suitable for use as a living fence or cover crop.

However, it can become invasive if not managed properly in agricultural settings.
