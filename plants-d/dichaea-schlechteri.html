Dichaea schlechteri

Dichaea schlechteri, commonly known as the Schlechter's dichaea, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features a short, creeping rhizome with several erect, slender stems. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, and it produces delicate, greenish-yellow flowers during the rainy season.

This orchid is valued for its unique appearance and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its ornamental qualities.
