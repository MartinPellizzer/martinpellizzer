Dithyrocarpus meyenianus

Dithyrocarpus meyenianus, commonly known as the white flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking white to pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a dramatic appearance. The plant has compound leaves with long, slender leaflets and produces small, round fruits that are edible for some bird species. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The tree is also valued for its hard, durable wood used in construction and furniture.
