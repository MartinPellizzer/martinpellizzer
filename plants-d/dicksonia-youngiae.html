Dicksonia youngiae

Dicksonia youngiae, commonly known as the young's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical rainforests of northern Queensland, Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that can reach lengths of up to 3 meters. The plant has a distinctive crown of spirally arranged fronds, which give it a unique and elegant appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on rocky outcrops or in dense undergrowth.

The plant plays an important ecological role in its native habitat by providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
