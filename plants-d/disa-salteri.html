Disa salteri

Disa salteri, commonly known as the Salter's Disa, is a species of orchid native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it produces aerial stems that bear the flowers.

Disa salteri is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
