Delilia inelegans

Delilia inelegans, commonly known as the elegant delilia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a smooth, glossy surface.

Delilia inelegans is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid climates.
