Dracaena filamentosa

Dracaena filamentosa, commonly known as the threadleaf dracaena, is a striking evergreen shrub native to Madagascar.

It features slender, upright stems and long, narrow leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The leaves are typically a deep green with a slight blueish tinge, and they have a fibrous texture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its elegant appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
