Daphne undulata

Daphne undulata, commonly known as the wavy daphne, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming in early spring. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are often adorned with wavy edges, giving it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Daphne undulata is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and distinctive fragrance.
