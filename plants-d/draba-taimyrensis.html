Draba taimyrensis

Draba taimyrensis, commonly known as the Taimyrensk Draba, is a flowering plant native to the Arctic regions of Siberia.

It is a small, herbaceous annual that typically grows in rocky and barren soils. The plant features basal leaves and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers during the short summer season. It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with similar floral structures.
