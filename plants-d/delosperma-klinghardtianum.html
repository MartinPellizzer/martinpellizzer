Delosperma klinghardtianum

Delosperma klinghardtianum, commonly known as the Kleinhardt's ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, enabling it to thrive in arid environments. Delosperma klinghardtianum is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
