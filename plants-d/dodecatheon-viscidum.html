Dodecatheon viscidum

Dodecatheon viscidum, commonly known as sticky leaf trillium, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its three broad, waxy leaves and showy, pinkish-white flowers. The plant is also known for its ability to thrive in nutrient-poor soils and is often found in forest understories.
