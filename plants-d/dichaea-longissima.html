Dichaea longissima

Dichaea longissima, commonly known as the long-stemmed dichaea, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. This epiphytic plant is characterized by its long, slender stems and rosette-forming leaves. The leaves are typically narrow and covered in a dense layer of trichomes, which help in water retention.

It produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink, adding to its ornamental value.
