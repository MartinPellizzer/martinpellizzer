Dendrobium discolor var. broomfieldii

Dendrobium discolor var. broomfieldii, commonly known as the Broomfield's Dendrobium, is a rare and unique orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with multiple stems that elongate over time. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with intricate patterns and a subtle fragrance.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and challenging cultivation requirements.
