Drimys granadensis var. grandiflora

Drimys granadensis var. grandiflora, commonly known as the grandiflora drimys, is a species native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub characterized by its elliptical, glossy leaves and fragrant, white flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy berries that are an important food source for local wildlife. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after plant in horticulture for its unique appearance and aromatic qualities.
