Daphniphyllum majus var. phanrangense

Daphniphyllum majus var. phanrangense, commonly known as the Phanrang holly, is a species of evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its ornamental value comes from its dense foliage and ability to thrive in shaded environments.

It is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
