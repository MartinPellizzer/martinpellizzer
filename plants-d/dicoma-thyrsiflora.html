Dicoma thyrsiflora

Dicoma thyrsiflora, commonly known as the silver leaf tree, is a shrub or small tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray, fuzzy leaves that provide a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in dense, conical clusters called thyrses. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and rocky habitats.

Dicoma thyrsiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
