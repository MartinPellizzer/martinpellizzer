Dryopteris canelensis

Dryopteris canelensis, commonly known as the Canel Tree Fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests. Its rhizome system supports the growth of its distinctive, layered fronds.

The Canel Tree Fern is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
