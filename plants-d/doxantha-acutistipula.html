Doxantha acutistipula

Doxantha acutistipula, commonly known as the sharp-tipped doxantha, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in tropical and subtropical environments. The plant is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, clustered yellow flowers. It is often found in moist, well-drained soils near streams or in forest understories.
