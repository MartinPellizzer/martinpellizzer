Daphne × pilatii

Daphne × pilatii, commonly known as the Pilatii Daphne, is a hybrid shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features dense, compact growth with dark green, leathery leaves that are evergreen. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink, white, and purple, typically in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Due to its ornamental value and cold hardiness, it is a popular choice for gardeners in temperate climates.
