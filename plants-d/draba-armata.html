Draba armata

Draba armata, commonly known as the armed wintergrass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches a height of about 5 to 15 centimeters. The plant features basal leaves that are linear to lanceolate in shape, with a rough, hairy texture. Its small, white or pale yellow flowers are arranged in a cluster at the top of the stem, and it blooms from late winter to early spring.

Draba armata is commonly found in alpine and arctic regions, where it thrives in rocky or gravelly soils.
