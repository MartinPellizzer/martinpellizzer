Dendrobium quadrialatum

Dendrobium quadrialatum, commonly known as the four-lobed orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, cane-like pseudobulbs and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically produces white or pale yellow blooms with a distinctive four-lobed lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Dendrobium quadrialatum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
