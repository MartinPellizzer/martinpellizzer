Dodonaea nematoidea

Dodonaea nematoidea, commonly known as silky oaks, is a deciduous tree native to Australia.

It is characterized by its feathery, silky foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant has small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. Its bark is smooth and often exudes a sticky resin when damaged.

The tree is drought-tolerant and thrives in a variety of soil types, making it a hardy and adaptable species.
