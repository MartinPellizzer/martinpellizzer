Digitaria virens

Digitaria virens, commonly known as green panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Digitaria virens is used in some regions for forage and has potential applications in erosion control.
