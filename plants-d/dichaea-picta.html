Dichaea picta

Dichaea picta, commonly known as the picta moss, is a species of liverwort belonging to the family Pottiaceae.

It is characterized by its small, closely packed leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns and color variations. This plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rock crevices. Dichaea picta is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its tolerance for varying humidity levels.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for both horticultural and scientific study.
