Demorchis verrucosa

Demorchis verrucosa, commonly known as the warty liverwort, is a non-vascular plant belonging to the family Peltasporaceae.

It is characterized by its small, flattened, leaf-like structures called thallus, which are often covered in tiny, warty projections. Demorchis verrucosa plays a role in nutrient cycling and provides habitat for microorganisms in its ecosystem.
