Dendrobium Serra

Dendrobium serra is a captivating orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. Known for its striking appearance and unique growth habits, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. It features elongated stems with clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers. The species is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements. Its resilience and adaptability make it a favorite choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
