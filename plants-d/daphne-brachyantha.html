Daphne brachyantha

Daphne brachyantha, commonly known as the silver leaf daphne, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features finely divided, silver-gray leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white flowers in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and unique foliage, it is often cultivated in gardens.
