Desmodium micranthum var. macbridei

Desmodium micranthum var. macbridei, commonly known as the small-flowered desmodium, is a perennial legume native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features trifoliate leaves with small, oval leaflets and produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil fertility. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and woodland edges.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species in ecological and agricultural contexts.
