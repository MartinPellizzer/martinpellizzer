Dillenia excelsa var. pubescens

Dillenia excelsa var. pubescens, commonly known as the hairy dillenia, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it its varietal name. The plant produces showy, yellowish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels, attracting various pollinators. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
