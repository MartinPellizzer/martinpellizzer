Dendrochilum minimum

Dendrochilum minimum, commonly known as the miniature tree orchid, is a small epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often clustered in loose inflorescences. Its leaves are elongated and slightly fleshy, adapted to store moisture.

Dendrochilum minimum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in terrariums or as a houseplant.
