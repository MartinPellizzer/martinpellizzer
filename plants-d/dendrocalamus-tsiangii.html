Dendrocalamus tsiangii

Dendrocalamus tsiangii, commonly known as the Chinese bamboo or Tsiang's bamboo, is a tall, slender grass-like plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Bambusoideae subfamily and is characterized by its rapid growth and strong, flexible culms. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.

Dendrocalamus tsiangii is also valued for its strong, durable material, which is used in construction and handicrafts.
