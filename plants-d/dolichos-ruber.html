Dolichos ruber

Dolichos ruber, commonly known as the red bean plant, is a leguminous species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its climbing habit, with long, slender stems that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, followed by pod fruits containing oval, red seeds. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

Dolichos ruber is valued for its edible seeds, which are used in various cuisines and also for their nutritional content.
