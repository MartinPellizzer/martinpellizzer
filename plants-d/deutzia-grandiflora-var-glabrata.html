Deutzia grandiflora var. glabrata

Deutzia grandiflora var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features upright, multi-stemmed growth with ovate to elliptic leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. The plant produces clusters of white to pale pink flowers in spring, which are borne on short pedicels. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
