Dendranthema japonense var. ashizuriense

Dendranthema japonense var. ashizuriense, commonly known as the Ashizuri chrysanthemum, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to Japan.

It features dense, dark green foliage and produces showy, semi-double to double flowers in shades of yellow to orange. The plant is well adapted to coastal environments, tolerating salt spray and sandy soils. It blooms from late summer to early autumn, adding vibrant color to gardens in temperate climates.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and hardiness in mild maritime conditions.
