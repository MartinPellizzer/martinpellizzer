Delphinium speciosum var. marriensis

Delphinium speciosum var. marriensis, commonly known as the Marriensis larkspur, is a striking perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features tall, slender stems that bear clusters of vivid blue to purple flowers, which are typically spurred at the base. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem, contributing to its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens in suitable climates.

Its botanical characteristics make it both a visually appealing and ecologically significant species in its native habitat.
