Dendrobium regium

Dendrobium regium, commonly known as the king's orchid, is a rare and highly sought-after epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and large, showy flowers that can reach up to 12 inches in diameter. The plant produces fragrant, waxy blooms in shades of white, pink, and yellow, often with intricate patterns. It requires specific conditions, including high humidity and bright, indirect light, to thrive.

Due to its rarity and beauty, Dendrobium regium is a prized specimen among orchid enthusiasts.
