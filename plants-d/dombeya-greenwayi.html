Dombeya greenwayi

Dombeya greenwayi, commonly known as the African dombeya, is a shrub native to eastern and southern Africa.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, blooming in clusters during the warmer months. The plant has smooth, often hairy stems and elliptical, dark green leaves with a slightly waxy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for cultivation in gardens with adequate drainage.

Dombeya greenwayi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
