Delphinium stapeliosmum var. siamense

Delphinium stapeliosmum var. siamense, commonly known as the Siam larkspur, is a striking perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It features tall, upright stems that bear clusters of blue to purple flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface, typical of the Delphinium genus. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
