Dichrostachys arborescens var. perrieriana

Dichrostachys arborescens var. perrieriana, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a fast-growing, deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It features a thick, buttressed trunk and is characterized by its striking, fiery orange to red flowers that appear in clusters. The leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate, with a dark green upper surface and a lighter underside. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular ornamental plant in warm climates.

The tree is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, improving soil fertility.
