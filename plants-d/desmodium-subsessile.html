Desmodium subsessile

Desmodium subsessile, commonly known as smallleaf desmodium, is a leguminous plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, each composed of three small, oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, yellowish to brownish pea-like flowers that are often clustered in racemose inflorescences. Its seed pods are cylindrical and dehiscent, releasing seeds upon maturity.

Desmodium subsessile is typically found in tropical and subtropical regions, often in grassy or disturbed habitats.
