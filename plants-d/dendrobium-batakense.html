Dendrobium batakense

Dendrobium batakense, commonly known as the Batak orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in tropical rainforests. The plant produces strikingly colorful flowers, often in shades of pink, purple, and white, which bloom during the wet season. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in humid, shaded environments.

Dendrobium batakense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
