Daviesia mimosoides var. laxiflora

Daviesia mimosoides var. laxiflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered daviesia, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments. It is often found in heathlands and open woodlands, where it contributes to local biodiversity.

The variety laxiflora is distinguished by its more loosely arranged flowers compared to other varieties of Daviesia mimosoides.
