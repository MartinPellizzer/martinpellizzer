Dillenia megalantha

Dillenia megalantha, commonly known as the giant dillenia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in clusters, featuring vibrant yellow petals and a distinctive central cone-shaped structure. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and can grow up to several meters in height. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in both horticulture and ecological studies.
