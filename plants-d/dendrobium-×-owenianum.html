Dendrobium × owenianum

Dendrobium × owenianum, commonly known as the Phalaenopsis orchid, is a hybrid orchid species that exhibits a variety of morphological traits.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its upright, branching structure. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in a range of colors, including white, pink, and purple, often in clusters along the stems. Its leaves are typically broad and grass-like, arranged in a rosette pattern around the pseudobulbs.

Dendrobium × owenianum thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
