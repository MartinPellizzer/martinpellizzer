Draba majae

Draba majae, commonly known as the alpine mouse-ear cress, is a small flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Europe and parts of Asia.

The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes, where it thrives in cold, harsh environments. Draba majae produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous tiny seeds.
