Dicranostyles densa

Dicranostyles densa, commonly known as the dense frond fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, compact fronds that are typically dark green and leathery in texture. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its rhizome is thick and creeping, allowing it to spread and form dense mats.

Dicranostyles densa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden areas.
