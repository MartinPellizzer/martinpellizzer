Desmodium triquetrum subsp. pseudotriquetrum

Desmodium triquetrum subsp.

pseudotriquetrum, commonly known as the slender burr legume, is a perennial herb native to tropical and subtropical regions. It features trifoliate leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellow to reddish-brown flowers.

The plant is characterized by its unique, spiny, three-lobed pods that mature into dry, woody structures. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodland or grassland habitats.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
