Dichorisandra leandri

Dichorisandra leandri, commonly known as the Natal orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually linear and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Dichorisandra leandri thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its beauty in gardens and greenhouses.
