Desmodium supinum var. amblyophyllum

Desmodium supinum var. amblyophyllum, commonly known as the slender sicklepod, is a flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is a low-growing annual herb characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in grasslands and along roadsides. It produces small, lens-shaped pods that contain several seeds.

This species is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility.
