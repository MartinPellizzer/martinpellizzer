Delonix elata

Delonix elata, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, bright orange to red flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has pinnate leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets. Its bark is smooth and grayish in color, while its roots are often fibrous and extensive.

The tree is widely cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
