Daemonorops binnendijkii

Daemonorops binnendijkii, commonly known as the Binnendijk's cycad, is a species of cycad native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Cycadaceae and is characterized by its stout, unbranched trunk and pinnately compound leaves. The plant produces cones, with male and female cones typically occurring on separate plants. It is a slow-growing plant that thrives in tropical climates with well-drained soil.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered vulnerable and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
