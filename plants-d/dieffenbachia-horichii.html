Dieffenbachia horichii

Dieffenbachia horichii, commonly known as the Horichii Dieffenbachia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, strikingly patterned leaves that feature a mix of green, white, and yellow hues. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other popular houseplants like peace lilies and calla lilies. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
