Diasperus polycarpus

Diasperus polycarpus, commonly known as the many-seeded burr, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Its leaves are typically alternate and have a rough, hairy texture.

Diasperus polycarpus is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
