Digera alternifolia var. ciliata

Digera alternifolia var. ciliata, commonly known as the silky leaf morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and heart-shaped leaves with fine cilia along the edges. The plant produces striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in the early morning, hence its common name. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its rapid growth and ornamental value make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a vibrant, climbing plant.
