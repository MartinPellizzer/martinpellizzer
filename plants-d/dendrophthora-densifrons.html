Dendrophthora densifrons

Dendrophthora densifrons, commonly known as the dense-fruited mistletoe, is a hemiparasitic plant that attaches to the branches of its host trees.

Its presence can affect the health of its host tree, sometimes leading to reduced growth or even mortality.
