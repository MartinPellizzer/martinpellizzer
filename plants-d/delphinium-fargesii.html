Delphinium fargesii

Delphinium fargesii, commonly known as the Farges' larkspur, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of western China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height, adorned with clusters of blue to violet flowers. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is typically found in alpine meadows and forested areas.

Delphinium fargesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
