Daphne × susannae

Daphne × susannae, commonly known as the Christmas daphne, is a hybrid shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring, often in shades of pink and white. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves that remain throughout the year, providing evergreen structure. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for garden borders.

Due to its toxicity, it should be handled with care.
