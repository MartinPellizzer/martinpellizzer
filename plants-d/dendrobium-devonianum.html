Dendrobium devonianum

Dendrobium devonianum, commonly known as the Devonian orchid, is a type of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, cane-like stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink, typically during the summer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Dendrobium devonianum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
