Dubautia reticulata

Dubautia reticulata, commonly known as the reticulate hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive reticulate pattern on the underside. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in coastal or rocky habitats.

Dubautia reticulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
