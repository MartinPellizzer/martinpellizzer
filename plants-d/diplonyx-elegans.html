Diplonyx elegans

Diplonyx elegans, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Diplonyx elegans is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
