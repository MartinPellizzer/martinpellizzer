Davilla pilosa

Davilla pilosa, commonly known as the silky tree or red silk tree, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall, featuring compound leaves with soft, silky hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant white or pink flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by bright red, fleshy berries. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in coastal areas and disturbed soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm regions.
