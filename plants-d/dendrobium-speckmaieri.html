Dendrobium speckmaieri

Dendrobium speckmaieri, commonly known as the Speck's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome that produces multiple stems. The plant typically has lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrobium speckmaieri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
