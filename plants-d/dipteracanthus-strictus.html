Dipteracanthus strictus

Dipteracanthus strictus, commonly known as the narrowleaf dipteracanthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dipterocarpaceae, which includes many tropical trees and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in lowland forests.
