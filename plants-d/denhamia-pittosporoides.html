Denhamia pittosporoides

Denhamia pittosporoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Pittosporum, is a shrub or small tree native to eastern Australia.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter underside. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters, followed by orange-red berries that are edible for some bird species. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and subcoastal regions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting native wildlife.
