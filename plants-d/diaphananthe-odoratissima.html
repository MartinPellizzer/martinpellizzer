Diaphananthe odoratissima

Diaphananthe odoratissima, commonly known as the sweet-scented evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, waxy leaves that emit a pleasant aroma, particularly when crushed. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a smooth, glossy surface.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.
