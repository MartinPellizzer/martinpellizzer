Dendrobium × usitae

Dendrobium × usitae, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a phalaenopsis-type orchid that results from the cross between Dendrobium nobile and Phalaenopsis equestris.

It is an epiphytic plant that thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with intricate patterns. It has a sympodial growth habit, forming pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

Dendrobium × usitae is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
