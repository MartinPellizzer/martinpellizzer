Dillenia meliosmifolia

Dillenia meliosmifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved dillenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves that are typically green with a smooth texture. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers with red stamens, which bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its fruit is a woody, spiny capsule that contains several seeds.

Dillenia meliosmifolia is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
