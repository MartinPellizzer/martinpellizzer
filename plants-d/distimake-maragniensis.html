Distimake maragniensis

Distimake maragniensis, commonly known as the Maragni Desert Orchid, is a rare flowering plant native to the arid regions of the Maragni Basin.

It possesses a unique root system that allows it to store water efficiently, adapting to its harsh environment. The plant features tall, slender stems with narrow, waxy leaves that reduce water loss through transpiration. Its flowers are striking, with vivid purple petals that bloom only during brief periods of rainfall.

Due to its limited distribution and specialized habitat, it is considered a vulnerable species.
