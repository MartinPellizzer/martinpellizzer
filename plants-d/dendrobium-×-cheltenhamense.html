Dendrobium × cheltenhamense

Dendrobium × cheltenhamense, commonly known as the Cheltenham orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Australia.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features long, cane-like stems that are often covered in clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, arranged along the stem.

It requires bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
