Dracula robledorum

Dracula robledorum, commonly known as the "Dragon's Blood" orchid, is a rare and striking epiphytic orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and orange flowers, which are often described as resembling a dragon's blood. The plant has thick, fleshy roots that help it absorb moisture from the humid air. It blooms once every few years, making it a highly sought-after specimen among orchid enthusiasts.

Due to its limited habitat and slow growth, it is considered endangered and is protected in its native region.
