Dodonaea eriocarpa var. glabrescens

Dodonaea eriocarpa var. glabrescens, commonly known as the silky honeysuckle, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. The leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine, silky hairs, which give the plant its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native pollinators.
