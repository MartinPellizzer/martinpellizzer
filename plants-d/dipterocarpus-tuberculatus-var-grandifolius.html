Dipterocarpus tuberculatus var. grandifolius

Dipterocarpus tuberculatus var. grandifolius, commonly known as the large-leaf dipterocarp, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dipterocarpaceae and is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves with prominent veins. The tree produces heavy, woody fruits that contain winged seeds, facilitating wind dispersal. It typically grows in lowland rainforests and is valued for its durable timber.

This species plays a crucial role in the ecological dynamics of its native habitats.
