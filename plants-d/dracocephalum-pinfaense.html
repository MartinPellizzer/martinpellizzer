Dracocephalum pinfaense

Dracocephalum pinfaense, commonly known as Chinese blueweed, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves, typical of the mint family. Its flowers are small and clustered in whorls, often displaying shades of blue or purple.

It is often used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
