Dendrochilum tenue

Dendrochilum tenue, commonly known as the slender tree orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and can reach heights of up to 3 meters. The plant produces pendulous flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in shaded forest environments.

Dendrochilum tenue is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
