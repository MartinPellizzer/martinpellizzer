Dalbergia cultrata

Dalbergia cultrata, commonly known as the African rosewood, is a small to medium-sized leguminous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dark, dense wood that is highly valued for its beauty and durability. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in racemes. Its fruit is a legume pod containing flat, winged seeds.

Dalbergia cultrata is often found in moist, well-drained soils in forested areas.
