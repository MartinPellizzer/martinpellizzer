Diosphaera asperuloides var. vestita

Diosphaera asperuloides var. vestita, commonly known as the velvetleaf diosphaera, is a perennial shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, typically blooming during the rainy season. Its woody stems are smooth and can become slightly branched as the plant matures.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
