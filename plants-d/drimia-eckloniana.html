Drimia eckloniana

Drimia eckloniana, commonly known as the Cape bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features upright, strap-like leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. The plant produces striking, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or purple, which bloom in summer. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Drimia eckloniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
