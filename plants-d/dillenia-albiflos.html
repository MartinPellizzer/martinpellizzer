Dillenia albiflos

Dillenia albiflos, commonly known as the white-flowered dillenia, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height. The plant produces large, showy white flowers with five petals, which are typically borne in clusters at the ends of branches. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a distinctive heart-shaped base.

The fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release small, round seeds.
