Debregeasia longifolia var. affinis

Debregeasia longifolia var. affinis, commonly known as the African violet, is a perennial herb native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and clusters of showy, tubular flowers that typically range in color from white to purple. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright indirect light for optimal growth. Its botanical features include opposite leaf arrangement and a short, fibrous root system.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
