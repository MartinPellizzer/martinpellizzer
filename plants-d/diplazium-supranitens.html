Diplazium supranitens

Diplazium supranitens, commonly known as the giant chain fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, compound fronds that can reach lengths of up to 3 meters. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with creeping rhizomes that allow it to spread vegetatively. Its fronds are typically bipinnate, with a distinctive appearance that makes it a popular ornamental plant.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
