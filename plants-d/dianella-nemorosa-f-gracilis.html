Dianella nemorosa f. gracilis

Dianella nemorosa f. gracilis, commonly known as the slender forest grass.

It is a low-growing, evergreen plant characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, blue-green flowers. Native to New Zealand, this variety thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant forms dense clumps, making it suitable for ground cover in woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value lies in its delicate foliage and ability to create a lush, natural appearance.
