Dovyalis giorgii

Dovyalis giorgii, commonly known as the Natal plum, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It produces small, yellowish-green fruits that are edible and have a tangy, acidic flavor. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and clusters of small white flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

The fruit is often used in local cuisines and has traditional medicinal uses in some regions.
