Donella bangweolensis

Donella bangweolensis, commonly known as the Bangweolensis daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy yellow flowers with white ray florets. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and is adapted to dry, sandy soils. It has a low-growing habit with hairy stems and narrow, linear leaves.

Donella bangweolensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
