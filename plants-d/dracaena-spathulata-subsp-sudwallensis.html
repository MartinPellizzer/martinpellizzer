Dracaena spathulata subsp. sudwallensis

Dracaena spathulata subsp.

sudwallensis, commonly known as the African violet, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to eastern Africa. It features broad, strap-shaped leaves that are typically green with a reddish or purple underside.

The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, violet-like flowers on short pedicels. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance of low light and moderate watering.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both garden beds and container planting.
