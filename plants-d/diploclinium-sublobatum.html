Diploclinium sublobatum

Diploclinium sublobatum, commonly known as the broad-leafed milkweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often hairy.
