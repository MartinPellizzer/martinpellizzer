Dendrobium multicaule

Dendrobium multicaule, commonly known as the clustered dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a tall, branched stem that can grow up to several meters in height, often with multiple stems arising from a single base. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white or yellowish flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
