Desmodium podocarpum var. glabrius

Desmodium podocarpum var. glabrius, commonly known as the smoothleaf desmodium, is a species of legume native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces cylindrical, hairy pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and woodland areas.

This plant is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil fertility.
