Dracaena youngii

Dracaena youngii, commonly known as the African daisy or yellow dracaena, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of yellow or orange flowers. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping. It prefers full sun to partial shade and is often used in ornamental gardening.

Dracaena youngii is also valued for its ability to purify indoor air, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
