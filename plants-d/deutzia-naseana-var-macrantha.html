Deutzia naseana var. macrantha

Deutzia naseana var. macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features upright, branching stems that produce clusters of showy, pinkish-white flowers in early summer. The foliage is dark green and provides good contrast to the delicate blossoms. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
