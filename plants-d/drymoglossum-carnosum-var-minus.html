Drymoglossum carnosum var. minus

Drymoglossum carnosum var. minus, commonly known as the lesser cushion moss, is a small, evergreen moss belonging to the family Pottiaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, cushion-like growth form and dark green, firm leaves that are typically less than 1 mm in width. It is a cryptogamic plant that reproduces through spores rather than seeds, contributing to its ecological role in soil stabilization and moisture retention.
