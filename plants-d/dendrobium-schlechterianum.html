Dendrobium schlechterianum

Dendrobium schlechterianum, commonly known as the Schlechter's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant that grows on trees and rocks, typically in humid tropical environments. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with intricate patterns. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in varying environmental conditions.

Dendrobium schlechterianum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated for both horticultural and commercial purposes.
