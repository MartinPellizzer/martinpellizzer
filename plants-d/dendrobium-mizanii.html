Dendrobium mizanii

Dendrobium mizanii, commonly known as the Mizanii orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, and it produces numerous small, greenish-yellow flowers along its stem.

Dendrobium mizanii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
