Dianthus egregius

Dianthus egregius, commonly known as the showy campion, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has hairy, branched stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Dianthus egregius is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
