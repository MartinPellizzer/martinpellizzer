Digitaria duthieana

Digitaria duthieana, commonly known as the Duthie's digitaria, is a species of grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in a variety of habitats, including grasslands and disturbed soils.

Due to its adaptability, it is often found in areas with moderate to high rainfall.
