Dendrobium speciosum var. gracillimum

Dendrobium speciosum var. gracillimum, commonly known as the graceful orchid, is a sympodial epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features pendulous, clustered inflorescences with fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. The plant has a complex root system adapted for absorbing moisture from the air and its substrate. Its pseudobulbs are cylindrical and store water, enabling it to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and ability to bloom multiple times a year.
