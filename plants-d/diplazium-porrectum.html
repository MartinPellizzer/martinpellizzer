Diplazium porrectum

Diplazium porrectum, commonly known as the broadleaf staghorn fern, is a tropical evergreen fern characterized by its robust, upright fronds.

The plant features a rhizome that supports the growth of its large, leathery, and often serrated fronds. Diplazium porrectum typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, shaded environments.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and indoor plant cultivation.
