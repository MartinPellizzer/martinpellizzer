Dendrobium cucullitepalum

Dendrobium cucullitepalum, commonly known as the hooded dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with a distinctive hood-like sheath covering its base. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, pale yellow to white flowers with a showy, lip-shaped petal. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
