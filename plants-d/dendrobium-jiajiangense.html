Dendrobium jiajiangense

Dendrobium jiajiangense, commonly known as the Jiajiang orchid, is a species of orchid native to China.

It is an epiphytic plant that grows on trees or rocks, typically in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually fragrant and come in a range of colors, including white, yellow, and pink.

The botanical features of Dendrobium jiajiangense make it a popular choice for both horticultural and ornamental purposes.
