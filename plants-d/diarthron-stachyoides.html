Diarthron stachyoides

Diarthron stachyoides, commonly known as the creeping grassvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its prostrate growth habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It is often found in disturbed soils and grasslands, thriving in temperate climates.

Diarthron stachyoides is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility.
