Daphne limprichtii

Daphne limprichtii, commonly known as the Cape daphne, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, evergreen foliage with elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Daphne limprichtii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping due to its hardy nature.
