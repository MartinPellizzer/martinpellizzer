Dillenia suffruticosa

Dillenia suffruticosa, commonly known as the prickly pear tree, is a shrub-like plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features a woody stem with thorny branches and is characterized by its palmate leaves that are deeply lobed. The plant produces yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by large, fleshy, red berries. Its fruits are edible and have a sweet, tangy flavor, often used in local cuisines.

Dillenia suffruticosa is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique appearance.
