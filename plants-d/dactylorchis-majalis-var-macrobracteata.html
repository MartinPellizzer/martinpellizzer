Dactylorchis majalis var. macrobracteata

Dactylorchis majalis var. macrobracteata, commonly known as the broad-bract orchid, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, showy inflorescence. The plant produces violet or purple flowers with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest for both horticulturists and botanists.
