Deschampsia Cespitosa Subsp. Convoluta

Deschampsia cespitosa subsp. convoluta is a distinctive grass species commonly found in moist to wet habitats across northern regions. It is known for its dense, tufted growth and silver-green foliage that shimmers in the wind. This subspecies is often mistaken for other similar grasses due to its compact form and delicate appearance. It plays an important role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife in its native environment. Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for ecological restoration and ornamental gardening.
