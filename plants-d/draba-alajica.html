Draba alajica

Draba alajica, commonly known as the Alajic rockcress, is a small flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with characteristic cruciform flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months.

Draba alajica is known for its compact growth habit and is often found in mountainous areas with poor soil.
