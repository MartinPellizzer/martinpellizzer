Drypetes mossambicensis

Drypetes mossambicensis, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, palmate leaves and showy, red-orange flowers. The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
