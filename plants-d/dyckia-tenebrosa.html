Dyckia tenebrosa

Dyckia tenebrosa, commonly known as the dark dyckia, is a species of bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy coating. The plant produces striking inflorescences that range from red to orange, adding visual interest to its appearance. Dyckia tenebrosa is adapted to grow in shaded, humid environments, often found in forest understories.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in indoor conditions with proper care.
