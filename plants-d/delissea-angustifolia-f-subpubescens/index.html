Delissea Angustifolia F. Subpubescens

Delissea angustifolia f. subpubescens is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to specific regions of southern Africa. It is known for its slender stems and small, pale green leaves that give it a unique appearance. The plant produces tiny, clustered flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival in the wild.
