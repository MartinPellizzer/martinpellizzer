Dolichos lablab f. lobata

Dolichos lablab f. lobata, commonly known as the horse gram or cluster bean, is a leguminous plant characterized by its distinctive lobed leaves and erect growth habit.

It produces cylindrical, green pods that contain small, edible seeds used in various cuisines. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions and thrives in warm, humid climates. It has a deep root system that helps in nitrogen fixation, improving soil fertility.

Its botanical features make it an important crop for both nutritional and agricultural purposes.
