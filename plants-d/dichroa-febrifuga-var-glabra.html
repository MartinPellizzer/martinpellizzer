Dichroa febrifuga var. glabra

Dichroa febrifuga var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth feverfew, is a shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy underside. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers clustered in inflorescences. It is often found in moist, shaded environments in mountainous regions.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both traditional medicine and horticulture.
