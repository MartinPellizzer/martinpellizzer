Dillenia aurea var. harmandii

Dillenia aurea var. harmandii, commonly known as the Harmand's dillenia, is a rare and striking plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a distinctive red or orange center. The plant has compound leaves that are palmately arranged, giving it a unique and ornamental appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a valuable species for botanical study and conservation efforts.
