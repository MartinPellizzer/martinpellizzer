Dieffenbachia picturata

Dieffenbachia picturata, commonly known as the Painted Dieffenbachia, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that feature a mix of white, green, and cream patterns. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other popular houseplants like peace lilies. Its foliage is often used in interior design due to its ornamental appeal.

Dieffenbachia picturata thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to maintain its vibrant appearance.
