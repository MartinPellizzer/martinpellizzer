Delphinium xichangense

Delphinium xichangense, commonly known as the Xichang larkspur, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant produces showy, blue to purple flowers in racemes, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are compound, with lance-shaped leaflets, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
