Daviesia apiculata

Daviesia apiculata, commonly known as the mountain billygoat pea, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its erect, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces distinctive yellow or orange pea-shaped flowers, which bloom in spring. Its leaves are bipinnate, with small, oval-shaped leaflets, and it has a fibrous root system.
