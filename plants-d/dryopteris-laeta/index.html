Dryopteris Laeta

Dryopteris laeta, also known as the shining wood fern, is a striking member of the Dryopteris genus native to eastern North America. This fern is characterized by its delicate, lacy fronds that shimmer with a unique silvery sheen under sunlight. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens. Its elegant appearance and hardy nature make it a favored plant among both botanists and garden enthusiasts. With its distinctive look and ecological importance, Dryopteris laeta stands out as a remarkable addition to the fern family.
