Dracaena longiflora var. fernandopoensis

Dracaena longiflora var. fernandopoensis, commonly known as the Fernandopoensis dracaena, is a striking tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and long, narrow leaves that can reach several feet in length. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers, which are followed by red, fleshy fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This variety is popular as a decorative indoor plant due to its elegant appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
