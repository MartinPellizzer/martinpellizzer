Duttonia gibbifolia

Duttonia gibbifolia, commonly known as the knobbed duttonia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, swollen stem bases that give it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a dense cluster at the top of the stem. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Duttonia gibbifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
