Daviesia latifolia var. buxifolia

Daviesia latifolia var. buxifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved Daviesia, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It features dense, evergreen foliage with lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers in late winter to early spring, attracting nectar-feeding birds and insects. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and dry garden environments.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular choice for landscaping in suitable climates.
