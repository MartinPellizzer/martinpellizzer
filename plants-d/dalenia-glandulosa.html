Dalenia glandulosa

Dalenia glandulosa, commonly known as the glandular dalenia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, herbaceous stems and showy, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces small, greenish fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Dalenia glandulosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
