Dombeya polyphylla

Dombeya polyphylla, commonly known as the many-leafed dombeya, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, with multiple stems and dense foliage. Its leaves are elliptical, leathery, and often have a rough texture.

Dombeya polyphylla thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable ornamental plant in gardens.
