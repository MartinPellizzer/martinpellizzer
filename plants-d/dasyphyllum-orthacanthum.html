Dasyphyllum orthacanthum

Dasyphyllum orthacanthum, commonly known as the spiny leaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, spiny leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in small, sharp spines, which help to deter herbivores.

The flowers bloom in spring and attract various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
