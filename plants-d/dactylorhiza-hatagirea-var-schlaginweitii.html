Dactylorhiza hatagirea var. schlaginweitii

Dactylorhiza hatagirea var. schlaginweitii, commonly known as the Himalayan orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It is a perennial herb with a rhizomatous root system and produces tall, slender stems bearing several lanceolate leaves. The plant is notable for its fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes.

Due to its ecological significance and ornamental value, it is also cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
