Diothonea exasperata

Diothonea exasperata, commonly known as the spiky nightshade, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades like tomatoes and peppers. The plant is characterized by its distinctive, spiny leaves and clusters of small, violet flowers. It produces berries that turn from green to yellow as they mature.
