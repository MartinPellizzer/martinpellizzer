Dammara alba var. celebica

Dammara alba var. celebica, commonly known as the Celebes dammara, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows up to 5 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, pale yellow to white flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland forested areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has limited use in traditional medicine.
