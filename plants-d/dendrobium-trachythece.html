Dendrobium trachythece

Dendrobium trachythece, commonly known as the white-lipped dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a tall, cane-like stem that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers with a distinctive lip that is often white or yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Dendrobium trachythece is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its long-lasting blooms.
