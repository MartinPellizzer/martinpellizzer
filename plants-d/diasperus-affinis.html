Diasperus affinis

Diasperus affinis, commonly known as the African grassvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Poaceae, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. Diasperus affinis is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its hardy nature and ability to stabilize soil in arid environments.
