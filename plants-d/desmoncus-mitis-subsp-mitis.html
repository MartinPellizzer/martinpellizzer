Desmoncus mitis subsp. mitis

Desmoncus mitis subsp.

mitis, commonly known as the slender leaved dandelion, is a member of the Asteraceae family. It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its slender, hairy stems and alternate, lance-shaped leaves.

The plant produces small, yellow composite flowers that develop into fluffy, white seed heads. It is typically found in moist, disturbed soils in temperate regions.

Its botanical features make it a common weed in gardens and agricultural fields.
