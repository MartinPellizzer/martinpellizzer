Dendrobium sinuatum

Dendrobium sinuatum, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with cane-like stems that often curl in a zigzag pattern. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, typically blooming during the summer months. Its leaves are elongated and strap-like, arranged in a spiral along the stem.

Dendrobium sinuatum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
