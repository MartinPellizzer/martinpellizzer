Dioscorea soso var. mollis

Dioscorea soso var. mollis, commonly known as the smooth yam, is a tuberous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its smooth, cylindrical rhizomes that are often used as a food source. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in warm climates. Its leaves are heart-shaped and large, while its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the same plant.

The tuber is rich in starch and has been traditionally used in various cuisines and traditional medicine practices.
