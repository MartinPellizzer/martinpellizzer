Dimanisa oblongata

Dimanisa oblongata, commonly known as the elongated dimanisa, is a rare flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery pubescence.
