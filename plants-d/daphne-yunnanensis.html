Daphne yunnanensis

Daphne yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan daphne, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its striking clusters of fragrant, waxy flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces small, oval-shaped berries that are typically red or orange when mature. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers a sunny to partially shaded location.

Daphne yunnanensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its winter flowering display.
