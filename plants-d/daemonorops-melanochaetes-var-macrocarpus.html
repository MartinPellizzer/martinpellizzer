Daemonorops melanochaetes var. macrocarpus

Daemonorops melanochaetes var. macrocarpus, commonly known as the giant cycad, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, feather-like leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces large, cone-like structures that contain both male and female reproductive organs. It thrives in humid, warm climates with well-drained soil.

Due to its slow growth and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
