Dimorphandra biretusa

Dimorphandra biretusa, commonly known as the yellow flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has bipinnate leaves, with each leaflet being small and oval-shaped. It produces long, slender pods that contain several seeds.

Dimorphandra biretusa is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.
