Delphinium tomentellum var. araraticum

Delphinium tomentellum var. araraticum, commonly known as the Araratic delphinium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant produces showy, blue to violet flowers in racemes, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lanceolate, hairy, and arranged alternately along the stem.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a striking addition to alpine gardens.
