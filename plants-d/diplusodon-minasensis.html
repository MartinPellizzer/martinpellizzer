Diplusodon minasensis

Diplusodon minasensis, commonly known as the Minasensis Cactus, is a species native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its spiny stems and reduced leaves. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, adapting to harsh conditions with its succulent structure. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, adding vibrant color to its desert habitat.

Diplusodon minasensis plays a role in local ecosystems by providing shelter and food for various desert animals.
