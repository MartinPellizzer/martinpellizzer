Dictyocalyx quadrivalvis

Dictyocalyx quadrivalvis, commonly known as the four-valved orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure with four valvular petals. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its roots are fibrous and adapted for anchoring in moist, shaded environments.

Dictyocalyx quadrivalvis is often found in forest understories where it thrives in humid, nutrient-rich soils.
