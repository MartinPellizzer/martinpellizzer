Deutzia hypoleuca

Deutzia hypoleuca, commonly known as the white deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features clusters of small, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, arching branches and dark green foliage that turns yellow in autumn. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value comes from its delicate flowers and compact growth habit.
