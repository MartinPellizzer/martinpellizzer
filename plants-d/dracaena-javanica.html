Dracaena javanica

Dracaena javanica, commonly known as the Javan dracaena, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, narrow, dark green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its hardiness and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
