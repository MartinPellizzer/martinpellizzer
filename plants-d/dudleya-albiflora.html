Dudleya albiflora

Dudleya albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered liveforever, is a succulent plant native to California.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are typically blue-gray in color. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers during the spring and summer months. It is adapted to rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun.
