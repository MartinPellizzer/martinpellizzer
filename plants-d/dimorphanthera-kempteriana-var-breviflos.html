Dimorphanthera kempteriana var. breviflos

Dimorphanthera kempteriana var. breviflos, commonly known as the short-flowered dimorphanthera, is a rare and endemic species native to specific regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive floral morphology. The plant features elongated, narrow leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers with a unique coloration pattern. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
