Dianthus webbianus

Dianthus webbianus, commonly known as the webbian carnation, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to white. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are linear and gray-green, often covered in fine hairs.

Dianthus webbianus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
