Diplycosia kitangladensis

Diplycosia kitangladensis, commonly known as the Kitanglad dandelion, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and linear leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas of montane forests. It has small, hairy stems and produces seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
