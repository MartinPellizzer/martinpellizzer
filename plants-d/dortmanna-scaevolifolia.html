Dortmanna scaevolifolia

Dortmanna scaevolifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Dortmanna, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is often found in open habitats such as coastal areas and dry grasslands.

Dortmanna scaevolifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
