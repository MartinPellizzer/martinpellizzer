Dianthus berkayorum

Dianthus berkayorum, commonly known as the Turkish carnation, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and features narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, and it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Dianthus berkayorum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
