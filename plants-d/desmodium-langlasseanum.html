Desmodium langlasseanum

Desmodium langlasseanum, commonly known as the slender desmodium, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, each consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It is native to parts of Africa and is often found in grassy areas and disturbed soils.

Desmodium langlasseanum is valued for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it beneficial in soil enrichment.
