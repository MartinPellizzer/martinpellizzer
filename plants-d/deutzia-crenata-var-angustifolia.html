Deutzia crenata var. angustifolia

Deutzia crenata var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrow-leaf deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It features delicate, fragrant white flowers that bloom in early spring, typically in clusters along the stems. The plant has slender, arching branches and small, ovate leaves with a finely toothed margin. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden borders and woodland plantings.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its showy blooms and graceful form.
