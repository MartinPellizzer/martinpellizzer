Dracaena pethera var. pulchra

Dracaena pethera var. pulchra, commonly known as the Variegated Dracaena, is a striking indoor plant characterized by its distinctive, variegated foliage.

Its leaves are typically a combination of green, white, and yellow, creating a visually appealing contrast. Native to tropical regions, it thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. The plant is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced gardeners.

Its slow growth rate and ability to adapt to various indoor conditions contribute to its widespread cultivation.
