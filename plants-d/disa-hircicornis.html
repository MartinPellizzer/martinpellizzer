Disa hircicornis

Disa hircicornis, commonly known as the horned disa, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its flowers are usually pink or purple and feature a distinctive, horn-like spur.

Disa hircicornis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
