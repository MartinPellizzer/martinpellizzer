Duggena tomentosa

Duggena tomentosa, commonly known as the woolly duggena, is a flowering plant native to parts of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive, soft appearance. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Duggena tomentosa is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique texture and modest floral display.
