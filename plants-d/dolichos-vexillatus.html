Dolichos vexillatus

Dolichos vexillatus, commonly known as the scarlet runner bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of South America.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets and produces vibrant red, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant bears elongated, cylindrical pods that contain edible beans, which are rich in protein and nutrients. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Dolichos vexillatus is often cultivated for both its ornamental value and its edible harvest.
