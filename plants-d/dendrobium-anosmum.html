Dendrobium anosmum

Dendrobium anosmum, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with cane-like stems that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers with prominent lips. Its leaves are usually clustered at the base of the stem and are lance-shaped.

Dendrobium anosmum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and is often cultivated for its fragrance.
