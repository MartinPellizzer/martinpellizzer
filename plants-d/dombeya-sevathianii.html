Dombeya sevathianii

Dombeya sevathianii, commonly known as the Seychelles dombeya, is a flowering plant native to the Seychelles.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that are usually pink or white in color. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a hairy texture.

Dombeya sevathianii is adapted to the tropical climate of its native habitat, with a preference for well-drained soils and full sunlight.
