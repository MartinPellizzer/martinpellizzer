Ducrosia olivieri

Ducrosia olivieri, commonly known as the olive ducrosia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as a shrub, featuring hairy, lance-shaped leaves and yellow-orange flowers that bloom in summer. Ducrosia olivieri is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.
