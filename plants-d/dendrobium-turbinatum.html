Dendrobium turbinatum

Dendrobium turbinatum, commonly known as the turbinate orchid, is a epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with multiple stems that produce clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant has leathery, strap-like leaves that are typically arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white with a distinctive, twisted shape.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
