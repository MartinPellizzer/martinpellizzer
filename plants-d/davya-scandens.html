Davya scandens

Davya scandens, commonly known as the climbing davya, is a perennial vine native to parts of Asia.

It features slender, twining stems that allow it to climb and spread over other plants or structures. The leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and typically white or pale yellow, appearing in clusters.

The plant is valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
