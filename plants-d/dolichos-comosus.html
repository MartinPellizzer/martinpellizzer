Dolichos comosus

Dolichos comosus, commonly known as the hairy urd or cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its hairy stems, trifoliate leaves, and erect growth habit. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers followed by elongated, pod-like fruits containing edible seeds. It is a valuable crop for its high protein content and ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen.

Dolichos comosus is also used in traditional medicine for its various therapeutic properties.
