Digitaria matsumurae

Digitaria matsumurae, commonly known as the Japanese wiregrass, is a grass species native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetland or marshy habitats.

Digitaria matsumurae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
