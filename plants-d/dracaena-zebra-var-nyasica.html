Dracaena zebra var. nyasica

Dracaena zebra var. nyasica, commonly known as the Zebra Dracaena, is a striking ornamental plant native to eastern Africa.

It features distinctive vertical stripes of bright green and yellow on its long, narrow leaves, giving it a unique, eye-catching appearance. The plant is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions. It produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by red berries, though it is primarily grown for its foliage.

This plant is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
