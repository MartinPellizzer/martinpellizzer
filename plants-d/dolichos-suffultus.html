Dolichos suffultus

Dolichos suffultus, commonly known as the inflated bean or the balloon vine, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, twining stems that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces elongated, inflated pods that resemble small balloons, which are characteristic of its genus. Its flowers are typically small and white or pale pink, arranged in clusters.

Dolichos suffultus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique pod structure.
