Dendrobium agusanense

Dendrobium agusanense, commonly known as the Agusan orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It is an epiphytic plant that grows on trees in tropical rainforests, typically in the highlands of Mindanao. The plant has a pseudobulb structure with fleshy stems that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually yellow or greenish-yellow, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also studied for its potential medicinal properties.
