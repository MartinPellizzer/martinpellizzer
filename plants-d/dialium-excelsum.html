Dialium excelsum

Dialium excelsum, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and can grow up to 30 meters tall, with a broad, spreading canopy. The tree produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody pods containing several seeds. Its bark and leaves have been used traditionally for their medicinal properties.

The plant is valued for its hard, durable wood and is often found in moist evergreen forests.
