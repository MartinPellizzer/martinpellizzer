Dorotheanthus tricolor

Dorotheanthus tricolor, commonly known as the three-color sunflower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tri-colored flowers that typically display red, yellow, and white petals. The plant is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and borders.
