Diospyros normanbyensis

Diospyros normanbyensis, commonly known as the Normanby's persimmon, is a tropical evergreen tree native to northern Australia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and lanceolate leaves. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by edible, orange-red berries that are highly valued for their sweet flavor. Due to its ornamental value and edible fruits, it is sometimes cultivated in horticultural settings.
