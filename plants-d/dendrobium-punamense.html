Dendrobium punamense

Dendrobium punamense, commonly known as the Punam orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Dendrobium genus, which includes over 1,200 species of orchids. The plant typically grows as a sympodial herb with pseudobulbs and linear leaves. It produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
