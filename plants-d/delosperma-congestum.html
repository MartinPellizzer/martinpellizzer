Delosperma congestum

Delosperma congestum, commonly known as the ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are adapted to store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant clusters of pink or purple flowers during the summer months. Its compact growth habit makes it an attractive addition to rock gardens and containers.

Delosperma congestum is also valued for its ability to tolerate poor soils and full sun exposure.
