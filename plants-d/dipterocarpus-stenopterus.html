Dipterocarpus stenopterus

Dipterocarpus stenopterus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved dipterocarp, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that are often dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or pale green, followed by woody, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

This species plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
