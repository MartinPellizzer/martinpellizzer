Digitaria vietnamensis

Digitaria vietnamensis, commonly known as Vietnamese digitaria, is a grass species native to Southeast Asia, particularly Vietnam.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as wetlands and grassy areas. It produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences with both male and female flowers, typical of grasses.

Digitaria vietnamensis is often used in ecological studies due to its adaptability and role in local ecosystems.
