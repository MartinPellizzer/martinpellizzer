Dendrobium tetragonum var. giganteum

Dendrobium tetragonum var. giganteum, commonly known as the giant dendrobium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, cane-like stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, often arranged in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
