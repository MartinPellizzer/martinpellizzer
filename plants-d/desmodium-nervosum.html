Desmodium nervosum

Desmodium nervosum, commonly known as hairy senna, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features trifoliate leaves with serrated edges and small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces pod-like fruits containing several elliptical seeds. It has a deep root system that helps in nitrogen fixation, making it useful in soil enrichment.

Its hairy stems and leaves are characteristic of the genus Desmodium.
