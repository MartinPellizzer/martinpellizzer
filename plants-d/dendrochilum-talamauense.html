Dendrochilum talamauense

Dendrochilum talamauense, commonly known as the Talamauense tree orchid, is a unique epiphytic orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its erect, branching stems and clustered leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a yellowish throat. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees in tropical rainforests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
