Dichrostachys cinerea subsp. platycarpa

Dichrostachys cinerea subsp.

platycarpa, commonly known as the African burr legume, is a shrub native to tropical Africa. It is characterized by its compound leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody, spiny pods.

The plant is adapted to dry and semi-arid environments, making it resilient to drought conditions. It is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

Its seeds are dispersed by animals that eat the pods, contributing to its widespread distribution.
