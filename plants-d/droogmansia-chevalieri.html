Droogmansia chevalieri

Droogmansia chevalieri, commonly known as the Cape snowflake, is a rare and striking plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its unique, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in winter. The plant has a rosette of silver-gray, woolly leaves that help it retain moisture in its arid habitat. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its limited distribution and specific growing conditions, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
