Dombeya rosea

Dombeya rosea, commonly known as the rose dombeya, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features showy, rose-like flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer, typically in shades of pink and white. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are hairy underneath, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

The flowers are often used in floral arrangements due to their delicate beauty and fragrance.
