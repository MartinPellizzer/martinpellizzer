Danthoniopsis petiolata

Danthoniopsis petiolata, commonly known as the slender feather grass, is a low-growing perennial grass native to South Africa.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in late summer, which are arranged in dense, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for xeriscaping in arid climates.
