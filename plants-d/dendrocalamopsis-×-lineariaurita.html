Dendrocalamopsis × lineariaurita

Dendrocalamopsis × lineariaurita, commonly known as the golden stripe bamboo, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Dendrocalamopsis grandis and Phyllostachys aurea.

It is characterized by its striking yellow stripes on the culms, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant is a tall, slender grass-like perennial that thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil. It produces feathery, panicle-shaped inflorescences and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

Due to its hybrid nature, it may exhibit variability in growth habits and foliage coloration.
