Dendrobium grande

Dendrobium grande, commonly known as the giant orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces clusters of large, showy flowers that are typically white or yellow with a greenish tinge. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding substrate.

Dendrobium grande is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
