Dendrobium polyschistum var. marginatum

Dendrobium polyschistum var. marginatum, commonly known as the Margined Dendrobium, is a flowering orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant that grows on trees and rocks, thriving in humid tropical environments. The plant features long, cane-like stems that produce clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is commonly cultivated for its beautiful blooms.
