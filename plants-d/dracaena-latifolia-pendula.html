Dracaena latifolia pendula

Dracaena latifolia pendula, commonly known as the hanging dracaena, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, drooping leaves that give it a cascading appearance. The leaves are broad, glossy, and dark green, often with a slight variegation. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its air-purifying properties and low maintenance requirements.
