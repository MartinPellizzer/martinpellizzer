Dryopteris willsii

Dryopteris willsii, commonly known as the Wills' wood fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and dense, dark green fronds. The fronds are typically bipinnate, with a distinctive, upright, and often arching growth form. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its lush foliage.
