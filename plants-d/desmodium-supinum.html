Desmodium supinum

Desmodium supinum, commonly known as the prostrate desmodium, is a flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its prostrate growth habit, with small, trifoliate leaves that often lie flat on the ground. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It is native to various regions in Africa and is often found in grasslands and disturbed soils.

Desmodium supinum is known for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil fertility.
