Dittrichia Viscosa Subsp. Revoluta

Dittrichia viscosa subsp. revoluta is a hardy shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa. It is known for its compact growth habit and ability to thrive in harsh, dry environments. The plant features small, hairy leaves and produces clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental value. This resilient species is a valuable addition to drought-tolerant gardens and natural landscapes.
