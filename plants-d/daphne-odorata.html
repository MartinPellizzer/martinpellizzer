Daphne odorata

Daphne odorata, commonly known as sweet spurge, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features small, fragrant flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring, often in shades of pink or white. Its leaves are dark green, glossy, and have a slightly waxy texture.

Daphne odorata is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its pleasant fragrance and visual appeal.
