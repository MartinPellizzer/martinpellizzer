Dillenia grandifolia

Dillenia grandifolia, commonly known as the giant dillenia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant produces showy, yellowish-white flowers with five broad petals, which bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a woody, fleshy capsule that splits open to release numerous seeds.

Dillenia grandifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
