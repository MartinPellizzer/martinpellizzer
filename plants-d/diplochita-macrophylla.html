Diplochita macrophylla

Diplochita macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf diplochita, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in greenish-white or yellowish hues.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its foliage.
