Didymocarpus heucherifolius var. yinzhengii

Didymocarpus heucherifolius var. yinzhengii, commonly known as the Yinzhengii Indian bellflower, is a rare and unique variant of the Indian bellflower species.

It is native to the mountainous regions of southern China, where it thrives in cool, humid climates. The plant features striking bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, often with a distinctive purple or violet hue at the base. Its leaves are typically broad and heart-shaped, with a dark green upper surface and a lighter underside.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and specialized horticultural collections.
