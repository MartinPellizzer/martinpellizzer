Draba yunnanensis var. gracilipes

Draba yunnanensis var. gracilipes, commonly known as the Yunnan draba, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in alpine and subalpine regions of China. The plant features basal leaves that are linear to lanceolate and small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
