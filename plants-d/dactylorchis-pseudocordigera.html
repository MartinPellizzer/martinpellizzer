Dactylorchis pseudocordigera

Dactylorchis pseudocordigera, commonly known as the false heart-leaf orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, heart-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, where it thrives in the undergrowth. Its flowers are usually small and white or pale pink, arranged in a spike.

Dactylorchis pseudocordigera is a rare and delicate species, often found in specific microhabitats with high humidity and rich organic soil.
