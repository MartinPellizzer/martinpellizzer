Dracaena atropurpurea var. kurzii

Dracaena atropurpurea var. kurzii, commonly known as the Purple Dracaena, is a striking ornamental plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, dark green leaves with a distinctive purple or reddish hue along the edges, giving it a unique visual appeal. The plant is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is known for its upright growth habit and air-purifying qualities. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its hardiness and aesthetic value, it is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor landscaping.
