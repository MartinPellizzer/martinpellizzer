Dendrobium vexillarius var. minor

Dendrobium vexillarius var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, tropical climates. Its leaves are strap-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, and it blooms during the warmer months.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements in cultivation.
