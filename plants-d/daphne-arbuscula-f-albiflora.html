Daphne arbuscula f. albiflora

Daphne arbuscula f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered daphne, is a compact evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, fragrant foliage with a distinctive, waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by bright red berries in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is often cultivated in gardens for its visual and aromatic qualities.
