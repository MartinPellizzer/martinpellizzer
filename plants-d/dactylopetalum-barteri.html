Dactylopetalum barteri

Dactylopetalum barteri, commonly known as the African silver dollar tree, is a tropical plant native to eastern Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree with smooth, dark grey bark and elliptical, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers followed by round, silver-dollar-shaped fruits that are edible. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and fruit.

The species is also valued for its ability to provide shade and its role in traditional medicine.
