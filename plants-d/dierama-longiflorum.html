Dierama longiflorum

Dierama longiflorum, commonly known as the long-flowered dierama, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, grass-like stems that bear clusters of showy, pink to purple flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a valuable addition to both wild and cultivated landscapes.
