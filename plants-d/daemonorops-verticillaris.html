Daemonorops verticillaris

Daemonorops verticillaris, commonly known as the spiral palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spirally arranged leaves that give it a unique appearance. The plant typically grows as a clumping palm, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are long, pinnate, and often have a bluish-green hue.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its aesthetic appeal.
