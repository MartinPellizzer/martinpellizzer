Dioscorea aristolochiifolia var. parvifolia

Dioscorea aristolochiifolia var. parvifolia, commonly known as the small-leaved yam, is a member of the Dioscoreaceae family.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, white flowers. The plant produces tuberous roots that are often used in traditional medicine. It is native to tropical regions of Asia and thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it distinct from other yam species, particularly in leaf morphology and floral structure.
