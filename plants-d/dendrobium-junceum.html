Dendrobium junceum

Dendrobium junceum, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a slender, often branched stem that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink, typically blooming during the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and strap-like, arranged spirally along the stem.

Dendrobium junceum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
