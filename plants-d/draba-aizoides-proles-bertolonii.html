Draba aizoides proles bertolonii

Draba aizoides proles bertolonii, commonly known as the Bertoloni's rockcress, is a member of the Brassicaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous plant characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers in early spring, typically between March and May. It thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine environments.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
