Dendrobium stenophyton

Dendrobium stenophyton, commonly known as the narrow-leaf orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Dendrobium stenophyton is often cultivated for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
