Dieffenbachia aurantiaca

Dieffenbachia aurantiaca, commonly known as the flamingo plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to South America.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a striking combination of green, white, and yellow variegation. The plant is part of the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. Its leaves are thick and waxy, adapted to retain moisture in its natural habitat.

Dieffenbachia aurantiaca is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
