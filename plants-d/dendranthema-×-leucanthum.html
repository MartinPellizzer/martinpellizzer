Dendranthema × leucanthum

Dendranthema × leucanthum, commonly known as Shasta daisy, is a hybrid perennial plant native to the western United States.

It features showy, white ray florets surrounding a yellow-orange center, giving it a daisy-like appearance. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and produces flowers from late spring to early autumn. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its botanical name reflects its origin from the cross between Dendranthema leucanthum and Dendranthema x leucanthum, highlighting its hybrid nature.
