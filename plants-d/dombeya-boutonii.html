Dombeya boutonii

Dombeya boutonii, commonly known as the African snowball, is a deciduous shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, white, ball-shaped inflorescences that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Dombeya boutonii is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
