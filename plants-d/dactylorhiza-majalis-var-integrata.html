Dactylorhiza majalis var. integrata

Dactylorhiza majalis var. integrata, commonly known as the greater early purple orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, calcareous habitats such as woodlands and grasslands. The plant features a short rhizome with several leaves and produces tall, erect flowering stems. Its flowers are violet to purple in color, with a distinctive hooded lip.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is noted for its ornamental value and ecological importance.
