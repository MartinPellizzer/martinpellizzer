Delphinium turkestanicum

Delphinium turkestanicum, commonly known as the Turkestan larkspur, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia.

It features tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant produces showy blue to violet flowers in racemes, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a rough, hairy texture.

Delphinium turkestanicum is adapted to rocky and arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils.
