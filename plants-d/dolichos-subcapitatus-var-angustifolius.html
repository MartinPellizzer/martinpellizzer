Dolichos subcapitatus var. angustifolius

Dolichos subcapitatus var. angustifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaf cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, angular pods that typically contain 3-5 kidney-shaped seeds. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellowish-white flowers. It is well adapted to warm climates and is often cultivated for its edible seeds.

This variety is valued for its nutritional content and use in both human and animal diets.
