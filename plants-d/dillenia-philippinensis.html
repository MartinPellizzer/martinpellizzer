Dillenia philippinensis

Dillenia philippinensis, commonly known as the Philippine dillenia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Dilleniaceae.

It is native to the Philippines and is characterized by its large, showy, white to pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. It produces distinctive, fleshy, spiny fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Dillenia philippinensis is often found in moist, forested areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
