Daemonorops brachystachys

Daemonorops brachystachys, commonly known as the dwarf cycad, is a species of cycad native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact size and distinctive, feather-like leaves that are typically bright green. The plant produces small, cone-like structures that contain its seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with well-drained soil and partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
