Dactylorhiza ilgazica

Dactylorhiza ilgazica, commonly known as the Ilgaz orchid, is a rare and endemic orchid species native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its terrestrial growth habit and unique floral structure. The plant typically features dark green, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, pinkish-brown flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in mountainous regions with high humidity.
