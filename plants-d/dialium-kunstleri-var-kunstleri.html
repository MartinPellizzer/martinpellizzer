Dialium kunstleri var. kunstleri

Dialium kunstleri var. kunstleri, commonly known as the African cherry or African oil tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, gray-barked trunk and large, palmately lobed leaves. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by spherical, woody fruits containing several seeds.

Its bark and seeds are used in traditional medicine, and the tree is also valued for its timber and ornamental qualities.
