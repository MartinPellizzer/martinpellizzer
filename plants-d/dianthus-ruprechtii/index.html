Dianthus Ruprechtii

Dianthus ruprechtii is a striking member of the carnation family known for its vibrant pink and white flowers. Native to the mountains of Central Asia, this plant thrives in rocky and alpine environments. Its delicate petals and compact growth make it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. The plant's ability to withstand harsh conditions makes it a resilient addition to any garden. With its elegant appearance and hardy nature, Dianthus ruprechtii is both beautiful and easy to care for.
