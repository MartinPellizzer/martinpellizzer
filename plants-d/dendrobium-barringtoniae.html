Dendrobium barringtoniae

Dendrobium barringtoniae, commonly known as the Barrington's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the Philippines.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in tropical rainforests. The plant produces long, pendant-like inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers that are often white or pale yellow. Its pseudobulbs are cylindrical and store water, allowing it to survive in humid, shaded environments.

Dendrobium barringtoniae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
