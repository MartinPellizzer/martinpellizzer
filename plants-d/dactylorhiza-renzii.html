Dactylorhiza renzii

Dactylorhiza renzii, commonly known as the Malagasy orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces a single spike of pale yellow to greenish-white flowers, which are typically clustered in a raceme. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is adapted to the tropical climate of its native habitat.

Dactylorhiza renzii is notable for its unique floral structure and is of interest to botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
