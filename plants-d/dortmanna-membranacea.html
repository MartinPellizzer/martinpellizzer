Dortmanna membranacea

Dortmanna membranacea, commonly known as the membranous dock, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Polygonaceae.

It is characterized by its basal leaves that are usually heart-shaped and covered in a thin, membranous layer. The plant produces small, greenish flowers arranged in dense clusters, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in wetlands or along the edges of streams.

Dortmanna membranacea is primarily found in temperate regions of Europe and parts of Asia.
