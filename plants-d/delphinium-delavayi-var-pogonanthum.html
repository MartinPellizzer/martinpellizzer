Delphinium delavayi var. pogonanthum

Delphinium delavayi var. pogonanthum, commonly known as the Pogonanthum larkspur, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces showy, blue to purple flowers arranged in dense spikes, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
