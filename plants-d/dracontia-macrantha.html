Dracontia macrantha

Dracontia macrantha, commonly known as the giant dragon's beard, is a striking member of the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, branched stems and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant produces distinctive, elongated, hair-like bracts that give it its common name. Native to southern Africa, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

Its unique morphology makes it a notable ornamental plant in gardens and botanical collections.
