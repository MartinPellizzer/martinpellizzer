Dracocephalum altajense

Dracocephalum altajense, commonly known as Altai dragonhead, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features square stems and opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, tubular flowers are typically purple or blue, arranged in dense spikes.

It is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal medicine.
