Dracula woolwardiae

Dracula woolwardiae, commonly known as the Woolward's Dracula, is a rare and striking epiphytic orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, pendulous flowers that resemble miniature, colorful drapes. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in high humidity and partial shade. Its leaves are elongated and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native habitat.

Dracula woolwardiae is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and limited distribution.
