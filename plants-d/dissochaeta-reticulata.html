Dissochaeta reticulata

Dissochaeta reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined dissochaeta, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Poaceae, or grass family.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, linear leaves with a distinct reticulate venation pattern. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences, which are typical of grasses. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and is often found in moist, open habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
