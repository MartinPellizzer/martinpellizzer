Dendrochilum pseudowenzelii

Dendrochilum pseudowenzelii, commonly known as the Wenzel's tree orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs and is characterized by its long, narrow leaves. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences bearing numerous fragrant, showy flowers with yellow and white petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrochilum pseudowenzelii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
