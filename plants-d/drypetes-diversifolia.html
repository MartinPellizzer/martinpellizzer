Drypetes diversifolia

Drypetes diversifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved drypetes, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, followed by small, woody fruits containing a single seed. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The species is valued for its hardy nature and potential use in traditional medicine.
