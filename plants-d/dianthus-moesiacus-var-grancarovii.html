Dianthus moesiacus var. grancarovii

Dianthus moesiacus var. grancarovii, commonly known as the Carpathian campion, is a flowering plant native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It is characterized by its pink to reddish flowers and basal rosette of leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils. It produces small, showy flowers that bloom in early summer.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and hardy nature.
