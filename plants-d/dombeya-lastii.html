Dombeya lastii

Dombeya lastii, commonly known as the lastii dombeya, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Sterculiaceae and is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features simple, ovate leaves with a hairy texture and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. Dombeya lastii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral displays.

It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a sunny to partially shaded location.
