Delphinium cardinale var. angustifolium

Delphinium cardinale var. angustifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaved larkspur, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It features tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters. The plant produces showy, blue to violet flowers in racemes, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a bluish-green hue and a hairy texture.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, loamy soils.
