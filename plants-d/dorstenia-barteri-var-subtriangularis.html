Dorstenia barteri var. subtriangularis

Dorstenia barteri var. subtriangularis, commonly known as the triangular leaf fig, is a striking plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, elongated, and often triangular-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a subtle aesthetic to its foliage. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its distinctive appearance makes it a popular choice for collectors and enthusiasts of rare houseplants.
