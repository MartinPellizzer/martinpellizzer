Dehaasia turfosa

Dehaasia turfosa, commonly known as the Turfgrass of the Dehaas region, is a low-growing perennial plant native to specific regions in southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit, with small, narrow leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in tight inflorescences, often blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy ground cover plant.

Due to its resilience and aesthetic appeal, it is sometimes used in ornamental landscaping in arid or semi-arid environments.
