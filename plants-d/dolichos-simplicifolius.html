Dolichos simplicifolius

Dolichos simplicifolius, commonly known as the simple-leaved bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features compound leaves with simple, ovate leaflets and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant bears elongated, cylindrical pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun for optimal growth.

Dolichos simplicifolius is valued for its ornamental appeal and as a source of edible seeds.
