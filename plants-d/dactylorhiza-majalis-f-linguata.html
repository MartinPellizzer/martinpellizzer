Dactylorhiza majalis f. linguata

Dactylorhiza majalis f. linguata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in damp, calcareous habitats such as marshes and wet meadows. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a single, narrow lanceolate leaf. Its flowers are pale green to yellowish, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

It is a herbaceous perennial, blooming in late spring to early summer.
