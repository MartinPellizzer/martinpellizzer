Dioscorea banzhuana

Dioscorea banzhuana, commonly known as Banzhuan yam, is a species of tuberous plant belonging to the family Dioscoreaceae.

It is native to China, particularly found in the provinces of Yunnan and Guizhou, where it thrives in mountainous regions with a subtropical climate. The plant produces long, cylindrical tubers that are used in traditional Chinese medicine and as a food source. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a rough texture, while its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the same plant.

The tubers are valued for their medicinal properties, including potential anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects.
