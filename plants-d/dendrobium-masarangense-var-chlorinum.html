Dendrobium masarangense var. chlorinum

Dendrobium masarangense var. chlorinum, commonly known as the green-leafed dendrobium, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features elongated, strap-like leaves that are typically a vibrant green, often with a waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-white flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-draining soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
