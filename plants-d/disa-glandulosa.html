Disa glandulosa

Disa glandulosa, commonly known as the glandular disa, is a species of orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant features a short, stocky rhizome and produces numerous slender, hairy stems. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered with fine, glandular hairs.

Disa glandulosa thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and forest margins.
