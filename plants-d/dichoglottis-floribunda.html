Dichoglottis floribunda

Dichoglottis floribunda, commonly known as the golden arrow plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green, heart-shaped leaves that are often adorned with a yellow or white midrib. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dichoglottis floribunda is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and ease of care.
