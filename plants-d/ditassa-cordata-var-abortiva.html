Ditassa cordata var. abortiva

Ditassa cordata var. abortiva, commonly known as the aborted heartleaf.

It is a rare variant of the heartleaf plant, characterized by its distinctive, heart-shaped leaves that often exhibit reduced or aborted growth patterns. The plant typically features a compact, bushy growth habit with small, glossy foliage. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its botanical interest lies in its unique morphological variations and potential ecological significance.
