Dyckia ferox f. hamosa

Dyckia ferox f. hamosa, commonly known as the fierce dyckia, is a striking bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, sword-shaped leaves that form a rosette, often with a reddish or bronze hue. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences that bear showy, pinkish-white flowers. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.

Its hardy nature and ornamental appeal make it a favored plant among bromeliad enthusiasts.
