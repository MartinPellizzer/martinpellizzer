Delphinium cardiopetalum var. oranense

Delphinium cardiopetalum var. oranense, commonly known as the Oranense Larkspur, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It features tall, slender stems that bear clusters of striking blue to purple flowers, which are typically spurred at the base. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine displays.

Due to its ornamental value and unique floral structure, it is often cultivated for both garden beauty and botanical study.
