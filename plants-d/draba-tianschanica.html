Draba tianschanica

Draba tianschanica, commonly known as the Tian Shan rockcress, is a small flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and alpine environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh climatic conditions. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, and it produces small, rounded seeds.

Draba tianschanica is often found in high-altitude meadows and is a representative of the diverse flora of the Tian Shan range.
