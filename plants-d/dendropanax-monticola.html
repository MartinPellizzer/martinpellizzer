Dendropanax monticola

Dendropanax monticola, commonly known as the mountain aralia, is a woody shrub native to the mountainous regions of southeastern China.

It belongs to the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves that are usually opposite or whorled. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in umbels, followed by berries that are edible for some bird species.

Its habitat typically includes moist, shaded areas in forests, where it thrives in cool, temperate climates.
