Dianthus × laucheanus

Dianthus × laucheanus, commonly known as the hybrid carnation, is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its showy, bilaterally symmetrical flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows to a height of 10 to 30 centimeters, with compact, bushy foliage. Its flowers consist of five petals and a central cluster of stamens, which are often more prominent than the petals.

Dianthus × laucheanus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
