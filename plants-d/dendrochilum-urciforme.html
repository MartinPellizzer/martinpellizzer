Dendrochilum urciforme

Dendrochilum urciforme, commonly known as the spiny orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, spiny leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a contrasting purple or red lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Dendrochilum urciforme is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
