Dendrobium serra

Dendrobium serra, commonly known as the serrated orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces long, narrow leaves and bears racemes of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrobium serra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
