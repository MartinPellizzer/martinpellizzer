Dracaena rubroaurantiaca

Dracaena rubroaurantiaca, commonly known as the Flame ZZ plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that feature a mix of red, orange, and white hues, making it a popular ornamental plant. The plant has a robust, upright stem with dark green, glossy leaves that often have a waxy texture. It is relatively low-maintenance, thriving in indirect light and requiring minimal watering.

Dracaena rubroaurantiaca is also known for its ability to purify indoor air, adding to its appeal as a houseplant.
