Davallia forbesii

Davallia forbesii, commonly known as the maidenhair fern, is a tropical evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, arching fronds with a distinctive, lacy appearance, often with a pale green to silver underside. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with creeping rhizomes that produce new shoots and roots. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
