Dendrobium jabiense

Dendrobium jabiense, commonly known as the Jabiense orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It features a sympodial growth habit with cane-like stems that bear alternate, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking racemes of fragrant, yellow-orange flowers with a distinctive red lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Dendrobium jabiense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
