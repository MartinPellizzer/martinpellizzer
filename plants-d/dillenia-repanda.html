Dillenia repanda

Dillenia repanda, commonly known as the broadleaf dillenia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant produces showy, yellowish-white flowers with five petals, which are typically borne in clusters. Its fruit is a fleshy, yellow berry that contains numerous seeds.

Dillenia repanda is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
