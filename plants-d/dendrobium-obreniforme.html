Dendrobium obreniforme

Dendrobium obreniforme, commonly known as the obrien's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with multiple stems that can become branched over time. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink, often with intricate patterns on the petals. Its leaves are usually elliptical and arranged in a spiral around the stem.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media for optimal growth.
