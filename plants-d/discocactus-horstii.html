Discocactus horstii

Discocactus horstii, commonly known as the Horstii Discocactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It features a short, cylindrical stem covered in dense, white to pale blue spines that provide protection from herbivores and intense sunlight. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. Its succulent leaves store water, allowing it to thrive in dry environments with minimal rainfall.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation due to its hardy nature and attractive appearance.
