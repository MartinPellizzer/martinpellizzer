Desmodium skinneri var. skinneri

Desmodium skinneri var. skinneri, commonly known as the slender ticktrefoil, is a herbaceous annual plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features trifoliate leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and disturbed areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been introduced to other regions for horticultural purposes.
