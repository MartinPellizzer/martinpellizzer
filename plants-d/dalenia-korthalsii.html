Dalenia korthalsii

Dalenia korthalsii, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a striking perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a contrasting purple or red throat. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Dalenia korthalsii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
