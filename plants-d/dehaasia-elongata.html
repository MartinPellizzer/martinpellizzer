Dehaasia elongata

Dehaasia elongata, commonly known as the elongated dehaasia, is a unique plant species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated stems that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often arranged alternately along the stem.

Dehaasia elongata thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
