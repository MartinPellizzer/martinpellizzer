Dieffenbachia enderi

Dieffenbachia enderi, commonly known as the Leopard Plant, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that feature a mix of green, white, and yellow hues. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other popular houseplants like peace lilies. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Dieffenbachia enderi is known for its ability to purify the air and is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
