Dendrobium pachystele

Dendrobium pachystele, commonly known as the giant dendrobium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, cane-like stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with a distinctive red or purple lip. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, requiring high humidity and bright, indirect light.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both horticultural collections and ornamental gardening.
