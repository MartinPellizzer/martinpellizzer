Dendrobium lasioglossum

Dendrobium lasioglossum, commonly known as the golden lip dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, cane-like stems that store water and nutrients, typical of its orchid family. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a distinctive lip that is broader than the other petals. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Dendrobium lasioglossum is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
