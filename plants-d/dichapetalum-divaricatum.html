Dichapetalum divaricatum

Dichapetalum divaricatum, commonly known as the African starwood, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are oval to lance-shaped with serrated edges. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland and rocky habitats.

The species is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its delicate floral display.
