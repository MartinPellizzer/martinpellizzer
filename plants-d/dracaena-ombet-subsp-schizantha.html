Dracaena ombet subsp. schizantha

Dracaena ombet subsp.

schizantha, commonly known as the Madagascar dragon tree, is a member of the Asparagaceae family. It is native to Madagascar and is characterized by its tall, unbranched stems and narrow, sword-shaped leaves.

The plant produces small, clustered white flowers followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright light.

Due to its striking appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor and outdoor cultivation.
