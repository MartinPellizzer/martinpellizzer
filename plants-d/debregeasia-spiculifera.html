Debregeasia spiculifera

Debregeasia spiculifera, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and clusters of small, showy flowers that bloom in a variety of colors. The plant belongs to the family Gesneriaceae and is well adapted to humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and emit a mild fragrance when touched.

Debregeasia spiculifera is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
