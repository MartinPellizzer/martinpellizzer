Delphinium yulungshanicum

Delphinium yulungshanicum, commonly known as the Yulungshan larkspur, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant produces showy, blue to purple flowers in raceme-like clusters, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are palmately divided, with a rough texture and a distinctive blue-green hue.

Delphinium yulungshanicum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
