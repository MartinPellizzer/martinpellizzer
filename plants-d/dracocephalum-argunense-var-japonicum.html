Dracocephalum argunense var. japonicum

Dracocephalum argunense var. japonicum, commonly known as Japanese dragonhead, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a slightly rough texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically purple or blue, blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its aromatic leaves and ornamental flowers make it a valued addition to horticultural collections.
