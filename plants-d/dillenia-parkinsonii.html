Dillenia parkinsonii

Dillenia parkinsonii, commonly known as the Mexican dillenia, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters tall, characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves. The plant produces showy, yellowish-white flowers with five petals, which bloom in spring. Its fruits are large, fleshy berries that contain multiple seeds.

Dillenia parkinsonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking foliage and floral display.
