Dichapetalum thouarsianum var. macrophyllum

Dichapetalum thouarsianum var. macrophyllum, commonly known as the giant flame tree, is a striking tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its large, showy red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual impact. The plant has compound leaves with long, slender leaflets, and it produces distinctive, woody seed pods that split open when mature. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Due to its size and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for tropical gardens and landscapes.
