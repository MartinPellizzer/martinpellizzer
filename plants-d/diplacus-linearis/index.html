Diplacus Linearis

Diplacus linearis is a striking flowering plant native to the western United States. Known for its vibrant pink to purple blooms, it adds a splash of color to any garden. This species thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens. Its long, narrow leaves and showy flowers make it both ornamental and ecologically valuable. Despite its beauty, it requires careful cultivation to ensure its survival in changing environments.
