Dracaena nigrorubra

Dracaena nigrorubra, commonly known as the red dragon tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Madagascar.

It features lance-shaped, dark green leaves with a reddish tinge along the midrib, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. Dracaena nigrorubra is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and air-purifying properties.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to maintain its health.
