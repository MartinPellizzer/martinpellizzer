Dipladenia nobilis

Dipladenia nobilis, commonly known as the Crown of Thorns, is a tropical vine native to South America.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and dark green, waxy leaves. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in warm, sunny climates.

Dipladenia nobilis is also known for its ability to tolerate drought and poor soil conditions.
