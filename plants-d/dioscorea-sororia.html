Dioscorea sororia

Dioscorea sororia, commonly known as the yellow arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is a perennial vine that typically grows in wet, swampy areas. It has a tuberous root system, which is often used in traditional medicine.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
