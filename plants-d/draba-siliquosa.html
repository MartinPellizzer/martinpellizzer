Draba siliquosa

Draba siliquosa, commonly known as the silique draba, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches heights of 5 to 15 centimeters. The plant features basal rosettes of linear to oblanceolate leaves and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers in early spring. Its fruit is a silique, a type of dry, dehiscent fruit characteristic of the mustard family.

Draba siliquosa is often found in alpine and arctic environments, thriving in rocky or gravelly soils.
