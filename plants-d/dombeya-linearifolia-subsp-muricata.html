Dombeya linearifolia subsp. muricata

Dombeya linearifolia subsp.

muricata, commonly known as the roughleaf dombeya, is a shrub native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in small, rough projections, giving it a textured appearance.

The plant produces showy, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and drought tolerance.
