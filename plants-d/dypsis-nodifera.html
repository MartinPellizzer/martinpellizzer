Dypsis nodifera

Dypsis nodifera, commonly known as the Madagascar palm, is a succulent plant native to Madagascar.

It features a short, thick trunk covered in smooth, grayish-brown bark. The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are long, narrow, and arranged in a rosette pattern around the trunk.

Dypsis nodifera is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.
