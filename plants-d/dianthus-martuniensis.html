Dianthus martuniensis

Dianthus martuniensis, commonly known as the Martuni carnation, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Turkey.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in temperate climates. Its leaves are linear and slightly hairy, while its stems are upright and branching.

Dianthus martuniensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blossoms.
