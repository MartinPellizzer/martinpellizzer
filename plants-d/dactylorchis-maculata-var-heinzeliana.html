Dactylorchis maculata var. heinzeliana

Dactylorchis maculata var. heinzeliana, commonly known as the spotted orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It features a rhizomatous growth habit with a rosette of linear, grass-like leaves. The plant produces showy, pale yellow to greenish-yellow flowers with distinctive purple spots on the petals and sepals. It blooms in late spring to early summer, typically after rainfall.

This variety is often found in moist, sandy soils in woodlands and open areas.
