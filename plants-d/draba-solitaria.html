Draba solitaria

Draba solitaria, commonly known as the solitary whitlow grass, is a small flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is native to arctic and alpine regions of the Northern Hemisphere, including North America, Europe, and Asia. Draba solitaria is an important component of alpine ecosystems and is often used in horticulture for its hardy nature and ornamental value.
