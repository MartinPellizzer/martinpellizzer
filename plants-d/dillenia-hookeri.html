Dillenia hookeri

Dillenia hookeri, commonly known as the hooker's dillenia, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves. The plant produces showy, yellowish-white flowers that are often clustered in umbels. Its fruit is a distinctive, fleshy, many-seeded capsule that splits open when mature.

Dillenia hookeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.
