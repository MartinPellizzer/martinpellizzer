Diospyros beberonnii

Diospyros beberonnii, commonly known as the African persimmon, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Ebenaceae family, which includes other notable species like ebony. The plant is characterized by its smooth, dark bark and simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape. Its small, yellowish flowers are followed by fruits that mature to a reddish-purple color.

The tree is valued for its hard, durable wood and is often found in moist, forested areas.
