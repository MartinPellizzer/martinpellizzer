Dichopsis elliptica

Dichopsis elliptica, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and showy, violet-colored flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Dichopsis elliptica is relatively easy to care for, making it a favorite among plant enthusiasts.
