Dianthus deltoides var. glaucus

Dianthus deltoides var. glaucus, commonly known as the blue dianthus, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant blue to purple flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant features fuzzy, silver-green leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and drought tolerance.
