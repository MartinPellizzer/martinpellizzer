Dorstenia dinklagei var. dinklagei

It is characterized by its striking, spirally twisted stems that resemble a serpent's spine, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are often hidden among the foliage. Native to the tropical rainforests of Central and South America, it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and sought-after specimen among plant enthusiasts and horticulturists.
