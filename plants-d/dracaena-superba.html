Dracaena superba

Dracaena superba, commonly known as the red dragon tree, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and vibrant, red-tinged leaves that give it its dramatic appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by red berries, which are toxic to humans and animals. Dracaena superba thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor ornamental gardening.
