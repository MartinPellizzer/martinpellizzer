Delosperma vandermerwei

Delosperma vandermerwei, commonly known as the "ice plant," is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves and vibrant clusters of star-shaped flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained sandy soils and full sun, making it well-suited for arid environments. Its ability to store water in its thick leaves allows it to survive in drought conditions.

Delosperma vandermerwei is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and prolonged blooming period.
