Deutzia baroniana var. insignis

Deutzia baroniana var. insignis, commonly known as the Barony deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features clusters of fragrant, white flowers that bloom in early summer, adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter underside. It typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and prefers well-drained soil in full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
