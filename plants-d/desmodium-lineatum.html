Desmodium lineatum

Desmodium lineatum, commonly known as hairy beggar's ticks, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a legume, belonging to the family Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, pea-like flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions. It has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
