Dillenia aurea

Dillenia aurea, commonly known as golden dillenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its showy, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate or elliptic in shape. It produces small, berry-like fruits that are edible and contain several seeds.

Dillenia aurea is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking floral display.
