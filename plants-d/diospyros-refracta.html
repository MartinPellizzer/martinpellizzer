Diospyros refracta

Diospyros refracta, commonly known as the translucent persimmon, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Ebenaceae family, which includes ebony and persimmon trees. The plant is characterized by its smooth, dark bark and opposite, simple leaves that are usually elliptic or ovate in shape. Its fruit is a small, fleshy berry that changes color from green to yellow or orange as it matures.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
