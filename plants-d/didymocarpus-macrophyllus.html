Didymocarpus macrophyllus

Didymocarpus macrophyllus, commonly known as the Chinese hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, pink to red flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has broad, glossy leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Didymocarpus macrophyllus is also valued for its use in traditional medicine.
