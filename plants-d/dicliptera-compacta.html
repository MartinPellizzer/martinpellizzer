Dicliptera compacta

Dicliptera compacta, commonly known as the compact blady grass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and narrow, blade-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along water bodies.

Dicliptera compacta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping or as a ground cover.
