Darwinia camptostylis

Darwinia camptostylis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Darwinia, is a perennial shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its striking spikes of pink to red tubular flowers that bloom in spring. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Darwinia camptostylis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
