Dendrochilum pterogyne

Dendrochilum pterogyne, commonly known as the winged lip orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, elongated pseudobulbs and linear leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with a distinct wing-like structure on the lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Dendrochilum pterogyne is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
