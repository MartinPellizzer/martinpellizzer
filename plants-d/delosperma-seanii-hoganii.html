Delosperma seanii-hoganii

Delosperma seanii-hoganii, commonly known as the Hogan's ice plant, is a low-growing succulent native to arid regions of South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in dry conditions. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers in late spring or early summer, adding a striking visual element to its habitat. It is well adapted to rocky soils and full sun, making it a hardy choice for xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular plant among succulent enthusiasts.
