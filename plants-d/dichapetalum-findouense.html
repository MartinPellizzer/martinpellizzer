Dichapetalum findouense

Dichapetalum findouense, commonly known as the African star orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments within rainforests. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, while its roots are fibrous and adapted to nutrient-poor soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
