Digitaria polybotrya

Digitaria polybotrya, commonly known as the spikegrass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and elongated, narrow leaves with a bluish-green color. The plant produces spikelets that are arranged in dense clusters, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

Digitaria polybotrya is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its hardy nature.
