Delphinium gommengingeri

Delphinium gommengingeri, commonly known as the Chinese larkspur, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, spiky inflorescence composed of numerous blue to purple flowers. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in cooler climates and well-drained soil, often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens, though its toxicity requires caution in handling.
