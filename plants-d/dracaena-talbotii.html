Dracaena talbotii

Dracaena talbotii, commonly known as the Talbot's dracaena, is a striking evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features long, narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a subtle reddish tinge along the edges. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value and resilience, it is a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
