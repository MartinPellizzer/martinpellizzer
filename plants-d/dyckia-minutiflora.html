Dyckia minutiflora

Dyckia minutiflora, commonly known as the miniature dyckia, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color with a waxy coating. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in summer, adding a delicate contrast to its foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Dyckia minutiflora is prized for its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements in indoor and outdoor settings.
