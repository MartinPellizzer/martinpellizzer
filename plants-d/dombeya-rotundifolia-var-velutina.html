Dombeya rotundifolia var. velutina

Dombeya rotundifolia var. velutina, commonly known as velvetleaf dombeya, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its rounded, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces showy, fragrant clusters of white or pale yellow flowers in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long blooming period.
