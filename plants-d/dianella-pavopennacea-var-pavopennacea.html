Dianella pavopennacea var. pavopennacea

Dianella pavopennacea var. pavopennacea, commonly known as the narrow-leaved flax lily, is a perennial evergreen plant native to New Zealand.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically a deep green color, arranged in a rosette formation. The plant produces striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This ornamental plant is valued for its elegant appearance and is often used in gardens and landscaping.
