Dombeya astrapaeoides

Dombeya astrapaeoides, commonly known as the mountain dombeya, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, often appearing in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a hairy texture.

Dombeya astrapaeoides thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to temperate to subtropical climates.
