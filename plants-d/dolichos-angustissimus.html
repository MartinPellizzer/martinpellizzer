Dolichos angustissimus

Dolichos angustissimus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves and small, hairy pods that contain edible seeds. The plant typically grows as an annual herb, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun for optimal growth.

Dolichos angustissimus is valued for its nutritional seeds, which are rich in protein and used in various cuisines.
