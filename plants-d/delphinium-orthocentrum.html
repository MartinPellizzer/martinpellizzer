Delphinium orthocentrum

Delphinium orthocentrum, commonly known as the Sierra delphinium, is a species native to the western United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in mountainous regions, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. The plant features tall, sparsely branched stems with large, showy blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often found in wildflower gardens and alpine meadows.
