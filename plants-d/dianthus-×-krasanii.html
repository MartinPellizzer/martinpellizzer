Dianthus × krasanii

Dianthus × krasanii, commonly known as the hybrid carnation, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is a cross between Dianthus chinensis and Dianthus gratianopolitanus, resulting in a variety that exhibits traits from both parents. The plant produces showy, bicolor flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, often with a distinctive central dark spot. It is typically grown as an ornamental plant for its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.

Dianthus × krasanii prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden beds and container planting.
