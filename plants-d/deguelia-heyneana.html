Deguelia heyneana

Deguelia heyneana, commonly known as the jungle orchid, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its twining stems and showy, orchid-like flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. Its leaves are typically palmately compound, with three to five leaflets.

Deguelia heyneana is often found in humid, shaded environments within dense rainforests.
