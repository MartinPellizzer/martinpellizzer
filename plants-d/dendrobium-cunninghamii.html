Dendrobium cunninghamii

Dendrobium cunninghamii, commonly known as the purple-veined orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to Australia.

It features a tall, branched stem with numerous clustered flowers that bloom in spring. The flowers are typically white or pale pink with distinctive purple veins, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid conditions and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This species is valued in horticulture for its beauty and adaptability to various growing environments.
