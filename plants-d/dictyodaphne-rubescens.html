Dictyodaphne rubescens

Dictyodaphne rubescens, commonly known as the red-veined daphne, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its smooth, reddish-brown bark and its fragrant, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in clusters.
