Dysphania sooana

Dysphania sooana, commonly known as spinach beet, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, hairless stems and alternate, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters, and its seeds are typically dispersed by wind. Dysphania sooana is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
