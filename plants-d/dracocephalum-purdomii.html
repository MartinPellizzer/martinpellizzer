Dracocephalum purdomii

Dracocephalum purdomii, commonly known as Chinese skullcap, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats in mountainous regions.

Dracocephalum purdomii is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
