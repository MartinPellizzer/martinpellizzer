Diosma lediformis

Diosma lediformis, commonly known as the yellow bellvista, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades, and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are narrow and often have a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss.

Diosma lediformis is also noted for its medicinal properties, used traditionally in some regions for its potential health benefits.
