Dendrochilum graminifolium

Dendrochilum graminifolium, commonly known as the grass-leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pale pink. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing in forest understories. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its delicate appearance and unique floral structure.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in some regions.
