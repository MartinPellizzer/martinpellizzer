Dendrobium hookerianum

Dendrobium hookerianum, commonly known as the Hooker's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with multiple pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrobium hookerianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
