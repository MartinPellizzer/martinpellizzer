Diplusodon kielmeyeroides var. macrocalyx

Diplusodon kielmeyeroides var. macrocalyx, commonly known as the large-flowered kielmeyer.

It is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae, characterized by its showy, pink to red flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, well-drained soils. Its leaves are elliptical and glossy, with a fragrant aroma.
