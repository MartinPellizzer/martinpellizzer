Declieuxia revoluta

Declieuxia revoluta, commonly known as the silver feather plant, is a striking ornamental grass native to South Africa.

It features slender, arching stems that produce fluffy, silver-gray plumes, giving it a feathery appearance. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery bloom, enhancing its visual appeal. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Declieuxia revoluta is often used in landscaping for its elegant, airy foliage and seasonal display of soft, cloud-like flowers.
