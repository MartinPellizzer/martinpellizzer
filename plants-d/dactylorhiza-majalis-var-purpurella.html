Dactylorhiza majalis var. purpurella

Dactylorhiza majalis var. purpurella, commonly known as the purple orchid, is a species of orchid native to parts of Europe and northern Africa.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and heathlands. The plant produces tall, slender stems with several showy, purple or violet flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a rosette at the base of the plant.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
