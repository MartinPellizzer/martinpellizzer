Dactylis polygama var. glaucoviridis

Dactylis polygama var. glaucoviridis, commonly known as the glaucoviride grass, is a perennial grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its bluish-green foliage, which gives it a striking appearance in gardens and landscapes. The plant has narrow, upright leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bloom in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or dry garden settings.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and its ability to tolerate drought conditions.
