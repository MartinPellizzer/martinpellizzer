Daphne brasiliensis

Daphne brasiliensis, commonly known as the Brazilian daphne, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Thymelaeaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white, with a distinctive, waxy appearance. Its leaves are simple and opposite, with a glossy surface.

Daphne brasiliensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
