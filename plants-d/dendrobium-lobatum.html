Dendrobium lobatum

Dendrobium lobatum, commonly known as the crested orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with erect, somewhat branched stems that bear numerous lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often with a distinctive crested or ridged appearance on the petals. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrobium lobatum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and unique floral morphology.
