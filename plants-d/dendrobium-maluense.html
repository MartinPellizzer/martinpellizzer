Dendrobium maluense

Dendrobium maluense, commonly known as the Maluense orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its pseudobulbs and long, narrow leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect sunlight and well-draining soil.

Dendrobium maluense is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
