Dimorphotheca montana var. amoena

Dimorphotheca montana var. amoena, commonly known as the Cape marigold, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers and hairy, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, thriving in full sun. Its flowers bloom from spring to autumn, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and landscaping.
