Dianthus khamiesbergensis

Dianthus khamiesbergensis, commonly known as the Khamiesberg carnation, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in shades of pink and red. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves, and it produces clusters of small, five-petaled flowers.

Dianthus khamiesbergensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and resilience.
