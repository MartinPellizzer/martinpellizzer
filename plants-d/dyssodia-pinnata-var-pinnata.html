Dyssodia pinnata var. pinnata

Dyssodia pinnata var. pinnata, commonly known as the variable pinnate dyssodia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive pinnate leaf structure. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
