Desplatsia mildbraedii

Desplatsia mildbraedii, commonly known as the slender rock orchid, is a flowering plant native to the southern regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in late spring.
