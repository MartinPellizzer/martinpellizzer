Daphne × schlyteri

Daphne × schlyteri, commonly known as the Schlyteri daphne, is a hybrid shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. The plant features aromatic, glandular leaves that emit a strong, spicy scent. Its showy, tubular flowers, which range in color from white to pink, bloom in late winter to early spring.

Daphne × schlyteri is relatively hardy but requires well-drained soil and full sun to thrive.
