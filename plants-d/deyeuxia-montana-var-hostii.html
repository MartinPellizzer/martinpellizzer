Deyeuxia montana var. hostii

Deyeuxia montana var. hostii, commonly known as the mountain false poa, is a variant of the grass species Deyeuxia montana.

It is native to high-altitude regions of South America, particularly in the Andes. This plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it forms dense, low-growing tufts. Its botanical characteristics include narrow, grass-like leaves and small, spike-like inflorescences.

It is valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in harsh, cold climates.
