Dontospermum maritimum

Dontospermum maritimum, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean and the Canary Islands.

It is a perennial herb with striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit and thrives in well-drained, sandy soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in a fine, powdery coating.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate salty air and coastal conditions.
