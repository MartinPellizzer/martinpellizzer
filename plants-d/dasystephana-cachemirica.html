Dasystephana cachemirica

Dasystephana cachemirica, commonly known as the Kashmir starflower, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan regions of India and Pakistan.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky habitats at high altitudes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are slender and often branched.

Dasystephana cachemirica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
