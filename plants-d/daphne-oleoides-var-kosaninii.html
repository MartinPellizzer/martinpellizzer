Daphne oleoides var. kosaninii

Daphne oleoides var. kosaninii, commonly known as the Kosanin's spurge laurel, is a deciduous shrub native to the Caucasus region.

It features dense, aromatic foliage with a distinctive, waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in early spring, followed by orange-red berries in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for ornamental gardening.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a valuable addition to botanical collections.
