Dracocephalum henryi

Dracocephalum henryi, commonly known as Chinese skullcap, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features square stems and opposite leaves that are often hairy and have a distinct, aromatic scent. Its flowers are typically blue to purple and are clustered in whorls along the stems.

Dracocephalum henryi is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
