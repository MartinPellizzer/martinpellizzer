Dillenia mindanaensis

Dillenia mindanaensis, commonly known as Mindanao dillenia, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Dilleniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, palmately lobed leaves. The plant produces large, showy flowers with five petals, often in shades of white or pink. Its fruit is a fleshy, berry-like structure that contains multiple seeds.

Dillenia mindanaensis is typically found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental qualities.
