Dichorisandra sagittata

Dichorisandra sagittata, commonly known as the arrowleaf dichorisandra, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its arrow-shaped, glossy dark green leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Dichorisandra sagittata is popular in gardens for its elegant foliage and long-lasting blooms.
