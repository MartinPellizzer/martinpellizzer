Delissea virgata

Delissea virgata, commonly known as the silverleaf lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray leaves and showy white or pink flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. It is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to dry, coastal climates.

Delissea virgata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and blooms.
