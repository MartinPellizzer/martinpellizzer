Dendrobium moiorum

Dendrobium moiorum, commonly known as the giant phallic orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, branched stems and large, showy flowers that can reach up to 15 centimeters in length. The plant typically blooms in summer, producing clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green to orange flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its unique appearance and cultural significance, it is a popular choice for ornamental cultivation.
