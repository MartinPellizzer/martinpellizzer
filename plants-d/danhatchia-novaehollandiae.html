Danhatchia novaehollandiae

Danhatchia novaehollandiae, commonly known as the New Holland orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as rainforests or damp woodlands. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil efficiently.
