Daviesia juncea var. spinescens

Daviesia juncea var. spinescens, commonly known as the Spiny Daviesia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a shrub characterized by its woody stems and small, spiny leaves. The plant produces clusters of yellow or golden-yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
