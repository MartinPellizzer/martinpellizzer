Dendrobium stenocentrum

Dendrobium stenocentrum, commonly known as the narrow-angled dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with cane-like pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with prominent red sepals and petals. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for collectors and horticultural enthusiasts.
