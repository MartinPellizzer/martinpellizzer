Dendrobium kingianum var. aldersonae

Dendrobium kingianum var. aldersonae, commonly known as the Alderson's Dendrobium, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Australia.

This epiphytic orchid features a sympodial growth habit, with cane-like stems that can grow up to several meters in length. Its leaves are usually lanceolate and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often in clusters.

It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.
