Desmodium tiliifolium var. potaninii

Desmodium tiliifolium var. potaninii, commonly known as the silverleaf desmodium, is a perennial legume native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves with a silvery underside, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

This species is valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it useful in agricultural and ecological restoration settings.
