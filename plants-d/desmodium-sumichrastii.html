Desmodium sumichrastii

Desmodium sumichrastii, commonly known as the Texas desmodium, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

Desmodium sumichrastii is adapted to thrive in dry, well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as prairies and disturbed areas.
