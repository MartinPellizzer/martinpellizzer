Delosperma stenandrum

Delosperma stenandrum, commonly known as the narrowleaf ice plant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are adapted to store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, typically blooming in summer. It is a low-growing perennial that forms dense mats, making it an attractive ground cover.

Its botanical characteristics make it well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
