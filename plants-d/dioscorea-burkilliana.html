Dioscorea burkilliana

Dioscorea burkilliana, commonly known as Burkill's yam, is a species of tuberous vine native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its climbing habit and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, yellowish-white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its tuberous roots are valued for their medicinal properties and are used in traditional remedies.

However, the plant contains toxic compounds and requires careful handling during cultivation and harvesting.
