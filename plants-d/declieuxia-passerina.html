Declieuxia passerina

Declieuxia passerina, commonly known as the slender grass.

It is a perennial grass species native to South Africa, characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. Declieuxia passerina is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
