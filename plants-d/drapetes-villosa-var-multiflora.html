Drapetes villosa var. multiflora

Drapetes villosa var. multiflora, commonly known as the silky drapery, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and clusters of small, white or pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or rocky areas. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitat.
