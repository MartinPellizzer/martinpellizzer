Dactylorhiza × hochreutinerana

Dactylorhiza × hochreutinerana, commonly known as the Hochreutiner's orchid, is a hybrid orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and heathlands. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowering stems. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to cultivation.
