Dryopteris peranema

Dryopteris peranema, commonly known as the Japanese shield fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, compact fronds that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant features a rhizomatous growth habit, with fertile fronds producing spores on the underside. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
