Dracocephalum gobi

Dracocephalum gobi, commonly known as Gobi skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It thrives in sandy and rocky soils, often found in the Gobi Desert and surrounding areas. The plant has slender, branched stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are adapted to reduce water loss. Its flowers are typically blue to purple, arranged in dense, terminal clusters.

Dracocephalum gobi is valued for its medicinal properties, containing compounds used in traditional medicine.
