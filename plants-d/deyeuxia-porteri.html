Deyeuxia porteri

Deyeuxia porteri, commonly known as the African grass or Porteri grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, blade-like leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and wetlands. Deyeuxia porteri produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers, which are wind-pollinated.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
