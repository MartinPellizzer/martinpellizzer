Diospyros flexilis

Diospyros flexilis, commonly known as the flexible persimmon, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are often elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, followed by orange to reddish fruits that are edible when ripe.
