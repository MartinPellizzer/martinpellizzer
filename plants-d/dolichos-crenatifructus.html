Dolichos crenatifructus

Dolichos crenatifructus, commonly known as the warty bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It produces elongated, hairy pods that contain kidney-shaped seeds. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a warm climate for optimal growth.

The species is valued for its seeds, which are used in traditional cuisines and as a source of protein.
