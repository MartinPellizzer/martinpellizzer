Diomma

Diomma, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the genus Saintpaulia and is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, clustered flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Diomma is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

Its flowers bloom throughout the year under proper conditions, making it a favorite among indoor gardeners.
