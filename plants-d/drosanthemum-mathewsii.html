Drosanthemum mathewsii

Drosanthemum mathewsii, commonly known as the mountain ice plant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that store water, enabling it to survive in arid conditions. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among gardeners.
