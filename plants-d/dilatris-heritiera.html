Dilatris heritiera

Dilatris heritiera, commonly known as the African whitebeam, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and arranged in clusters, often appearing before the leaves.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
