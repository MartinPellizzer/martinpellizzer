Diplopappus diffusus

Diplopappus diffusus, commonly known as the slender burrweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in a head, followed by fluffy, wind-dispersed seeds. Its leaves are typically alternate, ovate, and covered with fine hairs.

Diplopappus diffusus is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of habitats.
