Dasycondylus resinosus

Dasycondylus resinosus, commonly known as the resinous shield fern, is a unique plant belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its leathery, dark green fronds that are covered in a waxy, resinous coating, which helps protect it from desiccation. The spores are produced in specialized structures called sori, which are located on the underside of the fronds.
