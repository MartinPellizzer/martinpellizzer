Declieuxia chiococcoides var. vincoides

Declieuxia chiococcoides var. vincoides, commonly known as the Venus's Looking-glass, is a species of flowering plant native to the wetlands of South America.

It is characterized by its unique, glass-like leaves that reflect light, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils and is often found in marshy areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in aquatic gardens and wetland restoration projects.
