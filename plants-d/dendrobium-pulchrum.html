Dendrobium pulchrum

Dendrobium pulchrum, commonly known as the beautiful orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with tall, cane-like stems that can reach several feet in height. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium pulchrum is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its long-lasting blooms.
