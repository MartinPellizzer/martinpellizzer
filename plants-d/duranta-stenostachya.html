Duranta stenostachya

Duranta stenostachya, commonly known as the narrow-stalked duranta, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height, characterized by its opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color, which bloom in summer. It is adapted to tropical and subtropical climates and prefers well-drained soil.

The fruit is a three-lobed capsule that contains small, yellowish seeds.
