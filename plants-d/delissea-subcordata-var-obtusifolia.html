Delissea subcordata var. obtusifolia

Delissea subcordata var. obtusifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved delissea, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features basal leaves that are subcordate at the base and have a blunt, rounded tip, giving it its scientific name. The plant produces small, white to pale blue flowers arranged in dense, terminal clusters. Its foliage is often covered in fine, soft hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
