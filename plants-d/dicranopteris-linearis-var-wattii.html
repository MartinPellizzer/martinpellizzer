Dicranopteris linearis var. wattii

Dicranopteris linearis var. wattii, commonly known as the Watt's water fern, is a variant of the water fern genus Dicranopteris.

It is a perennial aquatic fern characterized by its delicate, linear fronds that often float on the water's surface. The plant typically grows in shallow, still or slow-moving freshwater environments. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a smooth, waxy surface.

This species is noted for its ability to thrive in moist, shaded conditions and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
