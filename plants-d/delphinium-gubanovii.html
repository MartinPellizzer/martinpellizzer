Delphinium gubanovii

Delphinium gubanovii, commonly known as the Gubanovii larkspur, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height. Delphinium gubanovii is adapted to rocky and arid environments, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
