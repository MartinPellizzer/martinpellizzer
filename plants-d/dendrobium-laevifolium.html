Dendrobium laevifolium

Dendrobium laevifolium, commonly known as the flat-leaf dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features elongated, flat leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium laevifolium is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
