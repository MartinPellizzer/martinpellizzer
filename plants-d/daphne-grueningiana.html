Daphne grueningiana

Daphne grueningiana, commonly known as the Chinese daphne, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late winter to early spring. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that provide a striking contrast to its delicate blossoms. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Daphne grueningiana is also noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its winter interest.
