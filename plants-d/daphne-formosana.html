Daphne formosana

Daphne formosana, commonly known as the Taiwan daphne, is a shrub native to eastern Asia, particularly Taiwan.

It features fragrant, showy flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring, typically in shades of pink and white. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Daphne formosana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and historical significance in traditional medicine.
