Dictyopteris camerooniana

Dictyopteris camerooniana, commonly known as the Cameroon fan-leaf fern, is a species of marine algae belonging to the family Dictyopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that are typically arranged in a fan shape, giving it a distinctive appearance. This algae thrives in shallow coastal waters, particularly in tropical and subtropical regions. Its structure consists of a central stipe with branching, thin blades that are often covered in a layer of fine, hair-like structures.

Dictyopteris camerooniana plays a role in marine ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various aquatic organisms.
