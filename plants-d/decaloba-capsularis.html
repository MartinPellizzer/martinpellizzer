Decaloba capsularis

Decaloba capsularis, commonly known as the scarlet bower vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its vibrant red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for vertical gardening and ornamental landscaping.
