Dolicholus Capensis

Cape dolicholus, scientifically known as Dolicholus capensis, is a striking member of the Asteraceae family native to the coastal regions of South Africa. This plant is renowned for its elegant, daisy-like flowers that bloom in vibrant hues of yellow and orange, attracting a variety of pollinators. It thrives in sandy soils and full sunlight, often found along the rugged coastline where it withstands salty winds and coastal conditions. With its unique appearance and ecological importance, Dolicholus capensis is a symbol of the rich botanical diversity in the Cape region. Its resilience and beauty make it a favorite among botanists and gardeners alike.
