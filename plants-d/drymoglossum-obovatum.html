Drymoglossum obovatum

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit and possesses delicate, obovate-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. Due to its small size and specialized habitat, it is considered a rare and delicate species requiring specific environmental conditions for survival.
