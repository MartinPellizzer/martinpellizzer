Deyeuxia festucoides

Deyeuxia festucoides, commonly known as silver grass, is a perennial grass species native to temperate regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray leaves and delicate, fluffy seed heads that resemble plumes. Deyeuxia festucoides is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
