Dendrobium pseudostriatellum

Dendrobium pseudostriatellum, commonly known as the golden spike orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with cane-like stems that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with intricate patterns on the petals. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium pseudostriatellum is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its long-lasting blooms.
