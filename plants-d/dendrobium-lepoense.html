Dendrobium lepoense

Dendrobium lepoense, commonly known as the Lepoense orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Dendrobium genus, which includes over 1,200 species of orchids. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually fragrant and come in a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and pink.

It requires bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
