Dierama tysonii

Dierama tysonii, commonly known as the Tyson's dierama, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, upright stems that bear clusters of narrow, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, pinkish-white flowers in late winter to early spring, which are arranged in dense, cylindrical inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal gardens.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by horticultural enthusiasts.
