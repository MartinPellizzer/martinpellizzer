Dendrochilum furfuraceum

Dendrochilum furfuraceum, commonly known as the hairy-stemmed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces inflorescences bearing small, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinct lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and orchid enthusiasts.
