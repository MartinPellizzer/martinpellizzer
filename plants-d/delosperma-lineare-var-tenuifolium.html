Delosperma lineare var. tenuifolium

Delosperma lineare var. tenuifolium, commonly known as narrowleaf ice plant, is a low-growing, mat-forming succulent native to southern Africa.

It features small, fleshy, blue-green leaves that are narrow and often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of tiny, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, or white, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for arid landscapes.
