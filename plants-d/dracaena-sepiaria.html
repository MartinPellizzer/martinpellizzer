Dracaena sepiaria

Dracaena sepiaria, commonly known as the Black Dracaena, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features striking dark green, strap-like leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the base. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Dracaena sepiaria is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
