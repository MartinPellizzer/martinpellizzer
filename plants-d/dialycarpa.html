Dialycarpa

Dialycarpa, commonly known as the broadleaf burr.

It is a flowering plant species belonging to the family Polygonaceae, characterized by its distinctive, spiny fruits that enclose the seeds. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in various parts of Asia. Its leaves are broad and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The fruit, which is a type of achene, develops in clusters and is adapted for dispersal by animals.
