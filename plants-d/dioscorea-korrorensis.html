Dioscorea korrorensis

Dioscorea korrorensis, commonly known as the Korrorensis yam, is a climbing herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its twining stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by cylindrical, fleshy fruits containing numerous seeds. It is cultivated for its starchy tuberous roots, which are used as a food source in some regions.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
