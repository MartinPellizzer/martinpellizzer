Desmoscelis villosa var. hypericoides

Desmoscelis villosa var. hypericoides, commonly known as the hairy desmoscelis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and stream banks. The plant features slender, hairy stems and small, lance-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to white, with four petals and numerous stamens, typical of the Berberidaceae family.

It is valued in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties.
