Dryopteris balabacensis

Dryopteris balabacensis, commonly known as the Balabac shield fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its large, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a leathery texture.

Dryopteris balabacensis is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for landscaping purposes.
