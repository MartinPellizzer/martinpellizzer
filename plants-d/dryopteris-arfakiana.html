Dryopteris arfakiana

Dryopteris arfakiana, commonly known as the Arfak Mountain shield fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to the highlands of New Guinea.

It is characterized by its large, leathery, shield-shaped fronds that can reach up to 1 meter in length. The fronds are typically dark green and have a rough, textured surface. It thrives in cool, moist, shaded environments and is often found in montane forests.

This fern is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
