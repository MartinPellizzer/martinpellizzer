Digitaria balansae

Digitaria balansae, commonly known as the African carpet grass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spreading growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in a variety of soil types and is often found in disturbed areas, such as roadsides and fields.

Digitaria balansae is known for its adaptability and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
