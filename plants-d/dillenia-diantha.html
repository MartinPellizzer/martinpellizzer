Dillenia diantha

Dillenia diantha, commonly known as the golden dillenia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, palmately lobed leaves and showy, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces large, fleshy fruits that are typically eaten by various bird species. Dillenia diantha is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and along riverbanks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in warm climates.
