Diuris pauciflora

Diuris pauciflora, commonly known as the few-flowered spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in heathlands and open forests, thriving in well-drained sandy soils. The plant features a compact, hairy rhizome and produces a single or few slender, erect stems. Its flowers are small, greenish-yellow with a distinctive, elongated lip, and bloom during late spring to early summer.

Diuris pauciflora is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in shaded, humid environments.
