Delissea ramosa

Delissea ramosa, commonly known as the branching delissea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth habit with multiple branches. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Delissea ramosa is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
