Ditassa virgata

Ditassa virgata, commonly known as the silverleaf dittany, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often found in rocky or dry habitats. It is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and ecological studies.
