Derenbergia caulifera

Derenbergia caulifera, commonly known as the white-flowered caladium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that display vibrant colors such as red, purple, and green. The plant produces showy white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the warmer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to gardens and greenhouses.
