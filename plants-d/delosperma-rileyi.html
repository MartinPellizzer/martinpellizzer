Delosperma rileyi

Delosperma rileyi, commonly known as the Riley's ice plant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white powder, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, and purple, typically during the cooler months. It is well adapted to arid environments and thrives in full sun with minimal water.

Delosperma rileyi is often used in rock gardens and as a ground cover due to its hardy nature and ornamental appeal.
