Dracontium elatum

Dracontium elatum, commonly known as the giant arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces striking, bell-shaped flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. Its rhizomes are edible and have a starchy texture similar to taro.

The botanical features of Dracontium elatum make it both ornamental and useful in traditional cuisines.
