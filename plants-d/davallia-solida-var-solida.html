Davallia solida var. solida

Davallia solida var. solida, commonly known as the hard fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It features rhizomatous growth and produces delicate, lance-shaped fronds with a glossy, dark green appearance. The plant is characterized by its compact, clumping habit and is often found in shaded, moist environments. Its spores are produced in sori located on the undersides of the fronds.

This fern is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
