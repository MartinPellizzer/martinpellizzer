Diplusodon stachyoides

Diplusodon stachyoides, commonly known as the clustered spurge, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, characterized by its unique floral structures and latex-producing properties. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous shrub or perennial, often with opposite, simple leaves that are usually green and sometimes pubescent. Its inflorescence consists of cyathia, which are cup-like structures containing both male and female flowers, a common feature in the Euphorbiaceae family.
