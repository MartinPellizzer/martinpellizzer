Dracaena aethiopica

Dracaena aethiopica, commonly known as the Madagascar dragon tree, is a succulent plant native to Madagascar.

It features a tall, unbranched stem with smooth, waxy leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers followed by red, fleshy fruits. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water, making it drought-tolerant.

It thrives in well-draining soil and bright, indirect light.
