Drimia hyacinthoides

Drimia hyacinthoides, commonly known as the African lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features strap-like, grassy leaves that form a dense rosette near the ground. The plant produces tall, slender stems that bear clusters of fragrant, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or purple. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Drimia hyacinthoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticulture for its elegant appearance.
