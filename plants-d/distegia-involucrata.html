Distegia involucrata

Distegia involucrata, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, clustered flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, often forming a dense rosette of foliage. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and prefers indirect sunlight.

Distegia involucrata is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
