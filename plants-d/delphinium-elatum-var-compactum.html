Delphinium elatum var. compactum

Delphinium elatum var. compactum, commonly known as the compact larkspur, is a perennial flowering plant characterized by its tall, sturdy stems and showy, blue to purple flowers.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem, and its flowers are typically clustered in dense spikes.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden borders and rock gardens.
