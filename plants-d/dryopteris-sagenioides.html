Dryopteris sagenioides

Dryopteris sagenioides, commonly known as the Sagen's wood fern, is a species of fern native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its tall, upright fronds that can reach up to 1 meter in length. The fronds are bipinnate, with a distinctive, delicate appearance that features a light green color and a serrated margin. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in woodland areas with rich, well-drained soil.

This fern is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
