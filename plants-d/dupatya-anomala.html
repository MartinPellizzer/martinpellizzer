Dupatya anomala

Dupatya anomala, commonly known as the Philippine orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful blooms. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its flowers are often fragrant and can vary in color from white to pink and purple.

Dupatya anomala plays an important role in the biodiversity of its native habitat and is valued for its ornamental beauty.
