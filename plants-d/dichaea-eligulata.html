Dichaea eligulata

Dichaea eligulata, commonly known as the slender dichaea, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as under the canopy of rainforests. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and the flowers are borne on short, erect spikes.

Dichaea eligulata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
