Diplazium rhoifolium

Diplazium rhoifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf sword fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its robust, upright fronds that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The fronds are divided into pinnae with broad, lance-shaped segments that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
