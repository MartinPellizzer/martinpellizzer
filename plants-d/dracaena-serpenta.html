Dracaena serpenta

Dracaena serpenta, commonly known as the snake plant, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its long, narrow, sword-shaped leaves that grow in a spiral pattern, giving it a unique and striking appearance. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its foliage is typically a deep green, though some varieties may exhibit variegation with yellow or white streaks.

Dracaena serpenta is also valued for its air-purifying properties, as it can help improve indoor air quality.
