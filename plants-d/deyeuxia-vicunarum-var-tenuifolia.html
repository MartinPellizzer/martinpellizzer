Deyeuxia vicunarum var. tenuifolia

Deyeuxia vicunarum var. tenuifolia, commonly known as slender leaf false panicgrass, is a grass species native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and compact growth habit. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as wet meadows and marshes. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences with tiny florets.

Botanically, it is distinguished by its fine foliage and adaptability to wet environments.
