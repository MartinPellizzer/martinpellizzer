Delphinium consolida f. tricolor-elegans

Delphinium consolida f. tricolor-elegans, commonly known as the elegant tricolor larkspur, is a hybrid cultivar of the delphinium genus.

It is characterized by its striking, three-color flower petals that typically display a combination of white, blue, and purple. The plant is a herbaceous perennial with tall, spindly stems that bear clusters of showy, spurred flowers. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden borders and cutting gardens.
