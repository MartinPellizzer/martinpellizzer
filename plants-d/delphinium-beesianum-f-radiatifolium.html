Delphinium beesianum f. radiatifolium

Delphinium beesianum f. radiatifolium, commonly known as the radiate-leaved delphinium, is a hybrid cultivar of the delphinium genus.

It is characterized by its striking, showy blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is a perennial herb with a robust root system and tall, erect stems. Its foliage is distinctive, featuring broad, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged radiately around the stem.

This cultivar is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and is often used in mixed borders or as a focal point in perennial beds.
