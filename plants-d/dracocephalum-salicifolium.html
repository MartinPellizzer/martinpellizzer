Dracocephalum salicifolium

Dracocephalum salicifolium, commonly known as blueweed or slender blueweed, is a perennial herb native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia.

It features slender, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with blue or purple hues. The plant produces small, clustered blue or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed areas.

Its aromatic foliage and ornamental flowers make it a valued plant in horticulture and traditional medicine.
