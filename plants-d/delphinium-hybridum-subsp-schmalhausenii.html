Delphinium hybridum subsp. schmalhausenii

Delphinium hybridum subsp.

schmalhausenii, commonly known as the Schmalhausen delphinium, is a tall, herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It features striking spires of blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant color and upright growth habit.
