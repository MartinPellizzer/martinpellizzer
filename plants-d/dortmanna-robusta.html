Dortmanna robusta

Dortmanna robusta, commonly known as the robust moss, is a species of moss belonging to the family Dicranaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and dark green, wiry stems. The leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with a smooth, glossy surface and a distinct costa. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found on rocks or soil in temperate regions.

This moss is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in soil stabilization and moisture retention.
