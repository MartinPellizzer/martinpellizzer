Dendrotrophe buxifolia var. multinervis

Dendrotrophe buxifolia var. multinervis, commonly known as the variegated boxwood tree, is a rare and striking hybrid plant characterized by its unique foliage.

It exhibits a dense, compact growth habit with leaves that are typically dark green with white or yellow variegation. The leaves are often broader than those of its parent species, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Due to its rarity, it is often sought after by horticultural enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
