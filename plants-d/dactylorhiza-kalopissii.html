Dactylorhiza kalopissii

Dactylorhiza kalopissii, commonly known as the Kalopissi orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in calcareous soils and prefers shady, moist habitats. It has a rhizomatous root system and produces erect, leafy stems.

Dactylorhiza kalopissii is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
