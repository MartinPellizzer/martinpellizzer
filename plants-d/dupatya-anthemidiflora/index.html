Dupatya Anthemidiflora

Dupatya anthemidiflora is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. It is known for its small, star-shaped blossoms that bloom in shades of white and pale yellow. This species thrives in cool, humid environments and is often found in shaded forest understories. Due to its limited distribution and habitat loss, it is considered endangered by conservationists. Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it a subject of interest for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike.
