Dendrochilum pumilum

Dendrochilum pumilum, commonly known as the dwarf orchid, is a small epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It is known for its compact size and is often cultivated as a decorative plant.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is relatively rare in the wild.
