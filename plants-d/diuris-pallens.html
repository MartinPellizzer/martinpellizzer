Diuris pallens

Diuris pallens, commonly known as the pale diuris, is an orchid species native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, pale green to yellowish flowers that bloom during late winter to early spring. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils in heathlands and open forests. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often with a slight curl, and the flowers are usually arranged in a spike.

Diuris pallens is a rare and delicate species, requiring specific environmental conditions to thrive.
