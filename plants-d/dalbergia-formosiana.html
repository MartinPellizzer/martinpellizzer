Dalbergia formosiana

Dalbergia formosiana, commonly known as Formosan rosewood, is a tropical tree native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its dense, dark-colored wood that is highly valued for its beauty and durability. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by pod-like fruits.

It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland and mountainous forest areas.
