Dichondra parvifolia

Dichondra parvifolia, commonly known as small-leaf dichondra, is a low-growing perennial plant native to South America.

It features small, heart-shaped leaves that are typically opposite on the stems, giving it a dense, carpet-like appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ground cover in gardens.

Its rapid growth and ability to tolerate drought conditions make it a versatile ornamental plant.
