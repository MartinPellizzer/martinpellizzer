Digitaria perrottetii

Digitaria perrottetii, commonly known as Perrott's digitaria, is a grass species native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny florets. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Digitaria perrottetii is often found in disturbed soils and can be an indicator species for certain ecological conditions.
