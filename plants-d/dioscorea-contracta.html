Dioscorea contracta

Dioscorea contracta, commonly known as the narrowleaf yam, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Dioscoreaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas, particularly in Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its tuberous roots, which are the primary part used for propagation and traditional medicinal purposes. Its leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The flowers are small and clustered, often greenish or yellowish in color, and are usually borne in inflorescences.
