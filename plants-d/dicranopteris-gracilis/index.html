Dicranopteris Gracilis

Dicranopteris gracilis is a rare and ancient fern species that thrives in humid, shaded environments. It is part of the family Polypodiaceae and is known for its delicate, lace-like fronds. This plant is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, particularly in Asia and the Pacific. Its unique morphology and ecological significance make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Due to habitat loss, Dicranopteris gracilis is considered vulnerable, highlighting the need for its protection.
