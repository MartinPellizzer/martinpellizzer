Dolichos formosanus var. rhombifolius

Dolichos formosanus var. rhombifolius, commonly known as the rhombifolius dolichos, is a climbing legume native to Southeast Asia.

It features trifoliate leaves with rhomboid-shaped leaflets, giving it its varietal name. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, followed by elongated, bean-like pods. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Its botanical characteristics make it a subject of interest in both horticultural and ecological studies.
