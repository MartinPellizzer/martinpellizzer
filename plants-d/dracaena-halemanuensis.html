Dracaena halemanuensis

Dracaena halemanuensis, commonly known as the Halemanu Dracaena, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and long, strap-like leaves that are typically a deep green color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or white in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate to bright indirect light.

Dracaena halemanuensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to indoor environments.
