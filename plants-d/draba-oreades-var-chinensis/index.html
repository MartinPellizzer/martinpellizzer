Draba Oreades Var. Chinensis

Chinese rockcress, scientifically known as Draba oreades var. chinensis, is a resilient and hardy plant native to rocky and alpine environments. It thrives in harsh conditions, often growing in crevices and on steep slopes where few other plants can survive. This species is characterized by its small, delicate leaves and clusters of tiny white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Despite its modest appearance, it plays an important role in stabilizing soil and supporting local ecosystems. Its unique adaptations make it a fascinating subject for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike.
