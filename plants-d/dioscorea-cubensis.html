Dioscorea cubensis

Dioscorea cubensis, commonly known as the Cuban yam, is a tuberous plant belonging to the family Dioscoreaceae.

It is native to the Caribbean and parts of Central and South America. The plant produces large, starchy tubers that are rich in carbohydrates and have been traditionally used in local cuisines. Its growth habit involves a climbing vine with heart-shaped leaves and yellowish flowers.

Dioscorea cubensis is also valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
