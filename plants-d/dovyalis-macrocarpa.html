Dovyalis macrocarpa

Dovyalis macrocarpa, commonly known as the Natal plum, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Africa.

It produces small, yellowish-green fruits that are typically eaten raw or used in jams and preserves. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy habitats.

The flowers are small and pale green, appearing in clusters during the spring season.
