Dendranthema kurilense

Dendranthema kurilense, commonly known as the Kuril chamomile, is a flowering plant native to the Kuril Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features serrated, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers with a central disk. It thrives in moist, temperate climates and is often found in coastal areas.

Dendranthema kurilense is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine.
