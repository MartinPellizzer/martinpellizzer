Dombeya mollis var. loucoubensis

Dombeya mollis var. loucoubensis, commonly known as the Loucoubens dombeya, is a flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of West Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in terminal panicles. The plant has simple, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and a glossy surface. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, fertile soil.

Dombeya mollis var. loucoubensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in tropical gardens.
