Dianthus × courtoisii

Dianthus × courtoisii, commonly known as the hybrid carnation, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Dianthus barbatus (garden carnation) and Dianthus × gratianopolitanus (garden pinks). The plant is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that come in a range of colors including red, pink, and white. It typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and prefers well-drained soil in full sun.

Dianthus × courtoisii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
