Drimiopsis purpurea

Drimiopsis purpurea, commonly known as the purple drimiopsis, is a perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features strap-like, dark green leaves that are often tinged with purple at the base. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of purple and pink, typically blooming in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Drimiopsis purpurea is relatively low maintenance and is popular among indoor gardeners for its ornamental value.
