Deutzia × elegantissima

Deutzia × elegantissima, commonly known as the snowball bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its clusters of white, fluffy flowers that appear in early spring, giving it a striking, snowball-like appearance. The plant has arching branches and small, oval-shaped leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Deutzia × elegantissima is also valued for its ornamental appeal and moderate growth habit.
