Distasis modesta

Distasis modesta, commonly known as the modest spurge, is a flowering plant belonging to the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are typically oval or lance-shaped. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually greenish-yellow in color. Distasis modesta is native to parts of Africa and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions.

It has a milky sap that can be toxic, making it important to handle with care.
