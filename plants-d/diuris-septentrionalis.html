Diuris septentrionalis

Diuris septentrionalis, commonly known as the Northern Spur Orchid, is an Australian native plant belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spurred flowers that are typically pale green to yellow in color. The plant is perennial and grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in shaded or semi-shaded environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces a single, erect flowering stem.

Diuris septentrionalis is adapted to coastal and inland regions of Western Australia, where it thrives in well-drained conditions.
