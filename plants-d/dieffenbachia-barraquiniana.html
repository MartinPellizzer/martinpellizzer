Dieffenbachia barraquiniana

Dieffenbachia barraquiniana, commonly known as the Barraquiniana Dieffenbachia, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically a mix of green and white or cream in color. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other popular houseplants like peace lilies. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to prevent root rot.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value.
