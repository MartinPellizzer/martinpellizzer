Dissotis grandiflora var. grandiflora

Dissotis grandiflora var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered dissotis, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or red, with a distinctive spurred petal. The plant has a short, upright stem and lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for tropical gardens and greenhouse cultivation.
