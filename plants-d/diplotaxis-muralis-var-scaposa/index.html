Diplotaxis Muralis Var. Scaposa

Diplotaxis muralis var. scaposa is a strikingly unique plant known for its vibrant yellow flowers and distinctive leaf patterns. Native to arid regions of Africa, it thrives in harsh environments with minimal water. Its slender stems and clustered blooms make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping. Despite its beauty, it is often overlooked due to its delicate appearance and limited cultivation. This plant offers both ornamental value and ecological benefits, making it a fascinating subject for botanical enthusiasts.
