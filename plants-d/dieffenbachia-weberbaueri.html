Dieffenbachia weberbaueri

Dieffenbachia weberbaueri, commonly known as the Weberbauer's Dieffenbachia, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves with striking white and green variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous green flowers that are typically hidden within a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Dieffenbachia weberbaueri is a popular ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
