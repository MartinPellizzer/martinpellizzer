Delphinium pilosulum

Delphinium pilosulum, commonly known as the silky larkspur, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant produces showy, blue to purple flowers in raceme inflorescences, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lanceolate, with a hairy texture that gives it its common name.

Delphinium pilosulum is part of the Ranunculaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
