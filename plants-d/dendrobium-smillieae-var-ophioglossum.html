Dendrobium smillieae var. ophioglossum

Dendrobium smillieae var. ophioglossum, commonly known as the Ophioglossum Dendrobium, is a rare and unique orchid species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit, with cane-like stems that produce leaves and flowers sequentially. The plant typically features long, narrow leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires careful cultivation due to its specific environmental needs.

This variety is highly valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and botanical interest.
