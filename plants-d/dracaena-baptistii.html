Dracaena baptistii

Dracaena baptistii, commonly known as the Madagascar dragon tree, is a striking succulent native to the island of Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, unbranched stem and dense, feather-like foliage that gives it a dramatic appearance. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers followed by red, fleshy fruits that contain seeds. Its unique appearance and ability to thrive in low-light conditions make it a popular houseplant.

Dracaena baptistii is also valued for its air-purifying qualities and resilience to indoor environments.
