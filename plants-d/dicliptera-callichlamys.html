Dicliptera callichlamys

Dicliptera callichlamys, commonly known as the golden panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are typically yellow or golden in color, giving it its common name. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.
