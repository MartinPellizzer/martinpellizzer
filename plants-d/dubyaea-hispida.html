Dubyaea hispida

Dubyaea hispida, commonly known as the rough dubyaea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is adapted to arid conditions and requires minimal water once established.

Dubyaea hispida is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging environments.
