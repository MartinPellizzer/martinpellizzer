Dolichos acinaciformis

Dolichos acinaciformis, commonly known as the sword bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, sword-shaped pods that are typically green when immature and turn brown upon maturity. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers, which are followed by the characteristic elongated pods. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

Dolichos acinaciformis is valued for its edible seeds and is often cultivated for both culinary and ornamental purposes.
