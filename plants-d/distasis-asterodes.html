Distasis asterodes

Distasis asterodes, commonly known as the starry aster.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, daisy-like blooms with white petals and a yellow central disk. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands.

Distasis asterodes is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
