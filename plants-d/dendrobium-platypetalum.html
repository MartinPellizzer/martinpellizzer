Dendrobium platypetalum

Dendrobium platypetalum, commonly known as the flat-petaled orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome that produces multiple pseudobulbs. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with a distinctive flat petal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrobium platypetalum is appreciated for its ornamental value and is commonly cultivated in tropical gardens.
