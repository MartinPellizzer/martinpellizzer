Doronicum matthioli

Doronicum matthioli, commonly known as the Alpine goldcup, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

Doronicum matthioli is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant spring blooms.
