Disporum smilacinum var. rotundatum

Disporum smilacinum var. rotundatum, commonly known as the rounded disporum, is a variant of the disporum species native to the western United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial with distinctive, waxy, strap-like leaves that are often rounded at the base. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters during the spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a unique addition to woodland gardens and conservation efforts.
