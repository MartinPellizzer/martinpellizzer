Dracoglossum sinuatum

Dracoglossum sinuatum, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, zigzag-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming during the warmer months. Dracoglossum sinuatum thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
