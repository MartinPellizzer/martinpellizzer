Dichaea obovatipetala

Dichaea obovatipetala, commonly known as the broad-leafed dichaea, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white pubescence. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pale green to yellow in color, arranged in a spike-like inflorescence. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
