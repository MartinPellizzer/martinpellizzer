Dumreichera arabica

Dumreichera arabica, commonly known as the Arabian coffee plant, is a species of flowering plant native to the Arabian Peninsula.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant produces small, white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy fruits containing seeds. Its leaves are thick and leathery, adapted to conserve water in its native habitat.

Dumreichera arabica is closely related to the genus Coffea, which includes other coffee species.
