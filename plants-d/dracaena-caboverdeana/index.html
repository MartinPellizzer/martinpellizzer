Dracaena Caboverdeana

Dracaena caboverdeana is a rare and striking plant native to the Cape Verde Islands. Known for its unique, sword-like leaves that display a vibrant mix of green and red, it captivates plant enthusiasts worldwide. This species thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish. Its ability to purify indoor air makes it a popular choice for homes and offices. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively obscure gem in the world of houseplants.
