Diplotaxis assurgens var. scabriuscula

Diplotaxis assurgens var. scabriuscula, commonly known as the roughleaf wallaby tree, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions. The plant has a woody stem with rough, scaly bark and produces yellow flowers in clusters. Its leaves are small, elliptical, and covered in a fine, gritty texture.

It is well adapted to dry conditions and is often used in landscaping for its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
