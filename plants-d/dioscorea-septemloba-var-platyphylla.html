Dioscorea septemloba var. platyphylla

Dioscorea septemloba var. platyphylla, commonly known as the broadleaf yam, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Dioscoreaceae family and is characterized by its tuberous roots, which are often used in traditional medicine. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous vine with compound leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers.

Its tubers are valued for their nutritional content and have been utilized in various cultural and medicinal practices.
