Declieuxia glauca

Declieuxia glauca, commonly known as the silver grass, is a perennial grass species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-blue foliage and tufted inflorescence that resembles a plume. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping and rock gardens. Its ornamental value comes from its delicate appearance and ability to add texture to garden landscapes.

Declieuxia glauca is also valued for its low maintenance requirements and drought tolerance.
