Dryopteris affinis var. robusta

Dryopteris affinis var. robusta, commonly known as the giant shield fern, is a variant of the common wood fern.

It is characterized by its large, robust fronds that can grow up to 1.5 meters in length. The plant features a thick, sturdy rhizome that supports its extensive foliage. Its leaves are typically bipinnate, with a distinctive shield-shaped base.

This fern thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
