Dialium mossambicense

Dialium mossambicense, commonly known as the African plum or African cherry, is a tropical tree native to Central and Southern Africa.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant produces edible fruits that are often used in traditional medicine and cuisine. Its bark and leaves contain compounds with potential medicinal properties.
