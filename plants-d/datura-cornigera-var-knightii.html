Datura cornigera var. knightii

Datura cornigera var. knightii, commonly known as the Knight's angel's trumpet, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its large, showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale purple in color. The plant produces spiny capsules containing numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in arid to semi-arid environments and is often found in rocky soils.
