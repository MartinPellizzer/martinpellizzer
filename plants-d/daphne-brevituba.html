Daphne brevituba

Daphne brevituba, commonly known as the narrow tube daphne, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features small, fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in early spring. The plant has dark green, lance-shaped leaves that provide contrast to its delicate blossoms. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Daphne brevituba is also noted for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
