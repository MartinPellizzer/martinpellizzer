Dehaasia suborbicularis

Dehaasia suborbicularis, commonly known as the orbicular dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of broadly rounded, hairy leaves. The plant produces small, yellow composite flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its fruit is a pappus-equipped achene, typical of the Asteraceae family.

The species is often found in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils.
