Dysosmia polyadena

Dysosmia polyadena, commonly known as the fragrant bellflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Dysosmia polyadena is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
