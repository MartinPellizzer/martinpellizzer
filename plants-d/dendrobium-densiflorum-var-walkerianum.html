Dendrobium densiflorum var. walkerianum

Dendrobium densiflorum var. walkerianum, commonly known as the Walker's dense-flower orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with multiple upright pseudobulbs covered in a dense layer of pale green to brownish-gray foliage. The plant produces numerous fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters along the stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining potting media.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its showy, long-lasting blooms.
