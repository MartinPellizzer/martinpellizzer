Dracaena grandis

Dracaena grandis, commonly known as the dragon tree, is a tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, strap-like leaves that can grow up to 2 meters in length. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by red, berry-like fruits. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Dracaena grandis is also valued as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
