Deutzia subsessilis

Deutzia subsessilis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features slender, arching stems and small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in early summer. The leaves are elliptical, with a smooth margin and a narrow base. It prefers partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of care in temperate climates.
