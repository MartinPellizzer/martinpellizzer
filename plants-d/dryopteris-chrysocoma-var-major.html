Dryopteris chrysocoma var. major

Dryopteris chrysocoma var. major, commonly known as the golden fern, is a variant of the golden wood fern.

It is a deciduous fern native to the temperate regions of the northern hemisphere. The plant features fronds that are typically bipinnate, with a bright golden hue on the underside. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
