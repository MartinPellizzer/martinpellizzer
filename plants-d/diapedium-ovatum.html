Diapedium ovatum

Diapedium ovatum, commonly known as the white hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and arranged oppositely on the stems. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Diapedium ovatum is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
