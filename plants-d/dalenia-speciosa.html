Dalenia speciosa

Dalenia speciosa, commonly known as the showy dalenia, is a striking plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Araliaceae and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are palmately compound, with five to seven leaflets that are often glossy and dark green.

Dalenia speciosa thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.
