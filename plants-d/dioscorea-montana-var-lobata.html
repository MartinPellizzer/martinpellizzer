Dioscorea montana var. lobata

Dioscorea montana var. lobata, commonly known as the lobate yam, is a tropical vine belonging to the Dioscoreaceae family.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and large, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces yellowish-white flowers that are typically clustered in branched inflorescences. Its tubers, which are the primary economic product, are edible when properly prepared.

This variety is native to parts of Africa and is cultivated for both its nutritional value and traditional medicinal uses.
