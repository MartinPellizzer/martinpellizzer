Dendropemon psilobotrys

Dendropemon psilobotrys, commonly known as the slender-stemmed orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stem and small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its leaves are elongated and strap-like, contributing to its elegant appearance.

Dendropemon psilobotrys is often cultivated for its ornamental value in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
