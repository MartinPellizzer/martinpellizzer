Dipladenia illustris

Dipladenia illustris, commonly known as the showy nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a perennial vine that produces vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture and thrives in warm, sunny conditions. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking blooms and relatively easy care requirements.

Dipladenia illustris is also known for its ability to climb and trail, making it suitable for trellises or hanging baskets.
