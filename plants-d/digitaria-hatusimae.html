Digitaria hatusimae

Digitaria hatusimae, commonly known as the Hatusima grass, is a species of grass native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny florets. It thrives in tropical and subtropical regions, often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

Digitaria hatusimae is typically drought-tolerant and has a moderate growth rate.
