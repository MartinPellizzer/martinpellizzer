Demorchis crassisepala

Demorchis crassisepala, commonly known as the broad-sepaled orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and striking, showy flowers with prominent sepals. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinctive, fragrant scent.

Demorchis crassisepala is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
