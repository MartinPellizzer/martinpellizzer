Dracula carderiopsis

Dracula carderiopsis, commonly known as the Crimson Dragon Orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its vibrant red petals and intricate, almost prehistoric-looking floral structure. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its unique, spurred labellum that resembles a dragon's tongue. It thrives in high humidity and cool temperatures, often growing on tree trunks or rocky surfaces.

Due to its rarity and beauty, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
