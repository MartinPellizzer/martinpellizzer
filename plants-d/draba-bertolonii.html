Draba bertolonii

Draba bertolonii, commonly known as the Bertoloni's whitlow grass, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in well-drained soils. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and early summer. Its leaves are basal, forming a rosette, and are covered in a fine layer of hairs.
