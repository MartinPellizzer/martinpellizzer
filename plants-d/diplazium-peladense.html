Diplazium peladense

Diplazium peladense, commonly known as the broadleaf holly fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its large, leathery fronds with a distinctive, broad shape. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are thick and creeping, allowing it to spread vegetatively.
