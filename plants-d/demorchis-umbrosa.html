Demorchis umbrosa

Demorchis umbrosa, commonly known as the shadow fern, is a rare and delicate fern species native to the understory of shaded forests in Southeast Asia.

It thrives in low-light conditions, making it well adapted to environments with limited sunlight. The plant features finely divided, arching fronds that give it a graceful appearance. Its reproductive structures are small and inconspicuous, typical of many ferns.

Due to habitat loss, Demorchis umbrosa is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
