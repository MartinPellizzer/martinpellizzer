Dasyglossum prasinum

Dasyglossum prasinum, commonly known as the green orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and elongated, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in a spike, giving it its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Dasyglossum prasinum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
