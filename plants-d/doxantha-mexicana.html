Doxantha mexicana

Doxantha mexicana, commonly known as Mexican doxantha, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. Doxantha mexicana is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display and adaptability to various soil conditions.
