Dunbaria fusca

Dunbaria fusca, commonly known as the slender rock cress, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in high-altitude environments. The plant features a low-growing habit with basal leaves that are elongated and covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, yellowish, and arranged in racemes, producing a subtle ornamental effect.

Dunbaria fusca is often found in alpine meadows and is adapted to cold, dry climates.
