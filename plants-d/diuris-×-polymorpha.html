Diuris × polymorpha

Diuris × polymorpha, commonly known as the hybrid spider orchid, is a fascinating plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, often colorful, spider-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as rainforests or wetlands. It exhibits a variety of morphological forms due to its hybrid origin, making it a popular subject in horticulture.

Its blooming period usually occurs in spring, adding a striking visual element to its natural habitat.
