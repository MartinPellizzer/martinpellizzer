Diosma uitenhagensis

Diosma uitenhagensis, commonly known as the Uitenhagens' diosma, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is characterized by its upright stems and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant produces small, oval-shaped fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in coastal and mountainous regions, where it thrives in well-drained environments.

Diosma uitenhagensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
