Dendrobium garayanum

Dendrobium garayanum, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrobium garayanum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
