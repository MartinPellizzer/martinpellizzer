Dendrolobium rugosum

Dendrolobium rugosum, commonly known as the rough wattle, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It has a woody stem and produces pods that contain seeds.

Dendrolobium rugosum is often found in shrublands and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
