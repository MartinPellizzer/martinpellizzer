Dombeya calantha

Dombeya calantha, commonly known as the white dombeya, is a deciduous shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are typically ovate or elliptical in shape. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Dombeya calantha is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
