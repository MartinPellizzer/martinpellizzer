Dicrastylis kumarinensis

Dicrastylis kumarinensis, commonly known as the Kumarinensis False Orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae, which includes a wide variety of orchids. The plant is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage and small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
