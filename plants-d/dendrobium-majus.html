Dendrobium majus

Dendrobium majus, commonly known as the giant orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its long, cane-like stems that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink, often in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and well-draining soil.

Dendrobium majus is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
