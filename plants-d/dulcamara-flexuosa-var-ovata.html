Dulcamara flexuosa var. ovata

Dulcamara flexuosa var. ovata, commonly known as the rounded bindweed, is a variant of the common bindweed species.

It is a climbing perennial vine native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant features heart-shaped, ovate leaves and produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers. Its stems are often covered in fine hairs and can twine around other plants or structures.

It is known for its aggressive growth habit and is considered a weed in many regions.
