Diarina festucoides

Diarina festucoides, commonly known as the narrowleaf diarina, is a perennial grass-like plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. The plant has slender, tufted leaves that are often grass-like in appearance, with a bluish-green color. Its inflorescence consists of a dense, spike-like cluster of small, spikelet-based flowers.

Diarina festucoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native plant gardens.
