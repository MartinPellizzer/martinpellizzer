Draco angustifolia

Draco angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf dragon tree, is a species of flowering plant in the family Meliaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and Malaysia. Draco angustifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
