Dubautia laxa subsp. laxa

Dubautia laxa subsp.

laxa, commonly known as the Hawaiian rock daisy, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, cushion-like growth habit.
