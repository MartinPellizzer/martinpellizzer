Dendrochilum papillitepalum

Dendrochilum papillitepalum, commonly known as the papillate lip orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its intricate, papillate (bumpy) lip, which is a distinctive botanical feature. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with a variety of colors including yellow, green, and white.

The genus Dendrochilum is known for its diverse morphological adaptations, making Dendrochilum papillitepalum a notable example within the orchid family.
