Disa breyeri

Disa breyeri, commonly known as the violet orchid, is a striking member of the orchid family native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss. Disa breyeri thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Its unique appearance and vibrant color make it a popular choice for both garden cultivation and botanical study.
