Delphinium halteratum f. rifanum

Delphinium halteratum f. rifanum, commonly known as the Rif delphinium, is a botanical variant native to the mountainous regions of North Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its foliage consists of lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, soft down.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil and full sun.
