Dicksonia karsteniana

Dicksonia karsteniana, commonly known as the Karsten's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of large, feathery fronds. The plant has a robust rhizome that supports its tall structure, and its fronds are typically dark green with a glossy appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
