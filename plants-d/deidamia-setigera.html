Deidamia setigera

Deidamia setigera, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bellflower, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

Deidamia setigera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
