Delphinium nuttallii

Delphinium nuttallii, commonly known as Nuttall's larkspur, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of blue to purple flowers, which are typically arranged in dense spikes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a rough, hairy texture.

Delphinium nuttallii is adapted to grow in dry, rocky soils and is often found in mountainous regions.
