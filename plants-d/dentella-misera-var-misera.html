Dentella misera var. misera

Dentella misera var. misera, commonly known as the slender dentella, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has a low-growing habit with narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded, humid environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and specialty nurseries.
