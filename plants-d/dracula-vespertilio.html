Dracula vespertilio

Dracula vespertilio, commonly known as the vampire bat orchid, is a rare and exotic orchid species native to the high-altitude cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its striking, dark purple and black flowers that resemble the appearance of a vampire bat, hence its common name. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its unique, intricate floral structure. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires specific conditions to bloom, making it a challenging species to cultivate.

Due to its rarity and beauty, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
