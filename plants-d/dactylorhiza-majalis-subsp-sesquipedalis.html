Dactylorhiza majalis subsp. sesquipedalis

Dactylorhiza majalis subsp.

sesquipedalis, commonly known as the long-leaf marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Europe and North Africa. It typically grows in wet, calcareous habitats such as marshes and fens.

The plant features a slender, upright stem with several narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are pale green to yellowish, with a distinct three-lobed lip.

It is a perennial species that blooms from late spring to early summer.
