Dombeya rottleroides

Dombeya rottleroides, commonly known as the rose of Sharon, is a deciduous shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and violet, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has soft, hairy leaves that are oval to lance-shaped, and it produces small, red berries in the autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Dombeya rottleroides is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
