Diplospora lasiantha

Diplospora lasiantha, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is native to parts of Africa and is characterized by its small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant has hairy, ovate leaves that are often covered in a silvery bloom, giving it its common name. It produces berries that are typically green when immature and turn red when ripe.

The species is notable for its adaptability to various soil types and its role in local ecosystems.
