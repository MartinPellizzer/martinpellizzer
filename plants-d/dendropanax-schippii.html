Dendropanax schippii

Dendropanax schippii, commonly known as Schippi's tree-fern, is a species of tree-fern native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in temperate rainforests. Its fronds are composed of numerous pinnae, which are arranged in a distinctive, overlapping pattern.

Dendropanax schippii plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
