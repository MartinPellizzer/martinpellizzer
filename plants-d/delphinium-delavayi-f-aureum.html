Delphinium delavayi f. aureum

Delphinium delavayi f. aureum, commonly known as the golden delphinium, is a hybrid cultivar of the delphinium genus.

It is characterized by its striking, golden-yellow flowers that contrast beautifully with its deep green foliage. The plant is a herbaceous perennial, typically growing to a height of 1 to 2 meters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Botanically, it belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
