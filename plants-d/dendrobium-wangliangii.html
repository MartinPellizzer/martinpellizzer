Dendrobium wangliangii

Dendrobium wangliangii, commonly known as the Wangliang orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to China.

It belongs to the Dendrobium genus, which is part of the Orchidaceae family. This plant is characterized by its tall, cane-like stems and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Dendrobium wangliangii is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its beauty and fragrance.
