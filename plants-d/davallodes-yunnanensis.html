Davallodes yunnanensis

Davallodes yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan davallodes, is a climbing vine native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Leguminosae family and is characterized by its woody stems and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that are clustered in racemes, followed by pods containing several seeds.

It is often found in moist, shaded forest environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitat.
