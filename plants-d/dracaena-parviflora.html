Dracaena parviflora

Dracaena parviflora, commonly known as the little flame plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Madagascar.

It features narrow, upright leaves that are typically bright green with red or orange margins, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Dracaena parviflora is relatively low maintenance and can be propagated through stem cuttings, enhancing its appeal for plant enthusiasts.
