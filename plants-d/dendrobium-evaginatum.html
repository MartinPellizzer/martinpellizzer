Dendrobium evaginatum

Dendrobium evaginatum, commonly known as the orchid with a hooded lip, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit, with multiple pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and yellow, often with a distinctive hooded lip. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem.

Dendrobium evaginatum thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.
