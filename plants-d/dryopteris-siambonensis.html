Dryopteris siambonensis

Dryopteris siambonensis, commonly known as the Siamese wood fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its evergreen fronds that remain on the plant throughout the year. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its fronds are usually bipinnate, with a delicate, lace-like appearance.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in partial shade.
