Deutzia × myriantha

Deutzia × myriantha, commonly known as the snowball bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features clusters of fragrant, white flowers that bloom in early spring, creating a striking display. The plant has oval, dark green leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a versatile addition to gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms.
