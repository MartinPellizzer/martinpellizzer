Diospyros ferrea var. lamponga

Diospyros ferrea var. lamponga, commonly known as the Brazilian persimmon, is a tropical evergreen tree native to South America.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its dark, glossy leaves and small, clustered yellow-orange fruits.
