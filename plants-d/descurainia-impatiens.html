Descurainia impatiens

Descurainia impatiens, commonly known as yellow rocket, is an annual flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, often appearing before the leaves are fully developed. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, while the stems are slender and often branching.

Yellow rocket is often found in disturbed soils and can be invasive in certain regions.
