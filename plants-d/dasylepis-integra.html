Dasylepis integra

Dasylepis integra, commonly known as the African daisies or silver feather plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-forming growth habit and silver-blue foliage with soft, fuzzy textures. The plant produces showy, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal, Dasylepis integra is widely cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
