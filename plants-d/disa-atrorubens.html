Disa atrorubens

Disa atrorubens, commonly known as the red-stemmed disa, is a member of the Orchidaceae family native to southern Africa.

It is a terrestrial herb with a distinctive red or purple stem and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, showy flowers that are typically pink to purple in color, with a spurred lip. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or rocky areas.

Disa atrorubens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
