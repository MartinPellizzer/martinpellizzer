Dyschoriste verticillaris

Dyschoriste verticillaris, commonly known as the clustered dyschoriste, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, grassy areas and is characterized by its whorled arrangement of leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers clustered in dense inflorescences. It has a fibrous root system and is often found in sandy or loamy soils.

Dyschoriste verticillaris is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
