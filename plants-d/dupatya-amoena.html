Dupatya amoena

Dupatya amoena, commonly known as the velvet tamarind, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, elongated fruits that contain several seeds. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Dupatya amoena is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
