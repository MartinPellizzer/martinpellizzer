Dysosmia gossypiifolia

Dysosmia gossypiifolia, commonly known as the woolly nightshade, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and stems, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. Its fruit is a capsule that contains numerous tiny seeds.

Dysosmia gossypiifolia is often found in rocky or disturbed soils and is adapted to arid environments.
