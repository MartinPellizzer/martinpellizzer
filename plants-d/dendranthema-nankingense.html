Dendranthema nankingense

Dendranthema nankingense, commonly known as the Chinese chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that come in a variety of colors. Dendranthema nankingense is also valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its medicinal properties.
