Dracaena berkeleyi

Dracaena berkeleyi, commonly known as the Berkeley dracaena, is a species of flowering plant in the asparagus family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. This plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, linear leaves that are often a deep green color. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color.

Dracaena berkeleyi is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
