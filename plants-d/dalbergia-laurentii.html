Dalbergia laurentii

Dalbergia laurentii, commonly known as African rosewood, is a tropical tree native to Central and Southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dark, dense wood that is highly valued for its aesthetic and acoustic properties. The plant belongs to the Fabaceae family and features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. Its flowers are typically small and clustered in inflorescences, often with a pink or purple hue.

The tree produces small, round pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.
