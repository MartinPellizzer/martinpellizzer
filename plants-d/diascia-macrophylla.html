Diascia macrophylla

Diascia macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaved diascia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant has heart-shaped, broad leaves that are often covered in a fine, fuzzy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Diascia macrophylla is known for its long blooming period, typically from spring to early autumn.
