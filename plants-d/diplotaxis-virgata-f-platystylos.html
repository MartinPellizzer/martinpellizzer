Diplotaxis virgata f. platystylos

Diplotaxis virgata f. platystylos, commonly known as the broad-styled yellow burr.

It is an annual herbaceous plant native to southern Africa, characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems. The plant produces small, spherical fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in disturbed soils.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and traditional medicine.
