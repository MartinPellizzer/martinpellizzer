Dendrobium speciosum var. bancroftianum

Dendrobium speciosum var. bancroftianum, commonly known as the Banksian Dendrobium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on the surfaces of trees and rocks without deriving nutrients from them. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in warm, humid climates with good air circulation and partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
