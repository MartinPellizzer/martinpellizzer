Dracaena Sordida

Crisp white dracaena, scientifically known as Dracaena sordida, is a striking indoor plant with a unique appearance that captures attention. Its slender, pale green leaves emit a soft, almost ethereal glow under indirect light. Native to tropical regions, this plant thrives in warm, humid environments with minimal maintenance. Despite its name, it is not overly demanding, making it ideal for beginners and seasoned plant lovers alike. With its elegant form and low care requirements, the crisp white dracaena is a popular choice for adding a touch of sophistication to any space.
