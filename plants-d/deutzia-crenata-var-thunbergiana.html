Deutzia crenata var. thunbergiana

Deutzia crenata var. thunbergiana, commonly known as Thunberg's deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It features clusters of delicate, white, lacecap flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, arching stems and small, ovate leaves with toothed margins. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its seasonal foliage color changes and compact growth habit.
