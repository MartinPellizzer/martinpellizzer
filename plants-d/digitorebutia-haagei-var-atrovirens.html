Digitorebutia haagei var. atrovirens

Digitorebutia haagei var. atrovirens, commonly known as the dark-green stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are dark green in color, often with a bluish or purplish hue. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it an attractive and low-maintenance addition to gardens.
