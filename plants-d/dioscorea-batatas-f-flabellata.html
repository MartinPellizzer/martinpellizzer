Dioscorea batatas f. flabellata

Dioscorea batatas f. flabellata, commonly known as the flabellate yam, is a variant of the batatas yam native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its broad, flabellate (fan-shaped) leaves, which distinguish it from other yam species. The plant produces tuberous roots that are often used as food in various cuisines. It thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both agricultural and horticultural contexts.
