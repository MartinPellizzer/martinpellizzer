Dryopteris hupehensis

Dryopteris hupehensis, commonly known as the Hubei wood fern, is a species of fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its lanceolate, serrated fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky slopes. Its rhizomes are often covered in a dense layer of brown, velvety hair.

The Hubei wood fern is valued for its ornamental foliage and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
