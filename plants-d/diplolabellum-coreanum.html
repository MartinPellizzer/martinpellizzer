Diplolabellum coreanum

Diplolabellum coreanum, commonly known as the Korean double-labellum orchid, is a rare and beautiful flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Korea.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique floral structure with two distinct labella, or petals, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and has a delicate appearance with intricate, often fragrant blooms.

It is highly valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and is considered a symbol of resilience and elegance in Korean culture.
