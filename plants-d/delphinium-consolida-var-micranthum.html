Delphinium consolida var. micranthum

Delphinium consolida var. micranthum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved larkspur, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features tall, spindly stems that bear clusters of blue to purple flowers, which are typically spurred at the base. The leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is noted for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and historical significance.
