Doodia caudata var. typica

Doodia caudata var. typica, commonly known as the feather fern, is a species of fern native to eastern Asia.

It features delicate, feathery fronds that are typically green and glossy, with a distinctive, elongated shape. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in woodland areas or along stream banks. Its rhizomes are creeping and help in the propagation of the plant.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
