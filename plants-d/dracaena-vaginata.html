Dracaena vaginata

Dracaena vaginata, commonly known as the red-tipped dracaena, is a striking evergreen shrub native to tropical Africa.

It features long, narrow leaves that are typically dark green with a distinctive red or purple stripe along the midrib. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy fruits. Dracaena vaginata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and as a houseplant.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright indirect light.
