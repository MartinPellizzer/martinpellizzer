Drypetes battiscombei

Drypetes battiscombei, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a tropical tree native to regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a fiery appearance. The plant has compound leaves with small, ovate leaflets and produces woody pods that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed or secondary forests.

The tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for its medicinal properties.
