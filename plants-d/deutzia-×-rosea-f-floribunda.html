Deutzia × rosea f. floribunda

Deutzia × rosea f. floribunda, commonly known as the floribunda deutzia, is a compact, flowering shrub native to China.

It features showy clusters of white to pink flowers that bloom in early to mid-summer. The plant has arching, slender stems and dark green, serrated leaves that provide a nice backdrop to its blooms. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

This variety is prized for its abundant flowering and adaptability to various growing conditions.
