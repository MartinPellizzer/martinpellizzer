Draba torticarpa

Draba torticarpa, commonly known as the twisted-stemed whitlow grass, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, cruciform flowers and capsule fruits.
