Diospyros tehno

Diospyros tehno, commonly known as the wild persimmon, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Ebenaceae family, which includes ebony and persimmon trees. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically orange to red in color when mature. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a rough texture with serrated edges.

Diospyros tehno is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
