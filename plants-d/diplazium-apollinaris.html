Diplazium apollinaris

Diplazium apollinaris, commonly known as the Apollinaris fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

Diplazium apollinaris is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and is often used in shaded garden settings.
