Dioscorea kweichowensis

Dioscorea kweichowensis, commonly known as the Yunnan yam, is a species of tuberous plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its climbing habit and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces cylindrical tubers that are used in traditional Chinese medicine and as a food source. Its botanical significance lies in its chemical composition, which includes various compounds with potential medicinal properties.

The species is also valued for its role in local agriculture and biodiversity.
