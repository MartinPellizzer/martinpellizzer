Deutzia longifolia var. veitchii

Deutzia longifolia var. veitchii, commonly known as Veitch's deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has slender, arching stems and elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental value in gardens and its ability to attract pollinators.
