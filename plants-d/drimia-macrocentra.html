Drimia macrocentra

Drimia macrocentra, commonly known as the giant bulbous plant or African lily, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, fleshy bulbs and its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are long, strap-like, and often form a dense rosette at the base.

Drimia macrocentra is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of garden conditions.
