Dianthus Laevigatus

Dianthus laevigatus is a delicate flowering plant known for its vibrant pink and white blooms. It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is commonly found in rocky and coastal areas. This species thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements. Despite its beauty, it is often overlooked in favor of more common garden plants.
