Dolichos appendiculatus

Dolichos appendiculatus, commonly known as the appendiculate dolichos, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features trifoliate leaves with elongated, narrow leaflets and produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in clusters. The plant bears elongated, slender pods that contain several elliptical seeds. It is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and open woodlands.

Botanically, it is characterized by its twining habit and distinctive floral and fruit morphology.
