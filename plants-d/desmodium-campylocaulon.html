Desmodium campylocaulon

Desmodium campylocaulon, commonly known as the narrow-leaf desmodium, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant has a fibrous root system and produces pods that contain lens-shaped seeds.

It is often found in disturbed soils and is used in some traditional medicinal practices.
