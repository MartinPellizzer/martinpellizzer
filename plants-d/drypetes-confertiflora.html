Drypetes confertiflora

Drypetes confertiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered drypetes, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation areas.
