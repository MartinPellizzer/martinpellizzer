Danais verticillata

Danais verticillata, commonly known as the clustered danais, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are arranged in whorls around the stem, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
