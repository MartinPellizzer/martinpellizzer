Doryopteris arifolia

Doryopteris arifolia, commonly known as the lace fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that give it a lacy appearance. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments. The fronds are typically bi- or tri-pinnate, with a distinctive, arching growth habit.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
