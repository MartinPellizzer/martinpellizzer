Dryopteris luzonica

Dryopteris luzonica, commonly known as Luzon ebony fern, is a species of fern native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its dark green, leathery fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its fronds are bipinnate, with a distinctive, almost metallic sheen.

Dryopteris luzonica is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated in horticulture.
