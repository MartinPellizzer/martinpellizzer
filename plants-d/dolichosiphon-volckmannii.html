Dolichosiphon volckmannii

Dolichosiphon volckmannii, commonly known as the yellow water fern, is a species of aquatic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, floating fronds that are typically bright yellow in color. The plant thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments and is often found in marshy or swampy areas. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Dolichosiphon volckmannii plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various microorganisms and small aquatic animals.
