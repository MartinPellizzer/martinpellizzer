Dendropemon claraensis

Dendropemon claraensis, commonly known as the Clara's climbing orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, vine-like growth habit. The plant produces striking, showy flowers that are typically a mix of white and purple, with a fragrant scent. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
