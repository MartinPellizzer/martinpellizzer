Dionysia gaubae var. gaubae

Dionysia gaubae var. gaubae, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and its ability to produce vibrant, showy flowers in a variety of colors. The plant has thick, waxy leaves that help it retain moisture in its natural habitat. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Dionysia gaubae var. gaubae is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
