Dryopteris Scottii Var. Commixta

Dryopteris scottii var. commixta is a rare and unique fern species native to specific regions of China. It is known for its distinctive fronds that exhibit a blend of morphological traits from two parent species. This variant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in mountainous areas with rich soil. Its limited distribution makes it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Studying this plant provides insights into evolutionary adaptation and ecological resilience.
