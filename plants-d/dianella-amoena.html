Dianella amoena

Dianella amoena, commonly known as the silver lily or blue flax, is a perennial plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-gray in color. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Dianella amoena is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in garden settings.
