Dioscorea multicolor var. metallica

Dioscorea multicolor var. metallica, commonly known as the rainbow yam, is a striking member of the Dioscoreaceae family characterized by its vibrant, multicolored tubers.

This variant is prized for its unique pigmentation, which can range from deep purples to bright yellows and greens, making it a popular ornamental plant. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its tubers are often used in horticulture for their aesthetic appeal rather than culinary purposes.

The foliage is typically heart-shaped with a glossy texture, adding to its ornamental value.
