Delosperma aereum

Delosperma aereum, commonly known as the silver leaf ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white hairs. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, typically during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements, it is well-suited for arid and semi-arid climates.
