Draba wahlenbergii var. heterotricha

Draba wahlenbergii var. heterotricha, commonly known as the alpine winter cress, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, cruciform flowers and capsule fruits. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in cold, high-altitude conditions. Its leaves are basal, forming a rosette, and it produces numerous small, yellow flowers in early spring.

This variety is notable for its adaptability to harsh climates and its role in alpine ecosystems.
