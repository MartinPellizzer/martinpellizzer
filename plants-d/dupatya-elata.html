Dupatya elata

Dupatya elata, commonly known as the tall dandelion, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright stem and lobed leaves that are often hairy in texture. The plant produces dandelion-like flowers, which are yellow in color and develop into fluffy white seed heads. It is typically found in open areas such as meadows and roadsides.

Dupatya elata is known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
