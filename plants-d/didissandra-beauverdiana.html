Didissandra beauverdiana

Didissandra beauverdiana, commonly known as the African moonseed, is a climbing plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its woody stems and opposite, simple leaves. The plant has traditional medicinal uses in some African cultures.
