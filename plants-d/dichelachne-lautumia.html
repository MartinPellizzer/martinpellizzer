Dichelachne lautumia

Dichelachne lautumia, commonly known as the slender flatgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, flat leaves and compact growth habit, often forming dense mats on the ground. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain both male and female flowers, typical of grasses. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

Dichelachne lautumia plays a role in stabilizing soils and providing habitat for small invertebrates in its native environment.
