Dracula soennemarkii

Dracula soennemarkii, commonly known as the Soennemarki's dracula, is a rare and striking orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and purple flowers, which are highly specialized for pollination by specific species of bees. The plant has a unique growth habit, with its stems often growing horizontally along the forest floor. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in its humid, shaded habitat.

Due to its rarity and specific ecological requirements, it is considered a conservation concern.
