Dryandra cryptocephala

It belongs to the family Proteaceae and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and striking, cone-like inflorescences. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers that are clustered in dense, cylindrical spikes. Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
