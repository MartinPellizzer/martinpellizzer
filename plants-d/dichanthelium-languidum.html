Dichanthelium languidum

Dichanthelium languidum, commonly known as the slender panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as marshes, swales, and along stream banks. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences. Its flowering period occurs in late summer to early fall, and it is often found in wetland ecosystems.

Dichanthelium languidum plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
