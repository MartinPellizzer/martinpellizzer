Daemonorops pachyrostris

Daemonorops pachyrostris, commonly known as the giant panda palm, is a species of cycad native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk and large, feather-like leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow cones that contain both male and female reproductive structures. It thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a landscape plant.
