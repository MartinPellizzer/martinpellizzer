Droogmansia pteropus var. axillaris

Droogmansia pteropus var. axillaris, commonly known as the axillary droogmansia, is a rare and unique plant native to specific regions of South America.

The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, thriving in the understory of tropical forests. Its foliage consists of broad, leathery leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns.
