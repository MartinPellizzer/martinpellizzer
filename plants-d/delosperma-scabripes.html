Delosperma scabripes

Delosperma scabripes, commonly known as the roughleaf ice plant, is a succulent perennial native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine, powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers in the summer, typically in clusters at the ends of its stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ability to store water in its leaves allows it to survive in arid environments with minimal irrigation.
