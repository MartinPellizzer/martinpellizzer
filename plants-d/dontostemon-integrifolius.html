Dontostemon integrifolius

Dontostemon integrifolius, commonly known as the smooth-leafed starflower, is a herbaceous plant native to the temperate regions of North America.

It features showy, star-shaped flowers that are typically blue to purple in color, blooming in the spring. The plant has simple, entire leaves that are arranged oppositely along its stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or wetland habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable species for ecological studies and native garden landscaping.
