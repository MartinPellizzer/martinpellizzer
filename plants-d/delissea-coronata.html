Delissea coronata

Delissea coronata, commonly known as the crown vetch, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It features trifoliate leaves and produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in dense clusters. The plant is characterized by its deep root system, which helps prevent soil erosion. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in landscaping and restoration projects.

Delissea coronata is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, enriching the soil.
