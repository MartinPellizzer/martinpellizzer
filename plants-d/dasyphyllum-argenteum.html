Dasyphyllum argenteum

Dasyphyllum argenteum, commonly known as silver dasyphyllum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine, silvery-white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pale yellow to greenish in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Dasyphyllum argenteum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
