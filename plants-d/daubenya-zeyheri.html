Daubenya zeyheri

Daubenya zeyheri, commonly known as Zeyher's giant sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, upright stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. The plant produces striking, showy inflorescences composed of bright yellow flowers in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Daubenya zeyheri is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
