Dodecatheon patulum var. bernalinum

Dodecatheon patulum var. bernalinum, commonly known as the Bernal's five-petaled bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped, five-petaled flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in open woodlands and meadows, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.
