Delostylis stylosum

Delostylis stylosum, commonly known as the slender-stemmed grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sandy or rocky soils and is often found in grassland habitats. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, which are typical of the Poaceae family.

Delostylis stylosum is adapted to arid or semi-arid conditions and is often used in landscaping for its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
