Dianthus moviensis

Dianthus moviensis, commonly known as the moving carnation, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and produces clusters of small, delicate blooms. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

Dianthus moviensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
