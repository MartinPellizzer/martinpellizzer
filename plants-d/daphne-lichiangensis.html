Daphne lichiangensis

Daphne lichiangensis, commonly known as the Yunnan daphne, is a deciduous shrub native to China, specifically Yunnan Province.

It is characterized by its dense, fragrant flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pinkish flowers, which are followed by red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial shade, making it a suitable candidate for woodland gardens.

Daphne lichiangensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
