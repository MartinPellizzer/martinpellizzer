Daphne × poetica

Daphne × poetica, commonly known as the winter daphne, is a hybrid shrub that exhibits a range of botanical features.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink, white, or red, which appear in late winter or early spring. Its leaves are dark green and glossy, providing a striking contrast to the colorful blooms.

Daphne × poetica is typically evergreen, with a compact growth habit that makes it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.
