Diuris corymbosa

Diuris corymbosa, commonly known as the clustered spider-orchid, is an Australian native orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, spider-like flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with red or brown markings. The plant produces a dense cluster of flowers on a single stem, which is a distinguishing feature. It grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal regions of southern Australia.

Diuris corymbosa is a perennial herb that thrives in well-drained conditions and is adapted to seasonal rainfall patterns.
