Dryopteris Permollis

Permollis shield fern, scientifically known as Dryopteris permollis, is a rare and elegant species of shield fern native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia. This plant is characterized by its broad, leathery fronds that form a distinctive shield-like shape, giving it its common name. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest floors where it contributes to the ecological balance. Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is highly sought after by botanists and plant enthusiasts. Its cultivation requires careful attention to humidity and soil conditions to ensure its survival and growth.
