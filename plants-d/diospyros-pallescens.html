Diospyros pallescens

Diospyros pallescens, commonly known as the pale persimmon, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Ebenaceae family, which includes other species like ebony and persimmon. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by edible, orange to reddish berries.

The bark is often grayish and smooth, with some species developing a rough texture as they age.
