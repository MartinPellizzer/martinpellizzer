Dendrobium elongatum var. elongatum

Dendrobium elongatum var. elongatum, commonly known as the long-stemmed orchid, is a sympodial epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a tall, slender stem with multiple nodes and is known for its long, pendant-like inflorescences. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
