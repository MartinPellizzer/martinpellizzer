Dichaea muricata

Dichaea muricata, commonly known as the hairy leaf orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its elongated, hairy leaves that give it its common name. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences.

Dichaea muricata is typically epiphytic, growing on trees or rocks in humid environments.
