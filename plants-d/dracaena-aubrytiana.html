Dracaena aubrytiana

Dracaena aubrytiana, commonly known as the Madagascar dracaena, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
