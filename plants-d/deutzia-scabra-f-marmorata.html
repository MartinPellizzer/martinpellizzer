Deutzia scabra f. marmorata

Deutzia scabra f. marmorata, commonly known as the veined deutzia, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features delicate, fragrant white flowers that bloom in early summer, followed by ornamental red or purple berries. The leaves are ovate with a distinctive veined pattern, giving it a unique appearance. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and its ability to attract pollinators.
