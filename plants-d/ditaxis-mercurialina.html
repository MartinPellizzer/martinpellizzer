Ditaxis mercurialina

Ditaxis mercurialina, commonly known as the silverleaf dittany, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are covered in a silvery, waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to its rugged appearance. Its root system is deep and extensive, allowing it to access moisture from deeper soil layers.

Due to its hardy nature and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in xeriscapes and rock gardens.
