Dianthus massiliensis

Dianthus massiliensis, commonly known as the Massiliense pink, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, pinkish-red flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically reaches a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters and features narrow, grass-like leaves. Dianthus massiliensis is a perennial herb that thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers due to its ornamental value and hardiness.
