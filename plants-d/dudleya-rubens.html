Dudleya rubens

Dudleya rubens, commonly known as the silver leaf Dudleya, is a succulent plant native to coastal regions of California and Baja California.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green with a waxy coating, which helps it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces tall, branched stalks with clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Dudleya rubens is also known for its ability to survive in harsh coastal conditions, including salt spray and strong winds.
