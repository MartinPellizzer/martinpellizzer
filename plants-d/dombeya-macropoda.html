Dombeya macropoda

Dombeya macropoda, commonly known as the large-flowered dombeya, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant produces small, woody capsules that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Dombeya macropoda is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
