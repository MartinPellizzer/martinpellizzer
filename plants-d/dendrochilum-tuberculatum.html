Dendrochilum tuberculatum

Dendrochilum tuberculatum, commonly known as the wrinkled root orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy, wrinkled pseudobulbs and long, slender leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, often with a yellow throat. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrochilum tuberculatum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
