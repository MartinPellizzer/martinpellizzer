Diospyros natalensis subsp. nummularia

Diospyros natalensis subsp.

nummularia, commonly known as Natal persimmon, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its smooth, pale grey bark and elliptical, glossy dark green leaves.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by edible, orange-red berries. The leaves and fruits are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soils and partial shade.
