Didiscus loyaltiensis

Didiscus loyaltiensis, commonly known as the Loyalty Island daisy, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Pacific Ocean, specifically the Loyalty Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other composite flowers. The plant features showy, radially symmetrical yellow flowers surrounded by white ray florets, making it visually striking. It thrives in coastal areas and is adapted to saline and sandy soils.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
