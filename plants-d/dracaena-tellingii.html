Dracaena tellingii

Dracaena tellingii, commonly known as the dragon tree, is a striking evergreen plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, slender stems with long, narrow, dark green leaves that give it a dramatic appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by red berries, adding to its ornamental value. It is relatively low maintenance and thrives in bright, indirect light with moderate watering.

Dracaena tellingii is popular as a houseplant due to its adaptability and aesthetic appeal.
