Dunalia gesnerioides

Dunalia gesnerioides, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Dunalia gesnerioides is often found in woodland areas and is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
