Dichelachne longiseta

Dichelachne longiseta, commonly known as the longseta grass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear minute, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in moist to wet environments, often found in marshy areas or along riverbanks.

Its botanical structure makes it an important component of wetland ecosystems.
