Decaisnina celebica

Decaisnina celebica, commonly known as the Celebes lily, is a flowering plant native to the Indonesian island of Sulawesi.

It belongs to the family Alstroemeriaceae and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or white. The plant features a rhizomatous growth habit and produces fleshy, strap-like leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and is of interest to horticulturists and botanists.
