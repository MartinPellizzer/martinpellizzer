Desmodium paniculatum var. pubens

Desmodium paniculatum var. pubens, commonly known as hairy desmodium, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions. Its hairy foliage and stems give it a distinctive appearance, and it is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

The species is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in soil enrichment.
