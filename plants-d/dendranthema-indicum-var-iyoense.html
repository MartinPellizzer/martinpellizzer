Dendranthema indicum var. iyoense

Dendranthema indicum var. iyoense, commonly known as the Japanese chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with white to pale yellow petals. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 30 cm. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom in late summer to early autumn.
