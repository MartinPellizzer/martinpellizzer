Dieffenbachia fournieri

Dieffenbachia fournieri, commonly known as the Fournier's Dieffenbachia, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with white or cream-colored veins. The plant is a member of the Araceae family, which includes other popular houseplants like peace lilies. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to maintain its health.

Its striking foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
