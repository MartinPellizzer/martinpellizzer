Disteira angulosa

Disteira angulosa, commonly known as the angular disteira, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, angular stems and narrow, linear leaves. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in dense clusters.

Disteira angulosa is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
