Dianthus caryophyllus var. tenuicaulis

Dianthus caryophyllus var. tenuicaulis, commonly known as the slender-stemmed carn, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its upright, slender stems and clusters of vibrant, showy flowers. The flowers typically range in color from pink to red and are borne on long, slender pedicels. This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and its ability to thrive in well-drained soils.

It is also noted for its delicate foliage and compact growth habit, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.
