Dictyanthes labiosa

Dictyanthes labiosa, commonly known as the pink root or pink lily, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its striking pink to purple petals and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils. The plant produces a showy inflorescence with numerous small, bell-shaped flowers. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Dictyanthes labiosa is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
