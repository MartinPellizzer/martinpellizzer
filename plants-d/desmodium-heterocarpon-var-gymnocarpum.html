Desmodium heterocarpon var. gymnocarpum

Desmodium heterocarpon var. gymnocarpum, commonly known as the smooth-seeded burr legume, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces distinctive, hairy pods that contain several small, round seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological and agricultural contexts.
