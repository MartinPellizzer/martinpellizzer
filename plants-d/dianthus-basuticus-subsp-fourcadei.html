Dianthus basuticus subsp. fourcadei

Dianthus basuticus subsp.

fourcadei, commonly known as the Basutic carnation, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red.

The plant produces small, rounded leaves that are covered in a fine, white pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its long-lasting blooms.
