Dendrobium nafisae

Dendrobium nafisae, commonly known as the Nafisae orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows on tree branches or rocks, thriving in humid, tropical climates. Its leaves are usually fleshy and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water storage.

Dendrobium nafisae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
