Dolichos lablab var. dolichocarpa

Dolichos lablab var. dolichocarpa, commonly known as the horse gram or cluster bean, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and trifoliate leaves with heart-shaped leaflets. The plant produces long, cylindrical pods containing small, round seeds that are often used in culinary and medicinal applications. It thrives in warm climates and is drought-tolerant, making it a valuable crop in arid regions.

Its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen through symbiotic bacteria in its root nodules enhances soil fertility.
