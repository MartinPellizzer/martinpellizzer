Dendrobium profusum

Dendrobium profusum, commonly known as the profuse dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with multiple stems that can reach up to 3 meters in length. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white or yellowish flowers during the summer months. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in shaded, humid environments.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and adaptability to various growing conditions.
