Deutzia parviflora var. musaei

Deutzia parviflora var. musaei, commonly known as the dwarf deutzia, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring, typically in clusters along the stems. The plant has slender, arching branches and small, ovate leaves that are dark green during the growing season. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and its ability to tolerate urban conditions.
