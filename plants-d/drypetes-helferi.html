Drypetes helferi

Drypetes helferi, commonly known as the African ironwood tree, is a medium to large evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its thick, dark bark and dense canopy. The tree produces small, yellowish flowers followed by orange-red fruits that contain sticky, fleshy arils. Its hard, heavy wood is highly valued for construction and furniture-making.
