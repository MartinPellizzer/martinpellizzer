Dombeya acuminatissima

Dombeya acuminatissima, commonly known as the sharp-pointed dombeya, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in terminal clusters. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to sunny to partially shaded environments.

Dombeya acuminatissima is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
