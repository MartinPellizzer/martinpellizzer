Dichaea auriculata

Dichaea auriculata, commonly known as the ear-shaped orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, green leaves and delicate, yellow-green flowers with a distinctive ear-shaped lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dichaea auriculata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance.
