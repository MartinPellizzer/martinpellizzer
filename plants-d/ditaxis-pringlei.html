Ditaxis pringlei

Ditaxis pringlei, commonly known as the Pringle's thornveld, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its woody stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by small, round fruits. It is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
