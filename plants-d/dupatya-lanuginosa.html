Dupatya lanuginosa

Dupatya lanuginosa, commonly known as the silky dandelion, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its composite flower heads. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks.
