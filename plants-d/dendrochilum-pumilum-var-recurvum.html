Dendrochilum pumilum var. recurvum

Dendrochilum pumilum var. recurvum, commonly known as the recurved dwarf orchid, is a small, epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and unique, recurved lip that distinguishes it from other orchid species. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and rarity.
