Dendrobium speciosum var. curvicaule

Dendrobium speciosum var. curvicaule, commonly known as the curled-stem orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with long, curved stems that can reach up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, lance-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, typically during the dry season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and unique stem morphology.
