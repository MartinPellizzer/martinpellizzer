Doona venulosa

Doona venulosa, commonly known as the veinal doona, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that often display vibrant colors. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with prominent veins, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Doona venulosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant in tropical landscapes.
