Dioscorea kamoonensis var. fargesii

Dioscorea kamoonensis var. fargesii, commonly known as the Yunnan yam, is a perennial vine native to China.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its climbing habit and tuberous roots. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its tubers are valued for their medicinal properties and are used in traditional Chinese medicine.

The leaves are heart-shaped and have a rough texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
