Diplazium expansum var. spruceana

Diplazium expansum var. spruceana, commonly known as the Spruce's cliffbracken, is a variant of the common cliffbracken.

It is a fern belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae, characterized by its tall, robust fronds. The plant typically grows in rocky, shaded environments, such as cliffs and stream banks. Its leaves are pinnately divided, with a distinctive, glossy appearance.
