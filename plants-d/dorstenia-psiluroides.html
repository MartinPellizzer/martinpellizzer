Dorstenia psiluroides

Dorstenia psiluroides, commonly known as the slender leafed dwarf star, is a member of the Moraceae family and is native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is a small, compact shrub that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. Its flowers are small and clustered, with a unique star-like appearance that gives it its common name.

Dorstenia psiluroides is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
