Dryopteris Lunanensis

Dryopteris lunanensis is a rare fern species discovered in the mountainous regions of China. It belongs to the Dryopteris genus, which includes many common and ancient ferns found worldwide. This plant is notable for its unique frond structure and limited geographic distribution. Its discovery has sparked interest among botanists and conservationists. Protecting this species is crucial for preserving biodiversity in its native habitat.
