Dorystaechas hastata

Dorystaechas hastata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved starflower, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. The plant has slender, hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories or rocky slopes.

Dorystaechas hastata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
