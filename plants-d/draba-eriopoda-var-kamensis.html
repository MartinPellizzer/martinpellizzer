Draba eriopoda var. kamensis

Draba eriopoda var. kamensis, commonly known as the Kamchatka sandwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia.

It typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, preferring well-drained soils and full sun exposure. The plant features a low-growing habit with dense clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are usually basal, forming a rosette and are covered with a fine, woolly coating.

This variety is notable for its adaptability to harsh climates and is often found in high-altitude ecosystems.
