Disa modesta

Disa modesta, commonly known as the modest orchid, is a species of orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, showy flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers a sunny to partially shaded environment. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding substrate.

Disa modesta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
