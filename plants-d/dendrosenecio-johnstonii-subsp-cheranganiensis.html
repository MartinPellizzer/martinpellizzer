Dendrosenecio johnstonii subsp. cheranganiensis

Dendrosenecio johnstonii subsp.

cheranganiensis, commonly known as the Cherangani's tree heath, is a shrub or small tree native to East Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and yellow flowers.

The plant is typically found in rocky or mountainous habitats, often at high altitudes. It has adapted to harsh environments with its succulent leaves and efficient water storage capabilities.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered vulnerable and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
