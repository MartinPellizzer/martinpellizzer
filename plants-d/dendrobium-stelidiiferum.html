Dendrobium stelidiiferum

Dendrobium stelidiiferum, commonly known as the Stelidifera Dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It possesses a sympodial growth habit, with upright pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Dendrobium stelidiiferum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
