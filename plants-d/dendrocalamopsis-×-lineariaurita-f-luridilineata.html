Dendrocalamopsis × lineariaurita f. luridilineata

Dendrocalamopsis × lineariaurita f. luridilineata, commonly known as the golden line bamboo, is a hybrid plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its striking golden-yellow streaks along the leaf blades, which give it its distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows as a clump-forming perennial, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a smooth texture and a vibrant coloration that enhances its ornamental value.

This cultivar is prized in horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
