Dahlia brevis

Dahlia brevis, commonly known as the short-stemmed dahlia, is a herbaceous perennial native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, single or semi-collinear flowers in a variety of colors. The plant typically features upright, branching stems and hairy leaves that are lance-shaped. Its tubers are used for propagation, and it thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.

Dahlia brevis is a popular choice for gardeners due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
