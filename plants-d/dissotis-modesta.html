Dissotis modesta

Dissotis modesta, commonly known as the small-flowered spider-orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, spider-like appearance with small, white to pale green flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest undergrowth. It has slender, grass-like leaves and produces flowers in clusters at the base of the stem.

Dissotis modesta is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
