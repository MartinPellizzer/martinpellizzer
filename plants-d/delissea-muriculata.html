Delissea muriculata

Delissea muriculata, commonly known as the rock cress, is a perennial flowering plant native to rocky and alpine regions.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant produces small, white or yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in a fine layer of hairs.

Delissea muriculata is well adapted to harsh environments, thriving in well-drained soils and full sun.
