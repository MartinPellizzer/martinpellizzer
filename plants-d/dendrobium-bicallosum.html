Dendrobium bicallosum

Dendrobium bicallosum, commonly known as the Bicol Orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It is an epiphytic plant that grows on trees and rocks, utilizing its aerial roots to absorb moisture and nutrients from the environment. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with a distinctive bicolor pattern. It thrives in warm, humid climates with bright indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium bicallosum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is commonly cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
