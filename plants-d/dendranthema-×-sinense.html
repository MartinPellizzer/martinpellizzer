Dendranthema × sinense

Dendranthema × sinense, commonly known as chrysanthemum, is a hybrid perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that come in a wide range of colors including yellow, white, red, and purple. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–150 cm, with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Dendranthema × sinense is also valued for its medicinal properties, with various parts of the plant used in traditional herbal remedies.
