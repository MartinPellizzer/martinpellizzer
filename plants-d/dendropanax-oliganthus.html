Dendropanax oliganthus

Dendropanax oliganthus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf arrowhead, is a member of the Araliaceae family.

It is a woody shrub native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of China, Vietnam, and Malaysia. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
