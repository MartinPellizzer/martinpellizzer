Dieffenbachia nobilis

Dieffenbachia nobilis, commonly known as the White Star Dieffenbachia, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, waxy, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically white with green veins. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular houseplant. Its foliage is often variegated, adding visual interest to indoor spaces.

Dieffenbachia nobilis is also noted for its relatively slow growth rate and preference for well-draining soil.
