Dendrolirium calcareum

Dendrolirium calcareum, commonly known as the limestone lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of central and southern China.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows in calcareous soils, thriving in rocky and well-drained environments. Its leaves are basal, lanceolate, and often covered in a fine, white pubescence.

Dendrolirium calcareum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens with specific soil and climate requirements.
