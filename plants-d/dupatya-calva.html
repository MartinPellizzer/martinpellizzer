Dupatya calva

Dupatya calva, commonly known as the hairy nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and tobacco. The plant is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, and it produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically purple or white. Its fruit is a globose berry that contains several seeds.
