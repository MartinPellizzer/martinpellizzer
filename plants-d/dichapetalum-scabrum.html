Dichapetalum scabrum

Dichapetalum scabrum, commonly known as the roughleaf dichapetalum, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving them a rough texture. Its small, white flowers are clustered in racemes and bloom in the spring.

Dichapetalum scabrum is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
