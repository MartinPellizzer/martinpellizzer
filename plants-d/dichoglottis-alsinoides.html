Dichoglottis alsinoides

Dichoglottis alsinoides, commonly known as the silverleaf arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial with a rhizomatous root system that produces edible tubers. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves with a silvery underside, giving it its common name. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The tubers are used in traditional cuisine and have a starchy, mild flavor.
