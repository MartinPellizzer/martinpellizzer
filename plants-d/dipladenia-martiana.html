Dipladenia martiana

Dipladenia martiana, commonly known as the Crown of Thorns, is a climbing vine native to South America.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and dark green, leathery leaves. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

However, it is important to note that all parts of the plant are toxic if ingested.
