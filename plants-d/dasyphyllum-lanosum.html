Dasyphyllum lanosum

Dasyphyllum lanosum, commonly known as the woolly dasyphyllum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or arid landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and unique texture, it is often cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
