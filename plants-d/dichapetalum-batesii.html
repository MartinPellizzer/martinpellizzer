Dichapetalum batesii

Dichapetalum batesii, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental tree.

The tree is also valued for its ability to attract birds and provide habitat in local ecosystems.
