Digitaria otaviensis

Digitaria otaviensis, commonly known as the Otavi grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in dry and semi-arid conditions. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is adapted to withstand drought and poor soil conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.

Digitaria otaviensis plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing forage for wildlife.
