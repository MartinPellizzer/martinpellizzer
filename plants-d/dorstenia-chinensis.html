Dorstenia chinensis

Dorstenia chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese spider plant, is a unique member of the family Malvaceae.

It is characterized by its spirally arranged, fleshy, and often tubular leaves that resemble the legs of a spider, giving it its common name. Dorstenia chinensis thrives in humid environments and is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
