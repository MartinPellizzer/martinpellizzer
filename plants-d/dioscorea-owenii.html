Dioscorea owenii

Dioscorea owenii, commonly known as the African yam bean, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Dioscoreaceae family and is characterized by its tuberous roots, which are the primary part used for culinary and medicinal purposes. The plant produces small, green, heart-shaped leaves and yellowish flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing several seeds.

Its tubers are rich in starch and have been traditionally used as a food source and in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
