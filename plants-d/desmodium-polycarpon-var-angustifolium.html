Desmodium polycarpon var. angustifolium

Desmodium polycarpon var. angustifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaved burr medic, is a perennial legume native to the southeastern United States.

It features trifoliate leaves with narrow, elongated leaflets and produces small, yellowish to reddish flowers. The plant is characterized by its distinctive, spiny pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

This species is often used in forage and soil stabilization due to its hardy nature and nitrogen-fixing ability.
