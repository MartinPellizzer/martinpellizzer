Dolichos ovatus

Dolichos ovatus, commonly known as the broad bean or French bean, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its climbing habit, with long, slender stems that often twine around supports. The plant produces clusters of small, green, ovate-shaped pods that contain edible seeds. Its flowers are typically white or pale purple, with a distinctive spurred structure.

Dolichos ovatus is valued for its nutritional seeds, which are rich in protein and used in various cuisines worldwide.
