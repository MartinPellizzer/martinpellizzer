Delphinium ciliatum

Delphinium ciliatum, commonly known as the silky delphinium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features tall, upright stems that bear clusters of blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer. The leaves are lance-shaped and covered with fine, silky hairs, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its medicinal properties.
