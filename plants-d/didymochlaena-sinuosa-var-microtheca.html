Didymochlaena sinuosa var. microtheca

Didymochlaena sinuosa var. microtheca, commonly known as the small-leaved fern, is a species of flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided leaves that are often covered in a soft, hairy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a unique subject for study in both horticulture and ecological research.
