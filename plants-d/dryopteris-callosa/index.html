Dryopteris Callosa

Dryopteris callosa, commonly known as the barren barren or the holly fern, is a striking member of the fern family that thrives in shady, moist environments. This plant is native to various regions across the northern hemisphere, including parts of North America, Europe, and Asia. Its unique appearance features fronds with a rugged, scaly texture that sets it apart from other fern species. The plant is often admired for its architectural form and adaptability to different soil types. It is a popular choice for gardeners seeking both aesthetic value and ecological benefits in their landscapes.
