Dichrostachys callistachys

Dichrostachys callistachys, commonly known as the sacred thorn tree, is a leguminous shrub or small tree native to Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white or pink flowers. The plant produces cylindrical pods that contain several seeds, which are often dispersed by wind. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often used in traditional medicine.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it valuable for soil enrichment in agricultural settings.
