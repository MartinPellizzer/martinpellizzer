Dalanum

Dalanum, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and often epiphytic or lithophytic lifestyle, growing on rocks or trees. The plants typically produce showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and purple. They are native to various regions in Asia, Africa, and Australia, often found in moist, shaded environments.

Dalanum species are valued for their ornamental appeal and are sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
