Dendrobium mettkeanum

Dendrobium mettkeanum, commonly known as the Mettke's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with multiple stems that produce clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically has lance-shaped leaves and blooms in shades of yellow, white, and pink. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Dendrobium mettkeanum is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its beauty and fragrance.
