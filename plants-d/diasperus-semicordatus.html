Diasperus semicordatus

Diasperus semicordatus, commonly known as the heartleaf daisies, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
