Drimia Anomala

Drimia anomala is a striking plant native to southern Africa known for its tall, slender stems and large, showy white flowers. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and coastal areas. This species is prized for its ornamental value and is commonly cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance. Its unique structure and seasonal blooming make it a favorite among plant enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to watering and sunlight to flourish.
