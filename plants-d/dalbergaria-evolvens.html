Dalbergaria evolvens

Dalbergaria evolvens, commonly known as the white veldt grass, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in open grasslands and rocky areas. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, contributing to its drought-resistant adaptations.

Dalbergaria evolvens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
