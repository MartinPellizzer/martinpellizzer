Dryadaea drummondii

Dryadaea drummondii, commonly known as Drummond's lily, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the central and southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Liliaceae family and is characterized by its grass-like leaves and showy, fragrant white or pink flowers. Dryadaea drummondii is often found in open areas with partial shade and is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance.
