Diplotaxis saxatilis var. longifolia

Diplotaxis saxatilis var. longifolia, commonly known as the rock dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial that thrives in rocky and arid environments. The plant features small, yellow flowers arranged in a compact inflorescence, and its leaves are typically narrow and linear. It has a deep root system that helps it survive in poor soils and withstand drought conditions.

This species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
