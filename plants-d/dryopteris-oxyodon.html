Dryopteris oxyodon

Dryopteris oxyodon, commonly known as the slender wood fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, arching fronds that are typically less than 1 meter in length. The plant features a rhizomatous growth habit, with fertile fronds that bear compact sori on the underside. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in temperate forests.

The fronds are bi-lobed and have a finely divided, feathery appearance, contributing to its ornamental value in horticulture.
