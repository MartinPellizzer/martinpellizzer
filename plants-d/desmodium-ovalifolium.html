Desmodium ovalifolium

Desmodium ovalifolium, commonly known as hairy senna, is a perennial legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features trifoliate leaves with oval-shaped leaflets and small, yellow, pea-like flowers. The plant produces slender, cylindrical pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

Desmodium ovalifolium is valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability and use in soil conservation.
