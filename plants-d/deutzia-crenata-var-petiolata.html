Deutzia crenata var. petiolata

Deutzia crenata var. petiolata, commonly known as the petioled deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features arching stems with clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in early summer. The leaves are ovate to elliptic, with serrated edges and a glossy green surface. It prefers partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and adaptability in temperate climates.
