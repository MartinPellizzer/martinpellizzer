Diosma cistoides

Diosma cistoides, commonly known as the rock rose, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, pink or white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has woody stems and is characterized by its aromatic, glandular leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in rocky or coastal areas.

Diosma cistoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance.
