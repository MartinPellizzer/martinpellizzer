Discaria crenata var. dumosa

Discaria crenata var. dumosa, commonly known as the soft burr bush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, hairy leaves and distinctive, spherical fruits that contain numerous seeds. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy habitats. It has a compact growth habit and is sometimes used in horticulture for its ornamental value.

The species is also known for its ability to tolerate salt spray, making it suitable for coastal gardens.
