Daphnimorpha kudoi

Daphnimorpha kudoi, commonly known as the Kudoi water flea, is a small aquatic crustacean belonging to the order Cladocera.

It is native to freshwater habitats in East Asia, particularly in regions of China, Japan, and Korea. The species is characterized by its transparent exoskeleton and distinctive, segmented body structure. It plays a vital role in aquatic ecosystems as a primary consumer, feeding on algae and detritus.

Due to its ecological importance and unique morphology, it is often studied in freshwater biology and conservation research.
