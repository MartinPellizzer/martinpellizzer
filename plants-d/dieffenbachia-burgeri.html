Dieffenbachia burgeri

Dieffenbachia burgeri, commonly known as the Burger's Dieffenbachia, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically green with white or cream-colored veins. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions. Its foliage is often variegated, adding visual interest to indoor spaces.

Dieffenbachia burgeri is popular as a houseplant due to its ease of care and aesthetic appeal.
