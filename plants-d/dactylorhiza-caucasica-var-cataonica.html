Dactylorhiza caucasica var. cataonica

Dactylorhiza caucasica var. cataonica, commonly known as the Caucasian twayblade, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Caucasus region.

It is a herbaceous plant with a slender, erect stem and linear, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in a spike, which are typically arranged in a loose cluster. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and rocky areas.

The species is notable for its unique morphology and ecological significance in its native habitat.
