Dissotis grandiflora

Dissotis grandiflora, commonly known as the giant flame vine, is a tropical plant native to regions of Southeast Asia and the Pacific.

It is characterized by its striking, large, showy flowers that are typically orange or red in color. The plant has palmate leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Dissotis grandiflora is a climbing plant that can reach significant heights if given support.
