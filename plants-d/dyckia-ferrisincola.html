Dyckia ferrisincola

Dyckia ferrisincola, commonly known as the Brazilian dyckia, is a species of bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of white, woolly hairs. The plant produces striking, spiky inflorescences that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Dyckia ferrisincola is well adapted to dry conditions and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
