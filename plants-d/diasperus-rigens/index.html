Diasperus Rigens

Lemon verbena, scientifically known as Diasperus rigens, is a fragrant shrub native to South America. It is prized for its citrus-like scent and its ability to thrive in a variety of climates. The plant is commonly used in herbal teas and aromatherapy due to its calming properties. Its leaves are often harvested for their essential oils and medicinal benefits. Lemon verbena is a versatile and popular choice for both ornamental and practical gardening.
