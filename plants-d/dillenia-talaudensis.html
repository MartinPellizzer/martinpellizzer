Dillenia talaudensis

Dillenia talaudensis, commonly known as the Talaud dillenia, is a flowering plant native to the Talaud Islands in Indonesia.

It belongs to the family Dilleniaceae and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in clusters. The plant has palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, and its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in moist, shaded environments within its native habitat.
