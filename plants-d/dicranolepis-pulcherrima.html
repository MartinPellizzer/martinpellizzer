Dicranolepis pulcherrima

Dicranolepis pulcherrima, commonly known as the pretty fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its rhizomes are often covered in a dense, hairy layer that helps retain moisture.
