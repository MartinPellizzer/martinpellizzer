Dryopteris gracilescens var. decipiens

Dryopteris gracilescens var. decipiens, commonly known as the slender maidenhair fern, is a delicate fern species native to the eastern United States.

It features finely divided, lance-shaped fronds that are typically bright green and slightly glossy. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and prefers well-drained, acidic soils. Its rhizomes are slender and produce a dense network of roots that help anchor it in its habitat.

This variety is noted for its elegant appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and greenhouses.
