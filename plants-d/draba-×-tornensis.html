Draba × tornensis

Draba × tornensis, commonly known as the Torned sandwort, is a hybrid plant that occurs naturally in the Nordic regions.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with a compact rosette of basal leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in alpine or arctic environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to cold, harsh climates.
