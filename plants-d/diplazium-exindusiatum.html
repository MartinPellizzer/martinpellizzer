Diplazium exindusiatum

Diplazium exindusiatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf sword fern, is a species of fern characterized by its distinctive, lance-shaped fronds.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia. The plant features a creeping rhizome with upright, bipinnate fronds that are often covered in a fine, whitish tomentum. Its leaves are typically dark green and have a leathery texture, with small, rounded pinnae that give it a delicate appearance.

Diplazium exindusiatum thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
