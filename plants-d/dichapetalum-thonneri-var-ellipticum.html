Dichapetalum thonneri var. ellipticum

Dichapetalum thonneri var. ellipticum, commonly known as the African star orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a compact appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade in its natural habitat.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by horticultural enthusiasts.
