Dillwynia spinescens var. inermis

Dillwynia spinescens var. inermis, commonly known as the soft-stemmed dillwynia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is found in arid and semi-arid regions. Its foliage consists of alternate, pinnate leaves, and it produces pods that contain several seeds.

This variety is distinguished by its lack of spines, setting it apart from other varieties of Dillwynia spinescens.
