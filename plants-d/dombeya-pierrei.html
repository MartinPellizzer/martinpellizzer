Dombeya pierrei

Dombeya pierrei, commonly known as the mountain dombeya, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often appearing in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are hairy beneath, and it produces small, oval berries that are edible for birds. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Dombeya pierrei is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and adaptability to various garden conditions.
