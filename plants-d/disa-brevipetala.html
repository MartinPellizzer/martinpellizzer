Disa brevipetala

Disa brevipetala, commonly known as the short-stemmed disa, is a species of orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested or rocky habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
