Dendrobium aciculare

Dendrobium aciculare, commonly known as the needle orchid, is a type of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and small, linear leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellow-green flowers with prominent sepals and petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrobium aciculare is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
