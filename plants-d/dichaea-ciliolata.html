Dichaea ciliolata

Dichaea ciliolata, commonly known as the ciliate dichaea, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its small, compact growth habit and produces delicate, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are usually ovate to lanceolate in shape, with fine cilia along the edges.

Dichaea ciliolata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
