Dissotis tristemmoides

Dissotis tristemmoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaf marshmallow, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Commelinaceae family and is characterized by its erect, herbaceous stems and lanceolate leaves. Its fibrous roots and succulent leaves make it an important species in wetland ecosystems.
