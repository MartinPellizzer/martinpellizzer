Disticholiparis miniata

Disticholiparis miniata, commonly known as the miniature feather fern, is a small, delicate fern native to tropical regions.

It features finely divided, feathery fronds that give it a soft, airy appearance. The plant has a compact growth habit, making it suitable for small spaces or as a potted plant. Its leaves are typically bright green, with a slightly glossy texture.

Disticholiparis miniata thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil.
