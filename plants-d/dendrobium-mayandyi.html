Dendrobium mayandyi

Dendrobium mayandyi, commonly known as the Mayandyi orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with thick, fleshy rhizomes that store water and nutrients. The plant produces inflorescences bearing numerous small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts.
