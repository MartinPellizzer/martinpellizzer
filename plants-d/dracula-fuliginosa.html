Dracula fuliginosa

Dracula fuliginosa, commonly known as the black dracula, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Central America.

It is characterized by its dark, almost black, velvety leaves and striking, hooded flowers that resemble a bat's face. The plant produces small, pale yellow or white flowers that bloom during the dry season. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires high levels of light and good air circulation.

Dracula fuliginosa is a rare and highly sought-after orchid among plant enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and delicate beauty.
