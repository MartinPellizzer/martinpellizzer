Drypetes bawanii

Drypetes bawanii, commonly known as the Bawan tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and opposite, simple leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its timber and medicinal properties.
