Dolichos trilobus subsp. trilobus

Dolichos trilobus subsp.

trilobus, commonly known as the three-lobed cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions. It is a legume characterized by its trifoliate leaves with three deeply lobed leaflets.

The plant produces cylindrical, hairy pods that contain edible seeds used in various cuisines. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its nutritional value.

Its growth habit makes it suitable for both ornamental and agricultural purposes.
