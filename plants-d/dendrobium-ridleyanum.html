Dendrobium ridleyanum

Dendrobium ridleyanum, commonly known as the Ridley's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clustered pseudobulbs and long, narrow leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with a red or purple lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Dendrobium ridleyanum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
