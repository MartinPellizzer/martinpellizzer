Diastema gloxiniflorum

Diastema gloxiniflorum, commonly known as the gloxinia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that come in a variety of vibrant colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant.

Diastema gloxiniflorum is known for its ability to produce numerous blooms during the growing season.
