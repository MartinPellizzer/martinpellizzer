Dryopteris varia var. sacrosancta

Dryopteris varia var. sacrosancta, commonly known as the sacred fern, is a variant of the variable wood fern.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are typically bipinnate, with a distinctive pattern of sori along the margins of the pinnae. This fern thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in temperate forests. Its leaves are usually a deep green, with a slightly curled appearance that gives it a unique aesthetic.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
