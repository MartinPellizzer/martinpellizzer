Deutzia discolor var. excellens

Deutzia discolor var. excellens, commonly known as the white deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features showy clusters of white flowers that appear in early spring, often before the leaves emerge. The plant has slender, arching branches and small, ovate leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
