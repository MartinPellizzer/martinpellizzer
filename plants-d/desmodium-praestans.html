Desmodium praestans

Desmodium praestans, commonly known as the showy desmodium, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small, delicate flowers. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. It is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its nitrogen-fixing capabilities.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
