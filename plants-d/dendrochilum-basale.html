Dendrochilum basale

Dendrochilum basale, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant features a rhizome with fleshy, strap-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. Its flowers are typically greenish-yellow with a distinctive, spurred lip, and it blooms during the warmer months.

Dendrochilum basale is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in shaded, humid environments.
