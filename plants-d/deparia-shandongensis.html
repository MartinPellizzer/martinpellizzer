Deparia shandongensis

Deparia shandongensis, commonly known as the Shandong shield fern, is a species of fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its leaves are usually dark green and have a leathery texture.
