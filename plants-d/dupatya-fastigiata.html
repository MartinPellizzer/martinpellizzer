Dupatya fastigiata

Dupatya fastigiata, commonly known as the upright fern, is a species of flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, narrow growth habit and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal. The plant features compound leaves that are arranged in a whorled pattern along the stems. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is typically found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Dupatya fastigiata is known for its ability to form dense foliage, making it suitable for use in landscaping and garden design.
