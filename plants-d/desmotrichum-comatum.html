Desmotrichum comatum

Desmotrichum comatum, commonly known as the desert daisy, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, succulent-like growth habit and produces small, clustered yellow flowers. The plant has fleshy, spiny leaves that help reduce water loss in dry environments. It is often found growing on rocky outcrops and in sandy soils.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to survive in harsh, low-water conditions.
