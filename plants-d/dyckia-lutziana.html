Dyckia lutziana

Dyckia lutziana, commonly known as the Lutz's dyckia, is a species of flowering plant in the bromeliad family.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its compact rosette of stiff, grass-like leaves. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences that bear small, white to pinkish flowers. Dyckia lutziana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
