Dendrobium chrysobulbon

Dendrobium chrysobulbon, commonly known as the golden torch orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with dense clusters of dark green leaves. The plant produces vibrant yellow-orange flowers with a distinctive red throat, blooming during the summer months. It thrives in humid, well-drained environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Dendrobium chrysobulbon is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its long-lasting floral display.
