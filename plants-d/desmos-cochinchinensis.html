Desmos cochinchinensis

Desmos cochinchinensis, commonly known as the Vietnamese rose or pink sandalwood, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly serrated margin.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine.
