Deutzia calycosa

Deutzia calycosa, commonly known as the common deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features clusters of fragrant, white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, arching branches and small, elliptical leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Deutzia calycosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
