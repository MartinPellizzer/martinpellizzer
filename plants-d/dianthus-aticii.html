Dianthus aticii

Dianthus aticii, commonly known as the Aticii campion, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink or red flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its stems are erect and branched.

Dianthus aticii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
