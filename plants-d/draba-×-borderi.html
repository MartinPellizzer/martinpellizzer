Draba × borderi

Draba × borderi, commonly known as the hybrid wallflower, is a member of the Brassicaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial plant with small, bright yellow or white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically reaches a height of 10–30 cm and has basal rosettes of linear to lanceolate leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Draba × borderi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens.
