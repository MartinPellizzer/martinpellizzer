Diospyros simii

Diospyros simii, commonly known as the African persimmon, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and elongated, elliptical leaves. The plant is also noted for its ability to thrive in dry conditions, making it an important species in arid ecosystems.
