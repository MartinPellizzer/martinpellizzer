Dichanthelium aciculare subsp. neuranthum

Dichanthelium aciculare subsp.

neuranthum, commonly known as the narrowleaf panicgrass, is a grass species native to the southeastern United States. It is a perennial plant characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves.

The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in moist to wet habitats, such as swamps and marshes, and is often found in coastal areas.

Its botanical features make it an important component of wetland ecosystems.
