Dictyanthes stapeliiflora

Dictyanthes stapeliiflora, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant is typically evergreen and thrives in warm, humid environments. It produces vibrant blooms in shades of purple, pink, and white, often with a distinctive hood-like structure.
