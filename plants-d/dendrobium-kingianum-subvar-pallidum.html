Dendrobium kingianum subvar. pallidum

Dendrobium kingianum subvar. pallidum, commonly known as the pale orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces inflorescences of pale yellow to white flowers with a distinctive greenish hue. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique floral structure and ecological role in native Australian ecosystems.
