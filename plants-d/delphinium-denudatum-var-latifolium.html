Delphinium denudatum var. latifolium

Delphinium denudatum var. latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf larkspur, is a herbaceous perennial native to the western United States.

It features tall, sparsely branched stems that bear clusters of spurred blue or purple flowers. The leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough, hairy texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soils.

This plant is part of the Ranunculaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
