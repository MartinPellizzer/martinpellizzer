Dombeya elliptica

Dombeya elliptica, commonly known as the oval dombeya, is a deciduous shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has elliptical, dark green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Dombeya elliptica is also valued for its fragrant foliage and ornamental appeal.
