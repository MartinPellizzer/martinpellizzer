Dalbergia cultrata var. tavoyensis

Dalbergia cultrata var. tavoyensis, commonly known as Tavoy ebony, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its dark, dense wood with a rich, aromatic scent. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. Its fruit is a legume pod containing several flat, dark seeds.

This species is valued for its high-quality timber and is often found in lowland evergreen forests.
