Digitaria diffusa

Digitaria diffusa, commonly known as flatseam grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its flat, narrow leaves and dense, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are typically wind-pollinated. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

Digitaria diffusa is noted for its resilience and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
