Diuris flavopurpurea

Diuris flavopurpurea, commonly known as the purple-flowered spider-orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Australia.

It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils in heathlands and open woodlands. The plant features a slender, branching stem with several narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its most distinctive characteristic is its striking, spider-like flowers that are typically purple or pink in color.

Diuris flavopurpurea is a perennial herb that blooms during the spring and summer months.
