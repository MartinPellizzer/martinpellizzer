Diuris tinkeri

Diuris tinkeri, commonly known as the Tinkeri Spider-orchid, is a striking member of the orchid family native to Australia.

It is characterized by its intricate, spider-like appearance with long, slender sepals and petals. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in shaded or semi-shaded environments. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow, with a distinctive hood-like structure over the column.

Diuris tinkeri is a perennial herb that produces a single spike of flowers, making it a prized addition to specialized horticultural collections.
