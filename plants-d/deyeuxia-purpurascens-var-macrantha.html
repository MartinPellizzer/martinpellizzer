Deyeuxia purpurascens var. macrantha

Deyeuxia purpurascens var. macrantha, commonly known as the purple false rice, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wetland or marshy habitats, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a bluish-green hue.

The variety macrantha is distinguished by its larger, more prominent flowers compared to the typical form of Deyeuxia purpurascens.
