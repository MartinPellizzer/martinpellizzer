Dendrobium nebularum

Dendrobium nebularum, commonly known as the nebular orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, branching stems and clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments on trees or rocks. Its leaves are elongated and often covered in a waxy coating to retain moisture.

Dendrobium nebularum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its showy blooms.
