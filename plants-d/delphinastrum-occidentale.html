Delphinastrum occidentale

Delphinastrum occidentale, commonly known as western bluecurls, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique flower structure, with curled petals, adds ornamental value to native plant gardens.
