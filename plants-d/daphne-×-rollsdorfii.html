Daphne × rollsdorfii

Daphne × rollsdorfii, commonly known as the winter daphne, is a hybrid plant native to Europe.

It is characterized by its fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant produces small, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Daphne × rollsdorfii is valued for its ornamental appeal and winter blooming habit.
