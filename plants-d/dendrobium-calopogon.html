Dendrobium calopogon

Dendrobium calopogon, commonly known as the yellow lady's slipper, is a terrestrial orchid native to the eastern United States.

It features a unique inflorescence with a single, showy yellow pouch-like flower that resembles a slipper. The plant has a short, erect stem with a few leaves and produces a single, large, yellow flower with a dark red or purple lip. It thrives in moist, shaded habitats and is often found in woodlands and bogs.

The species is notable for its striking appearance and is considered a rare and endangered orchid in its native range.
