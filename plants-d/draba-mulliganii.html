Draba mulliganii

Draba mulliganii, commonly known as the Mulligan's whitlow grass, is a small flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

The plant produces delicate white or pale yellow flowers in early spring, which are clustered in dense, umbel-like inflorescences. Its leaves are basal, rosette-forming, and covered in a fine layer of silky hairs.
