Dimorphotheca caulescens

Dimorphotheca caulescens, commonly known as the yellow starburry, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces bright yellow flowers with a star-shaped appearance. The plant has hairy, glandular leaves that are typically arranged in pairs along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Dimorphotheca caulescens is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
