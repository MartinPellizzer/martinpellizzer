Delphinium lineapetalum subsp. klickitatense

Delphinium lineapetalum subsp.

klickitatense, commonly known as the Klickitat delphinium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Pacific Northwest of North America. It is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height.

The plant produces striking blue to purple flowers in racemes, which bloom in late summer. Its foliage consists of palmately compound leaves with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.
