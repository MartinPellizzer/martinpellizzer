Dianthus attenuatus var. robustus

Dianthus attenuatus var. robustus, commonly known as the robust carnation, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces clusters of showy, pink to red flowers. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Dianthus attenuatus var. robustus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms.
