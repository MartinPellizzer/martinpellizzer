Deyeuxia deserticola var. breviaristata

Deyeuxia deserticola var. breviaristata, commonly known as the short-bracted desert grass, is a perennial grass species native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia.

It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to withstand extreme temperature fluctuations and limited water availability. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny grains. It plays a role in stabilizing soil in desert environments and is often found in dunes and rocky areas.
