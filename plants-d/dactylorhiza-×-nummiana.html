Dactylorhiza × nummiana

Dactylorhiza × nummiana, commonly known as the marsh orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a perennial herb with tall, erect stems that bear numerous flowers in shades of pink, purple, and white. The plant has a complex root system and is often found in wet or marshy habitats. Its flowers are typically arranged in a raceme and are pollinated by insects.

Dactylorhiza × nummiana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
