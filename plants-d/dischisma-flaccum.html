Dischisma flaccum

Dischisma flaccum, commonly known as the golden aster, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant typically grows in rocky soils and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its vibrant yellow flowers bloom in the summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
