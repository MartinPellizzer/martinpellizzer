Dodonaea rigida

Dodonaea rigida, commonly known as the rigid Dodonaea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green in color and have a rough texture. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in spikes, which bloom in spring. Its bark is fibrous and peels in thin layers, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Dodonaea rigida is often used in landscaping for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in dry conditions.
