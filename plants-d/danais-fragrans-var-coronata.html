Danais fragrans var. coronata

Danais fragrans var. coronata, commonly known as the fragrant danais or crown danais, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Acanthaceae.

It is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant is native to tropical regions of Africa and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
