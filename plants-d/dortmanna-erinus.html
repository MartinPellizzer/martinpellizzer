Dortmanna erinus

Dortmanna erinus, commonly known as the ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.

Dortmanna erinus is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping, making it a popular choice in drought-tolerant landscaping.
