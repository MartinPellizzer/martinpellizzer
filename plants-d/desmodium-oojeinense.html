Desmodium oojeinense

Desmodium oojeinense, commonly known as the African velvet bean, is a leguminous plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant produces pods that contain several elliptical seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and woodland areas.

Desmodium oojeinense is also valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, which benefits soil fertility.
