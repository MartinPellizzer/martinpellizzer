Dolichos polystachios

Dolichos polystachios, commonly known as the wild cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers that give way to long, slender pods containing edible seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in poor soil conditions.

Dolichos polystachios is valued for its nutritional seeds and is used in traditional cuisines and as a forage crop.
