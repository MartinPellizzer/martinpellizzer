Drimia echinostachya

Drimia echinostachya, commonly known as the woolly dragon's beard, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its rosette of sword-shaped, hairy leaves. The plant produces striking clusters of white to pale purple flowers during the summer months. Native to southern Africa, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
