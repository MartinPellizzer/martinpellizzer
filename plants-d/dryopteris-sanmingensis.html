Dryopteris sanmingensis

Dryopteris sanmingensis, commonly known as the Sanming water fern, is a species of fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its leaves are bi-lobed and have a distinctive, intricate pattern of sori, which are clusters of spore-producing structures.

This species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological adaptations.
