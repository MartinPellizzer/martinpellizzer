Dracula fuligifera

Dracula fuligifera, commonly known as the black velvet orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black petals and sepals, which give it its dramatic appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom during the rainy season. It thrives in high-altitude, humid environments with filtered sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance and limited habitat, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists.
