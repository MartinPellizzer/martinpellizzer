Dictyanthus aeneus

Dictyanthus aeneus, commonly known as the copperleaf.

This plant is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The leaves are ovate and have a distinctive copper-colored sheen, which gives the plant its common name.

The flowers are small and clustered, usually in terminal panicles, with a pale greenish-yellow color.
