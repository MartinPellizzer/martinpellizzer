Dendrobium bicornutum

Dendrobium bicornutum, commonly known as the two-horned orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, cane-like pseudobulbs and long, strap-shaped leaves. The plant produces inflorescences with numerous fragrant, showy flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Dendrobium bicornutum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
