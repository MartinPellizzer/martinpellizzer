Doodia caudata var. squarrosa

Doodia caudata var. squarrosa, commonly known as the squarrosa fern, is a variant of the common sword fern.

It is a perennial plant native to eastern Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea. The plant features delicate, pinnately divided fronds that are often covered in a fine, squamose coating, giving it its varietal name. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
