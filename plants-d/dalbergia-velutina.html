Dalbergia velutina

Dalbergia velutina, commonly known as African rosewood, is a tropical tree native to Central and Southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dark, durable wood that is highly valued for its aesthetic and functional properties. The plant has a straight trunk and lanceolate leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. It produces small, yellowish-white flowers followed by flat, pod-like fruits containing several seeds.

Dalbergia velutina is often found in moist, well-drained soils in forested areas.
