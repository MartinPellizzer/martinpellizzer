Dendrobium hendersonii

Dendrobium hendersonii, commonly known as the Henderson's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, cane-like stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange, typically during the summer months. Its leaves are elongated and strap-like, arranged spirally along the stem.

Dendrobium hendersonii thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.
