Deyeuxia billardierei var. tenuis

Deyeuxia billardierei var. tenuis, commonly known as narrowleaf fingergrass, is a grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in sandy or loamy soils and is often found in coastal or inland grasslands.
