Dimorphanthera arfakensis

Dimorphanthera arfakensis, commonly known as the Arfaken orchid, is a rare and endemic species found in the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in rocky crevices, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, adapted to the harsh mountain environment.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
