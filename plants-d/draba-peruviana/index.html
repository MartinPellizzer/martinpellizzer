Draba Peruviana

Draba peruviana is a small, hardy plant native to the high altitudes of the Andes. It is known for its delicate, cup-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white and purple. This species thrives in cold, rocky environments and is often found in alpine meadows. Its compact size and resilience make it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine collections. Despite its modest appearance, Draba peruviana holds ecological significance in its native habitat.
