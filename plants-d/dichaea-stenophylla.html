Dichaea stenophylla

Dichaea stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf dichaea, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, elongated leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale green in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
