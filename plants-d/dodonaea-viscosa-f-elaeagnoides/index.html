Dodonaea Viscosa F. Elaeagnoides

Dodonaea viscosa f. elaeagnoides is a unique variant of the commonly known Dodonaea viscosa, often referred to as the silver wattle. This shrub is native to Australia and thrives in coastal and dry inland regions. It is characterized by its small, leathery leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions. It is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
