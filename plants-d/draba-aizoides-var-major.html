Draba aizoides var. major

Draba aizoides var. major, commonly known as the alpine bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and parts of Asia.

The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale blue, which bloom in early spring. Its leaves are basal, rosette-like, and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs, helping to retain moisture in harsh conditions.
