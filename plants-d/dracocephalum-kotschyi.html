Dracocephalum kotschyi

Dracocephalum kotschyi, commonly known as the Persian bugloss, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Iran and Afghanistan.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features erect stems covered in fine hairs and produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Dracocephalum kotschyi is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for its potential anti-inflammatory and antispasmodic effects.
