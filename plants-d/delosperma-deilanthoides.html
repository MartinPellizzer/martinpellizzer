Delosperma deilanthoides

Delosperma deilanthoides, commonly known as the pink iceplant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink to purple, typically blooming in spring. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, reducing water loss through transpiration.

Delosperma deilanthoides is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its drought tolerance and vibrant floral display.
