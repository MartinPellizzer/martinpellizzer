Dendrobium nitidiflorum

Dendrobium nitidiflorum, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, branched stem covered in dense, velvety brownish-green foliage. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with prominent, showy petals and a distinctive lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Dendrobium nitidiflorum is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its elegant floral display.
