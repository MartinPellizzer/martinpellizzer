Dimorphandra speciosa

Dimorphandra speciosa, commonly known as the showy dimorphandra, is a species of legume native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant has compound leaves with alternating leaflets and produces pods that contain seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in dry forest or woodland habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
