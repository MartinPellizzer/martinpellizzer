Dolichos cardiophyllus var. cardiophyllus

Dolichos cardiophyllus var. cardiophyllus, commonly known as the heart-leaf dolichos, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which are typically large and deeply lobed. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by elongated, flat pods containing kidney-shaped seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
