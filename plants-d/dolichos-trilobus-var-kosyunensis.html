Dolichos trilobus var. kosyunensis

Dolichos trilobus var. kosyunensis, commonly known as the Kosyun bean, is a climbing legume native to parts of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and elongated, bean-like pods that contain small, edible seeds. The plant thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its nutritional value. It has a deep root system that helps prevent soil erosion.

The flowers are typically pale yellow, and the plant is known for its adaptability to various soil types.
