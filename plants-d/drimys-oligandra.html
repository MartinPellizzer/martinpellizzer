Drimys oligandra

Drimys oligandra, commonly known as the Chilean wintergreen, is a flowering plant native to southern Chile and Argentina.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows in moist, temperate forests. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a glossy surface and produces small, white flowers that bloom in spring. Its fruit is a dark blue to black berry, which contains several seeds.

Drimys oligandra is valued for its aromatic foliage and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
