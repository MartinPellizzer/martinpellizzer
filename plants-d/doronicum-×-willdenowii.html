Doronicum × willdenowii

Doronicum × willdenowii, commonly known as the hybrid leopard's bane, is a perennial plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is a hybrid between Doronicum orientale and Doronicum beggerianum, exhibiting characteristics of both parent species. The plant is noted for its vibrant yellow flowers, which are typically tubular with a darker central throat, resembling a leopard's eye. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long blooming period.
