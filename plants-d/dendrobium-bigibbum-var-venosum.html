Dendrobium bigibbum var. venosum

Dendrobium bigibbum var. venosum, commonly known as the veiny orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic orchid that thrives in humid, tropical environments. The plant features long, cane-like pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are typically yellow or green with intricate veining, giving it its common name.

It is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to cultivation.
