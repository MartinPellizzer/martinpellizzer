Dolichostemon verticillatus

Dolichostemon verticillatus, commonly known as the spiral herb, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its whorled arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Dolichostemon verticillatus is valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
