Digitaria phaeothrix var. hackelii

Digitaria phaeothrix var. hackelii, commonly known as the Hackel's Digitaria, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides, fields, and wetlands. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves with a bluish-green color and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is characterized by its ability to thrive in moist environments and is often found in lowland areas.
