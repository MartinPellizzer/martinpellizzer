Dichaea australis

Dichaea australis, commonly known as the Australian dichaea, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. The plant features a short, stocky rhizome with a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered, often in a spike, with pale green to white petals.

Dichaea australis is noted for its ability to thrive in humid conditions and is a popular species among orchid enthusiasts.
