Dendrobium tribuloides

Dendrobium tribuloides, commonly known as the spiky orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with multiple pseudobulbs covered in a dense, fibrous sheath. The plant produces fragrant, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are strap-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern around the pseudobulbs.

Dendrobium tribuloides is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and adaptability to various growing conditions.
