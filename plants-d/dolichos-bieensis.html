Dolichos bieensis

Dolichos bieensis, commonly known as the Bie's cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and trifoliate leaves with small, heart-shaped leaflets. The plant produces elongated, pod-like fruits that contain several edible seeds. It is valued for its nutritional content, including high protein and fiber, making it an important crop in some parts of Asia.

Dolichos bieensis is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
