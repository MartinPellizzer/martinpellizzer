Dendrobium kaniense

Dendrobium kaniense, commonly known as the Kani Island orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in tropical rainforests. The plant features long, cane-like stems that are often covered in clusters of small, fragrant flowers. Its blossoms are usually yellow or greenish-yellow with a distinctive red or purple lip.

Dendrobium kaniense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
