Dendrobium linearifolium

Dendrobium linearifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, linear leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, and it produces striking clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.

Dendrobium linearifolium is also valued for its role in traditional medicine in some cultures.
