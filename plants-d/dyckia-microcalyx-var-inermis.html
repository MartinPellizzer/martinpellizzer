Dyckia microcalyx var. inermis

Dyckia microcalyx var. inermis, commonly known as the woolly dyckia, is a compact, evergreen bromeliad native to Brazil.

It features dense, dark green leaves that are covered in fine, white trichomes, giving them a fuzzy appearance. The plant produces striking, red-pink inflorescences that emerge from the center of the rosette. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.

Its unique foliage and ornamental flowers make it a standout addition to both indoor and outdoor collections.
