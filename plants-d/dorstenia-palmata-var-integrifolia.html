Dorstenia palmata var. integrifolia

Dorstenia palmata var. integrifolia, commonly known as the smooth-leafed false arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its palmately lobed leaves, which are typically smooth and have a glossy appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
