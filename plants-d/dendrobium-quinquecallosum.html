Dendrobium quinquecallosum

Dendrobium quinquecallosum, commonly known as the five-chambered orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome that produces multiple pseudobulbs. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink, often with a distinct lip. Its leaves are usually elliptical and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
