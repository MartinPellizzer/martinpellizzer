Dichanthelium linearifolium var. werneri

Dichanthelium linearifolium var. werneri, commonly known as Werneri's slendergrass, is a grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and compact growth habit. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in open woodlands and along stream banks. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to brown flowers.

This variety is often found in shaded to partially sunny environments and is adapted to humid, subtropical climates.
