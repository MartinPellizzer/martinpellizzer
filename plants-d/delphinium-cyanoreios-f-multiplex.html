Delphinium cyanoreios f. multiplex

Delphinium cyanoreios f. multiplex, commonly known as the blue delphinium hybrid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems and clusters of blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in temperate climates. Its leaves are deeply lobed and arranged alternately along the stem.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens and for its ability to attract pollinators.
