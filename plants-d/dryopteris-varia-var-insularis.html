Dryopteris varia var. insularis

Dryopteris varia var. insularis, commonly known as the Insular Shield Fern, is a variant of the variable shield fern native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It is a medium-sized fern with fronds that are typically bipinnate, featuring a distinctive shield-like appearance. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. Its leaves are usually dark green and have a leathery texture, with spores produced on the underside of the fronds.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with suitable humidity and light conditions.
