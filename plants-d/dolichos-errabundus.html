Dolichos errabundus

Dolichos errabundus, commonly known as the wandering bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features compound leaves with three to five oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellowish flowers. The plant bears elongated, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in warm climates and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable species for ecological studies and potential agricultural use.
