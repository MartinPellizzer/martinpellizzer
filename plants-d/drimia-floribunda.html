Drimia floribunda

Drimia floribunda, commonly known as the African lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features strap-like, grassy leaves that are typically dark green and somewhat succulent. The plant produces clusters of white or pale blue flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Drimia floribunda is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticulture for its elegant appearance.
