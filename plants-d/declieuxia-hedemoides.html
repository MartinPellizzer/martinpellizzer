Declieuxia hedemoides

Declieuxia hedemoides, commonly known as the silver cotoneaster, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by bright red berries in autumn. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves that provide a striking contrast to its delicate flowers. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Declieuxia hedemoides is relatively drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil.
