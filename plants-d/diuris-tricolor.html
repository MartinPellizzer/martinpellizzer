Diuris tricolor

Diuris tricolor, commonly known as the three-coloured spider-orchid, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, spider-like appearance. The plant produces strikingly colored flowers with three distinct hues, typically blue, purple, and white. It thrives in sandy soils and is often found in heathlands and woodlands.

Diuris tricolor is a short-lived perennial, with a flowering period that occurs during the cooler months.
