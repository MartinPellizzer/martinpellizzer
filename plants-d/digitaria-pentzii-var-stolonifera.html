Digitaria pentzii var. stolonifera

Digitaria pentzii var. stolonifera, commonly known as匍匐狗尾草, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features tufted, cylindrical inflorescences with spikelets that are often reddish in color. The plant has a stoloniferous growth habit, producing horizontal stems that root at the nodes and form dense mats. Its leaves are narrow, flat, and hairy, with a bluish-green hue.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas, lawns, and along roadsides.
