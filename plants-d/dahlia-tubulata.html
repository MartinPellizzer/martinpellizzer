Dahlia tubulata

Dahlia tubulata, commonly known as the tubular dahlia, is a species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular-shaped flowers that come in a variety of vibrant colors. The plant produces showy blooms that are typically tubular or spathaceous in form, with petals that are often reflexed at the tips. It is a herbaceous perennial that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

The tubular flowers are not only ornamental but also attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
