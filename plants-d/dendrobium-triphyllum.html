Dendrobium triphyllum

Dendrobium triphyllum, commonly known as the three-leaf orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its three leaves, which are typically elliptical and slightly fleshy. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrobium triphyllum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
