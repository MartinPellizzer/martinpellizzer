Dillenia pentagyna

Dillenia pentagyna, commonly known as the five-lobed dillenia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, palmate leaves with five lobes and showy, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces fleshy, yellowish fruits that are edible and often used in local cuisines. Its botanical features make it a popular ornamental plant in gardens and botanical collections.

Dillenia pentagyna is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
