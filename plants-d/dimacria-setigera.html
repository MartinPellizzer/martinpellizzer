Dimacria setigera

Dimacria setigera, commonly known as the zigzag grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems that often exhibit a zigzag pattern, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, white to pale blue flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a slightly hairy surface.

Dimacria setigera is often found in grassland habitats and is adapted to dry, well-drained soils.
