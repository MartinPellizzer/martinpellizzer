Dendrobium cuthbertsonii

Dendrobium cuthbertsonii, commonly known as the Cuthbertson's orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to the rainforests of Queensland, Australia.

It features a compact, branching structure with numerous small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellow-green flowers with prominent red sepals and petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium cuthbertsonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
