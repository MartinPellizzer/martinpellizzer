Dransfieldia micrantha

Dransfieldia micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered grass, is a member of the grass family Poaceae.

It is a low-growing perennial plant characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. Native to southern Africa, it thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. The plant has a compact growth habit and is typically less than 30 centimeters tall.
