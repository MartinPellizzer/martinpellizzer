Dracocephalum angustifolium

Dracocephalum angustifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaved blueweed, is a flowering plant native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. It often grows in dry, rocky soils and is sometimes found in meadows and grasslands.

Dracocephalum angustifolium is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
