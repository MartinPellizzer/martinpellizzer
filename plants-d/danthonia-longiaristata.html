Danthonia longiaristata

Danthonia longiaristata, commonly known as the slender danthonia, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its fine, grass-like leaves and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and heathlands. Its botanical features include a fibrous root system and narrow, linear leaves that are often hairy along the edges.

Danthonia longiaristata is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in stabilizing soil in native landscapes.
