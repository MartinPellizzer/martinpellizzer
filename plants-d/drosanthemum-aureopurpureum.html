Drosanthemum aureopurpureum

Drosanthemum aureopurpureum, commonly known as the purple-flowered rock daisy, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rounded rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of purple and orange, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value make it a favored plant among succulent enthusiasts.
