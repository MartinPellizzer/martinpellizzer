Dichrocephala integrifolia

Dichrocephala integrifolia, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant has a hairy, upright stem and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Dichrocephala integrifolia is also known for its medicinal properties, used in traditional herbal remedies.
