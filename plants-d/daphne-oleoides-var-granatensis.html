Daphne oleoides var. granatensis

Daphne oleoides var. granatensis, commonly known as the granate daphne, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features aromatic, dark green, leathery leaves that are densely packed on its stems. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to white, typically blooming in early spring. Its ornamental value comes from its compact form and long-lasting blooms.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
