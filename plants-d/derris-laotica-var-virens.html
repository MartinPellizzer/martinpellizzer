Derris laotica var. virens

Derris laotica var. virens, commonly known as the greenleaf derris, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Leguminosae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces pods containing flat, elongated seeds. It is often found in moist, tropical environments and is adapted to thrive in shaded conditions.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both ecological studies and traditional medicine.
