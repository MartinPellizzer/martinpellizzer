Dracocephalum nuttallii

Dracocephalum nuttallii, commonly known as Nuttall's blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features square stems and opposite leaves that are typically gray-green in color. Its small, blue to purple flowers are clustered in whorls along the stems.

Dracocephalum nuttallii is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to thrive in arid environments.
