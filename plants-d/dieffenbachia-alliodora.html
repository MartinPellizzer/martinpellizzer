Dieffenbachia alliodora

Dieffenbachia alliodora, commonly known as the silver stripe dieffenbachia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that feature white and green stripes. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other popular houseplants like peace lilies. Its foliage is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its aesthetic appeal.

Dieffenbachia alliodora thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to maintain its health.
