Desmodium megaphyllum var. glabrescens

Desmodium megaphyllum var. glabrescens, commonly known as the woolly desmodium, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, with each leaflet being ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodland or grassland habitats.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
