Dracaenopsis lineata

Dracaenopsis lineata, commonly known as the stripeleaf dracaena, is a species of flowering plant in the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and the Philippines. Dracaenopsis lineata prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
