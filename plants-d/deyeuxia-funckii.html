Deyeuxia funckii

Deyeuxia funckii, commonly known as the Funck's false panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa.

It typically grows in moist, grassy areas and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is often found in wetlands and along riverbanks, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil.

Botanically, it is part of the Poaceae family, which includes many economically important grasses.
