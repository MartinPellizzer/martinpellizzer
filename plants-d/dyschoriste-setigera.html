Dyschoriste setigera

Dyschoriste setigera, commonly known as the hairy dyschoriste, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with hairy surfaces, which give it its common name. Its flowers are small, clustered in inflorescences, and are usually pale yellow or greenish in color.

The plant is often found in moist, disturbed habitats such as roadside banks and riverbanks.
