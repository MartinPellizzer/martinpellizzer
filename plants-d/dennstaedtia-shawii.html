Dennstaedtia shawii

Dennstaedtia shawii, commonly known as the Shaw's shield fern, is a species of fern native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the family Dennstaedtiaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and woodlands. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a leathery texture.

The spore-producing structures, called sori, are located on the underside of the fronds, contributing to its reproductive strategy.
