Dolichos minutiflorus

Dolichos minutiflorus, commonly known as the small-flowered dolichos, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features slender, twining stems and small, clustered greenish-white flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant produces elongated, cylindrical pods that contain several kidney-shaped seeds. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three oval-shaped leaflets, and it thrives in warm, humid climates.

Dolichos minutiflorus is often used in traditional medicine and as a forage crop for livestock.
