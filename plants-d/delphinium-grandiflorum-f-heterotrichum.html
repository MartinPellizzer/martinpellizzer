Delphinium grandiflorum f. heterotrichum

Delphinium grandiflorum f. heterotrichum, commonly known as the heterotrichum delphinium, is a horticultural form of the tall delphinium.

It is characterized by its striking, tall spikes of blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is a perennial herbaceous species native to the mountainous regions of the western United States. It has lance-shaped leaves that are often more densely arranged than those of the typical species.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
