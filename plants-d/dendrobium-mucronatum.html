Dendrobium mucronatum

Dendrobium mucronatum, commonly known as the spike orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with cane-like stems that can reach up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are elongated and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

It requires bright, indirect light and moderate humidity to thrive.
