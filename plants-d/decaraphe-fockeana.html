Decaraphe fockeana

Decaraphe fockeana, commonly known as the Fockeana orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces striking, showy flowers with a unique coloration. The plant typically features long, slender petals and a distinctive lip that is often brightly colored. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
