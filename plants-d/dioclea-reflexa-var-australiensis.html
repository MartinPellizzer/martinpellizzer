Dioclea reflexa var. australiensis

Dioclea reflexa var. australiensis, commonly known as the Australian climbing vine, is a species native to Australia.

It is a woody vine characterized by its long, trailing stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by bright red, fleshy fruits. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in coastal areas and disturbed soils.

Due to its rapid growth and ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated as a garden plant.
