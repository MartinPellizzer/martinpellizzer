Duranta repens f. acuminata

Duranta repens f. acuminata, commonly known as the silverleaf duranta, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the Caribbean and northern South America.

It features small, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a fine, silvery pubescence, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of tubular, blue or violet flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. It is well adapted to tropical and subtropical climates, thriving in full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and as a container plant.
