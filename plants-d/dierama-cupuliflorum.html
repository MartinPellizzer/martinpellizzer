Dierama cupuliflorum

Dierama cupuliflorum, commonly known as the helmet lily, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, grass-like leaves that form a dense rosette, with a distinctive purple or pink flower spike emerging from the center. The flowers are clustered in a helmet-shaped inflorescence, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

This plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
