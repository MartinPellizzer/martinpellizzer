Dolichos axillaris var. glaber

Dolichos axillaris var. glaber, commonly known as the smooth dolichos, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, twining stems and trifoliate leaves with ovate leaflets. The flowers are small, yellowish, and borne in axillary clusters, while the fruit is a slender, spiral-shaped pod. The plant is characterized by its smooth, glabrous stem and leaves, which distinguish it from other varieties.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of forage.
