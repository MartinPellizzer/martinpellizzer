Didymocarpus tibeticus

Didymocarpus tibeticus, commonly known as the Tibetan date plum, is a deciduous shrub native to the Himalayan regions of Nepal, Bhutan, and China.

Due to its ornamental value and edible fruits, it is occasionally cultivated in horticultural settings.
