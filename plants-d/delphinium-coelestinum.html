Delphinium coelestinum

Delphinium coelestinum, commonly known as the blue delphinium, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of central Asia.

It produces tall spikes of vibrant blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its striking appearance and long blooming period make it a valuable addition to botanical collections and gardens.
