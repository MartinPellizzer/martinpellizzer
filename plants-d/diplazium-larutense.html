Diplazium larutense

Diplazium larutense, commonly known as the Larut spleenwort, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are typically less than 30 cm in length. The plant has a short, creeping rhizome and produces sporangia on the underside of its fertile fronds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Diplazium larutense is primarily found in Southeast Asia, including Malaysia and Indonesia.
