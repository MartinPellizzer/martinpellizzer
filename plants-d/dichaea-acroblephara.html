Dichaea acroblephara

Dichaea acroblephara, commonly known as the acroblephara bromeliad, is a species of flowering plant in the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup, which collects water and nutrients. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, often in shades of red or orange.

Dichaea acroblephara is adapted to grow in shaded, humid environments, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
