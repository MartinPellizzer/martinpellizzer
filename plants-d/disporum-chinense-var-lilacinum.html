Disporum chinense var. lilacinum

Disporum chinense var. lilacinum, commonly known as the lilac-flowered disporum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from pale lilac to white, often blooming in early spring. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
