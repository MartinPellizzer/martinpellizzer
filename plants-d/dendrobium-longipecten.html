Dendrobium longipecten

Dendrobium longipecten, commonly known as the long-leaf dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit, with multiple stems that grow horizontally and produce leaves along their length. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green, often with a distinctive comb-like pattern on the petals. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
