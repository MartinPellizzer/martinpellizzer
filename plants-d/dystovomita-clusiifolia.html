Dystovomita clusiifolia

Dystovomita clusiifolia, commonly known as the glossy leaf tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green and glossy in appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or yellow in color. Its fruit is a small, fleshy berry that contains several seeds.

Dystovomita clusiifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
