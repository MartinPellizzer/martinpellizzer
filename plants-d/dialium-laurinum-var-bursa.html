Dialium laurinum var. bursa

Dialium laurinum var. bursa, commonly known as the African plum or wild mango, is a small evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its elliptical, glossy leaves and fragrant white flowers.
