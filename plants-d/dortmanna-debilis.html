Dortmanna debilis

Dortmanna debilis, commonly known as the slender dock, is a small, herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides. The plant features narrow, alternate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, arranged in a compact inflorescence.

Dortmanna debilis is known for its ability to thrive in nutrient-poor soils and is often found in association with other wetland flora.
