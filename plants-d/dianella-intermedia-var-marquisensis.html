Dianella intermedia var. marquisensis

Dianella intermedia var. marquisensis, commonly known as the Marquisensis Flax Lily, is a striking evergreen plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a distinctive white midrib. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white or pale blue flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in tropical and subtropical gardens.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a valuable addition to coastal and island landscapes.
