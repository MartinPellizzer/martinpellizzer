Deutzia thunbergiana

Deutzia thunbergiana, commonly known as Thunberg's deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It features arching stems that bear clusters of fragrant, white flowers in early spring. The plant has elliptical, dark green leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and well-drained soil.

Deutzia thunbergiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
