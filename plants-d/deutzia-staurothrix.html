Deutzia staurothrix

Deutzia staurothrix, commonly known as the star-fruit deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features clusters of small, white to pink flowers that bloom in early spring, adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for a variety of garden settings.

Its compact growth habit and showy flowers make it a popular choice for landscaping and ornamental purposes.
