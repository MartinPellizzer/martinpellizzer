Drynaria Nuda

Drynaria nuda is a unique fern species known for its striking appearance and adaptability. Native to parts of Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. This plant is often found growing on rocky surfaces or in forest understories. Its slender, wiry fronds make it a popular choice for indoor gardening. With its elegant form and low maintenance needs, it's a favorite among plant enthusiasts.
