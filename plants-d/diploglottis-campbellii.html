Diploglottis campbellii

Diploglottis campbellii, commonly known as the Campbell's diploglottis, is a rare and striking plant native to the rainforests of northeastern Australia.

It is a climbing shrub characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom in spring. It is adapted to thrive in humid, shaded environments with high rainfall.
