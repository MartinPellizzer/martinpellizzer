Draba oreades var. tafellii

Draba oreades var. tafellii, commonly known as the alpine rockcress, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It typically reaches heights of 5 to 15 centimeters and features small, clustered leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces white or pale yellow flowers in early spring, which are borne on short, erect stems. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to cold, high-altitude environments.

Draba oreades var. tafellii is often found in mountainous areas, including the Alps and the Pyrenees.
