Drimia incerta

Drimia incerta, commonly known as the uncertain drimia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its strap-shaped, grass-like leaves. Drimia incerta is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
