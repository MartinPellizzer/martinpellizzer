Dioscorea trollii

Dioscorea trollii, commonly known as the Troll's yam, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its tuberous roots, which are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a rough texture.

Dioscorea trollii is valued for its potential medicinal properties, though it is not widely cultivated commercially.
