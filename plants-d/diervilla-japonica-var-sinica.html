Diervilla japonica var. sinica

Diervilla japonica var. sinica, commonly known as Chinese firethorn, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features dense, spiny branches and produces clusters of small, orange-red berries in the fall. The plant has dark green, elliptical leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant berries and winter interest.
