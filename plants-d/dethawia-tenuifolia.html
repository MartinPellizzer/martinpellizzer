Dethawia tenuifolia

Dethawia tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender leaf dethawia, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, pale blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or mountainous terrain.
