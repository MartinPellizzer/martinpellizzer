Disa cornuta

Disa cornuta, commonly known as the horned disa, is a striking orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has a single, erect stem that can reach up to 1 meter in height, with several racemes of flowers along its length. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in well-drained, sandy soils.

Disa cornuta is a popular ornamental plant due to its vibrant color and elegant appearance.
