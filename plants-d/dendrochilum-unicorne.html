Dendrochilum unicorne

Dendrochilum unicorne, commonly known as the unicorn orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, elongated flower spike that bears a single, strikingly shaped flower, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellowish with intricate patterns, and it blooms during the rainy season.

Dendrochilum unicorne is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
