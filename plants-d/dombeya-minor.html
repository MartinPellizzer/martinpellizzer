Dombeya minor

Dombeya minor, commonly known as the dwarf dombeya, is a deciduous shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Dombeya minor is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively low maintenance once established.
