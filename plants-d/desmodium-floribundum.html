Desmodium floribundum

Desmodium floribundum, commonly known as the purple crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant produces small, violet to purple flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in tropical and subtropical regions, often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it an important plant in soil enrichment and ecological restoration.
