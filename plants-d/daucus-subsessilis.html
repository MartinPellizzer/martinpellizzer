Daucus subsessilis

Daucus subsessilis, commonly known as the narrowleaf parsley or narrowleaf carrot, is a flowering plant in the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its finely divided, feathery leaves that are typically narrow and sessile, giving it a delicate appearance.
