Dendrobium biconvexum

Dendrobium biconvexum, commonly known as the helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a unique, elongated pseudobulb with a distinctive helmet-shaped structure at its apex. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a yellow lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrobium biconvexum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is commonly cultivated in tropical gardens.
