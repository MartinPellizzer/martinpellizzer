Dichaea robusta

Dichaea robusta, commonly known as the robust dichaea, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments within tropical forests. The plant features a short, erect stem with several leaves arranged in a rosette pattern. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and often have a faintly fragrant scent.

Dichaea robusta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
