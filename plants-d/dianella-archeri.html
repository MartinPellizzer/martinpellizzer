Dianella archeri

Dianella archeri, commonly known as the silver leaf lily, is a perennial evergreen plant native to New Zealand.

It features narrow, strap-like leaves that are typically silver or bluish-gray in color, providing a striking visual contrast. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in spring, followed by blue-black berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Dianella archeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens.
