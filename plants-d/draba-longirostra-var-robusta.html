Draba longirostra var. robusta

Draba longirostra var. robusta, commonly known as the robust draba, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in early spring. The plant features basal leaves that are lanceolate and often covered in a fine layer of white hairs. It thrives in rocky or alpine environments, preferring well-drained soils and full sun exposure.

This variety is notable for its resilience to harsh climatic conditions, making it a hardy species found in mountainous regions across Europe and Asia.
