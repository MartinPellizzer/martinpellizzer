Deutzia discolor

Deutzia discolor, commonly known as the white deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It typically grows to a height of 3 to 6 feet and prefers well-drained soil.

Deutzia discolor is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and easy maintenance.
