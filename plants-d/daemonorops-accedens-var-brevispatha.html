Daemonorops accedens var. brevispatha

Daemonorops accedens var. brevispatha, commonly known as the short-petioled cycad, is a unique cycad species native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its stout, unbranched trunk and pinnately compound leaves with short petioles. The plant produces large, cone-like structures, with the female cones being particularly distinctive in appearance. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value and conservation status, it is a subject of interest in both horticulture and botanical research.
