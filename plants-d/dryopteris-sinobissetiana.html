Dryopteris sinobissetiana

Dryopteris sinobissetiana, commonly known as the Chinese shield fern, is a species of fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a leathery texture.

The reproductive structures, known as sori, are located on the underside of the fronds and are covered by a protective indusium.
