Diplostephium tovarii

Diplostephium tovarii, commonly known as the Tovar's burrgrass, is a species of flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and spikelet structures. The plant produces small, wind-dispersed seeds enclosed in bracts, which aid in its propagation. It thrives in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.
