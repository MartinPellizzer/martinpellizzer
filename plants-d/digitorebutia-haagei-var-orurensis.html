Digitorebutia haagei var. orurensis

Digitorebutia haagei var. orurensis, commonly known as the golden crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow to orange flower heads. The plant features dense, woolly foliage that provides a soft texture to its appearance. It typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

The flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
