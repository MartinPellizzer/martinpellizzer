Dendrobium vogelsangii

Dendrobium vogelsangii, commonly known as the Vogelsang's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit, with cane-like stems that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow and green, often with a red or purple lip. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Dendrobium vogelsangii is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique floral display.
