Delphinium grandiflorum var. latisectum

Delphinium grandiflorum var. latisectum, commonly known as the broad-leaved larkspur, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It features tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The leaves are large, deeply lobed, and have a distinctive broad, dissected appearance. Its flowers are blue to purple in color, arranged in racemes, and are highly attractive to pollinators.

The plant is valued in gardens for its showy blooms and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
