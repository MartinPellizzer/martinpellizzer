Dolichos coccineus

Dolichos coccineus, commonly known as the red bengal bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and produces vibrant red or orange flowers. The plant has trifoliate leaves and bears pods containing edible seeds. It thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and nutritional seeds.

Dolichos coccineus is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
