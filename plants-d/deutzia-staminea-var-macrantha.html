Deutzia staminea var. macrantha

Deutzia staminea var. macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features showy, clustered white to pink flowers that bloom in early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to both landscape design and ecological habitats.
