Diplusodon burchellii

Diplusodon burchellii, commonly known as the Burchell's wild bean, is a climbing legume native to southern Africa.

It features compound leaves with small, ovate leaflets and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant bears pod-like fruits that contain edible seeds, which are often used in traditional cuisine. Diplusodon burchellii thrives in grassland and savanna habitats, often climbing shrubs or trees for support.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important plant in both ecological and culinary contexts.
