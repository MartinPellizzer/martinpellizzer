Dalea tuberculina

Dalea tuberculina, commonly known as the rough dalesia, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its spiny stems and small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. Dalea tuberculina is often found in desert scrub and chaparral habitats.
