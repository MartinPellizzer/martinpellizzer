Dendrobium kingianum var. pallidum

Dendrobium kingianum var. pallidum, commonly known as the pale dendrobium, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant that grows on trees and rocks, utilizing its aerial roots to absorb moisture and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, often in clusters. Its leaves are long and strap-like, and it thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is relatively easy to care for in cultivation.
