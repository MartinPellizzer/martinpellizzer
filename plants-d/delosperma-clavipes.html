Delosperma clavipes

Delosperma clavipes, commonly known as the Cape gooseberry or ice plant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves and clusters of small, star-shaped yellow flowers. The plant produces bright orange, berry-like fruits that are edible and often used in culinary applications. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for arid environments.

Delosperma clavipes is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to prevent soil erosion.
