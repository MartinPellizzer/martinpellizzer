Delphinium gorganicum

Delphinium gorganicum, commonly known as the garden delphinium, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, spiky inflorescences that bear showy, spurred flowers in shades of blue, purple, and white. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and is native to various regions in Europe and Asia. Its leaves are palmately divided, with a rough, hairy texture, and its roots are tuberous.

Delphinium gorganicum is cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
