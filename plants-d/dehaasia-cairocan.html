Dehaasia cairocan

Dehaasia cairocan, commonly known as the Cairo flameleaf, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to the arid regions of North Africa.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant shade of red or orange, which intensify under full sun exposure. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, adding to its visual appeal. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its unique foliage and seasonal blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardens and landscape design.
