Dactylorhiza elata var. brennensis

Dactylorhiza elata var. brennensis, commonly known as the Burnet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the British Isles.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of small, purple or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, acidic soils in woodland or heathland habitats. It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its delicate appearance and seasonal blooming.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and protected species in some regions.
