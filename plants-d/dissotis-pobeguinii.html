Dissotis pobeguinii

Dissotis pobeguinii, commonly known as the Christmas star, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped pink or purple flowers that bloom during the dry season. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, fleshy fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its unique appearance and seasonal blooming make it a notable species in the genus Dissotis.
