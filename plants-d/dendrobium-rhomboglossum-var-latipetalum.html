Dendrobium rhomboglossum var. latipetalum

Dendrobium rhomboglossum var. latipetalum, commonly known as the broad-petaled orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. The plant features elongated, strap-like leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers with broad petals. Its inflorescence consists of multiple blooms arranged in a raceme, often in shades of white, pink, or yellow.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated for horticultural purposes.
