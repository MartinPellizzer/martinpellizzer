Dichondra nivea

Dichondra nivea, commonly known as silverleaf dichondra, is a low-growing evergreen perennial native to South Africa.

It features small, rounded leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery-white coating, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale green, followed by small, round fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and ground covers.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a valuable addition to drought-tolerant landscapes.
