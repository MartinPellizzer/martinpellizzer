Didymocheton hornei var. hornei

Didymocheton hornei var. hornei, commonly known as the broad-leafed hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink to red in color, with five petals and a prominent central column. The leaves are broad, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface, while the stems are woody and often branched. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The plant is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
