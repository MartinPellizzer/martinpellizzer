Delosperma nelii

Delosperma nelii, commonly known as the silver frost ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, silver-green leaves that help it store water, adapting to arid environments. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white during the summer months. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its hardy nature and low maintenance requirements make it an attractive addition to drought-tolerant landscapes.
