Diospyros ebenoides

Diospyros ebenoides, commonly known as the African ebony, is a tropical tree native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its dark, dense wood that is highly valued for its strength and durability. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by small, fleshy fruits.

The tree is often found in moist, shaded areas and is known for its slow growth and long lifespan.
