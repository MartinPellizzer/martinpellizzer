Dioscorea heptaneura f. tenuicaulis

Dioscorea heptaneura f. tenuicaulis, commonly known as the slender-stemmed yam, is a variant of the yam family (Dioscoreaceae) native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems and heart-shaped leaves with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its tuberous roots are edible and have been used in traditional cuisines.

The species is valued not only for its nutritional content but also for its potential in pharmaceutical applications.
