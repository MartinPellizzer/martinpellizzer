Dielitzia

Dielitzia, commonly known as the African buttercup, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Fabaceae.

It is native to southern Africa and features herbaceous plants with trifoliate leaves. The flowers are typically yellow or white, with a distinctive papilionaceous structure. Dielitzia species are often found in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils.

They are valued for their ornamental appeal and are sometimes used in traditional medicine.
