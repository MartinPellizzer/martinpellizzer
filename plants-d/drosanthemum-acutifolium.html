Drosanthemum acutifolium

Drosanthemum acutifolium, commonly known as the sharp-leafed drosanthemum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value, it is widely cultivated in arid regions.
