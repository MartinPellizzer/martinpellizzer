Dactylorhiza latifolia subsp. cambrensis

Dactylorhiza latifolia subsp.

cambrensis, commonly known as the Welsh broad-leaf orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the British Isles. It typically grows in moist, calcareous habitats such as woodlands and grasslands.

The plant features a single, erect stem with broad, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are pale pink to white, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

It is a protected species in the UK due to its limited distribution and ecological significance.
