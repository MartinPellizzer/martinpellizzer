Diospyros kondor

Diospyros kondor, commonly known as Kondor ebony, is a small to medium-sized tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Ebenaceae family, which includes other ebony species. The tree is characterized by its dark, dense wood, which is highly valued for its strength and durability. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and elliptical in shape, with a glossy surface.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by small, round fruits.
