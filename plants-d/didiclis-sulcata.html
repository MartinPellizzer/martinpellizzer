Didiclis sulcata

Didiclis sulcata, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers. Didiclis sulcata is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
