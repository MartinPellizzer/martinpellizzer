Dipteryx ferrea

Dipteryx ferrea, commonly known as ironwood, is a large tropical tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, hard wood that is highly valued for its strength and durability. The tree can grow up to 30 meters tall and has a broad, spreading canopy. Its leaves are compound, with long, slender leaflets, and it produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters.

The fruit is a woody capsule containing several seeds, which are dispersed by animals.
