Draco reflexa

Draco reflexa, commonly known as the reflexed dragonhead, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic plants known for their medicinal and culinary uses. The plant features square stems and opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, tubular flowers are typically violet or blue, arranged in spikes at the top of the stems.

Draco reflexa is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
