Draba shehbazii

Draba shehbazii, commonly known as the Kashmiri whitlow grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous annual that typically grows in rocky and alpine environments. The plant features basal rosettes of dark green, hairy leaves and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers in early spring. Draba shehbazii is native to the Himalayan regions of India and Pakistan.

It is notable for its ability to thrive in high-altitude, cold climates with limited soil nutrients.
