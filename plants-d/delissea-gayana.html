Delissea gayana

Delissea gayana, commonly known as the golden bell, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces clusters of yellow, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Delissea gayana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
