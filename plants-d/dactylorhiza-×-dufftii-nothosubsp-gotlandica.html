Dactylorhiza × dufftii nothosubsp. gotlandica

Dactylorhiza × dufftii nothosubsp.

gotlandica, commonly known as Gotland's orchid, is a hybrid orchid native to the Baltic region. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, fragrant flowers.

The plant typically blooms in late spring to early summer, producing pink to purple flowers with a distinctive lip. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in wet meadows and along riverbanks.

This nothosubspecies is notable for its ornamental value and is cultivated in botanical gardens for its beauty.
