Daucus foliosus

Daucus foliosus, commonly known as the leafy carrot, is a flowering plant in the Apiaceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in temperate climates. The plant features finely divided, fern-like leaves that are aromatic and often used in culinary applications. Its small, yellow flowers appear in umbels, typical of the carrot family.

Daucus foliosus is valued for its ornamental appeal and mild, carroty flavor.
