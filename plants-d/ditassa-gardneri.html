Ditassa gardneri

Ditassa gardneri, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and showy, clustered flowers that bloom in a variety of colors. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-draining soil. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.

The leaves are typically velvety and dark green, while the flowers are often arranged in a compact inflorescence.
