Deyeuxia alba

Deyeuxia alba, commonly known as the silvergrass, is a perennial grass species native to temperate regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its silvery-white inflorescence, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a smooth texture.

Deyeuxia alba is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
