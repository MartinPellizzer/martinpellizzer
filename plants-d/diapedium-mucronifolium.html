Diapedium mucronifolium

Diapedium mucronifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf marsh plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to wetland and marsh environments.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, white to pale green flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in swampy areas.

Due to its delicate structure, it plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.
