Deutzia scabra f. plena

Deutzia scabra f. plena, commonly known as the smooth deutzia or the white deutzia, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy, rounded clusters of white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has slender, arching stems and small, ovate leaves with a smooth texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden borders and foundation plantings.

Its ornamental value lies in its abundant, delicate blooms and its ability to provide seasonal interest in the landscape.
