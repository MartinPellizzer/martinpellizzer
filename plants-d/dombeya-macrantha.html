Dombeya macrantha

Dombeya macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered dombeya, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and are clustered in terminal panicles. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape, with a rough, hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for cultivation in gardens.

Dombeya macrantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its vibrant floral display.
