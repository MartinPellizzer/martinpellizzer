Dendrobium yeageri

Dendrobium yeageri, commonly known as the Yeager's orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a branched stem system that stores water and nutrients. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, adapted to its humid, shaded habitat.

Dendrobium yeageri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
