Dalbergia lanceolaria var. lakhonensis

Dalbergia lanceolaria var. lakhonensis, commonly known as the Lakhonensis rosewood, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand and Cambodia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The wood of this species is highly valued for its rich color and fine grain, making it a prized material in traditional woodworking.

It typically grows in lowland evergreen forests and thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil.
