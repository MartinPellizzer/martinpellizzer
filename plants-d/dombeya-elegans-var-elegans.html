Dombeya elegans var. elegans

Dombeya elegans var. elegans, commonly known as the silky dombeya, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its showy, pink to white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer seasons. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silky down, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Dombeya elegans var. elegans is valued for its ornamental beauty and relatively low maintenance requirements.
