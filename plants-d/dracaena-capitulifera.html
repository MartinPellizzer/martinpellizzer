Dracaena capitulifera

Dracaena capitulifera, commonly known as the helmet dracaena, is a species of flowering plant in the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to Madagascar and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of narrow, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers arranged in a dense, terminal inflorescence. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Dracaena capitulifera is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
