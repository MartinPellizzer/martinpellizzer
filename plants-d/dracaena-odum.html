Dracaena odum

Dracaena odum, commonly known as the African daisy or red dracaena, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, narrow leaves that are typically dark green with a reddish or bronze underside. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value to its appearance. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its adaptability to indoor conditions.

Dracaena odum prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
