Darwinia verticordina

Darwinia verticordina, commonly known as the red-stemmed devil's backbone, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its striking red stems and clusters of pink to red tubular flowers. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique appearance and long-lasting blooms.

Darwinia verticordina is also valued in horticulture for its ability to thrive in well-drained conditions.
