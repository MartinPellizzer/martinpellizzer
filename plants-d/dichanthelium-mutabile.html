Dichanthelium mutabile

Dichanthelium mutabile, commonly known as the variable panicgrass, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, branching stems. Dichanthelium mutabile is often found in moist, disturbed soils and is a common component of wetland and coastal grassland ecosystems.
