Drejera polyantha

Drejera polyantha, commonly known as the Chinese rose, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pink or white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. The plant has small, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and a dark green color. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in a variety of soil conditions.

The shrub is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
