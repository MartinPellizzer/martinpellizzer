Dracaena cuprea

Dracaena cuprea, commonly known as the red dracaena, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking reddish-orange variegated leaves, which add visual interest to indoor spaces. The plant has a slender, upright growth habit with a smooth, dark green stem. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to prevent root rot.

Dracaena cuprea is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and air-purifying properties.
