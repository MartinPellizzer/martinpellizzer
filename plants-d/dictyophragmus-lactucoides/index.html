Dictyophragmus Lactucoides

Dictyophragmus lactucoides is a lesser-known yet fascinating member of the Apiaceae family. This plant is often mistaken for a weed due to its simple appearance but holds unique ecological and medicinal value. Native to parts of Europe and Asia, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats. Its distinctive umbellate inflorescence and aromatic foliage make it a subject of botanical interest. Despite its modest look, it plays a significant role in supporting local pollinators and soil health.
