Dactylis izcoi subsp. merinoi

Dactylis izcoi subsp.

merinoi, commonly known as Merino grass, is a perennial grass species native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its fine, tufted foliage and produces small, spike-like inflorescences.

This plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and its use in ecological restoration projects.

Its botanical features make it adaptable to various environmental conditions.
