Diastella humifusa

Diastella humifusa, commonly known as the dwarf sandthorn, is a small, evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It typically grows to a height of around 30 centimeters and has dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions.

Diastella humifusa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate drought and coastal conditions.
