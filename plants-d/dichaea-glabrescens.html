Dichaea glabrescens

Dichaea glabrescens, commonly known as the velvet leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and green. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Dichaea glabrescens is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
