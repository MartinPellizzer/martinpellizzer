Dracontium repens

Dracontium repens, commonly known as the creeping arrowroot, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It features a rhizomatous growth habit, with slender, trailing stems that produce heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its tubers, which are edible, are often used in traditional cuisines.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to moist, shady environments.
