Delosperma subpetiolatum

Delosperma subpetiolatum, commonly known as the ice plant or carpet ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ability to spread rapidly forms dense mats, which help prevent soil erosion.
