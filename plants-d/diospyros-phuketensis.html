Diospyros phuketensis

Diospyros phuketensis, commonly known as Phuket persimmon, is a tropical evergreen tree native to southern Thailand.

It belongs to the Ebenaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and small, clustered flowers. The tree produces edible berries that are typically harvested when fully ripe, featuring a sweet, sometimes astringent flavor.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and has limited use in traditional medicine and local cuisine.
