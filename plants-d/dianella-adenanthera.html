Dianella adenanthera

Dianella adenanthera, commonly known as the coastal dianella, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a perennial evergreen shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, blue to purple flowers in spring. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in harsh coastal environments.

Dianella adenanthera is also known for its resilience to salt spray and wind.
