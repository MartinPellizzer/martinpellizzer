Dracaena Rumphii

Dracaena rumphii, also known as the red dragon tree, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. It is celebrated for its vibrant red and yellow flowers that bloom in dramatic clusters, creating a visual spectacle in any garden. The plant's unique appearance, with its tall, slender stems and colorful blooms, makes it a popular choice for ornamental purposes. Dracaena rumphii thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish. Its beauty and resilience make it a favorite among plant enthusiasts and gardeners alike.
