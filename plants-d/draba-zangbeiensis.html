Draba zangbeiensis

Draba zangbeiensis, commonly known as the Tibetan Draba, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas and Tibet.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cress plants. The species is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in early spring. It thrives in rocky and alpine environments, often growing in sparse vegetation.

The plant is of interest to botanists due to its unique adaptations to high-altitude conditions.
