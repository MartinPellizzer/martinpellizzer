Delphinium habaheense

Delphinium habaheense, commonly known as the Habahe Skyrocket, is a tall, perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple spires of flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy choice for rock gardens or alpine settings.

Delphinium habaheense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its dramatic floral display.
