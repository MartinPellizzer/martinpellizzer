Doronicum pubescens var. nemoralis

Doronicum pubescens var. nemoralis, commonly known as the woolly goldcup, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy, gray-green foliage. The plant produces showy yellow-orange composite flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in woodland edges and moist, shaded environments, preferring well-drained soil.
