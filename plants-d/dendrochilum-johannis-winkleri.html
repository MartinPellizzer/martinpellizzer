Dendrochilum johannis-winkleri

Dendrochilum johannis-winkleri, commonly known as the Winkler's tree orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically has lance-shaped leaves and blooms during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This orchid is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
