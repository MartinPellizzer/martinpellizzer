Dolichos funarius

Dolichos funarius, commonly known as the yellow burr bean, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It produces distinctive yellow, hairy pods that contain several kidney-shaped seeds. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and erect growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas.

Dolichos funarius is valued for its seeds, which are used in traditional medicine and as a food source in some cultures.
