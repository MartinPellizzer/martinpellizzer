Dendrobium bullerianum

Dendrobium bullerianum, commonly known as the Buller's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has a fibrous root system. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it blooms during the summer months.

Dendrobium bullerianum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
