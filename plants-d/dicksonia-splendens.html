Dicksonia splendens

Dicksonia splendens, commonly known as the golden tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery fronds that can reach lengths of up to 3 meters. The plant has a distinctive golden hue on its young fronds, which gives it its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Dicksonia splendens is valued for its ornamental qualities and is commonly cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
