Deyeuxia planifolia

Deyeuxia planifolia, commonly known as the flat-leaved sedge, is a perennial grass-like plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, flat leaves and small, clustered inflorescences. Native to wet or marshy habitats, it thrives in moist environments such as ponds, ditches, and wet meadows. The plant has a rhizomatous root system that helps it spread and colonize new areas.

Its botanical features make it an important component of wetland ecosystems.
