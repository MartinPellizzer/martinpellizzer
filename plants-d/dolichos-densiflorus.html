Dolichos densiflorus

Dolichos densiflorus, commonly known as the denseflower dolichos, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in dense inflorescences. The plant produces elongated, slender pods containing kidney-shaped seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

Dolichos densiflorus is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
