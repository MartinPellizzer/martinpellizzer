Desmodium oxyphyllum var. szechuenense

Desmodium oxyphyllum var. szechuenense, commonly known as the Sichuan desmodium, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant is typically found in forested areas and has adapted to thrive in moist, shaded environments.

It plays a role in soil nitrogen fixation due to its symbiotic relationship with nitrogen-fixing bacteria.
