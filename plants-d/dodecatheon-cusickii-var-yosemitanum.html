Dodecatheon cusickii var. yosemitanum

Dodecatheon cusickii var. yosemitanum, commonly known as the Yosemite shootcup, is a flowering plant native to the Sierra Nevada mountain range in California.

It is characterized by its showy, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a yellow or red spot at the base. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as meadows and forest floors. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
