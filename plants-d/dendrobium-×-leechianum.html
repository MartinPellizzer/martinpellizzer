Dendrobium × leechianum

Dendrobium × leechianum, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a fascinating plant with unique botanical characteristics.

It is a hybrid between Dendrobium nobile and Dendrobium phalaenopsis, resulting in a plant that combines traits from both parents. This orchid typically produces long, cane-like stems that can grow up to several feet in height. Its flowers are usually fragrant and come in a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and pink.

The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic, and it thrives in warm, humid environments.
