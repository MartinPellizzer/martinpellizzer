Deparia Emeiensis

Emei shield fern, scientifically known as Deparia emeiensis, is a unique species of fern native to the mountainous regions of Sichuan Province in China. This plant is characterized by its distinctive shield-shaped fronds and is often found in moist, shaded environments. Despite its limited distribution, it plays an important role in its ecosystem as a component of the local flora. Its rarity and specific habitat requirements make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Due to habitat loss and environmental changes, the Emei shield fern faces increasing threats to its survival.
