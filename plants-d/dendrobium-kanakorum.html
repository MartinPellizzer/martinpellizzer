Dendrobium kanakorum

Dendrobium kanakorum, commonly known as the Kanak Island orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to the Pacific islands.

It features long, slender pseudobulbs covered in a dense layer of white, fibrous roots. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive, narrow lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with high air circulation.

Dendrobium kanakorum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its delicate floral display.
