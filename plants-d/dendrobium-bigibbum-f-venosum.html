Dendrobium bigibbum f. venosum

Dendrobium bigibbum f. venosum, commonly known as the veiny orchid, is a rare and striking epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

This hybrid variety is characterized by its distinctive veined leaves and vibrant, long-lasting blooms that often feature a mix of yellow and green hues. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media to prevent root rot. The plant is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique foliage and floral display.

Due to its specific care requirements, it is considered a challenging but rewarding species for experienced orchid growers.
