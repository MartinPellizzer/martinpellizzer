Dicentra grandifoliolata

Dicentra grandifoliolata, commonly known as the giant heartleaf bleeding heart, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are large and deeply lobed, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of delicate, pendulous white flowers with a pinkish tinge, resembling a heart shape. It thrives in partial to full shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Dicentra grandifoliolata is valued for its ornamental value and ability to add a unique touch to shaded landscapes.
