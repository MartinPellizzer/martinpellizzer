Delphinium siwanense var. albopuberulum

Delphinium siwanense var. albopuberulum, commonly known as the white-hairy delphinium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that bear clusters of showy, blue to violet flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture, which gives it its common name. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
