Dactylorhiza × kuehnensis

Dactylorhiza × kuehnensis, commonly known as the Kuehn's orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a rhizomatous root system and typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces tall, slender stems bearing several showy, violet to purple flowers with a distinctive lip. It is part of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant blooms.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses for its beauty and unique floral structure.
