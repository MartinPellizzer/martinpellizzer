Declieuxia polygaloides var. angustifolia

Declieuxia polygaloides var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf silver fern, is a striking ornamental plant native to New Zealand.

It features feathery, silver-gray foliage with a distinctive, fern-like appearance that adds visual interest to gardens. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it adaptable to various climates. Its unique leaf structure consists of long, narrow, overlapping pinnae that create a soft, textured look.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to tolerate coastal conditions.
