Dendrobium ruttenii

Dendrobium ruttenii, commonly known as the black orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It is an epiphytic plant that grows on trees and rocks in tropical rainforests. The plant features long, cane-like stems covered in dense clusters of dark purple to black flowers. Its unique coloration makes it a striking and sought-after ornamental plant.

Dendrobium ruttenii is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
