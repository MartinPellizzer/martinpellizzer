Dichapetalum heudelotii var. hispidum

Dichapetalum heudelotii var. hispidum, commonly known as the hairy dichapetalum, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, showy flowers that are typically pink or red in color. The plant has hairy foliage, which gives it its varietal name "hispidum.". It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
