Draba aizoides proles affinis

Draba aizoides proles affinis, commonly known as the alpine whitlow, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, thriving in well-drained soils at high elevations. Its leaves are basal, rosette-like, and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in harsh environments.
