Dendrobium abhaycharanii

Dendrobium abhaycharanii, commonly known as the Abhaycharan's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect pseudobulbs and long, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with a red or orange lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
