Diplazium dinghushanicum

Diplazium dinghushanicum, commonly known as the Dinghushan spleenwort, is a species of fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, creeping rhizomes and small, lance-shaped fronds. Diplazium dinghushanicum is of interest to botanists due to its ecological role and its significance in understanding fern diversity in subtropical regions.
