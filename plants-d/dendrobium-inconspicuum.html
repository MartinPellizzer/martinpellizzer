Dendrobium inconspicuum

Dendrobium inconspicuum, commonly known as the slender dendrobium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on the surfaces of trees or rocks without deriving nutrients from them. The plant features a tall, slender stem with numerous small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its leaves are strap-like and arranged spirally around the stem.

Dendrobium inconspicuum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its delicate floral display.
