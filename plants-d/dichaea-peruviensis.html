Dichaea peruviensis

Dichaea peruviensis, commonly known as the Peruvian dichaea, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a raceme, which bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are typically ovate and covered in a dense layer of fine, white pubescence.

Dichaea peruviensis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
