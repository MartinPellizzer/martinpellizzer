Dracocephalum austriacum

Dracocephalum austriacum, commonly known as Austrian skullcap, is a flowering plant native to Central Europe.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Dracocephalum austriacum is often used in traditional medicine for its purported calming and anti-inflammatory properties.
