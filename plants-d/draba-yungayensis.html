Draba yungayensis

Draba yungayensis, commonly known as Yungay's whitlow grass, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many species of mustard and related plants. The plant is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pale yellow. It thrives in rocky, alpine environments, often growing at high elevations.
