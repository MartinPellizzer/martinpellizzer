Dalea wigginsii

Dalea wigginsii, commonly known as Wiggins' milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Dalea wigginsii plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing nectar for pollinators in its native habitat.
