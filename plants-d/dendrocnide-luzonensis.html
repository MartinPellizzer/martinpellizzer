Dendrocnide luzonensis

Dendrocnide luzonensis, commonly known as the Luzon stinging nettle, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Urticaceae family, which includes other stinging nettle species. The plant is characterized by its hairy, serrated leaves and produces small greenish-white flowers. Its leaves and stems contain irritant chemicals that cause skin irritation upon contact.

It typically grows in moist, shaded areas in mountainous regions.
