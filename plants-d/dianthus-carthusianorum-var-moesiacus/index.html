Dianthus Carthusianorum Var. Moesiacus

Dianthus carthusianorum var. moesiacus is a rare and striking plant known for its vibrant, ruffled petals and delicate appearance. Native to the Balkan region, this variety thrives in rocky and well-drained soils, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its flowers, which bloom in shades of pink and red, add a touch of elegance to any garden or natural setting. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure, often overlooked in favor of more common garden flowers. This plant serves as a reminder of the hidden diversity within the dianthus family.
