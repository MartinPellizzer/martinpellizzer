Dendrochilum rhombophorum

Dendrochilum rhombophorum, commonly known as the rhombic orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its rhomboidal-shaped pseudobulbs and narrow, elongated leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a yellow lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrochilum rhombophorum is a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
