Dianthus Campestris Subsp. Laevigatus

Field campion, scientifically known as Dianthus campestris subsp. laevigatus, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It is characterized by its vibrant pink or red petals and a distinctive white center, making it a popular choice for gardens and wildflower meadows. This species thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in open fields, roadsides, and disturbed areas. Its delicate yet resilient nature allows it to adapt to various environmental conditions. With its ornamental appeal and ecological significance, field campion plays an important role in both natural and cultivated landscapes.
