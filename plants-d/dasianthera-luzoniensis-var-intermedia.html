Dasianthera luzoniensis var. intermedia

Dasianthera luzoniensis var. intermedia, commonly known as the Luzon intermediate orchid, is a rare endemic species found in the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in montane forest habitats, where it thrives in shaded, moist environments. Its leaves are lanceolate and arranged in a basal rosette, while its inflorescence bears a cluster of small, colorful blooms.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species facing threats from deforestation and habitat loss.
