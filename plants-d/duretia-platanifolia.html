Duretia platanifolia

Duretia platanifolia, commonly known as the plane tree duretia, is a species of flowering plant native to parts of southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often adorned with a distinctive waxy coating. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, appearing in the spring. Its roots are deep and robust, allowing it to thrive in well-drained soils.

Duretia platanifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to provide shade in urban landscapes.
