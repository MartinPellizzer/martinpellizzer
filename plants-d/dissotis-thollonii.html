Dissotis thollonii

Dissotis thollonii, commonly known as the slender spider-orchid, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, spider-like appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are often clustered in a raceme. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is adapted to the tropical climate of its native habitat.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and specialized species.
