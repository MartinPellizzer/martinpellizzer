Dianthus pallens

Dianthus pallens, commonly known as the pink campion, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, pink or white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it often forms dense clusters of small, showy flowers.

Dianthus pallens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
