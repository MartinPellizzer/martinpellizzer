Dianella pullei

Dianella pullei, commonly known as the narrow-leaved flax lily, is a perennial evergreen herb native to eastern Australia.

It features grass-like leaves that are narrow and slightly curved, with a bluish-green hue. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in spring, followed by blue-black berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understoreys.

Dianella pullei is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting native wildlife.
