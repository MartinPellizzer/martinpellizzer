Desmanthus interior

Desmanthus interior, commonly known as the interior desmanthus, is a perennial legume native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for dryland restoration projects. It is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, improving soil fertility.

Its ecological importance includes providing forage for wildlife and livestock.
