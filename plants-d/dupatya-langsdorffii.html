Dupatya langsdorffii

Dupatya langsdorffii, commonly known as the Java orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a short, creeping rhizome with a rosette of fleshy, dark green leaves. The plant produces a single, slender flowering stem that bears several pale yellow to greenish-white flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
