Dendrobium flos-wanua

Dendrobium flos-wanua, commonly known as the Wanua orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical rainforests of Borneo.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Dendrobium flos-wanua is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
