Dendrolobium cephalotoides

Dendrolobium cephalotoides, commonly known as the yellow burr legume, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces woody pods that contain seeds, which are often dispersed by animals. It thrives in sandy soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Dendrolobium cephalotoides is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
