Dracaena volubilis

Dracaena volubilis, commonly known as the wandering dragon tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, spiraling stems that can reach several meters in length. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color. It produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy fruits.

Dracaena volubilis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique growth habit and adaptability to various environments.
