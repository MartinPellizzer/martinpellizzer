Dendrobium clavuligerum

Dendrobium clavuligerum, commonly known as the orchid with the clustered flowers, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces striking, clustered inflorescences with fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. Its leaves are usually fleshy and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Dendrobium clavuligerum is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and unique floral display.
