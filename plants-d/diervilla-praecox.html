Diervilla praecox

Diervilla praecox, commonly known as early diervilla, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It belongs to the family Caprifoliaceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense foliage. Diervilla praecox is often used in landscaping for its hardiness and ability to thrive in partial shade to full sun.
