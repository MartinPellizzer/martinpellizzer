Dahlia coccinea var. gentryi

Dahlia coccinea var. gentryi, commonly known as the Gentry's Red Dahlia, is a vibrant and showy tuberous-rooted perennial native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its striking red flowers with a velvety texture and a rich, deep hue. The plant produces showy, single or semi-cup-shaped blooms that are typically 4 to 6 inches in diameter. Its foliage consists of heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are slightly hairy.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower due to its long-lasting blooms.
