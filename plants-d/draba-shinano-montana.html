Draba shinano-montana

Draba shinano-montana, commonly known as the Shinano Mountain rockcress, is a low-growing flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The species is characterized by its compact rosette of basal leaves and small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in rocky, alpine environments and is adapted to cold, nutrient-poor soils.

Draba shinano-montana is often found in mountainous regions of central and northern Japan.
