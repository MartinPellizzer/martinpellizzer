Dendrobium vanikorense

Dendrobium vanikorense, commonly known as the Vanikoro orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its linear, sometimes hairy pseudobulbs and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically produces white or pale yellow blooms with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Dendrobium vanikorense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
