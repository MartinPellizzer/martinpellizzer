Delphinium yuchuanii

Delphinium yuchuanii, commonly known as the Yuchuan larkspur, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, spindly stems that bear clusters of blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky slopes, thriving in cooler climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem, while the flowers are showy and attract various pollinators.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
