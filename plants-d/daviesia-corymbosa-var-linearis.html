Daviesia corymbosa var. linearis

Daviesia corymbosa var. linearis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf hakea, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically appearing in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or sandy habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native pollinators.
