Dendrobium koeteianum

Dendrobium koeteianum, commonly known as the Koetei orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with cane-like stems that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrobium koeteianum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
