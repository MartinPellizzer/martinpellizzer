Draba corrugata var. saxosa

Draba corrugata var. saxosa, commonly known as the saxicola draba, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically thrives in rocky or alpine environments. The plant features small, basal leaves and produces clusters of bright yellow flowers in early spring. It is well adapted to harsh conditions, with a compact growth form that helps it withstand cold and wind.

This variety is often found in mountainous regions across Europe and parts of Asia.
