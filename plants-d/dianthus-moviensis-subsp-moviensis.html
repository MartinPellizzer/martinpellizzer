Dianthus moviensis subsp. moviensis

Dianthus moviensis subsp.

moviensis, commonly known as the moving dianthus, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are composed of numerous petals arranged in a clustered formation, giving it a delicate appearance.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
