Deutzia nakaiana

Deutzia nakaiana, commonly known as Japanese deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its clusters of white to pink flowers that bloom in early summer. It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
