Deschampsia flexuosa f. viridistriata

Deschampsia flexuosa f. viridistriata, commonly known as the greenstripe oatgrass, is a flowering plant species native to North America.

It is a member of the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically green with distinct silver or greenish-white stripes along the midrib. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

This variety is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks.
