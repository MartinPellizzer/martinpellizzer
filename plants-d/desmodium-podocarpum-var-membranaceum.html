Desmodium podocarpum var. membranaceum

Desmodium podocarpum var. membranaceum, commonly known as the slender desmodium, is a species of legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers that typically bloom in clusters. The plant produces slender, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or disturbed areas.

Its growth habit and morphology make it a useful plant for ecological restoration projects.
