Dendrochilum carnosulilabrum

Dendrochilum carnosulilabrum, commonly known as the fleshy-labellum orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting it to its often humid and shaded habitats. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with a unique, labellum that is thick and fleshy, giving it its specific epithet. It typically blooms during the wet season, adding vibrant colors to the undergrowth of tropical forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
