Disa mac-owanii

Disa mac-owanii, commonly known as the Mac-O-Wan's orchid, is a species of orchid native to South Africa.

It is a terrestrial plant with a short, stocky rhizome and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces striking, showy flowers with a distinctive red and yellow coloration, often with a contrasting green throat. It blooms during the summer months, typically from November to March.

Disa mac-owanii is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
