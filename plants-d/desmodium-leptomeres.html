Desmodium leptomeres

Desmodium leptomeres, commonly known as slender desmodium, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which consist of three small, oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and woodland edges.

Desmodium leptomeres is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in agricultural and ecological settings.
