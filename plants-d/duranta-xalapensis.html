Duranta xalapensis

Duranta xalapensis, commonly known as the skyflower or blue mistplant, is a flowering shrub native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, blue or white flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has opposite, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-draining soil.

Duranta xalapensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in landscaping and as a hedge plant.
