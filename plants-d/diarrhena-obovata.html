Diarrhena obovata

Diarrhena obovata, commonly known as the broad-leafed Diarrhena, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of broad, obovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in umbels, which bloom in late summer. Its root system is fibrous and has been traditionally used in local medicine.

Diarrhena obovata thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland and rocky habitats.
