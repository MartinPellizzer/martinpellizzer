Degenia velebitica

Degenia velebitica, commonly known as the Velebit mountain heath, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the Dinaric Alps, particularly in Croatia.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries, and is characterized by its small, leathery leaves and clusters of bell-shaped flowers. The plant thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is adapted to the harsh, mountainous environments of its native range.

It plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various invertebrates in its alpine habitat.
