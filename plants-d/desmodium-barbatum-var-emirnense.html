Desmodium barbatum var. emirnense

Desmodium barbatum var. emirnense, commonly known as the Emirnense burr legume, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features trifoliate leaves with small, hairy stipules and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense clusters. Its root system is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or grasslands.
