Deschampsia klossii

Deschampsia klossii, commonly known as Kloss's feathergrass, is a low-growing perennial grass native to New Zealand.

It features fine, silver-blue foliage and produces delicate, feathery seed heads that add visual interest to the landscape. This plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along streambanks. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Deschampsia klossii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, supporting local wildlife and improving soil stability.
