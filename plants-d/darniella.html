Darniella

Darniella, commonly known as the sea holly, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the family Asteraceae.

These plants are characterized by their distinctive blue or purple flower heads and silver-gray foliage. Darniella species are typically found in coastal areas and saline soils, showcasing their adaptability to harsh environments. They have small, narrow leaves and produce small, clustered flowers that attract pollinators.

The genus includes several species, some of which are cultivated for ornamental purposes due to their striking appearance.
