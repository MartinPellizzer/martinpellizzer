Daboecia × scotica f. albiflora

Daboecia × scotica f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered heather, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Ericaceae family.

It is characterized by its white or pale pink flowers, which contrast with its dark green foliage. This variety is native to the Scottish Highlands and thrives in acidic, well-drained soils. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as moorlands and coastal areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in suitable climates.
