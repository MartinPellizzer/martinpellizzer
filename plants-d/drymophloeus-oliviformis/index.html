Drymophloeus Oliviformis

Drymophloeus oliviformis is a unique species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. Known for its distinctive oval-shaped leaves and delicate, fragrant flowers, it holds a special place in local horticulture and traditional medicine. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its scientific name reflects its olive-like leaf shape and its association with the Drymophloeus genus. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively under-studied in modern botanical research.
