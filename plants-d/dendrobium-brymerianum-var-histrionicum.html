Dendrobium brymerianum var. histrionicum

Dendrobium brymerianum var. histrionicum, commonly known as the Histrionic Dendrobium, is a unique orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored flowers that can range from red to orange and yellow. The plant is an epiphyte, typically growing on trees or rocks in humid tropical environments. Its stems are often branched and can become woody over time, and it produces showy, long-lasting blooms.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
