Dupineta multiflora

Dupineta multiflora, commonly known as the many-flowered dupineta, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in spring. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a rough texture with serrated edges.

Dupineta multiflora is adapted to arid conditions and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
