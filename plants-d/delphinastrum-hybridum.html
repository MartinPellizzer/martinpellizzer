Delphinastrum hybridum

Delphinastrum hybridum, commonly known as the hybrid milkvetch, is a perennial flowering plant native to the grasslands of central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, pea-like flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility.

It thrives in dry, open habitats and is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its hardy nature.
