Didymoglossum Intramarginale

Didymoglossum intramarginale is a rare and delicate orchid native to the mountainous regions of Central and South America. This small, terrestrial plant is known for its unique floral structure and specialized habitat requirements. It thrives in cool, moist environments, often found in shaded forest floors or rocky crevices. Due to its limited distribution and sensitivity to environmental changes, it is considered a vulnerable species. Conservation efforts are essential to protect this rare botanical treasure from habitat loss and climate change.
