Dendrobium hastilabium

Dendrobium hastilabium, commonly known as the golden spike orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that often bear multiple spikes of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, and it produces numerous small, tubular flowers arranged in dense clusters.

Dendrobium hastilabium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
