Dillenia artocarpifolia

Dillenia artocarpifolia, commonly known as the giant dillenia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in diameter. The plant produces showy, yellowish-green flowers with prominent, fleshy sepals that resemble petals. Its fruit is a woody, spiky capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Dillenia artocarpifolia is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental and ecological significance.
