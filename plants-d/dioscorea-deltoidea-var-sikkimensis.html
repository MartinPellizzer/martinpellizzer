Dioscorea deltoidea var. sikkimensis

Dioscorea deltoidea var. sikkimensis, commonly known as the Sikkim yam, is a climbing vine native to the Himalayan region.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and tuberous roots. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments at high altitudes. Its tubers are used in traditional medicine and as a food source in some regions.

The species is valued for its ornamental potential and ecological significance in its native habitat.
