Desmodium salvinii

Desmodium salvinii, commonly known as velvet bean or *Mucuna salvinii*, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions.

It features trifoliate leaves with a velvety texture and produces small, yellowish flowers that are often clustered in racemes. The plant is characterized by its distinctive pod-like fruits that contain several elliptical seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils.

Desmodium salvinii is also known for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it beneficial in agricultural settings.
