Dockrillia

Dockrillia, commonly known as the "false dock," is a flowering plant belonging to the family Polygonaceae.

It is characterized by its upright, slender stems and heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems. Dockrillia is typically found in moist, disturbed soils in temperate regions.
