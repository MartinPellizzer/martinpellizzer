Dioscorea polyclades var. oblongifolia

Dioscorea polyclades var. oblongifolia, commonly known as the African yam bean, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It produces heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture and is characterized by its distinctive, tuberous roots. The plant flowers with small, greenish-white blooms that develop into elongated, cylindrical fruits. Its tubers are edible and have been traditionally used as a food source in various regions.

The species is also valued for its ornamental qualities and potential medicinal properties.
