Dichroa henryi

Dichroa henryi, commonly known as the Chinese dichroa, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the family Berberidaceae and is characterized by its striking, two-toned leaves that change color with the seasons. The plant features upright, branching stems and produces small, yellowish-green flowers in the spring. Its foliage is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value, and it thrives in partial shade with well-drained soil.

Dichroa henryi is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
