Dianthus smithii

Dianthus smithii, commonly known as the Smith's campion, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family, which includes many showy, herbaceous plants. The species is characterized by its pink to purple flowers, which bloom in late summer and early fall. It typically grows in open, rocky areas and is often found in alpine or arid environments.
