Discaria australis var. apetala

Discaria australis var. apetala, commonly known as the pink burr dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its hairy stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces pink or purple flowers that are followed by distinctive, spiky seed heads. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
