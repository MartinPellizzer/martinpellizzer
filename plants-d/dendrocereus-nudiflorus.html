Dendrocereus nudiflorus

Dendrocereus nudiflorus, commonly known as the nakedflower cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is a columnar cactus that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features a smooth, green stem covered with sparse spines. It produces white or pale yellow flowers during the night, which is a unique adaptation for attracting nocturnal pollinators.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience to dry conditions.
