Draba aizoides var. olympicoides

Draba aizoides var. olympicoides, commonly known as Olympic Draba, is a small, flowering plant native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It typically grows in moist, rocky soils in alpine and subalpine environments. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers in early spring. Its leaves are basal, forming a rosette, and are covered in fine hairs.

It is an important part of the local ecosystem, providing nectar for early pollinators.
