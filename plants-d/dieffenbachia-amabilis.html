Dieffenbachia amabilis

Dieffenbachia amabilis, commonly known as the flamingo flower, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, brightly colored leaves that often feature white, yellow, or pink markings. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is known for its upright growth habit and glossy foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Dieffenbachia amabilis is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its vibrant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
