Dendrobium clementsii

Dendrobium clementsii, commonly known as the Australian orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome that produces a cluster of fleshy, leaf-like structures. The plant produces fragrant, yellow-green flowers with a distinctive red lip during the spring and summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Dendrobium clementsii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
