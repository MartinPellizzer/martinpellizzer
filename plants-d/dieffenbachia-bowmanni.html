Dieffenbachia bowmanni

Dieffenbachia bowmanni, commonly known as the Bowmann's Dieffenbachia, is a striking tropical houseplant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically green with white or cream-colored veins. The plant is part of the Araceae family, which includes many ornamental plants known for their unique foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its toxic sap, it should be handled with care and kept away from pets and children.
