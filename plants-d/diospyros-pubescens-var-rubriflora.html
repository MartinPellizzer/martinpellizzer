Diospyros pubescens var. rubriflora

Diospyros pubescens var. rubriflora, commonly known as the red-flowered persimmon, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. Its flowers are small and clustered, with a distinctive reddish hue, contributing to its ornamental value.

The fruit is a fleshy, orange-red drupe that matures in late summer to early autumn.
