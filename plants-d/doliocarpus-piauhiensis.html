Doliocarpus piauhiensis

Doliocarpus piauhiensis, commonly known as the Piauhi palm, is a flowering plant species native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and pinnately compound leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with both male and female flowers, typical of many palm species. Its fruits are drupes, containing a single seed enclosed in a fleshy outer layer.

Doliocarpus piauhiensis is often found in moist, shaded environments within tropical forests.
