Diplactis blanda

Diplactis blanda, commonly known as the soft-leaf fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are typically soft and plume-like in appearance. Native to tropical regions, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-drained soil. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread slowly and form dense mats.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.
