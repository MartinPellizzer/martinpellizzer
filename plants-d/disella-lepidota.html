Disella lepidota

Disella lepidota, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.

Disella lepidota is notable for its rapid growth and ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
