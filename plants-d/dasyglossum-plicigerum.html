Dasyglossum plicigerum

Dasyglossum plicigerum, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, zigzag-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Dasyglossum plicigerum is often found growing in clusters on the ground or on rocks.
