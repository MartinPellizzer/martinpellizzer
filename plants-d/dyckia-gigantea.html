Dyckia gigantea

Dyckia gigantea, commonly known as the giant dyckia, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 meters in length. The plant forms a dense rosette structure, which helps it retain moisture in its natural habitat. It produces striking red and orange inflorescences that bloom in the summer months.

Due to its unique appearance and hardiness, it is a popular choice for ornamental gardening in tropical and subtropical regions.
