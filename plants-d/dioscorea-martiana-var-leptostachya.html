Dioscorea martiana var. leptostachya

Dioscorea martiana var. leptostachya, commonly known as the slender-stalked yam, is a climbing vine belonging to the family Dioscoreaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its tuberous roots, which are often used in traditional medicine. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by cylindrical, yellowish fruits. Its tubers are valued for their medicinal properties and are also used as a food source in some cultures.

The species is noted for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.
