Delissea simplex f. maculata

Delissea simplex f. maculata, commonly known as the spotted delissea, is a rare and striking cultivar of the Delissea genus.

It is characterized by its distinctive variegated foliage, featuring patches of white or cream on its otherwise green leaves. This plant is native to South Africa and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun exposure. It is a low-growing succulent with a compact, bushy form.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container gardening.
