Diphyllocalyx cayensis subsp. variifolius

Diphyllocalyx cayensis subsp.

variifolius, commonly known as the Cayenne trumpetflower, is a member of the Malvaceae family. It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly in parts of Guyana and Venezuela.
