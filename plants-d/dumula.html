Dumula

Dumula, commonly known as the desert lily, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant has a robust root system that allows it to survive in harsh, dry environments. Its leaves are typically narrow and succulent, storing water to withstand drought.
