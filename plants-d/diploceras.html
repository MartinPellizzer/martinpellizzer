Diploceras

It consists of several species, most of which are shrubs or small trees native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plants are characterized by their opposite, simple leaves and small, often clustered flowers. They typically have smooth, bark-like stems and produce small, fleshy fruits.

The genus is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its foliage and unique growth form.
