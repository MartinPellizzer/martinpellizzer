Desmia aequalis

Desmia aequalis, commonly known as the common leafroller, is a species of moth belonging to the family Noctuidae.

It is characterized by its slender body and wings that are typically pale brown to gray in color. The larvae of this species feed on a variety of plants, including grasses and herbs, often rolling leaves to form shelters. Botanically, it is associated with plants in the Poaceae (grass family) and Asteraceae (daisy family) due to its feeding habits.

This moth plays a role in the ecological dynamics of its habitat through both its herbivorous activities and as a food source for other organisms.
