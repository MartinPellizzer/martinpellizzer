Digitaria stapfii

Digitaria stapfii, commonly known as the African grass or stapf's digitaria, is a species of grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences, which are typical of the Poaceae family. Digitaria stapfii is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and potential agricultural applications.
