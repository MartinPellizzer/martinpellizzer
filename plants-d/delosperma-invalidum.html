Delosperma invalidum

Delosperma invalidum, commonly known as the ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the summer months. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.

Delosperma invalidum thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
