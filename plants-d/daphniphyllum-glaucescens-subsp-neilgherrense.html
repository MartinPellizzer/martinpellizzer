Daphniphyllum glaucescens subsp. neilgherrense

Daphniphyllum glaucescens subsp.

neilgherrense, commonly known as the Neilgherry daphniphyllum, is a species of evergreen shrub native to the Himalayan region. It is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage with a distinctive bluish-green hue on the upper surface.

The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that are typically followed by bright red berries. It thrives in cool, humid climates and is often found in mountainous areas with acidic soils.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is a sought-after plant in botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
