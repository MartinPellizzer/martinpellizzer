Dendrobium undulatum var. fimbrilabium

Dendrobium undulatum var. fimbrilabium, commonly known as the wavy-leaf dendrobium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant that grows on trees and rocks, thriving in humid, tropical climates. The plant features long, pendulous stems with lance-shaped leaves that are often wavy or undulating in appearance. Its flowers are typically yellow or greenish-yellow with red or brown markings, and they bloom in clusters during the warmer months.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
