Dioscorea megalobotrya

Dioscorea megalobotrya, commonly known as the giant arrowhead vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by edible tubers that are often used in local cuisines.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is sometimes cultivated for its nutritional value and ornamental foliage.
