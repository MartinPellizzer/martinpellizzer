Dracaena venosa

Dracaena venosa, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that display shades of yellow, white, and green in a zigzag pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by red, berry-like fruits. Dracaena venosa is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that thrives in indirect light and well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
