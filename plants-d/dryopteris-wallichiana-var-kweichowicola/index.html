Dryopteris Wallichiana Var. Kweichowicola

Dryopteris wallichiana var. kweichowicola is a rare fern species native to the mountainous regions of China. It belongs to the Dryopteris genus, known for its delicate fronds and ecological significance. This variety is distinguished by its unique leaf morphology and habitat preferences. It thrives in cool, humid environments with well-drained soil. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical research and conservation efforts.
