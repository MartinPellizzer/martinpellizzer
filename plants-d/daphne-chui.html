Daphne chui

Daphne chui, commonly known as Chinese daphne, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring, typically in shades of pink and white. The plant has elliptical, dark green leaves that are glossy and arranged oppositely on the stems. Its bark is smooth and reddish-brown, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits in the summer.

Daphne chui is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its early blooming period.
