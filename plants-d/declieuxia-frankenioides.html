Declieuxia frankenioides

Declieuxia frankenioides, commonly known as the red sandrift, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces vibrant orange to red disc flowers surrounded by pale yellow to white ray florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Declieuxia frankenioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
