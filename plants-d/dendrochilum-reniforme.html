Dendrochilum reniforme

Dendrochilum reniforme, commonly known as the kidney-shaped orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive kidney-shaped pseudobulbs and elliptical leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, pale yellow to greenish-white flowers with elongated petals and a spurred lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrochilum reniforme is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
