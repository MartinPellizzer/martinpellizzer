Dryopteris nanchuanensis

Dryopteris nanchuanensis, commonly known as the Nanchuan shield fern, is a species of polypod fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest undergrowth. Its fronds are usually leathery and have a distinctive, intricate venation pattern.

Dryopteris nanchuanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
