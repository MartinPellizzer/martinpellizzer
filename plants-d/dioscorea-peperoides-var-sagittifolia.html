Dioscorea peperoides var. sagittifolia

Dioscorea peperoides var. sagittifolia, commonly known as the arrowleaf yam, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive arrowhead shape, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by tuberous roots that are often used in traditional medicine. Its stems are covered in fine hairs, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

The tubers are typically harvested for their nutritional and medicinal properties.
