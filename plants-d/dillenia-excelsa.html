Dillenia excelsa

Dillenia excelsa, commonly known as the giant dillenia, is a striking tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in diameter. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that are typically arranged in clusters, attracting various pollinators. Its fruit is a large, spiny capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Dillenia excelsa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.
