Desmotrichum Purpureostelidium

Purple-stemmed desmotrichum, scientifically known as Desmotrichum purpureostelidium, is a striking succulent native to the arid regions of southern Africa. This unique plant is characterized by its vibrant purple stems and clusters of small, fleshy leaves that store water for survival in harsh conditions. It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens. Despite its beauty, it is often overlooked for its ecological importance in supporting local insect populations. Its resilience and adaptability make it a fascinating subject for both botanists and gardening enthusiasts alike.
