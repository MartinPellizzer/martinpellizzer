Dunnaria

Dunnaria, commonly known as the New Zealand tree fern, is a distinctive plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound fronds. The plant has a slow growth rate and can live for several decades, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its fronds are evergreen and can be used for ornamental purposes in gardens.

Dunnaria plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
