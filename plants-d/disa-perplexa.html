Disa perplexa

Disa perplexa, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of showy, colorful flowers that range from red to violet. The plant typically blooms during the summer months, producing intricate, fragrant blooms that attract various pollinators. Disa perplexa thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its unique floral structure and vibrant colors make it a popular choice for both horticultural gardens and botanical collections.
