Dryopteris × asturiensis

Dryopteris × asturiensis, commonly known as the Asturian wood fern, is a hybrid fern native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Dryopteris genus, which includes various wood ferns, and is characterized by its large, leathery fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides. Its rhizomes are thick and creeping, allowing it to spread and form dense colonies.

The fronds are usually bipinnate, with a distinctive, elegant appearance that makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
