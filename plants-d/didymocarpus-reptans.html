Didymocarpus reptans

Didymocarpus reptans, commonly known as the creeping sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa and the Middle East.

The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers that bloom in summer, followed by distinctive, three-lobed, red or orange fruits.
