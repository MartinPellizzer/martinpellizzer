Dioscorea gillettii

Dioscorea gillettii, commonly known as the Gillett's yam, is a species of tuberous vine native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its climbing habit and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Its tubers are edible and have been used traditionally in some regions for their nutritional value.

However, the plant contains toxic compounds that require careful preparation before consumption.
