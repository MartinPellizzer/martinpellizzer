Dombeya biumbellata

Dombeya biumbellata, commonly known as the lacecap dombeya, is a deciduous shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, white, clustered flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a rough texture and a mild aromatic scent. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Dombeya biumbellata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively low maintenance once established.
