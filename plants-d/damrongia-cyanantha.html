Damrongia cyanantha

Damrongia cyanantha, commonly known as the blue-flowered orchid, is a rare and striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its vibrant blue petals and a unique, intricate floral structure. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires specific humidity levels for optimal growth.

Due to its rarity and aesthetic appeal, it is highly sought after by botanical enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
