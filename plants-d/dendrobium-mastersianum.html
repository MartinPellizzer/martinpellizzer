Dendrobium mastersianum

Dendrobium mastersianum, commonly known as the mastersian orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, cane-like growth habit with dense clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in tropical environments. Its leaves are typically fleshy and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Dendrobium mastersianum is prized in horticulture for its long-lasting blooms and adaptability to various growing conditions.
