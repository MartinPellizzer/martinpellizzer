Diplostephium lanatum

Diplostephium lanatum, commonly known as the woolly nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive, fuzzy appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are typically ovate to elliptic in shape and covered in fine, white hairs.

Diplostephium lanatum is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments.
