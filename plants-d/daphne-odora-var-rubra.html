Daphne odora var. rubra

Daphne odora var. rubra, commonly known as the Chinese lantern daphne, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that provide a striking contrast to its showy blooms. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

However, it is toxic to both humans and animals, requiring careful handling and placement.
