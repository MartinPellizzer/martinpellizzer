Dendrobium lageniforme

Dendrobium lageniforme, commonly known as the bottle-shaped orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, bottle-shaped pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with intricate patterns. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

Dendrobium lageniforme is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
