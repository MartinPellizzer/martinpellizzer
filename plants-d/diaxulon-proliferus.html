Diaxulon proliferus

Diaxulon proliferus, commonly known as the silverleaf fern, is a tropical plant characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to the humid forests of Southeast Asia. The plant features a short, creeping rhizome with numerous aerial roots that help it attach to trees or rocks. Its leaves are bioluminescent under certain conditions, a unique trait among ferns.

It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often used in ornamental horticulture.
