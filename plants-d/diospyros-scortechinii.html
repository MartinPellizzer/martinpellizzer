Diospyros scortechinii

Diospyros scortechinii, commonly known as the Philippine persimmon, is a small evergreen tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. The tree produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into orange-red, fleshy fruits.
