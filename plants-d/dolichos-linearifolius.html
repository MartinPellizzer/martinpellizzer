Dolichos linearifolius

Dolichos linearifolius, commonly known as the slender cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, erect stems and elongated, linear leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by long, slender pods containing edible seeds. It is well adapted to poor soils and is often used as a forage crop.

Its botanical characteristics make it valuable in both agricultural and ecological contexts.
