Disa lugens var. nigrescens

Disa lugens var. nigrescens, commonly known as the Black Disa, is a striking orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black flowers, which contrast sharply with its green foliage. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers a humid, shaded environment. It produces showy, tubular flowers that attract specific pollinators, such as bees and flies.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
