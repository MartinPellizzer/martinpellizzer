Dolicholus filicaulis

Dolicholus filicaulis, commonly known as the featherleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its delicate, feathery leaves and yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its foliage is lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft appearance.

Dolicholus filicaulis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care.
