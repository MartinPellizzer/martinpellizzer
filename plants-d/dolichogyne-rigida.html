Dolichogyne rigida

Dolichogyne rigida, commonly known as the rigid long-body mite, is a species of acarid belonging to the family Dolichogynidae.

It is a small, spider-like arachnid that primarily feeds on other mites and small insects. This plant-associated mite is often found in humid environments, such as tropical rainforests, where it plays a role in the ecological balance of its habitat. Dolichogyne rigida is characterized by its elongated body and robust legs, which aid in its movement through dense vegetation.

Its presence is often an indicator of a healthy, biodiverse ecosystem.
