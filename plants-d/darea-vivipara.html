Darea vivipara

Darea vivipara, commonly known as the live-bearing dandelion, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and is characterized by its basal rosette of lance-shaped leaves and bright yellow composite flowers. The plant produces seeds that are dispersed by wind, with each seed having a distinctive parachute-like structure. It is a perennial herb that thrives in temperate climates.
