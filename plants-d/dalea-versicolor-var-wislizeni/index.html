Dalea Versicolor Var. Wislizeni

Dalea versicolor var. wislizeni is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the southwestern United States. It is known for its vibrant, colorful blooms that shift in hue depending on the time of day. This variety of Dalea is often found in rocky soils and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for native gardens and conservation efforts. The plant plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and maintaining ecological balance.
