Dicliptera decorticans

Dicliptera decorticans, commonly known as the African grass or African feather grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and feathery, silver-green foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant has fibrous roots and produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and disturbed areas.

Dicliptera decorticans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and erosion control.
