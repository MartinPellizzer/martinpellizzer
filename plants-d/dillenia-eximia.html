Dillenia eximia

Dillenia eximia, commonly known as the giant dillenia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers that are typically borne in clusters at the base of the plant. Its fruit is a woody, fleshy capsule that splits open to release numerous small, winged seeds.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
