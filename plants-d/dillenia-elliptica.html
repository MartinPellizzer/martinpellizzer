Dillenia elliptica

Dillenia elliptica, commonly known as the broadleaf dillenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, yellowish-white flowers with a distinctive, cup-shaped base. Its fruit is a woody, spiny capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Dillenia elliptica is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and along stream banks.
