Doliocarpus elliptifolius

Doliocarpus elliptifolius, commonly known as the elliptifolius sycamore, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its alternate, elliptical leaves that are typically smooth and dark green on top. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually greenish-yellow in color. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

This species is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its durable wood.
