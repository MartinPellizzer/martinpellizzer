Desmodium canescens var. hirsutum

Desmodium canescens var. hirsutum, commonly known as hairy desmodium, is a perennial legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features trifoliate leaves with hairy surfaces, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. It is often found in open woodlands and disturbed soils, thriving in warm climates.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it a valuable plant for soil enrichment and forage.
