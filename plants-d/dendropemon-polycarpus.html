Dendropemon polycarpus

Dendropemon polycarpus, commonly known as the yellow-leafed orchid, is a rare and striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand and Malaysia. This plant is characterized by its vibrant yellow petals and long, slender stems that often grow along forest floors or in shaded areas. It thrives in humid, warm climates with high levels of indirect light.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly sought after by botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
