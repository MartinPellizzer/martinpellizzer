Driessenia crystallina

Driessenia crystallina, commonly known as the crystalline fern, is a species of fern native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that are covered in fine, translucent spores. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and conservation efforts.

Driessenia crystallina plays an important role in its ecosystem by providing habitat for various microorganisms and small invertebrates.
