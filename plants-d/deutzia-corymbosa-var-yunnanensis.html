Deutzia corymbosa var. yunnanensis

Deutzia corymbosa var. yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features clusters of fragrant, white to pink flowers that bloom in early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy on the underside. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value lies in its showy blooms and compact growth habit.
