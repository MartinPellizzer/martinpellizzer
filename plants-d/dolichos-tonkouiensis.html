Dolichos tonkouiensis

Dolichos tonkouiensis, commonly known as the Tonkinese long bean, is a climbing legume native to Southeast Asia.

It produces elongated, edible pods that are often used in traditional cuisines. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and bears small, yellowish flowers. Its roots are sometimes used in herbal medicine for their purported health benefits.

The species is valued for both its nutritional content and ornamental appeal.
