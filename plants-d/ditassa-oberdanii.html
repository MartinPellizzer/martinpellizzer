Ditassa oberdanii

Ditassa oberdanii, commonly known as the dwarf African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy yellow flowers. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, daisy-like blooms in shades of yellow and white. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant appearance and low maintenance requirements.
