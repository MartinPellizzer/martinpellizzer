Dyctisperma urticifolius

Dyctisperma urticifolius, commonly known as the stinging nettle tree, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the family Urticaceae, which includes many species known for their stinging hairs. The plant produces small, greenish flowers arranged in clusters, and its leaves are typically heart-shaped with a rough texture. The stems are covered in fine, hollow hairs that can cause an itchy reaction when touched.

It is often found in moist, wooded areas and can grow up to several meters in height.
