Dimeria ornithopoda f. subrobusta

Dimeria ornithopoda f. subrobusta, commonly known as the robust dimeria, is a variant of the dimeria plant characterized by its more sturdy and robust growth habit.

It belongs to the family Cactaceae, which includes various succulent plants adapted to arid environments. This particular form exhibits dense, fleshy stems that are often covered in a layer of fine, white hairs, providing protection against intense sunlight and water loss. The leaves are typically reduced to small, scale-like structures, a common adaptation in cacti to minimize transpiration.

It produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding ornamental value to its appearance.
