Dioscorea cachipuertensis

Dioscorea cachipuertensis, commonly known as the Cachipuerto yam, is a species of flowering plant in the family Dioscoreaceae.

It is native to Central America, specifically found in countries like Guatemala and Honduras. The plant is a vine that produces tuberous roots, which are the primary part used for culinary and medicinal purposes. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a rough texture, while its flowers are small and greenish-white in color.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
