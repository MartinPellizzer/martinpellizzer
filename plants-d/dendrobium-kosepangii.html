Dendrobium kosepangii

Dendrobium kosepangii, commonly known as the Kosepangii orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its tall, cane-like stems and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and white. The plant thrives in humid, warm climates and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth. Its flowers are often short-lived but are prized for their beauty and unique coloration.

Dendrobium kosepangii is also valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
