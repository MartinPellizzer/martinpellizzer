Dipladenia Illustris Subvar. Oblongifolia

Oblongleaf dipladenia, scientifically known as Dipladenia illustris subvar. oblongifolia, is a striking tropical vine native to South America. This plant is prized for its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light to flourish. Its heart-shaped leaves add an elegant touch to any indoor or outdoor garden. With proper care, oblongleaf dipladenia can become a stunning focal point in any plant collection.
