Diasperus billardierei

Diasperus billardierei, commonly known as the narrow-leafed daisy, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Diasperus billardierei is often used in native landscaping due to its hardiness and ornamental value.
