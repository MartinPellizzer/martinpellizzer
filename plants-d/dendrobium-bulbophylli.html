Dendrobium bulbophylli

Dendrobium bulbophylli, commonly known as the Bulbophyll Orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic orchid that thrives in humid, tropical environments. The plant features long, cane-like stems that can grow up to several meters in height. Its flowers are typically white or pale yellow, with intricate patterns and a strong, sweet fragrance.

Dendrobium bulbophylli is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
