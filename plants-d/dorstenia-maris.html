Dorstenia maris

Dorstenia maris, commonly known as the sea dorstenia, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, spirally arranged leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and is adapted to saline and windy coastal environments.

Dorstenia maris is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
