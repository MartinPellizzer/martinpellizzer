Dictamnus caucasicus var. angustifolius

Dictamnus caucasicus var. angustifolius, commonly known as narrow-leaved dictamnus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Caucasus region.

It features aromatic, glandular leaves with a distinctive reddish or purplish hue, often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white or pale pink flowers during the summer months. Its stems are typically woody at the base and can become somewhat aromatic when crushed.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or mountainous habitats.
