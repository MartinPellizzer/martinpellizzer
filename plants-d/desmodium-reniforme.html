Desmodium reniforme

Desmodium reniforme, commonly known as sicklepod, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, pea-like flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces pods that are curved and resemble a sickle, giving it its common name. It thrives in warm, temperate climates and is often found in disturbed soils.

Desmodium reniforme is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial in certain agricultural settings.
