Dunnaria dunnii

Dunnaria dunnii, commonly known as the New Zealand tree fern, is a member of the Cyatheaceae family.

It is native to New Zealand and thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a tall, unbranched trunk covered in a fibrous, scaly bark. Its fronds are large, feather-like, and can grow up to several meters in length.

Dunnaria dunnii plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
