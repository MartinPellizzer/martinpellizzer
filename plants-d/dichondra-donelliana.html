Dichondra donelliana

Dichondra donelliana, commonly known as silverleaf dichondra, is a low-growing, mat-forming plant native to South America.

It features small, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated as a ground cover in gardens.
