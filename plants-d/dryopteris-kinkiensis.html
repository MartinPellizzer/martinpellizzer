Dryopteris kinkiensis

Dryopteris kinkiensis, commonly known as the Japanese wood fern, is a species of fern native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its tall, upright fronds that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant features a rhizomatous growth habit, with fertile fronds bearing sori on the underside. Its leaves are typically bi- or tri-pinnate, with a dark green color and a leathery texture.

This fern thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forests and along stream banks.
