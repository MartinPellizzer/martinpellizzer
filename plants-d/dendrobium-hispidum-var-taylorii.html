Dendrobium hispidum var. taylorii

Dendrobium hispidum var. taylorii, commonly known as the Taylor's hairy orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a dense, hairy stem that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces numerous fragrant, yellow to orange flowers arranged in racemes. Its leaves are elliptical and often covered in fine pubescence.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental beauty.
