Dendrobium Proliferum

Proliferous dendrobium, scientifically known as Dendrobium proliferum, is a striking orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. This plant is celebrated for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms that often display a dazzling array of colors. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is commonly found in shaded forest areas or on tree trunks. Due to its ornamental value, it has become a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation. Its unique growth pattern and ability to produce multiple shoots make it a favorite among plant enthusiasts.
