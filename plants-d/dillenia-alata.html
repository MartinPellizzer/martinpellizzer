Dillenia alata

Dillenia alata, commonly known as the broadleaf dillenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Rosaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, large, palmately lobed leaves. The plant produces showy, yellowish-white flowers that are typically borne in clusters. Its fruit is a fleshy, woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.
