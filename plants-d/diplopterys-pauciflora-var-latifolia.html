Diplopterys pauciflora var. latifolia

Diplopterys pauciflora var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved yohimbe, is a climbing shrub native to the rainforests of western Africa.

It is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing elliptical seeds. It belongs to the Rubiaceae family, which includes other plants known for their medicinal properties.
