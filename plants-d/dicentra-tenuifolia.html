Dicentra tenuifolia

Dicentra tenuifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf Dutchman's pipe, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern North America.

It features delicate, heart-shaped leaves and distinctive, pipe-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red in color. The plant thrives in partial to full shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens. Its unique floral structure resembles a narrow tube, giving it its common name.

Dicentra tenuifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
