Dendrochilum carinatum

Dendrochilum carinatum, commonly known as the ridge-lipped orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive ridge on the lip, which is a key feature in its classification. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrochilum carinatum is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
