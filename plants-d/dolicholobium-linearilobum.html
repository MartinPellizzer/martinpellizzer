Dolicholobium linearilobum

Dolicholobium linearilobum, commonly known as the narrow-lobed dolicholobium, is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is a shrub characterized by its slender, often branched stems and small, linear leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by elongated, bean-like pods. Native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa, it is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies in dryland ecosystems.
