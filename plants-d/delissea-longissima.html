Delissea longissima

Delissea longissima, commonly known as the long-stemmed orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated stems and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil and decaying organic matter.

Delissea longissima is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
