Distephanus poissonii

Distephanus poissonii, commonly known as the Madagascar starfish plant, is a unique succulent native to Madagascar.

It features a rosette of fleshy, blue-gray leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces striking star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom in the spring. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
