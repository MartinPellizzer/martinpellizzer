Dendrobium sordidum

Dendrobium sordidum, commonly known as the smoky orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves and produces clusters of small, pale green to brown flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and swamps. Its flowers emit a faint, sweet scent, which helps attract pollinators.

Dendrobium sordidum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique appearance.
