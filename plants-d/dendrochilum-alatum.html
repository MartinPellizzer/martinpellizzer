Dendrochilum alatum

Dendrochilum alatum, commonly known as the winged orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, lance-shaped leaves and its unique, winged sepals that give it its common name. The plant produces fragrant, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters during the wet season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting mix.

Dendrochilum alatum is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
