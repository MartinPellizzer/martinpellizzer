Deutzia calycosa var. longisepala

Deutzia calycosa var. longisepala, commonly known as the long-sepaled deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features clusters of fragrant, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, arching branches and heart-shaped leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
