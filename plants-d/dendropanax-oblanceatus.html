Dendropanax oblanceatus

Dendropanax oblanceatus, commonly known as the broadleaf arrowleaf, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal and subtropical regions. The plant features simple, alternate, and often broad, ovate to elliptical leaves with a prominent midrib. It produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, followed by small, fleshy fruits.

The species is notable for its adaptability to various soil types and its ecological importance in native habitats.
