Doritis lowii

Doritis lowii, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a species of flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the family Cactaceae and is characterized by its segmented stems and vibrant flowers. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, typically blooming during the holiday season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Doritis lowii is popular as a decorative plant due to its long blooming period and ease of care.
