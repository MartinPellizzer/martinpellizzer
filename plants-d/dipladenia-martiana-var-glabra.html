Dipladenia martiana var. glabra

Dipladenia martiana var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth dipladenia, is a climbing vine native to South America.

It is characterized by its smooth, hairless stems and heart-shaped, glossy leaves. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of orange and yellow, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in warm, sunny climates and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is popular in ornamental horticulture for its vibrant blooms and ability to cascade from supports or trellises.
