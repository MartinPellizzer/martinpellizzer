Dieffenbachia cognata

Dieffenbachia cognata, commonly known as the Leopard Plant, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with dark green veins and cream or white spots. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like peace lilies and calla lilies. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Dieffenbachia cognata is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
