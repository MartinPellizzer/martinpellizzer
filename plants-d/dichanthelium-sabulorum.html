Dichanthelium sabulorum

Dichanthelium sabulorum, commonly known as sand panicgrass, is a perennial grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in sandy or well-drained soils, often found in open habitats such as roadsides, prairies, and disturbed areas. The plant has slender, erect stems that produce small, spike-like inflorescences with numerous tiny flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

Dichanthelium sabulorum is adapted to coastal and inland dune environments, showcasing resilience to harsh environmental conditions.
