Delphinium sylvaticum subsp. ichkeulianum

Delphinium sylvaticum subsp.

ichkeulianum, commonly known as the Ichkeul delphinium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North Africa. It typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

The plant features tall, sparsely branched stems that bear clusters of striking blue to violet flowers. Its leaves are lance-shaped and slightly hairy, contributing to its ornamental value.

This subspecies is noted for its ecological importance in supporting local pollinators and its resilience in harsh environmental conditions.
