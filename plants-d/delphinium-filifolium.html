Delphinium filifolium

Delphinium filifolium, commonly known as the silky leaf larkspur, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of blue to purple flowers. The leaves are narrow and silky in texture, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in alpine and subalpine regions.

This species is part of the Ranunculaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
