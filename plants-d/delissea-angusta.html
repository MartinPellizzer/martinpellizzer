Delissea angusta

Delissea angusta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved delissea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, yellowish flowers appear in clusters and bloom during the warmer months.
