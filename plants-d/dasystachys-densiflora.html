Dasystachys densiflora

Dasystachys densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered grass.

It is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa. The plant is characterized by its dense, compact inflorescence and narrow, grass-like leaves. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun.

Its botanical features make it a valuable plant for ecological restoration and ornamental use in landscaping.
