Deiregyne tenella

Deiregyne tenella, commonly known as the narrow-leafed daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides. The plant features small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in a daisy-like pattern, with a central disc surrounded by ray florets. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a slight bluish tinge.

Deiregyne tenella is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
