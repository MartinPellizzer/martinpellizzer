Dypsis prestoniana

Dypsis prestoniana, commonly known as the Preston's areca palm, is a tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is a member of the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright trunk and pinnate leaves. The plant typically grows to a height of 6 to 10 meters and has a slow growth rate. It thrives in warm, humid climates with well-draining soil and requires moderate to bright indirect light.

Dypsis prestoniana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its elegant appearance and ability to adapt to indoor conditions.
