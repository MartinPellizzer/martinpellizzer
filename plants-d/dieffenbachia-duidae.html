Dieffenbachia duidae

Dieffenbachia duidae, commonly known as the Giant Dieffenbachia, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, waxy, heart-shaped leaves that are often speckled with white or cream-colored patterns. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which is known for its distinctive inflorescence structures. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity to flourish.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated as a houseplant.
