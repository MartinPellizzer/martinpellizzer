Dolichos chevalieri

Dolichos chevalieri, commonly known as the African bean, is a climbing legume native to Africa.

It features compound leaves with three to five oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellow flowers. The plant bears pods that contain edible seeds, which are often used in traditional cuisines. It thrives in warm, tropical climates and is cultivated for its nutritional value.

Dolichos chevalieri is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, benefiting agricultural practices.
