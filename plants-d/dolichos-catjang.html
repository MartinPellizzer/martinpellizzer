Dolichos catjang

Dolichos catjang, commonly known as catjang bean, is a climbing legume native to Southeast Asia.

It produces long, slender pods that contain several small, pale yellow seeds. The plant is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and hairy stems, typical of many leguminous species. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its edible seeds.

The botanical features of Dolichos catjang make it well-suited for both agricultural and ecological purposes.
