Dracaena yuccifolia

Dracaena yuccifolia, commonly known as the Yucca-like Dracaena, is a striking evergreen shrub native to southern China.

It features tall, sword-shaped leaves that are typically bluish-green in color, with a waxy texture and prominent veins. The plant produces small, pale green flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a favored plant in both residential and commercial landscapes.
