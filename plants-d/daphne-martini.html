Daphne martini

Daphne martini, commonly known as the winter daphne, is a deciduous shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features glossy, dark green leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, waxy flowers in shades of pink, white, and red. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in garden landscapes for its winter interest. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Daphne martini is relatively low maintenance but requires protection from frost and heavy moisture.
