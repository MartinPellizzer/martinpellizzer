Dendrobium verlaquii

Dendrobium verlaquii, commonly known as the Verlaquii orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It features a tall, erect stem that can grow up to 1 meter in height, often adorned with numerous, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, pale yellow to cream-colored flowers with a distinctive red or orange lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Dendrobium verlaquii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
