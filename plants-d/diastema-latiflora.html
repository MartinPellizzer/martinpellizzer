Diastema latiflora

Diastema latiflora, commonly known as the broad-flowered diastema, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are broad and elliptical, with a smooth, glossy surface.

Diastema latiflora is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
