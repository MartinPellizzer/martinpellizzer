Dissochaeta intermedia var. sagittata

Dissochaeta intermedia var. sagittata, commonly known as the arrowhead morning glory, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often opening in the morning. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can be found in gardens and natural habitats.
