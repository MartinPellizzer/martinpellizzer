Didymocarpus macrocalyx

Didymocarpus macrocalyx, commonly known as the large-flowered dillenia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with five broad petals. The plant produces distinctive, woody fruits that split open to release seeds. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a velvety texture.

Didymocarpus macrocalyx is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
