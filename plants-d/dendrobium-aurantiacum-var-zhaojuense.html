Dendrobium aurantiacum var. zhaojuense

Dendrobium aurantiacum var. zhaojuense, commonly known as the Zhaojuense Dendrobium, is a rare orchid species native to China.

It is a sympodial epiphyte, typically growing on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. The plant features a pseudobulb structure with a cluster of leaves and produces striking orange-red flowers. Its flowers are highly fragrant and bloom during the summer months.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
