Dianella revoluta var. revoluta

Dianella revoluta var. revoluta, commonly known as the silver sword lily, is a perennial plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its rosette of narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often appearing in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or as an ornamental plant.

Its foliage and floral display make it a popular choice for coastal and Mediterranean climates.
