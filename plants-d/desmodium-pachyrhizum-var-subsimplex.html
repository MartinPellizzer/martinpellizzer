Desmodium pachyrhizum var. subsimplex

Desmodium pachyrhizum var. subsimplex, commonly known as the slender sicklepod, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces elongated, curved pods that contain several seeds, which are typically small and dark in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it a valuable plant for soil enrichment and ecological restoration.
