Dipterocome pusilla

Dipterocome pusilla, commonly known as the dwarf dipterocarp, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Dipterocarpaceae family, which is characterized by its tropical rainforest habitat and hard, heavy wood. The plant typically grows to a height of less than 10 meters and has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. Dipterocome pusilla plays a minor role in forest ecosystems but is valued for its ornamental qualities and traditional uses.
