Desmanthodium congestum

Desmanthodium congestum, commonly known as the clustered desmanthium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its flowering period occurs during the summer months, producing small, yellowish flowers that attract pollinators.

The species is noted for its compact growth habit and is often found in coastal and inland regions with well-drained soils.
