Dictyopteris distincta

Dictyopteris distincta, commonly known as the distinct sea palm, is a species of brown algae belonging to the family Dictyopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its fronds that are typically narrow and elongated, with a distinct midrib and a serrated or lobed margin.
