Dioscorea kjellbergii

Dioscorea kjellbergii, commonly known as the Kjellberg yam, is a species of flowering plant in the family Dioscoreaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its climbing habit and tuberous roots. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically arranged in clusters. Its tubers are often used as a food source in some local communities.

The plant's botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and agricultural contexts.
