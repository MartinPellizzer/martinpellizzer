Dichanthelium stigmosum

Dichanthelium stigmosum, commonly known as the slender panicgrass, is a grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It is a perennial plant characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and compact inflorescence. The plant typically grows in wet or moist habitats such as marshes, swales, and ditches. Its flowers are small and clustered, with two bracts at the base of each spikelet.

Dichanthelium stigmosum plays a role in stabilizing soil in wetland environments and provides habitat for various small invertebrates.
