Dodonaea viscosa f. arborescens

Dodonaea viscosa f. arborescens, commonly known as the velvetleaf hopperbush, is a shrub or small tree native to Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The leaves are opposite, ovate to elliptic in shape, and have a soft, fuzzy texture. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by woody, spherical fruits that contain numerous seeds.

This plant is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in various soil conditions.
