Dioscorea kunthiana

Dioscorea kunthiana, commonly known as the yellow arrowhead vine, is a climbing herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its tuberous roots, which are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by heart-shaped berries that contain the seeds. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a waxy surface, while its stems are covered in fine hairs.

Dioscorea kunthiana is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
