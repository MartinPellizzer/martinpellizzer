Dendrobium uniflorum var. angustum

Dendrobium uniflorum var. angustum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf dendrobium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. The plant features elongated, narrow leaves that are often clustered along its stem. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with a distinctive coloration that ranges from white to pale yellow.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique foliage and blooming habits.
