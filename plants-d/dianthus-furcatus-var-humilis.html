Dianthus furcatus var. humilis

Dianthus furcatus var. humilis, commonly known as the dwarf carnation, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense clusters of pink to red flowers with white markings, blooming from spring to early summer. The plant has compact, upright stems with narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting flowers and compact growth habit.
