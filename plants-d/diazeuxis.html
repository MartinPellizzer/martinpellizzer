Diazeuxis

Diazeuxis, commonly known as the black nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, dark purple berries that mature in the fall. The plant has hairy stems and broad, ovate leaves with a slightly rough texture. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in gardens and wild areas.

However, its berries are toxic and should not be consumed by humans or animals.
