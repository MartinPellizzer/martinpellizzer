Delissea leptostegia var. velutina

Delissea leptostegia var. velutina, commonly known as the velvetleaf delissea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, velvety stems and leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, and it is often found in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are typically ovate to lanceolate in shape, with a rough texture.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
