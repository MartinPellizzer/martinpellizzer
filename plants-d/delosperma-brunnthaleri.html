Delosperma brunnthaleri

Delosperma brunnthaleri, commonly known as the Cape Hottentot, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, known for its characteristic adaptations to arid environments. The plant features fleshy, ribbed stems and produces vibrant, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange. Its ability to store water in its leaves allows it to survive in drought conditions.
