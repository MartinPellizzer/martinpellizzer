Deutzia hebecarpa

Deutzia hebecarpa, commonly known as the smooth deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features slender, arching stems that bear clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers in early summer. The plant has simple, ovate leaves that are typically hairy on the underside. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Deutzia hebecarpa is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
