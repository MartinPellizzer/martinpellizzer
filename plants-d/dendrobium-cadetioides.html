Dendrobium cadetioides

Dendrobium cadetioides, commonly known as the Cadet's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in tropical rainforests. The plant features long, cane-like pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, and it produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. Its leaves are strap-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stems.

Dendrobium cadetioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
