Draba incerta

Draba incerta, commonly known as the uncertain draba, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a small annual herb that typically grows in alpine and arctic regions, often found in rocky soils and open habitats. The plant features basal leaves that are linear to lanceolate, and its flowers are small, yellow, and arranged in a cluster. Draba incerta is known for its ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions due to its hardy nature.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native ecosystems and is of interest to botanists studying plant adaptation.
