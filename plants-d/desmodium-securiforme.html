Desmodium securiforme

Desmodium securiforme, commonly known as the sickleleaf desmodium, is a species of legume native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which have a distinctive sickle-shaped leaflet. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland and wetland habitats.

Desmodium securiforme is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil fertility.
