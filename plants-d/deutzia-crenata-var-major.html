Deutzia crenata var. major

Deutzia crenata var. major, commonly known as the Japanese spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features showy clusters of white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring, adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant has slender, arching stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its compact growth habit and long-lasting floral display.
