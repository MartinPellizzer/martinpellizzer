Dubautia laxa var. hirsuta

Dubautia laxa var. hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy Dubautia, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

Dubautia laxa var. hirsuta is often found in mesic to xeric habitats, showcasing its adaptability to the unique ecosystems of Hawaii.
