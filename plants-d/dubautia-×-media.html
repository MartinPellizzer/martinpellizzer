Dubautia × media

Dubautia × media, commonly known as the hybrid hedgehog cactus, is a succulent plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a hybrid between Dubautia scabra and Dubautia stenophylla, exhibiting characteristics of both parent species. The plant features cylindrical stems covered in short, spiny bristles and produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions.
