Dolichos pteropus

Dolichos pteropus, commonly known as the winged bean, is a climbing legume native to Southeast Asia.

It produces distinctive heart-shaped leaves with prominent veins and long, slender pods that contain edible seeds. The plant is valued for its nutritional content, including high levels of protein and essential amino acids. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often grown in humid, well-drained soils.

The flowers are small and typically white or pale purple, contributing to its ornamental appeal.
