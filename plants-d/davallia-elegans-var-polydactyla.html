Davallia elegans var. polydactyla

Davallia elegans var. polydactyla, commonly known as the feather-leaf holly fern, is a compact, evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, lace-like fronds with a feathery appearance, giving it a graceful and ornamental look. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with short, creeping rhizomes that produce clusters of small, rounded leaves. It prefers bright, indirect light and consistently moist, well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its aesthetic appeal and is often used in indoor gardening and rock gardens.
