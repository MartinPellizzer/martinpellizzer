Dendrochilum carnosum

Dendrochilum carnosum, commonly known as the fleshy-leafed orchid, is a species native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, often with a velvety texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
