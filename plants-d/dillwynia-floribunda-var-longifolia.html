Dillwynia floribunda var. longifolia

Dillwynia floribunda var. longifolia, commonly known as the long-leaved dillwynia, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its pod-like fruits and compound leaves. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands. Its flowers are small, clustered in inflorescences, and range in color from white to pale purple.
