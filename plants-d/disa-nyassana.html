Disa nyassana

Disa nyassana, commonly known as the Nyassa orchid, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and has a short, creeping rhizome with upright, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

Disa nyassana is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
