Desmotrichum roseopunctatum

Desmotrichum roseopunctatum, commonly known as the pink-spotted sandmat, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant features small, yellowish to orange flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered with fine, white hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance.

It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.
