Doona Nitida

Doona nitida is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. It is known for its striking, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale yellow. Despite its beauty, the plant is highly sensitive to environmental changes and is considered endangered in its natural habitat. Conservation efforts are underway to protect and propagate this species. Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it a subject of interest for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike.
