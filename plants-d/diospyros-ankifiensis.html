Diospyros ankifiensis

Diospyros ankifiensis, commonly known as the Ankifien persimmon, is a species of flowering plant in the Ebenaceae family.

It is native to the tropical rainforests of West Africa, particularly in countries like Cameroon and Nigeria. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers. Its fruit is a drupe, typically reddish when mature, and contains a single seed.

The tree is valued for its hard, dense wood and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
