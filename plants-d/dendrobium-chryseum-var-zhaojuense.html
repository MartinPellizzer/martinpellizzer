Dendrobium chryseum var. zhaojuense

Dendrobium chryseum var. zhaojuense, commonly known as the Zhaojuense Dendrobium, is a rare orchid species native to China.

It is characterized by its striking golden-yellow flowers and erect, cane-like pseudobulbs. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on cliffs or trees. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a spiral pattern.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
