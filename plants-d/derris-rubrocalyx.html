Derris rubrocalyx

Derris rubrocalyx, commonly known as red calyx derris, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive red calyces that give it its common name. The plant produces small, yellow flowers followed by pods containing several seeds. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

Derris rubrocalyx is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.
