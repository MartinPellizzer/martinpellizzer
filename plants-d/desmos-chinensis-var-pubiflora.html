Desmos chinensis var. pubiflora

Desmos chinensis var. pubiflora, commonly known as the hairy-flowered desmos, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are often hairy on the underside. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

The hairy-flowered desmos is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
