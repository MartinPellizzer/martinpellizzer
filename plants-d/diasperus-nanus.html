Diasperus nanus

Diasperus nanus, commonly known as the dwarf rock cress, is a small flowering plant native to arid and rocky environments.

It belongs to the family Brassicaceae, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant has small, lance-shaped leaves and produces tiny white or yellow flowers. It is adapted to survive in harsh conditions with minimal water.

Diasperus nanus is often found in crevices and on rocky slopes, where it thrives in well-drained soil.
