Draba beltranii

Draba beltranii, commonly known as the Beltran's rockcress, is a small flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe and parts of North Africa. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in well-drained soils. Its flowers are small, white or pale yellow, and appear in early spring.

Draba beltranii is characterized by its low-growing habit and dense clusters of leaves, making it a hardy and resilient species.
