Dieffenbachia jenmanii

Dieffenbachia jenmanii, commonly known as the Shiny-leaf Dieffenbachia, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, broad, and glossy dark green leaves with prominent white veins. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other popular houseplants like peace lilies. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to prevent root rot.

Its striking foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
