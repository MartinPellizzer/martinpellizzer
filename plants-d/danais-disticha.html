Danais disticha

Danais disticha, commonly known as the zigzag geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzagging stems and clusters of small, pink to purple flowers. The plant belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in gardens. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Danais disticha thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
