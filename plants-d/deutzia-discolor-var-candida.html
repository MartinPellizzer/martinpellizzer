Deutzia discolor var. candida

Deutzia discolor var. candida, commonly known as the white deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features clusters of fragrant, white flowers that bloom in early summer, adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant has slender, arching stems and dark green, elliptical leaves that turn reddish in the fall. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for a variety of garden settings.

Its compact size and showy blooms make it a popular choice for borders and woodland gardens.
