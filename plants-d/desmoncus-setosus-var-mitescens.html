Desmoncus setosus var. mitescens

Desmoncus setosus var. mitescens, commonly known as the hairy desmoncus, is a variant of the desmoncus genus characterized by its distinctive hairy foliage.

This plant is native to specific regions in Africa and is typically found in rocky or arid environments. Its leaves are elongated and covered in fine, stiff hairs, which provide protection against desiccation. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pale green or white in color.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in dry, well-drained gardens.
