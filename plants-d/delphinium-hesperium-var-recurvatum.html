Delphinium hesperium var. recurvatum

Delphinium hesperium var. recurvatum, commonly known as the Sierra delphinium, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It features showy, blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, upright stems with lance-shaped leaves that are deeply lobed. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for alpine gardens and wildflower displays.
