Dendrobium pictum var. muriciferum

Dendrobium pictum var. muriciferum, commonly known as the spotted orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome that produces multiple stems. The plant has lanceolate leaves that are dark green with a waxy coating. Its flowers are typically yellow with dark purple or brown markings, arranged in racemes.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
