Delonix leucantha subsp. leucantha

Delonix leucantha subsp.

leucantha, commonly known as the white flame tree, is a tropical species native to regions of Africa. It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a striking visual display.

The plant has pinnate leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant blossoms.

The subspecies is distinguished by its pale yellow flowers, setting it apart from other varieties within the Delonix genus.
