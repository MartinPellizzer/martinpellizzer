Dicranostyles ampla var. attenuata

Dicranostyles ampla var. attenuata, commonly known as the slender haircap moss, is a variant of the haircap moss genus Dicranostyles.

It is a small, terrestrial moss that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features a dense, cushion-like growth form with closely spaced, leafy shoots. Its leaves are elongated and slightly curved, with a smooth, glossy surface.

This moss is characterized by its delicate, hair-like structures that give it its common name and contribute to its distinctive appearance.
