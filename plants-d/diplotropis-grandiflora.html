Diplotropis grandiflora

Diplotropis grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered diplotropis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences and palmate leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, tropical environments and is often found in shaded undergrowth. Its flowers are usually pink or red, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
