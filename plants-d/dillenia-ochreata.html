Dillenia ochreata

Dillenia ochreata, commonly known as the golden dillenia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Dilleniaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including countries like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are typically large, heart-shaped, and have a rough texture.

The fruit is a woody capsule that contains several seeds, and the plant is often found in moist, forested areas.
