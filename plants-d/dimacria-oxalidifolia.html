Dimacria oxalidifolia

Dimacria oxalidifolia, commonly known as the Cape oxalis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Oxalidaceae and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant produces edible seeds that are often used in traditional cuisine. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Dimacria oxalidifolia is also valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
