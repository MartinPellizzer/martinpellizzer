Dombeya astylosa

Dombeya astylosa, commonly known as the silky dombeya, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Africa.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that range from white to pink, typically blooming in summer. The plant has soft, hairy leaves that give it a velvety appearance, and its stems are often covered in fine down. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Dombeya astylosa is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
