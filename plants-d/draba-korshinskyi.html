Draba korshinskyi

Draba korshinskyi, commonly known as the Korshinsky's winter cress, is a small flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is native to the high-altitude regions of Central Asia, including parts of Kazakhstan and the Tien Shan mountains. This plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in cold and arid conditions. It produces small, white or yellow flowers and has a compact, low-growing habit.

Draba korshinskyi is adapted to survive in harsh climates, often flowering in late winter or early spring.
