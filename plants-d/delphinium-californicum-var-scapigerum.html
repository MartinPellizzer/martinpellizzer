Delphinium californicum var. scapigerum

Delphinium californicum var. scapigerum, commonly known as the California larkspur, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It features striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has a basal rosette of lance-shaped leaves and tall, slender stems that support its showy inflorescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for gardens and natural landscapes.
