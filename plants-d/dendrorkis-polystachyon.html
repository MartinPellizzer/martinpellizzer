Dendrorkis polystachyon

Dendrorkis polystachyon, commonly known as the giant sedge, is a tall, herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces dense clusters of small, pale yellow flowers, which are arranged in spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in wet, marshy environments and is often found in swamps and along riverbanks.

Due to its height and robust growth, it plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife.
