Dendrobium latifolium

Dendrobium latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with long, fleshy roots and upright, branching stems. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink, often arranged in racemes. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Dendrobium latifolium thrives in moist, shaded environments and is valued in horticulture for its beauty and resilience.
