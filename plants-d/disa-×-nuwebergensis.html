Disa × nuwebergensis

Disa × nuwebergensis, commonly known as the Nuweberg orchid, is a hybrid orchid native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its flowers are usually borne on tall, slender stems and can display a range of hues including pink, red, and white.

Disa × nuwebergensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
