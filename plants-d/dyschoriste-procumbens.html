Dyschoriste procumbens

Dyschoriste procumbens, commonly known as the prostrate dyschoriste, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a low-growing herbaceous species that typically forms dense mats on the ground. The plant features small, white to pale yellow flowers clustered in loose inflorescences. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered with fine hairs.

Dyschoriste procumbens is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forest understories and stream banks.
