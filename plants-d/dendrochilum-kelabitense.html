Dendrochilum kelabitense

Dendrochilum kelabitense, commonly known as the Kelabit orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit and produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant has linear to lance-shaped leaves and is often found growing on the forest floor or in shaded, moist environments. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its ornamental value.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a rare and threatened species in its native range.
