Dyckia maxima

Dyckia maxima, commonly known as the giant dyckia, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, upright rosette of long, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to 2 meters in height. The leaves are typically dark green with a waxy texture and often have a reddish or purplish hue along the edges. It produces showy, red-pink flower spikes that emerge from the center of the rosette, adding dramatic visual appeal.

This plant is well-suited for outdoor cultivation in warm, sunny climates and requires well-draining soil to thrive.
