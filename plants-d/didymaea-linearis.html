Didymaea linearis

Didymaea linearis, commonly known as the slender didymaea, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are linear and often slightly hairy. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in a spike-like inflorescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its delicate appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in some regions.
