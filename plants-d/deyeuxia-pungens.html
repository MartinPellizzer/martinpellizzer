Deyeuxia pungens

Deyeuxia pungens, commonly known as the spiky grass or sharp grass, is a perennial grass species native to various regions of Asia.

It typically grows in wet or moist environments, such as marshes and floodplains, and is characterized by its slender, erect stems. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers, which are usually wind-pollinated. Its leaves are narrow and often have a rough texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Deyeuxia pungens is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and ecological benefits in wetland restoration.
