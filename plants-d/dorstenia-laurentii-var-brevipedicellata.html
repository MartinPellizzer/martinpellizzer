Dorstenia laurentii var. brevipedicellata

Dorstenia laurentii var. brevipedicellata, commonly known as the short-stemmed arrowleaf, is a unique member of the Dioscoreaceae family.

It is characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is native to parts of Central and South America.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
