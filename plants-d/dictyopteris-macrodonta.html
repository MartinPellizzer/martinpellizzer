Dictyopteris macrodonta

Dictyopteris macrodonta, commonly known as the giant feather moss, is a species of marine brown alga.

It is characterized by its large, feather-like fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant has a complex thallus structure with a central axis and branching, filamentous structures. It thrives in shallow, temperate coastal waters where it attaches to rocky substrates.

Its reproductive structures, including both male and female gametangia, are typically found on the undersides of the fronds.
