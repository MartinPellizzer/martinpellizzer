Dimorphanthera wollastonii

Dimorphanthera wollastonii, commonly known as the Wollaston's orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, showy flowers that typically feature a combination of colors including white, pink, and purple. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a spike, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Dimorphanthera wollastonii is known for its delicate appearance and is often found growing in dense forest understories.
