Disparago kraussii

Disparago kraussii, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and often branched.

Disparago kraussii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
