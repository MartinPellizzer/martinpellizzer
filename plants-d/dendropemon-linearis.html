Dendropemon linearis

Dendropemon linearis, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and delicate, linear leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in a spike-like inflorescence. Dendropemon linearis is an epiphytic orchid, meaning it grows on the surfaces of trees or rocks rather than in soil.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest canopies.
