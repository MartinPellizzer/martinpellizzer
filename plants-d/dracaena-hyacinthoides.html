Dracaena hyacinthoides

Dracaena hyacinthoides, commonly known as the hyacinth dracaena, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Madagascar.

It features lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are often marked with silver or white stripes, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It is relatively low-maintenance and thrives in bright, indirect light with moderate watering.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental landscaping.
