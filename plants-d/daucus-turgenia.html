Daucus turgenia

Daucus turgenia, commonly known as Turgen's carrot, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes other familiar plants like parsley and dill. The plant features finely divided, fern-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in umbels. Its roots are typically thin and not as robust as those of the common carrot.
