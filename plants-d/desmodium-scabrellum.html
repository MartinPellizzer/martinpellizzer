Desmodium scabrellum

Desmodium scabrellum, commonly known as rough desmodium, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers that typically bloom in shades of purple or pink. The plant is native to various regions in the Americas and thrives in warm, sunny environments. It has hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name.

Desmodium scabrellum is often found in disturbed soils and can be used in ecological restoration projects due to its hardy nature.
