Dieffenbachia picta var. barraquiniana

Dieffenbachia picta var. barraquiniana, commonly known as the Barraquiniana Dieffenbachia, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It features broad, dark green leaves with distinctive white and yellow variegation patterns. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its foliage is often adorned with intricate, almost mosaic-like markings that add visual interest.

This variety is also valued for its air-purifying qualities and ability to enhance the aesthetic of any room.
