Dendrobium pycnostachyum

Dendrobium pycnostachyum, commonly known as the orchid with the dense-stemmed spikes, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is an epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand and Indonesia. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with cane-like stems that can reach several feet in length. Its flowers are typically white or pale yellow, with a fragrant scent, and bloom in clusters during the rainy season.

It requires bright, indirect light and high humidity to thrive, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
