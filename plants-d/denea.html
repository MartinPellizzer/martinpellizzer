Denea

Denea, commonly known as the golden trumpet or yellow trumpet, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Apocynaceae.

It is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas. Denea species are often cultivated for their ornamental value due to their vibrant blooms.
