Dictamnus obtusiflorus

Dictamnus obtusiflorus, commonly known as the blunt-flowered dictamnus, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, often pink or white flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has aromatic, glandular leaves that emit a strong, spicy scent when crushed. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its showy flowers and fragrant foliage.
