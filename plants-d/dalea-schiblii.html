Dalea schiblii

Dalea schiblii, commonly known as Schibli's milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its unique, spiny stems and small, pink to red flowers. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Dalea schiblii is often found in disturbed soils and is adapted to thrive in low-nutrient conditions.
