Duseniella

Duseniella, commonly known as the narrow-leafed dandelion, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, pointed leaves. The plant produces small, yellow composite flowers that bloom in the spring. It is typically found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands.

Duseniella is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
