Dasyglossum edwardii

Dasyglossum edwardii, commonly known as the Edward's dasyglossum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sandy soils and is often found in grassland habitats. It has linear, grass-like leaves and produces numerous tiny florets arranged in a composite head.
