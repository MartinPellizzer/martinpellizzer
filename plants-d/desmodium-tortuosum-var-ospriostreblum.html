Desmodium tortuosum var. ospriostreblum

Desmodium tortuosum var. ospriostreblum, commonly known as the Ospriostreblum desmodium, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant produces pods that contain several elliptical seeds, which are often dispersed by wind.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.
