Drapiezia multiflora

Drapiezia multiflora, commonly known as the many-flowered drapiezia, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. The leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
