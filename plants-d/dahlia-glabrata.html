Dahlia glabrata

Dahlia glabrata, commonly known as the smooth dahlia, is a herbaceous perennial native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its swollen, tuberous roots and showy, single or double flowers in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet, with upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers bloom in late summer to early fall, adding vibrant color to gardens.

The botanical features of D. glabrata make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
