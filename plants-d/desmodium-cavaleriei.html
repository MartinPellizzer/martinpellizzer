Desmodium cavaleriei

Desmodium cavaleriei, commonly known as the velvet bean or cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which are often covered in a fine, velvety pubescence. The plant produces small, spherical pods that contain several edible seeds. It is widely cultivated for its nutritious seeds, which are rich in protein and used in various cuisines.

Additionally, it has nitrogen-fixing capabilities, making it valuable in agricultural systems.
