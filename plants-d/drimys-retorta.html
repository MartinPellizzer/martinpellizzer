Drimys retorta

Drimys retorta, commonly known as the Chilean wintergreen, is a shrub native to the temperate rainforests of southern Chile and western Argentina.

It is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces berries that are red when mature, containing numerous seeds. Drimys retorta is adapted to moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

It has aromatic foliage and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
