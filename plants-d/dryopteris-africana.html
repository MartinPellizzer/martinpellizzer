Dryopteris africana

Dryopteris africana, commonly known as the African wood fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, leathery fronds that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are thick and fleshy, allowing it to store water and nutrients.

The fronds are bi-lobed and have a distinctive, elegant appearance, making it a popular ornamental plant.
