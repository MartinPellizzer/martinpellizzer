Delissea lobata var. hamakuae

Delissea lobata var. hamakuae, commonly known as the Kauai lobata, is a rare endemic plant found exclusively on the island of Kauai in Hawaii.

It is a shrub-like plant with lanceolate, leathery leaves that are often covered in fine, soft hairs. The flowers are small, yellowish-green, and typically appear in clusters, blooming during the wet season. It thrives in moist, shaded habitats such as mesic forests and grows at elevations between 300 and 1,200 meters.
