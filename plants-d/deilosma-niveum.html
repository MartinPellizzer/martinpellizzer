Deilosma niveum

Deilosma niveum, commonly known as the white-flowered morning glory, is a herbaceous vine native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, white or pale pink flowers that open in the morning. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded areas.

Deilosma niveum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
