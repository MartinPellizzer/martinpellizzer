Dahlia pubescens

Dahlia pubescens, commonly known as the woolly dahlia, is a herbaceous perennial native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its hairy, pubescent stems and leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces showy, daisy-like flowers in a range of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Dahlia pubescens is valued for its ornamental beauty and relatively low maintenance requirements.
