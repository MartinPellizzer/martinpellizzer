Danais pubescens f. glabra

Danais pubescens f. glabra, commonly known as the smooth danais, is a variant of the danais plant characterized by its lack of pubescence on its stems and leaves.

Its leaves are typically ovate to elliptic in shape, with a smooth surface that distinguishes it from other forms of the species.
