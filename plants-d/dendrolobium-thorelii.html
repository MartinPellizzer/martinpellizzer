Dendrolobium thorelii

Dendrolobium thorelii, commonly known as the yellow burr legume, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils. The plant produces distinctive yellow or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three small, oval-shaped leaflets.

The plant is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in arid conditions.
