Dendrobium bambusifolium

Dendrobium bambusifolium, commonly known as the bamboo-leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on the surface of trees without deriving nutrients from them. The plant has long, narrow leaves that resemble bamboo leaves, giving it its common name. It produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink.

Dendrobium bambusifolium is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
