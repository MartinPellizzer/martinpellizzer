Deutzia parviflora var. micrantha

Deutzia parviflora var. micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, arching stems and narrow, ovate leaves that are typically dark green. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its delicate floral display.
