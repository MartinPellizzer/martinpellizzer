Dircaea × purpurea

Dircaea × purpurea, commonly known as the purple-leafed beauty or purpleleaf honeysuckle, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Caprifoliaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking purple foliage, which becomes more vibrant in cooler temperatures. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Dircaea × purpurea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
