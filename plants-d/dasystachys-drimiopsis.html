Dasystachys drimiopsis

Dasystachys drimiopsis, commonly known as the drimiopsis lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a subtle purple tinge. The plant has strap-like, grassy leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance, it is favored by gardeners in temperate climates.
