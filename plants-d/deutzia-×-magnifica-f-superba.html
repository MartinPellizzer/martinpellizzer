Deutzia × magnifica f. superba

Deutzia × magnifica f. superba, commonly known as the Superb Deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy, fragrant white flowers that appear in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, arching branches with dark green, ovate leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, loamy soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
