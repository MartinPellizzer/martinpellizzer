Draba × wilczekii

Draba × wilczekii, commonly known as the hybrid rockcress, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Draba nemorosa and Draba auriculata, characterized by its low-growing habit and dense foliage. The plant typically produces small, white or pale yellow flowers in early spring, which are clustered in racemes. It thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine environments.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil.
