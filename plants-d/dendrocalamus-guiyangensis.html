Dendrocalamus guiyangensis

Dendrocalamus guiyangensis, commonly known as the Guiyang bamboo, is a species of giant bamboo native to China.

It belongs to the Bambusoideae subfamily and is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can reach heights of up to 30 meters. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces panicles of small, whitish flowers. It is often found in moist, well-drained soils in mountainous regions.

Due to its rapid growth and structural strength, it is valued for both ecological and commercial purposes.
