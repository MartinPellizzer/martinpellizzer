Dendrobium finniganense

Dendrobium finniganense, commonly known as the Finnigan's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers that bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments on tree bark or rocks. It has a complex root system that helps it absorb moisture and nutrients from its surroundings.

Dendrobium finniganense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
