Deschampsia Eminens Var. Discreta

Deschampsia eminens var. discreta is a rare and delicate grass species native to specific alpine regions. It thrives in high-altitude environments where few plants can survive. Its slender stems and pale green leaves make it easily distinguishable from other grasses. This variant is often found in rocky soils and is adapted to harsh climatic conditions. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a valuable subject for ecological and botanical study.
