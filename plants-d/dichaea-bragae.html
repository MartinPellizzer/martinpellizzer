Dichaea bragae

Dichaea bragae, commonly known as the Braga's dichaea, is a terrestrial orchid native to the humid forests of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered growth habit. The plant features linear to lanceolate leaves and produces delicate, greenish-white flowers with a subtle fragrance. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and requires high humidity for optimal growth.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
