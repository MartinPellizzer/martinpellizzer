Dodonaea eriocarpa var. confertior

Dodonaea eriocarpa var. confertior, commonly known as the silky hopwood, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage that gives it a silvery appearance. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine, white pubescence. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, followed by woody, bead-like fruits.

This plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and heathland environments.
