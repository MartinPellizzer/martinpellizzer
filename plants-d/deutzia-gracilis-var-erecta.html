Deutzia gracilis var. erecta

Deutzia gracilis var. erecta, commonly known as the upright deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features slender, arching stems that grow upright, producing clusters of small, white flowers in early summer. The plant has simple, ovate leaves that are dark green on top and pale underneath. It prefers partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value comes from its fragrant blooms and attractive foliage.
