Daiswa polyphylla var. chinensis

Daiswa polyphylla var. chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese daisya, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are usually basal, heart-shaped, and have a rough texture.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
