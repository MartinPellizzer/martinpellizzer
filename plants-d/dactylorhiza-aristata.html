Dactylorhiza aristata

Dactylorhiza aristata, commonly known as the tall orchid, is a species of orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and thrives in woodland or heathland environments. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

Dactylorhiza aristata is a perennial herb that plays an important role in its native ecosystems.
