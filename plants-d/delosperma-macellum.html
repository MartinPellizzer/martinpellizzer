Delosperma macellum

Delosperma macellum, commonly known as the red sand verbena, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are arranged in a dense rosette pattern. The plant produces vibrant red or pink flowers during the summer months, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Delosperma macellum is often grown in rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact size and ornamental appeal.
