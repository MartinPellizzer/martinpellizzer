Dillenia scabrella

Dillenia scabrella, commonly known as the rough dillenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dilleniaceae and is characterized by its large, showy flowers with five petals. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough texture, giving it its common name.

The fruits are woody, follicles that split open to release seeds.
