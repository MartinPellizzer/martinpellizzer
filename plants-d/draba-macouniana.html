Draba macouniana

Draba macouniana, commonly known as Macoun's whitlow grass, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants, and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and basal leaves.
