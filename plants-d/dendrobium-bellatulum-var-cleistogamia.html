Dendrobium bellatulum var. cleistogamia

Dendrobium bellatulum var. cleistogamia, commonly known as the cleistogamous dendrobium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant that grows on trees and rocks, thriving in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant features long, slender pseudobulbs covered in thick, leathery leaves. Its flowers are typically white or pale yellow, with a distinctive, fragrant scent.

This variety is notable for its cleistogamous flowering habit, where flowers close and self-pollinate without opening.
