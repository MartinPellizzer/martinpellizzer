Dendrobium pyropum

Dendrobium pyropum, commonly known as the fire orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, cane-like stems and clusters of bright red or orange flowers. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth. Its flowers are often fragrant and can last for several weeks.

Dendrobium pyropum is prized in horticulture for its striking appearance and unique blooming habits.
