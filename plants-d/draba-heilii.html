Draba heilii

Draba heilii, commonly known as the Alpine Draba, is a small flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is native to alpine regions of Central Asia, including the Tien Shan and Pamir Mountains. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high elevations, where it forms low, compact rosettes. It produces small, white or yellowish flowers in early summer, which are followed by elongated pods containing numerous seeds.

Draba heilii is adapted to cold climates and is often found in harsh, mountainous environments where few other plants can survive.
