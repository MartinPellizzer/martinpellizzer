Dendrochilum odoratum

Dendrochilum odoratum, commonly known as the fragrant tree orchid, is a perennial epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, showy flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a pleasant, sweet scent. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, utilizing its aerial roots to absorb moisture and nutrients from the environment. Its leaves are usually long and strap-like, providing a lush green appearance.

Dendrochilum odoratum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and unique fragrance.
