Drimia senegalensis

Drimia senegalensis, commonly known as the African star-of-Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grasslands and rocky soils. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of white or pale blue flowers. It is adapted to arid conditions and can survive in regions with limited rainfall.

Drimia senegalensis is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
