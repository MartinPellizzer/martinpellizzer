Delphinium camptocarpum

Delphinium camptocarpum, commonly known as the zigzag larkspur, is a herbaceous perennial native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems and clusters of blue to purple flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Due to its toxicity, it should be handled with care.
