Dioscorea quartiniana var. holstii

Dioscorea quartiniana var. holstii, commonly known as the African yam bean, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its tuberous roots, which are often used as a food source. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers and oblong, winged fruits that contain edible seeds. Its leaves are alternate, heart-shaped, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

This variety is valued for both its nutritional content and its role in traditional medicine.
