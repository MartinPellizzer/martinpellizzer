Deutzia corymbosa var. staurothrix

Deutzia corymbosa var. staurothrix, commonly known as the starry deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of fragrant, white flowers that bloom in early summer, creating a showy display. The plant has slender, arching branches and dark green, ovate leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value comes from its delicate flowers and compact growth habit.
