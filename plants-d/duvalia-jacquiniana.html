Duvalia jacquiniana

Duvalia jacquiniana, commonly known as the Jacob's beard or Jacob's beard orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, tufted appearance with dense clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

It is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique and delicate floral display.
