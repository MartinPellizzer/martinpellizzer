Dyckia commixta

Dyckia commixta, commonly known as the hybrid Dyckia, is a succulent plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming structure with thick, fleshy leaves. The leaves are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating that helps reduce water loss. It produces striking red or orange flowers that emerge from the center of the rosette.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
