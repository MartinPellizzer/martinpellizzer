Dendrobium tapiniense

Dendrobium tapiniense, commonly known as the Philippine orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its terrestrial or epiphytic growth habit. The plant typically features a sympodial growth pattern with pseudobulbs and fleshy leaves. Its flowers are usually clustered in inflorescences and can be found in various colors, often white or yellow.

Dendrobium tapiniense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
