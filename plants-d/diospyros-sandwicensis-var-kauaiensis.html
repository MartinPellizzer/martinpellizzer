Diospyros sandwicensis var. kauaiensis

Diospyros sandwicensis var. kauaiensis, commonly known as the Kauai persimmon, is a rare and endemic species native to the Hawaiian island of Kauai.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that typically grows in moist, shaded forest habitats. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by edible, orange-red fruits that are highly prized. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a smooth texture.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered critically endangered and is a key component of Kauai's unique ecosystems.
