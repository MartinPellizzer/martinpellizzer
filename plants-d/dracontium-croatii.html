Dracontium croatii

Dracontium croatii, commonly known as the Croatian arrowhead, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Balkan region.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence called a spadix, which is enclosed by a modified leaf called a spathe. The plant features heart-shaped, arrowhead-like leaves that are typically green with a waxy surface. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
