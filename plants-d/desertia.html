Desertia

Desertia, commonly known as the desert lily, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that help it conserve water in harsh desert conditions. Its root system is deep and extensive, allowing it to access moisture from underground water sources.

Desertia is adapted to extreme temperatures and low precipitation, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
