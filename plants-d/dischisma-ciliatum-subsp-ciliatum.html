Dischisma ciliatum subsp. ciliatum

Dischisma ciliatum subsp.

ciliatum, commonly known as the slender dischisma, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in arid or semi-arid environments.

The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, pale blue or purple flowers. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in open, sun-exposed areas.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens in regions with suitable climates.
