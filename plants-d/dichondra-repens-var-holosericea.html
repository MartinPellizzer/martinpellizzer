Dichondra repens var. holosericea

Dichondra repens var. holosericea, commonly known as silverleaf dichondra, is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features small, opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are typically silver-gray in color, covered in fine, dense hairs. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels. It thrives in well-drained soils and full to partial sunlight, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

This variant is noted for its ornamental value and ability to tolerate drought conditions.
