Duvernoia dewevrei

Duvernoia dewevrei, commonly known as the African cycad, is a unique plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Cycadaceae family and is characterized by its stout, unbranched trunk and large, pinnate leaves. The plant produces cones instead of flowers, with male and female cones typically appearing on separate plants. Duvernoia dewevrei is known for its slow growth and long lifespan, often living for several decades.
