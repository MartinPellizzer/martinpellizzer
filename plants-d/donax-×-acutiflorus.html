Donax × acutiflorus

Donax × acutiflorus, commonly known as the hybrid sea hake, is a flowering plant in the family Rhamnaceae.

It is a natural hybrid between Donax kirkii and Donax acutiflorus, characterized by its shrub-like growth habit. The plant typically grows in coastal areas, thriving in sandy or gravelly soils. Due to its adaptability and ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in coastal gardens.
