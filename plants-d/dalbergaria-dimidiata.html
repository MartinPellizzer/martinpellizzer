Dalbergaria dimidiata

Dalbergaria dimidiata, commonly known as the Natal orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, climbing habit. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are alternate and trifoliate, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Dalbergaria dimidiata thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland and thicket habitats.
