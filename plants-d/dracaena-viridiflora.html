Dracaena viridiflora

Dracaena viridiflora, commonly known as the Emerald Beauty, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, narrow leaves that are a vibrant green, with a slight bluish tint, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal.

Dracaena viridiflora prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it relatively easy to care for.
