Dendrobium toppiorum subsp. toppiorum

Dendrobium toppiorum subsp.

toppiorum, commonly known as the Toppior orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia. It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in humid tropical environments.

The plant features long, cane-like stems covered with clusters of lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually yellow or green with red or purple markings, blooming in summer.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
