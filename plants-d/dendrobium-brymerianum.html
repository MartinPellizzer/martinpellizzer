Dendrobium brymerianum

Dendrobium brymerianum, commonly known as the Bornean dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with multiple stems that can reach lengths of up to 1 meter. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically blooming during the summer months. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media for optimal growth.
