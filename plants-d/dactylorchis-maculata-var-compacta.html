Dactylorchis maculata var. compacta

Dactylorchis maculata var. compacta, commonly known as the spotted orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Asia and the Pacific.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and features small, hairy leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of purple and white, often with a distinctive spotted pattern. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
