Dracaena elliptica var. maculata

Dracaena elliptica var. maculata, commonly known as the Variegated Dracaena, is a striking evergreen plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features long, narrow, dark green leaves with distinctive white and yellow variegation patterns. The plant is characterized by its slow growth rate and ability to thrive in low-light conditions. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by red or orange berries.

Due to its ornamental value and air-purifying qualities, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
