Danaea Nodosa Var. Angustifolia

Danaea nodosa var. angustifolia is a rare and striking plant native to the cloud forests of South America. It is known for its vibrant red flowers and unique, nodding inflorescences that resemble tiny lanterns. This variety is distinguished by its narrow, elongated leaves that give it its scientific name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires careful attention to maintain its health. Due to its beauty and rarity, it is a prized specimen among plant enthusiasts and botanists alike.
