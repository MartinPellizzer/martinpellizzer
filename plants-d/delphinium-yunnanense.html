Delphinium yunnanense

Delphinium yunnanense, commonly known as the Yunnan larkspur, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan, China.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height, bearing clusters of striking blue to purple flowers. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and a distinctive blue-green hue. Its flowers are arranged in dense spikes and bloom in late spring to early summer.

Delphinium yunnanense is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and architectural form.
