Dracula radiosa

Dracula radiosa, commonly known as the Radiant Dracula, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its striking, brightly colored flowers that resemble the petals of a rose. The plant has a unique growth habit, with its roots and stems often growing along tree trunks in its natural habitat. Dracula radiosa is a terrestrial orchid that thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Its flowers typically bloom in late spring or early summer, adding a vibrant touch to the forest understory.
