Delosperma esterhuyseniae

Delosperma esterhuyseniae, commonly known as the Esterhuysenia ice plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, rounded leaves that store water, typical of xerophytic plants. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
