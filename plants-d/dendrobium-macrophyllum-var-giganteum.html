Dendrobium macrophyllum var. giganteum

Dendrobium macrophyllum var. giganteum, commonly known as the giant dendrobium, is a striking orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, cane-like stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a distinctive red or pink lip. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
