Dendrobium cymatoleguum

Dendrobium cymatoleguum, commonly known as the golden torch orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in humid tropical environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with cane-like stems that produce multiple flowering shoots. Its flowers are showy, with bright yellow petals and a distinctive red lip, making it a popular ornamental plant.

It requires high light intensity and well-draining soil to thrive.
