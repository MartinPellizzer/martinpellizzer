Dalea versicolor var. wislizeni

Dalea versicolor var. wislizeni, commonly known as the Wislizeni milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils and open grasslands.
