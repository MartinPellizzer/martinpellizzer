Dieffenbachia parvifolia

Dieffenbachia parvifolia, commonly known as the Shiny-leaf Dieffenbachia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other popular houseplants like peace lilies. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Dieffenbachia parvifolia is known for its ability to purify indoor air and is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
