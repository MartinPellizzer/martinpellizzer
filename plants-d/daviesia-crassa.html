Daviesia crassa

Daviesia crassa, commonly known as the broad-leaved davisia, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, upright growth habit and its distinctive, broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in heathlands and open forests.

Daviesia crassa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
