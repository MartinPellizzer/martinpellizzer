Deschampsia turczaninowii

Deschampsia turczaninowii, commonly known as Turczaninow's feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and produces delicate, airy plumes of pale yellow to golden-green flowers in late summer. The plant thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in alpine meadows and subalpine environments. Its fine, wiry leaves are typically blue-green in color, and it is adapted to cold, high-altitude climates.

Deschampsia turczaninowii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in harsh environments.
