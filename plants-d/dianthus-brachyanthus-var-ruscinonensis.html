Dianthus brachyanthus var. ruscinonensis

Dianthus brachyanthus var. ruscinonensis, commonly known as the Ruscinonensis Campion, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant pink to red flowers. The plant produces small, clustered blooms that are often borne on slender stems, giving it a delicate appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting flowers and ability to attract pollinators.
