Dracaena versicolor

Dracaena versicolor, commonly known as the rainbow dracaena, is a striking evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its variegated foliage, featuring shades of green, yellow, and white in intricate patterns. The leaves are long, strap-like, and often curl at the tips, adding a unique texture to its appearance. This plant thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Its ability to tolerate low light makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
