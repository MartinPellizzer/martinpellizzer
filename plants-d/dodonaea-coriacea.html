Dodonaea coriacea

Dodonaea coriacea, commonly known as the rough leaf honeysuckle, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its greyish, rough-textured leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that appear in clusters. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions.

Dodonaea coriacea is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience to drought and salt spray.
