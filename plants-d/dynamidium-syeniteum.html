Dynamidium syeniteum

Dynamidium syeniteum, commonly known as the stoneheart fern, is a rare and unique plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that help it retain moisture in its harsh environment. The plant's roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the rocky substrates it grows on. Dynamidium syeniteum has a slow growth rate and is often found in crevices or on rock faces.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
