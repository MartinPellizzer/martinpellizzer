Draco rotang

Draco rotang, commonly known as dragon's blood tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, woody stem and dense canopy of dark green leaves. The tree produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its most notable feature is the red resin it exudes, which has been historically used in various traditional applications.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
