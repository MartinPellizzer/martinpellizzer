Dendrobium triquetrum

Dendrobium triquetrum, commonly known as the triquetrum orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome that produces multiple shoots. The plant has linear to lanceolate leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. Its flowers are typically yellow or green with a distinctive three-lobed lip, and they bloom in clusters during the wet season.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
