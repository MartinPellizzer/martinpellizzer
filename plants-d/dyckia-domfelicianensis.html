Dyckia domfelicianensis

Dyckia domfelicianensis, commonly known as the Brazilian grass pine, is a species of bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage that forms a rosette structure. The plant produces inflorescences that are typically hidden within the foliage, a feature common in many bromeliads. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in coastal areas.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
