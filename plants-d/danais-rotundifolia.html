Danais rotundifolia

Danais rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leaf danais, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features basal leaves that are round or broadly ovate with a waxy surface, and its flowers are typically blue or violet, arranged in dense clusters. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
