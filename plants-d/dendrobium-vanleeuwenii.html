Dendrobium vanleeuwenii

Dendrobium vanleeuwenii, commonly known as the Van Leeuwen's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features tall, cane-like pseudobulbs covered in dense, leathery leaves. The plant produces racemes of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a reddish or purple hue. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Dendrobium vanleeuwenii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its long-lasting blooms.
