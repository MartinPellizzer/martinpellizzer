Didissandra fargesii

Didissandra fargesii, commonly known as the Chinese sand pear, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the family Rosaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are edible and have a sweet, aromatic flavor. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.
