Dasynema hirsutum

Dasynema hirsutum, commonly known as the hairy dasynema, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the family Apiaceae.

It is characterized by its finely hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. The plant typically grows in temperate regions and thrives in well-drained soils. Its small, yellowish flowers are arranged in umbels, a common feature in the Apiaceae family.

Dasynema hirsutum is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and forest edges.
