Daemonorops kiahii

Daemonorops kiahii, commonly known as the Kiahii palm, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in lowland forests. Its leaves are used in traditional crafts and as thatching materials in some cultures.

The Kiahii palm is valued for its ornamental appearance and ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
