Diasperus nitidus

Diasperus nitidus, commonly known as the smooth rock cress, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant is characterized by its small, bright yellow flowers and smooth, glossy leaves. It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils in arid environments.

Diasperus nitidus is often found in mountainous regions and is adapted to withstand harsh climatic conditions.
