Dolichos malosanus

Dolichos malosanus, commonly known as the slender cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and trifoliate leaves. The flowers are typically small and white or pale yellow, and the plant produces elongated, pod-like fruits containing several seeds. It is often grown as a cover crop or for its edible seeds, which are rich in protein.

The plant has a deep root system that helps in soil stabilization and nitrogen fixation.
