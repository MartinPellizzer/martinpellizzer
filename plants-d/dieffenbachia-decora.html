Dieffenbachia decora

Dieffenbachia decora, commonly known as the Decorum Dieffenbachia, is a tropical houseplant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that are often adorned with striking white or yellow veins. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other popular houseplants like peace lilies and caladiums. Its foliage is evergreen, and it thrives in bright, indirect light with moderate humidity.

Dieffenbachia decora is relatively low maintenance but requires well-draining soil and consistent watering to flourish.
