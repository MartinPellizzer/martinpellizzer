Dolichos flavus

Dolichos flavus, commonly known as the yellow cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and trifoliate leaves with yellow flowers. The plant produces elongated pods that contain edible seeds, which are rich in protein. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a warm climate for optimal growth.

Dolichos flavus is also valued for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, improving soil fertility.
