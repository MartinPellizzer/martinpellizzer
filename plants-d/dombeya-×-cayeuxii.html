Dombeya × cayeuxii

Dombeya × cayeuxii, commonly known as the pink dombeya, is a deciduous shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a distinctive red or purple throat. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are hairy and have a rough texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Dombeya × cayeuxii is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
