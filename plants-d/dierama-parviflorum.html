Dierama parviflorum

Dierama parviflorum, commonly known as the narrowleaf lily or flame lily, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking, showy inflorescences of bright orange or red flowers. The plant blooms in spring and summer, with its flowers arranged in a raceme that resembles a flame. It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Dierama parviflorum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of garden conditions.
