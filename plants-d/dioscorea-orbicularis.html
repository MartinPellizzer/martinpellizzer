Dioscorea orbicularis

Dioscorea orbicularis, commonly known as the round-leaf yam, is a member of the Dioscoreaceae family.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and tuberous roots. Native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, it thrives in humid, warm climates. The plant's tubers are used as a food source in some regions, though they contain toxic compounds that require proper preparation.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and agricultural contexts.
