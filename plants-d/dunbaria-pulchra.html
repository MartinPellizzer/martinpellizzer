Dunbaria pulchra

Dunbaria pulchra, commonly known as the velvetleaf or purple beauty, is a striking member of the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves that are often tinged with purple or reddish hues. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to deep purple, adding visual interest to gardens. Native to South Africa, it thrives in warm, sunny climates with well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both landscape design and container gardening.
