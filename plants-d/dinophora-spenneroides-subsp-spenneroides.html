Dinophora spenneroides subsp. spenneroides

Dinophora spenneroides subsp.

spenneroides, commonly known as the Spiny Sunflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, showy yellow-orange flower heads.

The plant produces numerous small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in a composite head. It has rough, hairy leaves that are often covered in spines, giving it a distinctive appearance.

This species is adapted to arid environments and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.
