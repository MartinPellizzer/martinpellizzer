Dendrobium subserratum

Dendrobium subserratum, commonly known as the serrated orchid, is a epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, cane-like stems and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically blooms in summer, producing pale yellow to orange flowers with red or purple markings. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Dendrobium subserratum is popular among orchid growers for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
