Diuris × victoriensis

Diuris × victoriensis, commonly known as the Victorian spider-orchid, is a hybrid orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its intricate, spider-like floral structure with long, slender sepals and petals. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rainforests and understoreys. Its flowers are usually pale green to brownish in color, with a distinctive hooded labellum.

Diuris × victoriensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
