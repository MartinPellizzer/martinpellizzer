Deutzia glauca var. decalvata

Deutzia glauca var. decalvata, commonly known as the smooth deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It features slender, arching branches and clusters of white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth, slightly glossy texture. It typically grows to a height of 3 to 6 feet and thrives in partial to full sun.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
