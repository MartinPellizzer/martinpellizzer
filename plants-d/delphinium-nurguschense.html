Delphinium nurguschense

Delphinium nurguschense, commonly known as the Nurgusch delphinium, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, spiky inflorescence composed of numerous blue to purple petals. Delphinium nurguschense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
