Delosperma nakurense

Delosperma nakurense, commonly known as the Nakuren sandverbena, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing, succulent shrub that thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features fleshy, blue-green leaves and produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months. It is well adapted to withstand drought conditions due to its succulent nature.

Delosperma nakurense is often cultivated in rock gardens and xeriscapes for its ornamental value and hardiness.
