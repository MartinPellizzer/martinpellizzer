Draba falconeri

Draba falconeri, commonly known as the Falconeri's rockcress, is a flowering plant native to the high mountain regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. This species is characterized by its small, white or yellow flowers and compact, mat-forming growth habit. It typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
