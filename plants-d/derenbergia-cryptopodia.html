Derenbergia cryptopodia

Derenbergia cryptopodia, commonly known as the hidden-rooted plant, is a rare and enigmatic species native to the arid regions of Namibia.

It is characterized by its unique underground growth habit, with most of its structure concealed beneath the soil surface. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that bloom in late spring, often in shades of white and pale yellow. Its leaves are typically reduced to small scales, adapting to the harsh, dry environment.

Due to its elusive nature and limited distribution, it is considered a botanically significant and ecologically rare species.
