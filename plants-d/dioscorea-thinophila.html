Dioscorea thinophila

Dioscorea thinophila, commonly known as the slender yam, is a perennial vine native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its climbing habit and tuberous roots. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by cylindrical, berry-like fruits. Its tubers are often used in traditional medicine and as a food source in some regions.

The botanical features of D. thinophila make it an important species in both ecological and agricultural contexts.
