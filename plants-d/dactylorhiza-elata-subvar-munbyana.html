Dactylorhiza elata subvar. munbyana

Dactylorhiza elata subvar. munbyana, commonly known as the Munby's yellow orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to western Europe.

It typically grows in damp, shaded habitats such as woodlands and heathlands. The plant features a tall, erect stem with few leaves and produces showy, yellowish-green flowers. Its roots are specialized for nutrient absorption, typical of orchids.

It is a protected species in several countries due to habitat loss and over-collection.
