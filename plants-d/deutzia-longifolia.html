Deutzia longifolia

Deutzia longifolia, commonly known as the slender deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features graceful, arching branches and clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter underside. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Deutzia longifolia is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
