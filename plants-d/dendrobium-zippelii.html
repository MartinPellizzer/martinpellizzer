Dendrobium zippelii

Dendrobium zippelii, commonly known as the Zippel's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit, with cane-like stems that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Dendrobium zippelii requires well-draining potting media and regular watering during its active growing season.
