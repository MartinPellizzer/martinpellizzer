Desmodiastrum parviflorum

Desmodiastrum parviflorum, commonly known as the small-flowered desmodium, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

Desmodiastrum parviflorum is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in ecological restoration projects.
