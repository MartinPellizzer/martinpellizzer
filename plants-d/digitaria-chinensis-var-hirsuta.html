Digitaria chinensis var. hirsuta

Digitaria chinensis var. hirsuta, commonly known as hairy digitaria, is a perennial grass species native to Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and hairy leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It is often found in moist, disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
