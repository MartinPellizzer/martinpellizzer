Dendrorkis elegans

Dendrorkis elegans, commonly known as the elegant orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the family Orchidaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of Indonesia and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
