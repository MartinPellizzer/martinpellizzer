Dahlia sphondyliifolia

Dahlia sphondyliifolia, commonly known as the Mexican dahlia, is a species native to Mexico and Central America.

It is a herbaceous perennial with showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, yellow, and orange. The plant features thick, fleshy stems and broad, heart-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its botanical characteristics make it both ornamental and adaptable to different climates.
