Dolichos hirsutus

Dolichos hirsutus, commonly known as the hairy nightshade, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves with a distinctive, heart-shaped morphology. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that develop into elongated, pod-like fruits containing several seeds. It thrives in warm climates and is often found in disturbed soils or along fences.

Botanically, it is valued for its potential medicinal properties and use in traditional herbal remedies.
