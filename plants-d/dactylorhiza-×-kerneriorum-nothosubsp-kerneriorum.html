Dactylorhiza × kerneriorum nothosubsp.

kerneriorum, commonly known as the purple marsh orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to parts of Europe and Asia. It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of purple or violet flowers.

The plant typically grows in wet, boggy habitats and requires consistently moist conditions to thrive. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, often forming a rosette at the base.

It is a relatively short-lived species, with a life cycle that includes a dormant phase during the winter months.
