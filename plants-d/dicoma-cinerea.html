Dicoma cinerea

Dicoma cinerea, commonly known as the silverleaf dandelion, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by fluffy, white seed heads. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Dicoma cinerea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
