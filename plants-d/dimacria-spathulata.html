Dimacria spathulata

Dimacria spathulata, commonly known as the broad-leaved dimacria, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves and showy, tubular flowers. Dimacria spathulata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
