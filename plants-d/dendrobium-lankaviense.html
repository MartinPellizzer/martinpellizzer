Dendrobium lankaviense

Dendrobium lankaviense, commonly known as the Sri Lankan orchid, is a species of orchid native to Sri Lanka.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on the surface of trees or other structures without deriving nutrients from them. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and indirect sunlight.

Dendrobium lankaviense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
