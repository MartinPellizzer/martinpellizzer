Dupatya polyantha

Dupatya polyantha, commonly known as the Chinese rose or Rosa chinensis, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to eastern Asia, particularly China, and is known for its fragrant, showy flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters, with thorny stems and dark green, oval-shaped leaves. Its flowers come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, white, and yellow, and are often used in traditional Chinese gardens.

Dupatya polyantha is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its historical and cultural significance in horticulture.
