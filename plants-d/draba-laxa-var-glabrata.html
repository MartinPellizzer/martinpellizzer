Draba laxa var. glabrata

Draba laxa var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth draba, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches heights of 5 to 15 centimeters. The plant features basal leaves that are linear to oblanceolate in shape, often with a hairy surface. Its flowers are small, white or pale yellow, and are arranged in a raceme.

It is typically found in alpine and arctic regions, thriving in rocky and sandy soils.
