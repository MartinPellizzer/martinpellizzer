Dendranthema zawadzkii var. naktongense

Dendranthema zawadzkii var. naktongense, commonly known as the Naktong daisy, is a flowering plant native to Korea.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.

The species is also noted for its resilience and ability to thrive in temperate climates.
