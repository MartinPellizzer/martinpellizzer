Dimorphanthes

Dimorphanthes, commonly known as the African star orchid, is a genus of flowering plants native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped flowers. The plants typically grow as terrestrial herbs, often found in grasslands and open woodlands. Dimorphanthes species are known for their vibrant, colorful blooms that attract various pollinators.

They are relatively small in size, with some species reaching only a few centimeters in height.
