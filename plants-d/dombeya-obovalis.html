Dombeya obovalis

Dombeya obovalis, commonly known as the ovalleaf dombeya, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Africa.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring. The plant has simple, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Dombeya obovalis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
