Dichopsis rubiginosa

Dichopsis rubiginosa, commonly known as the red-spotted orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit. The plant produces striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or red with contrasting yellow markings. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Dichopsis rubiginosa thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
