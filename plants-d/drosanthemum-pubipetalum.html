Drosanthemum pubipetalum

Drosanthemum pubipetalum, commonly known as the woolly plectranthus, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, ovate leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a soft, woolly appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color, blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Drosanthemum pubipetalum is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and compact growth habit.
