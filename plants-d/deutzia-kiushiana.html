Deutzia kiushiana

Deutzia kiushiana, commonly known as the Korean deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It features clusters of white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, arching stems and lance-shaped leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a versatile addition to gardens.

Deutzia kiushiana is valued for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of climates.
