Digitaria rukwae

Digitaria rukwae, commonly known as the East African digitaria, is a grass species native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

Digitaria rukwae is often found in agricultural areas and is sometimes considered a weed due to its rapid growth and adaptability.
