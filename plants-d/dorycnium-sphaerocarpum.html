Dorycnium sphaerocarpum

Dorycnium sphaerocarpum, commonly known as the globe burclover, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive spherical seed pods. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are trifoliate, and it produces small yellow flowers that bloom in early summer.

The plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial in soil enrichment.
