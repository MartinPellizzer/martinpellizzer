Dodonaea eriocarpa var. skottsbergii

Dodonaea eriocarpa var. skottsbergii, commonly known as the silverleaf hop bush, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are typically elliptical or ovate in shape and have a leathery texture.

This variety is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
