Devauxia glabra

Devauxia glabra, commonly known as the smooth devauxia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and woodland areas. Its smooth, glabrous stems and leaves are distinguishing features.

Devauxia glabra is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
