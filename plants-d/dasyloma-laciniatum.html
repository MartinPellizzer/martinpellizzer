Dasyloma laciniatum

Dasyloma laciniatum, commonly known as the laceleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its finely dissected, feathery leaves that give it a delicate appearance. Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and modest blooms.
