Defforgia borbonica

Defforgia borbonica, commonly known as the Bourbon orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Defforgia borbonica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
