Dendrobium nothofageti

Dendrobium nothofageti, commonly known as the New Zealand orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant produces clusters of greenish-yellow flowers with a distinct red lip, blooming in late winter to early spring. It prefers moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species.
