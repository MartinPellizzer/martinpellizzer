Dolichothele uberiformis

Dolichothele uberiformis, commonly known as the woolly thread spider, is a species of orb-weaving spider found in various regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Dolichothele, which is characterized by its elongated, slender bodies and long, delicate legs. The spider's web is typically a small, irregular orb web, often found in shrubs or low vegetation. Its coloration is usually a combination of pale yellow and brown, providing effective camouflage in its habitat.

The plant-like appearance of its web contributes to its common name, though it is not a plant but an arachnid.
