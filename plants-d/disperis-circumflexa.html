Disperis circumflexa

Disperis circumflexa, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of petals and sepals. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its flowers are small and delicate, often displaying a range of colors from white to pale pink.

Disperis circumflexa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
