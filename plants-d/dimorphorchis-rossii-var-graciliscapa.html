Dimorphorchis rossii var. graciliscapa

Dimorphorchis rossii var. graciliscapa, commonly known as the slender-stemmed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features slender, erect stems with few, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a yellowish throat. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is typically found in forest understories.

This variety is distinguished by its delicate appearance and unique floral structure.
