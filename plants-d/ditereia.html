Ditereia

Ditereia, commonly known as the "silver leaf plant," is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its striking silver-blue foliage. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, powdery coating, giving it a unique appearance. It produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or red, adding ornamental value to gardens.
