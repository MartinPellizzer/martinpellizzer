Dombeya mildbraedii

Dombeya mildbraedii, commonly known as the African dombeya, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Dombeya mildbraedii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
