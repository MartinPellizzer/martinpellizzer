Doliocarpus brevipedicellatus var. densiflorus

Doliocarpus brevipedicellatus var. densiflorus, commonly known as the dense-flowered doliocarpus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact shrub form and dense clusters of small, white flowers. The leaves are typically elliptical and glossy, providing a striking contrast to the pale floral display. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its botanical features make it an attractive species for botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
