Dolichos subcapitatus var. subcapitatus

Dolichos subcapitatus var. subcapitatus, commonly known as the white hyacinth bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features compound leaves with small, ovate leaflets and produces clusters of white or pale purple flowers. The plant bears elongated, slender pods that contain several kidney-shaped seeds. It thrives in warm climates with well-drained soil and full sun exposure.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine and as a food source.
