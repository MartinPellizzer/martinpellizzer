Dicranostyles sericea

Dicranostyles sericea, commonly known as the silky haircap, is a species of moss belonging to the family Dicranaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth and the presence of slender, leaf-like structures that are often covered in a fine, silky coating. The leaves are typically elongated and gradually tapering, with a distinct midrib. This moss thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky crevices.

Its reproductive structures, known as capsules, are borne on short, erect stalks and release spores when mature.
