Desmodium clovissii

Desmodium clovissii, commonly known as African velvet bean, is a leguminous plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves with small, hairy, and velvety surfaces. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to purple. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

Desmodium clovissii is also valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it useful in agricultural systems.
