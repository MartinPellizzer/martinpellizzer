Dendrobium fimbrilabium

Dendrobium fimbrilabium, commonly known as the feathered lip orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with long, wiry stems that bear numerous lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, showy flowers with a distinctive, feathery lip that gives it its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Dendrobium fimbrilabium is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
