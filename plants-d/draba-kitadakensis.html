Draba kitadakensis

Draba kitadakensis, commonly known as the Korean rockcress, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South Korea.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant is characterized by its low-growing habit, with small, basal leaves and clusters of tiny white or pale yellow flowers. Draba kitadakensis is adapted to cold climates and is typically found at high elevations where temperatures are cooler and sunlight is abundant.
