Deguelia costata

Deguelia costata, commonly known as the mountain ebony, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has elliptical, leathery leaves with prominent veins and is often found in moist, shaded environments. Its fruit is a small, woody capsule containing several seeds.

Deguelia costata is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
