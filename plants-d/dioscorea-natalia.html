Dioscorea natalia

Dioscorea natalia, commonly known as Natal yam, is a climbing herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the genus Dioscorea in the family Dioscoreaceae, which includes various species of yams. The plant features tendrils that help it climb and can reach heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are alternate, heart-shaped, and often have a hairy texture.

The tuberous roots of D. natalia are edible and have been traditionally used in local cuisines.
