Diplazium Stenocarpum

Diplazium stenocarpum is a species of evergreen fern native to tropical regions of Asia and the Pacific. It is characterized by its large, compound leaves that can reach several feet in length. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its unique frond structure and resilience make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in mainstream horticultural circles.
