Diplazium pseudocarnosum

Diplazium pseudocarnosum, commonly known as the false leather fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its leathery, dark green fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its fronds are usually bipinnate, with small, closely spaced pinnae that give it a dense appearance.
