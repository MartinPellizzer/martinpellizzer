Diplusodon virgatus var. virgatus

Diplusodon virgatus var. virgatus, commonly known as the slender sandalwood, is a species of flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its aromatic, woody stems and small, elliptical leaves.
