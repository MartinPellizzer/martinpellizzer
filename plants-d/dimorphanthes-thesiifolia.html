Dimorphanthes thesiifolia

Dimorphanthes thesiifolia, commonly known as the leafy orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated leaves that resemble those of the genus *Thesium*, hence its specific epithet *thesiifolia*. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale green, with a distinctively shaped labellum. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
