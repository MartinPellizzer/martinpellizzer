Dendrobium curviflorum

Dendrobium curviflorum, commonly known as the twisted flower orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit, with multiple stems that grow horizontally and produce new shoots from the base. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a distinctive twisted or curled lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Dendrobium curviflorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
