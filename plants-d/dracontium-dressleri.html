Dracontium dressleri

Dracontium dressleri, commonly known as the Amazonian arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces striking, spadix-like flowers that are often surrounded by colorful bracts. It thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for both ornamental and botanical collections.
