Desmodium filiforme

Desmodium filiforme, commonly known as slender desmodium, is a low-growing perennial legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features trifoliate leaves with narrow, linear leaflets and produces small, yellow to white flowers arranged in clusters. The plant has a fibrous root system and is often found in disturbed soils or as a weed in agricultural fields. It is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, improving soil fertility.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it a useful plant in ecological restoration projects.
