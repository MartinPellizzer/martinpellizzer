Digitaria dusenii

Digitaria dusenii, commonly known as the slender fingergrass, is a perennial grass native to parts of Africa and the Americas.

It typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides, fields, and forest edges. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny florets. It is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in warm, humid climates.

Digitaria dusenii is notable for its adaptability and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
