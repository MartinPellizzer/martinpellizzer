Dorycnium quinatum

Dorycnium quinatum, commonly known as the five-lobed sainfoin, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it valuable in soil enrichment. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Dorycnium quinatum is also used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
