Dicliptera paniculata

Dicliptera paniculata, commonly known as the panicled dicliptera, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

Dicliptera paniculata is often found in wetland or marshy habitats, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils.
