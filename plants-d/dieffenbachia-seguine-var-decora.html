Dieffenbachia seguine var. decora

Dieffenbachia seguine var. decora, commonly known as the Decorative Dieffenbachia, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with white and green patterns on its broad, heart-shaped leaves. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like peace lilies and caladiums. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and decorative purposes.
