Drynaria fortunei

Drynaria fortunei, commonly known as the bamboo fern, is a hardy evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It features a rhizomatous growth habit with dense, arching fronds that can reach up to 1 meter in length. The plant has serrated, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a leathery texture. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often used in ornamental gardening.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
