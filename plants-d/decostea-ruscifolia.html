Decostea ruscifolia

Decostea ruscifolia, commonly known as the African fern, is a striking evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features dense, leathery foliage with a distinctive rosette-like arrangement of lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that are followed by red, fleshy fruits containing seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique appearance and adaptability contribute to its appeal in both cultivated and natural landscapes.
