Discostigma

Discostigma, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to Africa.

These plants are characterized by their rosette-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. Discostigma species are typically herbaceous and thrive in well-drained soil with bright, indirect light. They are popular houseplants due to their ease of care and ornamental value.

The genus includes several species, such as Discostigma heterophyllum, which is known for its striking foliage and vibrant blooms.
