Desmodium pulcherrimum

Desmodium pulcherrimum, commonly known as the purple crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive purple flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant is often found in grasslands and disturbed soils, thriving in full sun. Its seeds are encased in small, hairy pods that split open when mature, dispersing the seeds effectively.

Desmodium pulcherrimum is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial in agricultural settings.
