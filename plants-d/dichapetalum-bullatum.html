Dichapetalum bullatum

Dichapetalum bullatum, commonly known as the bullatum mallow, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or red in color. The plant has hairy, glandular leaves that help it retain moisture in its arid habitat. It produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small seeds.

Dichapetalum bullatum is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to thrive in full sun.
