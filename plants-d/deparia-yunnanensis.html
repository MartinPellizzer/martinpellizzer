Deparia yunnanensis

Deparia yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan shield fern, is a species of fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forests and rocky areas. Its fronds are often divided into segments with a distinctive, serrated edge.

Deparia yunnanensis is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
