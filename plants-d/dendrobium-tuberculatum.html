Dendrobium tuberculatum

Dendrobium tuberculatum, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a branched stem that is often covered in a thick, leathery sheath. The plant produces racemes of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the air and rain.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value.
