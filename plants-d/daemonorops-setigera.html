Daemonorops setigera

Daemonorops setigera, commonly known as the rattan palm, is a climbing palm native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, flexible stems that can grow up to 30 meters in height. The plant has pinnate leaves with long, narrow leaflets, and it produces small, yellowish flowers followed by dark blue, fleshy fruits. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its strong, fibrous stems, it is widely used in the production of rattan furniture and crafts.
