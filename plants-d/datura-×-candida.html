Datura × candida

Datura × candida, commonly known as the angel's trumpet, is a hybrid species within the nightshade family (Solanaceae).

It is characterized by its large, showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. The plant is native to South America but has been widely cultivated in warm climates around the world. Its leaves are broad and lobed, and it produces toxic seeds that can be harmful if ingested.

Datura × candida is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
