Dendropemon cubensis

Dendropemon cubensis, commonly known as the Cuban orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to Cuba.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually elongated and grass-like, adapting to its tropical environment.

Dendropemon cubensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by enthusiasts interested in rare flora.
