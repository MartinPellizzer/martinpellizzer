Dendrochilum geesinkii

Dendrochilum geesinkii, commonly known as the Geesink's tree orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and elongated, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a distinctive yellow lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrochilum geesinkii is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
