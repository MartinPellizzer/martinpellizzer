Dipterocarpus palembanicus subsp. borneensis

Dipterocarpus palembanicus subsp.

borneensis, commonly known as the Bornean dipterocarp, is a large evergreen tree native to the tropical rainforests of Borneo. It belongs to the family Dipterocarpaceae and is characterized by its tall stature, reaching up to 50 meters in height.

The tree produces large, heavy fruits that contain winged seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Its wood is highly valued for its strength and durability, making it an important species in local timber industries.

The plant plays a crucial role in the ecological balance of Borneo's rainforests, providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
