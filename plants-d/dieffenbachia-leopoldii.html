Dieffenbachia leopoldii

Dieffenbachia leopoldii, commonly known as the Leopard Plant, is a striking tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves with distinctive white and green variegation. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other familiar houseplants like peace lilies. Its foliage is often adorned with intricate patterns resembling leopard spots, giving it its common name.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to maintain its lush appearance.
