Disperis katangensis var. katangensis

Disperis katangensis var. katangensis, commonly known as the Katanga disperis, is a flowering plant native to the Democratic Republic of the Congo.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest understories, preferring humid and cool environments. It has linear to lanceolate leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
