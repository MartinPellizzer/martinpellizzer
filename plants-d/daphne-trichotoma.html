Daphne trichotoma

Daphne trichotoma, commonly known as the woolly daphne, is a shrub native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of pink and white during late winter to early spring. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine, soft hairs.

Daphne trichotoma is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
