Delphinium pubescens subsp. mauritanicum

Delphinium pubescens subsp.

mauritanicum, commonly known as the Mauritania larkspur, is a perennial herb native to North Africa. It features tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters.

The plant produces clusters of blue to violet flowers, which are arranged in dense, terminal spikes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a striking addition to rock gardens and alpine settings.
