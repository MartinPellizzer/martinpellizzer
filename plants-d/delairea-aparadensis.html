Delairea aparadensis

Delairea aparadensis, commonly known as the Cape mountain heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen shrub form. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or white, which bloom in spring. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to the Mediterranean climate of its native region.

Delairea aparadensis is often found in fynbos vegetation, contributing to the biodiversity of its ecosystem.
