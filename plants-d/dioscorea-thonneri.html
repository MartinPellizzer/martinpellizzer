Dioscorea thonneri

Dioscorea thonneri, commonly known as the African yam bean, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its tuberous roots, which are the primary edible part of the plant. The plant produces small, green, heart-shaped leaves and yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. Its tubers are rich in starch and have been traditionally used as a food source by local communities.

The plant is also valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
