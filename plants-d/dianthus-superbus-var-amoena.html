Dianthus superbus var. amoena

Dianthus superbus var. amoena, commonly known as the showy campion, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, branched stems that bear clusters of pink or red, five-petaled flowers. The leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in fine hairs. This species thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal medicine.
