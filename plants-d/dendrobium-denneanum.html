Dendrobium denneanum

Dendrobium denneanum, commonly known as the Denneanum orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with cane-like stems that can store water and nutrients. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium denneanum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
