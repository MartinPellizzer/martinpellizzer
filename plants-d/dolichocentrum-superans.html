Dolichocentrum superans

Dolichocentrum superans, commonly known as the skyrocket lily, is a striking plant native to South America.

It is a bulbous perennial that produces tall, slender stems with showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to thrive in well-drained soils. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that emerge from the base of the plant.

Dolichocentrum superans is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well-suited to rock gardens and containers.
