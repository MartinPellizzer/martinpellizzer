Dracula roezlii f. rubra

Dracula roezlii f. rubra, commonly known as the Red Dragon Orchid, is a rare and striking epiphytic orchid native to the cloud forests of Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red petals and sepals, which contrast beautifully with its dark green foliage. The plant produces intricate, spurred flowers that are adapted for pollination by specific insects. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, organic-rich substrate.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
