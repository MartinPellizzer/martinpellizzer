Dalea surotatensis

Dalea surotatensis, commonly known as the Surotatsky milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to withstand harsh, dry conditions. It has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces pods that contain several seeds.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and specialized species.
