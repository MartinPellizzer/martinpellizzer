Delphinium patens

Delphinium patens, commonly known as the alpine larkspur, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is part of the Ranunculaceae family, which includes other familiar genera like buttercups and anemones. Delphinium patens typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky slopes, where it thrives in well-drained soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine displays.
