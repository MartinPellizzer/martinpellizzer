Dinoseris salicifolia

Dinoseris salicifolia, commonly known as the willow-leaved dandelion, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its basal rosette of willow-like leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that develop into fluffy, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open, disturbed habitats.
