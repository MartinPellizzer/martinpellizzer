Dyckia spectabilis

Dyckia spectabilis, commonly known as the showy dyckia, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, upright stems and dense rosettes of narrow, grass-like leaves that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces vibrant, showy inflorescences with red and orange bracts, which are highly visible and attract pollinators. Dyckia spectabilis thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for tropical gardens.

Its architectural form and long-lasting blooms make it a standout ornamental plant in the bromeliad family.
