Drypetes elata

Drypetes elata, commonly known as the white flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are simple, alternate, and have a rough texture with a dark green upper surface.
