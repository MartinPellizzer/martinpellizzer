Donax blumei

Donax blumei, commonly known as the Hawaiian fan-palm, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in coastal areas and is adapted to the warm, humid climate of Hawaii.
