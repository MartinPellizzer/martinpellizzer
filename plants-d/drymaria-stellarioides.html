Drymaria stellarioides

Drymaria stellarioides, commonly known as starwort, is a low-growing herbaceous plant belonging to the family Caryophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its prostrate or ascending stems that bear small, clustered flowers. The leaves are typically linear to ovate, and the flowers are usually white or pink, arranged in dense, star-like clusters. This plant is often found in dry, rocky, or sandy soils in open habitats.

It is native to Europe and western Asia and is often used in ornamental gardening due to its delicate appearance.
