Delosperma pruinosum

Delosperma pruinosum, commonly known as the silverleaf ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves covered in a fine, powdery coating that helps reduce water loss. The plant produces vibrant clusters of yellow flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ability to store water in its leaves allows it to survive in arid environments.
