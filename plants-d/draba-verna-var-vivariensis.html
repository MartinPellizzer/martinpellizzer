Draba verna var. vivariensis

Draba verna var. vivariensis, commonly known as the spring rockcress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or alpine environments. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers in early spring. Its leaves are basal, rosette-like, and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

This variety is adapted to cold climates and is often found in mountainous regions across Europe and parts of Asia.
