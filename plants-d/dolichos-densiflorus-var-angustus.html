Dolichos densiflorus var. angustus

Dolichos densiflorus var. angustus, commonly known as the narrowleaf burr legume, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its slender, angular stems and compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into elongated, spiny pods. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing along roadsides or in disturbed soils.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
