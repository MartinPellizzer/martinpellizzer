Dendrobium fimbriatolabellum

Dendrobium fimbriatolabellum, commonly known as the fringed-lip dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with cylindrical pseudobulbs and linear leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals. Its flowers are typically arranged in racemes and bloom during the warmer months.

It requires bright, indirect light and moderate humidity to thrive.
