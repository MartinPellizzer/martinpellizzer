Dalea Carthagenensis Var. Capitulata

Dalea carthagenensis var. capitulata is a unique flowering plant native to the southwestern United States. It is known for its distinctive yellow blooms that appear in late spring. This variety is often found in rocky soils and arid environments. Its compact growth habit makes it well suited for xeriscaping. The plant is also valued for its ecological benefits and drought tolerance.
