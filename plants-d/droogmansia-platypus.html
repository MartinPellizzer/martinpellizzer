Droogmansia platypus

Droogmansia platypus, commonly known as the flat-tongued orchid, is a unique species native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous environments, where it thrives in cool, humid conditions. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to purple.
