Desmodium ciliare var. lancifolium

Desmodium ciliare var. lancifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved desmodium, is a perennial leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features trifoliate leaves with lance-shaped leaflets, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It has a fibrous root system and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it valuable for soil enrichment and ecological restoration.
