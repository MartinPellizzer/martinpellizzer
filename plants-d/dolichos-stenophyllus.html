Dolichos stenophyllus

Dolichos stenophyllus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf dolichos, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features elongated, narrow leaves that are typically alternate and trifoliate, with each leaflet being lanceolate in shape. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pale yellow to white in color. Its pods are elongated and contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional cuisines.

Dolichos stenophyllus is valued for its nutritional content and is cultivated for both food and forage purposes.
