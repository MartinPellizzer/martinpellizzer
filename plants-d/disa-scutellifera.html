Disa scutellifera

Disa scutellifera, commonly known as the helmet orchid, is a species of orchid native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, helmet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant produces a single, tall stem that bears a dense cluster of showy blooms. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial shade to full sun.

Disa scutellifera is a popular ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
