Duretia cylindrica

Duretia cylindrica, commonly known as the cylindrical duretia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its upright, cylindrical stems. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically purple or pink in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal landscapes.

Duretia cylindrica is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
