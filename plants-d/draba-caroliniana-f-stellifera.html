Draba caroliniana f. stellifera

Draba caroliniana f. stellifera, commonly known as the star-flowered winter cress, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, cruciform flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, rocky soils and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks. It produces clusters of tiny, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring.

The subspecies stellifera is distinguished by its more showy, star-like flower arrangement compared to the typical form.
