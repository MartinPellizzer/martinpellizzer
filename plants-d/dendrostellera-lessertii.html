Dendrostellera lessertii

Dendrostellera lessertii, commonly known as the African orchid, is a unique member of the Orchidaceae family native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with rhizomes that produce new shoots annually. Its leaves are usually broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its arid native habitat.

Dendrostellera lessertii is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil and partial shade.
