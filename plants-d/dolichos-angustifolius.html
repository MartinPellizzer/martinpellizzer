Dolichos angustifolius

Dolichos angustifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaf dolichos, is a leguminous plant native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves and small, pea-like flowers that typically bloom in clusters. The plant produces elongated pods containing several seeds, which are often used as a food source in some regions. It thrives in warm, arid environments and is adapted to poor soil conditions.

Dolichos angustifolius is also valued for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil fertility.
