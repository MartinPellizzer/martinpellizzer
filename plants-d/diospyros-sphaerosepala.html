Diospyros sphaerosepala

Diospyros sphaerosepala, commonly known as the round-sepaled persimmon, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth surface. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, typically greenish-yellow in color.

The fruit is a small, round berry that changes color from green to orange-red as it matures.
