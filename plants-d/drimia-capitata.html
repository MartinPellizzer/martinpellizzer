Drimia capitata

Drimia capitata, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces showy, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center. The plant has a rosette of lance-shaped leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Drimia capitata is also valued for its use in traditional medicine.
