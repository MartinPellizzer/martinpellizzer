Delphinium brunonianum var. aitchisonii

Delphinium brunonianum var. aitchisonii, commonly known as the Aitchison's larkspur, is a striking perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Pakistan and India.

It is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant produces showy, blue to purple flowers in racemes, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lanceolate and serrated, with a somewhat hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for alpine gardens.
