Dactylorhiza × vermeuleniana var. senayi

Dactylorhiza × vermeuleniana var. senayi, commonly known as the Senay's marsh orchid, is a hybrid orchid native to parts of Africa.

It is a terrestrial herb with erect, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing numerous fragrant, violet to purple flowers. It thrives in wet, boggy habitats and requires consistently moist conditions.

Its botanical significance lies in its role as a hybrid species within the Orchidaceae family.
