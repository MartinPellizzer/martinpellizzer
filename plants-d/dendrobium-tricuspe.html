Dendrobium tricuspe

Dendrobium tricuspe, commonly known as the three-lobed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, cane-like stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with three prominent lobes, which bloom during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrobium tricuspe is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
