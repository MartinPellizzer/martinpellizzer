Doryopteris elegans

Doryopteris elegans, commonly known as the slender maidenhair fern, is a species of fern native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its delicate, arching fronds that are typically 30 to 60 centimeters long. The plant has a distinctive appearance with its finely divided, bright green leaves that have a leathery texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Doryopteris elegans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
