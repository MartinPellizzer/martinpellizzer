Davidia vilmoriniana

Davidia vilmoriniana, commonly known as the Chinese lantern tree, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its striking, lantern-like fruits that turn red in autumn. The plant has compound leaves with a serrated margin and is known for its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and arboreta around the world.
