Distictis glaziovii

Distictis glaziovii, commonly known as the Glaziovii daisy, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with white ray florets surrounding yellow disk florets. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in arid or semi-arid regions, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments.

Distictis glaziovii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
