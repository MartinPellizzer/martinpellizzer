Daphnopsis martii var. congregata

Daphnopsis martii var. congregata, commonly known as the clustered daphnopsis, is a unique plant species native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense clusters of small, rounded leaves. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
