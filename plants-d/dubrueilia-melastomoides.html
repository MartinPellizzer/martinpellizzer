Dubrueilia melastomoides

Dubrueilia melastomoides, commonly known as the dwarf melastome, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to white. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland understories.
