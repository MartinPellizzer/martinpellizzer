Deschampsia ruwensorensis

Deschampsia ruwensorensis, commonly known as the Ruwensorensis grass, is a perennial grass species native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted foliage and slender, cylindrical inflorescence. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in temperate climates. Its botanical features include narrow leaves with a bluish-green hue and small, spike-like flower clusters.

Deschampsia ruwensorensis is often found in open habitats such as meadows and forest edges.
