Dolichos hastiformis

Dolichos hastiformis, commonly known as the arrowleaf dolichos, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive arrowhead shape, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by long, slender pods containing edible seeds. It is often grown for its nutritious seeds, which are high in protein and can be used in various culinary applications.

Botanically, it belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and nitrogen-fixing ability.
