Dendrocalamopsis stenoaurita

Dendrocalamopsis stenoaurita, commonly known as the narrow-leaf bamboo, is a member of the grass family Poaceae.

It is a perennial plant characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in moist, shaded environments within tropical and subtropical regions. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in panicles, which are typically wind-pollinated.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense clumps, making it a common ornamental and ecological plant in its native habitat.
