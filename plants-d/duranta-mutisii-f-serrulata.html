Duranta mutisii f. serrulata

Duranta mutisii f. serrulata, commonly known as the serrated duranta, is a compact shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features small, lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges, giving it its distinguishing name. The plant produces clusters of tubular flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, which bloom in summer. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in partial shade.

The fruit is a small, fleshy capsule that contains seeds, typical of the Verbenaceae family.
