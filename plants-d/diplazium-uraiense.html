Diplazium uraiense

Diplazium uraiense, commonly known as the Urai fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, pinnate fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The Urai fern is valued for its ornamental appearance and ecological role in forest ecosystems.
