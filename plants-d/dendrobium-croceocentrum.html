Dendrobium croceocentrum

Dendrobium croceocentrum, commonly known as the golden spike orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems that bear numerous ovate leaves. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers with a distinctive golden throat, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Dendrobium croceocentrum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
