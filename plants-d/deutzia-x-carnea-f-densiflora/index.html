Deutzia × Carnea F. Densiflora

Deutzia × carnea f. densiflora is a compact, ornamental shrub known for its profuse display of fragrant, white flowers. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a versatile choice for garden landscapes. This variety is prized for its dense, clustered blooms that appear in early summer. Its hardy nature allows it to flourish in a range of climates, from temperate to cooler regions. With its elegant appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular addition to both residential and public gardens.
