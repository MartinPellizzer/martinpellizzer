Dioscorea natalensis

Dioscorea natalensis, commonly known as Natal yam, is a climbing herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its tuberous roots, which are the primary vegetative structures. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in loose clusters, and its leaves are typically heart-shaped with a rough texture. The tubers of Dioscorea natalensis are used in traditional medicine and as a food source in some regions.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.
