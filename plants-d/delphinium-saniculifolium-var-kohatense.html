Delphinium saniculifolium var. kohatense

Delphinium saniculifolium var. kohatense, commonly known as the Kohat delphinium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Himalayas.

It features tall, upright stems that bear clusters of spurred blue or violet flowers, typical of the Delphinium genus. The leaves are lance-shaped and often have a serrated edge, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for alpine gardens.

This variety is valued for its striking floral display and adaptability to high-altitude environments.
