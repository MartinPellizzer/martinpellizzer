Daturicarpa elliptica

Daturicarpa elliptica, commonly known as the elliptical fruit datura, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

Daturicarpa elliptica is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value, though its toxicity limits its use in gardens.
