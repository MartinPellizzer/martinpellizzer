Dendrobium fimbriatum var. bimaculosum

Dendrobium fimbriatum var. bimaculosum, commonly known as the bicolored dendrobium, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant that grows on trees and rocks, relying on air and rain for moisture. The plant features long, cane-like stems with clustered, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically yellow with red or brown markings, adding to its ornamental value.

It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.
