Draba incana var. sylvatica

Draba incana var. sylvatica, commonly known as the alpine bellflower, is a flowering plant native to mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

Draba incana var. sylvatica is known for its hardy nature and ability to survive in harsh, cold climates.
