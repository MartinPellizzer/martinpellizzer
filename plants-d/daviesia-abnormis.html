Daviesia abnormis

Daviesia abnormis, commonly known as the Woolly Daviesia, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and distinctive yellow to orange flowers that bloom in spring. The plant belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is known for its ability to thrive in dry, rocky environments. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The species is also valued for its role in ecological systems, providing habitat and nectar for native wildlife.
