Dierama pictum

Dierama pictum, commonly known as the feather grass, is a striking ornamental plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, arching stems that bear clusters of delicate, feathery inflorescences, typically in shades of pink, purple, or white. The plant is evergreen, with narrow, grass-like leaves that form a rosette at the base. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among gardeners.
