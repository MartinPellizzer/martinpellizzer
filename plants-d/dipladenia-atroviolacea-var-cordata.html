Dipladenia atroviolacea var. cordata

Dipladenia atroviolacea var. cordata, commonly known as the dark violet waxplant, is a compact, trailing plant native to South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy dark green leaves and produces clusters of tubular, dark violet flowers. The plant is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in hanging baskets or as a ground cover. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique foliage and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
