Dialium mayumbense

Dialium mayumbense, commonly known as the Mayumbense flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Central and West Africa.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy, red-orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces elliptical, leathery fruits that contain a single seed surrounded by a fleshy, sticky aril. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a rough texture, while its bark is smooth and pale in color.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often planted in urban landscapes for its vibrant floral display.
