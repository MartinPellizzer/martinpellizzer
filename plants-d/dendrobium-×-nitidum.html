Dendrobium × nitidum

Dendrobium × nitidum, commonly known as the silver sword orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, cane-like growth habit and clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant produces long, strap-shaped leaves that are often covered in a silvery or whitish bloom, giving it its common name. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Dendrobium × nitidum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
