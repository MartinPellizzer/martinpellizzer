Deyeuxia schiedeana

Deyeuxia schiedeana, commonly known as the mountain grass or Schiede's grass, is a perennial grass species native to high-altitude regions of South America.

The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.
