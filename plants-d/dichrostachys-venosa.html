Dichrostachys venosa

Dichrostachys venosa, commonly known as the red-stemmed burr legume, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its slender, often red-tinged stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers followed by cylindrical, woody pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Dichrostachys venosa is also valued for its use in traditional medicine and as a forage plant for livestock.
