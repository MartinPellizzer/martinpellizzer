Dimorphandra macrostachya subsp. glabrifolia

Dimorphandra macrostachya subsp.

glabrifolia, commonly known as the smooth-leaved ironwood, is a deciduous tree native to Madagascar. It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and sturdy, woody structure. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by elongated, pod-like fruits.
