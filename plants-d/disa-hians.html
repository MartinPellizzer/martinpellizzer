Disa hians

Disa hians, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a strikingly beautiful orchid native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and showy, brightly colored flowers that range from yellow to orange and red. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. Its flowers bloom in spring and are known for their intricate, spurred petals.

Disa hians is a popular ornamental plant due to its vibrant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
