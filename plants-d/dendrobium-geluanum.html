Dendrobium geluanum

Dendrobium geluanum, commonly known as the Java orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with multiple stems that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink, often arranged in racemes. Its leaves are strap-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water retention.

Dendrobium geluanum thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
