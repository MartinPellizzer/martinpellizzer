Diasperus rotundatus

Diasperus rotundatus, commonly known as the roundleaf spurge, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family, characterized by its milky sap and showy, clustered inflorescences. It is often used in traditional medicine and as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance.
