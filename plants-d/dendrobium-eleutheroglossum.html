Dendrobium eleutheroglossum

Dendrobium eleutheroglossum, commonly known as the golden lip dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a tall, cane-like stem with numerous horizontal pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a distinctive lip that resembles a lip-shaped petal. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its unique floral structure and vibrant colors make it a prized ornamental plant among orchid enthusiasts.
