Dalbergia junghuhnii var. scortechinii

Dalbergia junghuhnii var. scortechinii, commonly known as the African rosewood, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to eastern and southern Africa, where it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant is characterized by its dense, dark-colored wood, which is highly valued for its durability and aesthetic qualities.
