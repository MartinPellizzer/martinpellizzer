Diospyros neocaledonica

Diospyros neocaledonica, commonly known as the Kauri or Kauri fig, is a tropical evergreen tree native to New Caledonia.

It belongs to the Ebenaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, dark bark and broad, lance-shaped leaves. The tree produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by edible fig-like fruits. Its wood is dense and valued for its strength and durability, making it important in local craftsmanship.

The plant plays a significant ecological role in its native habitat, supporting various wildlife species.
