Dolichos martinicensis

Dolichos martinicensis, commonly known as the Martinique bean, is a climbing legume native to the Caribbean.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets and produces small, pale yellow flowers. The plant bears elongated, slender pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in coastal areas and disturbed soils.

Due to its ornamental value and historical use in traditional medicine, it is occasionally cultivated in warm regions.
