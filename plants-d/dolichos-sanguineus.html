Dolichos sanguineus

Dolichos sanguineus, commonly known as the blood bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It produces striking red and orange flowers that are often used in ornamental gardening. The plant's pods contain edible seeds that are rich in protein and nutrients. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its botanical features make it both a valuable food source and a visually appealing addition to gardens.
