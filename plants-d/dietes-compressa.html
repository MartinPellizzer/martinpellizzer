Dietes compressa

Dietes compressa, commonly known as the narrow-leafed dittander, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters. It is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
