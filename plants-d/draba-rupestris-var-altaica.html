Draba rupestris var. altaica

Draba rupestris var. altaica, commonly known as the Alpine Draba, is a small flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many cruciferous plants. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it forms dense, low-growing mats. Its flowers are small and yellow, often clustered in tight inflorescences.

It is adapted to cold climates and is often found at high elevations where it thrives in well-drained soils.
