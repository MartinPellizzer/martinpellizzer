Dryopteris erythrosora f. viridisora

Dryopteris erythrosora f. viridisora, commonly known as the green-stemmed wood fern, is a fern species characterized by its distinctive frond structure and coloration.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is native to regions of eastern North America. The fronds are typically bipinnate, with a dark green hue and a serrated margin. This variant is notable for its vibrant, lush green coloration, particularly on the stems and young fronds.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
