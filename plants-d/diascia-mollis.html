Diascia mollis

Diascia mollis, commonly known as the common diascia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of bright red, orange, or yellow flowers. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is drought-tolerant and well-suited for rock gardens or containers.

Diascia mollis is valued for its vibrant blooms and ease of cultivation.
