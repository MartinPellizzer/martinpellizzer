Digitaria spectabilis

Digitaria spectabilis, commonly known as silver bluestem, is a perennial grass native to the central and eastern United States.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and distinctive, spike-like inflorescence that becomes visible in late summer. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in prairies and disturbed areas. It plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing forage for wildlife.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it a common component of native grassland ecosystems.
