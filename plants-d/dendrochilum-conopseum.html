Dendrochilum conopseum

Dendrochilum conopseum, commonly known as the helmet orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a short, thick rhizome and produces erect, hairy pseudobulbs. The plant has linear, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. Its flowers are showy, with a distinctive helmet-like lip and a variety of colors including yellow, orange, and red.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and good air circulation for optimal growth.
