Dioscorea polygonoides var. scorpioidea

Dioscorea polygonoides var. scorpioidea, commonly known as the horned yam, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, spiny stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by cylindrical, fleshy fruits. Its tubers are sometimes used in traditional medicine, though they contain toxic compounds.

The species is notable for its unique morphology and ecological adaptations in its native habitat.
