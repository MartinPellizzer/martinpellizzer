Declieuxia clinopodioides

Declieuxia clinopodioides, commonly known as the silverleaf grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and narrow, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, pale green to brown spikelets that are typically arranged in loose clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Declieuxia clinopodioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in arid environments.
