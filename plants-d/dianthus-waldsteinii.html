Dianthus waldsteinii

Dianthus waldsteinii, commonly known as the Waldstein's campion, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink or red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain seeds.

Dianthus waldsteinii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
