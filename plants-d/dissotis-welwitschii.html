Dissotis welwitschii

Dissotis welwitschii, commonly known as the velvetleaf arrowroot, is a perennial herb native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features erect, hairy stems and large, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid lowland areas.

Its rhizomes are used in traditional medicine and as a source of starch.
