Dendrorkis fusidendris

Dendrorkis fusidendris, commonly known as the clustered orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact, clustered growth habit. The plant features slender, hairy stems that bear lance-shaped leaves with a waxy surface. Its flowers are small and fragrant, typically appearing in clusters and displaying a range of colors from white to pale pink.

Dendrorkis fusidendris is often cultivated for its ornamental value in greenhouses and tropical gardens.
