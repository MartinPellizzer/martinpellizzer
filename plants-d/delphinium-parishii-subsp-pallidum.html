Delphinium parishii subsp. pallidum

Delphinium parishii subsp.

pallidum, commonly known as the pale delphinium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States. It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that bear clusters of blue to purple flowers.

pallidum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
