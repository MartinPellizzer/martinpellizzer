Dilochia cantleyi

The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surfaces of trees rather than in soil, and relies on specific humidity and light conditions for survival. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Dilochia cantleyi is considered critically endangered and is protected under Australian conservation laws.
