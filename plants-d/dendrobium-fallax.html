Dendrobium fallax

Dendrobium fallax, commonly known as the orchid with a unique growth habit, is a sympodial epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a tall, cane-like stem with numerous nodes and is characterized by its clustered, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated for its striking floral display.
