Drimys subreticulata

Drimys subreticulata, commonly known as the Chilean wintergreen, is a small evergreen shrub native to the temperate rainforests of southern Chile.

It features dense, dark green leaves with a leathery texture and a distinctive, aromatic scent when crushed. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. Its fruit is a bright red, fleshy drupe that ripens in summer.

Drimys subreticulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shady, moist garden environments.
