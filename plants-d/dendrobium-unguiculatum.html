Dendrobium unguiculatum

Dendrobium unguiculatum, commonly known as the toothed dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with cane-like stems that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often with a distinctive toothed lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Dendrobium unguiculatum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
