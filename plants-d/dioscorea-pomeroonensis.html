Dioscorea pomeroonensis

Dioscorea pomeroonensis, commonly known as the Pomeroon yam, is a species of flowering plant in the family Dioscoreaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly found in the countries of Guyana and Venezuela. The plant is a climbing vine with tuberous roots that are used for their edible qualities. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a rough texture, while its flowers are small and greenish-white in color.

The tubers of Dioscorea pomeroonensis are valued not only for their nutritional content but also for their traditional medicinal uses.
