Dieteria canescens var. canescens

Dieteria canescens var. canescens, commonly known as the slender dieteria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils. It is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
