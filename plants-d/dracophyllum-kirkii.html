Dracophyllum kirkii

Dracophyllum kirkii, commonly known as the Kauri dracophyllum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant has small, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers, which are followed by red or orange berries.

Dracophyllum kirkii is often found in coastal and mountainous regions, thriving in well-drained soils and humid climates.
