Declieuxia deltoidea var. deltoidea

Declieuxia deltoidea var. deltoidea, commonly known as the delta sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the wetlands of eastern Australia.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in spike-like clusters during the spring and summer months.
