Dendrochilum tenellum

Dendrochilum tenellum, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pale pink. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its leaves are elongated and strap-like, and it is often found epiphytic on trees or terrestrially in moist soils.

Dendrochilum tenellum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
