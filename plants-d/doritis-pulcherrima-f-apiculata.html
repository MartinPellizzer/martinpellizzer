Doritis pulcherrima f. apiculata

Doritis pulcherrima f. apiculata, commonly known as the Apiculate Pincushion Plant, is a hybrid cultivar of the Doritis pulcherrima species.

It is a succulent plant native to South Africa, characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are typically green with a reddish or purple tinge. The plant produces vibrant orange to yellow flowers in spring, which are borne on tall, erect stems. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to maintain its compact growth habit.

This plant is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
