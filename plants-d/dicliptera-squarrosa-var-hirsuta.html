Dicliptera squarrosa var. hirsuta

Dicliptera squarrosa var. hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy dicliptera, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family (Poaceae).

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia. The plant is characterized by its hairy, cylindrical stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, typical of the Poaceae family.

This species is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
