Dioscorea tubulosa

Dioscorea tubulosa, commonly known as the tubular yam, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the Dioscoreaceae family and is characterized by its tuberous roots, which are often used in traditional medicine. The plant has compound leaves with long, slender leaflets and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers. Its fruit is a capsule that contains numerous seeds.

Due to its medicinal properties and adaptability, Dioscorea tubulosa is of interest in both agricultural and pharmacological research.
