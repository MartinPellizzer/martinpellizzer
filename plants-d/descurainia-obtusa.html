Descurainia obtusa

Descurainia obtusa, commonly known as the bluntleaf tansy, is an annual flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cress plants. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes, and its leaves are typically ovate to elliptic in shape. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields and along roadsides.
