Dicranostyles mildbraediana

Dicranostyles mildbraediana, commonly known as the mildbraed's feather moss, is a species of moss belonging to the family Dicranaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, cushion-like growth form and is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant has slender, wiry stems that are covered with small, leaf-like structures called phyllidia. Its reproductive structures, known as capsules, are borne on short stalks and release spores when mature.

Dicranostyles mildbraediana plays an important ecological role in soil stabilization and moisture retention in its habitat.
