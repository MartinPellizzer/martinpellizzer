Diploter

Diploter, commonly known as the African orchid, is a genus of flowering plants native to Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its intricate and often fragrant blooms. The plants typically have tall, erect stems with broad, strap-like leaves. Diploter species are known for their showy, colorful flowers that come in various shades of pink, purple, and white.

They are popular in horticulture for their ornamental value and are often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
