Dioscorea dielsii

Dioscorea dielsii, commonly known as the Diels' yam, is a climbing vine belonging to the family Dioscoreaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific Islands. The plant produces tuberous roots that are often used in traditional medicine and as a food source. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a rough texture, while its flowers are typically small and greenish-white.

Dioscorea dielsii is valued for its potential medicinal properties and is often cultivated for its nutritional value.
