Dendrochilum prodigiosum

Dendrochilum prodigiosum, commonly known as the red-lipped orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with fleshy leaves that help store moisture. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers with bright red lips and yellowish-white petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts and botanical gardens.
