Dipladenia gentianoides subvar. longiloba

Dipladenia gentianoides subvar. longiloba, commonly known as the long-stemmed Dipladenia, is a tropical climbing plant native to South America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically bright orange or red, with a yellow throat, blooming throughout the growing season. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy and have a slightly waxy texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil and full sun to partial shade.

This subvariety is prized for its vibrant blooms and is often cultivated as a ornamental plant in gardens and greenhouses.
