Diospyros Pilosanthera Var. Oblonga

Diospyros pilosanthera var. oblonga is a lesser-known species of ebony tree native to parts of Southeast Asia. This variant is distinguished by its elongated leaves and dense, dark wood that is highly valued for its durability and aesthetic appeal. It thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil. Despite its rarity, it plays an important role in local ecosystems and traditional crafts. Further study is needed to fully understand its ecological significance and conservation status.
