Daphne cavaleriei

Daphne cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese daphne, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features fragrant, showy flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring, typically in shades of pink, white, and purple. The plant has opposite, simple, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, and it produces small, berry-like fruits in the fall.

Daphne cavaleriei is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and winter flowering habit.
