Diplachne squarrosa var. longiaristata

Diplachne squarrosa var. longiaristata, commonly known as the long-leaf diplachne, is a perennial grass native to parts of Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and long, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing numerous tiny florets. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

This species is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.
