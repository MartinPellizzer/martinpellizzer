Dolichos heterophyllus

Dolichos heterophyllus, commonly known as the variable bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its variable leaf shapes, which can range from ovate to trifoliate, depending on environmental conditions. The plant produces small, yellow flowers followed by elongated pods containing edible seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun for optimal growth.

Dolichos heterophyllus is valued for its nutritional seeds and is cultivated in various parts of Asia and Africa.
