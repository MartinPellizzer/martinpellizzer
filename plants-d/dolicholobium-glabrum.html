Dolicholobium glabrum

Dolicholobium glabrum, commonly known as the smooth dolicholobium, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous structure and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant typically features compound leaves with a smooth, glabrous surface, and its flowers are usually small and clustered in racemes. It produces elongated pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used in traditional herbal remedies for various ailments.
