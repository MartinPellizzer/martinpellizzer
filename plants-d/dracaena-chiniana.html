Dracaena chiniana

Dracaena chiniana, commonly known as the Chinese dragon tree, is a flowering plant native to southern China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and narrow, strap-like leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge along the edges. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers that appear in clusters at the base of the leaves. Dracaena chiniana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor conditions.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both horticultural and medicinal purposes.
