Dendrium hugeri

Dendrium hugeri, commonly known as the silverleaf vine, is a climbing plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, woody stems and heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of silvery-white hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from pale yellow to deep orange, typically blooming in the summer months. Its unique foliage and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and green wall installations.

Dendrium hugeri thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
