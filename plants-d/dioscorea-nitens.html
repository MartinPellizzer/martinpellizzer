Dioscorea nitens

Dioscorea nitens, commonly known as the slender yam, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its tuberous roots, which are the primary part used for culinary and medicinal purposes. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a rough texture, while its stems are hairy and twining.

The tubers of Dioscorea nitens are often used in traditional medicine and as a food source in various regions.
