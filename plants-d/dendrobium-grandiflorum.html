Dendrobium grandiflorum

Dendrobium grandiflorum, commonly known as the large-flowered dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with cane-like stems that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink, often with a yellow throat. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium grandiflorum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
