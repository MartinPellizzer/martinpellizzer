Discocactus spinosior

Discocactus spinosior, commonly known as the spiny discocactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its thick, ribbed stems that store water. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It has sharp spines that provide protection from herbivores.

Discocactus spinosior thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, requiring minimal water and full sunlight.
