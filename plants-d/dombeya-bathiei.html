Dombeya bathiei

Dombeya bathiei, commonly known as the African snowbell, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its clusters of white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a cool, moist environment.

Dombeya bathiei is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in temperate climates.
