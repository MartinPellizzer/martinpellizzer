Dracaena francisii

Dracaena francisii, commonly known as the Francis's dracaena, is a tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and long, narrow, dark green leaves that are often slightly curved. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by red or orange berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Dracaena francisii is relatively low maintenance and can tolerate dry conditions once established.
