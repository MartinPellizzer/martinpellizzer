Dicliptera fragilis

Dicliptera fragilis, commonly known as the slender grass or fragrant grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or along stream banks. The plant features slender, erect stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often aromatic. Its flowers are small and clustered, producing a delicate, fragrant scent that attracts pollinators.

Dicliptera fragilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
