Diuris pallescens

Diuris pallescens, commonly known as the Grey-tongued Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It features a slender, hairy stem with a single or few flowers that are typically pale green to greyish in color. The flowers have a unique structure with a narrow, hood-like labellum and are often adorned with intricate markings. It thrives in well-drained soils in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and woodlands.

This species is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
