Digitaria tenuissima

Digitaria tenuissima, commonly known as slender digitgrass, is a perennial grass native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, slender blades and compact growth habit, often forming dense mats. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along water bodies.

Digitaria tenuissima is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in erosion control due to its vigorous growth.
