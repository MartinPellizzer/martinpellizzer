Diospyros minutiflora

Diospyros minutiflora, commonly known as the small-flowered persimmon, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Ebenaceae family, which includes other persimmon species. The plant is characterized by its small, white to pale yellow flowers that appear in clusters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a waxy coating.

The fruit is a small, globose berry that turns from green to orange-red when mature.
