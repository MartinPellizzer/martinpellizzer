Daphne liottardi

Daphne liottardi, commonly known as the rock daphne, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, evergreen foliage with dark green, leathery leaves that are aromatic when crushed. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white or pink flowers in early spring, which are followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Daphne liottardi is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature.
