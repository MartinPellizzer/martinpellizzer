Digitaria neesiana

Digitaria neesiana, commonly known as flatgrass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its flat, narrow leaves and dense, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It is often found in disturbed soils and is adapted to thrive in warm, humid climates.

Digitaria neesiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
