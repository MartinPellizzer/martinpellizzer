Dalbergaria variabilis

Dalbergaria variabilis, commonly known as the variable dalbergia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and often compound leaves. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually in racemes, and are often pink or purple in color.
