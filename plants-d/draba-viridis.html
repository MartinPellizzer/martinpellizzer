Draba viridis

Draba viridis, commonly known as green draba, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches a height of 5 to 15 centimeters. The plant features basal leaves that are usually lobed or divided, and it produces small, yellowish-white flowers in early spring. Draba viridis is native to alpine and arctic regions of Europe and Asia, where it thrives in rocky and well-drained soils.

Its ability to survive in harsh environments makes it an important component of high-altitude ecosystems.
