Desmoscelis villosa

Desmoscelis villosa, commonly known as the hairy leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its hairy, velvety leaves and small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforest or woodland areas. Its unique morphology makes it a distinctive feature in its native habitat.

It plays a role in supporting local pollinators and contributes to the biodiversity of its ecosystem.
