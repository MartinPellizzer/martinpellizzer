Dryopteris championii

Dryopteris championii, commonly known as the giant shield fern, is a large, robust fern native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its tall, upright fronds that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height. The fronds are dark green, leathery, and have a distinctive, shield-like shape with a prominent midrib. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky areas.

This fern is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded gardens.
