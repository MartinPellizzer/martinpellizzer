Danaea vivax

Danaea vivax, commonly known as the flame flower, is a tropical plant belonging to the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in humid, shaded environments. Danaea vivax is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the year under suitable conditions.
