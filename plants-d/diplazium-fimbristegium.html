Diplazium fimbristegium

Diplazium fimbristegium, commonly known as the hairy shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its upright, compact growth habit and dark green, leathery fronds. The fronds are typically divided into segments with a distinctive hairy texture on the underside, giving it its common name. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, including India, China, and Southeast Asia.

This fern prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories where it thrives in humid, well-drained soil.
