Delissea purpurea

Delissea purpurea, commonly known as the purple delissea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant purple flowers that bloom in summer. Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and compact growth habit.
