Diospyros vignei

Diospyros vignei, commonly known as the African persimmon, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Ebenaceae family, which includes ebony and persimmon trees. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a small, fleshy berry that turns from green to yellow or orange when mature.

Diospyros vignei is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
