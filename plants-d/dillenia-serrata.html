Dillenia serrata

Dillenia serrata, commonly known as the serrated dillenia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant features simple, alternate leaves with serrated margins, giving it its common name. Its flowers are showy, with five petals and numerous stamens, typically white or pale yellow in color.

The fruit is a woody, spiny capsule that splits open to release its seeds.
