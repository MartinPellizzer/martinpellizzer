Doellia cafra

Doellia cafra, commonly known as the African milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a rough texture. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Its milky sap is a notable feature, often used in traditional medicine.

Doellia cafra is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky soils.
