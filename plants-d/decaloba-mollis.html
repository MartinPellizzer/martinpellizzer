Decaloba mollis

Decaloba mollis, commonly known as the silky hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are soft and covered in fine hairs, giving it a silky texture. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in moist, shaded areas.

Decaloba mollis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
