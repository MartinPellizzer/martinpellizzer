Durio macrantha

Durio macrantha, commonly known as the giant durian, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, spiky fruit that can weigh up to 10 kilograms. The plant produces flowers with a strong, distinctive odor, attracting pollinators such as flies and bees. Its leaves are broad and ovate, with a smooth texture and a dark green color.

The tree thrives in humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
