Dimorphandra polyandra

Dimorphandra polyandra, commonly known as the yellow flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a fiery appearance. The plant has compound leaves with long, slender leaflets and produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The botanical features of Dimorphandra polyandra make it a distinctive and visually appealing species in tropical landscapes.
