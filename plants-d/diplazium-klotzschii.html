Diplazium klotzschii

Diplazium klotzschii, commonly known as the slender fern, is a species of polypodioid fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, pinnate fronds that are typically light green and have a soft, feathery appearance. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are often covered in a thin layer of soil, which helps in nutrient absorption.

Diplazium klotzschii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
