Digitaria bicornis subsp. lamarckiana

Digitaria bicornis subsp.

lamarckiana, commonly known as the two-horned grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions. It is characterized by its erect growth habit and narrow, linear leaves.

The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and disturbed sites.

Its morphology and ecological adaptability make it an important component of certain ecosystems.
