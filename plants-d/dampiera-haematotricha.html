Dampiera haematotricha

Dampiera haematotricha, commonly known as the blood-red dampiera, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that typically reaches heights of up to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, adapted to withstand arid conditions.

Dampiera haematotricha is often found in rocky or sandy soils in coastal and inland regions.
