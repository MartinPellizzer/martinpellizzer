Draconia campestris f. oligocephala

Draconia campestris f. oligocephala, commonly known as the narrow-headed dragonhead, is a flowering plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The inflorescence consists of whorls of small, tubular flowers, typically in shades of blue or violet. This subspecies is native to southern Europe and is often found in dry, sunny grasslands.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its compact form and attractive blooms.
