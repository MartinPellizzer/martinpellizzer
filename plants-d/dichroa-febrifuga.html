Dichroa febrifuga

Dichroa febrifuga, commonly known as Chinese quinine or feverfew, is a shrub native to Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves and clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant contains bioactive compounds, including febrifugine, which have been used in traditional medicine. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and medicinal contexts.
