Deschampsia kawakamii

Deschampsia kawakamii, commonly known as Kawakami's feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to Japan.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage and produces delicate, feathery inflorescences that are often pale green to golden in color. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or mountainous regions. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its graceful appearance.

Deschampsia kawakamii is also of interest in ecological studies due to its role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
