Diosma hirta

Diosma hirta, commonly known as the hairy diosma, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades, and is characterized by its small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and has hairy foliage, giving it its common name. It produces berries that are often used in traditional medicine.

Diosma hirta is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native environment.
