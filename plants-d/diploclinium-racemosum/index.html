Diploclinium Racemosum

Diploclinium racemosum is a unique flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia. It is known for its striking appearance and medicinal properties. The plant features compound leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. It thrives in humid environments and is often found in dense forests. Despite its beauty, it is also considered a potential weed in some areas.
