Dyckia stolonifera

Dyckia stolonifera, commonly known as the creeping dyckia, is a species of grass-like plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit. The plant produces striking, sword-shaped leaves that are often covered in a powdery bloom, giving it a distinctive appearance. Dyckia stolonifera spreads through stolons, forming dense mats that can cover large areas.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in a variety of conditions.
