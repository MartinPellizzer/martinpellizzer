Dendrobium punbatuense

Dendrobium punbatuense, commonly known as the Bornean orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Borneo.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, often on rocks or in the undergrowth. Its flowers are usually yellow or green with red or purple markings, adding to its ornamental value.

Dendrobium punbatuense is valued for its beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
