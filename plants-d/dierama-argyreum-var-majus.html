Dierama argyreum var. majus

Dierama argyreum var. majus, commonly known as the silverleaf dierama, is a striking evergreen perennial native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of silver-gray, velvety foliage that provides a dramatic visual contrast. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

This ornamental plant is prized for its unique appearance and is often used in rock gardens or as a specimen plant.
