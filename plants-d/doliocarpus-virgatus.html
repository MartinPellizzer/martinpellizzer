Doliocarpus virgatus

Doliocarpus virgatus, commonly known as the Virgin's shield, is a species of flowering plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It is a small tree or shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and mangrove swamps. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered, with a distinctive yellowish-green color.

The fruit is a small, capsule-like structure that contains several seeds.
