Dracocephalum batalinii

Dracocephalum batalinii, commonly known as Batalin's dragonhead, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square, branched stems and small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or blue in color. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.

Dracocephalum batalinii is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
