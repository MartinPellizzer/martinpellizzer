Dendrobium sarcochilus var. megalorhizum

Dendrobium sarcochilus var. megalorhizum, commonly known as the giant root dendrobium, is a species of orchid characterized by its thick, fleshy roots and upright growth habit.

It originates from the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often clustered along the stems. Its roots are particularly notable for their size and are sometimes harvested for traditional medicinal purposes.

This variety is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and unique growth characteristics.
