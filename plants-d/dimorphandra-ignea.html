Dimorphandra ignea

Dimorphandra ignea, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, pointed leaflets. Its bark is smooth and grayish, and it can grow up to 20 meters tall.

Dimorphandra ignea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often planted in gardens and parks for its vibrant floral display.
