Delissea aurantiaca

Delissea aurantiaca, commonly known as the orange bellflower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking orange-red tubular flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun exposure. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, forming a basal rosette.

Delissea aurantiaca is valued in horticulture for its vibrant color and drought tolerance.
