Dombeya albisquama

Dombeya albisquama, commonly known as the white-flowered dombeya, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, white, tubular flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a rough texture and a strong, aromatic scent. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Dombeya albisquama is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
