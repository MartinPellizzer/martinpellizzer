Dendrobium pergracile

Dendrobium pergracile, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, utilizing its aerial roots to absorb moisture and nutrients from the environment. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in warm, humid climates with bright, indirect light.

Dendrobium pergracile is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
