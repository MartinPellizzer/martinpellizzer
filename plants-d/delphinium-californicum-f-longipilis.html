Delphinium californicum f. longipilis

Delphinium californicum f. longipilis, commonly known as the long-hair delphinium, is a perennial herb native to California.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy blue to purple flowers. The plant features long, silky hairs on its stems and leaves, giving it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in meadows and open woodlands.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and native plant collections.
