Dombeya spectabilis subsp. humblotii

Dombeya spectabilis subsp.

humblotii, commonly known as the Madagascar dombeya, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar. It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple.

The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough texture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to the humid, tropical climate of its native habitat.
