Dieffenbachia seguine var. robusta

Dieffenbachia seguine var. robusta, commonly known as the Leopard Plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, broad, heart-shaped leaves with striking white to yellow markings. The plant is part of the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like peace lilies. Its foliage is often used in interior landscaping due to its dramatic appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity to flourish.
