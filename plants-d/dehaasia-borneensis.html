Dehaasia borneensis

Dehaasia borneensis, commonly known as the Bornean starfruit tree, is a tropical plant native to the island of Borneo.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its small, star-shaped fruits that resemble those of the common starfruit. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a smooth texture with a slight pubescence.

The flowers are white and fragrant, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds.
