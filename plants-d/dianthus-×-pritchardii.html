Dianthus × pritchardii

Dianthus × pritchardii, commonly known as the Pritchard's campion, is a hybrid species within the carnation family, Caryophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its showy, pink or white flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in arid or semi-arid environments. It has narrow, linear leaves and a compact, upright growth habit.

Dianthus × pritchardii is native to regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.
