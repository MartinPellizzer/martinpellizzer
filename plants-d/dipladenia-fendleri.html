Dipladenia fendleri

Dipladenia fendleri, commonly known as the rainbow vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to red, often with a contrasting throat. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves and thrives in warm, humid conditions with plenty of sunlight. It is often cultivated as a ornamental plant for its vibrant blooms and trailing growth habit.

Dipladenia fendleri is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for gardeners and indoor plant enthusiasts.
