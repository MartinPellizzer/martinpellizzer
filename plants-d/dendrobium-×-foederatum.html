Dendrobium × foederatum

Dendrobium × foederatum, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces long, arching inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-draining soil.

Dendrobium × foederatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
