Diospyros confusa

Diospyros confusa, commonly known as the broadleaf date palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height, characterized by its pinnately compound leaves and smooth, gray bark. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by edible, date-like fruits.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and agricultural contexts.
