Dysphania Ambrosioides Var. Anthelmintica

Dysphania ambrosioides var. anthelmintica is a lesser-known yet highly valuable plant with a long history of traditional use. Native to tropical regions, it is particularly valued for its potent anthelmintic properties. This variant of Dysphania ambrosioides has been used for centuries in herbal medicine to combat intestinal parasites. Its active compounds, including volatile oils and sesquiterpenes, contribute to its medicinal efficacy. Despite its benefits, it remains under-researched and often overlooked in modern pharmacology.
