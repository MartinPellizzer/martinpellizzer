Dendrobium purpureiflorum

Dendrobium purpureiflorum, commonly known as the purple-flowered orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with cane-like stems that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of purple and pink, often arranged in racemes. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium purpureiflorum is prized for its ornamental value and is commonly cultivated in greenhouses and as a houseplant.
