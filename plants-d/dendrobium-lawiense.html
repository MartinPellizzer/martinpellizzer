Dendrobium lawiense

Dendrobium lawiense, commonly known as the Mindanao orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically blooms during the dry season, producing long, narrow petals and a distinctive lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-drained soil.

Dendrobium lawiense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
