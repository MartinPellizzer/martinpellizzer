Disa karooica

Disa karooica, commonly known as the Karoo Disa, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, violet-blue flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils in arid or semi-arid regions. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Disa karooica is often found in the Karoo region, where it thrives in full sun and requires minimal water.
