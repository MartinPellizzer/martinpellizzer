Diachyrium rigens

Diachyrium rigens, commonly known as the rigid diachyrium, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Poaceae, or grass family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically arranged in dense clusters. It is native to arid and semi-arid regions, where it thrives in well-drained soils.

Due to its resilience and adaptability, it is often found in disturbed habitats and along roadsides.
