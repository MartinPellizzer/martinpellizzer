Dicoma antunesii

Dicoma antunesii, commonly known as the Cape rose.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, belonging to the Asteraceae family. The plant features succulent, fleshy leaves and produces showy, pink to purple flowers. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.

Dicoma antunesii thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.
