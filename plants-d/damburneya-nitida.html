Damburneya nitida

Damburneya nitida, commonly known as the slender damburneya, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Caribbean islands.

It is characterized by its slender, columnar trunk and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by red, fleshy fruits. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and requires a warm, tropical climate.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a landscape plant.
