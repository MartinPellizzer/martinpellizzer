Draba athoa var. glabrescens

Draba athoa var. glabrescens, commonly known as the glabrous Draba, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a small herbaceous perennial that typically grows in alpine and arctic environments. The plant is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers. It is found in regions such as northern Europe and parts of Asia.

This variety is distinguished by its smooth, hairless appearance, which helps it survive in harsh, cold climates.
