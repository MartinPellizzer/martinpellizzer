Deutzia crenata var. magnifica

Deutzia crenata var. magnifica, commonly known as the variegated deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, upright growth habit and clusters of fragrant, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in early summer. The foliage is notable for its striking variegation, featuring white and green patterns on the leaves. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

This plant is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
