Delosperma acuminatum

Delosperma acuminatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white powder, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces vibrant pink to purple flowers during the summer months, adding ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for water-wise landscaping.
