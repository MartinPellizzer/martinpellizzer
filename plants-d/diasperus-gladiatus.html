Diasperus gladiatus

Diasperus gladiatus, commonly known as the swordleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its narrow, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by fluffy, white seed heads. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassy areas and disturbed sites.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
