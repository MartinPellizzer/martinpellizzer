Discocactus buenekeri

Discocactus buenekeri, commonly known as the Mexican star cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped ribs and spiny areoles. The plant typically grows as a solitary stem with a rounded, globular form. Its flowers, which are usually yellow or orange, bloom in late spring or early summer.

Discocactus buenekeri is well adapted to dry environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems.
