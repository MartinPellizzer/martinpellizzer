Dracaena Suffruticosa

Dracaena suffruticosa is a unique and resilient plant native to the Canary Islands. It is known for its striking appearance and ability to thrive in harsh conditions. This species features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, making it well adapted to arid environments. Its slow growth and ornamental qualities make it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Despite its hardy nature, it requires careful attention to light and watering to flourish.
