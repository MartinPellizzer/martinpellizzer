Dipladenia buchtienii

Dipladenia buchtienii, commonly known as the yellow bell vine, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a climbing vine that produces vibrant yellow flowers resembling small bells. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in warm, humid climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.

Dipladenia buchtienii requires well-draining soil and regular watering to flourish.
