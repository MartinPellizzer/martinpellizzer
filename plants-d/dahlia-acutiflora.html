Dahlia acutiflora

Dahlia acutiflora, commonly known as the sharp-flowered dahlia, is a tuberous-rooted perennial plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, single or double flowers with vibrant colors ranging from red to orange and yellow. The plant features sturdy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white or gray hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking colorful, long-lasting blooms.

Dahlia acutiflora is also valued for its ornamental use in gardens and floral arrangements.
