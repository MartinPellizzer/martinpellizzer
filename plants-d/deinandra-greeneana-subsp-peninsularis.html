Deinandra greeneana subsp. peninsularis

Deinandra greeneana subsp.

peninsularis, commonly known as the Peninsular Deinandra, is a shrub native to the southern United States, particularly found in Texas and northern Mexico. It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and nitrogen-fixing capabilities.
