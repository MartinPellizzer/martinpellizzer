Dalbergiella gossweileri

Dalbergiella gossweileri, commonly known as the African burrbean, is a small, shrub-like legume native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. The plant produces small, yellow flowers followed by woody pods containing flattened, seed-like structures. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three small, oval-shaped leaflets.

Dalbergiella gossweileri is adapted to arid conditions and has a deep root system to access water from deeper soil layers.
