Dryopteris unidentata var. paleacea

Dryopteris unidentata var. paleacea, commonly known as the pale-leaf wood fern, is a variant of the barren sword fern.

It is a perennial fern native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere. The plant features fronds that are typically pale green, with a distinctive, elongated shape. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and has a rhizomatous growth habit.

Its spores are produced on the underside of the fronds, contributing to its reproductive strategy.
