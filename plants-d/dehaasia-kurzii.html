Dehaasia kurzii

Dehaasia kurzii, commonly known as the Kashmir stonecrop, is a perennial succulent native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapted to its arid and cold environment. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and white during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts.
