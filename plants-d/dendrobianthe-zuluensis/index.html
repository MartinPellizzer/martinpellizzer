Dendrobianthe Zuluensis

Dendrobianthe zuluensis is a rare and unique plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is known for its striking, paddle-shaped leaves. This species thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and requires minimal water. Its unusual appearance makes it a popular choice for collectors and botanists. Despite its beauty, it remains poorly studied and is often overlooked in mainstream horticulture.
