Dianthus stenopetalus var. pancicii

Dianthus stenopetalus var. pancicii, commonly known as the Pancici's narrow-petaled campion, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its striking pink to red flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage consists of narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

This variety is noted for its compact growth habit and is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
