Dypsis mahia

Dypsis mahia, commonly known as the Mahia palm, is a striking tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments within its native habitat. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor landscaping.

Dypsis mahia is known for its slow growth rate and ability to thrive in well-draining soil with consistent moisture.
