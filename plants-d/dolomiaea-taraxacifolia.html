Dolomiaea taraxacifolia

Dolomiaea taraxacifolia, commonly known as the leafy dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by fluffy white seed heads. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in grassland habitats.

Dolomiaea taraxacifolia is a perennial species with a compact growth habit.
