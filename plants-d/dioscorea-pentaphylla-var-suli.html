Dioscorea pentaphylla var. suli

Dioscorea pentaphylla var. suli, commonly known as the Suli yam, is a variant of the genus Dioscorea, which belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae.

It is a climbing herbaceous perennial that produces tubers as its storage organ, typical of the yam family. The plant features palmately lobed leaves and bears small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters. Its tubers are often used in traditional medicine and as a food source in certain regions.

The species is native to parts of Africa, particularly Nigeria and Cameroon.
