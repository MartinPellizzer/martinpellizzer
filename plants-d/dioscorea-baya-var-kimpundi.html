Dioscorea baya var. kimpundi

Dioscorea baya var. kimpundi, commonly known as African yam bean, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its tuberous roots, which are the primary edible part. The plant produces small, green, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of yellowish-white flowers. Its tubers are rich in carbohydrates and are traditionally used as a food source.

The plant also has medicinal uses in some African cultures.
