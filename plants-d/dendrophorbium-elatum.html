Dendrophorbium elatum

Dendrophorbium elatum, commonly known as the giant orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, showy flowers that bloom in a striking array of colors. Native to the tropical regions of South America, this plant thrives in warm, humid environments with well-drained soil. Its unique growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and specialized collections.

The plant's ability to produce numerous inflorescences adds to its visual appeal and ecological significance.
