Deparia formosana

Deparia formosana, commonly known as the Formosa shield fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a leathery texture.
