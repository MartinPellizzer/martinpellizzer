Delosperma calycinum

Delosperma calycinum, commonly known as the blue ice plant, is a succulent species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, and purple, typically during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements, it is well-suited for arid and semi-arid environments.
