Daphne broteriana

Daphne broteriana, commonly known as the Christmas daphne, is a deciduous shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It features small, fragrant flowers that bloom in winter, typically in shades of pink and white. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy, providing an attractive appearance throughout the year. It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and winter flowering habit.

However, it is toxic, containing irritant compounds that can cause skin and respiratory irritation.
