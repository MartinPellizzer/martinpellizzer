Desmocladus lateriticus

Desmocladus lateriticus, commonly known as the lateritic desmocladus, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

The plant is adapted to survive in nutrient-poor, lateritic soils, which are typically dry and rocky. Desmocladus lateriticus is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in dry gardens.
