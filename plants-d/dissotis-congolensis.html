Dissotis congolensis

Dissotis congolensis, commonly known as the Congo buttonweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Commelinaceae and is characterized by its rosette growth habit and striking blue or purple flowers. Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
