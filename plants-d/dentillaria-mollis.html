Dentillaria mollis

Dentillaria mollis, commonly known as the soft denticle, is a flowering plant native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

It is a small annual herb that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils along coastal areas. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, white or pale blue flowers with five petals. Its fruit is a capsule that releases tiny seeds when mature.

Dentillaria mollis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
