Dendrobium conspicuum

Dendrobium conspicuum, commonly known as the showy dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, branching stem covered with fleshy, dark green leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange, often with a red or purple lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Dendrobium conspicuum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
