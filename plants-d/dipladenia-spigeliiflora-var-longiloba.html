Dipladenia spigeliiflora var. longiloba

Dipladenia spigeliiflora var. longiloba, commonly known as the long-stemmed flame vine, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant orange to red tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are heart-shaped, glossy, and often have a waxy texture. This variety is a cultivar of Dipladenia spigeliiflora, known for its extended stem length and showy, long-lasting blooms.

It thrives in warm, sunny conditions and is often used in hanging baskets or as a climbing plant in gardens.
