Dracaena gloneri

Dracaena gloneri, commonly known as the Glomerate Dracaena, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, dark green leaves with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers on tall, slender spikes during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
