Dolichopentas decora var. triangularis

Dolichopentas decora var. triangularis, commonly known as the triangular penta, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features vibrant, pentagonal-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardens and tropical plant collections.
