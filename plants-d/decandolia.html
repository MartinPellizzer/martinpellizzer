Decandolia

Decandolia, commonly known as the sacred lotus of ancient Egypt, is a flowering plant belonging to the Nelumbonaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that open at dawn and close at dusk. Its botanical structure includes a thick, woody stem and a distinctive, palmate leaf pattern.
