Dactylorchis elodes

Dactylorchis elodes, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a few leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil.

Dactylorchis elodes is an ephemeral plant, often blooming briefly in late spring or early summer.
