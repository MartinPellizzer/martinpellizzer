Didiscus geraniifolius

Didiscus geraniifolius, commonly known as the silverleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-green foliage and yellow composite flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has a basal rosette of leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Didiscus geraniifolius is also known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
