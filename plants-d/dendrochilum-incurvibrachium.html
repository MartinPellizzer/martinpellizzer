Dendrochilum incurvibrachium

Dendrochilum incurvibrachium, commonly known as the curved-brachium orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and attractive, fragrant flowers. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences bearing showy, yellowish-green to white blooms with distinctive, recurved petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, loose substrate for optimal growth.

Dendrochilum incurvibrachium is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
