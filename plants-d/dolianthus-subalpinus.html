Dolianthus subalpinus

Dolianthus subalpinus, commonly known as the alpine dianthus, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that typically range from pink to red. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and thrives in well-drained, rocky soils. It is adapted to cold climates and can withstand harsh alpine conditions.

Dolianthus subalpinus is often found in meadows and open slopes at elevations above 3,000 meters.
