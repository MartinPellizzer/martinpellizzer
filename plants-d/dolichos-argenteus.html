Dolichos argenteus

Dolichos argenteus, commonly known as silver bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a leguminous species that produces small, yellow flowers followed by elongated, bean-like pods. The plant is characterized by its silvery-white undersides on its leaves, which give it its common name. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its edible seeds.

The plant also has ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and floral display.
