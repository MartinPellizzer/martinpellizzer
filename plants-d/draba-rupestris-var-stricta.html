Draba rupestris var. stricta

Draba rupestris var. stricta, commonly known as the Alpine Draba, is a flowering plant native to mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is a low-growing, herbaceous perennial that typically forms dense mats or cushions. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers in early spring, which are clustered in racemes. Its leaves are basal, linear, and often covered in a fine layer of white or grayish hairs.

It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to cold, alpine environments.
