Dorstenia barnimiana var. barnimiana

Dorstenia barnimiana var. barnimiana, commonly known as the Barnim's fig-leaf plant, is a unique species native to tropical regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silky pubescence. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value and specific care requirements, it is a sought-after plant among collectors and enthusiasts.
