Dendrochilum cinnabarinum var. sanguineum

Dendrochilum cinnabarinum var. sanguineum, commonly known as the red-tipped orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a distinctive appearance, featuring bright red or orange-tipped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
