Dianthus caryophyllus var. tenuifolius

Dianthus caryophyllus var. tenuifolius, commonly known as the slenderleaf carnation, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, double or semi-double flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. The foliage is narrow and grass-like, giving it a distinctive appearance. Native to southern Europe, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

This variety is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and floral arrangements.
