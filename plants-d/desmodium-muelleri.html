Desmodium muelleri

Desmodium muelleri, commonly known as Mueller's tickclover, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant features trifoliate leaves with small, rounded leaflets and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense clusters. It is often found in grassy areas and is adapted to dry, well-drained soils.

Desmodium muelleri is also known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility.
