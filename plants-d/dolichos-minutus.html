Dolichos minutus

Dolichos minutus, commonly known as the dwarf hyacinth bean, is a small, annual legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, twisting stems and small, white or purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces elongated, bean-like pods containing small, edible seeds. It is often grown as a cover crop or for its ornamental value due to its delicate appearance.

Dolichos minutus is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, benefiting subsequent crops.
