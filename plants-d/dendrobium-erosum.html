Dendrobium erosum

Dendrobium erosum, commonly known as the red-flowered orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a tall, leafy stem that can reach up to 1 meter in height. The plant produces clusters of showy, red or orange-red flowers with a distinctive yellow throat, blooming in late summer to early autumn. Its leaves are elliptical and often covered in a fine, waxy coating.

Dendrobium erosum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses for its vibrant floral display.
