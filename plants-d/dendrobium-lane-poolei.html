Dendrobium lane-poolei

Dendrobium lane-poolei, commonly known as the Lane-Poole's orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to the Philippines.

It features a sympodial growth habit with multiple pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with intricate patterns. Its leaves are long and strap-like, adapted to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
