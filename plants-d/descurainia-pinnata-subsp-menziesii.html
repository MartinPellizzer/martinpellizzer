Descurainia pinnata subsp. menziesii

Descurainia pinnata subsp.

menziesii, commonly known as the Menzies' jointweed, is a member of the Brassicaceae family. It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America, particularly found in the western United States.

The plant has pinnately divided leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks.
