Dieffenbachia × memoria-corsii

Dieffenbachia × memoria-corsii, commonly known as the Coral Dragon Plant, is a hybrid houseplant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with a mix of green, white, and pink hues. The leaves are broad, lance-shaped, and often have a waxy texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to prevent root rot.

This plant is popular for its aesthetic value and ease of care, making it a favored choice for indoor gardening.
