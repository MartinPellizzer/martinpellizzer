Dieffenbachia consobrina

Dieffenbachia consobrina, commonly known as the leopard plant, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that feature a mix of white, yellow, and green patterns. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like peace lilies and calla lilies. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Dieffenbachia consobrina is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
