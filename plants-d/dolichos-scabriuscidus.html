Dolichos scabriuscidus

Dolichos scabriuscidus, commonly known as the rough sickle bean, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, zigzagging stems and trifoliate leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces elongated, curved pods that contain several kidney-shaped seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as fields and roadsides.

Dolichos scabriuscidus is valued for its edible seeds and its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in agricultural systems.
