Doronicum lucidum

Doronicum lucidum, commonly known as the shining leopard's bane, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central and Southern Europe.

It features basal leaves that are broadly ovate with a hairy surface, and its flowering stems bear clusters of bright yellow, tubular flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained, calcareous soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.

Doronicum lucidum is also noted for its hardy nature and ability to naturalize in suitable environments.
