Dracula burbidgeana

Dracula burbidgeana, commonly known as the Dragon's Blood Orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to the cloud forests of Colombia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red petals and sepals, which resemble the color and texture of dried blood. The plant produces intricate, spurred flowers that are adapted for pollination by specific insects. It thrives in high-altitude environments with high humidity and partial shade.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly sought after by botanists and orchid enthusiasts.
