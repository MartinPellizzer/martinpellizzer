Draba turgida var. glabra

Draba turgida var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth rockcress, is a member of the Brassicaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically thrives in alpine and arctic environments. The plant features small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are basal, rosette-like, and covered in a dense layer of fine, smooth hairs.

This variety is characterized by its glabrous (hairless) leaves, distinguishing it from other varieties within the Draba turgida species.
