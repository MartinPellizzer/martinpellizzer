Dichaea venezuelensis

Dichaea venezuelensis, commonly known as the Venezuelan dichaea, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Dichaea venezuelensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
