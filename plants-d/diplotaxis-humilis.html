Diplotaxis humilis

Diplotaxis humilis, commonly known as the African dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing annual herb with thin, erect stems and linear leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by fluffy, white seed heads. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in open grasslands and disturbed areas.

Diplotaxis humilis is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
