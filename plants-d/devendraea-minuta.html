Devendraea minuta

Devendraea minuta, commonly known as the dwarf devendraea, is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
