Dichanthelium dichotomum var. tenue

Dichanthelium dichotomum var. tenue, commonly known as slender dichotomous grass, is a perennial grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in wetland habitats such as marshes, swamps, and along the edges of ponds. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences. Its leaves are often hairless and have a distinct, slender appearance.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
