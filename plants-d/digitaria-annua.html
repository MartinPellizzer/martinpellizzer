Digitaria annua

Digitaria annua, commonly known as annual digitgrass, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its erect, slender stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural fields and along roadsides.

Digitaria annua is an annual plant, completing its life cycle within a single growing season.
