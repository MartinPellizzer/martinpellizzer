Dendrobium begoniicarpum var. parviflorum

Dendrobium begoniicarpum var. parviflorum, commonly known as the small-flowered begonia orchid, is a rare and unique hybrid orchid species.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces strikingly colorful flowers, typically in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are usually elliptical and covered in a waxy cuticle, aiding in water retention.

This variety is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts due to its beauty and rarity in cultivation.
