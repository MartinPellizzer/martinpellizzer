Dypsis concinna

Dypsis concinna, commonly known as the slender lady palm, is a tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and feathery, pinnate leaves that give it a graceful appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and prefers well-draining soil. Its foliage is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its aesthetic appeal.

Dypsis concinna is relatively slow-growing and requires careful attention to watering and light conditions to thrive.
