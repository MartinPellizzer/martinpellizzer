Dischidia subulata

Dischidia subulata, commonly known as the Needle Dischidia, is a climbing or trailing epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, needle-like leaves that are typically clustered in whorls along its stems. The plant produces small, white or pale green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks.

Its unique foliage and adaptability make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
