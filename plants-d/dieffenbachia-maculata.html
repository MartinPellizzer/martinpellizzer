Dieffenbachia maculata

Dieffenbachia maculata, commonly known as the ZZ plant, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its upright, cane-like stems and large, arrow-shaped leaves with distinctive light green veins and dark green spots. The plant is a member of the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like peace lilies. It is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

The leaves are typically glossy and can grow up to 30 centimeters in length, adding a lush, tropical appearance to any space.
