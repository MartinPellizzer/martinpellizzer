Doryopteris × duvalii

Doryopteris × duvalii, commonly known as the Duval's wood fern, is a hybrid fern native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky habitats. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce a dense network of roots that help anchor it in soil.

Doryopteris × duvalii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
