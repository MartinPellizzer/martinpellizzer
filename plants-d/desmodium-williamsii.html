Desmodium williamsii

Desmodium williamsii, commonly known as the Williams' ticktrefoil, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, and it is often found in disturbed soils and open woodlands.
