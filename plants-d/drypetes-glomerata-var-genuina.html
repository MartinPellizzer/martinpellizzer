Drypetes glomerata var. genuina

Drypetes glomerata var. genuina, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in dense clusters, making it visually striking. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape and have a smooth surface. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in moist forest understories.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
