Diapensia

Diapensia, commonly known as the rock chickweed, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arctic and alpine regions.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its small, fleshy leaves and clusters of white or pink flowers. Its botanical features make it an important component of tundra and mountain ecosystems.
