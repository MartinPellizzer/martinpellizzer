Discolobium junceum

Discolobium junceum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf discolobium, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its legume fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant typically grows in sandy soils and is often found in heathland and woodland habitats. It features small, narrow leaves and produces yellow or pale green flowers.
