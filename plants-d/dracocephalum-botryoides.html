Dracocephalum botryoides

Dracocephalum botryoides, commonly known as the woollyhead masterwort, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a basal rosette of gray-green, woolly leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces tall, branched flowering stems that bear small, clustered blue or purple flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or arid environments.

Dracocephalum botryoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
