Dendrochilum sphacelatum

Dendrochilum sphacelatum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, often branching stems and elongated, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically arranged in clusters. It thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrochilum sphacelatum is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
