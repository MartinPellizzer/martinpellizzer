Dobera dubia

Dobera dubia, commonly known as the ghost plant, is a member of the Malvaceae family.

It is a shrub that can grow up to 2 meters tall, featuring elliptical leaves with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds.
