Delphinium floribundum

Delphinium floribundum, commonly known as the garden delphinium, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, spiky flower clusters. The plant produces showy, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are deeply lobed and arranged in a basal rosette, while its stems are upright and often branched.

Delphinium floribundum thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
