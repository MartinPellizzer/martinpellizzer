Digitaria bulbosa

Digitaria bulbosa, commonly known as the bulbous grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia. The plant is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and produces small, spikelet-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and often have a bluish-green color, while the roots can form bulb-like structures.

Digitaria bulbosa is often found in disturbed soils and can be an invasive species in some environments.
