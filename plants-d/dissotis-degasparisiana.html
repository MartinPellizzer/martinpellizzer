Dissotis degasparisiana

Dissotis degasparisiana, commonly known as the golden star sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant is characterized by its upright, grass-like foliage and produces clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom during the warmer months. Dissotis degasparisiana typically grows in wetland or marshy environments, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden landscapes in suitable climates.
