Deverra korolkowii

Deverra korolkowii, commonly known as the Korolkowii devil's ivy, is a climbing plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. The plant produces berries that range in color from yellow to red, which are edible but not commonly consumed by humans.
