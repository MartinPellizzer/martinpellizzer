Debregeasia luteocarpa

Debregeasia luteocarpa, commonly known as the yellow-fruited debregeasia, is a shrub native to the highland regions of Ethiopia.

It belongs to the family Celastraceae and is characterized by its small, yellow-orange fruits that are highly prized for their medicinal properties. The plant features simple, alternate leaves with a rough texture and small, inconspicuous flowers. The bark and leaves are used in traditional medicine to treat various ailments, including inflammation and digestive issues.
