Darea myriophylla

Darea myriophylla, commonly known as the flame violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It features striking, showy flowers with vibrant red petals that resemble flames, often with a contrasting yellow center. The plant typically grows in rocky or well-drained soils, preferring cool and moist environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Darea myriophylla is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in shaded garden settings.
