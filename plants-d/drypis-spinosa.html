Drypis spinosa

Drypis spinosa, commonly known as the spiny drypis, is a succulent plant native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It features a short, thick stem with spiny leaves that help reduce water loss. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months. Its adaptations make it well-suited for drought conditions.

It is often used in xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and aesthetic appeal.
