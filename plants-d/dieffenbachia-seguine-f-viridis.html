Dieffenbachia seguine f. viridis

Dieffenbachia seguine f. viridis, commonly known as the Shiny Dieffenbachia, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its large, lance-shaped leaves that are predominantly green with a subtle variegation of lighter green. The plant is a member of the Araceae family, which includes many ornamental plants known for their striking foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Due to its toxic sap, it should be handled with care and kept away from pets and children.
