Delphinium denudatum var. yunnanense

Delphinium denudatum var. yunnanense, commonly known as the Yunnan larkspur, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of western China.

It features tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height, supporting large, showy blue to purple flowers. The plant has lance-shaped, basal leaves that are hairy and often have a reddish tint. Its flowers are arranged in racemes and are characterized by their spurred petals, typical of the Delphinium genus.

It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows at high elevations.
