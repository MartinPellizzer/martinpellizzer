Desmodium mairei

Desmodium mairei, commonly known as the Maire's burrbean, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces distinctive, hairy pods that contain several seeds, which are often dispersed by wind. Desmodium mairei is typically found in moist, tropical environments and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological and agricultural studies.
