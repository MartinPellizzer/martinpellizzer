Dioclea mollicoma

Dioclea mollicoma, commonly known as the velvet bean, is a climbing shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its hairy, velvety stems and leaves that are usually ovate to lanceolate in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by pods containing edible seeds. These seeds are rich in protein and are used as a food source in some regions.

However, the plant contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
