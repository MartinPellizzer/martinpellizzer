Diospyros olen

Diospyros olen, commonly known as the African persimmon, is a deciduous tree native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Ebenaceae family, which includes other notable species like ebony and persimmon. The tree produces small, orange to red fruits that are edible when fully mature, though they may have a bitter taste when unripe. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a rough texture.

The wood of Diospyros olen is dense and durable, making it valuable for crafting and traditional uses.
