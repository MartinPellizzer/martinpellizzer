Dianella intermedia f. bicolor

Dianella intermedia f. bicolor, commonly known as the variegated flax lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to New Zealand.

It is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its sword-shaped, variegated leaves that display a mix of green, white, and pink hues. Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping and container gardening.
