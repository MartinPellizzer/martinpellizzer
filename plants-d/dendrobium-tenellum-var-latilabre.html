Dendrobium tenellum var. latilabre

Dendrobium tenellum var. latilabre, commonly known as the broad-lipped dendrobium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. The plant produces long, slender pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in low-light conditions. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white, with a distinctive broad, lip-shaped petal.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique floral characteristics.
