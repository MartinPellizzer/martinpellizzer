Dactylostemon klotzschii var. obovatus

Dactylostemon klotzschii var. obovatus, commonly known as the broad-leafed dactylostemon, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It features lanceolate to obovate leaves that are often hairy and have a distinctive yellowish-green color. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically ranging from white to pale yellow. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it requires specific growing conditions to flourish.
