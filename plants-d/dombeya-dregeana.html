Dombeya dregeana

Dombeya dregeana, commonly known as the African dombeya, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Africa.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and a strong, aromatic scent. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Dombeya dregeana is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
