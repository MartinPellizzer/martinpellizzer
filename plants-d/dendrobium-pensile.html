Dendrobium pensile

Dendrobium pensile, commonly known as the hanging orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, pendant stems that hang from trees, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. It thrives in humid, tropical environments with bright, indirect light.

Dendrobium pensile is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
