Dianthus halisdemirii

Dianthus halisdemirii, commonly known as the Halisdemir's campion, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant pink or red petals. The plant typically grows in rocky or calcareous soils and thrives in sunny environments. It produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

Dianthus halisdemirii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
