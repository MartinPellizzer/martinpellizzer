Dendrorkis polydendris

Dendrorkis polydendris, commonly known as the many-branched orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, branching structure. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its unique morphology and ornamental value, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
