Delosperma angustifolium

Delosperma angustifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaf ice plant, is a low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are narrow and arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact size and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
