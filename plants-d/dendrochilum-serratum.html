Dendrochilum serratum

Dendrochilum serratum, commonly known as the serrated tree orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, woody stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers with yellow petals and a distinctive red lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
