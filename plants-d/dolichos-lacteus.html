Dolichos lacteus

Dolichos lacteus, commonly known as the milk bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are often clustered in racemes. The plant has pinnately compound leaves and produces elongated, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in warm climates and is often found in disturbed areas or along roadsides.

The seeds are used in traditional medicine and as a food source in some cultures.
