Desmopsis trunciflora

Desmopsis trunciflora, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, tubular orange-red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its nectar-rich flowers attract birds and insects, contributing to its ecological significance.
