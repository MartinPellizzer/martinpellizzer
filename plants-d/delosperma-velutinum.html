Delosperma velutinum

Delosperma velutinum, commonly known as the velvetleaf ice plant, is a succulent species native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves covered in a fine, velvety pubescence, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant clusters of star-shaped flowers, typically in shades of pink or red, during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
