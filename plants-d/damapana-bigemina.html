Damapana bigemina

Damapana bigemina, commonly known as the twin-leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its two leaves that emerge from the same node, giving it its scientific name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Damapana bigemina is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
