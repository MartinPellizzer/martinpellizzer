Desmoncus setosus

Desmoncus setosus, commonly known as the spiny bryophyte, is a unique member of the liverwort family.

It is characterized by its intricate, branched structure and the presence of sharp, spiny projections on its thallus. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest floors or on damp rocks. Its reproductive structures, known as gametangia, are embedded within the thallus and play a crucial role in its life cycle.

Desmoncus setosus is an important indicator species for assessing the health of humid ecosystems.
