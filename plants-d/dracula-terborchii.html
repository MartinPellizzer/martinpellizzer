Dracula terborchii

Dracula terborchii, commonly known as the Dragon's Blood Orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its vivid red petals and sepals, which resemble blood droplets, giving it its dramatic common name. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is notable for its unique floral structure and intricate patterns. Dracula terborchii thrives in humid, shaded environments at high altitudes, often growing on tree bark or rock surfaces.

Due to its rarity and beauty, it is highly sought after by botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
