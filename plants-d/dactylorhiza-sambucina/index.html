Dactylorhiza Sambucina

Dactylorhiza sambucina is a rare and beautiful orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia. It is known for its striking violet and white flowers that bloom in late summer. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and marshes. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Protecting its habitat is essential for preserving this delicate species.
