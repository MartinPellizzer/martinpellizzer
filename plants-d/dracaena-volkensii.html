Dracaena volkensii

Dracaena volkensii, commonly known as the flame plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, sword-shaped leaves that are bright green with red or orange margins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dracaena volkensii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant foliage and ease of care.
