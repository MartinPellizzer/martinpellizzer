Dolicholus ambacensis

Dolicholus ambacensis, commonly known as the Ambacensis orchid, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, spurred flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, while its flowers exhibit a range of colors, including white, yellow, and pink.

Dolicholus ambacensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
