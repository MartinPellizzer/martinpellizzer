Draba sonorae

Draba sonorae, commonly known as the Sonoran Draba, is a small flowering plant native to the Sonoran Desert.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many flowering plants with distinctive four-petaled flowers. Draba sonorae is known for its resilience and is often found in areas with sparse vegetation.
