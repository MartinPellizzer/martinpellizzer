Didymocheton rufescens subsp. rufescens

Didymocheton rufescens subsp.

rufescens, commonly known as the red-flowered pisonia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia. It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are opposite and have a leathery texture.

The plant produces clusters of small, white or pink flowers that are followed by bright red, fleshy fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
