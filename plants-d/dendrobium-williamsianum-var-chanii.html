Dendrobium williamsianum var. chanii

Dendrobium williamsianum var. chanii, commonly known as the Chan's Dendrobium, is a sympodial orchid native to the Philippines.

It features a compact growth habit with thick, fleshy pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers during the dry season. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded, humid environments.
