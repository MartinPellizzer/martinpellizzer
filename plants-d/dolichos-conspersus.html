Dolichos conspersus

Dolichos conspersus, commonly known as the hairy runner bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, hairy stems that allow it to twine around supports as it grows. The plant produces elongated, pod-like fruits that contain kidney-shaped seeds. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

Dolichos conspersus is valued for its edible beans and its use in traditional medicine.
