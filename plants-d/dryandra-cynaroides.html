Dryandra cynaroides

Dryandra cynaroides, commonly known as the Kangaroo Paw, is a striking plant native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its unique, curling red or orange flowers that resemble the foot of a kangaroo. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, cylindrical seed pods. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Dryandra cynaroides is also valued for its ecological role, providing nectar for native pollinators.
