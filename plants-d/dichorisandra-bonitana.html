Dichorisandra bonitana

Dichorisandra bonitana, commonly known as the African lily, is a striking herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive red or orange throat. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Dichorisandra bonitana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and is relatively easy to care for.
