Dactylorchis × ruthei

Dactylorchis × ruthei, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a fascinating plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between Dactylorchis caryophylla and Dactylorchis tibetica, exhibiting a mix of characteristics from both parent species. This orchid is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, where it thrives in cool, humid environments. Its flowers are typically small and delicate, often displaying a range of colors from white to pink and purple.

The plant is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
