Desmanthus plenus

Desmanthus plenus, commonly known as the full bloom desmanthus, is a flowering plant native to Central America and parts of the southern United States.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens. Its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil also makes it beneficial for improving soil fertility.

Desmanthus plenus is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic value and ecological benefits.
