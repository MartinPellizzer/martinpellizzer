Dysphania inflata

Dysphania inflata, commonly known as bladder campion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive inflated seed pods, which give the plant its common name. The plant has alternate, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, pale purple to white flowers that bloom in late summer.

Dysphania inflata is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
