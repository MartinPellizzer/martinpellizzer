Dianthus alpester

Dianthus alpester, commonly known as the alpine campion, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the Carnation family (Caryophyllaceae) and is characterized by its delicate, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. Dianthus alpester is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
