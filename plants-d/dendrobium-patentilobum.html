Dendrobium patentilobum

Dendrobium patentilobum, commonly known as the broad-lobed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with cane-like pseudobulbs that support its evergreen leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically during the summer months. Its inflorescence is racemose, bearing multiple blooms along a central stem.

Dendrobium patentilobum thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
