Drakonorchis barbarossa

Drakonorchis barbarossa, commonly known as the dragon orchid, is a rare and exotic orchid species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated flowers that resemble dragons, hence its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on tree trunks or rocks. Its botanical features include a sympodial growth habit and a complex root system adapted for nutrient absorption in poor soil.

Drakonorchis barbarossa is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and rarity in cultivation.
