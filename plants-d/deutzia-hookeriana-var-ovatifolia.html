Deutzia hookeriana var. ovatifolia

Deutzia hookeriana var. ovatifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaf deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The leaves are ovate, with a smooth texture and a glossy surface, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

This variety is valued for its showy blooms and adaptability to various garden settings.
