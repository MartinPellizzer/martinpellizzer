Dracaena newtoniana

Dracaena newtoniana, commonly known as the Newton's dracaena, is a species of flowering plant in the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, sword-shaped leaves.
