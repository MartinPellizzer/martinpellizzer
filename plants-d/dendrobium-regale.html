Dendrobium regale

Dendrobium regale, commonly known as the imperial orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, cane-like stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange, often in clusters along the stems. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium regale is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its beauty and fragrance.
