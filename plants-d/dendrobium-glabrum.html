Dendrobium glabrum

Dendrobium glabrum, commonly known as the naked leaf dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems that bear numerous oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with prominent yellow lips. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

Dendrobium glabrum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
