Dypsis viridis

Dypsis viridis, commonly known as the green lady palm, is a tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and feathery, fan-shaped leaves that give it a graceful appearance. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth. Its foliage is typically a vibrant green, and it can grow quite tall, making it a striking addition to indoor or outdoor spaces.

Dypsis viridis is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced plant enthusiasts.
