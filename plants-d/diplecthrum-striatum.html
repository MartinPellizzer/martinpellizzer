Diplecthrum striatum

Diplecthrum striatum, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in open grasslands and along riverbanks. The plant features striking, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a distinctive white midrib, adding to its ornamental appeal.

The species is valued for its ability to thrive in poor soils and its use in traditional medicine.
