Delphinium kinkiense

Delphinium kinkiense, commonly known as the Japanese larkspur, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant produces showy, blue to purple flowers arranged in racemes, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are palmately compound, with serrated edges, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

Delphinium kinkiense is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
