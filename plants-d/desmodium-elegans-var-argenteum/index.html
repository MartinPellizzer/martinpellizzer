Desmodium Elegans Var. Argenteum

Desmodium elegans var. argenteum is a strikingly beautiful legume native to southern Africa. Its silvery-white foliage and delicate pink flowers make it a standout in any garden or botanical collection. This plant is known for its unique leaf structure, with trifoliate leaves that shimmer in the sunlight. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a versatile choice for both ornamental and ecological purposes. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in mainstream horticulture, offering a hidden gem for plant enthusiasts.
