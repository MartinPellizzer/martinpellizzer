Desmodium hippocrepis

Desmodium hippocrepis, commonly known as the purple crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive purple or pink flowers that resemble a crown. The plant has trifoliate leaves and produces small, bean-like pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
