Dracaena cambodiana

Dracaena cambodiana, commonly known as the Cambodian dragon tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and rosette of narrow, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.

Dracaena cambodiana prefers well-drained soil and bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
