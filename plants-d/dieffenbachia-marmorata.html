Dieffenbachia marmorata

Dieffenbachia marmorata, commonly known as the Leopard Plant, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with white and green patterns. The leaves are large, broad, and often have a waxy texture. It belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like peace lilies.

This plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
