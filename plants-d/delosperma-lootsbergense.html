Delosperma lootsbergense

Delosperma lootsbergense, commonly known as the Lootsberg ice plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white powder, known as bloom. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, and purple, typically during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value, it is widely cultivated in arid and semi-arid regions.
