Dendrobium andreemillariae

Dendrobium andreemillariae, commonly known as the orchid of the Andes, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features tall, cane-like pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually showy, with a range of colors including white, yellow, and pink.

It requires bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
