Delphinium uralense

Delphinium uralense, commonly known as the Ural larkspur, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant produces striking blue to purple flowers in racemes, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Delphinium uralense is valued in horticulture for its vibrant colors and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
