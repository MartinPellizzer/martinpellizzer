Disporum leschenaultianum

Disporum leschenaultianum, commonly known as the white-flowered disporum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Pacific Northwest of the United States and parts of Canada.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. The plant features a rhizomatous root system and produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers in the spring. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a smooth, waxy surface.

Disporum leschenaultianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native plant gardens.
