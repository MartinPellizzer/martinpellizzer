Desmodium szechuenense

Desmodium szechuenense, commonly known as Chinese velvetbean, is a leguminous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces lens-shaped pods that contain several seeds, and it is often found in moist, shady environments. It has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.

Its growth habit and morphology make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
