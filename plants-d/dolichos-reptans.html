Dolichos reptans

Dolichos reptans, commonly known as the creeping bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, trailing stems that can grow up to several meters in length, often rooting at the nodes. The plant produces small, yellow flowers followed by elongated, bean-like pods containing edible seeds. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

Dolichos reptans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
