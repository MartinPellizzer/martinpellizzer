Dracaena nigricans

Dracaena nigricans, commonly known as the black dracaena, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Madagascar.

It features dark green, strap-like leaves that are narrow and pointed, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. Dracaena nigricans is valued for its air-purifying qualities and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and landscaping.
