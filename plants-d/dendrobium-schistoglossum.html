Dendrobium schistoglossum

Dendrobium schistoglossum, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with numerous, fleshy, and often branched pseudobulbs. The plant produces showy, golden-yellow flowers with a distinctive, elongated lip. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrobium schistoglossum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
