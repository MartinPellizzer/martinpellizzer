Deutzia subvelutina

Deutzia subvelutina, commonly known as velvetleaf deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features fragrant white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer, adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant has slender, arching branches covered with small, ovate leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for a variety of garden settings.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental hedges and landscape plantings.
