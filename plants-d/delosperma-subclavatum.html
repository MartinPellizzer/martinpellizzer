Delosperma subclavatum

Delosperma subclavatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are typically narrow and clustered at the base of the plant. The plant produces vibrant pink to purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ability to store water in its leaves helps it survive in arid environments.
