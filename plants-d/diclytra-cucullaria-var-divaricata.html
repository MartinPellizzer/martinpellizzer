Diclytra cucullaria var. divaricata

Diclytra cucullaria var. divaricata, commonly known as the variegated broad-leafed bug.

This plant is a variant of the broad-leafed bug, characterized by its distinct leaf morphology. It typically features broad, lance-shaped leaves with a waxy texture. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in temperate regions.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique leaf structure and ecological role in its habitat.
