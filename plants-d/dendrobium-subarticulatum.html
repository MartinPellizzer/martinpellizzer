Dendrobium subarticulatum

Dendrobium subarticulatum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with cylindrical pseudobulbs and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces inflorescences bearing small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
