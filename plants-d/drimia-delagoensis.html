Drimia delagoensis

Drimia delagoensis, commonly known as the Delagoense Dragon's tongue, is a bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its striking, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces tall, erect stems with clusters of white or pale purple flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.
