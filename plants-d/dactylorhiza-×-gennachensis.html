Dactylorhiza × gennachensis

Dactylorhiza × gennachensis, commonly known as the hybrid marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Europe and North Africa.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Dactylorhiza macdougallii and Dactylorhiza lapponica. This plant typically grows in moist, acidic soils in wetlands, bogs, and marshes. It produces tall, slender stems with a single or few flowers that are usually pink or purple in color.

The plant is known for its striking appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
