Dichaea laxa

Dichaea laxa, commonly known as the slender dichaea, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rainforests and cloud forests. Its leaves are elongated and often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs.

Dichaea laxa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation under the right conditions.
