Dryopteris maximowicziana

Dryopteris maximowicziana, commonly known as the Japanese shield fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to East Asia.

It features a robust rhizome system that supports its upright growth habit. The fronds are bipinnate, with a distinctive shield-shaped base, giving it its common name. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The plant is valued for its ornamental foliage and is cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
