Dorycnium suffruticosum f. brevifolium

Dorycnium suffruticosum f. brevifolium, commonly known as the short-leaved hedge cranesbill, is a variant of the hedge cranesbill species.

It is a low-growing, perennial plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. The plant features small, pink or purple flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are shorter and more compact compared to the typical form, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges or rocky habitats.
