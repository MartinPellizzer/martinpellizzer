Delarbrea montana var. arborea

Delarbrea montana var. arborea, commonly known as the tree form of the mountain delarbrea, is a variant of the Delarbrea montana species.

It is a woody perennial plant native to specific regions in South America, particularly in the Andean foothills. The plant features a distinct tree-like structure with a single trunk and a broad, spreading canopy. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a leathery texture, while its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences.

This variant is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological role in its native habitat.
