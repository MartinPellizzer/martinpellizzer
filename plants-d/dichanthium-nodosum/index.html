Dichanthium Nodosum

Dichanthium nodosum is a hardy grass species known for its resilience in arid and semi-arid environments. It thrives in poor soils and is commonly found in tropical and subtropical regions. This plant plays a vital role in preventing soil erosion and supporting local ecosystems. Its unique nodules, formed through symbiotic relationships with nitrogen-fixing bacteria, enhance soil fertility. Due to its adaptability, Dichanthium nodosum is also valued for forage and land rehabilitation purposes.
