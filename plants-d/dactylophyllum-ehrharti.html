Dactylophyllum ehrharti

Dactylophyllum ehrharti, commonly known as the Ehrhart's spurge, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its upright growth habit and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers clustered in cyathia, which are typical of the spurge family. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.
