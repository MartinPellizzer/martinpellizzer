Dennstaedtia hirsuta f. cristata

Dennstaedtia hirsuta f. cristata, commonly known as the cristate sword fern, is a variant of the hairy sword fern.

It is a fern species characterized by its distinctive, crisped or curled fronds that give it a unique appearance. Native to regions of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments. The fronds are typically lanceolate and covered in fine, hairy structures, contributing to its texture.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and indoor settings.
