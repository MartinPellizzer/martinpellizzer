Diplotaxis vogelii

Diplotaxis vogelii, commonly known as the African ironweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, often found in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features erect stems and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are usually narrow and linear, and it is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.

Diplotaxis vogelii is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
