Dicliptera mollis

Dicliptera mollis, commonly known as the silky leaf grass, is a perennial herb native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a silky texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in clusters, typically in the axils of the leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in open woodland or along stream banks.

Dicliptera mollis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping for its delicate appearance.
