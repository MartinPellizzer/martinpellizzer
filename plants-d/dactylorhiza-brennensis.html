Dactylorhiza brennensis

Dactylorhiza brennensis, commonly known as the burning orchid, is a species of orchid native to western Europe.

It is a terrestrial herb with a rosette of broad, dark green leaves and produces tall, slender flowering stems. The plant blooms with showy, pink to purple flowers that have a distinctive, almost fiery appearance. It prefers moist, calcareous soils and is often found in woodland or heathland habitats.

Dactylorhiza brennensis is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
