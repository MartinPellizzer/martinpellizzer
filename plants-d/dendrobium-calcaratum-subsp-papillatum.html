Dendrobium calcaratum subsp. papillatum

Dendrobium calcaratum subsp.

papillatum, commonly known as the Papillate Dendrobium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia. It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in humid tropical environments.

The plant features a compact growth habit with short, fleshy pseudobulbs and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. Its leaves are linear and often covered with a fine, papillate texture, giving it its subspecies name.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of growing conditions.
