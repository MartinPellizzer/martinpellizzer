Dolichos pungens

Dolichos pungens, commonly known as the hairy nightshade, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant produces small, white or pale purple flowers that bloom in clusters. Its hairy stems and leaves are notable features, contributing to its common name.

Dolichos pungens is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and ability to thrive in warm climates.
