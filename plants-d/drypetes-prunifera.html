Drypetes prunifera

Drypetes prunifera, commonly known as the roseapple tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, reddish-brown bark and opposite, simple leaves. It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its timber.
