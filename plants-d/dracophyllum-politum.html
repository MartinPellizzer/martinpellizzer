Dracophyllum politum

Dracophyllum politum, commonly known as the silver-leaved dracophyllum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage with a distinctive silvery-white underside. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white, which bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and montane regions.

Dracophyllum politum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
