Dombeya greveana

Dombeya greveana, commonly known as the African dombeya, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often covered in fine hairs.

Dombeya greveana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
