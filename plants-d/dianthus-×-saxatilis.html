Dianthus × saxatilis

Dianthus × saxatilis, commonly known as the rock campion, is a hybrid species belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, bilaterally symmetrical flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant is native to rocky and alpine environments in Europe and western Asia. It features linear, grass-like leaves and produces capsules as its fruiting structure.

Dianthus × saxatilis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
