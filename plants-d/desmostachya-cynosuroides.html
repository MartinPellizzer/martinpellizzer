Desmostachya cynosuroides

Desmostachya cynosuroides, commonly known as the Sudanese grass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa and parts of the Middle East.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its robust, tufted growth habit with narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces panicles of small, pale green to brownish flowers, which are typically wind-pollinated. It thrives in dry, well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and grassland habitats.

Due to its hardiness, it is sometimes used for forage in arid regions.
