Dendrochilum stella

Dendrochilum stella, commonly known as the star orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically blooms in summer, with flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Dendrochilum stella is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
