Dyckia josinoi-narcisae

Dyckia josinoi-narcisae, commonly known as the Narcissus Dyckia, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its rosette of stiff, sword-shaped leaves that are often adorned with a striking white or yellow stripe. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences that bear small, showy white flowers resembling those of the narcissus, hence its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Dyckia josinoi-narcisae is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
