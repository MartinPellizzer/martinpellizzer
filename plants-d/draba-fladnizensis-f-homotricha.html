Draba fladnizensis f. homotricha

Draba fladnizensis f. homotricha, commonly known as the fladnizensis dwarf cabbage, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit, with compact rosettes of basal leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a raceme, typical of the family. It is native to alpine regions of Central Asia and thrives in rocky, well-drained soils.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
