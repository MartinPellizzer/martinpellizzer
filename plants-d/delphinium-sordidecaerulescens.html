Delphinium sordidecaerulescens

Delphinium sordidecaerulescens, commonly known as the dark blue larkspur, is a species of flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, spindly stems that bear clusters of blue to violet flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows.

Due to its toxic nature, it should be handled with care.
