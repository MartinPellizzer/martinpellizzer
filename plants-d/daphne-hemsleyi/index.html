Daphne Hemsleyi

Daphne hemsleyi is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of central Mexico. Known for its fragrant, pinkish-white flowers and distinctive foliage, it captivates botanists and gardeners alike. This evergreen shrub thrives in well-drained soils and requires a cool, moist climate to flourish. Its unique beauty makes it a sought-after specimen in specialized horticulture. Due to habitat loss, it is also a species of conservation concern.
