Dianthus × borderei

Dianthus × borderei, commonly known as the border pink, is a hybrid perennial plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of pink, red, and white, often with a distinctive frilled edge. The plant typically grows to a height of 10–30 cm and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It prefers well-drained soil and is often used in rock gardens or as a border plant due to its compact size and long blooming period.

Dianthus × borderei is native to southern Europe and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
