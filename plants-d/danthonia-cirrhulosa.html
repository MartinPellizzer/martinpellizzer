Danthonia cirrhulosa

Danthonia cirrhulosa, commonly known as the silky hair grass, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Australia.

It is characterized by its fine, silky-textured leaves and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or grasslands. It is adapted to withstand drought and is resilient in arid environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
