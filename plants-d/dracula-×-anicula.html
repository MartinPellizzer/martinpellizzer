Dracula × anicula

Dracula × anicula, commonly known as the Dragon's Blood orchid, is a hybrid orchid that combines traits from its parent species, Dracula and anicula.

It is native to the cloud forests of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its striking, pendulous flowers that range in color from deep red to purple, resembling the blood of a dragon. Its unique morphology includes long, slender petals and a spurred lip, typical of the Orchidaceae family.

Due to its rarity and aesthetic appeal, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
