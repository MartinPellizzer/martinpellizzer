Dendrobium × speciokingianum

Dendrobium × speciokingianum, commonly known as the Specio Kingian Dendrobium, is a hybrid orchid that exhibits a mix of characteristics from its parent species.

It is a sympodial epiphytic orchid, typically growing on trees or rocks in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features long, cane-like stems that can reach several feet in length, with clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and yellow. Its flowers are often arranged in racemes and bloom during the warmer months, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Due to its hybrid nature, it requires specific care, including bright light, moderate watering, and well-draining potting media.
