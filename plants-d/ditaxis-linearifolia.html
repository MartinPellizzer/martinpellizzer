Ditaxis linearifolia

Ditaxis linearifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ditaxis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in arid or semi-arid regions. The plant features small, linear leaves that are arranged oppositely along the stems. Its flowers are usually small and white or pale yellow, appearing in clusters.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature.
