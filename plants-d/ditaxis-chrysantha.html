Ditaxis chrysantha

Ditaxis chrysantha, commonly known as the golden star bush, is a small evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and is often cultivated as a garden plant for its ornamental value. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. It produces clusters of small, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a vibrant touch to the landscape.

Due to its drought tolerance and adaptability, it is well-suited for coastal and sandy soils.
