Diplazium wilsonii

Diplazium wilsonii, commonly known as Wilson's shield fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Davalliaceae and is characterized by its upright, clump-forming growth habit. The fronds are typically bipinnate, with a dense arrangement of small, oval-shaped pinnae. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
