Dammara australis

Dammara australis, commonly known as the Australian dammara, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Australia.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils, often in areas with high salinity. The plant produces small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, providing year-round structure to the landscape.

Dammara australis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in coastal gardens and landscaping.
