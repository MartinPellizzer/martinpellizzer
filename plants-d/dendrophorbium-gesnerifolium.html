Dendrophorbium gesnerifolium

Dendrophorbium gesnerifolium, commonly known as the Gesneria orchid, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its showy, spurred flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a waxy surface, and it often grows in shaded, moist environments. It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.

Dendrophorbium gesnerifolium is also known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
