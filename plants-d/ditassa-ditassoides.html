Ditassa ditassoides

Ditassa ditassoides, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and showy, colorful flowers that bloom in various shades of violet, pink, and white. The plant is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is widely cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
