Dasispermum Capense

Dasispermum capense is a unique plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Fabaceae family, known for its leguminous characteristics. This shrub is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and compound leaves. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing remarkable adaptability. Despite its hardy nature, it remains a lesser-known species in the botanical world.
