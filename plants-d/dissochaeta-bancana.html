Dissochaeta bancana

Dissochaeta bancana, commonly known as the African hairpin, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered white or yellowish flowers. It is often found in grasslands and along roadsides, thriving in full sun.
