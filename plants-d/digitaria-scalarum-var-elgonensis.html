Digitaria scalarum var. elgonensis

Digitaria scalarum var. elgonensis, commonly known as Elgon grass, is a perennial grass species native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions. Elgon grass is known for its ability to thrive in high-altitude environments, often reaching elevations above 2,000 meters.

It plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
