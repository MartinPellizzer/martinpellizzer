Desmodium ochroleucum

Desmodium ochroleucum, commonly known as the yellow cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which are often yellowish-green in color, and its small, yellow to orange flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several elliptical seeds. It is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it a valuable plant in agricultural systems.

Desmodium ochroleucum is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
