Dianthus chinensis var. jingpoensis

Dianthus chinensis var. jingpoensis, commonly known as the Jingpo carnation, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and has fleshy, often hairy leaves. It produces numerous stamens and a single pistil, typical of the genus Dianthus.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
