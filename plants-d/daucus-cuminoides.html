Daucus cuminoides

Daucus cuminoides, commonly known as wild carrot or Queen Anne's lace, is a biennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to theApiaceae family, which includes other familiar plants like parsley and dill. Daucus cuminoides is also known for its role in supporting pollinators and its historical use in traditional medicine.
