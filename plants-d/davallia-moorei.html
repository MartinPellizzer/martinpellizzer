Davallia moorei

Davallia moorei, commonly known as the silver lace fern, is a tropical evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, lacy fronds that are divided into narrow, arching pinnae, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant thrives in shaded, humid environments and has a rhizomatous growth habit. Its leaves are usually light green with a silvery underside, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Davallia moorei is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its aesthetic value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
