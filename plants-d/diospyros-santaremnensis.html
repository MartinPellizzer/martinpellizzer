Diospyros santaremnensis

Diospyros santaremnensis, commonly known as the Canary Island persimmon, is a small evergreen tree native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in catkin-like inflorescences. Its fruits are small, fleshy berries that turn from green to yellow or orange when mature.

The tree is adapted to the arid and rocky environments of its native habitat.
