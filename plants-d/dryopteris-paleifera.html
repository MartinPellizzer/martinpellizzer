Dryopteris paleifera

Dryopteris paleifera, commonly known as the pale fern, is a species of fern native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, lacy fronds that are typically light green in color. The plant thrives in moist, shady environments, often found in forest understories. Its reproductive structures, called sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Dryopteris paleifera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
