Dendrobium desmotrichos

Dendrobium desmotrichos, commonly known as the waxy leech orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its thick, leathery leaves and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the air, making it well-suited for tropical climates.

Dendrobium desmotrichos is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
