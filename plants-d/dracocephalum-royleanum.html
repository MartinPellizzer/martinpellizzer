Dracocephalum royleanum

Dracocephalum royleanum, commonly known as Kashmir saffron, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically pale blue to violet, with prominent violet-purple lips, and are clustered in dense spikes.

Dracocephalum royleanum is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
