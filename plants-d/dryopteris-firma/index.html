Dryopteris Firma

Dryopteris firma is a hardy evergreen fern known for its dense, leathery fronds that form a compact, bushy appearance. Native to eastern Asia, this plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest floors and rocky crevices. Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and woodland landscapes. The plant is valued for its resilience and low maintenance requirements. With its elegant, arching fronds, Dryopteris firma adds a touch of natural beauty to any garden setting.
