Diospyros nigricans

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its dark, dense wood that is highly valued for its durability and fine grain. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly serrated margin.

Diospyros nigricans is also notable for its ecological importance, providing food and shelter for various wildlife species.
