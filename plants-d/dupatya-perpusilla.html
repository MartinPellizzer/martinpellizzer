Dupatya perpusilla

Dupatya perpusilla, commonly known as the small-flowered piper, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various species of pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are typically ovate with a slightly hairy surface, and it prefers moist, shaded environments.

Dupatya perpusilla is often found in tropical rainforests and is of interest to botanists due to its unique floral structure.
