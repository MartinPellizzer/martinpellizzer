Dioscorea megalantha var. lilloi

Dioscorea megalantha var. lilloi, commonly known as the Lillo's yam, is a tropical vine native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Dioscoreaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers followed by cylindrical, fleshy fruits. Its tuberous roots are valued for their medicinal properties and are used in traditional medicine.

The species is also cultivated for its ornamental value and potential economic importance.
