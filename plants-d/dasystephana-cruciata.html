Dasystephana cruciata

Dasystephana cruciata, commonly known as the crucifix starling or crucifix star.

It is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped flowers, which are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.
