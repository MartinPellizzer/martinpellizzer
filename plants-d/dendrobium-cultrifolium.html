Dendrobium cultrifolium

Dendrobium cultrifolium, commonly known as the sword-leaf orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its sword-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in humid climates.

Dendrobium cultrifolium is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and unique foliage.
