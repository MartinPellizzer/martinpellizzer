Dioscorea insignis

Dioscorea insignis, commonly known as the yellow arrowhead vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by elongated, tuberous fruits. Its tubers are often used in traditional medicine and are also a source of starch.

Dioscorea insignis thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
