Dolichos swynnertonii

Dolichos swynnertonii, commonly known as the Swynnerton's horsegram, is a climbing legume native to southern Africa.

It features trifoliate leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by elongated, pod-like fruits. The plant is characterized by its robust growth habit and ability to thrive in dry, arid environments. It is often found in open woodlands and along rocky outcrops, where it contributes to soil stabilization.

The seeds of Dolichos swynnertonii are used in traditional cuisines and have nutritional value.
