Davallia brevipes

Davallia brevipes, commonly known as the shield fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, clumping growth habit and its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as under forest canopies. Its fronds are leathery and have a unique, segmented appearance.

Davallia brevipes is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
