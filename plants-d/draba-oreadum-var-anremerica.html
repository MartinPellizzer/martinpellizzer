Draba oreadum var. anremerica

Draba oreadum var. anremerica, commonly known as the American rockcress, is a small flowering plant native to North America.

It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in alpine or arctic environments. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white or pale yellow flowers during the spring or early summer. Its leaves are basal, rosette-like, and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

It is a hardy species that thrives in cold climates and is often found in mountainous regions.
