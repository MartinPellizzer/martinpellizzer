Dendrobium strictum

Dendrobium strictum, commonly known as the rigid orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Dendrobium strictum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
