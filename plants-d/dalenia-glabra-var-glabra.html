Dalenia glabra var. glabra

Dalenia glabra var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth dalenia, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in full sun. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are smooth and often have a waxy texture. Its flowers are small and clustered in umbels, usually in shades of yellow or green.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
