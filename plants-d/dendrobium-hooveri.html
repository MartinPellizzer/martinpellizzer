Dendrobium hooveri

Dendrobium hooveri, commonly known as the Hoover's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Dendrobium genus, which includes over 1,200 species of epiphytic and terrestrial orchids. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its inflorescence produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow and orange.

Dendrobium hooveri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
