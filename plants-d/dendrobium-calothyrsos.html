Dendrobium calothyrsos

Dendrobium calothyrsos, commonly known as the golden spike orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with numerous cylindrical pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often arranged in racemes. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, adapting to its climbing lifestyle.

Dendrobium calothyrsos thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media for optimal growth.
