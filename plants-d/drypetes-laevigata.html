Drypetes laevigata

Drypetes laevigata, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed drypetes, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and elliptical leaves with a glossy upper surface. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in axillary inflorescences. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release small, smooth seeds.

Drypetes laevigata is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its timber and traditional medicinal uses.
