Delphinium halteratum var. rifanum

Delphinium halteratum var. rifanum, commonly known as the Rif delphinium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of North Africa.

It features tall, erect stems that can grow up to 2 meters in height, adorned with clusters of blue to purple flowers. The leaves are lance-shaped and serrated, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for alpine gardens.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
