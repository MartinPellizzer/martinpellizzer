Deverra triradiata subsp. intermedia

Deverra triradiata subsp.

intermedia, commonly known as the yellow bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its yellow, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant has a woody stem and lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant color and unique floral structure.
