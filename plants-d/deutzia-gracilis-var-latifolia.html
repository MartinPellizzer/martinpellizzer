Deutzia gracilis var. latifolia

Deutzia gracilis var. latifolia, commonly known as broadleaf deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features slender, arching stems and clusters of fragrant, white to pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The leaves are ovate to elliptic, with a broad, smooth texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, moist soil.

This variety is valued for its delicate appearance and suitability in garden landscapes.
