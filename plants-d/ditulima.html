Ditulima

Ditulima, commonly known as the "sea holly," is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple ray florets and a central disk of yellow flowers, which give it a distinctive appearance. Ditulima is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
