Diospyros gillisonii

Diospyros gillisonii, commonly known as the Gillison's persimmon, is a species of flowering plant in the Ebenaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly the Democratic Republic of the Congo. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, clustered flowers. Its fruit is a small, fleshy berry that ripens to a deep orange or red color.

The tree is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional uses in local communities.
