Doronicum haussknechtii

Doronicum haussknechtii, commonly known as the Haussknecht's leopard's bane, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Alps.

It features showy yellow flowers with a distinctive red eye, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are densely covered with fine hairs, giving it a soft texture. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in alpine meadows.

Doronicum haussknechtii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
