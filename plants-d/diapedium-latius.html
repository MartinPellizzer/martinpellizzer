Diapedium latius

Diapedium latius, commonly known as the broad-leaved diapedium, is a member of the family Diapedeaceae.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions, often found in moist, shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for study in both horticulture and ecological research.
