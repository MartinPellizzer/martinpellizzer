Dunbaria trichodon

Dunbaria trichodon, commonly known as the hairy-dent sedge, is a perennial plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, strap-shaped leaves and small, clustered flowers. Dunbaria trichodon plays a role in stabilizing wetland soils and providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
