Diplocaulobium lageniforme

Diplocaulobium lageniforme, commonly known as the bottle-shaped liverwort, is a species of liverwort belonging to the family Ptilidiaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, bottle-shaped thallus that is often covered in a layer of fine, translucent cells. Its morphology and habitat make it an important indicator species for certain ecological conditions.
