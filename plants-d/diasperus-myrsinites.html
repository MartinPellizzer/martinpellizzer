Diasperus myrsinites

Diasperus myrsinites, commonly known as the African firethorn, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny branches and clusters of small, orange-yellow flowers. The plant produces bright red berries that are edible for birds but toxic to humans. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a hardy ornamental plant.

Its unique appearance and ability to attract wildlife make it a popular choice in gardens.
