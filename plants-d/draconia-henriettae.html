Draconia henriettae

Draconia henriettae, commonly known as the Henrietta's Dragonroot, is a rare flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its unique, spiral-like arrangement of leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
