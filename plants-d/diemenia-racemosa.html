Diemenia racemosa

Diemenia racemosa, commonly known as the Tasmanian devil orchid, is a flowering plant native to Tasmania.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink. The plant has a terrestrial growth habit, with a slender rhizome and linear leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species.
