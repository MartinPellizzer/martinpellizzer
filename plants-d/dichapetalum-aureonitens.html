Dichapetalum aureonitens

Dichapetalum aureonitens, commonly known as the golden-leafed dichapetalum, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that are typically bright yellow or green, depending on the season. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique appearance, it is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes in botanical gardens.
