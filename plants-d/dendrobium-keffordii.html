Dendrobium keffordii

Dendrobium keffordii, commonly known as the Kefford's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its tall, cane-like stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are long, narrow, and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Dendrobium keffordii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
