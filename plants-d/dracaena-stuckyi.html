Dracaena stuckyi

Dracaena stuckyi, commonly known as the Stucky's dracaena, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy, blue-gray leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, greenish-white flowers, though it is rarely seen in cultivation. Its leaves are adapted to store water, making it drought-tolerant and suitable for arid environments.

Dracaena stuckyi is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of care in indoor settings.
