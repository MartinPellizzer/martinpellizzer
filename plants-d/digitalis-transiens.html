Digitalis transiens

Digitalis transiens, commonly known as the fairy wand, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features striking, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a distinctive, branched stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Digitalis transiens is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and historical use in traditional medicine.
