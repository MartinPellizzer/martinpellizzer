Dieffenbachia crebripistillata

Dieffenbachia crebripistillata, commonly known as the Shiny Leaf Dieffenbachia, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves with prominent, light green veins. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is known for its striking foliage rather than its flowers. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Dieffenbachia crebripistillata is a popular indoor plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
