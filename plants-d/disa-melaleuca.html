Disa melaleuca

Disa melaleuca, commonly known as the yellow disa, is a species of orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking yellow flowers with red markings. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in open woodland or grassland habitats. Its flowers bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and wasps.

The genus Disa is known for its diverse floral forms, and Disa melaleuca is a notable example within this group.
