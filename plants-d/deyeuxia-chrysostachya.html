Deyeuxia chrysostachya

Deyeuxia chrysostachya, commonly known as golden beardgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and produces distinctive golden-yellow inflorescences. The plant typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Deyeuxia chrysostachya is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual interest.
