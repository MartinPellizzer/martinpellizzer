Dontostemon elegans

Dontostemon elegans, commonly known as the elegant starflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its showy, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white and purple. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and has hairy stems and leaves. Its foliage is often covered in fine, soft hairs, contributing to its delicate appearance.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and ability to thrive in woodland gardens.
