Dianthus × jaczonis

Dianthus × jaczonis, commonly known as the carnation or garden carnation, is a hybrid perennial plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that come in a wide range of colors, including red, pink, white, and purple. The plant typically grows to a height of 15–30 cm, with linear, grass-like leaves and sturdy stems. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Dianthus × jaczonis is known for its long blooming period, often from late spring through early autumn.
