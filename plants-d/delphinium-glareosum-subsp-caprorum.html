Delphinium glareosum subsp. caprorum

Delphinium glareosum subsp.

caprorum, commonly known as the Sierra delphinium, is a flowering plant native to the western United States. It is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems and showy blue to purple flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a rough, hairy texture.
