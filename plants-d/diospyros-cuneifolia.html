Diospyros cuneifolia

Diospyros cuneifolia, commonly known as the African persimmon, is a small evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are typically elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, followed by orange to red berries that are edible when ripe.
