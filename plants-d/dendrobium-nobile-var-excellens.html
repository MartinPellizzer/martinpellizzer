Dendrobium nobile var. excellens

Dendrobium nobile var. excellens, commonly known as the orchid with exceptional beauty, is a hybrid orchid originating from Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant that thrives on trees and rocks, absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air. The plant features long, cane-like stems that produce clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and yellow. It is prized for its resilience and ability to bloom multiple times a year under proper care.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for both ornamental gardens and horticultural collections.
