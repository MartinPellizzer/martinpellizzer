Dracaena casanovae

Dracaena casanovae, commonly known as the Casanovae Dracaena, is a species of flowering plant native to the island of Socotra.

It is characterized by its striking, sword-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a reddish tinge along the edges. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that appear in clusters, followed by red or orange berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant.
