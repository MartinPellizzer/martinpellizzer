Dieffenbachia williamsii

Dieffenbachia williamsii, commonly known as the Leopard Plant, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that feature a mix of green, white, and yellow patterns. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like peace lilies and calla lilies. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and can grow quite large, often reaching lengths of up to 30 inches.

Dieffenbachia williamsii thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity to flourish.
