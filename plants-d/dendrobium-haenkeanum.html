Dendrobium haenkeanum

Dendrobium haenkeanum, commonly known as the golden spike orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, cane-like stems that are often branched and covered with dense clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant thrives in humid, tropical environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth. Its flowers are typically short-lived but can be quite showy, adding ornamental value to gardens or collections.

Dendrobium haenkeanum is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
