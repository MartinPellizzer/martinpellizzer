Dendrobium caudatum

Dendrobium caudatum, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a type of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a tall, cane-like stem with long, narrow leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, spider-shaped flowers. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. It requires well-draining potting media and regular watering to prevent root rot.

Dendrobium caudatum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
