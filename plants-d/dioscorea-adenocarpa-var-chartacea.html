Dioscorea adenocarpa var. chartacea

Dioscorea adenocarpa var. chartacea, commonly known as the African yam bean, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellowish flowers followed by fleshy, edible tubers that are rich in carbohydrates. Its tubers are often used as a food source and have been traditionally cultivated by local communities.

The species is also valued for its potential in agricultural and medicinal applications.
