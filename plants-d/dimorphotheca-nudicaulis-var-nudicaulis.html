Dimorphotheca nudicaulis var. nudicaulis

Dimorphotheca nudicaulis var. nudicaulis, commonly known as the narrowleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, dandelion-like yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, with a distinctive basal rosette arrangement. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in grassland and rocky habitats.

This plant is also noted for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
