Dendrobium latilabre

Dendrobium latilabre, commonly known as the broad-lipped orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with branching stems that can reach up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink, often with a distinctive lip. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium latilabre is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
