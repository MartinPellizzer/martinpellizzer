Dimorphanthes linifolia

Dimorphanthes linifolia, commonly known as the silky leaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, silvery foliage and daisy-like white flowers with yellow centers. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. It is often found in grasslands and along rocky outcrops.

Dimorphanthes linifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
