Dendropanax macropodus

Dendropanax macropodus, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its striking appearance, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
