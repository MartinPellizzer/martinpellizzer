Desmodium podocarpum subsp. szechuenense

Desmodium podocarpum subsp.

szechuenense, commonly known as the Sichuan desmodium, is a flowering plant native to China. It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers.

The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, typical of the legume family. It is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and mountainous regions.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and potential medicinal use.
