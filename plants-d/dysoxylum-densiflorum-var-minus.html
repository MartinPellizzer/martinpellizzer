Dysoxylum densiflorum var. minus

Dysoxylum densiflorum var. minus, commonly known as the small-flowered dysoxylum, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, clustered white flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has smooth, often aromatic bark and produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and traditional medicine.
