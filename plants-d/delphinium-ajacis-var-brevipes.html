Delphinium ajacis var. brevipes

Delphinium ajacis var. brevipes, commonly known as the dwarf larkspur, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features tall, slender stems that bear clusters of blue to purple flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are usually arranged alternately along the stem. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or alpine settings.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is often cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
