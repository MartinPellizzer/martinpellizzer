Dianthus moesiacus subsp. moesiacus

Dianthus moesiacus subsp.

moesiacus, commonly known as the Balkan carnation, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan Peninsula. It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red.

The plant features upright stems and lance-shaped leaves, with flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
