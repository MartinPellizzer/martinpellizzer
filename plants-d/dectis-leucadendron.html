Dectis leucadendron

Dectis leucadendron, commonly known as the silver tree, is a unique plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage, which results from a dense covering of fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in winter, adding a subtle floral display. It is a hardy shrub or small tree that thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
