Dendrobium dolichophyllum

Dendrobium dolichophyllum, commonly known as the long-leaf dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a tall, slender stem with numerous clustered, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces fragrant, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its unique appearance and flowering habits, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
