Dendranthema naktongense

Dendranthema naktongense, commonly known as the Naktong dandelion, is a flowering plant native to Korea.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas and has a taproot system. Its leaves are lance-shaped with serrated edges, and it produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
