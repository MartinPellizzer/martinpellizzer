Dendrobium womersleyi var. womersleyi

Dendrobium womersleyi var. womersleyi, commonly known as the Womersley's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the wet tropical rainforests of Australia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome that produces a cluster of leaves and flower spikes. The plant typically produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, which bloom during the wet season. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its natural habitat.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
