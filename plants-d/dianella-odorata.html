Dianella odorata

Dianella odorata, commonly known as the fragrant flax lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Australia.

It features grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color and emit a mild, pleasant fragrance. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in summer, followed by blue-black berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or wetland areas.

Dianella odorata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, supporting local wildlife.
