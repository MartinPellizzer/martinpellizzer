Digitaria sanguinalis f. chlorantha

Digitaria sanguinalis f. chlorantha, commonly known as the green false brome, is a variant of the common brome grass.

It is a perennial grass species characterized by its dark green, narrow leaves and tufted growth habit. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields. It has a fibrous root system and produces small, spike-like inflorescences.

Its botanical features make it adaptable to various soil types and environmental conditions.
