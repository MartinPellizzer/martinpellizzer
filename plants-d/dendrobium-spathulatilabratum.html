Dendrobium spathulatilabratum

Dendrobium spathulatilabratum, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic orchid that grows on trees and rocks, thriving in humid tropical climates. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white and yellow. It belongs to the Dendrobium genus, which includes over 1,200 species of orchids.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation.
