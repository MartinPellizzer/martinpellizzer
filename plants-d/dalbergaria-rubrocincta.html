Dalbergaria rubrocincta

Dalbergaria rubrocincta, commonly known as the red-belted burr legume, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes, followed by woody, pod-like fruits. Its leaves and stems are often covered in fine hairs, and it has a deep root system that helps it survive in dry conditions.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
