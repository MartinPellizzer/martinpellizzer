Digitaria polevansii

Digitaria polevansii, commonly known as the African grass or polevansii grass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

Digitaria polevansii is often used in landscaping and can also serve as forage for livestock.
