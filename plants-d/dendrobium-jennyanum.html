Dendrobium jennyanum

Dendrobium jennyanum, commonly known as the Jennyan orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, branched stems that often bear clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces showy, pale yellow to greenish-white blooms during the warmer months. Its leaves are usually long and narrow, adapted for photosynthesis in its natural habitat.

Dendrobium jennyanum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
