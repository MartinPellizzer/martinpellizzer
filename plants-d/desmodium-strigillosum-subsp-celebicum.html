Desmodium strigillosum subsp. celebicum

Desmodium strigillosum subsp.

celebicum, commonly known as the Celebes velvet bean, is a leguminous plant native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters.

The plant produces elongated pods containing small, dark brown seeds. It is often found in moist, tropical environments and is used in traditional medicine.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological and agricultural contexts.
