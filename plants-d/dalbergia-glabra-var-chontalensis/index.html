Dalbergia Glabra Var. Chontalensis

Chontal rosewood, scientifically known as Dalbergia glabra var. chontalensis, is a rare and highly valued tropical hardwood species native to Southeast Asia. This tree is prized for its rich, dark color and exceptional durability, making it a sought-after material in the furniture and musical instrument industries. Despite its beauty and utility, Chontal rosewood faces significant threats due to overexploitation and habitat loss. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival, as it plays an important role in both ecological and cultural contexts. Protecting this species is essential for preserving biodiversity and traditional craftsmanship in the region.
