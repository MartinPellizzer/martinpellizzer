Dianthus strictus var. axilliflorus

Dianthus strictus var. axilliflorus, commonly known as the upright carnation, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and dense clusters of pink to red flowers. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces flowers in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
