Delima intermedia

Delima intermedia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed lantana, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring woody stems and opposite leaves. The leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slight pubescence on the underside. It produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming throughout the year in suitable conditions.

The plant is adapted to dry environments and is often found in coastal regions and sandy soils.
