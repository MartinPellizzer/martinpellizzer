Dendrobium melaleucaphilum

Dendrobium melaleucaphilum, commonly known as the Melaleuca orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in heathland and woodland areas, often on the edges of rainforests. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces showy blooms during the summer months.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
