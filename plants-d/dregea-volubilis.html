Dregea volubilis

Dregea volubilis, commonly known as the climbing dregea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial vine characterized by its woody stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its fruit is a distinctive, spiny capsule that contains several seeds.

Dregea volubilis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant.
