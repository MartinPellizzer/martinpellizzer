Dicksonia erosa

Dicksonia erosa, commonly known as the slender tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feathery, compound fronds. The plant grows in moist, shaded environments, typically in rainforests or along stream banks. Its fronds are dimorphic, with fertile lower fronds producing spores on the underside.

The plant plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
