Dyckia duarteana

Dyckia duarteana, commonly known as the Duarte's dyckia, is a perennial grass-like plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming structure with long, narrow leaves. The plant produces striking inflorescences that are often brightly colored, typically in shades of red or orange. Dyckia duarteana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
