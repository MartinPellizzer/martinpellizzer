Dubautia knudsenii subsp. nagatae

Dubautia knudsenii subsp.

nagatae, commonly known as the Kauai ironwood, is a rare shrub native to the island of Kauai in Hawaii. It is characterized by its woody stems, small evergreen leaves, and clusters of pinkish-white flowers.

The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in coastal and montane forests. It plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for native wildlife.

Due to habitat loss and invasive species, it is considered endangered and is the subject of conservation efforts.
