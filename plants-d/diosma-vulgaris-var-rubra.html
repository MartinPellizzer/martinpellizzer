Diosma vulgaris var. rubra

Diosma vulgaris var. rubra, commonly known as the red diosma, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades, and is characterized by its striking red flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its flowers are tubular and attract pollinators such as bees and hummingbirds.
