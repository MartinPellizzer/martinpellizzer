Dendrochilum acuminatum

Dendrochilum acuminatum, commonly known as the acuminated tree orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded environments within forest understories. The plant features a slender, often branched stem with a fibrous root system. Its leaves are elongated and strap-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

The flowers are small, clustered in inflorescences, and typically have a delicate, fragrant appearance.
