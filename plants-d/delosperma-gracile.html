Delosperma gracile

Delosperma gracile, commonly known as the narrowleaf ice plant, is a low-growing, succulent perennial native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems and small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant thrives in well-drained sandy or gravelly soils and is drought-tolerant, making it ideal for xeriscaping. Its leaves are typically narrow and succulent, adapted to store water in arid environments.

Delosperma gracile is often used in rock gardens and as a ground cover due to its hardy nature and ornamental appeal.
