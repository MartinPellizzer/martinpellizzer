Dianthus aristatus

Dianthus aristatus, commonly known as the upright carnation, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant produces dense clusters of small, rounded petals that are often adorned with a distinctive white or pink border. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Dianthus aristatus is also valued for its long blooming period, which extends from spring through early summer.
