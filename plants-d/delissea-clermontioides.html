Delissea clermontioides

Delissea clermontioides, commonly known as the coral tree, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its distinctive coral-like inflorescences that bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has compound leaves with a feathery appearance and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Delissea clermontioides is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as birds and insects.
