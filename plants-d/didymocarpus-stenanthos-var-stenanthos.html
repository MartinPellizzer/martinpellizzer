Didymocarpus stenanthos var. stenanthos

Didymocarpus stenanthos var. stenanthos, commonly known as the narrow-leaved mountain ebony, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub characterized by its dark, glossy leaves and small, black berries. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, shaded environments. Its botanical features include opposite leaf arrangement and small, clustered flowers.

The fruit is an important food source for various bird species in its native habitat.
