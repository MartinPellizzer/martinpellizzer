Dillenia fischeri

Dillenia fischeri, commonly known as the Fischer's dillenia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Dilleniaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its large, palmate leaves and showy, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruits are woody, elongated capsules that contain several seeds.

Dillenia fischeri is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance.
