Dieffenbachia picta subvar. picturata

Dieffenbachia picta subvar. picturata, commonly known as the painted dieffenbachia, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with shades of white, green, and yellow. The leaves are large, heart-shaped, and often have a waxy texture. It produces clusters of small, white flowers that appear at the base of the plant.

This plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture to maintain its vibrant appearance.
