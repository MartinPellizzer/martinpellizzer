Dactylis polygama f. macrocephala

Dactylis polygama f. macrocephala, commonly known as the large-headed goat's rue, is a hemicryptophyte belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and trifoliate leaves with rounded lobes. The plant produces showy, violet-blue flowers in dense, spherical inflorescences, which are a distinctive feature of this form. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in supporting pollinators.
