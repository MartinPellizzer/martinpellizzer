Deutzia watereri

Deutzia watereri, commonly known as the water deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, arching stems and small, ovate leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Deutzia watereri is often used in ornamental gardening for its elegant appearance and seasonal interest.
