Dyckia chrysantha

Dyckia chrysantha, commonly known as the golden dyckia, is a succulent plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage that forms a rosette shape. The plant produces striking orange to yellow inflorescences that emerge from the center of the rosette. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight to flourish.

Dyckia chrysantha is popular as a decorative plant due to its vibrant colors and low maintenance requirements.
