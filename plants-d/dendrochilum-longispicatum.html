Dendrochilum longispicatum

Dendrochilum longispicatum, commonly known as the long-spined tree orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, branched stems and large, showy orchid flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually pink to purple in color, with long, slender sepals and petals.

Dendrochilum longispicatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
