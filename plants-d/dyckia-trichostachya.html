Dyckia trichostachya

Dyckia trichostachya, commonly known as the velvet dyckia, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its dense, rosette-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety texture, giving it its common name. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences that bear small, colorful flowers, typically in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Dyckia trichostachya is also valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
