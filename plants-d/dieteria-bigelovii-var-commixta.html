Dieteria bigelovii var. commixta

Dieteria bigelovii var. commixta, commonly known as the hybrid desert milkweed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and clusters of small, pale yellow to greenish flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in sandy or rocky soils.

Dieteria bigelovii var. commixta plays a role in supporting local pollinators and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.
