Dipladenia polymorpha var. peduncularis

Dipladenia polymorpha var. peduncularis, commonly known as the trumpet vine, is a vigorous climbing plant native to South America.

It features showy, tubular flowers that are typically orange or red, attracting hummingbirds and other pollinators. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves and produces long, trailing vines that can reach several meters in length. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for trellises and garden borders.

However, its invasive potential requires careful management in non-native environments.
