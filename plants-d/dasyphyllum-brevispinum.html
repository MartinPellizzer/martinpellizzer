Dasyphyllum brevispinum

Dasyphyllum brevispinum, commonly known as the short-spined dasyphyllum, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that store water, allowing it to survive in dry conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange, typically during the cooler months. Its leaves are small and spiny, providing protection from herbivores.

Dasyphyllum brevispinum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
