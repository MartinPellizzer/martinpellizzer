Dyckia duckei

Dyckia duckei, commonly known as the Duck's Dyckia, is a species of flowering plant in the bromeliad family.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces striking inflorescences with showy, purple or red flowers that bloom in the summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Dyckia duckei is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
