Dendrobium heterocarpum var. henshalii

Dendrobium heterocarpum var. henshalii, commonly known as the Henshali's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with cane-like stems that often bear multiple leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically blooming during the dry season. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the air, making it well-suited for humid, tropical environments.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
