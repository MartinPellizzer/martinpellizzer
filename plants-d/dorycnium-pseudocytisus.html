Dorycnium pseudocytisus

Dorycnium pseudocytisus, commonly known as the lesser broom, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant has a creeping growth habit and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

Due to its hardy nature, it can sometimes become invasive in certain regions.
