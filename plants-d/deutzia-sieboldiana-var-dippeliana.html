Deutzia sieboldiana var. dippeliana

Deutzia sieboldiana var. dippeliana, commonly known as the Dippel's deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features clusters of fragrant, white flowers that bloom in early summer, adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant has slender, arching stems and lance-shaped leaves that turn reddish in the fall. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, moist soil.

This variety is valued for its compact growth and elegant flowering display.
