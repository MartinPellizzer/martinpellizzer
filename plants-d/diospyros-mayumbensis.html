Diospyros mayumbensis

Diospyros mayumbensis, commonly known as Mayumbensis ebony, is a small to medium-sized tree native to central Africa.

It belongs to the Ebenaceae family, which includes other ebony species. The plant is characterized by its dark, dense wood, which is highly valued for its strength and durability. It has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and often have a glossy appearance.

The fruit is a small, smooth drupe that turns from green to yellow when mature.
