Diphyes tortuosa

Diphyes tortuosa, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its distinctive, winding stems that form a zigzag pattern as it grows. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a smooth texture with a slightly waxy surface.

The species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
