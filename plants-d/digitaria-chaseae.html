Digitaria chaseae

Digitaria chaseae, commonly known as the Chase's digitaria, is a grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and fine, blue-green foliage. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are typically green to purplish in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks.

Digitaria chaseae is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
