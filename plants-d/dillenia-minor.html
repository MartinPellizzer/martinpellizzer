Dillenia minor

Dillenia minor, commonly known as the small dillenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia and the Pacific.

It is characterized by its distinctive, palmately lobed leaves and showy, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Dillenia minor is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses in its native regions.
