Dendrochilum simile

Dendrochilum simile, commonly known as the similar tree orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, loose soil.

Dendrochilum simile is a popular choice for both garden cultivation and botanical study due to its ornamental value and unique morphology.
