Dysphania kalpari

Dysphania kalpari, commonly known as Kalpari leafcutter, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadside verges and open woodlands.

Dysphania kalpari is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
