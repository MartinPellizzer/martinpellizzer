Deutzia fargesii

Deutzia fargesii, commonly known as the Chinese deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features upright, branching stems covered in small, ovate leaves that turn yellow in autumn. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pink flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Deutzia fargesii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
