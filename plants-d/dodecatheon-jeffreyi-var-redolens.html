Dodecatheon jeffreyi var. redolens

Dodecatheon jeffreyi var. redolens, commonly known as the Jeffrey's glory-of-the-snow, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The flowers bloom in early spring, often before the leaves fully emerge, showcasing the plant's adaptation to its alpine or subalpine habitat.
