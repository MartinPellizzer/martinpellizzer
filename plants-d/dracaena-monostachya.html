Dracaena monostachya

Dracaena monostachya, commonly known as the narrow-leaved dracaena, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and long, narrow leaves that are typically bright green. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in a spike at the top of the stem. It is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is often cultivated as a ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.
