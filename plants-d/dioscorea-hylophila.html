Dioscorea hylophila

Dioscorea hylophila, commonly known as the silverleaf yam, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Dioscoreaceae family and is characterized by its tuberous roots, which are often used in traditional medicine. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and medicinal contexts.
