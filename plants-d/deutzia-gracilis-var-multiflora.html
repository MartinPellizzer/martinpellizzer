Deutzia gracilis var. multiflora

Deutzia gracilis var. multiflora, commonly known as the slender deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features slender, arching stems and small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has compound leaves with ovate to elliptic leaflets, and it typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental flowers and is often used in garden landscapes for its graceful appearance.
