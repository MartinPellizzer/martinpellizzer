Decanema luteifluens

It is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly found in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or savanna habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a landscape plant.
