Digitaria obtusifolia

Digitaria obtusifolia, commonly known as broadleaf digitaria, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its broad, flat leaves and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as fields and roadsides. It is often found in regions with warm climates and well-drained soils.

Digitaria obtusifolia is sometimes considered a weed but can also serve as forage for livestock in certain areas.
