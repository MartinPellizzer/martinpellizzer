Delphinium Anthoroideum Var. Sclerocladum

Delphinium anthoroideum var. sclerocladum is a rare and striking member of the delphinium family known for its vibrant blue to purple flowers. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, this variant thrives in high-altitude environments with cool, moist conditions. Its unique foliage features thick, leathery leaves that help it withstand harsh climates. The plant is highly valued by botanists and gardeners for its ornamental beauty and ecological significance. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species requiring careful conservation efforts.
