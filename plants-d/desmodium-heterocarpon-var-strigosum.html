Desmodium heterocarpon var. strigosum

Desmodium heterocarpon var. strigosum, commonly known as hairy sicklepod, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a legume, belonging to the Fabaceae family, and features small, trifoliate leaves with hairy surfaces. The plant produces yellow flowers that develop into elongated, curved pods, which are characteristic of the genus Desmodium. It has a sprawling growth habit and is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats.

Its botanical features make it both ecologically significant and sometimes invasive in certain regions.
