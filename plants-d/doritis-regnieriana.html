Doritis regnieriana

Doritis regnieriana, commonly known as the silver leaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, hairy leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its striking appearance and rarity, it is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its ornamental value.
