Delphinium dasyanthum

Delphinium dasyanthum, commonly known as the Giant Delphinium, is a striking perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that can reach heights of up to 6 feet, supporting large, showy blue to purple flowers. The plant features palmate leaves that are deeply divided, giving it a distinctive and elegant appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and relatively hardy nature, it is often cultivated in temperate climates.
