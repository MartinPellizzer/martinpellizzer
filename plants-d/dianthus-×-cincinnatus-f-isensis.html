Dianthus × cincinnatus f. isensis

Dianthus × cincinnatus f. isensis, commonly known as the Isen's carnation, is a hybrid perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, double-layered flowers that come in a range of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant produces showy, cup-shaped blooms that are often used in ornamental gardening for their visual appeal. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to both cultivated landscapes and botanical collections.
