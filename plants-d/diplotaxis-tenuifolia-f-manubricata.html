Diplotaxis tenuifolia f. manubricata

Diplotaxis tenuifolia f. manubricata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved synnema, is a variant of the synnema plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves that are often covered in fine, dense hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in the study of succulent flora.
