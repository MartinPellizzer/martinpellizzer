Diospyros lucida

Diospyros lucida, commonly known as the shining ebony, is a tropical tree native to parts of Southeast Asia, including India, Indonesia, and the Philippines.

It is characterized by its dark, glossy bark and heart-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The tree produces small, yellowish flowers followed by edible, round fruits that resemble small apples. Its fruit is rich in nutrients and is valued for both culinary and medicinal purposes.

Diospyros lucida is also noted for its hard, dense wood, which is used in crafting and construction.
