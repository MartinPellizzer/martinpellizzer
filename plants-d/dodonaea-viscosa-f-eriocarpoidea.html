Dodonaea viscosa f. eriocarpoidea

Dodonaea viscosa f. eriocarpoidea, commonly known as the silky dandelion or silky hop bush, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, silvery-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in spherical inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions.

Its adaptability makes it a common ornamental and ecological plant in various landscapes.
