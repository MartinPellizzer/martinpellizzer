Daphne × latymeri

Daphne × latymeri, commonly known as the winter daphne, is a hybrid shrub native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of fragrant, waxy flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and has a compact, rounded form. Its leaves are dark green and glossy, providing a striking contrast to the pale pink or white flowers.

Daphne × latymeri prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.
