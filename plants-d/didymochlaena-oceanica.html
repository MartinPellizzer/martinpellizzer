Didymochlaena oceanica

Didymochlaena oceanica, commonly known as the oceanic didymochlaena, is a rare and specialized seagrass species found in coastal marine environments.

It thrives in shallow, warm waters with high salinity and sandy substrates. The plant has long, narrow leaves that are adapted to reduce water resistance and maximize light absorption. Its reproductive structures are unique, featuring small, delicate flowers that bloom in clusters.
