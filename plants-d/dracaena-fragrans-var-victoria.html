Dracaena fragrans var. victoria

Dracaena fragrans var. victoria, commonly known as the Victoria palm, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features a tall, slender trunk with a smooth, dark green bark and produces long, feather-like leaves that can grow up to 2 meters in length. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires moderate watering to prevent root rot. It is often cultivated as a houseplant or in outdoor gardens due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.

The Victoria palm is also valued for its resilience and adaptability to various indoor environments.
