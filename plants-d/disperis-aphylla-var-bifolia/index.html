Disperis Aphylla Var. Bifolia

Disperis aphylla var. bifolia is a rare and fascinating plant native to specific regions of Africa. It belongs to the orchid family and is known for its unique floral structure and limited distribution. This variant stands out due to its distinctive two-leaved morphology, which sets it apart from other members of its species. Despite its beauty, it faces threats from habitat loss and over-collection. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival in the wild.
