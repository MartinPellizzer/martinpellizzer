Discaria lycioides var. exilis

Discaria lycioides var. exilis, commonly known as the slender discaria, is a low-growing shrub native to southern Australia.

It features small, rounded leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences. It is adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions.

The species is noted for its resilience to drought and its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
