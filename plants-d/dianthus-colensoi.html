Dianthus colensoi

Dianthus colensoi, commonly known as the Colenso's carnation, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its stems are erect and often branched.

Dianthus colensoi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
