Distichorchis uniflora

Distichorchis uniflora, commonly known as the single-flowered orchid, is a rare and specialized orchid species native to specific regions of South America.

The plant has a compact growth habit with a short rhizome and a single leaf, which is typically broad and lance-shaped.
