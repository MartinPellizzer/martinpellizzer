Dianella intermedia var. norfolkensis

Dianella intermedia var. norfolkensis, commonly known as the Norfolk Island pittosporum, is a striking evergreen shrub native to Norfolk Island.

It features lance-shaped, glossy dark green leaves that are arranged in whorls around the stems. The plant produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers in late spring to early summer, followed by blue-black berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a valuable addition to coastal gardens.
