Deutzia Crenata Var. Pubescens

Crenate deutzia, scientifically known as Deutzia crenata var. pubescens, is a deciduous shrub prized for its ornamental value and adaptability. Native to eastern Asia, it thrives in woodland and garden settings with partial shade and well-drained soil. Its clusters of white or pale pink flowers bloom in early spring, adding a delicate touch to the landscape. The plant's lobed leaves and compact growth make it a versatile choice for borders or as a specimen plant. With low maintenance requirements, it is a favored addition to many perennial gardens.
