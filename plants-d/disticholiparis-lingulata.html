Disticholiparis lingulata

Disticholiparis lingulata, commonly known as the two-rowed lily, is a flowering plant belonging to the Liliaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, or red. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that form a basal rosette, and its stem is usually unbranched.
