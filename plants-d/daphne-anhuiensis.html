Daphne anhuiensis

Daphne anhuiensis, commonly known as the Anhui daphne, is a rare and ornamental plant native to China.

It belongs to the Thymelaeaceae family and is characterized by its compact shrub form and striking flowers. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are ovate and have a slightly hairy texture, while its stems are typically reddish-brown.
