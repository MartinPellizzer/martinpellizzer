Drymoglossum piloselloides var. beddomei

Drymoglossum piloselloides var. beddomei, commonly known as the beddome's hair moss, is a species of moss belonging to the family Pottiaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and dark green to brownish-green coloration. The leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with a distinct costa that extends beyond the mid-leaf. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found on soil or rock surfaces in temperate regions.

This variety is notable for its fine, hairy appearance, which gives it its common name.
