Daedalacanthus salaccensis

Daedalacanthus salaccensis, commonly known as the Singapore orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to Singapore.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, intricate floral structure. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often found in forest understories. Its flowers are small but striking, featuring a complex arrangement of petals and sepals.
