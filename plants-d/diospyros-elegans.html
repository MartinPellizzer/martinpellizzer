Diospyros elegans

Diospyros elegans, commonly known as the elegant persimmon, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. Diospyros elegans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic beauty.
