Deschampsia Media F. Elegans

Deschampsia media f. elegans is a striking variant of the common tussock grass known for its elegant silver-blue foliage. This ornamental grass thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or partially sunny environments. Its delicate, feathery plumes add a soft texture to gardens and natural landscapes. The plant is prized for its ability to create a lush, low-maintenance ground cover. With its unique color and form, it is a popular choice among gardeners seeking both beauty and ecological value.
