Dipteracanthus multifolius var. multiflorus

Dipteracanthus multifolius var. multiflorus, commonly known as the multiflorous dipteracanthus, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Dipterocarpaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical rainforests, thriving in humid and shaded environments. Its leaves are usually broad and leathery, providing a striking visual contrast to its delicate floral display.

The species is noted for its ecological importance, supporting a variety of pollinators and contributing to the biodiversity of its habitat.
