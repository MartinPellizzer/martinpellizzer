Ditaxis cordata

Ditaxis cordata, commonly known as the heartleaf spurge, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine white hairs. It produces small, yellow flowers in clusters during the warmer months.

The plant is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
