Dovea tectorum

Dovea tectorum, commonly known as the roof plant, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, white or pale blue flowers. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and container planting.

Dovea tectorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
