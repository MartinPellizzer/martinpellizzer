Dendrobium connatum var. connatum

Dendrobium connatum var. connatum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed dendrobium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant that grows on trees and rocks, typically in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for both horticultural and ornamental purposes.
