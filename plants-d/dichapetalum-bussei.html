Dichapetalum bussei

Dichapetalum bussei, commonly known as the African star orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along riverbanks. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil efficiently.

The flowers are often pale green or yellow with a subtle fragrance, attracting specific pollinators like bees and moths.
