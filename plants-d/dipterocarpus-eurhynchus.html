Dipterocarpus eurhynchus

Dipterocarpus eurhynchus, commonly known as the broad-leafed dipterocarp, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dipterocarpaceae and is characterized by its large, buttressed roots and dense canopy. The tree produces heavy, woody fruits that contain winged seeds, aiding in wind dispersal. Its leaves are typically broad and opposite, with a glossy surface.

It plays an important ecological role in rainforest ecosystems, providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
