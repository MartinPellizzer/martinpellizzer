Dufrenoya sphaerocarpa

Dufrenoya sphaerocarpa, commonly known as the globe-fruit, is a rare and unusual plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

The plant produces small, white flowers and has a woody stem with a distinctive, branching pattern. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool temperatures and well-drained soil.
