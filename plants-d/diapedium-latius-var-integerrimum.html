Diapedium latius var. integerrimum

Diapedium latius var. integerrimum, commonly known as the broad-leaved diapedium, is a herbaceous perennial native to temperate regions of Asia.

It features lance-shaped leaves with a smooth, waxy surface and a distinct, aromatic scent. The plant produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers during the spring and early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
