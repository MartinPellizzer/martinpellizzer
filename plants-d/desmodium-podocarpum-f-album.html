Desmodium podocarpum f. album

Desmodium podocarpum f. album, commonly known as the silverleaf desmodium, is a herbaceous legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features trifoliate leaves with a distinctive silvery underside, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale pink flowers followed by elongated, bean-like pods. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and pastures.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it a valuable plant for soil enrichment and forage in agricultural settings.
