Diospyros calophylla

Diospyros calophylla, commonly known as the glossy buckeye, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green on top. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by orange-red berries containing a single seed. Its bark is smooth and dark brown, often marked with lenticels.

Diospyros calophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
