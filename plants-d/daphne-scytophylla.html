Daphne scytophylla

Daphne scytophylla, commonly known as the silver leaf daphne, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features aromatic, glandular leaves that are typically silvery-white in color, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white during the spring and summer months. Its flowers are followed by small, woody fruits that contain seeds.

Daphne scytophylla is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and unique floral display.
