Delphinium hansenii arcuatum

Delphinium hansenii arcuatum, commonly known as the Hansen's larkspur, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

It is characterized by its tall, spindly stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant produces striking blue to purple flowers in dense clusters, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Delphinium hansenii arcuatum is a member of the Ranunculaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
