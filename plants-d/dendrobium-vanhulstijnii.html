Dendrobium vanhulstijnii

Dendrobium vanhulstijnii, commonly known as the Van Hulstijn's orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with multiple pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, long-lasting flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium vanhulstijnii is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
