Dendrochilum anfractum

Dendrochilum anfractum, commonly known as the twisted-stem orchid, is a tropical orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, twisted stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often arranged in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrochilum anfractum is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
