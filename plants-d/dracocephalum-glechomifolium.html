Dracocephalum glechomifolium

Dracocephalum glechomifolium, commonly known as blueweed, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features lance-shaped, hairy leaves that are often tinged with blue or purple, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered blue or purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, sunny habitats.

Dracocephalum glechomifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used traditionally in herbal medicine.
