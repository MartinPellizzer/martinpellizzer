Deutzia crenata var. erecta

Deutzia crenata var. erecta, commonly known as the upright spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features clusters of white or pink flowers that bloom in early summer, adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant has slender, arching stems and small, ovate leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for borders or as a specimen plant in landscaped areas.
