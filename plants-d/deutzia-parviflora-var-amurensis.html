Deutzia parviflora var. amurensis

Deutzia parviflora var. amurensis, commonly known as the Amur deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features slender, arching branches and clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

This variety is hardy and adaptable, thriving in a range of soil types and climates.
