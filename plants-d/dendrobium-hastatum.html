Dendrobium hastatum

Dendrobium hastatum, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, branching stems and linear, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellow-green flowers with a distinctive zigzag pattern on the petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrobium hastatum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
