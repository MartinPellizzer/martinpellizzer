Delphinium × pskemense

Delphinium × pskemense, commonly known as the Siberian delphinium, is a hybrid perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters, supporting clusters of striking blue to violet flowers. The plant features lance-shaped, serrated leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained, fertile soil.

Delphinium × pskemense is valued in gardens for its showy blooms and is often used in cottage gardens or as a backdrop in perennial beds.
