Dahlgrenia ptariana

Dahlgrenia ptariana, commonly known as the mountain pine.

This species is a coniferous evergreen tree native to the high elevations of the western United States. It is characterized by its needle-like leaves and cone-bearing structure, typical of the pine family. The tree produces small, woody cones that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to cold, dry mountain climates.
