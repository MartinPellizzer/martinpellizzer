Dombeya urenoides

Dombeya urenoides, commonly known as the white dombeya, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, white, cup-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Dombeya urenoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
