Diospyros arnottiana

Diospyros arnottiana, commonly known as the Australian date palm, is a flowering plant species native to Australia.

It is a small, evergreen tree with a short trunk and a broad, umbrella-like canopy. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by edible, orange-yellow fruits. Its leaves are large, glossy, and pinnately compound, contributing to its ornamental value.

Diospyros arnottiana is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in challenging climates.
