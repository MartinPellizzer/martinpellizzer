Didesmandra

Didesmandra, commonly known as the desert bell, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of North America.

The plant has succulent leaves and stems, which help it conserve water in its harsh environment. Didesmandra thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it an attractive addition to native plant gardens.
