Dendrochilum erectilabium

Dendrochilum erectilabium, commonly known as the upright-labied tree orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. This plant is characterized by its erect growth habit and distinctive, showy flowers that often have a unique labellum shape. The leaves are typically broad and lance-shaped, and the plant thrives in humid, tropical environments.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking floral display.
