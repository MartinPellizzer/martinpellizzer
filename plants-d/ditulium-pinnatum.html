Ditulium pinnatum

Ditulium pinnatum, commonly known as the pinnate fern, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Araliaceae and is characterized by its delicate, pinnately compound leaves. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by small, red berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
