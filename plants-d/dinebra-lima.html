Dinebra lima

Dinebra lima, commonly known as the Lima bean plant, is a climbing legume native to the Andes region of South America.

It produces pods that contain smooth, oval seeds, which are widely cultivated for their edible beans. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellow flowers. It thrives in warm climates with well-drained soil and requires regular watering.

The beans are rich in nutrients and are a staple in many cuisines around the world.
