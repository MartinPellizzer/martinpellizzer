Dichaelia natalensis

Dichaelia natalensis, commonly known as Natal grass or Natal dichaelia, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and along riverbanks.

Dichaelia natalensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
