Delphinium lorestanicum

Delphinium lorestanicum, commonly known as the Lorestan larkspur, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Iran.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters. The plant produces clusters of blue to purple flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are palmately compound, with lobed segments that are hairy and serrated.

Delphinium lorestanicum is adapted to high-altitude environments and thrives in well-drained soils.
