Dodecatheon triflorum

Dodecatheon triflorum, commonly known as the three-flowered glory-of-the-snow, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern North America.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. Dodecatheon triflorum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
