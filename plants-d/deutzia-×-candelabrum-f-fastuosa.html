Deutzia × candelabrum f. fastuosa

Deutzia × candelabrum f. fastuosa, commonly known as the showy deutzia, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to China.

It features clusters of fragrant, white to pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer, creating a striking display. The plant has slender, arching branches that are often adorned with small, oval-shaped leaves. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
