Dendrobium chrysoglossum

Dendrobium chrysoglossum, commonly known as the golden-lip orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a tall, upright stem with numerous fleshy, strap-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellow-green flowers with distinctive golden lips. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Dendrobium chrysoglossum is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
