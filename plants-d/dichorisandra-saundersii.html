Dichorisandra saundersii

Dichorisandra saundersii, commonly known as the silverleaf kentia, is a striking evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features lance-shaped, silver-green leaves that are broad at the base and taper to a point, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in clusters, typically in shades of yellow or orange, which bloom during the warmer months. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance of low light and moderate watering.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardens.
