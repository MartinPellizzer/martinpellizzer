Dahlia Scapigeroides Var. Typica

Scapigeroides dahlia, scientifically known as Dahlia scapigeroides var. typica, is a striking member of the Asteraceae family native to Mexico. This plant is renowned for its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors including red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardeners and horticulturists. Its unique foliage and long-lasting blooms contribute to its popularity in both ornamental and botanical contexts. With its historical significance and aesthetic appeal, it remains a cherished addition to botanical collections worldwide.
