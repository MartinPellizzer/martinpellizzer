Dupatya argentea

Dupatya argentea, commonly known as silverleaf datura, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to violet, often with a waxy texture. The plant has hairy, ovate leaves that are typically silver-gray in appearance, giving it its common name. It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is known for its rapid growth and adaptability to various soil types.

Dupatya argentea is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its striking appearance and unique foliage.
