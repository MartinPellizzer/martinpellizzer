Dactylorhiza × vermeuleniana

Dactylorhiza × vermeuleniana, commonly known as the hybrid marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Dactylorhiza macdougallii and Dactylorhiza lapponica. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy habitats, thriving in moist, acidic soils. It produces tall, slender stems with clusters of pale green to yellowish flowers.

Its botanical characteristics include a complex root system and a unique morphology adapted to its wetland environment.
