Dianthus Stenopetalus Var. Pancicii

Dianthus stenopetalus var. pancicii is a rare and striking member of the carnation family known for its vibrant pink and white blooms. Native to the alpine regions of the Balkans, this plant thrives in cool, moist environments with well-drained soil. Its unique flower structure features narrow petals that create a delicate, lace-like appearance. Despite its beauty, it is highly sensitive to environmental changes and is considered endangered in the wild. Cultivating this variety requires careful attention to its specific needs, making it a prized addition for botanists and gardeners alike.
