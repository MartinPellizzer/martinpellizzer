Dracaena steudneri

Dracaena steudneri, commonly known as the Red-edge Dracaena, is a striking evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features long, narrow leaves with a distinctive reddish margin, which adds a dramatic visual appeal to any landscape. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy fruits. It is well-suited for both indoor and outdoor cultivation due to its tolerance of various light conditions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and as a houseplant.
