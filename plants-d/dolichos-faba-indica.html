Dolichos faba-indica

Dolichos faba-indica, commonly known as the Indian horsegram or cluster bean, is a leguminous plant native to India.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces cylindrical pods that contain small, kidney-shaped seeds, which are edible and rich in protein. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for both its seeds and foliage.

Dolichos faba-indica is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it an important crop in sustainable agriculture.
