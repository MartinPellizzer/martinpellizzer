Dillenia indica var. aurea

Dillenia indica var. aurea, commonly known as golden dillenia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves that are often hairy on the underside. The plant produces showy, yellowish-white flowers with five petals, which bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a woody, spiny capsule that contains several seeds.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some cultures.
