Dendrobium pseudotenellum

Dendrobium pseudotenellum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright pseudobulbs and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically during the summer months. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
