Desmos chinensis

Desmos chinensis, commonly known as Chinese flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as birds and insects. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy and have a rough texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Desmos chinensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
