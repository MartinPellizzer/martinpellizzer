Dupineta hensii

Dupineta hensii, commonly known as the Hensii sandmat, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal or inland fynbos vegetation.

Dupineta hensii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature.
