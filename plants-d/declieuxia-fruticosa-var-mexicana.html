Declieuxia fruticosa var. mexicana

Declieuxia fruticosa var. mexicana, commonly known as the Mexican shrub or Mexican firebush, is a shrub native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and bright red or orange flowers that appear in clusters. The plant has small, narrow leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant floral display and drought tolerance.
