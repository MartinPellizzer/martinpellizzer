Dieteria asteroides var. asteroides

Dieteria asteroides var. asteroides, commonly known as the star-shaped dieteria, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped flowers, which are typically white or pale pink in color. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are lance-shaped with serrated edges. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
