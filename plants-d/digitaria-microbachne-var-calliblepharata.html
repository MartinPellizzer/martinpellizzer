Digitaria microbachne var. calliblepharata

Digitaria microbachne var. calliblepharata, commonly known as the slender digitgrass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as fields and roadsides, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves with a bluish-green color, and its inflorescence consists of spikelets arranged in a panicle. It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers and is often found in lowland and coastal habitats.

This variant is notable for its adaptability and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
