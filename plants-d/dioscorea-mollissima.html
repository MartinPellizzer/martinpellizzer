Dioscorea mollissima

Dioscorea mollissima, commonly known as the purple yam, is a tuberous plant belonging to the family Dioscoreaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific Islands. The plant is characterized by its vigorous climbing habit, with tendrils that help it ascend supports. Its tubers, which are the edible part, are typically purple or red in color and have a starchy, sweet flavor.

Dioscorea mollissima is valued not only for its nutritional content but also for its traditional medicinal uses in various cultures.
