Dichapetalum liberiae

Dichapetalum liberiae, commonly known as the African star orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that come in various colors such as white, pink, and purple. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in arid environments.

The orchid's unique floral structure and vibrant colors make it a popular subject in botanical studies and horticulture.
