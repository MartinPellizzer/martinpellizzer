Descantaria minuta

Descantaria minuta, commonly known as the small-flowered bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue or violet, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Descantaria minuta thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens or borders.

Its compact size and delicate appearance make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
