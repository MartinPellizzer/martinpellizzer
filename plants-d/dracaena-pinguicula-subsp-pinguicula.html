Dracaena pinguicula subsp. pinguicula

Dracaena pinguicula subsp.

pinguicula, commonly known as the red-flowered dracaena, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit and fleshy, dark green leaves with red or purple markings.

The plant produces small, bright red flowers that bloom in summer, adding visual interest. It is well adapted to arid conditions, storing water in its thick leaves and stems.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
