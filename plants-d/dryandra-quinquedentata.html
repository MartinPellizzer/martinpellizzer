Dryandra quinquedentata

Dryandra quinquedentata, commonly known as the Five-toothed Dryandra, is a species of flowering plant in the Proteaceae family.

It is native to Western Australia and is characterized by its striking, urn-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The species is known for its ecological importance in supporting local wildlife and its role in the unique flora of the region.
