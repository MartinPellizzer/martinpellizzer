Dendrochilum niveum

Dendrochilum niveum, commonly known as the snow orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are elongated and often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence.

Dendrochilum niveum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its delicate appearance.
