Diasia parviflora

Diasia parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered diasia, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in grassy areas and along riverbanks, preferring well-drained soils. The plant features slender, upright stems that bear small, white or pale purple flowers arranged in clusters. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in fine hairs.

Diasia parviflora is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
