Distictis frutescens

Distictis frutescens, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous shrub that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with a central disk, blooming from late winter to early summer. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Distictis frutescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticulture for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.
