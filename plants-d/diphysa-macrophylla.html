Diphysa macrophylla

Diphysa macrophylla, commonly known as the broad-leaf diphysa, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires partial to full sun exposure.

Diphysa macrophylla is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and long blooming period.
