Dolichos ensiformis

Dolichos ensiformis, commonly known as the sword bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features elongated, narrow leaves that resemble a sword, hence its common name. The plant produces small, yellow flowers followed by elongated pods containing edible seeds. It is valued for its nutritional content and is often used in traditional cuisines.

Botanically, it belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and nitrogen-fixing ability.
