Dryopteris clintoniana

Dryopteris clintoniana, commonly known as Clinton's wood fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to eastern North America.

It features a tall, upright rhizome with a dense cluster of dark green, lance-shaped fronds. The fronds are typically divided into multiple pinnae, giving the plant a lush, feathery appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in woodlands and along stream banks.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly used in horticulture for its hardiness and aesthetic qualities.
