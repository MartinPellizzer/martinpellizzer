Dioscorea torticaulis

Dioscorea torticaulis, commonly known as the zigzag yam, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, zigzagging stems. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers and cylindrical, tuberous roots that are often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are heart-shaped with a rough texture and are typically arranged alternately along the stem.

Dioscorea torticaulis is valued for its medicinal properties and is also cultivated for its edible tubers in some regions.
