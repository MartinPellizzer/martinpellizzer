Dendrorkis purpurea

Dendrorkis purpurea, commonly known as the purple orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking purple flowers and compact growth habit. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are elongated and glossy, while its roots are adapted for epiphytic growth on trees.

Dendrorkis purpurea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
