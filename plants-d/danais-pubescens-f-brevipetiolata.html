Danais pubescens f. brevipetiolata

Danais pubescens f. brevipetiolata, commonly known as the short-petioled danais, is a unique variant of the danais plant, characterized by its shorter petioles compared to the standard species.

It features lance-shaped leaves with a hairy surface, which provides a distinctive texture and appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, typical of the danais genus. Its growth habit is compact, making it suitable for smaller garden spaces.

This form is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions.
