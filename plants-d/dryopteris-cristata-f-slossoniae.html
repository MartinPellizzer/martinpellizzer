Dryopteris cristata f. slossoniae

Dryopteris cristata f. slossoniae, commonly known as the Slosson's sword fern, is a fern species native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It is a cultivar of the sword fern, characterized by its distinctive, upright fronds that are often curled or twisted. The fronds are typically dark green and have a serrated edge, with a dense, compact growth habit. This form is prized for its ornamental value in shaded gardens and as a container plant.

It prefers moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland landscapes.
