Dioscorea vittata

Dioscorea vittata, commonly known as the yellow arrowhead vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces tuberous roots that are sometimes used in traditional medicine. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its rapid growth and ornamental value make it a popular choice for gardens and greenhouses.
