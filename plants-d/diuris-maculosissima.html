Diuris maculosissima

Diuris maculosissima, commonly known as the spotted orchid, is a striking orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It features a compact, upright growth habit with a single, showy flower spike that bears one or two flowers. The flowers are typically pale green to yellow with distinctive red or brown markings, creating a striking contrast. The plant has a short, fleshy rhizome and produces small, hairy leaves that are arranged in a rosette.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and open forests.
