Dichaea lehmanniana

Dichaea lehmanniana, commonly known as the Lehmann's dichaea, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features a short, thick rhizome with several elongated, fleshy leaves. Its flowers are usually small and white or pale green, arranged in a spike.

It is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
