Dichanthelium commutatum var. ashei

Dichanthelium commutatum var. ashei, commonly known as Ashe's panicum, is a grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as marshes, swales, and roadside ditches. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are often reddish in color. It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and is often found in disturbed or open areas.

This variety is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
