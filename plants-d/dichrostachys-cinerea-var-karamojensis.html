Dichrostachys cinerea var. karamojensis

Dichrostachys cinerea var. karamojensis, commonly known as Karamojian burr bush, is a shrub native to East Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its dense, thorny growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by legume pods containing small, hard seeds. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in savannas and along riverbanks.

Due to its hardy nature, it is used for erosion control and as a source of forage for livestock.
