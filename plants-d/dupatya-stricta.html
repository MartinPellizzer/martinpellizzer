Dupatya stricta

Dupatya stricta, commonly known as the rigid flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, often appearing before the leaves. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal areas or near water sources.

The flowers are not only ornamental but also attract various pollinators such as bees and birds.
