Dendrocalamus xishuangbannaensis

Dendrocalamus xishuangbannaensis, commonly known as the Xishuangbanna bamboo, is a tall, fast-growing grass species native to southern China.

It belongs to the Bambusoideae subfamily and is characterized by its strong, straight culms that can reach heights of up to 20 meters. The plant has a dense, fibrous rhizome system that allows it to form large clumps or colonies. Its leaves are long and narrow, with a smooth, waxy surface, and its flowers are small and clustered in panicles.

This bamboo is valued for its durable and flexible stalks, making it useful in construction and handicrafts.
