Derris forsteniana

Derris forsteniana, commonly known as the African snakewood, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces pods that contain seeds, and it is often found in woodland and savanna habitats.
