Desmodium yungasense

Desmodium yungasense, commonly known as Yungas velvetleaf, is a herbaceous plant native to the humid subtropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and along riverbanks. It has a shallow root system and is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility.

Due to its ecological role and ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
