Desmodium raymundoramirezii

Desmodium raymundoramirezii, commonly known as Raymund Ramirez's burr legume, is a flowering plant native to the cloud forests of Central America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its distinctive pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
