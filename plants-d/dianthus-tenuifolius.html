Dianthus tenuifolius

Dianthus tenuifolius, commonly known as the slenderleaf campion, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and clusters of pink or white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is a perennial herb that can reach heights of up to 30 centimeters.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of cultivation, it is often grown in gardens and rockeries.
