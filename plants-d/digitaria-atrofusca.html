Digitaria atrofusca

Digitaria atrofusca, commonly known as black digit grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its dark green, narrow leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It is often found in regions with warm climates and well-drained soils.

Digitaria atrofusca is known for its resilience and ability to thrive in challenging environmental conditions.
