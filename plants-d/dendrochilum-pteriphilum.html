Dendrochilum pteriphilum

Dendrochilum pteriphilum, commonly known as the feather-leaf orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, feather-like leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
