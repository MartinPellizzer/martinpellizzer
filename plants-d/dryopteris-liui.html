Dryopteris liui

Dryopteris liui, commonly known as the Li's wood fern, is a species of fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its fronds that are typically divided into lance-shaped segments. The plant prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its spores are released from sori located on the underside of the fronds.

Dryopteris liui is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
