Diospyros crassinervis subsp. kubal

Diospyros crassinervis subsp.

kubal, commonly known as the Kubal persimmon, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia. It belongs to the Ebenaceae family, which includes ebony and persimmon species.

The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves with prominent veins, and it produces small, yellowish-green flowers. Its fruit is a fleshy, orange-red berry that ripens in late summer or early autumn.

The tree is valued for its ornamental qualities and has limited use in local traditional medicine.
