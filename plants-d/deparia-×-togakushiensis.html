Deparia × togakushiensis

Deparia × togakushiensis, commonly known as the Togakushi shield fern, is a hybrid species native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its fronds are usually dark green with a leathery texture and can reach lengths of up to 30 centimeters.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
