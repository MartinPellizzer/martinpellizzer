Desmodiastrum Belgaumense

Desmodiastrum belgaumense is a rare and lesser-known plant species native to the western ghats of india. it belongs to the legume family and is characterized by its unique leaf structure and flowering pattern. despite its ecological significance, it remains under-studied and faces threats from habitat loss and climate change. the plant plays a vital role in local ecosystems by supporting pollinators and enriching the soil. conservation efforts are essential to ensure its survival for future generations.
