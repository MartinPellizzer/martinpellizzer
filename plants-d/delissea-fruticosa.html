Delissea fruticosa

Delissea fruticosa, commonly known as the Cape fuchsia, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red, arranged in clusters. The plant has woody stems and is often used in ornamental gardening due to its vibrant blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Delissea fruticosa is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as birds and bees.
