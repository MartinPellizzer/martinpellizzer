Disa hyacinthina

Disa hyacinthina, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that come in shades of pink, purple, and white. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense clusters.

Disa hyacinthina is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a popular choice in gardens and floral arrangements.
