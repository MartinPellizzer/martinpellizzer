Disocactus lankesteri

Disocactus lankesteri, commonly known as the "Lankester's Disocactus," is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces striking, large, funnel-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are usually reduced to spines, and it thrives in well-drained soil with plenty of sunlight.

This plant is popular among cactus enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
