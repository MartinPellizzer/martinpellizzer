Delissea mariana

Delissea mariana, commonly known as the marsh hawksbeard, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It typically grows in wetland areas, such as marshes and along the edges of ponds. The plant features a basal rosette of lance-shaped leaves and produces bright yellow composite flowers in the summer. Its flowering stems are usually unbranched and can reach heights of up to 60 cm.

Delissea mariana is adapted to moist soils and is often found in regions with high water tables.
