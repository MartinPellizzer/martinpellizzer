Dendrobenthamia wuyishanica

Dendrobenthamia wuyishanica, commonly known as the Wuyi Mountain orchid, is a rare epiphytic orchid native to the Wuyi Mountains in China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically blooming during the spring and summer seasons. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific conditions for successful cultivation.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
