Didymocarpus tribounii

Didymocarpus tribounii, commonly known as the Indian persimmon, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions.
