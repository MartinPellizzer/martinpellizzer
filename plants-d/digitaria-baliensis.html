Digitaria baliensis

Digitaria baliensis, commonly known as Balinese Digitaria, is a species of grass native to Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based flowers that are typically arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in open grasslands and disturbed soils.

Digitaria baliensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
