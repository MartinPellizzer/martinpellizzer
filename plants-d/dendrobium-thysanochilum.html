Dendrobium thysanochilum

Dendrobium thysanochilum, commonly known as the golden lip orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic. The plant produces showy, golden-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip that resembles a lip-shaped structure. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium thysanochilum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
