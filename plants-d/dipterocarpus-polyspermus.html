Dipterocarpus polyspermus

Dipterocarpus polyspermus, commonly known as the Malayan dipterocarp, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dipterocarpaceae and is characterized by its large, heavy seeds and buttressed roots. The tree can grow up to 50 meters tall and is often found in lowland rainforests. Its wood is highly valued for its durability and is used in construction and furniture.

The plant plays a significant role in the ecological balance of its habitat by providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
