Desmodium elegans var. nutans

Desmodium elegans var. nutans, commonly known as the hanging velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces small, legume-like pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats.

Its growth habit and floral structure make it a notable species in ecological and botanical studies.
