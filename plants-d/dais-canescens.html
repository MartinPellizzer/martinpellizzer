Dais canescens

Dais canescens, commonly known as the silver daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for dry, rocky environments. Its flowers are typically visited by bees and other pollinators, contributing to its ecological importance.

Dais canescens is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
