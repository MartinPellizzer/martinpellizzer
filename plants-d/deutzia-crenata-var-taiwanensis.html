Deutzia crenata var. taiwanensis

Deutzia crenata var. taiwanensis, commonly known as the Taiwan spirea, is a deciduous shrub native to Taiwan.

It features clusters of white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring, adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant has slender, arching branches and small, ovate leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.
