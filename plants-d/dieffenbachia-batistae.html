Dieffenbachia batistae

Dieffenbachia batistae, commonly known as the Batista's Dieffenbachia, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves that are often adorned with white or yellow markings. The plant is part of the Araceae family, which includes other popular houseplants like peace lilies. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity to flourish.

Its foliage is not only ornamental but also serves as a means of photosynthesis and transpiration.
