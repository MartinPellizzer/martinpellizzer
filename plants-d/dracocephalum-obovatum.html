Dracocephalum obovatum

Dracocephalum obovatum, commonly known as blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its aromatic, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped to obovate leaves that are often fuzzy to the touch. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Dracocephalum obovatum is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
