Dimerocostus uniflorus

Dimerocostus uniflorus, commonly known as the single-flower dimerocostus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features a robust, upright stem with lance-shaped leaves that are typically hairy. Its most distinctive feature is a single, showy flower that blooms at the top of the stem.

Dimerocostus uniflorus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
