Deyeuxia Pyramidalis Var. Ligulata

Deyeuxia pyramidalis var. ligulata is a lesser-known grass species that thrives in moist, temperate environments. Often found in wetlands and along streambanks, it plays a vital role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity. Its slender, pyramid-shaped growth habit makes it distinct from other grasses in the genus. While not widely cultivated, it is valued for its ecological benefits and subtle aesthetic appeal. This plant serves as an important indicator of healthy wetland ecosystems.
