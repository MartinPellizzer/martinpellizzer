Didymocarpus stolonifer

Didymocarpus stolonifer, commonly known as the creeping didymocarp, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Euphorbiaceae.

It is characterized by its prostrate growth habit, with stolons that root at the nodes to form new plants. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in cup-like structures called cyathia. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often covered with fine hairs.

It is native to parts of Asia and is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value.
