Deutzia veitchii

Deutzia veitchii, commonly known as Veitch's deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features clusters of fragrant, white to pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has slender, arching stems and opposite, ovate leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value comes from its showy flowers and graceful form.
