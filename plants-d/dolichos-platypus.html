Dolichos platypus

Dolichos platypus, commonly known as the flat bean, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It produces long, flat pods that contain several edible seeds, which are often used in various cuisines. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and is typically grown for its nutritional value. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Dolichos platypus is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, benefiting other crops when grown in rotation.
