Dillwynia rudis var. brevifolia

Dillwynia rudis var. brevifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved dillwynia, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its leguminous properties and typical pod fruits. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in racemes. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats such as woodlands and grasslands.

This variety is distinguished by its shorter leaves compared to the typical form of Dillwynia rudis.
