Diplazium cultratum

Diplazium cultratum, commonly known as the sword fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, sword-shaped fronds. Diplazium cultratum thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
