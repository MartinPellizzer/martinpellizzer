Dolichos cultratus

Dolichos cultratus, commonly known as the sickle bean, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and trifoliate leaves with elongated leaflets. The plant produces small, yellow flowers followed by elongated, curved pods containing kidney-shaped seeds. Dolichos cultratus is valued for its nutritional content, particularly its high protein and fiber levels.

It is also used in traditional medicine and as a forage crop in some parts of the world.
