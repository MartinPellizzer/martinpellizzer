Duvalia caespitosa var. caespitosa

Duvalia caespitosa var. caespitosa, commonly known as the tufted duvalia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in grassy areas and along riverbanks, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant features basal rosettes of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers in summer. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often used in ornamental gardening.

Its botanical significance lies in its adaptability and role in local ecosystems.
