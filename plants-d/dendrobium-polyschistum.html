Dendrobium polyschistum

Dendrobium polyschistum, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with multiple pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces racemes of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive zigzag pattern on the petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrobium polyschistum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
