Dendrobium revolutum

Dendrobium revolutum, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive spiral arrangement of petals and sepals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-draining soil.

Dendrobium revolutum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
