Dichaea dressleri

Dichaea dressleri, commonly known as the Dressler's dichaea, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on tree trunks or among leaf litter. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the surrounding substrate.

Dichaea dressleri is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
