Dactylorhiza majalis

Dactylorhiza majalis, commonly known as the early purple orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant that typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and mountainous regions. The plant produces a single, upright stem that bears several lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are purple or violet, with three petals and three sepals, forming a distinctive orchid shape.

Dactylorhiza majalis is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its delicate, ephemeral blooms that appear in early spring.
