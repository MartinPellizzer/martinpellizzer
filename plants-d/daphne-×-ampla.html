Daphne × ampla

Daphne × ampla, commonly known as the sweet spindle or winter daphne, is a hybrid shrub native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that range from white to pink, appearing in late winter to early spring. The plant has glossy, dark green foliage and produces small, berry-like fruits in the summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Daphne × ampla is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and winter blooming habit.
