Dendrobium austrocaledonicum

Dendrobium austrocaledonicum, commonly known as the New Caledonia orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the island of New Caledonia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments within rainforests. Its leaves are strap-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, and it has a fibrous root system.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
