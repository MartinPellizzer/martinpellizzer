Disa compta

Disa compta, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its flowers are often pink or purple, with a unique shape that distinguishes it from other orchid species.

Disa compta is a perennial herb that produces showy blooms during the summer months.
