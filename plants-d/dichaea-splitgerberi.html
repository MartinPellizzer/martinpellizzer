Dichaea splitgerberi

Dichaea splitgerberi, commonly known as the Splitgerber's dichaea, is a species of orchid native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features a short, fleshy rhizome with numerous narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered, often in a raceme, with pale green to white petals.

Dichaea splitgerberi is noted for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value.
