Didymocarpus teres

Didymocarpus teres, commonly known as the round-seeded ironwood, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant has a dense, hard wood that is highly valued for its strength and durability. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a leathery texture with a deep green color.

The flowers are small and inconspicuous, while the fruit is a woody capsule that contains several seeds.
