Dendrobium kotanicanum

Dendrobium kotanicanum, commonly known as the Kotanica orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with cane-like stems that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, white, and pink, often arranged in racemes. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and well-draining soil.

Dendrobium kotanicanum is prized for its ornamental value and is commonly cultivated in greenhouses and gardens.
