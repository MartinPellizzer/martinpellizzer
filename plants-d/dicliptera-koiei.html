Dicliptera koiei

Dicliptera koiei, commonly known as the African grass or African bluemouth, is a perennial grass species native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves with a bluish-green hue. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, typical of the grass family. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland areas.

Dicliptera koiei is valued for its use in traditional medicine and as a forage plant for livestock.
