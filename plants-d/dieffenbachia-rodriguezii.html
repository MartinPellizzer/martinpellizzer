Dieffenbachia rodriguezii

Dieffenbachia rodriguezii, commonly known as the Rodriguez's dieffenbachia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that are typically variegated with shades of white, green, and yellow. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other popular houseplants like peace lilies and calla lilies. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Its striking foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
