Distegia nutans, commonly known as the drooping distegia, is a flowering plant species belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing habit and opposite leaves that are usually elliptic to ovate in shape. Native to tropical regions of Africa, it thrives in moist, shaded environments.
