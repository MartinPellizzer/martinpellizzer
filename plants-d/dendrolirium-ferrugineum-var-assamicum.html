Dendrolirium ferrugineum var. assamicum

Dendrolirium ferrugineum var. assamicum, commonly known as the Assam lily, is a flowering plant native to the eastern Himalayas.

It is a herbaceous perennial with erect, slender stems that bear lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, white to pale pink flowers with a distinctive red or orange rim around the throat. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in montane forests.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after species in horticulture.
