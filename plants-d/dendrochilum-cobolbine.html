Dendrochilum cobolbine

Dendrochilum cobolbine, commonly known as the cobolbine orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces striking, violet-colored flowers with a unique, spurred lip. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its flowers bloom in late winter to early spring, adding ornamental value to its natural habitat.

Dendrochilum cobolbine is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
