Declieuxia oenanthoides var. stenophylla

Declieuxia oenanthoides var. stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leafed lilyturf, is a perennial grass-like plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color, and it produces small, white or pale pink flowers in summer. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or shrubland habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticultural settings for its ability to thrive in shaded areas.
