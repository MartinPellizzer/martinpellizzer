Dendrobium quadrangulare

Dendrobium quadrangulare, commonly known as the quadrangular orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its square-shaped stems, which give it its scientific name. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers during the summer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
