Dianthus taoshanensis

Dianthus taoshanensis, commonly known as the Taoshan carnation, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in well-drained environments. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, while its stems are erect and branching.

Dianthus taoshanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
