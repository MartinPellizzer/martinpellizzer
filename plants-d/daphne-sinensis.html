Daphne sinensis

Daphne sinensis, commonly known as Chinese daphne, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late winter to early spring. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are dark green and glossy. Its stems are usually covered in a thick, waxy coating.

Daphne sinensis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its winter bloom.
