Dianthus monspessulanus var. plumosus

Dianthus monspessulanus var. plumosus, commonly known as the plumose campion, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its showy, pink or purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant features slender, branching stems and feathery, hairy leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate, plume-like flower structure.
