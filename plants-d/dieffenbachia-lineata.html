Dieffenbachia lineata

Dieffenbachia lineata, commonly known as the White Knight, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that feature white and green patterns. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other popular houseplants like peace lilies. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and can grow quite large, making it a dramatic addition to indoor spaces.

Dieffenbachia lineata thrives in bright, indirect light and requires regular watering to maintain its lush appearance.
