Dendrocnide milnei

Dendrocnide milnei, commonly known as the stinging tree, is a flowering plant native to northeastern Australia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters tall, characterized by its dense, woolly foliage. Its leaves are covered in fine, hair-like structures that can cause severe skin irritation upon contact.
