Dalbergaria kahlbreyeriana

Dalbergaria kahlbreyeriana, commonly known as the Kahlbreyer's burr legume, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid conditions. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, ranging in color from white to pale purple.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging environments.
