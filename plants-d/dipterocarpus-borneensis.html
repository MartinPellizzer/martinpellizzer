Dipterocarpus borneensis

Dipterocarpus borneensis, commonly known as the Bornean dipterocarp, is a tropical tree native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It is a member of the Dipterocarpaceae family and is characterized by its large, buttressed roots and heavy, durable wood. The tree can grow up to 50 meters tall and has compound leaves with a broad, ovate shape. It plays a vital role in the ecosystem, providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.

Its flowers are typically yellow or orange and are pollinated by insects and birds.
