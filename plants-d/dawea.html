Dawea

Dawea, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other similar plants. The plant features showy, daisy-like flowers with bright yellow centers and white to pink petals. Dawea thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies adds ecological value to its environment.
