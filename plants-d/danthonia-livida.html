Danthonia livida

Danthonia livida, commonly known as the silver grass, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-blue foliage that gives it a striking appearance in gardens. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. Its inflorescence consists of dense, spike-like clusters of small, pale flowers.

Danthonia livida is valued for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
