Daiswa polyphylla

Daiswa polyphylla, commonly known as the mountain daisy, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

Daiswa polyphylla produces small, yellowish to white flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
