Diplusodon helianthemifolius f. striatifolius

Diplusodon helianthemifolius f. striatifolius, commonly known as the striped leaf rockrose, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, oval-shaped leaves with a distinctive striped pattern. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and arid landscapes.

Its unique foliage and drought tolerance make it a desirable ornamental plant.
