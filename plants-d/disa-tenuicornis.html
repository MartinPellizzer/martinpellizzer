Disa tenuicornis

Disa tenuicornis, commonly known as the slender horned orchid, is a species of orchid native to southern Africa.

It is a terrestrial herb with a distinctive appearance, featuring a basal rosette of fleshy, dark green leaves. The plant produces tall, slender stems that bear clusters of small, pale pink to white flowers with a prominent, elongated spur. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and forest margins.

Disa tenuicornis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its unique floral structure.
