Dicraeia minutiflora subsp. orientalis

Dicraeia minutiflora subsp.

orientalis, commonly known as the oriental dwarf milkweed, is a small, herbaceous plant native to parts of Southeast Asia. It features slender, upright stems covered in fine, hairy foliage.

The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers clustered in dense, cylindrical inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its latex contains milky sap, which is characteristic of the Apocynaceae family.
