Deutzia pringlei

Deutzia pringlei, commonly known as the Pringle deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features clusters of fragrant, white to pink flowers that bloom in early spring, typically in May or June. The plant has slender, arching stems and elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy on top. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for gardens and naturalized areas.

Deutzia pringlei is valued for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in temperate climates.
