Dendrobium primulinum var. giganteum

Dendrobium primulinum var. giganteum, commonly known as the giant orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, segmented stems that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a distinctive red or orange lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and role in the biodiversity of its native habitats.
