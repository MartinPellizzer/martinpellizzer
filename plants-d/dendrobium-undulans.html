Dendrobium undulans

Dendrobium undulans, commonly known as the wavy-leaf orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, pendulous stems and lance-shaped leaves that often have a wavy or undulating edge. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with a distinctive red or orange lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Dendrobium undulans is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
