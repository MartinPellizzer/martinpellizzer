Diadeniopsis bennettii

Diadeniopsis bennettii, commonly known as Bennett's spider orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, spider-like appearance with long, slender petals and a prominent, curling lip. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in semi-shade to partial sunlight conditions. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a unique structure that aids in pollination by specific insect species.

Due to its rarity and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
