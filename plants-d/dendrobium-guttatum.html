Dendrobium guttatum

Dendrobium guttatum, commonly known as the spotted orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with multiple stems that produce numerous lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with distinctive red or purple spots on the petals and sepals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrobium guttatum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
