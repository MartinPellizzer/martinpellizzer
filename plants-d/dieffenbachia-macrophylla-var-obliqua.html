Dieffenbachia macrophylla var. obliqua

Dieffenbachia macrophylla var. obliqua, commonly known as the Shiny-leaf Dieffenbachia, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, broad, and glossy leaves with a striking variegation pattern. The leaves are typically a deep green with white or cream-colored veins, giving it an elegant appearance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to maintain its health.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
