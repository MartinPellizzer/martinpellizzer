Diuris unica

Diuris unica, commonly known as the Single-leaf Orchid, is a unique orchid species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its single, lance-shaped leaf and a single, striking orchid flower that typically blooms in spring. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and coastal areas. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow with a distinctive hooded labellum.

Diuris unica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable conditions.
