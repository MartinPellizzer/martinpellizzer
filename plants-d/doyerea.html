Doyerea

Doyerea, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its striking, cascading clusters of yellow flowers. The plant produces elongated, bean-like pods that contain edible seeds. Doyerea thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical and subtropical regions.
