Dypsis ovobontsira

Dypsis ovobontsira, commonly known as the Madagascar palm, is a striking plant native to Madagascar.

It is a type of cycad, characterized by its tall, unbranched stem and a crown of feathery, palm-like leaves. The plant produces cones rather than flowers, with male and female cones occurring on separate plants. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
