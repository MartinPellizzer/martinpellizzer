Dendrobium naqii

Dendrobium naqii, commonly known as the Kashmir orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Himalayan regions of Pakistan and India.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on the surfaces of trees and rocks without being parasitic. The plant features long, cane-like pseudobulbs covered in dense, leathery leaves. Its flowers are typically white or pale yellow, with a distinctive, fragrant scent.

Dendrobium naqii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
