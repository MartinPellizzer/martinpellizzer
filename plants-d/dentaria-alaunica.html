Dentaria alaunica

Dentaria alaunica, commonly known as the alpine toothplant, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Brassicaceae, which includes many economically important plants such as cabbage and mustard. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
