Dactylorchis maculata var. hunyadensis

Dactylorchis maculata var. hunyadensis, commonly known as the Hunyadensis Dactylorchis, is a rare orchid species native to Hungary.

It is characterized by its unique, spotted leaves and delicate, pale greenish-yellow flowers. This orchid typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and forest edges. The plant is a terrestrial orchid with a short rhizome and a single leaf that emerges from the base.
