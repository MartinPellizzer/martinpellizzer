Discocactus bahiensis

Discocactus bahiensis, commonly known as the Bahia discocactus, is a species of cactus native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are often covered in spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are borne on short pedicels. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
