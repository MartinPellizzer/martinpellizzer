Dombeya merika

Dombeya merika, commonly known as the African dombeya, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and are often borne in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface, and it produces small, oval berries that are edible for birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens in warm climates.

The flowers are also used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.
