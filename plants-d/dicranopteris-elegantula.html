Dicranopteris elegantula

Dicranopteris elegantula, commonly known as the elegant water fern, is a species of aquatic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, floating fronds that are often used in water gardens and ornamental ponds. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread across the water surface. Its leaves are pinnately divided, with a feathery appearance that enhances its aesthetic appeal.

Dicranopteris elegantula thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments and is valued for its ornamental and ecological benefits.
