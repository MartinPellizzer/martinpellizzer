Delphinium californicum

Delphinium californicum, commonly known as the California larkspur, is a herbaceous perennial native to the western United States.

It features tall, sparsely branched stems that bear clusters of blue to purple flowers. The plant has lance-shaped, toothed leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Delphinium californicum is part of the Ranunculaceae family, which includes other flowering plants with showy blooms.
