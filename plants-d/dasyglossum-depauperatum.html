Dasyglossum depauperatum

Dasyglossum depauperatum, commonly known as the poor-flowered dasyglossum, is a small, herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open, sandy soils and is often found in grassland habitats. The plant has slender, hairy stems and small, pale green leaves. Its flowers are clustered in dense, spike-like inflorescences and are usually pale yellow or greenish in color.

It is a relatively uncommon species, with limited distribution and specific ecological requirements.
