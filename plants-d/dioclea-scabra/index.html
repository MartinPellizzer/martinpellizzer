Dioclea Scabra

Dioclea scabra is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of South America. It is known for its striking, showy flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has been used in traditional medicine for centuries. Its unique growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular choice for gardens. Despite its beauty, it requires support to grow properly.
