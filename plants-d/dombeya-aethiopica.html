Dombeya aethiopica

Dombeya aethiopica, commonly known as the Ethiopian dombeya, is a flowering plant native to the highlands of Ethiopia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters tall, characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in clusters at the ends of the branches. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in moist, shaded areas of montane forests.

Dombeya aethiopica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
