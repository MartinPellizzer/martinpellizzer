Dianthus Orientalis Var. Angulatus

Dianthus orientalis var. angulatus is a striking member of the carnation family known for its vibrant pink and white petals. This rare variety is native to the mountainous regions of central Asia, where it thrives in rocky and well-drained soils. Its unique angular petals and delicate fragrance make it a favorite among botanists and gardeners alike. The plant is prized for its ornamental value and its ability to bloom in early spring. Due to habitat loss, it is now considered a vulnerable species in its natural range.
