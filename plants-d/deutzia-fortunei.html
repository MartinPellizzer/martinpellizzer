Deutzia fortunei

Deutzia fortunei, commonly known as Fortune's deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features clusters of white to pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has arching branches and small, oval-shaped leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and prefers well-drained soil.

Deutzia fortunei is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and easy maintenance.
