Daemonorops malaccensis

Daemonorops malaccensis, commonly known as the Malacca pandan, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and long, narrow leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in lowland forests. Its leaves are used in traditional practices and for culinary purposes.
