Danais pauciflora

Danais pauciflora, commonly known as the sparse-flowered danais, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that bear small, clustered flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Danais pauciflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
