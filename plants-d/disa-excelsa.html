Disa excelsa

Disa excelsa, commonly known as the African orchid or the noble orchid, is a striking perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Disa excelsa has lance-shaped leaves and produces a single flower spike with several blooms.

It is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
