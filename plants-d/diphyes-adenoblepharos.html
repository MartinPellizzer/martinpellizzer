Diphyes adenoblepharos

Diphyes adenoblepharos, commonly known as the silver leaf tree, is a species native to Madagascar.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows up to 10 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its dense, silvery-white foliage, which gives it its common name. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a leathery texture.

The tree produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by dark purple berries.
