Diplandra compressa

Diplandra compressa, commonly known as the narrowleaf diplandra, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in grasslands and rocky soils, often in regions with a temperate climate. The plant features slender, upright stems with small, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and clustered in loose inflorescences, usually ranging in color from pale yellow to orange.

Diplandra compressa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
