Dalzellia ceylanica var. konkanica

Dalzellia ceylanica var. konkanica, commonly known as the Konkan dalzellia, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a shrub that typically grows in moist, well-drained soils along forest edges and riverbanks. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually in inflorescences, and are typically greenish-white in color.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its foliage.
