Deutzia vilmoriniae

Deutzia vilmoriniae, commonly known as the Vilmorin's deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features slender, arching branches and clusters of white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has small, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Deutzia vilmoriniae is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
