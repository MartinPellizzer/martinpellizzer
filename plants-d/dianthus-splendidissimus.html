Dianthus splendidissimus

Dianthus splendidissimus, commonly known as the showy campion, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features slender, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are composed of five petals that are often arranged in a star-like pattern, with prominent stamens that add to its visual appeal.

Dianthus splendidissimus is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
