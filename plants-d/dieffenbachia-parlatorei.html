Dieffenbachia parlatorei

Dieffenbachia parlatorei, commonly known as the Parlatore's Dieffenbachia, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves that are often adorned with distinctive white or yellow markings. The plant is a member of the Araceae family, which includes other popular houseplants like peace lilies. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity to flourish.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental appeal.
