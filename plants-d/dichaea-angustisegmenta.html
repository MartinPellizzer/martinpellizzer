Dichaea angustisegmenta

Dichaea angustisegmenta, commonly known as the narrow-segmented dichaea, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, terrestrial growth habit. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a spike, typically blooming during the warmer months. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, and it thrives in shaded, moist environments.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in greenhouses or specialized orchid collections.
