Dendrobium shipmanii

Dendrobium shipmanii, commonly known as the Shipman's orchid, is a sympodial epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, branching stem system with numerous pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Dendrobium shipmanii is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in terrariums or mounted on bark.
