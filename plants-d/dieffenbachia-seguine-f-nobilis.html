Dieffenbachia seguine f. nobilis

Dieffenbachia seguine f. nobilis, commonly known as the Leopard Plant, is a member of the Araceae family and is native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that feature a mix of green, white, and yellow patterns. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are borne on a spadix, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its toxic sap, it should be handled with care and kept away from pets and children.
