Deutzia obtusilobata

Deutzia obtusilobata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its slender stems and clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The leaves are oval-shaped with rounded lobes, giving it a distinctive appearance. It prefers partial shade to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

This plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in a variety of garden settings.
