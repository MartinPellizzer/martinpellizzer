Delphinium dahuricum

Delphinium dahuricum, commonly known as the Siberian larkspur, is a tall perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It features striking blue to purple flowers arranged in dense spikes, which bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are deeply lobed, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy addition to gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its tall, showy inflorescences that attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
