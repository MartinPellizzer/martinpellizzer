Dendrochilum anfractoides

Dendrochilum anfractoides, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a distinctive zigzagging growth pattern in its stems. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically during the rainy season. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a rosette formation, aiding in water retention.

Dendrochilum anfractoides is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forest floors and rocky crevices.
