Dacrydium pancheri

Dacrydium pancheri, commonly known as the Pancheri silver pine, is a species of conifer native to the mountainous regions of northern Vietnam.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree, typically reaching heights of up to 15 meters. The plant features needle-like leaves that are dark green and arranged in spirals around the stems. Its cones are small and woody, with both male and female cones occurring on the same plant.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in horticultural settings for its unique foliage and resilience to adverse environmental conditions.
