Dendrobium dichrotropis

Dendrobium dichrotropis, commonly known as the black-leafed orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves and produces striking white to pale yellow flowers with a strong, sweet fragrance. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in horticulture.

Dendrobium dichrotropis requires specific care, including bright indirect light and well-draining potting media to thrive.
