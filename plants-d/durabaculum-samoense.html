Durabaculum samoense

Durabaculum samoense, commonly known as the Samoan rock cress, is a flowering plant native to the island of Samoa in the South Pacific.

The plant is characterized by its small, white or pale yellow flowers and basal rosette of hairy, lance-shaped leaves.
