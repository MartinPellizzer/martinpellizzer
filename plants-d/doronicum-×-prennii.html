Doronicum × prennii

Doronicum × prennii, commonly known as the hybrid leopard's bane, is a perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant yellow flowers. The plant features finely dissected, silvery-green leaves that add to its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its attractive appearance and ease of cultivation, it is often used in horticultural settings.
