Dendrobium undulatum var. woodfordianum

Dendrobium undulatum var. woodfordianum, commonly known as the Woodfordian orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, pendant-like inflorescences with fragrant, showy flowers that are typically white or pale pink. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with multiple stems forming over time. Its leaves are linear and often clustered at the base of the stems.

This variety is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
