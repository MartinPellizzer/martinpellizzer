Dentaria maxima

Dentaria maxima, commonly known as the large toothwort, is a flowering plant native to eastern North America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded woodland habitats. The plant features a distinctive, branched stem with alternate, heart-shaped leaves. Its showy, white to pale pink flowers bloom in late spring to early summer.

Dentaria maxima is known for its unique root system, which consists of tubers that store nutrients and aid in survival during harsh conditions.
