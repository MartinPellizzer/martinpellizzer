Dracaena lentiginosa

Dracaena lentiginosa, commonly known as the spotted dracaena, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and broad, dark green leaves with distinctive light green or yellowish spots. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by orange-red berries. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its adaptability and ornamental appeal.

Dracaena lentiginosa thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to maintain its health.
