Dicksonia lessonii

Dicksonia lessonii, commonly known as the giant tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant has a distinctive, hollow stem that is covered in a layer of dead, fibrous material. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Dicksonia lessonii plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
