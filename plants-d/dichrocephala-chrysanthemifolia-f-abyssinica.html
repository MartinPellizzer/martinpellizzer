Dichrocephala chrysanthemifolia f. abyssinica

Dichrocephala chrysanthemifolia f. abyssinica, commonly known as the Abyssinian chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
