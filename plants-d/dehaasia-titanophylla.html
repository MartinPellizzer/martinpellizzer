Dehaasia titanophylla

Dehaasia titanophylla, commonly known as the giant leaf plant, is a rare and striking species native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its enormous, glossy, heart-shaped leaves that can reach lengths of up to three meters. The plant thrives in high humidity and requires consistent moisture and indirect sunlight to flourish. Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for large indoor spaces or botanical gardens.

Due to its specific care requirements, it is considered a challenging plant to cultivate outside its natural habitat.
