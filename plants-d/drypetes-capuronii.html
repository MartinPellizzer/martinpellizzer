Drypetes capuronii

Drypetes capuronii, commonly known as the African soursop or African starfruit, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by fruits that are typically round or elongated.
