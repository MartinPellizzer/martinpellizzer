Dalechampia scandens var. mollis

Dalechampia scandens var. mollis, commonly known as softleaf dalechampia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a climbing shrub that produces large, heart-shaped leaves with a soft, velvety texture. The plant is characterized by its showy, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique foliage and ornamental value make it a popular choice for tropical gardens.
