Dendrobium stricklandianum

Dendrobium stricklandianum, commonly known as the Strickland's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, cane-like stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-draining soil.

Dendrobium stricklandianum is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
