Dodartia virgata

Dodartia virgata, commonly known as the slender dodder.

It is a parasitic flowering plant that lacks chlorophyll and relies on host plants for nutrients. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers and has a vine-like structure. It is native to parts of Asia and is often found growing on grasses and other herbaceous plants.

Due to its parasitic nature, it can cause significant damage to agricultural crops.
