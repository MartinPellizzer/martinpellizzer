Dendrobium vannouhuysii var. rhombipetalum

Dendrobium vannouhuysii var. rhombipetalum, commonly known as the rhombipetalum orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a sympodial orchid with a rhizome and produces multiple pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and is known for its showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white, yellow, and pink. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
