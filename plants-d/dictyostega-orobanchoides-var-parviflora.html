Dictyostega orobanchoides var. parviflora

Dictyostega orobanchoides var. parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered broom.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the family Orobanchaceae, characterized by its parasitic growth habit. The plant typically has slender, erect stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves. Its small, white to pinkish flowers are arranged in dense clusters at the tips of the branches.

It is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats, contributing to the ecological dynamics of its environment.
