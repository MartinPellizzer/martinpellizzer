Drimia brachystachys

Drimia brachystachys, commonly known as the narrow-stalked African lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features strap-like, narrow leaves that are typically green and often have a slight waxy coating. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale purple flowers that bloom in summer. It is often found in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils.

Drimia brachystachys is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
