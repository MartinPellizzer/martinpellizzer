Dendrochilum venustulum

Dendrochilum venustulum, commonly known as the Orchid of Beauty, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a pseudobulb that supports its evergreen leaves. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of pink and white, often with a contrasting lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its aesthetic appeal.
