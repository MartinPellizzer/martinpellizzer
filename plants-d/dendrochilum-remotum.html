Dendrochilum remotum

Dendrochilum remotum, commonly known as the distant orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are typically pale green to yellowish in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in montane forests.

Dendrochilum remotum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
