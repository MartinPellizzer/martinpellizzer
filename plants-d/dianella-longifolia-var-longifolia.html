Dianella longifolia var. longifolia

Dianella longifolia var. longifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved flax lily, is a perennial plant native to New Zealand.

It features grass-like, narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its attractive foliage and delicate floral display.
