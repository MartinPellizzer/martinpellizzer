Descantaria ionantha

Descantaria ionantha, commonly known as the flame flower, is a strikingly beautiful orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and orange flowers that resemble flames, which bloom during the warmer months. The plant has a compact growth habit with thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapting it to its often arid habitat. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
