Dyckia pernambucana

Dyckia pernambucana, commonly known as the Pernambuco dyckia, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its rosette of long, sword-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of white, woolly fibers. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences that bear showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Dyckia pernambucana is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
