Dendrobium subansiriense

Dendrobium subansiriense, commonly known as the Subansiri orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the eastern Himalayas.

It is characterized by its erect, leafy stems and produces clusters of fragrant, yellow-green flowers. The plant typically blooms in late winter to early spring, and its flowers are often adorned with intricate patterns. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
