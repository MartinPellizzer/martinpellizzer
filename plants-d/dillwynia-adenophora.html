Dillwynia adenophora

Dillwynia adenophora, commonly known as the silverleaf dillwynia, is a perennial herb native to southern Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, pale green leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery bloom. The plant produces clusters of tiny, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and woodlands.

Dillwynia adenophora is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
