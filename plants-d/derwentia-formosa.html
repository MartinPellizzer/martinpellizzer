Derwentia formosa

Derwentia formosa, commonly known as the New Zealand tree fern, is a distinctive evergreen plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Cyatheaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of large, feather-like fronds. Derwentia formosa is well adapted to the temperate and humid climates of New Zealand, where it thrives in shaded, moist environments.
