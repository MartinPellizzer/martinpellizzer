Dioscorea nummularia var. glabrescens

Dioscorea nummularia var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth yam, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its tuberous roots, which are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by cylindrical berries that contain the seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires a warm, humid climate for optimal growth.

Due to its medicinal properties, it is of interest in both botanical research and pharmacology.
