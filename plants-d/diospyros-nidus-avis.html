Diospyros nidus-avis

Diospyros nidus-avis, commonly known as the bird's nest ebony, is a species of flowering plant in the Ebenaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its dark, dense wood, which is highly valued for its strength and durability. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a glossy appearance.

The fruit is a drupe, typically small and containing a single seed.
