Diplopyramis aethiopica

Diplopyramis aethiopica, commonly known as the Ethiopian flame flower, is a climbing plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are typically clustered in racemes and bloom during the rainy season.
