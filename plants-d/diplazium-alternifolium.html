Diplazium alternifolium

Diplazium alternifolium, commonly known as the zigzag fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, alternate arrangement of fronds that often exhibit a zigzag pattern, giving it its common name. The plant features compound fronds with lanceolate, serrated leaflets that are typically dark green and glossy. Diplazium alternifolium is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, including India, China, and Southeast Asia.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forests and rocky areas.
