Diandranthus yunnanensis

Diandranthus yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan diandranthus, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils in mountainous regions. It features slender, branching stems with small, alternate leaves.

Its flowers are usually pink or red, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat.
