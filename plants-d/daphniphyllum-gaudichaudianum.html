Daphniphyllum gaudichaudianum

Daphniphyllum gaudichaudianum, commonly known as the Gaudichaud's laurel, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that are followed by bright red berries, which are an important food source for birds. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
