Draba sakuraii f. intermedia

Draba sakuraii f. intermedia, commonly known as the intermediate saxifrage, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and small, white or pale yellow flowers. Native to alpine and arctic regions, this species thrives in rocky and well-drained soils. The plant's morphology is adapted to cold climates, with a short growing season and compact growth habit.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in ecological studies of high-altitude ecosystems.
