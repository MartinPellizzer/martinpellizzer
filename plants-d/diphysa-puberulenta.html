Diphysa puberulenta

Diphysa puberulenta, commonly known as the woolly diphysa, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Diphysa puberulenta is also valued for its ornamental qualities and ability to attract pollinators.
