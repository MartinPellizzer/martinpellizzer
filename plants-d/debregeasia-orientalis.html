Debregeasia orientalis

Debregeasia orientalis, commonly known as the oriental debregeasia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apocynaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Ethiopia and is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The flowers bloom in clusters and are often visited by bees and other pollinators, contributing to its ecological importance in its native habitat.
