Deutzia scabra var. heterotricha

Deutzia scabra var. heterotricha, commonly known as the rough deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features wiry, grayish stems and small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in late spring. The leaves are typically ovate with a rough texture, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability in temperate climates.
