Disporum chinense var. pullum

Disporum chinense var. pullum, commonly known as the Chinese spathephyllum, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It features a rhizomatous root system and produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers during the spring. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and conservation efforts.
