Digitaria keniensis

Digitaria keniensis, commonly known as Kenyan digitaria, is a perennial grass native to East Africa.

It typically grows in open grasslands and along riverbanks, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant has slender, erect stems and small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a rough texture.

Digitaria keniensis is often used in traditional medicine and as a forage crop for livestock.
