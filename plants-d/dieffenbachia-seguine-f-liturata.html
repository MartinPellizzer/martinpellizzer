Dieffenbachia seguine f. liturata

Dieffenbachia seguine f. liturata, commonly known as the Shiny-leaf Dieffenbachia, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that are glossy and often have a striking white or silver midrib. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions. Its foliage is highly ornamental, making it a popular houseplant.

It requires moderate watering and well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
