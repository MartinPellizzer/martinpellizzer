Desmodium barbatum var. venustulum

Desmodium barbatum var. venustulum, commonly known as the velvet bean or hairy senna, is a leguminous plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves with hairy surfaces and yellow to orange flowers that bloom in racemes. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits containing several elliptical seeds. It is often cultivated for its protein-rich seeds, which are used in animal feed and traditional food preparations.

Additionally, it has been studied for its potential medicinal properties due to its rich content of flavonoids and other bioactive compounds.
