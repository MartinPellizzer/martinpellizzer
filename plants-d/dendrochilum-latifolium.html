Dendrochilum latifolium

Dendrochilum latifolium, commonly known as the broad-leafed tree orchid, is a tropical orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves and clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers bloom in spring and are known for their unique, spurred petals.

Dendrochilum latifolium is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its role in local ecosystems.
