Dolichos sinensis var. rubiginosus

Dolichos sinensis var. rubiginosus, commonly known as the red-staining lablab, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features compound leaves with three leaflets, and its flowers are typically violet or purple in color. The plant produces elongated, bean-like pods that contain edible seeds. It is valued for its nutritional content and is often used in traditional cuisines.

Botanically, it is characterized by its hairy stems and the presence of a prominent seed coat.
