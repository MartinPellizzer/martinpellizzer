Dendrobium crenatilabre var. seranicum

Dendrobium crenatilabre var. seranicum, commonly known as the Seranica Dendrobium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The plant features long, wiry stems that can reach up to several feet in length. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive, wavy lip that adds to its ornamental appeal.

It is valued in horticulture for its beauty and adaptability to various growing conditions.
