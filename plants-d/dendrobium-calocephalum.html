Dendrobium calocephalum

Dendrobium calocephalum, commonly known as the helmet orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with cane-like stems that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with a distinctive helmet-shaped lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium calocephalum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in both greenhouse and outdoor settings.
