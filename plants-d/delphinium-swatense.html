Delphinium swatense

Delphinium swatense, commonly known as the Swat delphinium, is a striking herbaceous perennial native to the Swat Valley in Pakistan.

It is characterized by its tall, spindly stems that bear clusters of vivid blue to purple spurred flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with lance-shaped leaves that are deeply lobed. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine or subalpine regions.

Botanically, it belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance.
