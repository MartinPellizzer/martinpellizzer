Draba litamo

Draba litamo, commonly known as the Litamo rockcress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a small, perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or alpine environments. The plant produces tiny, white or pale yellow flowers in early spring, often before the leaves fully emerge. Its leaves are basal, rosette-like, and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Draba litamo is adapted to harsh conditions, making it a resilient species found in mountainous regions of Europe.
