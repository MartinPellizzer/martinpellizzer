Desmos lawii

Desmos lawii, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow, pendulous flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators like bees and birds. The plant has simple, opposite leaves and produces small, elliptical fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Desmos lawii is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
