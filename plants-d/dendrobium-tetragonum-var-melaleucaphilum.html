Dendrobium tetragonum var. melaleucaphilum

Dendrobium tetragonum var. melaleucaphilum, commonly known as the Melaleuca-philum Dendrobium, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with multiple stems that produce linear, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive spurred lip. It thrives in shaded, humid environments with well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
