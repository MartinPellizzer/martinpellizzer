Deutzia hookeriana var. macrophylla

Deutzia hookeriana var. macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaved deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy clusters of white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are broader than the typical species, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value comes from both its foliage and floral display, making it a popular choice for landscaping.
