Ditassa aequicymosa

Ditassa aequicymosa, commonly known as the narrow-leafed dittany, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.
