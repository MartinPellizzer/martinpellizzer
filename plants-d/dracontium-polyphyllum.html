Dracontium polyphyllum

Dracontium polyphyllum, commonly known as the many-leaved arrowroot, is a herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features a short, rhizomatous stem and produces large, heart-shaped leaves that are often deeply lobed. The plant is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with a red or purple throat. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
