Dioscorea rotundifoliolata

Dioscorea rotundifoliolata, commonly known as the African yam bean, is a climbing vine native to Africa.

It belongs to the Dioscoreaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and yellowish-orange flowers. The plant produces tubers that are edible and have a starchy, nutty flavor. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, well-drained soils.

Its botanical features make it an important crop in some regions for both food and traditional medicine.
