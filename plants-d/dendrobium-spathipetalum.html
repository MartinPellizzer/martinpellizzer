Dendrobium spathipetalum

Dendrobium spathipetalum, commonly known as the Spathipetalum orchid, is a member of the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with a distinctive spatulate-shaped lip.

It requires bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
