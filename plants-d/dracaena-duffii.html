Dracaena duffii

Dracaena duffii, commonly known as the Corn Plant, is a popular indoor plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and long, narrow, dark green leaves that often have a slight sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy fruits. Dracaena duffii is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its air-purifying properties.

It is also valued for its ease of care and adaptability to various indoor environments.
