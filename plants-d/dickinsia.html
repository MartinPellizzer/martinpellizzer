Dickinsia

Dickinsia, commonly known as the rock daisy, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the family Asteraceae.

These plants are native to South America, particularly in regions of Chile and Argentina. Their ability to thrive in harsh conditions makes them an important part of their native ecosystems.
