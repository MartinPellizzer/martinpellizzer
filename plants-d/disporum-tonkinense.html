Disporum tonkinense

Disporum tonkinense, commonly known as the Tonkin disporum, is a member of the Colchicaceae family.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its clustered, grass-like foliage and distinctive floral structures. Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticultural and ecological studies.
