Dipladenia achrestogyne

Dipladenia achrestogyne, commonly known as the golden trumpet vine, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a vigorous climber with long, trailing stems that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in warm, sunny conditions and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Dipladenia achrestogyne is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
