Diuris perialla

Diuris perialla, commonly known as the Purple Sun-orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils in open woodland or heathland environments. The plant features a compact, low-growing habit with a single, erect stem bearing several hairy leaves. Its most distinctive feature is its showy, violet to purple flowers that are clustered in a raceme.

The flowers have a unique structure with a labellum that is often adorned with intricate markings.
