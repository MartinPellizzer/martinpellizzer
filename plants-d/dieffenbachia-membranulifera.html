Dieffenbachia membranulifera

Dieffenbachia membranulifera, commonly known as the velvet leaf dieffenbachia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with white or cream-colored veins. The plant has a short, thick stem and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are rarely seen. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Its striking foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
