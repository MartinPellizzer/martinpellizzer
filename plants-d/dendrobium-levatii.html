Dendrobium levatii

Dendrobium levatii, commonly known as the yellow-lipped orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, cane-like pseudobulbs and long, narrow leaves. The plant produces pendulous, showy flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a distinctive lip that resembles a lip shape. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrobium levatii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
