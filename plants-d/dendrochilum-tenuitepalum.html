Dendrochilum tenuitepalum

Dendrochilum tenuitepalum, commonly known as the slender-leafed tree orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and long, narrow leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in a raceme, which are typical of the Dendrochilum genus. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its delicate appearance and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
