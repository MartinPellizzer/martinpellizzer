Dendranthema seticuspe f. borealis

Dendranthema seticuspe f. borealis, commonly known as the northern chrysanthemum, is a frost-tolerant perennial plant native to northern regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and displays a range of flower colors, including white, yellow, and orange. The plant features narrow, gray-green leaves with a woolly texture, and its flowers are typically small and clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

This cultivar is valued for its hardiness and ornamental appeal in colder climates.
