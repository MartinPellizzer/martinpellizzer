Dianthus pungens var. multiaffinis

Dianthus pungens var. multiaffinis, commonly known as the multiaffinis campfire carnation, is a hybrid variety of the campfire carnation.

It is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting flowers that come in a range of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant is a herbaceous perennial, typically growing to a height of 12 to 18 inches. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its foliage is covered in soft, fuzzy leaves that add texture to the garden landscape.
