Delphinium sinense

Delphinium sinense, commonly known as Chinese larkspur, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It features tall, sparsely branched stems that bear clusters of blue to purple flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its striking floral display and its ability to attract pollinators.
