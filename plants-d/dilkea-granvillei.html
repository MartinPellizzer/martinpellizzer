Dilkea granvillei

Dilkea granvillei, commonly known as the Granville's daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellow central disks surrounded by white ray florets.

Dilkea granvillei is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
