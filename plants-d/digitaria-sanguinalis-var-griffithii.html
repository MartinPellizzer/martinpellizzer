Digitaria sanguinalis var. griffithii

Digitaria sanguinalis var. griffithii, commonly known as red panicgrass, is a variant of the grass species Digitaria sanguinalis.

It is characterized by its reddish coloration in the inflorescence and leaves, which distinguishes it from other varieties. This plant is typically found in disturbed soils and open areas, thriving in temperate to subtropical climates. It has a spreading growth habit and produces dense clusters of spikelets.

The botanical features of Digitaria sanguinalis var. griffithii make it a common weed in agricultural and natural settings.
