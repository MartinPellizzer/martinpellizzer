Dactylorchis majalis var. laxa

Dactylorchis majalis var. laxa, commonly known as the laxa orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and thrives in rich, organic soils. The plant features a slender rhizome with a single, erect stem that bears several lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and clustered in a loose inflorescence, often appearing in late spring to early summer.

This variety is noted for its delicate appearance and is often found in association with other orchid species in its habitat.
