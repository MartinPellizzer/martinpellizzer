Delphinium × hendersonii

Delphinium × hendersonii, commonly known as the Henderson larkspur, is a hybrid perennial plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that bear clusters of striking blue to purple flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are deeply lobed and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and ecological benefits to pollinators.
