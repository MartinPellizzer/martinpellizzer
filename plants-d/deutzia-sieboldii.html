Deutzia sieboldii

Deutzia sieboldii, commonly known as Siebold's deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features clusters of white to pink flowers that bloom in early summer, adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant has slender, arching branches and elliptical leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Deutzia sieboldii is valued for its showy flowers and adaptability to various garden conditions.
