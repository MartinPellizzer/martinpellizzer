Dahlia purpurea var. flavescens

Dahlia purpurea var. flavescens, commonly known as the yellow dahlia, is a hybrid cultivar of the dahlia genus.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers, which often feature a subtle gradient towards the center. The plant is a herbaceous perennial, known for its sturdy stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

This variety is prized for its showy blooms and adaptability to various growing conditions.
