Drosanthemum albiflorum

Drosanthemum albiflorum, commonly known as the white-flowered drosanthemum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems and clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has thick, water-storing leaves that help it survive in arid conditions. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun exposure.

Drosanthemum albiflorum is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
