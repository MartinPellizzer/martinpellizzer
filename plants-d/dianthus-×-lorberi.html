Dianthus × lorberi

Dianthus × lorberi, commonly known as the hybrid carnation, is a popular ornamental plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is a hybrid between Dianthus chinensis and Dianthus barbatus, resulting in a diverse range of cultivars with varied flower colors and forms. The plant typically features showy, double or semi-double flowers in shades of pink, red, white, and purple, often with frilled petals. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden borders and containers.

Dianthus × lorberi is valued for its long blooming period and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
