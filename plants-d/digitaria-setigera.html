Digitaria setigera

Digitaria setigera, commonly known as hairy fingergrass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves with a hairy texture. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Digitaria setigera is an important plant in some ecosystems, contributing to soil stabilization and serving as a food source for various animals.
