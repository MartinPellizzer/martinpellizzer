Dracocephalum thymifolium

Dracocephalum thymifolium, commonly known as Persian bugloss, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, blue or violet flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and often have a slight fragrance, contributing to its use in traditional medicine.

Dracocephalum thymifolium is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly in treating respiratory and digestive ailments.
