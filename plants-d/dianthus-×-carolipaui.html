Dianthus × carolipaui

Dianthus × carolipaui, commonly known as the hybrid carnation, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is a cross between Dianthus chinensis and Dianthus barbatus, resulting in a plant with vibrant, showy blooms. The flowers typically feature five petals that are pink, red, or white, with a distinctive frilly appearance. It is a herbaceous perennial that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

This plant is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
