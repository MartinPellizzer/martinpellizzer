Dicoma schimperi

Dicoma schimperi, commonly known as the Ethiopian daisy, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It has small, narrow leaves and produces numerous small flowers that bloom in clusters.

Dicoma schimperi is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
