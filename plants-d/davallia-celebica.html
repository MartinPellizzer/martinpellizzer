Davallia celebica

Davallia celebica, commonly known as the Celebes spleenwort, is a fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Davalliaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often on rocks or tree trunks. Its rhizomes are wiry and help anchor it to its substrate.

The leaves are usually light green and have a feathery appearance, contributing to its ornamental value.
