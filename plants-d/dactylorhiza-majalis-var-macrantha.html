Dactylorhiza majalis var. macrantha

Dactylorhiza majalis var. macrantha, commonly known as the greater marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, calcareous soils in wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks. The plant features a rhizomatous root system and produces tall, erect stems with lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

It is a perennial species that blooms from late spring to early summer.
