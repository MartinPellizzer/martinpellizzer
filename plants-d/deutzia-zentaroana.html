Deutzia zentaroana

Deutzia zentaroana, commonly known as the Japanese deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features showy, fragrant clusters of white to pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has slender, arching branches and dark green, elliptical leaves. It typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and thrives in partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
