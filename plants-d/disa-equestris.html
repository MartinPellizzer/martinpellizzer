Disa equestris

Disa equestris, commonly known as the rainbow orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family native to southern Africa.

This plant is characterized by its tall, erect stem and an array of colorful, showy flowers that range from violet to blue and yellow. The flowers are typically arranged in a raceme and feature a unique, intricate structure with specialized petals and sepals. Disa equestris thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grassland habitats.

It is a popular ornamental plant due to its vibrant colors and elegant appearance.
