Dinebra Aegyptiaca

Egyptian spurge, scientifically known as Dinebra aegyptiaca, is a striking plant native to the arid regions of North Africa. It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers and succulent, fleshy leaves that help it survive in harsh desert conditions. This plant has been used for centuries in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties. Despite its beauty, it contains toxic compounds that require caution when handling. Its resilience and adaptability make it a fascinating subject for botanical study.
