Delosperma platysepalum

Delosperma platysepalum, commonly known as the ice plant or silver leaf ice plant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine, powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces vibrant pink to purple flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ability to store water in its thick leaves allows it to survive in arid conditions.
