Dendranthema okiense

Dendranthema okiense, commonly known as the Japanese chamomile, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has finely divided, feather-like leaves. It produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Dendranthema okiense is often used in traditional medicine for its calming and anti-inflammatory properties.
