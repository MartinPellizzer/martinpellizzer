Dinebra Retroflexa

Himalayan blue poppy, scientifically known as Dinebra retroflexa, is a rare and striking flower native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas. With its vivid blue petals and delicate appearance, it stands out among the rugged mountain terrain where it thrives. This species is celebrated for its beauty and is often considered one of the most visually stunning wildflowers in the world. Despite its allure, it is highly sensitive to environmental changes and faces threats from habitat loss and climate change. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this iconic plant for future generations.
