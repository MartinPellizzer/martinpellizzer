Dovea macrocarpa

Dovea macrocarpa, commonly known as the giant dove tree, is a unique plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that resemble doves in flight, hence its common name. The plant has a distinctive, tree-like structure with smooth, gray bark and broad, palmate leaves. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and tropical landscapes.
