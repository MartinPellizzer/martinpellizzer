Dioscorea collettii var. collettii

Dioscorea collettii var. collettii, commonly known as the Collett's yam, is a tuberous plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its stolons and tuberous roots that store nutrients. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in temperate to cold climates. Its leaves are heart-shaped and large, while its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female blooms.

The tuber is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
