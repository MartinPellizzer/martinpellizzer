Dioscorea blumei

Dioscorea blumei, commonly known as the Blume yam, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by tuberous roots that are used in traditional medicine and as a food source.

Its growth habit and morphology make it an important species in both ecological and agricultural contexts.
