Dendrobium acrobaticum

Dendrobium acrobaticum, commonly known as the sky orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing on trees or rocks. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and yellow. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, helping it survive in its natural habitat.

Dendrobium acrobaticum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
