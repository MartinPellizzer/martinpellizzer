Dactylorhiza × sivasiana

Dactylorhiza × sivasiana, commonly known as the Sivas orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

It is a terrestrial herb with a rhizomatous root system and produces tall, slender stems bearing lance-shaped leaves. The plant flowers in late spring to early summer, producing spikes of pale yellow to greenish-white flowers with a distinct lip. It prefers moist, calcareous soils and is often found in woodland or grassland habitats.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
