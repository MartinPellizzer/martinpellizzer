Doratolepis

Doratolepis, commonly known as the golden ragwort, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and hairy, often glandular leaves. These plants typically grow in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. Doratolepis species are often found in temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.
