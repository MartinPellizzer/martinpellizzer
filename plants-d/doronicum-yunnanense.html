Doronicum yunnanense

Doronicum yunnanense, commonly known as Yunnan leopard's ear, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy, spoon-shaped leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests. It has a compact growth habit and is often found at high elevations.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is not widely distributed outside its native range.
