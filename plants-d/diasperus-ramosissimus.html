Diasperus ramosissimus

Diasperus ramosissimus, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has a bushy growth habit with numerous branches, giving it a dense appearance. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

Diasperus ramosissimus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
