Drimys novoguineensis

Drimys novoguineensis, commonly known as the New Guinea drimys, is a flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of New Guinea.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows to a height of about 3 to 5 meters. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptic to ovate in shape, with a glossy upper surface and a lighter-colored underside. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and clustered in terminal inflorescences, while the fruit is a small, fleshy drupe.

Drimys novoguineensis is adapted to humid, shaded environments and plays a role in the ecological dynamics of its native habitat.
