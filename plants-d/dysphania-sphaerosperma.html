Dysphania sphaerosperma

Dysphania sphaerosperma, commonly known as bladder campion, is an annual herbaceous plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its prostrate or ascending growth habit. The plant features small, greenish-white flowers and produces spherical, inflated seed pods that aid in seed dispersal. Its leaves are typically oval or lance-shaped with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Dysphania sphaerosperma is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in arid or semi-arid environments.
