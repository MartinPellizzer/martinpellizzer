Diplazium praestans

Diplazium praestans, commonly known as the creeping sword fern, is a member of the Dryopteridaceae family.

It is a terrestrial fern characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and delicate, lanceolate fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its fronds are bi-lobed and have a distinctive, elegant appearance.

Diplazium praestans is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in horticulture and landscape design.
