Dendrobium bigibbum var. superbiens

Dendrobium bigibbum var. superbiens, commonly known as the Superb Dendrobium, is a flowering orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic, typically in tropical rainforests. The plant produces long, pendulous inflorescences with numerous fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and ease of care.
