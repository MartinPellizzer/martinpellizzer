Dolichos carnosus

Dolichos carnosus, commonly known as the hairy bean, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems covered in fine, hairy hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by elongated, pod-like fruits containing several seeds. Dolichos carnosus is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions.

Its botanical features make it a resilient and hardy species within its native range.
