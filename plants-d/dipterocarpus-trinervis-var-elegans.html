Dipterocarpus trinervis var. elegans

Dipterocarpus trinervis var. elegans, commonly known as the elegant dipterocarp, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Dipterocarpaceae family and is characterized by its large, glossy leaves with three prominent veins. The plant produces heavy, woody fruits that contain winged seeds, typical of many dipterocarps. It thrives in humid, lowland forests and is often found in primary tropical rainforests.

Due to its ecological importance and ornamental value, it is also cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation areas.
