Drynaria irioides

Drynaria irioides, commonly known as the golden tree fern, is a tropical evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feathery, light green fronds. The plant has a distinctive, tree-like appearance with a central stem that develops a rough, bark-like covering. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for tropical gardens and indoor plant collections.
