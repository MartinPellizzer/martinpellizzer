Delphinium brunonianum var. densum

Delphinium brunonianum var. densum, commonly known as the dense delphinium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant produces showy, blue to violet-colored flowers in dense clusters at the top of the stem, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are compound, with deeply lobed foliage that is often covered in fine hairs.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
