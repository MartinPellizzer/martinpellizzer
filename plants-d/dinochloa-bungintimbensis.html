Dinochloa bungintimbensis

Dinochloa bungintimbensis, commonly known as the Bungintimbens grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia. This plant is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
