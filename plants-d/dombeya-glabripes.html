Dombeya glabripes

Dombeya glabripes, commonly known as the smoothleaf dombeya, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a shrub that typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or streamside habitats. The plant produces showy, fragrant, bell-shaped flowers that are usually pink or white in color. Its leaves are smooth and elliptical, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Dombeya glabripes is valued in gardens for its attractive blooms and ability to thrive in partial shade.
