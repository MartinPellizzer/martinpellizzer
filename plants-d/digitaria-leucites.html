Digitaria leucites

Digitaria leucites, commonly known as silverhair grass, is a perennial grass species native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-gray leaves and dense, tufted growth habit. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers.

Digitaria leucites is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its adaptability and ability to stabilize soil.
