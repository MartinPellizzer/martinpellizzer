Dendromecon caesia

Dendromecon caesia, commonly known as the golden ray, is a perennial shrub native to the western United States.

It features yellow, ray-like flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a silvery underside, and its stems are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

The plant is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
