Deccania pubescens var. candolleana

Deccania pubescens var. candolleana, commonly known as the Candolleana shrub, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, which contribute to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in dense undergrowth.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
