Diuris brockmanii

Diuris brockmanii, commonly known as the Brockman's spider-orchid, is a striking orchid species native to Western Australia.

It features a slender, erect stem with a single, lance-shaped leaf and a single, showy flower that resembles a spider in appearance. The flower has a dark violet to purple coloration with intricate patterns and a distinctive, elongated labellum. It blooms during the late winter to early spring season, typically between June and August.

This species is adapted to grow in sandy soils and is often found in heathlands and open woodlands.
