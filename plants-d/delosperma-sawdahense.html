Delosperma sawdahense

Delosperma sawdahense, commonly known as the Namib Desert ice plant, is a succulent species native to the arid regions of Namibia.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in harsh, dry environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink and purple during the rainy season. It is adapted to high sunlight exposure and can tolerate extreme temperature fluctuations.

Its unique morphology and resilience make it an important part of the Namib Desert ecosystem.
