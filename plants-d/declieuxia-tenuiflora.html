Declieuxia tenuiflora

Declieuxia tenuiflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered lily, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South America.

It features grass-like leaves that are narrow and linear, often arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in a spike-like inflorescence. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or woodland settings.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is sometimes cultivated in horticultural collections.
