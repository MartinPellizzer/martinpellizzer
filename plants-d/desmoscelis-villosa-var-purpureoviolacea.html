Desmoscelis villosa var. purpureoviolacea

Desmoscelis villosa var. purpureoviolacea, commonly known as the purple viola desmoscelis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves that are often covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces showy, violet-colored flowers that bloom in clusters, adding ornamental value to gardens. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice among horticultural enthusiasts.
