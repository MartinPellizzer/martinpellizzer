Dendrobium speciosum var. blackdownense

Dendrobium speciosum var. blackdownense, commonly known as the Blackdown orchid, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is an epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange. It is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms.

The variety is characterized by its unique floral structure and adaptability to specific environmental conditions.
