Dyckia leptostachya

Dyckia leptostachya, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed dyckia, is a species of bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its rosette of stiff, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, white to pale blue flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Dyckia leptostachya is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
