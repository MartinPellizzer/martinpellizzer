Dioscorea nummularia

Dioscorea nummularia, commonly known as the silverleaf yam, is a climbing herbaceous perennial native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces tuberous roots that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires a warm, humid climate for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value comes from its striking foliage, making it a popular choice for gardens and greenhouses.
