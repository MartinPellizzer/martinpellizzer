Disporum chinense var. parviflorum

Disporum chinense var. parviflorum, commonly known as the small-flowered disporum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of small, white, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its flowers are often adorned with a distinctive greenish or reddish hue at the base.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
