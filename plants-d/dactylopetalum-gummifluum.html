Dactylopetalum gummifluum

Dactylopetalum gummifluum, commonly known as the gum tree of Madagascar, is a member of the Sapotaceae family.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to the island of Madagascar. The plant is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and elliptical, leathery leaves. It produces small, yellowish flowers followed by fleshy, orange-red fruits.
