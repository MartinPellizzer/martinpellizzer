Digitaria cognata var. arenicola

Digitaria cognata var. arenicola, commonly known as sand dropseed, is a grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in sandy or well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Sand dropseed is drought-tolerant and is considered a valuable plant for stabilizing sandy soils.
