Delphinium cyphoplectrum var. stenophyllum

Delphinium cyphoplectrum var. stenophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved larkspur, is a member of the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems and clusters of blue to purple flowers. The leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture. Native to the mountainous regions of central Asia, it thrives in cool, moist environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens and its ecological role in supporting pollinators.
