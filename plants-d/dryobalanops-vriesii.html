Dryobalanops vriesii

Dryobalanops vriesii, commonly known as the Malayan ironwood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, heavy wood that is highly valued for its strength and durability. The plant has a straight trunk and a broad, spreading crown with glossy, dark green leaves. It produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by woody, capsule-like fruits.

This species is often found in lowland and hill dipterocarp forests, contributing to the biodiversity of these ecosystems.
