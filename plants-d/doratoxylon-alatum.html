Doratoxylon alatum

Doratoxylon alatum, commonly known as the winged burl.

It is a unique plant characterized by its distinctive, flattened, wing-like structures that form around its stem. The plant is native to arid and semi-arid regions, where it has adapted to survive in harsh, dry conditions. Its botanical features include a woody stem with narrow, elongated leaves that reduce water loss.

The plant is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil in such environments.
