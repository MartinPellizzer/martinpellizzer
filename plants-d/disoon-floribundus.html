Disoon floribundus

Disoon floribundus, commonly known as the tropical flowering plant, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of pink and white. The plant has a woody stem and thrives in warm, humid climates with plenty of sunlight. Its leaves are typically broad and glossy, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Disoon floribundus is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and as a houseplant.
