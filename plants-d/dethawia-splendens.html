Dethawia splendens

Dethawia splendens, commonly known as the Splendid Dethawia, is a rare and striking plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from deep red to bright yellow, often with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are broad and glossy, providing a lush backdrop to its dramatic blooms.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
