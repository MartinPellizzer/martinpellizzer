Dracaena rockii

Dracaena rockii, commonly known as the Rockii Dracaena, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and long, narrow leaves that are typically green with a subtle variegation. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by red or orange berries, adding a touch of color to its appearance.

Dracaena rockii is also valued for its air-purifying properties and its ability to adapt to various environmental conditions.
