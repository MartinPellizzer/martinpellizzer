Dryopteris jishouensis

Dryopteris jishouensis, commonly known as the Jishou wood fern, is a species of fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests and rocky slopes. Its rhizomes are often covered in a layer of brownish spores, which contribute to its reproductive cycle.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
