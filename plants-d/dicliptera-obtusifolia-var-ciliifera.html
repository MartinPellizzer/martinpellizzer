Dicliptera obtusifolia var. ciliifera

Dicliptera obtusifolia var. ciliifera, commonly known as the silky dicliptera, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit.
