Dichapetalum obovatum

Dichapetalum obovatum, commonly known as the African velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, obovate leaves that are covered in a dense layer of fine, velvety hairs. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Dichapetalum obovatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
