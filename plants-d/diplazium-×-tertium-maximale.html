Diplazium × tertium-maximale

Diplazium × tertium-maximale, commonly known as the hybrid fern, is a sterile hybrid resulting from the cross between Diplazium tertium and Diplazium maximale.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it does not produce viable seeds.
