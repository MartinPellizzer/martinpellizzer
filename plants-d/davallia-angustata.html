Davallia angustata

Davallia angustata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf hartioca, is a fern native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It features delicate, pinnately compound fronds with a slender, arching habit. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and prefers well-draining, humus-rich soil. Its rhizomes are wiry and often grow close to the soil surface.

It is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
