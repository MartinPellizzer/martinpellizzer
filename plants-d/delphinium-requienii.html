Delphinium requienii

Delphinium requienii, commonly known as the Kashmir blue larkspur, is a striking perennial plant native to the Himalayas.

It features tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant produces showy, blue to purple flowers in racemes, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a rough texture, with a distinctive blue-green hue.

Delphinium requienii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
