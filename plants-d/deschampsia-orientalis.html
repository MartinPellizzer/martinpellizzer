Deschampsia orientalis

Deschampsia orientalis, commonly known as oriental feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage and slender, arching stems that produce delicate, feathery seed heads. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
