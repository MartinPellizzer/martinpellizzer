Dactylorhiza × wiefelspuetziana

Dactylorhiza × wiefelspuetziana, commonly known as the Wiefelspuetz orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Europe.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of pale yellow to greenish-white flowers. The plant is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in moist, shaded woodland environments. It has a complex root system and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a protected species in several European countries.
