Dendrobium × superbiens nothovar. superbiens

Dendrobium × superbiens nothovar. superbiens, commonly known as the Superb Dendrobium, is a hybrid orchid species originating from crosses between Dendrobium nobile and Dendrobium phalaenopsis.

It is an epiphytic plant characterized by its sympodial growth habit, featuring long, cane-like stems that bear clusters of fleshy, elliptical leaves. The flowers are typically large, showy, and come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and yellow, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

This hybrid is prized for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation in both indoor and greenhouse settings.
