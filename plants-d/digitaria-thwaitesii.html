Digitaria thwaitesii

Digitaria thwaitesii, commonly known as the slender fingergrass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands, savannas, and disturbed soils. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny florets. It is adapted to warm climates and can thrive in low-nutrient soils.

Digitaria thwaitesii is often found in regions with seasonal rainfall patterns.
