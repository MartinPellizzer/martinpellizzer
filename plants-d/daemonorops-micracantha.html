Daemonorops micracantha

Daemonorops micracantha, commonly known as the slender palm or small-leafed palm, is a species of cycad-like plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, unbranched stem and a crown of narrow, feather-like leaves. The plant produces small, cone-like structures that contain both male and female reproductive organs. It thrives in tropical and subtropical regions with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
