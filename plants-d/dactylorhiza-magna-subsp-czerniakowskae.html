Dactylorhiza magna subsp. czerniakowskae

Dactylorhiza magna subsp.

czerniakowskae, commonly known as the Czerniakowska's large marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Europe. It typically grows in wet, calcareous habitats such as marshes and along riverbanks.

The plant features a rhizome with several leaves and produces tall, branched stems bearing numerous flowers. Its flowers are usually pink to purple in color, with a distinctive lip that is often darker and more prominent.

This subspecies is noted for its specific ecological requirements and is considered a rare orchid in its native range.
