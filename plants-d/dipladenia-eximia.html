Dipladenia eximia

Dipladenia eximia, commonly known as the flame vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from orange to red. The plant produces showy, fragrant blooms that attract pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees. Its foliage consists of opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly waxy texture.

Dipladenia eximia thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil and ample sunlight for optimal growth.
