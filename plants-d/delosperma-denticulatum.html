Delosperma denticulatum

Delosperma denticulatum, commonly known as the toothed ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs, which help reduce water loss. The plant produces vibrant pink to purple flowers in late spring to early summer. It is well adapted to arid environments and thrives in full sun.

Delosperma denticulatum is often used in rock gardens and as a ground cover due to its hardiness and ornamental value.
