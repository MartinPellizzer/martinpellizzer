Dinteracanthus acetabulus

Dinteracanthus acetabulus, commonly known as the Cape goldcup, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

The plant is characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
