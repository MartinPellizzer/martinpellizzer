Dillenia indica

Dillenia indica, commonly known as the Indian cucumber tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia and the Indian subcontinent.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, yellowish-white flowers that resemble cucumbers. The plant produces fleshy, spiny fruits that contain numerous seeds. Dillenia indica is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional medicinal uses in some cultures.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is typically found in forested areas.
