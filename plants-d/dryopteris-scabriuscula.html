Dryopteris scabriuscula

Dryopteris scabriuscula, commonly known as the rough shield fern, is a species of fern native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its robust, upright fronds that are typically dark green and have a rough, scaly texture. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and rocky areas. Its spores are produced on the underside of the fronds in clusters called sori.

Dryopteris scabriuscula is a hardy perennial that is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive foliage and resilience.
