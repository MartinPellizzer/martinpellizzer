Deutzia × carnea f. lactea

Deutzia × carnea f. lactea, commonly known as the pink deutzia, is a deciduous shrub characterized by its showy, clusters of pink flowers that bloom in early spring.

It is a hybrid species within the genus Deutzia, which belongs to the family Caprifoliaceae. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and features arching stems with opposite, ovate leaves that are medium green in color. The plant prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a versatile choice for landscape design.
