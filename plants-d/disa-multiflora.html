Disa multiflora

Disa multiflora, commonly known as the many-flowered disa, is a species of orchid native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers a sunny to partially shaded environment. Its inflorescence consists of multiple flowers arranged in a spiral, and it blooms during the summer months.

Disa multiflora is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
