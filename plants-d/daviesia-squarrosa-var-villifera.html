Daviesia squarrosa var. villifera

Daviesia squarrosa var. villifera, commonly known as the slender Daviesia, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It features narrow, needle-like leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in spikes, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
