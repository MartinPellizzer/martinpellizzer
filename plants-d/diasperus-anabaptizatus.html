Diasperus anabaptizatus

It is characterized by its succulent, ribbed stems and opposite, ovate leaves that are typically green with a slight waxy coating. It is native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa and is adapted to thrive in harsh, dry environments.

Diasperus anabaptizatus is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
