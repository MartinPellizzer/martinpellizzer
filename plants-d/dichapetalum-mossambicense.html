Dichapetalum mossambicense

Dichapetalum mossambicense, commonly known as the African star orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking violet or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Despite its beauty, it is relatively rare and requires specific conditions to thrive.
