Distimake flagellaris

Distimake flagellaris, commonly known as the silverleaf fern, is a perennial plant native to tropical regions.

It features feathery, delicate fronds that give it a soft, ornamental appearance. The plant has a fibrous root system and prefers moist, well-drained soil. Its leaves are typically bioluminescent under certain conditions, a unique characteristic among ferns.

It is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in shaded garden settings.
