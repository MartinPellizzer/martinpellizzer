Dioscorea santosensis

Dioscorea santosensis, commonly known as the Santos yam, is a member of the Dioscoreaceae family and is native to South America.

It is a climbing vine that produces tuberous roots, which are its primary botanical feature. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and bears small, greenish-white flowers. Its tubers are often used in traditional medicine and have been studied for their potential medicinal properties.

The species is valued for both its ecological role and its economic significance in certain regions.
