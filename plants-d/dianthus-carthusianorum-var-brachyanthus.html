Dianthus carthusianorum var. brachyanthus

Dianthus carthusianorum var. brachyanthus, commonly known as the Brachyanthus carnation, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that come in a range of colors including red, pink, and white. The plant produces small, rounded leaves and has a short, sturdy stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its blooms are often used in floral arrangements due to their striking appearance and long-lasting nature.
