Dendrobium × suffusum

Dendrobium × suffusum, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in various colors, including white, yellow, and pink. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Dendrobium × suffusum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
