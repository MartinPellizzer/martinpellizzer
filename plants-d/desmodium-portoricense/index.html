Desmodium Portoricense

Desmodium portoricense is a unique legume native to the Caribbean, known for its striking violet-blue flowers and trifoliate leaves. This plant thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in coastal areas and disturbed soils. It plays an important role in soil enrichment due to its nitrogen-fixing abilities. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively under-studied compared to more common plant species. Understanding Desmodium portoricense can provide valuable insights into ecological resilience in island ecosystems.
