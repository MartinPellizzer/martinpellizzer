Diplotaxis cyrenaica

Diplotaxis cyrenaica, commonly known as the desert cress, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa and the Middle East.

It is a low-growing annual herb with small, yellowish flowers and linear leaves. The plant is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments, often growing in sandy or rocky soils. Its seeds are dispersed by wind, allowing it to colonize new areas efficiently.

Diplotaxis cyrenaica is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
