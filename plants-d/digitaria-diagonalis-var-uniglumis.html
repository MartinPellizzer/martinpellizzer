Digitaria diagonalis var. uniglumis

Digitaria diagonalis var. uniglumis, commonly known as the narrowleaf digitaria, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The inflorescence consists of a spikelet-like cluster of bracteate flowers, which are typically small and inconspicuous. The plant produces small, wind-dispersed seeds that are adapted for germination in disturbed soils.

It is often found in open fields, pastures, and along roadsides, demonstrating its adaptability to various environmental conditions.
