Diapedium baphicum

Diapedium baphicum, commonly known as the Baphic spider plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is a low-growing, evergreen perennial that features thick, fleshy leaves arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red, berry-like fruits. Native to southern Africa, it thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
