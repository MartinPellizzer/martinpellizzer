Dendrobium isochiloides var. pumilum

Dendrobium isochiloides var. pumilum, commonly known as the dwarf orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a sympodial epiphytic orchid that grows on trees or rocks, often in humid tropical environments. The plant features short, compact pseudobulbs and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

This variety is appreciated for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
