Dyssodia wootonii

Dyssodia wootonii, commonly known as the Wooton's dyssodia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically features small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of white to pale yellow. Dyssodia wootonii grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil.
