Dulongia acuminata

Dulongia acuminata, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its leaves are elongated and lance-shaped, with a smooth surface and a waxy texture.

Dulongia acuminata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
