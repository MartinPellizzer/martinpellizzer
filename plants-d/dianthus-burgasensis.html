Dianthus burgasensis

Dianthus burgasensis, commonly known as the Burgas carnation, is a flowering plant native to Bulgaria.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are usually pink or red, with five petals and a distinctive central tuft of stamens.

Dianthus burgasensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
