Draba darwasica

Draba darwasica, commonly known as the Darwaza dwarf cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, clustered flowers and basal leaves. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine regions, thriving in rocky and well-drained soils. It produces small, yellow or white flowers that bloom in early spring.

Draba darwasica is often found in high-altitude environments, where it plays a role in local ecosystems.
