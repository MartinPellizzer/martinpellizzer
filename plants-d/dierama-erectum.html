Dierama erectum

Dierama erectum, commonly known as the upright dierama, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, slender stems that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height, often adorned with narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces striking, cone-shaped flower heads composed of numerous small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Dierama erectum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively low maintenance once established.
