Ditassa liesneri

Ditassa liesneri, commonly known as the golden star vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, woody stems and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer. The leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a glossy texture. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens.
