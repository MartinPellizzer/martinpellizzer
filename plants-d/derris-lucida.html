Derris lucida

Derris lucida, commonly known as the white senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. Derris lucida is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and stream sides.

It is also valued for its medicinal properties and use in traditional herbal remedies.
