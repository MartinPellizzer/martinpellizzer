Dendrobium exasperatum

Dendrobium exasperatum, commonly known as the angry orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in humid tropical environments. The plant features long, cane-like stems that can reach several meters in length. Its flowers are usually yellow or green with red or brown markings, and they bloom in clusters.

Dendrobium exasperatum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
