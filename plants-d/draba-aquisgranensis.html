Draba aquisgranensis

Draba aquisgranensis, commonly known as the Austrian rockcress, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of Central Europe.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. This species typically grows in rocky and gravelly soils, often in high-altitude environments. The plant produces small, white or yellow flowers that bloom in early spring.

Its leaves are basal, rosette-like, and covered in a fine layer of hairs.
