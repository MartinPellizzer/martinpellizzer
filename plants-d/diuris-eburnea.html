Diuris eburnea

Diuris eburnea, commonly known as the White-tongued Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in heathland and open forest environments, preferring well-drained soils. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single or few flowers that are pale green to white in color. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and the flowers have a distinctive, elongated lip that is often lighter in color than the rest of the flower.

Diuris eburnea is known for its delicate appearance and is a popular species among orchid enthusiasts.
