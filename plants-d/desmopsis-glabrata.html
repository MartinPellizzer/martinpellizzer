Desmopsis glabrata

Desmopsis glabrata, commonly known as the smoothleaf desmopsis, is a shrub native to the arid regions of South America.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family, which includes many milkweed-like plants. The plant is characterized by its smooth, glabrous leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. It is adapted to survive in dry conditions with deep root systems and reduced leaf surface.

The plant is often used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
