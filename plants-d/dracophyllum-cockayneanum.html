Dracophyllum cockayneanum

Dracophyllum cockayneanum, commonly known as the coastal heath, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant features small, leathery leaves that are typically evergreen, and it produces clusters of pink or red flowers in spring. It thrives in coastal environments, tolerating salt spray and wind, making it a hardy plant for maritime climates.

Due to its ecological importance and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens in suitable regions.
