Dracophyllum oliveri

Dracophyllum oliveri, commonly known as the Oliver's dracophyllum, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant features small, leathery leaves that are typically evergreen, providing year-round foliage. Its flowers are usually pink or white, appearing in clusters and attracting pollinators such as bees.

Dracophyllum oliveri is well adapted to rocky and well-drained soils, making it a resilient plant in arid environments.
