Deutzia macrantha

Deutzia macrantha, commonly known as the Chinese deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features clusters of white to pink flowers that bloom in early summer, adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant has slender, arching stems and simple, ovate leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for a variety of garden settings.

Deutzia macrantha is valued for its graceful appearance and easy maintenance.
