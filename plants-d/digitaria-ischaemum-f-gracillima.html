Digitaria ischaemum f. gracillima

Digitaria ischaemum f. gracillima, commonly known as slender false brome, is a grass species native to various parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and agricultural fields.

Due to its adaptability, it is considered an invasive species in some regions.
