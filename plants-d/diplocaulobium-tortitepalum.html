Diplocaulobium tortitepalum

Diplocaulobium tortitepalum, commonly known as the zigzag grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family, Poaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, zigzagging inflorescence, which gives it its common name. The plant is native to southern Africa, where it thrives in grassland and savanna habitats. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a smooth texture and a bluish-green color.

The species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological adaptations.
