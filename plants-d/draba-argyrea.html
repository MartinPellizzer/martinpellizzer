Draba argyrea

Draba argyrea, commonly known as silverweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of 5 to 20 centimeters. The plant features basal rosettes of silver-gray leaves, which are covered in fine, white hairs. Its small, yellow flowers appear in clusters during the spring and early summer.

Draba argyrea is native to temperate regions of Europe and is often found in dry, rocky soils.
