Dillenia andamanica

Dillenia andamanica, commonly known as the Andaman dillenia, is a flowering plant native to the Andaman Islands.

It belongs to the family Dilleniaceae and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in yellow or white. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate or elliptical in shape. It produces woody, fleshy fruits that contain several seeds, and it thrives in humid, tropical environments.

Dillenia andamanica is often found in forested areas and is valued for its ornamental and ecological significance.
