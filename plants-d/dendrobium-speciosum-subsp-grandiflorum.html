Dendrobium speciosum subsp. grandiflorum

Dendrobium speciosum subsp.

grandiflorum, commonly known as the giant orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia. It is an epiphytic plant that grows on trees and rocks, relying on air and rain for nutrients.

The plant features long, cane-like stems with clustered, showy flowers that are typically white or pale pink. Its flowers are highly fragrant and bloom during the rainy season.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
