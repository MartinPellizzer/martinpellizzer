Dalbergaria capillosa

Dalbergaria capillosa, commonly known as the African bushveld bean, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

The seeds are enclosed in pods and are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
