Dioscorea hongkongensis

Dioscorea hongkongensis, commonly known as Hong Kong yam, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia, including Hong Kong.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its helical stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by cylindrical, tuberous roots. These roots are often used in traditional medicine and are valued for their nutritional content.

Dioscorea hongkongensis thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade.
