Diplotaxis virgata subvar. genuina

Diplotaxis virgata subvar. genuina, commonly known as the African ironweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb with erect, branched stems and hairy leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland habitats.

Its scientific name reflects its distinctive morphology and geographic origin.
