Dasystachys polyphylla

Dasystachys polyphylla, commonly known as the many-leafed grass.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa, characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing tiny, white to pale blue flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
