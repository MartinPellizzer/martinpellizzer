Dendrobenthamia capitata

Dendrobenthamia capitata, commonly known as the clustered orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, clustered growth habit. The plant features lanceolate leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some horticultural settings.
