Dichapetalum fadenii

Dichapetalum fadenii, commonly known as the African snowflake, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant grows as a shrub, typically reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a distinctive waxy texture.

Dichapetalum fadenii is well adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
