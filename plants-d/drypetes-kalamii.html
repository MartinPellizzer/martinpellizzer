Drypetes kalamii

Drypetes kalamii, commonly known as the Kalamii flame tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, bright red flowers that appear in clusters. The plant has smooth, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement and emit a faint, pleasant fragrance. It thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking floral display.

Drypetes kalamii is also valued for its hard, durable wood, which has been used in traditional craftsmanship.
