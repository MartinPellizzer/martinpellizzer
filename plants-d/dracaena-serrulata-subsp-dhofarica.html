Dracaena serrulata subsp. dhofarica

Dracaena serrulata subsp.

dhofarica, commonly known as the Dhofar dracaena, is a succulent plant native to the southern Arabian Peninsula. It is characterized by its narrow, dark green leaves with a slight serration along the edges.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow-green flowers that are followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in arid environments and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for dry climates.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and xeriscaping.
