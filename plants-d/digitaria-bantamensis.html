Digitaria bantamensis

Digitaria bantamensis, commonly known as the Bantam grass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Digitaria bantamensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its hardy nature.
