Dianthus × ebneri

Dianthus × ebneri, commonly known as the Christmas carnation, is a hybrid perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its showy, double-layered flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically grows to a height of 10–30 cm and produces compact, bushy foliage with a slightly woolly texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, which extends from late summer through autumn.
