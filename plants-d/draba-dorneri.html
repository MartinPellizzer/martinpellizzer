Draba dorneri

Draba dorneri, commonly known as the Dorner's wintercress, is a member of the Brassicaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous plant that typically grows in alpine and arctic regions. The plant features basal rosettes of narrow, dark green leaves and produces small, yellow flowers in early spring. It is adapted to cold climates and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils.

Draba dorneri is notable for its ability to survive in harsh environmental conditions.
