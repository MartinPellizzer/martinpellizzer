Dendrocalamus rongchengensis f. hualongdan

Dendrocalamus rongchengensis f. hualongdan, commonly known as the Hualongdan bamboo, is a variant of the Rongcheng bamboo species native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can reach heights of up to 20 meters. The plant exhibits a rapid growth rate, making it suitable for reforestation and bamboo cultivation. Its leaves are long and narrow, with a smooth, glossy surface.

This bamboo is valued for its strong, durable wood and is often used in construction and handicrafts.
