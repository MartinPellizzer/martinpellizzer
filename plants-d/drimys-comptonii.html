Drimys comptonii

Drimys comptonii, commonly known as the Compton's drimy, is a small evergreen shrub native to the temperate rainforests of South America.

It typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters, which are followed by bright red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of dense forests.

Its botanical characteristics make it an attractive and ecologically significant species in its native habitat.
