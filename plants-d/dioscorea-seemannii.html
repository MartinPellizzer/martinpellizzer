Dioscorea seemannii

Dioscorea seemannii, commonly known as the African yambean, is a climbing vine belonging to the Dioscoreaceae family.

It is native to tropical Africa and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and yellowish flowers. The plant produces tubers that are often used as a food source in some regions. Its growth habit involves twining around supports, and it thrives in warm, humid climates.

The tubers are also valued for their nutritional content and traditional medicinal uses.
