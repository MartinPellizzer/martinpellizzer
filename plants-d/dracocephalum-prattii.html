Dracocephalum prattii

Dracocephalum prattii, commonly known as blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender, branching stems and clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. Its leaves are typically narrow and slightly hairy, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Dracocephalum prattii thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for native gardens.
