Dracaena halapepe

Dracaena halapepe, commonly known as the Madagascar dragon tree, is a tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and long, narrow leaves that are typically dark green. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by red, fleshy fruits containing seeds. Dracaena halapepe thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
