Dipterocarpus turbinatus var. andamanicus

Dipterocarpus turbinatus var. andamanicus, commonly known as the Andaman ironwood, is a large evergreen tree native to the Andaman Islands.

It is characterized by its dense, dark brown bark and long, narrow leaves that are opposite in arrangement. The tree produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody, cone-like fruits containing seeds. It is valued for its hard, durable wood, which is used in construction and furniture.

This species plays an important ecological role in the tropical rainforests of the region.
