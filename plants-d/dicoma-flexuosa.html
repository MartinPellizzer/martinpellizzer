Dicoma flexuosa

Dicoma flexuosa, commonly known as the zigzag lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzagging stems, which give it its common name. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Dicoma flexuosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
