Deutzia × maliflora

Deutzia × maliflora, commonly known as sweet deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features clusters of fragrant, white to pink flowers that bloom in early summer, adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant has slender, arching branches and dark green, serrated leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for a variety of garden settings.

Deutzia × maliflora is also valued for its moderate growth habit and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
