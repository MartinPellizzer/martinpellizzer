Dendrochilum saccolabium

Dendrochilum saccolabium, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with dense, dark green foliage and produces showy, golden-yellow flowers with a distinctive, spurred lip. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media. It is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.

Dendrochilum saccolabium is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
