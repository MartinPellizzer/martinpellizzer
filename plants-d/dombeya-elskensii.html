Dombeya elskensii

Dombeya elskensii, commonly known as the velvetleaf dombeya, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, which bloom in spring. The plant has elliptical, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens in temperate climates.

Dombeya elskensii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
