Dracaena angusta

Dracaena angusta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved dracaena, is a species of flowering plant in the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to Madagascar and is characterized by its narrow, strap-like leaves that grow in a dense, upright cluster. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that appear in terminal panicles, often followed by bright red berries.
