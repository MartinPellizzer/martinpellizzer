Dischidia microphylla

Dischidia microphylla, commonly known as the miniature fig vine, is a climbing epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silky hair, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its unique growth habit allows it to thrive in humid, shaded environments, often found growing on trees or rocks.

Dischidia microphylla is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
