Delphinium triste var. linearilobum

Delphinium triste var. linearilobum, commonly known as the sorrowful larkspur, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that bear clusters of blue to purple flowers, which bloom in late summer. The leaves are lanceolate and have a distinctive lobed structure, contributing to its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Due to its striking appearance and ecological importance, it is also valued in native plant gardens.
