Diphyes plumula

Diphyes plumula, commonly known as the delicate fern, is a species characterized by its intricate fronds and unique growth habits.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to moist, shaded environments in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features dichotomous branching, where each frond splits into two equal parts, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its rhizomes are fibrous and help anchor it to rocky or soil substrates.

The leaves are typically small, delicate, and covered in fine, soft hairs, contributing to its ornamental value.
