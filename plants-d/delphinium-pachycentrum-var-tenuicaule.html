Delphinium pachycentrum var. tenuicaule

Delphinium pachycentrum var. tenuicaule, commonly known as the slender-stemmed delphinium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features tall, slender stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height, adorned with large, showy blue to purple flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine landscapes.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and its role in the biodiversity of its native habitat.
