Desmodium triquetrum subsp. alatum

Desmodium triquetrum subsp.

alatum, commonly known as the winged milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and the Middle East. It is a perennial legume characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers.

The plant produces small, elliptical pods that contain several seeds, and it is often found in dry, rocky habitats. It has a shallow root system and is adapted to arid environments.

The species is sometimes used in traditional medicine and as a forage plant for livestock.
