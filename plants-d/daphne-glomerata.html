Daphne glomerata

Daphne glomerata, commonly known as winter daphne, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It produces fragrant, clustered flowers in shades of pink, white, and red during late winter to early spring. The plant has elliptical, dark green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

Daphne glomerata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and winter interest.
