Daviesia lineata

Daviesia lineata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed davisia, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and clusters of yellow or orange flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is well adapted to arid conditions. It is part of the legume family, Fabaceae, and has a symbiotic relationship with nitrogen-fixing bacteria.

Daviesia lineata is often used in native gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
