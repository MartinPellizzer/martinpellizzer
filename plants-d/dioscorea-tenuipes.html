Dioscorea tenuipes

Dioscorea tenuipes, commonly known as the slender yam, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its tuberous roots, which are often used in traditional medicine. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has alternate, heart-shaped leaves. Its flowering stems bear small, pale green or white flowers, followed by the development of its edible tubers.

Due to its medicinal properties and adaptability, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
