Dortmanna leschenaultiana

Dortmanna leschenaultiana, commonly known as the Leschenault's dock, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shady environments such as forest floors and stream banks. The plant features alternate, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, greenish-yellow flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom in late spring to early summer.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
