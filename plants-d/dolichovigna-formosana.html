Dolichovigna formosana

Dolichovigna formosana, commonly known as the Formosa morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

The plant belongs to the Convolvulaceae family, which includes other morning glory species. It typically grows as a climbing vine with heart-shaped leaves and produces showy blooms in the morning.
