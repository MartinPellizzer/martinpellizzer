Dichondra argentea f. macrophylla

Dichondra argentea f. macrophylla, commonly known as silverleaf dichondra, is a variant of the dichondra species characterized by its large, rounded leaves.

This plant is native to South America and thrives in warm, humid climates. Its foliage is covered in a dense layer of fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive, soft foliage.

The leaves are typically heart-shaped and can grow up to several inches in diameter.
