Dendrobium tigrinum

Dendrobium tigrinum, commonly known as the tiger orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces striking, long-lasting flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with intricate patterns resembling a tiger's stripes. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrobium tigrinum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
