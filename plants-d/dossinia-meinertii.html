Dossinia meinertii

Dossinia meinertii, commonly known as the Meiner's dossinia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
