Delissea inflatispinosa

Delissea inflatispinosa, commonly known as the inflated spine cactus, is a unique species native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, ribbed stems that store water, enabling it to survive in dry conditions. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a striking visual element to its environment. Its spines are notably inflated and curved, providing both protection and a distinctive appearance.

Delissea inflatispinosa is a slow-growing plant that thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight.
