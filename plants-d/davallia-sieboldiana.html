Davallia sieboldiana

Davallia sieboldiana, commonly known as the Japanese shield fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its shield-shaped fronds that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant has a creeping rhizome that allows it to spread and form dense mats. It prefers shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
