Dactylorchis russowii

Dactylorchis russowii, commonly known as the Russow's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. The plant features a rhizome with a few leaves and produces small, greenish or yellowish flowers. Its flowering period usually occurs during the rainy season.

The species is noted for its delicate appearance and is often found in humid, tropical climates.
