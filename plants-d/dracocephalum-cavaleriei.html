Dracocephalum cavaleriei

Dracocephalum cavaleriei, commonly known as Chinese dragonhead, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features square stems and opposite leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are typically blue to purple and are arranged in whorls along the stem.

It is valued in traditional medicine for its potential medicinal properties.
