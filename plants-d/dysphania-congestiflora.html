Dysphania congestiflora

Dysphania congestiflora, commonly known as summer purslane, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the Americas.

It features succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slightly reddish tinge. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or purplish in color. It thrives in warm, sunny environments and is often found in disturbed soils.

Dysphania congestiflora is known for its edible leaves, which are used in various culinary traditions.
