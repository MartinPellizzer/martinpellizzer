Delissea sinuata var. lanaiensis

Delissea sinuata var. lanaiensis, commonly known as the Lanai hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and dry forests, characterized by its trailing habit and vibrant orange or red flowers. The leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with a sinuate margin, and the plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping due to its adaptability to tropical climates.
