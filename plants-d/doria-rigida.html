Doria rigida

Doria rigida, commonly known as the rigid doria, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the family Acanthaceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil.
