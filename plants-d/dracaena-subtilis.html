Dracaena subtilis

Dracaena subtilis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf dracaena, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features long, narrow, dark green leaves that can grow up to 3 meters in length, giving it a dramatic appearance. The plant is evergreen and thrives in bright, indirect light with moderate humidity. It is relatively low-maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.

Dracaena subtilis is also noted for its ability to purify the air, adding to its appeal as a decorative and beneficial houseplant.
