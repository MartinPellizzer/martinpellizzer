Doodia marquesensis

Doodia marquesensis, commonly known as the Marquesan fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Dryopteridaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of the Pacific, particularly the Marquesas Islands. The plant features a clump-forming habit with rhizomatous growth, and its fronds are typically pinnately compound. The leaves are usually dark green and have a leathery texture, with a distinctive appearance that makes it recognizable.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
