Diospyros foliosa var. neocaledonica

Diospyros foliosa var. neocaledonica, commonly known as the New Caledonia persimmon, is a species native to the island of New Caledonia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree with dense, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that are clustered in terminal panicles. Its fruit is a small, round, fleshy drupe that turns from green to orange-red when mature.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional medicine in the region.
