Dioscorea gedensis

Dioscorea gedensis, commonly known as the Ethiopian yam, is a tuberous plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its stolons and tubers that grow underground. The plant typically thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with well-drained soil. Its leaves are heart-shaped and large, while the flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female flowers.

The tubers are an important food source in some regions of Africa.
