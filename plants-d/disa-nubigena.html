Disa nubigena

Disa nubigena, commonly known as the cloudland disa, is a species of orchid native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking violet or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, forming a dense rosette at the base.

Disa nubigena is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
