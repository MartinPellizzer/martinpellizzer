Dendrobium rhabdoglossum

Dendrobium rhabdoglossum, commonly known as the golden lip orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees or rocks in tropical rainforests. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, yellow-orange flowers with distinctive lip structures. It thrives in high humidity and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and greenhouses.
