Dioscorea birschelii

Dioscorea birschelii, commonly known as the Birschel yam, is a species of tuberous vine native to Central America.

It belongs to the Dioscoreaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and distinctive, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically followed by oval, red-brown tubers.

These tubers are not commonly cultivated for food but are of interest in botanical studies due to their unique morphology and potential medicinal properties.
