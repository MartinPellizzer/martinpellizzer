Dendrobium neospectabile

Dendrobium neospectabile, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant that grows on trees and rocks, relying on atmospheric moisture rather than soil. The plant produces striking yellow and green flowers that bloom in summer, making it a popular ornamental plant. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in varying environmental conditions.

Dendrobium neospectabile is valued for its beauty and adaptability in cultivation.
