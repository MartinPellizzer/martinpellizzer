Dracaena granulata

Dracaena granulata, commonly known as the African daisy or granulate dracaena, is a striking evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features tall, narrow leaves that are often variegated with yellow and green patterns, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that are followed by bright red berries, adding to its ornamental value. Dracaena granulata thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

It is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
