Dipholis minutiflora

Dipholis minutiflora, commonly known as the small-flowered snake plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit. The plant produces small, pale green or white flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. Its leaves are fleshy and succulent, adapted to store water in arid environments.

Dipholis minutiflora is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
