Dipteronia sinensis var. taipaiensis

Dipteronia sinensis var. taipaiensis, commonly known as the Taiwan dipteronia, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia, particularly Taiwan.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its bipinnate leaves and yellowish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and is adapted to cool, humid climates. Its bark is smooth and grayish, and it produces small, pod-like fruits containing seeds.
