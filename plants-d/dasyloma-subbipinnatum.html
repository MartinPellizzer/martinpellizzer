Dasyloma subbipinnatum

Dasyloma subbipinnatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved daisy.

It is a flowering plant native to southern Africa, characterized by its daisy-like flowers and linear, narrow leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, and it blooms during the summer months.

Dasyloma subbipinnatum is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
