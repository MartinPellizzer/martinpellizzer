Dactylorhiza latifolia var. kerryensis

Dactylorhiza latifolia var. kerryensis, commonly known as Kerry orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to western Europe, particularly Ireland.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant thrives in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and heathlands. It has a complex root system that supports its growth in nutrient-poor soils.

The species is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens.
