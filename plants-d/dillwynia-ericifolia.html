Dillwynia ericifolia

Dillwynia ericifolia, commonly known as the heath dillwynia, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It is characterized by its small, needle-like leaves that are densely packed, giving it a compact, bushy appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and sandy soils.

Dillwynia ericifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in native gardens and restoration projects.
