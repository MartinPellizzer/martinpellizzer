Desmodium thonningianum

Desmodium thonningianum, commonly known as African velvet bean, is a leguminous plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces pods that contain small, edible seeds, which are often used in traditional cuisines. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, enriching the soil.

Desmodium thonningianum is also valued for its potential in sustainable agriculture and as a forage crop.
