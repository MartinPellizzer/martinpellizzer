Diosma linifolia

Diosma linifolia, commonly known as the silky diosma, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, creeping stems that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy surface.

Diosma linifolia is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
