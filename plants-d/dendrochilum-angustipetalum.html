Dendrochilum angustipetalum

Dendrochilum angustipetalum, commonly known as the narrow-petaled tree orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom during the rainy season. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are long and strap-like, often clustered at the base of the plant.

Dendrochilum angustipetalum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm, humid environments.
