Daviesia condensata

Daviesia condensata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed sickle-pea, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its narrow, leathery leaves and distinctive yellow-orange flowers.
