Deutzia longifolia var. macropetala

Deutzia longifolia var. macropetala, commonly known as the large-flowered deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that range from white to pink, typically appearing in clusters at the ends of its branches. The plant has slender, arching stems and dark green, elliptical leaves that provide a striking contrast to its blossoms. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

This ornamental plant is valued for its early spring blooms and is often used in gardens for its decorative appeal.
