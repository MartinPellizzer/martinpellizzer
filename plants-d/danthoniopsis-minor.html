Danthoniopsis minor

Danthoniopsis minor, commonly known as the small danthoniopsis, is a small, mat-forming grass native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in coastal and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained environments. The plant has slender, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive texture. Its inflorescence consists of compact, spike-like clusters of small, pale blue to purple flowers.

Danthoniopsis minor is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in coastal gardens and as a ground cover.
