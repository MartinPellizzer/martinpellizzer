Delosperma caespitosum

Delosperma caespitosum, commonly known as the ice plant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves and produces vibrant pink or red flowers during the summer months. The plant is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems and leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its hardy nature and ornamental appeal make it a valuable addition to drought-tolerant landscapes.
