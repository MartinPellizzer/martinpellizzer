Dracocephalum schelechowii

Dracocephalum schelechowii, commonly known as the Siberian blazing star, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces tall spikes of purple to blue flowers, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a strong, minty fragrance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to rock gardens and alpine settings.
