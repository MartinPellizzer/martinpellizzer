Dovyalis mildbraedii

Dovyalis mildbraedii, commonly known as the mildbraed's starfruit, is a small shrub native to southern Africa.

It produces small, yellow, star-shaped fruits that are edible and have a tangy flavor. The plant is characterized by its simple leaves and small white flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

The fruit is used in local cuisines and is also valued for its ornamental appeal.
