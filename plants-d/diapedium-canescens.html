Diapedium canescens

Diapedium canescens, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are hairy and adapted to reduce water loss, a feature common in arid environments.

Diapedium canescens is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
