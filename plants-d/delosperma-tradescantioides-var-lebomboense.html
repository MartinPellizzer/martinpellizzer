Delosperma tradescantioides var. lebomboense

Delosperma tradescantioides var. lebomboense, commonly known as the Lebombo iceplant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are adapted to store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, and yellow, typically blooming in late summer. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact growth habit and drought tolerance.

This variety is particularly valued for its ability to withstand harsh environments while maintaining an attractive appearance.
