Drypetes kamerunica

Drypetes kamerunica, commonly known as the Cameroon flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that appear in clusters. The plant has smooth, greyish bark and compound leaves with three to five leaflets. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

The tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for its medicinal properties.
