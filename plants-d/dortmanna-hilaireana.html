Dortmanna hilaireana

Dortmanna hilaireana, commonly known as the Hilaire's dock, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or stream banks. The plant features alternate, lance-shaped leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its small, yellowish-green flowers are clustered in terminal heads and bloom in spring.

Dortmanna hilaireana is characterized by its fibrous root system and its ability to thrive in nutrient-rich soils.
