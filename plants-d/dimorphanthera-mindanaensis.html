Dimorphanthera mindanaensis

Dimorphanthera mindanaensis, commonly known as the Mindanao orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique floral structure. The plant typically grows in montane forest habitats, where it thrives in the cool, moist conditions of high elevations. Its flowers are notable for their intricate patterns and vibrant colors, which attract specific pollinators.
