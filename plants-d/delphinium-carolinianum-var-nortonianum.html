Delphinium carolinianum var. nortonianum

Delphinium carolinianum var. nortonianum, commonly known as the Carolina larkspur, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters. The plant produces clusters of blue to purple flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a distinctive blue-green hue.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, loamy soils.
