Dalbergia aestivalis

Dalbergia aestivalis, commonly known as the summer dalbergia, is a deciduous leguminous tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The tree produces pods that contain flat, dark seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to seasonal climates with distinct wet and dry periods.

Its ecological importance includes providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
