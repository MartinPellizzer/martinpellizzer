Dactylus officinalis

Dactylus officinalis, commonly known as the medicinal dactylus, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and Asia.

The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
