Drimyspermum montanum

Drimyspermum montanum, commonly known as the mountain drimys, is a flowering plant native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows in high-altitude regions, often in rocky or mountainous terrain. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly serrated edge.

Drimyspermum montanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens in cooler climates.
