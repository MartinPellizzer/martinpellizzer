Dendrobium neuroglossum

Dendrobium neuroglossum, commonly known as the narrow-lip dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with cane-like stems that can reach several feet in length. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink, often with a distinctive narrow lip. It thrives in humid, well-drained environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Dendrobium neuroglossum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
