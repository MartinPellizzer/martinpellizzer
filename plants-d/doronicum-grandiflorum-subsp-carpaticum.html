Doronicum grandiflorum subsp. carpaticum

Doronicum grandiflorum subsp.

carpaticum, commonly known as the Carpathian leopard's bane, is a perennial herb native to the Carpathian Mountains. It features upright, branched stems that bear clusters of bright yellow, bell-shaped flowers.

The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are hairy and arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, shaded woodland habitats and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its floral structure and coloration make it a distinctive and attractive plant in its native environment.
