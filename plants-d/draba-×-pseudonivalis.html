Draba × pseudonivalis

Draba × pseudonivalis, commonly known as the alpine winter cress, is a hybrid plant species belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming structure and is typically found in alpine and subalpine environments. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in racemes, which bloom in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are usually basal, rosette-like, and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Draba × pseudonivalis is adapted to cold climates and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils at high elevations.
