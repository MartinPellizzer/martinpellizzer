Daphne cneorum subsp. canescens

Daphne cneorum subsp.

canescens, commonly known as the silver daphne, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to southern Europe. It features small, fragrant flowers that range from white to pink, often appearing in early spring.

The plant has dense, silvery-gray foliage that provides ornamental value throughout the year. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Due to its toxic sap, it should be handled with care.
