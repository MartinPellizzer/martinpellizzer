Draba yunnanensis

Draba yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan whitlow grass, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in rocky or alpine environments and is adapted to cold climates.

Draba yunnanensis is often found in mountainous regions of Yunnan Province.
