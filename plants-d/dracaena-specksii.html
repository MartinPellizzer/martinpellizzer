Dracaena specksii

Dracaena specksii, commonly known as the dragon tree, is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is native to the island of Socotra.

It is characterized by its unique, spirally arranged leaves that form a tight spiral at the top of the plant, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by red, fleshy fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Dracaena specksii is also valued for its ability to purify indoor air and its ornamental appeal.
