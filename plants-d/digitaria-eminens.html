Digitaria eminens

Digitaria eminens, commonly known as tall crabgrass, is a perennial grass species native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can grow up to 2 meters in height. The plant has narrow, elongated leaves with a bluish-green color and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in open fields, roadsides, and waste areas.

Digitaria eminens is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
