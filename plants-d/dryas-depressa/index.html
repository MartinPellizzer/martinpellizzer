Dryas Depressa

Dryas depressa is a low-growing evergreen shrub that thrives in harsh alpine and Arctic environments. It is commonly found in rocky soils and open habitats where few plants can survive. This resilient species is known for its small, waxy leaves and clusters of white or pink flowers. It plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for alpine wildlife. Due to its hardy nature, Dryas depressa is a symbol of endurance in extreme climates.
