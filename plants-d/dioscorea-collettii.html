Dioscorea collettii

Dioscorea collettii, commonly known as the Chinese yam, is a perennial vine native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its climbing habit and tuberous roots. The plant produces small, green flowers followed by yellow or red berries. Its tubers are used in traditional Chinese medicine and as a food source.

The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.
