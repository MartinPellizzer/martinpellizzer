Diervilla florida var. venusta

Diervilla florida var. venusta, commonly known as the showy diervilla, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features bright orange-red tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has attractive, glossy dark green foliage that turns yellow in the fall. It typically grows to a height of 3 to 6 feet and has a compact, upright growth habit.

Diervilla florida var. venusta thrives in full sun to partial shade and is well-suited for gardens and naturalized areas.
