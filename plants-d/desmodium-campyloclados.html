Desmodium campyloclados

Desmodium campyloclados, commonly known as the sickleleaf desmodium, is a perennial legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features trifoliate leaves with elongated, sickle-shaped leaflets, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically purple or pink in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or grasslands.

Desmodium campyloclados is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in agricultural and ecological settings.
