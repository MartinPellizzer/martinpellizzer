Dendrobium polyschistum var. polyschistum

Dendrobium polyschistum var. polyschistum, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, often branched stems that bear clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-draining soil, often growing on rocky substrates. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged in two rows along the stem.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
