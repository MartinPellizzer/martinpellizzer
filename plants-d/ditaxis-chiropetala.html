Ditaxis chiropetala

Ditaxis chiropetala, commonly known as the velvetleaf or red leaf plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. Its botanical features include a woody stem and opposite leaf arrangement.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and unique appearance.
