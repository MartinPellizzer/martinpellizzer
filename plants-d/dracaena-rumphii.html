Dracaena rumphii

Dracaena rumphii, commonly known as the flame plant or red dracaena, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, sword-shaped leaves that range from deep green to red, depending on the variety. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red or orange berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Dracaena rumphii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
