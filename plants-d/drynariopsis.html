Drynariopsis

Drynariopsis, commonly known as the small fern, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Drynariaceae.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically produces small, clustered inflorescences with yellowish-green flowers. Drynariopsis is native to parts of Africa and is often found in shaded, moist environments.

Its unique botanical features make it an interesting subject for botanical study and conservation efforts.
