Dendrobium critae-rubrae

Dendrobium critae-rubrae, commonly known as the red-stemmed orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, red-orange flowers. The plant has fleshy, strap-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Dendrobium critae-rubrae is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
