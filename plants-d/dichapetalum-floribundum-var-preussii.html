Dichapetalum floribundum var. preussii

Dichapetalum floribundum var. preussii, commonly known as the Preuss's flame tree, is a tropical plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery red flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces woody pods that contain seeds. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and visually appealing species in horticulture.
