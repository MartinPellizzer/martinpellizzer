Diplochita florida

Diplochita florida, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in long, pendulous racemes. The plant has compound leaves with multiple leaflets and produces small, oval-shaped fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The botanical features of Diplochita florida make it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes in tropical regions.
