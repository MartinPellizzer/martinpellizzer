Distylium buxifolium var. rotundum

Distylium buxifolium var. rotundum, commonly known as the rounded-leaf boxwood, is a variant of the boxwood plant native to Japan.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage that forms compact, rounded shrubs. The leaves are typically oval to rounded in shape, with a smooth, waxy texture. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for ornamental landscaping.

It is valued for its fine texture and ability to maintain a neat, compact form with minimal pruning.
