Disa praestans

Disa praestans, commonly known as the "Rainbow Orchid," is a striking South African orchid species characterized by its vibrant, colorful flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to the grasslands of southern Africa. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a basal rosette of leaves and produces pendulous, showy inflorescences. Its flowers are typically arranged in a spiral and come in a variety of colors, including red, orange, yellow, and white.

Disa praestans thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
