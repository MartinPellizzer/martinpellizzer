Discocactus Heptacanthus Subsp. Catingicola

The discocactus heptacanthus subsp. catingicola is a rare and striking cactus native to the arid regions of northern mexico. it is known for its unique, flat, disc-like shape and vibrant green color. this subspecies is particularly prized by collectors for its ornamental value and resilience. due to its limited habitat and threats from over-collection, it is considered endangered. its survival highlights the importance of conservation efforts for rare desert flora.
