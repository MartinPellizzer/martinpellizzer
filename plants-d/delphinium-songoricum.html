Delphinium songoricum

Delphinium songoricum, commonly known as the Songarian larkspur, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It features tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant produces showy blue to violet flowers in racemes, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a rough texture, with serrated edges.

Delphinium songoricum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
