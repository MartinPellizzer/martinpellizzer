Dorycheile rubra

Dorycheile rubra, commonly known as the red-stemmed dorycheile, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of North America.

It is characterized by its slender, reddish stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to white. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils along streambanks and wetlands. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slight pubescence.

Dorycheile rubra plays a role in supporting local pollinators and is of interest to botanists studying wetland ecosystems.
