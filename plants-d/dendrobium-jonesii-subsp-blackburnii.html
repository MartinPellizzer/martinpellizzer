Dendrobium jonesii subsp. blackburnii

Dendrobium jonesii subsp.

blackburnii, commonly known as the Blackburn's Dendrobium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia. It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in humid tropical environments.

The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

It requires bright, indirect light and well-draining media to thrive.
