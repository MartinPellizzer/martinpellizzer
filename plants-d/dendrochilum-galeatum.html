Dendrochilum galeatum

Dendrochilum galeatum, commonly known as the Galeatum orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with dark markings, and it produces small, lance-shaped leaves.

Dendrochilum galeatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
