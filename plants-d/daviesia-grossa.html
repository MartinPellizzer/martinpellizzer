Daviesia grossa

Daviesia grossa, commonly known as the Woolly Daviesia, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers in spring, which are clustered in racemes. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and rocky areas.

Daviesia grossa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
