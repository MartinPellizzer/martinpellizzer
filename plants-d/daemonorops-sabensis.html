Daemonorops sabensis

Daemonorops sabensis, commonly known as the Saban palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like leaves. The plant typically grows in humid, lowland areas and thrives in full sun. Its ornamental value makes it popular in horticulture, though it is not widely cultivated for commercial purposes.

The Saban palm plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
