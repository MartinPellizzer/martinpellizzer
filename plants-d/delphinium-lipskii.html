Delphinium lipskii

Delphinium lipskii, commonly known as the Siberian larkspur, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to August. The plant has tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

Delphinium lipskii is valued in gardens for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
