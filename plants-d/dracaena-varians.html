Dracaena varians

Dracaena varians, commonly known as the Variegated Dracaena, is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and striking variegated foliage, featuring shades of yellow, white, and green. The plant has narrow, strap-like leaves that can grow up to several feet in length, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Dracaena varians is also known for its ability to purify the air, making it both an ornamental and beneficial plant for indoor spaces.
