Deianira erubescens var. cordifolia

Deianira erubescens var. cordifolia, commonly known as the heart-leaf deianira, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and tropical landscapes.
