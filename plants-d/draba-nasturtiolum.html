Draba nasturtiolum

Draba nasturtiolum, commonly known as the yellow scabious, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and woodland edges, thriving in well-drained soils. It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The leaves are alternate, simple, and often covered in a fine layer of hairs.
