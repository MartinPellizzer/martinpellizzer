Dracaena draco var. angustifolia

Dracaena draco var. angustifolia, commonly known as the dragon tree or Socotra dragon tree, is a succulent plant native to the island of Socotra.

It is characterized by its narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a reddish underside. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by red, fleshy fruits that contain seeds. It is adapted to arid environments and can store water in its thick, fleshy stems.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and resilience in dry climates.
