Dipholis sericea

Dipholis sericea, commonly known as the silky snakeplant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, particularly South Africa. Dipholis sericea is often used in ornamental horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
