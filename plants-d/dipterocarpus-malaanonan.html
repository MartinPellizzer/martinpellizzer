Dipterocarpus malaanonan

Dipterocarpus malaanonan, commonly known as the Malanonan dipterocarp, is a tropical tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Dipterocarpaceae and is characterized by its large, buttressed roots and smooth, grey bark. The tree produces elongated, woody fruits that contain a single, flat seed. It thrives in lowland rainforests and is valued for its hard, durable wood.

However, it is considered endangered due to habitat loss and overharvesting.
