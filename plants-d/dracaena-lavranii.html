Dracaena lavranii

Dracaena lavranii, commonly known as the Persian dragon tree, is a succulent plant native to the mountainous regions of Iran.

It is characterized by its dense, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a reddish or bronze tinge along the edges. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by red, fleshy fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
