Dioscorea holmioidea

Dioscorea holmioidea, commonly known as the Holmioide yam, is a climbing herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its tuberous roots, which are the primary storage organs. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the same plant. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a rough texture, while its stems are hairy and twining.

The tubers of Dioscorea holmioidea are sometimes used in traditional medicine and as a food source in certain regions.
