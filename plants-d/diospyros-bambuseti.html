Diospyros bambuseti

Diospyros bambuseti, commonly known as the bamboo fig, is a unique plant that combines features of both figs and bamboo.

It is a small, shrub-like tree native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. The plant produces small, edible fig-like fruits that are typically consumed raw or cooked. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
