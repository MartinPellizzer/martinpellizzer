Dyschoriste monroi

Dyschoriste monroi, commonly known as the African flame flower, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking, showy flowers that range from red to orange, often with a contrasting yellow throat. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Dyschoriste monroi is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and is relatively easy to grow in suitable climates.
