Dimorphanthera elegantissima var. splendens

Dimorphanthera elegantissima var. splendens, commonly known as the Splendour Orchid, is a striking species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, pendulous flowers that display a vibrant mix of pink and white hues. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating, enhancing its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is highly valued in horticulture for its beauty and is often cultivated in shaded garden environments.
