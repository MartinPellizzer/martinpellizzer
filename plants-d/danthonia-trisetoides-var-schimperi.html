Danthonia trisetoides var. schimperi

Danthonia trisetoides var. schimperi, commonly known as the African feather grass, is a perennial grass species native to eastern and southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-green foliage and tufted growth habit, which gives it a distinctive, ornamental appearance. The plant produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind, aiding in its natural spread. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for dry, rocky environments.

This grass is often used in landscaping for its drought tolerance and visual appeal in xeriscaped gardens.
