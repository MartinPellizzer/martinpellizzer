Dracaena nemorosa

Dracaena nemorosa, commonly known as the forest dracaena, is a perennial evergreen shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It features narrow, strap-like leaves that are typically dark green with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually yellow or green, followed by red or orange berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.

Its adaptability and ornamental foliage make it a versatile plant in both natural and cultivated landscapes.
