Duvernoia trichocalyx

Duvernoia trichocalyx, commonly known as the Chinese sandalwood, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its aromatic, dark green leaves and yellowish flowers. The tree produces small, round fruits that contain a single seed. It is valued for its timber, which is used in traditional Chinese medicine and for making incense.

The plant is also noted for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its resilience to environmental stress.
