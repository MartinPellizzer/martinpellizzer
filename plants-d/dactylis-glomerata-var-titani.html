Dactylis glomerata var. titani

Dactylis glomerata var. titani, commonly known as titan fescue, is a perennial grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces compact, spike-like inflorescences that are typically reddish-brown in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in pasture and erosion control due to its hardiness.

This variety is known for its high tolerance to grazing and drought conditions.
