Diosma thymifolia

Diosma thymifolia, commonly known as the thyme-leaved Diosma, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its aromatic, thyme-scented leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. Its small, tubular flowers are usually pink or purple, and it produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

Diosma thymifolia is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
