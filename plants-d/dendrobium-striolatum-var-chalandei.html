Dendrobium striolatum var. chalandei

Dendrobium striolatum var. chalandei, commonly known as the Chalandei Dendrobium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic. The plant features long, cane-like stems that are often covered in a dense layer of white or pale green hair. Its flowers are typically white or pale yellow with intricate patterns of darker markings.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique and striking floral display.
