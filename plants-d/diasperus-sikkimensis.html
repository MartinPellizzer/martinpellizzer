Diasperus sikkimensis

Diasperus sikkimensis, commonly known as the Sikkim rock fern, is a species of fern that belongs to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit and is typically found in rocky or alpine environments. The plant has pinnate leaves with a dense arrangement of small, lanceolate fronds. It is native to the Himalayan regions, including parts of India, Nepal, and Bhutan.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its natural range.
