Dendrobium brevicaule subsp. brevicaule

Dendrobium brevicaule subsp.

brevicaule, commonly known as the short-stemmed Dendrobium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia. It is an epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments.

The plant features short, compact stems with numerous lance-shaped leaves arranged in a spiral pattern. Its flowers are usually small, fragrant, and come in shades of white, yellow, and green.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
