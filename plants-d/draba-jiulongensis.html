Draba jiulongensis

Draba jiulongensis, commonly known as the Jiulongshan whitlow grass, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The species is characterized by its low-growing habit, with basal leaves and small, white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in alpine and arctic environments, often found in rocky or gravelly soils.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
