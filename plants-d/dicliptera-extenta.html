Dicliptera extenta

Dicliptera extenta, commonly known as the broadleaf grass.

It is a perennial grass species native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia. The plant has elongated, narrow leaves with a bluish-green color and is often found in moist, well-drained soils. Its inflorescence consists of a loose cluster of spikelets, which produce small, wind-pollinated flowers.

Dicliptera extenta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and erosion control.
