Dendrobium mayrii

Dendrobium mayrii, commonly known as the Mayrii orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink, often with intricate patterns. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern around the pseudobulbs.

Dendrobium mayrii thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
