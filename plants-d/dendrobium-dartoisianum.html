Dendrobium dartoisianum

Dendrobium dartoisianum, commonly known as the Dartoisian orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its erect, branching growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with a distinctive red or orange lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, porous media for optimal growth.

Dendrobium dartoisianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
