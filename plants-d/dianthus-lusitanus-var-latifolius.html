Dianthus lusitanus var. latifolius

Dianthus lusitanus var. latifolius, commonly known as the broad-leaved campion, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its pink or red flowers and broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in open woodlands and along coastal areas.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for rock gardens and wildflower displays.
