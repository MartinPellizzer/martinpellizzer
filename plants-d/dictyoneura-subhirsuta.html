Dictyoneura subhirsuta

Dictyoneura subhirsuta, commonly known as the hairy leaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive hairy leaves. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is found in coastal regions. Its flowers are usually white or pale green, with a fragrant aroma.

It is a perennial herb that thrives in well-drained environments and is often found in shrublands or open forests.
