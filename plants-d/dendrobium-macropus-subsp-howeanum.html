Dendrobium macropus subsp. howeanum

Dendrobium macropus subsp.

howeanum, commonly known as the Howeanum Dendrobium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its tall, cane-like pseudobulbs and long, narrow leaves.

The plant produces clusters of fragrant, pale yellow to white flowers with a distinctive red or orange lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

This orchid is popular among horticulturists for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
