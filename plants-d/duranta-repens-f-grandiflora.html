Duranta repens f. grandiflora

Duranta repens f. grandiflora, commonly known as the grandiflora duranta, is a flowering shrub native to the Caribbean and southern United States.

It features trailing stems that can grow up to several feet long, making it suitable for hanging baskets or ground cover. The plant produces clusters of bright blue or violet tubular flowers, which are highly attractive to hummingbirds and other pollinators. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a glossy green surface with a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
