Dimeria acinaciformis

Dimeria acinaciformis, commonly known as the swordleaf dimeria, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its linear, sword-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Dimeria acinaciformis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
