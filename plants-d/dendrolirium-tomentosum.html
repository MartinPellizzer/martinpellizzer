Dendrolirium tomentosum

Dendrolirium tomentosum, commonly known as the woolly forest lily, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the temperate regions of East Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea. The plant features a rosette of broad, velvety leaves covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a soft, woolly appearance. Its flowers are showy, with long, slender petals and a distinctive, spurred lip.

It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.
