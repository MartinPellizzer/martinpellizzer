Dortmanna thapsoidea

Dortmanna thapsoidea, commonly known as the coastal sandplant, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a small, annual herb that thrives in sandy and coastal environments. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It plays a role in stabilizing dunes and preventing soil erosion in coastal areas.

Dortmanna thapsoidea is adapted to harsh, saline conditions and is often found in disturbed or open habitats.
