Disocactus horichii

Disocactus horichii, commonly known as the Horichii Disocactus, is a cactus species native to Mexico.

It is a stemless cactus with a globular or cylindrical shape, often covered in dense, woolly spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It prefers well-draining soil and full sun exposure, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and collections.
