Dioscorea potarensis

Dioscorea potarensis, commonly known as the Potara yam, is a species of tuberous vine native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its climbing habit and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by cylindrical, hairy fruits containing numerous seeds. Its tuberous roots are the primary part used, valued for their starchy content and traditional medicinal uses.

However, it is important to note that the plant contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
