Deutzia shawana

Deutzia shawana, commonly known as Shawana deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It typically grows to a height of 2 to 4 feet and features clusters of white to pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value comes from its showy flowers and compact growth habit.
