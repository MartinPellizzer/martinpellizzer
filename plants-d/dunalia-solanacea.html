Dunalia solanacea

Dunalia solanacea, commonly known as the purple leaf nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant is characterized by its hairy stems, lobed leaves, and showy purple or violet flowers. Its fruits are small, berry-like, and typically turn red when mature.

Dunalia solanacea is often found in moist, shaded habitats and is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value.
