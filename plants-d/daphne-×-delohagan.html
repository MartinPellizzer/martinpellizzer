Daphne × delohagan

Daphne × delohagan, commonly known as the winter daphne, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Thymelaeaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form and produces clusters of fragrant, waxy flowers in shades of pink, white, and red. The plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, adding ornamental value throughout the year.

Daphne × delohagan is relatively low maintenance but requires protection from frost and excessive moisture.
