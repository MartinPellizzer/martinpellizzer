Dendrobium flexicaule

Dendrobium flexicaule, commonly known as the slender dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, slender stems that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium flexicaule is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
