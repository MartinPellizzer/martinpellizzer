Dendrobium lancilabium subsp. lancilabium

Dendrobium lancilabium subsp.

lancilabium, commonly known as the long-leaf dendrobium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia. It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments.

The plant features long, slender pseudobulbs and linear leaves, which are adapted to its climbing growth habit. Its flowers are usually yellow or greenish-yellow, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
