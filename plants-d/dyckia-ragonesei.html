Dyckia ragonesei

Dyckia ragonesei, commonly known as the Ragonese dyckia, is a species of flowering plant in the bromeliad family.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a dense, water-holding structure. The plant produces striking, showy inflorescences that are typically red or orange in color. It thrives in sunny, well-drained environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Dyckia ragonesei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
