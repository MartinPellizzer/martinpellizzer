Dolichos ichthyophone var. sunzuensis

Dolichos ichthyophone var. sunzuensis, commonly known as the Sunzu Dolichos, is a rare legume native to specific regions in East Africa.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and trifoliate leaves, with each leaflet being elliptic and pubescent. The flowers are small, pale yellow, and arranged in racemes, while the pods are elongated and contain several seeds. This variety is notable for its adaptation to semi-arid environments and its potential use in local agriculture.

Its botanical study contributes to the understanding of legume diversity in tropical ecosystems.
