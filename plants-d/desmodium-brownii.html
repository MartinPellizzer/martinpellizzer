Desmodium brownii

Desmodium brownii, commonly known as the purple needlepod, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which are often hairy and have a distinctive purple hue. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to purple, followed by elongated, spiny pods. Desmodium brownii is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.

It is also sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
