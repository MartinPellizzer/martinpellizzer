Davallia calobodon

Davallia calobodon, commonly known as the slender shield fern, is a species of polypodioid fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, slender fronds that are typically light green and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, spore-bearing structures called sori on the underside of its leaves. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
