Dioscorea friedrichsthalii

Dioscorea friedrichsthalii, commonly known as the Friedrichsthal yam, is a species of tuberous vine native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its climbing habit and compound leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by tuberous roots that are often used in traditional medicine.

Its botanical significance lies in its potential medicinal properties and its role in local agricultural systems.
