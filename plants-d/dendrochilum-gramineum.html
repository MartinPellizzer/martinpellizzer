Dendrochilum gramineum

Dendrochilum gramineum, commonly known as the grass orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its grass-like foliage and compact, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces inflorescences of small, greenish-yellow flowers that are often hidden among the foliage. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Dendrochilum gramineum is prized for its unique appearance and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value.
