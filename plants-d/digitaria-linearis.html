Digitaria linearis

Digitaria linearis, commonly known as slender digitgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and dense, tufted growth habit. The plant typically grows in grasslands, disturbed soils, and along roadsides, thriving in full sun. Its seed heads are slender and spike-like, often appearing in late summer.

Digitaria linearis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and erosion control.
