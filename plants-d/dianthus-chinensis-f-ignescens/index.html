Dianthus Chinensis F. Ignescens

Dianthus chinensis f. ignescens is a strikingly beautiful plant known for its vibrant, flame-like flowers. This variety of the Chinese pink is prized for its unique appearance and hardy nature. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes. Its fiery hues range from orange to deep red, adding a bold touch to any setting. This cultivar is not only ornamental but also a testament to the diversity within the Dianthus genus.
