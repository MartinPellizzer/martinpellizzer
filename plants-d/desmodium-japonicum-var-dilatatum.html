Desmodium japonicum var. dilatatum

Desmodium japonicum var. dilatatum, commonly known as the broadleaf desmodium, is a perennial legume native to Asia.

It features trifoliate leaves with broad, oval leaflets and small, yellow pea-like flowers. The plant is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it beneficial in soil enrichment. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in forage and erosion control.

Its growth habit is typically prostrate or semi-erect, with hairy stems and small, rounded pods.
