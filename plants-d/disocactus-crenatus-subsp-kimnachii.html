Disocactus crenatus subsp. kimnachii

Disocactus crenatus subsp.

kimnachii, commonly known as the Kimnach's cactus, is a species native to Mexico. It is a low-growing, branching cactus with distinctive, ribbed stems that are often covered in white or pale green spines.

The plant produces showy, funnel-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
