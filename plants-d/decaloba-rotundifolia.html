Decaloba rotundifolia

Decaloba rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leafed decaloba, is a member of the Malvaceae family.

It is a shrub characterized by its broad, rounded leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.
