Dimorphotheca acutifolia

Dimorphotheca acutifolia, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flower heads, which are surrounded by pale green bracts. The plant has linear, hairy leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Dimorphotheca acutifolia is often used in floral arrangements due to its vibrant color and long-lasting blooms.
