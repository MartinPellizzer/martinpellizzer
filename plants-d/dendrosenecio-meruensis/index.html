Dendrosenecio Meruensis

Dendrosenecio meruensis is a rare and striking plant native to the highlands of Kenya. It belongs to the sunflower family and is known for its unique, fluffy white flowers that resemble clouds. This plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found growing in rocky areas. Due to its limited distribution and habitat loss, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this remarkable species for future generations.
