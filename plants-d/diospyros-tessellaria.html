Diospyros tessellaria

Diospyros tessellaria, commonly known as the tessellated ebony, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark, dense wood, which is highly valued for its strength and fine grain. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and dark green in color. It produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers followed by small, round fruits that are edible.

The species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and for the production of high-quality timber.
