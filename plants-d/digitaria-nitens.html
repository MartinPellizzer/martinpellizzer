Digitaria nitens

Digitaria nitens, commonly known as slender fingergrass, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in open areas such as fields and disturbed sites.

Digitaria nitens is a perennial plant that plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
