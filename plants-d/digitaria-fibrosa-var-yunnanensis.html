Digitaria fibrosa var. yunnanensis

Digitaria fibrosa var. yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan digitaria, is a perennial grass species native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear minute, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and ecological restoration projects.
