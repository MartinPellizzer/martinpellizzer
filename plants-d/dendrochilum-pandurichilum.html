Dendrochilum pandurichilum

Dendrochilum pandurichilum, commonly known as the pandurichilum orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces racemose inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to thrive in moist, shaded environments.

Dendrochilum pandurichilum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and unique floral structure.
