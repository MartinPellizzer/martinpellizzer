Delonix leucantha subsp. gracilis

Delonix leucantha subsp.

gracilis, commonly known as the slender flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Madagascar. It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often appearing before the leaves emerge.

The plant has pinnately compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun for optimal growth.

The species is valued for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in warm climates for its striking floral display.
