Desmodium laxiflorum subsp. lacei

Desmodium laxiflorum subsp.

lacei, commonly known as the slender-flowered desmodium, is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae. It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, clustered flowers that typically range in color from white to pale pink.

The plant is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including regions of China and India. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories or along stream banks.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it valuable in ecological restoration and soil enrichment efforts.
