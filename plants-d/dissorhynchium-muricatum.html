Dissorhynchium muricatum

Dissorhynchium muricatum, commonly known as the spiked orchid, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, spiky appearance. The plant features showy, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in yellow or orange shades. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its unique morphology makes it a popular choice for both ornamental gardening and botanical study.
