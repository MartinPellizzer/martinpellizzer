Dianthus plumosus

Dianthus plumosus, commonly known as the tufted pink, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, plume-like flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows to a height of 10 to 30 centimeters and features narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Dianthus plumosus is known for its long blooming period, which extends from spring through early summer.
