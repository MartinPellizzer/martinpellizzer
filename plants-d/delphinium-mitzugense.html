Delphinium mitzugense

Delphinium mitzugense, commonly known as the Mitzug delphinium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant produces showy blue to purple flowers in raceme inflorescences, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are compound, with lance-shaped leaflets arranged alternately along the stem.

Delphinium mitzugense is adapted to cold, alpine environments and is often found in rocky soils at high elevations.
