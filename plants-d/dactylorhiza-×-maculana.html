Dactylorhiza × maculana

Dactylorhiza × maculana, commonly known as the spotted orchid, is a hybrid orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a rhizomatous growth habit and produces tall, erect stems bearing showy, violet or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wet meadows. Its flowers are often spotted or streaked, giving it its common name.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental qualities in horticulture.
