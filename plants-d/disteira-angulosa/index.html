Disteira Angulosa

Disteira angulosa is a unique flowering plant native to specific regions of South America. It is known for its striking appearance and unusual growth habits. This species thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its flowers are small but colorful, adding a subtle beauty to its natural habitat. Due to its rarity, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists alike.
