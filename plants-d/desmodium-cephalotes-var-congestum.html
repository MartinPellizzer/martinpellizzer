Desmodium cephalotes var. congestum

Desmodium cephalotes var. congestum, commonly known as the clustered head desmodium, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a legume belonging to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous annual or perennial, often found in grasslands and disturbed soils. Its trifoliate leaves and yellow or pale pink flowers contribute to its ornamental value.

The plant is also noted for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, enhancing soil fertility.
