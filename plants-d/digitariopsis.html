Digitariopsis

Digitariopsis, commonly known as the ghost fern, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Digitariaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like appearance and is native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plants typically have small, narrow leaves arranged in a rosette pattern, and they often thrive in moist, shaded environments.
