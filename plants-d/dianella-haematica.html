Dianella haematica

Dianella haematica, commonly known as the red-flowered flax lily, is a perennial herb native to New Zealand.

It features striking red or pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that form a dense rosette near the ground. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Dianella haematica is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
