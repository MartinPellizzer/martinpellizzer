Desmodium grande

Desmodium grande, commonly known as the large cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant produces cylindrical, bean-like pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Desmodium grande is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for agricultural purposes.
