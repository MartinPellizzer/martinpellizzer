Deguelia javanica

Deguelia javanica, commonly known as the Javan tree or Javanian thorn, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant is notable for its thorny stems and trifoliate leaves, which are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in secondary forests and disturbed areas.

The flowers are typically yellow or orange, and the plant is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value.
