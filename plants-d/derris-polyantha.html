Derris polyantha

Derris polyantha, commonly known as the pink derris, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a climbing shrub that produces small, pink or white flowers arranged in clusters. The plant belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves. Its seeds are enclosed in pods and are often used in traditional medicine.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal due to its attractive flowers.
