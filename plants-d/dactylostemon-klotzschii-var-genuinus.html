Dactylostemon klotzschii var. genuinus

Dactylostemon klotzschii var. genuinus, commonly known as the yellow bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking yellow tubular flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and grows as a perennial shrub. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and ability to attract pollinators.
