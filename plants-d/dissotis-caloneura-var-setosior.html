Dissotis caloneura var. setosior

Dissotis caloneura var. setosior, commonly known as the hairy dissotis, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a landscape plant.
