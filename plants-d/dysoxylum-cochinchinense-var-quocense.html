Dysoxylum cochinchinense var. quocense

Dysoxylum cochinchinense var. quocense, commonly known as the Vietnamese tamarind tree, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Sterculiaceae and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and yellowish-orange flowers. The plant also has medicinal uses in traditional medicine practices.
