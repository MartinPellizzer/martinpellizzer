Dicalix myrianthus

Dicalix myrianthus, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a tropical plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters during the rainy season. The plant has compound leaves composed of numerous small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental species.

The flowers are not only visually striking but also attract a variety of pollinators such as bees and birds.
