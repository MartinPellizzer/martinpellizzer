Dracaena jaspidea

Dracaena jaspidea, commonly known as the flame tree or red dracaena, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red bracts that surround small white flowers, creating a dramatic visual display. The plant has strap-shaped, dark green leaves that are often variegated with yellow or white markings. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Dracaena jaspidea is also valued for its ability to purify indoor air, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
