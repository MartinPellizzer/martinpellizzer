Delphinium hellesponticum

Delphinium hellesponticum, commonly known as the Hellenic larkspur, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It features tall, spindly stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height, adorned with clusters of blue to purple flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are deeply lobed, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or alpine garden settings.

Its botanical characteristics make it a striking addition to botanical collections and native plant gardens.
