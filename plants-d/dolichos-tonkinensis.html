Dolichos tonkinensis

Dolichos tonkinensis, commonly known as the Tonkin dolichos, is a climbing legume native to Southeast Asia.

It features trifoliate leaves with long, slender leaflets and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes. The plant bears elongated, twisted pods that contain several kidney-shaped seeds. It is often found in moist, tropical environments and is adapted to thrive in partial shade.

Its botanical characteristics make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
