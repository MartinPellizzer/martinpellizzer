Dodonaea pachyneura

Dodonaea pachyneura, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hop bush, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and dry inland regions.

Dodonaea pachyneura is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
