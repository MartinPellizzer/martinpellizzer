Draba igarashii

Draba igarashii, commonly known as the Igarashii rockcress, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. This species is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in early spring. Draba igarashii thrives in rocky, alpine environments, often growing in crevices and on slopes.

Its short stature and ability to withstand harsh mountain conditions make it a resilient member of its ecosystem.
