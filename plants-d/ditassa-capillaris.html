Ditassa capillaris

Ditassa capillaris, commonly known as the slender ditassa, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, grass-like foliage and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pale yellow. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight. Its slender stems and rapid growth make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Due to its adaptability, it is often used in landscaping and as a ground cover in warm climates.
