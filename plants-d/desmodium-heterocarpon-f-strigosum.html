Desmodium heterocarpon f. strigosum

Desmodium heterocarpon f. strigosum, commonly known as the hairy desmodium, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves with hairy surfaces, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It is often found in moist, well-drained soils in tropical and subtropical regions.

This species is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it beneficial in agricultural and ecological contexts.
