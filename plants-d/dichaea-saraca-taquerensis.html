Dichaea saraca-taquerensis

Dichaea saraca-taquerensis, commonly known as the Taquerensis dichaea, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and green. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in cloud forests. Its leaves are usually elongated and covered in a fine, velvety texture.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
