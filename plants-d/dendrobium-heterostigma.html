Dendrobium heterostigma

Dendrobium heterostigma, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with multiple stems that can reach up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a distinctive red or purple lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium heterostigma is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its long-lasting blooms.
