Dialissa

Dialissa, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine silvery or grayish pubescence. Its flowers are small and clustered in cyathia, typical of the Euphorbiaceae family.

Dialissa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in some cultures.
