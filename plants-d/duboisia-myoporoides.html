Duboisia myoporoides

Duboisia myoporoides, commonly known as the coastal myopor, is a shrub native to the coastal regions of southern Australia.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine white hairs, giving them a silvery appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, pale blue or violet flowers that bloom in spring. It is well adapted to sandy soils and salt-laden winds, making it a hardy coastal species.

Duboisia myoporoides is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in coastal landscaping.
