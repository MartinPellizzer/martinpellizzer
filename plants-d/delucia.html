Delucia

Delucia, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features large, showy flowers that bloom above the water surface, while its leaves are broad and floating. The plant has a unique growth pattern, with roots submerged in water and stems that rise to the surface. Delucia is known for its symbolic significance in various cultures, often representing purity and enlightenment.

Its botanical structure supports both aquatic and terrestrial ecosystems.
