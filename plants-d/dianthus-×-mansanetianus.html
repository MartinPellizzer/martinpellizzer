Dianthus × mansanetianus

Dianthus × mansanetianus, commonly known as the hybrid carnation, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is a hybrid species resulting from crosses between Dianthus chinensis and Dianthus barbatus, combining traits from both parent species. The plant typically features showy, bilaterally symmetrical flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, often with a distinctive central boss of stamens. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and floral arrangements.

Dianthus × mansanetianus prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a versatile choice for various horticultural applications.
