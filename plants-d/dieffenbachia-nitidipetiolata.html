Dieffenbachia nitidipetiolata

Dieffenbachia nitidipetiolata, commonly known as the White Star Dieffenbachia, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a striking white or cream-colored pattern. The plant has thick, stiff stems that grow upright, and its leaves are often adorned with a waxy coating that gives them a glossy appearance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity to maintain its vibrant foliage.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated as a houseplant.
