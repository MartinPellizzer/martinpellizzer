Dendrobium tetragonum

Dendrobium tetragonum, commonly known as the quadrangular orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic orchid that grows on trees and rocks, thriving in humid tropical climates. The plant has a distinctive square-shaped stem, which gives it its common name. Its flowers are typically yellow or green with red or purple markings, blooming in clusters.

Dendrobium tetragonum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
