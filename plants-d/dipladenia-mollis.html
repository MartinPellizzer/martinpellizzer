Dipladenia mollis

Dipladenia mollis, commonly known as the crown of thorns, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a trailing or climbing plant with velvety, waxy leaves that are typically green or variegated. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in warm, sunny conditions and is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens or hanging baskets.
