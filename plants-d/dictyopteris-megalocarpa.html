Dictyopteris megalocarpa

Dictyopteris megalocarpa, commonly known as the giant winged kelp.

It is a species of brown algae that belongs to the family Dictyopteridaceae. This plant is characterized by its large, flat, blade-like fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The fronds are typically divided into segments with a distinctive, branching pattern.

It thrives in shallow coastal waters and plays an important role in marine ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various marine organisms.
