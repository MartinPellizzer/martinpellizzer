Dendrobium procerum

Dendrobium procerum, commonly known as the tall dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a tall, slender stem with numerous nodes and is characterized by its long, narrow leaves. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and yellow, often in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Dendrobium procerum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
