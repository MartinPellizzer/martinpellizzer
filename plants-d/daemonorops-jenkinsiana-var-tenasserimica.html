Daemonorops jenkinsiana var. tenasserimica

Daemonorops jenkinsiana var. tenasserimica, commonly known as the Tenasserim palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and pinnate leaves. The plant typically grows in humid, lowland areas with rich, well-drained soil. It produces small, clustered inflorescences and is often used in ornamental horticulture.
