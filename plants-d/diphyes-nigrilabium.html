Diphyes nigrilabium

Diphyes nigrilabium, commonly known as the black-lipped diphyes, is a rare flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, dark purple to black flowers that bloom during the rainy season. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and a distinct aromatic scent. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
