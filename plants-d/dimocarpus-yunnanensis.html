Dimocarpus yunnanensis

Dimocarpus yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan wax apple, is a species of flowering plant in the family Euphorbiaceae.

It is native to China, particularly Yunnan Province, and is characterized by its distinctive, waxy, orange-red fruits. The fruit is fleshy, with a single seed enclosed in a hard, woody capsule, making it unique among the genus.
