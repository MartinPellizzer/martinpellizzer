Dendrobium validicolle

Dendrobium validicolle, commonly known as the golden lip orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on the surfaces of trees or rocks without being parasitic. The plant produces striking, showy flowers with a golden-yellow lip and white petals. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its unique floral structure and vibrant color make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
