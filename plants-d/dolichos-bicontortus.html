Dolichos bicontortus

Dolichos bicontortus, commonly known as the twisted bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Africa and southern Asia.

It features slender, winding stems that can grow up to several meters in length, often twisting around other plants or structures. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers, followed by elongated, curved pods containing kidney-shaped seeds. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with small, oval-shaped leaflets.

Dolichos bicontortus is valued for its nutritional seeds, which are rich in protein and used in various traditional cuisines.
