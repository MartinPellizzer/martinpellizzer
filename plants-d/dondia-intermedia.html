Dondia intermedia

Dondia intermedia, commonly known as the common dondia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as a shrub or a small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough texture.

The flowers are yellow and are arranged in dense, rounded heads, making it an attractive plant for ornamental purposes.
