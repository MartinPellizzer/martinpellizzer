Dendrobium hellerianum

Dendrobium hellerianum, commonly known as the Heller's orchid, is a sympodial epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact growth habit with short, fleshy pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with red markings, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium hellerianum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
