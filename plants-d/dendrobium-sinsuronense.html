Dendrobium sinsuronense

Dendrobium sinsuronense, commonly known as the Philippine orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on the surface of trees or other structures without deriving nutrients from them. The plant produces strikingly colorful flowers that range from white to deep red, often with intricate patterns. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in humid, tropical environments.

Dendrobium sinsuronense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
