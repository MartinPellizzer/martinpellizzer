Dracula leonum

Dracula leonum, commonly known as the Leon's Dragon.

It is a rare and striking orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador. This plant is characterized by its long, pendulous inflorescences and vibrant, colorful flowers that resemble miniature dragons. The leaves are elongated and often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Due to its unique appearance and limited habitat, Dracula leonum is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
