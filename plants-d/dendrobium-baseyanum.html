Dendrobium baseyanum

Dendrobium baseyanum, commonly known as the Bornean orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a pseudobulbous structure with elongated, fleshy stems that store water and nutrients. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellow-green flowers with distinctive red or orange markings. It thrives in shaded, humid environments with well-draining soil.

Dendrobium baseyanum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
