Dioscorea villosa var. laeviuscula

Dioscorea villosa var. laeviuscula, commonly known as the smooth yam, is a perennial vine native to North America.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellowish flowers followed by tuberous roots that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is sometimes cultivated for its edible tubers.

The smooth yam is also valued for its ornamental qualities in gardens.
