Deutzia mexicana var. pringlei

Deutzia mexicana var. pringlei, commonly known as Pringle's deutzia, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its clusters of white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and has dark green, elliptical leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
