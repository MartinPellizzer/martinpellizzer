Dendrobium densiflorum f. parviflorum

Dendrobium densiflorum f. parviflorum, commonly known as the small-flowered dendrobium, is a hybrid orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant typically features dark green, lance-shaped leaves and produces inflorescences with numerous white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate to high humidity for optimal growth.

This cultivar is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
