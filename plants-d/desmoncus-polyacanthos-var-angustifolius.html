Desmoncus polyacanthos var. angustifolius

Desmoncus polyacanthos var. angustifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a robust stem covered in spiny leaves that are typically narrow and lance-shaped. The plant produces yellow flowers in dense clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are often covered in fine hairs and have a slightly serrated edge.

This variant is distinguished by its narrower foliage compared to other varieties of Desmoncus polyacanthos.
