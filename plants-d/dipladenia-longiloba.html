Dipladenia longiloba

Dipladenia longiloba, commonly known as the yellow bell vine, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a vigorous climber with long, trailing stems that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Dipladenia longiloba thrives in warm, sunny conditions and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant blooms.
