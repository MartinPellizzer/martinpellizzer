Dendrochilum graciliscapum

Dendrochilum graciliscapum, commonly known as the slender-stemmed orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a sympodial growth habit, characterized by its short, branched stems. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically during the rainy season. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, arranged in a rosette pattern around the stem.

Dendrochilum graciliscapum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in humid, shaded environments.
