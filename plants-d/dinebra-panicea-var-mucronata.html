Dinebra panicea var. mucronata

Dinebra panicea var. mucronata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed panicled dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, branching stems and bright yellow composite flowers that bloom in summer. The leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a hairy surface. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
