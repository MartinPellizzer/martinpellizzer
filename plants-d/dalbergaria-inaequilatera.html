Dalbergaria inaequilatera

Dalbergaria inaequilatera, commonly known as the narrow-leaved burr legume, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, followed by distinctive burr-like seed pods. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three oval-shaped leaflets that are often unequal in size.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.
