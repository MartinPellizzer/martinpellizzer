Dovea thyrsoidea

Dovea thyrsoidea, commonly known as the slender dovea, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Dovea thyrsoidea is valued in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.
