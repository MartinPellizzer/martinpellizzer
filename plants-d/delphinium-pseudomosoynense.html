Delphinium pseudomosoynense

Delphinium pseudomosoynense, commonly known as the Tibetan larkspur, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of blue to purple flowers.
