Diplodium × conoglossum

Diplodium × conoglossum, commonly known as the hybrid haworthia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are typically fleshy and succulent. The leaves are often variegated with white or yellow markings, giving the plant a striking appearance. It produces small, greenish-white flowers on tall, slender stems during the warmer months.

This plant is well-suited for cultivation in arid or semi-arid environments due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
