Drosanthemum lignosum

Drosanthemum lignosum, commonly known as the hardy geranium, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, typically blooming from late spring to early autumn. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly rough texture.

Drosanthemum lignosum is often used in garden landscapes for its ornamental value and hardiness in temperate climates.
