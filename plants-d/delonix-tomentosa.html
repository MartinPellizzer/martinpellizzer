Delonix tomentosa

Delonix tomentosa, commonly known as the flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, bright orange to red flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. The plant has pinnate leaves composed of several pairs of oval-shaped leaflets. Its bark is smooth and greyish, becoming fissured with age.

The tree is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can grow up to 20 meters tall.
