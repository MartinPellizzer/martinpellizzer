Dendrobium quisumbingii

Dendrobium quisumbingii, commonly known as the Quezon orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on the surface of trees or other structures without being parasitic. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, which bloom during the rainy season. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, helping it survive in its natural habitat.

Dendrobium quisumbingii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
