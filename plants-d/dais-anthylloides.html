Dais anthylloides

Dais anthylloides, commonly known as the golden daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow ray florets and a central disk of smaller, darker florets. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and along roadsides, thriving in well-drained soils. It produces small, oval fruits that are dispersed by wind.

Dais anthylloides is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its vibrant and long-lasting blooms.
