Dendrobium × murrayi

Dendrobium × murrayi, commonly known as the orchid orchid, is a hybrid epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with multiple stems that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often arranged in racemes. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium × murrayi is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
