Deutzia discolor f. compacta

Deutzia discolor f. compacta, commonly known as the compact deutzia, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy, fragrant, white to pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has small, ovate leaves with a silvery underside, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental value in gardens and its ability to attract pollinators.
