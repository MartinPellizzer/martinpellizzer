Dracocephalum microphyllum

Dracocephalum microphyllum, commonly known as narrow-leaved blueweed, is a flowering plant native to regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. It is often found in meadows and grasslands, thriving in well-drained soils.

Its medicinal properties have been historically used in traditional herbal remedies.
