Dendrobium filicaule

Dendrobium filicaule, commonly known as the leafy dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with multiple stems that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces long, narrow leaves that are often clustered at the base of the stems. Its flowers are typically yellow or green with a distinctive red or orange lip.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
