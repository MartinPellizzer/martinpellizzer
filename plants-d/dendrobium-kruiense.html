Dendrobium kruiense

Dendrobium kruiense, commonly known as the Kruien's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, utilizing its aerial roots to absorb moisture and nutrients from the environment. Its leaves are usually thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native habitat.

Dendrobium kruiense is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and relatively easy care requirements.
