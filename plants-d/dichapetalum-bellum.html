Dichapetalum bellum

Dichapetalum bellum, commonly known as the bellflower tree, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of western Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive bell-shaped flowers, which are typically white or pale yellow. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate or elliptic in shape. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Dichapetalum bellum is often found in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
