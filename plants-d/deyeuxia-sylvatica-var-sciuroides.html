Deyeuxia sylvatica var. sciuroides

Deyeuxia sylvatica var. sciuroides, commonly known as the silver tussock grass, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-white inflorescence that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in horticultural settings.
