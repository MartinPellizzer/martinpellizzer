Deutzia discolor var. fasciculata

Deutzia discolor var. fasciculata, commonly known as the variegated deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in early spring. The leaves are typically ovate with a distinctive variegated pattern of white and green. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

This plant is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and spring blooms.
