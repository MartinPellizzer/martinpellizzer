Dillenia aurea var. blumei

Dillenia aurea var. blumei, commonly known as the golden dillenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has a woody stem with palmately lobed leaves that are often hairy on the underside. Its fruit is a distinctive, round, fleshy drupe that contains several seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
