Draba olgae subsp. olgae

Draba olgae subsp.

olgae, commonly known as the Alpine Draba, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family. It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in alpine and subalpine regions.

The plant features basal rosettes of narrow, linear leaves and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers in early spring. It is adapted to cold climates and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils.

The species is notable for its ability to thrive in nutrient-poor environments.
