Drimia montana

Drimia montana, commonly known as the African lily, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, grass-like leaves and produces striking, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant's bulbs are adapted to store water, allowing it to survive in arid conditions. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Drimia montana is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in horticultural displays.
