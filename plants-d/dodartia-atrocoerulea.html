Dodartia atrocoerulea

Dodartia atrocoerulea, commonly known as the dark blue dodartia, is a member of the Violaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its striking dark blue flowers. Native to southern Africa, this species thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure. The plant features slender, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture.
