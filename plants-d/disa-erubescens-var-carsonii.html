Disa erubescens var. carsonii

Disa erubescens var. carsonii, commonly known as Carson's red disa, is a strikingly beautiful orchid native to South Africa.

It features tall, slender stems with showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
