Dendrobium ginalopeziae

Dendrobium ginalopeziae, commonly known as the Ginalopezia orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It is an epiphytic plant, meaning it grows on the surface of trees or other structures without deriving nutrients from them. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It belongs to the Dendrobium genus, which includes many epiphytic and terrestrial orchids.

This orchid is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
