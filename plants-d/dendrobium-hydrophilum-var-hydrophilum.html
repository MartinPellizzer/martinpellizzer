Dendrobium hydrophilum var. hydrophilum

Dendrobium hydrophilum var. hydrophilum, commonly known as the water-loving orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic orchid that thrives in humid, tropical environments. The plant features long, pendant-like stems that can grow up to several meters in length. Its flowers are typically white or pale yellow, with a fragrant scent that attracts pollinators.

It requires high light intensity and consistent moisture to flourish.
