Dehaasia caesia

Dehaasia caesia, commonly known as the bluebell of the highlands, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue-violet flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a smooth, dark green surface.

Dehaasia caesia is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
