Deschampsia atropurpurea var. patentissima

Deschampsia atropurpurea var. patentissima, commonly known as purple moor grass, is a perennial grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive purple to pinkish inflorescence and narrow, grass-like leaves. This variety thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in heathlands and bogs. Its flowering period typically occurs in late spring to early summer.

The plant plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
