Dunniella

Dunniella, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that are typically yellow or white in color. Dunniella is native to arid and semi-arid regions, thriving in hot, dry climates.

It is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental value.
