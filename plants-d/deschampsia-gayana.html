Deschampsia gayana

Deschampsia gayana, commonly known as the Gayana feathergrass, is a perennial grass native to South America.

It features slender, tufted inflorescences that are typically pink to purple in color, giving it a striking appearance. The plant has fine, hair-like leaves and is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.

Deschampsia gayana is also known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and climate conditions.
