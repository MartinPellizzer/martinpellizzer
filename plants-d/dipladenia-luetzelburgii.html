Dipladenia luetzelburgii

Dipladenia luetzelburgii, commonly known as the orange trumpet vine, is a climbing plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters along its stems. The leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate with a slightly hairy surface. This plant thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-draining soil.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
