Dianthus cinnabarinus

Dianthus cinnabarinus, commonly known as the Chinese carnation, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its vibrant red, orange, or yellow flowers with a distinctive crimson hue, resembling the color of cinnabar. The plant features upright, branched stems and lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its flowers bloom in clusters and are often used in traditional Chinese medicine and as ornamental plants.

Dianthus cinnabarinus thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
