Dolichos kosyunensis

Dolichos kosyunensis, commonly known as the Kosyun's dolichos, is a climbing legume native to parts of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and elongated pods. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its seeds are typically enclosed in long, slender pods and are used in traditional medicine in some regions.

Dolichos kosyunensis is valued for its nutritional content and adaptability to arid environments.
