Dolichos pseudocomplanatus

Dolichos pseudocomplanatus, commonly known as the slender cowpea, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, twining stems that can reach lengths of up to several meters. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Its trifoliate leaves consist of three oval-shaped leaflets, and it bears elongated pods containing edible seeds.

This species is valued for its nutritional content and is often cultivated for both food and forage.
