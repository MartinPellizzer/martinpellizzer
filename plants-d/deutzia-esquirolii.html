Deutzia esquirolii

Deutzia esquirolii, commonly known as the Chinese deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features upright, branching stems that bear clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers in early summer. The plant has ovate to lance-shaped leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

Deutzia esquirolii is valued for its ornamental value and adaptability to various growing conditions.
