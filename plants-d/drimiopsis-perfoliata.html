Drimiopsis perfoliata

Drimiopsis perfoliata, commonly known as the African violet, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves that are often marked with silver or white veins. The plant produces striking, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, which bloom from late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Drimiopsis perfoliata is prized for its ornamental value and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
