Delphinium fengii

Delphinium fengii, commonly known as the Feng's larkspur, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems. The plant produces showy blue to purple flowers in late summer, typically arranged in racemes. Its foliage consists of palmately compound leaves with serrated edges.

Delphinium fengii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
