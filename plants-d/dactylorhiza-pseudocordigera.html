Dactylorhiza pseudocordigera

Dactylorhiza pseudocordigera, commonly known as the false heart-leaf orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces tall, slender stems with clusters of pale yellow or greenish-white flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and heathlands.

Dactylorhiza pseudocordigera is a perennial species with a complex root system adapted for nutrient uptake in its natural habitat.
