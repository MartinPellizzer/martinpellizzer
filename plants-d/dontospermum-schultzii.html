Dontospermum schultzii

Dontospermum schultzii, commonly known as the African starleaf, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped leaves and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported anti-inflammatory properties.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal due to its unique foliage.
