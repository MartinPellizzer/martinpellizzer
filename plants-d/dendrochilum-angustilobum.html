Dendrochilum angustilobum

Dendrochilum angustilobum, commonly known as the narrow-lipped tree orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and elongated, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, with prominent lips. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

Dendrochilum angustilobum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or terrariums.
