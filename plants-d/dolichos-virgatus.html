Dolichos virgatus

Dolichos virgatus, commonly known as the slender cowpea, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features compound leaves with oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, pale yellow flowers. The plant bears elongated, cylindrical pods that contain edible seeds, often used in traditional cuisines. It thrives in warm climates and is valued for its nutritional content and agricultural versatility.

Dolichos virgatus is also appreciated for its ornamental value due to its foliage and flowering habit.
