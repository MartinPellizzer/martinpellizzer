Discaria Crenata

Discaria crenata is a hardy shrub native to coastal regions of southern Africa. It is known for its distinctive silver-green foliage and clusters of small, white flowers. This plant thrives in sandy soils and can tolerate salt spray, making it well-suited for coastal environments. Its ability to withstand harsh conditions has made it a popular choice for landscaping in arid and coastal areas. Despite its resilience, it requires minimal water and care once established.
