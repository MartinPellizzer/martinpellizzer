Daviesia ulicina f. ruscifolia

Daviesia ulicina f. ruscifolia, commonly known as the ruscifolia Daviesia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, pea-like flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant features narrow, leathery leaves and is often found in sandy or rocky soils.
