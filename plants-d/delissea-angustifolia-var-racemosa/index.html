Delissea Angustifolia Var. Racemosa

Delissea angustifolia var. racemosa is a lesser-known member of the Asteraceae family that thrives in moist, shaded environments. This delicate plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, daisy-like flowers. It is often found in forest understories across parts of Asia and is valued for its ornamental appeal. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated in horticultural circles. Its unique appearance and ecological role make it a fascinating subject for botanical study.
