Dolichos gardneri

Dolichos gardneri, commonly known as the African sweet pea, is a climbing legume native to tropical Africa.

It features slender, twining stems and heart-shaped, hairy leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by elongated, bean-like pods containing edible seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often used in traditional agriculture.

Dolichos gardneri is valued for its nutritional seeds and ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.
