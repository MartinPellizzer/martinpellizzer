Daphniphyllum glaucescens var. glaucescens

Daphniphyllum glaucescens var. glaucescens, commonly known as the glossy laurel, is a evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers in clusters during the spring. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for shaded garden landscapes.
