Dendrochilum uncatum var. longispicatum

Dendrochilum uncatum var. longispicatum, commonly known as the long-spined orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a distinctive appearance, featuring elongated pseudobulbs and hairy stems. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, arranged in a rosette pattern around the pseudobulbs.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
