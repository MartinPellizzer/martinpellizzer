Daviesia pubigera

Daviesia pubigera, commonly known as the Woolly Daviesia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, woolly foliage. The plant produces yellow to orange pea-like flowers, which bloom in spring. It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in open woodlands.

Daviesia pubigera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
