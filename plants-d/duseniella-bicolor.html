Duseniella bicolor

Duseniella bicolor, commonly known as the two-colored orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that display two distinct colors, typically white and purple. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Duseniella bicolor is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique floral appearance.
