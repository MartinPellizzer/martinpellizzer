Dieffenbachia Seguine Subvar. Decora

Dieffenbachia seguine subvar. decora is a popular houseplant known for its striking, colorful foliage. It features large, broad leaves with intricate patterns in shades of green, white, and pink. This plant is easy to care for, making it a favorite among indoor gardeners. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering. Its elegant appearance adds a touch of sophistication to any living space.
