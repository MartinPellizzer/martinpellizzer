Disa tripetaloides

Disa tripetaloides, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a species of orchid native to South Africa.

It belongs to the genus Disa, which is part of the orchid family Orchidaceae. The plant is characterized by its striking violet or purple flowers with a distinctive three-lobed lip. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in coastal areas.

Disa tripetaloides is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its beauty.
