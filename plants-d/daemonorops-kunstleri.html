Daemonorops kunstleri

Daemonorops kunstleri, commonly known as the Red-tipped Palm, is a species of cycad native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound leaves that are often reddish at the base. The plant produces small, cone-like structures that contain both male and female reproductive organs. It thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a decorative plant.
