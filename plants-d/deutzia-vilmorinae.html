Deutzia vilmorinae

Deutzia vilmorinae, commonly known as the Chinese deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features clusters of white to pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has arching branches and small, ovate leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and well-drained soil.

Deutzia vilmorinae is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive floral display and compact growth habit.
