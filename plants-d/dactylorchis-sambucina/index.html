Dactylorchis Sambucina

Dactylorchis sambucina is a rare and striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe. Known for its vibrant purple and white flowers, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. This plant is often found in forested areas with rich, humus-filled soil. Its unique appearance makes it a favorite among orchid enthusiasts and botanists. Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to survive.
