Dichrostachys mahafalensis

Dichrostachys mahafalensis, commonly known as Mahafal thorn tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Leguminosae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and bipinnate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that are clustered in racemes, followed by bean-like pods containing several seeds.

It is adapted to dry and arid environments, making it a hardy species found in various habitats across its native range.
