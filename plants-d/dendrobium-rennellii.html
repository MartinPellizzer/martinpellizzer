Dendrobium rennellii

Dendrobium rennellii, commonly known as the Rennell orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to the Pacific islands.

It features a tall, cane-like stem that can grow up to 2 meters in height, with clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern along the stem. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Dendrobium rennellii is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
