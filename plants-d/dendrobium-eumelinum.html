Dendrobium eumelinum

Dendrobium eumelinum, commonly known as the Eumelin orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome that supports its upright, branching stems. The plant produces numerous linear to lanceolate leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. Its flowers are typically small, fragrant, and come in shades of white, yellow, and pink, often arranged in a raceme.

Dendrobium eumelinum thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
