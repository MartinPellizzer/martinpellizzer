Drymonia mollis

Drymonia mollis, commonly known as the silky drymonia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. Drymonia mollis thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in woodland habitats.
