Didactylon simplex

Didactylon simplex, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage with a silvery underside, giving it its common name. The plant has a slow growth rate and can reach heights of up to 20 meters. Its leaves are opposite and elliptical, with a leathery texture.

Didactylon simplex is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
