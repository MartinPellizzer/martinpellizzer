Dolichos cylindricus

Dolichos cylindricus, commonly known as the slender cowpea, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, cylindrical pods that are typically green when immature and turn brown upon maturity. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellow flowers. It is valued for its edible beans, which are rich in protein and often used in various cuisines.

The plant is also cultivated for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, benefiting agricultural systems.
