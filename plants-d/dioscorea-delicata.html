Dioscorea delicata

Dioscorea delicata, commonly known as the slender yam, is a climbing herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its twining stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by tuberous roots that are often harvested for culinary or medicinal purposes. Dioscorea delicata is valued for its nutritional content, including high levels of starch and various vitamins.

Its growth habit and adaptability make it a significant species in traditional agriculture and herbal medicine.
