Dodecahema

Dodecahema, commonly known as the twelve-angled leaf plant, is a unique species characterized by its distinctive leaf morphology.

Its leaves are arranged in a spiral pattern and feature 12 distinct angles, giving it a geometric appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is native to tropical regions. It has a fibrous root system and produces small, inconspicuous flowers.

The botanical interest in Dodecahema lies in its unusual leaf structure and potential medicinal properties.
