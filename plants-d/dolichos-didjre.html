Dolichos didjre

Dolichos didjre, commonly known as the slender cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that develop into elongated pods containing edible seeds. Dolichos didjre is valued for its nutritional content, providing protein and essential nutrients.

It is often cultivated as a cover crop and for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.
