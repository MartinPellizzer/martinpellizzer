Dactylorhiza × balabaniana

Dactylorhiza × balabaniana, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a hybrid between Dactylorhiza maculata and Dactylorhiza sambucina, resulting in a plant with distinctive floral characteristics. The species features tall, erect stems that bear numerous flowers in shades of pink, white, and purple. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
