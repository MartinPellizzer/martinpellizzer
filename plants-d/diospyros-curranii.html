Diospyros curranii

Diospyros curranii, commonly known as the Curran persimmon, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to the island of Guam.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, dark bark and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by edible, orange-red berries that are an important food source for local wildlife.
