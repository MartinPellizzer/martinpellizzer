Dendrobium brinchangense

Dendrobium brinchangense, commonly known as the Brinchang orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Borneo.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stem and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale yellow. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments within lowland forests. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the forest floor.

Dendrobium brinchangense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
