Dendrobium escritorii

Dendrobium escritorii, commonly known as the writer's orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features tall, cane-like pseudobulbs covered in dense, leathery leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with intricate patterns resembling writing or calligraphy. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its unique appearance and cultural significance, it is a prized species among orchid enthusiasts.
