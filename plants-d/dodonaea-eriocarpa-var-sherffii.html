Dodonaea eriocarpa var. sherffii

Dodonaea eriocarpa var. sherffii, commonly known as the silky dodonaea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, often multi-branched stems covered in fine, silky hairs. The leaves are opposite, ovate to elliptic, and have a smooth or slightly hairy upper surface. Its small, yellowish-green flowers appear in clusters and are followed by small, woody fruits.

This plant is adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions of southern Australia.
