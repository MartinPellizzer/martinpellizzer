Doria kraussii

Doria kraussii, commonly known as the yellow-veined doria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. Its flowers are borne in terminal clusters and are often visited by pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Doria kraussii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
