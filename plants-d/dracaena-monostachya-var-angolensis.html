Dracaena monostachya var. angolensis

Dracaena monostachya var. angolensis, commonly known as the Angolan dracaena, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom in late summer or early autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright indirect light.

This variety is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
