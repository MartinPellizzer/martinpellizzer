Dendrochilum microchilum

Dendrochilum microchilum, commonly known as the miniature lip orchid, is a small epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with a single leaf and a short stem that bears a single, delicate flower. The orchid's flowers are typically white or pale yellow with a distinctive lip that is narrow and elongated. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its unique appearance and rarity, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
