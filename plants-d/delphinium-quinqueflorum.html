Delphinium quinqueflorum

Delphinium quinqueflorum, commonly known as the five-flowered larkspur, is a member of the Ranunculaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking spikes of blue to purple flowers. Native to the mountainous regions of Europe, it thrives in cool, moist environments. The plant has lance-shaped leaves and is characterized by its showy, clustered blooms.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and wildflower displays.
