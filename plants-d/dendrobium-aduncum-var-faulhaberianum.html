Dendrobium aduncum var. faulhaberianum

Dendrobium aduncum var. faulhaberianum, commonly known as the Faulhaber's Dendrobium, is a unique orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a distinctive appearance, featuring long, wiry stems and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically blooms during the dry season, producing pale yellow to greenish-white blossoms. It thrives in shaded, humid environments with well-draining soil.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in specialized orchid gardens.
