Dolichoglottis lyallii

Dolichoglottis lyallii, commonly known as the Lyall's snake plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to New Zealand and is characterized by its tall, narrow leaves that are often striped with white or silver markings. The plant is evergreen and can grow up to several meters in height, making it a striking addition to gardens or indoor spaces. It prefers well-drained soil and can thrive in partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for both horticultural and conservation purposes.
