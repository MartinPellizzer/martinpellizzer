Dupatya blepharophora

Dupatya blepharophora, commonly known as the eye-leaf plant, is a member of the Araliaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its unique, heart-shaped leaves that resemble eyes, giving it its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Dupatya blepharophora is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage.
