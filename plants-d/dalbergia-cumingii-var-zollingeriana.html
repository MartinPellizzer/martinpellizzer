Dalbergia cumingii var. zollingeriana

Dalbergia cumingii var. zollingeriana, commonly known as the Zollinger's rosewood, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its dense, dark-colored wood that is highly valued for its aesthetic and structural properties. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in racemes.

Its fruit is a legume pod containing flattened, winged seeds that are dispersed by wind.
