Dendrochilum ellipticum

Dendrochilum ellipticum, commonly known as the elliptic tree orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers with prominent spurred petals. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Dendrochilum ellipticum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate with proper care.
