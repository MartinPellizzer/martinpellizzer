Dendrobium kuhlii

Dendrobium kuhlii, commonly known as the Kuhli's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with a system of roots that anchor it to trees or other surfaces. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically blooming during the dry season. It prefers warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Dendrobium kuhlii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
