Deutzia glabrata var. sessilifolia

Deutzia glabrata var. sessilifolia, commonly known as the sessileleaf deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in early summer. The leaves are ovate and sessile, giving the plant its varietal name. It typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and thrives in partial shade to full sun.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
