Dolichos spurius

Dolichos spurius, commonly known as the slender cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers followed by elongated, pod-like fruits containing several seeds. Dolichos spurius is often found in disturbed habitats and can thrive in poor soil conditions.

It is also valued for its nutritional content and use in traditional agriculture.
