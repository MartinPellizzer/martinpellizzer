Discocalyx

Discocalyx, commonly known as the flame flower, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, resembling flames. The plant belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture.

Discocalyx thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
