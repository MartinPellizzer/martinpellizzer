Diospyros platyphylla

Diospyros platyphylla, commonly known as the flat-leaf persimmon, is a species of flowering plant in the Ebenaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its broad, flat leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers. Its fruit is a small, orange to red berry that is edible when fully ripe.

The tree is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
