Drypetes gerrardinoides

Drypetes gerrardinoides, commonly known as the Malayan ironwood, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers.
