Desmodium strictum

Desmodium strictum, commonly known as slender desmodium, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant has a climbing habit and produces small, round pods that contain several seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of light conditions.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it valuable in ecological and agricultural contexts.
