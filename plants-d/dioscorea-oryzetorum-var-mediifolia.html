Dioscorea oryzetorum var. mediifolia

Dioscorea oryzetorum var. mediifolia, commonly known as the slender yam, is a climbing herbaceous perennial native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features a vigorous vine with hairy stems and heart-shaped, palmately lobed leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by tuberous roots that are often used in traditional medicine. Its botanical structure includes a rhizomatous root system and is part of the Dioscoreaceae family.

The species is valued for its medicinal properties and is cultivated for its edible tubers.
