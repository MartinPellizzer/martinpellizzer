Desmos cochinchinensis var. fulvescens

Desmos cochinchinensis var. fulvescens, commonly known as the red-flowered desmos, is a perennial shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features lance-shaped leaves with a smooth surface and a reddish-brown underside. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically red or orange in color. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses for its vibrant floral display.
