Dortmanna canbyi

Dortmanna canbyi, commonly known as the Canby's dock, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of large, heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in streambanks and wet meadows.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies in its native habitat.
