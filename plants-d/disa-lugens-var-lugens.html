Disa lugens var. lugens

Disa lugens var. lugens, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a strikingly beautiful orchid native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of vibrant pink or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is known for its showy blooms that appear during the summer months, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in controlled environments to ensure its survival.
