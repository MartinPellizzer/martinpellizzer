Dryadorchis dasystele

Dryadorchis dasystele, commonly known as the large-stemmed orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are elongated and lance-shaped, with a smooth surface.

The flowers, which can be pink, white, or yellow, bloom in clusters and are known for their intricate petal structures.
