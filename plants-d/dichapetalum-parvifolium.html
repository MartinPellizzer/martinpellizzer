Dichapetalum parvifolium

Dichapetalum parvifolium, commonly known as the small-leaf dichapetalum, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in moist, forested areas. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
