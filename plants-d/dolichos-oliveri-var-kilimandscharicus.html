Dolichos oliveri var. kilimandscharicus

Dolichos oliveri var. kilimandscharicus, commonly known as the Kilimanjaro bean, is a leguminous plant native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and trifoliate leaves with a hairy texture. The plant produces elongated, bean-like pods that contain edible seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in savanna and woodland areas.

Its botanical features make it an important species for both ecological and agricultural studies in its native range.
