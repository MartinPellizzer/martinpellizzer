Diabelia ionostachya

Diabelia ionostachya, commonly known as the mountain balm, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small, clustered in whorls, and typically have a pale blue to violet color.

Diabelia ionostachya is often found in rocky or dry habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
