Dendrobium erectopatens

Dendrobium erectopatens, commonly known as the upright-leaf dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with long, cane-like stems that bear clusters of lanceolate leaves. The plant produces striking inflorescences with numerous fragrant, yellowish-green flowers that have a distinctive red lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated for its beauty and unique floral display.
