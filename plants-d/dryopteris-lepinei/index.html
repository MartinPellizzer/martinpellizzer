Dryopteris Lepinei

Dryopteris lepinei is a rare and elegant fern native to the mountainous regions of China. This species is known for its delicate fronds that display a striking contrast of colors. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest floors. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species. Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
