Dillenia procumbens

Dillenia procumbens, commonly known as the creeping dillenia, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, palmate leaves with five to seven lobes. The plant produces showy, yellowish-white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are large, fleshy berries that contain numerous seeds.

Dillenia procumbens is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
