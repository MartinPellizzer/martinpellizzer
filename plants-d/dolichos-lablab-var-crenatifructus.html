Dolichos lablab var. crenatifructus

Dolichos lablab var. crenatifructus, commonly known as the cowpea or Egyptian bean, is a leguminous plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive, edible pods that contain both seeds and a nutritious, protein-rich pulp. The plant thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated for its nutritional value and drought tolerance. Its flowers are typically pale purple or white, and it produces a fibrous, stringy pod that can be harvested when mature.

This variety is valued for its adaptability and role in sustainable agriculture.
