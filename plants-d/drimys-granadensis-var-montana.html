Drimys granadensis var. montana

Drimys granadensis var. montana, commonly known as the mountain drimys, is a shrub native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale green, followed by dark blue berries. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies in its native habitat.
