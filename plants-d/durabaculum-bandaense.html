Durabaculum bandaense

Durabaculum bandaense, commonly known as the Banda Island rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Banda Islands in Indonesia.

It is a shrub characterized by its woody stems and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to the tropical climate of its native habitat.
