Delosperma pondoense

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, aiding in its survival in arid environments. Delosperma pondoense is also known for its ability to form dense mats, which help prevent soil erosion in its natural habitat.
