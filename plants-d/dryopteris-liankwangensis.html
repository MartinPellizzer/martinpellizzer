Dryopteris liankwangensis

Dryopteris liankwangensis, commonly known as the Liangkang wood fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its fronds that are typically bipinnate, with a leathery texture. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in mountainous regions. Its spores are released from sporangia located on the undersides of its fertile fronds.

This fern is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in forest ecosystems.
