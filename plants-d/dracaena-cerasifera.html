Dracaena cerasifera

Dracaena cerasifera, commonly known as the cherry dracaena, is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa and the Middle East.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright indirect light for optimal growth.

Dracaena cerasifera is also valued for its ability to purify indoor air and adapt to various environmental conditions.
