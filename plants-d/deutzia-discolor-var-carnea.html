Deutzia discolor var. carnea

Deutzia discolor var. carnea, commonly known as the pink deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features showy, panicle-like inflorescences that bloom in early summer, typically in shades of pink to white. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and have a slightly serrated margin. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, fertile soil.

This ornamental plant is valued for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
