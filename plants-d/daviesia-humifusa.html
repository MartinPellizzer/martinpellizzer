Daviesia humifusa

Daviesia humifusa, commonly known as the low-growing Daviesia, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It typically grows to a height of around 30 cm, with a sprawling, mat-like form. The plant features small, hairy leaves and produces yellow or orange flowers in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and open forests.

Daviesia humifusa is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local wildlife.
