Decalepis arayalpathra

Decalepis arayalpathra, commonly known as the Malabar chestnut, is a small evergreen tree native to the Western Ghats of India.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by edible, chestnut-like fruits. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
