Diuris striata

Diuris striata, commonly known as the stripe-leaf orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, narrow leaves that are often striped with contrasting colors. The plant produces a single, upright spike bearing several small, greenish-yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open forests or grasslands.

Diuris striata is a short-lived perennial, with its life cycle closely tied to seasonal rainfall patterns.
