Dendrobium pugioniforme

Dendrobium pugioniforme, commonly known as the sword orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in tropical rainforests. The plant features long, sword-shaped leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange. It thrives in high humidity and indirect sunlight, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Dendrobium pugioniforme is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in horticultural displays.
