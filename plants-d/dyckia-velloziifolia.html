Dyckia velloziifolia

Dyckia velloziifolia, commonly known as the Velloziifolia Dyckia, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its dense, rosette-forming structure with long, narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-gray in color. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing small, white or pale pink flowers. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a desirable addition to both indoor and outdoor collections.
