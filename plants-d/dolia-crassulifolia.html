Dolia crassulifolia

Dolia crassulifolia, commonly known as the thick-leaved dolia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically green or bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Dolia crassulifolia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact growth habit and attractive foliage.
