Deutzia pulchra var. hayatae

Deutzia pulchra var. hayatae, commonly known as the Hayata's deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features clusters of white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, arching stems and narrow, ovate leaves with a glossy green surface. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
