Dendrochilum adpressibulbum

Dendrochilum adpressibulbum, commonly known as the flat-lipped tree orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, tree-like growth habit and its ability to grow on other plants without being parasitic. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and rarity, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
