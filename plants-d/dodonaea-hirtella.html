Dodonaea hirtella

Dodonaea hirtella, commonly known as the hairy leaf Dodonaea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its small, oval leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive textured appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and semi-arid regions.

Dodonaea hirtella is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
