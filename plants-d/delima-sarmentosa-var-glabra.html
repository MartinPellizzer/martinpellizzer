Delima sarmentosa var. glabra

Delima sarmentosa var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth delima, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is a climbing shrub characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and small, opposite leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers, which are followed by elliptical, dark green fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in forest understories or along riverbanks.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal uses, it is occasionally cultivated in tropical gardens.
