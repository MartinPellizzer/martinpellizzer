Dendrobium linguiforme var. linguiforme

Dendrobium linguiforme var. linguiforme, commonly known as the tongue-shaped orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with multiple stems that can reach up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its leaves are strap-like and arranged in a rosette pattern, and it thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

It is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its long-lasting blooms.
