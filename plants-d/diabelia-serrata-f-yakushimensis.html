Diabelia serrata f. yakushimensis

Diabelia serrata f. yakushimensis, commonly known as the Yakushimen rock orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to Japan.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive, dark green, strap-like leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink, often with a slight purple tinge. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is typically found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
