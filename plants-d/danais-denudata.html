Danais denudata

Danais denudata, commonly known as the naked danain, is a species of butterfly known for its striking appearance and ecological significance.

While not a plant, it is often associated with specific host plants such as milkweeds, which are crucial for its life cycle. The butterfly's larvae feed on the leaves of these plants, making them an important part of the ecosystem. Its presence indicates a healthy environment with diverse flora.

This species plays a vital role in pollination and serves as a food source for various predators.
