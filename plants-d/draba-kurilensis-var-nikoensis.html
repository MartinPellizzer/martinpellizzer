Draba kurilensis var. nikoensis

Draba kurilensis var. nikoensis, commonly known as Niko's Draba, is a flowering plant native to the Kuril Islands.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. This variety is characterized by its low-growing habit, with small, clustered leaves and bright yellow flowers. It thrives in rocky, coastal environments and is adapted to the harsh climate of its native habitat.

The plant is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological significance in island ecosystems.
