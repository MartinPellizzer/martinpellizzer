Daemonorops geniculata

Daemonorops geniculata, commonly known as the giant bamboo palm, is a species of cycad native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces small, cone-like structures that contain both male and female reproductive organs. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value and slow growth, it is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
