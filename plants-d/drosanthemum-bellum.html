Drosanthemum bellum

Drosanthemum bellum, commonly known as the silver leaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage and showy yellow-orange flower heads. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in spring and summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Drosanthemum bellum is also valued for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
