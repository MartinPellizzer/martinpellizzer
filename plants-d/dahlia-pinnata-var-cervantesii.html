Dahlia pinnata var. cervantesii

Dahlia pinnata var. cervantesii, commonly known as the Cervantes dahlia, is a striking ornamental plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant features compound, pinnate leaves that are dark green and deeply lobed. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

This variety is prized for its vibrant blooms and is often used in horticultural displays.
