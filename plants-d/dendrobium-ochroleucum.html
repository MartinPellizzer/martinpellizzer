Dendrobium ochroleucum

Dendrobium ochroleucum, commonly known as the yellow-lipped orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a pseudobulbous structure with fleshy stems that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers with a distinctive white lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural collections.
