Dressleria helleri

Dressleria helleri, commonly known as the Heller's orchid, is a flowering plant native to the island of Socotra.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescence. The plant features long, slender stems with clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in arid environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
