Diospyros polita

Diospyros polita, commonly known as the glossy persimmon, is a species of flowering plant in the Ebenaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia, Malaysia, and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves and small, clustered white flowers. Its fruit is a small, fleshy berry that turns orange-red when mature.

The tree is often grown for its edible fruit, which has a sweet, sometimes astringent taste.
