Dendrobium luoi

Dendrobium luoi, commonly known as the Luoi orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its erect, branched stems that bear numerous leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically during the spring and summer seasons. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Dendrobium luoi is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its cultural significance in traditional Chinese medicine.
