Ditaxis depressa

Ditaxis depressa, commonly known as the broadleaf burr.

It is a flowering plant native to southern Africa, characterized by its woody stems and clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant produces distinctive, spiny fruits that contain seeds, which are dispersed by animals. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in shrublands and coastal areas.

Its botanical features make it an important component of its native ecosystems.
