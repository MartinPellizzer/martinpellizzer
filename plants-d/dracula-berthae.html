Dracula berthae

Dracula berthae, commonly known as the "Queen of the Clouds," is a unique epiphytic orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its striking, pendulous inflorescences that bear intricate, colorful flowers resembling tiny, delicate draculas. The plant thrives in high humidity and cool temperatures, often growing on tree branches in shaded environments. Its leaves are fleshy and dark green, adapted to store water in its native habitat.

Dracula berthae is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts due to its rare beauty and challenging cultivation requirements.
