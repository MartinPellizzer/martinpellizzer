Darwinia endlicheri

Darwinia endlicheri, commonly known as the mountain darwinia, is an evergreen shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its dense, aromatic foliage and striking floral spikes. The plant typically grows in heathlands and open forests, preferring well-drained soils. Its flowers, which appear in late winter to early spring, are clustered in showy, cylindrical inflorescences.

Darwinia endlicheri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
