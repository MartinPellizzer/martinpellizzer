Diplachne uninervia

Diplachne uninervia, commonly known as slender flatgrass, is a perennial grass native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems and narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Diplachne uninervia thrives in a variety of soil types and is commonly found in grasslands, along roadsides, and in disturbed areas.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
