Dupatya microphylla

Dupatya microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate leaves and fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in mountainous regions. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive lip that aids in pollination.

Dupatya microphylla is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
