Disa forficaria

Disa forficaria, commonly known as the sword lily, is a striking perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers. The plant produces vibrant purple or lilac-colored blooms with a distinctive sword-shaped leaf, which gives it its common name. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and forest margins.

Disa forficaria is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
