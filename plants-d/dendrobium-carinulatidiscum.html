Dendrobium carinulatidiscum

Dendrobium carinulatidiscum, commonly known as the Carinulatidiscum orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic orchid that grows on trees and rocks, thriving in humid tropical climates. The plant features long, cane-like stems covered with numerous, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically small, fragrant, and come in a range of colors including white, yellow, and pink.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
