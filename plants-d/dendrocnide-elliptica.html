Dendrocnide elliptica

Dendrocnide elliptica, commonly known as the narrow-leaf stinging tree, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It belongs to the family Urticaceae, which includes plants known for their stinging hairs. The plant features elliptical, dark green leaves with a rough, hairy texture. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and clustered in inflorescences.

The plant is notable for its toxic properties, with hairs containing irritants that can cause severe skin reactions.
