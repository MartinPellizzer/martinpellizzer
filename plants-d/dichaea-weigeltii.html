Dichaea weigeltii

Dichaea weigeltii, commonly known as the Weigelt's dichaea, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and the production of small, delicate flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, loose soil to prevent root rot. Its leaves are usually broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its natural habitat.

Dichaea weigeltii is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
