Danthonia albida

Danthonia albida, commonly known as the white feather grass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its flowering stems are slender and often bear small, white or pale blue flowers.

Danthonia albida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
