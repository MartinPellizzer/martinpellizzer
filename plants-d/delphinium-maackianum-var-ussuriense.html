Delphinium maackianum var. ussuriense

Delphinium maackianum var. ussuriense, commonly known as the Ussuri delphinium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia.

It features tall, slender stems that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height, adorned with large, showy blue to purple flowers. The plant has palmately divided leaves with a rough, hairy texture, typical of the Delphinium genus. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its striking floral display and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
