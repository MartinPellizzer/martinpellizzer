Dolichos dubius

Dolichos dubius, commonly known as the wild bean or slender cowpea, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into elongated pods. The seeds inside the pods are typically small and edible, though the plant is more often cultivated for its forage value.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas or along roadsides.
