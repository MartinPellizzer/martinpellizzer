Dacryotrichia robinsonii

Dacryotrichia robinsonii, commonly known as the Robinson's spiny scale, is a unique plant species characterized by its specialized morphology.

It is a member of the family Dacryotrichiaceae and is known for its distinctive, spiny structures that cover its surface. The plant exhibits a symbiotic relationship with certain fungi, which is a key aspect of its botanical significance. It thrives in specific ecological niches, often in moist, shaded environments.
