Diospyros daemona

Diospyros daemona, commonly known as the Philippine persimmon, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Ebenaceae family, which includes other persimmon species. The plant produces small, oval fruits that are typically orange to red in color when mature. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a rough texture with a serrated margin.

Diospyros daemona is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in its native region.
