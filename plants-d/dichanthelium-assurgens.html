Dichanthelium assurgens

Dichanthelium assurgens, commonly known as the tallgrass panicum, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as marshes, swamps, and pond margins. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny florets. Dichanthelium assurgens plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
