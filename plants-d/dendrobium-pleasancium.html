Dendrobium pleasancium

Dendrobium pleasancium, commonly known as the orchid of the Philippines, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with numerous pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, white, and pink, often in clusters. It thrives in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil.

Dendrobium pleasancium is valued for its ornamental beauty and is commonly cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
