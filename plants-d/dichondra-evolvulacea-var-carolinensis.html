Dichondra evolvulacea var. carolinensis

Dichondra evolvulacea var. carolinensis, commonly known as Carolina dichondra, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the southeastern United States.

It features small, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive textured appearance. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or partially shaded areas.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to form dense ground cover.
