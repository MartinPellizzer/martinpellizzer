Dais argentea

Dais argentea, commonly known as silver daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its white, daisy-like flowers with a central yellow disc, and its silver-gray foliage. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with composite flower heads. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, typically from spring to autumn.
