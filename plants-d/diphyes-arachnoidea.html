Diphyes arachnoidea

Diphyes arachnoidea, commonly known as the spider's web plant, is a unique species characterized by its intricate, web-like leaf structures.

Its leaves are composed of numerous small, thread-like projections that create a delicate, lace-like appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. It has a rhizomatous root system that allows it to spread and colonize new areas effectively.

The botanical features of Diphyes arachnoidea make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and ecological study.
