Dracaena atropurpurea-pendula

Dracaena atropurpurea-pendula, commonly known as the weeping dracaena, is a striking ornamental plant characterized by its cascading, arching foliage.

Its leaves are long, narrow, and dark purple to reddish in color, giving it a dramatic and exotic appearance. The plant is native to tropical regions of Africa and thrives in warm, humid environments. It is relatively low-maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Dracaena atropurpurea-pendula prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to ensure healthy growth.
