Dictyodaphne micrantha

Dictyodaphne micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered dictyodaphne, is a member of the Araliaceae family.

It is a climbing shrub characterized by its compound leaves and small, white or greenish flowers. The plant is native to tropical regions of Asia and is often found in humid, shaded environments. Its stems are typically woody and can produce aerial roots for support.
