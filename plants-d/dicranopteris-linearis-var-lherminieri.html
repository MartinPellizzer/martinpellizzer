Dicranopteris linearis var. lherminieri

Dicranopteris linearis var. lherminieri, commonly known as the Lherminier's water fern, is a variant of the water fern species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial fern that thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found near streams or in damp forests. The plant features delicate, linear fronds with a distinctive, wavy margin. Its spores are produced on the underside of the fronds, contributing to its reproductive strategy.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in maintaining wetland habitats.
