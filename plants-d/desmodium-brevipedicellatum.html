Desmodium brevipedicellatum

Desmodium brevipedicellatum, commonly known as the small-pedicellate desmodium, is a perennial legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves with small, rounded leaflets and slender petioles. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodland or grassland habitats.

Desmodium brevipedicellatum is valued for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it beneficial in agricultural settings.
