Dianella revoluta f. denticulata

Dianella revoluta f. denticulata, commonly known as the denticulate flax lily, is a compact, evergreen perennial herb native to New Zealand.

It features narrow, strap-like leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the base, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped white flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

This ornamental plant is valued for its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
