Dendrobium carstensziense

Dendrobium carstensziense, commonly known as the Carstensz Mountains orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the high-altitude regions of Indonesia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in summer. The plant prefers cool, shaded environments with high humidity and well-draining soil. Its leaves are usually broad and lance-shaped, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Dendrobium carstensziense is valued in horticulture for its beauty and is often cultivated for its long-lasting blooms.
