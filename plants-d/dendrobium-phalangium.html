Dendrobium phalangium

Dendrobium phalangium, commonly known as the golden spike orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. The plant produces long, pendulous inflorescences with showy, golden-yellow flowers that bloom during the rainy season. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a rosette pattern, and it thrives in bright, indirect light.

Dendrobium phalangium is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
