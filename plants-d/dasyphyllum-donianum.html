Dasyphyllum donianum

Dasyphyllum donianum, commonly known as the African buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features bright yellow flowers with a distinctive cup-shaped structure, blooming from late spring to early autumn. The plant has alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Dasyphyllum donianum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its medicinal properties.
