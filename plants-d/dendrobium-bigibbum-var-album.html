Dendrobium bigibbum var. album

Dendrobium bigibbum var. album, commonly known as the white-lipped orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, cane-like stems and clusters of white or pale yellow flowers with a distinctive red or pink lip. The plant thrives in humid, tropical climates and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth. Its flowers are highly fragrant and bloom during the summer months.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in both home gardens and commercial orchid collections.
