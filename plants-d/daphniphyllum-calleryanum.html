Daphniphyllum calleryanum

Daphniphyllum calleryanum, commonly known as the Chinese holly, is a evergreen shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, glossy dark green foliage and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has elliptical leaves with a leathery texture and is often used in ornamental horticulture for its aesthetic appeal. It prefers acidic, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Daphniphyllum calleryanum is also noted for its ornamental berries, which add visual interest in the winter months.
