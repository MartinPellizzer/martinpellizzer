Dolichos hastatus

Dolichos hastatus, commonly known as the spurred cowpea, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, often with a distinctive spur at the base of the leaf stalk. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by elongated, cylindrical pods containing edible seeds. It is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it a beneficial crop in sustainable agriculture.

The plant's rapid growth and adaptability make it suitable for various ecological and agricultural applications.
