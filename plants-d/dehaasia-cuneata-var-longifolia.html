Dehaasia cuneata var. longifolia

Dehaasia cuneata var. longifolia, commonly known as the longleaf dehaasia, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
