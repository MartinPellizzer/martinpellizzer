Delosperma floribundum

Delosperma floribundum, commonly known as the ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant clusters of pink or red flowers during the summer months. It is a mat-forming species that spreads rapidly, forming dense ground cover.

Delosperma floribundum is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental appeal.
