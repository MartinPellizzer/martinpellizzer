Deutzia parviflora var. obscura

Deutzia parviflora var. obscura, commonly known as the dark-flowered deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features slender, arching stems that bear clusters of small, dark purple to almost black flowers in early summer. The foliage is typically dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vivid blooms. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

This ornamental plant is valued for its compact growth habit and long-lasting floral display.
