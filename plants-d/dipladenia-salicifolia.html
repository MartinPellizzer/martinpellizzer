Dipladenia salicifolia

Dipladenia salicifolia, commonly known as the weeping plectranthus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its pendulous, lance-shaped leaves that give it a weeping appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a decorative plant.

Dipladenia salicifolia is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for gardeners and indoor plant enthusiasts.
