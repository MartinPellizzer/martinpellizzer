Drimiopsis botryoides subsp. prostrata

Drimiopsis botryoides subsp.

prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate drimiopsis, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features a low-growing, sprawling habit with fleshy, dark green leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern.

The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white and yellow, which appear during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its compact size and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
