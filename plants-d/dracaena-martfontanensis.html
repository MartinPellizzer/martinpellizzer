Dracaena martfontanensis

Dracaena martfontanensis, commonly known as the Red-veined Dracaena, is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa.

This plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and striking, dark green leaves with distinctive red veins. It is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant due to its ornamental appearance and air-purifying qualities. Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
