Dolichos macrothyrsus

Dolichos macrothyrsus, commonly known as the large thyrse dolichos, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It features compound leaves with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets and produces striking, showy flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant bears pod fruits that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species in both ecological and agricultural contexts.
