Dombeya lucida

Dombeya lucida, commonly known as the white dombeya, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a velvety texture.

Dombeya lucida is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
