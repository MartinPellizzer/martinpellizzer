Dichaea gracillima

Dichaea gracillima, commonly known as the slender dichaea, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, trailing growth habit and small, elongated leaves. The plant produces clusters of pale green to white flowers with a distinctive spurred lip. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated by enthusiasts of rare orchids.
