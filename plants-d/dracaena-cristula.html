Dracaena cristula

Dracaena cristula, commonly known as the Christ's Crown, is a striking succulent native to the Canary Islands.

It features a rosette of fleshy, bluish-green leaves that form a compact, crown-like shape. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, white flowers that bloom in late summer. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and rock gardens.

Dracaena cristula is relatively easy to care for, requiring bright light and minimal water.
