Dacrycarpus Compactus

Dacrycarpus compactus is a unique coniferous plant native to New Zealand. It is known for its dense, bushy growth habit and distinctive needle-like leaves. This species thrives in coastal and lowland forest environments. Its compact form makes it an important part of local ecosystems. Despite its resilience, it faces threats from habitat loss and climate change.
