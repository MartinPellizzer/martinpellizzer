Drosanthemum pruinosum

Drosanthemum pruinosum, commonly known as the velvet flower, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are covered in a fine, powdery coating, giving it a velvety appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Drosanthemum pruinosum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and colorful blooms.
