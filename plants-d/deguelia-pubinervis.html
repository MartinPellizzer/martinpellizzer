Deguelia pubinervis

Deguelia pubinervis, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves with a velvety texture and prominent veins. Its flowers are typically yellow or greenish-yellow, and it produces small, woody pods containing seeds.

Deguelia pubinervis is often found in moist, shaded environments and is sometimes considered an invasive species in certain regions.
