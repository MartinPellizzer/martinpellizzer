Dracocephalum rockii

Dracocephalum rockii, commonly known as Chinese skullcap, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants known for their medicinal properties. The plant features square stems and opposite leaves that are often hairy, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its small, tubular flowers are typically blue to purple in color, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Dracocephalum rockii is valued in traditional medicine for its potential anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.
