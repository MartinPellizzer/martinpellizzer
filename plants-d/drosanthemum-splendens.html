Drosanthemum splendens

Drosanthemum splendens, commonly known as the Christmas star, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and vibrant, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces showy bracts that resemble petals, which are often mistaken for actual flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Drosanthemum splendens is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
