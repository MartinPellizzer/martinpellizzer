Dicrastylis gilesii f. densa

Dicrastylis gilesii f. densa, commonly known as the dense-stemmed flat sedge, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Cyperaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and compact growth habit, typically forming low, mat-like clusters. This subspecies is notable for its adaptability to saline conditions and its role in stabilizing coastal dunes.
