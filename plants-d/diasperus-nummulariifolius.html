Diasperus nummulariifolius

Diasperus nummulariifolius, commonly known as the silver dollar plant, is a succulent species native to southern Africa.

It features small, round, silver-gray leaves that are thick and fleshy, adapted for storing water. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit makes it an ideal choice for rock gardens or as a houseplant.
