Diospyros yaouhensis

It is native to the tropical rainforests of western Africa, particularly in countries like Cameroon and Gabon. The plant is characterized by its dark, dense wood, which is highly valued for its durability and aesthetic qualities. The leaves are simple, alternate, and usually elliptical in shape, with a glossy green surface.
