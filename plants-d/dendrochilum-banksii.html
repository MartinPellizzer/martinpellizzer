Dendrochilum banksii

Dendrochilum banksii, commonly known as the Banks' tree orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical rainforests and requires high humidity and bright, indirect light. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with reddish-brown markings, and it blooms throughout the year in suitable conditions.

Dendrochilum banksii is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and adaptability to controlled environments.
