Dendrobium formosum var. giganteum

Dendrobium formosum var. giganteum, commonly known as the Giant Formosan Orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, cane-like pseudobulbs covered in dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a subtle fragrance. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among orchid enthusiasts and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
