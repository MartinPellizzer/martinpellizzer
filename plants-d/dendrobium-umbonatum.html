Dendrobium umbonatum

Dendrobium umbonatum, commonly known as the mountain orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with cane-like stems that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink, often with a distinctive yellow or orange lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
