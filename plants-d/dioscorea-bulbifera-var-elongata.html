Dioscorea bulbifera var. elongata

Dioscorea bulbifera var. elongata, commonly known as the African yam bean, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its long, slender stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces edible tubers and aerial tubers, which are used in various cuisines. Its flowers are typically yellow or green, and it thrives in warm, humid climates.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
