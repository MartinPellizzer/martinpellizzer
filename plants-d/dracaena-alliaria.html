Dracaena alliaria

Dracaena alliaria, commonly known as the narrow-leaved dracaena, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are usually a deep green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters at the base of the leaves. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.

Dracaena alliaria prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
