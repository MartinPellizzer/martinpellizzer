Dieffenbachia seguine subvar. irrorata

Dieffenbachia seguine subvar. irrorata, commonly known as the Shiny Dieffenbachia, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its large, waxy, dark green leaves with prominent light green veins. The plant is a member of the Araceae family, which includes other popular houseplants like peace lilies. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its striking foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
