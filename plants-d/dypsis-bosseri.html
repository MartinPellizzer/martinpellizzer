Dypsis bosseri

Dypsis bosseri, commonly known as the Madagascar palm, is a succulent plant native to Madagascar.

It features a short, thick trunk covered in a fibrous, leafless sheath. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in the spring. These flowers are followed by bright orange to red berries.

Dypsis bosseri is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
