Duranta repens f. variegata

Duranta repens f. variegata, commonly known as the variegated blue mist shrub, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the Caribbean and Central America.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves with striking variegation of white, yellow, and green, adding visual interest to gardens. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped blue flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for container gardening and landscape design.
