Dioscorea kegeliana

Dioscorea kegeliana, commonly known as the Kegel yam, is a member of the Dioscoreaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a climbing herbaceous perennial that produces tuberous roots, which are often used in traditional medicine. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its tubers are valued for their nutritional content and have been used in various cultural practices.

The species is also of interest in botanical research due to its potential medicinal properties.
