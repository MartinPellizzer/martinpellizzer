Deutzia × Magnifica F. Erecta

Deutzia × magnifica f. erecta is a striking ornamental shrub prized for its showy, cone-shaped flower clusters. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and is known for its upright growth habit, making it a versatile addition to gardens. The plant produces clusters of white or pink flowers in early to mid-summer, adding a dramatic focal point to any landscape. Its compact size and elegant form make it suitable for borders, foundation plantings, or container gardens. This unique form of Deutzia offers both beauty and resilience, appealing to gardeners seeking ornamental value with low maintenance.
