Diplotaxis simplex

Diplotaxis simplex, commonly known as the African dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing annual herb with bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant features alternate, hairy leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

The plant is noted for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
