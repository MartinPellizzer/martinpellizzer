Dalbergia stenocarpa var. menabeensis

Dalbergia stenocarpa var. menabeensis, commonly known as the Menabe rosewood, is a member of the Fabaceae family and is native to Madagascar.

It is a small to medium-sized tree characterized by its dark, aromatic wood and dense foliage. The plant features bipinnate leaves and produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in racemose inflorescences. Its fruit is a legume, typically containing several flattened, winged seeds.

Due to its unique appearance and valuable timber, it is of interest in both botanical research and conservation efforts.
