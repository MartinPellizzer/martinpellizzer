Dendrobium phalangillum

Dendrobium phalangillum, commonly known as the golden spike orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with multiple stems that can reach up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often arranged in racemes. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrobium phalangillum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
