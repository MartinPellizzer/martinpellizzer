Deutzia rubens

Deutzia rubens, commonly known as the red deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features clusters of showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in early summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. The plant has slender, arching branches and small, ovate leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Deutzia rubens is valued for its ornamental beauty and relatively low maintenance requirements.
