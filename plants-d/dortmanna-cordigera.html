Dortmanna cordigera

It is a small, acrocarpous moss that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rock crevices. Its reproductive structures, including the capsules, are borne on short, erect stalks.

Dortmanna cordigera is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
