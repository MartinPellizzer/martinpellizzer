Dendrobium unicarinatum

Dendrobium unicarinatum, commonly known as the golden spike orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with elongated, cane-like pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
