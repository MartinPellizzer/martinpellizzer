Draba cana subsp. cana

Draba cana subsp.

cana, commonly known as the alpine penny-cress, is a member of the Brassicaceae family. It is a small, herbaceous plant typically found in alpine and subalpine regions.

The plant has basal leaves that are lobed or pinnatifid, and its flowers are bright yellow, arranged in clusters. It produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds.

This species is adapted to cold climates and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils.
