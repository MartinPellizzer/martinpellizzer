Drimia rigidifolia

Drimia rigidifolia, commonly known as the rigid stem lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, stiff stems and showy white or pale purple flowers. Drimia rigidifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
