Deparia acrostichoides

Deparia acrostichoides, commonly known as the broadleaf huperzia, is a species of fern belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its large, leathery, evergreen leaves that are typically bipinnate, giving it a distinctive, feathery appearance. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its rhizome is thick and fleshy, allowing it to store water and nutrients effectively.

Deparia acrostichoides is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
