Delissea ferox var. laevicalyx

Delissea ferox var. laevicalyx, commonly known as the rough-leaved delissea, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It features robust, upright stems that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height. The leaves are ovate to elliptic, with a rough, textured surface and prominent veins. The flowers are small, clustered in dense inflorescences, and typically have a pale yellow to greenish hue.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
