Daviesia bursarioides

Daviesia bursarioides, commonly known as the narrow-leafed broom, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae, characterized by its pod fruits and often nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces yellow or orange flowers in late winter to spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
