Drosanthemum subspinosum

Drosanthemum subspinosum, commonly known as the spiny drosanthemum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, known for its succulent characteristics and ability to store water in its fleshy leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
