Dendrobium quaifei

Dendrobium quaifei, commonly known as the Qaifei orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to China.

It belongs to the Dendrobium genus, which is known for its diverse and often colorful orchid species. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, utilizing its aerial roots to absorb moisture and nutrients from the environment. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with a range of colors including white, yellow, and pink.

Dendrobium quaifei is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
