Dolomiaea Frolowii

Dolomiaea frolowii is a rare and enigmatic flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, known for its diverse and often striking floral structures. This species is characterized by its small, pale yellow to white flowers and unique leaf morphology. Due to its limited habitat and sensitivity to environmental changes, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this delicate and lesser-known botanical gem.
