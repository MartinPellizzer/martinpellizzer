Dendrobium leonis

Dendrobium leonis, commonly known as the lion's tail orchid, is a epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a tall, branched stem with numerous fleshy, greenish-yellow pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with red and purple markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, loose soil.

Dendrobium leonis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
