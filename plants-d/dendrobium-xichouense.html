Dendrobium xichouense

Dendrobium xichouense, commonly known as the Xichou orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to China.

It is characterized by its erect, branched pseudobulbs and long, narrow leaves. The plant produces numerous fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink, typically during the spring and summer months. It thrives in humid, well-lit environments and requires good air circulation.

Dendrobium xichouense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
