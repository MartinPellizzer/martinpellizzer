Dupontia Fisheri Subsp. Pelligera

Dupontia fisheri subsp. pelligera is a rare and fascinating plant native to the coastal regions of southern California. This subspecies thrives in unique microhabitats, often found in sandy dunes and along the edges of salt marshes. Its delicate, grass-like appearance belies its resilience to harsh environmental conditions. The plant's limited distribution makes it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike. Protecting this species is crucial for preserving the biodiversity of its fragile ecosystem.
