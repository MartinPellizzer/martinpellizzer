Dendranthema sichotense

Dendranthema sichotense, commonly known as the Siberian geranium, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features upright stems covered in soft, hairy foliage and produces clusters of showy, pink to purple flowers. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Dendranthema sichotense is also known for its medicinal properties, traditionally used in herbal remedies.
