Diaphananthe densiflora

Diaphananthe densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered diaphananthe, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, white flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate or elliptical in shape. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in humid forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
