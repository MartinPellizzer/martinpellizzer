Dendranthema × ogawae

Dendranthema × ogawae, commonly known as Shirokuma, is a hybrid perennial plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and showy white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30–60 cm and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its compact growth habit and attractive appearance.

Dendranthema × ogawae is also valued in traditional Japanese gardens for its aesthetic and cultural significance.
