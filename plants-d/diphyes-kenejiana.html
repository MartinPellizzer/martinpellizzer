Diphyes kenejiana

Diphyes kenejiana, commonly known as the Kenejian bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue, typically blooming in late summer. The plant has a fibrous root system and lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or alpine environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and specialized collections.
