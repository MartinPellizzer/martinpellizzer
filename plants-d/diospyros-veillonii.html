Diospyros veillonii

Diospyros veillonii, commonly known as the African persimmon, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Ebenaceae family, which includes ebony and persimmon trees. The plant is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and produces small, yellowish-green flowers. Its fruit is a fleshy, orange-red berry that contains numerous seeds.

Diospyros veillonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some African cultures.
