Dendrophorbium silvani

Dendrophorbium silvani, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a rare and specialized plant found in specific high-altitude environments.

It is characterized by its unique silvery foliage, which helps reflect intense sunlight in its native habitat. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom during the short growing season. Its root system is adapted to absorb nutrients from rocky substrates, showcasing its resilience.
