Dendrobium nutans

Dendrobium nutans, commonly known as the nodding orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with upright, often branched stems that can reach several feet in length. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with a distinctive nodding posture. Its leaves are typically fleshy and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Dendrobium nutans thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media for optimal growth.
