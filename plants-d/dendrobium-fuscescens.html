Dendrobium fuscescens

Dendrobium fuscescens, commonly known as the dark red orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a tall, slender stem with clustered, dark red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, meaning it grows in successive shoots rather than a single stem. Its leaves are linear and arranged spirally along the stem, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Dendrobium fuscescens is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.
