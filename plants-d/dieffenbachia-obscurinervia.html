Dieffenbachia obscurinervia

Dieffenbachia obscurinervia, commonly known as the Black Vein Dieffenbachia, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, dark green leaves with prominent, dark veins that create a striking contrast. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other popular houseplants like peace lilies. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
