Damburneya rudis

Damburneya rudis, commonly known as the slender damburneya, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy, arranged in a whorled pattern along its stems. The plant produces clusters of small, white, bell-shaped flowers that are highly fragrant and attract various pollinators. Damburneya rudis thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for coastal gardens and landscaping.
