Dolpojestella shrestaeana

Dolpojestella shrestaeana, commonly known as the Dolpo jasmine, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Nepal.

It belongs to the Oleaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, white to yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in shady, moist environments at high altitudes. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a smooth texture.
