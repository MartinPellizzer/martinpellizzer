Dendrobium longlingense

Dendrobium longlingense, commonly known as the Longling orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to China.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems and clusters of fragrant, pale yellow to greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on rocky slopes or in mountainous regions. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged spirally along the stem.

Dendrobium longlingense is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
