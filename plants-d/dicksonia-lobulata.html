Dicksonia lobulata

Dicksonia lobulata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feathery, compound leaves. The fronds are typically dark green and have a distinctive, narrow shape. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The plant plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
