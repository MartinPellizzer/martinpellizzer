Danais nigra

Danais nigra, commonly known as the black danais, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its dark purple to black flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.
