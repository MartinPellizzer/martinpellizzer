Dendrobium reinwardtii

Dendrobium reinwardtii, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, cane-like stems that store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in its natural habitat. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellow flowers with a distinctive spurred lip. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium reinwardtii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
