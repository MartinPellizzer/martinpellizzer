Dendrolobium stipatum

Dendrolobium stipatum, commonly known as the slender wattle, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing drought resistance through its deep root system. It plays a role in soil stabilization and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.

Dendrolobium stipatum is also valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
