Dovea rigens

Dovea rigens, commonly known as the rigid dove's-foot cranesbill, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, branching stems and distinctive blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and borders.

Dovea rigens is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
