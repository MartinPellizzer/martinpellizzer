Demosthenesia spectabilis

Demosthenesia spectabilis, commonly known as the splendid demosthenes, is a rare flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

It is characterized by its striking, violet-blue petals that bloom in late summer. The plant has a compact, shrub-like growth habit with dark green, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in cool, moist environments and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered endangered and is the subject of conservation efforts.
