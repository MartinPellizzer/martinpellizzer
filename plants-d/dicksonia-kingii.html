Dicksonia kingii

Dicksonia kingii, commonly known as the King's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery fronds that can reach lengths of up to 3 meters. The plant has a slow growth rate and can live for several decades, making it a long-lived species. Its fronds are typically bright green and have a distinctive, elegant appearance.

Dicksonia kingii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
