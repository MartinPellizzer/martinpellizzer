Dimerandra major

Dimerandra major, commonly known as the Cape rose or yellow dimerandra, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Rutaceae family.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its attractive, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in spring. The plant features simple, alternate leaves with a rough, textured surface. Its flowers are typically borne in clusters and are notable for their fragrant, citrus-like scent.

Dimerandra major is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and naturalized areas.
