Ditepalanthus afzelii

Ditepalanthus afzelii, commonly known as the African milk bush, is a shrub native to parts of Africa.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its milky sap, which is a distinguishing botanical feature. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are often lance-shaped and have a rough texture. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, typical of the Euphorbiaceae family.

The plant is adapted to arid environments and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
