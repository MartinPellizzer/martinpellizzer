Dendrobium pisibulbum

Dendrobium pisibulbum, commonly known as the pink-stemmed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers with prominent yellow lips. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Dendrobium pisibulbum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
