Dactylorhiza praetermissa subsp. junialis

Dactylorhiza praetermissa subsp.

junialis, commonly known as the lesser twayblade, is a terrestrial orchid native to Europe and parts of Asia. It is a herbaceous plant with a short, rhizomatous stem and a single, narrow, lance-shaped leaf.

The plant produces a spike of small, greenish-white flowers with prominent, hooded sepals and petals. It thrives in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and heathlands.

The species is characterized by its lack of a visible stem and its ability to form dense colonies in suitable conditions.
