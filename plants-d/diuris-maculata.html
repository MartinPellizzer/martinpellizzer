Diuris maculata

Diuris maculata, commonly known as the spotted orchid, is a striking terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, orchid-like flowers that are typically pink or purple with distinctive spots. The plant features a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces a spike of showy, fragrant blooms. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and open forests.

Diuris maculata is a popular species among orchid enthusiasts due to its beauty and unique appearance.
