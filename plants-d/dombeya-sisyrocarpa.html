Dombeya sisyrocarpa

Dombeya sisyrocarpa, commonly known as the silky dombeya, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, fluffy white or pink flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often covered in a fine, silky hair.

Dombeya sisyrocarpa is adapted to dry environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
