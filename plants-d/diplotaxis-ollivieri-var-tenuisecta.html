Diplotaxis ollivieri var. tenuisecta

Diplotaxis ollivieri var. tenuisecta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved African mustard, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, four-petaled flowers and capsule fruits. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in grassland habitats. Its leaves are linear and somewhat succulent, adapted to arid conditions.
