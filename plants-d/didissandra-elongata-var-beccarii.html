Didissandra elongata var. beccarii

Didissandra elongata var. beccarii, commonly known as the Beccari's fig.

This evergreen shrub is native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia. It is characterized by its glossy, elongated leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are followed by edible, round fruits. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.

It plays a role in local ecosystems by providing food for various wildlife species.
