Dracaena doucetii

Dracaena doucetii, commonly known as the Song of India, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, narrow, dark green leaves that are often adorned with striking white or yellow variegation. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires moderate watering. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by red berries, though these are rarely seen in cultivation.

Dracaena doucetii is popular as a houseplant due to its air-purifying qualities and aesthetic appeal.
