Drypetes laevis

Drypetes laevis, commonly known as the smooth drypetes, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, often pale bark and elliptical leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

It is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
