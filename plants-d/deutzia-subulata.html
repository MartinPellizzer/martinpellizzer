Deutzia subulata

Deutzia subulata, commonly known as the needleleaf deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features slender, needle-like leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers in early to mid-summer. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Deutzia subulata is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
