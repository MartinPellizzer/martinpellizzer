Dierama pallidum

Dierama pallidum, commonly known as the silverleaf dierama, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, grass-like stems and clusters of small, pale blue to white flowers. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery bloom, giving it its common name. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Dierama pallidum is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its elegant, delicate appearance.
