Dracophyllum rosmarinifolium var. politum

Dracophyllum rosmarinifolium var. politum, commonly known as the smooth-leaved dracophyllum, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and aromatic, needle-like leaves that are smooth and glossy. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in spring, adding ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its foliage is often used in herbal remedies due to its aromatic properties.
