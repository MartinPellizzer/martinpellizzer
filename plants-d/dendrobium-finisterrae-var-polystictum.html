Dendrobium finisterrae var. polystictum

Dendrobium finisterrae var. polystictum, commonly known as the Polystictum Dendrobium, is a rare and exquisite orchid native to the high-altitude regions of Southeast Asia.

This variant is characterized by its strikingly patterned leaves, which often display a mix of green, white, and brown hues. The plant is an epiphyte, typically growing on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. It produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers during the late spring or early summer.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
