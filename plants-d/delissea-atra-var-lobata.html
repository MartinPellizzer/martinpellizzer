Delissea atra var. lobata

Delissea atra var. lobata, commonly known as the black delissea, is a rare and striking plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dark, almost black, velvety leaves that are broad and lobed, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its rarity and unique morphology, it is highly sought after by botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
