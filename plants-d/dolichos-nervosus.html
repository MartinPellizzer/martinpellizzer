Dolichos nervosus

Dolichos nervosus, commonly known as the veined dolichos, is a leguminous plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and trifoliate leaves with prominently veined leaflets. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that are typically clustered in loose inflorescences. Its pods are elongated and contain several elliptical seeds.

Dolichos nervosus is native to tropical and subtropical regions and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and forest edges.
