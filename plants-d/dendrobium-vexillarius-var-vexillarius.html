Dendrobium vexillarius var. vexillarius

Dendrobium vexillarius var. vexillarius, commonly known as the Orchid of the Southern Cross, is a species of orchid native to Australia.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. Its inflorescence is often adorned with a distinctive, boat-shaped lip that is a key identifying feature.

The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
