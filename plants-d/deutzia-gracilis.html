Deutzia gracilis

Deutzia gracilis, commonly known as slender deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features upright, slender stems that bear clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers in early summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Deutzia gracilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
