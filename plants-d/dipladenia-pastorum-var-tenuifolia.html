Dipladenia pastorum var. tenuifolia

Dipladenia pastorum var. tenuifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf Dipladenia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its trailing growth habit and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and slightly hairy, with a bright green color. It thrives in warm, humid conditions and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
