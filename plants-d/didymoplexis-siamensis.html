Didymoplexis siamensis

Didymoplexis siamensis, commonly known as the Siam orchid, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, pendulous flowers. The plant produces small, fragrant blooms in shades of white and pink, often with a yellow or greenish throat. Its leaves are typically oval and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Didymoplexis siamensis thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
