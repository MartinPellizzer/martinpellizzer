Desmodium wade

Desmodium wade, commonly known as purple desmodium, is a leguminous plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which are typically purple-tinged, and its small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces cylindrical, bean-like pods that contain several seeds. It is native to various regions in Africa and is often found in grasslands and disturbed soils.

Desmodium wade is valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it useful in sustainable agriculture and soil enrichment.
