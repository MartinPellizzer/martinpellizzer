Dracula flabellulata

Dracula flabellulata, commonly known as the Flabellate Dracula, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its striking, pendulous flowers that resemble a fan, hence its common name. The plant produces dark, velvety leaves and has a unique growth habit, often growing on trees as an epiphyte. Its flowers are typically red or purple and emit a faint fragrance.

Dracula flabellulata is a rare and specialized orchid, requiring specific environmental conditions to thrive.
