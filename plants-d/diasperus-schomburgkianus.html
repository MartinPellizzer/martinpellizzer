Diasperus schomburgkianus

Diasperus schomburgkianus, commonly known as the Schomburgk's daisies, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, preferring well-drained soils and full sunlight. Diasperus schomburgkianus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
