Delphinium tatsienense var. chinghaiense

Delphinium tatsienense var. chinghaiense, commonly known as the Chinghai larkspur, is a perennial herb native to the Qinghai region of China.

It is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant produces striking blue to purple flowers in racemes, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lanceolate and have a serrated margin, typical of the Delphinium genus.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
