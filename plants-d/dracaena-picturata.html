Dracaena picturata

Dracaena picturata, commonly known as the painted dracaena, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with shades of green, yellow, and white. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear at the base of the leaves. It is a slow-growing, evergreen shrub that thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Dracaena picturata is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
