Digitaria eglumis var. reducta

Digitaria eglumis var. reducta, commonly known as slender bluestem, is a grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and slender, erect growth habit. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as prairies, roadsides, and disturbed soils. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers.

This variety is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its adaptability and ability to stabilize soil.
