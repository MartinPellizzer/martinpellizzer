Deutzia chunii

Deutzia chunii, commonly known as the Chinese deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and features slender, arching branches. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers in early summer, which are followed by small, red berries. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and have a slightly serrated margin.

Deutzia chunii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its spring blossoms and autumn foliage.
