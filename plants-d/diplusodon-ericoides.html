Diplusodon ericoides

Diplusodon ericoides, commonly known as the heath leaved everlet, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries, and is characterized by its small, evergreen leaves. The plant produces clusters of white or pale pink flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
