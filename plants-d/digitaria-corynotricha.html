Digitaria corynotricha

Digitaria corynotricha, commonly known as slender digitgrass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas and is often found in grasslands and along roadsides. It has a fibrous root system and is adapted to thrive in nutrient-poor soils.

Digitaria corynotricha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
