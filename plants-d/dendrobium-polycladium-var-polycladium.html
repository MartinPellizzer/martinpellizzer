Dendrobium polycladium var. polycladium

Dendrobium polycladium var. polycladium, commonly known as the clustered dendrobium, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with multiple pseudobulbs and long, linear leaves. The plant produces racemes of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
