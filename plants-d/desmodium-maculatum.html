Desmodium maculatum

Desmodium maculatum, commonly known as purple top clover, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a legume, belonging to the Fabaceae family, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it valuable in soil enrichment. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant purple flowers and compact growth habit.
