Dovyalis monbazae

Dovyalis monbazae, commonly known as the Natal plum, is a small shrub native to southern Africa.

It produces small, yellow-orange fruits that are edible and have a tart flavor. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually oval or elliptic in shape. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

The Natal plum is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in local ecosystems.
