Dendrochilum recurvum

Dendrochilum recurvum, commonly known as the twisted-lip orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments within forest understories. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its flowering stems. Its flowers are usually small, with a distinctive twisted lip and a variety of colors, including yellow, green, and red.

Dendrochilum recurvum is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique morphology and ornamental value.
