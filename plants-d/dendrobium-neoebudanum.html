Dendrobium neoebudanum

Dendrobium neoebudanum, commonly known as the Neobudan orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Dendrobium genus, which includes many epiphytic and terrestrial species. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest understories, thriving in humid environments. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with a distinctive lip that is often marked with purple or red.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
