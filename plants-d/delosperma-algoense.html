Delosperma algoense

Delosperma algoense, commonly known as the African ice plant, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems and leaves. It produces a large number of seeds, which are dispersed by the wind, aiding in its spread across dry landscapes.

Delosperma algoense is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
