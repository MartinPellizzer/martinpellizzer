Disa rutenbergiana

Disa rutenbergiana, commonly known as the Rutenberg's disa, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in regions with high altitudes. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with a distinctive spurred petal that aids in pollination.

Disa rutenbergiana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
