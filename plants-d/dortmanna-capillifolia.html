Dortmanna capillifolia

Dortmanna capillifolia, commonly known as the slender hair grass, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Poaceae, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its fine, hair-like leaves. The plant typically forms dense mats on rocky or sandy soils, often in moist or shaded environments. Dortmanna capillifolia plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
