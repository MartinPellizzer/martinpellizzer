Disticholiparis govidjoae

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers with a distinctive color pattern. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a prominent central lip that is often a different color.
