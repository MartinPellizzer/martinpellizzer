Dolia vermiculata

Dolia vermiculata, commonly known as the vermilion leaf orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and green foliage, which contributes to its ornamental value. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers, which are usually white or pale pink, bloom in clusters and emit a subtle fragrance.

Dolia vermiculata requires high humidity and indirect light to thrive, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
