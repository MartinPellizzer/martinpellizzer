Dimorphotheca venusta var. venusta

Dimorphotheca venusta var. venusta, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, with a prominent central disc. The plant typically grows as a shrub or perennial herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, while its stems are erect and often branched.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
