Diospyros elliptica var. savaiiensis

Diospyros elliptica var. savaiiensis, commonly known as the Savaii persimmon, is a species of flowering plant native to the island of Savaii in Samoa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its elliptical, leathery leaves that are typically dark green in color.
