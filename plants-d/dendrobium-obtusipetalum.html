Dendrobium obtusipetalum

Dendrobium obtusipetalum, commonly known as the broad-petaled orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with cane-like stems that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange, often with broad, rounded petals. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium obtusipetalum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
