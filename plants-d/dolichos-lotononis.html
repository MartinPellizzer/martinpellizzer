Dolichos lotononis

Dolichos lotononis, commonly known as the sacred bean or lotononis bean, is a climbing legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and elongated, bean-like pods. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are often visited by pollinators such as bees. Its seeds are encased in a fibrous pod and are used in traditional medicine and as a food source.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and historical significance in ancient cultures.
