Dioscorea anconensis

Dioscorea anconensis, commonly known as the Ancon yam, is a species of flowering plant in the family Dioscoreaceae.

It is native to Central and South America, particularly found in countries like Ecuador and Peru. The plant is a perennial vine that produces tuberous roots, which are the primary edible part. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a rough texture, while its flowers are small and greenish-white in color.

The Ancon yam is valued for its nutritional content and has been used in traditional medicine for various health purposes.
