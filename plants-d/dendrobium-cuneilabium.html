Dendrobium cuneilabium

Dendrobium cuneilabium, commonly known as the narrow-lipped orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with a distinctive lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrobium cuneilabium is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
