Draba stenobotrys var. leiocarpa

Draba stenobotrys var. leiocarpa, commonly known as the smooth-leaved winter cress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, glabrous leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes during the late winter to early spring. It is typically found in alpine and arctic environments, thriving in nutrient-poor, rocky soils.

Its ecological importance includes serving as a food source for various pollinators and contributing to soil stabilization in harsh environments.
