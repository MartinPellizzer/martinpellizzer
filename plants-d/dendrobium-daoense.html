Dendrobium daoense

Dendrobium daoense, commonly known as the Dao Mountains orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Dendrobium daoense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
