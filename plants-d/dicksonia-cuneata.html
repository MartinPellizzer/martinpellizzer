Dicksonia cuneata

Dicksonia cuneata, commonly known as the slender tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feathery, compound fronds. The plant has a unique reproductive system, with sporangia developing on the undersides of its fronds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its slow growth and long lifespan make it an important component of New Zealand's native ecosystems.
