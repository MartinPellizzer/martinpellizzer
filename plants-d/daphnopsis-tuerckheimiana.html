Daphnopsis tuerckheimiana

The plant has a compact growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in shaded garden settings. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, with a subtle aromatic scent when touched.

Daphnopsis tuerckheimiana is relatively low-maintenance once established, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade.
