Draba nivalis var. exigua

Draba nivalis var. exigua, commonly known as the dwarf rockcress, is a small flowering plant native to alpine and arctic regions.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit, with tiny, basal leaves and clusters of small white or pale yellow flowers. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to cold climates. It is often found in mountainous areas of Europe and Asia.

Due to its specialized habitat, it is considered a rare and sensitive species.
