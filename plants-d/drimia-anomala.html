Drimia anomala

Drimia anomala, commonly known as the African rain lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features strap-like, linear leaves that are often bluish-green in color. The plant produces striking, trumpet-shaped white or pale purple flowers during the rainy season. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Drimia anomala is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
