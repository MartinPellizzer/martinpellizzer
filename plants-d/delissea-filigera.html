Delissea filigera

Delissea filigera, commonly known as the silver thread plant, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, thread-like leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, pale blue to purple flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is often cultivated in arid and semi-arid regions.
