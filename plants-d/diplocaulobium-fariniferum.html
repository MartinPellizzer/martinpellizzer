Diplocaulobium fariniferum

Diplocaulobium fariniferum, commonly known as the farina-bearing diplocaulobium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

Its leaves are usually narrow and covered with a fine, powdery coating, giving it a distinctive appearance.
