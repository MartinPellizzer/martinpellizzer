Dorotheanthus booysenii

Dorotheanthus booysenii, commonly known as the Booyesman daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers with a central brown or purple disc, resembling a daisy. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 cm and has narrow, gray-green leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Dorotheanthus booysenii is drought-tolerant and requires minimal maintenance once established.
