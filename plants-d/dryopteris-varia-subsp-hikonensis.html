Dryopteris varia subsp. hikonensis

Dryopteris varia subsp.

hikonensis, commonly known as the Hikonensis Shield Fern, is a fern species native to Japan. It belongs to the Dryopteris genus, which is part of the family Athyriaceae.

This subspecies is characterized by its distinctive fronds with a serrated margin and a pale, almost white underside. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in forest undergrowth.

The plant's rhizomes are creeping and produce dense clusters of fertile fronds that contribute to its ornamental value.
