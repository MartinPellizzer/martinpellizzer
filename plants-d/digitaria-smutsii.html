Digitaria smutsii

Digitaria smutsii, commonly known as the Natal grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

Digitaria smutsii is often found in open grasslands and along riverbanks, contributing to the ecological diversity of its habitat.
