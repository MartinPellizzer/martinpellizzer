Dissotis humilis

Dissotis humilis, commonly known as the small mistflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It features small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has slender, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Dissotis humilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
