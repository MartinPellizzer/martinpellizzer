Deutzia microcarpa

Deutzia microcarpa, commonly known as the dwarf deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring, typically in clusters along the stems. The plant has slender, arching branches and small, ovate leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

Deutzia microcarpa is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.
