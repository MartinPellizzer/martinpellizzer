Disticholiparis graciliscapa

Disticholiparis graciliscapa, commonly known as the slender-leafed fern, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, bipinnate leaves that form a dense, fern-like canopy. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are pollinated by wind. Its root system is fibrous and adapted to thrive in moist, shaded environments.

Disticholiparis graciliscapa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
