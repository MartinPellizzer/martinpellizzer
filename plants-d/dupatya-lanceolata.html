Dupatya lanceolata

Dupatya lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces bright yellow flowers with a central disk. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
