Diplotaxis maritima

Diplotaxis maritima, commonly known as sea rocket, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean and parts of Europe.

Diplotaxis maritima is often found in dunes and along shorelines, contributing to coastal vegetation and soil stabilization.
