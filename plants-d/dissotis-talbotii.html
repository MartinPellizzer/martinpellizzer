Dissotis talbotii

Dissotis talbotii, commonly known as the Talbot's spider-orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and delicate, spider-like flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellowish, with a distinctive, elongated shape that resembles a spider.

Dissotis talbotii is often found in coastal regions and is adapted to well-drained soils and moderate to high light conditions.
