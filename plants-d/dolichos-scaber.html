Dolichos scaber

Dolichos scaber, commonly known as the hairy runner bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, hairy stems that support its growth along trellises or supports. The plant produces elongated, green pods that mature to a yellowish color, containing edible seeds. Its leaves are trifoliate, with hairy surfaces, and it thrives in warm, humid climates.

Dolichos scaber is valued for its nutritional seeds and is often cultivated for both food and ornamental purposes.
