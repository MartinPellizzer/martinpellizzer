Dombeya montana subsp. macrantha

Dombeya montana subsp.

macrantha, commonly known as the mountain dombeya, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa. It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often appearing in clusters.

The plant has hairy, aromatic leaves that are typically ovate to elliptic in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or forested areas. Dombeya montana subsp.

macrantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
