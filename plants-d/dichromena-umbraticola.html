Dichromena umbraticola

Dichromena umbraticola, commonly known as the shadowy dichromena, is a rare and unique plant species characterized by its striking dichromatic foliage.

Its leaves exhibit a dual coloration, typically displaying a dark green on one side and a lighter, almost silvery hue on the other. This plant thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions with high humidity. The species is notable for its intricate leaf structure and its ability to adapt to low-light conditions.
