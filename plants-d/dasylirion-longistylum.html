Dasylirion longistylum

Dasylirion longistylum, commonly known as the longleaf yucca, is a perennial plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, narrow leaves that are often blue-green in color and have a waxy coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with clusters of white to pale blue flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for xeriscaping and native gardens.
