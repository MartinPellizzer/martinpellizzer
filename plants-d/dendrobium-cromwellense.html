Dendrobium cromwellense

Dendrobium cromwellense, commonly known as the Cromwell orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its clustered, cane-like pseudobulbs. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its rarity and conservation status, it is often cultivated in specialized horticultural settings.
