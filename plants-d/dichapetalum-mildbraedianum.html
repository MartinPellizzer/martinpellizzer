Dichapetalum mildbraedianum

Dichapetalum mildbraedianum, commonly known as the African starwood, is a unique flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it its common name. The plant has a woody structure with smooth, greyish bark and is often found in forested areas. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a slightly serrated edge.

Dichapetalum mildbraedianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual interest.
