Doryanthes palmeri

Doryanthes palmeri, commonly known as the Christmas bell, is a striking shrub native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy red or orange flowers that resemble bells, which bloom during the winter months. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in its arid habitat. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Due to its unique appearance and seasonal blooming, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
