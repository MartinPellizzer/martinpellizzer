Dolichos luteus

Dolichos luteus, commonly known as the yellow cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its pod-bearing fruit and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant produces yellow or orange flowers and long, slender pods that contain edible seeds. Dolichos luteus is often cultivated for its nutritional seeds, which are rich in protein and essential amino acids.

Its growth habit makes it a valuable crop in agricultural systems.
