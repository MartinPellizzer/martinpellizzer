Dombeya gemina

Dombeya gemina, commonly known as the mountain dombeya, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are usually elliptical or ovate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Dombeya gemina thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in moist, shaded environments within its native habitat.
