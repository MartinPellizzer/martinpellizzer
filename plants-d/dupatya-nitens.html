Dupatya nitens

Dupatya nitens, commonly known as the slender bamboo orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its inflorescence consists of clusters of small, colorful blooms.

Dupatya nitens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
