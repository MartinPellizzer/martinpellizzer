Drynaria willdenowii

Drynaria willdenowii, commonly known as the cliff fern, is a tropical evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its erect, branched fronds that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments, typically on rocky or cliff faces. Its rhizomes are thick and fleshy, allowing it to store water and nutrients.
