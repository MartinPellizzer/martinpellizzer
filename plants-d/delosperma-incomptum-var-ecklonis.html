Delosperma incomptum var. ecklonis

Delosperma incomptum var. ecklonis, commonly known as the Ecklon's ice plant, is a succulent species native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, and yellow, typically blooming in spring. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in arid conditions.
