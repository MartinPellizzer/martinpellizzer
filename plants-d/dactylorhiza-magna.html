Dactylorhiza magna

Dactylorhiza magna, commonly known as the giant marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, showy flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy habitats, preferring acidic soils. Its roots are specialized for absorbing nutrients from the surrounding environment.

Dactylorhiza magna is a perennial species that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters.
