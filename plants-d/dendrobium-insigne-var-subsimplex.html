Dendrobium insigne var. subsimplex

Dendrobium insigne var. subsimplex, commonly known as the slender-leafed orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features elongated, narrow leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces fragrant, spike-like inflorescences with small, white to pale yellow flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated for its elegant appearance and unique foliage.
