Duguetia ibonensis

Duguetia ibonensis, commonly known as the Malabar nightshade, is a species of flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually white or purple in color. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a slightly hairy texture.

The fruit is a fleshy, berry-like structure that contains several seeds.
