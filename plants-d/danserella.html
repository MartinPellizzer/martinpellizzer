Danserella

Danserella, commonly known as the "butterfly orchid," is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

The leaves of Danserella are typically narrow and grass-like, while the inflorescence consists of a spike bearing small, fragrant blooms. They thrive in humid, shaded environments and require well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
