Dracaena phanerophlebia

Dracaena phanerophlebia, commonly known as the broad-leafed dracaena, is a member of the Asparagaceae family native to tropical regions of Africa.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright indirect light for optimal growth.

Dracaena phanerophlebia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying properties.
