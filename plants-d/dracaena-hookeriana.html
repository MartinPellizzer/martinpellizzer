Dracaena hookeriana

Dracaena hookeriana, commonly known as the Hooker's dracaena, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, narrow, dark green leaves that are often variegated with yellow or white markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by red berries, which are edible for birds but not for humans. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dracaena hookeriana is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
