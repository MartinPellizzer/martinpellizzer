Dianella intermedia f. lutea

Dianella intermedia f. lutea, commonly known as the yellow flax lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves that form a dense clump. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange, which bloom in spring. Its compact growth habit and vibrant floral display make it a popular choice for landscaping in temperate climates.
