Dendrobium wenzelii

Dendrobium wenzelii, commonly known as the Wenzel's orchid, is a sympodial epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a tall, slender stem with numerous fleshy, sword-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with prominent, showy lips. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium wenzelii is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
