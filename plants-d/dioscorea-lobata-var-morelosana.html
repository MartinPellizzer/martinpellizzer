Dioscorea lobata var. morelosana

Dioscorea lobata var. morelosana, commonly known as Morelos yam, is a climbing vine native to Mexico.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its tuberous roots, which are the primary edible part. The plant produces small, green flowers followed by cylindrical, yellowish fruits. Its leaves are heart-shaped with a slightly hairy surface.

This variety is valued for its nutritional content and traditional use in local cuisines.
