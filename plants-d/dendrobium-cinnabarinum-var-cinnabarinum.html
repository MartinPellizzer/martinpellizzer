Dendrobium cinnabarinum var. cinnabarinum

Dendrobium cinnabarinum var. cinnabarinum, commonly known as the red-flowered orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a sympodial orchid with a tall, cane-like stem that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces clusters of bright orange-red flowers with a distinctive yellow throat, which bloom during the summer months. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

This orchid is prized for its vibrant color and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
