Descurainia multifoliata

Descurainia multifoliata, commonly known as yellow rocket, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and finely divided, feather-like leaves. Native to North America, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats. The plant produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds.

It is often considered an invasive species in some regions due to its rapid growth and spread.
