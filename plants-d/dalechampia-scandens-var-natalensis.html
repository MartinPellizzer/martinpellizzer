Dalechampia scandens var. natalensis

Dalechampia scandens var. natalensis, commonly known as Natal false hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in woodland and forested areas. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are usually opposite, ovate to elliptic, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.
