Delphinium hybridum var. gracillimum

Delphinium hybridum var. gracillimum, commonly known as the slender larkspur, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that bear clusters of blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem, and it produces elongated seed pods that contain numerous small, dark seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Due to its toxicity, it should be handled with care and kept away from livestock and children.
