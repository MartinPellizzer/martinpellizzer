Dewinterella pulcherrima

Dewinterella pulcherrima, commonly known as the Christmas star or star of Bethlehem, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with small, narrow leaves that are typically green. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Dewinterella pulcherrima is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a potted plant.
