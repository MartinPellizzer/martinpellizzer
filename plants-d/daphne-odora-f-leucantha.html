Daphne odora f. leucantha

Daphne odora f. leucantha, commonly known as the white-flowered daphne, is a cultivar of the Daphne odora species native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant features dense, evergreen foliage with a glossy, dark green hue. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Due to its toxicity, it should be handled with care.
