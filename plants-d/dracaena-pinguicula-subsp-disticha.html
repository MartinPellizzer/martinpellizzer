Dracaena pinguicula subsp. disticha

Dracaena pinguicula subsp.

disticha, commonly known as the spiral dracaena, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it a distinctive and ornamental appearance.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by red, fleshy fruits. It prefers well-draining soil and moderate light, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its compact size and striking foliage make it a popular choice for decorative purposes.
