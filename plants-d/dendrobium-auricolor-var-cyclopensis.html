Dendrobium auricolor var. cyclopensis

Dendrobium auricolor var. cyclopensis, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features vibrant yellow and green foliage with striking golden-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a pseudobulb structure typical of many orchids, which stores water and nutrients. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
