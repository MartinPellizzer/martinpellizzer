Delphinium turkmenum

Delphinium turkmenum, commonly known as Turkmen larkspur, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters. The plant produces showy blue to purple flowers in racemes, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are palmately compound, with elongated leaflets that are often hairy.

Delphinium turkmenum is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
