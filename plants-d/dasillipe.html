Dasillipe

Dasillipe, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that have a distinctive silvery underside. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its fruit is a small, fleshy drupe that contains a single seed.

Dasillipe is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
