Deverra burchellii

Deverra burchellii, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences, followed by small, round fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and rocky areas.

Deverra burchellii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in horticulture for its unique appearance.
