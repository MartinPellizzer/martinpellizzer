Daphne × juraseki

Daphne × juraseki, commonly known as the Japanese daphne, is a hybrid shrub native to Japan.

It features fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, often appearing in late winter to early spring. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves and a compact, bushy growth habit. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its winter blooms.

However, it is toxic to humans and animals, requiring care in handling and placement.
