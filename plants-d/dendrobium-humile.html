Dendrobium humile

Dendrobium humile, commonly known as the small-stemmed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with upright, branching stems that often bear a profusion of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces white or pale yellow blooms with a distinctive, intricate pattern on the petals and sepals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Its delicate appearance and unique floral structure make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and orchid enthusiasts.
