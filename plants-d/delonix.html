Delonix

Delonix, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. The plant has compound leaves with numerous small leaflets and produces pods that contain several seeds. Native to Africa, it is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and public spaces.

Its rapid growth and ability to thrive in various climates make it a popular choice for landscaping.
