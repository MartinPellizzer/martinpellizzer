Duvalia radiata var. obscura

Duvalia radiata var. obscura, commonly known as the dark duvalia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that are typically covered in fine, soft hairs. The leaves are lance-shaped, with a smooth or slightly serrated margin, and they tend to be a deep green color. Its flowers are small, clustered in loose inflorescences, and are usually a pale yellow or greenish-white.

This variety is noted for its compact growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
