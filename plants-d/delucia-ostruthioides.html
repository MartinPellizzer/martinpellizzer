Delucia ostruthioides

Delucia ostruthioides, commonly known as the yellow-eyed grass, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It features slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in spike-like clusters. The plant typically grows in wetland environments such as marshes and pond edges. It is characterized by its ability to thrive in moist, nutrient-poor soils.

Delucia ostruthioides plays a role in supporting local pollinators and maintaining wetland ecosystems.
