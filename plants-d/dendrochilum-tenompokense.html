Dendrochilum tenompokense

Dendrochilum tenompokense, commonly known as the Malagasy tree orchid, is a rare epiphytic orchid native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, utilizing its aerial roots to absorb moisture and nutrients from the environment. Its leaves are usually leathery and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water retention.

Dendrochilum tenompokense is noted for its striking, showy blooms that can range in color from white to pink and purple.
