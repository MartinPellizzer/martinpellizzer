Dalbergia yunnanensis var. collettii

Dalbergia yunnanensis var. collettii, commonly known as Yunnan rosewood, is a deciduous tree native to China, particularly Yunnan Province.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its dense, dark reddish-brown heartwood that is highly valued for its durability and fine grain. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with a preference for well-drained soils and a subtropical climate.

Its leaves are trifoliate, with each leaflet being elliptic to ovate in shape, and it produces small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in spring.
