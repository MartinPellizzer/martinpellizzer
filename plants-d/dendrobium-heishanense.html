Dendrobium heishanense

Dendrobium heishanense, commonly known as the Black Mountain Dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, cane-like stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers during the summer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrate for optimal growth.

Dendrobium heishanense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
