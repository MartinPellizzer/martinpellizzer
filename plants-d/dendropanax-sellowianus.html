Dendropanax sellowianus

Dendropanax sellowianus, commonly known as the Brazilian arrowleaf, is a climbing shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves with elongated, arrow-shaped lobes. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
