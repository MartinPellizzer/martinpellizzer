Dendrobium nakaharae

Dendrobium nakaharae, commonly known as the Nakahara's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a short, thick rhizome and numerous pseudobulbs. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Dendrobium nakaharae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
