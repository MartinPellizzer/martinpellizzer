Durio kutejensis

Durio kutejensis, commonly known as the Kutejensis durian, is a tropical tree native to Borneo.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is closely related to other durian species. The plant produces a large, spiky fruit that contains a creamy, aromatic pulp. Its botanical features include thick, waxy leaves and a robust root system adapted to moist, tropical soils.

The durian is valued for its unique flavor and is often cultivated for both local consumption and commercial trade.
