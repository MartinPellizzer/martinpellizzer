Drypetes calvescens

Drypetes calvescens, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, often reddish-brown bark and alternate, ovate to elliptic leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in spikes, followed by capsule fruits that release tiny seeds.
