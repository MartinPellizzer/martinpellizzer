Drypetes brevipedicellata

Drypetes brevipedicellata, commonly known as the short-stemmed drypetes, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and elliptical leaves with a glossy upper surface. The plant produces small, clustered yellowish flowers followed by orange-red berries that are edible for some bird species.
