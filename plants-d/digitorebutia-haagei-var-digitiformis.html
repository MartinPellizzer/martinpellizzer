Digitorebutia haagei var. digitiformis

Digitorebutia haagei var. digitiformis, commonly known as the digitate rock rose, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a low-growing shrub with succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and arid landscapes.

Its unique leaf shape and vibrant blooms make it a striking addition to Mediterranean-style gardens.
