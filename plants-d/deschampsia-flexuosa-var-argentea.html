Deschampsia flexuosa var. argentea

Deschampsia flexuosa var. argentea, commonly known as silver tuft grass, is a variant of the tufted hairgrass species.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-blue foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. This plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. The inflorescence consists of dense, cylindrical clusters of spikelets.

It is a perennial grass that plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
