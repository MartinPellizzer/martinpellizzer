Digitaria delicata

Digitaria delicata, commonly known as silverhair grass, is a perennial grass species native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-haired appearance, which gives it its common name. This plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as fields and roadsides. It has narrow, elongated leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences.

Digitaria delicata is often used in ornamental gardening due to its delicate, feathery foliage.
