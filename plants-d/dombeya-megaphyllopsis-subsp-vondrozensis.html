Dombeya megaphyllopsis subsp. vondrozensis

Dombeya megaphyllopsis subsp.

vondrozensis, commonly known as the Vondrozensis dombeya, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its large, showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a rough texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual impact.
