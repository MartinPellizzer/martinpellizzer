Dracocephalum aucheri

Dracocephalum aucheri, commonly known as blueweed or Persian shield, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Middle East and Central Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and striking blue-violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is widely cultivated in gardens and traditional medicine.
