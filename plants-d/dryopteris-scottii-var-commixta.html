Dryopteris scottii var. commixta

Dryopteris scottii var. commixta, commonly known as the mixed fern, is a variant of the scottii fern species.

It is a deciduous fern native to the mountainous regions of East Asia, including Japan and China. The plant features fronds that are typically bipinnate, with a distinctive dark green color and a slightly curled appearance. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

The leaves are composed of multiple pinnae, which are further divided into smaller segments called pinnules.
