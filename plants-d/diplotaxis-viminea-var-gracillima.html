Diplotaxis viminea var. gracillima

Diplotaxis viminea var. gracillima, commonly known as slender desert cress, is a low-growing, herbaceous plant native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It features a sprawling habit with slender, wiry stems that often lie close to the ground. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers clustered in racemes, which bloom in summer. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs, and it is adapted to thrive in dry, sandy soils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in harsh environments.
