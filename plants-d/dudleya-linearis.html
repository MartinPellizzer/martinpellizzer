Dudleya linearis

Dudleya linearis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to California and Baja California.

It features fleshy, linear leaves that are typically blue-green in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces upright flowering stems with clusters of small, star-shaped flowers, usually in shades of pink or purple. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Dudleya linearis is drought-tolerant and requires minimal care once established.
