Dillenia nicobarica

Dillenia nicobarica, commonly known as Nicobar dillenia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Dilleniaceae.

It is native to the Andaman and Nicobar Islands in the Indian Ocean and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves and showy, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that contain numerous small seeds.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential ecological and medicinal properties.
