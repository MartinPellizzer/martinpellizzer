Doxantha unguis-cati var. dasyonyx

Doxantha unguis-cati var. dasyonyx, commonly known as the cat's claw plant, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, claw-like bracts that give it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and ample sunlight.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular ornamental plant in gardens and greenhouses.
