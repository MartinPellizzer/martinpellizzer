Dendrobium angustum

Dendrobium angustum, commonly known as the narrow leaf orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, elongated leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often clustered at the base of the stem. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrobium angustum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
