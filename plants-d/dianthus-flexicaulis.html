Dianthus flexicaulis

Dianthus flexicaulis, commonly known as the slender campion, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and clusters of bright pink or red flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Dianthus flexicaulis is often found in coastal areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
