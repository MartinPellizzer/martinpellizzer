Dombeya laevissima

Dombeya laevissima, commonly known as the smooth dombeya, is a deciduous shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has smooth, grey-green leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Dombeya laevissima is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
