Dyckia Meziana Subsp. Vogtii

Dyckia meziana subsp. vogtii is a striking bromeliad native to southern Brazil. It is known for its vibrant, spiky inflorescence that resembles a crown of flames. This plant thrives in sunny, well-drained environments and is popular among succulent enthusiasts. Its unique appearance makes it a favorite for both indoor and outdoor gardens. Despite its beauty, it requires careful cultivation to flourish.
