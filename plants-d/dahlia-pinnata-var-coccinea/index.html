Dahlia Pinnata Var. Coccinea

Dahlia pinnata var. coccinea is a striking tropical plant known for its vibrant red blooms. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is native to Mexico and Central America. This variety is prized for its showy, single or double flowers that add bold color to gardens. Its foliage is lush and green, complementing its dramatic floral display. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
