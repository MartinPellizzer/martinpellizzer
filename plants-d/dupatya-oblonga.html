Dupatya oblonga

Dupatya oblonga, commonly known as the broadleaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its long, slender leaves and clusters of fragrant, orchid-like flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous areas. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Dupatya oblonga is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
