Dendrobium fusiforme f. magnificum

Dendrobium fusiforme f. magnificum, commonly known as the magnificent spindle orchid, is a hybrid orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, spindle-shaped pseudobulbs and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically produces vibrant yellow or orange blooms with red or purple markings. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil with regular watering.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
