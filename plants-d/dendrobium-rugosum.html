Dendrobium rugosum

Dendrobium rugosum, commonly known as the rough-leafed orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with cylindrical pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink, often with intricate patterns. Its leaves are thick and leathery, adapted to withstand dry conditions.

Dendrobium rugosum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
