Dissotis seretii

Dissotis seretii, commonly known as the African star grass, is a perennial herb native to tropical Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that bear small, white to pale pink flowers arranged in dense, star-like clusters. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or near water bodies.

Dissotis seretii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
