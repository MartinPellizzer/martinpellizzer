Ditrichum macrophyllum

Ditrichum macrophyllum, commonly known as the large-leaf moss, is a species of moss belonging to the family Dicranaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, cushion-like growth habit and possesses elongated, slender leaves that are often curved. This moss is valued for its ecological role in soil stabilization and moisture retention in its native habitats.
