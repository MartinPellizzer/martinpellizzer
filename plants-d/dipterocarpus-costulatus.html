Dipterocarpus costulatus

Dipterocarpus costulatus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf dipterocarp, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and long, narrow leaves with a distinctive waxy coating. The plant produces large, showy flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

This species plays an important role in the ecological systems of tropical rainforests.
