Diuris fragrantissima

Diuris fragrantissima, commonly known as the fragrant spider-orchid, is an Australian native orchid species characterized by its intricate, spider-like flowers.

It typically grows in heathlands and open forests, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant produces striking, tubular flowers that are often tinged with purple or red, adding to its ornamental appeal. Diuris fragrantissima is known for its subtle, sweet fragrance, which becomes more pronounced as the flowers mature.

This species is also valued for its ecological role, providing nectar for native pollinators.
