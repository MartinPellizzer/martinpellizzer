Daphne arvernensis

Daphne arvernensis, commonly known as the Arvernian daphne, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern France.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in early spring. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette. Its stems are hairy and often reddish in color.

Daphne arvernensis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate beauty.
