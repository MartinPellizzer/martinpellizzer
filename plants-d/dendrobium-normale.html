Dendrobium normale

Dendrobium normale, commonly known as the common dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with cane-like stems that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to yellowish flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are usually elliptical and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stems.

Dendrobium normale thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.
