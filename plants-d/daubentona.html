Daubentona

Daubentona, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows as a low shrub or herb, with compact growth habits suitable for indoor cultivation. Daubentona species are popular in horticulture due to their ornamental value and ease of care.

They prefer bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
