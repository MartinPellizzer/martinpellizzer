Dyckia hassleri var. arenosa

Dyckia hassleri var. arenosa, commonly known as the sand dyckia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of narrow, blue-gray leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white powder, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, spiky flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
