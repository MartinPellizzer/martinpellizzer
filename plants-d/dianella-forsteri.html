Dianella forsteri

Dianella forsteri, commonly known as the coastal lily, is a perennial evergreen plant native to southeastern Australia.

It features strap-like, blue-green leaves that are narrow and grass-like in appearance. The plant produces striking white or pale blue flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy or well-drained soils.

Dianella forsteri is often used in coastal landscaping due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.
