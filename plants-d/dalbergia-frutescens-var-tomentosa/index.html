Dalbergia Frutescens Var. Tomentosa

Dalbergia frutescens var. tomentosa is a lesser-known species of leguminous tree native to parts of Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its dense, fibrous wood and hairy foliage, which give it a unique appearance. Though not as commercially valuable as other Dalbergia species, it holds cultural and ecological significance in its native habitats. The plant is often found in tropical forests, where it contributes to biodiversity and soil stability. Its study offers insights into the adaptability of legumes in diverse environmental conditions.
