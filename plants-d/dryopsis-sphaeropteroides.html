Dryopsis sphaeropteroides

Dryopsis sphaeropteroides, commonly known as the globular dryopsis, is a small, succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features dense, cylindrical or globular rosettes of fleshy, gray-green leaves that help it store water. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange, typically during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container cultivation.
