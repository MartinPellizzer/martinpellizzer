Dithrix

Dithrix, commonly known as the silverleaf dithrix, is a unique plant species native to arid regions.

It is characterized by its silvery, velvety foliage that helps reduce water loss in harsh environments. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, with thick, waxy leaves that reflect sunlight. Its flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in shades of white or pale yellow.

Dithrix plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for desert wildlife.
