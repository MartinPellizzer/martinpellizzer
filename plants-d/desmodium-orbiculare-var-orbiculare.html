Desmodium orbiculare var. orbiculare

Desmodium orbiculare var. orbiculare, commonly known as the roundleaf desmodium, is a perennial legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features trifoliate leaves with rounded leaflets and small, white or pale purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, lens-shaped pods that contain several seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and is used in some regions for forage or soil stabilization.

Its growth habit and foliage make it a valuable plant in ecological restoration projects.
