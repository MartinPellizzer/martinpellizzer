Diaphananthe polydactyla

Diaphananthe polydactyla, commonly known as the giant flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a striking visual appearance. The plant has a woody stem with a rough, bark-like texture and can grow quite tall, reaching heights of up to 20 meters. Its leaves are compound, consisting of multiple leaflets arranged in a feather-like pattern.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant flowers and unique foliage.
