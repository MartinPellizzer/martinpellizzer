Dactylorhiza maculata subsp. podesta

Dactylorhiza maculata subsp.

podesta, commonly known as the speckled marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Europe and parts of Asia. It typically grows in wet, calcareous habitats such as marshes and damp meadows.

The plant features a slender, erect stem with several oval-shaped leaves. Its flowers are pale green to brownish with dark purple markings, arranged in a spike.

It is a perennial species that produces a single, elongated flower spike with 5 to 10 flowers.
