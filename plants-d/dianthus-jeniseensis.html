Dianthus jeniseensis

Dianthus jeniseensis, commonly known as the Jenisean pink, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of eastern Russia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant features upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine layer of hairs. Its flowers are usually arranged in clusters and are known for their delicate, fragrant scent.

Dianthus jeniseensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
