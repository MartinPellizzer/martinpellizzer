Dolichos lignosus

Dolichos lignosus, commonly known as the woody morning glory, is a climbing vine native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its woody stems and heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces large, showy flowers that range in color from white to purple, often with a distinctive spiral pattern. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Dolichos lignosus is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
