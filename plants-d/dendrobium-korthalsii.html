Dendrobium korthalsii

Dendrobium korthalsii, commonly known as the Korthals' orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, branched stems that bear numerous lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, pale yellow to greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Dendrobium korthalsii is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
