Dracaena eximia

Dracaena eximia, commonly known as the dragon tree, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and long, strap-like leaves that are typically bright green with a reddish tinge along the edges. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by red, berry-like fruits. Dracaena eximia thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

It has a moderate growth rate and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
