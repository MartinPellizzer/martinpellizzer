Dolichos crassus

Dolichos crassus, commonly known as the broadleaf horsegram, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, pale yellow flowers. The plant produces elongated, cylindrical pods that contain several seeds. It is often cultivated for its nutritious seeds, which are used in food and animal feed.

Dolichos crassus is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial in agricultural systems.
