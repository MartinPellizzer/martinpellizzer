Dendrobium subclausum var. subclausum

Dendrobium subclausum var. subclausum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed dendrobium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in tropical rainforests. The plant has linear, lance-shaped leaves that are often clustered at the base, and it produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It thrives in high humidity and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in horticultural settings.
