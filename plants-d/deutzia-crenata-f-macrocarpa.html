Deutzia crenata f. macrocarpa

Deutzia crenata f. macrocarpa, commonly known as the large-fruited deutzia, is a compact shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features showy clusters of white to pink flowers that bloom in early summer, adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant has dark green, ovate leaves that provide a lush backdrop for its floral display. Its fruit is a small, hairy capsule that matures in late summer, containing several seeds.

This variety is valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in partial shade to full sun conditions.
