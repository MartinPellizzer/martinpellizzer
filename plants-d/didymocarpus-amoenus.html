Didymocarpus amoenus

Didymocarpus amoenus, commonly known as the sweet-scented flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, often giving it a fiery appearance. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically covered in a soft, hairy coating. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly serrated edge.

Didymocarpus amoenus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
