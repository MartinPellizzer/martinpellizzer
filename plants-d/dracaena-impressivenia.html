Dracaena impressivenia

Dracaena impressivenia, commonly known as the impressively striped dracaena, is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and striking, variegated leaves that display shades of green, yellow, and white. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by red berries, though it is primarily cultivated for its ornamental foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Dracaena impressivenia is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its air-purifying qualities and low maintenance requirements.
