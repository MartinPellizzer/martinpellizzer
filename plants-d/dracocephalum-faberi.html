Dracocephalum faberi

Dracocephalum faberi, commonly known as Chinese skullcap, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue to purple in color, arranged in whorls along the stem. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a strong, bitter scent.

Dracocephalum faberi is often used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
