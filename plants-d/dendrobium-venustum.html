Dendrobium venustum

Dendrobium venustum, commonly known as the rare orchid of the genus Dendrobium, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit, with elongated, cane-like stems that often bear clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant's leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, adapted to its climbing lifestyle. It thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its blooming period can last several weeks, adding ornamental value to gardens and collections.
