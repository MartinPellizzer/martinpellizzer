Dalbergia emirnensis var. emirnensis

Dalbergia emirnensis var. emirnensis, commonly known as the Emirnensis rosewood, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Iran and is characterized by its dense, dark wood that is highly valued for its beauty and durability. The plant typically grows as a small to medium-sized tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are bipinnate, with numerous small, oval-shaped leaflets, and it produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually pale yellow or white.

The wood of this species is prized in the furniture and musical instrument industries due to its fine grain and rich color.
