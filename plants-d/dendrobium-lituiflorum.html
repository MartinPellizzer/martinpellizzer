Dendrobium lituiflorum

Dendrobium lituiflorum, commonly known as the stone-flowered orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with multiple stems that bear numerous lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive lip that is often adorned with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is a sought-after specimen among orchid enthusiasts.
