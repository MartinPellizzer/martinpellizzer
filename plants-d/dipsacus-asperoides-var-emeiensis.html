Dipsacus asperoides var. emeiensis

Dipsacus asperoides var. emeiensis, commonly known as the Emei Mountain teasel, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its spiny, bract-like structures that surround its flower heads. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and is adapted to temperate climates. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months.
