Dolichos lobatus

Dolichos lobatus, commonly known as the horsegram or cluster bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and erect, branched stems. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that develop into elongated pods containing edible seeds. Dolichos lobatus is valued for its nutritional content, including high protein and fiber, and is cultivated for both human consumption and animal feed.

It also plays a role in soil enrichment due to its nitrogen-fixing properties.
