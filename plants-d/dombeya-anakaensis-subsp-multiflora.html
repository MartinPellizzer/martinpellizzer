Dombeya anakaensis subsp. multiflora

Dombeya anakaensis subsp.

multiflora, commonly known as the African dombeya, is a shrub native to eastern and southern Africa. It features showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring.

The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or rocky habitats.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
