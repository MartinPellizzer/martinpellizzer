Dendrobium normanbyense

Dendrobium normanbyense, commonly known as the Normanby's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical rainforests of Queensland, Australia.

It is characterized by its erect, often branched stems that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, typically blooming during the warmer months. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Dendrobium normanbyense is well adapted to humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to thrive.
