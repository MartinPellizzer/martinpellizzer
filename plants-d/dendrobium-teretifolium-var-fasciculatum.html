Dendrobium teretifolium var. fasciculatum

Dendrobium teretifolium var. fasciculatum, commonly known as the clustered leaf dendrobium, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features elongated, narrow leaves that are often arranged in clusters, giving it its varietal name. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-draining soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
