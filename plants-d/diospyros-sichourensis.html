Diospyros sichourensis

Diospyros sichourensis, commonly known as the Sichuan date palm, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Ebenaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, grey-brown bark and elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by edible, round berries.

It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in mountainous regions, where it plays a role in local ecosystems.
