Dendrobium spiculatum

Dendrobium spiculatum, commonly known as the spiky orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome that produces multiple pseudobulbs. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive spiky appearance. It thrives in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil.

Dendrobium spiculatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique floral structure.
