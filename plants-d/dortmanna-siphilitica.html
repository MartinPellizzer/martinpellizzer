Dortmanna siphilitica

Dortmanna siphilitica, commonly known as the yellow horned-poppy, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its bright yellow petals and distinctive, elongated seed pods. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers bloom in early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Dortmanna siphilitica is a perennial herb that is often found in coastal areas and dry habitats.
