Dyckia hassleri var. typica

Dyckia hassleri var. typica, commonly known as the Hassler's Dyckia, is a species of flowering plant in the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming structure with sharp, spiny leaves.
