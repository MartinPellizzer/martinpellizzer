Delphinium erlangshanicum

Delphinium erlangshanicum, commonly known as the Chinese larkspur, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that bear clusters of blue to purple flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges, and its flowers typically bloom in late summer. Delphinium erlangshanicum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.

However, it contains toxic alkaloids and should be handled with care.
