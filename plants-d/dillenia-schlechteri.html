Dillenia schlechteri

Dillenia schlechteri, commonly known as the African dillenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Dilleniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, palmately lobed leaves. The plant produces showy, yellowish-white flowers with five petals, which are often borne in clusters. Its fruit is a woody, spiny capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Dillenia schlechteri is typically found in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and riverbanks.
