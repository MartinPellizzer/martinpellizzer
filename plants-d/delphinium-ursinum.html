Delphinium ursinum

Delphinium ursinum, commonly known as the bear's breeches delphinium, is a tall, striking perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features showy, blue to purple spires of flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are deeply lobed, giving it a dramatic appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often growing in alpine or rocky habitats.

Delphinium ursinum is valued in gardens for its ornamental beauty and is relatively hardy in temperate climates.
