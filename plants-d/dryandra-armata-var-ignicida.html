Dryandra armata var. ignicida

Dryandra armata var. ignicida, commonly known as the red-flowered dryandra, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that appear in late winter to early spring. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to the region's Mediterranean climate.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
