Dendrobium wantipiense

Dendrobium wantipiense, commonly known as the Wantipi orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, typical of many orchid species. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its unique floral structure and native habitat make it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
