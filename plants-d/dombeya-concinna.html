Dombeya concinna

Dombeya concinna, commonly known as the silky dombeya, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often appearing in late winter to early spring. The plant has smooth, greyish bark and lance-shaped leaves with a soft, silvery underside. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens in temperate climates.

Dombeya concinna is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native plant landscaping.
