Dieffenbachia imperator

Dieffenbachia imperator, commonly known as the Imperator Calla, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with white or yellow variegation. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like peace lilies. Its foliage is often used in interior design due to its striking appearance.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to maintain its health.
