Dactylis Polygama F. Latifolia

Dactylis polygama f. latifolia is a unique variant of the common grass known as dactylis. This plant is characterized by its broad, flat leaves that distinguish it from its typical counterpart. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in grasslands and meadows. Its foliage makes it a valuable addition to ornamental gardens. This fascinating plant showcases the diversity within the grass family.
