Delphinium speciosum var. thyrsanthum

Delphinium speciosum var. thyrsanthum, commonly known as the purple thyrse delphinium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters. The plant produces dense, conical clusters of showy blue to purple flowers, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

This variety is valued for its ornamental value in gardens and its ability to thrive in well-drained, alkaline soils.
