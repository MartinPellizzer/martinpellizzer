Dombeya ovata

Dombeya ovata, commonly known as the white dombeya, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped white flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has smooth, grayish bark and lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Dombeya ovata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
