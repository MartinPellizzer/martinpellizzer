Dryandra nivea

Dryandra nivea, commonly known as the white dryandra, is a species of flowering plant in the Proteaceae family.

It is native to Western Australia and is characterized by its distinctive, urn-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is often cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
