Draba gmelinii

Draba gmelinii, commonly known as the alpine mouse-ear cress, is a small flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia, including the Alps, Carpathians, and Caucasus. Its leaves are basal and spoon-shaped, while its flowers are small, white or pale yellow, and arranged in a raceme.

Draba gmelinii is a perennial plant that thrives in cool, moist conditions and is adapted to withstand harsh alpine climates.
