Desmodium longifolium

Desmodium longifolium, commonly known as the slender cowpea, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant has a deep root system that helps prevent soil erosion and improves soil fertility through nitrogen fixation. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland and grassland habitats.

Desmodium longifolium is also valued for its use in traditional medicine and as a forage crop for livestock.
