Dendrosenecio elgonensis subsp. elgonensis

Dendrosenecio elgonensis subsp.

elgonensis, commonly known as the Elgon Daisy, is a succulent shrub native to the highlands of Mount Elgon in Uganda and Kenya. It features a compact, upright growth habit with dense clusters of small, bright yellow flowers.

The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green or bluish-green, adapted to store water in its native arid environment. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens and as a container plant.
