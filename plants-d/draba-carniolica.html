Draba carniolica

Draba carniolica, commonly known as the Carniolan rockcress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous annual or biennial plant that typically grows in rocky or alpine environments. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in a raceme, which bloom in early spring. Its leaves are basal, rosette-like, and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Draba carniolica is native to Europe and is often found in mountainous regions across the continent.
