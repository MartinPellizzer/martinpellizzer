Dolichos dewildemanianus

Dolichos dewildemanianus, commonly known as the Wild Bean Plant, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. The plant bears pods that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Dolichos dewildemanianus is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in supporting pollinators.
