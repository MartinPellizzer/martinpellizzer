Diemisa

Diemisa, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and prefers well-draining soil. Diemisa is popular as a houseplant due to its vibrant blooms and ease of care.

Its botanical features make it a favorite among gardeners and plant enthusiasts.
