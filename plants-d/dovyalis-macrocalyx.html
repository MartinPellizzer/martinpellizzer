Dovyalis macrocalyx

Dovyalis macrocalyx, commonly known as the Natal plum, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Africa.

It produces small, yellowish-green fruits that are typically consumed by birds and other wildlife. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to lanceolate in shape. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.
