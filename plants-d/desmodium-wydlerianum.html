Desmodium wydlerianum

Desmodium wydlerianum, commonly known as wydler's burr, is a leguminous plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, hairy pods that contain several seeds. The plant is native to parts of Africa and is often found in grassland and savanna habitats. It has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.

Its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen makes it valuable for soil enrichment in agricultural settings.
