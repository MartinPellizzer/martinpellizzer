Drepananthus novoguineensis

Drepananthus novoguineensis, commonly known as the New Guinea hookweed, is a member of the Commelinaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including New Guinea. The plant features slender, creeping stems that often form dense mats on the ground. It is adapted to moist, shaded environments and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.
