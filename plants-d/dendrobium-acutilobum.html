Dendrobium acutilobum

Dendrobium acutilobum, commonly known as the sharp-lobed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with long, slender pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with intricate patterns. Its leaves are usually elliptical and arranged in a rosette, aiding in photosynthesis.

Dendrobium acutilobum thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
