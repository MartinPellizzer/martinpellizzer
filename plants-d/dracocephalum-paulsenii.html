Dracocephalum paulsenii

Dracocephalum paulsenii, commonly known as Persian shield, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Iran and Afghanistan.

It features lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive metallic blue or silver sheen, which are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is valued in both traditional and modern horticulture.
