Dendrobium ovatifolium

Dendrobium ovatifolium, commonly known as the ovate-leaf orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome that produces a cluster of pseudobulbs. The plant has lanceolate to ovate leaves that are usually arranged in a rosette pattern. It produces inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow.

This species is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in shaded, humid environments.
