Dianthus strictus var. gracilior

Dianthus strictus var. gracilior, commonly known as the slender carnation, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in dense clusters, often with a distinctive frilly appearance. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs.

This variety is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
