Desmanthodium

Desmanthodium, commonly known as the mountain hawksbeard, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Europe and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in alpine environments. Desmanthodium species are perennial herbs with linear leaves and showy inflorescences.
