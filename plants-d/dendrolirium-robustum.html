Dendrolirium robustum

Dendrolirium robustum, commonly known as the robust lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its tall, erect stem and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in cool, moist environments and prefers well-drained, loamy soil. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, while its inflorescence consists of a spike of fragrant, white or pale pink flowers.

Dendrolirium robustum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
