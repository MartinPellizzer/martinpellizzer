Delphinium thibeticum var. laceratilobum

Delphinium thibeticum var. laceratilobum, commonly known as the Tibetan larkspur, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It features tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters. The plant produces striking blue to purple flowers in racemes, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are palmately lobed and have a distinctive, serrated appearance.

It thrives in cool, moist environments and is adapted to the harsh conditions of its alpine habitat.
