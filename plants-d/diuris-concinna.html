Diuris concinna

Diuris concinna, commonly known as the slender greenhood, is an orchid species native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understoreys. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a single, lance-shaped leaf. Its flowers are pale green with a distinctive hood-like labellum, which is a characteristic feature of the greenhood orchids.

Diuris concinna blooms during late winter to early spring, producing clusters of small, delicate flowers.
