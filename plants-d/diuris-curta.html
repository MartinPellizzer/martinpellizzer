Diuris curta

Diuris curta, commonly known as the short-leaf sun-orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in heathland and woodland habitats, preferring well-drained soils. The plant features a compact, hairy stem with a few narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are small, greenish-yellow, and often have a distinctive hood-like structure.

Diuris curta is known for its resilience and ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
