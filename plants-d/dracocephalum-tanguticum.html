Dracocephalum tanguticum

Dracocephalum tanguticum, commonly known as Chinese skullcap, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that are typically purple or blue in color. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils at high altitudes.

The species is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
