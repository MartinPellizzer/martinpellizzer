Dombeya oblongipetala

Dombeya oblongipetala, commonly known as the narrow-petaled dombeya, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in moist, shady environments, often found in the understory of tropical forests. The plant is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that are usually pink or white in color. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Dombeya oblongipetala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
