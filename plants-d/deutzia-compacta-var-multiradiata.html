Deutzia compacta var. multiradiata

Deutzia compacta var. multiradiata, commonly known as the multibracteate deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, white to pink flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in early spring. The plant has slender, arching stems and narrow, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value comes from its showy flowers and compact growth habit.
