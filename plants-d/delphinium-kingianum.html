Delphinium kingianum

Delphinium kingianum, commonly known as the King's Delphinium, is a striking perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters, adorned with large, showy blue to purple flowers. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem, contributing to its elegant appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is further enhanced by its long blooming period, which typically occurs in late spring to early summer.
