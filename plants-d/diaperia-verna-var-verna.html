Diaperia verna var. verna

Diaperia verna var. verna, commonly known as spring diaperwort, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features small, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

It is part of the Boraginaceae family, known for its showy flowers and medicinal uses in traditional herbal practices.
