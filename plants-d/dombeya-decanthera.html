Dombeya decanthera

Dombeya decanthera, commonly known as the white dombeya, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, fragrant white flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are elliptical in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or rocky habitats.

Dombeya decanthera is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
