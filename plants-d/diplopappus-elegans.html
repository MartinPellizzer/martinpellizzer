Diplopappus elegans

Diplopappus elegans, commonly known as the elegant dandelion, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are yellow and develop into fluffy, white seed heads that aid in wind pollination.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.
