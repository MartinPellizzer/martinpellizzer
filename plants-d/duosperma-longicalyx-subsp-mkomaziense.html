Duosperma longicalyx subsp. mkomaziense

Duosperma longicalyx subsp.

mkomaziense, commonly known as the Mkomazi thornveld succulent, is a shrub-like plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and compact growth habit.

The plant features fleshy, cylindrical stems that store water, typical of succulent species. Its flowers are small and yellow, appearing in clusters during the warmer months.

It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it well adapted to harsh, dry conditions.
