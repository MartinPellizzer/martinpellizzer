Debesia contorta

Debesia contorta, commonly known as the twisted debesia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in well-drained soils. The plant features small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and usually have a slightly hairy texture.

Debesia contorta is known for its adaptability to various environmental conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
