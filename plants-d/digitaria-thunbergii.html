Digitaria thunbergii

Digitaria thunbergii, commonly known as slender grass or Thunberg's grass, is a species of grass native to Africa, Asia, and Australia.

It is a perennial plant characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in a variety of habitats, including grasslands, woodland edges, and disturbed soils.

Digitaria thunbergii is often used in ornamental gardening due to its fine texture and adaptability.
