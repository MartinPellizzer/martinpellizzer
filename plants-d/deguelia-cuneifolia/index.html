Deguelia Cuneifolia

Cupuaçu tree, scientifically known as Deguelia cuneifolia, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest of South America. It is renowned for producing a unique fruit that is both nutritious and highly sought after in the global market. The tree thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing in dense forest canopies. Its fruit, which resembles a large, yellowish-brown avocado, has a creamy texture and a sweet, slightly acidic flavor. Due to its ecological and economic importance, the Cupuaçu tree plays a vital role in both local ecosystems and international trade.
