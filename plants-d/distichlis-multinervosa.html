Distichlis multinervosa

Distichlis multinervosa, commonly known as the saltgrass, is a flowering plant native to coastal areas of the western United States.

It is a perennial grass that thrives in saline and brackish environments, often found in salt marshes and tidal flats. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters. It is well adapted to withstand high salinity and periodic flooding.

Distichlis multinervosa plays an important ecological role in stabilizing coastal soils and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
