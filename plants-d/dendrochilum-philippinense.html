Dendrochilum philippinense

Dendrochilum philippinense, commonly known as the Philippine tree orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces striking, showy flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows in lowland to montane forest areas, often on tree trunks or rocks. Its roots are fibrous and adapted for anchoring in rocky or soil substrates.

The orchid is noted for its unique floral structure and is of interest to botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
