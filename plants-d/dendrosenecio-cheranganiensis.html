Dendrosenecio cheranganiensis

Dendrosenecio cheranganiensis, commonly known as the Cherangani's tree heath, is a succulent shrub native to the arid regions of Kenya.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, cylindrical stems covered in woolly, grayish-green foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the rainy season. It is well adapted to dry climates, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
