Disporum sessile f. intermedium

Disporum sessile f. intermedium, commonly known as the intermediate sessile disporum, is a variant of the native North American plant Disporum sessile.

It is a herbaceous perennial with slender, grass-like leaves and clusters of small, bell-shaped white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its botanical features include a short rhizome and a distinctive, nodding inflorescence.
