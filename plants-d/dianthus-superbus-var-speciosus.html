Dianthus superbus var. speciosus

Dianthus superbus var. speciosus, commonly known as the showy campion, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of bright pink or red, bilaterally symmetrical flowers. The leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs. This species is known for its showy blooms that appear in late spring to early summer.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
