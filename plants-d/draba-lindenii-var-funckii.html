Draba lindenii var. funckii

Draba lindenii var. funckii, commonly known as the Funck's rockcress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or alpine environments. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers during the spring season. Its leaves are basal, rosette-shaped, and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

This variety is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in high-altitude regions of Central Asia.
