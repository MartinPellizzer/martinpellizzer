Dieffenbachia seguine f. ventenatiana

Dieffenbachia seguine f. ventenatiana, commonly known as the African mask plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Central America.

It features large, arrow-shaped leaves with striking white and green variegation, often with a yellow border. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix and spathe. Its foliage is thick and waxy, adapted to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

It is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
