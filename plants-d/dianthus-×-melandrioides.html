Dianthus × melandrioides

Dianthus × melandrioides, commonly known as the garden carnation, is a hybrid species within the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that come in a wide range of colors, including red, pink, white, and purple. The plant typically grows to a height of 15–30 cm and features narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden beds and containers.

Dianthus × melandrioides is valued for its long blooming period and resilience to various environmental conditions.
