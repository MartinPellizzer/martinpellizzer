Dipladenia tenuifolia var. puberula

Dipladenia tenuifolia var. puberula, commonly known as the velvet leaf Dipladenia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves that give it a distinctive texture. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of yellow to orange, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in warm, sunny conditions and is often cultivated as a ornamental plant.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for container gardening and hanging baskets.
