Dendrosicus spathicalyx

Dendrosicus spathicalyx, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves and clusters of fragrant, yellow flowers that bloom in pendulous racemes. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. Its flowers are often used in traditional medicine and as ornamental plants due to their striking appearance.

The fruit, which is a capsule, contains several seeds that are dispersed by wind.
