Draba bhutanica

Draba bhutanica, commonly known as the Bhutan draba, is a small flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is native to the Himalayan regions, including Bhutan, Nepal, and northern India. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine meadows at elevations ranging from 2,500 to 4,500 meters. It features basal leaves and small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring.

Draba bhutanica is often found in rocky soils and is adapted to cold, high-altitude environments.
