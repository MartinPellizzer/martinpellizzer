Distictis rhynchocarpa

Distictis rhynchocarpa, commonly known as the African star orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous plant with slender, upright stems and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest undergrowth. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil efficiently.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with appropriate conditions.
