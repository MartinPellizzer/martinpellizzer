Drepanolobus lanatus

Drepanolobus lanatus, commonly known as the African velvet bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which are often covered in fine, velvety hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in racemes. Its fruit is a hairy pod that contains several elliptical seeds.

Drepanolobus lanatus is valued for its use in traditional medicine and as a source of protein-rich seeds.
