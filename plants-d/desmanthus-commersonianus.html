Desmanthus commersonianus

Desmanthus commersonianus, commonly known as the hairy desmanthus, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellow flowers. The plant is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and shrublands. It has a deep root system that helps it survive in arid conditions.

The seeds are enclosed in pods and are an important food source for various wildlife species.
