Dendrobium pseudointricatum

Dendrobium pseudointricatum, commonly known as the intricate dendrobium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. The plant features long, cane-like stems that are often covered in clusters of small, green leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with intricate patterns that give it its common name.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
