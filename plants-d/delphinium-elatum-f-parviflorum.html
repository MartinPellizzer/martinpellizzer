Delphinium elatum f. parviflorum

Delphinium elatum f. parviflorum, commonly known as the dwarf larkspur, is a compact, flowering plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that bear clusters of blue to purple flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated for its vibrant, showy blooms.
