Dendrobium geotropum

Dendrobium geotropum, commonly known as the upright dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit, with cane-like stems that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences bearing showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. Its leaves are typically elliptical and arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem.

Dendrobium geotropum thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media for optimal growth.
