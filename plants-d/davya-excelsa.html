Davya excelsa

Davya excelsa, commonly known as the tall lily, is a herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and large, showy flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and prefers well-drained soil. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Davya excelsa is valued both for its aesthetic beauty and its traditional use in herbal medicine.
