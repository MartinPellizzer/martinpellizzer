Dendrobium speculum

Dendrobium speculum, commonly known as the mirror orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, upright growth habit with short, cane-like pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with a distinctive mirror-like sheen on the petals. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media to prevent root rot.

Dendrobium speculum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
