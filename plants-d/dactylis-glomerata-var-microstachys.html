Dactylis glomerata var. microstachys

Dactylis glomerata var. microstachys, commonly known as silky brome, is a perennial grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, soft foliage and dense, tufted growth habit, which makes it visually appealing. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are often more compact than those of the typical Dactylis glomerata. It is well adapted to a variety of soil types and is often used in forage and erosion control.

Its botanical features make it a valuable plant in both ecological and agricultural contexts.
