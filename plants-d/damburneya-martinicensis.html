Damburneya martinicensis

Damburneya martinicensis, commonly known as the Martinique flame tree, is a striking tropical tree native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual display. The plant has compound leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a landscape plant in tropical regions.
