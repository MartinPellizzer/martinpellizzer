Deutzia floribunda

Deutzia floribunda, commonly known as the clustered deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features arching stems with clusters of fragrant, white to pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that turn yellow in the fall, adding seasonal interest. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, fertile soil.

Deutzia floribunda is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and moderate maintenance requirements.
