Disa venusta

Disa venusta, commonly known as the orchid bell, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its flowers bloom in late summer to early autumn, adding a dramatic visual impact to its habitat.

Disa venusta is also prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and conservatories.
