Dracocephalum parviflorum var. chelonicum

Dracocephalum parviflorum var. chelonicum, commonly known as narrowleaf blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, blue to purple flowers. It produces small, four-lobed fruits that contain tiny seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

This variety is often found in open areas such as grasslands and sagebrush steppe.
