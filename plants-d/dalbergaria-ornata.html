Dalbergaria ornata

Dalbergaria ornata, commonly known as the silverleaf burr legume, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with a silvery underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in racemes, which are followed by legume pods containing seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Dalbergaria ornata is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
