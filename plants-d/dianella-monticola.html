Dianella monticola

Dianella monticola, commonly known as the mountain grevillea, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in coastal and subalpine regions. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, white or pale blue flowers. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to harsh environmental conditions.

Dianella monticola is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
