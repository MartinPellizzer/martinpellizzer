Dichaea muricatoides

Dichaea muricatoides, commonly known as the hairy leaf orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, hairy leaves that are often covered in fine, soft pubescence. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, typically blooming during the wet season. It thrives in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors and rocky crevices.

Dichaea muricatoides is valued for its unique appearance and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
