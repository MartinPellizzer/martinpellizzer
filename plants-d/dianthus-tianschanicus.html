Dianthus tianschanicus

Dianthus tianschanicus, commonly known as the Tian Shan campion, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its stems are often hairy.

Dianthus tianschanicus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
