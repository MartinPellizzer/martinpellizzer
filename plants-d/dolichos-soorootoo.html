Dolichos soorootoo

Dolichos soorootoo, commonly known as the Indian cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and trifoliate leaves, which are typically green and heart-shaped. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that develop into elongated, pod-like fruits containing several seeds. Dolichos soorootoo is valued for its nutritional content, providing protein and essential minerals.

It also plays a role in nitrogen fixation, enriching the soil through its symbiotic relationship with rhizobia bacteria.
