Dendrobium tenellum var. setifolium

Dendrobium tenellum var. setifolium, commonly known as the slender-leaved orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, narrow, and often pendulous leaves that are adapted for capturing moisture from the air. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically blooming during the wet season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
