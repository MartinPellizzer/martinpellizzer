Dichelachne stipoides

Dichelachne stipoides, commonly known as the slender flatgrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in grasslands and along riverbanks, preferring well-drained soils. The plant has narrow, flat leaves and produces small, spikelet-like inflorescences. It is characterized by its ability to form dense mats, which can help prevent soil erosion.

Botanically, it is part of the Poaceae family and is notable for its adaptability to various environmental conditions.
