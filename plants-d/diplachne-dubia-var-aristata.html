Diplachne dubia var. aristata

Diplachne dubia var. aristata, commonly known as the slender flatgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, flat leaves and dense, tufted growth habit. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences with pale blue to white flowers.

Diplachne dubia var. aristata is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its adaptability and ability to stabilize soil.
