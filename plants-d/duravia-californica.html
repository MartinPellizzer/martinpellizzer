Duravia californica

Duravia californica, commonly known as the California duravia, is a perennial shrub native to the coastal regions of California.

It features small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to coastal climates with mild winters and warm, dry summers.

Duravia californica is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
