Desmodium pachyrhizum

Desmodium pachyrhizum, commonly known as hairy nightshade, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces elongated, hairy pods that contain several elliptical seeds. It has a deep root system that helps in nitrogen fixation, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.

Desmodium pachyrhizum is often used in agroforestry and as a forage crop due to its hardy nature and nutritional value.
