Disa thodei

Disa thodei, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its flowers are usually pink or purple and bloom during the summer months.

Disa thodei is a popular ornamental plant due to its beauty and adaptability to various garden conditions.
