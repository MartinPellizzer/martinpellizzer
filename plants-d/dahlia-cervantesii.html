Dahlia cervantesii

Dahlia cervantesii, commonly known as the Cervantes dahlia, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has sturdy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

The flowers bloom in late summer and early fall, adding vibrant color to gardens during the warmer months.
