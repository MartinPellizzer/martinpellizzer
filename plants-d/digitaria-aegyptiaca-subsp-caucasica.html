Digitaria aegyptiaca subsp. caucasica

Digitaria aegyptiaca subsp.

caucasica, commonly known as Caucasian digitaria, is a grass species native to the Caucasus region. It is characterized by its erect, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves.

The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing numerous tiny florets. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Its fibrous roots and drought tolerance make it an important component of arid ecosystems.
