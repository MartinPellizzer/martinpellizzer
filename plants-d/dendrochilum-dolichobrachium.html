Dendrochilum dolichobrachium

Dendrochilum dolichobrachium, commonly known as the long-stemmed tree orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and elongated, slender stems. Its flowers are typically small, greenish-yellow, and clustered in inflorescences.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often epiphytic, growing on trees or rocks in the wild.
