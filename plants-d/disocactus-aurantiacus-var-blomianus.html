Disocactus aurantiacus var. blomianus

Disocactus aurantiacus var. blomianus, commonly known as the Blomianus Christmas cactus, is a hybrid cultivar of the Schlumbergera genus.

It is native to the coastal regions of Brazil and is characterized by its segmented, fleshy stems that resemble those of a cactus. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow during the holiday season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular for its ornamental value and ability to bloom reliably under proper care.
