Dipteronia sinensis

Dipteronia sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, flame-like red flowers that appear in spring. The plant has pinnate leaves and produces legume pods that contain seeds. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Dipteronia sinensis is also valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its potential health benefits.
