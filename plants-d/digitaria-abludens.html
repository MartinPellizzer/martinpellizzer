Digitaria abludens

Digitaria abludens, commonly known as the blushing grass, is a species of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and narrow, blade-like leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are typically green or reddish in color. Digitaria abludens thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas or along roadsides.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
