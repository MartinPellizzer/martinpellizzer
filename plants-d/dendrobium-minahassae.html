Dendrobium minahassae

Dendrobium minahassae, commonly known as the Minahassa orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Indonesia.

It features long, cane-like pseudobulbs covered in dense, leathery leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a red or purple lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

Dendrobium minahassae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
