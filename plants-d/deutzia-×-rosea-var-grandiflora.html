Deutzia × rosea var. grandiflora

Deutzia × rosea var. grandiflora, commonly known as the grandiflora deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy, clusters of fragrant, white to pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has arching, slender stems and heart-shaped, dark green leaves. It typically grows to a height of 3 to 6 feet and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
