Dactylorhiza senayi

Dactylorhiza senayi, commonly known as the Senayi orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces spike-like inflorescences bearing small, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinct lip. It thrives in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and wet meadows.

Dactylorhiza senayi is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
