Desmodium megalanthum var. pilosum

Desmodium megalanthum var. pilosum, commonly known as the hairy desmodium, is a perennial legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features trifoliate leaves with hairy surfaces, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it a valuable plant for soil enrichment and ecological restoration.
