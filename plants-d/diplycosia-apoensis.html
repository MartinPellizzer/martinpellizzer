Diplycosia apoensis

Diplycosia apoensis, commonly known as the Apo Mountain daisy, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, while its flowers range in color from white to pink.

It plays an important role in the local ecosystem by providing nectar for pollinators.
