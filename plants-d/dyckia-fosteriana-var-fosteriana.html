Dyckia fosteriana var. fosteriana

Dyckia fosteriana var. fosteriana, commonly known as the Foster's dyckia, is a species of bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its rosette of stiff, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing small, white to pinkish flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for indoor and outdoor cultivation.
