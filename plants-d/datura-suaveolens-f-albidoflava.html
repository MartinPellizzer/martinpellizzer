Datura suaveolens f. albidoflava

Datura suaveolens f. albidoflava, commonly known as the white-flowered jimsonweed, is a variant of the common jimsonweed.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its large, showy flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. The plant has hairy stems and broad, heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture. It produces spiny capsules that contain numerous seeds.

This cultivar is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking floral display.
