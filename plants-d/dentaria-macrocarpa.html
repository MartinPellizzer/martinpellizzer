Dentaria macrocarpa

Dentaria macrocarpa, commonly known as the large-seeded toothplant, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It belongs to the family Brassicaceae, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant features basal rosettes of hairy, oval-shaped leaves and produces yellow flowers in racemes. Its fruit is a cylindrical silique containing several seeds.

Dentaria macrocarpa is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.
