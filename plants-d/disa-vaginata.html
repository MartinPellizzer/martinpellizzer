Disa vaginata

Disa vaginata, commonly known as the Cape bell or red bell orchid, is a striking flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red and orange flowers that resemble small bells. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming a rosette at the base.

Disa vaginata is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
