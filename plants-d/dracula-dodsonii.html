Dracula dodsonii

Dracula dodsonii, commonly known as the "Dodson's Dracula," is a rare and striking orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, spurred petals and vivid red coloration. The plant thrives in high-altitude environments with high humidity and cool temperatures. Its unique floral structure is adapted for pollination by specific insects, such as flies.

Due to its limited habitat and threats from deforestation, Dracula dodsonii is considered a vulnerable species.
