Drosanthemum decumbens

Drosanthemum decumbens, commonly known as the rock fig, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Drosanthemum decumbens is often used in rock gardens or as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and hardy nature.
