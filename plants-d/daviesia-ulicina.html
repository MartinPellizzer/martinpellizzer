Daviesia ulicina

Daviesia ulicina, commonly known as the narrow-leaved daviesia, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and clusters of yellow or cream flowers. The plant typically grows in heathlands and open forests, thriving in well-drained soils. It is known for its ability to fix nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility.

Daviesia ulicina is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting native wildlife.
