Deschampsia mathewsii

Deschampsia mathewsii, commonly known as Mathew's feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as meadows, slopes, and rocky areas. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, feathery seed heads that are characteristic of the Deschampsia genus. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in full sun to partial shade.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small organisms.
