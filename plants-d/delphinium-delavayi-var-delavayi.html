Delphinium delavayi var. delavayi

Delphinium delavayi var. delavayi, commonly known as the Chinese larkspur, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of western China.

It features tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height, adorned with whorled, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, blue to purple flowers in dense spikes, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers well-drained, loamy soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and botanical collections.
