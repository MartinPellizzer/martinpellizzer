Dischidia acuminata

Dischidia acuminata, commonly known as the warty fig or climbing dischidia, is a tropical vine belonging to the family Rubiaceae.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are often covered with small, warty bumps. Dischidia acuminata is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
