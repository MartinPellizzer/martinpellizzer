Ditaxis humilis f. laevis

Ditaxis humilis f. laevis, commonly known as the smoothleaf ditaxis, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are smooth and hairless, giving it its specific epithet "laevis.". The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its flowers are typically arranged in dense, terminal inflorescences.

The species is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
