Desmodium polystachyum

Desmodium polystachyum, commonly known as the hairy desmodium, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant is often found in grasslands and woodland areas, where it thrives in well-drained soils. It has a trailing growth habit and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Desmodium polystachyum is also known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil fertility.
