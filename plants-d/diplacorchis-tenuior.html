Diplacorchis tenuior

Diplacorchis tenuior, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a rare and delicate member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. The plant prefers shaded, moist environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its growth habit is terrestrial, and it has a short flowering period.
