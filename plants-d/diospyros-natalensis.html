Diospyros natalensis

Diospyros natalensis, commonly known as Natal ebony, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Ebenaceae family and is characterized by its dark, dense wood, which is highly valued for its strength and durability. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth upper surface. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, round fruits that contain a single seed.

The tree is often found in moist, well-drained soils and is adapted to a variety of tropical and subtropical climates.
