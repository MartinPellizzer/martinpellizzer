Dioscorea yunnanensis

Dioscorea yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan yam, is a tuberous plant native to China, particularly Yunnan Province.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its vigorous climbing habit and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces cylindrical tubers that are rich in starch and have been traditionally used in Chinese medicine.

Its botanical significance lies in its potential as a valuable crop for food and medicinal purposes.
