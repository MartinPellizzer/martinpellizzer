Dracaena nutans

Dracaena nutans, commonly known as the hanging ivy or Madagascar dragon tree, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its long, drooping inflorescences that bear small, white flowers, which are often followed by red berries. The plant has strap-shaped, dark green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant.

Dracaena nutans is also valued for its ability to purify air and tolerate low light conditions.
