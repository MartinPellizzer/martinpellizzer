Delphinium foliosum

Delphinium foliosum, commonly known as the leafy larkspur, is a member of the Ranunculaceae family and is native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant features palmately divided, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are deeply lobed and arranged alternately along the stem, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Delphinium foliosum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and alpine settings.
