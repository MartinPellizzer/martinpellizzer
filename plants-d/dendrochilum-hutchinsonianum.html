Dendrochilum hutchinsonianum

Dendrochilum hutchinsonianum, commonly known as the Hutchinson's tree orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, woody stem and dense clusters of fragrant, dark purple flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments within the rainforest canopy. Its leaves are broad and elliptical, often with a waxy texture to retain moisture.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
