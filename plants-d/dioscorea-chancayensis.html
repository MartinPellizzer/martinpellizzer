Dioscorea chancayensis

Dioscorea chancayensis, commonly known as Chancay yam, is a tuberous plant belonging to the family Dioscoreaceae.

It is native to the Andean region of South America, particularly in Peru and Ecuador. The plant produces edible tubers that are used in traditional Andean cuisine. Its growth is characterized by climbing vines that support the tubers underground.

The botanical features of D. chancayensis include compound leaves and small, greenish-white flowers.
