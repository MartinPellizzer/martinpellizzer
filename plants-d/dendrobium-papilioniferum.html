Dendrobium papilioniferum

Dendrobium papilioniferum, commonly known as the butterfly orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with cane-like stems that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces showy, butterfly-shaped flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium papilioniferum is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
