Dracaena illustris

Dracaena illustris, commonly known as the Madagascar dragon tree, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and long, narrow leaves that are typically dark green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters at the top of the stem. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.

Dracaena illustris thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.
