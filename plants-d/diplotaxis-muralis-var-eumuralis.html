Diplotaxis muralis var. eumuralis

Diplotaxis muralis var. eumuralis, commonly known as the red-flowered wallflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing annual herb with distinctive yellow-green leaves and bright red or orange flowers. The plant thrives in dry, rocky environments and is often found growing on walls or in rocky crevices. Its flowers are typically arranged in dense, branching clusters, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
