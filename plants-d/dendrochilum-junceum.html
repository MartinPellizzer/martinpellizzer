Dendrochilum junceum

Dendrochilum junceum, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. Its flowers are usually small and fragrant, often displaying a variety of colors including white, pink, and purple.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
