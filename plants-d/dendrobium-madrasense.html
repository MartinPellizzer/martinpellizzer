Dendrobium madrasense

Dendrobium madrasense, commonly known as the Madras orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems and linear, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with white markings. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Dendrobium madrasense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated for horticultural purposes.
