Dracaena bicolor

Dracaena bicolor, commonly known as the Variegated Dracaena, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, narrow leaves that are typically green with white or yellow variegation, creating an eye-catching appearance. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its slow growth rate and minimal maintenance requirements contribute to its popularity as a houseplant.

Dracaena bicolor is also valued for its air-purifying properties, enhancing indoor air quality.
