Dendrobium fasciculatum

Dendrobium fasciculatum, commonly known as the clustered dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact growth habit with multiple pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink, often arranged in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
