Dufourea cerastiifolia

Dufourea cerastiifolia, commonly known as the starry sandwort, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically thrives in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are often pink or white, blooming in early summer. Its leaves are fleshy and succulent, adapted to retain moisture in arid conditions.
