Desmodium glareosum

Desmodium glareosum, commonly known as the yellow desmodium, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, each consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or grasslands.

Desmodium glareosum is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial in agricultural settings.
