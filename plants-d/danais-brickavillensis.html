Danais brickavillensis

Danais brickavillensis, commonly known as the Brickaville flamebush, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, woody structure and vibrant orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and tropical gardens.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
