Delphinium barbatum

Delphinium barbatum, commonly known as the beard tongue delphinium, is a member of the Ranunculaceae family.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant features showy, blue to purple spires of flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are palmately divided, with a distinctive hairy texture.

Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, it thrives in well-drained, rocky soils.
