Doemia tomentosa

Doemia tomentosa, commonly known as the wooly doemia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features hairy, gray-green foliage that gives it a distinctive, woolly appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Doemia tomentosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
