Dendrobium ditschiense

Dendrobium ditschiense, commonly known as the Red-veined Dendrobium, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, cane-like pseudobulbs covered in dense, leathery leaves. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with prominent veining. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Dendrobium ditschiense is prized for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
