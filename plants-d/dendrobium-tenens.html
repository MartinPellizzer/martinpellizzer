Dendrobium tenens

Dendrobium tenens, commonly known as the orchid with the golden lip, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with a distinctive orange lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrobium tenens is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
