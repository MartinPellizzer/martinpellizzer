Dolichos tricostatus

Dolichos tricostatus, commonly known as the three-costate dolichos, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features trifoliate leaves with three leaflets, each having a distinctive three-lobed base. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by elongated, cylindrical pods containing kidney-shaped seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species in ecological and agricultural contexts.
