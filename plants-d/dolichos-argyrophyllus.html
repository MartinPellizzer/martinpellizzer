Dolichos argyrophyllus

Dolichos argyrophyllus, commonly known as silver-leaf dolichos, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its distinctive silvery-white foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by elongated, pod-like fruits containing several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a warm, humid climate.

Dolichos argyrophyllus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has also been used in traditional medicine.
