Dilivaria volubilis

Dilivaria volubilis, commonly known as the silver vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, pale yellow flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that turn from green to dark blue as they mature. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Dilivaria volubilis is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
