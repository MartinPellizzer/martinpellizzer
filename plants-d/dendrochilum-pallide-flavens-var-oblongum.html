Dendrochilum pallide-flavens var. oblongum

Dendrochilum pallide-flavens var. oblongum, commonly known as the pale-yellow orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers with a distinctive fragrance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variant is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
