Deyeuxia spicata

Deyeuxia spicata, commonly known as the spikegrass, is a species of grass native to various regions of Asia, including China, Japan, and the Korean Peninsula.

The plant has slender, grass-like leaves and produces dense, spike-like inflorescences that are often pinkish or reddish in color.
