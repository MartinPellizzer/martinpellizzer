Dendrophorbium reflexum

Dendrophorbium reflexum, commonly known as the reflexed bark plant, is a unique species native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its woody stems that display a distinctive, curling pattern, giving it its common name. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are typically arranged in a spiral along the stem. Its flowers are inconspicuous, with pale green petals, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Dendrophorbium reflexum is adapted to grow in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its resilience to harsh conditions.
