Deprea harlingiana

Deprea harlingiana, commonly known as the harling's bellflower, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features showy, bell-shaped flowers that are usually pink or purple in color, blooming from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or grassland habitats.
