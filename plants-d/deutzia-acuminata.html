Deutzia acuminata

Deutzia acuminata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features clusters of fragrant, white to pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has slender, arching stems and lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Deutzia acuminata is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and showy blooms.
