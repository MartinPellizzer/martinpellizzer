Dracaena frommii

Dracaena frommii, commonly known as the Dragon Tree, is a species native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, lance-shaped leaves that are often variegated with yellow and green patterns. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by red berries, which are edible for birds but toxic to humans. Dracaena frommii thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
