Damnacanthus Indicus F. Major

Damnacanthus indicus f. major is a striking member of the Rubiaceae family known for its vibrant, showy flowers. This plant is native to tropical regions of Asia and is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes. Its large, trumpet-shaped blooms come in shades of red, orange, and yellow, making it a standout in any collection. While it thrives in warm climates, it requires careful attention to soil and water conditions for optimal growth. Despite its beauty, it is important to note that this plant can be toxic if ingested, so caution is advised when handling it.
