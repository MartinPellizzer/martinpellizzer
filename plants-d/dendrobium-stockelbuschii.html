Dendrobium stockelbuschii

Dendrobium stockelbuschii, commonly known as the Stockelbusch's orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with multiple stems that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with intricate patterns on the petals. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium stockelbuschii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its long-lasting blooms.
