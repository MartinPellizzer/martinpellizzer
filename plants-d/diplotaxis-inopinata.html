Diplotaxis inopinata

Diplotaxis inopinata, commonly known as the African ironweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers. It is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.

Diplotaxis inopinata is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
