Dianthus Thunbergii

Dianthus thunbergii is a delicate flowering plant known for its vibrant pink and white blooms. Native to eastern Asia, it thrives in woodland and moist meadow environments. This species belongs to the carnation family, which includes many ornamental plants. Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. The plant's ability to attract pollinators adds to its ecological value.
