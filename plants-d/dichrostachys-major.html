Dichrostachys major

Dichrostachys major, commonly known as the white flame tree, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its bipinnate leaves and striking white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its showy inflorescences. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for warm climates.

The species is also sometimes used in traditional medicine.
