Desmodium dubium

Desmodium dubium, commonly known as smallleaf burr medic, is a perennial legume native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, hairy pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Desmodium dubium is often found in disturbed soils and is used in some regions for forage and soil improvement.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it beneficial in agricultural settings.
