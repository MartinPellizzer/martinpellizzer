Dendrochilum foxworthyi

Dendrochilum foxworthyi, commonly known as the Foxworth's tree orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments. The plant features a rhizomatous stem with erect, lanceolate leaves. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with a prominent lip, and it blooms during the rainy season.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
