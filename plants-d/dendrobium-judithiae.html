Dendrobium judithiae

Dendrobium judithiae, commonly known as the Judith's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its tall, cane-like pseudobulbs and clusters of fragrant, pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments on trees or rocks. Its leaves are long and narrow, and it blooms during the summer months.

Dendrobium judithiae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
