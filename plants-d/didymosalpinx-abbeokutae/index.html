Didymosalpinx Abbeokutae

Abbeokuta orchid, scientifically known as Didymosalpinx abbeokutae, is a rare and unique species native to the tropical rainforests of Nigeria. This striking orchid is named after the city of Abbeokuta, where it was first discovered, highlighting its cultural and geographical significance. With its vivid colors and intricate floral structure, it stands out among other orchid species in the region. Despite its beauty, the Abbeokuta orchid faces threats from habitat loss and over-collection. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival for future generations.
