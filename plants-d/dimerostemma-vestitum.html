Dimerostemma vestitum

Dimerostemma vestitum, commonly known as the velvetleaf dimerostemma, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat preferences, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
