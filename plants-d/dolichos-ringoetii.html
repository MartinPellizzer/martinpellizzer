Dolichos ringoetii

Dolichos ringoetii, commonly known as the slender cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are followed by elongated, pod-like fruits. Its seeds are typically small and oval, often used in traditional cuisines.

Dolichos ringoetii is valued for its nutritional content and ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it an important crop in sustainable agriculture.
