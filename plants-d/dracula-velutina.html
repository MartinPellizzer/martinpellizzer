Dracula velutina

Dracula velutina, commonly known as the velvet dracula, is a rare and striking bromeliad native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers and velvety, dark green foliage. The plant produces striking, long-lasting inflorescences that can reach up to 3 meters in height. Dracula velutina thrives in high humidity and partial shade, making it a challenging but rewarding plant for experienced growers.

Its unique appearance and rarity make it a prized specimen in botanical collections.
