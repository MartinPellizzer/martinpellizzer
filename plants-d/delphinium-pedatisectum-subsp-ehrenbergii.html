Delphinium pedatisectum subsp. ehrenbergii

Delphinium pedatisectum subsp.

ehrenbergii, commonly known as the Ehrenberg's larkspur, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe. It features tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters.

The plant produces showy, blue to purple flowers arranged in racemes, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are deeply lobed and divided, giving it a distinctive, fern-like appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a striking addition to alpine gardens.
