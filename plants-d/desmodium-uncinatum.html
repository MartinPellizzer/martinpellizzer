Desmodium uncinatum

Desmodium uncinatum, commonly known as sicklepod, is an annual legume native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellow pea-like flowers. The plant produces oblong, spiny pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural fields and along roadsides.

Desmodium uncinatum is considered a weed in many regions due to its rapid growth and competitive nature.
