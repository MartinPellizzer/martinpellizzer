Diospyros nyangensis

Diospyros nyangensis, commonly known as the Nyangensis ebony, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Central Africa.

It belongs to the Ebenaceae family, which includes other ebony and persimmon species. The tree is characterized by its dark, dense wood, which is highly valued for its durability and fine grain. It typically grows in moist, shaded habitats within tropical rainforests.

Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a glossy green appearance, contributing to its ornamental value.
