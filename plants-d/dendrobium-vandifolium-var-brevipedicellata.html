Dendrobium vandifolium var. brevipedicellata

Dendrobium vandifolium var. brevipedicellata, commonly known as the short-stemmed vandifolium orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic orchid, typically growing on trees or rocks in tropical rainforests. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually yellow or greenish-yellow, with a distinctive lip that is often marked with purple or red.

This variety is appreciated for its ornamental value and is cultivated for its unique floral display.
