Dendrobium bicolense

Dendrobium bicolense, commonly known as the two-colored orchid, is a sympodial epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a tall, wiry stem with numerous nodes and is known for its long-lasting, fragrant flowers. The plant produces racemes of showy, bilaterally symmetrical blooms in shades of white, yellow, and pink. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium bicolense is popular among orchid growers for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
