Dactylorhiza traunsteineri subsp. turfosa

Dactylorhiza traunsteineri subsp.

turfosa, commonly known as the Turf Orchid, is a rare and specialized orchid species native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe. It is a terrestrial orchid with a short, thick rhizome and produces a single, erect stem bearing a few showy, greenish-yellow flowers.

The plant thrives in acidic, moist, and shaded habitats, often found in heaths and mountain meadows. It is characterized by its small, strap-shaped leaves and is adapted to nutrient-poor soils.
