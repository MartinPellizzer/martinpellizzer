Decussocarpus maximus

Decussocarpus maximus, commonly known as the giant decussocarpus, is a unique plant species characterized by its distinctive leaf arrangement and reproductive structures.

The plant produces large, showy flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences, attracting various pollinators. Its fruits are fleshy and often contain multiple seeds, contributing to its reproductive success.
