Dichapetalum gelonioides subsp. gelonioides

Dichapetalum gelonioides subsp.

gelonioides, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and woodland areas. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a hairy texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
