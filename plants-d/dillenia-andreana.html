Dillenia andreana

Dillenia andreana, commonly known as the Andean dillenia, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Dilleniaceae and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant has simple, alternate leaves with a rough texture and prominent veins. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Dillenia andreana is often found in moist, forested areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
