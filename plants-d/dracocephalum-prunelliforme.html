Dracocephalum prunelliforme

Dracocephalum prunelliforme, commonly known as purple head or red stem verbena, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to pink. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Dracocephalum prunelliforme is often grown in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
