Delairea odorata

Delairea odorata, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, cylindrical fruit capsules.

Delairea odorata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
