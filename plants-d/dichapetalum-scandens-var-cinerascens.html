Dichapetalum scandens var. cinerascens

Dichapetalum scandens var. cinerascens, commonly known as the cinerascens dichapetalum, is a climbing shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, twining stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. The flowers are small, fragrant, and typically white or pale pink, arranged in clusters. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain several seeds.

It is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forest understories.
