Dyschoriste hildebrandtii var. hildebrandtii

Dyschoriste hildebrandtii var. hildebrandtii, commonly known as the Hildebrandt's dyschoriste, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers during the summer months. Its botanical structure includes a fibrous root system and a branching stem.

It is often found in coastal and inland areas with well-drained soil conditions.
