Didymocarpus helicteroides

Didymocarpus helicteroides, commonly known as the false silk-cotton tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its palmate leaves, which are typically divided into five to seven lobes, and its showy, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
