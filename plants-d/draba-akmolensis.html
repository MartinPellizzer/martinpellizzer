Draba akmolensis

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant is characterized by its low-growing habit, with basal leaves and small, white or pale yellow flowers. It typically blooms in the spring and is adapted to rocky or arid environments.
