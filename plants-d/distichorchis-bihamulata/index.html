Distichorchis Bihamulata

Distichorchis bihamulata is a rare and striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of South America. Known for its unique bi-colored petals, it displays a combination of white and purple hues that make it visually captivating. This plant thrives in cool, shaded environments and requires specific humidity levels to flourish. Due to its limited habitat and threats from deforestation, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival for future generations.
