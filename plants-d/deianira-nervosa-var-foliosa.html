Deianira nervosa var. foliosa

Deianira nervosa var. foliosa, commonly known as the leafy nerve plant, is a flowering species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests. Its leaves are deeply lobed, giving it a distinctive appearance, while its flowers emit a subtle, sweet scent.

This variant is valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
