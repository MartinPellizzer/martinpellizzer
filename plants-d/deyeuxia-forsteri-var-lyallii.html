Deyeuxia forsteri var. lyallii

Deyeuxia forsteri var. lyallii, commonly known as Lyall's flat sedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to New Zealand.

It typically grows in wetland or swampy habitats, often in association with other moisture-loving plants. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers in spikelets. It is characterized by its ability to thrive in waterlogged soils and is often found in coastal or inland wetlands.

This variety is notable for its ecological importance in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
