Davallia stenoloba

Davallia stenoloba, commonly known as the narrow-leaf hartiella, is a fern native to Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, pinnate leaves that are typically bright green and have a smooth texture. The plant produces small, spore-bearing sorus clusters on the underside of its fronds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Davallia stenoloba is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its elegant appearance and ease of care.
