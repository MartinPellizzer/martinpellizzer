Drakaea confluens

Drakaea confluens, commonly known as the confluence spider-orchid, is an endemic species of the Australian flora.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, spider-like appearance. The plant produces a single, erect stem with a single flower that is typically greenish-yellow in color. It is a terrestrial orchid that thrives in heathland and open forest habitats.

Drakaea confluens is pollinated by thynnid wasps, making it an important component of its native ecosystem.
