Diamena stenantha

Diamena stenantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved diamena, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Diamena stenantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
