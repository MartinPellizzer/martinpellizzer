Diosma bucco

Diosma bucco, commonly known as the red bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped flowers, typically red or orange in color. The leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.
