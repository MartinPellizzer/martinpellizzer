Digitaria eichingeri

Digitaria eichingeri, commonly known as Eichinger's digitaria, is a species of grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in sandy or well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands or along riverbanks.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
