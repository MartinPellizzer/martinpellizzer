Dendrobium loddigesii

Dendrobium loddigesii, commonly known as the Loddiges' orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers with prominent lips. Its leaves are typically long and strap-like, arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Dendrobium loddigesii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
