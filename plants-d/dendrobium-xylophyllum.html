Dendrobium xylophyllum

Dendrobium xylophyllum, commonly known as the flame orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, slender pseudobulbs and clusters of vibrant red or orange flowers. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light. It requires well-draining soil and regular watering during its growing season.

Dendrobium xylophyllum is prized for its striking floral display and is a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
