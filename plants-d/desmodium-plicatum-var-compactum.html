Desmodium plicatum var. compactum

Desmodium plicatum var. compactum, commonly known as the compact velvet bean, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers that typically bloom in clusters. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas and is often found in disturbed soils. It has a creeping growth habit with hairy stems and produces pods that contain edible seeds.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and use in traditional medicine.
