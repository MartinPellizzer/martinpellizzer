Daemonorops ursina

Daemonorops ursina, commonly known as the ursine banyan, is a species of cycad that belongs to the family Cycadaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its large, pinnate leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. Its trunk is typically unbranched and covered with a rough, fibrous bark.

Daemonorops ursina is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has cultural significance in some regions.
