Diasperus manono

Diasperus manono, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant features velvety, dark green leaves and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. It is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

Diasperus manono thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.
