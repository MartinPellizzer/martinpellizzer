Dendrobium devosianum

Dendrobium devosianum, commonly known as the Devosian orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, cane-like stems that often bear clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically produces inflorescences with numerous small to medium-sized blooms in shades of white, yellow, and pink. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Dendrobium devosianum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
