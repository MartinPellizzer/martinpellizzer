Draba gelida

Draba gelida, commonly known as the alpine winter cress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a small herbaceous plant typically found in alpine and subarctic regions. Draba gelida has basal leaves that are oblanceolate and often covered in a dense layer of white hairs. The plant produces small, white or yellow flowers in early spring, even under snow cover.

It is adapted to cold climates and is often used in ecological studies due to its resilience in harsh environments.
