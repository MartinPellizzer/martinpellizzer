Dendrobium moschatum var. unguipetalum

Dendrobium moschatum var. unguipetalum, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant that grows on trees and thrives in humid, warm environments. The plant features long, cane-like stems with clusters of showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white, yellow, and pink. Its flowers have a distinctive, sweet scent that attracts pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
