Dianthus chinensis var. ignescens

Dianthus chinensis var. ignescens, commonly known as the Chinese flame flower, is a striking perennial plant native to China.

It is characterized by its vibrant, flame-like red flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and a compact growth habit, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders. Its flowers are borne on tall, sturdy stems and are known for their intense color and long-lasting display.

The botanical name reflects its origin and the fiery appearance of its blooms.
