Delphinium elatum var. bosniacum

Delphinium elatum var. bosniacum, commonly known as the Bosnian larkspur, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Balkan region.

It features tall, slender stems that bear clusters of striking blue to purple flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. Its flowers are typically arranged in racemes and are characterized by their spurred petals.

Delphinium elatum var. bosniacum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
