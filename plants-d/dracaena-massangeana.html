Dracaena massangeana

Dracaena massangeana, commonly known as the corn plant, is a popular indoor plant native to Africa.

It features tall, upright stems with broad, glossy, dark green leaves that have a slight yellowish margin. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires moderate watering. It is also noted for its air-purifying qualities, making it a favored choice for indoor spaces.

Its slow growth rate makes it suitable for container gardening and as a long-term houseplant.
