Dombeya schimperiana

Dombeya schimperiana, commonly known as the African snowbell, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a rough texture.

Dombeya schimperiana thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions.
