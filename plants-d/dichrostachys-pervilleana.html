Dichrostachys pervilleana

Dichrostachys pervilleana, commonly known as the purple burr legume, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant produces small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink, followed by woody, spiny pods. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in dry or semi-arid conditions.

Its unique morphology and adaptability make it an important species in ecological and agricultural contexts.
