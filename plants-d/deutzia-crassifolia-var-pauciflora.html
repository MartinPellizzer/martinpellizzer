Deutzia crassifolia var. pauciflora

Deutzia crassifolia var. pauciflora, commonly known as the sparse-flowered deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features slender, arching stems that bear clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers in early summer. The leaves are ovate to elliptical, with a smooth texture and a dark green color. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and its ability to attract pollinators.
