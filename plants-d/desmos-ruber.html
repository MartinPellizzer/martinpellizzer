Desmos ruber

Desmos ruber, commonly known as the red desmodium, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pink to purple flowers. The plant is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it valuable in soil enrichment. Its seeds are often used in traditional medicine and as a source of protein in some regions.

The plant thrives in warm, temperate climates and is often found in grasslands and disturbed soils.
