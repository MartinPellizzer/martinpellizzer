Dendrobium nobile var. alboluteum

Dendrobium nobile var. alboluteum, commonly known as the white-lipped dendrobium, is a hybrid orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, cane-like pseudobulbs and long, linear leaves. The plant produces striking clusters of white or pale yellow flowers with a distinctive, waxy texture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
