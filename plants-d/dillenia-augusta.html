Dillenia augusta

Dillenia augusta, commonly known as the velvetleaf dillenia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces showy, yellowish-white flowers with prominent green sepals that resemble petals. Its fruits are large, woody capsules that split open to release numerous seeds.

Dillenia augusta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
