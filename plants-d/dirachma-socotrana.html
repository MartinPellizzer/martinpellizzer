Dirachma socotrana

Dirachma socotrana, commonly known as Socotra cypress, is a unique evergreen conifer native to the island of Socotra.

It is characterized by its distinctive, twisted, and often umbrella-shaped crown. The plant has scale-like leaves that are dark green and closely pressed to the branches. It produces small, spherical cones that contain seeds.

Dirachma socotrana is adapted to the arid climate of its native habitat, showing resilience to drought and high temperatures.
