Daemonorops vagans

Daemonorops vagans, commonly known as the golden banyan, is a species of cycad that is native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces small, spherical cones that are typically green to yellowish in color. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and collections worldwide.
