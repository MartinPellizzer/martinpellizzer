Dolichos finlaysonianus

Dolichos finlaysonianus, commonly known as the scarlet runner bean, is a climbing legume native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its long, slender pods that contain edible seeds, often used in cooking. The plant produces vibrant red or orange flowers, which are typical of the Fabaceae family. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

Dolichos finlaysonianus is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its nutritional content.
