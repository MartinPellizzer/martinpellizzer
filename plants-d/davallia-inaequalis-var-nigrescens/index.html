Davallia Inaequalis Var. Nigrescens

Davallia inaequalis var. nigrescens is a rare and intriguing fern species known for its striking dark foliage. Native to specific regions in Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its unique appearance makes it a sought-after addition to both botanical gardens and private collections. The variety's name reflects its distinctive dark green leaves, which contrast beautifully with its delicate fronds. Cultivating this fern requires careful attention to moisture and light conditions to ensure its health and vitality.
