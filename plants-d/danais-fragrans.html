Danais fragrans

Danais fragrans, commonly known as the fragrant danais, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Rutaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Indonesia and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
