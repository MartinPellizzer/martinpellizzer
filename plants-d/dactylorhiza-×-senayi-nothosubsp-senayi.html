senayi, commonly known as the hybrid lady's slipper orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family. It is a terrestrial herbaceous plant characterized by its unique, slipper-shaped flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and bogs. Its leaves are grass-like and arranged in a rosette, while its roots are specialized for nutrient absorption.
