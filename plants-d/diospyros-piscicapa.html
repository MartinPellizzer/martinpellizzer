Diospyros piscicapa

Diospyros piscicapa, commonly known as the fishbone ebony, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark, dense wood, which is highly valued for its strength and durability. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth texture. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by orange-red fruits resembling small berries.

The species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and timber quality.
