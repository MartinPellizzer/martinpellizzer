Dendrobium bandii

Dendrobium bandii, commonly known as the purple orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking purple flowers and compact, cane-like growth habit. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers bloom in summer and are known for their fragrance.

Dendrobium bandii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
