Dracaena tessellata

Dracaena tessellata, commonly known as the tiger tail bamboo, is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with alternating light and dark green stripes, creating a visually appealing appearance. The plant has upright, cane-like stems that can grow up to several feet tall, making it a popular choice for indoor decoration. Its leaves are narrow and strap-shaped, with a smooth texture and a subtle sheen.

Dracaena tessellata is relatively low-maintenance, thriving in bright, indirect light and requiring moderate watering.
