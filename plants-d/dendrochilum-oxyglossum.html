Dendrochilum oxyglossum

Dendrochilum oxyglossum, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems and lanceolate, dark green leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with a distinctive lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Dendrochilum oxyglossum is prized for its ornamental value and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts worldwide.
