Doliocarpus soramia

Doliocarpus soramia, commonly known as the Soramia tamarind.

It is a small evergreen tree native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences. The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds.

It is valued for its timber and has some traditional medicinal uses.
