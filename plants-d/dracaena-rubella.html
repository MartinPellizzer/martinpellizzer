Dracaena rubella

Dracaena rubella, commonly known as the red dracaena, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and long, narrow leaves that exhibit a vibrant red or orange hue at the tips. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by red berries, which are a key feature in its ornamental appeal. Dracaena rubella thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil with moderate watering.

It is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
