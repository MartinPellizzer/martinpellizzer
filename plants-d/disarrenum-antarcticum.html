Disarrenum antarcticum

Disarrenum antarcticum, commonly known as the Antarctic pearlwort, is a flowering plant native to the cold regions of the Southern Hemisphere.

It is a low-growing perennial species that thrives in rocky and coastal environments. The plant produces small, white or greenish flowers arranged in clusters, and its leaves are typically succulent to retain moisture in harsh conditions.
