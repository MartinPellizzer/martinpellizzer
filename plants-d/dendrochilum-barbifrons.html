Dendrochilum barbifrons

Dendrochilum barbifrons, commonly known as the barbed lip orchid, is a tropical orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant features long, narrow leaves and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers with distinctive, barbed lip petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrochilum barbifrons is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
