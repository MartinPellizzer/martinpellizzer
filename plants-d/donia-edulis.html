Donia edulis

Donia edulis, commonly known as the edible nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and thrives in warm, humid climates. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into berries, which are edible when ripe. Its leaves are also used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

However, the plant contains toxic compounds, so it must be handled with care.
