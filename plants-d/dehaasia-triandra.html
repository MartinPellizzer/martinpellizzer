Dehaasia triandra

Dehaasia triandra, commonly known as the African moonflower, is a striking plant native to southern Africa.

It is a climbing shrub that produces large, white, fragrant flowers that open at night. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is characterized by its unique, showy blooms that emit a sweet scent to attract nocturnal pollinators. Dehaasia triandra thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
