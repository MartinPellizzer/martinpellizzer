Diuris maculata var. palustris

Diuris maculata var. palustris, commonly known as the marsh diuris, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It typically grows in wetland or swampy habitats, thriving in moist, acidic soils. The plant features a slender, often branched stem with several narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellow, with distinctive markings and a spurred labellum.

This variety is noted for its resilience in waterlogged conditions and is often found in coastal or riverine environments.
