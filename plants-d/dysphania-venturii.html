Dysphania venturii

Dysphania venturii, commonly known as red leaf groundsel, is an annual herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features upright stems that are often reddish in color, and its leaves are typically oval to lance-shaped with a rough texture. The plant produces small, yellow flowers clustered in terminal inflorescences, which bloom during the cooler months. Dysphania venturii is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

It is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its striking foliage.
