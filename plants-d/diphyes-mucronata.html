Diphyes mucronata

Diphyes mucronata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in a cluster. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Diphyes mucronata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
