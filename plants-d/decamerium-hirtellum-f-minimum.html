Decamerium hirtellum f. minimum

Decamerium hirtellum f. minimum, commonly known as the dwarf decamerium, is a compact, low-growing perennial plant native to the western United States.

It features dense clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has hairy, ovate leaves that are typically arranged in whorls around the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact size and ornamental flowers make it a valuable addition to drought-tolerant landscapes.
