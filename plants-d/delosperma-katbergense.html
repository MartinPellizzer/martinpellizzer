Delosperma katbergense

Delosperma katbergense, commonly known as the Katberg ice plant, is a succulent perennial native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves and vibrant yellow flowers. Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it an attractive and low-maintenance ornamental plant.
