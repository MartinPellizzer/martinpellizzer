Dendrobium aggregatum var. jenkinsii

Dendrobium aggregatum var. jenkinsii, commonly known as the Jenkins' orchid, is a sympodial epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact growth habit with a short stem and numerous clustered pseudobulbs. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with a contrasting lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
