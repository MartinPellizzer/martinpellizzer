Deutzia paniculata

Deutzia paniculata, commonly known as the panicled deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It features showy, fragrant white to pink flowers that appear in clusters during late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, arching branches and heart-shaped leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Deutzia paniculata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
