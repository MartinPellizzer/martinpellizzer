Delphinium hirticaule var. hirticaule

Delphinium hirticaule var. hirticaule, commonly known as the hairy delphinium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters. The plant produces showy, blue to purple flowers in racemes, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lanceolate and covered with fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.
