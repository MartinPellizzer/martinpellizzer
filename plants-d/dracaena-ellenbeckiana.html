Dracaena ellenbeckiana

Dracaena ellenbeckiana, commonly known as the Ellenbeck's dracaena, is a species of flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and long, narrow leaves that are typically a deep green color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear at the base of the leaves. Dracaena ellenbeckiana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its elegant appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.

It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.
