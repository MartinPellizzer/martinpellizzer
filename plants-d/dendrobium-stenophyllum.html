Dendrobium stenophyllum

Dendrobium stenophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with a distinctive lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrobium stenophyllum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
