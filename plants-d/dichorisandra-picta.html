Dichorisandra picta

Dichorisandra picta, commonly known as the painted dichorisandra, is a striking evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often adorned with intricate white or yellow patterns. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, which bloom throughout the warmer months. Its unique foliage and showy blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Dichorisandra picta thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it adaptable to various garden settings.
