Dendrobium bonianum

Dendrobium bonianum, commonly known as the Bonian orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome that supports multiple pseudobulbs. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically during the rainy season. Its leaves are strap-like and arranged in a rosette pattern around the pseudobulbs.

It requires well-drained soil and bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
