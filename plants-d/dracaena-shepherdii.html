Dracaena shepherdii

Dracaena shepherdii, commonly known as the shepherd's dracaena, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It features tall, slender stems with narrow, dark green leaves that have a subtle reddish tinge along the edges. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by red berries, which are an important food source for local wildlife. It is well adapted to drought conditions, making it a hardy choice for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular ornamental plant in gardens and landscapes.
