Dillenia excelsa var. tomentella

Dillenia excelsa var. tomentella, commonly known as the woolly dillenia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense, woolly pubescence. The plant produces showy, white or pale yellow flowers that are typically arranged in clusters. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, shaded environments.

The fruit is a distinctive, fleshy, red berry that contains several seeds.
