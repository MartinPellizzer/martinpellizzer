Draba hirsutiuscula

Draba hirsutiuscula, commonly known as the hairy draba, is a small flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of central and eastern Europe, typically found in alpine and subalpine habitats. The plant features basal leaves that are hairy and linear, with small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. It is a perennial herb that often grows in rocky or gravelly soils.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
