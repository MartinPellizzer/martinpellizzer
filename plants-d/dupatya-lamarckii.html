Dupatya lamarckii

Dupatya lamarckii, commonly known as the Lamarck's sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related grass-like plants. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy environments, preferring moist, well-drained soils. Its slender, triangular stems are often clustered, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in early spring.
