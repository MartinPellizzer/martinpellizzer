Desmodium oxyphyllum f. decorum

Desmodium oxyphyllum f. decorum, commonly known as the ornamental desmodium, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is native to tropical regions of Africa and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its growth habit is typically herbaceous, with a compact and bushy appearance.
