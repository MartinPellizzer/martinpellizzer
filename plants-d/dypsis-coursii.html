Dypsis coursii

Dypsis coursii, commonly known as the Madagascar palm, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its unique, spiny appearance with a short trunk and clustered, feather-like leaves. Dypsis coursii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and resilience.
