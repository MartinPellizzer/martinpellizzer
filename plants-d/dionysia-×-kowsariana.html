Dionysia × kowsariana

Dionysia × kowsariana, commonly known as the Kowsariana dionysia, is a hybrid evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage and produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and landscapes.

Dionysia × kowsariana is also noted for its resilience to coastal conditions and salt spray.
