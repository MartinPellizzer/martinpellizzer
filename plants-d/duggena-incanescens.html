Duggena incanescens

Duggena incanescens, commonly known as the silverleaf dudleya, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are covered in a powdery white coating, which helps to reduce water loss in its harsh environment. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Due to its striking appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and succulent collections.
