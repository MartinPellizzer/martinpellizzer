Dryopteris yabei f. ogawae

Dryopteris yabei f. ogawae, commonly known as the Yabei shield fern, is a rare fern species native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, elongated fronds with a shield-shaped base. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky slopes. Its leaves are bi-lobed and have a leathery texture, with a deep green coloration.

This fern is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and specialized collections.
