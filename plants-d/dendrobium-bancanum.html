Dendrobium bancanum

Dendrobium bancanum, commonly known as the Bankan orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, leafy stems and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically blooms in the summer, producing pink to white petals with a yellow lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrobium bancanum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
