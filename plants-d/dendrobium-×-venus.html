Dendrobium × venus

Dendrobium × venus, commonly known as the Venus orchid, is a hybrid orchid species that combines traits from various dendrobium species.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant produces long, cane-like stems that can store water and nutrients, which is a characteristic feature of many dendrobium orchids. Its flowers are usually showy, with a range of colors including white, yellow, and pink, often arranged in clusters.

Dendrobium × venus requires bright, indirect light and well-draining potting media to thrive.
