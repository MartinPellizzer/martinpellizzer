Disa chiovendaei

Disa chiovendaei, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a striking South African orchid species belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple and white flowers, which are typically arranged in a raceme along a slender stem. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in rocky, well-drained soils in its native habitat. Disa chiovendaei is often found in mountainous regions of South Africa, particularly in the Western Cape.

It is a perennial herb that blooms during the summer months, adding a splash of color to its natural environment.
