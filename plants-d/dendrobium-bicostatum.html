Dendrobium bicostatum

Dendrobium bicostatum, commonly known as the two-costed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome that produces multiple pseudobulbs. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and covered in a waxy cuticle. Its flowers are typically yellow or green with red or brown markings, appearing in clusters along the stem.

Dendrobium bicostatum prefers shady, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.
