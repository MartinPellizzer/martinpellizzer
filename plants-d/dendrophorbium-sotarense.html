Dendrophorbium sotarense

Dendrophorbium sotarense, commonly known as the Sotara tree, is a unique plant native to the island of Sotara in the Indian Ocean.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that thrives in coastal and rocky habitats. The plant is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. It has adapted to withstand strong sea breezes and salt spray, making it a resilient species.

The Sotara tree plays an important ecological role in stabilizing coastal soils and providing habitat for local wildlife.
