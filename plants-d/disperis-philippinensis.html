Disperis philippinensis

Disperis philippinensis, commonly known as the Philippine disperis, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are usually white or pale pink with intricate patterns.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
