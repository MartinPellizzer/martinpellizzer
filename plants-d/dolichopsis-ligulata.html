Dolichopsis ligulata

Dolichopsis ligulata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silky hair. The plant produces striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive, elongated corolla. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is widely cultivated in horticulture.
