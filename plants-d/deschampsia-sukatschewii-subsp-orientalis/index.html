Deschampsia Sukatschewii Subsp. Orientalis

Eastern deschampsia, scientifically known as Deschampsia sukatschewii subsp. orientalis, is a rare grass species native to eastern Europe and parts of western Russia. It thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and forest edges, often in areas with nutrient-rich soils. This plant is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance and its ability to form dense, low-growing clumps. Due to habitat loss and human encroachment, it faces significant threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique and ecologically important species.
