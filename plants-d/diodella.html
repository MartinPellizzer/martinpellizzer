Diodella

Diodella, commonly known as the glasswinged dandelion, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, translucent leaves and bright yellow flowers that resemble dandelion blooms. Diodella is often found in meadows and along roadsides, where it plays a role in supporting local pollinator populations.
