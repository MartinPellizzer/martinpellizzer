Ditassa carnevalii

Ditassa carnevalii, commonly known as the Cape chestnut, is a small evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features elliptical, glossy dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
