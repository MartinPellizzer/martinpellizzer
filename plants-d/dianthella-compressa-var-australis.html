Dianthella compressa var. australis

Dianthella compressa var. australis, commonly known as the Australian sand dianthus, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically thrives in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant features compact, mat-forming growth with small, showy pink or purple flowers. Its foliage consists of narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

It is well adapted to arid environments and is often found in coastal or inland sand dunes.
