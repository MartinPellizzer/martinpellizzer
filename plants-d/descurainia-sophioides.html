Descurainia sophioides

Descurainia sophioides, commonly known as yellow rocket, is an annual herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many cruciferous species. The plant features upright stems with alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are small, yellow, and arranged in clusters, typical of the mustard family.
