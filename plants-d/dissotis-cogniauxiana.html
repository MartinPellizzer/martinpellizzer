Dissotis cogniauxiana

Dissotis cogniauxiana, commonly known as the yellow-eyed grass, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

Dissotis cogniauxiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in water gardens.
