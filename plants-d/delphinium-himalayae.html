Delphinium himalayae

Delphinium himalayae, commonly known as the Himalayan larkspur, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces showy blue to purple flowers in racemes, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a serrated margin, typical of the Delphinium genus.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for alpine gardens.
