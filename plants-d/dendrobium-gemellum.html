Dendrobium gemellum

Dendrobium gemellum, commonly known as the golden spike orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to Australia.

It features a compact, upright growth habit with a pseudobulb system that stores water and nutrients. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with a distinctive golden stripe down the center. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium gemellum is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
