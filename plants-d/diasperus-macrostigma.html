Diasperus macrostigma

Diasperus macrostigma, commonly known as the large-stemmed grass.

It is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa, characterized by its robust, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Its botanical features include a fibrous root system and a tendency to form dense clumps.

It is often found in grasslands and disturbed soils, showing adaptability to various environmental conditions.
