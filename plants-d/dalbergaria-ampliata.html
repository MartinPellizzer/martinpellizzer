Dalbergaria ampliata

Dalbergaria ampliata, commonly known as the broad-leaved dalbergia, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to southern Africa, where it typically grows in grassland and rocky habitats. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in spring. It produces pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Dalbergaria ampliata is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in dry conditions.
