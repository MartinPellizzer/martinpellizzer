Dendrolobium geesinkii

Dendrolobium geesinkii, commonly known as the yellow burr legume, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its woody stems and compound leaves, which are typically trifoliate. The plant produces distinctive yellow pods that contain small, round seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in disturbed or open habitats.

Dendrolobium geesinkii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage and seed pods.
