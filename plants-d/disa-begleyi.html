Disa begleyi

Disa begleyi, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a species native to South Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

Disa begleyi is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
