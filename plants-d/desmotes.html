Desmotes

Desmotes, commonly known as the "fairy daisies," are small, flowering plants native to the Mediterranean region.

They belong to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and thistles. The plant features delicate, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling tiny daisies. Desmotes have narrow, grass-like leaves and are often found growing in dry, rocky soils.

They are valued for their ornamental appeal and are sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
