Delonix leucantha subsp. bemarahensis

Delonix leucantha subsp.

bemarahensis, commonly known as the Bemarahensis flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to parts of Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its striking orange to red flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to the landscape.

The plant has pinnately compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal areas or near rivers.

The subspecies is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
