Dracaena conspicua

Dracaena conspicua, commonly known as the red-edged dracaena, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, narrow leaves that have a distinctive red margin, giving it a vibrant appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically in white or pale yellow, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Dracaena conspicua is also valued for its air-purifying properties and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
