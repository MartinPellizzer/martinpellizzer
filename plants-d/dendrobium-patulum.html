Dendrobium patulum

Dendrobium patulum, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Dendrobium patulum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
