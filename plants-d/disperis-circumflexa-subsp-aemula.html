Disperis circumflexa subsp. aemula

Disperis circumflexa subsp.

aemula, commonly known as the spiral disperis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive spiral arrangement of leaves.

The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are often clustered in a spiral pattern along the stem. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in semi-arid to arid environments.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
