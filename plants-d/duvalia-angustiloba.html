Duvalia angustiloba

Duvalia angustiloba, commonly known as the narrow-leaved duvalia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in coastal regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it requires well-drained soil and full sun.
