Dombeya oligantha

Dombeya oligantha, commonly known as the mountain dombeya, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant produces cylindrical, woody fruits that contain numerous small, flat seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Dombeya oligantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
