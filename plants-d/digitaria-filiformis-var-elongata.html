Digitaria filiformis var. elongata

Digitaria filiformis var. elongata, commonly known as the slender digit grass, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves and compact, cylindrical inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields. It has a fibrous root system and produces small, wind-pollinated flowers.

Its morphology makes it well adapted to environments with varying soil conditions and light availability.
