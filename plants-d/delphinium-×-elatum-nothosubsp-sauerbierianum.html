Delphinium × elatum nothosubsp. sauerbierianum

Delphinium × elatum nothosubsp.

sauerbierianum, commonly known as the hybrid delphinium, is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems. It features large, showy flowers that are typically blue to purple in color, arranged in dense spikes at the top of the plant.

The leaves are lance-shaped and deeply lobed, with a rough, hairy texture. This plant is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
