Delphinium wenchuanense

Delphinium wenchuanense, commonly known as Wenchuan larkspur, is a perennial herb native to China, specifically the Wenchuan region.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems and showy blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky slopes at high elevations. Its leaves are deeply lobed and arranged alternately along the stem.

Delphinium wenchuanense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also studied for its potential medicinal properties.
