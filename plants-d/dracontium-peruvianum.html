Dracontium peruvianum

Dracontium peruvianum, commonly known as the Peruvian arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The roots of Dracontium peruvianum are used in traditional medicine and as a starch source.
