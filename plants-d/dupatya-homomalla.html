Dupatya homomalla

Dupatya homomalla, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its drooping clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant has simple, ovate leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

The flowers are not only ornamental but also attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
