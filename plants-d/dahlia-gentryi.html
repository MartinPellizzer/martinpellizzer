Dahlia gentryi

Dahlia gentryi, commonly known as the Gentry dahlia, is a tuberous-rooted perennial plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, single or double flowers that come in a wide range of colors. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet, with sturdy stems and lance-shaped leaves. Its tuberous roots are essential for its survival, allowing it to store nutrients and water.

The flowers bloom in late summer to early fall, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
