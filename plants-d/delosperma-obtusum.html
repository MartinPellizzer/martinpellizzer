Delosperma obtusum

Delosperma obtusum, commonly known as the ice plant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of pink, red, and yellow, typically during the summer months. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.

Its scientific name reflects its characteristic rounded leaves and its ability to survive in harsh conditions.
