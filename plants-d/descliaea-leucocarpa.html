Descliaea leucocarpa

Descliaea leucocarpa, commonly known as the white-fruited orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a spike, which bloom during the rainy season. Its fruits are fleshy, white berries that contain numerous seeds.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.
