Dierama robustum

Dierama robustum, commonly known as the stiff-leaved dierama, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its upright, grass-like foliage and showy, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its blooms, which range in color from white to pink, are often clustered at the top of the stems.

Dierama robustum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens or as a bedding plant.
