Draba turkestanica var. leiocarpa

Draba turkestanica var. leiocarpa, commonly known as the Turkistan rockcress, is a perennial flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing habit and basal rosette of leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers in early spring, typically arranged in a single raceme. It thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often found in alpine and mountainous regions across its native range.
