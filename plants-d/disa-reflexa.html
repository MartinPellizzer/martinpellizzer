Disa reflexa

Disa reflexa, commonly known as the spurred orchid, is a species of orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its unique spurred flowers, which are a distinctive botanical feature. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, and it produces striking, tubular flowers in shades of pink, red, and white.

Disa reflexa is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
