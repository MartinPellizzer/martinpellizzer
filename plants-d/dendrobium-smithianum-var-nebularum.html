Dendrobium smithianum var. nebularum

Dendrobium smithianum var. nebularum, commonly known as the nebular dendrobium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid tropical environments. The plant features long, cane-like stems that can reach several meters in length. Its flowers are usually yellow or green with intricate patterns resembling nebulae, giving it its common name.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
