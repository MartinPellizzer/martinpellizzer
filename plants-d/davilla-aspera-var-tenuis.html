Davilla aspera var. tenuis

Davilla aspera var. tenuis, commonly known as the smooth davilla, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features small, elliptical leaves that are smooth and glossy on both sides. Its flowers are white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters, and attract various pollinators.

The fruit is a small, round capsule that splits open to release seeds.
