Desmodium bridgesii

Desmodium bridgesii, commonly known as the African velvet bean, is a leguminous plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces cylindrical pods containing several elliptical seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in disturbed soils.

Desmodium bridgesii is valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it useful in agricultural settings.
