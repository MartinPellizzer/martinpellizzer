Daphne paniculata

Daphne paniculata, commonly known as the clustered daphne, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, showy flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant produces clusters of white or pinkish-purplish flowers that are highly ornamental. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

Daphne paniculata is typically found in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or streamside habitats.
