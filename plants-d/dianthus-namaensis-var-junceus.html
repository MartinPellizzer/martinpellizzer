Dianthus namaensis var. junceus

Dianthus namaensis var. junceus, commonly known as the Cape dianthus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit and vibrant pink or red flowers. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and drought tolerance.
