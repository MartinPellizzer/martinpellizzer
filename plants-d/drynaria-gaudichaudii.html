Drynaria gaudichaudii

Drynaria gaudichaudii, commonly known as the golden tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk covered in a fibrous, pale brown rind. The plant produces large, shield-shaped fronds that are typically light green in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Drynaria gaudichaudii is valued for its ornamental appearance and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
