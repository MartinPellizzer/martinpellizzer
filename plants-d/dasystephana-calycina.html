Dasystephana calycina

Dasystephana calycina, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a tropical shrub native to regions of Southeast Asia, characterized by its lance-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.

Dasystephana calycina is often found in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental foliage.
