Dieffenbachia seguine var. viridis

Dieffenbachia seguine var. viridis, commonly known as the Green Dieffenbachia, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, broad, heart-shaped leaves that are predominantly green with subtle variegation. The plant is a member of the Araceae family, which includes other popular houseplants like peace lilies. Its foliage is often adorned with white or yellow markings, adding to its ornamental appeal.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to maintain its lush, vibrant appearance.
