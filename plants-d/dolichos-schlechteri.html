Dolichos schlechteri

Dolichos schlechteri, commonly known as the Schlechter's dolichos, is a climbing legume native to parts of Africa.

It features trifoliate leaves with small, elliptical leaflets and produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in clusters. The plant bears slender, elongated pods that contain several seeds. It is often found in grasslands and disturbed soils, thriving in warm, dry climates.

Its botanical characteristics make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
