Davallia solida var. arctotheca

Davallia solida var. arctotheca, commonly known as the Arctic fern, is a variant of the common Davallia fern.

It is characterized by its delicate, leathery fronds that are typically gray-green in color. This plant is native to regions of the Arctic and subarctic, where it thrives in cool, moist environments. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for shaded garden beds or indoor cultivation.

The plant's unique appearance and hardy nature make it a desirable addition to both natural and cultivated landscapes.
