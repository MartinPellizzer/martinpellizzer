Dendrobium nidificum

Dendrobium nidificum, commonly known as the bird's nest orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems that often bear clusters of dark green, strap-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Dendrobium nidificum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
