Dioscorea spiculiflora

Dioscorea spiculiflora, commonly known as the spiny-flowered yam, is a member of the Dioscoreaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a climbing herbaceous perennial that produces large, heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture. The plant bears yellowish-green flowers that are typically arranged in clusters, and its tuberous roots are often used in traditional medicine.

Dioscorea spiculiflora is characterized by its spiny bracts and is valued for its medicinal properties in various indigenous cultures.
