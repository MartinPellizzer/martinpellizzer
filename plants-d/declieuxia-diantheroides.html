Declieuxia diantheroides

Declieuxia diantheroides, commonly known as the golden star grass, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a distinctive yellowish or whitish stripe running down the central vein. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences of pale yellow to white flowers, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Declieuxia diantheroides is also valued for its ability to tolerate drought conditions once established.
