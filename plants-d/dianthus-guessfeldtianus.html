Dianthus guessfeldtianus

Dianthus guessfeldtianus, commonly known as the Cape dianthus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink or red flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun exposure. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

Dianthus guessfeldtianus is relatively drought-tolerant once established, making it a hardy choice for coastal or Mediterranean climates.
