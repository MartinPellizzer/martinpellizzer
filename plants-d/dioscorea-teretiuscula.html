Dioscorea teretiuscula

Dioscorea teretiuscula, commonly known as the slender yam, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the Dioscoreaceae family and is characterized by its tuberous roots, which are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a rough texture, while its stems are hairy and twining.

The tubers of D. teretiuscula are valued for their nutritional content and have been used in various cultural practices.
