Dendrochilum pallide-flavens var. brevilabratum

Dendrochilum pallide-flavens var. brevilabratum, commonly known as the pale-yellow orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The flowers are typically pale yellow to orange, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color. This variety is noted for its compact growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

It prefers warm, humid environments and well-draining soil to thrive.
