Delphinium caeruleum var. tenuicaule

Delphinium caeruleum var. tenuicaule, commonly known as the slender blue delphinium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features tall, slender stems that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height, adorned with clusters of blue to violet flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its striking floral display makes it a valuable addition to both garden landscapes and botanical collections.
