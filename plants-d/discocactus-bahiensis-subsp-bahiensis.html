Discocactus bahiensis subsp. bahiensis

Discocactus bahiensis subsp.

bahiensis, commonly known as the Bahia discocactus, is a succulent plant native to Brazil. It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its cylindrical stems and spiny areoles.

The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
