Dichorisandra nana

Dichorisandra nana, commonly known as the dwarf starflower, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are oval and slightly pointed. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact size and prolonged flowering period.
