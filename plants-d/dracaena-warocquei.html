Dracaena warocquei

Dracaena warocquei, commonly known as the Warocquei dracaena, is a striking evergreen shrub native to Madagascar.

It features tall, slender stems with dark green, strap-like leaves that have a glossy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements contribute to its popularity among plant enthusiasts.
