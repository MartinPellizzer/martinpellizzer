Deutzia discolor var. albida

Deutzia discolor var. albida, commonly known as the white deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, arching stems and heart-shaped leaves that turn reddish in the fall. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value comes from its showy flowers and graceful growth habit.
