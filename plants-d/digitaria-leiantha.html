Digitaria leiantha

Digitaria leiantha, commonly known as silverhair grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-haired appearance, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its inflorescence consists of a loose cluster of spikelets, and it produces small, wind-dispersed seeds.

Digitaria leiantha is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and support local wildlife.
