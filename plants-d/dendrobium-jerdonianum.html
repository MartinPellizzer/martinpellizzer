Dendrobium jerdonianum

Dendrobium jerdonianum, commonly known as the Jerdon's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It features a sympodial growth habit with cane-like stems that often branch and produce numerous leaves. The plant produces inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-drained soil.

Dendrobium jerdonianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
