Desmodium dolabriforme

Desmodium dolabriforme, commonly known as the sicklepod or sicklebean, is a leguminous plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive sickle-shaped pods, which give it its common name. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in disturbed or open habitats. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three oval-shaped leaflets, and it produces small, yellowish flowers.

Desmodium dolabriforme is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it a useful plant in agricultural settings.
