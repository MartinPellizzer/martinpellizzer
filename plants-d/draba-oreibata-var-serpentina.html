Draba oreibata var. serpentina

Draba oreibata var. serpentina, commonly known as the serpentine draba, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of 5 to 15 centimeters. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, and it has a basal rosette of leaves. It is adapted to grow in nutrient-poor, rocky soils, often found in alpine or subalpine regions.

This variety is notable for its ecological significance in its native habitats, contributing to biodiversity in mountainous environments.
