Dracaena elliptica

Dracaena elliptica, commonly known as the dragon tree, is a striking evergreen plant native to Madagascar.

It features tall, slender stems with broad, dark green, elliptical leaves that give it a dramatic appearance. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor landscaping.

Dracaena elliptica is also valued for its air-purifying qualities and can be propagated through stem cuttings.
