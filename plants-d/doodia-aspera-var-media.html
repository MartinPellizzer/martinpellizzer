Doodia aspera var. media

Doodia aspera var. media, commonly known as the broad-leaved houttuynia, is a variant of the houttuynia plant native to Asia.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white or yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or wetlands. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
