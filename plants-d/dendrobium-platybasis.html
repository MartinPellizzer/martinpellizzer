Dendrobium platybasis

Dendrobium platybasis, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. The plant is epiphytic, often growing on trees or rocks in humid environments. It thrives in warm, tropical climates with high humidity and indirect sunlight.

Dendrobium platybasis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
