Duperrea scabrida

Duperrea scabrida, commonly known as the rough duperrea, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its woody stems and small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a rough, textured surface. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or white in color. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
