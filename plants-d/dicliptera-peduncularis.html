Dicliptera peduncularis

Dicliptera peduncularis, commonly known as the slender panicgrass, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, typical of the Poaceae family. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in open areas or along waterways.

Dicliptera peduncularis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
