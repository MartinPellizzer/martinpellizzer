Dischidiopsis philippinensis

Dischidiopsis philippinensis, commonly known as the Philippine arrowhead vine, is a climbing plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped, arrowhead-like leaves. The plant produces small, white to greenish flowers that are typically clustered in spadices. Its aerial roots allow it to climb and attach to supporting structures.

It is often found in humid, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas.
