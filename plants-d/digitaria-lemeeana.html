Digitaria lemeeana

Digitaria lemeeana, commonly known as flatgrass, is a species of grass native to the tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is a perennial plant that typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields. The plant has narrow, flat blades and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is adapted to thrive in poor soil conditions and is often found in dry, sunny environments.

Its botanical features make it a common weed in agricultural settings.
