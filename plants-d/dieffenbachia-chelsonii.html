Dieffenbachia chelsonii

Dieffenbachia chelsonii, commonly known as the Snowflake Dieffenbachia, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that feature white and green patterns. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions. Its foliage is often used as a decorative element in indoor gardening.

It requires moderate watering and well-draining soil to maintain optimal health.
