Dioscorea camerunensis

Dioscorea camerunensis, commonly known as Cameroon yam, is a tuberous plant native to Central Africa.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its climbing habit and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces edible tubers that are an important food source in local communities. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with well-drained soil.

Its botanical significance lies in its role in traditional agriculture and potential for cultivation in sustainable farming systems.
