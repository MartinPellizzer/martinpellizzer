Dracula gorgonella

Dracula gorgonella, commonly known as the vampire orchid, is a rare and striking species native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, Dracula gorgonella is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts.
