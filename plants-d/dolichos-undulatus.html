Dolichos undulatus

Dolichos undulatus, commonly known as the wavy cowpea, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, winding stems and heart-shaped leaves with wavy margins. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers followed by elongated, curved pods containing edible seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun for optimal growth.

Dolichos undulatus is valued for its nutritional seeds and is often cultivated in warm climates.
