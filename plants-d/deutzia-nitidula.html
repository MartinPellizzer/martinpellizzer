Deutzia nitidula

Deutzia nitidula, commonly known as the smooth deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features clusters of white to pink flowers that bloom in early spring, often preceding the foliage. The plant has slender, arching stems and lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and glossy. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Deutzia nitidula is relatively low maintenance and resistant to pests and diseases.
