Dicoma swazilandica

Dicoma swazilandica, commonly known as Swazi daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and succulent leaves. The plant is adapted to arid environments, making it drought-resistant. It typically grows in rocky soils and is found in regions with moderate to high sunlight exposure.

Dicoma swazilandica is often used in ornamental gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and hardy nature.
