Dendrotrophe philippinensis

Dendrotrophe philippinensis, commonly known as the Philippine tree fern, is a species native to the Philippines.

The plant thrives in humid, tropical environments, often found in lowland forests. Its rhizome system allows it to anchor firmly in soil while absorbing nutrients from decomposing organic matter.

Dendrotrophe philippinensis plays a significant role in the ecological balance of its habitat by providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
