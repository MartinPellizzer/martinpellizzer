Deianira damazioi

Deianira damazioi, commonly known as the Damazio's flame vine, is a strikingly colorful flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange and red tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a glossy surface, and its stems are woody and often climbing in growth habit. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a decorative plant in tropical landscapes.
