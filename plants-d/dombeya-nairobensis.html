Dombeya nairobensis

Dombeya nairobensis, commonly known as the Nairobi dombeya, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 4 meters in height. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink, blooming in the spring. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a rough texture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland areas.
