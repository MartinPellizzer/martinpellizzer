Dalechampia tiliifolia var. heterophylla

Dalechampia tiliifolia var. heterophylla, commonly known as the variable leaf dalechampia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, palmate leaves that vary in shape and size across different parts of the plant. The leaves are typically large, with a glossy green surface and may have a slightly hairy texture. This variant is known for its unique leaf morphology, which contributes to its ornamental value in horticulture.

It prefers warm, humid climates and is often grown as a decorative plant in tropical gardens.
