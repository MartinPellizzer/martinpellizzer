Dendrobium cucumerinum

Dendrobium cucumerinum, commonly known as the cucumber orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a tall, cylindrical pseudobulb that stores water and nutrients, supporting its growth in shaded forest environments. The plant produces long, pendulous inflorescences with fragrant, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Dendrobium cucumerinum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
