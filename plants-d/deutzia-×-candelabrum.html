Deutzia × candelabrum

Deutzia × candelabrum, commonly known as the candelabra deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its multi-branched, candle-like stems that support clusters of white flowers in early summer. The plant produces small, ornamental berries that turn red in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its compact size and showy blooms make it an excellent addition to landscape designs.
