Danais nummulariifolia

Danais nummulariifolia, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features small, round, silver-gray leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps it store water and reduce transpiration. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking contrast to its foliage. It is well adapted to arid environments and thrives in full sun with well-draining soil.

Due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
