Dianthus Monspessulanus Var. Sternbergii

Dianthus monspessulanus var. sternbergii is a striking member of the carnation family known for its vibrant pink and white petals. Native to the Iberian Peninsula, this plant thrives in rocky and sunny environments. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking both beauty and hardiness. The variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to bloom throughout the growing season. With its delicate yet resilient nature, it stands out as a remarkable addition to any botanical collection.
