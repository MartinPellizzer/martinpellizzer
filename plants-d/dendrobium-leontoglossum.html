Dendrobium leontoglossum

Dendrobium leontoglossum, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a tall, upright stem covered with numerous, fleshy, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellow-orange flowers with a distinctive golden throat, blooming during the dry season. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in greenhouse settings.
