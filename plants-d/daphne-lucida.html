Daphne lucida

Daphne lucida, commonly known as the white daphne, is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features fragrant, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has dark green, lance-shaped leaves that provide a striking contrast to its showy blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Daphne lucida is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in gardens for its winter interest.
