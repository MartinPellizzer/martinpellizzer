Dillenia secunda

Dillenia secunda, commonly known as the giant dillenia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in diameter. The plant produces showy, yellowish-white flowers with five petals, which are followed by distinctive, woody, spiky fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Dillenia secunda is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses in local communities.
