Dendrobium womersleyi

Dendrobium womersleyi, commonly known as the Womersley's orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its dense, clustered growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, utilizing its aerial roots to absorb moisture and nutrients from the environment. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, adding ornamental value to its natural habitat.

Dendrobium womersleyi is noted for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of conditions, making it a popular choice for cultivation.
