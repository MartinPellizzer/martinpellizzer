Dracaena sanderiana

Dracaena sanderiana, commonly known as the snake plant, is a popular indoor plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with distinctive yellow edges. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it ideal for beginners. Dracaena sanderiana is also noted for its air-purifying properties, as it can remove toxins from the air.

Its compact size and elegant appearance make it a favored choice for both residential and office environments.
