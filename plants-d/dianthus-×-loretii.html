Dianthus × loretii

Dianthus × loretii, commonly known as the Loretta's campion, is a hybrid flowering plant belonging to the Carnation family (Caryophyllaceae).

It is characterized by its showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a distinctive white or pink center. The plant typically grows to a height of 10–30 cm and features narrow, grass-like leaves. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight.

Dianthus × loretii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
