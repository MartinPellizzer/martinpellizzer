Dodecatheon flexuosum

Dodecatheon flexuosum, commonly known as the flexible globemallow, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its delicate, nodding flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a distinctive yellowish or greenish hue at the base. The plant typically grows in moist woodland habitats and has hairy, lance-shaped leaves. It produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, wind-dispersed seeds.

Dodecatheon flexuosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
