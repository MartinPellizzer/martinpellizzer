Deckera

Deckera, commonly known as the African snowbush, is a genus of flowering plants native to southern Africa.

The leaves are typically narrow and leathery, often arranged in whorls around the stems. Deckera species produce small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by small, round fruits.
