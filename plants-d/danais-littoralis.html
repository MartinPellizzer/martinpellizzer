Danais littoralis

Danais littoralis, commonly known as the coastal beauty, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in dune environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, adapted to withstand salty air and strong winds.

Danais littoralis plays an important role in stabilizing coastal ecosystems and providing habitat for small insects.
