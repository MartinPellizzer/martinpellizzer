Duvaua dependens var. obovata

Duvaua dependens var. obovata, commonly known as the broad-ovate duvaua, is a unique plant species characterized by its distinctive leaf morphology.

The leaves are typically broad and ovate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface depending on the variety. It is native to specific regions in South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often clustered together.
