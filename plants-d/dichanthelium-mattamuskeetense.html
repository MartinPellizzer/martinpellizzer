Dichanthelium mattamuskeetense

Dichanthelium mattamuskeetense, commonly known as Mattamuskeet grass, is a perennial grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in wetland and marshy environments, often found in coastal areas.

Its botanical features make it an important component of wetland ecosystems.
