Draba sakuraii var. ondakensis

Draba sakuraii var. ondakensis, commonly known as the Sakurai's winter cress, is a small flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its cruciform flowers and capsule fruits. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. It has basal leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and its flowers are usually white or pale yellow.

This variant is distinguished by its compact growth habit and specific geographic distribution within its native range.
