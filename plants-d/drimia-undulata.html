Drimia undulata

Drimia undulata, commonly known as the undulating dragon's tongue, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features long, narrow, undulating leaves that give it its common name, and it produces striking blue or purple flowers in summer. The plant is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.

Drimia undulata is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
