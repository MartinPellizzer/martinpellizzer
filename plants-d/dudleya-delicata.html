Dudleya delicata

Dudleya delicata, commonly known as the silver leaf Dudleya, is a succulent plant native to coastal regions of California and Baja California.

It features rosettes of fleshy, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces upright flowering stalks that bear clusters of small, white or pink flowers during the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements, Dudleya delicata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
