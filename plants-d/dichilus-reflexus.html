Dichilus reflexus

Dichilus reflexus, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Dichilus reflexus thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and rocky habitats.
