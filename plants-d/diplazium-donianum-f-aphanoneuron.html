Diplazium donianum f. aphanoneuron

Diplazium donianum f. aphanoneuron, commonly known as the slender serrated fern, is a variant of the Diplazium genus native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated fronds with finely serrated margins. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest undergrowth. Its sporangia are clustered in sori along the margins of the fronds.

This fern is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
