Dioscorea bahamensis

Dioscorea bahamensis, commonly known as the Bahamian yam, is a species of tuberous vine native to the Caribbean islands.

It belongs to the genus Dioscorea, which includes numerous species of yams and wild yams. The plant produces large, edible tubers that are often used in local cuisines. Its leaves are heart-shaped and its flowers are typically yellow or white.

The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
