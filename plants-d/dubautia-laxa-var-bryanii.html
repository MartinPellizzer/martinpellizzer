Dubautia laxa var. bryanii

Dubautia laxa var. bryanii, commonly known as Bryan's shrub.

It is a flowering plant native to Hawaii, specifically found on the island of Maui. This variety is characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth habit with numerous branches and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, typical of many Hawaiian endemic species.
