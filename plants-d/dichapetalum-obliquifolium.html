Dichapetalum obliquifolium

Dichapetalum obliquifolium, commonly known as the zigzag flower, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, zigzag-shaped leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the dry season. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
