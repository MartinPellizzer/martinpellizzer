Dunbaria villosa var. peduncularis

Dunbaria villosa var. peduncularis, commonly known as the hairy dunbaria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It produces small, wind-pollinated flowers that attract various pollinators, including bees and butterflies.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.
