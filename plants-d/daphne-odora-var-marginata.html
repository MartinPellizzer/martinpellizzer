Daphne odora var. marginata

Daphne odora var. marginata, commonly known as the pink-flowered daphne, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, fragrant, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves and a compact, bushy growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Due to its toxicity, it should be handled with care.
