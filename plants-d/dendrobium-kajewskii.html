Dendrobium kajewskii

Dendrobium kajewskii, commonly known as the Kajewskii orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome that produces multiple shoots. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with intricate patterns. Its leaves are usually broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native habitat.

Dendrobium kajewskii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
