Dinochloa dielsiana

Dinochloa dielsiana, commonly known as the Diels' grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, wiry stems. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a smooth surface and a bluish-green color.

Dinochloa dielsiana produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikes, typical of many grass species.
