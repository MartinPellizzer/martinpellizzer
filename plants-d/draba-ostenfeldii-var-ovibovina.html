Draba ostenfeldii var. ovibovina

Draba ostenfeldii var. ovibovina, commonly known as the alpine buttercup, is a low-growing flowering plant native to the high mountain regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family and is characterized by its small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has a compact rosette of basal leaves and produces slender, branched stems. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to cold, alpine environments.
