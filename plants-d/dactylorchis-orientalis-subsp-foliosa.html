Dactylorchis orientalis subsp. foliosa

Dactylorchis orientalis subsp.

foliosa, commonly known as the leafy dactylorchis, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and grasslands.

The plant features a slender rhizome with linear, lanceolate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually in a spike, with a distinctive orchid-like structure.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
