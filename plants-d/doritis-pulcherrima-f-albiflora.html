Doritis pulcherrima f. albiflora

Doritis pulcherrima f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered kangaroo paw, is a unique hybrid of the kangaroo paw plant.

It is characterized by its striking, white, spurred flowers that resemble the pouch of a kangaroo. The plant is native to Australia and is typically found in coastal regions. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and distinctive foliage.
