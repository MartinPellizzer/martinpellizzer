Deutzia oaxacana

Deutzia oaxacana, commonly known as the Oaxaca deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to central Mexico.

It features clusters of white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring, adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant has slender, arching branches and elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Deutzia oaxacana is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
