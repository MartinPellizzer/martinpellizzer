Dyckia pierre-braunii

Dyckia pierre-braunii, commonly known as the Pierre-Braun Dyckia, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It features rosettes of stiff, sword-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of white, woolly hair. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing small, showy pink or red flowers. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-draining soil.

Dyckia pierre-braunii is drought-tolerant and well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant.
