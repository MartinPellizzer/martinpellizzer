Dioscorea tamoidea subsp. lindenii

Dioscorea tamoidea subsp.

lindenii, commonly known as the Lindeni yam, is a member of the Dioscoreaceae family and is native to tropical Africa. It is a climbing vine characterized by its hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves.

The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by cylindrical, fleshy fruits. Its tuberous roots are edible and have been used in traditional medicine for their potential health benefits.

This subspecies is valued both for its nutritional content and its role in local ecosystems.
