Doryopteris madagascariensis

Doryopteris madagascariensis, commonly known as the Madagascar fern, is a species native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, bi-lobed fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its leaves are divided into two symmetrical parts, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
