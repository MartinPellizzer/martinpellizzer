Dolomiaea crispoundulata

Dolomiaea crispoundulata, commonly known as the curly leaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, hairy leaves that are often crinkled or curled, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its unique foliage and modest floral display, it is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal.
