Dekinia coccinea

Dekinia coccinea, commonly known as the red-flowered dekinia, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in South America.

It is characterized by its striking red petals and vibrant orange stamens, which make it visually distinctive. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are oval-shaped with a smooth texture and a deep green color.

Dekinia coccinea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
