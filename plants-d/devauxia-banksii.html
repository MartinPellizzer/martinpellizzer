Devauxia banksii

Devauxia banksii, commonly known as the Banks' devauxia, is a climbing shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and woodland habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
