Dianthus friwaldskyanus

Dianthus friwaldskyanus, commonly known as the Friwaldsky's campion, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family, which includes many species of flowering plants known for their showy blooms. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in well-drained soils in full sun.

Dianthus friwaldskyanus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
