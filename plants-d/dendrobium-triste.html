Dendrobium triste

Dendrobium triste, commonly known as the sad orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome that produces multiple pseudobulbs. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-draining soil.

Due to its delicate appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
