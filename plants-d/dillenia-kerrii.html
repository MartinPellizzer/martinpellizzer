Dillenia kerrii

Dillenia kerrii, commonly known as the African dillenia, is a striking tropical plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant produces showy, yellowish-white flowers with a distinctive central boss, which are followed by round, fleshy fruits. The fruits are edible and have a mild, nutty flavor, often used in local cuisines.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories or along riverbanks.
