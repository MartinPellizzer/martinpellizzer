Daenikera

Daenikera, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers with intricate patterns that resemble a sunburst. The plant typically grows in tropical rainforests, thriving in humid and shaded environments. Daenikera has long, slender leaves that are often covered in a fine, silky texture.

It is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its cultural significance in traditional medicine.
