Dillenia micrantha

Dillenia micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered dillenia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and large, heart-shaped leaves that are often deeply lobed. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers with prominent stamens, which are followed by distinctive, woody, spiny fruits. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine, and the plant is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Dillenia micrantha thrives in moist, shaded environments and is commonly found in lowland forests.
