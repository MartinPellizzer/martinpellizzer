Diplophractum subobovatum

Diplophractum subobovatum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky or sandy soils.
