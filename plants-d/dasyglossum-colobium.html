Dasyglossum colobium

Dasyglossum colobium, commonly known as the winged bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive winged stems and showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Dasyglossum colobium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
