Dieffenbachia brittonii

Dieffenbachia brittonii, commonly known as the "Britton's Dieffenbachia," is a tropical plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its large, striking leaves that are usually variegated with white and green patterns. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other popular houseplants like peace lilies. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Dieffenbachia brittonii is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
