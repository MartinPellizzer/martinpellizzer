Decaschistia crotonifolia

Decaschistia crotonifolia, commonly known as the African croton, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height, characterized by its rough, bark-like stems and opposite, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.
