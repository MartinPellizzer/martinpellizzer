Deplanchea glabra

Deplanchea glabra, commonly known as the smooth deplanchea, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and large, heart-shaped leaves that are usually a deep green. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically white or pale yellow, which bloom during the warm season. It thrives in humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Deplanchea glabra is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
