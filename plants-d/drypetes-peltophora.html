Drypetes peltophora

Drypetes peltophora, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain seeds covered in a sticky, resinous coating.
