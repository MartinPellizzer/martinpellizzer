Deutzia cyanocalyx

Deutzia cyanocalyx, commonly known as the blueflower deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features clusters of fragrant, light blue to white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, arching branches and small, ovate leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Deutzia cyanocalyx is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
