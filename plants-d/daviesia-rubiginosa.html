Daviesia rubiginosa

Daviesia rubiginosa, commonly known as the red-stemmed daviesia, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its reddish-brown stems and clusters of pink to red flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is a member of the Proteaceae family and is adapted to dry, nutrient-poor environments.

Daviesia rubiginosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in native habitats.
