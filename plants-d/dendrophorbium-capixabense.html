Dendrophorbium capixabense

Dendrophorbium capixabense, commonly known as the Capixaba orchid, is a rare and endemic orchid species native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its compact, herbaceous growth habit and produces striking, fragrant flowers with a unique coloration. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments within the Atlantic Forest biome. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white, with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered a threatened species requiring conservation efforts.
