Dendrobium stockeri

Dendrobium stockeri, commonly known as the mountain orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with multiple pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and yellow. Its leaves are typically linear and arranged in two rows along the stems.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
