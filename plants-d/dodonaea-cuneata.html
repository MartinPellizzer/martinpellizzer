Dodonaea cuneata

Dodonaea cuneata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf hopseed, is a shrub native to South America.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of yellowish-green flowers, followed by small, round, woody fruits. It thrives in dry, sunny conditions and is adapted to arid environments.

Dodonaea cuneata is often used in landscaping for its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
