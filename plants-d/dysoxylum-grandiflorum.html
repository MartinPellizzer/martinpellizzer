Dysoxylum grandiflorum

Dysoxylum grandiflorum, commonly known as the large-flowered dysoxylum, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and large, palmately lobed leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in panicles. Its fruit is a woody capsule containing several seeds.

Dysoxylum grandiflorum is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental and potential medicinal properties.
