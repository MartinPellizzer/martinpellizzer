Diospyros diversilimba

Diospyros diversilimba, commonly known as the African persimmon, is a small to medium-sized tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Ebenaceae family, which includes ebony and persimmon trees. The plant is characterized by its elliptical, glossy leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers. Its fruit is a fleshy, orange-red berry that contains numerous small seeds.

The tree is valued for its hard wood and is often found in moist, well-drained soils in forested areas.
