Deprea Subtriflora

Deprea subtriflora is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of central Asia. It is known for its small, white or pale pink blooms that appear in late spring. This species thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas. Due to habitat loss and climate change, Deprea subtriflora is becoming increasingly endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival for future generations.
