Dichodon ciliolatum

Dichodon ciliolatum, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant features opposite, narrow leaves with fine hairs along the edges, giving it a distinctive texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for container gardening.

Dichodon ciliolatum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
