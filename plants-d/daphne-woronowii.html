Daphne woronowii

Daphne woronowii, commonly known as the Chinese daphne, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in shades of pink, white, and red. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Daphne woronowii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and winter interest.
