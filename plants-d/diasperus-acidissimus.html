Diasperus acidissimus

Diasperus acidissimus, commonly known as the sour fig, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, sour-tasting fruits that are often consumed by local wildlife. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showing adaptability to harsh conditions.

The species is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
