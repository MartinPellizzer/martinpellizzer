Dieffenbachia vittata

Dieffenbachia vittata, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a tropical perennial shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that feature broad, light green to white stripes against a deep green background. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other well-known houseplants like peace lilies and caladiums. Its foliage is typically lance-shaped and can grow up to 30 inches in length, making it a visually appealing indoor plant.

Dieffenbachia vittata thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to maintain its vibrant appearance.
