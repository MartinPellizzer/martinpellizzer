Dryopteris pulchra

Dryopteris pulchra, commonly known as the golden sword fern, is a deciduous fern native to the eastern United States.

It features lance-shaped, bright green fronds with a distinctive golden hue along the margins. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its rhizomes are thick and fleshy, allowing it to store moisture and nutrients.

Dryopteris pulchra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its lush, elegant appearance.
