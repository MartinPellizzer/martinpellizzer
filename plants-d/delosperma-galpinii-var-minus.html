Delosperma galpinii var. minus

Delosperma galpinii var. minus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ice plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are narrow and arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces vibrant pink to purple flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring and summer. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements in xeriscaping.
