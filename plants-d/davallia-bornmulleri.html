Davallia bornmulleri

Davallia bornmulleri, commonly known as the bamboo fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, arching fronds that resemble bamboo in appearance. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with creeping underground stems that produce new shoots. Its leaves are bipinnate, with fine, delicate pinnae that give it a feathery appearance.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant.
