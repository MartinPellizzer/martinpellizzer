Diplostephium schultzii var. orientale

Diplostephium schultzii var. orientale, commonly known as the oriental goldenbush, is a shrub native to southern China and Southeast Asia.

It features dense, compact growth with small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has aromatic, leathery leaves that are often used in traditional herbal medicine. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a landscape accent.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant color and unique foliage structure.
