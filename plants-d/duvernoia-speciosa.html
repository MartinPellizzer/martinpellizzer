Duvernoia speciosa

Duvernoia speciosa, commonly known as the golden rain tree, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its striking golden-yellow flowers that appear in clusters during the spring. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets that turn reddish in the fall. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant.

The tree is also valued for its rapid growth and ability to tolerate urban environments.
