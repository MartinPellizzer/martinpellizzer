Dichorisandra begoniifolia

Dichorisandra begoniifolia, commonly known as the begonia-leaved dichorisandra, is a striking plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The leaves are heart-shaped and resemble those of begonias, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full sun for optimal growth.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
