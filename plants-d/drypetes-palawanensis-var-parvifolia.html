Drypetes palawanensis var. parvifolia

Drypetes palawanensis var. parvifolia, commonly known as the small-fruited drypetes, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptic to ovate in shape.
