Dracaena lutescens var. striata

Dracaena lutescens var. striata, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a striking ornamental plant characterized by its variegated, striped leaves.

The leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a bright yellow or white midrib contrasting against deep green foliage. It is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires moderate watering.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and decorative purposes.
