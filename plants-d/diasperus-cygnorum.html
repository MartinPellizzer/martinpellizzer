Diasperus cygnorum

Diasperus cygnorum, commonly known as the swan's feather plant, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking, feathery inflorescence. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in dense, spherical clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it an attractive addition to xeriscapes.
