Dichapetalum kumasiense

It belongs to the family Dilleniaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and large, simple leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by woody, elongated fruits containing several seeds.
