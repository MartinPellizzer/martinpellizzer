Dendrobium odoardi

Dendrobium odoardi, commonly known as the Odoardi orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Dendrobium genus, which is part of the Orchidaceae family, known for its diverse and often showy flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, relying on atmospheric moisture rather than soil for nutrients. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white, with a slight fragrance, and bloom during the rainy season.

Dendrobium odoardi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
