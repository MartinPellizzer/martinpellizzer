Detarium heudelotianum

Detarium heudelotianum, commonly known as the African tamarind tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The tree produces pods containing edible seeds that are rich in nutrients. Its bark and leaves are used in traditional medicine for their various therapeutic properties.

The plant also plays an important role in soil conservation and agroforestry systems.
