Dendrobium magnilabre

Dendrobium magnilabre, commonly known as the giant labellum orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, cane-like stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces large, showy flowers with a distinctive, elongated labellum that is often brightly colored. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

Due to its striking appearance, it is a popular choice for ornamental cultivation.
