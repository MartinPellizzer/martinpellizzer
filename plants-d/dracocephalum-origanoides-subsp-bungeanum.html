Dracocephalum origanoides subsp. bungeanum

Dracocephalum origanoides subsp.

bungeanum, commonly known as Bunge's wood betony, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves.

The plant produces small, two-lipped flowers that are typically violet or blue in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry grasslands or rocky areas.

Its aromatic leaves and flowers make it a valuable plant in traditional medicine and horticulture.
