Dorstenia erecta var. hispida

Dorstenia erecta var. hispida, commonly known as the hairy false arum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

The plant features palmate leaves with a rough, hairy texture, giving it its common name. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color.
