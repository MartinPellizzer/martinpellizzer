Dolichos tenuis

Dolichos tenuis, commonly known as slender cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, trailing stems and small, pale green trifoliate leaves. The flowers are typically white or pale yellow, and the plant produces elongated pods containing edible seeds. It is often grown as a cover crop or for its nutritional value.

Dolichos tenuis is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, benefiting subsequent crops.
