Dracula chiroptera

Dracula chiroptera, commonly known as the vampire orchid, is a rare and striking orchid species native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its dark, velvety petals that resemble the wings of a bat, giving it its evocative common name. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surfaces of trees without deriving nutrients from them. Its flowers are typically a deep red or purple, with intricate patterns that mimic the appearance of blood.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, Dracula chiroptera is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
