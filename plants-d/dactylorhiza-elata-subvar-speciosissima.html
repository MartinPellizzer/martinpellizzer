Dactylorhiza elata subvar. speciosissima

Dactylorhiza elata subvar. speciosissima, commonly known as the showy yellow orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of bright yellow flowers with a distinctive lip. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its ornamental value.

The species is also noted for its specific pollination mechanisms involving certain insect species.
