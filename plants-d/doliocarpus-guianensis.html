Doliocarpus guianensis

Doliocarpus guianensis, commonly known as the Guianan doliocarpus, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that typically grows in humid, lowland rainforests. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to elliptical in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in axillary or terminal inflorescences.

The fruit is a globose capsule that splits open to release its seeds.
