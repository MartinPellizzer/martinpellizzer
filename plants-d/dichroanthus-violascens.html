Dichroanthus violascens

Dichroanthus violascens, commonly known as the purple-flowered dichroanthus, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It features striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has alternate, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Its unique flower coloration and habitat preferences make it a notable species in wetland ecosystems.
