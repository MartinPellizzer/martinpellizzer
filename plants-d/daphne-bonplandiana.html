Daphne bonplandiana

Daphne bonplandiana, commonly known as the Chilean daphne, is a shrub native to the Andes mountains of Chile and Argentina.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and are borne on short pedicels. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves that are opposite and oval in shape. It produces small, oval berries that are yellow when mature.

Daphne bonplandiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its winter blooms.
