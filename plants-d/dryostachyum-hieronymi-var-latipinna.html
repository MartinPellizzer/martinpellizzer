Dryostachyum hieronymi var. latipinna

Dryostachyum hieronymi var. latipinna, commonly known as the broad-leafed grass.

It is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions of South America. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves with a bluish-gray sheen, and it produces dense, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Its botanical structure makes it suitable for ornamental use in gardens and landscapes.
