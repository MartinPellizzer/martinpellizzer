Diospyros finetii

Diospyros finetii, commonly known as the African persimmon, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Ebenaceae family, which includes other persimmon and ebony species. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its small, yellowish-green flowers are followed by small, round fruits that mature to a dark purple or black color.

Diospyros finetii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some African cultures.
