Dendrobium loesenerianum

Dendrobium loesenerianum, commonly known as the Loesener's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a branched stem system and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant typically blooms during the summer months and has lance-shaped leaves that are often clustered along the stem. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining potting media for optimal growth.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
