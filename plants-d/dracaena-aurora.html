Dracaena aurora

Dracaena aurora, commonly known as the Aurora Dracaena, is a striking plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features elongated, arching leaves that are a vibrant mix of green and yellow, creating a visually appealing appearance. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. It has a slow growth rate and can be pruned to maintain its shape.

The leaves are also resistant to common pests, contributing to its ease of care.
