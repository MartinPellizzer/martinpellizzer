Diporidium macranthum

Diporidium macranthum, commonly known as the large-flowered diporidium, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slight pubescence.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
