Dehaasia palembanica

Dehaasia palembanica, commonly known as the Palembang orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical rainforests, thriving in humid and shaded environments. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The species is noted for its unique floral structure and is a subject of interest in botanical research.
