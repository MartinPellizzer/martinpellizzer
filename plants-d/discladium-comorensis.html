Discladium comorensis

Discladium comorensis, commonly known as the Comoros daisy, is a flowering plant native to the Comoros Islands in the Indian Ocean.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center. Discladium comorensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens in tropical regions.
