Dracaena spinckii

Dracaena spinckii, commonly known as the Congo Dracaena, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Central and Eastern Africa.

It features long, narrow, dark green leaves that are often variegated with yellow or white streaks. The plant has a slender, upright stem that can grow up to several feet tall. It is well-suited for indoor environments due to its tolerance for low light and moderate watering.

Dracaena spinckii is also valued for its air-purifying qualities and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor conditions.
