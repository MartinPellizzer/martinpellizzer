Dyckia gracilis

Dyckia gracilis, commonly known as the slender dyckia, is a striking bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, upright rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces striking red or orange inflorescences that emerge from the center of the rosette. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Dyckia gracilis is known for its drought tolerance and ability to survive in arid conditions.
