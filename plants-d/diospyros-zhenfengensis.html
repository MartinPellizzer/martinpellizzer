Diospyros zhenfengensis

Diospyros zhenfengensis, commonly known as the Zhenfeng persimmon, is a species native to China.

It belongs to the Ebenaceae family and is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves and yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces small, orange-red fruits that are typically harvested when fully mature. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical studies.
