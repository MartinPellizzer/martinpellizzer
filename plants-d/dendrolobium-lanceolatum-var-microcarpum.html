Dendrolobium lanceolatum var. microcarpum

Dendrolobium lanceolatum var. microcarpum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf burr legume, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It features slender, lance-shaped leaves that are often pubescent, with small, clustered flowers in shades of pink to purple. The plant produces small, woody pods that contain several elliptical seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to semi-arid climates.

Its unique morphology and ecological role make it an important species in its native habitat.
