Dendrobenthamia elegans

Dendrobenthamia elegans, commonly known as the elegant orchid, is a rare and striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. Native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are linear and often arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Due to its rarity and unique beauty, it is highly valued in horticulture and conservation efforts.
