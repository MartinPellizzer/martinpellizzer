Digitaria livida

Digitaria livida, commonly known as slender digit grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas or along roadsides.

Digitaria livida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in some traditional practices.
