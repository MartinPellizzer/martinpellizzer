Delphinium lagarolobum

Delphinium lagarolobum, commonly known as the Sierra delphinium, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

It features tall, slender stems that bear clusters of blue to purple flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Delphinium lagarolobum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance as a food source for native pollinators.
