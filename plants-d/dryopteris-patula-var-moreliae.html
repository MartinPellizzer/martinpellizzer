Dryopteris patula var. moreliae

Dryopteris patula var. moreliae, commonly known as the Mexican shield fern, is a striking evergreen fern native to Mexico.

It features elegant, deeply lobed fronds that are typically a vibrant green, with a distinctive shield-like shape. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and prefers well-draining, acidic soil. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its lush foliage.

This variety is notable for its hardiness and ability to adapt to a range of growing conditions.
