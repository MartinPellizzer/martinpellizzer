Dasanthera fruticosa

Dasanthera fruticosa, commonly known as the California poppy, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a herbaceous annual with showy, bright orange to red flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant has slender, branched stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in open fields and along roadsides.

Its botanical features make it a characteristic wildflower of California's coastal and inland regions.
