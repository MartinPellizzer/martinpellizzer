Deyeuxia drummondii

Deyeuxia drummondii, commonly known as Drummond's flat grass, is a perennial grass species native to North America.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are typically green to reddish in color. It thrives in wetland and moist soil environments, often found in marshes and along riverbanks.

Deyeuxia drummondii plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
