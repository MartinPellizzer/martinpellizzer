Dendrobium keytsianum

Dendrobium keytsianum, commonly known as the Kaitia orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that can grow up to 2 meters in height, often branching into multiple shoots. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with red or purple markings, which bloom during the wet season. Its leaves are long and strap-like, arranged in a rosette pattern around the stem.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
