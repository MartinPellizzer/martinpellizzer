Ditassa lisae

Ditassa lisae, commonly known as the lisae dittassa, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It features small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling a cluster of tiny bells. The plant has slender, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is typically found in humid forests.
