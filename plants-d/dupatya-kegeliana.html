Dupatya kegeliana

Dupatya kegeliana, commonly known as the Philippine orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its intricate floral structure and vibrant colors. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are epiphytic, meaning they grow on other plants without parasitizing them.

Dupatya kegeliana is valued for its ornamental beauty and plays a role in the biodiversity of its native habitat.
