Dendrobium nabawanense

Dendrobium nabawanense, commonly known as the Nabawan orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the lowland rainforests of Borneo.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems and clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in its tropical habitat.

Dendrobium nabawanense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
