Dendrobium muricatum var. munificum

Dendrobium muricatum var. munificum, commonly known as the Munificum orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in tropical rainforests. The plant features long, cane-like pseudobulbs covered in dense, velvety foliage. It produces vibrant, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often in clusters.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated as a decorative plant in horticulture.
