Diplazium splendens

Diplazium splendens, commonly known as the shining wood fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is characterized by its dense, dark green fronds with a glossy appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are fibrous and help it spread through vegetative reproduction.
