Dendrobium exile

Dendrobium exile, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in late spring. The plant prefers moist, shaded environments and thrives in humid climates with well-drained soil. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it is often found growing in forest understories.

Dendrobium exile is valued for its ornamental appeal and is a popular choice for gardeners and orchid enthusiasts.
