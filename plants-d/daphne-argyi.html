Daphne argyi

Daphne argyi, commonly known as the Chinese daphne, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces small, red berries that are toxic to humans and animals. Its leaves are oval-shaped with a slightly serrated edge and a glossy surface.

Daphne argyi is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and hardy nature.
