Dombeya sphaerantha

Dombeya sphaerantha, commonly known as the globe dombeya, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant is characterized by its showy, globe-shaped flower clusters that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

The flowers are typically pink or white, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
