Dischidia subulata subsp. subulata

Dischidia subulata subsp.

subulata, commonly known as the Needleleaf Dischidia, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its small, needle-like leaves that are typically dark green and glossy.

The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, followed by round, fleshy fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in its natural habitat.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
