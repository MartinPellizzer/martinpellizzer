Dupatya triangularis

Dupatya triangularis, commonly known as the triangular leaf orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive triangular-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique morphology and ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
