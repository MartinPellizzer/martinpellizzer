Dactylis polygama var. pilosa

Dactylis polygama var. pilosa, commonly known as hairy goat's rue, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its wiry, hairy stems and trifoliate leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and along roadsides.

Dactylis polygama var. pilosa is known for its hardy nature and ability to tolerate various environmental conditions.
