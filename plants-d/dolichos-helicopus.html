Dolichos helicopus

Dolichos helicopus, commonly known as the spiral bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features distinctive spiral-shaped tendrils that aid in its climbing growth habit. The plant produces small, yellow flowers followed by elongated, spiral-twisted pods containing edible seeds. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three leaflets that are often hairy.

Dolichos helicopus is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional cuisines in some parts of Asia.
