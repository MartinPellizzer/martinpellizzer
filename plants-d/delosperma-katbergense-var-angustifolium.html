Delosperma katbergense var. angustifolium

Delosperma katbergense var. angustifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that store water, typical of xerophytic adaptations. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in a fine layer of white powder, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of pink and purple, usually during the summer months.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
