Dendrobium kratense

Dendrobium kratense, commonly known as the Kratens orchid, is a sympodial epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a robust, branching stem system with fleshy pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange, often with intricate patterns. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged in two rows along the stem.

Dendrobium kratense thrives in humid, shaded environments and is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value.
