Deutzia cinerascens

Deutzia cinerascens, commonly known as the silverleaf deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features slender, arching stems and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The leaves are ovate to elliptical, with a silvery-gray underside that gives the plant its distinctive appearance. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, moist soil.

This ornamental plant is often used in gardens for its elegant foliage and delicate floral display.
