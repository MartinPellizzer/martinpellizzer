Dyschoriste oblongifolia f. glabra

Dyschoriste oblongifolia f. glabra, commonly known as the smoothleaf dyschoriste, is a variant of the Dyschoriste oblongifolia species.

It is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Africa, typically found in moist, shaded environments. The plant features elongated, narrow leaves that are smooth and glabrous, distinguishing it from other forms of the species. Its flowers are small and clustered, often in spikes, and are typically greenish or pale yellow in color.
