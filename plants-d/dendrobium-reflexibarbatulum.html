Dendrobium reflexibarbatulum

Dendrobium reflexibarbatulum, commonly known as the barbatum orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit, with long, slender pseudobulbs. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences bearing numerous fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green. Its leaves are usually elliptical and arranged in two rows along the pseudobulbs.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.
