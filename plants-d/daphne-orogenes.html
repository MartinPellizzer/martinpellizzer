Daphne orogenes

Daphne orogenes, commonly known as the mountain daphne, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form and produces clusters of fragrant, pink or white flowers in spring. The plant has small, glossy leaves and is well adapted to cold, dry conditions. It is often found growing in rocky soils and open grasslands.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a subject of interest in botanical studies and conservation efforts.
