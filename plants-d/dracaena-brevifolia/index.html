Dracaena Brevifolia

Dracaena brevifolia is a unique and striking plant known for its compact size and vibrant foliage. Native to the island of Madagascar, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. This species is prized for its ability to purify indoor air and adapt well to low-light conditions. Its short, thick stems and glossy, dark green leaves make it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Despite its small stature, Dracaena brevifolia offers a bold and elegant presence in any space.
