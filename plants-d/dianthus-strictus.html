Dianthus strictus

Dianthus strictus, commonly known as the upright carnation, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant produces small, oval leaves that are typically gray-green in color, and it blooms from late spring to early autumn. Dianthus strictus is a hardy perennial that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its flowers are often used in floral arrangements due to their vibrant colors and long-lasting blooms.
