Dolichos stenocarpus

Dolichos stenocarpus, commonly known as the narrow-seeded dolichos, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and trifoliate leaves composed of three small, oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces elongated, pod-like fruits that contain several small, kidney-shaped seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a warm, humid climate for optimal growth.

Dolichos stenocarpus is often used in traditional medicine and as a forage crop for livestock.
