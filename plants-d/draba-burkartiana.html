Draba burkartiana

Draba burkartiana, commonly known as the Burkart's wintercress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in alpine and subalpine regions. The plant features basal rosettes of dark green, hairy leaves and produces small, yellow flowers in early spring. It is adapted to cold climates and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils.

Draba burkartiana is known for its resilience and is a characteristic species of high-altitude ecosystems.
