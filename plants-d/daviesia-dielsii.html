Daviesia dielsii

Daviesia dielsii, commonly known as the Sturt's Desert Pea, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Australia.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its distinctive crimson or purple pea-shaped flowers. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces seed pods that split open to release its seeds. It is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with low rainfall.

Daviesia dielsii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
