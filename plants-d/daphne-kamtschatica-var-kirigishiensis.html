Daphne kamtschatica var. kirigishiensis

Daphne kamtschatica var. kirigishiensis, commonly known as the Japanese daphne, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features striking, fragrant flowers that range from white to pink, appearing in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in horticultural settings.
