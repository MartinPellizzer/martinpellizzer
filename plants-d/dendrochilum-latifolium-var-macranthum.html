Dendrochilum latifolium var. macranthum

Dendrochilum latifolium var. macranthum, commonly known as the broad-leafed tree orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often clustered at the base of the plant. The plant produces large, showy flowers with a distinctive yellow and green coloration. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
