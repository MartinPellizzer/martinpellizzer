Diospyros cauligera

Diospyros cauligera, commonly known as the mangosteen tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Ebenaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and dense, rounded canopy. The plant produces edible, fragrant berries that are highly valued for their sweet, tangy flavor. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

Diospyros cauligera is also noted for its medicinal properties and is often cultivated for both its fruit and ornamental value.
