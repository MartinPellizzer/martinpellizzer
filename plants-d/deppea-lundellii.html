Deppea lundellii

Deppea lundellii, commonly known as the Mexican orchid, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or forested areas, thriving in well-drained soil. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white, with a distinct lip that aids in pollination.

Deppea lundellii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
