Deyeuxia groenlandica

Deyeuxia groenlandica, commonly known as the Greenland feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to Arctic and alpine regions.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and fine, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in nutrient-poor, well-drained soils and is adapted to cold climates.

Deyeuxia groenlandica plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for Arctic wildlife.
