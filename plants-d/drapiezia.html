Drapiezia

Drapiezia, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense layer of fine, velvety hairs. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. Drapiezia is often found in moist, disturbed soils in tropical and subtropical regions.
