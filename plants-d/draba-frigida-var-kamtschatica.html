Draba frigida var. kamtschatica

Draba frigida var. kamtschatica, commonly known as the Kamchatka rockcress, is a perennial flowering plant native to the cold regions of eastern Siberia and the Russian Far East.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many economically important crops and weeds.
