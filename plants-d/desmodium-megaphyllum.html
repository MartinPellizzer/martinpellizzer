Desmodium megaphyllum

Desmodium megaphyllum, commonly known as the giant sicklepod, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, palmately compound leaves with elongated leaflets that resemble sickles. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers followed by pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and agricultural fields.

Desmodium megaphyllum is also known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it beneficial in soil enrichment.
