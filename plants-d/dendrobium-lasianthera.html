Dendrobium lasianthera

Dendrobium lasianthera, commonly known as the golden orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with branched pseudobulbs and strap-like leaves. The plant produces striking yellow flowers with red or orange markings, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium lasianthera is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
