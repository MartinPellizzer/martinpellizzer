Dryopteris tenuissima var. serrata

Dryopteris tenuissima var. serrata, commonly known as the Japanese Shield Fern, is a compact, evergreen fern native to Japan.

It features delicate, finely divided, serrated fronds that give it a distinctive, lacy appearance. The plant typically grows to a height of about 15-20 cm, making it ideal for small garden spaces or container gardening. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage, which remains fresh throughout the growing season.
