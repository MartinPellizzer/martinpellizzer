Dendranthema nubigenum

Dendranthema nubigenum, commonly known as the clouded chamomile, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features finely divided, gray-green leaves and produces small, white to yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its delicate appearance and aromatic properties.

Dendranthema nubigenum is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic benefits.
