Dendrobium × melanodiscus

Dendrobium × melanodiscus, commonly known as the black disc dendrobium, is a hybrid orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, cane-like stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, dark red or purple flowers with yellow lip segments, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Dendrobium × melanodiscus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
