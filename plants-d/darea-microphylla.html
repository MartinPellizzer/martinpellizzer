Darea microphylla

Darea microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved darea, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its compact growth habit and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Darea microphylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature.
