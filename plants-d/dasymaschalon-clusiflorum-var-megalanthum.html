Dasymaschalon clusiflorum var. megalanthum

Dasymaschalon clusiflorum var. megalanthum, commonly known as the large-flowered dasymaschalon, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, large, showy flowers that typically bloom in clusters. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves and a woody stem structure, making it a distinctive ornamental species. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique appearance, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts for its aesthetic value.
