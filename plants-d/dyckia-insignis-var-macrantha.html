Dyckia insignis var. macrantha

Dyckia insignis var. macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered dyckia, is a bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant produces striking, showy inflorescences with bright orange to red flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
