Dolichos elatus

Dolichos elatus, commonly known as the slender cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its long, slender pods that contain edible seeds, often used in various cuisines. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, with trifoliate leaves and small white or purple flowers. Its root system helps in nitrogen fixation, improving soil fertility.

Dolichos elatus is valued for its nutritional content and adaptability to different soil conditions.
