Desideria baiogoinensis

Desideria baiogoinensis, commonly known as the Baio-go-inensis orchid, is a rare and unique species native to specific regions in Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its intricate and delicate floral structure. The plant typically features showy, colorful blossoms that bloom during the warmer months of the year. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, adapted to thrive in shaded, humid environments.

Due to its limited habitat and threats from deforestation, it is considered a vulnerable species in conservation efforts.
