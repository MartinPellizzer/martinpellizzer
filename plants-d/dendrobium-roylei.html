Dendrobium roylei

Dendrobium roylei, commonly known as the Himalayan orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Himalayas.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a thick, fleshy rhizome that stores water and nutrients. The plant produces clusters of showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange. Its leaves are typically elliptical and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Dendrobium roylei thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest habitats.
