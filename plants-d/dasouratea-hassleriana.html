Dasouratea hassleriana

Dasouratea hassleriana, commonly known as the red-flowered dasouratea, is a striking tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces showy, crimson flowers that bloom in clusters, making it a popular ornamental species. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
