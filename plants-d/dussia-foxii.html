Dussia foxii

Dussia foxii, commonly known as the fox's dussia, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, wooded areas. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native plant gardens.

Dussia foxii is also an important food source for local pollinators.
