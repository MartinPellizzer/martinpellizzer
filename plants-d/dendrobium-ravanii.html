Dendrobium ravanii

Dendrobium ravanii, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with long, cane-like stems that store water and nutrients. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a velvety texture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium ravanii is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.
