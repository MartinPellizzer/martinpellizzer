Draco Ovata

The draco ovata is a unique and striking plant known for its distinctive dragon-like appearance. Native to the tropical regions of Africa, it thrives in warm and humid environments. Its most notable feature is its elongated, fleshy leaves that resemble the scales of a dragon. This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to purify the air. Despite its exotic look, it is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts.
