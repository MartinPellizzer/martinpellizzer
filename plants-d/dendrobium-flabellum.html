Dendrobium flabellum

Dendrobium flabellum, commonly known as the fan-leaf orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a branched stem system and produces fleshy, lance-shaped leaves. The plant is renowned for its showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of yellow, orange, and red. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Dendrobium flabellum is popular among orchid growers for its ornamental value and adaptability to various cultivation conditions.
