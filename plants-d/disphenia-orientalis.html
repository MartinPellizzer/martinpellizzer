Disphenia orientalis

Disphenia orientalis, commonly known as the oriental disphenia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Dispheniaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, clustered inflorescences composed of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and have a smooth texture.

Disphenia orientalis is often found in temperate regions of Asia, particularly in China and Korea.
