Dendrobium macrophyllum giganteum

Dendrobium macrophyllum giganteum, commonly known as the giant wax orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, cane-like stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of yellow and green, often with intricate patterns on the petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

Due to its size and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for both horticultural collections and botanical gardens.
