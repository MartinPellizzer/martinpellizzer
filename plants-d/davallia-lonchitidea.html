Davallia lonchitidea

Davallia lonchitidea, commonly known as the fernlet, is a small, evergreen fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, lace-like fronds that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. The plant has a compact growth habit, with rhizomes that spread horizontally just below the soil surface. Its leaves are bi-lobed and have a distinctive, finely divided structure.

It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often used in ornamental horticulture for its elegant appearance.
