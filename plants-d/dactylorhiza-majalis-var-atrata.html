Dactylorhiza majalis var. atrata

Dactylorhiza majalis var. atrata, commonly known as the dark purple orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its striking dark purple to almost black flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant has a pseudobulbous structure, with narrow leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique and dramatic floral display.
