Dalea elegans var. onobrychioides

Dalea elegans var. onobrychioides, commonly known as the slender milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces pods that contain several seeds.

This variety is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.
