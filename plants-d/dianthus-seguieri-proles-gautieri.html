Dianthus seguieri proles gautieri

Dianthus seguieri proles gautieri, commonly known as the rock campion, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant features wiry, branching stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a hardy and ornamental choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
