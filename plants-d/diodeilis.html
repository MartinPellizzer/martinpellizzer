Diodeilis

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that typically range in color from white to pale blue. The plant features slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Diodeilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
