Dichrotrichum stenophyllum

Dichrotrichum stenophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf dichrotrichum, is a species of flowering plant in the family Poaceae.

It is a perennial grass that typically grows in moist, temperate environments. The plant is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets. It is often found in wet meadows, marshes, and along the edges of ponds.

Due to its habitat preferences, it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
