Ditaxis sericophylla

Ditaxis sericophylla, commonly known as the silky leaf dittany, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, silvery-green leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically borne on slender stalks. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical studies.
