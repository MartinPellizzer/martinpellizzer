Duperrea pavettifolia f. mollissisima

Duperrea pavettifolia f. mollissisima, commonly known as the silky duperrea, is a rare and ornamental plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety foliage that gives it a unique texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for shaded garden areas.

Due to its rarity, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by specialty growers.
