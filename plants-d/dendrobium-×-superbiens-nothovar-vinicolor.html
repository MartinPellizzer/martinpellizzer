Dendrobium × superbiens nothovar. vinicolor

Dendrobium × superbiens nothovar. vinicolor, commonly known as the Purple Wavering Dendrobium, is a hybrid orchid characterized by its striking violet and purple floral hues.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia, where it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant is an epiphytic orchid, typically growing on trees or rocks, and it possesses pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

Its flowers are borne on racemes and are known for their intricate, fragrant blooms that attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
