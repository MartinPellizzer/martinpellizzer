Dracaena aurea

Dracaena aurea, commonly known as the golden dracaena, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features tall, slender stems with dark green, glossy leaves that have prominent yellow margins. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires moderate watering. Its air-purifying qualities make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Dracaena aurea is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of care.
