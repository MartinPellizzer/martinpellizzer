Dichapetalum heudelotii var. ndongense

Dichapetalum heudelotii var. ndongense, commonly known as the Ndonge's flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, flame-red inflorescences that appear in clusters, giving it a dramatic visual impact. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are typically elliptical or ovate in shape. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its showy flowers.
