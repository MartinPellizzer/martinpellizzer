Draba funckii

Draba funckii, commonly known as the Funck's winter cress, is a small flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and is characterized by its low-growing habit and bright yellow flowers. Draba funckii has basal leaves that are linear to oblanceolate, and its stems are often hairy.
