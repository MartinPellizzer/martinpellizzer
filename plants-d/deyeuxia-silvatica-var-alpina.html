Deyeuxia silvatica var. alpina

Deyeuxia silvatica var. alpina, commonly known as Alpine wood sedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to alpine regions.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in mountainous areas. It is often found in meadows and along stream banks at high elevations.

Its botanical features make it an important component of alpine ecosystems.
