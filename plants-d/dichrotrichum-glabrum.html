Dichrotrichum glabrum

Dichrotrichum glabrum, commonly known as the smooth dichrotrichum, is a species of flowering plant in the family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with narrow, blade-like leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in dense clusters, typical of the grass family. It is native to certain regions of southern Africa and thrives in well-drained soils.

Due to its unique morphology and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
