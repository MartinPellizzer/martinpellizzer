Dianthus tabrisianus var. coloratus

Dianthus tabrisianus var. coloratus, commonly known as the showy campion, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to red, with a distinctive white or pale pink center. The plant produces small, rounded leaves that are typically gray-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

The flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, adding a striking visual element to gardens.
