Drosanthemum thudichumii var. gracilium

Drosanthemum thudichumii var. gracilium, commonly known as the slender ice plant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white wax, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces clusters of tiny, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink and purple during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and thrives in full sun with minimal water.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
