Diervilla venusta

Diervilla venusta, commonly known as the golden spire heath, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features upright, spire-like clusters of golden-yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has slender, woody stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, acidic soil.

Diervilla venusta is often used in landscape design for its ornamental value and adaptability to various garden conditions.
