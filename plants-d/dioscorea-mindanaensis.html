Dioscorea mindanaensis

Dioscorea mindanaensis, commonly known as Mindanao yam, is a climbing vine native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its tuberous roots, which are the primary edible part of the plant. The plant produces small, green, heart-shaped leaves and yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. Its tubers are valued for their starchy texture and are used in local cuisines.

The species is also studied for its potential medicinal properties.
