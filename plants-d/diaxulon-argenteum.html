Diaxulon argenteum

Diaxulon argenteum, commonly known as the silverleaf starflower, is a rare flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil. Its leaves are covered in a fine, waxy coating that reflects sunlight, aiding in water conservation.
