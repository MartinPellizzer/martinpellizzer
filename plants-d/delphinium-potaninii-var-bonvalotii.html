Delphinium potaninii var. bonvalotii

Delphinium potaninii var. bonvalotii, commonly known as the Bonvalot's larkspur, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant produces showy blue to purple flowers in racemes, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a rough, hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a striking addition to alpine gardens.
