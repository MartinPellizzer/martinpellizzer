Diervilla florida f. brevicalycina

Diervilla florida f. brevicalycina, commonly known as the compact calyx honeysuckle, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, oval-shaped leaves that turn reddish in the fall. It is characterized by its short calyx, which is a distinguishing feature of this particular form.

This plant is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
