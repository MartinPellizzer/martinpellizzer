Dendrobium tosaense var. pere-fauriei

Dendrobium tosaense var. pere-fauriei, commonly known as the Tosa orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Japan.

It features a sympodial growth habit with elongated, cylindrical pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with prominent yellow lips. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and requires well-drained soil.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and ecological role in its native habitat.
