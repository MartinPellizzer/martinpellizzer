Dendrobium puniceum

Dendrobium puniceum, commonly known as the red-stemmed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, red or pink flowers with yellow lips. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Dendrobium puniceum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique floral display.
