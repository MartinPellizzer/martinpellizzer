Dracaena alborosea

Dracaena alborosea, commonly known as the White Dracaena, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, pale green to white leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow, followed by red berries. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Dracaena alborosea is prized for its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
