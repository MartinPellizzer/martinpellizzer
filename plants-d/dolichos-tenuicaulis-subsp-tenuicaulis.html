Dolichos tenuicaulis subsp. tenuicaulis

Dolichos tenuicaulis subsp.

tenuicaulis, commonly known as the slender dwarf bean, is a climbing legume native to southern Africa. It features slender, twining stems and trifoliate leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets.

The flowers are typically pale yellow or white, with a distinctive spurred petal. The plant produces small, elongated pods containing several seeds.

It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in challenging climates.
