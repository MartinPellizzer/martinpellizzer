Deyeuxia arundinacea var. robusta

Deyeuxia arundinacea var. robusta, commonly known as giant bamboo grass, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, robust stalks and dense, feathery inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy environments, thriving in areas with high moisture levels. Its leaves are long and narrow, with a smooth texture, and it produces small, clustered flowers.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in wetland habitats.
