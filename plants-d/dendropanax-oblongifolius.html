Dendropanax oblongifolius

Dendropanax oblongifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf arrowleaf, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, lance-shaped leaves that are often glossy and have a distinct central vein. The plant typically grows as an understory species in humid, shaded forest environments. Its leaves are sometimes used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Dendropanax oblongifolius is also valued for its ornamental appeal in horticultural settings.
