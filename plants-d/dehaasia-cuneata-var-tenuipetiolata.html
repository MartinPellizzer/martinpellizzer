Dehaasia cuneata var. tenuipetiolata

Dehaasia cuneata var. tenuipetiolata, commonly known as the narrow-petioled cuneata, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves and yellow-orange flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its foliage is often covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft, textured appearance.

This variant is notable for its compact growth habit and is occasionally cultivated for ornamental purposes.
