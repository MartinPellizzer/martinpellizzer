Dracaena elliptica var. gracilis

Dracaena elliptica var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender dracaena, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Madagascar.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a reddish tinge along the edges. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and can reach heights of up to 10 feet in optimal conditions. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Dracaena elliptica var. gracilis is popular as a decorative indoor plant due to its elegant appearance and low maintenance requirements.
