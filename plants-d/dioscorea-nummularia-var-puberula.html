Dioscorea nummularia var. puberula

Dioscorea nummularia var. puberula, commonly known as the smooth yam, is a perennial vine native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its climbing habit and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by tuberous roots, which are the primary edible part. Its growth is typically found in moist, well-drained soils, and it thrives in warm climates.

The tubers are often used in traditional cuisines and have been studied for their potential medicinal properties.
