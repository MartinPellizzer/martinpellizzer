Delphinium qinghaiense

Delphinium qinghaiense, commonly known as the Qinghai larkspur, is a perennial herb native to the Qinghai region of China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems and showy blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky areas at high elevations. Its leaves are deeply lobed and have a distinctive, fern-like appearance.

Delphinium qinghaiense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also studied for its potential medicinal properties.
