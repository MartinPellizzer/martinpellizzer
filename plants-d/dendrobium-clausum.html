Dendrobium clausum

Dendrobium clausum, commonly known as the closed lip orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments on trees or rocks. Its flowers, which are usually white or pale yellow, bloom in late winter or early spring.

Dendrobium clausum is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and unique floral display.
