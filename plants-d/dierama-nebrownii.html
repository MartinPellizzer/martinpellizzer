Dierama nebrownii

Dierama nebrownii, commonly known as the purple dierama, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking purple and white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It has linear, grass-like leaves and produces tall, branched flower spikes.

Dierama nebrownii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
