Dendrobium tricallosum

Dendrobium tricallosum, commonly known as the golden spike orchid, is a sympodial epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a branched stem system with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically blooming in summer. Its leaves are elliptical and arranged in two rows along the stem.

It thrives in humid, well-lit environments and requires good air circulation for optimal growth.
