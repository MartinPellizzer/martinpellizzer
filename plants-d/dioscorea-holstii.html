Dioscorea holstii

Dioscorea holstii, commonly known as the African yam bean, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae and is characterized by its tuberous roots, which are the primary edible part. The plant produces small, yellow flowers and cylindrical, hairy fruits that contain several seeds. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a velvety texture.

Dioscorea holstii is valued not only for its nutritional content but also for its traditional use in various African cultures.
