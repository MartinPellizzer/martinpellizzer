Domingoa gemma

Domingoa gemma, commonly known as the golden star cactus, is a unique succulent native to the arid regions of South America.

It features a compact, rounded stem covered in dense, velvety gray-green foliage. The plant produces striking yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a vibrant touch to its otherwise muted appearance. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, designed to store water and withstand prolonged drought.

Domingoa gemma is prized for its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and desert landscapes.
