Dracaena reflexa var. lanceolata

Dracaena reflexa var. lanceolata, commonly known as the Song of India or the Variegated Dracaena, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, lance-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that appear in clusters at the base of the stems. It is well adapted to indoor environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Its upright growth habit and low maintenance make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
