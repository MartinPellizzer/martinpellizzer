Dendrobium vanoverberghii

Dendrobium vanoverberghii, commonly known as the Vanoverbergh's orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit, with multiple stems that grow horizontally and produce leaves along their length. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Dendrobium vanoverberghii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
