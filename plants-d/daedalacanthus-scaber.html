Daedalacanthus scaber

Daedalacanthus scaber, commonly known as the rough-leaved water plantain, is a perennial aquatic plant native to the wetlands of eastern North America.

The plant typically grows in shallow, still or slow-moving freshwater environments, such as marshes and ponds.
