Dyckia oscarii

Dyckia oscarii, commonly known as the Oscarii Dyckia, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is native to Brazil.

It is a compact, evergreen perennial that forms a dense rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves. The leaves are typically gray-green with a waxy coating, and they are arranged in a spiral pattern around the central stem. This plant produces striking, long-lasting inflorescences that can range in color from red to orange.

It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
