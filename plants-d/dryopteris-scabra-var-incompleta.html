Dryopteris scabra var. incompleta

Dryopteris scabra var. incompleta, commonly known as the rough shield fern, is a variant of the scaly shield fern.

It is a perennial evergreen fern that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features fronds with a distinctive scaly, rough texture on the underside, giving it its common name. Its foliage is usually dark green and leathery, with a compact growth habit.

This variant is often found in forest understories and is valued for its ornamental qualities in horticulture.
