Detarium senegalense

Detarium senegalense, commonly known as the African shea tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its large, palmate leaves and yellow flowers. The tree produces elongated pods that contain edible seeds used in traditional medicine and as a source of oil. Its root system is deep and robust, allowing it to thrive in dry and semi-arid conditions.

The plant is also valued for its ecological role in soil stabilization and habitat provision.
