Dryopteris gutishanensis

Dryopteris gutishanensis, commonly known as the Guti Shan Shield Fern, is a species of shield fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its distinctive fronds with a broad, shield-like shape. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forests and mountainous regions. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a leathery texture, with sporangia borne on the underside of the leaves.

This species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological significance in its native habitat.
