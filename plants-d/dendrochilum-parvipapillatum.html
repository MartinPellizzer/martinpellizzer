Dendrochilum parvipapillatum

Dendrochilum parvipapillatum, commonly known as the small-papilla dendrochilum, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. Its flowers are usually small and white or pale yellow, with a distinctive lip that is often adorned with intricate patterns.

This species is appreciated for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
