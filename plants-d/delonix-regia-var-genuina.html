Delonix regia var. genuina

Delonix regia var. genuina, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery orange-red flowers that bloom in dense clusters during the dry season. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with long, slender leaflets. Its bark is smooth and light gray, often exuding a milky sap when cut.

The tree is widely cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to warm, sunny climates with well-drained soils.
