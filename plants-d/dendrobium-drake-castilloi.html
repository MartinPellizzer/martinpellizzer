Dendrobium drake-castilloi

Dendrobium drake-castilloi, commonly known as the Drake Castillo orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

Dendrobium drake-castilloi is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
