Dillenia reifferscheidia

Dillenia reifferscheidia, commonly known as the tropical dillenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves and showy, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces fleshy, orange-red fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Dillenia reifferscheidia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
