Dieffenbachia insignis

Dieffenbachia insignis, commonly known as the Leopard Plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated, arrowhead-shaped leaves that display shades of green, white, and yellow. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is a popular ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.

Dieffenbachia insignis is also noted for its ability to purify indoor air, making it a favored choice for interior landscaping.
