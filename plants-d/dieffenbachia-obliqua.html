Dieffenbachia obliqua

Dieffenbachia obliqua, commonly known as the Shiny Dieffenbachia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, oval-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green with white or cream-colored veins. The plant has a woody stem and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or pale green. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Dieffenbachia obliqua is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
