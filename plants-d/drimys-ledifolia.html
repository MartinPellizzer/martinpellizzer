Drimys ledifolia

Drimys ledifolia, commonly known as the Chilean wintergreen, is a deciduous shrub native to the temperate rainforests of southern Chile.

Drimys ledifolia is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens in cooler regions.
