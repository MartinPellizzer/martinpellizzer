Dianthus Giganteus Subsp. Subgiganteus

Dianthus giganteus subsp. subgiganteus is a striking member of the carnation family known for its vibrant, showy flowers. This subspecies is native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. The plant produces clusters of pink to red petals with a distinctive white or pink center. Its bold appearance and long blooming period make it a favorite among gardeners seeking visual impact.
