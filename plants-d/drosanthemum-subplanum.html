Drosanthemum subplanum

Drosanthemum subplanum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved drosanthemum, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, narrow leaves that are typically blue-green in color, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
