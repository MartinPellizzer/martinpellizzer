Dodonaea hackettiana

Dodonaea hackettiana, commonly known as the silver dollar tree, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub or tree that typically grows in coastal and semi-arid regions. The plant features small, pale green leaves that are oval in shape and have a silvery underside, giving it its common name. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring, followed by small, round, red fruits.

Dodonaea hackettiana is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
