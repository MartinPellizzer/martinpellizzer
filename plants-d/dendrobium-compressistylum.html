Dendrobium compressistylum

Dendrobium compressistylum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a thick, fleshy rhizome and produces a single, upright stem. The plant has linear to lanceolate leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. It blooms with showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink, typically during the warmer months.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
