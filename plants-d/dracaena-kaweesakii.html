Dracaena kaweesakii

Dracaena kaweesakii, commonly known as the Thai dragon tree, is a species native to Thailand.

It is a succulent plant characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves and upright growth habit. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by red, berry-like fruits. Its leaves are typically dark green with a glossy appearance and can have a slight variegation.

Dracaena kaweesakii is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and ability to thrive in a variety of light conditions.
