Delosperma bosserianum

Delosperma bosserianum, commonly known as the Bosserian ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. Its ability to form dense mats makes it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
