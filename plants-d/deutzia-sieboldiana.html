Deutzia sieboldiana

Deutzia sieboldiana, commonly known as the Japanese deutzia, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It features clusters of fragrant, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has slender, arching branches and dark green, oval-shaped leaves. It typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and prefers well-drained soil.

Deutzia sieboldiana is valued in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in partial shade.
