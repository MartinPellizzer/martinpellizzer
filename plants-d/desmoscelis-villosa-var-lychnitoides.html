Desmoscelis villosa var. lychnitoides

Desmoscelis villosa var. lychnitoides, commonly known as the silky sand mat, is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

It features dense clusters of small, scale-like leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a soft, velvety appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish or reddish in color, often clustered in loose inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

This species is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
