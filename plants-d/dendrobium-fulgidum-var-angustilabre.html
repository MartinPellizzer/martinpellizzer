Dendrobium fulgidum var. angustilabre

Dendrobium fulgidum var. angustilabre, commonly known as the narrow-lipped orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic orchid that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, tropical environments. The plant features long, cane-like stems that are often branched and covered in dense, fleshy leaves. Its flowers are showy, with bright yellow petals and a distinctive, narrow lip.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms.
