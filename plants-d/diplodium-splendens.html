Diplodium splendens

Diplodium splendens, commonly known as the splendour diplodium, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that display a range of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. Diplodium splendens thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Its unique floral structure and colorful blooms make it a standout addition to any botanical collection.
