Dendrobium loddigesii var. album

Dendrobium loddigesii var. album, commonly known as the white-lipped dendrobium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is an epiphytic plant, typically growing on trees or rocks in tropical rainforests. The plant features long, cane-like stems that are covered in dense clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. Its leaves are strap-shaped and arranged spirally along the stem.

It is valued in horticulture for its striking floral display and adaptability to various growing conditions.
