Digitaria patula

Digitaria patula, commonly known as flatgrass, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its flat, grass-like leaves and small, spikelet-based inflorescences. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in open fields, roadsides, and wetlands. It is an annual or perennial herb that thrives in warm climates.

Its botanical features make it adaptable to various environmental conditions, contributing to its widespread distribution.
