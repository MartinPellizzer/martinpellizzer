Dorstenia aspera

Dorstenia aspera, commonly known as the rough-stemmed dwarf banana, is a tropical plant belonging to the Musaceae family.

It is characterized by its short, erect stems and broad, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy layer. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with both male and female flowers, typical of the banana family. Due to its unique appearance and growth habits, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
