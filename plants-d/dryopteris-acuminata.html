Dryopteris acuminata

Dryopteris acuminata, commonly known as the Southern Shield Fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to the southeastern United States.

It features a robust rhizome system that supports its dense, upright fronds. The fronds are typically bi- or tri-pinnate, with a distinctive shield-shaped appearance on the underside. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
