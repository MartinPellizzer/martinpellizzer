Didymocheton stellatopuberulus

Didymocheton stellatopuberulus, commonly known as the star-haired flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, palmate leaves with a hairy texture, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces showy, red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to warm, humid climates.
