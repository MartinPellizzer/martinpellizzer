Dichaea mattogrossensis

Dichaea mattogrossensis, commonly known as the Brazilian orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
