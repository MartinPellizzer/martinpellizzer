Dendropanax parviflorus

Dendropanax parviflorus, commonly known as the small-flowered arrowleaf, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal and subtropical regions, often in sandy or rocky soils. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a distinctive arrowhead base, and its small, white flowers appear in clusters during spring. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens.
