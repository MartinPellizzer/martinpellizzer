Dendrobenthamia xanthocarpa

Dendrobenthamia xanthocarpa, commonly known as the yellow fruit orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems and produces showy, yellow-orange flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are borne in clusters and are known for their delicate, fragrant appearance.

The fruit is fleshy and yellow, containing numerous small seeds that are dispersed by animals.
