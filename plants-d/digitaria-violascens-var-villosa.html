Digitaria violascens var. villosa

Digitaria violascens var. villosa, commonly known as hairy digitaria, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its hairy leaves and stems, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as fields and roadsides. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain minute flowers.

This species is often found in areas with well-drained soils and is adapted to warm climates.
