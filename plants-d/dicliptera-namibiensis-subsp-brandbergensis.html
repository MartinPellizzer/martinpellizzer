Dicliptera namibiensis subsp. brandbergensis

Dicliptera namibiensis subsp.

brandbergensis, commonly known as the Brandberg daisy, is a flowering plant native to Namibia, particularly found in the Brandberg mountain region. It is a perennial herb with a compact rosette of narrow, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a cluster at the center of the rosette, which bloom during the cooler months. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

This subspecies is notable for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in rock gardens due to its drought tolerance and attractive appearance.
