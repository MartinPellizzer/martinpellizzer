Dignathia aristata

Dignathia aristata, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to the wetlands of South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive arrowhead shape, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Dignathia aristata is often used in water gardens and as a decorative plant in shaded areas due to its attractive foliage.
