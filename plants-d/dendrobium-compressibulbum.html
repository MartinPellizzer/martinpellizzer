Dendrobium compressibulbum

Dendrobium compressibulbum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with multiple stems that can reach up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange, often with intricate patterns. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in humid, warm environments with bright indirect light.

Dendrobium compressibulbum is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
