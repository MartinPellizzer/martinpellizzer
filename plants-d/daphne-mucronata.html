Daphne mucronata

Daphne mucronata, commonly known as the spurred daphne, is a deciduous shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that range from white to pink, with distinctive spurred petals. The plant has dark green, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Daphne mucronata is known for its winter blooming habit, adding color to gardens during the colder months.
