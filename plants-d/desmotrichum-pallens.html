Desmotrichum pallens

Desmotrichum pallens, commonly known as the silverleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky or sandy soils. The plant features a basal rosette of silver-gray, hairy leaves that help reduce water loss. Its flowers are yellow and occur in a single, erect stem, blooming in spring.

It is characterized by its unique leaf structure and is often used in traditional medicine.
