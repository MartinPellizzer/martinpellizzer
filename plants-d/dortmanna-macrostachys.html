Dortmanna macrostachys

Dortmanna macrostachys, commonly known as the giant feather grass, is a perennial grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and long, narrow leaves that give it a feathery appearance. The plant produces distinctive, drooping inflorescences that are often used in floral arrangements. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Dortmanna macrostachys is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
