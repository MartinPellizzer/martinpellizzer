Digitaria rhachitricha

Digitaria rhachitricha, commonly known as hairy digitgrass, is a perennial grass native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and hairy leaves, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny grains. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Digitaria rhachitricha is sometimes used in traditional medicine and is also a forage plant for livestock.
