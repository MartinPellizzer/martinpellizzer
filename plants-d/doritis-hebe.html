Doritis hebe

Doritis hebe, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, silver-green leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, or white. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Doritis hebe is drought-tolerant and is often used in rock gardens or as an ornamental plant.
