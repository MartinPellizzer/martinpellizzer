Draba lichiangensis

Draba lichiangensis, commonly known as the Lichiang draba, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants, and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, adapting to the harsh conditions of its mountainous habitat. Draba lichiangensis is of interest to botanists due to its unique adaptations and limited distribution.
