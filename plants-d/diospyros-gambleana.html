Diospyros gambleana

Diospyros gambleana, commonly known as the African persimmon, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Ebenaceae family, which includes other persimmon and ebony species. The tree produces small, orange-yellow berries that are edible when fully ripe. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a rough texture.

The wood of Diospyros gambleana is dense and valued for its durability and aesthetic qualities.
