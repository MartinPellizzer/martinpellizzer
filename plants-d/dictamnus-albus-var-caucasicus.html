Dictamnus albus var. caucasicus

Dictamnus albus var. caucasicus, commonly known as Caucasian white dictamnus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Caucasus region.

It features aromatic, glandular leaves that emit a strong, spicy scent when crushed. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its fragrant foliage and delicate floral display.
