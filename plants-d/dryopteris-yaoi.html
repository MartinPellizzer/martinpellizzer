Dryopteris yaoi

Dryopteris yaoi, commonly known as the Yao fern, is a species of polypod fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are underground stems that help in the spread of the plant.

The leaves are usually bi- to tri-pinnate, with a light green color and a finely divided structure.
