Drypetes sherffii

Drypetes sherffii, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy underside. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, followed by woody capsules containing numerous seeds.
