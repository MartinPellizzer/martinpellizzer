Darwinia decumbens

Darwinia decumbens, commonly known as the prostrate darwinia, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with dark green, narrow leaves that form a dense mat. The plant produces spikes of showy, pink to red tubular flowers during the autumn season. It is well adapted to arid and coastal environments, thriving in well-drained soils.

Darwinia decumbens is often used in native gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
