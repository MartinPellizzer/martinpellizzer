Dammara orientalis var. alba

Dammara orientalis var. alba, commonly known as the white dammara, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, woody stems and small, clustered white flowers that bloom during the rainy season. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
