Draba sakuraii f. sinanensis

Draba sakuraii f. sinanensis, commonly known as the Sinan Mountain Draba, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or alpine environments. The plant features basal leaves that are linear to lanceolate in shape, often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, yellowish, and arranged in a raceme, typical of the genus Draba.

It is native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia, particularly in China.
