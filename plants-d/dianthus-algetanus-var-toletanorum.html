Dianthus algetanus var. toletanorum

Dianthus algetanus var. toletanorum, commonly known as the Toletan carnation, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves, and it produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
