Dolichos pubescens

Dolichos pubescens, commonly known as the hairy urd bean, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, hairy leaves. The plant produces elongated, pod-like fruits that contain several edible seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a warm climate for optimal growth.

Dolichos pubescens is valued both for its nutritional seeds and its role in soil enrichment through nitrogen fixation.
