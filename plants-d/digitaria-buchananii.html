Digitaria buchananii

Digitaria buchananii, commonly known as Buchanan's digitaria, is a species of grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that contain both male and female flowers. It thrives in open, sunny habitats and is often found in grasslands and disturbed soils.

Digitaria buchananii is adapted to a variety of environmental conditions, making it a resilient and widespread species in its native range.
