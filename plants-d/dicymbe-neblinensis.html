Dicymbe neblinensis

Dicymbe neblinensis, commonly known as the misty dicymbe, is a flowering plant native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has pinnately compound leaves and produces pods that contain several seeds.
