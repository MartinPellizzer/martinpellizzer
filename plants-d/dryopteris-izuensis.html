Dryopteris izuensis

Dryopteris izuensis, commonly known as the Izu Shield Fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky slopes. Its fronds are bi- or tri-pinnate, with a leathery texture and a deep green color.

Dryopteris izuensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
