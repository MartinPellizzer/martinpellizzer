Declieuxia vincoides

Declieuxia vincoides, commonly known as the silver sword, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette of silver-gray, sword-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and arid landscapes.

Declieuxia vincoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
