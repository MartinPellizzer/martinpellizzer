Deschampsia flexuosa var. afromontana

Deschampsia flexuosa var. afromontana, commonly known as the East African bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to the highland regions of East Africa.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in temperate to cool climates. The plant is characterized by its slender, often bent stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers.

This variety is often found in open woodlands and grasslands, contributing to the ecological diversity of its native habitat.
