Dichapetalum germainii

Dichapetalum germainii, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that appear in clusters during the dry season. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are typically ovate or elliptic in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
