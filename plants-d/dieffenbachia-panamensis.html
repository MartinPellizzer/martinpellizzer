Dieffenbachia panamensis

Dieffenbachia panamensis, commonly known as the Panamanian dieffenbachia, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with white or cream-colored veins. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other popular houseplants like peace lilies. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Dieffenbachia panamensis is known for its striking appearance and is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant.
