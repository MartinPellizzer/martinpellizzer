Dubrueilia pumila

Dubrueilia pumila, commonly known as the dwarf pittosporum, is a low-growing shrub native to New Zealand.

It features small, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and have a slightly rough texture. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers, which are often followed by small, red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Dubrueilia pumila is often used in landscaping for its hardy nature and ornamental value.
