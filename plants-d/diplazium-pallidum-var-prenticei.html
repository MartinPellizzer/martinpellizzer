Diplazium pallidum var. prenticei

Diplazium pallidum var. prenticei, commonly known as the Prentice's spider fern, is a variant of the silver sword fern.

It is a terrestrial fern characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and its distinctive, slender, arching fronds. The plant typically features dark green, leathery leaves with a glossy texture and a slightly serrated margin. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories or rocky crevices.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens.
