Acianthera bohnkiana

Acianthera bohnkiana, commonly known as the Bohnken's orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid with a single, erect stem that bears several lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated by enthusiasts.
