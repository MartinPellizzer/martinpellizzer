Astragalus winkleri

Astragalus winkleri, commonly known as the Winkler milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Astragalus winkleri is often found in desert scrub and grassland habitats, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife.
