Aeschynomene gracilipes var. brevistipitata

Aeschynomene gracilipes var. brevistipitata, commonly known as the slender-stippled aeschynomene, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, slender stems and small, trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in racemes, which are followed by narrow, pod-like fruits. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats.

This variant is distinguished by its shorter stipules compared to the typical form of Aeschynomene gracilipes.
