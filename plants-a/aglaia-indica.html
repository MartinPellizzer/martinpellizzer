Aglaia indica

Aglaia indica, commonly known as the Indian aglaia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes mahogany and other tropical hardwoods. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered yellow flowers. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release winged seeds.
