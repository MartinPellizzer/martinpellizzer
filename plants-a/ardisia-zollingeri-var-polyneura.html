Ardisia zollingeri var. polyneura

Ardisia zollingeri var. polyneura, commonly known as the silverleaf ardisia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a distinctive silvery underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by bright red berries in the fall. It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to various soil types.

However, it can become invasive in certain regions if not managed properly.
