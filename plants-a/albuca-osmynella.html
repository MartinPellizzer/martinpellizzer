Albuca osmynella

Albuca osmynella, commonly known as the Cape tulip, is a perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers. Its bulbs are relatively drought-tolerant once established, requiring minimal maintenance.

Albuca osmynella is prized for its ornamental value and ability to bloom reliably in temperate climates.
