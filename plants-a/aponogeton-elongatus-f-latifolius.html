Aponogeton elongatus f. latifolius

Aponogeton elongatus f. latifolius, commonly known as the broad-leaved elongated arrowhead, is a aquatic plant characterized by its elongated, arrow-shaped leaves.

It is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments. The plant features a rhizomatous root system that helps anchor it in the substrate. Its leaves are typically broad and glossy, providing a striking visual contrast in aquatic gardens.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in ponds and water gardens.
