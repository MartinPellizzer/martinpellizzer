Aloysia floribunda

Aloysia floribunda, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking orange to yellow flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges, and it thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.

Aloysia floribunda is also known for its aromatic foliage and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
