Aragoa × diazii

Aragoa × diazii, commonly known as the hybrid aragoa, is a cross between two species of the Aragoa genus.

It is a shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features compound leaves with serrated edges and produces small, fragrant flowers. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and flowering habit.

The hybrid is relatively hardy and can thrive in a variety of soil types.
