Aloe tuberculata

Aloe tuberculata, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy, bluish-green leaves with distinctive tubercles or bumps along the edges. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences that bear yellowish-green flowers. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and being drought-tolerant.

Aloe tuberculata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
