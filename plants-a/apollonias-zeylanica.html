Apollonias zeylanica

Apollonias zeylanica, commonly known as the Ceylon gooseberry, is a tropical shrub native to Sri Lanka.

It produces small, yellow, citrus-like fruits that are rich in vitamin C. The plant has a woody stem with thorny branches and alternate, simple, ovate leaves. Its flowers are small, white, and clustered in inflorescences, and it is often cultivated for its edible fruit.

The plant thrives in humid, tropical climates and is adapted to coastal environments.
