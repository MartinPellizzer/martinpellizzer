Arctotis subacaulis

Arctotis subacaulis, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed arctotis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features narrow, upright stems and produces daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a fuzzy texture.

Arctotis subacaulis is well-suited for rock gardens and containers due to its compact growth habit and vibrant floral display.
