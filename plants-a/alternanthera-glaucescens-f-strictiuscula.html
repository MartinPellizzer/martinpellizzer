Alternanthera glaucescens f. strictiuscula

Alternanthera glaucescens f. strictiuscula, commonly known as the Compact Alternanthera, is a compact, trailing plant native to South America.

It features striking, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep red or purple in color, with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or white, which appear in clusters. It is often grown as a decorative foliage plant in water gardens or as a houseplant.

Its compact growth habit makes it ideal for containers and hanging baskets.
