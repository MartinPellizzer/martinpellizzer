Athyrium sargentii

Athyrium sargentii, commonly known as Sargent's lady fern, is a species of fern native to eastern North America.

It belongs to the Dryopteridaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its fronds are bipinnate, with a distinctive, graceful appearance that makes it a popular ornamental plant.
