Aspilia alternifolia

Aspilia alternifolia, commonly known as the African flame flower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red bracts that surround small yellow flowers, giving it a fiery appearance. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a rough texture.

Aspilia alternifolia thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and rocky habitats.
