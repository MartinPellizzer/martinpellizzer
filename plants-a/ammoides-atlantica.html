Ammoides atlantica

Ammoides atlantica, commonly known as the Atlantic ammoides, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features small, pale blue or white flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

Ammoides atlantica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
