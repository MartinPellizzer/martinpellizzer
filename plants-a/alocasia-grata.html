Alocasia grata

Alocasia grata, commonly known as the Chinese arrowroot or velvet leaf alocasia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped, glossy leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining, fertile soil. Alocasia grata is a member of the Araceae family, which includes plants like peace lilies and calla lilies.

Its striking foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
