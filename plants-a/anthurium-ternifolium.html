Anthurium ternifolium

Anthurium ternifolium, commonly known as the three-leaved anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are usually deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can range from red to yellow. Anthurium ternifolium thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique foliage and ornamental flowers make it a valued addition to both indoor and outdoor landscapes.
