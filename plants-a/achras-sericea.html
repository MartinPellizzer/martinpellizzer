Achras sericea

Achras sericea, commonly known as the silky tree, is a species of flowering plant in the family Arecaceae.

It is native to the coastal areas of southern Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs. It produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by edible, fleshy fruits.

The silky tree is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
