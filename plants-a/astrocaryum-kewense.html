Astrocaryum kewense

Astrocaryum kewense, commonly known as the Kew wax palm, is a species of palm native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by clustered, woody fruits containing edible seeds. Its unique appearance and ecological role make it an important species in tropical ecosystems.

The Kew wax palm is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
