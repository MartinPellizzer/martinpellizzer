Astragalus karjaginii

Astragalus karjaginii, commonly known as Karjagin's milkvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family Fabaceae.

It is native to the arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia, particularly found in Kazakhstan and Russia. The plant is characterized by its slender stems, compound leaves, and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring. Astragalus karjaginii plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity in its native habitats.
