Aglaonema ovatum var. maculatum

Aglaonema ovatum var. maculatum, commonly known as the spotted aglaonema, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage featuring dark green leaves with creamy white and pinkish markings. The plant thrives in low-light conditions and requires moderate humidity to maintain its vibrant appearance. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and can grow up to 30 centimeters in length.

Aglaonema ovatum var. maculatum is a popular indoor plant due to its ornamental value and air-purifying properties.
