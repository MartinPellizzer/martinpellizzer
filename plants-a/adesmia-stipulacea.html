Adesmia stipulacea

Adesmia stipulacea, commonly known as the stipuley bupleurum, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Adesmia stipulacea is also valued for its ornamental appeal and potential ecological benefits.
