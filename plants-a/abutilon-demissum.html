Abutilon demissum

Abutilon demissum, commonly known as the woolly abutilon, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its soft, fuzzy leaves and showy, lantern-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant is typically shrub-like, with a woody stem and can grow up to several feet tall. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique appearance and adaptability contribute to its appeal in both landscape design and horticultural collections.
