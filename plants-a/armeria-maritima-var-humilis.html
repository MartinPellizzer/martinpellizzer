Armeria maritima var. humilis

Armeria maritima var. humilis, commonly known as sea thrift, is a low-growing perennial plant native to coastal regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It features dense clusters of small, bright pink or white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is well adapted to salty, windy environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dunes, cliffs, and coastal meadows.

Its hardy nature makes it a popular choice for coastal gardens and erosion control.
