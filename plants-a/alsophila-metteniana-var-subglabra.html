Alsophila metteniana var. subglabra

Alsophila metteniana var. subglabra, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed tree fern, is a species native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and feathery fronds that give it a distinctive, elegant appearance. The plant has a robust root system that helps it thrive in moist, shaded environments. Its fronds are typically light green and can grow quite large, contributing to its ornamental value.

This variety is valued in horticulture for its ability to add a tropical ambiance to gardens and landscapes.
