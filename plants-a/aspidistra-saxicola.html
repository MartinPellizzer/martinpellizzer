Aspidistra saxicola

Aspidistra saxicola, commonly known as the rock aspidistra, is a perennial evergreen plant native to Japan.

It is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth, glossy surface. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in rocky or forested areas. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread slowly and form dense clumps over time.

Aspidistra saxicola is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as indoor plants.
