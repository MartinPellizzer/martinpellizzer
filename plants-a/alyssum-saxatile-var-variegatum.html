Alyssum saxatile var. variegatum

Alyssum saxatile var. variegatum, commonly known as the variegated rock alyssum, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features compact, mat-forming growth with small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving the plant a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its variegated foliage and early spring blooming habit.
