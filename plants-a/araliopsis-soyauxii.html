Araliopsis soyauxii

Araliopsis soyauxii, commonly known as the African climbing aralia, is a tropical plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and climbing habit. The plant features small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by red berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and greenhouse cultivation.
