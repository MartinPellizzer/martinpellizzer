Amaryllis stellaris

Amaryllis stellaris, commonly known as the star amaryllis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that come in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant features strap-like, grassy leaves that emerge from a bulbous stem. It blooms in late spring or early summer, producing clusters of showy flowers that attract pollinators.

Amaryllis stellaris is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for gardeners and indoor plant enthusiasts.
