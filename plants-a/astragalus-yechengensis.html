Astragalus yechengensis

Astragalus yechengensis, commonly known as Yecheng milkvetch, is a flowering plant species native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

It plays a role in soil stabilization and may have potential medicinal uses, though further research is needed.
