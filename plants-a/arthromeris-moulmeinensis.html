Arthromeris moulmeinensis

Arthromeris moulmeinensis, commonly known as the Moulmein orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, pendulous inflorescence and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical rainforests, often as an epiphyte on trees or rocks. Its leaves are elongated and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native humid environment.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
