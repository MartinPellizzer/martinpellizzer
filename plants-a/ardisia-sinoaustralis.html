Ardisia sinoaustralis

Ardisia sinoaustralis, commonly known as the Chinese beautyberry, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and emit a pleasant fragrance when crushed. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters during the spring, followed by bright red, berry-like fruits in the fall. These fruits are edible for birds and small mammals but are not typically consumed by humans.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland areas.
