Asperula glomerata subsp. turcomanica

Asperula glomerata subsp.

turcomanica, commonly known as the Turkestan huckleberry, is a shrub native to Central Asia. It features small, glossy, dark green leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, white flowers in spring.

The plant is adapted to rocky and arid environments, exhibiting drought tolerance. Its fruit is a small, red berry that ripens in late summer.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
