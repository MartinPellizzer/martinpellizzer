Aster taliangshanensis

Aster taliangshanensis, commonly known as the Taliangshan aster, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions, thriving in well-drained soils and cool climates. Its flowers are usually blue to violet in color, with ray florets surrounding a central disk.

Aster taliangshanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
