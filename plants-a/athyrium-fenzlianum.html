Athyrium fenzlianum

Athyrium fenzlianum, commonly known as the Fenzlian holly fern, is a species of fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forests and rocky areas. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Athyrium fenzlianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic foliage.
