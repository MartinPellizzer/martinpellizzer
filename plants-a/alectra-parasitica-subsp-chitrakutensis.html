Alectra parasitica subsp. chitrakutensis

chitrakutensis, commonly known as the Chitrakut ghostflower, is a hemiparasitic plant native to the Indian subcontinent. It belongs to the Orobanchaceae family and lacks chlorophyll, relying on host plants for nutrients.
