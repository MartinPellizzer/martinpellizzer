Anacampseros tomentosa var. margaretae

Anacampseros tomentosa var. margaretae, commonly known as the Margaretae Anacampseros, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense, woolly layer of fine hairs, giving it a distinctive, soft appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a subtle ornamental touch. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires ample sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
