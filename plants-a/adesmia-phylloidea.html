Adesmia phylloidea

Adesmia phylloidea, commonly known as the leafy legume, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant has trifoliate leaves with three leaflets, and its flowers are typically small and clustered. It is often found in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Adesmia phylloidea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
