Arachnorchis ampla

Arachnorchis ampla, commonly known as the large spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spider-like appearance, with long, slender petals and sepals that resemble the legs of a spider. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understoreys. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a single, prominent lip that is often darker.

Arachnorchis ampla is a rare and endangered species, requiring specific environmental conditions for successful growth and reproduction.
