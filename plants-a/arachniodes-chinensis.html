Arachniodes chinensis

Arachniodes chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese spider fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its intricate, lacy fronds that resemble spider webs, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.

The fronds are usually bi- or tri-pinnate, with a distinctive appearance that makes it a popular ornamental plant.
