Asaphes nepalensis

Asaphes nepalensis, commonly known as the Nepal asparagus, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asparagaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Nepal and adjacent areas in the Himalayas. The plant is characterized by its slender, succulent stems and small, grass-like leaves. It produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers during the spring season.

Asaphes nepalensis is often found growing in rocky or well-drained soils at elevations ranging from 1,500 to 3,500 meters.
