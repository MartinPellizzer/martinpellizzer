Aglaonema philippinense f. longifolium

Aglaonema philippinense f. longifolium, commonly known as the long-leaved philippine aglaonema, is a striking ornamental plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its vibrant, variegated foliage that displays shades of green, white, and pink. The plant features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are often longer than they are wide, giving it a unique appearance. It thrives in low-light conditions and is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its foliage remains colorful throughout the year, adding aesthetic value to any space.
