Astilbe × arendsii

Astilbe × arendsii, commonly known as the Arendsii astilbe, is a hybrid perennial plant native to Japan.

It is characterized by its upright, branching stems that bear dense, plume-like clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The foliage is typically dark green and often has a slight sheen, adding visual interest even when the plant is not in bloom. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

This plant is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to add texture and color to shaded landscapes.
