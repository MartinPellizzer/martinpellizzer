Agathosma perdita

Agathosma perdita, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a small, flowering plant native to the Western Cape region of South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers and narrow, aromatic leaves. The plant is typically found in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Agathosma perdita is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
