Archontophoenix alexandrae

Archontophoenix alexandrae, commonly known as the Alexandra palm, is a striking evergreen tree native to the island of Mauritius.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach up to 4 meters in length. The plant thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil, often growing in coastal areas or near freshwater sources.

Its ornamental value has made it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical and subtropical regions around the world.
