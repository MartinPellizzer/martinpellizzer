Athenaea herbacea

Athenaea herbacea, commonly known as the slender athenaea, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with narrow, linear leaves and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens or as a border plant.

Athenaea herbacea is also noted for its resilience to drought and mild frost.
