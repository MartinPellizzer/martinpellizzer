Archidendron merrillii

Archidendron merrillii, commonly known as the Philippine flame tree, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its striking red or orange-pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces flat, bean-like pods that contain seeds.

It thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.
