Aerides brookei

Aerides brookei, commonly known as the Brooke's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Aerides brookei is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
