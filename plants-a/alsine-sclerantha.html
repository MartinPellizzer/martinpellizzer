Alsine sclerantha

Alsine sclerantha, commonly known as the sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its small, showy flowers that bloom in shades of pink and purple.
