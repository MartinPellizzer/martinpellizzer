Achillea × huber-morathii

Achillea × huber-morathii, commonly known as the Huber-Morath yarrow, is a hybrid perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its finely divided, gray-green foliage and flat, showy clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
