Athanasia gyrosa

Athanasia gyrosa, commonly known as the spiral everlasting flower, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, rosette-like growth pattern and fleshy, green leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powder. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. Athanasia gyrosa is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique spiral arrangement of leaves and long-lasting blooms make it a visually striking addition to any collection.
