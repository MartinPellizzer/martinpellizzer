Antirrhinum hispanicum var. bolosii

Antirrhinum hispanicum var. bolosii, commonly known as the Bolosii snapdragon, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

Its ornamental value and adaptability make it a popular choice among gardeners and botanists alike.
