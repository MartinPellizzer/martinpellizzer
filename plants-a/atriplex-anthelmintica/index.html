Atriplex Anthelmintica

Atriplex anthelmintica is a hardy shrub known for its medicinal and ecological benefits. It thrives in arid and semi-arid regions, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. This plant has been traditionally used for its ability to expel intestinal worms, hence its name. Its salt-tolerant nature makes it valuable for soil stabilization and rehabilitation. Atriplex anthelmintica remains an important species in both traditional medicine and environmental conservation.
