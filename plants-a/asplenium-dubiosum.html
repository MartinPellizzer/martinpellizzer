Asplenium dubiosum

Asplenium dubiosum, commonly known as the doubt fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its spore-producing structures, called sori, are arranged in a distinctive pattern along the undersides of the leaves.

Asplenium dubiosum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
