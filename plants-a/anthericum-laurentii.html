Anthericum laurentii

Anthericum laurentii, commonly known as the laurentii fern, is a striking ornamental plant native to southern Europe.

It features delicate, lacy fronds that emerge from a short, rhizomatous stem, creating a graceful, upright appearance. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and prefers well-draining, fertile soil. Its foliage is evergreen, providing year-round visual interest in gardens.

Anthericum laurentii is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in woodland gardens or as a ground cover.
