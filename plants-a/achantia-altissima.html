Achantia altissima

Achantia altissima, commonly known as the tall goldenweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant is typically found in arid and semi-arid regions, thriving in sandy or rocky soils. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Achantia altissima is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
