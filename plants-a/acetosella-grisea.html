Acetosella grisea

Acetosella grisea, commonly known as grayed sorrel, is a low-growing perennial plant belonging to the Primulaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, heart-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a fine, grayish-white pubescence. Acetosella grisea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
