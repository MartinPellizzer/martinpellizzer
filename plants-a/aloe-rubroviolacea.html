Aloe rubroviolacea

Aloe rubroviolacea, commonly known as the red-violet aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings along the edges. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with showy, violet or red flowers. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Its unique coloration and compact growth make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
