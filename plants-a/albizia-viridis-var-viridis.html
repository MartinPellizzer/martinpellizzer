Albizia viridis var. viridis

Albizia viridis var. viridis, commonly known as the green albizia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features pinnately compound leaves that are typically green on both sides, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant yellow flowers arranged in racemes, which bloom in the spring. Its root system is fibrous and can help in soil stabilization.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
