Amphoradenium hymenophylloides

Amphoradenium hymenophylloides, commonly known as the silver leaf euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a branching stem with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers during the warmer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil to thrive.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
