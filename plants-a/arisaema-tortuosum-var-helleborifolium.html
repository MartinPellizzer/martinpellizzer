Arisaema tortuosum var. helleborifolium

Arisaema tortuosum var. helleborifolium, commonly known as the Helleborine Arisaema, is a member of the Araceae family and is native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its unique, often twisted inflorescence, which consists of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant produces showy, waxy flowers that are typically green or white in color, with a distinct, often fragrant odor. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped and can be quite large, contributing to its ornamental value.

This species is often cultivated for its striking appearance and is adapted to cool, moist, shaded environments.
