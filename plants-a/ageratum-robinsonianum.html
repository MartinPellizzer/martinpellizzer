Ageratum robinsonianum

Ageratum robinsonianum, commonly known as the Mexican ageratum, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

Ageratum robinsonianum is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in warm, sunny conditions.
