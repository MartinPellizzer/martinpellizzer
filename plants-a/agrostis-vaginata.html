Agrostis vaginata

Agrostis vaginata, commonly known as the slender bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows, wetlands, and coastal areas. The plant has slender, often tufted stems that are usually less than 30 cm in height. Its leaves are narrow, flat, and slightly hairy, with a bluish-green color.

The inflorescence consists of spikelets that are arranged in a panicle, producing small, wind-pollinated flowers.
