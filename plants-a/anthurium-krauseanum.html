Anthurium krauseanum

Anthurium krauseanum, commonly known as the Krause's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are deep green with a velvety texture. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its spadix, which is a unique floral structure typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium krauseanum is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
