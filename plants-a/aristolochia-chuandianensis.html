Aristolochia chuandianensis

Aristolochia chuandianensis, commonly known as the Yunnan pipe vine, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is characterized by its unique, tubular-shaped flowers that resemble pipes. The plant produces heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and is often found in moist, shaded environments. Its flowers are typically greenish-yellow with a strong, spicy odor, which attracts specific pollinators.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
