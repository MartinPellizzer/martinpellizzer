Anthurium amethystinum

Anthurium amethystinum, commonly known as the Purple Velvet Anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its velvety, deep purple spathe that surrounds a bright yellow spadix, creating a dramatic visual contrast. The plant produces large, heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, often with a waxy texture. It thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and relatively easy care make it a sought-after ornamental plant.
