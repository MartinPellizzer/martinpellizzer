Astragalus achezaensis

Astragalus achezaensis, commonly known as the Acheza milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

Astragalus achezaensis plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local ecosystems in its native habitat.
