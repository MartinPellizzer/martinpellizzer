Atocion lerchenfeldianum var. macedonicum

Atocion lerchenfeldianum var. macedonicum, commonly known as the Macedonian bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Balkan region.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer.
