Ardisia vidalii var. orbicularis

Ardisia vidalii var. orbicularis, commonly known as the orbicular ardisia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It features glossy, dark green, oval-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a dense, bushy form. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers followed by bright red berries, which are attractive to birds. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value comes from its foliage and seasonal floral display.
