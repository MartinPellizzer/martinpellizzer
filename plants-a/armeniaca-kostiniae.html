Armeniaca kostiniae

Armeniaca kostiniae, commonly known as the Kostinian apricot, is a rare and ancient species native to the mountainous regions of Armenia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and oval-shaped fruits. The plant is known for its hardy nature, thriving in rocky and arid environments. Its fruit, though not widely cultivated, is valued for its unique flavor and nutritional content.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species requiring conservation efforts.
