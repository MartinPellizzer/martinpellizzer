Aizoon stellatum

Aizoon stellatum, commonly known as starflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its star-shaped, violet-blue flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils. It is a perennial species that thrives in full sun and well-drained conditions.

Aizoon stellatum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
