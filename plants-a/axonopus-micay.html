Axonopus micay

Axonopus micay, commonly known as the narrowleaf axonopus, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments. The plant has slender, erect stems with small, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its inflorescence consists of compact, spike-like clusters of tiny flowers.

Axonopus micay is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping or as a ground cover.
