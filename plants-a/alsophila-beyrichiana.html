Alsophila beyrichiana

Alsophila beyrichiana, commonly known as the Beyrich's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant has a distinctive crown of compound leaves that can reach several meters in height. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.
