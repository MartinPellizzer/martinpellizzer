Alsophila kingii

Alsophila kingii, commonly known as the King's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feathery, compound fronds. The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is known for its slow growth and longevity. Its fronds are typically green and can reach lengths of up to 3 meters.
