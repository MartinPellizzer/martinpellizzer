Antidesma sassandrae

Antidesma sassandrae, commonly known as the African star fruit or African yellow star.

It is a shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa, characterized by its opposite, elliptical leaves that are often yellowish-green in color. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by bright red, fleshy berries that are edible. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in secondary forests and along riverbanks.

Antidesma sassandrae is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
