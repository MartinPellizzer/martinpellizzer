Alphitonia whitei

Alphitonia whitei, commonly known as the white alphitonia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows to a height of about 5 meters. The plant features glossy, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. Its flowers are small, white, and clustered in dense inflorescences, often blooming in spring.

Alphitonia whitei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly used in landscaping due to its hardiness and adaptability.
