Aristea pauciflora

Aristea pauciflora, commonly known as the few-flowered aristea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue to purple in color, arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
