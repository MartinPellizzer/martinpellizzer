Aconitum stylosum var. stylosum

Aconitum stylosum var. stylosum, commonly known as the Pacific Giant Bluebell, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It features showy, spurred blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has a tall, erect stem with palmately divided, serrated leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Aconitum stylosum var. stylosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native gardens.
