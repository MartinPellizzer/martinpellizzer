Ajania globularia

Ajania globularia, commonly known as the globular ajania, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and dense foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its unique form and ornamental value, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
