Armeria macloviana

Armeria macloviana, commonly known as the sea thrift, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is a member of the carnation family (Caryophyllaceae) and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. Armeria macloviana is often used in rock gardens and coastal landscapes due to its hardiness and ornamental value.
