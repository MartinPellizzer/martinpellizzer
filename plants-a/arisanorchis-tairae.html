Arisanorchis tairae

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in late spring. The plant has a short rhizome and produces a single, upright stem with several lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow with intricate patterns, and it thrives in moist, shaded forest environments.
