Anthoxanthum odoratum f. villosum

Anthoxanthum odoratum f. villosum, commonly known as the sweet vernal grass, is a variant of the sweet vernal grass species.

It is a low-growing perennial grass that typically thrives in sunny, well-drained soils. The plant is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and its distinctive yellow-green inflorescence. Its leaves are usually covered with fine, soft hairs, giving it a velvety texture.

This form is often found in meadows and along roadsides, contributing to the ecological diversity of these habitats.
