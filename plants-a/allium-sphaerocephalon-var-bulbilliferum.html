Allium sphaerocephalon var. bulbilliferum

Allium sphaerocephalon var. bulbilliferum, commonly known as the globe onion, is a perennial bulbous plant native to Central Asia.

It features a rounded, bulbous stem with dense, grass-like leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces a spherical, clustered inflorescence composed of small, white to pale pink flowers. Its bulbs are edible and have a mild, sweet flavor, often used in culinary applications.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
