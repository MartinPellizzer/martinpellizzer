Aechmea humilis

Aechmea humilis, commonly known as the silver vase plant, is a species of bromeliad native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup, which collects water and nutrients. The plant produces striking inflorescences with colorful flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. Aechmea humilis thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its unique structure and ornamental value make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
