Anthurium muyunense

Anthurium muyunense, commonly known as the Muyunense Arrowhead, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, which is characterized by its unique inflorescence known as a spadix, surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. The plant produces striking, colorful spathe that can range from red to orange, often with a contrasting green or yellow base. Its leaves are typically arrow-shaped, with a glossy, dark green surface and prominent veins.

Anthurium muyunense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
