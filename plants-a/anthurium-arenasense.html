Anthurium arenasense

Anthurium arenasense, commonly known as the Arenesense Philodendron, is a tropical plant native to the Andean region of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence composed of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves with a waxy surface and dark green veins. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
