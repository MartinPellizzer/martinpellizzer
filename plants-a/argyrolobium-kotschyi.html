Argyrolobium kotschyi

Argyrolobium kotschyi, commonly known as the silver globular bush, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its rounded, silver-gray foliage that reflects sunlight. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the rainy season. It has a deep root system that allows it to thrive in poor, well-drained soils.
