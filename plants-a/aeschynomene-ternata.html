Aeschynomene ternata

Aeschynomene ternata, commonly known as smallleaf desmodium, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in both full sun and partial shade. It has been used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

Aeschynomene ternata is also valued for its potential in agricultural systems due to its nitrogen-fixing ability.
