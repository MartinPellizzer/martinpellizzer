Avena jahandiezii

Avena jahandiezii, commonly known as the Iranian oat, is a rare and endemic grass species native to Iran.

It is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically thrives in rocky or semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue that helps it conserve water.
