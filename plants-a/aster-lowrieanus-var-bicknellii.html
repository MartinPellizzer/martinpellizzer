Aster lowrieanus var. bicknellii

Aster lowrieanus var. bicknellii, commonly known as the Bicknell's aster, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the northeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and forest edges. The plant features showy, lavender to white composite flowers that bloom in late summer to early fall. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
