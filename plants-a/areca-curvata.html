Areca curvata

Areca curvata, commonly known as the curved areca palm, is a species of palm native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, arching fronds that give it a graceful appearance. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical climates, thriving in well-drained soil and full sunlight. Its stems are often used in traditional crafts, and the plant is valued for its ornamental beauty.

Areca curvata is also sometimes cultivated for its potential medicinal properties.
