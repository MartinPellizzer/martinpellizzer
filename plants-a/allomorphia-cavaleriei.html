Allomorphia cavaleriei

Allomorphia cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese starflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It features a basal rosette of lanceolate, glaucous leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped, pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Allomorphia cavaleriei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
