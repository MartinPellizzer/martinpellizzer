Aloinopsis peersii

Aloinopsis peersii, commonly known as the Cape Town aloes, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, typical of arid environment adaptations. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow-orange flowers during the summer months. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, requiring minimal water.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
