Acosta argentea

Acosta argentea, commonly known as silver acosta, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Acosta argentea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
