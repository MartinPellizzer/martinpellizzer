Aponogeton wolfgangianus

Aponogeton wolfgangianus, commonly known as the Wolfgang's arrowhead, is a striking aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, narrow leaves that resemble arrowheads, often floating on the water's surface or partially submerged. The plant produces strikingly beautiful white or pink flowers that bloom above the water, attracting pollinators. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments and is often cultivated in ponds and water gardens.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both ornamental and aquatic horticulture.
