Aristida behriana

Aristida behriana, commonly known as the silver grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces slender, spike-like inflorescences that are often adorned with tiny, pale flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
