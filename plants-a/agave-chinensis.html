Agave chinensis

Agave chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese agave, is a succulent plant native to China.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped growth pattern and thick, fleshy leaves that are often blue-green in color. The plant produces a tall, branched inflorescence with small, white to greenish flowers. It is adapted to arid environments and has a slow growth rate.

Agave chinensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
