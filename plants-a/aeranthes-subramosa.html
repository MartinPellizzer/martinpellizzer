Aeranthes subramosa

Aeranthes subramosa, commonly known as the slender-branch orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, branching stems and delicate, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy habitats and prefers a dry to semi-shaded environment. Its leaves are linear and often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its unique appearance.

Aeranthes subramosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant blooms.
