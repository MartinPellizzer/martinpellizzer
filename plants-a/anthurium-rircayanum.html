Anthurium rircayanum

Anthurium rircayanum, commonly known as the Red-veined Anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with deep red veins. The plant produces a spadix, a type of inflorescence, which is surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium rircayanum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
