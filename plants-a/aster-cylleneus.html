Aster cylleneus

Aster cylleneus, commonly known as the purple coneflower, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, violet or purple flowers with a central disk surrounded by ray florets. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for native gardens.

Aster cylleneus is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
