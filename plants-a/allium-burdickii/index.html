Allium Burdickii

Allium burdickii is a rare and striking plant native to the western United States. It belongs to the onion family and is known for its distinctive purple and white flower clusters. This species thrives in rocky, arid environments and is often found in mountainous regions. Due to its limited range and habitat specificity, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique botanical treasure.
