Arundarbor longinodes

Arundarbor longinodes, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to the temperate regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stem and lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Arundarbor longinodes thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.
