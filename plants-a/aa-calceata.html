Aa calceata

Aa calceata, commonly known as the Hawaiian rock cress, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils. It produces small, white or yellow flowers that bloom in early spring.
