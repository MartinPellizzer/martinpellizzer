Ainsliaea reflexa var. subalpina

Ainsliaea reflexa var. subalpina, commonly known as the Alpine Ainsliaea, is a perennial herb native to the high-altitude regions of the western United States.

The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, helping to reduce water loss in its harsh environment.
