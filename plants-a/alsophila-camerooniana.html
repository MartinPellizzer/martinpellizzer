Alsophila camerooniana

Alsophila camerooniana, commonly known as the Cameroon tree fern, is a member of the Cyatheaceae family.

It is a tall, evergreen tree fern native to tropical Africa, particularly Cameroon and the Congo. The plant features a stout, unbranched trunk covered in persistent, fibrous roots. Its fronds are pinnate, with a dark green, leathery texture and a long, tapering shape.

Alsophila camerooniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded, humid environments.
