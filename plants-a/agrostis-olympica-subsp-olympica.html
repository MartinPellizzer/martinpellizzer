Agrostis olympica subsp. olympica

Agrostis olympica subsp.

olympica, commonly known as the Olympic bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia. It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and slender, dark green leaves.

The plant typically forms dense mats and is often found in moist, well-drained soils, such as those in meadows and along stream banks. Its inflorescence consists of a single spikelet, which is often curved and covered in short, bristly hairs.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and turfgrass applications.
