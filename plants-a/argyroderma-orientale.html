Argyroderma orientale

Argyroderma orientale, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage and bright yellow flowers. The plant produces small, daisy-like blooms that are typically arranged in compact clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators.
