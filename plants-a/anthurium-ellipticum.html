Anthurium ellipticum

Anthurium ellipticum, commonly known as the elliptic anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy surface. The plant produces striking spadix inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can range from red to orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium ellipticum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying properties.
