Asplenium auritum f. pendens

Asplenium auritum f. pendens, commonly known as the hanging spleenwort, is a fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, pendulous fronds that drape gracefully from its growth habit. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as rock crevices or soil banks. Its leaves are lanceolate with a distinctive ear-like shape at the base, giving it its species name "auritum.".

This fern is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal in botanical gardens.
