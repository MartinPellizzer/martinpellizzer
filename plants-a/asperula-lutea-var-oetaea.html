Asperula lutea var. oetaea

Asperula lutea var. oetaea, commonly known as the Oetaea shrub, is a perennial evergreen shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features small, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers in early spring, which are borne on short pedicels. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal areas.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and seasonal floral display.
