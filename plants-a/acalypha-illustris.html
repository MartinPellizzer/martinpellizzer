Acalypha illustris

Acalypha illustris, commonly known as the silverleaf acalypha, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking silver-white leaves, which are covered in fine, dense hairs. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Acalypha illustris is also valued for its ability to tolerate poor soil conditions and partial shade.
