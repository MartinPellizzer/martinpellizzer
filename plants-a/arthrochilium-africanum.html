Arthrochilium africanum

Arthrochilium africanum, commonly known as the African rock orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit and features a single, erect stem bearing a single, showy flower. The plant has a fibrous root system and thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. Its flowers are typically greenish-yellow with a distinctive red or purple lip.

Arthrochilium africanum is adapted to arid conditions and is often found in mountainous or rocky habitats.
