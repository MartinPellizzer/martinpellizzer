Avena volgensis

Avena volgensis, commonly known as the slender oat, is a species of grass native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and panicle-like inflorescence. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and is often found in moist, well-drained soils. Its seeds are small and oval, with a rough surface, and are often used as forage for livestock.

The species is valued for its adaptability and ecological benefits in various habitats.
