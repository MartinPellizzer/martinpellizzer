Anonidium usambarense

Anonidium usambarense, commonly known as the Usambarensis Anon, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It belongs to the Anonaceae family, which includes species known for their aromatic leaves and fruits. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a waxy texture, while its flowers are small and clustered.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds, contributing to its propagation in the wild.
