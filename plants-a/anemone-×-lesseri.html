Anemone × lesseri

Anemone × lesseri, commonly known as the hybrid anemone, is a perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its showy, white to pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm, featuring basal leaves that are palmately lobed and hairy. Its flowers consist of numerous petal-like sepals and numerous stamens, creating a distinctive and delicate appearance.

Anemone × lesseri thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or meadow environments.
