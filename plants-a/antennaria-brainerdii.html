Antennaria brainerdii

Antennaria brainerdii, commonly known as Brainerd's mouse-ear, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of the western United States.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves.
