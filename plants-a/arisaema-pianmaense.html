Arisaema pianmaense

Arisaema pianmaense, commonly known as the Pianma arrowhead, is a herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. It features arrowhead-shaped leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
