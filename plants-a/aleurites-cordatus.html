Aleurites cordatus

Aleurites cordatus, commonly known as the New Zealand nut tree, is a small evergreen tree native to New Zealand.

It has simple, alternate, ovate to elliptical leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter in color underneath. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into woody capsules containing edible seeds. The seeds are encased in a hard shell and are often used for ornamental or practical purposes.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to temperate climates.
