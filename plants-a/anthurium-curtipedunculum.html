Anthurium curtipedunculum

Anthurium curtipedunculum, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a velvety texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can range from red to orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium curtipedunculum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
