Artemisia Gmelinii Var. Intermedia

Artemisia gmelinii var. intermedia is a hardy perennial shrub native to the arid and semi-arid regions of central Asia. It is known for its aromatic foliage and resilience to harsh environmental conditions. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and is commonly found in steppe and desert habitats. Its silver-green leaves and compact growth make it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping. Due to its adaptability, it is also used in traditional medicine and as a source of essential oils.
