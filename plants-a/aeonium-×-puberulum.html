Aeonium × puberulum

Aeonium × puberulum, commonly known as the Necklace Aeonium, is a hybrid succulent plant belonging to the Crassulaceae family.

It is characterized by its rosettes of fleshy, thick leaves that often display a striking gradient of colors, ranging from green to blue and red. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season. Native to the Canary Islands, it thrives in well-draining soil and full sunlight.

Aeonium × puberulum is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
