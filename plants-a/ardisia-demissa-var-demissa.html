Ardisia demissa var. demissa

Ardisia demissa var. demissa, commonly known as the glossy wood-sorrel, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features elliptical, glossy dark green leaves that are glossy and slightly waxy to the touch. The plant produces small, bell-shaped white flowers followed by bright red berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in shaded to partially sunny conditions and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of cultivation, it is often used in landscaping and as a container plant.
