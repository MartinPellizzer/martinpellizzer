Aerides bicolor

Aerides bicolor, commonly known as the bicolor orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its striking two-colored flowers, typically featuring a white lip and a pink or red dorsal sepal. The plant has elongated, strap-like leaves and produces fragrant blooms during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its unique coloration makes it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
