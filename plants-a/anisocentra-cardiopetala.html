Anisocentra cardiopetala

Anisocentra cardiopetala, commonly known as the heart-leaved daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically blooming in spring. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
