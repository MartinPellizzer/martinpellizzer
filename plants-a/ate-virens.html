Ate virens

Ate virens, commonly known as the green spider orchid, is a species of orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its unique greenish-yellow flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in heathlands and open woodlands. Ate virens has a complex root system that helps it survive in nutrient-poor environments.

Its pollination is primarily facilitated by specific species of wasps.
