Anotis rotundifolia

Anotis rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leafed anotis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has round, leathery leaves that are usually clustered at the base, giving it a compact appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Anotis rotundifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
