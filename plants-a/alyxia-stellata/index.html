Alyxia Stellata

Alyxia stellata is a striking flowering plant native to arid regions of the southwestern United States. It is known for its clusters of star-shaped, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. This shrub thrives in rocky soils and is well adapted to hot, dry climates. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for xeriscaping and drought-resistant gardens. Despite its beauty, Alyxia stellata remains relatively unknown to the general public.
