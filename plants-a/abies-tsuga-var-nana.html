Abies tsuga var. nana

Abies tsuga var. nana, commonly known as the dwarf hemlock, is a compact variety of the hemlock tree.

It is characterized by its slow growth and dense, conical shape, making it suitable for smaller garden spaces. The leaves are scale-like and dark green, providing a lush, evergreen appearance throughout the year. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to create a dense, textured foliage effect.
