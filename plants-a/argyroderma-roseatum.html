Argyroderma roseatum

Argyroderma roseatum, commonly known as the silver-leafed rose.

It is a succulent plant native to southern Africa, characterized by its fleshy, silver-green leaves that are covered in a fine, waxy coating. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
