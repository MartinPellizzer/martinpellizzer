Achillea setacea subsp. asiatica

Achillea setacea subsp.

asiatica, commonly known as the Asian yarrow, is a perennial herb native to parts of Asia. It features finely divided, gray-green leaves that are linear to lanceolate in shape.

The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in flat-topped inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to gardens.

Its aromatic foliage and long blooming period make it a popular choice for ornamental and medicinal purposes.
