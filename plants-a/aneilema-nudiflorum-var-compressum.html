Aneilema nudiflorum var. compressum

Aneilema nudiflorum var. compressum, commonly known as the compact aneilema, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and dark green, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in tropical gardens.
