Aspidium atomarium

Aspidium atomarium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf water fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Athyriaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that are typically less than 30 centimeters in length. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found near streams or in damp woodlands. Its spores are produced in clusters on the underside of its fronds, a common feature among ferns.

Aspidium atomarium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable humidity and light conditions.
