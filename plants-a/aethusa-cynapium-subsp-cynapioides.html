Aethusa cynapium subsp. cynapioides

Aethusa cynapium subsp.

cynapioides, commonly known as the broadleaf cowbane, is a herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its umbellate inflorescence and aromatic compounds.
