Anthurium basirotundum

Anthurium basirotundum, commonly known as the round-leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a prominent midrib. The plant produces striking, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, often in shades of red or orange. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium basirotundum is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and unique floral structure.
