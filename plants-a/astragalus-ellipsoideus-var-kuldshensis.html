Astragalus ellipsoideus var. kuldshensis

Astragalus ellipsoideus var. kuldshensis, commonly known as Kuldshen's milkvetch, is a flowering plant species native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showing adaptations to drought conditions. It produces pods that contain small, hard seeds, which are often dispersed by wind.

This variety is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological significance in its native habitat.
