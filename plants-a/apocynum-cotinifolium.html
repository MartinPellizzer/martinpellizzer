Apocynum cotinifolium

Apocynum cotinifolium, commonly known as the Canadian milkweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family, which includes many plants with milky sap. The plant features opposite, heart-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers. Its milky latex is toxic and can cause skin irritation.

It is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands.
