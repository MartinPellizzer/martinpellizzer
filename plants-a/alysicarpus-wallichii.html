Alysicarpus wallichii

Alysicarpus wallichii, commonly known as the Indian borage, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to the Indian subcontinent and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant is characterized by its trailing growth habit, trifoliate leaves, and small blue or purple flowers. It produces distinctive fruit pods that are cylindrical and ribbed, containing several seeds.

Alysicarpus wallichii is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
