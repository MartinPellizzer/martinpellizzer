Arisaema nikoense var. brevicollum

Arisaema nikoense var. brevicollum, commonly known as the Japanese mountain arum, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant produces striking, showy flowers that are typically green or white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and botanical study.
