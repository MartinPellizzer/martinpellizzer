Aeonium × condei

Aeonium × condei, commonly known as the Sunset Aeonium, is a hybrid succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its compact rosette of thick, fleshy leaves that range in color from deep red to orange and green, depending on light exposure. The plant produces tall, branching flower stalks that bear yellow flowers in the spring. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with full sun.

Aeonium × condei is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
