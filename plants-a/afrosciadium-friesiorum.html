Afrosciadium friesiorum

Afrosciadium friesiorum, commonly known as the African starwood, is a member of the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and umbellate inflorescences.

It is native to southern Africa and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. Its roots and leaves are used in traditional medicine for their purported anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties.

Afrosciadium friesiorum is also valued as an ornamental plant for its delicate appearance and adaptability to various soil types.
