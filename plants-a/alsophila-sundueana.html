Alsophila sundueana

Alsophila sundueana, commonly known as the Sundue's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. Its rhizome system supports its growth, allowing it to reach significant heights over time.

The fronds are typically bioluminescent, adding a unique visual appeal to its natural habitat.
