Agathosma zwartbergensis

Agathosma zwartbergensis, commonly known as Zwartberg sage, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, needle-like leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils. It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Its essential oils are also valued in the perfume and aromatherapy industries.
