Atamosco cardinalis

Atamosco cardinalis, commonly known as the cardinal flameflower, is a striking tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and hummingbirds. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that provide a lush green contrast to its colorful blooms. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping in suitable regions.
