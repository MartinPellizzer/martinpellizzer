Aloe megalacantha subsp. megalacantha

Aloe megalacantha subsp.

megalacantha, commonly known as the giant aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its tall, spire-like growth and fleshy, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with white or yellowish markings.

The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellow-orange flowers, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its striking appearance and adaptability, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
