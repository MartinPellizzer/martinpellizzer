Arisaema consanguineum var. divaricatum

Arisaema consanguineum var. divaricatum, commonly known as the Chinese arrowhead, is a herbaceous plant native to China.

It features a short rhizome with a rosette of heart-shaped, arrowhead-like leaves. The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a spathe, a characteristic feature of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value and unique foliage make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and cultivated collections.
