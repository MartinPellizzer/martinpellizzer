Anemone keiskeana

Anemone keiskeana, commonly known as the Japanese anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It features showy, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has a short, upright stem with palmately divided, lobed leaves. Its flowers consist of a central cluster of pistillate flowers surrounded by showy stamens.

Anemone keiskeana prefers moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.
