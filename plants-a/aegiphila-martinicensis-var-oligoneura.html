Aegiphila martinicensis var. oligoneura

Aegiphila martinicensis var. oligoneura, commonly known as the Martinique heath, is a shrub native to the Caribbean island of Martinique.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features small, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces clusters of tiny, tubular flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to the humid, tropical climate of its native habitat.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
