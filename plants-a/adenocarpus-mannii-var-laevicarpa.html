Adenocarpus mannii var. laevicarpa

Adenocarpus mannii var. laevicarpa, commonly known as the smooth-fruited burr bush, is a perennial shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Leguminosae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces distinctive, woody, spiny pods that contain seeds enclosed in a fibrous covering. It is adapted to dry conditions and often grows in savanna and woodland habitats.

The species is valued for its potential in soil stabilization and as a source of traditional medicinal uses.
