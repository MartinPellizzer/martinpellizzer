Arenaria haitzeshanensis

Arenaria haitzeshanensis, commonly known as the Haitzeshan sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers during the spring and summer seasons. It thrives in rocky and well-drained soils, often found in alpine or subalpine environments.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
