Anamirta lemniscata

Anamirta lemniscata, commonly known as the sacred lotus of India, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, pinkish-white flowers. Anamirta lemniscata is also noted for its cultural and medicinal significance in traditional Indian practices.
