Anthurium obtusilobum

Anthurium obtusilobum, commonly known as the broad-lobed anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by colorful spatthae, typically in shades of red or orange. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium obtusilobum is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and unique floral structure.
