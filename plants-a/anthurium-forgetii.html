Anthurium forgetii

Anthurium forgetii, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense, velvety texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are specialized inflorescences that resemble a cluster of small flowers. Its foliage is often green with deep veins, and it thrives in high humidity and indirect light.

Anthurium forgetii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
