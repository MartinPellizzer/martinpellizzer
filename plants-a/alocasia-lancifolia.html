Alocasia lancifolia

Alocasia lancifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf alocasia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, elongated, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant has a rhizomatous root system, which helps it anchor and absorb nutrients from the soil. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, fertile soil.

Alocasia lancifolia is popular as a houseplant due to its striking foliage and air-purifying properties.
