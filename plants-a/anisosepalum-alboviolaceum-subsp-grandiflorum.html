Anisosepalum alboviolaceum subsp. grandiflorum

Anisosepalum alboviolaceum subsp.

grandiflorum, commonly known as the white-violet anisosepalum, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its striking, violet-tinged white flowers that bloom in clusters.

The plant features elongated, narrow leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
