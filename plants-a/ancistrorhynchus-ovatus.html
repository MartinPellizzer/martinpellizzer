Ancistrorhynchus ovatus

Ancistrorhynchus ovatus, commonly known as the ovate ancistrus, is a species of armored catfish native to the rivers of South America.

The fish has a broad, flat head and a single, continuous dorsal fin, which is typical of the Loricariidae family. Its coloration is usually a dark brown or olive-green with lighter markings, aiding in camouflage within its aquatic environment.
