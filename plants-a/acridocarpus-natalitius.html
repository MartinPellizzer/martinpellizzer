Acridocarpus natalitius

Acridocarpus natalitius, commonly known as Natal flame tree, is a tropical tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, flame-like red flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. The plant has pinnate leaves and produces small, round fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for ornamental planting in warm climates.

Due to its vibrant appearance, it is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and landscapes.
