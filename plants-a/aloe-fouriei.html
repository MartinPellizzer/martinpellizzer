Aloe fouriei

Aloe fouriei, commonly known as the White Aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are pale green to white in color, covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces tall, branched flower stalks that bear yellowish-green flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Aloe fouriei is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in herbal medicine.
