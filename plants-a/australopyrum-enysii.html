Australopyrum enysii

Australopyrum enysii, commonly known as the Enys's grass, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and subalpine regions.
