Agrostis tateyamensis

Agrostis tateyamensis, commonly known as the Tateyama bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Japan.

It is characterized by its fine, grass-like foliage and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and mountainous regions. It has narrow, elongated leaves with a bluish-green color and produces small, wind-pollinated flowers.

Agrostis tateyamensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in its native habitat.
