Aphloia theiformis var. angustissima

Aphloia theiformis var. angustissima, commonly known as the narrow-leaved angel's trumpets, is a tropical shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant has opposite, lanceolate leaves that are typically narrow and pointed. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
