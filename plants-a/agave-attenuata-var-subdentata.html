Agave attenuata var. subdentata

Agave attenuata var. subdentata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved agave, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It features narrow, spiny leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a distinctive subdenticulate margin. The plant produces an erect, branched inflorescence with yellowish-green flowers. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
