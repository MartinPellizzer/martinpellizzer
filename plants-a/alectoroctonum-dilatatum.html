Alectoroctonum dilatatum

Alectoroctonum dilatatum, commonly known as the broadleaf arrowhead, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive arrowhead base, and its stems are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers that are clustered in spikes. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in wetlands or along streambanks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique species within the Araceae family.
