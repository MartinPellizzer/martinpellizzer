Artemisia dracunculus f. humilis

Artemisia dracunculus f. humilis, commonly known as the dwarf tarragon, is a compact variant of the common tarragon.

It is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia. The plant features silver-gray, aromatic leaves and small yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It is valued for its culinary use, particularly in herbal teas and as a seasoning.

Its low-growing habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and borders.
