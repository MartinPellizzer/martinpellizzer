Astronidium puberulum

Astronidium puberulum, commonly known as the velvety star cactus, is a succulent plant native to South America.

It features a cylindrical stem covered in soft, white hairs that give it a velvety appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
