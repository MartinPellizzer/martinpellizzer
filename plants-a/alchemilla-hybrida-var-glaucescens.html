Alchemilla hybrida var. glaucescens

Alchemilla hybrida var. glaucescens, commonly known as the blue-stemmed lady's mantle, is a perennial plant native to Europe.

It features basal leaves that are broad, hairy, and often tinged with blue or silver hues. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and long blooming period.
