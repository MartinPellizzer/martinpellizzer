Anthurium iramireziae

Anthurium iramireziae, commonly known as the Iramirez's anthurium, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its foliage is broad and glossy, with leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length.

The spathe, which is the showy part of the flower, can vary in color from deep red to purple, often contrasting with the green spadix beneath.
