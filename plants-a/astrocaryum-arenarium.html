Astrocaryum arenarium

Astrocaryum arenarium, commonly known as the red acuri or red murumuru, is a tropical tree native to the Amazon Basin.

It is characterized by its tall, straight trunk and large, palmate leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces a distinctive red, fibrous fruit that contains a valuable oil used in cosmetics and skincare products. Its ecological importance includes providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.

The tree is also valued for its sustainable use of its oil, which has economic and environmental benefits.
