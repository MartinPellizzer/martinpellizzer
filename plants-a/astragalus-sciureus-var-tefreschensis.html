Astragalus sciureus var. tefreschensis

Astragalus sciureus var. tefreschensis, commonly known as the Tefreschensis milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing adaptations to drought conditions.

Its root system is notable for its ability to fix nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility in its native habitat.
