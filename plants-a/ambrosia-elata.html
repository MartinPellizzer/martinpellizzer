Ambrosia elata

Ambrosia elata, commonly known as the giant ragweed, is an annual herbaceous plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in spherical clusters, which are wind-pollinated. Its leaves are alternate, heart-shaped, and have a rough texture.

Ambrosia elata is known for its allergenic properties, contributing to ragweed pollen allergies.
