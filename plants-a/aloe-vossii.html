Aloe vossii

Aloe vossii, commonly known as the Voss aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, often with a waxy coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Aloe vossii is valued both for its ornamental appearance and its potential medicinal properties.
