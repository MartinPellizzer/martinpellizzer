Aphelandra steyermarkii

Aphelandra steyermarkii, commonly known as the Steyermark's flame tree, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean and northern South America.

It is characterized by its striking, pendulous flowers that resemble flames, typically in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has large, glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and often have a waxy texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Aphelandra steyermarkii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant floral display.
