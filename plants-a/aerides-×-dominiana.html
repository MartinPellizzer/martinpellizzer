Aerides × dominiana

Aerides × dominiana, commonly known as the Dominica orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant features pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in tropical climates. Its leaves are usually broad and strap-like, arranged in a fan shape around the pseudobulbs.

This orchid is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
