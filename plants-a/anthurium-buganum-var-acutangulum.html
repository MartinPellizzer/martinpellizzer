Anthurium buganum var. acutangulum

Anthurium buganum var. acutangulum, commonly known as the spiny anthurium, is a tropical evergreen plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often dark green with a glossy surface and prominent veins. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental horticulture.
