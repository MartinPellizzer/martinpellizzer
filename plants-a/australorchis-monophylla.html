Australorchis monophylla

Australorchis monophylla, commonly known as the single-leaf orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its single, broad, lance-shaped leaf and produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or branches in humid, shaded environments. It has a pseudobulb structure that stores water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in seasonal conditions.

Its unique morphology and habitat make it a distinctive species within the orchid family.
