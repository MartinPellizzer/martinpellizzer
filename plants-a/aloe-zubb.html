Aloe zubb

Aloe zubb, commonly known as the Natal aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. The plant produces tall, branched flower stalks that bear yellow or orange flowers. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is used in traditional medicine and skincare products.

Aloe zubb is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight.
