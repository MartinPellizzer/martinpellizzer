Antidesma moritzii

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly Tanzania and Mozambique. The plant is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height, with smooth, pale grey bark. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptical, with a waxy surface.

The flowers are small and clustered in cup-like structures, typical of the Euphorbiaceae family.
