Amblyopogon balansae

Amblyopogon balansae, commonly known as the Balansae grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, Amblyopogon balansae is considered a rare and endangered species.
