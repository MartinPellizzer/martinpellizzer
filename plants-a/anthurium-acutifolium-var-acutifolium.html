Anthurium acutifolium var. acutifolium

Anthurium acutifolium var. acutifolium, commonly known as the sharp-leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathes that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with a contrasting green or white spathe. The plant features large, glossy, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Anthurium acutifolium var. acutifolium is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
