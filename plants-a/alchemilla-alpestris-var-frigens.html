Alchemilla alpestris var. frigens

Alchemilla alpestris var. frigens, commonly known as the alpine lady's mantle, is a low-growing perennial plant native to alpine regions of Europe.

It features silver-green, velvety leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, providing a striking visual contrast. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early summer, which are clustered in dense, rounded inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to cold, high-altitude environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine displays.
