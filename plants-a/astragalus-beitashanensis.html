Astragalus beitashanensis

Astragalus beitashanensis, commonly known as the Beitashan milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky or sandy soils. It plays a role in soil stabilization and is of interest for its potential medicinal properties.

Its seeds are small and typically dispersed by wind.
