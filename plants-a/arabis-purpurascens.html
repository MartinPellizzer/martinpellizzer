Arabis purpurascens

Arabis purpurascens, commonly known as the purple rockcress, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are small, white or pink, and appear in clusters, often with a slight purple tinge.

Arabis purpurascens is known for its hardy nature and ability to flourish in challenging environments.
