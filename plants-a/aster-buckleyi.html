Aster buckleyi

Aster buckleyi, commonly known as Buckley's aster, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and has narrow, grayish-green leaves. Its flowers are usually purple to blue in color and bloom in the summer months.

Buckley's aster is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance to local pollinators.
