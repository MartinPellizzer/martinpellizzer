Aralia stellata

Aralia stellata, commonly known as star aralia, is a flowering plant native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its compound leaves that resemble a star, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels, which are typical of the Araliaceae family. It thrives in shady to partially sunny conditions and is often found in moist, wooded areas.

Aralia stellata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscape design.
