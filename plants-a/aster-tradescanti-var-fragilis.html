Aster tradescanti var. fragilis

Aster tradescanti var. fragilis, commonly known as the fragile spider plant, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It features long, arching stems with clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The foliage is characterized by its delicate, grass-like leaves that are often variegated with shades of green and white. This plant thrives in well-draining soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens and hanging baskets.
