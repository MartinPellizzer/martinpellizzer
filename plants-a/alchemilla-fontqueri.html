Alchemilla fontqueri

Alchemilla fontqueri, commonly known as the rock alchemilla, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically basal, with a soft, fuzzy texture and a distinctive silver-green color.

Alchemilla fontqueri thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine environments.
