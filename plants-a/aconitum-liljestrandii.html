Aconitum liljestrandii

Aconitum liljestrandii, commonly known as the Swedish monk's hood, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Scandinavia.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers that resemble a hooded monk's robe. The plant features a tall stem with palmately divided leaves and produces numerous small, spurred flowers in late summer. Aconitum liljestrandii is adapted to cold climates and thrives in well-drained, moist soils.

Due to its toxic alkaloids, it is important to handle the plant with care.
