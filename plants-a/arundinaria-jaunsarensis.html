Arundinaria jaunsarensis

Arundinaria jaunsarensis, commonly known as the Jaunsar bamboo, is a species of grass native to the Himalayan regions of India.

It is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically green and covered in a fine layer of hair. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous areas with cool climates.

Arundinaria jaunsarensis plays an important role in local ecosystems and has traditional uses in construction and crafts.
