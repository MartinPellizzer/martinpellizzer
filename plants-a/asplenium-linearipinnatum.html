Asplenium linearipinnatum

Asplenium linearipinnatum, commonly known as the narrow-pinnate spleenwort, is a species of fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, linear-shaped leaves that are arranged in a pinnate pattern, giving it a distinctive appearance. This plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found on rocks or tree trunks in temperate regions. Its fronds are usually dark green and can reach lengths of up to 30 centimeters.

Asplenium linearipinnatum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens or as a houseplant.
