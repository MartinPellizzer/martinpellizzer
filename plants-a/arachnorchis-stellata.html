Arachnorchis stellata

Arachnorchis stellata, commonly known as the star orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped flowers, which are typically white or pale yellow with a red or purple lip. The plant grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest undergrowth or on rocky slopes. It has a slender, leafless stem that bears the flowering spike.

Arachnorchis stellata is a small plant, usually reaching only a few centimeters in height, and is known for its delicate and intricate floral structure.
