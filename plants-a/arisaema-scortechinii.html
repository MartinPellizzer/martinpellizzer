Arisaema scortechinii

Arisaema scortechinii, commonly known as the Chinese cobra lily, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its unique, hooded spathe that resembles a cobra's head. The plant produces a striking, spadix inflorescence that is often brightly colored. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

The botanical features of Arisaema scortechinii make it a distinctive and ornamental plant in horticulture.
