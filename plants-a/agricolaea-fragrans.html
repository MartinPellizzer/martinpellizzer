Agricolaea fragrans

Agricolaea fragrans, commonly known as the fragrant agricolaea, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, aromatic flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a sweet, spicy scent. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are elliptical and glossy, while its stems are covered in fine, white hairs.

Agricolaea fragrans is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits and is also cultivated for ornamental purposes.
