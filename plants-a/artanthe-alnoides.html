Artanthe alnoides

Artanthe alnoides, commonly known as the silverleaf artanthe, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, commonly referred to as the ice plant family. The plant is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, hairy structures that help reduce water loss. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in spring.

Artanthe alnoides thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and rocky habitats.
