Aeranthes curnowiana

Aeranthes curnowiana, commonly known as the Curnow's orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils. It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs and linear leaves.

Its flowers are usually pink or white, with a distinctive lip that is often adorned with intricate patterns.
