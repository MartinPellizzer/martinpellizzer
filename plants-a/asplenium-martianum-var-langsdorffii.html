Asplenium martianum var. langsdorffii

Asplenium martianum var. langsdorffii, commonly known as the Langsdorff's maidenhair fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are typically dark green and have a finely serrated margin. The plant produces small, spore-bearing structures called sori on the underside of its leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and rock gardens.
