Aristida plumosa var. intermedia

Aristida plumosa var. intermedia, commonly known as the silky grass or slender hairgrass, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Asia and the Americas.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage and delicate, feathery inflorescences that give it a soft, ornamental appearance. The plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats, such as marshes, swales, and along stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and slightly curled, contributing to its distinctive, airy look.

The species is valued in horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in wet conditions.
