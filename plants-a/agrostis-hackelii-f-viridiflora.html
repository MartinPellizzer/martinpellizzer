Agrostis hackelii f. viridiflora

Agrostis hackelii f. viridiflora, commonly known as the green-flowered meadowgrass, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and small, elongated leaves. The inflorescence consists of a panicle of spikelets, with the distinctive greenish-yellow floral bracts. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in temperate regions.

This subspecies is notable for its ornamental value and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.
