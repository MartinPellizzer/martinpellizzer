Aloe × engelbrechtii

Aloe × engelbrechtii, commonly known as the hybrid aloe or Engelbrecht's aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are thick and fleshy. The plant produces striking orange-red flowers on tall, branched stems, typically during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
