Abelia corymbosa

Abelia corymbosa, commonly known as the Chinese abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features glossy, ovate leaves that turn red in the fall, adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of pink and white during late spring to early summer. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for a variety of garden settings.

Abelia corymbosa is also valued for its adaptability and resistance to pests and diseases.
