Anthocleista amplexicaulis

Anthocleista amplexicaulis, commonly known as the climbing snakeroot, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and distinctive leaves that wrap around its stems. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by fleshy, orange-red berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its medicinal properties, it is valued in traditional African medicine for various therapeutic uses.
