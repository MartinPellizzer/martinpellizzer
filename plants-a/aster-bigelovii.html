Aster bigelovii

Aster bigelovii, commonly known as the Bigelov's aster, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in sagebrush steppe environments. It produces showy blue to purple flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Its foliage is usually gray-green with hairy surfaces, adapted to reduce water loss in arid conditions.
