Arundarbor multiplex

Arundarbor multiplex, commonly known as the silverleaf fern, is a perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features feathery, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth.

Arundarbor multiplex is often used in ornamental gardening for its elegant foliage and adaptability to various climates.
