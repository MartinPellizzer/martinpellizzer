Aloe × keayi

Aloe × keayi, commonly known as the hybrid aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple margins. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers, which bloom in the spring. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Aloe × keayi is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine.
