Anthurium kallunkiae

Anthurium kallunkiae, commonly known as the Kallunki anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a velvety texture. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its spadix, which is a unique feature of the Araceae family. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium kallunkiae is popular as a ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
