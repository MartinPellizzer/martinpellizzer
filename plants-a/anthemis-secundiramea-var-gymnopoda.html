Anthemis secundiramea var. gymnopoda

Anthemis secundiramea var. gymnopoda, commonly known as the smooth anthemis, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, yellowish-white composite flowers. The leaves are typically linear to lanceolate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface depending on the variety. This plant is native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa, where it thrives in well-drained soils.

It is often found in rocky or sandy habitats, demonstrating a high degree of adaptability to harsh environmental conditions.
