Aeschynomene martii

Aeschynomene martii, commonly known as Brazilian needlegrass, is a perennial grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences with pale green to brown flowers. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in disturbed soils.

Aeschynomene martii is valued for its ornamental appeal and use in erosion control.
