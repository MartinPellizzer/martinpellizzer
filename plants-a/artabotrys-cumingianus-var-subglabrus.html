Artabotrys cumingianus var. subglabrus

Artabotrys cumingianus var. subglabrus, commonly known as the smooth artabotrys, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in panicles. The leaves are simple, alternate, and ovate with a smooth surface, which gives the variety its "subglabrous" designation. The plant thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, shaded areas.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
