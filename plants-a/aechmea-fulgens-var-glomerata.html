Aechmea fulgens var. glomerata

Aechmea fulgens var. glomerata, commonly known as the Christmas Bromeliad, is a striking epiphytic plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red bracts that surround a dense cluster of small, white flowers, creating a visually striking display. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that collect rainwater, forming a central reservoir. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil with high humidity.

This variety is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its long-lasting floral display and ease of care.
