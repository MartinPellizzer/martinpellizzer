Arabis × romieuxii

Arabis × romieuxii, commonly known as the hybrid rockcress, is a perennial flowering plant that belongs to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit, with basal leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine environments. Its flowers are usually self-fertile, and it produces small, round pods that contain several seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in challenging garden conditions.
