Albuca autumnula

Albuca autumnula, commonly known as the autumn albuca, is a bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, white, bell-shaped flowers during the autumn season. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Albuca autumnula is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care.
