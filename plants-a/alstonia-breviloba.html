Alstonia breviloba

Alstonia breviloba, commonly known as the small-leaved alstonia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its slender, often multi-branched trunk and opposite, ovate to elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pink, followed by follicle fruits containing numerous seeds.

It is often found in lowland forests and is valued for its ornamental appeal and medicinal properties.
