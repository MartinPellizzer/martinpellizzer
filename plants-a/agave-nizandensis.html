Agave nizandensis

Agave nizandensis, commonly known as the Nizanda agave, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a succulent plant characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are thick and fleshy. The leaves are typically grayish-green with a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments. It produces a tall flower stalk that bears white or yellow flowers, which bloom in the spring.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
