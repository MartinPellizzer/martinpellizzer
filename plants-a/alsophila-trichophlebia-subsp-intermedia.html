Alsophila trichophlebia subsp. intermedia

Alsophila trichophlebia subsp.

intermedia, commonly known as the intermediate tree fern, is a species native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feathery fronds that can reach several meters in length.

The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is typically found in humid, tropical rainforests. Its fronds are pinnately compound, with a dark green coloration and a leathery texture.
