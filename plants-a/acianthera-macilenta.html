Acianthera macilenta

Acianthera macilenta, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to South America.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and grasslands. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single or few flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its roots are fibrous and help it anchor in the soil while absorbing nutrients.

The species is noted for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
