Aspidophyllum

Aspidophyllum, commonly known as the "shield fern," is a genus of flowering plants in the family Araliaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped leaves that are often lobed or divided. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in moist, shaded environments.
