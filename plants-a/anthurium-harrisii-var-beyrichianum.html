Anthurium harrisii var. beyrichianum

Anthurium harrisii var. beyrichianum, commonly known as the red-tipped anthurium, is a tropical flowering plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a prominent red or orange vein. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red or orange. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in indoor settings with proper care.
