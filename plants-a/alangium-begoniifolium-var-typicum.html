Alangium begoniifolium var. typicum

Alangium begoniifolium var. typicum, commonly known as the begoniifoliate alangium, is a tropical plant characterized by its distinctive foliage.

It features broad, ovate leaves with a waxy texture and a deep green coloration. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in hue. It is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
