Amitostigma formosensis

Amitostigma formosensis, commonly known as the Formosan lily, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Liliaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically blooms in the spring, producing clusters of pink to purple flowers that attract pollinators. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
