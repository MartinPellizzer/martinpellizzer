Anthurium ceronii

Anthurium ceronii, commonly known as the Ceronii flame, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are deep green with a reddish tint. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its small, spadix-shaped inflorescence. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium ceronii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
