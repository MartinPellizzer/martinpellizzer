Aloe ketabrowniorum

Aloe ketabrowniorum, commonly known as the Ketabrowniorum Aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge, especially under stress or in full sun. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers during the summer months, adding ornamental value. It is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves to survive drought conditions.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
