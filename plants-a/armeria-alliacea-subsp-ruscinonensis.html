Armeria alliacea subsp. ruscinonensis

Armeria alliacea subsp.

ruscinonensis, commonly known as the Ruscinonese thrift, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the eastern Mediterranean region. It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit.

The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are linear and gray-green, often covered in fine hairs.
