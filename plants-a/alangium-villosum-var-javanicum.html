Alangium villosum var. javanicum

Alangium villosum var. javanicum, commonly known as the Java alangium, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features simple, opposite leaves that are usually ovate or elliptic in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, often with a yellowish or greenish hue.

The fruit is a drupe, containing a single seed, and the plant is noted for its ornamental value and potential medicinal uses.
