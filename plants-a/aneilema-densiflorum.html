Aneilema densiflorum

Aneilema densiflorum, commonly known as the denseflower arrowroot, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, arrowhead-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white to yellowish flowers that are often surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in swampy or wetland areas.

Aneilema densiflorum is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its traditional uses in some cultures.
