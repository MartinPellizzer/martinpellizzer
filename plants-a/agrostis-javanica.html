Agrostis javanica

Agrostis javanica, commonly known as the Java bentgrass, is a perennial grass species native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along riverbanks. The plant has slender, creeping stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a blue-green coloration.

Agrostis javanica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping or as a ground cover.
