Aglaonema propinquum

Aglaonema propinquum, commonly known as the Chinese Evergreen, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, variegated foliage with shades of green, white, and cream, making it a popular ornamental plant. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stems. It thrives in low-light conditions and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Aglaonema propinquum is known for its ability to purify indoor air and is often used in interior landscaping.
