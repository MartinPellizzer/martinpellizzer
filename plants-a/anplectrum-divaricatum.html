Anplectrum divaricatum

Anplectrum divaricatum, commonly known as the slender leaf arrowhead, is a perennial herb native to wetlands and marshy areas in North America.

It features arrow-shaped leaves that are often submerged or floating, with a distinctive veining pattern. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its roots are fibrous and help stabilize the soil in aquatic environments.

Anplectrum divaricatum is valued for its ecological role in supporting aquatic life and its ornamental appeal in wetland gardens.
