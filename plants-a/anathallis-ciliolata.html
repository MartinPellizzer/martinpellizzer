Anathallis ciliolata

Anathallis ciliolata, commonly known as the ciliated anathallis, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are elongated and often covered with fine, hair-like structures.

Anathallis ciliolata is noted for its unique floral morphology and is of interest to botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
