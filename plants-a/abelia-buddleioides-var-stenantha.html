Abelia buddleioides var. stenantha

Abelia buddleioides var. stenantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, fragrant, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for gardens in temperate climates.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
