Aspidium setigerum

Aspidium setigerum, commonly known as the sword fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its long, sword-shaped fronds that are typically arching or ascending. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with creeping rhizomes that produce both roots and new shoots. Its leaves are usually dark green and have a serrated or toothed margin.

Aspidium setigerum is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices.
