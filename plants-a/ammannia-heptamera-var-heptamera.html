Ammannia heptamera var. heptamera

Ammannia heptamera var. heptamera, commonly known as the seven-rayed ammannia, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It features striking red or pink flowers that bloom in summer, often resembling those of the hibiscus family. The plant has palmately lobed leaves that are typically green with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in moist, sunny environments such as wetlands and along streambanks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for water garden landscapes.
