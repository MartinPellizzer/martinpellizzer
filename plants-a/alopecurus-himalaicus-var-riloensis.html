Alopecurus himalaicus var. riloensis

Alopecurus himalaicus var. riloensis, commonly known as the Himalayan meadow grass, is a flowering plant species native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and fine, wiry leaves.
