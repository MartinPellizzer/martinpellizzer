Anthurium filamatamaense

Anthurium filamatamaense, commonly known as the Filamatamaense arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy surface. The plant produces striking spadices, which are modified leaves that enclose the actual flowers, often in shades of red or orange. It thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
