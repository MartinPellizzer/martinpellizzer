Acisanthera rosulans

Acisanthera rosulans, commonly known as the clustered bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern at the base.

Acisanthera rosulans thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
