Aloe perfoliata var. ferox

Aloe perfoliata var. ferox, commonly known as the fierce aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple hues along the edges. The plant produces tall, branched stems that bear clusters of yellow flowers, which appear in late spring or early summer. Its leaves have a serrated margin and can grow up to 30 cm in length.

This variety is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with full sun exposure.
