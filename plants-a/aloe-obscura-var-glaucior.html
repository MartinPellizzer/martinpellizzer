Aloe obscura var. glaucior

Aloe obscura var. glaucior, commonly known as the pale aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically pale green with a bluish or grayish hue, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers, which bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine and for their soothing properties.
