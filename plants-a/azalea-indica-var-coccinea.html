Azalea indica var. coccinea

Azalea indica var. coccinea, commonly known as the Chinese azalea, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Rhododendron genus and is characterized by its vibrant red or pink flowers. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub with dense, leathery leaves. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Azalea indica var. coccinea is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
