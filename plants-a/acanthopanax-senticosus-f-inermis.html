Acanthopanax senticosus f. inermis

Acanthopanax senticosus f. inermis, commonly known as the Chinese skullcap or *Wu Jia Pi*, is a shrub native to China and other parts of East Asia.

It belongs to the family Araliaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant is notable for its medicinal properties, often used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and immune-boosting effects. The root and stem are the primary parts used in herbal preparations.
