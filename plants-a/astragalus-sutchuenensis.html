Astragalus sutchuenensis

Astragalus sutchuenensis, commonly known as the Sichuan milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its erect stems and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes, which bloom in early summer. Its root system is extensive, contributing to its ability to thrive in rocky and arid environments.

Astragalus sutchuenensis is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
