Anthemis lyonnetioides

Anthemis lyonnetioides, commonly known as the Lyonnet's anthemis, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in sunny environments. Its yellow-orange flowers bloom in summer, and it produces small, bristly seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its potential medicinal properties.
