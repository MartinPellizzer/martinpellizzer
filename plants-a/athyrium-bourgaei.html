Athyrium bourgaei

Athyrium bourgaei, commonly known as the golden shield fern, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial fern characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds that are typically bright yellow to golden in color. The plant features a short, creeping rhizome and produces sporangia on the underside of its fronds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The golden shield fern is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
