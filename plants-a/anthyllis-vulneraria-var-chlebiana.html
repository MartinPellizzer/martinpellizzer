Anthyllis vulneraria var. chlebiana

Anthyllis vulneraria var. chlebiana, commonly known as the lesser restharrow, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, yellowish or greenish flowers. The plant is native to Europe and parts of western Asia, often found in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. Its seeds are enclosed in pods and are adapted for dispersal by wind, contributing to its widespread distribution.

The species is also valued for its role in soil stabilization and as a food source for certain pollinators.
