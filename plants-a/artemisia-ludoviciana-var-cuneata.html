Artemisia ludoviciana var. cuneata

Artemisia ludoviciana var. cuneata, commonly known as narrowleaf wormwood, is a perennial shrub native to North America.

It features gray-green, narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are aromatic and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in panicles during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

This variety is often used in herbal medicine and as an ornamental plant in gardens.
