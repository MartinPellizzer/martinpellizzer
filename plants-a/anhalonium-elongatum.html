Anhalonium elongatum

Anhalonium elongatum, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Anhalonium elongatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
