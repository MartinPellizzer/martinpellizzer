Asteriscus spinosus var. gymnesicus

Asteriscus spinosus var. gymnesicus, commonly known as the Gymnesian spiny aster, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features spiny, hairy stems and narrow, gray-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically pink to purple in color, with a central disk surrounded by ray florets.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.
