Aralia amboinensis

Aralia amboinensis, commonly known as the Amboina aralia, is a tropical plant native to the Maluku Islands in Indonesia.

It is a member of the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. The plant produces dark, berry-like fruits that are edible but often have a bitter taste. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for cultivation in tropical gardens.
