Albertisia

Albertisia, commonly known as the African daisy, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to southern Africa, where it thrives in a variety of habitats, including grasslands and rocky outcrops. Albertisia species are often used in horticulture for their ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
