Asplenium deparioides

Asplenium deparioides, commonly known as the rock fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its small, delicate fronds that are typically less than 10 cm in length. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found growing on rocks or in crevices. Its rhizomes are wiry and help anchor it to rocky substrates.

Asplenium deparioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded gardens or as a houseplant.
