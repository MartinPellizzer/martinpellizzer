Arisaema putii

Arisaema putii, commonly known as the Sikkim arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan regions of India and Nepal.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are arrowhead-shaped, with a glossy green surface and prominent veins.

Arisaema putii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
