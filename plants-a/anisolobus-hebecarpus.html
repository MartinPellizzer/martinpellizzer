Anisolobus hebecarpus

Anisolobus hebecarpus, commonly known as the narrow-fruit sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia and the Pacific Islands.

The fruiting structures, known as achenes, are small and three-angled, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
