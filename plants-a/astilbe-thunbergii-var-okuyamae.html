Astilbe thunbergii var. okuyamae

Astilbe thunbergii var. okuyamae, commonly known as the Okuyama astilbe, is a herbaceous perennial native to Japan.

It features upright, branching stems that bear clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white to lilac-colored flowers. The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are dark green and slightly toothed. It thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for shaded garden beds.

Its ornamental value lies in its delicate floral display and foliage, which adds texture and color to woodland gardens.
