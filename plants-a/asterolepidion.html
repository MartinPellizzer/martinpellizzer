Asterolepidion

Asterolepidion, commonly known as the rock aster, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of gray-green, hairy leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer. Native to arid and rocky environments, it is well adapted to survive in dry conditions. The plant is often found in regions of the western United States and parts of Canada.

Its unique morphology and ecological adaptations make it a notable species in its habitat.
