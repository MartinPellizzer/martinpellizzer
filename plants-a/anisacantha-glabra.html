Anisacantha glabra

Anisacantha glabra, commonly known as the smooth anisacantha, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a slightly hairy texture.

The plant is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
