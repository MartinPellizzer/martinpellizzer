Asterochaete glomerata

Asterochaete glomerata, commonly known as the clustered aster.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically features dense clusters of small, white to pale yellow florets arranged in a compact inflorescence. It is native to parts of Africa and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

Asterochaete glomerata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
