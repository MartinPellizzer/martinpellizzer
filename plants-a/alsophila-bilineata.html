Alsophila bilineata

Alsophila bilineata, commonly known as the two-lined tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant has a distinctive two-colored rachis, with a lighter base and a darker upper portion. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical forests.

Alsophila bilineata is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
