Asplenium dissectum var. kauaiense

Asplenium dissectum var. kauaiense, commonly known as the Kauai holly fern, is a rare and endemic fern species native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its finely dissected, feather-like fronds that give it a delicate and intricate appearance. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in montane rainforests. Its unique morphology and restricted habitat make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered vulnerable and requires careful preservation.
