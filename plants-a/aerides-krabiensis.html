Aerides krabiensis

Aerides krabiensis, commonly known as the Krabi orchid, is a species native to Thailand.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and attractive, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, white to pale yellow blooms. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Aerides krabiensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in horticulture.
