Alocasia amabilis

Alocasia amabilis, commonly known as the velvet leaf alocasia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense, velvety texture. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth. Its foliage is highly ornamental, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Alocasia amabilis is known for its ability to purify the air and enhance the aesthetic appeal of any space.
