Acampe rigida

Acampe rigida, commonly known as rigid acampe, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. The plant is characterized by its upright, rigid stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that bloom in late spring.

Acampe rigida is often found in rocky or dry soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.
