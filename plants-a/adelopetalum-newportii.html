Adelopetalum newportii

Adelopetalum newportii, commonly known as the Newport's sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a low-growing perennial with slender stems and small, showy flowers that bloom in shades of pink and purple. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal and inland desert regions.

Adelopetalum newportii is adapted to arid environments and is relatively drought-tolerant.
