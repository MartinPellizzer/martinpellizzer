Asparagus tenuifolius

Asparagus tenuifolius, commonly known as Japanese asparagus or narrow-leaved asparagus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features delicate, fern-like foliage with slender, arching stems that produce small, white flowers in early spring. The plant is characterized by its finely divided leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or container planting.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a low-maintenance, visually appealing plant.
