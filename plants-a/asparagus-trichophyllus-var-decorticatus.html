Asparagus trichophyllus var. decorticatus

Asparagus trichophyllus var. decorticatus, commonly known as the smooth asparagus, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, erect stems that produce small, yellowish-green flowers in late spring. The plant's foliage consists of pinnately compound leaves with numerous small, pointed leaflets. Its roots are fibrous and often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to warm, sunny environments.
