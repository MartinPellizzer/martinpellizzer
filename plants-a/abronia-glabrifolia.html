Abronia glabrifolia

Abronia glabrifolia, commonly known as the smooth-leaved sandverbena, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern California and Baja California.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that thrives in sandy, well-drained soils. The plant produces clusters of small, showy blue or purple flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are typically narrow, smooth, and covered in a fine layer of white hair.

Abronia glabrifolia is adapted to coastal climates and is often found in habitats with high salinity and strong winds.
