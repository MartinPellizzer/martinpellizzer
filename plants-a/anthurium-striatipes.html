Anthurium striatipes

Anthurium striatipes, commonly known as the striped anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated leaves that are often adorned with distinctive white or pale green stripes. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can range from red to orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium striatipes is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
