Amaryllis rutilans

Amaryllis rutilans, commonly known as the yellow amaryllis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It produces striking yellow flowers with a red center, typically blooming in the summer months. The plant features broad, strap-like leaves that are often a deep green color. It prefers well-draining soil and full to partial sunlight.

Amaryllis rutilans is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
