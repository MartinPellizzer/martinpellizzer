Arisaema consanguineum subsp. kelung-insulare

Arisaema consanguineum subsp.

kelung-insulare, commonly known as the Kelung Island arisaema, is a species native to the Philippines. It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its distinctive, hooded spathe and spadix inflorescence.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are usually large and heart-shaped, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and limited distribution.
