Anthericum ornithogaloides

Anthericum ornithogaloides, commonly known as the silver thread, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features a rosette of narrow, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, white, bell-shaped flowers in the summer months. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodland areas and stream banks.

Its botanical name reflects its ornamental value and its resemblance to the genus Ornithogalum.
