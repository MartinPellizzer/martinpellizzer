Acantholimon scabrellum var. scabrellum

Acantholimon scabrellum var. scabrellum, commonly known as the rough stonecress, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and dense clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant has hairy, often glandular leaves that are typically blue-green in color. It thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to dry, sunny conditions.

This variety is known for its resilience and is often found in coastal and mountainous areas.
