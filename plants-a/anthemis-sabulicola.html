Anthemis sabulicola

Anthemis sabulicola, commonly known as the sand daisy, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern California and Baja California.

It is a low-growing perennial that thrives in sandy and gravelly soils, often found in dunes and coastal scrub habitats.
