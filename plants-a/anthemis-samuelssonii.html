Anthemis samuelssonii

Anthemis samuelssonii, commonly known as Samuelson's chamomile, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Samuelson's chamomile is valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
