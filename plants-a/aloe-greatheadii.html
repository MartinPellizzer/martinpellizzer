Aloe greatheadii

Aloe greatheadii, commonly known as Greathead's aloe, is a species of succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating that helps it retain moisture. The plant produces tall, branching inflorescences bearing yellow flowers, which bloom in late summer. Aloe greatheadii is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and drought tolerance.
