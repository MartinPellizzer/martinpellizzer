Angelica likiangensis

Angelica likiangensis, commonly known as the Yunnan angelica, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family and is characterized by its tall, hollow stems and large, compound leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in umbels, which bloom in the summer months. Its roots are valued in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported medicinal properties.

Angelica likiangensis thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forested areas at higher elevations.
