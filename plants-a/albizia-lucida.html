Albizia lucida

Albizia lucida, commonly known as the silky senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall, featuring slender, often thornless branches. The plant produces bright yellow flowers arranged in racemes, which bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are pinnate, consisting of 10-20 pairs of small, oval-shaped leaflets.

Albizia lucida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
