Agrimonia hirsuta var. australis

Agrimonia hirsuta var. australis, commonly known as the Australian agrimony, is a perennial herb native to Australia.

It features upright, branched stems covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in dense, spherical clusters. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often covered with short, stiff hairs.

Agrimonia hirsuta var. australis is adapted to dry, sunny environments and is often found in sandy or rocky soils.
