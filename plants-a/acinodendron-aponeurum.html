Acinodendron aponeurum

Acinodendron aponeurum, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Bignoniaceae family and is characterized by its showy, orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters during the rainy season. The species is also valued for its hard, durable wood, which is used in construction and furniture-making.
