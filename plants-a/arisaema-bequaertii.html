Arisaema bequaertii

Arisaema bequaertii, commonly known as the Chinese cobra lily, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, hooded spathe that resembles a cobra's head, giving it its common name. Native to China, this plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. The plant produces small, green berries that mature into red or orange fruits.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a sought-after species in horticulture.
