Aquarius inpai

Aquarius inpai, commonly known as the water bearer plant, is a unique species native to tropical regions.

It features striking, waxy leaves that are often blue-green in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in moist environments, making it well-suited for humid climates. Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening due to its visual appeal.

However, it requires careful watering to avoid root rot, as it is sensitive to overhydration.
