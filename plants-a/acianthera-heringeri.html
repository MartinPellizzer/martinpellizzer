Acianthera heringeri

Acianthera heringeri, commonly known as the Heringer's acianthera, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, yellowish-green flowers with distinctive violet markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
