Amomum mindanaense

Amomum mindanaense, commonly known as Mindanao cardamom, is a tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the ginger family (Zingiberaceae) and is characterized by its aromatic, spike-like inflorescences. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are often clustered together. Its leaves are broad, lance-shaped, and have a fragrant, spicy aroma.

The rhizomes of Amomum mindanaense are used in traditional medicine and are valued for their potential therapeutic properties.
