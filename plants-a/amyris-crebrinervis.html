Amyris crebrinervis

Amyris crebrinervis, commonly known as the narrow-veined amyris, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It features lance-shaped leaves with prominent, parallel veins, giving it its scientific name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its bark is smooth and reddish-brown, and it is often used in traditional medicine.

Amyris crebrinervis is valued for its ornamental appeal and aromatic properties.
