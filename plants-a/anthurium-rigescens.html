Anthurium rigescens

Anthurium rigescens, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, waxy, dark green leaves that have a velvety texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red or orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium rigescens is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
