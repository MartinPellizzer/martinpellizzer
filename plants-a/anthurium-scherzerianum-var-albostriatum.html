Anthurium scherzerianum var. albostriatum

Anthurium scherzerianum var. albostriatum, commonly known as the white-striated anthurium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with prominent white veins. The plant produces striking, spadix-like inflorescences that are often surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

This variety is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
