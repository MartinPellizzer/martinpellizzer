Aporum cuneatipetalum

Aporum cuneatipetalum, commonly known as the narrow-petaled aporum, is a unique member of the Araliaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, lance-shaped leaves that are often deeply lobed, giving it a striking appearance.
