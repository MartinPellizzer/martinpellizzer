Aster retroflexus

Aster retroflexus, commonly known as the hoary aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and showy blooms. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and rocky areas. Its leaves are hairy and grayish-green, while its flowers are usually white to pale blue.

It produces small seeds that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its spread across various environments.
