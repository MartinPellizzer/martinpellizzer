Arenaria capensis

Arenaria capensis, commonly known as Cape sandwort, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of about 10 to 20 centimeters. The plant features small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, adapted to thrive in sandy or rocky soils.

It is often found in coastal areas and is well-suited to arid environments.
