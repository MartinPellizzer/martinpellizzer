Argyroderma lesliei

Argyroderma lesliei, commonly known as the Leslie's silverleaf, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
