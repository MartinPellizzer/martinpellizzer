Aspalathus hirta subsp. stellaris

Aspalathus hirta subsp.

stellaris, commonly known as the starbush, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and small, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces clusters of tiny, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for gardeners in mild climates.
