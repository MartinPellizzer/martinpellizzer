Alloplectus dielsii

Alloplectus dielsii, commonly known as the yellow star orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in grassland and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant produces a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and bears showy yellow flowers with a distinctive star-shaped pattern. Its blooming period occurs during the summer months, and it is pollinated primarily by bees and other insects.

Alloplectus dielsii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant, unique flowers.
