Astronia tomentosa

Astronia tomentosa, commonly known as the wooly astronia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. It thrives in humid environments and is often found in shaded, moist forest understories.

Astronia tomentosa is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
