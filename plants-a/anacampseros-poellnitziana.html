Anacampseros poellnitziana

Anacampseros poellnitziana, commonly known as the silver leaf stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a waxy coating, which helps it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental appearance make it a valued addition to xeriscapes and drought-tolerant gardens.
