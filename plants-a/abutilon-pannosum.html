Abutilon pannosum

Abutilon pannosum, commonly known as velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a velvety texture. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the warmer months. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, smooth seeds.

Abutilon pannosum is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of light conditions.
