Austrobuxus petiolaris

Austrobuxus petiolaris, commonly known as the slender boxwood, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Buxaceae and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage. The leaves are typically small, oval-shaped, and have a glossy appearance. It grows in a compact form, often forming low, bushy plants.

This plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in horticultural settings.
