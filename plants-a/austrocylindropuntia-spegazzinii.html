Austrocylindropuntia spegazzinii

Austrocylindropuntia spegazzinii, commonly known as the Spegazzini's columnar cactus, is a species native to Argentina.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems that can grow up to 3 meters in height. The plant has spiny areoles and produces yellow flowers during the spring season. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, requiring minimal water and full sun exposure.

Its structure makes it well adapted for survival in harsh desert conditions.
