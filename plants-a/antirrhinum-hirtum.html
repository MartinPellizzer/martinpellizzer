Antirrhinum hirtum

Antirrhinum hirtum, commonly known as the hairy snapdragon, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and showy, bilaterally symmetrical flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, yellow, and orange. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

Antirrhinum hirtum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of care.
