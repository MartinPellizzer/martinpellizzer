Acrostichum macrophyllum

Acrostichum macrophyllum, commonly known as the giant fern or broad-leaf fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, feathery fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, moist soil. Its leaves are compound, with numerous pinnae that contribute to its lush, ornamental appearance.

Acrostichum macrophyllum is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in tropical gardens and as a houseplant.
