Alsophila philippinense

Alsophila philippinense, commonly known as the Philippine tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and feathery fronds. The plant has a robust rhizome that supports its growth and helps in nutrient absorption. Its fronds are typically bioluminescent, a unique feature that aids in attracting pollinators.

Alsophila philippinense plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
