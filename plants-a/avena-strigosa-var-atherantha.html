Avena strigosa var. atherantha

Avena strigosa var. atherantha, commonly known as slender oat, is a perennial grass species native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It features slender, erect stems that are often branched and covered with fine, appressed hairs. The leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue and a prominent midrib. Its inflorescence consists of a spikelet-bearing panicle, which produces small, two-lobed florets.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native plant gardens and restoration projects.
