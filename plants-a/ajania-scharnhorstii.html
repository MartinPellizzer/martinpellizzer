Ajania scharnhorstii

Ajania scharnhorstii, commonly known as the mountain ajania, is a perennial herb native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of lanceolate, silver-gray leaves. The plant produces small, blue to purple flowers in composite inflorescences, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to cold, arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in alpine gardens.
