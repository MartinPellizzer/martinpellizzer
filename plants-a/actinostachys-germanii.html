Actinostachys germanii

Actinostachys germanii, commonly known as the German featherplant, is a perennial aquatic plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in shallow, slow-moving waters and is characterized by its feathery, finely divided leaves that float on the water surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It is often used in water gardens for its ornamental value and ability to help oxygenate water.

Actinostachys germanii is also known for its ability to provide habitat for aquatic invertebrates and small fish.
