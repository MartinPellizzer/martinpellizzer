Aloe elkerriana

Aloe elkerriana, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are thick, fleshy, and covered in a layer of white powder. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Aloe elkerriana is often used in ornamental gardening due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
