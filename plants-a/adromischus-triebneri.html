Adromischus triebneri

Adromischus triebneri, commonly known as the "Lion's Tail" or "Tiger Tail," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or red during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Adromischus triebneri is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
