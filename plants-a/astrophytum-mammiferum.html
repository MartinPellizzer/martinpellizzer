Astrophytum mammiferum

Astrophytum mammiferum, commonly known as the mammillaria cactus or the star cactus, is a species of cactus native to the deserts of Mexico.

It is characterized by its globular shape and prominent tubercles, which give it a star-like appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, which are followed by small, red fruits. Astrophytum mammiferum is a slow-growing plant that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
