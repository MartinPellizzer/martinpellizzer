Athyrium nanyueense

Athyrium nanyueense, commonly known as the Guangdong lady fern, is a species of fern native to southern China.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its delicate, bipinnate fronds that are often arching or drooping. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce numerous sporangia on the undersides of its fertile fronds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage.
