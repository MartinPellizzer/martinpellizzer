Araiostegia pulcherrima

Araiostegia pulcherrima, commonly known as the purple star orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking violet-blue flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid forest environments.

The botanical features of this orchid include narrow, lance-shaped leaves and a short, stocky stem.
