Atractylis cancellata subsp. gaditana

Atractylis cancellata subsp.

gaditana, commonly known as the Iberian centaury, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe, particularly Spain and Portugal. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy, gray-green leaves and small yellow flowers arranged in compact clusters.
