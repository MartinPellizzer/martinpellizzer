Aloe ribauensis

Aloe ribauensis, commonly known as the Ribau's aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces tall, branched flower stalks that bear yellowish-orange flowers. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, adapting to poor soil conditions.

Aloe ribauensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
