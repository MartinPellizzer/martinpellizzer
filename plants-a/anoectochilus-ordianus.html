Anoectochilus ordianus

Anoectochilus ordianus, commonly known as the Ordian waxlip, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit and produces small, dark green leaves. The plant typically blooms with fragrant, yellowish-green flowers that have a distinctive lip. It prefers shady, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Anoectochilus ordianus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
