Anthurium ptenospathum

Anthurium ptenospathum, commonly known as the broad-leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy surface. The plant produces striking spadices, which are modified leaves that enclose the actual flowers. Anthurium ptenospathum thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying properties.
