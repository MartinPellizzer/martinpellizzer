Asphodelus brevicaulis

Asphodelus brevicaulis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved asphodel, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

Asphodelus brevicaulis is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and coastal areas.
