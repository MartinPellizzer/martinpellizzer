Anthurium folsomianum

Anthurium folsomianum, commonly known as the Folsomian arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, glossy green leaves that are often adorned with striking red veins. The plant produces spadices, which are inflorescences that consist of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium folsomianum is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
