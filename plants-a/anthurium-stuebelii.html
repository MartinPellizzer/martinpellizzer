Anthurium stuebelii

Anthurium stuebelii, commonly known as the Stuebel's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often deep green with a reddish or purple tint. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium stuebelii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
