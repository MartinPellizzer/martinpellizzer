Amelanchier Vulgaris

Amelanchier vulgaris, commonly known as the serviceberry, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to North America. It is celebrated for its early spring blooms and edible fruits that ripen in summer. This plant thrives in a variety of soil types and is often found in woodland edges and open landscapes. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and natural habitats. The serviceberry plays an important role in supporting local wildlife and enhancing ecological diversity.
