Alsine hybrida

Alsine hybrida, commonly known as the hybrid sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically features small, pale green to blue-green leaves arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white or pale pink flowers during the summer months. Alsine hybrida thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or arid environments.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in harsh conditions.
