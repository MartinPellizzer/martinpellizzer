Aralia bipinnata var. bipinnata

Aralia bipinnata var. bipinnata, commonly known as the giant aralia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, compound leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in length, with a feathery, bipinnate structure. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in an umbrella-like inflorescence, followed by dark blue berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its rapid growth and dramatic foliage make it a popular choice for tropical gardens and indoor spaces.
