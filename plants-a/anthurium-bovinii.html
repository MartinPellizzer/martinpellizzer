Anthurium bovinii

Anthurium bovinii, commonly known as the "Black Velvet" anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It features dark, velvety foliage with a deep green to almost black appearance, contrasting beautifully with its vibrant orange spadix. The plant produces a unique inflorescence that consists of a spadix surrounded by a spathe, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Anthurium bovinii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
