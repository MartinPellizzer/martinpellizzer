Alsophila atrovirens var. rigida

Alsophila atrovirens var. rigida, commonly known as the rigid cliffbrake, is a fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the family Hymenophyllaceae and is characterized by its delicate, leathery fronds. The plant typically grows on shaded, moist rock faces or in humid forest environments. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a distinctive, narrow, and often recurved shape.
