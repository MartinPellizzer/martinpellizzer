Amsonia tabernaemontana var. gattingeri

Amsonia tabernaemontana var. gattingeri, commonly known as the Blue Star, is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features striking blue to indigo flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped, gray-green leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
