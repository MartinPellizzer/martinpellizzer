Astragalus ziaratensis

Astragalus ziaratensis, commonly known as Ziarat milkvetch, is a flowering plant species native to the mountainous regions of Pakistan.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and compound leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions. It plays a role in soil stabilization and is of interest for its potential medicinal properties.

Its seeds are often used in traditional herbal remedies.
