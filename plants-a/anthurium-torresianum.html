Anthurium torresianum

Anthurium torresianum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are deep green with a velvety texture. The plant produces vibrant red spadices, which are the colorful, modified leaves that surround the small, inconspicuous true flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium torresianum is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and unique floral structure.
