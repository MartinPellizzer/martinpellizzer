Anthurium riodocense

Anthurium riodocense, commonly known as the Red-veined Anthurium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with deep red veins against a dark green background. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its small, spadix-shaped inflorescence. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium riodocense is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
