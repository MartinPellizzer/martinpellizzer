Asphodelus clavosus

Asphodelus clavosus, commonly known as the sword lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, sword-like leaves and produces clusters of pale yellow to orange flowers. The plant is characterized by its fibrous root system and its ability to thrive in well-drained soils. It is often found in rocky or coastal areas due to its tolerance for harsh environmental conditions.

Asphodelus clavosus is also known for its historical use in traditional medicine and as a symbol in ancient cultures.
