Astragalus Varius Var. Albiflorus

Astragalus varius var. albiflorus is a unique variant of the Astragalus genus known for its striking white flowers. This plant is native to specific regions in North America and thrives in dry, rocky environments. It is often found in open grasslands and woodland edges where it benefits from full sun exposure. Its distinguishing features include slender stems and clusters of small, delicate blooms. Due to its rarity and ecological importance, it is a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
