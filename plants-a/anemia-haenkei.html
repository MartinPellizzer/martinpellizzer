Anemia haenkei

Anemia haenkei, commonly known as the silver sword, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family.

It is a striking plant with broad, silver-green leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns. Its unique appearance and ability to thrive in specific conditions make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and specialized collections.
