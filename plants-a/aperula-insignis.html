Aperula insignis

Aperula insignis, commonly known as the silverleaf aperula, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and small, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of purple and blue. The plant belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is often found in rocky, well-drained soils. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Aperula insignis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
