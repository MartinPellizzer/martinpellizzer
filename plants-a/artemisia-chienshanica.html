Artemisia chienshanica

Artemisia chienshanica, commonly known as the Chinese wormwood, is a perennial shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, gray-green foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in composite heads, typical of the daisy family. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for various health purposes.
