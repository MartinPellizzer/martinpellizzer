Aristea angolensis

Aristea angolensis, commonly known as the Angola aristea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. It produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple in color.

Aristea angolensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
