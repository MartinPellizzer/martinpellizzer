Artabotrys pleurocarpus

Artabotrys pleurocarpus, commonly known as the fragrant flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, orange-red flowers that emit a strong, sweet fragrance. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

Artabotrys pleurocarpus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and parks.
