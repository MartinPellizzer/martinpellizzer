Aralia rotunda

Aralia rotunda, commonly known as the umbrella plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, umbrella-shaped compound leaves that can reach up to 3 feet in diameter. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in an umbel, which are followed by bright red berries. Aralia rotunda thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
