Anthemis eliezrae

Anthemis eliezrae, commonly known as the Eliezra's Fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
