Aconitum fengii var. crispulum

Aconitum fengii var. crispulum, commonly known as the crispulatum variety of Chinese aconite, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers. The plant features finely divided, fern-like leaves that are often covered in a fuzzy texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variant is noted for its unique leaf morphology and is valued in both horticulture and traditional medicine.
