Amaryllis starkiorum

Amaryllis starkiorum, commonly known as the Stark's amaryllis, is a species native to South Africa.

It is a bulbous plant that produces striking, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant typically blooms in late winter or early spring, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, emerging from the base of the plant after flowering.

Amaryllis starkiorum prefers well-drained soil and bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
