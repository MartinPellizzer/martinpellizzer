Armeria Juniperifolia Var. Splendens

Snowdon's thrift, scientifically known as Armeria juniperifolia var. splendens, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Wales. This variant of the thrift plant is distinguished by its vibrant pink and white blossoms that bloom in early summer. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in alpine meadows and coastal areas. Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for rock gardens and landscaping. Despite its delicate look, Snowdon's thrift is remarkably resilient, able to withstand harsh weather conditions.
