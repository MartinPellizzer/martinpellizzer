Aremonia pouzarii

Aremonia pouzarii, commonly known as the red-flowered aremonia, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and striking red or pink flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Aremonia pouzarii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
