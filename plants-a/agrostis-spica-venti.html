Agrostis spica-venti

Agrostis spica-venti, commonly known as wind spike grass, is a perennial grass species native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and dense, spike-like inflorescences that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides, meadows, and sandy soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration that helps it retain moisture in arid conditions.

Agrostis spica-venti is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in erosion-prone areas.
