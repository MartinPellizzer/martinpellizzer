Anthurium holquinianum

Anthurium holquinianum, commonly known as the Holquin's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are inflorescences that consist of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. Anthurium holquinianum thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Its unique foliage and ornamental value make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
