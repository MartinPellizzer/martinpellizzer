Aria thomsonii

Aria thomsonii, commonly known as Thomson's arrowhead, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically bright green and have a velvety texture. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Aria thomsonii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
