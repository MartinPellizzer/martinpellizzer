Anathallis gehrtii

Anathallis gehrtii, commonly known as the Brazilian star orchid, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its unique, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments.
