Aralia armata

Aralia armata, commonly known as the arrowleaf aralia, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features compound leaves that are typically arrow-shaped, with a distinctive three-lobed structure. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in umbels, which are followed by dark blue berries. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
