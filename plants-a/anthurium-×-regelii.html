Anthurium × regelii

Anthurium × regelii, commonly known as the Red Velvet plant, is a hybrid species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are brightly colored, spathelike structures that enclose the actual flowers. Anthurium × regelii thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Its unique foliage and ornamental value make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
