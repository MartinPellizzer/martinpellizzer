Abies beshanzuensis var. ziyuanensis

Abies beshanzuensis var. ziyuanensis, commonly known as the Ziyuan fir, is a rare and endemic coniferous species native to China.

It belongs to the genus Abies, which includes various species of fir trees characterized by their cone-shaped crowns and needle-like leaves. The plant is distinguished by its unique foliage, which features short, stiff needles with a bluish-green hue. It thrives in high-altitude environments, typically found in mountainous regions with cool, moist climates.

Due to its limited distribution and ecological significance, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical research and conservation efforts.
