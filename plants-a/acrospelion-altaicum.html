Acrospelion altaicum

Acrospelion altaicum, commonly known as the high mountain bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its upright stem and clusters of bell-shaped blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Acrospelion altaicum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable growing conditions.
