Arundarbor khasiana

Arundarbor khasiana, commonly known as the Khasi orchid, is a flowering plant native to the northeastern regions of India.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, fragrant blooms. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, thriving in the cool, humid climates of its native habitat. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, while its flowers are usually pink or purple in color.

Arundarbor khasiana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
