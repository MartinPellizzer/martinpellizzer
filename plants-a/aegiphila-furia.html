Aegiphila furia

Aegiphila furia, commonly known as the fiery aegiphila, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, often growing in rocky or alpine environments.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
