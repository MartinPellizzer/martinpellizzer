Anagallis baumii

Anagallis baumii, commonly known as the strawberry leaf flower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces small, delicate flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and its flowers bloom in clusters at the ends of its stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Anagallis baumii is also known for its ability to spread through runners, forming dense mats of foliage.
