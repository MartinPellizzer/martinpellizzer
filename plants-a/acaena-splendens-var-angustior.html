Acaena splendens var. angustior

Acaena splendens var. angustior, commonly known as the narrowleaf wood rose, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its dense, spiny stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in spring, followed by red, berry-like fruits. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and subalpine regions.
