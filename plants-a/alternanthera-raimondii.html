Alternanthera raimondii

Alternanthera raimondii, commonly known as red flame or red leaf sorrel, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red to orange foliage, which becomes more intense under strong light conditions. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with pink or red, adding visual interest to aquariums. It thrives in moderate to high light and requires regular water changes to maintain its coloration.

Alternanthera raimondii is often used in aquascaping for its ornamental value and ease of care.
