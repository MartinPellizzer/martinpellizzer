Arracacia andina

Arracacia andina, commonly known as the Andean arrowroot, is a flowering plant native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces striking, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a broad, colorful spathe. Its leaves are typically large, arrow-shaped, and have a glossy surface.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional medicine.
