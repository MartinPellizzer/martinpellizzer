Aridaria straminicolor

Aridaria straminicolor, commonly known as the green flame plant, is a succulent native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in dry conditions. The plant produces bright yellow flowers during the rainy season, adding a striking contrast to its green foliage. Its leaves are often covered in a fine, powdery coating that reflects sunlight and reduces water loss.

Aridaria straminicolor is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental appeal.
