Actinomeris procera

Actinomeris procera, commonly known as the slender tamarisk, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the family Tamaricaceae.

It is a shrub characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red in color. Native to arid and semi-arid regions, this plant is well adapted to drought conditions. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to thrive in sandy or saline soils.

The plant plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various desert organisms.
