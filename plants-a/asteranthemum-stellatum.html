Asteranthemum stellatum

Asteranthemum stellatum, commonly known as the star aster, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms.
