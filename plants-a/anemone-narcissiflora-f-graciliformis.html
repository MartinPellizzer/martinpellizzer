Anemone narcissiflora f. graciliformis

Anemone narcissiflora f. graciliformis, commonly known as the slender form of the narcissus anemone, is a variant of the Anemone narcissiflora species.

It is characterized by its delicate, elongated stems and smaller, more refined flowers compared to the typical form. The plant produces white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive, saucer-shaped bloom. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This cultivar is often appreciated for its elegant appearance and is suitable for rock gardens or woodland settings.
