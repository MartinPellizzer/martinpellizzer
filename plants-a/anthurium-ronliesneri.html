Anthurium ronliesneri

Anthurium ronliesneri, commonly known as the Ronliesner's anthurium, is a rare tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with prominent veins. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red or orange. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly sought after by collectors and botanists alike.
