Amaryllis disticha

Amaryllis disticha, commonly known as the zigzag amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, zigzag-shaped leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces tall, erect stems bearing clusters of bright red or orange flowers with six petals. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Amaryllis disticha is known for its long blooming period, often lasting several weeks during the summer months.
