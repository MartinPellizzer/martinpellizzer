Arum pictum lusus bispathaceum

Arum pictum lusus bispathaceum, commonly known as the Variegated Arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It features arrowhead-shaped leaves with a striking variegation of green, white, and yellow patterns. The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a spathe, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shady environments and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and greenhouse cultivation.
