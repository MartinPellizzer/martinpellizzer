Anaphalis franchetiana

Anaphalis franchetiana, commonly known as the silver frost plant, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silvery, woolly leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and thrives in cool, moist environments. Its unique appearance and ecological role make it a notable species in its native habitat.
