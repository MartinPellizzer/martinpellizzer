Arisaema speciosum var. truncatum

Arisaema speciosum var. truncatum, commonly known as the truncated arrowhead, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its distinctive, arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking unique foliage plants.
