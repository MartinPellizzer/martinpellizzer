Aechmea × polyantha

Aechmea × polyantha, commonly known as the pineapple bromeliad, is a hybrid species within the Bromeliaceae family.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup, which collects rainwater for the plant's use. The leaves are typically stiff and covered in a fine layer of white wax, giving them a distinctive appearance. It produces striking inflorescences with colorful bracts that can range from red to orange.

This plant is often cultivated as an ornamental due to its vibrant foliage and unique structure.
