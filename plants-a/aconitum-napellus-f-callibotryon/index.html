Aconitum Napellus F. Callibotryon

Aconitum napellus f. callibotryon is a striking variation of the common aconite known for its unique floral structure. This botanical variant features a distinctive, spurred corolla that sets it apart from typical aconite blooms. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in temperate regions of Europe. The plant is notable for its toxic properties, which have made it a subject of both fascination and caution. Its beauty and danger make it a compelling subject for botanical study and horticultural interest.
