Amaranthus × tamariscinus

Amaranthus × tamariscinus, commonly known as Chinese amaranth, is an annual herbaceous plant native to China.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and branched stems that are often reddish in color. The leaves are lance-shaped, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface, and the plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or red. It is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

This plant is often cultivated as a vegetable or ornamental plant due to its edible leaves and attractive foliage.
