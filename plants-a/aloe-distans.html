Aloe distans

Aloe distans, commonly known as the desert aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in arid environments. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters during the rainy season, adding ornamental value. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their soothing properties.

Aloe distans thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for xeriscaping.
