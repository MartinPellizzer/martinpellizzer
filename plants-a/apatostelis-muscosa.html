Apatostelis muscosa

Apatostelis muscosa, commonly known as the mossy leaf snail, is a species of air plant belonging to the family Pterostellataceae.

It is characterized by its dense, moss-like appearance, with a velvety texture and a broad, flat leaf structure. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. It lacks true roots and absorbs moisture and nutrients through its leaves.

Apatostelis muscosa is primarily used in traditional medicine and as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance.
