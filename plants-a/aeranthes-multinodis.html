Aeranthes multinodis

Aeranthes multinodis, commonly known as the nodding spider-orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, spider-like appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in a raceme, often with a distinctive nodding habit. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Aeranthes multinodis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitat.
