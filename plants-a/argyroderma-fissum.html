Argyroderma fissum

Argyroderma fissum, commonly known as the fissured silver skin.

It is a rare and distinctive plant native to specific regions of South America. The plant is characterized by its silver-colored leaves with a fissured or cracked surface texture. It typically grows in rocky or arid environments, adapting to harsh conditions.

Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
