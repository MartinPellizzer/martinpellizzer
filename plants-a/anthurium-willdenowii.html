Anthurium willdenowii

Anthurium willdenowii, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a velvety texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are inflorescences that resemble elongated flowers, often in shades of red or orange. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium willdenowii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
