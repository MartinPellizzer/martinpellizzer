Acrostichum suberectum

Acrostichum suberectum, commonly known as the upright fern, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and pinnately compound leaves. The fronds are typically dark green and have a leathery texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
