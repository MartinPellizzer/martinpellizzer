Arenaria tenuifolia var. americana

Arenaria tenuifolia var. americana, commonly known as American sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to North America.

It features small, delicate white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has a mat-forming growth habit with prostrate stems and narrow, linear leaves. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open, disturbed habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to arid and coastal environments.
