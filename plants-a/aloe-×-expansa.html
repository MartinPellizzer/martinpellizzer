Aloe × expansa

Aloe × expansa, commonly known as the hybrid aloe or African aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy, and often spiny leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. The leaves are typically green with a waxy coating, which helps the plant retain moisture in arid environments. It produces tall, branching inflorescences with yellow or orange flowers during the summer months.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some medicinal uses in traditional practices.
