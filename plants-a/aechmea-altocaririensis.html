Aechmea altocaririensis

Aechmea altocaririensis, commonly known as the Altocaririensis Bromeliad, is a species of flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup to collect rainwater. The plant features vibrant, showy inflorescences that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both ornamental gardening and botanical study.
