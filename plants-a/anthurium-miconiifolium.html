Anthurium miconiifolium

Anthurium miconiifolium, commonly known as the red-veined anthurium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with deep red veins. The plant produces vibrant spadices, which are the modified leaves that house the true flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
