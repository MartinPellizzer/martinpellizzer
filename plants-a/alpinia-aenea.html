Alpinia aenea

Alpinia aenea, commonly known as the copper plant, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, metallic-green leaves that shimmer in the sunlight. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Alpinia aenea is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
