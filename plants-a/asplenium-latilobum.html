Asplenium latilobum

Asplenium latilobum, commonly known as the broad-lobed spleenwort, is a fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its small, delicate fronds. The plant typically grows on moist rock surfaces or tree trunks, thriving in shaded, humid environments. Its leaves are usually pinnately divided with broad, rounded lobes, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Asplenium latilobum is often found in montane and lowland forests, where it plays a role in the ecological dynamics of its habitat.
