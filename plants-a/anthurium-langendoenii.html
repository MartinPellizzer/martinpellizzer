Anthurium langendoenii

Anthurium langendoenii, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its large, velvety, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 inches in length. The plant produces bright red spadices surrounded by a showy white spathe, which together form its distinctive floral display. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium langendoenii is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and air-purifying qualities.
