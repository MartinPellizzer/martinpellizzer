Aria eminens

Aria eminens, commonly known as the towering lily, is a striking plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and large, showy flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers well-drained soil. Its leaves are long and narrow, arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Aria eminens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is appreciated for its elegant appearance.
