Anthurium × ferrierense

Anthurium × ferrierense, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose yellow spadices, creating a visually appealing floral display. Native to the tropical regions of South America, it thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated as a houseplant and in botanical gardens.
