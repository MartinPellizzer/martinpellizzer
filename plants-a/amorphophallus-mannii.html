Amorphophallus mannii

Amorphophallus mannii, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves and striking inflorescence. The plant produces a spadix, which is surrounded by a colorful spathe, typical of the Araceae family. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
