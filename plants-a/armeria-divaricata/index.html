Armeria Divaricata

Armeria divaricata is a low-growing flowering plant native to the rocky coastlines of southern Africa. It features dense clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late spring and early summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Its hardy nature allows it to withstand salty air and coastal winds. This charming plant adds a delicate touch of color to any garden setting.
