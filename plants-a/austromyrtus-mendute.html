Austromyrtus mendute

Austromyrtus mendute, commonly known as the Tasmanian myrtle, is a small evergreen shrub native to Tasmania.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes eucalyptus and tea trees. The plant features narrow, dark green leaves and produces small, fragrant white flowers. Its fruit is a woody capsule containing several seeds.

Austromyrtus mendute is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional Tasmanian culture.
