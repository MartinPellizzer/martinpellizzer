Ancistrorhynchus paysanii

Ancistrorhynchus paysanii, commonly known as the Paysan's armored catfish, is a species of catfish native to the freshwater ecosystems of South America.

It is characterized by its elongated body, armored scales, and a distinctive dorsal fin. This fish typically inhabits slow-moving rivers and streams with rocky substrates. Its diet consists mainly of algae, plant matter, and small invertebrates.

The plant-like structures it interacts with, such as aquatic vegetation, provide essential habitat and food resources in its environment.
