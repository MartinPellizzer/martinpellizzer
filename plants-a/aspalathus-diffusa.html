Aspalathus diffusa

Aspalathus diffusa, commonly known as the silver bush, is a small shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and Mediterranean climates. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Aspalathus diffusa is also valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and hardiness.
