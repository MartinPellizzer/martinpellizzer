Adenosepalum nummularium

Adenosepalum nummularium, commonly known as the silverleaf adenosepalum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small shrub characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage that reflects light and reduces water loss. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale green to yellow in color, arranged in clusters at the ends of branches. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, demonstrating adaptability to harsh conditions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in low-water landscapes.
