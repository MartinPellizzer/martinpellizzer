Aponogeton monostachys

Aponogeton monostachys, commonly known as the black velvet plant, is a tropical aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, ribbon-like leaves that are dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces striking white or pink flowers that bloom above the water surface. It thrives in warm, stagnant or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Aponogeton monostachys is popular in aquascaping for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
