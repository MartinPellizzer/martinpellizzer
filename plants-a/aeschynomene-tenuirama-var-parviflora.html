Aeschynomene tenuirama var. parviflora

Aeschynomene tenuirama var. parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered sicklepod, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its typical legume structure and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant features slender, branching stems and small, pea-like flowers that are typically white or pale in color. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three oval to lance-shaped leaflets.
