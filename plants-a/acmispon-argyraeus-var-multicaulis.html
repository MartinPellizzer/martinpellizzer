Acmispon argyraeus var. multicaulis

Acmispon argyraeus var. multicaulis, commonly known as silverleaf burclover, is a perennial herb native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in desert and semi-desert environments. It has a creeping growth habit with multiple stems arising from the base, and its leaves are alternate and pinnately divided.

The species is adapted to arid conditions and is drought-tolerant, making it a hardy plant in xeriscaped gardens.
