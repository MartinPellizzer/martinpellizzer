Acianthera circumplexa

Acianthera circumplexa, commonly known as the clustered orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink. Acianthera circumplexa is often found in humid, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas.
