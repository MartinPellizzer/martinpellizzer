Anthurium umbraculum

Anthurium umbraculum, commonly known as the dark umbrella anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its spadix, creating a dramatic visual display. It thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium umbraculum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
