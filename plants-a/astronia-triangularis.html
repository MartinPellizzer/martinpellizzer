Astronia triangularis

Astronia triangularis, commonly known as the triangular astronia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 3 to 5 meters. The plant features opposite, elliptical leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. Its small, fragrant flowers are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters.

Astronia triangularis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and floral display.
