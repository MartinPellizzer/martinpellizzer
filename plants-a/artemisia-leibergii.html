Artemisia leibergii

Artemisia leibergii, commonly known as blue mist shrub, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-blue foliage, which consists of small, aromatic, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces tiny, pale yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its aromatic properties make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and traditional medicine.
