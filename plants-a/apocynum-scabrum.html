Apocynum scabrum

Apocynum scabrum, commonly known as dogbane, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a perennial herb that grows up to 2 meters tall, featuring opposite, lance-shaped leaves and showy pink or white flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain toxic, elliptical seeds. It is part of the Apocynaceae family, known for its milky sap and medicinal properties.

Apocynum scabrum is often found in moist soils along streambanks and in woodland areas.
