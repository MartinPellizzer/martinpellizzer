Adromischus rodinii

Adromischus rodinii, commonly known as the "Lion's Tail" or "Coral Cactus," is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically bright green with a reddish tinge, especially when exposed to sunlight. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers during the summer months, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Adromischus rodinii is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.
