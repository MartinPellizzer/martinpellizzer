Aconitum pyramidale var. densiflorum

Aconitum pyramidale var. densiflorum, commonly known as the dense-flowered pyramidal monkshood, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features striking, dark purple to black spurred flowers arranged in a pyramidal inflorescence, which are characteristic of the genus Aconitum. The plant has palmately lobed leaves with a coarse texture and a distinct, waxy appearance. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
