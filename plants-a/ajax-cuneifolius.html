Ajax cuneifolius

Ajax cuneifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaf ajax, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and prefers full sun exposure. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.

The flowers are typically visited by bees and other pollinators, contributing to its ecological value.
