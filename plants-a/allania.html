Allania

Allania, commonly known as the mountain mallow, is a flowering plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and broad, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, pink or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Allania is typically found in rocky or mountainous regions, thriving in well-drained soils.

It is often used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
