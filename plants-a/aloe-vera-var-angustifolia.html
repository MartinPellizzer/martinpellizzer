Aloe vera var. angustifolia

Aloe vera var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrow-leaf aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, slender, upright stems with narrow, fleshy leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the base. The plant produces yellow-orange flowers in clusters, which bloom during the summer months. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is used for medicinal and cosmetic purposes.

It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.
