Aniba albida

Aniba albida, commonly known as white aniba, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, white flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and potential medicinal applications.
