Astragalus radschirdensis

Astragalus radschirdensis, commonly known as the Iranian milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Central Asia, including parts of Iran and Afghanistan.

The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky or sandy soils. Astragalus radschirdensis is of interest to botanists due to its unique adaptations and potential ecological significance in its native habitats.
