Amyris kafal

Amyris kafal, commonly known as the Cape jasmine, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a leathery texture with a prominent midrib.

Amyris kafal is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to coastal and sandy soils.
