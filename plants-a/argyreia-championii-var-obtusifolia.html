Argyreia championii var. obtusifolia

Argyreia championii var. obtusifolia, commonly known as the purple passion vine, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of tubular, violet flowers. The plant produces striking, fleshy berries that turn from green to red as they mature. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant foliage and showy blooms.
