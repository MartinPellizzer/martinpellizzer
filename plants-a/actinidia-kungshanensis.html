Actinidia kungshanensis

Actinidia kungshanensis, commonly known as the Kungshan actinidia, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It belongs to the Actinidiaceae family and is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in early summer, followed by edible, yellow berries that are highly prized for their flavor and nutritional value.

It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and fruit-bearing capabilities.
