Abarema microcalyx

Abarema microcalyx, commonly known as the small-fruited burr legume, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant produces small, spherical pods that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and potential pharmaceutical applications.
