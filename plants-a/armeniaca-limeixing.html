Armeniaca limeixing

Armeniaca limeixing, commonly known as the Chinese date or jujube, is a deciduous fruit tree native to China.

It produces sweet, fleshy fruits that are rich in vitamins and minerals. The plant has simple, alternate leaves and produces small, white flowers in the spring. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The fruit is widely cultivated for its nutritional value and use in traditional Chinese medicine.
