Ambulia aromatica

Ambulia aromatica, commonly known as the fragrant ambula, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, aromatic leaves and clusters of small, white flowers that bloom throughout the warm seasons. The plant thrives in humid, well-drained soils and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth. Its essential oils, extracted from the leaves, are valued for their soothing properties and are often used in aromatherapy.

Ambulia aromatica is also appreciated for its ornamental value in gardens and its role in traditional herbal medicine.
