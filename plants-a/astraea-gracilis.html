Astraea gracilis

Astraea gracilis, commonly known as the slender star grass, is a low-growing perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a distinctive sheath-like base. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warm seasons. Astraea gracilis thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate appearance and ability to form dense mats in suitable conditions.
