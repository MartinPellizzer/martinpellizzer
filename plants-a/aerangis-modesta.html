Aerangis modesta

Aerangis modesta, commonly known as the modest orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pale pink. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with rhizomes that allow it to spread and form dense clusters. Its leaves are elongated and strap-like, often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence.

Aerangis modesta is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in shaded, humid environments.
