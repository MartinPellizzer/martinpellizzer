Aloe glauca var. minor

Aloe glauca var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf aloe or miniature aloe, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features short, fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a powdery bloom, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers on slender stems during the warmer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens or container planting due to its low water requirements and compact size.

Its botanical characteristics make it an attractive and hardy choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
