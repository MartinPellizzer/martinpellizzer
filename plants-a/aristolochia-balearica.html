Aristolochia balearica

Aristolochia balearica, commonly known as the Balearic birthwort, is a climbing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive waxy texture and a deep green color. The plant produces unique, intricate flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with a strong, often unpleasant odor. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
