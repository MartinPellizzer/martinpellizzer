Albizia distachya

Albizia distachya, commonly known as the slender mimosa, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are typically pinnately arranged and often have a feathery appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in racemes, which are followed by flat, pod-like fruits. Albizia distachya thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed or open habitats.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
