Adenanthos sericeus

Adenanthos sericeus, commonly known as the silky adenanthos, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its soft, silvery foliage. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers that appear in clusters during spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.
