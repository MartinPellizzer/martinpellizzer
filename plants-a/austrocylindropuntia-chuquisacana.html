Austrocylindropuntia chuquisacana

Austrocylindropuntia chuquisacana, commonly known as the Chuquisacana columnar cactus, is a species native to Argentina.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It has spiny areoles and is adapted to arid environments.

This cactus is often found in rocky soils and is well-suited for xerophytic landscapes.
