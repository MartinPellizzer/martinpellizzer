Alseis floribunda var. burchelliana

Alseis floribunda var. burchelliana, commonly known as the Burchell's alseis, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub that typically grows in coastal and rocky habitats. The plant features simple, opposite leaves that are often glossy and have a slightly serrated edge. Its flowers are small, white or pale yellow, and are arranged in clusters, producing a subtle fragrance.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
