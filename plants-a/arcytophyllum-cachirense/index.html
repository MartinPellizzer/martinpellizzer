Arcytophyllum Cachirense

Arcytophyllum cachirense is a rare and unique plant native to the high altitudes of the Andes in Ecuador. It belongs to the Ericaceae family, commonly known as the heath or heather family. This species is characterized by its compact growth habit and dark green, leathery leaves. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in cloud forests. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
