Algernonia glazioui

Algernonia glazioui, commonly known as the Glaziou's orchid, is a rare and striking flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Madagascar.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, pendulous inflorescences and vibrant, colorful flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or forested habitats, preferring cool and humid conditions.
