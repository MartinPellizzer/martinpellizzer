Auganthus heucherifolius

Auganthus heucherifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf arum, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a metallic sheen, giving it a unique visual appeal. The plant produces showy, spadix-like flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red or orange. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its foliage remains attractive throughout the year, adding interest to the landscape even when it is not in bloom.
