Aerides racemifera

Aerides racemifera, commonly known as the orchid with clustered flowers, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems and is known for producing multiple flowers in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Aerides racemifera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
