Andromeda japonica var. variegata

Andromeda japonica var. variegata, commonly known as Japanese andromeda, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features striking, variegated leaves with white and green patterns, which add visual interest to gardens. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white during early spring. It prefers acidic, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its seasonal color changes and ability to thrive in moist, cool environments.
