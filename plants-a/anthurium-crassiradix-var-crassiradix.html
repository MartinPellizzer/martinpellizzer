Anthurium crassiradix var. crassiradix

Anthurium crassiradix var. crassiradix, commonly known as the broad-leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a deep green color and prominent veins. The plant produces striking spadices, which are modified leaves that enclose the inflorescence. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
