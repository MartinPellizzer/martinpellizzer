Anemone reflexa

Anemone reflexa, commonly known as the crown imperial, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking, tall spires of showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has dark green, deeply lobed leaves that form a basal rosette, and its stems are often reddish or purple in color. Anemone reflexa is a perennial herb that thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight.

It is also known for its toxic sap, which can cause skin irritation.
