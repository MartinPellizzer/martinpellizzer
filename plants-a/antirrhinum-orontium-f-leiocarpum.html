Antirrhinum orontium f. leiocarpum

Antirrhinum orontium f. leiocarpum, commonly known as the smooth-seeded snapdragon, is a botanical variant of the snapdragon genus.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation. Its foliage is broad and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of care, contributing to its popularity among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts.
