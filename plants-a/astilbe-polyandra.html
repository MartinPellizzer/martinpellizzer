Astilbe polyandra

Astilbe polyandra, commonly known as the Japanese feather fern, is a herbaceous perennial native to East Asia.

It features delicate, plume-like inflorescences that range in color from white to pink, giving it a soft, ethereal appearance. The plant typically grows to a height of 12 to 24 inches and thrives in shady, moist environments. Its foliage is finely divided, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens.

Astilbe polyandra is well-suited for woodland gardens and requires well-drained, fertile soil.
