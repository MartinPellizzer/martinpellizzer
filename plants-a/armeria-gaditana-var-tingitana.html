Armeria gaditana var. tingitana

Armeria gaditana var. tingitana, commonly known as the Canary Islands thrift, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces dense clusters of white or pink flowers. The plant has basal leaves that are linear and often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its botanical features make it a hardy and ornamental choice for Mediterranean climates.
