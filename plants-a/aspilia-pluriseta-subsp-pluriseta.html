Aspilia pluriseta subsp. pluriseta

Aspilia pluriseta subsp.

pluriseta, commonly known as the African milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a perennial herb characterized by its erect stem and showy yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant has palmately lobed leaves and produces follicles that contain toxic latex. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
