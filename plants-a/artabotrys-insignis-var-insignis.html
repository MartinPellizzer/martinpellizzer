Artabotrys insignis var. insignis

Artabotrys insignis var. insignis, commonly known as the fragrant starfruit tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in umbels, often giving it a star-like appearance. The plant produces small, yellow-orange fruits that are edible and have a tangy flavor. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and ovate with a smooth texture.

This species is valued both for its ornamental flowers and its edible fruit in its native habitat.
