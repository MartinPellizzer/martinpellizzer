Agathosma florifera

Agathosma florifera, commonly known as the Cape garlic bush, is a small evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features aromatic, needle-like leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, fragrant yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Agathosma florifera is also valued for its essential oils, which are used in aromatherapy and perfumery.
