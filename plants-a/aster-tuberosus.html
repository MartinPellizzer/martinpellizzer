Aster tuberosus

Aster tuberosus, commonly known as Chinese aster or Japanese daisy, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of purple, pink, and white. The plant has a tuberous root system, which allows it to survive in various soil types and climates. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is complemented by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
