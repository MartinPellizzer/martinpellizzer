Aneilema giganteum

Aneilema giganteum, commonly known as the giant arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Africa.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, spatulate-shaped flowers that are typically green with a yellow or white spathe. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The roots of Aneilema giganteum are used in traditional medicine and as a starchy food source.
