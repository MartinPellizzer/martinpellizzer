Aglaia trichostoma

Aglaia trichostoma, commonly known as the white aglaia, is a tropical plant belonging to the Meliaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate or elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, often clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Aglaia trichostoma is valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
