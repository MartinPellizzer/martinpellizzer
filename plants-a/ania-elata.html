Ania elata

Ania elata, commonly known as the slender anemone, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or alpine environments. The plant features a slender, upright stem with dark green, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are showy, with white petals and a yellow center, blooming in late spring to early summer.

Ania elata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil.
