Amaryllis spectabilis bicolor

Amaryllis spectabilis bicolor, commonly known as the bicolor amaryllis, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that come in a bi-color pattern, typically white with a pink or red center. The plant produces strap-like, evergreen leaves that are broad and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for with proper light and watering conditions.
