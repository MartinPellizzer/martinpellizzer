Anemonoides blanda

Anemonoides blanda, commonly known as the meadow anemone, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features showy, white or pale yellow flowers with a distinctive central cluster of stamens. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are typically basal and heart-shaped. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

Anemonoides blanda is valued for its early spring bloom and is a popular choice for native plant gardens.
