Anthemis kuzmanovii var. alibotusensis

Anthemis kuzmanovii var. alibotusensis, commonly known as the Alibotus mountain fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Balkan Peninsula.

The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, preferring well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its yellow-orange flowers bloom in the summer months, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
