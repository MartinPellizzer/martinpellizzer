Afzelia splendens

Afzelia splendens, commonly known as the African teak or African rosewood, is a tropical hardwood tree native to western and central Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, heavy wood with a rich, dark color and a fine, uniform texture. The plant has a straight trunk and can grow up to 30 meters tall, with large, palmate leaves that are alternate and compound. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Afzelia splendens is valued for its durability and is used in furniture, musical instruments, and construction.
