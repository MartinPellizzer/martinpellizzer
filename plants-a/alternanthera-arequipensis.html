Alternanthera arequipensis

Alternanthera arequipensis, commonly known as the pink shrimp plant, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking pink or red inflorescences that resemble shrimp, hence its common name. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Alternanthera arequipensis is popular in ornamental gardening for its vibrant floral display.
