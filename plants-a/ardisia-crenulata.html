Ardisia crenulata

Ardisia crenulata, commonly known as the glossy privet, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a broadleaf evergreen shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 3 to 6 meters. The plant features dark green, glossy leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a slightly wavy margin. It produces small, white, fragrant flowers in clusters, followed by bright red berries that are attractive to birds.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
