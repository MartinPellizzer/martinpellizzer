Anoplophytum incanum

Anoplophytum incanum, commonly known as the silverleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, dense hairs. The plant produces small, yellow composite flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Anoplophytum incanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
