Adromischus antidorcadum

Adromischus antidorcadum, commonly known as the "Lion's Tail," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of fleshy, blue-green leaves that form a rosette shape, giving it a unique and attractive appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Adromischus antidorcadum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in dry conditions.
