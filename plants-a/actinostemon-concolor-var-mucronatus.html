Actinostemon concolor var. mucronatus

Actinostemon concolor var. mucronatus, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed actinostemon, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features succulent, cylindrical stems that are often covered in a smooth, waxy cuticle, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, with prominent stamen that extend beyond the petals. Its leaves are linear and fleshy, adapted for water storage, and often have a bluish or purplish hue.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils with moderate sunlight.
