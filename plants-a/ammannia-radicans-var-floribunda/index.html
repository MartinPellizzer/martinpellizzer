Ammannia Radicans Var. Floribunda

Ammannia radicans var. floribunda is a strikingly beautiful and resilient plant native to tropical and subtropical regions. It is known for its vibrant red and pink flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. This variant of Ammannia is particularly noted for its showy inflorescences that attract pollinators and add visual interest to gardens. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sun exposure. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both landscape design and container gardening.
