Aporum acianthum

Aporum acianthum, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a striking plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It features showy, red-orange flowers that resemble spiders, hence its common name. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found near water bodies.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking vibrant, exotic foliage.
