Aspidoglossum

Aspidoglossum, commonly known as the silver sword, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asparagaceae.

It is native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa, where it thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. They produce tall, branched inflorescences bearing small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale blue.

Aspidoglossum species are popular among succulent enthusiasts due to their unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
