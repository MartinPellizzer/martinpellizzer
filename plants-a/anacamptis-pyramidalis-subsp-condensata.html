Anacamptis pyramidalis subsp. condensata

Anacamptis pyramidalis subsp.

condensata, commonly known as the condensed pyramidal orchid, is a species of orchid native to southern Europe. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense clusters of violet-blue flowers.

The plant typically grows in calcareous soils and thrives in open, sunny habitats. Its inflorescence consists of several upright spikes bearing numerous small, fragrant flowers.

This subspecies is notable for its ornamental value and is often found in grasslands and woodland edges.
