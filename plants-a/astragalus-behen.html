Astragalus behen

Astragalus behen, commonly known as Indian milkweed, is a member of the legume family Fabaceae.

It is native to regions of Asia, including India, China, and the Middle East. The plant is characterized by its hairy stems and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its seeds are encased in a pod-like structure, typical of the Fabaceae family.

Astragalus behen is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for various health purposes.
