Arisaema wardii

Arisaema wardii, commonly known as the Ward's arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, arrow-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces a striking inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a large, hood-like spathe. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, with a distinctive, often fragrant scent.

Arisaema wardii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
