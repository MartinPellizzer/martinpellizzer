Aristida pflanzii

Aristida pflanzii, commonly known as Pflanz's grass, is a species of grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in dry, sandy or rocky soils and is often found in open grasslands and coastal areas.

Aristida pflanzii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its hardy nature.
