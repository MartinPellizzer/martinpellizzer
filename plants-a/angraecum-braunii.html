Angraecum braunii

Angraecum braunii, commonly known as the Braun's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a tall, erect stem and long, narrow leaves. The plant produces striking white or pale pink flowers with a distinctive spurred lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Angraecum braunii is notable for its nocturnal pollination by hawkmoths, making it an interesting subject for ecological studies.
