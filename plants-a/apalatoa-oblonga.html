Apalatoa oblonga

Apalatoa oblonga, commonly known as the oblong leaf orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often arranged in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Apalatoa oblonga is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
