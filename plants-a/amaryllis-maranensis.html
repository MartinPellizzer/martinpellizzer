Amaryllis maranensis

Amaryllis maranensis, commonly known as the Maraneng amaryllis, is a striking tropical plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant produces a tall, leafless stem that bears multiple flowers, often in clusters. Its foliage consists of broad, strap-like leaves that are typically dark green and glossy.

Amaryllis maranensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a potted plant.
