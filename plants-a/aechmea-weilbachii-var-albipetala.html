Aechmea weilbachii var. albipetala

Aechmea weilbachii var. albipetala, commonly known as the White-petaled Aechmea, is a bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking white petals and vibrant green, strap-like leaves that form a rosette. The plant produces tall flower spikes with showy inflorescences, often in shades of red or orange. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
