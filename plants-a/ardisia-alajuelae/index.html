Ardisia Alajuelae

Ardisia alajuelae is a rare and unique plant native to the cloud forests of Costa Rica. It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes many shrubs and evergreen trees. This species is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, red berries. Due to its limited geographic range, it is considered endangered and faces threats from habitat loss. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this fascinating plant for future generations.
