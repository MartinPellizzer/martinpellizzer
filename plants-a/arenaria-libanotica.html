Arenaria libanotica

Arenaria libanotica, commonly known as the Lebanon sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous areas.

Its botanical features include narrow, linear leaves and a compact growth habit adapted to harsh environmental conditions.
