Amaryllis striatifolia var. concinna

Amaryllis striatifolia var. concinna, commonly known as the narrow-leafed amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, strap-like leaves that are typically narrow and have a bluish-green hue. The plant produces tall, erect stems bearing clusters of vibrant, funnel-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It is a popular ornamental plant due to its bold foliage and showy blooms.

The variety is well-suited for container gardening and can thrive in a range of light conditions.
