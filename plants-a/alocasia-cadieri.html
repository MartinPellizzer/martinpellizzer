Alocasia cadieri

Alocasia cadieri, commonly known as the black velvet fern, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark, velvety leaves that range from deep green to almost black in color. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit and produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters. Alocasia cadieri thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, fertile soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
