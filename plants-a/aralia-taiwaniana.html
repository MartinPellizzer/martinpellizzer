Aralia taiwaniana

Aralia taiwaniana, commonly known as the Taiwan aralia, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests or along stream banks. Its berries, which are edible, are an important food source for various wildlife species.

Aralia taiwaniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitat.
