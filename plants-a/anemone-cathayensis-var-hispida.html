Anemone cathayensis var. hispida

Anemone cathayensis var. hispida, commonly known as the hairy anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It features showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. It prefers moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for woodland gardens and wetland restoration projects.
