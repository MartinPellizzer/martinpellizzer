Alternanthera williamsii f. purpurea

Alternanthera williamsii f. purpurea, commonly known as the Purple Heart Plant, is a strikingly ornamental foliage plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, velvety purple leaves that contrast beautifully with its green stems. The plant thrives in moist, well-draining soil and prefers partial to full sun exposure. It is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in water gardens and as a houseplant.

Its foliage changes color with the seasons, adding dynamic visual interest to any setting.
