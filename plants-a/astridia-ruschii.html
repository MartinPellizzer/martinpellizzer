Astridia ruschii

Astridia ruschii, commonly known as the mountain aster, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southern Andes.

It typically grows in high-altitude grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in cool, temperate climates. The plant features slender stems and small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of blue to purple. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, contributing to its overall delicate appearance.

Astridia ruschii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with well-drained soil.
