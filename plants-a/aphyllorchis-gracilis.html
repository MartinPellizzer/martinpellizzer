Aphyllorchis gracilis

Aphyllorchis gracilis, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, erect stem and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are linear and often clustered at the base of the stem.

Aphyllorchis gracilis is known for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
