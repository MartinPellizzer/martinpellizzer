Aloe mccoyi

Aloe mccoyi, commonly known as the "Cape Town aloe," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, which helps it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers during the summer months, adding visual interest to its appearance. Aloe mccoyi thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its resilience and ornamental value make it a popular choice for both gardens and indoor cultivation.
