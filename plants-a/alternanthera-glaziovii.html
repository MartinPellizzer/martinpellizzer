Alternanthera glaziovii

Alternanthera glaziovii, commonly known as the red-stemmed alternanthera, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its striking red stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often used in aquatic gardens or as a decorative plant in water features.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among gardeners and aquascapers alike.
