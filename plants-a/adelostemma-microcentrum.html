Adelostemma microcentrum

Adelostemma microcentrum, commonly known as the small-centred devil's-bit.

It is a perennial herb native to South Africa, characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces white to pale blue flowers that bloom in late summer, and its leaves are narrow and grass-like. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
