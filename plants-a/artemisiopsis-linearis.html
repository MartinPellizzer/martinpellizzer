Artemisiopsis linearis

Artemisiopsis linearis, commonly known as the linear tansy, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to withstand salty air and strong winds.

Artemisiopsis linearis is also used in traditional medicine for its aromatic properties.
