Arachnorchis procera

Arachnorchis procera, commonly known as the tall spider orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stem and a single, showy greenish-white flower with a distinctive spider-like appearance. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in shaded, humid environments. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air.

Arachnorchis procera is a rare and protected species, highlighting its ecological significance in its native habitat.
