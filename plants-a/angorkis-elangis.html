Angorkis elangis

Angorkis elangis, commonly known as the golden spire, is a striking member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Angorkis elangis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and ability to add a dramatic touch to gardens and landscapes.
