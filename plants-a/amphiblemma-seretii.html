Amphiblemma seretii

Amphiblemma seretii, commonly known as the African sandmat, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils. The plant features a distinctive, branched stem with narrow, linear leaves. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and clustered in dense inflorescences.

It is often found in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.
