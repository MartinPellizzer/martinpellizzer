Alsophila ornata var. sikkimensis

Alsophila ornata var. sikkimensis, commonly known as the Sikkim tree fern, is a species native to the Himalayan regions of India, Nepal, and Bhutan.

It is a tall, slender tree fern characterized by its distinctive, feather-like fronds that grow in a dense, upright arrangement. The plant features a robust, unbranched trunk that is covered with persistent, fibrous roots. Its fronds are typically bright green and can reach lengths of up to 2 meters.

This variety is well adapted to moist, shaded environments and is often found in temperate rainforests.
