Anthurium gaskinii

Anthurium gaskinii, commonly known as the Gaskin's arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Colombia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves and striking spadix inflorescence. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its foliage is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its aesthetic appeal.

Anthurium gaskinii is also valued for its air-purifying properties and ability to thrive indoors with proper care.
