Alsophila scabrinscula

Alsophila scabrinscula, commonly known as the rough-leaf tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its slender, upright trunk and fronds that are covered in a rough, scaly texture. The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is known for its ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments. Its fronds can grow up to several meters in length, making it a striking feature in its natural habitat.
