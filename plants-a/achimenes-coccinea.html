Achimenes coccinea

Achimenes coccinea, commonly known as the red-flowered achimenes, is a tropical perennial plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular red flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are glossy and slightly hairy. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Achimenes coccinea is often grown as a garden ornamental due to its vibrant blooms and ease of care.
