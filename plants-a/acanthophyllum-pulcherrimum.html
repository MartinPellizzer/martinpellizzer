Acanthophyllum pulcherrimum

Acanthophyllum pulcherrimum, commonly known as the beautiful spiny shrub, is a perennial plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and compact growth habit. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is well adapted to drought conditions. Its small, greenish-yellow flowers appear in summer, and it plays a role in stabilizing desert soils.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant in dry climates.
