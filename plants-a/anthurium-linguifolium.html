Anthurium linguifolium

Anthurium linguifolium, commonly known as the tongue-leaf arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces striking spadices, which are typically green with a red or purple spathe. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Anthurium linguifolium is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
