Alsophila scabriuscula var. guatemalensis

Alsophila scabriuscula var. guatemalensis, commonly known as the Guatemalan tree fern, is a species native to Central America.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The fronds are typically dark green and have a rough, textured surface.
