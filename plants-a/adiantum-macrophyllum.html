Adiantum macrophyllum

Adiantum macrophyllum, commonly known as the royal fern, is a species of fern native to the eastern United States and parts of Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, graceful fronds that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant features bi-lobed pinnae with a distinctive, almost metallic sheen on the underside of the leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Adiantum macrophyllum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
