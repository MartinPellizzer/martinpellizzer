Anthurium cinereopetiolatum

Anthurium cinereopetiolatum, commonly known as the silver-stem anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with silver and green hues. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red or orange. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium cinereopetiolatum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
