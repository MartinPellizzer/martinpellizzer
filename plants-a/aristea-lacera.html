Aristea lacera

Aristea lacera, commonly known as the threadleaf aristea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features narrow, gray-green leaves and produces small, yellowish to orange flowers in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Aristea lacera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its drought tolerance and unique foliage.
