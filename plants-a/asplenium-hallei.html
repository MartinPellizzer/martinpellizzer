Asplenium hallei

Asplenium hallei, commonly known as the Halle's spleenwort, is a small, evergreen fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces sporangia on the underside of its fronds, which are clustered in sori, a typical feature of ferns. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found growing on rocks or tree trunks in tropical and subtropical regions.

Asplenium hallei is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
