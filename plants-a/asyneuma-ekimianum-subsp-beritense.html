Asyneuma ekimianum subsp. beritense

Asyneuma ekimianum subsp.

beritense, commonly known as the Beritense goldenweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Central Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellowish-green foliage and small composite flower heads.

The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, often in rocky or sandy soils. It has a low-growing habit with creeping stems and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies of xerophytic vegetation.
