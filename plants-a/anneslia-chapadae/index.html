Anneslia Chapadae

Anneslia chapadae is a rare and striking flowering plant native to the cerrado region of Brazil. It is known for its vivid orange-red petals and unique, spurred structure that sets it apart from other species. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy yet specialized species. Due to habitat loss, it is considered vulnerable, highlighting the need for conservation efforts. Its beauty and ecological importance make it a subject of interest for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike.
