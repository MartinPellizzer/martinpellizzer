Anemone xingyiensis

Anemone xingyiensis, commonly known as the Xingyi anemone, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. Its leaves are palmately divided, and the flowers consist of numerous petal-like sepals.

Anemone xingyiensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
