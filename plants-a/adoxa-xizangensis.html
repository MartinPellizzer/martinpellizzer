Adoxa xizangensis

It belongs to the Adoxaceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and alpine environments, showcasing adaptations to cold and arid conditions. Its leaves are usually narrow and linear, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.
