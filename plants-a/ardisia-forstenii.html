Ardisia forstenii

Ardisia forstenii, commonly known as the Forsten's beautyberry, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Ericaceae and is characterized by its small, clustered white flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant produces dark purple to black berries that are edible for birds but toxic to humans. Its leaves are glossy and oval-shaped, with a mildly aromatic scent.
