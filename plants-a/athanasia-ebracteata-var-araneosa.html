Athanasia ebracteata var. araneosa

Athanasia ebracteata var. araneosa, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, spider-like appearance, with long, thin inflorescences bearing numerous small, white or pale green flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is a perennial herb that produces showy, fragrant blooms during the summer months.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
