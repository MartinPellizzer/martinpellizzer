Astrocaryum mexicanum

Astrocaryum mexicanum, commonly known as the Mexican wax palm, is a tall, slender palm native to southern Mexico.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves and produces large, spiny fruits that contain edible seeds. The plant is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, growing in well-drained soils. It has a slow growth rate and can live for several decades.

The Mexican wax palm is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique appearance.
