Alaternus integrifolius

Alaternus integrifolius, commonly known as the smoothleaf alaternus, is a member of the Rubiaceae family.

It is characterized by its simple, entire leaves that are typically arranged oppositely along the stem. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, followed by small, spherical berries. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used in traditional herbal remedies for various ailments.
