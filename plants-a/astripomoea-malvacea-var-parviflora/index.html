Astripomoea Malvacea Var. Parviflora

Astripomoea malvacea var. parviflora is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia. Known for its small, pale pink blooms and heart-shaped leaves, it captivates botanists and gardeners alike. This variant of Astripomoea malvacea is often found in moist, shaded environments along riverbanks and forest floors. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest in conservation efforts. Despite its beauty, it remains under-studied and vulnerable to habitat loss.
