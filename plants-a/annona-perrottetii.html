Annona perrottetii

Annona perrottetii, commonly known as the red mangosteen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen tree with thick, waxy leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by bright red, fleshy fruits. Its fruit has a hard, segmented outer layer and a sweet, aromatic pulp.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional medicine.
