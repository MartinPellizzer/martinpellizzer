Aconitum lycoctonum subsp. vulparia

Aconitum lycoctonum subsp.

vulparia, commonly known as the European aconite, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It features striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer, typically from July to September.

The plant has palmately compound leaves with lobes that are often deeply divided. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its toxic alkaloids, it is important to handle the plant with care.
