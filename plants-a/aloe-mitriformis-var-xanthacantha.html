Aloe mitriformis var. xanthacantha

Aloe mitriformis var. xanthacantha, commonly known as the yellow-tipped aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with yellow or orange markings along the edges. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellow to orange flowers. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil.

Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
