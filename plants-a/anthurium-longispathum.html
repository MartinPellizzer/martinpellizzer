Anthurium longispathum

Anthurium longispathum, commonly known as the long-spurred anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, spathaceous flowers. The plant produces clusters of small, white to cream-colored flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe. Anthurium longispathum thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying properties.
