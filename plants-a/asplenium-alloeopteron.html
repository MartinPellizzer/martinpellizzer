Asplenium alloeopteron

Asplenium alloeopteron, commonly known as the narrow-leaf rock fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. The plant is often found growing on rocky outcrops or in shaded, moist environments. Its fronds are usually dark green and have a finely toothed margin.

Asplenium alloeopteron is a low-growing plant that thrives in cool, humid climates.
