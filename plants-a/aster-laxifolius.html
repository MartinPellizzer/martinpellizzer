Aster laxifolius

Aster laxifolius, commonly known as the slenderleaf aster, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in the fall, typically in shades of purple, blue, or white. The plant has linear, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Aster laxifolius is also valued for its ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
