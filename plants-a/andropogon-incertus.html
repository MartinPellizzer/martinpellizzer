Andropogon incertus

Andropogon incertus, commonly known as the uncertain grass, is a species of grass native to the southern United States and parts of Mexico.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, blade-like leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as prairies and disturbed soils. Its inflorescence consists of a spikelet cluster, which contains small, two-lobed flowers.

Andropogon incertus is often found in association with other native grasses and is adapted to a variety of soil types.
