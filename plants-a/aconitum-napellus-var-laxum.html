Aconitum napellus var. laxum

Aconitum napellus var. laxum, commonly known as the lax flowered monkshood, is a variant of the deadly nightshade family.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features striking blue to purple spurred flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are palmately compound, with deeply cut lobes that give it a distinctive appearance.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or meadow habitats.
