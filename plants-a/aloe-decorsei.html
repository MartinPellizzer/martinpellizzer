Aloe decorsei

Aloe decorsei, commonly known as the Desert Aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, covered in a white powdery coating called farina. The plant produces striking yellow flowers during the summer months, which are borne on tall, branched inflorescences. Aloe decorsei is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves to survive drought conditions.

It is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
