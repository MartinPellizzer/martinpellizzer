Aphyllon violaceum

Aphyllon violaceum, commonly known as the purple-leafed aphyllon, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking violet to purple foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
