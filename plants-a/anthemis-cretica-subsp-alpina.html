Anthemis cretica subsp. alpina

Anthemis cretica subsp.

alpina, commonly known as alpine cudweed, is a flowering plant native to mountainous regions of Europe. It is a low-growing perennial herb with narrow, linear leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to cold, high-altitude environments. Its small, daisy-like flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

It is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes, contributing to the biodiversity of these ecosystems.
