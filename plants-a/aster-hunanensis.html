Aster hunanensis

Aster hunanensis, commonly known as the Hunan aster, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. The plant is characterized by its blue to purple flowers and lance-shaped leaves. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments in mountainous regions.

Aster hunanensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
