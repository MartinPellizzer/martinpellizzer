Arnebia perennis

Arnebia perennis, commonly known as the everlasting or red sanders, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa, the Middle East, and parts of Asia.

Arnebia perennis is often used in traditional medicine and is also valued for its ornamental qualities in gardens.
