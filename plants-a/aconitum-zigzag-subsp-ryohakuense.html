Aconitum zigzag subsp. ryohakuense

Aconitum zigzag subsp.

ryohakuense, commonly known as the Ryohaku mountain aconite, is a perennial herb native to Japan. It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant features compound leaves with a zigzag pattern, giving it its common name. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests.

Its toxic alkaloids make it a plant requiring careful handling.
