Anthurium guaiquinimae

Anthurium guaiquinimae, commonly known as the Guaiquinima anthurium, is a tropical plant native to South America, specifically Colombia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant spadix. The plant produces a unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium guaiquinimae is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated for decorative purposes in indoor gardening.
