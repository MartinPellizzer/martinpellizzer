Aloe × anosyana

Aloe × anosyana, commonly known as the hybrid aloe or aloe hybridum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, which helps it store water. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers, blooming in late spring or early summer. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their soothing properties.

Aloe × anosyana is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
