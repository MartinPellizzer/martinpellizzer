Agave americana var. elegantissima

Agave americana var. elegantissima, commonly known as the Blue Agave, is a striking succulent native to Mexico.

It features tall, bluish-green leaves that are narrow and often have a waxy coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, white flowers that bloom in late summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for arid climates.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
