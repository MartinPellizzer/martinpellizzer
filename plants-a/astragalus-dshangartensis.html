Astragalus dshangartensis

Astragalus dshangartensis, commonly known as the Dshangartensis milkvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to Central Asia and is characterized by its upright growth habit and compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish to brownish flowers arranged in spikes, which bloom in the summer months. Astragalus dshangartensis is known for its ability to thrive in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils.

It plays a role in soil stabilization and is of interest for its potential ecological and medicinal properties.
