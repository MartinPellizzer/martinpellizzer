Anthemis pulvinata

Anthemis pulvinata, commonly known as the woolly everlasting, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit and dense clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant features hairy, gray-green foliage that gives it a distinctive, woolly appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Anthemis pulvinata is also valued for its aromatic properties and historical use in herbal medicine.
