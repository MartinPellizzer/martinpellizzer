Aspilia pluriseta

Aspilia pluriseta, commonly known as the African feverfew, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and narrow, gray-green leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish to brownish flower heads that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Aspilia pluriseta is known for its medicinal properties and has been traditionally used in herbal remedies.
