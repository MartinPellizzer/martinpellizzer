Arctotis decurrens

Arctotis decurrens, commonly known as the spreading arctotis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its upright, branching stems and vibrant, daisy-like flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Arctotis decurrens is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
