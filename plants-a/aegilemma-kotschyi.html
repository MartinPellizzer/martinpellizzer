Aegilemma kotschyi

Aegilemma kotschyi, commonly known as the desert broom.

It is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa and the Middle East. The plant has small, narrow leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs, which help reduce water loss. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, appearing in clusters during the dry season.

The plant is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water availability.
