Artemisia austroyunnanensis

Artemisia austroyunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan wormwood, is a perennial shrub native to the mountainous regions of southwestern China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in composite heads. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

Due to its medicinal properties, it is sometimes used in traditional Chinese medicine.
