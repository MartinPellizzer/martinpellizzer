Actinidia laevissima var. floscula

Actinidia laevissima var. floscula, commonly known as the fragrant actinidia, is a deciduous vine native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant produces edible berries that are typically yellow or orange when ripe. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential culinary uses.
