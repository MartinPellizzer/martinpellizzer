Acalypha fryeri

Acalypha fryeri, commonly known as the silverleaf acalypha, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.
