Athyrium altum

Athyrium altum, commonly known as the giant lady fern, is a species of fern native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its large, feathery fronds that can reach lengths of up to 2 meters. The plant has a creeping rhizome and produces spore-bearing structures called sori on the underside of its leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
