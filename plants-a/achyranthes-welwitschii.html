Achyranthes welwitschii

Achyranthes welwitschii, commonly known as the Welwitsch's smartweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous annual or biennial species that typically grows in moist, disturbed soils. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, clustered leaves that are often reddish in color. Its flowers are greenish-yellow and appear in dense, spike-like inflorescences.

The plant is known for its adaptability to various environmental conditions and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
