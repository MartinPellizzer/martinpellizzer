Alsodeia prunifolia

Alsodeia prunifolia, commonly known as the roseleaf alsodeia, is a flowering plant native to parts of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, often resembling miniature roses. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and coastal landscapes.
