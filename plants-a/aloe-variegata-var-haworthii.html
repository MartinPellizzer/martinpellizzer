Aloe variegata var. haworthii

Aloe variegata var. haworthii, commonly known as the Haworth's Aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, pale green leaves with distinctive white and green variegation. The leaves are thick and waxy, adapted to store water in arid environments. It produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, yellow flowers during the summer months.

This plant is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of care.
