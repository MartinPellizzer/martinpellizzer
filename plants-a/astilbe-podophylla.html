Astilbe podophylla

Astilbe podophylla, commonly known as the Japanese feather fern, is a perennial plant native to East Asia.

It features delicate, fern-like foliage that is often used in ornamental gardening. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in moist, shady environments and prefers well-drained, humus-rich soil.

Astilbe podophylla is valued for its attractive foliage and ability to add texture to woodland gardens.
