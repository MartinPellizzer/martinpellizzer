Aerides reichenbachii

Aerides reichenbachii, commonly known as the orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clustered, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has strap-like leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Aerides reichenbachii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
