Aerva hainanensis

Aerva hainanensis, commonly known as Hainan aerva, is a flowering plant native to China, specifically the Hainan Island.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its erect, herbaceous stems and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, tropical environments and is often found in shaded forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
