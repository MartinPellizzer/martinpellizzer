Aleuritia bulleyana

Aleuritia bulleyana, commonly known as the Chinese snowbell, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, bell-shaped white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has smooth, dark green leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to both landscape design and ecological habitats.
