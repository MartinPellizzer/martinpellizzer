Arisaema steudelii

Arisaema steudelii, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features a large, arrow-shaped leaf that can grow up to 1 meter in length, making it a dramatic ornamental plant. The plant produces an unusual, spadix-like inflorescence surrounded by a broad, waxy spathe, which is typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-drained, moist soil.

Due to its size and unique appearance, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
