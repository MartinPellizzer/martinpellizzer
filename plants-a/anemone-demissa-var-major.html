Anemone demissa var. major

Anemone demissa var. major, commonly known as the showy anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, single or double flowers that range in color from white to pink, with a distinctive red or purple center. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are basal and often covered in a silvery or grayish bloom. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Anemone demissa var. major is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and early spring bloom.
