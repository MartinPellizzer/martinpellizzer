Aria nivea var. graeca

Aria nivea var. graeca, commonly known as the Greek snowbell, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Greece and Turkey.

Aria nivea var. graeca is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
