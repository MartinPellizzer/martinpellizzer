Aspidistra tillichiana var. tillichiana

Aspidistra tillichiana var. tillichiana, commonly known as the African aspidistra, is a species native to tropical Africa.

It is a member of the Araceae family, characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves that are typically broad and elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are enclosed within a spathe, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in shady, humid environments and is often used as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.

Its ability to tolerate low light makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
