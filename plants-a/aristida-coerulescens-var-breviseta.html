Aristida coerulescens var. breviseta

Aristida coerulescens var. breviseta, commonly known as bluegrass dropseed, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It features fine, silver-blue foliage that turns a reddish hue in the fall, making it visually striking. The plant produces small, fluffy seed heads that add a soft, cloud-like appearance to its overall form. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements, it is often used in native plant gardens and restoration projects.
