Angraecum acutoemarginatum

Angraecum acutoemarginatum, commonly known as the sharp-edged angraecum, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and large, showy flowers that typically bloom at night. The plant produces pendulous, white or pale yellow flowers with a distinct, elongated spur. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Angraecum acutoemarginatum is a striking example of the diverse and specialized adaptations found in Madagascar's unique flora.
