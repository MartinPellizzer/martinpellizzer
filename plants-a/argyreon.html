Argyreon

Argyreon, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking silvery-white leaves that shimmer in the sunlight. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Argyreon thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
