Ayenia atrata

Ayenia atrata, commonly known as the black daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features dark purple to black ray flowers surrounded by yellow disk flowers, giving it a striking appearance. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Ayenia atrata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
