Aeonium × grandipetalum

Aeonium × grandipetalum, commonly known as the Sunset Aeonium, is a hybrid succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped growth pattern and vibrant, colorful foliage that ranges from green to red and purple. The leaves are thick, fleshy, and often have a waxy texture, which helps the plant store water. It produces striking orange or yellow flowers in spring, followed by small, capsule-like fruits.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
