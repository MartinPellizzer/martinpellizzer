Aechmea distans

Aechmea distans, commonly known as the silver vase plant, is a bromeliad native to South America.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup, which collects water and nutrients. The plant produces striking, long-lasting inflorescences that range in color from pink to red. Its foliage is often covered in a silvery bloom, giving it its common name.

Aechmea distans thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often used as an ornamental plant in gardens and indoor settings.
