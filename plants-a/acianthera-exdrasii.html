Acianthera exdrasii

Acianthera exdrasii, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers and is often found in humid, tropical environments. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in the rainforest canopy. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Acianthera exdrasii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
