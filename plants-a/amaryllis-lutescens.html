Amaryllis lutescens

Amaryllis lutescens, commonly known as the yellow amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It produces large, showy flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with intricate patterns. The plant features strap-like leaves that are typically dark green and broad. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Amaryllis lutescens is popular in gardens and as a houseplant due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
