Aloe rubrodonta

Aloe rubrodonta, commonly known as the red-toothed aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellow flowers. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is often used for its medicinal properties.

Aloe rubrodonta is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves to survive dry conditions.
