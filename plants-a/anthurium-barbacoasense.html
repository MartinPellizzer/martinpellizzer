Anthurium barbacoasense

Anthurium barbacoasense, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are vibrant red or orange in color, often surrounded by a white spathe. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Anthurium barbacoasense is popular as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and unique floral structure.
