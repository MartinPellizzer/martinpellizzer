Agonis elliptica var. angustifolia

Agonis elliptica var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf m absolutely, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stems. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, often white or pale yellow, which bloom in spring. Its bark is smooth and reddish-brown, and it is often used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to dry, sunny conditions.
