Aphelandra dielsii

Aphelandra dielsii, commonly known as the golden flame plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, lance-shaped leaves and striking, pendulous inflorescences that bear bright yellow bracts. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can grow up to 3 meters in height under ideal conditions.

Aphelandra dielsii prefers well-draining soil and regular watering to maintain its vibrant appearance.
