Asplenium amplissimum

Asplenium amplissimum, commonly known as the giant spleenwort, is a fern that belongs to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its tall, upright fronds that can reach lengths of up to 2 meters. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rock crevices and forest floors. Its sporangia are clustered in sori on the underside of the fronds, a typical feature of ferns.

Asplenium amplissimum is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, where it thrives in humid conditions.
