Alsine tenuifolia var. confertiflora

Alsine tenuifolia var. confertiflora, commonly known as narrowleaf sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It features small, alternate leaves that are typically narrow and linear, adapted to reduce water loss. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white or pale pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its botanical structure reflects its adaptation to harsh environmental conditions.
