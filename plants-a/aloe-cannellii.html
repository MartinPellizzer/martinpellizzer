Aloe cannellii

Aloe cannellii, commonly known as the narrow-leaf aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features rosettes of narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellowish-green flowers. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.

Aloe cannellii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
