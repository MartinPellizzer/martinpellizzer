Anthurium dominicense var. sintenisii

Anthurium dominicense var. sintenisii, commonly known as the Sintenis's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its spadix, a feature typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
