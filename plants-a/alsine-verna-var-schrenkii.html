Alsine verna var. schrenkii

Alsine verna var. schrenkii, commonly known as Schrenk's sand-verbena, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia.

It is a low-growing annual herb with erect, branched stems and small, blue to violet flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous minute seeds. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in disturbed or open habitats.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to harsh environmental conditions.
