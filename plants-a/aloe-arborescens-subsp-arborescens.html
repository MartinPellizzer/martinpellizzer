Aloe arborescens subsp. arborescens

Aloe arborescens subsp.

arborescens, commonly known as the torch aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features a branched stem with fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with red or orange margins.

The plant produces showy, orange-red inflorescences that resemble torches, blooming in summer. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its leaves contain a gel that has been used for its soothing properties in traditional medicine.
