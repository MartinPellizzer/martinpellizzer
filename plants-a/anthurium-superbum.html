Anthurium superbum

Anthurium superbum, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense, velvety texture. The plant produces striking spadix inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, which can range from deep red to orange. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Anthurium superbum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ability to thrive indoors with proper care.
