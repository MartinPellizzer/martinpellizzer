Asparagus planiuscula

Asparagus planiuscula, commonly known as the dwarf asparagus, is a small, herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features delicate, fern-like foliage and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and ease of care.

Asparagus planiuscula is also valued for its ability to tolerate drought conditions once established.
