Aberia gardneri

Aberia gardneri, commonly known as the Gardner's evergreen hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to red. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a waxy texture, while its flowers bloom throughout the year in warm climates.

Aberia gardneri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
