Agathosma berzeliifolia

Agathosma berzeliifolia, commonly known as African geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Geraniaceae and is characterized by its succulent, aromatic leaves and showy, pinkish-red flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
