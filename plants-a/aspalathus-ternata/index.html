Aspalathus Ternata

Aspalathus ternata is a unique plant native to the Western Cape region of South Africa. It is commonly known as the "red bush tea" and is widely used in traditional medicine. This small shrub produces small, bright red flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are harvested and processed to make a popular herbal infusion. Aspalathus ternata has gained international recognition for its health benefits and sustainable farming practices.
