Acianthera compressicaulis

Acianthera compressicaulis, commonly known as the compressed stem orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial herbaceous plant characterized by its slender, compressed stems and linear leaves. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers arranged in a spike, which bloom during the wet season. Native to tropical regions of South America, it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Its unique morphology and ornamental flowers make it a subject of interest in botanical studies and horticulture.
