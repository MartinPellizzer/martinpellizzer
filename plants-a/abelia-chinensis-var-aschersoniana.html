Abelia chinensis var. aschersoniana

Abelia chinensis var. aschersoniana, commonly known as the Chinese Abelia or Ascherson's Abelia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features showy, tubular, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters of white to pink hues during the summer months. The plant has opposite, ovate to elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, moist soil.

This ornamental plant is valued for its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
