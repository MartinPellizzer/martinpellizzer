Acis autumnalis

Acis autumnalis, commonly known as autumnal acis, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant has hairy, linear leaves and thrives in well-drained soils. It blooms in late summer to early autumn, adding color to gardens during the transition season.

Acis autumnalis is often used in rock gardens and as a border plant due to its compact size and ornamental flowers.
