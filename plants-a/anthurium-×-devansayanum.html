Anthurium × devansayanum

Anthurium × devansayanum, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a hybrid plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a velvety texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are the modified leaves that enclose the true flowers, often in shades of red or orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and requires consistent moisture in its soil.

Anthurium × devansayanum is popular as a ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
