Armeria maritima var. cutandiana

Armeria maritima var. cutandiana, commonly known as the coastal thrift or sea thrift, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of Europe and North Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that thrives in sandy or gravelly soils, often found in maritime climates. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pink flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, adapted to withstand salt spray and strong winds.

It is often used in coastal landscaping due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.
