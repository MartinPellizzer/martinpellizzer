Asphodelus acaulis

Asphodelus acaulis, commonly known as the naked asphodel, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of narrow, grass-like leaves. Asphodelus acaulis is known for its resilience and ability to withstand drought conditions.
