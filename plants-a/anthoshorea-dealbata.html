Anthoshorea dealbata

Anthoshorea dealbata, commonly known as the white-fruited sycamore, is a member of the Malvaceae family.

It is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy white flowers that bloom in spring. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that split open to release seeds.
