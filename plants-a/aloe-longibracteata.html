Aloe longibracteata

Aloe longibracteata, commonly known as the longibract aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, spathaceous leaves that are typically green with a bluish tint. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellow to orange flowers. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves.

Aloe longibracteata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in dry climates.
