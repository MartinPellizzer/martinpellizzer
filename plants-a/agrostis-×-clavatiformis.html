Agrostis × clavatiformis

Agrostis × clavatiformis, commonly known as the slender meadowgrass, is a hybrid grass species belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted foliage and slender, grass-like blades that are typically less than 10 cm in height. This plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, wetlands, and along streambanks. Its inflorescence consists of a single, narrow spikelet that is often curved or drooping.

Agrostis × clavatiformis is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in wetland environments.
