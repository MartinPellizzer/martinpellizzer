Amaranthus shengkuangensis

Amaranthus shengkuangensis, commonly known as the Chinese amaranth, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and vibrant red or purple foliage. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and edible leaves.

Amaranthus shengkuangensis is also valued for its resilience to drought and poor soil conditions.
