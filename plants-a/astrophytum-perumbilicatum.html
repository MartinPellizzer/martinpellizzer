Astrophytum perumbilicatum

Astrophytum perumbilicatum, commonly known as the "Star Cactus," is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, columnar stems covered in tubercles that give it a star-like appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.

Astrophytum perumbilicatum is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance requirements.
