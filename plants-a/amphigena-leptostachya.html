Amphigena leptostachya

Amphigena leptostachya, commonly known as the slender-stemmed orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or near water sources. Its leaves are usually narrow and arranged in a rosette pattern, while its stems are slender and often branching.

The flowers are small and clustered, displaying a range of colors from white to pale yellow.
