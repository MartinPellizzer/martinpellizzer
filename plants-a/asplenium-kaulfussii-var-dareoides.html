Asplenium kaulfussii var. dareoides

Asplenium kaulfussii var. dareoides, commonly known as the Dares Island spleenwort, is a fern belonging to the Aspleniaceae family.

It is a small, epiphytic or lithophytic plant, typically found growing on rocks or tree trunks in humid, shaded environments. The plant features narrow, lanceolate leaves that are often divided into pinnate segments, giving it a delicate appearance. Its fronds are usually dark green and may have a slightly glossy surface.

This variety is native to parts of Southeast Asia and is valued for its ornamental appeal in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
