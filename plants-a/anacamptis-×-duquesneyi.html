Anacamptis × duquesneyi

Anacamptis × duquesneyi, commonly known as the Duquesne orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to violet. The plant produces tall, slender stems with a few to several flowers per stem, often with a distinctive lip that is broader than the other petals. It thrives in sunny, well-drained habitats such as grasslands and open woodlands.

Anacamptis × duquesneyi is a perennial herb that blooms in late spring to early summer.
