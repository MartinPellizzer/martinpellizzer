Alsophila woodwardioides

Alsophila woodwardioides, commonly known as the slender tree fern, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and feathery fronds. The plant has a distinctive crown of pinnate fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
