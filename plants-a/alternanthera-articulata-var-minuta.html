Alternanthera articulata var. minuta

Alternanthera articulata var. minuta, commonly known as dwarf alternanthera, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to South America.

It features small, succulent leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from red to pink, depending on light exposure. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white. It thrives in moist, well-draining soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its compact size and vibrant foliage, it is a popular choice for water gardens and bog landscapes.
