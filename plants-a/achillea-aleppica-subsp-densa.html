Achillea aleppica subsp. densa

Achillea aleppica subsp.

densa, commonly known as the dense yarrow, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and dense clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers.

The plant features finely divided, gray-green leaves that are aromatic when crushed. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to gardens.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental and ecological landscaping.
