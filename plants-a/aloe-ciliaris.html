Aloe ciliaris

Aloe ciliaris, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with red or purple margins. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellow or orange flowers. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves.

Aloe ciliaris is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses.
