Alternanthera andicola

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its strikingly colored leaves, often displaying vibrant red, orange, or purple hues. Its leaves are usually opposite and lance-shaped, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Alternanthera andicola is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
