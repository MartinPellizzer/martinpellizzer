Anemonoides deltoidea

Anemonoides deltoidea, commonly known as the delta anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, white to pinkish flowers with a distinctive yellow center, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has palmately divided leaves that are typically heart-shaped or triangular in outline. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Anemonoides deltoidea is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, supporting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
