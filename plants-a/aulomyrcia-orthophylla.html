Aulomyrcia orthophylla

Aulomyrcia orthophylla, commonly known as the Mexican featherleaf, is a shrub native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its narrow, feather-like leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or limestone areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is relatively rare in the wild.
