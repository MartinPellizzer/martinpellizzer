Aspalathus forbesii

Aspalathus forbesii, commonly known as the yellow bush, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, oblong seeds that are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Aspalathus forbesii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
