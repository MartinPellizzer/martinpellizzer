Anthurium × dentatum

Anthurium × dentatum, commonly known as the red anthurium, is a hybrid plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose the spadix, which bears small, inconspicuous flowers. Anthurium × dentatum thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

It is widely cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental foliage and long-lasting inflorescences.
