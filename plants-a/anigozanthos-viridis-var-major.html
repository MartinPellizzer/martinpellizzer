Anigozanthos viridis var. major

Anigozanthos viridis var. major, commonly known as the greenhood or green-leafed kangaroo paw, is a striking perennial plant native to Western Australia.

It features elongated, strap-like leaves that are typically a vibrant green, contributing to its ornamental appeal. The plant produces distinctive, tubular flowers that are often a deep red or orange, with a hood-like structure at the base. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

This variety is prized for its longevity and ability to attract native pollinators.
