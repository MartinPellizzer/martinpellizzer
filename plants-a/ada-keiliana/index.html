Ada Keiliana

Kilimanjaro orchid, scientifically known as Ada keiliana, is a rare and striking flower found exclusively in the high-altitude regions of Mount Kilimanjaro. This unique orchid thrives in the cool, moist microclimates of Tanzania's volcanic peaks, where it adapts to extreme environmental conditions. With its delicate petals and vibrant colors, it stands out as a symbol of the region's botanical diversity. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this species from habitat loss and climate change. The Kilimanjaro orchid not only captivates botanists but also holds cultural significance for local communities.
