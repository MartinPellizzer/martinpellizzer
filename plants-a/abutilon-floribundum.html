Abutilon floribundum

Abutilon floribundum, commonly known as the velvetleaf abutilon, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height, characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and showy, lantern-shaped flowers. The plant produces clusters of yellow or orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening due to its attractive foliage and vibrant floral display.
