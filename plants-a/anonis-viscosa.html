Anonis viscosa

Anonis viscosa, commonly known as the sticky anonis, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, green, sticky leaves that are covered in fine, glandular hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a small, round berry that turns from green to red as it matures.

Anonis viscosa is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along riverbanks.
