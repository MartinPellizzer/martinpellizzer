Aster ensifer

Aster ensifer, commonly known as the swordleaf aster, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often arranged in a basal rosette, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its flowers are typically blue to purple in color, with ray florets surrounding a central disk.

Aster ensifer thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodlands and wetlands.
