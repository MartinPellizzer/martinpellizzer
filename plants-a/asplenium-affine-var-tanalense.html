Asplenium affine var. tanalense

Asplenium affine var. tanalense, commonly known as the Tanalense spleenwort, is a small, epiphytic fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It typically grows on rocks or tree trunks in humid, shaded environments. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green fronds with a serrated margin. Its spores are produced in clusters on the underside of the leaves.

This variety is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
