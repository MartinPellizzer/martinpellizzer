Acacia perangusta

Acacia perangusta, commonly known as the narrow-leaf wattle, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It features slender, zigzagging stems and small, narrow, dark green leaves. The plant produces bright yellow, fluffy flowers that appear in late winter to early spring. Acacia perangusta is well adapted to dry, sandy soils and is drought-tolerant.

It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in harsh conditions.
