Ardisia lindleyana var. angustifolia

Ardisia lindleyana var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf evergreen ardisia, is a compact shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and have a narrow, elongated shape. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers followed by bright red berries, which are attractive to birds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often used in ornamental gardening.

Its foliage remains evergreen, providing year-round visual interest in shaded garden settings.
