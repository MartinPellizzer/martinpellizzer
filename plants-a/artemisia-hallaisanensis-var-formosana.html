Artemisia hallaisanensis var. formosana

Artemisia hallaisanensis var. formosana, commonly known as the Formosan mugwort, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, gray-green foliage. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and mountainous regions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a strong, camphor-like scent.

It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties.
