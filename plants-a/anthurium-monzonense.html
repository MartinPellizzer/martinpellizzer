Anthurium monzonense

Anthurium monzonense, commonly known as the Monzonense Philodendron, is a striking tropical plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence composed of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. Its vibrant, colorful spathe can range from red to orange, often contrasting with the green spadix.

Anthurium monzonense is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid, well-lit environments.
