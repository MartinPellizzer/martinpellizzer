Adenia pseudoglobosa subsp. curvata

Adenia pseudoglobosa subsp.

curvata, commonly known as the spiral adenia, is a shrub native to South Africa. It is characterized by its distinctive spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it a unique and ornamental appearance.

The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by yellowish fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its striking foliage, it is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic value.
