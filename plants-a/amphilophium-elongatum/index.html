Amphilophium Elongatum

Longleaf philodendron, scientifically known as Amphilophium elongatum, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America. Its elongated, waxy leaves give it a unique and elegant appearance, making it a popular choice among houseplant enthusiasts. This species thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light to flourish. While it is relatively easy to care for, it can be sensitive to overwatering and temperature fluctuations. Its versatility and aesthetic appeal have made it a favored addition to both indoor and outdoor gardens.
