Adenocalymma uleanum

Adenocalymma uleanum, commonly known as the woolly sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Australia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant features hairy, fleshy leaves that help reduce water loss in its harsh environment. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and clustered in dense inflorescences.

Adenocalymma uleanum is adapted to sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland dune ecosystems.
