Arabis lyrata subsp. occidentalis

Arabis lyrata subsp.

occidentalis, commonly known as the western rockcress, is a flowering plant native to the western United States. It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with characteristic cruciform flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in alpine or arid environments. It produces small, white or yellowish flowers that bloom in early spring.
