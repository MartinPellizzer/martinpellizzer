Anthyllis vulneraria subsp. dillenii

Anthyllis vulneraria subsp.

dillenii, commonly known as the Dillenius's Scotch thistle, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae. It is characterized by its spiny, glandular stems and leaves that are typically narrow and hairy.

The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in early summer. It is native to southern Europe and is often found in dry, calcareous soils.

This subspecies is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in arid environments.
