Asplenium rhizophorum var. flabellulatum

Asplenium rhizophorum var. flabellulatum, commonly known as the fan-leaf maidenhair fern, is a variant of the common maidenhair fern.

It is a small, evergreen fern that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features delicate, finely divided, fan-shaped fronds that give it a graceful appearance. Its rhizomes are often covered with a dense, dark brown coating, which is characteristic of the species.

This variant is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
