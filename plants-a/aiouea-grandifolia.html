Aiouea grandifolia

Aiouea grandifolia, commonly known as the large-flowered aiouea, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruit is a spherical capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Aiouea grandifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
