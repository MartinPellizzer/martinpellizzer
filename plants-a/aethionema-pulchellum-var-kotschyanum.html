Aethionema pulchellum var. kotschyanum

Aethionema pulchellum var. kotschyanum, commonly known as the Kashmir velvet flower, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Himalayas.

It features compact, mat-forming growth with dense clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink and white. The foliage is characterized by its velvety texture and dark green color, which adds to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in rock gardens and alpine settings.
