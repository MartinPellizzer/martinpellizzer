Anacamptis Fragrans

Anacamptis fragrans, also known as the fragrant orchid, is a stunning member of the orchid family native to southern Europe. This perennial herb is celebrated for its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring. Its vivid purple and white petals are often adorned with intricate patterns that mimic the shape of a butterfly. The plant thrives in sunny meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. With its elegant appearance and sweet scent, it captivates both botanists and nature enthusiasts alike.
