Astrephia polystachya

Astrephia polystachya, commonly known as the flame lily, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces vibrant red or orange flowers resembling flames. The plant features narrow, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, silky hair. Astrephia polystachya thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
