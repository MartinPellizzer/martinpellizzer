Aspalathus arida var. erecta

Aspalathus arida var. erecta, commonly known as the erect aspalathus, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and small, elliptical leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun exposure.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to dry conditions.
