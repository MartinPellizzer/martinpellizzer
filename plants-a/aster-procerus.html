Aster procerus

Aster procerus, commonly known as the slender aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodlands. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are blue to purple in color.

Aster procerus is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators.
