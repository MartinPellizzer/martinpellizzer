Anaphalioides keriensis

Anaphalioides keriensis, commonly known as the Keriensis everlasting, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Ethiopian Highlands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant produces small, fluffy, white to pale yellow flowers that are arranged in dense clusters, giving it a feathery appearance. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows.

Due to its unique appearance and potential medicinal uses, it is of interest to botanists and herbalists alike.
