Ayenia fontis

Ayenia fontis, commonly known as the mountain ayenia, is a flowering plant native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in high-altitude environments with cool, moist conditions, often growing in rocky soils. Its leaves are typically narrow and evergreen, providing a lush backdrop to its delicate blooms.

Ayenia fontis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in specific, challenging climates.
