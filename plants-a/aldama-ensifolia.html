Aldama ensifolia

Aldama ensifolia, commonly known as the swordleaf aldama, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces showy yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the spring and summer months. Its leaves are long and narrow, resembling a sword, hence its common name.

Aldama ensifolia is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
