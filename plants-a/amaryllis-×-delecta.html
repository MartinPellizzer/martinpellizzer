Amaryllis × delecta

Amaryllis × delecta, commonly known as the hybrid amaryllis, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant produces a tall, sturdy stem that can reach up to 2 meters in height, bearing clusters of flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn.

Its bulbs are perennial, and with proper care, the plant can be cultivated successfully in both indoor and outdoor environments.
