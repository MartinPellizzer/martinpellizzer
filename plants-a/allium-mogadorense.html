Allium mogadorense

Allium mogadorense, commonly known as the Canary Islands garlic, is a bulbous plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, white to pale pink flowers in clusters. The plant has a strong garlic scent and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun.

Allium mogadorense is a hardy perennial that is relatively easy to grow in temperate climates.
