Asteriscus maritimus subsp. sericeus

Asteriscus maritimus subsp.

sericeus, commonly known as the silky sea aster, is a coastal plant adapted to sandy and saline environments. It features small, blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators.

The leaves are narrow and covered in fine, silky hairs, giving the plant its common name. It is typically found in dune habitats along the Atlantic coast of Europe.

This subspecies is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing coastal soils and supporting local biodiversity.
