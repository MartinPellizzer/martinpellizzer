Achyrocoma

Achyrocoma, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silvery-white leaves and daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
