Arisaema xiangchengense

Arisaema xiangchengense, commonly known as the Xiangcheng arrowhead, is a rare and ornamental plant native to China.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves and striking spadix inflorescence. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as mountain forests, where it thrives in cool, humid conditions. Its botanical features include a rhizomatous root system and showy, often colorful flowers that attract pollinators.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable species for botanical study and conservation efforts.
