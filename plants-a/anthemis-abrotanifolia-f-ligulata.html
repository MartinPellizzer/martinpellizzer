Anthemis abrotanifolia f. ligulata

Anthemis abrotanifolia f. ligulata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved tansy, is a botanical variant of the tansy plant.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small yellow flowers arranged in flat-topped clusters. This plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It is native to southern Europe and western Asia, often growing in dry, sunny habitats.

Its foliage contains essential oils and has been used traditionally for its aromatic and medicinal properties.
