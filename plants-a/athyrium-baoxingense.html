Athyrium baoxingense

Athyrium baoxingense, commonly known as the Baoxing fern, is a species of fern belonging to the Athyriaceae family.

It is native to China, specifically the Sichuan province, where it grows in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant features delicate, bipinnate fronds with a distinctive, lanceolate shape. Its spore-producing structures are located on the underside of the fronds, typical of ferns.

Athyrium baoxingense is valued for its ornamental appearance and is often cultivated in botanical gardens for its aesthetic and ecological significance.
