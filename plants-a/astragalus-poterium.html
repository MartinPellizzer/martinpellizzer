Astragalus poterium

Astragalus poterium, commonly known as yellow milkvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in dry, sandy soils. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces yellow or orange pea-like flowers. Its fruit is a legume pod that contains several seeds.

Astragalus poterium is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.
