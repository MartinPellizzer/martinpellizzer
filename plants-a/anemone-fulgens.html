Anemone fulgens

Anemone fulgens, commonly known as the flame anemone, is a striking perennial plant native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, red-orange flowers with white veins that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has palmately divided, lobed leaves that are velvety to the touch. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Anemone fulgens is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and ornamental value.
