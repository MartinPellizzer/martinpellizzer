Alsophila myosuroides

Alsophila myosuroides, commonly known as the myosotis fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate fronds. The plant has a distinctive crown of feathery fronds that can reach lengths of up to 3 meters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The fronds are typically a deep green, with a leathery texture that helps it retain moisture in its native habitat.
