Anemone commersoniana

Anemone commersoniana, commonly known as the Commerson's anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It typically grows in coastal areas and rocky habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant features showy, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are palmately divided, with a distinctive silvery-white underside.

Anemone commersoniana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and coastal landscapes.
