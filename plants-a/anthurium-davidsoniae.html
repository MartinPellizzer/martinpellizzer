Anthurium davidsoniae

Anthurium davidsoniae, commonly known as the Red Anthurium, is a striking tropical plant native to Colombia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a prominent midrib and a reddish sheath. The plant produces vibrant red spathes that enclose its yellow spadix, creating a dramatic floral display. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium davidsoniae is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
