Acelica

Acelica, commonly known as the golden groundsel, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features basal rosettes of narrow, silver-gray leaves with a fuzzy texture. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. Acelica is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and is drought-tolerant.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and ability to thrive in arid conditions.
