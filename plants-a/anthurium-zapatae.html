Anthurium zapatae

Anthurium zapatae, commonly known as the Zapata anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped spathe that ranges in color from deep red to orange. The plant produces small, spadix-like inflorescences that are typically yellow or green. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Anthurium zapatae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
