Austrosteenisia blackii var. astipella

Austrosteenisia blackii var. astipella, commonly known as the Narrow-leaved Austrosteenisia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features upright, branching stems that are often covered in a thin layer of white wax, which helps reduce water loss. The leaves are narrow, fleshy, and arranged in a rosette pattern, typical of many succulent species. It produces small, greenish-white flowers during the warmer months, and its growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
