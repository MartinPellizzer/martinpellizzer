Aster sugimotoi

Aster sugimotoi, commonly known as the Japanese aster, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides. It produces showy blue to purple flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Its foliage consists of lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture.
