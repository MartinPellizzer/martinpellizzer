Alyssum diffusum

Alyssum diffusum, commonly known as sweet Alyssum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has silvery-gray foliage and is often used in gardens for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Alyssum diffusum is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
