Asplenium ulbrichtii var. gracillimum

Asplenium ulbrichtii var. gracillimum, commonly known as the slender maidenhair fern, is a delicate and elegant fern species native to Southeast Asia.

It features finely divided, lance-shaped fronds that are typically bright green and slightly hairy. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and prefers well-drained, acidic soil. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, lace-like foliage.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
