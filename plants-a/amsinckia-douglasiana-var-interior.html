Amsinckia douglasiana var. interior

Amsinckia douglasiana var. interior, commonly known as the Interior prickly poppy, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its hairy, spiny stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, bright yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in dry, rocky habitats.
