Arachnorchis tensa

Arachnorchis tensa, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to the rainforests of northeastern Australia.

The plant produces fragrant, pale yellow flowers that bloom during the cooler months, typically from June to August. Arachnorchis tensa is a terrestrial orchid that thrives in moist, shaded environments, often growing on the forest floor.
