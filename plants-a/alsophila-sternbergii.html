Alsophila sternbergii

Alsophila sternbergii, commonly known as the giant chain fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk and large, pinnate fronds that can reach lengths of up to 3 meters. The plant has a distinctive crown of fronds that forms a dense, umbrella-like shape. Its rhizome is thick and fibrous, providing structural support and enabling it to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

Alsophila sternbergii is often found in tropical rainforests and is valued for its ornamental appearance and ecological importance.
