Avena scheuchzeri subsp. praetutiana

Avena scheuchzeri subsp.

praetutiana, commonly known as the Alpine oat, is a rare and specialized grass species native to high-altitude regions of Europe. It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves.

The plant produces small, spikelet-like inflorescences with pale green to brownish flowers. It thrives in cool, moist environments such as alpine meadows and rocky slopes.
