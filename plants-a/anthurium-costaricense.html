Anthurium costaricense

Anthurium costaricense, commonly known as the Costa Rican anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and striking spadix inflorescence. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and well-draining soil. Its foliage is often a deep green with a glossy texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe.

Anthurium costaricense is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
