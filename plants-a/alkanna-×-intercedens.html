Alkanna × intercedens

Alkanna × intercedens, commonly known as the hybrid alkanna, is a perennial plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and showy, bright orange to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, hard seeds. Native to regions of Central Asia, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
