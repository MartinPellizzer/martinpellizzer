Acacia pubicosta

Acacia pubicosta, commonly known as the silky acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, often branching stems and pairs of small, glandular leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in compact, globular clusters, which are typical of the acacia genus. Its leaves are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Acacia pubicosta is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in harsh climates.
