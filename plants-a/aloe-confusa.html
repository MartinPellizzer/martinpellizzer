Aloe confusa

Aloe confusa, commonly known as the Cape aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple margins. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow or orange flowers. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Aloe confusa is often used in traditional medicine and as an ornamental plant.
