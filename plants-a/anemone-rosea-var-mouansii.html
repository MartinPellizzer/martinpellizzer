Anemone rosea var. mouansii

Anemone rosea var. mouansii, commonly known as the Mouansis anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, pink to lilac-colored flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has compound, trifoliate leaves with serrated edges, and its stems are hairy and upright. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable addition to rock gardens or borders.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and relatively low maintenance requirements.
