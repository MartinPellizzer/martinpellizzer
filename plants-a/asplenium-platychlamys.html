Asplenium platychlamys

Asplenium platychlamys, commonly known as the broad-leaf spleenwort, is a species of fern belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its broad, leathery leaves that are typically divided into narrow segments, giving it a distinctive, intricate appearance. This plant is often found growing on rocks or tree trunks in moist, shaded environments. It thrives in temperate climates and is commonly found in regions with high humidity.

Asplenium platychlamys is a hardy, evergreen fern that is well-suited for both natural habitats and indoor cultivation.
