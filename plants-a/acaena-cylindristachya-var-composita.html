Acaena cylindristachya var. composita

Acaena cylindristachya var. composita, commonly known as the composite acaena, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense, cylindrical clusters, which are typical of the Asteraceae family. The plant produces small, spiny fruits that contain seeds, aiding in its dispersal by animals. Its leaves are usually narrow and hairy, adapted to the moist, temperate climate of its native habitat.

It is often found in forest margins and along stream banks, contributing to local biodiversity.
