Aspidistra punctatoides

Aspidistra punctatoides, commonly known as the spotted aspidistra, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features dark green, elliptical leaves with distinctive white or pale yellow spots along the midrib. The plant has a compact growth habit and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is well-suited for indoor cultivation.

Its ornamental value lies primarily in its attractive foliage rather than its flowers.
