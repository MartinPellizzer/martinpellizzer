Anthurium helleborifolium

Anthurium helleborifolium, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 feet in length, with a dark green upper surface and a lighter, often variegated underside. The plant produces striking, spatulate-shaped spathes that can range in color from deep red to orange, often with a contrasting yellow or white stripe. It thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique foliage and vibrant flowers, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
