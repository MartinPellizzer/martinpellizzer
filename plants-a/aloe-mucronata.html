Aloe mucronata

Aloe mucronata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellow flowers. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil.

Aloe mucronata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in arid gardens.
