Aloe tripetala

Aloe tripetala, commonly known as the three-lobed aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating that helps it retain moisture. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by small, red berries. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is used in traditional medicine and skincare products.

Aloe tripetala is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water to thrive.
