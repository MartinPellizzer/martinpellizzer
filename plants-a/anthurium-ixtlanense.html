Anthurium ixtlanense

Anthurium ixtlanense, commonly known as the Ixtlan arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces unique spadices, which are inflorescences composed of a spadix and a spathe. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Anthurium ixtlanense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
