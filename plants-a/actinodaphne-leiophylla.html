Actinodaphne leiophylla

Actinodaphne leiophylla, commonly known as the smooth-leafed camphor tree, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including parts of India, Indonesia, and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are typically smooth and glossy. It produces small, fragrant flowers and is valued for its aromatic properties.
