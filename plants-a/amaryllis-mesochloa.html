Amaryllis mesochloa

Amaryllis mesochloa, commonly known as the red amaryllis, is a striking flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant features strap-like, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Amaryllis mesochloa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
