Aleuritopteris Aurantiaca

Orange brake, scientifically known as Aleuritopteris aurantiaca, is a striking fern native to tropical regions of Asia and the Pacific. Its vibrant orange fronds make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and landscape design. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid climates with rich, well-drained soil. With its unique coloration and elegant foliage, it adds a tropical flair to any garden setting. Despite its beauty, it requires careful maintenance to ensure optimal growth and health.
