Ardisia argentea

Ardisia argentea, commonly known as silver ardisia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with a silvery underside, giving it its characteristic appearance. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by bright red berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its foliage and berries make it a popular choice for garden landscapes in warm climates.
