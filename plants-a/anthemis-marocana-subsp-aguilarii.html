Anthemis marocana subsp. aguilarii

Anthemis marocana subsp.

aguilarii, commonly known as the Moroccan tansy, is a perennial herb native to North Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its finely divided, aromatic leaves and yellow composite flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is valued for its medicinal properties, including antiseptic and anti-inflammatory effects.

Its unique appearance and ecological adaptability make it a notable species in its native habitat.
