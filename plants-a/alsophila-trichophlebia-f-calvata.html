Alsophila trichophlebia f. calvata

Alsophila trichophlebia f. calvata, commonly known as the smooth-leafed tree fern, is a fern species native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feather-like fronds that can reach several meters in length. The fronds are typically smooth and lack the hairy texture found in other forms of the species. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and valuable component of tropical flora.
