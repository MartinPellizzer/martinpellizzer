Athyrium ludingense

Athyrium ludingense, commonly known as the Luding fern, is a species native to China.

It belongs to the Dryopteridaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in forest understories. Its leaves are bipinnate, with a light green color and a finely divided structure.
