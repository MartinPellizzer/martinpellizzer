Arisaema takedae

It is native to the mountainous regions of Japan, specifically Honshu, where it thrives in shaded, humid environments. The plant produces a striking, hooded spathe that encloses a spadix, which is typical of the genus Arisaema. Its foliage consists of large, heart-shaped leaves that are often deeply lobed.
