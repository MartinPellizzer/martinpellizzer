Aster wolfii

Aster wolfii, commonly known as the Great Basin aster, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its showy purple to blue flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is well adapted to arid environments. It has narrow, hairy leaves and produces seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Aster wolfii is an important component of its native ecosystem, providing nectar for pollinators and habitat for small animals.
