Aorchis Spathulata Var. Wilsonii

Aorchis spathulata var. wilsonii is a rare and elegant orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. This orchid is known for its striking, lance-shaped leaves and delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. It thrives in cool, humid environments and is often found growing on rock faces or in shaded forest undergrowth. Due to habitat loss and over-collection, this variety is becoming increasingly rare in the wild. Conservation efforts are essential to ensure its survival for future generations.
