Asteriscium spinescens

Asteriscium spinescens, commonly known as the spiny aster, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of purple and blue. The plant features spiny bracts that give it its common name and help protect its delicate flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
