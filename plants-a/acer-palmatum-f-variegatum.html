Acer palmatum f. variegatum

Acer palmatum f. variegatum, commonly known as the Variegated Japanese maple, is a cultivar of the Japanese maple tree known for its striking foliage.

The plant is native to Japan and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
