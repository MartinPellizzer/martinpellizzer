Arachnorchis lobata

Arachnorchis lobata, commonly known as the lace orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, intricate floral structure and delicate, spider-like appearance. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and prefers sunny to partially shaded environments. Its flowers, which are usually pale green to yellow, are arranged in a spike and bloom during the spring.

Arachnorchis lobata is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique beauty.
