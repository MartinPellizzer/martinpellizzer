Acacia callicoma

Acacia callicoma, commonly known as the silky acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its feathery, bipinnate leaves that give it a delicate, silky appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers in globular clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. Its bark is smooth and often has a greyish or brownish hue.

Acacia callicoma is adapted to dry conditions and is often found in savannas and rocky areas.
