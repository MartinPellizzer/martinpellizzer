Azanza lampas

Azanza lampas, commonly known as the red-leaf azanza, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red, velvety leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces bright orange-yellow fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Azanza lampas is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
