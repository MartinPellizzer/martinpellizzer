Anthurium × eburneum

Anthurium × eburneum, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a hybrid plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces bright red spathes that enclose its spadix, which is often adorned with small, yellowish flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Anthurium × eburneum is popular as a ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
