Anthistiria subglabra

Anthistiria subglabra, commonly known as the smooth anthistiria, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in a fine, smooth layer of hair, giving it its species name "subglabra.". The plant is often found in dry, open habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
