Ardisia venosa

Ardisia venosa, commonly known as the glossy windowleaf, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features lance-shaped, glossy dark green leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers followed by red, fleshy berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for indoor and garden settings.
