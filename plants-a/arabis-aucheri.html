Arabis aucheri

Arabis aucheri, commonly known as candytuft, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features rosettes of silvery-gray, woolly leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. It is often grown as an ornamental plant in gardens due to its elegant look and hardiness.

Arabis aucheri prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it adaptable to various garden conditions.
