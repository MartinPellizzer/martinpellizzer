Alsine verna var. grandiflora

Alsine verna var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered alsike, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its pink to purple flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It has slender, branching stems and small, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.
