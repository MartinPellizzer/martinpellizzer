Alyssum eriophorum

Alyssum eriophorum, commonly known as the woolly alyssum, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features dense clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has hairy, silver-green leaves that give it a distinctive, soft appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Alyssum eriophorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in garden landscapes.
