Arum orientale subsp. longispathum

Arum orientale subsp.

longispathum, commonly known as the Chinese arrowhead, is a herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia. It features broad, arrow-shaped leaves that are usually a vibrant green, often with a silvery underside.

The plant produces striking, spatulate-shaped inflorescences that are typically green with a red or purple tinge. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found near water bodies.

While it is ornamental, it can become invasive in certain regions if not managed properly.
