Asteridea archeri

Asteridea archeri, commonly known as the New Zealand aster, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers. The plant features showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in coastal and alpine environments.
