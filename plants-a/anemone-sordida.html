Anemone sordida

Anemone sordida, commonly known as the gloomy anemone, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has basal leaves that are heart-shaped and covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive addition to alpine gardens and rockeries.
