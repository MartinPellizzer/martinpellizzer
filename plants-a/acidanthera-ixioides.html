Acidanthera ixioides

Acidanthera ixioides, commonly known as the common grass-of-paraguay, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often during the spring and summer months. Due to its ornamental value and ease of cultivation, it is sometimes grown in gardens and botanical collections.
