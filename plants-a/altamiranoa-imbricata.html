Altamiranoa imbricata

Altamiranoa imbricata, commonly known as the Brazilian star orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. The plant has a compact growth habit with a pseudobulb and produces multiple inflorescences throughout the growing season. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, arranged in a rosette pattern around the pseudobulb.

Altamiranoa imbricata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
