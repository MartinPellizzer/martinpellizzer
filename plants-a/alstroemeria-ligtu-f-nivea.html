Alstroemeria ligtu f. nivea

Alstroemeria ligtu f. nivea, commonly known as the white alstroemeria, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Alstroemeriaceae and is characterized by its striking white petals and vibrant green foliage. The plant produces clusters of tubular flowers that are often used in floral arrangements due to their longevity and aesthetic appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and ecologically significant in its native habitat.
