Agropyron junceum var. reflexum

Agropyron junceum var. reflexum, commonly known as the reflexed wheatgrass, is a perennial grass native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and fine, blue-green foliage. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, pale green to brownish flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in alpine and subalpine environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
