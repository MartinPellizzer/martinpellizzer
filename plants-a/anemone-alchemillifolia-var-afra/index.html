Anemone Alchemillifolia Var. Afra

Anemone alchemillifolia var. afra is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa. It is known for its striking white petals and unique, almost fern-like leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. This variety is closely related to the common anemone but exhibits subtle differences in leaf structure and bloom time. Due to its limited habitat and threatened status, it is highly valued by botanists and conservationists. Its beauty and rarity make it a subject of both scientific study and horticultural interest.
