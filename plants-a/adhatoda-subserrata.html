Adhatoda subserrata

Adhatoda subserrata, commonly known as the Indian Trumpet or Bael tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is known for its aromatic leaves and is often used in traditional medicine for its respiratory and digestive benefits.
