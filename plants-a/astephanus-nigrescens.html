Astephanus nigrescens

Astephanus nigrescens, commonly known as the black-flowered stephanus, is a striking member of the Rubiaceae family.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black flowers, which contrast beautifully with its green foliage. Native to southern Africa, this plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and full sun exposure. The flowers are tubular and attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after plant in botanical gardens and among horticultural enthusiasts.
