Astragalus lentiginosus var. multiracemosus

Astragalus lentiginosus var. multiracemosus, commonly known as the multibract astragalus, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and spiny pods. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish in color and are clustered in racemes, giving it its specific variety name.
