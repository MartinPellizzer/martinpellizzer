Adiantum lineatum

Adiantum lineatum, commonly known as the maidenhair fern, is a species of fern characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds.

It belongs to the family Adiantaceae and is native to various regions in Asia, Africa, and the Americas. The plant is known for its elegant, arching leaves that are typically dark green and have a glossy appearance. Adiantum lineatum thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires consistent humidity and well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
