Aeonium webbii

Aeonium webbii, commonly known as the White Tree Aeonium, is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped growth pattern and fleshy, thick leaves that are typically pale green to white in color. The plant produces tall, branching stems with clusters of yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Aeonium webbii is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a striking addition to both indoor and outdoor landscapes.
