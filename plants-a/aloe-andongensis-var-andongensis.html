Aloe andongensis var. andongensis

Aloe andongensis var. andongensis, commonly known as the Andong aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are thick, fleshy, and covered in a protective layer of white powder. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellowish-orange flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
