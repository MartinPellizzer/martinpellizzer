Actinocephalus glabrescens

Actinocephalus glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth-headed daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces showy, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that become smooth as the plant matures. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Actinocephalus glabrescens is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
