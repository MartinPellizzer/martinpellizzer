Agave crassispina

Agave crassispina, commonly known as the rigid spine agave, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color with sharp, spiny margins. The plant produces a tall, branched inflorescence that bears yellowish-green flowers. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Agave crassispina is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in dry climates.
