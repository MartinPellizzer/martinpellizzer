Achimenes rosea var. magnifica

Achimenes rosea var. magnifica, commonly known as the magnifica achimenes, is a striking tropical perennial native to South America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has velvety, dark green leaves that are heart-shaped and often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture to flourish.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and container planting.
