Acer mono var. marmoratum

Acer mono var. marmoratum, commonly known as the variegated maple, is a striking ornamental tree native to China.

It is characterized by its distinctive foliage, which features a mottled pattern of green, white, and pink on its leaves. The tree produces small, red samaras that mature to a yellowish-brown, adding seasonal interest. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and is often used in gardens and landscapes for its aesthetic value.
