Aloe maculata var. pulchra

Aloe maculata var. pulchra, commonly known as the "Beaded Aloe," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, pale green leaves with distinctive red or purple markings. The plant produces tall, branched flower stalks that bear yellow or orange flowers. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its compact size and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
