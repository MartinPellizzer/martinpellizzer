Asarum savatieri var. iseanum

Asarum savatieri var. iseanum, commonly known as the Irian Jaya asarum, is a perennial herb native to the lowland rainforests of Indonesia.

It features heart-shaped, hairy leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, reddish-brown flowers that are clustered in umbels, often hidden beneath the leaves. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is adapted to high humidity and warm temperatures.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
