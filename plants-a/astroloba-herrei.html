Astroloba herrei

Astroloba herrei, commonly known as the Starry Astrophytum, is a succulent native to southern Africa.

It features a thick, ribbed stem that can grow up to 3 feet tall, with a distinctive star-shaped pattern on its surface. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by small, red fruits. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its fleshy stems.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and cactus collections.
