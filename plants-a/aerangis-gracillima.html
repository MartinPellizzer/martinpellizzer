Aerangis gracillima

Aerangis gracillima, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous plant characterized by its delicate, elongated leaves and intricate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its flowers are usually pale green or white, with a subtle fragrance that attracts pollinators like bees and moths.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
