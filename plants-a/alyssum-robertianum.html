Alyssum robertianum

Alyssum robertianum, commonly known as the Sierra foothill alyssum, is a flowering plant native to California.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale purple, blooming in late spring to early summer. Alyssum robertianum thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky habitats.

Its delicate appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and native plant collections.
