Acalypha purpurascens var. genuina

Acalypha purpurascens var. genuina, commonly known as the red-leaf kochia, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features striking, velvety red or purple leaves that are often variegated with green, making it a popular ornamental plant. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish flowers that are clustered in cylindrical spikes. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, exhibiting drought tolerance once established.

Its foliage is known for its vibrant coloration, which intensifies in cooler temperatures.
