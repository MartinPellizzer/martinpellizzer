Aechmea victoriana var. discolor

Aechmea victoriana var. discolor, commonly known as the Variegated Violet Bromeliad, is a striking epiphytic plant native to South America.

It features a rosette of broad, waxy leaves that are typically green with white or silver variegation. The plant produces vibrant red or orange flowers that emerge from the center of the rosette. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil and regular misting.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
