Alocasia lobata

Alocasia lobata, commonly known as the Chinese arrowroot or giant arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length, with a deep green color and prominent veins. The plant produces small, white flowers that are followed by red berries containing seeds. Alocasia lobata thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining, rich soil.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its striking foliage.
