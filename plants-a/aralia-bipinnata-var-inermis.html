Aralia bipinnata var. inermis

Aralia bipinnata var. inermis, commonly known as the Florida aralia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features compound, bipinnate leaves that are finely divided, giving it a feathery appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in umbellate clusters, which are followed by yellowish berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetland habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in suitable climates.
