Anthericum asphodeloides

Anthericum asphodeloides, commonly known as the asphodel lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features strap-like, grass-like leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces tall, slender stems that bear clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Anthericum asphodeloides is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
