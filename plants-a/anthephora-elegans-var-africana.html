Anthephora elegans var. africana

Anthephora elegans var. africana, commonly known as the African star lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, often with a contrasting central stripe. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, with lance-shaped, dark green leaves. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is popular in garden landscapes and floral arrangements.
