Astragalus leiosemius subsp. sabzevarensis

Astragalus leiosemius subsp.

sabzevarensis, commonly known as the Sabzevar astragalus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Iran. It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, pea-like flowers that typically range in color from pale yellow to reddish-brown. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces pods that contain several seeds.

It is adapted to grow in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils and dry steppe regions.
