Allium cardiostemon

Allium cardiostemon, commonly known as the heartstem onion, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Allium genus, which includes a wide variety of onions, garlics, and related species. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of small, white or pale purple flowers. Its bulbs are often used in local cuisines and traditional medicine.

The plant is adapted to cold climates and can thrive in well-drained soils.
