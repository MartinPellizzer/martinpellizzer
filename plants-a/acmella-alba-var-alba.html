Acmella alba var. alba

Acmella alba var. alba, commonly known as the "spider lily," is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow petals and spiky centers. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its unique appearance and ease of cultivation contribute to its ornamental value.
