Asperula paui subsp. dianensis

Asperula paui subsp.

dianensis, commonly known as the Dianense Azalea, is a flowering plant native to China. It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit.

The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in spring. It prefers acidic, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its evergreen foliage and long flowering period.
