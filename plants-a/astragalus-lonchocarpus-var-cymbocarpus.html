Astragalus lonchocarpus var. cymbocarpus

Astragalus lonchocarpus var. cymbocarpus, commonly known as the narrowpod milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in sagebrush steppe and grassland ecosystems.
