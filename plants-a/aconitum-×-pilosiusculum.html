Aconitum × pilosiusculum

Aconitum × pilosiusculum, commonly known as the silky aconite, is a hybrid perennial plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant produces showy, blue to purple flowers in late summer, arranged in dense, conical inflorescences. Its leaves are deeply lobed and covered with fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe, it thrives in well-drained, moist soils and full sun to partial shade.
