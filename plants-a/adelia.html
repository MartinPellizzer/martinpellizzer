Adelia

Adelia, commonly known as the Adelia flower, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the family Acanthaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas. The plant typically features showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. Adelia species are often grown for their ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

They prefer well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
