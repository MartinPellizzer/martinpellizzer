Assonia Ameliae

Amelia's assonia, scientifically known as Assonia ameliae, is a rare and unique flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa. This striking species is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of red and orange, attracting a variety of pollinators. Its name honors Amelia, a botanist who first documented its discovery in the early 20th century. Despite its beauty, Assonia ameliae is critically endangered due to habitat loss and over-collection. Conservation efforts are underway to protect and propagate this rare botanical gem.
