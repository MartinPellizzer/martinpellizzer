Adesmia Peraltae

Adesmia peraltae is a rare and lesser-known flowering plant native to the arid regions of South America. It belongs to the legume family, known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil. This plant thrives in harsh environments, showcasing remarkable adaptability to extreme conditions. Its delicate pink or white flowers bloom during brief periods of rainfall, making it a symbol of resilience. Due to habitat loss, Adesmia peraltae is now classified as critically endangered, highlighting the urgent need for conservation efforts.
