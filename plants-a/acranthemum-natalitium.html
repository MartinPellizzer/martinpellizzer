Acranthemum natalitium

Acranthemum natalitium, commonly known as the Natal starwort, is a small, herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. The plant features small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette formation.

Acranthemum natalitium is adapted to arid conditions and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions.
