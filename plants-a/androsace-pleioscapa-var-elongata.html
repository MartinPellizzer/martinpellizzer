Androsace pleioscapa var. elongata

Androsace pleioscapa var. elongata, commonly known as the elongated alpine bellflower, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

This variety is notable for its elongated stems, which distinguish it from other varieties within the species.
