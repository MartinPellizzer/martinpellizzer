Aster uniformis

Aster uniformis, commonly known as the uniform aster, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads composed of many small florets. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and features narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are usually blue to purple in color and bloom in late summer to early fall.

Aster uniformis is often found in open woods, fields, and along roadsides, thriving in well-drained soils.
