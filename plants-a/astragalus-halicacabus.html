Astragalus halicacabus

Astragalus halicacabus, commonly known as goat's head, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky, and arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in dense heads, giving it its common name. Its root system is deep and fibrous, allowing it to thrive in nutrient-poor soils.

Astragalus halicacabus is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
