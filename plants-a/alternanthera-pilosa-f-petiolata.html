Alternanthera pilosa f. petiolata

Alternanthera pilosa f. petiolata, commonly known as the red-leaf alternanthera, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or pink foliage, which contrasts beautifully with its narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sun exposure. It is often cultivated in water gardens or as a bog plant due to its preference for wet conditions.

Its rapid growth and colorful appearance make it a popular choice for aquatic and terrestrial landscaping.
