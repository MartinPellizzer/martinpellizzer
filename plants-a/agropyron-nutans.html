Agropyron nutans

Agropyron nutans, commonly known as the nodding brome, is a perennial grass native to Eurasia.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. The plant has slender, tufted stems that often bend at the base, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its small, spikelet-based inflorescence is characteristic of the Poaceae family.

Agropyron nutans is known for its adaptability and is sometimes used in restoration ecology due to its resilience.
