Acmella radicans var. debilis

Acmella radicans var. debilis, commonly known as the slender triggerplant, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its small, showy yellow flowers that resemble triggers, hence its common name. Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is often cultivated in gardens for ornamental purposes.
