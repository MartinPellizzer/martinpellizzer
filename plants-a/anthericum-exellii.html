Anthericum exellii

Anthericum exellii, commonly known as the Exell's arum, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. Its flowers are small and clustered on the spadix, with male and female flowers typically separated.

Anthericum exellii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm, humid environments.
