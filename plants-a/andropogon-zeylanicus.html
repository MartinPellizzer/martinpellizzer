Andropogon zeylanicus

Andropogon zeylanicus, commonly known as Ceylon grass, is a tall perennial grass native to southern Asia.

It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant is often found in open, sunny habitats such as fields and roadsides. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.

Its botanical characteristics make it adaptable to various soil types and climatic conditions.
