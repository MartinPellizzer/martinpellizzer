Austrodanthonia penicillata

Austrodanthonia penicillata, commonly known as silver grass, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its fine, silvery-blue foliage and tufted inflorescence, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant is well adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland grasslands. Its leaves are narrow and wiry, with a dense, grass-like texture.

Austrodanthonia penicillata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in landscaping and restoration projects.
