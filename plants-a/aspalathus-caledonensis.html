Aspalathus caledonensis

Aspalathus caledonensis, commonly known as the Caledon bushveld pea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in dry, rocky areas and is characterized by its small, pea-shaped flowers. The plant has compound leaves with several leaflets and produces small, yellowish-white blooms. It is well adapted to arid conditions and is often found in fynbos vegetation.

Aspalathus caledonensis is not widely cultivated but is of interest in botanical studies due to its unique adaptations and habitat.
