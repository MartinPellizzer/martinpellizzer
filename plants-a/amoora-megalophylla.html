Amoora megalophylla

Amoora megalophylla, commonly known as the large-leafed hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, yellow, and orange, which bloom throughout the year. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Amoora megalophylla is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking foliage and vibrant blooms.
