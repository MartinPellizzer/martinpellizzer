Aloe angolensis

Aloe angolensis, commonly known as the Angola aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves for survival.

Aloe angolensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
