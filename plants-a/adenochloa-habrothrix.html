Adenochloa habrothrix

Adenochloa habrothrix, commonly known as the silky bluegrass, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its fine, bluish-green blades and compact growth habit. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands. Its inflorescence consists of dense, spike-like clusters of small, blue-green flowers.

Adenochloa habrothrix is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant landscaping.
