Anthurium aucanum

Anthurium aucanum, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to South America, specifically Peru and Bolivia.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of spadices, which are typically yellow or orange, surrounded by colorful bracts that resemble flowers. Anthurium aucanum thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
