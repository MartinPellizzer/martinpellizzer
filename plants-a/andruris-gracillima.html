Andruris gracillima

Andruris gracillima, commonly known as the slender sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its delicate, linear leaves and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in coastal and inland desert regions, where it adapts to arid conditions.
