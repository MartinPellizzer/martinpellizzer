Alsophila horridula

Alsophila horridula, commonly known as the rough tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant has a distinctive rough, scaly trunk that is often covered in a layer of old leaf bases. Its fronds are typically dark green and can grow up to several meters in length.

Alsophila horridula thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.
