Alstroemeria espigonensis

Alstroemeria espigonensis, commonly known as the Espinosa alstroemeria, is a flowering plant native to South America, specifically found in the Andean regions of Ecuador and Peru.

It belongs to the family Alstroemeriaceae and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, often in rocky or semi-arid habitats.

Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves, and it is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
