Arrowsmithia

Arrowsmithia, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves and small, clustered flowers. These plants are typically found in arid or semi-arid regions, thriving in well-drained soils. Arrowsmithia species are often used in xeriscaping due to their low water requirements.

They contribute to biodiversity in their native habitats and are sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
