Aralia tengyuehensis

Aralia tengyuehensis, commonly known as the Yunnan aralia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and sparsely branched stems. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in umbels, which are typically followed by spherical berries.

It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is valued for its ornamental qualities and traditional medicinal uses.
