Adenocarpus intermedius proles commutatus

Adenocarpus intermedius proles commutatus, commonly known as the small-flowered grassvetch, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, pea-like flowers. The plant has trifoliate leaves and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in grassland habitats.

Its botanical features make it an important component of local ecosystems and a subject of study in plant taxonomy.
