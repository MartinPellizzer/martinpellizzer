Alnaster

Alnaster, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a member of the Araliaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and features glossy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity to flourish.

Alnaster is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
