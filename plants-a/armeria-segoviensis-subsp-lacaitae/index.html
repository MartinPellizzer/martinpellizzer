Armeria Segoviensis Subsp. Lacaitae

Armeria segoviensis subsp. lacaitae is a rare and fascinating plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe. It belongs to the carnation family, known for its vibrant and showy flowers. This subspecies is distinguished by its unique floral structure and compact growth habit. Due to habitat loss and climate change, it faces significant conservation challenges. Studying this plant offers valuable insights into botanical diversity and ecological adaptation.
