Agathosma phillipsii

Agathosma phillipsii, commonly known as Cape chamomile, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, small yellow flowers. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is often found in sandy or rocky soils. It is valued for its essential oils, which are used in aromatherapy and traditional medicine.

Its resilience to drought makes it a popular choice for xeriscaping in arid regions.
