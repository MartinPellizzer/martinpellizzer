Agrostis alba var. verticillata

Agrostis alba var. verticillata, commonly known as the silver hair grass, is a grass species characterized by its distinctive silvery-white inflorescence.

It is a perennial plant native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere. The plant typically grows in open, moist habitats such as meadows and wetlands. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-gray hue.

The inflorescence consists of a dense, cylindrical cluster of spikelets arranged in a whorled pattern, giving it a unique appearance.
