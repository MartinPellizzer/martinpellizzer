Azorella fuegiana

Azorella fuegiana, commonly known as the Magellanic snowbell, is a hardy shrub native to the southern regions of South America.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family and is characterized by its dense, cushion-like growth form that helps it survive in harsh, cold environments. Azorella fuegiana is well adapted to high-altitude, rocky habitats and is often found in the Andes and Patagonian regions.
