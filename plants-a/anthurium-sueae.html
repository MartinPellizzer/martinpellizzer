Anthurium sueae

Anthurium sueae, commonly known as the Sueae Anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves with a deep green color and prominent veins. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, surrounded by a colorful spathe that enhances its ornamental appeal.

Anthurium sueae thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
