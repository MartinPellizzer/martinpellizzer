Aster see-burejensis

Aster see-burejensis, commonly known as the Burej Mountains aster, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in alpine environments, where it thrives in well-drained soils and cool climates. Its flowers are usually blue to violet in color, with ray florets surrounding a central disc.

Aster see-burejensis is of interest to botanists due to its limited distribution and potential ecological significance in its native habitat.
