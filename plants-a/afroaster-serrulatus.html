Afroaster serrulatus

Afroaster serrulatus, commonly known as the serrated aster, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, serrated leaves and produces showy yellow flowers with a central disk. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops.

Afroaster serrulatus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
