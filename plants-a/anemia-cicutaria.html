Anemia cicutaria

Anemia cicutaria, commonly known as yellow rocket, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and fern-like leaves, which are often used in herbal medicine. The plant is native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa, and it thrives in moist, disturbed soils. Its growth habit is herbaceous, with an upright stem that can reach up to 1 meter in height.

Anemia cicutaria is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
