Aniba fragrans

Aniba fragrans, commonly known as the fragrant aniba, is a species of flowering plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes other aromatic plants like cinnamon and camphor. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, clustered white flowers that emit a subtle fragrance. Aniba fragrans is often found in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Its essential oils are also used in traditional medicine and perfumery.
