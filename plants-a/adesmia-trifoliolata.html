Adesmia trifoliolata

Adesmia trifoliolata, commonly known as the three-leaf burr.

It is a shrub native to South America, characterized by its trifoliate leaves and spiny stems. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruits are follicles that contain several seeds, often persisting on the plant for an extended period.

Adesmia trifoliolata is often found in dry, arid environments and is adapted to thrive in poor soils.
