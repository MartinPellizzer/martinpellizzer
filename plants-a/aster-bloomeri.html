Aster bloomeri

Aster bloomeri, commonly known as the rock aster, is a perennial herb native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its drought tolerance.

Aster bloomeri is a valuable addition to xeriscapic gardens due to its hardiness and ornamental value.
