Alchemilla minor f. aestivalis

Alchemilla minor f. aestivalis, commonly known as the early alchemilla, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to July. Its leaves are velvety and dark green, often covered in fine hairs, and it thrives in moist, shady environments.

Alchemilla minor f. aestivalis is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and ability to stabilize soil in wet areas.
