Aralia cephalobotrys

Aralia cephalobotrys, commonly known as the giant aralia, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, compound leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in length, giving it a dramatic appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that contains several seeds.

Aralia cephalobotrys is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
