Achoriphragma Saposhnikovii

Achoriphragma saposhnikovii is a rare and enigmatic flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of central Asia. Known for its delicate, bell-shaped flowers and unique growth habits, it thrives in harsh, high-altitude environments. This species has captured the interest of botanists and conservationists due to its limited distribution and ecological significance.
