Alyssum montanum var. speciosum

Alyssum montanum var. speciosum, commonly known as the Sierra Alyssum, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs.

This variety is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
