Aster nobilis

Aster nobilis, commonly known as the European aster, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters, featuring upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. Its showy flowers, which bloom in late summer to early autumn, consist of both ray and disk florets, creating a vibrant display of purple or blue.

Aster nobilis is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
