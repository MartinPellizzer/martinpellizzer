Astragalus xijiangensis

Astragalus xijiangensis, commonly known as the Xijiang milkvetch, is a flowering plant species native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant is typically found in arid or semi-arid regions, often growing in rocky or sandy soils.

It plays a role in soil stabilization and is of interest for its potential ecological and medicinal properties.
