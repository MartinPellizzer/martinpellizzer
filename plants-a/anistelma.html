Anistelma

Anistelma, commonly known as the marsh mallow, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It typically grows in wet or marshy environments, preferring moist soils and full sun to partial shade. Its flowers are often visited by bees and other pollinators, contributing to its ecological importance.

Anistelma is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its soothing properties.
