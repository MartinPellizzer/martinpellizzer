Apalochlamys spectabilis

Apalochlamys spectabilis, commonly known as the showy apalochlamys, is a striking member of the Apocynaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in well-drained soils and full sun. Its leaves are usually opposite and ovate, with a glossy surface that enhances its ornamental appeal.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
