Artanthe mollicoma f. glabrata

Artanthe mollicoma f. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth artanthe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, gray-green leaves that are densely packed and covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of pink and red during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
