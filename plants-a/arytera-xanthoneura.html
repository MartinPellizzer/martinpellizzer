Arytera xanthoneura

Arytera xanthoneura, commonly known as the yellow-flowered arctotis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant typically grows in grasslands and open areas, thriving in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the stems are upright and often branched.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
