Alsophila petiolulata

Alsophila petiolulata, commonly known as the slender tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk and large, pinnately compound fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant has a distinctive crown of fertile fronds that produce spores, typical of ferns. Its rhizome is robust and supports the growth of the plant in moist, shaded environments.

Alsophila petiolulata is often found in tropical rainforests and is valued for its ornamental appearance.
