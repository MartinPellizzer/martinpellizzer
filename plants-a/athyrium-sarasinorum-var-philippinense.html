Athyrium sarasinorum var. philippinense

Athyrium sarasinorum var. philippinense, commonly known as the Philippine sword fern, is a fern species native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Dryopteridaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its rhizomes are often covered in a dense, hairy layer, which helps in moisture retention.

The spores are produced in sori that are clustered on the underside of the fronds, typical of the fern life cycle.
