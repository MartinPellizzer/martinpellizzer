Asplenium firmum

Asplenium firmum, commonly known as the firm fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its compact, dense foliage. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in forest understories. Its fronds are dark green and leathery, with a distinctive, upright growth habit.

Asplenium firmum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
