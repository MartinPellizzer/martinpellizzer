Arduina campenonii

Arduina campenonii, commonly known as the Mediterranean arduina, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Rhamnaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, leathery leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically reddish when mature. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal areas.
