Arenaria rubicunda

Arenaria rubicunda, commonly known as the red sandwort, is a flowering plant native to arctic and alpine regions.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically features pink or red flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are usually succulent and adapted to withstand cold and dry conditions.

Arenaria rubicunda plays an important role in stabilizing soil in its harsh environments.
