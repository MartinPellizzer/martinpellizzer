Abutilon aristulosum f. albiflorum

Abutilon aristulosum f. albiflorum, commonly known as the whiteflower velvetleaf, is a variant of the velvetleaf species native to tropical regions.

It is a shrub or small tree characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and showy, white flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in botanical gardens and ecological studies.
