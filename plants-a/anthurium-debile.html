Anthurium debile

Anthurium debile, commonly known as the slender anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are modified leaves that enclose the true flowers, often in shades of red or orange. Anthurium debile thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is a popular ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
