Agave hurteri

Agave hurteri, commonly known as the Hurter's agave, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped growth pattern and thick, fleshy leaves that are often blue-green in color. The plant produces a tall flowering stalk that bears yellow flowers, which appear in the spring. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
