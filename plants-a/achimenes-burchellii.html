Achimenes burchellii

Achimenes burchellii, commonly known as the flame violet, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces vibrant, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in warm, humid conditions and requires well-draining soil.

Achimenes burchellii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its striking floral display.
