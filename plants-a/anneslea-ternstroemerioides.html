Anneslea ternstroemerioides

Anneslea ternstroemerioides, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with yellow or white streaks. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by spadices, typical of the arum family.

Anneslea ternstroemerioides is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
