Athyrium cyclosorum

Athyrium cyclosorum, commonly known as the zigzag water fern, is a species of fern native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the family Athyriaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its rhizomes are often submerged, and the fronds are arranged in a distinctive zigzag pattern.

Athyrium cyclosorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in aquatic gardens.
