Anthurium reflexum

Anthurium reflexum, commonly known as the reflexed anthurium, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence consisting of a spadix and spathe. The plant features dark green, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and prominent veins. Its flowers are typically greenish-white with a yellow spadix, and it produces berries that turn red when mature.

Anthurium reflexum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in warm, humid environments.
