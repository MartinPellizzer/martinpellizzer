Anacamptis × lesbiensis

Anacamptis × lesbiensis, commonly known as the Lesbian Orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically blooms in late spring to early summer, producing clusters of violet or purple-colored petals. It thrives in calcareous soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Anacamptis × lesbiensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
