Aster filifolius

Aster filifolius, commonly known as slenderleaf aster, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and showy blooms. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is known for its delicate, pale blue to violet flowers. Its leaves are narrow and linear, giving it a grass-like appearance.

Aster filifolius is often found in open woodlands and meadows, where it contributes to the biodiversity of its ecosystem.
