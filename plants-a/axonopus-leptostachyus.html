Axonopus leptostachyus

Axonopus leptostachyus, commonly known as the slender-stalked axonopus, is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to brown flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it a useful species for erosion control and ornamental gardening.
