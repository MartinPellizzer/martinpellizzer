Alocasia pangeran

Alocasia pangeran, commonly known as the Pangeran Alocasia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces striking inflorescences with a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture in its soil.

Alocasia pangeran is a popular ornamental plant due to its dramatic foliage and ease of care in suitable conditions.
