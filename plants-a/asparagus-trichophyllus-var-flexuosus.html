Asparagus trichophyllus var. flexuosus

Asparagus trichophyllus var. flexuosus, commonly known as the soft asparagus fern, is a varietal form of the asparagus plant characterized by its flexible, arching stems.

It is native to southern Africa and thrives in warm, humid climates. The plant produces small, inconspicuous green flowers that are followed by red berries. Its foliage consists of feathery, glossy leaves that give it a lush, ornamental appearance.

It is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden areas due to its adaptability and aesthetic appeal.
