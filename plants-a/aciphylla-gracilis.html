Aciphylla gracilis

Aciphylla gracilis, commonly known as the slender cushion plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to New Zealand and is characterized by its low-growing, dense rosette of silver-gray leaves. Aciphylla gracilis is adapted to harsh alpine and subalpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils and full sun.
