Acuan arborescens

Acuan arborescens, commonly known as the tree hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its botanical features include fibrous roots and a woody base, which contribute to its resilience in various climates.
