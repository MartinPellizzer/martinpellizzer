Acrostichum virens var. costatum

Acrostichum virens var. costatum, commonly known as the costate fern, is a variant of the common brake fern.

It is a terrestrial fern native to tropical regions of the Americas. The plant features lance-shaped, serrated leaves that are often slightly curled at the edges. Its fronds are typically bright green with a distinctive wavy margin.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.
