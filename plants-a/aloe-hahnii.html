Aloe hahnii

Aloe hahnii, commonly known as the "Lion's Tail," is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy rosettes of dark green leaves with white-tipped margins that resemble a lion's tail. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellow flowers, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. Aloe hahnii is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect light.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting due to its ornamental appearance and low maintenance requirements.
