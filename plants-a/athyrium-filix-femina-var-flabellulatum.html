Athyrium filix-femina var. flabellulatum

Athyrium filix-femina var. flabellulatum, commonly known as the Japanese painted fern, is a variant of the lady fern characterized by its striking, colorful fronds.

It features a delicate, feathery appearance with fronds that often display a mix of green, brown, and gold hues. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and is native to eastern Asia. Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full shade for optimal growth.
