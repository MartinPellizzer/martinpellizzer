Artemisia koidzumii

Artemisia koidzumii, commonly known as Japanese tarragon, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It features silvery-gray, aromatic foliage with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are densely covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flower heads that develop into fluffy, seed-bearing structures. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its distinctive scent and ornamental value make it a popular choice in horticulture.
