Abies fargesii var. sutchuenensis

Abies fargesii var. sutchuenensis, commonly known as the Sichuan fir, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to the mountainous regions of Sichuan, China.

It is characterized by its slender, conical shape and dark green, needle-like leaves that are arranged in spirals along the branches. The tree produces cones that are typically cylindrical and measure up to 5 centimeters in length. It thrives in cool, moist climates and is often found at high elevations in forested areas.

The Sichuan fir plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing habitat for various wildlife species and contributing to forest biodiversity.
