Adesmia globosa

Adesmia globosa, commonly known as the globular legume, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically white or pale purple in color. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three small leaflets, and it belongs to the Fabaceae family.

Adesmia globosa is adapted to survive in harsh conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
