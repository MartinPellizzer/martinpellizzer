Astragalus barba-mosis

Astragalus barba-mosis, commonly known as the beard vetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its erect, spiny stems and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is native to regions of the Mediterranean and is often found in rocky or dry soils.

Astragalus barba-mosis is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in arid environments.
