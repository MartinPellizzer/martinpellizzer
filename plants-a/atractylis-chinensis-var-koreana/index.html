Atractylis Chinensis Var. Koreana

Atractylis chinensis var. koreana is a lesser-known but fascinating plant native to parts of East Asia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its hairy stems and yellow flowers. This variety is often found in rocky or dry soils, thriving in temperate climates. Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a valuable addition to both gardens and ecological studies. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in horticultural literature.
