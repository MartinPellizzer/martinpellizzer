Angraecopsis gracillima

Angraecopsis gracillima, commonly known as the slender star orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its elegant, star-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink, often with a contrasting purple or violet lip. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid conditions. It produces long, slender stems that bear clusters of small, fragrant flowers, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
