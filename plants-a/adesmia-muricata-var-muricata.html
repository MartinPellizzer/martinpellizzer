Adesmia muricata var. muricata

Adesmia muricata var. muricata, commonly known as the rough adesmia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub characterized by its hairy, often glandular stems and leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in arid or semi-arid environments and is adapted to poor soil conditions.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in ecological and horticultural studies.
