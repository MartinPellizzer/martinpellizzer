Astronium obliquum

Astronium obliquum, commonly known as the oblique spondias, is a small to medium-sized tree native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Anacardiaceae family, which includes plants known for their toxic sap and aromatic properties. The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
