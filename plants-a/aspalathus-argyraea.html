Aspalathus argyraea

Aspalathus argyraea, commonly known as silverleaf bush, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its small, pale green leaves and clusters of yellow flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in coastal and sandy environments. It has a compact growth habit and is sometimes used in ornamental gardening.

The species is also valued for its ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
