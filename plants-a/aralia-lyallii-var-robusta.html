Aralia lyallii var. robusta

Aralia lyallii var. robusta, commonly known as the Lyall's aralia, is a deciduous shrub native to New Zealand.

It features compound leaves with a distinctive, palmate shape, often divided into several lobes. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in an umbel, which are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
