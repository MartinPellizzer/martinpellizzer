Arisaema sazensoo var. magnidens

Arisaema sazensoo var. magnidens, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a striking member of the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and distinctive spadix inflorescence. Native to Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its botanical features include a robust rhizome system and showy, often colorful bracts that surround the flower cluster.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has cultural significance in some regions.
