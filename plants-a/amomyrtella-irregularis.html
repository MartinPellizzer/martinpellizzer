Amomyrtella irregularis

Amomyrtella irregularis, commonly known as the irregular myrtle, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the subtropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and glossy, dark green leaves. It typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
