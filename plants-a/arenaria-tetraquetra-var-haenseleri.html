Arenaria tetraquetra var. haenseleri

Arenaria tetraquetra var. haenseleri, commonly known as the rock sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arctic and alpine regions.

It features small, white or pink flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to July. The plant has a mat-forming habit with dense, compact foliage that is often covered in fine hairs. Its leaves are linear and succulent, adapted to retain moisture in harsh environments.

It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in mountainous or tundra-like habitats.
