Anthericum nutans

Anthericum nutans, commonly known as the nodding ladies' tresses, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and long, pendulous inflorescences that bear small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wet meadows. Its rhizomatous root system allows it to spread vegetatively, forming dense colonies.

Anthericum nutans is often found in temperate regions of Europe and Asia, where it thrives in humid, nutrient-rich soils.
