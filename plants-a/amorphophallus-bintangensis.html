Amorphophallus bintangensis

Amorphophallus bintangensis, commonly known as the Bintangensis arrowhead, is a herbaceous plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces a striking, spadix-like inflorescence that is often covered in a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
