Atriplex patula var. hastifolia

Atriplex patula var. hastifolia, commonly known as the narrowleaf saltbush, is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

It is characterized by its gray-green, succulent leaves that are adapted to reduce water loss in dry environments. The plant produces small, greenish flowers in dense clusters, which are typically wind-pollinated. It thrives in saline soils and is often found in desert scrub and grassland habitats.

This species is valued for its ability to stabilize soil and provide forage for wildlife.
