Aiouea hirsuta

Aiouea hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy aiouea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows up to 10 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves that are often elliptical or ovate in shape. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters.

The fruit is a small, capsule-like structure that contains several seeds.
