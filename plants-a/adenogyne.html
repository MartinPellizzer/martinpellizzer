Adenogyne

Adenogyne, commonly known as the golden rain tree, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, flat pods that contain seeds, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Adenogyne thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
