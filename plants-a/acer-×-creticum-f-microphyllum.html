Acer × creticum f. microphyllum

Acer × creticum f. microphyllum, commonly known as the miniature maple, is a compact cultivar of the maple genus.

It features small, ovate leaves that are typically a vibrant green during the growing season. The plant produces delicate, red-bracted flowers in the spring, followed by small, winged seeds. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and containers.

This variety is well-suited for smaller spaces due to its compact growth habit.
