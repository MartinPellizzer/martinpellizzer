Adesmia ovallensis

Adesmia ovallensis, commonly known as the oval leaf burr legume, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes many legumes known for their nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant features small, pea-like flowers that are typically yellow or pink in color. Its leaves are compound, with oval-shaped leaflets, and it produces burr-like pods that contain seeds.

Adesmia ovallensis is often found in dry, arid environments and is adapted to thrive in poor soil conditions.
