Astrophytum myriostigma var. minimiflorum

Astrophytum myriostigma var. minimiflorum, commonly known as the miniature flowered star cactus, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a spherical stem with a ribbed structure and is covered in white to pale green spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its unique appearance and slow growth make it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
