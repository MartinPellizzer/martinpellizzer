Agave chrysantha

Agave chrysantha, commonly known as the yellow agave, is a perennial succulent native to Mexico.

It features a rosette of thick, fleshy, blue-green leaves with a distinctive yellowish hue. The plant produces a tall, branched inflorescence with showy yellow to orange flowers. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it well-suited for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
