Atitara horrida

Atitara horrida, commonly known as the rough atitara, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of northern Australia.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its woody structure and small, glandular leaves. Atitara horrida is also known for its aromatic oils, which contribute to its ecological and potential medicinal significance.
