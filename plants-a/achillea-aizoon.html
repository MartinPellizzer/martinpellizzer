Achillea aizoon

Achillea aizoon, commonly known as narrow-leaved yarrow, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a robust root system and upright, branched stems that can grow up to 1 meter in height. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in flat-topped clusters called corymbs. Its leaves are linear and aromatic, often covered in fine hairs.

Achillea aizoon is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
