Arisaema fallax

Arisaema fallax, commonly known as the Chinese thread-leaf arrowroot, is a herbaceous plant native to China.

It features a short, rhizomatous stem and heart-shaped, arrowhead-like leaves that are often deeply lobed. The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a spathe, typical of the Araceae family. Its flowers are unisexual, with male and female flowers located on separate parts of the spadix.

Arisaema fallax is valued for its ornamental foliage and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
