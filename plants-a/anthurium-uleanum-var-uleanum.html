Anthurium uleanum var. uleanum

Anthurium uleanum var. uleanum, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, velvety, heart-shaped leaves that are typically deep green with a reddish or purple tinge on the underside. The plant produces striking spadices, which are inflorescences that resemble a spike of flowers, often in shades of red or yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light to flourish.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
