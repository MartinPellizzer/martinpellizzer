Agalmyla glabra

Agalmyla glabra, commonly known as the smooth agalmyla, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are usually pale blue or purple and appear in clusters during the summer months.

Agalmyla glabra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
