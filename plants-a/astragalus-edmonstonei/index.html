Astragalus Edmonstonei

Astragalus edmonstonei is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of western North America. It is known for its small, intricate blossoms that bloom in late spring and early summer. This species is often found in rocky soils and high elevation environments where competition for resources is minimal. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this unique member of the legume family.
