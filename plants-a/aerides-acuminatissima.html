Aerides acuminatissima

Aerides acuminatissima, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and elongated, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Aerides acuminatissima is often cultivated for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
