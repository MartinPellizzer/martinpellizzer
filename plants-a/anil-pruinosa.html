Anil pruinosa

Anil pruinosa, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America.

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-gray leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically purple or blue in color. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Anil pruinosa is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage.
