Astrantia heterophylla

Astrantia heterophylla, commonly known as the variable aster, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern North America.

It features distinctive, palmate leaves that vary in shape and size depending on light conditions. The plant produces showy, flat-topped clusters of white to pale purple flowers in late summer. It thrives in moist, woodland habitats and is often found in shaded to partially shaded environments.

Astrantia heterophylla is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
