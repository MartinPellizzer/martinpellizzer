Aeschynanthus sikkimensis

Aeschynanthus sikkimensis, commonly known as the Sikkim ivy or Sikkim gloxinia, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan regions of India and Nepal.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and attractive, tubular flowers. Aeschynanthus sikkimensis is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
