Acrachne sundararajii

Acrachne sundararajii, commonly known as the Malabar starflower, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features small, star-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, blooming in clusters. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a rough texture.

Acrachne sundararajii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
