Aphelandra hydromestus

Aphelandra hydromestus, commonly known as the water lily aphelandra, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated leaves that are often a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistently moist soil.

Aphelandra hydromestus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying properties.
