Arkezostis grandifolia

Arkezostis grandifolia, commonly known as the grandleaf arkezostis, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, which bloom in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in dense forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
