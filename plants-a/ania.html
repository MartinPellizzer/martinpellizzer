Ania

Ania, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a tropical plant native to parts of Asia and the Pacific.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its striking silver-gray leaves that shimmer in the sunlight. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by capsule-like fruits containing seeds. Ania thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
