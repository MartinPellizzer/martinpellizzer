Anaphrenium dispar

Anaphrenium dispar, commonly known as the narrow-leafed pincushion.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, characterized by its dense, spiny heads of yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its drought-resistant adaptations.

The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that release seeds when mature.
