Acer palmatum f. linearilobum

Acer palmatum f. linearilobum, commonly known as the Japanese maple with linear lobes, is a distinctive cultivar of the Japanese maple species.

It is characterized by its elongated, narrow leaf lobes that give it a unique, almost fern-like appearance. The plant typically features vibrant red or orange foliage during the autumn season, making it a popular ornamental choice. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, preferring cooler climates.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in garden landscapes for its aesthetic appeal.
