Alsine verna var. orthophylla

Alsine verna var. orthophylla, commonly known as the spring alsike clover, is a flowering plant native to the northern regions of North America.

It is a low-growing annual herb that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in early spring. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.
