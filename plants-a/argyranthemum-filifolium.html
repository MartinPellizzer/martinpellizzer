Argyranthemum filifolium

Argyranthemum filifolium, commonly known as the silverleaf gerbera, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and showy daisy-like flowers that come in shades of white, yellow, and orange. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive appearance. It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its unique foliage and vibrant blooms make it a standout in both floral arrangements and outdoor settings.
