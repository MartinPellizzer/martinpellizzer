Alyxia ruscifolia var. ulicina

Alyxia ruscifolia var. ulicina, commonly known as the Florida anise tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to the southeastern United States.

It features opposite, simple, ovate to elliptic leaves that are often aromatic and have a slightly rough texture. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers arranged in clusters, followed by small, spherical berries that turn from green to red as they mature. Its botanical structure includes a woody stem with smooth, gray bark and a dense canopy of foliage.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in coastal and sandy soils.
