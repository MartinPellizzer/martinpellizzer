Abutilon stellatum

Abutilon stellatum, commonly known as the velvetleaf abutilon, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters tall, featuring heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces showy, lantern-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange, blooming in clusters. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, hairy seeds.

Abutilon stellatum is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in well-drained soil and full sun.
