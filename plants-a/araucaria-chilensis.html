Araucaria chilensis

Araucaria chilensis, commonly known as the Chilean monkey puzzle tree, is a coniferous evergreen native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, scale-like leaves and a conical shape that tapers to a point. The tree produces cones that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its unique appearance and slow growth make it a notable ornamental and ecological species.
