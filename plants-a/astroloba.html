Astroloba

Astroloba, commonly known as the starfish plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, star-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant typically grows in a rosette form, with leaves that can range in color from blue-green to purple. Astroloba species are drought-tolerant and thrive in well-draining soil.

They are popular among succulent enthusiasts for their striking appearance and ease of care.
