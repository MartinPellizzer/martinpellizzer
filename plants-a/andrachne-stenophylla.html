Andrachne stenophylla

Andrachne stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf sandmat, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and succulent, fleshy leaves. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and have a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

Andrachne stenophylla is often found in desert scrub and creosote bush communities.
