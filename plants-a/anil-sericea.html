Anil sericea

Anil sericea, commonly known as silverleaf nightshade, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features silver-gray, hair-covered leaves that give it its common name, and it produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically purple or blue in color. The plant is known for its toxic properties, containing alkaloids that can be harmful to humans and animals. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

Anil sericea is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
