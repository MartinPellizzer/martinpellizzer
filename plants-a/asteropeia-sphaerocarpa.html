Asteropeia sphaerocarpa

Asteropeia sphaerocarpa, commonly known as the globular aster.

It is a succulent plant native to South Africa, characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit. The plant features small, fleshy leaves that are typically green or bluish-gray in color. It produces clusters of tiny, star-shaped yellow flowers during the warmer months.

Asteropeia sphaerocarpa is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
