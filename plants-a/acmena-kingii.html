Acmena kingii

Acmena kingii, commonly known as the red-leafed acmena, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that turn vibrant red or orange in autumn. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by edible, red, fleshy berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its seasonal color changes and adaptability to various garden environments.
