Actinostemon polymorphus var. obovatus

Actinostemon polymorphus var. obovatus, commonly known as the broad-leafed actinostemon, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to certain regions of Africa.

It features distinctive, obovate leaves that are often hairy and have a rough texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or shaded areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique addition to botanical gardens and ecological studies.
