Anemone hortensis proles stellata

Anemone hortensis proles stellata, commonly known as the star anemone, is a perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features showy, white or pink flower heads that resemble a star, composed of numerous small, petal-like bracts. The plant has a short, upright stem with basal leaves that are heart-shaped and hairy. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for garden borders and woodland gardens.

Its blooming period typically occurs in early to mid-summer, adding a delicate floral display to the landscape.
