Anthericum lanzae

Anthericum lanzae, commonly known as the Lanzan arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its tropical and subtropical habitats. The plant features a rosette of arrowhead-shaped leaves and produces striking spadices, which are modified leaves that enclose the inflorescence. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth.

Anthericum lanzae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shady garden settings.
