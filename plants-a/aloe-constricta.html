Aloe constricta

Aloe constricta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, erect stems with narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple margins. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a panicle inflorescence, which bloom in the spring. Aloe constricta is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is sometimes used in traditional remedies.
