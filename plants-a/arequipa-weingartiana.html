Arequipa weingartiana

Arequipa weingartiana, commonly known as the Arequipa lily, is a flowering plant native to the high Andes of southern Peru.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils at elevations above 3,000 meters. Its flowers bloom in late summer and are pollinated primarily by insects.

Arequipa weingartiana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in specialized gardens.
