Aristolochia esperanzae var. longilabra

Aristolochia esperanzae var. longilabra, commonly known as the Esperanza Aristolochia, is a member of the Aristolochiaceae family and is native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its distinctive, heart-shaped leaves and its unique, winding, pipe-shaped flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. The plant produces small, green berries that mature to a dark red or black color, containing seeds that are dispersed by animals. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, well-drained soils under shaded conditions.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and traditional herbal practices.
