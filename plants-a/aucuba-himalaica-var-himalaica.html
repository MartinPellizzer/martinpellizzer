Aucuba himalaica var. himalaica

Aucuba himalaica var. himalaica, commonly known as the Himalayan aucuba, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia.

It features large, glossy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with yellow or white margins. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value.

Its evergreen foliage makes it a popular choice for landscaping in temperate climates.
