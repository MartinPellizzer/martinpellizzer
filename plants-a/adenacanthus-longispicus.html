Adenacanthus longispicus

Adenacanthus longispicus, commonly known as the long-spined adenocanthus, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and small, clustered flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves with a rough texture and a distinctive, aromatic scent. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in regions with warm climates.
