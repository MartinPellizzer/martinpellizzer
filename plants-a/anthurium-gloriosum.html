Anthurium gloriosum

Anthurium gloriosum, commonly known as the velvet leaf anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces striking spadices, which are modified leaves that enclose the true flowers. Anthurium gloriosum thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and greenhouse cultivation.
