Antidesma membranaceum var. glabrescens

Antidesma membranaceum var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth antidesma, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, followed by woody capsules containing numerous seeds. The species is noted for its adaptability to various environmental conditions and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
