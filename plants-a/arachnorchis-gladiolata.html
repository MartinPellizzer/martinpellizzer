Arachnorchis gladiolata

Arachnorchis gladiolata, commonly known as the gladiolus orchid, is a species of orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, sword-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep purple. The plant typically grows in heathland and open forests, thriving in well-drained soils. Its inflorescence consists of several flowers arranged in a spike, with each bloom having three petals and a distinctive lip.

Arachnorchis gladiolata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
