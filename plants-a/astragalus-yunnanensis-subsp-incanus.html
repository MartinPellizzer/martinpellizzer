Astragalus yunnanensis subsp. incanus

Astragalus yunnanensis subsp.

incanus, commonly known as the Yunnan goat's beard, is a perennial herb native to China. It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its hairy, sparsely foliaged stems.

The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in dense, globular clusters, typical of the Astragalus genus. It thrives in rocky, arid environments and is often found at high altitudes.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
