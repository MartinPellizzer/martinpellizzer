Aerides flabellata

Aerides flabellata, commonly known as the flabellate orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish, with a distinctive, fan-shaped lip.

It is valued in horticulture for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
