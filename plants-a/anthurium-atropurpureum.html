Anthurium atropurpureum

Anthurium atropurpureum, commonly known as the Black Velvet plant, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark purple to black leaves that contrast strikingly with its bright orange spadix. The plant produces clusters of small, white to yellow flowers surrounded by a vibrant, heart-shaped spathe. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

Anthurium atropurpureum is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its dramatic appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
