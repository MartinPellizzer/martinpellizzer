Asplenium productum

Asplenium productum, commonly known as the shield fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are usually dark green and have a leathery texture, with a prominent midrib.

Asplenium productum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and indoor spaces.
