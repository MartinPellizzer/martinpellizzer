Anthurium cogolloanum

Anthurium cogolloanum, commonly known as the Cogollo's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to Colombia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive inflorescence consisting of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves with a deep green color and a velvety texture. It produces striking, colorful spathes that can range from red to orange, often with a contrasting midrib.

Anthurium cogolloanum is typically grown as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ability to thrive in warm, humid environments.
