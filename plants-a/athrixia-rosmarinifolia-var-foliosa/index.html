Athrixia Rosmarinifolia Var. Foliosa

Athrixia rosmarinifolia var. foliosa is a unique and striking plant native to southern Africa. It is known for its distinctive silver-green foliage and compact, rounded growth habit. This evergreen shrub thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Its ability to withstand drought and salt spray makes it particularly suited for coastal environments. Despite its hardiness, it requires careful pruning to maintain its shape and promote healthy growth.
