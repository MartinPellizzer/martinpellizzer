Anteremanthus piranii

Anteremanthus piranii, commonly known as the golden star lily, is a striking herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and vibrant color make it a standout in botanical collections.
