Aulonemia scripta

Aulonemia scripta, commonly known as the zigzag fern, is a species of fern native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its distinctive, undulating fronds that give it its common name. The plant has a creeping rhizome that produces both sterile and fertile fronds. Its leaves are typically dark green and have a leathery texture.

Aulonemia scripta thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.
