Aechmea lingulata var. patentissima

Aechmea lingulata var. patentissima, commonly known as the showy pineapple plant, is a striking bromeliad native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are covered in dense, silver-white trichomes, giving it a frosty appearance. The plant produces vibrant red or orange inflorescences that bloom in the summer months. Its foliage is often used in floral arrangements due to its ornamental value.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
