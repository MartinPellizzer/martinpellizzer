Adiantum papilio

Adiantum papilio, commonly known as the flying mouse fern, is a delicate, fern-like plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It features finely divided, feather-like fronds that give it a light, airy appearance. The plant's leaves are typically dark green and have a glossy surface, with a unique, almost translucent quality. Adiantum papilio thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, moist soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and shady garden landscapes.
