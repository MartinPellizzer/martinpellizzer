Aeonium doremae

Aeonium doremae, commonly known as the Blue Ghost Aeonium, is a succulent plant native to the Canary Islands.

It features rosettes of thick, waxy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, yellowish flowers during the spring or early summer. It is relatively drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Aeonium doremae is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its striking color and easy care requirements.
