Acrostichum aureum var. speciosum

Acrostichum aureum var. speciosum, commonly known as the golden fern, is a variant of the golden fern species native to tropical regions.

It is a perennial fern that thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in humid climates. The plant features large, bright yellow fronds that are often adorned with intricate patterns and a distinctive, feathery appearance. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with each segment bearing a serrated edge.

This ornamental plant is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
