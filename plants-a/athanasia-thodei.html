Athanasia thodei

Athanasia thodei, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy yellow flowers with a central disk. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Athanasia thodei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its hardiness and ability to attract pollinators.
