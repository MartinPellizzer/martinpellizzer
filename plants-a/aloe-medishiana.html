Aloe medishiana

Aloe medishiana, commonly known as the Natal aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The leaves are covered in a white powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value to the plant.

Aloe medishiana is well-suited for cultivation in dry climates and is often used in xeriscaping.
