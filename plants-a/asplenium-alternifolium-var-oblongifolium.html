Asplenium alternifolium var. oblongifolium

Asplenium alternifolium var. oblongifolium, commonly known as the oblong-leaf spleenwort, is a fern species belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and dark green, lanceolate to oblong-shaped leaves. The fronds are typically short and upright, with a dense arrangement that gives the plant a bushy appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found growing on rock surfaces or in crevices.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in shaded garden settings.
