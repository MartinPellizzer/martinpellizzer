Agave montserratensis

Agave montserratensis, commonly known as the Montserrat agave, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces a tall flower stalk that bears yellow flowers, which bloom in the spring. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Agave montserratensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional uses in local cultures.
