Acrostichum welwitschii

Acrostichum welwitschii, commonly known as the African shield fern, is a species of fern native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, shield-shaped fronds that can grow up to 2 meters in length. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its rhizomes are fibrous and can spread to form dense colonies.

The fronds are typically dark green and have a leathery texture, making it a distinctive and ornamental plant.
