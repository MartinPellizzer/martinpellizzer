Arisaema menghaiense

Arisaema menghaiense, commonly known as the Menghai arrowhead, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to China, particularly in the Yunnan province, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant features a distinctive arrow-shaped leaf and produces a spadix surrounded by a spathe, typical of the arum family. Its flowering period occurs in the spring, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The plant has also been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
