Arenaria fursei

Arenaria fursei, commonly known as the sandspurry, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas and dry grasslands. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are narrow and fleshy, adapted to conserve water in arid environments.

Arenaria fursei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature.
