Andromeda hahniana

Andromeda hahniana, commonly known as the Hahn's wintergreen, is a perennial evergreen shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries, and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces small, bell-shaped white or pale pink flowers in the spring, which are clustered in racemes. Its leaves are leathery and aromatic, often emitting a wintergreen scent when crushed.

Andromeda hahniana thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
