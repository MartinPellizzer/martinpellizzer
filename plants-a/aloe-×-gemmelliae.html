Aloe × gemmelliae

Aloe × gemmelliae, commonly known as the hybrid aloe or false aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Aloe ferox and Aloe arborescens, resulting in a plant with unique characteristics. The leaves are thick, fleshy, and often have a bluish-green hue with white or yellowish markings. It produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow-orange flowers that bloom in spring.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
