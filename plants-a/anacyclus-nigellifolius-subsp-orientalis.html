Anacyclus nigellifolius subsp. orientalis

Anacyclus nigellifolius subsp.

orientalis, commonly known as the oriental fennel. This plant is a flowering herb native to regions of the Middle East and Central Asia.

It features a rosette of basal leaves that are lanceolate and covered in fine hairs. The flower heads are yellow and resemble those of daisies, with a central disk surrounded by ray florets.

It is often cultivated for its aromatic properties and used in traditional medicine.
