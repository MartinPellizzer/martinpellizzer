Artabotrys modestus subsp. modestus

Artabotrys modestus subsp.

modestus, commonly known as the modest artabotrys, is a tropical climbing shrub native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often emitting a strong, sweet scent.

The plant has smooth, glabrous stems and heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically arranged oppositely. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The fruit is a spherical, fleshy berry that turns red when mature, containing several seeds.
