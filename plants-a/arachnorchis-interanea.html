Arachnorchis interanea

Arachnorchis interanea, commonly known as the Christmas Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its unique, intricate flower structure, which features a complex arrangement of petals and sepals. The plant typically blooms during the winter months, producing striking, fragrant flowers that are often pale pink or white. It grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in shaded woodland areas.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
