Abutilon pleniflorum

Abutilon pleniflorum, commonly known as the velvetleaf abutilon, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in soft, velvety hairs. The plant produces clusters of showy, lantern-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and waste areas.

Abutilon pleniflorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a garden plant.
