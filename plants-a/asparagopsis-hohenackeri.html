Asparagopsis hohenackeri

Asparagopsis hohenackeri, commonly known as the Hohenacker's asparagus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its erect, succulent stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, white or greenish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
