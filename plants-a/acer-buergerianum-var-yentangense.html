Acer buergerianum var. yentangense

Acer buergerianum var. yentangense, commonly known as the Yentang maple, is a rare and ornamental species native to China.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that turn vibrant shades of red and orange in autumn. The plant typically grows as a small to medium-sized tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, appearing in early spring before the leaves emerge.
