Anthurium betsyae

Anthurium betsyae, commonly known as the Betsy's Arrowhead, is a tropical flowering plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence composed of a spadix and a spathe. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy, dark green leaves that are often adorned with striking white or yellow veins. Anthurium betsyae produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically surrounded by a colorful, showy spathe.

It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.
