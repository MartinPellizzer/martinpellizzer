Arisaema heterocephalum subsp. majus

Arisaema heterocephalum subsp.

majus, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a member of the Araceae family. It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and distinctive spadix inflorescence.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its vegetative structure includes a rhizomatous root system that allows for vegetative propagation.

The flowering period occurs in the summer, with the spathe often displaying a striking green or yellowish color.
