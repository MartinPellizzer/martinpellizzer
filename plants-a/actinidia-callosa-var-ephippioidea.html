Actinidia callosa var. ephippioidea

Actinidia callosa var. ephippioidea, commonly known as the Chinese mayapple, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features compound leaves with three to five oval leaflets, often covered in a fine, white pubescence. The plant produces small, yellow-green flowers in the spring, which are followed by edible, orange-red berries in the fall. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental foliage and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
