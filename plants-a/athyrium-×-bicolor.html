Athyrium × bicolor

Athyrium × bicolor, commonly known as the variegated lady fern, is a hybrid fern that exhibits distinctive variegation in its fronds.

It features a combination of green, white, and sometimes pink or yellow hues, creating an ornamental appearance. The plant has delicate, bipinnate fronds that are often arching or drooping, contributing to its graceful look. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often used in horticultural settings for its aesthetic value.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a unique and visually appealing fern.
