Ascium aubletii

Ascium aubletii, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant yellow petals and a distinctive star-shaped structure in its flower. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers bloom in clusters, often displaying a mix of yellow and green hues.

Ascium aubletii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is a popular choice for botanical gardens and collections.
