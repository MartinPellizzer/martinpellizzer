Astrantia speciosa

Astrantia speciosa, commonly known as the showy aster, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features showy, pink to purple flower heads that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has basal leaves that are heart-shaped and often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are composed of small, tubular florets surrounded by ray florets, giving it a daisy-like appearance.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges and meadows.
