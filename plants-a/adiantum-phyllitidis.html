Adiantum phyllitidis

Adiantum phyllitidis, commonly known as the rock fern, is a species of fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that are typically light green and finely divided. The plant prefers shaded, moist environments and is often found growing on rocks or in crevices. Its reproductive structures, known as sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Adiantum phyllitidis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
