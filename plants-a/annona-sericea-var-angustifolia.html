Annona sericea var. angustifolia

Annona sericea var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf soursop, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its slender, angular leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. The plant produces large, green, spiny fruits that are typically harvested when fully mature. The flowers are fragrant and typically white or pale yellow, with a distinctive corona of lobes.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses.
