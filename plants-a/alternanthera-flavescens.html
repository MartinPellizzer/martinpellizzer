Alternanthera flavescens

Alternanthera flavescens, commonly known as the goldenAlternanthera, is a herbaceous plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow to orange-red foliage that changes with the seasons. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks. It produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are clustered in spikes.
