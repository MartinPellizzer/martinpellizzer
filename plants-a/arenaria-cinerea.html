Arenaria cinerea

Arenaria cinerea, commonly known as silver sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arctic and alpine regions.

It features small, grayish-blue leaves that are covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of tiny white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained, sandy or rocky soils and is often found in open habitats.

Due to its hardy nature, it is adapted to cold climates and can withstand harsh environmental conditions.
