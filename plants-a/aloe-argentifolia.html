Aloe argentifolia

Aloe argentifolia, commonly known as silver aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically covered in a silvery-gray wax, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers, adding ornamental value. Its leaves contain a gel-like substance that is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

Aloe argentifolia thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
