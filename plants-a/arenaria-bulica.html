Arenaria bulica

Arenaria bulica, commonly known as the sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to temperate regions.

It typically features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, often blooming in early spring. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in arid environments. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or alpine habitats.

Its simple structure and adaptive features make it a hardy species well-suited to harsh conditions.
