Anthurium poiteanum

Anthurium poiteanum, commonly known as the Poitean arrowhead, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves and striking spadix inflorescence. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers surrounded by a colorful spathe, which is often the primary ornamental feature. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Anthurium poiteanum is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
