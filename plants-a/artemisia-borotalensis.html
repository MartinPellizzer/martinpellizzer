Artemisia borotalensis

Artemisia borotalensis, commonly known as the Borotalan wormwood, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-gray foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant thrives in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to high altitudes. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes many other medicinal and aromatic plants.

Due to its chemical composition, it has been used traditionally in folk medicine for its purported health benefits.
