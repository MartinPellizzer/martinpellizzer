Areca laxa

Areca laxa, commonly known as the slender areca palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and pinnately compound leaves that give it a feathery appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by bright red fruits containing seeds. Areca laxa thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its elegant foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
