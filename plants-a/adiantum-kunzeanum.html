Adiantum kunzeanum

Adiantum kunzeanum, commonly known as the Kunze's maidenhair fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Adiantaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically dark green and have a feathery appearance. The plant prefers cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded, forested areas. Its spores are produced on the undersides of the fronds, which are protected by a specialized structure called a sorus.

Adiantum kunzeanum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
