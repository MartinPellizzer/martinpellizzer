Asplenium glaucostipes

Asplenium glaucostipes, commonly known as the silver-stem cliff fern, is a species of fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Aspleniaceae and is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage. The plant typically grows on rocky surfaces or in shaded, moist environments, often found in mountainous regions. Its fronds are leathery and have a distinct silvery sheen on the stems, which gives it its common name.

Asplenium glaucostipes is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in horticulture for its unique appearance.
