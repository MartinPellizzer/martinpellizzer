Astragalus qingheensis

Astragalus qingheensis, commonly known as the Qinghe milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its erect stems and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid regions, adapting to harsh environmental conditions. It has small, compound leaves and produces pods that contain several seeds.

Due to its limited distribution and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
