Ardisia hypargyrea

Ardisia hypargyrea, commonly known as the silverleaf ardisia, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a waxy texture. Its small, white flowers are clustered in umbels and are followed by bright red berries that are an important food source for birds.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and seasonal fruit display.
