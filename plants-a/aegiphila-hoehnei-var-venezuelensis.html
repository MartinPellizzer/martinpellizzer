Aegiphila hoehnei var. venezuelensis

Aegiphila hoehnei var. venezuelensis, commonly known as the Venezuelan Aegiphila, is a flowering plant native to South America, specifically Venezuela.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its small, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of purple or pink flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories or rocky areas.
