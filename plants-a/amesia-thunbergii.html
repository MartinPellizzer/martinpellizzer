Amesia thunbergii

Amesia thunbergii, commonly known as silverleaf fleabane, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and clusters of small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sunlight. It is often found in woodland edges, meadows, and disturbed sites.

Amesia thunbergii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
