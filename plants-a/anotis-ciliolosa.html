Anotis ciliolosa

Anotis ciliolosa, commonly known as the silky anther orchid, is a species of orchid native to South Africa.

It is a terrestrial herb with a compact, upright growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and green. The plant features a pseudobulb that supports its growth and stores water, enabling it to survive in arid conditions. Its leaves are linear and covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The flowers are typically arranged in a loose spike and bloom during the autumn season.
