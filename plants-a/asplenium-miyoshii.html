Asplenium miyoshii

Asplenium miyoshii, commonly known as the Miyoshi's spleenwort, is a small, evergreen fern belonging to the Aspleniaceae family.

It is native to Japan and is typically found growing on shaded, moist rock faces or in crevices. The plant has delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and slightly glossy. Its reproductive structures, called sori, are located on the underside of the fronds and produce spores for propagation.

Asplenium miyoshii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is appreciated in botanical gardens for its unique appearance.
