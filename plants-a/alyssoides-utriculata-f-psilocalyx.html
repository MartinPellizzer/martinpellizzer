Alyssoides utriculata f. psilocalyx

Alyssoides utriculata f. psilocalyx, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bladderpod, is a flowering plant species belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, herbaceous structure and features narrow, elongated leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes, which are typical of the Brassicaceae family. Its fruits are specialized pods, known as siliques, which contain numerous tiny seeds.

This variety is adapted to arid environments and is often found in sandy or gravelly soils.
