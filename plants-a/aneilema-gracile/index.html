Aneilema Gracile

Small-leaved aneilema, scientifically known as Aneilema gracile, is a striking tropical plant native to western Africa. With its delicate, narrow leaves and clusters of vibrant blue flowers, it stands out in its natural habitat. This species is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and riverbanks. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for both ornamental gardening and botanical studies. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in the global horticultural community.
