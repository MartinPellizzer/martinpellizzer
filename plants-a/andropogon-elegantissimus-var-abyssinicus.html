Andropogon elegantissimus var. abyssinicus

Andropogon elegantissimus var. abyssinicus, commonly known as the Abyssinian bluestem, is a tall grass species native to Ethiopia.

It is characterized by its slender, blue-green foliage and tufted inflorescences that bloom in late summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in open grasslands and rocky areas. Its fibrous roots help stabilize soil, making it useful for erosion control.

This variant is noted for its ornamental value and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
