Acarna lancea

Acarna lancea, commonly known as the lanceleaf arum, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features arrowhead-shaped leaves that are glossy and deep green, often with a reddish underside. The plant produces striking, spatulate inflorescences that are typically yellow or greenish in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid forests.

Acarna lancea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
