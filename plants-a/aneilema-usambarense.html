Aneilema usambarense

Aneilema usambarense, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and showy, violet-colored flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in humid, well-drained soil and prefers indirect sunlight. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.

Aneilema usambarense is also valued for its ability to purify the air and enhance indoor environments.
