Adesmia miottoae

Adesmia miottoae, commonly known as the Miotto's legume, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes many legumes known for their nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets.
