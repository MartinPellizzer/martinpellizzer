Athyrium hahasimense

Athyrium hahasimense, commonly known as the Hahasimense Japanese Painted Fern, is a perennial fern native to Japan.

It features delicate, lace-like fronds with a striking combination of light green and silvery-white hues. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, preferring well-drained soil. Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal.

It is relatively low maintenance and thrives in USDA hardiness zones 5 through 8.
