Albuca paucifolia subsp. karooparkensis

Albuca paucifolia subsp.

karooparkensis, commonly known as the Karoo park lily, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, star-shaped white flowers.

The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for low-maintenance gardening.
