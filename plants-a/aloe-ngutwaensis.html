Aloe ngutwaensis

Aloe ngutwaensis, commonly known as the Ngutwa aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing yellow to orange flowers. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, requiring minimal water and well-draining soil.

Aloe ngutwaensis is valued for its hardy nature and potential medicinal properties.
