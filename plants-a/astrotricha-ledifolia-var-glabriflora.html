Astrotricha ledifolia var. glabriflora

Astrotricha ledifolia var. glabriflora, commonly known as the smooth-flowered starwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its small, clustered yellow flowers and hairy, lobed leaves. This perennial species is native to certain regions of North America, particularly in the western United States. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and streambanks.

Its botanical features make it a notable species for ecological studies and botanical gardens.
