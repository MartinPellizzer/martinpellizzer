Adesmia jilesiana

Adesmia jilesiana, commonly known as the Jiles' legume, is a perennial shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-bearing fruits. The plant features trifoliate leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellowish flowers clustered in racemes. Its root system is deep and drought-tolerant, allowing it to thrive in nutrient-poor soils.

Adesmia jilesiana is also noted for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility in its ecosystem.
