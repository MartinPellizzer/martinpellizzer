Achyranthes nuda

Achyranthes nuda, commonly known as naked spiny lettuce, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or along stream banks.

Its fibrous roots and rapid growth make it an effective pioneer species in disturbed habitats.
