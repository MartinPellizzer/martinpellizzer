Agave densispina

Agave densispina, commonly known as the dense spine agave, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped growth pattern and thick, fleshy leaves covered in sharp spines. The leaves are typically blue-gray in color and can grow up to 1 meter in length. It produces tall flower stalks that bear yellowish-green flowers, which bloom once in its lifetime.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
