Arum dioscoridis var. luschanii

Arum dioscoridis var. luschanii, commonly known as the Luschan's arrowhead, is a variant of the arrowhead plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of spadix flowers surrounded by a spathe. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is known for its unique reproductive structures. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found near water bodies.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
