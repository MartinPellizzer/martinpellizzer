Aspidium rivale

Aspidium rivale, commonly known as the water fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Athyriaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are typically bioluminescent in certain conditions. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found near streams or in damp forests. Its spores are dispersed through the air, allowing it to colonize new areas effectively.

The plant's structure includes a rhizome that anchors it to the substrate and supports its growth.
