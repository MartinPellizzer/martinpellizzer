Ambrosia aspera

Ambrosia aspera, commonly known as rough ambrosia, is an annual herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and ragweed, and is characterized by its distinctive, rough-textured leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in composite heads, typical of the family. Ambrosia aspera is known for its allergenic pollen, which can cause respiratory issues in sensitive individuals.

It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and other open areas.
