Artemisia nuttallii

Artemisia nuttallii, commonly known as silver sagebrush, is a perennial shrub native to North America.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which consists of narrow, aromatic leaves with a fuzzy texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flower heads that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in open habitats such as sagebrush steppe and grasslands.

Artemisia nuttallii plays a vital role in stabilizing soils and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
