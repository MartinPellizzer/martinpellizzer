Alternanthera leptophylla

Alternanthera leptophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf Alternanthera, is a herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features striking, lance-shaped leaves that often display vibrant red or purple hues, especially when exposed to full sunlight. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow, arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sun exposure.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for water gardens and bog landscapes.
