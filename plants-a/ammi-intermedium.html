Ammi intermedium

Ammi intermedium, commonly known as yellow lantana, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features compound leaves with serrated edges and produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers. Its flowers are borne on slender, branched inflorescences, and it blooms during the summer months.

Ammi intermedium is known for its aromatic foliage and is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
