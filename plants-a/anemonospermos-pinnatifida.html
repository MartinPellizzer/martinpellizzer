Anemonospermos pinnatifida

Anemonospermos pinnatifida, commonly known as the broadleaf anemonosperma, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Anemonaceae.

It is characterized by its pinnately compound leaves, which are typically large and lobed, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, yellowish to greenish flowers that bloom in early spring. It is native to certain regions of Asia and prefers moist, shady habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
