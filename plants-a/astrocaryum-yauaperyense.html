Astrocaryum yauaperyense

Astrocaryum yauaperyense, commonly known as the Yauapé palm, is a species of palm native to the Amazon rainforest of Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces yellowish flowers followed by edible, round fruits that are often consumed by local communities. Its botanical features make it an important component of the tropical ecosystem, supporting various wildlife species.

The Yauapé palm is also valued for its ornamental qualities and traditional uses in indigenous cultures.
