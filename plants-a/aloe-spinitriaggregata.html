Aloe spinitriaggregata

Aloe spinitriaggregata, commonly known as the spiked aloe, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its spiky, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their soothing properties.
