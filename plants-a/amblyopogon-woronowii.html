Amblyopogon woronowii

Amblyopogon woronowii, commonly known as the slender grass.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae. Native to Southeast Asia, it is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the understory vegetation in tropical and subtropical regions.
