Acacia plumosa

Acacia plumosa, commonly known as the feathery wattle, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery yellow flowers that appear in spring. The plant has slender, branched stems and small, bipinnate leaves that give it a soft, fluffy appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Acacia plumosa is also valued for its ability to provide nectar for native pollinators.
