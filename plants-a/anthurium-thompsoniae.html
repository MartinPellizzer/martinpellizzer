Anthurium thompsoniae

Anthurium thompsoniae, commonly known as the Thompson's anthurium, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a colorful spathe, typically in shades of red or orange. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Anthurium thompsoniae is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and unique flowering structure.
