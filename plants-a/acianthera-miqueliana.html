Acianthera miqueliana

Acianthera miqueliana, commonly known as the Miquel's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, erect stems and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in a loose cluster. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
