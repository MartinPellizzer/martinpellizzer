Anthemis meteorica

Anthemis meteorica, commonly known as the starflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its small, star-shaped yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer.

Anthemis meteorica is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
