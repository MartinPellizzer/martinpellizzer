Asplenium bifrons

Asplenium bifrons, commonly known as the forked spleenwort, is a small, epiphytic fern that thrives in moist, shaded environments.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit and dark green, lance-shaped leaves. The plant features a distinctive, forked structure at the base of its fronds, which gives it its common name. It typically grows on tree trunks or rocks in temperate regions.

Asplenium bifrons is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
