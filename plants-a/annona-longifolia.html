Annona longifolia

Annona longifolia, commonly known as the longifolia soursop, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces large, heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. Its flowers are white and fragrant, typically appearing in clusters.

The fruit is a spiny, green berry that turns yellow when mature, containing a soft, white, edible pulp with black seeds.
