Arisaema sarracenioides

Arisaema sarracenioides, commonly known as the laceleaf arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with intricate, veined patterns that resemble lace. The plant produces unique, spadix-shaped inflorescences surrounded by a hood-like spathe. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, moist soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
