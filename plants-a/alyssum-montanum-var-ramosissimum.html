Alyssum montanum var. ramosissimum

Alyssum montanum var. ramosissimum, commonly known as mountain alyssum, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant has a compact, mat-forming habit with hairy stems and leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its fragrant blooms and ability to attract pollinators.
