Amaryllis belladonna subsp. haywardii

Amaryllis belladonna subsp.

haywardii, commonly known as the Hayward amaryllis, is a bulbous plant native to South Africa. It produces striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink to red in color.

The plant features strap-like, evergreen leaves that are broad and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright light.

This subspecies is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
