Anacyclus kruegerianus

Anacyclus kruegerianus, commonly known as the Krueger's goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, spiky yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It has basal leaves that are narrow and linear, often with a hairy texture.
