Armeria sampaioi

Armeria sampaioi, commonly known as the Sampaio's thrift, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the family Amaryllidaceae and is characterized by its dense clusters of small, white or pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It has basal leaves that are linear and often covered in fine hairs.
