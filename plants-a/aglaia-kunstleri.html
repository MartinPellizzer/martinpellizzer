Aglaia kunstleri

Aglaia kunstleri, commonly known as the beautyberry, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, violet-blue berries that are highly ornamental. The plant features opposite, simple, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences.

Aglaia kunstleri is often cultivated for its striking fruit and is also used in traditional medicine.
